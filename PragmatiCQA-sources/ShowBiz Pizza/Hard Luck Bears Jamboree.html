Not to be confused with the The Hard Luck Bears .

The Hard Luck Bears Jamboree are three modified Rock-afire Explosion bands that once operated at the “Gulliver's“ theme parks in the United Kingdom.

Info

All three of these modified Rock-afire Explosion shows operated at the Gulliver's Kingdom , Gulliver's Land , and Gulliver's World amusement parks, located in Matlock Bath, Warrington and Milton Keynes.

Mitzi , Fatz , Dook , and Rolfe wear original Hard Luck Bears masks, and have custom western themed cosmetics. Billy Bob , Looney Bird , Beach Bear , Choo-Choo , Earl , the Sun , and the Moon remained unchanged.

The characters' voices also sound very different than their Rock-Afire counterparts. Billy Bob is voiced by entertainer Roger Dunn, who also voices the park's mascot, Gully Mouse.

There are 4 Gulliver’s theme parks but only 3 feature the show, the shows all have small differences between each park.

Gulliver’s Kingdom in Matlock, Derbyshire has a setup similar to Disney’s Country Bear Jamboree show, with Looney Bird and Choo-Choo in front of the center stage, raising from the floor, instead of next to Billy Bob, and also features a carousel behind Rolfe and Earl.

Gulliver’s Land in Milton Keynes has Choo-Choo in front of Beach Bear instead of next to Dook’s drums, and no rain curtain on Rolfe’s stage.

Gulliver’s in Warrington has Billy Bob wearing a cowboy hat.

Gulliver’s also have a Billy Bob walkaround currently located at the Matlock park, as well as “Billy Bob’s Bi-Planes” - a children’s airplane ride themed around Billy Bob and the gang.

As of 2017, none of the three shows are running, and the signs have been removed.

The theatre at Gulliver's World in Warrington is now being used for a magic show with the show abandoned behind black curtains, whilst the theatre at Matlock is being used as an employee changing room. The theatre at Milton Keynes is being used for storage.

Trivia

  • As mentioned before, the characters wear masks taken from the original Hard Luck Bears Show. These masks were supplied by Creative Engineering when Gulliver’s bought the shows.
  • Fatz has a brown-painted Billy Bill mask, Dook has a Billy Boy Mask, Mitzi has a modified Pete Thornsberry mask with makeup, and Rolfe has a Repete Thornsberry mask.
  • Earl also has custom cosmetics, but keeps the same mask and fur.
  • There are several showtapes that were created for the show by Gulliver’s. Each has Gulliver’s employees voicing the characters. These showtapes, aside from one, have become lost media.
  • The 2008 showtaoe features the songs “Hillbilly Rock, Hillbilly Roll“, “9 to 5”, “Let Your Love Flow”, “Rhinestone Cowboy”, and “Dance The Night Away”. Clips of this show appear on YouTube.
  • The 2011 showtape features “Hillbilly Rock, Hillbilly Roll“, “Tell Me Ma”, “Big Rock Candy Mountain”, “Hoedown Throwdown”, “The Runaway Train” and “5,6,7,8”. This show can be found on YouTube.
  • The 2005 Christmas show features “Jingle Bells”. Not much else is known about this.
  • The 2011 Christmas show features “Jingle Bells“ and “The Christmas Alphabet”. This show is also lost.
  • More showtapes exist, but not much is known about them.
  • The shows have special signs and cosmetics for Christmas and Halloween.
