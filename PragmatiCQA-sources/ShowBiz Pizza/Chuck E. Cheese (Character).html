Chuck E. Cheese (full name: Charles Entertainment Cheese ) is the mascot of the Chuck E. Cheese restaurant chain (formerly Pizza Time Theatre ), and frontman of Munch's Make Believe Band .

References in Pop Culture

  • Don't Tell Mom the Babysitter's Dead (1991) - CEC Walkaround spotted in the Chuck E Cheese scene.
  • Made (2001) - The characters are at Chuck E Cheese's.
  • Garfield (2004) - Mentioned by Garfield.
  • American Dad! (animated series) (2005) - Roger was in a Chuck E. Cheese animatronic.
  • Yes Dear (2006) - Greg is seen being chased as Chuck E.
  • The Hangover (2009) - Alan says he is not supposed to be within 200 feet of a school or Chuck E Cheese.
  • The Middle (series) (2009) - Bob, Frankie's co-worker and friend, says he was the birthday rat at Chuck E Cheese's
  • Free Birds (2013) - CEC Pizza boxes are delivered to Reggie, and at the end as the new Thanksgiving meal.
  • The Muppets (series) 2015-2016 - Miss Piggy states to Ed Helms that they had an engagement party at Chuck E Cheese's
  • Alvin and the Chipmunks: The Road Chip (2015) - The Chipmunks and Dave stop at a Chuck E. Cheese's on the drive to Miami.
  • Ralph Breaks the Internet (2018) - A clip of Chuck E. Cheese in the Galaxy 5000 plays on the video wall in the BuzzzzTube website as one of the trending videos.
  • Robot Chicken (animated series) (2019) - One night after closing, Chuck E., Helen, and Munch are talking, when they notice Pasqually has frozen. He soon catches fire, and the others try to run for help, but find they're bolted to the stage, causing them to catch fire. Oddly, Jasper is absent in this skit.
