Duncan Brannan (born June 25, 1970) is an American voice actor who has provided voices for a number of children's entertainment characters, English language versions of Japanese anime films, and other commercial properties.

From 1994 to 2012, he provided the voice for Chuck E. Cheese at Chuck E. Cheese's restaurants. He also voiced Mr. Munch from 1994 to 1999, Jasper T. Jowls briefly in 1994 and 1996-1997, and Pasqually briefly in 1996-1997 and 1999. Additionally, Brannan provided the singing vocals for the children's icon Barney The Dinosaur on several direct to video specials based on the kids show Barney & Friends (but never did the PBS show due to him leaving the series shortly after he got the role) in the early 2000s, replacing Bob West.

Brannan has also done voices for commercials, phone-casting, industrials, narrative, and religious markets. A few of his credits are Brinker's International for Chili's, Maggiano's, On the Border and Romano's Macaroni Grill, First United Bank, Lone Star College System, Methodist Health Systems, and Sean Hannity.

Brannan also works as a freelance writer, speaker, and fine arts consultant.

He has also been credited as Duncan Brennan.
