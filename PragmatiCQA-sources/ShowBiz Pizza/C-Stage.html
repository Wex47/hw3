The C-Stage was a house variation of the Balcony Stage . All characters on the C-Stage were half-bodied.

History

The C-Stage was first installed in San Antonio, Texas in 1988.

The stage began to be phased out in the early 1990s in favor of the Road , 1 , and 2-Stages . Some restaurants continued to have the C-Stage between 1995 and 2003, and have had the 90's Helen cosmetics to replace the Broadway Helen cosmetics. (the last one being located in Tacoma, Washington)

The C-Stage can be thought of as a combination of the Balcony Stage and Rocker Stage as it seems to be a house variation of the Balcony while this was the main theme of the Rocker.
