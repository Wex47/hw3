Pasqually P. Pieplate is the drummer for Munch's Make Believe Band at Chuck E. Cheese restaurants. He is also chief pizza chef and an aspiring comedian. His name is a phonetic misspelling of the Italian name .

Bio

Pasqually P. Pieplate was an original Pizza Time Player along with his friends, Chuck E. Cheese , Jasper T. Jowls , Crusty the Cat , and The Warblettes . He was originally placed behind a set of doors and called the pizza orders (this was later stopped because it required too much work from the technicians).

In his Balcony Stage incarnation, he played the accordion, but later became the main drummer for Munch's Make Believe Band. He is known for having unusual hobbies, such as collecting mold, and for being an avid fan of polka, much to the band's chagrin.

On the 3-Stage , the animatronic used for Pasqually was originally that of Dook LaRue from ShowBiz Pizza Place , which he replaced during Concept Unification . Note that in the Concept Unification Prototype , he was going to replace Fatz Geronimo .

Trivia

  • He is the first Chuck E. Cheese character besides Chuck E. Cheese himself to get a brand trademark, that being "Pasqually's Pizza & Wings", the order service of Chuck E. Cheese on GrubHub.
  • Unlike other characters such as Jasper T. Jowls and Helen Henny , Pasqually’s surname "Pieplate" is rarely used; he is generally referred to as simply "Pasqually" or as "Pasqually the Chef".
