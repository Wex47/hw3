Looney Bird's was a small short-lived restaurant chain that spun off of ShowBiz Pizza Place . It contained The Rock-afire Explosion and gave its focus on the character Looney Bird , which was the chain's mascot. Three locations existed before they closed down. The first location was a test location in Orlando, Florida with a New Rock-afire Explosion Stage , which opened in 1993 . The two other locations where located in Jackson, Tennessee and Covington, Louisiana, however, both of these locations had the Classic 3-Stage Rock-afire Explosion .
