The Miru Nuva was the Kanohi Nuva Mask of Levitation , formed when a destined Kanohi Miru was exposed to Energized Protodermis and transformed. The Miru Nuva gave its user and those nearby the power to float and glide on air currents. When used in conjunction with a Kakama Nuva , the mask allowed the user(s) to levitate upwards at high speeds. The only beings able to activate this mask's power were Toa Nuva .

Example Usage

In Comic 8: The End of the Toa? , Lewa Nuva combined the power of his Miru Nuva with Pohatu Nuva's Kakama Nuva to help all the Toa Nuva escape the collapsing Bohrok nest.

Set Information

  • A green Miru Nuva was released in 2002 with the Lewa Nuva set. The Miru Nuva was also released in red, blue, brown, black, and white as part of the Mask Packs, representing the Kanohi Nuva collected by the other Toa Nuva. It also came in a pearl silver version to indicate the depowered form of Lewa Nuva.
  • The green Miru Nui was also used to represent the Rua Nuva of Wairuha Nuva .
  • A lime green Miru Nuva was released in 2008 with the Lewa Nuva Phantoka set to represent the sky-adapted form his mask and armor assumed.
  • A slightly different version of the Miru Nuva was also released in 2008 as part of the Axalara T9 set to represent the vehicle-adapted form Lewa's mask took.
