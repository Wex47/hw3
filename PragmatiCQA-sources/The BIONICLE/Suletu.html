Suletu
Toa Inika Kongu's organic Great Suletu
Mask of Telepathy
Power(s) Let user read minds and project thoughts
Kanoka Unknown
Notable Wearer(s) Krakua , Kongu (Formerly)
Pronunciation SOO-lay-TOO

The Kanohi Suletu was the Mask of Telepathy . It gave its user the power to project their thoughts into the minds of others and read the thoughts of others. One common attack method with this Kanohi was creating mental noise in an opponent's mind. A Suletu is always on at a low level.
