Weapon Information

Status Destroyed

The Fire Greatswords were the pair of Toa Tools used by the Toa Mangai Lhikan to channel his Elemental Fire abilities. The swords could be combined, allowing them to function either as a large shield or a flying surfboard. When Lhikan transformed into a Turaga , the Fire Greatswords shrank and fused together into a shield. They were absorbed into Makuta Teridax at the time of Lhikan's death, destroying them in the process.

Example Usage

Toa Lhikan wielded the Fire Greatswords in combat against the Dark Hunters Nidhiki and Krekka in BIONICLE 2: Legends of Metru Nui . He also used their flight mode to escape from the conflict when outmatched and cornered in the Great Temple .
