Species Information
Colors Blue and white
Habitat Places with a lot of electrical power
Tools and Abilities Various
Pronouciation

Vo-Matoran were Matoran with innate Lightning powers. Although not familiar in places such as Metru Nui , Mata Nui , Voya Nui , or Mahri Nui , Vo-Matoran were known to settle around areas with an abundance of power. Their elemental trait was that they had electrical resistance. Vo-Matoran were mainly blue and white in color, and were all female.

Known Vo-Matoran

The following Toa were once Vo-Matoran, but transformed into Toa:

  • Nikila - Deceased
  • Chiara

Trivia

"Vo-" is a reference to the word "volt," referencing their association with electricity.
