History

Metru Nui

Not much is known of Shasa's life before the Great Cataclysm . She was, like all Matoran on Metru Nui, called to the Coliseum . There she was placed into a Matoran Pod where she was forced into a deep sleep.

She was later saved by the Toa Metru , along with the other Matoran, and taken to the island of Mata Nui in an Airship . She was awakened by the Toa Metru's Toa Power .

Mata Nui

Shasa guarded Ga-Koro for a while during the Rahi attack during the Battle of Kini-Nui .

Later, Shasa lived in Ga-Koro and worked as a Weaver. She helped her Matoran friend Marka to build boats, making nets, sailcloths, and valuable rigging.

Return to Metru Nui

She later returned to Metru Nui, helping to rebuild it until the Staff of Artakha was used to repair the rest of the city.

Spherus Magna

She and the other Matoran later evacuated the Matoran Universe onto Spherus Magna . She is currently living with the other Ga-Matoran and the Water Tribe by the Great Sea .
