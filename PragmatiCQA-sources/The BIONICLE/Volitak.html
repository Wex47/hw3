Volitak
Toa Mahri Nuparu's altered Great Volitak
Mask of Stealth
Power(s) Made user semi-invisible and silent
Kanoka Unknown
Notable Wearer(s) Nuparu , Chiara , Nidhiki (formerly)
Pronunciation Vohl-ih-TACK

The Kanohi Volitak was the Mask of Stealth . The mask could mute common noises that the user made and render the user semi-invisible. Despite this, the user would still cast a shadow. The only masks that could see through a Volitak's usage were Arthron , Elda and Rode .
