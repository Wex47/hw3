Spirit Star
Item Information
Primary User(s) Toa
Primary Location Matoran Universe , Red Star
Functionalities Represent Toa

A Spirit Star was a star that represented the spirit of a Toa . As long as the star burned in the sky, the Toa it represented was alive. A Spirit Star would correspond to the color of its Toa's Element (i.e. brown star for a Toa of Stone ). Only Toa who accomplished a great deed could gain a Spirit Star, although this was not always the case. After the Toa Metru united together to defeat Makuta Teridax , Toa Mangai Lhikan 's Spirit Star split into six others, each representing one of the Toa Metru in the Metru Nui sky. On the island of Mata Nui , six particular stars were believed to represent the Toa Mata . Shortly before the appearance of the Seventh Toa , a seventh Spirit Star appeared alongside the Toa Mata's Stars. The Toa Inika generated their Spirit Stars immediately after their collective transformation. These Stars also orbited outside the Matoran Universe , circling around the Red Star above Voya Nui .
