History

Zadakh were one of the six primary Vahki models designed by Nuparu at the request of Turaga Dume and created by the Po-Matoran .

When Teridax caused the Great Cataclysm , Metru Nui 's power grid was overloaded, and the sudden surge of power either reprogrammed or destroyed the Vahki in their hives. The Vahki that were reprogrammed gained the sudden thought that the city was safest if nothing was alive.

The Zadakh, along with the other surviving Vahki, were destroyed by the Visorak .

Abilities and Traits

The strongest of the Vahki, Zadakh were fearless to the point of recklessness and always eager to jump into a fight.

Their Staffs of Suggestion left a target willing to follow orders from almost anyone.

Set Information

Trivia
