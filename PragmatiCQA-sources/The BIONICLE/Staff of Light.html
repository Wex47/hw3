The Staff of Light (also called the Kolhii Staff of Light ) was Takanuva 's Toa Tool . Takua received it upon donning the Avohkii and becoming a Toa. It could channel Takanuva's light power, and be used to play Kolhii . It was able to create flashes of light, a beam of concentrated light, and balls of light.

While in an alternate universe , Takanuva had his staff taken by Axonn , but it was soon replaced with a Power Lance .

Set Information

The Staff was included with the 2003 set Takanuva and Ussanui . The Staff was constructed with an axle and the blade piece. The Staff could be clipped onto Takanuva's back for storage.
