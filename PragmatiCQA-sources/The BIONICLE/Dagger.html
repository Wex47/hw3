A Dagger was the weapon wielded by Pohatu , Master of Stone. The Dagger was a sidearm used for close-ranged combat by Pohatu.

Users

  • Pohatu

Set Information

  • The Dagger came with Pohatu, and was only one piece.
  • The Dagger was originally a part of Guurahk 's Staff of Disintegration .
