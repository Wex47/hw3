Bohrok-Kal Kaita Za was a merged being formed by the combination of a Tahnok-Kal , Pahrak-Kal , and Nuhvok-Kal . Although it was possible for the Bohrok-Kal to temporarily fuse together into this being, the Bohrok-Kal Kaita Za was never formed in the storyline.

Powers and Equipment

Had it had existed, the Bohrok-Kal Kaita Za would have been controlled by the Krana-Kal Za-Kal. It would also have possessed the Elemental powers of Gravity , Plasma , and Lightning . It would have been equipped with the Plasma Shields of Pahrak-Kal, and the Gravity Shields of Nuhvok-Kal.

Set Information

The Bohrok-Kal Kaita Za could be built from the Bohrok-Kal sets Tahnok-Kal, Nuhvok-Kal, and Pahrak-Kal and instructions are divided between the three booklets. The Bohrok-Kal Kaita Za features lever situated behind either arm, which caused the corresponding "hand" to thrust forward, in a manner similar to that of the heads of the normal Bohrok and Bohrok-Kal.
