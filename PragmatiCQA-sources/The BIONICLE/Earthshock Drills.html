Earthshock Drills
Weapon Information
Power None
Function Channeling Elemental energy, drilling
User(s) Toa Metru Whenua
Status Transformed into the Drill of Onua

The Earthshock Drills were Whenua 's Toa Tools that he used while he was a Toa Metru . They were able to control his power over Earth as well as spin at very high speeds, allowing him to dig his way through anything in a short time. They also produced an extremely high pitched hum, which was hard to listen to.

When Whenua was mutated into a Toa Hordika , the Earthshock Drills mutated into a pair of Thumpers .

When he later became a Turaga , they transformed into the Drill of Onua .

Example Usage

In BIONICLE Adventures 2: Trial by Fire , Whenua used his Drills' high-pitched frequency to drive away a Giant Mutant Ussal while he and Nuju were searching for the Onu-Metru Great Disk .

Set Information

  • The Earthshock Drills were released in the 2004 Toa Metru Whenua set. They could be fitted together to form one large, oval-shaped drill.
  • The drills were later released in the 2006 Velika set, as the Power Carvers .

Trivia

  • In BIONICLE Heroes , the Earthshock Drills were the second pair of weapons Nuparu used. They were later upgraded to the Laser Drill .
