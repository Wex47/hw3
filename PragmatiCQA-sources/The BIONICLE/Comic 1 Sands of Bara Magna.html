Sands of Bara Magna was the first issue of the relaunched BIONICLE: Glatorian comic series and the first comic of 2009 . It was released with the January/February 2009 edition of the LEGO Club Magazine and was the first comic to feature Pop Mhan as the artist. The comic was later collected in BIONICLE 9: The Fall of Atero . This issue served as the introduction to the new world of Bara Magna .

Plot

Berix runs for his life. While he was out looking for buried treasure to scavenge, he was attacked by Fero and his mount Skirmix . Before the Bone Hunter can deal the final blow to the Agori , Gresh interrupts him with a well-placed shot from his Thornax weapon. Although Fero threatens the Glatorian , he finds himself out-manned and out-gunned by Strakk and Tarix as well. After the fight and on their way to the Vulcanus arena, the group is ambushed by a gang of wild Vorox from the Sand Tribe . Mid-way through the fight, the Vorox are called back by their leader, the exiled Glatorian Malum . The group continues without further incident to the Fire Tribe 's village of Vulcanus.

At the arena, village leader Raanu argues with Glatorian recuiter Metus , while a Skrall bullies his way into a good seat. Raanu begins the ceremonial combat, and the fight is on! Despite Gresh's ferocity, the Skrall easily overwhelms him in a matter of minutes. Upon destroying the Jungle Tribe Glatorian's shield, the Skrall attempts a death blow, only to be stopped by Tarix. Later, the Skrall meets with a hidden figure who gives him confidential information to pass on to the Skrall leader. The Skrall returns to Roxtus , where he confers with Tuma . Tuma gloats that soon the Skrall will be able to rise up and conquer all of Bara Magna!

Notes

Continuity

  • This issue is the first installment of a soft reboot for BIONICLE, with the story shifting away from the Matoran Universe of the last eight years and now focusing on the desert world of Bara Magna.
  • The mysterious Agori traitor is first seen in this issue. His plan would later be revealed in BIONICLE: The Legend Reborn .
  • Tuma's master plan and primary motivation for conquest would be explained in Rise and Fall of the Skrall .

Trivia

  • This is the first comic which artist Pop Mhan illustrated. He would go on to illustrate the rest of the BIONICLE: Glatorian series until its final issue in 2010 .
