Elda
Toa Inika Hahli's organic Great Elda
Mask of Detection
Power(s) Let user detect hidden beings and the Ignika
Kanoka Unknown
Notable Wearer(s) Hahli (Formerly)
Pronunciation ELL-dah

The Kanohi Elda was the Mask of Detection . It gave the user the power to detect the presence of nearby hidden beings (such as a Huna or Iden user) and track the Kanohi Ignika . Few Great Elda existed, as their primary purpose was to track the Kanohi Ignika's location. The Noble Elda only allowed its user to see hidden beings. Kanohi Elda were always on at a low level.
