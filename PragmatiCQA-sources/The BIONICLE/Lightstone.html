Lightstone
Item Information
Primary User(s) Most inhabitants of the Matoran Universe
Primary Location Matoran Universe
Functionalities Illumination

Lightstones were a type of glowing crystals that grew in caves and were mined by Matoran , specifically Onu-Matoran . They were used for lighting up dark places, most notably the tunnels of Onu-Koro , Onu-Wahi , and Onu-Metru . Lightstones were typically yellow, although they were known to exist in other colors, such as blue and red.

One time on Mata Nui , the Lightstone Mine was cut off from the workers by an unexpected lava breach, thus causing Onu -Koro's work to come to a slow crawl because no one could see. Torches were instead used during the crisis. Later, thanks to the efforts of wandering amnesiac Takua , the mine was reachable again and work continued.
