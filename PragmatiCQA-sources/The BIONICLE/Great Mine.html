The Great Mine was a large cavern opposite the Le-Koro and Ta-Koro highways. It was the greatest of the Onu-Matoran mines and a work place for many Onu-Matoran , including Mamru , Taipu and an Onu-Matoran prospecter wearing a noble Matatu , named Azibo .

When Takua came here, they found a sundial that led to a room that had a golden mask. It was revealed later to be an entrance to Mangaia.

The Great Mine was flooded when the Gahlok invaded Onu-Koro . The flooding had a large economic effect on Onu-Koro. The Great Mine was later abandoned once the Matoran returned to Metru Nui . The Bohrok later destroyed it when they cleansed the island.
