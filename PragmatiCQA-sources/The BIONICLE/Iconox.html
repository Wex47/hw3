Iconox was the village of the Ice Tribe on Bara Magna . It was positioned in the southern White Quartz Mountains .

History

Iconox became the main village of the Ice Tribe after the Core War . Its inhabitants were involved in the development of the new social system. Iconox mostly thrived from the Exsidian located in the White Quartz Mountains.

Iconox lost an amount of Exsidian to the Fire Tribe , and Strakk , Gresh , Kirbold , and Tarduk were asked to take a caravan of Exsidian to the Fire Tribe. After much trouble, the Exsidian finally made it to Vulcanus , keeping the social system intact.

After the Battle of Roxtus , the Ice Tribe allied with the other tribes, living in a mega-village created by the other tribes.
