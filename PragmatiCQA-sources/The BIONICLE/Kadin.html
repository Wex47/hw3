Kadin
Toa Inika Nuparu's organic Great Kadin
Mask of Flight
Power(s) Gave user self-powered flight
Kanoka Unknown
Notable Wearer(s) Nuparu (Formerly), Jovan (Formerly)
Pronunciation kay-DIN

The Kanohi Kadin was the Mask of Flight . The Great Kadin gave its user the power to fly under their own power for long periods of time. The Noble Kadin only gave its user self-powered flight for a brief period of time. Both versions allowed flight from a standing start.
