The Mahiki was the Mask of Illusion . The Great Mahiki gave its user the power to shapeshift. The Noble Mahiki did not allow the user this ability, and only let them project physical illusions. Certain Kanohi, such as the Elda , Arthron and Rode , were able to see through the usage of a Mahiki.

Mahiki were carved out of a mixture of Reconstitute At Random and Weaken Kanoka on Metru Nui .

Set Information

  • The Noble Mahiki was first released in 2001 in a light green color as part of the Turaga Matau small set. The Mahiki was also released as part of collectible Mask Packs in orange, khaki, dark grey, light grey, and light blue to represent the collected Kanohi of the other Turaga.
  • The Great Mahiki was first released in 2004 in dark green as part of the Toa Metru Matau canister set. The Great Mahiki was re-released a year later in 2005 with the Toa Hordika Matau canister set, this time in a "mutated" form.
