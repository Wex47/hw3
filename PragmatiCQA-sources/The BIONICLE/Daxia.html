Daxia was an island that served as the Order of Mata Nui 's base.

History

Daxia was the first and largest of all the Order of Mata Nui 's bases. All the Order's members had standing orders to return to the island should any of their positions be overrun. It was here that the Toa Mata first walked and plans regarding the universe and Mata Nui were made.

Unlike most other islands, Daxia was almost void of any Rahi , except for a few species. The majority of these species were very dangerous.

Daxia was later destroyed by Makuta Teridax when he possessed the body of the Great Spirit Mata Nui.

Geography

A hot tropical landscape filled with exotic plants, Daxia was the secretive base of the Order of Mata Nui. Equipped with the latest weapons available, the island was capable of self-destruction when anyone discover it's location .

Trivia

  • There is a sculpture in Daxia which shows a Bohrok and an Av-Matoran. This was meant to symbolize the relationship between the Av-Matoran and the Bohrok. The Av-Matoran also bears physical similarities to Balta .
