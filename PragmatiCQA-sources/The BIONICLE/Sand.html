Sand
Element Information
Matoran Prefix None
Associated Color(s) Tan
Associated Location(s) Bara Magna

Sand was an Elemental Power found only on Spherus Magna , and later, Bara Magna .

Examples

  • Creating sand.
    • Creating sand from Stone .
  • Controlling sand.
    • Changing the form of sand.
  • Absorbing sand.
  • "Possessing" sand. - Element Lord -exclusive

Combinations

  • When combined with Fire , Sand could create glass.
  • When combined with Water , Sand could create mud.
  • When combined with Air , Sand could create a sand cyclone.

Users

These beings and objects were wielders of the Element of Sand:

  • The Element Lord of Sand
  • Various weapons with Sand powers that the Vorox used during the Core War
  • Pohatu (2015)

Locations

These locations were associated with the Element of Sand:

  • Bara Magna
    • Wastelands
  • Cavern of Sand

Trivia

  • In the Matoran Universe , the Great Beings did not establish Sand as an Element
  • The Dark Hunter "Devastator" and the elite Vahki Kranua both possessed the power to transform themselves into sand and reform.
