" Fohrok ", or F ake B ohrok , were a breed of false Bohrok created by the Nynrah Ghosts for the Brotherhood of Makuta . With their blue and yellow markings, they resembled a Pahrak . They served in the Brotherhood's army until the Toa Hagah destroyed them when they rebelled against the Brotherhood. Unlike Bohrok, the "Fohrok" didn't use Krana . The tribe of Nynrah Ghosts who built them were executed by Dark Hunters hired by the Makuta upon learning that they had been sabotaged.

Appearances

  • Comic 25: Birth of the Rahaga - First appearance; in flashback

Trivia

  • " Fohrok " is a non-canon fan-made name, but the machines themselves are canon.
  • Fohrok were exact duplicates of Bohrok found in a nest by a member of the Brotherhood of Makuta.
