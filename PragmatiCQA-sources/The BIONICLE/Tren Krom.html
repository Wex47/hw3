Tren Krom can refer to:

  • Tren Krom , the mysterious being who watched the Matoran Universe while Mata Nui was still under construction on Spherus Magna .
  • The Tren Krom Peninsula , the peninsula that was named after the being.
  • Tren Krom's Island , an island where the being was eventually exiled to.
  • The Tren Krom Break , a small location on the island of Mata Nui .
