Amaya's Hut was Amaya 's residance. It was originally on Metru Nui , but the one there was destroyed by the Great Cataclysm .

A new hut for Amaya was made. It was in Ga-Koro and located near Hahli's Hut and Amaya's Jetty . Amaya primarily made flax here with Hahli . Inside it was a Flax Weaver , and a pile of Harakeke plants . There was also a Jellyfish Lamp . The one on Mata Nui was destroyed by the Bohrok . Her hut on Metru Nui was later abandoned when she and the other Matoran migrated to Spherus Magna .
