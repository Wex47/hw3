BIONICLE: Rahi Beasts is a book by Greg Farshtey and Jeff James about various Rahi . It was published by Scholastic Inc. and was released on June 1, 2005. The book tells about some of the Rahi such as Nui-Jaga and, if known, their origin and abilities. The book also mentions several previously unknown Rahi such as Fader Bulls , created by fans. The first place Rahi, the Tahtorak , was featured in Bionicle Adventures 8: Challenge of the Hordika and on the cover of the book. At the bottom of each page is a quote from one of the six Rahaga . In the back of the book is the Appendix, which lists some Rahi which did not receive a page in the book.
