Certavus was a Prime Glatorian who worked for the Ice Tribe . One of the most famous warriors of his time, Certavus influenced Glatorian for generations to come, becoming a symbol of the arena on Bara Magna .

History

Certavus fought as a commander in the Core War under the Element Lord of Ice . After the Shattering , he ended up on Bara Magna . He became the Prime Glatorian for the Ice Tribe and was a famous fighter on Bara Magna, important for his strategies and skill.

He won the Great Tournament in the Arena Magna once, and won many other battles in many arenas. He also once wrote a book, called the Book of Certavus , that detailed his strategies and battle plans. The book was later lost in an old arena.

Certavus evenutally died of natural causes, but he was remembered by many for his skill and mind.

Abilities and Traits

Certavus was one of the greatest fighters of his time. He was smart and astute, knowing how to act in times of danger. Many other Agori and Glatorian respected him. Certavus was also very skillful and strong.

Powers and Tools

Certavus wielded a sword and carried a Thornax Launcher . However, he is known for being a master of all weapons, and during the Core War, he was also known to use a crossbow. He also briefly used a bow to wound Tarix .
