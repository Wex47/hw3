Crast
Mask of Repulsion
Power(s) Let user repulse target
Kanoka Unknown
Notable Wearer(s) Krika (formerly)
Pronunciation KRAST

The Kanohi Crast was the Mask of Repulsion . It gave its user the power to repel whatever they wanted for offense or defense. By repelling the ground, the user could achieve a limited type of flight. The user can only affect one target at a time, and can control how hard they repulse the target.

Example Usage

Known Wearers

Great

  • Makuta Krika - Deceased

Trivia

  • Most Toa would not usually choose to wear a Crast, as they considered its powers to be immoral, since the Crast repelled nature itself via its power.
  • The Crast was designed by BIONICLE part designer Erland Refling Nielsen.
  • The Crast worn by Krika was created out of protosteel . As a side-effect of being worn by a shapeshifter, whenever Krika shapeshifted, his Crast would change with him.
