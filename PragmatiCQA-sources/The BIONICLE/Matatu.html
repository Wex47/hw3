The Kanohi Matatu was the Mask of Telekinesis . It gave its user the power to move targets in their range of sight with their mind and to project force via thought (e.g. a telekinetic kick or punch). However, the user could not lift themselves. The only differences between the Great and Noble versions were the power duration and the mental strength given by the Kanohi.

Matatu were made from Teleport and Shrink Kanoka on Metru Nui .

Set Information

  • The Noble Kanohi Matatu was first released as a mask for Turaga Nuju in 2001 , and additionally appeared in the various Turaga mask colors in the Mask Packs. It also had the colors of the Toa Mata as misprints. It later appeared on the Kopeke set in 2003.
  • The Great Kanohi Matatu was first released in 2004 as the Kanohi for the Toa Metru Nuju set. It was later reused as the mask for the Matoran Kazi set in 2006 .
