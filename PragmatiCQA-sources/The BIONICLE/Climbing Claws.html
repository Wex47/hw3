Climbing Claws
Weapon Information
Power None
Function Channeling Elemental energy, melee combat, transportation
User(s) Toa Nuva Pohatu (formerly) Akamai Nuva (never formed)
Status Discarded

The Climbing Claws were Pohatu Nuva's Toa Tools . They could be thrown at enemies or combined to create the Kodan Ball and could also focus his control over Stone . He commonly used them to climb the canyons in Po-Wahi .

After receiving Adaptive Armor , Pohatu replaced these with his Twin Propellers .

Set Information

  • The climbing claws were released in the 2002 Pohatu Nuva set.
  • Putting the two climbing claws together using the black cylinder connector rods can create a Kodan Ball. Pohatu could kick this around with his gear action.
  • They have been included in the Takanuva in 2003, the Makuta Teridax set from 2003, the Turaga Dume and Nivawk set in 2004, the Roodaka set from 2005, and the Cendox V1 in 2009.
