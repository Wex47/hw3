Quake Breakers
Weapon Information
Power None
Function Channeling Elemental energy, melee combat, digging, transportation
User(s) Toa Nuva Onua (formerly)
Status Discarded

The Quake Breakers were twin chainsaw-like weapons that were Onua Nuva's Toa Tools . He used them to channel his Earth Elemental powers. They could also dig through earth and rock easily, and could even attach to his feet to serve as all-terrain treads. The Quake Breakers were later replaced with a Multi-Resistance Shield when Onua received his Adaptive Armor from Artakha .
