Mount Valmai was a mountain volcano located on Voya Nui . It was extremely active. The Ignika was buried deep beneath it, and Valmai held a massive complex devoted to this purpose. Leading to the mask was a staircase of 777 steps. Three teams have been known to travel there and survive: Toa Jovan's team , the Piraka , and the Toa Inika .

It is unknown if Valmai survived the trip back to the Northern Continent . It was abandoned when the Matoran evacuated to Spherus Magna .
