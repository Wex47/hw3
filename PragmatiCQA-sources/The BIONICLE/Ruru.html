The Kanohi Ruru was the Mask of Night Vision . The Great Ruru gave its user the power to see clearly in darkness and a limited amount of x-ray vision. It also allowed them to cast a beam of light for illumination, which could be used to blind opponents. The Noble Ruru only allowed its user to see clearly in darkness.

Ruru were carved out of a mixture of Teleport and Enlarge Kanoka on Metru Nui .

Set Information

  • The Noble Kanohi Ruru was first released in 2001 in dark grey as part of the Turaga Whenua small set. It was also released in five other colors (orange, light blue, light green, light grey, and khaki) as part of the collectible Mask Packs. Two more orange Ruru were part of the Manas set, and four transparent blue versions were exclusively in the Nui-Rama set. A black Ruru also came with Hafu in his 2001 and 2003 sets. In 2004 , the Ruru was re-released again, this time in dark red and silver as part of the Nuhrii small set.
  • The Great Kanohi Ruru was first released in 2004 in black as part of the Toa Metru Whenua canister set. In 2005 , the Great Ruru was released again in the Toa Hordika Whenua canister set. This time, in its Toa Hordika -mutated version. A black Ruru came with the 2006 Umbra set. An exclusive dark blue version was included in the 2007 Karzahni set, worn by Idris .
