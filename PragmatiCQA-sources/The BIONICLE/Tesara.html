Tesara , also known as " the Twin Village ," was the city of the Jungle Tribe on Bara Magna .

History

Tesara was an outpost during the Core War on Spherus Magna . After the Shattering it became the main village of the Jungle Tribe. However the Tesara Arena was built in the middle of the village, basically splitting the village in two, giving the city its nickname "the Twin Village".

Tarix of the Water Tribe recently traveled here in order to engage in a practice match with the Jungle Tribe's Secondary Glatorian , Gresh . However, Tarix beat Gresh and pointed out his weakness of relying on the same moves.

Mata Nui , Ackar, Kiina , Gresh and Berix later traveled here while Tarix and Vastus were fighting a match. Ackar interrupted the match and announced that the Skrall and Bone Hunters had formed an alliance, which shocked the crowd. The match was later stopped, and the Agori worked together to bring the two main structures of Tesara together, forming one of the pieces of the prototype robot .

After the Skrall and Bone Hunters were defeated, the main structures of the other villages were dragged to the main structure of Tesara to form the prototype robot. The part of Tesara that made up the robot later disintegrated with the rest of it after Mata Nui used it to fight Teridax and repair Spherus Magna.
