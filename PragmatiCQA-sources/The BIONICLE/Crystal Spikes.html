Crystal Spikes
Weapon Information
Power None
Function Channeling Elemental energy, melee combat, climbing
User(s) Toa Metru Nuju (formerly)
Status Transformed into Ice Pick

The Crystal Spikes were Toa Metru Nuju 's Toa Tools. They helped him to climb from building to building, and could control his power over Ice . When Nuju became a Toa Hordika , these were mutated into Hordika Teeth , but when Nuju was later cured, they changed back to Crystal Spikes. They were transformed into the Ice Pick after Nuju became a Turaga .

Set Information

In the Toa Metru Nuju set, the Crystal Spikes could be attached to his feet to double as climbing cleats or snow shoes.

Trivia

  • In BIONICLE Heroes , Crystal Spikes are the second weapons Matoro uses, upgraded from the Ice Sword . They could later be upgraded to the Energized Ice Sword .
