The Mountain Stronghold was the fortress for the Voya Nui Resistance Team . According to the Matoran , it was built in the time before Voya Nui split off from the Mainland . It appeared on VNOG, on Inika Island Assault where Avak appeared as boss, and on Matoran Escape , where Thok was the chapter boss.

It was destroyed when Voya Nui returned to the Southern Continent .
