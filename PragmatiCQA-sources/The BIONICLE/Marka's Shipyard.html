Marka's Shipyard was a place where Marka crafted boats . When Takua first visited it, Marka maintained the boat to Po-Koro . After the threat of the Bohrok-Kal past, Marka had a ship in drydock.

It was destroyed after the Bohrok swarms were reawakened by the Toa Nuva .
