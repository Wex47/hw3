The Rahkshi Kaita Vo was the Kaita fusion made from the three Rahkshi Panrahk , Guurahk , and Vorahk .

History

The Rahkshi Kaita Vo was seen only once, with Rahkshi Kaita Za . The two Kaitas fought Toa Nuva Lewa , Kopaka , and Tahu , but were ultimately defeated by Kopaka's quick thinking.

Powers and Equipment

The Kaita Vo possessed the abilities of its component Rahkshi: energy-draining, disintegration beams, and fragmentation blasts. It also possessed the power to absorb its enemies' attacks and release them again in a blast of pure power.
