History

After the Shattering , Tajun became the main village for the Water Tribe. An arena was constructed in the village to host Glatorian matches. Tajun was located near Skrall River, a place controlled by Skrall forces for drinking.

By the time Mata Nui arrived on Bara Magna, Tajun had been suffering from daily Bone Hunter raids and very little supplies were getting to the village. Eventually, the Bone Hunters and Skrall invaded and completely demolished the Village of Water. Fortunately, the Agori escaped. The main structure of Tajun was later dragged away by the Agori to form the prototype robot .

A hidden laboratory built by the Great Beings was also located somewhere in Tajun.
