Golden Kanohi were incredibly powerful and unique Kanohi Masks which allowed the wearer access to six powers at once, though only one could be actively used at a time.

History

The Golden Kanohi appeared only once, when the Toa Mata collected their Great Masks, brought them to the Kini-Nui , and placed them onto life-sized carvings of themselves. They wore these masks when they fought and defeated Makuta Teridax and the Bohrok swarms, but when they became Toa Nuva the Golden Kanohi ceased to exist. Takua also once discovered a hologram of a Golden Hau in the Great Mine of Onu-Wahi .

The Golden Kanohi were crafted by Artakha .

Example Usage

In Comic 4: The Bohrok Awake , all the Toa Mata used their Golden Kanohi's power of Speed, to travel from the Kini-Nui to Ta-Koro in moments.
