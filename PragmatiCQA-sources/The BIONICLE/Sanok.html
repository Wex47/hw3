Sanok
Toa Inika Hewkii's organic Great Sanok
Mask of Accuracy
Power(s) Let user always hits their target with any object
Kanoka Unknown
Notable Wearer(s) Hewkii (Formerly), Tobduk
Pronunciation sah-NOCK

The Kanohi Sanok was the Mask of Accuracy . It gave its user the power to use almost any inanimate object as a projectile weapon and hit almost any target that he or she aimed for. A Sanok was always active at a low level.
