The Sculpture Fields were located in Po-Metru .

It is where Po-Matoran carved sculptures that were too big for any building.

Ahkmou was supposedly "trapped" by Nidhiki on the top of a statue in these fields, but Toa Onewa knew that Ahkmou climbed up and down these for fun.
