Nivawk was a large, hawk-like Rahi of an unknown species. However, it was not a dangerous predator, as instead of hunting, it would scavenge for food.

When Teridax came to Metru Nui to pose as Turaga Dume , he used Nivawk to spy on his enemies. During the Great Cataclysm , Makuta absorbed Nivawk, along with Nidhiki and Krekka into his essence, so he could shapeshift into a more powerful form.

It was later revealed that there are more than one Nivawk, as shown early in BIONICLE 3: Web of Shadows , though it is unclear if Nivawk is the species name, or just the name attributed to the one that served Teridax.
