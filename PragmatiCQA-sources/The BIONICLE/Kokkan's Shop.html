Kokkan 's Shop was the shop he owned as a Trader on Mata Nui . Hahli visited it during her travels to practice her Kolhii skills. Items that could be purchased include Rope , Tackles, Bamboo Poles, Air Bladders , and Sailcloths.

The building was abandoned when the Ko-Matoran , along with all the other islanders, returned to their former home of Metru Nui .

The shop was later destroyed after the Bohrok swarms were re-released.
