Phantom was a Nynrah Ghost who later became a member of the Dark Hunters .

History

When the Matoran on Nynrah felt that there was a need for a hero since no Toa were present, Phantom was changed into a being with the powers of increased strength, limited flight, dramatically improved eyesight and the ability to become invisible. Sadly though, the Matoran were frightened and ashamed of what they had done and shunned him.

Later on, he was recruited by the Dark Hunters.

When the Great Spirit Robot was severely damaged, Phantom emigrated from the Matoran Universe to Spherus Magna .

Abilities and Traits

Phantom has incredible strength, limited flight, improved eyesight and the ability to become invisible at will. Phantom does not behave like most members of the organization as he still has good values and morals, making him only partially useful to "The Shadowed One".
