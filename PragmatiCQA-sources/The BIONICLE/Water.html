Water
Element Information
Matoran Prefix Ga -
Associated Color(s) Primary: Blue Secondary: Various shades of blue
Associated Location(s) Ga-Metru , Ga-Wahi , Ga-Koro

Water was a tranquil, yet potentially devastating Elemental Power involving the essence of purity in the Matoran Universe and Spherus Magna . This was one of the most common elements , and was used frequently by Toa , Turaga , and Matoran .

Toa of Water had near-perfect control of the element, while a Ga-Matoran simply had the ability to stay underwater much longer than others. Turaga of Water had powers somewhere in the middle.

All users of this element were passively able to stay underwater for much longer than other beings.

Combinations

With all wielding different elements, six Toa could combine their powers to create a Protodermis Cage , capable of sealing away powerful beings, such as the likes of Makuta Teridax , or the Bahrag Queens. Freeing trapped entities required a shard of the prison to be charged with the same elemental powers used to construct it.

Beings other than Toa could not combine their powers at all, making them the only ones able to do so.

  • When combined with the Elemental Power of Air , it can be used to create a storm or cyclone.
  • When combined with the Elemental Power of Fire , it can be used to create steam.
  • When combined with the Elemental Power of Earth , it could be used to create mud.
