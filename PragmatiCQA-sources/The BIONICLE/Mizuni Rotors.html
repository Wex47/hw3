Mizuni Rotors
Weapon Information
Power None
Function Underwater propulsion
User(s) Toa Nuva Gali (formerly) Wairuha Nuva (formerly)
Status Discarded

The Mizuni Rotors were a pair of small propellers attached to Toa Nuva Gali 's wrists. These rotors came into existence after Gali and her team were exposed to Energized Protodermis and transformed by its powers. They primarily served to aid Gali in swimming and diving. When Gali was given a set of Adaptive Armor by Artakha , she discarded these.
