The Golden Swords were two weapons wielded by Tahu Master of Fire .

Functionality

The Golden Swords were used as sidearms for Tahu when he was lava boarding, or if he had lost his Fire Blades .

Usage

Tahu used the Golden Swords as sidearms to battle the Lord of Skull Spiders when the latter had knocked away the former's Fire Blades.

Set Information

The Golden Swords came with Tahu , and each was 1 piece.
