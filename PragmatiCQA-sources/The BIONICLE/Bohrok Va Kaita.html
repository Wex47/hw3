Bohrok Va Kaita were a hypothetical merging of three different breeds of Bohrok Va .

Set Information

Two Bohrok Va Kaita have been released:

  • One was a merge between the Lehvak Va , Gahlok Va , and Kohrak Va .
  • The second was a merge between Tahnok Va , Nuhvok Va , and Pahrak Va . The second one also had two krana.
