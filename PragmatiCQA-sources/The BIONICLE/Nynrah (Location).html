Nynrah was an island in the Matoran Universe . The island was populated by some Fe-Matoran , most notably the Nynrah Ghosts . There was at least one village, which was located on a high cliff. Nynrah was located south of Odina and north of Visorak . After Makuta Teridax 's death, the inhabitants of Nynrah evacuated the island to Spherus Magna .

Trivia

  • Nynrah was the only place known to have a native population of Fe-Matoran .
