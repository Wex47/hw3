Time was a Legendary power within the Matoran Universe . It was too powerful for any being to fully harness.

Examples

  • Slowing down time in a certain area or around a target.
  • Speeding up time in a certain area or around a target.
  • Sensing disturbances in the fabric of time.
  • Knocking back a target's perception of time, making it extremely difficult for them to fight.

Combinations

As Time is a legendary power, it cannot be combined with any element, nor can it be used in the formation of a Protodermis Cage.
