Vakama 's Hut was a home built and used by Turaga Vakama in Ta-Koro . The Wall of History was created and located here. The hut was located, on the southern edge of Ta-Koro. A lit fire (which is the sacred fire) dominated the room, and there were plenty of gemstones of various colors, candles, Mata Nui Stone and carvings. There was a weathered carving of a Kanohi Hau at the back of the room behind the fire.
