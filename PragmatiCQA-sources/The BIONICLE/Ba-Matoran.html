Ba-Matoran were Matoran of Gravity . These Matoran had small amounts of Elemental Gravity Energy in them. It is known that at least some of them lived on the Southern Continent . Ba-Matoran often wore purple and black armor and Kanohi . Their trait is that they had a resistance to Gravitational pressure.

Known Ba-Matoran

  • Several Ba-Matoran who previously inhabited a village on the Southern Continent - Status unknown
  • A Ba-Matoran that became a Toa of Gravity and was part of the first Toa Team - Status deceased
  • A Ba-Matoran that became a Toa of Gravity and was killed by Lariska - Status deceased
  • A Ba-Matoran that became a Toa of Gravity and was the leader of Varian's Team - Status unknown

Trivia

  • The prefix "Ba-" comes from the Greek word "baros", meaning "weight".
  • In Reign of Shadows , Mazeka and a parallel version of Teridax travelled to an abandoned Ba-Matoran village.
