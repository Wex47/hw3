The Zamor Launchers were used to fire Zamor Spheres , and were the sidearms of the Toa Inika and the Piraka .

History

The prototypes for the launchers were created by the Nynrah Ghosts , but were stolen by Nidhiki and Krekka on orders of "The Shadowed One" . Nidhiki wanted to hide them and come back for them later, but Krekka forced him to bring them back.

When the Piraka arrived on Voya Nui , Avak used pieces of the Toa Canisters they came in to create several Zamor Launchers. The Piraka loaded them with Zamor Spheres filled with Antidermis , which they use to enslave the Matoran of Voya Nui. One of the launchers was stolen by Garan and Kazi when Avak was distracted by Velika , and after studying it, Balta created more for the Matoran and Toa .

The mutated Rahi Protodax also had Zamor Launchers, and they created their own Zamor Spheres, which contain a liquid that shrinks anything it touches to the size of a Protodite .

Set Information

Zamor launchers were released inside the Piraka and Toa Inika sets. They shot the spheres by pushing them out of the weapon. The Toa Inika's launchers had barrel-like magazines on top that could hold four spheres for rapid fire functions as opposed the the Piraka's single shot.
