Plantlife
Element Information
Matoran Prefix Bo -
Associated Color(s) Green, blue
Associated Location(s) Tesara , Bota Magna

Plantlife , also known as Jungle , was an Elemental Power involving the vegetation in the Matoran Universe and Spherus Magna . It was one of the rarer elements , and was used infrequently by Toa , Turaga and Matoran .

Toa of Plantlife had near-perfect command over the element, while Bo-Matoran possessed a passive ability. Turaga of Plantlife had powers somewhere in the middle.

Users of this element had a better understanding of plants, and plant-based poisons.
