Disk Launcher (Vakama)
Weapon Information
Power None
Function Shooting Kanoka , channelling Elemental energy, flight
User(s) Toa Metru Vakama
Status Destroyed

The Disk Launcher was Toa Metru Vakama 's Toa Tool . It launched Kanoka disks, and doubled as a jetpack. It also enhanced Vakama's control over Fire .

Vakama temporarily lost this tool when he was mutated into a Toa Hordika , the Disk Launcher itself mutated into Blazer Claws . It was eventually restored by Keetongu 's mutation-curing abilities.

It was destroyed during a confrontation with "The Shadowed One" when Vakama returned to Metru Nui for the Kanohi Vahi .

Set Information

The Disk Launcher worked like all the other Kanoka launchers included in many sets, except that it had a different shape. Squeezing the two sides propelled the disk from the launcher. The launcher came with Vakama's Toa Disk , which had an image of his great Huna printed on it.

Trivia

  • In BIONICLE Heroes , the Disk Launcher was the second weapon Jaller used. It was later upgraded to the Energized Flame Sword .
