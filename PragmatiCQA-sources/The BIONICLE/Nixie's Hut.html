On Mata Nui , Nixie 's Hut was built from Seaweed . She her astrologer equipment inside and a separate observatory level was added sometime between Makuta's defeat and the attack of the Rahkshi . It was abandoned when Nixie moved back to Metru Nui . It was later destroyed by the newly awakened Bohrok swarms.
