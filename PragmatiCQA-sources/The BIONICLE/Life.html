Life was one of the three Legendary Powers, along with Time and Creation . The only object able to control life was the Kanohi Ignika , the Legendary Mask of Life. This means that no Matoran or Turaga of Life exist. In fact, no true Toa of life exist, as Toa Ignika was merely a body created by the Kanohi Ignika, and Mata Nui 's body was created by the Ignika. This element is too powerful for any Toa to use and even requires the death of the user in order to be used.

Examples

  • Creating life forces.

  • Absorbing life forces.

  • Creating a Life Nova Blast . (Toa Ignika exclusive)

Combinations

As Life is a legendary power, it cannot be combined with any element or other legendary power, nor can it be used in the formation of Protodermis.

Users

  • The Ignika and any being using it:
    • Matoro
    • A Toa in Jovan's Team
    • Mata Nui

Trivia

  • Although Mata Nui called the body that the Ignika created for him "his 'Toa' form", he is not considered a Toa of Life.
