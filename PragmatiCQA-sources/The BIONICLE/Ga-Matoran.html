Ga-Matoran were Matoran with innate Water powers.

Ga-Matoran came in all shades of blue and sometimes silver, and generally had yellow or orange eyes. These water Matoran lived on the shores of the sea, so they had a sleeker build, better lung capacity, and other physical advantages for swimming. All Ga-Matoran were female, whilst most of the other types were composed entirely of males. Most Ga-Matoran tended to be peaceful and friendly, though there were exceptions, such as Dalu . Ga-Matoran culture put the most value on the virtue of Unity and its principle of Purity . In Metru Nui , about half of the Ga- Matoran were teachers and most of the other half were students; the best were promoted to work on protodermis purification. On Mata Nui , they were mostly boaters and fisherwomen. A general interest in sea travel was common among them, with Mini-Boat Racing being a popular pastime in Ga-Metru.

The Ga-Matoran tended to be very dedicated to honoring the Great Spirit and focused on Unity above all else. The only Matoran who generally visited Ga-Metru were Ko-Matoran .

After Teridax 's death, the Ga-Matoran all migrated to Spherus Magna .
