Protodites were violent, microscopic Rahi . Protodites were known to have the ability to fly. Some Protodites escaped from the Archives , and became the favorite prey of Archives Moles . The body of Zaktan was composed of billions of Protodites, each of which held a part of his consciousness.

Protodax

A group of protodites were transformed into Protodax by the Mask of Life to serve as guardians. They were placed in a room with Umbra . When the Toa Inika arrived in their chamber, Umbra "destroyed" the Protodax using his Laser Lance .
