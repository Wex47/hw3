Laser Crossbow
Weapon Information
Power Shooting lasers
Function Channeling Elemental energy, ranged combat
User(s) Toa Inika Kongu (formerly)
Status Transformed into powerless Toa Tool and discarded

The Laser Crossbow was Kongu 's Toa Tool when he was a Toa Inika . It could shoot energy bolts that shattered its target to atoms and could also be used to control Kongu's power over Air . It transformed into a melee weapon when Kongu transformed into a Toa Mahri , but he later replaced it with a second Cordak Blaster .

Set Information

  • The Laser Crossbow was included with the Kongu Inika set 8731 in 2006. Activating a button on the side of the Crossbow made it light up and flash a blue light in the tube in the center. It had two handles Kongu could hold.
