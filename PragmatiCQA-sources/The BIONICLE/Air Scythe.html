A Toa Tool was the weapon of choice which a Toa used. The Toa used these tools to channel to their Elemental Powers , rather than for killing, although some still channeled it through their hands. The tools were mostly swords, but they could also be axes, spears, clubs, drills, staffs, claws, shields or projectile weapons like different launchers, blasters, crossbows or harpoons. The Suva in the Great Temple of Metru Nui had a collection of Toa Tools. The Toa Metru took their tools from that collection. When the Toa Mata appeared on Mata Nui , their Toa Tools came with them.

Other Toa Tools

Below is a list of various Toa Tools which either were not seen in-story, or not enough information is known about them to constitute a separate page.

Toa Hagah

  • Sub-Zero Spear - One of Toa Hagah Kualus 's Toa Tools, used to channel his elemental Ice powers and for combat. Lost when he became a Rahaga --since restored.
  • Seismic Spear - One of Toa Hagah Bomonga 's Toa Tools, used to channel his elemental Earth powers and for combat. Lost when he became a Rahaga--since restored.
  • Tidal Spear - One of Toa Hagah Gaaki 's Toa Tools, used to channel her elemental Water powers and for combat. Lost upon when she became a Rahaga--since restored.
  • Avalanche Spear - One of Toa Hagah Pouks 's Toa Tools, used to channel his elemental Stone powers and for combat. Lost when he became a Rahaga--since restored.

Toa Mangai

  • Air Scythe - Toa Mangai Nidhiki 's former Toa Tool, used to channel his elemental Air powers and for melee combat. Lost when he was mutated by Roodaka .
  • Barbed Broadsword - Toa Mangai Tuyet 's Toa Tool, used to channel her elemental Water powers and for melee combat.

Dark Hunters

  • Tri-Claw - The Dark Hunter Savage 's Toa Tool, which can liquefy solid matter.
  • An axe able to absorb and hurl back elemental energy. Stolen from a dead Toa by the Dark Hunter Charger .

Others

  • A spiked mace and a shield wielded by Toa Helryx , both of which are crafted from Protosteel . Used to channel her elemental Water powers and for melee combat.
  • A trident used by Toa Cordak Nikila until her death. Used to channel her elemental Lightning powers and for melee combat.
  • Combat Staffs - A set of six staffs used by the Toa Hordika during the Visorak invasion of Metru Nui . Used to channel their various elemental powers and for melee combat; later abandoned.
  • Laser Sight - The form that Toa Nuva Gali 's adaptive weapon assumed upon entering Karda Nui 's Swamp of Secrets . It functioned as a scope attached to her Nynrah Ghostblaster . The Laser Sight was made out of Protosteel by Artakha , and given to Gali when she and the rest of the Toa Nuva visited the island named after him.
  • A powerless melee weapon abandoned by Toa Mahri Kongu and replaced with a second Cordak Blaster . Used to channel his elemental Air powers and for melee combat.
  • A flail used by a Toa at a Toa Fortress until his death.
  • A mace used by another Toa at the Toa Fortress until his death.
  • A pair of blades Toa Varian used to wield on her elbows. Used to channel her elemental Psionics powers and for melee combat.
