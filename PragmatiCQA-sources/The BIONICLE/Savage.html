"Savage" was a Toa of Earth who was mutated into a Toa Hordika and became a Dark Hunter .

History

"Savage" was once a Toa of Earth who was detained by the Visorak . After being mutated into a Toa Hordika by Hordika Venom , he escaped to his teammates. However, they didn't recognize him and attacked him, believing him to be a monster. As a result his anger and despair grew out of control. This accelerated his feral side to take control, losing his speech and self-control until he heavily injured his team.

He was later found by the Dark Hunters, and The Shadowed One convinced him his allies had forsaken him and to join the Dark Hunters. Since he occasionally became mad from his insanity as a Toa Hordika, he was locked in a cell and damage he committed was taken from his profits.

After the Battle of Bara Magna , in which the Great Spirit Robot was heavily damaged, "Savage" migrated to the newly-reformed planet of Spherus Magna.

Abilities and Traits

"Savage" felt constant anger and insanity after his transformation to the point where he had to be jailed for everyone's protection at times.

"Savage" wielded a Tri-Claw that could liquidize solid matter, and his Rhotuka could paralyze victims.
