Bone Hunters are the name given to a nomadic tribe on Bara Magna . They resemble the Rock Tribe Agori from Roxtus and rely greatly on their Rock Steeds . They used to be sworn enemies with the Skrall until Metus brought them together to destroy the other tribes. This plan was foiled by Mata Nui , Kiina , Ackar and Gresh as they led a force of other Glatorian and Agori.
