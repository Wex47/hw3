Nathalie Joanne Emmanuel (born 2 March 1989) is an English actress, best known for her former regular role on the soap opera Hollyoaks .

Via Twitter, Emmanuel revealed she would be working on the third season of Game of Thrones . She tweeted a picture of a the front page of the script for the sixth episode of the season. At the San Diego Comic-Con on 13 July 2012, it was confirmed that she would be playing the role of Missandei .

Nathalie Emmanuel joined the TV series as a recurring cast member in Season 3, and continued as a recurring cast member into Season 4. Emmanuel was later promoted to a starring cast member for seasons 5 and 6.

Emmanuel first caught Benioff and Weiss's eyes when they saw her work in Misfits .

Nathalie has starred in Furious 7 , the seventh installment in the The Fast and the Furious franchise as well in the recent installment The Fate of the Furious where she reprises her role as the computer hacker, Ramsey.
