Iain Alan Sutherland Glen (born 24 June 1961) is a Scottish actor. He stars in HBO 's Game of Thrones as Ser Jorah Mormont . He was announced in the role on 20 August 2009. He was part of the initial starring cast and remains a member of the starring cast for the second season .

He is arguably best known for his role as the villain in the film Lara Croft: Tomb Raider and a multi-award-winning performance as Larry Winters in Silent Scream , as well as portraying Dr Alexandra Issacs as the overarching antagonist in the Resident Evil film series . He also had a cameo as King Richard the Lionheart in Kingdom of Heaven . He is also known for his role as Prince Hamlet in the 1990 film adaptation of Tom Stoppard's play Rosencrantz and Guildenstern are Dead .

He had a supporting role as Octavian in "Flesh and Stone" and "The Time of Angels" of the 2010 season of Doctor Who . In 2011, he was cast in a recurring role in Downton Abbey as Sir Richard Carlisle, a newspaper mogul and suitor to Lady Mary Crawley. In 2012 he began a starring role in the BBC drama Prisoner's Wives as Paul, a convicted drug trafficker. He also stars in the title role in the TV3 miniseries .

He appeared in the first season of the BBC drama Ripper Street , playing the former commanding officer of Jerome Flynn 's character, Sgt. Bennet Drake.
