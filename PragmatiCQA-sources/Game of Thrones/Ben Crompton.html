Benjamin Lorton Crompton (born 1974) is a British actor. He has starred in the BBC sketch comedy Man Stroke Woman , the sitcom Ideal and the films All or Nothing and 102 Dalmatians . His debut role was a guest appearance in The Thin Blue Line , a series which also featured fellow Game of Thrones actors Mark Addy , Owen Teale and Rupert Vansittart , as well as Kevin Allen, the uncle of Alfie Allen .

In Game of Thrones , he portrays the role of Eddison Tollett , a member of the Night's Watch . Crompton was confirmed in the role on August 9, 2011. He joined the cast as a guest star in the second season .
