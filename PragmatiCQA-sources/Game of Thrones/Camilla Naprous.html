Camilla Naprous is a horse master and stuntwoman. She is the daughter of Gerard Naprous and the sister of Daniel Naprous .

In Game of Thrones , she has been a horse master in every episode of the series to date except for the third season episode " The Bear and the Maiden Fair ", the fourth season episode " The Mountain and the Viper " and the eighth season episodes " The Bells " and " The Iron Throne ".
