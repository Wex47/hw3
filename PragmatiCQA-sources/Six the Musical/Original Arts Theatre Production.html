The Original Arts Theatre Production , also known as the Off-West End Production , is the first professional and second overall production of Six the Musical . It opened on the 18 December 2017, and closed on the 22 January 2018.

History

In the success of the Student Run Production, Six the Musical caught the attention of Kenny Wax and Global Musicals, who offered to produce the next iteration of the show. On 24 November 2017, the show dates for the production were announced.

On 30 November 2017, the cast was announced, with Renée Lamb as Catherine of Aragon , Christina Modestou as Anne Boleyn , Natalie Paris as Jane Seymour , Genesis Lynea as Anna of Cleves , Aimie Atkinson as Katherine Howard , and Izuka Hoyle as Catherine Parr . During the period between the cast announcement and the opening of the show, a number of promotional images and teasers were released, including a clip of the Six the Musical cast recording.

On 18 December 2017, the musical made its professional debut at the Arts Theatre, performing subsequently for three more days. Towards the end of the production's run, a new song was added after the finale: The Megasix , a medley of the show's various songs. Additionally, due to popular demand, the production's run of four shows were extended to six, with an extra show added to the last two show days. On 22 January 2018, the Original Arts Theatre Production had its closing performance, with all but two cast members leaving the show.

Not long after the production ended, the show was picked up for a UK Tour , with the returns of Natalie Paris and Aimie Atkinson.

Trivia

  • During the period between the cast announcement and opening of the show, the official Six Instagram account posted magazine cover mock-ups of its cast portraying the queens of the show, with each being a parody of an existing publication.
    • Catherine of Aragon is featured on Catholicism Weekly , which might have been based on The Catholic Weekly .
    • Anne Boleyn is featured on Bonjour! , which shares its name with The Bonjour Magazine but is more likely a parody of the royalist publication Hello! .
    • Jane Seymour is featured on Cosmo Queen , a parody of Cosmopolitan .
    • Anna of Cleves is featured on Good Palacekeeping , a parody of Good Housekeeping .
    • Katherine Howard is featured on Dazed & Beheaded , a parody of Dazed & Confused .
    • Catherine Parr is featured on Vague , a parody of Vogue .
  • Many aspects of this production differ from the current state of the show.
    • Many of the songs in this production are performed quite differently to their current iterations; with different instrumentals, lyrics, and choreography.
    • Every
    • The Ladies in Waiting did not exist within the show yet, with a standard band playing the instruments throughout the show instead.
    • The costumes that the queens wore in the show were not explicitly colour-coded.
    • The cast has no swings, alternates, or understudies.
  • The cast of the Original Arts Theatre is also known as the Album Cast, due to being the cast involved in the the Six studio album.
  • On 28 October 2021, an exclusive TikTok event celebrating the launch of the Six studio album vinyl is streamed, seeing the reunion of the Original Arts Theatre production cast (sans Genesis Lynea, who at the time was busy filming a movie).
