Tom Curran is a British composer, arranger, copyist, and orchestrator. He composed the orchestrations for numerous productions of Six the Musical .

Trivia

  • In 2008, Tom was one of the winners of the BBC Proms Young Composers Competition and was commissioned to write a piece for the 2009 Proms season.
  • Tom was featured in the 38th episode of The Queendom Podcast - A SIX: The Musical Podcast .
