" All You Wanna Do " is the seventh song in Six the Musical . It is Katherine Howard 's solo.

Theme

The song is a depiction of Katherine's life beginning at the age of 13, leading up to her time as queen and her beheading. It tells of how she was taken advantage of and sexually abused by several men at a young age when all she ever wanted was a friend.

Trivia

  • The four verses in order are about Henry Mannox , Francis Dereham , Henry VIII , and Thomas Culpeper respectively.
  • In live performances, there is dialogue leading into each verse, temporarily pausing the song each time.
    • While some of these bits are present in the cast recording, they are shortened and therefore do not contain the full lines.
  • In the 2017 Arts Theatre production, the other actresses wore masks while the song was being sung.
  • While Katherine says during the song that Mannox was 23 when he became involved with her, his actual age is unknown, with estimates ranging from 21 to 36. Likewise, as Katherine's exact date of birth is unknown, it is possible that she was younger than 13 when the "relationship" began.
  • In the second chorus of the song, Katherine mentions wearing a corset; this is anachronistic as the corset did not become a common article of clothing until roughly 50 years after her death.
  • Musically, the song resembles the Britney Spears songs Toxic , Womanizer , and If U Seek Amy .
