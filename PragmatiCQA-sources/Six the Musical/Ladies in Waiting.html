The Ladies in Waiting are the backing band in Six the Musical . They are named after the queens' actual ladies in waiting.

Members

  • Maggie — Guitar
  • Bessie — Bass
  • Joan — Keyboard
  • Maria — Drums

Real-Life Counterparts

  • Maggie - Lady Margaret Lee (served Anne Boleyn )
  • Bessie - Elizabeth "Bessie" Blount (served Anna of Cleves and Catherine of Aragon )
  • Joan - Jane/Joan Meutas (served Jane Seymour )
  • Maria - María de Salinas (served Catherine of Aragon)
