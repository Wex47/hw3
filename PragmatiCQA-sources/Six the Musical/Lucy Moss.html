Lucy Moss is an English writer and director. She composed and co-wrote Six the Musical with Toby Marlow . She is also the co-director of most of the productions of the show.

Trivia

  • At age 26, Lucy became the youngest female-identifying Broadway director in history, breaking the 42-year long record of Elizabeth Swados, who directed Runaways at age 27 in 1978.
  • Lucy's favourite song in Six is Ex-Wives .
  • Lucy's favourite joke in Six is "I look more rad than Lutheranism" from Get Down .
  • Lucy's favourite line from Six is "She wants another turn", which is said by Catherine Parr .
    • Lucy's favourite cut line is "Wow, what a stressful situation that never happened", which was said by Anne Boleyn .
  • If Lucy could be any queen, she would be Anna of Cleves .
  • Lucy also co-wrote the musical Hot Gay Time Machine with Toby Marlow and Zak Ghazi-Torbati. She also directed the show.
  • Lucy directed the Ratatouille benefit concert, which also featured the Broadway cast of Six .
  • Lucy's favourite musical is & Juliet .
  • If Lucy could direct any other musical, she would want to direct Sweeney Todd .
