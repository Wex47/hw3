Edward VI was King of England and Ireland from 28 January 1547 until his death on 6 July 1553. He was the son of King Henry VIII and his third wife, Jane Seymour . He is mentioned several times in Jane's song Heart of Stone , as well as multiple other times throughout Six the Musical .

Trivia

  • When Edward found out that his father had died, he was with his sister, Elizabeth , and the two cried together.
  • Edward was the first English monarch to be raised as a protestant.
  • While writing the show, Toby Marlow and Lucy Moss considered adding Edward into the show as a backing vocalist alongside the other children; however, they instead decided that the cast would be comprised of the queens alone.
