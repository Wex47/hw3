Alice Angliss is an Irish percussionist, drummer, and musician. She is currently in the band of the West End production of Six the Musical as Maria , the drummer, as well as the standby music director/ Joan, having begun the roles on 17 January 2019 with the original West End cast. She was also the drummer in the 2017 Arts Theatre and 2018 UK Tour productions.

Trivia

  • Alice first covered Joan in June 2019.
  • Alice is a member of the London-based percussion trio, Beaten Track Ensemble.
  • In 2016, Alice spent six weeks in Sicily as principal Timpani for the Mediterranean Opera Festival in 2016.
  • Alice has taught whole-class music in primary schools for the Da Capo Music Foundation and piano and drums at their Saturday school and Wren Academy.
  • Alice has taught percussion at Tiffin Girls' School and teaches percussion, drums, and piano privately from her home studio.
