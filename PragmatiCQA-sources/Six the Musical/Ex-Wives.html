Trivia

  • The instrumental breaks in the song are a techno version of Greensleeves , an English folk song which was rumoured to have been written by Henry VIII for Anne Boleyn .
    • Anne also makes a direct reference to this song in her verse when she sings, "My sleeves may be green/But my lipstick's red".
  • The line, "I'm the survivor, Catherine Parr" is a direct reference to Destiny's Child 's song Survivor .
