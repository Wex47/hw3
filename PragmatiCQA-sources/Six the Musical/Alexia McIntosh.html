Alexia Elizabeth "Lexi" McIntosh is a Jamaican-British actress, singer, and dancer. She played Anna of Cleves in the West End Production of Six the Musical . She also played the same role in the show's 2018 UK Tour , beginning on 11 July 2018. Alexia left the show on 14 November 2021 and her role was taken over by Dionne Ward-Anderson .

Trivia

  • Alexia is fluent in Jamaican Patois.
  • Alexia temporarily withdrew from Six due to a factoring knee injury. She returned to the production after approximately a six-week break from the show on 21 January 2020.
    • She withdrew from Six again in May 2021, but she returned to the production on 14 July 2021. During her absence, Cherelle Jay acted as her temporary replacement.
  • Alexia was in the same production of Sister Act as Sophie-Rose Middleton .
  • Shekinah McFarlane filled in for Alexia at the Six Flash-mob at the Tower of London on 31 August 2019.
  • Alexia was featured on the thirty-first episode of the Films I love most: The sequel podcast.
  • Alexia is the only cast member to have worn the Cleves 3.0 costume.
    • She was also the first cast member to wear the Cleves 4.0 costume.
