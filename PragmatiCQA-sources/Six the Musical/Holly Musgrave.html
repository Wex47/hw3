Holly Musgrave is a British actress, singer, songwriter, and voiceover artist. She played Jane Seymour in Original Edinburgh and Cambridge Production of Six the Musical , beginning on 31 July 2017. She left the show on 14 October 2017.

Trivia

  • Holly began writing original music when she was ten years old.
  • Holly's debut album, Jouster , was released on 15 June 2018.
  • Holly was the first white actress to portray Jane Seymour.
  • Holly was the youngest principal actress to play Jane Seymour, being 21 years old at the time of her debut.
  • Holly is the only cast member to have worn the Seymour 1.0 costume.
