Shimali de Silva is a Sri Lankan and Hong Kongese-Australian actress, singer, and director. She played Catherine Parr at the Edinburgh Fringe 2017 and Cambridge performances of Six the Musical , beginning on 31 July 2017. She left the show on 14 October 2017.

Trivia

  • Shimali was the first actress of colour to perform in Six.
    • She was also the first principal actress of colour, as well as the first Asian principal actress, to portray Catherine Parr.
  • Shimali was the youngest actress to play Catherine Parr, being 18 years old at the time of her debut.
  • Shimali is the only cast member to have worn the Parr 1.0 costume.
