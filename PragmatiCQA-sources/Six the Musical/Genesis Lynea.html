Spotlight

Genesis "Gen" Lynea is a Bermudian-born British actress, singer, songwriter, dancer, model, and choreographer. She played Anna of Cleves in the 2017 Arts Theatre production of Six the Musical , beginning on 18 December 2017. She also played the same role on the cast recording. She left the show on 22 January 2018.

Personal Life

Genesis openly identifies as queer and is dating fellow actress Aimie Atkinson , who played Katherine Howard in the 2017 Arts Theatre production.

Trivia

  • In 2010, Genesis appeared in the music video for Do it Like a Dude by Jessie J, who is her childhood friend.
  • Genesis attended drama school with Gabriella Stylianou-Burns .
  • Genesis was in the same production of The Bodyguard as Danielle Steers .
  • Genesis was in the same production of In the Heights as Aimie Atkinson.
  • Genesis has returned to the show as an emergency cover for Anna of Cleves twice; first on 21 April 2019, then on 28 July 2019.
    • The second time she was called back was the same day as show writer Toby Marlow 's emergency performance.
  • Genesis was one of three former Six cast members from the 2017 Arts Theatre production to perform at Aimie Atkinson's solo concert at The Other Palace, the other two being Renée Lamb and Christina Modestou .
  • Genesis was the only principal actress to not have a solo line in the Worldwide Six video.
  • Genesis was the first actress of colour to portray Anna of Cleves.
    • She was also the first Black principal actress to portray Anna, as well as being one of the first Black actresses to perform in Six, along with Renée Lamb and Izuka Hoyle .
  • Genesis played Dr. Archie Hudson in the BBC medical drama, Casualty , having joined in the show's thirty-third series in 2019. Her final episode in the role was in the twenty-seventh episode of the thirty-fourth series in 2020.
  • Genesis played a minor role in the first episode of the Netflix original series Shadow and Bone .
  • Genesis is the only cast member to have worn the Cleves 2.0 costume.
