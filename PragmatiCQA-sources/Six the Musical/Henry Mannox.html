Henry Mannox

Historical Information

Birth Name

Henry Mannox

Dates

1500~1515 – 15??

Henry Mannox was Katherine Howard 's childhood music teacher and one of her abusers. He is the subject of the first verse of All You Wanna Do .

Trivia

  • While Katherine says during All You Wanna Do that Mannox was 23 when he became involved with her, his actual age at the time is unknown, with estimates ranging from 21 to 36. Likewise, as Katherine's exact date of birth is unknown, it is possible that she was younger than 13 when the "relationship" began.
  • With the exception of Henry VIII , he was the only one of Katherine's abusers to escape execution, as his involvement with her ended before her marriage.
