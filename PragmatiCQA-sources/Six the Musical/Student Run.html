The Original Edinburgh and Cambridge Production , also known as the Student Run , is the first production of Six the Musical . It opened on the 31 July 2017, and closed on 14 October that same year.

History

In late 2016, Toby Marlow was selected by the Cambridge University Arts Society to write a musical to be performed at the Edinburgh Festival Fringe the next summer. The concept for Six occured to Toby during poetry class, and eventually decided to get his friend Lucy Moss involved in the writing process. Marlow researched by reading Antonia Fraser's The Six Wives of Henry VIII , while Moss viewed a documentary series, Six Wives , by Lucy Worsley. At their first writing session together, they watched a 2011 Beyoncé concert and story-telling performance, Live at Roseland: Elements of 4 . Over the course of approximately 10 nonconsecutive days, the pair established the foundation for the show.

From the 3 to 15 May, the cast of the first-ever production of Six the Musical was gradually announced, with Megan Gilbert as Catherine of Aragon , Ashleigh Weir as Anne Boleyn , Holly Musgrave as Jane Seymour , Oliver Wickham as Anna of Cleves , Annabel Marlow as Katherine Howard and Shimali de Silva as Catherine Parr . . Tom Curran 's involvement with the show as a producer is also announced. .

The world premiere production of Six took place at the Edinburgh Festival Fringe in 2017, presented by Cambridge University Musical Theatre Society. Although the production did not win any big awards, it was well-received and sold out of tickets at the festival. On 14 October 2017, the Student Run had its closing show, with all cast members leaving.

Trivia

  • Due to being the first and only non-professional production of Six the Musical , many aspects differ from the current state of the show.
    • Many of the songs in this production are performed quite differently to their current iterations; with different instrumentals, lyrics, and choreography.
    • The Ladies in Waiting did not exist within the show yet, with a standard band playing the instruments throughout the show instead.
    • The Megasix did not exist yet.
    • The costumes that the queens wear in the show were not explicitly colour-coded or standardised.
    • The cast does not have any swings, alternates, or understudies.
  • Annabel Marlow was the first actress to be announced for the show.
  • A short mockumentary was filmed, depicting the queens of Six (portrayed by the Student Cast) living together in a single flat.
