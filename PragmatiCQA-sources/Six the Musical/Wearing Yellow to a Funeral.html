" Wearing Yellow to a Funeral " is a joke song in Six the Musical . It is performed by Anne Boleyn and is brought up after Don't Lose Ur Head , however, Anne is cut off before even finishing the first line. The title is a reference to how Henry VIII and Anne Boleyn both wore yellow following Catherine of Aragon 's passing. This could be interpreted two ways; yellow is a colour of mourning in Catherine's home country, Spain; however, this song seems to go with the theory that the two were celebrating Catherine's death.
