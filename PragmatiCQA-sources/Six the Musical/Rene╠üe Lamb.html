Renée Emily Frances Lamb is a British actress, singer, and dancer. She played Catherine of Aragon in the 2017 Arts Theatre production of Six the Musical , beginning on 18 December 2017. She also played the same role on the cast recording. She left the show on 22 January 2018.

Trivia

  • Renée was in the same production of Little Shop of Horrors as Christina Modestou .
  • Renée was in the same production of Malory Towers as Izuka Hoyle .
  • Renée was in the same production of Be More Chill as Millie O'Connell and Grace Mouat .
  • Renée was in the same production of Ain't Misbehavin' as Carly Dyer .
  • Renée returned to the show for the last few performances of the 2018 UK Tour in late December of 2018 as an emergency cover for Catherine of Aragon, since Grace Mouat was the only swing at the time and she was already performing other roles.
  • Renée was one of three former Six cast members from the 2017 Arts Theatre production to perform at Aimie Atkinson 's solo concert at The Other Palace, the other two being Christina Modestou and Genesis Lynea .
  • Renée will be a guest performer at Six 's "Queens Uncrowned" concert alongside Vicki Manser , Grace Mouat, Alicia Corrales-Connor , and Maiya Quansah-Breed , as well as the 2019-20 UK Tour cast.
  • Renée has the same birthday as Lauren Drew .
  • Renée was the first principal actress of colour to portray Catherine of Aragon.
    • She was also the first black principal actress to portray Catherine, as well as being one of the first Black actresses to perform in Six, along with Genesis Lynea and Izuka Hoyle .
  • Renée was the youngest principal actress to play Catherine of Aragon, being 20 years old at the time of her debut.
  • Renée is the only cast member to have worn the Aragon 2.0 costume.
