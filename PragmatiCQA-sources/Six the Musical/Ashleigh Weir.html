Trivia

  • Ashleigh was the first white actress to portray Anne Boleyn.
    • Ashleigh was also the first and currently the only principal actress to portray Anne Boleyn who hasn't had brunette or black hair.
  • Ashleigh was the youngest principal actress to play Anne Boleyn, being 20 years old at the time of her debut.
  • Ashleigh will be in the same production of Gatsby: The Musical as Jodie Steele .
  • Ashleigh is the only cast member to have worn the Boleyn 1.0 costume.
