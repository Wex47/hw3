Katy R. Richardson is an English pianist, musician, music director, and teacher. She is currently the UK musical supervisor West End production of Six the Musical . She previously acted as the Australian Tour's musical supervisor.

Prior to that she played Joan , the pianist and musical director, as part of the original West End cast from 17 January 2019. She announced her decision to step down from the roles on 30 April 2021, but played her final show on 15 December 2020 due to Covid-19 closures.

Trivia

  • Katy had originally wanted to be a musical theatre actress.
  • Katy hosts the series " Sunday Sessions at Six ", as well as its live online edition, "Sunday Sessions Live".
  • Katy was featured in the 17th episode of The Queendom Podcast - A SIX: The Musical Podcast .
  • Katy can play the piano, clarinet, multiple types of saxophones, flute, and bassoon.
  • Katy's favourite songs from Six are I Don't Need Your Love and the remix.
  • Katy has helped with the casting of most productions of Six , with the only exception being the Broadway production.
  • Katy's most memorable experience with Six was performing at the 2019 Laurence Olivier Awards.
  • Katy was in New York for the Broadway production's press night.
  • Katy teaches at the London Academy of Music and Dramatic Art, the Urdang Academy, and the Royal Academy of Music, all of which are located in London.
