Aimie Allen Atkinson is a British actress, singer, dancer, and songwriter. She played Katherine Howard in the West End Production of Six the Musical . She also played the same role in the Original Arts Theatre Production , on the cast recording, and in the show's 2018 UK Tour , beginning on 18 December 2017. Aimie left the show on 13 October 2019 and her role was taken over by Vicki Manser .

Trivia

  • Aimie is one of three members of the British girl group Goldstone.
  • Aimie's mother, Faith Allen, took her to see The Phantom of the Opera for her 13th birthday. She describes it as the play that changed her life.
    • She also mentioned that, career-wise, Six also changed her life and described it as "a big turning point" for her.
  • Aimie was in the same production of In the Heights as Genesis Lynea.
  • When Aimie originally auditioned for the show, she read for Anne Boleyn and Katherine Howard.
    • At her initial audition, she sang Rolling in the Deep by Adele .
  • Aimie is currently the second-longest running cast member of Six , the first being Natalie Paris . Both actresses played the roles for at least two years; however, due to Aimie's departure from the show in 2019, Natalie has played a role in the show longer.
  • At Aimie's solo concert at The Other Palace, three other actresses who have played Katherine Howard joined the performance: Samantha Pauly , Jodie Steele , and Annabel Marlow .
    • Jodie and Aimie previously appeared together in a production of Legally Blonde .
    • Aimie's former Six cast mates Renée Lamb , Christina Modestou , and Genesis Lynea also performed at her concert.
  • In an interview, Aimie has said that if she could play any other queen, she would want to play Catherine of Aragon or Anne Boleyn.
  • Aimie is the only cast member to have worn the Howard 3.0 costume.
    • She was also the first cast member to wear the Howard 4.0 costume.
