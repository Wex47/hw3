Henry FitzRoy, Duke of Richmond and Somerset was the illegitimate son of Henry VIII and Bessie Blount . He is briefly mentioned during Catherine of Aragon 's song No Way , where Catherine says "[Henry has] had one son with someone who don't own a wedding ring".

Trivia

  • He was the only illegitimate child that Henry acknowledged.
