Arthur Tudor was Prince of Wales, Earl of Chester and Duke of Cornwall. He was the elder brother of Henry VIII and the first husband of Catherine of Aragon . He is mentioned a couple of times by Catherine in Six the Musical .

Trivia

  • As the eldest son of Henry VII, Arthur was intended to become king instead of Henry VIII. However, he passed away due to illness at the age of 15, five months after his marriage to Catherine of Aragon.
