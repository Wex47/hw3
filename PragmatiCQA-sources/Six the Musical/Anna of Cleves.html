Anna of Cleves , also referred to as Anne of Cleves , was Queen of England from 6 January to 9 July 1540 due to her marriage to King Henry VIII . She was his fourth wife and was succeeded by Katherine Howard . She is currently being played by Dionne Ward-Anderson in the West End production of Six the Musical , and by Brittney Mack in the Broadway production. In the original cast recording, her role was played by Genesis Lynea .

Trivia

  • Not much is known about Anna before 1527, apart from her date of birth.
  • Henry met Anna privately in disguise on New Year's Day 1540 at Rochester Abbey in Rochester, as this was a courtly-love tradition. It is possible that Anna was unaware that the man in disguise was Henry, as she "regarded him little".
    • Some believe that this instance was why Henry referred to Anna as "ugly", since he was deeply offended by her failure to reciprocate the loving gesture.
  • Anna was friends with Katherine Howard, and it is possible that Katherine served as Anna's maid of honour.
  • After Anna's divorce from Henry, one of the residences given to her was that of the Boleyn's, Hever Castle.
    • Due to the generous divorce settlements that Anna received from Henry, she is often called "the King's Beloved Sister".
  • After Katherine Howard's beheading, Henry was pressured by Anna and her brother William to remarry her; however, Henry refused.
  • Anna appears to have disliked Catherine Parr , stating, "Madam Parr is taking a great burden on herself" upon finding out about her marriage to Henry.
  • Anna was the last of Henry's wives to die; she died nine years after Catherine Parr, his sixth and final wife.
  • For swings and alternates that have two first covers, the role of Anna of Cleves is most often paired with that of Catherine of Aragon .
    • For swings and alternates that have three first covers, the role is most often paired with that of Catherine of Aragon and Anne Boleyn .
  • Over all, out of the four alternate costumes, the black costume is most associated with the role of Cleves; four out of the seven actresses cast as the black alternate have had Cleves as one of their intended first covers.
  • In the musical, Anna's 'queenspirations' were Rihanna and Nicki Minaj .
  • In an Instagram story, Toby Marlow stated that if Six were to be made into a movie, he would like either Lizzo or Cardi B to play Anna.
