Thomas Seymour, 1st Baron Seymour of Sudeley was a brother of Jane Seymour and the fourth and final husband of Catherine Parr . He is mentioned by Catherine before and during I Don't Need Your Love .

Trivia

  • Thomas was executed for treason under the rule of his nephew, Edward VI , on 20 March 1549.
