Jane "Joan" Meutas (née Astley ) was a lady of the privy chamber of Jane Seymour . She is currently being played by Lauren Hopkinson in the West End production of Six the Musical , and Julia Schade in the Broadway production.

Trivia

  • Joan is mentioned in the show by both Jane Seymour and Catherine Parr .
  • Joan's musical and stage directions in the show are more comedically written than the other Ladies in Waiting .
