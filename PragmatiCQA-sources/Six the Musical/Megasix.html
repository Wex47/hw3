" Megasix " is the fourteenth and final song in the stage version of Six the Musical . The song is a mashup of each queen's solo, and is performed as an encore which fans are allowed to record.

Trivia

  • Before July of 2019, Jane sang over Anne rather than stopping when she interrupted her. The new version was first introduced in the North American tour production.
  • The lyrics may vary depending on the performance, as actresses may forget or alter lines.
  • The Megasix is not allowed to be filmed on Broadway or any current or upcoming cruise productions due to union rules, so Catherine of Aragon and Anna of Cleves ' beginning lines are changed to "Stay on your feet, (venue)" and "I wanna see everybody clap your hands" respectively.
    • It was also not allowed to be filmed in the Cambridge and St. Paul productions, and Anna of Cleves' beginning line was changed to "Tell your friends that they're gonna wanna see this".
