Christina Modestou is a Welsh-Greek actress and singer. She played Anne Boleyn in the Original Arts Theatre Production , beginning on 18 December 2017. She also played the same role on the cast recording. She left the show on 22 January 2018.

Trivia

  • Christina has the same birthday as Bryony Duncan .
  • In 2015, Christina was in the same production of Shrek as Candace Furbert and Jennifer Caldwell , with Christina acting as Candace's understudy.
  • Christina was also in the same production of Little Shop of Horrors as Renée Lamb .
  • Anne Boleyn was one of the hardest roles for Christina to play because it was not her casting type or her vocal style.
  • Christina's favourite Six song is the original Megasix .
  • Christina actually wanted spacebuns because Izuka Hoyle had them in one day, but the producer had her keep her hair bobbed.
  • Christina's favourite musical is In the Heights .
  • Christina was the first white actress to portray Anne Boleyn in a professional production of Six.
  • Christina was the oldest principal actress to play Anne Boleyn, being 28 years old at the time of her debut.
  • Christina was one of three former Six cast members from the 2017 Arts Theatre production to perform at Aimie Atkinson 's solo concert at The Other Palace, the other two being Renée Lamb and Genesis Lynea .
  • Christina was featured in the 17th episode of Robbie's Backstage Bants.
  • Christina was featured in the 11th episode of The Queendom Podcast - A SIX: The Musical Podcast .
    • In the podcast, Christina revealed that the songs were originally read as monologues and not songs.
  • Christina is the only cast member to have worn the Boleyn 2.0 costume.
