Spotlight

Oliver Wickham is a British performer, singer, visual artist, and writer. They played Anna of Cleves at the Edinburgh Fringe 2017 and Cambridge performances of Six the Musical , beginning on 31 July 2017. They left the show on 14 October 2017.

Personal Life

Oliver is the child of actor Rupert Wickham and actress Janie Dee. They are non-binary and use they/them pronouns.

Trivia

  • Oliver is the first openly non-binary performer to portray Anna of Cleves.
    • They are also the first openly non-binary performer to participate in Six .
  • Oliver was the youngest principal performer to play Anna of Cleves, being 21 years old at the time of their debut.
  • Oliver is the only cast member to have worn the Cleves 1.0 costume.
