Francis Dereham

Historical Information

Birth Name

Francis Dereham

Dates

1506~09 – 10 December 1541 (aged 31-35)

Cause of Death

Execution by beheading

Family

  • John Dereham (father)
  • Isabel Paynell (mother)

Francis Dereham was a courtier and secretary who engaged in a sexual relationship with Katherine Howard during her youth. He was executed for supposedly continuing his relationship with Katherine after her marriage to Henry VIII . He is the subject of the second verse of All You Wanna Do .
