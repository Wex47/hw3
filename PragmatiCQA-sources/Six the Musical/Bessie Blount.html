Elizabeth "Bessie" Blount was a lady-in-waiting to Catherine of Aragon and Anna of Cleves , and a mistress of Henry VIII . She is currently being played by Rachel Espeute in the West End production of Six the Musical , and Michelle Osbourne in the Broadway production.
