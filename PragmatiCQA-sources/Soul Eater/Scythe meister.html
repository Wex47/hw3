Debut

Chapter 1

Scythe-meisters (鎌職人, Kama Shokunin ) are meisters whom are capable of employing the usage of a demon scythe , fighting with it similarly to that of a pole-arm like weapon.

Overview

When training, aspiring scythe-meisters typically learn scythe-handling skills and basic maneuvers such as twirling. Among scythe-meisters, the witch-hunt slash is a common techniques learned among their skills although it is considered advanced. Superiorly skilled scythe-meisters can also employ advanced variants of this moves, those it is more selected and are dependent on the amount of courage one possesses.
