Demon shadow weapons (魔暗器, Maanki ) is a demon weapon capable of transforming themselves into Japanese weaponry fit for an assassin and are used by shadow weapon meisters .

The Nakatsukasa Clan is a notable family made up of demon shadow weapons, with the Uncanny Sword being among their most infamous weapon forms.
