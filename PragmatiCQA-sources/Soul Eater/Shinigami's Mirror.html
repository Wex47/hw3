Lord Death's mirror (死神さんの鏡, Shinigami-san no Kagami ) is the mysterious device in the Death Room in which Death often resides in literally and uses as a communication and surveillance medium.

Appearance

The mirror simply appears as a tall, wooden mirror with a reflective, dark blue surface and a skull mask feature that matches Death's own on top of the wood.

Abilities

42-42-564

This "mirror number" is used to communicate via mirrors or any transparent window, to contact Shinigami's Mirror and call Death . For example, the user will find a mirror and blow air onto it to make it condensed, then they will write the "mirror number" of the person they want to call. For instance, Death's number is "42-42-564".
