Sanzu Falls Shot (サンズフォールショット, Sanzufōrushotto ) is a technique used by Death the Kid upon connecting the first (middle) Line of Sanzu .

Overview

During this technique, the user raises his arms up while firing and then subsequently brings them down while slowly separating them, until they are fully split, where his arms now become parallel with the ground. The resulting appearance of the combined shots is reminiscent of a cascading waterfall. The stream that is fired down is capable of striking multiple targets at once.

Kid used this attack upon Mosquito , damaging the Arachnophobia member even in his power form from 400 years ago , an impressive feat considering he is composed of eight thousand " bats .
