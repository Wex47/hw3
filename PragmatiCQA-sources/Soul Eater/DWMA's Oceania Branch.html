The DWMA's Oceania Branch is a branch of Death Weapon Meister Academy that oversees the Oceania region of the world. It is currently headed by Azusa Yumi .

History

The Oceania branch of the DWMA would find itself under the leadership of Marie Mjolnir shortly after her crafting as a death weapon, as she saw the branch as the easiest to manage.

Synopsis

A Fight to the Death at the Big Bash arc

In the wake of Asura's resurrection, the Oceania branch is contacted by Sid Barrett at the behest of Death to develop a counterstrategy for dealing with the kishin's madness wavelength. The head of the branch, Marie Mjolnir , answers and makes her way back to Death City .

In order to fortify the academy, Marie is instead assigned as Stein's official partner within the school and given the duties of an instructor. In her place, Death assigns Azusa Yumi to head both the DWMA East Asia Branch & the Oceania branch.

Overview

This branch of DWMA is far lighter in workload and the region is generally peaceful. Marie selected being in charge of this region because she know being in charge of this region was easier compared to all the other branches.

In the anime , it seems that this branch has responsibilities relating to technology, with members like Joe Buttataki acting as a expert and consultant on technology from Oceania.
