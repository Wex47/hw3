Overview

Demon Weapon's Absorb

Also referred to being "consumed", Demon Weapons have the capability of absorbing the soul/spirit of other beings. This can be done via oral consumption. Various weapon types such as gun types can absorb through their gun magazine or a knife type weapon can absorb souls through their sheathe.
