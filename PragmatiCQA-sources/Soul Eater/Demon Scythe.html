Demon scythes (魔鎌, Magama ) are demon weapons that can turn into scythes.

Overview

All demon scythes are characterized by their weapon form being rather polearm-like similar to that of a staff and a , curved blade at the end capable of cutting through the flesh of other beings. Commonly during soul resonance with attacks such as witch-hunt , the scythe's blade appearance may change in the period of the attack's usage.
