Instantaneous Maximum Firepower (瞬間最大火力, Shunkan Saidai Karyoku ) is a fighting style used by the Meister, Kilik Rung .

Overview

As the name imples, this style of fighting allows the user to attack with the max firepower of their capability in an instant. This makes for a devastating style with powerful attacks from the user. This style, however, is useless against a target with Black Blood (ex. Black Clown ).
