History

Synopsis

Death Fest arc

After Shaula Gorgon's attack on Death City, Princess Anastasia heads back to her home country for a time to report all that had took place during her stay in Death Weapon Meister Academy. Despite the danger, Anastasia's parents agreed to let her stay enrolled in the DWMA.

Overview

A high class royal family , House Yngling is influential, having connections with DWMA's central intelligence agency concerning the safety of Princess Anastasia who enrolled in the DWMA as "Anya Hepburn", an agreement in which she is to return home should danger befall her.

House Yngling practices various customs, including wearing the family crest at all times and training in specialized royal martial arts .

Special Abilities

Royal Martial Arts (イヤル武術, Iyaru bujutsu ): Members of the royal family are trained in these specialized martial arts.
