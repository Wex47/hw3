Death's family is a powerful and wealthy family in Death City consisting of gods of death .

Overview

Perharps one of the (if not, the most) powerful families in the world, Death's family is a highly influential one, particularly in both Death City and within Death Weapon Meister Academy that allows them to be considered a important member of the DWMA and are treated with a three-star status regardless of their actual rank. They also possess great wealth.
