Adjust (相殺, Sōsai ) is a wavelength control technique used by Franken Stein .

Overview

Adjust is an advanced technique that allows a meister to match up to another, making it possible to cancel out soul wavelength -related attacks. This special technique requires acute soul perception capabilities and a flexible soul.
