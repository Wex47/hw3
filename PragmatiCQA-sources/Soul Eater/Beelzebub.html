Beelzebub (ベルゼブブ, Beruzebubu ) is Death the Kid 's flight-capable skateboard.

Contents

Appearance

The skateboard has red wheels and a green deck with a picture of two skulls connected symmetrically at the jaw. While in flight mode, the wheels rotate 90°, so that they point downwards and shoot jets.

Abilities

Beelzebub is capable of allowing the user to fly at great speeds. The max speeds that it's seen flying in is 300 mph, capable of keeping up with the Runaway Express , which is powered by the Eternal Spring .
