Madness Attack (狂気攻撃, Kyōki kōgeki ) is a technique in attacks a user with madness.

Overview

A technique that targets a user by using madness against them. The effects of a madness attack is dependent on the user's madness wavelength:

  • Arachne's madness targets other individual's mind, induces illusions, mentally attacks people, and pulls them into madness.
  • Those with the madness of black blood induces other's into powerful illsions and pulls them into madness similar to Asura and Arachne's.
