The Trial Enrollment Arc is the eighth story arc in Soul Eater . As Crona and Ragnarok are admitted into Death Weapon Meister Academy , Arachne Gorgon is stirred from her eight hundred years of inactivity from Asura's Madness and launches her network into the opening: Arachnophobia .

Notable Instances

  • After considering their position, Death places both Crona and Ragnarok as trial students in the DWMA in hopes of converting the both of them into new, powerful allies.
  • Arachne Gorgon pieces herself together after eight hundred years of splitting her soul into spiders and watching the world. With Mosquito and Giriko acting as her henchmen, Arachnophobia becomes an active threat against Death Weapon Meister Academy .
