Overview

An elite team consisting of former top one-star ranked meisters and weapons, Spartoi's objective is to help with efforts to put down the of the Kishin , his agents , and threats from the Witch Order . In order to do so, Spartoi members received additional special training. The clothing of each member of "Spartoi" follows a single, uniformed aesthetic: white and blue. The style of clothing for Spartoi is very similar to a classic uniform—a white top, blue pants, and white shoes.
