The mother of Maka and ex-wife of Spirit Albarn was a renowed DWMA academy student and scythe-meister whom crafted her ex-husband and weapon partner into a death weapon .

Personality

In the anime , Maka describes her mother's personality as like that of a hippo (in the English dub, a bear), as she is strong, stout, and uncontrollable when angered. Maka also describes her to having an "air of tranquility".

Power

Her level and skill is comparable to that of Maka Albarn , whom also excelled and was at the top of her class like her own mother. Despite her apparent skill, however, Spirit mentioned that her talent and skill is not in Franken Stein 's league.

Special Abilities

Scythe-Meister (鎌職人, Kama Shokunin ): Fellow scythe-meister Death considered Maka's mother to be skilled, having positively compared her to her own daughter.

Anti-Demon Wavelength (退魔の波長, Taima no Hachō ; FUNimation "Anti-Magic Wavelength") : Maka's mother possess the Anti-Demon Wavelength that her daughter inherited, which allowed her to transfer said wavelength into her weapon partner and execute two of the Great Traditional Techniques of the Scythe-Meister . Strong Will : He will is so strong that she possess a high resistance to Madness . Spirit mentions that madness "just bounces right off her".

History

During her timeas a student in the academy, she was the one to have noticed Franken Stein 's experimentation on Spirit Albarn in his sleep. After alerting Spirit and putting to a stop to it eventually, she later became his meister.

Overtime, both her and Spirit fell in love. She eventually became pregnant with their daughter, Maka . She would give birth to Maka when her husband was eighteen. However his womanizing costed her trust with Spirit and she eventually filed for divorce and moved away from Death City.

Synopsis

Remedial Lessons arc

With her divorce finalized with her former husband, Spirit, she gained full, legal custody of Maka while also denying requiring any need of child support or punitive damage from Spirit.

Operation Capture Baba Yaga Castle arc

In the anime , At some point after the relaying of Arachnophobia 's madness relay base devices are scattered throughout the world, she sends more post cards to Maka, one in particular that had the world "courage" ("شجاعت", "šajāʿat") written in Persian.

Trivia

  • The erroneous belief that Maka's mother is named Kami comes from a mistranslated Japanese etiquette. A wife that is no longer present, either due to divorce or death, is often referred to as "moto kami san" (roughly meaning "ex-wife") in lieu of their actual name.
