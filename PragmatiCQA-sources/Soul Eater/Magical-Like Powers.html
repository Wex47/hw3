Overview

As the name implies, wavelength control is an ability that allows a user to control their own soul wavelength . Both meisters and weapons possess this skill to varying degrees ranging from rudimentary to more advanced levels. Special demon weapons known as death scythes possess a more higher-level of wavelength control known from the consumption of a witch 's soul. New abilities birthed from these souls can appear similarly to magic.
