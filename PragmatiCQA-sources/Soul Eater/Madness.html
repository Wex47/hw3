Overview

A force possessed all living things within their souls, however large or small , this force plays a subtle to significant manner in dictating the behaviors of an individual dependent on the individual. This force also possess special abilities in higher, strong concentrations such as jamming signals & and inducing powerful, convincing illusions.

Madness possess an duality-type relationship with magic: considered by some to be an envolving power, madness holds the ability to cancel out certain magical attacks due to being considered a more progressive power with magic being viewed as "obselete". Madness can also distort magics such as spatial magic in strong concentrations. However, certain magics can be boosted using madness as as ource.

Madness Wavelength

Main article: Madness Wavelength

While most people possess a litle madness within them, select individual possess madness at a height level and develop a madness wavelength capable of influenceing inviduals to perform certain actions or be in an emotional state dependent on their madness wavelength..
