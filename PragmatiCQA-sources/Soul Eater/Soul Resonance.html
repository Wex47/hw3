Soul resonance (魂の共鳴, Tamashī no Kyōmei ) is a special technique performed exclusively by a meister and a demon weapon .

Overview

A technique in which a meister sends their wavelength into their weapon, in which it then amplifies it and sends it back to the meister. The process is done repeatedly, generating a strong wavelength that can be used in a technique to be executed in. This relationship has been compared to the usage of an electric guitar and an AMP by Death , who explains that while the sound (soul wavelength) that comes from an electric guitar (meister) by itself is rather faint, it gains a lot of power when connected to the AMP (weapon).

During soul resonance, individuals in resonance are able to commune with each other's souls and as such, can communicated to one another in a manner almost telepathically. Certain abilities are also boosted from resonance such as soul perception.

Talented meisters are capable of resonating between more than one souls of the same type of weaponry at a time, a feat noted to be quite difficult among them. Meisters whom are capable of resonating between different weaponry simultaneously are known as utility meisters .

Bonds

Through what is known as bonds , weapons and meisters are able to resonate with family members and relatives. Uniquely, bonds are impervious to techniques that disrupt the usual process of soul resonance between two different individuals. However, attacks under bonds are not as powerful due to the similarity in wavelengths.
