A necromancer ( 死霊使い ( ネクロマンサー ) , Nekuromansā ) is a witch whose magical powers enable them to take control of the dead.

Overview

Necromancers are capable of using their magical powers, combined with a demon book such as the Book of the Dead to reanimate and control the dead like that of a mummy and/or an evil spirit. However, some evil spirits can prove to be too powerful to control and even turn on the necromancer.
