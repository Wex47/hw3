Overview

In Death Weapon Meister Academy , the the header is the leader of the academy and and oversees all branches of the organization, including those with military capabilities. Addressed with a "-sama" honorific (or Lord), the headmaster is the highest ranking title available within Death Weapon Meister Academy. Only a Reaper is able to hold the title. They're responsible for the student registry, has the ability to decide whether or not students should take a remedial class, and supervises student progress as well as make the final decisions on the lessons that are taken.

There's a special ceremony in which is known as the New Reaper Coronation in which a new grim reaper is officially made the headmaster of Death Weapon Meister Academy.
