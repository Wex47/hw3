Forced Burial (強制土葬, Kyōsei Dosō ) is a soul resonance technique used by Sid Barrett and Mira Naigus .

Overview

A technique in which teletransports people from graves created from a user (like Sid), it is performed when the user's weapon form changes shape and slams the base of the tombstone-shaped weapon into the ground, a quantity of tunnels going directly downwards, appear beneath multiple individuals in the immediate vicinity of the user simultaneously. The technique can be used to effectively escape from a situation though it takes time to utilize and how many graves and far it can go is dependent on the user's strength.
