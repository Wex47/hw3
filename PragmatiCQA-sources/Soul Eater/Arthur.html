King Arthur (キング·アーサー, Kingu Āsā ) was the legendary warrior knight, king, and meister of Excalibur .

History

During his lifetime, he was once a meister of Excalibur and king of Britain, earning fame from his tenure as the legendary blade's meister.

Equipment

Holy Sword (聖剣, Seiken ): King Arthur once held the legendary blade most powerful weapon in the world, the Holy Sword. Using this sword enabled him to at least use its standard powers and allowed him to summon Wings of Light , instantaneous teleportation, and to slice through space itself.

Trivia

Arthur is based on the legendary British king of the same name, who was also said to be the owner of Excalibur’s namesake sword.
