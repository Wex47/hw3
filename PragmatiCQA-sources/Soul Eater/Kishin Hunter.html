Kishin Hunter (鬼神狩, Kishingari ) is a technique used by powerful scythe-meisters and is the most powerful of the variants of Witch Hunter .

Overview

A even more powerful variant of the Genie Hunter technique, it can be assumed to work similarly to that witch hunter, being a more powerful variant unlocked after having a great amount of courage and being very reluctant against madness as well as enhancing one's wavelength.

Like the other similar techniques, the scythe's blade takes a different shape dependent on the user's weapon and is implied to be powerful enough to kill a kishin . Despite it's potential power, the technique is can be rendered useless if used by a person with a Anti-Mangic Wavelength and applied to an opponent if they're not truly evil and they're powerful enough to nullify the technique.

Trivia

  • This attack's form was included in the game, Terraria , but was later removed.
