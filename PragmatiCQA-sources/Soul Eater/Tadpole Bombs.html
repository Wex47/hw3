Tadpole Bomb (おたまボム, Otama Bomu ) is a type of Magic which revolves around the usage of bombs in the appearance and form of a tadpole.

Overview

This magic allows its user to create tadpole-like creatures that explode either after being sent to hop after the enemy, or being thrown forcefully. It seems that they can be created in differing sizes, thus with differing explosive power. Although a single bomb will not do much damage, when exploding in large numbers, they can be quite a reasonable threat.
