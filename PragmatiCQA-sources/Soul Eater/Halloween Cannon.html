Pumpkin Cannon (パンプキン砲, Panpukin Hō ) is a powerful spell used by Blair .

Overview

The spell is a magical projectile used as an offensive attack that upon contact explodes. The user fires a magical pumpkin from one of her hands that results in an incredibly destructive blast upon contact with the opponent. This spell also has a linear blast version which can be fired.

Trivia

  • The Prologues chapters call this attack "Halloween Cannon" but after the Prologues, Blair's usage of this spell is renamed "Pumpkin Cannon".
