A fragment is a grim reaper whose existence was born by being created from a piece of another reaper's soul is one of the methods in which they use to reproduce.

Overview

The "parent" is able to either make them "complete" or "incomplete" grim reapers and limit the power they inherit from the sanzu lines. It's also shown that the fragment carries some of the parent's traits, such as Death the Kid's inheriting of the Madness of Order and being born without fear due to Death lacking them during Kid's creation, as Asura already possessed and embodied his fears.

While a fragment will have the soul wavelength of their parent within their soul, after two connections of the Lines of Sanzu, a reaper can forfeit their own parent's soul wavelength with their soul. When this is performed by Death the Kid , in order to power his own attack , he did so and remarked he didn't want to "ride" on his "father's coattails".
