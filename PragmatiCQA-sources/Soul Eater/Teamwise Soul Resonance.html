Teamwise Soul Resonance (チームでの魂の共鳴, Chīmu de no Tamashī no Kyōmei ) is a special technique in which allows teams of Meister/Demon Weapons to resonate with two or more people.

Overview

Making an indirect connection from one souls to another (which can also be achieved with special abilities such as the usage of Soul's Piano ), a Soul Resonance is performed between two or more Meisters, allowing those within the team to become empowered. This level of empowerment varies from the number and actual users but can be used to overwhelm a powerful enemy the teams otherwise individually cannot contend with.
