Overview

Weapon variant

Working in opposite of the soul resonance , soul possession instead allows the weapon to possess their meister of choice (even by force) and take over their soul. The end result allows the weapon with soul possession to devour their meister's soul. However, it is possible to defeat the technique by using other wavelength control techniques , as a weapon cannot control two soul wavelengths at one time.
