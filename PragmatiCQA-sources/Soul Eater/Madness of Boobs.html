Description

This madness type influences an individual into having a much larger preference of female breasts and makes those under its influence fascinated with it enough to have the urge to repeatedly fondle, grab, or think of them often. According to Death the Kid , this madness came into being due to the fact that Crona seeks breasts, the symbol of motherhood. This is because of the fact Crona never recieved any motherly love from Medusa Gorgon .
