The Immortal Clan (不死の一族, Fushi no Ichizoku ) is a werewolf clan renown for their immortality .

History

The immortal clan once co-existed along with humans . However, those within the immortal clan often threaten the lives of ordinary humans and broke the rules established by Death Weapon Meister Academy and were killed as a result. When their numbers dwindled, they were instead taken into custody.

Special Abilities

  • Immortality (不死, Fushi ): Their most famed ability, the Immortal Clan's immortality allows them to survive and heal from most extreme injuries, regeneration, and allows them to possess great physical resilience against attacks of most nature.

Fighting Styles

  • Fighting Wolf Fist (闘狼拳, Tōrōken ): A aggressive fighting martial art in which takes advantage of a immortal's body.

List of Immortals

Within the Immortal Clan, each of the members when addressed by those outside the clan itself are often referred to as a " immortal (不死, Fushi )" for short.
