Overview

Similarly to witches, wizards appear similarly to human males and solely consist of males. They're capable of performing magic and (can) have the same support system of using specific mantras to envoke their magical power. Additionally, their souls are also of a purple hue. Within societies led by witches, it's not uncommon for wizards to serve as teachers of magic to witches .

Special Abilities

  • Magic (魔法, Mahō ): Like witches, wizards are able to control this type of energy at will to invoke supernatural effects both offensively and defensively.
  • Longevity : Wizards are capable of living for centuries (the oldest being Eibon , whom lived for over 800 years).

Equipment

  • Spellbooks (魔法書, Mahōsho ): Spellbooks are books imbued with magical power. Some wiards use them to perform magic.Wizards are known for carrying them on their hips.
