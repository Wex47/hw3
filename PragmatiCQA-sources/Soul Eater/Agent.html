Overview

Agents of Lord Death

Agents of Lord Death (死神の 使い ( つか ) , Shinigami no Tsuka ; lit "Messenger of Shinigami") is a term used to identify individuals loyal to Death . Their affiliation with Death puts them at odds with those loyal to the Asura, such as his agents , the Clown Army , and Asura himself. The E.A.T Class , general military members of Death Weapon Meister Academy , Death Weapons , and former members of the Eight Reaper Legions are all assumed to be agents of Shingami.

Agents of the Kishin

Agents of the Kishin (鬼神の手先, Kishin no tesaki ) are people who pledge aligeance to Asura . Varying in mindsets, agents look to Asura as a benevolent figure and view madness in a more efficacious perspective. Due to the affiliation with Asura, agents of the Kishin stand in opposition to the likes of Death's agents such as those of Death Weapon Meister Academy and the E.A.T Class .

Trivia

  • The specific designation of "Arachnophobia agent" exists only in the Yen Press's translation. In the original Japanese, the intro card to Fisherking is "Arachnophobia Fisherking" (蜘蛛恐怖症 フィッシャーキング, Arakunofobia Fisshākingu )
