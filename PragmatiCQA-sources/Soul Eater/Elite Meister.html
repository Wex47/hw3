A elite meister (上級職人, Jōkyū shokunin ) is the highest-ranking title attainable by meisters in Death Weapon Meister Academy .

Overview

Usually among the three-star rank or a member of Death's family , elite meisters have privileges' higher than any student or ordinary instructors of the E.A.T Class star-ranking system and have information clearance of Level 4. Roughly, their rank is equivalent to that of a ranked Death Scythe .
