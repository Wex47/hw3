A Magic Lifeform (魔法生命体, Mahō Seimeitai ) is a living life form which is made up of Magic and often assist the likes of a user of Magic.

Overview

Magic Lifeforms often take the theme of their respective owner, their usage being similar to that of pets, transportation, and even combat situations.
