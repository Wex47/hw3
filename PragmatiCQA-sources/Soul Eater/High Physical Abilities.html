High physical abilities (高い身体能力, Takai karada nōryoku ) refer to inhuman acts and capabilities an individual may physically possess.

Overview

A broad term to describe an individual with physically superhuman capabilities, these abilities can be gained by training, a factor of an individual's race (such as a Werewolf naturally having physical abilities beyond humans), or a combination of both.
