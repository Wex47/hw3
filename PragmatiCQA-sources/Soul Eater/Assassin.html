Assassins (暗殺者, Ansatsusha ) is an individual who specializes in the art of killing other individuals without detection. As such, many assassins are proficient in the art of assassination .

Several organizations utilize assassins like Death Weapon Meister Academy and Arachnophobia . Special assassin clan families like the Star Clan also exist.
