Calculation spells (演算魔法, Enzan Mahō ) is magic related to mathematics.

Overview

This sort of magic uses mathematics to create " place makers" for placing the exact coordinates of an even more complex magical spell. It is best used for creating portals, positioning attacks, and supporting magic like Spatial Magic . The usage of the magic requires an individual to have a vast understanding in math.
