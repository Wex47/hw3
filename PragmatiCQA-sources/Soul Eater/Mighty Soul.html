A mighty soul (強靭な魂, Kyōjin'na Tamashī ; Yen Press Translation "Strong Soul") is an incredibly rare soul-type that signifies great power.

Overview

A indicator of a individual possessing great, inherent power , humans who possess this type of soul are worth 99 human souls when consumed.
