The Evans family (エヴァンス家, Evansu Ka ) are a well-established family of musicians.

History

The family's history dates back to at least 800 years ago, when one of it's members were of demon weapon heritage. Overtime, the knowledge of their weapon heritage remained unknown to the family.

In the modern era, the Evans family were a well-known musician family; in one instance, the Evans family, particularly Soul Evans, gave a concert for the Yngling Family at Yngling Castle .

Overview

A family of famous, wealthy musicians, their musical skills are well-regarded; Wes Evans 's is a renown violinist regarded as the "Virtuoso Violinist". However, the family was unaware of their lineage containing demon weapon blood.
