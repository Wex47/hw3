History

Al Capone's Family became relatively infamous in their crimes, the gang boss being known as the "Underworld King" and Death having placed him on his list to be executed by an assassin from Death Weapon Meister Academy . The assassin chosen was Black☆Star . During this time, the organization sought out the location of Angela Leon to harness her Witch power.

Synopsis

Prologues Arc

While eating with his gang members, Al Capone is targeted by Black☆Star and Tsubaki Nakatsukasa , his weapon partner. However, due to his incompetence and desire for attention, the family find themselves surprised at him announcing his presence. Failing to kill them, the duo disappear much to the family's bewilderment. A member of Capone's family approaches the boss and informs him that they have found Angela Leon , a Witch they have been searching for, at Demon Cinder Castle . The crime family moves in to aprehend the Witch and collect her soul to make their organization more powerful but is instead met by Mifune and got wiped out by the bodyguard.

Overview

With the exception of it's leader, each member of Al Capone's Family is identified by their distinct outfit, consisting of a tuxedo with a black tie and a distinguishable black and white mask. As a criminal organization, they also use various weapons such as pistols and automatic weaponry. Their sole interest is improving the scope of their organization and as such, have invest interest in gaining the power of a Witch . The head of the gang is granted the title, "Don".

Under Al Capone's leadership, the gang was notorious enough to sanction the use of assassins by Death Weapon Meister Academy and resourceful enough to have tracked down Angela Leon . Their leader was noted by Mifune to be known as the "King of the Underworld". Despite their apparently proficiency, Mifune was easily capable of destroying the entire gang in a single instance.

In the anime , all members of the gang consume souls, are Kishin Eggs , and 99 members exist within this criminal organization.
