Rasputin (ラスプーチン, Rasupūchin ) was a phantom monk and an Evil Person on Shinigami's List .

Personality

Having only appeared for a short time, his personality is not that much known. However, it appears he has pride in his abilities, boasting to Maka and Soul that not even bullets can hurt him.

Appearance

He takes on the form of a giant figure, easily twice as tall as the average human being and has an even wider girth, with his shoulder area wider than the rest of his body. His arms are long whilst his legs are stubby, and he has large hands. He is dressed in a black monk's habit and beneath it, he seems to wear diamond-patterned stockings. He wears an black ecclesatial hat similar. He has a white beard that ends in the shape of a cross and a string of beads around his neck. His eyes are round and black with white pupils and he has sharp teeth.

Power

Rasputin is one of the weakest quest enemies within the game. While his HP is superior to Jack the Ripper and on the same level as that of Sonson-J , Sonson-J possess stronger attacks. Furthermore, both Ajarri and Booth , Phantom Monks , are far stronger than Rasputin.

Special Abilities

Phantom Monk (怪僧, kaisō ; FUNimation "Evil Monk"): As an Phantom Monk, Rasputin has access to some special, physical techniques with red-colored energy of unknown origin.

  • Pay with my Left Arm (左腕で払う, Hidariude de Harau ): A simple left-handed slap.
  • Pay with my Right Arm (右腕で払う, Migiude de harau ): A simple right-handed slap.
  • Lower Fist Swing (拳振り下ろし, Ken furi oroshi ): Rasputin surrounds both his hands with red energy and slams them downwards against his target.
  • Hip Attack (ヒップアタック, Hippu Atakku ): Rasputin leaps towards his opponent, releasing a shockwave of red energy when he lands.
  • Enhanced Durability : Rasputin claimed that not even bullets have been able to affect him.

Synopsis

Remedial Lessons Arc

The night before their Supplementary Class Lessons Maka and Soul faced off against Rasputin. Maka managed to deal the Evil Person a kick that sent him crashing through a church window, slamming him onto the floor inside the building. As Maka readied her next attack, Soul claimed that Rasputin's soul was now his. Despite Rasputin commenting "Don't waste your time!! Not even bullets have any effect on me!!", Maka, being a Scythe Meister, and Soul made quick work of Rasputin, slicing him in half. His soul was then devoured by Soul.

Media Appearance

Soul Eater: Monotone Princes

  • HP : 700

Raputin appears as one of the bosses fought as part of the Kishin Egg quest line and is recommended to encounter after achieving Level 6. The best strategy is to dodge his "Hip Attack" and immediately attack him thereafter. Every few hits staggers Rasputin and can leave him open for a Soul Resonance attack.

Trivia

  • Rasputin may have been a reference to the Rasputin who manipulated Tsar Nicholas II because the Bolsheviks tried to kill Rasputin in several ways including being shot several times. Rasputin mentioned that bullets was ineffective against him.
