A Shadow Weapon Meister ( 暗 ( あん ) 器 ( き ) 職人, Anki Shokunin ; FUNimation "Dark Arm Meister") is a meister who is presumably skilled in the usage of traditional Japanese assassin weaponry.

Overview

A shadow weapon meister is skilled in a variety of Japanese weaponry in which may including: a katana, chain scythe, smoke bomb, shurikens, and ninja sword. Additionally, a shadow weapon meister can be likely capable of utilizing the art of assassination when employing the weapons.
