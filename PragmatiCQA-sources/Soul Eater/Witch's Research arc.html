The Witch's Research Arc is the seventeenth named story arc in Soul Eater . With Crona and Medusa finally resurfacing in Africa , members of Spartoi are deployed under the supervision of Azusa Yumi to intercept the witch fugitive.

Notable Instances

  • Since her disappearance from Baba Yaga Castle , Medusa Gorgon has developed her own clowns , one of which, the Black Clown , will serve as an additional partner to Crona for the remainder of the manga.
  • Black☆Star and Tsubaki Nakatsukasa discover one of Medusa's lairs , where they will fight Crona .

Trivia

  • In the original Japanese, Atsushi Ohkubo includes yet another musical allusion through Kilik and Liz's puns on the word "jam," referring to not only bullets stuck in a gun's chamber but also to play improvisationally.
  • Medusa's research facility includes an eyeball in a jar, similar to one that appeared in her other research facility in the anime before Eruka Frog blew it up, along with Sid Barrett and Mira Naigus .
  • Liz and Patty use varying honorifics when referring to Azusa. Liz refers to her with "-onee" ("big sister"), to show that Liz respects Azusa's age and talent but sees her more as a peer than as an instructor. Patty, on the other hand, refers to Azusa as "Azusa-san," showing camaraderie for Azusa but less respectful address than Liz.
  • Kilik's fight against the Black Clown resembles Franken Stein 's first battle against Crona, when the erupted of Black Blood off of the Clown and Crona's bodies from Kilik and Stein's attacks hangs momentarily before forming Bloody Needles to pierce at the meisters.
  • Kim and Jacqueline's Volantern attack is a portmanteau of "vol" ("flight") and "lantern."
