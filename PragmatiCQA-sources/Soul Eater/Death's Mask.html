Appearance

Death's mask appears as a comical skull with three simple holes through it. Formally, his former mask possessed a more intimidating appearance, with rounded edges compared to his current as well as them being mended together and not pointed downwards.

Abilities

Death's Mask can convey its wearer's entire emotions, acting as their face (it should be noted, however, that the mask itself is an object, as he expressed that he already has a face underneath it). It also appears to have a special connection to its wearer, as when the wearer is being killed or dying, the mask crumbles too.
