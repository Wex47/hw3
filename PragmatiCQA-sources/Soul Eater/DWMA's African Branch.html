The DWMA's African Branch is a branch of Death Weapon Meister Academy that oversees activities in the Africa . It is headed by the Death Scythe, Dengu Dinga .

Synopsis

A Fight to the Death at the Big Bash arc

In the aftermath of the resurrection of the Kishin, Death Weapon Meister Academy 's representative Sid Barrett requested the convening of all the Death Scythes under the instruction of Lord Death . However, the African branch rejected their call altogether.

Reunion Express arc

Coordinating with Sid Barett , the African Recovery Team intercept the Runaway Express during Death the Kid 's assignment of retrieving the eternal spring demon tool. Spotting a witch and watching them escape, Sid would order them to let her go due to achieving their prime objective.

Clown arc

After the main branch lost the arms race of "BREW" to Arachnophobia seemingly, the African branch came under attack by Arachnohpobia forces, attacking their main office, sustaining heavy damages.
