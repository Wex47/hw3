The Art of Self Defense (護身術, goshin jutsu ) is a fighting style used by magic weapons in which allows them to battle alone without a meister .

Overview

Magic weapons proficient in this style utilize their own weapon transformative abilities to defend and attack opponents. Some even develop their own particular style derived from this capability.
