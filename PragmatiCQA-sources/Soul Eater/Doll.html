A Doll (人形, Ningyō ) is a artificially animated beings created by Death .

Overview

Dolls, while can vary in appearance, tend to have a round body, a mask of some sort in place of a face, and some sort of alteration of a large glove . Their purpose is to act as training dummies for Death Weapon Meister Academy , though may run loose in Death City from time to time. When created, they seemingly spawn from the ground and when killed, vanish in a display of black and purple smoke.

Special Abilities

Varying in attack power, Dolls only seem to take from the physical capabilities from Death such as his gloves.
