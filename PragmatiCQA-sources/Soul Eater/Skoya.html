Skoya (スコヤ, Sukoya ) is a witch wanted by the DWMA Intelligence Agency for unknown reasons. Her sketch was with some of the witches displayed in the Anti-Witch Headquarters .

Special Abilities

Mind Control (マインドコントロール, Maindo Kontorōru ): Because of the kind of witches investigated and the presence of Arachne Gorgon 's data nearby Skoya's, she appears to have some skill in Mind Control .

Trivia

  • "Sukoya" is Japanese for "square", as in "machinist square" or "speed square". The name therefore is likely a reference to Square Enix , the publisher of the Soul Eater franchise.
