The Grigori Arc is the 16th story arc of the Soul Eater series. In the aftermath of the dissolution of Arachnophobia , Maka Albarn becomes a target for assassination by Noah's Group .

Notable Instances

  • All members of Spartoi train to harness their skill, having been promoted to two-star weapons and meisters . Maka learns of her unique Grigori soul and with Soul Eater 's advanced weapon abilities, the pair are able to learn flight.
  • Under Noah's orders, Gopher targets Maka Albarn 's soul so his master can use it to harness her acute soul-sensing powers to find Asura . His overall goal and reason for wanting to find Asura is expressed in the Salvage arc .
  • Gopher fails to gather her soul and Maka spares him on account of not being on Shinigami's List . Due to him outing Noah's name to the enemy, he is humilated and beats Death the Kid as a way to vent his frustration.
