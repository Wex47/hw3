Vajra (ヴァジュラ, Vajura ) was the demon weapon partner to Asura and a warlord of the Eight Reaper Legions .

Personality

Death 's comments suggest that Vajra was intimidating enough to scare his meister into resorting to consuming him.

Power

Vajra's weapon form has enormous power and is strong enough to penetrate through Lord Death 's defenses.

Special Abilities

Full-Weapon Transformation (全身武器, Zenshin buki ): As a demon weapon, Vajra is capable of transforming from a human form to that of a weapon.

  • Vajra (ヴァジュラ, Vajura ): As his namesake implies, he can transform into a literal vajra. His weapon form can resonate with his meisters and produce powerful blasts of lasers and orbs of energy. Such blasts can vary from intensity, resembling that of nuke.

History

Born some after the creation of demon weapons by Arachne 's hand, Vajra eventually joined the ranks of the Eight Reaper Legions as one of Death 's warlords and served as the weapon partner of his son, Asura . He was likely one of the members later present as Death was informed that Asura managed to kill off the Witches in the East on his own by the time members of the group arrived at a designated location to deal with their threat.

Eventually, Asura's paranoia and fear would cause him to yearn to become a Kishin in hopes of attaining power necessary to curb his fear of death. He would began secretly feeding Vajra souls not on Shinigami's list . Having come to fear his weapon partner, Asura then decided to consume Vajra whole within his weapon form to gain the ability to consume souls himself. Though this act seemingly killed Vajra himself, his weapon form remained inside Asura and granted him both its powers and its soul consuming ability necessary for him to become a Kishin.
