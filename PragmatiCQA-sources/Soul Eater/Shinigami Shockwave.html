Shinigami Shockwave (死神衝撃波, Shinigami Shōgekiha ) is a technique that allows the ability to shoot powerful waves of electricity.

Overview

After performing the Katsu (optionally preceding this attack), the user then sends electricity down towards the target. Shinigami Shock Waves was powerful enough to destroy Asura's Wrapping and cause some damage to Asura.
