Overview

Administration

Administration of both the militaristic and schooling side of the academy is ran by the headmaster, in which he is assisted by instructors of the school. As a school, the academy has various rules in which it's students follow less they risk varying punishment and/or expulsion in other cases. Exceptions are rarely made, as it is seen as undermining the stability of the entire school.

School

Faculty

Among the DWMA's faculty, instructors in the academy are able to choose their own lessons plans and classes in which they wish to teach though they follow a criteria of being worthwhile subjects of matter by the Headmaster , who can choose to prohibit certain subjects.

Students

Students wanting to join the academy sign an academy agreement in which specifies the rules in which they must follow. Additionally, there's also a trial-based system for more troubled individuals to get them to eventually become actual students. Split between Freshmen, Sophomores, Juniors, and Seniors, ages vary even in the same classes.
