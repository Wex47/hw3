Overview

High-level spells are powerful forms in magic that allow a user to perform great feats, some of it including having access to certain magic and spells capable of trapping even true gods ( Independent Cube ) and having powerful camouflaging abilities ( Soul Protect ). However, high-level spells also come with critical weaknesses, being far more difficult to cast and some spells and magic may even limit the ability of a witch in performing magic.

Certain high-level spells also may be limited in how long it may last, Death revealing some high-level spells only able to last at an hour maximum.
