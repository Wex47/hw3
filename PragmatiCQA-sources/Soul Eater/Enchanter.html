Overview

With the use of magic to knead and shape soul through their gloves, they're known for being capable of creating and programming golems . However, enchanters can also knead and shape other things such as snow. The most well known location of enchanters is Loew Village , a small town in which 80%of the people living are enchanters.

Equipment

  • Enchanter's gloves : Enchanters utilize large, brown gloves imbued with magic to knead and shape soil for their purposes.
  • Golem : Golem are large creations made by enchanters programmed and used to protect clients and ward off evil.

Synopsis

Trial Enrollment arc

After Arachne 's revival, many enchanters were seen collaborating and being one of the various fighting force employed for Arachnophobia during their battles with the powerful organization known as Death Weapon Meister Academy .
