An evil person (悪人, akunin ; also " evil people ") is an individual whose soul harbors true evil and as such, are added upon Shinigami's List .

Overview

As mention before, those under this classification contain a significant level of evil intent to the point their souls can be hunted. Unlike the souls of the innocent, consumption of it does not gain as much power nor does it risk turning one into a Kishin .

Although most of the personalities of Evil People reflect this, some also possess more noble and positive traits that makes them open for redemption dependent on the individual (i.e Mifune and Masamune ).

Trivia

  • Although evil people exist within the anime of Soul Eater , all evil people instead have a kishin egg and can, thus, be seen as evil by users of any sort of soul-sensing abilities .
