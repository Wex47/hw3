Spatial Magic: Forwarding Vision (空間魔法・ 映像転送 ( フォワーディング・ビジョン ) , Kūkan Mahō Fowādingu Bijon ) is a spatial magic from the demon eye involving using space for illusions.

Overview

This magic enables the user to project a vision of themselves into another location, creating a hologram. The hologram cannot be touched, nor can the hologram touch others; in addition, the movement made by the hologram is also an image of the actual movement of a user.
