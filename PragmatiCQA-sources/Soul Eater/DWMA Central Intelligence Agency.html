DWMA Central Intelligence Agency (死武専中央情報局, Shibusen Chūō Jōhōkyoku ) is a branch of Death Weapon Meister Academy dedicated to developing countermeasures for matters caused by witches .

History

Coming into contact with House Yngling after Princess Anastasia ran away from her home country to Death City , an agreement was set up between the family and the agency to protect Anastasia and return her to her home country should danger befall the face of the royal family.

Overview

As mentioned above, the intelligence branch of the DWMA is responsible for investigations and developing countermeasures against the threat of witches . Members of this branch consist of EAT students and non-student two-star mesiter and weapons . Meisters within this branch are considered highly-skilled.

Resources

DWMA's Central Intelligence has some technology and demon tools in which they utilize. Some of these includes:

  • Incom (隠会無, Inkamu ): DWMA CIA agents possess incredible, custom made headsets. Seeming standard for active field agents to have, the demon tool is noted for the amount of clarity it possess. However, it can be jammed by a powerful madness wavelength .
  • Flack Jackets : Members of the DWMA's intelligence agency wear flack jackets during missions for protections.
  • Sniper Rifles : Some members of the intelligence agency, notably the sniper team, possess sniper rifles.
  • Binoculars : Some members carry binoculars.
  • Combat Knife : For close range combat, members of the agency also carry combat knives.

Trivia

  • Seemingly, the DWMA-CIA were first shown in Chapter 44 unnamed; although their affiliation wasn't outright expressed, the meisters and weapons present in the background of both Chapter 44 and Chapter 45 were under the command of both Sid and Mira with Death the Kid also present, whom was expressed to be responsible for investigating witches in place of his father as a grim reaper in the Soul Eater Super Guidebook: How to Make a Death Scythe . However, the same background characters that appeared in anime were instead expressed to be within the anime-exclusive DWMA's Defense Squad .
