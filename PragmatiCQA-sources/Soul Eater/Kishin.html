A kishin (鬼神, Kishin ; FUNimation "Afreet") is a being created from the over-hunting of a large amount of pure, good people human souls and possess dangerous, destructive power.

History

Birth of the first Kishin

It is known that around 800 years prior, the Eight Shinigami Legions were on a mission to sustain the state of peace within the world, and continued to put in practice the law that Meisters are only allowed to hunt the souls of beings on Shinigami's List. However, at one point, a Meister came to face an opponent in a battle and he thought he was going to die. Afraid of this fact, he wanted more power so that he could overcome his fear. He then succumbed to breaking this law, and took the soul of a good human and foreseeing the path of an Afreet by secretly feeding his Weapon partner good human souls. Asura then became known as the first generation Afreet.

Prevention of another Kishin

After the events of Death becoming wary of Asura's actions, he stripped Asura of his skin and sealed his soul and body within it by making a sack. Death did this to contain all of the Madness that the Kishin Asura emitted, as well as his soul and body. As a sacrificial consequence, Death had to implant his soul into the same spot that he made the seal, making his movement limited to what later became to known as Death City . Death then creates the Death Weapon Meister Academy , a school for young Meisters and Demon Weapons to train together and enforce the purpose of preventing the rebirth of a Afreet.

Overview

Considered a threat capable of destroying the world, a kishin is created when either a demon weapon or a ultimate weapon meister type (as a meister needs to have a weapon's absorb ability) manages to consume a exorbitant number of souls. The number of souls required is unknown though the amount was high enough to the extent that Crona wasn't showing any signs of becoming a kishin according to Franken Stein and Medusa's agenda changed to revive the Asura instead.

However, "BREW" is also capable of turning a individual into a kishin (i.e Crona), though the conditions are unknown. Tuning one's self to a present kishin by absorbing them into their being with black blood can also create a kishin.

Those who become a kishin gain a third eye on the center of their head.

Special Abilities

  • Madness Wavelength (狂気の波長, Kyōki no hachō ): Kishins possess this particular wavelength and the capability to wreck havoc onto the world and put it towards potential annihilation, this extreme level of power requires a true death god to intervene. Due to this high level, their madness wavelength also gives birth to unique forms of madness (i.e Madness of Boobs ).
  • Absorb (吸収, Kyūshū ): Whether through being a demon weapon or a ultimate weapon meister type, kishins possess the ability of consuming souls.

Weaknesses

In the anime , kishins are susceptible to the most powerful variant of the great traditional techniques of the scythe-meister , the kishin-hunt .
