The Demon Twin Guns (二丁魔拳銃, Nichō Makenjū ) are the weapon transformations of sisters, Elizabeth Thompson and Patricia Thompson .

Appearance

The Demon Twin Guns appear as aa silver-themed . The handle-grip is coloured black and has a small Death mask on it. On the slide assembly are three darker silver triangles next to each other.

Special Abilities

Wavelength Compression : According to Liz, The Demon Twin Guns do not fire regular bullets. Instead, the weapon form has the ability to compress the wavelength of their Meister and fire shots of it towards an opponent. It's power depends on the Meister using the weapon form.
