The Remedial Lessons Arc is the second story arc in the Soul Eater series .

With both Weapon Meister teams having failed to maintain proper soul collecting ability, Death comes up with a remedial lesson in which Maka , Soul , Black☆Star , and Tsubaki must investigate the mysterious resurrection of former teacher Sid Barrett as a Zombie and who is behind it. In the aftermath of the lesson, Kid and the Thompson Sisters join Death Weapon Meister Academy and have their first day.

Notable Instances

  • Maka Albarn awakens her Soul Perception for the first time.
  • Maka and Soul perform Witch-Hunt for the first time but failed.
  • Death the Kid joins Death Weapon Meister Academy . After "losing" his fight with Black☆Star and Soul, he was bed ridden for a week and sent to counseling after recovering.
  • Sid Barrett re-establishes himself within Death Weapon Meister Academy and DWMA Central Intelligence Agency after having been gone for roughly a year.
  • Franken Stein finally joins the DWMA upon Death's insistence after having been tried by Sid to join the academy as an instructor during the events of Soul Eater Not! . In the next arc, we learn his purpose for being called back to the academy is to stop the Demon Sword and it's master .

Manga and Anime Differences

  • There is no mentioning of the aftermath of Kid's battle with Soul and Black☆Star in the anime.
