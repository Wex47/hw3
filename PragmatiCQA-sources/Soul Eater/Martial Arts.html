Overview

Capable users of hand-to-hand combat are able to utilize punches, kicks, elbows, knees, and grappling techniques to physically assault an opponent. Despite the naming, it may also include users of melee weapons.

Martial Arts

Martial arts (武道, Budō ; also translated as "Martial Way") are codified systems and traditions of combat practices, which are practiced for a variety of reasons: self-defense, competition, physical health and fitness, entertainment, as well as mental, physical, and spiritual development.

Specialists

Several known people and groups specialize in hand-to-hand and/or fields of martial arts:

  • House Yngling (Practioners of royal martial arts )
  • Hoshi Family Dojo & Star Clan (Practioners of old ways )
  • Mifune (Infamous user of the Infinite One-Sword Style )
