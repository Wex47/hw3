Overview

Spawning a powerful shield a shape depending on the user's given theme, the user is able to block powerful attacks. In the anime of Soul Eater and Battle Resonance video-game , however, the Death Block can quickly become an offensive attack. By spawning it and controlling the shield, the user can crush his enemies with the attack. By spawning it, the force of Death Block is also capable of sending opponents back.
