The Witches in the East (東の魔女, Azuma no majo ) were a witch faction once opposing the Eight Reaper Legions .

Cultural References

The "Witches of the East" possibly alludes to the Witch of the East from The Wizard of Oz , as well as that book's many adaptations in popular culture.

History

For reasons yet specified, the Witches in the East came into conflict with the immensely powerful Eight Reaper Legions . However, they were later eradicated by Asura , whom committed this act alone without his fellow warlords.

Overview

Much of the information on the Witches of the East is unknown, though whatever their ideals and/or objective warranted the attention of the Eight Reaper Legions . In addition, while their level of power is mostly unknown but below that of Asura, Death mentioned that witches around this time period had magic that caused far more damage than in the present time.
