The pull of magic is the witch destructive instinct directly a result from the usage of Magic . This instinct is the consequence of magic due to it's power, in which often leads to destruction. However, the kind of "destruction" for each individual varies. Among witches, while some are prone towards outward destructive tendencies, others are more incline towards more "subtle" destructive tendencies such as emotional destruction.

Despite those who innately manipulate magic are subjected to this instinct, Death the Kid noted that those with this instinct can value order and are not fundamentally incompatible with beings of order such as Reapers and aren't true beings of destruction.
