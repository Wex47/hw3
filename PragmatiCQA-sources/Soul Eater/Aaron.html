Aaron (アーロン, Aron ) is a shy demon weapon enrolled in the N.O.T Class at Death Weapon Meister Academy .

Personality

He is described as being dazed and shy, having not decided on a meister partner on account of the latter. His only known response in conversation thus far is a nonchalant "Haah".

Appearance

Aaron is a lanky boy with short, light, sandy brown hair that completely obscures his eyes. He is always seen wearing shinigami skull headphones and a long-sleeved pale green shirt with an archer's bow emblem and dark pants. During gym, he wears a similar outfit but the shirt is short-sleeved.

Special Abilities

Full-Weapon Transformation (全身武器, Zenshin Buki ): As a demon weapon, Aaron possess the potential of fully transforming into a weapon.

  • Weapon Mode (武器モード, Buki Mōdo ): Aaron is capable of taking the form of a bow.

Weaknesses

Aaron has poor control over his weapon powers and as such, is often injured from sudden and odd transformations.
