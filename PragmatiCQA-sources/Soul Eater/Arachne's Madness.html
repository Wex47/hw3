Arachne's madness (アラクネの狂気, Arakune no Kyōki ) is a madness type originating from the madness wavelength of the witch, Arachne Gorgon .

Overview

Those under the effect of this madness feel painful sensation within their heads, described by Kilik Rung as spiders inside his head that are ripping it apart and begin losing their sanity.

Limitations

However, there are some limitation to this particular madness; Individuals like Harvar D. Éclair , who is described as being 'hardheaded all the time', and those possessing the Anti-Demon Wavelength are immune to this madness. Black☆Star and some of the DWMA forces showed that willpower can resist this madness. In addition, Noah (Greed) , most likely due to being an Icon and created from the Book of Eibon , which carries the madness of a Great Old One , felt this madness as some sort of " refreshing breeze ".
