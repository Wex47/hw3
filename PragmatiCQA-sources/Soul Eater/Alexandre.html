Alexandre (アレクサンドル, Arekusandoru ) is the meister to Dengu Dinga , the Death Scythe in charge of Africa.

Personality

Being a polite individual, he's selfless, as he was willing to sacrifice his own life for DWMA. He is also capable of taking his superiors advice for inspiration such as being inspired by Spirit to live on and fight rather than embracing death for a worthy cause and willing to protect comrades such as Death the Kid .

Appearance

Alexandre is completely bald and wears white war paint in the shape of a keyhole on his face, with none of his partner's piercings. He wears a black sleeveless vest over his chest with various bandages wrapped around part of the right side. A version of Death 's old skull mask can be seen on his belt, and wears two bangles. Like his partner, he also sports a skirt with hieroglyphs, on which another black skirt is, and has four black lines tattooed on his right shoulder.

Power

As a death scythe's meister , Alexandre is considered one of Death Weapon Meister Academy 's more proficient meisters.

While his exact standing is unknown in terms of skill, he is inferior to both Franken Stein and Black☆Star due to the both of them being seen as Death Weapon Meister Academy 's most proficient meisters. His skill is also inferior to that of Feodor , whose battle prowess in combat was stated to be on the same level as Black☆Star .

Special Abilities

Meister (職人, Shokunin ): Alexandre is a proficient Meister skilled with an axe. He was able to fend off and kill countless Clowns during the battle on the Moon despite feeling fatigued over time from their regenerative abilities. He also was skilled enough to battle against the Galoot Clown despite its superior size.

Wavelength Control (波長コントロール, Hachō Kontorōru ): Per being a Meister, Alexandre is capable of controlling his own Soul Wavelength.

  • Soul Resonance (魂の共鳴, Tamashī no Kyōmei ): Alexandre is capable of sending his wavelength into a Demon Weapon , allowing it to amplify it and send it back, and then repeat the process to perform specialized techniques.

Enhanced Conditioning : Alexandre was capable of fighting the Clown Army alongside his fellow DWMA comrades for long periods of time.

Enhanced Speed : Alexandre was fast enough to move and leap into the air to use Light Refraction against lasers from Moonlight despite the lasers having moved first prior.

Enhanced Agility : He is shown to be able to leap incredible distances into the air.

Equipment

Rainbow Axe (虹の斧, Niji no Ono ): With the Rainbow Axe on hand, Alexandre is capable of using its form to cut opponents and utilize light-based techniques.

  • Rainbow (レインボー, Reinbō ): A powerful overhead swing, resulting in the creation of a distinctive rainbow-like trail that follows the axe-head's previous movement. This semi-circular arc not only allows the normally blunt axe blade to actually cut a target, but it also greatly increases the cutting area of the axe itself, as the arc is significantly wider than the original axe-head.
  • Light Refraction (光屈折, Kōkussetsu ): A defensive, Soul Resonance technique used to bend/alter light itself. It can be used to alter the trajectory of incoming beam-like attacks. However, the technique can only protect one point at a time.

Synopsis

War on the Moon

Alexandre flew with an army of DWMA soldiers to the moon. As they approached, the airship was attacked by the clown, Moonlight . With being attacked from all sides and his weapon wanting to protect the ship, Alexandre performed a soul resonance with his weapon and repelled the attacks. However, the clown then attempted to hit the backside, though he failed.

The DWMA eventually landed on the moon and began fighting against the clowns. Together with Zubaidah and Jinn Galland, Alexandere and Dengu fought against a giant clown. By means of the soul resonance Rainbow, they could cut off a leg and the clown was then finished off by Zubaidah and Jinn 's soul resonance attack. However, the clown would then be revived.

Witch Trial

As the Airship was taking off back to Death City , it came under attack, and Alexandre used his weapon's capabilities to defend the airship.
