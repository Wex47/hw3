Vector Plate (ベクトルプレート, Bekutoru Purēto ) is a Snake spell relating to the use of plating and force.

Overview

According to Medusa, this spell allows her to create a plate in which throws the subject in the direction in which the plate is facing. This spell can be performed in midair in addition.
