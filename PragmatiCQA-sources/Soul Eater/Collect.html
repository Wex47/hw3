Collect (回収, Kaishū ; also "Soul Collect") is an exclusive Shinigami Power the Reaper involving the collection of souls.

Overview

Because of their nature, a Reaper is able to collect the souls of other beings and store it within their body without actually consuming it. It can be collected physically, in which the Reaper holds it within their hand and simply collect it. It's also heavily implied it can be collected in a mass, though was interrupted before the process could be seen.
