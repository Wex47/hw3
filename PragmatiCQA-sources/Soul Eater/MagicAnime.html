Overview

A extremely powerful and potent energy, Magic is seemingly nearly limitless in quantity and possessed by a select few races (i.e Witches and Wizards ). When performing magic, select individuals also utilize personal chants to invoke the effects.

Magic Techniques

Magic techniques (魔法技術, Mahō gijutsu ) are special techniques that (usually) operate under a specific type of magic. Dependent on the magic type, a magic technique can be used for offensive, defensive, collaborative, and other sort of purposes.
