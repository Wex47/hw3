A Kishin Egg (also Afreet Egg ) is a soul type which an evil individual gains from acts of evil and runs the risk of becoming a Kishin .

The Kishin Egg replaced the Shinigami's List concept and is exclusive to the anime of Soul Eater and its affiliated video games, Soul Eater: Monotone Princess , Soul Eater: Battle Resonance , and Soul Eater: Medusa's Conspiracy .

Overview

Individuals who become Kishin eggs enter the first stage of becoming a Kishin . Due to their crimes, their souls becomes corrupt. The soul appears to be a dark hue red, the tail appearing to be a much more light and brighter red color. The middle of the soul is purple and surrounded by hexagonal plates. However, undergoing through the process makes one lose their sanity and even humanity.

Although it's been stated by Death it that only demon weapons can consume souls such as Kishin eggs, , it appears that any individual are capable of eating souls and transform into a Kishin Eggs such as Al Capone and his gang.

Demon Weapons wishing to become a Death Scythe must eat 99 Kishin eggs along with one witch soul in order to become one. However, Kishin souls that are not consumed are seen buried into the Death Room .
