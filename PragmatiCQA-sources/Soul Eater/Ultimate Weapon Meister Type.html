The Ultimate Weapon Meister Type (究極武器と職人の形, Kyūkyoku Buki to Shokunin no Katachi ) is a unique classification type in which describes a meister and weapon merged into one.

Overview

When a weapon and meister are engineered to join one another, this creates a new form of bonding that allows for the meister to gain the special abilities usually associated with demons weapons, namely the ability to absorb souls.

There are only limited methods to achieving this type of bond; only through the use of black blood and a demon weapon being melted from it and implanted into a meister. Alternatively, the strange physiology of a Reaper can somehow merge a weapon within their own being, adopting their weapon abilities as well as use them in battle though this process seemingly kills the weapon.
