Soul Menace (魂威, Kon'i ) is an advanced, meister technique in which users attack with their soul wavelength .

Overview

Contrasting to the usual usage of a demon weapon to amplify one's wavelength to attack, Soul Menace is an attack which allows the user to strike their opponent by channeling their own wavelength without the usage of a weapon. This results in an electrical-like attack with powerful concussive force capable of even damaging a weapon in their weapon form.

This technique can also be used underwater, which is more powerful to the point it produces a powerful linear shockwave from one hand if performed by a proficient user.
