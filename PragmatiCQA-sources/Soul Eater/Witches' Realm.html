The Witches' Realm (魔女世界, Majo Sekai ) is a sealed Space contained by Queen Maba 's Spatial Magic as well as the home to the Witches and the Witch Order .

Overview

Entering the Witches' Realm is a closely guarded secret, with members of the Witch Order prohibited from showing the way of entry towards any non-witch individual as a rule. The only way into the Witches' Realm is through a Ritual in which the youngest witch (or multiple witches) use "butt-writing" to write "Witch" in hiragana to connect to the world's "channel" and open a gate.

Appearance

The Witches' Realm appears to have a dark and somewhat glum atmosphere, being covered in various buildings in disrepair, though it follows various witch themes. A large castle can be seen in the realm and it seemingly appears to always be night.

Key Locations

Named

  • Witch Prison : Located within a deep, dark forest somewhere in the Witch's Realm is where the Witch Prison resides in. Those who have broken laws made in the Witch's Realm are sent here to serve their sentence.
  • Dark Forest : The Witches' Realm seems to be surrounded by a forest. The trees within the forest are dead and it keeps true to the glum atmosphere seen in the dimension. It also seems to be covered in snow. Whether or not this is because the realm also had its own seasons is unknown.
  • Witches' Courtroom : Witch trials are held inside an undisclosed courtroom within the Witch's Realm. The ceiling appears to be based upon spider webs, with various animals, caged or not, inside the room as well. Being a court, there are seating on both sides and a judge's panel in front. Maba, when participating in a trial, takes a seat on the judge's right side. However, it is seen that the back part of the courtroom has no roof, with the walls in serious disrepair.
