Spatial Magic (空間魔法, Kūkan Mahō ) is a form of magic involving the warping of space.

  • 1 Overview
    • 1.1 Weaknesses
  • 2 List of techniques

Overview

This magic allows its user to warp space for a variety of purposes. Part of its usage requires a user to have a level of "control" over the placement of spells. As such, spatial magic sometimes can being assisted by calculation spells . While it can be cast through willpower, spatial magic can also be used by utilizing specific pentagrams.

Weaknesses

This magic along with its spells is known to be difficult to cast onto a area full of madness from a user's madness wavelength as well as being difficult to utilize on a Great Old One . Special magical bindings can also inhibit the capability of performing spatial magic.
