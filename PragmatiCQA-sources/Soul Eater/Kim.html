Personality

Befitting her theme as a tanuki witch, Kim is initially shown to value money and compensation and is remarked to be rather intimidating. According to Eternal Feather , a victim of her manipulation at her insecurities in her appearance, her nasty personality combined with her misleading appearance earned her the nickname "Witch of the Girls' Dorm" by her classmates at Death Weapon Meister Academy . Additionally, she is a lax individual and can appear insensitive to others, often insulting others with seemingly little regard for how her words affect another, especially notable in her initial treatment of Jacqueline due to her "hardass" personality and strict manner.

However, much of her nasty personality is somewhat of a facade in order to keep others at a distance in order to better hide the fact she is a true witch , utterly terrified at the perceived treatment she will receive should anyone find out. This fear was strong enough to incline her to cut off her long hair (something she had pride in) and dress as a tomboy in an attempt to keep others away. Due to her nature of using regeneration magic, lacking the usual witches' pull of magic because of it, and the suffering that came from it, Kim is conflicted with her own nature as a witch and only truly comes to terms with it after returning to the DWMA upon Ox Ford 's insistence of DWMA accepting her nature as a witch.

Due to her close friendship with Jacqueline overtime, forging friendship with others like Tsugumi Harudori , Ox Ford 's eventual optimism in her acceptance in DWMA regardless of her being a witch, much of her true personality manifests: one that shows compassion for others, especially to friends and dogs. Though her attachment to money and wealth remained as well as her general shameless manipulation (such as by creating lies and/or fake crying to make it more believable according to Death the Kid), the latter became more done for smaller and even well-intended reasons. She also began showing off more of her feminine side such as loving to draw light-hearted and "cute" drawings and interest in wearing Deathbucks Café 's maid outfit. As a young witch, she is also prone to using terminology used by witches such as "witchin'" (similar to the word kidding) and vulgar terms like "bull-witch". S

Comically, Tsugumi remarked that Kim is more easily teased than she gives off and is utterly embarrassed at showing off her feminine traits initially. It's also shown that abandonment for a small, harmless period of time makes her also more attached to others upon reuniting with them, Jacqueline remarking that it made her "heart grow fonder".

Special Abilities

Magic (魔法, Mahō ): As a tanuki witch, Kim is inherently capable of performing and manipulating magic at will. She's shown the be capable of transferring her own magic into another at will, an ability that can awaken special powers of people such as earth shamans .

  • Regeneration Magic (再生魔法, Saisei Mahō ): Kim admits that this type of magic is her forte; this type of magic allows her to heal herself or other individuals of mortally serious injuries within the span of seconds. In addition to this, this magic doesn't heal only physical ailments but also reverts certain things back to their original state, such as repairing the a tear of her shirt or the tearing of one's head (though they cannot shampoo their head for another two or three days).
  • Calculation Spells (演算魔法, Enzan Mahō ): Kim is capable of performing certain Calculation Spells. This allows her to place "coordinates" to assist in various magical feats such as transporting Spartoi into the Book of Eibon via it's maniscript.
  • Sexy Magic (色気魔法, Iroke Mahō ): Kim is also skilled in the usage of sexy nagic. This allows her to increase the apparent sex appeal of an individual to heighten levels, causing those around them to consider them more attractive.

Hand-to-Hand Combat ( 体術 ( たいじゆつ ) , Taijutsu ): Kim's weapon of choice is primary a demon lamp , capable of using her own partner's transformation to fend of elite unites within Arachnophobia . With the Change "Pixie" form, she can use it to ride the form like that of a broomstick, befitting her nature as a witch. She also has great unarmed skills; Kim was able to easily take down Misery with a single kick to her head. Receiving further training by Mira Naigus , she also was able to at least hold her own against the likes of Patty Thompson, who's surprisingly skilled in hand-to-hand combat enabled her to beat the likes of Ox Ford before hand. However, once defeated, Kim even admitted herself that Patty is "way out of my league".

Transformation (変身, Henshin ): As a tanuki witch, her theme is that of a tanuki (raccoon dog) and as such, is capable of transforming into said animal.

Con Artist : Kim is a skilled con artist, able to forge both documents and other people's signature and hand writing as well as use her looks to her advantage.

Intimidation : Kim possess a personality that makes her seem intimidating to others during her tenure in the N.O.T Class , earning her the nickname "Witch of the Girls' Dorm".

Weaknesses

Although Kim possesses healing magic that allows her to recover from fatal injuries and maintain her youthful appearance, she could conceivably be killed in combat if she was unable to use magic on her injuries in time. Her magic can be limited by the extent of the injury to heal, as even multiple applications of regeneration magic could not completely remove deep scars such as the one inflicted by Mifune onto Black☆Star .

According to Harvar D. Éclair , Kim is not as mobile as a meister; a demon weapon competent enough in physical combat can contend with Kim.

Equipment

Demon Lamp (魔角灯, Ma Kakutō ): When partnered with Jacqueline, Kim is capable of using her Demon Lamp transformation. With it, she can create explosive blasts of fire that can incinerate her targets by pulling back it's chain and aiming the weapon at her opponents. She can also use the Demon Lamp in a chain weapon-like manner.

  • Change "Pixie" (C H A N G E 「P I X Y」, Chenji "Pikushī" ): A transformation in which Kim attaches a collapsible bar and Jacqueline extends it into the length more reminiscent of a pole-arm weapon or broomstick. This allows Kim the capability of taking flight similar to a witch with their flying broomstick .

List of techniques

  • Magic Calculation (マジックカリキュレーション, Majikku Karikureshon ): This spell allows Kim to place "coordinates" and allow for a vareity of effects. With assistance from Eruka Frog , Tabatha Butterfly , and Taruho Firefly , she was able to help transport Spartoi into the Book of Eibon and place the coordinates to aim the Forwarding Protection spell at the Clowns.
  • Volantern (ヴォルランタン, Vorurantan ): A technique in which after swinging the Demon Lamp at a target and it makes contact, a powerful, close-range explosion is created as a result.

Operation: Capture Baba Yaga's Castle

After Kim's witch heritage is discovered by Shibusen, she flees with Jacqueline in fear. In reality the school had full intention to hear her out but the two men sent to approach her acted in a too hostile manner, provoking her into running. Kim and her weapon then become allied with the group Arachnophobia, a group that serves the witch Arachne . It was here that they were betrayed and forced to suffer the Morality Manipulation Machine.

During the Shibusen attack on Arachnophobia's base, Kim is confronted by Ox, who risks his life to break Kim free from the brainwash caused by the machine. When Ox breaks his antenna-like hairstyle (which he openly states is intentional) he gets through to her, offering to create a place where she can go. She now seems to reciprocate Ox's feelings. They both then searched out and found Kilik but before they could proceed any further the group were severely affected by Arachne's insanity, which had spread throughout the castle. Thanks to the actions of Maka and Soul, they were saved and able to aid in the conclusion of the assault.

Tying Things Up, and New Beginnings

Due to the preceding circumstances; Kim, with her hair newly cut shorter then before, is summoned to Shinigami's room alongside Jacqueline, Tsubaki, and Angela. Sid and Shinigami discuss the issue of what they should do with the two witches. Sid notes that Kim's regeneration magic is in conflict with the natural destructive magic, known as the witches sway of magic and is unlikely to be influenced; and that Angela is still too young for the sway of magic to have taken any hold, but it is no guarantee that she will not become a threat as she grows older. Horrified by this, Kim proposes that, if she were to periodically expose Angela to her regenerative magic, she might be able to suppress Angela's destructive instincts. She also proposes that, if that does not work, there is the option of using the Moral Manipulation Machine, brought back from Baba Yaga's Castle, on Angela.

Without much thought, Shinigami agrees to let Angela stay.

Kim is introduced in the middle of sparring with Patti—who is in her fifth match in a row—but is both quickly and easily defeated by her. Kim, along with her partner are seen to have become apart of the new Shibusen Elite Unit " Spartoi ".

When she subsequently starts arguing with Soul, Kim considers leaving but realizes her duty is to accompany them back. Although, it doesn't take much convincing afterward to provoke her into returning without the others.

Kim is one of the witches (alongside Risa and Arisa) that assists Eruka in her Arithmetic Magic spell in order to transfer members of Spartoi into the Book of Eibon. Later, she hears from Ox that Tezca Tlipoca has died, and is visibly worried for her peers.

Trivia

  • Kim's animal theme as a witch is the tanuki, a rare species of canid indigenous to Japan and which resembles a raccoon. Kim's animal theme suits her personality: the tanuki is portrayed in Japanese folklore to be associated with wealth, gold and money. Likewise, Kim is also very attached to money. Also, tanuki in mythology are said to have healing powers and transformation abilities, both of which are associated with Kim. Like tanukis, Kim also enjoys dining on omelets.
  • Despite having forged her background to get into the DWMA, Sid Barrett and the DWMA Intelligence Agency had already suspected of her being a young witch slipping into the student background. It is implied, however, that they awaited to see her intentions.
