Overview

For a weapon to become a death scythe, a demon weapon must capture 99 evil human souls that are on Shinigami's List and one witch soul.

Special Abilities

Death's weapons often get some variation of the same abilities in which include:

  • Magical-Like Powers (魔法的な力, Mahō Tekina Chikara ): Advanced variant of wavelength control gained from the consumption of the witch soul that enables for special abilities that resembles magic .
  • Shapeshifting ( 形体変化 ( けい たいへん ) , Kei Taihen ): Shapeshifting allows a death Weapon to change the shape of their weapon form. This ability is noted as not only a standard trait but also one of the most basic and can give rise to more unique techniques and abilities.
