The Demon Eye (悪魔の眼, Akuma no Me ) is the left eye of the Witch Queen, Mabaa .

Overview

This magic eye allows the user to cast magic once exclusive to Mabaa . In addition to that, it carries its own magical abilities. The demon eye, however, has finite energy and must go through a charging period before being able to perform spells.
