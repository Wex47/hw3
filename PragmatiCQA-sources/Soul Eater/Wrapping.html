Wrapping (包帯, Hōtai ) is a weapon used by various characters.

Description

Wrappings are weapons controlled by a user that allows them to mold the wrapping into various kinds of forms in a paper-like manner for offensive or defensive means. Depending on the user themselves and it's content (such as being made from elastic skin), wrappings may be extremely durable.
