Soul cocoons are special magic cocoons spun by the Paraponera Clan .

Overview

Spun by only members of the Paraponera Clan (a clan whose main members presumably consisting of ant-themed witches), soul cocoons are capable of extracting a human soul and turning it into a kishin egg after a given amount of time(a feat thought to be impossible by Death Weapon Meister Academy ), accumulating kishin power as a result.
