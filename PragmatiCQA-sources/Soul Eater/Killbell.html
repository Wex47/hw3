Killbell (キルベル, Kiruberu) is a Bloodthirsty Killer who appears in the game, Soul Eater: Monotone Princess . He fights with a bell.

Appearance

Killbell is a man with brown, short hair. He has a yellow eye, which in contrast to his other white eye with no iris or pupil, is wide open. His face looks a little deformed. He has some facial hair. His back is a hump. He has very large hands, but he has short legs. His skin is pale, almost a pale green.

He wears a light green jacket, and, on the front, there is a small bell design in the place of a breast pocket. On the back of this jacket, there is a large bell with 'BELL' written on it. He wears dark blue/cyan pants and black shoes.

Killbell's expanded soul is an empty green outline in the shape of a circle with the canons of a church bell in place of the usual "tail" shape on top.

Personality

Killbell is a murderous person who considers the church he resides in his "terrible territory" and is quick to threaten whoever trespasses into it. He is mainly motivated by his hunger for souls, leading him to attempt to attack Grimoire so he can eat his soul. He also wants to become more powerful, which combined with his obsession with eating souls has lead to him agreeing to help Grimoire in exchange for Soul Cocoons .

Synopsis

Killbell first appeared as an Evil Person that attacks Grimoire while he is gathering humans in his church, claiming the church to be his territory and threatening to eat his soul. Grimoire asks him if he wants to become stronger and offers him a Soul Cocoon, saying that they can allow him to "be reborn" and become more powerful, and that Grimoire can give him more of them if he helps him.

Later, Black☆Star tried to attack Killbell with Tsubaki in the attic of his church, causing the floor to collapse and forcing both of them into the bottom of the church. They fought each other until Maka appeared in the church and Killbell struggled with a bell against them. However, he was killed by Maka's Witch-Hunt .

Special Abilities

Having been empowered through the consumption of Soul Cocoons , Killbell is an extraordinarily powerful human whose strength surpasses the likes of Jack the Ripper and Rasputin , and allows him to content with powerful Meisters on his own.

High Physical Abilities (高い身体能力, Takai karada nōryoku ): Killbell's most notable ability is his heightened physical might and agility. He is capable of contending and overpowering Black☆Star and can take numerous hits from him, including special attacks like Stud☆Star before being taken down.

Versatile Hidden Tactics and Techniques (多才謎戦術とテクニック, Tasai Nazo Senjutsu to tekunikku ): Killbell has some amount of skill using his large bell as a blunt weapon, which he can wield in one hand despite its weight.

Paraphernalia

Bell : Killbell's primary weapon is a massive church bell which he uses as a bludgeon. It can deal critical damage to powerful individuals like Black☆Star.

  • Bell Fallout ( Unnamed ): By expanding his soul, Killbell is able to summon multiple duplicates of it above him and make them fall down on his opponent.

Soul Cocoon : Killbell was given special cocoon-like items from Grimoire which store multiple souls within them. Consuming these Soul Cocoons has granted him immense power compared to a normal Evil Person.

Trivia

  • Killbell is probably based off of the fictional character, Quasimodo from the Hunchback of Notre-Dame.
  • His name is probably a reference to Kill Bill by Quentin Tarantino.
