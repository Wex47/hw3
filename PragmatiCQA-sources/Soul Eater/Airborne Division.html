Airborne Division ( 空工拠所 ( くう ていぶ たい ) , Kūtei Butai ) is a branch of Death Weapon Meister Academy specializing in combat within specialized conditions such as low-gravity.

Overview

As mentioned before, Airborne troops are soldiers able to fight in specialized conditions such as locations with lower gravity. Airborne troops in this division utilize primarily melee weaponry but have access to explosives.
