The Multi-Form Ability is a special and extremely rare ability in which allows a Demon Weapon to possess multiple, different weapon transformation. This trait is exclusive to the Nakatsukasa Clan .

Overview

As expressed by Masamune Nakatsukasa , the Multi-Form Ability allows the user possessing weaponized genes to not only take form of the transformations born to them but also the weapon forms in which their previous ancestors have taken during their time.
