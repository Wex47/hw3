Overview

The NOT Class is the curriculum for non-combatants; Ninety percent of the students in the DWMA make up and follow this curriculum. Most meisters in this curriculum join for varying reasons, including career opportunities.. Demon weapons , however, join in order to control their weapon abilities. As such, the NOT Class's lessons and subjects aim to train demon weapons to control their weapon abilities.

Unlike the EAT Class, the NOT Class has no unique ranking systems and follows a more ordinary school classification of freshmen, sophmore, junior, and senior.

Class subjects

In the NOT Class, Death Weapon Meister Academy offers both general education and physical educations classes that range in varying difficulties with the aim of improve a students and developing a strong mind and body :

  • PE (Physical Education) class : Aimed at improving a meister and weapon's physical abilities, physical education classes are notorious in DWMA for their difficulty. Scheduled during the morning time, students often are known to skip meals to avoid vomitting from the intensive exercises.
  • Soul Studies : Classes and lectures aimed at teaching students about souls and abilities such as wavelength control and soul resonance
  • Practice lessons: On occasion, weapon students in the NOT Class practice perfecting their full-weapon transformation abilities alongside a chosen meister.
  • Summer School sessions : Classes in the summer that focuses on watching and evaluating the growth of weapon students. EAT Class seniors often assist in these sessions.

There are also classes that are exclusive to meisters and demon weapons .

Testing

There are specialized testing in the NOT Class in which include:

  • Athletics Test
  • Midterms Test
  • Weapon Evaluation Test
