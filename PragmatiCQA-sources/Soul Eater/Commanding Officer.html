A commanding officer (指揮官, Shiki Kan ; also translated as "Commander") is a rank denoting a person in command of a unit, typically of a military. In DWMA Central Intelligence Agency , the commanding officer is the chosen leader of the organization. Both Sid Barett and Mira Naigus served as the commanders of the organization.
