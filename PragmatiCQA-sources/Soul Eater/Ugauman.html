Oogeyman (ウギーマン, Ugīman ) is a powerful serial killer who wields a giant axe. He appears as a quest boss in the final chapter of Soul Eater: Monotone Princess .

Appearance

Oogeyman is a somewhat short, obese man with greenish skin and muscular arms. He wears long jeans pants, a white shirt and a jeans apron with two suspenders on his shoulders, and his head is covered in a potato bag, a large piece of which has been torn off and has a piece of white cloth stitched over it. He is prominently seen holding his large, heavy hatchet in one hand.

Special Abilities

High Physical Abilities (高い身体能力, Takai karada nōryoku ): Oogeyman is one of the physically strongest, fastest and most durable bosses in Monotone Princess , and is able of easily handling his very large and heavy axe in one hand. He is capable of easily contending with Meisters on Maka Albarn 's caliber and has an in-game HP value greater than the likes of The Can and DFrefre .

Master Axe-Wielder : Oogeyman uses a giant axe resembling a lumberjack's hatchet as his weapon, and is highly efficient in cutting and slashing with it, allowing him to match and even overpower Maka.

Equipment

Giant Hatchet : Oogeyman carries an oversized axe with him to use as a weapon. The axe is extremely sharp and heavy, as its in-game max attack power exceeds even that of Grimoire and Witch ??? .

Trivia

  • Oogeyman's design and in-game attack patterns closely resemble those of Sonson-J and The Can .
  • Oogeyman appears to be named after the Bogeyman , a name given to the Devil and later a monster in European folklore.
