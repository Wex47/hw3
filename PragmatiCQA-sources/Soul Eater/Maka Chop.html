Maka Chop (マカチョップ, Maka Choppu ) is a punishment techique used by Maka with the usage of a book, most likely inspired by the Sid Chop .

Overview

With a book on hand, the user strikes their intended target from the side, creating a comedic and animated dent into the other as punishment for their antics.
