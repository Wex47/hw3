A Crystal Ball is a magical tool used by Witches for surveillance purposes.

Description

Through the use of a Witch's Magic medium (such as Medusa's Snake ), the user of a crystal ball can conjure an image from a long distance and observe an event as well as also being capable of broadcasting and receiving audio.

In the non canonical Monotone Princess videogame , it is revealed that the Crystal Ball are created from Broken Crystal Pieces and are items that can be collected as part of a quest .
