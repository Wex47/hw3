The Eight Shinigami Legions (死神八武衆, Shinigami Hachi Bushū ) were a group of eight guards led by Death in maintaining world order. They're the predecessors of the Death Scythes .

History

Eight hundred years ago and after the creation of the demon weapons by Arachne Gorgon, the Eight Shinigami Legions were active. Serving under Death , these weapon and meisters collected souls alongside the death god while disallowing the hunting of those not on Death's list .

Eventually, the group came into conflict with the witch faction refereed to as " Witches in the East ". However, Asura had ended up wiping out the entire faction himself, a fact that was later reported to Death and other warlords by an unknown member of the group.

In a quest for power due to being terrified of death, Asura later began secretly feeding his weapon partner the souls of good people . In the end, he succeeded and became the first known kishin in history. Confronting the traitorous warlord, Asura battled Death and was not only defeated but sealed away in a bag made of his own skin containing his soul and madness underneath the land that would become Death City .

Overview

Like their successor, Death Weapon Meister Academy , they hunted the souls of evil people to protect the world and prevent the uprising of a kishin and kept a consistent rule throughout their tenure: the souls of innocent people were not allowed to be hunted.

The group consisted most of powerful beings known as the Great Old Ones , whose madness wavelength pull men and women into madness from their very existence. Although members of the group were renown (i.e Eibon & Excalibur ), the strongest and best of the warlords of the squad was said to be Asura .
