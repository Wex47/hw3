Sonson-J (ソンソンJ, Sonson Jei ) was an Evil Person whom was a target on Shinigami's List and infamously known as the " Bloodthirsty Killer of Emerald Lake ".

Appearance

Sonson wore a t-shirt, with "13th" printed in red both on the upper left of the front and the entire back of the shirt, paired with dark pants. His face is never seen as it is always covered by a brown paper bag, with roughly cut-out eye holes. He also appears holding a large knife with blade facing downwards, both features add to his serial killer appearance. This kind of knife is known as a machete.

Personality

Not much is known of Sonson's personality but he is shown to be extremely bloodthirsty and shows no remorse at all for his victims, even going as far as to complain that it's disgusting and not fair that only Demon Weapons and their Meisters get to kill as much as they want.

Synopsis

Demon Sword arc

Sonson was a killer, who would target victims at Emerald Lake. Being an Evil Person , he was added to Shingami's list. Maka and Soul were assigned to Florence to hunt down his soul as an extra assignment. After Maka lists his felony, he sobs about Meisters and Demon Weapons being free to kill as much as they please. Maka easily cuts him in two and states that it's not true that they are able to kill whatever they want.

Trivia

  • Sonson J. could be seen as a parody of Jason Voorhees, the famous serial killer from the slasher film series Friday the 13th .
