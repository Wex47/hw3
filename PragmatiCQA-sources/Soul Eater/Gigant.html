The Gigants (ギガント, Giganto ) are special, ant-like Kishin creatures born from the Paraponera Clan 's special cocoons containing Human souls.

Overview

Resembling ant-like mechanical beings, Gigants are born from pure Human souls within the Paraponera Clan's cocoons. Gigants take around 4-6 days to develop, in which the Human souls are turned into Kishin Eggs during the process and attain Kishin Powers . Being consider Kishins , they also possess Kishin Egg souls.

Although considered Kishins , they are not individually as powerful as the likes of the first generation Kishin, Asura .

Special Abilities

Mechanical Physiology : Gigants possess a unique body, their skin being metallic in nature.

  • Enhanced Durability : Gigants can withstand certain attacks from Demon Weapon forms such as the Tsubaki Mode Change: Chain Scythe transformation.
  • Spinning Attack : A Gigant is able to curl its body into a spinning top-like form to attack its foes at high speed.

Kishin Powers (鬼神の力, Kishin no Chikara ): As artificial Kishins, Gigants possess a formidable level of Kishin Power. This kind of energy can be used by Gigants offensively. Their Kishin Power is characterized by a red aura and floating, magical text.

  • Wavelength Compression (波長圧縮, Hachō Asshuku ): Gigants can compress their wavelength into a series of bullets they spit out from their mouths.
  • Laser (レーザ, Rēza ): Gigants are able to surround themselves with circles of magical runes before firing laser beams from the top of their body, which rotate around them and cause destruction around them.
  • Shield (シールド, Shīrudo ): Gigants can create a wall of red energy in front of them, also surrounding themselves with floating orbs of light and creating Magic Circles on the floor while doing so.
