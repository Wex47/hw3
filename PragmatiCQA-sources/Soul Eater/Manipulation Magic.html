Manipulation Magic (操作系の魔法, Sōsakei no Mahō ) is magic that allows a form of mind control .

Overview

This magic allows the user to grant control over another with control over their victim's will to follow their command through the use of elements of mind control such as poison and strange chanting. Usage of this magic requires the caster to be nearby.
