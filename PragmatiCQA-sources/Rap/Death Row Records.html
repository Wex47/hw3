DEATH ROW RECORDS SOUTH Death Row Records was a record label that was founded in 1991 by Dr Dre and Suge Knight, and was once home to some of West coast hip hop's most well-known Rappers, including 2pac, Dr.Dre, Snoop Dog , and Tha Dogg Pound. Death Row Records went bankrupt after a big lawsuit against Suge Knight. The Death Row Records trademark expired and wasn’t renewed. Damien "D'aveli" Vivona & Julie "Mia" Gonzalez then took over the name in 2006 and added “South”. They restarted the notorious Death Row Records and now they own Death Row Records South. With a new roster that includes such artists as Tha Social Club, Slick Gambino and Colby red they will take the label into the next millinuem....
