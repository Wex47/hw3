DJ Yella is the stage name of Antoine Carraby (born December 11, 1967), a DJ , music producer , and film director from Compton , California . He was a member of the World Class Wreckin' Cru along with Dr. Dre . He later became a founding member of the pioneering gangsta rap group N.W.A (comprised of Yella, Dr. Dre, Ice Cube , MC Ren , and Eazy-E ). Along with Dre, he produced Eazy-E's debut album Eazy-Duz-It and all three N.W.A albums.
