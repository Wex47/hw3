Kurupt (born Ricardo Emmanuel Brown ) is an American rapper and former Executive Vice President of Death Row Records . He is a member of the rap group Tha Dogg Pound , and is mostly known for his work with Death Row Records .

Also known as Young Gotti and The Kingpin , he is one half of the rap duo Tha Dogg Pound along with Daz Dillinger . He is also a member of underground supergroup The HRSMN .
