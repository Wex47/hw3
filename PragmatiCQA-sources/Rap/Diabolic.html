Sean George , better known as Diabolic , is an American political underground hip hop artist from Huntington Station, Long Island , New York . Diabolic raps about politics in the United States mostly, as well as many other things. He has rapped with artists such as Immortal Technique , Canibus , Vinnie Paz , and the United Kingdom rap group Rhyme Asylum . In 2010 he released his debut album Liar & A Thief which was #76 on Billboard's R&B/Hip Hop Albums on April 24, 2010. Diabolic released his second album, Fightin' Words, on September 16, 2014 which reached #47 on Billboard's R&B/Hip Hop Albums. He works independent and with his own label, WarHorse Records. His newest upcoming album, labeled The Disconnect, is scheduled for release in February 2018.
