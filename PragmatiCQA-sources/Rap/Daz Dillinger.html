Delmar Drew Arnaud (born on May 25 1973 in Long Beach, California ), better known by his stage name Daz Dillinger (and formerly Dat Nigga Daz ) is an American hip hop producer and rapper mostly known for his membership in the rap duo Tha Dogg Pound , with Kurupt , and his work with Death Row Records . He is the cousin of Snoop Dogg , Nate Dogg , Lil' 1/2 Dead and RBX .
