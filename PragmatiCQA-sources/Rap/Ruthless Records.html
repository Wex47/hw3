Ruthless Records is a Record label , co-founded by gangsta rapper Eazy-E and NWA manager Jerry Heller .

Ruthless Records was started in the 1980's when Eazy-E, a former drug dealer, used the money he had earned from selling drugs to start a record label with the financial management of Jerry Heller. Eazy-E was in a new Gangsta rap group called N.W.A
