"How Do U Want It" is a song of 2Pac featuring K-Ci and JoJo and was the third single from his album All Eyez on Me. It matched the success of his previous single "California Love", giving 2Pac his second consecutive #1 single in the U.S. It was on the B-side of the single "California Love/How Do You Want It". In the UK, the song peaked at #17.

It samples Quincy Jones's "Body Heat" from his 1974 album Body Heat

The song includes a diss of noted gangsta rap critic C. Delores Tucker.
