Bullet is a 1996 crime drama film directed by Julien Temple and starring Mickey Rourke, Tupac Shakur, John Enos III, Ted Levine and Adrien Brody. The screenplay was written by Rourke and Bruce Rubenstein. The film was released a month after Tupac's murder.

Mickey Rourke was also the music supervisor of the film. It had limited distribution in theatres in US and was shot in New York with a significant portion in Brooklyn.
