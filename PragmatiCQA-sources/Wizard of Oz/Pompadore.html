Prince Pompadore , also known by his nickname Pompa , is the crown prince of the little kingdom of Pumperdink in the Gillikin Country of Oz , being the son of King Pompus and Queen Pozy. After adventuring with Kabumpo the Elegant Elephant, Pompa married Princess Peg Amy of Sun Top Mountain . ( Kabumpo in Oz )

Pompa and Peg had a daughter named Pajonia. The royal family and some of their retainers were kidnapped by the wicked princess Faleero , but were rescued by Kabumpo and his new friends Randy and Jinnicky . ( The Purple Prince of Oz )

Pompadore was one of many Ozian intellectuals who were mesmerized by the Wishing Horse Chalk into thinking that Skamperoo was the rightful Emperor of Oz. ( The Wishing Horse of Oz )

Later, when Kabumpo goes missing from the court of Pumperdink, Pompa goes in search of him, resulting in further adventures. ( Prince Pompadore in Oz )
