The Skeezers are a people who live in a remote corner of the Gillikin Country . They are best known for their conflict with the Flatheads .

Description

Unlike the Flatheads, the Skeezers are anatomically normal humans. They live a more technologically advanced lifestyle than most people of Oz , with their submersible city in the middle of a lake, and their submarine boats.

During the Flathead conflict they were ruled by their queen Coo-ee-oh , a witch and a tyrant; the Skeezers were glad to be rid of her. ( Glinda of Oz )
