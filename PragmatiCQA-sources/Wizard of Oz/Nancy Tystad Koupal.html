Nancy Tystad Koupal is a contemporary scholar on L. Frank Baum . She is the author of Baum's Road to Oz: The Dakota Years (2000), and has edited Baum's series of newspaper columns, Our Landlady , for a modern edition (1999).

Nancy Koupal is Director of Research and Publishing for the South Dakota Historical Society. She edits the journal South Dakota History , and also serves as the northern plains editor of The Encyclopedia of the Great Plains. She is the co-author, with James P. Ronda, of Finding Lewis and Clark: Old Trails, New Directions (2004).
