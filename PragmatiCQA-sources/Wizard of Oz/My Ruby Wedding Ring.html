"My Ruby Wedding Ring" is an early short story by L. Frank Baum . it was copyrighted on 12 October 1896 by the Bacheller Syndicate , and sold by the syndicate to the American Press Association in 1903. It was first published in The Daily Picayune (now The Times-Picayune ) on October 11, 1896 . It was printed in The Baum Bugle in 1965.

"My Ruby Wedding Ring" is one of Baum's early attempts at a supernatural story, comparable to " Who Called 'Perry'? " and " A Shadow Cast Before ."
