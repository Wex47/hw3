Sun Top Mountain is a peak in the Winkie Country , ruled by Princess Peg Amy and Prince Pompadore .

Description

Though located in the yellow Winkie Country, Sun Top is a steep purple elevation surmounted by a golden castle that flies a hundred pennants. The castle's grounds, gardens, and arbors flow down the mountain's slopes from the summit. A winding path leads up to the castle gates. ( Kabumpo in Oz )
