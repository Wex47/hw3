Tales Told in Oz is a book that explores the folklore of the Land of Oz in The Wicked Years saga.

Stories

  • "The Legend of Said Aelphaba of the Waterfall" - How a saint, the namesake of Elphaba , was trapped in a cave.
  • "Four Improbable Handshakes" - The story of Jack Pumpkinhead .
  • "The Witch and the Fox Babies" - How some Fox babies, left home alone, were nearly eaten by the Witch.
  • "Skelleybones Fir-Cloak" - How Lurline settles a dispute among time-traveling Trolls; The creation of Oz.
  • "Quadling Quacklings" - Sayings and rhymes among the Quadling people.
