Wilhelmina Witch (nicknamed Willie by Lion and Wil by Mitch ) is the niece of the Wicked Witch of the West , introduced in Dorothy and the Wizard of Oz as one of it's main antagonists (alongside her aunt).

She was away at Witch Finishing School during the events of the original movie, and during the flashback explaining the witch's survival after being vapourized by Dorothy, it shows her getting back just after the witch was defeated and before the Wizard left via balloon.

This was analagous to an earlier flashback where Ozma was shown getting back from Princess Finishing School before ascending the throne as Queen.

She considers herself a kid, saying "kids like me" to her aunt in Oztember Surprise .

Appearance

In Time After Time she describes herself as having "elegantly manicured and delicate green hands". Like her aunt, she has green skin. She also has black hair with green streaks, rosy cheeks and black eyes.

Outfit

She wears a purple dress with a black belt and a gold buckle, a purple witch hat with a black band, and a purple cape. She also wears a pair of black boots and purple tights.

Family

Since she is the niece of the Wicked Witch of the West (she regularly calls her "auntie") this means she is the child of one of West Witch's siblings.

Although the identity of this parent has not yet been clarified in one of the episodes, some interpretations of Oz that represent the West/East witches as sisters led to fan speculation that this is the case in this cartoon adaptation, and that she might be the daughter of the Wicked Witch of the East killed by Dorothy's house.

This idea was reinforced by describing the Ruby Slippers (which belonged to the East Witch prior to her death) as "rightfully mine", which would make sense if she was the East Witch's daughter. It would also explain why the Witch of the West feels entitled to the slippers, being the surviving sister.

Eventually part two of Battle for the Silver Slippers answered these questions: the East and West witches were indeed sisters, however Wilhelmina is also the niece of the East Witch, referring to both witches as "aunties" when they are reunited. The east witch initially calls Wil "second favorite niece" and Wil retorts she is her "only niece". This would indicate that Wil has no sisters, that the Witch of the West has no daughters, and that if Wil had any female cousins on that side of the family it could only be the East Witch's daughter.

This means there would be a third sibling not yet revealed who is one of Wil's parents.

Personality

Wilhelmina Witch is the spoiled and sarcastic niece of the Wicked Witch of the West, and the Wicked Witch of the East. Like her aunt, Wilhelmina's always scheming of ways of trying to get her hands on Dorothy's Ruby Slippers, but she never succeeded. Until in "The Return of the Silver Slippers," when her aunt tricked Dorothy and the gang disguising herself as the Magic Cobbler.

Wilhelmina may be young, but she's not all sugar and spice as you thought she'd be. She can be very rude, sassy, mean and abusive to nearly everyone, especially Dorothy and her friends, and even to her two banana-brained monkeys, Frank and Lyman.

However, Wilhelmina has shown to have a good side in her, although she very rarely shows it. This was shown when she did good deeds to get rid of a Reflection Beast in " Mirror Madness ", and in " Home Away From Home ", she tearfully hugged Dorothy, when she was about to leave Oz permanently, before the latter fixed it, though she could have been delighted at that due to her dislike of Dorothy.

Powers

Eyes

Even when Ozma strips her magical abilities, her eyes still turn red when angry at getting wet.

Flight

She is able to fly with the aid of a broomstick.

Wilhelmina's Broom apparently requires the aid of her magic (can't fly on his own) since in The Old Classic she is unable to fly when Ozma strips her of her powers for three days.

Invisibility

She turns invisible and takes the ruby slippers when Dorothy is swimming without them, but Toto stops her.

Pyrokinesis

In Time After Time when she is angry at a flowerpot falling on her head, it bursts into flames, indicating she has at least latent pyrokinetic abilities like her aunt, though perhaps not as developed (such as throwing fireballs).

Waterproof

Unlike her aunt, water does not seem to bother her, as her hair gets wet walking home in The Old Classic .

Magic

She is able to cast certain spells, sometimes requiring vocalizing words.

Strength

She's strong enough to carry Cowardly Lion by his tail, spin him around and then throw him.
