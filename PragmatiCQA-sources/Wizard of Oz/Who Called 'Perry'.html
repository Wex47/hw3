"Who Called 'Perry'?" is an early short story by L. Frank Baum . It was first published in the 19 January 1896 edition of The Chicago Times-Herald , and was reprinted in The Baum Bugle in 1975. It is one of Baum's early attempts at a supernatural story, like " A Shadow Cast Before " and " My Ruby Wedding Ring ."

The newspaper printed the story because Baum had entered it into one of its fiction contests. A month after its appearance, another Baum story won a prize in another Times-Herald contest — see " Yesterday at the Exposition ."
