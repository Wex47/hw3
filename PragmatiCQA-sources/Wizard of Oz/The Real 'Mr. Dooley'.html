"The Real 'Mr. Dooley'" is a nonfiction article written by L. Frank Baum , on the genesis of the character created by Finley Peter Dunne. It was published in the January 1900 issue of Home Magazine , with illustrations by W. W. Denslow and Ike Morgan .

The article contains a passing reference to a reporter named Sam Steele; Baum would later borrow the man's name for his Sam Steele adventures for boys.
