Books of Wonder is a well-known publisher of children's books. Among many other selections, the firm has released facsimile editions of the books of L. Frank Baum , and new editions of books by Ruth Plumly Thompson , John R. Neill , Jack Snow , and Eloise Jarvis McGraw .

During the 1990s Books of Wonder maintained a separate imprint called Emerald City Press , which was devoted to issuing new Oz books by contemporary authors.
