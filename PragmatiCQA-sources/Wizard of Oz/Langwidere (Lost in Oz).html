Langwidere is the sister of Cyra , aunt of West and great granddaughter of the Wicked Witch of the West .

She hated the Gales for what they(the original Dorothy Gale by any chance) done to her great grandmother and great, great aunt, the Wicked Witch of the East and wanted revenge. She plans to avenge her family legacy by erasing everyone's memories about Glinda and the Gales and change them that the Wicked Witches ruled all of Oz.

Her name is the same as Princess Langwidere , probably named after her for an honour because she was a ally of Mombi and the Nome King .
