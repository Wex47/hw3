Sherwood Smith (born 1951) is a veteran author of fiction for children, juveniles, and adults in the fantasy and science fiction genres. The L. Frank Baum Family Trust chose Smith as a new " Royal Historian " of Oz, to continue the series of Oz novels . She was commissioned to write four new novels; the first two of these, The Emerald Wand of Oz and Trouble Under Oz , were published in 2005 and 2006. The third entitled Sky Pyrates over Oz was stalled until 2014 with the note that it would be the last of a trilogy instead. She is also the writer of various historical fiction.
