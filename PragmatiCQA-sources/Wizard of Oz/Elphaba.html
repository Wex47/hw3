Portrayal in film

For the Wicked (film) she will be played by Cynthia Erivo .

With ABC owning the rights to create a miniseries based solely on the book, they were previously in development but such a project has been stalled if not cancelled. It had been speculated that the character Zelena in Once Upon a Time was not only inspired by Elphaba but originally intended to be a version of her.
