The Hidden History of Oz is an alternate Oz book series by Tarl Telford that is a prequel to The Wonderful Wizard of Oz . It takes place before the Wizard arrived in Oz and is seen through the viewpoint of a teenaged Glinda in a way similar to the Wicked series .

After being originally released from 2012-2015, the Glinda trilogy was revised and expanded with new titles given. Both release dates and titles are provided as to not cause confusion.

Premise

The series is darker and more mature than most Oz novels but keeps the overall spirit of Baum's work while attempting to explain some of their inconsistences. King Pastoria , for instance is depicted as a last minute replacement for his deceased brother, King Oz. In addition, the series contains a few nods to the 1939 film as the Witches of the East and West, Kaliyana and Ondri-baba respectively are depicted as sisters. Also Ondri-baba's magic eye is said to give off a glow that makes her appear green. The series focuses on the origins of Baum's classic characters from the prospective of a young Glinda as she evolves into the great and wise Witch of the South.
