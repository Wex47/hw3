Kiki Aru is a Munchkin boy, one of the Hyups of Mount Munch . At one point in his youth he discovered an old magical formula of his father's ; this gave him the power of transformation. Kiki Aru proved to have a selfish and amoral streak unusual among the inhabitants of Oz ; he partnered with Ruggedo the Nome King in a campaign of malicious mischief.

In the end, Kiki Aru had to drink the Water of Oblivion from the Forbidden Fountain ; purged of his memories and his evil inclinations, he was no longer a disruptive influence. ( The Magic of Oz )
