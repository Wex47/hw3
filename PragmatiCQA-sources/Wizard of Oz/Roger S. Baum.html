Roger Stanton Baum (born 1938) is the great-grandson of L. Frank Baum , descended through Baum's eldest son Frank Joslyn Baum . Like his great-grandfather and grandfather, Roger S. Baum has written various books about Oz published through his company Toto Too, Inc. that tend to be within the realm of alternate Oz and are referred to as the "Keepsake Adventures of Oz" . He is one of the few Baum descendants who write Oz books. He currently lives in Springfield, Missouri.
