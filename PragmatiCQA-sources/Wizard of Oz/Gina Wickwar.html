Gina Wickwear is a modern Oz author. Her novel The Hidden Prince of Oz (2000). Her article recounting the writing of that book appeared in The Baum Bugle , Vol. 45 No. 2 (Autumn 2001).

Wickwar attended the University of Alaska; she wrote her master's degree thesis on L. Frank Baum 's Oz novels ("An American in Fairyland: A Study of L. Frank Baum's Oz Books"). Her manuscript for Hidden Prince won the centennial contest for a new Oz book sponsored by The International Wizard of Oz Club .

Wickwar's second Oz novel, Toto of Oz , was published in 2006. (It should not be confused with Chris Dulabone 's Toto in Oz of 1986.)
