The Munchkin River is a river that flows through the Munchkin Country of the Land of Oz . It interrupts the path of the Yellow Brick Road near a Poppy Field .

History

The Scarecrow was briefly stranded on a pole in the middle of the river. He was rescued by a passing Stork . ( The Wonderful Wizard of Oz )

Background

In his non-canonical Dorothy of Oz , Roger S. Baum includes a voyage through the Gillikin Country on the Munchkin River on a tugboat named Tugg .
