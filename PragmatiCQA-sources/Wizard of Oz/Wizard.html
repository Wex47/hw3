Wizard is a title given to male magicians in Oz and other places such as

  • Oscar Diggs the Wizard of Oz, an ordinary man who came to Oz from Omaha via a balloon. He was mistaken for a Wizard and decided to carry on a charade within the Emerald City . When he was revealed to be not a real wizard, he tried leaving Oz by balloon but he gets caught in the Land of the Mangaboos.
  • Conjo , the creator of the Shaggy Man 's Love Magnet who only appears in The Shaggy Man of Oz . He tries to take over Oz but is defeated by the Fairy King of the Beavers who sprays water of oblivion onto him.
  • Wicked Wizard , a villain who only appears in The Magical Monarch of Mo as he stole a toe from Princess Truella to use in a magic potion that would make him taller. She defeats him and he falls into a deep gulf.
