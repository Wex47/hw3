The Magic Dishpan is a very special dishpan used by Cayke to bake the best cookies in the land of the Yips . It has been passed down from mother to daughter since the beginning of time. This dishpan is made of beaten gold with three rows of diamonds encircling it. When a very certain magic word is uttered to it, the dishpan grows as large as a wash tub and will transport its passenger to anywhere in the Land of Oz . It was stolen by Ugu and used to launch his wicked scheme to control all the magic in Oz.
