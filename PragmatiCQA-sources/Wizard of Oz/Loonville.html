Loonville is the home of the Loons , a balloon-like people ruled over by King Bal, making Loonville one of Oz's small autonomous kingdoms. It is located in a dense forest in the Gillikin Country near the southern border with the Winkie Country . A sign on the path to Loonville warns strangers not to enter the city.

It is a location visited by Woot , the Tin Woodman and the Scarecrow in The Tin Woodman of Oz .
