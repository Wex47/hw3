Allegro da Capo is a man who lives in a valley between Dunkiton and the home of the Scoodlers . He is also known as the Musicker .

Description

The Musicker is a little fat man with a round face and faded blue eyes. He wears a red, braided jacket, a blue waistcoat, and white trousers with gold stripes down the sides. His head is bald and he wears a little, round, red cap held in place by an elastic band under his chin. He wears white cotton gloves and leans on a stout, gold-headed cane.

His lungs are full of reeds so that his breathing makes constant music, sounding like an incessant harmonica. Most people find his company unbearable, so he is forced to live as a recluse.

Dorothy Gale , Toto , the Shaggy Man , Button-Bright , and Polychrome met Signor Capo on their journey from Butterfield to Oz. Button gave Capo the nickname "Musicker." Shaggy speculated that Capo had swallowed an accordion in his youth. Capo asked for an invitation to Ozma 's birthday party, but the wise queen decided, sadly, that he should not attend, because his uncontrollable vocal tic might arouse violence against him. ( The Road to Oz )

Trivia

Allegro is the Italian word for "happy". Da capo in the same language means "from the head," figuratively "from the beginning." Both phrases are common directions in sheet music.
