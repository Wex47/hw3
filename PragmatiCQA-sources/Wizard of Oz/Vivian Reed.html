Vivian Reed (17 April 1894 – 19 July 1989) was an actress with the Oz Film Manufacturing Company in 1914 and 1915.

A cropped head shot of Reed costumed as Princess Ozma served as the logo of the company. Reed played the minstrel Quavo in The Magic Cloak of Oz , and Gloria in His Majesty, the Scarecrow of Oz . She had the role of Aneth Consinor, the female lead, in The Last Egyptian .

Reed's film career faded after 1919, though she returned for a few small parts in the 1930s.
