Lauren Lynn McGraw (born 15 August 1944) is one of the last Royal Historians of Oz . She is listed as co-author, along with her mother Eloise Jarvis McGraw , of Merry Go Round in Oz , the last book in the "Famous Forty" Oz series , and also of The Forbidden Fountain of Oz .

The elder McGraw, a professional children's book author, did the actual writing of the books; she credited her daughter Lauren with story contributions. In the prefatory note to Forbidden Fountain , Eloise McGraw calls Lauren Lynn "Assistant Inventor and Head Trouble-Shooter."

Lauren McGraw, a librarian by profession, went by the name Wagner during the period of her marriage. The younger McGraw was also a capable graphic artist and illustrator.
