Hoopers are people that are ten feet tall and incredibly flat and thin. They move by grabbing their toes, so they turn into giant hoops, and rolling. They are ruled by King Rollo the Royal/Worst. They live in a 'large, pleasant park, with dazzling white paths running in every direction' in a forest near Kimbaloo , in the Gillikin Country .

In The Lost King of Oz , Mombi , Snip , and Pajuka come across the Hoopers' park. The Hoopers roll themselves at the travelers. Snip retaliates by hitting them in the head with a stick.
