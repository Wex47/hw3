Mortal for an Hour is a short play for children by L. Frank Baum . It was first staged in Macatawa Park, Michigan in 1908, in a benefit performance for the Fresh Air Fund. The play was printed under the alternative title The Fairy Prince in the December 1908 issue of Entertaining , and was reprinted, under another alternative title, Prince Marvel , in L. Frank Baum's Juvenile Speaker in 1910.

The play adapts the story of Prince Marvel from Baum's novel The Enchanted Island of Yew .
