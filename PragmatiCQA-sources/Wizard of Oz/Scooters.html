Scooters are people with sails stretching from their fingertips to their ankles and small, ski-like things on their feet. They live in a river in the Gillikin Country , right by a bridge made of water. They wear waterproof hats and coats and spend their time scooting across the water. Young Scooters are called Scoots until their sails grow. Scooters' diets consist of watercress, watermelons, and raw fish. Scooters can catch colds if their feet get dry.

Dorothy and Humpy met the Scooters in the Lost King of Oz . Two Scooters named Flub Blub and Mouldy helped them across the river.
