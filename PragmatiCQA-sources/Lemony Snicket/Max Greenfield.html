Max Greenfield is the American actor in the Netflix adaptation of A Series of Unfortunate Events . He portrays Frank , Ernest and Dewey Denouement .

A Series of Unfortunate Events

Greenfield was originally reluctant to have a mustache as part of his character as the Denouements, as he believed he would look like Freddie Mercury . After it was tested, though, he emailed Barry Sonnenfeld asking to keep it. "It looked fantastic and so period."
