Location

Hungry's is positioned across the street from Partial Foods and nearby Black Cat Coffee .

Description

Hungry's is described as a small and narrow place ; it is described as not looking like anything special. There are a few booths along the wall, and a counter where customers can sit and watch the chef prepare their meal. A lot of their food comes from the garden tended by Jake Hix and Cleo Knight .

It is owned by Hungry Hix . Her nephew, Jake , works as an apprentice chef.

Jake tends to give Lemony Snicket , Pip Bellerophon and Squeak Bellerophon free food, to his aunt's dismay.
