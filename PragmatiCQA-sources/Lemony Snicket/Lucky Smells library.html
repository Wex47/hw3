Description

The library was created at the insistence of Charles , one of the few things Sir allowed him to do. It is situated down the hall from Sir's office.

It is a large room, filled with elegant wooden bookshelves and comfortable-looking sofas. One wall includes a row of windows, letting in light, and another wall has a row of landscape paintings. Unfortunately, due to a lack of funding for books, only three donations are included.

The library was visited by Violet Baudelaire in attempt to research the ailment of her brother, Klaus . Much later, Shirley and Flacutono were kept prisoner there as a substitute jail, guarded by Phil .

Known Books

  • Advanced Ocular Science (donated by Georgina Orwell )
  • The History of Lucky Smells Lumbermill (donated by Sir)
  • The Paltryville Constitution (donated by the mayor)

Netflix Series Divergent Canon

In the Netflix adaptation , Charles added the library in attempt to boost morale. It is filled with books, but only several copies of the same book- The History of Lucky Smells Lumbermill . Violet Baudelaire searches through the books in attempt to find the source of the Paltryville fire. The only other book included is a Verified Functional Dictionary which Bertrand Baudelaire had written in.
