Biography

Mr. Mann was famous in Stain'd-by-the-Sea for his famous and expensive fish scale mosaics, which hung in his home, the Mann Mansion . He also appeared in several plays at the the Stain'd Playhouse , most notably The Man Who Looked Somewhat Like Winston Churchill.

He was married to his wife for thirty-seven years prior to his death, and they had two children: Hans Mann and a daughter . His cause and time of death is currently unknown.
