Biography

“ It can be hard to find the company you are looking for. ”
— Lemony Snicket , Goldfish Ghost

Goldfish Ghost is born on the surface of a fishbowl in a boy's room. He stares at the pleasant and familiar room's ceiling for a while, but as there is nobody to talk to, Goldfish Ghost floats out the window to find company. Outside is a seaside town, and the fish goes first to the pier, with interesting shrieking birds, but they don't make good company as they pay no attention to Goldfish Ghost.

The wind changes, and Goldfish Ghost follows the breeze into town. However, all the interesting-looking people have somebody else with them, so Goldfish Ghost follows a loud jeep to the beach. None of the swimmers glance at him, and while Goldfish Ghost meets several ghosts of sea creatures, floating above the sea, he does not feel at home atop the ocean.

He rests atop a beach umbrella to think of what to do, eventually deciding as the day got late to head back to the bowl. When he returns, he finds another, living goldfish in the bowl, and while she is nice enough, she is not good company, and the moon calls Goldfish Ghost back outside.

He hears a voice from the lighthouse, and floats over there to meet the ghost of the Lighthouse Keeper , who had been watching him from the lantern room. She takes him in his quiet hands and places him where the light had once shone out to sea, and the two settle in to watch the world. They are very good company.
