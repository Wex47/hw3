Description

The office has a yellow-and-orange trim on the office, and a garden with matching zinnias in the backyard.

There is an examining room, where the valuable Amaranthine Newt is kept.

The owners, the Doctors Sobol , left town, leaving the store in the care of Oliver Sobol , their son, until they could fetch him.
