Swords is a fencing equipment / sword store in the old business district of Stain'd-by-the-Sea .

Description

Swords is a shop in an old district of town, described as having buildings (likely including Swords) that once looked very impressive but now had faded or fallen, with weeds pushing through the cobblestones of the courtyard and rusty chairs tipped over on the street.

While the store is not described much, it is known to sell swords and likely similar fencing equipment. It has been owned by the Distinguished family, most recently Muriel Distinguished .

It is up the street from Cords , and across the square from Gourds .
