The Cozy family was known to own the only rocking chair store in Stain'd-by-the-Sea , entitled Cozy's . It is said that before the economic collapse of the town, the rocking chairs were "prized possessions." After the draining of the sea, they fell on hard times.

The family lived in a cramped apartment above their store. Mr. Cozy died by choking on a fishbone, and Thomasina attempted to merge their family with the swing set family by marrying her daughter to Baron von Pendle . This presumably failed.

The family also has a furry dog, Tabitha .
