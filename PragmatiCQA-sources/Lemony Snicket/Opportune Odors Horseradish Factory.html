Opportune Odors Horseradish Factory is, as its name implies, a horseradish factory located along Lousy Lane , and is responsible for causing the smell of horseradish in the surrounding area. It produced horseradish as an antidote to the Medusoid Mycelium .

History

The factory was the last big order of the Lucky Smells Lumbermill .

Kit Snicket , when writing to Gregor Anwhistle , mentions the factory is producing horseradish to combat the possible weapon of the Medusoid Mycelium , but she does not believe it will be enough; she begs him to stop production, and he eventually perishes in a fire, though the medusoid survives.

In The End , Ishmael claims that he plans to take the poisoned islanders to the factory to save them.

In the TV Series Divergent Canon, it is possible that they delivered hybrid apples to Prufrock Preparatory School , as Isadora Quagmire claims Prufrock's apples taste like horseradish.
