Gourds is a decorative squash and gourd store in the business district of Stain'd-by-the-Sea .

Description

Gourds is a shop in an old district of town, described as having buildings (likely including Gourds) that once looked very impressive but now had faded or fallen, with weeds pushing through the cobblestones of the courtyard and rusty chairs tipped over on the street.

It is known to sell decorative squashes. One known customer is Muriel Distinguished . It is also across the square from her store, Swords .
