Shouldn't You Be in School?
Stain'd Secondary School , also known as Stain'd Secondary , was a school in Stain'd-by-the-Sea . It was set on fire by Hangfire in Shouldn't You Be in School? . The schoolchildren were subsequently moved to Wade Academy as a part of Hangfire's plan.

Description

Stain'd Secondary was described as having a large campus. There was a tall, wide building that curved slightly like a seashell- an auditorium or a gymnasium- with several smaller buildings grouped into its shadow. At least one of the buildings was a school library.

The Inhumane Society burnt down the school in order to have the students transferred to Wade Academy to be drugged and held hostage.

Known students

The only named student is Ornette Lost ; however, Snicket mentions that he recognized a few other students from his time in town.
