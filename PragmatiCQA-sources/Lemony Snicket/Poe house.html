The Poe house is a small, cramped abode in the City . It is where Mr. Poe resides with his wife, Polly , and their two sons, Edgar and Albert .

Background information

The Baudelaire children stayed there for a brief period of time not long after the death of their parents , before they went to live with Count Olaf . Sadly, Edgar and Albert's impudent personalities made the Baudelaires' stay at the house unbearable.
