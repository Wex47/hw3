Biography

Midnight Demon/Panicked Feet

Tabby lives in Stain'd-by-the-Sea with the Cozy family- Thomasina , Tatiana and Treacle - and seems to spend most of her time asleep in her basket. After pretending to be his sister, Treacle hides his wig underneath her, but Snicket reveals it by calling for her, which prompts the dog to stand up and go to him for attention.

Physical Appearance

“ Except for the dog's steady breathing, it looked more like a basket of hair than a pet. ”
— Lemony Snicket ,

Snicket describes Tabby as a cozy, furry dog, and so fluffy that it is hard to tell her head from her tail.
