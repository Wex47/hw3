Biography

Much of Gary Dorian's life is unknown, but he was a resident of Stain'd-by-the-Sea and became a famous cosmetician. He was famous enough that an oil painting of him was considered a distinguished item for sale.

Trivia

  • Gary Dorian's name is a reference to Dorian Gray from Oscar Wilde 's 1890 novel The Picture of Dorian Gray , in which a vain Dorian sells his soul in order to ensure his beauty will never fade, and his true age and sins are recorded in an oil painting.
