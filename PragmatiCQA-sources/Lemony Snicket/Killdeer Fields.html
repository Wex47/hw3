Killdeer Fields was a town near Stain'd-by-the-Sea that was flooded after the sea was drained in an attempt to save the latter's economy from collapsing.

Location

Ellington Feint describes Killdeer Fields as "up the road a ways" from Stain'd-by-the-Sea . It was definitely close enough to be affected by the draining of the sea ordered by Ignatius Knight since the water that had been drained flooded the fields.

Description

Ellington describes the town as "a nice enough place." It includes, or was located inside, a valley. There was at least one school that Ellington Feint attended when she previously resided there.

When Stain'd-by-the-Sea was drained, the valley of Killdeer Fields was flooded. "Countless creatures" were drowned, and the inhabitants of an entire village was forced to leave their homes because the flooding left the area unsuitable for living; It is mentioned that several villagers left after the flood had occurred.
