The Herpetological Society is a society of herpetologists, scientists of reptiles and amphibians (but mostly reptiles).

Known Members

  • Dr. Montgomery Montgomery †
  • Gustav (Monty's assistant)
  • Bruce (director of marketing)

Mentions

The Reptile Room - Uncle Monty mentions he works for the Herpetological Society and will play a prank on them, in return for their constant mocking of his name. His prank would have been to name the new snake he found the Incredibly Deadly Viper , to make everyone think it is deadly, and pretend it has escaped. Later, the Herpetological Society collects Monty's reptiles after his death, with Bruce leading the operation.
