V.F.D. Tunnels

Location Information

Location

Under The City

Affiliation

Volunteer Fire Department

Type

Tunnels
The V.F.D. tunnels are a network of passages under the City connecting the residences and buildings of various organization members to each other, such as the Baudelaires , Snickets , Quagmires , etc. It was most likely created for the noble purpose to allow volunteers to travel inconspicuously but was later also used as a method of escape by Count Olaf on some of his many schemes.

Canon

In the book series, Lemony Snicket mentions there are many tunnels, however the routes of only two tunnels are described:

  • Tunnel between 667 Dark Avenue and the Baudelaire Mansion
  • Tunnel between the Quagmire Mansion and Montgomery Montgomery's house

In All the Wrong Questions , it is revealed that a tunnel under the Fountain of Victorious Finance led to the Museum of Items , which Kit Snicket exploits in order to commit a heist.

TV Series Divergent Canon

In the Netflix adaptation , the tunnels are frequently shown. They are often used by Lemony Snicket while narrating, as well as by other characters.

Tunnel entrances include:

  • In the theater where The Marvelous Marriage was held.
  • A manhole in the street across 667 Dark Avenue .
  • The fake wall at the bottom of ersatz elevator in 667 Dark Avenue.
  • The floor of the Baudelaire Mansion.
  • The stage floor in Veblen Hall .
  • A prison near Peru .
  • Hotel Denouement 's sub-basement library card catalog .
