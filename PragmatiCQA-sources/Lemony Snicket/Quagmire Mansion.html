The Quagmire Mansion was the home of the triplets Duncan , Isadora and Quigley , and their parents, Mr. Quagmire and Mrs. Quagmire .

Like the Baudelaire Mansion , it was also destroyed in a fire .

History

Like many other buildings in the story, the Quagmire Mansion has a secret passageway , this one leading to Montgomery Montgomery's House . Because of this, the mansion must be located at or near Lousy Lane , and is probably not in the City .

This passageway was used by Quigley to flee the mansion at the time of the Quagmire fire. Both of the Quagmire parents perished along with the burning mansion.

In the TV series, a mysterious person is seen burning it down with a laser device that can set fire from a distance.

It was thought the arsonist was Esmé Squalor due to the fashionable outfit, but it was later discovered the outfit was a regular Firestarting outfit from the VFD disguise kit worn by both men and women.

There are various theories on who set the mansion on fire including Georgina Orwell , Woman with hair but no beard , Man with a beard but no hair or Esmé Squalor .
