Barry Sonnenfeld is an executive producer of both the film and TV series adaptations of A Series of Unfortunate Events .

In the TV series, he also directed several of the episodes and portrays Ike Anwhistle , as well as the voice of " Mr. Tamerlane " in The Austere Academy: Part One . In The Hostile Hospital: Part Two , a woman is named after him with the anagram Barney N. Dolfners .

Cameos

In each book adaptation, he has a cameo in some art such as a painting or a statue.

  • The Bad Beginning: A painting in Justice Strauss' library.
  • The Reptile Room: A painting on Monty's wall.
  • The Wide Window: Ike Anwhistle in Josephine Anwhistle's library.
  • The Austere Academy: Sir Barrymore Feint in Nero's office.
  • The Ersatz Elevator: A painting in the Squalor's formal, semi-formal and informal dining rooms. He is wearing different clothes in all of them.
  • The Vile Village: In the village's fire department.
  • The Hostile Hospital: On a cranioectomy poster.
  • The Carnivorous Carnival: On art on a tent, picturing him with the Volunteer Feline Detectives .
  • The Slippery Slope: On art on another tent at the top of Mount Fraught.
  • The Grim Grotto: Captain Widdershins painting, as well as on the wasabi label.
  • The Penultimate Peril: A bust on one of the floors, as well as in a painting in the Indian restaurant.
  • The End: In a painting above the juke box in the soda shop and his fireman portrait reappears in the Island arboretum.
