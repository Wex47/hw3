Biography

“ My family has run this business for years. I'm grateful that you came, Officers, but I'm not sure what the police can do. ”
— Bob Old, , "Bad Gang"

Bob lives in Stain'd-by-the-Sea with his son, Kevin . He runs the store Boards , which sells different models of wood and has been a family business for years. Though his son takes an interest in fencing and pirate books, and begs for a sword, Bob refuses, thinking the interest is foolish, though Kevin insists it would be useful to defend themselves.

On a Friday, Boards is vandalized by the Big Bad Brick Gang, who throw a brick through his window and steal a bestselling model board. Kevin tells him about the Gang, and he calls the police. Harvey and Mimi Mitchum don't do much but argue, and Bob informs Lemony Snicket , who was passing by, of the situation.
