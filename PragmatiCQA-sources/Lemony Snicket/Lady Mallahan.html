Biography

Lady Mallahan, according to legend, slew the Bombinating Beast on one of her voyages, hundreds of years before the events of All the Wrong Questions . However, some people believe that she instead just found a dead walrus at the bottom of the lighthouse's cliffs, and the local townspeople's gossip inflated the story.

It's possible she owned the statue that could control the Bombinating Beast, as it was passed down through the Mallahan family.
