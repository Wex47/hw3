Cords is an electrical supply store in the business district of Stain'd-by-the-Sea .

Description

Cords is a shop in an old district of town, described as having buildings (likely including Cords) that once looked very impressive but now had faded or fallen, with weeds pushing through the cobblestones of the courtyard and rusty chairs tipped over on the street.

It is down the street from Swords .
