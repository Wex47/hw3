Biography

Known Facts

As shown from the Snicket family tree, I. is the child of D. Snicket and the (presumably youngest, seeing as their name is listed last both on the tree and alphabetically) sibling of G and H . They are the grandchild of Chas. Snicket , the grand-nibling of A and B , the nibling of E and F , and the cousin of Jacques , Kit , Lemony , M , N and O . This would make Beatrice Baudelaire II their first cousin, once removed.

Theories/Speculated Identity

I is theorized to be Ike Anwhistle , which would make his sibling G Gregor Anwhistle . As many VFD families are interconnected, it would make sense that the Snickets and Anwhistles were related, and if D. was the parent of Ike Anwhistle, it would make sense for them to be his mother, who was described by his wife Josephine as having one eyebrow and one ear. H's identity would still be a mystery, though they are occasionally theorized to be Hector .

If I is Ike, that would make them the second cousin of the Baudelaire family, and would mean that he is deceased.
