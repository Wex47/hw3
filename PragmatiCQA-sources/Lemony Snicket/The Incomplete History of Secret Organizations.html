TV Series Canon

This book was found in Justice Strauss' , Monty Montgomery 's, and Aunt Josephine's libraries .

When the Baudelaires arrived at Prufrock Preparatory School , Jacquelyn Scieszka intended for Larry Your-Waiter to deliver an annotated copy to the Baudelaires. However, an accident resulted in librarian Olivia Caliban taking it. Duncan and Isadora Quagmire read parts of this copy, although they were abducted by the Hook-Handed Man , who failed to obtain the book. Olivia eventually found the book and read it, leading her to investigate the situation of the Quagmires and the Baudelaires. This copy was later taken to Caligari Carnival by Olivia Caliban (disguised as Madame Lulu) and then burnt in the fire that destroyed the carnival.
