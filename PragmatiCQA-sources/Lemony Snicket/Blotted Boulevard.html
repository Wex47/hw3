Description

The road is considered the emptiest in Stain'd-by-the-Sea, and there isn't a single building left. It is described as filled with empty lots stretching for thirteen blocks and only populated by the occasional pile of rubble.

History

Who Could That Be at This Hour?

In an attempt to steal the Bombinating Beast statue from Lemony Snicket , Hangfire called him and mimicked the voice of Ellington Feint , claiming to be captured and held at 1300 Blotted Boulevard. While Snicket wasn't fooled, he does take the Bellerophon Taxi to the street, just to make certain.

Snicket once again returns to 1300 Blotted Boulevard, as the Bellerophons' mechanic, Jackie , was given a ransom note, telling them to bring a complete set of Dugga Drills to that location at midnight in exchange for their kidnapped dog, Lysistrata. Hoping to catch the ransomer, Jackie and Snicket arrive an hour early and wait. Nobody arrives, but Snicket has figured out who the dognapper was and asks Jackie to take him for another ride.
