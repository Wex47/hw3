Muriel Distinguished is a woman who lives in Stain'd-by-the-Sea . She appears in , in the fifth incident, "Bad Gang."

Biography

Muriel Distinguished is the owner of her family business, Swords , selling the items it was named after. She buys her decorative squashes from Gourds , across the square.

On a Saturday, Muriel calls the police after finding her store, Swords, vandalized. A window was smashed with a brick, and a sword on display was stolen. The Big Bad Brick Gang left a note claiming responsibility for the crime, saying that nobody will ever figure out what makes them choose a place to strike, and that the police can call them the BBBG for short if they want.

Lemony Snicket asks Muriel if there are other stores with rhyming names- as the previous vandalized store was called Boards- and she responds that down the street was Cords , across the square was Gourds - and she gives him the information, though the Officers Mitchum doubt he has an actual lead.
