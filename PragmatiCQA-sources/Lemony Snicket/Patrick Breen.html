Biography

Patrick Breen was born on October 26, 1960 in Brooklyn, New York, USA as Joseph Patrick Breen. He is an actor and writer, known for Galaxy Quest (1999), Men in Black (1997) and Whole Day Down (2011). He has been married to Nadine Van der Velde since 1992. They have two children.
