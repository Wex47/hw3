Verdant Flammable Devices are signaling devices used by volunteers on the fire-fighting side of V.F.D. , and are an obvious reference to the initials "V.F.D.". They are described as small green tubes. These are first mentioned in The Slippery Slope . When lit, they give off a dark green smoke and smell like burning vegetables. Quigley Quagmire found them in the V.F.D. headquarters and showed Violet and Klaus how to use them. The Man With a Beard But No Hair also found one and gave it to Esmé Squalor , thinking it was a cigarette. Later, Sunny uses the same "cigarette" to cook lox for Count Olaf 's troupe and also to signal to her siblings.
