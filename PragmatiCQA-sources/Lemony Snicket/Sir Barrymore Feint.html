— Vice Principal Nero , " The Austere Academy: Part One "

Sir Barrymore Feint was a violinist that founded Prufrock Preparatory School at an unspecified point in time, according to the television series. Other than the fact that he wrote a grant to the school that funded its residential facilities, not much else about his other accomplishments nor his life are known. His portrait can be seen on display in the Vice Principal's office.
