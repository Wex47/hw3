In Netflix's The Miserable Mill , Jimmy was one of the workers at Lucky Smells Lumbermill . A known action of him is offering a 50% Ahab Memorial Hospital coupon to Phil when a hypnotized Klaus accidentally hurts his leg. Jimmy also appeared in The Penultimate Peril at the Hotel Denouement , invited by Justice Strauss .
