Unicorn (ユニコーン, Yunikōn ) is America's national team, appearing for the first time in Inazuma Eleven 3: Sekai e no Chousen!! taking part in the Football Frontier International .

Uniform

Unicorn's uniform consists of a white colored, dark blue collared t-shirt, with a red colored neckline. This is fitted with dark blue shorts, white colored socks, and dark blue cleats. The goalkeeper uniform consists of a long sleeved, dark blue shirt, with white colored accents, and dark grey shorts. Mark Kruger assumes the captain's band, which is teal.

Matches

Unicorn VS. Red Matador

When Unicorn first appeared in episode 68 , it was seen that they won against Red Matador with a score of 3-0 during an extra match.

Unicorn VS. Orpheus

This match was never seen, but in the game, anime and manga, it says that Unicorn lost.

Unicorn VS. Inazuma Japan

It was a heated contested match between the two teams, but finally, Inazuma Japan won with a score of 4-3. However, this win was possible because Ichinose was switched with Eddie Howard since he got exhausted during the match.

Unicorn VS. Knights of Queen

This match was never seen as well. Though in the game, it's mentioned that Unicorn won.

Unicorn VS. The Empire

Unicorn had to win to finally make it to the final phase of FFI, but they lost against The Empire 1-0 due to Teres ' Iron Wall and were eliminated.
