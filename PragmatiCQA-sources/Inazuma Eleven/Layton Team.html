Layton Team (レイトンチーム Reiton Chīmu ) is a special team that can be randomly found and challenged in the first game .

They're guest characters from Layton Kyouju or Professor Layton , another series created by Level-5.

Match

To have a match against the Layton Team go to the Inabikari Shuurenjou once the game is cleared. The Layton Team replaces some of the Soccer Battle opponents.

After being defeated, Layton promises that they'd face each other again. They might drop a manual for Sabaki no Tettsui .
