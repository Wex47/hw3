Jimon Daiki ( 寺門 ( じもん ) 大貴 ( だいき ) ) is a forward for Teikoku Gakuen and then later on, a defender for Neo Japan .

In the alternate timeline of Inazuma Eleven Ares no Tenbin he plays as a forward for Teikoku Gakuen .

Appearance

Jimon has tanned skin and black eyes. He has brown dreadlocks and lacks eyebrows. He has been seen wearing both the Teikoku Gakuen jersey and the Neo Japan jersey. If you look closely, he looks a little bit like Someoka .

Plot

Season 1

His team lost in the Football Frontier preliminaries finals to Raimon by a score of 1-2. In the Nationals , his team was crushed by Zeus . In Teikoku he has one of the strongest shots.

Season 2

He's one of Teikoku players that didn't join Shin Teikoku Gakuen .

Season 3

He joined Neo Japan along with the other players to fight for the national seats against Inazuma Japan , but finally didn't qualify.

Inazuma Eleven Orion no Kokuin

He appeared in episode 21 when Kazemaru Ichirouta and Fudou Akio visited Teikoku Gakuen before the departure to Russia, for the FFI tournament.
