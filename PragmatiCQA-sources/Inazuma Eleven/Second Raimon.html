Raimon ( 雷門 ( らいもん ) ) is the protagonist team of the second game and second season of the original anime series .

They are also known as the Second Raimon (セカンド 雷門 ( らいもん ) , Sekando Raimon ) and Chijou Saikyou Raimon ( 地上 ( ちじょう ) 最強 ( さいきょう ) 雷門 ( らいもん ) , lit. The Strongest on Earth Raimon ).

Info

The team is formed by the remaining members of the original Raimon team that weren't injured in the match against Gemini Storm travel across the country to find the strongest player on earth to fight against Aliea Gakuen .

In Inazuma Eleven 3: Sekai e no Chousen!! - The Ogre , this team can be challenged in the Tournament mode. All members are between the levels 35 and 60.

In Inazuma Eleven GO Galaxy , this team is the second team in Gojou Masaru 's taisen route . All members are at level 99.

Uniform

They wear Raimon 's wear, along with their spikes and gloves.
