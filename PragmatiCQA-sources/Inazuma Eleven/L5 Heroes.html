L5 Heroes (L5 ヒーローズ, L5 Hīrōzu ) is a game exclusive team introduced in Inazuma Eleven GO 2: Chrono Stone .

Uniform

They wear the Raimon (GO) first team's away uniform in Chrono Stone and in Galaxy they wear the Raimon uniform.

Info

They can be challenged at Furukabu 's taisen route after defeating Chrono Storm . All the players are at level 99.

Trivia

  • The L5 in the name stands for Level-5 .
  • This team is based on the main emphasized characters in game series by Level-5, mainly Inazuma Eleven (Endou, Kidou and Gouenji), Inazuma Eleven GO (Tenma, Shindou and Tsurugi), Danball Senki W (Ban and Hiro), Professor Layton (Layton, Luke and Remi), Danball Senki Wars (Arata) and Youkai Watch (Keita and Fumika).
