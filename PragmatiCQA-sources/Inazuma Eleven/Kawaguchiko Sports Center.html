Kawaguchiko Sports Center ( 河口湖 ( かわぐちこ ) スポーツセンター, Kawaguchiko Supōtsu Sentā ) is the campground for Inazuma Japan during the Asian qualifiers in Inazuma Eleven Orion no Kokuin .

Info

Kawaguchiko Sports Center is located near a lake, a forest, and Mount Fuji. It serves as the campgrounds for Inazuma Japan , and is run by Kazeaki Yone and Kino Aki . Inazuma Japan splits up and shares the dorm rooms which were decided by a lottery draw. There is also a practice field inside the building, often used by Inazuma Japan for training. It probably serves for other sports, as we can see the presence of baseball and tennis fields, and also a running track.
