Ohisama En (お 日 ( ひ ) さま 園 ( えん ) , lit. Sun Garden ) is an orphanage ran by Kira Seijirou and Kira Hitomiko .

Info

It was originally Kira Hitomiko 's idea, which she suggested to her father after his son, Kira Hiroto died in a mysterious accident. In the original Inazuma Eleven series, it was seen in the anime that it mostly consisted of players from The Genesis but all the members of Aliea Gakuen were living and raised in the orphanage. It is known in GO that Kariya Masaki lives in this orphanage.

In Inazuma Eleven GO , it is still being run by the same people, along with the help of Kira Hiroto . Kariya was live in Ohisama En at an age of 11 because his father couldn't care for him anymore, due to his father lost his job.

Unfortunately, in GO, soccer is not as popular there anymore. This is known because Kariya Masaki often trained alone, it is assumed that after a while Hiroto and Midorikawa Ryuuji came to train with him, though.

In Ares no Tenbin , this orphanage was seen during the flashback of the Eisei Gakuen children childhood, while the kids playing soccer with each other.
