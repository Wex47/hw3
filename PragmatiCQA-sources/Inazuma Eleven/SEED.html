A SEED (dub: Imperial ) is a player placed on a team by Fifth Sector in order to exert control over it.

Info

SEEDs have greater power, speed and strength than any of the other members of their team.

All SEEDs went through harsh training in God Eden , which resulted in their superior abilities and is likely how they learned to use their Keshin.

Trivia

  • In the anime, not all SEEDs have Keshin . However, all of them are noted to be one of the best players of their team.
  • As shown in the anime, Eito is the only team that doesn't have a SEED member in Inazuma Eleven GO .
  • In episode 22 , Kariya said that he was a SEED, but he was actually lying to tease Kirino .
  • Kaiou Gakuen , Dragonlink , Unlimited Shining and Ancient Dark are teams composed entirely of SEEDs. The same could also be assumed for the members of Seidouzan , a team trained directly by the Holy Emperor Ishido Shuuji .
    • Fifth Sector did not assign any SEEDs to Arakumo Gakuen because it was believed the team was so powerful on its own it wouldn’t need any help to win the Holy Road tournament; however, its players all seem to follow whatever instructions they are given by Fifth Sector, and their captain even has a close bond with the Holy Emperor.
