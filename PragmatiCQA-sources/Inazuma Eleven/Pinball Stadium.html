The Pinball Stadium (ピンボールスタジアム, Pinbōru Sutajiamu ) is a stadium in the Holy Road nationals tournament.

Info

Anime

It is one of the six stadiums of the Russian Roulette . The semi-final match between Raimon and Genei Gakuen took place here. It was first seen in episode 33 .

Game

There is also a path that leads to the stadium called the Playing Path, and it is required to go through it the first time you visit the stadium in the game. The Playing Path also has the community master for Aliea Gakuen , along with several shops.
