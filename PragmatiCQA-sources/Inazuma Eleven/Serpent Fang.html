Info

Hebino used Serpent Fang during the match between Raimon and Zero . He attempted it thrice but all attemps failed. The first attempt was to stop the chain shoot of Shindou Takuto 's Sousha Maestro 's Harmonics , Tsurugi Kyousuke 's Death Drop and Matsukaze Tenma 's normal keshin shoot. When the Raimon trio shot with Matei Gryphon 's Sword of Fire , Hebino tried to use Serpent Fang but before he was able to execute it, the keshin hissatsu scored. His last failed attempt was again a hissatsu from the Raimon trio, this time being Evolution .

Usage

The user adquires a blue aura and the earth rumble. A giant, white snake appears and the user uses both their hands to make a fang. When the catch is successful, the snake disappear and the user has the ball with both of their hands.
