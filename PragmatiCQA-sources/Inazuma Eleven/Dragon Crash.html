Info

Inazuma Eleven

Dragon Crash was Someoka Ryuugo 's main hissatsu throughout season 1 and part of season 2 of the anime. He created it in episode 3 and used his newly learned technique to score two goals against Occult in the following episode. However, during his next two scoring attempts, it was stopped by Nata Juuzou 's Yugamu Kuukan . It was later combined with Gouenji Shuuya 's Fire Tornado , creating Dragon Tornado .

In episode 8 , Dragon Crash was used twice in Raimon 's match against Mikage Sennou . First, the shoot was weakened when going through Mikage's line of players, allowing their goalkeeper Sugimori Takeshi to easily catch it. Later, it was stopped by Sugimori's Shoot Pocket . During the match against Shuuyou Meito , it missed the goal twice due to Aidoru Sou 's Goal Zurashi . However, it was able to score against the hissatsu later by being redirected by a header from Megane Kakeru , creating Megane Crash . Afterwards, Dragon Crash was used once more to score Raimon's winning goal.

It was used again in episode 13 during the regional finals against Teikoku Gakuen , but was stopped by Genda Koujirou 's Power Shield . It was also stopped in the following episode by Hibiki Seigou 's God Hand in Raimon's match against the Inazuma Eleven . Dragon Crash was used twice in the quarter-finals against Senbayama . It was first stopped by Ayano Yuuichi 's Makiwari Chop , but later managed pass him and score Raimon's winning goal. In episode 19 , Someoka used Dragon Crash during practise, where it was stopped by Endou Mamoru 's Nekketsu Punch .

In episode 27 , it was used in Raimon's first match against Gemini Storm , but it was easily blocked by Reize . It failed to score again in episode 29 , where it was stopped by Tetsukabe Ken 's Safety Protect against the SP Fixers . In episode 32 , it was blocked once more by Fubuki Shirou in Raimon's match against Hakuren . Dragon Crash was used for the final time in Raimon's third and final match against Gemini Storm, where it was stopped by Gorleo 's Black Hole .

Usage

The user starts out with their back turned, before turning their body around, raising their right leg back, causing the ball to glow blue and a large blue dragon flying in from the right. The user then kicks the ball, causing the dragon to crash into it. As the ball flies forward, the blue dragon coils behind it and follows the ball.

In its incomplete state in the anime, the user kicks the ball, causing it to glow blue with the absence of the blue dragon.
