The Galaxy Nauts Gou (ギャラクシー・ノーツ 号 ( ごう ) , Gyarakushī Nōtsu Gou ; dub: Orion Express ) is an intergalactic train that Earth Eleven used to travel space to play in the Grand Celesta Galaxy tournament. It debuted in episode 19 of Inazuma Eleven GO Galaxy .

Info

In episode 19 , when Earth Eleven was about to depart to Sandorius , the dormitories and Odaiba 's meeting centre combined and formed Galaxy Nauts Gou, which transported Earth Eleven to different planets.

In episode 20 , Earth Eleven were eating within the vehicle that Kabata Shizune made. When they made themselves ready for the warp, Tetsukado Shin noticed that the door was moving and asked who was there. With no response, the members searched the vehicle, afraid that it might be a space monster. After finding the “monster”, who was Nishizono Shinsuke , he was finally accepted by Kuroiwa Ryuusei to be an Earth Eleven memeber. Afterwards, the warp began and ended very quickly, making the members feel not very well. Seeing Sandorius in sight, Bitway Ozrock appeared as a hologram and informed them about the planet. After that, the Galaxy Nauts Gou arrived and stopped at the Sandorius Station .
