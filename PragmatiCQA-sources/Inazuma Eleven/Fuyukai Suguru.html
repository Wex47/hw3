Profile

Inazuma Eleven GO Galaxy

  • "The teacher who became Fifth Sector's yes-man. After the revolution was done, he disappeared from Raimon."

Appearance

Fuyukai has fair skin and curly brown hair. He also wears black rimmed glasses and he has black eyes. In GO, he has aged a bit and he has beige hair. His eyes also became smaller.

Personality

At first sight he is quiet person who gets scared easily. He is immensely afraid of Kageyama, to that point he was ready to harm Raimon players. He was never fond of soccer club, nevertheless he did revealed to Onigawara some informations about Project Z.

Plot

Inazuma Eleven

He was the coach of the Raimon Eleven , also a follower of Kageyama giving him any information about the happenings during different matches. In episode 10 , Natsumi found out that he was going to eliminate Raimon from the upcoming match against Teikoku Gakuen by removing the brake oil of the van which eventually got him fired from being a coach and a teacher. On the same day he was rejected by Kageyama as well. Then in episode 23 , it showed that Detective Onigawara asked him a few questions about Kageyama's plan. He's never seen again in the original series, apart from flashback in episode 121 when he watched Endou and Aki starting soccer club.

Inazuma Eleven GO

He made his return as the assistant principal of the Raimon Junior High School he follows the chairman along with his plan of getting rid of Kudou Michiya . Most of the time, he is seen with the principal.

In episode 2 , he was worried about Kuro no Kishidan being sent by Fifth Sector .

He appeared again in episode 36 , having a talk with the principal about Raimon's soccer club.
