Info

Debuting in episode 25 in the anime and in the first game , God Knows was used by Aphrodi . It was able to score against God Hand , but was effectively stopped by Majin The Hand .

It evolved somewhere between episode 26 and episode 54 becoming God Knows Kai, able to gain the first point for Raimon against Diamond Dust . It was used twice more in episode 58 , and it scored Raimon's second goal against Chaos , breaking through Burnout the first time, and scored the fourth goal without letting Ooiwa Kurando touch the ball.

In episode 77 , Saginuma Osamu seemed to have remastered the move into God Knows Kai with Demeter and Hera 's help since they were both from Zeus . However, it was blocked by Seigi no Tekken G5 .

It reappeared during the match opposing Inazuma Japan to Fire Dragon . Aphrodi , as Fire Dragon 's player and forward, showed his "Shin" God Knows which was effectively stopped by Mugen The Hand G5 .

It was seen briefly in the opening of Inazuma Eleven Reloaded , with Aphrodi in the air, getting ready to fire it towards the goal.

Usage

The user grows a pair of white wings and crouch down, before flying up into the air and crossing their arms, the ball flying up with the user. As the user uncross their arms, the ball is engulfed in a white aura. The ball gets fully charged with a bright light and the user kicks it towards the goal.
