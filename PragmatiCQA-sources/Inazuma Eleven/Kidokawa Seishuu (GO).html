Kidokawa Seishuu ( 木戸川 ( きどかわ ) 清修 ( せいしゅう ) ) is the new Kidokawa Seishuu team in Inazuma Eleven GO .

Info

They had won the previous Holy Road against Raimon by 2-1. They were Raimon's opponent in the current Holy Road during the the rounds of 16 but lost 3-2.

Kidokawa Seishuu's opinion on Fifth Sector was a very divided as a team, while some members wished to join Raimon in their revolution others believed it was foolish to try and stand up to Fifth Sector. Kidokawa's coach Afuro Terumi was hired by Fifth Sector yet he remained neutral in his options on the revolution, instead he wanted his team to make the choice for themselves, a decision which ended in them standing up to Fifth Sector after the defeat by Raimon.

Uniform

Kidokawa Seishuu's uniform consists of a red jersey with black stripes on the sleeves and the collar. The shorts are white with black edges, and the socks are red with black stipes. Their cleats are white and lilac purple. The captain's band is yellow. The goalkeepers have mint green jerseys with black stripes, gray shorts, and gray socks.

Matches

Kidokawa Seishuu Vs. Raimon

In the final of the previous Holy Road , Kidokawa Seishuu won the match against Raimon by 2-1.

Now, they played with Raimon in the 1/8 finals of the Holy Road , in the Water World Stadium . They lost by 2-3.

Trivia

  • In the Chrono Stone game, Taki Yoshihiko replaces Kamiya Kouta as a forward.
  • Kidokawa has two confirmed SEED in their lineup, that being Taki Sousuke and Katayama Tsuyoshi . However, after their defeat by Raimon both SEEDs joined the Kakumei Senbatsu Team as part of the revolution against Fifth Sector with Taki playing as a forward and Katayama as their second goalkeeper.
