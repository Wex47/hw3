Island

The stadium is located on Yamaneko Island. It was built over a waterfall of raging waters.

Info

In both the game and anime, this stadium was used for the match between Inazuma Japan and The Empire . During the last round, the match between The Empire and Unicorn also took place here.

Match

Inazuma Japan vs. The Empire

During this match, Endou , Kidou , Fudou and Sakuma were not able to participate in the match because of Kageyama delaying and they were too far from the stadium as well. Thus, Inazuma Japan struggled to fight without their strategist and captain. Tachimukai Yuuki was unable to stop Leone Batigo's Hellfire with Mugen The Hand G5 but he learned Maou The Hand later in the match and stopped it. Inazuma Japan also managed to break through The Empire's defense. Although they managed to make a goal with their new hissatsu Grand Fire , Inazuma Japan eventually lost with a score of 2-1. Kurimatsu also got injured in this match, and had to leave the team, being replaced by Fubuki Shirou .
