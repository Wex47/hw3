Info

Original series

Twin Boost is a combination shoot hissatsu used by Kidou Yuuto and one other player: Sakuma Jirou , Ichinose , or Gouenji .

Twin Boost F is the result of a chain shoot of Fire Tornado and Twin Boost, combined. It is used by Kidou , his partner being Gouenji .

Inazuma Eleven Ares no Tenbin

Twin Boost was used during the match between Inakuni Raimon and Teikoku Gakuen . Sakuma and Fudou used it and broke through Umihara Norika 's Uzumaki The Hand , though Mansaku Yuuichirou helped Norika to stop it with his knee.

Usage

In the original series, the first user kicks the ball in the air and does a back flip. The second user jumps up and uses their head to hit the ball back down to the first user. The first user then kicks the ball towards the goal, causing the ball to be covered in a orange aura.

In Inazuma Eleven Ares no Tenbin , the first user kicks the ball up in the air. The first and second users quickly run and jump up in the air. The first user kicks the ball downwards while the second user kicks upwards at the same time, their foot creating a yellow slash, covering the ball in a yellow aura. Both users then kick at the same time, covering the ball in a fiery aura, leaving a trail of smoke behind it as it flies towards the goal.
