Info

Original series

It was used by Toramaru to pass Hijikata during practice, but it wasn't marked as an actual technique. It also appeared during the match against Desert Lion .

In the manga, it was used by Fideo Ardena to get pass Kabeyama Heigorou in the match between Inazuma Japan and Orpheus .

Inazuma Eleven GO Chrono Stone

This hissatsu was also used in episode 24 by Taiyou in the match against Zanark Domain , but still wasn't marked as an actual hissatsu.

Inazuma Eleven Ares no Tenbin

During the match between Inakuni Raimon and Seishou Gakuen , Haizaki used it to get past Hiyori , Mansaku and Golem , and Mansaku named the technique used, Hitori One-Two.

Usage

The user kicks the ball giving a powerful effect making it spin to one side, while he jumps to the other side, surrounding the opponent. Later the ball spins to the user so he can get away from him with the ball.
