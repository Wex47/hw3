Inazuma Legend Japan (イナズマレジェンドジャパン, Inazuma Rejendo Japan ) is a team introduced in the Inazuma Eleven GO VS Danball Senki W movie. All team members were a member of Inazuma Japan during the third season of the original series.

Uniform

Their home uniform is blue with white shorts. The goalkeeper's uniform is black with white detail. Their away uniform is a white shirt with yellow shorts, and the goalkeeper's uniform is white with green detail.
