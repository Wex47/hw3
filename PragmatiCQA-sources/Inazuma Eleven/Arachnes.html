Profile

Inazuma Eleven GO

  • "Using dark powers on the pitch, she inspires fear with her spidery moves."

Appearance

Arachnes has green hair with two curls on the sides, tan skin, black lips, and purple eyes. Her hair may mimic a spider's shape, and her name, Arachnes, means "spider" as well.

Personality

Arachnes is a seemingly sly player who supports Desuta . She refers to Inazuma Japan as "naughty humans," and talks in a rather evil way. She also refers to their actions as "cute," as if making fun of them.

Plot

She plays along with other Makai Gundan Z team members in order to stop Kidou from rescuing Haruna . In episode 108 , she was seen with Desuta kidnapping Haruna. She uses Devil Ball against Kidou during the match, and got surpassed by Tsunami once. Her team was defeated by Kidou's team resulting in Haruna's release. She was not chosen to be a member of Dark Angel .

Recruitment

Inazuma Eleven GO

In order to recruit Arachnes, the following requirements are needed:

  • Item : Tenshi to Akuma no Ningyou (天使と悪魔の人形, randomly dropped from Dark Angel at Gouenji Yuuka's taisen route )
  • Player : Belial
  • Player : Iyabe Satoshi ( Seidouzan 's community master)
  • Manual : Trick Ball

After this, she can be scouted for an amount of 3240 Kizuna points.

Inazuma Eleven GO 2: Chrono Stone

In order to recruit Arachnes, The Lagoon has to be defeated first in the story mode. Also, the following requirements are needed:

  • Player : Belzebu
  • Player : Mephisto
  • Player : Kumo

After this, she can be scouted for an amount of 2600 Kizuna points.

Inazuma Eleven GO Galaxy

In order to recruit Arachnes, the following requirements are needed:

  • Coin : × 3
  • Item : Angel and Demon's Romance (天使と悪魔のロマンス, randomly dropped from Real Legends at the Shadow Person's left taisen route )
  • Item : Supersonic Zipping Car (超速のビュンカート, randomly dropped from Edge Works (エッジワークス) at the Steel Tower Plaza )
  • Item : Tornado Fan (竜巻扇風機, randomly dropped from Volcano Red (ボルケノレッド) in Gurdon 's city )
  • Photo : Smokey Pot (煙たい壺の写真, taken outside Odaiba's Aqua Mall's hihodo store )

After this, she can be scouted.
