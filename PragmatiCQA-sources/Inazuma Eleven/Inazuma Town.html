Inazuma Town ( 稲妻 ( いなずま ) 町 ( ちょう ) , Inazuma Chou ) is where Raimon Jr High is situated in and is the main setting of Inazuma Eleven, Inazuma Eleven GO and Inazuma Eleven GO Chrono Stone.

Landmarks

Steel Tower Plaza

Steel Tower Plaza is the centerpiece of Inazuma Town, beholding the Steel Tower. Endou Mamoru practices the goalkeeping techniques written down by Endou Daisuke in his notebooks here, with a tire tied to a tree.

Town

The town is bustling with shops (notably the sweet shop that Endou visits to buy sweets), and the subway station that leads out of the district (to Teikoku Gakuen, etc.).

Riverbank

It is near Raimon Junior High . This is where both the Raimon Team and Inazuma Japan team trains. The Elementary school students also practice their soccer here. Above is a bridge that faces Raimon Junior High, and the riverbank itself.
