Element Master (エレメントマスター, Eremento Masutā ) is a game exclusive team introduced in Inazuma Eleven GO .

Info

Inazuma Eleven GO

They appear as the sixth team in Geimu ’s lower route. All the team members are level 66 and the match is played in the Pinball Stadium .

Inazuma Eleven GO 2: Chrono Stone

They can be challenged at Tokugawa Yoshinobu 's route after defeating Gourmets . All the players are level 47.

Trivia

  • In Chrono Stone and Galaxy, all of the original series’ 'Big Four', Byakko , Suzaku , Seiryuu and Genbu , as well as GO’s futsal aces, Yamakaoru , Kiyoraka , Rinrin and Hizakuro , are part of this team.
  • In Galaxy, a purple ribbon with the word “elements” was added to the team’s emblem. The color is probably a reference to the fifth element, Void, which was added in the GO series and whose hissatsu’s names are written in purple in the games.
