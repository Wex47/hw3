Info

It first appeared in episode 16 in the match of Inazuma Japan and Storm Wolf . Rolan Lazarev used it and succesfully passed Nozaki Sakura .

It was used again in episode 17 by Rolan. He used it to get pass Minaho Kazuto and succeeded in doing it.

Usage

The user dribbles forward in a straight path, until the background changes to a desert. The user then comes into contact with the opponent as a shining light blinds the opponent, allowing the user to escape with the ball.
