Inazuma Eleven Orion no Kokuin (イナズマイレブン オリオンの 刻印 ( こくいん ) , lit. Inazuma Eleven Mark of Orion ) is the sequel to Inazuma Eleven Ares no Tenbin . It aired from October 5, 2018 till September 27, 2019.

Overview

First announced at "Inazuma Eleven Fes. 2018 + Japan National Team Roster Announcement", Orion no Kokuin continues from where Ares no Tenbin ended, covering Inazuma Japan 's matches in the FFI . The story will focus on Inamori Asuto , Haizaki Ryouhei , Nosaka Yuuma and new character Ichihoshi Mitsuru .
