Info

Inazuma Eleven

It was first used in episode 29 by Kidou to get past Sumisu Eiji and succeeded. It was used again by Demonio Strada .

Inazuma Eleven GO

It was first used in GO in episode 31 by Shimizu Ryuuto to get past Kariya and succeeded.

Usage

The user holds the ball between their legs and does a summersault, letting the ball bounce of the ground, creating three copies of the ball. The balls revolve around the user, confusing the opponent. While the opponent is distracted, the user dribbles through the opponent with the real ball. In the game and in GO, the user has to do a frontflip to make the copies of the ball.
