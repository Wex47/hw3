Azuma Kyou ( 東 ( あずま ) 京 ( きょう ) ) is a scout character introduced in Inazuma Eleven . However, he is only playable since Inazuma Eleven 3: Sekai e no Chousen!! .

Recruitment

Inazuma Eleven 3

You can find him in the techs shop in England's area. You first need to tell him the password and then defeat him and Ootani . The password to scout him is: エンター (in the European games, the password is "letsplay"). He can then be recruited with the Gacha machine by using the red coin at Inazuma Town 's shopping district. He will be at level 8.

Inazuma Eleven GO 2: Chrono Stone

In order to recruit Azuma, the following requirements are needed:

  • Item : Main Album (おもいでのアルバム, randomly dropped from Friendships (フレンドシップス) at Raimon 's schoolyard [past] )
  • Photo : Nostalgic School Chapter (なつかしい校章の写真, taken at Raimon [past] )
  • Player : Shintou

After this, he can be scouted for an amount of 100 Kizuna points.

Inazuma Eleven GO Galaxy

In order to recruit Azuma, the following requirements are needed:

  • Coin : × 7
  • Item : Romantic Astronomical Telescope (ロマンチック天体望遠鏡, randomly dropped from Artist Team at Kisaragi Mako 's left taisen route )
  • Item : Bouquet of Happiness (しあわせの花束, randomly dropped from Chronicles at Kisaragi Mako's taisen route )
  • Item : Angel and Demon's Romance (天使と悪魔のロマンス, randomly dropped from Real Legends at Shadow Person's left taisen route )
  • Record : I want your players (あなたの選手がほしい, trade players through wireless communication )

After this, he can be scouted.

Trivia

  • The Kanji characters, 東京, for his name, Azuma Kyou, are the same Kanji characters for Tokyo .
    • His dub name, Andy, is derived from handy .
  • His classmates are Endou , Gouenji , Aki and Ootani .
  • Shintou can be perceived as Azuma's GO counterpart since both their names are derived from Tokyo and they're both classmates of the main character in their respective timelines.
