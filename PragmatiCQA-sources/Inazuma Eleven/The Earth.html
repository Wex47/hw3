Info

The Earth was used by Raimon as the winning goal against The Genesis . It first broke through the defenders of The Genesis, to which Gran and Ulvida tried to stop it. However, they failed to stop it.

In order for it to work, all hearts must be united as one. Then with this, Fubuki , Gouenji and Endou used The Earth and scored a goal against The Genesis.

In Inazuma Eleven GO Galaxy , this hissatsu has a evolved form called The Earth ∞ .

Usage

Three users stand in a triangle position around the ball gathering it with blue-orange arua. The three then jumps up with the ball as it also go up in a stream of blue energy. As soon as they got up, the ball burst with energy, forming a sphere of blue and green energy. The three users then kick the ball covered in orange energy, making it go towards the goal followed by blue arrows-like energy.
