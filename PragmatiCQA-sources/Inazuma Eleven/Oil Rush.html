Info

Said Ashraf used Oil Rush twice during the match between Inazuma Japan and Shamshir . He was able to score both times with it for his team, easily passing Ibuki Munemasa .

Usage

The user first pounds their fists into the ground, causing it to crack which results in oil sprouting upwards. The user then kicks the ball to the goal, which is surrounded with oil.
