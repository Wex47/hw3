Big Waves (ビッグウェイブス, Biggu Weibusu ) is Australia's national team in the Football Frontier International . They are apparently known as the "men of the ocean".

Uniform

Their uniform is an yellow shirt with green sleeves and green collar, fitted along with green shorts. Socks are yellow, and cleats are dark green. The goalkeeper uniform is light green, long-sleeved with purple sleeves fitted along with dark green shorts, white gloves, purple socks and light green cleats. Nice Dolphin wears a yellow captain's band.

Match

Big Waves Vs. Inazuma Japan

Big Waves dominated against Inazuma Japan through their hissatsu tactic , but Inazuma Japan was able to pull through by discovering the weakness. Inazuma Japan's goalkeeper and captain, Endou Mamoru , evolved his Seigi no Tekken to block Megalodon . In addition to this, Tsunami Jousuke was able to perform a new hissatsu called The Typhoon and Gouenji Shuuya created Bakunetsu Screw , which scored two goals for Inazuma Japan. In the end, Big Waves lost with 2-1 and Inazuma Japan won, which meant that Big Waves have been eliminated from the tournament.
