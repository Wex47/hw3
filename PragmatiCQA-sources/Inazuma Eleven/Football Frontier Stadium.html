The Football Frontier Stadium (フットボールフロンティアスタジアム, Futtobōru Furontia Sutajiamu ), or FF Stadium , is the stadium held for the Football Frontier matches.

Info

As suggested by its name, this stadium is the Football Frontier 's main stadium, in which are held the final matches.

However, the tournament's final wasn't held in Football Frontier Stadium but in Zeus ' one.

It reappeared in the second season of the original series, when Raimon played against Diamond Dust .

It reappeared again in the third season of the original series for the Football Frontier International perliminaries.

The Football Frontier Stadium was seen once again in the Chrono Stone series, when Tenmas played against Protocol Omega .

It reappeared again for the Ares no Tenbin series. It is where the final matches are played.
