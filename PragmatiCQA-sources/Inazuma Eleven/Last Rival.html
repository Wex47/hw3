Uniform

In the first GO game, they wear the away uniform of Dragonlink, which is completely white, while the goalkeeper uniform is mainly black with a white design on the front. In Chrono Stone, they wear Raimon ’s away uniform. In Galaxy, they wear Teikoku ’s away uniform.

Trivia

  • This team is a combination of the players from rival teams which the protagonist teams had to face at the end of all seasons, games and movies ( Zeus , The Genesis , Chaos , Little Gigant , Dark Angel , Ogre , Dragonlink , Zero , The Lagoon , The Excellar , Ixal Fleet and Space Rankers ).
    • Ironically, in spite of this, their Galaxy uniform is a clear reference to Teikoku Gakuen , which was the series’ first ever opponent, although there are no Teikoku players in any Last Rival installment.
    • In the first GO game, Dark Emperors was the only final team to not be represented in this team. Starting with Chrono Stone, Rococo and Walter were excluded from the team’s lineup, meaning that Little Gigant is not represented either.
  • Ulvida is the only female player to appear in any version of this team.
  • All members of Last Rival’s Chrono Stone and Galaxy installments have been captains on at least one other team.
