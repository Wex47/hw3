Fukutsu no Seishin ( 不屈 ( ふくつ ) の 精神 ( せいしん ) , lit. Indomitable Mind ) is a skill.

Effect

This skill increases the user's success rate in command duels when their GP is low and they are sweating.

In Inazuma Eleven SD , it increases the user's hissatsu technique power by 10% when their GP is below 50%.
