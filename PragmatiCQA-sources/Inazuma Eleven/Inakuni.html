Inakuni ( 伊那国 ( いなくに ) ) is the soccer team of Inakuni Jr High , and where most of Inakuni Raimon used to play.

Uniform

Their kit is a full white t-shirt, with a plum collar. They also have plum shorts. The socks are plum until the end of the socks. The shoes are white at the front and blue at the back. The goalkeeper uniform is an orange shirt with white on the underarm to the wrist. There isn't a collar, but there is a blue lining where the collar would be. There is also blue lining at the end of the arms. The shorts are blue and the socks are blue until the top where it is white. The shoes are white at the front and blue at the back for the keeper as well.

Info

Inakuni was seen playing with each other at the beginning of episode 1 . Asuto and Michinari wanted the team to play against a mainland team but Fuyukai said the team was going to be disbanded due to no sponsor. However, Fuyukai arranged Inakuni members to transferred to Raimon to competed in the Football Frontier and gave them a condition that they need to won one match and he would not disband the team.

In Inazuma Eleven Orion no Kokuin , after the Football Frontier International ended, Inakuni members transferred back to their school and competed in the Football Frontier Autumn Tournament.

Sponsor

After the Football Frontier International ended, Inakuni is sponsored by Island Kankou (アイランド 観光 ( かんこう ) ), the tourism company owned by Shimabukuro Ikutarou that was a sponsor for Inakuni Raimon.
