Nippon Daihyou Kouho B ( 日本代表 ( にっぽんだいひょう ) 候補 ( こうほ ) B, lit. Japan Representative Candidate B ) is the second team for choosing the representatives for the national team, Inazuma Japan .

This team is also known as Team B and Kidou Team in the anime, and Team Kidou in the manga.

Uniforms

The uniform consists of the same attire as Team A, only differences being B's jersey shirts are in reverse color (white background, blue stripes), with their shorts being blue. The goal keeper uniform is a dark, navy blue, with white accents, and orange lining on the sleeves and collar. The shorts in this uniform is also navy blue.

Match

Team A vs. Team B

During this match, this was to determine which members will be making the whole Inazuma Japan team. No combination hissatsu allowed, only individual moves. Despite Gouenji and Midorikawa making two goals with Bakunetsu Storm and Astro Break , Team A won in the end, with the score 3-2.
