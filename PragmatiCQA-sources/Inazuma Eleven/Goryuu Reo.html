Profile

Inazuma Eleven 2: Kyoui no Shinryakusha

  • "This sturdy keeper boasts that he could even stop a meteorite."

Appearance

Gorleo has a big body with teal hair and a small nose. He has a pale skin tone and he wears the Gemini Storm goalkeeper uniform.

Plot

Anime

He first appears along with the whole Gemini Storm team and had a match against Raimon . He catches Inazuma Break with only one hand and it shows how powerful Gemini Storm is.

Game

In the game, he first appears with his team. After Gouenji joins the team, Kidou performs Inazuma Break and he stops it with one hand.

Recruitment

Inazuma Eleven 2

You can recruit him from Hibiki's machine after you encounter Reize in chapter 4. He can be found next to the Tower in Tokyo.

Inazuma Eleven GO Galaxy

In order to recruit Gorleo, Ixal Fleet has to be defeated first in the story mode. Also, the following requirements are needed:

  • Coin : × 3
  • Item : Aliea Soap (エイリア石鹸, randomly dropped from The Genesis at Fuyukai Suguru 's right taisen route )
  • Photo : Bodice Log (胴着を着た丸太の写真, taken in Raimon's budoukan )
  • Topic : Far Universe (遥かなる宇宙の話題, obtained on the first floor of Raimon's second building )
  • Topic : Powerful People (パワフルな人の話題, obtained in Raimon's gym )

After this, he can be scouted.
