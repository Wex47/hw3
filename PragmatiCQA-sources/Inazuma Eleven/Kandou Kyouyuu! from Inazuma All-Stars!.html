Kandou Kyouyuu! from Inazuma All-Stars! (感動共有! from イナズマオールスターズ, lit. Share the Excitement! from Inazuma Eleven All-Stars ) is the character song of Matsukaze Tenma , Shindou Takuto , Ibuki Munemasa and Tetsukado Shin . It is sung by their seiyuus, Terasaki Yuka, Saiga Mitsuki, Suzuki Tatsuhisa and Tai Yuuki.
