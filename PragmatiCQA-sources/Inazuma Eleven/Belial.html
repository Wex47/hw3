Profile

Inazuma Eleven 3: Sekai e no Chousen!!

  • "He uses his magic powers to clearly perceive the dark underbelly of the world."

Inazuma Eleven GO

  • "He uses his magic powers to clearly perceive the world's dark underbelly."

Appearance

He has a fair skin and long green hair with "horns" on each side. His eyes are blue.

Plot

He played along with other Makai Gundan Z members to awaken the Demon King and to prevent Kidou from saving Haruna . He was not chosen to play in Dark Angel .

Recruitment

Inazuma Eleven GO

In order to recruit Belial, the following requirements are needed:

  • Item : Tenshi to Akuma no Ningyou (天使と悪魔の人形, randomly dropped from Dark Angel at Gouenji Yuuka's taisen route )
  • Player : Kuniki Tetsuo ( Eito Gakuen 's community master)
  • Player : Nagisawa Nagisa ( Kaiou Gakuen 's community master)
  • Manual : Senpuujin

After this, he can be scouted for an amount of 3240 Kizuna points.

Inazuma Eleven GO 2: Chrono Stone

In order to recruit Belial, The Lagoon has to be defeated first in the story mode. Also, the following requirements are needed:

  • Item : Right Watch (ちょうどいい腕時計, randomly dropped from Ogre at Fuyukai's taisen route )
  • Topic : Mysterious Pencil (不思議なエンピツの話題, obtained at Raimon's second building 2F )

After this, he can be scouted for an amount of 2600 Kizuna points.

Inazuma Eleven GO Galaxy

In order to recruit Belial, the following requirements are needed:

  • Coin : × 3
  • Item : Angel and Demon's Romance (天使と悪魔のロマンス, randomly dropped from Real Legends at the Shadow Person's left taisen route )
  • Photo : Talking Parrot (おしゃべりオウムの写真, taken in room 202 of the Kogarashi Manor )
  • Photo : Acrobatic Dolphin (曲芸イルカの写真, taken in Odaiba's Mermaid Hall )
  • Photo : Yellow Elephant (黄色いゾウの写真, taken in dorm car 4 of the Galaxy Nauts Gou )

After this, he can be scouted.
