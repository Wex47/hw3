Sazanaara (サザナーラ, Sazanāra ) is a planet in Faram Dite 's planetary system. The planet's representative team in the Grand Celesta Galaxy is Sazanaara Eleven .

Inhabitants' appearance

Sazanaara's inhabitants are blue skin humanoids. They are differentiated by two upward strands that have different lenghts and shapes, resembling a horn. Their hair tips have different colours; their hair also seems to be somewhat related to their health, as in episode 25 , after Hilary Flail drained Powai Pichori 's life force, her hair was slightly paler.

Ability

They have the ability of seeing one's Azur (dub: Aethyr ). According to Van Tareru , Azur is seen as a light orb that's attached to a person's head, which represents their emotions through its colour and shape.

It was first shown in episode 24 when some Sazanaara inhabitants had a soccer battle against Earth Eleven and they were able to read Matatagi 's "Dark Side".

Sazanaara Eleven also used this ability during their match with Earth Eleven , intercepting their passes easily. They could also see all of Earth Eleven's Azurs.

Plot

In episode 24 , Earth Eleven arrived in Sazanaara and had a tour through the planet by Ishigashi Gorham . Unlike Sandorius , Sazanaara seems to be very quiet and tame. Its inhabitants weren't welcoming to Earth Eleven, glaring at them with unfriendly eyes. Later, some Sazanaara inhabitants challenged Earth Eleven to a soccer battle. They were able to block Earth Eleven's passes and took an interest on Matatagi. They easily won the match and Guraami approached Matatagi and insulted him as a lonely person who doesn't have someone to care about him.
