Liocott Island (ライオコット 島 ( とう ) , Raiokotto-Tou ), also known as Soccer Island (サッカーアイランド, Sakkā Airando ), is the island where the Football Frontier International was held. It was the main setting for the third season and game of Inazuma Eleven.

History

Apparently, there were two teams who have been fighting on this island which are Tenkuu no Shito and Makai Gundan Z , which oddly decides the fate of the world.

Info

All of the stadiums are in this island to which this is the island where the FFI takes place. There is a large volcano in the center of the island. Around the island are other mini sub islands where there are stadiums that hold the tournament itself. Most of the islands contain a stadium.

Each competing team has a personal camp, so that they may train in much more comfortable circumstances. However, only Japan, Italy, England, Brazil, Cotarl, Argentina and America’s camps were shown and are accessible in the games.
