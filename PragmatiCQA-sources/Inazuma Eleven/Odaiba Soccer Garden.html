Odaiba Soccer Garden (お台場サッカーガーデン, Odaiba Sakkā Gāden ) is a location.

Info

This is the place where Inazuma Japan trains and resides.

In episode 5 , there was the withdrawal exam for some members of Inazuma Japan being performed.

In episode 18 , when the members of Earth Eleven were wondering how they will go to space, the dormitories and the meeting centre combined together and became Galaxy Nauts Gou , a train that will transport them around the space.

Trivia

  • Odaiba is a large artificial island in Tokyo Bay, Japan, across the Rainbow Bridge from central Tokyo.
