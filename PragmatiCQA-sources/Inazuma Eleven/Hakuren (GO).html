Hakuren ( 白恋 ( はくれん ) ) is the new Hakuren team in Inazuma Eleven GO , coached by Fubuki Shirou . They are a participant in the nationals of Holy Road . They played against Raimon in the 1/16 finals in the Snowland Stadium , losing 3-2 in the end.

During the tournament Hakuren were under the control of Fifth Sector , though the majority of the Hakuren's players did not support them. During their match against Raimon, their newly issued coach ordered them to play roughly, but soon after the team turned on him and began to play their "soccer" again.

Uniform

Their uniform is nearly the same as the old one; they wear navy blue shorts with also a white long-sleeved shirt with light blue at the top, white long sockes and nave blue shoes. The goalkeeper wears white shorts with a gray shirt. The captain's mark is red.

Match

Raimon Vs. Hakuren

Hakuren fought Raimon in the Holy Road, they first appeared in episode 26 in Snowland Stadium at the beginning. Hakuren found it easy, since they knew how to run on the icy floor. But it was hard for Raimon, because they were not able to cut through their hissatsu tactics. Hakuren was in the lead after the first half thanks to Yukimura 's Panther Blizzard and his keshin hissatsu Icicle Road . But at the end, Hikaru entered the match and Zettai Shouheki was broken and Raimon scored three goals thanks to Hikaru , Shindou and Tsurugi . At the end, Raimon won the match with 3-2.
