Profile

Inazuma Eleven GO

  • "A tricky manipulator who uses words and actions to influence his foes."

Appearance

His short hair is a dark shade of red-violet which is tied into two buns. His eyes are colored violet and has shaded brown marks around them. He has a pale skin tone.

Personality

Mitsuyoshi laughs maniacally when he released his Keshin , Kijutsuma Purim . When he speaks, he seems to drawl and/or cackle, similarly to a witch.

Plot

Mitsuyoshi was a SEED . During the game against Raimon in the Holy Road tournament, he used his Keshin shoot to score the second goal against Raimon. When he was about to score another goal with Magician's Box , he was stopped by Kurumada 's Dash Train . He was shocked and held his head with both hands when Mannouzaka lost the match with 3-2.

Recruitment

Inazuma Eleven GO

In order to recruit Mitsuyoshi, the following requirements are needed:

  • Player : Shinoyama Mitsuru
  • Item : Mannouzaka Tea (まんのうざかティー)
  • Photo : Monshouiri Sekihi (紋章入り石碑)
  • Item : Karaoke Mic (カラオケマイク, randomly dropped from Trickstars (トリックスターズ) at Raimon 's second building )

After this, he can be scouted for an amount of 1290 Kizuna points.

Inazuma Eleven GO 2: Chrono Stone

In order to recruit Mitsuyoshi, the following requirements are needed:

  • Item : Convenient Seed Notebook (便利なシード手帳)
  • Player : Busujima Kanezane
  • Topic : Beautiful Night Sky (きれいな夜空の話題)

After this, he can be scouted for an amount of 700 Kizuna points. Be aware, in order to recruit him, at least three players have to be scouted first from the same community master to make him a scoutable option.

Inazuma Eleven GO Galaxy

In order to recruit Mitsuyoshi, the following requirements are needed:

  • Coin : × 2
  • Item : World's Slope Catalog (世界の坂道カタログ, randomly dropped from Bad Lucks (バッドラックス) in Inazuma Town )
  • Item : Romantic Astronomical Telescope (ロマンチック天体望遠鏡, randomly dropped from Artist Team (アーティストチーム) on Kisaragi Mako 's left taisen route )
  • Photo : Stone Monument of the Courtyard (中庭の石碑の写真, taken on the terrace of the Inazuma General Hospital )
  • Topic : Singing Voice (うっとりする歌声の話題, obtained outside Raimon's second building )

After this, he can be scouted.
