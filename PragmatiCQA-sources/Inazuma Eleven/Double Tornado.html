Info

To do this hissatsu, a partner with Back Tornado is required.

You can buy this move from the convience store near the station in Inazuma Eleven 2 Blizzard only.

Usage

The user spins as using Fire Tornado , the other spins as using Back Tornado . Then the users kick on the ball and the ball will be surrendered with red and blue lines and goes to the goal.

Trivia

  • It is a combination of Fire Tornado and Back Tornado . However, in the manga, it's a combination of Fire Tornado and Dark Tornado .
  • This hissatsu bears some similarity to Fire Tornado DD in a few ways. Both are the combination of two tornadoes and, upon initiating the hissatsu, the users in both hissatsu spin into the air in a similar fashion.
  • It is also similar to Crossfire and Fire Blizzard . Not only do they all combine fire and wind elements, but they also all create a similar aesthetic when the ball is kicked.
    • One noticeable difference between Double Tornado and the two aforementioned is that this hissatsu is wood element, as if it were combining the elements of the users' combined hissatsu.  On the other hand, Crossfire and Fire Blizzard's types depend on who initiates the hissatsu.
