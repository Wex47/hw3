Tobitaka Seiya ( 飛鷹 ( とびたか ) 征矢 ( せいや ) ) is a supporting character introduced in Inazuma Eleven 3: Sekai e no Chousen!! . He is a defender for Inazuma Japan . In Inazuma Eleven GO , he is a chef at Rairaiken.

Appearance

He has dark purple hair shaped like hawk wings, though a lot of fans depict his hair to be shaped like a moose's or a deer's horns. He has big black eyes with black circle below them. He is also quite tall. In Inazuma Eleven Go, he retains the same appearance but ties a white headband that covers most of his forehead.

Personality

The first time he was introduced, he resembled a gangster and was mostly quiet and sarcastic. After he joined Inazuma Japan , however, he was shown to be a person with a big heart, especially for Hibiki; when Hibiki was in the hospital he and Endou kept waiting until the last moment. Tobitaka is also shown to be good at cooking; perhaps this is why he has a job as a cook at RaiRaiken in GO .

Plot

Before Inazuma Eleven

Tobitaka used to be a gangster, and was known as "Tobi the Kicker" due to his excessive use of his legs in fights. One day his legs were grabbed in a fight, and as he was about to be beaten up, when Hibiki saved him and offered him the chance to play soccer, since Hibiki saw how much of an asset his kicks could be. Since he had never played soccer before, Hibiki taught him to play, and Tobitaka won a place in Inazuma Japan as a result of somehow 'shifting the air'; slowing down Dark Tornado until there is absolutely no more power around the ball.

Season 3

Tobitaka is a slick punk from Inazuma Town who leads a street gang and often gets into fights. He made a name for himself with his powerful kick, and was discovered by Hibiki in the woods in the midst of another fight with a rival gang. Hibiki urged Tobitaka to make "better use of that kick" and began to teach him how to play soccer in secret.

Tobitaka gets a shot at joining the country's national team, Inazuma Japan , and manages to get a spot for himself. He did not play very well but he was able to render Dark Tornado powerless with the air shift that he had created. Slowly, he improves his skills and even becomes a sort of role model to the young Toramaru. He's never seen without his trademark hair-comb.

Before the Asia Finals of FFI against Fire Dragon , Inazuma Japan was harmed by a gang lead by Karasu , a former rival of Tobitaka, on their way to the stadium. Tobitaka bravely stood up and was going to let the team go on without him. Fortunately, Suzume 's gang had also appeared, and helped the team to avoid conflict with Karasu . During the match, Tobitaka was able to master his hissatsu, Shinkuuma , which was used to steal the ball from Suzuno Fuusuke , Nagumo Haruya and Afuro Terumi before they could have the chance to use Chaos Break .

During the match against The Empire , he gives confidence to Tachimukai , which helped him master the technique Maou The Hand .

In episode 123 , he stole the ball from Shinti Hanpa with Shinkuuma V3 and proceeds to make a pass to Gouenji . The striker then tried to score a goal with 真 Bakunetsu Screw , but, much like Someoka and Hiroto 's shoots, it was easily blocked by Rococo 's God Hand X .

Inazuma Eleven GO

Tobitaka appeared in episode 28 . He still has the same hairstyle, but wears a white headband. He works as a chef, just like Hibiki , since he admires him; Hibiki used to teach him all sorts of cooking in the past. He now owns the second branch of Rairaiken ramen shop, which Amagi , Hikaru , and Shinsuke go to. He cooked for them; he also heard them talking about Raimon and how hard it is with Kidou as their coach. As a result, he went to Kidou 's office and brought him some food which he had cooked. He talked to him a bit and left, causing Kidou remember their time with Inazuma Japan .

Movie

Inazuma Eleven the Movie

He joins and helps Raimon fight against Ogre along with Toramaru , Fideo , Hiroto , Fubuki , and Kanon . During the movie, when Ogre begins to injure Raimon's players with the ball, he was the only one who wasn't injured besides Endou . He later stops Death Spear using Shinkuuma .

Recruitment

Inazuma Eleven GO

In order to recruit Tobitaka, the following requirements are needed:

  • Item : Legendary Training Note (伝説の特訓ノート, obtained by beating Furukabu 's lower route (S-ranking the route is not needed) )
  • Player : Megane Kakeru
  • Manual : Fuujin no Mai
  • Manual : Senpuujin

After this, he can be scouted for an amount of 1720 Kizuna points.

Inazuma Eleven GO 2: Chrono Stone

In order to recruit Tobitaka, the following requirements are needed:

  • Item : Tattered Soccer Magazine (ボロボロのサッカー雑誌, randomly dropped from Protocol Omega 3.0 at Handa's taisen route )
  • Topic : Countermeasure (ねぐせ対策の話題, obtained in the Sengoku era )
  • Player : Nisumi

After this, he can be scouted for an amount of 700 Kizuna points.

Inazuma Eleven GO Galaxy

Young form In order to recruit Tobitaka, Ixal Fleet has to be defeated first in the story mode. Also, the following requirements are needed:

  • Coin : × 5
  • Item : Prestigious Trophy (栄えあるトロフィー, randomly dropped by Team Endou at Fuyukai Suguru's taisen route )
  • Item : Tattered Soccer Magazine (ボロボロのサッカー雑誌, randomly dropped from Noisy Nose at Kanda Satoko 's right taisen route )
  • Item : Friendly Dryer (やさしいドライヤー, randomly dropped from Dream Hairs (ドリームヘアーズ) at Odaiba Soccer Garden )
  • Topic : Great Hairstyle (すごい髪型の話題, obtained at Raimon's schoolyard )

After this, he can be scouted. Be aware, to make Tobitaka a scoutable option, at least 10 players from the same community master have to be recruited first.

Adult form In order to recruit Tobitaka, the following requirements are needed:

  • Coin : × 2
  • Item : Cayenne Pepper (烈火のトウガラシ, randomly dropped from Element Master at Handa Shinichi 's left taisen route )
  • Photo : Scooter (スクーターの写真, taken in Inazuma Town's shopping district's arcade hall )
  • Photo : Pork Bun Taro-kun (豚まん太郎くんの写真, taken in Inazuma Town's shopping district's arcade hall )
  • Record : Unbreakable Destructive Power (ありえない破壊力, get a total of 3000 power during a game )

After this, he can be scouted.

Trivia

  • The Kanji character for "Taka", 鷹, in his surname, Tobitaka, means hawk , a reference to his animal symbol and the shape of his hair.
  • In Inazuma Eleven GO , as the owner of the ramen shop Rairaiken, he gets the nickname "Hot Water Tobi" ( 湯切 ( ゆぎ ) りのトビー, Yugiri no Tobī ).
    • Furthermore, despite his adult version being in the first GO game, he isn't recruitable until Inazuma Eleven GO Galaxy .
  • His character song in the Inazuma Eleven Character Song Original Album is " Bad Boys Brother's Blues " with Someoka , Tsunami , and Fudou .
  • He has a habit of combing his hair often, even during some matches.
