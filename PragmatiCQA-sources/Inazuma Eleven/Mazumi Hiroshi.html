Profile

Inazuma Eleven GO

  • "Acts blunt and rough, but really he's incredibly loyal and dutiful."

Appearance

He has spiky orange hair that falls over his shoulders and he has blue eyes. He has a slightly tanned skin.

Plot

He first appeared in episode 36 in a match against an unknown team. He was seen to make a pass to Nebuchi Umitomo , who then scored a goal.

In episode 38 , he and his team fought against Raimon. At first they were leading when Taiyou unleashed his Keshin , but later Shindou and Tsurugi scored a point and they tied with 2-2.

In episode 39 , he got motivated by Taiyou. After Tenma scored with his Majin Pegasus Arc , he lost the ball quickly. At the end of the match, They losed with 4-3 due to Shindou, Tenma and Tsurugi unleashing their Keshin Fusion, Matei Gryphon and scoring the winning point.

Recruitment

Inazuma Eleven GO

In order to recruit Mazumi, the following requirements are needed:

  • Player : Kida Mokuren
  • Item : Tensai Striker Kokoroe (天才ストライカー心得, randomly dropped from Arakumo Gakuen at Furukabu's taisen route )
  • Item : Nekketsu Pendant (ねっけつペンダント)

After this, he can be scouted for an amount of 1920 Kizuna points.

Inazuma Eleven GO 2: Chrono Stone

In order to recruit Mazumi, the following requirements are needed:

  • Player : Hinano Kinsuke
  • Photo : Effort and Guts (努力・根性の写真)

After this, he can be scouted for an amount of 400 Kizuna points.

Inazuma Eleven GO Galaxy

In order to recruit Mazumi, the following requirements are needed:

  • Coin : × 1
  • Item : Romantic Astronomical Telescope (ロマンチック天体望遠鏡, randomly dropped by Artist Team (アーティストチーム) at Kisaragi Mako 's left taisen route )
  • Photo : Fighting Spirit (気合の写真, taken at Raimon 's Budoukan )
  • Photo : 3 Color Cones (3色のカラーコーンの写真, taken on Kogarashi Manor's attic )
  • Topic : Gap to See (見ためとのギャップの話題, obtained in front of Raimon's soccer building )

After this, he can be scouted.

Trivia

  • " Hi " (火) in his name refers to " Kasei " (火星), the Japanese name for Mars .
  • The Mixi Max between Gouenji Shuuya and Someoka Ryuugo is a recolour of him.
  • His dub forename (Deimos) makes reference to both a moon of Mars conforming to his connotations of Mars and the teams wider theme of celestial bodies whereas his surname (Mars) makes reference to a planet of the same name also conforming to both his connotations of both Mars and the wider theme of celestial bodies. Both Deimos and Mars are prominent figures in mythology also implying overtones of Mythology.
