Inazuma Eleven

Appearance

Gori has a gorilla-like appearance. He's quite big and doesn't look like a teenager. He seems to have a habit of swinging because of his shoot hissatsu.

Personality

Along with most of his teammembers, they are noisy and lacks manners.

Plot

He played against Raimon in first round of Football Frontier preliminaries. He used his shoot hissatsu; Tarzan Kick but failed to make it in since it was stopped by Endou Mamoru . In the end, Nose lost by 0-1 score.
