Chouwaza! (ちょうわざ！, lit. Super Technique! ) is a skill.

Effect

This skill increases the power of hissatsu techniques at the cost of using more TP.

In Inazuma Eleven 2: Kyoui no Shinryakusha and Inazuma Eleven 3: Sekai e no Chousen!! , power and TP cost are both increased by 20%.

In Inazuma Eleven GO , power is increased by 25% while TP cost is increased by 50%.

In Inazuma Eleven SD , power is increased by 10%.
