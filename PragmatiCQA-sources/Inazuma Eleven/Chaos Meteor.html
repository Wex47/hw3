Info

It was used in the Inazuma Eleven GO VS Danball Senki W movie. Fran made this hissatsu more powerful, by using the pieces of the LBX stadium. Eventually, it was caught by God Hand W .

Usage

The user covers themselves in dark red energy, then that energy started to transfer to the ball. The user does a backflip and kicks to ball towards the goal.

In the movie, the dark red energy appears to be lines of energy when it is transfered to the ball. Also Fran uses her power to gather rubbles of the LBX stadium to make the shoot larger and stronger.

Trivia

  • It is one of the few hissatsu that didn't have a typography in the movie.
  • In Inazuma Eleven GO Strikers 2013 , Fran uses this hissatsu while Mixi Maxed and wearing Keshin Armed, however those two last during the hissatsu cinematic only. Also, Chaos Meteor without the use of Mixi Max and Keshin Armed is in the games code, but it is only accessible through hacking. Should that hidden version be used, there is also data in the game that allows for Fran to use her voice data for the hissatsu, suggesting that she was, at one point in development, at least considered being given a normal version of the move.
