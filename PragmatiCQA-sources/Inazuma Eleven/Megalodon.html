Info

Inazuma Eleven

It first appeared in episode 71 , used by Joe Jones . It scored the first and unique goal of Big Waves . However, Endou Mamoru later was able to stop it, using Seigi no Tekken G3 .

Inazuma Eleven GO Galaxy

Megalodon reappeared in episode 6 , this time used by Cole LaRuze . It scored twice for Big Waves against Inazuma Japan , as Ibuki Munemasa couldn't stop it bare hands. However, as Cole used it a third time, Ibuki used Wild Dunk and stopped it. The Megalodon here and 10 years back are different in terms of the colour.

Usage

A sea appears on the floor and the user shoots. The ball has an aura of water and is followed by a giant shark. 10 years ago when the shark follows the ball, the aura changes to orange, but now the water aura is maintained.
