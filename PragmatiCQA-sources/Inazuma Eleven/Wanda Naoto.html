Profile

Inazuma Eleven GO

  • "Finds everyday life boring. Collects rumours of supernatural phenomena."

Appearance

Wanda has chest length, braided turquoise hair. He has black eyes, fair skin, and is tall in height. He wears the Kaiou uniform.

Personality

Just like most members of Kaiou, they are loyal to Fifth Sector . Wanda also seems to be caring for the team, just as most members in the their team.

Plot

He played against Raimon along with Kaiou in the Kanto preliminaries finals of the Holy Road tournament.

He first appeared in episode 19 , dribbling toward the goal of Raimon but the ball was stolen from him by Kyousuke, after one of the members scores a goal against Raimon, he smiled like most members.

In episode 20 , he used his keshin for make a assistance to Namikawa . Later, he used his keshin again for make a goal but his shot was stopped by Tenma 's keshin .

Then he used his keshin again a last time and shot but failed due to Sangoku 's new hissatsu technique .

Though in the end, his team, Kaiou lost with 4-3.

Recruitment

Inazuma Eleven GO

In order to recruit Wanda, the following requirements are needed:

  • Player : Ide Norikazu
  • Item : Aranami no Bottle Ship (荒波のボトルシップ, randomly dropped from Kaiou Gakuen at Furukabu 's taisen route )
  • Player : Hijitobi
  • Uniform : Daichi no Wear (だいちのウェア)

After this, he can be scouted for an amount of 3400 Kizuna points.

Inazuma Eleven GO 2: Chrono Stone

In order to recruit Wanda, the following requirements are needed:

  • Player : Touga Tooru
  • Player : Yoshimine Misaki
  • Player : Henge

After this, he can be scouted for an amount of 1690 Kizuna points.

Inazuma Eleven GO Galaxy

In order to recruit Wanda, the following requirements are needed:

  • Coin : × 1
  • Item : Otegaruna Ikari (おてがるなイカリ, randomly dropped from Grand Bangs (グランバングス) at Odaiba's ocean view bridge )
  • Photo : Timeworn Refrigerator (ふるぼけた冷蔵庫の写真, taken on the first floor of Kogarashi Manor )
  • Topic : Far Universe (遥かなる宇宙の話題, obtained on the first floor of Raimon 's second building )
  • Record : Photographer (フォトグラファー, get 100 Kizuna photo's )

After this, he can be scouted.

Trivia

  • Wanda’s dub name (Dakkar Nemo) is derived from a character depicted in two novels by the French novelist Jules Verne (Twenty Thousand Leagues Under the Sea and The Mysterious Island respectively) named Captain Nemo. Captain Nemo is also referred to as Prince Dakkar hence his dub surname.
