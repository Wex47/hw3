Info

Hakuryuu used his keshin and its hissatsu during the match between Raimon and Zero . It scored the second goal for Zero, easily breaking Sangoku 's Fence of Gaia . Hakuryuu used later a more intense White Breath, which was powerful enough to beat Raimon's keshins and Kirino Ranmaru 's Deep Mist , Kariya Masaki 's Hunter's Net and Amagi Daichi 's Viva! Banri no Choujou . However, it was stopped by Sangoku's Fence of Gaia, with the help with Shindou Takuto and Tsurugi Kyousuke .

Usage

The user charges the ball with light using the hand (without touching it), lifting it to Seijuu Shining Dragon's mouth. Seijuu Shining Dragon roars and releases a powerful beam of light at the same time that the user shoots.
