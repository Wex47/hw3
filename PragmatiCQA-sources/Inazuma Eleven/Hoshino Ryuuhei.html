Profile

Inazuma Eleven GO

  • "He considers the star-shaped sticker on his hair a personal trademark."

Appearance

He has spiky blue hair with a yellow star piece accessory and black eyes. He has a fair skin and an average height.

Personality

He seems to care for the team, but he can also be quick tempered.

Because of the recent happening involving with Kuro no Kishidan, he quits the team with no choice along with all Raimon second team members even if Matsukaze persisted them to stay.

Plot

Along with most members of the Raimon second team, they get badly beaten and injured.

Because of this happening, Hoshino quits the Raimon team, like most of the Raimon second team.

He reappeared in episode 40 with the rest of the second team. He watched with his team the finals of the Holy Road , where Raimon played against Seidouzan in the first half and Dragonlink in the second half.

Recruitment

Inazuma Eleven GO

In order to recruit Hoshino, the following requirements are needed:

  • Player : Ishikari Raita
  • Item : Second Gloves (セカンドグローブ, randomly dropped from Raimon 2gun at Kisaragi Mako 's taisen route )
  • Item : Hishigata Pendant

After this, he can be scouted for an amount of 480 Kizuna Points.

Inazuma Eleven GO 2: Chrono Stone

In order to recruit Hoshino, the following requirements are needed:

  • Item : Second Raimon's Theme Song (雷門２軍のテーマ曲, randomly dropped from Raimon 2gun at Handa Shinichi's upper route )
  • Topic : Beautiful Night Sky (きれいな夜空の話題)

After this, he can be scouted for an amount of 360 Kizuna Points.

Inazuma Eleven GO Galaxy

In order to recruit Hoshino, Ixal Fleet has to be defeated first in the story mode. Also, the following requirements are needed:

  • Coin : × 1
  • Item : Miniature Caravan (ミニチュアキャラバン, randomly dropped from Legend 2 Nensei at Fuyukai Suguru 's taisen route )
  • Item : Romantic Astronomical Telescope (ロマンチック天体望遠鏡, randomly dropped by Artist Team at Kisaragi Mako's taisen route )
  • Topic : Far Universe (遥かなる宇宙の話題, obtained on the first floor of Raimon's second school building )
  • Topic : Self-Trend Fashion (自己流ファッションの話題, obtained at Odaiba's ferris wheel )

After this, he can be scouted.

Trivia

  • 'Hoshi' (星) means 'star', referring to the star piece on his hair.
    • His hissatsu, Star Reflection , is also a pun on the star on his head.
  • Hoshino's dub forename (Sirius) refers to the brightest star in the night sky of the same name whereas his dub surname (Starsky) refers to his appearance implying both of his names act as puns on his appearance.
