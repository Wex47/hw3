Info

GO movie

It was used by the young Endou during the match between Raimon and Zero to stop Kibayama 's Fuurinkazan Destroyer and it succeeded in stopping the hissatsu.

Inazuma Eleven GO Chrono Stone

It was used by Endou right after the match between Raimon and Protocol Omega 2.0 which Raimon lost. He used the technique to protect Fei Rune from being sealed by Beta 's Sphere Device , however, it was swallowed up by the device and Endou was sealed instead.

Usage

The user turns back in a similar way to Endou 's first Majin The Hand , and a yellow shine appears from his hand. Then, they face the shoot and releases two golden wings and finally release a bigger, golden God Hand that stops the shoot.
