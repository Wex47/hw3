Profile

Inazuma Eleven

  • "Just a glance at a full moon gives "wolf boy" violent tendencies."

Inazuma Eleven 2: Kyoui no Shinryakusha

  • "The merest glance at a full moon brings out Wolfy's vicious streak."

Appearance

Tsukimura has brown skin and long purple, messy hair. His facial features greatly resemble a werewolf. He has sharp fangs and pointy ears and 3 whisker-like stripes on his cheeks. He also has brown eyes and pointed eyebrows.

Plot

Season 1

His team played against Raimon in friendly match that was intended by his coach to test out only Gouenji 's abilities. However, they ended up losing with a score of 4-3. It was also mentioned that they lost to Shuuyou Meito in the Football Frontier preliminaries quarter-finals.

Inazuma Eleven Reloaded

He was watching the match between Raimon and Barcelona Orb with his teammates.
