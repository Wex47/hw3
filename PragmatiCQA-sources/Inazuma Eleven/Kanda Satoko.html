Profile

Inazuma Eleven GO Galaxy

  • "As a child she used to always play with Kusaka although one day she started distancing herself..."

Appearance

Satoko has pale skin, black eyes, and semi-length redwood hair. She wears a red flower hair clip on the left side of her bangs. She also wears a school uniform from an unknown school.

Personality

During the match against Shamshir, Satoko only observed Kusaka silently, not even wavering any change of emotion even though he attacked members of Shamshir and gained a foul. However, after he punched Tenma who tried to stop him, she began to have a change of expression. Later, she stopped him and told him that she has never hated him which showed that she cared for him a lot.

Plot

She first appeared in episode 8 , watching the match between Inazuma Japan and Shamshir . Later, her younger self appeared in Kusaka's flashback of how he failed to protect her from the boys who bullied her and his second personality awakening.

In episode 9 , she stopped Kusaka from attacking the gang that he injured in the previous episode. She apologized to him and told him that she has never hated him which let him be able to have control over his Berserker Mode. She was happy for Inazuma Japan's victory, especially for Kusaka.

She reappeared again in episode 19 with Kusaka and his gang. When Kusaka told them about Grand Celesta Galaxy , she thought that he was just joking since his gang also thought that as well.
