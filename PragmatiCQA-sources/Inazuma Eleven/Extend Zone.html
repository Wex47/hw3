Info

Inazuma Eleven GO

Hikaru first used Extend Zone during the match between Raimon and Genei Gakuen after he was switched with Kurama Norihito . Receiving the ball from Shindou Takuto , he used it before the flipper could react and broke through Utsurogi Sanemi 's Kage Tsukami , scoring the second goal for Raimon.

It was used once again during the match between Raimon and Dragonlink where it was used to break through Dragonlink's defense. With the help of Nishiki Ryouma , who boosted Hikaru into the air, Hikaru was able to perform Extend Zone in mid-air and it broke through Gomaki Tetsurou 's Majo Queen Redia W and Gouishi Senma 's Bannin no Tou Rook W . Although it lost its firepower, it was meant as a pass for Matsukaze Tenma and Tsurugi Kyousuke .

After Raimon won the Holy Road, the team watched a special program that was commemorated to them on TV, showcasing their hissatsu, keshin and tactics they used and the opponents and their moves they faced during the tournament.

GO movie

Hikaru used it during the match between Raimon and Zero . Instead of shooting it towards the goal, Hikaru used it as a feint to pass the ball to Nishiki and it created a chain shoot with Nishiki's Bushin Renzan . It scored the second goal for Raimon.

Inazuma Eleven GO Chrono Stone

Hikaru used it in a soccer battle against Zanark Domain . It scored the one and only goal of the match, giving Raimon the victory as after their opponents disappeared.

Usage

The user spins the ball with their leg, covering both the ball and the user in a spiral of purple energy. The user and the ball disappear into another dimesion. The user then spins a couple of times before kicking the ball toward the goal covered with a mass of purple energy.
