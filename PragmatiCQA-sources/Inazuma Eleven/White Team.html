White Team is a team formed by most of the Inazuma Japan players and some players from the other FFI teams. In the game this team is known as Kidou Whites (鬼道ホワイツ).

Info

This team can be fought after the story mode is done. The team can be challenged by talking with Kira Hitomiko , as the penultimate team in the lower route. The members' levels range from 68 to 83.

All members from Inazuma Japan are in their " Neo Raimon " form, meaning they have moves and skill sets the normal player cannot obtain in the game. Ex: "Neo Raimon" or " Real Inazuma " Sakuma has Offense Force , Zokusei Kyouka , Koutei Penguin 3gou G5 and Killer Fields V3 . The Sakuma you can obtain in game has a hissatsu set of : Offense Force, Reppuu Dash , Koutei Penguin 2gou and Space Penguin .

Match

White Team Vs. Makai Gundan Z

They had a match against Makai Gundan Z to rescue Haruna, and won with a score of 3-2. First, Makai Gundan Z took the first points because of Black Thunder and Desuta 's Dark Matter . But White Team fought back and scored because of Dylan's normal shot, Toramaru's Gladius Arch and Kidou's, Sakuma's and Fudou's Koutei Penguin 3gou .
