Uniform

This team wears the Inazuma Japan 's alternate uniform.

Info

Inazuma Eleven 3: Sekai e no Chousen!!

This team can only be matched in The Ogre version by tournaments in the Chaos, Angel Ball or Ogre cups. All his members are at level 99, and uses the special version of the Inazuma Japan members with the stats higher than normal. Depending on the rules of the cup, this team can not spend TP.
