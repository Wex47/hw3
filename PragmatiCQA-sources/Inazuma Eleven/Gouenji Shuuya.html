Gouenji Shuuya ( 豪炎寺 ( ごうえんじ ) 修也 ( しゅうや ) ) is one of the main characters of the original Inazuma Eleven series. Introduced in the first game as a transfer student from Kidokawa Seishuu , he soon joined the Raimon soccer club.

After leaving for a period of time during Inazuma Eleven 2: Kyoui no Shinryakusha , he rejoined the team and appeared again in the following game as a representative of Japan's national youth soccer team for the Football Frontier International.

In Inazuma Eleven GO he initially appeared under the guise of the antagonist Ishido Shuuji (イシド シュウジ), the Holy Emperor of Fifth Sector and coach of Seidouzan , but later revealed his true identity and motives. In Inazuma Eleven GO VS Danball Senki W , he is a forward for Inazuma Legend Japan .

In the Inazuma Eleven GO Galaxy series, he acts as the chairman of the Nippon Shounen Soccer Kyoukai .

In Inazuma Eleven Chou Jigen Dream Match , he is a forward for Inazuma Best Eleven .

In Inazuma Eleven Ares no Tenbin , he is a forward for Kidokawa Seishuu . He also plays the forward position for Inazuma Japan in Inazuma Eleven Orion no Kokuin .

Appearance

Gouenji has spiky light-blonde hair, reminiscent of flames, slightly tanned skin and dark brown eyes. His casual outfit consists of an orange jacket over a white hoodie, brown pants and red sneakers.

When wearing the Raimon uniform, he wears orange socks instead of the standard white ones, and he has a habit of raising the collar of any soccer shirt he wears.

In an of Gouenji in the Inazuma Eleven game, he was shown to be wearing a hoop earring in his right ear, however he has never been shown to be wearing an earring in the anime (apart from when he was acting as Ishido in the GO series) or in any of the other games.

As an adult, Gouenji has straight hair. In Chrono Stone, he simply keeps his hair tied in a low ponytail, while in Galaxy he keeps it loose. He also has a darker skin tone.

Ishido Shuuji

As Ishido Shuuji, his hair has azure highlights in it and is kept loose. On his left ear are two pearl earrings, one green and one violet. His outfit consists of a red suit, a white shirt, a gray scarf, and gray combat boots worn over his pants. He also wears two necklaces, a gold one around his neck and the other, which has orange and green beads, over his shirt.

Personality

Inazuma Eleven game description:

Inazuma Eleven 2 game description:

Gouenji is cool and calm in most situations, and capable of quick thinking. He cares deeply for his sister and has a strong passion for soccer.

In the manga, he is portrayed as more mysterious, and is easily annoyed by Endou Mamoru 's attempts to recruit him into the soccer club. Even after joining, at first, he wants to quit because the club isn't functioning well.

In GO, when he was revealed to be Ishido Shuuji, his personality has drastically changed, he starts with having no remorse for controlling soccer. He states that soccer cannot be brought back anymore and he doesn't seem to care for his old friends, teammates and those who love playing soccer. He also started having the "sly" smile while watching the matches in the Holy Road tournament. Though, some of his characteristics like cool, calm and serious remained. He also starts to deny his past identity of being "Gouenji Shuuya" and states his name is Ishido Shuuji when he was confronted by Endou. He does have some guilt in controlling soccer as shown during the series.

In Galaxy, Gouenji is shown to be very serious in his job as chairman of the Nippon Shounen Soccer Kyoukai . He has a close relationship with Kidou Yuuto, as they were friends in the past. He is not easily persuaded, as both Shindou and the vice-coach for Inazuma Japan asked Gouenji to change the coach, Kuroiwa Ryuusei, but he denied.

Plot

Background

Even in his childhood, Gouenji was shown to be a talented player. His parents would often come to his games to cheer for him, saying that he had a talent for soccer and that they were tremendously proud of it. However, after his mother's death, his father, Gouenji Katsuya became a completely different person. Despite his pride for his son in the past, his father wanted Gouenji to quit soccer and become a doctor like himself and has been arguing about this issue with Gouenji ever since.

Before the finals in the Football Frontier — a year before the series' event — which was a match between Kidokawa Seishuu and Teikoku Gakuen , Gouenji's younger sister, Yuuka , she had become a victim of an accident and was in a coma ever since. Due to this, his father's condition grew worse. Yuuka was then hospitalized in Inazuma General Hospital, where Gouenji's father worked.

Ever since the accident, Gouenji thought that if he hadn't played soccer, the accident wouldn't have happened to Yuuka , and wondered how he can simply relax and play soccer when she is suffering. Gouenji keeps an amulet made by Yuuka, transferred to Raimon , and swore that until she wakes up, he wouldn't play soccer.

Season 1

Gouenji's sister, Yuuka , was in a coma because of Kageyama 's interference to stop him from playing against Teikoku Gakuen . He keeps an amulet made by her and vowed to never play soccer again. He first appears when Endou was training with the Inazuma KFC . After seeing a punk spit on the ball and then kicking it to the direction of Kisaragi Mako , he intervenes, shooting the ball back which hit the punk's face. But then he was struck by Endou 's "never give up" attitude telling him that his sister would have wanted him to play soccer the most, he then regains his passion for the game. He changes his promise to become the champion of the Football Frontier . He is firstly not accepted by Someoka , but later, when he sees Gouenji working hard to complete Inazuma Otoshi , his respect for him as a teammate and the ace striker of Raimon grows, and Someoka does not accept anybody other than Gouenji as Raimon's ace striker, as it is seen when he does not accept Fubuki as Raimon's new ace striker after Gouenji left the team in season 2. In this season he was known as the legendary striker.

Season 2

In season 2, he was kicked out of the team after he got distracted in a match against Aliea Gakuen by Coach Hitomiko . Later, Raimon heard rumors of the "Flame Striker" somewhere in Okinawa and traveled there to find him, hoping that he is Gouenji. He is seen hiding in the shadows watching Raimon practice, and even saw the confrontation with Burn and Gran . Later on, in the match against Epsilon Kai , Gouenji views the match in a disguise (an orange and black hood) and it is shown that he and Hijikata Raiden know each other. Later on in the match, three mysterious men try to take Gouenji away but he tricks them with the help of Onigawara . He runs back to the match and rejoins the team at the last minute. The Raimon Eleven are delighted that he returned to them. Together once again, they finally beat Epsilon Kai with Gouenji's Fire Tornado and with his new hissatsu, Bakunetsu Storm .

Season 3

In season 3, he was a candidate to join Japan's national team, Inazuma Japan , and was placed in Team B , Kidou Yuuto 's team. During the selection match, he made his team first goal with Bakunetsu Storm . Later, he and some of his teammates were chosen by Coach Kudou , to represent Japan. Later, he was almost out of the team again before the finals, because his father wanted him to study in Germany and become a doctor. However, he was able to show his father how much he loves soccer and wants to stay with the rest of the team, as he and Toramaru bring out Tiger Storm . He makes a new hissatsu with Hiroto and Toramaru between the match with The Empire , even though they lose with the score of 2-1. Later he followed the Kidou's team into the depths of hell to the Demon's Gate , in an effort to save Haruna from Makai Gundan Z .

In the match of Inazuma Japan vs Unicorn, Gouenji used Bakunetsu Screw to try and score, but it failed against Billy Rapid 's Flash Upper because Domon Asuka used Volcano Cut V2 to weaken the shot. However, in the second half, he used it again to score the final and winning goal for Inazuma Japan using Bakunetsu Screw.

Then afterward when Inazuma Japan was fighting against The Kingdom , he scored the second point for Inazuma Japan, along with Someoka and Hiroto in a chain shoot consisting of their individual hissatsu. He scored a goal with Toramaru using Tiger Storm against Team Garshield . He, along with Toramaru and Endou scored the winning goal for Inazuma Japan against Little Gigant in the finals using Jet Stream which broke through Rococo 's Tamashii The Hand G2.

In episode 126 , he graduated from Raimon, and when it was Endou's turn to receive the diploma, Gouenji along with the rest of Raimon was seen crying because they were about to go their separate ways.

Then in episode 127 , they had one last graduation match with each other, which ended in both groups not scoring a goal, since they were all equally matched. All of them listened to Endou's last speech, and promised that they'll keep on playing soccer from Endou's word that he said "Let's play soccer!" to which everyone happily agreed on.

Inazuma Eleven Ares no Tenbin

Gouenji first appeared in Kozoumaru 's flashback in episode 3 . While Kozoumaru was chasing a thug who had stolen a lady's purse, Gouenji stopped him with Fire Tornado. He found out that Kozoumaru wasn't a good defender, no matter how hard he had been training and advised him to look on the opposite side. Since that day, Kozoumaru became a big fan of Gouenji and started to play as a forward.

Before Kidokwa Seishuu 's match against Seishou Gakuen , he was being interviewed by Tominaga Jun . Gouenji said that Haizaki was both their strong point and their weak point because unlike Kidokawa's strong teamwork, Haizaki's preference to work alone prevented his team from having one as well. Haizaki saw his interview on TV after he went out of the hospital where he visited Akane and got annoyed by Gouenji's words. Haizaki then came to Seishou Gakuen's clubroom, where the members were discussing strategies for their next match. Kidou told them they had to be careful at Gouenji, but Haizaki refused to believe he would be that bad. Much to Haizaki's surprise and dismay, he discovered he had to play as a goalkeeper.

The match started and Gouenji shot towards the goal, using Fire Tornado. Haizaki froze when the shot came towards his goal and was unable to catch it. Fortunately, two of Seishou's defenders stopped it. Kidou complimented Gouenji, saying that he expected no less from him. Gouenji told him that Kidokawa's teamwork wasn't always that good and he had a flashback which showed him after returning to his old team as a reinforcement committee. The Mukata triplets still believed they were better forwards, so they requested Gouenji to have a 3 to 3 contest to see who is better. Gouenji chose Hoshino and Mito in his team, two first-years from Kidokawa's second team. The Mukata triplets thought he was intentionally trying to lose since he chose weak people in his team. However, Gouenji's team managed to win due to their better teamwork than the brothers'. They accepted their loss and after that, they started to work better with their teammates, which is what Gouenji was trying to achieve in the first place.

He combined his Fire Tornado with the brothers' Triangle Z to create the override hissatsu, Bakunetsu Storm, which scored the first goal for his team. After Haizaki let in another goal, shot by the brothers' Triangle Z, he left the goal and ran to the opposing team's goal to try to score, but he got stopped by Nishikaki. Seeing that Seishou's goal was empty, Gouenji tried to score with Fire Tornado, but all of Seishou's defenders manage to stop it after combining their forces. He scored another goal with Fire tornado after he stole the ball from Sasotsuka Eiji in the second half, but in the end, Kidokawa lost with a score of 3-4.

He was later seen watching Inakuni Raimon 's matches against Zeus and Tonegawa Tousen together with Kazemaru. They were joined by Kidou, Endou and Sakanoue to watch the match against Outei Tsukinomiya in the finale.

Inazuma Eleven Orion no Kokuin

In Episode 001 (Orion) Gouenji met with Endou and Kidou at the stadium where the members of Inazuma Japan were announced. They were very excited about the upcoming ceremony and were talking about Raimon ’s reinforcement committee members whose work raised the level of Japan's soccer. Later when he was sitting at the stands with his teammates, Gouenji was wondering who Zhao Jinyun would choose. It turned out that he was selected as one of the forwards for the team. His interest aroused Ichihoshi Mitsuru who played on an overseas team. Later Gouenji gathered with his teammates in Inazuma Japan's uniform and celebrated their selection. The next day during the first training, Inazuma Japan was surprised with an unexpected visitor who was the captain of the Spanish team Barcelona Orb , Clario Orvan . The fact that he was going to face alone against the 18 players, shocked the team even more. Clario easily passed all of them, one by one and score a goal with his technique Diamond Ray . Inazuma Japan was overwhelmed by what happened but Clario stated that their level raised. He revealed that he saw one Japan's player whose technique astonished him and now he wanted to back to his country to practice more. When the team seemed to lose their fighting spirit, Gouenji stated with a smile that Clario's intention wasn't to scare them but see how serious they were.

During Episode 002 (Orion) first match in the FFI preliminaries Inazuma Japan played against South Korea's team Red Bison . Gouenji with Haizaki was main forwards in this match. Right after the beginning, Inazuma Japan began to attack but Korea reacted quickly and steal the ball from them. They attempted to score but when all their actions failed, Red Bison started to play rough. Haizaki was upset about their dirty play and Gouenji explained that it was Korea's strategy to take away Japan's composure. After Red Bison scored their first goal before Park Ji-Won obstructed Endou's view, Japan stood shocked and in disbelief. Gouenji admitted that Korea's striker wasn't just ordinary. When Japan figured out how to avoid their attacks, it was time to use Juu to Gou tactic. The team got through the opponent's defense and Gouenji presented his spectacular shot Last Resort thanks which Inazuma Japan scored their first goal. Gouenji explained to Kazemaru and Hiura Kirina that he's been training day by day to compete with the world and this shot was the answer that his soccer has given. It turned out that it was Gouenji who amazed Clario Orvan during the practice, made him appreciate Japan's soccer. When Gouenji intended to score once again, he got injured his leg after he was blinded. Japan's players gathered around him asking if he was alright. Gouenji admitted that something blinded him for a second and now he wasn't sure if he was able to continue this match. With the help of Ichihoshi, he left the field and sit at the bench. After the first half of the match ended, Kidou kept asking Gouenji about how he felt. Gouenji stated with a smile that he wasn't a doctor to know how serious was his wound. He told Kidou that he couldn't see what happened on the field but something shone into his eyes just for that moment, making Kidou wondering if the opponent's team was cheating. Gouenji agreed that something strange was with this match. Kidou was seriously worried about Gouenji and confessed to him that he wanted to go to the world with him and Endou, he didn't want to lose him now as well.

Gouenji was hoping that his injuries wouldn't interfere with him playing in the future matches. Ichihoshi came to him clearly absorbed by his state and brought him another bag with ice cubes. When the match resumed Kira Hiroto replaced Gouenji as a forward, next to Haizaki. Soon Inazuma Japan scored their second goal after Inamori Asuto ’s shot. When Hiroto managed to avoid fatal injury after he was blinded, Gouenji said it was a good job avoiding that. The match ended with Inazuma Japan winning with a score 3-2. While the team celebrated their success in their locker room, Gouenji's wound got dressed and the man from the medical section told him it would definitely need time to be healed. Endou came to him and ensured that they would try their best until he came back. Gouenji stated that the team would be definitely able to handle it and would continue their way to the victory. Ichihoshi once again offered him help and accompanied him on a journey to the hospital. Sometime later Kidou visited Gouenji in the hospital as well. He stated that his injuries weren't so bad as he thought earlier, although it would be rather difficult for him to return for the Asia preliminaries. Gouenji looked at the picture of his sister Gouenji Yuuka and Kidou said that she had to be really worried about him. Gouenji admitted once again that there was something specious in the tournament. The fact that Gouenji got injured after a simple collision didn't make sense for Kidou either. Gouenji confirmed that he saw the light at the same moment when he was knocked down, making Kidou wondering if he was intentionally blinded by someone. Later the other members of Inazuma Japan came to visit Gouenji, laughing and joking. Goujin Tetsunosuke stated that he would replace Gouenji as an ace striker until he healed, causing the team to feel embarrassed. Although they had a common goal, he was going to play seriously against Japan with all powers they got. Gouenji stated that Inazuma Japan would be number one and then take back soccer. Gouenji smiled, it was something expected from Kidou. He told him not to worry about that because he was going to pass it to someone else in Inazuma Japan. When Kidou stated that it was impossible to inherit that technique, Nosaka appeared saying that three people could do it. Episode 021 (Orion) before the departure to Russia Gouenji met with Endou and Kidou at Steel Tower Plaza . Bergamo Regult teased Gouenji saying that Japan always bluffed so easily and Clario calmed him down, stating that everything would turn out during the match. Later when the American team Star Unicorn appeared, everyone assured each other that they were all against the Orion Foundation and would protect the soccer. Gouenji along with Endou, Domon Asuka and Ichinose Kazuya recalled their former plays for Japan. They were joking about Ichinose being more American than Japanese. While they were waiting for the bus, Gouenji, Endou, and Kazemaru were recalling their first match with Spanish team Barcelona Orb. Trivia

  • The second kanji in Gou en ji (炎) means flame, in reference to his element and most of his hissatsu.
  • All of his hissatsus are shoot hissatsus.
    • In an interview in the January 2014 issue of Animage, Hino Akihiro reveals that Gouenji's name was originally going to be called Gouenji Harusuke ( 豪炎寺 ( ごうえんじ ) 春介 ( はるすけ ) ).
  • He is one of the few people in the series to be left-footed.
    • There are seven hissatsus he uses his right foot for, however; Honoo no Kazamidori , Inazuma Otoshi , Inazuma 1gou Otoshi , Koutei Penguin 2gou , The Earth , Jet Stream and Prime Legend
  • His classmates are Endou , Aki , Ootani and Azuma .
  • He was the only player on Inazuma Japan to play every minute of the FFI tournament .
  • It's known that after Inazuma Eleven 3: Sekai e no Chousen!! , he played for a pro-league team.
  • As stated by the commentator of the match between Raimon and Dragonlink , Gouenji had used Fire Tornado DD when he was playing for a pro-league team. The person he used it with, however, is unknown.
  • Because his hair had fallen in the GO series instead of it being spiky, in the Crossover movie it was seen before the match that he uses gel to do his hair exactly like it was in the original series, as a teenager.
    • However, in the manga, he keeps his hair as it is currently instead of spiking it.
  • Of the characters included in the Inazuma Eleven Strikers series, Gouenji has the largest number of combination shoot hissatsu.
  • In the Galaxy game, he is the only character that can be recruited in six different forms (including his Mixi Max form with Fubuki ).
  • He wears the number 10 in all the teams he played in.
    • Except in Ares no Tenbin , as he wears the number 17 in Kidokawa Seishuu .
  • In Inazuma Eleven Ares no Tenbin , he's known as the Flame Ace Striker (炎のエースストライカー).
  • Gouenji goes to the barber on the 13th of each month, as revealed by Takita Atsushi , the owner of the beauty parlor "The Edge", in the sixth episode of Inazuma Eleven Outer Code .
  • In the Inazuma Eleven SD Twitter event Choujigen Valentine 2020 (超次元バレンタイン2020), where people tweets to gift chocolates to their favorite characters, he's in top 3 winners and get the limited White Day-theme figure release to the game.
  • He has some character songs: Honoo no Riyuu , Ashita no Hero (with Shindou Takuto ) and Honoo no Pride (with Fubuki Shirou and Someoka Ryuugo ).
  • A scene from the Drama CD revealed that he makes delicious Takoyaki but doesn't do this in front of Rika because he said he didn't want to end up like Ichinose .
  • He appeared as an obtainable Yo-kai in the collaboration with Yo-kai Watch Puni Puni , another game by Level-5.
  • In Inazuma Eleven Go Strikers 2013, he was intended to have Heat Tackle, Spark Edge Dribble and Screwdriver and can all be used with a voice via hacking.￼
