Profile

Inazuma Eleven GO

  • "An aggressive and instinctive player. Does "Kira" remind you of anyone?"

Appearance

Kira has dark forest green hair styled in spikes. He has a fair skin, and black eyes. Compared to other members of the Raimon second team, he is a bit short, despite that he has a lot of leg strength.

Plot

After his team gets beaten by Tsurugi , he quits the Raimon team after discussing it. Overall, all the members of the Raimon second team has quit.

He reappeared in episode 40 with the rest of the second team. They also return to the second team. He and his team had a match against the first team in order to help Tenma in his new role as Raimon 's captain.

He watched with his team the finals of the Holy Road , where Raimon played against Seidouzan in the first half and Dragonlink in the second half.

Recruitment

Inazuma Eleven GO

In order to recruit Kira, the following requirements are needed:

  • Player : Momoyama Gamon
  • Item : Second Gloves (セカンドグローブ, randomly dropped from Raimon 2gun at Kisaragi Mako 's taisen route )
  • Player : Koishi
  • Topic : Middle School Friends (中学のともだち, obtained at Raimon )

After this, he can be scouted for an amount of 1220 Kizuna points.

Inazuma Eleven GO 2: Chrono Stone

In order to recruit Kira, the following requirements are needed:

  • Player : Kanenari Atsunori
  • Player : Oshii Tsuyoshi
  • Player : Kotegawa Sakio
  • Photo : A Pine Watching the Castle (城を見守る松の写真, taken in the Sengoku era )

After this, he can be scouted for an amount of 630 Kizuna oints.

Inazuma Eleven GO Galaxy

In order to recruit Kira, Ixal Fleet has to be defeated first in the story mode. Also, the following requirements are needed:

  • Coin : × 2
  • Item : Miniature Caravan (ミニチュアキャラバン, randomly dropped from Legend 2 Nensei at Fuyukai Suguru 's taisen route )
  • Photo : Family Photo Frame (家族の写真立ての写真, taken at Kogarashi Manor room 201 )
  • Topic : Parents (両親の話題, obtained around Raimon's gym )
  • Topic : Osananajimi (幼馴染の話題, obtained in Inazuma Town )

After this, he can be scouted.
