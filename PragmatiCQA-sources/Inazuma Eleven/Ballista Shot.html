Info

It was used during the Holy Road finals between Raimon and Seidouzan by Kurosaki Makoto . The shoot was too fast for Sangoku Taichi , as it approached the goal very quickly, so he didn't have enough time to use a hissatsu to stop the shoot, scoring the first goal for Seidouzan.

Usage

First, a ballista will appear behind the user. The user twirls and kicks the ball while in midair, and the ball will start to have a green glow. As the ball heads toward the goal, the giant ballista will also shoot a javelin from its barrel.
