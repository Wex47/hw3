Setsuyaku! (セツヤク！) is a skill.

Effect

This skill reduces the TP consumption of hissatsu techniques by 25% in the original trilogy, 20% in GO trilogy and 5% in Inazuma Eleven SD .

If the resultant TP after this move's modifier is applied is not a whole number it will be rounded to the nearest whole number.

  • For example, if a hissatsu technique costs 32 TP in Inazuma Eleven GO , the skill would reduce its consumption by 6.4 TP (20%) and give a resultant cost of 25.6 TP, therefore the actual cost of this move in game would be 26 TP after rounding.
