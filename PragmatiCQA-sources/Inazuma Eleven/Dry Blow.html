Info

It first appeared in episode 9 used by Sultan Karam . It failed twice to stop Parkour Attack and Bicycle Sword but it was able to stop Tenma 's God Wind , with some help from Shamshir 's defense by lesser the power of the shoot. Later it was used against Kusaka 's Kyoubou Head but failed.

Usage

The user charges their hand with heat energy and then hits the ball on the side, making it spin. The ball continues to spin until it burns, stops and crumbles into ashes.
