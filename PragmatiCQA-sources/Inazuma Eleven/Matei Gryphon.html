Info

Anime

Matei Gryphon was used during the match between Raimon and Arakumo Gakuen , at the Desert Stadium in the semi-finals of the Holy Road . Shindou, Tsurugi and Tenma fused their keshin and were able to stop Amemiya Taiyou 's Sunshine Force from advancing, revealing Matei Gryphon. Taiyou then decided to battle Matei Gryphon with his keshin, Taiyou Shin Apollo , and the Raimon trio were struggling with the battle, since Taiyou was able to push Sunshine Force towards them, until their teammates started to encourage them. Due to their encouragement, they were able to win the battle and sent Sunshine Force towards Arakumo's goal. Sata Tosamaru called his keshin, Teppeki no Gigadoon , but failed to stop the shoot from the Raimon trio, so they scored the fourth and winning goal for Raimon.

Movie

Tenma, Shindou and Tsurugi used Matei Gryphon in the match of Raimon against Zero . It was able to beat Shuu 's and Hakuryuu 's Sei Kishi Arthur and then scored the fifth goal with Sword of Fire for Raimon.
