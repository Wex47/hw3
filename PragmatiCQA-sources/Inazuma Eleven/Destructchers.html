Destructchers (デストラクチャーズ, Desutorakuchāzu ) is an antagonist team introduced in the Inazuma Eleven GO VS Danball Senki W movie.

Info

This team appeared in the crossover movie and fought against Shinsei Inazuma Japan . At the end of the match, they lost with 3-2.

Uniform

The uniform for the Destructchers have two shades of black: a darker one, and a lighter shade. The shirt is mostly of the lighter shade, with jet black on the sides, as well as bright red circular patches on the shoulders and the front. The shorts are also of this pattern: lighter black on the front, and darker black on the sides, with thin red stripes on the front. The socks are of the lighter shade of black, with a single red stripe on the front. The cleats are mostly jet black with a red stripe through the center. The captain's band is cyan.

The goalkeeper's uniform is mostly white or light gray, and instead of red stripes, he has purple stripes and circular patterns around the outfit.
