Profile

Inazuma Eleven GO

  • "Ogre's goalkeeper boasts that he can bring down any kind of shot."

Appearance

His eyes have thick black borders and he wears black and red goggles which covers his right eye only and wrap across his forehead. He is extremely large, has blonde spiked hair, and dark skin. In addition, he also has two very large bottom teeth that extend up past his nose.

Personality

He has an antagonistic personality and doesn't seem to care what happens to others that much.

Plot

He came from the future along with the Ogre team to stop Raimon in the Football Frontier . However, Ogre lost to Raimon 3-2 and returned to the future.

Recruitment

Inazuma Eleven GO

In order to recruit Zagomel, the following requirements are needed:

  • Item : Ogre Ninshikihyou (オーガ認識票, randomly dropped from Ogre at Gouenji Yuuka's taisen route )
  • Player : Sandayuu Mishima
  • Player : Gebo Torangas
  • Player : Bubo Torangas

After this, he can be scouted for an amount of 4550 Kizuna points.

Inazuma Eleven GO 2: Chrono Stone

In order to recruit Zagomel, The Lagoon has to be defeated first in the story mode. Also, the following requirements are needed:

  • Player : Sandayuu Mishima
  • Player : Drache Gyunter
  • Player : Daikko Buuka

After this, he can be scouted for an amount of 4000 Kizuna points.

Inazuma Eleven GO Galaxy

In order to recruit Zagomel, the following requirements are needed:

  • Coin : × 3
  • Item : Ookami Puppet (オオカミのパペット, randomly dropped from Nihil Niyaris at Kanda Satoko 's right route )
  • Item : Pixie Doll (ピクシーたちの人形, randomly dropped from Galaxy Rivals at space alien's right taisen route )
  • Photo : Pork Bun Taro-kun (豚まん太郎くんの写真, taken in Inazuma Town's shopping district's arcade hall )
  • Record : Miracle Performer (ミラクルパフォーマー, get 30 celebrations )

After this, he can be scouted.
