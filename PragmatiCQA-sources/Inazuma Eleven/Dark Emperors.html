Dark Emperors (ダークエンペラーズ Dāku Enperāzu ), is a main antagonist team in the Inazuma Eleven series, the final team to be played in season 2 and Inazuma Eleven 2: Kyoui no Shinryakusha .

History

The Dark Emperors were the original members of Raimon under control of the Aliea Meteorite , and the last team of the Aliea Gakuen . The team's captain was Kazemaru Ichirouta . The team only had one match and the members have disbanded.

Uniform

Their team jerseys are dark blue and light blue jumpsuits, with long sleeves, and stopping up to above the knee. The light blue and dark blue colors are patterned like a 'V' shape, with a light blue strip going down each sleeve, and they have white shoulder pads on the jerseys. Their cleats (or rather, boots) are white with a dark grey toe cap, like The Genesis . They wear white gloves with this. The goalkeeper outfit is about the same as the normal jersey, but just with a recolor; the dark blue and light blue is replaced by grey and yellow (respectively), the shoulder pads are dark grey in color, the gloves are dark grey with yellow patterns, and the cleats are yellow. The only difference between the goalkeeper and the normal version are that the jumpsuit goes longer than other one. Kazemaru assumes the role of captain, but he doesn't have a captain band in the anime, though in all Wii games he does and it's red.
