Onigawara Gengorou ( 鬼瓦 ( おにがわら ) 源五郎 ( げんごろう ) ) is one of the supporting characters in the Inazuma Eleven series.

Appearance

He appears to be in his 50's, has brown hair, tan skin, and a grey beard. He usually wears yellow trousers, a cream-colored shirt with a forest green coat outside.

Plot

He works behind the scenes investigating things that can upset the balance of Endou Mamoru or any other soccer team. These problems are usually caused by Kageyama Reiji . He heads to the main FFI grounds to find out what Kageyama's true objective is. While talking to Mamoru, he tells him that he was a big fan of the original Inazuma Eleven and Daisuke , and despite not knowing soccer, he still loved the game. The reason he became a detective was to investigate Inazuma Eleven 's so-called accident .

In season 2, he is helping Gouenji Shuuya to hiding during his sister 's kidnapping by Aliea Gakuen . During most of this Season, he is investigating Aliea Gakuen and their plans. After Second Raimon defeats Aliea Gakuen best team The Genesis , he asked Kira Seijirou the reason why Seijirou undertook the Genesis plan is because Seijirou's son Kira Hiroto was killed by a accident in 10 years ago. After Aliea Gakuen collapses by the hands of Kenzaki Ryuuichi , he arrests Seijirou for his what he had done to Japan.

In season 3, he is seen investigating Kegeyama Reiji's part in the FFI and his boss, Garshield's. In episode 107 , he was injured by Garshield 's plot, but was able to arrest him after the match between Team Garshield and Inazuma Japan .
