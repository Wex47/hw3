Info

This was used during the match of Dark Emperors and Raimon . It scored a goal by breaking through Mugen The Hand .

Later it was used in the representive match against Inazuma Japan A but it was stopped by Endou thanks to Tobitaka .

Later, in episode 127 , he evolved it to 真 Dark Tornado, but failed due to Endou 's 真 Ijigen The Hand .

Usage

Dark Tornado is very similar to Fire Tornado . The user leaps and spins vertically upwards in the air with the ball covered with dark, purple flames, unlike the orange flames from Gouenji 's Fire Tornado . The user then kicks the ball towards the goal.
