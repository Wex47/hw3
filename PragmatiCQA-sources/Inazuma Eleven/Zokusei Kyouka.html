Zokusei Kyouka (ぞくせいきょうか, lit. Element Strenghthening ) is a skill .

Effect

This skill boosts the user when they have an elemental advantage over the opponent.

In Inazuma Eleven 2: Kyoui no Shinryakusha , the user's element is boosted by 10%.

In Inazuma Eleven 3: Sekai e no Chousen!! , the user's element is boosted by 150%.

In Inazuma Eleven SD , the user's element is boosted by 10%.
