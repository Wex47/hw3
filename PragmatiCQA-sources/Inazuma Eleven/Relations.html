Trivia

All of the members have a relative on the team, hence the name, Relations:

  • Endou Mamoru and Endou Kanon (great-grandfather & child)
  • Touchi Shuuji and Touchi Ai (brother & sister)
  • Fubuki Shirou and his Fubuki Atsuya form (twin brothers)
  • Otonashi Haruna and Kidou Yuuto (brother & sister)
  • Tenjin Usuke and Tenjin Atarou (brothers)
  • Gouenji Masato and Gouenji Shuuya (cousins)
  • Fumio and Kazuo (brothers)
  • Yousuke and Amehiko (twin brothers)
