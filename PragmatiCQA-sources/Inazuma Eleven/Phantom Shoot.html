Info

It was used in episode 4 by Yuukoku Hiroyuki and Tsukimura Kenichi .

Obtainment

Inazuma Eleven 2: Kyoui no Shinryakusha

Phantom Shoot's manual can be bought in Hokkaido's Hissatsu store for 250 Nekketsu Points.

Usage

The user kicks the ball up from behind with their heel, jumps up and turns 180 degrees to face the ball. Then the user then kicks the ball, causing it to glow purple and splitting apart into 6 different shots.
