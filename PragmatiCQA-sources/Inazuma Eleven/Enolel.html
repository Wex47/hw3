Profile

Inazuma Eleven GO

  • "He seems to be able to harness supernatural powers using mushrooms."

Appearance

He has a pale skin tone and short emerald green hair that slightly resembles a maitake mushroom. He is tall in height and he has somewhat of a muscular build. He also has a single grey eye.

Personality

Enolel is courteous, competitive, and apologetic, as shown when he apologizes to Tenkuu no Shito during the match when he was not able to stop Fideo 's Odin Sword .

Plot

He was first seen in episode 109 , as the goalkeeper for Tenkuu no Shito. Inazuma Japan took a hard time breaking through his hissatsu Holy Zone , however, it was later broken by Edgar 's Excalibur . Later, their team was defeated by Inazuma Japan resulting in the release of Rika . He was not chosen to be a member of Dark Angel .

Recruitment

Inazuma Eleven GO

In order to recruit Enolel, the following requirements are needed:

  • Item : Tenshi to Akuma no Ningyou (天使と悪魔の人形, randomly dropped from Dark Angel at Gouenji Yuuka's taisen route )
  • Player : Piiman
  • Player : Kettle
  • Topic : Tabehoudai no Mise

After this, he can be scouted for an amount of 3240 Kizuna points.

Inazuma Eleven GO 2: Chrono Stone

In order to recruit Enolel, The Lagoon has to be defeated first in the story mode. Also, the following requirements are needed:

  • Item : Right Watch (ちょうどいい腕時計, randomly dropped from Ogre at Fuyukai's taisen route )
  • Topic : Taguimaredake (タグイマレダケの話題, taken in Grinwell Village )

After this, he can be scouted for an amount of 2600 Kizuna points.

Inazuma Eleven GO Galaxy

In order to recruit Enolel, the following requirements are needed:

  • Coin : × 3
  • Item : Cayenne Pepper (烈火のトウガラシ, randomly dropped from Element Master at Handa Shinichi 's left taisen route )
  • Item : Angel and Demon's Romance (天使と悪魔のロマンス, randomly dropped from Real Legends at the Shadow Person's left taisen route )
  • Item : Bazugun no Kakushiaji (バツグンの隠し味, randomly dropped from Good Men's (グッドメンズ) at Inazuma Town 's station's entrance )
  • Photo : Aqua Mall Guide Plate (アクアモール案内板の写真, taken in Odaiba's Aqua Mall )

After this, he can be scouted.
