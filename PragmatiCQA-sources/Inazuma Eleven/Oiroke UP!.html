Oiroke UP! (おいろけUP！, lit. Sexy UP! ) is a skill.

Effect

This skill increases the user's success rate in command duels against male characters. In Inazuma Eleven 2 and Inazuma Eleven 3 , it was gender locked to female players but, starting from Inazuma Eleven GO , it is no longer gender locked.

In Inazuma Eleven SD , when the opponent is male in the command battle, it reduces the power of his hissatsu technique by 10%.
