Info

It was first used in episode 42 by Amase Reia in the finals of the Holy Road tournament. He succeeded in stealing the ball from Matsukaze Tenma .

Usage

The user first creates a sphere of air in their hands by swiftly rotating his/her arms, he/she then lets it drop and then kicks it at their opponent, blowing them away while the ball goes to the user.
