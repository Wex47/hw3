Info

This team first appeared in Inazuma Eleven 3: Sekai e no Chousen!! Spark and can be optionally challenged after finishing the game. It also appeared in The Ogre version of the game.

Once you connect to Inazuma Eleven 3 Bomber that has beaten Makai Gundan Z, you can fight Dark Angel . In The Ogre version of the game, their players are not recruitable.

Uniform

Players of Tenkuu no Shito wear a long-sleeved one piece skin-tight uniform. It is white with a light blue stripe pattern, black near the top. There is a black and red target-looking shape in the middle. They wear a golden ring on both arms. Socks are black and shoes are golden-colored. The goalkeeper wears a yellow suit instead of a white one with a black part to their arms. The captain wears a mark that is orange in colour.

Plot

The angels of Tenkuu no Shito have fought against Makai Gundan Z many centuries prior to the series to seal away a demon lord, and their task is to keep it sealed. They captured Rika in order to make her marry the Demon King so they can seal him again. The Red Team goes up Mount Magneto and arrives at Heaven's Garden , confronting them in a match with Rika's future on the line. They are able to break through Ijigen The Hand Kai and block Edgar 's Excalibur , but eventually lose by 2-1.
