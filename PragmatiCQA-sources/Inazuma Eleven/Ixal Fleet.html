Ixal Fleet (イクサルフリート, Ikusaru Furīto ) is an antagonist team and the final team in Inazuma Eleven GO Galaxy .

Info

If you play in the story mode, Ixal Fleet will be the final team you face in the Galaxy game. Though when being affected by their tactic, Berserk Ray , Galaxy Eleven will replace your original Earth Eleven in the second-half.

In Galaxy's taisen route , Ixal Fleet can be challenged at the space alien's route in Sazanaara , being the seventh team and the members' levels are 74. They can also be challenged at Natsumi 's route, being the eleventh team and the members' levels are 99.
