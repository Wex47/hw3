Raimon Souichirou ( 雷門 ( らいもん ) 総一郎 ( そういちろう ) ) is the founder of the Raimon Junior High School and the father of Natsumi . He's also a big fan of the Inazuma Eleven . In GO, he is one of the Resistance members.

Profile

Inazuma Eleven GO Galaxy

  • "He used to be the chairman of Raimon. Natsumi's dad and Hikibi's old classmate."

Appearance

He wears a green-gray suit coupled with a white shirt and a red tie, as well as red-framed glasses. He has brown hair tied in a small braid and shaved at the sides and a beard of the same colour. He is very tall in height.

Plot

Season 1

He promised Raimon he'll improve the soccer club if they won the preliminary matches, but Endou Mamoru didn't accept it. He was involved in a bad accident which saddened Natsumi greatly.

Season 2

In episode 39 , as Raimon returned to Inazuma Town , he talked with Hitomiko and Hirai Shinzou . He explained that after seeing all of Aliea Gakuen 's attacks, he estimated that their base would have to be somewhere near Osaka. He also appeared episode 64 , calling Zaizen Sousuke to say that he ws on the way to Raimon. This was because they heard that Raimon were fight against Dark Emperors , which was made up of most of the original Raimon members. They were also powered up by the Aliea Meteorite .

Season 3

In episode 68 , he brought Natsumi to the airport so she could travel. After that, he called Gouenji so that he could remove him from the team because of Gouenji Katsuya 's reasons. In episode 126 , he was happy seeing his daughter graduate.

Inazuma Eleven GO

He appeared in GO in episode 18 , with Hibiki Seigou and the old chairman of Raimon , as a member of the Resistance .﻿
