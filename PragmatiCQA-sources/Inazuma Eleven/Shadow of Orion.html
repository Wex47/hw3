Shadow of Orion (シャドウ・オブ・オリオン, Shadou obu Orion ) is Irina 's personal team.

Uniform

Their uniform is black with bright purple stripes on the sleeves and neck. The symbol of Orion is also on the uniform in the same purple color. The goalkeeper's uniform has the same patterns, except the shirt is gray with black stripes. All of them wear black shorts. The captain's band, worn by Yurika Beor , is a bright teal color.
