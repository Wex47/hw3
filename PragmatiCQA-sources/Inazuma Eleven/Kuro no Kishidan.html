Kuro no Kishidan ( 黒 ( くろ ) の 騎士団 ( きしだん ) , lit. Black Knights ) is the team that Tsurugi Kyousuke was the captain of before joining Raimon .

Uniform

Kuro no Kishidan's members wear a black shirt with yellow linings and, a yellow lightning bolt, black shorts, yellow black striped socks and black shoes. The goalkeeper wears a white long-sleeved shirt with a vertical dark red stripe with a blue-green collar, white and, blue-green gloves, dark red shorts, dark red white striped socks and blue-green shoes.

Match

Kuro no Kishidan Vs. Raimon

They played a match against Raimon, easily overpowering them with Tsurugi 's keshin and hissatsu . They could have won with a score of 13-0 but Kuroki Zenzou called off the match.
