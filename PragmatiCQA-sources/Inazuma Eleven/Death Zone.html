Info

Original series

Death Zone was first used in episode 2 during the match between Raimon and Teikoku Gakuen . It was used by Sakuma Jirou , Jimon Daiki , and Doumen Shuuichirou to score Teikoku's eleventh goal. The hissatsu was later used again, but was stopped by Endou Mamoru 's God Hand .

Death Zone was used again during the regional finals between Raimon and Teikoku in episode 13 , but was blocked by Domon Asuka , injuring him.

In episode 56 , Endou, Kidou , and Domon began trying to learn Death Zone during a friendly match between Raimon and Teikoku Gakuen with them playing for Teikoku. After numerous failed attempts, they were eventually able to master it, despite their timing being out of sync. This disharmony in their version of the shoot led to the creation of Death Zone 2 .

Inazuma Eleven Ares no Tenbin

Death Zone was used during the match between Inakuni Raimon and Seishou Gakuen . Haizaki Ryouhei and two other members of Seishou used the hissatsu and Umihara Norika wasn't able to stop the shoot, tying the score 1-1.

Death Zone was later used again during the match between Seishou Gakuen and Kidokawa Seishuu . It was used to score the final goal in the match, passing Oogosho Yakata , thus giving Seishou Gakuen the 4-3 win.

Usage

In the original series, one user runs forward while two other users emerge from behind and jumps upward. The first user then jumps up with them, all three users spinning as they jump into the air. The three users form a triangle formation around the ball. The three users all individually spin, forming a purple triangle. The three users then slowly move closer to each other. Instead of individually spinning, they all spin in a circle on top of the ball, causing it to glow in a purple aura. The three users all kick away from the ball, causing it to fly towards the goal.

In Inazuma Eleven Ares no Tenbin , the one user gets ready to kick the ball while the other two users run forward. The first user kicks the ball into the air and jumps up with the other two users in a triangle formation, surrounding the ball. The three users spin, forming a purple triangle. The ball in the center is covered in a purple aura with purple lightning being pulled in it. Each user then makes contact with the ball, with the last user doing a bicycle kick. The ball leaves the triangle and flies toward the goal.
