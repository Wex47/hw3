Furukabu ( 古株 ( ふるかぶ ) ) is the driver for the Inazuma Caravan in the Inazuma Eleven series. During the match between Gemini Storm and Inazuma Eleven he replaced Hibiki Seigou as the goalkeeper.

Profile

Inazuma Eleven GO Galaxy

  • "He works as a school clerk at Raimon. The oldest of his look-a-like brothers."

Appearance

He is quite large-bodied and has a white mustache. His uniform is similar to a janitors uniform, and he also wears a maroon or sometimes white cap. He's got big, round nose.

Plot

Inazuma Eleven

He first appeared in episode 5 where he is seen talking to Raimon about the legendary Inazuma Eleven . He seems to know many of its players. In the Aliea Gakuen Arc, he is the one who drives the Inazuma Caravan.

Inazuma Eleven GO

He appeared in GO in episode 2 where he is seen to be the referee for the match between Raimon and Kuro no Kishidan .

In order to recruit Furukabu, the following requirements are needed:

  • Coin : × 2
  • Photo : Massara Blackboard (まっさらな黒板の写真, taken on the third floor of Raimon's second building )
  • Photo : Gymnasium Podium (体育館の壇上からの写真, taken in Raimon's gym )

  • Record : Miracle Performer (ミラクルパフォーマー, get 30 celebrations )

After this, he can be scouted.
