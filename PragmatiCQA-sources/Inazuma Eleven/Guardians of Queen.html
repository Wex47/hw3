Guardians of Queen (ガーディアンズオブクイーン, Gādianzu obu Kuīn ) is the representative team for Italy in the Football Frontier International in Inazuma Eleven Orion no Kokuin .

Info

Italy is under the direct control of Irina and wear mysterious supporters on their arms and legs. According to Sekiya Tomoari in episode 45 the supporters are said to 'amplify the power' of the users 'muscles' causing there speed and strength to be boosted.

At the beginning of Episode 44 Zhao Jinyun describes the team as being 'guardians who are absolutely loyal to their one and only queen'. He proceeds to exclaim that 'the one they serve isn't a queen. She is a cold and ruthless witch'.

Uniform

The team jersey is a white top with a curved blue stripe in the middle of it; the team shorts are also the same shade of blue. The goalkeeper's outfit is a dark navy blue top with a white patch on the left (covering the shoulder and the sleeve). The captain's band is a bright teal color.
