Info

It was first used in episode 28 by Yasha to block Fei after he Mixi Maxed with Tyrano . It was later used twice in episode 29 to block Tenma .

Usage

The user jumps up and spin several times as two small fireballs gather on their feet. The user then slide tackle the opponent from above with a shape of a screw.

  • Gouenji Shuuya was supposed to have this move in Inazuma Eleven GO Strikers 2013 : they didn't make it unlockable but it was voiced.
