Info

It was first used against Nose in the Football Frontier in episode 6 . It scored and made 1-0 for Raimon .

It is used again in episode 8 against Mikage Sennou , but was blocked by Rocket Kobushi .

In episode 13 it combined with Inazuma 1gou to form Inazuma 1gou Otoshi to break through Genda 's Full Power Shield .

It was used for the last time against Senbayama in episode 18 as one of the many failed attempts to break through Mugen no Kabe .

Usage

The two users jump up one by one, then the second user leans back for the first user to step on and do an overhead kick. The ball is charged with blue electricity and is kicked forward, followed by a blue beam and blue electricity as the first user kicks it.
