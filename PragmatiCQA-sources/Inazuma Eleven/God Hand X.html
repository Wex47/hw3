Info

Inazuma Eleven

God Hand X, firstly used by Rococo , was able to stop many of Inazuma Japan 's shots like 真 Bakunetsu Screw , Dragon Slayer V3 and Ryuusei Blade V3 but it was finally broken through by Hiroto 's Tenkuu Otoshi .

Later, when Cain Saito replaced Rococo as the goalkeeper, he was able to stop Tenkuu Otoshi with the help of Walter 's Ground Quake . Later, this hissatsu evolved into 改 to block Grand Fire G2 and then into 真 to block Koutei Penguin 3gou G3 . However, it finally was broken by Big Bang .

Inazuma Eleven GO Chrono Stone

During El Dorado Team 01 's training for their upcoming match, Sangoku successfully used 真 God Hand X to stop Dhanna 's Shoot Command 06 . It was then revealed that Sangoku learned this hissatsu from Endou Daisuke and practiced it during the training with everyone else who wasn't chosen to timetravel.

It was later seen again in episode 41 . Sangoku succeeded in catching Rodeo 's Heavy Aqua Lance and then evolved it to 絶 level to stop Jipusu 's shoot and succeeded too.

Movie

It was used twice in the Inazuma Eleven Chou Jigen Dream Match movie by Rococo. He used it the first time to block Gouenji 's Bakunetsu Screw and succeeded. He used it again to block Gouenji and Tsurugi 's Fire Tornado DD , but failed to stop it.
