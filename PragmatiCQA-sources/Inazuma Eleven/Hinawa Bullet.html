Info

It first appeared in episode 13 . It was used three times by Kutou and Maebayashi . The first two times, it scored a goal but the third time, it was stopped by Shinsuke by using his mastered Keshin Armed .

Usage

Kutou kicks the ball to the air and loads the gun, while Maebayashi coats his right foot with fire. He does a front flip and scissor kicks the ball in a way like Back Tornado and lights the rope, while Kutou assists by kicking it with him.
