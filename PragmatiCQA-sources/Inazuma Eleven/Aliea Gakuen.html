Aliea Gakuen (エイリア学園, Eiria Gakuen ; dub: Alius Academy) is an academy consisting of 'alien' teams that fight using the power of the Aliea Meteorite , with the exception of The Genesis , who were trained to be the best by competing against the teams that have used the power of the Aliea Meteorite.

The man who started the academy, Kira Seijirou , had originally intended to use it for military purposes, but the Prime Minister Zaizen Sousuke , Touko 's father, declined. As the Prime Minister loved soccer, Seijirou used it for soccer purposes to cause destruction in Japan until the Prime Minister Zaizen Sousuke accepted.

Aliea Gakuen turns out that they aren't aliens, instead they are the human children from Ohisama En Orphanage.
