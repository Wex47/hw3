Info

Endou was able to create this hissatsu because his grandpa , disguised as Little Gigant 's coach, gave him a hint to block Edgar 's move during the competition. It debuted in episode 88 during Inazuma Japan's match against England's representatives, Knights of Queen . Ijigen The Hand blocked Edgar Valtinas's all-powerful technique, the Excalibur used at the other side of the pitch with power added from Dragon Slayer , but wasn't able to block Fideo 's Odin Sword or Queraldo 's Sling Shot .

Endou used Ijigen The Hand again during the match between Inazuma Japan and Unicorn . The technique was broken by Ichinose Kazuya 's Pegasus Shot the first time it is used, but Endou managed to stop it the second time. The third time Endou faced this shot, despite Pegasus Shot breaking through Ijigen The Hand, it's deflected into the goal post and into Endou's hands. The catch technique was broken through easily by Mark Kruger and Dylan Keith 's Unicorn Boost and Ichinose, Mark and Dylan's Gran Fenrir later in the match, letting in the score 2-1 and 3-2 respectively.

In episode 106 , during Inazuma Japan's match against Orpheus , Endou evolved Ijigen The Hand into Ijigen The Hand 改 and defended the goal from Hide Nakata 's Brave Shot , though later it was broken through by Sein 's Heaven Drive .

In episode 111 , he was able to evolve it again during the fight against Dark Angel , it became 真 Ijigen The Hand and it stopped Shadow Ray but Roniejo 's Strike Samba V2 and V3 and Coyote 's Gunshot broke through 真 Ijigen The Hand easily, which made Endou having to create a new, more powerful technique for the finals against Little Gigant. In episode 118 , Rococo found a resemblance in God Hand X and Ijigen The Hand involving the hand movement and the way of gathering energy for the move.

Usage

One of the user's fists glows into a yellowish aura. The user then jumps up in the air, and hits the ground with the fist making an 'outer dimension barrier', making any shoot move upwards along the 'outer dimension' unless the shoot is too powerful, which then the ball breaks through the "outer dimension" barrier and crashes into the goal.
