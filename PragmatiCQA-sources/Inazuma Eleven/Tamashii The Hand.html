Info

Anime

This catch technique was created by the wish to win of Rococo that seeing Big Bang , instead of using God Hand X after crossing his arms on chest he summoned this new hissatsu from the heart. It is the strongest technique Rococo has. It was used in episode 125 , and stopped Big Bang.

Rococo used this technique again near the end of the match to stop Jet Stream , but latter is so ridiculously powerful, even the evolved G2 form of this technique used is not enough to stop it, letting in the winning goal of 3-2 by Inazuma Japan .

Game

It has a TP of 60, that is the highest TP for GK moves, it is cited that it is one of the most powerful catch hissatsu, along with Omega The Hand , Maou The Hand , The End , Holy Zone and High Voltage as well as Jikuu no Kabe in the third game.

Usage

The user crosses his/her arms over their chest and gather energy to finally release a red energy hand. He/she then direct the red energy hand to stop the incoming shoot.
