Info

Inazuma Eleven

Inazuma Break was used for the first time in the match against Senbayama . It successfully broke through Mugen no Kabe , scoring the first goal for Raimon , as well as breaking Senbayama's record for no points lost.

Inazuma Break was used again against Gemini Storm , but it was effortlessly stopped by Gorleo .

Inazuma Break was used once again against Diamond Dust . However, the ball was stolen by IC as Kidou kicks the ball up, leaving the goal wide open since Endou joined the attack as the goalkeeper.

Inazuma Break was used again against Neo Japan , in its V2 form. It successfully broke through 真 Mugen no Kabe to score the second goal for Inazuma Japan .

Crossover movie

It was used in the crossover movie in a combined attack with Fire Tornado DD and Last Death Zone to destroy the Holy Road stadium scoreboard in order to get rid of the LBX army.

Inazuma Eleven Reloaded

Inazuma Break was used during the match between Raimon and Barcelona Orb after Alonso easily stopped Gouenji 's Fire Tornado with one finger. Alonso threw the ball back, and Raimon decided to use the Inazuma Break instead. However, Alonso easily stopped it by jumping on the ball.

Obtainment

Inazuma Eleven 3: Sekai e no Chousen!!

The manual for Inazuma Break can be bought for 10000 Nekketsu points at Cotarl's hissatsu store.

Usage

The first user kicks the ball to the air while the second and third run past him. Then the ball creates a purple sphere. The purple sphere creates lightning, and it strikes downwards with the ball. The three users jump, the first one with a normal kick, and the second and third doing a bicycle kick. They all kick the ball at the same time while in the air, and the shot heads towards the goal.

In Inazuma Eleven Reloaded , the first player kicks the ball in the air. The ball leaves a trail of purple lightning, with dark clouds forming, almost as if it's being pulled into the ball, as it reaches higher in the air. As the ball reaches higher, the three players jump towards it, leaving a white trail behind them. All three players turn upside down, and kick the ball at the same time. As the ball heads toward the goal, it is now covered in a yellow aura, with purple lightning coming out of it.
