Info

Inazuma Eleven GO

It first appeared on scans, then in game and finally onscreen too in episode 34 , against Raimon , in the 1/4 finals of the Holy Road in the Pinball Stadium . It scored two goals for Genei Gakuen .

It's used again in episode 35 and scored a third goal. But it was finally blocked by Amagi's Atlantis Wall .

Inazuma Eleven GO Galaxy

It was used in the practice match between Inazuma Japan and Resistance Japan to score the second goal for the his team, easily passing through Ibuki .

Usage

The user crouches down with the ball, creating many large green flames that quickly fly into the ball, causing it to spin and glow green. The user then kicks the ball causing the ball to fly forward, while disappearing and reappearing. The ball disappears and reappears, passing through the opponent's hissatsu.
