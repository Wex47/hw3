Info

Tobitaka first used an incomplete version to block Shadow 's Dark Tornado in the match between Inazuma Japan A vs. Inazuma Japan B.

Tobitaka mastered the move in the match versus Fire Dragon as he used it to steal the ball before letting Aphrodi, Nagumo and Suzuno perform Chaos Break.

The move evolved to V2 in the match against the Kingdom to stop Roneijo's Strike Samba V3

It was later used by Tobitaka in the FFI Final and it was already in its V3 state.

Usage

The user spins around, pulling their right leg back causing wind to form near the user's foot. The user spins around again and kicks, creating a blackish, purple rift, drawing in air and the ball, like a vacuum. This hissatsu can be used to steal the ball from opponent player, or used to stop an incoming shot. If it's an incoming shot, the ball will be engulfed in a transparent blue sphere and comes to a stop.

There are two incomplete variation for this hissatsu. The first variation, the user pulls their right leg back and kicks upwards, creating an air current that stops the incoming shot. The second variation, the user jumps in front of an incoming shot and swings their right leg upwards quickly, creating an air drift that stops the ball.
