Info

Inazuma Break CG was used by Endou , Gouenji and Kidou after Endou runs up the field having sent the ball flying in the opposite direction using Super Megaton Head . The technique went past Procyon before he had a chance to use his hissatsu technique , scoring the second goal for Zhao Jinyuns .

Usage

It's similar to Inazuma Break , however, the electrifying purple energy around the ball is now greater than the previous one.
