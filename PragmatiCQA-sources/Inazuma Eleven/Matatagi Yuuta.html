Profile

Inazuma Eleven GO Galaxy

  • "Second son of the Matatagi family. Always taking care of his brother."

Personality

He is very cheerful along with his brother, Shun . Both of them are very supportive of their older brother, Hayato .

Plot

He is first seen in episode 2 calling his older brother along with Shun. Hayato then showed some few tricks of controlling the ball that he learned recently, which amazed them. Tenma then arrived and complimented their older brother saying that he's getting really good. As Tenma was leaving, the two young brothers said something to Hayato. However, he states that they shouldn't talk about it here and the two make a promise.

The next day, the Matatagi brothers are shown to be watching the match intently along with the Raimon members, and the brothers do this for most of Inazuma Japan's matches.

In episode 17 , he and his brother watched the match and fell asleep by the end of the match due to a strange light that made audience put to sleep.
