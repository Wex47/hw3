Info

Inazuma Eleven

Shippuu Dash is a technique created by Kazemaru used for the first time in the second match against Teikoku in episode 13 to pass Sakuma . It is stronger, faster, and gives off a purple shine when used with the Aliea Meteorite .

Inazuma Eleven GO

This hissatsu appeared again 10 years later in the Inazuma Eleven GO Movie in the match against Zero . Kazemaru used it and successfully passed Hikita Goushirou .

Inazuma Eleven Ares no Tenbin

Kazemaru used this hissatsu in the match between Teikoku Gakuen and Inakuni Raimon . He used it to successfully dribble past Hiyori Masakatsu .

Usage

In the original series are two variations of this hissatsu. In the first variation, the user dashes in a zigzag pattern rapidly, dodging an opposing player. When under the Aliea Meteorite's control, the user is seen stepping with their right, then left foot. They then dash forward very quickly with their arms extended. A purple glow is seen emitted where the Aliea crystal is worn.

In the game, user moves so fast that their movements cannot be seen clearly before they disappear out the opponent's sight, jump upward, and reappear behind them.

In Inazuma Eleven Ares no Tenbin , the user waves their right arm and swing it back. The user then raises both of their arms back and wind starts to form. As the user runs forward, large gusts of wind complete surrounds the user and they dash forward very quickly, blowing the enemy player forward.

Obtainment

Inazuma Eleven 2: Kyoui no Shinryakusha

Shippuu Dash's manual can be bought in Nara's Hissatsu store for 150 Nekketsu points.
