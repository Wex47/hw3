Info

Dragon Tornado was created with Dragon Crash and Fire Tornado . Dragon Crash was easily stopped by Yugamu Kuukan twice since Someoka looked at Nata Juuzou 's hands. Gouenji worked this out and Someoka shot Dragon Crash upwards, for Gouenji to shoot downwards. From there Megane Kakeru gave the shot its name. Dragon Tornado has scored 4 times in the first season. It scored 2 goals against Occult in episode 4 , 1 against Mikage Sennou against Sugimori Takeshi 's Shoot Pocket and the last time it scored a goal was when it broke through Genda Koujirou 's Power Shield . In the match against Senbayama , it was stopped by Mugen no Kabe . In the match against Zeus it was stopped by Poseidon's Tsunami Wall . In the first match against Gemini Storm it was easily stopped by Reize .

Obtainment

Inazuma Eleven 2: Kyoui no Shinryakusha

Dragon Tornado's manual can be bought in Okinawa's hissatsu store for 2800 Nekketsu points.

Usage

One user uses Dragon Crash to kick the ball up, then another user uses Fire Tornado to kick it forward. The blue energy turns red and the ball is set ablaze. The dragon also turns red and follows the ball.
