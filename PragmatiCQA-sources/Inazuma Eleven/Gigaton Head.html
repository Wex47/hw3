Info

It was used in the movie as a chain shoot with Sparkle Wave to score the first point for Inazuma Legend Japan .

Usage

The user stomps his feet at the ground to strengthen the head and to face it towards the ball. Then, a yellowish aura releases from the head that's similar to the aura released in Megaton Head , form a fist as it hit the ball to the user's head. The user then release the energy fist with the ball toward the goal.
