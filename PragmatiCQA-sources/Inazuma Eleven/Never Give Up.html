Never Give Up (ネバーギブアップ, Nebā Gibu Appu ) is a skill . When it was introduced in Inazuma Eleven GO , it became a keshin skill.

Effect

This skill increases the success rate of all teammates if the user's team is losing.

In Inazuma Eleven GO onwards, the effect lasts as long as the keshin is active.

In Inazuma Eleven SD , it increases the power of all teammates's hissatsu if the team is losing, with more goal difference increases more power.
