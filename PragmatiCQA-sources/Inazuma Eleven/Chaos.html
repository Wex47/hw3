Chaos (カオス, Kaosu ), also called The Chaos (ザ・カオス, Za Kaosu ), is a team formed by the merging two teams: Diamond Dust and Prominence from Aliea Gakuen .

Info

When Gazel and Burn found out that Gran 's team, Gaia, was chosen to be The Genesis , they decided to merge their team, Prominence and Diamond Dust to defeat Raimon to become The Genesis themselves. When they play against Raimon, Prominence's players were not cooperative with Diamond Dust. The match was 11 for Chaos and 7 for Raimon, but the match was interrupted by Gran, since they were not allowed to make a merged team. This team has the upper hand in both attack and defense.

In Inazuma Eleven 2: Kyoui no Shinryakusha , they can be fought post-game by connecting both version of the game.

In the Inazuma Eleven GO game, they appear as the third team in Gouenji Yuuka 's lower route. All the team members are around level 64.

Uniform

The shirt's color are red with dark gray collars and azure pattern on it, shorts are dark gray, socks are dark blue, and cleats are dark gray, white and blue. The goalkeeper's uniform is long-sleeved, dark gray with purple stripes and white top, shorts are dark gray, and the gloves are white. Burn wears the captain's band, which is black.
