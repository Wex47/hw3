Info

This team is a stronger version of Epsilon.

All the members' eyes are completely red, and have a slight change on their facial expressions, being more evil-looking. On the team formation for Epsilon Kai, Desarm 's position is switched with Zel 's position, making Desarm a one-top forward.

Uniform

The only difference in their original uniform is that their eyes have become red.
