Info

This team did not play against Raimon despite its captain, Burn , meeting the team during the search for the " flame striker " in episode 48 . He pretended to be the "Flame Striker", though the striker in the rumor was actually Gouenji Shuuya . Prominence later teamed up with Diamond Dust and formed Chaos , because they planned to overthrow The Genesis (Team Gaia ) and steal "The Genesis" title.

Uniform

Prominence's uniform consists of a long sleeved, white shirt, with reddish-orange colored sleeves, and patterns, with a purple orb-like object embedded in the middle of the shirt. This is fitted with a dark grey shorts, socks in the same shade of red as sleeves, and dark grey cleats. The goalkeeper uniform is dark gray, long-sleeved with red patterns, purple orb-like object embedded in the middle of the shirt, fitted along with red shorts, white gloves, socks in white and purple and red cleats. Burn assumes the captain's band, which is white.
