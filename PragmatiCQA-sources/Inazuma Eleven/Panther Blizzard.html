Info

Inazuma Eleven GO

It is first seen in the newest trailer of Inazuma Eleven GO , being used by the ace striker of the current Hakuren team, Yukimura Hyouga . It is said to be the evolution of Eternal Blizzard since this is Yukimura's first personal hissatsu technique as he learned it after the original Eternal Blizzard with help of Fifth Sector. It is first used in the anime in episode 26 . It gets to the goal before Sangoku could use his Fence of Gaia due to its speed, but failed on his second attempt.

Crossover movie

It appeared when members of Shinsei Inazuma Japan were fighting with a Danball Senki W group. Yukimura became angry and used Panther Blizzard at Asuka Koujou. However, the hissatsu missed its target.

Inazuma Eleven GO Galaxy

It was used by Yukimura in the match between Resistance Japan and Inazuma Japan in episode 14 . It made a chain shoot with Hakuryuu 's White Hurricane and easily scored the first goal.

Usage

The user jumps in the air and spins to the right, using the back of their right foot to kick, creating a blue slash. The user spinning around, then kicks upward using their left leg, creating another blue slash, creating a large snow storm. Through the storm, a pair of eyes ignite red, revealing a large white panther, with the ball covered in a white cloud snow, pulling in large amounts of snow. The user jumps up again, spinning to the right, kicks the ball with their left leg. The ball flies forward with the panther running beside it.
