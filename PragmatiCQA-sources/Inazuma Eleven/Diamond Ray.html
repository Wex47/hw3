Info

Inazuma Eleven Reloaded

Diamond Ray was used by Clario during the match between Raimon and Barcelona Orb . He used it to score the last goal for Barcelona Orb and it easily passed Endou Mamoru as the shoot was too fast for him to stop it.

Inazuma Eleven Orion no Kokuin

Clario used his Diamond Ray after he got past the forwards of Inazuma Japan . The shot blew away the midfielders and defenders, as well as Nishikage Seiya and Saginuma Osamu . Endou Mamoru tried stopping it with his Fuujin Raijin , but the shot was too powerful, and the shot went in.

Clario used Diamond Ray again during the match between Muteki no Giant and Inazuma Japan, scoring the first goal for Spain, just right after the beginning of the match. Later Clario used it again but the shot was stopped with new Endou’s technique Diamond Hand , after it evolved from Super God Hand . Later Clario used it one more time to punish Reinaldo Baraja who turned out to be one of the Disciple of Orion and removed him from the field. Clario tried to score again using this technique but this time he tricked Endou and curl the ball around him, scoring the second goal for Spain. Later, Clario used this curved Magaru Diamond Ray again, but this time, Endou managed to think of a new technique with Nishikage's help, being Diamond Arm , and he saved the shot.

Usage

The user stands, and the ball rises. The user kicks the ball quickly multiple times around the edges, shaping the ball into a round diamond. After that the user kicks the ball with a sideway kick towards the goal.
