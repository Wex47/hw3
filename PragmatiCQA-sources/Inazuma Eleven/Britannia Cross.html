Info

It first used during the match between Raimon and Teikoku Gakuen by Itsumi Kunihiko and Sasaki Iwao . They successfully passed Hayami Tsurumasa .

It was used once again during the Holy Road finals between Raimon and Dragonlink but this time by Ichino Nanasuke and Aoyama Shunsuke . They succeeded in passing through Gouishi Senma , who used Bannin no Tou Rook W to stop them, and Senguuji Yamato was surprised that they only used a technique to pass through a keshin user.

After Raimon won the Holy Road, the team watched a special program that was commemorated to them on TV, showcasing their hissatsu, keshin and tactics they used and the opponents and their moves they faced during the tournament.

Usage

Two players are involved in this technique. The two users jump in the air, in an "X" formation. A glowing, blue runic circle then forms in the air above the target. The users ascend just above the runic circle and kick the ball simultaneously, in which a X Blast -like wave strikes the opponent.
