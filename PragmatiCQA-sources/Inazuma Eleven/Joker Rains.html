Info

Game

It is first appeared in scans, then in the GO game and in Wii games. To get this move in the Wii games, 75% friendship between Shindou and Tsurugi is required.

Movie

It was used by Shindou and Tsurugi and it scored a goal after Fudou gave some advice to Tsurugi. It broke Mutou 's Gravity Point and scored the third goal for Raimon .

Usage

Two users are required. The first users jumps up as the second one flips the ball up with their heel as the first user kicks the ball up again and the second user jumps and also kicks the ball up. Then they both kick the ball together with their soles charging it with blue and black energy.
