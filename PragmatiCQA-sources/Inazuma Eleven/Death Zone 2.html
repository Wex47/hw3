Info

This hissatsu is formed by three players and it was used in episode 58 . The use of this move is similar to the Death Zone , the three users spin in midair and a triangle forms between them, then the energy is focused on the ball between them, when the energy is strong enough they kick it with more intense power than the original then they kick the ball again from the air. The Death Zone 2 was perfected because of disharmony, unlike the original which was formed because of unity. Kidou stated that since their shot was formed out of disharmony. It is more powerful than the original.

In the match against Chaos, it was able to break through Burnout and it also scored a second goal against Chaos. Later, it was able to break through Jikuu no Kabe in the match against The Genesis .

After the battle with Aliea Gakuen, they were not be able to use this technique again because Domon left the team, and joined America's team ( Unicorn ).
