Info

It's Gouenji Shuuya 's previous team. Kidokawa had made it to the semi-finals against Raimon , but in the end they lost.

In a cutscene of the second game , Kidokawa's team faces Gemini Storm . They lost 86-0 in the first half since the result table is destroyed.

Uniform

Their uniform is a red t-shirt with black streaks and white team mark, fitted along with white shorts with black streaks at the end. The socks are red with black streak and black cleats. The goalkeeper's uniform is dusty-green, long-sleeved with black streaks, black collar, white team mark and black shirt under it fitted along with grayish-black shorts and black streaked at the end, white gloves, dusty-green socks with black streaks and black cleats. The captain's band is green.
