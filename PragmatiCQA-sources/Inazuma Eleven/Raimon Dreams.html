Raimon Dreams ( 雷門 ( らいもん ) ドリームズ, Raimon Dorīmuzu ) is a game exclusive team introduced in Inazuma Eleven GO .

Info

Inazuma Eleven GO

They appear as the second team in Yuuichi 's top route. All the members are at level 60. The Dream Ticket is required to unlock this team.

Inazuma Eleven GO 2: Chrono Stone

They appear as the second team in Tochan 's right route. All the members are at level 38.

Inazuma Eleven GO Galaxy

They appear as the eight team in Roglos Gordon 's left route. All the members are at level 75.
