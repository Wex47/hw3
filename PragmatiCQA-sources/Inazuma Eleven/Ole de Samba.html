Ole de Samba (オーレ・デ・サンバ, Ōre de Sanba ) is the representative team for Brazil in the Football Frontier International in Inazuma Eleven Orion no Kokuin .

Info

All members of Brazil were in the past working in the same factory. Arthur met Bernard Girikanan there and started playing soccer since. Little by little, all the members started to play soccer together. Before their match against Inazuma Japan , Brazil is said to have not allowed their opponents to score a single point. They also defeated England to reach quarter finals. All members of the team are Disciples of Orion and are Bernard's personal elite.

Uniform

The team uniform is a yellow jersey with green borders on the neck and sleeves, and they wear green shorts. The goalkeeper's outfit consists of a dark navy blue jersey with red borders and red shorts. They all wear black cleats and socks; most of the team's socks have green hems, but the goalkeeper's socks have red hems. The captain's band is a bright teal color.
