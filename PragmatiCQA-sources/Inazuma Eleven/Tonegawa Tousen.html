Uniform

Tonegawa Tousen's uniform is a light green shirt with a white top, as well as the word "PANDA" on it with white lettering. The shorts are black, the socks are green, and the cleats are black as well. The goalkeeper's outfit consists of an orange shirt with a dark turquoise stipe on the top, turquoise shorts and cleats, and white cleats. The captain's armband is red.
