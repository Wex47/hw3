Shin Sedai Japan ( 新世代 ( しんせだい ) ジャパン, lit. New Generation Japan ) is a game exclusive team in Inazuma Eleven GO .

Info

They appear as the second team in the lower route of Geimu in the Shine version of the game. All the members are level 50.
