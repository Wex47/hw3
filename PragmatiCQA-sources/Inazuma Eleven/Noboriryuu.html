Info

It was used by Nishiki , near the end of the match opposing between Raimon and Zero . It passed through some adverse members.

Usage

When the user is surrounded by the opponents, they suddenly jump up. As the surrounding players attempt to take the unguarded ball, the user summons a dragon from the ground which takes the ball in its mouth, knocking them away. The user then jumps on the dragon to dribble past the opponents.
