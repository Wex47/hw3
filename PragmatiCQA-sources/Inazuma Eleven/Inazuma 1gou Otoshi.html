Info

It is Inazuma 1gou combined with Inazuma Otoshi , appeared for first time in episode 13 . It broke through Genda Koujirou 's Full Power Shield and scored the last and decisive goal for Raimon .

Usage

Three users jump up. The main user lies on his back in the air, letting the two other users bounce higher on his belly. As the two users jump up, the scream for energy. They flip backwards and kicks the ball upside-down toward the goal. While it was kicked, the ball gathers a blue and yellow electricity.
