Kujaku Stadium (クジャクスタジアム, Kujaku Sutajiamu, lit. Peacock Stadium ) is a stadium located on Liocott Island , and was used in the Football Frontier International .

Info

In both the game and anime, this stadium was used for the match of Inazuma Japan and Unicorn .

In the Ogre version of the game, Rose Griffon fought against Team Ogre in this stadium.

Match

Inazuma Japan Vs. Unicorn

This time, Inazuma Japan fought against Unicorn, in which Ichinose is in. During the second half, Ichinose was switched with Eddie Howard because his injury was still not cured. Unicorn fought tremendously but Inazuma Japan won by 4-3. This is Fubuki's debut at the Football Frontier International world level after his injury was cured and he came back to the team.

Rose Griffon vs. Ogre (The Ogre only)

In The Ogre version of the game, Rose Griffon have an ill-fated match with Ogre. Ogre managed to win the match, with an impractical score of 36-0. When the match ended, Pierre Godin declared to Baddap that his plays were not human and then escaped with his team.
