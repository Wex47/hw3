Ancient Dark (エンシャントダーク, Enshanto Dāku ) is a team that appeared in Inazuma Eleven GO the Movie: The Ultimate Bonds Gryphon .

Uniform

The uniform appears to be black with a gray stripe covering the upper part of the body, as well as a gray circle in the middle. The collar is colored purple and sleeves have a purple pattern embedded on them. The captain band is light blue. The goalkeeper uniform is purple and black.

Info

GO movie

They challenged Raimon when Raimon wanted to use God Eden 's forest to train. Ancient Dark's members were able to easily outplay Raimon's members and scored a goal while hurting Raimon's players. The match wasn't finished as Matsukaze Tenma saved a lamb from getting hurt by the ball and Raimon's attention went to the lamb. Later, some of their members joined Zero .

Inazuma Eleven GO Chrono Stone

Ancient Dark was seen in Tenma's flashback when Raimon met Shuu again on God Eden after he saved them from Team A5 .
