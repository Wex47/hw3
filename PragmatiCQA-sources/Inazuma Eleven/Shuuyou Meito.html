Shuuyou Meito ( 秋葉名戸 ( しゅうようめいと ) ) is a team in the Football Frontier . Their members are a group of enthusiasts (" otaku" ) who want to win so that they can use the prize money to go to America in order to get Cosmic Pretty Reina, a limited edition doll.

Uniform

Their uniform is a blue t-shirt with pink sleeves with blue streaks, blue collar and white team mark, fitted along with light purple shorts with blue streaks. Their socks are dusty pink, and cleats are pinkish purple. The goalkeeper uniform is green, long-sleeved with purple sleeves, green streaks, white team mark and black shirt under it fitted along with black shorts, white gloves, pale pink socks and pinkish purple cleats. The Captain's band is light green.

Match

Shuuyou Meito VS Raimon

They conserved their stamina in the first half by making odd moves to lower

Raimon's guard. In the start of the second half, they unleashed their true abilities and scored a goal, then dropped back into defense. They managed to advance to the semi-finals with this strategy but as Megane Kakeru discovered their strategy and revealed it, Shuuyou Meito lost to Raimon.
