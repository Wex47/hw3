Inazuma Eleven (イナズマイレブン, Inazuma Irebun ), currently known as Raimon OB ( 雷門 ( らいもん ) OB), was once a legendary team. Their coach was Endou Daisuke who invented many hissatsu techniques which he wrote in his notebooks. Their captain was Hibiki Seigou , who later in the first season became Raimon 's coach. Daisuke and all members of his team were in an accident before their match against Teikoku Gakuen because of interference with the bus, which caused them to stop playing soccer. However, the accident was masterminded by Garshield Bayhan , who was using Kageyama Reiji to prevent them from interfering with his plan.
