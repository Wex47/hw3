Hitomi no Naka no Shouri ( lit. The Victory Within the Eyes ) is the character song of Kidou Yuuto . This is sung by his seiyuu, Yoshino Hiroyuki.

Information

Lyrics: Kodama Saori Composition/Arrangement: Nishioka Kazuya From the album
