The Water World Stadium (ウォーターワールドスタジアム, Wōtā Wārudo Sutajiamu ) is a stadium in the Holy Road nationals tournament. It is one of the six stadiums of the Russian Roulette .

Description

The grounds of this stadium is right on top of the water. The grounds, or the field, is made out of wooden planks held together by metal reinforcements.

Info

Anime

It appeared in episode 29 , where the 1/2 nationals in the Holy Road nationals match takes place, during the match between Raimon and Kidokawa Seishuu .

During the first half of the game, Taki Sousuke , a forward of Kidokawa Seishuu, was about to attempt a point against Raimon. However, just before kicking the ball, a wave of water emerged from the field leaving a gap, this gap of water isolated Raimon's defense line and goalkeeper from the rest of the field. It was the stadium's special feature 'pitch-down'.

Game

The stadium appeared in the GO game as well. During the match, some plates of wood will shine red moments before a pitch down occurs.

There is also a path that leads to the stadium called the Water Path, and it is required to go through it the first time you visit the stadium in the game. The Water Path also includes the ship that can be used to go to God Eden after beating Dragonlink in the story mode.
