Info

It was used in the GO movie by Shuu and scored the third goal for Zero against Raimon , easily passing Sangoku Taichi .

Usage

In the movie, the user gets covered by purple aura that has black energy which begins to surround the ball. The user then kicks the ball towards the goal in a stream of black energy.

In the game, the user kicks the ball upwards and then downward before kicking it once more towards the goal, with the ball covered by black energy.
