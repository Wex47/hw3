Tenmas (テンマーズ, Tenmāzu ) is an improvised team in the Inazuma Eleven GO Chrono Stone series.

Uniform

Their overall uniform is red, with the kanji of the word sky (天) printed overall white in the middle and the color of the captain band is yellow. Their second uniform, as shown in the Wii game, is exactly the same but light blue in color. The color of the captain band differs as well and is yellow green.
