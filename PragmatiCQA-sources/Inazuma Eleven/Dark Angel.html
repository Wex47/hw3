Uniform

Dark Angel's uniform has an orange background with green colored wings. Their uniform has similarities with the uniform of the combined teams; Tenkuu no Shito and Makai Gundan Z . The goalkeeper's uniform is dark blue and white. The goalkeeper's gloves are white.

In Inazuma Eleven GO , they wear Dragonlink 's away uniform, along with the Dragon Shoes and Gloves.

In Inazuma Eleven GO Galaxy , they wear Ancient Dark 's away uniform.

Info

Dark Angel is a combined team of Tenkuu no Shito and Makai Gundan Z . They came together to defeat Inazuma Japan when both Tenkuu no Shito and Makai Gundan Z lost. The players from Tenkuu no Shito seem to be under a spell, as Sein's eyes glowed and he frowned and pleaded for Endou Mamoru to defeat them, and then the evil smile was on his face again. In Inazuma Eleven 3: Sekai e no Chousen!! , this team is playable by linking the Spark and Bomber together or playing through the event in Ogre . You need to go to Mt Magnitude and once Bomber is linked with a Spark where Tenkuu no Shito or Spark linked with a Bomber that has defeated Makai Gundan Z or if you defeat both team in Ogre, the gate will open and you are able to go to Legendary Land where you will see Sein and Desuta . Once you speak to them you will play them.

In the GO game, they appear as the fourth team in Yuuka 's lower route. All members are around level 66.

In the Galaxy game, they appear as the fourth team in Natsumi 's route. All members are around level 99.

Match

Dark Angel Vs. Inazuma Japan (with the captains in the FFI)

During this match, Dark Angel used Shadow Ray to score a goal but was blocked when Endou Mamoru evolved his Ijigen The Hand . Afterwards, Inazuma Japan wins the match with 2-1 in the end when Endou used Megaton Head G3 .
