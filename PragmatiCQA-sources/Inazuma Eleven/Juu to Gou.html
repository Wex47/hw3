Info

Juu to Gou was initiated after the members of Inazuma Japan figured out how to get past Red Bison 's rough play. The tactic set Gouenji Shuuya free in front of the opponents goal, so that he could score with his Last Resort .

The tactic was used during the match against Shining Satans as well. Inazuma Japan used it to advance past some Australian players, and get the ball to Inamori Asuto . He then passed to Kidou Yuuto who scored the 1-2 with his new Overhead Penguin .

Usage

Three users line up in a row and pass to the left in high, arching passes. After the third user receives the ball, they kick it high up to the right. A fourth user from behind leaps into the air and intercepts the ball, shooting it forward while they are airborne.
