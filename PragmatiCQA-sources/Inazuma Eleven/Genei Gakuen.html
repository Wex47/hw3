Info

They first appeared in the quarter finals of the Holy Road and started their match against Raimon in episode 34 , in the Pinball Stadium . At the end of the match, they lost with 4-3 against Raimon.

Genei Gakuen was in large support for Fifth Sector , especially their captain, Mahoro Tadashi , who originally loved soccer. During the match against Raimon however, they were inspired to play their "soccer" and, by the end, a majority of the team's members, including Mahoro, realised the true meaning of soccer.

Uniform

Genei Gakuen's uniform consists of dark red shorts and purple shirts adorned with yellow stripes heading from the neck down the sleeve over both shoulders for field players while the goalkeeper's uniform consists of black shorts with a white long sleeve shirt topped with matching yellow stripes to the field kit. Each player's number is printed in yellow on the back of their shirt regardless of position. The captain's band is light-blue.
