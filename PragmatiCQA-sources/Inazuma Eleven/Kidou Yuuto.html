Kidou Yuuto ( 鬼道 ( きどう ) 有人 ( ゆうと ) ) is one of the main protagonists in the Inazuma Eleven series. He was a midfielder and the captain of Teikoku Gakuen , but later became a midfielder of Raimon and Inazuma Japan . In Inazuma Eleven GO , Kidou was at first the commander of Teikoku , and then became the trainer and temporary coach of Raimon . In Inazuma Eleven GO Chrono Stone , he became the coach of El Dorado Team 01 . In Inazuma Eleven GO VS Danball Senki W , he is a midfielder for Inazuma Legend Japan .

In Inazuma Eleven Ares no Tenbin , he is a midfielder and reinforcement committee for Seishou Gakuen . He is also a midfielder for Inazuma Japan in Inazuma Eleven Orion no Kokuin and later on Zhao Jinyuns .

Appearance

Kidou has brown hair that he keeps braided and tied, and red eyes. He always wears goggles, which were gifted to him by Kageyama , for the purpose of better focusing his attention on the soccer ball. In addition to the standard soccer uniform, he wears a cape on his shoulders, of different color depending on the uniform. His casual outfit consists of a red jacket, a black shirt, blue jeans, and combat boots.

As an adult, his hair is kept longer and only partially tied up, and he still wears goggles, albeit a different pair gifted to him by his sister Haruna . His outfit consists of a gray coat, gray pants, a red tie, and burgundy shoes. In GO Galaxy he wears a black coat with a blue tie instead.

Personality

Initially, Kidou was shown to be shady and haughty. He was also very obedient to Kageyama and always listened and obeyed his orders. However, he begins to change for the better as he realizes what kind of man Kageyama is later on. He starts going against his orders, searching around the academy for any traps, and threatening to reveal them to the public.

Kidou also seems very logical and reasonable, as he is a genius game strategist, Japan's command tower, knowing every single important thing there is to know about his potential opponents. He gets to know about everyone in 10 minutes and tilted the team's position correctly giving them commands. Kidou also cares for his teammates, and more importantly, his sister, like any other typical (elder) brother. He will do anything to help them although during the third season he had an identity crisis thinking that he couldn't escape Kageyama's curse. Kageyama almost tricked Kidou, saying that everything he is now is what he learned from Kageyama, but Kidou was able to surpass this.

In the manga, his shadiness stays, even as he enters the team, and sometimes ends his remarks with "what a wimp".

In GO , after ten years, he still retained some of his old personality, though he is more mature and calmer than ten years before. He also likes talking (or has the tendency to talk) about the past, as shown in episode 21 when he talked with Endou Mamoru .

Plot

Background

When Yuuto and Haruna were still very young, they lost their parents due to a plane crash and grew up in an orphanage. In a flashback, Yuuto was seen fighting with some bullies because they bullied Haruna. Yuuto was 6 years old and Haruna was 5 years old at that time. The only thing about their parents that Kidou has is an old soccer magazine, which is the only bond that connects him to his father. That's why Kidou started to play soccer; whenever he kicked the ball, he felt as if he was with his father.

Both him and Haruna were separated to different families: Haruna to the Otonashi family, while Haruna was immediately recommended by Kageyama Reiji to the Kidou Financial Group after he recognized Yuuto's talents for soccer at the age of 6. To bring his sister back, Kidou made a deal with his adoptive father : he'll get consecutive National championships in the Football Frontier during his three years in junior high. If he can't win consecutively, Kidou won't be able to take back his sister. If he loses in a district match, Kidou may be forced to leave his own family.

Ever since they were separated, he has never tried contacting his sister even once, leading her to believe she's in his way. However, the fact is, it was for Haruna's own sake. In order to live together with her, Kidou tolerates everything and has never forgotten her.

Season 1

Kidou makes his debut at the beginning of episode 1 , where he gives the command to destroy an unknown school after Teikoku beat them by 13-0. He reappears by the end of the episode, leading his teammates as they arrive at Raimon . Standing before the school, Henmi Wataru asks him why they were going to face a team like that, as he thinks doing so wouldn't hone their skills. Kidou replies by saying they might see something interesting, confusing Henmi.

As Teikoku approaches the field, Endou runs up to him and introduces himself, as well as thanking them for agreeing to play a practice match against Raimon. Endou tries to offer him a handshake, but he immediately turn his head to the side and asks if it would be okay for Teikoku to make a little warm-up before the match, since the field is new to them. Endou agrees. During the warm-up, after noticing Endou's captain band, Kidou snaps his fingers; Jimon and Henmi immediately pass the ball to him, and he shoots it at the Raimon's goalkeeper. Endou manages to hold it, but his gloves get several burn marks, much to his awe. Kidou smirks at the latter's reaction, though Endou quickly recovers and states it is starting to get interesting.

In episode 2 , he and his teammates had a match against Raimon. At first, Kidou was only interested in Gouenji , rather than the rest of the Raimon team. He sneaks around Raimon to investigate the team, making sure no one catches him. But then after Domon said that the Raimon bus was tampered so that they can't make it to the nationals, he begins to question Kageyama. In the match for the district national finals, Kidou checks the school making sure nothing has been tampered with. After seeing Kageyama's wrongdoings, he and the rest of Teikoku Gakuen made a decision to forfeit the nationals match, but their decision was left undone due to Endou's determination to play against them, they did. Despite the fact Teikoku lost in the nationals, they are qualified as the last time winners to get in again. In their first match with Zeus , Teikoku lost. Kidou had injured his leg in the match with Raimon and since he doesn't have any information about Zeus he decided not to participate. He soon thinks of himself to be a disgrace. Eventually, he joins Raimon, changing his cape to blue. At first, no one in the Raimon team accepted Kidou, even his own sister. But after Endou protests, and Kidou's strategic mind helped them win the match, they accepted him. In the end, he decided to stay in Raimon. He helped Raimon to win the match against Kidokawa Seishuu and they won the Football Frontier and defeated Zeus.

When the duo kept shooting at Ichihoshi and other Australian players, Shining Satans decided to exchange players. Luci Fanos came on the field and targeted Sakanoue Noboru , cutting his leg with blades hidden in cleats. When Kidou noticed that, he was terrified that the opponents went so far, it wasn't something what he could approve. Kidou said that there was time when the actions should be taken to protect what was the most important. Sakanoue told him that he had an idea how to deal with that and later easily outsmarted Luci, using additionally Hiura's technique Koori no Ya to freeze him. With a little help of Raimon Natsumi , Endou figured out how to stop Australian shot Time Trance . Kidou stood astonished with the other teammates and said that Endou was able to react to the time lag, by bringing out another majin. Now it was time for Inazuma Japan to counterattack and Kidou, Haizaki and Fudou Akio combined Hiura's Koori no Yari with new technique Death Crusher Zone thanks which they scored the fourth goal for Japan. Kidou came to Ichihoshi who was knocked down by the power of their last shot and asked if he was alright. When Ichihoshi grabbed his hand, he let him go, saying that his hand slipped.

When only few minutes left to the end of the match, Kidou kept shooting at Ichihoshi with Haizaki and Hiroto. When Asuto interrupted their actions, he got hit instead of Ichihoshi and had to leave the field with him. Asuto told Kidou that if he wanted to protect the team, then he would protect soccer and his teammates because soccer wasn't something that could be used to hurt someone or to throw anger and hatred at each other. When the match resumed Kidou easily stole the ball from Satan Gaul and passed it to Haizaki and Hiroto, who later scored the fifth goal for Inazuma Japan. Later Kidou stood at the stadium thinking that in the next match he would expose all Ichihoshi's wrongdoing and eliminate him for good. When Inazuma Japan celebrated their winning in the locker room, Kidou was trying to force coach to eliminate Ichihoshi from the team. He became even more frustrated when Zhao didn't react and seemed to have fun of this conversation. Then Kudou Michiya stood and told him firmly that voicing his dissent towards the coach, disrupts team discipline. Management had no need to explain its decisions to the players as well. Kidou stood trembling, being cool down by his friends Kazemaru and Endou. But he couldn't give up and informed the team about specious behavior of Ichihoshi and asked them for help to eliminate him. The team was confounded by what they heard but they agreed. Unexpectedly some men from the FFI bureau came in and started to investigate players’ lockers. They found a small box in Kidou's locker and immediately took him, causing him of doping as well.

Some time later Kidou met with Nishikage after the training and asked him if he finished his investigation about Ichihoshi. Nishikage confirmed that there was no doubt that he was affiliated with some kind of organization. He was leaving the camp frequently what was more strange because he could contact with those people in many other ways. Kidou decided to shadow Ichihoshi and when Nishikage asked if he wasn't going to back to the team, Kidou explained that he wasn't allowed to do it so far but he would help the team on his own. During the match with Arab no Hinotori Gundan coach Zhao revealed to the team that he asked Kidou to investigate strange behavior of Ichihoshi. Kidou went to the hospital, using the address from the letters written to Iwato Takashi by Ichihoshi's brother. To his surprise there was no one in his room and no one even appeared through the whole day. It turned out later that Ichihoshi was the younger brother Hikaru, and the older brother Mitsuru died in a car accident with their father years ago. Hikaru in his despair assumed the older brother's personality, he behave as Hikaru being in the hospital and leaving outside he was like the older Mitsuru.

Kidou met with Gouenji in Hibiki Seigou ’s noodle shop Rairaiken to watch the match between Inazuma Japan and Chinese team Soccer Zatsugidan . He said that instead of fighting in the match, he decided to take measurements against the Orion Foundation now.

Kidou along with Gouenji watched the press conference with Inazuma Japan, after they won the final match in preliminaries. He smiled when Endou told that he would like to fight first with Clario Orvan. Later they unexpectedly appeared before the team which aroused their huge enthusiasm. Ichihoshi came to Kidou and said sorry for what he's done before. Kidou looked at him serious, stating that he always paid back his debts. Right after that, a stranger man appeared, introducing himself as an employee of the FFI administration bureau. He showed everyone a small box that was supposedly found in Ichihoshi's room. He asked if there were doping drugs and told Ichihoshi to check it out. Everyone was surprised and frightened that Kidou used the same methods as before Ichihoshi did. As it turned out later there was only plush toy in the box and it was just a joke prepared by Kidou and his butler Hakamada to prank Ichihoshi. Kidou smiled and told him that he already knew all circumstances and welcomed Ichihoshi in a team, giving him hand which he eagerly shook. Later at the party held by the World Soccer Association, it was revealed that first Japan's opponent would be Spanish team Muteki no Giant . Kidou smiled to Endou, stating that it was exactly the way as he wanted before. At the party Kidou met Clario Orvan, who was the special guest as well. They were talking about the Orion Foundation and its unfair activities in soccer. Clario was against this and committed himself to fight with Orion, as Inazuma Japan did. Later Kidou and Gouenji were talking about the upcoming match with Spain and Gouenji's injure. Somehow Kidou found out that his leg wasn't in a good condition yet. The meaning of this was clear, Gouenji wasn't able to use Last Resort on his own. But luckily he got a plan already to pass down the hissatsu to someone else in the team. Much to Kidou's surprise Nosaka appeared, stating that it could be done with three people and Gouenji promised to make it come true.

Kidou met with Endou and Gouenji at Steel Tower Plaza just like they were doing in the old days. They were talking about how far they got and Kidou stated that certainly the world tournament wouldn't be journey without obstacles. Unexpectedly he revealed that he wasn't going with them to Russia. Kidou planned to search for strong players for Inazuma Japan, to strengthen the team and Nosaka along with Ichihoshi would replace him on the field. Endou and Gouenji were surprised by what they just heard but they admitted Kidou was right.

Kidou was seen later in Fubuki's flashback when they were talking with Kudou Michiya about Fubuki Atsuya joining the team. As it turned out Atsuya was already on the list as the new member of Inazuma Japan. Kidou appeared again in Aphrodi ’s flashback when he scouted him for Inazuma Japan. He watched Aphrodi’s efforts to change his team Zeus and lead them on a new path. Kidou also asked him to lend his power to Inazuma Japan to which Aphrodi eagerly agreed.

Kidou returned in Episode 48 as he joins Zhao Jinyuns to fight Shadow of Orion . He helps them to beat Shadow of Orion by using Koutei Penguin 2gou feat. Shark , Inazuma Break CG , scoring 2 goals for them.
