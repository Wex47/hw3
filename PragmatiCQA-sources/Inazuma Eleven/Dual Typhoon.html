Info

It was first used in episode 88 against Knights of Queen and was created by Fudou Akio and Kidou Yuuto . First, 4 players surround Fudou, then 4 players surround Kidou. Then the two central players give directions to others, creating a formation like typhoons. It was used again against Team Garshield in episode 118 .

Usage

The tactic requires ten people. Two people will stand in the middle, and four others will stand around each of them. Tornadoes will begin spiraling around the two central users and within the groups. This creates Dual Typhoon.
