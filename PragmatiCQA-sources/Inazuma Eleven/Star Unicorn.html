Star Unicorn (スターユニコーン, Sutā Yunikōn ) is the representative team for America in the Football Frontier International in Inazuma Eleven Orion no Kokuin .

Uniform

Star Uniform's jackets are mostly red, with a few white stripes and a large blue section on the top, resembling the American flag. Their actual soccer uniform is largely white, with navy blue collars and a red neckline on the top. They also have navy blue shorts, blue shoes, and white socks with a single red stripe on the top. For the goalkeeper, the jersey is navy blue with two vertical white stripes and a red collar, and the shorts are red. The captain's band is dark green.
