Appearance

He has light tan hair that have two brown-colored points at the top, like a feline's ears. He also wears a scarf that has a cheetah's fur pattern on it. His eyes, which resemble a cheetah's, are yellow, and his nose also resembles a cheetah's nose. He also has tooth outwards that resembles the teeth of a cheetah.

Plot

They played against Raimon in first round of Football Frontier preliminaries, where he is shown to have a great speed. In the end they lost by a score of 0-1.

Recruitment

Inazuma Eleven GO 2: Chrono Stone

In order to recruit Cheetah, the following requirements are needed:

  • Item : Classic Melody (なつかしのメロディ, randomly dropped from Team Bomber at Fuyukai 's taisen route )
  • Record : Rough Data (そこそこのデータ, have 10% or more of the player binder )
  • Topic : Great Warazi (すごいワラジの話題, obtained at the castle town in the Sengoku era )
  • Player : Raiou

After that, he can be scouted for an amount of 910 Kizuna points.

Inazuma Eleven GO Galaxy

In order to recruit Cheetah, the following requirements are needed:

  • Coin : × 9
  • Item : Supersonic Zipping Car (超速のビュンカート, randomly dropped from Edge Works (エッジワークス) at the Steel Tower Plaza )
  • Photo : Thunder (雷の写真, taken in Odaiba's Aqua Mall's hihodo store )
  • Photo : Wind (風の写真, taken in Odaiba's Aqua Mall's hihodo store )
  • Topic : Favorite Animal (好きな動物の話題, obtained outside the Kogarashi Manor )

After that, he can be scouted.
