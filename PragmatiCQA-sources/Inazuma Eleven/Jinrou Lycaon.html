Info

Gamma used Jinrou Lycaon, while being Mixi Maxed with Zanark Avalonic , in the match between Raimon and Protocol Omega 3.0 . Gamma shot the ball towards Raimon's goal with his Keshin's added power and it easily passed two of Raimon's players, Kirino Ranmaru and Hamano Kaiji , and easily scored a goal against Nishizono Shinsuke .
