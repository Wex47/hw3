Info

It was first used to defeat all of Raimon's second team. Then it was used to hit Matsukaze , but surprisingly, he was able to block it. It was used yet again in the match between Kuro no Kishidan and the Raimon First Team and it won against Burning Catch . And it was used again when Endou came back to the Raimon team, telling them that he is the new coach. Tsurugi used Death Sword in response to getting irritated at Coach Endou. Though when Tsurugi used Death Sword aimed directly at Coach Endou and it was fast, amazingly, Coach Endou dodged it even though it was fast and looked impossible to be dodged to which surprised everyone.

At Inazuma Town 's riverbed, Tenma, Shindou Takuto , Nishizono Shinsuke , Sorano Aoi , Yamana Akane and Seto Midori were thinking back at the opponents Raimon has faced so far during the Holy Road tournament and this hissatsu was seen in their flashback.

After Raimon won the Holy Road, the team watched a special program that was commemorated to them on TV, showcasing their hissatsu, keshin and tactics they used and the opponents and their moves they faced during the tournament.

Usage

The user puts the ball over his/her feet and stands the leg. He/She then releases the ball and shoots it, making it surrounded with a blue aura. Finally, the user launches the ball against the goal.
