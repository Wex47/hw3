Info

GO movie

Tenma used his keshin during the match between Raimon and Zero . Nishizono Shinsuke used his hissatsu, Buttobi Jump , and Tenma used his hissatsu to create a shoot chain with Justice Wing. The shoot chain easily scored the fourth goal against Zero.

Inazuma Eleven GO Chrono Stone

During the match between Raimon and Protocol Omega 2.0 , Tenma received the ball and summoned his keshin, Majin Pegasus Arc. He then proceeded to use Justice Wing to score a goal for Raimon but failed as Beta easily stopped the hissatsu by only using her body, shocking Tenma.

Usage

First, the user calls their Keshin . Then, the user jumps and kicks the ball surrounded by feathers and green energy.
