Uniform

Kaiou members wear slightly tannish-white shirts with lined red stripes, long red sashes tied around their waist, navy blue shorts and grayish-blue cleats over white socks with a red stripe at the top. The goalkeeper wears a black and white long-sleeved shirt, red cuffs, blue gloves, white shorts, black socks and blue shoes; their sash is also red. The captain wears a yellow captain band.

Match

Kaiou Vs. Raimon

They are the team that won in Block B and so forth, and they fought against Raimon since Raimon won in Block A. They appeared in episode 19 , playing in finals against Raimon . It was confirmed by Megane and Kudou that all Kaiou players are SEEDs . They scored three goals against Raimon , although Raimon won at the end with a 3-4 score.
