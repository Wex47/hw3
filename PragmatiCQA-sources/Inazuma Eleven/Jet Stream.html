Info

This hissatsu was first developed by Gouenji , Toramaru and Fudou but it wasn't until Endou replaced Fudou that it actually succeeds. This hissatsu is so strong that it broke through Tamashii The Hand G2. It is the strongest shoot hissatsu in the anime as well as in the third game.

Usage

The user, together with two more people, start to run quickly around the ball surrounding it in a hurricane. While running, the users simultaneously kick the ball into the air and drive it directly to the opponent's goal.
