Kira Seijirou ( 吉良 ( きら ) 星二郎 ( せいじろう ) ) is the main antagonists in the second season of the Inazuma Eleven series. He is the owner/chairman of Kira Zaibatsu and also the creator/owner of Ohisama En orphanage, and also the creator/leader of Aliea Gakuen . He is also the father of Kira Hitomiko and Kira Hiroto , and also the Aliea Gakuen members/orphan foster father.

In Inazuma Eleven Ares no Tenbin , he is also the principal/chairman of Eisei Gakuen .

Appearance

Seijirou is a short old man with slightly tanned skin and big ears. He has short dark yellowish-green hair and his eyes are usually closed, though he opened them a few times and are shown to be black.

Personality

His real personality is that of a caring and loving person, both in regards to his own biological children and the foster children from his orphanage. However, his personality changed drastically after the influence of the Aliea Meteorite corrupted him to seek revenge over his son 's death, without any consideration to achieve his ambitious. He used the orphan children from Ohisama En as tools for revenge, turning them into his personal soldiers, and his own daughter Kira Hitomiko . However, he later realized that what he did was wrong, so he eventually reformed and regained his original personality.

Plot

Background

Ten years before Inazuma Eleven 2 , when Seijirou had lost his son , Hitomiko was the one who suggested for Seijirou to create Ohisama En to adopt the orphan children that lost their relative. However, five years ago, when he had discover the Aliea Meteorite , he used it on the orphans children from Ohisama En . Hitomiko tried to persuade her father let go the died of his son but failed.

Season 2

He made the Aliea project with help from Kenzaki Ryuuichi that utilized the Aliea Meteorite . He used the meteor to make powerful soccer players. He said his purpose of doing it is because he wanted to get revenge for his deceased son named Kira Hiroto , and take over the world with the help of his orphanage children. In episode 43 , he mentions Alien Gakuen as the making of "gu" poison in which there is a survival of the fittest. Later, he was arrested by the police after the Academy collapses by the hands of Kenzaki Ryuuichi who took eleven pieces of the great Aliea Meteorite after the defeat of Genesis. He is not seen again after the defeat of Aliea Gakuen when he was in the prison, he possibly died before the events of Go , as Kiyama Hiroto is now head of Kira Zaibatsu.

Inazuma Eleven Ares no Tenbin

Seijirou appeared as the principal/chairman of Eisei Gakuen. He also watch the match Inakuni Raimon against his school team.

Inazuma Eleven Orion no Kokuin

Seijirou appeared during the meeting with Hiroto, Tatsuya and Saginuma before they left to Russia for the FFI tournament. The team gathered in Eisei Gakuen to welcome their teammates with all due respect and praise them for what they've already achieved.
