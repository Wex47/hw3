Info

Inazuma Eleven Ares no Tenbin

Hiura used this technique in episode 4 after the special training he received by his coach Zhao Jinyun , before the match against Minodouzan . He used it to get past the gap that was between the Rensa The Wall users and pass the ball to Inamori Asuto , who proceeded to score the second goal for Inakuni Raimon .

Hiura used this hissatsu again during the match between Inakuni Raimon and Hakuren to pass the ball to Asuto, preventing Fubuki Shirou to get the ball. Asuto received the ball and scored the third goal with Shining Bird .

Koori no Ya was used as well during the match between Inakuni Raimon and Eisei Gakuen . Hiura passed the ball to Asuto, who shot for goal. However, Saginuma stopped the shot with Wormhole .

Hiura used this hissatsu again during the match between Inakuni Raimon and Tonegawa Tousen but this time he used it as a shoot. He wasn't able to score for his team since Endou Mamoru stopped it by using Majin The Hand .

During the match between Inakuni Raimon and Outei Tsukinomiya , Hiura used the technique to pass the ball to Goujin Tetsunosuke after he was surrounded by 4 Outei Tsukinomiya players.

Inazuma Eleven Orion no Kokuin

This technique was used during the match between Inazuma Japan and Shining Satans . Sakanoue used this technique to get rid of the blade Luci Fanos had in his shoe. He got the ball stuck on the blade and then used Koori no Ya to freeze the ball and shoot it, so the blade broke.

Usage

The user kicks the ball in the air just in front of his body. He then spins around and covers the ball in cold mist. This freezes around the ball, making it solid ice. The user then proceeds to volley the ball high and far into the opponents half. The ice around the ball breaks on impact.
