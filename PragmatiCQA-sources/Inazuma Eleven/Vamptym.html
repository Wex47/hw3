Info

To challenge Vamptym for the first time in Raimei, you need to have already beaten Destructchers . When you return to the Inazuma TM Caravan , Vanfeny will be in it and will give an item to Tenma . Use it as an artifact to go to the Vamptym's place. They will be around level 70.
