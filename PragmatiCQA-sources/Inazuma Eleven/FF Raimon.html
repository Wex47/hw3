Info

Inazuma Eleven GO

This team can be challenged after finishing the game. Afterwards, go to Raimon's old soccer club room and talk to Gouenji Yuuka . It will be the second one in the challenge route. All the members are around level 70.

Inazuma Eleven GO 2: Chrono Stone

Once you have completed the game, go to Raimon in the past and enter the main building. There, go talk to Fuyukai and then you'll have to beat Raimon U-13 's. Once beaten it, you can challenge FF Raimon.
