Inabikari Shuurenjou (イナビカリ 修練場 ( しゅうれんじょう ) , lit. Lightning Training Center ) is the Inazuma Eleven 's secret gym.

Info

The gym was built under Raimon Junior High and it was used first by the legendary team, Inazuma Eleven . In the gym, Raimon evolved their individual strength and learned a lot of techniques. The training center is totally isolated and the door is locked by a timer set on 9999 seconds, which is equivalent to a training session.

Training

Inazuma Eleven

The training is divided into six courses (in the game):

  • Attack Course
  • Defence Course
  • Speed Course
  • Technique Course
  • Strength Course
  • Special Course (Game only)

Inazuma Eleven SD

In Inazuma Eleven SD , each course has 3 stages with items and coins as stage cleared reward, the harder the stage, the better the reward. Each stage also has a chance of dropping items for upgrade the figure. At the end of the course, there is a chest with one of the random rewards inside, including items for figure rank up.

Every stage need a ticket to play, the player receives 3 free tickets everyday, and can buy more tickets with Inazuma Coin (50 Inazuma Coin for 1 ticket). Each stage can be cleared only once per day, but can be repeat if the player's unable to clear it.

  • Offense Course: Rewards and drops items for Kick and Dribble stats upgrade. The chest random reward includes Forward and Midfielder rank up items.
  • Defense Course: Rewards and drops items for Block and Catch stats upgrade. The chest random reward includes Defender and Goalkeeper rank up items.
  • Physical Course: Rewards and drops items for GP and TP stats upgrade.
  • Hissatsu Technique Course: Rewards and drops items for hissatsu technique upgrade. This course is only unlocked on Friday, Saturday and Sunday. However, the player can unlock the course on the other days with the cost of 150 Inazuma Coin.
