Barcelona Orb (バルセロナ・オーブ, Baruserona Ōbu ) is a team Raimon faced in Inazuma Eleven Reloaded who also reappear in Inazuma Eleven Orion no Kokuin . They are the Spain Youth League champions.

Info

Barcelona Orb played against Raimon in a practice match. They destroyed them with a score of 0-13, showing Japan that their soccer is not on the level of the world and has to become better. During the match, Alonso stopped Gouenji 's Fire Tornado with one finger and also stopped the Inazuma Break . When Raimon went for The Phoenix , Clario intervened and stole the ball from Ichinose when he was about to shoot. Clario scored a high number of goals, even breaking through Endou 's Majin The Hand with a normal shot. The last goal was scored with Clario's Diamond Ray , showing their true power. Due to this result, Raimon was disbanded temporary and the members were all sent to different schools as committee members, in order to raise the level of Japan's soccer.

Trivia

  • Their name is based on the real life football team FC Barcelona which is one of the most successful teams in Spain.
    • Ironically, Barcelona Orb's colors are closer to the biggest rival of FC Barcelona; Real Madrid, with white and purple.
