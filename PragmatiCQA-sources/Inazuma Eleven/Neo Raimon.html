Neo Raimon (ネオ 雷門 ( らいもん ) ) is a game exclusive team based on the Raimon team appeared in Inazuma Eleven the Movie: The Invasion of the Strongest Army Ogre .

Info

This team can be challenged when you talk to Rushe after you beat the game, the Neo Raimon team will be the second to the last on her list, but this only applies to The Ogre version of the game.

Uniform

Their uniform is a blue t-shirt with black streaks on sleeves, yellow streak around neck and gray shirt under it, fitted along with yellow shorts with black stripes on each side. Socks are blue, and cleats are black, blue and white. The goalkeeper uniform is black, long-sleeved with yellow sleeves, red mark and red shirt under it fitted along with yellow shorts, red gloves, red and white socks and red and black cleats.

  • Their uniform is the same as of the team appearing in Inazuma Eleven Future . This might be a pun, as this team is based on future characters.
