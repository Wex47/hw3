Info

In the anime, because of Tenma's determination to bring back Shindou to the team again, he was able to create a new hissatsu move dubbed Soyokaze Step and was able to pass through Shindou. Nishizono, Shindou, Endou, and Tenma himself were all surprised with this new hissatsu.

After creating it, he used it in the matches against other teams during the Holy Road tournament.

At Inazuma Town 's riverbed, Matsukaze Tenma , Shindou, Nishizono Shinsuke , Sorano Aoi , Yamana Akane and Seto Midori were thinking back at the opponents Raimon has faced so far during the Holy Road tournament and this hissatsu was seen in their flashback.

After Raimon won the Holy Road, the team watched a special program that was commemorated to them on TV, showcasing their hissatsu, keshin and tactics they used and the opponents and their moves they faced during the tournament.

In episode 49 of Chrono Stone, it reappeared and was used by Matsukaze Tenma to pass through Saryuu Evan in its S stage and succeeded in doing it.

In the movie Inazuma Eleven Chou Jigen Dream Match it was used twice by Tenma, the first time to pass Fudou Akio and the second time to pass Bitway Ozrock .

Usage

The user dashes forward with the ball, leaving a lime colored trail behind them. Once the user is close to the opponent, they do a side step and make a 180° turn around the opponent, sliding with the ball and creating a gust of wind that lifts the opponent in the air, spinning them around.
