Info

The technique was first used during the match between Inazuma Japan and Muteki no Giant . Endou faced Clario Orvan 's Diamond Ray for the second time in the match, and he used his Super God Hand , trying to stop it. However, it was being pushed back. By channeling his inner feelings about the former match between Raimon and Barcelona Orb , Endou made the technique evolve into Diamond Hand, stopping Diamond Ray. Later, he faced Diamond Ray again, however, as Endou was using Diamond Hand again, Clario made Diamond Ray curl around Endou, bypassing the technique entirely into the goal. This made the score 2-1 for Spain.

Endou used this technique again during the match against Navy Invader . Diamond Hand was easily broken when Cobra shot with his Carronade Hou and scored the first goal. Later he attacked again but this time Endou was able to stop him using the same technique.

After the Star Unicorn trio of Mark , Domon and Ichinose made their entrance, Endou soon faced their combination shot The Phoenix , which he used to be a part of. Endou used Diamond Hand trying to stop it, but the technique overpowered it, scoring the equalizing goal and irritates Endou. Later, the trio used it once more, but this time, Endou managed to save it with Diamond Hand.

It was used once more in a minigame after the arrival of Mizukamiya Seiryuu . Kozoumaru Sasuke used Over Cyclone but Endou saved it with Diamond Hand.

Endou used Diamond Hand twice during the finals between Inazuma Japan and Perfect Spark . He failed to stop Froy Girikanan 's Double Head Eagle the first time but managed to stop it the second time. After the finals got interrupted and the teams got changed to Zhao Jinyuns and Shadow of Orion , Endou failed to stop Yurika Beor 's Orion Crossviper with Diamond Hand both times it was used, letting in the 1st and 3rd goal respectively.

Usage

The movements are identical to Super God Hand , with the exception being when the user pulls the yellow hand down after summoning it, the yellow hand expands and wraps itself around the user's right hand, solidifying into a diamond like glove. The user swings their right arm back, with a white aura following their hand. The user then thrusts their hand forward to catch the shot.

Trivia

  • This is the evolved version of Super God Hand, and can be considered as an upgrade to God Hand by absorbing all the energy from the opponent's Diamond Ray and fusing it.
  • Despite being stronger, Diamond Hand still shares the same weakness from Super God Hand - due to the narrow radius, it may only be used to stop shoots that travel in a straight line. This weakness was exploited by Clario's Magaru (Curving) Diamond Ray, which completely bypassed the technique. To make up for this weakness, Endou created Diamond Arm , which copies ideas from Nishikage 's Casting Arm , and Diamond Punch , which is an alternate use of Diamond Hand.
  • In the Cantonese dub, when Endou first evolves Super God Hand into Diamond Hand, it was named 钻石神掌 ( lit. Diamond God Palm ), but was later changed to 钻石之手 ( lit. Diamond Hand ).
