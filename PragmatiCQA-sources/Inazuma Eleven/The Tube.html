Info

It was used once by Tsunami in episode 99 and scored a goal during the match of Inazuma Japan and Unicorn. It consists of riding a wave, shoots and scores a goal from the corner.

In the game, this can be used during a free kick or a corner kick.

Usage

The user jumps and conjures a tube of water, then flings his leg out to kick the ball. The ball rapidly travels down the water tube and swims the surface of the water while it goes toward the goal.

In-game effects

It works just like a normal shoot hissatsu, thus element and strength attributes apply. The only differences are a tighter, but curved arc (useful for chain-shooting) and a TTP usage rather than a TP use. Additionally, it can only be performed while shooting a free kick.
