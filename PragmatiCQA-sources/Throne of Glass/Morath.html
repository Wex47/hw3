Morath is a merchant city in Adarlan , ruled by Duke Perrington , a noble closely related to the Havilliards . In the Old Language, the name Morath literally means "Black Mountains".

Description

As depicted on Sarah J. Maas 's Pinterest, Morath is a distinguishly evil-looking place, located near the sides of mountains.

History

When the Valg King Erawan was defeated 1,000 years ago by Elena Galathynius and Gavin Havilliard , he was trapped underground in the mountains of Morath, which served as a prison.
