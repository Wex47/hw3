Skull's Bay is a city on a mountainous island in the archipelago of the Dead Islands, ruled by the Pirate Lord, Captain Rolfe . There are several vendors, shops, and taverns like The Sea Dragon, but the port city is primarily composed of ramshackle, wooden buildings, and streets filled with prostitutes, orphans, and pirates. The city is mostly known for its defensive weapons, the Ship-Breaker, and several catapults atop the nearby watchtowers. Built centuries ago, Ship-Breaker is a giant chain that hangs across the horseshoe-shaped bay and can snap the mast of any ship that tries to enter or exit the harbor without permission.

In The Assassin and the Pirate Lord , Celaena Sardothien and Sam Cortland destroy the Ship-Breaker when helping two ships full of slaves escape, forcing Rolfe to repair it, the nearby watchtower, and building a sister tower on the opposite side of the bay.
