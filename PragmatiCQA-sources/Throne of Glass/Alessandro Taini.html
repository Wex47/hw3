Biography

Born in Genoa, Italy, Alessandro Taini is an art director, concept artist, and illustrator. He is best known for his work at video-game publisher Ninja Theory, where he worked on the games Heavenly Sword, Enslaved: Odyssey to the West, DmC: Devil May Cry, and Hellblade.

After leaving Ninja Theory in September 2015 after 12 years with the company, Alessandro became a freelance artist working for various clients such as Riot Games, The Imaginarium Studio, Reel FX, Prime Focus World, and Luma Pictures. He is also the illustrator of several fantasy book series for Random House, Bloomsbury Publishing, and Marvel Books.

Some of Alessandro’s most notable works include the best-selling Spook’s series by Joseph Delaney, Throne of Glass by fantasy writer Sarah J. Maas, and Black Widow: Forever Red by Margaret Stohl.
