Noll is a small town located at the farthest end of the Deserted Peninsula. It has active volcanoes, so it's always dark, because the ash covers everything. The volcanoes are viewed as sacred.

History

Noll was a small town in the south of Erilea . The King of Adarlan had it conquered so he could build a tower there; one of three that were used to wipe out magic in Erilea. It was given a garrison to defend it, even though no one would attack it. The soldiers there reported headaches and other symptoms of sickness while in the vicinity of the tower. Some of soldiers went mad, some got nosebleeds. Those who went mad bashed their own skulls in, as if they couldn't get the pain out.
