House Ytger is the former ruling house of Eyllwe, currently sworn to House Havilliard.

Recent Background

After the conquest of Adarlan, Eyllwe had become part of the Empire. The Ytgers, unlike House Galathynius, weren't slaughtered or abolished. The King had been allowed to keep his royal title; however, the Havilliards have become their overlords. Still, some of them, including princess Nehemia, the deceased crown princess, were eager to regain the kingdom's freedom and sovereignty.
