The King's Champion Tournament was a competition taking place in Adarlan 's capital, Rifthold with the victor becoming the King's Champion. Celaena Sardothien was the only female participant out of the twenty-four competitors. The tournament served as the main plot of Throne of Glass . Each competitor was selected by a member of the King's council.

The tournament lasted thirteen weeks, with a test once a week. Someone would be eliminated after each test. In between tests the champions would be able to train and learn from Theodus Brullo , the Weapons Master. Brullo also served as the judge of the compitition. The week after Yulemas, the four remaining champions would face each other in a duel to win the title of King's Champion.
