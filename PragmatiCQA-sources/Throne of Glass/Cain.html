Biography

Throne of Glass

Cain was from the White Fang Mountains and descended from the local freefolk. He was found by Duke Perrington and was his candidate in the King's Champion Tournament. Cain was the most feared candidate and almost everyone thought he would win. He mocked Celaena all the time, trying to make her angry enough so she would reveal her true identity. Cain murdered several of his rivals by calling on the Ridderak using Wyrdmarks . As a result, Cain gained the strength of his opponents and magical powers, which helped him during the Tournament. In the final round, he almost defeated Celaena Sardothien , but with Elena's help, she triumphed over him. Cain then attempted to murder her, but Chaol saw what he was doing and killed him to defend Celaena.

Personality

A resident of the White Fang Mountains, Cain was a skilled warrior. To add to this, he displayed blatant ambition and bloodlust, willing to harm or kill any who stood in his way. He was ruthless and arrogant. It is unknown if these malicious traits were his true personality or just amplified by the Valg who possessed him.

Physical Description

Cain was an extremely huge, bulky man with dark hair. He was also described as incredibly muscular. During the King's tournament, his size and power increased, affecting his use of Wyrdmarks and the Valg's ability to consume his victims' power. He also wore a black Wyrdstone ring, inducing the Valg to live inside him.
