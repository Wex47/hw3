Gregori was an assassin of the Rifthold Assassin's Guild and was considered one of Arobynn Hamel's seven most trusted assassins.

History

The Assassin and the Pirate Lord

While on a mission, Gregori is captured by the Royal Guard and held in the royal dungeons. As a result, he is assassinated on order of Arobynn Hamel to prevent him from talking under torture.

Trivia

  • He threw a dagger at 10 year old Celaena for feeding his horse candy and subsequently received a scar on his cheek from her return throw.
