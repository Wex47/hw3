“ She hated Innish. Hated the reek of trash and filth, hated the heavy blanket of mist that shrouded it day and night, hated the second-rate merchants and mercenaries and generally miserable people who occupied it. ”

–- Celaena Sardothien, The Assassin and the Healer

Innish was a port town located in Melisande , near the Gulf of Oro. It is a town so small, it isn't on most maps. The White Pig Inn, where Yrene worked for a year, is considered the best by Innish standards. Aelin also went to the White Pig Inn in the Assasin's Blade.
