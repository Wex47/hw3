Biography

Fleetfoot was given as a Yulemas gift from Prince Dorian to Celaena. Princess Nehemia Ytger often looked after her. Fleetfoot would most likely have been put down if Dorian hadn't gifted her to Celaena.

The dog would have been killed, but Celaena begged Dorian to spare her, and he agreed to try to find a home for her. Celaena was surprised that Dorian was showing mercy and kindness to Fleetfoot and was happy. When he couldn't find a home for her, he gave her to Celaena as a Yulemas gift, along with three pounds of candy.

Crown of Midnight

Fleetfoot jumps through a portal to another world when Celaena opens the portal to try and speak with Nehemia.

Heir of Fire

When Celaena is in Wendlyn , Dorian looks after Fleetfoot. Fleetfoot frequently sleeps in Dorian's bed. When Dorian reveals his magic to the King of Adarlan , Chaol escapes, taking Fleetfoot with him.

Queen of Shadows

Celaena, now Aelin, and Fleetfoot are reunited when Rowan is taken to a country home in Oakwald Forest to be patched up by a midwife.

Empire of Storms

Fleetfoot is left in Terrasen with Evangeline and Murtaugh Allsbrook .

Kingdom of Ash

Fleetfoot appears in last chapter laying on her bed in Aelin and Rowan's room.

Personality

Fleetfoot is quite cautious of strangers and does not like to be touched or stroked by anyone other than Aelin. Fleetfoot is also good friends with Evangeline and enjoyed playing with Nehemia before she was killed.

Physical description

Fleetfoot is a big dog with golden fur and bright eyes. She is first described as slightly larger than the others, with a silky, silvery gold coat; and a purebred appearance despite her mixed heritage. She is later depicted with a slender snout, a more pronounced gold coat, folded velvet-soft ears, and golden-brown eyes that reminded Celaena/Aelin of molten caramel. She is also described as having huge paws hinting that she will grow large and swift during the time of Dorian's gifting.
