The Glass Castle is a palace at the heart of Rifthold made entirely of glass. It houses the Royals of Adarlan, and the nobility.

History

The King of Adarlan ordered the top architects and most talented builders to build a castle entirely of glass as a show of power. Architects and builders from all over Adarlan's empire helped build it.

In Queen of Shadows , Dorian Havilliard shattered the Glass Castle using his magic.
