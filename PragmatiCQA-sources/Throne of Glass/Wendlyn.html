Wendlyn is a kingdom located east of Erilea , across the Great Ocean. It is ruled by House Ashryver . It is directly next to Doranelle .

History

Crown of Midnight

Assassin Celaena Sardothien is sent to Wendlyn by the King of Adarlan to assassinate the royal family, but this is more a way for her to escape her secret.

Heir of Fire

Celaena spends most of her time in Wendlyn after refusing to kill Prince Galan Ashryver before leaving for Mistward with Rowan Whitethorn .

Monarchy

Wendlyn is ruled by House Ashryver. The current King is Glaston Ashryver , and his son and heir is Prince Galan Ashryver . The Ashryvers have always been fair and just rulers, creating a kingdom of good.
