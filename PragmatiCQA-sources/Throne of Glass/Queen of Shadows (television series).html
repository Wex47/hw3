Queen of Shadows is an upcoming television series based on the Throne of Glass book series. Named after the fourth book of the series, it is currently being developed by Hulu.

Production

In September 2016, Hulu announced that they were in the process of developing the series. with Kira Snyder ( the 100 ) set to write the adaption and Anna Foerster ( Underworld: Blood Wars ) set to direct the first episode, Celaena .

However, as of 2021, no further information about the adaption has been released, leading to speculation that production has been halted or the adaption shelved.
