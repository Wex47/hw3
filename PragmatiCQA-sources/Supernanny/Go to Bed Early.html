The Go to Bed Early technique is a punishment version of Stay in Bed and Sleep Separation . The steps were not shown. This was only used in The Addis Family , The Del Re Family , The Hallenbeck Family , The Miller Family , The Potter Family , and The Davis Family .

Step (Spoilers)

Note: It has the steps only one time on the Hallenbeck Family

  1. Tell child that they have to go straight to bed after dinner for doing a very bad thing (ex. encouraging your brother to name-call while on timeout)
  2. Send child to bed and put pajamas on them. If you fail, put them on as soon as child falls asleep
  3. Tell child they’re not allowed out of their room
  4. If child gets out, return them back to their room with no communication
  5. Remove a privilege if child does something wrong (e.g. spill water on the floor)
  6. Wait until child falls asleep
  7. Put pajamas on child if you failed Step 2, and put them in bed
  8. If the child gets up, return them back with no communication (If it’s the go to bed early technique, they should just point to the bed and say “BED!”
