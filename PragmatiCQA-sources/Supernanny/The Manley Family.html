Episode summary

Jo Frost visits John and Sara Manley in Southern California. John (46) and Sara (45) have been separated for over six months. They have two children: 7-year-old Max and 4 1/2-year-old Claire.

Max is very disrespectful and has anger issues. He calls terrible names, throwing tantrums, bites, hits and also back-talks. Claire has also started to copy Max's bad behavior, she is way worse than Max as she is aggressive, defiant, and would throw massive fits if she doesn’t get her way, she will go on and on until she gets what she wants.

John has his own apartment, but has tried not to disrupt his children's lives, so he has daily meals and spends every weekend in the family home, but overnights in his own apartment. Max and Claire are worried that their parents will get divorced, but the parents already are.

Grocery store outings turn into a disaster with the kids running off in different directions. The kids will also throw epic tantrums if they don’t get what they want. The tantrums become so intense, that many people are petrified by them. Max tells Jo that he keeps contemplating why his dad moved out and doesn't know why. Sara did not want to divorce, but John did.

Can Jo help John and Sara help the children to accept the divorce?
