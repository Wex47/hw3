Episode summary

Jo visits San Diego, California where parents with Christianity and Religion named Mark & Melissa Mann need help with their 4 children: 5-year-old Naomi & 3-year-old triplets Norah, Nathaniel, & Madeleine.

Naomi is called "the mistress of mayhem" by her parents, acts defiant towards them along with screaming & throwing tantrums.

The triplets are worse than their older sister as they have picked up her behavior, the trio will throw tantrums and refuse to go to bed.

Melissa and Mark's method of discipline is corporal punishment as spankings including hand and wooden spoon which doesn't seem to be working. Mark admits that he is "downright desperate" and feels unable to take of the kids on his own and even a visit to the beach ends up in disaster. Can Jo help this family?
