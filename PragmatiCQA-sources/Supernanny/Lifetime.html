Lifetime is an American pay television channel that is part of Lifetime Entertainment Services, a subsidiary of A&E Networks, which is jointly owned by the Hearst Communications and The Walt Disney Company. It features programming that is geared toward women or features women in lead roles. On March 27, 2019, it was announced that Supernanny will return for a 20-episode season that will premiere on NBC and Lifetime in 2020, and Jo Frost will also return as the host.
