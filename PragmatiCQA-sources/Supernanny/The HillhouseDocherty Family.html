Episode summary

In the Series 3 premiere, Jo travels to the small town of Ayrshire, Scotland to help a family. Kerry has a nine-year-old boy, Ryan, and her live-in boyfriend Steven, have three-year-old identical twin boys, David and Declan. Ryan has a daily routine of kicking and swearing, while Declan & David are prone to violent meltdowns. Steve is a complete pushover while Kerry's rage fails to tame the rebellious boys.

Why Jo failed to help the family

During the Family Test Run, after Ryan destroys the Reward Chart , Ryan spilled milk in front of Kerry. She spills milk back at him and Steven carries Ryan upstairs to an early bath, Ryan ended up bumping his head, and now they were now back to square one as the Family had gone from weakness to weakness.

In the news

  • According to various news articles, the scene where Kerry loses it after Ryan deliberately spills a cup of milk became a minor YouTube sensation with over 15,000 views. The news articles all feature Kerry complaining about the embarrassment of this video, despite the fact that all the news coverage would only make it more likely for people to look up the video and watch it.
