Episode summary

Jo Frost visits Marietta, Georgia to meet parents stay at home mom Kate (26) and coffee shop employee Roy (27) who are struggling with their three boys: 4-year-old Denver, who has smart aleck attitude and sometimes escapes the house, 3-year-old Logan, who is a ringleader. and 1 1/2-year-old Jack, who throw fits. Kate and Roy own a coffee shop and whenever the boys are there, things can get chaotic.

Logan is the ringleader of trio and misbehaves more than his brothers. He is very aggressive and destructive. He could rip the wall paper off the walls, hit the computer with the stick, throwing couch pillows, knocking things over, hit, kick, punch, bite (especially to his little brother Jack's diaper), and can throw temper tantrums.

Meanwhile, big brother, Denver is starting to follow suit and copy some of Logan's behavior. He’s strong willed and has a smart aleck attitude. He would escape the house sometimes.

Jack the baby of the family is also copying Logan’s behavior by throwing temper tantrums.

When the boys misbehave, Kate takes them to the coffee shop. All three of the boys act out in the coffee shop. The wreck havoc, run around the shop, annoy customers and throw temper tantrums. Roy doesn't know how to control the boys and get his work done.

Stay at home mom, Kate has no idea what else to do with the angry little boys. Can Jo calm down these young boys?
