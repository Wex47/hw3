Be Good Johnny is a song performed by the Australian band Men at Work in 1981.

Colin Hay, lead singer of Men at Work, covered a solo version on his album Man @ Work , and this version is used as the theme song of the American version of Supernanny from seasons 1 to 6 (Hulu's print only).

Lyrics

♪Be good, be good, Be good, be good, Johnny! Be good, be good, Be good, be good, Johnny! (Instrumental) Johnny!♪
