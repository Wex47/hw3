Episode summary

Jo heads off to Florence, South Carolina to meet Greg (44) and Amber Benton (33) with their two chaotic boys: River (10) and Hunter (8) River is dependent on his parents, and Hunter is very strong-willed. The boys are aggressive and mouthy due to their parents' constant arguing, which threatens the stability of their marriage. The boys bicker and cause all sorts of mischief around the household. Not only do the boys act out, but the parents can be aggressive and rude. Can Jo fix Greg and Amber's marriage and get the boys to tow the line or will the family fall apart?
