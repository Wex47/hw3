Jo Frost: Extreme Parental Guidance is a British TV series hosted by Jo Frost. It was broadcast on Channel 4 from February 9, 2010 to August 5, 2012 and it lasted two series.

Like Supernanny , the show involves Jo helping out various families with their issues. Unlike Supernanny , however, the show covers Jo helping multiple families in a single episode as well as doing a number of other things.
