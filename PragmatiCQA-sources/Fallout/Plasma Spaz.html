Plasma Spaz is a perk in Fallout: New Vegas .

Effects

Action point (AP) costs for all plasma weapons (including grenades ) are reduced by 20%.

Affected weapons

Weapon AP before AP after

Plasma Defender 20 16

Plasma grenade 24 19
Plasma mine 35 28
Plasma pistol 30 24
Plasma rifle 30 24

30 24

Notes

  • Before patch 1.4.0.525 , the perk only reduced AP costs by 10%.
  • When stacked with Fast Shot , and/or Loose Cannon , and Math Wrath , the effects are multiplicative, not additive (e.g. the Q-35 matter modulator becomes 16, not 14).
