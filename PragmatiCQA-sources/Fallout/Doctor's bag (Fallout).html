The doctor's bag is a medical item in Fallout , Fallout 2 , Fallout Tactics and Van Buren .

Characteristics

Unlike a set of lock picks , there is a limited amount of equipment in a doctor's bag. One can use them 3 times in Fallout and Fallout 2 . Gives a 20% bonus to the Doctor skill when used.

In Fallout Tactics , the Doctor skill can only be used with one of these items in a character's inventory, or a paramedics bag . Doctor's bags not only heal HP , but also cure status ailments ( Conditions ). Still confers a 20% skill bonus upon usage.

Locations

Fallout

  • Killian Darkwater may give this to the Vault Dweller , as a reward for getting Gizmo's confession .
  • Lorri carries two of these.
  • One can be found in the bookshelf in Jon Zimmerman 's basement hideout at Adytum .

Fallout 2

  • Doc Holliday has one in his inventory.
  • Can be found in one of the lockers on Level 3 in Vault 13 .
  • Sometimes sold by Lao Chou .

Fallout Tactics

  • One is carried by Stitch .
  • The player character will start with one in their inventory, if they tag the Doctor skill.
  • Sold by Celsius .
