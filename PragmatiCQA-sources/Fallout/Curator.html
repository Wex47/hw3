Effects

Curator doubles the duration of magazine and bobblehead bonuses from 30 minutes to 60 minutes.

Notes

  • The Percepti-bobble and Pannapictagraphist perks will aid in locating both items.
  • Besides perks that have group effects, perks can be selected to be shared among team members. Each player can only choose one perk to be shared at a time, and the selected perk can be changed at any time during gameplay. However, the player must have a Charisma point level that is at least three times the rank of the perk card that is to be shared, regardless of what SPECIAL attribute the shared perk card belongs to. Shared perk cards will not stack effects if duplicates are equipped or shared.

Bugs

  • PC Prior to patch 1.0.5.10 , after using a bobblehead or magazine for at least one hour but prior to the second hour expiration due to Curator, it was possible to log out of the server and rejoin, resetting the time remaining.
  • PC Additionally, prior to patch 1.0.5.10 during the second hour it was possible to then consume an additional bobblehead or magazine of the same type and stack the effects for increased intensity, whereas normally stacking is not permitted and the additional item simply resets the timer.
