Voice actors perform voice-overs to portray characters in the Fallout series .

Background

Every released Fallout game has featured voice acting, though it has appeared in many different forms. In Fallout and Fallout 2 , only talking heads were voiced, though the transition to a 3D environment with Fallout 3 created the need for characters. Since then, all characters that appear in a Fallout game are voice acted, though often times there are overlap in actors who perform multiple roles.

Bethesda Softworks has worked with Blindlight, LLC in West Hollywood, California , for voice actor acquisition, production, and direction on the Fallout series.
