Killian Darkwater is the mayor of Junktown in 2161 . He runs his own general store called Darkwaters and acts as the head of the Junktown guard .

Background

The grandson of the founder of Junktown and son of its previous mayor , Killian Darkwater spent time in his youth travelling with caravans but eventually settled down and opened "Darkwaters", a general store in Junktown. Killian took on the role of mayor of Junktown following the death of his father, which the public generally approves of citing him as a hard and strong man that acts fairly and will protect Junktown as best he can.

As a leader, Killian is hard on crime and shady practices with a focus on Gizmo and his casino , though he is not above making deals with local gangs to help keep the peace. This attitude has earned him the ire and wrath of Gizmo, who believed his casino was the only reason Junktown was prosperous following Killian's rise to the position of mayor.

Notes

  • The random number of bottle caps in his inventory will reset any time a game is loaded or upon entering the area.
  • After talking to him for the first time - when Kenji enters the building - Killian's inventory changes. The exact details vary, but he will always have a new Desert Eagle if the first one was bought or stolen from him.
  • Killian's dog tags are lootable from his corpse, whether or not Gizmo requested them for the proof of death.
  • Killian bears a very strong physical resemblance to his voice actor .
  • If caught picking any locks in the store, he will stop the Vault Dweller. Entering dialogue with him after that puts the Vault Dweller in Junktown's jail.
  • The dog tags Killian wears most likely belonged to his grandfather, who was a soldier.
