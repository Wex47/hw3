Plot

The update focuses on the other vaults in Appalachia , other than Vault 76 and the fate of their inhabitants.

The player character can relive the last days of Vault 51 as they attempt to claim the title of vault overseer and the vault's supercomputer , through a battle royale-style game mode. In this mode, players attempt to survive as long as possible before "the Nuclear Storm" (a ring of fire) closes in, killing their opponents if necessary.

The player character can also explore inside Vault 94 , teaming up with others to fix an emergency that would otherwise destroy the vault.
