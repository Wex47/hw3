The throwing knife spear is a weapon in the Fallout: New Vegas add-on Dead Money .

Characteristics

The throwing knife spear is similar in shape and design to the knife spear , except it has only one cosmic knife aerodynamically tied to the end.

Throwing knife spears are used to create and maintain the knife spear .

Locations

They are plentiful in the Villa. They are carried by ghost harvesters who use them as their main weapon. Each ghost harvester yields 5 of them upon death.

Notes

  • Compared to standard throwing spears , throwing knife spears have a slightly higher weight and lower damage. However, throwing knife spears have a high (2x) limb damage modifier, while standard throwing spears lack any limb damage bonus.
  • Throwing knife spears can be used to repair both knife spears and knife spears clean . Owing to the thrown weapon's high health, as well as normally being found in perfect condition, this will repair either of the melee weapons to perfect condition.
  • They are limited upon return to the Mojave .
  • They can be successfully coated in Cloud Kiss (or any other poison) to provide additional damage over time.
  • When thrown in a first person view outside the Sierra Madre, the spear will have a trail of the cloud.
  • Even though it is classified as a "throwing spear," it cannot be used toward the GRA challenge " You Don't Belong In This World! "
  • Throwing knife spear is marked as a "Player Only" weapon, meaning it cannot be given to companions for use.
  • Throwing knife spears are not affected by the Loose Cannon trait.

Bugs
