Cutscenes are sequences in a game that consists of either cinematics or in-game footage.

Narrator

The "narrator" voices the intro cinematics, chapter overviews, and ending slides across the Fallout series . In all games save Fallout: Brotherhood of Steel and Fallout 4 , Ron Perlman voices the narrator, including the series-wide quote, "War... War never changes." Tony Jay provides the narration in Fallout: Brotherhood of Steel and in Fallout 4 , the narrative voices are provided by the Sole Survivor 's male and female voice actors, Brian T. Delaney (intro and male character endings) and Courtenay Taylor (female character endings only).
