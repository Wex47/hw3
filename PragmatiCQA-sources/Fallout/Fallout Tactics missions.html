    General information

    There are 22 missions in the Fallout Tactics single-player campaign, including the bonus mission available after installing a bonus pack. After you complete a mission, you need to return to the Brotherhood bunker, where you are given either the next mission by the general (first Barnaky , later Dekker ) or you can choose between two missions (you have to complete all the missions anyway, but you can choose the order).
