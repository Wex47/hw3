X-cell is a consumable item in Fallout 76 .

Contents

  • 1 Background
  • 2 Characteristics
  • 3 Locations

Background

Created before the Great War as a general performance enhancer, X-cell never got out of the prototype stage, but was widely distributed on the black market.

Characteristics

It appears to be some sort of inhaler, and boosts all SPECIAL attributes by two points for two minutes, but is highly addictive.

Locations

  • Two can be found at Harpers Ferry :
    • One in the medical clinic, downstairs around the lockers.
    • One on top of a chemistry station at the Free States trading post.
  • One can be found at the Kanawha Nuka-Cola plant , in the medical room upstairs to the left.
  • One can be found in a chem box underneath one on the airplane seats at Horizon's Rest .
  • One can be found on a shelf halfway up a windmill just northwest of Top of the World .
  • One can be found on a shelf next to the purifiers at Hornwright air purifier site 02 .
  • One can be found at the Watoga Underground , in the final shopping plaza where the Claw is encountered, on the lower floor, in the northeastern corner.
