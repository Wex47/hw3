Friendly Foe is a perk in Fallout .

Effects

With this perk, companions and allies (like members of a caravan while escorting a caravan for example) are highlighted green in combat instead of red, saving you the confusion of friends shooting other friends by mistake.

Notes

  • This perk has no game mechanics effects, and just changes the highlight color of allied NPCs.
  • This perk is misnamed " Friend-or-Foe " system.
  • Green highlighting is the default setting in Fallout 2 and Fallout Tactics , making this perk obsolete.
