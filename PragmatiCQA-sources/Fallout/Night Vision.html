“ With the Night Vision Perk, you can see in the dark better. Each level of this Perk will reduce the overall darkness level by 10%. ” — in-game description, Fallout

Night Vision is a perk in Fallout , Fallout 2 , and Fallout Tactics .

Effects

With the Night Vision Perk, you can see in the dark better. Each level of this perk will reduces darkness and darkness penalties by 10% ( Fallout ) or 20% ( Fallout 2 and Fallout Tactics ).

Also only in Fallout , in dark indoor areas or outdoor areas at night-time, this results in an increase in monitor brightness and ranged weapon accuracy. The perk increases ranged accuracy by 15%, and possessing all three levels increases ranged accuracy by 30% in total.

Notes

In Fallout 2 , this perk comes with an equipped FN FAL (night sight) in the form of Weapon Night Sight .

Bugs

  • The ranged accuracy bonus from the Night Vision perk is temporarily lost if a savegame is reloaded. In outdoor areas such as towns, the accuracy modifier is re-applied after approximately a minute of game time. In indoor areas, the area must be re-entered before the accuracy is re-applied.
  • The above also applies after the Night Vision perk is selected. In other words, if selecting this perk while indoors, re-enter the area so that the accuracy bonus for this perk is applied.
