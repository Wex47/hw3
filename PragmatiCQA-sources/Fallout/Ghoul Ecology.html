Ghoul Ecology is a perk in the Fallout 3 add-on Point Lookout .

Effects

All your weapons do an additional 5 damage against ghouls .

Obtaining

The perk is gained by reading Plik 's journal inside the coastal grotto . The book itself is located on a table inside Plik's bedroom next to a mini nuke and a chessboard .

10 Indisputable Rules on Ghouls

The following is based on Fallout 3 cut content.

At some point during development the Ghoul Ecology perk was meant to be obtained by reading a book titled " 10 Indisputable Rules on Ghouls ". The book is still present in the DLC's code, and if somehow obtained will grant the perk on use. For PC users, this can be accomplished by using the command player.additem 00FC30 1 .

A similar book was created for the Mirelurk Ecology perk, called " Marsh Hunting Tips Vol IV: Mirelurks ".

End of information based on Fallout 3 cut content

Notes

After receiving this perk, weapons will show with the damage increase in the Pip-Boy. Rapid-fire weapons such as Vengeance or the Xuanlong assault rifle show very large increases in the DAM stat as the +5 damage bonus is applied per shot.

Bugs

PC Playstation 3 Xbox 360 Due to a misplaced condition, damage is applied to all targets. The condition is placed into the conditions list used for perks that are selectable at level-up, instead of in the Perk Entry's Target condition where it belongs. This means that the damage bonus is not just restricted to ghouls like the Entomologist perk is against insects, but instead the damage is added against everything .

  • The bonus is applied on each shot fired in V.A.T.S. Therefore, hunting rifles (which fire once) get +5 and Chinese assault rifles (which shoot 3 times at once) get a bonus of +15. This can be useful for players who use weapons that fire many shots in V.A.T.S. , such as the minigun , which fires 8 times providing a bonus of +40 damage.
