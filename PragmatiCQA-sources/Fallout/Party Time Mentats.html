Party Time Mentats are a consumable item in Fallout: New Vegas .

  • 1 Characteristics
    • 1.1 Crafting

Characteristics

Party Time Mentats are a modified form of Mentats . Due to the added ingredients, the drug gives a greater boost to the user's personality, increasing Charisma more markedly than the standard version. Their effects last for one minute, a far shorter duration than that of standard Mentats.

Crafting

This item can be crafted by the Courier .

Locations

  • Red Rock drug lab - Sold by Jack if the Courier teaches him the recipe.
  • One is located on top of a fluorescent light along with a Fixer in Puesta del Sol . The light is above the Hard locked gate leading to the service route . The room has a Very Hard Terminal.

Notes

  • The effects are stackable with normal Mentats and with other items which increase Charisma, such as alcohol , which make this chem an effective way to boost Barter and Speech for player characters with minimal Charisma. This is also a good way to boost Intelligence for subsequent increases to Medicine and Repair .
  • Although they are addictive, there are no negative status effects listed in the Pip-Boy.
