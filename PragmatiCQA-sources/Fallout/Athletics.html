“ Athletics describes your ability to apply your physical strength and agility, know your limits, but also how to focus during physical exertion. ” — Fallout: The Roleplaying Game Core Rulebook p.44

Athletics is a skill in Fallout: The Roleplaying Game .

Background

Athletics is paired by default with the Strength attribute. However, depending on the context of the action, use of Agility or Endurance can also be utilized in physical situations.

Behind the scenes

  • The Athletics skill was first used in J.E. Sawyer's Fallout Role-Playing Game .
  • The icon for the Athletics skill is visually similar to the Marathoner perk from Fallout 76 .
