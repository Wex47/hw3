“ The Pathfinder is better able to find the shortest route. With this Perk, your travel time on the world map is reduced by 25% for each level. ” — in-game description, Fallout

Pathfinder is a perk in Fallout , Fallout 2 and Fallout Tactics .

Effects

With each level of this perk, your travel time on the world map and the drain on the Highwayman 's battery is reduced by 25%. This is the in-game time that is lowered, and not the actual time to travel between towns. Your actual speed on the map will not decrease.

Notes

The Fallout 2 manual erroneously states that the Outdoorsman requirement is 60%.

Behind the scenes

The image for this perk is a reference to the DC Comics superhero Flash .
