Scout is a perk in Fallout , Fallout 2 and Fallout Tactics .

Fallout

With this perk, you increase the size of explorations on the world map by one square in each direction.

Fallout 2 and Fallout Tactics

With this perk, you will increase the amount of the map you can see while exploring and make finding the special random encounters a little easier.
