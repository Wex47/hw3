    In Fallout 76 , the player character can complete certain tasks, known as challenges . These challenges function much like in Fallout: New Vegas : the player character can complete a specific task for rewards, usually Atoms . Atoms can be spent on in-game cosmetics in the Atomic Shop . With Seasons , daily and weekly challenges currently award S.C.O.R.E. points instead.

    Challenges are tied to account; for example, if the player collects 760 wood, they can not create another character on the same account to collect 760 wood to obtain Atoms again. However, progression is shared between characters; for example, a player could collect 500 wood as one character and 260 wood on their other character in order to obtain the Atoms. Challenge progression is possible in private adventure, however, they do not progress in Fallout Worlds 's Public Worlds or Custom Worlds.

    Many challenges are unable to be done until its previous tier has been done. For example, using 76 bobbleheads requires the player to use all 20 types of bobbleheads first.

    Note that the "Complete many events" challenge is currently unfinishable because Pass the Buck is disabled.

    When Nuclear Winter (NW) was removed, all the NW challenges were removed as well.
