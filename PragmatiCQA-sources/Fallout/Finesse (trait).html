“ Your attacks show a lot of finesse. You don't do as much damage, but you cause more critical hits. ” — Fallout 2 in-game description

Finesse is a trait in Fallout , Fallout 2 and Fallout Tactics .

Effects

Critical Chance is increased by 10%. The 10% is additive, not multiplicative, meaning a character with 10% Critical Chance will have 20% if this trait is taken. However, all attacks do -30% damage , but this is done by adding a +30 Damage Resistance modifier to all attacks, which is ignored by armor-bypassing Critical Hits .

Fallout Tactics

All attacks do -25% damage. Critical Chance is increased by 10%.
