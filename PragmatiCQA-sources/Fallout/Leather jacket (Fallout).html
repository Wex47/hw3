Characteristics

The leather jacket is pre- War attire, but it is possible that craftsmen of the post-War world are making it to this day. It is constructed of tanned brahmin hide, much like leather armor. While it lacks in terms of protection, there are special things that can happen while wearing it:

  • Can be used to recruit Dogmeat in Junktown .
  • Sometimes a male character wearing a leather jacket can convince the Khans that he is the Death-Hand, Garl 's father, returned from the dead after Garl killed him.

Behind the scenes

This leather jacket is based on the one worn by Max Rockatansky in Mad Max 2: The Road Warrior .
