Fallout , Fallout 2 , Fallout Tactics

Healing rate is a derived statistic in Fallout , Fallout 2 , and Fallout Tactics .

Initial Level: Starting healing rate is equal to 1/3 Endurance , with a minimum of 1. Average characters will have a healing rate of 1.

  • The Fast Metabolism trait increases healing rate by 2.
  • The Faster Healing perk increases the healing rate by 2 per rank.

Fallout: New Vegas

The healing rate in New Vegas is set according to a character's Endurance stat. An Endurance of 9 or 10 gives a healing rate of 10, Endurance 6-8 gives a healing rate of 5, and Endurance 5 or less gives no healing rate at all. Each point of healing rate restores 0.066HP per in-game hour, which translates to 1HP every three hours for a rate of 5, and 2 HP every three hours for a rate of 10. The effect is applied in real-time, during waiting, and while sleeping in Hardcore mode .
