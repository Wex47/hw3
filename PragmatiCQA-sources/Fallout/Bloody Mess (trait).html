Bloody Mess is a trait in Fallout , Fallout 2 and Fallout Tactics .

Effects

This trait does not alter gameplay other than the fact that violent death animations appear more often. However, it does have an effect on the ending of Fallout .

Bloody Mess has a positive impact if the plan is to use sledgehammers , or related heavy melee weapons. If someone dies, the death is instant instead of first sliding away.
