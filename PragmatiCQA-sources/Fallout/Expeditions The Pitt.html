Background

After the Great War , the area became known as the Pitt . It is dominated by mutated monstrosities and other threats. Former Vault Dwellers from Appalachia 's Vault 76 will visit its ruins.

Gameplay

Overall, Expeditions are story driven, repeatable missions to various locations, starting with the Pitt in 2022.
