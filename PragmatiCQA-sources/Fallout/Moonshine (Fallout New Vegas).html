Moonshine is a consumable item in Fallout: New Vegas .

Characteristics

Moonshine is an alcoholic beverage that has a more pronounced effect on the user's personality than other types, giving a greater boost to Charisma . Like other alcohol, it raises Strength , but lowers Intelligence for the duration of its effects. Moonshine's effects stack with other alcohol types. Unlike other drinks, its effects are not modified by high Survival skill.

Crafting

This item can be crafted by the Courier after learning the recipe through dialogue with Cass .

Locations

  • Goodsprings - Five bottles in a red shack connected to the back of the Prospector Saloon . They can be stolen, or purchased from Trudy .
  • Mojave home - One can be found inside the Average locked house, on a table to the right of the entrance. The house is northeast of Freeside's north gate and north of the mole rat ranch . Opening the door results in Karma loss, but items inside can be taken without penalty.
  • Nipton hall - In the locked room, in the crate with a lot of other aid items.

Notes

  • Moonshine does not have a flag set for medicine or food, which means that it does not scale with any skill and therefore yields no raised effects.
  • It is valued at only 20 caps , lower than its cost in Fallout 3 . Moonshine is worth substantially less than its components.
