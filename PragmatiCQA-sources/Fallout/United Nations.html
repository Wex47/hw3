The United Nations or UN was an international organization that existed prior to 2052 .

Contents

  • 1 Background
  • 2 Notes

Background

The United Nations actively worked to restore peace during international crises but ultimately was not successful, disbanding on July 26, 2052. United Nations President Sakugama Okiri made a statement lamenting the events. Its former headquarters in New York City peaked the interest of several organizations who began the bidding process on the prime real estate shortly thereafter.

Notes

  • The manual for Fallout: Brotherhood of Steel states that the United Nations fell apart in 2025. It is not clarified if "falling apart" means they disbanded or if this simply meant this was the beginning of a gradual decades-long downfall.
  • According to the Fallout Bible , in May 2052 the United Nations began to collapse as heated debates resulted in several countries withdrawing.
