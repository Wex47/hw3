    General information

    In Fallout Tactics , there are 91 perks divided intoÂ :

    • 69 for all
    • 3 ghoul only
    • 3 super mutant only
    • 4 deathclaw only
    • 1 not for ghoul
    • 1 not for super mutant
    • 1 not for deathclaw
    • 9 not for deathclaw and dog

    Humans can pick a new perk every 3 levels . The Perk Rate is 4 for ghouls , super mutants and deathclaws , and 2 for dogs . Robots cannot pick perks at all, i.e. their perk rate is 99.

    You are able to save up perks to use at a later time.
