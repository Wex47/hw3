A small dusty box of some sort is a miscellaneous item in Fallout and Fallout 2 .

Characteristics

This item has no actual purpose beyond being sellable, though its weight makes it impractical for even that.

Locations

Fallout

  • Inside a bookcase in Junktown , in the building next to the Crash House .

Fallout 2

  • One in the fridge at the Ghost Farm .
  • In the fridge under the Malamute Saloon .
  • On a bookshelf in the backroom of New Reno Arms .
  • In the lower rightmost room on the second level of Vault 8 .

Behind the scenes

The design of the box is taken directly from a Swanson beef pot roast TV dinner.
