Drunken Master is a perk in Fallout Tactics .

Effects

You fight better when you are drunk . You receive 20% to your Unarmed skill while under the influence of alcohol .

Behind the scenes

This perk is a reference to the 1978 movie Drunken Master .

Bugs

Due to the lack of control over the level of intoxication, it is impossible to obtain a Drunk state and activate this perk.
