“ A set of locksmith tools. Includes all the necessary picks and tension wrenches to open conventional pin and tumbler locks. ”

Lock picks are a set of locksmith tools. This set includes all the necessary picks and tension wrenches to open conventional pin and tumbler locks. Using lockpicks on a locked door will provide a bonus to the player character's Lockpick skill. An advanced set also exists.

Characteristics

Lock picks increase the Lockpick skill by 20% when used, but only on a regular tumbler lock. Mechanized doors and elevators require electronic lock picks to break into.

Variants

  • Expanded lock pick set

Locations

Fallout

  • Inside a fridge at the Khan Base .
  • If Lockpick is tagged, the Vault Dweller will start off with a set.
  • Given as a welcoming prize for joining the Thieves' Circle in the Hub .
  • Sold by the Shopkeeper .
  • On the floor of the deathclaw warehouse 's basement.

Fallout 2

  • In Gecko junkyard .
  • Corpse outside Sierra Army Depot .
  • In the Chop Shop , inside one of the lockers in T-Ray 's office

Notes

Lock picks are replaced by bobby pins in Fallout 3 , Fallout: New Vegas , Fallout 4 , and Fallout 76 .
