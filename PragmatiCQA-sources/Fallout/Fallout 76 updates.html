    Overview

    This page describes major updates for Fallout 76 . With the online nature of the game, what were previously known as add-ons now functions as a series of updates spanning a certain period of time featuring new content. All of this content is free and is incorporated into the game. Bethesda released a roadmap of Fallout 76 updates for the Xbox One, PS4, and PC in 2019 on February 21, 2019. The 2020 roadmap was released on May 14, 2020.
