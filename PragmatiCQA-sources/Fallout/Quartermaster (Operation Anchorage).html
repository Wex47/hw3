The quartermaster is one of the soldiers in the U.S. Army field headquarters during the Anchorage Reclamation simulation in 2277 .

Background

In charge of allocating weaponry to the troops engaged on the front lines of Anchorage , the quartermaster appears to be logical and by the book. With the correct guile and fast-talking, he can be convinced to provide additional firepower, including weaponry previously thought to be classified. He's a valuable ally and a man to befriend, even after one has already brought their requisition holotape to him.

Other interactions

  • The quartermaster can provide a scoped Gauss rifle in addition to the equipment already chosen if one passes his Speech challenge.
  • He is the only person that can be killed in the simulation that won't de-materialize, allowing one to search and loot him.
