Mark Morgan (L) and Brian Fargo (R) during Wasteland 2 Development

Mark Morgan composed the Fallout soundtrack and Fallout 2 soundtrack . In 2010, a compilation album, Vault Archives , was released. Some of the songs he composed for Fallout 1 & 2 were reused in Fallout: New Vegas in specific locations.
