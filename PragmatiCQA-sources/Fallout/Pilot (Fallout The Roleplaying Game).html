“ The Pilot skill covers your ability to operate vehicles, from buggies and motorcycles, to Vertibirds and tanks. ” — Fallout: The Roleplaying Game Core Rulebook p. 45

Pilot is a skill in Fallout: The Roleplaying Game .

Background

Pilot is used to operate and drive ground and flying vehicles. By default, it is paired with the Perception ability score but, depending on the context of the action, the Fallout: The Roleplaying Game Core Rulebook also recommends the possible use of Strength , such as maneuvering out of a dangerous situation, or Agility to perform skillful piloting.

Notes

The image for the skill is the same image used for the V.A.N.S. perk in Fallout 4 .
