“ This home-made knife is as dangerous as it is easily concealed. ” — In-game description , Fallout 2

The shiv is a melee weapon in Fallout 2 .

Characteristics

A home-made knife, made from what looks to be a syringe needle taped to a wooden handle. This weapon isn't strong at all and is really cheap, making them almost useless.

Locations

  • New Reno 2nd Street , found on a prostitute's corpse.
  • San Francisco Chinatown , purchased from Lao Chou at the Flying Dragon 8.
  • PMV Valdez , purchased from Cal.
  • Carried by some Boxing fans .

Bugs

Due to a bug, this weapon can't be used.
