Coyote tobacco chew is a consumable item in Fallout: New Vegas .

  • 1 Background
  • 2 Characteristics

Background

Harvested from wild and domestic coyote tobacco plants common in the Mojave Desert , coyote tobacco chew is a reinvigorating plant that can be consumed raw to reduce sleepiness.

Characteristics

Consuming this item gives a +1 boost to Perception and Agility , and a -30 to Sleep in Hardcore mode . It is possible to become addicted to this item, the effects of which are a -1 to Perception and Charisma . With the addition of the Honest Hearts add-on , this item is also used to brew black coffee .

Locations

  • There is one loose leaf on a grave in Goodsprings Cemetery .
  • Six leaves can be found in a duffle bag at the top of a ramp in the train yard immediately north of Boulder City .
  • Some can be stolen from the Mojave Outpost , inside the barracks on the shelf.
  • Westside has 25 plants - Ten in the planters in the easternmost part of the area just northeast of the north cistern and 15 in the planters east of the Westside Co-Op, ten in one planter and five in another.
  • Four plants can be found at Wolfhorn ranch .
  • Three can be found near Ranger Station Echo - One directly west of the tower structure while the other is very close to the half-way point between the station and Coyote Mines , just west of a water-filled crater. The third is found directly ahead of the second to the southwest, near a small, lone Joshua tree .
  • Two can be found to the right of Raul's shack .
  • Two can be found at Goodsprings - One at the wreckage between Victor's shack and the schoolhouse , one at the house between Doc Mitchell's house and the General store .
  • One plant grows slightly northeast of the Primm water tower .
  • Directly south of the marker for the Boulder City ruins .

Notes

  • Although coyote tobacco chew is addictive, the beverage black coffee with that ingredient and craftable with Honest Hearts , is non-addictive.
  • In one of Boone 's endings , he communicates his thoughts to Legate Lanius by spitting tobacco chew into his eyes, in front of the Legion armies.
