    These consumable items can heal the player, temporarily change the player's SPECIAL stats and add various other unique effects. Some of these drinks can be crafted at chemistry stations found across Appalachia . When drinking alcoholic consumables such as beer and wine, there's a chance the player can become addicted, which will result in decreased stats until the addiction is cured. To prevent addiction , the Professional Drinker perk must be leveled. The fermentable and vintage variants of alcohols are omitted.

    Drinks are categorized into alcohol , juice , liquor , Nuka-Cola , tea , and water .

    Chems

    Main article: Chems (Fallout 76)

    Each chem has an individual profile and cannot be "stacked" - only one may be active at a time. Taking multiple chems of the same type at once does not increase their duration. Instead, chem duration can be increased with the perk Chem Fiend . Different chems can restore Hit Points , temporarily change SPECIAL stats, and a variety of other effects. Many of these chems can be crafted at chemistry stations located across Appalachia or at one's C.A.M.P . Chems can be purchased from vendors or medical supplies vending machines .

    Abusing chems results in addiction , a type of negative condition . Addiction is temporarily relieved by substance use but otherwise results in decreased stats until the addiction is cured by Addictol or tasty radscorpion egg omelette .
