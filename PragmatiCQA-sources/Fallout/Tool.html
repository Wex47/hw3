Characteristics

A super tool set, containing various useful tools, including pliers. Its user gains a 20% bonus to Repair when the item is used.

Uses

Tools are worth a lot of money if sold to the right people, and they are quite common. Valerie can be given a set of tools and a wrench in exchange for a super tool kit one day later.

Locations

Fallout

  • If Repair is tagged, the Vault Dweller will have one of these at the start.
  • Mitch in the Hub 's General Store has one.
  • Doc Morbid has one on his person.
  • One in the Hall of the Dead in Necropolis , in the desk in a room to the west when using the main entrance.

Fallout Demo

In the Fallout Demo , the item is called wire cutters (in possession of Skippy ) and can be used to disable Scrapheap 's power generator. The two gangs then leave town and the demo ends.

Fallout 2

  • Next to the destroyed Mister Handy in Klamath 's Rat Caves .
  • Inside a locker in Smitty 's shack in the Den .
  • On a shelf in Skeeter 's workshop in Gecko .
  • In a locker on second floor in Vault 8 .
  • The Professor in Broken Hills has one in his inventory.
  • Hoss carries this.
