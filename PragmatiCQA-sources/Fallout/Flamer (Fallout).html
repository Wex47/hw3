The flamer is a weapon that projects an ignited stream of flammable liquid in Fallout and Fallout 2 .

Characteristics

The flamer is a sort of middle ground between the big guns. It has the shortest range of any big gun but does quite a bit more damage per shot compared to miniguns . Fire resistance is often lower than normal resistance, so a flamer can usually do more damage to an enemy in power armor than a minigun, and has less danger to the user compared to the BigBazooka rocket launcher . Floaters , especially, have very low fire resistance. The only downside to the flamer is the heavy ammo, which weighs 10 pounds for a cluster of 10 shots.

One can be upgraded into an improved flamer in Fallout 2 . It increases the flamer's minimum damage by 5 and makes it one pound heavier. Loaded with volatile flamethrower fuel Mk II , a flamer can roast enemies at close range, while miniguns destroy targets at medium range, and a BigBazooka rocket launcher annihilates far-away foes.

Locations

Fallout

  • Talius will give a flamer along with fuel to player characters with an Intelligence above 4, but only the flamer itself if one has killed the mutants at the Necropolis Watershed .
  • Barry , a mutant inside the Watershed , also carries one.
  • Can be bought from Jake in Old Town .
  • Nightkin use them.

Fallout 2

  • Carried by female "Big Gun Thugs" in gang encounters around New Reno and rogues in Broken Hills ' area.
    • Used by one NCR ranger around the NCR and with caravans that also go to Vault 15 also have.
  • Darion in Vault 15 has one.
  • Another big gun thug is used in random encounters with the Morton brothers .
  • Super mutants in levels 2 and 3 of the Mariposa Military Base use them.
  • Around San Fransisco , a female Hubologist uses one.
  • Carried by some Enclave soldiers in advanced power armor.
