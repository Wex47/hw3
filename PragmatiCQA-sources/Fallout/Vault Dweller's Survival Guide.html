Background

It is styled as a Vault-Tec publication that is issued to inhabitants of the company's line of vault -model fallout shelters.

Behind the scenes

  • The publication is mentioned in a promotional item that came with the real world Pip-Boy 2000 Mk VI FM radio module, Boston Bugle Volume 12.
  • In the instrumentation section, the image of the terminal includes several lines from the real world Fallout Protection: What To Know And Do About Nuclear Attack booklet.
  • A copy of Coping With Mr. Virus! appears in concept art and the 2010 teaser trailer for Fallout Online .
