Glowing One is a Fallout Tactics trait available only to ghouls . It was also supposed to appear in the canceled Fallout MMO developed by Interplay, Project V13 .

Notes

  • This trait references glowing ones .
  • The Glowing One trait is the only characteristic in Fallout Tactics that has no image.
