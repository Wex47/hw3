“ Just like dear old Dad , you’ve devoted your time to intellectual pursuits. You gain an additional 5 points to both the Science and Medicine skills. ” — In-game description

Daddy's Boy or Daddy's Girl (depending on the Lone Wanderer 's sex) is a Fallout 3 perk .

Effects

Each rank of this perk will permanently increase the player character's Science and Medicine skills by five points, up to a maximum of three ranks.
