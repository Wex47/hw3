For the location in Fallout 3 , see Lock and Load . For the perk in Fallout 76 , see Lock and Load (perk) .

Locked & Loaded is the seventh major update for Fallout 76 , following Steel Dawn . It features improvements to the C.A.M.P. and Shelter system and SPECIAL loadouts , which allow the player to rearrange their character builds when they wish. It was followed by Steel Reign .
