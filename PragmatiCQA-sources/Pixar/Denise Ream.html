Denise Ream (born September 26, 1965) began her Pixar career in 2006 as the associate producer of Up . She then became the producer of Cars 2 and later The Good Dinosaur . Prior to joining Pixar, she spent 13 years at ILM , working on films such as Harry Potter and the Sorcerer’s Stone and Star Wars: Episode III – Revenge of the Sith .

Trivia

  • In Cars 2 , there is a cameo character, released as a die-cast, named Denise Beam , whose name is a reference to Denise Ream.
