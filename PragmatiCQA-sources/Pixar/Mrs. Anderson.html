Mrs. Anderson (A.K.A. " Bonnie's Mom ") is a character in Toy Story 3 . She also appears in some of the Toy Story Toons .

Toy Story 3

She is the receptionist at Sunnyside Daycare and Bonnie 's mother. She is also friends with Andy's slightly aged mom . She is first seen when Ms. Davis is donating a box of toys to the Daycare. Later on, she is seen coming in the Caterpillar Room looking for Bonnie.

When she is seen at the end of the film, she is amazed at how Andy has grown up to become a college student as Andy visits Bonnie's house to donate his toys to Bonnie. After Andy leaves, she takes Bonnie inside for some lunch.

She is last seen during the credits carrying another donation box of toys to the Butterfly Room which includes an Emperor Zurg .

Toy Story 4

Bonnie's Mom is back in Toy Story 4 .

Toy Story Toons

Hawaiian Vacation

In the first episode of Toy Story Toons , she is briefly heard when Bonnie packs up to go on vacation. Shortly, before Barbie and Ken arrive.

Small Fry

Mrs. Anderson is seen taking Bonnie to Poultry Palace .

Partysaurus Rex

Mrs. Anderson is heard telling Bonnie it is bathtime. She appears telling Bonnie that there is too much water in the bathtub and decides that they shall go to dinner at Bonnie's grandmother's house instead. She also appears in the end talking to the water man, when the water in the bathtub at Rex's party overflowed and flooded the house, which she didn't know what happened.

Toy Story of Terror!

Mrs. Anderson and Bonnie are driving along the countryside at night when a bump causes a flat tire, forcing them to turn into the Sleep Well motel. Using the office phone, she calls for a tow truck, only to learn that they won't come until 6:00 am, forcing them to stay the night.

The next morning, the tow truck arrives and replaces Mrs. Anderson's car tire. As she and Bonnie began the check-out, the curtain behind the manager's desk came off, revealing Bonnie's missing toys behind a glass cabinet. Bonnie reclaims her toys and Mrs. Anderson calls the police to arrest the manager for theft before departing.

Toy Story That Time Forgot

In the special, Mrs. Anderson is first heard off-screen telling Bonnie to get ready to leave the house. She then is seen bringing Bonnie to Mason 's house.

During the final scenes, she is heard arranging the next time Bonnie will visit Mason, along with Mason's father.
