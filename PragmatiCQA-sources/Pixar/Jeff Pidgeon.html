Jeffrey Roger "Jeff" Pidgeon (born March 19, 1965) is a Pixar artist who has served variously as animator, writer, storyboard artist, and voice actor. He has also been heard as a voice actor in several Pixar features, such as the Aliens in Toy Story , Toy Story 2 , Toy Story 3 , Toy Story 4 , and Toy Story Toons , Thaddeus Bile in Monsters, Inc. and Monsters University , and additional voices in A Bug's Life , Finding Nemo and The Incredibles .

Outside of Pixar, Pidgeon's animation experience includes work on The Simpsons , the feature film Ferngully: The Last Rainforest , and the cult student film Bring Me the Head of Charlie Brown .
