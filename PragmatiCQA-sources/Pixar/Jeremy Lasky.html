Jeremy Lasky is a cinematographer at Pixar.

Filmography

  • A Bug's Life ( 1998 ): layout artist
  • Monsters, Inc. ( 2001 ): layout artist
  • Finding Nemo ( 2003 ): director of photography
  • Cars ( 2006 ): director of photography - camera
  • Ratatouille ( 2007 ): Pixar Productions
  • WALL•E ( 2008 ): director of photography - camera
  • Toy Story 3 ( 2010 ): director of photography - camera
  • Cars 2 ( 2011 ): director of photography - camera
  • Hiccups ( 2013 ): director
  • Bugged ( 2013 ): director
  • Spinning ( 2013 ): director
  • The Radiator Springs 500½ ( 2014 ): writer
  • Finding Dory ( 2016 ): director of photography - camera
  • Cars 3 ( 2017 ): director of photography - camera
  • Onward ( 2020 ): layout artist
  • Soul ( 2020 ): Pixar Studio Team: custom animation production

Lasky also directed the TV commercials for Combat Carl , Old Timer , and Transitron included on the Toy Story of Terror! home media release.
