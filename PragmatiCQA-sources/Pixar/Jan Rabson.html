Jan Rabson (born June 14, 1954) is an American actor and voice actor. He voice of Axel in A Bug's Life . He performed voices in Cars , WALL•E , Monsters University , the Toy Story Toons episode Small Fry , the Cars Toons: Mater's Tall Tales episodes Tokyo Mater , Monster Truck Mater , Air Mater , and Time Travel Mater , the construction worker and the TV announcer in Up , and Sparks in Toy Story 3 and Toy Story 3: The Video Game . Prior to Pixar , voicing Boris in GeoTrax , voicing characters in the films such as Theodore Rex , The Hunchback of Notre Dame , Hercules , Mulan , The Prince of Egypt , Ice Age: The Meltdown , Surf's Up , Happily N'Ever After , Horton Hears a Who! , and Cloudy with a Chance of Meatballs . He also worked on animated shows such as Animaniacs , Batman Beyond , James Bond Jr. , Justice League , Pinky and the Brain , Rugrats , Teenage Mutant Ninja Turtles , and The Yogi Bear Show .
