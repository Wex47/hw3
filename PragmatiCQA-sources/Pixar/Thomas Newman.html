Thomas Newman was born in 1955 in Los Angeles, California. Thomas Newman has composed the musical scores for many Pixar movies including Finding Nemo , WALL•E and Finding Dory . Besides working for Pixar, he also composed music for other movies such as The Shawshank Redemption , Cinderella Man and Fried Green Tomatoes .

Works

  • Finding Nemo
  • WALL•E
  • Finding Dory

Awards

  • Finding Nemo - Nominated for Academy Award for Best Original Score / Won 1 Award
  • WALL•E - Nominated for Academy Award for Best Original Score and Best Original Song " Down to Earth " / Won 3 Awards

Trivia

  • Thomas' cousin is Randy Newman , a well known musical composer who has worked on many films for Pixar.
