Ronald Lee Ermey (March 24, 1944 - April 15, 2018) was an American actor, voice actor and former military solider who performed the voice of Sarge in Toy Story , Toy Story 2 , and Toy Story 3 . He also reprised his role as Sarge in Toy Story 3: The Video Game .

He is primarily famous for his role as the drill instructor Gunnery Sergeant Hartman in Full Metal Jacket . Ermey passed away on April 15, 2018 from complications of pneumonia.
