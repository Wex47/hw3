The Human World is where monsters working at Monsters, Inc. gain access through various doors to collect screams (later laughter) from children to supply Monstropolis with power.

Known locations

  • Boo's Room (from where Boo invades the Monster World)
  • The Himalayas , in Nepal (where the Yeti , and briefly Mike and Sulley , get banished to)
  • An unnamed boy's room (shown when Mike entertains him to make him laugh)
  • Camp Teamwork Cabin (shown when Mike enters the Human World in Monsters University )
  • The home of Timmy and his parents in Party Central

Locations shown during the factory chase sequence

  • Hawaii
  • Japan
  • Paris, France
  • Trailer (home to Trailer Son and Mom )
