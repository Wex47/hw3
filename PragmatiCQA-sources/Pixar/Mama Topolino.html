Cars 2

"To all who know her, Luigi ’s aunt, Mama Topolino, is a masterful cook with the best fuel in the village of Carsoli , near Porto Corsa , Italy. Though she has a loving, but fiery relationship with her husband, Uncle Topolino , she shows her love and generosity for both family and friends by feeding everyone her renowned delizioso fuel."

Trivia

  • Topolino is a tribute to the Fiat 500 "Topolino" as well as a Disney in-joke: Topolino ("little mouse") is Mickey Mouse's name in Italian.
  • She is a 1951 Bella Machina Model B. She also resembles a 1963 Renault Dauphine.
  • According to her die-cast, her license plate reads "FI-26 45N1".
  • In non-English versions of Cars 2 , Mama Topolino is voiced by Sophia Loren .
