Bonnie Hunt (born September 22, 1961) is an American comedian, actress, voice artist, director, producer, writer, and host who performed the voices of Rosie in A Bug's Life , Flint in Monsters, Inc. , Sally in the Cars franchise, Dolly in Toy Story 3 and Toy Story Toons , and Karen Graves in Monsters University . This marks her fifth character she has voiced for a Pixar movie, making her the second-most employed voice actor at Pixar behind John Ratzenberger . She recently returned as Dolly in Toy Story 4 .

Bonnie has admitted that working on a Pixar movie is her favorite job to have:

“ I've been so fortunate to do so many things in my career, but I can say that I'm the most proud of the association with Pixar, my favorite place to work. I'm just honoured to be a part of it. ”
