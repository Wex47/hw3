Joshua "Josh" Cooley is a story artist and director at Pixar Animation Studios . He served as a storyboard artist for The Incredibles , Cars , Ratatouille , Up , and Cars 2 , and served as an additional story supervisor on Cars 2 . He wrote and directed the short film, George and A.J. , and also was the story editor of the Toy Story Toons short, Small Fry . He was then the solo director of Toy Story 4 . He performed the voice of Omega in Up , Roger in the Cars Toons: Mater's Tall Tales short, Moon Mater , the cashier and Lizard Wizard in Small Fry , Jangles the Clown in Inside Out , and Vroomaroundus Bugus in the Cars Toons: Tales from Radiator Springs short, Bugged . He also voiced Officer Phillips in Toy Story of Terror! .

Filmography

  • The Incredibles (2004): story artist (uncredited)
  • Cars (2006): additional storyboarding
  • Ratatouille (2007): story artist
  • Up (2009): story artist and voice of Omega
  • George and A.J. (2009): director and writer
  • Cars Toons (2010-2013): voices of Roger Shuttle (1 episode), Bugs (1 episode), and additional voices (2 episodes)
  • Cars 2 (2011): additional story supervisor
  • Toy Story Toons (2011): story editor (1 episode) and voices of Cashier (1 episode) and Lizard Wizard (1 episode)
  • Brave (2012): story artist (uncredited)
  • Toy Story of Terror! (2013): voice of Officer Phillips
  • Inside Out (2015): writer, story supervisor, and voice of Jangles the Clown
  • Riley's First Date? (2015): director, writer, and voice of Dad’s Sadness
  • Toy Story 4 (2019): director, original story, and additional screenplay material
  • Soul (2020): additional dialogue contributions
  • 22 vs. Earth (2021): writer
