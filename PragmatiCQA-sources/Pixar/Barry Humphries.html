John Barry Humphries , AO , CBE (born February 17, 1934; age 87) is an Australian comedian, satirist, dadaist, artist, author, and character actor who is best known for his television and on-stage alter egos Dame Edna Everage and Sir Les Patterson .

He provided the voice of Bruce in Finding Nemo . He reprised the role in Finding Nemo Submarine Voyage and Kinect Disneyland Adventures .
