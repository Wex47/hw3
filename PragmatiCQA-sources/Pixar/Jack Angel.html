Jack T. Angel (October 24, 1930 - October 19, 2021) was an American voice actor and former radio personality, who did the voice of Rocky Gibraltar in Toy Story and Toy Story 2 , Thud in A Bug's Life , Mr. Johannsen in Finding Nemo , and Chunk in Toy Story 3 and Toy Story 3: The Video Game . He also provided additional voices in Monsters, Inc. , Cars , Cars Toons: Mater's Tall Tales and Monsters University .

Prior to Pixar , Jack voiced Nikki in Balto , Papa Smurf in some Smurfs media, and an Old Time Who in Horton Hears a Who! . He also had very minor roles in non-Pixar films such as The Little Mermaid , Beauty and the Beast , Aladdin , Hercules , Ice Age: The Meltdown , The Lorax , and Despicable Me 2 .
