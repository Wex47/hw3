Sally (Toy Story)

Series information

Appearance

Toy Story

Sally is Hannah 's replacement doll for Janie when Sid turned it into a mutant toy .

Toy Story

Sally only appears in Toy Story after Sid becomes afraid of the toys being alive. Hannah tries to torture him by having him look at the doll, and Sid is thinking that the toy is alive. Sally is similar to Janie, though Sally has brunette hair while Janie has blonde hair. Also, Sally has a different dress and Sally has no shoes while Janie does. It is possible that Janie and Sally are made from the same company.
