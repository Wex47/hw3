Kelsey Grammer (born February 21, 1955) is an American actor, voice actor, producer, director, writer, singer, and activist who performed the voice of Stinky Pete in Toy Story 2 and in the television show, Woody's Roundup .

He is best known for his long-running role as Dr. Frasier Crane on the highly successful sitcom Cheers (co-starring with John Ratzenberger ) and it's equally successful spin-off Frasier . He is also known for voicing recurring villain Sideshow Bob on The Simpsons .

Trivia
