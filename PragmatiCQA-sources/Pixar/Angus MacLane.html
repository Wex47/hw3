Angus MacLane is a Pixar employee. Starting as an animator and story artist, he has progressed to directing the short films BURN-E and Small Fry , the TV special Toy Story of Terror! , and the film Lightyear . MacLane grew up in Portland, Oregon. He is a LEGO hobbyist and has built and posted online many models, including his CubeDudes and LEGO renditions of several Pixar characters. On February 5, 2015 , LEGO Ideas (a subdivision of LEGO that turns fan-built models into official LEGO products) announced that they had chosen his model of WALL•E to be a future official set.
