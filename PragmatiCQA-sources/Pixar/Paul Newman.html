Paul Newman (January 26, 1925 – September 26, 2008) was an American actor, film director, producer, indyCar driver, enterpreneur, and activist who performed the voice of Doc Hudson in Cars . He was also one of the special racing consultants.

He passed away on September 26, 2008 from lung cancer at age 83. Doc didn't appear in Cars 2 because, according to Nathan Stanton , he too passed away in the gap between Cars and Cars 2 .

Unused voice clips of Paul Newman originally recorded for the first film, as well as one from Cars: The Video Game , were used for Doc in Cars 3 .
