Toy Story: Animated Storybook is a CD-ROM game that was published by Disney Interactive in 1996 . It was based on the 1995 Pixar movie, Toy Story . Hamm is the narrator in the story.

Trivia

  • Tom Hanks didn’t voice Woody in this CD-ROM game, as he was replaced by Jim Hanks , his brother.
  • Some of these Pixar short film characters from Luxo, Jr. and Knick Knack made cameo appearances in this CD-ROM game.
