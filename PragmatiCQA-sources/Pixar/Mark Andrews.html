Mark Andrews is a director, writer and story artist at Pixar Animation Studios . He was the story supervisor for The Incredibles , directed the short film One Man Band and co-wrote the short films Jack-Jack Attack and One Man Band . He also co-directed Brave , which he took over from Brenda Chapman .

On January 14, 2013 , Andrews announced at the Golden Globe Awards, when Brave won Best Animated Feature Film , that he is writing and directing another original film at Pixar.
