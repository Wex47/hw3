Wallace Michael Shawn (born November 12, 1943) is an American actor, voice actor, playwright, essayist and comedian who performed the voice of Rex in Pixar 's first movie Toy Story . He reprised the role of Rex in Toy Story 2 , Toy Story 3 , Toy Story 4 , Toy Story Toons , Toy Story of Terror! , Toy Story That Time Forgot and various video games and spin-offs. He also had the role of Mr. Huph in The Incredibles .

Shawn has also had a number of roles in non-Pixar films, including Principal Mazur in A Goofy Movie , Wally Shawn in My Dinner with Andre , and Vizzini in The Princess Bride . He also had a role in Kung Fu Panda: Legends of Awesomeness as Taotie .
