Kristen Schaal (born January 24, 1978) is an American comedian, actress, voice actress, and writer who performed the voice of Trixie in Toy Story 3 . She reprised her role in Hawaiian Vacation , Toy Story of Terror! , and Toy Story That Time Forgot and will reprise it again in Toy Story 4 . She is also a comedian often featured in Comedy Central .

Trivia

  • She wore a Triceratops dress to the Toy Story 3 Red Carpet as a reference to her character Trixie.
  • She also voiced Mabel in the Disney XD show Gravity Falls .
  • She also plays Louise in the FOX cartoon Bob's Burgers who she would be recognized as later on
