Loop is an experimental Pixar animated short film from the new SparkShorts division of the studio. It will be directed by Erica Milsom and produced by Michael Warch . Danielle Feinberg is the Supervising Technical Director and Director of Photography, Lighting for the film. It premiered on Disney's new direct-to-consumer streaming service Disney+ in early 2020 .
