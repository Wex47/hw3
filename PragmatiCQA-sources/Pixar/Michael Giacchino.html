Michael Giacchino (born October 10, 1967, in Riverside, New Jersey) has composed the musical scores for many Pixar movies including The Incredibles , Ratatouille , Up , Cars 2 , Inside Out , Coco and Incredibles 2 . He also has worked on a few Pixar short films. Besides working for Pixar, he has composed music for other movies such as Super 8 , Jurassic World , Mission: Impossible - Ghost Protocol and John Carter .

Trivia

  • In Coco , the orchestra conductor at the Sunrise Spectacular is a caricature of Giacchino.
  • So far, Giacchino has composed every Pixar film that Brad Bird has directed, including The Incredibles and its sequel , as well as Ratatouille .
  • In addition to scoring the music for The Incredibles and its sequel , he also scored the music for the Incredicoaster (formerly called "California Screamin'") at Disney California Adventure.
