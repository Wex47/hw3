Cars 2

"You know him better than you know your own brother, even if you don't have a brother. He's the Caddy with the golden touch, Mel 'Are you there, Caller?' Dorado — host of the 14 Gold Fender-winning syndicated talk show, the 'Mel Dorado Show.' With a career spanning more than three decades, when he started as an assistant at WCNC (World Car News Chicago), nobody steers an interview quite like Mel."

At the occasion of the forthcoming World Grand Prix , Mel Dorado interviewed Miles Axlerod and Francesco Bernoulli on his show before receiving Mater as a caller.

Trivia

  • As hinted by his name, Mel Dorado is a 1976 Cadillac Eldorado.
  • Mel has a similar car body to Tex Dinoco , a car from the first Cars movie who owns Dinoco , and Dex , the record company owner in Heavy Metal Mater .
  • His license plate reads FSHBWL, which stands for "fish bowl" just like Acer , Fred Pacer , Carlo Maserati and Cruz Besouro .
