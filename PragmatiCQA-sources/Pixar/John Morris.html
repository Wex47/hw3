John Charles Morris (born October 2, 1984 ) is an American voice actor who performed the voice of Andy Davis in Toy Story and Toy Story 2 as a child. He voiced the grown-up Andy in Toy Story 3 as a teenager. He said in a recent interview that playing the role of Andy has been a truly great experience in his career and that out of all the Toy Story voice cast, John Ratzenberger , who voices Hamm , is his favorite because Ratzenberger has a role in all Pixar films.
