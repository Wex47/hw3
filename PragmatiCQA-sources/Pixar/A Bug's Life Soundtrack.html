The A Bug's Life soundtrack was composed by Randy Newman . [[pl:Dawno temu w trawie/Ścieżka dźwiękowa|

]]The first track is a song called " The Time of Your Life " written and performed by Newman, while all the others are orchestral cues. The album has gone off the market but is available for purchase on iTunes.

The score won the Grammy Award for Best Instrumental Composition.
