Construction Foreman Tom

Series information

Appearance

Up

Portrayed by

Tom is a minor character in Up .

Up

Tom tries to persuade Carl to go to Shady Oaks Retirement Village so a shopping center can be built on his house's land. He is not seen again throughout the rest of the entire film.

Day & Night

Tom makes a cameo appearance in Day & Night as the lumberjack.

Trivia

  • As his name applies, he is a construction foreman.
