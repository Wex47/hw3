Kathy is a minor character in Finding Nemo and Finding Dory .

Finding Nemo

She is a student of Mr. Ray resembling a blue and green spotted fish with prominent buck teeth. Her only dialogue in the film is "Oh, my gosh! Nemo 's swimming out to sea!"

Finding Dory

In Finding Dory , Kathy has more dialogue. She appears to be a triggerfish of some sort. She gets very concerned about Mr. Ray coming back from migration.

Trivia

  • She is named after her voice actress, Katherine Ringgold .
