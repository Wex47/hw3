Mickie McGowan is an American casting director and voice actress who was a casting director at Pixar.

Mickie has performed the voice of Police Officer Edith in Up , and also did additional voices in Toy Story , A Bug's Life , Toy Story 2 , Monsters, Inc. , Finding Nemo , Cars , WALL•E , Tokyo Mater , Toy Story 3 , Monsters University , and Inside Out .

Prior to Pixar, Mickie voices Princess Tara in Super Friends: The Legendary Super Powers Show . She also does additional voices in movies such as The Little Mermaid , Beauty and the Beast , Aladdin , Hercules , Mulan , Tarzan , Lilo & Stitch , Home on the Range , Ice Age: The Meltdown , Surf's Up , Horton Hears a Who! , Cloudy with a Chance of Meatballs , The Lorax , Despicable Me 2 , and Minions .
