Julia Louis-Dreyfus (born January 13, 1961) is an American actress, comedian, and producer who performed the voice of Princess Atta in A Bug's Life . She also voices Laurel Lightfoot , the mother of the Lightfoots in Onward . Dreyfus is well known for her role of Elaine Benes in the sitcom Seinfeld , and now appears in her own sitcom The New Adventures of Old Christine .

Trivia

  • She also voiced the character Rochelle in DisneyToon Studios ' film Planes .
  • Her Seinfeld co-stars Wayne Knight and Estelle Harris would later voice Al McWhiggin and Mrs. Potato Head in Toy Story 2 , with Harris reprising her role of Mrs. Potato Head in Toy Story 3 and 4 .
