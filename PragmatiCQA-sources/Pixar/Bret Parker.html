Bret Parker is a Pixar animator and voice actress. She performed the voice of Kari McKeen , Jack-Jack 's babysitter, in The Incredibles and Jack-Jack Attack (and in a deleted scene from The Incredibles 2 ), as well as the voice of the title character in Purl . Bret has also provided the voice of DJ Blu-Jay in Small Fry . She also voiced the mother living with her son in a trailer by the swamp in Monsters, Inc. . She also has additional voices in Monsters University and Inside Out .
