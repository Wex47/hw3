A Bug's Life

"The colony's "resident thespian," Mr. Soil is an ant educator and a trusted advisor to the Queen and Princess Atta , but his true love is the theater! He and the children from the South Tunnel Elementary second grade put on a play depicting the upcoming battle with the grasshoppers—complete with a gory mural. A natural teacher, Mr. Soil also uses his persuasive talents to calm panicked ants. "We are trained professionals," he says as he leads them around a leaf blocking the line to the offering stone."

He is a thespian: the Queen tells the circus bugs he has played lead roles in colony productions such as Picnic. He is also good at rescuing ants from difficult situations. He helps a group of ants circling a fallen leaf, claiming it is nothing compared to a fallen twig in 1993 . He leads the celebration upon the arrival of the circus bugs, demonstrates his ability to play a banjo, and leads the play portraying the future battle between the bugs and the grasshoppers . He has purple eyes instead of blue.
