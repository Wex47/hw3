Cars 2

"Alexander Hugo is not who you think he is. He is also known as Chop Shop Alex and Alexander the Not-So-Great, among other names. He's got a rap sheet a mile long and connections to the Hugo organized crime family. He's currently wanted in France, Germany, the Czech Republic, and Serbia, so check his rap sheet before you go anywhere with him."

Alexander is a black car who works as one of the personal guards of Victor Hugo . He is part of the Hugo family that works with Professor Z . He is the only black bodyguard lemon that talks in the film. He appears to be close friends with Ivan . Near the end of the film, Alexander is arrested in London with his fellow lemons.

Trivia

  • He is modeled after a Yugo.
  • His license plate is PA TRON 93, the same as Victor Hugo.
  • Alexander has no regular die-cast, only a version with a party hat.
