Blake Clark (born February 2, 1946) is an American stand-up comedian, actor, and voice actor. He is the current voice of Slinky Dog in the Toy Story franchise, replacing the late Jim Varney (who voiced Slinky in the first two films ).

He debuted as the voice of Slinky in Toy Story 2 On Ice , and later became the official voice starting with Toy Story 3 .

Trivia

  • Clark and Jim Varney knew each other for a long time and were good friends since they met on the set of Fast Food . In an interview for Toy Story 3 , Clark described Varney as a good man and one of his best friends, stating that he misses him.
  • According to John Lasseter , Clark sounds "an awful lot like him (Varney)" due to his deep, graveled voice.
