Owen Wilson (born November 18, 1968 ) is an American actor, producer and screenwriter who performed the voice of Lightning McQueen in Cars , Mater and the Ghostlight , Cars 2 , The Radiator Springs 500½ , and Cars 3 . He also played Hansel McDonald in Zoolander and for an interview for Zoolander 2 on the Italian Vanity Fair site, and he played Jedadiah in the Night at the Museum franchise, and Nate Pullman in Wonder , Wilson confirmed he would reprise his role as Lightning in Cars 3 .
