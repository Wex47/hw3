Alexander Gould (born May 4, 1994 ; age 27) is an American actor most wildly known for his role in Finding Nemo as the titular character Nemo . He also starred in the Showtime sitcom Weeds as Shane Botwin . He did not return to reprise his role as Nemo in Finding Dory due to a change in his voice, and was replaced by a child actor named Hayden Rolence . However, he has voiced a different character who is named Carl .
