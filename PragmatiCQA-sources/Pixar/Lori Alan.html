Lori Alan (born July 18, 1966 ) performed the voice of Mrs. Anderson in Toy Story 3 , Toy Story 4 , and Toy Story Toons . She has also voiced Tae-Kwon Doe in Small Fry . She is also known for the voice of Pearl the Whale, Mr. Krabs' daughter in SpongeBob SquarePants , and Diane Simmons in Family Guy . She also had an additional voice in the Cars Toons episodes Air Mater and Time Travel Mater . She also voiced the MU Bus Driver in Monsters University and Riley's mother 's Sadness emotion. She will reprise her role as Mrs. Anderson in Toy Story 4 .
