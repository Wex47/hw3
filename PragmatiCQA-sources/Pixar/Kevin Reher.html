Kevin Reher is a producer and editor at Pixar . He produced A Bug's Life and Cars 3 . Reher continues to be casting director for almost every film since.

Filmography

  • Toy Story ( 1995 ): production controller
  • A Bug's Life ( 1998 ): producer
  • Finding Nemo ( 2003 ): casting
  • The Incredibles ( 2004 ): casting
  • Cars ( 2006 ): casting
  • Ratatouille ( 2007 ): casting
  • WALL•E ( 2008 ): casting
