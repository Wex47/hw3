Appearance

The Lost girl is a minor character who appears near the end of Toy Story 4 . She is Gabby Gabby 's owner.

Toy Story 4

While Woody , Bo Peep , Duke Caboom , Ducky and Bunny make their way to the carousel, Gabby Gabby sees a lost girl sobbing. Woody and Bo Peep encourage Gabby Gabby to get adopted by her. After Gabby Gabby positions herself, Ducky and Bunny roll a baseball to lead the girl to notice Gabby Gabby. Picking up the doll, the girl asks if she too is lost and pulls her string, making Gabby Gabby ask for friendship via her voice box. Deciding to help Gabby Gabby, the girl then stops a passing security guard and asks her to help find her parents. Fortunately, they are not too far away, asking another policewoman about their daughter's whereabouts, when the girl finds them. Once reunited with her parents, the girl goes away, taking Gabby Gabby with her.
