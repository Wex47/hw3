Debi Derryberry is an actress and voiceover artist. She provided the voice for the Pizza Planet Intercom in Toy Story . Another voice from Toy Story 2 was Amy . She also performed in A Bug's Life and Incredibles 2 .

Some of her non- Pixar work includes Jimmy Neutron and Planet Sheen for Nickelodeon, Clay on Playhouse Disney, Patty in Walt Disney’s Carousel of Progress at the Magic Kingdom, Gnochi from the Curious George series on PBS Kids, and characters in Ice Age 2 . Her acting work includes shows such as iCarly and Get a Life .
