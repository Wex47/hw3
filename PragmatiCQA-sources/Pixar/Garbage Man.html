The Garbage Man makes a brief appearance in Toy Story 3 . He works for Tri-County Sanitation .

Toy Story 3

In the film, he finds Lotso next to his garbage truck's tire after he stops. He picks the bear up, claiming he has once owned a Lots-o'-Huggin' Bear as a kid. Sticking to his love for the teddy bear (as well as his strawberry scent), he then straps Lotso to the front of his truck with a few other toys he has also collected. This unknowingly gives Lotso his well-deserved comeuppance.
