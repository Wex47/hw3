Up was released on Blu-ray and DVD in the United States on November 10, 2009 , and in the United Kingdom on February 15, 2010. It features the film plus the theatrical short Partly Cloudy and the new short , as well as an audio commentary by director Pete Docter, the documentary Adventure is Out There on the filmmakers' research journey to South America, The Many Endings of Muntz (an alternate ending of sorts), and a digital copy. The film was re-released on 2-disc Blu-ray/DVD combo pack on July 5, 2011 .
