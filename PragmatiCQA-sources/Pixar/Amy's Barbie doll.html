Amy 's Barbie doll is a minor character in Toy Story 2 .

Toy Story 2

She is a Barbie doll with some paint tattoos on the right side her face and some of her hair cut off, which was from her owner, Amy . Despite that, she still loves Amy.

Amy's Barbie doll is only seen at the Tri-County International Airport at the end of the film, telling Stinky Pete (who has been shoved into the backpack by Woody and his friends to "learn the true meaning of 'playtime'") that he will like Amy because she's an artist (which becomes true in an interview with Stinky Pete after the release of Toy Story 2 ).
