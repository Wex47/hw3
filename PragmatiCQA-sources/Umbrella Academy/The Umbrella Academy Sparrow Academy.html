The Umbrella Academy: Sparrow Academy is the announced fourth volume in The Umbrella Academy comic series created and written by Gerard Way .

History

Gerard Way began working on Vol. 4 some time around September 2019. On July 29, 2020, he shared in an interview with Forbes that Vol. 4 would be entitled The Umbrella Academy: Sparrow Academy . Way stated that it would finally start to address what happened to the other children born on the same day as the Umbrella Academy siblings. As suggested by the title, it will feature the Sparrow Academy , introduced as the Sparrows in Volume 3 .
