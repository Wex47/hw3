Hotel Oblivion is a large hotel tower built by The Monocle located on a distant planet. It served as a prison for notorious criminals caught by the Umbrella Academy , including but not limited to Dr. Terminal , Perseus XI , The Murder Magician , and Obscura . It was guarded by the Scientific Man . Sometime around 1980, Perseus X broke every prisoner out of the Hotel.
