This article is about the Temps Aeternalis, an organisation from the comics . You may be looking for the Temps Commission from the Netflix adaptation .

The Temps Aeternalis is a large organisation dealing with, and overseeing time travel and associated processes. They capture Number 5 and experiment on him, thereby combining his DNA with that of various serial killers. This makes Number 5 a deadly assassin with a 100% success rate.
