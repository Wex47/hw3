The Lucifer Clark Show is a talk show hosted by Lucifer Clark .

History

Lucifer Clark had the Murder Magician and The Assistant on to interview them about the murder of The Rumor . The Murder Magician unveils the Murderbot , which wreaks havoc in the studio and destroys a good portion of it. The studio was further destroyed by the arrival of the Umbrella Academy and their battle with the Murderbot .
