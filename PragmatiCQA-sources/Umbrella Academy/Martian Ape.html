A Martian Ape is a species that appears in The Umbrella Academy .

History

Not much is known of Martian Apes, except that they live on Mars . When Spaceboy was in a horrible accident on his trip to Mars, The Monocle performed an experimental surgery placing his head on a Martian Ape's body. The Monocle kept the head in his laboratory.

Abilities

Martian Apes are incredibly strong and have a high invulnerability.
