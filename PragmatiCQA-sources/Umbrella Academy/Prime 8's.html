Prime 8’s is a band that was established by the Kraken and Vanya, during the Academy’s adolescent phase.

History

Shortly after it formed, an ape known as Body acted as the drummer. The band later disbanded shortly after the Kraken was called into active heroism, forcing Vanya to depart the city via aeroplane. The only album the band ever produced was entitled I Don’t Wanna Kill the President , the title of which confused the Monocle, until Vanya reminded him that it was intended as a political statement.

Trivia

  • The name of the band is most obviously a pun on the word “primate”.
