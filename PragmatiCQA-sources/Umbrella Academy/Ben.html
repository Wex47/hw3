Biography

Ben is a robot who served at Annihilation Control on the Moon as Spaceboy 's servant. He addressed Spaceboy as "Number One." Ben informed Spaceboy that Dr. Pogo was calling for him, a call that would ultimately reveal that Sir Reginald Hargreeves had died. Ben readied Spaceboy's ship for the trip to Earth. As he prepared to leave, Spaceboy asked Ben to remind him to have the robot reprogrammed when he returned since he wanted to stop Ben calling him "Number One," since it was a name that only his father used.

Trivia

Ben is presumably named after Spaceboy's deceased brother, Ben Hargreeves .
