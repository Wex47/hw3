Paris is a city on the planet Earth , known for the Eiffel Tower designed by Gustave Eiffel . Ten years after the event that saw the simultaneous birth of forty-three babies to mothers with no previous signs of pregnancy, the Zombie-Robot Gustave Eiffel used the Eiffel Tower to cause terror in Paris by throwing visitors off the monument to their deaths. The members of the Umbrella Academy defeated Eiffel and saved Paris. The battle resulted in the death of Eiffel and the launch of the Eiffel Tower because it was secretly a spaceship.
