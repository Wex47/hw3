The Murderbot was created by the Murder Magician to cause chaos. It appeared in the story " ...But the Past Ain't Through With You ."

History

The Murderbot was created by the Murder Magician with the money he received from the Oppenheimer Prize for Villainy . He unveiled it on a talk show where it attempted to create as much chaos as possible before being destroyed by Spaceboy , The Horror , and The Séance .

Abilities

The Murderbot's only known ability was incredible strength.
