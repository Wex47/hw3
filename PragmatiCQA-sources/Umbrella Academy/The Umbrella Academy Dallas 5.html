The Umbrella Academy: Dallas 5 ( All the Animals in the Zoo ) is the fifth issue of The Umbrella Academy: Dallas . Published on March 25, 2009, it was written by Gerard Way with art by Gabriel Bá .

Synopsis

Propelled by nuclear armageddon, The Séance, Spaceboy, and The Kraken race through time to solve the mystery of Number Five's past, and find themselves thrown into the turmoil of war, assassination, and strange visitations into their own childhoods.

This imaginative series nears the conclusion of its second story arc-Dallas, in which the history of the United States is radically rewritten, and not a thing is changed.
