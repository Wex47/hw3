Inspector Lupo is a police officer, detective, and friend of The Umbrella Academy . He appears in 4 issues of the comics however in the Netflix adaptation he is replaced by Detective Patch .

Physical Appearance

Inspector Lupo appears to be old-ish with a thick mustache and little or no hair on the top of his head. He mostly wears a hat and glasses and is often seen with a smoke in his mouth.
