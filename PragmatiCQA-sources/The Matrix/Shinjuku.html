Shinjuku was a neighborhood located far north in the International District , as well as located beside Kowloon . The residing contact is the exile known as Dame White , while the residing gang are the Jade Moons . The neighborhood is known for being a major residential area. Shinjuku is also notable for having one of the best hotels in the city, The White Lotus, owned by dame White. The club in this neighborhood is the Masamune .
