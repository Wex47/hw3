You have a problem with authority, Mr. Anderson. You believe that you are special, that somehow the rules do not apply to you. Obviously, you are mistaken.

”
― Mr. Rhineheart to Neo

Mr. Rhineheart was Neo's former boss at MetaCortex . He wore a suit and a tie, similar to what the Agents wear.

The Matrix

After meeting Trinity in a nightclub , Neo woke up late the workday after and had shown up late for work. Mr. Rhineheart then called Neo into his office and told him off, reminding him that he was not above the rules. Rhineheart also threatened Neo to an ultimatum: either get to work on time or find another job.

The Matrix Reloaded and Revolutions

It is unknown what happens to Mr. Rhineheart after Neo's escape from the Matrix .
