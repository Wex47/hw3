A code bomb is a virtual weapon of mass destruction, like a digital version of a nuclear weapon. Morpheus used these as part of a protest to convince the Machines to return Neo 's remains to Zion . They were notable by how they displayed the truth about the nature of the Matrix , revealing the underlying code beneath the virtual world, and with the shock proving severe to nearby Bluepills who witness the display.

Rumors

TaeCross , liaison to E Pluribus Neo (EPN), was rumored to be working with Morpheus a full year after his last known sighting. Due to the frustrations of repeated setbacks and poor dealings, TaeCross carried out attacks unto the city using code bombs. It is presumed that TaeCross got the technology from Morpheus himself. In finality, TaeCross simultaneously unleashed a set of 8 code bombs at the First Union Church in Camon Central . His final whereabouts after that is unknown.
