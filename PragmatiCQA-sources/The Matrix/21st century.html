The 21 century (2001–2100 A.D. in the Gregorian Calendar ) saw the creation of Artificial Intelligence and the Machines , the invention of the hover pad , and the start of the Second Renaissance . According to Agent Smith, the early 21st century was considered "the peak of your[human] civilization."

A.I.

The Resistance of the Seventh Matrix believed that humanity created (in Morpheus ' words "gave birth to") a single Artificial Intelligence during the early part of the 21 century. This A.I. then in turn created ("spawned") the sentient Machines and Programs .

For a time, there was peace and harmony as Machines were programmed to serve humans, cleaning households and performing in other hazardous working conditions Humans . As a result, the humans became increasingly lazy and complacent while the Machines became more and more advanced with different models of each decade or so. It eventually got to a point where the Machines somehow started to gain sentience and a sense of self-preservation.

Hovercraft

Hoverpad technology seems to have been invented by 2026 at the latest, when the oldest known Hovercraft to survive to the time of the Seventh Matrix , the Pequod , was built in the United States of America . This was followed by the Nebuchadnezzar ( 2069 ), and the Osiris ( 2079 ), with the Logos , Vigilant , and Mjolnir being built in the early 22 century .

The Machines improved on hover pad technology and built the 01 Versatran , the first commercially available flying car.

Start of the Second Renaissance

The creation of the Machines led to the start of the Second Renaissance in 2090 .
