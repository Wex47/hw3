The Matrix: Comic Book Preview was a sixteen page booklet printed in March 1999 and meant to be given away at the release of The Matrix but was recalled instead when the content was deemed too controversial.

It included the Series 1 introduction , the comic " Déjà Vu ", pin-ups by Bill Sienkiewicz , Ted McKeever and Aron Weisenfeld as well as adverts for the comics , the film and The Matrix: Music from the Motion Picture .
