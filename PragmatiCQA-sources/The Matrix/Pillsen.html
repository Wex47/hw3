Pillsen was a neighborhood in the Downtown district. The residing contact is the Chef , while the gang that stalks this neighborhood's streets are the Shades .

Pillsen has become the mecca for the office workers who drive the accounts of Industry Square . It is mostly common to see a café packed with people, who are silent save for the clicking of their personal computers, spreadsheets, tracing errors in accounts and checking the numbers. They are also considered in some ways the most blue of the bluepills, being dedicated to make the Matrix into a more orderly place.
