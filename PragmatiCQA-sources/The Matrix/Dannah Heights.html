Dannah Heights , formerly known as Hammerville , was a neighborhood in Richland which was located north of Apollyon and southwest of Kedemoth . The residing Exile contact is The Digger and the dominant gang are the Silver Bullets , who were fighting a war against the Lupine Exiles, particularly their bitter rivals known as the Furies gang. At night they make the neighborhood their personal hunting ground, mainly getting any Lupines they find, but were also known to hunt a stray red pill in order to keep their coffers full. The club in this neighborhood is Club Sphinx .
