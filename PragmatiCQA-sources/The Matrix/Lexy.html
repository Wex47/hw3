Lexy is a character in The Matrix Resurrections , and an ally of Bugs .

Lexy is a loyal crewmember of the Mnemosyne , but she doesn't particularly believe in the One as a concept (though she still wants to find Neo the man). Instead, Lexy reveals that she actually idolizes Trinity , her legacy as one of Zion's greatest warriors even sixty years after her death. Lexy says she always wished she could be as powerful and fearless as Trinity was.

Notes

  • Lexy has the same tribal tattoos along her torso in the Real world as she does in her Matrix RSI . It's unclear whether this is a case of her RSI reflecting her real-life appearance, or if it was the other way around - that in real life she actively started trying to match the appearance of her Matrix avatar. Many people outside the Matrix look similar to their avatars, but don't have luxuries like jewelry, hair product, cosmetics, good clothing, etc. She has a tattoo on the back of her neck that looks somewhat like a plug socket with a red oval-like line and a black oval-like line. This likely represents her real-life plug on the back of her neck in the matrix.
