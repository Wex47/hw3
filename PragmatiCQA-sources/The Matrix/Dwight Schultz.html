Dwight Schultz (born William Dwight Schultz , November 24, 1947) is an American stage, television, and film actor, who voiced Nonaka in Matriculated , and various characters in Beyond and The Second Renaissance from The Animatrix . He was brought to the project by English language version director Jack Fletcher , who got the voice actors from the English Final Fantasy X - Matt McKenzie , James Arnold Taylor , John DiMaggio , Tara Strong , Hedy Burress and Dwight Schultz.

Career

He is best known for his roles as Captain " Howling Mad" Murdock on the 1980s action show The A-Team and as Reginald Barclay in Star Trek: The Next Generation , the film Star Trek: First Contact , Star Trek: Voyager and Star Trek: Elite Force II . He is also well known in animation as the Mad Scientist Dr. Animo in the Ben 10 series, Chef Mung Daal in the children's cartoon Chowder , and Eddie the Squirrel in CatDog .
