Baldwin Heights was a neighborhood surrounded by other neighborhoods in Downtown . This place serves as the home for the middle class managers and young "up-and-comer" bluepill workers. it is also the home to the Exile contact known as the Coroner . However, one of the downsides of this neighborhood is the residing gang known appropriately as the Assassins , led by Voltan , who view their murders as a work of art. Sergeant Wynn is a collector in the area that is at war with the gang.
