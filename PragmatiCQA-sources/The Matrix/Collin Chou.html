Collin Chou is a veteran actor of about 100 action films from Asia. In The Matrix franchise , he played the program Seraph , the Oracle's loyal bodyguard who appeared in The Matrix Reloaded , The Matrix Revolutions , Enter the Matrix , The Matrix: Path of Neo and The Matrix Online .

As well as The Matrix franchise , he is also known for his performances in films such as SPL: Sha Po Lang , Fearless and Flash Point co-starring with Jet Li, Donnie Yen and Sammo Hung.
