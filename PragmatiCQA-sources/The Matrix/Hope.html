Hope was a gifted little girl who, in the Matrix , had the ability to change her RSI at will and sometimes adopting the body of an elderly woman or a grown man. She resided in Saint George's Orphanage and had a white rabbit stuffed doll.

After The Oracle cryptically told some Resistance members about the child by saying "and the child would be hope", crew members of an unnamed hovercraft would attempt her extraction and would assume that she was gifted or aware. The cryptic message would later be correctly interpreted by Link as also meaning that the name of the child is Hope.

The Agents , perhaps sensing Hope being aware, had burned the orphanage in their search for her. The next day, Eight Ball went into the deserted orphanage in order to extract Hope but got "burned", only managing to tag Hope before getting killed by the Agents. Soon after, Link attempted the same extraction, encountering multiple people while following the tag program, before identifying Hope and realizing that she had the ability to shape-shift. Hope, sensing the approaching Agents and warning Link that "the dragons are coming" and that "the dragons are here", ran to the roof. Link followed suit while simultaneously being pursued by Agents.

Hope used her abilities to fly both of them out of the rooftop and to her eventual escape from the Matrix and, the next day, was introduced to the captain of the crew in the Real World .
