Daniel Bernhardt (born August 31, 1965, in Worblaufen BE, Switzerland) is a Swiss actor and martial arts expert known for his role of Agent Johnson from The Matrix Reloaded and The Matrix Resurrections , the second and fourth films of The Matrix franchise .

Biography

He has appeared in a number of films and shows featuring martial arts action sequences, including little-known films like Future War (1997), film series like Bloodsport 2, 3, and 4 (in which he replaced actor and martial artist Jean Claude Van Damme, star of the original film), and game-inspired television show Mortal Kombat: Conquest .
