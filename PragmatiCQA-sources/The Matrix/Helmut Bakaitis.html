Helmut Bakaitis (born 26 September, 1944) is an Australian actor, director and screen-writer, who plays the humorless Architect in both The Matrix Reloaded and The Matrix Revolutions films, who is responsible for the creation of the various versions of the Matrix .

The Architect features an analytic and emotionless genius whose informative speech shed light tot the truth behind the Matric and The One's Function .
