Shimada is the first mate of the new Neo's Hope hovercraft, which is captained by Kid . Both Kid and Shimada form E Pluribus Neo in order to counter the emerging threat of the Cypherites .

Death

While Shimada was jacked inside the Matrix, Cypherite officer Veil managed to sneak on-board Shimada's hovercraft and managed to pull Shimada's cranial jack, instantly killing her.
