Ashely is Lisa 's twin sister, daughter of Maggie . They both resided aboard a ship full of potentials .

The sisters wanted to surprise their mum for her birthday by hacking the Matrix to trace her in order to see her. Ashley jacked Lisa into the Matrix during meal time, and the tracing was detected. Sentinels found Maggie's ship, the Calappidae .

While Maggie hugs her daughter, the ship is destroyed, killing everyone jacked in.
