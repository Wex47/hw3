I Kant is a comic written and illustrated by Kaare Andrews with 3D models and compositing by Ron Turner , color design by Dave McCaig and letters by Comicraft . As one of three stories first published in The Matrix Comics Volume 2 , it was not included in any of the three webcomic series nor available online. It was later republished in The Matrix Comics: 20th Anniversary Edition . The title is a pun based around the name of the philosopher Immanuel Kant and the Kid 's early statement of disbelief: "I can't".
