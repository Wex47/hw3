Ueno was a neighborhood in the International District . It was located on the left side of international and was next to Murasaki . The residing exile contact was Mr. Bishop , while the residing gang were the Shurikens , who are led by Jiang . The Shurikens were highly protective of this neighborhood. If anyone were seeking something that is not native to the city, Ueno is the place to go. It was also the home of some of the largest importing companies, including the Chotte Brothers Imports.
