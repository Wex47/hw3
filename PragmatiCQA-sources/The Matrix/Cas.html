Cas was the widow of the Nebuchadnezzar s former pilot, Dozer . She lived in Zion with their two young children. She also spent time with her sister-in-law, Zee , and maintains friendly rapport with Zee's husband, Link .

Behind the Scenes

  • Cas was played by Gina Torres, who was the then real-life wife of Laurence Fishburne , who played Morpheus .
