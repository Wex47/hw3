Jada Pinkett Smith is the actress who portrays Niobe , captain of the Logos in The Matrix Reloaded , The Matrix Revolutions , Enter the Matrix and The Matrix Resurrections . She originally auditioned for the role of Trinity in the first Matrix film.

She is also famous for playing Fish Mooney in the television series Gotham .
