Gina Torres played Cas in The Matrix Reloaded and The Matrix Revolutions . She is also famous for playing Zoe in the cult classic television series Firefly and its sequel film Serenity .

She is married to Laurence Fishburne . The couple became engaged in February 2001, shortly before filming began on The Matrix Reloaded and The Matrix Revolutions on March 1. They married on September 22, 2002, at The Cloisters museum in New York City, a month after filming had concluded on August 21.
