Matt McColm (born January 31, 1965) is a prolific stunt actor. He portrayed Agent Thompson in The Matrix Reloaded . He appeared again with co-star Keanu Reeves in the 2014 action thriller John Wick , where his character was killed by Reeves in a shootout. Other appearances by McColm include stuntwork in the award-winning Argo and the popular comic book film Iron Man 2 , as well as Transformers and xXx: State of the Union .
