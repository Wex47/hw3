Marcus Chong (born Marcus Wyatt on July 8, 1967) is an American actor who played Tank in The Matrix .

Previous to The Matrix , he was most famous as his role Huey P. Newton in the 1995 Mario Van Peebles movie Panther .

Controversy

Marcus Chong, who played Tank, pilot of Morpheus' hovership the Nebuchadnezzar in the 1999 sci-fi blockbuster, filed a lawsuit at Los Angeles Superior Court on Friday against Warner Bros and parent company AOL Time Warner.
