Daniel "Walrus" Myers, also known as Walrus , was an administrator on the Massive Multi-player online game known as The Matrix Online .

During the merging of the pre-monolith servers into the three for SOE, Walrus announced that due to the various inconsistencies, the storyline concerning the saving Neurophyte would be dropped.

He was also involved in ending the game in July 31st, 2009.
