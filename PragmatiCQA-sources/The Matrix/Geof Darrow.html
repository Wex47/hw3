Geofrey Darrow was the conceptual designer and storyboard artist for The Matrix , The Matrix Reloaded , The Matrix Revolutions and the upcoming film The Matrix Resurrections . He also drew the comic " Bits and Pieces of Information " for The Matrix Website based on a script written by the Wachowskis . He also worked with the Wachowskis on Shaolin Cowboy for Burlyman Entertainment and the film Speed Racer .

Darrow is well-known for creating imagery of bio-mechanical equipment and beings. Before working with the Wachowskis, Darrow did the artwork for Frank Miller's graphic novel, .
