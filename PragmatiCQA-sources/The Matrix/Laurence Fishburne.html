Laurence Fishburne (born July 30, 1961) played Morpheus , one of the prominent characters in The Matrix franchise . He was born in Georgia, America.

Archival footage for his performance from the first three live-action Matrix films was reused for The Matrix Resurrections .

He has also starred in Mission Impossible III , Man of Steel , Batman v Superman: Dawn of Justice , and has a frequent starring role in CSI: Crime Scene Investigation .
