Shane Black was a human who served as the Liaison for the E Pluribus Neo organization in the Recursion instance of The Matrix Online game.

During the observation of the Unlimit battle in the aqueduct, the memories from the destroyed Smith briefly resurfaced, as evidenced by his vocabulary.

He was later killed.
