The Wachowskis , Lana Wachowski (born June 21, 1965) and Lilly Wachowski (born December 29, 1967), are American film directors, writers and producers, most famous for creating The Matrix franchise including writing and directing the Matrix trilogy.

The Matrix franchise

Main article: The Matrix franchise

The Matrix franchise primarily consists of a trilogy of science-fiction-action films. The films' are later revealed to also serve as trans allegories and the stories were "all about the desire for transformation," adding that it came from "a closeted point of view" because the (corporate) world was not ready to hear the message at the time.
