Ellster

Ellster is the medical officer on the hovership Mnemosyne . She is the granddaughter of Roland .

Biography

Ellster believed in the legend of Neo because her grandfather Roland knew him personally. She explains to Neo that Roland always laughed joking how he'd never believed in Neo as " the One ". She goes on to reveal, however, that in private Roland said that Neo's sacrifice during the Battle of Zion proved him wrong, and in the process Neo essentially freed his mind a second time.
