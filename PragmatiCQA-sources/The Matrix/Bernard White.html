Bernard White was an actor who played Rama Kandra , an exile program , the husband of Kamala and father of Sati . He first appears in a brief shot in The Matrix Reloaded , leaving The Merovingian 's restaurant Le Vrai just as Neo , Morpheus and Trinity arrive, but his main scene is in The Matrix Revolutions where he discusses Karma with Neo at Mobil Avenue .
