A Corrupted Program is a program created by the mysterious Assassin in his own image, flies, with the use of a virus. Stronger versions are called the Complete Corrupted . There also exists at least one entity called the Wasteland Corrupted , which consists of gigantic mass of flies that swarm around dangerously corrupted code.

After the Assassin killed Morpheus , he spawned various programs known as corrupted from his essence, which were flies. Fought and found in The Matrix Online .
