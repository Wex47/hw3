Don Davis was the music composer, conductor and orchestrator for The Matrix , The Matrix Reloaded , The Matrix Revolutions and The Animatrix . His music was also reused in the unofficial fan project The Fanimatrix .

Inspiration

He is said to have inspired his music whilst noting that mirrors and reflections were frequently seen throughout the movie. Davis then focused on this theme, alternating between sections of the orchestra and attempting to incorporate contrapuntal ideas.
