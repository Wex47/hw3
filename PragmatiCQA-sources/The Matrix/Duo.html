Duo was a crew member aboard an unnamed hovercraft, along with Cis and Kaiser . However, he is actually a Program that serves as Cis' foil.

History

Cis and Duo went inside a training simulation that resembles Feudal Japan.

Behind the Scenes

He, along with the rest of the crewmembers appear in the Animatrix short story known as Program . He was voiced by Phil LaMarr .
