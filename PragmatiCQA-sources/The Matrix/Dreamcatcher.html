Dreamcatcher is a 2003 sci-fi film, based on the Stephen King novel of the same name . The Animatrix segment Final Flight of the Osiris was first shown in cinemas with Dreamcatcher , to promote the upcoming release of The Matrix Reloaded . Like the Matrix trilogy, and The Animatrix , Dreamcatcher was produced by Village Roadshow and released by Warner Brothers .

Release

Dreamcatcher premiered on March 21, 2003, eight weeks before the general release of The Matrix Reloaded (May 15, 2003).
