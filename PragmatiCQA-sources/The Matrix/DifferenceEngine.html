DifferenceEngine

Appearances

The Matrix Online

Purpose

  • Protect the Matrix
  • Ensure positive relations between Machine factions and Zion
DifferenceEngine was an Agent who was also one of the former Machinist liaisons, until he was replace by Turring when he was exhibiting some of the symptoms of the Smith virus.

Biography

Seeing the successful results with the new liaison, Agent pace, the machines have decided in producing another successful program to interact with the humans. This program, named Differenceengine, was given the job liaising with the humans so the Agents can concentrate on their own duties (ensure the protection of the matrix world).

Seen in The Matrix Online .

Trivia

The Difference Engine was the name of an early version of the computer, invented by Charles Babbage.
