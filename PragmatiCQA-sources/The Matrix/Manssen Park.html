Manssen Park was a neighborhood in the Westview district. it is located to the left of the district, where it is below Bathary Row and next to Stamos . The residing gang of this neighborhood are the Disciples, a gang of blood drinkers. At night the neighborhood becomes a shadowy nightmare for any bluepill unlucky enough to be caught in the open there. However, due to the gang becoming a threat to the inhabitants by the matrix, the exile bounty hunter known as Greene , has been located in that neighborhood, who specializes in taking down vamps.
