S.W.A.T. (an acronym for Special Weapons and Tactics ) are a force of bluepill humans in the Matrix that assist and serve as enforcers for the Agents .

In The Matrix

A SWAT team assists the Agents in the Hotel Ambush and capturing Morpheus .

The SWAT team in the Hotel Ambush were wearing sold black BDUs , armored gaiters, black leather combat boots, black Kevlar helmets with protective goggles, black balaclavas, and body armor with "POLICE" in white letters on the front.

All the SWAT team officers were armed with Australian-made Leader Dynamics T2 Mk5 "Commando" assault rifles fitted with custom tactical foregrips containing tactical flashlights and laser sights.

In Enter the Matrix

The SWAT team appear as an enemy in Enter the Matrix . They appear the first time in the second level called 'Get to the Hard Line.' In this level, police cars will chase you but when one of them is driving close to you and the passenger is shooting out of the police car you can see it is a SWAT member.

The SWAT team are the police special forces, so they generally have better equipment. As you progress in the story, the SWAT team will bring better weapons and tougher types of SWAT officers to fight against you.

In The Matrix: Path of Neo

In Path of Neo , Neo will encounter the SWAT team multiple times, with the goal of stopping Neo at all costs. Initially, SWAT are only armed with SMGs, but later on helicopters, Riot Shields and Grenade Launchers will be brought to fight against Neo.

Armored SWAT

This type of SWAT officer is better armed and armored, being tougher to kill than regular SWAT officers. They are easily recognizable by their heavy armor, balaclava and earphones. Be wary on using firearms against these enemies, as you'll likely drain a good portion of your ammo; use your melee instead.
