Lamar was a neighborhood in the Downtown district. It includes the Mahath Tower wreckage site and the Exile contact known as The Landlord . The neighborhood was also the location of the Dog Pound gang. During The fall of the General live event, The Merovingian again focused his efforts on assassinating The Oracle , which involved recovering The Keymaker 's chain from the Landlord at the Mahath Tower in Lamar.
