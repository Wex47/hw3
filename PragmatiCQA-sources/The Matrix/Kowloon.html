Kowloon was a neighborhood located next to Shinjuku and above Shirikaba in the International District . The residing contact is the exile known as Mr. Po and local gang is the Chang Wings , a group of exiles that used to be counter-security test protocol programs in the matrix, and are led by Wang Dan .

The neighborhood mainly consisted of tightly packed residential high-rise buildings, narrow streets, and markets that sell various stuff from food to consumer electronics.
