Novalis II is a hovercraft in The Matrix Online , which was said to have been built from the wreckage of the original Novalis , both of which were designated as Pacer Class Hovercraft. the newer hovercraft was numbered 227.

Former crewmember, Toorima, killed her fellow crewmember, Vashuo . She later renamed herself as Veil and joined the Cypherites . Another blow to the crew was when Neurophyte was infected by a kill-code virus which threatened her health. In the pre-merged servers there was a variety of endings, from where she dies or lives, or switching her alliance to the Machines. However, the storyline has now been dropped altogether, because of the confusion.
