The Witch (or Witch Boss ) is a powerful program that has abilities very similar to Neo 's. She appeared in a level of the video game The Matrix: Path of Neo .

Battle with the Witch

The level starts out as avoiding objects hurled by the witch. It is then transported to combat, where the player must use various weapons to defeat the witch. Then Neo is able to leave the Chateau .
