Beirn was a lieutenant in the Unlimit group, who was obsessed with Persephone . He was captured by the faction led by The Merovingian and gave him the information needed against Anome . The Merovingian extracted some cheat codes, using one to return The Twins to their functioning state, and his code enhanced blood for a kill-code made by The Apothecary to destroy The Oracle . He is the only surviving former member and currently works for The Merovingians.
