Mary Alice is an American actress known for playing The Oracle in The Matrix Revolutions , after the previous actor, Gloria Foster died from diabetes. The change of appearance in the movie was explained that the Oracle had to acquire a new shell, because The Merovingian had deletion codes of her previous shell at his disposal.
