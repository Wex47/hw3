The Caduceus was a Zion hovercraft .

After the Crisis Meeting , Ballard offered to help Morpheus and remain behind and wait for contact from The Oracle . While receiving her message, the crew member Bane was attacked by Smith , who overwrote his mind.

During the defense of Zion , Bane/Smith killed the entire crew and used the ship's EMP to disable at least five ships (including itself, the Icarus , the Gnosis , and the Novalis ). Bane was the only survivor and was saved by the Mjolnir .

Name

The name of the Caduceus comes from the Greek word " caduceus ", which is the symbol of two snakes wrapped around a winged staff, originally a symbol of Hermes, messenger of the gods (among other duties). Its confusion with the staff of Asclepius, Greek god of medicine and healing, has led its widespread use as a medical symbol, especially in the United States.
