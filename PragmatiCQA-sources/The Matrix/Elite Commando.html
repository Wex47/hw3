Elite Commandos work for the General and are enhanced version of the normal Commandos , wearing a synthetic black body suits suit with single-lensed thermal goggles. The suits that they wear enable them to avoid detection from standard code scans.

Like the commandos and the general, they were originally the robotic sentinels who rejected the truce between the main Machine group and the Zionites.

The Machines, led by Agent Gray, hacked into the General's network and successfully captured an Elite commando.
