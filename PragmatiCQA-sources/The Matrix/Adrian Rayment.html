Adrian Rayment and his twin brother Neil each played one of The Twins , who were henchmen of The Merovingian in The Matrix Reloaded .

The brothers were carpenters, whom the producers of the Matrix movie trilogy decided to cast, because they fit the profile of being identical twins and capable of martial arts stunts.
