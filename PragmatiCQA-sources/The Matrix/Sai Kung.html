Sai Kung was a neighborhood in the International District . The neighborhood is widely known for its huge textile businesses there. The local contact was The Seamstress while the local gang were the Golds . The Golds are unique compared to the rest of the gangs for they are view favorably by the local residents there. However, this does not mean that they welcome any intrusions from any outsider.

The open air Sai Kung Canton was well known place where redpills can sometimes find hard-to-obtain items, although for steep price.
