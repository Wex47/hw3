Union Hill was a neighborhood located on the north-eastern side of Downtown . It was located next to Baldwin Heights and below Stratford Campus . It was the oldest neighborhood in this district, which originally housed a fort, which has already disappeared. Despite the local gang, the neighborhood had a reputation for being a decent place to live. However, no ambitious worker would want to be caught dead living there, for its addition reputation for being a second rate address.

The residing contact is the Auditor , while the local gang call themselves The Hellions .
