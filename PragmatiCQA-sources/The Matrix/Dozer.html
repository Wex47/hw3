Biography

Assistant Operator

Dozer was first seen reporting to Morpheus on the progress of some medical procedures . The crew of the Nebuchadnezzar were trying to reverse muscle atrophy suffered by Neo following his extraction from a power plant . Part of the process resembled acupuncture but appeared to employ advanced technology. Shortly thereafter, Morpheus formally introduces Neo to Dozer, alongside Dozer's younger brother Tank . In a mealtime scene, Dozer described the composition of the food with which the crew makes do with . He is later credited with brewing the ship's stock of alcohol .

Cypher's Betrayal and Death

In his final appearance, Dozer witnessed his brother being shot by Cypher with a lightning rifle . Dozer wailed with rage and charged the mutineer, only to be shot himself . Dozer's remains are later tended to by Trinity who crudely enshrouds him alongside the three other murdered crew members .
