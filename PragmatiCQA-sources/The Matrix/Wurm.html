Wurm was a crewman of the Gnosis .

When the Zion ship captains meet within the Matrix for the Crisis Meeting , Wurm and his comrade Corrupt are assigned to guard the door to the meeting place.

When Smith arrives "looking for Neo ", Corrupt tells him to 'piss off' after being given an envelope containing his earpiece before Neo came up, asking who it was, surprising Wurm before Neo tells them to warn the captains of the arrival of a trio of Matrix Agents .

The Gnosis later is part of the fleet sent to intercept the invading Sentinels ; it is disabled when Bane sets off the EMP and subsequently destroyed, probably along with its crew.
