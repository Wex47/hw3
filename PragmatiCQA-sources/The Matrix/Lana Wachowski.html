Lana Wachowski (born June 21, 1965) is an American film director, writer and producer, famous for creating the Matrix series together with sister Lilly Wachowski .

Following the success of The Matrix , they went on to write and produce a number of other films, including V for Vendetta (2006), and Cloud Atlas (2012). They also co-created the web TV series Sense8 (2015) but Lana started working separately from Lilly starting the 2nd season of the series with Lana staying.

Lana alone will serve as director, co-writer and producer for The Matrix Resurrections .
