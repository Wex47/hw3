Simulatte is a coffee shop within the Mega City of the Matrix . It appears in The Matrix Resurrections .

Its name is a portmanteau of "simulation" and "latte".

Other meaning could be the fussion of the words "simulation" and "Lete" (river Lethe in italian). In mythology, the dead drank from river Lethe (Oblivion) to forget their lives, before reincarnation. This river was opposite to the river Mnemosyne in the Hades. Also, "latte" and "Lete" have similar pronunciation in italian and other languages.
