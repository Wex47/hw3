The Matrix
Joseph Peter Pantoliano (born September 12, 1951) is an American actor. He portrayed Cypher in The Matrix .

Career

Pre- Matrix his first major role was as the evil Francis Fratelli in The Goonies , followed by a role in Bad Boys . He also starred in the Wachowski movie Bound .

Post- Matrix , Joe played Ralph Cifaretto in The Sopranos as well as Goose the pelican in Racing Stripes .
