The Effectuator was an Exile who served The Merovingian as a specialist in Constructs . Despite working for the Merovingian, he was outside the ranks of the Merovingians .

History

When Niobe was trapped in a construct by the traitorous Anome , the Merovingian offered the assistance of this Exile in good faith. This was likely to cool down tensions between him and the Zionites when it was discovered that he had hired The Assassin that killed one of the Zionites' leaders, Morpheus .

Abilities

The Effectuator is known to be a specialist in the creation of Constructs and the ability of teleportation. Another talent he has is splitting himself into multiple copies of himself, which is why he is nearly unmatchable and unable to be deleted.
