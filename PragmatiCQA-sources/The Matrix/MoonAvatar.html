MoonAvatar was a female redpill who was presumably a member of the Cypherites . She was one of the people that were unknowingly overwritten by the Machines .

Biography

While watching a phenomenon with some of their friends, Seraph, who previously dropped from the General's helicopter into the water appeared from the water. He then attacked MoonAvatar without warning. He placed his foot on her neck and performed some sort of code-exorcism, where it was revealed that she was actually overwritten by the machines. Seraph said to her friends that she is now fine, but she won't remember the past few days.
