Enmascarado was a member of the Cypherites in The Matrix Online , who once resided on the Zionite hoverbarge known as Terra Nova . He was one of the speculated suspects, along with Gemaskeerd , who helped freed the traitor known as Toorima .

In the end Enmascarado was killed by The Assassin , releasing a flame virus attached to his body.
