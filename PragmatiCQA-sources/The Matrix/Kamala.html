Kamala is a program and also an interactive software programmer. She is also the wife of Rama Kandra and mother of Sati .

While in Mobil Avenue train station, Neo meets Kamala's husband Rama Kandra, whom he has a conversation with, along with their daughter as they are to enter the Matrix . A brusque-mannered woman, Kamala demands to know why Neo is here and tells him he was unwelcome to Rama's discomfort, who promptly apologises on Kamala'a behalf, explaining her direct demeanour.

In The Matrix Resurrections , Sati reveals to a resurrected Neo that Rama and Kamala had been purged by the Analyst .
