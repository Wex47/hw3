The Matrix Comics Series 1 is the first collection of webcomics set in the Matrix universe . It was released on the official website through 1999 and includes two short stories, seven comics, and four pin-up pictures. The supposed eighth comic, The Man Who Knew Too Much , was published on the official website but was later removed for unknown reasons.

Pin-ups

Series 1 had four pin-ups:

  1. by Vince Evans of an Agent shooting at a Resistance operative as he tries to exit .
  2. by Geof Darrow of pods from the fetus fields like those seen in Morning Sickness , also used as the cover of The Matrix: Comic Book Preview
  3. by Aron Weisenfeld of Saga Talmer falling to street level for Run, Saga, Run from The Matrix Comics Series 2 , included partly colored in The Matrix: Comic Book Preview
  4. by Michael Kaluta of Fria Canning climbing for System Freeze ; a detail from it was later colored for the short story and included in The Matrix: Comic Book Preview
