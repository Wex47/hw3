This article or section is based on information from the game - The Matrix Online - and the information may contradict situations and events from The Matrix Resurrections .

The Zionites are a group of redpills that appear in the course of gameplay of The Matrix Online . The Zionites uphold the ideals of Zion . A new character featured is Tyndall , who can no longer jack into the Matrix except for constructs.
