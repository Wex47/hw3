Isaiah 54:16 is a passage from the Book of Isaiah , found in the Old Testament of the Bible . It is the number on the license plate of Smith 's car in The Matrix Reloaded .

The passage reads,

"Behold, I have created the smith who blows the fire of coals, and produces a weapon for its purpose. I have also created the ravager to destroy;"

The passage is a reference to Smith, the chief agent tasked with hunting down members of the human Resistance in The Matrix , and who later became "unplugged" after he was destroyed by Neo at the end of that film. In the sequels, The Matrix Reloaded and The Matrix Revolutions Smith was a free agent who was able to copy himself much like a computer virus and spread throughout the Machine City , however, he is once again destroyed by Neo in a final showdown.
