Uriah ( Uriah Industrial Park ) was a neighborhood that was located in the northern Richland . It was labeled a free fire zone after the truce was signed. The zone is full of warehouses, where disputes were often settled with violence without fear of attracting too much attention. The bluepill workers in this neighborhood are used to seeing police each morning, and cleaning up after gang-related violence. The gang that resides here are known as the Choppers , a gang of mostly hardened criminals known and named after their main trade of stealing and disassembling vehicles. However, they are also known to be violent. The exile contact in the area is Mercury .
