  • In the movie "Escape 2 Africa", one of the tourist called her one tough cookie, and she responds: "Brownies, troop 416, Yonkers".
    • In certain cities of America, there are girl scout groups named "Brownie Scouts", which may be an intentional reference. This is also supported by the fact that she excels in camping.
  • Nana seems very attached to her handbag, often using it as a flail to attack foes, and claims that she can't survive without it.
  • In "Christmas Caper" it is revealed that she does her Christmas shopping last-minute with venders on the streets, and despite such methods, she always expects high-quality merchandise.
  • Nana's love for Mr. Chew and enmity with Alex seems to indicate that she is a dog person.
  • Nana's strength and resilience were greatly exaggerated and sometimes inconsistent. An adult human of Nana's age would not be able to accomplish all the feats she is capable of.
    • An adult African Lion in real life similar to Alex/Zuba/Makunga would be able to easily defeat most humans in CQC due to their immense strength, especially considering that Nana does not have any effective weapons.
    • When she fires the rifle at the Super Plane, the recoil pushes her back significantly, despite her physical strength.
