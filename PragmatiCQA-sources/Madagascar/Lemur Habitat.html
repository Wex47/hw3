The Lemur Habitat is home to King Julien and his subjects, Maurice and Mort . This is also Clemson's habitat in the episode Right Hand Man , as well as that of at least two keel-billed toucans in the first Madagascar movie. In Street Smarts , Private becomes the new "cute guy" until Mort is saved. King Julien's throne is located here. It is located to the WEST of the Penguin Habitat . The pedestal used to be in Alex's Habitat , but when Joey took over, the pedestal was moved here and redressed with lemur decorations and a throne for King Julien was placed on top. However, if you look at certain views and angles of the exhibit, then the lemur habitat is Alex's.
