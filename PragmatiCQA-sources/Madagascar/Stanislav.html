Stanislav is a character from All Hail King Julien and All Hail King Julien: Exiled . He was part of the Soviet Union's space program and was sent to space by the Russians. He ended up in Madagascar, an island off the coast of Africa, and got lost there, before meeting King Julien , King of the lemurs, who failed going to the moon, who with the help of Timo , a scientist, who just happens to be a tenrec, helped him return to Russia.

He later appeared in Exiled and explained that the Soviets sent him and his comrades away, as they didn't need the monkeys anymore after the Soviet Union fell and he once again ended up in Madagascar, but was planning to return home and take over the country with his troop.
