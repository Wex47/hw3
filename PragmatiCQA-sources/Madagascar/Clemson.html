Bio

Clemson is a golden bamboo lemur that was shown in Right Hand Man . Even though Clemson appeared innocent at first, he was evil. Clemson's plot was to move up to the kings "right hand man" and then transfer Julien to Hoboken so he could take over as king. However, thanks to Maurice and Mort, they along with the penguins were able to stop him before he succeeded, at which he was transferred to Hoboken instead of Julien.

He appears again in The Hoboken Surprise . In the beginning, he is shown to be very happy at the new and improved Hoboken Zoo. It is later revealed that after Skipper saw his men having a massage, Clemson was captured by Frances Alberta, along with all of the other animals at the Hoboken Zoo, and was replaced by a android duplicate off screen. After escaping in the belly of Savio he participates in the final battle against the robot duplicates. His personality in this episode remains very descriptive and extravagant, though he doesn't appear to be as power hungry.

He appears again in King Me where he tries again to take over as king. The penguins use their brain switching machine to help Julien defeat Clemson. When Julien and Rico switch brains Julien succeeds but when Clemson discovered their brain switching machine he succeeds and Julien switched brains with Private, Kowalski and Skipper he failed so when Clemson tries to get the animals to worship him. Kowalski uses the brain switching machine and switched Juilen and Clemson's brains. Julien in Clemson's body said that Clemson in Juilen's body is king and Juilen in Clemson's body insults the gorillas just before Juilen and Clemson's brains are switched back. When their brains are switched back the gorillas chase Clemson away.

It's currently unknown what happened to him after that but he could have been beaten up by the Gorillas if they catch him.

Personality

Clemson originally appeared to be subordinate, sucking up to Julien in any way he could and often using extravagant flattery to gain his favor. However, he turned out to be manipulative and psychopathic - the title of 'right-hand man' had ulterior motives. He's extremely power-hungry and will go to great lengths to get what he wants, and this is no different for Julien's crown and title.

Whilst he has a way with words, he's also extremely cunning as well as observant. He caught on to the penguins' plans to thwart him and turned it against them, enticing and tricking each of them into events in which they'd be at a disadvantage.

Clemson also has a tendency to talk too much, even to himself; he even addresses this at a point. He is also very exuberant and descriptive ("You clearly have the wizend, which is good, that means wise!","You'll be number one, that's right, number one! Hahahahahah! that mean's king. Hahahahaha!")
