Tammy is a character from All Hail King Julien. She has a son named Todd , and although she loves him, but tends to put a lot of pressure on him throughout the series.

She claims Todd has won tons of awards and contests, even before he was born.

She is constantly showing off her son to the other lemurs, like in the episode Daddy Julien, where she bragged to King Julien about the tournaments and contests Todd had won just that week.

Her husband is Butterfish, and Tammy is frequently annoyed with his laziness and won't hesitate to have an affair with someone else (such as Brodney, who Butterfish did not care about).
