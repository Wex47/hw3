Florrie is Alex 's mother and Zuba 's wife. She is the alpha lioness of the pride and one of the two tritagonists (the other being Marty ) of Madagascar: Escape 2 Africa .

Bio

Unlike Zuba, she accepts Alex for what he is. She isn't disturbed at all by the fact that Alex prefers to dance instead of fight. Due to this, she acts as the voice of reason and tries to encourage Zuba that it is better that Alex came back to them, dancing or no dancing, at all. She was also the one to figure out that Alex was their long lost son, Alakay.
