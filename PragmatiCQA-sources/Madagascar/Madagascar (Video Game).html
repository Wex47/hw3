Madagascar is a video game of the adventure drama epic fantasy genre released in 2005 by Toys for Bob for consoles & Vicarious Visions for the Gameboy Advance & Nintendo DS. The game is based on the animated movie of the same name. Madagascar: Operation Penguin was next to be released on the Game Boy Advance. The animation is not as good as the movie animation.

Story

It is about a group of animals named Alex, Marty, Melman, and Gloria. The game begins on Marty's 10th birthday. They get locked in crates, but they fall in the ocean. They land on Madagascar, a small island in Africa. They work with lemurs to try and escape.
