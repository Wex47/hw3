Chauncey is a character from All Hail King Julien . He acts as Karl 's spy, pet, and implied significant other. Karl bosses him around and rewards his good doing with waste-matter, but it also appears that he has an incredibly strong soft spot for the hissing cockroach, as he is often seen petting him, cooing at him, and even making air kisses at him. Chauncey became jealous when Karl and Mary Ann began working with each other and wanted to get revenge on Mary Ann by tying her up. However, he forgives Karl for betraying him, and rescues him when Mary Ann retaliates.
