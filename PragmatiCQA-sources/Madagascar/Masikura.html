Masikura is a recurring character from the TV series All Hail King Julien and a minor character in its sequel series . She is very religious, and can sometimes come off as cranky or grumpy.

Bio

Masikura is a physic chameleon who appears when someone calls her. She can stick her tongue on one's head and analyze them for predictions or thoughts. Her prophecies can be interpreted in several ways. For example, the prophecy that said "The one who wears the crown tomorrow will get eaten", only meant King Julien would get bit. The prophecy did not say that all of him would get eaten.

She was first seen in the movie but was called 'Masikuro', meaning in the movie, she was originally a male. She was also called Masikuro on the B= (pronounced 'Be equal') 'Madagascar' computer game, as well as the preview of the game on the 'Madagascar' DVD. In the movie, it was seen that she (or rather 'he') writes the records for the lemur meetings and was seen with no spots on her back.

Physical Appearance

She has colorful red, yellow and purple spots on her back, as well as on her tail and legs. She has yellow circles by her temples.
