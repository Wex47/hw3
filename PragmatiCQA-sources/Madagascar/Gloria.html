History

Madagascar: A Little Wild

Gloria appears as a calf in Madagascar: A Little Wild along with Alex , Marty , and Melman , also appearing as young animals.

Madagascar

During Marty 's birthday, Gloria was another animal residing in the Central Park Zoo and she was one of the many attractions, drawing crowds that enjoyed seeing her swim about in her water tank. She would also receive the star treatment as one of the zoo's main attractions and attend Marty’s birthday party. At the party, Marty announced his wish upon blowing out the candles on his cake: to go to the wild. Gloria, Alex and Melman tried to have Marty come to his senses by telling him that life out in the wilds was savage and difficult, whereas life in the zoos was adequate. Marty was unhappy at hearing this and Gloria told Alex to go and console Marty. Later that night, however, Marty acted out his wish and headed straight for the Terminal Station so as to take a train and go to Connecticut, where Marty heard that wide-open spaces were found. Gloria urged both Alex and Melman to come with her so as to find Marty and bring him back before any people would notice. They were found, regardless, alongside Mason and Phil , the two chimps; and a troupe of secretive penguins, all ending up tranquilized with darts.

When they all woke, they found themselves in crates bound for Africa. When the penguins hijack the ship, the crates fall into the ocean and wash up on the island of Madagascar. After some confusion, they start to set up their own society and attract the attention of a lemur colony.

Merry Madagascar

Gloria is happy to help her friends save Christmas, and shows her usual amount of enthusiasm throughout the special, even when helping Melman out of various situations.

It is also shown (perhaps guessed) that she loves children, as when the girl named Abby hugs her friends, and then her, Gloria hugs her back.

Madagascar: Escape 2 Africa

On the flight home, Gloria expresses a desire to join the zoo breeding program, in the hopes of finding a boyfriend. When the animals crash land in Africa, she attracts the attention of a hippo named Moto Moto (meaning "Hot Hot" in Swahili). Though she thinks he is the ideal boyfriend at first, she realizes that he only loves her because she is fat, while Melman loves her for who she is. After Melman tells Moto to treat Gloria right, she realizes that she loves Melman back, and convinces him not to sacrifice himself into the volcano. Just as he is about to fall into the volcano, Gloria grabs him by the horn and says: "It's crazy to think that I had to go halfway around the world to find the perfect guy and he was right next door..." and then joins the other animals in rescuing Alex and Zuba from Nana . At the end of the film, Gloria ends up starting a relationship with Melman (who has found out that he's not dying after all).

Madly Madagascar

It's her first Valentine's Day with Melman and as such she's quite excited but when she wants to spend time with him, he's busy planning a surprise for her. During the day Gloria has many questions on her mind like if Melman is avoiding her and wondering where he is. When she spots him, Gloria thinks he's dumping her on Valentine's Day. But later, she finds his list of things for her surprise and tells him that all she wanted to know is that he was thinking about her.

Madagascar 3: Europe's Most Wanted

After spending a year in Africa, the group decides that they've waited long enough and it's time to go and find the penguins.

When they reach Monte Carlo, the group runs into misfortune when they are spotted in the casino and are pursued by Chantel DuBois from animal control. They manage to escape her but crash the plane in the middle of some railroad tracks on the way. The group ends up hiding with Circus Zaragoza in order to stay out of the way of the law. They find out that this circus, if it puts on a good enough show in London, will earn a contract for an American tour, which means a stop in New York City. They spend the penguins' winnings on the circus.

When the see that the circus is failing since Vitaly lost his confidence, Gloria and her friends immediately begin fine-tuning the show in order to ensure that they get the contract. This includes coming up with new acts for the show. Gloria decides that it would be a blast to do a dancing routine with Melman , only to find that he is unsure of himself in regard to his dancing abilities. It takes a pep-talk and a lesson on top of a tightrope for the couple to find their routine, dancing while on the tightrope! This act is one of several ends up getting the circus the contract. Sadly, they are also exposed as zoo animals and feeling guilty, they leave the circus on the way to New York.

When they finally reach New York City however, the gang finds out that the zoo is not exactly what they remembered. Gloria is especially unhappy to find that there is a big wall between her and Melman's enclosures. Reflecting back on their adventures in Madagascar, Africa, and Europe, the Zoosters realize that they are better off wild and free than left in cages, so they decide to go back to the circus. Gloria and Melman are especially happy to join the show permanently because there's no better life than traveling the world with the one you love.

Appearance

Like every hippo in the franchise, Gloria is fat, her skin is grey, but in the belly, she is a little lighter than the rest of her body. Even as Hippos goes, Gloria is made apparent to be heavier than other female Hippos. Infamously known, in the second movie, are Moto Moto's remarks at Gloria's «plumpiness», «chunkiness» etc. Gloria's upper body is substantially slimmer than her stomach and give her an pear-like shape. Her chest is more prominent than the male hippos, she has an extremely wide and fat stomach, bouncing out and doubling her size. The stomach gives her an enormous diameter around her hefty and chunky hips, giving her unparalleled girth and weight. Further down she has a large, flabby and ski-slope butt with a visible, long crack running down and a lighter tan colour on the top of her giant, round buttocks. She also has a triangular belly button. A running gag is that her huge size and weight causes many problems, such as in the third movie where she stayed stuck in the pipe due to the big range of her hips and when she destroyed the glass ceiling by simply walking on it and she and her friends fell to the casino. Or in the second movie, where she has a seat stuck to her behind and cracked the stone cliff Melman stood on in during the volcano sacrifice. Even in the first movie, the animals fall over the boat only when Gloria’s crate hit them as well, landing in the ocean because of the impact of her weight. She is also very beautiful.
