Crocodile Ambassador is a character from All Hail King Julien and All Hail King Julien: Exiled . He is the leader of the Crocodile Kingdom .

He has a cunning personality, and often tries to work together with other parties for his or his kingdom's advantage. During the War of the Beasts , he made an agreement with King Koto of the Mountain Lemur Kingdom to participate on his side during the war and provide military support, in return of having his daughter, Princess Amy , marry Koto, despite him previously being on the Malagasies' side during the early stages of the war.

He also does advisory and consultancy works in relation to the implementation of etiquettes among the citizens of the island.
