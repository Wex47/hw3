Grandpa Mort was a character in All Hail King Julien . He was a criminal, thrown into jail by King Julien IV. Upon getting out of jail, he helped Mort get into Karl-Con, to get him to become King Julien's arch-nemesis to get his revenge on the Julien Kings. He was killed by Mort during an attempt to absorb his essence.
