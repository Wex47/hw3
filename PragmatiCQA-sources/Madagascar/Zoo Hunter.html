The Zoo Hunter was the guard of Central Park Zoo in Madagascar: The Video Game . According to Ted , the Zoo Hunter runs night time security, and builds a "fortress thingy out of hay bales in front of the main gate". He also carries a heavy duty tranquilizer dart gun. Once the player as Marty kicks the janitor cart beside the hay bale fortress, he jumps over it and taunts Marty before a statue falls down and knocks the Zoo Hunter out. He was also travelling on the Cargo Ship in Penguin Mutiny. He is the boss of two levels: Marty's Escape! and Penguin Mutiny.
