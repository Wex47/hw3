Circus Master is a minor character who appears in Madagascar 3: Europe's Most Wanted . Formerly the owner of Circus Zaragoza , he and the rest of the human performers left after he sold the circus to the King of Versailles, who was really Mason and Phil in disguise. In the eyes of the animals, he was strangely and incredibly pleased to sell Circus Zaragoza, laughing as he and the human performers packed up and left. It wasn’t until the circus’ act in Rome when the zoosters saw the terrible acts the circus provided that they realized the Circus Masted had ripped them off, hence his excitement to sell the circus.
