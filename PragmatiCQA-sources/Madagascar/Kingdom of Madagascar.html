The Lemur Kingdom , also known as "King Julien's Kingdom of Madagascar", is a sovereign kingdom located on the island of Madagascar . The kingdom was in the royal seat of the 13 kings of the Julien Bloodline, it was temporarily ruled by King Koto of The Mountain Lemur Kingdom , as well as King Mort when King Julien The 13th was unavailable, before the position was passed down to King Stevie by King Julien The XIII in the film, Madagascar: Escape 2 Africa . The Lemur Kingdom could be considered The primary kingdom of the Island of Madagascar due to the fact that it is the main location of All Hail King Julien , and Madagascar 01 (in the scenes which were followed after the Zoosters were decided to sent to Kenya), as well as the fact that the Lemur Kingdom has the most importance than the rest of the kingdoms.
