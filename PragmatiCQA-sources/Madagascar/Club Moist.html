Club Moist is a hospital in the Kingdom of Madagascar , located inside a cave, and owned by Doctor S . The hospital was previously a night club, before being converted into a hospital after the events of The Phantom of Club Moist . Since its opening, many inhabitants of the Lemur Kingdom go to Club Moist whenever a medical crisis occurs, as it is the only hospital in the kingdom.
