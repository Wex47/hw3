Hans is a character in All Hail King Julien . He was a successful national athlete of the Kingdom of Madagascar, before his death years before the 64th Every-So-Often Jungle Games.

He was found dead by Ted and Pancho in his house, on his bed, crushed by his own trophies. King Julien wanted him to compete in the Jungle Games against Uncle King Julien . Doctor S then brought him back to life, so he could compete in the games, but he proved useless when he tossed his spear and his arm flew off. He lost because he did not let go of the spear. Julien then left him with Mort. After that, they become close friends.

It is unknown what species of lemur Hans is.
