Todd is a character from All Hail King Julien .

Personality

Todd strives to make his overachieving mother proud because he feels very intimidated by her. He is also known to be demonic as he slips into a robotic/entranced state when he's with his mother, and he'll only snap out of it if she yells his name. Karl attempted to exercise Todd as shown in another episode and Tammy (Todd's mother) says "now the only way to stop him is by stabbing him in the heart with a stake, and even then, you don't have a priest to do the ritual". When he isn't with her, he is a normal, sweet and easygoing boy, making him very popular with the adult lemurs, and many of them were shown at his birthday party.

Skills/habits/traits

Todd is known for being a child prodigy. He is an excellent juggler, even being able to do this while singing well. He is shown often winning competitions and pageants. Todd is also a skilled harmonica player. He also has a scary war face.
