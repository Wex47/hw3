Skipper marries his bobble-head girlfriend at the end of Madagascar 2 . By the time the series starts she is nowhere to be seen, and never mentioned. She is not seen at all in Madagascar 3: Europe's Most Wanted either. By Penguins Of Madagascar: The Movie , Skipper is flirting with a French bobble-head girl. In the series, there's no reference to her existence, ever.

What happened to her is at the moment a mystery. Some people think she may have been left at the hotel in Monte Carlo after the Zoosters' sudden arrival.
