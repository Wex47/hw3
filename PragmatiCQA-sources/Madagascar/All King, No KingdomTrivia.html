Episode based

  • The penguins are playing cards again. The pot in the center is all fish that they are using to bet.
  • Private mentions his Uncle Nigel again.
  • Julien's balloon animals are an earth worm, tape worm, hookworm and flatworm.
  • Julien has named his stuffed animals. The green bear is Janice (who wants to be a dental technician), and the yellow bear is Emile (who thinks that Janice isn't suited for it). The pink bunny is Rosalita. The purple monkey is unnamed.
  • It is hinted that Mort likes to eat glue, much to Maurice's disapproval.
  • Running Gag: King Julien wants to hang out with Skipper , Kowalski , Private and Rico .
  • Bada and Bing are Maurice's body guards again ever since Kingdom Come
  • Marlene did not talk during episode since she made a cameo appearance.
  • Antagonist(s): Maurice and Mort at the beginning and King Julien until he tells Maurice and Mort that they are his friends again.

Foul Ups, Bloops, and Blunders

  • There is a "Bob" mentioned in the end credits, but there is no "Bob" in this episode (although the unnamed purple monkey could be Bob.)
  • If you look very closely, near the end of the episode, the tip of Julien's tail will actually go through the object behind him, for a few seconds.
