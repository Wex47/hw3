Bio

Horst is a character from All Hail King Julien . Not much is known about him. He's kind of a loner, fears change, and is often seen holding a beverage. He will sometimes be the DJ of different events, although he isn't very good at acting "cool" as he does this, and he doesn't take song requests. He is also married to a fossa named Mary Ann , but their marriage didn't last after Mary Ann had mauled him multiple times and thought he was dead. They reunited in Episode 12 of Exiled, The Day After Yesterday and were once again together until Episode 12 of Season 5, The End is Near , where Mary Ann sacrificed herself to save the kingdom. It was not mentioned if they remarried or not.
