Afro Circus/I Like To Move It is a song played in the end credits of Madagascar 3: Europe's Most Wanted , the soundtrack version is performed by Chris Rock and Danny Jacobs . The score version, which was used in the film, is performed by Chris Rock and Sacha Baron Cohen . The instrumental version is also used as the music for the main menu of Madagascar 3: Europe's Most Wanted.

Trivia

  • In the credits of the film rather than being edited to make it shorter, the song is actually extended.
  • In the score version of the song Sacha Baron Cohen's Voice is remixed at certain points to sound robotic.
  • Both versions can be filtered to hear the rest of the characters singing.

Appearances

Madagascar 3: Europe's Most Wanted The score version of the song is played during the end credits

The Penguins of Madagascar (film) The score version of this song serves as a background song and it's played in a scene called the Glorious Future it is when the Penguins decide to leave the circus while Alex , Marty , Melman and Gloria 's shadows can be seen dancing to it, King Julien later appears in an after credits scene
