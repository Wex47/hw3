Alex (born as Alakay ) is the overall main protagonist in the Madagascar franchise. He is an African lion who, for a short time, was raised in an African wildlife reserve, before being captured by poachers, and eventually ended up falling out of the poachers' truck into the ocean, and floating to New York City, where he was sent to the Central Park Zoo, becoming one of the most popular exhibits in the zoo. He is also one of the protagonists in Madagascar: A Little Wild , and appears in a single special episode of The Penguins of Madagascar , as well as having a brief cameo in the finale of All Hail King Julien .

Biography

Alex was the self-proclaimed king of the New York City Central Park Zoo . As a cub, Alex was born on an African wildlife reserve and named Alakay. He was the son of the alpha lion, Zuba , and was constantly given lessons on hunting, although he displayed no interest in the activity. He would instead dance around rather than pounce on the toy his father used to train him. During one of the sessions, Alex was captured by poachers and in an attempt to save his son, Zuba chases the vehicle, damaging its safety harness. Zuba was soon shot in the ear and briefly delayed. As the truck turned, the crate Alex was contained in fell into a river, and began floating away. Zuba failed to retrieve him, believing that he was still captured by poachers. He eventually gave up and presumed Alex dead. Alex arrives in New York after floating over the Atlantic Ocean in his transport crate. He was later brought to the Central Park Zoo and proved to be very popular with the public. Due to spending most of his life at a Zoo, Alex didn’t remember his life in Africa or his birth name.

Madagascar: A Little Wild

in A Little Wild Alex appears as a cub along with Gloria , Marty and Melman . He also has a love for acting and is shown feeling excited when he was about to go on Broadway in order for Gloria to become a ballerina dancer. He's a big fan of a animal actor named Harley Hooves .

Madagascar

As an adult, he was first seen emerging from the bush, seeing Marty, he goes after him. He appears right in front of Marty, it was just a dream. Alex gives Marty a birthday present. After Marty leaves the zoo in an effort to take a train to the wild, Alex, Gloria and Melman pursue him and were all shot with tranquilizer darts by humans when they caught up to him at Grand Central Station. Animal-rights activists pressure the zoo into releasing them back into the wild, but on the ship back to the reserves located in Kenya, Alex and his friends fall into the ocean when the Penguins take over the ship. They eventually wash ashore on the beaches of Madagascar, and while Marty is excited to be free, Alex wants to go home, however, he later changes his mind. He grows very hungry after days of being unable to eat, not realizing that steak is animal flesh, and starts to regain his hunting instincts. Hunger drives Alex into a crazed state, which causes him to hallucinate everyone as steak and tries to eat Marty. After realizing what he did to Marty, Alex barricades himself in the predator area of the island. Marty pursues him, unwilling to leave his friend, but is attacked by the fossas, the native predators of Madagascar. After overcoming his hunting instincts, Alex works to defend Marty, and is later given sushi by the Penguins to satisfy his hunger.

Before Alex (whose birth name is revealed to be Alakay) encounters his parents, Zuba and Florrie who were overjoyed to see their son, he re-encounters Nana the old lady, and starts fighting but intentionally allows Nana to win so he could acquire her purse and use her cell phone to search for help. Makunga , Zuba's rival, persuades Alex to challenge Teetsi during Alex's rite of passage. Teetsi was revealed to be a bulky and strong lion and Alex quickly loses. With the help of the others, the dam was destroyed and they safely returned to the reserve. His father learns to respect his habits, and they decided to stay in Africa instead of flying home with the penguins.

Appearance

Alex is a brown and yellow lion with a glossy mane, which is usually shaped like a pentagon in most viewpoints, like the back or front, with a portion over his eyes extending outward. On certain angles, his mane looks more hexagonal because of this. Since the second film, he's had an Africa-shaped birthmark on his right hand. He also has blue eyes, just like his mother's. In most of the franchise, it is said that Alex has a soft fur and a silky mane.

His color varies as the animation progresses, in the first film his fur was darker and in the third film his fur is lighter.

Personality

Alex has the personality very similar to that of a celebrity because he became the star of the zoo at a very young age.

Alex loves the zoo and was surprised when he heard Marty's wish to leave and live in the wild. As the main attraction of the zoo, he is featured heavily on merchandise and gives an "Alex the Lion" snow globe and other merchandise to Marty for his birthday. He also shows an infatuation with steak, though later remarks that sushi tastes better.

In the first film, he was very scared when he discovered his predatory nature and did his best to isolate himself from others to protect them. Despite his pride and selfishness, Alex is very loyal to his other Zooster friends, especially Marty. Alex never loses the opportunity to show off and boast that he is a star, but sometimes some situations make him nervous, like when he tries to deal with the penguins or his attempts to flirt with Gia in the circus. He is also determined to fix things when he knows he spoiled them, like when he fights with Marty, or his attempt to re-fit into the lion's pride or to convince the Zoosters to return to the circus and apologize to everyone.

Alex is also very energetic, he is able to raise morale to everyone around him and also has good leadership skills, especially in the third film, when he even manages to lead a complete circus.

Florrie

Florrie loves her son, and she also felt devastated when she lost him, believing that the hunters had killed him. She was the first to recognize him when he saw him again, she said it was him when she saw the birthmark but she could actually recognize it when she saw his eyes.

Florrie has a really motherly character, she accepts her son as he is, even if he decides to dance and not to fight. It proved to be very encouraging when her son decided to do something, like when they introduced him to the pride, she was excited when they named him "King", or when Alex decided to do the rite and Zuba had to choose who would start first, but Florrie was too excited to encourage him, preventing Zuba from concentrating. She and Zuba still remember Alex's habits when he was young, even she let him keep his "foofie” even if Zuba did not want to, letting him be himself.

She has loving habits like kissing him in the forehead when he said "good night", showing that she really loves him. She is also overprotective and is often terrified of losing him again, as when Zuba told her he was not a real lion, she scolded him and told her not to lose him once again. She was worried when Makunga told them that he left the reserve, but he was relieved when he saw them both (Zuba and Alex) safe and sound. She and Zuba are still calling him by his birth name, "Alakay."

Powers and Abilities

Incredible Strength

Alex seems to be fairly strong, but the fact that Alex loses the challenge in Madagascar: Escape 2 Africa , and is kicked in the crotch by Nana in Madagascar gives the impression that he is weak; although this is not true, because he reveals right after that he let her win in order to get her purse.

Indeed, even for a lion, Alex is shown to be unusually strong, such as when he broke the zoo's public telephone, and lifted Melman , Marty and Gloria (a being whose weight is more than enough to break apart a thick concrete wall) all at once almost effortlessly. He also defeated many fossa almost single-handedly.

However, he lacks hunting and fighting skills due to his tamed upbringing in the zoo.

Great Agility

Alex is also very agile, running on four legs and able to reach a moving jeep. (Though weighing an unusual 250 kg or 550 lbs, according to DuBois). He is also good at jumping as he took a great leap on the moving train, making him an incredible trapeze artist. Alex and Gia could be considered the most agile animals in the circus, both were able to take a great turn together from the trapeze to the flying platform of the circus on the Zoo. Alex's agility may be due to his fascination with dancing since he was young, which is demonstrated when he danced with his father in Africa.
