Biography

Bada is a grey gorilla. He lives with Bing in the Gorilla Habitat inside the Zoo . He is strong, agile, and seems to be smarter than Bing. He served as a guard for Maurice in Kingdom Come and King Julien on multiple occasions. He doesn't like sharing anything, especially air space and bananas.
