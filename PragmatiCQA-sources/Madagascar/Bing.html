Bio

Bing is a gorilla. He serves as a guard for Maurice in Kingdom Come and sometimes King Julien . He is also a character in The Lunacorns which he is a fan of. He doesn't like sharing. He and Bada like stealing as shown in Jungle Law and The Big Move .
