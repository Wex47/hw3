Episode based

  • This is the third time that Mort gets caught in a penguin's mouth ( Rico ), the first time was All Choked Up (Rico), the second The Helmet ( Kowalski ).
  • Kowalski indirectly reveals he believes Skipper is an idiot and he should be their leader when rationalizing Maurice 's revolution with a graph of "intelligence" vs. "frustration".
  • This is the second time (since Two Feet High and Rising ) in the series that King Julien allows Mort to touch his feet.
  • Julien briefly mentions Carlotta (the name he gave Rico's Doll Ms. Perky when he briefly "owned" her in Penguiner Takes All ) in his dreams.
  • Kowalski also said that Maurice is a intelligent lemur while Julien is not.
  • When Julien is doing pull-ups on the tree he briefly says me-eleven ( Lemur See, Lemur Do ).
  • King Julien Day from Happy King Julien Day! is mentioned.
  • Julianuary is mentioned again in the series since April Fools and Merry Madagascar .
