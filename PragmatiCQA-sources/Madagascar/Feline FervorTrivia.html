Episode based

  • There is a possible Portal 2 reference in this episode, because the old woman's name is Gladys which sounds like the games antagonist GLaDOS, and Zoe said " I want you gone." And "Want you gone" is the name of one of the games songs.
  • Private gets picked last, when he sees a toy like Julien and gets Julien back to the zoo he says "And you picked me last."
  • The penguins still have the weapon from Maurice at Peace and Field Tripped .
  • Second appearance of Gladys, the first was Gut Instinct .
  • First appearance of Zoe .
  • When determining who gets to lead the aerial unit to find King Julien, Kowalski and Skipper play a game called "Leopard Seal, Orca, Great White" which is probably the penguin's version of the game "Rock, Paper, Scissors."
  • Julien purrs for the first time (ring-tailed lemurs are capable of purring).
  • There is a running gag in which twice, Julien, from the point Zoe turns off the radio to when Julien leaves, he tries to kiss her, but fails and falls over or is interrupted by something disturbing that he cannot bear to leave unhandled.
