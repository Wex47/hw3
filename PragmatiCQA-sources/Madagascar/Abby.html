First Appearance

Merry Madagascar

Voiced by

Willow Smith

Alex , Marty , Melman , and Gloria crash into her house while filling in for Santa in Merry Madagascar . This surprises Abby when she sees this, and she thinks of them as Santa's hairy helpers. Making herself confident around them, she hugs Alex, Marty, Melman and Gloria (the last of whom hugs her back). She then blows dust off a plate of four cookies and gives it to them. She hugs Alex again, and tells him not to tell Santa she was up. Abby then goes back to bed, despite thinking she can't sleep from meeting "Santa's hairy helpers." She starts falling back down the stairs (much to the Zoosters' shock), thinking she broke her collarbone. It turns out she didn't, and then she actually goes back to bed this time.
