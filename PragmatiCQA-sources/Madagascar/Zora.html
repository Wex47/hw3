Zora is a character from All Hail King Julien : Exiled. She fell in love with Mort after he seduced her into joining the good side, and the two got married after the war ended.

Soon enough, Zora began to realize Mort wasn't really interested in her and fell in love with Uncle King Julien after he expressed his feelings for her in love notes he wrote in prison. Zora escaped prison and joined Uncle King Julien in starting their new life in Feartopia. At first, Mort seemed distraught that he lost Zora forever, but he later laughed, glad he no longer had to deal with her.
