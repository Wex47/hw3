Manu and Maya (pronounced:Man-Yoo) are performing elephants from Circus Zaragoza in Madagascar 3: Europe's Most Wanted .

Bio

Before the Zoosters came, the elephants used to barely balance on rubber balls (Manu got a kid stuck in his bottom). Their new act is swinging on silk ropes blowing beautiful colorful fire out of their trunks. They are painted like circus clowns. They used their act to save the Zoosters from animal control by bashing into them. In the end, they are seen behind Marty wearing rainbow afros.

Trivia

  • The DVD Commentary reveals their names and that they are German, but their dialogue all got cut in the final -meaning they do not speak in the movie- and they're very nice and great to work with.
