Rhonda is a walrus who works for Dr. Blowhole . She appeared in the episode Roomies . She is Agent 12 and was sent by Dr. Blowhole on a class F freighter to get Kowalski 's plasma cutter. She was Marlene 's disgusting roommate (who was secretly a spy), that was seen farting, blowing mucus out of her nose without a tissue and singing "Take me out to the ballgame" which only bothered Marlene. She thought Marlene was a rat at first and called her rat face.

She was sent from the Hoboken Aquarium to live with Marlene. She weighs 1500 lbs and is very giant, annoying, disgusting and obnoxious. At the end of the episode she is seen with Kowalski's plasma cutter heading for the Hoboken aquarium, in NJ possibly going to meet Dr. Blowhole.

She also appears in The Hoboken Surprise . Where she, along with the Other Hoboken Zoo Animals, were happy at the Hoboken Zoo, and was excited to see the Elitiest of the Elite (the Penguins). after Skipper saw his men having a massage, Rhonda was imprisoned by Francis Alberta along with all of the other animals at the zoo, and was replaced by a android duplicate off screen, She and the other Hoboken Zoo Inhabinates later helps the Penguins escape by launching Savio, and all of the animals in his belly, out of the hole created by Skipper. She somehow escapes herself and participates in the battle against the android duplicates. It is unknown if she is still a spy for Blowhole, as after The Hoboken Surprise she was never seen again.

Personality

Rhonda is rude and obnoxious. Even Marlene, who normally is able to see the good in people, was repulsed by her behavior. She is extremely nasty and has an overall bad attitude. She farts everywhere without manners and uses Marlene as a tissue at times.
