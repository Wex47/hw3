Mom-Bot is a character in All Hail King Julien: Exiled . She is a robot who was made by Timo to act as his mother and security system.

However, she went haywire and almost killed Timo and Mort. But, Mort shut her down, and then Timo fixed her. She also helped Smart Mort exit Mort's mind to help Timo and the Mort horde to come back to the kingdom to help King Julien.

Appearance

Mom-Bot is a bot that has the same shape as the Frank Bot made by Karl . She has yellow parts instead of red or blue, and also has glasses that are pink and a grey wig.
