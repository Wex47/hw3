Biography and Personality

Barry is an adorable Poison Dart Frog - however his touch can cause total paralysis. He is a transfer from the Hoboken Zoo , and he uses his poisonous touch to terrify other animals, and boss them around into doing whatever he wants. All he has to do is lift one finger as a threat. He is not the "hugging type" but he became nicer after Private shows him affection through a contactless hug.

He appears in The Big Squeeze , but has no speaking role. Ironically if he had got out, he could have stopped Savio cold in his tracks.

He had a small part in Byte-Sized , where he got his tongue stuck on the cotton candy machine, and was saved by the penguins.

He had a very small cameo in Thumb Drive and again he has no speaking role.

Barry will appear again in the episode Best Foes , where he is seen to infect all the penguins after they accidentally attack him.
