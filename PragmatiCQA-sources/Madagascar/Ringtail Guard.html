The Ringtail Guard is the royal guard to the royal family of the Kingdom of Madagascar , currently led by Captain Theodore Pantskin as the Captain.

It was stated in the first episode of All Hail King Julien ( King Me ) that there used to be more members of the guard, but they never returned after they ran away from the Foosa . At the end of the episode, King Julien , seeing Clover's bravery, nominated her as Captain. She kept her position and protected the kingdom well all throughout the series, until the end of Season 5 (The End is Near + The End is Here) when she married Sage left to become Queen of the Mountain Lemur Kingdom- leaving Ted in charge.
