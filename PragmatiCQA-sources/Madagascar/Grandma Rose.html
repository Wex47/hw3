Grandma Rose was the grandmother of Crimson and Clover . Her husband was Grandpa Rose , whom she married after eating Maxine, Grandpa Rose 's ex-girlfriend. She was known to have trained her granddaughters in wrestling and fighting but was tragically killed in a back-alley brawl with a murderous wildebeest. She has also been known to have killed King Julien II while in his wild "Night Creature" state, due to his allergy to gecko milk that turned him into a monster when he drank it. This same condition was also shown later along the Julien bloodline, as it went to King Julien the 13th. Her daughter-in-law, Clover's mother, forbade Clover from wrestling practice, but Rose took her to wrestling practice anyway.
