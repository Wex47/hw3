The Butterfly Queen is a character from All Hail King Julien. She was the main antagonist of the episode The Butterfly War when she led all her butterfly colony to declare war on King Julien . Julien tries to make peace with the butterflies, as and gives the queen a contract and a flower as a gift. The queen takes the contract and tears it in half, declaring to take over Madagascar. However, the flower eats her, killing her instantly. As a result, the other butterflies cheer for her demise and thank Julien. She later appears as a ghost on All Hail King Julien: Exiled in a couple episodes with the other villains King Julien 'killed'.
