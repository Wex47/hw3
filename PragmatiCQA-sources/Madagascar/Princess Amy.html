Princess Amy is a character from All Hail King Julien and All Hail King Julien: Exiled . She is the daughter of the Crocodile Ambassador . Unlike her polite and warm-hearted father, Amy is rude, disrespectful, whiny and obnoxious to everyone, including her father. She speaks in stereotypical teen talk and uses the word “literally” improperly. [[Category:Characters]
