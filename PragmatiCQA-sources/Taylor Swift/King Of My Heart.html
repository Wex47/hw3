" King of My Heart " is the tenth track from Taylor Swift 's sixth studio album, reputation . It was released on November 10, 2017, through Big Machine Records .

This song is speculated to be about Taylor's current boyfriend, Joe Alwyn .

Background

For her series on DirecTV, Taylor Swift NOW, she released a video of her developing this song with Max Martin and Johan Shellback .

Live performances

This song was performed as a part of the Reputation Stadium Tour setlist. An HD version was recorded in Arlington, Texas for the Netflix special, Taylor Swift’s Reputation Stadium Tour .

In February 2019, Taylor performed the first acoustic version of this song for her friends Alexander Goldshmidt and Ross Girard’s engagement party.
