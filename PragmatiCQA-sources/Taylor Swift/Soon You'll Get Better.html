Background

Taylor wrote this song for her mother, Andrea Swift , who has been struggling with cancer since 2015. She was diagnosed in April of 2015, and was in remission until she was re-diagnosed in March 2019.

During Taylor’s YouTube livestream Lover’s Lounge on August 22, Taylor stated that this sng was the hardest song to write on the album.

“ There’s a song called “Soon You’ll Get Better” on Lover that was really really hard to write, and it was also a family decision whether to even put it on the album. And I think songs like that that are really hard for you to write emotionally, maybe they are really hard to write and hard to sing because they are really true. ”
