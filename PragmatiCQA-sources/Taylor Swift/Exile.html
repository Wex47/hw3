" exile " is the fourth track from Taylor Swift 's eighth studio album, folklore ; it features Bon Iver. It was released on July 24, 2020, through Republic Records . It is the second single from the album that was be sent to radio stations on August 3, 2020.

This song was confirmed on Taylor’s social media accounts on July 23, 2020.

Awards and nominations

"exile" received one nomination at the 2021 Grammys for best pop/duo performance, but lost to "Rain on Me" by Lady Gaga and Ariana Grande.

Background

In the Folklore: The Long Pond Studio Sessions it's revealed that Taylor's boyfriend, Joe Alwyn , had written the entire piano part of the song. Taylor heard Joe singing the entire first verse and asked him if they could keep writing on it. Because of Joe's low voice and how well it fit the song, they decided that it needed to be a duet with someone who has a low register. They asked Bon Iver as they are both big fans of him. Justin Vernon, lead singer of Bon Iver, was inspired by the song and wrote the bridge.
