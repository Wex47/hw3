" Marjorie " (stylized as " marjorie ") is the thirteenth track from Taylor Swift 's ninth studio album , evermore . It was released on December 11, 2020, through Republic Records .

Taylor's grandmother is named Marjorie Finlay , giving this song it's name. The song tells about Marjorie's morals and her impact on Taylor's life.
