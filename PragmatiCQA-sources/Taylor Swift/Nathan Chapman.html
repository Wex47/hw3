Nathan Chapman is an American record producer who works in the field of country music. He is known primarily for working with Taylor Swift , having produced her albums Taylor Swift , Fearless , Speak Now , Red and 1989 . The former was also the first album that he produced. He is a 2001 graduate of Lee University.

At the 52nd Grammy Awards in 2010, Chapman won the Grammy Award for Album of the Year. Other acts for whom Chapman has produced include Jypsi, Jimmy Wayne and The Band Perry. In addition to his production work, Chapman is a session musician who plays guitar, banjo and mandolin.
