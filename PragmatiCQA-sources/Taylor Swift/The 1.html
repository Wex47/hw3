" the 1 " is the first track from Taylor Swift 's eighth studio album, folklore . It was released on July 24, 2020, through Republic Records . It was released as the fourth single on October 9, 2020, it was only sent to German radio.

This song was confirmed on Taylor’s social media accounts on July 23, 2020.

Explanation

Taylor describes this song as informing a former lover on your new life, but also says it has a duel meaning of where she was in her life during its writing.
