Billboard awards

Taylor Swift has been nominated and won numerous awards at the Billboard awards . Due to the amount of wins she had, she had the most wins of any gender until May 2nd, 2019 when Drake surpassed her. This bumped her down to being the female artist with the most Billboard wins.

Award Process

“

"Unique among awards shows, Billboard Music Awards (BBMA) finalists are determined by performance on the Billboard charts, and are not chosen by a voting committee or membership organization. BBMA finalists and winners are based on key fan interactions with music (including album and digital song sales, streaming, radio airplay, social engagement), tracked by Billboard and its data partners, including MRC Data. - Billboard, 2021 BBMAs

”

Unique to the award show itself, it is based off merit from their charts in comparison awards like the Grammy Awards , where a panel of secretive music members vote. It is not focused on charts outside of the organization itself. Some awards are based off a single chart, as in the case of the Top Hot 100 Artist, while others are a culmination of their performances across multiple charts, as in Top Artist and the exclusive Artist of the Decade.
