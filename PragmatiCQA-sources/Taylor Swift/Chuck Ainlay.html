Chuck Ainlay is a record producer. He worked with Taylor Swift as an engineer on the Speak Now Karaoke album. He has worked with Mark Knopfler on much of Knopfler's solo work, in addition to some Dire Straits material.

Along with Knopfler and Bob Ludwig, in 2006 he collected the Grammy Award for Best Surround Sound Album for the surround production of the famous Dire Straits album Brothers in Arms .
