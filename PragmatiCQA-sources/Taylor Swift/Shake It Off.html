" Shake It Off " is the sixth track from Taylor Swift 's fifth studio album, 1989 . It was announced as the first single from the album on her Yahoo livestream for 1989 , on August 18, 2014, when it premiered.

The song was written by Taylor Swift along with Max Martin and Shellback .

Background

The songs lyrics are in response to Swift's haters. Swift said on her official Youtube channel that, "I've learned a pretty tough lesson that people can say whatever they want about us at any time, and we cannot control that. The only thing we can control is our reaction to that."

During a Rolling Stone interview Swift said "I've had every part of my life dissected — my choices, my actions, my words, my body, my style, my music. When you live your life under that kind of scrutiny, you can either let it break you, or you can get really good at dodging punches. And when one lands, you know how to deal with it. And I guess the way that I deal with it is to shake it off."

Trivia

  • Taylor Swift recorded this song to ignore rumors, such as ones she had mentioned in the song's lyrics.
  • Many people have misheard lyrics from this song, instead of "fakers gonna fake," people thought she sang, "bakers gonna bake."

Music video

The music video for "Shake It Off" has over 3 billion views, and features Taylor looking out of place during different types of dance performances. The moral of the video is to show that everyone should be happy in their own individual ways, and embrace their differences.

On August 18, 2014, the music video was uploaded to Taylor Swift's official YouTube channel. It is now available for digital download.

Summary

The video opens up with ballerinas warming up on a beam, and Taylor seen out of place from the professionals. She is next seen with a group of breakdancers, posing with a boombox. The following scene is during a ballet performance, where in comparison to the professionals, Taylor is just having fun dancing. Taylor after is in a group with modern dancers. She looks extremely confused during the scene. She then messes around when dancing with animators, a type of dancer. She tries to finger tut with the professionals, but cannot figure out how. She is then in a group of twerkers, here she is seen wearing a snake necklace, which is interesting, because in 2016, Taylor was called out as a snake, inspiring her to write her sixth studio album, reputation . After this, she is singing with a band, having a great time. During the bridge, Taylor is a cheerleader, being thrown in a basket toss in the air, and not being as coordinated as the others. The end of the video is a combination of the dancers from each scene and fans that Taylor invited to be a part of the music video. Everyone is dancing and having a great time, for a perfect ending!

Hidden message

Hidden message: She danced to forget him.

Following the hidden message storyline of the album, we find out that the breakup of the story's relationship was hard to move past for the girl, as this hidden message says, she danced to forget him, trying to take her mind off of him. This song also ties into the message due to the theme of dancing through your troubles.
