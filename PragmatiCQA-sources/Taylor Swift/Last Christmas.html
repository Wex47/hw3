" Last Christmas " is a cover song recorded by Taylor Swift as a part of her extended play Sounds of the Season: The Taylor Swift Holiday Collection , released on October 14, 2007.

This song was written by George Michael from the band Wham! as a part of their album Music from the Edge of Heaven , released in 1986.
