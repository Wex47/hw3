American Music Awards

The American Music Awards (better known as the AMAs) is an award show focused in the United States that covers music. Unlike other major music awards, nearly all of its awards are voted by the fans, with some helping off Billboard's charts . Taylor Swift has been been the artist with the most awards there since 2019 when she surpassed Michael Jackson, taking home 6 more awards, and now has 32.

Award Process

“

"Fans will have the opportunity to vote and select the winners of the following awards which will be given at or in connection with the 2021 American Music Awards ® ceremony (“Program”) taking place on November 21, 2021, and broadcast live on ABC at 8:00 p.m. Eastern Time (“ET”) / 7:00 p.m. Central Time (“CT”) - AMAs, Voting Rules, 2021

”

The process in which the nominees are chosen is very simple, being similar to Billboard's awards, with performance on Billboard's charts being the main factor. After that, fans vote amongst themselves. For the first time ever in 2021, fans could vote via TikTok, where you would be searching AMAs then the artist you wished to win. Another route was just going on the website and voting there.
