The Secret Sessions are events that take place before album releases where fans that are hand picked by Taylor Swift go to her houses or hotels and listen to the album with her. The sessions have been taking place ever since the release of Taylor’s fifth studio album, 1989 . Fans get to talk with Taylor and eat baked goods that Taylor makes. During the night, fans can take a few photos with Taylor of their choosing. Some fans have held Grammys or even her cats.

The Lover secret sessions began in London on August 2nd, 2019.
