" The Way I Loved You " is a song written by Taylor Swift . It is the tenth track off of her second studio album, Fearless . This song discusses Taylor's internal struggle between having the ideal relationship, but missing the excitement and chaos that came with her past relationship, full of arguments and discrepancies.

Hidden message

Hidden message : We can't go back

Taylor realized that the relationship she had was too perfect, and she needed something more. She says that they can never go back to their relationship that they once had.
