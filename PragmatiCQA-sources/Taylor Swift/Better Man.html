Music video

"Better Man" is a song written by Taylor Swift . She pitched the song to Little Big Town , who recorded it and put it on their eighth studio album, The Breaker . A demo of the song recorded in 2011-2012 leaked on October 13, 2021. Taylor recorded the song herself in 2021 and released it as the 22nd track on .

Performances

Taylor has performed Better Man as a surprise song during the Reputation Stadium Tour on August 25, 2018, in Nashville, Tennessee. She also performed it during her surprise set at The Bluebird Cafe in 2018.
