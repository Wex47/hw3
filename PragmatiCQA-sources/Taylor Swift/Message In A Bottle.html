" Message In A Bottle " is the 25th track off of , released on November 12, 2021. It was announced on August 5, 2021 as a part of a word scramble for the different From the Vault songs to be released on the album. Fans have made connections between this song and " Come Back...Be Here ". The song was later released to U.S. pop and adult pop radio stations at an unknown date as the 2nd single from the re-recorded album
