Demetria Devonne "Demi" Lovato (born August 20, 1992) is an American singer-songwriter, musician, and actress. They were also a judge on the American version of the British TV talent show The X-Factor from 2012 to 2013.

They used to be friends with Taylor Swift .

Feud

Demi and Taylor began having drama after Taylor started to become closer with Selena Gomez , who used to be Demi's best friend.

In 2019 drama arose again after Taylor’s controversy with Scooter Braun . They were in defense of Braun, saying he is a good person, only having been signed to him for a month. On Twitter, they argued about it with Todrick Hall , who before walking away, had been signed to Braun for years. Demi later deleted the tweet and took a break from social media.

Reconciliation

On September 2nd, 2019, Demi put an end to their feud with Taylor a week after the release of Lover , writing "Life is too short for women not to support other women, especially when women release great music. Great job Taylor Swift."

They then posted a screenshot of them playing Cruel Summer on their phone, calling it a jam. Taylor responded to this by saying "this is so awesome and put the biggest smile on my face," adding, "thank you Demi Lovato".
