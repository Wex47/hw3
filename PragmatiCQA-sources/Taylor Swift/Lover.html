Background, recording and release

On Jimmy Fallon, Taylor stated that she toyed around with naming the album Daylight , before she wrote " Lover ". On her Instagram live on June 13, 2019, Taylor announced that the album would be called Lover and that the second single, " You Need to Calm Down " would be released on June 14, 2019 at midnight EST. There are four deluxe editions of the album, which are available at Target. The deluxe editions include voice memos of two of her favorite songs from the album, " I Forgot That You Existed " and " Lover ".

Swift talked about the album at the 2018 American Music Awards: "I always look at albums as chapters in my life," she said:

“ And to the fans, I'm so happy that you like this one... But I have to be really honest with you about something: I'm even more excited about the next chapter. ”

She also stated at the iHeartRadio Music Awards:

“ I just wanted to let you know, when there is new music, you will be the first to know. ”

On February 24, 2019, Taylor posted a photo of seven palm trees on her Instagram, which, in her Entertainment Weekly interview, she confirmed was posted on the date that she finished the album.

Lover debuted at number one on the Billboard 200 with 867,000 units, of which 679,000 are pure sales, making Swift the first female artist to have six albums sell more than 500,000 copies in a single week. The album sold more copies than all the other 199 albums on the chart combined that week, the first album to achieve this feat since her own reputation in 2017. All 18 tracks from the album charted simultaneously on the Billboard Hot 100 breaking records such as the most simultaneous Hot 100 entries for a female artist and the most simultaneous Hot 100 debuts by a female artist. Lover was certified platinum by the RIAA, denoting one million equivalent album units earned.

On September 17, 2019, Swift announced Lover Fest , her sixth worldwide concert tour, in support of the album. The tour consisted of 3 legs and 17 shows. In the wake of the Coronavirus pandemic, the tour was rescheduled and later cancelled.

The album was released on vinyl on November 15, 2019.

Singles

  • The first single off of Lover is titled " ME! ". It was released on April 26, 2019, featuring Brendon Urie of the band Panic! At The Disco.
  • The second single off of Lover is titled " You Need To Calm Down ". It was released on June 14, 2019.
  • The third single off Lover is titled " Lover " and was released on August 16, 2019. This was confirmed on Taylor’s Instagram live when she announced the album name, and the release date was confirmed on the Teen Choice Awards 2019.
  • The fourth single off Lover is titled " The Man ". It was released on January 28, 2020.
