" The Very First Night " is the 29th track off of , released on November 12th, 2021. It was announced on August 5, 2021 as a part of a word scramble for the different From the Vault songs to be released on the album.

Background

Although Swift has so far not addressed any of the rumors, many believe it is about either Jake Gyllenhaal or her ex after, Harry Styles . The evidence, according to Jason Pham of Style Caster for "Haylor" was "We were built to fall apart," which is echoed in Out of the Woods . As noted by critics, it also is a prequel to 1989 with the catchy, poppy songs that Max Martin had made for it, where Styles is mentioned many times, most explicitly in Style . As noted before, it could also be about Jake Gyllenhaal, as most of the songs beside that on Red are based off him. It draws parallels to All Too Well with the lyric "Dance in the kitchen, chase me down through the hallway", as she states in All Too Well "We're dancing 'round the kitchen in the refrigerator light."

Critical reception

The Very First Night received generally upbeat and positive reviews, as mostly a passing mention with the likes of All Too Well . Bobby Olivier of Spin called it along with Message In A Bottle " bygone-era sugar bops". This would be a repeated sentiment throughout reviews, with two songs being explicitly tied. This is shown in Paul Bridgewater of Line of Best Fit review, where he said "“Message in Bottle” and “The First Night” are A-grade Swift and would fit easily on Red’s follow-up 1989 ". A staff member of Clash reviewed it by simply saying " Equally poppy is ‘The Very First Night’".
