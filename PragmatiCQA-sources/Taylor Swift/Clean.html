" Clean " is the thirteenth and last track from Taylor Swift 's fifth studio album, 1989 . It is a synth-folk, soft rock, indietronica and ambient electronica song written by Swift and Imogen Heap .

When she performed this during The 1989 World Tour , she said a speech before performing the song, about being genuine and happy with yourself.

Hidden message

Hidden message : She lost him but she found herself and somehow that was everything.

Taylor's hidden message story has a kind ending with a good moral. She showed that she only needed herself to find true happiness. She was able to find who she truly wanted to become and be satisfied.

This message was also featured in the Out of the Woods music video.
