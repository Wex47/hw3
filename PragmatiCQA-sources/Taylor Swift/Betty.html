Music video

" Betty " (stylized as " betty ") is the fourteenth track from Taylor Swift 's eighth studio album, folklore . It was released on July 24, 2020, through Republic Records . It was released as the third single on August 17, 2020.

This song was confirmed on Taylor’s social media accounts on July 23, 2020.

Trivia

  • The song is part of Taylor's " Teenage Love Triangle " along with " cardigan " and " august ".
