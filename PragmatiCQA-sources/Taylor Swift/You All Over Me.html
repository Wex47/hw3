" You All Over Me " is a previously unreleased song by Taylor Swift , written for her 2008 album Fearless , released for the first time in 2021 with her re-release of the same album. It is one of the six vault songs for the album. It was released on March 26th, 2021. It is produced by Aaron Dessner, and features background vocals from Maren Morris.
