History

In the Spotify storyline for this song, Taylor states that she "wrote this song about the energy and effort that some people put into spreading negativity. With all of the trolling, cancel culture, telling people how to live their lives, or pitting women against each other... you're being too loud. This is a song where I'm just saying, "You Need to Calm Down." Go outside, it's the summer." This song touches heavily on the topic of negativity in many different forms, and Taylor is telling these people to focus their energy away from tearing other people down, as negativity is unnecessary.

Music video

The music video, filmed on a set in Santa Clarita, California, was released on June 17, 2019 as a premiere on Good Morning America and YouTube. It featured over 21 special guests, most of which were a part of the LGBT+ community.

Much of Taylor’s new merchandise was featured in the video as part of the wardrobe and accessories worn by Taylor, like the Cat watch, sleeping mask that says “calm down”, and her glittery phone case that reads “Lover”.
