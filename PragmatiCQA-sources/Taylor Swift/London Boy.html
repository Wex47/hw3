Theme

“London Boy” is an ode to Swift’s boyfriend as of the song’s release, British actor Joe Alwyn . Other songs on the album, including “ Lover ”, are about their relationship. Having mostly escaped the public eye during her relationship with Alwyn, Swift finally shares some of the most memorable experiences she’s enjoyed while they were dating.
