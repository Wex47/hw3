Background

“Hey Stephen” was written for a guy who opened a few shows for her while she was on tour, that she had a crush on, but who never knew she liked him. She sent him a text message when the album came out, saying “Hey, Track 4.” He then sent her a long e-mail saying “Oh, my God!”

Hidden Message: Love and theft
