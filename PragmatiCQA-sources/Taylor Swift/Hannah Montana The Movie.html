Hannah Montana: The Movie was released on April 10, 2009. It featured a young Miley Stewart, played by Miley Cyrus , trying to get grounded to reality in her hometown of Tennessee.

Taylor was featured as an artist performing at a restaurant that Miley was at. She performed Crazier , a song she wrote for the movie. She also co-wrote the song “ You'll Always Find Your Way Back Home ”, which was performed by Cyrus.
