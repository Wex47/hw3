" Innocent " is a song written by Taylor Swift , as the eleventh track from her third studio album, Speak Now .

This song was written about the 2009 Video Music Awards incident , when Kanye West ran on stage to interrupt Taylor's acceptance speech.

Hidden message

Hidden message: Life is full of little interruptions.

The interruption discussed in “Innocent” was anything but little — rapper Kanye West stormed the stage after Taylor won an award at the 2009 Video Music Awards and insisted that Beyoncé deserved it. Not only did he humiliate Taylor, but he humiliated himself as well.
