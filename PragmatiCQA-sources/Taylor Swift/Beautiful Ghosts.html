"Beautiful Ghosts"
" Beautiful Ghosts " is a song written by Taylor Swift and Andrew Lloyd Webber, released on November 15, 2019, for the movie adaptation of the musical Cats. Taylor posted a studio version of this song.

Background

Webber had seen that Victoria, a character in the musical, needed a song. He played the music he had composed for Taylor and she came up with the lyrics during the meeting.

“ I played this to you and I said it was a new song. You said ‘I’ll do the lyric[s]’, and you did it then and there, more or less. ”

—Andrew Lloyd Webber

Taylor said that she wanted to emulate the lyrics of T.S. Elliot, the original lyricist for the musical.

“ T.S. Elliot is such a specific type of writer, and uses such specific language and imagery. So reading through, like, his work and everything I just really wanted to reflect that within... and you know you can’t write a modern lyric for Cats... so if you can’t get T.S. Elliot, like, get T.S. ”

—Taylor Swift
