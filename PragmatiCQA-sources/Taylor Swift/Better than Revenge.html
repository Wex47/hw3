“ Better Than Revenge ” is a song on the 2010 album Speak Now . It is about her relationship with Joe Jonas and the girl he dumped her for, actress Camilla Belle. Taylor writes about the actress with the line, "She's better known for the things that she does on the mattress".

Musically, this song is a departure from Taylor Swift's typical country pop, using influences of pop punk while being compared to Paramore's Misery Business.

“ The song "Better Than Revenge" is about a girl, who a few years ago, stole my boyfriend. I think she probably thought I forgot about it, but I didn't. ”

Hidden message

Hidden message : You thought I would forget

This refers to the fact that Taylor allegedly wrote this song about Camilla Belle, the girl who "stole" Joe Jonas from her.

Although the song is only rumored to have been about her, certain lyrics make it rather obvious, namely the “so much better” line at the end. “Much Better” is the name of a Jonas Brothers song.
