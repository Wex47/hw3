Speak Now Chronology

" If This Was A Movie " "Superman"
" Superman " is seventeenth song on the Deluxe Edition of Taylor Swift 's album third album Speak Now . It was released as promotional single together with " If This Was A Movie " through iTunes and Amazon.com on November 8, 2011.

This track is rumored to be about her ex-boyfriend, John Mayer .
