"How You Get The Girl" is the tenth track from Taylor Swift 's fifth studio album, 1989 . It was featured in an ad for Diet Coke which starred her kitten Olivia . The song is an upbeat, catchy pop song written by Swift, Max Martin & Shellback in 2013.

Hidden message

Hidden message : Then one day he came back.

The hidden message story line in this album shows that there is hope for the lost relationship that Taylor wrote the messages about.
