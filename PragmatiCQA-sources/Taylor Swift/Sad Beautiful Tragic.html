Trivia

According to CelebBuzz, the song is theorized to be about either John Mayer , Jake Gyllenhaal , or Taylor Lautner .

  • Taylor wrote this song on her tour bus while she was thinking about a relationship that had ended several months ago.

Hidden message

Hidden message : While you were on a train.

With ' Back to December ' in mind, we hear regret in her voice as she looks back on what could have been, so fans are led to believe the ballad is about a romance that ended peacefully. Taylor Lautner , perhaps.
