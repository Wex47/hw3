Ellen Lee DeGeneres (born January 26, 1958) is an American comedian, actress, and Emmy Award-winning talk show host who has hosted The Ellen DeGeneres Show since it debuted in 2003. She is also a close friend of Taylor 's. Taylor has been on the Ellen show ever since the beginning of her career, as a guest. She did interviews in all of her eras except for reputation . Taylor did not do any interviews during that time.

Ellen joined Taylor on stage during The 1989 World Tour as a surprise guest during the song Style . She walked out wearing a sparkly zip-up jacket, a shiny tutu and silver tights. The jacket was supposed to look like Taylor's dress.

Ellen made a special guest appearance in the music video for You Need To Calm Down , the second single and fourteenth track from Taylor's seventh studio album, Lover .
