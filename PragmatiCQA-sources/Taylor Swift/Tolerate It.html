" Tolerate It " (stylized as " tolerate it ") is the fifth track from Taylor Swift 's ninth studio album , evermore . It was released on December 11, 2020, through Republic Records .

“tolerate it” is the track 5, of Taylor Swift’s 9th album, evermore . This track is known to always be reserved for her most vulnerable song on each album. This song is about the continuous struggle of wanting love from someone who isn’t even paying attention to anything you do for them.
