" Death by a Thousand Cuts " is the tenth track off of Taylor Swift 's seventh studio album, Lover .

Background

This song was inspired by the Netflix film Someone Great . The film was inspired by the final track on Taylor’s fifth studio album, 1989 , “ Clean ”.

Taylor has said her favorite line from this song is “our songs, our films, united we stand. Our country, guess it was a lawless land. Quiet my fears with the touch of your hand. Paper cut stings from our paper thin plans.”
