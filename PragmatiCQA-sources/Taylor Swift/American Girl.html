"American Girl" is a digitally released song by Taylor Swift . The song is a cover version of Tom Petty’s "American Girl" .

Background and release

Taylor and her band, The Agency , teamed up with Rhapsody and Verizon to record a cover version of Tom Petty’s "American Girl" as recorded on her 2009 Fearless Tour , using Rhapsody (now Napster) and Verizon’s mobile recording studio. The song was released with a video featuring exclusive footage captured on Taylor’s sold-out arena tour June 30, 2009. The track is available for full-song download, ringtone download, ringback download. Currently, the song is only available digitally on Napster.
