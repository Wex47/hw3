Music video

" No Body, No Crime " (stylized as " no body, no crime ") ft. HAIM , is the sixth track from Taylor Swift 's ninth studio album , evermore . It was released on December 11, 2020, through Republic Records .

“no body, no crime” is an imaginative take on Swift’s part on a true crime story, featuring HAIM band member Este Haim—good friends of Swift's—as the center of a missing persons case after being cheated on by her husband.
