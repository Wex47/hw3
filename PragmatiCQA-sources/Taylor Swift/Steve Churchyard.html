Steve Churchyard is a British born record producer, engineer and mixer who began his career at the Sir George Martin’s AIR Studios in London. He currently resides in the United States. He has been nominated for 4 Grammy awards, and in 2008 he won a Latin Grammy for Juanes’ album “La Vida... Es Un Ratico.”

He worked as the engineer for Taylor Swift on her Speak Now album.

He has also worked with a large number of other famous musicians, such as Katy Perry and Jason Mraz.

Steve Churchyard was featured in the book Behind the Glass by Howard Massey.
