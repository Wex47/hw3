" Illicit Affairs " (stylized as " illicit affairs ") is the tenth track from Taylor Swift 's eighth studio album, Folklore . It was released on July 24, 2020, through Republic Records .

This song was confirmed on Taylor’s social media accounts on July 23, 2020.
