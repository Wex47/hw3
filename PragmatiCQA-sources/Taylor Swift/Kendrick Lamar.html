Kendrick Lamar (born Kendrick Lamar Duckworth on June 17, 1987) is an American rapper, best known for his hits "i" and "King Kunta". He is featured on a remix of Taylor Swift 's song Bad Blood and appeared in the music video. Besides having collaborated with her, he has also worked with other artists such as Imagine Dragons (remix of "Radioactive"), Big Sean, and Eminem, among others. He has won 7 Grammy Awards.
