Taylor Swift and Def Leppard is an episode of the CMT television show CMT Crossroads . The episodes features performances by American singer-songwriter Taylor Swift and English band Def Leppard .

The performance made its television premiere on November 7, 2008, and was released on June 16, 2009 via DVD exclusively to US Wal-Mart stores by Big Machine Records .

Performances

The program featured songs by both Swift and Def Leppard as well as interview segments.

  1. " Photograph "
  2. " Picture to Burn "
  3. " Love Story "
  4. " Hysteria "
  5. " Teardrops on My Guitar "
  6. " When Love & Hate Collide "
  7. " Should've Said No "
  8. " Pour Some Sugar on Me "

DVD release

When the program was released on DVD it contained additional interviews and three bonus songs not featured in the original program.

Bonus performances included on the DVD:

  1. "Love"
  2. " Two Steps Behind "
  3. " Our Song "
