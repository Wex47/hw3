" Invisible " is a song written and performed by Taylor Swift and is the thirteenth track from the deluxe edition of her debut album, Taylor Swift . Other songs on the deluxe edition include " A Perfectly Good Heart ", " I'm Only Me When I'm With You " and " Teardrops On My Guitar " (pop version).
