Hayley Nichole Williams (born December 27, 1988) is the lead singer for the rock band Paramore and a friend of Taylor Swift .

Besides being Taylor's friend, Hayley has achieved great success with her band Paramore, having such hits as "Misery Business", "Crushcrushcrush", "Decode", and "The Only Exception". She even scored a hit with the rapper B.o.B. on his hit single "Airplanes".

In September 2011 Taylor Swift brought Hayley on stage with her at Nashville to sing a duet. She evens appears as The Crimson Curse in the Bad Blood music video.
