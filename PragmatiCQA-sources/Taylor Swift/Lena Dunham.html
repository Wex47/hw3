Lena Dunham (born May 13, 1986) is an American actress and filmmaker who is a friend of Taylor's. She is best known as the creator, star, executive producer, and head writer of the popular HBO television series Girls. She also appears as Lucky Fiori in the Bad Blood music video.

In an interview with Glamour magazine she states that she shares a close friendship with Taylor Swift, and that the two often talk on the phone.

“ She helps me be stronger and make better decisions ”
“ She has amazing creative instincts. She's really a proponent of not feeling pressure from anybody else and recognising that you have a better connection to what your 'brand' is than anybody else. ”

Taylor has worked with Lena's ex-boyfriend, musician Jack Antonoff , on " Out of the Woods " and other songs from 1989 . They both love cats. For her 25th birthday, Lena gave a necklace to Taylor, which Taylor called her "new most cherished possession". [1]
