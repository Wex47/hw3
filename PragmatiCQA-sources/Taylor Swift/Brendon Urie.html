Brendon Urie (born April 12, 1987) is an American singer, songwriter, and musician, best known as the lead vocalist of pop rock band Panic! at the Disco, of which he is the only original member remaining.

Relation to Taylor

He is the featured artist on Taylor's song " ME! ", released on April 26, 2019.

“ Brendon has always been one of my favorite performers and I’ve always had it in the back of my mind that it would be INSANE to find the right thing to collab on and when I wrote this chorus I KNEW. ”

—Taylor via YouTube chat

Taylor also mentioned him in her ELLE UK 2019 magazine essay.

“ When I hear 'I Write Sins Not Tragedies' by Panic! At The Disco, I’m transported back to being sixteen and driving down the streets of Hendersonville, Tennessee, with my best friend Abigail, euphorically screaming the lyrics. ”

—Taylor Swift via ELLE UK
