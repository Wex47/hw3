Emily Jean "Emma" Stone (born November 6, 1988) is an American actress who is a friend of Taylor Swift 's.

Career

In addition to being Taylor's friend, Emma, who was born and raised in Scottsdale, Arizona, has acheived success as an actress with hit films such as The Help and The Amazing Spider-Man. She earned an Academy Award nomination for Best Supporting Actress for her role in the 2014 film Birdman . She won an Academy Award for Best Actress for her role in the 2016 film La La Land.

Friendship with Taylor

Emma told MTV that she met Taylor Swift at the Young Hollywood Awards for Hollywood Life magazine, where she listened to some of her music and then decided to email her. They then started hanging out together after that.

Emma and Selena Gomez are often seen with Taylor now.

Emma says that "Taylor's a pretty normal girl".

Both of them enjoy baking.

In one interview, Taylor says that she can count on Emma for advice after she has encountered a huge breakup with one of her boyfriends.

Personal Life

Emma dated her Amazing Spider-Man co-star Andrew Garfield from 2011-2015. Their second film together, The Amazing Spider-Man 2 , was released on May 2, 2014. They briefly split in early 2015 but later reunited. They split up again in October 2015.

Other friends

Emma introduced Taylor to actress Hailee Steinfeld . The two became friends. Another mutual friend of is Hunger Games star and Oscar winning actress Jennifer Lawrence .
