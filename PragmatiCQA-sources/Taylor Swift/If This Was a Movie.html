" If This Was A Movie " is a song from the Target deluxe edition of Taylor Swift 's third studio album , Speak Now . It was released as a promotional single by iTunes and Amazon.com on November 8, 2011 as a song from the Deluxe Edition of Taylor Swift 's album Speak Now . It is the only song from Speak Now to be co-written by Johnson & Swift.
