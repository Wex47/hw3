" Nothing New " is a song written by Taylor Swift . It is the 23rd track on , which was released on November 12, 2021. It features Phoebe Bridgers and is the first time a female artist featured on a Swift song has had their own verse. It was announced on August 5, 2021 as a part of a word scramble for the different From the Vault songs to be released on the album. The song is about her fame and how she fears what will happen to her when she loses it.

“ I've been thinking a lot about getting older and relevancy and how all my heroes have all ended up alone. I wrote a song on it called "Nothing New" and it's about being scared of aging and things changing and losing what you have. ”

—Taylor Swift

Background

It was revealed in the deluxe edition journals accompanying her seventh studio album, Lover . It was written for her 2012 album Red , but later left off the album. It was inspired by Joni Mitchell's "A Case Of You".
