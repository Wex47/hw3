" The Lakes " (stylized as " the lakes ") is the seventeenth track from the deluxe edition of Taylor Swift 's eighth studio album, folklore . It is only available on the deluxe version of the album, which released on August 7, 2020, through Republic Records .

This song was confirmed on Taylor’s social media accounts on July 23, 2020.
