" I'm Only Me When I'm With You " is a song by Taylor Swift and serves as the twelfth track from her self-titled debut studio album .

Background

The song is about Swift and her best friend Abigail Anderson . It speaks volumes of how they know each other so well and feel free to be themselves when they are together. The video features Abigail and other of Swift’s friends and family. It has a laid-back setting and shows a more family-oriented side of her.

Music Video

The music video was released on March 3, 2008, and it was later uploaded to Swift's YouTube channel on June 16, 2009.
