" The Moment I Knew " is the seventeenth track on Taylor Swift 's fourth studio album and the first track on the second CD in the physical copy of the deluxe edition of Red . It was released as a promotional single on January 8, 2013. The song is about Jake Gyllenhaal , her ex-boyfriend , missing her twenty-first birthday party.

Former Agency member Elizabeth Huett performs background vocals on the track.

Taylor explained in an interview with Yahoo! Music:

“ 'The Moment I Knew' was a song about my 21st birthday party, which was the worst experience ever. ”
