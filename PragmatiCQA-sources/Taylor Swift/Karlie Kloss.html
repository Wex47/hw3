Karlie Elizabeth Kloss (born August 3, 1992) is an American fashion model and entrepreneur. She was a Victoria's Secret Angel from 2011 to 2014. She used to be one of Taylor Swift 's best friends.

Friendship with Taylor Swift

In 2015, Kloss and one of her best friends, Taylor Swift, covered the March issue of American Vogue together. Kloss also starred in the music video for Chic's "I'll Be There", which was directed by Inez van Lamsweerde and Vinoodh Matadin. Kloss also portrayed the role of "Knockout" in the music video for Swift's song " Bad Blood " which premiered at the 2015 Billboard Music Awards.

Kloss appeared in several video segments during Taylor Swift's 1989 World Tour and appeared live on stage with Swift at the performance of June 27, 2015 at London's Hyde Park, as well as on July 11, 2015, at the MetLife Stadium in East Rutherford, New Jersey.

Personal life

Kloss has been dating businessman Josh Kushner since 2012. The couple announced their engagement on July 24, 2018 and were married on October 18, 2018.

Feud

Taylor and Karlie stopped talking after the controversy with Scooter Braun , a good friend of Karlie’s. She chose Scooter over Taylor, and she has since unfollowed Taylor on all social media. She has been seen with Scooter and others that he is associated with, and she has liked tweets that are against Taylor.
