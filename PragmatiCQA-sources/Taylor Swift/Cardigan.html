Music video

" cardigan " is the lead single and second track from Taylor Swift 's eighth studio album, folklore . This song was confirmed on Taylor’s social media accounts on July 23, 2020. It was released on July 24, 2020, through Republic Records , along with its official music video.

Music video

The music video was released on July 24, 2020, and it was edited by Chancler Haynes, with special effects by David Lebensfeld and Grant Miller and set design by Ethan Tobman. The entire shoot was overseen by a medical inspector, everyone wore masks, stayed away from each other, and Taylor did her own hair, makeup, and styling. It was directed by Taylor, with cinematography by Rodrigo Prieto, production by Jil Hardin, it was executively produced by Rebecca Skinner and AD Joe ‘Oz’ Osborne.

Premise

The video revolves around pianos, Taylor travels through them to different locations, sometimes through the top, also through the piano bench. In the end of the video, Taylor puts on a cardigan, the same one available for merchandise.

Trivia

  • The song is part of Taylor's " Teenage Love Triangle " along with " betty " and " august "
