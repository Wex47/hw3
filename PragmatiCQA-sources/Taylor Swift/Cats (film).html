A Hollywood Movie adaption of " Cats ", directed by Tom Hooper, began filming in late 2018, with a planned release date of December 20, 2019. The project is being adapted by Lee Hall, with choreography by Wayne McGregor. Taylor Swift was casted as one of the lead roles, as Bombalurina .

Taylor's character Bombalurina is described as an "adult, red, flirtatious cat."
