“ Girl at Home ” is a song from Taylor Swift 's fourth studio album, Red Deluxe Edition . It was released as a promotional single on January 8, 2013.

This song is rumored to be about Zac Efron. After she had worked with him on the movie The Lorax , there was a rumor that Zac Efron was trying to date Taylor, when he was already dating someone else.

It was re-recorded at an unknown date, with all the tracks off Red, entitled Red (Taylor's Version) , and now has an electropop sound.
