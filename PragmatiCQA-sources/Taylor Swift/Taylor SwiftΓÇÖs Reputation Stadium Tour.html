Taylor Swift’s Reputation Stadium Tour is the Netflix original special featuring American singer-songwriter Taylor Swift , in support of her sixth studio album, Reputation . The special was released to Netflix on December 31st. It was filmed at AT&T Stadium in Arlington, Texas.

This special has created many new fans as a result of being released.
