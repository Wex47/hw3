Enchanted Wonderstruck is a perfume spin off of Wonderstruck . It was released in September 2012, during the Speak Now era.

This scent comes in a perfume bottle, perfume roller, perfume crayon, body mist, lotion and body gel.

Appearance

Enchanted Wonderstruck comes in a spherical bottle with a red holographic coating. It says "Enchanted Wonderstruck Taylor Swift" on the bottle in gold font.

The cap has designs covering it. They are identical stretching across the cap. On the top, there is a flower design.

The gold chain attached to the neck of the bottle has four gold-colored charms on it. these charms include:

  • A textured bird attached by the tail.
  • A clear egg shaped gem design bead.
  • A long, textured feather.
  • A gold rose, with a clear gem in the middle.
