“ To be recognized by the Grammys is the ultimate honor. ”

The Grammy Awards are presented annually by the National Academy of Recording Arts and Sciences of the United States for outstanding achievements in the music industry.

Taylor Swift has won 11 Grammy Awards from thirty-two nominations. She is the second youngest person to win the Album of the Year award and the twelfth female artist with the most awards. She is the first female artist to win Album of the Year thrice.
