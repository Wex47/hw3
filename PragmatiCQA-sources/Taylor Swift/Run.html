" Run " is the 28th track off of . It features Ed Sheeran . It was announced on August 5, 2021 as a part of a word scramble for the different From the Vault songs to be released on the album.

Background

This is the first song Taylor and Ed ever wrote together when meeting for the first time in 2012.
