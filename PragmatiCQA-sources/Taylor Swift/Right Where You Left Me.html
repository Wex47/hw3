" Right Where You Left Me " (stylized as " right where you left me ") is the first deluxe track from Taylor Swift 's ninth studio album , evermore . It was released on December 23, 2020, through Republic Records . It was released digitally on January 7, 2021, along with the deluxe version of the album.
