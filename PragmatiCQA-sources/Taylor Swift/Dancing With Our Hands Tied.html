" Dancing With Our Hands Tied " is the eleventh track from Taylor Swift 's sixth studio album, reputation . It was released on November 10, 2017, through Big Machine Records .

Trivia

  • A line from this song was featured on Taylor's ...Ready For It? Music video. The first line, "I loved you in secret", was written in graffiti on the walls.
  • It is Swift's brother Austin Swift 's favourite track off of Reputation .
