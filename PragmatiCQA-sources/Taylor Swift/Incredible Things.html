Incredible Things is Taylor Swift's third perfume. It's name comes from Taylor's song “ Blank Space ”, using the lyric "I could show you incredible things". It's believed that the look of the bottle was inspired by Taylor's paintings. It was first released in fall of 2014.

Many products have been made using the scent of Incredible Things. It has been made as not only a perfume but as a body lotion, body wash, bath gel and fine fragrance mist.

Product description

According to its description on Walmart's website:

“ A reflection of Taylor's romantic spirit and modern artistry, Taylor Swift Incredible Things is an intriguing "floriental" fragrance. Like Taylor herself, the scent is fresh, feminine, and unforgettable. The scent is composed of velvety petals and creamy woods wrapped in a soft veil of vanilla and musk. Pink pepper, sparkling grapefruit, and wild passion flower create desire. Inspired by the lyrics from Taylor's song " Blank Space ," the rich sophistication of this fragrance instantly captures attention. ”

Appearance

The Incredible Things bottle has a colorful base with different water colors and Taylor's silhouette with a gold cap. On the bottom of the bottle there is text saying 'Taylor Swift Incredible Things' in black.
