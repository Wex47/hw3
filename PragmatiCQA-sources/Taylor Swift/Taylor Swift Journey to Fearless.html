Taylor Swift Journey to Fearless is a three episode production that shows Taylor Swift on her Fearless Tour . It also includes home movies from when she was younger. Originally shown on The Hub for three nights starting on October 22, 2010, it was later released on Blu-ray and DVD on October 11, 2011. It is 135 minute long.
