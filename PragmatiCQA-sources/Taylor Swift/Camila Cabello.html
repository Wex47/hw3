Karla Camila Cabello Estrabao (born March 3, 1997) is a Cuban-born American singer who is a friend of Taylor Swift 's. She is a former member of the all-female pop group Fifth Harmony.

Friendship with Taylor

Camila became friends with Taylor through her former boyfriend, Austin Mahone, who is also friends with Taylor.

Taylor even threw Camila a party for her eighteenth birthday. The guests included Selena Gomez , Hayley Williams , Sarah Hyland , and Zedd .

She and her Fifth Harmony group mates even attended Taylor's Billboard Music Award after-party on May 17, 2015.

Fifth Harmony even got to perform their mega hit "Worth It" with Taylor when the 1989 World Tour hit Santa Clara, California on August 14, 2015.

On March 1, 2018 Taylor announced that Camila would be the opening act of the Reputation Stadium Tour along with Charli XCX .

Personal life

Camila had previously dated singer Austin Mahone in 2014.

Trivia

  • Taylor's friend Demi Lovato was on the judging panel for season 2 of The X Factor. Camila and her group, Fifth Harmony, competed on that season with British record executive Simon Cowell as their mentor.
