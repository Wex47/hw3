"All You Had to Do Was Stay" is the fifth track from Taylor Swift 's fifth studio album, 1989 . It was written by Swift and Max Martin .

Hidden message

Hidden message : They paid the price.

Following the hidden message storyline of the album, the song ties into the message with the overall theme of this song. "AYHTDWS" revolves around the broken relationship of people who couldn't be together because the boyfriend wasn't open and loyal enough.
