Selena Marie Gomez (born July 22, 1992) is an American singer and actress. She is best known for portraying Alex Russo in the hit Disney comedy, Wizards of Waverly Place . She is also the lead singer of the band Selena Gomez and The Scene.

Selena is best friends with Taylor Swift . They have been seen at many red-carpet events and even went to a Notre Dame football game together. They hang out a lot, and Selena calls Taylor her "Mentor" and has even described her as an "old soul". They also sung Who Says together. Gomez once told radio host Ryan Seacrest [1] . Taylor has also sat with Selena, along with Katy Perry on the 2012 Nickelodeon Kids Choice Awards.

Taylor had been there for Selena especially after her breakup with her boyfriend of two years, Justin Bieber . The two also talked via FaceTime with their then friend, Demi Lovato . Despite rumors, Selena and Justin have not gotten back together. There were reports, however, that Taylor's friendship with New Zealand singer Lorde , who was very critical of Selena in various interviews, may have strained their friendship. This was later proven not to be true when the two were later seen at the Met Gala in New York. Taylor even attended the American Music Awards in 2014 with Lorde to support Selena when she performed her single "The Heart Wants What It Wants".

Selena recently released her album 'Rare'.

Taylor also invited Selena to perform with her on Taylor's Speak Now World Tour , performing "Who Says," at The RED Tour , and The 1989 World Tour , where they sang "Good For You." They performed Selena's song "Hands To Myself" together on the Reputation Stadium Tour .
