“ I Forgot That You Existed ” is the first track from Taylor Swift ’s seventh studio album, Lover . This song was announced on August 16 on Taylor’s social media, the day of the release of her song “ Lover ”.

This song is theorized to be about an ex, most likely Calvin Harris .

Background

This song’s beginning stages were recorded on the piano and released on the deluxe editions of Lover as a voice memo, placed as the 19th track on the CD. A notable difference between this and the final cut is that the second line in the chorus used to be “I thought that I would miss you but I didn’t.” This was later replaced by “I thought that it would kill me but it didn’t.”
