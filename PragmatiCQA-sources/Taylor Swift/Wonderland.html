"Wonderland" is the fourteenth track on the deluxe edition of Taylor Swift 's fifth studio album, 1989 . It is a fan favourite, with many covers on YouTube and Instagram. The song references to the Lewis Caroll book "Alice In Wonderland" and has a major theme of insanity. The song explains how Swift's relationship is like Alice's Trip in Wonderland.

Taylor has performed this song as a surprise song during a few shows from The 1989 World Tour and the Reputation Stadium Tour .
