" It's Time to Go " (stylized as " it's time to go ") is the second and final deluxe track from Taylor Swift 's ninth studio album , evermore . It was released on December 21, 2020, through Republic Records . It was released digitally on January 7, 2021, along with the deluxe version of the album.
