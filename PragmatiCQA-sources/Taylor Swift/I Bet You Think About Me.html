Music video

" I Bet You Think About Me " is the 26th track off of , released on November 12, 2021. The song features Chris Stapleton. It was announced on August 5, 2021 as a part of a word scramble for the different From the Vault songs to be released on the album, and was released on November 12, 2021.

Music video

The music video for this song was released on November 15, 2021. It was directed by Blake Lively and Taylor Swift. It features Miles Teller as Taylor's ex-boyfriend as she crashes his wedding, painting it red.
