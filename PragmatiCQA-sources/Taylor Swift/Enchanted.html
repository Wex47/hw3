" Enchanted " is the ninth track on the 2010 album Speak Now . The song became the theme song for her fragrance, Wonderstruck. It is probably due to the word being mentioned in the lyrics. It featured on the MTV-released trailer of the 2012 movie The Vow .

The song is about Adam Young - known for his musical project entitled Owl City. He later remixed the song, adding her name to it and a few minor changes. [1]

“

I wrote "Enchanted" about a guy who I was enchanted to meet, obviously. He was somebody that I had talked to a couple of times on email, and then I was in New York and went to meet him.

I remember just the whole way home thinking, "I hope he's not in love with somebody." It was just wonderful, that feeling. Like, "Oh my gosh. Who's he with? Does he like me? Does he like somebody else? What does it mean?" I got home and he had emailed me and said something like, "Sorry I was so quiet. I was just wonderstruck meeting you." And so I incorporated the word wonderstruck, into the song as a, "Hey this one's sorta for you."

”

Swift sang the song on multiple tours, with the first being at the Speak Now World Tour . Following that, she did it as a surprise song on one of her Red Tour performances, and mashed it up with “ Wildest Dreams ” on The 1989 World Tour . Finally, she surprised the audience by singing it during the Reputation Stadium Tour at the MetLife Stadium in East Rutherford, NJ on July 21st, 2018.

Hidden message

Hidden message : Adam

The Adam in question is Adam Young, the creative mastermind behind Owl City. The two had chatted online and over the phone before meeting for the first time at a party.

Adam later recorded and posted his version of “Enchanted” in response to Taylor, with this sweet message attached:

“ Dearest Taylor,

I’ll be the first to admit I’m a rather shy boy and since music is the most eloquent form of communication I can muster, I decided to record something for you – as sort of a “reply” to the breathtaking song on your current record. This is what I wanted so badly to tell you in person but could never quite put into words: Everything about you is beautiful. You’re an immensely charming girl with a wonderful heart and more grace and elegance than I know how to describe. You are a true princess from a dreamy fairy tale; a modern Cinderella. I’m terribly sorry it’s taken me such a long time to reply but I figured Valentine’s Day was the perfect time to write this note to you and simply say… I was enchanted to meet you too.

Love,

Adam

”

Despite their mutual feelings, the two never officially dated. Years later, Taylor would date a different Adam — Adam Wiles, better known as his alias Scottish DJ Calvin Harris.
