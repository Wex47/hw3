" Silent Night " was cover song recorded by Taylor Swift for her extended play, Sounds of the Season: The Taylor Swift Holiday Collection , released on October 14, 2007.

This song was written by Franz Xaver Gruber, first performed on December 24, 1818, and published in 1833.
