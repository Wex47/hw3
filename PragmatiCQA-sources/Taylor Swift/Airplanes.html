"Airplanes"
" Airplanes " is a song originally by American rapper B.o.B, featuring vocals from Hayley Williams of American rock group Paramore. On October 8, 2011, during the Arlington, Texas date of her Speak Now World Tour, Taylor Swift performed the song with B.o.B.
