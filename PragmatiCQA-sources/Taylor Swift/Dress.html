" Dress " is the twelfth track from Taylor Swift 's sixth studio album, reputation. It was released on November 10, 2017, through Big Machine Records .

Live performances

Taylor performed this song on the second b-stage during the Reputation Stadium Tour , after “ Blank Space ”.
