Todrick Hall

General information

Born

April 4, 1985

Occupation

Rapper, singer, songwriter, actor, dancer, director, choreographer, executive producer, YouTuber, Judge on RuPaul's Drag Race

Years active

1999-present
Todrick Hall and Taylor Swift met backstage after Todrick had made a video titled 'For Taylor' performing a mashup of Swift's songs. The two had since become very good friends. He was also a backup dancer in the Look What You Made Me Do music video and featured in the You Need To Calm Down music video.
