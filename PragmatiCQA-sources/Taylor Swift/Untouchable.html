" Untouchable " is a song by Luna Halo, covered by Taylor Swift . It was a track on Taylor's extended album, Fearless Platinum Edition .

It was a cover of the song by the same name by the rock band Luna Halo.

Taylor performed this song during the Speak Now World Tour at Sacramento, California on September 3 2011 as a surprise cover song.

Hidden message

Hidden message : We always want what we can't reach

Taylor's version of this song is a much slower, softer version than the original, and she used this to show what her hidden message intends, and how she interprets the song.
