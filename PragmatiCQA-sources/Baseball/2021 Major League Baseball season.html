The 2021 Major League Baseball season began on April 1 and is scheduled to end on October 3. The 91st All-Star Game scheduled to be held on July 13 in Denver, CO (before, it was in Atlanta, GA). The World Series is scheduled to begin on October 26 and a potential Game 7 is scheduled for November 3. The entire schedule was released on July 9, 2020.

In the regular season, each of all 30 MLB teams will play 162 games and 52 series in the span of 186 days from April 1 to October 3. The postseason will begin on Tuesday, October 5. The World Series will begin on Tuesday, October 26.

The 2021 Atlanta Braves have won the 2021 World Series in 6 games against the 2021 Houston Astros on Tuesday, November 2, 2021. Therefore, the 2021 MLB season officially ends after Tuesday, November 2, 2021.
