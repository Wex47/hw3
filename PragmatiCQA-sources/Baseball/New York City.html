New York City (officially the City of New York) is the most populous city in the United States and one of the major global cities of the world.

There have been fourteen World Series championship series between New York City teams; such matchups are called Subway Series . The city's two current Major League Baseball teams are the New York Yankees and the New York Mets , which enjoy a fierce rivalry. New York City is also home to two minor league baseball teams, the Brooklyn Cyclones and Staten Island Yankees .
