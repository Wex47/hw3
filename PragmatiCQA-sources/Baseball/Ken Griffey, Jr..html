George Kenneth Griffey, Jr. (born November 21 , 1969 in Donora, Pennsylvania), nicknamed " Junior " and " The Kid ", is a retired American professional baseball player who played center fielder and played for most of his career for the Seattle Mariners and Cincinnati Reds and also played for the Chicago White Sox . He is the son of Ken Griffey, Sr. .
