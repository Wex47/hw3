The Baseball Wiki is a free baseball encyclopedia and reference site which is being written collaboratively by its contributors. The Baseball Wiki project was started in July 2005. There are currently 8,386 articles at Baseball Wiki at the moment. We want to become the most up to date and the most reliable source for information about baseball and overall one of the best sites for all things baseball!

Baseball Wiki is a wiki , meaning that anyone can contribute to any article, including you . You can contribute by clicking on the Edit this page button on the top of each page. To learn more about editing articles to this site, see . Also, it is suggested that you to contribute, but it is your choice. Every change you make can improve Baseball Wiki further, so if you know anything about baseball, and help out. We'll be sure to welcome you, and this is all entirely free. It's very fun also!
