Chizuru Hari ( 針 ( はり ) 千 ( ち ) 釣 ( づる ) , Hari Chizuru ) is a character in the Jujutsu Kaisen series. He was a jujutsu sorcerer participating in the Culling Game and was aligned with Reggie Star 's group.

Appearance

Chizuru is a relatively tall man with a slim build and a toned figure evidenced by his abs. He has light-colored hair that is styled so most of it is cut low. However, there are longer locks of hair going down the middle of his head that extends into a ponytail. Chizuru also has very thick eyebrows and small dark eyes.

During the Culling Game, Chizuru did not sport a shirt and only wore cargo pants tucked into combat boots with a sash hanging from his waist.

Personality

Chizuru didn't show much of his personality during his time in the Culling Game. He aggressively followed Reggie and fought to kill any sorcerer that opposed him. As a fighter, Chizuru has some degree of respect for his opponents.

After getting overpowered by Megumi and slammed several stories into the ground by Nue, Chizuru climbed back up and admitted his opponent wasn't bad. Desiring to get even, Chizuru attempted to finish Megumi off after he was injured by Iori Hazenoki 's explosions. However, Chizuru severely underestimated Megumi and was killed before ever getting to utilize his cursed technique .

Synopsis

History

At some point before Megumi and Yuji entered the Tokyo No. 1 colony, Chizuru joined Reggie Star's group of culling game participants.

Tokyo No. 1 Colony Arc

Chizuru attacked Megumi after the latter's negotioations with Reggie failed, only to be thrown off the building and slammed into the ground below by Nue . He later climbed back up and confronted his opponent inside one of the apartments, right as a new rule got added to the culling game. Chizuru then swiftly got battered off the building once again, getting fatally stabbed in the face by Megumi when he landed.

Abilities and Powers

Overall Skill : Little of Chizuru's combat prowess has been on display. He is a skilled enough sorcerer to score 28 points in the Culling Game and to be considered as one of Reggie's allies. However, he was easily overpowered by Megumi on two seperate occassions. Chizuru was even killed by him in their second skirmish without ever revealing the power of his cused technique.

Jujutsu

Innate Technique
Chizuru's technique allows him to shift his fingers into dark claws. It is unknown if their offensive ability was purely physical or if they had some effect on impact. Chizuru attempted to strike Megumi by changing the fingertips on his left hand into claws but failed to land a single hit.
