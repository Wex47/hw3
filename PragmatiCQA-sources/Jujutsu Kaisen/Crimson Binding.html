Crimson Binding ( 赤 ( せき ) 縛 ( ばく ) , Sekibaku ) is a Blood Manipulation attack.

Contents

  • 1 Description
  • 2 Usage
  • 4 Navigation

Description

This technique transforms the shape of the user's blood into a net capable of binding targets with great efficiency.

Usage

By throwing a single pack of his own blood, Noritoshi was able to shape his blood into a large net that bound Megumi 's Nue , immobilizing it instantly.
