Tamamo-no-Mae Incarnate ( 化 ( け ) 身 ( しん ) 玉藻前 ( たまものまえ ) , Keshin Tamamo no Mae ) is an imaginary vengeful cursed spirit and one of the sixteen special grade cursed spirits. It was controlled and used by Suguru Geto 's Cursed Spirit Manipulation technique .

Appearance

Tamamo-no-Mae is a cursed spirit with four eyes and long back hair that extends all the way down to the back of its legs. They have two lines around the edges of their mouth similar to a puppet. Tamamo-no-Mae has a humanoid shape but their feet are much different. They stand on the front of their foot with four larger toes with dark toenails. Tamamo-no-Mae wears a light-colored kimono with designs accented around each sleeve and dark pants.

Synopsis

Cursed Child Arc

At some point Tamamo-no-Mae incarnate came under Suguru Geto's possession. He chose not to use them for the Night Parade of a Hundred Demons and instead saved Tamamo-no-Mae for the end of his battle with Yuta Okkotsu . In the climax of the fight, Suguru summoned Tamamo-no-Mae incarnate and explained they are one of sixteen special grade cursed spirits. Immediately after, Suguru activated Cursed Spirit Manipulation: Maximum: Uzumaki and combined their power in a final clash with Yuta. Suguru barely escaped the exchange with his life and Tamamo-no-Mae Incarnate has not been seen since.

Abilities

Overall Skill : Tamamo-no-Mae Incarnate is one of sixteen special grade curses and that rank reflects their strength. However, its abilities are completely unknown. Suguru Geto summoned it alongside his Maxiumum: Uzumaki technique, but both were overpowered by Yuta and Rika's final attack.

Trivia

  • Tamamo-no-Mae Incarnate is based on the legendary Tamamo-no-Mae of Japanese mythology.
