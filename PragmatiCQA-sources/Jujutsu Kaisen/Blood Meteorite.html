Blood Meteorite ( 血星磊 ( けっせいせき ) , Kesseiseki ) is a Blood Manipulation attack.

Contents

  • 1 Description
  • 2 Usage
  • 4 Navigation

Description

The user hardens some of their blood and throws it at a target. Even through this technique hardens blood, it does not have the speed or power of Piercing Blood .

Usage

Choso used this technique against Yuji Itadori during their confrontation at metro station by shooting a Blood Meteorite to his vital organs without he was noticed.
