Trivia

  • In the scene where Yuji and his friends are in the park, more characters are added as the series continues.
    • In Episodes 1 and 2 , only Yuji, Junpei, Megumi and Gojo can be seen.
    • In Episode 3 , Nobara and Masamichi are added.
    • In Episode 6 , Toge, Maki, Panda, Shoko and Kiyotaka are added, and Nobara changes positions to stand next to Maki.
    • In Episode 10 , Nanami and Todo are added.
    • The scene is also based on Claude Monet's Le Déjeuner sur l'herbe (1867), the earlier version of which is unfinished.
  • In Episode 9 , the opening reveals Gojo's eyes. Prior to this episode, the shot cut away before Gojo uncovered his blindfold.
  • In Episode 13 , the last scene in the opening shows tears coming from Yuji's eyes.
  • Kaikai Kitan was nominated for the "Best Opening Sequence" award in the Crunchyroll Anime Awards 2021.
