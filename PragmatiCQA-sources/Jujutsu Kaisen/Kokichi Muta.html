Kokichi Muta ( 与 ( むた ) 幸 ( こう ) 吉 ( きち ) , Muta Kōkichi ) is a supporting character in the Jujutsu Kaisen series. He was a second-year at Kyoto Jujutsu High using Ultimate Mechamaru as his proxy to attend school. Kokichi himself could not due to the sickly and fragile body he was born with as a result of his Heavenly Restriction .

Appearance

Kokichi is a slim young man with light peach skin and sharp brown eyes. He had black hair tied into a top knot above his head and the majority of his body was obstructed by the bindings of his Heavenly Restriction. His entire body was scarred with grotesquely thin wrinkled skin that he dressed in bandages. His right forearm was missing entirely and both legs were too weak to support his weight beneath the knees. Immobile, Kokichi sat inside a bathtub attached to several tubes inside a remote cave.

Following his healing at the hands of Mahito 's Idle Transfiguration , Kokichi obtained a healthy body. Only a single scar on his cheek remained and he was able to stand up on his own. Like his Mechamaru stand-in at school, Kokichi donned a normal jujutsu high uniform with a white puffy scarf-like cloth around the collar of it.

Personality

Kokichi was a reclusive and reserved individual who resented being born without a future outside of the jujutsu world. He proudly admitted he'd give back every ounce of cursed energy to gain a normal body.

Kokichi's resentment lead to him projecting on those undeserving. During the Goodwill Event , Panda suggested he and Mechamaru should get along since they're both cursed corpses . Kokichi took exception to this and thought of Panda as nothing more than just a doll. This was because Panda got to live free in the sun while Kokichi was trapped inside, ill with his restriction.

Around his classmates, Kokichi embraced the quiet robotic type persona while acting as a student through Mechamaru. He wanted to walk amongst them one day in spite of his condition and however they might have treated him. Kokichi felt especially close to Kasumi Miwa , who confessed to sharing romantic feelings with.

In order to achieve his dream, Kokichi was willing to engage in a Binding Vow with the enemy in order to gain a healthy body. He leaked information with them but included the conditions that they couldn't hurt his fellow Kyoto students in the contract. Kokichi attempted to kill Mahito and Pseudo-Geto after his body was healed. Fighting desperately to achieve his goal, Kokichi bet everything on his planning and determination. However, his desperation, unfortunately, lead to his demise before he could meet his classmates and Kasumi.

Abilities and Powers

Overall Skill : Kokichi Muta was a semi-grade 1 jujutsu sorcerer while he was still a student at Jujutsu High. He could control several puppets at once remotely from a great distance, with each puppet possessing high combat abilities worthy enough to earn him his rank.

In order to defeat Mahito, Kokichi was prepared to unleash his entire life's worth of reserves of cursed energy to win. Pseudo-Geto acknowledged that Mahito's skill and strategies were exceptional in this battle. It was nearly enough to exorcise the special grade curse or as Mahito puts it, Kokichi puts too much stock into his strategy. He was ultimately defeated because Mahito was able to lure him into a false sense of security after believing he had achieved his objective.

Great Tactical Intellect : Kokichi was a highly tactical thinker and fighter who tried to always stay a step ahead of his opponent. While in combat with Panda, Kokichi ascertained Panda's strengths and weaknesses as well as his own at every stage of the fight. Pseudo-Geto also praised Kokichi for having decent tactical prowess while fighting Mahito. Kokichi was well prepared for their fight and his tactics nearly succeeded in wearing Mahito out and damaging his body while neutralizing his technique.

Jujutsu

Cursed Energy Manipulation
Immense Cursed Energy : As a result of his Heavenly Restriction, Kokichi's cursed energy was vast enough to cover the entire nation of Japan. He can exceed the limits of his cursed energy output and use his Puppet Manipulation across an immensely wide range. While piloting Mechamaru Mode: Absolute , Kokichi was prepared to use all seventeen years, five months, and six days of cursed energy he collected over his entire lifespan. Energy charges condensed from years' worth of cursed energy discharge at special grade output levels, even if only momentarily.
Heavenly Restriction
Kokichi was born without a right arm and both of his legs were useless. He had no feeling below his waist, his skin was so sensitive it couldn't be touched by natural light and the pores on his body always felt like they were being stabbed with needles. In exchange, he was granted boundless amounts of cursed energy that spreads over a vast range.
Innate Technique
Puppet Manipulation ( 傀儡操術 , Kairai Sōjutsu ) : Kokichi's technique allowed him to remotely control cursed corpse puppets over a wide range. Puppets such as Mechamaru are capable of using discharging cursed energy as heat rays among a bevy of other abilities.
Puppets
Ultimate Mechamaru ( 究極メカ丸 , Arutimetto Mekamaru ) : A mechanical cursed corpse that Kokichi originally used as his proxy to attend school and missions.
Sword Option ( 刀源解放 , Sōdo Opushon ) : Multiple blades protrude from Mechamaru's forearm, along with his nails extending. Mechamaru is also capable of increasing the size of his forearm and having spikes protrude of it.
Ultra Spin ( 絶技抉剔 , Urutora Supin ) : Mechamaru spins his hand and forearm, and swings his right arm at the target. This technique is capable of easily piercing through a tree trunk.
Ultra Shield ( 剣山盾 , Urutora Shīrudo ) : Mechamaru puts up his guard, with his forearm in front of him, to block incoming attacks.
Boost On ( 推力加算 , Būsuto On ) : Mechamaru releases cursed energy from his elbow to increase the speed of his attack.
Ultra Cannon ( 大祓砲 , Urutora Kyanon ) : Mechamaru fires a blast of cursed energy from the palm of his hand.
Mode: Albatross ( 砲呪強化形態 , Mōdo Arubatorosu ) : Mechamaru protrudes a cannon from his mouth.
Ultimate Cannon ( 三重大祓砲 , Arutimetto Kyanon ) : Mechamaru fires a blast of cursed energy from his mouth.
Ultimate Mechamaru - Mode: Absolute ( 究極メカ丸絶対形態 , Arutimetto Mekamaru Mōdo Abusoryūto ) : A large mechanical puppet that Kokichi is capable of piloting from the cockpit in the puppet's head. It is capable of large scale attacks by using up cursed energy that was saved beforehand.
Ultra Cannon ( 大祓砲 , Urutora Kyanon ) : Kokichi fires a blast of cursed energy from the palm of the puppet's hand. This technique consumes one year's worth of cursed energy that Kokichi has saved up.
Miracle Cannon ( 二重大祓砲 , Mirakuru Kyanon ) : Kokichi fires a blast of cursed energy from both of his puppet's hands. This technique consumes two years' worth of cursed energy that Kokichi has saved up.
Pigeon Viola ( 追尾弾～五重奏～ , Bijon ~Biora~ ) : Kokichi creates 5 orbs made of cursed energy and blasts from them when released. The blasts follow the target until they come in contact with something. This technique consumes five years' worth of cursed energy that Kokichi has saved up.
During the Shibuya Incident , Kokichi had small Communication Puppets prepared to guide his sorcerer comrades. These devices were running on the remains of Kokichi's cursed energy after his death. Once the energy ran out, they deactivated.
Barrier Techniques
Technique Charge - Simple Domain (簡易領域 Kan'i Ryōiki ): Kokichi invented four tubes that contain the Simple Domain cursed technique. It was the only way he could make the Domain work. The tubes are entered into a panel and activated with a Technique Charge. From there, Mechamaru can shift his fingers into spikes or shooting barrels that can inject Simple Domain's effect into Kokichi's desired target. It can also be used to protect the cockpit from an enemy Domain Expansion.
Binding Vows
Kokichi Muta had a Binding Vow with Mahito, promising to give them information as a mole in exchange for a healthy body. One of the conditions Kokichi had was for Mahito's group to not hurt anyone from Kyoto Jujutsu High.
