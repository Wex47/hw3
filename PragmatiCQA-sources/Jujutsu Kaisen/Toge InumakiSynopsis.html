History

Cursed Child Arc

Toge is with Maki Zenin and Panda , as they discuss the new transfer student that they will be getting. Later, when Yuta Okkotsu is introduced to the class, Toge notices that he's cursed and prepares to attack. Satoru Gojo warns them, but Rika Orimoto shows up and defends Yuta from the three. After Gojo explains Yuta's situation, he introduces Yuta to the class. Toge is then paired with Panda during the class session.

Three months later, Toge is watching Maki and Yuta train when Gojo shows up. After a little while, Toge and Yuta are given a mission to attend to. Before they can leave, Gojo explains Toge's ability to Yuta and what he is capable of. Toge and Yuta are then driven to the Hapina Shopping district by Kiyotaka Ijichi . Once they arrive, Kiyotaka explains the details of the mission, but Toge parts ways with Yuta to buy some throat medicine.

Toge and Yuta head into the shopping district, where they find a large amount of cursed spirits. Toge easily exorcises the spirits and leaves. Toge and Yuta notice that the screen has not been lifted, when suddenly a cursed spirit appears behind them. As the spirit attacks, Toge pushes Yuta out of the way and easily crushes the spirit's arm. As Toge starts to cough, Yuta saves him and brings him to safety, but leaves his throat medicine behind. After a short rest, Toge prepares to face the spirit again. Yuta decides to join Toge even though Toge doesn't want him to. Yuta faces the spirit and manages to get the throat medicine to Toge. After Toge drinks the medicine, he uses his Cursed Speech to exorcise the spirit.

After the mission is complete, Toge and Yuta head back to the college. At the college, Toge tends to some plants while Panda tells Yuta that Toge was worried about him. Days later, Toge is seen with Panda, Maki and Yuta when Suguru Geto and his forces show up. Toge listens as Geto explains to Yuta that he wishes to kill all non-sorcerers. As Geto is forced to leave, Toge listens to him announcing his plan for an all-out war.

On December 24, Toge is in Shinjuku to fight against Geto's forces. Suddenly Gojo tells them that Geto is back at the college, and uses his power to send him and Panda back to the college. After arriving at the college, Toge and Panda take on Geto but are easily overpowered. Yuta shows up and saves Toge, Panda, and Maki while also healing them. After Yuta manages to defeat Geto, Toge along with Maki and Panda check to see if he is okay. Once Rika's curse is suddenly undone, Yuta listens as Gojo shows up and explains how Yuta had accidentally cursed Rika when she had died. Yuta also listens when Gojo revealed that Yuta is a distant relative of his. Later, Toge is with Maki and Panda as they wait for Yuta and Gojo.

Synopsis

Cursed Training Arc

Toge, along with Maki and Panda, greets first-years Megumi Fushiguro and Nobara Kugisaki when they're mourning over Yuji Itadori 's death. The three talk with Megumi and Nobara about joining the Kyoto Goodwill Event, which the two agree to.

Toge, along with Maki and Panda, helps train Megumi and Nobara before the event. Later when Megumi talks with the others about how he should carry weapons without occupying his hands, Toge points out that Megumi has managed to figure out a way to with his shadow.

Days later, Toge is with Maki and Panda when Panda worries about the first years since the some of the Kyoto students might accompany the Kyoto principal. Toge and Panda then come to Megumi's rescue, with Toge forcing Aoi Todo to stop with his Cursed Speech when Megumi is attacked by the latter.

Kyoto Goodwill Event Arc

On the day of the Kyoto Goodwill Event, Toge is with the other Tokyo students when the Kyoto students and faculty show up. Toge is also present when Gojo reveals to everyone that Yuji is still alive. Toge then attends a pre-event meeting with the other Tokyo student and discuss their plans for the event.

When the event starts, Toge travels with the others in a group. Once Aoi shows up, the group splits up and Toge joins Nobara and Panda. Toge then talks with Panda and Nobara about how the Kyoto students might be trying to kill Yuji, and heads out to face the cursed spirits while Panda and Nobara take on the Kyoto students.

Later Toge is given Mechamaru 's phone, which he uses to call Kasumi Miwa and put her to sleep. Toge suddenly notices a ominous energy and encounters Hanami . Toge battles against Hanami, until they come across Noritoshi Kamo and Megumi and forces them to run away. Toge then continues to fight Hanami, along with Noritoshi and Megumi.

Toge uses his power to stop Hanami, so that the other two can attack and then run away. As the three prepare another attack, Toge's throat gives away, allowing Hanami to attack Noritoshi. As Megumi attempts to attack Hanami, Toge stops him and uses the last of his strength to blast away Hanami. Toge is then taken outside of the screen that is surrounding the site by Momo Nishimiya along with Noritoshi.

After the intruders have been dealt with, Toge agrees with the other that they should continue the event, where they play baseball.

Shibuya Incident Arc

Toge arrives in Shibuya to help Yuji by dealing with the mutated humans, and letting Yuji enter the Shibuya Station. He is last seen watching over a crowd of people who are later killed by Sukuna 's Domain Expansion . Toge has been revealed to have lost his left arm in Sukuna's attack.
