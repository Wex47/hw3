History

Mahito was born from the hate and fear humans have for each other.

At some point Mahito enacted a binding vow with Kokichi Muta , in which Mahito would heal Kokichi. In return, Kokichi would act as mole within Jujutsu High and supply them with information.

Synopsis

Cursed Training Arc

Mahito is first seen sitting under an umbrella on a beach chair within Dagon 's Domain Expansion and reading a book when Pseudo-Geto first arrives. Mahito agrees with Geto, about sealing Satoru Gojo on a set date that he will contact them with.

Vs. Mahito Arc

At a later day, Mahito meets Junpei Yoshino after having killed three people at a theater. He then heads into the sewer with Junpei, where Mahito explains to Junpei what cursed spirits are. Junpei asks what kind of spirit Mahito is, to which Mahito replies that he is a cursed spirit that was created from people fearing other people. Mahito and Junpei then talk with about human emotion while Mahito shows Junpei his human experiments.

Later Mahito encounters Kento Nanami , and the two start to fight. After a little bit, Mahito starts to explain his abilities. The two continue to fight, Mahito notices that Nanami's power is increasing after it hits 6 o'clock. As Mahito prepares to continue fighting, Nanami escapes by burying Mahito in rubble. Mahito manages to escape from the rubble after sometime and meets with Geto.

After Junpei's mother is killed, Mahito shows Junpei the cursed object that caused it and suggest that it was the classmate that was bullying Junpei that did it. Mahito then help Junpei prepare to attack his classmates.

Mahito shows up during the fight between Yuji Itadori and Junpei. Mahito restrains Yuji, and uses his abilities to transforms Junpei. Mahito then laughs when Sukuna refuses to help Yuji. Mahito continues to laugh even after Junpei dies, at which point Yuji punches him. Mahito notices that there was something different about that punch and realizes that Yuji can naturally injure him. Mahito then takes on Yuji, and manages to force Yuji to the courtyard. Mahito manages to pin Yuji down and tries to force Yuji to switch with Sukuna, but Sukuna forces Mahito out of his territory and tells him there won't be another chance.

Mahito and Yuji continue to fight, until Nanami shows up and attacks Mahito. Mahito then takes on both Yuji and Nanami, and manages to dodge most of their attacks. Mahito manages to distracts Yuji and restrain Nanami. As Mahito prepares to finish Nanami off, Yuji shows up and hit Mahito. Mahito is then repeatedly hit by both Yuji and Nanami, until he uses his Domain Expansion to trap Nanami. Mahito prepares to attack Nanami but when Yuji forcibly enters his territory, Mahito is wounded by Sukuna. Mahito uses the last of his power to distract Yuji and escape from the area. Mahito then recovers in the sewer, and thinks about how powerful Sukuna is and how he will kill Yuji next.

Kyoto Goodwill Event Arc

Later Mahito and Geto meet up with Jogo. Mahito talks with Geto and Jogo about how he is for Geto's plans and how they will retrieve the fingers that are at the Tokyo College. Mahito later attends another meeting with the others about how they will invade the Tokyo College.

During the Kyoto Goodwill Event, Mahito shows up at the site along with Hanami , Juzo Kumiya , and Haruta Shigemo . Mahito heads over to where Sukuna's fingers are at, while the others distract the sorcerers. Mahito manages to obtain the fingers, along with three of the Death Painting Wombs. Mahito then meets up with Hanami and Haruta, where he helps a wounded Hanami leave the site.

Death Painting Arc

Days later, Mahito feeds the Death Painting Wombs to humans, in order to give them bodies. Later Mahito is playing a board game along with Geto and Choso , when Choso reveals that his brothers are dead. Mahito is more concerned by the fact that Choso had destroyed a piece, but smile when he learns that Yuji and his classmate are the ones who are responsible.

Yuji kills the duplicate only to see Mahito transfigure Nobara's face, blasting it open and destroying one of her eyes

With his new form, Mahito decides that killing Yuji will mark the start of his true life. His overwhelming power and impenetrable skin place Mahito on an entirely new level.
