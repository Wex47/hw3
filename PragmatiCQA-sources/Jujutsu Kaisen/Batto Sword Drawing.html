Description

An iai sword-drawing technique optimal for striking enemies situated directly in front of the user. It covers the blade with cursed energy to greatly improve the drawing speed of the blade. Batto Sword Drawing is the fastest technique within the New Shadow Style.

Usage

Kasumi Miwa

Kasumi had learned this technique sometime before the 2018 Kyoto Sister-School Goodwill Event . She first used it in combination with Simple Domain against Yuji Itadori , when the Kyoto students first ambushed him. However, Sukuna's vessel managed to dodge it, much to her surprise. She then unsuccessfully attempted to use it against Maki Zenin before being defeated. She used it for the third time against Pseudo-Geto , but this time the attack was blocked by him, breaking her sword.

Atsuya Kusakabe

Kusakabe seems to be an experienced user of the New Shadow Style, so his uses of this technique were so far more successful. He first used it against two curse users during the Shibuya Incident, killing them.
