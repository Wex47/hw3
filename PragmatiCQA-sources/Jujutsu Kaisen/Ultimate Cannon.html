Ultimate Cannon ( 三重大祓砲 ( アルティメットキャノン ) , Arutimetto Kyanon ) is a Puppet Manipulation extension technique that is used by Kokichi Muta while using Ultimate Mechamaru .

Description

Mechamaru's strongest move, Ultimate Cannon releases a massive blast of cursed energy fired from Mode: Albatross 's mouth cannon.

Weaknesses

Mechamaru cannot fire off Ultimate Cannon consecutively due to the incredible amount of cursed energy it uses.

Usage

Ultimate Cannon is meant to be a finishing move that completely annihilates Mechamaru's opponent. By immobilizing the opponent and firing it at close range, Mechamaru believes he can ensure victory. However, if the target survives then Mechamaru's cursed energy reserves will be depleted.
