Ultra Shield ( 剣山盾 ( ウルトラシールド ) , Urutora Shīrudo ) is a Puppet Manipulation extension technique used by Kokichi Muta while remotely piloting Ultimate Mechamaru .

Description

While Mechamaru's arm is utilizing Sword Option , Ultra Shield causes several small spikes to protrude from his arm, granting him defensive capabilities. The spikes will stick into anyone who strikes the arm with a physical attack.

Usage

Kokichi used this technique against Panda . However, it was ineffective against the latter's physical abilities .
