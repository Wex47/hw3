Fumihiko Takaba ( 髙 ( たか ) 羽 ( ば ) 史 ( ふみ ) 彦 ( ひこ ) , Takaba Fumihiko ) is a character in the Jujutsu Kaisen series. He is a failing comedian who was turned into a sorcerer by Kenjaku in order to participate in the Culling Game .

Appearance

Fumihiko is a tall man with his short dark hair slicked back and tapered sideburns. He wears a typical business suit, a white shirt under a black jacket, black pants and a checkered tie.

Personality

Fumihiko is an exceptionally goofy individual who lives to entertain above all else. He's very outspoken and comedy-focused in everything he does. While Fumihiko is the only person who thinks he's fun, he'll still tell jokes in serious situations that will get no laughs in response. This is why Fumihiki is a failing comedian. Despite the awkwardness or seriousness of any situation, Fumihiko will always try to entertain anyone who's around.

In the deadly Culling Game, Fumihiko never appears phased by the fact he's participating in a deathmatch. He helped Megumi simply because he's being ambushed and despite the stark contrast in their personalities, they were able to work well together. Fumihiko claims he can tell if someone is good or bad by their face and chose to help Megumi anyway despite having a "bad one". While fighting against Iori Hazenoki , Fumihiko continued to make jokes until the former eventually gave up dealing with him.

Synopsis

Perfect Preparation Arc

Fumihiko is at a comedy club where he closes his show to a bored audience who slowly clap. In the dressing room, his manager scolds him for his poor performance and tell Fumihiko he should quit comedy or find something else, saddening him. His coworker, Ken tells him his jokes aren't bad, but there are two types of comedians, ones who are funny and ones who think they're funny. He asks which of them is he, and Fumihiko replies he's about 50:50, but then rephrases by saying 70:30.

Tokyo No. 1 Colony Arc

When Megumi Fushiguro is cornered by Reggie Star and Iori Hazenoki , Takaba arrives at the last minute and protects the teenager, taunting his opponents and calling them cowards for going two against one.

Abilities and Powers

Takaba's power is Comedian . When he comes up with something that he's certain will be funny, it becomes a reality. It's a cursed technique capable of opposing even Satoru Gojo's. But when it comes to his own cursed technique, he's utterly clueless.
