Kyoto Prefectural Jujutsu High School ( 京 ( きょう ) 都 ( と ) 立 ( りつ ) 呪 ( じゅ ) 術 ( じゅつ ) 高 ( こう ) 等 ( とう ) 専 ( せん ) 門 ( もん ) 学 ( がっ ) 校 ( こう ) , Kyōto-ritsu Jujutsu Kōtō Senmon Gakkō ) , commonly referred to as Kyoto Jujutsu High , is the sister school of Tokyo Jujutsu High . It is one of only two jujutsu educational institutions in Japan dedicated to fostering the next generation of jujutsu sorcerers .

Description

While the school itself has yet to physically appear in the series, Kyoto Jujutsu High's relevance is ever-present.

Alongside Tokyo Jujutsu High, the sister schools are considered to be the pillars of the jujutsu community in Japan. They are equally responsible for training students and assigning missions to jujutsu sorcerers.

Location

As made obvious by the school's name, Kyoto Jujutsu High is located in jujutsu's sacred land of Kyoto.

School Traditions

Kyoto Sister-School Goodwill Event : Every year both campuses of the Jujutsu Schools hold a competition between two teams comprised of their first, second, and third-year students. The competition takes place on the campus of the previous years winner.
