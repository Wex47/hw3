Larue ( ラルゥ , Laruu ) is a character in the Jujutsu Kaisen series and its prequel Jujutsu Kaisen 0: Jujutsu High . He was a curse user and one of Suguru Geto 's commanders. Like Miguel , Larue only wanted to see Suguru become king and unaligned himself with the other curse users after his death. Larue has since become an ally of Yuki Tsukumo .

Appearance

Larue is a large man with a muscular physique with light-colored eyes and mid-length blonde hair kept down by a blue headband. He also has two large hearts on his pectoral muscles where his nipples should be. The only clothing Larue wears is a pair of loose pants. He prefers to remain shirtless and barefoot.

Personality

Not much is known about Larue's personality other than he shares Suguru Geto's ideology and considers all members of their group as family. He stopped a squabble between Nanako and the other remnants of their group because Suguru wouldn't want his family fighting. Larue didn't believe either side was wrong and told everyone to go their separate ways. He made it clear that he just wanted Suguru to become king and promised the day will come when they reunite.
