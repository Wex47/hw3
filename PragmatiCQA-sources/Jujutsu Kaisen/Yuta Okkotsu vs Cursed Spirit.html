Yuta Okkotsu vs. Cursed Spirit is an exorcism performed by Tokyo Jujutsu High second-year student Yuta Okkotsu . It that takes place in the desolate remains of Shibuya when Yuta rescues a little girl from an anglerfish-like cursed spirit .

Prologue

Following the Shibuya Incident , all twenty-three wards of Tokyo have become a no-mans-land and a haven for demons.

One little girl stranded in the city is hungry and takes what's leftover at an abandoned convenience store. A strange person appears at the door and talks to the girl in a very peculiar manner. She offers them a drink and walks outside to give it to them.

As soon as the little girl takes one step outside the door, a large cursed spirit with sharp teeth attacks her. It's a curse that uses its tail to manipulate a corpse-like figure to communicate and lure its prey. The curse's tail pulls the "person" away just before the little girl hands over the drink. She looks to the side and sees the fish-like monster open its mouth to consume her in a single bite.

Battle

Suddenly, Yuta Okkotsu comes down from above and stabs the cursed spirit with his sword. He descends with enough strength and weight to forcibly smash the curse's mouth closed and crack the street below. The impact of his strike appears to crush the cursed spirit into submission and fortunately only knocks the little girl down.

Having rescued the girl in time, Yuta takes his sword out of the curse and apologizes for scaring her. He asks if she's hurt and if she's with anyone like her parents. The child isn't very sure what her situation is. Yuta knows this area of Shibuya is a commercial district and doubts that the girl is from anywhere around here.

While Yuta tries to encourage his new companion, the cursed spirit abruptly springs back to life behind him. The curse tries to take its hands around Yuta so it can consume him with its sharp teeth, but it disappears instantaneously. The large curse's sudden disappearance is accompanied by a loud splattering sound. Aware the little girl can see curses, Yuta covers her eyes and guides her away from the only remains of the cursed spirit, a massive bloodstain that covers the building behind them.

Aftermath

After successfully exorcising the cursed spirit, Yuta returns to Jujutsu High and presents himself before the higher-ups. Yuta is known to have ties to Satoru Gojo and he only recently returned from overseas. For these reasons, the higher-ups decided to test his loyalty. However, the conservative members of authority arent' convinced Yuta will follow their commands regardless of how many exorcisms he performs

Yuta offers to enter a binding vow and swears to kill Yuji Itadori for severing Toge Inumaki 's left arm in Shibuya. Jujutsu headquarters promptly releases a notice that Yuji Itadori's death sentence is re-instated and is to be carried out immediately. It also reveals that special grade jujutsu sorcerer Yuta Okkotsu has been officially appointed the executioner.
