History

Maki was born into the Zenin family as the daughter of Ogi Zenin and the older twin sister of Mai Zenin . Due to her inability to see cursed spirits, Maki was often seen as the bravest of the two twins. Maki wanted to become stronger even with her inability to see cursed spirits, so she left the Zenin family in order to hone her skills, but only after declaring she will become the head of the family someday. However, the Zenin clan sought to stifle her efforts at every stage, going as far as to prevent her promotion from a grade 4 sorcerer, despite her obvious talent.

Synopsis

Cursed Child Arc

Maki, Toge Inumaki and Panda are seen talking about their incoming fourth classmate of the first year, Yuta Okkotsu . Yuta finally arrives and introduces himself to them, but Maki seems unimpressed with him and proceeds to berate him, causing Rika Orimoto to attack her and the others. Later Maki is assigned with Yuta on a mission, but are attacked by a very powerful cursed spirits due to Suguru Geto 's scheming. In great danger, Maki questioned Yuta for his purpose and encouraged him to use the power of Rika.

After that, Yuta held the injured children and Maki ran to safety. After the incident, Maki started to train Yuta in melee combat. Panda mischievously tried to get the two together when he saw them started to get along well. Suguru Geto finally approached Yuta, insulted Maki and declared war at the college.

Right before war, Maki explained to Yuta about her lack of curse power and her motivation as a Sorcerer. Suguru then brutally wounds Maki to the point of death, and then injures Panda and Toge as well. Yuta then tried his best to heal them and manages to defeat Suguru. After the war, Yuta released the curse on Rika, finding him to be a very talented sorcerer, Yuta remained as a classmate with his friends.

Cursed Training Arc

A year later, Maki approaches first-years Megumi Fushiguro and Nobara Kugisaki , questioning them why are they unhappy. From a far distance, Panda whispers to her that their classmate Yuji Itadori recently died. This shocks Maki, causing her to lash out at Panda and Toge for not telling her earlier.

Regardless of that, she requests the first-years to consider participating in the Kyoto Goodwill Event, explaining the overall structure of the event to them. Motivated to become stronger for the sake of their fallen friend, Megumi and Nobara accept Maki's request to join the Kyoto Goodwill Event.

Maki along with Toge and Panda, help train both Megumi and Nobara before the event, she then challenges Megumi so he can improve his close-range fighting capabilities. Later when Megumi talks with the others about how he should carry weapons without occupying his hands, Toge points out that Megumi manages to figure out a way with his shadow.

Later, when principal Yoshinobu Gakuganji came to Tokyo, Aoi Todo and Maki's twin sister, Mai Zenin were brought along as well. They provoked Megumi and Nobara, who were sent by Maki to do errands. While Megumi confronted Aoi Todo, Mai started to attack Nobara. The second-years manage to intervene these fights. Mai and Maki mock each other for both their lack of talent. As Maki continues to provoke Mai, this allows Nobara to sneak attack the latter, putting her in a chokehold. Afterwards, Maki explains to Nobara about her family situation. Nobara then grows to admire Maki for remaining strong even though the latter's family had disowned her.
