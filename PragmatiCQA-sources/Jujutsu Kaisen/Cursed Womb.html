Description

Cursed wombs that grow and change shape are expected to produce special grade cursed spirits. A cursed womb can be created from one of Sukuna 's fingers. After enough time has passed, a cursed womb created with one of Sukuna's fingers can produce a special grade cursed spirit called a Finger Bearer .

In Dagon 's case, he was a sentient cursed womb that resembled a cursed spirit. After being attacked and going through the emotional trauma of losing a comrade , Dagon was able to shed his cursed womb form and realize a much more powerful state of being.

Death Painting Womb

A Cursed Womb: Death Painting ( 呪 ( じゅ ) 胎 ( たい ) 九 ( く ) 相 ( そう ) 図 ( ず ) , Jutai Kusōzu ) is a type of cursed womb that is half-human, half cursed spirit . It is unclear how they are formed precisely. In the Meiji era, the jujutsu sorcerer Noritoshi Kamo experimented on the nine aborted fetuses from a girl who could give birth to children who were half curse. The first three— Choso , Eso , and Kechizu — were strong enough to become special grade curses.

Trivia

  • Kusōzu ( 九 ( く ) 相 ( そう ) 図 ( ず ) , Kusōzu literally "Nine Phase Diagram") is a Buddhist series of nine paintings that depict the stages of death and decomposition of a corpse.
