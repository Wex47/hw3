Executed with the goal of sealing Satoru Gojo inside Prison Realm , the Shibuya Incident takes place on October 31, 2018. The conflict spans several stages and encompasses numerous battles to decide the future of Japan.

Prologue

Having failed to get rid of the the Six Eyes numerous times in the past, Kenjaku switched his method from ending the life of the Six Eyes user to sealing them away permanently. He began his search for Prison Realm because two user's of the Six Eyes cannot appear at the same time but killing one will just result in another appearing eventually. Kenjaku acquired Prison Realm at some point in 2012 and took over Suguru Geto 's body after his death in 2017.

Now "Pseudo-Geto", the curse user allies himself with Mahito 's group of unregistered special grade cursed spirits . In July of 2018, Geto meets with Jogo , Hanami , and Dagon to discuss their mutual interests. Jogo expresses their group's desire to replace humanity and take their place as the true rulers of the world. He asks Geto for help defeating the jujutsu sorcerers in an all out war. The curse user advises that they'll need to meet certain conditions to win such a war, the primary of which is taking Satoru Gojo out of the equation completely.

Geto suggests that together they focus on trying to seal Satoru Gojo inside the special grade cursed object : Prison Realm. Jogo initially refuses and tries to kill Satoru himself in exchange for Prison Realm. Naturally, Jogo is effortlessly defeated and Geto uses this exchange to emphasize that Satoru must be sealed at the right time and place using every advantage they have. Following the battle, Geto tells Mahito and everyone else that they'll proceed on October 31st in Shibuya, to which they agree on.

The alliance spends their time preparing for October bolstering their numbers by recruiting curse users like Haruta Shigemo and Juzo Kumiya . In order to prepare themselves adequately for sealing Satoru, Geto used the recruits to aid Mahito and Hanami's invasion of Jujutsu High 's Goodwill Event . The goal of the invasion is to acquire several cursed objects from Jujutsu High's cursed warehouse.

Mahito successfully steals six of Sukuna 's fingers and Cursed Womb: Death Paintings numbers one to three. Choso , Eso , and Kechizu were successfully incarnated from the cursed womb's and added to Mahito's faction. In September, the Death Painting brothers attempt to collect more of Sukuna's fingers in preparation for Shibuya.

In order to stay a step ahead of Jujutsu High while planning their scheme, Geto and Mahito forged a binding vow with one of their students. They to heal Kokichi Muta 's body with Mahito's Idle Transfiguration in exchange for Kokichi acting as their spy. He watched everything that took place over the last several months and fed Geto information that helped his cause. Geto even wanted to use Kokichi for Shibuya but their business concludes on October 19th.

Kokichi feels their vow was broken when Mahito's allies attacked Kokichi's classmates during the invasion of the Goodwill Event. He demands Mahito heal him as per their agreement and afterward, they immediately come to blows and engage in a fight to the death.

During Kokichi's battle with Mahito, his main objective is to contact Satoru Gojo. Kokichi wants Satoru to rescue him, and he also can use the opportunity to warn Satoru about the Shibuya plans. However, Kokichi is unsuccessful. He's unable to reach Satoru and is killed by the special grade curse. Immediately afterward, Geto regroups with Mahito and confirms they've finished putting the final touches on the plan. Just over ten days later, October 31st arrives.

Early Stages

7:00 P.M. - 8:31 P.M. Shibuya (Outside the Curtain)

The Shibuya Incident begins around 7:00 PM on October 31st. A curtain with a four-hundred meter radius is cast around Shibuya. The center of the curtain is at the Tokyu Department Store. It only traps non-sorcerers, making it so civilians can enter but not exit. Sorcerers can enter and exit the curtain at will but it depends on the person when it comes to windows. A secondary effect of the barrier doesn't allow for cellphone reception and it can't be broken by brute force, meaning dispelling the curtain is only possible by taking out its caster.

All the civilians inside the curtain are spread out around its perimeter asking for the same thing: " Bring Satoru Gojo ." An advanced barrier technique and targeting Satoru Gojo points to the culprits being the same as the invaders of the Goodwill Event. The higher-ups instruct Satoru Gojo to work alone in order to suppress the Shibuya Incident and minimize any potential casualties. While some are sure help is on the way, others are on edge after seeing dozens of others sucked into Shibuya Mark City from the outside intersection. His team isn't not permitted to help Satoru Gojo, but rather than remain on standby at the cemetery, the Mei Mei Team is routed to Meiji-Jingumae Station where another curtain has suddenly appeared. Hanami's roots fill the atrium Satoru descended through, creating a blind spot. Satoru can't break it recklessly in the case of people are on the other side. Pseudo-Geto prepared Jogo and the others for this battle by informing them that Satoru is at his strongest when he's alone. Even strong sorcerers get in Satoru's way so Geto and the others planned on using inferior non-sorcerers to restrict his movements and his ability to activate powerful cursed techniques including Domain Expansion . Geto emphasized that he needs the cursed spirits to buy him at least twenty minutes to prepare Prison Realm. In that time, Jogo and the others must force Satoru to waste time thinking about countermeasures and how to save innocent humans.

Satoru quickly figures out his opponents' game and removes his blindfold, revealing the Six Eyes. He taunts the curses for thinking any strategy could give them an advantage and confronts them directly. The strongest jujutsu sorcerer fights and overwhelms Jogo and Hanami with pure taijutsu without the use of the Limitless. He lures Hanami into deactivating his Domain Amplification and then exposes his weak point to severely weaken him. This makes it so the next time Hanami and Jogo attempt to deactivate the Infinity, Satoru simply amplifies his technique to overpower Hanami and exorcises him completely.

8:51 P.M. - 9:15 P.M. Meiji-Jingumae Station

At 8:51 PM in Harajuku, the Mei Mei Team arrives at Meiji-Jinguemae Station just over ten minutes after departing Aoyama Cemetery on foot. They meet with an auxiliary manager at exit 2. She debriefs them on the full extent of the situation they're expected to tackle. A second curtain has appeared with the ability to block out sorcerers from entering B5F of any of the Tokyo Metro stations. This curtain is centered at Meiji-Jinguemae Station and it is believed its caster is taking the risk of remaining outside of the barrier in order to strengthen it. Somewhere in between the curtains is the caster and a swarm if transfigured humans to back them up.

Mei Mei uses her crows to scout out the inside of the Meiji-Jinguemae Station. She finds that transfigured humans are attacking trapped civilians at B4F and forcing them down to B5F. Many of her crows were taken out between B1F and B2F, meaning the caster is most likely in that area. This could be due to the caster wanting to prevent civilians from escaping or a part of the vow strengthening the barrier. Mei Mei is unsure of why B5F is chosen as the cutoff point and Yuji asks if the special grade patch face-curse might be down there.

Mei Mei suggests splitting up so she can rescue the civilians on B4F before they're all killed by transfigured humans. She and Ui Ui head to exit 7 and go straight down to B4F while Yuji descends through exit 2 to find the curtain's caster. Their best-case scenario is to regroup after the curtain has been lifted when the civilians are freed and the objectives of the enemy at BF5 have been discovered.

Yuji descends to B2F and discovers a Grasshopper Curse there devouring a man's head. The Grasshopper considers themselves to be "smart". Based on talking to the curse, Yuji surmises that it's unlikely that such a low intelligence creature would be able to cast a complicated barrier. The Grasshopper also mentions needing to "protect" the curtain, making Yuji realize it's an item responsible for the curtain instead of a caster. He makes it his goal to break the cursed object projecting the barrier and fights the Grasshopper Curse one-on-one. The Grasshopper Curse believes it has a superior body to a human. However, Yuji's skill is so far ahead that he completely overcomes any natural disadvantages. The gap in skill is so wide that Yuji effortlessly exorcises the curse and breaks the stake-like cursed object to dispel the barrier.

Mei Mei kills a group of transfigured humans and secures the civilians on B4F. She notices the curtain lifting and tells Ui Ui they'll wait to regroup with Yuji before heading down to B5F. At the same time on BF5, Mahito gets ready to board a train going from Harajuku to Shibuya Station.

Yuji meets back up with Mei Mei and Ui Ui at B4F at 9:15 PM. They head down to B5F but they find only one person who's already been transfigured. Mahito already boarded the train full of transfigured humans and is headed directly for Satoru Gojo's location.

8:51 P.M. - 9:26 P.M. Fukutoshin Line Platform

After exorcising Hanami, Satoru puts Jogo on the defensive and forces him to hide amongst the crowd of civilians and rely solely on hit-and-run tactics. People who can't see curses start to avoid Satoru and their depleting numbers give Satoru a higher chance of spotting his enemies. Satoru understands he can't save everyone but he swears to exorcise the curses in return. Pseudo-Geto watches the chaos with Dagon from a safe distance away. He comments that it's still too easy for Satoru and they'll need to get him more on edge. At 9:15 PM, Mahito's train arrives on the Fukutoshin Line Platform of Shibuya Station. Frightened civilians try to escape on the newly arrived train. Unbeknownst to them its filled with transfigured humans that attack them as the doors open.

Mahito regroups with Jogo and Choso while numerous transfigured humans chase after the civilians running away in a panic. Satoru doesn't understand why his adversaries would whittle down the number of non-sorcerers even further but Mahito reveals there are plenty more to sacrifice for their cause. The atrium that was being blocked by Hanami's roots suddenly opens up and a massive crowd of people falls through it. Satoru realizes there are more curses or curse users on the upper floors of the Hikarie Building dumping everyone they can down to B5F.

Choso, Mahito, and Jogo use their cursed techniques to attack the crowd along with the transfigured humans. They understand Satoru is capable of dealing with some sacrifices, but only those killed by curses and not himself. His enemies believe this puts Satoru in a situation where he can't activate Domain Expansion at risk of harming any nearby non-sorcerers. However, Satoru proves them wrong and releases an all-or-nothing Domain Expansion of just 0.2 seconds.

Satoru guesses that 0.2 seconds is all a normal human could take inside Unlimited Void without suffering lasting aftereffects. It feeds about half a year's worth of information into everyone's mind, leaving them standing unconscious. This level of Unlimited Void could allow the cursed spirits to awaken at any moment, so Satoru focuses on destroying all the transfigured humans as quickly as possible. The strongest sorcerer annihilates approximately one thousand transfigured humans in 299 seconds.

Immediately after his tremendous feat, Satoru is surprised when Prison Realm appears right next to him. Without wasting any time, Geto commands the cursed object to open. Prison Realm breaks out of the paper talismans constraining it and opens up into a gate-like shape with a giant eye in the center that meets Satoru's gaze. The sorcerer instinctively moves to get away from Prison Realm but Pseudo-Geto intercepts him. This is the first time Pseudo-Geto has revealed his existence to Satoru. Satoru is left speechless at the sight of his former best friend's body functioning despite having killed Geto with his own hands.

Prior to the incident, Geto informed Jogo and the others that they would need to hold Satoru in place for one minute after Prison Realm's gate is opened. Jogo believed this to be an impossible task, but Geto only needs "one minute" of time inside of Satoru's high processing brain. Upon seeing Geto, three years of memories race through Satoru's mind. This gives Prison Realm more than enough time to activate and take hold of him.

Immobilized and unable to access his cursed energy, Satoru understands this is checkmate. He demands to know who this imposter is despite the Six Eyes telling him it's Suguru Geto. The imposter takes off the top of his head and reveals a cursed brain inside. It's a technique that allows the imposter to move from body to body. He took over Geto's body after the Night Parade of a Hundred Demons. Pseudo-Geto fastens his scalp back on and tells Satoru the seal will be over in a thousand years or so, bidding him farewell until they meet again in the new world.

Around 9:26 PM, Geto commands Prison Realm to close its gate, sealing Satoru inside the cubed form of the cursed object. Mahito and the others wake up from their Unlimited Void induced trance and regroup with Geto. He reveals Prison Realm can only contain one person at a time and it needs time to process someone as powerful as Satoru Gojo. In spite of this minor delay, Pseudo-Geto's plan has been successful and the seal is complete.

Geto reveals they won't be able to move Prison Realm while it's processing Satoru. Their conversation is overheard by one of Mechamaru's communication devices. The information is delivered directly to the jujutsu sorcerers. Geto and Mahito both notice the device after already revealing they can't move Prison Realm. Mahito destroys the device, but Geto acknowledges they acted a moment too late, admitting the jujutsu sorcerers got them.

Mechamaru relays the information that Satoru has been sealed. Kokichi explains this communication device is a contingency plan before he was killed by Mahito on October 19th. Yuji and Mei Mei are skeptical of him at first but he manages o convince them he's telling the truth.

Curtain C will block the Mei Mei Team from reaching Satoru Gojo if they continue along the tracks without dispelling it first. Mechamaru asks Yuji to go above around and inform all the teams on standby that Satoru has been sealed. They need to organize a collaborative operation to rescue Satoru Gojo.

There is a curse user on their way to attack the Mei Mei Team. Mechamaru asks Mei Mei to deal with them and open a path for Yuji to head back above ground. The Mei Mei Team agrees to comply with Mechamaru's directions. The curse user Niji Ebina arrives shortly afterward and engages the Mei Mei Team.

While he goes to meet with Ijichi, Nanami orders Ino to take Yuji and Megumi on a mission to dispel the curtain.

The first issue is the Gojo Family will fall from power and all those protected by them will become political targets of the higher-ups again. Secondly, curse users and cursed spirits deterred by Satoru's strength will make their move. If Jujutsu High is too busy with political squabbles, they will be defeated by the influx of adversaries. Yuji and Megumi confirm they understand the severity of their assignment. Ino confidently declares they'll have the curtain lifted before Nanami returns and they'll rescue Satoru Gojo no matter what.

Rising Stages

Ino, Yuji, and Megumi reach the curtain blocking out sorcerers. Attempting to force their way has no effect on the extremely sturdy barrier. Yuji is unable to break through it with brute force, even with a punch at full strength. While his Divine Dog: Totality helps save civilians from transfigured humans, Megumi suggests finding a weak point in the curtain. Ino says that won't be possible without getting inside the curtain first. Yuji questions Ino's line of thinking while also fighting off transfigured humans to help save innocent bystanders.

Based on the basic fundamentals of barrier techniques, Ino believes the caster will be inside the curtain. Yuji informs him of how the curtain worked in Harajuku. With this information, they accurately surmise that the curtain's caster is probably in the most obvious spot nearby. This would give them the greatest amount of payoff in return for the increased risk of being discovered.

The curse users Awasaka , Granny Ogami , and her grandson are posted on top of Shibuya C Tower. Awasaka and Ogami's grandson agree the skyscraper overlooking the entire city is the most obvious spot in Shibuya inside of the curtain. They expect a response from enemy jujutsu sorcerers but the inside of tower is filled with transfigured humans inside to keep them at bay.

Yuji and Ino completely take the curse users off guard by suddenly flying up behind them by riding on the Megumi's shikigami, Nue . Flying on Nue allowed them to fly right to the roof without getting slowed down by transfigured humans. Yuji uses some wrist wires he got from Maki to bind the curse users but Ogami and her grandson slip out of the way. Awasaka is flung clean off the tower and falls dozens of stories to the ground below.

Before they attacked, Yuji informed Ino and Megumi about commissioned curtains. These curtains are imbued with barrier techniques that only need to be charged with cursed energy and protected once they're activated. Ino finds one stake-shaped cursed object sticking out of the ground and breaks it. However, he notices that there were originally three there. Two were distractions and only one maintains the curtain. Just before Awasaka is thrown off the side of the tower, Ino notices the other two curtains in his hand.

While Yuji and Megumi deal with Awasaka on the ground, Ino fights Ogami and her grandson atop the roof. Determined to impress Nanami by fulfilling the role given to him, Ino uses his Auspicious Beasts Summon to go on the offensive. Down below on the highway, Yuji and Megumi pummel Awasaka. However, no matter how much they beat on him, the old man doesn't suffer any significant damage. Meanwhile, Ino quickly realizes the young man is sacrificing his body to protect Granny Ogami from the Auspicious Beasts. This gives Ogami enough time to complete her incantation and activate her Séance Technique , shapeshifting her grandson into Toji Zenin .

Awasaka and Ogami associated themselves with Pseudo-Geto because the birth of Satoru Gojo ruined the prime of their lives. They used to live as curse users who could do as they pleased to make money. Desiring to reclaim this lifestyle by getting rid of Satoru Gojo, Awasaka fights with the determination to retain the prime of his life. Yuji and Megumi continue to struggle with Awasaka's cursed technique, which is somehow nullifying the impact of their strikes. Judging off of Awasaka's reaction to Rabbit Escape and verbal confirmation that he cannot defeat someone like Satoru, Megumi is able to deduce how his cursed technique functions.

While Toji Zenin makes short work of Ino, Yuji and Megumi figure out how to counter Awasaka's " Inverse " cursed technique. By creating openings by relentlessly barraging the old man with a mix of strong and weak attacks that he's forced to invert at the same time. Yuji changes the force of his punch at the last moment in order to fool Awasaka, dealing a devastating blow that incapacitates the curse user.

Megumi and Yuji break the two curtains, dispelling the barrier to complete their assignment from Nanami. Before they're able to regroup with Ino, his body is flung from the top of the tower. Megumi catches Ino before he plummets to his death. He decides to forgo regrouping with Nanami and splits up with Yuji so he can get Ino to Shoko for immediate medical treatment.

On the top of the tower, Ogami asks her grandson to continue killing jujutsu sorcerers throughout Shibuya. However, Toji's personality overwrites his vessel and he quickly turns on Ogami. She always makes sure not to summon the soul's information in case of situations like this, but Toji's body is special. He ironically follows her command to kill sorcerers and murders Ogami with a single strike.

9:27 P.M. - 10:02 P.M. Between Meiji-Jinguemae and Shibuya Station

Just earlier than around 9:27 PM, Mei Mei created the opening for Yuji to escape above ground and engaged Niji Ebina in combat. Since then, she has easily overwhelmed him and severely injured the cursed spirit in his possession. Ebina attempts to surrender and talk his way into escaping. However, Mei Mei doesn't see any value in his service potential and she cuts him down without a second thought. Geto notices the curse user's quick defeat from down the Fukutoshin Line in Shibuya, and is amused by Mei Mei's display of power.

9:22 P.M. - 9:40 P.M. Shibuya Station

Sometime after 9:22 PM, the Zenin Team enters the curtain. They fight to suppress the aggressive transfigured human presence and rescue civilians in Shibuya Station.

Akari informs Nobara that it sounded as if Ijichi was attacked while they were on the phone. This occurred after he split up with the Nanami Team so Nobara understands this could be a serious problem.
