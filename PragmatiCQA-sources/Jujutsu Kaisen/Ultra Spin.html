Ultra Spin ( 絶技抉剔 ( ウルトラスピン ) , Urutora Supin ) is a Puppet Manipulation extension technique used by Kokichi Muta while remotely piloting Ultimate Mechamaru .

Description

While Mechamaru's arm is utilizing Sword Option , he can rotate his wrist quickly to create a piercing drill-like attack.

Usage

In conjunction with Boost On , Ultra Spin's destructive power is enough to blast a hole in a fully grown tree.
