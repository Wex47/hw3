Jujutsu Kaisen Official Fanbook ( 呪術廻戦 ( じゅじゅつかいせん ) 公 ( こう ) 式 ( しき ) ファンブック , Jujutsu Kaisen Kōshiki Fanbukku ) is the second data book for the Jujutsu Kaisen series by Gege Akutami and covers the entire prequel series, as well as the main series from chapters 1 to 133 .

Covert Art and Illustration

The cover artwork features Yuji and Sukuna. They're posted over a black background inside a white square.

Contents

The Fanbook features exclusive information about the series, character profiles, author commentaries, an interview and a special dialogue between Gege Akutami and BLEACH author Tite Kubo.
