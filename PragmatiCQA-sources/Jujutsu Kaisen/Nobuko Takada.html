Nobuko Takada ( 高 ( たか ) 田 ( だ ) 延 ( のぶ ) 子 ( こ ) , Takada Nobuko ) better known as Tall Idol Takada-Chan is a popular Japanese idol, Aoi Todo being one of her biggest fans.

Appearance

Nobuko is a tall young woman with golden eyes and wears her long black hair tied into twin tails with her wispy bangs covering her forehead. She wears stylish clothes, a choker and platform heels.

Personality

Nobuko has a cheerful and upbeat personality. She will gladly pose for her fans and even ask them about their well-being. She manages to persuade even such crude and unshakable people like Mai Zenin .

Synopsis

Cursed Training Arc

Nobuko was having a meet and great in Tokyo. She later meets Aoi Todo , shakes his hand and performs her signature Taka-tan Beam.

Kyoto Goodwill Event Arc

Nobuko appears in Aoi's makeshift memories, where she attended the same middle school as Aoi and Yuji Itadori . Aoi confesses his love to her, but she rejects him, stating she already has someone she likes.

Abilities and Powers

As a normal human being, Nobuko has no additional abilities.
