Takeru ( タケル , Takeru ) is a cursed corpse created by Masamichi Yaga . He was created from the soul information of Kusakabe 's nephew and resides in a forest alongside many other cursed corpses.

Appearance

Takeru is a small anthropomorphic dog-looking cursed corpse with small limbs compared to the rest of his body. He wears a simple tie and black briefs.

As a human, he had light, spiky hair and a band aid on his right cheek. He also wore a light shirt.

Personality

Takeru is a caring and cheerful cursed corpse who cares deeply for his creator: Masamichi Yaga .

History

Perfect Preparation Arc

Takeru is with Masamichi Yaga , sitting on a log in a forest. He worries for Yaga and tries to cheer him on. Yaga states he will going on a long outing and won't be back for a while. Later, Takeru goes with Yaga to meet his wheelchair bound mother. His mother jumps out of her chair and rushes to hug Takeru the moment he says "aren't I a genius", reminiscing on his supposed catchphrase when he was alive.
