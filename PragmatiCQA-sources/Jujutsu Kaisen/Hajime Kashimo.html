Hajime Kashimo ( 鹿紫雲 ( かしも ) 一 ( はじめ ) , Kashimo Hajime ) is a character in the Jujutsu Kaisen series. They are a jujutsu sorcerer from 400 years ago currently inhabiting the body of someone Kenjaku prepared as a vessel. Hajime is currently serving as one of the players participating in the Culling Game .

Appearance

Hajime is a slim person with light-colored eyes that have short zig-zag lines on the edge of their bottom eyelids. They have light-colored hair in a disheveled style. The hair on top of their head is parted and tied into two buns on each side. Hajime sports bangs in front of their face and allows the hair on the back of their head to hang loose as well, falling just below the back of their neck.

Hajime wears simple, light-colored clothing including a large robe with a scrunched collar around the neck area. The sleeves of their robe are slightly rolled up to reveal that Hajime's forearms are wrapped in bandages. Hajime also wear matching pants and shoes of the same color as well.

Personality

Hajime is a perfect example of the type of incarnated sorcerer from the past who lives for fighting strong opponents. They value life differently than modern-day people and want to test their strength against someone powerful. Hajime quickly became bored with the Culling Game after slaughtering over forty players and decided to focus on seeking out Sukuna for a real challenge.

Synopsis

Perfect Preparation Arc

Having killed a male sorcerer, Hajime expresses frustration over how weak the opponents in Tokyo Colony No. 2 are and wonders where Sukuna is. Hajime decides to add a rule to the Culling Game using 100 points from her current total of 200. The 9th Rule of the Culling Game as specified by Hajime allows all players to access information about other players by accessing Kogane . These including; full names, amount of points, number of rules a player has added and their current colony.

Before entering the Culling Game, Yuji's group use the information provided by Hajime's new rule to plan their attack with Kinji Hakari deciding to target Hajime in Colony 2 because they appear to be the strongest fighter in the game.

Abilities and Powers

Overall Skill Level : Little is known about Hajime's abilities. What is known is that Hajime is a powerful sorcerer from the past who was well acquainted with Kenjaku. Hajime agreed to a binding vow with Kenjaku and existed for hundreds of years as cursed object per Kenjaku's plan. For over 400 years, Hajime laid dormant until Kenjaku broke the vow and released them. This allowed Hajime to incarnate into the body of the person Kenjaku had ingest the cursed object.

As per their agreement, Hajime uses this vessel to participate in Kenjaku's Culling Game. They become one of the first players to score over 100 points. At one time, Hajime even had 200 points before spending 100 to add a rule to the game. Accumulating this score means that Hajime killed at least 40 other players in 12 days.

Hajime considered every one of the aforementioned players they eliminated to be far too weak. They openly displayed their strength by outclassing someone of Panda's caliber. Panda stated that Hajime is incredibly fast and strong, with speed, strength, and durability that quickly overpowered Panda's Gorilla Mode. Able to withstand Unblockable Drumming Beat and overcome the cursed corpse with superior-close combat skills, Hajime considered Panda to be extremely weak. Even when faced with Panda's trump card, Kashimo easily overcame his opponent's Triceratops Mode using their electrified cursed energy that struck like a bolt of lightning.

Jujutsu

Cursed Energy Manipulation
Electrified Cursed Energy : Hajime is one of the strongest players in the Culling Game and likely possesses immense reserves of cursed energy. Their cursed energy has the properties of an electric current, putting their body in a constant state of electrification. This makes Hajime's cursed energy reinforced physical attacks impossible to defend against, as they shock the opponent upon impact.
Innate Technique
Electrification : Hajime's innate cursed technique coordinates directly with their electrified cursed energy. Combining their cursed energy's special trait and their cursed technique is what makes Hajime's attacks unblockable. Additionally, Hajime can freely separate the charges of their cursed energy like electricity. By applying the positive charge to their target immediately, Hajime can discharge the negative charge without losing any electricity to the ground. This results in a powerful lightning strike that rends the air and is guaranteed to hit without the need for Domain Expansion. After discharging this attack, Kashimo needs time to recharge before they can fire it again. (Unnamed)

Equipment

Cursed Tools
Bo Staff : Hajime wields a staff that resembles a lightning rod in battle. It's capable of conducting the electrical properties of Hajime's cursed energy making it a suitable weapon for the sorcerer to use in close combat. Hajime generally mixes in polearm attacks along with normal taijutsu, even using the staff in more crude ways like to break off an arm.
