Description

A technique that in which the blood is compressed and condensed to its limit between the palms of user. Then, compressed blood sphere can be used to perform an extension technique. Because it requires time to apply Convergence to blood, user is vulnerable against fast and quick attacks.

Usage

Noritoshi Kamo

Noritoshi Kamo used this to utilize its extension technique against Hanami . Thanks to Toge Inumaki `s Cursed Speech , the special grade cursed spirit is immobilized for a period of time which allowed Noritoshi to bypassthe time requirement to charge the technique.

Choso

Choso, thanks to his half-cursed spirit half-human physiology can use this technique repetitively without exhausting himself. It is one of Choso's most used techniques as it is key charging up his most powerful attacks. He can keep condensed blood sphere around him and manipulate them as he wishes to activate its extension techniques. He is able to use Piercing Blood which is the strongest known technique of Blood Manipulation and also Supernova which is invented by himself. There is a traditional stance for activating Convergence but Choso has used other ways to cast it as well. Choso has also used the stance as a feint to trick opponents into thinking he's about to fire Piercing Blood. Thanks to this versatility he have, he was able to defeat Naoya Zenin who has knowledge on Blood Manipulation and its extension techniques.

Related Techniques
Piercing Blood ( 穿血 , Senketsu ) : After using Convergence, the user fires a long ranged beam of blood with extreme flesh piercing capabilities. Used by Noritoshi Kamo and Choso.
Supernova ( 超新星 , Chōshinsei ) : After using Convergence to create multiple small orbs of blood, the user has the orbs shoot out in every direction like a buckshot. Created and used by Choso.
