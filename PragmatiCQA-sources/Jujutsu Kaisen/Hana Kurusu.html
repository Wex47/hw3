Appearance

Hana appears as a young girl with light colored eyes and short light hair with bangs that curve inward. She wears a large turtleneck sweater, dark pants and heeled ankle boots. She appears to have a halo and a pair of wings.

Personality

Not much is known about her personality.

Synopsis

History

Hana was mentioned to have been alive a thousand years ago.

Perfect Preparation Arc

Hana is seen floating above an area with multiple buildings underneath.

Abilities and Powers

According to Tengen , Hana has a cursed technique that can extinguish any other cursed techniques , even being able to open up the back of a Prison Realm . She is also capable of flight.
