Biography

Mortal Coil

Brobding attended an auction in Deadfall 's pub along with Hieronymus Deadfall, Hokum Pete , Lightning Dave and two other sorcerers. However, Tesseract had attended the auction too, unknown to the others. Tesseract killed all six of the men, including Brobding, in order to get information out of Scapegrace.

Magic and abilities

Brobding, as he was a giant, took a while for Tesseract to kill. First he was accidentally shot twice by Lightning Dave's electricity, then had his foot stabbed by a sorcerer's dagger. Then, Tesseract broke his leg, and then destroyed his ribcage. It is unknown whether Brobding's classification as a 'Giant' was as a species or whether he was merely giant in size.

Trivia

  • Brobding is named after the fictional island of Brobdingnag, an island inhabited by giants in Jonathan Swift's novel, 'Gulliver's Travels'.
