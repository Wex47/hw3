Stentor was one of Kenspeckle Grouse 's first assistants. He was killed when the Grotesquery rose.

Biography

Playing With Fire

Stentor, along with Civet , was an assistant to Kenspeckle Grouse. He was helping Civet move the Grotesquery when it fell on him. Grouse got angry at him, and more so when he said that it moved. The Grotesquery awoke and killed Civet. Stentor tried moving out of the way, but he was too slow and his head was crushed by the Grotesquery's giant hands like an egg.
