Goblins are mischevious creatures. A group of them stole Skulduggery Pleasant's skull when he was meditating. Skulduggery said that goblins love gold, but prefer eating babies.

Goblins have only appeared in " Gold, Babies And The Brothers Muldoon ".

However, the Muldoons were turned into goblins by Skulduggery Pleasant, and only collect gold because it helps them become human again.
