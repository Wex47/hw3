The Darquesse Society , sometimes known as The Darquesse Cult , is a group of sorcerers , presumably all Roarhaven citizens, who gather frequently to discuss Darquesse and attempt to aid in her return. Some members hide the fact that they worship Darquesse and others are hated because of it. Despite the generally negative goal, The Darquesse Society is generally considered a happy group of people. The cult was started in Tantalus's living room.
