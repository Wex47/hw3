Biography

History

Before he was murdered, he and Emmett Peregrine went to see Fletcher Renn , trying to persuade him to let them train him in the art of teleportation, but Fletcher refused.

Light was a powerful teleporter. Skulduggery said that he once saw him teleport a ballroom of people at once, without even batting an eyelid. He was in his home when Batu found him and stabbed him to death in his living room.

The Faceless Ones

After Cameron was murdered by Batu, his body was inspected by Skulduggery and Valkyrie .

Trivia

  • He had many mortal friends, and he had no trouble hiding the fact that he was actually a sorcerer from everyone else.
