Miss Nuncio was an Adept who appeared in Dark Days . She specialised in Linguistics and Etymology. She was able to speak any language that has ever existed.

Biography

Attack at the Midnight Hotel

When Miss Nuncio is staying at the Midnight Hotel, the Revengers' Club attacks the Hotel with Zombies so they can steal a Remnant . Miss Nuncio helps Skulduggery , Valkyrie , and Anton Shudder defeat the Zombie Horde at the Midnight Hotel , but is eaten and dies. She uses, during the fight, one of the legs from the table broken by Sanguine . While Miss Nuncio is holding off four Zombies , she slips on the gore. The zombies descend on her, and after cursing the Zombies in just about twenty different languages, she is killed.

It is said that the only good thing about her dying was that there was not enough of her left for it to come back to life. Miss Nuncio gets angry when it is mentioned that she has not chosen a combat discipline and says that she can still be of use, and she will still defend herself.

Magic

Miss Nuncio doesn't use her Magic as a combat skill. Instead she specialises in Linguistics and Etymology.
