Adedayo Akinde is an untrained teenage sorcerer . The protagonist of Apocalypse Kings , he is unexperienced but relatively powerful with magic , although he can only use magic when he is calm. He is later seen over ten years later in the Nigerian Sanctuary by Valkyrie and Skulduggery .
