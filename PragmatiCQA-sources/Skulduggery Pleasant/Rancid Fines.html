Biography

The Lost Art Of World Domination

Skulduggery Pleasant had been attempting to capture Rancid and his partner, Christophe Nocturnal and to retrieve The Crystal of the Saints from them.

Dead or Alive

Skulduggery and Valkyrie , who had been chasing Fines for thirteen years, finally found he and a Sorcerer named Kiln trying to bring the Faceless Ones back.
