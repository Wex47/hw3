Biography

History

Not much was revealed about his past, but he and his siblings encountered Skulduggery Pleasant and claims that he was the one who turned them into goblins. This causes him to hold a grudge against Skulduggery.

Gold, Babies And The Brothers Muldoon

He and his siblings kidnap three babies in exchange for gold. Valkyrie and Skulduggery manage to defeat them and bring the babies back safely.
