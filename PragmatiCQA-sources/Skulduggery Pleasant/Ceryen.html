Ceryen was a female sorcerer who worked for the Torment . She also was acquainted with Graft , who she regarded as being inferior to her. She was present when Tesseract was tranquilised and buried alive, constantly reminding Graft of his lowliness.

Ceryen then left to meet with two Children of the Spider , Portia and Syc . She was dismissed very soon, and turned to leave. Tesseract broke her leg, making her scream in pain and beg for her life. Amid her screams of yelling that she had a family, Tesseract caved her skull in.
