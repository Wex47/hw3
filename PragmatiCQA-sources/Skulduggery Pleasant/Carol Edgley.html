Carol Edgely is the twin sister of Crystal , and daughter to Fergus and Beryl Edgley . They were jealous of what Valkyrie got from Gordon , and believed that she only lied to get his possessions. Valkyrie called the two the "Toxic Twins".

Biography

Skulduggery Pleasant

Carol attends the funeral of her uncle Gordon and later briefly appears when Valkyrie knocks on their house. She and her twin Crystal start complaining about how Valkyrie got most of Gordon's possessions.

Playing With Fire

Carol and her sister attend the Edgley family reunion. They try to bully Valkyrie but fail to scare her, after they found her in the room they wanted to smoke. They then barely miss looking at Gordon while he was in Echo Stone form.

The Faceless Ones

Carol and Crystal only appear when Tanith comes to speak to Stephanie while she is staying with them. They are eating lunch and snigger when they hear that the reflection was hiding under the bed.

Mortal Coil

The twins are first seen at Christmas party where nobody likes them. Valkyrie pities them, and is actually friendly to them.

Later on, the two see Valkyrie beat up a policeman, who was possessed by a Remnant . Carol and Crystal find out about magic, and Carol starts to have a panic attack. They both fall in love with Fletcher .

Death Bringer

Crystal and Carol first appear at Alison Edgley 's christening.

Later on, they beg Valkyrie to teach them magic . She finally agrees tries to teach them at the beach. They think that magical skills comes instantly, and are surprised when it takes them more than a second to pick up the pebble. Fergus arrives and asks what they are doing. Valkyrie lies and says she is teaching them self-defense. He realises that they are actually attempting magic, so he sends them home.

Kingdom of the Wicked

Carol finally admits to Valkyrie that she has been raised wrong. She confesses to Valkyrie that she feels fat, and her sister is too thin, while her mother and father spoil her too much. She tells Valkyrie that she is learning elemental magic.

Later Carol is seen at her house with Valkyrie's reflection , though Carol doesn't know it is not the real Valkyrie. The Reflection lies to Carol and tells her it will teach Carol magic. It makes a Reflection for Carol and stabs her afterwards. She uses the magic from Carol to fuel the Sceptre of the Ancients , and then fires it on Carol, turning her to dust.
