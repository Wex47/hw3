Biography

Resurrection

Nero teleported into the blind sport of prison with Cadaverous Gant ,then proceeded to teleport Cleavers and mages guarding the prison out and teleporting Lethe and other Anti-Sanctuary mages into the prison.

Nero was next seen teleporting Lethe , Memphis , Razzia among others to fight Valkyrie Cain and Skulduggery Pleasant, where he teleported after them to stab Fletcher Renn in the stomach with his knife before disappearing.

He teleported to and from Corrival Academy with Lethe to meet with the First Wave, where he accidentally teleported Omen Darkly along with him to the prison without realising it.

Powers and Abilities

Magic

Nero is only kept alive because of his unique Teleportation skills. He can follow other teleporters to unknown locations at will. He can also teleport despite sigils preventing others of his kind doing the same, making him able to teleport into Sanctuaries or their associated prisons with ease. He also doesn't need to touch others to teleport them, and if he does, he doesn't have to go with them.

His range of teleportation is unknown, although he appears to be able to teleport more than a few miles at a time.

Combat

Nero carries a knife, which he uses to stab Fletcher Renn at will. He hasn't not shown any great skill with using the knife. His unarmed combat is unknown.

Character

Nero is described as a good-looking thin young man with "shockingly" platinum, bleached hair. Upon meeting him, Valkyrie Cain remarks that she just knew he was the Teleporter. Cadaverous Gant finds him to be whiny. He will obey orders, but not unquestionable. This demonstrates Nero is capable of teamwork and respects certain members of his team, but can still threaten lower ranking members of his team, though its unknown if he was ordered to pass along the message.

He was not above gloating towards his enemies, but will stop when told to by Lethe and seemed to take pleasure in stabbing Fletcher Renn.

Relationships

Cadaverous Gant

Nero and Cadaverous Gant don't like each other. Nero irritates Cadaverous, often unintentionally, and Cadaverous is seen as boring and creepy by Nero. Lethe often has to break things up between them.

Memphis

Nero and Memphis are often partners, and they often fail in their missions. Memphis doesn't completely have faith in Nero, but Nero often shows his of Memphis. Nero blames Memphis to avoid getting in trouble after Memphis accidentally kills a vital Neoteric.

Fletcher Renn

Nero and Fletcher have only encountered each other once, but rivalry has already developed. They both completely underestimated the other which resulted in Nero putting Fletcher out of action for the book, and Fletcher stopping Lethe and Nero from killing Skulduggery and Valkyrie.
