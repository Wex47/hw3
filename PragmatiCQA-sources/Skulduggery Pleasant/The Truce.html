The Truce was an agreement between the forces of Mevolent , who had been killed a few months before and The Council of Elders . Serpine agreed to not partake in any criminal activity and was provided amnesty. Serpine broke the Truce in the first book: Skulduggery Pleasant , but Skulduggery had to take a while to convince the Elders and Grand Mage that he wasn't joking about it. After that, the truce was broken and forgotten about. Those arrested by sanctuary forces before the conclusion of the War, notably Dreylan Scarab and Baron Vengeous , were not freed.
