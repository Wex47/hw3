Kes is a nickname that Valkyrie uses for Darquesse 's ghost. In Resurrection , Kes is seemingly hostile and unfriendly towards Valkyrie, but they both grow more endearing to each other. Kes is described as a "piece Darquesse left behind". Only Valkyrie can see Kes. Kes is a benevolent part of Darquesse, and sometimes helps Valkyrie on her adventures. During Seasons of War , she encourages Valkyrie to be more positive and to stop using The Music Box, comparing it to a drug.
