Biography

Resurrection

Temper first appears on his final mission, which goes horrifically wrong. After Azzedine Smoke corrupts him, he tells them everything he knows and is thrown in a prison cell in Coldheart Prison until Omen Darkly gets him out. Temper joins Omen, Never , and Valkyrie Cain on the Skulduggery Pleasant rescue mission and discovers that Lethe is really Savant Vega .

Powers and Abilities

Temper keeps his magic a secret, but is a pretty good fighter, even taking on Lethe . Like Anton Shudder , Temper is in possession of a gist. Unlike him, he has a lot less control over his. After using it to free himself from the rogue city guards of Roarhaven, he remarks that his next activation will force him to be a part of his gist instead.

In Seasons of War, however, it is revealed that Temper's magical disciple had changed from a Gist to the same Parasites in his hands that Razzia had. When Razzia had died, her Parasites, Hansel and Gretel, had transferred to Temper. He uses them to fight Gleeman Shakespeare .

Relationships

He has been romantically involved with Kierre of the Unveiled , Serafina Dey 's younger sister.

Personality

Temper is laid-back, practical, and calm. He tries to be charming but often fails.

Description

Temper has dark skin and an American accent. He's also mentioned to be young, though for sorcerers this is a flexible term.
