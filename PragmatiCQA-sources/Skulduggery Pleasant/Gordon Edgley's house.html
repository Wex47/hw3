Part of Ireland

Gordon's Estate, also known as Grimwood House, is a big old house in North County Dublin. It was the residence of well-known author Gordon Edgley before his death, when it was passed down to his niece, Valkyrie Cain . For a few years, the only inhabitant was Echo-Gordon , and then it sat empty until Valkyrie returned to Ireland. She now lives in the house with her dog Xena .

History

The house was built hundreds of years ago by a sorcerer named Anathem Mire . Mire studied the Ancients and the Faceless Ones and built the house over the entrance to the Caves of The Void , where he made several expeditions. Just before he died, Mire conjured a replica of the house down in the caves.

Description

The house sits on a hill surrounded by trees, with vast grounds and grand, heavy gates framing the narrow road. The house itself is “ridiculously big” with a great number of rooms, including a ballroom and a gym, and long corridors lined with Gothic paintings. There are also several secret doors and hidden rooms.
