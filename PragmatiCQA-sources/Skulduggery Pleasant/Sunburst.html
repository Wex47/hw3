A Sunburst is a magic defibrillator that is used in The Dying of the Light on Valkyrie Cain and Alice . It revives Valkyrie after Darquesse is expelled from her body. Valkyrie later uses the Sunburst on Alice to revive her after transferring ownership of the Sceptre of the Ancients .

Description

A seven pointed star made of thick metal and inscribed with dozens of delicate symbols. Unlike mortal defibrillators, a Sunburst delivers an electrical shock to both the heart and the nervous system, increasing the chance of survival.
