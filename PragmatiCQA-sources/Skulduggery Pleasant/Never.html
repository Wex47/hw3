Never is a student at Corrival Academy . He/she is in the same year as Omen Darkly and is one of his very few friends at school. He/she is romantically involved with Auger Darkly .

Biography

Early Life

Little is known of Never's early life besides the fact that his/her family lived in Roarhaven during the events of Darquesse 's rampage and his brother was crushed and killed by a building in the carnage. This caused Never to have negative feelings toward Valkyrie in the future.

When Never first started attending Corrival Academy, he/she showed an aptitude for Teleportation and studied under Fletcher Renn.

Skulduggery Pleasant: Resurrection

Towards the beginning of Ressurection , Never and Omen Darkly go about their lives. Two-thirds in, however, Valkyrie comes to Never for help as he can teleport. Never initially refuses, but Omen persuades him/her. Never takes the group to San Francisco where they find a new lead. Never takes Temper Fray to New York, then teleports back to San Francisco right into the middle of a fight between Valkyrie and Gleeman Shakespeare . Panicked, Never teleports an unconscious Omen away first but comes back for Valkyrie.

The Irish Sanctuary reaches out to Never and asks him/her to teleport a group of Cleavers to help Valkyrie Cain. However, Omen calls and urges Never to take him and Temper with her. Never takes the group to Coldheart Prison and back.

Relationships

Never is in a relationship with Auger Darkly .

Personality

Never has been shown to both care and tease Omen, and to gently nudge his/her friend to work harder at school. A particular noteworthy quality of Never is his/her hatred for Valkyrie Cain, caused by Darquesse causing the death of his older brother. Never also seems wary of Skullduggery Pleasant.

Description

Magic

Like all students at Corrival Academy, Never has been trained in multiple fields of magic and has yet to experience the Surge . However, he/she is a naturally gifted teleporter under the tutelage of Fletcher Renn . She also has an aptitude for Elemental Magic and Omen describes her as "pretty good at everything".

Sexuality and Gender

Never is a genderfluid character, switching between male and female identification and presentation. He/she uses “he” when male and “she” when female. When she is referred to but is not present, both are used interchangeably.

Physical Appearance

Never has long, ash-brown hair which he/she uses as an indicator of his preferred pronouns (tied up for male, down for female). As the Third Year at Corrival Academy, Never wears the standard uniform of a black blazer (with purple piping and the school crest on the left breast), black trousers or skirt, white shirt, and purple tie. She wear the trousers when identifying as male, and the skirt as female.

Trivia

  • Never is the first genderfluid character in the series.
  • Given that Never's brother was killed by building falling on him (caused by Darquesse either on Devastation Day or before), it is highly likely Never's family were Roarhaven Mages who were aware of Erskine Ravel's Plan to rule over mortals. It is unknown how exactly Never feels towards mortals themself.
