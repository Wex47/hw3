Biography

The War

Morwenna was present at the attack on the necromancer temple.

Skulduggery Pleasant

She meets Skulduggery and Valkyrie at a meeting discussing his Skulduggery's theory about Serpine. Morwenna was killed by the Sceptre of the Ancients along with Eachan Meritorious at the hands of Nefarian Serpine after the two Elders are betrayed by Sagacious Tome .

Kingdom Of The Wicked

She was mentioned by the Resistance in the Alternate Dimension .

Resurrection

She was cited as an example of a notable necromancer alongside Melancholia St Clair and Lord Vile .

Magic

She was a Necromancer ; this was shown when she appeared from the shadows before Serpine killed her. Her cloak contained her Necromancer Powers.

Description

Crow's appearance is an elderly woman who wore black.

Trivia

  • She is the only Necromancer to become an Elder.
  • Derek Landy revealed that she was one of the few Necromancers who have abandoned the Temple .
