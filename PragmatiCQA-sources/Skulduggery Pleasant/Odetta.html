Odetta , formerly Odetta Troth (presumably), was a minor character in Resurrection and is mentioned in the following books. She is the ex-wife of Bennet Troth and is the mother of their son, Kase . She is currently dating a Hollow Man named Conrad .

Biography

Resurrection

At the begin of the book, Odetta is presumed to be kidnapped. Bennet, with the help of Sebastian Tao , ends up finding her but is saddened when he learns that she was not kidnapped and that she ran off with a hollow man. The reason why she left Bennet was his obsession with Darquesse . Bennet invites Sebastian to The Darquesse Cult in repayment for Sebastian's help.

Trivia

  • It is stated that Kase spends more time with her than he does with Bennet
