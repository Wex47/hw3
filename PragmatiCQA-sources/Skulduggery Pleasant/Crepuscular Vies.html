Crepuscular Vies is an elusive man who previously worked with Martin Flanery , but is now a friend of Omen Darkly . He was previously Skulduggery's partner but Skulduggery believes he is dead. He has no lips and his face is covered in scars.

Biography

Midnight

He kills Wilkes and says that he will help Flanery. He wants to kill Skulduggery.

Bedlam

He kills Razzia , Abyssinia and Nero .

Seasons of War

He is revealed to be Skulduggery Pleasant's first partner (mentioned in the first book) that Skulduggery thought was dead. Vies makes friends with Omen, and helps him to save his brother from Reznor Rake .
