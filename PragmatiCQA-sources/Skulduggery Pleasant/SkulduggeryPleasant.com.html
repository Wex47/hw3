SkulduggeryPleasant.com is the official website of the Skulduggery Pleasant series. It contains excerpts of the books, competitions, interviews and downloads. The site is split into three regional sites - Australia and New Zealand, America, and the United Kingdom. Each regional site has the same content, but different layouts.

Sections

Home

This contains links to other pages, latest news, a countdown to new books and the option to sign up to the newsletter. It also has a link to Derek's Blog. At the bottom of the page is a Facebook "like" counter and links to Skulduggery Pleasant on social media.

The Books

This page has a list of book covers and the ability to buy the books and e-books. Clicking on each cover will allow you to read a brief synopsis. There are  sample chapters and audio extracts available also.

There is no copy of The End of the World or any of the short stories.

News

This section contains a scroll-down list of news that also appears on the homepage. It also contains the ability to sign up to the newsletter and a feed to the Skulduggery Pleasant Facebook group.

Characters

This section contains the 10 profiles released during August 2012 of: Skulduggery Pleasant , Valkyrie Cain , Ghastly Bespoke , China Sorrows , Solomon Wreath , Melancholia St. Clair , Fletcher Renn , Caelan , Dr Nye and Vaurien Scapegrace and Profile Links.

Competition

This section allows people to sign up to the latest competitions.It also contains the ability to sign up to the newsletter and a feed to the Skulduggery Pleasant Facebook group.

Schools

This section contains feedback from schools concerning the series . Teachers provide comments about the quality of the books. It also contains the ability to sign up to the newsletter and a feed to the Skulduggery Pleasant Facebook group. It conatins resources that could be used in class.

About Derek

This section includes a picture of Derek and Skulduggery himself. Along with videos with the Golden God and a short Biography on him.
