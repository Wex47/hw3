Luke Skywalker was a Dimensional Shunter and an apprentice Dead Man that went on the mission to the Leibniz Universe to kill Mevolent .

Early Life

Luke was born to an unnamed mother and an unnamed father in America. He took his name because it symbolises hope, the kind of feeling he wanted to inspire. He became a Dimensional Shunter when he discovered that there was a discipline that let him explore new worlds. At a young age, he joined the American Sanctuary .

Biography

Seasons of War

He is first mentioned by Skulduggery Pleasant , describing him as ‘America’s Finest Shunter’ before he walks into the room. Skulduggery introduces him to Valkyrie Cain , Tanith Low , Saracen Rue , Dexter Vex , and Nefarian Serpine as Luke Skywalker. Dexter and Tantih say they can't call him that. Skulduggery tells them to respect his name.

When they all get geared up, a revolver is handed to Luke, as it is "perfect for someone who didn’t know what the hell they were doing". They then shunt over to the Leibniz Dimension.

While running away from a mortal with wielding an axe, Luke falls and gets stuck in a cave. After Valkyrie descends to rescue him, Luke asks her out on a date, to which she replies that she is seeing someone. They are both then attacked by a large swarm of Draugar , which drag Luke down deeper into the cave and proceed to eat him alive.
