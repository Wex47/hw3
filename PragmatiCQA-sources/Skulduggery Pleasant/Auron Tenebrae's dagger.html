Auron Tenebrae 's dagger is the object that Tenebrae uses to channel his Necromancy power. It was mentioned that Skulduggery Pleasant had used the dagger without Tenebrae's permission to defeat an enemy.

Powers

Being the High Priest of the Necromancer Order, it is asumed that the dagger is very powerful. Tenebrae was not seen using his magic in the books.
