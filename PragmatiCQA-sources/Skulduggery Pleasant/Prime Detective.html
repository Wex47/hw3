The Prime Detective is the lead Detective in The Sanctuary . They would investigate the bigger cases and be the first called for any Sanctuary business.

List of Prime Detectives

The Irish Sanctuary

  • Remus Crux : May 2008 - October 2008 . Dropped after his mind broke from glancing at a Faceless One
  • Davina Marr : Unknown - September 2009 . Let go after commiting mass murder by blowing up the Irish Sanctuary using Myron Stray .
  • Skulduggery Pleasant : December 2009 - April 2015. Let go after becoming an Arbiter.
