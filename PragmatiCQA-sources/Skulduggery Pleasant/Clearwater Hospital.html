Clearwater Hospital is a location that first appears in Playing With Fire .

The Battle

The Battle at Clearwater was between The Irish Sanctuary and Baron Vengeous , and thenupon the Baron's defeat, the Grotesquery . The battle ended when the weakened Grotesquery was defeated by Valkyrie Cain .

A small battle happened earlier between Springheeled Jack and Billy-Ray Sanguine after Jack found out he was being used to revive the Faceless Ones . Neither side won, and Sanguine left the battle.
