Tanith Low 's sword was used as her primary weapon. She risked her life more than once to retrieve it, and would never willingly abandon it. It was also used by Valkyrie Cain to defeat the Grotesquery , and Valkyrie had used the same weapon to cut open Billy-Ray Sanguine's stomach.

Notes

  • The sword may have magical properties as it could withstand a blow from a cleaver scythe.
