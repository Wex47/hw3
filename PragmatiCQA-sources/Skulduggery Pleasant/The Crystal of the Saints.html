The Crystal of the Saints was an ancient artefact that could bring the Faceless Ones back. In 2007 it was in the possession of Rancid Fines and Christophe Nocturnal . It was later revealed in Dead or Alive that Fines had held onto it for thirteen years.

Trivia

  • It may have been one of the Lost Artifacts .
  • It also may have been connected to the Black Crystals .
