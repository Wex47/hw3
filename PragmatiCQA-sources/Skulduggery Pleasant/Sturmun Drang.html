Sturmun Drang was the German Grand Mage in the Skulduggery Pleasant series.

Biography

Drang is Grand Mage of the German Sanctuary, working under the Supreme Mage China Sorrows . He is unlike his associates in the fact that he does not feel the need to constantly impress China Sorrows, which earns her respect for him.

In Seasons of War , he is killed by Rune using a spear.

Appearance

A single scar runs from the corner of his eye to the hinge of his jaw.

Trivia

  • Drang is an atheist.
