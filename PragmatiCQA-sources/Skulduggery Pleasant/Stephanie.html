Valkyrie Cain (aka Stephanie Edgley or Darquesse ) is a young woman who was thought to be descended from the last of the Ancients , however according to Dusk , her heritage is actually as a descendant of the Faceless Ones . Initially, she practiced Elemental magic, but later, she also became a Necromancer , storing her power in a ring. She fights with a powered concussion stick some time after. After losing her True Name and experiencing her Surge , her magical repertoire takes on a totally unique form, allowing her to manipulate so-called "white energy", sensitive powers, flight and mimicry.

Originally called Stephanie Edgley, Valkyrie had to take a new name to protect herself from other mages who desired to influence her. Skulduggery Pleasant is her magic mentor, Sanctuary detective partner, and best friend. Solomon Wreath was her Necromancy mentor until 2011 . Before a Remnant possessed her, Tanith Low was her fighting mentor, "big sister" and other best friend. During her time in Colorado, Valkyrie was trained in handling various melee weapons, as well as military moving and handling of guns by her boyfriend at that time, Coda .

She has been romantically involved with Fletcher Renn , Caelan , Coda and Militsa Gnosis .

She now owns a German Shepard called Xena .

Valkyrie Cain is a member of the Arbiter Corps and lives at Grimwood in Haggard , Ireland .

Biography

Skulduggery Pleasant

Stephanie Edgley is introduced when her wealthy uncle, Gordon Edgley , a writer of horror fiction, dies under mysterious circumstances. He left Stephanie, whom he was especially fond of, his mansion and fortune in his will. After the reading of the will, whilst spending the night at her uncle's house by herself, Stephanie was attacked by Vindick Leather , sent by Nefarian Serpine , demanding a "key." As the man attacked Stephanie, the mysterious man in the tan overcoat from Gordon's funeral, known as Skulduggery Pleasant , arrived and saved her, blasting down the door to get in, throwing fireballs at her attacker and shooting him in the shoulder. Skulduggery's disguise of a hat, wig and sunglasses fell off during the fight to reveal that he is a skeleton held together by magic.

Stephanie, wanting to escape her previously boring and tedious life, helped Skulduggery investigate in his current case. Skulduggery took her to China Sorrows , who informed them about a rumour that Serpine was searching for the Sceptre of the Ancients . After their visit at China's Library , they were attacked again by Vindick Leather. Stephanie managed to escape him, but he chased her and they fell into a river, resulting in his death (who was immune from fire but vulnerable to water). Skulduggery took her back home, but she still accompanied him the next day. They visited a friend of Skulduggery, Ghastly Bespoke , whose family was in the possession of rare books regarding the Ancients , located at the Vault . After a dispute between Stephanie and Ghastly, he was unwilling to show them his families collection and Skulduggery and Stephanie decided that it was necessary to break into the Vault to gather the important information about the Sceptre.

They discover that the Vault has two Vampires as security guards and decide to break into the Vault at the next day, hoping to sneak past the vampires.

To enable Stephanie to work with him further without anyone noticing that she was gone, Skulduggery gave Stephanie a Reflection , which lived her life while she was gone. He also gave her black protection clothes, made by Ghastly.

Skulduggery brought her to The Irish Sanctuary to meet the The Council of Elders , after the Elders had heard that Skulduggery endangered her life and brought her, a seemingly mortal girl, into the world of magic.

After their meeting with the Elders, the pair broke into the Vault and consulted an Echo Stone version of a sorcerer named Oisin , an expert in the history of the Ancients. During their conversation it was revealed that Gordon hid the Sceptre in the caves underneath his house. In order to get into the caves, they needed to find the key before Serpine did.

At Gordon's house, they encountered Serpine and his Hollow Men , who captured Skulduggery, but Stephanie manages to escape. She then went to China for help but China refused and used Stephanie's Given Name against her to prevent her from informing the Elders about Skulduggery's capture.

In order to overcome China's influence over her, Stephanie was forced to take the name of Valkyrie Cain. She then informed the Council of Elders with the help of Ghastly and they rescued Skulduggery from Serpine´s Castle with the help of Tanith Low and two Cleavers .

During his captivity, Skulduggery found out where the key to the caves is. It is a cheap brooch, which Gordon bequeathed his other brother 's wife, Beryl Edgley . Valkyrie visited them and stole the brooch. She then practices magic at the beach, discovering that she indeed is able to use it, by moving a shell by manipulating the air.

Her father talks to her there and tells her about a family legend, which says, that they are descended from the last of the Ancients.

Later, Valkyrie and Skulduggery meet Mr. Bliss , who is supposed to accompany them to the Caves, but he steals the brooch from them for Serpine.

They follow him into the Caves, reaching the Sceptre about the same time as Serpine and fight him. During their fight, Skulduggery manages to set Serpine on fire, which would have killed him, but he had undergone a strange procedure, making him a Zombie with an enormous healing ability.

Stephanie and Skulduggery, aided by an immensely strong tailor named Ghastly Bespoke , and English professional swordswoman Tanith Low attempt to prevent the villain, Nefarian Serpine , from obtaining the Sceptre.

Now working as a detective and with Stephanie's help, Skulduggery defeats Serpine, destroying him with the Sceptre to protect Valkyrie, breaking the Sceptre's power in the process. After defeating Serpine Skulduggery visits her and offers to take her on as his partner and student in sorcery and martial arts.

The Lost Art Of World Domination

A villain called Scaramouch Van Dreg has captured Skulduggery Pleasant, and both are in a dungeon. Scaramouch had been trying to capture Skulduggery's attention but finds out that Skulduggery had been sleeping the whole time. Scaramouch wakes him, and Skulduggery is surprised to find Scaramouch. Skulduggery tells him that he was trying to arrest Rancid Fines and Christophe Nocturnal , much to Scaramouch's anger, who had thought that Skulduggery was trying to arrest him.

Skulduggery asks Scaramouch what he is planning, and Scaramouch realises that Skulduggery only views him as "relatively deadly." Scaramouch tells Skulduggery he is planning to take over the world, not bringing the Faceless Ones back using the Crystal of the Saints . Skulduggery tells Scaramouch all the work and trouble ruling the world would be, but Scaramouch believes that he could still do it. Scaramouch then shows Skulduggery a container, which had been enchanted by twenty-three spells by twenty-three different sorcerers. The container has an insect which could drain the magic of sorcerers. It is one of the Lost Artifacts . Skulduggery asks if he knows if the insect is inside, and Scaramouch panics.

Skulduggery suggests opening the box to find out if the insect is even in the box. Scaramouch places the container on the floor and opens the lid. The insect rises out and lands on the floor. Skulduggery squishes it just as Valkyrie walks in. She uses air magic and the two arrest Scaramouch. Scaramouch is outraged when Valkyrie doesn't recognise him. She, on the other hand, claims that they have real bad guys to stop, but cuffs Scaramouch anyway. As they walk out, Skulduggery tells Scaramouch that there should be more people who want to take over the world and that all the other bad guys think that it's just silly.

Playing With Fire

A year after discovering the magical world, Stephanie Edgley, now known as Valkyrie Cain, continues to work with Skulduggery Pleasant, the wise-cracking skeleton detective, capturing villains for the Sanctuary , which is now ruled by Thurid Guild in a new location after the massacre in the previous Sanctuary. Soon, Baron Vengeous , one of the original Three Generals of Mevolent , escapes his prison and begins searching for Lord Vile ’s armour. He planned to resurrect the Grotesquery to bring back the Faceless Ones.

Vengeous confronts China Sorrows in her apartment whilst Valkyrie is visiting. Valkyrie hides whilst China and Vengeous talk. Vengeous reveals that he was released from prison by an assassin named Billy-Ray Sanguine who promptly arrives along with Dusk. A battle ensues, during which Valkyrie reveals herself and defends China. Valkyrie is soon chased by Sanguine. After being in a struggle, Valkyrie is saved by Tanith. In the process, Valkyrie steals Sanguine's razor, his primary weapon, leaving the psychopathic assassin with a grudge against her.

Valkyrie and Skulduggery, along with a convict called Vaurien Scapegrace , go to the shoddy sorcerer town Roarhaven to find The Torment – a mysterious old man who may know where the Grotesquery is. After a battle in a seedy pub with some thugs that once were "friends" of Scapegrace, they find the Torment and ask for his help. The Torment only says he will help them find Vile's armour if Skulduggery kills Valkyrie. Since she is a descendant of the Ancients, The Torment despises her, thinking any power will corrupt civilisation. Skulduggery takes Valkyrie around a corner and they summon her reflection , a living mirror image of Valkyrie who she uses as a decoy, enabling her to live a double life. Skulduggery takes Valkyrie's reflection in front of the Torment and shoots it. The Torment is satisfied and tells Skulduggery the whereabouts of the Grotesquery.

Skulduggery and Valkyrie return Scapegrace to prison and go to find the Grotesquery. Unfortunately, Vengeous' minions have already obtained it. Soon after, Skulduggery and Valkyrie take the reflection back to Valkyrie's house. Valkyrie touches the mirror to absorb its memories and remembers what it is like to be shot. Valkyrie then notices that whenever she tries to look back in her memories one part always remains blank and she cannot pin it down. She realises that her reflection hid something from her, and finds it disconcerting and dangerous. However, she goes on with Skulduggery without mentioning it to him.

Later, Valkyrie is kidnapped by Sanguine who takes her to an old abandoned church of the Faceless Ones where she is taken before the altar where Vengeous awaits her. The evil sorcerer cuts Valkyrie's palm and spills some of her blood onto the Grotesquery's body before using the armour's shadow powers which mingle with the blood. Skulduggery subsequently arrives and rescues Valkyrie, by throwing a collapsible jack onto the Grotesquery and making it out to be a bomb. After a stealing the Grotesquery, Valkyrie is incapacitated and wakes up in hospital . Whilst wearing a "respectable" blue hospital gown, Skulduggery wears a pink one decorated with bunnies and elephants for the doctor 's amusement.

Skulduggery turns the lifeless Grotesquery over to the Sanctuary's top scientist, Grouse, to take apart. However, the Grotesquery has already absorbed a lot of power from Vile's armour and Valkyrie's blood and so wakes up in the middle of the night whilst being operated on. Skulduggery and Tanith arrive and the three of them attack the Grotesquery before escaping. Skulduggery works out that Vengeous is actually a pawn in someone else's plan and accuses Guild of being a traitor. In a rage, Guild fires Skulduggery who decides to go after Vengeous anyway. Valkyrie begrudgingly goes to her family reunion as a distraction to Dusk. Valkyrie is subsequently attacked by vampires and forced to flee. Dusk corners her and vows that when he has transformed her into a vampire he's going to set her loose on her parents while in her bloodlust. Valkyrie stabs Dusk in the leg with the syringe he uses to curb his vampiric side whilst he is transforming and as a result, he is caught between vampire and human and put in intense pain. Springheeled Jack comes and rescues her and defeats Dusk so as to frustrate Vengeous' plans because he doesn't want the Faceless Ones to return.

Accompanied by Tanith, Mr. Bliss and some Cleavers , Skulduggery and Valkyrie go to Clearwater Hospital and do battle with the Grotesquery which, due to being part Faceless One, is virtually invincible. Mr Bliss is incapacitated but the Cleavers mercilessly attack the Grotesquery and almost overpower it when Vengeous arrives along with China, whom he has taken captive. Together with the Grotesquery, he kills the Cleavers and beats Skulduggery and Valkyrie into submission. Valkyrie "tricks" Vengeous into releasing her, saying that she will join him and kill Skulduggery, but she releases China who attacks Vengeous and the Grotesquery. This gives Valkyrie time to free Skulduggery, who joins in the assault against Vengeous, tearing off his helmet and breastplate. Valkyrie ignites the protective clothing beneath with a fireball and Skulduggery shoots him in the stomach. In a swoon, Vengeous crawls toward the Grotesquery but his god callously breaks the baron's neck. The Torment subsequently arrives and attacks the Grotesquery but he is defeated. In the ensuing battle Valkyrie stabs the Grotesquery through the heart, killing it. Before it dies it utters a terrible scream, which calls to the Faceless Ones trapped in another universe.

Valkyrie is later seen talking to Skulduggery on a pier. They reason that they must find out who Vengeous and Sanguine were really working for and if Guild is in league with him. Skulduggery ominously tells Valkyrie "Bad things are coming."

Gold, Babies And The Brothers Muldoon

Valkyrie arrives late to a meeting with a zombie. Valkyrie is here to negotiate with the Muldoons: a goblin trio of brothers who have kidnapped three babies and are demanding gold in exchange for their lives. The zombie is to act as a liason. He claims that the Muldoons were descended from powerful sorcerers, possibly even the Ancients, although Valkyrie proves him wrong, explaining they made it up to appear more intimidating. They attacked a group of mortals, and the Council of Elders ordered them to be ambushed. They escaped, but all three were transformed into goblins, with gold as their only source of returning to human form, making them resort to stealing and exchanging babies. Valkyrie and the zombie reach a ruined church, and the zombie leaves for the exchange to take place.

The first of the brothers, Liam , demands Valkyrie's gold, but she persuades him to give her the babies first. He calls his brothers Colm and Fintan , who come with the babies. After she gives them the gold, they reveal that it is a double-cross. Skulduggery was the person responsible for turning them into goblins, and they plan to send Skulduggery Valkyrie's head in order to make him come at them.

The session is interrupted when Skulduggery bursts through the ceiling and crashes onto the floor painfully. The goblins reveal that Skulduggery was attacked by their sister, Peg . She crashes through the ceiling too, covered in wedding dresses and shouting out that she loves Skulduggery. It appears that Skulduggery and Peg have met before, with Skulduggery throwing an entire mountain on her to try and kill her.

Skulduggery and Peg engage in combat, while the Muldoons attack Valkyrie. She burns Fintan and he smacks into a wall. Colm tries to kill her, but she sets him on fire and elbows him in the jaw. Liam and Peg eventually get the better of the two and overpower them. Liam throttles Valkyrie, but she throws him across the church. Skulduggery summons up a piece of rubble to smash into Peg's face, but soon after, Liam rushes for the babies. Valkyrie quickly elbows him and knocks him unconscious.

Peg has an emotional breakdown after Skulduggery explains to her that they don't actually love each other. She leaves, saddened, and Skulduggery is left to be teased by Valkyrie.

The Faceless Ones

Valkyrie is investigating the murders of four Teleporters with Skulduggery. They go to tell Guild what they know. He refuses to rehire them, reminding them that he has hired Remus Crux instead, a bumbling detective that blindly follows Guild's orders in an attempt to impress him. After discovering the recent murders may have been linked to a Teleporter murder 50 years ago, Skulduggery and Valkyrie go and talk to the only Sea Hag that lives in a lake to see if they can get any information about the 50-year-old murder.

They discover a man named Batu killed the Teleporter and talk to the dead Teleporter whose body is lying at the bottom of the lake. After promising to bury him on dry land, the Sea Hag is enraged and tries to drown Valkyrie, but Skulduggery saves her. They also discover that an object called the Isthmus Anchor and a Teleporter would be able to open a gateway to the Faceless Ones allowing them to enslave the earth.

With China's help, they locate a natural, but unskilled young Teleporter called Fletcher Renn . They manage to save him from Billy-Ray Sanguine who is working for the Diablerie . Fletcher is told about the rest of the magical world and given a talking to by both Skulduggery and Valkyrie to choose the right side. They bring him to Kenspeckle Grouse, who grudgingly agrees to look after the boy. Grouse tells Valkyrie she should be doing normal teenage things and gets annoyed at Skulduggery for endangering Valkyrie. Valkyrie is uncomfortable when Grouse admonishes Skulduggery. then go to Finbar Wrong , a Sensitive , who is able to tell them that the location of the gate where the Faceless Ones can get to earth is on Aranmore Farm . After that, they meet with a Necromancer, Solomon Wreath , who offers the help of himself and three other Necromancers. Skulduggery accepts Wreath's offer, punches him in the face, then leaves.

Skulduggery and Valkyrie go to Aranmore Farm and talk to Paddy , the owner of the land. They tell him to keep a lookout for strange activity on the farm but avoid the subject of magic. While they are talking, Skulduggery realises that the remains of the Grotesquery could be used as the Isthmus Anchor. Skulduggery and Valkyrie go to the Sanctuary to steal the Grotesquery and are attacked by Crux, though he is easily defeated by Skulduggery. They set Vaurien Scapegrace loose to distract the Cleaver, but are still beaten to the objective by the Diablerie. Sanguine deactivates the Grotesquery's defenses and escapes with it. Skulduggery and Valkyrie use an emergency exit to avoid being captured by the Sanctuary forces.

Ghastly wakes up at Grouse's clinic after being a statue for two years. Upon a brief reunion scene, they decide to go to Bespoke Tailors. Fletcher takes Valkyrie by teleportation, something she did not consent to and responds by punching him in the face. They bond while waiting for the others on the rooftop as Fletcher tells Valkyrie about his past. The others arrive and Ghastly is told what happened while he was a statue. Ghastly then starts making Valkyrie new clothes, as the old ones were getting tight. While fashioning her boots he tells her about his mother , a Sensitive, and a vision she had – Valkyrie dying, screaming in pain, facing an enemy of "unimaginable evil" – in an effort to make Valkyrie quit magic and return to normal teenage life. Valkyrie tells him that she has already made the choice to stay.

They receive a call from Paddy, who reports a strange man lurking on his land. The quartet visit Aranmore Farm and quickly tell Paddy about magic and the Diablerie's plan. Fletcher locates the exact point where the gate will open.

Skulduggery and Valkyrie go to get the Sceptre of the Ancients and find another black crystal in case the Faceless Ones make it back to earth, as the last crystal was destroyed. Skulduggery tells Valkyrie that there are black crystals in the caves beneath Gordon's house, which is mightily convenient. Skulduggery and Valkyrie go into the caves and find a copy of Gordon’s house in the caves that was created by the previous owner of Gordon’s house, Anathem Mire .

Anathem is inside the house, dead, and Skulduggery wakes up his ghost. After first attacking them, Anathem calms down and shows them a vein of black crystals. Valkyrie, to Skulduggery's initial shock, removes a large one. She explains that she guessed she could touch them without disintegrating because she is a descendent of the Ancients. They return to find Anathem allowing Skulduggery to leave but wanting Valkyrie to stay with him and be his "queen". Valkyrie is repulsed by the notion and manages to escape by angering him, making him lose control of his magical house.

Returning to the surface with Skulduggery, Valkyrie goes home to say goodbye to her parents who are leaving on their second honeymoon. Surprisingly, Remus Crux arrives at her door and, after the departure of her parents, Valkyrie is chased down and eventually arrested. Crux intentionally puts her in a cell with Scapegrace who was badly beaten up by the Cleavers after his little distraction stunt. Scapegrace tries to kill Valkyrie but she manages to knock him out and ties him to a bed by his broken fingers. She is then able to trick a security guard into letting her out of her cell and she knocks him out and takes Scapegrace with her as he could be used as a distraction.

Valkyrie witnesses the new Administrator attempting to kill Mr Bliss, revealing herself as the traitor, not Guild. Scapegrace flees in the moment of distraction. Valkyrie attempts to stop the Administrator who pins her up against a wall. The Cleavers chase Scapegrace back into the corridor and, upon seeing the betrayal, advance on the Administrator. She tries to run, letting Valkyrie fall to the ground as she does, and Valkyrie trips her causing her to fall into the blue light, killing her and saving Bliss. Valkyrie then runs out of the Sanctuary and escapes with Skulduggery to Aranmore Farm, where the battle has already ensued.

Upon reaching the farm they discover Ghastly and Tanith running for cover as they are attacked by the Diablerie. Tanith, Ghastly, Skulduggery and Valkyrie hide with Paddy in his farmhouse. Ghastly informs Valkyrie that her new clothes are in the van outside, so Valkyrie runs to get changed. The Diablerie force Fletcher to open the Gateway and Skulduggery, Tanith and Ghastly go out to attack. They are ambushed by two or three hundred Hollow Men . Paddy persuades Valkyrie to put on his mother's ring, claiming he had intended to give it to his eventual finacé.

China, a dozen Cleavers and the Necromancers appear and help take out the Hollow Men. Tanith is about to be killed by Murder Rose but is saved by the White Cleaver . Injured, Tanith gets inside the house. Mr Bliss arrives and starts beating up Gruesome Krav . While Valkyrie and Paddy are looking for first aid supplies for Tanith, Sanguine bursts through the wall and attacks Tanith. Paddy tries to shoot Sanguine with his shotgun but is knocked out. Valkyrie picks up Tanith's sword and slashes open Sanguine's stomach, causing him to retreat in horror.

Then, three Faceless Ones come through the fully opened gate and Fletcher manages to escape after the shockwave caused by their arrival. One of the Faceless Ones takes over Krav’s body and then kills Mr Bliss. Paddy gets Valkyrie the Sceptre then reveals himself to be Batu as he knocks Tanith out and hits Valkyrie with the Sceptre. Valkyrie realises that the ring Batu gave her is preventing her from using magic and removes it allowing her to burn Batu’s side. Batu takes the Sceptre and hides it in the trees, being followed by Valkyrie.

Unable to find Batu, she stumbles across Remus Crux, who attempts to arrest Valkyrie again. She knocks him down. She then watches Skulduggery battle the Faceless One and a Necromancer be killed.

China finds Crux and realises he must have glimpsed a Faceless One, as he is muttering to himself and seems to have gone insane. He had also seen Batu bury the Sceptre and retrieved it himself. China coaxes the Sceptre from Crux and then turns to see Valkyrie running towards her with a Faceless One right on her tail. Valkyrie takes the spectre and fires black lightning twice into the Faceless One that had consumed Krav, killing it. The second Faceless One attacks China, Skulduggery and Valkyrie. Fletcher appears and teleports Valkyrie out of the way. The Faceless One pulls the Sceptre out of Valkyrie's hand and shatters it, destroying the black crystal, and itself.

Skulduggery decides to get the last Faceless One to chase Valkyrie and go through a new portal which Fletcher should open. The Faceless One charges at Skulduggery instead, lifting him off the ground. Wreath, with a broken leg, gives Valkyrie his cane full of his Necromantic power and she uses it to knock the Faceless One back as the cane is destroyed. The Faceless One shoots out tentacles made of its organs in a last attempt to save itself. The tentable whips around Skulduggery and pulls him through the portal with it. The portal closes before anyone can do anything to help. Valkyrie and her team are victorious, but also defeated by the loss of Skulduggery.

Later Solomon Wreath approaches Valkyrie who angrily tells him that Guild has given all the credit of the victory to Mr Bliss. Wreath tells Valkyrie that she can still save Skulduggery by using his original skull as an Isthmus anchor. With new hope, she is also told she is a natural at Necromancy, seeing how skilfully she was able to wield Wreath's cane. Wreath offers to train her, as she will need more power to get through her new mission.

Dark Days

After Valkyrie has spent nine months obsessively looking for Skulduggery's skull , a vampire called Caelan manages to set up a meeting for her with a man called Thames Chabon , who apparently has the skull. It is revealed that Wreath has indeed been teaching her Necromancy during her search for the skull, and she has placed her power into a ring .

The night before the meeting with Chabon, a deranged Remus Crux manages to gain entry into her house and tries to kill her. Wreath saves Valkyrie, who deflects her parents' worries by telling them a bat had caused all the havoc. The next morning, after talking to Ghastly, Tanith and Fletcher, she learns about Esryn Vanguard and his death; a man called Dreylan Scarab had assassinated him during the war for making advocations for peace. Scarab has just been released from prison a day early after serving his sentence. Valkyrie tells the others about what happened last night with Crux. Tanith and Ghastly don't trust Wreath and think he is trying to use Valkyrie.

They meet Chabon the next day, who manages to swindle them with a different skull rather than Skulduggery's. Realising the con, they chase him through the streets of Dublin and pin him down, asking him about the real skull. Chabon reveals that Davina Marr , the new Prime Detective, had bought the skull for triple the price just that morning. A new plan is made to steal Skulduggery's skull from the Sanctuary.

The plan goes wrong, however, and they are arrested. Valkyrie is interrogated by Marr. Valkyrie manages to overpower her with her Necromancer ring and frees the other three. Tanith and Ghastly distract the incoming Cleavers while Fletcher teleports Valkyrie to Guild's office, where they finally retrieve the skull. They teleport to China's library and she joins the two. The three go to Aranmore Farm, where the portal is opened. Valkyrie insists that she goes in alone, causing Fletcher to kiss her.

After entering the portal, Valkyrie finds herself in a dead world, full of empty houses built into mountain walls and vast streets. She is amazed at the fact that she is on an alien world. She quickly finds Skulduggery, who thinks she is a hallucination. To convince him of her existence, she agrees to get one of his bones back. Powers and abilities

Elemental Magic

Throughout the series, Valkyrie's skill in elemental magic steadily grows, from an absolute amateur incapable of performing magic when panicking to the stage of a reasonably powerful Elemental, capable of besting sorcerers far more experienced than she herself is. She displays great potency with Fire and Air but shows little aptitude with Water and even less in Earth, due to her not being interested in the former and the fact that the latter turns one into a stone statue. However, she loses this power after she is stripped of her magic.

Necromancy

After seeing her use his cane with great skill despite her lack of training at Aranmore Farm, Wreath decided that Valkyrie needed Necromancy training. She displays a strange skill and talent at the discipline and grasps it very well. Valkyrie has her power forced into a ring and she uses it to create shadow whips, barriers and tendrils, as well as shrouding her hand in solid darkness to increase the power of her punches and launch projectiles of darkness. However, she loses this power after she is stripped of her magic.

Choice

When Valkyrie was to reach full maturity, usually at the age of 19 or 20, she would have had to choose her magical discipline before she experiences The Surge . After this choice, she would lose the ability to use other types of magic, so if she had chosen Necromancy she would have lost her Elemental powers and vice-versa. Derek Landy confirmed that she "can't have both."

However, when Darquesse is split from her, all of her powers, Elemental and Necromantic, are taken, leaving her effectively mortal. However, when her surge is kick-started, she develops the ability to create waves of energy and to summon white lightning. This is possibly a manifestation of Energy-Throwing.

Darquesse

When in grave danger - such as being on the brink of death, Valkyrie subconsciously allowed Darquesse to take over her mind and body. As Darquesse, Valkyrie could wield a unique type of magic, possibly Ancient or Faceless Ones' magic, which granted her a huge amount of strength, allowing her to break boulders, crush skulls, and punch through bones with ease. It also gave her the ability to fly, the ability to see and manipulate someone's consciousness. Darquesse can also dull pain and has the ability to heal even her brain and organs, making her practically invincible. Darquesse can survive injuries that would easily kill a normal mage, even one as powerful as Skulduggery. Darquesse can even withstand having her head ripped off, though she admitted there was a strong chance she'd have died had she not magically repaired herself within seven seconds. Complete destruction of the brain would kill her - however, this is almost impossible do due to her ability to heal any and all wounds magically. Darquesse has been shown to be capable of disintegrating regular humans, turning them to ash with a mere gesture. In addition, Valkyrie's normal skills, such as Necromancy and Elemental Magic are increased vastly, superior to any average sorcerer. In Death Bringer , she is shown to be capable of fighting on par with Lord Vile ; however, it is shown that her power has grown greatly, as she defeats Lord Vile with moderate difficulty, and even later states that she has surpassed him. She is also shown to have the ability to see Magic, sensing its presence and nature in other beings. She can understand different people's abilities, how strong they are, and how they will use it. She can also manifest her magic in the form of telekinesis, such as using it to break Kitana Kellaway 's leg and moving her body towards her decapitated head.

Currently, the precise limits of Darquesse's power are unknown. As of 2012 , she is roughly equal to Mevolent , if not slightly stronger - however, it has been shown that her power is increasing exponentially every time she emerges. Argeddion also states that she is learning and becoming powerful faster than he did, as she easily mastered the ability to see magic, while it took him many months. It is known that Darquesse has been prophesied to extinguish all life on Earth, and eventually the planet itself. By 2013 Darquesse has completely taken over. She could tear another mage apart with a casual flick of her wrist, destroy a thousand powerful Witches and an army of Warlocks, other mages, Wretchlings and several supercharged sorcerers. By 2014 she is sufficiently powerful to destroy a large part of Roarhaven, absorb thousands of remnants, atomise living beings and even travel to the dimension of the Faceless Ones. It was also said that even weakened, Darquesse could defeat Lord Vile (even after he was supercharged by the Accelerator). At almost her full power, she survives a combined assault by Valkyrie (with invincibility), Melancholia, and supercharged Lord Vile, and manages to defeat all of them with a large blast of energy. However, it was stated that she could not defeat more than two Faceless Ones.

Although the real Darquesse opened a portal and travelled to the dimension of The Faceless Ones, a part of Darquesse remains in the real world calling herself Kes. Significantly weakened, Kes spends practically all her energy in simply existing and haunting talking to Valkyrie. She is able to deliver a short shock of energy to defeat Lethe , but this leaves her even weaker than before. It is unclear whether she will return to her full, or even average power, however she appears to be weakening and is on friendly terms with Valkyrie.

In Seasons of War, Darquesse returns from fighting the Faceless Ones and merges with Kes.

Other skills

As well as being a gifted Sorcerer, Valkyrie has displayed other talents throughout the series, such as being highly intelligent, a gifted detective and an expert hand-to-hand fighter. Her official profile released by Derek Landy states that she practices Kenpo Karate , Muay Thai , Krav Maga and Capoeira . Valkyrie has shown proficiency in punches, kicks, elbows, headbutts, and throws among other techniques.

Surge

After losing Darquesse and having her Surge kick-started, Valkyrie has been able to manipulate magical energy to create white lightning, create large blasts, make a white shield around herself, and float. She is also able to see magic like Darquesse. This is implied to be due to the fact that losing her True Name meant she had no restrictions and was "closer to warlocks and witches than sorcerers.", however it could be that she has gained access as an 'empty shell' to her heritage as a Child of the Faceless Ones. Additionally, she shares some psychic traits. These are subliminal, though enough to cause her headaches in certain areas of Roarhaven (where other Sensitives cannot go) and are activated in Cassandra Pharos ' old cottage, triggering a vision.

As of Bedlam , her abilities have grown to include advanced sensitive powers such thought control and perusal, as well as future visions where she can control the speed and intensity of a vision. She can also better control her white lightning ability, however intense use of this will burn away her clothing, and her shielding is stronger than what even China Sorrows can create with her sigils. Valkyrie can also see Auras/Souls similar to the ability exhibited to Darquesse, and she has displayed the unique ability to disperse souls which shocked even Nye . Valkyrie has also displayed in dire circumstances the ability to absorb, replicate and enhance magical disciplines far beyond the skill of normal sorcerers, such as gaining access to enhanced healing through experiencing a healing on herself in a near death situation where she then replicated the healing ability to regrow teeth which is far beyond a normal Healer's ability. After having replicated that ability Valkyrie noticed that mentally she could access a room in her mind where this ability was stored, however her access quickly faded until she needed it again.

The extent of her powers will be revealed and discussed in the future.

Personality

Valkyrie is shown to be a stubborn, exceptionally mature, and independent girl, who often ignores orders. When approached by strangers, she usually ignores them. She hates when she is treated like a child or talked down to. She is loyal and caring to her friends and also her family. Valkyrie can also be very selfish, as Fletcher states. She admits to it later on. Her uncle Gordon Edgley described her as "strong-willed, intelligent, sharp-tongued, doesn't suffer fools gladly." After she had to kill Caelan , she became hostile to vampires, thinking them to be horrible creatures.

She tends to glare a lot.

It has been noted on various occasions that Valkyrie is extremely witty and sarcastic, frequently joking and bantering in life-or-death situations, possibly to mask her own fears or anxieties.

As Darquesse, Valkyrie's personality undergoes a drastic, dark transformation. While still retaining her core behaviour and principals, as Darquesse is simply a darker version of her and not a separate entity, they are greatly twisted. She retains her witty, sarcastic personality, but it becomes cruel and malicious rather than playful. She relishes in causing and feeling pain and violence and is constantly seeking opponents who can actually challenge her. However, it has been noted that Darquesse is not inherently evil - simply cold, uncaring and above all curious in regards to her abilities and their limits. She is willing to ally herself with those who would usually be opposed to her, valuing self-preservation above all else.
