Davit Maybury was a mage and one of the Maybury sextuplets. He was an ememy of Elwood Satchel who stole his girlfriend from him. Geoffrey Scrutinous , along with Skulduggery Pleasant and Valkyrie Cain visited his house to find out more about Satchel in The Wonderful Adventures of Geoffrey Scrutinous . They found him dead in a concealed room in his house where he suffocated because the room was airtight. He was later mentioned by Valkyrie when she and Skulduggery were searching for his brother Deacon Maybury to stop the Doomsday Machine .

Relationships

Davit was one of six Maybury brothers, of which the five others have also appeared in the series: Daffyd , Deacon , Daveth , Davon and Dai . They are all based on the real-life author David Maybury.
