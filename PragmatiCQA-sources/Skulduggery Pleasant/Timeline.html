The Ancients

Time Details
???
  • The Faceless Ones arrive.
  • The Ancients discover Magic .
  • The Ancients steal the Black Crystal.
  • The Ancients create the God-Killer weapons
  • Among the God-Killers, the Ancients created the Sceptre of the Ancients. The Black Crystal was embedded in the hilt, granting the Sceptre the power to destroy anything. It was fuelled by the lust for freedom.
  • The Ancients turn on the Faceless Ones using the weapons, banishing them to an alternate universe.
  • The Ancients create the Book of Names .
  • The Ancients turned against one another. The Sceptre became fuelled by hatred. The Ancients wiped themselves out, with the exception of one. After realising what he had done, the Last Ancient hurls the Sceptre deep within the Earth.

The War Years (not in perfect order)

  • Mevolent starts an army to bring back the Faceless Ones; recruiting Nefarian Serpine and Baron Vengeous as his generals and Dreylan Scarab as an assassin.
  • Skulduggery Pleasant, Auron Tenebrae and Morwenna Crow were pursuing Mevolent's forces, who attacked the Irish Necromancer Temple . An ogre attacks Tenebrae and he falls, injured, but Skulduggery uses Tenebrae's dagger to kill it, without his permission; this should have killed Skulduggery. Tenebrae sees great potential in Skulduggery. Morwenna leaves the Necromancer Order.
  • The Sanctuaries, previously an unofficial refuge for sorcerers, become worldwide organizations which enforce magical laws, and more importantly, oppose Mevolent. The Irish Sanctuary elects Eachan Meritorious , Sagacious Tome and Morwenna Crow as the Council of Elders .
  • Under Serpine's command, Mevolent's forces once again attack the Necromancer Temple.
  • Tenebrae teaches Serpine the Red Right Hand - a technique which causes agonising death to anyone he points his hand at. However, Tenebrae hearing that Serpine intends to use it against Skulduggery Pleasant, alters the technique out of personal interest. Now, if it is ever used against Skulduggery, the technique will have the additional effect of binding his soul to his body.
  • The Necromancers declare themselves neutral in War.
  • A part of a Faceless One is discovered in a temple. Mevolent assigns Baron Vengeous the task of resurrecting the remains. Vengeous creates the Grotesquery by stitching the remains of the Faceless One to other parts of various mythical beasts.
  • China Sorrows creates the Diablerie (Herself, Baron Vengeous, Gruesome Krav , Murder Rose and Jaron Gallow )
  • Corrival Deuce forms the Dead Men (Skulduggery Pleasant, Ghastly Bespoke, Hopeless , Erskine Ravel, Dexter Vex , Anton Shudder , and Saracen Rue in that order)
  • While the Dead Men are on a mission, Hopeless is killed. However, Larrikin replaces him.
  • Ghastly's mother defeats Serpine and Vengeous.
  • Anathem Mire dies on his ultimate expedition to the caves under his mansion.
  • At a pub in Belfast, plans to take down Mevolent end with Mr. Bliss revealing Myron Stray 's True Name , Laudigan, during an argument. Stray's life falls apart.
  • Thurid Guild creates the Exigency Programme . They frame Dreylan Scarab for the assassination of Esryn Vanguard - a man attempting to create a peaceful solution to the war. Scarab is imprisoned.
  • The Diablerie, under Serpine's command, is ordered to lure Skulduggery into a trap. China Sorrows and Murder Rose kidnap his wife and child. Serpine murders Skulduggery's family in front of his eyes and, his mind fuelled by hatred, Skulduggery grabs a dagger in an attempt to physically attack Serpine. However, the dagger was poisoned and Skulduggery was captured.
  • Skulduggery Pleasant died on the 23rd of October in the year 1700, tortured to death by Serpine. His body is hung on a stake and then burned. His bones are put in a bag and then thrown in a river.
  • Skulduggery pieces his bones back together and returns to the battlefield with an unquenchable hatred for Serpine.
  • A man in a black suit of armour bearing the name Lord Vile appears at a Necromancer Temple and receives necromancy training.
  • Vile, believed to be the Death Bringer , leaves the temple out of distaste for their ways. TheNecromancers consequently lose their Death Bringer and Vile does not recieve knowledge of The Passage .
  • Requiring an enemy, Lord Vile joins Mevolent as his third General, and single-handedly obliterates battlefields. Among the thousands killed, he kills Ghastly's Mother.
  • Skulduggery eventually overcomes Lord Vile and abandons his armour in a mountain. He returns to fight for the Sanctuaries.
  • China Sorrows betrays the Faceless Ones and leaves the Diablerie. She goes on to set up her library .
  • A battle occurs between Skulduggery and Baron Vengeous. It ends with Skulduggery throwing dynamite at Vengeous.
  • Larrikin is killed while on a mission with the Dead Men. He sacrificed himself for Dexter Vex, who was about to suffer Serpine's Red Right Hand.
  • Mevolent is killed, and the Sanctuaries win the War .
  • Serpine forms a truce with the Sanctuaries, providing him protection.
  • Vengeous attempts to escape, aided by his friend Argus . However, a shed that Argus provided was discovered by the Sanctuary and Vengeous was arrested and imprisoned.

As Serpine used the White Cleaver Experiment on himself, he was the living dead. The Sceptre can only be used by the person who killed its previous owner, so when Skulduggery killed Serpine using the Sceptre, he ended its power.
