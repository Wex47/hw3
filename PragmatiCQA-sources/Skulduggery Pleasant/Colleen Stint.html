Colleen Stint is an ex-student of Corrival Academy , and was a former classmate of Omen Darkly . She was part of Arcanum's Scholars , a study group organised by Mr Lilt that would actually be the First Wave, part of Abyssinia 's plan.

Trivia

  • The name "Colleen" comes from the Irish word for girl, cailín.
