Mr. Jib is an Elemental who appears in the fourth book.

Biography

Dark Days

While Mr. Jib was staying at The Midnight Hotel , the Zombie Horde led by Vaurien Scapegrace attacks the hotel. He volunteers himself to help Valkyrie , Skulduggery , Shudder , Miss Nuncio and Sanguine to defeat the Zombies . However, he mistakes the Zombies as stupid creatures, and after he gets too close to a window, the Zombies drag him through it and tear him apart.
