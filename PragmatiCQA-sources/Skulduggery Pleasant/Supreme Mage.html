Supreme Mage is a new title assumed by the previous Irish Grand Mage China Sorrows . The Supreme Mage has control over all the major Sanctuaries , evident by the fact that there is no Council of Elders at the Supreme Mage's level and instead an advisor's board of three Grand Mages: the English, the American and the German Grand Mage. It is unclear how a new Supreme Mage is appointed, as China created the position herself.
