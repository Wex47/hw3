Delafonte Mien is the warden of Hammer Lane Gaol . He used Silas Nadir in order to shunt his prison through a cycle of dimensions. He was ambitious: hoping to implement this strategy in larger prisons, he was also an enthusiastic designer of technology: bragging about having created the console with which he controls the prison. This marks him out as one of just a few sorcerers who have an excellent grasp of technology.
