The Night of Knives is an event that occurred between The Dying of the Light , and Resurrection . At precisely the same time in four European countries, assassins unknown slit the throats of eleven psychics as they slept. Two of the victims include Cassandra Pharos and Finbar Wrong . It is said by Tipstaff that the "early-warning system" for "potentially catastrophic global events" has been quite limited ever since the night.

It is possible that they were killed in order to protect the identity of the mysterious King of the Darklands.
