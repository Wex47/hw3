Biography

Resurrection

He first appears in Resurrection where he sees Valkyrie Cain being attacked by three Roarhaven citizens, bitter over what she had done as Darquesse. Sebastian takes out the third mage by jumping on him from a rooftop. He informs her that he is here to help, that he is on her side. He also asks Valkyrie not to tell Skulduggery Pleasant about him. Valkyrie agrees, then promptly tells Skulduggery about him.

He meets Bennet , a member of a cult that also worships Darquesse called The Darquesse Society . Sebastian attempts to rescue Bennet's wife Odetta who has presumably been kidnapped. It turns out Odetta hadn't been kidnapped at all but had fallen in love with a Hollow Man she calls Conrad . She leaves Bennet for Conrad but Bennet still appreciates Tao's help and agrees to assist him in return by bringing Darquesse back.

Midnight

In Midnight , Sebastion, Bennet, and Tantalus sneak into the Dark Cathedral in order to get the scythe with the blood of a Faceless one on it. The reason why they were going after the scythe is that in theory, Darquesse is where ever the Faceless Ones are and they could use the blood of the Faceless one as an Isthmus anchor to get Darquesse back. This entire plan revolved around an if, though. There was a high chance that Darquesse is dead and if that is the case, the Darquesse society would be endangering the lives of 7.4 billion people for nothing.

Once they enter the Dark Cathedral though a secret passage, the team splits up so they can cover more ground. Sabastian later runs into Tanith Low after she saved him from being discovered. Tanith herself was on a mission which she later reveals to be to kill China Sorrows who was visiting Arch-Canon Damocles Creed that day. Sebastian needed China for his plan to work so he stopped her from killing China by distracting Tanith long enough for China to leave. Tanith was furious but didn't kill him.

Sebastian took the scythe from its case causing the alarm to ring. Sebastian got back to the secret passage to find Tantalus who had sent Bennet ahead. Tantalus saw Sebastian as a threat to his leadership and thought that he would take his unofficial title of leader away from him so he attacked Sebastian. Sebastian ultimately won the fight and made his way back to the rest of the Darquesse society members. They found out about what Tantalus did and they kicked him out of their group.

Bedlam

Sebastian and the rest of the Darquesse Society, using the Faceless blood, track Darquesse through dimensions, Bennet and Sebastian become closer friends, but were interrupted by the finding of Darquesse. Sebastian, using Lily 's scooter, finds Darquesse in The Faceless One's realm, and returns her to our world.

Seasons of War

In the book seasons of war, Darquesse lived in Sebastian's house. She also impregnated herself to find out if the world was worth saving, she asked Sebastian to raise herself.

Physical appearance

Sebastian is always seen wearing a beak-shaped mask of a plague doctor and a wide-brimmed hat. Sebastian acknowledges that the mask makes him look equal parts ridiculous and intimidating, but reasons that plague doctors had always looked strange. However, this causes some confusion and an inclination not to take him seriously. The mask earns him the nickname. "Beakface" by Valkyrie after he refuses to give her his Taken Name . One boy asks why he is dressed as a bird.

Sebastian also wears a long coat that flaps in the wind, due to the amount of time he spends on rooftops.
