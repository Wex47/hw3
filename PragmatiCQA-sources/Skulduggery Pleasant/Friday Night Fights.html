Friday Night Fights was a short story included in Armageddon-Outta-Here . It was released on the 3rd July 2014. .

Plot

Valkyrie Cain is kidnapped and meets Caelan for the first time. They are forced to fight another vampire but manage to escape. Caelan says that because she helped him, he would talk to some people to arrange a meeting for her with Chabon .
