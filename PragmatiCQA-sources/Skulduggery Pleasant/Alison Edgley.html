Alison "Alice" Edgley is the daughter of Melissa and Desmond Edgley , and the sister of Valkyrie Cain . In "Dead or Alive" it is revealed that in the future, she takes "Malice" as her taken name, as it sounded similar to "Alice".

Biography

Death Bringer

Alice first appeared in the series at her naming ceremony. She cried when Beryl Edgley moved towards her to take her from her older sister, as she said Valkyrie was holding her wrong, much to Beryl's annoyance.

Alice was later babysitted by both Valkyrie and Fletcher Renn . She was woken by all the noise and began crying when Ian Moore attacked Valkyrie.

Valkyrie was dubious as to whether she would introduce Alice into the world of magic .

Kingdom of the Wicked

Valkyrie spoke and played with Alice a number of times during the Summer of Light . She also put Alice in the care of her reflection .

Last Stand of Dead Men

Valkyrie still couldn't decide about revealing her secret identity to her family. After her Reflection forced her to flee her house, Alice was left in the care of it. Alice accidentally touched the Sceptre of the Ancients which panics the Reflection for a moment until it realises that she was also descended from The Ancients and immune from the power of Black Crystals .

The Dying of the Light

Alice is kidnapped by the Remnants who take her and her family including Valkyrie to Roarhaven in time for the final battle with Darquesse . During the battle, Valkyrie is forced to kill her using the Deathtouch Gauntlet so Valkyrie could obtain the Sceptre of the Ancients to battle Darquesse. She is immediately revived by a Starburst , however it is unknown at the time how this temporary death will affect her.

Resurrection

As a result of the tremendous guilt that Valkyrie felt for having to kill her sister, Alice has not seen her sister for 5 years. However at the conclusion of the Coldheart Prison adventure, Valkyrie decides that she will be more present in her life and sets aside her guilt.

Midnight

Alice is first seen playing in her house when Valkyrie comes around for dinner. She tells her sister in secret that she has overheard her parents talking and is subsequently aware of true Magic, and that her sister is a powerful mage. Valkyrie rebuffs her sister’s request to be taught, stating she needs to be at least 12 before she can start to learn. Later she is abducted by Cadaverous Gant who challenges Valkyrie to find her Sister. After a long battle Alice is the one to kill Gant by crushing him with her hands through the Midnight Hotel in the Midnight Hotel. In the aftermath of the battle it is revealed that when Valkyrie killed her, Alice's soul was split into three parts.

Bedlam

The first piece of Alice's soul was found within a fortune-teller named Margaret Kennelly . Valkyrie retrieved the soul using a soul catcher. The other piece of the soul was found at the Necropolis . After retrieving both soul fragments, Valkyrie returned them to her.

Valkyrie has a vision after returning the soul fragments. In the vision, she sees Alice eight years into the future. She is wearing protective clothing, similar to what Ghastly has made for Valkyrie. This Alice is a sorcerer, preparing to defeat her "arch-nemesis". Valkyrie begins to fret, however, Alice reassures her that "I've been doing this for years". Alice says that with this battle, the world actually hangs in the balance. Alice from the future says that she thinks she is about to change, as "everyone must sacrifice something for the future they want". Alice tells Valkyrie that this cannot be prevented, it is the final battle between the Child of the Faceless Ones and the Child of the Ancients. At the end of the vision, Alice says to Valkyrie "I miss you every single day".

Afterwards, Valkyrie goes back to Alice's room and finds her crying, something she did not do before having the soul fragments returned to her.

Seasons of War

Alice joins her family at Fergus 's birthday party. She reveals to Valkyrie that there's a new boy in her class, Dima , and that he said that she was beautiful and that he loved her after she gave a welcome card to him which was written in Russian. Much to Valkyrie's amusement, she shows annoyance when Beryl joins her when going to the bathroom.

Description

Alice has blue eyes. Thus, she was the only one of the four Edgley-children who doesn't have brown eyes.

Alice is described in Skulduggery Pleasant: Midnight as being constantly happy and content, even in situations where she should be distressed. When Valkyrie utilised her aura vision, she was unable to see the aura of Alice, implying that her temporary death at the hands of the Deathtouch Gauntlet caused her soul to split into three parts, of which only one of which returned to her.

Trivia

  • In Last Stand of Dead Men it was stated that she was eighteen months old. However, she was born in February 2011 and Last Stand of Dead Men is set in August/October 2013 which implies she should have been 30 months old. Armageddon Outta Here has established a slightly different timescale to the series, which may rectify this problem.
  • The Sceptre of the Ancients was able to bind to Alice, meaning she must be magical.
  • Derek Landy promised that whatever the gender of his niece would be, Valkyrie's new little brother or sister would be the same. His niece is a female and her name is Clara.
  • As Alice does not have an aura and it is implied she is missing her soul, it is unknown if she will still have access to any magic she may have been able to access prior to her temporary death
