Axelia Lukt is a Fourth Year student of Corrival Academy . She is from Iceland and she has an accent. She has blonde hair and blue eyes.

Biography

Resurrection

Through Resurrection , it's been said that Omen Darkly has a crush on her.

Midnight

At the start of Midnight , she is asked out by Omen, but she declines. They still remain friends after that.

Bedlam

In Bedlam she is good friends with Omen, even helping him stop hurling insults at himself, such as when he calls himself a 'fat ninja' whilst sneaking through the corridors of Corrival Academy.

Seasons of War

In Seasons of War, she helps Omen and Never to steal the Obsidian Blade from Reznor Rake and Tancred.

Trivia

  • Her name is commonly misread as 'Alexia'.
