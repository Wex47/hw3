Auger Darkly is the Chosen One and the older twin brother of Omen Darkly . He belongs to a Legacy family , and as such only chose his first name. He is romantically involved with Never .

He attends Corrival Academy along with his brother, both in their fifth year. He is very popular and people recognize his name even outside his school. Omen is often mistaken with Auger. His two closest friends include Kase and Mahala , fellow classmates who help him with his many heroic adventures. He chose them as his friends because they don't only like him because of his popularity or the prophecy. He loves his brother Omen, who feels his life is boring and uneventful, and tries to shield him from his adventures. It is shown that Auger dislikes his life, and feels immense pressure to live up to the prophecy.
