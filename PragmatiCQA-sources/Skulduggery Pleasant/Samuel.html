Biography

The End of the World

It is later revealed in the final battle that he is a vampire, and he attacks Skulduggery Pleasant . Skulduggery responds by lifting him up into the air and dropping him, breaking all the bones in his body. Samuel survives as he is a vampire, but is arrested.

Last Stand Of Dead Men

When the war breaks out, Supreme Council operatives break Samuel out of his prison, along with Foe , Charient and Obloquy . They are recruited by the Supreme Council to hunt down China Sorrows. Samuel caused Chin great harm, breaking her ribs during the ambush at her estate and breaking her collarbone and ribs(again) at Eliza Scorn 's Church of the Faceless. Later, along with the remainder of Foe's gang, he is tortured by China Sorrows and agrees (under threat of death) to work for China.

The Dying of the Light

He worked for Darquesse along with the rest of Foe’s Gang. After Obloquy and Mercy are killed, he and Foe confront Valkyrie Cain and her family. Valkyrie unleashes her new, unknown power of white lightning on Samuel, instantly charring him to a smoking carcass, killing him.

Description

Samuel was described as being very quiet and dangerous. It was said multiple times that he looked and dressed like an accountant.
