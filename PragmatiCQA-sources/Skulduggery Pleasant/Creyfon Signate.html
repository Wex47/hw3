Creyfon Signate was a Dimensional Shunter who worked for the Man with Golden Eyes and the Children of the Spider . He shunted Roarhaven City into the normal dimension .

Biography

Early Life

He came from a long line of Dimension Shunters and had not been in contact for 60 years prior to 2014.

Last Stand Of Dead Men

He was seen shunting Madame Mist into a dimension where the new Roarhaven was being built,and then was attacked by Scapegrace and Thrasher . When questioned, he revealed the terrifying nature of the Roarhaven mages' plan.

Later, he was thanked by The Man with Golden Eyes who announced to the world, via Global Link , that Roarhaven was about to become much bigger.

The Dying of the Light

He is approached by Skulduggery Pleasant and Valkyrie Cain to find the frequency to bring them to Mevolent's dimension . He agrees and finds the frequency. He then shunts there, finds a safe location to shunt Ravel to and returns. When he returns to Mevolent's Dimension with Ravel and a few others they are attacked by Redhoods . He shunts back with an injured Cleaver and tells Skulduggery and Valkyrie what happened. He is not mentioned for the rest of the book.

Dead or Alive

Signate tells Cadaver Cain, Crepuscular Vies and Omen Darkly about Creed ’s seven pillars, before he is killed by Uriah Serrate.
