Bernadette Maguire is an old woman who Paul Lynch spoke to about his visions. She worked at a shelter somewhere and used to be a teacher. She was seen at least once by Kenny Dunne .

Biography

Death Bringer

Bernadette is first mentioned by Kenny Dunne while he is being interviewed by Detective Inspector Me , because she had met Paul Lynch who was a Sensitive who had forseen the rise of the Death Bringer .

She was briefly suspected of murdering him. She was found dead in her house, Adept magic having been used there recently. She was presumably killed by Dragonclaw .
