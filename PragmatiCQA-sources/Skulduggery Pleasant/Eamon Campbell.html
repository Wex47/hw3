Eamon Campbell was a proud and efficient milkman in Ireland .

Biography

Mortal Coil

Eamon was doing his shift when Remnants possessed the people on the street he was travelling by. Eamon was attacked by a nearby jogger, whose Remnant quickly left his body to possess a little girl. The girl then attacked Eamon, giggling as he fell down by his milk van. It is unknown whether he survived or not.
