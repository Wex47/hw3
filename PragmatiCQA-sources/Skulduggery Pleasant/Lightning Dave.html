Lightning Dave is a minor character who appears in Playing with Fire and Mortal Coil . He is murdered by Tesseract in Mortal Coil . He lived in Roarhaven , and hated Vaurien Scapegrace .

History

Dave lived in Roarhaven and goes to Deadfall's pub (used to be Scapegrace's pub).

Biography

Playing With Fire

He gets into a bar fight with Skulduggery and Valkyrie . He is defeated by Skulduggery.

Mortal Coil

Dave attends am auction at the pub, which is held by Vaurien Scapegrace . After getting into an argument with Tesseract , he murders Dave by shooting him.

Magic

Dave is an Adept. His magic allows him to use lightning.
