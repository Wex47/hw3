The Doherty Clan were a family who lived in Brisbane .

Biography

The Doherty Clan were cursed as Zombies . In 2012, Hayley Skirmish and Tane Aiavao met with Skulduggery Pleasant and Valkyrie Cain to remove the curse of the Doherty Clan in a graveyard.

Skulduggery offers a staff to the zombies by throwing it in the hole where they came out of, before saying it's their's to destroy. According to Skulduggery, if they did not get out of the graveyard by midnight, they would inherit the curse. A hound then appeared and proceeded to chase them, probably with the intent of eating them. They escaped, and the hound vanished.
