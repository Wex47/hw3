Xena is a German Sheppard owned by Valkyrie Cain . She seems to show particular distaste towards Danny at first but warms to him quickly.

She doesn't pee in cars and respects Skulduggery as a figure of authority (she is alone in this).

Trivia

  • Xena is based on a German Shepherd that Derek used to own called Allie.
