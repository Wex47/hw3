Regis was a soldier who worked for The Supreme Council during the War of the Sanctuaries. He worked under General Mantis .

Biography

Last Stand of Dead Men

He led a group of soldiers, including Ashione , through The Midnight Hotel into Ireland . He was then ordered to lead a battle against The Irish Sanctuary . Later, in The Battle of Roarhaven , one of Mantis' plans backfired & both were captured. He was tied to a stake and burnt alive. Skulduggery Pleasant ordered him to be shot out of mercy, so his suffering could be reduced.

Magic

He was an Elemental.
