The Yetis were a huge set of monsters that lived in the Alps. They were installed as guardians for Tyren Lament 's cave.

Biography

Kingdom Of The Wicked

They battled Skulduggery Pleasant and Valkyrie Cain in their quest to find Tyren Lament. When one battled with the duo, it had Valkyrie's head in it's mouth but was eventually pushed off a mountain.

Description

They were described as "big and furry" by Valkyrie and they were indigenous to the alps. They were also called Kang Admi by many.

They were deadly. Skulduggery said, 'If you saw one running at you, it was already too late' and that they were strong, fast and fierce.
