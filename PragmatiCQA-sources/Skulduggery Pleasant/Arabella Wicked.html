Arabella Wicked is a teacher at Corrival Academy . She speaks in a precise manner and has a very commanding air, though she never has to raise her voice for obedience. She saves Omen Darkly 's life by stopping Jenan Ispolin from strangling him. She makes sure Ispolin is expelled.

Magic and abilities

Miss Wicked is an adept, however, her exact ability is unknown. She is able to resist punches and impacts that may significantly hurt any other person. It is possible that she has learned Enhancement – the same ability that made Mr Bliss as strong as he was.
