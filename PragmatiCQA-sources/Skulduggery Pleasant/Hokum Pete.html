History

Hokum Pete was once friends with Vaurien Scapegrace , although he did not like him very much. He now dislikes him.

Biography

Playing With Fire

Scapegrace tells Skulduggery and Valkyrie that the pub he once owned has an area in the back. They travel there and meet Hokum Pete, Deadfall among others. They get into a bar fight with Skulduggery and end up losing.

Mortal Coil

Hokum Pete meets up with Scapegrace when he is claiming to know where Skulduggery lives. Tesseract kills him and the others to force the information out of Scapegrace.

Magic

It is unknown what magical discipline Hokum Pete used.
