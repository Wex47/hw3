Aurnia is a mortal girl born in the Leibniz Universe, but she currently lives in the mortal camp in Roarhaven, dubbed the City of Tents. Omen Darkly asks her out but she has a boyfriend. She appears in Midnight and Bedlam.

Midnight

Omen asked her to take a tour of Roarhaven City with her but she told him she is not allowed to leave the camp. He then asked if she could give him a tour of her camp but she says her friends and family fear sorcerers and they wouldn’t understand.
