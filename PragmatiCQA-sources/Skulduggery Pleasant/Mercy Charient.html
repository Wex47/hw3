Biography

The End of the World

She is the one who tricks Skulduggery, Valkyrie, and Ryan into thinking she's a middle-aged woman named Francine. She and Obloquy try to activate the Doomsday Machine, but they are both knocked unconscious by Valkyrie and Skulduggery and arrested.

Last Stand Of Dead Men

She was hired to kill China Sorrows after being broken out of prison by the Supreme Council . After failing to kill her twice, she was tortured by her and then was forced to do her will.

The Dying of the Light

She takes sides with Darquesse and is later killed by Tanith Low when her head is chopped off in the caves beneath Gordon Edgley 's house.

Magic

Mercy Charient is an Energy Thrower, however, she emits the stream from her mouth rather than her hands. China Sorrows states that this is more powerful but less accurate.
