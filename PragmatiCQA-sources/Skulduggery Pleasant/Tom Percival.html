Tom Percival is the cover artist for the series . He made 3 covers for each book in Phase 1 (books 1-9). He also did character arts. However, he did not return as cover artist for Derek Landy's Demon Road Series . The End Of The World is dedicated to Tom Percival.

Tom also wrote a children's book series called Little Legends , which got a little mention in The Last Stand Of Dead Men which is almost unnoticeable unless you look for it very hard.

History

Nothing has been released about Percival's history on any of the Skulduggery Pleasant websites. However, there is quite a lot on Wikipedia.

Trivia

  • Tom has also made the new covers for the paperbacks.
  • He also made the covers for the three novellas.
  • Tom has been one of the people subject to Derek's praise in the acknowledgments in the book series. In it, Derek says that he could easily do the art for the covers, so Tom deliberately does a stand- up job each time to avoid this. He then threatened to replace Tom by more dubious means.
  • Tom's series, 'Little Legends' got a little mention in book 8.
