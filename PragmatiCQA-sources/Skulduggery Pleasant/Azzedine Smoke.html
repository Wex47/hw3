Biography

Resurrection

Smoke first appears corrupting Temper Fray . Later, he arrives to witness the infamous battle between Lethe and Skulduggery Pleasant . He later corrupts Skulduggery himself. After Skulduggery frees himself from his control, he kills Smoke by knocking him into the lake of energy in Coldheart Prison.

Powers and Abilities

Magic

Smoke is a Neoteric with the ability to corrupt people with a single touch. It lasts for two days and makes them not only insane but obedient to him whether willing or not. Valkyrie Cain describes the aura his victims have as being orange but covered by a yellow-grey stain. He can also charge objects with this, as shown by corrupting Savant Vega by putting him in a suit and corrupting the suit, creating Lethe.

Personality

Smoke is reckless and doesn't consider his actions. He's also described as unoriginal and selfish.

Description

All that is known about Smoke's appearance is that he had a black braided goatee.
