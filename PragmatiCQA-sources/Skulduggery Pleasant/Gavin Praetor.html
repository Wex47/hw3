Gavin Praetor is the American Grand Mage , replacing Zafira Kerias before him. He is described as a "trim man with a distinctly feline quality to his movements."

Biography

Praetor became Grand Mage of the American Sanctuary under the rule of Supreme Mage China Sorrows . Like the English Grand Mage, Vespers , Praetor is desperate to please China, which gets on her nerves.

Praetor also has a low opinion of the American President Martin Flanery , calling him "A boorish man. Arrogant. Narcissistic. Greedy for money and power." However, he uses this to his advantage in order to control the President.
