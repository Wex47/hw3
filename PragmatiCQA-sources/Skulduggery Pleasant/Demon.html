In the Skulduggery Pleasant universe , a Demon is a being that appeared in the original version of the Theatre of Shadows . This creature was later changed to an entity from another plain of reality in Armageddon Outta Here 's version of Theatre of Shadows. They play a far more prominent role in Derek Landy 's second series Demon Road .
