Henry is a Builder whose name was seen engraved on the wall of the Glade in the Maze Runner film . His name was not crossed out at the time, so he was alive when Thomas arrived. He is one of the few Gladers who survived the Griever attack, and is also one of the 13 Gladers who stayed with Gally in the Glade while Thomas and the others escape the Maze . It is possible Henry was picked up by WCKD just like the others. He did not appear in the books.
