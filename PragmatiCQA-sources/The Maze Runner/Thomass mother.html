Thomas' mother appeared in many of Thomas 's memory-dreams. She seemed to love her son very much.

Biography

In The Scorch Trials , Thomas had a memory-dream of her crying as she told young Thomas, "I don't understand why they chose you." Presumably, she was referring to WICKED having discovered that he was Immune and choosing him for the Trials .

In , she comforted and defended Thomas against her Flare -infected husband until people came to take him away.

Eventually, she contracted the Flare herself, presumably going mad before dying.

Appearance

Her appearance is not really described in Thomas' flashbacks. She appeared to be young, perhaps in her thirties, and light-skinned.
