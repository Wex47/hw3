In the Maze Runner film , Chuck had crafted an item he made out of wood as to remember and give to his parents if he ever got out from the Maze . But Chuck is shot in the chest by Gally , and bleeds to death, then hands Thomas his relic for he was grateful to have had him as a friend and Thomas keeps it as a reminder of him.

In the Scorch Trials film , we see Thomas going back to the helicopter where he and the Gladers are dropped off the WCKD facility to pick up Chuck 's relic. Then he hands it to Brenda and tells her the story of how his friend died.
