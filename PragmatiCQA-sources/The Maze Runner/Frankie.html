Biography

The Fever Code

In The Fever Code , Frankie is mentioned by the name Frank. He entered the Maze the month before Chuck .

The Maze Runner

Frankie was not mentioned in The Maze Runner , but it is clear that he was one of the Gladers who survived the Maze .

The Scorch Trials

In The Scorch Trials , Frankie was the first one killed in the tunnels by the Metal balls while making a joke with Winston .

Films

The Maze Runner

Frankie is only seen a few times in the Glade . he survives the Griever attack as you can see him in the background and it is assumed he is dead because in the Death Cure film , the name Frank is seen on the memorial rock along with the names of the victims in the war with WCKD .
