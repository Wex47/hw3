Aidan is a Glader whose name was seen engraved on the wall of the Glade in the Maze Runner film . His name was not crossed out at the time and therefore he was alive when Thomas arrived. He is not seen during the Griever attack, but the next day, his name was later crossed out after the attack, proving he was killed by a Griever. He did not appear in the books.
