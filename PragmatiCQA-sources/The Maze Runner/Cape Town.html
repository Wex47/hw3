Cape Town was the chosen filming location for the Last City .

Description

Cape town is one of the capitals of South Africa, a port city in the nation's southwest coast, located in a peninsula beneath the imposing Table Mountain. It was chosen due to fitting the description for the desert-like theme of the third installment of the Maze Runner movies, Maze Runner: The Death Cure .

Trivia

The scenes in the WICKED Tower was filmed in the Cape Town International Convention Center (CTICC). The street and nearby signs were covered up with logos of WICKED to make it look like it was really the WICKED Tower. Furthermore, the building structure was not an actual structure, but 3D rendered.
