The Purge was a major event which took place during The Fever Code . It occurred when most of WICKED 's subjects had gone into the two mazes (for Group A and Group B ) and there was only Thomas , Teresa , Rachel and Aris Jones left. Chancellor Kevin Anderson had recently passed the Gone and most of WICKED's staff had the The Flare but were trying to hide it. Ava Paige decided to launch the Purge. This was a backup plan for whenever the Flare got out of control within WICKED and many members of staff got infected. Ava gave all four of them a backpack filled with syringes and said that, since they were all immune to the Flare, they would be the best people for the job. It's unknown exactly what was in the syringes. It was now their duty to euthanise Chancellor Anderson and the other nineteen Flare-infected members of WICKED's staff. At first, Rachel and Thomas disliked the idea, however, Teresa and Aris talked the both of them into thinking that they were doing it for a good reason. The four of them all worked together to travel throughout WICKED and carry out the task of the Purge. During the Purge, The Creators were killed and the Purge was a success. After the Purge was complete, Teresa and Thomas were left to train the staff who were replacing the Creators (such as A.D. Janson ).
