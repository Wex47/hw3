Walton Goggins is an American actor who portrayed Lawrence in Maze Runner: The Death Cure .

Goggins is well known for his roles in "Sons of Anarchy" and "Justified"

In the film Maze Runner: The Death Cure , Goggins portrays the Crank whom Thomas and Brenda find in the underground tunnels in Maze Runner: The Scorch Trials , who constantly repeats "Rose took my nose, I suppose".
