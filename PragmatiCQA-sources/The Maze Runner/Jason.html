Jason is a Glader who appeared in the Maze Runner film . His name was on the wall and was not crossed out, so he was alive when Thomas arrived. He is confirmed dead as his name was seen on the Memorial Rock at the Safe Haven , so he most likely died during the Griever attack or was killed by WCKD . He did not appear in the books so no more information about him is known.
