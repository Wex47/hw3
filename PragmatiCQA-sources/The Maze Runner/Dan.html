Dan is a Builder whose name was seen engraved on the wall of the Glade in the Maze Runner film . His name was not crossed out at the time, and therefore he was alive when Thomas arrived. Dan is named after film editor Dan Zimmerman. Dan had long blond hair, had a serious attitude, and wore a blue (or grey) shirt. He was good friends with Gally and Hank and was often seen with them. When Thomas runs into the Maze , Dan has a shocked expression on his face. Dan was a Runner , but after Alby was stung by a Griever , he and Hank talk with Gally and then become Builders. Dan was one of the people who was against Thomas and disagreed with most of the things that he did and thought that he should be punished for going out into the Maze at night. When the Griever attack starts, Dan and Hank stayed loyal to Gally and stayed in the Box with him during the Griever attack. While hiding inside of the Box, Gally tells Dan and Hank to be quiet, then a Glader tries to join them, but is killed right in front of them by a Griever. After the attack is over, Dan and an unnamed Glader follow Gally, who approaches Thomas and punches him in the face. The next day, Dan grabs Teresa and drags her over to the pole to become an offering to the Grievers, but Teresa kicks him in the groin. Since Dan was a loyal friend of Gally, he and 13 others decided to stay in the Glade and not join the others in leaving. Dan survives and is seen in the background of the Safe Haven . He does not appear in the books.
