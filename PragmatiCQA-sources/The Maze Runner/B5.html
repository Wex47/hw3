B5 is a member of Group B . She survived the Maze in unknown circumstances. She was only ever seen at the start of Scorch Trials after the train carriage was taken in an attempt to recapture Minho . It is possible that she passed the Scorch Trials , reached the safe haven with the other Group B members, and had her Swipe removed. However, her fate is unknown.
