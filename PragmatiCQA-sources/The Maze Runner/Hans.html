Hans was a man who formerly worked as a scientist for WICKED before abandoning the organization.

  • The Death Cure

Brenda and Jorge take Thomas , Newt , and Minho to Denver where they plan to bring the boys to a doctor called Hans. Hans turns out to be an older man who lives with his wife in Denver, and he is in hiding since WICKED has offered a bounty for his capture. It is said that Hans used to work for WICKED as a scientist, but he disagreed with the unsafe procedures and had to leave before he could be punished. Hans had contacted Brenda over the Netblock and told her that he was moving to Denver, so Brenda was later able to find him there. After Hans removes the device that allowed WICKED to remotely control Thomas and Minho, the teens and Jorge offer Hans an escape from Denver. Hans refuses the offer.
