Slicers feed and raise the livestock such as pigs, hogs, cows, sheeps, chickens, and turkeys, which are all kept in pens in the Blood House area. They also clean, fix fences, and scrape up klunk . They are the ones who slaughter the animals, such as hogs, so they can be prepared by the Cooks for eating. The Keeper of the Slicers is Winston .
