The WICKED Complex is where Thomas is kept for a month in isolation after being rescued from the Scorch . He meets Janson again and reunites with the surviving Gladers and Group B . Thomas, Newt , Minho , Brenda , and Jorge later escape the Warehouse in a berg . It can be assumed that the complex was destroyed when the Right Arm attacked it in the end of the novel.

Description

The complex has a berg warehouse, weapons depot, labs, and some prison cells.
