Wes is a Glader whose name was seen engraved on the wall. His name was not crossed out at the time when Thomas arrived and therefore was alive at that time. During the Griever attack, he is killed as his name is seen on the memorial rock at the Safe Haven. He did not appear in the books.

Trivia

  • Wes could be named after the director Wes Ball , or Glader Actor Weston Rachal. His name is seen carved in the memorial rock in the death cure film, confirming his fate.
