Asheville is a real-life city in North Carolina, USA.

In the Maze Runner series , Asheville is where the Post-Flares Coalition 's main building was located. It is seen in The Kill Order when Mark and Alec deliver Deedee to WICKED through a Flat Trans in the PFC building. Bruce and other PFC turncoats are seen climbing a wall to attack the PFC. Mark and Alec flew their berg into the building to destroy the Flat Trans after Deedee got though; this way no Cranks could get to the WICKED complex. Asheville is not mentioned in any other books, and it is unknown whether its society collapsed after that or continued thriving.
