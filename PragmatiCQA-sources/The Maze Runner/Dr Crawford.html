Dr. Crawford appears briefly in the Scorch Trials film . She is one of the doctors of WCKD organization.

Biography

When Thomas and his friends try to escape through the tunnels under the Scorch to find Teresa , Dr. Crawford surprises them in midway. The Gladers take her hostage and force her to the medical block where they find Teresa sleeping on a bed. They tie up Dr. Crawford along with the other doctors of the WCKD organization and escape out of the block.

Appearance

Dr. Crawford appears in the film with dark hair and a dark skin tone.
