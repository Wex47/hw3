Med-jacks are specialized Gladers who acted as the equivalent to doctors in the Glade . Their roles were prominent in the first book, The Maze Runner . There are only two Med-jacks in the Glade: Clint , who is the Keeper of the Med-jacks, and Jeff .

After Teresa arrives in the Box , in a state of comatose, Newt calls for the Med-jacks, making Thomas realize that they were the closest things the Gladers had to doctors. Unfortunately, neither Clint or Jeff survived to the Scorch Trials .
