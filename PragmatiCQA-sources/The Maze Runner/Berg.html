Berg

Owner

WICKED and the Post-Flares Coalition

Appearances

Books: The Scorch Trials The Death Cure The Kill Order Films: Maze Runner: The Scorch Trials Maze Runner: The Death Cure

Abilities

Can vertically take off and land

Size

Aircraft sized

A Berg is a very large aerial vehicle employed by WICKED as transportation during the Maze Runner series . Bergs were also used by the Post-Flares Coalition to spread the Flare . Apparently, there was a large international fleet before the Sun Flares .

In the film adaptations , Bergs have two large helicopter-like blades on either side, with rotatable jet engines on the back that are pointed down when the craft lands. Bergs also have twin GAU-8/A miniguns located towards the front of the aircraft (as seen in the Death Cure film ).

Known appearances

  • In The Scorch Trials , a Berg appears at the end of the book to pick up Thomas and his group after they fought off the Bulb Monsters . Also used in the movie to bring in WCKD soldiers to find Thomas and his group.
  • In The Death Cure , Thomas and his group steal a Berg to escape WICKED 's headquarters and get to Denver .
  • In The Kill Order , the Post-Flares Coalition used Bergs to spread the Flare in various settlements. Alec and Mark also stole a Berg to get to Asheville .

Trivia

  • "Berg" is German for mountain, possibly referring to the large size. To avoid misunderstandings, it is referred to as "Berk" in this language.
