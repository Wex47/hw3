Dylan O'Brien is an American actor born in New York City. He is most known for his role as Stiles Stilinski on the MTV show Teen Wolf . In addition to acting in film and television, he has produced, directed, and starred in a number of comedic short films produced for and released through his personal YouTube channel.

O'Brien portrays Thomas in the film adaptations of James Dashner 's The Maze Runner , its sequel Maze Runner: The Scorch Trials , and Maze Runner: The Death Cure , the final installment in the Maze Runner Movie trilogy.
