Maze Runner: The Death Cure

When Lawrence rallies his Crank army outside of the Last City 's walls, he convinces them to follow, before he sacrifices himself to destroy the doors of the city. This allows the army to pour in and consume the city. They move on from fighting the WCKD guards and take to destroying some of the buildings, including WCKD Tower . In the chaos, Newt , Ava Paige , Janson , and Teresa are killed. By the end of the movie , the Cranks had taken out one quarter of the city.
