Ki Hong Lee , (이기홍 I Gi-hong ) is an actor and writer, known for the short movie She Has a Boyfriend (2013), This Is How We Never Met (2013), The Nine Lives of Chloe King (2011), and many others.

Lee plays Minho in the film adaptation of James Dashner 's The Maze Runner , and reprises the role in Maze Runner: The Scorch Trials , released in 2015, and Maze Runner: The Death Cure , released in 2018.

Recently, on his Instagram account (@kihonglee), Lee was proud to announce his new mini series Mr. Soulmate , in which he is the lead actor, director, and producer.

Ki Hong Lee is married to Hayoung Choi.
