Dexter Darden is an actor and singer most known for his roles in Minutemen (2008), Cadillac Records (2008), and Joyful Noise (2012).

Darden portrays Frypan in the film adaptation of James Dashner 's The Maze Runner , and reprises the role in Maze Runner: The Scorch Trials , released in 2015, and Maze Runner: The Death Cure , released in 2018.
