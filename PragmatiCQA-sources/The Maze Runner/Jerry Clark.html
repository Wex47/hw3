Jerry Clark is an American actor that portrayed Peter in The Maze Runner . He was born in the southeastern parts of USA.

Biography

He has most recently been cast as a Treblemaker in the feature film, "Pitch Perfect 2, Peter in "The Maze Runner", and TV pilot, "Help Wanted". Jerry has also worked with Saint Studios as an assistant DOP and PA. Jerry is pursuing his degree in journalism and theatre at Louisiana College while working on various independent film projects. Jerry's experience is not limited to acting; he has toured with the Memphis Pop Punk band, The Vital Society, and has collaborated on music projects with Universal Motown's Forever the Sickest Kids. He has also performed at Vans Warped Tour with pop punk band, Frenchie.
