William Jack " Will " Poulter is an English actor who is most known for his roles as Lee Carter in Son of Rambow (2007), Eustace Scrubb in The Chronicles of Narnia: The Voyage of the Dawn Treader (2010), and Kenny in We're the Millers (2013).

Poulter portrays Gally in the film adaptation of James Dashner 's novel The Maze Runner , and reprises the role in Maze Runner: The Death Cure , released in 2018.
