The Death Cure
Dr. Wright is introduced to Thomas as the lead Psych of WICKED by Assistant Director Janson , also known as Rat Man, when Thomas returns to the WICKED headquarters to help the Right Arm by planting a device in the headquarters that will render the organization's weapons useless. She takes part in the discussion with Janson, Dr. Christensen and Thomas about what Thomas will have to do as Final Candidate. She is described as a woman with long red hair, wearing a dark pants suit and thin-framed glasses, with a soft and pleasant voice that exuded intelligence.
