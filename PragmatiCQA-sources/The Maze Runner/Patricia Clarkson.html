Patricia Clarkson is an American actress. She has starred in many leading and supporting roles in numerous well-known films such as The Untouchables , The Green Mile , and Vicky Cristina Barcelona , and she was nominated for an Academy Award for Best Supporting Actress for her performance in Pieces of April .

Clarkson played Chancellor Ava Paige in the film adaptation of James Dashner 's The Maze Runner , and reprises the role in Maze Runner: The Scorch Trials , released in 2015, and Maze Runner: The Death Cure , released in 2018.
