In the hierarchy of the Glade , the Second-in-Command is in charge whenever the leader is not available. There seem to be two instances of teens with the role of Second-in-Command in the books . Newt is the Second-in-Command below Alby , and it seems that Sonya could have the same role within Group B , second to Harriet . This is an interesting coincidence since Newt and Sonya are siblings.

Second-in-Command

  • Sonya (presumably)
