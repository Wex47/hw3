Wil was a Glader whose name was seen engraved on the wall of the Glade in the Maze Runner film . Their name was uncrossed at the time meaning they were alive when Thomas arrived. Their name is not seen when the camera points at the wall, so it is unknown if he died. It is possible he is portrayed by actor William Buckley, but it is not confirmed. He did not appear in the books.
