Julio Ramirez was a member of WICKED , as well as a chief of security.

Biography

In The Fever Code , Ramirez escorts Thomas to an observation room to watch a Griever be unleashed on Minho , as the boy's punishment for trying to escape WICKED. Later, he is among the group of WICKED members who Dr. Paige infects with the Flare to make sure the Trials were not stopped after their first phase. Ramirez ends up getting killed during the Purge.

Physical Appearance and Personality

Ramirez is described as "a serious-looking man with darker skin, gray hair, and stubble on his cheeks." He also did not speak very much, but could be stern and authoritative when he did.
