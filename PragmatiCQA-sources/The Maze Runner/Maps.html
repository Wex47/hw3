Maps are made by the Runners everyday after they run through the Maze in an effort to find a way out to escape the Maze. They are kept in the Map Room in the Glade . In the Maze Runner film , the Runners build one big map of the Maze using twigs and small branches along with other similar objects. They write each Section of the Maze on a rock and place it in its proper place.

Uses

Maps are helpful in understanding the patterns of the Maze . Thomas traces the maps onto wax paper and places each section on top of another, which results in him, Teresa , Newt , Minho , and the others working out the code.
