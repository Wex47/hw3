Wyck is a Glader whose name was seen engraved on the wall of the Glade in the Maze Runner film . He is named after the producer, Wyck Godfrey. His name was not crossed out at the time and therefore was alive when Thomas arrived. He is confirmed deceased as his name was on the memorial rock, he most likely died during the Griever attack or by Wicked. He does not appear in the books.
