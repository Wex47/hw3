Mark Gibson is a Canadian actor, director and screenwriter, living and working in Toronto. He was born and raised in Winnipeg, Manitoba and graduated for Acting Conservatory at York University in Toronto. Gibson is mostly known for his work in the indie film Exit Humanity (2011). Along with pursuing his passion for indie film, Gibson continues to work in both Canadian and American television. During The Maze Runner (film) , he portrayed a Glader named Alec. He was last seen in Maze Runner: The Death Cure as the same character.
