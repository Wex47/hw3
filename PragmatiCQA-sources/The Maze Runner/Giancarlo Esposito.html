Giancarlo Esposito is an American actor, director, and producer. He is best known for playing Gustavo "Gus" Fring on Breaking Bad.

Esposito plays Jorge in Maze Runner: The Scorch Trials , and reprises his role in Maze Runner: The Death Cure .
