Each Glader is assigned a role based on what they can do best. There is a Keeper for each job, which the Newbie works with for a day. At the end of two weeks, the Newbie is chosen for a role in one of the jobs based on the work they did best.

Jobs

First-in-Command

The first in command is the leader of the Gladers, and of the gathering. The first of command is usually the glader that has been in the glade the longest. They make sure everything is going well. The first-in-command is Alby .

Second-in-Command

The second in command is like the first in command, but is number 2. If the first in command isn't able to do his duties, the second in command is the leader. The second-in-command is Newt .

Runner

Runners are the only ones who are allowed to go out into the Maze . They are defined as the strongest, fastest, and best of the Gladers . Runners run the Maze everyday, trying to find a way out. The maps they make are saved in the Map Room . They leave when the doors to the Maze open in the morning, and return right before the doors close. They also have equipment like trainers and watches that are unavailable to the rest of the Gladers. The Keeper of the Runners is Minho .

Builder

Builders are the ones who build most of the things around the Glade , such as structures and other simple items that would help with carrying and moving things around. They also upgrade the structures and buildings. The Keeper of the Builders is Gally .

Cook

Cooks are the Gladers responsible for preparing the meals of the Glade . They spend most of their time in the kitchen. They make food from the work of the Blood Housers and Track-hoes. The Keeper of the Cooks is Frypan .

Slicer

Slicers feed and raise the livestock such as pigs, hogs, cows, sheeps, chickens, and turkeys, which are all kept in pens in the Blood House area. They also clean, fix fences, and scrape up klunk . They are the ones who slaughter the animals, such as hogs, so they can be prepared by the Blood Housers and Cooks for eating. The Keeper of the Slicers is Winston .

Blood Housers

Blood Housers take the meat of the slicers and prepare it before giving it to the cooks. The Keeper of the Blood Housers is not mentioned in the book.

Track-hoe

Track-hoes do difficult work related to plants, such as trenching. In the movie, they do gardening, but in the book, they do other plant-related work. The Keeper of the Track-Hoes is Zart .

Gardeners

Gardeners prepare fruits and vegetables for the cooks. They take care of the plants in the Gardens: weeding, pruning, planting seeds, and harvesting veggies are examples of the things they do. The Keeper of the Gardeners was not mentioned in the book.

Med-jack

Med-jacks are specialized Gladers who act as the equivalent to doctors in the Glade . Their roles are prominent in the first book, The Maze Runner . There are only two Med-jacks in the Glade. The Keeper of the Med-Jacks is Clint .

Slopper

Sloppers are the Gladers who are not good at any of the jobs, so they help around the Glade mostly by doing the dirty tasks that other Gladers don't want to do, such as cleaning up the Blood House, the kitchen, toilets, and showers. The Keeper of the Sloppers isn't mentioned in the book.

Bricknick

Bricknicks are the Gladers who have similar responsibilities to the Builders , but are mainly tasked with repairing structures instead of building them. Bricknicks were not mentioned in the book, nor was the keeper. There might have been Bricknicks in the book, just they weren't mentioned.

Bagger

Baggers are the ones in charge of burying dead Gladers . They also act as guards and police around the Glade . The Keeper of the Baggers is Billy .

Map-maker

Map-makers are the ones who make the maps of the Maze with the runners. They spend their day studying the maps while the runners run. The Keeper of the Map-makers wasn't mentioned in the book.

Trivia

  • Each Glader takes part in more than one job, helping other Gladers around the Glade whenever they can. However, their priorities lie with the jobs they were originally chosen for.
  • Slopper is the lowest ranked job a Glader can have.
  • There are no Bricknicks mentioned or introduced in the book and little to no information has been given about them or the work they do.
  • In The Maze Runner , Newt says that they always send the Greenies to the Slicers as their first job.
  • Zart refers to the Baggers as "creepy fellas".
  • There are no known Map-makers in the Glade.
  • In the book, Track-hoe and Gardener are two different jobs. In the movie , it is all the same.
