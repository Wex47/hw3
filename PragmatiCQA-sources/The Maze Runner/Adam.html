Adam was mentioned by Newt in The Maze Runner . He was taken by the Grievers the day after Gally was taken. Thomas felt guilty for feeling no sorrow at the news of Adam's apparent death, as he did not know the boy.

In the Maze Runner film , Adam's name is seen engraved on the wall of the Glade . During the Griever attack, Adam is killed by a Griever .
