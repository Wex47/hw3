Mary Cooper
Lili Anne Taylor is an American actress, born on February 20, 1967. She portrayed Mary Cooper in Maze Runner: The Scorch Trials .

She is known for being in indie films, such as Mystic Pizza (1988), Say Anything ... (1989), Dogfight (1991), Short Cuts (1993) and I Shot Andy Warhol (1996). She is also known for being in films like Ransom (1996), The Haunting (1999) and The Conjuring (2013), and appeared in the television show Six Feet Under (2002–2005).
