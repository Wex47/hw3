Rob is a Glader whose name was seen engraved on the wall of the Glade in the Maze Runner film . His name was not crossed out at the time, therefore he was alive when Thomas arrived. His name is not seen when the camera points at the wall after the griever attack, so it is unknown if he died. He did not appear in the books.
