John Michael was one of the first, if not the first, chancellors of WICKED . Beforehand, he was chancellor of the Post-Flares Coalition , from which WICKED came. With his approval, The Flare virus was released to control the population. He was also a friend of Katie McVoy before she committed suicide.

Michael appears in The Fever Code as the Flare-ravaged former WICKED chancellor. After Thomas , Teresa , Minho , and Alby are forced to venture into the Crank pits , Michael appears, holding a small chalkboard reading "WICKED is good." He begs for the kids to help find a cure , and then, per his request, Randall Spilker shoots him.
