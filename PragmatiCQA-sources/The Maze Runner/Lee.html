Lee is a Glader whose name was seen engraved on the wall of the Glade in the Maze Runner film . His name was not crossed out at the time, therefore he was alive when Thomas arrived. The name Lee is a reference to the producer of the movie, Lee Stollman. Lee was killed in the grievers attack as his name was crossed out. He did not appear in the books.
