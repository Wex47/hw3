Alec is a Builder whose name was seen engraved on the wall of the Glade in the Maze Runner film . Alec survived during the Griever attack. He was one of the Gladers who helped tie up Thomas and Teresa along with Dan . He then attempts to tie Thomas to a wooden beam for the offering. However, Thomas wakes up and hits Alec in the face with a spear, knocking him to the ground. Alec had no choice but to stay with Gally in the Glade. It is possible Alec was picked up by WCKD just like the others. He does not appear in the books.
