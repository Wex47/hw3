A Flat Trans (short for Flat Transportation) is a transporter, or teleporter, that is described as being a "shimmering wall of grey". The Gladers use one at the start of The Scorch Trials to enter the tunnel under the Scorch , and, later on, find out that they are extremely expensive, which is why no one believes their story of how they got in the Scorch.

It is seen in the Fever Code that it scrambles your molecules and reassembles them at the end of the Flat Trans.

All of the Immunes use a Flat Trans at the end of The Death Cure to escape from the WICKED headquarters into a place they call paradise . It is last place on Earth that hasn't been destroyed by the Sun Flares or Cranks .

A Flat Trans is also referenced in The Kill Order several times. At the end of the book, the Post-Flares Coalition scientists as well as Deedee escape from Asheville through a Flat Trans. Upon walking through the Flat Trans, the feeling is described as a cold line running across your body as you step through.

It is revealed that the Flat Trans can be controlled when Brenda makes it disappear from existence using a control panel next to it in The Death Cure .

In The Fever Code , Thomas , Brenda, and Jorge use a Flat Trans to return from their expedition to the Scorch.
