Train Heist

Objective:

Thomas and the Right Arm Resistance to storm WCKD train and find Minho

Casualties

Several unnamed WCKD guards

Status:

Failed

Movie:

Maze Runner: The Death Cure

Maze Runner: The Death Cure (Movie)

Thomas , Brenda , Jorge , Vince , Tim and Billy intercepted the train via jeeps. Thomas and Vince boarded the train before Vince realized that WCKD had sent out a Berg to follow them. Thomas informs Brenda about that, so she and Jorge decide to distract it. Thomas and Vince go across the train, but are intercepted by the WCKD guards that are onboard. Thomas distracts the guards, allowing Vince to plant a small explosive that disconnects the carriages. Thomas finds Minho in one after carriages after he bangs on the side and recognizes Minho's scream. Thomas sends Newt and Billy to start disconnecting the carriage. Brenda and Jorge corner the Berg, overpower the guard, and hijack the Berg. They lower a winch, which Thomas helps connect to the carriage. They lift the carriage off the train and leave. The title card appears.

After the title card, we see Janson and some WICKED soldiers examining the scene where the train heist occurred. This is when we first find out that they didn't manage to get Minho back during the heist. Janson says "They didn't get what they really wanted." whilst he looks down at Minho.

When they get back to New Headquarters, they find out that oddly, Minho was not on board, leading Thomas and his friends to find him inside of the Last City .

Members:

Thomas, Newt, Vince, Brenda, Jorge, Frypan/Siggy, Tim, Billy, right arm members.
