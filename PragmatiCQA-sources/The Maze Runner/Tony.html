Tony was a Berg pilot who worked for WICKED . When Teresa Agnes and most of Group A and Group B escaped from WICKED, they forced Tony to come along and fly them to Denver . Jorge mentioned this afterward to Thomas and the others. Tony is an extremely minor character, and was only mentioned once.
