Jackson is a Glader whose name was seen engraved on the wall of the Glade in the Maze Runner film . His name was not crossed out at the time, therefore he was alive when Thomas arrived. Jackson is immediately killed by a Griever as seen his name was crossed out after the attack, unlike in the book where he makes it to the Death Cure film .
