Alan Tudyk is an American actor and voice actor. He portrayed Marcus in Maze Runner: The Scorch Trials .

Tudyk has had many film roles throughout the years, including the droid K2-SO in Rogue One: A Star Wars Story . He is also widely known for his voice acting career in Walt Disney Studios such as King Candy from Wreck it Ralph, Alistair Krei in Big Hero 6, the chicken, Hei Hei in Moana and many more.
