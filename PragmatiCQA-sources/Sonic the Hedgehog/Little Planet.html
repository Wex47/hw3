Little Planet , sometimes called "Miracle Planet" , is a tiny planetoid that passes Sonic's World once a year.

History

Metal Sonic once kidnapped a young Amy Rose on the Little Planet. She was eventually rescued by Sonic the Hedgehog . Some time later, Dr. Eggman encapsulated Little Planet inside his Death Egg Mk. II , which was eventually brought offline by Sonic and Tails . ( VG : SCD , StH4 , StH : #260 )
