Worlds Unite Battles are a pair of special one shot issues released in conjunction with the Worlds Unite crossover event. The purpose of the series is to show case the various battles between the countless characters of the crossover that there was no space to include in the main story.

Issues

  • Mega Man: Worlds Unite Battles
  • Sonic the Hedgehog: Worlds Unite Battles

Background Information

  • While the stories in the books are all considered canon to the crossover, none of them contain any major plot developement.
    • The only story in either book that is still canon even after the events of the crossover are wiped by the paradox is the first books "The Maverick Hunters vs. Vile" (with the exception of the last three panels), as it occurs before the moment when Xander Payne changes the timeline.
