Espio Man was the Roboticized Master version of Espio the Chameleon , created using the robotics of the Year 20XX and the Roboticization process of Mobius .

History

Transformations

Having been hired by Sonic the Hedgehog to locate the missing Tails , Amy Rose , and Knuckles the Echidna , Espio and his teammates were subdued and captured by the Copybot and delivered to Dr. Eggman and Dr. Wily . Converted into Espio Man, he joined the other Roboticized Masters in attacking Sonic and his team of allies-including Wily's nemesis Mega Man -as they made their way deeper into the Skull Egg Zone . During the battle, Mega Man's brother Proto Man broke away from the fighting, and Espio Man was left to battle Sonic and the other heroes alongside Vector Man and Charmy Man while the other Masters pursued him. While Espio Man and his teammates proved formidable adversaries, the heroes were able to defeat them after Tails modified Mega Man's Mega Buster weapon to duplicate the powers of Sonic's Spin-Dash . As a result, Espio Man was restored to normal. ( StH : #249 , SU : #51 , #52 )

Abilities

Like his organic self, Espio Man was capable of turning completely invisible, an ability that manifested as his Chroma Camo power. He also retained his proficiency with kunai, but like his fellow Roboticized Masters was vulnerable to the combined powers of Sonic and Mega Man.

Background Information

  • While fighting Espio Man, Sonic-in his usual attempts at banter with his enemies-suggested that he might be called "Sting-" before being cut off by an attack. This was a reference to the Mega Man enemy Sting Chameleon .
