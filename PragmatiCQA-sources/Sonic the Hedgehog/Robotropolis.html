Mobotropolis is a city nestled in the Royal Hills Zone on Westside Island and ruled over by King Acorn . It was taken over by Dr. Eggman when he seized control of the island, but eventually liberated by the Freedom Fighters under Sonic the Hedgehog . Since then it has returned to its capital status and served as a home for the Freedom Fighters.

History

Threatened by Eggman

Mobotropolis was built on Westside Island , and eventually came to be ruled by King Acorn . After Dr. Eggman initiated his coup, he took over the city, but left it in its previous condition, something that Rotor saw when he arrived in the city. Years later, the Knothole Freedom Fighters would liberate the city from Eggman's clutches and restore King Acorn to the throne. However, his reign would briefly be interrupted when wizard Walter Naugus imprisoned him using technology stolen from Dr. Eggman. Fresh from the events of Worlds Collide , Sonic awoke in the middle of the city (on a rainy day) to discover the place was being attacked by the Tails Doll -which had arrived in pursuit of Naugus-and that he and Tails were already in the process of fighting it. After shaking off his confusion at why everything was different, Sonic successfully disabled the demented doll and kicked it over the horizon. ( FCBD 2014 , StH : #252 )

Later on, the city was on attack again when Metal Sonic was on a mission to kill Sally Acorn. She was also accompanied by Cream, Amy Rose, Bean and Bark (though the latter two escaped because they were wanted criminals), who have just returned from their adventures in the Sol Zone. Sonic later came to the resuce to direct Metal Sonic away from the city, and because he had other urgent matters at hand, Tails, on the Tornado , fired a missile at Metal, though he survived the attack enough to fly back to the Death Egg . (StH: #256 )

Shattered World Crisis

Mobotropolis and the rest of Westside Island were fortunately spared the worst effects of The Shattered World Crisis . ( StH : #257 )

Background Information

  • Mobotropolis is one of the only elements in the Post-Super Genesis Wave Timeline to retain any reference to the planet's Pre-Super Genesis Wave name of Mobius.
