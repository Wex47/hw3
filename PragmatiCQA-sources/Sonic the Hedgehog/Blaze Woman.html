Blaze Woman is the Robian version of Blaze the Cat , created using the robotics of the Year 20XX and the Roboticization process of Mobius . She is one of the Roboticized Masters .

History

Evil Enforcer

Having been captured by Dr. Eggman and Dr. Wily , Blaze was converted into one of their Roboticized Masters . Paired with Silver Man , she was dispatched to retrieve the green Chaos Emerald , a task that she quickly accomplished. The pair would subsequently join their fellow Masters in attacking Sonic the Hedgehog and Mega Man 's team. Blaze Woman and most of her teammates subsequently left the fight with the main group of heroes to the Roboticized Chaotix while they went after Proto Man . When the trio of detectives were restored to normal, Eggman recalled Blaze Woman and Silver Man to the Wily Egg as a precautionary measure. ( StH : #249 , SU : #52 )

Restored Hero

Following Shadow Man 's restoration, Blaze Woman and her partner were sent to engage the heroes as well. Blaze Woman ended up battling Sonic and Tails, who were at a loss as to how they could counter her fire powers. Help arrived in the form of Mega Man and a restored Silver , who used his psychic powers to hold the Roboticized Master long enough for Mega Man to return her to her organic form. ( MM : #26 )

Abilities

Blaze Woman retains her organic form's pyrokinetic abilities, particularly in the form of her Fire Tornado attack. She possessed the same vulnerability to Sonic and Mega Man's powers as the other Roboticized Masters, and could also be immobilized by Silver the Hedgehog 's psychic abilities.

Background Information

  • Blaze Woman's teamup with Silver Man is likely a nod to the friendship between their organic forms in Sonic the Hedgehog (2006).

Appearance

Blaze Woman resembles a Robot Master-esque version of Blaze, with a flattened gray muzzle and lavender armor. Her forehead jewel has become fused to her metallic body, as have her mantle, gloves, and boots. Her wrists feature red bands with spikes erupting from them, and her limbs are bulkier and more rounded while her torso and shoulders are boxier. Her organic form's ponytail is manifested as a single spike, and her lower legs are purple while her waist and thighs are white. She retains her tail, which is purple and segmented and has a flame burning at the tip of it.
