Isolated Island is an island on the planet Mobius in the Prime Zone and home to Dr. Ellidy .

History

Paradise of Geniuses

Having once been part of an operation conducted by Dr. Eggman , many Badniks were abandoned on the island until Ellidy reprogrammed them to be his attendants. The island was also found to be home to a Lake of Rings , which went curiously dormant during the Shattered World Crisis prior to producing a Red Star Ring . Dark Gaia's Minions attacked it as well, but were kept at bay by Ellidy's Badniks until Phage took control of them. She did so in part because she detected a Chaos Emerald present on the island, and sought to claim it for Eggman. Fortunately, several members of the Knothole Freedom Fighters had arrived on the island previously to investigate Phage's initial attack on Dr. Ellidy. ( VG : KC , SU : #71 , #72 , #73 )
