The Eusebes Shrine is a mystical shrine situated in a Chao Garden in an undersea cave near the city of Meropis . The shrine is the home and workplace of a priestess, whose responsibilities involve caring for the Chao and maintaining the protective shield that keeps the city safe from dangers beyond the Anostos Rift . The Shattered World Crisis found Coral the Beta serving as priestess, with Pearly the Manta Ray serving as her apprentice. The shrine is also protected by a guardian, and at the time of the crisis this office was filled by Razor the Shark .
