This article concerns continuity on a story-to-story basis. As such, issues may be placed in order of approximate occurence or narrative consistency. For the current timeline of events on Sonic's World, see Sonic's World .

The Post-Super Genesis Wave Timeline , also called the New Sonic Comic Universe , is the current continuity of the Archieverse , occurring after the attempted reversion of the reality changing Super Genesis Wave by Super Sonic with Chaos Control was interrupted by an attack from Dr. Eggman .
