Aleah Baker is a reoccuring writer and colorist for the Archie Sonic titles . She has mostly lent her talents to the Off-Panel strips as a writer and a colorist. She recently started writing proper stories for the comic, beginning with StH#260 's back-up story.

She is the wife of current head Sonic writer Ian Flynn .
