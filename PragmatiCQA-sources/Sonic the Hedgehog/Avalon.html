Avalon is a region of Eurish in Sonic's World . It is the host region to the Eurish Egg Army led by Egg Boss Lord Hood , and is also home to the Avalon Freedom Fighters .

History

At some unknown point, Mordred Hood was established as leader of the Egg Army unit stationed in Avalon. However, his forces came under harassment from the Avalon Freedom Fighters under Bow Sparrow . Prior to the Shattered World Crisis , their efforts had harried the army enough that a G.U.N. aircraft carrier was able to move in offshore and begin harassing the army themselves. However, Dr. Eggman put an end to their efforts by attacking the carrier with a walker built from his Egg Mobile . ( StH : #254 )
