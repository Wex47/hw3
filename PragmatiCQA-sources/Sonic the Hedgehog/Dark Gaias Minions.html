The Dark Gaia Creatures are a group of malevolent creatures who are the result of Dark Gaia breaking apart due to premature awaking. Taking many forms and appearing in virtually every locale, they posed a severe threat to Sonic's World and its inhabitants during The Shattered World Crisis .

History

Free to Terrorize

Shortly after The Shattered World Crisis began, the Dark Gaia Creatures began appearing in various locations around the world . G.U.N. forces contended with them in Westopolis , while Knuckles the Echidna and the Chaotix were forced to battle them in the Pumpkin Hill Zone . A large concentration of the creatures took up residence in the Anostos Rift near the undersea city of Meropis , where they were kept back only by the protective shield projected by the Eusebes Shrine . Unfortunately, mounting pressure on Coral the Beta , the priestess charged with maintaining the shield, led to a double breakdown: her emotional state and the shield itself. No longer impeded, the minions began swarming the city, clashing with the Meropis City Guard . They also began attacking the shrine, leading to skirmishes with Sonic the Hedgehog , Amy Rose , Rotor Walrus , and Razor the Shark . Their leader, a Dark Guardian , engaged Sonic and Rotor but was then attacked by Chaos , and all were destroyed when the shield was raised anew. ( StH : #260 , #261 , #262 , #263 ; SU : #63 )

At about the same time, additional minions invaded Pumpkin Hill Zone and Aquatic Mine , led by a Dark Gaia Titan , but were defeated by Knuckles and the Chaotix. Several of them later attacked the Sky Patrol but were held off by several of the Knothole Freedom Fighters until the sun rose and caused them to dissipate. More also appeared on Isolated Island , and were held off on several occasions by Dr. Ellidy 's reprogrammed Badniks . However, the two forces later became one in attacking Ellidy and the Freedom Fighters after the robots were commandeered by Phage . Fortunately, her defeat saw the Badniks return to normal, and the minions were defeated. ( SU : #65 , #66 , #71 , #72 , #73 , #74 )
