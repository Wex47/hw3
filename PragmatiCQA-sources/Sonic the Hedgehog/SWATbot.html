SWATbots were military robots developed by Dr. Ivo "Eggman" Robotnik which served in a multitude of roles from infantry to vehicle crews.

History

Eggman's Enforcers

The SWATbots served as part of Eggman's Badnik Horde during his early campaigns of conquest, with a number of them being present during King Acorn and Walter Naugus ' banishment to the . However, they were eventually phased out and replaced by a later model, the Egg SWAT . ( SSD : #12 ; StH : #252 )

Personality

SWATbots are not known to have possessed any personality other than blind loyalty to Dr. Eggman.
