The Egg Mobile is a personal transport craft employed by Dr. Ivo "Eggman" Robotnik in his efforts to conquer the world . It can be fitted with weaponry or even attached to other vehicles to enable the doctor to battle various enemies, most often Sonic the Hedgehog .

History

Vehicle of Evil

The Egg Mobile served as Eggman's personal transport throughout his early battles with Sonic the Hedgehog and the Freedom Fighters . This included usage on South Island , where one is known to have been piloted by Coconuts as well, and Westside Island . Eggman also utilized it in his machinations on Little Planet and Angel Island , notably using it to steal the Master Emerald from Knuckles the Echidna . Eggman would go on to pilot the Egg Mobile in his schemes surrounding Chaos , with it serving as the cockpit of his Egg Viper mech in a showdown with Sonic. A walker version was utilized by Eggman when he worked alongside Shadow the Hedgehog and Rouge the Bat against Sonic, Knuckles, and Miles "Tails" Prower , and Metal Sonic even utilized one during his impersonation of Eggman. Dr. Eggman Nega also utilized an Egg Mobile of his own during his team up endeavors with Dr. Eggman against Sonic and Blaze the Cat . ( VG : StH1 , StH2 , StH3 , S&K , SA , SA2 , SRu , SRuA , StH : #268 )

A Whole New World

After being ejected from a previous timeline and crash-landing in the Efrika Plains in the remains of the Egg-Wily Machine X , Eggman salvaged his damaged Egg Mobile from the wreckage and set out to return to his Death Egg . Incapable of flight, the Egg Mobile was rolled until the Efrika Egg Army located Eggman, Orbot , and Cubot , who were then taken back to the Egg Army base with their damaged craft in tow. A quick overhaul made it flight capable once again, allowing Eggman and his minions to travel to the Eurish Egg Army 's headquarters, which he found under attack by a G.U.N. carrier. Converting the Egg Mobile into a walker mech, Eggman used it to attack and sink the G.U.N. carrier, sacrificing it in favor of a fighter jet. ( StH : #251 , #252 , #253 )

Design and Abilities

Green Seaside Zone

When used in Green Seaside Zone , the Egg Mobile was modified to be a chopper with a machine gun.

Background Information

  • The Egg Mobile is based on the craft piloted by Dr. Robotnik in the original Sonic video games.
  • While originally piloted by Dr. Eggman, the Egg Mobile has had several other pilots.
  • An alternate version of Dr. Eggman is known to also use an Egg Mobile.
  • In the SatAm series, Robotnik rode in a simple hovercraft. In the comics, which follow both the series and the games, Robotnik rides in his trademark Egg Mobile.
