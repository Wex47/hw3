Crystal Cave is, as its name implies, a beautiful crystalline cave. ( StH : #261 )

History

The cave's location and accompanying Chaos Emerald were under the surveillance of the local Egg Bosses , Cassia and Clove , till a group of Freedom Fighters lead by Sally Acorn made off with the Emerald by disabling its security defenses. ( StH : #261 , #262 , #263 , #264 )

Background Information

  • This area is apparently based on the Crystal Cave level from Sonic and the Black Knight .
