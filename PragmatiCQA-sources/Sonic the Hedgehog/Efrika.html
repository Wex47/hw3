Efrika is a continent in the eastern hemisphere of Sonic's World known to be home to the vast Efrika Plains .

History

Dr. Eggman landed in the Efrika Plains in the wreckage of the Egg-Wily Machine X following his first teamup with Dr. Albert W. Wily . He and Orbot and Cubot subsequently encountered Axel the Water Buffalo and the Efrika Egg Army , who had been attempting to conquer the region for Eggman for some time. ( StH : #252 , #253 )

Background Information

  • Efrika is the equivalent of Africa in Sonic's World.
  • Proceeding Soumerca , Efrika was the first of several regions of Sonic's World revealed to have survived the events of Worlds Collide with its original name intact. Mercia , Downunda , and the Northern Tundra were renamed Avalon , Tralius , and Artika respectively.
  • In one of the bonus pages of Sonic the Hedgehog issue #259 , the continent is misspelled as Efryka.
