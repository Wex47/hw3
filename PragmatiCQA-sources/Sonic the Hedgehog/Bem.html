The Bem are an alien race of scientists who in the past have traveled the galaxy exploring new planets and encountering new forms of life. Many times they interfere in the affairs of other species with the intention of helping them, only to end up making a situation worse. All Bem appear identical, seem to be female (as they refer to the rest of their species as "sister Bem"), and all known specimens have the name "Ceneca" along with a multiple-digit number attached to the end. They live on the planet Argentium , which is also the site of a powerful artificial wormhole that they have used to travel to the Mobius solar system. At the present, the Bem's homeworld has been decimated by fighting between the Xorda and Black Arms races.

History

The Creation of De-Roboticization

Having developed interstellar space travel, the Bem made contact with numerous alien races, including the inhabitants of Biotex , which unfortunately led to unintended extinction of the race. They later reached Mobius, with scientist Ceneca-9009 observing and taking a personal interest in the First Robotnik War . Despite the collective pleas of herself and Amadeus and Rosemary Prower (whom she rescued), the Bem took no part in the conflict against Dr. Robotnik , leaving it to the Mobian Freedom Fighters . Eventually, she returned to Mobius during the Second Robotnik War , and at the orders of the Bem High Council conducted an experiment evaluating the performance of Robians against purely organic beings using Sonic the Hedgehog , Miles "Tails" Prower , Dr. Eggman , and Snively Kintobor . Based on Sonic and Tails' victory, the Bem decided to Roboticize all of Mobius' inhabitants, a decision which Ceneca-9009 defied, choosing instead to return all Robians to normal and make all Mobians immune to the effects of Roboticization . ( StH : #118 , #123 , #129 )

Saving Super Shadow

The Bem soon fled the Mobius system as the Xorda arrived seeking vengeance for actions committed against their race by the planet's inhabitants when it had been known as Earth . Before doing so, they imbued Shadow the Hedgehog , whom they had found floating in space and studied, with enough energy to assume his Super Shadow form and thus hold off the enemy ship. When Ceneca-9009 returned, she was charged with disobedience of the High Council's decision and sentenced to death, a fate made seemingly inescapable by the Bem's lock down of any and all travel in order to avoid provoking the Xorda. Fortunately, she was able to escape with the help of Sonic, who had arrived on Argentium following his own battle with the Xorda. ( StH : #124 , #129 )

War Over Their World

The Bem have since been left stranded on Argentium, their world devastated by a conflict between the Xorda and the Black Arms . ( StH : #129 , #170 , #171 )

Background Information

  • The Bem share their name with a character from Star Trek: The Animated Series , and the term originates from the science fiction phrase " B ug E yed M onster." The character from Star Trek was, like the race, a green-skinned being with advanced technology, but unlike all known Bem was known to be male.
