Mobians are a race of humanoids that take the form of anthropomorphic animal, entities who inhabit much of Sonic's World , co-existing with Humans and other races such as the mystical Chao and the undeveloped Mobini . The Mobian population in general, like the rest of the rest of the planets inhabitants, is threatened by the villainous Dr. Eggman , although some Mobians choose to serve as members of his Egg Army .

History

Threatened by Dr. Eggman

Mobians have established various nations across their planet, including the city of Mobotropolis on Westside Island and the underwater kingdom of Meropis . Additionally, a number of Mobians came to share the United Federation the humans and some a dozen mobian nations joined the United Federation after it was formed Unfortunately, all Mobians came under threat with the rise of the villainous Dr. Eggman , who set out to dominate the planet and establish his Eggman Empire .
