History

The Royal Army served as the armed forces of the Kingdom of Acorn for an unknown period of time prior to Ivo Robotnik's coup d'Ã©tat . The army was presumably disbanded after Dr. Ivo "Eggman" Robotnik 's deception of King Acorn , which allowed him to gain control of Mobotropolis and most of the kingdom. However, the Freedom Fighters were later able to overthrow Eggman's dominion of Westside Island , and the army was reformed to protect it. ( SSD : #12 ; FCBD : 2014 )
