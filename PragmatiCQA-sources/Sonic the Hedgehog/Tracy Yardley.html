Tracy Yardley! is a regular artist and occasional writer for the Archie Sonic titles .

Yardley's first appearance in an Archie Sonic publication was in Sonic X #5, but his main Archieverse debut would be in StH #160.

Yardley is currently one of the most prolific artists on the Archie Sonic team, having continuously drawn interior art and covers for several issues, as well as writing several arcs for Sonic Universe .
