Personality

Knuckles is shown to be the least intelligent of his friends, even less so than his Prime Zone counterpart. He is often slower to grasp concepts, particularly when it comes to elaborate plans set up by his friends. All in all, he's incredibly loyal to his teammates.

Appearance

This version of Knuckles differs quite drastically from other versions physically: he is considerably taller and more muscular, with his body type resembling that of Thrash the Devil . This version also wears fingered gloves covered in sports tape, the tape also covering his upper arms and lower legs. He still possesses the recognisable features of his Prime Zone counterpart, such as his dreadlocks, crescent-moon shaped chest marking and, of course, the spiked knuckles of which he's named after.

Background Information

In the original continuity, Knuckles made his

debut in Sonic The Hedgehog 3 and is the guardian of Angel Island. In Sonic Boom, he lives on Bygone Island . He also seemingly lacks the ability to glide. In the games and the series, Knuckles was voiced by Travis Willingham.
