Capcom is a video game company from Japan that has produced a number of franchises, including Mega Man and Mega Man X . It is through Capcom that the Archie Comics company has the license to produce the Mega Man comic series , and that Archie has been able to produce the Worlds Collide and Worlds Unite crossovers, which feature not only Mega Man but other Capcom properties interacting with SEGA properties, most notably that of Sonic the Hedgehog .
