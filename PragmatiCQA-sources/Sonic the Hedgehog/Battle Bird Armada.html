The Battle Bird Armada is a Mobian army consisting entirely of birds led by Battle Lord Kukku XV .

History

The Armada set up operations on Cocoa Island , but their plans were interrupted by the actions of Miles "Tails" Prower . Working his way across the island, Tails fought the Armada's forces all the way up to the Battle Lord himself. ( TA )

Technology

The Armada commands a wide array of technology, from handheld weapons to vehicles capable of moving in air, on or under the sea, and on land.

Members

Background Information

  • The Batte Bird Armada originated in the Game Gear game, Tails Adventure (though in the Japanese version of the game they are called the Battle Kukku Army).
