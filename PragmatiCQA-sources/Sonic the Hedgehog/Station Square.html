History

Damage Control

At some point in the not-too-distant past, Station Square was on the receiving end of an attack by Perfect Chaos. A while after, the city was one of the many places hit hardest by the planet splitting apart due to the Super Genesis Wave . As such, it was the nearest location requiring help from the Knothole Freedom Fighters . ( StH : #257 )

Points of Interest

SS Central Station

Located in the centre of the city, trains for the nearby Mystic Ruins come and go from the station regularly. A popular point of interest for researchers intent on exploring the ruins.

Twinkle Park

A theme-park with an outer-space theme. The entrance can be found in the main square near the beach.

Casinopolis

The local casino, located behind the station.
