Crabmeat is a model of crab-based Badnik created by Dr. Ivo "Eggman" Robotnik . Crabmeat had a Super-Badnik variant.

History

Tails encountered a Super-Badnik variant of the Crabmeat in the Green Seaside Zone , being captured by the robot. However Sonic the Hedgehog arrived and destroyed it after some minor trouble. ( SMD )

Background Information

  • The Super-Badnik Crabmeat resembles the enlarged form of Crabmeat from before the reboot.
