Matt Herms is a penciller and colorist for Archie Sonic the Hedgehog , having worked on the main series, Sonic Universe , Sonic Boom , Sonic Super Digest and the Mega Man volumes. Matt Herms is currently more colorist than anything else, he often worked for Ian Flynn in Archie comics and IDW Publishing
