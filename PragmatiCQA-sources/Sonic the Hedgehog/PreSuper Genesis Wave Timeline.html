Original Continuity

This article concerns continuity on a story-to-story basis. As such, issues may be placed in order of approximate occurance or narrative consistency. For the original timeline of events on Mobius, see Mobius .

The Pre-Super Genesis Wave Timeline was the original continuity of the Archieverse , occurring before the attempted reversion of the reality changing Super Genesis Wave by Super Sonic with Chaos Control was interrupted by an attack from Dr. Eggman .
