The Egg-Wily Uppity Robot Scrambler Cannon is a device created by Dr. Eggman and Dr. Wily during their unlikely alliance with Mega Man , Sonic the Hedgehog , X , and their various heroic teammates. It was used to defeat the mind-controlled Deadly Six by destroying their Sigma Armor and rendering the Robot Masters and Maverick Hunters they had taken control of inactive. Unfortunately, it overheated after its first-and only-use, something that Eggman blamed on Sally Acorn rushing him and Wily in its construction. Sadly, before it could be made operational again for use against Sigma-2 's Time-Cloned Maverick army, it was crushed under the weight of Tornado Tonion . ( SB : #9 ; StH : #274 ; MM : #51 )
