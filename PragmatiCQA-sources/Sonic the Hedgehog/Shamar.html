Shamar is a city that is centered in a desert area in Midesta . It is also home to the Shamar Freedom Fighters and Ehsan , the local Guardian of the Gaia Keys .

History

Shamar had been protected by the Shamar Freedom Fighters for some time, its citizens having acknowledged the group's efforts. During the Shattered World Crisis , Sonic the Hedgehog , Antoine D'Coolette , Big the Cat and Chip traveled to Shamar in search of Ehsan and his Gaia Keys , where they received help from the Shamar Freedom Fighters. After searching the city though, they learned Eshan had been kidnapped by the Midesta Egg Army . The challenge forced them to not only call for backup, but to enlist the aid of the local Freedom Fighters . ( StH : #278 , #279 )

Background Information

  • Shamar was first featured in the game Sonic Unleashed.
  • It is possible that the city of Shamar also existed in the Pre-SGW timeline . It is mentioned in the Sonic Super Digest that Professor Pickle hails from the University of Spagonia and the University of Shamar . However, it is not yet clear if this is canon.
