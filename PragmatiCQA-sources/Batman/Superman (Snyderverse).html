Superman is the alias of Kal-El , son of Jor-El and Lara Lor-Van of the planet Krypton. Sent to Earth before the destruction of Krypton, Kal-El was later found and adopted by Jonathan and Martha Kent, which they gave him the name of Clark Joseph Kent . After beating the kryptonian tyrant Zod and saving the city of Metropolis, he became a reporter for the Daily Planet.
