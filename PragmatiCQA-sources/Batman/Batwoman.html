Batwoman is the name of several superheroines, usually mysterious, but love-interests of Batman. The mainstream version is Kate Kane and the Pre-Crisis version is Kathy Kane, on Earth 11, she is the reverse-gender counterpart of Batman himself, in JLA: The Nail , Selina Kyle , takes the name after Batgirl and Robin were killed by the Joker . Different versions of the character appear in other media, however, Kate Kane, as Batwoman, appears in DC Universe Online .
