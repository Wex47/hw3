John Glover is an American actor who voiced the Riddler in Batman: The Animated Series , as well as the Sega CD based on the series, and portrayed Dr. Jason Woodrue in Batman & Robin . Glover is also famously known for playing Lionel Luthor , the father of Lex Luthor , in the television show Smallville . He later also portrayed Mr. Sivana in the film Shazam! .
