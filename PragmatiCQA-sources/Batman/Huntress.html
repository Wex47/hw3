Huntress is a mask-vigilante and superheroine. She was originally introduced as Helena Wayne , the daughter of the Batman and Catwoman on Earth-Two . Following DC's Crisis on Infinite Earths , the character was reestablished as Helena Bertinelli, the daughter of a mob boss turned vigilante on New-Earth and Prime-Earth . Both characters have been adapted into other universes and forms of media.
