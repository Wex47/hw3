Doug Moench

General Information

Birth Date:

February 23, 1948

Birth Place:

Chicago, Illinois USA
Doug Moench is an American writer. Moench is a prolific contributor to Batman projects for DC Comics , he is best known for creating Nightslayer , Black Mask and Film Freak .
