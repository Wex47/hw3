Character(s):

J.B. Blanc voiced Alfred Pennyworth , the Batcomputer , The Key and Lunkhead in Beware the Batman . He also voiced Bane in Batman: Arkham Origins , Batman: The Enemy Within and the LEGO video game series. Blanc also portrayed Penguin and Ra's al Ghul in the latter.
