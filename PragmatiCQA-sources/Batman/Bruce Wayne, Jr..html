Bruce Wayne, Jr. is the name given to son of Bruce Wayne . First introduced in Batman #131 , the character is often depicted as the Batman of the Future or on parallel earths. Some stories have also depicted him as Robin to other potential future Batmen, including Dick Grayson and Tim Drake . Bruce Wayne Jr. mother has often been depicted as either Kathy Kane / Batwoman or Selina Kyle / Catwoman .

History

Silver-Age

This section is a stub . You can help the Batman wiki by expanding it.


A Bruce Wayne, Jr. first appeared in the Batman #131 story "The Second Batman and Robin Team!". Depicted as a tale written by Alfred Pennyworth , he is the son of Bruce Wayne and Kathy Kane after they retire from crime-fighting and marry. Bruce Wayne, Jr. takes on the Robin identity next to Dick Grayson's Batman. Though initially depicted as a fictional character, subsequent events and retellings placed the story in DC's Earth-Forty .

Bruce Wayne, Jr. would later be adapted into another possible future in World's Finest #154 . Depicted as another imaginary story, he befriends Superman 's son Kal-El, Jr. and becomes the Batman of the Future. This story also introduced the concept of the Super Sons , which would be adapted into later incarnations of the characters or reimagined, notably with Damian Wayne and Jonathan Samuel Kent .

In Other Media

  • In Batman: The Brave and the Bold , the episode " The Knights of Tomorrow! " takes inspiration from the original story featuring Bruce Wayne Jr., placing Damian Wayne in his role. The story also depicts the show's version of Catwoman as his mother.
