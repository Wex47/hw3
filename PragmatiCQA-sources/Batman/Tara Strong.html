Tara Strong voiced Barbara Gordon/Batgirl in The New Batman Adventures , Batman Beyond: Return of the Joker , Batman: Mystery of the Batwoman , Batman: Vengeance , and Batman: Rise of Sin Tzu , replacing Melissa Gilbert from Batman: The Animated Series .

In The Batman movie, The Batman vs. Dracula , she voiced Vicky Vale . In Batman: The Brave and the Bold she voiced Dr. Helena Bertinelli/Huntress .

Recently, she took over the reigns of Arleen Sorkin as Harley Quinn , in the video game Batman: Arkham City and later in Injustice: Gods Among Us and its sequel Injustice 2 and She also reprises the role of Barbara Gordon/Batgirl in Super Best Friends Forever . Tara portrayed Batman himself in an episode of Justice League Action in which Batman (normally voiced by Kevin Conroy ), Zatanna, Doctor Fate and Constantine were transformed into children.
