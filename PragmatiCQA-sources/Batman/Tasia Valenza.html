Tasia Valenza provided the voice of Poison Ivy in both Batman: Arkham Asylum , Batman: Arkham City and Batman: Arkham Knight .

She also provided the voice of Martha Wayne in Batman: Arkham Asylum .

She was also credited for the voice of Mariam, one of the personal bodyguards of Baby-Doll , in Batman: The Animated Series . Yet in The New Adventures of Batman and Robin , she was uncredited for her role as a female cat cultist.
