Bruce Timm

General Information

Birth Name:

Bruce Walter Timm

Birth Date:

February 8, 1961

Batman Media:

Batman: The Animated Series Mask of the Phnatasm Batman Beyond Batman: Strange Days Justice League: Gods and Monsters Batman: Caped Crusader
Bruce Timm is an American character designer, animator and producer. He is also a writer and artist working in comics, and is known for his contributions building the modern DC Comics animated franchise, starting with Batman: The Animated Series in 1992.
