Batman Media:

Batman Forever Batman & Robin
Peter MacGregor-Scott produced 1995's Batman Forever and 1997's Batman & Robin alongside director Joel Schumacher .

After Batman Unchained was cancelled he produced the OnStar commercials featuring Bruce Thomas as Batman . The shorts reused some of the sets and batsuits from his Bat-films but the Batmobile was reverted to the iconic 1989 original .
