Injustice: 2 is a fighting video game for the Xbox One, PlayStation 4, and PC. It is the sequel to Injustice: Gods Among Us . It is developed by NetherRealm Studios, who are well known for creating Mortal Kombat , and is published by DC Entertainment and Warner Bros. Games.
