Uma Thurman is a famous actress and model who portrayed Dr. Pamela Isley/Poison Ivy in Batman & Robin .

She is best known for her roles as Mia Wallace in the 1994 film Pulp Fiction and Beatrix Kiddo/The Bride in the Kill Bill movie series.

She was also married to Gary Oldman between 1990 and 1992 and Ethan Hawke between 1998 and 2005.

Collaborations

  • Thurman co-starred with Michelle Pfeiffer in 1988's Dangerous Liaisons , both of whom would later have villain roles in the Batman film franchise: Michelle as Catwoman in Batman Returns and Uma as Poison Ivy in Batman & Robin .
  • Thurman co-starred with Batman actress Kim Basinger in 1992's Final Analysis .
  • Thurman co-starred alongside Batman Begins star Liam Neeson in 1998's Les Misérables .
  • She appeared alongside fellow Batman & Robin actress Vivica A. Fox in the 2003 film Kill Bill: Volume 1 and it's 2004 sequel Kill Bill: Volume 2
