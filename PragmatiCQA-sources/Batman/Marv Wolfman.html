Marvin A. "Marv" Wolfman (born May 13, 1946) is an American writer. Wolfman has a written titles related to Batman and The New Teen Titans . He is the creator of the Nightwing identity for Dick Grayson .

He was involved with writing Double Helix's Justice League game at one point.
