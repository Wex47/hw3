Batman: Year One

David Mazzucchelli is an American artist, known for his collaborations with Frank Miller in comics. After a successful run on Miller's modern version of Daredevil at Marvel, they pair were invited to do a gritty retelling of Batman 's origin in the Year One storyarc. DC hoped to duplicate the success of that style of crime drama, specifically the critically acclaimed Born Again storyline.
