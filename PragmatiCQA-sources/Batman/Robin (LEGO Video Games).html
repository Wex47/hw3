Tim Drake is deuteragonist of the LEGO Batman series. Originally using the alias Robin , he was the sidekick to Batman , a member of the Bat-Family and the Dark Knight's ward. The Robin identity was previously used by Dick Grayson and Jason Todd . Tim Drake served in the role from before mass breakout at Arkham Asylum to after an encounter with Lord Voltech , though abandoned it to become Red Robin .

History

LEGO Batman: The Videogame

Tim Drake serves as Robin during LEGO Batman . Summoned to Gotham City by the Batsignal , Robin helped Batman investigate three plots orchestrated by the Riddler , the Penguin and the Joker .

LEGO Batman 2: DC Super Heroes

Tim Drake continues to serve as Robin. He helps Batman, Superman , and eventually the Justice League to stop Lex Luthor and The Joker from winning the presidential campaign.

LEGO Batman 3: Beyond Gotham

Tim Drake appears once again as Robin. He helps Batman and the Justice League stop Brainiac 's plans to collect cities and later gather the various Lantern Corps.

LEGO Dimensions

Tim Drake appears one last time as Robin. During the opening, he is kidnapped by Lord Vortech and held hostage by the gathering of villains. Robin is later merged with Frodo Baggins and MetalBeard into a robot called the Tri, though the process is reversed and the group is separated after the heroes defeat it.

LEGO DC Super-Villains

Damian Wayne is now Robin, with Tim now operating as Red Robin.

Suits

LEGO Batman: The Videogame

  • Default/Classic Suit - The default appearance for Robin. The suit will be equipped at the beginning of every level and does not provide additional abilities.
  • Technology Suit - Allows Robin to use technology panels, which he can use to control machines and vehicles. Can be upgraded to create decoys.
  • Scuba Suit - Allows Robin to dive underwater. Can be upgraded to throw Freezing Batarangs.(Console Version only)
  • Magnetic Suit - Allows Robin to walk on magnetic surfaces, allowing him to climb these and walk upside down.
  • Attract Suit - Allows Robin to vacuum up red, yellow and green LEGO pieces, which can be deposited into canisters to assemble structures or machines. Can be up upgraded to track pieces. (Console Version only)
  • Bio Suit - Protects Robin from poisonous gases and toxic waste, allowing him to access certain areas. (Handheld Version only)

LEGO Batman 2: DC Super Heroes

  • Default Suit - The default appearance for Robin. The suit will be equipped at the beginning of every level and does not provide additional abilities (will allow acrobatics in Handheld Version).
  • Hazard Suit - Allows Robin to dive underwater and protection from poisonous gases and toxic waste. Can also absorb liquids, which can be sprayed into enemies and containers, and clear up toxic hazards (with water).
  • Acrobat Suit - Allows Robin to perform acrobatic abilities, such as wall jumping, pole swinging and double jump. Can also deploy a Zorb ball, which can be used to ball sockets. (Console Version only)
  • Ice Suit - Allows Robin to freeze objects with the Freeze Gun and protects him from cold temperatures.
  • Magnet Suit - Allows Robin to climb magnetic surfaces and pull magnetic objects.
  • Classic Suit - Robin's costume from LEGO Batman . (Handheld Version only)

LEGO Batman 3: Beyond Gotham

  • Default Suit - The default appearance for Robin. The suit will be equipped at the beginning of every level and does not provide additional abilities.
  • Hazard Suit - Allows Robin to vacuum up red, yellow and green LEGO pieces, which can be deposited into canisters to assemble structures or machines. Also provides protection from poisonous gases and toxic waste.
  • Techno Suit - Allows Robin to interact with Technology Panels and summon the Toy Wonder drone.
  • Illumination Suit - Allows Robin to light up dark areas, though requires charging before use.
  • Dive Suit - Allows Robin to dive underwater and use the blaster, allowing him to melt ice and cut through gold LEGO bricks.
  • Helmet Suit - Allows Robin to mind control certain individuals.
  • Magnet Suit - Allows Robin to climb magnetic surfaces and pull magnetic objects.
  • Sphere Suit - Allows Robin to use Zorb Ball sockets, though restricts throwing and grappling abilities.
