Grey DeLisle , also credited as Grey Griffin , is an American voice actor. She is best known for provided the voice of Catwoman in various works, including Batman: Arkham City , Injustice: Gods Among Us , Batman: Arkham Knight , Injustice 2 , and LEGO DC Super-Villains .

DeLisle also voiced Vicki Vale in Batman: Arkham City , and Batman: Arkham Origins , Harley Quinn and Batgirl in LEGO Batman: The Videogame , and Magpie in Beware the Batman . She also voiced various characters in The Batman and Black Canary in Batman: The Brave and the Bold
