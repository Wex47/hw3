John DiMaggio

General Information

Birth Name:

John William DiMaggio

Birth Date:

September 4, 1968

Birth Place:

North Plainfield, New Jersey, U.S.

Character(s):

Rhino and Mugsy Aquaman The Joker Mr.Freeze Mad Hatter
John DiMaggio voiced Rhino and Mugsy in The Batman , Aquaman , Gorilla Grodd and Black Mask in Batman: The Brave and the Bold , The Joker in Batman: Under the Red Hood , and King Shark in Batman: Assault on Arkham .
