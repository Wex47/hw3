Nolan North is an American voice actor. He is best known for voicing the Penguin in the . North also provided his voice to other characters in the series, including Black Mask in Arkham City and KGBeast in Assault on Arkham .

North also voiced various Batman characters in the video games, including Scarecrow , Captain Boomerang and Hush in LEGO Batman 2: DC Super Heroes , Mad Hatter and Condiment King in LEGO Batman 3: Beyond Gotham , and Music Meister in LEGO DC Super-Villains . North also voiced Superboy , Superman , Zatara and Clayface in Young Justice . North also voiced General Zod in Injustice: Gods Among Us and LEGO Dimensions , and Superman in Batman: Death in the Family .
