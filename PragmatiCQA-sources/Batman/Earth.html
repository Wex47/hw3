General Information

Official name:

Earth

First Appearance:

Detective Comics #27

Galaxy:

Milky Way

Star System:

Sol
Earth is a rocky planet orbiting the Sun, and the home planet of humankind, including Batman and the vast majority of the individuals he encountered. Earth is just one life-compatible planet of the Milky Way Galaxy , which itself is just one of countless galaxies in the Universe .

On rare occasions Batman would depart Earth to join the Justice League of America , help extraterrestrial beings, or being taken hostage by hostile extraterrestrials.

History

Batman was born Bruce Wayne in Gotham City in the United States of America on planet Earth. A close friend and ally of his, Superman was born on Krypton , a planet millions of lightyears away from Earth.
