General Information

Character(s):

Oswald Cobblepot/The Penguin

Danny DeVito is an American actor. He portrayed Oswald Cobblepot/The Penguin in Tim Burton 's Batman Returns . DeVito also contributed to the Gotham City Villains Anniversary , writing a story about the Penguin .

Characters Portrayed

Oswald Cobblepot/ The Penguin

Collaborations

  • He appeared with Batman Returns co-star Michael Keaton in the movie Johnny Dangerously and also the Disney movie .
  • He appeared with fellow Batman villain Jack Nicholson in the Tim Burton film Mars Attacks! , as Burton has had a reputation for reusing actors. However, they shared no screen time together. They also both appeared in the Miloš Forman film One Flew Over the Cuckoo's Nest . He also co-starred with Nicholson in Hoffa , a film which he also directed.
  • DeVito hired cinematographer Stefan Czapsky to shoot his Matilda movie.
  • He starred in two films with Arnold Schwarzenegger , who would later star as Mr. Freeze in Batman & Robin : Twins (1988), and Junior (1994).
