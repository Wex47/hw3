Character(s):

Harvey Dent/Two-Face
Tommy Lee Jones is an actor and film maker who portrayed Harvey Dent/Two-Face in Batman Forever .

His many film roles have included US. Marshal Samuel Gerard in The Fugitive and Agent K in the Men in Black film franchise.

Characters portrayed by Tommy Lee Jones in the Batman Universe

Harvey Dent
Two-Face

Trivia
