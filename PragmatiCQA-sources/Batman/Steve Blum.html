Steven Jay Blum (born April 29, 1960) is an American voice actor. He voiced Killer Croc in Batman: Arkham Asylum , Batman: Arkham City and The Season of Infamy DLC for Batman: Arkham Knight .

He was uncredited for the minor voices of Jordan Fraser , Ian Kennedy , and Mike in Batman: Arkham Asylum .

He was uncredited for the voices of Sickle and various inmates in Batman: Arkham City.

He also provided the sound effects of Killer Moth, The Joker, Two-Face, Batman, and Killer Croc in LEGO Batman: The Videogame .

Trivia

  • Like Fred Tatasciore, he voiced the same character from LEGO Batman: The Videogame , in Batman: Arkham Asylum and Batman: Arkham City .
