Robin Atkin Downes (born September 6, 1976) is an English screen and voice actor. He has voiced several characters within the Batman franchise, including Slade Wilson/Deathstroke in Beware the Batman , the Grandmaster of the Court of Owls in Batman vs. Robin , Alfred Pennyworth in LEGO Batman 3: Beyond Gotham and the Riddler in Batman: The Enemy Within . Downes also provided both voice and motion capture for Doomsday in Batman v Superman: Dawn of Justice .
