Comics

  • The Dark Knight - the first comic book series called Dark Knight (February 1986 to June 1986).
    • Most trade paperback collections have been retitled Batman: The Dark Knight Returns
    • This continuity has been advertised as Dark Knight Universe .
  • Batman: The Dark Knight (Volume 2) - the second comic book series called Dark Knight (2010 to 2011).
  • Batman: The Dark Knight (Volume 3) - the third comic book series called Dark Knight (May 2011 to 2014).

Characters

  • Bruce Wayne/Batman
  • Azrael (Jean-Paul Valley) - in the Knightfall Arc Jean is also referred to as the Dark Knight.
  • The Dark Knights - corrupted versions of Bruce Wayne / Batman throughout the Dark Multiverse .
