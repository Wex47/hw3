Laura Bailey is an American voice actress. She has provided the voice to characters in the Batman mythos, including Wonder Woman in LEGO Batman 2: DC Super Heroes , Catwoman in Batman: The Telltale Series , Supergirl in Injustice 2 and Lois Lane in the Injustice film adaptation.

Trivia

  • Bailey is married to voice actor Travis Willingham , who portrayed Harvey Dent/Two-Face in Batman: The Telltale Series . Incidentally, her character, Selina Kyle/Catwoman , was dating a pre-disfigured Dent in the first episodes of the game.
