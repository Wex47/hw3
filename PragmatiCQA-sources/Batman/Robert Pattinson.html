Trivia

  • In the fall of 2019 Pattinson broke his silence on signing to play Batman in a Variety article, admitting to have been "furious" when his audition leaked to the internet.
  • Pattinson acknowledged to have been "obsessed" with the Tim Burton films starring Michael Keaton as a child in Variety's video feature.
  • Pattinson asked Christopher Nolan for advice on playing the role while playing the lead in his film, Tenet , after his casting announcement.
  • Pattinson will be the first actor in 14 years to debut as Batman in his own standalone film, the last version of Batman to debut in a solo film was Christian Bale in Batman Begins .
