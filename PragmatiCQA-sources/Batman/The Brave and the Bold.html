The Brave and the Bold

General Information

Type:

Anthology series

Published:

1955 - 1983

Creators:

Julius Schwartz
The Brave and the Bold is a anthology series that eventually began to feature Batman team-ups in every issue. When Batman took over the title it featured covers drawn by Carmine Infantino .

It also notable for later being where Neal Adams began illustrating the character, which lead to him going to the mainline titles after fan demand. It also inspired an animated show of the same name in 2008.
