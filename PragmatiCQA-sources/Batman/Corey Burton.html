Corey Burton provided the voice of Scarecrow , The Russian , and Marshall in the movie Batman: Gotham Knight .

He provided various voices in Batman: The Brave and the Bold , including Thomas Wayne, Red Tornado, Doctor Mid-Nite, and Doc Magnus.

In Young Justice , he voices The Brain, Uncle Dudley, Hamilton Hill, and Jim Gordon.

Recently, he provided the voice of Hugo Strange , one of the main antagonists, in Batman: Arkham City .

Trivia

  • He provided the voice of one of Superman's villain, Brainiac, for over 10 years, from the animated television series to DC Universe Online . He is one of the few voice actors from the DC animated universe, who voiced a villain, to continuously voice the character (Similar to Mark Hamill 's The Joker and Arleen Sorkin 's Harley Quinn ).
