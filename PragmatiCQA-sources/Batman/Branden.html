Branden was the leader of the GCPD's S.W.A.T. team underneath the corrupt administration of Commissioner Gillian B. Loeb .

History

Branden was noted for his overly violent approach to law enforcement, and Jim Gordon said to his face that his force was a "lunatic gestapo". He also briefly served as Acting Police Commissioner under Mayor Peter Grogan , when Gordon was suspended for his involvement with Batman . Although he was quickly indicted afterwards for his usage of excessive force, Branden was eventually murdered by the Hangman Killer during the events of Batman: Dark Victory .
