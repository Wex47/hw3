The Old Gotham City Zoo was located within the overall Gotham Park area of Gotham City . It was closed down and abandoned thirty years prior to Batman 's emergence in Gotham.

History

Penguin's hideout

The Penguin 's returned here in adulthood and set up a base of operations in the Arctic World exhibit, where he was raised a small child. The entire site ended up being destroyed by the missiles from the Penguin Commandos . ﻿
