Anthony Ingruber is a voice actor and impressionist. He has voiced "John Doe"/The Joker in Batman: The Telltale Series and its sequel, Batman: The Enemy Within .

Characters portrayed

The Joker

Trivia

  • According to an interview with IGN, Ingruber was approached by Telltale Games to voice their iteration of the Joker after seeing a video where he filmed himself impersonating various incarnations of the Joker, including those seen in Tim Burton's Batman , The Dark Knight and Batman: The Animated Series .
