Birds of Prey is a movie loosely based on the DC Comics team of the same name , the film focuses more on Harley Quinn rather than the Bat-Family crimefighters. It is the eighth release taking place in the shared universe.

Plot

After splitting up with Joker Harley Quinn and three other female superheroes - Black Canary, Huntress and Renee Montoya - come together to save the lives of a little girl (Cassandra Cain) from an evil crime lord.
