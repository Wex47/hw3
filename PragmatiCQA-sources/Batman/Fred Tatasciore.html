Fredrick "Fred" Tatasciore (born June 15, 1967) is an American voice actor. He is best known for providing the voice of Bane and Solomon Grundy in various projects, including Batman: Arkham Asylum , ' Batman: Arkham City , Injustice: Gods Among Us , Batman Unlimited: Monster Mayhem and Injustice 2 .

Tatasciore also voiced the decoy Ra's al Ghul in the video game adaptation of Batman Begins , in lieu of Ken Watanabe . He also provided the voice of Sgt. Rock in Batman: The Brave and the Bold , Deathstroke in Young Justice , and Gorilla Grodd in Batman Ninja .

Tatasciore has also provided the voice of several characters in LEGO video games, most prominently their versions of Bane , Solomon Grundy and Killer Croc . He also voiced Hush in LEGO Batman: The Videogame , and Clayface and Man-Bat in LEGO Batman 2: DC Super Heroes .
