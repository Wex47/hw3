Batman has devices that send out an ultrasonic signal that attracts bats. A swarm of local bats then approach the area or target.

History

Batman originally kept this gadget in the heel of his boot. It was used to distract a SWAT team sent by Commissioner Gillian B. Loeb , when Batman was trapped in a condemned building.

Background Information and Notes

Writer Frank Miller first conceived this idea in Batman #406 , released in April of 1987. The comic presents the device as one of Batman's final lines of defense, using it only after being cornered by the GCPD's corrupt SWAT Unit . At the time of its creation, the Ultrasonic Bat Beacon was kept in Batman's boot rather than the utility belt.

In Other Media

Films

  • Batman Returns (1992) - An ultrasonic bat beacon appears as a function of Batman 's Signal Jammer . During his final confrontation with the Penguin , he trick Cobblepot into stealing the remote. In addition to activating the Penguin Commandos ' attack on the Old Zoo , it releases a swarm of bats from the Batskiboat and attracts them to attack Penguin. A similar attack was intended to occur to the Joker in the proceeding film's drafts , but technical limitations at the time prevented its use.
  • Batman Begins : The beacon appears in a similar fashion to its appearance in Year One . In the film, Batman activates it to cover his escape from the Gotham City SWAT and Arkham Asylum . Like the comic, the beacon is stored in the heel of the Batsuit 's boots.
  • Batman: Year One : The beacon's appearance in the comic is faithfully adapted in its animated adaptation.

Television

  • Justice League : In the season two finale " Starcrossed ", Batman makes use of batarangs fitted with ultrasonic bat beacons against Thanagarian forces .
  • Gotham : The ultrasonic bat beacon appears as a former Wayne Enterprises project called the " Nightwing Project ". Bruce Wayne uses to escape Bane during their fight in " Mad City: Beware the Green-Eyed Monster ".

Video games

  • Justice League: Heroes : the device is thrown down at a desired spot and a swarm of bats will attack nearby enemies as a super move. Its appears to be a small item with a blinking red light, the move is referred to as Bat Swarm.
  • Mortal Kombat vs. DC Universe : One of Batman's "Heroic Brutalities" also called Bat Swarm, the device is portrayed as a special Batarang that emits large visible purple signal waves.
  • : In both Batman: Arkham City and Batman: Arkham Origins , Batman can unlock and perform a combo move called Batswarm. This summons a large dissipating vortex of bats in the area, which attack and harm nearby enemies. It is never explained how Batman summons the bats, though some have speculated it may be through use of the beacon.
  • Batman: The Telltale Series : The Ultrasonic Bat Beacon appears as a Batsuit function in the series. Batman uses the beacon to summon them whilst battling Two-Face in " Guardian of Gotham ".
  • Injustice 2 : Batman's "character power" move, Summon Bats, brings three robotic bat-drones with neon blue lights, that surround him and hit the opponent.
