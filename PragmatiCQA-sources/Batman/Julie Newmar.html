Julie Newmar

Birth Date:

Birth Place:

Los Angeles, California

Character(s):

Catwoman

Julie Newmar was the first actress to portray Catwoman in the 1960s Batman television series. She later reprised the role by voicing her character in the two animated feature films Batman: Return of the Caped Crusaders and Batman vs. Two-Face .
