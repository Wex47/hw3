Lance Bruner (created by Bob Haney in 1969 with the comic book The Brave and the Bold #83) is the son (already motherless at age 2) of a good friend of Thomas Wayne's, father of Bruce Wayne, who dies after a accident.

History

He was adopted by Bruce after his father died in an accident. He will pick up Robin's mask to help Batman defeat Grantland Stark, a thief who stole millions of dollars in oil, but will die sacrificing himself for Batman.

Batman at first thought Dick sacrificed himself for him until Lance revealed himself.

Teen Titans and Bruce performed a small ceremony for Lance's disappearance, with a carved bust for him in what appears to be the Wayne Mansion garden.
