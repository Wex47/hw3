Batman Incorporated was a global non-profit organization publicly funded by Wayne Enterprises and Bruce Wayne . The group selects vigilantes from across the world to train and supply for crime-fighting purposes while also making them swear oaths.

The team was created as a modern reimagining of the Silver Age's Batmen of All Nations which was a Batman-lead team-up of Batman-inspired vigilantes from all over the world.
