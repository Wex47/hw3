History

Origin

After a great deal of training in Europe by the Sacred Order of St. Dumas , Abraham Arlington was the first Azrael of the Order of Purity to don the Suit of Sorrows since it was taken by Ra's al Ghul . He acted as Azrael for the order in Gotham City for a short time during Battle for The Cowl , but was driven insane after just six weeks of wearing the suit. After he decapitated an undercover police officer, the Gotham chapter of the Order of Purity placed him in a mental hospital and found his replacement in Michael Lane .

Powers and Abilities

Powers

  • Suit of Sorrows gave him increased strength, speed, and stamina. He also wielded the flaming Sword of Sin .
