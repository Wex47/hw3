Arleen Sorkin voiced Harley Quinn in Batman: The Animated Series , , The New Adventures of Batman and Robin , Batman: Arkham Asylum , Justice League , Static Shock , Gotham Girls , DC Universe Online , and Batman Beyond: Return of the Joker . She's uncredited as a girl named Bambi in Batman: Mask of the Phantasm .

Her role as Harley would eventually be succeeded by Tara Strong , who also voices Batgirl .
