Len Wein was an American comics writer. His most notable contributions to the Batman mythos include villains like the Preston Payne version of Clayface and Sterling Silversmith . Wien was also one of the co-creators of Amanda Waller and the original version of Lucius Fox . In an interview with Wizard magazine, Alan Moore mentioned that Wein was responsible for approving Barbara Gordon to be shot and crippled by the Joker in Batman: The Killing Joke .

Wein also wrote for Batman: The Animated Series , penning the episodes " Off Balance ", " Moon of the Wolf " and " Blind as a Bat ". He also co-wrote " The Demon's Quest Part II " with Dennis O'Neil .
