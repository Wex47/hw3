Neal Adams

General Information

Birth Date:

June 6, 1941

Birth Place:

Governors Island, Manhattan

Batman Creations:

Ra's al Ghul Sensei Man-Bat Tim Drake
Neal Adams is an American artist. Adams is one of the most famous Batman artists of of all time. He redefined the look of Batman's cowl and the set a new standard for anatomy in the medium in general.

He also created the original designs for Man-Bat , Ra's al Ghul and Tim Drake .
