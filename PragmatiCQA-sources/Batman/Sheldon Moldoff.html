Sheldon Moldoff

General Information

Birth Name:

Sheldon Moldoff

Birth Date:

April 14, 1920

Birth Place:

New York City, New York

Death Date:

February 29, 2012

Batman Creations:

Aunt Harriet Cooper Bette Kane Bat-Mite Julie Madison Calendar Man Clayface (Matt Hagen) Doctor Hurt Lew Moxon Mr. Freeze Poison Ivy
Sheldon Moldoff was one of Bob Kane 's anonymous cartoonists. Comic-readers would buy Batman comic-books thinking that they were Bob Kane's, when they were really Sheldon Moldoff's. Sheldon Moldoff helped to co-create Calendar Man , Poison Ivy , Clayface (Matt Hagen) and many other characters. Besides making Batman comic-books, he drew the covers for the Green Lantern in All-American Comics #6 and the Flash in Flash Comics #1. Sheldon Moldoff has pencilled 146 issues of Detective Comics , 121 issues of Batman, and 61 issues of World's Finest Comics.

Creations

Heroes/Allies

  • Aunt Harriet Cooper (co-created with Bill Finger )
  • Bette Kane (co-created with Bob Kane )
  • Julie Madison (co-created with Bob Kane and Gardner Fox )
  • Bat-Mite (co-created with Bob Kane and Bill Finger )

Villians

  • Julian Gregory Day/Calendar Man (co-created with Bob Kane )
  • Matthew "Matt" Hagen/Clayface (co-created with Bill Finger )
  • Simon Hurt/Doctor Hurt (co-created with Charles Paris . Character would not be given the Simon Hurt persona until 2008 by Grant Morrison )
  • Lew Moxon (co-created with Bill Finger )
  • Mr. Freeze (co-created with Bob Kane and David Wood ( uncredited ),
    with the characters original name being "Mr. Zero")
  • Dr. Pamela Lillian Isley/Poison Ivy (co-created with Robert Kanigher )
  • Lord Death Man / Death Man (co-created with Robert Knaigher)
  • Abner Krill / Mr. Polka-Dot

Note: Whilst he did co-create Mr. Freeze aka Mr Zero, he did not create his alter ego Dr. Victor Fries.
