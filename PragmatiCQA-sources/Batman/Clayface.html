Clayface is the alias of eight villains appearing in comics.

  • Basil Karlo
  • Matthew Hagen
  • Preston Payne
  • Sondra Fuller
  • Cassius 'Clay' Payne
  • Claything
  • Todd Russell
  • Johnny Williams
