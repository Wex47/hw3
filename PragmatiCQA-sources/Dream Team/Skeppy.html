Servers

InvadedLands

Skeppy owns a Minecraft server called , created in 2015. InvadedLands was the focus of many of Skeppy's YouTube videos, which brought the server to fame. InvadedLands has five servers: KitPVP (the most popular server), UHC, Survival, Skyblock, and Soup.

Zelkam, or ZelkTheElk, hosts events as well as Skeppy. He is also a manager on the server.

PVPLands

Skeppy used to host events on InvadedLands. However, he decided that the queueing system was too unfair in InvadedLands so he moved the events to a new server called PVPLands where one can buy passes to move up the queue. However, one can also still enter an event without a pass. On December 4, 2020, it was merged into InvadedLands.

SMP

Skepina/Lya

Lya, known as Skepina during her appearance, is Skeppy's assistant. On December 26, 2020, she played on the Dream SMP via Skeppy's account with Lani (on Tubbo 's account) and Ranboo while pretending to be Skeppy's sister.

SMP visit

Skepina visited the SMP on December 26, 2020 on Skeppy's account. Prior to Skepina's debut, Lani was decorating L'Manberg with Tubbo through his account. While decorating, they saw that Skeppy had logged on, and Lani introduced herself via in-game chat, announcing that she was Tubbo's sister on his account. Skepina then responded, stating that she was Skeppy's sister playing on her brother's account. At first, the two of them thought it was Skeppy playing a joke on them and asked her to join voice chat. Upon joining, Skepina spoke and shocked the two, as they thought it was Skeppy's sister.

The two in L'Manberg, and upon meeting, Skepina offered to help with decorations. Ranboo also joined the call. Tubbo encouraged Lani to give a tour of the SMP to Skepina with her "limited knowledge" with Ranboo being security. They started to walk around the server. Outside Punz's house was a sign saying, "use twitch prime :)", which then prompted a visit to Church Prime. Since the bell was missing, Ranboo made bell noises to represent twitch primes in Tubbo's chat. However, Skeppy took control and killed Ranboo and himself with lava. This destroyed all of Skeppy and Ranboo's items that weren't netherite, including Antfrost's trident and Ranboo's memory book . Lani managed to save Skeppy's two netherite pickaxes and Ranboo's netherite stuff.

After dying, Lani respawned by HBomb's OWO sign and Ranboo and Lani had to fetch her. They then went through Eret's castle, the Community House, the Christmas tree connected to the community house, Niki and Puffy's flower shop, Ranboo and Fundy's ice cream shop, and Punz's yard. There, Skepina opened up a present left for Skeppy under the tree from Nihachu and read the book left for him. Since she needed food, they went to Purpled's base and obtained some baked potatoes.

They then decided to head to the Badlands to show where Skeppy lived with BadBoyHalo . On the way, Lani sold Yeezys to Skepina, visited Tommy's old base, and showed Skepina TommyInnit's " " video. Skepina also became distracted by the "Little Penis" sign, so Ranboo and Lani distracted her with the apiary. They then remembered about heading to the Badlands finally showed off the mansion. There, Ranboo lead them back to show off Skeppy's Skywars map replica, but both Lani and Skepina died due to falling off. BadBoyHalo then joined the call. After Bad spoke, Skeppy immediately logged off the server and left the voice call. The two didn't return, ending the visit.
