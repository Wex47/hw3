The L'Manbergian Election was an era of campaigning, mainly between the two parties SWAG2020 and POG2020 , for the presidency of L'Manberg . Much campaigning was done during this era, which even included a presidential debate

Details

September 3, 2020

  • Wilbur decides to officially run for president through a democratic vote, with Tommy as his running mate. They plan to keep the election secret and be the only party to run for office, effectively giving the citizens of L'Manberg no other choice.
    • Quackity becomes aware of their plot and decides to run against them before Wilbur can close the election ballot.
    • Wilbur and Tommy decide to name their party POG2020, while Quackity names his party SWAG2020. He says that he will announce his running mate soon.
    • Wilbur and Tommy write down a list of their policies in a book .

September 7, 2020

  • Tommy builds a "POG2020" sign in the sky near his base. This sign is later vandalized by supporters of the opposing parties in the lead-up to the election, temporarily reading "ASS2020" and eventually ending up reading "CRY".

September 8, 2020

  • Tommy creates a wall of signs filled with "quotes" from Quackity's Twitter on L'Manberg's wall in an attempt to run a smear campaign against SWAG2020.
  • Both parties decide to hold a presidential debate in Tubbo's sky courthouse .
    • Before the debate, Quackity announces that George will be his running mate for the election.
    • During the debate, Fundy attempts to join with his own party, but is rebuffed by Wilbur. He later takes Quackity's side, leaving Wilbur distraught.
    • The debate ends with no clear winner. Wilbur leaves angrily, accusing the moderators of bias towards the SWAG party.
    • Tommy is assassinated by Dream .
  • In a flashback to the past on Quackity's stream on April 12, 2021, Quackity and Wilbur are then seen talking on top of a hill near L'Manberg.
    • Quackity asks Wilbur what his drive is, Wilbur responding that he wants protection for his people.
    • Quackity asks if he can get to know Wilbur a bit better, but Wilbur declines, stating it's 12 days before the election.
    • Quackity continues, saying that there is a good side to everyone, and that he tried to join L'Manberg, but was rejected. Wilbur then rejects what he says.
    • Wilbur continues, saying that to make changes, he will need power through revolutions, as scenes from the SMP history (yet to come, from Quackity and Wilbur's perspective) flash across the screen.

September 13, 2020

  • Tommy and Tubbo burn down Quackity's unfinished mansion and then start work on a nuclear fallout bunker near Fundy's base to be used for worst-case scenarios.
  • Tubbo starts construction on his secret bunker .
  • Sapnap fills the interior sections and the entrance of Dream's base with iron doors, referencing an unfortunate incident that occurred during MCC9 for Dream.

September 14, 2020

  • Tommy attempts to convince Sapnap to join his side and vote for POG2020.
    • Sapnap tells Tommy that he must publicly denounce Fundy if he wants his vote.
    • Tommy proceeds to do so, which causes Fundy to officially form his party, Coconut2020, with Niki as his running mate. He keeps this campaign a secret from Wilbur.
  • While non-canonically speaking to Schlatt in a VC, Tommy starts construction on a Reverse Coaster in the sky that "begins" in L'Manberg.
  • Throughout a few streams, Quackity repeatedly builds houses on Tommy's lawn and above his house, but they keep getting taken down. He eventually settles on a hastily built McDonald's restaurant.

September 16, 2020

  • Ponk , with Fundy's help, makes a small shrine with armor stands and fireworks near the Watch Tower as a gift for Dream reaching 10 million subscribers on YouTube.
  • Niki gifts Dream a book and a chest full of food from her bakery, which she leaves at his base.

September 19, 2020

  • Tommy spends a stream building various things.
    • Tommy removes Quackity's house from his property.
    • Tommy and Wilbur fix the "POG2020" sign, which was vandalised to say "ASS2020".
    • Tommy, Wilbur, and Quackity build the White House of L'Manberg.

September 20, 2020

  • Tommy and Tubbo prepare the L'Manberg podium for the election rally night, adding chairs on the ground in front of it.
    • Tubbo creates a new alter ego named Toob .
  • The two main parties, POG2020 and SWAG2020, give speeches to the citizens of L'Manberg and the Dream SMP.
    • Fundy and Niki join to announce their campaign, Coconut2020, much to Wilbur's annoyance. Fundy gives a convincing speech, but when Wilbur asks if they have any endorsements, both members abruptly log off in a panic.
    • SWAG2020 brings forward KSI as their endorsement, but can't provide any proof that he's actually endorsed them.
    • POG2020 bring forward both a video of endorsement from Vikkstar.
    • Jschlatt is unbanned from the server, and is brought forward as POG2020's second endorsement.
    • However, instead of endorsing POG2020, Schlatt accidentally endorses Coconut2020 and then announces his campaign running under the party Schlatt2020.
    • Dream gives Schlatt his prized crossbow and the two begin hunting down their competitors.
  • The poll on Wilbur's Twitter officially opens, with the two new emerging parties also included as options.
  • While hiding from Schlatt, Wilbur attempts to get Quackity to agree to combine their parties into one to secure their possible win.
    • Quackity refuses, saying that they would only try to use him.
    • He instead agrees to form a coalition with Schlatt, as he has been promised a position in his cabinet if Schlatt wins.
  • In Karl's wedding venue in his Harry Potter house , Quackity goes through three consecutive failed marriages with Karl , Schlatt and then Eret .

September 21, 2020

  • Fundy builds a "better looking" second White House next to one of Eret's watch towers overlooking Manberg.

September 22, 2020

  • HBomb94 joins the server.
    • He explores Eret's castle and fights Tubbo.
  • Tommy and Tubbo reminisce about the Disc War on top of the L'Manberg walls.
    • They go to their bench next to Tommy's house and listen to a disc (a second "Cat").
    • Tubbo reveals to Wilbur and Tommy that he has built a bunker underneath L'Manberg in case things go wrong during the reveal of the election results.
  • The presidential candidates and some of the server members, namely Jack Manifold , Punz , HBomb, Tubbo, and Ponk, arrive at the election podium to find out the results of the poll.
    • Wilbur announces the turn out of the votes, Coconut2020 with 9%, Schlatt2020 with 16%, SWAG2020 with 30%, and POG2020 with 45%.
    • Because of the combination of Schlatt2020 with SWAG2020, their combined votes come out to be 46%, and therefore Quackity and Schlatt win the presidency.
  • The results of the election spark the beginning of the next era, the Electoral Fallout Era .
