New Manifold Land is a faction founded by Jack Manifold on August 19, 2021 after feeling that he wanted to get away from the entire Dream SMP. He vowed to move away from the SMP mainland and live alone in New Manifold Land.

History

Foundation

On his one-year anniversary on the Dream SMP on August 3, 2021, Jack Manifold toured the Dream SMP looking at all of the things he built. He felt that most of the places he loved had been destroyed in some form or the other so he decided to move away from the SMP completely and establish a new faction called New Manifold Land.

Jack found a far away secret location in a desert off-stream, and on August 19, he declared it New Manifold Land. He decided to emulate L'Manberg and build a van to sell items from.
