A New Era
The Manberg Festival

The New Era saw the return of some original members and some growth in each faction. Members were divided and continued preparing for what was what to come. Ninja visited the server for a day, and Andrea Botez was allowed on the server after winning against George in a chess match. The War of the Burning Eiffel Tower occurred during this era following the death of Henry . The Dreamon Invasion also occurred during this era, starting the Dreamon Hunter business.

Details

September 23-24, 2020

  • Awesamdude and Alyssa return to the server after several months offline and Antfrost joins for the first time.
  • HBomb rents a room in Eret's castle to reside in.
  • Antfrost moves into BadBoyHalo's closet in his room under Punz's house .
  • In addition to the potato farm, Technoblade makes a cow farm and a small underground base in a river near Pogtopia .
  • Items Conflict :
    • Sapnap burns down sections of Punz's house before going on yet another rampage; attempting to kill all of Punz's pets, as well as other people's pets.
    • Sapnap kills Karl , Sam, Ant, and Bad. This leads Karl to question his relationship with Sapnap.
    • Punz patches up some of the damage later.

September 25-28, 2020

  • Ninja joins the server and Tommy gives him a tour around the area. He buys drugs (cocoa beans) from Quackity in his alleyway , marries George , and builds his own house in the front yard of Tommy's old home, the former L'Manberg Embassy .
  • Fundy rebuilds the giant Manberg flag with obsidian and magma blocks whilst Quackity builds his new home in front of Jack's house. Fundy, Quackity, and Schlatt continue to clean up the area of Manberg. Quackity changes Eret's "POGTOPIA" sign in the Dream SMP lands to the word "CRY" made of cobblestone.
  • In Manberg, Schlatt berates Quackity for having a "flatty patty". In an attempt to win back Schlatt's approval, Quackity builds a power rack made of iron bars, but almost immediately turns it into a gravestone .
    • Afterwards, Quackity and Schlatt wander around the Greater Dream SMP and go for a date in the Socializing Club , which Quackity briefly converts into a restaurant.
  • Karl, with the help of Quackity, BadBoyHalo, Fundy, HBomb, and Punz, builds Party Island in Manberg to welcome Pokimane whenever she joins the server. During construction, Quackity turns the Camarvan into a Taco Bell drive thru. Karl commissions Bad to build a Pokimane statue on the water facing the island.
  • Karl builds a Karaoke stage with an underground nightclub near Punz's house.
  • Karl and HBomb extend the original wooden prime path of the server by forming another " scenic route " starting from the entrance of the Therapy Room and branching to different parts of Manberg such as the Pufferfish Alleyway and Party Island.
  • Karl works on building a cornerside tavern along the "scenic route".

September 29, 2020

  • MrBeast hid a Taco Bell gift card worth $10,000 on the server, which leads to a wild scavenger hunt for the chest that lasts nearly 6 hours. Eret finds the 'Taco Bell' book in the chest at the coordinates 6969, 69, 420 after searching for three hours.
    • In honor of Eret winning the gift card, Bad makes a statue of Eret standing atop the entrance to their castle.
  • Technoblade begins to breed horses, seeking to find the "perfect" horse with maxed out stats.
  • Skeppy and BadBoyHalo work on building a quartz mansion in the place of Skeppy's old house at the end of the path.
  • Fundy builds a secret bunker underneath the Manberg flag pole.
  • Fundy frees Niki's bee Beelloon , a refugee from the Pet War and tries bringing it back to Manberg, but it presumably dies on the boat trip back.
  • Skeppy hands over one of Tommy's valuable discs, "Cat", to BadBoyHalo. They both make a promise to trade the disc between each other and no one else.

October 1, 2020

  • Tommy relocates his pet cow Henry from the holiday home to the Eiffel Tower in order to keep him safe.
    • Tommy, Wilbur , Technoblade, George, and Fundy attempt to do HBomb's parkour .

October 2, 2020

  • Tommy and Techno build the Intimidation Tower , a large cobblestone tower outside of Pogtopia, for intimidation purposes.
    • They temporarily take Tubbo and Antfrost hostage and force them to mine cobblestone.
    • Tubbo creates another alter ego, Subbo .
  • Eret takes down Jack Manifold's unfinished Tesco store build and replaces it with a beacon.
  • HBomb acquires the rare music disc, Pigstep, becoming the only member on the server to own this disc.
  • HBomb makes a Leap of Faith obstacle course in the hills beyond Eret's castle. George is the first player to successfully finish the course. Technoblade also completes the course in a later stream.
  • Fundy joins a VC with Iskall85 (not whitelisted on the server) and gives him a brief tour around the SMP. Iskall helps Fundy make a 3x3 redstone piston door in his secret bunker .

October 3, 2020

  • Antfrost and BadBoyHalo decorate the Intimidation Tower with rings of pink wool as revenge for Ant being taken hostage the day before.
  • Antfrost, with Bad and Awesamdude's help, starts building an animal sanctuary behind Eret's castle.
    • He travels to other biomes, collecting a polar bear, an ocelot, parrots and salmon, and then begins naming the animals and placing them in enclosures.

October 4-5, 2020

  • Andrea Botez joins the server after George loses a bet while competing in a chess match against her.
    • As a result of losing the bet, George is instructed to build a large "I ♥ ANDREA" sign in the sky near the Reverse Coaster . Andrea is promptly banned from the server after she witnesses the sign. The next day, Fundy changes the last letter of the sign to an 'F'.
  • Technoblade uses a faraway Nether Fortress to farm Wither skulls, which Eret and Awesamdude eventually discover.
  • Awesamdude sets up his first base, a mini stone fortress near the Socializing Club.
  • Awesamdude builds a packed ice highway in the Nether above the community Nether hub that leads to a portal for his other base; bearing similarities to the transportation for Tubbo's jungle base .
    • His other base is concealed behind a stone wall in a mountain, with a decoy house in front of the Nether Portal. During a later stream, George visits the secret base and reluctantly agrees to be roommates with Awesamdude.
  • Sapnap and Karl come across Tommy's cow Henry at the Eiffel Tower where Tommy left him. Sapnap takes Henry hostage as leverage because Tommy stole Karl's "Only 4 Poki" book from Party Island. Sapnap accidentally kills Henry with fall damage, with Karl as the sole witness.
  • After finding out about Henry's death, Tommy, together with Dream and Niki, grief the Eiffel Tower, beginning the War of the Burning Eiffel Tower .

October 6-8, 2020

  • Tubbo builds a straight tunnel from his hidden bunker underneath Manberg to Pogtopia.
  • Tubbo, Quackity, Techno and later, Tommy, start a drug dog fighting ring which Tubbo calls "Dogs On Drugs", also known as "Operation: DOD". They use an area next to Flatty Patty's grave in Manberg as their base of operations where they breed the wolves.
    • Tommy and Quackity start excavating the inside of a hill for the location of the actual fight ring itself.
    • The operation quickly falls apart after many incidents of Tommy attempting to murder the dogs.
    • The remaining members turn into cops, arrest Tommy, and make him dig his own grave.
  • Mexican Dream makes his first appearance, meeting Awesamdude and Tubbo in Manberg.
  • Quackity gives Awesamdude a tour around Manberg.
  • Tubbo and Tommy continue construction on the tunnel system from Manberg to Pogtopia. Tommy makes a plan to add a railway running through the tunnels.
  • BadBoyHalo builds another obsidian vault below his and Skeppy's mansion after Skeppy reveals the location of the first vault during a stream. He surrounds the foundations of the mansion with obsidian, builds stone statues on the front lawn and works on a tree farm below the grounds.
  • Karl lightly griefs the Manberg Embassy by removing the doorway and replacing some of the floors with magma blocks.
  • Eret builds a giant cobblestone pyramid using lava-casting in an ocean past spawn, near Ponk's unfinished "Project Snowzone" build . They place a beacon in their castle and burn down most of the walls surrounding spawn, making an easy route to escape.
  • Eret excavates a perfect chunk error outside of Lemon City . It is unofficially named "The Hole" . Ponk currently uses the Hole to store most of his animals.
  • Alyssa builds a wooden horse barn with a stone roof near the ocean, in walking distance from Antfrost's animal sanctuary.
  • Fundy and Tubbo take on the roles of demon (Dreamon) hunters and successfully perform an exorcism on Dream. DreamXD (Dream's alt) joins the server and torments the pair before leaving again.
  • Under Schlatt's orders, Fundy and Tubbo continue the cleanup of Manberg.
    • They patch up holes in the land, take down the Ninja Drug Alley and fix the burnt Election Podium.
    • Fundy builds a couple of small attractions: a berry farm and a fountain in the lake.
    • Fundy tells Tubbo about Schlatt's plan to take down the Camarvan, but Tubbo is hopeful that he can convince Schlatt otherwise.
  • Schlatt announces the Manberg Festival , a celebration of Manberg and democracy.
  • Wilbur schemes with Dream to blow up Manberg during the festival .
    • Tommy realizes that Wilbur's mental health has declined sharply since they were exiled, and starts opposing his idea.
