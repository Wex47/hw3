Video

Minecraft Speedrunner VS PRO is a video released by Dream to his main channel on June 23, 2020. This video is different from the rest of the videos in the series. The point of the challenge is that either Dream or IlluminaHD , a professional Minecraft speedrunner who broke the record of the fastest speedrun in Minecraft 1.7 once and Minecraft 1.14 five times, has to beat Minecraft while running away from being hunted from each other, making this video act as a double Minecraft manhunt.
