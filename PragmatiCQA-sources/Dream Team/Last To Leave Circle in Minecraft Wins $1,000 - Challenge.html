Video

Last To Leave Circle in Minecraft Wins $1,000 - Challenge is a video released by Dream to his main channel on September 14, 2019 featuring Sapnap , Skeppy , Shadowgirl, GeorgeeeHD , BadBoyHalo , Antfrost , and a6d .

In this video, Dream's friends try to survive for as long as they can in a circle to win $1,000. They can knock each other off at any point.

LiamTBN is credited in the description for recording the video.

Video Details

Many times throughout the video, BadBoyHalo shows reluctance to hit Skeppy off. This is most likely because BBH is a naturally nice person and is very good friends with Skeppy.

Shadowgirl proposed a deal when there was 5 people left including her. She said that if they let her win, she would split the money 5 ways. All of them said it was a scam and George said that the only person trustworthy enough to do that was him.
