3 Minecraft Speedrunners VS Hunter ft. MrBeast is a video released by Dream on May 24, 2020. In this video, Dream is the hunter, and MrBeast and two of his friends, Chandler and Karl Jacobs , are the speedrunners.

Each speedrunner had 1 life and could respawn after death to help the others, but if all three have died the speedrunners' team have lost.

Trivia

  • This is the first time MrBeast played with Dream.
  • Karl had slain Dream once at 16:01.
  • This is the only Minecraft Manhunt video that has a title that doesn't begin with "Minecraft Speedrunner".
  • This manhunt has several differences from most other manhunts:
    • This is the manhunt with the highest number of speedrunners.
    • This is the only manhunt in which the hunter is required to directly kill speedrunners to achieve victory, and deaths by other causes don't count.
    • This is the only manhunt in which speedrunners are allowed to respawn, and can assist and defend unkilled speedrunners.
    • This is the only manhunt to end before any players enter the Nether.
    • This is the only manhunt in which players used facecams.
