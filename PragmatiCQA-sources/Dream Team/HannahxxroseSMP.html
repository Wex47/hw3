Physiology

Appearance

Hannah is an individual with light skin, long brown hair, and green eyes. She wears a pink sweater and skirt, white boots with roses on them, and a flower crown made of roses. One of her arms is also covered with a rose vine.

When leeched, her shirt and skirt are all white and the roses drained of red.

She also has a business skin in which her hair is shorter and roses slightly larger and shifted around. She also now wears a deep red suit trousers and buttoned red up shirt.

Biology

Her species has been confirmed to be a fairy. Although she appears human-like, she appears to have a connection with the flowers around her house, which tie very closely to her life force and her health to the point that even when thousands of blocks away from her home, she grew very sickly and weak when the vines leeched at her roses.

Hannah the content creator has also stated that her character "grows stronger with the roses surrounding her house, and can even control the environment around her to an extent".

Personality

Hannah is a kind and helpful individual who spends much of her time on the SMP cleaning up messes and decorating areas. She is an excellent builder and decorator, having cleaned up and decorated multiple public places on the SMP such as the tree farm and spawn. She has also gifted her roses to multiple people on the server.

However, when faced with conflict, she does not hesitate to intervene and take action. She is very unlike some on the server, who are hesitant or avoidant of the conflicts around them to some extent and do not want to cause more damage. Hannah will readily take extreme actions in the name of solving problems and keeping peace--such as when she killed her dogs so that they would not suffer and succumb to the influence of the Crimson.

Flaws

Hannah is sometimes unaware of the extent of certain conflicts on the server and can underestimate them, believing them to be easily solved by something as simple as placing a rose. However, as seen with the Crimson, this is often not the case, and it can lead her to be put in danger or harmed.

History

Retribution Arc

Hannah joined the server and was shown around the server by both Purpled and CaptainPuffy . Puffy welcomed her into the server with some netherite tools and unenchanted diamond armor, and Hannah thanked her by giving her a rose. With Foolish 's aid, Hannah built her home behind a spider spawner and named it the " Rose Residence ." She thanked Foolish by giving him a rose as well as some yeezys (diamond boots).

Empowerment Arc

Peace

For the first few months on the server, Hannah wasn't involved in any conflicts. She built a path to the Rose Residence and gave all of the members a rose from her. She also went mining in the nether a lot, and managed to get hold of a lot of netherite. She interacted with a lot of people, but always seemed to make friends with them. Hannah spent a lot of her time harvesting Wither Roses and planting them around the Rose Residence. She also started a wheat farm and used the products to create an entire field of cakes for everyone.

Failed bargaining

Hannah was mining for diamonds when Ponk sent a message to her telling her that she will be mugged. Not wanting to be mugged, Hannah asked for a deal and Ponk said that because they were friends, she could give him 10 gold blocks and he would never try to mug her. Hannah agreed and went mining for gold, but only came up with seven causing Ponk to try and kill her. Hannah said she would give him nine more to keep the deal and Ponk gave her an item frame telling her to put it up. Hannah places the picture to find it is a huge picture of Steve Harvey. She destroys it and burns the picture in lava. She wakes up the next morning to find a ladder up the side of her house and an attic at the top. The attic is covered in pictures of the Kanye picture she burned and one picture on the floor, which is of her in her house taken from a bird's eye view. Her blast furnaces had also been stolen. Ponk arrived at her house for the gold, but she didn't have it yet so Hannah hid in the attic. Ponk tried to break into her house through the ground but she dropped lava on top of him. Ponk left, vowing revenge. Hannah returned to her house after mining for a little while to discover her entire house had been filled with dirt by Ponk. Hannah then went to nether to get the gold, but the portal was blown up by Ponk and she ended up falling into the lava with all of her stuff including the gold and dying.

Blood Vines

Hannah was around 10,000 blocks from her house picking roses and planting trees for days. Her health had been steadily decreasing, and she claimed to be dizzy and sick; she noticed something pulling her back to her house. She notices blood vines growing on her house, and concludes that the Egg was the thing pulling her back and damaging her health due to her close relationship with nature. She says that the Egg has to 'go' (meaning be destroyed).

She talks to Sam about her house, and Sam informs her about the vines. Sam and her clean up her house, and BadBoyHalo and Antfrost show up to confront Hannah about the cleaning. Sam makes excuses to try and save Hannah, saying she was allergic to the vines, but this doesn't phase Bad and Ant, and they take her to the Egg room.

Hannah is very clearly sick whenever she is in proximity to the Egg and tries to leave but Ant and Bad won't let her. Instead she places roses, hoping that they would combat the strength of the Egg and saying they improve her health. However, The Egg tells Bad and Ant it likes Hannah and not to kill her. Instead, they trap her in a box right above the Egg, and Hannah states she will be there for a while, and that she suspected something like this may happen. Hannah then shows her roses in her inventory, saying that she thought that they were enough to last her the night, but it wasn't after Ant took some. She then complains about feeling faint before passing out in the Egg room.

The next time Hannah wakes up from fainting is the next day when Sam comes to save her, but the two discover that her skin has been completely leached out of all color. Sam walks down the Egg, making Hannah follow him. She says that she still feels very ill with all her roses gone, and could not even sprint, but that the Egg was speaking to her and she wants to listen. Sam walks her away trying to convince her not to return.

When they reach the house, Hannah discovers that it is covered in blood vines, the color had been leeched out of the walls like her skin and all of her roses had vanished and all that was left were white tulips. Hannah attempts to convince Sam not to destroy the blood vines, despite the fact he pointed out they'd almost destroyed all of her roses. Hannah said the Egg was making her punch Sam, be rather angry and more, and Sam tries to reconvince Hannah about how harmful the Egg could be to her.

Hannah then takes down the blood vines hesitantly with Sam, but the Egg continues speaking to her. When they start burning the vines Hannah starts to feel a lot of pain and says that destroying the Egg hurts her. Once the vines have been burnt she starts replanting the roses, still unsure whether she believes Sam or not. Later, the Eggpire comes to her house after finding out that she escaped. Sam tells her to hide in the basement, and she does, deciding that she trusts him over the Egg. However, once she has hidden in the basement she hears the Egg telling her to go and reveal herself. Hannah climbs out of it and goes around the house to where she can see Sam and the Eggpire arguing, seeing this she doesn't know whether to reveal herself like the Egg wants her to or hide like Sam wants her to. Before she can come to a decision, the Eggpire leaves. Sam gets angry at Hannah for leaving her basement on the Egg orders, but Hannah promises not to listen to it anymore. The two part ways, and Hannah wonders whether to keep the promise.

Bargaining and the Bank

Hannah meets Sam again near the community tree farm, and Sam gives Hannah TNT to use to blow up the Cat Maid Café. Sam then places the TNT himself after Hannah refuses. Hannah moves one of the cats out of the café, and Sam states that he would get consent from HBomb through Twitter. Sam then makes Hannah promise to support him. Hannah moves the rest of the cats out of the café. Sam then hands her a flint and steel to light the TNT, and Nihachu comes to confront them. Sam frames Hannah for placing the TNT and telling him to set it off. Niki meets the two at the café, and starts removing the TNT. HBomb later meets them at the café to remove the rest of the TNT. Hannah defends herself, and claims that Sam was the one who had put down the TNT and tried to kill the cats. Sam then suggests to Niki and HBomb to blow up Hannah's house, and Hannah panics when they are inclined to agree. She then points out that the cafe is on her property.

HBomb notices that the "cash register" is missing. Hannah throws it at Sam to frame him. Sam tries to defend himself, and Hannah states that his bank is on her property as well. She asks for 10% of the bank's profit, and Sam agrees to employ her at his bank. HBomb also offers Hannah a job at the café, and Hannah starts requesting netherite payment. Sam refuses Hannah's request to choose her payment, and gives her the role of manager of the bank. Niki warns Sam and Hannah that she would take action if they touch the café, and leaves.

The three make their way to Hannah's house, where HBomb places down TNT in the graveyard as a warning. He warns Hannah, stating that he will blow up her house and kill her pets if she did anything to the café. He also adds that he would get Sapnap to help. Sam also tells HBomb that he would fire Hannah if she touches the café. Niki seems to dislike Hannah until she makes it up to her by giving Niki a nether star.

Memory Loss

Hannah loses her memory, and believes that she has just joined the server, her skin has also got its color back for the first time since she was trapped by the Egg. She decides to go on her 'first' ever tour of the server to see what it's like. Hannah gets some items from a chest, and states that her levels were from killing mobs. She gets stone tools and leather armor from Eret's museum storage, and dyes the armor pink. She finds Puffy's Friendship Flower at the mansion, and jumps off the Big Innit Hotel. Hannah goes to the L'Manberg crater, the white house, and searches the chests near the crater. She gets food from Purpled's cabin, and comments on the prank on the cabin.

After a little while, Hannah says she needs to build a house out of pink wood before it is too late. She harvests crimson wood at the community tree farm, and starts building a house near Fundy's gameshow set. Realizing that she doesn't have enough wood, Hannah goes in search for more and says she feels a pull to a certain area, meaning the wood must be there. It turns out to be the Egg and as she walks down it starts speaking to her - telling her to come and join it. Hannah doesn't want to join it, but still thinks that the materials for her house are in there so is forced to enter. Before she does, Sam arrives and slowly leads her away.

Hannah tells Sam that the last time he met him, she was about to go to sleep. Sam remembers, and Hannah tells him that she was searching for wood for her house. Sam, confused tells her that she had already had a house, and Hannah starts thinking that she had been dreaming, and that she woke up in the server. Sam tells her that she had joined a few months ago, while Hannah repeats that she had just joined. Sam shows Hannah's first house, and states that she had built it. He also tells her that she had made the path to the house, along with the cakes along it. Sam also tells her that she was the manager of the bank, and Hannah seems to remember. Hannah tells him that she was building her house, while Sam tells her that she had already had a house. Hannah seems to remember her old house, and Sam reminds her of her animals, and the two go to Hannah's graveyard. They find all Hannah's animals gone, and the sheep pen broken. Hannah remembers her animals, and repeats that she had fallen asleep, and had woke up in the server. Sam states that she had been on the server for a while, and Hannah tells Sam that she was searching for wood in the Egg room for her house. Hannah accuses Sam of stealing her animals, but they find the rest of her animals are safe.

Hannah then takes Sam to her new house, and Sam destroys it with TNT. Sam tells Hannah that she already had a house, and Hannah argues that her dream told her to build the house, and had instructed her to get crimson wood from the Egg room. Hannah hits Sam with a sword, and the two clear the remains of the house. She starts thinking that she had been sleepwalking a few times, and Sam tells her to get rest, and that she needed to get her things. Hannah asks Sam if he had removed part of the crewmate statue at the bank, and Sam replies that he didn't. Sam states that he would be working on the bank soon, and asks Hannah if she could still work as the bank manager. Hannah states that she could still be the manager of the bank, and promises to get some rest. She gives Sam some obsidian to keep her inside her house, which he reluctantly does.

Eggpire Planning

The two meet Hannah at the Community House. Sam joins them on the way to Foolish's totem statue through the nether. Sam breaks the portal when they arrive at the statue, and Foolish gives each of the three a Rolex. Foolish and Puffy then show off their suits for the banquet. Sam asks the three if they had gotten an invite to the banquet, and states that the Eggpire was turning a new leaf. Foolish and Hannah mention that the Eggpire promised free food. The group talks together a lot and Hannah stays quiet almost the entire time. The group decides to hide weapons in the table at the Banquet and hide armor in their inventories. Sam gives the three a code word: "platypi platypi," and Foolish suggests "spruce boat," which they eventually agree on.

Hannah decides to rebuild her house with pink wood again, as the color still hasn't returned from when the Blood Vines covered it. She also makes it bigger with holes in the walls and a bigger basement so Pro-Omelette could hide weapons and supplies in there. Antfrost came to her house to officially invite her to the Red Banquet, Hannah seemed extremely skeptical and told him she didn't want to return to the Egg.

Betrayal

Hannah joined the Red Banquet quite early, she spent most of the time chatting to the other guests and partying. She listened to the speeches and even had hushed words with Puffy. When lava fell and trapped them in she seemed outraged until Puffy opened the chest where the weapons were hidden and discovered it was empty. Hannah revealed her own plan and joined the Eggpire, telling Puffy and Sam she was 'Sorry' and that she 'Didn't have a choice'. Hannah was seemed the least panicked when Sam TNT trapped the Egg, not even screaming like the others and even laughing before their plan was revealed. She offered up Eret for execution because they spoke out against her betrayal and the Egg in general. Foolish however stepped in to save Eret and Hannah led him up to the sacrificial chamber. Whilst Bad and Puffy argued, Hannah stood in the background quietly chanting 'Sacrifice.' Ant murdered Foolish and, although a bit has taken back, Hannah immediately asked who to kill next.

When Quackity, Technoblade, and Purpled turned up, Hannah stood by the Eggpire and was up for trying to take them on. However, when it was revealed Puffy had an axe and murdered Antfrost with it, Hannah immediately started to fight. When they began to retreat, Hannah protested against it - saying they could take them, but was forced to leave. Once the Eggpire left, Bad commanded them all to split up as Purpled was still chasing them. It is unknown where Hannah went after that as nobody has seen her since. In a short lore explanation, Hannah said that her character is still heavily influenced and infected by the Egg, but also confused - not realizing the consequences and still believing she can run the bank with Sam.

Getting a Job at Sam's Bank

Hannah arrives at the Bank starts her first day as the bank manager. Sam introduces Boomer to Hannah and Boomer states that Hannah seems familiar and Hannah agrees, but they both don't know exactly how they know each other. Sam says that Boomer would have a higher position than Hannah in the Bank because of her betrayal at the Red Banquet. Hannah argues that it was just one bad thing she has done and thought that she has fixed problems with this strategy before, but Sam says that he told her to not go down there. Sam eventually asks Boomer for his opinion, and after speaking to his hat, Boomer stated that Hannah seemed trustworthy. Sam eventually agreed to employ Hannah as the bank's manager and stated that Boomer is her superior.
