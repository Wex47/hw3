George "Geo" Anderson , better known online as Spifey , is a Minecraft YouTuber who produces challenge and troll videos. Spifey is associated with Skeppy , who allowed JustVurb to join the Dream SMP through his account. During Vurb's SMP visit, Spifey helped Vurb through voice chat by providing opinions and suggestions.
