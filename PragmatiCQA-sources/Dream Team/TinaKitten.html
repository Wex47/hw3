Christina "Tina" Kenyon , also known as TinaKitten , is a South Korean streamer and a close friend of the Dream Team . She joined the Dream SMP on October 23, 2021.

Trivia

  • Tina is the thirty-sixth member to join the Dream SMP.
    • She is the fifth female member after ItsAlyssa , Nihachu , CaptainPuffy , and Hannahxxrose .
    • She is also the first 100 Thieves member to join the SMP.
  • Tina is left-handed.
  • Tina is part of 100 Thieves, a gaming organization that is owned by another friend of the Dream Team, Valkyrae.
  • She was a prominent member of the OTV SMP alongside Pokimane and 5up .
  • Tina thought that Dream was joking about adding her to the SMP after he mentioned inviting her to the Dream SMP during her Hypixel stream.
