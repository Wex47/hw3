LazarBeam , more simply known as Lazar or The Beam , is the twenty-ninth member of the Dream SMP, joining on November 23, 2020. He formed the faction Boomerville with Vikkstar123 .

Physiology

Lazar is a gingerbread man with white eyes and blue furrowed brows. His mouth is curved into a frown, and his mouth is open with a red line outlining his lips. He wears a green armband along with red gloves and shoes. The skins design is based off of his preferred outfit of choice from the game Fortnite Battle Royale called Merry Marauder which he nicknamed "Gingy."

Personality

Due to Lazar being old, he is trying to stay out of trouble most of the time. He does not seem to be very fond of L'Manberg and has even referred to it as a cult on multiple occasions. He also is not too fond of El Rapids as they have been building on his front lawn.

Ambitious and righteous, Lazar believes that he can obtain the most powerful items on the server to rival or be treated as equals with the stronger members of the server.

One of the goals that he has talked about consistently is the building of a Membership Monastery as an opposition to the Church of Twitch Prime. He also has a fondness for melons due to them being the primary food source of his own survival world from 2019.

History

Lazar joined the Dream SMP on November 23. He joined with Vikkstar and was given a tour by Dream and Karl Jacobs before co-founding Boomerville with Vikkstar. He has since built the Church of Boomerville to worship the YouTube gods and have the CEO of YouTube say the masses. He also decided to build a membership monastery in the far off mountains after seeing the Holy Land for Twitch Prime. Lazar is also particularly fond of melons, with him making a melon farm in Boomerville during his first week on the server.
