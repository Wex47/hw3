Physiology

Tina is a humanoid demon with light brown hair, pale skin, and brown eyes. She wears white and purple clothing with pastel boots. At first glance, Tina looks like she has cat ears, but they are actually horns covered in wool. She also wears a carrot clip in her hair.

Tina is one of the few Dream SMP characters to have official art depicting their appearance since her actress shared it on her alt Twitter.

History

Empowerment Arc

First day

Tina joined the server on October 23, 2021 and was given a tour by Karl Jacobs , Sapnap , Punz , and Callahan . The first location they visited was Church Prime . There, Tina was baptized and participated in her first service.

After Church Prime, Tina was led throughout the main locations of the Greater Dream SMP and was told the server's history by Sapnap up until the Disc Confrontation , albeit with some bias. Tina was then shown Pandora's Vault . While at Pandora's Vault, Tina expressed a desire to meet Dream and expressed concern for Dream being alone in prison. However, Sapnap told her that there is not a day that goes by that he doesn’t think about Dream, but Dream cannot be freed.

Following the showing of Pandora's Vault, the group traveled to Kinoko Kingdom . Tina expressed interest in attending Sapnap and Karl's wedding, but it was revealed that only Kinoko citizens and Dream can attend.

On November 27, 2021, it was confirmed by Sapnap that Tina joined Kinoko Kingdom.
