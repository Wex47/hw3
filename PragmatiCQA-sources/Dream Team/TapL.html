Harvey Lee , better known online as TapL , is an American Minecraft YouTuber who is well known for his Bedwars, Ultra Hardcore, and Skywars content. He commonly collaborates with Dream SMP members Skeppy and Technoblade .

Trivia

  • On November 23, 2020, TapL made a joke tweet that he was invited to join the Dream SMP. It was revealed that this was fake because he leaked the link in the tweet, which lead people to join a joke server called "joe f*ckin momma GOTTEM." Spifey and JustVurb also helped him spread the joke. VelvetIsCake also fell for the prank.
  • His original username was "appleman158", later changing it to "TehApple", then "Tapple" and finally to "TapL".
  • Harvey is bisexual, coming out in a tweet on January 1, 2020.
  • TapL has played in nine Minecraft Championships , winning MCC 12 with GeorgeNotFound , Wilbur Soot , and Philza on the Green Guardians.
  • TapL has the same birthday as MrBeast .
