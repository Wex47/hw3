Skeppy is the sixteenth member of the Dream SMP, joining on July 18, 2020. He is a citizen of the Badlands and formerly, of the Dream SMP . He missed some key conflicts, being seen missing during the Disc Confrontation and during both festivals. He is a firm supporter of the Badlands and is a friend of multiple people on different factions on the SMP. He played a major role in the blood vines, being the first person to be corrupted by the Egg and BadBoyHalo's main motivation throughout the arc.

In the Early Dream SMP, Skeppy was most notable for having possession of one of Tommy's discs , Cat. Skeppy was best friends with BadBoyHalo and joined the Badlands alongside him. As the Egg emerged, Skeppy was horrified of the effects it was having on his friends, mainly BadBoyHalo. Skeppy encased the Egg with obsidian, but trapped himself inside. When he was released, he had turned red and had expressed no emotions or desire other than being with the Egg. Skeppy had lost a canon life to BadBoyHalo by him accidentally pushing him into lava after an argument.

Physiology

Skeppy appears as a sentient anthropomorphic diamond block. He has black eyes and a mouth with a red tongue. He also occasionally wears a duck onesie. When exposed to the Egg, the color of his skin turns red, although his skin is likely still made of diamonds.

Personality

Usual Personality

He is cocky and energetic to a degree, as his main goal on the server seemingly being to cause as much chaos as possible.

Egg Possession

After a prolonged period of exposure to the Egg's influence, Skeppy became completely overtaken by its influence. He talked in a monotone voice and expresses no emotion other than happiness when being close to the Egg. He also did not show any interest in anything unrelated to the Egg.

History

Early Dream SMP Arc

Skeppy joined before the Dream Team SMP vs. L'Manberg War only to troll Tommy and grief his house. He burned one of Tommy's music discs, painfully souring their relationship.

L'Manberg Independence Arc

After the war, Skeppy rejoined the server and started playing. He built his house at the end of Prime Path and managed to gain possession of Tommy's Cat disc after trading Spirit's leather with Dream .

Manberg Rebellion Arc

After another period of inactivity, Skeppy returned to the server during the Manberg Rebellion to find his house griefed. He still had possession of Cat, but transferred it to BadBoyHalo and made a promise to keep it between them. The two then demolished his old house and replaced it with a giant quartz mansion, protecting the foundation with obsidian and building a vault below the mansion. He subsequently joined the Badlands because of Bad's involvement in the faction and general alignment with their goals.

Non-Canon: August 1-10 (idk the date)

Skeppy logs on to the Dream SMP, and he says that no one is on, and goes around exploring the SMP.

Retribution Arc

Corruption

As the Egg brainwashed Antfrost and BadBoyHalo, Skeppy was left seemingly unaffected. He was disturbed by both it and Bad's relation with it, asking for Awesamdude to help clean up its growths within his mansion. After the Egg was covered in obsidian, effectively freeing the brainwashed people, Skeppy broke the shell, causing those formerly affected to become brainwashed again. Later on, Skeppy seemingly sacrificed himself by covering the Egg with obsidian and trapping himself within. This caused the formerly-brainwashed trio to work to destroy the surrounding growths and blood vines, trying to figure out a way to save Skeppy.

Bad ended up freeing Skeppy, but the prolonged period of close exposure to the Egg heavily brainwashed him, turning him bright red as opposed to his normal blue. Bad attempted to bring back Skeppy's memories and autonomy to him (using sand, the world-edit axe, blue dye, 14, etc.), but he showed no interest. Skeppy expressed that he felt "euphoric" thanks to the Egg. Unlike the previously corrupted bunch, Skeppy did not attempt to lure anyone under the Egg's influence. Instead, he just wanted to go back to his obsidian prison. Skeppy also moved from his house into a small hut .

Empowerment Arc

First Canon death

On April 18th, 2021, BadBoyHalo went into the Egg Room that had been decorated with a table and a dance floor in preparation for the Red Banquet. He noticed Skeppy on top of the Egg and tried to coax him down, to which Skeppy refused, telling Bad to leave him alone. When trying to convince Skeppy to go on a walk with him, Bad said that if Skeppy hadn't been there when Quackity tried to blow up the Egg, it would have been destroyed. Bad pleaded with Skeppy, telling him to stay away from the Egg. Skeppy yelled back, calling him selfish for not wanting him to spend time with the Egg. Bad retaliated in disbelief and shock, demanding Skeppy to apologize for calling him selfish after all the sacrifices he's made for their friendship. Skeppy refused, which lead to Bad hitting him a couple of times before finally punching him into a small pool of lava, killing him and taking away his first canon life. Skeppy then appeared at Big Daddy Island , calling out to Bad. It was unclear if BadBoyHalo was also at the island.
