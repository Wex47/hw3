Video

Minecraft Speedrunner VS 3 Hunters FINALE is a video released by Dream to his main channel on June 19, 2020. The point of the challenge is that Dream has to beat Minecraft while running away from three hunters instead of one for the third time. The hunters are GeorgeNotFound , Sapnap , and BadBoyHalo .
