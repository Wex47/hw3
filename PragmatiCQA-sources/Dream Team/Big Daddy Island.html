History/Lore

Establishment

On February 16, 2021, BadBoyHalo and Skeppy traveled to an island far from spawn via nether portal. Upon arrival, Bad had Skeppy and himself place all their items in buried chests before setting off to settle. Bad claimed he was taking Skeppy far away so Skeppy could brush up on his Minecraft skills by playing on survival.

Together, the two collected materials and built another house together, declaring this island as their vacation home.

No other people are allowed to come to the island. Visitors are only allowed if Bad and Skeppy are given prior warning, and if the visitor is blindfolded so they don’t know how to travel there.

Skeppy's exhaustion

On Skeppy’s March 3, 2021 stream, Skeppy suddenly became very exhausted in a way that was reminiscent of how he acted when corrupted by the egg . He claimed that he looked at himself and his face was all red. He pleaded with Bad to get him golden apples, so Bad led Skeppy into the mine to find gold. Eventually, they smelted enough ore to craft a golden apple, which made Skeppy return to normal.

After Bad and Skeppy's confrontation

During Bad and Skeppy's confrontation in the Egg Room on April 18th, Bad punches Skeppy into a small pool of lava, killing him and taking a canon life. The screen goes black and the scene switches to Blue Skeppy standing in their house on the island. He starts calling for Bad, lightheartedly at first but getting increasingly worried. The camera backs out, exiting the front door and revealing the front of the house as Skeppy begins to beg Bad to answer him.

It is currently unclear if this was a dream, a hallucination, or something else entirely.

Other inhabitants

ShortBoyHalo

ShortBoyHalo is a wolf tamed by Skeppy. It was named "ShortBoyHalo" after an argument about heights with Bad.

Mr. Bons

Mr. Bons (pronounced Mr. Bones) is a skeleton that followed Skeppy back into the house during an argument with Bad. Bad initially didn't want the skeleton in the house since it was shooting arrows all over the room, but eventually conceded. Mr. Bons lives in a boat in the main room of the house.

Structures/landmarks

Bad and Skeppy’s vacation home

Bad and Skeppy’s vacation home is a house built by Bad and Skeppy comprised mostly of oak and birch logs, planks, and fences. It floats above the water off the coast of the main island. Bad originally made the roof out of dirt so they could grow a farm on top of it, but Skeppy disliked how it looked, and changed it to wood while Bad was offline. There are currently three rooms inside with an open area on the roof.

Bad and Skeppy's bed

Bad and Skeppy’s shared bed. Bad placed the bed in the ocean outside of their house to keep creepers from blowing it up and breaking their respawn point.

BadBoyHalo statue

The statue of BadBoyHalo was built by Skeppy as a surprise present for Bad. It is located on a smaller island adjacent to the main island and made of cobblestone, netherrack, sand, and birch planks.

Wheat farm

A small wheat farm near the entrance to the mine that also includes saplings and a rose bush that Skeppy gave to Bad as a gift.

Mine

Originally a two block wide staircase where Bad and Skeppy strip-mined to Y-level 12 that has since grown to branch off into nearby cave systems.
