Darryl Noveschosch, better known online as BadBoyHalo and formerly as SaintsofGames , is a popular YouTuber and the unofficial fourth member of the Dream Team . He is also best friends with YouTuber Skeppy and owner of the Minecraft server MunchyMC .

History

Meeting Dream

Dream met Bad when Dream played frequently on MCPvP. Bad was staff on the server at the time, and they became good friends. After MCPvP shut down, Bad invited Dream to be staff on his new server, MunchyMC. Dream is now a co-owner of the server, and owns Prison.

Meeting George

George coded plugins for MunchyMC, where he met Dream and Bad. He is currently a staff member on the server.

Meeting Skeppy

Skeppy and Bad were fans of each other, and had several small interactions beforehand. They officially met when Skeppy applied for staff on MunchyMC to troll Bad for a video. Skeppy pranked Bad several more times on the server, and after continually talking, they became very good friends.
