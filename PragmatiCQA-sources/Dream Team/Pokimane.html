SMP visits

Pre-debut

Pokimane was invited by TommyInnit when they teamed up in MC Championship 10. Pokimane agreed, but never joined, either due to not being whitelisted or her forgetting to join. Karl and the others started building Party Island , Party Park and the Pokimane War Bunker for her arrival. Additionally, Karl kept asking Pokimane on Twitter to join the SMP, and she finally agreed to join on December 12, 2020.

Arrival

When Pokimane finally joined the server, Karl gave her a tour of the server, showing her many of the locations such as the Community House . Throughout her visit, Karl attempted to try to impress her to get her attention, and he became irritated when she paid attention to others players on the server. George , LazarBeam , Punz & Sapnap also accompanied her during this tour. The tour concluded with Pokimane stating that it was the best tour she had ever had.

Second visit

On April 12, 2021, Pokimane joined the server once again to help Karl with rebuilding Party Island in Kinoko Kingdom .
