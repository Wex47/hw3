Video

Minecraft Speedrunner VS 4 Hunters is a video released by Dream to his main channel on September 5, 2020. It is the first Minecraft Manhunt video that features a fourth hunter, who is Antfrost . The other hunters are BadBoyHalo , GeorgeNotFound , and Sapnap .

Results

Show/hide spoiler
Dream described the result as a "technical draw" due to the dragon dying from TNT lit by Dream who was slain by Sapnap before the TNT exploded.

Dream makes a blunder of rushing in with TNT even though it also could've ended with a diamond axe swing as well, it is debatable whether it should be Dream's win since it was undeniable the odds were in his favor or it should be the Hunters win because Dream made a mistake despite it being in his favor. It was eventually decided that the Hunters had indeed won.

Trivia
