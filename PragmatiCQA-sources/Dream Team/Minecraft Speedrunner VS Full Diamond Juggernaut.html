Video

Minecraft Speedrunner VS Full Diamond Juggernaut is a video released by Dream to his main channel on January 4, 2020. It serves as the eighth Minecraft Manhunt video.

In this video, the point of the challenge is that Dream has to beat Minecraft while running away from Sapnap , who is plotted to stop Dream by killing him to make him lose the challenge. However, unlike in the previous videos, Sapnap gets full diamond armor and a diamond sword to increase the intensity of this challenge.
