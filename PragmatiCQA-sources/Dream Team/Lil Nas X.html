Montero Lamar Hill , better known online as Lil Nas X , is an American rapper, singer, songwriter. His song "Old Town Road" holds the record for the longest time on the Billboard Top 100 chart. He has won two Grammy Awards.

SMP Visit

During Lil Nas X's visit to the Dream SMP on March 27, 2021 he was toured by Karl Jacobs , Corpse Husband , GeorgeNotFound and Ranboo . He also learned how to play Minecraft and built a treehouse in Kinoko Kingdom during the tour.

Trivia

  • He is openly gay.
  • His first time playing Minecraft was during his tour.
  • He was originally a Nicki Minaj fan account before he became known.
  • His father is Robert Stafford, and his mother’s name is Tamikia Hill. They divorced when he was six years old. His father then married Mia Stafford, who has been a second mom for Lil Nas.
  • He has two brothers (Lamarco & Tramon Hill) as well as three half-brothers (Robert Sleepy, Jamal Newsome, Labrock Anderson.) Also, he has two older half-sisters (Bianca & Shaquisha Hill.)
  • He has two Bernese Mountain Dogs.
  • He has the same birthday as SMP member TommyInnit .
