Video

Minecraft Survivor VS 3 Hitmen is a video released by Dream on his main channel that premiered on March 11, 2021. It is similar to the videos in the Minecraft Manhunt series on Dream's channel, though this time, the objective differs. It features Dream as the survivalist and BadBoyHalo , GeorgeNotFound , and Sapnap as the hitmen. The hitmen have one hour and a half to kill Dream, and if they do so, they win $10,000. There's a 500x500 border that they have to stay inside during the video, and there's a constant glowing effect around Dream as well. This installment however, introduces an invincibility period every 10 minutes.
