Demetrius , known online as Jikishi , is a YouTuber and streamer who joined the Dream SMP on October 23, 2021. Demetrius was removed days after on October 25, 2021 for grooming underaged fans.

Grooming

On October 25, 2021, a user by the name of eron on Twitter came forward with a past interaction with Demetrius as a minor. After the initial post by eron, multiple other victims also came out with their own stories. After Dream inquired Demetrius about the accusations, Jikishi admitted to the activities and was removed from the Dream SMP .
