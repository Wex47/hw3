Callahan is a coder and server moderator who is a friend of the Dream Team . He is a part of the group's SMP server and moderator of the team's Twitch streams. In addition, he is a developer of MunchyMC and the Dream SMP. Callahan is known for having never spoken online, using the in-game chat when 'speaking' in the Dream SMP.
