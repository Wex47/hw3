Arran Willis , better known online as JustVurb , is an English online gaming content creator who mainly focuses on making content on Minecraft, with his recent videos mostly focusing on him using Dream as clickbait in his videos.

His videos are based around trolling players and friends in both games collaborating alongside friends Landon , Skeppy , Spifey , Doni Bobes , F1NN5TER and more. He never collaborated with the Dream Team directly, but they follow each other on Twitter and had a brief confrontation on the SMP with Sapnap when Skeppy gave Vurb his account for a day.

Platforms

Dream SMP

In July 2020, JustVurb was given permission by Skeppy to use his Minecraft account for one day. After using Skeppy's account on PvPLands, Vurb logged onto the Dream SMP, where he asked Punz and Tubbo if he could borrow their armor. While Punz declined, Tubbo agreed and gave JustVurb his armor. After being giving the armor, JustVurb ran away and eventually combat logged.

After logging back in, JustVurb attempted to inform the online members that he wasn't Skeppy, however, they didn't believe him. JustVurb was then taken to the courthouse where he was shot to death by Sapnap .

After his death at the courthouse, JustVurb was quizzed on Sapnap's and Skeppy's meet-up that occurred on July 15, 2020, with the goal of figuring out if it was Skeppy or not. After it was determined that he was not Skeppy, JustVurb attempted to run away, but was eventually slain by Tubbo.

After respawning, JustVurb went to Sapnap's House , where he stole carrots and broke some glass. JustVurb then fled to Purpled's UFO . Eventually, Tubbo found him, however, and JustVurb jumped off the UFO, MLG horsed, and logged off.
