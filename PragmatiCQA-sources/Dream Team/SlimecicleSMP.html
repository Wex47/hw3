Physiology

Appearance

Charlie appears as a human with light skin, neon green eyes, and light brown hair. He wears a white shirt with 3 red lines on it (referencing "Yeah, I'm a Gamer" merch) and light blue pants. He has slime on his head and shoulder from his iconic Slimecicle character. He also wears a pair of glasses with eyes printed on them and a sash on his waist (a reference to his DND character, Br'aad , who can be found wearing the same sash).

In later appearances, he has a scar across his eye mimicking Quackity.

Biology

When he first joined the server, he had not fully written his character. As a result, several statements he made are no longer considered canon.

After a long hiatus, Slimecicle returned to the server, where it was revealed that he was completely made of slime and was only pretending to be human. As a slime, he has no bones and can squeeze into small spaces. He also cannot close his eyes, and has described that "everything is a membrane". Slimes are also seemingly capable of disguising as other objects, such as fauna, flora, and blocks, and changing their appearances. He may also be very old as he implied that it took hundreds of years to take human form. Quackity has officially confirmed that Slime is over millions of years old.

It is confirmed that his clothes are made of slime when he changed them to match the Las Nevadas official outfit, saying he "goopmorphisized" and claiming his clothes could not crease because it was "all goop". In this same video, Slimecicle says he sees in full panoramic vision. It is noted that Slime reacts strangely to water, claiming that he must keep himself hydrated. Enough water could hypothetically make him expand and grow to massive sizes.

Slimecicle is constantly coughing and expelling slime, which if consumed in small numbers allows him to access their senses as seen in Fundy. If someone takes part of his slime, as Tommy did to kidnap him, he is forced to follow to retrieve his slime and become whole again, in a similar fashion to a magnet. His body itself is extremely malleable, allowing him to move through small spaces and go through doors.

When he died, he seemed to enter a completely dormant state, covered in slime. It's implied he cannot react to the outside world and that he doesn't remember anything in this state, though this remains unconfirmed. During this dormant state, he wrote in a book and left, which could mean he still retained some autonomy despite being asleep.

Personality

Slime is generally unaware of human social cues, and can come across as naive as a result. It’s difficult to take what he says seriously because of his generally playful and fun nature. He believes everything anyone tells him, unless they are contradictory to each other's statements.

Slime also likes to refer to people in a specific way, saying their full name and often where they're from whenever he addresses someone. For example, Quackity is "Quackity from Las Nevadas."

Slime also has a nihilistic side, wherein he may sometimes mention the death of the universe, and the inevitability of death for everyone.

During Lessons from Quackity, over the course of the stream he began to get quieter and more timid, showing a more introspective side and speaking calmly about the horrors he'd seen. By the end of it, his outward personality had changed entirely, leaving him more human-like and afraid similar to Quackity.

Pre-Las Nevadas/Backstory

[Note that everything in and following this section is canon to the Dream SMP storyline]

Slimecicle's character is a piece of slime that has presumably spent most of his life in the ground, developing into a sentient being with human-like features over millions of years of growth. Even before he officially joined the server, it is heavily implied that he was observing people and events on the SMP without anyone knowing he even existed, until finally being discovered by Quackity on May 22, 2021. He seems to have extensive knowledge of most everyone on the server, and is not very secretive about this fact. When meeting Foolish for the first time, he refers to him as "Foolish Gamers from long ago," implying he was observing things on the server from even centuries ago.

Las Nevadas

Meeting Quackity

Slime was in the walls of the Las Nevadas Restaurant when Quackity mines out the wall Slime was in. Quackity proceeds to stab him, knocking Slime unconscious.

Slime wakes up in a hole (This is the cause for Slimecicle entering holes whenever he meets new people, Large groups of fans call these greeting holes) and Quackity begins to question him on what he had seen while in the restaurant. He reveals that he overheard a conversation about a purple guy ( Purpled ), two green guys ( Awesamdude and Dream ), a guy with a red shirt ( TommyInnit ), and a guy who was dead but isn't anymore ( Wilbur ). Quackity threatens to kill Slime for knowing too much, but quickly changes his mind after realizing that Slime is an accidental spy and could be useful to Las Nevadas. Slime appeared unfazed by this incident, eagerly accepting Quackity's offer of friendship. Quackity gives Slime both a job and a new name (Guy), and the two later call each other best friends.

Learning how to gamble

On May 27, 2021, Quackity introduced Slime to Las Nevadas, and tried to teach him concepts like gambling and self-defense. He explained that gambling was only good for them if other people did it. He then brought Slime to a finished machine in the wall of the casino, and tried to teach him about it. He gave Slime some diamonds to spend on it, but Slime eventually wasted them all, because he got addicted to gambling, as well as spinning around and pressing the button and the machine's lights and sounds. Quackity dragged him away and was trying to teach him how to eat when Connor arrived there. Quackity had to restrain Slime from attacking Connor in accordance with his self-defense lesson. Quackity then showed Connor the machine and gave him some money to use. Connor wasted the money and asked for more, prompting Quackity to give both Slime and Connor loans, but they wasted that money too. Connor expressed his desire to stop gambling at one point, but Quackity told Slime to keep Connor from leaving by threatening him with a diamond sword that Quackity provided for him.

After Connor left, Quackity gave Slime some diamonds he could use to "purchase" the diamond sword for himself, and used the opportunity to teach Slime how purchases work. Quackity then explained to Slime that he was in debt to the casino because of all the loans Quackity had provided. When Slime asked how one goes about paying off a loan, Quackity gave Slime a job he could do for him to erase his debt - spy on a purple guy ( Purpled ) who, according to Quackity, tried the machine and got angry enough to attack him, and storm out of the casino. Quackity then provided Slime with a bed in his home before leaving Slime to sleep.

Meeting Fundy

Slime is present when Fundy formally joins Las Nevadas, becoming acquainted with him and revealing he knows Fundy was part of L'Manberg , and that Wilbur is Fundy's father. After meeting Fundy, Slime entered a greeting hole, upon suggestion from slime, fundy dapped him up, causing slime to leave Slimecicle's body, this disgusted Fundy. A few minutes later, Slime asked Fundy to smell him, and describe it to him, Fundy did so, but accidentally inhaled some of Slime, (possibly) Making it possible for Slimecicle to hear through Fundys ears.

Spying on Purpled

On June 10, Slime was spying on Purpled until Purpled spotted him spying at a distance. Slime accused Purpled of spying on him while continue asking Purpled to dap him up. Purpled found out that Quackity sent Slime to spy on him, and told Slime about his UFO being blown up by Quackity, and that his legacy was gone while also knowing very little about the SMP's current state.

After Purpled blows up his home, he asks Slime how to get to Las Nevadas. The two then travel to Las Nevadas where they later met Quackity.

Tommy's prisoner

During Charlie's first encounter with Tommy. Tommy, who initially wanted to get his shovel Linda back from his failed gambling initiative, met Charlie after his attempt to gain information on Las Nevadas' current situation. He then lured Charlie, despite Foolish's attempts to get him to return. Tommy then held Charlie as a hostage, a pawn to regain Linda back from Foolish. After Foolish failed to break Charlie out of Tommy's prison, Foolish then gave Linda back in return for Foolish returning to Las Nevadas, however; Charlie was taught by Tommy to betray Foolish and Las Nevadas, going along with Tommy after his betrayal.

Meeting Ranboo

On June 18, 2021, Ranboo was working on the Snowchester outpost near Las Nevadas when he heard an explosion outside the walls. When he went to investigate, he found Charlie, which ended up in them having a lengthy discussion, during which Charlie wrongly assumed that Ranboo was also a slime, as Ranboo had mentioned not being human. Ranboo then attempted to teach Charlie how friendship worked, and the two of them showed off their hybrid abilities to each other.

Lessons from Quackity

Quackity streamed on November 27th, 2021. The stream began with Quackity speaking with Charlie, discussing how he was leaving for the prison a few weeks later. He told Charlie that he could die, making him upset. Quackity compared Charlie to a younger version of himself, claiming that the only reason he could trust Charlie was because he knew very little. He promised to rear Charlie into being his apprentice, and promising that when Quackity died, he would take his place. Charlie was taught many lessons, over time learning about Quackity and his trials and tribulations in the SMP. He learned about every betrayal, and over time began to sound more and more human. They grew close, sharing quiet moments and bonding over time until Quackity left for the prison. Quackity claimed that Charlie was his closest and longest friend. During this, we also learn that Charlie has his own room.

When Quackity returned, Charlie was quickly ushered to Las Nevadas in a panic. It was revealed that he had taken on Quackity's face scar. They came across Purpled, and after a short shouting match, Purpled lead them to a chest. Charlie checked inside the chest and found nothing, only to turn around and see Purpled with a lever. The ground fell beneath them and they fell into a cobblestone room with lava underneath, Purpled demanding for the revival book while Slime hid in a corner, telling Quackity that Purpled was "trying to help, but [he] didn't realize". After a small altercation, Purpled pearled to Charlie and pushed him into the lava. Quackity jumped in after him as Charlie died, thanking Quackity for showing him what it was like to be human before dying. Quackity called after Purpled in tears, making Charlie lose his supposed first canon life.

The stream cuts to weeks later, with a disheveled Quackity holding a book and entering the hole he had trapped Charlie in when they first met. He falls into a small room and approaches a green, dormant Slime. He tells Slime that he's been writing everything about their friendship in books and journals and leaving them in chests in hopes that Slime would read them and remember. Quackity says that Slime needs a name, and he decides to name him as he's more than just slime. He's named Charlie, and Quackity continues to speak to Charlie. He says that he hopes that he can hear Charlie talk and joke one day, and checks inside the chest and finds a strange, new book. At first, it's seemingly full of gibberish that Charlie wrote. Quackity is ecstatic to see that Charlie is alive, and runs off to decode it.

Foolish and Quackity decipher spaced out letters in the book, spelling out "LEGACY". Quackity returns to Charlie with his findings, only to find him gone. It's unknown where Charlie went. Dubiously canon, during the merch advertisement, Quackity left behind a box with a note reading;
