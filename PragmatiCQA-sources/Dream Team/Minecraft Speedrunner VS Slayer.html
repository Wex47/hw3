Video

Minecraft Speedrunner VS Slayer is a video released by Dream on January 14, 2020. It features BadBoyHalo as the hunter (or as the video title and description mention, the "slayer") and Dream as the speedrunner. It is the fifth Minecraft Manhunt video.

Results

Show/hide spoiler
BadBoyHalo won the challenge. However, Dream was pretty close to winning this challenge since he was fighting the Ender Dragon. At 28:19, when Dream was on the top of a pillar and the Ender Dragon was about to attack him, he tried escaping by falling and MLG water before landing, but placed it too late, ultimately killing him and making BadBoyHalo win this challenge.

Trivia

  • This is the first Minecraft Manhunt video to feature someone else other than GeorgeNotFound , and this Video marks the debut appearance of BadBoyHalo in the Minecraft Manhunt series.
  • Strangely, the End generated with all ten crystals already broken.
