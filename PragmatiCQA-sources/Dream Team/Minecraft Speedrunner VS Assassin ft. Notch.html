Video

Minecraft Speedrunner VS Assassin ft. Notch is a video released by Dream to his main channel on January 22, 2020. It is the sixth Minecraft Manhunt video ever, and in this video, the point of the challenge is that Notch, the creator of Minecraft, has to beat Minecraft with GeorgeNotFound protecting him from Dream, the "assassin", who is plotted to stop Notch by killing them to make them lose the challenge.
