Jschlatt , also known as jschlattLIVE or Schlatt , is an American YouTuber and Twitch streamer who makes variety content. He is most well-known for playing on SMPLive , where he often talked with Wilbur Soot , and for being a member of the now-defunct Lunch Club . He has a distinct over-the-top and crass persona. He first interacted with the Dream Team when he joined the Dream SMP without Dream 's permission and was subsequently banned. He later became friends with Dream through playing Among Us games and chatting on TommyInnit 's streams. He was unbanned from the Dream SMP on September 20, 2020 for the SWAG2020 vs POG2020 Election , and became a major part of the Manberg Rebellion arc .
