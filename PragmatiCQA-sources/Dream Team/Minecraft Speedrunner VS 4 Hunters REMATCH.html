Video

Minecraft Speedrunner VS 4 Hunters REMATCH is a video released by Dream to his main channel that premiered on October 20, 2020. It is the second Minecraft Manhunt video that features a fourth hunter, who is Antfrost . The other hunters are BadBoyHalo , GeorgeNotFound , and Sapnap . This video is basically a rematch of Minecraft Speedrunner VS 4 Hunters
