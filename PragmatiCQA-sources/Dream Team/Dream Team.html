The Dream Team is a group of three YouTubers who create content about Minecraft plugins they coded. Their current YouTube accounts were created in 2019 and are still active. Along with creating content on YouTube, they stream on Twitch and have numerous social media, being most active on Twitter. They also have an SMP server that is streamed on daily by their members.

History

The Dream Team were first featured together in a YouTube video in Dream's video " Last To Leave Circle in Minecraft Wins $1,000 - Challenge " released on September 14, 2019.

The first time all members of the Dream Team including Bad were featured in a Minecraft Manhunt video is in Dream's " Minecraft Speedrunner VS 3 Hunters " released on May 14, 2020.

Trivia

  • TommyInnit once asked Dream to state his number one best friend among the team. In response, Dream said that they are all tied. Dream then stated that it would be by the order he met them (Sapnap, BadBoyHalo, and GeorgeNotFound) if he had to rank them.
  • Dream agrees that Antfrost should also be a part of the Dream Team.
  • Sapnap and Dream have lived together since January 2021. At the time Sapnap moved in with Dream, they tried to get George to travel to Florida, but weren't able to due to travel restrictions with COVID-19.
  • MC Championship All-Stars marked the first time in a MCC for the Dream Team be on the same team. The Dream Team won the event.
  • During the Team Seas event in a voice chat with Sapnap and GeorgeNotFound, MrBeast mentioned the possibility of the Dream Team moving in and merging with his squad. Sapnap agreed and mentioned that he and George don't really care in where they live while both Dream and MrBeast prefer to stay where they live at. Sapnap also joked about MrBeast buying them houses and offering four million dollars each to get them to move to North Carolina.
