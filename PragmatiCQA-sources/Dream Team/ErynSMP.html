Physiology

Eryn appears as a human/demon hybrid with dark skin and dark brown hair. He has heterochromia, with one of their eyes being red and the other a grayish color. They wear an all-black outfit with a hood and a black headband. He has blood staining one of his arms from tearing out someone's heart.

End of content warning.

Personality

Eryn is a violent and chaotic individual. He has threatened to kill people and their pets, and destroyed buildings. Based on what he stated about Dream, he believes that violence and murder are fine. In contrast to this, he was against Dream escaping prison.

He misunderstands what's good for others, as he believes that getting Tommy thrown back into prison would give Tommy a purpose again. Despite this, he can still recognize when a situation is for better or worse. He stood alongside the guards against Dream and chased the escapees until they arrived at the Nether portal. However, Eryn allowed Niki to escape, presumably because she did not directly assist in the prison break-in and only spawned the withers after the escape party exited Pandora's Vault .

Eryn has little respect for "sacred" locations or objects, saying he has no issue with desecrating the Holy Land due to being atheist. He used this to justify stealing the Prime Bell for his Dream shrine.

Eryn seems to have trouble speaking about serious things, as he stumbled on his words while trying to explain where the prison escapees went. He was mistaken for lying when he just had trouble talking, though Sapnap came to his defense.

History

Empowerment Arc

On his first day of the SMP, he was greeted by Tommy , who provided Eryn with some resources, introduced Eryn to other members, and helped build a house. After speaking with Tommy, Eryn spent the rest of the stream and his first few days searching for resources. Eryn's first few days also involved a bit of violence and chaos. He mentioned killing Michael multiple times, jokingly threatened Fran, and bombed the Cat Maid Café .

Eryn tried to convince Sam to let Dream out of prison, saying that "he's learned his lesson" and that he "just killed some people or something."

On November 15, 2021, Eryn began to construct a shrine to Dream under Tommy's base. His goal was to make it look like Tommy built it so that Tommy would be thrown into prison again, which he believed would give Tommy a purpose in life again. It was later discovered on December 4 by Tommy after falling in a trap Dream made for him.

Legacy Arc

Jailbreak

On November 28, Technoblade, assisted by the Syndicate , broke Dream out from the prison. Sam alerted Eryn and various others of the situation and brought them to the area outside the prison. After Eryn mentioned how he'd never actually met Dream, Tommy gave a speech about everything Dream had done to him. Despite having doubts on whether Dream was a bad guy, Eryn stayed with Sapnap after Tommy ran from the scene. When the escape party exited the prison and fighting began, Eryn joined Sam's side in the battle . The battle led to a chase through the Nether. As the chase ensued, Eryn's ignorance of the situation caused him to block Sapnap from reaching Dream. This gave Dream, Technoblade, and Philza time to escape and the battle came to a conclusion. Eryn's stuttering and complicated explaination caused the others to severly mistrust him until Sapnap came to his defense.
