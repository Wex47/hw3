Floris , better known online as Fundy , is a popular Dutch YouTuber and Twitch streamer who is a friend of the Dream Team . He is known for his videos in which he creates mods in Minecraft to create new challenges. He has collaborated in a video with both Dream and GeorgeNotFound and participated in MC Championship in a team with Dream. Fundy is also a member of the Dream SMP .
