Antfrost is a Twitch streamer and a friend of the Dream Team . He first made a YouTube channel on September 5, 2020 after his first appearance on Dream's channel, and joined the Dream SMP on September 23, 2020, which he now streams usually on Twitch.

Dream and BadBoyHalo agree that Antfrost should be apart of the Dream Team .

History

Before Antfrost's appearance in Minecraft Speedrunner VS 4 Hunters , he was found on BadBoyHalo's server, MunchyMC , playing games such as KitPvP and Survival Games with Sapnap . He had also made his first appearance on Dream's channel in his video Last To Leave Circle in Minecraft Wins $1,000 - Challenge , winning the challenge alongside Skeppy .

Antfrost is the head administrator on MunchyMC, which was how he became associated with the Dream Team. The first time Antfrost was mentioned was in one of BadBoyHalo 's now-private livestreams.

Face reveal

Antfrost did a face reveal on Twitter on National Boyfriend Day (October 3, 2020) with a picture of him and RedVelvetCake . Antfrost now streams with a face camera on his Twitch.
