November 6, 2020

  • Tommy and the others leave Pogtopia after the announcement of the war.
  • Ponk gives Tommy some essential oils and follows Tommy to his base, signing a new deal where Ponk would become the doctor of Pogtopia.
  • Tubbo logs on and Tommy fills him in on the current situation. Tommy, Tubbo, Dream, Ponk, and Sam meet up at the Manberg Embassy . Tommy questions why Dream had sided with Schlatt, and Dream replies that he only looked out for himself and was never on Pogtopia's side.
  • Tommy then asks where his original Cat disc is, and Bad reveals that he has given it back to Skeppy . Tubbo claims that he has the real Cat disc, but Dream says that it is a fake. Tommy gets ready to burn the fake Mellohi and Cat discs, but Dream collects them before Tommy could.
  • Wilbur , Fundy , and Quackity stay back in Pogtopia and discuss who their allies and enemies are in the war. As the trio travel back to Manberg, they theorize on who the traitor might be and begin to suspect Tommy, as he was missing from the group.
  • All of the Pogtopians and their new allies (Fundy, Awesamdude, BadBoyHalo, and Ponk) meet up near the Manberg flagpole to discuss their plans to defeat Schlatt on the 16th of November. Dream was present during this meeting. Wilbur partially sides with Dream's ideas of causing chaos, but states that he will still fight for the Pogtopians.
  • George joins the server and Quackity yells at him for being absent during election night and the festival, indirectly causing Schlatt to become president.
  • Wilbur finds a natural tree in Manberg that has not been burned or destroyed throughout the timeline of the server. It was then named L'Mantree .
    • Everyone makes an agreement to leave the tree untouched during the war. Dream suggests placing an obsidian barrier around the tree to protect it from any explosives.
  • Sam gives Tommy some potions. George tries to take a few of the potions Sam is giving to Tommy and accidentally kills Tubbo.
  • Tommy plans with the others to make a cobblestone railway from Manberg to Pogtopia called Operation: Pogback . The railway begins behind one of Eret's watch towers .
  • Quackity tells Tommy that he thinks Tommy is the traitor. Tommy says he is not the traitor since he would have to betray both Wilbur and Tubbo, which he does not want to do.
    • Tommy also mentions he might have to go against Sapnap in another war soon since Sapnap stole his horse, Juorse . Sam said that the only way to stop the war is to kill the one who started the violence.
    • Sam also gifts Tommy with a Creeper mob head, which can be used to summon him in Tommy's time of need.
  • Punz continues to search for a trident but remains unlucky.

The goal of their faction is to maintain eternal conflict between the two main factions so that they can control everything outside of their borders.
  • Sam meets up with Ponk at the Community House and Ponk tries to get Sam to sign an "essential oils" contract (similar to the one that Tommy signed) in exchange for a soul speed book.
    • Through various code words and a translated Korean phrase in the contract, it is revealed that Ponk may still be loyal to Schlatt.
    • Sam realizes this and refuses to sign, accusing Ponk of being the traitor. Ponk burns the soul speed book that he intended to trade with Sam.
  • Ponk steals TNT from Eret's secret chest under his castle. Sam, Eret, and soon Fundy start attacking him, and he retreats to Lemon City . Ponk returns the stolen TNT to Eret before leaving. Sam eventually stops chasing Ponk, goes back to his base, and continues to trade with Piglins in the Nether.
  • Fundy meets up with Ponk at the Community House and asks what had happened. Ponk shows Fundy the contract, and Fundy alters the conditions of the contract to scam Ponk back for arrows of strength.
  • Later, Fundy steals some crossbows from Lemon City. Ponk, in response, puts a pumpkin with curse of binding on Fundy, which he counters with a texture pack that he installs to clear his vision.
  • Ponk builds Thiccatron higher and adds some oak and spruce leaves on the sides of the encircling curved staircase.
  • Ponk debunks accusations that he is the traitor in Pogtopia, stating that he is not enough of a central character in the conflict. He also explains that he had helped Quackity escape by providing Wilbur with the invisibility arrows.
  • One of Eret's cats gets killed in an accident.
  • Fundy attempts to kill Eret with crossbows twice but inflicts little damage. The two then make their way to Eret's Castle where Fundy shows Eret all the flamingos in the base.
  • Fundy tries to scam Eret and take ownership of all his property and possessions using a verbal contract, but Eret counters Fundy's contract with his own, stealing a book from Fundy's base. They both agree to invalidate each other's contracts with a final contract signed by the pair.
  • Eret continues to work on the museum. He slightly moves the pillars, finishes the walls of the museum and starts working on the interior. He also gets white concrete to recreate the original design of the Camarvan .
    • He completes the Camarvan as the first exhibit of the museum.
  • Sam tries to take dolphins back to his soul sand tunnel. He keeps the one by the soul sand tunnel after the first one had died. Sam takes another dolphin to the tunnel and fills in the tunnel with water. After another failed attempt with the dolphin dying, he found another two dolphins and named them Finn and Dolly.
