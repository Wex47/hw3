Video

Minecraft, But Mobs Are Randomly Hostile... is a video uploaded by Sapnap on his channel on April 29, 2020. In the video, BadBoyHalo and Sapnap try to beat Minecraft, but every mob in the game has a chance of being hostile, or passive.

Description

“ Minecraft, But Mobs Are Randomly Hostile. This was super surprising, we never knew which mobs were going to attack us!

This is a new Minecraft 1.15 Challenge coded by Dream. I try to beat the game while every mob that spawns, regardless of what it usually is, has a random chance of being hostile. This means that a chicken could attack me while a creeper would not and vise versa. This challenge was extremely funny but also hard. I really enjoy doing challenges like this.
