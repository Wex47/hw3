Noah Brown , better known online as Foolish Gamers or FoolishG , is a Minecraft speedrunner YouTuber, and a friend of the Dream Team . Foolish met Dream after Dream took notice of Foolish's speedrunning skills. He is also a member of the Dream SMP .

Dream Team history

On November 28, 2020, Dream unexpectedly joined the Foolish Gamers Discord server and got in touch with Noah. This later led to Noah being sent a Christmas package on December 13, 2020 containing custom merch Noah would frequently wear on speedrunning streams for 'good luck'.

On January 16, 2021, he was invited to the Dream SMP in the middle of a casual speedrun stream. Before entering the SMP, he claimed that he was loading up a new seed for another run.
