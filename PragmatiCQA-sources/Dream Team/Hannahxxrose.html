Hannah , better known online as Hannahxxrose , is a Twitch streamer, TikToker, and YouTuber known for playing Hypixel Bedwars and Ultra Hardcore. She is very involved in the Hypixel Bedwars community, being close friends with Purpled , BoomerNA , Wallibear, Nihachu , CaptainPuffy , and Awesamdude . She joined Dream SMP on January 18, 2021.
