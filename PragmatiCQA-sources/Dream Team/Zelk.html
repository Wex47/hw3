Jacob "Jake" , better known online as Zelk , ZelkTheElk , and Zelkam , is a Minecraft Youtuber who produces challenge and trolling content. Zelk is associated with Skeppy and is a manager of Invadedlands. Due to Skeppy's association with JustVurb , Vurb was allowed to visit Dream SMP through his login.
