Alyssa , also known as ItsAlyssa , is a former member of the Dream SMP and the sixth of the eight original members of the SMP. She joined on April 28, 2020 and was a citizen of the Dream SMP .

Physiology

Alyssa is a human with light skin, blue eyes, eyelashes, and blonde hair. She wears a black sweater, black shorts, black shoes, and sometimes wears a black mask.

Personality

Alyssa is a kind girl who usually stays out of conflict other than occasionally going on killing sprees with her friends. Alyssa is most often seen with Ponk , Awesamdude , Dream , and Sapnap . However, she often keeps to herself in terms of interacting with newer members, and rarely appears on the server.

History

Early Dream SMP Arc

Pre-Disc Saga

Alyssa was one of the eight original SMP members along with George , Sapnap , Dream , Callahan , Awesamdude , Ponk and BadBoyHalo . Along with the other original seven members of the server, she built the Community House .

Sapnap and Alyssa built houses near the lake, hers being made out of spruce and featuring a fish tank. It houses her horse as well.

Early Dream SMP

Alyssa accused Tubbo of stealing her diamonds, forcing him to prove his innocence by getting a diamond achievement. She has had bad relationships with allies of him ever since.

After Sapnap burnt down Ponk's second lemon tree , Alyssa helped Ponk retaliate by burning down Sapnap's house . As a result, both of them got into many skirmishes with the opposing side which contained Sapnap, Tommy and Tubbo. Eventually, Ponk and Alyssa were beaten and she was repeatedly killed by Tommy due to it, however it made a lasting effect as when Dream joined to protect her and Ponk it would start a chain of events which would later be dubbed 'The Disc Saga'.

L'Manberg

When L'Manberg was formed by some members of the server, Dream attempted to convince them to stop by using Alyssa as bait, but it failed. Soon afterwards, she burnt down most of the trees and wildlife by L'Manberg, but was spotted running away and was trapped before being killed over and over.

Manberg Rebellion Arc

After a long period of inactivity, Alyssa and Awesamdude returned to the server on the same day. Upon her return, Alyssa built a wooden horse barn with a stone roof near the ocean in walking distance from Antfrost's animal sanctuary . Niki also confirmed at the time that Alyssa still played on the server while everyone else was offline.

Present

Although Awesamdude went on to be extremely active on the server, Alyssa continued her hiatus from the server after her brief return. She later confirmed that she is not on the server anymore in a conversation with Punz on Hypixel where she stated that she did not have the IP after it got changed. Dream himself also confirmed that she is not on the SMP anymore during an interview with Anthony Padilla.

Ponk has stated that Alyssa may make an appearance in the server's lore by providing voice lines for Ponk's lore videos despite her not having the IP.
