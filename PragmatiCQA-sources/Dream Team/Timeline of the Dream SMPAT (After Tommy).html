The After Tommy Era of the Dream SMP saw a large growth of members, nearly doubling the amount of the Before Tommy Era . As a result, the SMP had many more notable events and builds than before. During this era, The Disc Saga and The Socializing Club Conflict occurred.

Details

July 4, 2020

  • TommyInnit joins the server. He is temporarily banished by Dream after repeatedly killing George .
    • Tommy later walks around the server and claims every building as his own. He also partially blows up the Courthouse .
    • Tommy builds his base in a hill near Ponk's second lemon tree.
  • Ponk and Tommy start arguing about land and then they agree to build a fence to separate each other. They both make chicken pens near their bases.
    • Ponk traps Tommy's house and Sapnap takes credit for it but Tommy avoids it. He falls for this trap in a later stream.

July 7, 2020

  • Tubbo , Punz , and Fundy are added to the server.
  • Punz starts to build his house near the wooden path.
  • Tubbo also starts to build his house near the Prime Path.
    • He also acquires his pet pufferfish, Phukkit.
  • Tommy decorates the inside of his base and kills two of Ponk's pet chickens, including Kanye.
  • Fundy makes his secret base in a cave system.
  • Dream makes a hidden base in a mountain down the path from Tommy's house.
  • Tommy makes a watch tower of his own within Ponk's land.

July 8, 2020

  • Tubbo works on his house.
  • Tommy finds a zombie spawner in the hill where his base is located.
    • He finds his disc Cat in a chest inside the spawner room.
    • He later also builds the bench in front of his house that he and Tubbo will use many times in the future.
  • Tommy and Fundy drink invisibility potions and prank Tubbo by changing blocks in his house and killing his pet chicken.
    • Tubbo stops them before they can do any more damage.

July 9, 2020

  • Purpled logs onto the server for the first time after being whitelisted the previous day and begins building his distinctive UFO base .
  • Sapnap "accidentally" burns down most of Ponk's second lemon tree.
    • Ponk and Alyssa burn down Sapnap's house in retaliation which leads to Sapnap and Tommy killing Ponk and Alyssa repeatedly.
    • Dream tries to stop the conflict but gets killed instead. While Tommy and Sapnap brag to Ponk and Alyssa about what they'd managed to do, Dream secretly steals Tommy's prized music discs, "Mellohi" and "Cat".
  • Sapnap, Tommy, and Tubbo fight Dream many times and are miraculously able to obtain the discs back. Tommy then covers his screen and hides his discs, thinking that they were safe. This was the start of the very first war of the server, The Disc Saga .

July 10, 2020

  • The next day, Dream, still angry that he was defeated, digs out all of the land around Tommy's base and is able to steal the discs back.
  • Tommy and Tubbo begin to fight Dream after finding out that he was in possession of the discs. After Tommy and Tubbo are killed many times, trade negotiations are made. Tommy is successfully able to scam Dream and retrieve his discs. However, it was revealed that they were counterfeit discs, meaning that Dream still had the real ones.

July 11, 2020

  • Purpled works on his base.
  • The Socializing Club Conflict takes place.
    • Fundy logs on to try and kill Dream. However, Dream is not online, so he kills Tubbo instead and steals his diamond pickaxe.
    • He then tries to kill Purpled. However, Purpled is able to successfully trap and kill him instead.
    • The Socializing Club is built by Fundy in order to peacefully solve the conflict.
  • After Tubbo finishes up his situation with the Socializing Club, he and Tommy plan to use a glitch which would allow them to essentially X-ray underground so that they can find Dream's secret base .
    • Once they locate the base, the two search inside Dream's chests, but the discs are nowhere to be found. Tommy gives up and decides to make trade negotiations with Dream, giving Dream his Netherite chestplate in exchange for the discs.
    • Tommy begins finding resources to make an Ender chest, and Dream begins to chase him. Tommy manages to successfully place the discs into an Ender chest, marking the ending of the first disc conflict.

???

  • Over the course of several streams, Tubbo surrounds his house with several different types of items and mob farms.
  • Ponk relocates to a secret base with a Nether portal and a tree farm below the remains of his second lemon tree. He also uses a snow golem to make a snow farm in the middle of the ocean past Fundy's base for an upcoming giant build called " Project Snowzone ".
    • He never completes this build and it is left abandoned. Later on, Tommy and Tubbo stumble across the unfinished build while exploring and call it the "Staircase to Heaven".
  • Tommy makes a war room and a music room for the jukebox in his base.
  • Sapnap claims a zombie spawner in a cave behind Tommy's house and turns it into the server's first official XP farm.
    • In a later stream, Ponk discovers and destroys this spawner. Sapnap and Tubbo find another zombie spawner behind Purpled's UFO, which they use to make another temporary XP farm. This farm is abandoned later in favor of the spider spawner near Punz's house.
  • Punz creates his bee farm on the top floor of his house.
  • Tubbo and Sapnap dig out and renovate a large room below Tubbo's house for a secret villager farm.
    • Tubbo also builds brick walls near his house that run along the main wooden path.
    • He also begins collecting villagers and iron golems.

July 12, 2020

  • Purpled rigs the entrance of the Socializing Club with a fall trap. Fundy, Tommy, Dream, and even Purpled himself all later succumb to this trap.
  • Tommy burns down Tubbo's house after he accidentally kills Spins, a bee that Tubbo had gotten from Punz.
    • Dream and Sapnap help repair the damage in a later stream and gift Tubbo some valuable items in a chest.
    • Punz gives Tubbo a new bee, which he names Spunz.
  • Wilbur Soot joins the server and is toured by Tubbo and Fundy.

July 14, 2020

  • Tommy, Purpled, Fundy and Dream have a feud over items stolen due to Purpled's trap at the Socializing Club.
  • Tommy and Fundy later play a game of "donation roulette" with Dream in Tommy's base.

July 15, 2020

  • Purpled logs onto the server.
    • He goes mining and crafts diamond armor.
    • He also later kills Sapnap for punching his skeleton horse.
    • He makes a cow farm in order to get leather to craft books to enchant his new set of diamond armor.
    • He decides to build Area 51 underground underneath his UFO.
  • Tommy logs onto the server.
    • He works on his list of enemies and concludes that he needs to get revenge on Purpled for killing him.
    • He goes to speak to Fundy, and they head to Fundy's base.
    • Tubbo joins their VC, and the three of them discuss Fundy being a fox. Tommy insists that Fundy is a furry, while Tubbo and Fundy agree that Fundy is just a fox.
    • They also trade music discs. Fundy gives Tubbo Cat, and they negotiate for several minutes as Tubbo tries to convince Fundy to give him Chirp.
    • Meanwhile, Tommy starts to farm music discs. He gets 13, Mall and Far.
  • Purpled sets off to kill Tommy due to a 1000 bit donation.
    • Tommy invites Purpled back to his base to listen to his new discs. They listen to 13.
    • Purpled's assassination attempt fails and he dies from fall damage while trying to escape Tommy. Tommy then steals his items, including the diamond armor Purpled crafted earlier on, and demands payment of 25 diamonds to give it back.
    • Purpled tramples some of Tommy's crops and they negotiate some more.
    • They end up compromising at 5 diamonds and a jukebox. Purpled gives Tommy the jukebox, Tommy gives Purpled the armor, and Purpled runs away. Tommy chases him, but Purpled kills him.
    • They hold a court trial, overseen by Tubbo and Fundy.
      • Purpled is locked in the jail cell.
      • Tommy and Purpled both explain their sides of the story.
      • Tommy declares himself the judge and demands diamonds from Purpled. He also bans Tubbo from the courthouse.
      • While Tommy and Tubbo are arguing about this, Purpled mines the back wall of the jail cell and escapes.

July 16, 2020

  • Wilbur and Jschlatt join the server for a cameo appearance to surprise Tommy.
    • Wilbur builds his hanging wooden ball home . Fundy fills it with water in a later stream.
    • Jschlatt builds the Cuck Shed near Tommy's house before getting banned by Dream because he was not meant to be whitelisted.
  • George kills some of Punz's bees in his house and then accidentally kills Ponk's bee, Kanye.

July 17, 2020

  • Ponk and Purpled form an alliance after Purpled rescues Ponk from the Nether.
  • Tommy and Sapnap go around taking hostages.

July 18, 2020

  • Skeppy joins the server to grief Tommy's house and then logs off again.

July 19, 2020

  • Eret joins the server.
  • Tommy confronts Skeppy for griefing his house, which leads to Skeppy burning one of Tommy's music discs. Skeppy donates £1,000 to Tommy's stream as a compromise.

July 20, 2020

  • Eret builds their castle .
  • Withers are summoned on the server for the first time by Tubbo, Fundy, Tommy, and Eret to collect Nether Stars.
  • Tommy builds a sewer system under the main path that connects to other homes and bases.
  • Fundy and Eret start a "prank war" that lasts for several streams. The pranks mostly center around harmlessly griefing each others' bases, causing minor inconveniences.
    • One of the most notable pranks involves Eret exposing Fundy's secret cave base with a giant obsidian sign ('SECRET BASE HERE') and an arrow pointing downwards.
  • Punz continues to expand his territory and builds tall blackstone walls around his land. His backyard includes a carved pumpkin patch and a Speed beacon.
  • Eret builds a hollow chunk-sized tower near Fundy's cave base, which Fundy makes his new home.
  • The Spider Spawner/XP Farm is built below Punz's backyard. This is the location of the community enchanting table and still remains the main source for XP collection on the server.
  • Purpled works on his Area 51 themed underground base right below his main house.
  • Dream kills all of Tubbo's villagers in the villager farm below his house to prevent OP trades. He also halts the progress on Tubbo's iron golem farm for the same reasons.
  • Tubbo relocates to a jungle base that is only accessible via the ceiling of the Nether, using a boat on a packed ice highway. He begins building more item farms and makes a villager transportation system.
    • Fundy takes part ownership of Tubbo's jungle base and creates a redstone note block circuit underneath the base that plays the "Able Sisters" theme from Animal Crossing, one of Tommy's signature stream songs.
  • Eret converts the entire floor of the Community House to crafting tables.
  • Tommy takes down his old watch tower and builds his Power Tower that connects to the underground sewer system after Ponk moves out of his area. Ponk leaves a small monument for the second lemon tree in place of the remains.
  • Ponk and Punz both build even taller towers that reach the build limit of the server. Ponk's tower resembles the fictional Barad-dûr (Sauron's tower from Lord of the Rings). Punz's tower has several floors and is located in his backyard with a water fountain outside of the entrance.
