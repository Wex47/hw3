Video

Minecraft Speedrunner vs 4 Hunters GRAND FINALE is a video released by Dream on his main channel on February 5, 2021 and is the fifth Minecraft Manhunt video to feature a fourth hunter, Antfrost . The other hunters are GeorgeNotFound , Sapnap , and BadBoyHalo .

Results

Show/hide spoiler
At 39:00, Dream killed the ender dragon with a crossbow, hence making him winner of the challenge.

On his Twitter, Dream proposed that if he won, the next video will be titled " Minecraft Speedrunner VS 5 Hunters ", and if he lost, it would've been titled "Minecraft Speedrunner VS 4 Hunters GRAND FINALE REMATCH" . Given that he won in this video, the next video will be him against 5 hunters, with the 5 hunter now being revealed to be awesamdude.
