Video

Minecraft Speedrunner VS 4 Hunters FINALE REMATCH is a video released by Dream to his main channel on December 26th, 2020 and it is the fourth Minecraft Manhunt video that features a fourth hunter, who is Antfrost . The other hunters are BadBoyHalo , GeorgeNotFound , and Sapnap . It is basically a rematch of Minecraft Speedrunner VS 4 Hunters FINALE .

Trivia

  • This video was delayed for a couple hours due to technical problems, according to Dream's tweet.
    • During a Scuffed Podcast on Trainwreckstv's stream, he revealed that him, George, and Sapnap were all watching a Belle Delphine video the same day the video was originally going to be posted. This started a meme on Twitter that when he would announce a video was delayed, people would jokingly respond asking if he was watching Belle Delphine again.
