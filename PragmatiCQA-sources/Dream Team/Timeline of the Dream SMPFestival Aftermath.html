The Festival Aftermath is an era of changing loyalties and major disturbance in the Jschlatt Administration . New structures and schemes were formed, and events such as the Dreamon Hunter Invasion , Ponk's Experiment , and the Second Pet War occurred in lieu of the ongoing Manberg Rebellion .

Details

October 16, 2020

  • While the events in Pogtopia occurred, the remaining Manbergians, Quackity , Schlatt , Ponk , and Fundy continue to discuss the future of Manberg . Schlatt takes down Fundy's festival "brollys" and Quackity makes Tubbo Traitor's grave next to Flatty Patty's grave.
    • Quackity and Schlatt continue to disagree over what to do with the original White House and they plan to make a settlement at a later time. Fundy shows Schlatt the unused Second White House that he had built before the election and gives him the option to live there, which Schlatt considers.
  • On his own, Quackity states that Schlatt does not respect his opinions and says that if the original White House is gone when he logs on next, there will be an "issue", hinting towards a possible betrayal. He also finds out about the death of Henry and stops by Tommy's holiday home to reminisce.
  • As the chaos eased for the day, Fundy traveled along the Underground Prime Path to Pogtopia. As he slowly explored the empty ravine, he realized that he'd been wrong to side so blindly with Schlatt. He now found himself unsure of his allegiances to either side of the conflict.
    • After an unsuccessful attempt to contact Tubbo , he began to write a new entry in his diary . This entry described his experiences at the festival, his outrage at the discovery of Tubbo's true intentions, and his disappointment with Schlatt's treatment of the living creatures who inhabited the server. He then walked back to Manberg, and finally made contact with Tubbo.
  • Meanwhile, Antfrost , BadBoyHalo , and Awesamdude meet and make a mutual agreement, which was that they do not agree with the ideals of neither Pogtopia nor Manberg. They decide to create their own nation and faction that promotes "freedom and prosperity" called the Badlands .
    • The trio form a council between themselves and plan to recruit other neutral SMP members for their faction such as Skeppy , George , Sapnap , Punz , and Eret . They also consider claiming land around the areas of Pogtopia and Manberg and discuss how to establish their borders, their logic being that with Eret's castle on the south side of the Greater Dream SMP, and Skeppy's mansion to the north, they'd have control of the perimeter.
    • HBomb logs on after the chaos of the festival has mostly subsided. Bad and Antfrost give HBomb a brief recap of what happened at the festival and explain the goals of their new faction to him. HBomb, who doesn't trust anyone on the server, chooses to remain neutral for the time being.

October 17, 2020

  • The next day, Tommy joined with a vendetta. He traveled to Manberg with the intention of killing Quackity and Schlatt. Tommy listened to the men as they planned out what they'd do next. Schlatt wanted to stick with his plan of deconstructing The White House, which Quackity still disagreed with. The men began to argue as Tommy listened from a distance.
    • Schlatt screamed at Quackity, commanding him to destroy the White House, which he reluctantly started to do. Tommy aimed at Schlatt with the intention to kill him, but as he listened to them argue, he decided not to shoot. As Schlatt continued to harass his Vice President, Quackity quickly became fed up with how he was being treated and decided to resign. Quackity then killed Schlatt and retreated from Manberg.
  • Ponk starts setting up scaffolding near his fifth lemon tree in preparation for a hotel build. He briefly helps Schlatt dismantle the White House, before logging off.
  • Schlatt completely takes down the White House, leaving a small emerald monument to "Crying About It" in its place.
  • Tommy mounted his horse and chased Quackity into the woods. When Tommy caught up to Quackity, the two of them talked about what Tommy had just witnessed. Quackity told him that he wanted to overthrow Schlatt. Tommy then asked if Quackity wanted to join Pogtopia, which Quackity did.
    • The two slowly began to bond, almost committing arson together. They ultimately decided to travel to Pogtopia. Upon reaching Pogtopia, Tommy gave Quackity a tour of the depths and Quackity was very impressed.
    • Wilbur soon joined and made his way to Pogtopia from Manberg. By then, Tommy and Quackity were walking along the underground prime path towards Manberg. Wilbur searched for Tommy in Pogtopia, but he was already gone. He soon followed them through the tunnels and joined them in Manberg.
  • Tommy and Quackity filled Wilbur in on their new alliance, then the three of them began to vandalize the Manberg flag by building a large cobblestone phallus in front of it. They also visited the remains of The White House and reminisced about the early days of the election.
  • Wilbur admitted he'd made mistakes. He then walked the other two to the hill where he hid the button. Wilbur brought the two of them to a room inside the hill, a room lined with TNT, and the lyrics of the L'Manberg National Anthem written on signs.
  • Wilbur tried to press the button but was restrained with cobblestone by Tommy and Quackity. They conversed for a long time, trying to convince Wilbur not to destroy it all. Wilbur then escaped the cobblestone, and stood staring at Tommy, who blocked Wilbur from pressing the button. Calmly, Wilbur asked him to move, which Tommy did. After a long moment of silence, Wilbur broke the button.
    • Wilbur asked Tommy and Quackity to wait for him outside. As they left, Wilbur placed the button back on the wall, just in case. He then joined the other two outside.
    • The trio then traveled back to Pogtopia and planned out how they'd take down Schlatt.
    • Together, they formed three different plan options, one being a meeting with Schlatt, the other a death trap, and the final one involving the usage of Wilbur's explosives, though this would be the last resort.
    • The trio soon went their separate ways, and for the first time in a long time, things were seeming good for the Pogtopians.

October 18-19, 2020

  • Techno travels to the Nether to gather resources, specifically gold and blaze rods. He then visits Manberg, placing a "Subscribe to Technoblade" sign on the electoral podium before returning to his base.
  • Ponk removes the cobblestone phallus created by the Pogtopians, then begins construction on the Manberg Hotel , located across from the The Pufferfish Alleyway and near his fifth lemon tree.
  • BadBoyHalo shows Fundy the newly expanded old Dreamon Hunter base, unaware that it will never be used by the hunters. Fundy, hesitant to tell Bad the truth, instead gives him the task of simulating the sky, sun and moon above the base for future rituals.
  • Ponk shows Fundy the new hotel under construction in Manberg.
    • The two of them discuss the events of the festival, Schlatt's declining health and questionable actions, as well as the disappearance of Quackity.
    • Ponk observes that the medicine he has prescribed to Schlatt has not cured his illnesses and also notes that George was absent from the festival, theorizing that he may be a witch putting a curse on Schlatt.
  • Fundy visits Tubbo in the XP farm and informs him of Ponk's theories about Schlatt's possible possession. They begin to plan their next job together as the Dreamon Hunters to perform an exorcism on Schlatt.
  • The two hunters revisit Tubbo's abandoned jungle base and then travel through the oceans in search of tridents. Though they did eventually find a trident, Fundy threw it not knowing it would despawn, which it did. Tubbo loses the motivation to find one again and leaves Fundy to search for one himself. Fundy later gives up on the search after a couple of hours at sea.
  • Tommy and Tubbo build an underground bathhouse near Ponk's pet cemetery. As Tommy had lost all of his important items, he tried to sell "Tubbo Bath Water" in glass bottles in exchange for valuables.
    • After an unsuccessful attempt with BadBoyHalo, Tommy later sells the product to Awesamdude, who buys a sample so that he can become British. Tommy tries to take ownership of Sam's mountain base but accidentally drinks a sample of the bathwater, mistaking it for one of Sam's potions.
  • Tubbo and Awesamdude travel to a desert and collect copious amounts of sand with the goal of completely draining an ocean monument beyond Tubbo's jungle base. Sam uses the super smelter in his mountain base to create stacks of glass to mark the perimeter of their project.

October 20-23, 2020

  • Over the next couple of days, Tubbo and Awesamdude continue working on their ocean monument project. They clear the monument, surround the temple with a large circular glass wall and completely mine out the area inside. The pair also begin to fill the area with sand. BadBoyHalo, Antfrost, Punz, and Ponk assist them with building and providing supplies.
    • The monument base will be the location of a new XP farm . After several name changes, Tubbo decides to call the new base: "Casa de Hole".
  • Eret begins searching for a trident and restates that he is still a supporter of Pogtopia. He reveals that his initial plan with Niki was to blow up the Second White House with the TNT that they had. He finds Dream's prized crossbow that Tommy lost during the Manberg Massacre in a chest on the election podium. He puts the crossbow in his Ender Chest for safekeeping. Eret also accidentally discovers one of the many Strongholds on the server while traveling at sea.
  • Eret takes down the outline of Tubbo's unfinished bee house near Tubbo's old house and levels the ground to begin construction on a giant museum to take its place. He considers adding a theatre and physical recreations of past events of the server's history.
  • Ponk makes a secret laboratory to "experiment" on cats inside the hollow center of Eret's stone pyramid.
  • BadBoyHalo builds a concrete statue of Tommy above his old base as a gift for him reaching 2 million subscribers on YouTube. He rescues a cat from Ponk's laboratory but Ponk later finds and drowns it while the statue is under construction.
  • Tommy re-claims his old base, the embassy, from Manberg and begins cleaning the area around and within his base in general, hoping to restore it to its former glory.
  • HBomb prepares to move out of his room in Eret's castle. He builds a small wooden cabin made of Nether wood on a snowy hill near his Leap of Faith parkour course.
  • Antfrost resumes construction on his animal sanctuary, extending the wooden path and making an aquarium tunnel for his many fish. Ponk joins to help Ant build a public toilet building that connects to Tommy's sewer system.
  • Fundy and HBomb, in the guise of a catgirl maid, use Tubbo's Nether ice highway to transport Fundy's new pet cats back to his tower base in the Greater Dream SMP.
  • Eret builds a two-story cat cafe called " Catfe " on Karl's scenic route to Manberg. He starts collecting tamed cats to put in the café. Awesamdude provides Eret with some building materials and designs the interior space.
    • Whilst Eret builds his Catfe, Ponk arrives and harasses Eret while he builds the exterior of the Catfe. Ponk builds a tower of scaffolding near the Catfe, leading up to the Sky Courthouse . After killing Ponk multiple times, Ponk came back and arrived, floating meters off the ground. Ponk unleashed his inner Dreamon, saying that he sold his soul for science. Eret managed to kill him again, and thus the Catfe was finished.
  • Eret places a beacon in the center of the Community House and colors the beacons in his castle to match his rainbow theme.
  • Ponk sets up a bakery as a front for his cat laboratory in Eret's pyramid.
  • Awesamdude obtains a trident by chance, while searching for pandas with Antfrost. He enchants his trident with enchantments such as Channeling, Impaling and Loyalty. Sam begins to search for zombie villagers and continues the sand grid for his and Tubbo's ocean monument project.

October 24-26, 2020

  • Over the next few streams, Tubbo and Sam resume work on their ocean project, filling up the sand grid and draining the water with sponges. Punz begins to contribute more to the project and is promoted to third-in-command of the building crew by Sam. HBomb and Ponk also join to assist at some point, while Tubbo is offline. Sam uses his new trident to charge Creepers and collect various mob heads.
  • Karl, George, and Wilbur join the server after the events of MCC11 and visit Karl's nightclub with Awesamdude. After Wilbur leaves, Karl and George celebrate their victory with Sam by drinking potions and listening to Karl's music discs in Karl's house, followed by eating cake at the Catfe. Karl officially becomes Sam's third roommate.
  • Ponk builds Thiccatron , a cylindrical wooden building filled with scaffolding that was intended to be the trunk base of yet another lemon tree. This building was located near awesamdude's mountain base.
  • Ponk's Experiment takes place:
    • Ponk renames his dog "b0nk" to " Fran ", the name of awesamdude's pet dog and then leaves him in the mountain base, hiding the real Fran.
    • When Sam logs on, he witnesses Ponk kidnap fake Fran as a prank and lead the dog into creepers, killing him. Sam, believing that Ponk has killed the real Fran, seeks revenge against Ponk.
    • He blows up Thiccatron then travels to Lemon City , hiding Ponk's chicken "Bebbles the 2nd" and covering the top of Ponk's fourth lemon tree with TNT, threatening to blow it up.
  • HBomb arrives at Lemon City to meet Sam and Ponk after hearing about the conflict.
    • Ponk taunts HBomb repeatedly, which only leads to HBomb igniting the TNT and destroying the top half of the tree.
    • Ponk frames HBomb for starting a war and destroying his base, claiming that the entire ordeal has been an elaborate revenge plot on HBomb for making the difficult Leap of Faith parkour course.
    • Ponk then reveals to Sam that the dog that died was actually the fake "Fran" and apologizes to him for the prank, which Sam forgives him for.
  • HBomb travels to the Nether and collects two more copies of the disc Pigstep, planning to use them as prizes for completing his various mini games
  • Fundy starts building a fox cafe, an exact replica of the Catfe, across the scenic path from the original cafe. He breeds his foxes and then plans to steal resources and blocks from the Catfe. He never completes the replica.
  • Sam adds another crop farm to his mountain base and continues the draining of the ocean monument base until it is 50% complete
  • Antfrost plays some harmless Halloween-style pranks around the server, which include setting up a spawn trap for George using a fake-out lava pit, concealing Techno's cow farm, leaving a cow named "Ghost Henry" in Tommy's old base, and filling Awesamdude's mountain base with string and cobwebs.

October 27-31

  • Antfrost continues his Halloween pranks around the server, and with Sam's help, decorates several houses and other buildings with Jack o'lanterns.
  • Tommy finds Ghost Henry in his base and kills him. He then traps the entrances of his enemies' houses (Punz and Sapnap) with redstone fall traps.
  • Ponk builds Thiccatron v2.0, in place of the old Thiccatron, this time resembling a giant inverted glass fishbowl within a spruce log base.
  • Fundy uses the spider XP farm to enchant his diamond tools, and goes mining.
  • Karl, Niki, and Quackity build a haunted mansion with mainly polished blackstone bricks and crimson wood planks near Lemon City. Sam, HBomb and Eret contribute to the build by providing supplies.
    • George logs on and Karl shows him around the mansion. George and Quackity impulsively decide to hunt down Karl, killing him repeatedly, causing Purpled to log on to the server to defend him. Later, Quackity is killed while AFK and all of his items despawn, including his set of netherite armor and HBomb's enchanted netherite helmet.
  • Tommy makes a small fighting pit in a lake within the Holy Land, the only place where fighting will be allowed in the borders. With Sam and Quackity's help, he also digs a 2x2 wide pit to Hell (bedrock) lined with Nether wart blocks. Sam adds a button-activated redstone piston door to conceal the opening of the pit.
  • HBomb builds a giant wooden "OwO" sign between his parkour course and Antfrost's animal sanctuary
  • Ponk discovers Pogtopia and explores the base, finding Karl's "Only 4 Poki" book that Tommy had stolen. He puts the book in his Ender Chest and then steals (taxes) stacks of potatoes from a hidden chest near the potato farm. He attempts to get a Mending book from HBomb in exchange for these potatoes and his own emeralds. The next day, Ponk ends up finding and stealing a Mending book from HBomb's room.
  • Tommy gives Techno some of his rare Phantom membranes so that he can make Slow Falling potions. Techno searches for rare Halloween mobs and captures a skeleton and a zombified Piglin with Jack o'lanterns on their heads, making them "butlers" in his base.

November 1-2, 2020

  • Dream and Sapnap rename Fundy Fun Land and use the room to surprise George for his birthday. After being led astray by Dream through the Nether roof, George receives 24 birthday punches/stabs each from Dream, Sapnap, and Karl. George blows up and burns most of Sapnap's house after Dream places the TNT and provides him with a flint and steel.
  • Sam adds scaffolding on his Nether ice bridge to avoid obstacles caused by mobs spawning. He works on a bee farm shaped like a large honeycomb near his decoy house and sets up a scavenger hunt with books as clues for Ponk to get his emerald blocks back. While checking up on the ocean base, he acquires another trident, making him the only SMP member to own two tridents. He enchants the second trident with Riptide.
  • BadBoyHalo builds a statue of Skeppy on top of their mansion.
  • Karl builds a mini haunted mansion with gifts in three chests near the main haunted mansion as a surprise for Niki's birthday.
  • Karl organizes a birthday party for Niki to be hosted at the dining hall in the basement of the haunted mansion. The guests include: Skeppy, BadBoyHalo, Punz, Wilbur, Callahan, Sam, and Quackity.
    • Sam brings snow golems to the mansion and Quackity performs a stripper routine, which traumatizes the guests.
    • Fundy briefly joins the party but leaves after witnessing Quackity's antics.
    • After all the guests leave, Wilbur and Quackity sing songs for each other while Karl gifts Niki one of Sam's snow golems, now named "NIKI JR".
  • Wilbur suddenly makes the decision to detonate the TNT under Manberg and heads towards the room with the button.
    • Quackity, Karl, and Niki sprint to Manberg, desperately trying to convince him not to go through with the plan.
    • Quackity ultimately convinces Wilbur not to press the button by telling him that he will arrange a meeting with Schlatt as previously planned .
    • Wilbur warns Quackity that if the meeting does not occur by Friday, he will blow up Manberg, before logging off in the button room.

November 3-4, 2020

  • Tommy signs a contract with Ponk, allowing permission for him to access Pogtopia to sell his essential oils, in exchange for emerald blocks and Dream's "Happy Birthday" book for George. He finds a lone horse and names him " Juorse ", keeping the horse under his holiday home. He steals a saddle from one of Karl's horses in order to ride Juorse.
  • Karl and Niki start to work on building a snowy Christmas-themed town on a stone mountain by the ocean past Fundy's base.
  • Ponk completes Sam's scavenger hunt and then makes a mini aquarium with a chest of gifts for Niki's birthday. The aquarium is located next to his Dream 10 mil shrine across from the outdoor theatre stage. Ponk also gifts Niki a cat named Zuko (the name of Niki's irl cat).
  • Ponk takes down the bottom half of his fifth lemon tree (Festival/Christmas Tree) in Manberg and then builds a curved wooden staircase around Thiccatron.
  • After finding out about Sam's second trident discovery, Punz tries searching for a trident as well, but later gives up.
  • Fundy goes on a prank marathon where he tries to prank as many people as possible within a 2-hour time period:
    • He covers the interior of the haunted mansion with rainbow colored wool, flowers, and torches to make it look 'less scary'
    • He builds a " holiday resort " made out of iron doors and trapdoors for Dream, near Karl's Harry Potter house .
    • He fills the lower floor and the grounds of Eret's castle with various pink wool flamingos.
    • He completely covers the walls of Pogtopia with different types of wooden buttons.

November 5, 2020

  • Ponk continues to work on the wooden staircase around the Thiccatron, adding a few red mushroom blocks and shroomlight blocks to the staircase.
  • Niki logs on to view Ponk's gifts and later finds out that NIKI JR has died of unknown causes when she returns to the haunted mansion.
  • Karl and Niki continue to work on the Christmas town, crafting snow golems and replacing the ground with snow blocks and glowstone along with some lamp posts. Eret joins to help with the project, collecting snow from the snow golems.
  • Sapnap kidnaps Tommy's new horse, Juorse, and hides him in a secret location. He plans to hold the horse for ransom until Tommy returns his most valuable items, his netherite sword (Sapnap's Schlong) and his fish, Mars .
  • HBomb and Tubbo both go searching for netherite to re-complete their armor set.
  • Punz goes on the search for a trident again and finds a panda. Sapnap helps him transport the panda back to his base.
  • The Second Pet War takes place after Punz and Sapnap's new panda (unofficially named "Dumptruck") is accidentally killed, resulting in a victory for the members of the Badlands: Antfrost, Bad, and Sam.
  • Fundy attempts to scam Ponk and Purpled.
    • He tells Ponk that his fourth lemon tree is surpassing the height limit of the server and gets arrows of invisibility from Ponk in return after Ponk makes Fundy kill one of the unnamed cats in his city, which Fundy names Sheninamum.
    • Fundy makes up a few fake enforcements and warns Purpled that he could be "banned" for making futuristic builds, such as his UFO base . Purpled concludes that there is nothing wrong with his build and leaves the conversation.
  • Tommy plans to ban fireworks on the entire server after hearing of the true meaning of Guy Fawkes Day. Tommy and Tubbo make a small building for their new order, the Queen's Rajuls/Jays , made of ancient debris, hay, glass, and obsidian. Fundy follows both Tommy and Tubbo while invisible and is shot by them near the Community House . Fundy notes down the violations and then confronts Tommy and Tubbo, who distracts him by asking what Fundy's opinion of fireworks are.
    • Tommy makes a cross made of hay with a firework in an item frame in Manberg near the Manberg flagpole. They manage to convince Fundy to join them while heading to ban fireworks in Pogtopia , only to find the walls of the ravine covered with different types of buttons from Fundy's prank marathon, which they ended up blaming on an increasingly insane Wilbur. Tommy then puts a hay cross in Pogtopia as well.
    • The trio make their way to Sam's base . While in the Nether, a ghast breaks the portal and pushes Fundy off a ledge, killing him.
    • Tommy and Tubbo both try to retrieve Fundy's stuff, even though it seemed like it had despawned. They both die from fall damage, making them hurry back in a panic. After all three of them retrieve their stuff, Tommy brings a flint and steel to light the portal again.
    • Tommy puts a cross made of smokers by Sam's base, since he lacked the materials to make one with hay. Techno then joins them and Tommy asks him what he thinks about fireworks. Techno replies that they were both good and useless at the same time, if they were not used in combat or such.
  • Tommy, Tubbo, and Techno then all return to Tommy's base, only to be contacted by a frantic Quackity, who tells Tommy about Wilbur's plan to blow up Manberg tomorrow if the meeting to assassinate Schlatt does not occur.

November 6, 2020

  • Techno makes a secret villager farm accessible by a tunnel through the spider XP grinder, which Sam and Ponk discover on separate occasions
  • Antfrost and Ponk build a little makeshift village for cats and dogs/wolves in Ant's animal sanctuary. Ant transfers all of his pets from Sam's mountain base to the village. Ponk also starts building his sixth lemon tree in the same area.
  • Sam breaks his public Nether portal and constructs a new secret portal hidden in a wall in the Nether that leads to his mountain base after he realizes that people are stealing from him. He also makes a backup portal with an elevator next to the secret entrance.
  • Quackity plans to make Schlatt sign a book/contract with a secret clause that will transfer Schlatt's leadership to him. As he looks for a book, Quackity falls for the fall trap in Tommy's base.
  • After retrieving his stuff, he asks Ponk for a book, but ultimately finds some spare books instead. Quackity goes somewhere else to write the book since he does not trust Ponk or Fundy and writes the Official Manberg Hotel Building Permit.
  • Fundy and Tubbo go on a trap-building spree:
    • Tubbo makes a fall trap activated by a tripwire on the aboveground prime path. Fundy catches Tubbo testing the trap and Tubbo asks him to test it out for him, telling Fundy that there was water at the bottom of the trap. Fundy dies from fall damage from the trap and he and Tubbo both rush toward the sewage system to retrieve Fundy's stuff.
    • Fundy shows Tubbo his arrows of invisibility that he got from Ponk and both of them plan other traps to prank people on the server.
    • Tubbo accidentally falls into his trap and another alter ego of Tubbo is created by Fundy called "Dumbbo".
    • Tubbo and Fundy both trap the staircase to the community Nether portal with another fall trap that leaves the victim with only half a heart of health. They add a wooden arch in the front of the nether portal to make the tripwire less visible. Fundy also adds a black "trapmingo" on top of the arch.
    • The two both decide to trap Tommy's house, but Tubbo gets spawn glitched and Fundy falls into the Nether portal trap while trying to get Tubbo's stuff and getting to spawn. After Fundy helps Tubbo out of the spawn glitch and gets his stuff back, they start to trap the entrance of Tommy's base. They try to make Awesamdude fall for the trap by the prime path, but Sam flies over it using his trident.
    • Sam then helps Tubbo and Fundy work on the fall trap at Tommy's base. They manage to complete it before Tommy logs on.
  • Tommy joins the server and immediately falls down Tubbo and Fundy's traps. Quackity meets with Tommy at Ninja's house where he tells Tommy about his plans. He shows Tommy the book, and Tommy approves of the plan.
  • Tommy travels to his holiday home and finds out about the disappearance of Juorse but says that he will settle this conflict with Sapnap at a later time.
  • Wilbur discovers Fundy's prank on Pogtopia with wooden buttons covering the walls. He takes down the hay cross that Tommy placed to try and ban fireworks.
  • Quackity meets with Schlatt at the Manberg Podium and tries to rebuild his trust as Tommy and Wilbur observe from a distance. Sam gives Tommy some pumpkin pie while Ponk supplies Wilbur with a crossbow and invisibility arrows.
  • As Schlatt is about to sign Quackity's contract, he reveals that he knows about the TNT under Manberg. Wilbur shoots Quackity with an invisibility arrow as they escape to the woods. Schlatt catches up with Tommy and Quackity and is held at arrow-point.
    • Schlatt finds out the truth about the book and claims that he has relocated the TNT to Pogtopia. He starts attacking Tommy and Quackity, and two try to contain Schlatt, but end up killing him instead. Meanwhile, Wilbur finds out that the redstone in the button room has been disconnected.
  • Tommy, Wilbur, and Quackity make their way back to Pogtopia. Fundy meets them there, and he reveals that he is working for Pogtopia. Fundy shows Wilbur a spy's diary as proof. Wilbur reads the last entry of the diary, which documents Schlatt's recent actions and his poor health status.
  • BadBoyHalo then comes into Pogtopia and Tommy starts cussing at him, thinking that Bad is an enemy. Bad and Sam reveal that their faction, the Badlands, supports the Pogtopian effort as well.
    • Fundy explains that Schlatt no longer has any true allies, and Wilbur uses that information to claim that blowing up Manberg will only affect Schlatt. The group concludes that there is no reason to blow Manberg up.
  • Dream joins the call and tells them that Schlatt has given him something (which he keeps secret) that he would have to defend Manberg for. He also states that he doesn't care if Manberg is blown up, as he only craves destruction.
    • He then reveals there is an unsuspecting traitor among the Pogtopians.
    • The two sides set a date for the future war in ten days’ time.
    • Wilbur warns the group that he will still blow up Manberg if they do not succeed in reclaiming it.
  • This war was dubbed as the Manberg vs Pogtopia War , sparking a new era in the timeline of the SMP: The Manberg-Pogtopia War Era .
