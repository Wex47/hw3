Dylan , known online as BoomerNA and often referred to as Boomer , is a Twitch streamer and YouTuber known for playing Hypixel Bedwars. Boomer is friends with Sapnap and SMP members Antfrost , Awesamdude , Hannahxxrose , Punz , and Purpled . He visited the Dream SMP multiple times through Hannahxxrose and Awesamdude's accounts before he joined on October 29, 2021, making him the most recent member.
