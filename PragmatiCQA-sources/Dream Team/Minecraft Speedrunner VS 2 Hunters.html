Video

Minecraft Speedrunner VS 2 Hunters is a video released by Dream to his main channel on April 19, 2020. It is the tenth Minecraft Manhunt video ever, and in this video, the point of the challenge is that Dream has to beat Minecraft while running away from two hunters instead of one. The hunters are GeorgeNotFound and Sapnap .

Trivia

  • This video has a .
  • This is the first Minecraft Manhunt video to have two hunters, but not the first Minecraft Manhunt video to have three players. Minecraft Speedrunner VS Assassin ft. Notch was the first to have three participants, but in that video, Notch was the speedrunner, Dream was the hunter, and George was rather a protector on Notch's side than a speedrunner himself.
  • This is the first and only Minecraft Manhunt where Dream wins in his first try against an additional hunter. In his other first manhunt videos against one, three, four, and five hunters the first time, Dream lost. In his extra video of Minecraft Speedrunner VS 5 Hunters , he forgot about it.
  • This Manhunt is the only one not to have a rematch.
