The Prison Lockdown Era was an era of the Dream SMP following the Imprisonment Era , and the second era of the Empowerment Arc .

Following the security incident at Pandora's Vault , TommyInnit is locked into the prison alongside Dream . Awesamdude increases prison security, hiring more guards. Nihachu and Jack Manifold are conspiring together to kill Tommy after Project Dreamcatcher failed. With the absence of Tommy, Jack Manifold takes over the Big Innit Hotel and forms a partnership with Quackity , who is also starting a business in an attempt to gain power, all the while the Eggpire and the Blood Vines continue to expand. Meanwhile, CaptainPuffy officially starts her resistance against the Eggpire, which includes other members such as Technoblade .
