Video

Minecraft Speedrunner VS 3 Hunters is a video released by Dream to his main channel on May 13, 2020. It is the eleventh Minecraft Manhunt video ever, and in this video, the point of the challenge is that Dream has to beat Minecraft while running away from three hunters instead of one. The hunters are GeorgeNotFound , Sapnap , and BadBoyHalo .
