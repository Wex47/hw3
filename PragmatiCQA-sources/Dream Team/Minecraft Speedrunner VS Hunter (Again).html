Video

Minecraft Speedrunner VS Hunter (Again) is a video released by Dream to his main channel on March 22, 2020. It is the ninth Minecraft Manhunt video, and in this video, the point of the challenge is that Dream has to beat Minecraft while running away from GeorgeNotFound , the "hunter", who is plotted to stop Dream by killing him to make him lose the challenge.
