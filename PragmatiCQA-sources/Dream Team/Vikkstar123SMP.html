Vikkstar123 , often known simply as Vikkstar or Vikk , is the twenty-eighth member of the SMP who joined on November 23, 2020. He founded the territory of Boomerville alongside his friend LazarBeam .

Physiology

Vikkstar is a human with brown eyes and black hair. He is wearing a blue shirt and jeans. The blue and red design on his shirt resembles the color scheme of the logo of his main channel. Vikk has had this skin since he started playing Minecraft.

Personality

Vikk joined the server with LazarBeam and is currently living in a house with the latter in Boomerville. He is trying not to get involved in or cause any trouble. Even if most of the people on the server admit to watching him during their childhood and some still do, such as Tommy , Vikk doesn't get too ahead of himself.

History

Election night cameo

On election night, Vikkstar made a cameo appearance via a pre-recorded video endorsement for POG2020 .

Official debut

Vikk joined the Dream SMP on November 23, 2020. He joined with LazarBeam and was given a tour by Karl . After the tour, Vikk decided to form Boomerville with Lazar.
