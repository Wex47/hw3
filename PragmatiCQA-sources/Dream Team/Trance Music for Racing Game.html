Trance Music for Racing Game , also known to the Dream Team community as the " Manhunt Music " or the " Dream Speedrunning Music, " is an upbeat tune of the trance music genre used by Dream often in his edited Minecraft Manhunt videos or as a joke by other streamers, namely on the Dream SMP . It was composed by the musical artist Bobby Cole for his album "The Sound of Rugby."

Another Bobby Cole song frequently used by Dream and his friends is titled " ."

Technical information

  • The track runs with a F♯ (F-sharp)/G♭ (G-flat) key and a major mode.
  • The tempo of the song is 169 BPM, which means it can also be used half-time at 85 BPM.
  • It also has a time signature of four beats per bar.
