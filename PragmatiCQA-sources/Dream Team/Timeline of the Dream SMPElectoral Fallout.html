The Electoral Fallout occurred after The L'Manbergian Election . After POG2020's loss, L'Manberg saw a huge shift in power, and was rebranded to Manberg under the new leadership of Jschlatt . The power shift in L'Manberg and the banishment of Wilbur and Tommy sparked a rebellion , and many plots to overthrow Schlatt were developed.

Details

September 22, 2020

  • Schlatt becomes president and holds his inauguration speech.
    • He immediately revokes Wilbur and Tommy's citizenship, forcing them to run or be killed. Wilbur and Tommy escape though Tubbo's secret bunker , and then return to watch the rest of his speech from on top of a building. As they are running, Wilbur is shot by Punz , losing his second canon life.
    • Schlatt enstates Tubbo as his Secretary of State, and George as a cabinet member.
    • He orders Tubbo to find Tommy and Wilbur and escort them out of L'Manberg . Tubbo goes to look for them, while Niki tries and fails to get him to help her resist Schlatt. He doesn't find them.
    • Schlatt holds his second presidential speech, decreeing that the wall of L'Manberg is to be taken down.
    • Eret joins Tommy and Wilbur's VC offering help, but Tommy dismisses them, not wanting to be betrayed again.
    • Tubbo, Jack , Punz, Fundy , Eret and Schlatt begin taking down the walls while Quackity , HBomb and Eret watch.
    • Tubbo goes to the Camarvan and reminisces on the War for L'Manbergian Independence .
      • Schlatt finds him there and wants to take down the Camarvan . Tubbo asks him not to do so. Schlatt berates Tubbo for wanting to go against him, but agrees to keep the van.
    • Meanwhile, Wilbur and Tommy build a hole in a hill to live in. They sneak back to L'Manberg to see the walls being torn down, and Tommy steals Fundy's skeleton horse Fundy had gotten a while back before they return to their hill.
  • Technoblade tweets at Wilbur and Tommy as they are fleeing, and asks if they need help.
    • After some pressuring from Tommy, Wilbur agrees to accept Techno's help. Wilbur and Tommy trade armor and Tommy retrieves Techno from spawn while he kills Ponk.
    • Wilbur tames a dog, and names it L'Dog.
    • Schlatt holds his third presidential speech and renames L'Manberg to Manberg .
    • Techno agrees to help train Wilbur and Tommy, as well as build a massive potato farm to feed them.
    • They then expand the hole in the hill they made, and find a ravine while doing so. They then make it their official base.
  • Tommy, Wilbur, and Techno convince Tubbo to act as a double agent for them, and feed them information, as well as sow seeds of doubt into both Quackity and Schlatt about the other.
  • Quackity and Schlatt begin to argue over their power dynamic, with many people calling for Quackity to overthrow Schlatt and take over Manberg as its sole president.
    • Schlatt and Quackity eventually agree to a joint-power dynamic, with Quackity reminding Schlatt that he is only in office because he allowed him to and demands respect, which Schlatt agrees to, though tensions between the two are still high.
  • BadBoyHalo joins the server and is quickly questioned by Quackity as to where his loyalties lie.
    • He follows the Manbergians around as they discuss the events that have occurred.
    • They speak to Nihachu, who refuses to comply with the new government.
  • Tommy, Wilbur, Techno, Schlatt, and Quackity, along with Manberg's cabinet, agree to a meeting called by the Ex-L'Manbergians.
    • At the meeting, Tommy asks Schlatt for a Visa into Manberg for a single day, and when Schlatt asks why, Tommy reveals that he managed to get Ninja to agree to come onto the server. Schlatt agrees to the Visa, and tells Tommy that for one day, there will be peace.
    • During this, Niki speaks out against Schlatt, and has her taxes raised.
    • While escorting the Ex-L'Manbergians and Techno out of Manberg, Tubbo warns Tommy not to trust Techno.
  • Schlatt goes to sleep, but ends up on life support, and is tended to by Tubbo, Punz, and Jack. Quackity once again is pressured to take power. Here, his chat heavily insists that he overthrows Schlatt but instead of directly doing so Quackity makes multiple gestures pointing towards assassinating Schlatt such as removing the plug for his life support.
    • Schlatt recovers enough to move and they discuss current events. At one point, Quackity held a crossbow in his hand loaded with one arrow seemingly ready to fire at Schlatt's head to assassinate him for the presidential title, even having Ponk tell him to do so, though he does not go through with it.
  • Tommy, Wilbur, and Techno establish their new base as Pogtopia .
    • Bad and Eret track the Pogtopians to their base and slowly begin exploring the inner depths of the ravine.
    • Tommy discovers a strip mine within Pogtopia that leads back to his secret bunker, and to an extent, his home back in enemy territory.
    • Bad and Eret discover Techno within the ravine in the potato farm he'd been constructing to feed Pogtopia.
    • Wilbur and Tommy then make a cobblestone shrine to honor Twitch prime near Manberg.
    • Upon returning to the surface, Bad and Eret find Wilbur crouched beneath a tree. Eret explains to Bad that he supports Wilbur, and so the two make their way back to the main area of the server.
  • Fundy comes to Eret asking him about his opinion on the destruction of the L'Manbergian wall, Eret claims he believed it was disrespectful to L'Manbergian history and disagrees with the dictatorial role that Jschlatt holds.
    • Fundy tells Eret of a plan he has to win the full trust of Jschlatt so he can gain the power to take him down from the inside. Eret claims he is willing to support Fundy's plan. Fundy then reveals to Eret and Niki that he wants the L'Manbergian flag to be removed, which creates tension between Fundy and Niki.
    • Soon after, Eret catches Fundy attempting to destroy the flag, and kills him. Eret also states that he is one who respects the server's history and strives to support its people, whether they are his friends or enemies.
    • Fundy later burns down most of the L'Manberg flag, much to Niki's dismay. Eret and Niki just barely stop the fire, and the flag is reduced an only quarter of what it once was.
  • Eret and Niki retell the events of the L'Manbergian Election to a newly joined Sapnap . Sap claims that the Pogtopians should be taken out, though Eret and Niki passively disagree with him. Sapnap then concludes that Dream needs to be spoken to and informed of what had occurred, he then leaves.
    • Eret later decides to burn down the electoral podium, claiming that everything that happened was not history, it was tyranny.
  • Niki tries to talk Quackity into betraying Schlatt, and he seems tempted to help her, but ultimately only agrees to hide the fact she is sparking a rebellion. Schlatt asks him where he is and Quackity starts messing around, prompting Niki to punch him and accidentally kill him.
    • Angry at this, Quackity tells Schlatt about her ideas and he orders her to be imprisoned.
    • Niki escapes to the balcony before being trapped and imprisoned by Quackity. Everyone surrounds her, and Schlatt decides she should be put on trial and be given the worst possible outcome.
    • Overwhelmed with guilt, Quackity broke the bars of her prison and she escaped - cheering loudly about how L'Manberg would never die.
    • Eret covered up the escape by saying Quackity wasn't near her at the time.

September 23, 2020

  • Tubbo returned to the server after being told by Wilbur to spy and write down what he experiences throughout Manberg. Soon after, Niki attempted to convince Tubbo to officially join The Manberg Rebellion after conversing with HBomb and touring him around the server.
    • It appeared that while Tubbo was interested, he was reluctant to commit to the cause due to the fact that he did not want to betray Schlatt's trust completely. The two of them headed to Tubbo's bunker beneath Manberg and together, they began to plan the archive which would be created to monitor Schlatt's actions.
  • Tubbo found himself as the receiver of messages from various server members on their opinions of Schlatt. One of the said members was Punz, who asked Tubbo to come to the Camarvan to discuss matters. Punz offered him various items of importance, Tubbo reluctantly took the items, then the two went their separate ways.
  • Later, Wilbur and Techno invited Tubbo to join them in Pogtopia, Tubbo agreed to help them automate their farm upon Wilbur's request.
    • Schlatt then joined and began asking Tubbo questions about where he'd been, and to come back to Manberg to assist him. Schlatt also revealed more plans to take down various builds from the original L'Manbergian land.
    • Schlatt soon began to threaten Niki with taxes, so Wilbur decided to go after her with Techno close behind him. He managed to contact her, and tell her to lay low, and that he didn't have the resources to bring more people into Pogtopia quite yet.
    • Wilbur then ordered Techno to shoot Fundy, after seeing that he was remaking the flag, this time with a purple design. Techno fired at Fundy, but missed. Schlatt then nearly discovered that Techno was there, so Wilbur and Techno retreated towards Pogtopia.
    • Tommy then joined, only to find the flag half-burned, and undergoing changes by Fundy's hand. He aggressively threatened to burn the flag down, and managed to make a good distraction for the escape of Wilbur and Techno. Tommy and Niki later burned down most of the flag at different times, including all of Fundy's changes to it.
  • Similar to Tubbo, Fundy also kept a spy diary to track what happened throughout the server. Fundy also planned to take Jschlatt down from the inside, although both Tubbo and Niki remained unaware of this.
  • Niki and Eret began to conspire about what they might do to help the Pogtopians.
    • They ended up amassing a large amount of TNT and gunpowder. Eret also revealed that he had been secretly working on gathering resources to give to Pogtopia, and had cleared an area under his castle for them to use. The underground area had a massive potato farm, a turtle farm, and a haste beacon.
  • Technoblade received supplies from Fundy, BadBoyHalo, and Dream, wanting to help the Rebellion. Dream, however, said that he couldn't get too involved at the current moment due to a peace treaty, stating that he had to "be in the shadows".
End of spoiler warning.
