Andrea Botez is a Twitch streamer who plays chess professionally. She shares a Twitch channel with her sister Alexandra, where they play chess online. She is a friend of the Dream Team introduced via Love or Host . In addition, she plays with and is good friends with fellow streamers JustaMinx , Karl Jacobs , and Kaceytron.

Dream Team

Andrea was on GeorgeNotFound and Sapnap 's Love or Host episodes. She got very far in both George and Sapnap's shows, coming in second place for George's and making it to the semi-finals in Sapnap's show.

After her Love or Host with George, she started featuring Minecraft content creators in her chess streams and videos. George and Sapnap were featured in many streams/videos and partook in Andrea and Alexandra's chess tournament, BlockChamps, featuring many Minecraft creators that are also friends of the Dream Team.

SMP visits

Andrea visited the SMP to confirm that George built a sign that stated "I ♥️ Andrea" after he lost a chess match. She also appeared once more with a different name for a date with Jack Manifold .
