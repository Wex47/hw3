History

Username

Ponk stated on livestream that he got the username idea from his cousin. He liked the word, but later after starting to use it, he found out that it can be used as a slang word for weed.

Meeting George

According to Ponk's livestream on June 24, 2020, he met George through MCPvP after George joined a TeamSpeak channel without asking. Ponk got into an argument with George, but they eventually became good friends and often hang out together. The two went to Winter Wonderland together and sold designer sneakers for profit.

Starting off

Ponk was first introduced to the viewers of the Dream Team during Dream's SMP stream on May 8, 2020. Ponk has since gained many followers on Twitch, and regularly streams himself playing the Dream SMP , Minecraft events, Minecraft speedrun attempts, drunk streams, and has also streamed himself playing horror games such as Outlast, Outlast 2, and Five Nights at Freddy's. Ponk occasionally will stream with a face cam while wearing his mask.
