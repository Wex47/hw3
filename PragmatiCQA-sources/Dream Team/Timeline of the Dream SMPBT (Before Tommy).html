The Before Tommy Era was the first era of the Dream SMP. In the beginning, the server was referred to as the survival world, and the members at the time only included the Dream Team and a few close friends. Everyone lived close to each other, and the Community House was the heart of the server.

Details

April 24, 2020

  • Dream creates the SMP with GeorgeNotFound (George) on April 24, 2020.
    • The pair join the server for the first time and gather basic survival resources.
    • Dream and George find a lava pool, and Dream teaches George how to build a nether portal quickly. Before going to the nether, BadBoyHalo (Bad) joins the VC.
    • When the duo enters the nether portal, they spawn in the sky above a lava pool, so they bridge across to the mainland.
    • Dream and George find a bastion and begin to explore it. However, when looting a chest, Dream is killed by a piglin, and George dies shortly after by a magma cube.
    • They re-enter the nether, but Dream dies again. Then, when digging down in the overworld, he falls into a ravine and nearly dies. They both begin gathering resources in the ravine. After leaving the ravine, he dies again.
    • George hits Dream, causing the dogs that George tamed to be aggro'd on Dream. Dream then kills both the dogs, much to George's dismay.
    • After gathering more resources, they head back to the bastion, however George dies to fall damage when entering. George returns and finds netherite, but then dies again. George returns then dies again, before they both continue looting the bastion.
    • Dream dies whilst trying to bridge, dropping the netherite, and then dies again whilst re-entering the bastion. Dream tries to get the netherite, but falls into lava and quits the game.
  • Later on, George goes exploring whilst Dream is online and Bad is in their VC. George catches a fish, and then returns home and shows the foundations of their first base , which they had built off-stream.
    • George names the fish he caught Beckerson , and puts the fish in a small hole of water in their farm.
    • Dream finishes the animal pens and George breeds some sheep. They then move their chests into the base whilst talking about how they all had met each other.
    • Callahan joins the server and Sapnap joins shortly after. They all begin working on the base, and Sapnap builds an iron golem, despite the other members seeing it as a waste.

April 28, 2020

  • Awesamdude (Sam) and ItsAlyssa (Alyssa) join the server for the first time. They both get some food.
    • Sapnap removes a structure built by Callahan near their base.
    • George gets the Bad Omen effect, and goes to a village with Dream to fight a pillager raid. Sapnap also follows behind them, although the two don't notice him initially. All three complete the raid and return home.
    • Dream kills Alyssa's first dog, which causes Sapnap to nearly kill Beckerson. Dream apologizes and Sapnap lets the fish live, however after Alyssa threatened to kill it, Sapnap picked up the fish in a bucket. Sapnap puts the fish back later.
    • Sam tries returning to the base after a mining trip in a ravine, but gets surrounded by monsters. George then rescues him.
    • George and Sam brings two cows back to the base. Whilst they were doing this, Dream drowns trying to mine some diamonds, losing all of his items. However, Sam accidentally kills one of the cows. They then go to get three pigs and one cow, however Sapnap accidentally kills a pig, and finally gets the pigs and the cow in their pens.
    • George and Sapnap have a duel, and Sapnap wins. Dream then donates $20 to George's stream.
    • They all begin fishing in the lake.

April 29, 2020

  • Whilst Dream was working around the base, a creeper in the animal farm blew up, causing all the animals to escape. They fix the animal farm, although most of the animals died in the process.
    • The group begins working on a fish tank for Beckerson on top of their base. Dream complains that the original design for the fish tank was bad, and they begin making a new design.
    • George and Sapnap begin attacking each other. Dream breaks up the fight by threatening to kill a dog if they don't stop.
    • Dream begins complaining about the new design for the house and the fish tank. They then make a few modifications to make it look better, and then fill up the fish tank with water and put the fish in there.
