Minecraft Speedrunner VS 5 Hunters FINALE is a video released by Dream to his main channel on September 20, 2021. It is the third Minecraft Manhunt video that features a fifth hunter, Awesamdude . The other hunters are Antfrost , BadBoyHalo , Sapnap , and GeorgeNotFound .

“ We decided to call this series Minecraft Manhunt. This video is a different rendition of it, where there are not FOUR hunters but FIVE instead of just one.

This is a new Minecraft but, 1.16 challenge that we have decided to try. I try to beat the game, while my friend George AND my friend Sapnap AND my friend BadBoyHalo AND my friend Antfrost AND my friend awesamdude tries to prevent me from doing it. It's a race, and it's super intense and we had a lot of laughs. I love doing these challenges.

If this video gets 2,000,000 likes we'll do a rematch!

Not Minecraft, But Water Rises or Minecraft, But Lava Rises, or any other but challenge like that. This is a speed run / speed runner of Minecraft against a killer / assassin

Trivia

  • It is the first Minecraft Manhunt to be played with some of the new world generation features of the Caves & Cliffs Part II update.
  • It is the first and only (known) Minecraft Manhunt to be played on a snapshot.
  • This is the shortest traditional Minecraft Manhunt since Minecraft Speedrunner VS Hunter (Again) .
  • Though Dream usually prevents himself from doing plays he did in previous Minecraft Manhunts, this is the first Manhunt in which two things were definitively banned: ender pearl stasis chambers and exploiting the dragon's perch mechanic.
  • Dream uploaded an extended cut version of this Manhunt on his Minecraft Manhunt channel called .
    • This is the first time Dream released an extended cut version of a Minecraft Manhunt.
