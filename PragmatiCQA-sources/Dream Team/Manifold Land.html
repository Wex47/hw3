Manifold Land was an independent nation on the Dream SMP comprised of Jack Manifold's house (Ze Haus) and what was formerly Su Puta Madre . It seceded from Manberg on November 15, 2020, and its leader, Jack Manifold fought alongside Pogtopia in the Manberg vs Pogtopia War to secure its independence.

History

Jack Manifold returned to streaming on the SMP on November 15, 2020 and became aware of the Manberg Rebellion . Tired of everyone stealing his items from his house and wanting to stay out of conflict, he decided to separate into his own territory, Manifold Land. He claimed the land around his house and Su Puta Madre as part of this new territory before beginning the process of fencing off the land. The following day, he defended his land, but eventually joined Pogtopia in the Manberg vs Pogtopia War, hoping to be able to keep his land safe by building allies.

After the Estate Conflict on December 15, 2020, Jack established relations with the Badlands by making Antfrost a citizenship of Manifold Land with the Document of Manifold Citizenship .

Jack Manifold sided with L'Manberg during the Doomsday War to ensure the safety of Manifold Land, which resulted in small areas of Manifold Land being blown up. The house was largely still intact.

Wanting to be free after Tommy 's death on March 1, Jack burnt down most of Manifold Land, officially disbanding the faction.
