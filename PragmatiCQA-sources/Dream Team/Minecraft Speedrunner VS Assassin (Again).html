Video

Minecraft Speedrunner VS Assassin (Again) is a video released by Dream to his main channel on January 29, 2020. It is the seventh Minecraft Manhunt video ever, and in this video, the point of the challenge is that Dream has to beat Minecraft while running away from GeorgeNotFound , who is plotted to stop Dream by killing him to make him lose the challenge.
