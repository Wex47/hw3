Video

Minecraft Speedrunner VS Hunter is a video released by Dream to his main channel on January 7, 2020. It is the fourth Minecraft Manhunt video ever, and in this video, the point of the challenge is that GeorgeNotFound has to beat Minecraft while running away from Dream, the "hunter", who is plotted to stop George by killing him to make him lose the challenge.

Results

Show/hide spoiler
Dream won this challenge. Although he died at 13:30 by falling into a lava pit in the Nether and he died by falling into a hole at 27:14, he still didn't lose the challenge because he was the hunter, not the speedrunner. How he won the challenge was fairly simple: when George was building a cobblestone bridge to reach from his obsidian platform to The End, Dream pushed him at 27:38 and killed him, making Dream the winner of this challenge.
