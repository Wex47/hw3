Minecraft Speedrunner VS 2 Assassins is a video released by Dream on his main channel that premiered on July 27, 2021 featuring GeorgeNotFound and Sapnap . The objective is similar to the videos in the Minecraft Manhunt series on Dream's channel, though George and Sapnap can win by punching Dream once. However, the two freeze if they are in Dream's crosshair.
