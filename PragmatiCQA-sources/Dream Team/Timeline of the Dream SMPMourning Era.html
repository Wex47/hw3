The Mourning Era was an era of the Dream SMP after the Prison Lockdown Era , and the third era of the Empowerment Arc . Following Tommy 's death, the various members of the server mourn (or celebrate) his death, building statues and memorials in his honor and investigating the details of what happened before and during his death. Meanwhile, the Syndicate expands its influence and the Eggpire continues to unleash the Egg .
