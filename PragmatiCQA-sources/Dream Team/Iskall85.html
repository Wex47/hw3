Viktor , better known online as Iskall85 and Iskall , is a Swedish member of Hermitcraft . He was featured on the Dream SMP through a voice call tutorial on October 2, 2020 by vocally leading Fundy on a tutorial on how to create a 3x3 piston door for his secret bunker .

SMP visit

Iskall85 was a visitor to the Dream SMP. Fundy invited Iskall to a video call by spamming Iskall's Twitch chat. After gaining Iskall's attention, Fundy plotted to troll Iskall during his visit. During his voice-call visit, Iskall instructed Fundy on building a redstone door for his secret bunker while being trolled by Fundy pretending to be redstone and Minecraft-knowledge illiterate.

Prior to the call, Fundy trolled Iskall by placing diorite all over the place they would make the door. Iskall has said multiple times that he hated diorite because it looked like "bird poop."

After Iskall joined the call, Fundy gave Iskall a small tour of the SMP. Fundy started the tour by throwing his armor off the Prime Path leading from Manberg and showing showed Ninja's house. Upon seeing the house, Iskall stated that Ninja had the ugliest house he had ever seen and suggested that Ninja stay with Fortnite . Iskall also stated that the only thing that could've made it any more ugly was adding diorite. The two then "improved" the house, changing wood planks with random blocks from Fundy's inventory.

After moving on from Ninja's house, Fundy walked down the Prime Path and showed the different builds along the path. He eventually ran into Tubbo and Technoblade , and Iskall joked about not knowing who Technoblade is. Tubbo then started punching Fundy back down the path and demanding for a pass, and Fundy started retreating towards Manberg to his bunker.

Upon arriving at the bunker entrance, Fundy showed his redstone capabilities by demonstrating the entrance to his bunker. After Fundy entered the bunker and opened the materials chest, Iskall immediately asked Fundy to destroy the diorite. While pretending to be illiterate at Minecraft , Fundy crafted more redstone materials and a flint and steel to burn the diorite.

Eventually, Iskall eventually caught on with the troll (presumably by looking at chat) and semi-trolled Fundy back by pretending to make static noises with his mouth through his microphone. This didn't stop Fundy, who continued to frustrate the Minecraft veteran with his apparent lack of knowledge.

After the door was built and functional, Fundy placed a trapdoor in the middle to annoy Iskall since it remained in the center after the door retracted. Iskall conceded, and Fundy started noting that the redstone was exposed and that the roof was unaligned. Fundy suggested using pistons to push the door up and even rebuilding the door. Iskall replied with a stern "No" and countered this by saying that Fundy would mine into a tunnel above. He, instead, suggested adding glass to cover the exposure so that people can admire Fundy's redstone genius. After placing the glass, the two called the build finished and ended the project.

The two then went to ridicule Ninja's house . Iskall instructed Fundy to place a sign for him stating "THIS HOUSE IS REALLY UGLY," but Fundy refused to write "UGLY." Iskall replaced "UGLY" with "FAN" and told Fundy that it secretly stands for "fantastically ugly." They then ended the visit.

The Door ended up being named after Iskall himself, holding the title of "The Iskall Door."
