Video

Minecraft Speedrunner VS Assassin is a video released by Dream to his main channel on January 4, 2020. It is the third Minecraft Manhunt video ever, and in this video, the point of the challenge is that Dream has to beat Minecraft while running away from GeorgeNotFound , the "assassin", who is plotted to stop Dream by killing him to make him lose the challenge. There is a catch however, in which the assassin only has to hit the hunter once to win.
