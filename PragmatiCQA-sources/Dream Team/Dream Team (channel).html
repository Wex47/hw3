The Dream Team YouTube channel is a channel used by the Dream Team to upload joint content that they don't want to upload to an individual channel. The channel was created on November 14, 2019 by Dream. It has been inactive since their last post on May 26, 2020.

Sapnap is currently in possession of the channel's 100k Play Button.
