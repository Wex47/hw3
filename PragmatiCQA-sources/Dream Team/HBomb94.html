Liam Heneghan , better known online as HBomb94 , is a popular Minecraft Twitch streamer and YouTuber who is a friend of the Dream Team . HBomb94 is primarily known for his SMP and UHC content on The Cube , as well as his participation in MC Championship . HBomb first became acquainted with the Dream Team through MC Championship. His first time appearing on the Dream SMP was during the SWAG2020 vs POG2020 Election on September 22, 2020.
