Grayson , better known online as Purpled , is a popular YouTuber who is friends with the Dream Team . His YouTube channel mostly consists of storytelling and Bedwars on Hypixel, and his Twitch channel also consists of a variety of many other games in addition to storytelling and Bedwars. Purpled is a member of the Dream SMP .
