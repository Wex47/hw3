Dream's Escape Battle - also known as The Final Stand , the Jailbreak , the Escape Skirmish , or The Darkest Day (by Jack Manifold) - was a major conflict that occurred on November 28, 2021, during and after Dream's escape from Pandora's Vault . It revolved around Technoblade breaking Dream, Ranboo, and ConnorEatsPants out of prison. The battle featured two sides: Technoblade , Dream , and the people who helped him escape. The other side was led by Sam in an effort to stop Dream from escaping. This battle serves as the finale of the over 10-month long Empowerment Arc and beginning of the Legacy Arc .

Preparation for Battle

On the day of the Jailbreak, The Syndicate met up and Techno showcased his plan to break Dream and Ranboo out. The previous day, Techno had spotted Ranboo being taken - seemingly against his will - inside the prison by Sam, which had concerned the former deeply. During the meeting, they moved the discussion into Techno's cabin and refined the plans some more. The blueprints were broken down a little and a short bit about a missing part of the map took place. All too soon, Techno took the blueprints down and all three prepared their inventories (and extra inventories, such as ender chests containing items for Dream and Ranboo) before setting off for Pandora's Vault. Niki was given materials to spawn some Withers in case a distraction was necessary, and Techno decided to bring a newly named Apollo, a dog that happened to be prancing along Techno's cabin for some reason or another at the time.

First Battle

Inside of Pandora's Vault

Techno started digging down right next to the prison. A quick strip mine led him to the only exposed part of the prison, which was so deep underground it didn't need to be protected any more than its hundreds of gallons of lava. Navigating under and through, Techno and Apollo swam with fire resistance through the prison's lavafalls until he found Dream's cell. Dream, in a moment of utter shock, had the brilliant idea to ask how Techno got in. Their chat was short-lived - Techno quickly came upon the conclusion that TNTing his way through was their only option and promptly blasted a hole in the wall, setting off the prison alarms in the process. The mentioned alarms alerted the guards immediately, and the duo made a run for it, inevitably, unluckily, running into Sam and BadBoyHalo , who didn't really run into them - they were just hot on Dream's and Techno's heels and gaining. The four of them then more or less consecutively ender pearled through a lava tunnel; an epically short high school fight occurred in another room in which Bad got brutally murdered several times over and Apollo got demolished, thereby unfortunately not making it out for Techno to announce casually that he strolled in, took his dog for a walk, dragged Dream on the way out (probably, somewhere in there), and went back to sleep.

Sam made it back to the Guard's prison room for whatever reason, most likely to plan his actions, and Techno used milk and TNT to announce his way through the prison. To everyone's surprise, ConnorEatsPants was in one of the cells on the way to Ranboo's; no one was even aware that Connor had been missing, stuck in jail for "stolen valor" from some war or something similar. Techno made the brilliant executive decision to bring Connor with them and stole Ranboo out as well. The four of them swam back through the lava and left through the hole Techno used to get in. Sam chased the four of them all the way out of Pandora's Vault.

Outside of Pandora's Vault

Phil and Niki watched Technoblade dig in with Apollo - a rather anticlimactic few minutes in the moment - and wondered what to do. For a while they maintained a light conversation, up until Niki noticed that they were being watche... I mean actively stared at by the prison guards. Phil hid in a hole and Niki wandered around outside, pretending to pick bamboo. Niki wandered around on her own for a while, slowly growing more and more panicked, until after a long time the prison alarms were set off; Niki ran to try and join a group for an alibi, but nobody else had heard the sirens, so the miscellaneous group she ended up with brought her along with them to a joke robbery of McPuffy's . Meanwhile, Sam suddenly appeared, emerged out onto the surface, and shouted at everybody to get to the prison because Dream and Technoblade were loose inside. Everyone present ran to the prison and stood outside with tense apprehension building up, Eryn began interrogating Niki, but a newly arrived Tommy took charge and interrupted them. A last few stragglers joined the crowd and Niki broke off from the group, watching from afar as they waited, fidgeting, for the escapees to surface.

Second Battle

The second battle began when Techno, Dream, Connor and Ranboo finally came back out of the prison and were ambushed by Eryn , Sapnap , Tommy , Punz , Michael and Jack . Everyone targeted Dream, but Sam tried a new tactic: taking Ranboo hostage. He was hoping to use Ranboo as leverage to get Dream to come back, but only Technoblade cared what happened to Ranboo. "YOU HAVE THE WRONG HOSTAGE!", he said.

Sam ended up taking a canonical life from Ranboo. It is still uncertain whether this was his last or not; however, Boo (or Ghostboo as dubbed by the community) came back in his place (see Aftermath), suggesting that Ranboo was in limbo and had indeed lost his final life.

Out of hate, anger, and grief, Techno attacked Sam and Bad, screaming at them in disbelief. Niki summoned a couple of withers from far behind, confusing and distracting the others. As Niki tried to set off another Wither, she was ambushed by Sapnap, who managed to get her pickaxe, and she was forced to flee the battlefield. Phil joined up with the others and helped them to escape. Eryn pursued the escapees closely but eventually allowed Niki to escape in the Nether through a portal to Logstedshire despite having an opportunity to stop her. In his ignorance, he refused to disclose to his allies which route Dream, Techno and Phil took to escape, allowing the escapees to return to their base unmolested. Afterward, Techno traveled to Snowchester; before death, Ranboo had given him a picture of MICHAEL, and went to Tubbo for answers. After being told he was Ranboo and Tubbo's adopted son, they decided to check on MICHAEL, only to realize he was missing. Shortly after, Techno ended the stream.

Aftermath

After Techno, Dream, Connor, Niki and Phil escaped, Sam let the rest of the SMP know that Dream was at large. Punz broke off from his allies, agreeing with Sapnap to split up and search for Dream. Punz used the opportunity to secretly meet up with Dream at a rendezvous point and restock him with supplies. Geared up again, Dream set off to collect the Axe of Peace and gave it to Techno, returning the favor he owed to Techno when Techno gave him refuge and escape from Pandora's Vault.

Tommy began to prepare by building bee-striped walls around his base (out of yellow concrete and obsidian; given to him by Quackity , meant to remind him of Manberg ), blocking up all of its entrances and exits, as he knew that Dream would go after him to obtain the Discs . Tommy encountered Dream at the remains of Logstedshire when he tried to find the Axe of Peace (he'd hidden it underneath the cross next to the Tnret) before Dream, but somehow, Dream had found it first, using the axe to attempt torturing Tommy again. While being chased by Dream with that axe (believed by most to have been a hallucination as the result of Tommy's PTSD), Dream told Tommy about how he would manipulate everyone into thinking that Tommy was crazy; Tommy hid inside Phil and Techno's house. Phil admitted that he had helped Techno break Dream out of prison and also said that he would kill Dream if he tormented Tommy again.

Tommy completed the walls around his base with the help of Eryn, Phil, and Boo, ensuring full lockdown on his base.

The next week, Tommy met up with Phil on him and Techno's training area and has talked about what happened during the Disc Confrontation and after Tommy has said that he hesitated to give his discs to save Tubbo's life, Phil had burned Tommy's Rapple that Boo has given to him. He also trained Tommy and has thinked that Tommy could beat Dream. Shortly after, Tommy returned to his House and has fell down in a trap that Dream made for him and contains the 11 Disc, also known as the scratched disc. Dream had putted a message on the disc, reminding Tommy of the days he got tormented by Dream.
