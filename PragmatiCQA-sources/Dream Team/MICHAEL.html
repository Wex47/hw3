MICHAEL (full name, " MICHAEL_Beloved ") is Tubbo and Ranboo ’s adopted son. They found him on December 1st, while gathering materials for the Meeting Room, and he was left there until a few months later, where on February 14, 2021 he was rescued from the Nether. He now lives in Tubbo's house in Snowchester , in the attic.

Ranboo's protection

Ranboo is attempting to protect MICHAEL. He is working on making sure MICHAEL doesn't get hurt by anyone, and is planning on getting him netherite armor.

Ponk has implied that he wishes to kill MICHAEL, though his reasoning and commitment are unclear. Tubbo and Ranboo seem unaware of Ponk's plans, and it is unclear how Ponk even came to know of MICHAEL's existence. Ponk chose to spare him on their most recent encounter, and Awesamdude has tried to convince Ponk not to attack MICHAEL.

When Tommy hinted that he could potentially hurt or kill MICHAEL to break up Tubbo and Ranboo, Ranboo stated that they would kill Tommy if he did. Tommy has not yet attempted to harm MICHAEL.

The Egg Incident

During one of GeorgeNotFound's streams; Sapnap, Hannah, George, Punz, Eret, Nihachu and Callahan had been hanging out in the attic of Tubbo's home in Snowchester.

George threw an egg at Michael, causing Michael to attack him back. George ran away from the zombified piglin baby and ran outside of the house, and meanwhile, Sapnap killed Hannah for no apparent reason. George was laughing about how he threw an egg at Michael, and climbed onto the roof.

George then realized his armor had the Thorns enchantment, which hurts anybody who deals damage to you. His boots are shown to have Thorns III, which could have killed Michael. He then was hit by Sapnap with a sword, making him fall back into the house, resulting in Michael chasing him and ending up outside of the house, where he could be killed more easily.

Eret put Michael in a boat and steered the boat inside while the others (minus Nihachu and Callahan, since Nihachu wanted to protect Michael, and Callahan doesn't talk) joked around.

Sapnap killed Hannah a second time, again for no apparent reason.

George requests that Sapnap throw an egg at Michael, then George proceeds to pelt Michael with eggs, Hannah briefly attempting (and failing) to block the eggs from hitting Micheal.

Hannah suggests they 1v1v1v1 in Bedwars and the loser has to kill Michael, but while they are gone

At this moment, it is unclear how many elements of this event are canon lore, so please take that into consideration.

Ranboo's death

Before dying Ranboo gave Technoblade a picture of Michael leading him to visit Tubbo. Upon visiting Tubbo it was discovered that Michael is missing, possibly because of Awesamdude. His location is currently unknown.
