The L'Manberg execution of Tubbo occurred on October 16, 2020, where Tubbo was exposed as a double agent for Pogtopia at the Manberg Festival and was executed by Technoblade using a firework crossbow. The firework also killed Emperor Jschlatt , which would then prologue the Manberg Massacre .

The execution

Once Techno was bought among the three, crowd members began getting even more concerned. Former president Wilbur Soot and former Treasury of State TommyInnit had been hiding atop of a building for the entirety of this incident beginning. Schlatt began another sentence as the following,

“ Tubbo as the enemy of the state, and as a perpetrator to these awful, awful people that you are helping. Technoblade please, if you would, if you would be so kind, take care of this traitor. Take care of him. ”

Tubbo as the enemy of the state, and as a perpetrator to these awful, awful people that you are helping. Technoblade please, if you would, if you would be so kind, take care of this traitor. Take care of him.

At first, Techno assumed Schlatt meant to just take him out of his confinement and get him some breakfast. However, Schlatt became more clear on what he really meant, blurting out by the end of the discussion,

“ Techno I need you to uhh, to take him out. ”

Techno I need you to uhh, to take him out.

Quackity then began questioning Schlatt, after what he thought was just going to be a jail sentence. Schlatt made it very clear to Techno to kill him, becoming more angered by the second, and the crowd becoming more upset.

Techno first swung once at Tubbo with a pickaxe, getting him down to moderate level health. Techno repeatedly apologized to Tubbo and attempted to make sure it was a painless death, with Schlatt snickering in the background still.

Finally, after stalling for about 20 seconds, Technoblade fired a single firework head on towards Tubbo, killing Tubbo, Schlatt, and Quackity insantly, with the two latter being accidental deaths (and Quackity's death being non-canon), with about 14 people witnessing the demise. Tommy is then instructed rather worryingly by Wilbur to teleport over to the podium in which he does so, putting Quackity and many others in the crowd into a shock of disbelief.

Aftermath

Quackity and crowd members began frantically running up to the podium, in which Tommy killed Quackity, and began fleeing, but not before being caught and killed by other members of the festival.

Wilbur, who had gone in the opposite direction, was having a hard time trying to figure out where he had made the room to the button that could explode Manberg.

Nihachu , who was already close to breaking point, was pushed off the edge at the scene of her friends death and started screaming at Schlatt because of it. This caused her to be the target of another execution, but she escaped to Pogtopia before they could kill her.

The events of the Manberg Massacre took place shortly after, and Technoblade was deemed a traitor by Tommy for Tubbo's murder, sparking the Duel at Pogtopia .
