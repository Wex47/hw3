Video

Minecraft Speedrunner VS Hunter (SECOND EVER) is a video released by Dream to his main channel on 31 December 2019/1 January 2020. It is the second Minecraft Manhunt video ever, and in this video, the point of the challenge is that Dream has to beat Minecraft while running away from GeorgeNotFound , the hunter, who is plotted to stop Dream by killing him to make him lose the challenge. This video is a rematch and is preceeded by the previous video with the same rules, Minecraft Speedrunner VS Hunter (FIRST EVER) .
