3826 Build-A-Bob was a LEGO set released in 2006 from the SpongeBob SquarePants Theme . It consists of a large LEGO model of SpongeBob with interactive components.

Features

From behind, one can see a view of SpongeBob's "brain", with Plankton controlling him, which was inspired by the SpongeBob episode "Plankton!", in which Plankton takes over SpongeBob's brain. This area contains various gears and consoles, with the gears providing interactivity.

Plankton on the brain!

Just when you thought things couldn't get any wackier in Bikini Bottom'¦Plankton has taken over SpongeBob's brain and is making him do some wild things!
