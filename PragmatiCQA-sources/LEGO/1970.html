Events

  • The company grows to 1,000 employees.
  • Jesse Shwayder , the founder of the Samsonite Corporation , dies at the age of 88.
  • The LEGO Group starts the process to regain the USA LEGO license from Samsonite.
  • The first plastic roadway plate was introduced, 50x50 green and gray, in USA/Canada only.
  • The LEGO Group breaks ground on a second production facility at Hojmarksvej, not far from the original facility at Systemvej.

Themes introduced or discontinued

  • Town Plan sets were discontinued.
  • Minitalia sets were introduced.
