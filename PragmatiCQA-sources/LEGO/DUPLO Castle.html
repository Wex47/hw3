Castle

DUPLO Castle is a DUPLO subtheme that lasted from 2004 to 2008 . It was similar to the System Castle theme, but aimed at a younger audience. It consisted of boy-and-girl oriented lines. The boys line, just known as Castle, was based on knights and dragons, while the girls line, Princess Castle , was based on princesses. In 2014 , it was re-released.
