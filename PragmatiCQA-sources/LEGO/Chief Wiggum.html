Chief Clancy Wiggum is a minifigure released in 2014 as a part of The Simpsons theme .

Background

Clancy Wiggum is an obese and incompetent Springfield Policeman. His son is Ralph Wiggum .

Appearances

  • 71005 The Simpsons Series
  • 71016 Kwik-E-Mart

Video Game Appearances

  • LEGO Dimensions

Notes

  • He is the only Minifigure to have a Megaphone Piece with two colors on it.
  • This is the first time the baton has been featured in grey.
  • Wiggum is voiced by Hank Azaria who also voiced Moe Szyslak , Carl Carlson , Snake Jailbird , Apu Nahasapeemapetilon , Professor Frink and Comic Book Guy in addition to the older version of Milhouse Van Houten in the episode "Hardly Kirk-ing".
