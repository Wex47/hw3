Piglet is a DUPLO Figure that appears in the Winnie the Pooh theme. He is a small pink pig. He also stutters a lot. He is a bit of a "scaredy cat" among the others, and is the smallest out of the group. He is Winnie 's best friend. He was later released as a minifigure in the LEGO Ideas Winnie the Pooh set in 2021.
