3051 Blaze Attack is a Ninja set released in 1999 . The set includes 143 parts and 2 red ninjas .

Description

Blaze Attack is a medium-sized Ninja set. It is made up of two ninjas, each with two "Dragon-Carts". The first one, which is smaller, is handheld. It has two wings and breathes fire, and rolls on two wagon wheels. The other " Dragon Cart" is larger and is ridden by a Ninja. If you take the top part of its head off, you will find three crystals inside. The part of its head above its mouth raises slightly. On the bottom is a cannon and a pair of halberds. It has a pair of wings and claws, and rolls on four wooden wheels.
