Overview

The theme was released as part of Star Wars in 2014 to coincide with the release of the Star Wars: Rebels TV series. Ten sets and one product collection have been released so far in the subtheme.

Notes

  • Unlike Star Wars: The Clone Wars , the minifigures in the Rebels subtheme have regular eyes instead of the larger cartoon eyes.
  • Sabine Wren, Agent Kallus and the Inquisitor were the only main characters who were not released in the first wave. However, they were released in 75090 Ezra's Speeder Bike , 75083 AT-DP and 75082 Tie Advanced Prototype respectively in 2015 .
  • Many of the characters, creatures, and vehicles introduced in the TV Show have not been represented in physical LEGO form (in the subtheme of Star Wars Rebels , at least). Notably, Quarrie , who was introduced in Rebels , later appeared in a LEGO Star Wars: The Freemaker Adventures set.
  • Additionally, some characters, creatures, and vehicles that have appeared in other Star Wars themes have not had Rebels versions released, while some Rebels characters have had multiple variants that have not been released.
