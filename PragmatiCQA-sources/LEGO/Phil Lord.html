The LEGO Movie

Phil Lord is a minifigure that appeared in The LEGO Movie in 2014 . Lord is the co-director of The LEGO Movie along with Chris Miller . He and Chris are seen celebrating with the Master Builders and citizens of Bricksburg at the end of the movie.

Notes

He, Chris Miller and Shaquille O'Neal are the only minifigures in the movie based on real people who are still alive.
