Class 4

Power Functions

Years

2008 - Current

Subtheme of

TECHNIC

Power Functions (abbreviated as PF ) is a TECHNIC -based system of electronic components, including motors, lights, remote control units and receivers or different power units. These parts can be connected by cables with brick-shaped connectors. The system was first released in June 2007 , in the sets 4957 Ferris Wheel , 4958 Monster Dino and 8275 Motorized Bulldozer .
