Background

Ralph Wiggum is the son of Chief Clancy Wiggum . He is developmentally challenged and says random things. He is often seen in odd scenarios and is the odd man out among the school yard.

Notes

  • His minfigure holds a Valentine card that says "I Choo-Choo-Choose you", which was featured in the episode "I Love Lisa". Lisa Simpson gave him the valentine because she felt sorry for Ralph and this made him develop a crush on her.
  • Originally, Moe Szyslak was going to be in the Minifigure series, but due to the fact that he is a bartender, he was replaced by Ralph.
  • Wiggum along with Bart Simpson and Nelson Muntz were voiced by Nancy Cartwright.
