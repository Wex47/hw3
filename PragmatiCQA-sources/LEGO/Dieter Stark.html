Dieter Stark is a character who appears in LEGO Jurassic World .

Background

Dieter Stark was a cruel and bloodthirsty hunter who never really used guns, but tasers instead. Dieter was hired by Peter Ludlow along with his fellow hunters, led by Roland, were hired to capture dinosaurs off Isla Sorna. He was constantly antagonized by Compsognathus during his time on the island and managed to capture one on the game trail. After the destruction of John Hammond 's team's mobile lab RV, Dieter was one of the hunters in the group that was staring at Roland after he saved Sarah Harding from not reaching the cliff. After that, Dieter got into a fight with Nick van Owen . During a break in the day, Dieter went to urinate in the woods, encountering Compies again and got lost in the forest. After a brief chase down the river, Dieter was finally killed by the compies.

Trivia

  • Instead of getting killed by compies in LEGO Jurassic World , he is eaten by the female tyrannosaurus rex during the camp invasion.
  • Peter Stormare (Dieter's portrayer) reprised his role as Dieter in LEGO Jurassic World .
