Class 3

This is a list of LEGO themes , or lines of LEGO sets, according to their official categorization.

This article is an attempt to create a list of all lines of LEGO sets, commonly called "themes," such as "LEGO Town" and "BIONICLE" and the years they were introduced and removed. This does not include years where a certain theme was dropped and brought back the next year.
