Cho Chang (Chinese: 張秋 ) is a Ravenclaw minifigure who first appeared in LEGO Harry Potter: Years 1-4 and LEGO Harry Potter: Years 5-7 .

Description

Cho has a black hair piece, a shirt and blue tie with the Ravenclaw symbol on it, and black pants.

Background

Cho was a Ravenclaw student who also attends Hogwarts and was a part of Dumbledore's Army. Cedric Diggory was her boyfriend before he was murdered by Lord Voldemort . She was Harry Potter's love interest when he was in his fifth year, but they later broke up.

Notes

  • In the first video game she is only seen before and after she is rescued from the black lake by Cedric Diggory and in the Great Foyer once the fourth year begins.
  • She has the same hairstyle as Marion Ravenwood .
  • She seems to have the same hairpiece in LEGO Harry Potter: Years 5-7
  • In LEGO Harry Potter: Years 1-4 , she can be obtained by going to the Owlery and going to the second floor and playing all the records on the Gramophone. You have to find the disks that are hidden in the room.
