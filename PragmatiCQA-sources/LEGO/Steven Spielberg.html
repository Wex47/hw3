Steven Spielberg is a minifigure based on the real-life film director, screenwriter and producer of the same name. He is seen at the ending of " Raiders of the Lost Brick " along with George Lucas . Spielberg also appeared as a playable character in LEGO Jurassic World .

Notes

  • The Director minifigure appearing in many Studios sets is based on Steven Spielberg. It looks similar to this, but with yellow skin colour instead of light nougat.
  • Steven Spielberg is shown wearing the hat titled "E.T. II" in spite of the fact that the film was canceled.
  • In LEGO Jurassic World, Spielberg is able to throw golden minifigure statuettes similar to the Academy Award or "Oscar".
