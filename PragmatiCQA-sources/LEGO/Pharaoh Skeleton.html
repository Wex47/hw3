Notes

  • Pharaoh Skeleton is in LEGO Racers shown as a part of a trap (yellow brick) with three white bricks. There are green flames under the skull of Pharaoh Skeleton when unused and when the rider touches it, the skull will start circling around him with a weird sound and start to spoil his riding by reversing his steering so the player can't drive normally for a few seconds. If someone bumps into the cursed driver (affected by this trap), the affected player will start either driving backwards, or driving at a very high speed.
