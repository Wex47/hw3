3102 Men's Sportwear is a Scala set that was released in 1998 .

Description

The set consists of several pieces of clothing, and a 4X4 baseplate. There are two sets of clothes. One is made up of a pair of bluejeans and a green T-shirt with a "C" emblazoned on it, and the other polka-dotted blue shorts, and a plain blue T-shirt. The set also comes with two pairs of shoes, which can be directly connected into the baseplate, which features a unique moulded flower design. A clothes-hanger is also included.
