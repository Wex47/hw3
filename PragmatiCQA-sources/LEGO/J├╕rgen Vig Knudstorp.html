Jørgen Vig Knudstorp is the Executive Chairman of The Lego Group and former CEO of the LEGO Group , from October 2004 - 2007 . He took over from the previous owner Kjeld Kirk Kristiansen , who still owns the company, but does not run it. This is because LEGO is a family-run business, and Kjeld's grandfather first started up the company, so the ownership was passed onto his son. Jørgen Vig Knudstorp joined the LEGO Group in 2001 .

Jørgen in Cartoon Form

Jørgen regularly appears in his own cartoon strips, on the popular LEGO blog .
