3900 Bracelet is a Scala set released in 2001 .

Description

The set consists of a string of orange flower-shaped designs, looped into a bracelet. Various objects are attached into the holes in the centre of the flowers, by attaching a flower-shaped round stud brick on the inside part of the bracelet, connecting it through a hold with a regular stud piece on the outside. Various objects (three transparent pink dishes, and three transparent clear flowers, in total) are the attached to the studs, giving the bracelet decoration.
