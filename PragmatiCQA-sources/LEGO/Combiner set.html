Class 3

Combiner Models are models that can be created by combining the parts of multiple other sets. Many have been released, either as individual sets, promotionaly, or as specials at certain stores, while many others by buying the necessary sets. Some of the earliest combiner models were part of the BIONICLE theme, such as the Matoran Nui (see right), and the various Toa Kaita.

As time passed, combiner models became more common. Many of these were released in LEGO Magazine, some in the magazine as instructions, and some through Club Codes on LEGO.com. More, such as the Zivon , were released exclusively as Brickmaster combiners. There are many other online models that users can obtain the instructions for. Nowadays, all combiner models are available exclusively online, with none of them being available as separate sets or physical instructions.
