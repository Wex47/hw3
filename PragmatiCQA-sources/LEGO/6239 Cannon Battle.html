6239 Cannon Battle is a 2009 Pirates set. It was one of the first sets released upon the revival of the theme, and contains a spring loaded pull-back cannon, a breakable wall, a pirate and a Imperial Soldier .

Details

The soldier is armed with a sword and has a fire torch which can also be inserted in the canon, while the pirate has a rifle and a treasure map. The pirate actually has a dark green headcloth as seen on the box to the right, not sky blue as in the other picture. The wall consists of several loosely connected segments (with tiles) in order to fall apart when fired upon.
