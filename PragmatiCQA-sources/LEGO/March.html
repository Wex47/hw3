March is the third month of the year in the Gregorian Calendar. It has 31 days.

Events

  • March 20 , 1999 - LEGOLAND California opened for business.
  • March 22 , 2011 - LEGO Star Wars III: The Clone Wars is released.
  • March 20 or March 21 , 2012 - Sets 9561 Kai ZX and 9567 Fang-Suei are released.
  • March, 2014 - Mixels is set to be released.
  • March, 2014 - 70810 MetalBeard's Sea Cow is released to the public.
