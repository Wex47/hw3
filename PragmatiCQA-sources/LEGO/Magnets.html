Uses

LEGO magnets are used in themes such as M-Tron , LEGO Trains , and in refrigerator magnets.

Notes

  • Starting January 2012 , mainstream magnet sets that include Minifigures had them glued to their base.
  • Starting April 2013 , mainstream magnet sets that include Minifigures had them glued to circular baseplates, and shipped with only one magnet instead of three.
