3100 Girl's Sportswear is a Scala set released in 1998 .

Description

The set consists of assorted articles of clothing and accessories. The first set of clothing is composed of red pants, a filmy pink skirt, and a red long-sleeved shirt printed with white polka-dots. The second set of clothing is made of a pair of bluejeans, and a short sleeveless green top printed with flowers. Two pairs of shoes are contained in the set (one white, one orange), as well as two watches (one red, one orange), a small orange blanket or towel, and a 4X4 white baseplate with a raised flower design instead of studs.
