Black Falcon Knights also known as Falcon Knights are higher-ranking Black Falcon Soldiers . There are various different Black Falcon Knights, who have appeared in various sets . A newer, heavily armored Falcon Knight appears in a 2012 Kingdoms set and two knights with remade classic torsos appears in 2021 Ideas set.
