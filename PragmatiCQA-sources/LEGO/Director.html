The Director is a minifigure introduced in the Studios theme. He wears a black cap, white shirt, orange vest, and black trousers. He is also seen at the ending of " Raiders of the Lost Brick ", with flesh colours and an "E.T. II" logo in his cap. He appears next to the George Lucas minifigure .

The figure is a caricature of Steven Spielberg , featuring his trademark beard and glasses, who lent his name as endorsement on the first Studios set (and to the foreword of the accompanying booklet), 1349 LEGO Studios Steven Spielberg MovieMaker Set .
