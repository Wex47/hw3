Class 4

Action Wheelers

Years

2000 - 2001

Subtheme of

DUPLO

Action Wheelers was the DUPLO version of Race . It began in 2000 , but was discontinued in 2001 , due to Race being discontinued. A total of nine sets were released. It was a subtheme of DUPLO Toolo .
