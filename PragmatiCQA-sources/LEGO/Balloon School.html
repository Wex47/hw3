Balloon School is an attraction in the Traffic area of LEGOLAND Windsor .

Ride description

The ride consists of six gondolas which go into the air and rotate round the centre. Guests can increase height by pulling the string.

It was created by .

LEGOLAND description

This is a description taken from LEGOLAND Windsor. Do not modify it. ( )

Experience the ups and downs of a hot air balloon ride. Seated in a LEGO® themed gondola, guests pull on the "gas valve" cable to increase altitude, whilst the ride rotates at a constant speed.

Anything Else I Need To Know?

  • Guests under 1.3m height must be accompanied by an adult
  • Q-Bot's are available for this ride
  • 1 adult can accompany up to 3 children.
