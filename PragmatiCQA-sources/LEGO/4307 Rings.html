4307 Rings is a Scala set released in 1980 . It contains 15 pieces.

Description

The set includes four black rings, which can be customized with the various included tiles. The tiles are as follows: One black with white printing and a purple polka-dot, one black with purple printing and a white polka-dot, one printed with a flowery bracelet, one white printed with pure red, one black printed with pure white. Two whites printed with red hearts, one black printed with white, and two purple hearts set next to a purple dot; another, white one printed with a flowery bracelet (the difference being that this tile is circular); one white printed with a large red flower, and one black printed with white, and a circle of purple hearts.
