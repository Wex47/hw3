Imperial Transport Pilots are Star Wars minifigures introduced in 2018 .

Description

Imperial Transport Pilots are a class of Imperial Troops which did not appear in any of the films, only appearing in the Hyper-Reality Experience Star Wars: Secrets of the Empire. They appear to resemble Imperial Hovertank Pilots .
