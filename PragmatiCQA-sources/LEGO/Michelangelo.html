Michelangelo is a Teenage Mutant Ninja Turtles minifigure released in 2013 . He is one of the three main protagonists of the TMNT franchise. He also appeared as a cameo character in The LEGO Movie .

Description

Michelangelo's head is a new mould made for the turtles. It is bright green with an orange bandana around it. In 79100 Kraang Lab Escape he has an excited smile, but in 79104 The Shellraiser Street Chase he has a determined smile with his tongue sticking out he has an excited smile. He wears a new turtle shell piece made exclusively for the Teenage Mutant Ninja Turtles line. It is tan, has a turtle shell pattern, and a brown strap over the piece. The front of Michelangelo's torso piece is green with turtle scales and a continuation of the strap piece from the shell piece. The back of the torso features turtle scales and a brown strap at the bottom. His legs are green, just like the rest of his body, and finish off the scale printing. The legs have printing of brown knee pads.

Background

2012 Animated

Michelangelo was formerly, along with his three, older brothers, a normal turtle, who was bought in a pet store by Splinter , when he was a human named Hamato Yoshi. Yoshi then saw many strange men ( The Kraang ) meeting. Those men carried a mutegen and when confronted by Yoshi, it was dropped onto the turtles spilled cage and Yoshi. The mutagen mutated Michelangelo and his three brothers into larger, more intelligent mutants, while turning Splinter into a giant rat. He then hid out in the sewers for 15 years, until the four turtles persuaded Splinter to let them go up to the surface. In the show, he is one of the main protagonists alongside his three brothers. He is the one who loves to party and goof off.

Film

The LEGO Movie

In The LEGO Movie , Michelangelo is one of the Master Builders of the LEGO world. He is among the Master Builders that meet in Cloud Cuckoo Land. Vitruvius distinguishes this Michelangelo from Michelangelo Buonarroti by pronouncing their names slightly different. The turtle's name is pronounced normally, with a long "I" as in "Mike", while he pronounces the artist's name with a short "I" as in "Mick". He was captured by Lord Business, but is freed and aides the LEGO World from the glue and Micro Managers . In a flashback, Michelangelo is a member of MetalBeard 's Master Builder crew in a raid on the Octan Tower. As Michelangelo was seen in Cloud Cuckoo Land, it can be assumed that he was among those who escaped the Octan Tower.

Notes

  • He and Donatello are a brighter shade of green than Leonardo and Raphael .
  • In Ninja Turtles: The Next Mutation , he is voiced by the late Kirby Morrow, who also voiced Cole , Anakin Skywalker and Cyclops .
  • In the 2012 TMNT series, Michelangelo is played by Greg Cipes who also plays Beast Boy in the original Teen Titans series and Teen Titans Go! .
  • He is an unused character in The LEGO Movie Videogame due to licencing issues with Nickelodeon. He can also be found in the files of The LEGO Movie Videogame and made playable again.
    • He uses his variant from 79104 The Shellraiser Street Chase . Though in the movie, his variant is from 79122 Shredder's Lair Rescue .
    • He also lacks a character icon if made playable again.
