The Islanders were introduced in 1994 during the Imperial Guard era of the theme, and briefly brought back in 2001 . They were loosely based on natives of the Pacific islands, and introduced a third faction into the theme. The Islanders were considered to be neutral, but were known to have pirate and Imperial prisoners. They have also appeared in LEGO Battles as an ally of the pirates. In UK catalogs they were known as the Indigo Islanders .

The Islanders were based on their Enchanted Island , and had a smaller outpost at the Forbidden Cove . They used no firearms, unlike the other two factions, but instead used bows, arrows, and spears, as well as traps. All sets were released in 1994, but only one set, Enchanted Island, was re-released in 2001.

Island Warrior , an Islander minifigure, was featured in the Series 11 minifigure set, released on September 1 , 2013 .
