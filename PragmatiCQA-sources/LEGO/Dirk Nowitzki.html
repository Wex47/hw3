Dirk Nowitzki is a LEGO Sports minifigure released in 2003 . He is included in the set 3562 NBA Collectors . He is based upon the retired Basketball player of the same name.

Notes

  • Other than NBA Player 10 , he is the only minifigure of the Basketball subtheme to have a hairpiece.
