8594 Jaller and Gukko is a BIONICLE Titans set released in 2003 . It featured the Ta-Matoran Jaller and a Gukko bird. Combine with 8593 Makuta and 8595 Takua and Pewku to make Makuta Nui .

LEGO.com Description

This is a description taken from LEGO.com. Please do not modify it. ( )

Jaller searches from the sky!

Jaller, Captain of the Ta-Koro Guard, has never flown a Gukko bird before. But he has to learn in a hurry so he and his friend Takua can find the Seventh Toa! Use the trigger on the rear of the model to make the Gukko's wings flap. Combine this model with #8593 Makuta and #8595 Takua and Pewku to build the awesome Makuta Nui!

Notes

  • Turning the gear on Jaller's back will make his arms move.
  • Jaller is the same character as Jala. LEGO was forced to rename the character after a lawsuit from the Maori people.
