Description

The Bracelet is made up of 38 pieces. It includes white "Scala Jewelry Plates" which can lock into each other to form a circle shape, allowing the white tiles also included in the set that are printed with Butterflies, Birds, and Wreaths to decorate the Bracelet and can be easily swapped out by removing the tile and adding the new tile.

Notes

  • This set is very similar to 308 Bracelet , the only difference being that it is yellow instead of white , and has different printings on the tiles.
