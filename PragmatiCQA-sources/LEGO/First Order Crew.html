First Order Crew is a Star Wars minifigure that was introduced in 2015 in .

Description

The First Order Crew is equipped with the standard First Order black tunic, a signature helmet, and a blaster pistol.

Background

First Order crew were crew members of the First Order. They were responsible for repairing ships, as well as other day to day tasks. They were also responsible for coordinating ships, and clearing debris off landing pads to ensure the safety of the ship and pilot.
