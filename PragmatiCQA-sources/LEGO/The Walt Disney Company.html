Class 4

The Walt Disney Company

Years

1999 - Present

The Walt Disney Company , named after its founder and commonly known as Disney , is a famous television and film company, popular for the Disney, Pixar, Pirates of the Caribbean films, Marvel comics and films, and Star Wars . It is one of the two largest media groups worldwide, the other being Comcast. Disney was the first licensed LEGO toy, starting back when the wooden toys were produced. The only product at this time was Wooden LEGO Pluto . Disney products did not appear again until 1999 and lasted until 2000 . In 2010 , it was renewed again with many new subthemes and sets spanning both System and DUPLO . Recently, Disney has started its streaming service with the name of Disney+.
