Hanne Christiansen was the second daughter and third child of Godtfred Kirk Christiansen , who was the son of Ole Kirk Christiansen (founder of LEGO) and the CEO of LEGO from 1957 to 1979. She had an older sister, Gunhild Kirk Johansen , and an older brother, Kjeld Kirk Kristiansen , who will become the CEO of LEGO from 1979 to 2004.

Death

in October 1969 , Hanne and her father, Godtfred were driving home from a movie in the neighbouring town of Give. Their car skidded off and hit a tree. Godfred was able to survive with serious injuries, however Hanne was killed.
