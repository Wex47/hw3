Baby

Years

1983 - 1987 , 1989 - 1990 , 2000 - 2001 , 2004 - 2006

Subtheme of

DUPLO

Related themes

QUATRO , Primo

Baby was a theme of DUPLO from 1987 until its discontinuation in 1990 . It came back in 2000 and finally was dropped in 2006 . It was aimed for children aged 0 - 3 (Toddlers). It was replaced briefly by Primo for 1996 . It used the same bricks Primo used.
