Swords are weapons that are among the most commonly used elements in many popular themes, including Pirates , all of the Castle themes, and many modified versions are found in BIONICLE and Knight's Kingdom II .

It has been found in many forms such as: a scimitar, a greatsword, a serrated blade, a crudely shaped sword, a beast-fang-like sword, and a longsword.

Notes

  • The Ninja katana can come in two styles- with a square hilt or an octagonal hilt.
  • The Sword of Triton is a unique pirate sword. It has a bejewled, gold large hilt and a straight blade.
