QUATRO

Related themes

DUPLO , Baby , Primo

LEGO QUATRO was a theme in preschool series introduced in 2004 and discontinued after 2006 . It was designed for children age 1 to 3.

QUATRO bricks, hence the name, are designed to be twice the dimensions of LEGO DUPLO bricks. The name "QUATRO" also signifies that the blocks are quadruple the dimensions of the traditional LEGO System blocks. QUATRO bricks are compatible with DUPLO and System bricks. These pieces were baby-friendly, and were specially designed to endure teething. In addition, they are the largest-scale elements ever created.

The logo of QUATRO is an elephant which is the same as the old logo of Primo , but in a different colour. It was first released under LEGO Explore package, and then in LEGO QUATRO packaging.

Years

2004

2004 was the first year for QUATRO starting off with 4 sets (5355-5358), all which include a plastic bucket to contain, store, and build from and had a lid that was a building plate to start creations on.

  • 5355 Small QUATRO Bucket
  • 5356 Medium QUATRO Bucket
  • 5357 Large QUATRO Bucket
  • 5358 QUATRO XL

2005

During 2005 QUATRO packaging was changed to boxes instead of buckets, an option that would later be carried on to 2006 . The first QUATRO vehicle was released in 5363.

  • 5361 QUATRO 50
  • 5349 QUATRO 100
  • 5363 Mountain Climber

2006

In the last year QUATRO brought out 3 sets, and the only QUATRO Figure included in 5470.

  • 5470 My First QUATRO Figure
  • 5476 My First QUATRO Set
  • BAG14 Bags of Bricks

Notes

  • The word " QUATRO " means four in Spanish referring to the size.
