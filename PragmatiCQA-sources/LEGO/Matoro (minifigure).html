Matoro is a minifigure form of Matoro , a great BIONICLE warrior. He has been released in both Toa Inika and Toa Mahri forms. A similar version of his Inika variant without movable arms appears in the 2006 Voya Nui Board Game.

Notes

  • His Toa Mahri variant has the head mold that should have been used on Toa Mahri Nuparu's minifigure .
