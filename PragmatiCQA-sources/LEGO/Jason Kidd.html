Jason Kidd is a Basketball player. His number was 5 with New Jersey(now Brooklyn). He was then traded to Dallas and later signed by New York. He then retired and was hired as the head coach of the Brooklyn Nets. In 2014, he got hired as the coach of the Milwaukee Bucks in 2014. He got fired as the head coach of the Milwaukee Bucks in 2018. He's currently an assistant coach for the Los Angeles Lakers.
