1788 Treasure Chest is a Pirates set released in 1995 . The set includes 154 pieces and four minifigures , two Islanders , King Kahuka and one unnamed islander, and two Renegade Pirates , Captain Ironhook and Gonzo Goldbar . It also features a Canoe and a Crocodile . The set is one of the last to be released with the Islanders in it.

Notes

  • This set was also known as 1788 Pirate's Chest.
