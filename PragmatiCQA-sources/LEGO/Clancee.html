Clancee is a Ninjago minifigure introduced in 2016 . He is a Serpentine member of the Sky Pirates, the villains in season 6 of Ninjago: Masters of Spinjitzu .

LEGO.com Descriptions

This is a description taken from LEGO.com. Please do not modify it. ( )

Clancee is a nervous, peg-legged Serpentine who occupies the lowest rung among the Misfortune’s Keep crew. All he ever wanted was to be a pirate, but he tends to get seasick and airsick…and both are in the job description.

Description

Clancee shares his green and brown legs-one of which is a peg leg-with one of the Sky Pirate Foot Soldiers . His green epaulettes also came with versions of Doubloon , Flintlocke , and the Saxophone Player ; the former two also had versions that shared Clancee's shoulder armor. His printed torso is unique, while his green head was previously molded in green with red printed eyes from Chop'rai and Kapau'rai ; the piece was later molded in purple again for Eyezorai .

Appearances

  • 70594 The Lighthouse Siege (with shoulder armor)
  • 70603 Raid Zeppelin (with epaulettes)

Notes

  • He could be half Anacondrai and half Venomari, making him a hybrid.
  • He is the first serpentine to have a stutter.
