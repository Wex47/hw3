The LEGO® Story is an animated documentary based on the story of how Ole Kirk Christiansen and his son Godtfred Kirk Christiansen started The LEGO Group . The story starts in the year 1932 and ends in the year 1968 , before returning back to present day. It was released on August 10 , 2012 to celebrate LEGO's 80th anniversary.

Notes

  • It is one of few LEGO-related media to include expletives, as the shopowner on the England Ferry says "Damn this industry!".
