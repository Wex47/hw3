1887 Rocket Detector ( also known as Scout Patrol Ship ) is a Blacktron II set released in 1992 in 1891 Bonus Value Pack . It includes a small black, open spacecraft and a Blacktron II astronaut minifigure .

Description

The Rocket Detector is a small Blacktron Future Generation space ship. It has two wings at an angle from the "cockpit" with a light on each one. It has a laser directly in front of the ship. There is a control panel for the pilot and three thrusters behind the ship.
