Momo the Flying Lemur is an animal who appears in the Avatar: The Last Airbender theme. He is Aang's pet throughout the series, and was introduced in the third episode of Book I: Water.

Description

Momo is made of a single part. He has legs and arms moulded into his body, and a brown face with very large ears attached to it.

Notes

  • Momo is voiced by Dee Bradley Baker , who also voices Captain Rex , Commander Cody , Commander Fox , and other Clone Troopers in Star Wars .

LEGO.com Description

This is a description taken from LEGO.com. Please do not modify it. ( )

Momo Momo, Aang's companion, is a small creature called a flying-lemur, discovered at the ruins of the Southern Air Temple. He can fly on bat-like wings but otherwise behaves pretty much like a monkey. With his big round eyes and long ears, he has great senses of hearing and smell, but is also a bit mischievous.
