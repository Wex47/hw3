Newt Scamander, is a playable character in LEGO Dimensions Year 2. He is a member of the Ministry of Magic and a Magizooloigist. He is the author of the book " Fantastic Beasts and Where to Find Them ", which can be found in the library at Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry . He is the love interest of Porpentina Goldstein .
