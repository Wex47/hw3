Gunhild Kirk Johansen was the first born daughter of Godtfred Kirk Christiansen . She did not follow her brother Kjeld Kirk Kristiansen into the LEGO company but is co-owner (with Kjeld) of Kirkbi AG, the Swiss holding company that owns the patents and intellectual property of the LEGO Group . Her husband Mogans Johansen was on the LEGO board of directors from 1977—2007.
