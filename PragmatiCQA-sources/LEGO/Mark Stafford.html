Mark John Stafford (born September 9 , 1971 ) is a set designer for The LEGO Group , most notably for themes such as Exo-Force and Ninjago .

Biography

Mark Stafford was a LEGO fan as a child and later as an adult. He began work as a LEGO set designer in October 2006 .
