Town Jr. was a subtheme of Town that was introduced in 1997 . Its sets featured especially simplified building styles and designs . Most of Town' s elements, like police and firefighters, were absorbed into Town Jr. which was eventually discontinued and succeeded by City Center in 1999 . In 2002 one of its sets was re-released.
