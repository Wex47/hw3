Kingdoms is a Castle sub-theme, that was the direct successor to the Castle theme from 2007 . It was introduced in mid- 2010 and discontinued in early 2012 , and the next Castle theme was Castle (2013) .

Description

The Kingdoms theme takes place in a medieval time period with sets in a European style from the 15th century. Unlike the more fantasy-themed Castle from 2007 , Kingdoms is based off of two human kingdoms fighting each other, the Lion Kingdom and the Dragon Kingdom . The Lion Kingdom is led by the King ; the Dragon Kingdom's leader is unknown, although sometimes implied to be the the Dragon Wizard . Additionally, the presence of the Falcon Knight implies the existence of other factions, such as the Black Falcons .
