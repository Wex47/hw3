6704 Space Mini Figures is a minifigure collection for the Space theme, released in 1991 . It includes one Space Police I Officer , two M-Tron Astronauts , two Blacktron Future Generation Astronauts , and one Blacktron Astronaut .

Description

The set also comes with enough accessories to equip all of the minifigures. The equipment includes a metal detector, a walkie-talkie, a loud hailer, gripper arm, a jet pack (the new version from Blacktron II) and a combination of a wrench and a jack hammer. One of the M-Tron astronauts wears an old jet pack piece instead of air tanks.

Notes

  • Blacktron Astronaut is the only minifigure of that theme released after 1990 .
