Dino Safari is an explorers ride. Opened in 2005 alongside the Dino Dipper (Now called Knight's Quest ). in Adventure Land at LEGOLAND Windsor .

Ride Experience

The ride consists of getting in LEGO jeeps and going round a track, seeing dinosaurs and other models, including a man eating plant.

LEGOLAND description

This is a description taken from LEGOLAND Windsor. Do not modify it. ( )

Go back to prehistoric times on the Dino Safari. Set off in a camouflaged jeep and explore an ancient jungle full of animated LEGO brick dinosaurs.

Anything Else I Need To Know?

  • Must be a minimum of 0.9m to ride.
  • Q-Bot's are available for this ride
  • 1 adult can accompany 1 child.
  • Guests under 1.3m height must be accompanied by an adult
