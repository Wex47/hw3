Description

Ferris Wheel

Mounted on the tiles are bricks and slopes leading up to an arch attached to a turning mechanism to turn around a triple axle connected to long sticks holding three seats that can fit in minifigures.

Ice Cream Shop

To the left of the building is a quadrupled rounded brick serving as a stand, slopes serving as indented walls, leading up to an arch with bright red and medium blue slopes attached. To the right is a slope following up to a sign and a flag, then going downward is an arch leading back toward the tiles. The shop will also introduce a new chocolate ice cream piece.

Minifigures

All the minifigures included are either redesigned, or just another variation, such as SpongeBob SquarePants which features a new face printing, Patrick which features a new printed head and torso, and Sandy Cheeks which features a new torso, and a new colour of legs. The Ice Cream Vendor is exclusive to this set.
