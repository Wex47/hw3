3122 123 Marie is a Scala set that was released in 2001 . It includes a Marie doll , a skateboard, and some clothing.

Description

The set consists of a yellow skateboard, printed with a swirling purple and blue design (and black pawmarks), as well as Marie, who wears bluejean overalls with a single shoulder loop, orange shoes, and a light blue shirt with white sleeves. Her hair is tied up with a yellow rubber-band. A large prurple jacket is also included.
