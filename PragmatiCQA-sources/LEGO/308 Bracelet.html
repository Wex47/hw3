Description

The Bracelet is made up of 38 pieces. It includes yellow "Scala Jewelry Plates" which can lock into each other to form a circle shape, allowing the yellow tiles also included in the set that are printed with Flowers, Apples, Numbers and Stripes to decorate the Bracelet and can be easily swapped out by removing the tile and adding the new tile.
