Mickey Mouse is a figure from the Mickey Mouse and the Disney's Baby Mickey themes . He appeared in all eight sets of the themes. He was introduced in the year 2000 , with four different variations. He also appears in the Mickey Mouse & Friends theme represented by DUPLO bricks. He was later released as a minifigure in 2016 .
