Uri is the 195th chapter of Akira Amano 's Katekyo Hitman Reborn!

Synopsis

Uri has just turned into a leopard . Gamma sends his Black Foxes against Gokudera, but Uri blocks their charge. With their Box Weapons evenly matched, Gokudera and Gamma continue their fight. Gokudera loads his Sun Cartridge and fires his Sun Flame -enhanced Storm Bullets, but Gamma blocks them with a Lightning Flame shield. Unfazed, Gokudera continues his assault and loads his Lightning Tube , unleashing his Flame Thunder attack. Gamma uses Shot Plasma , but Gokudera dodges it, loads his Cloud Tube , and fires his Cloud and Storm Flame propagating laser. As Gamma is hit, he has a flashback of Uni and his past...
