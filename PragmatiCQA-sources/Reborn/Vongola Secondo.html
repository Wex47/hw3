Vongola Secondo was the second sucessive boss of the Vongola Famiglia, inherited from Giotto . He is known for his unique Dying Will Flame , the Flame of Wrath . It is said that during his reign, he was the fear of the underworld because of his Flame's incredibly destructive power.

Character Outline

Personality

As his personality has been compared to Xanxus , it can be inferred that he was impulsive and brash. And since Demon decided to follow him, it can be assumed that he was also merciless to his enemies and won't accept any weakness within the Vongola.

Appearance

Secondo has long spiky black hair that is tied to a slim ponytail. He wears a maroon two-piece suit with an olive dress shirt. Secondo has green eyes, forked eyebrows, and V-shaped sideburns.

Weapons and abilities

Secondo was stated to have unrivaled combat abilities and an intensity which Xanxus also shares.

  • Flame of Wrath - Vongola Secondo was the only Vongola Boss that did not use a weapon, but instead an ability called the "Flame of Wrath." It was said to be the most destructive Flame among all of the Vongola Bosses. When using it, he wields no supporting weapon and instead only shoots his Flames out from his hands.

Trivia

  • According to Xanxus , it was because of Secondo's power that Giotto moved to Japan, out of fear of facing the new boss's anger himself. Demon Spade , however, later states that this is false, claiming that it was he who forced Giotto to step down despite being one of his Guardians.
  • He has often been misnamed 'Ricardo' in the fandom, however, canonically and officially his name has never been revealed.
