Julie Kato is a member of the Simon Famiglia . He previously attended Simon Middle School , but he and his fellow Guardians transferred to Namimori after an earthquake hit their area. Julie was possessed by the first generation Mist Guardian , Demon Spade , to challenge the Vongola Decimo & Guardians , but was freed from Demon Spade's control after his defeat.

Appearance

Like most members of his Family, Julie is always seen in his Simon school uniform. He keeps the uniform top opened, revealing a yellow shirt, which seems to be barely tucked in and buckled with a brown belt. The features that set him apart from the others is that he wears a white fedora, which he has on in almost all his appearances. When his fedora is taken off, he reveals a somewhat combed-backed hair and sideburns that end just below where his cheekbone is. He wears large-frame glasses over his slightly slitted eyes. Finally, he sports a petit goatee. As a child, Julie lacked his goatee and wore a hat turned backwards in lieu of a fedora.

Personality

Julie comes off as a laid-back, nonchalant person who moves at his own pace, and is constantly seen with a smile on his face. He seems to prefer being outside, wandering about than following his duties. His most obvious personality trait is that he is a slacker. In his first appearance, he missed an entire day of school. His laziness brings out the disapproval of Kouyou Aoba , and his comment about Adelheid Suzuki hints that he has gotten into trouble sometime in the past. According to Kouyou's comments towards Julie during their meeting, he seems to have a habit of gambling and chasing after women. Another trait is that he can be blunt and honest-saying whatever is on his mind. When Kouyou made a comment on Julie doing nothing all day, Julie retorted by telling him to shut up and then insulted him on his failing marks. He also did not hesitate to call Enma Kozato , the boss of the family, pathetic. Moreover, he had no reservations on admitting he was stalking Chrome Dokuro and, when he met up with her, he immediately flirted with her and doesn't really care when Chrome rejected him.

His personality can also be described as being the total opposite of Adelheid.

While she is committed to her Famiglia and her duties, Julie is apathetic, making him the deviant of the Family. Despite coming off as incredibly playful, he can quickly change attitudes should his freedom be jeopardized. In this case, he becomes aggressive and threatening enough to stop Adelheid from giving him orders without any retaliation. According to Adelheid, Julie was never like this in the past, though the threat was due to Demon Spade's possession of him.

Previously commented on by Adelheid, Julie was a more affable man in his past. Despite being as carefree and frivolous as he is now, he had much more respect for his family and admitted that he deeply cared for Adelheid and the other Famiglia members. He went on to say that he would put his life on the line for Adelheid and the others.

Weapons and Abilities

  • Desert Simon Ring : Julie possesses the Desert Simon Ring that was sealed by the Vongola Sin. When activated, it turns into a weapon that covers his hand. It closely resembles a set of claws.
