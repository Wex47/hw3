Two Fights is the 121st episode of the Katekyo Hitman Reborn! anime.

Synopsis

Yamamoto runs into the Millefiore Black Spell member Nosaru once again. Meanwhile, Tsuna, after a long battle, defeats the mysterious "Ultimate Cube." Yamamoto in his battle manages to push back his enemy, and Merone Base is moved once more.
