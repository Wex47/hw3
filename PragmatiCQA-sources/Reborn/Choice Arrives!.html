Synopsis

Byakuran tells the Vongola that for the upcoming fight, they have to bring their non-Vongola friends. They agree to take all of them with the exception of the weakened Lal Mirch , who, despite protesting, is still not brought along. On the day of the fight, both Mafia Famiglias compete in a game called Choice in which the selected fighters have to defeat the opponent's main target to win the game. For the Vongola, the participants are Tsuna , Gokudera and Yamamoto , while the non-guardian participants are Spanner and Shoichi Irie , the latter being Millefiore's target. The Millefiore's participants are Kikyo , Saru (actually a disguised Genkishi), Torikabuto , and Deisy , the latter being the Vongola's target. When it starts, Tsuna confronts Mist Funeral Wreath Torikabuto and is able to overpower him with his Vongola Box Weapon . Yamamoto is attacked by Genkishi, who is disguised as one of Torikabuto's subordinates, Saru. However, Genkishi is unable to defeat Yamamoto who has improved his skills with help from Squalo , and defeats Genkishi with his dad's 8th form of the Shigure Soen Ryu , Pelting Rain , stating that the Shigure Soen Style was flawless, completely invincible, and second to none.
