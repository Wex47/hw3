Behind the scenes information

Manga Debut

Chapter 286
The Pesca Famiglia is a Mafia family that sent one of their assassins to eliminate the Vongola Decimo .

Plot Overview

Inheritance Ceremony Arc

To interfere with the Inheritance Ceremony , the Pesca Famiglia sent one of their assassins to attack Tsuna. The assassin used Cloud Flames to enlarge his own body. As Tsuna went into Hyper Dying Will Mode , he defeated the assassin while the assassin was distracted by Nuts . According to Reborn, the assassin was in Hyper Dying Will Mode using an alternate version of the Dying Will Bullet .

Trivia

  • Pesca means "fishing" or "peach" in Italian.
