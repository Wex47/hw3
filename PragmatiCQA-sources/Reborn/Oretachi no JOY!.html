Oretachi no JOY! is a character song duet by Yamamoto's and Gokudera's voice actors, Suguru Inoue and Hidekazu Ichinose . It is the cover song of the album Oretachi no JOY! , along with ...loop and Minna Suki Daze . It is also included in the compilation album Katekyo Hitman Reborn! Official Character Song Collection - Ultimate CD Box .
