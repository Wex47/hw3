What You Believe In is the 91st episode of the Katekyo Hitman Reborn! anime.

Synopsis

With no escape routes available, and Mukuro Rokudo being in a weakened state, Mukuro explains to Chrome Dokuro that it is up to her to win the fight. Drawing power from both her trust in Mukuro and the Vongola Ring , Chrome's illusions become far more effective, to where even Glo Xinia can't tell whether or not they are real. The fight soon concludes in a victory for Chrome, and the ten-years-later Ryohei Sasagawa is introduced at the very end of the episode.
