No Control is the character song of Shoichi Irie , sung by Toshiyuki Toyonaga . It is included in the album Perfect World/No Control with Perfect World . It is also included in the compilation album Katekyo Hitman Reborn! Official Character Song Collection - Ultimate CD Box .
