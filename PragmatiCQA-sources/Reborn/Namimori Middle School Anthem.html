Namimori Middle School Anthem is the school hymn of Namimori Middle School . It is sung by Hibird's voice actor, Rie Tanaka . The song was included in the compilation album Katekyo Hitman Reborn! Official Character Song Collection - Ultimate CD Box .

Trivia

  • Namimori Middle School Anthem was requested on the school radio, presumably by Hibari .
  • Hibari's ringtone is set to this.
  • Hibird always sings this song in his appearances.
  • This song was sung in ReboCon 3 as an encore song.
  • The full version of the Namimori Middle School Anthem is not heard in the entire anime series.
