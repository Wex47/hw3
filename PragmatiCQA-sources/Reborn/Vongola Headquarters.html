Vongola Headquarters is the home and base of operations of the Vongola Famiglia . Located in a lightly wooded area in Italy, it is the official residence of the Vongola Bosses and their Guardians.

Varia Arc

The Headquarters was stormed by the CEDEF , led by Iemitsu Sawada , in an attempt to confirm the status of the Vongola Nono, Timoteo , due to the Boss' uncharacteristic sanctioning and non-interference in the Ring Conflict. During this siege, the lower levels of the Headquarters were revealed.

It was later revealed to be the venue of the Cradle Affair , which led to Xanxus being frozen in ice.

Future Arc

The Headquarters was destroyed by the Millefiore Famiglia 's siege. The residents', especially that of the Vongola Nono's as well as his Guardians', status was unknown.
