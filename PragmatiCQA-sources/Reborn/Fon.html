Character Outline

Appearance

Fon looks similar to Kyoya Hibari and Alaudi , though his hairstyle differs from theirs in that he has a long braid. A doll resembling him would later be given to I-Pin by Ryohei. Early in the series, he wore a disguise that comprised of purple clothes and dark glasses with large, round lenses. He is otherwise usually dressed in red.

As an adult it's revealed he has a Chinese dragon tattoo that wraps around his left arm where the dragon head ends on his left peck.

Personality

He is a calm and fair person, normally seen with a slight smile. He is also kind and polite to everyone he speaks to, always greeting first while bowing. He is not one that is easy to be angered or provoked, facing people who have short temper with patience and remain polite to them while trying to give his advice, though some people like Mammon misunderstood his intention as him looking down on them. Fon cares for the well-being of his student, I-Pin, and adores her, but never spoiling her, trying her best to give her valuable lessons so she can grow.

Abilities

Fon is a martial arts champion with extremely high hand-to-hand combat level and claimed to be able to move as fast as microns. Aside from combat, he's also knowledgeable of medical aid as shown when he scanned Basil's wounds.

  • Gyoza-Kempo : This attack sends a wave of garlic straight to the enemy's brain.
  • Exploding Lotus Kempo : This attack unleashes a red Storm dragon that has the Storm characteristic of Disintegration .
  • Exploding Gale Fist : A fighting style used by Fon that is said to be invincible.
  • Exploding Dragon Fire Dance : This technique unleashes a red Storm Flame dragon, similar to the Exploding Lotus Kempo, but stronger and bigger.

Trivia

  • While his name is officially spelled as Fon, the Mandarin romanization of Fon's name is spelled as Fēng. The traditional Chinese is the same as the Kanji, but the simplified Chinese reads 风 instead of 風.
  • Fon knows 107 martial art styles other than the Gyoza-Kempo.
  • Fon's favorite food is Mapo Tofu, which he can't eat since he was turned into a baby because it is spicy, which makes him choke and tear up.
  • His favorite drink is oolong tea.
  • In a recent strength poll, Fon ranked 14th.
  • Fon, Hibari, and Alaudi have the same appearance and voice actor. Though, Fon's hair is much longer than the other two's.
  • Fon inferred that he and Hibari were related, but changed the subject saying that Hibari would get angry if Fon explained the relationship.
