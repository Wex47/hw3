Character Outline

Appearance

He has black hair, is normally seen smiling, always wears a suit, and wears glasses. He is shown with a white tuft of hair in the Future Arc. He was first seen when Dino was tutoring Kyoya Hibari , and became fast friends with Tetsuya Kusakabe .

Plot Overview

Daily Life Arc

Romario is first seen in a flashback with Dino's Father . After that, he is briefly seen every now and then alongside Dino when a problem arises or an event that requires Dino to be composed occurs.

Varia Arc

Romario is momentarily seen accompanying Dino for the Ring Battle.

Future Arc

Romario is mostly seen drinking with Kusakabe. When Tsuna and the others came back from the Choice game, Hibari leaves because he hates crowds, and Kusakabe and Dino follow them. When Dino tripped from the stairs because his men weren't there, Romario came to Dino a few seconds later and is seen having liquor to drink with Kusakabe. Dino tells Romario to follow him.

The Curse of the Rainbow Arc

Romario is seen greeting Tsunayoshi Sawada as he comes home, ushering him in the house. Later, Romario is seen in the city, pulling Dino up from the ground and telling him that they were staying in the same hotel as the Varia .

In Chapter 409 , the final chapter, he makes a cameo alongside Dino and Enzio .

Trivia

  • In the Card Games Romario is attributed to have Rain Flames .
  • Romario is almost always the subordinate that Dino has around to stop him from being so clumsy.
  • In the future, Romario and Dino trained, so that now, Romario or one of Dino's subordinates can now be 1km away from him without him being clumsy. It is shown that this still needs refinements as Dino still falls down the stairs when Romario is just mere meters away.
  • He appears dining in a restaurant during Gokudera's past story flashback. The reason is unknown.
  • Yamamoto calls Romario, "Old Timer."
