Oretachi no Yakusoku is a character song duet by Yamamoto's and Gokudera's voice actors, Suguru Inoue and Hidekazu Ichinose . It is included in the album Mamorubeki Mono/Oretachi no Yakusoku with Mamorubeki Mono . It is also included in the compilation album Katekyo Hitman Reborn! Official Character Song Collection - Ultimate CD Box .
