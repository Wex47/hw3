Super Gravita Black Hole is an ability used by Enma Kozato . By increasing the gravity of his gravity sphere, Enma can cause them to collapse in on themselves and create a black hole. These produce much more gravity than the aforementioned spheres.

Enma can also create a black hole in front of his own body with more power than the ones emitted from the gravity spheres.
