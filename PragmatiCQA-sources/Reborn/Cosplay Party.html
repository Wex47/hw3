Cosplay Party is one of the character songs of Reborn , sung by NEEKO . It is included in the album Cosplay Party/Onemu Uta , with the Onemu Uta . It is also included in the compilation album Katekyo Hitman Reborn! Official Character Song Collection - Ultimate CD Box .
