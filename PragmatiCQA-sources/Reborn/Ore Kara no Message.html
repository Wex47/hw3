Ore Kara no Message is one of the character songs of Reborn , sung by NEEKO as Arcobaleno Reborn and Ken Narita as Reborn's True Form. It is included in the album Hitotsu Dake/Ore Kara no Message , with Hitotsu Dake . It is also included in the compilation album Katekyo Hitman Reborn! Official Character Song Collection - Ultimate CD Box .
