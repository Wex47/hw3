Manga Debut

Chapter 401
The Condensed X-Burner is a more focused version of Tsuna's Oath Flame X-Burner . In order to limit the damage of the X-Burner to a smaller area, while keeping its usual destructive power, Tsuna utilizes the Earth Flame 's Gravity Manipulation in order to pinpoint the technique on just his target and minimize the usual damage caused by the technique on the surrounding areas.
