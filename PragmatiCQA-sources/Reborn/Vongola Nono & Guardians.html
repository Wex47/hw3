Overview

Led by Timoteo , the Vongola Nono, half the members of this generation appear to be beyond their fifties, and it is possible that they are the longest-lasting generation.

Timoteo sought out their successors, the Vongola Decimo & Guardians , who are to succeed them sometime in the future.

The Guardians seem to be well-prepared for unexpected enemy attacks, as they quickly gathered around their Boss during the attack on Inheritance Ceremony and performed Difesa A Circolo .

Trivia

    • Coyote Nougat refers to nougat .
    • Brabanter Schnitten refers to a traditional Austrian dessert, brabanter biscuits.
    • Nie Brow Jr. refers to brownies .
    • Visconti refers to biscotti .
    • Ganauche III refers to ganache .
    • Croquant Bouche refers to croquembouche .
