4 Opened Boxes is the 223rd chapter of Akira Amano 's Katekyo Hitman Reborn! .

Synopsis

The battle between Rasiel and Olgert and Belphegor and Fran begins. Rasiel unleashes multiple Pipistrello Tempesta , Bel's Visone Tempesta hops onto his shoulder, and Olgert sends out one of his Elefante Forte Pioggia . Mink uses his Tail Whirlwind and blocks the elephant. When Bel asks Fran why he didn't send out his Box Weapon , Fran replied that he needed to decide on a pose before opening his Box Weapon such as "both hands to the front, raise left hand, raise right hand too, haaaa, OPEN!" Fran finished his thought by telling Bel he couldn't raise his arms because of his hat. Annoyed, Bel says that he'll beat Rasiel without Fran, but his left ear and his face suddenly explode in a burst of blood. Mink and Fran suffer the same fates, and they all fall to the ground, defeated.

The Heavy Rain Elephant crushes a few Varia members and Olgert sends out two more to perform the Elefante Triplice . The Elephants fly towards the castle. Squalo sees them and tells Lussuria to defend the castle, but it's too late. The Elephants obliterate the castle. While Rasiel laughs maniacally about the Varia's death, a voice from the castle ruins tells him to shut up. The voice's owner is seen reclining on a chair, and a Flame of Wrath lights up the darkness. Xanxus appears, truly ticked off and ready to kill Rasiel.
