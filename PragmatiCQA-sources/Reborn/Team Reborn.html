Team Reborn is the Sun Arcobaleno Reborn 's representative team, being formed to fight for Reborn in the Representative Battle of the Rainbow .

On the fourth day of the Representative Battle, Tsuna defeated the last remaining Boss, Jaeger of Team Bermuda , declaring victory of the Representative Battle of the Rainbow.
