Frozen Rain is an attack that utilizes hardened Rain Flames . This was first seen when Ugetsu Asari battled Takeshi Yamamoto to test his resolve. As a sign of his considerable power, Asari revealed that he made up both this attack and Kuzuryu Kawakuzure on the spot during the trial.
