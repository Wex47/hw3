The Reverse One Ton Mallet is an item that only Reborn has used. In the manga, the mallet forcibly deactivates the person hit with the mallet's Dying Will Mode .

When hit, the target's Dying Will Bullet in their head is forcibly ejected. After the mallet is used, the target will think that their experience in Dying Will Mode was just a dream. However, in the anime, the mallet is just used for comedic purposes. The mallet weighs "one ton," but Reborn can swing it around as if it were as light as a feather.

Trivia

  • In the anime, Leon uses his shapeshifting abilities to become the mallet, but in the manga, the mallet is a separate entity.
