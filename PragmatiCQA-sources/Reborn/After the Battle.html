After the Battle is the 177th episode of the Katekyo Hitman Reborn! anime.

Synopsis

After Tsuna and his friends escape from Byakuran with the help of Mukuro Rokudo , Reborn visits Lal Mirch and updates her on the events happened so far about Choice . As Lal hears every word, Reborn states that now it was up to them to protect Uni from Byakuran and protect the Future.
