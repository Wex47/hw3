Versus Alejandro is the first battle that occurred during the fourth day of the Representative Battle of the Rainbow , comprised of the Allied Team members Tsunayoshi Sawada , Enma Kozato , and Basil against the Team Bermuda member Alejandro and his puppets Ginger Bread .

Prelude

Alejandro was originally after Team Mammon , but they are revealed to be decoy puppets . Tsuna, Enma, and Basil then ambush Alejandro.

Synopsis

Tsuna's group explains to Alejandro that they knew that if the three teams waited in separated places, Team Bermuda would split into three groups that consists of Bermuda and Jaeger confront Reborn to hear his answer, two Vindice confront Team Verde, and only one will go after Team Mammon because Team Mammon was the most wounded out of the remaining teams. Therefore, Tsuna's group decides to defeat Team Bermuda one by one.

Alejandro, however, easily overpowers Enma's technique and announces that he did not come alone. Alejandro inject flames into the ground, summoning two beings from the coffins he carries around. The two beings were puppets of the magician's doll Ginger Bread and another version of him which is a more larger size. Alejandro explains that he had been in Vindice almost as long as Bermuda and created dolls of Ginger Bread to meddle into wars and destroy Famiglias themselves from within in secret. Ginger Bread begins to attack, but failed to injure Tsuna's group because Tsuna, Enma, and Basil conjoined their attacks in a sequence. With that attack, Alejandro was distracted by it and failed to protect his battler watch, which Tsuna destroyed.

Aftermath

After defeating Alejandro, the trio flee the battlefield to eliminate the Vindice attacking the decoys of Team Verde .
