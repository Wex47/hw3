Canvas is the sixteenth ending of the Katekyo Hitman Reborn! animated series by Artland. The song was also sung by Tsuna's and Reborn's voice actors, Yukari Kokubun and NEEKO , in Katekyo Hitman Reborn! Official Character Song Collection - Ultimate CD Box .
