COMSUBIN is an Italian combat force and stands for "Commando Raggruppamento Subacquei Ed Incursori Teseo Tesei." COMSUBIN's members are combat experts and extremely skilled with traps and guns. In Colonnello's Trial of Combat , he sent a huge rock that explodes and sets up some more traps too; however, Reborn states to Tsuna that he hasn't seen anything yet, with Lal Mirch adding on that he was just playing with them, as COMSUBIN members didn't use those kinds of tricks, giving a hint as to what COMSUBIN members were capable of.
