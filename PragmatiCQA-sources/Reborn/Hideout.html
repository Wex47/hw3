Hideout is the 75th episode of the Katekyo Hitman Reborn! anime.

Synopsis

Tsunayoshi Sawada and Hayato Gokudera venture with Lal Mirch in the woods. They come across a dangerous machine called a Moska . The future Takeshi Yamamoto defeats the robot and leads them into the hideout where they find out the truth and the secrets. There, Tsuna meets Reborn .
