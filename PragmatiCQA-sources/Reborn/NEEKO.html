Isuzu Nishihagi ( 西萩 五十鈴 , Nishihagi Isuzu ) , more commonly known as NEEKO ( ニーコ , Nīko ) (born October 28, 1983 in Hyogo Prefecture, Japan) is a Japanese voice actress, voicing Reborn for the anime adaptation of Katekyo Hitman Reborn! and its subsequent video game releases and cameos.

NEEKO has also reprised her role as Reborn in Katekyo Hitman REBORN! the STAGE from 2018 until the present.
