Character Outline

Appearance

Ganauche III has brown hair on the back half of his head and white hair in the front. He wears the suit and tie outfit commonly seen amongst Vongola members. Additionally, Ganauche appears to be relatively younger than the other 9th generation Guardians.

Personality

He seems rather light-hearted and amiable, since he was the only one amongst Timoteo's Guardians to be amused when Tsunayoshi Sawada called Timoteo "grandpa", and tousled Tsuna's hair afterwards.

Plot Overview

Inheritance Ceremony Arc

He first appeared along with Timoteo and the other guardians when they arrived at Japan, and are staying at hotel. At the hotel, Ganauche and Coyote Nougat discussed about the Giegue Famiglia , who had already found the whereabouts of the Family that intended to sabotage the Inheritance Ceremony. They say that the Giegue Family will wipe out the culprit, as a gift for the Tenth Boss. The Ninth's Guardians later appear at Tsuna's house and escourt him to the hotel, where the Ninth is staying. Timoteo greets Tsuna, and comes to the decision that Tsuna is too kind to be a mafia boss, but he is the only one that can change the corrupted Vongola to its original purpose, a vigilante group. When Tsuna says that he needs to think about it, Ganauche escorts him out of the hotel. The next day, Tsuna tells the Ninth that he has decided to not be a boss. Timoteo accepts his decision, but Croquant Bouche interrupts their meeting, and says the Tsuna has emergency phone call from Hayato Gokudera . Tsuna than rushes to the hospital, and Ganauche asks the Ninth's opinion. At the Inheritance Ceremony, he defended Tsuna and Timoteo when they realized the enemy have moved to take the "Sin," and was surprised, like everyone present, when he found out that the Simon Famiglia was the one who attacked them.

Weapons and Abilities

Equipment

  • Gun : Ganauche used a gun, but it was destroyed by a member of the Simon Famiglia.

Abilities

  • Scudo Fulmine ( 雷シールド ( スクード・フールミネ ) , Sukūdo Fūrumine ) (lit. Lightning Shield): An electromagnetic barrier created by his Lightning Flames .
  • Difesa A Circolo : A defensive circle created by him and the other five guardians with the Flames of their Rings.

Trivia

  • As with the other 9th generation guardians, Ganauche's name comes from a dessert of some kind. In his case, "Ganauche" comes from "ganache," a glazing, filling, or icing for pastries made from chocolate and cream.
