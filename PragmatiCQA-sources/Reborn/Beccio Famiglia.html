The Beccio Famiglia is a Mafia Famiglia allied to the Vongola Famiglia . Its members wear formal suits. They have a good relationship with the Vongola and seem to obey them due to the difference in size, as shown in Mafia Land .

Trivia

  • Beccio could be a mistranslation of becco , lit. "beak" in Italian.
