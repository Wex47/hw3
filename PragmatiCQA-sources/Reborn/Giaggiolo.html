Giaggiolo is a filler character in Katekyo Hitman Reborn! , making his appearance before the Merone Base Invasion when he, like Ape , attempts to find the Vongola base. He is a member of the Black Spell of the Millefiore Famiglia and is a former Giglio Nero Famiglia member. He has Cloud Flames and, like most future characters, fights using a Box Weapon , the Violet Tornado . Both Lambo and Tsunayoshi Sawada mispronounce his name as "Giolo", much to his dismay.

Plot Overview

Future Arc

Merone Base Invasion

Giaggiolo first appeared when he was looking for the Vongola Hideout. During his search, Future Futa passed him while on his way to fix a camera. While he was doing so, Lambo, who was next to Giaggiolo, told Future Futa to buy him lollipops. Giaggiolo grabbed him to use as bait to reveal the Vongola Hideout. When he did this, Lambo used a hand grenades and they both flew away. When they woke up, Giaggiolo asked him for the Vongola Hideout location. Lambo replied that he would tell him only if Giaggiolo bought him lollipops. Giaggiolo said yes, but he wasn't really going to. Lambo told him the fake location. Tsuna and Futa knew where they were because Lambo was dropping his candy. When Giaggiolo and Lambo stopped for a while, Tsuna and Future Futa managed to catch up with them. When he noticed, he used his Violet Tornado to stop them. Future Kyoya Hibari appears to attack Giaggiolo and easily wins, but it turns out he only fought for Giaggiolo's Cloud Ring, which he took. It is unknown what happened to Giaggiolo.
