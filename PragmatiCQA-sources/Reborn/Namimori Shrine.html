Namimori Shrine

Details

Location

Namimori , Japan
Namimori Shrine is a location within Namimori . Most likely located in the middle of Namimori. Some major events have taken place just outside the shrine.

Appearance

The shrine has the typical appearance of one. It is painted in red in color on the exterior. Since it is built on a hill, there is a long staircase in front of the shrine. The main gate is located at the top of these steps. Its roof is greyish-yellow in colur. The shrine also has some trees and shrubs surrounding it.

Events

  • Viper's Arcobaleno Trial
  • Teleportation device of Choice was located here
  • Tsuna and his Family travelling to the future using the Ten-Year Bazooka after the Arcobaleno Trials Arc and Primo Family Arc
  • Hibari, Tsuna, Yamamoto and Gokudera fighting the thieves & watching fireworks afterwards.
  • Used as a disguise for Hibari's hidden entrance to the Vongola Underground Base in the future.
  • Ugetsu Asari's inheritance trial
  • Gokudera and Yamamoto's first fight against Gamma in the future.
