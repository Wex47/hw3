Lambo-san no Yabou is the character song of Lambo , sung by Junko Takeuchi . It is included in the album Gyoza Gyuudon Set no Uta , with the cover song and Ramen Nobichau no Uta . It is also included in the compilation album Katekyo Hitman Reborn! Official Character Song Collection - Ultimate CD Box .
