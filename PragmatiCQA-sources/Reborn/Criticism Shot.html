The Criticism Shot was used for the first time during Tsuna's fight against Mukuro Rokudo . This bullet came from Leon 's cocoon stage after he lost his tail, emerging the same time that the X-Gloves did. The effect of this Bullet is for the target to experience real-time rebukes from people that they know. This allows the internal limiters to be lifted and causes the target to enter Hyper Dying Will Mode . The result of the Hyper Dying Will Mode is to increase the battle sense of the user, and, in a Vongola Boss's case, greatly increases the Vongola Hyper Intuition . This allows them to sense illusions and to understand people (both mentally and physically).

Trivia

  • When Tsuna used it for the first time, his body ached for two weeks because he was not accustomed to the pressure it put on his body.
  • Ingesting two Dying Will Pills will have the same effect as the Rebuke Bullet.
