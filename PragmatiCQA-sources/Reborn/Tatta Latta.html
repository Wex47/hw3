Tatta Latta is a character song chorus by the voice actors of Kyoko Sasagawa , Haru Miura , Chrome Dokuro , Lambo , and I-Pin . It is included in the album RED - famiglia , and in the compilation album Katekyo Hitman Reborn! Official Character Song Collection - Ultimate CD Box .
