Lancia , before he went back to Italy with Basil , gave Tsuna his Boss's Ring .

Tsuna brought this Ring along with him to the future, and later, it protected him from Byakuran 's Mini White Dragon .

It is black with a silver dragon and a small orange circle in the centre.
