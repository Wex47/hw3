Student's Growth is the 106th episode of the Katekyo Hitman Reborn! anime.

Synopsis

To avoid being trapped by Millefiore, Lal Mirch volunteers to distract them. Tsuna volunteers in her place since he is the most mobile with his X-Gloves. The team make their way deeper into the base while Tsuna battles Moskas. He is overpowered and gets dragged underwater.
