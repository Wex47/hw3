Motorcycles , or Airbikes , are modes of transport created by Giannini . They are used by the Vongola Famiglia Choice participants to combat the mobility issues relayed by the 10-km battleground.

Appearance

The Motorcycles are based on real-life motorcycles from popular manufacturers.

  • Tsunayoshi Sawada : Honda 250cc NSR Rothmans.
  • Hayato Gokudera : Harley-Davidson Sportster XL 883L or 883C (883cc) with customized gas tank
  • Takeshi Yamamoto : Ducati Monster S4 (916cc) with Termignoni dual exhaust pipes
  • Ryohei Sasagawa : Honda CBR 400cc RR with customized livery
  • Basil : Unknown design, but it was stated that he practiced with the others, specifically Gokudera.

In the anime, the Airbikes have identical appearances aside from differences in color.

  • Tsuna: White, Blue, and Orange Airbike
  • Gokudera: Red and Black Airbike
  • Yamamoto: Green Airbike.
  • Ryohei: Yellow and Green Airbike.
  • Basil: Blue Airbike.

Description

Giannini stated that the motorcycle was a model that he admired for its racing skills. They run on Dying Will Flames and are internally equipped with Mammon Chains to avoid radar detection. A considerable amount of energy is required to support the power needed to hover. In that respect, Dying Will Flames are more than sufficient.

In the anime, the motorcycles were changed to airbikes, using streams of pressurized air to hover. They also ran on Dying Will Flames, and the entire cowl and engine cover are made of the same material as Mammon Chains. Highly pressurized air shoots out of the nozzles to lift the entire vehicle. Aerial movements also utilize the pressurized air. It has the same principle as a hovercraft. The body of the airbike is made from nano-carbon ceramic to keep it extremely light. It weighs 30% of what a normal motorcycle would. At the same time, it can endure 60% more stress.

Giannini and Spanner customized the Bikes to suit each user. For example, Ryohei's motorcycle is reinforced to handle the stress that he puts on it by constantly driving at the maximum speed (often straight into walls).

The Vongola Members' Adaptability

  • Yamamoto did not take to his motorcycle at first, but since he is the best athlete in the Vongola Famiglia, he has fast reflexes and can adjust to any form of physical activity quickly and steadily. After a few rounds of riding, he was already able to ride flourishingly well.
  • Gokudera was the first to successfully ride his motorcycle, and it seems that he has a natural-born talent for it, claiming riding to be easy. He bases his skill on theory and technique.
  • Ryohei rides his motorcycle fast and hard, without any knowledge of how the bike works, and without any experience. The first few times he rode his bike, he rode it straight into the wall at maximum speed, declaring that "extreme men did not turn." However, since he is never afraid of anything, he got back up from his encounter with the wall without severe injuries, and continued to practice until he succeeded. His bike is reinforced to handle the stress that he puts on it by constantly driving at the maximum speed.
  • Tsuna was the slowest to learn how to ride and was thrown off his motorcycle several times. It took the help and encouragement of all his friends to start the engine and keep practicing. He has the slowest bike out of everyone and is still clumsy when it comes to riding.
  • Basil was on the same page as Gokudera and rode his bike successfully in one try.
