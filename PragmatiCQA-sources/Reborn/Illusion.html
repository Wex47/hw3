Normal Illusions

When the series started, Mukuro Rokudo was the first person who showed the power of illusions . An illusionist uses their psychic powers to make their enemy feel, see, hear, or smell things that aren't there. Basically, every illusion is dealt through the mind. In the case of a first-class Illusionist, it's possible to even kill the enemy with illusions; for example, Chrome Dokuro created an illusion of lava erupting and Viper (Mammon) said that if they had believed it was real for one second, they would have burned. This proves, however, that if the enemy is aware that they are experiencing an illusion, it will have no effect on them and does not exist. Normal illusions take control of fear and force other people to be afraid.

Real Illusions

When an Illusionist uses their illusions with the power of Mist Flames ' Construction characterization, they will create Real Illusions. This would make it possible for their Illusion to be experienced in reality.

During the introduction of Mukuro's First Realm , it was stated that it grants him the power to create Real Illusions, capable of manifesting in the physical/real world. However, it wasn't until during the Future Arc that Real Illusions were seen. The difference between Real Illusions and normal Illusions is difficult to distinguish. Real illusions can also physically harm people.

Mukuro also made use of this to materialize himself during the Mist Ring Battle to fight Mammon, when fighting Glo Xinia in Kokuyo Land with Chrome in the future with Ken and Chikusa , and during the Choice Arc , when Tsuna and his friends were fleeing from Byakuran .

Illusion battle

When two illusionists are going to fight, the role of an illusion is different. An illusionist will try to enchant his enemy with his illusion (this will mean controlling the enemy’s brain which governs their five senses). The enemy needs to survive by completely recovering this control and casting an illusion that will mirror the other illusion. The first one that has his illusion completely cast/returned by the opponent and be unable to recover their control completely will lose, and then they will be left to their opponent's mercy. For example, Chrome created an illusion of lava erupting, but Mammon wasn’t completely fooled and cast an illusion that the fire/lava would be turned into ice. Chrome, unable to recover, was turned into ice and slammed against the wall by Mammon as a result.

Illusionists

Illusionists, or better known as spellcasters or technique-user﻿s, are the ones that cast illusions or psychic powers into the enemies' mind and thus, controlling their enemies' brain which governs their five senses, making them lose their perception and senses with things that aren't even there. Illusionists in general are said to be weak to physical pain.

Spirit Possession

In conversation during chapter 336, it was hinted that illusionist have abilities to possess people; when Mukuro's body was possessed by Demon Spade , Reborn said that he for the first time heard of "an illusionist to have their body stolen by another illusionist while possessing someone." However, so far, the only people capable of performing this feat were Mukuro and Demon. This might be because it needs specific conditions necessary for it to work and even without any specific conditions, it's still an exceptionally difficult technique.

It also seems that if an illusionist cuts off their attachment with their own body, the illusionist will become a living spirit with their illusions becoming stronger from time to time. Demon is, for now, the only one who is known to have done this for years. But to do this means that the illusionist must have a body to possess or else its spirit form will gradually disappear and the illusionist will finally die. How much power the illusionist would gain is dependent on the vessel they are possessing. According to Daemon, this action was considered taboo.

Vindice

Only three illusionists have the power to fool the Vindice; Fran and Mukuro Rokudo are two of the known three; however Viper , in their released form, has shown that they too are capable of using illusions powerful enough to fool the Vindice, though it was stated that the same trick would not work a second time.

Rings

  • Normal Rings : Normal Rings can produce a Mist Flame to construct the Illusion into a real Illusion. The higher the quality of the Ring, the more power an Illusion will have.
  • Hell Rings : Hell Rings are Rings that can create an Illusion more powerful than an Illusion with a normal Ring. However, while powerful, it is said that the rings bears curses that can consume the illusionist's soul.

Trivia

  • Normal Illusions are illusions that the Illusionist puts in the mind of one person, while Real Illusions are illusions that are put on reality and have an actual existence and mass.
  • The Mist and Desert Flames, the Illusions Flames of the Dying Will Flames of the Earth and Sky , are said by Demon to be the "joker" Flames of Illusions.
