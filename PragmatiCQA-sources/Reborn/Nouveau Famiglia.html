The Nouveau Famiglia is a gangster-style Mafia Famiglia that hails from New York. They have a good relationship with the Vongola Famiglia , respecting them due to the difference in size, as shown in Mafia Land .

Trivia

  • 'Nouveau' literally translates to 'new' in French and means modern or fashionable. It's also short for nouveau riche which refers to people who have recently acquired wealth, and are typically perceived as ostentatious or lacking in good taste.
