Manga Debut

Chapter 392
Jaeger is the Vindice who appeared before Giotto and Simon Cozzato after the two agreed that the Simon Famiglia would disappear from the Mafia world after the betrayal of Demon Spade . He claims to be a Vindice shining like Bermuda von Veckenschtein , and is now partaking in the Representative Battle of the Rainbow as his team's boss. He is said to be the strongest Vindice warrior.

Plot Overview

Inheritance Ceremony Arc

Jaeger appears to Giotto and Cozzato and announces to them that he would be overseeing the bond between the Simon Famiglia and the Vongola Famiglia , and later oversees the battle between the Simon and the Vongola. Later, he entrusted the task of subduing Demon Spade to Tsuna and his Guardians . When they finally subdue Demon, Jaeger shows them an eighth memory: a memory of Giotto and Cozzato's faith in their descendants.

The Curse of the Rainbow Arc

Jaeger, along with several other Vindice, including Jaque , suddenly appeared and defeated Team Skull in a matter of seconds on the second day of the Representative Battle of the Rainbow . Jaeger was in the lead, and Bermuda von Veckenschtein was on his shoulder. Jaeger took Enma's boss watch and became the Boss of Team Bermuda . During the third day of Representative Battle, Jaeger and Bermuda observed the battle between their team against Team Reborn and Team Verde.

After Reborn and Tsunayoshi Sawada are taken by the Warp Hole, it is revealed that the Arcobaleno , The Fated Day and the Representative battle are all a cycle in which Checker Face chooses the next Arcobaleno. Jaeger reveals that he himself was a past Arcobaleno and that if Checker Face succeeded, Reborn and the other Arcobaleno's bodies would turn out the same way. After Bermuda and Jaeger reveal their goal to kill Checker Face at the cost of their own lives and the current Arcobaleno, Tsuna declares that he won't let them win the Representative Battle. Angered, Jaeger proceeds to injure Tsuna. Reborn declines Bermuda's invitation to join the latter's team and chooses to follow Tsuna. At this answer, Jaeger engages Tsuna in a battle, claiming that they won't let them leave with that answer. Jaeger easily overpowers Tsuna with one attack and is stopped by Bermuda before delivering the final blow. Bermuda explains that he only wants to make it clear that Tsuna won't stand a chance against his team. Bermuda then tells Reborn to consider his offer once again and leaves.

At the fourth day of the battle, he and Bermuda confront Reborn to hear his answer. Reborn refuses and Bermuda tells Jaeger to kill them if necessary. Jaeger attacks Hayato Gokudera , only to find out that he's only a puppet. Tsuna, Takeshi Yamamoto , and Dino turn out to be puppets also. Reborn explains that all the remaining teams have joined forces to defeat the Vindice. Realizing their plan, Jaeger suggests that Bermuda should go to Big Pino and Small Gia , but they are stopped by Dino, Mukuro Rokudo , Superbia Squalo , Xanxus , and Byakuran , who were hiding behind the special coat specially made by Verde , Shoichi Irie , and Spanner .

Byakuran attacks Jaeger with his White Dragon , but Jaeger easily defeats it and then cuts off Xanxus's arm in an instant. Jaeger proceeds to attack Squalo next and stabs through his chest. Jaeger carries on, easily stabbing Byakuran and slicing through Xanxus's legs. After confronting and destroying Spanner's Green Moska , Jaeger takes down Dino and Mukuro.

When Tsuna arrives, Jaeger attempts an attack from behind using his Short Warp , only to be blocked by Mukuro and Hibari. Jaeger stabs their shoulders, but Mukuro uses his Illusions to seal Jaeger's arms to his and Hibari's shoulders, thus preventing Jaeger from moving. Tsuna uses a Condensed X-Burner and it pierces through Jaeger's abdomen, stunning him. After defeating Bermuda, Tsuna destroys Jaeger's Boss Watch and wins the Representative Battle of the Rainbow.

Weapons & Abilities

  • Vindice Chains : Like Vindice in common, Jaeger uses chains as his main weapon.
  • Warp Hole : Jaeger is able to go to different locations in short time by using his Flame of Night to create portals and teleport via the said portals.
  • Short Warp : Jaeger can use his teleportation abilities to instantly warp short distances. This technique is used to move behind enemies or dodge their attacks. He can short warp even part of his body in this way he can attack from different directions or hit a target outside his attack range.
