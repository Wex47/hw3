Katekyō Hitman Reborn! Vongola 77 , or simply Vongola 77 , is a databook containing comprehensive information about the Katekyo Hitman Reborn! series created by Akira Amano . It was released on October 4, 2007.

Summary

Most of the information in the book contains trivial material, but helps the reader better know the series. The book's contents is arranged in chronological order, based on the story, the characters' profiles included, making it easier for the reader to remember events and the order of appearance of each character in the manga.

Vongola 77 summarizes the first three story arcs of the series – the Daily Life Arc , the Kokuyo Arc , and the Varia Arc .

Page types

There are three types of data pages with a 'how-to read' guide.

Event page

  1. Event number.
  2. Date when it happens.
  3. Level of the incident based off of Reborn's judgment. Out of five.
  4. Icon.
  5. Summary
  6. Bonds. The event's witness's opinion.
  7. mini 77. An event that was happening in the background.

Character page

  1. Profile. Basically includes character's age, birthday, astrological sign, blood type, height, weight, country of origin. And then miscellaneous things that vary from person to person. Things like favorite food, things they dislike, the music they like, what they want to be in the future, etc.
  2. Personality
  3. Specialty. Things they're good at or a notable character trait.
  4. Daily Life.
  5. Human Relations. Quotes on several people the character has about them. e.g. Tsuna about Takeshi: "Reliable!" or Tsuna about Gokudera: "We're friends! Though he's a bit scary..."

Battle page

  1. Skill.
  2. Weapon.
  3. Mission.
  4. Progress. How strong they've gotten from the experience.

Daily Life Arc

  • Vongola Famiglia bosses and allies, and the Sawada family tree.
  • Profiles of characters that appeared in this arc
  • Character concepts by Akira Amano
  • Sketches and illustrations by Akira Amano
  • Fashion check
  • Lambo teaching Italian
  • Haru's HaruHaru Interview with most everyone

Kokuyo Arc

  • Kokuyo Junior High Gang & Vindice profiles
  • Pictures of battles, abilities, and techniques that occurred and were shown during this arc
  • Dangerous Boy Mitsu... , illustrated by Akira Amano and written by Koyasu Hideaki
  • KenKen Pah~ interview with the Chikusa, Chrome, and Mukuro

Varia Arc

  • Varia & CEDEF profiles
  • Pictures of the Vongola Ring Conflict , abilities, and techniques that were shown during this arc
  • Reborn! manga prototype that was first published in the Weekly Shōnen Jump .
  • A bonus manga chapter
  • Lussuria's Third Street interview with the Varia

Closure

  • List of Futa's rankings
  • Interview with Akira Amano, the interviewer being Mukuro
  • A short manga scene of the Future Arc
