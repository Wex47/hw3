Another Sky is the 140th episode of the Katekyo Hitman Reborn! anime.

Synopsis

Xanxus returns Bester to his box, warning Rasiel that when he releases Bester, he will be finished. Xanxus demands Sil to call Byakuran. Sil refuses and attacks Xanxus with his Storm Bats. Xanxus releases Bester and kills Sil. Tsuna and the others receive news that the battle in Italy was successful. At that moment, past Ryohei arrives. Shoichi opens up the White Capsule containing the ten years later version of Tsuna and his Guardians and gives Tsuna the Vongola Box from his future self.
