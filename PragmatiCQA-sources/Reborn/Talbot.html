Appearance

His age is evident in his physical appearance. He has no hair except a small clump on the crown of his head and wears a black covering over his eyes. He wears simple, tattered robes and a necklace made of claw-like ornaments.

Personality

He is rather friendly, generous and helpful. However, he is also come across as eccentric, poking Tsuna with his stick out of curiosity even though knowing he is injured.

History

Talbot is a craftsman who has been working for the Vongola since Primo's generation. Giotto once requested him to make golden pocket watches for him and his guardians as proof of their eternal friendship. Talbot has been researching the mysteries behind the Arcobaleno and is quite knowledgeable about Bermuda von Veckenschtein and the Arcobaleno Pacifiers . It is unknown how he is able to live for as long as he has, and what he had done during that time.

Plot Overview

Inheritance Ceremony Arc

After the Vongola were defeated by the Simon, Talbot appears and offers to fix the Vongola Rings . He states the risks of such an endeavor could potentially destroy the rings completely, which Tsunayoshi Sawada accepts. After a bit of work, he brings the rings back in a completely dead-like state. When everyone was down, he asks the extent of their resolve and to light the flames, warning them that a weak attempt would destroy the souls in the rings for good. Tsuna, Hayato Gokudera , Kyoya Hibari and Ryohei Sasagawa all manage to awaken the new Vongola Rings' form, the Vongola Gear .

The Curse of the Rainbow Arc

Talbot is mentioned to have created new Varia Rings , the payment being Mammon's entire fortune, and upgraded Xanxus's Varia Ring to allow his Box Weapon, the Ligre Tempesta di Cieli , to enter Cambio Forma . After the third day of the Representative Battle of The Rainbow , Tsuna met with Talbot and proposed an idea to save Reborn's generation of Arcobaleno.

On the final day of Representative Battle, after Tsuna defeated Team Bermuda , Talbot appears before Checker Face could make the next generation of Arcobaleno. He proposed another and much safer way to protect the Tri-ni-sette without having to sacrifice Arcobaleno. He created special jars that are able to contain the Dying Will Flames of the Sky , using Bermuda's Flame of Night within the jar to accelerate the flames to provide enough power to replace the Arcobaleno Pacifiers.

Powers and abilities

Talbot can hear the spirits of the Vongola Rings and even knows about the Animal Rings . He seems to be well informed, knowing about the blood of Simon Cozzato and the Simon Rings , something that even Timoteo was unaware of.

Talbot is an expert metal craftsman who can even repair the highly damaged Vongola Rings. He is also capable of upgrading Box Weapons to enable them to have a Cambio Forma through the use of Rings , as shown through his Cambio Forma upgrade to the Xanxus's Varia Ring.

Equipment

Underneath his robes, he carries various vials and trinkets that serve an unknown purpose. It is also unknown how Talbot acquired them.

  • Penalty - a small vial containing the blood of Vongola Primo .
  • Moon Rock Fragments ( 月の石のかけら , Tsuki no ishi no kakera )
  • Lævateinn's Hilt ( レーヴァテインの柄 , Rēvatein no tsuka )
  • Hammer - Talbot's hammer was used to repair the highly damaged Vongola Rings.

Trivia
