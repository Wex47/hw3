Hitotsu Dake is one of the character songs of Tsunayoshi Sawada , sung by Yukari Kokubun . It is included in the album Hitotsu Dake/Ore Kara no Message , with Ore Kara no Message . It is also included in the compilation album Katekyo Hitman Reborn! Official Character Song Collection - Ultimate CD Box .
