English

The memories were surely there, of dreams that hindered my hand once... Those had been mine. This tearful face that I wouldn't show anyone, the scars I so treasured... Those had also been mine. So your eyes can get used to the darkness, even if it's only a little bit at a time, go and find what you need. My feelings swayed, swayed on the nights I cried alone. Hurry, hurry, there's still time; wave your arm widely as you go and when you find that treasure, scream out, "it's still not over!" If you don't show up even as I wait, I'll go to you now.
