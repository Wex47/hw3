Katekyo Ondo is a character song duet by Tsuna's and Reborn's voice actors, Yukari Kokubun and NEEKO . It is the cover song of the album Katekyo Ondo . The character songs Tsuna Life and Reborn Blues are included in the album. Katekyo Ondo is also included in the compilation album Katekyo Hitman Reborn! Official Character Song Collection - Ultimate CD Box .
