Reborn Blues is one of the character songs of Reborn , sung by NEEKO . It is included in the album Katekyo Ondo , with the cover song and Tsuna Life . It is also included in the compilation album Katekyo Hitman Reborn! Official Character Song Collection - Ultimate CD Box .
