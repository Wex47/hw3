Character Outline

Appearance

Like many other Vindice, Big Pino's true form is that of an rotting zombie-like corpse. Though, his and Small Gia's are more human-like. However, unlike the other Vindice, he has a bulky build, wears a potato sack with one eyehole on his head, and dons a nondescript, plain article of clothing on his upper body. Once he opened his sack, he's shown to be bald and only wearing a pants with Small Gia's weapons attached around his body.

Personality

Big Pino seems to be unable to form coherent sentences and only mumbles "pipupipipu". He has demonstrated complete obedience to Small Gia 's orders.

Plot Overview

The Curse of the Rainbow Arc

Big Pino obeys Bermuda's orders and separates from the main Team Bermuda to attack Team Verde along with Smorgia , but is shocked to discover the use of the Allied Team 's Decoy Puppet .

Subsequently, Illusions cast by Fran and Chrome Dokuro attack them to stall the Team Bermuda duo, while Tsunayoshi Sawada , Enma Kozato , and Basil head over to eliminate Smorgia and Big Pino.

While this is occurring, Bermuda von Veckenschtein is remotely aware of these facts. Jaeger expresses his desire to assist Smorgia and Big Pino, but is blocked by the five Allied Team members Xanxus , Superbia Squalo , Byakuran , Dino , and Mukuro.

Following Small Gia's order, Big Pino attack the spot where Hayato Gokudera is protecting Chrome and Fran, successfully destroying Sistema C.A.I. 's shields. He attacks again to kill Gokudera, Takeshi Yamamoto , Chrome, and Fran, but they are saved by Enma's Gravity Manipulation . With the arrival of the commanding unit, Big Pino reveals all of Small Gia's weapons that he carries around his body as the latter switches his weapons. He and Small Gia able to destroys Basil's wristwatch, but then caught off guard when Yamamoto and Gokudera interferes that battle. With Enma's ability, his movements are stopped while Gokudera snuck from behind him and then destroys his wristwatch with his dynamites along with Yamamoto destroys Small Gia's wristwatch.

Weapons and Abilities

  • Flame Cannon : Big Pino's weapon is attached to one his arms and can shoot a blast of Dying Will Flames powerful enough to destroy the shields from Sistema C.A.I. and Yamamoto's Shigure Kintoki.
  • Warp Hole : As a Vindice, Big Pino can use his Flame of Night to create portals and teleport via said portals.
