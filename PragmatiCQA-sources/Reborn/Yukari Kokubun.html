Yukari Kokubun ( 國分 優香里 , Kokubun Yukari ) (born February 8, 1983 in Saitama Prefecture, Japan) is a Japanese voice actress, voicing Tsunayoshi Sawada for the anime adaptation of Katekyo Hitman Reborn! and its subsequent video game releases and cameos.

Background

She is known for providing voices for Kaname Oku in Gokujyo: Gokurakuin Joshikōryō Monogatari , Camilla in Holy Knight , and Uru and Zellia in Atelier Iris: Eternal Mana .
