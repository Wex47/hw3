Character Outline

Appearance

Vongola Terzo wears a dark grey double-breasted suit with a white low-collared shirt and black necktie. He has red eyes and is mostly bald with two tufts of black hair shaped like a horn on the top and at the back of his head.

Plot Overview

Future Arc

Vongola Terzo, along with the other Vongola Bosses, manifested during Tsuna's Vongola Trial . After Vongola Primo accepted Tsuna's resolve, the latter was granted .

Weapons and Abilities

Equipment

  • Knife ( ナイフ , Naifu ) – His knife has a short blade with a gold guard with ornate designs and a gold hilt. It can be imbued with Sky Flames . Although his knife fighting skills were never shown, it can be assumed that he was very skillful and powerful in his handling.
