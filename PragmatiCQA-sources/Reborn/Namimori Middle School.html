Namimori Middle School ( 並盛中学校 , Namimori chūgakkō ) is a middle school in Namimori , Japan. It is the primary setting where most of the main events in the Katekyo Hitman Reborn! series occur. Namimori Middle is based on real life middle schools in Japan. Students study for three years, encompassing 7th to 9th grade and is the last instance of compulsory education.

The school opened on February 19th.

Education

The standard subjects are Japanese language, social studies, mathematics, science, a foreign language elective (almost always English), music, fine arts, health and physical education, and industrial arts or homemaking. The school is also required to supply Homeroom, an ethics course, and extracurricular activities. Supplementary classes are also provided for students who failed some subjects, where Tsuna and Yamammoto are regularly attending.

Physical Education is taught by Enomoto (which included swimming class), Mathematics by Dohachiro Nezu , and Homemaking & English is temporarily taught by Dino .

Additionally, Namimori Middle School ran an Athletics Festival.

Uniform

As is the general norm for Japanese schools, there are two sets of seasonal school uniforms, for the summer and winter. These uniform changes occur on June 1st and October 1st, regardless of the weather. Namimori Middle follows the Western-influenced blazer style, but when the school first opened they wore the then common, gakuran -style uniform, before switching out. The Disciplinary Committee follows tradition by wearing the old set.

Standard

Summer uniform

Girls wear a short-sleeved a white blouse, a red bow-tie, and a blue skirt. Boys wear a white button-up shirt, a red or blue tie and blue or black trousers.

Winter uniform

Girls wear a long-sleeved white blouse, a red bow-tie, and a khaki skirt. Boys wear a long-sleeved white button-up shirt, a blue tie, and khaki trousers. Both have options between navy blue sweater vests, sweater cardigans or blazers.

Boys and girls appear to have an optional yellow blazer jacket, presumably for colder weather. It has the middle school's crest emblazoned over the left breast.

Disciplinary Committee uniform

The Disciplinary Committee, the yellow blazer jacket, and vest are replaced by a black gakuran with a red inner lining. They wear black trousers, with the red committee armband pinned to the left sleeve and (excepting the leader) all have pompadour-style hair.

Tetsuya Kusakabe wears a white round-collar shirt under his Gakuran.

Hibari has also been shown wearing a sweater vest with short and long sleeves while foregoing his gakuran jacket.
