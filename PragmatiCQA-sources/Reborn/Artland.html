Artland, Inc. ( 株式会社アートランド , Kabushiki-gaisha Ātorando ) is a Japanese animation studio that made the anime adaptation of Katekyo Hitman Reborn! . Founded on September 14th, 1978, it began its animation of Katekyō Hitman Reborn in 2006 and took an indefinite hiatus of its animation in September 2010. As of 7 July 2017, the studio has closed due to bankruptcy.
