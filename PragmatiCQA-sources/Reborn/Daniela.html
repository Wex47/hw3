Daniela was the eighth generation boss of the Vongola Famiglia , inherited from Fabio . She was the only female Vongola Boss.

Character outline

Appearance

She had black hair, brown eyes and a red flower tattoo on her face. Her hairstyle was similar to Haru's . She wore a red pantsuit with white sleeve cuffs and a matching hair ribbon.

Personality

Not much is known about her personality but according to Giannini , Daniela was a tomboy in her youth and "fought like Athena". She regarded Giotto as her hero, hinting that she might have shared some of his ideals.

History

She was born to Fabio , the Vongola VII. Later in life, she had a son named Timoteo . Through Timoteo, she gained three grandsons and one adoptive grandson ( Xanxus ).

Plot Overview

Future Arc

Daniela appears along with the rest of the previous Vongola Bosses when Tsuna was to undergo the Vongola Trial. While Tsuna was trapped in the hedgehog, his Vongola Ring started to emit violent images that were caused by the Vongola into his head. The previous Vongola Bosses stated that they were the past sins and crimes of the Vongola, and demanded Tsuna, who was the current wielder of the Sky Vongola Ring, to accept the bloody history of the Vongola Famiglia if he wanted to be strong; however, Tsuna refuses to do so and stated that he would rather destroy the Vongola than accept their sins. Vongola Primo and the other bosses accepted Tsuna's resolve as the successor to the Vongola and granted him the proof of the Vongola. Tsuna was then given the as proof that he had earned the Vongola inheritance.

Inheritance Ceremony Arc

During the Inheritance Ceremony, the Eighth appears in a small flashback of the Ninth, where it is revealed that Sin belongs to the Simon Famiglia and that it was the blood of Simon Cozzato ; however, Timoteo states that he had never heard of a such thing before, and that when he received the "Sin" from the previous boss and his mother, Daniela, she told him that "this is war blood that must never be forgotten."
