Funny Sunny Day is the seventh opening of the Katekyo Hitman Reborn! animated series. SxOxU also released an English version of the song with a slightly different meaning. The song was sung by Belphegor's , Lussuria's , and Squalo's voice actors, Koichiro Yuzawa, Yuki Fujiwara , Hiroki Takahashi , in Katekyo Hitman Reborn!
