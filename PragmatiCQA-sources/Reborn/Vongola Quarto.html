Character Outline

Appearance

He has been shown wearing a grey suit over matching trousers, a white tie and a black undershirt. His eyes and hair are both brown and he has sideburns, as well as a goatee.

Plot Overview

Future Arc

Vongola Terzo, along with the other Vongola Bosses, manifested during Tsuna's Vongola Trial . He is the first to speak and one of the few to do so. After Vongola Primo accepted Tsuna's resolve, the latter was granted .

Weapons and Abilities

Equipment

  • Fork : His weapon is a silver fork with the crest of the Vongola Famiglia on the top side. It can be imbued with Sky Flames . His fork fighting skills were never shown, but it can be assumed that he was very skillful and powerful in his handling.
