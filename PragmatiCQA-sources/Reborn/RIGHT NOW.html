RIGHT NOW is a character song duet by Tsuna's and Basil's voice actors, Yukari Kokubun and Yuka Terasaki . It is included in the album RED - famiglia . It is also included in the compilation album Katekyo Hitman Reborn! Official Character Song Collection - Ultimate CD Box .
