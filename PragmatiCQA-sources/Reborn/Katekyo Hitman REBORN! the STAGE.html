Katekyo Hitman REBORN! the STAGE ( 家庭教師ヒットマンREBORN! the STAGE , Katekyō Hittoman REBORN! the STAGE ) is a series of stage plays based on the Katekyo Hitman Reborn! series. It was produced by Marvelous, Pony Canyon, and d-rights.

Stage plays

Katekyo Hitman REBORN! the STAGE

In the 27th issue of Weekly Shōnen Jump 2018, Marvelous announced that a stage play adaptation of the series, named Katekyo Hitman REBORN! the STAGE , will be performed in Tokyo and Osaka in fall. NEEKO will reprise her role as Reborn and Ryohei Takenaka as Tsunayoshi Sawada . From June 4 to June 9, the actors who will portray Hayato Gokudera , Takeshi Yamamoto , Ryohei Sasagawa , Lambo , Mukuro Rokudo , and Kyoya Hibari were revealed on each day respectively. Visual art for the revealed cast was released on June 11.

The first stage show adaptation was performed from September 21, 2018 to September 30, 2018 at The Galaxy Theater in Tokyo, and on October 3, 2018 to October 6, 2018 at Mielparque Hall in Osaka.

It follows the first two story arcs of the series, the Daily Life Arc and the Kokuyo Arc .

Katekyo Hitman REBORN! the STAGE -vs. VARIA part I-

vs. VARIA part I is the second in the stage shows and part one of the adapted Varia Arc . It was performed at the Tokyo Theatre 1010 from June 14, 2019 to June 23, and at the Osaka Kashihara Citizen's Culture Center Riviere Hall from June 27 to June 30.

The first half of the show starts with Iemitsu Sawada returning home after being gone for two years. It shows Basil getting attacked by Squalo and Tsuna's Guardians all getting their individual tutors. Then focuses on everyone's training.

The show then goes into the Vongola Ring Conflict and covers the Sun , Lightning , and Storm Battles, with the Rain Battle as the climax.

Katekyo Hitman REBORN! the STAGE -vs. VARIA part II-

vs. VARIA part II is the third in the stage shows and part two of the adapted Varia Arc . It was performed at The Galaxy Theater in Tokyo from January 6, 2020 to January 13, and at the Umeda Arts Theater in Osaka from January 17 to January 19.

Katekyo Hitman REBORN! the STAGE –SECRET BULLET-

–SECRET BULLET- ( 家庭教師ヒットマンREBORN! the STAGE –隠し弾(SECRET BULLET)- , Katekyō Hittoman REBORN! the STAGE –Kakushi-dan (SECRET BULLET)- ) is the fourth in the stage shows and an adaptation of two stories from the Katekyō Hitman Reborn! Secret Bullet light novels― X-Fiamma and Bucking Horse Stampede . It was performed at The Galaxy Theater in Tokyo from November 7, 2020 to November 15, and then at the Kyoto Theater in Kyoto from November 19 to November 22.

Katekyo Hitman REBORN! the STAGE -episode of FUTURE-

-episode of FUTURE- is the fifth in the stage shows and is a stage adaptation of the Future Arc . Due to the size of the play and cast it has been split into two halves; Zenpen (前編 lit. first part) and Kouhen (後編 lit. latter part), with each having slightly different cast lineups due to the story material covered in each half. Zenpen ran from July 16 to July 22, 2021 at THE GALAXY THEATRE in Tokyo, and from August 6 to August 8, 2021 at Sankei Hall Breeze in Osaka. Kouhen ran from July 27 to August 1, 2021 in Tokyo, and was scheduled for August 13 to August 15, 2021, in Osaka.

Due to COVID-19 concerns, performances on July 27, 2021, were cancelled. It was later announced that James Takeshi Yamada would be briefly replaced by Daiki Miyoshi in the role of Dino while he was being quarantined and tested for the virus. On July 31, 2021, it was announced that James was negative for the virus and would be resuming his role on August 1, 2021. On August 11, 2021, it was announced that an unspecified cast or staff member had tested positive for COVID and as such for the safety of remaining cast, staff, and audience members all the Kouhen performances for Osaka were cancelled.
