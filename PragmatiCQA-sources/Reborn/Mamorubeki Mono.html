Mamorubeki Mono is one of Tsuna's character songs , sung by Yukari Kokubun . It is included in the album Mamorubeki Mono/Oretachi no Yakusoku with Oretachi no Yakusoku . It is also included in the compilation album Katekyo Hitman Reborn! Official Character Song Collection - Ultimate CD Box .
