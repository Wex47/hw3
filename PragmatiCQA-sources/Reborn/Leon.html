Leon is Reborn's shapeshifting animal partner. By transforming into a green gun, he becomes Reborn's main weapon in the Katekyō Hitman Reborn! anime adaptation. He is mostly seen resting on Reborn's fedora.

Synopsis

History

When Reborn was still training Dino to become the boss of the Cavallone Famiglia , Leon created Enzio and Dino's signature bullwhip .

Kokuyo Arc

During the attacks on Namimori Middle School students, Leon's tail falls off and he changes forms uncontrollably. He eventually spins a cocoon, indicating that Reborn's student, Tsunayoshi Sawada , will go through a life-or-death battle.

Leon eventually emerged from the cocoon, along with the newly created X-Gloves in its mitten form and the Criticism Shot .

Varia Arc

Leon created threads that are resistant to Dying Will Flames . These threads were used before the Sky Ring Battle to make clothes for Tsuna that looked like his school uniform.

Choice Arc

Before the Choice battle with the Millefiore's Six Funeral Wreaths , the Flame-resistant threads were used to make black suits for all of the tenth generation Vongola Guardians .

Abilities

Despite his small size, Leon is durable and resistant to strong attacks. He was able to defend against the attacks made by Tsuna and his Guardians and even deal serious damage during Reborn's trial. Leon can also create objects such as threads from inside himself.

Known shapeshifting forms

Leon is able to change his form into anything that he has seen before.

  • Gun - Reborn uses this as his main weapon in the anime. Reborn also uses this to shoot Tsuna with the Dying Will Bullet or the Criticism Shot.

  • Camouflage - Leon is able to transform into his surrounding, and allows Reborn to conceal his presence.
