Kokoro no Hoshi is the character song of Uni , sung by Yoshino Nanjo . It is included in the album Kokoro no Hoshi/Believe in You with Believe in You . It is also included in the compilation album Katekyo Hitman Reborn! Official Character Song Collection - Ultimate CD Box .
