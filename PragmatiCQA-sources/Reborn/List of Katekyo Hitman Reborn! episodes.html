This is a list of all current episodes of the anime adaptation of the Katekyo Hitman Reborn! series by Akira Amano . It began airing in Japan on the television network TV Tokyo on October 7, 2006. It ended its weekly airing on September 25, 2010, with a total of 203 episodes, each are referred to as a Target ( 標的 ( ターゲット ) , Tāgetto (Hyōteki) )
