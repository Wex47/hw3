Unthinkable Crime of Three Siblings is the 69th episode of the Katekyo Hitman Reborn! anime.

Synopsis

The youngest of the Three Criminal Brothers , Saburo Shibaki robs Nana on her way to buying dinner. The Three Criminal Brothers then attempt to rob Nana again but are stopped by Tsunayoshi Sawada and friends.
