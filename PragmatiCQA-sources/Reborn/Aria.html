Aria is the ninth boss of Giglio Nero Famiglia and the second Sky of the last generation of Arcobaleno . In the Future Arc , she was shown to have died due to an illness, likely because the Sky Arcobaleno are cursed with short lifespans.

Character Outline

Appearance

﻿Aria doesn't look very much like her mother and daughter as they do to each other. Unlike them, she doesn't wear the Sky Arcobaleno uniform but in a brief scene from Gamma's flashback she is shown wearing one.

She has shoulder-length hair with parts of it on both sides of her head braided up and tied at the back. She has sharp blue eyes and in the past is shown wearing the Sky Pacifier around her neck. She wears a long-sleeved red shirt with a black necktie, black skirt and black high-heels.

In the future, her appearance hasn't changed at all, but when Gamma last saw her alive, her hair was undone and she wore a white outfit.

Like her mother and daughter, she has the Giglio Nero Famiglia's emblem below her left eye.

Trivia

  • Aria and her mother Luce have the same voice actor.
  • Like all Sky Arcobaleno, Aria possesses the mysterious power of foresight.
  • Aria , lit. "air", is a musical piece for one voice, with or without instrumental or orchestral accompaniment.
