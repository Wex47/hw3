The Animal Rings are Rings that contain the future Vongola Box Weapons ' Box Animals, as they couldn't be brought back to the past by Tsuna and his Guardians . These Rings still retain the right to make noises. After the Vongola Rings were damaged in a battle with the Simon Famiglia , Talbot used the power of the Vongola Box Rings and Penalty to upgrade the Vongola Rings into the Vongola Gear .
