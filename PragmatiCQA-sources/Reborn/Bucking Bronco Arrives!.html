Synopsis

Dino , the current boss of the Cavallone Famiglia , arrives in Japan to meet Tsunayoshi Sawada and to reunite with his former tutor, Reborn . During his stay, his pet turtle, Enzio , incidentally turns into a giant several times, but since he loses his physical abilities when his subordinates are not present, he is always unable to help, though Reborn shoots Tsuna with the Dying Will Bullet to stop it each time. However, Tsuna is injured in the process and is checked into a hospital, where each of his friends cause a ruckus when they visit, annoying both the nurse and Kyoya Hibari , who punished Tsuna for their actions. When Reborn later takes control of a class at Namimori, he ends up attacking the students who answer wrong.
