Hush Before Entering the Hospital is the 17th episode of the Katekyo Hitman Reborn! anime.

Synopsis

Tsuna is recovering from his wounds from the previous episode and discovers that recovering in peace and quiet isn't possible with not only his friends visiting, but the fact that Hibari is in the same hospital.

Tsuna is in a room with bullies and they are always commanding Tsuna but when he's about to obey them, Dino appears with his subordinates following him. Then the nurse comes in and scolds Tsuna for having too many guests and switches him to another room. Then, Dino apologizes to him and tells his subordinates to wait for him outside, then Yamamoto appears with his father , having prepared sushi for Tsuna and shortly after, Gokudera appears.

Trivia

  • In the manga chapter, Hospitalized , Tsuna gets hospitalized by Enzio crushing him after being accidentally thrown into a well by Tsuna, using Dino's whip. In the anime however, he was crushed by Enzio on the mountain of death (see previous episode).
  • Tsuna was shifted into a graveyard-theme /horror room in the last page in the manga.
  • Haru , Kyoko , I-Pin and Reborn first visited Tsuna when he was hopitalized in the private ward in the manga, not the graveyard-theme /horror room as shown in the anime.
