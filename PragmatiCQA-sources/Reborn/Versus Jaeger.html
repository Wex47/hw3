Versus Jaeger is the third battle that occurred during the fourth day of the Representative Battle of the Rainbow . The battle happened between the Allied Team members Tsunayoshi Sawada , Green Moska , Byakuran , Xanxus , Superbia Squalo , Dino , Kyoya Hibari , Mukuro Rokudo , Chrome Dokuro , Fran , Enma Kozato , and Team Bermuda 's Jaeger .

Prelude

The elite members of the Allied Team confront Jaeger and Bermuda von Veckenschtein to prevent them from going to assist Big Pino and Small Gia .

Synopsis

Byakuran attacks Jaeger first with his White Dragon . Jaeger easily and quickly defeats the dragon and instantly appears behind Xanxus , cutting off his right arm. Superbia Squalo attacks Jaeger, but Jaeger shatters Squalo's sword before stabbing through his chest. Jaeger finds out that it's real blood and he didn't kill an illusion. This shocks everyone in place. Xanxus orders Squalo to stand up, but Squalo doesn't move, angering Xanxus and making his scars more prominent. Verde comments that Jaeger is too powerful, which Reborn agrees as he thinks that the situation is bad with Jaeger's intent to kill.

Mukuro is wondering how they can stop Jaeger from short warping to their backs. Shells materialise behind them just as Jaeger strikes Byakuran . Dino realises that it is a steel cover made by Mukuro using Verde's device. However, Jaeger calls it a useless trick and stabs Byakuran through his chest, making him bleed profusely. Uni 's group reveals themselves and wonders if Jaeger can warp part of his body. Byakuran grabs Jaeger's hand and tells Xanxus that this is his chance. Xanxus shoots but it misses as Jaeger appears in front of Xanxus and attacks his legs using chains. Xanxus cries out in pain and falls to his knee before collapsing, unconscious.

Reborn says that Jaeger's strength exceeds his expectations and they will not be able to resist until Tsuna arrives. Verde releases his curse and thus, the power of his flames increase, boosting the power of his weapons. Also, with longer limbs, he can control a wider range of objects. Verde commands Spanner to fire. As the object flies, Verde leaps into it and it is revealed to be a Green Moska . Verde explains that he used the latest mosca frame built by Spanner and loaded it with his advance weapons to create a super robot. Jaeger attacks by short warping his hands, causing Green Moska to malfunction due to the detection of multiple heat sources. The disembodied hands slice through Green Moska's arms easily and it falls to pieces.

Jaeger moves on to his next target, Mukuro , and appears behind him. Mukuro attempts to block the assault but his trident breaks and he is injured. Jaeger prepares to stab Mukuro again but finds his hand binded by Dino . However, by short warping and leaving his hand behind, Jaeger slashes Dino's torso. Jaeger's hand escapes the bullwhip and heads for Dino's face but it is deflected by Hibari , who tells Dino that he has repaid his debt. Hibari attacks Jaeger, who deflects the attack. Reborn wonders why Jaeger did not make use of his short warp. Hibari and Mukuro reveal that they had noticed too. However, Jaeger comments that his short warp has no weakness.

Hibari and Mukuro conceal themselves behind the needle spheres created by Hibari. Jaeger attacks but ends up destroying more of Mukuro’s flame decoys . Hibari finds a chance and attacks with his once more. However does not work as Jaeger is able to short warp and reappear behind Hibari. Before Jaeger's attack can land, he is punched in the face by Tsuna .

Tsuna clashes with Jaeger and is promptly kicked away. Tsuna notices that Jaeger did not short warp and the latter is breathing heavily like Small Gia did. Jaeger attacks again, but was stopped by Chrome , Enma , and Fran . After Jaeger incapacitates the three of them and once again did not short warp from Hibari's attack, Tsuna realizes that the Vindice rely on Bermuda 's Flame of Night to recharge their energy as they cannot produce Flame of Night on their own. Wonomichi promptly appears and forbids Bermuda from recharging Jaeger or making any contact wth him unless he release his curse. Jaeger attacks Tsuna again, but Hibari and Mukuro stops him. Jaeger attacks Mukuro and Hibari at the same time, which give Mukuro an opportunity to hold Jaeger's movement. Tsuna attacks with X-Burner with the help of the gravity ability from Enma's ring to prevent the attack from hitting Hibari and Mukuro to blast Jaeger away.

Aftermath

Before Tsuna can finish Jaeger off, Bermuda releases his curse and discards his robes and bandages, revealing a pale, sickly-looking young man emanating the Flame of Night.
