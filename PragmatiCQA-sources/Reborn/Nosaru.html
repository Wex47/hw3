Nosaru is a young member of the Giglio Nero Famiglia and was once a D-rank Black Spell member of the Millefiore Famiglia 's 3rd Squad, serving under Tazaru and captain Gamma until all three defected to join the Vongola Famiglia . Nosaru is highly respectful of Gamma and Tazaru and refers to both of them as Bro (though it is unclear if they are actually related). He also wears the same outfit as Tazaru and uses the same weapons.

Character outline

Appearance

He has long, straight pink hair, large yellow eyes and a slender build. In the future, he wears the uniform of a Millefiore Black Spell member.

Personality

He is very loyal to Uni and his "brothers", but also quite hotheaded and violent. Due to his young age, he is still somewhat immature compared to his older peers.

Plot overview

Future Arc

Nosaru first appeared at an abandoned factory alongside Tazaru while attacking Adult I-Pin , Lambo , Haru and Kyoko . Adult Yamamoto , along with Tsuna and Gokudera , soon arrived to aid their comrades and while Nosaru engaged Yamamoto in combat Tazaru chased Tsuna. Although clearly losing Nosaru managed to knock Yamamoto out, once he was replaced with his younger self. Nosaru then fought Gokudera who had figured out how to use his Box Weapon and used it to defeat Nosaru. Later, after Gamma's defeat, Nosaru was nearly beaten up while trying to calm him, but Tazaru took the hit instead.

Future Final Battle Arc

As the Vongola Famiglia were being chased by the Six Funeral Wreaths , Nosaru and Tazaru attacked Torikabuto after Gamma took Uni back; however, they were both easily defeated.The next day, they joined Ryohei , Lambo and Basil to ambush Kikyo . However, his first attack with Tazaru failed, leaving Kikyo free to release an army of Nuvola Velociraptor after which Nosaru was swiftly defeated. Later, he watched in tears as Gamma and Uni sacrificed themselves to revive the Arcobaleno .

The Curse of the Rainbow Arc

The present Nosaru is present as the future Uni walks out of the forest and catches the Sky Pacifier, which was expelling Flames. He, along with Gamma and Tazaru, are Uni's representatives from the Giglio Nero, while Byakuran and the Six Funeral Wreaths are Uni's other representatives.

Weapons and abilities

  • Dark Scythe : A scythe covered in Storm Flames that is able to shoots projectiles coated in Storm Flames that explode upon impact.

Trivia

  • Due to his appearance and voice in the anime, Nosaru was mistaken for a girl by many fans until he was seen without a t-shirt.
  • According to an "extra chapter" after Chapter 193 , Nosaru's habit of exclaiming "shooaa" comes from remembering the Japanese phrase "'ssha" incorrectly ("'ssha" is a shortened form of "yossha", which expresses victory or success).
