The Foundation ( 風紀財団 , Fūki zaidan ) is an independent group that serves under Kyoya Hibari . The group's core members are former members of the Namimori Middle Disciplinary Committee .

The Foundation travels around the world studying and researching Box Weapon technology. Tetsuya Kusakabe reaffirms his position as Hibari's second-in-command.

Their hideout is directly connected to the Vongola Underground Base via a steel door which is never opened to keep the secret and also because Hibari does not like crowds.

Known Members

  • Future Kyoya Hibari - Leader
  • Future Tetsuya Kusakabe - Second-in-command
