Character outline

Appearance

He has light blonde hair that reaches his shoulders, dark blue eyes and a winged-shaped tattoo on his forehead. He wears a white cloak over a black suit, and has white gloves.

Plot overview

Future Arc

Vongola Quinto, along with the other Vongola Bosses, manifested during Tsuna's Vongola Trial . After Vongola Primo accepted Tsuna's resolve, the latter was granted .
