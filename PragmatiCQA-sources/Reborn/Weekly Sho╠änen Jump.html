Weekly Shōnen Jump ( 週刊少年ジャンプ , Shūkan Shōnen Janpu ) , stylized in English as WEEKLY JUMP , is a collection of manga published weekly by Shueisha. Its manga series consists mostly of action scenes with bits of comedy, and targets young male readers. Katekyo Hitman Reborn! has been featured in weekly issues throughout its run from May 24, 2004 – November 12, 2012. Due to the series' popularity, characters from the series were included in some covers of Weekly Shōnen Jump .
