Reborn 's primary weapon of choice is a gun , a semi-automatic pistol, original CZ 75 made in Czechoslovakia. In the anime, Leon transforms into a similarly shaped gun whenever Reborn needs it.

Reborn usually uses the weapon to shoot enemies or obstacles. The gun can shoot various types of bullets , including the Dying Will Bullets and Rebuke Bullet .

The gun can also transform into a sniper that can shoot a great distance, as demonstrated when Reborn uses it to influence events at Namimori Middle School without actually being close by.

The gun can cause widespread damage, but Reborn seems to be able to control its power in addition to being skilled at aiming all forms.
