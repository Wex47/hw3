X-Stream is an attack used by Tsuna . Tsuna dives down at his enemy and rapidly begins to circle them from bottom to top, Dying Will Flames lagging behind his feet until his target is completely trapped in a tornado of Sky Flames . This move was only used once during his fight with the Deadly Stem Force under Iris of the White Spell . It was capable of separating the merged Death Stalk Unit members back into their individual forms.
