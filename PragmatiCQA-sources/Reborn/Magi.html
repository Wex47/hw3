Magi is one of children of Simon Famiglia shown in the Eighth Key of memories that Simon Cozzato , the Simon Primo, and Giotto , the Vongola Primo, left behind.

Plot Overview

Inheritance Ceremony Arc

In the eighth key, she made an appearance with other Simon Famiglia children, playing at Simon Island's waterfall while Cozzato and the other adults were watching them. Magi went up to Cozzato and stated that she wanted more friends, but Cozzato apologized to her and said to her to bear it for a while longer, since Simon Famiglia had decided to hide in the shadows to deceive Demon Spade about the truth of their survival from the war; however, Cozzato stated that he and Giotto believed that Magi's descendants would someday be able to laugh together with the Vongola descendants.
