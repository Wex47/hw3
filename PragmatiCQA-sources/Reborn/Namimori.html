Namimori ( 並盛 , Namimori ) is a fictional Japanese small city and the most prominent setting in the Katekyo Hitman Reborn! series, where most of the main protagonists gathered and lived. Namimori is the catalyst setting for most friendships, rivalries, and battles.

Locations

Schools

  • Midori Middle School ( 緑中 , Midori-chū ) - An elite, all-girls school where Haru Miura studies. It is only a walking distance from Namimori Middle.
  • Namimori Middle School - Namimori's main educational establishment. It is a public middle school with a huge main building and numerous classrooms. Most of the events of the Daily Life Arc and the Varia Arc occurred in Namimori Middle.
  • Yumei Private Middle School - Where Shoichi Irie studies.

Restaurants

  • La Namimorine ( ラ・ナミモリーヌ , Ra Namimorīnu ) - A famous cake shop located at Namimori's shopping district in front of Namimori Station. Both Haru and Kyoko are big fans of the place and even get I-Pin hooked on their desserts. They serve a famous chocolate drink there that Mukuro was interested in enough to go visit, and unexpectedly shared his interest with Haru and Kyoko.
  • Namimori Diner - Following day 1 of the Representative Battles, Team Reborn reconvened in this restaurant to discuss their fights.
  • Namimori Hall ( 並盛堂 , Namimori Dō ) - They sell Kyoko's favorite limited edition cream puffs and baked cheesecake.
  • Rakurakuken ( 楽々軒 , Rakuraku-ken , lit. Comfortable; easy place ) - The ramen place 10-year-later I-Pin works at and Kawahira frequently orders from.
  • TakeSushi - A sushi restaurant owned by Tsuyoshi Yamamoto and frequented by Belphegor when he's in Namimori.
  • Tedsteak - A restaurant Dino takes Gokudera to offer a possible promotion in the Vongola hierarchy for him.
  • Nammy's - A restaurant where most of Tsuna's guardians and Enma's guardians met up after Tsuna was attacked by an assassin of the Pesca Famiglia .

Hospitals

  • Nakayama Surgeon Center ( 中山外科医院 , Nakayama Gekai-iin ) - A small, if well equipped, clinic. This is where Dino used as a base for others' recuperation. Including Lambo , Squalo , and Chrome Dokuro during the Ring Battles.
  • Namimori Central Hospital ( 並盛中央病院 , Namimori Chūōbyōin ) - The main hospital that Tsuna and friends have frequented. Hibari holds some influence over it. The head nurse there is Toshiko Sano.

Dojos

  • Asari-gumi ( あさり組 , Asari-gumi , lit. Asari clan; Clam group ) - The dojo the Yamamotos use.
  • Chibe Dojo ( 知部道場 , Chibe Dōjō ) - A Karate training center that fell victim to dojo yaburi.
  • Namimori Dojo ( 並盛道場 , Namimori Dōjō ) - The dojo Ryohei , Tsuna, and 10-year-later I-Pin defended from dojo yaburi.

Stores

  • JACK, Pumpkin Specialty Store ( カボチャ専門店JACK , Kabocha Senmon-ten Jakku )
  • Kawahira Realtor ( 川平不動産 , Kawahira Fudōsan )
  • Secondhand Books ( 古本 , Furuhon )
  • A coffee shop Kaori Furuta works at.
  • A convenience store Gokudera works part-time at.

Lodging

  • Namimori Guesthouse ( なみもり民宿 , Namimori Minshuku ) - The house the Enma and his Guardians rent in after Simon is hit by an earthquake and they transfer into Namimori Middle School. It's run by a landlady who usually cooks their meals.
  • A castle the Vongola Famiglia rented out for Tsuna's inheritance ceremony.
  • A large house rented by Team Uni during the Representative Battles.
  • A high-rise hotel that Vongola Nono & his Guardians stayed in for the Inheritance Ceremony, as well as Team Mammon and the Cavallone Famiglia stayed in during the Representative Battles.

Other locations

  • Namimori Town Community Center ( 並盛町公民館 , Namimori-chō Kōminkan )
  • Namimori Ball ( 並盛ボール , Namimori Bōru ) - A batting center Takeshi is a regular customer of. It's run by Sadaharu Uchida.
  • Namimori Graveyard
  • Namimori Island A small island just off the coast where Reborn held his trial for the Arcobaleno seals.
  • Namimori Public Pool ( 並盛市民プール , Namimori Shimin Pūru , lit. Namimori townspeople pool ) - The local swimming pool. Tsuna learned how to swim here with the dubious help of his friends.
  • Namimori Shrine
  • Namimori Station ( 並盛駅 , Namimori-eki )
    • Merone Base - (10-years-later)
  • Namimori Zoo ( 並盛動物園 , Namimori Dōbu~tsuen )
  • Vongola Underground Base - (10-years-later)
  • Namimori River ( 並盛川 , Namimori-kawa ) - A river with a gentle flow that separates Namimori and the neighboring town. It flows into Namimori Coast. In the spacious riverbeds, baseball and soccer athletes are seen practicing and the people who are fishing. Haru fell and nearly drowned on the river before being saved by Tsuna. On New Year's, the riverside is the stage for the "Family Battle" against the Cavallone Famiglia .

Unnamed

Unnamed but semi-prominent or at least notable locations.

  • A beach - Located nearby. Ryohei's boxing club senpai, Eiji Kuroda is a lifeguard there along with his buddies Yukio Kisanuki and Takuya Ookura. None of them take their jobs seriously and would sooner allow a child to be swept up by the sea than risk their own lives.
  • A bowling alley - The location of the group date Longchamp set up between himself, Tsuna, and Gokudera, with Hanako Tsuruta , Sawako Narita , and Reborn disguised as "Reboko."
  • A chapel that Bianchi tried to marry Reborn in.
  • A public bathhouse. Shitt P! floated through one during her chase around Namimori with Gokudera .
  • A warehouse district where Adelheid Suzuki killed members of the Giegue Famiglia .
  • Rocky mountains, a parking garage, and an open park with a large fountain in the middle were grounds for the last battles of the Representative Battle. The park was where the transfer of the Arcobaleno Pacifiers to the Pacifier Jars happened, and where the Curse was finally lifted.
  • A mountain that Reborn adjusted to model after Italy's infamous Death Mountain.

Notable events

Namimori holds many significant events and locations:

  • All of the Ring Battles are at Namimori Middle School .
  • The battle between Gokudera , Yamamoto , and Future Hibari vs Gamma is at the Namimori Shrine.
  • The Merone Base Invasion during the Future Arc takes place there.
  • The battle between the Vongola Famiglia and Millefiore Famiglia takes place there during the Future Arc.
  • It is where the Arcobaleno Trials took place.
  • The Vongola Primo & Guardians appear here during the Primo Family Arc .
  • The Vongola Decimo 's Inheritance Ceremony occurs in a castle nearby.
  • The Vongola Underground Base in the future is situated here.
