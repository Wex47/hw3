The Last Guardian's Battle is the 118th chapter of Akira Amano 's Katekyo Hitman Reborn!

Synopsis

Hibari is about to be finished off by Gola Moska , but just then, Tsuna wakes up, revealing that the vision was merely a nightmare. Worried about Hibari, Tsuna heads to Dino 's place and encounters Chrome , who greets him and promptly leaves.

Tsuna then enters the building and is greeted by Dino, who instantly guesses why Tsuna is visiting. When Tsuna and Dino walk to the living room, Tsuna finds Gokudera , Ryohei , and Yamamoto , who had all come to check Hibari's progress, but were reassured by Dino that Hibari was doing well.

Gokudera, Ryohei, and Yamamoto, relieved, fell asleep. Reborn then arrives in a costume and tells Tsuna that he must perfect the Zero Point Breakthrough today. In Italy, Turmeric , Oregano and Lal Mirch continue their infiltration of the Vongola Base and discover a weapon testing room. There, they find diagrams that are very similar to a certain Varia member.

Back in Japan, Xanxus tells Gola Moska that he was looking forward to the Cloud Ring Battle . Tsuna continues his training by fighting against Basil . Reborn tells him that they have to miss Hibari's match to perfect the Zero Point Breakthrough. At Namimori Middle School , Hibari arrives, completely confident of his victory.
