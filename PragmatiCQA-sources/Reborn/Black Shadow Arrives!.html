Synopsis

As the battle between the Vongola and the Simon continues, Julie Kato informs Enma about Tsuna's relation with external advisor of the Vongola, Iemitsu Sawada . This information causes Enma to spring into action shortly after Shitt P! 's defeat. He reveals that Iemitsu killed his parents and sister, causing Tsuna to suffer a mental breakdown. Tsuna is nearly killed by Enma and is only spared when Enma's Simon Ring loses control. Back at Julie's room, Chrome tries to escape, but Julie is already back. Seeing her efforts and response, he reveals that he's the one who arranged the battle between the Vongola and the Simon, revealing his true identity as Demon Spade and controlling Chrome's mind. Meanwhile, Adelheid challenges Tsuna to duel with her, but they are interrupted by the arrival of Hibari , who then fights her instead.
