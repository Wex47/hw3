Simora is the sixth generation boss of the Vongola Famiglia , inherited from Vongola Quinto . His name was briefly seen on a Vongola family tree shown by Reborn .

Character outline

Appearance

﻿Simora has short, dark gray hair with a side parting. He wears a monocle and a light brown, long-sleeved trench coat over a white shirt and a black tie.

Plot overview

Future Arc

Simora, along with the other Vongola Bosses, manifested during Tsuna's Vongola Trial . After Vongola Primo accepted Tsuna's resolve, the latter was granted .

Weapons and abilities

Equipment

  • Boomerang : His weapon is a silver boomerang with the crest of the Vongola Famiglia in the center. It can be imbued with Sky Flames .
