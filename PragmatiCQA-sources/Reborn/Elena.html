Manga Debut

Chapter 344
Elena was a woman who was once in a relationship with Demon Spade . She was the one who introduced Demon to Vongola Primo, Giotto , and persuaded him to join the Vongola along her.

Appearance

She is regarded by both Tsuna and Demon to be a beautiful woman. She had long blond wavy hair, large eyes and has so far only been seen wearing a dress with a ribbon around her neck and wrist. The only other appearance she is shown is in the cover of Chapter 409, where she's shown wearing black suit with white shirt underneath and black ribbon.

Personality

From Demon's description, she was a gentle woman who showed concern for weak people and wished to help them. Just like Demon, she doesn't follow the social hierarchy between nobles and commoners, instead sharing Demon's opinion that the aristocrats around them had no social standing in the eyes of the public, and wished to fix this. After she joined the Vongola Famiglia with Demon, she grew to love the Vongola more than anything else and dedicated herself to the Famiglia in order to protect the weak and defenseless.
