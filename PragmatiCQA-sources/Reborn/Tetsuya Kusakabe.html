Tetsuya Kusakabe is the second-in-command of the Namimori Middle Disciplinary Committee and, in the future, the second-in-command of the Foundation ; as such, Kusakabe is extremely loyal to Kyoya Hibari . In the future, he has travelled around the world with Hibari researching the Rings and Box Weapons .
