Darryl Vidal
Darryl Vidal is an American actor, producer, author, and martial artist. He plays a character with the same name as him in the 1984 film The Karate Kid .

Biography

Vidal is a martial artist off screen. He currently runs a Kenpo Karate school in Murrieta, California. He also teaches Kali, a Filipino style of stick fighting, and Wing Chun, a variant of Kung Fu.
