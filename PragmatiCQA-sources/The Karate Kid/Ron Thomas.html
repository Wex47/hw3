Ronald Lee Thomas (Born November 15, 1961) is an American actor. He plays Bobby Brown in The Karate Kid , The Karate Kid Part II , and Seasons 2 and 3 of Cobra Kai .

Biography

Thomas was born in Reno, Nevada. His most notable acting work outside of The Karate Kid , The Karate Kid Part II , and Cobra Kai is The Big Bet in 1985.
