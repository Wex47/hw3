Harry is a supporting character in the 2010 film The Karate Kid . He is Dre's first friend in China.

Biography

Early Life

Not much is known about Harry's early life. It can be presumed that Harry moved to China at some point and learned to speak Mandarin Chinese at least at a conversational level.

The Karate Kid (2010)

Harry notices Dre and Sherry moving in and decides to help them take their bags to their place. Harry later plays basketball with a few friends and invites Dre to play with them. Harry notices Dre starring at Meiying which Harry tells him to talk to her. Dre reluctantly accepts. He sees Dre getting beaten by Cheng and asks him to stop but is not successful.

Harry is later seen at the tournament supporting Dre .

Personality

Harry is shown to be a nice, friendly, charming, welcoming and helpful person.

Trivia

  • Harry's main series counterpart is Freddy Fernandez since they are both the first friend the protagonist makes after moving and they have the same welcoming nature.
