Gianni Decenzo is an American actor. He mostly known for portraying Demetri Alexopoulos in the YouTube Premium and Netflix series, Cobra Kai .

Biography

Decenzo started off his career as Jonathan Presscott in short movie The Lucky One . He later appearing in several TV shows, including The Middle , Liv and Maddie , and Gortimer Gibbon's Life on Normal Street . In 2015, Decenzo landing as recurring role Arthur Pickwickle in 100 Things to Do Before High School .
