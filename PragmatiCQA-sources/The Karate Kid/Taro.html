Taro is the quaternary antagonist of The Karate Kid Part II . He is another one of Chozen 's friends, who helps the latter wreak havoc, and bully Daniel .

He is portrayed by Marc Hayashi.

Biography

The Karate Kid Part II

Daniel accidentally exposed corruption in Chozen's sharecropping collection which in response Chozen accuses him of ruining his honour and sends his two cronies to vandalize the Miyagi family property.
