Big Red

  • Christopher Ryan Lewis is the real-life older brother of Shane Donovan Lewis, who portrays Lil Red.
  • Big Red's surname could be Caldwell or Johnson due to Counselor Blatt saying Caldwell, Johnson and Rickenberger can go, in The Right Path .

Big Red joined Cobra Kai after the events of the All Valley Karate Tournament , but is turned away. The following day, he and his brother and a couple of other students join the dojo and begin to learn the way of the fist. When John Kreese enters the Dojo he along with the other Cobra Kai's are confused about why he is there. Nonetheless, Big Red is seen goofing off again during the warm-up sequence which provokes Johnny. During the making of the Cobra Kai commercial, Big Red is in the back and again, during Valley Fest. Though hearing Daniel's speech, Big Red is one of the students who stay loyal to Johnny and Kreese while his younger brother, Lil Red, joins Miyagi-Do . He soon forms a friendship with both Edwin and Hawk . The three are always around each other and pull off devious task for Kreese. During the Coyote Creek challenge, Big Red on the red team with Kreese and is eliminated. The day before school started, he was at Moon's party and drinking and partying. During the school fight, he saw Miguel fall off the balcony. He along with many other Cobra Kai's sided with Kreese after the school brawl leaving Johnny.

Frank

  • Frank was one of two (him and Abe) students that was from the first batch of Johnny Lawrence 's next generation Cobra Kai students before defecting to Miyagi-Do.

Frank can be seen during the events of Mercy Part II when brand new students want to join Cobra Kai, but Johnny shoots them down saying come back tomorrow. He is one of the students who take what Johnny says about "mercy" into consideration which is one of his turning points. When Kreese showed up to the dojo, Frank along with the other Cobra Kai's are confused about why he is there. Nonetheless, Frank is seen goofing off again during the warm-up sequence which provokes Johnny. He is seen at the cement yard though he isn't one of the students who turned the truck. During the making of the Cobra Kai commercial, Frank can be seen in the back and is later seen during Valley Fest. After that, he is seen during Tory and Miguel's fight and surprised to see Tory win. He quits Cobra Kai along with fellow students Chris , Abe , Lil Red, and Nathaniel due to Kreese's vicious teachings. He then joins Miyagi-Do , but Robby Keene is against them joining due to Chris attacking Demetri at the mall, and Robby thinking that the former Cobra Kai students vandalized the Dojo. Daniel lets them join and they all settle in with the Miyagi teachings. Frank was present when Daniel was teaching Shochu-Geiko and Kangeiko. When Daniel teaches Kangeiko to the Miyagi-Do students, Frank was surprised when Demetri was able to block Chris's punches and punched him back. He is seen at Moon's party with Lil Red cheering Sam, who is in a drinking contest with Tory . After Demetri roasts Hawk at the party, he is seen with the rest of the Miyagi-Dos ready to fight the Cobra Kais right before the police showed up. Later, he is seen when he was cheering the Miyagi-Dos in the school fight along with Abe. He is amongst those that witness Miguel's fall.

Lil Red

  • Shane Donovan Lewis is the real life younger brother of Christopher Ryan Lewis (Big Red)

Lil Red (born 2004) was a former Cobra Kai student who defected to Miyagi-Do Karate . He is the brother of current Cobra Kai student, Big Red. He and his brother originally join Cobra Kai after witnessing Miguel Diaz win the All Valley Karate Tournament just like many other's. Thanks to Daniel, he quits Cobra Kai along with fellow students Chris , Abe , Frank, and Nathaniel due to Kreese's vicious teachings. He then joins Miyagi-Do , but Robby Keene is against them joining due to Chris attacking Demetri at the mall, and Robby thinking that the former Cobra Kai students vandalized the Dojo. Daniel lets them join and they all settle in with the Miyagi teachings. Lil Red was present when Daniel was teaching Shochu-Geiko and Kangeiko. He is seen at Moon's party with Frank cheering Sam, who is in a drinking contest with Tory .After Demetri roasts Hawk at the party, he is seen with the rest of the Miyagi-Dos ready to fight the Cobra Kais right before the police showed up. Later, he is seen when he was cheering the Miyagi-Dos in the school fight and is amongst those that witness Miguel's fall.

Rick

Rick is a semi-wealthy man and drug addict who whisks Shannon Keene away to Cabo, Mexico. He was kicked out of the apartment by Robby who mistook Rick for a burgular. Shannon viewed Rick as a distraction from Robby and the life they had together. Rick promised that the rent and ulilities would be paid by Rick, however, the man never went through with is promise. He and Shannon didn't return from Cabo as quickly as they had hoped. When Shannon did return, she wasn't with Rick any longer and went into rehab.

Stiven

Stiven joins Miyagi-Do sometime in the summer of 2018. He first appears when the Miyagi-Do students were about to learn Shochu-Geiko. When it was Demetri's turn, Stiven's number was called and he sweep kicked Demetri . When Daniel decided to change to Kangeiko, Stiven was called and he kicked Demetri in the chest. Stiven was surprised when Demetri was able to block Chris's punches and punched him back. He is seen again at the school fight going at it with Mikey . He then fought Hawk and was able to land a punch on him. He was winning in his fight with Mikey until Stingray arrived and threw him to Hawk, who then got him in a choke hold. As the fight proceed to a different part of the school, Mikey tackled Stiven, who then started to hit him repeatedly.
