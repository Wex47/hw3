Dennis is a supporting antagonist in The Karate Kid Part III . He is the strong but silent henchman of Mike , along with Snake .

He is portrayed by William Christopher Ford .

Biography

Dennis oversaw Mike's training for the upcoming tournament. Dennis is not as skilled as Mike nor Daniel , due to the fact that Daniel had easily defeated him during a second confrontation on Miyagi's bonsai shop and a sparring match against Mike on the Cobra Kai dojo , in which he lost.

Trivia

  • Dennis rarely speaks. He only has two lines throughout the entire movie.
