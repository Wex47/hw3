Zhuang is a minor character and quaternary antagonist in the 2010 film The Karate Kid .

Biography

Zhuang was friends with Cheng and Liang . His first appearance is when he is cheering on Cheng when he is fighting with Dre in the Playground. And he continues to bully Dre in the school. He is one of the people who chases down Dre after he pours water on them. Then, when Mr. Han and Dre go to the Fighting Dragons Studio , he gets slapped brutally by Master Li for showing mercy when he beats Liang in a training fight. He is last seen in the tournament and loses against Dre in a fight.

Fighting Style

He has a good, accurate fighting skill and heavy punches that he used to hurt Dre .
