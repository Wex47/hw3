Hannah Kepple (Born November 20, 2000) is an American actress. She plays Moon in the Netflix series Cobra Kai .

Biography

Not much is known about Kepple's personal life. It is known that she has taken ballet classes as a child. She has also started dating co-star Xolo Maridueña who plays Miguel after the shooting of Season 1 . Their pictures together have been removed from their social medias so the current status of their relationship is unknown. Her most notable acting works outside of Cobra Kai are Tell Me Your Secrets and Your Worst Nightmare.
