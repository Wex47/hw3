Barrett Carnahan (Born September 22, 1992) is an American actor. He plays young John Kreese in Season 3 and Season 4 of the Netflix series Cobra Kai .

Biography

Carnahan was born in Ohio. He later relocated to Los Angeles. He is currently married to Nina Kubicki. His most notable acting roles outside of Cobra Kai are Link in The Thundermans , Aiden in Alexa and Katie , Nathan Bowman in Constantine , Sherman in Sleepy Hollow , Darren Kelsey in Reckless , Dave in Grey's Anatomy , The Rival Frat Guy in Modern Family , Himself in Drop Dead Diva, and more.
