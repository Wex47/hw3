Mr. Lawrence is the unnamed father of Johnny Lawrence and the ex-husband of Laura Lawrence . He is also the ex-father-in-law of Shannon Keene and the grandfather of Robby Keene .

Biography

Little is known about him except he married Laura and they had Johnny but he and Laura later divorced and he never knew his son. It is highly obvious by Sid Weinberg 's insult about him being a loser that he was a lowlife deadbeat man. It is hinted by Johnny that he abandoned Laura and Johnny. In Season 4, In a flashback, Laura revealed when young Johnny saved his real fathers items that he left her and Johnny and was a worthless deadbeat.
