Jaden Christopher Syre Smith (born July 8. 1998) is an American actor, rapper, singer, and songwriter. He plays Dre Parker in the 2010 film The Karate Kid .

Biography

Smith was born on July 8, 1998 in Malibu, California to Will Smith and Jada Pinkett Smith. He has an older half brother named Trey and a younger sister named Willow. His first major acting role was in The Pursuit of Happyness in 2006 where he starred with his father as an on screen father and son duo. He was also in the 2008 film The Day the Earth Stood Still. In 2010, he featured on Justin Bieber's single Never Say Never . On November 17, 2017, he released his debut album Syre.
