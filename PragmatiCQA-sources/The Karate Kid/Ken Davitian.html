Kenneth Davitian (Born June 19, 1953) is an American actor, comedian and restaurateur. He plays Armand Zarkarian in the Netflix series Cobra Kai .

Biography

Davitian was born in Los Angeles, California to an Armenian family. He was a graduate of Whittier College. He idolized Burt Reynolds. He made his acting debut in 1975 in Sons of Sassoun . In 2003, he founded a restaurant called The Dip. He speaks fluent Armenian.
