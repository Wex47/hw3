Hilary Swank (born July 30, 1974) is an American actress and film producer. She played Julie Pierce in T he Next Karate Kid .

Biography

Swank was born in Lincoln, Nebraska. She made her film debut in 1992 with the movie Buffy The Vampire Slayer.
