Terayle Hill (Born February 9, 1994) is an American actor. He plays Trey in the Netflix series Cobra Kai .

Biography

Hill was born in Los Angeles, California. He is an alumni of Clark Atlanta University. His most notable acting works outside of Cobra Kai are Step Up: High Water , Blue Bloods , Love, Simon , Tyler Perry's Acrimony , and more.
