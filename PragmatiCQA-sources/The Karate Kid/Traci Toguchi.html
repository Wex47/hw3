Traci Toguchi (Born November 9, 1974) is a Japanese-American model and actress. She plays Yuna in The Karate Kid Part II and Season 3 of Cobra Kai .

Biography

Toguchi was born in Honolulu, Hawaii. She made her acting debut in The Karate Kid Part II at the age of 12. She was named Miss Hawaii in 1995. Her most notable works also include Magnum P.I. , Lost , Hawaii-Five-O and more.
