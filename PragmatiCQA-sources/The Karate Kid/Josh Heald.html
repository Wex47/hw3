Josh Heald is an American producer, director, and writer.

Josh created Cobra Kai , together with Hayden Schlossberg and Jon Hurwitz .

Biography and Career

Josh was born on December 12, 1977 in Red Bank, New Jersey, USA. He is known for Hot Tub Time Machine (2010) and Cobra Kai (2018). In advance of production on All You Can Eat, Josh competed in a Nathan's Hot Dog Eating Contest Qualifier at the New York, New York Hotel-Casino in Las Vegas, NV on May 18, 2006. Despite his lack of training and rookie status, Josh consumed 11 hot dogs and buns in twelve minutes. The event winner, Joey "Jaws" Chestnut, set a new American record with 50, all but assuring himself a cameo in the movie. Josh was also the college roommate of Jon Hurwitz at the University of Pennsylvania, and the two went on to become close friends and writing partners.
