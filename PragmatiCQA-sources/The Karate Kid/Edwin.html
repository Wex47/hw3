Edwin is a character in the Netflix Original Cobra Kai .

Edwin is a close friend of Big Red from their shared time in Cobra Kai Dojo . is one of Cobra Kai's most powerful students and he was formerly apart of Hawk 's gang. However, his friendship with Hawk comes to an end after Hawk betrayed Cobra Kai.

Cobra Kai

Cobra Kai (Season 1)

He first appeared shortly after witnessing Miguel beating up Kyler and his band of goons and like many others is the main reason for joining Cobra Kai. Though being made fun of by Johnny , he is one of the students who didn't quit. He along with Tim were one of the students who flinched causing all the Cobra's except Miguel and Aisha to get punched in the face. He, along with many other Cobra Kai's are shown training in the junkyard and was chased after by the dogs. The night before the All Valley Karate Tournament , Edwin was invited to the part at the cliff and was seen drinking and partying. More than likely he saw Miguel and Sam break up that night. On the day of the All Valley, he hears Johnny’s speech about No Mercy and is one of the students who ran out chanting Cobra Kai. It is unknown how far he made it into the tournament as he isn't shown competing along with many other students.

Cobra Kai (Season 2)

Unlike the season prior, Edwin has a bigger role but still has no dialogue throughout the season. He along with many Cobra Kai's go out for dinner after Miguel's victory at the All Valley tournament. He is seen behind Hawk and Aisha with Abe and Kev. The next day, he along with the other Cobra Kai's goof off and get scolded by Johnny . The following day when John Kreese enters the Dojo he along with the other Cobra Kai's are confused about why he is there. Nonetheless Edwin is seen goofing off again during the warm-up sequence which provokes Johnny. He is seen at the cement yard though he isn't one of the students who turned the truck. During the making of the Cobra Kai commercial, Edwin can be seen in the back and is later seen during Valley Fest. After that, he is seen during Tory and Miguel's fight and surprised to see Tory win. Though hearing Daniel's speech, Edwin is one of the students who stay loyal to Johnny and Kreese. After Johnny had to go visit his dying friend in the hospital, Edwin can be seen during the intense training montage. During the Coyote Creek challenge, Edwin is shown to be on the red team with Kreese and is shown to be eliminated, though it is unknown who did so. The day before school started Edwin was at Moon's party and was shown drinking and having a good time. He was surprised to see Miyagi-Do show up. Nonetheless he still had a great time, during this event, it is shown he is a good street dancer. During the school Brawl, he is shown to fight Abe , a former friend who defected to Miyagi-Do. Though it is unknown how well he did during the fight. One thing is for sure he saw Miguel fall off the balcony. He along with many other Cobra Kai's sided with Kreese after the school brawl leaving Johnny.

Cobra Kai (Season 3)

He is first shown with Hawk and other Cobra Kai's when Hawk is confronted by Demetri and Sam. He is next shown walking with Hawk and other Cobra Kai's on the school field. During the soccer game, the Miyagi Do's and Cobra Kai's get aggressive, but Edwin isn't shown fighting. Fortunately, for Edwin and the other Cobra Kai's, they don't get in trouble. In the arcade fight, Edwin is shown arriving with Tory and Big Red and joining the other Cobra Kai's in pressuring Hawk to break Demetri's arm. The next time he is shown is in the dojo, when Kyler , Brucks , and some other potential recruits arrive. He witnesses Kyler defeat Mitch and Hawk defeat Bruck's. He is next shown at the cafeteria with Hawk and some other Cobra Kai's when Hawk tells Mitch to get lost since the latter was kicked out of Cobra Kai for losing to an untrained Kyler. He is then shown in the hallway with Hawk and some other Cobra Kai's when Johnny tries to persuade them to join his new dojo Eagle Fang Karate. After that, he is shown with Hawk, the other Cobra Kai's, and Kreese at the park, when Kreese attempts to convince Johnny to rejoin Cobra Kai. He and the other Cobra Kai's are then shown being trained by Kreese at Cobra Kai. He is last seen in Cobra Kai when Hawk walks toward Kreese and his enemy Robby Keene. He is not seen in the fight at Sam's house, but it can be assumed that he is still loyal to Kreese and ended his friendship with Hawk after he defected from the dojo.

Personality

Though little is known about his personality, he is shown to be a very aggressive and that he is a follower more than a leader. This is predominantly shown when he, and other Cobra Kai students, carry out dirty work John Kreese and sometimes even Hawk . Just like most Cobra Kai students, Kreese's merciless methods didn't sit with him at first. But he as learned to accept his way of teaching. Edwin's attitude costs him his friendship with Abe , which makes him more impulsive and reckless in fights.

Fighting Style

Edwin is actually one of the best fighters in the new Cobra kai dojo, as well as one of the most powerful fighters in Hawk's gang

He has shown a good amount of strength which he demonstrates during the school brawl when he is shown fighting Abe throughout the fight. He is able to overpower his enemies during fights by using his body strength, this was shown during the soccer game against Miyagi-Do. Though during this fight he does loose.

His fighting style seems to mostly incorporate punches, kicks and using his overall body strength to take down and defeat his opponents. During his fights, he is shown to be aggressive and just as he is taught he shows no mercy. This was evidently shown during the school brawl, when him and Abe going at each other.

Overall Edwin is one of the most powerful and strongest fighters at Cobra Kai, with Kreese topping him as one of the top 8 most powerful fighters.

Relationships

Hawk

Hawk was one of Edwin's closest friends while in Cobra Kai Dojo , though throughout season 1 they weren't really seen together but still joined Cobra Kai after Miguel defeats Kyler. But during the start of season 2, Edwin started to hang around Hawk more frequently and even started to become part of his crew. He preformed devious task with Hawk, including vandalizing Miyagi-Do Karate. Just like Hawk, Edwin stays with Cobra Kai after Daniel tries to persuade students to come to his dojo. Like many others, Edwin ultimately stays with Cobra Kai and sides with Kreese after the school brawl which leaves Miguel in a coma.

In season 3, they have formed a closer bond with Hawk and the two seem to hang around each other more frequently than the seasons prior. Just like the season prior, Edwin still help's out Hawk, in many devious task such as the Arcade Fight. Edwin was also one of the students who pressured Hawk to break Demetri's arm. When Hawk defected to Eagle Fang Karate, Edwin ended their friendship despite not joining the house brawl.

Big Red

Big Red is one of Edwin's closest friends. They meet the day after the All Valley Tournament when Big Red join's the dojo, following Miguel's victory. The soon form a close friendship and are seen more frequently together. Big Red is also part of Hawk's gang. Just like Edwin, Big Red help vandalize Miyagi-Do, and stayed loyal to Cobra Kai and Johnny Lawrence when Daniel tried to persuade students to come to his dojo. Like many others Big Red ultimately stays with Cobra Kai and sides with Kreese after the school brawl which leaves Miguel in a Coma

In season 3, the 2 hang around each other more frequently, this causes their bond to grow even closer result in the two to become practically inseparable. The two seen together during many fight scene's and even come together as a pair during the arcade fight. Big Red was also one of the students who pressured Hawk to break Demetri's arm. Just like Edwin, Big Red didn't follow when Hawk defected to Miyagi-Do, It can be assumed that Edwin and Big Red are still very close friends

John Kreese

John Kreese is Edwin's sensei in Cobra Kai Dojo . The two meet at the start of season 2 when he is just an observer for the dojo, and just like many other's he is confused about who he is. He quickly learns that he was Johnny Lawrence's old sensei and Edwin start's to gain Kreese's trust after that. He soon fall's under Kreese's manipulation and soon becomes very loyal to him. Kreese ordered Edwin to vandalize Miyagi Do with Hawk, Big Red and Mitch. After the School Brawl, Edwin stayed loyal to Kreese and even was put in Kreese's top 8 most powerful pupils.

During the event's of season 3, Edwin's loyalty to Kreese doesn't change. When Hawk defected to Eagle Fang Karate, Edwin didn't follow, showing that he is loyal to Kreese no matter what.

Robby Keene

Robby is Edwin's friend and former enemy. They become friends when Robby decides to join Cobra Kai in season 3. The two originally started out as enemies when Robby join Miyagi-Do Karate under the teachings of Daniel LaRusso. The tension between the two rose when Robby kicked Miguel off the school balcony which ended up paralyzing him.

In Season 3, the two are still shown as enemies and is shown to still hate Robby for what he did to Miguel. Though when Kreese welcomes him into the dojo, the two are shown to get along and end up being friends.

Mikey

Mikey is one of Edwin's closets friends, throughout their time in Cobra Kai Dojo . The two meet after Miguel's victory against Kyler in the lunchroom fight. The two soon form a close bond and are seen frequently. Mikey is also joined Hawk's gang while in the dojo. Just like Edwin, Mikey stays with Cobra Kai after Daniel tries to persuade students to come to his dojo. Like many others, Mikey ultimately stays with Cobra Kai and sides with Kreese after the school brawl which leaves Miguel in a coma.

In season 3, the two hang around each other more frequently, this causes their bond to grow even closer result in the two to become practically inseparable. At a high-school soccer scrimmage, he walks right behind Hawk, with Doug and Dirk next to him with the rest of the Cobra Kai members behind him. When they begin walking on the side of the soccer field, the other people make way for them, some people cheering for them. After the soccer game started, Sam tackeled Mikey to the ground and stepped on him. The game proceeds with team Miyagi-Do and team Cobra Kai hurting each other in soccer-style until the coach blows her whistle and tell them all to go to the principal's office Mikey is not present when Miyagi-Do and Eagle Fang join their forces in the following morning. It can be assumed that Mikey decided to stay loyal to Cobra Kai even after Hawk left the dojo meaning Edwin and Mikey are still friends.
