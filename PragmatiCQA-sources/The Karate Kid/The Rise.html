Trivia

  • The Rise is the longest episode of the season, with a run time of 45 minutes.
  • The conclusion of the All Valley Karate Tournament takes place, with Cobra Kai Dojo winning the competition after a close match between Tory Nichols and Samantha LaRusso .
  • Miguel Diaz leaves the valley to find his biological father.
