Owen Morgan (Born March 10, 2004) is an American actor. He is best known for portraying Bert in the Netflix series, Cobra Kai .

Biography

Morgan landed his professional first acting role in 2018. Off screen he is a black belt in Karate. He is currently best friends with Nathaniel Oh who plays Nathaniel .
