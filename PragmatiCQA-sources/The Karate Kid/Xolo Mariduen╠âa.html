Xolo Maridueña (born June 9, 2001) is an American actor. He is best known for portraying Miguel Diaz in the YouTube Premium series, Cobra Kai .

Personal Life

Xolo also is a streamer on Twitch with his own channel called Xolo Crunch streaming different games on it. He also dates Hannah Kepple who plays Moon in real life they started dating after Season 1 of the show. Their photos together have been removed from their social medias so the current status of their relationship is unknown. Xolo's first on-screen kiss was with Mary Mouser for Cobra Kai . The approximate pronunciation of his name is "show-low."
