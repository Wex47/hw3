Peyton Roi List (born April 6, 1998) is an American actress and singer. She portrays Tory Nichols in Cobra Kai . She was previously known for the role of Emma Ross on the Disney Channel series Jessie and its spinoff Bunk'd .

She was also known for her role in the film adaptation of Diary of a Wimpy Kid series as Holly Hills the main protagonists love interest.
