Big Red

  • Christopher Ryan Lewis is the real-life older brother of Shane Donovan Lewis, who portrays Lil Red.
  • Big Red's surname could be Caldwell or Johnson due to Counselor Blatt saying Caldwell, Johnson and Rickenberger can go, in The Right Path .

Big Red is with Hawk and the other Cobra Kai's when Hawk is confronted by Demetri and Sam . Big Red is next shown walking with Hawk and other Cobra Kai's on the school field. During the soccer game, the Miyagi Do's and Cobra Kai's become aggressive, though Big Red isn't shown fighting. During the arcade fight, Big Red is shown arriving late with Tory and Edwin. They join the other Cobra Kai's in pressuring Hawk to break Demetri's arm. He appears next in the dojo when Kyler , Brucks , and Sarah arrive. He witnesses Kyler defeat Mitch and Hawk defeat Bruck's. He is next shown at the cafeteria with Hawk and some other Cobra Kai's when Hawk tells Mitch to get lost since the latter was kicked out of Cobra Kai for losing to an untrained Kyler. He is then shown in the hallway with Hawk and some other Cobra Kai's when Johnny tries to persuade them to join his new dojo Eagle Fang Karate . After that, he is shown with Hawk, the other Cobra Kai's, and Kreese at the park, when Kreese attempts to convince Johnny to rejoin Cobra Kai. He is last seen in a montage when he and the other Cobra Kai's are then shown being trained by Kreese at Cobra Kai.

Councilperson Roberts

portrayed by Zele Avradopoulos

Councilperson Roberts appears in the season 3 episode The Good, The Bad, and The Badass . She is an elected member of the NorthRidge City Council and serves as it's senior member or president. Earlier, she informs the All Valley Karate Tournament of the intent for the city to cancel their permit for the upcoming 2019 All Valley Karate Tournament, due to the continuing lingering tensions that came from the brawl at West Valley High School. She would hold an open hearing for the Northridge City Council, and her hearing would be attended by all three of the sensei's, as well as many of their students. During the hearings she hears testimony from John Kreese, Daniel LaRusso, and Johnny Lawrence. She also orders Amanda LaRusso to be removed from the chamber when she is informed that John Kreese has a legal restraining order preventing her from being within 100 feet of him. She seemed to be heading to keep the ban in place, but is swayed otherwise by an impassioned set of speeches by Samantha LaRusso and Miguel Diaz.

Dieter

Dieter is first shown with Hawk and other Cobra Kai's when Hawk is confronted by Demetri and Sam. He is next shown walking with Hawk and other Cobra Kai's on the school field. During the soccer game, the Miyagi Do's and Cobra Kai's get aggressive, but Dieter isn't shown fighting. Fortunately, for Dieter and the other Cobra Kai's, they don't get in trouble. The next time he is shown is in the dojo, when Kyler , Brucks , and some other potential recruits arrive. He witnesses Kyler defeat Mitch and Hawk defeat Bruck's. He is next shown at the cafeteria with Hawk and some other Cobra Kai's when Hawk tells Mitch to get lost since the latter was kicked out of Cobra Kai for losing to an untrained Kyler. He is then shown in the hallway with Hawk and some other Cobra Kai's when Johnny tries to persuade them to join his new dojo Eagle Fang Karate. After that, he is shown with Hawk, the other Cobra Kai's, and Kreese at the park, when Kreese attempts to convince Johnny to rejoin Cobra Kai. He and the other Cobra Kai's are then shown being trained by Kreese at Cobra Kai. He is last seen in Cobra Kai when Hawk walks toward Kreese and his enemy Robby Keene. He is not seen in the fight at Sam's house, but it can be assumed that he is still loyal to Kreese and ended his friendship with Hawk after his defection from Cobra Kai.

Dirk

He is first shown walking with Hawk and other Cobra Kai's on the school field. During the soccer game, the Miyagi-Do's and Cobra Kai's get aggressive, but Dirk isn't shown fighting. Fortunately for Dirk and the other Cobra Kai's, they don't get in trouble. The next time he is shown is in the dojo, when Kyler , Brucks , and some other potential recruits arrive. He witnesses Kyler defeat Mitch and Hawk defeat Brucks. Though it is unknown if he defects from Cobra Kai or got kicked out, he joins Johnny's newly-founded Eagle Fang Karate along with Miguel, Mitch, Bert, and two others. Dirk is present when Kreese and the rest of Cobra Kai confront Johnny during their training, offering Johnny a last chance to re-join the dojo. In the last scene of the season finale, he is shown at Miyagi-Do with the Miyagi-Do and Eagle Fang Karate Students, as well as former Cobra Kai students Mitch and Hawk , preparing to train for the next All Valley Tournament.

Frank

Frank appears again in the carwash fundraising for Miguel's surgery run and managed by Samantha LaRusso and Moon and the Miyagi-Do's who started it up. He is a carwash participant and the group raises $2500. At the end of the day, he and Abe are exchanging cash from what they worked on from the car wash with Nathaniel and are fist-bumping together. He is then seen outside of the LaRusso's door with Miyagi-Do telling Sam that Cobra Kai beat Nathaniel up and took the money away from him. He has seen again when the Miyagi-Do students take a stand during what escalates into an intense soccer game, even though Frank was not seen being involved. Nevertheless, he has received detention along with several other Miyagi-Dos. Next scene he is at the lunchroom, with Abe and Lil Red, trying to find a table. However, when they witness Kyler bullying Demetri by drawing a penis on his cast, they feel bad for Demetri. He can be seen in the background talking to two high school students just as Sam and Miguel bump in to each other. In the last scene of the season finale, he is shown at Miyagi-Do with the Miyagi-Do and Eagle Fang Karate Students, as well as former Cobra Kai student Hawk , preparing to train for the next All Valley Tournament.

Lil Red

Two weeks after the school brawl, Lil Red is shown to still care for Miguel despite being on opposite side's. This was shown during the car wash scene when all of Miyagi-Do tries to raise money for Miguel's surgery. He has seen again when the Miyagi-Do students take a stand during what escalates into an intense soccer game, even though Lil Red was not seen being involved. Just like many other's, Lil Red joins Sam when trying to open back up Miyagi-Do but fail's to do so once Sam's mother show's up. When Daniel decides to re-open the dojo after his trip to Okinawa, Lil Red rejoins the dojo to take down Kreese once and for all Though he didn't participate in the LaRusso home fight, he is shown to still stay loyal to Miyagi-Do and Eagle Fang Karate after the two dojo's merge.

Officer Dawson

A corrections officer at Sylmar Juvenile Corrections (SJDC) , he monitors the movements and behaviors of the inmates. He intervenes in Robby Keene 's fight again Shawn Payne .

Paul

Paul join's Cobra Kai Dojo sometime late 2018. He first appears while Hawk , Tory, Robby, Kyler , and another unnamed Cobra Kai student are attempting to steal a snake from the local zoo, to carry out the task that John Kreese had put them up to do. Paul and the unnamed Cobra Kai stood guard while the others went to get the snake, but they weren't able to due to Kyler forgetting the Snake Pole. However, the crew ended up being successful thanks to Robby snatching the snakes without any equipment. While Eagle Fang and Miyagi-Do were trying to plan for their dojo's to merge, the two dojo's were interrupted by Cobra Kai with Paul included. First, he was fighting Stiven with Hawk, but went to fight Sam after. He fought Sam for a bit, but was defeated. When Hawk realized he is fighting on the wrong side, Paul was beating Mitch and kicking him to the ground. Then, he faced off against Demetri and Hawk with Mikey and an unnamed Cobra Kai student due to Hawk defecting, but they were quickly defeated. It can be assumed that Paul is still loyal to John Kreese, just like many other Cobra Kai Students.

Ron

Ron is a member of the All Valley Karate Tournament, and appears to serve as it's secretary or president of affairs. He appears also in the season 3 episode The Good, The Bad, and The Badass , where he arrives at Miyagi-Do Dojo and informs Daniel that the city is cancelling the upcoming All Valley Under 18 Karate Tournament. He is very impressed by the landscaping at the Miyagi-Do Dojo, as he notices the koi pond.

Sarah

Sarah is scouted for Cobra Kai by John Kreese due to her athleticism as a basketball player on the West Valley High School team. She goes to the Cobra Kai Dojo , where she is given a tryout match against Tory . Tory beats her and also chips her tooth, which angers Sarah. Kreese asks Sarah to leave the mat and the dojo.

Stacy

After the West Valley High School fight, most of the the Cobra Kai students arrive for their first day back looking for a "round 2". Just then, Stacy and two other freshmen girls walk past Hawk , Bert and Mitch . Hawk attempts to flirtily put the moves on them by offering his “services” as their bodyguard: “So if anybody gives you any trouble, just come to me. I’m the guy—” However, it backfires on him as Stacy interrupts and embarrasses him in front of Bert and Mitch by saying, “Who wets the bed and got kicked into that trophy case? Yeah, we know.” Mitch and Bert started laughing at the roast Hawk receives.

Stacy and her friends then walked past Demetri , Chris and Nathaniel , with Stacy saying hi to Demetri while giving him a flirty look; Demetri was surprised by the greeting, and remarked that a "human female" actually knows him. Chris replied that he is famous after the school fight.

Stiven

Stiven is first seen at the soccer game being push to the ground by Hawk, who attempted to flirt with Moon but failed. He is seen again with Sam , Demetri , and an unnamed Miyagi-Do student when they went to help Chris Who was having some Cobra Kai Problems at the Arcade. During the fight, he was briefly seen fighting Mikey as Demetri and Hawk were about to face off. Later, he is seen at Christmas party at the LaRusso House and was surprised when he saw Eagle Fang Karate students walking through the door with Sam and Miguel suggesting that the two dojos work together. While Stiven along with the others sans Miguel and Sam don't agree with this, they soon come to a truce. Then, several Cobra Kai's break in, resulting in a fight breaking out. Stiven was fighting both Hawk and Paul before Paul went to fight Sam. Stiven was able to put a few hits on Hawk, but Hawk was still winning the fight. Stiven then attempted to fight Tory , but was quickly defeated. Stiven then continued to fight Hawk, but was thrown to the ground by the stronger foe. Stiven and his friends ultimately win the fight, partly due to Hawk defecting and helping his side. In the last scene of the season finale, he is shown at Miyagi-Do with the Miyagi-Do and Eagle Fang Karate Students, as well as former Cobra Kai student Hawk , preparing to train for the next All Valley Tournament.
