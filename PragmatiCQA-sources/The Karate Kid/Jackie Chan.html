Chan Kong Sang, better known as Jackie Chan is a Hong Kong actor, martial artist, stuntman, filmmaker, action choreographer and singer. He played Mr. Han in the 2010 film The Karate Kid .

Biography

Chan was born on April 7, 1954 at Victoria Peak, British Hong Kong. He started as a child actor at the age of 5. At the age of 17, he was a stuntman for Bruce Lee. His breakout film was The Snake in the Eagle's Shadow. He eventually started acting in Hollywood films. Some of his most notable works are Rush Hour, Rush Hour 2, Rush Hour 3, Kung Fu Panda, Kung Fu Panda 2, Kung Fu Panda 3, The Spy Next Door, and more.
