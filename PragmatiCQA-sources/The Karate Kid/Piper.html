Piper Elswith is a minor character in Cobra Kai Season 2 and a recurring character in Season 4 . She is the ex-girlfriend of Moon and a star athlete at West Valley High School . She is later recruited into Cobra Kai .

Biography

Season 2

Piper is at Moon 's party where Hawk attempts to seduce her, only to learn that Piper is Moon's new girlfriend. The pair kiss by the fireplace.

Season 4

Miguel and Johnny visited a gymnastics school to recruit Piper into Eagle Fang , since they needed girls in their dojo for the tournament . She said yes at first, though her and her teammates later joined Cobra Kai after receiving merch boxes. After joining Cobra Kai, Piper started practicing with the rest of the students. During a one-on-one exercise where Kreese and Silver hand-select students to fight one another, Piper was selected by Silver. Kreese picked Tory Nichols and they fight lasted one a few seconds, with Tory easily taking the girl down. Piper merely smirked as they should do it again sometime, though Tory scoffed her off. Piper later attended the West Valley High School prom.

Piper participated in the All Valley Karate Tournament with the rest of the Cobra Kai students. She helped Kenny with the board breaking during the skills competition. She entered the girls division of the tournament and faced Samantha LaRusso in the quarter finals. Piper won the first point, by countering Sam's Miyagi-Do techniques, but was quickly defeated when Sam used more offensive attacks. Despite her loss, Cobra Kai still won the All Valley Karate Tournament when Tory defeated Sam in the girls competition.

Fighting Style

Piper incorporates elements of gymnastics into her fighting style. She used cartwheels and various acrobatic moves as opening gambits or to set up her attacks. According to Tory, Piper's weakness is her left side, as she telegraphs her kicks. Despite only having a few weeks of training with Cobra Kai, Piper managed to understand the Miyagi-Do style well enough to counter it during her fight against Samantha LaRusso at the tournament. She was quickly overwhelmed when Sam switched to more offensive tactics that she learned from sensei Johnny Lawrence .
