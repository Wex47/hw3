Jesse Kove (Born November 2, 1990) is an American actor. He plays David in flashbacks on Season 3 of Cobra Kai .

Biography

Kove was born in Los Angeles, California to Martin Kove (who plays John Kreese ) and Vivienne Kove. He has a twin sister named Rachel.
