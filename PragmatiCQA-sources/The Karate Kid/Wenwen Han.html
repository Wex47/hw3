Han Wenwen (Born August 24, 1995) is a Chinese actress, violinist and dancer. She plays Meiying in the 2010 film The Karate Kid .

Biography

Han was born in Xian, China. Before she started acting she trained as a violinist and dancer. She speaks both English and Mandarin Chinese
