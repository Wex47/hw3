Judy is a minor character in Cobra Kai . She is a spoiled customer who Johnny Lawrence argued with, resulting in him getting fired from his maintenance job and setting up the events of the TV series.

She is played by Kylie Delre.

Biography

Season 1

Judy had Johnny Lawrence do maintenance in her home, even ordering him to put a large television near the door while she relaxed. However, she began to notice that he put it in the wrong side of the wall resulting in her arguing with him. Judy told Johnny that the television was supposed to be across from the door, not far from it and decided to have him move the TV before her company came over. Johnny stated that he still needs to fill in the holes for the TV and that it only takes him an hour to get to the hardware store but Judy stated it was not her problem as she was not responsible for not putting up the TV. Despite Johnny telling her that he will resolve the problems by tomorrow, as well as to not "bitch at him", he was then fired from his maintenance job as a result.

Season 2

Johnny met up with random women through a dating app at a bar. One of the women was Judy. They both looked at each other and appeared to recognize each other, but they could not recall where they knew each other from. Judy recognizes him and immediately starts arguing with him about their last meeting, before she leaves the bar and says she is done with these 'shitty apps' (dating apps).
