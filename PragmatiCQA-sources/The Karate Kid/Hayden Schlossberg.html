Hayden Schlossberg is an American writer, director, and producer. Hayden, along with Josh Heald and Jon Hurwitz , created Cobra Kai .

Biography

Hayden was born on June 9, 1978 in Livingston, New Jersey, USA. He is best known for Harold & Kumar Escape from Guantanamo Bay (2008), A Very Harold & Kumar 3D Christmas (2011) and Cobra Kai (2018).

Trivia

  • In a Twitter fan Q&A, Hayden claimed that Terry Silver is his favorite Karate Kid character.
  • Although he wrote episodes with Heald and Hurwitz, every episode he directed was was alongside Hurwitz.
