Lynn (often just referred to as Homeless Lynn ) is a homeless woman from the Karate Kid universe, first shown on Cobra Kai . The character was introduced in Ace Degenerate , and her first interaction was with Johnny Lawrence , when she thought he wanted her spot on the outside of a convenience store. She made new brief appearances after that, later on the same season, and seasons 2 and 3.

Biography

Season 1

Lynn was first shown in the first shot of the Mini Mart of Reseda Flats, although her name was never mentioned there. She was outside, looking for food on a garbage can.

After detecting Johnny on her surroundings eating stale pizza, she accuses him of being invading her territory, and yells him to leave, misjudging him for a bum. Johnny informs her that he's not homeless, that he owns a car; and keeps eating his pizza.

When Johnny tells Kyler to stop bullying Miguel , Kyler pushes him and Johnny drops his pizza. Lynn is seeing eating his slice moments later, and then she watches the whole fight (Johnny vs Kyler and his friends).

On episode 3, "Esqueleto" , Lynn was contacted by Johnny to spin a sign of his recently opened dojo , offering her to get paid for this. She then tells him that she's gonna spend the money on meth. Johnny replies that he doesn't care. Later on that day, Johnny spotted her neglecting the job, using the cardboard sign he gave her as an improvised roof while she eats something sitting on the floor.

Season 2

Lynn was only seen once in season 2 smoking outside of the Cobra Kai dojo.

Season 3

Lynn was at the CS Centre where the SJDC is serving food to the homeless including her, she shouts at Johnny and tells him to quit holding the line up and threatens to throw her shoe at him while Johnny tried to talk to Robby. Lynn was being annoying to Johnny and making him give his soup.

Season 4

Lynn was only seen eating near the dojo and witnessed Tory interacting with her aunt Kandace.
