Mrs. Mills is a minor character in The Karate Kid and Cobra Kai season 3. She is the wealthy mother of Ali Mills and a wife to Mr. Mills .

Biography

The Karate Kid

Mrs. Mills meets Daniel LaRusso as he arrives at their residence in the Hills to take Ali on a date. Although Mrs. Mills acts polite with Daniel, her facial expression suggests a certain concern about her daughter dating him. She can be seen later having a dinner with her family at a country club, accompanied by Johnny Lawrence who asks her for a dance. Halfway through the dance, she hands Johnny over to Ali, in the hopes of giving the two a chance to talk through their relationship-related issues. This escalates in Johnny kissing Ali, which is spotted by Daniel, who stumbles upon the restaurant staff, resulting in Daniel being covered in spaghetti and embarrassed in public. Mrs. Mills reacts to the incident with a confused facial expression and hand gesture until Ali hits Johnny in the face and storms off angrily, much to her and Mr. Mills' dismay.

Cobra Kai

Season 3

As of season 3, Mrs. Mills still resides in the same house. Her daughter Ali comes to stay with her mother during the Christmas holidays, and they make plans to attend a dinner on the 19th of December at the Encino Oaks Country Club.

Trivia

  • Shannon WIlcox, who played Mrs. Mills in The Karate Kid, didn't reprise her role for Cobra Kai. Instead, she is replaced by Deborah May.
