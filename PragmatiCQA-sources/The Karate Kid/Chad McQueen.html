Chadwick Steven McQueen (born December 28, 1960) is an American actor, film producer, martial artist and race car driver. He plays Dutch in the films The Karate Kid and The Karate Kid Part II .

Biography

McQueen was born in Los Angeles, California to Steve McQueen and Neile Adams. He started riding dirt bikes at the age of 9 and eventually moved on auto racing. He started a racing company called McQueen Racing LLC. in 2010. He was given an offer to reprise his role as Dutch in Cobra Kai but he refused wanting to focus on his business. The script was written so that Dutch is in jail to explain his absence.
