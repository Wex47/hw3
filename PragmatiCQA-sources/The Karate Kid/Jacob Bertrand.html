Jacob Bertrand is an actor known for portraying Eli Moskowitz in Cobra Kai .

Career

Prior to his work for Disney, Bertrand had several roles in Nickelodeon productions: He was a regular on the sitcom Marvin Marvin , voiced the main character Gil in season 3 of the animated series Bubble Guppies , appeared as a minor character on the teen sitcom iCarly as Chip Chambers, and co-starred as Charlie on the fantasy-comedy film Jinxed . He also appeared as Kirby in Disney XD's Kirby Buckets As of 2018. In 2017, Jacob landed a starring role in Cobra Kai , a continuation of the iconic 1980 film series, The Karate Kid , which aired on YouTube Premium before its renewal on Netflix . He portrays Eli "Hawk" Moskowitz.
