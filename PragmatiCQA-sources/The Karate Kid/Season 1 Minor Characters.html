Bob

Bob is seen shortly after witnessing Miguel being attacked by Kyler and his gang. This inspires Bob to join the Cobra Kai Dojo . He is met with mocking vonacular by Johnny Lawrence , his new sensei, but remains a student in spite of the abuse. He, and the rest of the class, are taught "The Way Of The Fist". The group trains in the junkyard and is chased by a pair of dogs that Johnny releases. Bob also attends the Canyon party, where he dances happily. The day of the All Valley Karate Tournament , he hears Johnny’s speech regarding his "No Mercy" teachings, and is one of the students who ran out chanting Cobra Kai.

Daryl

Daryl is a member of the All Valley Under 18 Karate Tournament Executive Committee. He appears in the season one episode All Valley, where he is going over the upcoming 2018 Tournament. He is a big advocate for changing the colors of the mats and the fists in the All Valley banners and mats to help give a new look and drive up attendance numbers, which have been slipping. He is impassive at first when Johnny Lawrence appears before the committee to appeal the lifetime ban that was enacted against the Cobra Kai Dojo enacted after the 1985 tournament. He is emphatic that he believes the Cobra Kai name is a great name for a karate dojo. He remains in the room when a closed door vote is called for by council president Ron. While his vote is now shown, based on the two votes that were clearly in favor of keeping the lifetime ban in place (Daniel LaRusso and George), it is believed he voted to reinstate Cobra Kai. He would also appear in the season one finale Mercy, where in addition to serving as an official for the event, also doubles as the main announcer for the event.

Dieter

Dieter's first appearance is shortly after witnessing Miguel beating up Kyler and his band of goons, this like many others is the main reason for joining Cobra Kai . Though being made fun of by Johnny , he is one of the students who didn't quit. He, along with many other Cobra Kai's are shown training in the junkyard and was chased after by the dogs. The night before the All Valley Karate Tournament , Dieter was invited to the part at the cliff and was seen drinking and partying. More than likely he saw Miguel and Sam break up that night. On the day of the All Valley, he hears Johnny’s speech about No Mercy and is one of the students who ran out chanting Cobra Kai. It is unknown how far he made it into the tournament as he isn't shown competing along with many other students.

Frank

Frank was a former Cobra Kai student who defected to Miyagi-Do Karate . Frank joins Cobra Kai Dojo along with many others after Miguel's self-defense demonstration in the school cafeteria. He soon begins learning the "Way of the Fist" just as Miguel and Aisha Robinson had. He, along with many other Cobra Kai's are shown training in the junkyard and was chased after by the dogs. The night before the All Valley Karate Tournament , Frank was invited to the Cliff party where he is seen drinking and just, in general, having a good time. It is possible he saw the breakup between Sam and Miguel. On the day of the All Valley, he hears Johnny’s speech about No Mercy and is one of the students who ran out chanting Cobra Kai. It is unknown how far he made it into the tournament as he isn't shown competing along with many other students.

George

George is a member of the All Valley Karate Tournament executive committee. He appears in the season 1 episode ALL VALLEY, where the executive committee is discussing issues leading up to the upcoming 2018 karate tournament. When Johnny Lawrence appears before the committee, he seconds fellow committee member Sue's assertion about some sort of mistake in 2017 with a corndog vendor. He is confused at first when Johnny says he is there to appeal the lifetime ban on Cobra Kai, wondering if they even had the power to ban anyone. When Daniel LaRusso appears and files are accessed, he reads out the 1985 executive committee's decision to ban Cobra Kai for the actions of John Kreese, Terry Silver, and Mike Barnes. As the issue is discussed before the committee, George makes it clear that while he does not know Johnny Lawrence, that he knows Daniel LaRusso and backs his position that the ban on Cobra Kai from the All Valley Karate Tournament continue to remain in place. He remains in the room when the closed door vote takes place, and it is assumed he voted to keep the ban. He would later appear in the episode MERCY, in his capacity as an official overseeing the event.

Keith

Keith appears in the episode, Strike First . He enters the Cobra Kai Dojo in his official capacity as a health inspector for the city health department. Johnny at first believes he is a prospective student. Keith is not impressed with the condition of the Cobra Kai Dojo safety status. He and Johnny have an exchange where Keith informs Johnny of the requirements Johnny will need to bring the exercise studio up to code, as well as information Johnny will need to acquire the insurance he also needs to open the studio. Before he leaves, he comments on what he thinks is a rattlesnake logo of the dojo, but is corrected by Johnny before he leaves. Later in the epsiode, Johnny is shown filling out all of the forms and paperwork that Keith had left him.

Reid

Reid is a karate student whom trains out of Yoshukai Karate , presumably out of the valley. He appears to be under the age 15-17. He has a black belt and is eligible to compete in the 2018 All Valley Karate Tournament. He faces and defeats several opponents in the preliminary rounds, including Bert from Cobra Kai Dojo. He does well enough to advance to the quarterfinals, where he is matched up against Miguel Diaz . Miguel defeats Reid in the quarterfinals.

Ron

Ron is a member of the All Valley Karate Tournament, and appears to serve as it's secretary or president of affairs. He is shown in the episode All Valley , where he is conducting issues that involve the All Valley Tournament and it's preparations for the 2018 tournament. He adjudicates the entire issue involving the life-time ban on competition against the Cobra Kai Dojo that was instituted in 1985 for the actions of Cobra Kai under John Kreese, Terry Silver, and Mike Barnes. He calls for a closed door vote after Johnny Lawrence makes an impassioned speech for Cobra Kai to be reinstated. While his vote was not shown, based on where at least two votes were clearly in favor of keeping the ban (Daniel LaRusso and George), it can be assumed that Ron voted to reinstate Cobra Kai. He would appear in the season 1 episode MERCY, where he can be seen conducting business at the All Valley Karate Tournament with the other executives.

Rory

Rory is one of three bullies that target Miguel Diaz . When Johnny intervenes in the fight, Rory and his friends attempt retaliation by ganging up on Johnny, but their attempts are futile. He appears later in the season at a pool party held by Moon . She records him swimming and doing a backflip into the pool. During the Halloween dance, Rory and his friends once again attack Miguel, this time in the locker room. The fight results in Miguel being badly injured. His final appearance comes when he hangs out with Kyler, who is spreading sexual rumors about Samantha LaRusso . They begin to taunt Sam, who is ready to defend herself physically when Miguel intervenes. Thanks to his karate lessons, he defeats the group of bullies.

Sheila

Sheila is one of the receptionists at LaRusso Auto Group .

Sue

Sue is a member of the All-Valley Under 18 Karate Tournament Executive Committee. She appears in the season 1 episode ALL VALLEY. She addresses some issues regarding the upcoming 2018 tournament with regards to the new colors of the mats and banners that are going to be put up. When Johnny Lawrence appears before the committee, she at first believes he is there in regards to an issue with the corndog vendor from 2017. When Johnny establishes that he is there to appeal the lifetime ban on the Cobra Kai Dojo she is confused that they could even ban a dojo. Later in the episode, when the 1985 ban is read aloud to the committee, she is impassive at first, she also asks Johnny about "What's your relationship to this Terry Silver?" to which Johnny answer he has no idea who "this Terry Silver was?". She is clearly moved by Johnny's impassioned speech at what the All Valley Tournament meant to him and to his students. She is part of the closed door vote to determine the Cobra Kai's dojo fate. While not shown, based on the two votes that were established against lifting the ban (George and Daniel LaRusso), it is clear she voted to lift the ban. She would appear later in the season one episode MERCY, where she is there with the other All Valley Karate Tournament executives overseeing the event.
