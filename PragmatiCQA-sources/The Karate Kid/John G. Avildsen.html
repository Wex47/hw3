John Guilbert Avildsen (born December 21, 1935, died June 16, 2017) was an American film director. He directed The Karate Kid and the first two sequels.

He won the Academy Award for Best Director in 1977 for Rocky . Other films he directed include Joe (1970), Save the Tiger (1973), Fore Play (1975), (1980), Neighbors (1981), For Keeps (1988), Lean on Me (1989), Rocky V (1990), The Power of One (1992), 8 Seconds (1994) and Inferno (1999).
