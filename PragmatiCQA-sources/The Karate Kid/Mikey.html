Mikey is a minor character in Cobra Kai . He is a member of the Cobra Kai Dojo and a former member of Hawk 's group.

Cobra Kai

Season 1

Mikey joins Cobra Kai after Miguel publicly defeats Kyler . He partakes in the training that Johnny gives the students, including running from dogs. He later attends the bonfire party with Demetri and Edwin . Mikey also supports Miguel at the All Valley Karate Tournament .

Season 2

Mikey and a fellow Cobra Kai student named Doug form a friendship with Hawk . The trio works together to carry out various tasks against the Miyagi-Do students. The trio confronts Demetri in a comic book store after Demetri leaves Cobra Kai a negative review online. When Demetri refuses to remove the post, the trio attacks Demetri, who shoves Mikey and Doug. Fellow Miyagi-Do students Robby and Sam intervene, resulting in a fight breaking out.

Upon encouragement by Kreese , Mikey helps Hawk vandalize the Miyagi-Do dojo. They also steal Mr. Miyagi 's Medal of Honor, which the Miyagi-Do's interpret as an act of violence. During the Cobra Kai training at Coyote Creek, Mikey is on the opposite team of Tory and Miguel , who work together to defeat Mikey and steal his headband.

On the first day at school, Mikey participates in the hallway brawl. He notably fights with Stiven and another student, until Stingray intervenes. When Miguel and Robby are fighting near the staircase, Mikey and Doug enter the fight to attack Robby. This gives Miguel the chance to escape the fight. Robby ultimately kicks Mikey down the steps.

Season 3

With Cobra Kai now being run by John Kreese , Mikey continues to be a loyal student. During a high-school soccer scrimmage, Hawk, Doug, and Dirk appear on the same team. As the game progresses, the Cobra Kai's and Miyagi-Do's begin to take cheap shots at one another, though the fighting is instigated by the Cobra Kai's. Sam ultimately tackeles Mikey to the ground and steps on him. Both teams are ultimately sent to the Principal's office.

Mikey, Hawk, and Doug hangout at an arcade where they harrass a working Miyagi-Do student. The trio breaks into a laser tag place to drink alcohol. The lights in the store are suddenly cut, and moments later, the Miyagi-Do students arrive looking for retribution for their harassed teammate. During the fight, Mikey manages to beat up a Miyagi-do student by kneeing the student's face, before getting kicked in the chest by Demetri. When Hawk has Demetri's arm in a hold, Mikey along with the others, encourages Hawk to beak it. After Hawk breaks his arm, Mikey and the others cheer.

Mikey is amongst the Cobra Kai students to attack the Miyagi-do and Eagle Fang students in the LaRusso's house. He at first, fights Miguel, who throws Mikey into the living room table head-first. He fights several people, including Mitch , who seems to be his primary target in the fight. He spent a brief portion attacking Hawk, who had betrayed Cobra Kai, and Demetri. Demetri and Hawk work together to render Mikey unconscious. Ultimately, Mikey and the Cobra Kai's lose the house fight and escape before the LaRusso's return.

Personality

Mikey is very aggressive and that more of a follower than a leader. This is predominantly shown when he, and other Cobra Kai students, carry out dirty work ordered by John Kreese or Hawk.

Fighting Style

Mikey is brazen in his attacks as he takes on Demetri and Stiven. However, Mitch doesn't have the skill or technique to support his brazen antics. He is easily defeated by Mitch and every other opposing fighter. Mikey fairly athletic, as he runs long distances without becoming winded. Despite having strength and athleticism, Mikey has yet to win a single fight, showing that he is an inexpierenced and overconfident fighter with minimal combat skills.
