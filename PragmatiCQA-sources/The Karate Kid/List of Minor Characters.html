The Karate Kid

Alan

Alan was a Cobra Kai student, along with Johnny, Dutch, Bobby, Jimmy, Tommy, and many others. He is present at the Cobra Kai Dojo when Daniel LaRusso enters the dojo. He is later shown training in the dojo when Mister Miyagi and Daniel enter, seeking a peaceful end to their hostilities. During the training session, Alan momentarily loses his concentration when sensei John Kreese is making his rounds. Kreese immediately takes Alan to the ground, and admonishes him for losing his concentration in a fight. Kreese then orders Alan to do 60 pushups on his knuckles as a punishment. He is shown wearing a brown belt, and would thus be eligible to compete in the All Valley Karate Tournament. If he did, then he did not advance to the elimination stage. Alan is not shown in the parking lot after the event, but would have heard what happened in the parking lot when Kreese attacked Johnny and his friends. Alan would leave Cobra Kai along with all of the other students over that. Alan is not shown in any of the scenes that take place at West Valley High School and may not have even gone to school there, going to some other school in the valley and training at Cobra Kai.

Barbara

Barbara was one of Ali Mills closest friends. She would witness the beginning of Daniel's relationship with Ali during the course of the day, and would be present to witness the first fight between Johnny and Daniel. She would make several appearances throughout the film, most notably during the Halloween party when Daniel LaRusso was dressed as a shower. Though she would appear in many scenes, she wouldn't have any lines. She was one of the people who despises Daniel when they first meet, saying how she doesn't know what Ali see's in him. She was present in the crowd with Freddy and Ali during the 1984 All Valley Karate Tournament, and would be one of the many that swarmed Daniel after winning the tournament.

Bruce

Bruce is a member of the All Valley Karate Tournament, where he serves as a member of their council, and also doubles as the event announcer for the 1984 All Valley Under 18 Karate Tournament. He can be seen doing sign ins, answering questions and generally helping get people where they need to be. He then acts as the public address announcer for the event. He shows his displeasure and is very quick to announce Bobby Brown's disqualification when Brown lands an illegal strike against Daniel LaRusso's knee during the semi-finals. Later, he is about to proclaim Johnny Lawrence as the 1984 champion, but is interrupted by Ali Mills, whom informs him that Daniel LaRusso will indeed fight. When Daniel defeats Johnny in the 1984 Finals, he makes the proclamation and is about to give Daniel the trophy, but Johnny Lawrence takes it from Bruce to hand it over to Daniel himself.

Chucky

Chucky was one of Freddy Fernandez 's friends that was present at the beach party in Karate Kid. He would witness the beginning of Daniel's relationship with Ali during the course of the day, and would be present to witness the first fight between Johnny and Daniel. He would appear the next day in school during soccer tryouts, making several comments to Daniel about losing to Johnny the way he did. He was present in the crowd with Freddy during the 1984 All Valley Karate Tournament, and would be one of the many that swarmed Daniel after winning the tournament.

Don

Don is a Cobra Kai member, along with Johnny, Bobby, Tommy, Dutch, and many others. He is present in the Cobra Kai Dojo when Daniel LaRusso enters, wishing to check out their training. He is present again at the dojo when Mister Miyagi and Daniel enter seeking a peace treaty with Johny and his allies. He is a brown belt, and competes in the 1984 All Valley Under 18 Karate Tournament. He is defeated in the preliminary round by Darryl Vidal. He can be seen impassive when he witnesses Bobby Brown's illegal strike against Daniel LaRusso in the semi-finals. He is not shown after the tournament, but would have learned about Kreese's assault on Johnny and the others in the parking lot from other students. He would leave Cobra Kai as a result of this. He is not shown in any of the scenes at West Valley High School and may not have even gone there, attending school elsewhere in the valley. He is never shown being part of Johhny's inner circle of friends.

Eddie

Eddie is a Cobra Kai member, along with Johnny, Bobby, Tommy, Jimmy, Dutch, Jerry, and many others. He is present in the Cobra Kai Dojo when Daniel LaRusso sneaks into the dojo to observe the training. He is in the dojo later when Mr Miyagi and Daniel enter to confront the Cobra Kais and the deal to settle things at the 1984 All Valley Karate Tournament. He has a black belt. He competes in the tournament, and is defeated by Daniel LaRusso in the preliminary stage of the tournament. He would not advance to the elimination round of the tournament.

Jerry Robertson

Jerry Robertson is a student at Cobra Kai Dojo , where he is a brown belt. He loses to fellow Cobra Kai student Bobby Brown in a brief sparring match. He is the first Cobra Kai to be defeated by Daniel LaRusso in the All Valley Karate Tournament , losing in the second round. He is last seen in the stands following Johnny's victory over Darryl Vidal in the semifinals.

  • He is portrayed by Larry B. Scott, who achieved fame later in 1984 for his portrayal of Lamar in Revenge of the Nerds and later, as Reggie in Iron Eagle .
  • Coincidentally, Johnny Lawrence mentions the film Iron Eagle in Season 1 of Cobra Kai , stating it's one of his favorite films.

Peter

Peter is a Cobra Kai member, along with Johnny , Bobby , Tommy , Jimmy , Dutch , Jerry , and many others. He is present when Daniel LaRusso has entered the dojo to check out the training, and sneaks out when he realizes that Johnny and his friends are all there. He is there later, when Mr Miyagi and Daniel have returned to confront the Cobra Kais and the deal to settle things at the All Valley Karate Tournament. He has a black belt. He competes at the 1984 All Valley Karate Tournament, but does not advance to the elimination stage of the event. He can be seen showing his disapproval when he witnesses Bobby Brown's illegal strike against Daniel in the the semi-finals. Peter is not shown in the parking lot after the event, but would have certainly learned of it from other students about what Kreese did to Johnny and the others after the event, and leaves Cobra Kai at that time. Peter is not shown at West Valley High School, and may not have even attended West Valley, going to another high school in the San Fernando Valley and only trained at Cobra Kai. He is never shown being in Johnny's inner circle of friends.

  • Andrew is the real life younger brother of Elisabeth Shue (Ali Mills).
  • He would go on to achieve great success in the 1990's drama Melrose Place , portraying Billy Campbell.

Yahoo #1 and Yahoo #2

The two yahoos are belligerent, drunken men who are at the beach when Daniel and Mr. Miyagi are busy training in the ocean, practicing balance. Mr. Miyagi and Daniel return to Mr. Miyagi's truck, to find two local yahoos there with beer bottles strewn all over the place. Both of them make condescending racial slurs against Mr. Miyagi, who kindly asks them to remove the bottles from the truck. Yahoo #1 mocks Mr. Miyagi's Asian appearance, and challenges him to do it himself with another racial slur. Calmly and with a single strike, Mr. Miyagi chops the beer bottles in half, which intimidates both yahoos; they immediately remove the bottles from the truck. Mr. Miyagi claims it is the first time he has ever done that.

  • Larry Drake is one of the yahoos. He would go on to win several Emmy awards for Outstanding Supporting Actor in a Dramatic series for his role as Benny Stulwicz in the hit 1980's drama L.A. LAW.
