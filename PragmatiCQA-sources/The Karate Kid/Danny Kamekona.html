Danny Kamekona (November 15, 1935-May 2, 1996) was a Japanese-American actor who played Sato Toguchi in The Karate Kid Part II .

Kamekona was found dead in his Los Angeles, California apartment on May 12, 1996, at the age of 60. The cause of his death was never revealed.
