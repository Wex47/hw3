Yuji Okumoto (born Yuji Don Okumoto ) is a Japanese-American actor who played Chozen Toguchi in The Karate Kid Part II , and in Season 3 and Season 4 of Cobra Kai .

Career

Yuji Okumoto was born on April 20, 1959 in Los Angeles, California. In addition to playing Chozen Toguchi in The Karate Kid Part II (1986), he has also appeared in Inception (2010) and Johnny Tsunami (1999). He reprised his role as Chozen in Cobra Kai for the show's third season .

Personal Life

Yuji Okumoto has been married to Angie Okumoto (née Mar) since 2001, and together they have three daughters. He and his family live in Seattle, Washington, where they own a Hawaiian-themed restaurant: Kona Kitchen.
