History

The Karate Kid

Golf N' Stuff first appears in the original Karate Kid movie, when Ali and Daniel go on their first date. They play air hockey, golf and take photos in the photo booth. Their time at Golf N' Stuff isn't broken down completely, only the key moments of there time there. The arcade is then re-visited after Daniel sees Johnny kiss Ali. Ali and her friends try to shrug off Daniel when he confronts he about it. Once the truth is revealed, Ali and Daniel share a kiss outside of the arcade.

Cobra Kai

Season 1

After breaking up with Kyler , Sam is asked to go on a date with Miguel . She explains she doesn't want to start a new relationship right after breaking up with Kyler so Miguel insists that it isn't a date and the would just hang out as friends instead. They go to Golf N' Stuff, Sam explains she used to always go there when she was a kid. The pair spend their night there and at the end of it all share a kiss leading to them becoming a couple. In the episode Molting, Johnny mentions to Miguel that he and Ali went on their first date to Golf N' Stuff and kissed on the ferris wheel.

Season 3

During the episode Miyagi-Do , Hawk , Mitch , Mikey and Doug cause trouble at Golf N' Stuff. They smack a basketball out of a kids hand and take a girls pile of tickets before coming face to face with former friend and Miyagi-Do student Chris who is also an employee at the arcade. They steal toys from behind the counter, resulting to Chris texting Sam and later on fighting Cobra Kai in a deserted laser tag arena.

The arcade's latest appearance is when Ali and Johnny hang out together and re-live the good times they shared in the 80s.

Trivia

  • Golf N' Stuff is notorious for being a go-to for first dates, as seen in the 1980s with Daniel LaRusso and Ali Mills . It appears again in the 2000s when Miguel Diaz and Samantha LaRusso have their first date here.
  • Chris is the only major character to be an employee at Golf N' Stuff.
  • Golf N' Stuff has been seen in The Karate Kid , Ace Degenerate (archive footage), All Valley , Pulpo (flashback), Now You're Gonna Pay (flashback), Miyagi-Do , and Feel The Night .
