Overview

Netflix is a multi-million dollar streaming platform company that originated in the United States. It initially offered a delivery DVD service for a monthly fee, before branching into digital streaming. With its branch to digital media, Netflix quickly became one of the largest hubs for television and movies.

Karate Kid Franchise

Films

Netflix initially held the streaming rights to all of the Karate Kid films, with the exception of the spinoff film and 2010 remake . The rights to the films fluctuated as they and went from the platform throughout 2020 and 2021.

Cobra Kai

After YouTube Premium canceled Cobra Kai Netflix picked up the streaming sensation for the a third season. While the platform initially only planned to air the third season, the success of the series prompted them to renew the series through a fourth season. All seasons of the series were made available for streaming upon a membership subscription.
