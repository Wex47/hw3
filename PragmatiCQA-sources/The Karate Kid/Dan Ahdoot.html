Dan Ahdoot is an Afghan-Iranian Jewish American actor, comedian and writer. He plays Anoush in Cobra Kai .

Biography

Ahdoot was born and raised in Great Neck, New York. He completed pre-med studies at John Hopkins University but dropped out to pursue stand up comedy. His most notable acting works outside of Cobra Kai are Workaholics , Shameless , Kickin It , Bajillion Dollar Propertie$ , Super Fun Night and more.
