Bret Ernst is an American actor and stand up comedian. He plays Louie LaRusso Jr. in Cobra Kai .

Biography

Ernst was born in Princeton, New Jersey. He has an older brother named Scott, who was a theatre actor that passed away at the age of 33, and a younger brother named Keith, who is also a stand up comedian. Ernst grew up in Passaic, New Jersey. He went to Plantation High School in Plantation, Florida. He went to Long Island University and played College Football.
