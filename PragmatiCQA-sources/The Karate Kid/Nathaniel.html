Nathaniel "Nate" is a supporting character in Cobra Kai .

He joins the Cobra Kai Dojo at first, but after realizing that Cobra Kai's ruthless antics are going too far, he switches to Miyagi-Do Karate . Despite maintaining Cobra Kai's mentality when he first arrives at Miyagi-Do, he is soon able to let go of that and becomes a part of Miyagi-Do.

Biography

Season 2

Nate was part of a wave of teenagers that joined the Cobra Kai Dojo shortly after seeing Cobra Kai's success at the All Valley Karate Tournament . However, after Daniel LaRusso , the sensei of the rival Miyagi-Do Karate Dojo confronts Cobra Kai's sensei Johnny Lawrence about trashing his dojo, Nathaniel leaves the dojo along with Chris , Frank , Abe , and Lil Red , realizing that Cobra Kai's ruthless teachings have gone too far.

After leaving Cobra Kai, Nathaniel along with the others join Miyagi-Do Karate, much to the chagrin of the Miyagi-Do students Sam , Robby and Demetri . Despite struggling to grasp the teachings of this new dojo, as they were already into the Cobra Kai philosophy, Nate and the other students eventually started to work together as a team.

At Moon's party on the last night before the first day of school, Nathaniel is shown participating in a dance battle with Edwin , with the crowd cheering for him. After Demetri roasts Hawk at the party, he is seen with the rest of the Miyagi-Do's getting ready to fight the Cobra Kai's right before the police showed up and everyone at the party had to run away.

When a school karate war occurs on the first day of school, Nathaniel fights the Cobra Kai student Bert , and initially gains the upper hand. However, after some time Bert is able to break his glasses; an angry Nathaniel brutally tackles Bert and starts punching him until a security guard picks them both up (and possibly takes them to the principal’s office).

Season 3

After Miguel Diaz gets a broken spine at the end of the karate war at school, the Miyagi-Do students make a fundraiser to fund Miguel's surgery, with Nathaniel collecting the money. At the end of the day however, Hawk , Mitch and two other Cobra Kai's corner Nathaniel, taking the money from him and beating him up. After being beaten up, Nathaniel and the other Miyagi-Do's show up at Sam's house, where he tells her what happened, this prompts Sam to re-open Miyagi-Do and train with Nathaniel and the others without his father.

Later on, when Sam attempts to form a truce between Miyagi-Do and Johnny's Eagle Fang Karate Dojo to fight against Cobra Kai at her house, most initially disagree, with Nathaniel and Bert insulting each other. However, everyone soon agrees after a speech from Demetri telling them off. Unfortunately, the same night Sam's house was broken into by the Cobra Kai students lead by Tory, who attacked both dojos in an all-out brawl. During the battle, Nate seems to be fighting with Bert and Demetri before being thrown at a christmas tree, he then is seen fighting one Cobra Kai student along with Bert. Nate's side eventually wins the fight because of Hawk defecting and siding with the Miyagi-Do's and Eagle Fang's.

The morning after the fight, Miyagi-Do and Eagle Fang merge into one and are training at Miyagi-Do's dojo, with Johnny and Daniel as their senseis. Now befriending each other, Nathaniel and Bert exchange high-fives before training.

Personality

Nathaniel is an impressionable, foul-mouthed and cocky nerdy child. Even after joining Miyagi-Do in season 2 , he initially has trouble letting go of the Cobra Kai creed of "Strike First. Strike Hard. No Mercy." nor does his training limit his aggression as seen when he fights Bert. However, he does show prominent skill in karate, as shown he was able to overpower and pummel Bert without needing his glasses to see.

In season 3 , he helps out with the fundraiser for Miguel's surgery. However, after the Cobra Kai's take the money from the fundraiser which Nathaniel was holding on to, and beat him up, Nathaniel complains to Sam. Later on, when Sam attempts to get Miyagi-Do and Eagle Fang to form a truce, most disagree, with Nathaniel and Bert trading insults, though everyone eventually agrees thanks to Demetri giving them a good telling-off. The day after the truce and fight with Cobra Kai, Nathaniel is shown to be friendly with Bert. The students begin training with Daniel and Johnny.

In season 4, both he and Bert are friends, and they bully Kenny who has recently joined Cobra Kai.

Fighting Style

In his fight with Bert during the school brawl, Nathaniel uses his defensive skills to counter Bert's strikes, and offensive skills to hit and take down Bert. Even though Bert breaks his glasses, he is able to take him down and land several punches on his face, before the fight is broken up by a security guard. Despite this, in Season 3 Nate is seen to be easily overwhelmed when the Cobras steal the fundraiser money and during the fight at Sam's house, he and Bert have trouble keeping up with the other kids due to their height. In season 4, he is defeated by Kenny in the All Valley Tournament, partly due to Kenny utilizing Miyagi Do's technique he learned from Robby, Despite his skill being better than Bert's, Nate is easily one of the weaker fighters of the series and posible the weaker one from Miyagi-Do.
