Japan , officially the State of Japan , is an island country in East Asia, bounded by the Pacific Ocean on the east and south, the Sea of Japan on the west and the Sea of Okhotsk on the north. It is a constitutional monarchy headed by an emperor. Its capital city is Tokyo . Mr. Miyagi is from Okinawa , Japan. Other notable residents of Okinawa , Japan include Chozen Toguchi , Kumiko , Sato Toguchi , Taro , Toshio , Yuna , and Yukie .
