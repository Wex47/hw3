The Bay of a Thousand Scales is a spiral of islands that make up the "tail" of the dragon -shaped continent of Pyrrhia , and could be considered to be an archipelago. It is said to have hundreds of islands, making it easy to hide the Summer Palace among them. It is the only landmass in the Kingdom of the Sea , and is ruled by Coral . The bay is also home to the ruins of the Summer Palace, which were enchanted centuries ago by Albatross , an animus SeaWing , to grow on its own from a rock. In addition, it is home to the Deep Palace and the location of the Island Palace . The SeaWings are currently working on a new palace that is not hidden and will be used for diplomacy and Queen Coral's scroll readings.

Description

The Bay of a Thousand Scales is composed of vast expanses of clear, empty ocean, dotted here and there with solitary outcroppings of rock. Green-and-white islands glow like scattered jewels in the ocean. Several of them are shaped like claws, curving neatly through the water. Some of the islands are small patches of sand, barely big enough for a dragon to land on, and others are composed of towering, jagged rocks shooting out of the water. The Bay is circled by a spur of land that resembles a dragon's tail, becoming narrower as it reaches the end. There are palm trees, islands, coastlines, beaches, and cliffs. These islands also contain several human settlements that were described to look like miniature castles surrounded by by small fortresses and several fires.

An island that was used by Blister as a soldier encampment was described to be a wildly overgrown jungle, with a hollow between several fallen trees, palm fronds, and tree trunks. Through a break in the jungle, there is a lagoon that is overlooked by a shadowy tree containing bushes, spiderwebs, and large bird nests.

Many of the islands are not listed on the map, as hundreds cannot be charted. There is a sea breeze.

The SeaWing palaces are located in the Bay of a Thousand Scales, and they are also the home to some more private dragons that live by themselves.

History

The Dragonet Prophecy

The Lost Heir

The Bay of a Thousand Scales was both the place where Riptide first met the dragonets of destiny and the location of Blister's hideout. The Summer Palace was hidden on one of the islands, along with the SeaWings' above-water prisons, and is the book's main setting. Kestrel , after being murdered by Blister and Morrowseer , was also found on an island close to the Summer Palace. It was firebombed in a SkyWing attack, and is currently in ruins.

The Jade Mountain Prophecy

Talons of Power

The Bay of a Thousand Scales was seen near the end when Turtle travels there to stop Anemone from massacring her tribe.
