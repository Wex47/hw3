Piranha is an adult female SeaWing who was introduced in The Lost Heir . She is a soldier under Coral and a member of the SeaWing council as War Commander. She currently resides in the Kingdom of the Sea . She was Tempest 's third-in-command until Tempest's assassination.

Appearance

Piranha is wiry, with small eyes and dark gray-blue scales. She is disfigured from many battle scars, has multiple broken teeth, and half of one of her horns is broken off. Tsunami stated that she appeared to have "more scars than scales."

Personality

Piranha often speaks in a growl or a barking tone of voice. When Tempest died, she reacted with snarling and fury. She was quick to blame as suggested when she snapped at the MudWings ordering them to be killed when Tempest was assasinated.

Biography

The Dragonet Prophecy

The Lost Heir
After Kestrel 's body was found a few islands away from the Summer Palace , Queen Coral ordered Commander Shark and Piranha to fly with her to the body.

Afterward, Coral called the SeaWing Council to a Council meeting. It was there that Tsunami realized she was the war commander, as she sat in the war pool. Piranha reported to Coral that there was still "no information on the dead SkyWing ." She then brought in two wounded soldiers, one of them being Herring's brother , and tells them to report about a recent battle they had with the SkyWings to Coral.

Later, when Webs arrived at the Summer Palace, Piranha captured a SeaWing suspiciously lurking around the same area. This SeaWing turned out to be Riptide , and both Webs and Riptide were imprisoned in the Electric Eel Prison .

Winglets

Assassin
Piranha was first seen coming out of the water next to Commander Tempest and was seen as her third-in-command. She became furious at the MudWings, thinking that they murdered Tempest due to the murder weapon being a MudWing spear.

Relationships

Tempest

Piranha seemed to care a lot about her commander as she shrieked with rage when Tempest died. She immediately blamed the MudWings on Tempest's death, demanding for them to be executed immediately.
