Biography

The Jade Mountain Prophecy

Moon Rising

While Secretkeeper was dropping Moonwatcher off at Jade Mountain Academy, Moonwatcher spotted two MudWing dragonets laughing and racing to the school. It was likely that one of these MudWings was Newt and the other was Sepia .

After Sunny and the rest of the dragonets made the speech on the bombing in the history cave , Newt was seen briefly walking with Sepia, looking for a muddy spot to lie in around Jade Mountain .
