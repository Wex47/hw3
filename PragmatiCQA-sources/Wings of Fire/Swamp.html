Swamp is an adult male MudWing who was introduced in Assassin . He served as a MudWing general and was one of the MudWings located in Blister's hideout at the time.

Personality

Swamp appeared to have a short temper and was easily agitated, as shown through his reaction to Piranha 's message. He was extremely loyal to his tribe, as he quickly insisted that they had done nothing wrong and threatened a SandWing heir in defense of his own tribe. Swamp was also quick to point the blame at others, as shown as when he blamed Blister for Tempest 's death.

Biography

Winglets

Assassin

General Swamp, along with several MudWing soldiers, were seen meeting with Princess Blister and Commander Tempest to discuss a move for the SandWing throne. After dropping his weapons, he and his troops entered a tent for a meeting with Blister's forces. Commander Tempest was assassinated by Deathbringer a short moment after; the guards stationed outside the tent were blamed, due to how she was killed by a MudWing spear and how no one else was around to witness it other than the then unconscious Quickstrike . This led to a note accusing the SeaWings of making a secret deal with Blister. Swamp stated that if his soldiers were punished, he would revoke his alliance with the latter princess. Seeing as the MudWings were, shortly afterward, he revoked the alliance.
