Sepia is a female MudWing dragonet who was introduced in Moon Rising . She is currently attending Jade Mountain Academy as a member of the Silver Winglet .

Biography

The Jade Mountain Prophecy

Moon Rising

While being dropped off at Jade Mountain , Moonwatcher spotted two MudWings laughing and racing toward the school. It was likely that one of the MudWings was Sepia and the other was Newt .

Sepia was in the music room when the Jade Winglet and Silver Winglet were put together. She danced, along with Anemone , Turtle , Kinkajou , and Boto , to Qibli 's drumming.

Sepia was later shown tramping down the mountainside with Newt to find somewhere cool and muddy to lie down.

Darkness of Dragons

Sepia attended a history class along with the remnants of the Gold and Jade Winglets. When Winter defended Darkstalker after he was called a murderer, Sepia called Icicle a murderer and questioned his judgment. When Winter responded that Sora was the one that blew up the history cave, Sepia calmly answered that it was because of Icicle's actions in the first place. Qibli had to step between the two to prevent a fight, and the class got back to studying after an interjection from Webs .
