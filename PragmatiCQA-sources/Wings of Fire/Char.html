Char was an adult male SandWing who was introduced in Deserter . He served as the SandWing king alongside Oasis until he was killed by a mysterious illness that swept the SandWing stronghold .

Appearance

Char was tall and burly.

Personality

Char was stern and gentle. He appeared pleased with himself after recruiting Dune and Six-Claws into the army.

Biography

Winglets

Deserter

When Six-Claws saved Blaze from the sandstorm, Char scolded his daughter for venturing outside of the palace. Blister asked him if they should point out the culprit who lured Blaze into the desert, and Dune piped up in Six-Claws' defense. Upon noticing Six-Claws' claws, Blaze jumped away in fear and rudely criticized his talons, which resulted in another scolding from Char. Char, pleased by Six-Claws' deed, invited both Six-Claws and Dune to join the army as loyal soldiers of the queen.

Char is later said to have died of a mysterious sickness that swept through the palace, the same one that killed Six-Claws' father, Quicksand , sometime before Oasis was murdered by humans .

Relationships

Oasis

Char only had as much power as Queen Oasis let him have. Sometimes he accompanied her everywhere, welcomed into advisory meetings and diplomatic gatherings, and sometimes they would fight and he would be exiled for the palace for months at a time. He often fell out of favor with Oasis.
