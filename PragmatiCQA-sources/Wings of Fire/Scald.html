Scald was an adult male SandWing who was introduced in Deserter . He was one of Char and Oasis 's three sons. He was killed by Burn during the War of SandWing Succession .

The Dragonet Prophecy

The Brightest Night

Scald was mentioned indirectly when Smolder told Sunny that his brothers were killed because they "made the wrong dragons mad." This included Scald, though he was not named.

Winglets

Deserter

Scald was shown to be ineffectively comforting Smolder after the disappearance of Palm . He was later killed by Burn. It was mentioned that he had three girlfriends.
