Dazzling is an adult female RainWing who was introduced in The Hidden Kingdom . She was one of the six RainWing queens . Dazzling was listed as the current queen in A NightWing Guide to the Dragons of Pyrrhia before Glory .

Appearance

Dazzling is a quite large and fat RainWing.

Personality

Dazzling, as described by Kinkajou , was a queen who would grant anybody's petition as long as they gave her enough tribute. She appeared to be greedy and confident, eating the fruits intended for the contest despite risking the chance of the opponent winning the round.

Biography

The Dragonet Prophecy

The Hidden Kingdom

Dazzling participated in the fruit‐gathering contest against Mangrove during the competition for the RainWing throne. It was revealed when the fruit was counted that Dazzling had eaten three of the nineteen fruits Magnificent had set up to help ensure a victory. Dazzling lost the competition by one fruit.
