Kindle is an adult female SandWing who was introduced in Deserter . She formerly worked as a nurse in Burn 's army, likely in the same encampment as Six-Claws on the outskirts of the Ice Kingdom , but later fled to the Scorpion Den with Dune and Six-Claws. She is currently in a relationship with Six-Claws.

Personality

Kindle tried her best to heal Dune's injuries, and seemed like she wanted to intervene against Burn's cruelty but did not dare. She was horrified by Burn and pitied Dune, and she always seemed calm and efficient when taking care of her other patients.

Biography

The Jade Mountain Prophecy

Darkness of Dragons
Though she did not appear directly, Qibli remembered how she and Six-Claws asked him to look after Ostrich . A drawing of her and Six-Claws is shown in Ostrich's cave while Qibli was searching Jade Mountain Academy for her, later discovering that Ostrich had been taken captive by Qibli's grandfather, Vulture .

Winglets

Deserter
Kindle was formerly a nurse in Burn's army, specifically stationed on the outskirts of the Ice Kingdom with Six-Claws' battalion, but left with Six-Claws to help carry Dune after Burn ripped apart Dune's wing (as a demonstration to Six-Claws of what would happen when her orders were questioned repeatedly and meaningfully). The trio then fled to the Scorpion Den together to flee the horrors of Burn's army.
