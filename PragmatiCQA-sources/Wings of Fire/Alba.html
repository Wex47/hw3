Biography

The Jade Mountain Prophecy

Moon Rising

Moon mentioned a trio of IceWings, which Alba was likely a part of, along with the other IceWing students, Changbai and Ermine . At the assembly, Winter and Icicle were surrounded by four other IceWings, forming a sort of barricade between them and the other dragons. Alba was likely with them.

Talons of Power

Alba was shown in the library with two other IceWings, Changbai and Ermine, and may have been the dragonet to attack and then get pinned to the ground by Darkstalker when he arrived at Jade Mountain .
