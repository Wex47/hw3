Boto is a male RainWing dragonet who was introduced in Moon Rising . He is currently attending Jade Mountain Academy as part of the Silver Winglet .

Appearance

Boto is placid-looking.

Biography

The Jade Mountain Prophecy

Moon Rising

He was described to be dancing with both Sepia and Kinkajou when the Jade and Silver Winglets used instruments together in the music room at Jade Mountain Academy. Later in the book, he was shown resting in a patch of sunlight along with two other RainWing students, Coconut and Siamang .

Talons of Power

Boto appeared when Peril had accidentally set a scroll on fire, which turned out to be his homework. He stammers an apology to her after Peril says to keep his mess in his cave. Boto turned an "even paler shade of green" and vanished into his cave after Peril apologized and said he should be thankful it was not his tail.

Darkness of Dragons

Qibli saw Boto attending a history class along with the Jade, Gold, and Silver Winglets. When Darkstalker 's murderous past was brought up, Boto's talons turned lime green, and he began anxiously asking whether it was likely that they would be murdered.
