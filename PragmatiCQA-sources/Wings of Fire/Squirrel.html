Squirrel is an adult male human who was introduced in Dragonslayer . He currently lives in Valor and is a Wingwatcher .

Personality

Squirrel appears to be cautious, as he continually tries to keep Daffodil and Violet from raising their voices while outside of Valor, preferring to stay behind with Ivy , Daffodil, and Violet while Foxglove scouted the situation with the ice dragon .

Biography

Legends

Dragonslayer

Squirrel was guarding the exit to Valor alongside Foxglove. He taught Ivy how to become a Wingwatcher illegally and was known for illegally conspiring against Heath , Ivy's father. He also went up into a tree with Violet, Daffodil, and Ivy to make sure they didn't get hurt by any dragons .

Squirrel was there when Ivy, Daffodil, and Violet went searching for Heath's hidden treasure. Later in the book, he was mentioned to be taken prisoner by Heath.
