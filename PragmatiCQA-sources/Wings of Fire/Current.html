Current was an adult male SeaWing who was introduced in Darkstalker . He resided in the Kingdom of the Sea as a prince , and was a survivor of the Royal SeaWing Massacre .

Appearance

Current was large but thin, with blue scales and haunted blue eyes.

Personality

Current is polite. After the Royal SeaWing Massacre, Current was haunted, grieving, and shivered constantly. He could not look at Fathom without flinching away, and was desperately in denial about the true nature of Albatross . He was a shell of his former laughing, confident, easy-to-smile self.

Biography

Legends

Darkstalker

Prince Current was first mentioned indirectly by Fathom when he said that his cousins mostly ignored him, politely uninterested in his stories.

Pearl later said Current liked "those macadamia things," and would eat them all. He escaped the massacre by flinging himself into the ocean. Current swam nonstop for days before being washed into a cave in the Night Kingdom , where he was discovered by Clearsight and Darkstalker . He then was brought to Queen Vigilance 's palace, where he remained for some time until he was sent back to the SeaWings with a message and an offer. When Fathom was summoned by Queen Pearl to the throne room, Current was brought in. He seemed haunted by Fathom and begs the guards to not be forced to look at him. Current is led away at his own request.

Relationships

Albatross

Much like most of the other royal SeaWings, Current loved Albatross and was in disbelief after the massacre. He was in denial, claiming that something else must have turned the animus mad. Albatross's massacre left Current in a traumatized state, affecting him to the point where he grew anxious around Albatross's grandson Fathom.

Fathom

Current is said to mostly ignore Fathom. Despite being his cousin, Current's relationship with Fathom is strained after the massacre. He seems to be traumatized due to Albatross and fears Fathom due to his magic.
