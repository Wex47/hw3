Biography

The Dragonet Prophecy

The Lost Heir

Pearl was seen eating breakfast with Queen Coral , their daughters, and their council. Pearl was the dragon who sat in the Treasury pool. Queen Coral asked Pearl to report and Pearl replied by saying that all of Queen Coral's jewels were safe, that scavengers cannot get to them, and no one would dare steal them.
