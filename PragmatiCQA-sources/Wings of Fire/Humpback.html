Humpback was an elderly male SeaWing who was introduced in Darkstalker . He served as the king of the SeaWing tribe alongside Lagoon , and resided in the Kingdom of the Sea before being murdered by Albatross during the Royal SeaWing Massacre .

Appearance

Humpback had a creaky old voice.

Personality

Humpback would often corner others into conversation about the grand old days of his youth.

Biography

The Jade Mountain Prophecy

Moon Rising

Although not mentioned by name, Humpback was alluded to within the The Animus Histories as one of the Royal SeaWing Massacre's nine victims.

Talons of Power

Although not mentioned by name, Humpback was mentioned as one of the victims murdered by Albatross during the Royal SeaWing Massacre.

Legends

Darkstalker

When Pearl , Fathom , and Indigo were getting dressed for the party, Pearl helped adjust Indigo's pearls. Indigo thanked her, and Pearl told Indigo that in return, she could distract Humpback whenever he tried to drone on and on about the old days and his scavenger supply to her. This broke the three into a laughing fit.

At the party, Humpback was seen alongside his wife, Queen Lagoon, and their daughter, Splash . He was stabbed and killed by Albatross immediately after Albatross killed Lagoon.
