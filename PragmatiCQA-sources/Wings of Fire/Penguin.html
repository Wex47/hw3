Biography

Legends

Darkstalker

In the prologue, Arctic mentioned to Foeslayer that Penguin created the gift of diplomacy, which contributed to making the area around the IceWing guest dome warmer so that guests from other tribes would feel more comfortable. Arctic considered improving the dome so it would be protected from fire, but dismissed it. Arctic wanted to make a very useful and important animus gift, like Frostbite 's gift of light .
