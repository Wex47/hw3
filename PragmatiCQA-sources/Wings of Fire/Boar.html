Personality

Boar possesses an aura of someone who would cheerfully kick anyone in the face for money. He is dramatic.

Biography

Legends

Dragonslayer

Boar was a spy sent by the Invincible Lord to watch Heath , under the alias that he was a close guard.

He appeared in the epilogue, bringing back news to the Invincible Lord, who had summoned Undauntable . Boar talked about the events in Valor he had witnessed, including Heath losing his role as lord of Valor, and of Wren and Sky scaring Heath into giving back the treasure he stole from Queen Oasis .
