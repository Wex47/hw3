Appearance

Meerkat is young and handsome.

Personality

Meerkat seems friendly or welcoming, giving Winter and his friends a friendly nod. He also appears to have a powerful authority over others, as he managed to tell off Eagle , though this might be due to Meerkat's rank in the Enclave.

Biography

The Jade Mountain Prophecy

Winter Turning

Meerkat is an acquaintance of Qibli 's who appeared briefly, escorting Kinkajou and the others to Mayfly 's office. They are said to have met when Meerkat was paying tribute to the new queen , Thorn . Eagle is shown to respect him when he breaks up the conflict between Eagle and confused Hailstorm by providing him with a cover story about supposedly having a head injury. He seems to have the authority of some force called the Enclave, which he refers to when telling Eagle to back down.
