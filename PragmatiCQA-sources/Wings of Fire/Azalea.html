Azalea is a female human who was introduced in Dragonslayer . She was banished from Valor by Heath , and was formerly a Wingwatcher .

Appearance

Azalea has short, wild hair and wears a torn and threadbare forest-green Wingwatcher uniform.

Personality

Azalea was mentioned to have kicked the guards at her banishment. She was angry and distrustful of Ivy no matter what the latter would say, despite it not being her fault.

Biography

Legends

Dragonslayer

Azalea appeared when Ivy, Daffodil , Violet , and Foxglove were searching the ruins of the burned-down village for the Dragonslayer's treasure. Foxglove asked the three girls if they could keep a secret, and when they said yes, Azalea, along with Pine , emerged from the shadows of the city. Foxglove introduced them, and Azalea angrily asked Foxglove why she had brought Ivy to the burnt village, stating that they had already discussed not to do it. Ivy then remembered the latter kicking the guards at her banishment. Azalea was surprised when Foxglove told her that Ivy asked to help and addressed her as a princess, asking her what she planned to do with the treasure. When the group heard a dragon overhead, Azalea instantly dove under a pile of leaves in a corner.
