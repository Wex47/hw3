Truthfinder was an adult female NightWing who was introduced in Darkstalker . She served as the principal of the NightWing school within the lost city of night , and had the ability to read minds.

Appearance

Truthfinder had one silver teardrop scale glittering in the outside corner of each of her eyes.

Personality

Truthfinder was tired and forgetful, and spoke slowly, sternly, suspiciously, and absently towards Clearsight .

Biography

Legends

Darkstalker

She was first seen giving Clearsight the scroll to her classroom on her first day of school.

Clearsight, when wondering if she could study her future scrolls, saw that she and her parents would have an uncomfortable trip to Truthfinder's office in the future if she did.

Clearsight mentioned her later when trying to convince Darkstalker to shelter her thoughts from mind readers.

Later, the scroll that Darkstalker enchanted was described to sound like her when it was giving a history report.
