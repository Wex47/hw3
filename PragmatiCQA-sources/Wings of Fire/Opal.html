Opal was a female IceWing animus who was introduced in The Dangerous Gift . She is a spirit who guides inexperienced IceWing queens into becoming more open-minded and helps them learn important lessons through the gift of vision .

Appearance

Opal has unusual scales that have a hint of another color beyond the white in each one, similar to an opal. She is elegant.

Personality

When speaking to Snowfall , Opal hid a smile and laughed good-naturedly. She smiled and wished Snowfall good luck before disappearing.

Biography

The Lost Continent Prophecy

The Dangerous Gift

Snowfall often yelled at the gift of vision and its creator. She wondered what kind of IceWing animus would do this, along with who would even ask for it.

Opal appeared in Snowfall's vision the night after she returned from Sanctuary . She explained to Snowfall how the ring appeared for queens who were not quite ready to rule and gave her one final vision as Glacier . After the vision, the ring slid off of Snowfall's claw easily and disappeared when put back on the pedestal where she first found it.
