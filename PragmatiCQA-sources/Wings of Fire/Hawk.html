Personality

Hawk is usually good-humored around other dragons, especially his sisters. Ruby suspects this is because he has seen Scarlet kill many of his sisters and that his humor is from knowing that he is not important enough to be killed. He was the only one of Ruby's brothers who was kind to her. Hawk also seems to have a strong belief in SkyWing law, showing animosity towards Peril due to her having firescales .

Escaping Peril

Hawk appeared in the prologue as one of the important dragons of that Queen Scarlet ordered into her throne room. When Ruby arrived in the throne room, Hawk gave her a "friendlyish" nudge of acknowledgment. When Hawk and the others were first introduced to Peril, he hissed that she should have been killed and how it was an abomination to keep a firescales dragon alive.
