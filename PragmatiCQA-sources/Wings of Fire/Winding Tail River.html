The Winding Tail River is a river in the southern half of the Sky Kingdom .

Description

The Winding Tail River begins high in the Claws of the Clouds Mountains and runs past Jade Mountain into the sea. Near a bend in the river and west of Queen Moorhen 's Lake, there is a scavenger den that is believed to be the largest in Pyrrhia . The river is surrounded by mountains and forests. There are tiny, golden, silver fish in the river, as well as prey that is rumored as much easier to catch than the humans .

History

The Jade Mountain Prophecy

Winter Turning

Kinkajou , Moonwatcher , Qibli , and Winter passed through the Winding Tail River on their way to search for Scarlet . They stopped to catch wild rabbits and collect peaches, and while resting on a cliff face, the group was shot at by a flaming projectile weapon from a nearby scavenger den. Winter saved their lives by breathing frostbreath on the spear in midair.

Escaping Peril

Peril and Turtle followed the river on their way to Possibility to track down Scarlet.

Talons of Power

Turtle passed through the Winding Tail River on his way to the Kingdom of the Sea to prevent Anemone from murdering her tribe .
