Octopus is a male SeaWing dragonet prince , hatchmate to Turtle and Cerulean , and one of the thirty-two sons of Gill and Coral . He currently lives in the Deep Palace .

Biography

The Dragonet Prophecy

The Lost Heir

When Tsunami asks Anemone if she has any brothers, Anemone says that she has lots. Octopus is one of their brothers.

The Jade Mountain Prophecy

Moon Rising

Prince Octopus was indirectly mentioned when Turtle told Tsunami that she had thirty-two brothers.

Talons of Power

Octopus was shown in the prologue with his hatchmates, Turtle and Cerulean, when King Gill came to ask them for help. He was also shown at the Talons of Power ceremony with his two brothers.

Throughout the book, Turtle often thought about him and Cerulean, including how he enchanted a piece of kelp to be faster than them in races and how his brothers exiled him for a month after the incident involving Snapper .

Darkness of Dragons

Coral indirectly mentions him in the letter she sent to Turtle, noting that Turtle's brothers believed he played a prank on them last time he was at the palace, warning him to check his bed for lobsters the next time he visits.
