Description

The palace's location is approximately a day's flight away from the rainforest , and is nearby a large lake leading into the Diamond Spray Delta . It is located near the southern edge of the Mud Kingdom , as Kestrel says the "lowest born MudWings crawl" near the outskirts of the kingdom.
