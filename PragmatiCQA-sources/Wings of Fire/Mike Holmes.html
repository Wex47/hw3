Mike Holmes is a Nova Scotia-based illustrator. He is the lineartist of the graphic novel adaptations of The Dragonet Prophecy , The Lost Heir , The Hidden Kingdom , The Dark Secret , and The Brightest Night . The lineart was then colored by Maarta Laiho . He has also worked on other graphic novel series, such as Secret Coders .
