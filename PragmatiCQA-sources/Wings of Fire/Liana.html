Liana is a female RainWing who was introduced in The Hidden Kingdom . She was one of the first RainWings, alongside Jambu , who Glory and Starflight encountered after Clay , Tsunami , Webs , and Sunny were tranquillized with RainWing sleeping darts . She currently resides in the Rainforest Kingdom .

Appearance

Liana's scales are usually dark blue with dapples of gold.

Personality

Liana is rude, lazy, and cheeky. She wrinkled her nose at the thought of spending time on hunting and noted that Glory's scales were "mousy." She expressed surprise and irritation when Glory asked her to define suntime — Glory speculated that the former had hoped to prod more of a reaction after showing off her scales. Liana also laughed when she scared Starflight for fun, stating that it was "so worth it." When choosing members for the RainWing Royal Challenge , Glory mentioned that Liana did not seem like a team player.

Biography

The Dragonet Prophecy

The Hidden Kingdom

After the dragonets of destiny were taken to the Rainforest Kingdom, Liana informed Glory what the tranquilizer darts were and explained what the concept of suntime was. She was visibly irritated when Glory reacted calmly to not knowing about those things. Glory thought that Liana realized she was stolen from the hatcheries , which led Starflight to figure out that the lack of sunshine in the caves the dragonets of destiny had grown up in had made Glory harsher and grumpier, her scales duller than a typical RainWing's. Liana and the rest of the RainWing group then led the dragonets back to the RainWing village, where suntime began and Liana left. Later, Liana returned to escort the dragonets to Magnificent 's room.

In Part Three of the book, Glory considered having Liana on her team for the RainWing Royal Challenge, but picked Tamarin instead.

The Dark Secret

Liana and Grandeur used the NightWing spears to free captured RainWings. Liana was then seen completing tasks with Grandeur.
