(Not to be confused with the Winglets of Jade Mountain Academy , which are groups of different dragonets .)

Wings of Fire: Winglets is a series of books written by Tui T. Sutherland as a companion to the Wings of Fire series.

The first Winglets book is titled Prisoners , which is told from Fierceteeth 's perspective. The book came out on March 31, 2015. For plot details, see its page.

The second Winglets book is titled Assassin , which focuses on Deathbringer 's perspective. It was released on September 29, 2015. For plot details, see its page.

The third Winglets book is titled Deserter , which is told from Six-Claws ' perspective. It was released on March 29, 2016. For plot details, see its page.

The fourth Winglets book is titled Runaway , which is told from Foeslayer 's, Snowflake 's and Arctic 's perspectives. It was released on September 27, 2016. For plot details, see its page.

The first three Winglets ( Prisoners , Assassin , and Deserter ) have been released as a paperback collection called A Winglets Collection: The First Three Stories . The book was first available to be ordered and arrived by late September or early October of 2016.

A flip story sampler or flip book was released exclusively on Independent Bookstore Day, April 27, 2019. It has Assassin and Deserter with a letter from Tui T. Sutherland.

The Winglets Quartet is a collection of the first four winglets e-books: Prisoners , Assassin , Deserter , and Runaway . It was released on October 6, 2020.
