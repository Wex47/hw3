Listener was a female NightWing dragonet who was introduced in Darkstalker . She was a mind-reader who lived in the lost city of night and attended the NightWing school before fleeing the city.

Appearance

Listener was large, with a long neck and curves that suggested that she was a successful hunter. She had dark scales and silver teardrop scales indicative of mind-reading in the outside corner of each eye.

Personality

Listener was sarcastic, curious, easy-to-laugh, and would rather be surprised by life than know too much. She acted coy and uttered a fake laugh around Thoughtful .

Biography

The Jade Mountain Prophecy

Darkness of Dragons

Moonwatcher mentioned Listener when she realized that Clearsight (actually Fierceteeth , enchanted by Darkstalker ) did not seem to be herself. She then went on about how she read a scroll about her written by Listener, describing her friend as being one of the most brilliant NightWings of all time, and how no one else was able to send their mind down multiple futures like her.

Legends

Darkstalker

Listener first appeared when Clearsight entered the school on her first day. Clearsight immediately started reading into her futures while Listener read her mind. She told Clearsight that she would prefer to live out her life without knowing what was going to happen, as her family was "superstitious about seers" then told Clearsight she planned to free the school's scavengers . She and Clearsight become quick friends, however with very conflicting interests, as she often disagreed with Clearsight and had different ideas. Listener showed her dislike of Whiteout , calling her "Weirdout," and saying that she thought Darkstalker was creepy.

Listener later appeared to execute her plan of freeing the scavengers. After some difficulty catching them, she and Clearsight went to a beach to free them, where an IceWing scout hiding on the beach ate one of the scavengers. Clearsight assured Listener that she would not tell anyone why they were there before going to tell Vigilance about the scout.

Later on, at a party, Listener was thinking of asking out a handsome NightWing named Thoughtful. She was hanging out with Darkstalker, Clearsight, Fathom , and Whiteout when he approached. Clearsight redirected Thoughtful's attention to Whiteout, because of possible futures involving Thoughtful and Whiteout, much to Listener's confusion, anger, and dismay.

Near the end of the book, when Listener was among the crowd watching Arctic 's violent self-disembowelment, Clearsight sought her out and warned her to take everyone and leave the Night Kingdom for their own safety, in case her plan did not work. Listener understood immediately, although called Darkstalker Clearsight's "psycho boyfriend." The two friends exchanged heartfelt goodbyes, then went their separate ways.

Relationships

Clearsight

Despite Clearsight and Listener initially being wary of one another's NightWing abilities of mind-reading and precognition, they quickly became best friends. All of the futures involving Listener were warmer for Clearsight, with much more laughing. Clearsight felt genuinely happy around Listener, and even though she regarded Listener's obsession with scavengers slightly strange, she accepted her regardless. Clearsight was very affectionate towards her.
