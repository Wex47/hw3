Sapphire was an adult female SeaWing who was mentioned in Darkstalker . After losing her claws to a clamshell enchanted by Albatross , she was rumored to have gone insane, and was stationed on a remote island within the Kingdom of the Sea under the care of two very well-paid servants.

Appearance

Sapphire had no claws.

Personality

Sapphire was mocking towards Albatross, reminding him that once she began ruling the Kingdom of the Sea, she would own everything. After the clamshell incident, she devolved into insanity.

Biography

Pre-Series

When Lagoon and Sapphire were dragonets , they teased Albatross while he was playing with a clamshell. Sapphire attempted to take the clamshell away, taunting Albatross and claiming that one day, everything would be hers, even "stupid beach trash" such as the clamshell. Albatross was not aware of his animus powers at the time, and therefore never expected his verbal order to be followed, but he commanded the shell to "Bite her! Bite all her claws off!"

Since that day, Sapphire had been unable to hunt, battle, or swim efficiently. She avoided the SeaWing palaces and was stationed on a remote island with the company of two particularly well-paid servants. It was mentioned that Sapphire developed both insanity and a probable hatred for Albatross after the incident on the beach. Lagoon was the only known dragon to have ever visited her.

Legends

Darkstalker

Albatross recites this tragic story of himself and Sapphire to Fathom of how he discovered his animus magic. Later, after the Royal SeaWing Massacre , she is mentioned to be the only other member of the royal family besides Pearl who could rule the kingdom.
