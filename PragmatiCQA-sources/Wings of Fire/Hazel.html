Hazel is a female LeafWing who was introduced in The Poison Jungle . As Sequoia is currently under the control of the othermind , Hazel is serving as the queen of the LeafWings. She currently resides in Sanctuary .

Appearance

Hazel has green scales, green wings, large brown eyes, and deft dark green claws.

Personality

Hazel enjoys reading and making books, as she was always putting together her own creations or reading others'. She was shown to be critical of the shoddy binding on the Book of Clearsight , but also carefree and humorous at a point. Hazel is kind and smart, as well as an able-minded and considerate leader who genuinely cared for her suffering tribe. She is regal and poised towards her subjects, with sufficient training from Sequoia on becoming a good queen.

Biography

The Lost Continent Prophecy

The Poison Jungle
Hazel was seen sewing sheets of speckled paper together to make a book on Queen Sequoia's throne. She was ordered off the throne by her great-grandmother and was confused if she was allowed to talk to Sundew normally. Hazel, Willow , and Sundew went to see the new strangers, who turned out to be Tsunami , Turtle , and Io . The three spent the rest of the night in a pile of leaves in Hazel's room. Upon waking up, Sundew notices Hazel perched in her hammock, reading a book. She asked if they were late to see Queen Sequoia, to which Hazel responded that she would call them when she was ready, going back to her book. Willow asked if Hazel wanted to come with her and Sundew to take breakfast to the visitors, but Hazel declined, saying that she is at a really good part in her book.

Hazel arrived while Sundew and Willow are visiting Tsunami and Turtle, asking them if they could hear the roaring of Commander Belladonna .

Hazel witnessed Queen Sequoia's telling of the Legend of the Hive . She was horrified and wanted to fight with the other LeafWings. Sequoia put her in charge of the SapWings while she goes to war, not wanting to lose her great-granddaughter.

After Sundew, Willow, Mandrake , Nettle , and Bumblebee returned from the Eye of the jungle with the "heart of salvation," Hazel fed the root to Inchworm . When it seemed to work, she sent Sundew, Willow, and Mandrake back to Queen Sequoia, who was preparing for war.

Hazel uncovered Clearsight 's map to Pyrrhia . She became the new queen of the LeafWings and led them across the chain of islands leading to Pyrrhia.
The Dangerous Gift
After arriving in the Ice Kingdom , Hazel pleaded Queen Snowfall for shelter. When Snowfall's army was revealed, Hazel agreed to move on to the Kingdom of Sand . On the way there, Hazel attempted to engage Snowfall in a discussion about being a young queen, and they traded stories of how the previous queens were lost.

Sundew briefly mentioned Hazel when Cobra Lily suggested the idea of Sundew becoming queen.

When it was time for the Pantalans to leave for Sanctuary after temporarily resting in Jerboa III 's island, Hazel leaned wistfully against the palm trees and gazed up at their swaying leaves. During the journey, Snowfall caught up with Hazel and asked about the LeafWings who were left behind in Pantala. Hazel tried not to cry, explaining how most of them were presumably left to be mind-controlled by the breath of evil. Snowfall then asked Moonwatcher whether the latter had received any visions about Pantala, in which Moon replied by restating the prophecy . Hazel wondered about life in Pyrrhia, in which Lynx intervened to answer those questions.

Hazel took part in organizing the Pantalan refugees when they arrived at Sanctuary. She also attended the Pyrrhian queens' summit to settle Moon's prophecy, and she was mentioned frequently afterwards in the book. By the end of the story, Hazel decided to stay in Sanctuary, and she chose Sundew as the LeafWing representative.

Relationships

Sequoia

She has a very close bond to her great-grandmother, seeing her as a kind of motherly figure. She takes after Sequoia when addressing her subjects and giving them order, showing that Queen Hazel looks up to her great-grandmother, and thinks of her as a good queen.

Snowfall

Hazel and Snowfall are shown to have formed a friendship bond. Hazel and Snowfall somewhat relate to one another, both being quite young and inexperienced queens. Hazel thinks that she could learn a couple things from Snowfall, and that Snowfall could learn some from her.

Sundew

Sundew and Hazel have only met each other recently, but Sundew is thought to think kindly about the queen, and it is thought Hazel does also. Hazel is a bit of a calming influence for her. Sundew believed that Hazel was the rightful queen of the LeafWings and does not try to take that leadership from Hazel.

Willow

Hazel is quite close to Willow, so close that Sundew even worried that Willow might have feelings for the princess, but Willow assured Sundew that they were just friends.
