Urchin is a male SeaWing who was introduced in the The Lost Heir . He resides in the Kingdom of the Sea as a messenger for Queen Coral .

Appearance

Urchin is small and lithe.

Personality

Urchin bowed as low as he could to Queen Coral and covered his head whilst giving a report.

Biography

The Dragonet Prophecy

The Lost Heir

Urchin was the first SeaWing to find Kestrel 's dead body, which was just a few islands away from the Summer Palace . He told Moray about the it, who then informed Queen Coral.

Afterward, Urchin sat in the SeaWing Council as Coral's messenger, despite him not being a Council member.

Urchin later arrived at the scene when Webs returned to the Summer Palace and reported to Coral that Piranha had captured a suspicious dragon, possibly one working with Webs. This dragon turned out to be Riptide .
