Diamond was an adult female IceWing who was introduced in Darkstalker . She was an animus dragon and the queen of the IceWing tribe during the era of Darkstalker . She heavily influenced the causation of the NightWing-IceWing War due to her hatred towards Arctic and Foeslayer 's relationship.

She was one of the two only known IceWings in Pyrrhian history to use their animus power more than once. After capturing Foeslayer, Diamond imprisoned her within the Ice Kingdom by enchanting two frozen shackles and several spears, ultimately creating the Diamond Trial .

Appearance

Diamond wore ice-blue diamonds on her ears, neck, and tail band. She had sharp, glittering eyes and a superior, condescending voice.

Personality

Diamond was ruthless, cunning, vengeful, insane, judgemental, and placed heavy value upon noble IceWing traditions. She was unpredictable, and changed her mind often.

Legends

Darkstalker
Diamond was infuriated and refused to accept that Prince Arctic merely ran off with Foeslayer on his own free will. She had wanted him to marry Snowflake , a noble First Circle IceWing engaged to Arctic. She then lied and told her entire tribe that the NightWing, Foeslayer, coveting the power of the IceWings' animus dragons, had kidnapped Arctic so that her tribe could benefit from these powers as well. Since Arctic left, there were no more animus IceWings, making all IceWings detest NightWings for "stealing" their last animus. Diamond then started the NightWing-IceWing war.

All the IceWings had believed that Foeslayer had gotten away, but Diamond managed to catch her when Foeslayer took off her protective earring. In her fury, Diamond used her powers to get her revenge on Foeslayer. First, Diamond cast a spell that ordered Foeslayer to come to her, which took effect when Foeslayer took off Arctic's earring, and then Diamond locked Foeslayer up in shackles that could only be broken by the frostbreath of a member in the royal IceWing family. These shackles prevented Foeslayer from ever leaving the Diamond Caves, and made it so that if Foeslayer ever died, she would do so in excruciating pain and be frozen again, only to unfreeze when tapped with one of the enchanted diamond spears.

Furious, she unfroze and killed Foeslayer forty times. Then, before she died, Diamond decided other IceWings could kill Foeslayer too, but for a different purpose: The Diamond Trial.

She was implied to have been put to sleep by a potion Snowfox bought from a RainWing .

Winglets

Runaway
She first appeared when Arctic went over to Foeslayer and Prudence to explain the situation to the latter. Diamond was hurrying after Arctic with other IceWing aristocrats.

Later, in a flashback of Snowflake, she was inconsiderate of her feelings of the marriage, saying that Snowflake should understand that she doesn't care about her opinions or if Snowflake doesn't have any. Diamond also assessed Snowflake's strength, poise, and diplomacy throughout many tests that had been deliberately unfair.

She later confronted Arctic before he was about to meet with Foeslayer. She was initially suspicious of Arctic's behavior because of his yawning during breakfast with Snowflake. When Arctic was thinking about one conversation with Foeslayer, Diamond was angered by her son's delayed response. She assigned two guards to guard Arctic's room, preventing him from seeing Foeslayer, to no avail.

Relationships

Arctic

Diamond constantly yelled at and reminded her son about his duties as an IceWing prince. She was judgmental and controlling towards him, but despite her attempts to control his life, he eventually fled the Ice Kingdom in order to live with Foeslayer. Diamond claimed to love Arctic, but was quick to remind him about her disapproval towards his choices.

Foeslayer

Diamond fiercely hated Foeslayer, and by extension the entire NightWing tribe, for "stealing" her son. She went as far as lying to her subjects that the NightWings were plotting to gain animus magic. When she finally captured Foeslayer, she created the gift of vengeance so she could be kept alive just to be able to suffer, and even killed Foeslayer the first forty times herself just to vent her anger. She also enchanted the Royal IceWing Crown to make every wearer hate the NightWings as much as she does, further emphasizing her hate of Foeslayer bringing animus magic to the NightWing tribe.

Snowflake

Despite assigning her to marry Arctic, Diamond cared little for Snowflake. She brushed off whether she had opinions or not, as well as assessed her unfairly. The two were bitter of each other due to the arranged marriage.
