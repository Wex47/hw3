Bryony is an adult female LeafWing who was introduced in The Poison Jungle . She is a part of the LeafWing rebellion, led by Commander Belladonna and Hemlock . The prologue of The Poison Jungle is from her perspective, and she helped orchestrate the Burning of Bloodworm Hive . She currently resides in Pantala , and is in a relationship with Grayling .

Appearance

Bryony has green wings.

Personality

Bryony is quiet and worried. Like most PoisonWings , Bryony is fiercely in opposition to the HiveWings , claiming that they murdered and stole all the LeafWing trees. Bryony has a bitter sense of humor, and originally did not care about the SilkWings at all, thinking them as weak and pathetic. After meeting Grayling and the rest of the Bloodworm Hive Chrysalis , she changed her mind greatly and felt remorseful over some of her violent thoughts.

Biography

The Lost Continent Prophecy

The Poison Jungle

Bryony appeared in the prologue of the book, getting ready to burn down Bloodworm Hive. She was with two other LeafWings, Hemlock and Pokeweed , and the SilkWing Grayling. While getting ready she mocked and laughed about how the HiveWings took all the trees then decided to make little houses to grow plants, with Pokeweed and Hemlock ignoring this.

The Dangerous Gift

One of Snowfall 's visions were in Bryony's point of view. Bryony, along with her companions, were fleeing in fear from the empty Hives. Snowfall is then shown feeling strongly after the vision because of Bryony's love for her companion Grayling.

Relationships

Grayling

Bryony is in love with Grayling, and he thinks she is a hero. She would protect him at all costs, and they are physically affectionate towards one another. Bryony is reassuring towards him and feels affection towards him, believing him to be special and beloved.

Hemlock

Bryony describes Hemlock as "stoic and boring." However, she seems to respect him more than Pokeweed.

Pokeweed

Like Hemlock, Bryony thinks of Pokeweed as "stoic and boring." She seems to be competitive with the male LeafWing, jumping to do things before he can.

Sundew

At one year older than her, Bryony and Sundew grew up with each other. When Sundew told her about the Chrysalis, she thought the younger LeafWing was insane.
