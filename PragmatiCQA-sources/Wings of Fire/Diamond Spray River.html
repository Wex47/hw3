The Diamond Spray River originates in the part of the Sky Kingdom near Queen Ruby's palace , and flows down the east coast of Pyrrhia , forming the Diamond Spray Delta , which is near the Bay of a Thousand Scales . It was used as an escape route for the dragonets of destiny to get away from the Sky Kingdom, and is the second-longest river in Pyrrhia, being shorter than the Great Five-Tail River .

History

The Dragonet Prophecy

The Dragonet Prophecy

The dragonets of destiny use the river to escape from the SkyWing palace, following it until they can get to the Mud Kingdom , where they stopped and broke off from the river.

The Jade Mountain Prophecy

Escaping Peril

Peril and the Jade Winglet flew over the Diamond Spray River in attempt to chase down Chameleon , who had fled from the Sky Kingdom with Darkstalker's scroll . They had a showdown, in which Turtle stole the scroll with animus magic . The Jade Winglet abruptly left, with Chameleon screaming before them.

Legends

Dragonslayer

Wren followed the Diamond Spray River when leaving the Indestructible City .
