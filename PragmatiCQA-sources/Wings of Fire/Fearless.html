Fearless is a female NightWing dragonet who was introduced in Moon Rising . She is currently attending Jade Mountain Academy as a member of the Silver Winglet and owns a bracelet enchanted by Darkstalker to gift the wearer with super-strength.

Talons of Power

Upon seeing Darkstalker, Fearless, Mightyclaws , and Mindreader all attempted to attack him. She was then later introduced to Darkstalker by Moonwatcher , and Fearless asked him questions about the lost city of night . After Darkstalker revealed Moonwatcher's prophetic and mind-reading abilities, she asked a barrage of questions. Darkstalker mentioned he could enchant objects, and Fearless quickly made a bracelet with strands of twisted wire. She requested and was given super-strength, which she tested out by slamming her tail into a stalactite.

She later went with Anemone , Darkstalker, Mindreader, Moonwatcher, and Sunny to save Stonemover when it was revealed that he was in danger. Later in the book, she traveled to the Rainforest Kingdom with most of her tribe. She returned to the Lost City of Night shortly after.

Darkness of Dragons

Fearless was shown briefly during the Battle of Jade Mountain , attempting to attack a skilled young IceWing fighter with blue freckles. Winter came to the IceWing's rescue and used his frostbreath on Fearless' wing, causing her to crash. Wounded, she dragged herself into the shelter of a small cave.

Later, Clay mentioned that Fearless, Pike , and Tamarin were working on a school presentation about Darkstalker's history.
