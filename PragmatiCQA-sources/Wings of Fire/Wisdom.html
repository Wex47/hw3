Wisdom is a NightWing of unknown gender who was mentioned in The Dark Secret .

Biography

The Dragonet Prophecy

The Dark Secret

Wisdom was one of the guards stationed by the tunnel between the NightWing island and the Rainforest Kingdom . Besides the three other NightWing guards who had rushed into the library to report to Mastermind that the latter was needed in the council chamber immediately, Wisdom had been one of the three dragons who tried to kidnap Tsunami , though they were attacked in the process and failed. Tsunami was mentioned to have injured every one of the three NightWing guards and nearly bit Wisdom's ear off.
