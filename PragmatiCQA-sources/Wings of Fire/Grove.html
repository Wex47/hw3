Appearance

Grove has a long, shaggy dark mop for hair, burn scars on his hands and face, and long legs. He also had narrower eyes than the other villagers. He often wears clothing in bright colors such as orange and his eyes are narrower than the other villagers'. He has a charming grin.

Biography

Legends

Dragonslayer
Grove was first mentioned by Rowan, who said that he used to live in the Indestructible City , and had known a few wandering travelers who had met the Dragonslayer . Later on, it was mentioned that Rowan preferred to be off with him instead of with her younger siblings.

The first time Grove appeared formally in the book was when he came to warn Leaf and Rowan about the bell that signaled dragons coming. He, Leaf, Rowan, and Butterfly ran into a shelter. Rowan then told Leaf that Grove knew Leaf wanted to become a dragonslayer. Grove added that he wished he could become one too.

Leaf went on to mention that Grove was the only person near their age who had traveled beyond Talisman . He also stated that Grove had gotten burn scars from a time when dragons had set fire to his previous village, and that he and his father never talked about it. Leaf then asked if Grove had really lived in the Indestructible City. Grove said yes, but that he and his father didn't like it because it had too many rules, and that the Invincible Lord had ideas that he disagreed with, for example that everyone was expendable.

Grove next appeared after Leaf had become an apprentice to the dragonmancers . He asked Leaf if he had found any 'secrets' in Trout 's house. Leaf said no, but Grove asked the apprentice if he could let him (Grove) know the next time the dragonmancer was out.

Six days later, Grove and Leaf broke into Trout's study and found a map of the SkyWing palace made by dragons, along with a lot of treasure which the dragonmancers or their apprentices had stolen. Leaf decided to come up as often as he could to copy out the blueprint.

Months later, Leaf had finally completed copying out the map. Grove thanked Leaf, and said he and the other teenagers would study it. However, two days later, the blueprint was stolen by Mushroom . Crow and Gorge found out about it, and said that they would sacrifice Grove if the others did not bring back treasure from the palace.
