Mushroom was an adult male human who was introduced in Dragonslayer . He was Thyme 's twin brother. His head was bitten off by Queen Scarlet while escaping after stealing SkyWing treasure.

Appearance

Mushroom was described as more square-jawed, taller, and a lot gloomier than Thyme. Mushroom was also described as short and pale, with a furry head. He had two long, thin legs and two dangling arms that ended in "floppy, clawless talons." He reached barely as high as Clay 's shoulder. He had dark hair with a red tunic and blue-gray pants, with buck teeth.

Personality

Mushroom was skeptical, gloomy, jealous, and tended to complain whenever he was tired. Leaf mentioned that it was likely for Mushroom to mess things up. When Thyme tried to save Mushroom from the SkyWings, Mushroom flung his bag of treasure at his brother, leaving him defenseless against the dragons .

Biography

The Dragonet Prophecy

The Dragonet Prophecy

The scavenger Clay saw shortly after escaping the mountains was Mushroom. The scavenger ran off after seeing Clay and Tsunami , but Scarlet caught him and bit his head off. Mushroom was the scavenger stealing Queen Scarlet's treasure.

Legends

Dragonslayer

Mushroom was shown to be training with other humans in hope of becoming dragonslayers. Later, he stole their map and went to the SkyWing palace to steal treasure. However, he was then found by the SkyWings, who chased after him. He escaped, endangering his brother, Thyme, by throwing a piece of the treasure at him.

Relationships

Cranberry

Leaf mentioned that Mushroom was seen glaring at Thyme behind his back more than once, especially whenever Thyme had Cranberry 's full attention.

Thyme

Mushroom was jealous of Thyme, and left him defenseless against the dragons when Thyme tried to save him.
