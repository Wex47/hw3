Siamang is a RainWing dragonet of unknown gender who was introduced in Moon Rising . They are currently attending Jade Mountain Academy as a member of the Quartz Winglet .

Personality

Siamang appears to not be afraid of Peril and likes to do culinary experiments with her, such as making applesauce with her.

Biography

The Jade Mountain Prophecy

Moon Rising

Siamang was shown resting in a patch of sunlight with two other RainWing students at the school, Coconut and Boto .

Darkness of Dragons

In the epilogue, it is revealed that Siamang likes experimenting with Peril, who uses her firescales on fruits. The two seemed to have reinvented a version of applesauce, a "cooked mashed-up apple thing," as Peril refers to it.
