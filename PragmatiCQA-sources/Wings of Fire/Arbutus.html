Appearance

Arbutus wore his dark hair in a waist-length ponytail and had a long and angular body.

Personality

Arbutus was said to be a very morbid, morose fellow.

Biography

The Dragonet Prophecy

The Dragonet Prophecy

Arbutus, along with Rowan , Cardinal , and Cranberry , were the humans who fought Tsunami and Starflight in the SkyWing Arena. He was one of the humans to have been killed in the arena, along with Cardinal.

Legends

Dragonslayer

Arbutus was the human asleep on a cow when Leaf , Rowan, Cranberry, and Thyme were taken to the palace . He was one of the humans chosen for the arena, and he did not survive it.
