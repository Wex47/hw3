Appearance

Thyme is rather small.

Personality

Thyme is described as charming and he jokes a lot. He never took training seriously which is why he did not climb out of the palace with Leaf . He has a love for treasure which he shared with the rest of the group except Leaf. He seemed to care about his brother, Mushroom, and the rest of the group.

Biography

The Dragonet Prophecy

The Dragonet Prophecy

Thyme was one of the scavengers chosen for the SkyWing feast, the other being Leaf. He was the scavenger crawling underneath the tables to hide.

Legends

Dragonslayer

Thyme is part of the group who helped Leaf train to become a dragonslayer. Thyme is envied by his brother, Mushroom, who has stayed in the background. When Mushroom goes off on his own, the group training with Leaf followed. When they arrived, the group saw Mushroom heading out of the palace with a sack of gold. Then, Queen Scarlet swooped down and bit Mushroom's head off. Thyme and the training group were subsequently captured by the SkyWings and imprisoned. Thyme was chosen, along with Leaf, to be food for Queen Scarlet's banquet. They both survived due to the trouble the dragonets of destiny were causing. Thyme later escaped along with Rowan and Cranberry , they returned to the village of Talisman . Thyme helped overthrow the dragonmancers .

Relationships

Cranberry

Thyme and Cranberry both want to fight the dragons. They appear to be friends.

Leaf

Leaf and Thyme are friends. Thyme has called Leaf "crazy brave."

Mushroom

Mushroom is Thyme's brother. Though Mushroom seems to be openly jealous of Thyme, Thyme still seems to care for him as a brother and was shocked when Mushroom threw a piece of treasure at him to distract an attacking dragon to save himself and his stolen treasure.
