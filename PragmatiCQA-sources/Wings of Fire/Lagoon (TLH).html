Lagoon is an adult female SeaWing who was introduced in The Lost Heir . She is a member of the SeaWing Council and is in charge of preparing food for the council meetings. Her official Council title is that of aquaculture.

Biography

The Dragonet Prophecy

The Lost Heir

After the dragonets were imprisoned, Queen Coral told Lagoon to make sure they were well fed. However, Lagoon neglected them, and only fed them tiny crabs and other small breakfast leftovers from the feast that was served during the council meetings. When Tsunami visited the dragonets, she found out that they were not given enough food, and that Clay had been chained up, forced to wait out the storm, which Clay would have most likely died. She told her mother, Queen Coral, and the SeaWing queen sent Lagoon to prison. Tsunami suspected that her uncle, Shark , or even Queen Coral, of being the one who gave the order for Clay to be shackled, though it is still unknown who did this. Queen Coral pretended to be shocked and angry because Lagoon and Shark disobeyed her commands and had the two of them imprisoned, but Tsunami knew that the two would not be punished harshly and that they would be soon released due to their status and role in the SeaWing government. Lagoon, when being confronted, began to protest, saying something along the lines of "I did it on your orders," and Queen Coral flashed her wing stripes, saying " silence " in Aquatic to Lagoon before she was led away as Tsunami realized her mistake.
