Animus enchantments are any spell cast by a dragon with animus magic . They are generally cast on objects, though dragons, animals, and plants may be enchanted as well.

Vulture Darkstalker Enchanted first to obey Darkstalker 's every command, then to have the mind of a one-year-old dragonet, then to undo the last spell and to make him feel guilty for all the things he has done, then to be a grandfather who loves Qibli more than anything else in the world. Winter Darkstalker Presumably enchanted to believe Darkstalker is a good, noble leader and vehemently defend his goodness.

IceWing gifts

Target Enchanted by Enchantment
Gift of defense Unknown IceWing A huge cliff between the Ice Kingdom and the rest of the mainland , enchanted to shoot icicles at anyone who is not an IceWing , with the exception of IceWing hybrids .
Gift of diplomacy Penguin Three bracelets, enchanted to let the wearer pass the Great Ice Cliff despite their tribe and keep them warm in the Ice Kingdom , as well as a heated dome near the SandWing border so they do not need to wear the bracelet.
Gift of elegance Unknown IceWing The inner walls of the IceWing palace , enchanted to mimic the weather outside.
Gift of healing Diamond A set of five narwhal horns, enchanted to cure any IceWings of frostbreath wounds.
Gift of light Frostbite The Moon Globe Tree, a frosty white tree claw-carved from ice by Frostbite . Enchanted to grow moon globes, small silver spheres of light that can be picked up and float where they are released.
Gift of order Unknown IceWing A wall in the courtyard of the IceWing palace , enchanted to show every IceWing 's name and their current ranking.
Gift of splendor Unknown IceWing twins The IceWing palace , enchanted to grow from the ground and never be cracked, melted, or harmed in any way from the outside.
Gift of stealth Unknown IceWing A pair of diamond-encrusted wristbands. When worn and smashed together three times, the wearer can cause themselves or any other dragon to become totally invisible.
Gift of strength Unknown IceWing A delicate, elegant tiara that grants the wearer super-strength.
Gift of subsistence Unknown IceWing Three lakes on the outskirts of the Ice Kingdom , enchanted so that if a dragon sticks their claws into the lake, they will always catch a seal.
Gift of vengeance Diamond The Diamond Caves , enchanted so that anyone who touches Foeslayer , a NightWing locked inside with animus-touched shackles, with a diamond-tipped spear can unfreeze her. When stabbed again, Foeslayer dies a painful death before being re-frozen. Later turned into a ceremony called the Diamond Trial .
Gift of vision Opal A ring set with an opal and imbued with Opal 's spirit, causing the wearer to experience the perspective of other dragons. Intended for young queens who rose to the throne too soon, to help them grow and become better queens. It cannot be removed from the wearer's claw until its work is done.

Dreamvisitors Darkstalker Three star-shaped sapphires that glow with an eerie blue light, enchanted to grant the user the ability to enter the dreams of another dragon. If the target is in shallow sleep, the user will only see the dream. If the dragon is in a deep, calm sleep, the user can talk to the dreamer. Sometimes dragons experiencing nightmares cannot be reached. One can only dreamvisit someone they have seen before. Originally created to connect Darkstalker , Fathom , and Clearsight .

Earring Darkstalker Enchanted to give Mightyclaws the ability to make his drawings real.

Enchanted to decide whether or not the holder would be an acceptable queen of the SandWings , without taking their bloodline into account. However, it destroys any dragon unworthy to be the SandWing queen by summoning a form of magical lightning that turns the dragon into a pile of dust.
Golden glass shell necklace Arctic Enchanted to make Whiteout want to go with Arctic to the Ice Kingdom to raise dragonets there, to make her forget about her love for Darkstalker and Thoughtful , and to change her speech and thought patterns.
IceWing crown Diamond Enchanted to give its wearer nightmares and induce extreme paranoia towards all NightWings, ensuring that all future IceWing queens would continue to resent and wage war against the NightWings far into the future.
Indigo 's pearl necklace Fathom Enchanted during the Royal SeaWing Massacre to save Indigo 's life.
Moonstone earrings Darkstalker Enchanted to prevent Clearsight from seeing any negative visions of the future.
Obsidian Mirror Unknown An oblong piece of glittering black obsidian, enchanted to show the current actions and words of a named dragon to the user. The mirror is activated by naming a dragon and breathing smoke onto the surface, and the dragon it shows is represented by a curl of colored smoke, matching their scales.
Pearl necklace Anemone Accidentally enchanted to explode.
Peril 's necklace Chameleon Enchanted with Darkstalker 's scroll to remove Peril 's firescales and all memories of Clay as well as to make her more loyal to Scarlet .
Qibli 's earring Turtle Enchanted to make whichever dragon wears it immune to all of Darkstalker 's spells, past, present, and future.
Scroll Darkstalker Enchanted to hold Darkstalker 's animus magic, granting the written wish of anyone who writes on it. Also enchanted to give him a "mental twinge" whenever someone else uses it.
Silver bracelets Darkstalker Enchanted to give the wearer superstrength and invincibility.
Silver snake earring Darkstalker Enchanted to make everyone who meets Darkstalker think him to be exceptionally handsome and charming.
Soul reader Darkstalker A telescope with a small golden hourglass mounted on the side, enchanted to represent how much of a dragon's soul is good and how much is evil using sand. The white sand represents evil, and good is represented by black sand.
Tailband Darkstalker Enchanted to kill whoever Darkstalker touches with it.
Turtle 's skyfire armband Turtle Enchanted to protect Turtle 's soul from the use of his animus magic. Both the armband and Anemone 's bracelet stipulate that they can only cast magic that does not affect another dragon's free will or any magic that is selfishly motivated.
Weather bracelets Anemone Enchanted to let the wearer control the weather around them, such as by creating sandstorms, thunderstorms, and standalone flashes of lightning.
