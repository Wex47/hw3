Moray is an adult female SeaWing who was introduced in The Lost Heir . She serves as Coral 's scroll publisher, communications herald, and the Councillor of communications as a member of the SeaWing Council . She currently resides in the Kingdom of the Sea .

Appearance

Moray is large, with dull, gray-green scales and small, colorless eyes that closely resemble those of Shark .

Personality

Moray is smug, and was described by Anemone to be a dragon who is perfect, boring, and can do no wrong, as well as drippy, fatuous, and uninteresting. Anemone also believes her to be strange, like no one could act as worshipful towards Coral as she does all the time and really mean it. Despite her affection towards Coral, she was not gentle towards the injured Webs.

Biography

The Dragonet Prophecy

The Lost Heir

Moray first appeared to notify Queen Coral of a dead body that had been found near the Summer Palace . The queen showed little interest, yawning, until Moray mentioned it was a SkyWing . Coral ordered Moray to fetch Piranha and Shark, and Moray accompanied them to investigate the body.

The next morning, Moray was present during a council meeting. While Whirlpool described how well Coral's newest scrolls were selling, Moray interrupted by mentioning that the underwater editions, which she spent much of her time promoting, were especially popular. She also mentioned that SeaWing schools had adjusted their curriculums to ensure that dragonets had ample time to read more of Coral's scrolls .

The Jade Mountain Prophecy

Talons of Power

Moray was first mentioned as one of the figures of authority who were able to impose a punishment to the SeaWing princes when they did something wrong. Later, in Turtle 's flashback to the Talons of Power ceremony , Moray was shown as one of several SeaWings overseeing the test. She was also the first of the viewers to notice Anemone's newfound animus powers .

Relationships

Coral

Moray worried about Coral all night after the queen was faced with Kestrel's death, and later supported her physically after the death of Coral's dragonets. She spends all of her energy promoting Coral's underwater scrolls. She adores and gazes worshipfully towards Coral and seems to resent Anemone, Orca , and Tsunami , as they are potential challengers to Coral's throne. She fawns over Coral constantly, and Tsunami once wondered if Moray would even be willing to kill Coral's daughters to keep the queen safe. Moray once stated that she wants Coral to remain queen forever, and would rather die than challenge her.
