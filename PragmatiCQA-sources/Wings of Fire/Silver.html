Silver is a female sloth that first appeared in The Hidden Kingdom . She was asleep on top of Glory when she woke up after her first-ever suntime in the Rainforest Kingdom . She is currently Glory's pet.

Appearance

Silver has long, silvery-gray fur and enormous dark eyes. She is sleek and beautiful. Snowfall described her as a "floppy yawn-noodle" curled around Glory's neck.

Personality

Silver expresses her feelings for sun-time even though she can't speak. She loves Glory and is very attached to her, as shown in multiple scenes of the series. She also seems to enjoy spending time with Sunny , likely because of her warm scales and personality. She knows Glory is pretty disagreeable but chose her anyway, showing that she is considering, despite only being a pet sloth.

Biography

The Dragonet Prophecy

The Hidden Kingdom

Glory found Silver when she woke up from her first sun-time with a sloth on her neck. Glory initially was confused and tried to shoo the sloth away, but was stopped by Jambu , who explained that the sloths were the pets of quite a few other RainWings . He claimed that Glory had been "chosen" quite fast. Upon visiting the other dragonets , Tsunami expressed interest in eating the sloth (earning some disapproving looks from RainWings nearby). Sunny reacted to seeing Silver by squealing and asking to hold the sloth, showing that she thought it was cute. Silver's presence caused a fight between Glory and Tsunami when Glory got defensive about Tsunami wanting to eat Silver. Starflight was astounded that the sloth wasn't afraid of Glory and was keen to take some notes.

When they found a half-dead, decaying sloth by the tunnel , Glory blocked the gruesome sight from Silver with her wing.

Later, when Glory was exploring the tunnel leading to the Kingdom of Sand , she mentioned to the other dragonets that she had named the sloth Silver and gave her to Sunny to keep safe.

At the RainWing Royal Challenge , Magnificent startled Silver by running right at her and making a loud noise. Silver, afraid, ran toward Glory and revealed her hiding spot. This caused Glory to lose, which caused some disapproval towards Queen Magnificent.
