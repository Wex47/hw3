Dragon , referred to as Cat by Wren , is a female cat owned by Undauntable who appeared in Dragonslayer . She lives in the Indestructible City . Wren does not believe that the cat should be called Dragon, as she acts nothing like one.

Appearance

Dragon is enormous, being too big for Undauntable. She has long, silky orange fur and a flat face.

Personality

Dragon is somewhat lazy and does not scratch. She also acts gorgeously grumpy.
