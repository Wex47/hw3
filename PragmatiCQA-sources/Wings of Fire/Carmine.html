Biography

Legends

Darkstalker

Princess Sunset , one of Carmine's relatives, and a noble named Eagle , were sent to negotiate with Queen Lagoon about shoreline villages on Carmine's behalf. During this diplomatic mission, Sunset was killed by Albatross in the Royal SeaWing Massacre . After the massacre, Queen Pearl promised to gift large sums of gems over the span of five years, and that the SeaWings would never cross onto SkyWing territory again. In turn, Carmine was to keep quiet about the massacre.
