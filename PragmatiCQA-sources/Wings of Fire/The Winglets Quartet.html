Summary

Discover the first four dragon-bite-sized Winglets stories from the #1 New York Times bestselling Wings of Fire series, now newly available together!

Everyone knows the stories… but does anyone know the truth?

Fiercetooth [sic], a NightWing obsessed with what could have — and should have — been. Deathbringer , desperate to prove himself as the next great NightWing assassin. Six-Claws , a loyal SandWing , who will soon find that loyalty comes with a price. Foeslayer the NightWing, a dragon in love turned kidnapper, and Prince Arctic of the IceWings , a runaway turned captive.

In these four short stories, dig deeper into the world of Pyrrhia to discover what really happened.
