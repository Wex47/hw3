Tempest was an adult female SeaWing who was introduced in Assassin . She was a commander of the SeaWing army under Coral , and she was killed by Deathbringer during a diplomatic meeting with several MudWing leaders. Her death ultimately caused the MudWings to leave Blister 's alliance and join Burn 's army.

Appearance

Tempest was towering, enormous, and powerfully built, with turquoise scales. She was described to be as large as Morrowseer , with broad shoulders, gleaming teeth, and a healing burn scar on her neck. She had a long trident strapped to her back and a booming, big, jovial voice. She had a startling, booming bark of a laugh.

Personality

Tempest was loud, intelligent, and fearless. She was quite influential among the SeaWings, and seemed to be well-liked among her tribe.

Biography

The Dragonet Prophecy

The Dark Secret

Deathbringer mentioned her indirectly to Starflight when he asks if Starflight noticed that if a side was winning the war, her top general would mysteriously disappear, indirectly mentioning the death of Commander Tempest.

Winglets

Assassin

Tempest arrived at Blister's secret camp along with King Gill and Piranha , the council member in charge of warfare. They met with several MudWing officials, including General Swamp , to formulate battle strategies and plans. After a full day of planning, they were leaving when a MudWing spear thrown by Deathbringer pierced Tempest's heart, killing her. Her last words were "Well, son of a starfish," as she fell to the ground, dead.
