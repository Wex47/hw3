Moon Rising

Snail followed Anemone and the other SeaWing students when Anemone attempted to use her animus magic to find out who set off the dragonflame cactus bomb in the history cave .

When Darkstalker was observing Anemone, all of the SeaWing students were in the underground lake hanging out together and playing games, which most likely included Snail.

Before Peril almost bumped into Turtle , it was noted that the SeaWings were at an underground lake, which may have included Snail.
