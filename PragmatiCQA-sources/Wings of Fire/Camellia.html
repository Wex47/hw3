Appearance

Camellia was described as bigger than Wren.

Personality

Wren stated that Camellia is sweet towards others. While Wren was waiting to be eaten, she was thinking about how the dragons would've liked Camellia better than her, as " she'd be so oily and sugary the dragons could choke on her ."

Camellia also seemingly does not feel remorse, as Wren thinks that Camellia would play with Wren's dolls and stay inside while a dragon eats her sister.

Biography

Legends

Dragonslayer

Camellia is mentioned in the first and second chapters by Leaf and Wren. Both of the siblings do not seem to prefer her over the others. She was shown to have lots of toys, especially dolls. She also likely took Wren's toys after the siblings were told that a dragon had eaten Wren.

Wren thinks about Camellia after escaping being sacrificed to the dragons and angrily demanded why they did not choose her to be sacrificed instead. Wren then realized that Camellia always listened to the dragonmancers with wide eyes and pretended to be interested in what they say, whereas Wren was "never polite and rarely good."
