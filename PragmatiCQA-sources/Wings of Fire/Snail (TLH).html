Snail is a female SeaWing guard who was introduced in The Lost Heir . She currently works as a guard under Commander Shark in the Kingdom of the Sea .

Personality

Snail strongly believed in the Dragonet Prophecy , and trusted that the dragonets of destiny would end the War of SandWing Succession and bring peace to Pyrrhia . It can be inferred that she is reasonable and fair, as she gave up the keys that would unlock Clay 's chains after Tsunami explained why they needed him alive, despite direct orders from Shark. She is selfless, and seems to care deeply about other dragons.

Biography

The Dragonet Prophecy

The Lost Heir

Along with Herring , Flounder , and Kelp , Snail was one of the SeaWings that guarded the keys to unlock the chains to Clay's prison in the Summer Palace . She was skeptical to hand over the key to Tsunami, fearing punishment from either Coral or Shark. However, Tsunami was eventually able to convince Snail and the other guards that letting a dragonet of destiny die was a worse crime, as it would doom them to perpetual war. Snail gave her the keys, and in return it made Tsunami promise to have their families taken care of if they were punished.
