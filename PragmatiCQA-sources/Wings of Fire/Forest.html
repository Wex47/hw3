Forest is a young male human who appeared in Dragonslayer . He is a member of the Wingwatchers and Commander Brook 's son. He is a resident of Valor , and has a crush on Daffodil .

Personality

Forest is mentioned to enjoy goofing around, as he is noted by Ivy to be the class clown. Ivy thought "he couldn't make it through an entire silent patrol without pretending to fart, laughing his head off, falling out of a tree, or accidentally setting something on fire."

Biography

Legends

Dragonslayer
Forest was mentioned very briefly when Ivy thought back to why adults were allowed to lie but not children, using the example of Heath not getting in trouble for his lies, while Forest and Daffodil got in huge trouble for lying all the time.

Forest was also mentioned when Ivy, Daffodil, and Violet think of getting banished for going to the old village to find Heath's treasure. Violet mentioned bringing Foxglove and Squirrel on their journey so that if they got banished, they would all get banished together and therefore be able to stick together. Daffodil mentioned that she would like Forest to get banished as well since she thinks Forest is funny. Violet disagreed, calling Forest horrifying.

He appeared at the Wingwatcher initiation, surprisingly having made it to the team despite his lack of focus. He appeared offended when Commander Brook claimed that Ivy, Daffodil, and Violet were her favorites, and still looked skeptical when she claimed that they were favorite recruits, not her favorite son.

Later on, when Ivy and Daffodil were hiding in the old village, they met up again with Forest, who informed them that the Dragonslayer's reason for arresting all the Wingwatchers was claiming that they were working with the Invincible Lord and Leaf to assassinate him, despite the Lord only wanting Heath to come work for him in the Indestructible City .

He made one last appearance when Ivy almost crashed into him when she brought Sky into Valor. After he asked Ivy what she was doing, she told him to gather everyone in the main hall for her, Sky, Leaf, and Wren 's plan to dethrone the Dragonslayer.
