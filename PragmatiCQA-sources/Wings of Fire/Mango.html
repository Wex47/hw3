Appearance

Mango has orange-gold scales.

Personality

Mango seems to be overenthusiastic as a guard, as she yelled at Fatespeaker when she "captured" her.

Biography

The Dragonet Prophecy

The Dark Secret

When Starflight , Fatespeaker, and Flame came through the tunnel from the NightWing island to the rainforest , she appeared in front of Starflight and commanded him to surrender, put his talons on his head, and run for his life, among other things. Tsunami then scolded her, telling her she could not just yell random things at other dragons.
