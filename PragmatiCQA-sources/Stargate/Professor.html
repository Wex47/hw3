A Professor is an educator at the university level. There duties include conducting lectures and seminars in their field of study, performing advanced research in their fields and teaching campus-based or online courses with the help of instructional technology. Several members of the Stargate Program were professors before joining the program, and such as Samantha Carter served as visiting professors at the Air Force Academy .
