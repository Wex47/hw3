SG-25 was an SG team operating from Stargate Command . Composed entirely of members from the U.S. Army , ( SG1 : " Uninvited ") it was created sometime before 2005 . ( SG1 : " Moebius, Part 1 ") This team was attacked by a Mutated Creature . ( SG1 : " Uninvited ")
