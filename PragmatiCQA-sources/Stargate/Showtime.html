Showtime is a subscription television brand used by a number of channels and platforms around the world, but primarily refers to a group of channels in the United States of America .

Stargate series

Showtime is the television network that originally aired seasons 1 through 5 of Stargate SG-1 until it was canceled in 2002 . The series was then, however, picked up by The Sci Fi Channel , which aired the show until 2006 when it was not renewed for an eleventh season.
