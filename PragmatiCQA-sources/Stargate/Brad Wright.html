Brad Wright is the creator of Stargate: Atlantis together with Robert C. Cooper and writer of multiple Stargate SG-1 , Stargate: Atlantis and Stargate Universe episodes. He won the 2009 Gemini Award for Best Writing in a Dramatic Series for the Atlantis Season 5 episode, " The Shrine ", and was also nominated for a 2008 Nebula Award for Best Script for the same episode. Past Nebula winners include Pan's Labyrinth by Guillermo Del Toro and Serenity by Joss Whedon.
