< Universe

A galaxy is a collection of stars and planets following a circular orbit around a central point. Galaxies can come in groups, including the Local Group . According to Lt. Colonel Samantha Carter , there are hundreds of millions of galaxies in the known Universe . ( SG1 : " Reckoning, Part 2 ") However this is a gross underestimation as there are actually approximately between 170 and 200 billion galaxies in the known universe, with newer estimates being 2 trillion.

Known galaxies

  • Alteran Home Galaxy
  • Ida Galaxy
  • Kaliam Galaxy
  • Milky Way Galaxy
  • Othalla Galaxy
  • Pegasus Galaxy
  • An Unnamed Galaxy four million light years away, where SG-1 appeared after the Battle of the Vorash system . This galaxy was known to the Replicators , so may also have been known to the Asgard as well. It may even have been Ida.
  • The Ancients launched a number of unmanned ships on an intergalactic mission to seed Stargates throughout the universe. Although it is unclear exactly how many galaxies have been seeded by these ships, a star map on Destiny reveals that a minimum of 38 galaxies were seeded after Pegasus. It is likely that many additional galaxies have been seeded because many Stargate seeding ships were dispatched in the first place. No one knows how many ships were dispatched to seed Stargates throughout the universe, and it is unclear how many galaxies have been visited by Destiny. ( SGU : " Air, Part 1 ")
