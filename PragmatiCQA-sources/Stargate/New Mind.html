New Mind are a faction of Goa'uld that developed in the years prior to the fall of the Goa'uld Empire . ( Stargate Worlds )

Overview

They possess their own Goa'uld Queen and homeworld. This group believes in more direct action against their enemies when compared to the other symbiotes. They are also a member of the Praxis .
