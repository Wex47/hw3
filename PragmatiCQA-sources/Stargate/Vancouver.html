History

Dr. Rodney McKay 's sister, Jeannie Miller and her family live in a house in Vancouver. ( SGA : " Miller's Crossing ")

Vancouver is also the home of the Vancouver Canucks hockey team. In 2002 , when asked by Colonel Jack O'Neill who he believed would win the Stanley Cup , Teal'c remarked that he favored the "Canucks of Vancouver", stating that they were "superior warriors". ( SG1 : " The Other Guys ")

Behind the scenes

Stargate SG-1 and Stargate: Atlantis is filmed in and around the Vancouver area, including Bridge Studios , where many sets including Stargate Command and Atlantis are filmed. Also many of the cast and crew, including Michael Shanks , Paul McGillion and Amanda Tapping , reside in Vancouver.
