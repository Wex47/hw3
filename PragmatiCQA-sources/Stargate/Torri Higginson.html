Torri Higginson is a Canadian actress. She was born in Burlington, Ontario, Canada, on December 6, 1969 and is most well known for her roles in Stargate: Atlantis and the TekWar movies and series, and The English Patient . She is also a theater actress and has appeared in Three Tall Women , Weldon Rising and Picasso at the Lapin Agile .
