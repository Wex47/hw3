Blood is a liquid within the body of living creatures that provides oxygen and other nutrients throughout the body.

The Goa'uld release naquadah into the bloodstream of their hosts . Symbiote blood is fatal to hosts, and mixing their blood with their host will lead to the death of the host. The Goa'uld also release a toxin into the bloodstream of the host when they die. ( SG1 : " Children of the Gods ", " The Enemy Within ", " Singularity ", " Crossroads ", " Summit ", " Last Stand ")

The Untouched believe that blood is part of their lifeforce, and were offended when Teal'c asked for a sample of their blood. ( SG1 : " The Broca Divide ")

Blood colors

  • Goa'uld : Blue
  • Human : Red
  • Stragoth : Purple
  • Unas : Green
  • Wraith : Red on-screen in "Runner" and "The Last Man" and on prop pieces sold at auction; Black and Green in the tie-ins
  • Ancient : Red
  • Alien creature : Blue
