Honduras is a small Spanish -speaking country in Central America , Earth .

History

There is a temple in the rainforest that is the location of the Telchak device. In 2003 , Dr. Daniel Jackson and Dr. Bill Lee went there to find the device that could defeat Anubis' Kull warriors . However, they were taken hostage by Honduran guerrillas. ( SG1 : " Evolution, Part 1 ", " Evolution, Part 2 ")
