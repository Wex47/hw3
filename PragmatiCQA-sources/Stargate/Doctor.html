Doctor is the highest title awarded to university graduates demonstrating intensive knowledge in a particular field of a subject. It is also used to refer to medical practitioners allowed to administer medical care without supervision. Often, the title Doctor is earned through a demonstration of expertise such as a new and unique contribution through scientific research or a more practical demonstration of knowledge through a comprehensive exam or evaluation.
