The Prim'ta was the ceremony of implanting a larval Goa'uld in a young Jaffa . Prim'ta was also the word that referred to the larval Goa'uld itself. The ceremony marked a Jaffa's coming of age, and the beginning of their warrior training. While a prim'ta biologically requires a human vessel, they are incapable of effectively controlling the host . It was also the way the Goa'uld insured that the Jaffa were forever tied to them, as the prim'ta effectively functioned as the Jaffa's immune system . The first prim'ta ceremony took place on the planet Dakara . ( SG1 : " Children of the Gods ", " The Enemy Within ", " Bloodlines ", " Bane ", " Reckoning, Part 1 ")

Possible Etymology (non-canon)

It is possible that prim'ta is a compound word made up of Latin word fragment prim (like in primitive or primal) and the Chinese word ta (它), a word which today means "it" but in ancient times meant "snake".
