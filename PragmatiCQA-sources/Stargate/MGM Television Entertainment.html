Metro-Goldwyn-Mayer Television (commonly known as MGM Television and later MGM/UA Television among other names) is an American television production/distribution company launched in 1955 and a subsidiary of Metro-Goldwyn-Mayer Studios, Inc.

MGM Television distributes the four Stargate television series: Stargate SG-1 , Stargate: Atlantis , Stargate Universe and the non-canon animated series Stargate Infinity (co-produced with DiC Entertainment ). It also distributes the original Stargate movie for syndicated and pay television.
