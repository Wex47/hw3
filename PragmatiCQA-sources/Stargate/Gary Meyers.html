Dr. Gary Meyers, Ph.D was a scientist working for Catherine Langford .

Biography

1996

He attempted to translate the coverstones , but his translation had many errors that were corrected by Dr. Daniel Jackson , a young archaeologist who Meyers was apparently very contemptuous of. However, he was finally impressed when Daniel identified the " point of origin " symbol on the Stargate and remarked that it had been "in front of us the whole time." ( Stargate )

Behind the scenes

The actor who portrayed Gary, Richard Kind , would later play the character Lucius Lavin on Stargate Atlantis .
