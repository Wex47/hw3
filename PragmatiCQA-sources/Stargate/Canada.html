Canada is a country on Earth , in North America , north of the United States . It is part of the Gate Alliance Treaty and sent personnel to Atlantis . ( SGA : " Rising ")

History

In 1969 , Michael Clark and his girlfriend Jenny planned to move to Canada via upstate New York , because of trying to avoid the Vietnam War . After hearing this, Teal'c mistakenly believed that the war was with Canada. ( SG1 : " 1969 ")

Dr. Meredith Rodney McKay and his family (namely, Jeannie Miller ) are Canadian. On a few occasions, McKay's teammates sometimes tried to insult his Canadian heritage, much to the annoyance of McKay, particularly involving sports such as Hockey and Canadian Football . ( SGA : " Hide and Seek ", " Echoes ")
