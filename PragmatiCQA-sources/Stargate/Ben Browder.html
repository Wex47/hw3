Trivia

  • In preparation for his role on Stargate SG-1 , Browder watched every single episode of Stargate SG-1 , a process that took him two and a half weeks to achieve and which is occasionally a running gag in the Stargate SG-1 show. In addition to that, Browder also performed the majority of his own stunts.
  • Browder was originally in the running for the role of Major John Sheppard on Stargate: Atlantis , but had to pull out due to filming commitments for Farscape: The Peacekeeper Wars . Joe Flanigan got the part and Browder was kept in mind when the role of Mitchell was formed.
