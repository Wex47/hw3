Milton French Stewart (born February 20, 1964), known professionally as French Stewart, is an American actor who originally played Lt. Feretti in the feature film . Two years after the release of Stargate , he became famous for his role as nitwitted "Harry Solomon" on Third Rock From The Sun (also featuring David DeLuise ). Since then, he has been typecast in comic character parts. Stewart joined Alexis Cruz ( Skaara / Klorel ), Erick Avari ( Kasuf ), and Richard Kind ( Gary Meyers and Lucius Lavin ) as actors who have appeared in both the original movie and in the television franchise. Like Kind, Stewart did not reprise his character from the film, instead playing Dr. Andrew Covel in SGU. French currently lives in West Hollywood, California, US.
