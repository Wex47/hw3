Martin Wood is a director and producer for Stargate SG-1 and Stargate: Atlantis . He also writes, directs and executive produces for Sanctuary , another Syfy series starring Amanda Tapping . Much like Peter DeLuise , Martin attempts to put himself in the episodes he directs in minor background roles, usually as Major Wood . In an ongoing inside joke, he can often be seen with MSgt. Sylvester Siler and a massive wrench.
