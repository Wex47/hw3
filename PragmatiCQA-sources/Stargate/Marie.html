Biography

2007

After Teyla Emmagan is seriously injured in an explosion, Marie aids Dr. Carson Beckett in the surgery and expresses surprise at Beckett being able to stop a major bleeding. After Beckett is warned that Dr. James Watson is infected with an explosive tumor , he orders his medical staff out while he closes down the OR level. However, Marie refuses to go, telling Beckett he can't do it by himself. While Beckett is initially frustrated by this, he thanks Marie for staying. Together, the two remove the tumor from Watson, saving his life. Beckett then leaves Marie to close Watson up while he takes the tumor to an ordinance disposal technician. However, moments later, the tumor explodes, killing Beckett and the tech. ( SGA : " Sunday ")

After Atlantis was lost in space, she helped Dr. Jennifer Keller operate on Dr. Elizabeth Weir , who had suffered grave injuries after the brush with the beam from the Asuran Stargate satellite . After Weir's Nanites were reactivated, Marie looked through her progress, and was stunned when Weir unpredictably awoke from her coma. ( SGA : " Adrift ")

While infected with the entity from the Crystal of M3X-387 , Keller has a nightmare in which she is called to the infirmary where an Iratus bug hatches from Teyla's stomach. Marie is one of the nurses in the nightmare treating Teyla. ( SGA : " Doppelganger ")

While treating those infected with Kirsan fever , Marie collapsed even while wearing a hazmat suit, showing that the disease had breached quarantine. Despite being infected, Marie continues to work to treat the patients and warns Keller when Doctor Gerald Baxter starts crashing and dies despite their best efforts. Marie is eventually locked in the mess hall with everyone else and loses her memory, showing fear at McKay's plan to break out due to the soldiers. Despite this, she takes part in the breakout effort and gets stunned along with all but Carter and McKay. She was later cured along with most everyone else when the sap from the enchuri plant was distributed throughout Atlantis. ( SGA : " Tabula Rasa ")

2008

She helped Dr. Jennifer Keller and Beckett's clone save Nabel Golan , a known mercenary for Michael Kenmore , so that he could give the expedition information regarding Teyla Emmagan , who was captured by him. She also witnessed Beckett faint from his Cellular degradation . ( SGA : " The Kindred, Part 2 ")

Around two months later, she was called to look over Keller, after she was exposed to a Wraith pathogen , before she was ordered to leave the area she was in. ( SGA : " The Seed ")

In the aftermath of Atlantis' Stargate detonating from the activation of the Attero device , Marie and a medical team searched for survivors, and found Lt. Colonel John Sheppard and Dr. Radek Zelenka . She was last seen removing fragments of glass from Sheppard's back. ( SGA : " The Lost Tribe ")

When Dr. Radek Zelenka was stabbed by Neeva Casol , she assisted Dr. McBride in repairing the wound. ( SGA : " Identity ")

2010

After Zelenka got information from an informant regarding a compound added to the water of the missing Pride of the Genii , Beckett and Marie went over it and discovered that it was intended to disable the artificially-induced ATA genes . The two set to work on creating an antidote. ( SGA : " Pride of the Genii ")
