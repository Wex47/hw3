History

It contains scores of government buildings, including the White House and The Pentagon . Colonel Jack O'Neill claims that he always gets lost whenever he goes there. ( SG1 : " Secrets ", " Inauguration ", " Lost City, Part 1 ", " Lost City, Part 2 ")

Alternate realities

  • In an alternate reality that Dr. Daniel Jackson visited via the Quantum Mirror on P3R-233 in 1998 , Washington D.C., along with many other of Earth 's major cities, had been wiped out by Apophis ' forces. ( SG1 : " There But for the Grace of God ")

Alternate timelines

  • In an alternate timeline , the Stargate was located at the J.R. Reed Space Terminal in Washington D.C. in 2010 . ( SG1 : " 2010 ")
  • In another alternate timeline, during the Goa'uld siege of Earth in 2009 , Washington was one of the first cities that was bombarded from space by Goa'uld Ha'tak vessels. It is unknown if President Henry Hayes was killed during the attack, since he was in a bunker at the time. ( Stargate: Continuum )
