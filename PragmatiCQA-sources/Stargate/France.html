History

The French government was informed of the Stargate 's existence in 2003 , along with the British and Chinese, and has had a limited participation in the program ever since. France is part of the Gate Alliance Treaty and sent personnel to Atlantis . ( SGA : " Rising ")

One of the International Oversight Advisory members; Jean LaPierre is from France. ( SG1 : " The Scourge ")
