Tactical vests are special vests that various SG teams at Stargate Command and Atlantis use as a means of protection, presumably against all kinds of enemy fire and storage of ammunition and gear. The vest are stored in the Stargate Command armory and Atlantis armory fully equipped for fast gear ups.

In 2003 , Dr. Bill Lee developed vest inserts to protect against staff weapon blasts. ( SG1 : " Heroes, Part 1 ")
