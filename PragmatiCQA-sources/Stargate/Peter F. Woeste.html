Peter Woeste is a German-born Canadian TV director , cinematographer and camera operator . He was nominated for an ASC Award in 1997 for In Cold Blood in the category "Outstanding Achievement in Cinematography in Mini-Series", and won a 1997 Blizzard Award in the category "Best Cinematography - Dramatic" for Paris or Somewhere . He was nominated for a Gemini Award in the category "Best Photography in a Dramatic Program or Series" three times: in 1994 for Neon Rider , in 1997 for The Legend of the Ruby Silver , and in 2004 for the Stargate SG-1 episode " Nightwalkers ". He was nominated for a Leo award in 2008 in the category "Best Cinematography in a feature length production" for Stargate: Continuum . He is a regular camera operator, director of photography and director for the Stargate television franchise. He played Foma Zhilin in " The Tomb " & Wedding Guest in ep. " 200 ".
