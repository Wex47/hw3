Wormhole X-Treme! Writer
Robert C. Cooper is one of the directors and head writers of Stargate SG-1 , Stargate: Atlantis and Stargate Universe (the last two in which he is also the co-creater along with Brad Wright ). He became one of the Executive Producers and show runners of SG-1 in its seventh season . Cooper was responsible for several major back-stories in the Stargate universe including the creation of the Ancients and the Alliance of Four Great Races .

He also made a cameo appearance as a writer for Wormhole X-Treme! in the episode of the same name . In " Flesh and Blood ", he had his daughter Emma Rose play the four year-old version of the Orici , Adria , and in " Talion ", he had his older daughter Megan Elizabeth Cooper play a young Jaffa female.

In addition to writing numerous episodes for Stargate SG-1 , Stargate Atlantis and Stargate Universe, Cooper has also gone on to direct episodes of all three, making his directional debut with the Stargate SG-1 Season 9 episode, Crusade .
