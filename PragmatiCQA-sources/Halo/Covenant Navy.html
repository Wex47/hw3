The Covenant Navy is a branch of the Covenant Empire responsible solely for naval operations; its roles include ship-to-ship combat, orbital bombardment , the deployment of fighters and bombers and the method of delivery for the Covenant Army . At its best, the Covenant Navy fielded tens of thousands of warships, most being able to carry fighters, dropships, ground troops and vehicles.

Great Schism

Throughout the majority of the Covenant Civil war , moreÂ commonly known as the "Great Schism" (especially among the Covenant separatists ), Covenant Separatists split away from the rest of the Covenant as they followed the Arbiter against the Covenant. As the battle raged, the High Prophet of Truth formally transferred command of the Covenant Navy to the Jiralhanae , a position once exclusively held by the Sangheili . The Covenant Separatists wiped out the primary Loyalist fleet at the Battle of Installation 00 .
