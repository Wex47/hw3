Ancilla were the Forerunner version of human AI . As exemplified by the Monitors , the Forerunners have developed a civilization with an extremely advanced knowledge of artificial intelligence technologies. Forerunner Ancilla have managed to achieve full sentience and are highly intelligent. The Forerunner Bornstellar-Makes-Eternal-Lasting had an Ancilla in his Forerunner armor as do all Forerunners with armor.
