History

In 2558 , Osiris was tasked with tracking down Dr. Catherine Halsey , and potentially eliminating Jul 'Mdama , at Kamchatka .

Later, Osiris was tasked by the Office of Naval Intelligence with hunting down an AWOL Blue Team . They began their search by talking to the Arbiter Thel 'Vadam . At some point while with the Arbiter, Osiris participated in the Battle of Sunaion .
