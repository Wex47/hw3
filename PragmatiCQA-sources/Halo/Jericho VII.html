Jericho VII was a lightly populated UEG colony world located in the Lambda Serpentis system .

History

In 2525 , the UNSC launched a counterinsurgency operation on the colony to repress the Insurrectionist presence. The SPARTAN-IIs and a force of ODSTs were each deployed in this engagement.

During the Human-Covenant war , the planet was glassed by the Covenant via orbital bombardment in the final stages of the Battle of Jericho VII in 2535 . At the end of the battle, John-117 asked Captain De Blanc for permission to watch Jericho VII being glassed. A large number of orphans from this planet enlisted for the SPARTAN-III project.

Economy

Prior to the Battle of Jericho VII, the planet had been known as the sole producer of Alt Burgundy , a fine and very expensive alcoholic beverage.

Physical Aspects

Topography

The world contained a biosphere suitable for human life, characterized by vast green oceans and rolling plains of palm grass.

Astronomy

Natural satellites included four uninhabited "silver" moons.
