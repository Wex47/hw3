Lieutenant is a junior commissioned officer rank in the UNSC Navy , equal in rank to Captain in the UNSC Marine Corps , the UNSC Air Force , and in the UNSC Army .

Title

This rank is sometimes referred to as "Full Lieutenant" to distinguish it from the subordinate rank of Lieutenant, Junior Grade . Informally, Lieutenants are often addressed as "L.T."

While Lieutenant is a rank specific to the UNSC Navy, the title "Lieutenant" is often used to refer to several ranks in the UNSC Defense Force :

Navy

  • Lieutenant
  • Lieutenant, Junior Grade

Marine Corps , Air Force , and Army

  • First Lieutenant
  • Second Lieutenant
