Master Chief Petty Officer is an E-9 rank of the UNSC Navy , and is a non-commissioned officer . It is equal in rank to Master Gunnery Sergeant in the UNSC Marine Corps , Sergeant Major in the UNSC Army , and Chief Master Sergeant in the UNSC Air Force . Master Chief Petty Officer ranks just above Senior Chief Petty Officer .
