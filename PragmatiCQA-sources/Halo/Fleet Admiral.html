Fleet Admiral is the highest commissioned officer rank in the UNSC Navy , although promotion to this rank is restricted to times of war. It is equal in rank to General of the Air Force in the UNSC Air Force , and General of the Army in the UNSC Army . Fleet admirals are generally well experienced and battle-hardened naval commanders.

As there is no equivalent in the UNSC Marine Corps , fleet admirals outrank all United Nations Space Command military organizations' Marine Officers.
