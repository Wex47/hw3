Lieutenant, Junior Grade is a junior commissioned officer rank of the UNSC Navy , equal in rank to First Lieutenant in the UNSC Marine Corps , UNSC Air Force , and the UNSC Army .

Style of address

  • Lieutenants, Junior Grade are referred to as "Junior Lieutenant" or "Second Lieutenant" in informal settings.
  • Lieutenant, Junior Grade can be abbreviated as or "LTJG."

Trivia

  • Preston Cole was likely a Lieutenant, Junior Grade when he was aboard the UNSC Las Vegas during the Callisto Incident . Though he is referred to as a " Second Lieutenant ," that is not a Navy rank, therefore he was most likely a Lieutenant, Junior Grade, or the Navy equivalent to 2nd Lieutenant, Ensign .
  • Jacob Keyes was this rank when he first traveled with Catherine Halsey on the shuttle, Han , to seek out potential SPARTAN-II candidates.
