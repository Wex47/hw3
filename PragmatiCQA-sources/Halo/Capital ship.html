There is more information available on this subject at Capital ship on the English Wikipedia .

Capital ships are the most important and largest warships . There is usually no formal criterion for the classification other than that these ships tend to be heavily armed and armored. A capital ship is generally a leading or primary ship in a fleet , battlegroup, or strike force.
