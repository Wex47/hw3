The Chieftain of the Jiralhanae is the central political and military leader within the Jiralhanae contingent of the Covenant , responsible for representing their interests in the Covenant. The position ultimately became the entire Covenant's military leadership after the Great Schism . The position was created by the Prophet of Truth , towards the end of the Human-Covenant war .

Function

A Chieftain of the Jiralhanae has requirements and functions beyond those of regular Chieftains . He represents the Jiralhanae as a populace and operates as a centric emissary on the Covenant's behalf, likely to the Ministry of Concert , and is also the Jiralhanae Commander in Chief of his entire race. The only known Chieftain of the Jiralhanae was trusted enough to be given a task vital to the Great Journey ; Tartarus was given the task of recovering the Index from Installation 05 and activating the installation.

Notable Chieftains of the Jiralhanae

  • Tartarus - Served as Chieftain in 2552 , until the end of the Battle of Installation 05 in November 2552 , in which he was killed by the Arbiter while doing Truth 's bidding.
  • Maccabeus - Served as Chieftain in 2525 , until the end of the Battle of Harvest in February 2525 , and was slain in a fight to the death by his nephew, Tartarus.

Trivia

  • After the Great Schism this position replaced its Sangheili equivalent, Imperial Admiral . Both ranks served as the ultimate commanding rank in the Covenant military.
  • After the death of Tartarus at the hands of Arbiter Thel 'Vadam , another Chieftain of the Jiralhanae was not immediately instituted, as Cethegus sought to claim the empty position during the Battle of Installation 00 .
