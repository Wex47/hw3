Palamok is the Yanme'e homeworld. Its atmospheric composition is similar to that of Earth, yet inhospitable to Humans , having average surface temperatures from 29 °C to 42 °C (84.2 °F to 107.6 °F), and a gravitational pull twice the strength of the Earth 's.
