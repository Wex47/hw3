Honor Guard refers to Covenant warriors that defend the Prophets . The term is used six or more times in the Halo universe:

  • Honor Guard of the Covenant - The protective service of the High Prophets and Council .
  • Sangheili Honor Guardsman - The typical Imperial Protectors of the Covenant before the events that led to the Civil War of the Covenant .
    • Honor Guard Ultra - The commanders of the Sangheili Honor Guard.
    • Honor Guard Councilor - A glitch in Halo 2 resulting in an Sangheili with the body armor of an Honor Guard Sangheili but the head of a Sangheili High Councilor .
  • Jiralhanae Honor Guardsman - The newer Honor Guards, chosen for their stronger loyalty to the Prophets.
  • Honor Guard Pike - A Covenant melee weapon, which is the highest weapon of any Honor Guard.
  • Honor Guard Wraith - A variant of the standard Wraith which has the same look, but has a Honor Guard-colored armor.
