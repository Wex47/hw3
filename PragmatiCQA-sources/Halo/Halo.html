Halo may refer to the following:

  • The Halo Franchise - The collection of games, books and other media which take place in the Halo Universe .
  • The Halo Universe - The collection of places, people and events in which the Halo Franchise develops.
  • The Halo Array - The network of 7 Installations built by the Forerunners designed to combat the Flood by wiping the galaxy of all sentient organic life.
  • Halo - The second level in Halo: CE .
  • Halo - The final level in Halo 3 .
  • The Halo - The penultimate level in Halo Wars 2 .
  • Halo - The iconic music piece also known as the Halo Theme , which is the 26th track in the Halo: Original Soundtrack .
  • Halo Theme MJOLNIR Mix - The remix of the original piece, as the 1st track in the Halo 2: Original Soundtrack .
  • Halo Theme Gungnir Mix - A remix of Halo Theme MJOLNIR Mix, featured in the Halo 2: Anniversary Original Soundtrack
  • Halo Theme Scorpion Mix - Another version of Halo Theme MJOLNIR Mix, featured in the Halo 2: Anniversary Original Soundtrack
  • Halo Reborn - The 22nd track in the Halo 3: Original Soundtrack .
  • Halo - The 6th track in the Halo Legends Original Soundtrack .
