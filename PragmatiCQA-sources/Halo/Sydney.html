Sydney is a city in the country of Australia on Earth . It houses the headquarters of the UNSC ( HIGHCOM Facility Bravo-6 ), ONI , and the UEG .

History

In November 2552 , during the Battle of Earth , Sydney was heavily bombarded by Covenant forces, due to its importance to the UNSC war effort. The harbor was mostly destroyed and repairs were in full effect by January 2553 .

On October 28, 2558 the Created sent a Guardian to Sydney. Admiral Serin Osman and Fleet Admiral Terrence Hood managed to evacuate the city prior to it activating its EMP.
