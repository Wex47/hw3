History

Members of the SPARTAN-IV program train with one another inside the War Games simulator on the UNSC Infinity , Anvil Station , Munera Platform A7 and many other facilities.

Simulations

Various locations around the galaxy have been recreated for these training purposes. However, certain military assets represented within these simulations can be changed by request of admirals or captains. This is to hopefully prepare for any type of situation that might arise.

Gameplay

Halo 4 matchmaking contains multiple playlists which can be accessed by any players unless the playlist requires DLC content. The network matches Spartans with other Spartans based on their Competitive Skill Ranks (CSR) rather than SR ranks.
