Rebel may refer to:

Human

  • Frieden Rebels : A secessionist faction that fought against the UN in the Interplanetary War .
  • Koslovic Rebels : The neo-Communist faction that fought against the UN in the Interplanetary War.
  • Insurrectionists : The general name for rebels during the Insurrection .
    • Secessionist Union : A rebel union on Harvest led by Jerald Mulkey Ander .
    • Unnamed Rebel Group : An unknown rebel group that resides on Harvest and Arcadia .
    • United Rebel Front : The larger faction of human rebels united against the UNSC government.
      • Eridanus Rebels : The faction of human rebels in the Eridanus system .
    • New Colonial Alliance : A rebel group that formed in the aftermath of the Human-Covenant war .

Covenant

  • Sesa 'Refumee's separatist faction : A breakaway faction that follows Sesa 'Refumee and rejects the Covenant's belief in the Great Journey .
  • Covenant Separatists : An Elite -led faction that fought against the Covenant, after the Great Schism ejected them from the hegemony.
  • Unggoy Rebellion : A Grunt -led uprising against the rest of the Covenant which led to the creation of an Arbiter .
