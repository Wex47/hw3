Private First Class , or PFC, is the second enlisted rank in the UNSC Marine Corps and third in the UNSC Army . A Marine PFC is equal in rank to Crewman Apprentice in the UNSC Navy and an Airman in the Air Force . An Army PFC is equal to Crewman in the Navy and Airman First Class in the Air Force. In the Marine Corps, it is one rank above Private , and one rank under Lance Corporal . In the Army, it is one rank above Private (E-2) and one rank below .
