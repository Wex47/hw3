A shotgun , also known as a scattergun and peppergun , is a firearm that is usually designed to be fired from the shoulder, which uses the energy of a fixed shell to fire a number of small spherical pellets called shot, or a solid projectile called a slug.

Shotgun can refer to the following:

  • DTM Series - A series of shotguns by Weapon System Technologies .
    • DTM/Law Enforcement
    • M90 Close Assault Weapon System - The type of shotgun featured in the Halo trilogy .
  • M45 Tactical Shotgun - A shotgun featured in Halo: Reach .
    • M45D Tactical Shotgun - A shotgun featured in Halo 4 and Halo 5: Guardians .
    • M45E Tactical Shotgun - A shotgun featured in Halo: Combat Evolved Anniversary .
  • Shotgun (Halo 4 Commendation) - A multiplayer commendation in Halo 4.
  • Type-52 Pistol - A shotgun-like Jiralhanae weapon, also known as Mauler.
  • Underslung shotgun - A weapon attachment usable with some other firearms.
  • Z-180 Close Combat Rifle/Asymmetric Engagement Mitigator - The Forerunner equivalent of a shotgun known as a Scattershot.
