Sergeant is the fifth enlisted rank (E-5) of the UNSC Marine Corps and the UNSC Army , and is a non-commissioned officer . It is equal to the rank of Petty Officer Second Class in the UNSC Navy and Staff Sergeant in the Air Force . Sergeants in the Army may lead fireteams of around 4 soldiers, while Marine Sgts. typically lead squads of about 12 Marines.

While Sergeant is a rank of its own, the title "Sergeant" is often used to refer to any of the enlisted ranks of the UNSC Marine Corps or Army which carry the title Sergeant:
