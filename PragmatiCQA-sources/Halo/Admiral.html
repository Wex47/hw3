Admiral is a senior commissioned officer rank of the UNSC Navy , equal in rank to General in the UNSC Marine Corps , UNSC Air Force , and UNSC Army . It is immediately below the rank of Fleet Admiral , the highest known UNSC Naval rank.

Title

While Admiral is a rank of its own, the title "Admiral" is often used to refer to any of the flag officer ranks of the UNSC Navy:

  • Fleet Admiral
  • Admiral
  • Vice Admiral
  • Rear Admiral (Upper Half)
  • Rear Admiral (Lower Half)

Due to the ambiguity of the title "Admiral," Admirals are often known as a "Four Star Admiral" or "Full Admiral" to distinguish it from subordinate ranks of Admiral. The term "Four Star Admiral" comes from the rank insignia worn on the uniform.
