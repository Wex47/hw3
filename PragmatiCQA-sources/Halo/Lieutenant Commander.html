Lieutenant Commander is a commissioned officer rank of the UNSC Navy , equal to the rank of Major in the UNSC Marine Corps , UNSC Air Force , and the UNSC Army .

So far, Lieutenant Commander is the third highest rank held by a Spartan-II , as seen in the cases of Kurt-051 and Fhajad-084 . Catherine-B320 , a Spartan-III , also held the rank of Lieutenant Commander as a member of Noble Team during the Battle of Reach . The highest being Commander.

Under military customs and courtesies, a Lieutenant Commander is addressed as "Commander."
