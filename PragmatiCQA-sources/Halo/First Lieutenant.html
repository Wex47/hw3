First Lieutenant is a junior, company grade commissioned officer rank of the UNSC Marine Corps , Army , and Air Force , equal in rank to Lieutenant, Junior Grade in the UNSC Navy . They usually serve as the XO (executive officer) of a Company , but can be given command of platoons, such as the case of First Lieutenant Melissa McKay in Halo: The Flood .

A Second Lieutenant usually receives a promotion to First Lieutenant after 18 to 24 months service.

In informal settings, First Lieutenants are referred to simply as "Lieutenant" or "LT."
