Commander is a commissioned officer rank of the UNSC Navy , equal in rank to Lieutenant Colonel in the UNSC Marine Corps , UNSC Air Force , and UNSC Army .

Commanders typically serve as the Executive Officer (XO) (second-in-command) of a UNSC Warship; however, they may also command their own UNSC Warship.
