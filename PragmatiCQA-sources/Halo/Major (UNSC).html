Major is the lowest field-grade commissioned officer rank of the UNSC Marine Corps , UNSC Army , and UNSC Air Force , equal in rank to the Lieutenant Commander in the UNSC Navy .

A Major is typically found as an executive officer in a battalion . However, they can be found in command of battalions , such as the case of Major Antonio Silva during the Battle of Installation 04 , or in service as staff officers for larger units.
