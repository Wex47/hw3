Gameplay

Spartan Abilities

In Halo 5: Guardians , armor abilities are replaced with Spartan Abilities . Spartan Abilities are distinct from Armor Abilities, in that they are not limited by cooldowns or pickups. Rather, they are base player abilities which can be used by all at any time, similar to Dual Wielding or Boarding . The seven Spartan Abilities are:

  • Thruster Pack: Similar to Thruster Pack from Halo 4 , except now there is movement in any direction.
  • Ground Pound: While in air, you can slam down into the ground, potentially killing anyone below.
  • Clamber: If you miss a ledge, you can pull yourself up.
  • Slide: While sprinting, you can slide.
  • Charge: While sprinting, you can slam into an enemy.
  • Sprint: Sprinting like in Halo 4 , but after a certain amount of time, you will achieve the perfect velocity to activate the previous two abilities. Sprinting will stop shield recharge.
  • Smart Scope: A zoom function that applies to all weapons. Each weapon has a different degree of zoom.

Multiplayer

Multiplayer in Halo 5: Guardians is split into two main play styles: the classic Arena experience, and the new 24-player game, Warzone . Big Team Battle is not present in the game at launch, instead it was replaced with Warzone, however it was added post-launch. Spectator mode is allowed within multiplayer matches. There are over 20 multiplayer maps at the launch of Halo 5 . At least an additional 15 maps were added post launch, before June 2016.

Standard Edition

The standard edition of the game includes the disc and a 14-day Xbox Live Gold trial, and was released October 27th, 2015 .

Digital Edition

The digital edition contains a digital download of the game, without a disc or game case.

Limited Edition

Including collectible items such as a limited edition game disc and a steelbook case, the Halo 5: Guardians limited edition is available for pre-order before the release of the game. In addition to the limited edition case and disc, the bundle includes a 14-day Xbox Live trial, Halo: The Fall of Reach Animated Series , a Warzone REQ bundle , dossiers on Blue Team , Fireteam Osiris and Spartan Locke 's orders, and finally a metal model of a Guardian . The edition costs $99.99.

Limited Collector's Edition

The Halo 5: Guardians Limited Collector's Edition contained, in addition to the Limited Edition collectible content, a statue of Master Chief and Agent Locke . This statue can be pulled apart from its default state, to present the pair as enemies rather than a team like is presented by default. It cost $249.99.

Controversially, the Limited Collector's edition originally contained a digital download code rather than a physical disc. This was intended to allow purchasers of the Limited Collector's edition to pre-load the game. In response to a fan uproar at the lack of a disc, 343 Industries stated that they were looking for a solution to satisfy those who want a disc. Later, they confirmed that customers would be able to opt to have a disc rather than the default digital download code.

Digital Deluxe Edition

A Digital Deluxe edition was also available for pre-order before the game's release. It cost $89.99, and contained a digital download of the game itself, a REQ bundle , and Halo: The Fall of Reach Animated Series .

Development

Iterations

Early Concepts

In the earlier iterations of the game, Fireteam Osiris was actually supposed to be Fireteam Majestic , composed of Olympia Vale , Holly Tanaka , Jameson Locke , and Gabriel Thorne .

Beta

At least one known version of the game exists, the Halo 5: Guardians Multiplayer Beta . This version includes different menu systems featuring a different background and different music, armor descriptions feature the manufacturers, Empire had slightly different pathing, and armor colouring was able to be individually selected.

Xbox One Reveal

Halo (Xbox One) was confirmed to be in development for the Xbox One during the Xbox One Reveal event on May 21, 2013.

E3 2013

Xbox Wire

On May 16, 2014, Bonnie Ross, General Manager of 343 Industries, announced on Xbox Wire that the Halo game will be named Halo 5: Guardians and will be released in Fall 2015. Bonnie also announced that Halo 5: Guardians will run on a new and more powerful engine.

Hunt the Truth

On March 19, 2015, Frank O'Connor tweeted a link to a page called . On the page, there was countdown that had ended on March 22, 2015 at 1:00am UTC and had an image of the word "Traitor" engraved onto a bullet. The page revealed to be about a journalist and war photographer, Benjamin Giraud , who is trying to find the truth about the Master Chief.

Trivia

  • The Halo 5: Guardians multiplayer beta was accessible through Halo: The Master Chief Collection .
  • Exclusive content unlocked in Halo: The Master Chief Collection will carry over to Halo 5: Guardians .
  • Halo 5 ' s Theater mode writes replay files to the dedicated servers as the player is playing. These files can be accessed in real-time, enabling players to spectate others live.
  • Halo 5 ' s internal development code name for the AI companions was "Musketeers."
  • Halo 5: Guardians is the first Halo first-person shooter to be given an ESRB rating of T.
