The Assembly Forges were a series of manufacturing plants located in High Charity that produced material for the Covenant military. The Assembly Forges produced most of the Covenant's ground vehicles, aircraft, starfighters, stationary weapons emplacements, and heavy infantry weapons. The Forges were lost to the Covenant when High Charity was conquered by the Flood and later destroyed.
