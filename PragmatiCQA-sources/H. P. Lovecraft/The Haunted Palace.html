The Haunted Palace is a 1963 American horror film starring Vincent Price and Lon Chaney Jr.

Synopsis

Charles Dexter Ward arrives at a small village to visit the house he inherited from his ancestor who died there 100 years ago.

Behind the Mythos

Directed by Roger Corman in 1963, produced by Alta Vista Productions, and distributed by American International Pictures, The Haunted Palace was marketed as part of Corman's series of films based on the works of Edgar Allan Poe despite the fact that it was (loosely) based on The Case of Charles Dexter Ward by H. P. Lovecraft .
