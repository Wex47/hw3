Atlantis

Type

Continent
The lost continent of Atlantis hosted an ancient civilization on Earth, having been destroyed and sunk beneath the ocean in a cataclysmic event.

In Lovecraft Circle Works

  • " Atlantis ", Clark Ashton Smith 's poem offers a brief description of the continent.
  • " Spawn of Dagon ", Henry Kuttner 's short story deals with inhuman creatures attempting to bring about the destruction of Atlantis in prehistory.

Known Inhabitants

  • Klarkash-Ton , an alter ego of Clark Ashton Smith, was an Atlantean priest of the Great Old One Tsathoggua .
