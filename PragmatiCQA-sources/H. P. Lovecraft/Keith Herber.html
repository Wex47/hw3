Overview

A professional bass guitarist who has written both gaming supplements and novels for White Wolf Publishing, Keith Herber's relevance to the Cthulhu Mythos is his work on the Call of Cthulhu roleplaying game by Chaosium . Already a freelance writer for Chaosium, he joined the staff in 1989 and worked as the Call of Cthulhu line editor for the next five years until his departure in 1994; however, even after leaving the company Herber continued to provide additional material for the game through until 2008.
