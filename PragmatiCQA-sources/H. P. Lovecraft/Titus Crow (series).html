The Titus Crow series, sometimes referred to as The Cthulhu Cycle Deities series, is a Cthulhu Mythos story arc by noted British author Brian Lumley . It comprises six novels and eleven short stories featuring the eponymous protagonist Titus Crow and those allied with him against the forces of the Great Old Ones , primarily Ithaqua , but including several other Mythos entities.

Novels

  • The Burrowers Beneath (1974)
  • The Transition of Titus Crow (1975)
  • The Clock of Dreams (1978)
  • Spawn of the Winds (1978)
  • In the Moon of Borea (1979)
  • Elysia (1989)
