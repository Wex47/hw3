He was one of many writers published by Weird Tales under the editorship of Dorothy McIlwraith (beginning in April 1940). His greatest fame is as the creator of Fafhrd and Gray Mouser and their Lankhmar setting in the Swords series of novels and stories, creating as it did an ironic and almost post modern twist on the Sword and Sorcery genre created by Robert E. Howard . There is nothing in the Swords series of any real Mythos connection or significance.

Influence on the Mythos

Created in The Terror From the Depths , the Tunnelers Below were later expanded upon by Chaosium in their Call of Cthulhu (1994) role-playing game.
