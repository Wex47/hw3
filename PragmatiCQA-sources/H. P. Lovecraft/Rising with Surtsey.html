" Rising with Surtsey " is a short story by British author Brian Lumley .

Synopsis

Phillip Haughtree has to have his brother Julian committed, as the latter is showing signs of severe mental distress. When Julian is released, he appears to have been rehabilitated, but is still displaying an obsession with the occult. When Phillip confronts him about his mania, Julian reveals that he is actually a servant of the Great Old One s, and Phillip kills him accidentally before being himself committed for raving about ancient monsters.

Characters

  • Phillip Haughtree
  • Julian Haughtree

Publication History

"Rising with Surtsey" was first published in the 1971 Arkham House anthology Dark Things , edited by August Derleth . It has since been reprinted in a number of different collections, many of which were edited by Brian Lumley himself.
