Alert

First appearance

HPL : The Call of Cthulhu

Created by

H.P. Lovecraft
The Alert was a heavily armored steam yacht which originated in Dunedin, New Zealand, and at one point was manned by "a queer and evil-looking crew of Kanakas and half-castes," in addition to being members of the Cthulhu cult. The crew of the Alert was slain in retaliation for attacking the schooner Emma , and upon being discovered by the Vigilant , the Alert was towed to Sydney, Australia where it was sold for commercial use. ( HPL : " The Call of Cthulhu ")
