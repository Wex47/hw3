The Cthulhu Companion is a Call of Cthulhu supplement from roleplaying games manufacturer Chaosium .

Overview

A compilation of articles from a wide variety of writers, the Cthulhu Companion contains additional skills, details on prison systems of the 1930s world, an expansion of the Sanity condition, a selection of gods and monsters, and three ready-to-play scenarios. Along with poetry by H. P. Lovecraft himself, it features cover art by Chris Marrinan .

Publication History

First released in 1983, the Cthulhu Companion has since gone out of print, but is currently available as a digital PDF download direct from Chaosium.
