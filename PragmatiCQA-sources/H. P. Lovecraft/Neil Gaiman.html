Neil Richard MacKinnon Gaiman is a prolific British author of fantasy, horror, science fiction and black comedy, a number of whose works are either set in, or feature connections to, the Cthulhu Mythos . Many of his writings have been adapted for film, television and comic book mediums.

Selected Works

  • Good Omens (1990) [with Terry Pratchett; contains characters named Hastur and Dagon , although they are not related to the mythos]
  • " Only the End of the World Again " (1994)
  • " A Study in Emerald " (2003)
