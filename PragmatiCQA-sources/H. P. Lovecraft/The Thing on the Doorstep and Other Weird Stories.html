The Thing on the Doorstep and Other Weird Stories is an anthology of short works by American author Howard Phillips Lovecraft .

Overview

First released in 2001 by Penguin Classics and edited by H. P. Lovecraft scholar S. T. Joshi , The Thing on the Doorstep and Other Weird Stories is Penguin's second collection of Lovecraft's work. Like it’s predecessor, The Call of Cthulhu and Other Weird Stories , the versions of the stories contained within are for the most part the revised editions published earlier by Arkham House , and also edited by Joshi. The exception is At the Mountains of Madness , which is presented in a newly-edited "definitive edition" with an introduction by China Miéville.
