Emma

First appearance

HPL : The Call of Cthulhu

Created by

H.P. Lovecraft
The Emma was a fictional ship created by H. P. Lovecraft , which makes its only appearance in the 1928 short story The Call of Cthulhu . It is a two-masted schooner which originated in Auckland, New Zealand, and is manned by eleven crew members. These are Captain Collins , First Mate Green , Second Mate Gustaf Johansen , William Briden , Rodriguez , Donovan , Hawkins , Guerrera , Angstrom , Parker and one unnamed man. The Emma was damaged and presumably sunk in the aftermath of an encounter with an armored steam yacht, the Alert , manned by Cthulhu cultists.
