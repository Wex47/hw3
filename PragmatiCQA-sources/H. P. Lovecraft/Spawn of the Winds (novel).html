Spawn of the Winds is the fourth novel in the Titus Crow series by Brian Lumley . It should be noted that Titus Crow is entirely absent from this novel however.

Plot Summary

Outraged by the killing of his brother by Ithaqua , psychic Texan Hank Silberhutte gathers a team together to try and find a way to permanently defeat or destroy the Wind Walker. Whilst en route in an aircraft, Hank and his team are swept to a world ruled by Ithaqua and a planetary romance style adventure unfolds.

Absent Titus Crow and friends, the action is of the two-fisted pulp variety complete with an alien princess as ally.
