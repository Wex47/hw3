Colin Wilson (26 June 1931 – 5 December 2013) was an English writer. He wrote novels, nonfiction books on the occult, and works on philosophy, especially philosophy in the Gurdjieff and Existentialist traditions. His novels included Lovecraftian elements such as cosmic horror and impersonal if hostile entities from outer space. Writing in the 1970s, his works are also more overtly sexual and explicitly violent than those of earlier writers. His works are not within the Mythos but some of them are definitely Mythos-adjacent. He seems to have had a particular liking for writing fiction about various exotic forms of vampire .
