Curse of the Chthonians was a sourcebook for the Call of Cthulhu roleplaying game by Chaosium .

Overview

Containing a selection of four subterranean-themed, ready-to-play scenarios (two of which are linked to form a mini-campaign of sorts), along with a background section on Kabbalistic Science, Curse of the Chthonians runs to 88 pages (first edition) and 160 pages (second edition). It was written by renowned games designer Sandy Petersen .

Contents

  • "The City Without a Name" by William Hamblin
  • "The Curse of Chaugnar Faugn" by William A. Barton
  • "Dark Carnival" by David A. Hargrave
  • "Thoth's Dagger" by William Hamblin

Publication History

First released in 1984, Curse of the Chthonians was later expanded and reissued in 2011.
