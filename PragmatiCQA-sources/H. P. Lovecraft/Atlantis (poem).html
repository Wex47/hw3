" Atlantis " is a Cthulhu Mythos poem by the late Clark Ashton Smith , and forms the first part of his Poseidonis story cycle.

Synopsis

As it’s title might suggest, "Atlantis" comprises a description of the lost continent from which the poem takes its name.

Publication History

First published in the Clark Ashton Smith collection The Star Treader and Other Poems in November 1912, "Atlantis" has been reprinted many times in several different publications.
