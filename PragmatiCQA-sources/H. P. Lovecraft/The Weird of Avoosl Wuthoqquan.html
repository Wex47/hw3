" The Weird of Avoosl Wuthoqquan " is a short story written by Clark Ashton Smith and features the character of the title, the avaricious money-lender Avoosl Wuthoqquan , chasing a pair of ill-gotten emeralds that mysteriously rolled away from his possession, out of the city of Commorium and into a far-away cavern. Inside this cavern he finds a great trove of precious gems, as well as its monstrous owner, the latter of whom devours Avoosl Wuthoqquan before he can sink beneath the pile of treasure.
