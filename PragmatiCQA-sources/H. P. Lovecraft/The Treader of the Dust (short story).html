" The Treader of the Dust " is a short story by Clark Ashton Smith . It was published in August 1935 in the Magazine of Fantasy and Science Fiction .

Synopsis

Antiquarian John Sebastian obtains the Testament of Carnamagos , a tome of forbidden knowledge, from a book seller and in the process is exposed to the horror and hazard of Quachil Uttaus .

Returning to his house, Sebastian is captivated by the Testament and held in place until Quachil Uttaus slowly advances towards him across a gulf of time and space, ultimately- turning the man to dust.
