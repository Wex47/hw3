Wildside Press is an American independent publishing company whose output includes work from H. P. Lovecraft 's fictional Cthulhu Mythos .

Overview

Originally founded in 1989 by author John Gregory Betancourt and his wife Kim as an outlet for speculative fiction, Wildside's scope has since expanded to cover a number of genres, including mystery, science fiction, romance, and nonfiction. The company also produces a range of audiobooks (both in digital and compact disc formats) and downloadable eBooks, and maintains a print on demand service as well as a reprint project dedicated to publishing issues of old pulp magazines in their original format.

Wildside Press incorporate the imprints Borgo Press, Cosmos Books and Point Blank.
