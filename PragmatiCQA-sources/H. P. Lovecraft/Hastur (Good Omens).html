Overview

Hastur was once an angel, who decided to took part in Lucifer's revolution, alongside 10 million other angels. All rebel-angels were cast into Hell, and became the demons. Hastur himself became a "Duke of Hell".

Hastur became a key-player in setting up Armageddon, overseeing Crowley and the young antichrist.

Personality

Like most demons, Hastur is a sadisitic being, enjoying to torment and kill humans and even other demons.

Trivia

  • Hastur is one of the two demons named after Great Old Ones, alongside Dagon .
