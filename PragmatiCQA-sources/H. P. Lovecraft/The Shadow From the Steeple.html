" The Shadow from the Steeple " is a short story by Robert Bloch .

It is the third and final story in a trilogy by Bloch and H.P. Lovecraft started by Bloch in 1935 with The Shambler from the Stars , and continued by H. P. Lovecraft in 1936 with The Haunter of the Dark . The trilogy concluding in this short story is also the final "canonical" exploration of the mystery surrounding the " Shining Trapezohedron ".

The three stories in the trilogy also feature the playful and mutually consensual killing off of the two authors by each other. This was an idea begun by Robert Bloch.
