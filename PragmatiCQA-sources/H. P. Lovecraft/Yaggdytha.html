Yaggdytha ( The Incandescent One ) is twin brother of Vorvadoss , and first-born of Nodens , the Lord of the Abyss. Yaggdytha is also associated with Bel Yarnak . It manifests as a great amorphous, incandescent ball of cyan living energy, spread itself into a web of giant talons of light.

It is mentioned in the Book of Iod , The Celaeno Fragments , The Revelations of Glaaki , and an untitled work by Johannes Henricus Pott.
