Demons by Daylight is an anthology of short horror stories by British author Ramsey Campbell . It is divided into three thematically-linked sections, Nightmares , Errol Undercliffe: a tribute , and Relationships , and contains several stories linked to the larger Cthulhu Mythos .

Publication History

First published in 1973 by Arkham House , Demons by Daylight had originally been scheduled for a 1971 release; however, the death of chief editor August Derleth that year caused a two-year delay. In 1979, a revised edition was published by Jove/HBJ with "The Second Staircase" removed and replaced with two other stories, "Reply Guaranteed" and "The Telephones," but the 1990 Carrol & Graf reprint restored the original story line-up. It was again reissued by Lume Books in 2017.
