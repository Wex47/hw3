The H.P. Lovecraft Historical Society , or HPLHS , is an organization created in 1984 that is devoted to the promotion of the works of Howard Phillips Lovecraft and the Mythos . The produce a wide range of media based on that material.

They created the Cthulhu Lives Live Action Role Playing (LARP) game and produced film and audio pieces; usually adaptations of Lovecraft stories.

They also ran the Strange Eons fanzine.
