The Moon-Lens is a short story written by Ramsey Campbell .  It was first published in the 1964 collection The Inhabitant of the Lake and Less Welcome Tenants .  It was later published in Campbell's collection Cold Print . Later it was published in the anthology The Shub-Niggurath Cycle: Tales of the Black Goat With a Thousand Young , edited by Robert M. Price .
