" Old One " is an ambiguous term in the Cthulhu Mythos , and may refer to:

  • Great Old Ones , a pantheon of dead alien deities buried inside the Earth, including, Cthulhu . ( The Call of Cthulhu ).
  • Outer Gods , a pantheon of deities who break inside the universe through Yog-Sothoth ( The Dunwich Horror ).
  • Entities who can defeat the Deep Ones using a swastika symbol ( The Shadow Over Innsmouth ).
  • Inhabitants of K'n-yan ( The Mound ).
  • Elder Things , a species of alien lifeforms ( At the Mountains of Madness ).
  • The inhabitants of the City of the Winged One , whose last representative slaughtered the Black Corsairs and Bêlit , and was killed by Conan ( Queen of the Black Coast ).
