Necronomicon Press is a small-press publisher of H. P. Lovecraft -related content based in the United States.

Overview

Established in 1976 as an outlet for the writings of H. P. Lovecraft, over the years Necronomicon Press have expanded their scope to include other Cthulhu Mythos contributors, as well as critical works from many other authors (generally published in journal form), and at least one biography of Lovecraft's life.
