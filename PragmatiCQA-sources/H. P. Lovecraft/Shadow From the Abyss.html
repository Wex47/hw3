" Shadow From the Abyss " is an American comic book strip.

Overview

An adaptation of the Howard Phillips Lovecraft novella The Shadow Out of Time , "Shadow From the Abyss" was drawn by cartoonist Larry Todd . It appeared in Skull Comics #5 in August 1972, and was issued by small press comic publisher Last Gasp.
