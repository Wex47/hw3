The Malleus Monstrorum (intended to be Latin for "Hammer of the Monsters") is a gaming supplement for the Chaosium Call of Cthulhu RPG .

It consists of two large volumes. Volume I contains descriptions and illustrations of all the published monsters. Volume II is a similar compilation of all the known deities as adapted and published by Chaosium.

Publisher summary

Here, shall you know the terrors of the void, the nightmare bringers, and the unspeakable lurkers.

Ancient secrets, whispered lore, and collected facts concerning the alien and otherworldly horrors of the Cthulhu Mythos . A cavalcade of monsters and god-like alien intelligences beyond human understanding, all vividly detailed and portrayed. With over 250 entries to inspire countless adventures.

It is currently available in electronic and physical form.

Contents

It introduced three new entities alongside preexisting ones, following a Chaosium competition. The new entities unique to Malleus Monstrorum are:

  • Robert Horowitz 's Dust-Men
  • Bruce L. Priddy 's Father-Of-All-Sharks
  • Chad J. Bowser 's Pallid Dancers

Behind the Mythos

The title of this work is a play on the name of the Medieval book 'Malleus Maleficorum', "Hammer of the Evil-Doers", usually translated as "Hammer of the Witches".
