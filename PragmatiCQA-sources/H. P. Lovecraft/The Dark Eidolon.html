The Dark Eidolon is a short story by Clark Ashton Smith , taking place in his Zothique setting.

It was first published in Weird Tales in 1935 and was republished in the anthology The Spell of Seven , edited by L. Sprague de Camp .

Synopsis

Namirrha, an arrogant sorcerer, is destroyed by his own hubris. He makes war on the Emperor Zotulla and his Empire of Xylac. At a feast he first summons female Ghoul dancing "girls", before summoning horse-like monstrosities from beyond called the coursers of Thamogorgos to destroy the empire. Whilst this begins, Namirrha then mind swaps with the Emperor. However, all does not go as planned for the prideful spellcaster...

Behind the Mythos

The " eidolon " of the title references both the ghostly form of the Emperor after he is driven from his body and exists disembodied as a phantom over the dead body of the sorcerer, and of course the dark desires of Namirrha himself.
