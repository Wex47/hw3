Nightmare's Disciple is a crime / Mythos novel by the late Joseph S. Pulver, Sr.

Synopsis

Detective Chrisopher James Stewart pursues a serial killer in Schenectady, NY. following clues indicating deep links to the Cthulhu Mythos . The story is a blend of crime novel and Mythos story.

Behind the Mythos

A similar work in terms of its setting to that of the Arkham Detective Agency stories for which Pulver also wrote.
