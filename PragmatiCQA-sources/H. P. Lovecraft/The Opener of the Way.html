The Opener of the Way is an anthology of fantasy and horror shorts by Robert Bloch .

Overview

Published by Arkham House in 1945, The Opener of the Way was released as a limited edition in a run of 2,065 copies. Panther Books issued a British hardcover release in 1974, and in 1991, it was translated for release in Italian.
