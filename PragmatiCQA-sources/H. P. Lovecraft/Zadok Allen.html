Zadok Allen was one of the few remaining humans in Innsmouth . After relating to traveller Robert Olmstead the history of Innsmouth he was captured and sacrificed to the Deep Ones by the Esoteric Order of Dagon .

Expanded Mythos

Allen also appeared as an important non-player character in Bethesda Softworks' Call of Cthulhu: Dark Corners of the Earth video game in 2005.

In Robert M. Price 's short story " The Transition of Zadok Allen ", he is revealed to have in fact been taken below the waves and transformed into a Deep One, where he presumably still lives.
