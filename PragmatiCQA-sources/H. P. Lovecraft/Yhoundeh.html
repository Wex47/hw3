Yhoundeh or Y'houndeh (The Elk-Goddess) is the wife of Nyarlathotep and the spawn of the androgyne animal Archetype Zyhumé . ( CIRCLE : letter to Robert H. Barlow , 10 September 1934 ( Clark Ashton Smith ))

Her priests banned the worship of Tsathoggua and punished any heretics. She was worshipped in the waning days of Hyperborea . As the Hyperborean civilization was coming to an end, Yhoundeh's followers returned to worshipping Tsathoggua. ( CIRCLE : " The Door to Saturn ")
