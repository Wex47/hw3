Alan Moore's The Courtyard is a 2-part mini-series by Antony Johnston who adapted it from a 1994 prose story by Alan Moore .

Synopsis

The FBI agent Aldo Sax is investigating 3 ritual murders, leading him to a night club in New York City where he meets a mysterious veiled man named Johnny Carcosa . It's the prelude to the story Neonomicon .
