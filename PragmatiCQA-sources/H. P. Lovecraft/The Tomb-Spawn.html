Synopsis

Two brothers on the future continent of Zothique are on the run from a pack of ferocious Ghorii . In their hunt for water and sanctuary, they stumble across the cursed tomb of long-dead sorcerer-king Ossaru and his faithful alien servitor Nioth Korghai . However, the corpses have long since evolved into something else, and the two men are killed by the creature for violating its resting place

Characters

  • Milab and Marabac , jewel traders travelling Zothique's desert wastes.
  • Ossaru , once a powerful wizard and ruler of half of Zothique, now deceased.
  • Nioth Korghai , an alien being who was befriended by Ossaru and became his advisor, now deceased.

Publication History

"The Tomb-Spawn" was first published in the May 1934 edition of Weird Tales . It has since been reprinted in a multitude of periodicals and anthologies, and has been translated into French, Dutch and German.
