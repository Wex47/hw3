Author

H.P. Lovecraft

Publication

Arkham House

Date of writing

1911-1937

Publication date

1965-1976
Selected Letters , colloquially known as the Selected Letters of H. P. Lovecraft , is a five volume compilation of extracts of Lovecraft's known correspondance published by Arkham House .
