Shadows of Yog-Sothoth: A Global Campaign to Save Mankind is a campaign sourcebook for the Call of Cthulhu roleplaying game.

Overview

The first scenario supplement for Call of Cthulhu , Shadows of Yog-Sothoth contains a seven-part campaign and two stand-alone adventures. The campaign takes the players across the globe in pursuit of the cult of the Hermetic Order of the Silver Twilight , from America’s east coast to Scotland to the South Pacific. The two adventures consist of "The People of the Monolith", a trip to Hungary in search of a mysterious poet, and "The Warren", the hunt for a missing friend in Boston.

Publication History

Released in 1982, the first edition of Shadows of Yog-Sothoth ran to 72 pages and featured artwork by Tom Sullivan . In 1989, it was collected along with other previously-published playing material and an all-new scenario in Cthulhu Classics , while in 2004, it was re-released in an updated format for the sixth edition of Call of Cthulhu along with new material and artwork. This version ran to 144 pages.
