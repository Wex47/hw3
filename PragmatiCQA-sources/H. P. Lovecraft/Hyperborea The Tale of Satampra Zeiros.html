Hyperborea: The Tale of Satampra Zeiros , is a comic book adaptation of Clark Ashton Smith's short story The Tale of Satampra Zeiros . Released in 2004, it is illustrated by American artist Jason Thompson and includes a map of Hyperborea along with additional artwork.
