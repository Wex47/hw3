Obed Marsh was a sea captain from Innsmouth . At the height of his career he had command of three ships, the Columbia , the Hetty , and the Sumatra Queen . He learned from the inhabitants of a Polynesian island a ritual to summon the Deep Ones . This was done on Devil Reef before entering into a pact with them, offering human sacrifices in exchange for a constant supply of fish and the occasional golden artefact. Marsh at some point founded the Esoteric Order of Dagon . He died in 1878 and his family continued their control of Innsmouth. He is the great-great-grandfather of Robert Olmstead .
