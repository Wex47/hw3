The Lamp of Alhazred is a short story by August Derleth , repurposing a fragment recounting hiking Neutaconkanut Hill from Lovecraft's final and unfinished piece of correspondence. ( HPL : Selected Letters of H. P. Lovecraft 5.930) This is one of the controversial "posthumous collaborations" between Lovecraft and Derleth which were for all intents and purposes simply short stories written by Derleth himself.
