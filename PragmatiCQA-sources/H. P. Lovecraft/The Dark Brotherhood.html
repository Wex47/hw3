" The Dark Brotherhood " is a short story by H. P. Lovecraft and August Derleth . One of the "posthumous collaborations" between the two, it was in fact written almost entirely by the latter, based on a fragment by the former.

Synopsis

A pair of friends whose habit is to explore the city by night meet a mysterious stranger who appears to share their enjoyment of after-dark walks. As their relationship develops, the woman is beguiled by the stranger's charm while the man uncovers a cult who are scheming to draw something deadly to Earth.

Publication History

"The Dark Brotherhood" was first published in the collection The Dark Brotherhood and Other Pieces by Arkham House in 1966.
