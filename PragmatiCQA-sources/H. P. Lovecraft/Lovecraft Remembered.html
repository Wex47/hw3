Lovecraft Remembered is a non-fiction collection edited by Peter Cannon and published by Arkham House in 1998. It contains the majority of the memoirs on Howard Phillips Lovecraft from previous Arkham House publications, many from those who were his friends or associates, including C. M. Eddy, Jr. , Sonia H. Greene , Robert E. Howard , Clark Ashton Smith , Frank Belknap Long , Zealia Bishop , Hazel Heald and many others.
