Born of the Winds is a fantasy short story by Brian Lumley . It first appeared in The Magazine of Fantasy and Science Fiction , December 1975.

It is part of a sequence of stories detailing action adventure struggles of heroic humans against the children of Ithaqua the Wind Walker. It features the children of Ithaqua, the Spawn of the Winds although they were fully fleshed out by Chaosium for their RPG .
