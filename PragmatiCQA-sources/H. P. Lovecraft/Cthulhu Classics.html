Cthulhu Classics is a sourcebook for Chaosium ’s Call of Cthulhu roleplaying game.

Overview

An anthology collecting a seven-scenario campaign and five individual scenarios, the adventures contained within Cthulhu Classics are reprinted from earlier publications, with the exception of "The Warren" which is new to this collection and a continuation of Shadows of Yog-Sothoth .

Cthulhu Classics was a 152-page softcover with a full-colour cover, black and white interiors, a set of 8 colour plates and various player handouts.

Contents

  • "Dark Carnival" by David A. Hargrave
  • "The Pits of Bendal-Dolum" by Doug Lyons
  • "The Secret of Castronegro" by Mark Pettigrew and Sandy Petersen
  • Shadows of Yog-Sothoth by Mark Hutchison , Sandy Petersen, John Scott Clegg , John Carnahan and Randy McCall
  • "The Temple of the Moon" by Michael Szymanski and Scott David Aniolowski
  • "The Warren" by Ed Gore (bonus scenario for Shadows of Yog-Sothoth )

Publication History

First published in 1989, Cthulhu Classics is now out of print; unlike many of Chaosium's older scenario books, it is not currently available as a PDF download.
