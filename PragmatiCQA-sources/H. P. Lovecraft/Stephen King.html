Stephen King is a world-renowned author, best known for his horror and fantasy work, who has made several contributions to the expanded Cthulhu Mythos . He has stated on multiple occasions that H. P. Lovecraft is one of his greatest influences, possibly due to having spent some of his childhood growing up in Maine where many of his own works are set.
