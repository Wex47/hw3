Robert Olmstead ( HPL : " Notes for The Shadow Over Innsmouth ") is the narrator of " The Shadow Over Innsmouth " and a member of the Marsh Family . It is remarked that his eyes have the same, strange quality seen in descendants of Obed Marsh . In the story's final pages he learns he himself is the great-great-grandson of Obed Marsh and a Deep One and in time will become one of the amphibious monsters he witnessed in Innsmouth .

He first goes to Innsmouth on the way to discover more of his family roots.

Behind the Mythos

His name is never given within the original story.
