Theodore "Eibon" Donald Klein is an American writer and editor.

Overview

Although his fiction output has been relatively low, Klein has garnered praise for the construction in his work, which includes stories such as " The Events at Poroth Farm ". He was, along with others, responsible for the creation of the World Fantasy Convention in 1975, and "The Events at Poroth Farm" was nominated for Best Short Fiction at the first event. Amongst the publications he has edited, the Twilight Zone magazine is perhaps the most notable.

Trivia

  • Klein wrote his honours thesis on H. P. Lovecraft while attending Brown University, New York.
  • He added "Eibon" to his name in part as a nod to the character created by Clark Ashton Smith .
  • He had a hand in encouraging Ramsey Campbell with his earlier work.
