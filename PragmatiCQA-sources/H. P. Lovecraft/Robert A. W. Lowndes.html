Robert A. W. Lowndes (4 September 1916-14 July 1998) was an American editor and author, and a correspondent of H. P. Lovecraft . An avid horror fan, he wrote a number of pieces both set in and inspired by the Cthulhu Mythos , and issue #61 of the Crypt of Cthulhu fanzine was dedicated to his works.
