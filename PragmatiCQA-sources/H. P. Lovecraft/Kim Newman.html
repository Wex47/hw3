Kim James Newman is a prolific British author, editor and film critic whose works fall for the most part into the science fiction, fantasy and horror genres. He has written several full-length novels under the pen name Jack Yeovil, the majority of these as tie-ins for British wargames manufacturer Games Workshop, and frequently collaborates with Stephen Jones in editorial projects.
