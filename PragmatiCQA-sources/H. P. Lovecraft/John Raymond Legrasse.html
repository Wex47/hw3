Inspector John Raymond Legrasse is "a commonplace-looking middle-aged man," and a New Orleans police inspector who led the raid on a Cthulhu cult on November 1, 1907.

Behind the Mythos

  • Horror author C. J. Henderson wrote a series of short stories with Legrasse as their protagonist, which were collected under the title The Tales of Inspector Legrasse .
  • Legrasse also appeared as a friend and confidant of Justin Sabbath in H. P. Lovecraft's Cthulhu: The Whisperer in Darkness graphic novel, written by Mark Ellis.
