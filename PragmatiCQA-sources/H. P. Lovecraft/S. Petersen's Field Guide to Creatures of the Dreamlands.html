S. Petersen's Field Guide to Creatures of the Dreamlands is a sourcebook for Chaosium 's Call of Cthulhu roleplaying game.

Overview

Collecting an assortment of creatures which inhabit the Dreamlands , the Field Guide features full-colour illustrations, size comparisons, monster habitats, behaviours and recognition guides, along with maps of the Dreamlands. It was written by Sandy Petersen with contributions from Lynn Willis and Tom Sullivan , and artwork by Mark J. Ferrari .

It should be noted that, like its companion book S. Petersen's Field Guide to Cthulhu Monsters , Creatures of the Dreamlands contains only flavour text and images; no actual gameplay information is included.

Publication History

S. Petersen's Field Guide to Creatures of the Dreamlands was published in 1989 and is currently out of print. However, Chaosium released a successor of sorts in 2015 with the publication of S. Petersen's Field Guide to Lovecraftian Horrors .
