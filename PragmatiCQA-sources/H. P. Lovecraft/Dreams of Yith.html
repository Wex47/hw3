Dreams of Yith

Original title

Dreams of Yid

Author

  • Duane W. Rimel
  • H.P. Lovecraft (uncredited)

Publication

The Fantasy Fan

Publication date

July 1934
Dreams of Yith , by Duane W. Rimel is a series of sonnets which were much admired by H. P. Lovecraft who did an uncredited revision of the poem. Lovecraft changed the title, noting that the original name "Yid" accidentally was a derogatory name for Jewish people.
