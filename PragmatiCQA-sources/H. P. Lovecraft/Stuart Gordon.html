Stuart Alan Gordon (11 August 1947-24 March 2020) was an American film director, screenwriter, producer and self-confessed fan of H. P. Lovecraft . He filmed a number of works - both for the big screen and television - either based on or influenced by Lovecraft's fiction; Gordon frequently collaborated with screenwriter Dennis Paoli and actors Jeffrey Combs and Barbara Crampton on these productions.
