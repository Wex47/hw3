" The Star-Seed " is a short story by American author James Ambuehl . It takes place against the backdrop of his Braving, Minnesota mythos setting.

Synopsis

A Royceton University professor with an interest in the occult investigates an abandoned mansion in the woodlands near his hometown with an evil reputation. There he comes across a decaying library of dark knowledge, and a basement-dwelling plant-creature which he narrowly escapes.

Learning what he can from the books he took, and consulting others in Royceton's Rare Book room, he deduces this being to be Ei'lor , a Great Old One . Utilising the protection of the Elder Sign and an incantation to summon Yaggdytha , he is successful in ridding the planet of the being, but later comes to the realisation that it was not Ei'lor itself, but merely one of its invasive Star-Seeds.

Publication History

Originally written for the Chaosium collection Made in Goatswood , for which it was rejected, "The Star-Seed" was later published in Cthulhu Codex #7 in May 1996. Ambuehl later rewrote the story to incorporate it into his Braving, Minnesota milieu, although this version appears to have only been published online.

Trivia

  • A book which the narrator references, Tales of Braving and it’s Insidious Environs , is stated as being written by one James Ambrel, a clear nod to the author's own name.
