Kalem Club

General Information

Location

New York, USA

The Kalem Club was an informal group of writers which coalesced around American author Howard Phillips Lovecraft during his stay in New York in the mid- to late-1920s. It took its name from the fact that it's members surnames all began with the letters K, L or M, adopted phonetically to spell "Kalem".

Members

  • George Willard Kirk
  • Rheinhart Kleiner
  • Herman Charles Koenig
  • Frank Belknap Long
  • H. P. Lovecraft
  • Samuel Loveman
  • Henry Everett McNeil
  • James F. Morton
