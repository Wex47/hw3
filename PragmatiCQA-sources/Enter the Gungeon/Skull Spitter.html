Ammonomicon Entry
Hard Headed
Even in death, bullets fly. This gun was crafted by Nuign the Cursed, from the skeleton of a Gundead that had become a Spectre.

Nuign's body still remains somewhere deep in the Hollow, and it is from his bones that all Curse arises.

Skull Spitter is a gun that fires homing, piercing skulls.

Notes

  • Dawn of the Gundead - If the player also has Zombie Bullets , the chance for Skull Spitter ammo to be refunded upon missing is doubled.
  • Jolly Roger - If the player has the VertebraeK-47 , it gets the ability to freeze enemies, while Skull Spitter fires 3 skulls that are linked together.
