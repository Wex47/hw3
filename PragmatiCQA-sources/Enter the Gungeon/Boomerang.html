Boomerang is an active item .

Effects

  • Throws a boomerang that travels around the room and attempts to stun all enemies.

Notes

  • Crave the Glaive - If the player also has the Kruller Glaive , the Glaive will try to hit every enemy in the room before returning, similar to the Boomerang.
  • Gunnerang - If the player also has Utility Belt , thrown guns deal 50% more damage.
  • Get Equipped With -Q- - If the player also has Megahand , enables Quick Boomerang mode, which fires small boomerangs when uncharged that stun enemies. Modes can be switched by reloading the Megahand with a full magazine.
  • Can be useful for finding Wall Mimics .

Trivia

  • This item is a reference to the Magical Boomerang from The Legend of Zelda: A Link to the Past .
  • The Gunnerang synergy is likely a reference to Batman's Batarangs. It could also be a reference to the Gunerang from the Borderlands series.
