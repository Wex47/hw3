Flip Multiplier

Chapter three of the "Tabla Sutra." And in flipping the table, we are bettered. He who knows the table knows himself.

Table Tech Sight is a passive item .

Effects

  • Flipping a table briefly slows down time and triples the player's shots for three seconds.

Notes

  • All Out Of Law - If the player has Colt 1851 , it fires three tight spreads of three bullets upon reloading. These projectiles can ignore tables when fired from behind them, and they can reveal secret rooms without angering the shopkeeper Bello.
  • Hidden Tech Time - If the player has Liquid Valkyrie or Bloody Eye , flipping a table briefly stops time. This only stops enemies currently on the screen, new ones spawning (e.g. by a Blobulon splitting) will not be stopped.
  • Paperwork - If the player has Origuni , the first shot of every Origuni magazine fires three planes.
  • If duplicates of this item are somehow obtained, further multiplies the player's shots.
    • Sometimes flipping tables with duplicates may not activate time slowing down but shots still remain multiplied as usual.
