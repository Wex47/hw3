Flare Gun is a gun that fires flares which have a chance to set enemies on fire .

Notes

  • - If the player also has Medkit , reload time is decreased by 66%, damage is increased by 50%, and random enemies will periodically have explosions erupt around them, damaging them and hurting other enemies around them in a short distance. This explosion can also do damage to the player.
  • Firing With Flair - If the player also has Amulet of the Pit Lord , Master of Unlocking , Copper Ammolet , or Springheel Boots , the Flare Gun turns green and sets green fire, which leaves enemies permanently burning.
  • Solar Flare - If the player also has Mr. Accretion Jr. , the Flare Gun fires comets which will push enemies along its path and explode on impact with a wall.
  • If a flare hits a wall, it will continue burning for a short time, producing light, but will not damage anything.

Trivia

  • The Synergy with Medkit may be a reference to how flare guns and medical supplies are often kept together for emergency situations.
  • The quote “Over Here” may be a reference to the original Jurassic Park movie.
  • This gun has a visual and functional resemblance to one of the secondary weapons for the Pyro in Team Fortress 2.
