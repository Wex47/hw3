Effects

  • Reduces weapon spread by 50%, increasing accuracy.
  • Adds a laser sight that points where the player is aiming.

Notes

  • Future Gangster - If the player has Thompson Sub-Machinegun , it fires lasers.
  • Particle Accelerator - If the player has Particulator , whenever a split Particulator shot dissipates, a laser is automatically fired in its direction.
  • Particle Flow - If the player also has RPG , Stinger , Zorgun , or Laser Lotus , crosshairs will appear on enemies and the guns will home in on them.
  • Peripheral Vision - If the player has Light Gun , a dog occasionally appears and attacks enemies.
  • Pistol Machine - If the player has Machine Pistol , its shots pierce enemies and are connected with electricity.

Trivia

  • The description title is a reference to King of The Dot , the Canadian rap battle league of the same name.
  • The Future Gangster synergy may be a reference to Fallout: New Vegas's Laser RCW .
  • The Peripheral Vision synergy refers to the NES Zapper, which was sold as a peripheral for the NES and served as the model for the Light Gun.
