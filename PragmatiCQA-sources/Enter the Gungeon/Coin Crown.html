A pre-Gungeon relic.

Coin Crown is a passive item in both Enter and Exit the Gungeon .

Effects

  • Adds a chance to gain money upon clearing a room.
    • If Coin Crown triggers, it will spawn a 5 shell on top of the player.

Notes

  • Great Queen Ant - If the player also has Gungeon Ant , it becomes an ant queen and shoots rocket ants.
  • King Bomber - If the player also has Lil' Bomber , its bombs become gold and spawn every time they hit an enemy.
  • Queen of Hearts - If the player also has Heart Purse , when at full health, picking up hearts will turn them to coins.
  • Two Heavy - If the player also has Crown of Guns , the chance for Coin Crown to spawn money is doubled.
  • It is easy to unlock with the Briefcase of Cash . If the player already has 50 then the 250 will immediately unlock it.
  • Obtaining the Gold Junk will automatically unlock it. This is because the Gold Junk grants the player 500 when picked up.
  • If duplicates of this item are somehow obtained, increases the chance of a 5 shell spawning upon clearing a room and allows multiple Coin Crowns to be activated at once.

Trivia

  • This could be a reference to Crypt of the Necrodancer's Crown of Greed.
  • The "Two Heavy" synergy is a nod to the old adage, "Heavy is the head that wears the crown," as in this case, you would be wearing two.
