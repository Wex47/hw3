Ammonomicon Entry
Fires Shotguns
This strange gun, shaped like a shotgun shell, fires bullets that are shaped like shotguns.

Those shotgun-shaped bullets will fire a spray of rounds upon impact, much like a shotgun would.

For the game's currency, see Pickups .

Shell is a gun that fires a spread of 3 shotguns, each of which fires 2 bullets upon hitting an enemy or obstacle.

Notes

  • Blammo! - If the player also has Bullet , the guns are dual-wielded.
  • This gun seems to be in reference to two colloquial sayings that mean relatively the same thing - "Firing a gun" and "Firing a shell". The meanings are swapped, but still technically are true.
