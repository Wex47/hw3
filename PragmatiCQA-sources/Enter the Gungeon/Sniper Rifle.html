Handcrafted by the Rifle Gundead, each Sniper Rifle performs slightly differently. Though the Gungeon rarely offers many opportunities for distance shooting, this gun still rewards a steady hand.

Sniper Rifle is a gun that fires bullets that can pierce through one enemy. Its shot speed is extremely fast, and its shots leave lines of smoke.

Notes

  • 360 Yes Scope - If the player also has Scope , spinning 360 degrees before firing grants a 3-second buff that makes the next shot deal 50% more damage.
    • This can be activated multiple times, providing a stacking 1.5x damage multiplier for each spin. It caps at 6 rotations.
    • This can be activated by multiple Scopes, providing a stacking 1.5x damage multiplier for each Scope. It can even include Lich's Eye Bullets .
    • The buff will persist even if the player switches to another gun.
    • Glass Cannon is extremely effective against bosses with this synergy due to DPS cap penetration.
  • Diazepam - Muscle Relaxant increases this gun's damage by 50%.
  • Sniper Woof - If the player also has Wolf , Sniper Rifle's reload time is decreased by 30%. Standing still for long enough causes the player to glow yellow, making Sniper Rifle shots deal 50% more damage. Sniper Shells and Professionals become permanently charmed.

Trivia

  • Altered: Diazepam - Prior to the Advanced Gungeons & Draguns Update , Diazepam tripled damage instead of increasing it by 50%.
  • The synergy 360 Yes Scope is a reference to the meme spawned by Call of Duty 4: Modern Warfare .
  • The Diazepam and Sniper Woof synergies are referencing Metal Gear Solid's Sniper Wolf character.
  • "Scope Creep" is a reference to a common problem in many projects where the original goals expand while the project is in progress.
  • Sniper Rifle's shots are able to penetrate flipped tables, provided the table is the first thing the shot hits.
