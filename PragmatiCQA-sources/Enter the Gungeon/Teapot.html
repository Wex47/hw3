Teapot is a gun that shoots heart-shaped steam clouds. The last shot of every magazine is pink, and deals extra damage. When reloaded, it whistles and creates a circular area around the player, dealing constant damage and igniting all enemies within the area. Enemies must be fully inside the area to be ignited.

Notes

  • Monsters and Monocles - If the player also has Mustache , the AoE effect range of Teapot is increased.
  • Tea for Two - If the player has almost any familiar (including Ser Junkan , Wolf , Turkey , Clown Mask , Chicken Flute , Baby Good Mimic , Baby Good Shelleton , Pig , R2G2 , Super Space Turtle , Blank Companion's Ring , or Badge ), the familiar will have the AoE effect while reloading.
    • While Dog and Turtle Problem will not activate this synergy, they will gain the AoE effect if another familiar activates it.
  • In co-op, the last shot of each magazine heals the other player for half a heart if shot at them.
