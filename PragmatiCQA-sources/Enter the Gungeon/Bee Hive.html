A beehive is nature's gun! Give it a good shake and let the bullets fly.

Bee Hive is a gun which fires bees that home in on and damage enemies.

Notes

  • Apiary - If the player has Box , Bee Hive turns into an apiary and automatically regenerates ammo over time.
  • BEES - If the player has Honeycomb or Bumbullets , while Bee Hive is held, projectile size is doubled, damage is increased by 25%, and rate of fire is decreased by 34%.
  • Hidden Tech Bees - If the player has Table Tech Blanks , a swarm of bees replaces enemy bullets when a table is flipped.
  • Inconsistent Scale - If the player has Gungeon Ant , while it is held, a beehive orbits the player and spawns bees.
  • If the player feeds both this gun and RPG to a Muncher , it will spit a Stinger in result.
  • Bees will not home in on the weakened remnant of Blobulord .
  • If the player has the BEES synergy and dodge rolls with the Cat Bullet King Throne , the bullets fired by the throne will also be doubled in size.
    • This effect only happens while the player is holding the Bee Hive.
