Irradiated Lead is a passive item and a bullet upgrade .

Effects

  • Adds a chance to fire green bullets that poison enemies, dealing 3 damage per second for 2.5 seconds.
    • The chance of poisoning enemies is 50% for projectile guns, 25% per second for beam weapons and 100% for exploding weapons.

Notes

  • Added Effect - Poison - If the player has Alpha Bullet , the first shot of each magazine is guaranteed to be a poison bullet.
  • Beta Ray - Gamma Ray 's beam grows larger and deals more damage, but no longer travels instantaneously.
  • Emerald Weapon - If the player has Omega Bullets , the last shot of each magazine is guaranteed to be a poison bullet.
  • Natural Selection - If the player also has Evolver , it evolves after every 3 unique enemy types killed instead of 5.
  • Pandemic Pistol - If the player has Plague Pistol , its bullets bounce and are connected with electricity.
  • Every explosive weapon may occasionally leave a small pool of poison goop on the floor.

Trivia

  • Emerald Weapon is a reference to Final Fantasy VII, specifically one of its Super Bosses.
  • Lead is formed as polonium undergoes atomic decay until it achieves almost total non-radioactivity. While lead still retains enough radioactive particles to be detected by geiger counter, lead cannot be made dangerously radioactive again.
