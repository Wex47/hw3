Place to create a zone of infinite ammo.

Often found in gungeon doctors´ offices, this rack displays magazines of all sorts. The clips contained within should prove useful, and plentiful, for even the most inaccurate of Gungeoneers.

Magazine Rack is a rechargeable active item .

Effects

  • Creates a pool of infinite ammo for a short time.

Notes

  • Just Shoot Me - If the player has Camera , this shoots at nearby enemies while active.
  • Junk Mail - If the player has Mailbox , activating the Magazine Rack causes Mailbox to only shoot out packages.
  • Magazine Clip - If the player has Drum Clip , Magazine Rack's radius doubles.
  • Beam weapons will still use ammo while in Magazine Rack's radius.
  • The item can be used in Winchester's game to have more than four shots per game.

Trivia

  • The Just Shoot Me synergy may refer to the sitcom where the characters work at a fashion magazine and photograph models.
  • Magazine Rack appears to be a pun on the magazine of a gun.
  • While holding GuNNER inside the infinite ammo circle, it will not spawn its blue skull.
