Ammonomicon Entry
Pest Control
This ancient tool is theorized to have been used in bizarre pagan rituals involving spiders and flame.

Nail Gun is a gun that fires a rapid stream of low damage nails.

Notes

  • Careful Iteration - If the player has Casey , Casey becomes spiked and fires a spread of 6 nails each time it is swung.
  • Hammer and Nail - If the player also has H4mmer , maximum ammo is increased by 50% for both guns. H4mmer additionally gets 5 hammer shots at the end of every magazine and spins a wood beam around the player when reloading an empty magazine.
  • Nailed It - If the player has Sunglasses , Blast Helmet , +1 Bullets , H4mmer , Cobalt Hammer , or Heavy Bullets , Nail Gun becomes perfectly accurate and fires larger nails that do triple damage.
  • Thorn Bath, ooh! - If the player has Armor of Thorns , Nail Gun's damage is increased by 30%. While Nail Gun is held, thorns are periodically fired in all directions.
  • This gun is among those Gunsling King and Manservantes can challenge the player to clear a room with.

Trivia

  • The Ammonomicon entry is a reference to the 1990s horror film Arachnophobia .
  • This weapon may also be a reference to Home Improvement: Power Tool Pursuit! , as the character in that game can also wield a nail gun, which shares the inaccuracy, fire rate, and base ineffectiveness.
