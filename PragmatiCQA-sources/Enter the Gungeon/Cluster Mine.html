Area Hazard
Creates a cluster of proximity mines.

Cluster Mine is an active item .

Effects

  • Places 5 small mines that explode when an enemy comes into contact with them, dealing 60 damage.

Notes

  • Cerebral Bros - If the player also has Bullet Bore , the gun shoots an extra drill and turns red.
  • Mine Craft - If the player also has Mine Cutter , mines home in on enemies.
  • Can deal explosive damage to the player.
  • Occasional bug: the mines will sometimes not trigger when enemies come in contact with them
  • The mines will go through fake walls, showing secret rooms. This does not show a crack in the wall, so the player must be observant.
  • The mines will sink into the water in the Gorgun's arena, rendering them useless.
