Notes

  • Behold! - If the player also has Eye of the Beholster , M1911 , Void Marshal , Machine Pistol , and Trank Gun (the weapons the Beholster uses in game), when the Eye of the Beholster is held, the other five guns rotate around the player and shoot at nearby enemies, still using up ammo from each gun. Obtainable through the Beholster shrine, although it can be activated without.
  • Commammo Belt - If the player also has Ammo Belt , Com4nd0 gains double magazine size and changes appearance, becoming light green and featuring an ammo belt around it.
  • Heavy Artillery - If the player also has the Vulcan Cannon , the guns are dual wielded.
  • Hunter-Seeker Tables - If the player also has Table Tech Rocket , table rockets will home in on enemies.

Trivia

  • This gun is a reference to Arnold Schwarzenegger's movie performances. "You're fired" is an iconic one-liner from a scene in , although the rocket launcher is seen in another movie,
  • The Beholster can be seen wielding this weapon during its boss fight, complete with identical homing rockets.
  • The mechanized walkers in the Ammonomicon entry likely refers to those seen throughout the Metal Gear series.
  • According to the Ammonomicon, this gun was designed to be attached to a combat vehicle, but is available as a man-portable weapon. It shares this trait with the Raiden Coil and the Void Core Cannon .
