Heart of Ice is a passive item .

Effects

  • Grants a heart container.
  • Taking damage releases bouncing ice bullets that split into three upon impact.
  • Increases coolness by 1.

Notes

  • Arctic Warfare - If the player also has A.W.P. , it turns orange and white, deals 62.5% more damage, and its shots are guaranteed to freeze enemies.
  • Dragunice - If the player also has Dragunfire , it will both freeze and ignite enemies.
  • Frosty Distraction - If the player also has Decoy , the decoy will freeze enemies that attack it.
  • Frostier Distraction - If the player also has Explosive Decoy , the decoy freezes enemies that attack it and explodes after taking enough damage.
  • Ice To Meet You - If the player also has Ice Cube or Frost Bullets , the first bullet from any gun will be accompanied by an ice projectile that splits into two on impact.
  • Jotun Time - If the player also has Frost Giant , thrown guns deal 180% more damage and Frost Giant rapidly chills and freezes nearby enemies while reloading.
  • Willing To Sacrifice - If the player also has Freeze Ray , it gains piercing and slowly regenerates ammo over time.
  • The ice bullets will cause water on the ground to freeze into ice.
  • Despite being able to be found in , and rank chests, it will always have to be bought from a store at rank price.
    • Although it is considered rank, it does not increase magnificence .
  • Like all heart container items, The Robot will receive 5 to 10 instead.
  • This is the only rank item to not also give a key when obtained out of an rank chest.

Trivia

  • The synergy Arctic Warfare is in reference to the AWP Asiimov skin from Counter-Strike: Global Offensive.
  • The synergy Willing To Sacrifice is in reference to the song Cold as Ice by Foreigner.
  • The synergy Jotun Time is in reference to the homeland of the Jötnar, the giants in Norse mythology.
  • Prior to the A Farewell to Arms Update , it could be found in rank chests, and it was treated as an item.
