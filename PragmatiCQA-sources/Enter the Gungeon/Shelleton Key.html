The ultimate tool in the Gungeon Acquisitions' Department's arsenal.

Shelleton Key is a passive item .

Effects

  • Allows any chest or locked door to be opened without using a key .
  • Increases curse by 1 while held.

Notes

  • Akey Breaky - If the player has the AKEY-47 , the AKEY-47 receives a damage boost, infinite ammo, changes in appearance to resemble a Shelleton, and can no longer open locks by shooting at them.
  • Key Witness - If the player has Master of Unlocking , guns will periodically fire an AKEY-47 shot.
  • Shell-A-Ton - If the player has Baby Good Shelleton , Baby Good Shelleton moves and shoots much faster.
  • Can't be used to open the hatch to the Resourceful Rat's Lair or Rat Chests .
  • Although it opens chests and locked doors for free, purchasing from Flynt still costs keys.

Trivia

  • Unused: Heart Unlocket - If the player has Heart Locket , it grants an extra heart container.
  • This item's name is a portmanteau of Shelleton , a demon found in the Gungeon, and Skeleton Key , a type of master key reduced to its essential parts.
  • This item's tagline is a reference to the fact that locks in the Gungeon are sentient, and thus they literally die to the player when the Shelleton Key is used.
