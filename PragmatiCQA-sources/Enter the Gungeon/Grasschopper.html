Ammonomicon Entry
Noisy
This petite weapon packs a punch, and the recoil can knock the user backwards.

Grasschopper is a very tiny gun that fires powerful blasts that have very strong knockback and delete bullets, like a small Blank effect.

Notes

  • To Serve Android - If the player has Badge , the police officer turns into a robot and becomes invincible.
  • The explosions created on impact are not blanks, despite being able to delete bullets. They will not open secret rooms or trigger Ammolets .
  • If a Gunjurer catches and returns a shot from this gun, the blank effect will still damage the player.

Trivia

  • Unused: Galaxy Defender - If the player has Sunglasses , it causes an unknown effect.
  • This weapon is a reference to the Noisy Cricket from Men in Black .
