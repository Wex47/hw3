Ammonomicon Entry
Just A Box
The ultimate in stealth technology. Surprise attacks deal additional damage.

No one is perceptive enough to see through such an elaborate ruse.

Box is an active item .

Effects

  • While wearing the box, enemies will not target the player. Most actions other than moving will remove the box, including firing a weapon, charging a weapon, dodge rolling, flipping a table, and using teleporters.
    • Firing a shot while under the box deals double damage and removes the box.
    • Reloading does not remove the box, even if the player has Hip Holster .
    • Has no effect on entering boss rooms.
  • Box can be used to steal from shops as long as it is activated before entering the shop.

Notes

  • Apiary - If the player has Bee Hive , it turns into an apiary and automatically regenerates ammo over time.
  • Flat Stanley - If the player has Mailbox , the Mailbox's clip size is reduced by 50% and its reload time is reduced by 33%, allowing packages to be fired more often.
  • I need scissors! 61! - If the player also has Trank Gun , Trank Gun changes appearance, its rate of fire is decreased, damage and reload time are increased, and its bullets will inflict slow and stun .
  • Pretty Good - If the player has SAA , coolness is increased by 2.
  • What A Thrill - If the player has Patriot , it has a chance to transmogrify enemies into snakes.
  • Has no effect if used while enemies are already targeting the player.
  • While it has no effect on regular bosses as already stated, you can use this to be hidden for the first phase of the Resourceful Rat ’s boss fight.
  • When firing a shot from Vorpal Gun while using Box, the shot is guaranteed to be a critical hit.

Trivia

  • This item is a reference to the famous box-disguise from Metal Gear .
  • The synergy Pretty Good is a reference to the popular quote from Metal Gear Solid, "You're pretty good".
  • The synergy Flat Stanley is a reference to Flat Stanley , an American children's book series written by Jeff Brown.
  • The synergy What a Thrill! is a reference to the theme song of Metal Gear Solid 3: Snake Eater.
  • The synergy Apiary might be a reference to the character The Pain from Metal Gear Solid 3: Snake Eater, a special operative with the ability to control bees and use them in combat.
  • The synergy I need scissors! 61! is a reference to a quote from Metal Gear Solid 2: Sons of Liberty.
