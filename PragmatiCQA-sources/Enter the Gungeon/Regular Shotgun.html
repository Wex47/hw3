A regular shotgun. There's nothing special about it!

Favored by the Shotgun Kin that roam the Gungeon, as it reminds them of their home.

Regular Shotgun is a gun .

Notes

  • Iron Stance - If the player also has Heavy Boots , the gun's damage and knockback are increased by 20%, its shot speed is increased by 50%, and its reload time is decreased by 20%.
  • Not So Sawed-Off - If the player also has Buzzkill , its saw blades are linked with lightning, the saw blades move slower, and it changes appearance.
  • Regular Old Guns - If the player also has AK-47 , both guns fire rates are quadrupled but their damage is decreased by 25%.
  • The Regular Shotgun is in the SHOTGUN gun class, meaning it benefits from the Shotgun Affinity synergy.

Trivia

  • The Regular Shotgun has an equip animation in which the gungeoneer racks the pump.
  • The Regular Shotgun has nearly identical stats to the Winchester , having a lower fire rate, slightly slower reload, and more spread, but more max ammo.
