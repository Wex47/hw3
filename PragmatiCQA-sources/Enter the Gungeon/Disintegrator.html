Fires a beam that decoheres matter into its constituent particles.

This disintegration ray is outlawed on almost every planet with some form of government, and feared by all beings made primarily of matter. It can be fired legally in only two places: the Gungeon, and the waste disposal facilities of Popolon V.

Notes

  • Weird Science - If the player also has Science Cannon , three Science Cannon beams branch off perpendicular to the Disintegrator's main beam.
    • If the player also has Singularity , shooting an upgraded Disintegrator beam at or near the black hole will cause a massive number of beams to split off in every direction.

Trivia

  • The Disintegrator is a reference to the "RYNO" series of guns from the Ratchet & Clank games, primarily the fourth (in that it fires a gigantic laser) and the fifth (its design).
