Chance Bullets is a passive item and a bullet upgrade .

Effects

  • Upon firing, adds a chance to fire projectiles from the player's other guns. Shots fired this way from other guns do not consume ammo from that gun.

Notes

  • Maximize Spell - If the player also has Flame Hand , Flame Hand becomes green and creates pools of green fire. Green fire does not damage the player, and enemies that touch green fire are permanently aflame.
  • Turret Link - If the player also has Portable Turret , Portable Turret will sometimes fire shots from guns in the player's inventory.
  • This item has no effect if the player only has one gun.
  • This item does not work when wielding a weapon with infinite ammo, and other guns using this effect will not shoot bullets from infinite-ammo weapons.
  • This item can occasionally shoot Makeshift Cannon 's bullets with other guns, but the chance is smaller than with other guns.
  • This item will not cause beam guns to fire other projectiles.
  • If a beam gun's beam is fired from another gun, it will be fired for about a second in the direction the player was initially aiming, and will not move to follow the crosshair.
  • This item also works on the Prize Pistol .
    • Other shots fired from the gun do not break the targets.
    • It is recommended to drop the item before playing Winchester's game, as items cannot be dropped while playing.
