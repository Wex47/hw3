Drum Clip is a passive item .

Effects

  • Increases the magazine size of all weapons by 50%.
    • Does not affect guns with only one round in each magazine, as numbers are rounded down.
    • In Exit The Gungeon, this effect is removed and single-round guns do gain an extra bullet.

Notes

  • Alternative Rock - If the player has Face Melter , instead of firing four streams, Face Melter fires two parallel columns of notes. Reloading fires a guitar pick with a chance to stun.
  • Dr Worm - If the player has Heck Blaster , its ammo automatically replenishes slowly.
  • - If the player has Hegemony Rifle , it fires piercing lasers.
  • Keeping The Beat - If the player has Metronome , it grants +4% damage per kill instead of +2%, and maxes out at +250% damage instead of +150%.
  • M1 Multi-Tool - If the player has M1 , decreases M1's reload time by 20% and increases its magazine size, and maximum ammo. Increases movement speed while M1 is held.
  • Not Quite As Mini - If the player has Vulcan Cannon , its maximum ammo is increased by 100 and its fire rate greatly increases.
  • Magazine Clips - If the player has Magazine Rack , Magazine Rack's radius roughly doubles.
  • Drum Clip can negatively affect weapons that have special effects on the final shot, such as Eye of the Beholster .
  • If duplicates of this item are somehow obtained, further increases guns' magazine size. Doesn't affect guns with one round.
