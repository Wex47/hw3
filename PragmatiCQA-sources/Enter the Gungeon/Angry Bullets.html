Ammonomicon Entry
Hungry For More
Hitting an enemy has a chance to refire the projectile at a nearby enemy.

These bullets are imbued with the white-hot rage of Kaliber herself.

Angry Bullets is a passive item and a bullet upgrade .

Effects

  • Bullets that hit enemies will bounce off them into a random direction.
    • Reflected bullets can trigger the same effect.
    • Reflected bullets deal half of the original bullet's damage.
    • Piercing weapons will always reflect at nearby enemies, or randomly if no enemies are nearby.

Notes

  • Needless Acrimony - If the player also has Shotgun Full of Hate , rage duration is greatly increased.
  • Rabid - If the player has Lower Case r , the words LOSER, DORK, CHUMP, NOODLE, and TAFFER are spelled out.
  • Beam guns will be deflected at a random angle upon hitting an enemy.
  • Most explosive weapons will not explode upon contact with an enemy. Because of this, it is advisable to drop Angry Bullets during a fight if you intend on using explosive weapons during that fight.
    • An exception to this is Void Core Cannon , whose projectiles will still explode even with Angry Bullets.
  • Angry Bullets work well with the Railgun , allowing it to easily clear rooms.
  • Angry Bullets also work well with Cat Claw , allowing shots to strike multiple targets due to the homing nature of the rounds; even striking the same target multiple times in some instances.
  • Magic Lamp can hit large bosses multiple times, allowing for the genie effect to trigger much more often.
  • Quad Laser 's projectile moves extremely slowly, allowing it to strike enemies multiple times, and creating a near stationary barrier that enemies can be lured into.
