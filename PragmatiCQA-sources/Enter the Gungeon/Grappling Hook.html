This is a kind of grappling hook that can be shot. Known to some as the Shoothook.

Grappling Hook is an active item .

Effects

  • Allows players to hook onto and then pull themselves towards walls at a fast speed.
    • The hook has unlimited range.
    • Hitting an enemy with the hook stuns it and deals 10 damage.
    • Hitting an item with the hook pulls it towards the player.
    • The player is invulnerable when traveling with the grappling hook, and will travel over pits and hazards.
    • This item allows players to steal items from the shop .

Notes

  • Black Flag - If the player also has Corsair , each Corsair shot will periodically fire two bouncing cannonballs perpendicular to itself as it travels.
  • Raizo's Stuff - If the player also has Bloodied Scarf , bullets shot while being pulled with the grappling hook gain homing.
  • If the player cannot reach the hook's location (e.g. table blocking the way), the hook will automatically deactivate after a few seconds.
  • The Grappling Hook can be used to cheat in Winchester's Games.
  • Using Grappling Hook to steal items while inside the shop will sometimes cause the player to get caught, even on the first theft.
  • The Grappling Hook is not able to steal the Prime Primer or the Gnawed Key .
  • The Grappling Hook can be used to easily get the Planar Lead .
  • Firing Grappling Hook at Baby Good Mimic will stun it and cause it to attack.

Bugs

  • If Grappling Hook is dropped mid-use, the player will become invincible but unable to move or dodge roll.
    • Opening the map will restore the ability to move, while retaining invincibility and inability to dodge roll but allows "flying" over pits and other hazards.
    • The invincibility persists until the player uses Grappling Hook again, picks up any health or shields, teleports, or enters a new floor.
    • The inability to dodge roll persists until the player uses Grappling Hook again.
    • If playing Co-op, the second player does not gain invincibility, but still can fly.

Trivia

  • This item is a reference to the Hookshot, an item from the The Legend of Zelda franchise.
  • Interestingly enough, the sprites related to this item refer to it as "scorpion_hook", in reference to Scorpion of the Mortal Kombat series, who uses a hook on a metal chain to pull enemies closer to himself.
  • Black Flag is a possible reference to the game Assassin's Creed IV: Black Flag .
