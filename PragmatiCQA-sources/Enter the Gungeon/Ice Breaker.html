The Ice Breaker was designed by Edwin, the legendary gunsmith. The only gun to combine freezing and shattering technology into one convenient package!

Ice Breaker is a gun . The first two shots of each magazine fire a shotgun blast of ice bullets, and the final shot fires a single, piercing, explosive shot.

Notes

  • Gunderlord - If the player also has Patriot , Ice Breaker fires single high-velocity shots and slowly regenerates ammo over time. Patriot shots become connected with electricity.
  • Ice Accumulation - If the player has Snowballets , Ice Breaker's shot size is greatly increased, and the amount of damage it gains from Snowballets is increased.
  • It's Your Destiny - If the player has The Exotic , Ice Breaker fires single high-velocity shots and slowly regenerates ammo over time. The Exotic's rockets split into 14 smaller rockets instead of 7.
  • Despite firing like a shotgun, the Ice Breaker is not in the SHOTGUN gun class , and as such does not benefit from the Shotgun Affinity synergy.
  • Prior to the Supply Drop Update , Ice Breaker had 100 maximum ammo.
  • The bullets of this gun will freeze any water on the ground.
  • If the magazine size of this gun is increased, only the number of ice shots per magazine will be increased; only the final shot will be the explosive shot.
  • Ice Breaker's large metal slug can pierce 5 enemies before breaking on the 6th; this includes the synergies besides Ice Accumulation .
  • When using Ice Breaker with its Gunderlord or It's Your Destiny synergy, the metal slug flies faster than the initial two freezing shots.
  • Damage from the metal slug is uniform even if the enemy is frozen.

Trivia

  • "Never Let Go" references the final scene in the film Titanic.
  • This is also a reference to Destiny's IceBreaker indicated by its color and synergy with The Exotic . Its synergies with Patriot and The Exotic change its appearance to match the Destiny gun.
    • The Gunderlord synergy, which connects Patriot shots with electricity, is a reference to the Destiny gun Thunderlord.
