Lousy T-Shirt
This souped-up T-Shirt Cannon fires tainted apparel.

Enemies defeated after contracting the pox will leave a pool of deadly ooze behind.

Poxcannon is a gun that fires poisonous t-shirts that have significant knockback. Enemies poisoned by this gun leave behind a pool of poison creep upon death.

Synergies

  • The Return - If the player has Pig , it turns green and fires flaming bats.
  • Well Dressed - If the player also has T-Shirt Cannon , both guns fire explosive wardrobes at the end of their magazines.

Trivia

  • Despite the Poxcannon poisoning enemies, the gun is not a member of the POISON gun class .
  • This weapon may be a reference to a practice of American colonists of infecting American Indians with blankets infected with smallpox.
  • This weapon may be a reference to the 'Pax Pox', a blanket term for diseases contracted at conventions.
  • The tagline refers to the merchandising meme "I [achieved something] and all I got was this lousy t-shirt".
    • The term "Lousy" in this case may also refer to Lice, which are parasitic insects.
