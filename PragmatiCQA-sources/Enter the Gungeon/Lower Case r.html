Ammonomicon Entry
Alphabetical!
The letter r looks like a gun.

It is rare, but not unheard of, for abstract concepts to take gun form when discussed near the Gungeon.

Lower Case r is a gun that fires a burst of 6 bullets that spell out the word BULLET. While firing, the gun will voice out the word "bullet".

Notes

  • Hacker - If the player also has Bracket Key , damage is increased by 25%.
  • Just In Case - If the player has Bomb , the gun becomes a capital letter R and transforms into a one shot rocket launcher. It fires the word ROCKET with cloud sprites when shot, and creates a cartoony BOOM cloud when it hits something.
  • Rabid - If the player has Angry Bullets , the words LOSER, DORK, CHUMP, NOODLE, and TAFFER are spelled out.
  • Radiant - If the player has Silver Bullets , the words SILVER, BLESSED, and WEREWOLF are spelled out. When it shoots "WEREWOLF", the firing sound is "DIE WEREWOLF DIE".
  • Ravishing - If the player has Charming Rounds , the words HANDSOME, CHARM, and CUTE are spelled out, and shots become charming.
  • Reactive - If the player has Explosive Rounds , the words ROCKET, BOOM, KABOOM, and POW are spelled out, and shots become explosive.
  • Reanimate - If the player has Ghost Bullets , the words SPOOKY, GHOST, and BOO are spelled out.
  • Reinforced - If the player has +1 Bullets , BULLET+1 is spelled out.
  • Remnant - If the player has Omega Bullets , an omega appears after BULLET.
  • Replete - If the player also has Finished Gun , the words COWABUNGA, BOOYAKASHA, THANK YOU, THANKS, HASTA LA VISTA, TOODLES, GOODBYE, FAREWELL and GAME OVER are spelled out.
  • Research - If the player has Book of Chest Anatomy , the words CHEST and MIMIC are spelled out, and the gun can unlock locked chests and doors.
  • Resplendent - If the player has Gilded Bullets , the words CASH, COINS, SHELLS, and GOLD are spelled out.
  • Root - If the player has Alpha Bullets , an alpha appears before BULLET.
  • Runic - If the player has Magic Bullets , the words ABRA and KADABRA are spelled out, and the words turn dark purple.
  • All synergies that affect what letters Lower Case r fires increase the base damage per letter from 2.2 to 5, and causes the gun to voice out whatever word was just fired.

Trivia

  • The voice that says the words shot by the gun out loud belongs to Doseone , the game's composer.
  • The reload animation consists of deleting the letter, through use of backspace, and typing a new one.
  • Lower Case r's bullets leave behind the word BLAM!! whenever they hit something.
  • This could be a reference to old ASCII Adventure games, where letters would commonly be used to represent items, pickups and weapons based on their visual similarities.
  • The pickup quote "Alphabetical!" could be a reference to Finn from Adventure Time exclaiming "Mathematical!"
  • The word "TAFFER" which can be fired with Angry Bullets is a reference to the same word used as an insult by guards in Thief: The Dark Project .
