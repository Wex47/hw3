Ammonomicon Entry
Electric Terror
Fires linked projectiles.

This gun fires powerful electron bursts that imitate ancient spells of chained lightning.

The Emperor is a gun that fires a burst of four big green bullets connected by electricity, identical to the effect of Shock Rounds .

Notes

  • Starburst Fire - If the player also has Laser Rifle , firing either The Emperor or Laser Rifle once will cause it to rapidly fire its entire magazine. Increases damage by 20% and doubles fire rate.
    • You can cancel the effect by reloading or dodge-rolling while firing.

Trivia

  • Unused: The Star War - If the player has Void Marshal , it causes an unknown effect.
  • This gun is a reference to Emperor Palpatine from Star Wars .
    • This is further reinforced by the name of the above unused synergy, and the fact that the Void Marshal is also a reference to Star Wars.
  • This gun's bullets and firing mechanism are a reference to Emperor Zurg from Toy Story.
