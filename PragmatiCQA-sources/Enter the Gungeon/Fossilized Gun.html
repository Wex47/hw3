Ammonomicon Entry
Proof That Guns Once Existed
Reloading this skull produces a gout of flame that can ignite oil.

This is a spectacularly preserved specimen of a gun that went extinct during the Ballistac era.

Fossilized Gun is a gun that fires a stream of oil. Reloading causes the skull to spit fire, which can ignite the oil.

Notes

  • Cryptic Cryptids - If the player also has Phoenix , both guns gain piercing and deal more damage, and Phoenix shoots rows of fire on the ground. The player is also immune to fire when either gun is equipped.
  • Dawn of the Gundead - Doubles the chance for Zombie Bullets to refund missed Fossilized Gun shots.
  • Reloading will only ignite the oil; it will neither damage enemies nor explode barrels, and will not anger Bello . It can be reloaded even with no ammo left, but this is mostly useless.
  • With Bouncy Bullets , the oil stream will bounce and cover the whole room with oil, and can set the whole room on fire.
    • Oil streams that bounce back at the player will still leave an oil-free safe zone at the player's position.

Tips

  • If the player is immune to fire goop, a useful tactic when entering rooms is to move just close enough to the door to open it, then fire a spray of oil through the door and ignite that, then enter the room. This provides an instant ignition source when attacking the rest of the room.
  • Even without immunity to fire, it is possible to survive indefinitely in a flaming area by dodge-rolling continuously.
  • By clicking once and quickly reloading, it is possible to not consume any ammo.
  • This weapon is basically a forest fire starting kit, so it's important to be careful.

Trivia

  • This gun's mechanics and appearance are a reference to the conceit that petroleum comes from dead dinosaurs, as well as to fire-breathing dragons.
