Gungeon Pepper is a passive item .

Effects

  • Continuously deals damage to nearby enemies.

Notes

  • Maximize Spell - If the player has Flame Hand , it turns green, and green flames will leave pools of green fire upon hitting enemies. Green fire does not damage or burn the player, and sets enemies permanently on fire.
  • Sausage and Pepper -  If the player has Meatbun , Gungeon Pepper's range will be doubled. The synergy is lost when Meatbun is consumed.
  • Phoenix Up - If the player has Phoenix , changes the look of the gun and summons a Phoenix companion that fires at enemies.
  • Does not affect some enemies such as Kill Pillars .
  • Despite being depicted on Table Tech Heat , it does not synergize with it.
  • Owning duplicates of this item will deal more damage to nearby enemies.
