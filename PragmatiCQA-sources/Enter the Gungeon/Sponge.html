Effects

  • Removes all liquids/webbing near the player.
  • Allows the player to understand Professor Goopton (not an actual game-changing mechanic).

Notes

  • Soft Air - If the player also has JK-47 , when holding the JK-47, the player regains ammo when walking over liquids, and increases fear range while reloading.
  • Spengbab - If the player also has Tangler , its knockback and range are increased by 50%.
  • Sponge cannot be sold.
  • This item nullifies the effect of Bug Boots .
  • This item nullifies the ammo recovering ability of guns that require you to stand on liquid, such as the Plunger and the Gungine .
