Ammonomicon Entry
You Only Get One Shot
A makeshift weapon filled with gunpowder and shrapnel. Immensely powerful, but it can only be fired once.

Makeshift Cannon is a gun that fires a blast of 6 colored balls that deal extremely high damage.

Notes

  • The Return - If the player also has Pig , the Pig turns green to resemble Cannon , and shoots bat projectiles at nearby enemies.
  • The Makeshift Cannon is one of the few guns able to ignore the Boss DPS cap . Because it only has one shot, it is recommended to only use it during boss fights.
  • When the shots hit enemies and get destroyed they make an explosion, but it takes no damage.
  • Holey Grail can refill Makeshift Cannon's ammo upon taking damage.
  • Having Magazine Rack will allow you to create a loop. Using the item will make this gun have infinite bullets, allowing you to destroy bosses easily, while at the same time, recharging Magazine Rack almost instantaneously.
  • Duct Taping this to other weapons can immensely increase its ammo count.
    • Plunger is extremely effective when Duct Taped to Makeshift Cannon as it can instantly refill its ammo when standing on goop.
  • Relodestone can refill the Makeshift Cannon right after firing it, making it possible to fire multiple Makeshift Cannon shots in rapid succession.
  • Ring of Triggers 's effect can shoot Makeshift Cannon's bullets in all directions, dealing high damage without consuming its ammo.
    • Active items could also be recharged immediately due to the high damage.
  • An Ammo Spread will only restore its shot if picked up while being held, since the 50% replenishment rounds up to 1 and the 20% replenishment rounds down, to 0.
  • Turkey 's effect applies even if the player switches weapons between shots. This can be used to endlessly restore ammo for the Makeshift Cannon.
  • Because of Makeshift Cannon's piercing properties, which effectively guarantees its projectiles to always be destroyed upon hitting a wall, Orbital Bullets can significantly increase Makeshift Cannon's damage output, however this will also be very loud.

Trivia

  • The weapon's appearance and function is a reference to the Star Trek episode Arena , in which Kirk makes such a weapon out of bamboo, hand-mixed black powder, diamonds, and vines.
  • The gun's pickup quote, "You Only Get One Shot", may be a reference to a line from the song, "Lose Yourself" by Eminem.
  • The weapon takes on a special broken appearance after being fired, which is restored after its ammo is refilled.
