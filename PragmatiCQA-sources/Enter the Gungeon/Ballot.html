Effects

  • Increases Coolness by 3, which decreases the cooldown of active items and increases the chance of items dropping upon clearing a room.

Notes

  • Paperwork - If the player also has Origuni , the first round of each magazine fires three airplanes instead of one.
  • Revolution - If the player also has Dueling Pistol , the Dueling Pistol's bullets will deal 25% more damage and bounce five times instead of twice.

Trivia

  • Unused: College Ball - If the player has Nanomachines , it causes an unknown effect. This is a reference to the video game " Metal Gear Rising: Revengeance" , quoting the character Senator Armstrong.
  • This item being in a gun-themed game is likely a reference to the Abraham Lincoln quote "The ballot is stronger than the bullet."
