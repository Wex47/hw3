Bananas resemble guns, and are high in potassium. A convenient snack and/or weapon for Gungeoneers.

Banana is a gun that fires an explosive banana that sends out three more bouncing explosive bananas.

Notes

  • Hornucopia - If the player has Charm Horn , the explosive banana will send out five bouncing explosive bananas instead of three, one of which explodes into 4 additional exploding bananas. Additionally, using Charm Horn fires a banana at each enemy it charms. Banana must be equipped to get this effect.

Trivia

  • Unused: Fruits & Vegetables - If the player has Broccoli , it causes an unknown effect.
  • This gun likely is a reference to the game Gorillas , itself referenced with a weapon of the same name from Worms .
  • The pickup quote is a reference to the plantain , a banana cultivar subgroup.
