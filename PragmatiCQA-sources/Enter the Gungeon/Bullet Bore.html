The Bullet Bore is an exotic weapon from the past, when the only creatures to carry guns were dinosaurs.

Bullet Bore is a gun that fires drills which home in on enemies, stun them, and explode after a short delay.

Notes

  • Cerebral Bros - If the player also has Cluster Mine , Bow , Charmed Bow , or Explosive Rounds , Bullet Bore turns red and fires two drills at a time.
  • Get Equipped With -C- - If the player has Megahand , enables Crash Bomber mode on Megahand, which fires Bullet Bore shots when fully charged. Modes can be switched by reloading the Megahand with a full magazine.
  • This gun can hit Cannonbalrog and High Priest during attacks where not normally possible.
  • Helix Bullets will cause the bullets to only shoot and have piercing abilities, without the explosion and stunning effect.
  • The drills fired are able to kill the mines spawned by the Mine Flayer .

Trivia

  • This gun is a reference to the Cerebral Bore from Turok 2: Seeds of Evil .
    • This is further shown by the synergy Cerebral Bros which is a slightly altered version of the Cerebral Bore's name.
