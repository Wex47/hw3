A legendary weapon manufactured by Zilla Industries. This one was created bespoke for a powerful warrior, whose name has been lost to shadow.

Yari Launcher is a gun that rapidly fires homing rockets.

Notes

  • Hail To The King - If the player also has Oiled Cylinder , Yari Launcher's magazine size is increased by 50% and its damage is increased by 20%.
  • Shadow Warrior - If the player also has Zilla Shotgun , when either gun is held, the player gains an orbiting Demon Head that fires on empty magazine reload. Reduces the guns' spread by 60%, increasing their accuracy.
  • Prior to the Supply Drop Update , the Yari Launcher had 80 max ammo.
  • The Yari Launcher is one of the few guns able to break the Boss DPS cap .

Trivia

  • The gun is a reference to the rocket launcher in the 2013 reboot of Shadow Warrior .
    • The orbiting Demon Head gained through the Shadow Warrior synergy is likely a reference to one of the game's weapons, a demon head that shoots a laser out of its mouth.
  • If the Yari Launcher is duct-taped to another gun while Hail To The King - is active, the 50% magazine size increase is applied to the new magazine size.
  • The synergy Hail To The King is likely a reference to Duke Nukem 3D, whose protagonist would utter the catch phrase when equipping a rapid fire rocket launcher of similar style featured in the game.
    • This, in turn, is a reference to Army of Darkness, wherein the protagonist Ash Williams utilizes the same wisecrack.
