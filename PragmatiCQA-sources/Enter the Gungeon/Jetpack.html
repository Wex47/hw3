Ammonomicon Entry
To Fly
Grants flight, but restricts the dodge roll.

Only a Gungeoneer with a deathwish would give up their dodge roll. Luckily, this jetpack can easily be toggled on or off.

Jetpack is an active item .

Effects

  • Can be toggled. While active, the player's movement speed is increased by 2.5 and the player gains flight, but cannot dodge roll .
    • Passing over puddles of oil or webs while flying will set them on fire.

Notes

  • Barrage Shot - If the player also has Charge Shot , charge time is reduced by 25%, reload time is increased by 50%, and shot size is increased.
  • MM6 Mini Rocket - If the player has Backpack , activating the Jetpack fires a rocket in the direction of the crosshair. This has a cooldown of about 5 seconds.
  • In combination with another item that grants flight such as Wax Wings or the Balloon Gun , the player receives the speed increase of the Jetpack when active, but retains the ability to dodge roll.
  • If Jetpack is active when entering the Resourceful Rat's Lair , the loading screen will be the default loading screen instead of the rat variant.
