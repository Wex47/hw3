Hot Lead is a passive item and a bullet upgrade .

Effects

  • Adds a chance to fire red bullets that ignite enemies.
    • The ignition chance is 20% for projectile weapons, and 25% per second for beam weapons.

Notes

  • Added Effect - Fire - If the player also has the Alpha Bullets , the first bullet of a weapon's magazine is guaranteed to be a fire bullet.
    • Get Equipped with -H- - If the player also has the Megahand , enables Atomic Fire mode on the Megahand.
  • Hidden Tech Flare - If the player also has Table Tech Heat , the radius of effect of heat from flipping tables triples (the area of effect is 9× as large).
  • Hot Ones - If the player has Copper Ammolet , the chance to shoot fire bullets is significantly increased.
  • Phoenix Up - If the player also has the Phoenix , a phoenix companion that fires at enemies is summoned.
  • Plasma Beam - If the player also has the Heroine , enables the Plasma Beam mode on the Heroine.
  • Ruby Weapon - If the player also has the Omega Bullets , the last bullet of a weapon's magazine is guaranteed to be a fire bullet.
  • - If the player also has the Molotov Launcher , Molotovs create green pools of fire, which do not harm the player and permanently light enemies on fire.
  • Every explosive weapon may occasionally leave a pool of fire goop on the floor.
  • Any weapon that would leave an icy patch will leave a burning patch instead.

Trivia

  • This item may be a reference to the phrase "Eat hot lead!", which is frequently said by characters in shooter games and action movies.
  • The Get Equipped with -H- -synergy is a reference to Mega Man II - the Atomic Fire weapon is gained from defeating Heat Man, and represented on the weapon select screen with an H
