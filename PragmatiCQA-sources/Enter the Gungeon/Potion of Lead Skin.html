Potion of Lead Skin is an active item .

Effects

  • While active, the player turns gray, becoming invincible and reflecting bullets back at enemies.
    • Reflected bullets deal 10 damage.

Notes

  • Five O'Clock Somewhere - If the player also has Crestfaller , reloading it will fire a glass bottle that has a chance to stun enemies hit. Reloading Crestfaller while standing on a liquid replenishes the ammo missing in the current magazine, similar to Gungine .
  • Terminated, Too - If the player has Grenade Launcher , the grenades fire 8 bullets in all directions upon exploding that can light enemies on fire.
  • While active, the item does not prevent damage from falling into pits.

Trivia

  • This item is a reference to the classic Potion of Stoneskin from Dungeons and Dragons.
  • Both the tagline and the item's effect could be references to Metal Gear Rising's Sundowner, a boss who employs a shield to deflect the player's attacks while boasting of his invincibility.
  • The tagline could also be a reference to the James Bond movie Goldeneye, in which computer technician Boris Grishenko repeatedly yells,
  • The synergy "Terminated, Too" likely references the movie Terminator 2: Judgment Day .
