Ammonomicon Entry
Rolling Reload
These handcrafted shells are extremely easy to handle. So easy, in fact, that a single shell can be reloaded during a standard dodge roll.

Reversable bullets crafted by Edwin. In his old age, he grew tired of squinting to see which end of the bullet was up.

Easy Reload Bullets is a passive item and a bullet upgrade .

Effects

  • Dodge rolling reloads a single bullet.

Notes

  • Reload Roll - If the player has Magnum , M1911 , Dungeon Eagle , , Colt 1851 , Makarov , or SAA , dodge rolling reloads six bullets, and dodge roll distance is increased by 20% when the gun is held.
  • This item can be used to repeatedly reload and fire the special last shot of guns like H4mmer .
  • If duplicates of this item are somehow obtained, increases the amount of bullets reloaded by dodge rolling.

Trivia

  • Unused: Easy Enough - If the player has Dart Gun , it causes an unknown effect. Removed because it contains a starting weapon.
