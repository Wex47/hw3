Notes

  • Future Gangster - If the player has Bionic Leg , Laser Sight , Shock Rounds , or Nanomachines , the Thompson Sub-Machinegun fires lasers. Each laser does 6.5 damage instead of the normal 5 damage.

Trivia

  • "Myeah, See!" references a line spoken by Edward G. Robinson's gangster character in the 1931 film Little Caesar .
  • This gun is used in The Convict's past.
  • The Future Gangster synergy is possibly a reference to the Laser RCW, a weapon from Fallout: New Vegas, which resembles a Thompson SMG but is a rapid-fire laser assault rifle.
