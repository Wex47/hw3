This ancient technique causes tables to fire off like rockets when flipped.

The final chapter of the "Tabla Sutra." The pages are badly scorched, and barely legible.

Table Tech Rocket is a passive item .

Effects

  • Flipping a table causes it to launch forward and explode.

Notes

  • Hunter-Seeker Tables - If the player has Com4nd0 , table rockets home in on enemies.
  • Paperwork - If the player has Origuni , the first shot of each magazine will fire three planes.
  • If the player has any items that increase bullet size, flipped tables will be enlarged.
  • If the player has Bouncy Bullets , flipped tables will bounce.
  • Prior to , the explosions could hurt the player.
