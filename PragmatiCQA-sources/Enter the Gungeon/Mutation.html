Mutation is a gun that fires a slightly homing continuous beam with heavy recoil, leaving a trail of red liquid . Mutation also turns enemies red, though this is a purely cosmetic effect.

Notes

  • Contrail - If the player has Alien Engine , its flame will turn green, igniting enemies with permanent green fire . Alien Engine also grants flight while held.
  • Neo Tech, Yo - If the player has Bionic Leg or Shock Rounds , Mutation's beam turns blue, leaves puddles of electrified water, and its ammo capacity is increased to 800. The player is immune to electricity while Mutation is held.
  • Venom Veins - If the player has Monster Blood , Bloody Eye , Bloody 9mm , Bloodied Scarf , or Blood Brooch , Mutation poisons enemies.

Trivia

  • The synergy between the Mutation and Bionic Leg/Shock Rounds may be a reference to Tetsuo from the 1982 manga series Akira . Tetsuo similarly possesses a bionic arm, and famously undergoes extreme and disturbing mutations. The synergy's name, "Neo Tech, Yo" also seems to allude to Neotokyo, the setting where the story takes place.
  • This gun may be a reference to the game Prototype , where the protagonist's mutated arm attacks in a similar fashion.
  • "Didn't Need Two Anyway" refers to the look of the weapon in-game, which envelops the player's whole arm.
    • This visual effect is also shared with Robot's Right Hand , Robot's Left Hand , Heroine , Flame Hand and Megahand .
