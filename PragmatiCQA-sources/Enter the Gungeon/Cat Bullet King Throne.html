A cat throne.
Shoots miniature bullets on Dodge Roll.

This throne was originally intended to be included with the physical edition. Due to a manufacturing defect, the scale is way off, and only suitable for a cat. It was eventually tossed into the Gungeon.

Cat Bullet King Throne is a passive item .

Effects

  • Grants flight.
  • Shoots bullets around the player upon dodge rolling. This has a 2-second cooldown.
    • The bullets benefit from all bullet modifiers.

Notes

  • Mad Cats - If the player is holding 3rd Party Controller the bullets shot from Cat Bullet King Throne home in on the enemy.
  • Usurper - If the player has Crown of Guns , the Crown appears to fire more rapidly and also makes the Crown automatically charm Bullet Kin and Veteran Bullet Kin when held.
  • These bullets are affected by Orbital Bullets.
  • These bullets can be controlled by Remote Bullets , which can allow for great amounts of damage.
  • If the player also has Jetpack , they can dodge roll even while Jetpack is active.
  • The chance for bullet modifiers to activate is dependent on the gun held.
  • When in the Aimless Void, the player retains the ability to shoot bullets by rolling but loses the ability to fly.
  • Due to it giving flight, the player cannot pet Dog while it is equipped.
  • The bullets can reveal secret rooms.
    • Bello ignores the Cat Bullet King Throne firing, even if a bullet makes contact with him, making it useful for finding secret rooms.
  • The item makes your sprite a few pixels higher than your shadow, and this effect stacks when you have multiple Cat Bullet King Thrones.
    • This effect is merely cosmetic however and does not benefit the player in any way.
  • You can double the bullets shot by dodge-rolling if you have 2 Cat Bullet King Thrones and so on.

Bugs

  • While having Blank Bullets and holding BSG in hand, all bullets sprayed when rolling cause a blank.
  • If you're holding another item that grants flight, and you drop the second item, the cat bullet king's throne will not grant flight until dropped and picked up again.
