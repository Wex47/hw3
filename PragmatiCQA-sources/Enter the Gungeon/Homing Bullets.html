Fire And Forget
First invented by the Grey Mauser to cheat at one of Winchester's games. Though Winchester eventually caught on and rebuilt his targets to appear friendly, these bullets are still exceptionally useful.

Homing Bullets is a passive item and a bullet upgrade .

Effects

  • Bullets have a 20% chance to home in on enemies.
    • This chance increases for weapons that have a slower fire rate: Weapons like the A.W.P. and the Black Hole Gun are guaranteed to trigger Homing Bullets.

Notes

  • Dumb Smart Bullets - If the player has Scattershot , bullets unaffected by Homing Bullets become larger and deal 50% more damage.
  • Smart Bombs - If the player also has Bomb or Ice Bomb , they will home in on targets and blow up slower.
  • This item grants stronger homing than Crutch , but does not apply to all bullets.
