Space Friend is a passive item .

Effects

  • Follows the player and fires lasers at enemies which deal 5 damage.
  • Blocks enemy bullets.

Notes

  • RAIDEN - If the player also has Raiden Coil , its maximum ammo is increased by 25%, it will fire pairs of bullets alongside its laser, and its ammo consumption is significantly reduced.
  • Space Best Friend - If the player also has Alien Engine , Space Friend will turn blue and fire its lasers at a much faster rate.
  • If positioned carefully, the Space Friend will almost entirely protect you from Wallmonger 's attacks.
  • Space Friend is affected by Magic Bullets .
  • If the player has Helix Bullets , Space Friend's bullets travel in a zigzag pattern, but its shots will not be doubled and its damage is unaffected.
  • Space Friend's laser can be captured by Gunjurers .
  • Once you enter the Aimless Void , Space Friend will fly in from off-screen and begin following you again. This is due to the Aimless Void being a separate room in the forge, to the right of the rest of the map.
  • If duplicates of this item are somehow obtained, grants another Space Friend ally to the player.
    • The Space Friends follow behind each other in a single file fashion, similar to duplicated Owls .

Trivia

  • This passive's appearance references the player ship in the arcade shoot 'em up series Raiden , specifically that of player 1; the Space Best Friend synergy changes it to the second player's color scheme instead.
