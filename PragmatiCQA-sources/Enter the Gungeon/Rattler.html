Ammonomicon Entry
Snakes On A Gun
The Rattler was one of Emmitt Calx's worst, and last, ideas. This is a Winchester shotgun with an extremely venomous snake wrapped around it, adding a poisonous blast to each volley. Requires extreme dexterity to reload.

Rattler is a gun that simultaneously fires a shotgun blast and a beam that has a chance to poison enemies.

Notes

  • Not So Sawed-Off - If the player also has Buzzkill , Buzzkill's saw blades move slower and are linked with lightning.
  • Twin Snakes - If the player also has Snakemaker , the Rattler's shots become beams that have a chance to poison enemies and the Snakemaker's shots gain a homing effect.
  • Despite firing like a shotgun, the Rattler is not a part of the SHOTGUN gun class and as such does not benefit from the Shotgun Affinity synergy

Trivia

  • The pickup line, "Snakes on A Gun," is a reference to the film .
  • The Twin Snakes synergy is a reference to the Metal Gear Solid series.
  • The gun's name is likely a reference to the poem "The Rattler," in which a man is confronted by a snake similar in color to the one wrapped around the gun.
