An improvised gun that fires trash, sending poison in all directions. A threat to anyone in the room, including the wielder.

Becomes more powerful the more Junk a Gungeoneer is carrying.

Notes

  • Garbage Collecting - If the player has Unity , hitting an enemy with Trashcannon causes it and its bullets to briefly freeze. Bullets slow down to a very low percentage of its original travel speed, having a very slight amount of motion.
  • Grouch - Junk increases the damage of the Trashcannon by 25%. This effect does not stack with multiple Junk (despite the Ammonomicon entry claiming otherwise).
  • Recycling Bin - Starpew transforms the Trashcannon into a recycling bin, causing it to shoot high damage recycled shells instead of trash, and also greatly increasing its magazine size.
  • The Return - If the player has Pig , it turns green and shoots fire bats.
  • Does not synergize with Ser Junkan except in the unused synergy Trashkan .
  • Can be used to put out the fireplace.

Trivia

  • Unused: Trashkan - If the player has Ser Junkan , it causes an unknown effect.
  • The Grouch synergy is a reference to Oscar the Grouch from the show Sesame Street, who lives in a trashcan.
  • This weapon is likely a reference to the Junk Jet from Fallout 4 as well.
