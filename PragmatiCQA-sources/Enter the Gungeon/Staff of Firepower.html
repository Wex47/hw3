One of the earliest gun experiments, before the fires of the Forge were lit. This "magic revolver" was created when the then-master of the Gungeon taped one of his staves of power to the top of the barrel.

Staff of Firepower is a gun . Reloading alternates between a rapid fire pistol and a beam-like weapon that can set enemies on fire.

Notes

  • This gun fires faster when shot rapidly like a semi-automatic weapon, rather than holding the trigger like an automatic.
  • Chicken Arise - If the player also has Magic Bullets , the chance for Staff of Firepower to transmogrify enemies and size of bullets are increased.
  • - If the player has Owl , the gun's and Owl's shots bounce, and Owl turns skeletal and fires letters at enemies.
    • With this synergy, quickly switching between a synergy gun and a non-synergy gun will make Owl rapidly blank if bullets are near and rapidly shoot.
    • This synergy strangely also effects Baby Good Shelleton , making its lasers bounce off of walls and enemies.
  • The beam-like bullets can be absorbed by Gunjurers .

Trivia

  • This gun is a reference to the Fire Rod from the Legend of Zelda series.
  • The synergy Chicken Arise Is a reference to Aqua Teen Hunger Force episode "Video Ouija" - In which a witch doctor named Billywitchdoctor.com attempts to resurrect Master Shake, who had been killed earlier in the episode, by chanting "ARISE CHICKEN, CHICKEN ARISE!!" repeatedly.
