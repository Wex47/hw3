Ammonomicon Entry
Lightning In A Bullet
One of the first energy weapons created in the Gungeon. When lightning struck an old gunsmith's gun-rack, he decided to sand it down into a gun. The result is crude, as far as energy weapons go, but effective.

Thunderclap is a gun that fires small balls of lightning. Any water that the lightning passes over will become electrified. While reloading, a circular field surrounds the player that damages nearby enemies.

Notes

  • Alistair's Ladder - If the player has Shock Rounds , Thunderclap fires two parallel balls of lightning with every shot, linking the bullets together in a ladder pattern.
    • If the synergy is obtained through Lich's Eye Bullets , the Shock Rounds effect will not be applied.
  • In The Mood! - If the player has BSG or Strafe Gun , shot speed is increased by 25% and the player's movement speed is slightly increased.

Trivia

  • Altered: Diazepam - Prior to the Advanced Gungeons & Draguns Update , this gun was included in Diazepam .
  • The damaging field while reloading was added in A Farewell to Arms .
  • This weapon's appearance is a reference to the Thunderbolt weapon from the video game series Quake and the Plasma Gun from Doom .
  • This gun is a reference to Stephen King's series The Dark Tower .
  • The Alistair's Ladder synergy is a reference to the electrical Jacob's ladder , a device in which electric arcs climb upward between two vertical wires.
