Sunglasses is a passive item .

Effects

  • Slows down time and increases dodge power during explosions.
  • Increases coolness by 2.

Notes

  • Kung Fu Hippie, Rappin' Surfer - If the player also has Rad Gun , its magazine size is increased to 20 and its reload time is increased, giving more time to hit the mark. Grants 1.33 coolness . Additionally, the green skateboard of the gun will turn purple and the red hat will turn around so the bill is backwards.
  • Nailed It - If the player also has the Nail Gun , it fires larger nails that do about 6 damage each and cause bleeding.
  • Spicy D-Boys - If the player has Strafe Gun , coolness is increased by 2 and nearby enemies are lit on fire whenever reloading Strafe Gun.
  • The Line of Fire - If the player has Composite Gun , its shots leave a trail of fire.
  • Thermal Imaging - If the player also has The Predator , it fires two lasers instead of one.
  • Has ~13 seconds cooldown between explosions. For example, Roll Bomb won't trigger it every time the player rolls.

Trivia

  • Unused: Galaxy Defender - If the player has Grasschopper , it causes an unknown effect.
  • This item is a reference to the , a movie cliché popularized by action flicks where a "cool" character (sometimes wearing sunglasses) walks in slow motion as explosions go off behind them. Online, this is a more commonly known as the meme, popularized by a song by comedy music group "The Lonely Island".
  • In terms of design, the sunglasses closely resemble those of , the protagonist of CSI: Miami who is well known for slowly putting on similar shades when announcing a one-liner based on the episode's plot.
  • The subtitle is a reference to the song "The Future's So Bright, I Gotta Wear Shades" by Timbuk 3.
