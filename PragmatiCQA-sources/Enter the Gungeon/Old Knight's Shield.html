Ammonomicon Entry
Heavy With Experience
The shield of Ser Manuel, emblazoned with his House sigil, the Cup of Knowledge.

There is a diagram on the back illustrating how to strap on a shield and examples of when to raise it. Remarkably free of wear.

Old Knight's Shield is a passive item .

Effects

  • Grants two pieces of armor .

Notes

  • Pea Cannon - If the player also has Pea Shooter , its appearance changes, damage is increased and its shots cause small explosions.
  • Tutorialized - If the player also has Old Knight's Helm , Old Knight's Shield will orbit the player and block bullets.
  • This item is obtained in a secret room in the Halls of Knowledge . To find it, go to the room on the left of the first room after Manuel leaves the player. Using a blank will open the secret room where the Old Knight's Helm and the Old Knight's Shield can be found.
    • Talking to Ser Manuel with Old Knight's Helm and Old Knight's Shield after defeating him unlocks the Blockner fight.
  • After unlocking it, Old Knight's Shield can occasionally be found for sale from the Blacksmith at a item's price.
  • After unlocking it, Old Knight's Shield can occasionally be obtained as a starting item for The Paradox .
