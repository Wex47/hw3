In an attempt to combine the combat efficiency of bullets with the stealth and recoverability of arrows, modern archers developed the Gunbow. Though it proved ineffective in normal combat, it has taken on new life in the Gungeon.

Gunbow is a gun that fires fast-moving bullets that bounce seven times.

Notes

  • Shot Across The Bow - If the player has Rocket-Powered Bullets , Air Strike , iBomb Companion App , or Explosive Rounds , the Gunbow will fire rockets that explode on contact with enemies. Rockets deal 35 damage on contact and the explosion deals 35 damage. This removes the Gunbow's piercing shot.
  • Prior to the Supply Drop Update , Gunbow had 70 maximum ammo.

Trivia

  • Enemies appear to vaporize upon defeat when killed with this weapon, leaving no corpse behind.
  • The name of this gun could be a reference to the Monster Hunter series, which features Bowguns.
