Omega Bullets is a passive item and a bullet upgrade .

Effects

  • The final shot of a weapon's magazine deals double damage.

Notes

  • Diamond Weapon - If the player has Frost Bullets , the last shot of each magazine is guaranteed to be an ice bullet.
  • Emerald Weapon - If the player has Irradiated Lead , the last shot of each magazine is guaranteed to be a poison bullet.
  • Ruby Weapon - If the player has Hot Lead , the last shot of each magazine is guaranteed to be a fire bullet.
  • J am - If the player has Alpha Bullets , reload times for all guns are decreased by 33%.
  • Remnant - If the player has Lower Case r , adds an omega after each BULLET.
  • This item is very effective for guns that have a single bullet in each magazine, as every shot will have increased damage, as well as guns that have a unique bullet at the end of the clip, such as The Judge .
  • This item only affects Blasphemy 's sword beams, and only the sixth beam after doing a double tap reload.
  • This item has no effect on beam guns.

Trivia

  • The synergies Ruby Weapon, Diamond Weapon, and Emerald Weapon are all bosses from Final Fantasy VII .
  • The letter "Ω" (Omega) is the final letter of the Greek alphabet and is frequently used as symbolism for the end or finality, and vice versa for Alpha (α) bullets.
  • The J am synergy could possibly be a reference to the Bible, as God is repeatedly referred to as "I am" beginning in Genesis, and ending in Revelations 22:13, "I am the Alpha and the Omega, the First and the Last, the Beginning and the End."
