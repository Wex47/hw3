Ice Bomb is an active item .

Effects

  • Throws a bomb that deals 8 damage and freezes enemies.

Notes

  • Smart Bombs - If the player has Homing Bullets or Crutch , Ice Bomb will home in on enemies.
  • Battle Mode - If the player has Orbital Bullets , bombs start to generate and orbit around the player. These bombs won't deal damage to the player, just knock them around.
  • Super Bomberpal - If the player has Lil' Bomber , it fires two bombs instead of one, charges twice as fast, and has 20% lower reload time.
  • Unlike the regular Bomb , it does not open secret rooms.
  • This bomb is useless against the Gorgun and will disappear instead of exploding.
