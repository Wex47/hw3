An experimental treatment administered by the Gungeon's medical unit.

Antibody is a passive item .

Effects

  • Adds a 50% chance to heal the player for an extra half a heart whenever they receive healing.

Notes

  • Antichamber - If the player has Dark Marker , it alternates between firing red and blue shots that become very slow and large after traveling a short distance. If any red and blue shots touch, the blank effect is activated.
  • The Powerhouse of the Cell - If the player has Evolver , it evolves to its next stage.
    • Due to the ammo refill from evolving, dropping Antibody and picking it up again will give the player an unlimited supply of ammo for the Evolver.
