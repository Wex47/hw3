Effects

  • Increases movement speed by 1.5.

Notes

  • Five O'Clock Somewhere - If the player also has Crestfaller , reloading will fire a glass bottle that has a chance to stun enemies hit. Reloading Crestfaller while standing on a liquid replenishes the ammo missing in the current magazine, similar to Gungine .
  • Shotgun Affinity - If the player also has Shotgun Coffee , all weapons in the SHOTGUN gun class gain piercing, increased knockback, and increased shot speed.
    • Shotgun style guns not in the SHOTGUN class such as the Shotgun Full of Love or Tangler are unaffected by this synergy.

Trivia

  • The name and effect of the Item is based on the German caffeinated chocolate , which is used as a combat stimulant.
  • The part of the description stating "save the cap" is a reference to the Fallout item Nuka Cola , where Nuka Cola bottle caps are used as currency.
    • This may also be a reference to Coca-Cola's My Coke Rewards program where the customer earns points towards rewards by inputting the codes on bottle caps at Coca-Cola's website.
  • The item may be based on "shotgunning", a term where one takes a drinking can, usually beer, and pokes a hole in it to drink from rather than the top lid.
  • The name of the synergy Five O'Clock Somewhere refers to a popular expression used to justify drinking at any time of day, given that somewhere in the world it's 5:00 p.m.
