Freeze Ray is a gun that fires a continuous blue laser that has a chance to freeze enemies.

Notes

  • Ice Cap - If the player also has Camera or Flash Ray , Freeze Ray freezes enemies much quicker.
  • Willing To Sacrifice - If the player also has Ice Cube , Heart of Ice , or Frost Bullets , Freeze Ray gains piercing and slowly regenerates ammo over time.

Trivia

  • Has a similar look and basic abilities as the weapon used by Mr. Freeze in various "Batman" media.
  • This gun's flavor text is a reference to Arnold Schwarzenegger's campy performance as Mr. Freeze from the movie .
  • The synergy "Willing to Sacrifice" is a reference to the 1977 song by Foreigner.
