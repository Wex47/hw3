Effects

  • Orbits the player, firing homing 20-damage rockets at enemies every 5 seconds.
  • Blocks bullets.

Notes

  • Pilot Wingsman - If the player also has Alien Engine , Wingman's sprite will become blue and will fire 4 missiles at a time.
  • Wingman's missiles can be controlled by the player if they have Remote Bullets .
  • The Wingman's homing missiles target a single enemy and if that enemy dies before the missile has reached it, it will not home onto another one.
  • Wingman's missiles are affected by Battle Standard .
  • Wingman's missiles ignite any flammable Goop they fly over.

Trivia

  • The Wingman is likely a miniature version of Z's ship from the Pilot 's past, reinforced by its entry in the Ammonomicon, its unlock method, and its resemblance to Z's ship.
  • The Pilot Wingsman synergy is a reference to the Nintendo game Pilotwings .
