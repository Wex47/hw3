Unfinished Business
Occasionally refunds ammo when bullets miss.

Missed shots sometimes return from the dead, mindlessly seeking the closure they never found in life.

Zombie Bullets is a passive item and a bullet upgrade .

Effects

  • Adds a 25% chance to refund ammo when a shot misses.

Notes

  • Dawn of the Gundead - If the player also has Gunslinger's Ashes , Shellegun , Skull Spitter , Fossilized Gun or VertebraeK-47 , the chance to refund ammo for those guns is doubled.
  • Electron Pack - If the player also has the Proton Backpack , kills with the Proton Backpack spawn Gunslinger's Ashes ghosts that attack enemies.
  • Missed Roll Bombs and shots from Super Space Turtle can trigger Zombie Bullets and refill ammo for the player's currently held gun.
  • If duplicates of this item are somehow obtained, increases the chance of the effect and allows more ammo to be refunded when shots miss.
