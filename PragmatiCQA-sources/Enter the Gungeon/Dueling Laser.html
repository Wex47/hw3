Uses no ammo, but recharges like an item.

A thousand worlds away on a war-torn planet, this pistol is used in duels to the death. Its powerful beam consumes no ammo, but takes time to recharge. A hasty shot comes with a price.

Dueling Laser is a gun that fires a large, high-damage piercing laser. Like an active item, it must recharge after each shot by dealing damage to enemies.

Notes

  • Banshee Cry - If the player has Screecher , it fires three sound waves per shot instead of one.
  • Death Square - If the player has Dueling Pistol , damaging enemies with the Dueling Pistol rapidly recharges the Dueling Laser.
  • During a blessed run , the Dueling Laser does not recharge like an active item. Instead it automatically reloads in around 7 seconds.Upon verification, the first chamber boss recharged in four shots. Probably three times as fast, judging from the gauge buildup.
  • Since it has infinite ammo, it cannot reveal secret rooms, nor be fed to Munchers .
  • This gun is affected by Ice Cube , which allows it to partially recharge itself.
    • With enough damage, this gun can fully recharge itself, where it then reloads like normal.
  • Hip Holster grants an extra shot immediately after firing.
    • With Hip Holster as well as Cursed Bullets and the player has high Curse it will be recharged immediately, but it often won’t update until you switch weapons and switch back

Trivia

  • This gun is a reference to the Dueling Laser Pistol from the book Dying of the Light , the first novel of George R. R. Martin .
  • The sound effect for the Dueling Laser when it is fully charged up is the same sound effect as the Gun That Can Kill The Past.
