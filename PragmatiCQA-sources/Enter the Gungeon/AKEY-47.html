The AKEY-47 is a masterpiece of Gungeoncraft, perfectly combining the masterful gunplay of an AK-47 with the incredibly useful ability to open locks. An inscription along the frame reads, "EX ARCHA, LIBERTAS."

AKEY-47 is a gun that fires keys. Shooting a locked chest or door will unlock it. Alternatively, opening a chest or door with the AKEY-47 equipped will unlock it for free.

Notes

  • Akey Breaky - If the player has the Shelleton Key , AKEY-47 fires skulls that do 10 damage per skull, receives infinite ammo, and changes in appearance to resemble a Shelleton. Its shots will no longer open locks or reveal secret rooms, though chests can still be opened due to possessing the Shelleton Key.
    • If the player has obtained this synergy via Lich's Eye Bullets , opening a chest or door with the AKEY-47 equipped will still unlock it for free. However, on Switch , this will instead destroy the chest.
  • Has identical statistics to the AK-47 .
  • Can be sold by Flynt for 3 keys once unlocked.
  • Its bullets cannot open the padlocks on the trapdoor to the Oubliette and the tunnel to the Resourceful Rat's Lair ; however, they can be opened by hand for free with the AKEY-47 equipped.
    • However, AKEY-47 cannot be used in place of the Gnawed Key to open the lock on the hatch that drops down to the Resourceful Rat's Lair , and as of v2.0.6 , cannot open the doors and chests that require special keys dropped by Resourceful Rat.
  • If a lock no longer functions because of Trusty Lockpicks , shooting it with AKEY-47 will not open it.
    • Door locks (ones that look similar to Flynt ) that are no longer functional, due to failure of Trusty Lockpicks , can still be opened with AKEY-47.

Trivia

  • The inscription "Ex Archa, Libertas" is a reference to the Latin phrase "Ex gladio libertas", or 'From the sword, freedom', with 'Archa', the word for 'Chest', replacing 'gladio', the word for sword.
  • The synergy Akey Breaky is probably a reference to the song Achy Breaky Heart popularized by Billy Ray Cyrus.
