The younger bullet brother's handgun. It is unhelpfully labelled with an 'S'.

Shopkeepers found Smiley's good nature disarming, often giving him the advantage when negotiating prices.

Smiley's Revolver is a gun that decreases shop prices by 15%.

Its fire rate also increases dramatically for a short period of time after rolling.

Notes

  • Trigger Twins - If the player also has Shades's Revolver , ammo capacity is doubled and magazine size is increased by 50% for all guns, and the two guns are dual wielded.
    • While Lich's Eye Bullets doesn't give the dual wielding portion of the synergy, it will give the 2x ammo capacity and 50% magazine size increase.
    • Since both Smiley's Revolver and Shades's Revolver are in the PISTOL gun class , having one makes finding the other less likely- and therefore slightly decreases the chances of obtaining the Trigger Twins synergy.

Trivia
