Chase Carey (born 22 November 1953) is an American businessman who currently serves as chairman and CEO of the Formula One Group . He replaced Bernie Ecclestone following Liberty Media 's takeover of the sport.

He was installed as chairman in September 2016 as Liberty Media announced the acquisition, becoming CEO in January 2017, following the completion of the takeover.
