Spain is a country in South-West Europe. It is bordered by France , Portugal and Andorra. It is one of the more successful nations in Formula One, having produced one double-World Drivers' Champion from its 13 drivers. Spain also hosts its own Grand Prix at Barcelona , and has held the European Grand Prix on 7 occasions. One constructor, HRT , has originated from Spain.
