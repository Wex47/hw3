Jacques Bernard "Jacky" Ickx ( or ; born January 1 , 1945 in Brussels, Brussels-Capital Region, Belgium ) is a Belgian former racing driver who competed in Formula One from 1967 to 1979 , finishing as runner-up on two occasions ( 1969 and 1970 ), winning eight Grands Prix and finishing on the podium 25 times.

He drove for Matra (as a Tyrrell Racing entrant), Cooper , Ferrari , Brabham , McLaren (one race), Williams (one race), Lotus , Wolf-Williams , Ensign and Ligier .

Outside of F1, he achieved great success in sports car racing, winning the 24 Hours of Le Mans six times, a record that stood until 2005.
