Minardi was a Formula One team who raced between 1985 and 2005 , competing in nearly 350 races, and due a well-known low budget, did not achieve a single podium. Overall, they scored just 38 points. At the end of the 2005 season, they went up for sale and were brought by Red Bull , creating a new team, known as Scuderia Toro Rosso , to keep the Italian roots. The team's founder was Giancarlo Minardi and the final team owner was Paul Stoddart , an Australian businessman who took over in 2001 to save the team from folding. He still owns the Minardi name.
