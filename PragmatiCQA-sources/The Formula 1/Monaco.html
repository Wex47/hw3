The Principality of Monaco is a city-state located on the coast of the Mediterranean Sea. It is surrounded on three sides by France , and the fourth by the sea, but is less than 10 km from the Italian border. With a population of almost 40,000 crammed into ~2.1 km , it is the most densely populated country in the world. It is host to the Monaco Grand Prix .
