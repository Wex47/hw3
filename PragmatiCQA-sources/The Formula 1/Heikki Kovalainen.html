Heikki Johannes Kovalainen (born October 19 , 1981 in Suomussalmi, Kainuu, Finland) is a Finnish Formula One motor racing driver who previously drove for Caterham - Renault and Lotus - Renault in 2013 , having previously raced for Renault , McLaren and the Malaysian Lotus team (now Caterham). Kovalainen has driven in F1 from 2007 to 2013 and has achieved his one and only win at the 2008 Hungarian Grand Prix , while driving for McLaren - Mercedes . He is also the 100th driver to win a Formula One Grand Prix when he won it.

After a difficult season with McLaren in 2009 , he was left without a drive after the 2009 World Champion Jenson Button signed for the team for the following season. On 14 December 2009, it was announced that he would be driving for the new Lotus team, alongside Jarno Trulli . Despite signing for Lotus and remaining with the team after its renaming to Caterham , he would never score any points again and was dropped at the end of 2012 in favour of Charles Pic and Giedo van der Garde . He remained on good terms with Caterham, often doing free practice outings at various races during the 2013 season. In the last two races of the season, he replaced Lotus F1's Kimi Räikkönen for the final two races in the United States and Brazil , finishing 14th.

Kovalainen was without a drive in 2014 , but even if he was linked as a test driver for Mercedes , it has not been officially confirmed by the team.

He is currently racing in the Japanese Super GT series.
