The 2001 Formula One Season was the 52nd season of the Drivers' World Championship and the 44th season of the Constructors' Championship.

It began on March 4 in Australia and ended on October 14 in Japan .

The Drivers' World Championship was won by Michael Schumacher of Ferrari , 58 points ahead of David Coulthard of McLaren - Mercedes . Rubens Barrichello of Ferrari was third.

The Constructors' Championship was won by Ferrari , 77 points ahead of McLaren - Mercedes .

Changes from last season

Team changes

  • Jordan Grand Prix changed engines from Mugen-Honda to Honda .
  • Benetton changed engine suppliers from Playlife to Renault .
  • Arrows changed engine suppliers from Supertec to Asiatech .
  • Minardi changed engine suppliers from Fondmetal to European .
  • Prost changed engine suppliers from Peugeot to Acer .

Driver changes

  • Fernando Alonso : Astromega (F3000) → Minardi - European
  • Enrique Bernoldi : RSM Marko (Formula Renault) → Arrows - Asiatech
  • Luciano Burti : Jaguar - Cosworth Test → Race seat
  • Jenson Button : Williams - BMW → Benetton - Renault
  • Pedro de la Rosa : Arrows - Supertec → Prost - Acer (test)
  • Pedro Diniz : Sauber - Petronas → Prost - Acer (Management position)
  • Marc Gené : Minardi - Fondmetal → Williams - BMW (test)
  • Nick Heidfeld : Prost - Peugeot → Sauber - Petronas
  • Johnny Herbert : Jaguar - Cosworth → Arrows - Asiatech (test)
  • Tarso Marques : Dale Coyne (CART) → Minardi - European
  • Gastón Mazzacane : Minardi - Fondmetal → Prost - Acer
  • Juan Pablo Montoya : Chip Ganassi (CART) → Williams - BMW
  • Olivier Panis : McLaren - Mercedes (test) → BAR - Honda
  • Kimi Räikkönen : Manor Racing (Formula Renault) → Sauber - Petronas

  • Alexander Wurz : Benetton - Playlife → McLaren - Mercedes (test)
  • Ricardo Zonta : BAR - Honda → Jordan - Honda (test)
