Christopher Arthur "Chris" Amon MBE ( ; born 20th July , 1943 in Bulls, New Zealand - died 3rd August , 2016 ) was a former motor racing driver who competed in Formula One between 1963 and 1976 . Amon was labelled as one of the best F1 drivers never to win a race.

Amon is third in the list of most podiums without a win, with 11, while also holding the record for most poles without a win with five. Nick Heidfeld tops the former list, with 13 podiums but no wins to his name, although the New Zealander did win two non-championship races: the 1970 International Trophy and the 1971 Argentine Grand Prix .
