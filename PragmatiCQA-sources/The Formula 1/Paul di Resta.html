Paul di Resta ( ; born 16th April 1986) is a British racing driver who drove for Force India from 2011 to 2013 and Williams - Mercedes in 2017 .

Without a drive for 2014 , he returned to DTM , although it was not until 2015 that the Scot found his former pace. He signed for a seventh season in the DTM in 2016, before being announced as the Williams F1 test and reserve driver for the 2016 Formula One season on 14 March 2016. He was once again confirmed as their reserve driver for 2017 , before confirmed to compete at Hungary after Felipe Massa fell unwell.
