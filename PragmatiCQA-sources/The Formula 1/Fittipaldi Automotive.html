Fittipaldi Automotive , sometimes reffered to as Copersucar after its title sponsor, was the only Formula One motor racing team and constructor ever to be based in Brazil. It was formed in 1974 by Wilson Fittipaldi and two-time world champion Emerson Fittipaldi .
