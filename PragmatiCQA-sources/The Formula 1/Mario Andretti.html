Mario Gabriele Andretti (born February 28 , 1940 in Montova, d'Istria, Italy (now Motovun, Croatia)) is a retired racing driver, who is one of the most successful Americans in motorsport and its most successful in Formula One , winning a World Championship in 1978 . He also won the Indianapolis 500 in 1969. He is one of a few drivers to score a pole position on his début. He is also the only person to win the World Championship, the Indianapolis 500, and the Daytona 500 stock car race.

He drove for Lotus (twice), March , Ferrari (twice), Parnelli , Alfa Romeo and Williams .
