Denis Clive "Denny" Hulme OBE (born June 18 , 1936 in Motueka, New Zealand – died October 4 , 1992 in Bathurst, New South Wales, Australia) was a racing driver from New Zealand who raced in Formula One , winning the World Championship in 1967 . He drove for only two teams in his career, Brabham and McLaren . He often raced in the Can-Am series and drove in the Indianapolis 500 four times, finishing fourth twice.

He is the only New Zealander to win the World Championship.
