Lotus F1 Team was a British Formula One team that raced from 2012 to 2015 , following the renaming of the Renault F1 team. They were based in Enstone, Oxfordshire (United Kingdom).

The team competed as Lotus Renault GP in 2011 before renaming themselves to Lotus F1 Team in 2012. They were purchased by Renault in late 2015, and are now once again competing as Renault in 2016 .
