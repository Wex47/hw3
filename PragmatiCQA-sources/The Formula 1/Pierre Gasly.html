Pierre Gasly is a French racing driver who is currently racing for AlphaTauri - Honda . He made his debut for Toro Rosso at the 2017 Malaysian Grand Prix , replacing Daniil Kvyat . He did not compete at the United States Grand Prix , due to being scheduled in the final race of the 2017 Super Formula Championship, although that race was cancelled due to bad weather. He returned at the Mexican Grand Prix .

Gasly would be promoted to the full Red Bull - Honda team ahead of the 2019 season, replacing Daniel Ricciardo after his move to Renault . However, after a poor start to the season Gasly would lose his Red Bull seat to Alexander Albon , resulting in the Frenchman rejoining Toro Rosso from the 2019 Belgian Grand Prix , through to the end of the season.

He took his maiden Formula One win at the 2020 Italian Grand Prix .

His car number is 10.
