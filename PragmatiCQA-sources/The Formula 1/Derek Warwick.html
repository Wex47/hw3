Derek Stanley Arthur Warwick (born August 27 , 1954 in New Alresford, Hampshire, United Kingdom) is a former British Formula One driver who competed from 1981 until 1993 . Warwick had little success in Formula One but has been regarded by many within the F1 fraternity as one of the best drivers to never have won a race. Since 2010 , Warwick has regularly sat on the Stewards panel as the racing driver's representative at a Grand Prix meeting.
