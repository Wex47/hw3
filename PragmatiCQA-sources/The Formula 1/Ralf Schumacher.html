Ralf Schumacher (born June 30th , 1975 in Hürth-Hermülheim, Rhein-Erft-Kreis, North Rhine-Westphalia, West Germany) is a race car driver from Kerpen, Germany. He is the younger brother of Michael Schumacher . In his career, he drove for Jordan , Williams , and Toyota , winning six races. All of his wins came from his stint at Williams - BMW . Since retiring from F1 after not being able to find a drive for the 2008 season , Schumacher has raced in DTM touring cars.
