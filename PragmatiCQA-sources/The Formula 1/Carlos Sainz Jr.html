Carlos Sainz Vázquez de Castro (born 1 September 1994 in Madrid, Spain), commonly known as Carlos Sainz, Jr. , is a racing driver who made his Formula One début for Toro Rosso in 2015 , alongside Max Verstappen , who also made his début. Starting from the 2017 United States Grand Prix , he raced for Renault . He later moved to McLaren in 2019 , and then Ferrari in 2021 .

Sainz and Verstappen are the first two drivers to race in F1 who were born after the death of Ayrton Senna .

Sainz was inducted into the Red Bull Junior Team in 2010, and won both the 2011 Formula Renault 2.0 Northern European Cup and the 2014 Formula Renault 3.5 Series .

He is the son of double World Rally Champion Carlos Sainz.
