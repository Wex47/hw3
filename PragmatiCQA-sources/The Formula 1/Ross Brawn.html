Ross James Brawn OBE (born 23 November , 1954 in Manchester, Lancashire, UK) is an English motorsport engineer who currently serves as Managing Director, Motor Sport for Formula One Group following the takeover by Liberty Media .

Prior to this, he was the team principal of Mercedes AMG from 2010 until the end of 2013 . After a possible speculation to join McLaren in 2014, he announced his retirement on 1st February 2014.

Brawn quit the Mercedes team because he did not trust Toto Wolff and Niki Lauda .
