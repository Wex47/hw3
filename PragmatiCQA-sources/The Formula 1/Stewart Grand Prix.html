Stewart Grand Prix was a Formula One team that only competed for three seasons, between 1997 and 1999 , yet did win a Grand Prix - the 1999 European Grand Prix , by Johnny Herbert . It was founded by three-times World Champion Jackie Stewart , hence the name 'Stewart Grand Prix'.

Their 1997 and 1998 season were hampered with reliability problems, and they were successful in the 1999 season.

Rubens Barrichello competed in all 49 of the team's races, with his best finish being second at the 1997 Monaco Grand Prix . Jan Magnussen was the first Dane to score a point.

The team was bought out and renamed as Jaguar Racing in 2000.
