The French Grand Prix is a race on the F1 calendar. It was last held in 2019 at the Circuit Paul Ricard , after a 10-year absence before 2018 . The previous race was in 2008 at Magny-Cours .

The first French Grand Prix was held in 1906 , on a public road circuit outside of Le Mans. This is regarded as the very first Grand Prix ever.
