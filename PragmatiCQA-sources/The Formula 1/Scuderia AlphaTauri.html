Scuderia AlphaTauri is an Italian based entrant and constructor in the 2020 and 2021 FIA Formula One World Championship , using engines developed by Honda and succeeding Toro Rosso . One of two teams owned by Austrian beverage manufacturer, Red Bull, AlphaTauri is widely known as the junior team for Red Bull Racing , created to develop young drivers for the senior team and promote their fashion brand.
