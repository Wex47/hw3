This is a list of every driver that has appeared on the entry list in at least one Formula One race weekend since 1950 . This includes starters, non-starters and drivers that eventually didn't participate in the weekend.

Drivers in bold indicate drivers currently competing in the 2022 Formula One season .

This list also includes third drivers and Indy 500-only drivers.

For a list of non-championship drivers, see List of non-championship drivers .

As of the 2020 Spanish Grand Prix , 1011 drivers have competed in the Formula One World Championship.
