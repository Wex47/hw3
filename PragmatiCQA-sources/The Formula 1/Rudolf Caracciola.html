Otto Wilhelm Rudolf Caracciola ( January 30 , 1901 – September 28 , 1959) was a German racing driver of Italian descent, the greatest to ever have competed in the European Championship , winning three titles, in 1935, 1937 and 1938. He was also the last official champion in the series, as it was cancelled in 1939 before a champion could be crowned.
