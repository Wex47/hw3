The Lap Record for a particular circuit is the fastest time ever recorded in a Formula One Grand Prix around that circuit. For a Lap Record to stand, it must be posted during the race , not in practice or qualifying. Any change in the circuit's layout will mean that a new lap record has to be set.

Due to the cars since the 2017 Formula One season being widened, many Lap Records on current circuits are from the 2018 , 2019 , 2020 and 2021 Formula One seasons.

Circuits listed in this page are based on their latest layout configurations and also the latest event held on the venues.
