Henry O'Reilly "Harry" Schell ( June 29 , 1921 in Paris - May 13 , 1960 at Silverstone ) was a United States driver. In 57 races, he had two podiums and scored 32 points. He was killed while practicing for the 1960 BRDC International Trophy .
