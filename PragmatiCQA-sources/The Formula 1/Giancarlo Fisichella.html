Giancarlo Fisichella (born January 14th , 1973) is an Italian Formula One driver who has driven for Minardi , Jordan , Benetton , Sauber , Renault , Force India and Ferrari during an 14-year career in the sport. He is most notable for winning the 2003 Brazilian Grand Prix for Jordan, scoring 77% of the team's points that season in that one race.
