The 2022 Monaco Grand Prix (officially the Formula 1 Grand Prix de Monaco 2022 ) will be the seventh race of the 2022 FIA Formula One World Championship , to be held at the Circuit de Monaco on the weekend of May 27 and May 29 2022.

This race is the 79th edition of the grand prix held in the notoriously narrow streets of the principality, with all drivers and teams taking part. In support of the F1 event, Formula Two and Porsche Supercup are holding their own events this weekend.

This weekend was the first time in the history of the Monaco Grand Prix where the race schedule has deviated from the traditional format held in the past. The first two free practice sessions would now be held on Friday instead of Thursday.

According to the commentators on F1 TV, the track has been resurfaced, which could result in some unexpected incidents this weekend. However, it's worth noting the F2 and PSC events will lay down a respectable amount of rubber to provide ample grip to the new track surface.

There is an 80% chance of rain expected for the race on Sunday. If it rains, the water will wash away most of the rubber burned in throughout the weekend. This combination is quite reminiscent of the conditions at the 2021 Belgian Grand Prix .

Practice

FP1

Valtteri Bottas suffered a failure in his MGU-K unit on his first lap, which necessitated a replacement, plus a rebuild of his gearbox. The problem also caused him to enter the pits 21.9 km/h too fast, which earned him a €1,000 fine.

FP2

On his first timed lap, Daniel Ricciardo went hard into the barrier opposite the swimming pool, and was out of the session. Ricciardo was unhurt, but the car needed a major rebuild.
