Johnny Herbert is a retired British Formula One driver, currently working for Sky Sports F1 as a commentator. Herbert competed for numerous teams including Benetton , Stewart and Jaguar from 1989 to 2000 . During this time, Herbert won three races, including the only ever Grand Prix victory for Stewart at the 1999 European Grand Prix .
