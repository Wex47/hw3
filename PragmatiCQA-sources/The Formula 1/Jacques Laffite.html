Jacques Henri-Laffite (born November 21 , 1943) is a former French Formula One racing driver who competed in the sport from 1974 until 1986 . Laffite was one of the most experienced racers of his generation, in his last Formula One race at the 1986 British Grand Prix , Laffite equaled Graham Hill 's record for most Grand Prix started. Laffite co-held this record with Hill until 1989 when the record was broken by Riccardo Patrese . Since 1997 , Laffite has been an analyst and commentator for the French Formula One coverage on TF1 .
