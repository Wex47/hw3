Walter "Walt" Faulkner ( February 16 1918 - April 22 1956) was a driver from the United States. He competed solely in US oval track racing, including five Indianapolis 500 races. By qualifying for the pole for the 1950 race , he briefly held the record for the youngest driver to win the pole of a Formula 1 race, even though he was 32 at the time.

Faulkner was killed in qualifying for a stock car race in northern California in 1956.
