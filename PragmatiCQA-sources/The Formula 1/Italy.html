Italy is a country in Southern Europe which has produced numerous successful Formula One drivers and constructors. It currently hosts the Emilia Romagna Grand Prix and Italian Grand Prix .

The most recent Italian driver to start a race is Antonio Giovinazzi , who last competed in 2021 .
