This is a list of constructors who have won the FIA Formula One World Championship for Constructors , which from 1984-1985 was known as the Formula One World Championship for Manufacturers , and from its creation in 1958 to 1980 was known as the International Cup for Formula One Constructors .

It was first awarded in 1958 to Vanwall .
