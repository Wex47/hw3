Guanyu Zhou (周 冠宇 in Chinese; born 30 May 1999 in Shanghai, China ) is currently racing for Alfa Romeo , with teammate Valtteri Bottas .

In 2014, he joined the Ferrari Driver Academy. In 2019, he switched to the Renault Sport Academy and served as a development driver for the Renault F1 team until 2020. In 2021, he competed as a Formula Two driver for UNI-Virtuosi Racing and was concurrently a test driver for the Renault-rebranded Alpine F1 Team .

Zhou made his debut with Alfa Romeo in the 2022 , making him the first Chinese driver to race in Formula One . Zhou's Formula One debut was not without controversy, as several users on social media called him a "pay driver". He also suffered racist abuse, which was condemned by the users of r/formula1, a subreddit dedicated to Formula One content.
