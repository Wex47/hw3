The 1970 Italian Grand Prix was held at Monza on September 6 . Clay Regazzoni won the race in a Ferrari , followed by Jackie Stewart in a March - Ford and Jean-Pierre Beltoise in a Matra . The weekend was marred by yet another death: this time it was championship leader Jochen Rindt in a crash at the Parabolica curve on Saturday. The Year Of Death had captured the ultimate victim.
