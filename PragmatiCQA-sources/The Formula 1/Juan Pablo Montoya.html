Juan Pablo Montoya Rold√°n (born 20 September 1975) is a Colombian racing driver who competed in Formula One from 2001 until 2006 . Montoya was the first and only Colombian driver to participate in a Formula One Grand Prix. Montoya enjoyed success at the Williams and McLaren teams, winning races for both constructors. Since retiring from Formula One, Montoya moved on to the NASCAR series in which he competed until 2013. He then returned to the IndyCar series in 2014.
