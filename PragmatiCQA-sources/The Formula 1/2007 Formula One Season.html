The 2007 Formula One season is the 61st season of FIA Formula One motor racing. It featured the 58th FIA Formula One World Championship . It started in Melbourne on March 18 , to Brazil , October 21 . Kimi Räikkönen became the World Champion with Lewis Hamilton and Fernando Alonso runner-up by one point. Scuderia Ferrari won the constructor championship after McLaren was disqualfied.
