Carlos Alberto Reutemann (born April 12 , 1942 – died July 7 , 2021) was an Argentine Formula One racing driver who competed in the sport from 1972 until 1982 . After concluding his Formula One career, Reutemann moved into Argentine Politics where he was elected as Governor of Santa Fe in 1985, a position he held until 1995. In 1999 he was re-elected as Governor of Santa Fe and held the position until 2003. From 2003 through his death in 2021, Reutemann was a Senator in the Argentine Legislative. He had been regularly been requested to run for the Presidency of Argentina but on all accounts rejected the proposals.
