Toyota Racing was a team that competed in Formula One from 2002 to 2009 . They did not win a single race, but managed a total of 3 pole positions and 13 podium finishes.

Toyota's successful season was 2005 . In hands of Italian Jarno Trulli and German Ralf Schumacher , the team scored some decent results, and finished 4th in the Constructors' Championship with a total of 88 points.

Due to the financial crisis of the team's parent company, Toyota withdrew after the 2009 season with immediate effect. Prior to this decision, the team had already started work on the TF110 chassis.
