John Marshall Watson is a former British Formula One driver who competed in the sport from 1973 Formula One Season until 1985 . After the conclusion of his Formula One career, Watson was a noted Formula One commentator for Eurosport between 1990 and 1996 . He then worked as a commentator for ESPN in 1997 and Sky + in 2002 .
