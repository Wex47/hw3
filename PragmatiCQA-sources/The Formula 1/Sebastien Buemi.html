Sébastien Buemi is a Formula One driver currently in his second stint of testing for Red Bull Racing , following three years as a race driver for Scuderia Toro Rosso . Buemi has accumulated a total of 29 points during his 3 seasons with Red Bull's sister team, before returning to the main Red Bull team.

Buemi is also signed up to compete for Toyota at the 2012 24 Hours of Le Mans.

For the past 11 years since 2012 , he has been a test and reserve driver for Red Bull and sister team AlphaTauri (formerly known as Toro Rosso ). Since 2020 , he has been competing in Formula E .
