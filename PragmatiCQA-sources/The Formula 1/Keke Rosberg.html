Keijo Erik "Keke" Rosberg ( ; born December 6 , 1948 in Solna, Stockholm County, Sweden) is a Finnish former Formula One racing driver. Rosberg has driven for Theodore , ATS , Wolf , Fittipaldi , Williams and McLaren between 1978 and 1986 , winning 5 races and the 1982 World Drivers' Championship .

He is the father of former Mercedes Grand Prix driver and 2016 Formula One champion Nico Rosberg .
