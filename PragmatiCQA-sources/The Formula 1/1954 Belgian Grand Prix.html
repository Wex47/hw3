The 1954 Belgian Grand Prix (officially XVI Grand Prix de Belgique) was race number three of the 1954 Formula One Season . It took place at the Circuit de Spa-Francorchamps on June 20 1954. The race was won Maserati driver Juan Manuel Fangio with Maurice Trintignant of Ferrari in second and Stirling Moss of Maserati in third
