The 2006 Formula One Season was the 57th season of FIA Formula One motor racing. It began on March 12 in Australia and ended on October 22 in Brazil .

The title was won by Fernando Alonso of Renault , he second title in successive seasons, ahead of Ferrari 's Michael Schumacher . This was the final season of Schumacher's "first career", as he retired after the season, announcing it after winning the Italian Grand Prix . He returned in 2010 with the Mercedes team.

Renault also won their second Constructors' title in succession.

Testing

Pre-season testing was originally planned to take place at Barcelona from February 24 to 26, but following calendar changes to avoid a clash with the 2006 Commonwealth Games , teams agreed to host pre-season testing at Bahrain from March 3 to 5, the weeks before the season-opening race.
