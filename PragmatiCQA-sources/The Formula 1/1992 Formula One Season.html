The 1992 FIA Formula One World Championship was the 43rd season of the Drivers' World Championship and the 35th season of the Constructors' Championship.

It began on March 1 in South Africa and ended on November 8 in Australia after 16 events.

The Drivers' World Championship was won by Nigel Mansell of Williams - Renault , finishing 52 points ahead of his nearest competitor in his team-mate Riccardo Patrese with Michael Schumacher finishing 3rd in the Benetton - Ford .

Williams - Renault won the Constructors Championship by 65 points over McLaren - Honda and another eight points ahead of Benetton - Ford .
