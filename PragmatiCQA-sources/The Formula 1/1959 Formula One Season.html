The 1958 Formula One season was the 10th season of the FIA Formula One motor racing. It commenced in Monaco on May 10 , and concluded on December 12 in United States , after 9 races.

The Drivers' Title went to the Australian Jack Brabham , his first Formula One title, and the Constructors' Title went to Cooper .
