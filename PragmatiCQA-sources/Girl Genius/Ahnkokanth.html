Ahnkokanth is a Great Cetacean who captures/rescues the two British submarines manned by our heroes as they attempt to flee the destruction of the undersea dome of the Queen's Society . The Cetacean is so vast the subs are taken intact into a buoyancy chamber which is at least the size of a large cathedral. Ahnkokanth does this because he/she/they have sensed the presence of the Cetaceans' old nemesis Dimitri Zapnoople in the aforementioned dome, along with the poisoning of an important stretch of ocean by Cursed Waters . The contingent initially sent to talk to Agatha and Co. is led by His Serenity . (See below.) During this talk, a case of mistaken identity (sort of, it's complicated) leads to Agatha being selected as "worthy" of meeting sort-of in person with the Cetacean. After some frantic Sparky-preparation, this meeting goes well enough that Agatha is told about the contagion, and how the Cetaceans used to work with (evidently) the Great Ancient Civilization . Promising to investigate the situation further, the humans are returned safely to Londinium with a Deepdweller diplomatic contingent.

Additional Bodies

His Serenity

His Serenity is a Deepdweller living inside Ahnkokanth who has been appointed to the office of Deepspeaker, that is, of speaking on behalf of his god-host.
