Appearances

Doctor Parnassus O'Flynn is a member of Her Majesty the Queen's Right Puissant Society of Sages, Adepts, and Prometheans and works in the Society's laboratory complex. He once met Barry Heterodyne .

Appearance

He has spiky black hair, heading toward male pattern baldness but not yet far advanced. At the party where we meet him, he is dressed in a yellow and brown outfit, remarkably normal apart from a glowing yellow belt buckle. One slightly remarkable thing is that, unlike most of the party-goers, he has no visible sigil.

Possibly Relevant Outside Information

Parnassus is a mountain in central Greece, adjacent to the site of the ancient city of Delphi, which in Greek mythology was sacred to Apollo and the Corycian nymphs, and was the home of the Muses, therefore the home of poetry, literature, and learning.
