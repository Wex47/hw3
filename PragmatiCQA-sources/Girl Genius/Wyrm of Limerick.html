The Wyrm of Limerick is a Corbettite Railway passenger train, one of he many mobile monаstеries that service the people of Europa's spiritual and transportation needs. Agatha caught this train at Clank Head Crossing on her way to Paris , along with some other passengers . Continuing on its journey the Wyrm revealed to hold Numerous interesting personages , along with some other notable Stowaways .

The locomotive is equipped with a massive flame blaster in the front ✣ , for clearing out avalanches obstructing the railway, an enhancement made by its chief engineer, Brother Matthias .
