The Girl Genius Complete List of Absolutely Everybody is/was an incredibly valuable resource set up by the Professors Foglio on the main Girl Genius site; URL http://www.girlgeniusonline.com/info/cast/everybody.php . As hinted by the name, basic information on the overwhelming majority of characters in the story appear there. It's sometimes called GGCLAE for short.

The GGCLAE has come and gone in terms of Internet availability over years. If it is currently among the missing again, a version is available from the Internet Archive Wayback Machine: http://web.archive.org/web/20080212023400/http://www.girlgeniusonline.com/info/cast/everybody.php

The GGCLAE isn't quite perfect. Its information is now several years out of date, not everyone has their portrait included, and there are a few misspellings and such. For example, the old guy who's Seneschal Emeritus for House Heterodyne appears as Carson von Mekkhan in the story itself ✣ , but the spelling in the GGCLAE is von Mekk ah n. On the other hand, it has listed some "canon" folks who even now are yet to appear in the main comic, such as Princess Urania of England .

It has been officially replaced with cast lists for the individual volumes in the series; currently the site has:

  • Girl Genius --Volume Seven Cast
  • Girl Genius --Volume Eight Cast
  • Girl Genius --Volume Nine Cast
