The Awful Tower is the headquarters of Simon Voltaire , the Master of Paris . It stands, perhaps, as a monument to the Spark in Europa.

It has been completely redesigned ✣ by Colette Voltaire and now stands suspended well above the ground.

Rooms

  • The Awful Office ✣
  • The Great Ivory Chamber ✣

Possibly Relevant Outside Information

Europe has an analogous Eiffel Tower (French: La tour Eiffel ). The French initially thought of this architectural folly as a great eyesore, but eventually came to treasure it. It now stands as a monument to French engineering.

The Centre Pompidou has an inside-out design that resembles the midsection of the Awful Tower.

The Oval Office is used by the President of the United States for official business.
