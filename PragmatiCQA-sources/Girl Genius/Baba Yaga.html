One of the most temperamental of the vehicles employed by Master Payne's Circus of Adventure , Baba Yaga was a wagon on mechanical legs. She was apparently difficult to drive, constantly needing maintenance or refueling, and had a tendency to lean to one side when left standing for any length of time. However, she also produced eggs which the Circus needed ✣ , so it was kept and given to the "newbie".

Agatha , as the newest member of the Circus, had to drive her (at least Agatha got to bunk solo, not counting Krosp ). Agatha's plans for improvements were taken up by Dingbots , who eventually incorporated her into the Battle Circus .

The resulting Baba Yaga clank was able to fly, and successfully dispatched ✣ several Hoomhoffers before crashing. She happened to fall right on top ✣ of the Baron , effectively ending the battle.

Possibly relevant outside information

Baba Yaga is the name of a witch who appears in the folklore of many Eastern Slavic cultures ( Russian, Polish, and Ukrainian, among others ). She rides a mortar propelled by a pestle and has a house perched on chicken legs. The wagon is thus apparently named in her honor.
