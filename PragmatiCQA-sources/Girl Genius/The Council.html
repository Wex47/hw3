The Council (possibly The High Council ) is apparently a well-funded conspiracy to establish a new Storm King empire, throwing off the Baron's Peace . However, it is only mentioned thrice in the canon , and while these three mentions are sufficiently consistent that they may refer to the same organization, there is a small amount of room for doubt.
