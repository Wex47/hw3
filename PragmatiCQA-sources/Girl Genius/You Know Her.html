You Know, Her has only been seen in The Works . Her garments are similar to those of the few Skifandrian examples seen so far, but her overall appearance is sufficiently different to cause doubt as to her nationality.

She is categorized as Queen, Legend and Spark; her card is not sepia-toned.

Possibly relevant outside information

The name is a play on H. Rider Haggard's classic character, a beautiful and immortal queen of an African 'lost city', who was known as ' She ' (short for 'She Who Must Be Obeyed').
