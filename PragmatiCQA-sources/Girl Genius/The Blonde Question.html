Appearances

A Smoke Knight who is a member of a four-person team guarding/taking orders from Leopold von Blitzengaard in The Refuge of Storms . Her name is fan-assigned, taken from her hair color and the question mark she has on one cheek, either a tattoo or a birthmark. She is the last survivor of her group, including Leopold, after the rest are immediately tweedled upon encountering the newly-returned Martellus von Blitzengaard . She swears loyalty to Tweedle and answers a few questions while being half-strangled by him, but the instant his attention is distracted, she stabs his thumb with a poisoned dagger, and joins her compatriots when Tweedle returns the favor.
