Agatha H. and the Clockwork Princess is the second volume of the novelization , published on April 17, 2012. It covers the material from volumes IV through VI of the graphic novel: Agatha Heterodyne and the Circus of Dreams , Agatha Heterodyne and the Clockwork Princess , and Agatha Heterodyne and the Golden Trilobite .

A preview of the PRELUDE, PROLOGUE, and the first two chapters can be found at the page for the book on the Baen Books website (click on the Sample button).
