Studio Foglio encompasses all of the artistic endeavors of Phil and Kaja Foglio - comics, painting, writing, etc. There is a website associated with this name, but it is merely a single page graphic which links to the Foglios' other online endeavors: Girl Genius , their weblog , and their Airship Entertainment is the name of their publishing imprint for all non-X-rated titles (i.e. Girl Genius and Buck Godot , but not Xxxenophile ).
