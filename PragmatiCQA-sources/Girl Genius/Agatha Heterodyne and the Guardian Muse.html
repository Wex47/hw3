Agatha Heterodyne and the Guardian Muse is the tenth collected volume of Girl Genius . It begins with Vanamonde and Carson von Mekkhan confirming the 'death' of Castle Heterodyne .

The colors were done by Cheyenne Wright .

The book contains the web comic pages identified as volume X, and the first four web comic pages identified as Volume XI. The first page of Volume X begins here: December 14, 2009 ✣ . This volume can be purchased on or through local comic book shops .

Extras

This volume has several extras. Preceding the comic there is a photograph of the Professor's second Hugo award and their second Hugo acceptance speech in comic form (Jäger-style). Following the comic is the short story " Maxim Buys a Hat " and several full pages of art.

  • Zoing luvs Snaug
  • Woman riding mechanical bear past giant fallen clank in the snow (double-page spread)
