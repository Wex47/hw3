The Mopey Tortoise is a cargo-hauling aircraft officially registered under the British crown , run by Captain Hawkins , a gal of Europan pirate extraction. Already taking a shipment of Cloud Baboon dung to England from Paris , t he Tortoise is hired by Seffie to transport Tarvek there as well , in the hope that he will woo and win Agatha , thus leaving poor Gil with no one to marry but Seffie.

Alas, many things ✣ get in the way of this plan. When the ship finally does reach England, most of the crew are dead, and it is carrying three unexpected passengers , two of them unwilling.
