Airship Entertainment is the name of Studio Foglio 's publishing arm. The front page of the " Airship Entertainment " webpage reads "Books, Games, Comics and Art from Phil and Kaja Foglio ".

Titles

Although Girl Genius has its own website, it is still an Airship Entertainment publication. Other titles presented are the light adventure SF comic, Buck Godot , currently in preparation for trade paperback publication; and What's New with Phil and Dixie , a humorous look at gaming products and culture.
