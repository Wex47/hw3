A waffle is a type of cake, made from a batter or dough, cooked in a waffle iron , an appliance whose core consists of hinged metal plates that impress a (typically) honeycomb pattern (albeit in a square grid, not actual hexagons) on the cake whilst it is baked.

The Baron Klaus Wulfenbach , the greatly esteemed and well-beloved (is he still listening?) tyrant of Europa , is said by his son to have a love of waffles.  As traditional waffle-making areas are outside the Wulfenbach Empire, this suggests that the Baron traveled widely in his youth.

There is a Haus of Waffles in Mechanicsburg .

Jiminez Hoffmann likes them too ✣ , as do Violetta and Honker .

The master-Spark Van Rijn included a heavily-encoded top-secret waffle recipe ✣ in his notebook .

Semi-Canonial Information

According to Othar's Twitter-based adventures , at least one of his alternate selves has been occasioned by waffles and suffered epiphany . At another point, he notes that the principality of Holfung-Borzoi is famous for its cherry cream waffles.

Speculation

In a disguised Klaus Wulfenbach furthering his love of waffles can be seen in the lower left-hand corner. In the upper portion just right of the center is a building that looks very much like the Haus of Waffles .

Possibly Relevant Outside Information

The eponymous hero (and protagonist) of TPU professor of music Tom Smith 's comic opera The Last Hero On Earth is The Waffle (making the world safe for breakfast).
