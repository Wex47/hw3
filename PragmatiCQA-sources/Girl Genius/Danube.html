The Danube is a major river running roughly west to east through Central and Eastern Europe (known as Europa in the Girl Genius universe), rising in the Black Forest , flowing north of the Alps , through Vienna , Budapest , and Belgrade and south of the Carpathians before emptying through the Danube Delta into the Black Sea .

Mechanicsburg was founded on the Danube in our story. Students should pay attention to Europa's geographical highlights as the TPU professors will likely have a pop quiz on this later.
