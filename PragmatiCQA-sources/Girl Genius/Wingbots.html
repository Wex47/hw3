"Wingbots" is a fan-coined term, shorthand to refer to the quartet of flight-enabled dingbots ✣ which Agatha dispatches to carry her unaltered Sturmhalten warning message re: the mental return of The Other to Wulfenbach forces. As normal dingbots walk (scurry) ✣ or climb ✣ (are hoisted) ✣ , having the specialized and witty term for Agatha's air force was spontaneously adopted. (Though it should be noted that only one of them actually has wings.)

Unfortunately, the foursome immediately attracts the attention of a resident Geisterdragon , which hunts down and destroys all but the "Copterbot," before being tricked into latching onto a falling cannonball, sending it plummeting. When last seen during the Sturmhalten plot-arc, the survivor is shown chasing after ✣ the Gil -created flying machine piloted by Ardsley Wooster .
