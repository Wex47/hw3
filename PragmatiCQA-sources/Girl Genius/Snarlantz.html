Appearances

A Spark , a follower of The Other , and apparent member of the The Council , Gottmurg Snarlantz was tasked with watching over many of the latter's Hive Engines . He was fascinated by them, and worked on improving them. The most important results of his work were a prototype spark-infecting wasp and the wasp that apparently turned the entire town of Passholdt into a mutated form of revenant . It's unknown what exactly happened to Snarlantz himself, but it is strongly implied he did not survive the experience.

In the print novel Agatha H. and the Clockwork Princess , it is commented that he possessed an "unfortunate" set of teeth.
