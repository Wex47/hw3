Island of the Monkey Girls is a nightclub in Paris where Zola "Heterodyne" worked as the " head waitress " (and never just a chorus girl — although her card may have said "dancer"). Gilgamesh Wulfenbach and Othar visited ✣ the club while in Paris. Evidently Tarvek was also familiar with her act.
