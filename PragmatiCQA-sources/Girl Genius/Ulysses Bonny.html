Appearances

Ulysses Bonny is an Englishman who, when we first meet him, is working ✣ as a waiter during a soiree being held by the Queen's Society in their undersea Londinium dome. While doing his duties, he is shown taking a interest in Zeetha's tale of her trip to Europa from Skifander , and her resulting destruction of an entire pirate base.

We eventually learn he is, like many of his countrymen, an ex-pirate ✣ . Following a series of murders, he and everyone else are ordered sealed inside the dome while an investigation is conducted. He decides to make a covert exit, not because he is guilty of the murders, but because he has seen a chance to make a big score, by passing along Zeetha's story to certain interested parties.

In the process of this, he encounters ✣ Ariadne Steelgarter , who is also trying to escape the dome, and the two of them form a very temporary alliance of convenience. They successfully make their get-away and separate, with Mr. Bonny then staking ✣ his claim to "the Devil's Prize", which is being offered up by Bangladesh DuPree . It turns out that yes, this is indeed information about the destruction of Bang's pirate-base by previously unknown parties. The last we see of him, he's about tell Bang what he's learned; whether she pays him and/or lets him leave alive are probably now riddles for the ages.
