The Mechanical Orchestra was built by Gilgamesh Wulfenbach in his lab. on the Castle Wulfenbach and programmed with music he had written himself while he was at university . The musicians are all anthropomorphic animals, sitting in a band-shell partly designed to hide the driving mechanisms.

It was later partly disassembled by Agatha Heterodyne when she sleep-constructed her Rescue Clank to bail out Othar Tryggvassen (not realizing at the time that was actually a mistake... merciful, true, but a mistake).
