The crypt of the Heterodyne Family lies deep beneath Mechanicsburg , near Castle Heterodyne , and is accessed by serpentine tunnels from the Mechanicsburg cathedral .

According to Carson von Mekkhan , "The Heterodynes always come home in the end." . . Presumably, this currently excludes Bill or Barry .

The most recent interred Heterodyne is Klaus Barry Heterodyne , Agatha's older brother, who died as a toddler.
