The Human to Pet Mind Transplant Device is one of the devices found in Iago Heterodyne 's Red Playroom . It is first mentioned as Agatha and Gil are trying to figure out a way to save ✣ Tarvek Sturmvoraus . Gil brings it up as a way to prevent "death trauma memory loss".

Violetta suspects that Tarvek has already had his brain removed when he calls for her, however this is not the case.
