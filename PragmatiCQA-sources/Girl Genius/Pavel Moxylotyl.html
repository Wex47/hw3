Professor Pavel Moxylotyl teaches at Transylvania Polygnostic University . Exactly what he teaches has not been made clear, but he believes Agatha still owes him a report for one of his classes, and she seems to agree.

We encounter him as Agatha leaves the Incorruptible Library pushing a cart full of the unconscious King of the Silver Lands , also a professor (of Color Theory) at TPU. Our Professoressa accompanies him.
