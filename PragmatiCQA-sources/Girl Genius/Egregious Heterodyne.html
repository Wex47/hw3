Appearances

Egregious Heterodyne is one of the Old Heterodynes , and thus one of Agatha's ruthless ancestors. He destroyed the first Castle Heterodyne , built on the site of the spring that was the family's power source. Having concluded the spring was too small he decided, like a true spark, to increase its flow in the most egregiously destructive manner possible ✣ , with massive consequences for everybody around. The result was the destruction of the original castle and the creation of the River Dyne . He considered this a success.
