Perhaps Phil Foglio 's first major claim to fame, What's New with Phil and Dixie appeared in Dragon Magazine and satirized the world of fantasy-role-playing gaming. It was many people's introduction to Phil's work.

Aside from sharing Phil's writing and artwork, What's New with Phil and Dixie also has some more direct links with Girl Genius . In various pages from the series there are:

  • A "guest appearance" by Agatha and Krosp (the characters are not named, but they appear in panel four)
  • Three appearances by Krosp where he is referred to by name:
    • Applying for the job of Volrath's minion
    • As Volrath's minion in love
    • As the companion/sidekick of Genixa, God-Queen of Light
  • One more brief appearance (panel five) by an unnamed Krosp
  • A poster for Girl Genius appears in the background of three panels of this page

The entire run of these comics can be seen online on the appropriate section of the Foglios' web site . (The Phil and Dixie section of the Foglios' website is currently offline, so links to copies of the pages on the Internet Archive are provided.
