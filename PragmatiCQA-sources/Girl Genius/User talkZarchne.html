Promoting non-canonical section titles to have them show up on the appearances pages

I have always been liberal in adding new "non-canon" sections to the chronology pages I have worked on; to the point of absurdity in the case of some of the short stories inserted onto the comic proper. As you well know, I didn't start this practice and have followed the convention of making the added section titles H3 headings as opposed to the H2 headings used for the canonical section titles. I never really agreed with this practice because it seems to me to be nonsensical semantic markup to make things that are not subsections into subsections. Although I assumed that that the reason for doing this was to help distinguish the "real" section titles from the added "fake" ones, I felt that, because I had clearly labeled the non-canon sections as such, the further distinction wasn't necessary. In fact, I did make one or two of my non-canon sections headings into H2 headings, but they were corrected to H3 headings. I don't remember who did this now, but I decided to go with the flow after that. ðŸ”§

However, since your appearances pages only show the H2 sections headings, I wanted to ask if it would be too much trouble to modify your code bring in the H3 level headings as well. Or, as an alternative, what do you think of the idea of promoting at least some of my non-canon H3 headings into H2 headings, so that they will be added to the appearances page automatically? This is most important for cases where Kaja forgets to add a new section title to the page on which the story resumes after an art interruption. In those cases, pages of story can be "hidden" under a section heading for the art posting. ðŸ”§

Here is a good example of that: Halloween Wallpaper . After the single chronology entry devoted to a single art page, three pages of story are also found under the Halloween Wallpaper H2 heading. Since the non-canon H3 sections headings, added to clarify this situation, are not shown on the appearances pages, it can be hard to realize that last three of of the four pages under the "Halloween Wallpaper" heading on the appearances pages are not more wallpaper images but actual pages of the comic. -- ðŸ”§ ( talk ) 04:42, 2 July 2021 (UTC)
