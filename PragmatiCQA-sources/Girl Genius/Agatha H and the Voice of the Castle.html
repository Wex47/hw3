Agatha H. and the Voice of the Castle is the third novel. It covers the material from volume VII through half of volume X of the graphic novel: Agatha Heterodyne and the Voice of the Castle , Agatha Heterodyne and the Chapel of Bones , Agatha Heterodyne and the Heirs of the Storm , and Agatha Heterodyne and the Guardian Muse (until 2010-06-25 (Friday) ✣ ).
