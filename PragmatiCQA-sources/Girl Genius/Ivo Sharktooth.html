Ivo Sharktooth is a Mechanicsburg -based Jägermonster , and is the star ✣ of the Ivo Sharktooth PJ in-comic side-story set in the "future" ✣ , evidently at some point after the end of the Other War and the freeing of Mechanicsburg from Baron Wulfenbach's time-bubble.

He is an atypical Jäger in many respects; he dislikes fighting, considering it a waste of time, and is "not much of a joiner". These attitudes have estranged him from his fellow Jägers, and to date his encounters with them have been jovially hostile.

He also has a history with Luzette, AKA "The Anvil", who tends bar at the local landmark The Stopped Clock, and a strained relationship with Watch Chief Drozeki .

Ivo operates as a private detective for hire, ("PJ" standing for "Private Jäger") with the proviso that once you hire him to pursue a case, you cannot unhire him. Perhaps unsurprisingly, this leads to his not being overwhelmed with clients, at least at the time the story starts. Nevertheless, following the theft ✣ of the Golden Snail Trophy for the Mechanicsburg Harvest Festival snail race, Vanamonde von Mekkhan brings Mechanicburg's new(ish) ruler Agatha Heterodyne to see him, and his services are engaged.
