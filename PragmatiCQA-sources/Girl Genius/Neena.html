Appearances

Neena is an English princess, one of the countless daughters of Queen Albia living in the capitol Londinium . She's on the younger end of the spectrum, and is among those tapped to perform "Galloping Heroes of the Waves" at an official soiree thrown by her mother to celebrate, among other things, the arrival of Deepdweller ambassadors. When we first meet her, ✣ she is preparing for this event with two of her sisters, "Rosie" and an unnamed blonde gal.

She's on the search for an appropriate husband, and evidently considers Tarvek to be a worthy (or at least "nice") candidate; they were studying in Paris at the same time back in the day and are on friendly terms. According ✣ to him at least, she's a brilliant scholar who has a reputation for causing museum-based havoc in the course of her (admittedly successful) archeological-type studies. When they get together for a dance at the party, she tempts him with the possibility of a peek at her latest discovery: an intact copy of Luceat's Life of Van Rijn , but this stratagem is disrupted by Agatha launching her Circus-abetted prank.

Later, it's shown that she works in the Royal Observatory (where again she has a reputation for causing daily disasters), and gets very excited when Zeetha casually reveals ✣ that she can read the Mysterious Text on a plaque on display. The Warrior Princess goes on to translate enough of certain old writings that Neena can in turn give Agatha a good target for the next stop in her hunt for Prende's Lantern : a certain island in the vast British archipelago. An expedition is assembled, with Neena tabbed to accompany it.
