Too Much Tingle Pack guide at StrategyWiki

The Too Much Tingle Pack is a Tingle DSiWare app. It includes several applications like a calculator, a dancing Tingle image, the Tingle Reader , a timer, and a coin-flipping minigame. The game can be downloaded for 500 Nintendo Points. It is currently only available in Japan, as DSiWare is region-locked.
