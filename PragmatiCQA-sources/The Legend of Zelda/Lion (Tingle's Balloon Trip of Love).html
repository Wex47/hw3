Lion is a character in Ripened Tingle's Balloon Trip of Love .

Biography

Lion is a muscular humanoid lion. He is one of Tingle 's partners in the picture book world, beneath Kakashi and Buriki . Despite Lion's appearance and strength, he is a cowardly character. His strength allows him to perform actions Tingle and his other two friends cannot, such as removing certain obstacles.

Trivia

  • Lion is based on the Cowardly Lion from the Oz books.
