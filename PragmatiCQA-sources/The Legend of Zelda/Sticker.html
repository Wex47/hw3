Stickers as seen in Super Smash Bros. Brawl

Stickers are collectible items in Super Smash Bros. Brawl . Each sticker has artwork of various characters that have appeared in the games pertaining to the fighters appearing in Super Smash Bros. Brawl . They can be found in several ways: by collecting them during Brawls, winning them through Spectator Mode , defeating enemies in the Subspace Emissary's stages, won in the Coin Launcher , or by beating them out of the Sandbag . However, if the player gets a Game Over during the Subspace Emissary, they will lose the stickers that they have collected in that certain stage.

The main purpose of the stickers is to give characters certain advantages in the Subspace Emissary, such as increased attack power. They cannot be used in other modes such as Brawls or the Stadium . The stickers must be added to the trophy base of the character that the player wishes to use them on.

There are a total of 700 stickers in Super Smash Bros. Brawl , with each sticker having a different rarity level. Collecting all 700 stickers will unlock with the Stickers trophy.

Sticker Effects

Each sticker has a different effect, although some of these can only be used by certain characters. These effects only work in the Subspace Emissary mode.

Trophy Stand Drops Increases the odds of having a Trophy Stand appear.
Shield Resistance Increases the fighter's shield strength against attacks.
Dizzy Time Reduces the fighter's dizziness time.
Shield Recovery Reduces the amount of time for the fighter's shield to recover.
Food Effect Increases the effect of Food .
Maxim Tomato Effect Doubles the effect of the Maxim Tomato . Only Kirby , Meta Knight , and King Dedede can use this.
Heart Container Effect Increases the effect of the Heart Container . Only Link , Zelda , Toon Link , Sheik , and Ganondorf can use this.
Swim Time Increases the amount of time that the fighter can swim.
Carry When entering a new area, the fighter will start off with the item specified on the sticker, such as a Bob-omb , a Beam Sword , or a Mr. Saturn .
