Ezlo , also known as Cap Ezlo when transformed into a hat by Vaati , is a character and Link 's main companion in The Minish Cap .

Appearance

While in his cap form, Ezlo has the appearance of a green cap much like the one Link usually wears, albeit with a bird-like face, complete with a large yellow beak.

Abilities

Ezlo gives Link advice and background information throughout their adventure, both spontaneously and on demand with use of the Select Button . Ezlo has the ability to shrink Link to Minish size whenever he nears a Minish Portal , and also serves as a parachute when Link jumps into Updrafts , similar to the Deku Leaf from The Wind Waker .

Biography

Minish Ezlo ( Figurine from The Minish Cap )
Minish Ezlo The Minish Ezlo, before he was cursed by Vaati. He is a famous Minish sage. Even among Minish inventors, he was renowned for his amazing creations.
Ezlo and Link ( Figurine from The Minish Cap )
Ezlo and Link A young boy on a quest with Ezlo to restore the Four Sword. With the sword's power, he hopes to remove the curse on Princess Zelda.
Ezlo (Cap) ( Figurine from The Minish Cap )
Ezlo (Cap) A strange creature that looks at first like a cap. He speaks roughly and treats Link like a child, but he actually really likes Link.

Link finds Ezlo in the Minish Woods being attacked by two Red Octoroks . After Link saves him, Ezlo joins him on his quest to defeat Vaati and return the Picori Blade to its former power.

Before meeting Link , Ezlo was a renowned Minish craftsman and sage who was working on the Mage's Cap , a Cap that would grant the wishes of its wearer. The Cap was intended as a gift for the Humans , but when he finished it, his apprentice Vaati (who was also a Minish at the time) stole it from his work desk and donned it, using its power to turn himself into a powerful sorcerer. Ezlo attempted to stop Vaati , but was unsuccessful. Vaati used the power he had received from the Mage's Cap to place a Sorcerer's Curse on Ezlo , transforming him into a creature resembling a mix between a green hat and a bird. Ezlo pursued Vaati into Hyrule through the Minish Door , leading to his meeting with Link .

After Link defeats Vaati in Dark Hyrule Castle , Ezlo is transformed back into his original form. He tells Princess Zelda to combine her power with the Mage's Cap in order to undo all the damage done by Vaati . Before the Minish Door closes for another hundred years, Ezlo gives Link a green hat similar to the form he took while under Vaati 's Curse , remarking that it is the first time he has seen him wearing one and that it suits him.

Trivia

  • The menu of The Minish Cap displays several images of Ezlo in the background.
  • Ezlo 's face is only seen when he is talking or if the player wants advice; on occasions other than these, Link 's hat model is the same as in Four Swords .
  • When Link 's Life Energy is full, Ezlo appears with his beak open and held high. After losing Hearts , Ezlo 's beak closes and is held low. If Link only has one Heart Container remaining, Ezlo appears exhausted.
  • Ezlo 's cap form has similar properties to his sage form; namely, the swirl of hair on his head, and the appearance of his eyes. His staff also has a beak and the swirl of hair similar to his cap form.
  • Ezlo is almost identical in function to the Gnat Hat from Four Swords .
