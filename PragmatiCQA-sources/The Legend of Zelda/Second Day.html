Second Day
Main appearance(s)
Majora's Mask
Location(s)
Termina

The Second Day is the second of the three total days that Link has before the Moon crashes into Clock Town and destroys all of Termina in Majora's Mask . From the dawn of this day, the young hero and Tatl have 48 hours left to complete different tasks.

Overview

By now, even more townsfolk have become aware of the impending fate that could befall them, and some of them have even begun to make plans to flee the town. The environment and the theme of Clock Town reflect their restlessness, for the weather is unpleasant with the constant rain and the Clock Town music has taken a faster pace.

Events

Below is a chart showing most of the major events that occur during the Second Day, including the time, place, and a brief description of said event.

Rain will cause the Termina Loach to swim near the water's surface and the Goodta Goby to swim deeper underwater, making them easier to catch with standard lure and sinking lure respectively.

10 12:00 PM to 6:00 PM Stock Pot Inn Gorman is found sleeping in the Stock Pot Inn lamenting his troupe's cancelled performances. Link can alleviate his stomach problems by delivering Mystery Milk from his brothers.
11 3:00 PM to 12:00 AM Post Office The Postman is training his mind with the timing game. Link can play it to receive a Piece of Heart from him.

14 5:00 PM to 9:00 PM Stock Pot Inn Link can deliver the Pendant of Memories to Anju to reassure her of Kafei's love for her.
15 6:00 PM to 7:00 PM Romani Ranch Cremia prepares to take the milk cargo via wagon to Clock Town's Milk Bar. Link can accompany her (provided he saved the cows on the First Day) and receive Romani's Mask as a reward for protecting the milk from the bandits.
16 6:00 PM to 6:00 AM East Clock Town A Stray Fairy is floating around East Clock Town. Link can take her to the Fairy's Fountain in North Clock Town to receive a Magic Meter or the Great Fairy's Mask
17 6:00 PM to 6:00 AM West Clock Town The Rosa Sisters are practicing their choreography. Link can show them Kamaro 's dance moves to receive a Piece of Heart.
18 6:00 PM to 6:00 AM Laundry Pool Guru-Guru is practicing his music box. Link can talk to him to open up Guru-Guru's true feelings and receive the Bremen Mask as a sign of gratitude.
19 9:30 PM Stock Pot Inn's Knife Chamber Link can overhear a conversation between Anju and her Mother if he has the Room Key.
20 10:00 PM to 6:00 AM Milk Bar Gorman can be found drowning his sorrows at the Milk Bar. Link can cheer him up by helping Toto do a sound check of the Ballad of the Wind Fish and receive the Troupe Leader's Mask during the process.
21 12:00 AM to 1:00 AM Curiosity Shop Sakon will try to sell his stolen goods to the Man from Curiosity Shop .

23 12:00 AM to 6:00 AM Termina Field Kamaro is found dancing atop the rocks near the Snowhead entrance. Link can heal his soul to receive Kamaro's Mask .
