The Minish Cap

Enemies appear both in the Overworld and inside Dungeons in The Minish Cap . Many of the Enemies from Four Swords reappear in The Minish Cap , though some of their appearances have been altered.

Enemies do not appear at all in the game until Vaati opens the Bound Chest at the Picori Festival , releasing monsters into Hyrule . After Vaati is defeated by Link at Dark Hyrule Castle and Princess Zelda uses the Mage's Cap to undo the damage he caused to Hyrule , all the Enemies disappear from across the land.
