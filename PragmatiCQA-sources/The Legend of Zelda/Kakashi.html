Kakashi is a character in Ripened Tingle's Balloon Trip of Love .

Biography

Kakashi is one of Tingle 's partners in the picture book world, beneath Buriki and Lion . The cheerful scarecrow is mentally lacking and is in search for intelligence. He is the smallest of Tingle's three friends and is able to get into small spaces due to his size. Kakashi joins Tingle's quest on Page 2 . On Page 11 , he is kidnapped by Segāre , and hidden in a cave behind Usotami Village .

Trivia

  • Kakashi is based on the Scarecrow from The Wizard of Oz .
