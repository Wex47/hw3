Octavo's Ode is a Downloadable Content update for Cadence of Hyrule .

Overview

Octavo's Ode introduces a new Mode called Story Mode — Octavo wherein Octavo is added as a playable character. Additionally, Dungeon Mode allows players to play through single dungeons without an overworld in a similar fashion to the classic Crypt of the NecroDancer .
