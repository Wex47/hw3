Biography

Renado lives in Kakariko Village with his daughter Luda , and it is he who finds the children from Ordon Village and shelters them when the Village is covered in Twilight and overrun by Zant 's minions. After Ilia and Ralis come to the Village, he takes care of both of them as well. Telma apparently has a romantic attraction to Renado, although these feelings are unrequited. When he gives Link his Letter for Telma, he specifically says he does not wish to see her. He also mentions that he was friends with Mayor Bo and sends Link back to Ordon Village to find him and ask him how to best the Gorons of Death Mountain .
