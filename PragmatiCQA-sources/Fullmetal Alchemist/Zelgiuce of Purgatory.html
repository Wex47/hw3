Zelgius of Purgatory is a Fire Priest in the game Fullmetal Alchemist 3: The Girl Who Surpasses God and leader of the priests who control the city where Sophie Bergmann lives. An intimidating figure, he wears a demonic mask and the symbol of a lion, along with a large, spiked collar. In the past, Zelgius worked together with Sophie's father on experiments, which likely led to the development of Sophie's mysterious powers.
