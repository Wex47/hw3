Fullmetal Alchemist: Premium OVA Collection is the English DVD, released by Funimation in 2009, containing four original video animations which were not previously available outside of Japan. The OVAs feature the original English voice cast from the first anime series . The DVD also contains a number of special features, including the original TV commercial, a textless closing song, and trailers. See the list of Fullmetal Alchemist OVAs for more information.
