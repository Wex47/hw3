2003 anime

In the first anime series, Fuery plays little more than a small supporting role in the storyline, providing comic relief and backup alongside his comrades Jean Havoc , Heymans Breda and Vato Falman . Notable instances

of Fuery's involvement include the point at which he and 2nd Lt. Havoc are both lightly wounded by ricochet fire while attempting to sneak up on Edward and Alphonse Elric near Resembool and his mission, near the end of the series.  After this, as Mustang is given a promotion to Brigadier General and sent to fight in the North, Fuery is told that he won't be going, as, during his last physical exam, he had been diagnosed with athlete's foot, and Lt Hawkeye tells him, "We now believe it is affecting your brain."  This, however, is a diversion so that Fuery can be disguised as Riza with Lt Havoc masquerading as General Mustang, to free Mustang and Hawkeye for an attempted coup in Central City.

In the movie Conqueror of Shamballa he is seen near Sheska in a diplomatic hall, and fighting with other soldiers when the Thule Society attacks Central in force.
