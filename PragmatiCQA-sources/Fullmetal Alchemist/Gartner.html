Major General Gärtner was the commanding officer that was sent to Briggs by Central Command to call for Major General Armstrong to report to Central so she could be questioned about the whereabouts of Lt. General Raven .

In her absence, to Gärtner was given full command of Fort Briggs . His role was to assist in creating a crest of blood as part of the Nationwide Transmutation Circle , but the forces of Briggs' will proved too strong.

His last appearance was on chapter 84 where he, being the representative of Briggs Army, greets Grumman on the long-awaited joint training exercise with the Eastern forces. After The Promised Day events, his whereabouts is unknown, but it's possible that he was arrested as a conspirator under the Senior Staff.
