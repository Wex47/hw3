Inerasable Sin (消せない罪 Kesenai Tsumi ) is the first ending theme to Fullmetal Alchemist. It is performed by Nana Kitade . A shortened version of the original is played in the dub. A special version of this ending is played in episode 7 when Nina Tucker dies, featuring clips of her and her dog Alexander with the Elric Brothers.
