Kanama is a small slum on the outskirts of Central City . Edward's party first encounters and battles the Homunculus Pride along with Gluttony in the dense forest outside of the slum. During the battle, Edward knocked out power to the village in order to keep Pride's shadow at bay, causing grief for the residents. This grief turned into danger when Pride knocked a lantern out of a villagers hand during his battle with Heinkel , causing a brush fire which quickly spread to the nearby forest. The fire was likely subdued, due to Edward's party continuing to operate from there until their attack on Central Command .

Trivia

  • The message Pride sent in Morse Code while trapped in the dark was "Kanama SOS", leading Kimblee to his location.
