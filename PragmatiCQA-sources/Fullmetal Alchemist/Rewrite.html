Rewrite is the fourth and final opening (second in the English dub on Adult Swim ) for Fullmetal Alchemist . It is performed by Asian Kung-Fu Generation . The song won the Best Anime Theme Song at the American Anime Awards in 2007. There's also an official English version written by the Darling Thieves .
