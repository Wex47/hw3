" Melissa " (メリッサ Merissa ) was the first opening and briefly the first ending theme of the 2003 anime Fullmetal Alchemist . It was performed by Porno Graffitti as their twelfth single and was released on September 29th, 2003. The opening version of the song was included in the Fullmetal Alchemist Original Soundtrack 1 and the full version in the Fullmetal Alchemist Complete Best album.
