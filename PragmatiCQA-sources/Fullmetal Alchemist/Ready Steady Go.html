Ready Steady Go! by L'arc~en~Ciel is the second opening song in the original Fullmetal Alchemist anime, used in Episodes 14 through to 25. When the English-dubbed version of the show was first broadcast on [Adult Swim], the second opening and ending were used throughout the first 26 episodes — skipping the first opening and ending — but the DVDs released by Funimation show the correct openings and endings for the first 13 episodes. The music video for Ready Steady Go! was included as an extra feature on the Volume 4: The Fall of Ishbal DVD. [SPOILERS] After Maes Hughes 's death in Episode 25, the usual ending sequence is replaced with a simple credits roll.
