Janice Of The Hurricane is an original character who has only appeared in Fullmetal Alchemist 3: The Girl Who Surpasses God . She was made specifically for the game.

Characteristics

Janice is a young woman with blonde hair and suggestively sexual attire. She bears the symbol of an eagle on her upper left thigh. She is the Wind Priest of the group led by Zelgiuce. She has the ability to levitate in midair and fly.
