Sophie Bergmann ( ソフィ・ベルクマン , Sofi Berukuman ) is a mysterious fourteen-year-old girl and a principal character in the video game Fullmetal Alchemist 3: The Girl Who Surpasses God with a strange transmutation circle-like symbol resembling a flower embedded in her left hand. Like Alphonse , she has a fondness for stray cats. Edward and Alphonse attempt to save her when she is about to be attacked by Leoneed at the beginning of the story, but Sophie ends up saving them instead when she emits a strange and powerful light that radiates throughout the metropolis.

Sophie is, so to speak, the vessel in the game for the witch named Velza . She ends up getting Velza's soul into her body. Sophie's father's name was Victor, but for some reason, was known as Gilbert, and was murdered in front of her by a man known as Zelgiuce of Purgatory .

Plot

In the game, she revives Edward as he is stabbed and instantly killed by Trigger in an ally. She is put into a coma-like state, leaving a grieving Edward, Alphonse, and Winry to believe she had died.

Appearance

Sophie has Purplish White Hair, wears a uniform similar to Amestrian's with a Black Bow-Tie and has faded Blue Eyes.
