Riza Hawkeye ( リザ・ホークアイ, Riza Hōkuai) is an officer in the Amestrian State Military as well as Colonel Roy Mustang's trusted second-in-command and bodyguard. A firearms specialist and sharpshooter, Lt. Hawkeye is an invaluable asset to the Colonel both in office and on the battlefield and serves as his closest and most supportive subordinate.
