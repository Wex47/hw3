Mr. Han is a smuggler specializing in transporting people illegally and secretly between Amestris and Xing . It is his agency that allows Ling Yao , Lan Fan , and Fu to cross the Great Desert safely and enter Amestris without detection.

His Part in the Story

When Ling is requested to secure safe passage for Maria Ross out of the country, he calls on Han once more to help with the job, and after the deed is done, Han is asked to rendezvous with 2nd Lt. Heymans Breda , Major Alex Armstrong and Edward Elric in the town of Resembool so as to smuggle them across the border into the Xerxes ruins.

After the conference at Xerxes, Han departs for Xing with Fu, Ross and a few unnamed travelers under his watch.

Trivia

  • Like several other characters, Han's introduction is completely cut from the 2009 anime , leaving his presence with the group to be implied.
