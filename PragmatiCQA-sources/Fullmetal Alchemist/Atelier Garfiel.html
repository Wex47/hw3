Atelier Garfiel , also known as Studio Garfiel, is an Automail shop in Rush Valley . It is owned and operated by Garfiel and is also Winry's place of employment.

Not many important events happen at this shop, but here Edward Elric and Alphonse Elric encounter Ling Yao , the Xingese prince, for the second time and Winry meets him for the first time in Chapter 33: Showdown in Rush Valley and Episode 15: The Envoy From the East . Other than that, not many important events happen in this shop, but it is common for Winry, Paninya , and Mr. Garfiel to have converstations here. This shop is also included in the end credits of the third season of the 2009 anime.
