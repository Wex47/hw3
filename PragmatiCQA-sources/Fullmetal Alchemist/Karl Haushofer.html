Karl Ernst Haushofer ( カール・ハウスホーファー ) is a villain of the Fullmetal Alchemist movie Conqueror of Shamballa . He was a German general, geographer, and geopolitician. Through his student Rudolf Hess , Haushofer's ideas may have influenced the development of Adolf Hitler 's expansionist strategies.

Background

After retiring from the German army, Haushofer became a professor at the University of Munich in 1921. There he meets Hohenheim for the first time, who recently had installed in the city. Confessing to having a great knowledge of alchemy, Hohenheim earns the respect of Karl and in the someway both are shown, in the last episode from the 2003 anime, to participate in a secret organization called Thule Society .

Trivia

  • His character is clearly based on an actual historical figure by the name of Karl Haushofer .
  • His voice actor, John Swasey , went on to voice Van Hohenheim , father of the Elric brothers, in the 2009 series .
