Rockbell Automail (called Rockbell Prosthetic Limb Outfitters in the 2009 dub ) is the family-run automail atelier maintained by Pinako and Winry Rockbell out of the Rockbell home in Resembool , Amestris . This house also serves as the Elric brothers' quarters when they visit their hometown, as they burned down their original house, which belonged to their Mother and Father, at the start of both animes.
