Rie Kugimiya (釘宮 理恵, Kugimiya Rie , born May 30, 1979) is a Japanese seiyū affiliated with I'm Enterprise who was born in Osaka and brought up in Kumamoto. Kugimiya is best known for playing the role of tsunderes, a marked difference from her role in FMA, Alphonse Elric , who she has voiced in both the 2003 and 2009 series, both films, and three of the video games.
