The Modified M1910 or "Mad Bear G" is a lightweight, cold-climate prosthetic arm for use in combat. Made of a carbon-fiber alloy, the M1910 is lightweight and durable. The fingertips have been sharpened and hardened with the use of diamonds. It is said to be one of the best types in cold-climate automail.

Trivia

  • The Mad Bear is one of the models Buccaneer wears, along with the Crocodile .
  • The model number of both arms worn by Buccaneer( the Modified M1910 as well as the M1913-A ) is very similar to that of the Colt M1911 pistol.
