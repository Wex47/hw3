I Will

I Will is the fourth and final ending theme for the 2003 Fullmetal Alchemist anime . It was performed by Sowelu. The sequence is notable for depicting a montage alternating an image of Ed and Al , with images of their mother Trisha , Nina Tucker , Scar and Maes Hughes , all of whom the Elrics had been close to but have since passed away.
