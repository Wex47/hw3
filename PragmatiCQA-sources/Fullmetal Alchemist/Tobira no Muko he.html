"Tobira no Muko he" (扉の向こうへ lit. "Beyond the Gate"), sometimes called "Beyond The Door" supposedly from a mistranslation is the 2nd Ending Theme from the 2003 Fullmetal Alchemist Anime . It is performed by Yellow Generation (known as YeLLOW Generation in Japan), who was a female J-Pop trio signed under Sony Music Japan's DefSTAR Record label from 2002-2006. This song is one of their biggest hits to date.
