Jesse Hutch (born February 12, 1981) is a Canadian-born film and television actor. He spends his time travelling between the USA and Canada.

Career

He worked on the television show American Dreams as Jimmy Riley, romantic interest of main character Meg Pryor (Brittany Snow). Most recently, he played a major character on the Sci-Fi Channel TV-movie Termination Point.

He plays as Trey Cooper in Freddy vs. Jason .
