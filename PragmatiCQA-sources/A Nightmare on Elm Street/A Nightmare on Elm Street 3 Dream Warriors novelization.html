A Nightmare on Elm Street 3: Dream Warriors is the 1987 novelization of A Nightmare on Elm Street 3: Dream Warriors .

It is based on the screenplay .

The screenplay and the novelization are both very different than the film.

It shouldn't be confused with the book adaptation by Bob Italia .

It is a part of the book The Nightmares on Elm Street Parts 1, 2, 3: The Continuing Story .

Plot

  • Kristen Parker is Kirsten Parker .
  • At the beginning, Kirsten Parker is talking on the phone to a friend.
  • The girl starts out not on the tricycle and then gets on it.
  • Inside the house, Kirsten sees HUNDREDS of tricycles and a BICYCLE.
  • Neil is Neil Guinness instead of Neil Gordon .
  • Neil reads about Hypnocil in a book, not on a computer.
  • It isn't stated that Roland Kincaid is black.
  • Taryn White is black.
  • Taryn has the dream power of breathing fire. She sets Freddy Krueger on fire.
  • Super strength is Joey Crusel's dream power.
  • Donald Thompson disappears shortly after the death of his wife.
  • Jennifer Caulfield has the dream power of becoming incorporeal and/or disappearing.
  • Freddy Krueger's head appears on the TV screen, not on top of the TV.
  • Freddy Krueger was born at 1428 Elm Street.
  • 1428 Elm Street once was the Hathaway House.
  • Amanda Krueger dies in childbirth.
  • There is a scene with Neil and Nancy in a bed together.
  • Freddy Krueger takes the form of a crow.
  • Freddy Krueger kills Elaine Parker.
