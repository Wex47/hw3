A Nightmare on Elm Street is a series of book adaptations of the first six films of A Nightmare on Elm Street (film series) by Bob Italia. At the time these book adaptations were written, the first six films were the whole film series. Freddy vs. Jason , didn't exist at the time.

In places, they are significantly different than the films. This is at least partially because they are based on pre-film screenplays, which are also significantly different than the films.

A lot of the profanity that was used in the films has either been removed or replaced by milder words.

A lot of the sexual references in the films have been removed.

Some, but not all, of the references to child murder in the films have been either removed or made less explicit.
