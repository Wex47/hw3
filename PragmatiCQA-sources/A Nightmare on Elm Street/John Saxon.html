John Saxon , born Carmine Orrico , (August 5, 1936 - July 25, 2020) was an American actor, known for his roles as Roper in the 1973 Bruce Lee film Enter the Dragon and as Donald Thompson in A Nightmare on Elm Street and Dream Warriors .

He is a character in Wes Craven's New Nightmare and Wes Craven's New Nightmare (novelization) , and played himself in the former.
