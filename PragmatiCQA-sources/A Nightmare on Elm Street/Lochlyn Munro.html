Richard Laughlain "Lochlyn" Munro (born February 12, 1966) is a Canadian actor who plays as Deputy Scott Stubbs in Freddy vs. Jason .

Career

Munro is mostly known for roles in films, notably A Night at the Roxbury, Scary Movie, White Chicks, and Freddy vs. Jason.

For television, he is perhaps best known for his recurring role as Jack Sheridan in the supernatural drama Charmed (1999-2000). He is credited for more than 200 roles in television dramas and movies.
