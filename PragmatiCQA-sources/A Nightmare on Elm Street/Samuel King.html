Samuel King

General information

Occupation

Doctor

Appearances

A Nightmare on Elm Street

Portrayal

Charles Fleischer

Dr. Samuel King is a doctor at the Katja Institute for the Study of Sleep Disorders that treated Nancy Thompson when her mother began to worry about her lack of sleep and nightmares. However, Nancy only had intentions of asking him if they could give her drugs that'd prevent her from dreaming. However, Dr. King protested, saying everyone needed to dream. The experiment gave Nancy more information than she planned as the next nightmare she had with Freddy Krueger . She discovered she could bring things out of her dream, such as his hat.
