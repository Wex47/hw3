A Nightmare on Elm Street: Dreamspawn is a 2005 novel written by Christina Faust and published by Black Flame.

It is the second in a series of five novels.

It, and the first and third novels in the same series, were later reprinted in 2006 in the compilation A Nightmare on Elm Street: Ripped from a Dream: A Nightmare on Elm Street Omnibus .

Official Summary

"With the inhabitants of Elm Street sleeping soundly at night, dreamstalker Freddy Krueger gets to work with a passion doing what he does best-hunting his prey in the twisted world of their nightmares. The teenagers of Elm Street soon learn that if they die in their dreams, they'll never wake up!

In this brand new dark adventure, cult anti-hero Freddy Krueger's quest is to ensure that nobody on Elm Street will rest until they're dead!
