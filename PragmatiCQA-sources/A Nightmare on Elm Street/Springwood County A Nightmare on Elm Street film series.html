Springwood County is a fictional county in the fictional version of Ohio in the A Nightmare on Elm Street film series.

No county with this name exists in the real life Ohio.

It includes the fictional town of Springwood .

It has a hospital referred to as "County General".

It is policed by the Springwood County Sheriff's Department .
