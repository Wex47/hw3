A Nightmare on Elm Street is the 1987 novelization of the 1984 film of the same name , written by Jeffrey Cooper and published by St. Martins Mass Market Press.

It is based on the screenplay .

It shouldn't be confused with the book adaptation by E.L. Flood or the book adaptation by Bob Italia .

It is a part of the book The Nightmares on Elm Street Parts 1, 2, 3: The Continuing Story .

Notes

  • It is stated to take place a decade after Freddy Krueger was burned to death.
  • Freddy Krueger making his glove isn't described. He obviously did so, however.
  • It describes Tina's dream after describing her wake up. The actual order of the events is the same, however.
  • It is stated that Elm Street has at least one elm tree. It is implied that the street is named after it.
  • It is stated that Don's job as a police officer was a strain on his and Marge's marriage. This is only implied in the film.
  • It is stated that Freddy Krueger was drunk when he was burned to death. It isn't stated one way or the other in the film.
