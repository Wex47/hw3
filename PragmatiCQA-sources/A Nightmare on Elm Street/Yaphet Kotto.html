Yaphet Kotto (born November 15, 1937 – March 15, 2021) was an African-American actor. He was best known for his films including The Running Man (a film that starred Arnold Schwarzenegger ), Alien , and Live and Let Die . He appeared in Freddy's Dead: The Final Nightmare as Doc .
