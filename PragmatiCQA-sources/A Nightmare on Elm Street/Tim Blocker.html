Timothy "Tim" Blocker is a character in the TV series Freddy's Nightmares: A Nightmare on Elm Street: The Series .

Biography

He is a member of the Springwood Police Department .

He appeared in the pilot episode " No More Mr. Nice Guy ".

He killed Freddy Krueger and was killed by Freddy Krueger.

In one episode, it's actually stated that he died in 1988.

He is the husband of Sarah Blocker and the mother of Lisa Blocker and Merit Blocker .

Death

He was killed by Freddy Krueger.
