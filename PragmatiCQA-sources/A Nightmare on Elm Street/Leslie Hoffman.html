Hall Guard
Leslie Hoffman is an actress. She played the Hall Guard in A Nightmare on Elm Street .

Three of the of iconic lines came from that scene.

"Where's your pass?"

"Screw your pass!"

"Hey Nancy, no running in the hallway!" Though spoken by her, they were later dubbed in by Robert Englund .

It has been noted that fans even today are "freaked out" by Freddy Krueger 's voice coming out of her mouth in that scene.

Robert Englund and Leslie Hoffman are the only two that wore the sweater that had no stripes on the sleeves.

Leslie Hoffman is both a Stuntwoman and Actress.
