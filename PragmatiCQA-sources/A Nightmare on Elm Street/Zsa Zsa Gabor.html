Herself
Zsa Zsa Gabor was a Hungarian-born American socialite and actress who acted in supporting roles in movies, on Broadway, and occasionally on television. She is known for her role in films like: We're Not Married and Touch of Evil , and television shows like: Burke's Law and Bonanza . Gabor is known by A Nightmare on Elm Street fans for having a cameo appearance within A Nightmare on Elm Street 3: Dream Warriors .

Cameo in A Nightmare on Elm Street 3

In 1987, Jennifer Caulfield , and other Elm Street Children part of the Dream Warriors , were recuperating after witnessing the death of their friend, Phillip Anderson . While everyone else was in their rooms trying to help other sleep while being guarded by one another, Jennifer was in the recreation room watching television to learn more about the acting business in Hollywood and to keep herself from sleeping.

However, Jennifer falls asleep even after purposely burning herself in an attempt to stay awake. In her dream, she changes the channel to find an interview between Zsa Zsa Gabor and host Dick Cavett. The short conversation between Gabor and Cavett was about Gabor promoting people to reach out to their dreams and pursue acting careers if they desire to become involved with the film making business.

During the interview, Cavett asked Gabor if he could ask her another question. After positively complying, Cavett suddenly turns into Freddy Krueger and points his razor glove to Gabor and angrily asked, "Who gives a fuck what you think?". Then Freddy stood from his chair across from Gabor and attempted to slice Gabor across her face. Before the action could be done, the television shuts off and eventually, Jennifer was killed by Freddy, who turned into the television and smashed Jennifer's face through the television's glass monitor.
