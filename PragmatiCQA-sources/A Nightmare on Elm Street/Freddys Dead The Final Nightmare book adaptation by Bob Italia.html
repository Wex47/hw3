Freddy's Dead: The Final Nightmare is the sixth book in A Nightmare on Elm Street (book adaptations of the film series by Bob Italia) .

It is a book adaptation of the film of the same name .

Differences from the film

Freddy Krueger killing the hampster isn't described. However, he is described as writing "I will be kind to animals." over and over again on a chalkboard, strongly implying that he did kill the hampster.

Freddy Krueger's stepfather's name isn't given.
