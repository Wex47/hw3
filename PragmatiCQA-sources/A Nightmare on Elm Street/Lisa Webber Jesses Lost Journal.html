Story

While the events depicted in Jesse's Lost Journal are not part of the series timeline or canon , Mark Patton (who played Jesse Walsh in A Nightmare On Elm Street 2: Freddy's Revenge) wrote a string of events that he believes could go down in history as "what really happened." Following the timeline of the film series, the events we see in Freddy's Revenge take place in 1985, while the events described in Jesse's diary are mentioned as occurring in 1982 and 1985.

It is mentioned that after what happened in the factory, Jesse collapsed on the ground and Lisa watched as Freddy had possessed him, Jesse had a delusional vision where he believed he had killed Lisa but in reality he had not.

Lisa walked away, witnessed all this and called her father and then the police, they came and after a few seconds they took Jesse away in an ambulance. After long hours of interrogations and questions from her relatives and her police, Lisa decided that it was best to lie about what happened because no one would believe what had really happened.

Lisa spoke up at Jesse's hearing and told authorities that Jesse had been having a nervous breakdown for months, that she thought she had a split personality, and that was why Jesse believed there was a man who took over his body (she also used what happened with Schneider and other events to "support" the facts related to the traumas and their consequences). Lisa found out that Schneider had kidnapped a boy for a month and repeatedly abused him and that was why no one sought justice for him.

Finally Jesse is declared as a person with mental problems and that is why he is admitted to the Westin Hills Psychiatric Hospital, where he was isolated for a year basically in a coma. Lisa's parents moved to Arizona and there is no mention of why Eddie was not put behind bars for accidentally killing a young man on the night of the party.

Lisa got away from all that and moved to New Jersey, where she began to study Art Therapy and married one of her friends from university, it is precisely by investigating the art scene in New York that she manages to find Jesse (now with a new identity) and buys one of his works as a sign that he has found it. It is during all that time that Lisa carries her pregnancy, Jesse and she had had unprotected sex, Lisa decided that the name of the child would be Jesse in honor of his father.

Personality

Lisa is sociable, sensible, warmhearted and independent as well as sensitive and intelligent. After being aware of Jesse's odd behavior she researches for the murderous killer Freddy Krueger, and encourages Jesse to fight against Freddy’s influence.

Being strong and independent, Lisa's courage and love for her boyfriend has ultimately defeated Freddy and saves her boyfriend.

Physical appearance

Lisa Webber is introduced as a young woman with blue-gray eyes.

Relationships

Jesse Walsh

Lisa was the first to want to meet Jesse when she moved to Springwood. He usually takes her to school every morning and, as the story progresses, she ends up falling in love with him. She knows that Jesse is in trouble and that's why she never judges him, she always tries to be as understanding as possible with him, so she wants to be with him most of the time to protect him.

Kerry Hellman

Kerry seems to be the kind of stalwart friend who is always looking for advice to Lisa, managing to put a smile on her face when she is sad, she spends with her most of the time when they are in high school.
