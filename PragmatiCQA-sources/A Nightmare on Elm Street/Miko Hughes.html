Miko Hughes (born Miko John Hughes) is an American actor that appeared in Wes Craven's New Nightmare as . He is mostly known for his film roles as a child, including Joseph in Kindergarten Cop (a film that starred Arnold Schwarzenegger ), Gage Creed in Pet Sematary , and as Simon Lynch in Mercury Rising . His most known television role is his recurring role as Aaron Bailey , a friend and classmate of Michelle Tanner in the sitcom Full House .
