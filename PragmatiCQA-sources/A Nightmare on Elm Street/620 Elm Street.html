620 Elm Street is a residential lot on the version of Elm Street in A Nightmare on Elm Street (film series) .

It's central feature is a house. There is a fence in front of the front yard.

The house has two above ground stories, not including an attic. It likely also has a basement.

The ground floor includes a living room.

The second above ground floor includes at least two bedrooms and a hallway. It likely also includes a bathroom.

Trivia

In real life, it is 620 Milwood Avenue Los Angeles, California.
