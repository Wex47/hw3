A Nightmare on Elm Street 4: The Dream Master is the fourth book in A Nightmare on Elm Street (book adaptations of the film series by Bob Italia) .

It is a book adaptation of the film of the same name .

It shouldn't be confused with the novelization .

Differences from the film

  • Instead of saying Fred Krueger is history, Kincaid says Freddy Krueger is history.
  • There's no mention of Roland Kincaid 's dog Jason urinating.
  • It is stated "a flaming stream ran along the ground". It isn't stated that the stream is made of dog urine.
  • The line "How's THIS for a wet dream?" is left out.

Notes

It is explicitly stated that the Elm Street House was Freddy Krueger's house.
