A Nightmare on Elm Street is a film series with seven film to date. The seventh film in this series is also a part of the Friday the 13th film series.

Wes Craven's New Nightmare technically isn't a part of this film series, but is associated with it.

A Nightmare on Elm Street (2010 film) isn't a part of this film series.

This film series is about the character Freddy Krueger and is predominantly set in the town/city of Springwood .

There are novelizations of all the films of the series except Freddy's Dead: The Final Nightmare . All of these novelizations are significantly different than the films.

Bob Italia wrote book adaptations of the first six films of this film series . All of these are also significantly different than the films.
