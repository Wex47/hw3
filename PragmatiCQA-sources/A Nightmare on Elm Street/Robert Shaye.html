Robert Shaye is an American director, producer and actor. He is perhaps best know for founding New Line Cinema and producing A Nightmare on Elm Street , A Nightmare on Elm Street 2: Freddy's Revenge , A Nightmare on Elm Street 3: Dream Warriors , A Nightmare on Elm Street 4: The Dream Master , A Nightmare on Elm Street 5: The Dream Child , Freddy's Dead: The Final Nightmare , and Freddy vs. Jason .
