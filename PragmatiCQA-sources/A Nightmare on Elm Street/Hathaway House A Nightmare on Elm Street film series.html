The Hathaway House was a fictional asylum for the criminally insane.

It was first mentioned in A Nightmare on Elm Street 3: Dream Warriors . It was maintained by a Catholic sisterhood. It was located in Springwood and is presently abandoned.

History

In the 1940s, this building was maintained by a Catholic sisterhood. At the time it was presumably the state asylum as well due to its size.

It was closed some time after the same Sister hung herself in the tower several years later. Beyond that not much is known about the asylum other then a few fun facts. It is presumed that it was built under the direction of a Dr. Hathaway around the turn of the century, but no evidence has been found to support that claim.
