Lisa Webber (A Nightmare on Elm Street film series)
Kim Myers is an actress who appears in the second film of the A Nightmare on Elm Street film series as Lisa Webber . She also appears as herself in the documentary film Never Sleep Again: The Elm Street Legacy in 2010.

In recent years since her appear on Freddy's Revenge , she's best known for her recurring role on the former NBC television series The Pretender .
