Breckin Meyer (born May 7, 1974) is an American actor, comedian, writer, producer, and drummer. He is best known for playing Jon Arbuckle in the Garfield film series as well as being the second person to voice Joseph Gribble (after the late Brittany Murphy) in King of the Hill . He was also known for being as Spencer Lewis in Freddy's Dead: The Final Nightmare .
