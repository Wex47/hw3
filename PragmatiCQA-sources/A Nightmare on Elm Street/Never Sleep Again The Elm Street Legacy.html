Never Sleep Again: The Elm Street Legacy is a 2010 American four hour direct-to-DVD documentary film that chronicles the entire A Nightmare on Elm Street (franchise) franchise and the rise of New Line Cinema. The four hour documentary features most people involved in the A Nightmare on Elm Street series, from cast members such as Robert Englund and Heather Langenkamp , to crew members such as Wes Craven , who directed the original film , Rachel Talaly , who also directed Freddy's Dead: The Final Nightmare , and Robert Shaye , who produced the whole A Nightmare on Elm Street film series. The documentary goes in-depth with all of the films of the series, as well as the anthology TV show Freddy's Nightmares: A Nightmare on Elm Street: The Series . The documentary notably ignores the reboot , although interviewees touch on the subject in the special features.
