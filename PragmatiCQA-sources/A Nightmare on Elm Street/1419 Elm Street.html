1419 Elm Street is a residential lot located on Elm Street in the town of Springwood .

It is directly across from 1428 Elm Street .

It's central feature is a house. The house is surrounded by a yard, with a driveway, a sidewalk, and at least one tree.

The house has two above ground stories, not including an attic. It likely also has a basement.

The ground floor includes a living room.

The second above ground floor apparently includes at least one bedroom, which was Glen Lantz's at the time, and a hallway. It likely includes at least one other bedroom and a bathroom.

The house first appears in A Nightmare on Elm Street (1984 film) .

At the time of A Nightmare on Elm Street , it was the home of Mr. Lantz , Mrs. Lantz , and Glen Lantz .
