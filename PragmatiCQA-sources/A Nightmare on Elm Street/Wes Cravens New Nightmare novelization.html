New Nightmare (full title: Wes Craven's New Nightmare ) is the 1994 novelization of the film of the same name . It is very similar to the film. Like the film, it references the A Nightmare on Elm Street (film series) , not its novelizations. Wes Craven is referred to as 's "jailor".
