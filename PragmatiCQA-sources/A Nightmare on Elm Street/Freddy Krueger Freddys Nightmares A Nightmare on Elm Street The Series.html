Freddy Krueger is a serial killer in Freddy's Nightmares: A Nightmare on Elm Street: The Series . He isn't the main antagonist of all the episodes.

The Freddy Krueger of Freddy's Nightmares: A Nightmare on Elm Street: The Series is similar to the Freddy Krueger of the A Nightmare on Elm Street film series, and, like him, is played by Robert Englund and looked like Robert Englund before being burned to death, and, in a sense, looked like Robert Englund after being burned to death, and, also like him, murdered younger than teenage children before being burned to death, but is still a different version of the character.

It is stated that he killed at least one teenager before he was burned to death (a 14-year old boy), while in the film series it seems to be left ambiguous whether or not ANY of his pre-death victims were teenagers.

He had a pre-trial hearing, but was set free due to the arresting officer failing to read him his Miranda rights, without ever going to trial.

It is straight out stated in the episode " It's My Party and You'll Die If I Want You To " that he molested at least some of his pre-death victims.

He was burned to death by Tim Blocker .

This version of Freddy Krueger obviously knew before he was killed that he would continue in the Dream World after his physical death.

This version of Freddy Krueger graduated from Springwood High School in 1970, which was two years after the Freddy Krueger of the film series was burned to death.

While in the film series, most of the adults Freddy Krueger kills after being burned to death were either involving with him being burned to death or were helping the teenage characters work against him, in Freddy's Nightmares Freddy Krueger, after being burned to death, kills many adults who apparently had nothing to do with burning him to death.

It's stated in the episode " It's My Party and You'll Die If I Want You To " that he's been systematically killing his former schoolmates from Springwood High School in their dreams. By this time, they were well past teenage.

Biography

Pre-death

Teenage

He went to Springwood High School, from which he graduated in 1970. He was considered quiet and unpopular in school, but he did have one friend, the meek Howard Nehamkin . This was two years after the Freddy Krueger of the film series was burned to death.

Springwood Slasher

He murdered and attempted to murder both younger than teenage children and teenagers. He murdered at least one adult (Lou, a police officer).

It is straight out stated in the final episode "It's My Party and You'll Die If I Want You To" that he molested at least some of his victims.

Post-death

He kills many of his former Springwood High School schoolmates when they are well past teenage.

He kills far more adults than he was ever depicted as killing in the film series.
