Jason Ritter (born Jason Morgan Ritter) is an American actor who portrayed Will Rollins in Freddy vs. Jason . He is the son of late actor John Ritter and actress Nancy Morgan . As an actor, he is best known for his roles as Kevin Giradi in Joan of Arcadia (Joan of Arcadia also featured his Freddy vs. Jason co-star Chris Marquette ), Sean Walker in the short-lived TV series The Event , and Dipper Pines from Gravity Falls .
