Kelly Rowland

Information

Date of birth

February 11, 1981 (age 31)

Film(s)

Freddy vs. Jason
Kelly Rowland (born Kelendria Trene Rowland) is an American actress and singer who portrayed Kia Waterson in Freddy vs. Jason . She is best known for being a member of the 90s R&B girl group Destiny's Child, which she was a part of from 1997 to 2006.
