This is about the Springwood High School of the TV series Freddy's Nightmares: A Nightmare on Elm Street: The Series. If you are looking for one or more other versions of Springwood High School, please check the Springwood High School Disambiguation .

Springwood High School is a public high school in Springwood .

It's main building has male and female shower rooms.

As well as the main building, it has a running track, which is featured in the episode Killer Instinct .

It is featured in many episodes of Freddy's Nightmares: A Nightmare on Elm Street: The Series .

Freddy Krueger was a student there and graduated in 1970. He "participated" in the 1990 class reunion.
