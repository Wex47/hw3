" Dream Warriors " is a song by heavy metal band, Dokken , written for the movie A Nightmare on Elm Street 3: Dream Warriors . The song plays during the closing credits of the film.

"Dream Warriors" was released as a single from the film and also appeared on the band's album Back for the Attack . It reached number 22 on the Mainstream Rock charts in 1987.

Music Video

A music video for the song received airplay on MTV. The video used scenes from the movie, as well as new scenes filmed specifically for the video. Patricia Arquette and Robert Englund appeared in the new scenes, playing Kristen Parker and Freddy Krueger , respectively.

In the original scenes, Freddy is tormenting Kristen. She is eventually saved by the band members of Dokken. Their dream power is that their music is able to repel Freddy. Freddy then wakes up in his own bed and screams, revealing that the entire video was his own nightmare. He wonders who the band members of Dokken were as the video closes.

Trivia

  • "Dream Warriors" was composed by Dokken band members George Lynch and Jeff Pilson.
  • The song was released as a single along with two other Dokken songs, "Paris is Burning" and "Back for the Attack."
  • The single was certified platinum.
