Freddy's Nightmares is a four issue comic series.

It is a reprint of the comic series Freddy's Dead: The Final Nightmare , Freddy Krueger's A Nightmare on Elm Street , and Nightmares on Elm Street . It isn't original material.

It shouldn't be confused with the TV series Freddy's Nightmares: A Nightmare on Elm Street: The Series , which is original material.
