Katharine Isabelle (born Katharine Isobel Murray) is a Canadian actress who portrayed Gibb Smith in Freddy vs. Jason .

Career

As an actress, she is best known for her role as Ginger Fitzgerald in the Ginger Snaps trilogy as well as her portrayal of Margot Verger , the sister of Mason Verger , in the NBC thriller-horror series Hannibal , and Katrina in the Goosebumps episode, " It Came from Beneath the Sink! "
