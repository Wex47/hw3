Springwood High School is a public high school in Springwood . It is a part of the Springwood Independent School District .

It's main building has a main entrance, with two pairs of doors. The number "3939" is on the front of the building.

There is a set of four steps leading down from the main entrance to a walkway. This walkway goes to another set of four steps. This set of steps leads down from the walkway to a sidewalk.

There is a lawn on both sides of this walkway.

This building has at least two floors, a ground floor and a basement. There's at least one stairway between the ground floor and the basement.

The ground floor has a main entrance, with two pairs of doors, at least two intersecting hallways, various classrooms, a cafeteria, a gym, male and female shower rooms, etc..

Coach Schneider was killed in its male shower room.

-

As well as the main building, it has a parking lot and an outdoor baseball, basketball, and archery area.

Its parking lot is shown in A Nightmare on Elm Street 2: Freddy's Revenge and A Nightmare on Elm Street 4: The Dream Master .

Its outdoor baseball, archery, and basketball area is shown in A Nightmare on Elm Street 2: Freddy's Revenge .
