Sarah Blocker is a character in Freddy's Nightmares: A Nightmare on Elm Street: The Series .

She is the wife of Tim Blocker and the mother of Lisa Blocker and Merit Blocker .

She is featured in the episodes No More Mr. Nice Guy and Sister's Keeper .
