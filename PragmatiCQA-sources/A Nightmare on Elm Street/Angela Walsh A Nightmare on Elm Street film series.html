Angela Walsh is a character in A Nightmare on Elm Street 2: Freddy's Revenge .

She is Jesse 's younger sister, thus she is the daughter of Ken and Cheryl Walsh . She is portrayed by the actress Christie Clark .

Story

A Nightmare On Elm Street 2: Freddy's Revange

Angela is worried at first by her brother's screams every morning due to the recurring nightmares she suffers from, however, her mother always tries to calm her down. It is at that very moment that she manages to complete her collection of plastic nails from the cereal box, nails similar to Freddy's metal claws, when Jesse sees this she is taken aback.

When Jesse learns from Ron's remarks in the locker room that the house they had moved into was fraught with a history of murder and dead bodies, he confronts his father in the kitchen. This terrifies Angela and her mother tells Jesse that it was not a place to be talking about such things, although it was also clear that she was angry with Ken for not telling them about the previous events in that house.

There were two nights that Freddy almost murdered Angela. The first is when Jesse, in his dreams, opens the room and sees his sister jumping rope and singing the typical song " One, Two, Freddy's Coming For You ". The second night was when Freddy enters Angela's room and Jesse (possessed by Freddy) manages to fight against instinct and finally she is not attacked, Jesse still confused seeks to clothe her and it is there that he realizes that he has the metallic claws of Freddy in his hand.

Personality

Angela Walsh's personality was not explored but what little is known about her is that she is shy, funny but above all a quiet girl who worries about her older brother and his nightmares. She loves to play, paint and take care of her birds.

Physical appearance

Angela Walsh is introduced as a blonde-haired, fair-skinned girl with gray-green eyes.

Relationships

Jesse Walsh

She is the only one in the family who does not judge Jesse at any time, always seeks to understand him and worries about the yelling in the morning.

Other media

Jesse's Lost Journal

While the events depicted in Jesse's Lost Journal are not part of the series timeline or canon , Mark Patton (who played Jesse Walsh in A Nightmare On Elm Street 2: Freddy's Revenge) wrote a string of events that he believes could go down in history as "what really happened." Following the timeline of the film series, the events we see in Freddy's Revenge take place in 1985, while the events described in Jesse's diary are mentioned as occurring in 1982 and 1985.

Trivia

  • Angela is one of the few survivors of Freddy Krueger , having survived him twice.
