This is about the Springwood 5th Precinct Police Station of the A Nightmare on Elm Street film series.

The Springwood 5th Precinct Police Station is the headquarters of the 5th Precinct of the Springwood Police Department . It has "5th Precinct" written on the front. Presumably, Springwood has at least four other police stations. Presumably, they have their precinct number written on their fronts, too.

It appears to have at least two floors: A floor partially above ground and partially underground and a floor entirely above ground.

Rod Lane was incarcerated and killed by Freddy Krueger there.
