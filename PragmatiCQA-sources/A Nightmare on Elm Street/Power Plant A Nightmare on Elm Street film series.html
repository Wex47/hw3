The Power Plant is a location in Springwood .

Freddy Krueger worked there before he was arrested.

It has various other buildings all around it.

It has train tracks next to it.

It includes the Boiler Room and a hallway next to it. Much of its interior has never been shown, either in the physical world or the Dream World.

It's physical world version was first shown in A Nightmare on Elm Street 2: Freddy's Revenge .

The Dream World version of it's Boiler Room and the hallway next to it were first shown in A Nightmare on Elm Street , though its exterior wasn't shown. Parts of its interior obviously weren't shown, either.
