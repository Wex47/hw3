It's My Party and You'll Die If I Want You To is the 44th and final episode of Freddy's Nightmares: A Nightmare on Elm Street: The Series overall and the 22nd and final episode of season two .

Plot

After Mara Ruleen , a fake psychic, goes into a trance, Freddy Krueger possesses her body and starts killing people. Later her friend Berry Dean tries to exorcise Freddy out of her, but ends up being possessed himself.

In the second story, Freddy crashes his class reunion to seek revenge on Denise Charlotte who stood him up at the prom. He ends up killing the whole class and Larry Delson who tries to write a screenplay about him. Only person left to live is his former friend Howard Nehamkin , who takes the script and takes it to Hollywood.

Notes

  • According to this episode, the version of Freddy Krueger in Freddy's Nightmares: A Nightmare on Elm Street: The Series graduated Springwood High School in 1970. The version of Freddy Krueger in the A Nightmare on Elm Street film series was supposed to have been burned to death in 1968.
  • It is straight out stated in this episode that Freddy Krueger molested at least some of his pre-death victims.
  • According to information provided in this episode, this episode takes place in 1990.
