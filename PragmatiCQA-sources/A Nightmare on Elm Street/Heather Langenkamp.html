Heather Langenkamp is an actress who appears in the first and third films of the A Nightmare on Elm Street (film series) series as Nancy Thompson and also plays in Wes Craven's New Nightmare . She also hosted and narrated Never Sleep Again: The Elm Street Legacy in 2010. She was a main cast member on the ABC sitcom Just the Ten of Us (1988-1990) which also featured fellow Nightmare cast members Brooke Theiss and JoAnn Willette.

In 2012, she acted in Star Trek Into Darkness and she is a makeup artist for the FX series "American Horror Story".
