Danny Hassel (born December 7, 1967) is an American actor who is perhaps best known for his role as Dan Jordan in the 1988 hit horror film The Dream Master and in the 1989 sequel The Dream Child .

Hassel also made guest appearances on television shows such as Simon & Simon , Murder, She Wrote , and Houston Knights . Hassel enlisted in the U.S. Army after the World Trade Center attacks on September 11, 2001.
