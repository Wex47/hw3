Jackie Earle Haley , born Jack E. Haley , is an American actor known for playing the role of Freddy Krueger in the 2010 A Nightmare on Elm Street remake. He is the son of Haven Earle "Bud" Haley, a radio show host/disc jockey and actor. His first marriage was to Sherry Vaughan in 1979. He has two children: Christopher (born 1986) with his second wife and Olivia (born 1998) from a previous relationship. He married his third wife, Amelia Cruz, in 2004. He resides in San Antonio, Texas.
