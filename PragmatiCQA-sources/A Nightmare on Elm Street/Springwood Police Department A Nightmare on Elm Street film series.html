The Springwood Police Department is the Police Department of Springwood .

It has at least five precincts. Logically, each would have its own police station. The Springwood 5th Precinct Police Station is shown in A Nightmare on Elm Street (1984 film) .

Donald Thompson , Sergeant Parker , and Sergeant Garcia were members at the time of A Nightmare on Elm Street (1984 film).

It shouldn't be confused with the Springwood County Sheriff's Department .
