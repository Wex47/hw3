Plot

Stafford was a security guard who worked at the Westin Hills Psychiatric Hospital in Westin Hills, Ohio. In addition to routine patrols, Stafford was also tasked with monitoring the hospital's surveillance systems and reader card systems.

One night, he noticed someone accessing an entrance to the hospital using a stolen I.D. badge. He left the security command center to investigate.

While patrolling a corridor, Stafford heard a loud banging coming from another room and drew his sidearm. As he approached the steel door, he could tell that something from the other side was trying to tear the door off its hinges.

He pulled out his gun then masked serial killer Jason Voorhees pushed the door off its hinges onto Stafford, crushing him to death.

Notes & Trivia

  • The character of Stafford was created by director Ronny Yu and screenwriters Damian Shannon and Mark Swift.
  • In the film, Stafford was credited only as Asylum guard. His last name is revealed on his name tag.
  • Stafford had tattoos on his right bicep, but it is unclear what they were tattoos of.
  • Stafford is the twentieth character death presented in Freddy vs. Jason. This includes deaths that take place in a dream, revealed in flashback or referenced by other characters. He is the thirteenth actual character murdered by Jason Voorhees in the film. He is the sixteenth total character killed by Jason if one includes three characters that he killed in a dream sequence. He is the twelfth male character killed in the film.
