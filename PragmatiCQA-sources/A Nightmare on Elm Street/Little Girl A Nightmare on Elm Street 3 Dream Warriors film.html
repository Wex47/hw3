This article is about a character credited simply as Little Girl in the closing credits of A Nightmare on Elm Street 3: Dream Warriors . She is probably a separate character from the character credited as "Little Girl" in the closing credits of A Nightmare on Elm Street 4: The Dream Master , even though they are both played by Kristen Clayton.

Biography

She is seen riding a tricycle in Kristen Parker 's first dream in A Nightmare on Elm Street 3: Dream Warriors.

It can be determined from her own words to Kristen Parker that she was one of Freddy Krueger 's pre-death victims.

What Kristen Parker and the audience sees of her is presumably her spirit.

No information has been given about her actual name or her life.
