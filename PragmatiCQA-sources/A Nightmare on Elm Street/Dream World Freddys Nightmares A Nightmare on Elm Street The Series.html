This is about the Dream World of Freddy's Nightmares: A Nightmare on Elm Street: The Series. If you are looking for one or more other versions of the Dream World, please check the Dream World (disambiguation) .

The Dream World is a dimension parallel to the physical world.

It is even more vaguely defined in Freddy's Nightmares: A Nightmare on Elm Street: The Series than it is in the A Nightmare on Elm Street film series.

In the episode Dreams That Kill , it is pretty clearly shown to interact with peoples' physical brains.

In the episode The End of the World , it is shown to have a lot of power over the physical world and that people with certain psychic powers can have a lot of power over the physical world through using it.
