Freddy's Nightmares: A Nightmare on Elm Street: The Series Season Two

The second season of Freddy's Nightmares: A Nightmare on Elm Street: The Series debuted on October 2, 1989 with the episode " Dream Come True ." It had a total of 22 episodes, concluding on March 12, 1990 with " It's My Party and You'll Die If I Want You To ." This was also the final season of the show.
