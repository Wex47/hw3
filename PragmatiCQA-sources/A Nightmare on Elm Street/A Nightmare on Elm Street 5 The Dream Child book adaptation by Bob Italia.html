A Nightmare on Elm Street 5: The Dream Child is the fifth book in A Nightmare on Elm Street (book adaptations of the film series by Bob Italia) .

It is a book adaptation of A Nightmare on Elm Street 5: The Dream Child by Bob Italia.

It shouldn't be confused with the novelization .

Differences from the film

Alice doesn't take a shower.

The man implied to be Freddy Krueger's father isn't in bed with Alice.
