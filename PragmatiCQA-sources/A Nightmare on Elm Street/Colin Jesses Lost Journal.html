Colin is a character in Jesse's Lost Journal .

Colin is a photographer / artist in charge of art for VOGUE magazine, having his own studio in New York where he does his photo shoot.

Story

While the events depicted in Jesse's Lost Journal are not part of the series timeline or canon , Mark Patton (who played Jesse Walsh in A Nightmare On Elm Street 2: Freddy's Revenge) wrote a string of events that he believes could go down in history as "what really happened." Following the timeline of the film series, the events we see in Freddy's Revenge take place in 1985, while the events described in Jesse's diary are mentioned as occurring in 1982 and 1985.

Personality

Colin's personality is not fully explored in the journal entries, however, it could be said that he is compassionate and loving. He is very devoted to art and his work in general, he does not allow himself to be stressed by it and always shows a tranquility that characterizes him.

Physical appearance

There was no physical description of the character. It is simply mentioned that he is similar to Ron Grady.

Relationships

Jesse Walsh

He always cares about Jesse's well-being, they both met for the love of art, New York and because he reminded him of Ron Grady in terms of his physical appearance. He is affectionate and romantic with him, even seeking to include him in photo shoots and make him feel loved.

Trivia

  • Colin is one of the few original characters alongside James Van Landingham , Henry , Stan and Johnson that Mark Patton wrote for the story.
