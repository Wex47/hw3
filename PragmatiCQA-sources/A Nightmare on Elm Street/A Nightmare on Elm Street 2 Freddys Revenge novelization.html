A Nightmare on Elm Street 2: Freddy's Revenge is the 1987 novelization of the film of the same name .

It is based on the screenplay .

It shouldn't be confused with the book adaptation by Bob Italia .

It is a part of the book The Nightmares on Elm Street Parts 1, 2, 3: The Continuing Story .

Plot

  • It is stated that Jesse Walsh previously lived in an urban environment, and is unused to a suburban environment.
  • Lisa is Lisa Poletti instead of Lisa Webber.
  • It is stated that the parakeets are named Mr. Blue and Mr. Green.
  • Driving out to the beach is mentioned. (It was also mentioned in the screenplay and book adaptation by Bob Italia.)
  • Shirley(?) says to Ken, "Blow it out your ass, Ken!".
  • In the end scene, Freddy's gloved hand comes out of Lisa's chest, instead of Kerry's.
