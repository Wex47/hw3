John Patrick Lowrie is an Hawaiian-American voice actor who provided the voice for Odessa Cubbage and the male Citizens for Half-Life 2 and Half-Life 2: Episode One . For the Half-Life 2: Episode Two Citizens, he shares the credits with Adam Baldwin .

He is also the voice actor for the Sniper in Team Fortress 2 , the house radio in Left 4 Dead , Harold Keegan in F.E.A.R. 2: Project Origin , and Police Commissioner Kinsler in the audio drama Sadie's Story , included in Halo 3: ODST . Since 1999 he has been voicing Sherlock Holmes for Jim French's Imagination Theater.

He currently lives in Seattle with his wife Ellen McLain , who also performs voice acting for Valve .
