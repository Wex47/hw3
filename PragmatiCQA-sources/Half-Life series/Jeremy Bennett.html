Biography

Bennett is originally from Weta where he has been the Conceptual Designer for King Kong , the Visual Effects Art Director for The Lord of the Rings trilogy and Conceptual Artist for The Water Horse .

Bennett was awarded the Visual Effects Societies award for "Best Art Direction in a Motion Picture" for The Two Towers along with Alan Lee, Christian Rivers and Gino Acevedo.

For the Half-Life series, Bennett created concept art for Portal , Half-Life 2: Episode Two and Half-Life 2: Episode Three , one of them with Ted Backman and Tristan Reidford . Two of his Episode Two and Episode Three works were selected for the 2007 and 2008 Into the Pixel Collections .

He also made art for a story titled "The Perfect Wave", by his co-worker Marc Laidlaw .

He designed most of the Left 4 Dead and Left 4 Dead 2 areas.
