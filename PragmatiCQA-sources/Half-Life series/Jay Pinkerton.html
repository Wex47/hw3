Jay Pinkerton (born June 15, 1977) is a nationally published Canadian humorist hired by Valve to co-write Portal 2 ’s plot with Erik Wolpaw and Chet Faliszek .

Biography

Pinkerton is a former editor of both Cracked.com and Cracked magazine. Prior to joining Cracked , he served as the managing editor of NationalLampoon.com , the website of the national comedy and film brand. Since joining Cracked , Pinkerton has helped make Cracked.com a leading comedy site, including by bringing in new contributors.
