On its Xbox 360 release, The Orange Box received 99 achievements across all five games on the disc, worth 1000 gamerscore points.

On their release, the Steam versions of Half-Life 2: Episode Two , Portal and Team Fortress 2 all featured achievements, while Half-Life 2 and Half-Life 2: Episode One did not until the first days of the Steam UI Beta, when all of their respective achievements were added to the games' library and store pages on May 26th, 2010. The PlayStation 3 version has no equivalent trophies.
