Drill Instructor Sharpe is a Marine Drill Instructor stationed in Santego Military Base , Arizona. He introduces the PCV to Adrian Shephard and teaches him several training moves from surviving environmental hazards, night vision and firing a Desert Eagle to commanding a squad and using a radio.
