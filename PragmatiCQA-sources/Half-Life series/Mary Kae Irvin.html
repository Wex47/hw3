Mary Kae Irvin Lindsey is an American actress who voiced the female Rebels and Citizens in Half-Life 2 and its episodes.

Biography

She studied at the University of Washington where she earned her Master of Fine Arts in the Professional Actor Training Program. She is competent in Russian, Swedish dialects as well as Standard British, Irish and Cockney. She can sing (alto), she plays the flute and is a wine educator specializing in NW wines. She's classically trained and has played many stage roles, provided many voice-overs for the radio and television, was in many commercials, films, TV series, corporate videos and CD-ROMs. She also voiced Civilians in the video game Half-Life 2 and Halo 3 , and the Station AI in the opening tram sequence of Gunman Chronicles (which also featured fellow Half-Life 2 voice actor Jim French ). She is married to Kelland Lindsey and currently lives in Seattle.
