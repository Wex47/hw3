Bill Van Buren is a producer and designer working at Valve .

Biography

On Valve 's official website, his function is described as follows: "Like a guest at the dinner party in Bunuel's "The Exterminating Angel," Bill signed on for a short stint to help ship Half-Life and found himself unable to leave. Bills pre-Valve experience includes positions at Starwave and Microsoft where he was responsible for art and design direction, production management, and creating art, animation, and the odd bit of music."
