Kristy Junio Pitchford (also known as "Kristie" or "Christie" ) is a writer and game developer for Gearbox Software . With Stephen Bahl and Rob Heironimus , she co-wrote the story for Half-Life: Opposing Force . She is married to Gearbox's Randy Pitchford .
