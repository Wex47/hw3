The Hazardous Environment Combat Unit Sentry Gun , commonly referred to as the HECU Sentry Gun , is a fully autonomous weapon used by the HECU , and Black Ops , to guard areas of strategic value.

Overview

The sentry gun consists of a powerful machine gun built onto a tripod, mounted on a turntable unit allowing it a 360 degree field of view, normally accompanied by one or more laser sensors. Once an enemy has tripped the laser beam, was detected automatically, fired close to or simply damaged it the Sentry Gun activates, emitting a high pitched whine, and shooting at the sighted target. Once alerted, the sentry will remain in this state for a short time, making a steady beeping sound as it scans for targets, before turning back into its standby mode.

Despite its comical appearance, these guns are in fact rather resilient to gunfire and difficult to knock over, but any sufficient type of damage will knock them over, with explosives like grenades being the most effective tactic, though this can be hard to use.

Gordon Freeman encounters a Sentry Gun for the first time after reaching the High Security Storage Facility at Sector D. They were left behind by the HECU to guard areas after they hav entered the facility. Some of these guns relied upon an independent laser sensor to detect targets while others were deployed, without one.

Tactics

Sentry Guns are powerful enemies but they do not move at all. When encountering one, stepping back and considering tactics is advised. Looking for a way around it or destroying it with explosives is a good move.

Sentry Guns simply fall over once they have taken a set amount of damage but can take a considerable amount of damage so opt for hard-hitting attacks such as: a well-placed Colt Python shot, a Shotgun double-blast, or explosives like: Grenades , Satchel Charges , or the RPG .

Avoiding their laser sensors eliminates the threat entirely.

Trivia

  • Unlike the ceiling counterpart , these Sentry Guns do not go into standby mode and retract into its body if there are no targets in range. The only way to disable the turret is through the use of explosives, gunfire or a crowbar , as they do not have switches to deactivate them.
  • The HECU Sentry Gun shares its firing sounds with the MP5. In fact, if the HD Pack is installed, the M4 Carbine sounds are used for the sentry gun.
