The Qidan Clan (pronounced " chee-DAHN " and sometimes spelled as " Qidon ") is a clan seen in Kung Fu Panda: Legends of Awesomeness . It consists of strong water buffalo warriors and are led by their king, Temutai . Their first appearance was in " The Princess and the Po ", where Po (with the assistance of Tigress and Mantis ) was assigned to escort an emperor's daughter on a peacemaking visit to the clan. They have since appeared in other episodes alongside appearances by Temutai.

Trivia

  • The clan's name may be a reference to ancient China's Khitan ( traditional and simplified Chinese : 契丹 ; pinyin : Qìdān ), a group of nomadic Mongolic people originating from Mongolia and the northeastern region of modern-day China.
  • Temutai's sons Chulun and Bataar are likely named in reference to the capital city of Mongolia. "Ulaanbaatar"
