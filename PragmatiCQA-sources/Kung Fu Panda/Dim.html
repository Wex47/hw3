Dim is a resident of the secret panda village in Kung Fu Panda 3 . He is the twin brother of Sum and a cousin of Po . He is voiced by Willie Geist .

Biography

In Kung Fu Panda 3

When Li returns to the Panda Village with Po, Dim and his brother rush to greet their uncle. Li then introduced the two to their cousin who they are excited to meet as Po is since he has more family he didn't know about.

Personality

Not much was shown of his or his brother's personalities but they seem friendly, playful, easygoing, and somewhat lazy.

Fighting style and abilities

He and his brother Sum can sling themselves into the sky by using their hammocks as slingshots and can hit their target at precise range, thanks to Po's training that allowed them to hit their target at a better accuracy.

Trivia

  • Dim and his brother Sum are named after the popular Cantonese dish style dim sum , a serving of food containing small bite-sized portions served traditionally in small steamer baskets or on small plates. The dim sum style of dining is used with many various dishes, but is most popularly prepared with steamed buns, dumplings, and rice noodle rolls.
