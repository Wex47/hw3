Li's hut is the living quarters of Li , located in Panda Village . It was featured in Kung Fu Panda 3 , where Li showed his son Po his small shrine dedicated to his deceased wife , and later where Mr. Ping helped to reconcile Li after his argument with Po.

Description

The rustic design of the hut, as well as its placement perched atop a mountain peak, was inspired by "little pagodas" made to be "hiking stops in China". These pagodas, according to Kung Fu Panda 3 production designer Raymond Zibach, were "made out of rough tree branches, some of which are built right into the trees."
