History

In The Paws of Destiny

One thousand years before the event which occurs in Paws of Destiny, the original four constellations hid the wellspring onto the peach statue in order for Jindiao wouldn't be able to use the energy from the wellspring and become godlike. The wellspring can only be open through certain moves which are used to hide it. It is said that only the power from the wellspring itself is powerful enough to trap Jindiao.
