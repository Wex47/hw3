Tigress' parents are tigers and the biological parents of Tigress . Their names were never mentioned. Their only appearance so far was in a flashback sequence from Kung Fu Panda: Legends of Awesomeness .

History

In Legends of Awesomeness

In the episode " Kung Fu Day Care ", Tigress had a flashback of the night she was left on the doorstep of the Bao Gu Orphanage as a baby girl. It remains unknown why her parents abandoned her at the orphanage.

With each other

Tigress’ parents evidently loved each other, as is shown by their being together and having a child. They also stood close together throughout the act of leaving Tigress at Bao Gu.

Tigress

Tigress’ parents seem to have loved her, but apparently felt that they either could not keep her or would be unable to give her a good life if they did. As such, they chose to leave her at Bao Gu to be cared for.
