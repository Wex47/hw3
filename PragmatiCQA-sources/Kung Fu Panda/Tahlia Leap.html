Kung Fu Panda Holiday

The Tahlia Leap is a kung fu technique that involves one running up a vertical surface and somersaulting off of it to perform an offensive attack. It was used in Kung Fu Panda Holiday by Tigress to scale a column and toss several lanterns towards the ceiling.
