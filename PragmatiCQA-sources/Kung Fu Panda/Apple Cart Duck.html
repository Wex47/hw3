Muh Lin (also known as Apple Cart Duck ) is a minor character in the Nickelodeon television series Kung Fu Panda: Legends of Awesomeness . He lives in the Valley of Peace and makes his living by selling apples to its villagers.

Biography

In Legends of Awesomeness

Muh Lin has appeared many times in the show as a joke-cameo. His first appearance was in the episode " Master Ping ", when Mr. Ping gave him a bowl of soup. He later appeared in the episode " Kung Fu Club where he was fighting among members of the Kung Fu Club .

Relationships

Mr. Ping

The exact relationship between Muh Lin and and Mr. Ping is not certain as Muh Lin is a minor cameo character and has not undergone intense character development. Mr. Ping was first seen with Muh Lin in the episode " Master Ping ", where Mr. Ping gave him a bowl of noodles at the Jade Palace and asked how he was doing. It could be inferred that they are friends.

Fighting style

In his time training with Po and Peng and the other club members of the Kung fu fight club, Muh Lin was shown to have learned to be able to develop his own style of kung fu, and was able to physically handle himself in hand to hand combat, as demonstrated when he was able to go up against Tong fo's thugs without sustaining any injuries.

Clothing

Muh Lin has only been known to wear a blue robe with a yellow belt accenting his figure. In " Master Ping ", he wore a small blue hat. No further information has been given regarding the nature of Muh Lin's wardrobe.

Trivia

  • His name is never actually mentioned on-screen and may be retroactively given; while having a mutual conversation in one episode, Mr. Ping simply calls him "Apple Cart Duck".
  • His voice is provided by Peter Hastings , the executive producer of Kung Fu Panda: Legends of Awesomeness .
