Wupo is a character in the Nickelodeon television series Kung Fu Panda: Legends of Awesomeness . She is a traveling fortune teller who sells black magic items on the Moshu Market . She was featured in the episode " Kung Shoes " where she sold her magical Kung Fu Shoes to Po .
