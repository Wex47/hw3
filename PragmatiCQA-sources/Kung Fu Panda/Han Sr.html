Han Sr. is a character in the Nickelodeon television series Kung Fu Panda: Legends of Awesomeness . He is the father of Han who appeared in the episode " Hall of Lame ", where he was kidnapped and ransomed for the dagger hidden in Han's stuffed rabbit doll .
