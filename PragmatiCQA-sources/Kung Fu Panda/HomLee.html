Biography

In Kung Fu Panda 3

Hom-Lee is among the villagers who greets Po upon his arrival at the village. Po is disturbed by his crooked teeth, but manages not to mention this fact.

Later, Mei Mei strikes him in the mouth, inadvertently straightening his teeth. The unexpected change in his appearance attracts Mei Mei, to Hom-Lee’s apparent pleasure.

Personality

At the end, he appears to have an interest in Mei Mei .
