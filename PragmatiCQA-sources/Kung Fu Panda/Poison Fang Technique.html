The Poison Fang is a kung fu technique involving the use of venomous fangs to fell an enemy. It was demonstrated in Secrets of the Furious Five by Great Master Viper , whose venom was deadly enough to fell fifteen gorilla warriors and a mid-sized crocodile (according to Po ).

Description

According to legends, poisons of Viper clans are signs of descending from the fire-breathing Dragon Gods . As demonstrated by Great Master Viper , the victim often gets paralyzed and falls to the ground. The only known users are Great Master Viper and Fu-xi .
