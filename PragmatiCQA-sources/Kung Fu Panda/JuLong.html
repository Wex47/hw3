Personality

Ju-Long is described by Shifu as "deadly and unbalanced". He is crazy, and likes to inflict suffering and chaos simply for fun.

Fighting style

This section needs revising. The content below could use some extra attention. You can help by !

Ju-Long's small size lets him easily evade larger opponents. He also has help from his hundreds of brothers and sisters, the Lao Shu.

He used Po 's hunger against him, offering him food and then attacking him while his guard was down.

He uses a conveniently-sized mallet for a weapon, and likes to use explosives.
