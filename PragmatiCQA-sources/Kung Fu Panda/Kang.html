Kang Wolf (or simply Kang ) is the main antagonist in the theme park attraction Kung Fu Panda: The Emperor's Quest . He is the leader of a pack of river pirate wolves . When he learns about Po 's mission to deliver the Liquid of Ultimate Power to the Emperor , he and his pack attempt to stop him and obtain the bottle of liquid.
