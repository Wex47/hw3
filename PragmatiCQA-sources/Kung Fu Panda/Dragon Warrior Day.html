Dragon Warrior Day is a celebration held in honor of the Dragon Warrior by the citizens of the Valley of Peace .

In the episode " Rhino's Revenge " from Kung Fu Panda: Legends of Awesomeness , decorations in the likeness of Po were displayed throughout the streets, one of which was a large statue. Though unconfirmed, it is possible that Dragon Warrior Day is held either on the day Po was chosen as the Dragon Warrior , or the day that he defeated Tai Lung .
