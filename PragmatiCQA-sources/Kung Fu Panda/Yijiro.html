Yijiro ( as Yojiro ) is a character in the Nickelodeon television series Kung Fu Panda: Legends of Awesomeness . He is a prawn and a highly trained samurai warrior of the Ishida Clan . He made his first appearance in the episode " The Way of the Prawn ", where he aided Po and the others in stopping Kira from taking over China.
