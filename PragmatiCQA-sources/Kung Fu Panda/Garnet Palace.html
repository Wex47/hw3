The Garnet Palace is a large palace that serves as the home of Mistress Mugan and her servants—including her former student, Wu Yong . Viper mentioned to Po that the Garnet Palace is built like a fortress. It was featured in the episode " A Tigress Tale " from Kung Fu Panda: Legends of Awesomeness .
