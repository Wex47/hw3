At least Master Chicken has high physical potential as a kung fu master. He was seen to possess strength to hold high Master Bear with one wing, and stamina and speed enough to keep up with other four-legged masters in spite of being two, short-legged. He also equips blades on wings and ankles.

He does not seem to be able to fly or glide with his small wings as being seen that he in jombie state directly fall after being launched by the Dragon Warrior 's kick.
