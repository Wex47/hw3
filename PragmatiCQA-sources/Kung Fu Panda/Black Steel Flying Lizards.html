The Black Steel Flying Lizards are a group of Chinese flying lizards from the Cliffs of Anguish who presumably used steel as an alloy for their black armor.

History

Nothing else is known about this group of lizards other than the legend that tells how they were once a great burden for the Chih Kuan Crane Clan seven hundred years prior to the events in Kung Fu Panda . However, they were soon defeated when the Crane clan received help from Master Oogway . It is unknown what he actually did to help the Crane clan achieve victory, but it is noted to have been a great battle named the Battle of Burning Sky , taken place at the Hour of the Rat .
