The Yaoguai (pronounced " yow-GWAHY ") is the name of an ancient group of creatures who seek to return the universe to chaos. They first appeared in the Kung Fu Panda: Legends of Awesomeness episode " Eternal Chord " where they fought against Po , Shifu , and Lun for possession of the Wu Di instruments in hopes to accomplish their goal.
