The tortoise shell restraint was a tortoise-shell-shaped locking device created by Oogway to contain Tai Lung after his attack on the Valley of Peace . It was worn by the snow leopard during his twenty years of incarceration in Chorh-Gom Prison , and later destroyed during his escape in Kung Fu Panda .

Description

The device appeared to be made of a tortoise shell and contained several acupuncture needles with decorative dragon-headed pins held on the back. With the restraint worn like a jacket, these needles could target areas of the body, blocking the chi of whoever wore it and immobilizing them. In the center of the shell was a zig-zag-shaped lock, which kept the needles in place and the shell itself whole.
