The Silken Moon Touch is a kung fu technique that was devised and created by Master Wushen after, at Oogway 's suggestion, took a vow of silence to improve his concentration and training. Eventually, he created this technique, which requires immense focus, enabling him to quite literally blow away an opponent or a group of enemies with a single touch, which causes a temporary after-effect of turning day into night once it's successfully used.

Description

The user, when performing the move, uses multiple hand stances before moving their finger forward. If the finger connects to a target, it creates a rippling wave of blue energy that temporarily darkens the sky before returning to normal.
