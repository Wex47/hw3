Sanzu is a character from the Nickelodeon television series Kung Fu Panda: Legends of Awesomeness . He served as the main antagonist in the episode " Po Picks a Pocket ", where he housed and organized a group of orphans to steal money and valuable goods from the Valley of Peace villagers.
