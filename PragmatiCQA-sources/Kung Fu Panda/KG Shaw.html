Biography

In Kung Fu Panda

As KG Shaw and his brother JR Shaw made their way to the Jade Palace for the Dragon Warrior Tournament , they passed by a distressed Po , who was struggling to bring his father 's noodle cart up the long staircase. Seeing his plight and predicting he wouldn't be able to make it to the tournament in time, the two brothers sympathized with him briefly. KG Shaw patted him on the back and told him sorry, and after his brother offered to get him a souvenir, they continued their way up the stairs.

Trivia

  • The "KG" in his name are the initials of his voice actor, Kyle Gass , who is the other half of Jack Black 's band Tenacious D .
  • KG Shaw and his brother JR Shaw were named in homage to the Shaw Brothers Studio , known for producing many of the Kung Fu films from the 1970s.
