Tsao is a character in the Nickelodeon television series Kung Fu Panda: Legends of Awesomeness . He is a pig who is associated with two other pigs, Bao and Lao . He and his comrades first appeared in " Master Ping ", where they sought to learn the secret moves of kung fu .
