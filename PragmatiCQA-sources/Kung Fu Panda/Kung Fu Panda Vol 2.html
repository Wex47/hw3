Synopsis

The second volume of the Kung Fu Panda graphic novel series, featuring all the lovable characters from the Kung Fu Panda franchise , including Po and the Furious Five ( Tigress , Viper , Crane , Mantis and Monkey )!

The Valley of Peace is rocked by a raging elemental storm that seems to have a life of its own. Po is just about capable of fighting anyone - but how can he battle the elements? And is a mystery villain behind the stormy situation?
