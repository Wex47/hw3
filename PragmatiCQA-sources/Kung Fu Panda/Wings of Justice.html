" Wings of Justice " is a kung fu technique that involves an avian warrior using their wings to create a powerful gust of wind. Crane performed this technique in Kung Fu Panda 2 when he used it to blow a pair of ships into the Gongmen Harbor gates and block the exit for Shen 's fleet .
