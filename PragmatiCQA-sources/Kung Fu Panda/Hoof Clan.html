Kung Fu Panda: Legendary Warriors

The Hoof Clan is a group of villains from Kung Fu Panda: Legendary Warriors . It consists of Great General Ox and Yak .

They are among the villains assembled by Tai Lung to help him in his revenge against Po and the Furious Five .

The Hoof Clan ended up capturing a rabbit villager from the Valley of Peace . Once the Hoof Clan were defeated, the rabbit villager was rescued.
