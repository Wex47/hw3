" Levitation " is a mysterious kung fu technique that allows its user the ability to float in mid-air. The Furious Five (sans Crane ) learned it while they were away in the Kung Fu Panda: Legends of Awesomeness episode " In With the Old ", and were later able to fly in the air with ease. It is also used by General Tsin in the end of " See No Weevil ".
