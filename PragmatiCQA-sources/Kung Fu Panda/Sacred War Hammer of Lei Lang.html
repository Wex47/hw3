The Sacred War Hammer of Lei Lang is a weapon of mass destruction that causes objects to explode when in contact with the hammer. It was featured in the Kung Fu Panda: Legends of Awesomeness episode " Jailhouse Panda ".

Description

The hammer has a head-heavy design that resembles a fist. It also can obliterate any buildings, hills, mountains and villages with just one slam in the ground. When it was thrown or dropped it can explode like a nuke.

History

Background

Shifu briefly mentioned that Tong Fo had used it in the past to cause abomination to the Valley of Peace . After he was defeated by the Furious Five , the hammer was kept in the Jade Palace for safekeeping. Unfortunately for them, Shifu soon uncovered that the hammer kept in the palace was a fake, and concluded that Tong Fo may have hid it somewhere before justice got to him.
