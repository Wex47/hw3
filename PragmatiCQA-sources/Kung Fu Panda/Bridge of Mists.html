Description

The Bridge of Mists is a set of two small rock islands between two cliffs, forming an incomplete bridge across the misty abyss below. The bridge is clouded by thick mist, hence its name.

History

In Secrets of the Masters

The Bridge of Mists was seen when Oogway led Rhino , Ox and Croc towards the Hubei Volcano to defeat the Wu Sisters . The Bridge appeared impassable to the three until Oogway revealed that only by working together could they manage to cross. When the others expressed doubt, Oogway promptly made his point by tossing all three fighters until they were propped between the gaps in the bridge. Ooway then used them to cross the bridge himself, helping Rhino up as he went. Rhino then helped up Croc as he followed Oogway, and Croc in turn pulled up Ox until all four were on the other side. The experience served to teach Rhino, Ox and Croc about the importance of teamwork, just as Oogway intended. The Bridge was one of the last hazards the group encountered before reaching Wu Fortress .
