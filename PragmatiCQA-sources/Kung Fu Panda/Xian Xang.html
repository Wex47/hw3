Xian Xang (referred to simply as "the doll ") is the name given to a stuffed rabbit doll seen in the episode " Hall of Lame " from Kung Fu Panda: Legends of Awesomeness . It was presumably crafted by Han Sr. , who used the doll to hide inside it a mystical dagger that he wanted kept away from Jong . The doll was given to his son Han , thinking it would be the last place anyone would look for the powerful weapon, but its true purpose was eventually discovered. Thanks to Po 's efforts, the doll was rescued from being lost like the dagger, and was used as a trophy at Po's Warrior Ceremony .
