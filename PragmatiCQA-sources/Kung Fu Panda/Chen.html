Chen is a character from the Nickelodeon television series Kung Fu Panda: Legends of Awesomeness . He is a young goat child who is currently one of the three members of Po 's fan club . He made his first appearance in " Po Fans Out " where he, Lam and Yang met Po and watched him fight the Mongolian Fist Demon .
