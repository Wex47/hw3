Nu Hai is a young panda and twin sister to Bao . She, her brother, and two other panda children ( Jing and Fan Tong ) are the protagonists of the Amazon Prime series Kung Fu Panda: The Paws of Destiny .

Biography

In The Paws of Destiny

Nu Hai is first introduced to be living with her brother, Bao , and her Grandmother . She is initially shown to be eager for adventure, but is shown to be a bit over her head.

Nu Hai is one of the four pandas who absorbed the chi of the Four Constellations upon accidentally stumbling upon a cave beneath the Panda Village . There, she absorbed the chi of the Blue Dragon , who was the leader of the original Four Constellations. Initially, she has trouble controlling her chi, but by learning to slow down under pressure, she was able to fully master her abilities and is now a skilled warrior and leader.

Personality

... the four kids [are] led by Nu Hai, the energetic leader of the group. Nu Hai harbors big dreams of being a kung fu master, and what she lacks in skill she makes up for in heart and determination. Her admiration of Po borders on obsession, and she helps the group stay focused throughout their training. By absorbing the chi of Blue Dragon , Nu Hai is able to relax and hone in on her power.

Nu Hai is the strong-willed leader of the group. She may be a bit bossy, but only because she's trying to keep the rest of the pandas focused on their mission to save the world. Just like Po, she is all heart and grit when she’s determined.
