The Paralyzing Touch Point is a kung fu technique used in the Kung Fu Panda: Legends of Awesomeness episode " The Maltese Mantis " where it was accidentally used by Po and later undone by Shifu.

Description

Similar to the nerve attack , by jabbing various pressure points that block the person's chi the technique will cause the target to become paralyzed. If after one hour the technique is not undone, the paralysis will become permanent. The victim is also unable to talk but somehow move their eyes. The only was to unparalyzed the victim is to do the move over again.
