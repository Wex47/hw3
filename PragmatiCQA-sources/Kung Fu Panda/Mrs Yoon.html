Personality

Mrs. Yoon has been shown to be kind, always wishing the best for Po and Mr. Ping . For example, she came to the Peach Tree of Heavenly Wisdom to see Po with concern about Mr. Ping's whereabouts when the goose had gone missing in " Master Ping ". She also told Po to give her best to his father after trying to defeat him to become the Dragon Warrior in " Challenge Day ". She is also shown to to be obsessed with Monkey.
