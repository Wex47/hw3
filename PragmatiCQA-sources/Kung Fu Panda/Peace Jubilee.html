The Peace Jubilee is an event held in the Valley of Peace that celebrates the spirit of peace and cooperation, in which fighting is to be avoided even amongst those who are enemies. The celebration involves ceremonies, holding kung fu tournaments, among other activities traditional to the event. It took place in the episode " The Kung Fu Kid " from Kung Fu Panda: Legends of Awesomeness .
