The Emperor's Palace is the residence of the Imperial family , most notably the Emperor (title) . It was first featured in Kung Fu Panda: Tales of Po , later in the Nickelodeon series Kung Fu Panda: Legends of Awesomeness , and most recently in the theme park attraction Kung Fu Panda: The Emperor's Quest .
