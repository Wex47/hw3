Biography

As a venerated warrior, Hippo was well recognized enough for his statue to be situated at the Master Garden , along with other famous masters. His statue, standing between Master Lizard and Master Goat , was seen to dress in the vestments of a monk, with prayer beads around his neck, and a Shaolin spade.
