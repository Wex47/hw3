Lippy is a character from the Nickelodeon television series Kung Fu Panda: Legends of Awesomeness . He was one of the orphans featured in the episode " Po Picks a Pocket ", where he and his fellow orphans craftily stole money and valuable goods from the Valley of Peace villagers for their greedy caretaker, Sanzu .
