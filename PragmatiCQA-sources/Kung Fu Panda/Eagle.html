Master Eagle is a character briefly mentioned by Po in Secrets of the Masters , and by Shifu in Kung Fu Panda 3 . He/she is a credited master of kung fu .

Biography

Master Eagle was briefly mentioned in Secrets of the Masters . According to Po , Master Eagle once owned the Blade of Bao Ding , which he/she had used to defeat the Hyenas of Handan .

In Kung Fu Panda 3 , Master Eagle was again mentioned, being one of the masters who sent word to Shifu at the Jade Palace after presumably falling prey to Kai and his jombies .
