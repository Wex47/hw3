The Chao Wa Punch Kick is a kung fu technique used in the Kung Fu Panda: Legends of Awesomeness episode " Master Ping ". It was first shown by Shifu as he demonstrated it to Po and the Furious Five by using it on the Adversary .

Description

One strike of the Chao Wa Punch Kick in the pressure points causes immobilization of the body, meaning that it is possibly a nerve attack technique. It possible that the technique isn’t permanent and it eventually wears off, as Bao, Tsao, and Lao were able to move again in their next appearance. According to Shifu, this technique came from the sacred scrolls , as shown in the episode " Master Ping ".

History

In Legends of Awesomeness

Fighting exhaustion resulting from having Mr. Ping as a houseguest, Shifu explains the move to a similarly tired Po and the Furious Five before performing it. Mr. Ping watches but is unimpressed. Later, after being captured by Bao and his gang due to being mistaken for Shifu, Mr. Ping tries to stall them by saying he will teach them the punch kick. He is unexpectedly able to perform the move perfectly, and further horrified as Bao anticipates using it on Po. After stalling the confrontation for as long as possible, he reveals his blunder to Po. Fortunately, his unexpected kick paralyzed one pig, and Bao accidentally immobilize his other comrade before being struck by another move from Ping.
