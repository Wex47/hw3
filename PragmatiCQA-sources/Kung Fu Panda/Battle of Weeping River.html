The Battle of Weeping River was mentioned briefly in Kung Fu Panda by Po in his conversation with Crane .

Summary

No other details were provided about this battle aside from the fact that it involved the Furious Five , who "were outnumbered a thousand to one," according to Po. This detail indicates an enemy count of at least 5,000.

It is uncertain whether or not the Five won the battle, but it can be assumed that they did, as Po frolicked with joy at the idea of his heroes fighting so valiantly.
