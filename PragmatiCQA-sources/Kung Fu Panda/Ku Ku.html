Ku Ku (pronounced " KOO koo ") is a young resident of the secret panda village in Kung Fu Panda 3 . He is the twin triplet of Meng Meng and Shuai Shuai . He is voiced by Knox Jolie-Pitt.

Trivia

  • Ku Ku's name translates from Chinese to English as "cool" ( traditional and simplified Chinese : 酷酷 ; pinyin : kù kù ).
  • Ku Ku and his siblings are named after the world's only surviving set of panda triplets, who were born July 29, 2014 in China's Chimelong Safari Park where they currently live. They were named in accordance to a prominent trait of the individual panda's personality, which was also incorporated into the film characters.
  • Ku Ku and his siblings are all voiced by the children of Angelina Jolie , voice of Tigress .
