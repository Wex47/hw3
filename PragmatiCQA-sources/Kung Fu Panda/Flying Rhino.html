Biography

In his lifetime

One tale tells of the armor he used in a bloody battle against the warriors of Guangdong Province . Triumphing over the weary battle, his armor still bears the scars of the stealthy lizard assassins' spinning butterfly knives, which were the only weapons of the warriors who were able to get close enough to make a mark.

It is also said that Flying Rhino was the martial arts teacher of Commander Vachir and had personally recommended him to Master Oogway to be the chief in charge of Tai Lung 's incarceration. Flying Rhino mentioned him to be the only one brave enough, strong enough, and ruthless enough for the job.

His son, Thundering Rhino , also studied under him.

In Secrets of the Masters

The canonicity of this section is questionable. Information in this section may conflict with previously established canon.

Flying Rhino made a cameo appearance in the imagination of young Thundering Rhino . After Oogway mentioned to him (as well as Ox and Croc ) of how stopping the Wu Sisters would bring them "riches", Rhino imagined how he would earn the respect of his father by hearing him say, "I'm proud of you, my son."

In Kung Fu Panda

While Po was gawking at all the historical artifacts on display in the Hall of Warriors , he immediately recognized Flying Rhino's armor , commenting on its "authentic battle damage" (referring to the scratches).

Personality

Other than presumably being a brave and heroic figure, Flying Rhino's son also envisioned him as a proud father, as he was shown to be pleased with his son's achievements.

Oogway

Oogway and the Flying Rhino seemed to have an unknown but clearly friendly relationship: The Flying Rhino recommended Vachir to lead the Anvil of Heaven and guard Chorh-Gom Prison and Oogway approved of the Flying Rhino's choice, clearly showing Oogway trusted the Flying Rhino's judgement. Oogway also commented, although slyly, that the Flying Rhino would not be proud of his son even if he tried to win him over with riches and fame and only with honor could the Thundering Rhino earn his father's pride, showing he knew the Flying Rhino well. It was possible that the Flying Rhino may have been Oogway's student.

Clothing

From what is seen of his brief appearance in Thundering Rhino 's mind, Flying Rhino wore a robe with shoulder covers that jutted out to a point, very similar to that of his son's robe in Kung Fu Panda 2 . However, he has not been seen wearing his famous armor at any point in the franchise .
