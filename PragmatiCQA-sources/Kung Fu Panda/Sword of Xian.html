The Sword of Xi'an was a powerful, cursed weapon once used by the demon Xi'an , hence its name. It was seen in the Kung Fu Panda: Legends of Awesomeness episode " The First Five ", where it resurfaced in Mount Huo Shan and had its power used by Master Elephant .
