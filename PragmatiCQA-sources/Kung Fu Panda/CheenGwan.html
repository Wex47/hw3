Cheen-Gwan is a character in the Nickelodeon television series Kung Fu Panda: Legends of Awesomeness . He is a warlord and leader of his clan of gorillas. He appeared in the episode " Shoot the Messenger " where he and his brother sought to declare war on each other's clans.
