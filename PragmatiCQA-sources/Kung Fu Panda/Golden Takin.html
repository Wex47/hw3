Master Golden Takin was a briefly mentioned kung fu master.

Biography

Golden Takin is credited as the constructor of the nine-hundred-year-old Training Hall . So being, he was also the first martial arts instructor, appointed by Master Oogway himself.

Trivia

  • Though it is not directly mentioned, his name specifies him as a golden takin , an endangered goat-antelope native to China .
