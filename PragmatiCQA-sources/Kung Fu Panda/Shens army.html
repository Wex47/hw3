Shen's army is the name given to Lord Shen 's military force, which he used to help bring him to power as ruler of Gongmen City . The army consists of two main species of animals: wolves and gorillas .

In Kung Fu Panda 2

Composed of hundreds of wolves and dozens of gorillas, Lord Shen used his army to occupy Gongmen City after taking it over from the Kung Fu Council . Its metalwork factories were used to churn out large amounts of cannons, which Shen would use during his bid to conquer China. In addition to the foot soldiers and cannons, Shen also built a large fleet of ships that he would use to transport his army from Gongmen City to the rest of China.
