In The Paws of Destiny

The Rooster Brothers first appeared in the episode " Poison in the Pit of Plum " trying to start a fight with Po . The name Rooster Brothers is introduced firstly by Wong as a possible name of their group. In order to avoid the fight, Po lied to them by saying that he isn't Po. When Rooster finds out that he's being lied, they quickly start a fight with Po and his students.
