The Sacred Onyx Council (or the Sacred Onyx Shaolin ) is a group of esteemed kung fu masters in Kung Fu Panda: Legends of Awesomeness . In the episode " Sight for Sore Eyes ", two masters from this council visited the Jade Palace to evaluate Shifu 's qualifications as a teacher, appearing to have the power to determine whether he would continue being master of the palace or not.
