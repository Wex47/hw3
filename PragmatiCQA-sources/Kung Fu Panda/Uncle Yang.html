Uncle Yang wears a red robe with a cloud pattern with black trim, as well as a black belt. He also wears a yellow and dark green hat.

Trivia

  • The exact nature of Uncle Yang's relationship to Po or Mr. Ping has not been explained. As he is a different species from them both, it is possible that he is either a step-brother of Mr. Ping, an adoptee(like Po) or that Po is calling him "Uncle" because he is a close friend of the family. The lattermost would make sense, as in Asian cultures, it is common to address unrelated elders as "Uncle" or "Auntie".
