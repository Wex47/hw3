Ying is a minor character in the Nickelodeon television series Kung Fu Panda: Legends of Awesomeness . He is a grocer who lives in the Valley of Peace and sells various food items for a living. He first appeared in the episode " Huge " where he was robbed by Fung and the Croc Bandits .
