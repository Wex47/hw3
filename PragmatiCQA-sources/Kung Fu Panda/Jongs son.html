Jong's son is a minor character in the Nickelodeon television series Kung Fu Panda: Legends of Awesomeness ; his specific name was never mentioned. He is the young son of Jong , the ruler of Jong's Land . He appeared in " Good Croc, Bad Croc " when he was kidnapped by Fung (with the protection assistance of Po who was unaware of the kidnapping).

Biography

In Legends of Awesomeness

Fung tricked Po into thinking that Fung's brother was captured by Jong so that he can abduct Jong's son. In Jong's fort , Po held off the guards while Fung snuck into Jong's son's room and smuggled the child out in a small box. Po learned this too late when two of Jong's guards came to arrest Po. When Fung met up with his fellow bandits and learned what happened to Po, he felt remorse and decided to return Jong's son. Just as Jong was about to invoke the trespassers penalty on Po, Fung arrived in his Water Buffalo Guard disguise and released Jong's son to him. However, Jong ordered for Po and Fung to be tortured anyway, and Jong's son was last seen blowing a raspberry at the two as his father carried him away.

Personality

Not much is known about Jong's son, but he's shown to be vengeful like his father, as he does nothing to stop him from ordering Po and Fung from getting tortured and killed. He blows a raspberry at Po and Fung, which may indicate that he is very spoiled.
