Legend of the Legendary Warrior is an animated video micro-series developed by DreamWorks Animation to celebrate the theatrical release of Kung Fu Panda . It recounts the adventures of the Legendary Warrior as he encounters various enemies, kung fu legends and historical sites.

Plot

Synopsis

To celebrate the upcoming release of Kung Fu Panda , DreamWorks Animation presents an animated comic book inspired video micro-series entitled the "Legend of the Legendary Warrior." The six-chapter micro-series recounts the tales of The Legendary Warrior , the most awesomeness Kung Fu warrior of all time. Follow the Warrior as he comes face-to-face with deadly adversaries, legendary kung fu heroes and through his travels discovers the birth place of kung fu .
