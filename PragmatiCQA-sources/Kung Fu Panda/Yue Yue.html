Yue Yue (pronounced " YOO-yoo ") is a character from the Nickelodeon television series Kung Fu Panda: Legends of Awesomeness . She was one of the orphans featured in the episode " Po Picks a Pocket ", where she and her fellow orphans craftily stole money and valuable goods from the Valley of Peace villagers for their greedy caretaker, Sanzu .

Biography

In Legends of Awesomeness

Evicted from their orphanage, Yue-Yue and her fellow orphans were left on their own, facing it out in the wilderness and eating nothing but rocks. When they arrived at a village, no one would adopt them. No one… except a Chinese Pangolin named Sanzu. He raised them to steal stuff for him and become rich, while he acted as an innocent bystander.

Fighting style and abilities

Despite her age, Yue-Yue is a force to be reckoned with. She usually uses a slingshot to shoot stones at her foes. After Sandy betrays them and almost kills Shifu unintentionally, Yue-Yue beats the Chinese Pangolin senseless, despite his hard armor.
