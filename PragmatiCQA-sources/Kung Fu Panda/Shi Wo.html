Shi Wo is a character in the Nickelodeon television series Kung Fu Panda: Legends of Awesomeness . He is an actor and the leader of an out-of-work acting troupe . He appeared in the episode " The Real Dragon Warrior " where he and his troupe helped Cheng in his fraudulent charade as the Dragon Warrior . After he was exposed by Po , however, Shi Wo and his troupe was once again out of work and became bandits to obtain money.
