Master Boar is a character who was originally going to be a member of the Kung Fu Council in Kung Fu Panda 2 , but was scrapped before production. He instead made a cameo appearance in the film as well as a featured appearance as a bandit in Kung Fu Panda Holiday .

Biography

In Kung Fu Panda Holiday

A band of boar bandits greatly resembling Master Boar's design invaded the Valley of Peace , but were easily defeated by Po and the Furious Five . Notably, the remaining boar was tossed around by the warriors before Po knocked him out by sitting on his neck.

In Kung Fu Panda 2

When Storming Ox declared that kung fu was dead, an outraged Po delivered a rousing speech that he would stop Shen and prove that "kung fu still lives." In another cell, Boar responded with a sarcastic cheer.
