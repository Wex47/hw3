Biography

In The Paws of Destiny

This section needs revising. The content below could use some extra attention. You can help by !

Shi Long was abandoned as an infant at the Imperial Palace(the identities and fates of her biological parents, not to mention the reason for being abandoned, being unknown), where she was adopted by Emperor Kang Zi and his wife, since at that time his wife was unable to bear a child. For a few years life was perfect for Shi Long, until a miracle happened and she had a sister. The moment her adoptive sister was born, the emperor and empress shifted all their attention to her and ignored Shi Long. In her sadness she met with the White Bone Demon in the underground and made a deal to free the White Bone Demon in exchange that she will rule with her.

As Xiao 's adopted sister, Shi Long had gained her trust. She lied to Xiao about the terracotta warriors, that it was supposed to protect Kang Zi in the afterlife, but in reality, Shi Long used them as soldiers to grow an army. Her plan was to assassinate Po and his students. Shi Long's plan had failed, and she decided to convince Xiao to abdicate the throne. She ended up abdicating, but Po and his students stopped her. Shi Long caged Xiao and went to summon the White Bone Demon . The White Bone Demon was powered a powerful Red Jade heart. Eventually, the demon ended up eating Shi Long.

Fortunately, Shi Long was saved by the efforts of Po and the others. She expressed remorse to Xiao for her previous actions, and was informed that she would be allowed to remain at her sister’s side-albeit in a cage.

Personality

Shi Long is a strange Komodo dragon. She tries to comfort the princess just to make sure she doesn't suspect anything. She becomes serious when it comes to finishing a task. She holds grudges for a long time.

Fighting style and abilities

Shi Long can control her tail, as seen with her throwing Jade Tusk against a wall with it. She has quick reflexes, as seen with her dodging a dagger at a close range in her fight with Xiao.

Relationships

Kang Zi

She loved her adoptive father until her sister was born. Once her sister was born all the attention she had enjoyed was transferred to her sister and led to her being neglected. She secretly was resentful towards him and felt betrayed. He trusted Shi Long as his trusted advisor. She eventually killed him by poisoning him.

The empress

She loved her adoptive mother as well until her sister was born. Like her husband, the empress transferred all her attention to her newborn biological daughter and neglected Xiao. Since Shi Long has never once mentioned her adoptive mother, it can be assumed that she died early, perhaps right after Xiao was born.

Xiao

She once had a good life until Xiao was born, upon which her parents instantly payed attention to her only. She was jealous and resentful towards her sister due to their father favoring her and tried to overthrow her so she can claim the throne for herself.

White Bone Demon

Shi Long found a mysterious object which held the White Bone Demon inside of it. She makes a deal with the demon in exchange that she will rule by her side. After the White Bone Demon managed to get an iron body, she calls the deal off and betrayed Shi Long by putting her into her mouth.

Trivia

  • Shi Long's voice actress, Sumalee Montano , has also voiced all three Wu Sisters in Secrets of the Masters .
  • Shi Long shares some similarities with Jade Tusk, another antagonist from the first arc of season 1.
    • Both served under the leadership of a main antagonist (Jindiao for Jade Tusk and White Bone Demon for Shi Long)
    • Both were betrayed by their former masters and reformed as a result. However, unlike Jade Tusk, who was easily forgiven and not incarcerated in jail, Shi Long was put in jail because of her evil schemes of killing the Empress' father.
