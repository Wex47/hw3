Lam is a character from the Nickelodeon television series Kung Fu Panda: Legends of Awesomeness . She is a young rabbit child who is currently one of the three members of Po 's fan club . She made her first appearance in " Po Fans Out " where she, Chen and Yang met Po and watched him fight the Mongolian Fist Demon .
