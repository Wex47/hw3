Trivia

  • Rooster was introduced to "out" Po and challenge him to fight in a moment where he and the panda kids are trying to fly under the radar. The character and his warthog henchmen ( Wing and Wong ) became so popular with the show writers, especially after the casting of his voice actor Michael Rivkin , they decided to bring him back.
  • Rooster's voice actor, Michael Rivkin, went to school with Po's voice actor Jack Black , and they remain friends to this day. Rivkin's comedy rock band SuperFloss also occasionally tours with Black's comedy rock band Tenacious D .
