History

In Art of Balance

On one occasion in the province's history, severe flooding had endangered the region's crops, prompting Master Storming Ox to come to the people's aid. While taking a break for lunch he was attacked by rat bandits , whose superior numbers gave them a temporary edge. However, Storming Ox's great speed and power won the day, allowing him to drive the rats off, upon which he returned to his labors.
