Kung Fu Panda

" Feet of Fury " is a kung fu technique that involves kicking multiple enemies in mid-air in rapid succession. It has been demonstrated at least two times so far, each time by Po : the first time in Kung Fu Panda in his dream as the Legendary Warrior , and then a second time in Kung Fu Panda 2 during the wolf raid on the Artisan Village .
