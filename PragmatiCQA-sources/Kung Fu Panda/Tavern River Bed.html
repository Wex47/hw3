— The Art of Kung Fu Panda 3 , p. 146

The Tavern River Bed is a dried-up river bed where the ruins of an abandoned junk ship are nestled in the river bed's rocks. It is unknown precisely how old the river or the ship are. This location was first featured in Kung Fu Panda 3 where Crane , Mantis , Bear , Chicken , and Croc confront Kai , who has been using the ship ruins as his hideout while his jombies attacked kung fu masters.
