Description

Inside the temple there's a wellspring with four statue of the four constellations surrounding it.

History

In The Paws of Destiny

The temple was built presumably 1000 years before the events of paws of destiny. The temple was discovered by four panda kids, even though Jindiao already sent out his henchman to search it out.
