Master Gorilla was a famed kung fu master and a minor character in Kung Fu Panda 3 . His statue has been held among with other noble kung fu masters at the Jade Palace 's Master Garden , which stands at the front of the column to Oogway 's one and in front of Master Porcupine 's, next to Master Elephant 's statue.

Biography

In Kung Fu Panda 3

At some point, the ape master had encountered Kai in the Spirit Realm , who stole his chi and used a Jombie version of him to do his bidding.

When Kai was defeated due to overflowing of chi by the Dragon Warrior , his soul and chi was presumably released and backed to peace.

His jombie was briefly used by Kai to fight for him, meaning he was deemed as a skilled warrior. He was seen to be unarmed; however, being a gorilla, he likely possesses brute strength and durability.
