Personality

Huifang is a very boisterous, yet dim-witted water deer who is often seen as a nuisance by her collegues, especially Jade Tusk.

When first introduced, Huifang is shown to be very vocal, speaking her mind even in the most innapropriate of times. The most notable is when Po inquires as to how Jindiao and the Poison Clan had found them at Bunnidharma's den, leading Huifang to begin an explanation before being interuppted and subsequently pulled away by Jade Tusk. However, she is shown to have some level of restraint in this matter, as shown when she pantomimes zipping her mouth shut when told to be quite by her superiors.

Huifang is also shown to pocess an air of child-like innocense and wonder. Such behavior is seen in her first appearance when she inquires about the identy of Pious Chan, and later on when she is amazed as three of the Four Constelaltions escape the Plum Blssom Temple by jumping from the roof.

Huifang also appears to prone to episodes of confusion, a trait made even more unbareable for her collegues by the fact that she can be easily distracted, as evident when she chose to search for her "lucky sea shell" while ransanking the training hall of Po's students, rather than the Scroll of the Four Constellations. Her confusion is also evident when she accidentally defends Bao by mistakingly attacking her collegues.

Although not expressedly adressed, Huifang is infered to have a large appetite, as evedient when Jindiao sugests removing her from his presence with a snack, and later on when her stomach rumbles while in search of Nu Hai, Bao and Fan Tong, this time openly asking for snacks.

However, it is shown that Huifang's lack of intelligence makes her an ideal follower, following orders without much thought or need of incentive, as evident when she was willing to harm defenseless children, as well as destroy the Spirit Urn in order to prevent Jindiao's capture at the hands of the pandas.

Fighting style and abilities

Despite being annoyingly empty-headed, Huifang has been show to hold her own in a fight, cabale of taking on Master Po in close-quarter combat with a combination of speed, agility, and precision.

Huifang is also show to be adept at using weapons, namely swords.
