Inbe no Akita (斎部秋田 inbe akita ), or Akita for short, is a minor character in The Tale of the Princess Kaguya , a film directed by Isao Takahata of Studio Ghibli .

He is a very important member of the royal court, which is located in Heian-kyō . He organize ceremonies and he gives people a name. The last -entioned task is important for people to become noble.

Personality

He is honest and try to courage people. He talks to Sanuki no Miyatsuko , that he doesn't need to worry about his daugthht K,aguya . He will find a fitting name, even if she doesn't fit the noble norm. He named her Nakanaka Airashī Himegimi (なかなか愛らしい姫君) for now. It means beautiful and delightful princess . Later on K,aguya showed up and he named her Nayotake no Kaguya-Hime (なよたけのかぐや姫). It means bright/glowing princess from the mountains.

At the ceremony he talks about her beauty. But the five noblemen doe't like it, because she came from the Forest Village .
