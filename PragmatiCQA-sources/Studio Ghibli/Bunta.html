Bunta Mizunomizawa is a character from Pom Poko . He is a racoon dog who is part of the main tribe. He is semi good at metamorphosis. He is voiced by Takehiro Murata and by Kevin Michael Richardson in the English dub.

He is sent to Sado Island on a three-year journey to look for one of the legendary Transformation Masters, only to learn he was shot dead by a hunter years ago. When he comes back, he's shocked to find how much Tama Hills have changed, and remarks he feels like Urashima Taro.

He is described in the English dub as being "husky and robust", but has moments where he can be mellow and sensitive.

Appearance

Bunta is a tanuki with light brown fur, and has a patch of dark brown fur over the left side of his face. He's sturdy, though not as heavyset as Gonta. In human form, he appears as a stocky young man with thick, wavy black hair, a button nose and a facial mole similar to the fur patch he has in tanuki form; he wears a white T-shirt and pale blue jeans.
