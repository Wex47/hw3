ウシオニ ( Ushioni )

⓵ Appearance

Spirited Away
The Ushioni (eng. Cow Ogres ) are spirits seen in the film " Spirited Away " directed by Hayao Miyazaki of Studio Ghibli .

Physical Appearance

They have antlers and are frequent customers at the Bathhouse . The Kanji on their yukata (Japanese bathrobe) says, Oil of the Bathhouse . They often seen alongside the Radish Spirit .
