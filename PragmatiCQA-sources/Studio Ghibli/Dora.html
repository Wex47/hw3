Dōra is a minor character in the film, Kiki's Delivery Service . She is voiced by Shou Saito in the original Japanese version, Diane Michelle in the Streamline version and Fay Dewitt in the 1998 Disney dub.

Appearance

Dora has grey hair and wears glasses. She wears a light red dress with a red scarf and uses a staff-like cane.

History

She is a kind old lady who seems to have known Kiki 's mom, Kokiri , ever since she moved to Karikiya when she was about Kiki's age. She is a regular patron of Kokiri's potion business.
