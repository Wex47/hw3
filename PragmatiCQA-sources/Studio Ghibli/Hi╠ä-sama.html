Hī-sama is the wise woman in Princess Mononoke , a film directed by Hayao Miyazaki of Studio Ghibli . She was voiced by Mitsuko Mori in the Japanese version and Debi Derryberry in the English version. She is the elder and shaman of the Emishi Village .

Plot

She is first seen after Nago 's attack. She carries hot water in a jar, which Kaya pours on Ashitaka 's arm. Then she bows to Nago and she pleads to him not to pour his anger on the village.

She finds a bullet in Nago's body and believes that it is the reason Nago became a demon . Ashitaka has to leave the village to find a cure for the curse on his arm. Hī-sama's diagnosis is that Ashitaka will die from that curse the same way Nago did.

Quotes

"You can not alter your fate, my prince Ashitaka , but you can rise to meet it, if you so choose."

Trivia

The emblem of the Emishi can be found on the front and back of her clothing.
