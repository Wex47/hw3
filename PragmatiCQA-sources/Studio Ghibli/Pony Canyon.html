Pony Canyon Inc. (株式会社ポニーキャニオン, Kabushiki gaisha Ponī Kyanion ) is a Japanese company which publishes music, DVD and VHS videos, movies, and video games.

The company are co-distributors of Studio Ghibli's catalogue on home media in Japan, alongside Walt Disney Japan , mostly relying on rentals, although they do occasionally self-distribute Ghibli releases.
