Atsuko Takahata (高畑 淳子 Takahata Atsuko, born on October 11, 1954, in Kagawa) is a Japanese actress and voice actress.

She was born on October 11, 1954, in Zentsuji, Kagawa Prefecture, Japan and was educated at the Toho Gakuen College of Drama and Music.
