Toki is the wife of Kohroku who appears in Princess Mononoke . She is a brothel girl and works the bellows in Irontown with the rest of the women.

Appearance

Toki wears a red kimono with a brown little belt and white turban.

Story

Toki is first seen when she meets Ashitaka after he finds her husband, Kohroku , and brings him back to Irontown .
