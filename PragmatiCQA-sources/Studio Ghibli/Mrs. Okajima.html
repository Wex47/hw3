Mrs. Okajima is the mother of Taeko , Nanako , and Yaeko Okajima , as well as the wife of Mr. Okajima in Only Yesterday . She was voiced by Michie Tereda in Japanese dub and later Grey DeLisle by English dub GKIDS .

Personality

Mrs. Okajima is sweet, caring, and motherly.
