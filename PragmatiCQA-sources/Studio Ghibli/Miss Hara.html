Miss Hara also known Satsuki's teacher is a minor character in My Neighbor Totoro . She working on school and teaches all the children in lesson. During the lesson she sits Mei drawing Totoro between Satsuki & Michiko .

She was voiced by Machiko Washio , Edie Mirman in Streamline dub, and Tress MacNeille in the Disney dub.

Trivia

  • In French, Satsuki erroneously refers to Miss Hara as a male character when she promises to talk to her teacher about Mei, saying " mon prof". "Mon" is the masculine (and default) form of the possessive "Mon" in French. The proper form of "mon" that Satsuki should have used is the feminine, "ma".
