Aniyaku (兄役 , aniyaku ) i is a minor character in the Japanese animated film " Spirited Away " directed by Hayao Miyazaki .

He, among many of the other employees of the Bathhouse , at first regards Chihiro Ogino with harshness and disrespect due to her status as a human being, but gradually comes to accept her and cheer for saving him from No-Face .

Appearance

Aniyaku has small, black eyebrows and a mustache. He resembles a frog, like Chichiyaku and the other bathhouse employees.
