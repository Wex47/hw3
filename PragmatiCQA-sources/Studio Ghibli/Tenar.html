Tenar is a character of Tales from Earthsea . She is farm lady of Therru she lives on cottage on grassland.

Appearance

She used to be a priestess at the tombs of Atuan where she met Sparrow Hawk or Ged whom had led her to freedom from the place. Much later she takes in Therru who has suffered abuse and burns from her parents whom had abandoned her. Later she is kidnapped by Hare who was sent by Cob as bait to lure Sparrow Hawk to Cob's castle. Later she and Sparrow Hawk are freed thanks to Arren and Therru.
