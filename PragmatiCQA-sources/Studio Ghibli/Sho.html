Sho , known as Shawn in the US dub, is the male lead of The Secret World of Arrietty .

Appearance

Sho has navy blue/black hair and brown eyes. He wears a white shirt and blue trousers, and is also seen wearing pyjamas in the movie when it is his bedtime.

Personality

Sho is shown to be a quiet and very polite boy. However, he is initially listless and pessimistic about his operation, as he has no will to live, thinking that every living person will die, eventually, and he also has a low chance of survival from the operation.

This changes when he meets Arrietty , and he is inspired and amazed by her perseverance and strong will in rescuing her mother and thus gains the will to live on. It is shown in the very beginning of the film that he survives the operation, as he is reminiscing the time he met Arrietty.

Story

He is a sick boy who has a rheumatic fever and needs medicine that is given by Sadako Maki (Sho's aunt) as he has heart conditions that cause him to not be able to do what other boys at his age do.

At the beginning of the film, he and his aunt are on their way to her home for him to relax and be in peace as he has an operation in a few days, but he believes that it is hopeless until he meets Arrietty .
