Riku Matsuzaki is the little brother of Umi Matsuzaki and Sora Matsuzaki in From Up on Poppy Hill .

Appearance

Riku has brown hair and brown/black eyes and seems to be a bit taller than his two older sisters.

Relationships

  • Hana Matsuzaki (grandmother)
  • Ryoko Matsuzaki (mother)
  • Yūichirō Sawamura (father)
  • Umi Matsuzaki (older sister)
  • Sora Matsuzaki (older sister)

Trivia

  • In the original manga his surname is Komatsuzaki (小松崎).
