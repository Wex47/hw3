Kazuo is a minor character from Only Yesterday .

Story

He lives and works in Yamagata , where he helps to harvest Safflower . He calls Toshio's Mother by her pet name, Banchan . He is the husband of Kiyoko and father of Naoko . Toshio is his second cousin.
