Daveigh Elizabeth Chase (pronounced DUH-vay ; born Daveigh Elizabeth Chase-Schwallier ; July 24, 1992) is an American actress, singer and voice-over artist who is best known for her role as Rhonda Volmer in the HBO drama series Big Love from 2006 to 2011, Lilo from Lilo and Stich, and as Samara Morgan in the 2002 American horror remake, The Ring , which earned her the 2003 MTV Movie Award for Best Villain .

She also voiced Chihiro in the English dub version of Studio Ghibli 's Spirited Away .
