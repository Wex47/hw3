Takayuki Miyauchi (宮内 孝幸 Miyauchi Takayuki, August 4, 1929 – June 2, 1995), better known by the stage name Kōhei Miyauchi (宮内 幸平 Miyauchi Kōhei), was a Japanese actor and voice actor.
