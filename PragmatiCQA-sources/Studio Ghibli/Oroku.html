Oroku is a character in 1994's film by Studio Ghibli 's Pom Poko . She is peacekeeper of the Raccoons Dogs . She is rational and wise, and very strong-willed. All the tanukis respect her and call her "grandmother". At the end of the film, she becomes a fortune teller.

Appearance

Oroku is a tanuki with black hair pinned neatly, and a red kimono. In human form, she looks almost exactly the same, but has the appearance of a middle-aged woman.

Voice actor

Oroku was voiced by Nijiko Kiyokawa and Tress MacNeille in English.
