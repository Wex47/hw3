Pazu's Dad is a character in Castle in the Sky . He is merely known as such; his name has never been revealed.

History

Pazu's Father's greatest discovery was the legendary floating city of Laputa during one harsh exploration, locating the city within the center of particularly harsh cyclone. He managed to snap a shot of the city with his camera before the storms forced his aircraft from the area.

Despite the evidence he had obtained, critics immediately called the photo a fraud, and Pazu's father would later die as the result of guilt for being called a liar.

His son Pazu , would follow along in his father's footsteps and attempt to prove to the general public that Laputa was no myth.

While flying through the storm clouds leading directly into Laputa, Pazu sees a vision of his father in his own airship, leading the way.

He is voiced by Eddie Frierson.
