Okino Residence is the home of Kiki and her family in the film Kiki's Delivery Service .

Background

The Okino Residence is located in the small town of Karikiya and is home to Kiki's family, which includes her father, Okino and her mother, Kokiri . The house is surrounded by gardens and flowers, and includes a sun room and a potion-making workshop. Kiki's bedroom room is located on the second floor.

Behind Scenes

Kiki's home is based on concepts by Hiroshi Ono and Katsuya Kondō . According to background artist Hiroshi Ono , he "wanted to emulate the original story's image of Kiki's house being surrounded by flowers, giving it a fairy tale look."

Ono elaborated on the interiors, "I wanted the interior and exterior of the sun room to look bright, but I ended up having to reduce the light quite a bit. This turned out to be better though."
