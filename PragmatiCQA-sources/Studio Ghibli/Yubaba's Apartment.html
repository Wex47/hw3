Yubaba's Apartment is Yubaba's place of residence, and is found at the top floor of the Bathhouse .

Background

Yubaba's grand apartment is lined with giant vases on gold-plated pedestals, chandeliers, marbled floors, a grand fireplace, and luxurious carpeting and furnishings. The foyer includes an ornate, East-Asian style entrance lies beneath an emblem of the Bathhouse .

Alongside her home is large playroom for Boh , and a kitchen and dining space. One of the living spaces includes a fantastic view of the sea.
