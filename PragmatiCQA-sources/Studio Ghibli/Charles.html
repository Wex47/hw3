Charles is Dola 's second-oldest son and a member of her gang in Castle in the Sky . He was voiced by Tazuko Kamiyama in Japanese, Barry Stigler in the Streamline English version, and Michael McShane in the Disney English version.

Appearance

Charles has dark eyes and light brown hair with a very bushy beard and mustache. Like the rest of the boys in the gang, Charles almost always wears a brown shirt with pink pants, a belt, a red hood and brown slip-on shoes.

History

Charles is the strongest member of the Dola's Gang. He challenges Mr. Duffi by flexing his chest and arm muscles until his shirt rips to pieces. However, Mr. Duffi is even stronger than Charles, and when he flexes, he is bigger and shreds his shirt even more.
