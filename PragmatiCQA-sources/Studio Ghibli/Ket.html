Ket is a character who is introduced in Kiki's Delivery Service . He is the nephew of Maki and has a loyal dog named Jeff . Ket is voiced by Yuriko Fuchizaki in the Japanese version, Lara Cody in the Streamline dub and Pamela Adlon in the 1998 Disney dub. He is voiced by in the Russian version.

Appearance

Ket has light brown hair and brown eyes. He wears a white undershirt, green pants (with purple suspenders), white socks and light blue shoes.

History

Kiki's first customer (Ket's Aunt) asks her to deliver the present for her nephew's birthday: a stuffed cat looking very much like Jiji in a fancy cage. Ket is initially excited about his gift from his Aunt, but is unimpressed when he sees the stuffed cat (which is actually Jiji posing as the stuffed cat as the toy fell out of the cage while Kiki was delivering it). Ket briefly throws Jiji around before watching some television while waiting for his party guests to arrive, at which point his mother prompts him repeatedly to switch the television off, which he does.
