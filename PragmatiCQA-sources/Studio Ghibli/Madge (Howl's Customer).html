Madge is one of Howl 's regular customers, whose name is not mentioned in the film Howl's Moving Castle directed by Hayao Miyazaki of Studio Ghibli .

She lives with her parents in Porthaven . She bought a spell to protect their boat. Markl takes her request because Howl wasn't at home when she showed up. She asks if Sophie is a witch. Then Sophie approves her guess with a smile.

Madge gets self-conscious about it.
