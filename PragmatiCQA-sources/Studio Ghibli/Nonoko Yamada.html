Nonoko Yamada is the seven-year-old sister of Noboru Yamada in My Neighbors the Yamadas . She was voiced by Naomi Uno in the Japanese version and Liliana Mumy in the English version.

Appearance

Nonoko has black hair. She typically wears a beige shirt with a white collar and orange skirt.

Relatives

  • Noboru Yamada (brother)
  • Takashi Yamada (father)
  • Matsuko Yamada (mother)
  • Shige Yamano (grandmother)
