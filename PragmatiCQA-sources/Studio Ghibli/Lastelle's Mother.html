Lastelle's Mother is the mother of Lastelle and Asbel and born in Pejite of Nausicaä of the Valley of the Wind . She asks Nausicaä with the new cloth. She is voiced by Patricia Parris in Warriors of the Wind and Jodi Benson in Disney version.
