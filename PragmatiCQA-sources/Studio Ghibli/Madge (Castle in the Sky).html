Madge is the daughter of Okami in Castle in the Sky and voiced by Tarako , Barbara Goodson (Streamline) and Debi Derryberry (Disney).

Personality

Madge seems very young, but she expresses frustration at not being able to watch the Dola Gang fight with the townspeople.

History

Madge has a limited role in the film, appearing when the Dola Gang first attempt to capture Sheeta before being pulled inside her house by her mother. Later that evening, she is chasing a piglet with a broom when Pazu returns from Fort Tedus .
