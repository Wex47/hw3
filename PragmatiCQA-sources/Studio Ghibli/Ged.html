Ged , also known as Sparrowhawk , is the deuteragonist from Tales from Earthsea . At the beginning of the film, he runs into Prince Arren and saves him from a pack of wolves that are chasing him through the desert.

Personality

Ged is the arch-mage of Earthsea and is thought to be the best in his field. He is rivaled by Cob .

Behind the Voices

Ged was voiced by Bunta Sagawara in Japanese and Timothy Dalton in English.
