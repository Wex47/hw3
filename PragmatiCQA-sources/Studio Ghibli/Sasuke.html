Sasuke is a minor character in Pom Poko . Sasuke, unlike almost all of the raccoons wears glasses. He is married after one Year passed in the Five-year plan. Sasuke along with Shokichi helped Master Kincho and Tamasaburo with getting revenge on the Wonderland President, and with Shokichi and Tamasaburo, failing to stop Gonta from fighting the riot police. He is voiced by Megumi Hayashibara in Japanese and Marc Donato in English.
