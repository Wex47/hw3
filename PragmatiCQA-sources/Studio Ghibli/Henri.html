Henri is Dola 's youngest son and a member of her gang of air pirates in Castle in the Sky . He was voiced by Sukekiyo Kameyama in Japanese, Eddie Frierson in the Streamline English version, and Andy Dick in the Disney English version.

Appearance

Henri has reddish-brown hair, dark eyes, freckles, and no facial hair. Like the rest of the boys in the gang, Henri almost always wears a brown shirt with pink pants, a belt, and a red hood. When he dresses up in white to go into town, his top hat is so large, it covers his eyes.

Plot

Henri is the most playful of Dola's sons and likes to crack jokes.
