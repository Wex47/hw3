Laura Ann Cody (born September 8, 1957, in Los Angeles, California as Deanna Morris ), better known as Lara Cody, is an American voice actress. She also goes under the name Deanna Morris. She is best known for voicing multiple voices in Studio Ghibli films as well as Rosemary in Metal Gear Solid 2: Sons of Liberty and Metal Gear Solid 4: Guns of The Patriots.

Filmography

  • Castle in the Sky - Sheeta and Okami ( Streamline dub, credited as Louise Chambell)
  • My Neighbor Totoro - Female Motorist and Bus Attendant (Troma dub)
