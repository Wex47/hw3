Foreman is a minor character in Studio Ghibli 's film Spirited Away .

Story

He is the manager of Yubaba 's Bathhouse , who distributes Bath Tokens . He is seen on Chihiro Ogino 's first day in the Bathhouse. Chihiro helps Lin to clean up the Big Tub. For that task they need a Bath Token. The Foreman refuses to give Chihiro one and command her to scrub more. No-Face gives her secretly one.

The Foreman is later seen at the entrance of the workplace to stop the Stink Spirit from entering.
