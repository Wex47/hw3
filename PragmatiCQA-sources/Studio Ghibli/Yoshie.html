Yoshie (ヨシエ , Yoshie ) is one of Senior Center inhabitants in the film, Ponyo .

Appearance

Yoshie has blond hair and wears a pink dress, She was often see sitting in a wheelchair and in the ending, she was seen walking around without any sort of support during the time with the other seniors and Granmamare when she states, "Life Begins Again".
