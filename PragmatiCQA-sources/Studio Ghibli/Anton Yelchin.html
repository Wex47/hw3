Anton Yelchin (11 March 1989 – 19 June 2016) was an actor from Southern California who dubbed and provided the voice for Shun Kazama in From Up on Poppy Hill . Anton Yelchin also played Pavel Chekov from Star Trek in 2009 and Star Trek Into Darkness .
