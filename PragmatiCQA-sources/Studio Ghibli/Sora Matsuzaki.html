Sora Matsuzaki ( 松崎 空 , Matsuzaki Sora ) is a supporting character of From Up on Poppy Hill film. She is the little sister of Umi Matsuzaki and is a student of Konan School.

Appearance

For her general appearance, Sora has a bob haircut similar to Umi, and a pink barrette on the right side of her hair. For her school attire, she wears a standard sailor school uniform with a green ribbon that is tied into a bow.

Story

During the events of the movie, Sora is shown to do anything that interests her such as tidying her hair, supporting Umi and her relationship with Shun Kazama, and even bonding with the rest of her family in the Coquelicot Manor.

Following a stunt by Shun the other day, Sora asked for Umi's help to visit the Quartier Latin together to get Shun's autograph. This action alone sets off a chain of events where she would eventually fall in love with Shiro Mizunuma, inspiring Umi to inspire the students of the Quartier Latin to save the building from demolition by rebuilding it, bringing Shun and Umi together, and putting their relationship into a test by discovering about their past together.

Personality

Sora is a stereotypical teenage girl who always do what a normal teenager does. Best shown during her debut where she took most of her time tidying her hair before having breakfast with the family.

She is also depicted as a supportive sister to Umi whenever they visit the Quartier Latin together even agreeing to help her and the other students to restore the building just to save it from being demolished.

Another part of her personality is that she is quite graceful with how Umi and Shun are a couple together, despite that Sora has a crush on Shun beforehand.

  • In the original manga her surname is Komatsuzaki (小松崎).
