Obaba , also known Old Lady , is a character in Nausicaä of the Valley of the Wind . She is the aunt of Nausicaä .

In Film

She is a blind old woman who also appears to be the wise woman of the Valley of the Wind .

Behind the Voice

Obaba was provided her voice by Hisako Kyoda, Linda Gary in the Warriors of the Wind -dubbed version, and Tress MacNeille in the Disney version.
