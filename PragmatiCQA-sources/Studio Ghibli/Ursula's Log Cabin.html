Ursula's Log Cabin (森の小屋 , Mori Nokoya ), also known as Ursula's Atelier is Ursula's forest retreat near the city of Koriko , as seen in the film Kiki's Delivery Service .

Background

Kiki stumbles upon Ursula's cabin after trying to find the missing gift for Ket and his dog Jeff . It is here that Kiki and Ursula find solace and solidarity from the outside world. The cabin is filled with books, painting canvases and materials and other amenities.
