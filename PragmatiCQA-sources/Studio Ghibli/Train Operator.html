The Train Operator drives the train in the mining town in Castle in the Sky . He drives a small orange steam engine on the industrial railway that serves the mines.

History

The operator helps Pazu and Sheeta run from Dola 's gang of air pirates and the military , where he and his engine are confronted by an armored train.

Cast

The operator is voiced by Tomomichi Nishimura in the Japanese version of the film, Daniel Foster in Streamline dub, and Matt K. Miller in Disney dub.
