Ryouko Matsuzaki (松崎 良子 Matsuzaki Ryōko ) is a character of From Up on Poppy Hill film. She is a daughter of Hana Matsuzaki , a widow of Yūichirō Sawamura and a mother of Umi , Sora and Riku Matsuzaki .

Trivia

  • In the original manga her surname is Komatsuzaki (小松崎).

Etymology

  • The name Ryoko means "good, virtuous, respectable" (良) ( ryo ) and "child" (子) ( ko ).
  • Ryoko's surname Matsuzaki means "pine tree, fir tree" (松) ( matsu ) and "cape, peninsula" (崎) ( saki / zaki ).
