Seiji Okuda (奥田 誠治 , Okuda Seiji ) is a film producer in Japan. He's served as a Councilor of the Tokuma Memorial Cultural Foundation for Animation and Chairman of the All Nippon Producers Association of Japan.

After working as Director of the Film Center, Director of the Film Organization and Director of the Film Business Department of Nippon Television Network Corporation, deputy director of the Business Bureau, and General Producer of the Telelab Office on the same day, he is currently an Executive Producer of the Video Planning Department of the Video Division of Matsutake Co., Ltd.
