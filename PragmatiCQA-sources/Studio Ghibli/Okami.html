Okami is the wife of Mr. Duffi and mother of Madge in the film Castle in the Sky . She is voiced by Machiko Washio in the Japanese version, Lara Cody in the Streamline English dub, and Tress MacNeille in the Disney English dub.

Appearance

Okami has dark eyes and bright orange hair which she wears pulled up in a fluffy ponytail. She wears a green dress with a white apron around her waist.

History
