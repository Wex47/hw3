Dakota Fanning (born Hannah Dakota Fanning on 23 February 1994 in Conyers, Georgia) is an American actress, who voiced Satsuki Kusakabe in My Neighbor Totoro in the Disney dub. She is the older sister of actress Elle Fanning , who voiced Satsuki's younger sister Mei Kusakabe in My Neighbor Totoro .
