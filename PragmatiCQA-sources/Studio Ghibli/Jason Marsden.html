Jason Christopher Marsden is an American actor, voice actor, director, and producer largely known for numerous voice roles in various animated films, video games, and television series. He is best known for his voice roles as Chester McBadbat in the Nickelodeon animated TV series, The Fairly OddParents , and as Nermal in The Garfield Show .

His Disney roles include as Haku in Spirited Away .
