Akemi Okamura (岡村 明美 Okamura Akemi, born on March 12, 1969) is a Japanese voice actress. She is also a calligraphy teacher in Mausu Promotion . Her voice is best known as Nami of One Piece and Risa Koizumi of Lovely Comeplex .
