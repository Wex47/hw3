Toki (トキ , Toki ) is an elderly woman from Ponyo . She lives in the Local Senior Center along with Noriko and Yoshie .

History

Toki first greets Sōsuke at the Senior Service Center in Koganei, but he goes to his nursery school when Ponyo streams water all on her dress.
