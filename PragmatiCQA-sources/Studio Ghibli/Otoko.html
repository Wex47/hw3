Otoko is a minor character in My Neighbor Totoro . He is the husband of Ryōko and is seen in his tractor when Satsuki searches for her missing little sister Mei . He is voiced by Daiki Nakamura in Japanese, Kerrigan Mahan ( Streamline Edition ) and Matt Adler ( Disney Edition ).

Appearance

Otoko has brown hair with beige farm hat he wears a red shirt and blue trousers.
