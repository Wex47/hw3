Motro is a character from Castle in the Sky . He is Dola's husband and works on board the airship Tiger Moth as its engineer. He is voiced by Ryuji Saikachi in the Japanese version, Clifton Wells in the Streamline English dub, and Eddie Frierson in the Disney English dub.

Appearance

Motro is short and bald with a large, brown mustache. He always wears round, tinted glasses. He is similar in design to Kamajī in Spirited Away , and to Lonebach, the antagonist of a Lupin III episode Miyazaki directed titled Wings of Death: Albatross .

History

When Dola makes Pazu help Motro repair the ship, he is impressed with the boy's ability. Motro is later shown playing chess with Dola.
