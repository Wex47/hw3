The Tiger Moth is an airship used by the air pirate Captain Dola and her gang . Its name comes from an actual flying insect, the .

Appearance

The Tiger Moth is a blimp-like airship constructed from metal, wood and fabric with wings and propellers. The airship's features include a hangar that carries four flaptors , a crow's nest lookout that detaches to become a kite, a galley to prepare the enormous amount of food Dola's Gang eats, and living quarters for the gang.

Role in the film

The Tiger Moth appears at the beginning of the film, when Dola's Gang raids Colonel Muska 's airship in pursuit of Sheeta and her crystal necklace . Later, Dola and Pazu rescue Sheeta from imprisonment and take her to the Tiger Moth, where Sheeta helps out by working and cooking in the galley. The gang flies the Tiger Moth to Laputa , but the airship is severely damaged by an attack by Air Destroyer Goliath , and later completely destroyed when Pazu and Sheeta use the Spell of Destruction and cause the lower portion of Laputa to crumble.
