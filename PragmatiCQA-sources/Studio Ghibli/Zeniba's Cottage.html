Zeniba's Cottage (銭婆の家) is located in Swamp Bottom .

Background

Zeniba's home is a cozy, one-story, cottage down by Swamp Bottom . The interiors are done in a simply, western-style, contrasting her sister's more opulent apartment and office.

Additionally, unlike her sister, Zeniba applies her magic on inanimate objects to help with her day-to-day activities. No-Face decides to settle here after Chihiro leaves to return to the Bathhouse.
