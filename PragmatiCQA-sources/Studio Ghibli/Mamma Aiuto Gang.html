Mamma Aiuto (マンマユート , Manmayūto ) is an air pirate gang lead by "Boss" in the film Porco Rosso . They are infamous throughout the Adriatic Sea and are constantly in pursuit by Porco Rosso .

The gang operates on a heavily modified Dabohaze seaplane.

Trivia

  • Members of the gang are voiced by Rodger Bumpass , Bill Fagerbakke , Kevin Michael Richardson and Frank Welker in the Disney dub version.
  • The Ghibli Museum shop is named "Mamma Auito", and "Boss" is used as the shop's mascot.
