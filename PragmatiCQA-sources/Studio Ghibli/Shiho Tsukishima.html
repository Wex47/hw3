Shiho Tsukishima is the older sister of Shizuku .

Appearance

Like majority members of the Tsukishima family, Shiho has brown hair that reaches just below her shoulders. Her height is average for an eighteen-year-old, college student; although she is the tallest in her family. She has dark to almost black eyes, high nose, and arched eyebrows. She often comes in the house wearing a colored T-shirt (often beige or pink) or a sleeveless top (white with blue collars) matched with jeans. When cooking, she is seen wearing either a beige or pink sweater, jeans, and a blue apron. Her bedtime clothing consists of a beige T-shirt accompanied with dark, navy blue shorts.
