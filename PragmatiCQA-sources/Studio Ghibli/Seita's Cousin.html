Yokokawa cousin is the paternal cousin of Seita Yokokawa and Setsuko Yokokawa .

History

Very little is known about her except that she, unlike Seita, went to school and resumed her normal activities during the war. She is seen thrice having dinner with her mother, her cousins and a houseguest. She was last seen having dinner with her mother as she complained to her and the guest about Seita and Setsuko's behavior. She seemingly sides with mother and asks if she scolded her cousins.

It is unknown what happened to her after the war ended, except that she survived.
