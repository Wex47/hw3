Kohroku is the husband of Toki in Princess Mononoke directed by Hayao Miyazaki of Studio Ghibli . He fears Kodamas .

Plot

Ashitaka has to rescue him after he, Lady Eboshi and the rest of the working men ran into San and the wolves, while bringing their goods back to Irontown . He was injured and thrown into a river, where Ashitaka found him and brought him back to the safety of Irontown.
