Grandmother Okajima is the mother of Mr. Okajima in Only Yesterday . She is also the grandmother of Taeko Okajima , Yaeko Okajima , and Nanako Okajima . She is often seen sitting on dinner or living room table.

She is a kind and patient person. She liked to talk about her husband that he stayed in memory of his children and his grandchildren.
