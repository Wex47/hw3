The Senior Witch is a minor character in the film, Kiki's Delivery Service . She is a young witch Kiki meets on her way to Koriko . She is played by Yuko Kobayashi in the Japanese version, Wendee Lee in the Streamline version and Debi Derryberry in the 1998 Disney dub.

Appearance

Senior Witch has light brown hair with two plaits and teal eyes. She wears a dark purple dress, two heart-shaped gold earrings and a red necklace. Her broomstick is brown and she flies with a black cat with radiant green eyes and a lantern. She is possibly 13-years old like Kiki .

History

While flying towards Koriko , Kiki encounters her and finds out that she had just completed her witch training. She states that her specialty is in fortune-telling.
