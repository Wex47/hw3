Ōtori-Sama

Japanese name

ひよこのオオトリ様 ( Hyioko no ootorisama )

Species

Spirit

First Appearance

Spirited Away

The Ōtori-Sama are minor characters in the film Spirited Away directed by Hayao Miyazaki of Studio Ghibli .

They are spirits resembling giant chickens or ducks that are clients of the Bathhouse , here they put themselves into bath tubs. They are servants of the deity Ōtori. One of them is seen in the short film Iblard Time , and a plush of one is visible on a shelf at the senior center in Ponyo .
