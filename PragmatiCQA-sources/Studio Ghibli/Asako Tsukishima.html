Asako Tsukishima is the mother of Shizuku and Shiho and as well wife of Seiya Tsukishima in the film Whisper of the Heart .

Appearance

Asako Tsukishima has brown hair and black eyes. She wears a light purple turtle neck with beige cardigans.
