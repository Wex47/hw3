Akemi Miyazaki (née Akemi Ota ) is an animator married to Hayao Miyazaki since 1965. Her sons are Goro Miyazaki and Keisuke Miyazaki . She first met her husband on the work of Gulliver's Travels Beyond the Moon .

For Studio Ghibli , she worked on Nausicaä of the Valley of the Wind and My Neighbor Totoro .
