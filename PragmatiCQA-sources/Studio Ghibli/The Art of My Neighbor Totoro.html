The Art of My Neighbor Totoro: A film by Hayao Miyazaki is an rt book by Hayao Miyazaki . The book goes over the details of the artwork that brought life to the film My Neighbor Totoro . It has a generous collection of concept sketches, fully rendered character and background drawings, paintings and cell images. There are principal characters, interviews, and comments with the production staff.
