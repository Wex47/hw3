The Hollow Bastion Restoration Committee (later renamed Radiant Garden Restoration Committee ) is an organization seen in Kingdom Hearts II . It was founded some time after the end of Kingdom Hearts . Their meetings take place in Merlin 's house.

Goals

The Restoration Committee was founded in the hope of restoring Radiant Garden to its former glory and eradicating the Heartless infestation from the town for good.

Accomplishments

Within a year's work, the committee manages to restore most of the town's former image, but is still far off from completely restoring it.

Cid created a defense system called Claymores that would destroy weaker Heartless and occupy stronger ones until a committee member could arrive to dispose of them. Sora and company assist Leon in finding Ansem the Wise 's secret lab, Ansem's computer and Xehanort 's notes on the Heartless.

While using the computer, Sora and company enter the digital realm of Space Paranoids , and with the assistance of Tron are able to return to search for a password that would open up the dataspace. The Master Control Program obtained access to the dataspace and started to destroy the town. Two areas of the town were partially destroyed by this but gave access to new areas.

Heartless swarmed the town and the Committee, with the assistance of Cloud , Tifa , Stitch , and the Gullwings (who had to be bribed into joining their side) defended the town from a Heartless Invasion. Sora fought against the Heartless in the Battle of the 1000 Heartless .

When Sora returned to Hollow Bastion, he found out that the MCP had hacked into the town's defense system and was making Heartless in the Heartless Manufactory in Ansem's study. Merlin and Cid put together a program that could rid Space Paranoids of the MCP. Sora reentered the computer and helped Tron destroy the MCP and gave Tron full control of the digital realm. This would allow Tron access to restricted files in Ansem the Wise's computer. Tron showed the citizens of Hollow Bastion how their home used to look and revealed its original name of Radiant Garden.

Members

All members of the Hollow Bastion Restoration Committee carry a membership card. Sora's card was able to give him access to the worlds of The Land of Dragons and Beast's Castle by opening one of the World Gates .
