Guard ( リフレクトガード , Rifurekuto Gādo , lit. "Reflect Guard") , also translated as Block in Kingdom Hearts 358/2 Days , Kingdom Hearts Birth by Sleep , and Kingdom Hearts Re:coded , is an ability which appears in Kingdom Hearts , Kingdom Hearts Final Mix , Kingdom Hearts: Chain of Memories , Kingdom Hearts II , Kingdom Hearts II Final Mix , Kingdom Hearts 358/2 Days , Kingdom Hearts Birth by Sleep , and Kingdom Hearts Re:coded . In Kingdom Hearts 358/2 Days , it is an ability panel , and it can be upgraded by linking Block LV+ Ⓛ ( ガードアップⓁ , Gādo Appu Ⓛ , lit. "Guard Up Ⓛ") panels to it. It can be leveled from Block LV1 up to Block LV2 , Block LV3 , and Block LV4 , which increase blocking speed and decrease the recoil distance that occurs after a successful block. It can also be linked to various sub-panels, which can be synthesized at the Moogle Shop.

Learning Guard

Kingdom Hearts

  • Sora learns Guard at level 9 with the Dream Shield , 24 with the Dream Rod , or 36 with the Dream Sword .

Kingdom Hearts Final Mix

  • Sora learns Guard at level 15 with the Dream Shield , 24 with the Dream Sword , or 33 with the Dream Rod .

Kingdom Hearts: Chain of Memories and Kingdom Hearts Re:Chain of Memories

  • The Large Body card has Guard as its effect.

Kingdom Hearts II

  • Roxas learns Guard after defeating the Twilight Thorn .

Kingdom Hearts II Final Mix

  • Roxas learns Guard after defeating the Twilight Thorn.
  • Limit Form has Guard as a default ability.

Kingdom Hearts 358/2 Days

  • Block ② is a clear bonus for Mission 07 .
  • Block ④ can be purchased for 2400 Heart Points once Roxas is promoted to Master rank.
  • Block LV+ Ⓛ can be purchased for 2400 Heart Points once Roxas is promoted to Master rank; can be purchased for 6000 Heart Points once Roxas is promoted to Legend rank; and is in a chest at the Bridge in Mission 22 .

Panels which link to Block

  • Perfect Block Ⓛ ( ジャストガードⓁ , Jasuto Gādo Ⓛ , lit. "Just Guard Ⓛ") : Allows you to block just as an attack hits to eliminate recoil.
  • Block-Counter Ⓛ ( ガードカウンターⓁ , Gādo Kauntā Ⓛ , lit. "Guard Counter Ⓛ") : Allows you to respond with a powerful counterattack immediately after blocking.
  • Block-Retreat Ⓛ ( ガードバックⓁ , Gādo Bakku Ⓛ , lit. "Guard Back Ⓛ") : Allows you to recoil farther after you block an attack.
  • Sliding Block Ⓛ ( スライドガード , Suraido Gādo Ⓛ , lit. "Slide Guard Ⓛ") : Allows you to quickly slide forward as you block.
  • Block-Jump Ⓛ ( ガードエスケープⓁ , Gādo Esukēpu Ⓛ , lit. "Guard Escape Ⓛ") : Allows you to jump right after you block without any delay.
  • Fire Block Ⓛ ( ファイアガードⓁ , Faia Gādo Ⓛ , lit. "Fire Guard Ⓛ") : Allows you to ignite enemies sometimes when you block (15% base chance).
  • Blizzard Block Ⓛ ( ブリザドガードⓁ , Burizado Gādo Ⓛ , lit. "Blizzard Guard Ⓛ") : Allows you to freeze enemies sometimes when you block (15% base chance).
  • Thunder Block Ⓛ ( サンダーガードⓁ , Sandā Gādo Ⓛ , lit. "Thunder Guard Ⓛ") : Allows you to jolt enemies sometimes when you block (15% base chance).
  • Aero Block Ⓛ ( エアロガードⓁ , Earo Gādo Ⓛ , lit. "Aero Guard Ⓛ") : Allows you to air-toss enemies sometimes when you block (15% base chance).
  • Block Bonus Ⓛ ( ガードボーナスⓁ , Gādo Bōnasu Ⓛ , lit. "Guard Bonus Ⓛ") : Allows you to generate HP prizes sometimes when you block.
  • ‎ Round Block Ⓛ ( ラウンドガードⓁ , Raundo Gādo Ⓛ , lit. "Round Guard Ⓛ") : Allows you to block any attack, even if it comes from behind.
  • Auto-Block Ⓛ ( オートガードⓁ , Ōto Gādo Ⓛ , lit. "Auto Guard Ⓛ") : Allows you to automatically block frontal attacks.

Kingdom Hearts Birth by Sleep

  • Terra is equipped with Block at the beginning of the game.
  • Ventus is equipped with Block at the beginning of the game.

Kingdom Hearts Re:coded

  • Data-Sora learns Block from a panel in the Stat Matrix.
