Demon Tower

The Demon Tide is a Pureblood Heartless that appears in Kingdom Hearts 0.2 Birth by Sleep -A fragmentary passage- and Kingdom Hearts III . It is a massive cluster of Shadows formed into a cyclone-like formation; found at the "head" of the cyclone is a glowing blob of Shadows fused together that controls the rest of the formation.
