Water ( ウォータ , Wota ) , Watera ( ウォタラ , Wotara ) , Waterga ( ウォタガ , Wotaga ) , and Waterza ( ウォタガン , Wotagun , lit. "Watergun") are a series of Magic in Kingdom Hearts 3D: Dream Drop Distance and Kingdom Hearts III , though it appears as early as Kingdom Hearts II , where is it Demyx 's attribute. They allow the user to unleash water-based attacks at enemies.

Mechanics

In Kingdom Hearts 3D: Dream Drop Distance , Waterga is an command that deals Water damage and submerges the opponent's touch screen for 15 seconds.

In Kingdom Hearts III , Water, Watera, and Waterga are Magic spells that briefly surround the user in a barrier of water before firing a projectile of water that homes in on the enemy, dealing Water damage. The blast radius varies depending on the tier of Water used. Repeatedly casting Water, Watera, and Waterga respectively results in the Watera, Waterga, and Waterza Situation Commands , which unleash stronger bubbles.

  • R & R Seal can use Waterga as its evolved command.

Kingdom Hearts III

  • Sora learns Water after defeating the Darkside .
  • Sora learns Watera after putting out the fire at the Accessway in The Power Plant in Monstropolis .
  • Sora learns Waterga after defeating the Heartless Armada in The Caribbean .
