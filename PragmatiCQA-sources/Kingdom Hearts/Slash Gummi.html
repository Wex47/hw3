Slash Gummis are a form of offensive Gummi Blocks found in Kingdom Hearts II . Slash Gummis perform powerful strikes against enemy ships using the Slash Gauge, which fills up as other weapons do damage up to a maximum of nine gauges. An attack can be triggered by pressing

List of Slash Gummis

Orichalcum/G

Orichalcum/G attacks directly ahead of the Gummi Ship.

  • Size: 1x2x1
  • Maximum Capacity: 4
  • Cost: 129
  • Offense: 300
  • HP Bonus: 1
  • Damage: 500

Masamune/G

Masamune/G attacks vertically up and down the screen in front of the Gummi Ship.

  • Size: 1x3x1
  • Maximum Capacity: 4
  • Cost: 134
  • Offense: 300
  • HP Bonus: 1
  • Damage: 500

Excalibur/G

Excalibur/G attacks horizontally across the screen in front of the Gummi Ship.

  • Size: 1x3x1
  • Maximum Capacity: 4
  • Cost: 126
  • Offense: 300
  • HP Bonus: 1
  • Damage: 500

Infinity/G

Infinity/G performs a perimeter attack.

  • Size: 1x3x1
  • Maximum Capacity: 4
  • Cost: 160
  • Offense: 300
  • HP Bonus: 1
  • Damage: 500
