Elixir ( エリックサー , Erikkusā ) is a healing item in each game of the Kingdom Hearts series thus far that fully heals one character's HP and MP .

In Kingdom Hearts: Chain of Memories the Elixir card reloads all attack and magic cards. It can be used on the field, in battle and from the Main Menu. In Kingdom Hearts 358/2 Days , Elixirs restore all of your HP and Magic.

Elixirs can be found in chests in Worlds throughout both games, as well as in Trinities in Kingdom Hearts , and are occasionally awarded as prizes for completing certain tasks and quests. They can be synthesized in both games with help from the Moogles .

Recipe

Kingdom Hearts

In Kingdom Hearts , they are created from from List I by using:

  • 2 Blaze Shard
  • 2 Frost Shard
  • 2 Thunder Shard
  • 4 Mythril Shard

Kingdom Hearts II

In Kingdom Hearts II , the Star Recipe used to create them is found in Disney Castle in the Courtyard. After successfully creating one Elixir, adding a Serenity Gem to the recipe creates a Megalixir .

  • 3 Mythril Stone - Reduces to 2 later
  • 1 Power Stone
  • 1 Dark Stone

Kingdom Hearts 358/2 Days

The Elixir can be synthesized for 1600 Munny once Roxas is promoted to Novice rank.

  • 1 Elixir Recipe
  • 1 Hi-Potion
  • 1 Hi-Ether
  • 1 Shining Shard

Etymology

Elixir is a sweet flavored liquid, usually containing alcohol, used in compounding medicines to be taken orally in order to mask an unpleasant taste and intended to cure one's illness.
