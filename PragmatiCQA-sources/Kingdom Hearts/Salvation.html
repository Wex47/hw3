Salvation ( ホーリーライズ , Hōrī Raizu , lit. "Holy Rise") is a technique in Kingdom Hearts Birth by Sleep and Kingdom Hearts Birth by Sleep Final Mix . The attack consists of Ventus charging energy in a steady form, then swinging his Keyblade up. Columns of light continuously grow from the ground around him, damaging any nearby enemies while simultaneously healing Ventus .

Mechanics

In Kingdom Hearts Birth by Sleep , Salvation is an Ultimate Attack Command that is exclusive to Ventus . It takes up two slots in the Command Deck, has a maximum level of 6, a normal reload time of 25 seconds, and fills the Command Gauge by 8%. Salvation also recovers a small amount of HP, which varies depending on the level of the Command.

Learning Salvation

Kingdom Hearts Birth by Sleep

  • Ventus can create Salvation through Command Melding .
  • Ventus can obtain Salvation through Crown Stickers for 100 points.

Recipes

Kingdom Hearts Birth by Sleep

Salvation is a Ultimate-class Attack Command that can be melded through one recipe, listed in the Giga Attack Recipe .
