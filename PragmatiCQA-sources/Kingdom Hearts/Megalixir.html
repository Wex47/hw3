Megalixir is a rare healing item in the Kingdom Hearts series that completely fills the entire party's HP and MP . It can be used on the field, in battle, and from the Main Menu. They can be found in chests in Worlds throughout both games, as well as in Trinities in Kingdom Hearts , and are occasionally awarded as prizes for completing certain tasks and quests, especially late in the games. They can also be synthesized in both games.

In Kingdom Hearts: Chain of Memories , the Megalixir is the ultimate Item Card , obtained by opening the Room of Rewards on the 12th floor, Destiny Islands. It restores all Attack Cards and Magic Cards , and even resets the reload counter. It also is the most crucial piece of the powerful Sleight Holy.

In Kingdom Hearts 358/2 Days , Megalixir is an Item Panel which fully restores all of HP and Magic to you and your comrades in the same map.

In Kingdom Hearts Re:coded , Megalixir is an Item Command which fully restores all HP, cures all negative status effects, and raises Data-Sora 's clock level to its max.

Recipes

Kingdom Hearts

In Kingdom Hearts , it is created from List IV by using:

  • 5 Lucid Gem
  • 3 Lucid Crystal
  • 2 Gale
  • 1 Dark Matter

Kingdom Hearts II

In Kingdom Hearts II , the ability to create them is unlocked after successfully creating one Elixir . Adding a Serenity Gem to the recipe creates the Megalixir.

  • 2 Mythril Stone
  • 1 Power Stone
  • 1 Dark Stone
  • 1 Serenity Gem

Kingdom Hearts 358/2 Days

The Megalixir can be synthesized for 2500 munny once Roxas is promoted to Novice rank.

  • 1 Megalixir Recipe
  • 1 Mega-Potion
  • 1 Mega-Ether
  • 1 Shining Crystal
