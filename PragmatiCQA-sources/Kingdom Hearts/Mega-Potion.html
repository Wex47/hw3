Mega-Potion is an item found in Kingdom Hearts , Kingdom Hearts Final Mix , Kingdom Hearts: Chain of Memories , Kingdom Hearts Re:Chain of Memories , Kingdom Hearts 358/2 Days , Kingdom Hearts II , Kingdom Hearts II Final Mix , Kingdom Hearts Birth by Sleep , Kingdom Hearts Birth by Sleep Final Mix , Kingdom Hearts 3D: Dream Drop Distance and Kingdom Hearts III that restores HP . In the first game, it restores 30 HP for each party member, while in the second game it restores 40% of the entire party's HP.

In both games, it can be equipped for use in battle. In Kingdom Hearts: Chain of Memories and Kingdom Hearts Re:Chain of Memories , it quickly restores all attack cards and resets the reload counter. In Kingdom Hearts 358/2 Days , it heals 40% HP to all players in the same map.
