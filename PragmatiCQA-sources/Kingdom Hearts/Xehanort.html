Xehanort may refer to:

Xehanort is the main antagonist of the Kingdom Hearts series, he appears in many forms.

  • Master Xehanort is the original Xehanort as an old man and the main antagonist of Birth by Sleep and Kingdom Hearts III . He is the leader of the real Organization XIII .
    • Young Xehanort is the younger self of Master Xehanort who had power over time, and a member of the real Organization XIII.
    • No Heart is a data simulation of Xehanort as an adult in his armor.
  • Terra-Xehanort is Master Xehanort's heart possessing Terra 's body, and a member of the real Organization XIII.
    • The Dark Figure is a Heartless formed by the conflict between Terra and Xehanort within Terra-Xehanort, acting as a container for Terra's heart on occasions.
    • Xemnas is the Nobody of Terra-Xehanort and the leader of the original Organization XIII, as well as a member of the real Organization XIII.
    • Robed Figure is Terra-Xehanort's Heartless in his natural state.
      • Ansem, Seeker of Darkness is Terra-Xehanort's Heartless upon gaining a body, as well as a member of the real Organization XIII.
        • Riku-Ansem is the result of Riku taking on the form of Ansem, Seeker of Darkness after using the power of darkness to defeat Roxas .

Numerous characters throughout the series also act as hosts for a fragment of Xehanort's heart.

  • Braig became host for a piece of Xehanort's heart prior to his battle with Aqua.
    • Xigbar is a host for a piece of Xehanort's heart, as well as a member of the former and real Organization XIII.
  • Vanitas became host for a piece of Xehanort's heart at some point prior to his final battle with Ventus in Birth by Sleep , and is a member of the real Organization XIII.
    • Ventus-Vanitas was the result of Vanitas taking Ventus' body, the resultant entity retained the fragment of Xehanort's heart which Vanitas possessed.
  • Saïx became a vessel for a piece of Xehanort's heart prior to 358/2 Days , as well as a member of the former and real Organization XIII.
    • Anti-Saïx is a non-canon boss in Mission Mode.
  • The Real Organization XIII and their reserves each acted as a vessel for a piece of Xehanort's heart.
    • Luxord
    • Marluxia
    • Larxene
    • Dark Riku
    • Xion
    • The Replica Xehanorts
    • Demyx
    • Vexen
  • Anti-Aqua is the result of Aqua being corrupted by darkness thanks to Ansem, Seeker of Darkness.
