The No Name ( ノーネーム , Nō Nēmu ) is a Keychain for Terra's , Ventus's , and Aqua's Keyblades that appears in the North American, PAL, and Final Mix versions of Kingdom Hearts Birth by Sleep and Kingdom Hearts Birth by Sleep Final Mix . Terra , Ventus , and Aqua are rewarded with the No Name for defeating Young Xehanort . Young Xehanort later wields a variation of this Keyblade in Kingdom Hearts 3D: Dream Drop Distance and Kingdom Hearts III .

Design

The Keyblade is predominantly colored in different shades of blue and is decorated with elaborate, spiked shapes. The tip of No Name appears to be a stopwatch, while the teeth are diamond shape spikes that are connected by a black web structure similar to the design on the base of the top spire of Memory's Skyscraper .

The Keychain shares the time theme represented in the blade, as the charm on the Keychain appears to be an hourglass. The No Name resembles the weapons used by Young Xehanort; the blade features an ever-present cyan glow, similar to the same weapons wielded by Young Xehanort.

"No Name" refers to the mystery that surrounded Young Xehanort's identity, at the time he was introduced.
