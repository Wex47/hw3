Armor Gummis add to the maximum limit of the Gummi Ship 's Armor Gauge. In Kingdom Hearts and Kingdom Hearts Final Mix , they come in the most unique shapes of all. Armor Gummis come in three types: Protect-G, Shell-G, and Dispel-G. All of these are named after spells from the Final Fantasy video game series ; the spells all have to do with protection, or removing protection.

Protect-G

Protect-G are the weakest of the Armor Gummis. They are easily able to acquire early in the game from simply driving from world to world in the Gummi Ship. Its primary color is red, and can only have one color, unlike the more powerful Armor Gummis. Protect-G are the only Armor Gummis that can be purchased from Cid in Traverse Town .
