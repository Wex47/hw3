The Yellow Opera is an Emblem Heartless that is found throughout the Kingdom Hearts series.

Design

A Yellow Opera has a smooth, conical, yellow body and has a Heartless emblem is emblazoned on its chest. The top of its body flares outward and forms a jagged "collar" below the Heartless's spherical, pitch black head. It also wears a conical, orange hat that has a jagged brim, a yellow and green band, and a thin, slightly bent tip. Unlike many Heartless in its family, this tip is very long and did not curl up in Kingdom Hearts , though it did in Kingdom Hearts 358/2 Days . Like many Heartless, the Yellow Opera has glowing yellow eyes. Its seemingly useless feet sport three small, thin toes.

Like other Heartless in its family, the Yellow Opera's name refers to a color—in this case, yellow—and a musical term—in this case, an opera, which is a theatrical work combining drama, music, song and sometimes dance, and specifically the name refers to the score for such a work here.

Strategy

Yellow Operas utilize Thunder magic. Because of this, the Yellow Opera absorbs any kind of Thunder magic that any party member attempts to cast on it, but are affected by all other types of magic. They are the fastest of their kind, and dart here and there like bees. Like the rest of their family, Yellow Operas are most common in Wonderland once the Hollow Bastion Keyhole is revealed.
