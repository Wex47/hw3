Wing Gummis are Gummi Blocks that improve the maneuverability of the Gummi Ship in Kingdom Hearts and Kingdom Hearts II .

Kingdom Hearts

Like the Armor Gummis , the Wing Gummis can be sold to Cid in Traverse Town , but for rather low prices. However, these Gummi Blocks can come in great quantity during travel between worlds. Like all of the Gummi Blocks' from Kingdom Hearts , Wing Gummis share their name with spells from the popular Final Fantasy video game series .

In such, these spells all have Wind-based effects, either dealing Wind damage, making the user float, or dropping the enemy's health to a single low digits with a tornado. Three of these spells, Aero , Aerora , and Aeroga , also appear in Kingdom Hearts . For the best handling, use the Tornado-G Gummi Blocks. Aero-G can be purchased from Cid from Traverse Town for 20 Munny , and the Aerora-G for 40 Munny .
