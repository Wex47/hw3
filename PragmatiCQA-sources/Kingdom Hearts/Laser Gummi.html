When using the Laser Gummis , a blue icon will appear when an enemy is in the distance. At this time, your Laser Gummi(s) has locked onto an enemy. Press the Circle button the fire away, probably obliterating the enemy ship! You can fire at as many enemies as you have lasers attached to your ship. Using the Laser Gummis to attack enemy ships will drain the Power Gauge. Like all other Gummi Pieces, the Laser Gummis are named off of spells from the Final Fantasy video game series . These particular Gummi Pieces are named off of the most powerful spells in the Final Fantasy video game series: Comet, Meteor, and Ultima.

Comet-G

The Comet-G is the weakest of the three models of Laser Gummis. The Comet-G fires one simple, straight laser during flight. Early in the game, the Comet-G is available commonly in treasure chests around the worlds in Kingdom Hearts . Their default primary color is red, they have a small white trim, and have a yellow cone on its front end. The Comet-G can be purchased from Cid in Traverse Town for 500 Munny .

Comet-G

  • Size: 1x1x2
  • Max: 8
  • Price: 500
  • Armor: 30
  • Power: 100
  • Power Use: 0.1

Meteor-G

The Meteor-G is the middle in order of strength of the three models of Laser Gummis. During flight, the Meteor-G fires its lasers at a large slant, which can be quite aggravating. The Meteor-G isn't quite as commonly found in treasure chests in Kingdom Hearts as the Comet-G. Their default primary color is red, they too have a small white trim, and a yellow cone's tip, similar to the one on the Comet-G, is visible inside the hollow of the Meteor-G, suggesting that the Comet-G may be underneath. The Meteor-G can be purchased from Cid in Traverse Town for 1500 Munny.

Meteor-G

  • Size: 1x1x2
  • Max: 6
  • Price: 1500
  • Armor: 35
  • Power: 300
  • Power Use: 0.1

Ultima-G

The Ultima-G is the most powerful Laser Gummi, or weapon for this matter, to mount on a Gummi Ship . Its laser will obliterate the first enemy ship it finds, and then continue on to hit a second enemy! If the first enemy to be hit is extremely powerful, the Ultima-G's laser will circle around and attack the enemy again, hitting the same enemy twice! The Ultima-G is incredibly rare; few are found in treasure chests. Their default primary color is red, with another small white trim. It also has a light blue cone on its front end, similar to the yellow cone on the Comet-G. The Ultima-G can be purchased from Cid in Traverse Town for 2500 Munny.

Ultima-G

  • Size: 1x1x3
  • Max: 4
  • Price: 2500
  • Armor: 40
  • Power: 1000
  • Power Use: 0.1
