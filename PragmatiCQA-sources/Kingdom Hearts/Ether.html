An Ether ( エーテル , Ēteru ) is an item that appears in the Kingdom Hearts series , usually used to restore a certain amount of Magic or MP . In Kingdom Hearts and Kingdom Hearts Final Mix , an Ether restores 3 MP. In Kingdom Hearts II and Kingdom Hearts II Final Mix , it fully restores MP if Sora has at least one MP remaining. If he is in MP charge, it can be used to reduce the MP Charge Gauge.

In Kingdom Hearts: Chain of Memories and Kingdom Hearts Re:Chain of Memories , an Ether card quickly reloads all magic cards. In Kingdom Hearts 358/2 Days , it is an item panel that restores one cast of each magic panel that is installed. In Kingdom Hearts Birth by Sleep and Kingdom Hearts Birth by Sleep Final Mix , it restores some of the Focus Gauge . In Kingdom Hearts Re:coded it raises Data-Sora 's clock level by one.
