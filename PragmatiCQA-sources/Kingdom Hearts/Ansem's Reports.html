Ansem's Report is a series of pages collected throughout Kingdom Hearts and Kingdom Hearts II . The report explores the mythology of the series, and focuses largely on the nature of beings, what they become ( Heartless and Nobodies ), and the nature of darkness and nothingness.

The second report, also known as the Secret Ansem's Report , also explains the background and plans of specific characters, primarily Xehanort , Naminé , and Organization XIII . Though both reports are originally thought to be written by Ansem the Wise , he claims in his Secret Ansem's Report that he is only responsible for Ansem's Report part 1, while his apprentice Xehanort stole his identity and wrote Ansem's Report parts 2-13. It is much more clear, though, that Xehanort wrote the Ansem's Report parts 10–13.

Story

Ansem began work on the report in order to study the darkness in the hearts of beings, and to determine ways to protect his homeworld, Radiant Garden , from its ravages. The reports were later written at some point by Xehanort. Ansem later wrote a new set of reports after his escape from the Realm in Between.

Trivia

  • Ansem mentions "earlier reports" detailing his research into darkness, though he claims that he only wrote the first report, which merely posed his question. The earlier reports also chronicle experiences that were only his, such as his meeting with King Mickey. While some of this can be explained by Xehanort's assumption of Ansem, which may have led him to pretend to have Ansem's memories as well, it does not explain Ansem's mention of earlier reports. Especially because the pages in Kingdom Hearts were called simply the "Ansem's Report", it is possible that there were other side reports that Ansem wrote between Ansem's Report 1 and Ansem's Report 2.
  • Simlarly, Xehanort mentions in Report 12 that he will record more details of the Realm of Darkness in another report, but this report has never surfaced. It is possible that this was going to be the original plan for the phrase "Ansem's other report" in the conceptual Another side, Another story... video, but the plan was changed to Ansem the Wise's secret reports instead.
  • A further inconsistency appears with the mention of Ansem's disciples working with Xehanort to study the Heartless. No mention is made of them in the original report, and indeed, it seems to imply that Xehanort was working completely alone.
