Story

Kingdom Hearts

Dumbo survived the destruction of his homeworld, but only as the Summon Gem Watergleam , which wound up in Monstro where it was found by Sora. Sora took the gem to the Fairy Godmother in Traverse Town , and she activates the gem to allow Dumbo to assist Sora as a summon.

Abilities

When summoned, Dumbo hovers in midair with Sora riding on his back. The two are invulnerable to harm until Dumbo is dismissed or runs out of MP. Dumbo blasts a jet of water from his trunk at the enemies, drenching them, pushing them backwards or defeating them. Dumbo can also ascend and descend in flight. Dumbo is a useful Summon and is best used when surrounded by Heartless .

When Sora summons Dumbo in Neverland , Dumbo is able to fly freely in the air instead of just hovering around. Sora shrinks to a smaller size to ride Dumbo during this summon.

In Kingdom Hearts: Chain of Memories , Dumbo appears as a Summon Card as a reward for completing Monstro . In the remake, Kingdom Hearts Re:Chain of Memories , summoning Dumbo causes Sora to leave the battlefield for the duration of the technique.
