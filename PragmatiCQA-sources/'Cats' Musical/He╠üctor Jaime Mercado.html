Biography (1982)

HECTOR JAIME MERCADO's ( Alonzo ) last B'way appearance was the role of Bernardo in West Side Story . Other B'way credits: Your Arms Too Short... , 1600 Pennsylvania Avenue , Dr. Jazz . Mr. Mercado's career began as a principal dancer with Alvin Ailey and has since expanded with numerous film and TV credits: Slow Dancing in the Big City , Hair , A Question of Honor , "The Guiding Light," "Love of Life," "One Life to Live."
