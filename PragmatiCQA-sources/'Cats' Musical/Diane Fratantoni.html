Biography (1982)

DIANE FRATANTONI ( Standby ) of Wilmington, Delaware, graduated from Carnegie-Mellon University. She is a veteran of the Pittsburgh Civic Light Opera and played Diana Morales in the international, national and Broadway companies of A Chorus Line . Credits include: No Place To Be Somebody , Ring 'Round the Moon and Bus Stop and concerts for Marvin Hamlish and Ricky Ian Gordon.
