Anna McNeely is an American actress who originated the role of Jennyanydots in the Broadway Production of Cats . She remained in the production for almost seven years.

Biography

1982

ANNA McNEELY ( Jennyanydots ) made her Broadway debut last season as Mrs. Kenworth in Little Johnny Jones , a role she originated at the Goodspeed Opera House. She has performed in three productions of Side by Side by Sondheim and is a six-season veteran of Illinois's Little Theatre on the Square.

1987

ANNA McNEELY ( Jennyanydots ) created the role of Jennyanydots, the Gumbie Cat, as a member of the original Broadway cast of Cats . She made her Broadway debut as 'Mrs. Kenworth' in Little Johnny Jones , a role she originated at the Goodspeed Opera House. Also to her credit are 3 regional productions of Side by Side by Sondheim. She is a six-season veteran of Illinois's Little Theatre on the Square.
