Biography (2019)

Naoimh graduated from Laine Theatre Arts and was a member of the ensemble in Mary Poppins , West Side Story , and Cinderella . She was also a featured singer/dancer in Broadway to the Bay , P&O Australia Cruises , Sleeping Beauty , Flying at 40 , and Miranda Sings! Naoimh played Rumpleteazer in the 2018 leg of the UK/International Tour and reprised the role in the 2019 Cats movie .
