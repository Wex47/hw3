Kenneth "Ken" Page (born January 20, 1954) is an American actor and cabaret singer, best known as the voice of Oogie Boogie in the Tim Burton movie The Nightmare Before Christmas , and Old Deuteronomy in the original Broadway production and 1998 film version of Cats .

Biography (1982)

KEN PAGE ( Old Deuteronomy ) made his Broadway debut as Nicely-Nicely Johnson in the revival of Guys and Dolls winning a Theatre World Award. He next appeared as The Lion in The Wiz . On Broadway and TV, Ken appeared in Ain't Misbehavin' for which he won the N.Y. Drama Desk Award as Best Actor in a Musical.
