The original Australian production in Sydney released a full cast recording, produced by EMI and released in 1986, the year following the show's opening. It is currently available as a double CD, with a complete lyric booklet.

This recording is notably the only English language recording with every song from the show (excluding alternate versions of the same songs).

  • Musical Director: Peter Casey
  • Deputy Musical Director: David King
  • Conductor: Peter Casey
