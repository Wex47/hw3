Biography

1987

Suzanne Viverito has toured with Bob Fosse's Dancin', the national company of A Chorus Line, and the Hubbard Street Dance Co. Other musical credits include featured roles in Chicago, Brigadoon, West Side Story and choreography of Merrily We Roll Along and the children's Nutcracker . Suzanne is married to actor Vince Viverito who is always working, but always there.

2000

SUZANNE VIVERITO (Dance Captain) is grateful to be at the Winter Garden for many cat lives. She toured with Dancin' , A Chorus Line & Hubbard Street Dance Co. Suzanne also teaches and choreographs in her spare time. Love & thanks to husband Vince & son Andrew.
