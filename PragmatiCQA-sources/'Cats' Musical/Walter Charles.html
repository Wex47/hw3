Biography (1982)

WALTER CHARLES ( The Cats Chorus ) appeared on Broadway in Sweeney Todd , Grease , 1600 Pennsylvania Avenue and Knickerbocker Holiday at Town Hall. TV credits include the 1981 Tony Awards, "The Doctors," "Love of Life" and "All My Children." He has worked in stock and regional theatres from coast to coast.
