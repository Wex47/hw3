Role

Jellylorum / Griddlebone

Susan Jane Tanner is an English stage actress. She originated the roles of Jellylorum and Griddlebone in the London production of Cats , as well as Madame Thénardier in the London production of Les Misérables . She reprised her role as Jellylorum in the 1998 Cats film .

Biography

1981

SUSAN JANE TANNER started her career travelling the world with a dance act. She then joined a theatre group in America performing plays and musicals on board a cruise ship. Returning to England she appeared in a number of musicals including Kiss Me Kate and The Boyfriend and took over Barbara Windsor's part in Calamity Jane on the national tour. In summer 1980 she worked with John Inman in comedy sketches in Blackpool and at Christmas 1980 played in the musical Worzel Gummidge at Birmingham.
