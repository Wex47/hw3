Mette Towley is an American dancer who plays Cassandra in the 2019 Cats movie .

When she was initially cast, producers had not yet decided if her role was going to be Jemima or Cassandra. As a result, early press releases incorrectly announced that she would be playing Jemima . Eventually, the producers decided to go with Cassandra over Jemima for her character.

Career (2019)

Mette has previously worked with artists Pharrell Williams and Rihanna and has been featured in multiple music videos as both a dancer and choreographer. She is also a global brand ambassador for Revlon.
