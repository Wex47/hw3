Kolton Krouse is an American actor and dancer. He played multiple roles in the 2016 Broadway revival of Cats and was in the chorus of the 2019 Cats movie .

Biography

2016

KOLTON KROUSE ( Tumblebrutus ) was born and raised in Gilbert, Arizona, and is currently a student at The Juilliard School. He was recently awarded the Adele Astaire Scholarship Award. Thank you Wanda Manville, my family and teachers for all your support throughout the years.

Career

Kolton earned the title of National Senior Male Outstanding Dancer at the NYC Dance Alliance in 2014. He graduated from Julliard in 2018.
