Biography (1982)

CAROL RICHARDS ( The Cats Chorus ), a graduate of Northwestern University, has appeared on Broadway as April in Company , Bobbi in Last of the Red Hot Lovers and Gloria Upson in Mame . She played Rosella Gray on "The Edge of Night" and her voice can be heard on numerous radio and TV commercials.
