Career (2019)

Robbie made his Broadway debut as Jerry Mulligan in An American in Paris , later becoming nominated for a Tony Award. He was a principal dancer in the New York City Ballet until retiring in early 2018. Robbie portrays dancer Ted Shawn in the upcoming film adaptation of The Chaperone .
