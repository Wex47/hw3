Richard Armitage is a prominent English actor, known for his roles in Captain America: The First Avenger , The Hobbit and Oceans 8 . Prior to his film career, he played Macavity in the UK productions of Cats . He was also credited as a swing and stand-in in the 1998 Cats movie .
