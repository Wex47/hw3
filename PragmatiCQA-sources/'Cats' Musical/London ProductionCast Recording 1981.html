The cast recording of the original London cast was released in the UK in July 1981, and in the US in July 1982. Two versions were released: a two-disc "complete" edition, and a single-disc "highlights" edition. The recording was nominated for Best Cast Show Album at the 25th Annual Grammy Awards, but did not win.

Elaine Paige 's rendition of " Memory " from this recording was also released as a single, reaching #6 on the UK Singles Chart in July 1981.
