Biography (1982)

BOB MORRISEY ( Standby ), last seen as Rooster Hannigan in Broadway's Annie , also appeared as Pee Wee Reese in The First and in the Grand Tour . He was Lefty in the revival of Oh Kay! , and Off-Broadway credits include Billy Crocker in Anything Goes and Company , both at ELT.
