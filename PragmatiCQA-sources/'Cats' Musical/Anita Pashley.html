Biography (1981)

Anita Pashley graduated from the Bush Davies Schools with Honours in the three major dance forms - ballet, modern, and tap. She has since appeared in several pantomimes and summer shows and also a six-part television series with Billy Boyle. Her West End debut was as the Princess of Abubu in Kismet . After a season at the Haymarket Theatre Leicester she returned to the West End playing both the Upstairs Maid and the Scullery Maid in the highly successful revival of My Fair Lady .
