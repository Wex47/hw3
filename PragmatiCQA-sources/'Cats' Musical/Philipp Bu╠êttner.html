Biography (2019)

Philipp Büttner is a German Actor. He currently portrays Aladdin in Disney's Musical Aladdin in Stuttgart. His other theatre credits include: American Idiot (Johnny), West Side Story (Tony), Das Wunder Von Bern (Bruno).
