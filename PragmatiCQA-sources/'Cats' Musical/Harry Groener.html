Harry Groener is an American actor. He originated the role of Munkustrap in the original Broadway production of Cats, a role which earned him one of his three Tony nominations.

He has also had a successful television career, appearing in numerous television shows including Buffy the Vampire Slayer, Breaking Bad and Star Trek.

Biography (1982)

HARRY GROENER ( Munkustrap ) was last seen on Broadway in Is There Life After High School? and played West Mousada in Oh, Brother! He was nominated for a Tony Award and a Drama Desk Award and won a Theatre World Award for his performances as Will Parker in the Broadway revival of Oklahoma!
