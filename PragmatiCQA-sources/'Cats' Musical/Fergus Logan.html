Fergus Logan is a Scottish dancer, choreographer, and director who played Mistoffelees in London and Tumblebrutus in Cats: the Movie , along with numerous other roles in various West End shows, including Fosse and Oklahoma!

He has been responsible for recreating Cats for numerous productions, including the 2005 US Troika Tour , the 2006 UK Tour , and productions in Italy, Portugal, and Germany.
