"Beautiful Ghosts" is a song written especially for the 2019 Cats movie , with music by Andrew Lloyd Webber and lyrics by Taylor Swift .

The song is sung three times in the movie, first by Francesca Hayward 's Victoria ("Victoria's Song"), then a reprise by Hayward's Victoria and Judi Dench 's Old Deuteronomy ("Reprise"), and lastly by Taylor Swift over the end credits ("Single Version").

Swift's rendition of the song was released as a single by Polydor Records on 15 November 2019. It was later released again on 20 December 2019 as one of the songs on the movie's soundtrack , along with "Victoria's Song".

History

There are three versions of the song:

Victoria's Song

The main version of "Beautiful Ghosts" is credited as "Victoria's Song" in the movie's soundtrack . It is sung by Francesca Hayward 's Victoria in response to hearing Jennifer Hudson 's Grizabella sing " Memory Act 1 ". This version of the song is 2 minutes and 50 seconds long.

Lloyd Webber says this about the song: "What she's saying is, 'Ok, It's all very well for you. But you're looking back on a life where you did something wonderful in your life. You were glamorous. You had beautiful ghosts. I've nothing at all. I've been abandoned.' Victoria is saying, 'Maybe one day I'll dance like you did with beautiful ghosts.' "

Reprise

A 30-second reprise is sung by Hayward's Victoria and Judi Dench 's Old Deuteronomy , in response to hearing Hudson's Grizabella sing " Memory ".

Single Version

The third version is the Single Version sung by Taylor Swift . This rendition plays over the movie's end credits and was released as a single by Polydor Records on 15 November 2019. This version of "Beautiful Ghosts" is 4 minutes and 21 seconds long, with an additional verse not included in "Victoria's Song".
