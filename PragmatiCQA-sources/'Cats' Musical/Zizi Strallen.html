Zizi Strallen is a British actress, singer, and dancer, best known for portraying Mary Poppins in the West End. She has been involved in multiple productions of Cats .

Biography (2019)

She first appeared on Merrily We Roll Along as Meg Kincaid in 2013, later portraying Young Phyllis in Follies on West End in 2017 and the titular role in Mary Poppins during the UK Tour in October 2015. Zizi was announced to reprise her role in autumn 2019 at the Prince Edward Theatre. She portrayed Demeter in Cats on the UK Tour and in the West End Revival in 2014, and portrayed Tantomile in the 2019 Cats Movie .
