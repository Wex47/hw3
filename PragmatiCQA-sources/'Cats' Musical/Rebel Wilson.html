Rebel Melanie Elizabeth Wilson is an Australian actress, writer, and producer. She is best known for her comedy work and portrayal of 'Fat Amy' in the Pitch Perfect franchise. She portrays Jennyanydots in the 2019 feature film adaptation of Cats .

Career (2018)

She first rose to prominence after writing and performing in her debut stage musical, the Westie Monologues in Sydney, Australia. Her follow-up stage productions, which she wrote, produced and starred in include Spunks and Confessions of an Exchange Student . Rebel later joined the Judd Aptow film, Bridesmaids, as well as starring in all three Pitch Perfect films to critical acclaim. She has appeared as Ursula, the sea witch, in a Hollywood Bowl production of The Little Mermaid in June 2016. She made her West End debut in Guys and Dolls in the supporting role of Miss Adelaide. She is set to star in a remake of Private Benjamin in 2019, and was cast in Cats in November 2018.
