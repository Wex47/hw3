The Cats Movie 2019 Soundtrack was released by Polydor Records, a Universal Music Group record label.

The soundtrack includes a new song for Victoria titled " Beautiful Ghosts ", with lyrics by Taylor Swift and music by Andrew Lloyd Webber . In the movie, a 30-second reprise of this song is sung by Old Deuteronomy , in addition to a rendition by Taylor Swift that plays over the end credits.

Cats: Highlights From The Motion Picture Soundtrack was released on 20 December 2019. This is a highlights edition that does not include all of the songs from the movie.
