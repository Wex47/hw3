Fiona Grace McDougal is a British singer and was the vocal coach for both the 2016 Broadway Revival and the 2019 Cats Movie .

Cats History

Broadway 2016 - Vocal Coach

Cats Movie 2019 - Vocal Coach

Career (2019)

Fiona graduated from the Paul McCartney Liverpool Institute of Performing Arts with a degree in Music, and is based in London. She has previously assisted the casts of School of Rock , Cats (Revival), the X Factor , Chess the Musical , Phantom of the Opera and Love Never Dies.
