Biography (1981)

Sharon Lee-Hill began her professional career at the age of 15 in her native Australia, appearing in theatre and television. Her theatrical debut in Britain was as Tricia in A Chorus Line and covering the leading role of Cassie, a part she played for several weeks. Her other West End musical appearances include Marie in Bar Mitzvah Boy and Gina in Flowers for Algernon . She also has extensive experience in repertory and fringe theatre and has appeared on television in A Moment In Time.
