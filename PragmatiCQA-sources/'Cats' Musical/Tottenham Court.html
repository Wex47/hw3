Tottenham Court Road is an area of London mentioned in " Grizabella, the Glamour Cat " in relation to Grizabella . She apparently visited it a lot during her heyday. It is implied to be a particularly seedy place, and the real Tottenham Court Road was a prostitution area during T S Eliot 's time. The area is defined by reference to local pubs, "The Rising Sun" and "The Friend At Hand"

“ She haunted many a low resort Near the grimy road of Tottenham Court; She flitted about the No Man's Land From The Rising Sun to The Friend at Hand . ”
— " Grizabella, the Glamour Cat "
