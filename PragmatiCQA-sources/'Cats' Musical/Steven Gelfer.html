Steven Gelfer was an American stage actor, singer, and dancer. He originated the role of Carbucketty in the Broadway Production of Cats . Steven died of leukemia in 1988 at the age of 39.

Biography (1982)

STEVEN GELFER's ( Carbucketty ) New York credits include West Side Story , Gypsy , The Beggar's Opera , American Dance Machine , Whoopee , and N.Y.C. Opera. A graduate of N.Y.U. and Indiana University, he has choreographed productions for Equity Library Theatre, Pittsburgh Civic Light Opera, and Lakewood, the State Theatre of Maine.
