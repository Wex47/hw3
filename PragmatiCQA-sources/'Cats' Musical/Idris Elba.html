Idris Elba , OBE, is a British actor, producer, musician and DJ. He is known for his dramatic roles on the BBC One Series Luther as well as playing the title role in Mandela: Long Walk to Freedom . Idris portrays the antagonist Macavity in the 2019 Cats movie .

Career (2018)

Idris left school in 1988, and won a place in the National Youth Music Theatre thanks to a £1,500 Prince's Trust grant. He first appeared on BBC children's dramas and deejayed nightclubs in his early twenties. He appeared in a 2001 episode of Law & Order and later had a multi-episode arc on the American version of The Office. He starred in the six-part TV series Luther, for which he won a Golden Globe award. Idris first appeared in the film Daddy's Little Girls, playing the lead role, and later joined the casts of Thor, Pacific Rim , and the Dark Tower . He was announced as part of the cast in Cats in October 2018.
