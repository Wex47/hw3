Biography (1982)

SUSAN POWERS ( The Cats Chorus ) most recent performances include Radio City Music Hall, Neil Simon's revival of Little Me and Woman of the Year with both Lauren Bacall and Raquel Welch. Susan is a native of Dallas and has been in New York for the last three years.
