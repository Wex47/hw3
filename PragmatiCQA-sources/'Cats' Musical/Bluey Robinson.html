Career (2019)

Bluey was born in Stockholm, Sweden, before moving to South London at 3 months old. As a child, he performed at an event for the Prince of Wales and gained the role of Young Simba in The Lion King on West End. He performed a recurring role on East Enders and has since released two mixtapes, titled The Late Shift (2012) and The Red Room (2016) respectively. Bluey previously opened for the tours of Justin Bieber, D'angelo, Chance the Rapper, and Alicia Keys.
