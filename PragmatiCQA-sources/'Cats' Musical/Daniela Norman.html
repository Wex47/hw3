Career (2019)

Daniela previously danced with the English National Ballet in Swan Lake, The Nutcracker, Le Corsaire, The Sleeping Beauty, Coppelia, The Dream, La Fille Mal Gardee, and Don Quixote. She also was a member of the Ensemble in the 2018 film version of An American in Paris: The Musical.
