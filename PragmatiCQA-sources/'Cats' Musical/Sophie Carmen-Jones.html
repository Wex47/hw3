Biography (2019)

Born in Swanesa, Sophie made a long journey to London in 2007 and graduated from Laine Theatre Arts in 2010. Upon graduating, Sophie was cast in the West End production of WE WiLL ROCK YOU, covering the role Meatloaf. After leaving the show she landed a role in the ensemble of WICKED at the Apollo Theatre. Then Sophie would go on to appear in the Spice Girls musical VIVA FOREVER, covering the roles Viva and Holly. She has also played the role of Francine in JERSEY BOYS at the Piccadilly Theatre. Sophie then starred alongside famous John Partridge (Billy Flynn) as Velma Kelly. Sophie made her Television debut in 2016 playing the guest role of Kate in the BBC Drama CASUAlTY.
