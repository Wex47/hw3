The Maitre D' (meaning head waiter) is portrayed by Cory English in the 2019 Cats movie .

Role

“ When Bustopher Jones, who is played by James Corden , starts looking for something to eat I'm the one to say, 'Ohhh try this bin. Here's this.' So I do a lot of stuff with James Corden. ”
— Cory English

The Maitre D' is a meek cat that follows Bustopher Jones around until the latter is kidnapped by Macavity . When Macavity's agents crash the " Jellicle Ball ", he sticks close to Old Deuteronomy , scared but still trying to protect the Jellicle leader.

Originally, the Maitre D' was just meant to be a small cameo in the Bustopher Jones sequence . His role was extended after a week of rehearsals such that he appears throughout the film.

Appearance

Maitre D' is a tuxedo cat.
