Reed Jones was a dancer and choreographer whose credits included Skimbleshanks in the original Broadway Production of Cats and Big Deal in the Jerome Robbins revival of West Side Story . He died due to AIDS-related illness in 1989 at the age of 35.

Biography (1982)

REED JONES ( Skimbleshanks ) seen on Broadway in Peter Pan, West Side Story and recently, Play Me A Country Song; on national tour with A Chorus Line; with N.Y.C. Opera in Street Scene (PBS broadcast); and on film as Ian Scott in The Fan. Reed directed/choreographed Radio City's Count Basie tribute.
