Biography (2002)

Training: Julie trained as a gymnast and then at the Joyce Butler School of Dancing and Arts Educational School.

Theatre: Julie appeared as Meg Giry in The Phantom of the Opera (Hamburg) and understudied and played the part of Meg Giry in the UK tour of The Phantom of the Opera , original cast of Fame, The Musical (Cambridge Theatre), ballet dancer in Song & Dance (European tour), understudied and played Frenchie, Miss Lynch and Cha Cha in Grease (national tour), the Enchantress in Disney's Beauty and the Beast (Dominion Theatre), and, most recently, Dick Whittington (Sadler's Wells Theatre).

Television: Julie has appeared in several television shows including The Generation Game , Ann & Nick , Talking Telephone Numbers , Children in Need and The Big Breakfast .
