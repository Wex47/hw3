The Cast Recording of the Original Broadway Cast was released by Geffen Records on January 26, 1983. Two versions were released: a two-disc "complete" edition, and a single-disc "highlights" edition.

The album was recorded by the entire cast, including the swings , in October 1982. It was certified Platinum by the Recording Industry Association of America on December 5, 1988, and has sold over 2 million copies. The recording also won Best Cast Show Album at the 26th Annual Grammy Awards.
