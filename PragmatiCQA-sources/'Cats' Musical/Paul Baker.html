Roles in Cats

Cats Movie 1998

Role

Credited Dubbed Voice
Paul Baker is a West End Actor, most well known for playing Rusty in Starlight Express and Phillip Sallon in Boy George's musical "Taboo".

Cats Dubbing

Paul is credited as providing extra vocals for Mungojerrie in the film of Cats . However anyone familiar with Drew Varley can confirm that it is Drew Varley's voice as Mungojerrie, not Paul Baker's.

Other Work

Paul has featured in many West End musicals including Chicago, Starlight Express, Taboo, and the title role in a big-budget musical about "Napoleon".
