Biography (1982)

JOEL ROBERTSON ( The Cats Chorus ) was Fyedka on Broadway and the national tour of Fiddler on the Roof . Off-Broadway Joel performed in Snapshot and Getting Out . Regional and stock shows include West Side Story and Diamond Studs . Joel was a member/dancer at the Maine State Ballet Company.
