Corey John Snide is an American actor and dancer. He played Coricopat in the 2016 Broadway revival and was in the chorus of the 2019 Cats movie .

Biography

Corey competed in multiple dance competitions as a child, representing the United States in Riesa and winning the Outstanding Dancer Award from the NYC Dance Alliance. He portrayed the titular role in Billy Elliot on West End in 2007, and in Sydney in 2008. He was an understudy for Evan in 13, and graduated from Julliard in 2015. He was the dance captain of the CATS Broadway Revival while also playing the role of Coricopat , as well as covering Mistoffelees and all 9 male tracks in the show.
