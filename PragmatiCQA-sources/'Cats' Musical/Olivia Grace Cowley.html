Biography (2019)

Olivia trained at The Royal Ballet Upper School and then graduated into The Royal Ballet Company. She was promoted to soloist in 2013. She has originated many roles for Wayne McGregor .

Ballet Credits: Mayerling, Anastasia, The Invitation, Apollo, The Dream, Swan Lake, La Sylphide, Winter Dreams, Cinderella, Manon, Sleeping Beauty, Romeo and Juliet, The Nutcracker, Don Quixote, Medusa , and more.
