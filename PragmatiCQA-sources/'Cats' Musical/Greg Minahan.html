Biography (1987)

GREG MINAHAN ( Dance Captain ), since graduating from Harvard ('76), has performed on Broadway with the American Dance Machine, where he met his wife Nancy Chismar; in Peter Pan, during which they were married; and in the New York company of Cats, which shared the arrival of little Sheena Mari.
