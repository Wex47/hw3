Biography (2019)

Roxanne is a former World Trampolining Champion. She also has an extensive dance background and is one of the best b-girls in the world. She has been featured in countless stage shows, T.V projects, and commercials such as The Royal Variety show and The Beijing Olympics . She holds two Guinness World Records for headspins, and has represented the UK at numerous international competitions including the UK B-Boy Championships World Finals, Battle of the Year World Finals, The Notorious IBE, Outbreak and being the first female to ever compete at the R16 world finals.
