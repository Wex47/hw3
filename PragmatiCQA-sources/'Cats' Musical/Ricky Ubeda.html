Ricky Ubeda is an American dancer and actor. He is best known for winning Season 11 of the popular televised dance competition So You Think You Can Dance , as well as playing the role of Mistoffelees in the Broadway revival of Cats .

Biography (2016)

RICKY UBEDA ( Mistoffelees ). Broadway: On the Town . Off-Broadway: Freddie Falls in Love . Television: “So You Think You Can Dance” (season 11 winner), “Live with Kelly and Michael,” The Ellen DeGeneres Show.” Ricky has also performed with Shaping Sound Dance Co. and as a soloist for Sia.
