Seeta Indrani is an English dancer and actress who originated the role of Cassandra . She has since acted in various television series, most notably as WPC Norika Datta in The Bill (1989-1998) and as Dr. Lily Hassan in Doctors (2008-2010).

Biography (1981)

Seeta Indrani trained at the London School of Contemporary Dance. Since leaving she has worked as a soloist with Schweizer Kammerballet, appeared with the Maas Movers dance company and performed with ID Dance Co. She has played Simon of Legree in The King and I at the London Palladium and in Aladdin at the Shaw Theatre. On television she played the part of an Amazon in Timon of Athens . She also teaches dance and is part of the Contemporary Dance Trust Teaching Team.
