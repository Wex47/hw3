Christine Langner is an American actress and dancer. She originated the roles of Etcetera and Rumpleteazer on Broadway . She has since retired from performing and is now a Pilates instructor.

Biography

1982

CHRISTINE LANGNER ( Etcetera / Rumpleteazer ) started her career in the "Milliken Show" and in numerous commercials. She made her Broadway debut in Bring Back Birdie , played Cathy in "Daddy I Don't Like This" on CBS and toured Japan as a New York Fire Cracker. She recently appeared in Sleep Around Town at Playwrights Horizons.

1986

Christine Langer decided that after doing Cats on Broadway she was in need of a feline fix and is pouncing back after attending NYU as a drama major! (Phew) Other theatre credits include Bring Back Birdie on Broadway and Sleep Around Town at Playwrights Horizons. On television, she was seen in Heartbeat, The Almost Royal Family, The Cosby Show and Roy's Baby on October 22nd.
