Biography (2019)

Jonadette was born in the Philippines. As a child, she did ballet at a local studio but also danced hip hop for fun. Upon moving to South Wales at the age of seven, she picked up different styles of streetdance. She has previously joined World of Dance, International Battle Paris, and BBC Young Dancers. She also served as a backup dancer for singer Dua Lipa. She is a member of Buckness Personified, an all-female Krump crew.
