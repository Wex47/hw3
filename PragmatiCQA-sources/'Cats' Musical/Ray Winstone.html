Ray Winstone is an English film and television actor. He played Growltiger in the 2019 Cats movie .

Career (2019)

Ray first performed What a Crazy World at Theatre Royal Stratford, London, while attending Corona Theatre School, and took part in multiple stage productions before transitioning to television. He portrayed Will Scarlet in Robin of Sherwood , and later appeared on The Sweeney , Get-back , and Fairy Secret Army . Ray has starred in the hit film Love, Honour, and Obey , as well as in Sexy Beast , To The Green Fields Beyond , and King Arthur . He has been nominated for 2 BAFTA awards and 1 Emmy award for his work.
