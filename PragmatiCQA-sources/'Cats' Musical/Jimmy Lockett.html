Jimmy Lockett is an American actor and composer. He was the final performer to portray Old Deuteronomy in the original Broadway production of Cats .

Biography (2000)

JIMMY LOCKETT ( Old Deuteronomy ) attended Julliard School (Composition). Broadway: Big River . Off-Broadway: Little Shop of Horrors . National and international tours: Little Shop of Horrors , Big River , Starlight Express , The Music of Andrew Lloyd Webber and Cats . Composer: Opus Dance Ensemble, Kirk Offerle Dance Company and Ralph Perkins Dance Company in Las Vegas.Jimmy is a licensed paramedic (NREMT-P).
