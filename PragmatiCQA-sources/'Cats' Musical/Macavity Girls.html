The Macavity Girls are minor characters in the 2019 Cats movie , appearing only in the " Macavity " number. They are portrayed by Hannah Faith Marram , Sophie Carmen-Jones , Chelsea Marie Hogg and Jade Albertsen .

Role

The Macavity Girls work for Macavity and Bombalurina . They help catnip the jellicles and join Bombalurina in singing about Macavity.

Appearance

They are ginger cats with light bengal markings.They all wear bows around their neck.
