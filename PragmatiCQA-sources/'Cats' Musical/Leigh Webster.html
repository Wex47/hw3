Biography

1985

Leigh Janine Webster, a native Californian, has played Judy Turner and Kristine in the national and international companies of A Chorus Line. Her films include Pennies from Heaven, One From The Heart, Fast Forward and Girls Just Want to Have Fun.

1987

Leigh Webster was Cassandra in the L.A. company of Cats . She played Judy with the National and International companies of A Chorus Line . Films include Pennies From Heaven, One From The Heart, Fast Forward and Girls Just Wanna Have Fun .
