Biography (2019)

Jon graduated from Laine Theatre Arts in 2009, and went on to join the Ensembles of Mamma Mia, Peter Pan, and Miss Saigon . He portrayed Bill Bailey from 2015-2016 in both Blackpool and London, before joining the cast of the 2019 movie. Jon has been previously cast in featured roles in Cinderella, Mary Poppins Returns, and Arifa . He has choreographed and directed productions of Hamilton and The Wind in the Willows.
