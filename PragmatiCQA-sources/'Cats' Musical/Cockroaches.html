The Cockroaches are eight minor characters in the 2019 film adaptation of Cats . They are portrayed by Abigayle Benton , Erica Jayne Alden , Ella G , Emma Caffrey , Hannah Amin , Jazz Peters , Selina Hamilton , and Bethany Huckle . They are confirmed to appear in the Old Gumbie Cat sequence of the film.
