Role

At the Jellicle Ball, she is kidnapped by Macavity after refusing to make him the Jellicle choice. He disappears her to Growltiger's ship. Then Growltiger starts making her walk the plank. Luckily, Mistoffelees saves her before she falls into the water.

Old Deuteronomy sings The Moments of Happiness , Ad-Dressing of Cats , and parts of Beautiful Ghosts reprise and Old Deuteronomy .
