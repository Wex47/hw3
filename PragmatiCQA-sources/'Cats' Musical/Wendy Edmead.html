Wendy Edmead is an American stage and film actress. She originated the role of Demeter on Broadway .

Biography (1982)

WENDY EDMEAD ( Demeter ), a graduate of Performing Arts H.S., a TV spokeswoman and covergirl, she made her B'way debut in The Wiz . She played Sammy Davis Jr.'s daughter in Stop The World . . . and was one of the stars of Dancin' . Wendy was featured in Radio City Music Hall's productions of America and Encore .
