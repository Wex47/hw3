Biography (1981)

Donald Waugh has appeared in musical shows which include Bubbling Brown Sugar , Only in America and The Wiz. His film credits include Bugsy Malone , Sky Hunter , Shock Treatment and Country Man and on television his appearances include Grange Hill , Are You Being Served , The Foundation , The Wednesday at Eight Show , Kids , Fearless Frank and Play for Today . He has also released two single records.
