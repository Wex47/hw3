Brian Blessed is a British theatre, television and film actor known for his sonorous voice and "hearty, king-sized portrayals". He is famous for a variety of roles, including creating the roles of Old Deuteronomy and Bustopher Jones in the original London cast of Cats , Clayton in the Disney animated film Tarzan , Boss Nass in Star Wars Episode 1: The Phantom Menace , and his repeated roles as King Lear.
