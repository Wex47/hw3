Cats UK Tour ran from 1993 to 1995, and was essentially a mirror of the London production minus the ensemble characters Bill Bailey , Etcetera , Victor and George . While cutting four ensemble kittens was common, it was unique for this production to include an extra female kitten in Electra, while cutting an additional male kitten in Bill Bailey . The production was a slow tour, spending several weeks or months at large regional theatres.
