Janet Hubert-Whitten is an American actress, most famous for playing Vivian Banks in the television sitcom Fresh Prince of Bel-Air from 1990 to 1993. She also originated the role of Tantomile in the original Broadway production of Cats .

Biography

1982

JANET L. HUBERT ( Tantomile ) appeared in the Jackie Robinson story The First as Opal. Her other credits include Dancin' and Joseph and the Amazing Technicolor Dreamcoat . She is an alumna of the Juilliard School of Drama. Janet has appeared in numerous film and print ads.
