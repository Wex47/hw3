Biography (1981)

John Chester has played in many West End shows including Anne of Green Gables , Peter Pan and A Chorus Line . On tour he has also played the Fiddler in Fiddler on the Roof and toured Europe and South America with Disney on Parade . He has recently appeared in Company in Newcastle. He spent three years with Dougie Squires' Second Generation appearing in many television specials and has also performed in a vocal harmony trio, "Jingles", both in England and Europe.
