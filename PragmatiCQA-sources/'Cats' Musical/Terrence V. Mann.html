Terrence Mann , also known as Terrence Vaughan Mann, is an Tony Award nominated American theatre and television actor, singer, dancer, choreographer, and director who originated the role of Rum Tum Tugger in the Broadway production of Cats .

He made his Broadway debut in the musical Barnum , but is most well known for his Tony-nominated role of Inspector Javert in Les Misérables. He has also received two other Tony-nominations for his performances of the Beast in Beauty and the Beast and King Charles in the 2012 revival of Pippin.

Mann has appeared in numerous other Broadway shows, the most recent being Tuck Everlasting , Finding Neverland , and The Addams Family .

He is currently in the Netflix science-fiction drama Sense8 as the main antagonist Mr. Whispers.

Mann is married to former Cats co-star Charlotte d'Amboise .

Biography (1982)

TERRENCE V. MANN ( Rum Tum Tugger ) started in the original B'way company of Barnum with Jim Dale. He then did the role of the Ringmaster in both the B'way and national company and played the title role at matinees and on Sundays in San Francisco and L.A. He also appeared in the American Theatre for Actor's Premiere of A Night at the Fights .
