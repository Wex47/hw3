Career (2019)

Jaih first began dancing Capoeira, Ballet, Jazz, and Contemporary since the age of 5. He later joined a dance crew in Oxford, dubbed 'Messy Jam', and trained at Tring Performing Arts for five years. He is a member of the dance company Chrysalis London and has been featured on shows Got to Dance, BGT, and the X Factor.
