Kaye Brown in an English actress and dancer, originally from the West Indies and later moving to England. She played Tantomile in the 1998 Cats film .

Biography

Trained: BA (Hons) in dance and drama and The London Contemporary Dance School.

Theatre: Several years in touring companies in Britain and Europe, Kaye first came to the West End in John Caird's Children of Eden . She has been in Cats both in the West End and on tour and was Artistic Co-ordinator of the tour in 1995. Miss Saigon (Theatre Royal, Drury Lane), Follies (Leicester Haymarket) - playing Margie, and the title role in The Snow Queen (Warwick), Doctor Dolittle (Hammersmith Apollo).
