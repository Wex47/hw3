Laurie Davidson is an English actor, best known for playing a Young William Shakespeare in the TNT series Will . He portrays Mistoffelees in the 2019 Cats movie .

Career (2019)

Laurie shot the pilot of Will in January 2016 before graduating drama school in July. He previously had a small role in the film Vampire Academy . He portrayed Michael Lewis in the TV film Diana & I, and has been cast in the 2019 film The Good Liar alongside Helen Mirren.
