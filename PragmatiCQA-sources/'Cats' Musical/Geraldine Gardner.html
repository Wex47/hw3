Biography (1981)

Geraldine Gardner has appeared in many musicals, her favourites being Guys and Dolls , The Boyfriend and as Sheila in A Chorus Line . Her list of television credits includes appearances in Hazell , Danger UXB , The New Avengers and Rosie . She has worked in a wide variety of repertory companies including the Bristol Old Vic and at Joan Littlewood's Theatre Royal Stratford East in plays ranging from Romeo and Juliet to There's a Girl in my Soup. She has recently completed a record-breaking season in Bristol as Aladdin with Danny La Rue.
