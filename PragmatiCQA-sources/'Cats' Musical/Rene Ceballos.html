Rene Ceballos was an American dancer who originated the role of Cassandra in the Original Broadway Production of Cats . She had a short career in film during the 90's, but was mostly a chorus dancer in a variety of musicals. Rene passed away in 2016 after a long battle with many health issues.

Biography (1982)

RENÉ CEBALLOS ( Cassandra ) was one of the original members of Dancin' . Her debut was as Bebe in A Chorus Line . Trained classically at the Joffrey and Ballet Russe, she has toured throughout Europe and the Middle East. René special interest lies in her music which she soon will be recording.
