Personality

Jellylorum is a sweet kitten. She is always seen with a smile on her face.

Role

Jellylorum is often seen around the other female kittens such as Tantomile and Syllabub . When Syllabub is laying on the floor in " Skimbleshanks: The Railway Cat ", Jellylorum makes sure to not leave her behind and helps her up before dancing again.

Jellylorum sings a couple of shared lines in " Jellicle Songs for Jellicle Cats ". She joins the other queens in fawning over the Rum Tum Tugger , swooning over his falsetto and squealing at the end of his song . Along with fellow kittens Tantomile and Coricopat , Jellylorum is seen looking upset when Gus calls them out for not being well trained.

Appearance

Jellylorum is bright ginger tabby. Her coloring stands out compared to the other brown toned kittens. She wears a red and yellow collar.
