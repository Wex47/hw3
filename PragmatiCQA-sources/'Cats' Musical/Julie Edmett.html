Biography (1981)

Julie Edmett trained at the Doreen Bird School of theatre dance. She began her career in summershows in Eastbourne, Bristol, Plymouth and the Isle of Wight. She has worked with Wayne Sleep before in cabaret and in Pinocchio at Birmingham where she also appeared in Kiss Me Kate , Babes in the Wood and the new musical Worzel Gummidge with Jon Pertwee. She has also worked with Peter Gordeno in cabaret, on television and on a QE2 world cruise.
