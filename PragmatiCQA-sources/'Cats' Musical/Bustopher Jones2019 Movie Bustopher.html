Role

Bustopher appears to be crude and ill-mannered, spitting food in Victoria's face - a departure from the very proper stage version of the character.

Bustopher is often seen in the movie with Maitre D' who tells him what to eat and what not to eat in the " Bustopher Jones " sequence of the film.

Appearance

Bustopher Jones is a very round Tuxedo cat that wears a black top hat and white spats.
