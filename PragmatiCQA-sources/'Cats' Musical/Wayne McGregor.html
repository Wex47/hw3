Wayne McGregor, CBE is a British choreographer and director known for his work with the Royal Ballet and multiple feature films. Wayne was originally announced as the choreographer in the film adaptation of Cats, and participated in the casting process before withdrawing due to scheduling conflicts. He was replaced on the project by Andy Blankenbuehler , who had previously choreographed the 2016 Broadway Revival production.
