Career

After graduating form Millennium Performing Arts, Danny appeared in Chekov International Theatre Festival as a performer, and has been a part of the New Adventures Dance Company since 2009. He has previously been a part of the Ensemble in Barnum and portrayed Frank Shultz in romantic musical Show Boat.
