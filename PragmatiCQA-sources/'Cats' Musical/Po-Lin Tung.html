Biography (2018)

Born in Taiwan, Po-Lin studied at the Dance Department of Taipei National University of the Arts. A member of Fang Yi Sheu and Artists from 2012 to 2013, Po-Lin toured with the company across Taiwan and also the United States. In 2013, Po-Lin worked with the Gelsey Kirkland Ballet in New York, and from 2014 performed with the Tania Perez-Salas Compania we Danza in Mexico City. In 2015, Po-Lin was based in New York where he worked as a freelance dance artist with a number of companies. Po-Lin danced with the London-based Company Wayne McGregor from 2016 to 2018.
