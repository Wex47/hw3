Biography (1982)

RENÉ CLEMENTE ( Coricopat / Mungojerrie ) gained critical acclaim on Broadway as Paul San Marco in A Chorus Line and for his performance in the short-lived musical Play Me a Country Song . René's regional and stock credits range from Benvolio in Romeo and Juliet to Val in Orpheus Descending from Rodolfo in La Boheme to Jesus in Godspell .
