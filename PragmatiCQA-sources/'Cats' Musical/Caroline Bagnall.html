Biography (2001)

Caroline grew up in Scarborough.

TRAINING: Three years at Laine Theatre Arts in Epsom, where she gained a diploma in musical theatre.

THEATRE: Caroline's credits include: the title role in "Sleeping Beauty," Oakwood Pembrokeshire, Alice Fitzwarren in "Dick Whittington," Lyceum Theatre, Sheffield, Wendy in "Peter Pan," Palace Theatre, Manchester, and "A Cockney Knees Up," Hackney Empire.

In the West End, Caroline has appeared as Donna-Sue in "Grease," Cambridge Theatre and "La Cava" at the Victoria Palace and Piccadilly Theatres, where she understudied and played the role of Isabel. She can also be heard on the original cast recording.

Caroline would like to dedicate her performance in CATS to her Granny and Grandad.
