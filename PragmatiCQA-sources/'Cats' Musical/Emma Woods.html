Biography (2000)

TRAINING - Baugi Academy of Dance and then at the Northern Ballet School, Manchester, from which she graduated in 1998. During this time she was part of many celebrations with BBC North and also the Manc Dance Company performing both at the Dancehouse Theatre, Manchester, and the Alhambra Theatre, Bradford.

THEATRE - Credits include Singin' in the Rain (Leicester Haymarket) and Spirit of the Dance (Reno, USA). She played the role of Bet in Oliver! (Leicester Haymarket) and has most recently completed a UK tour of Hotstuff playing the role of Sylvia Suburb.
