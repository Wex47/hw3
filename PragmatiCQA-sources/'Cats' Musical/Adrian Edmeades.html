Biography (1987)

Adrian Edmeades is seventeen years old and started gymnastics at the age of six. He progressed to the Bellairs School of Dance and Drama in Guildford and he is still a part-time student of the London Studio Centre. He has appeared on such television programmes as Saturday Superstore , Freetime and Splash . Cats is Adrian's West End debut.
