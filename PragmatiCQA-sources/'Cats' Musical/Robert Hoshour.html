Robert Hoshour is an American theatre actor famous for his roles in Cats and The Little Prince and the Aviator . He played Tumblebrutus in the Original Broadway Production of Cats .

Cats History

Broadway - 7/10/1982 - 08/1984 - Tumblebrutus ( cover Munkustrap )

Biography (1982)

ROBERT HOSHOUR ( Tumblebrutus ) first appeared on Broadway in Barnum as a swing (literally) for the male ensemble. Marlowe and The Little Prince and the Aviator followed. He was trained at North Carolina School of the Arts, and can be seen in the HBO Special "Some Call Them Freaks."
