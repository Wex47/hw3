Role

Makeup Designer
Karen Dawson is a make-up designer who joined the CATS team in 1989 for the UK tour , where she was asked to create a stylised look for the production. Her designs have since been used for London and subsequent productions.

In 2020, she designed face masks for the Korean production to allow the cast to stroll through the audience while following the South Korean COVID-19 safety measures.

Karen designed the cats make-up on the faces of the actors, because after all the make-up will end up on the face - a 3D image. Once the make-up has been designed photographs of the front and the two sides of the face are then taken. The charts give detailed instructions for colors and shading. Each actor is given a make-up chart, a photograph of how they should look and a bag of the correct cosmetics.
