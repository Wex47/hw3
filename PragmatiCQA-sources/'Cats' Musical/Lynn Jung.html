Biography (2019)

Lynn is one of the world's leading freerunners based out of London UK and Luxembourg. She believes her greatest achievement to date is winning Best Female Athlete. She has extensive experience in freerunning and parkour. Coming from a strong dance and circus background, Lynn is also a trained stage performer.
