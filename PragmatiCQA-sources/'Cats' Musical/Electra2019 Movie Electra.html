Electra is an ensemble character in the 2019 Cats movie . She is portrayed by Ida Saki .

Role

Electra is an ensemble dancer. She takes part in all the group numbers but has no solo vocal lines. During " The Rum Tum Tugger ", she joins the other queens in fawning over the titular Rum Tum Tugger . The character's age is unclear, but she is possibly an adult as she is paired with Skimbleshanks for the pas de deux at the Ball.

Appearance

Electra is a greyish brown cat with a simple black collar.
