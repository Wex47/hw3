Hey, Mr. Producer! was a concert honoring theatre producer Cameron Mackintosh as a benefit for the Royal National Institute of the Blind and the Combined Theatrical Charities. It was performed at the Lyceum Theatre in London on 7 and 8 June 1998.

It featured performances from many of the hit musicals that Mackintosh has produced including Cats . The Cats cast consisted of former London cast members, including original cast members Elaine Paige (Grizabella), Brian Blessed (Old Deuteronomy) and Paul Nicholas (Rum Tum Tugger). The cast performed three songs: " The Jellicle Ball " (short segment), " Jellicle Songs for Jellicle Cats " and " Memory ".

The concert was filmed and recorded and has been released on CD and DVD.
