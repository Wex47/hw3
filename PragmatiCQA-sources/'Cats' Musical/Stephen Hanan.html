Biography (1982)

STEPHEN HANAN ( Bustopher Jones/Asparagus/Growltiger ) made his Broadway debut as Kevin Kline's Lieutenant in The Pirates of Penzance . Other credits include the Delacorte, Goodspeed, cabaret, and five years as San Francisco's reigning street-singer. He writes plays, songs and poetry, practices Yoga, and reveres the earth and it's sustaining spirit.
