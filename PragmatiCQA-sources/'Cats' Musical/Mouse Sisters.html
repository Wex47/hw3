Mouse Sisters

Character

Gender

Female
The Mouse Sisters are portrayed by Harriet Turnbull , Meesha Garbett , Chase Collard , and Olivia Brooks .

Role

The mouse sisters are mice that Jennyanydots lives with. She teaches them music, crocheting and tatting. However, Jennyanydots talks about eating them to the rest of the cats. The sisters are scared of most of the jellicle cats and are glad when they aren't eaten. They are also especially scared of Skimbleshanks .

Appearance

They are very small brown and grey mice.
