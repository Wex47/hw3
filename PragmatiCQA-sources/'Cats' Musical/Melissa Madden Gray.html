Career (2019)

Melissa previously performed 'Meow Meow In Concert' for three nights at the Apollo Theatre, and was named Best Cabaret Performer at the 2012 Helpmann Awards for her show, Little Match Girl. She was awarded the Edinburgh International Festival Fringe Prize in 2012. She debuted 'An Audience with Meow Meow' at the Berkeley Repertory Theatre in California in 2014. Melissa performed in La Clique at the Roundhouse and portrayed Maitresse in the West End adaptation of The Umbrellas of Cherbourg at Gielgud Theatre.
