Biography (2018)

Chrissy was one of the original students at 'The Young Dancers Academy' studying ballet with the wonderful Anna Du Boisson until the age of 16. She then went on to 'Laine Theatre Arts' where she graduated in 2012 with a diploma in Dance.

Shortly after that Chrissy landed her first job in London's 'West End' in 'Phantom of the Opera' as a ballet girl. She was then Cast in the original Uk tour of 'Wicked' playing the part of 'Melena' from 2013-2015. Upon returning to London Chrissy worked for a year doing jobs such as Robbie Williams' european tour and his music videos 'Party Like a Russian' and 'Heavy Entertainment Show'. Scott Alan's 'Again'.

She appeared in Steven Spielberg's new movie 'Ready Player One' and was a dancer on Ant and Dec's 'Saturday Night Takeaway'.

Chrissy and her boyfriend Jonny Labey are the first proud winners of ITV's new Sunday night show 'Dance Dance Dance'. Chrissy also performed in the original London Cast of 'An American in Paris'.
