Helsinki 1986

Location

Kaupunginteatteri, Helsinki, Finland

Opening Date

1st September 1986

Closing Date

December 1988

Production Team

Director

Jorma Uotinen

On 1st September 1986, Cats opened at Helsingin Kaupunginteatteri (Helsinki City Theatre), Finland and ran until December 1988. This was a non-replica production. Many characters had alternates, and there was a large ensemble of named chorus cats as well as swings and a "Cats Chorus" of booth singers.

Production Specifics

  • Translation - Jukka Virtanen
  • Director/Choreographer - Jorma Uotinen

As a non-replica production, the character roles varied from the usual arrangement: Growltiger was a full character or played by Munkustrap; Bustopher Jones was a full character; Griddlebone played by Jennyanydots, Cassandra or Coricopat; Coricopat was female.
