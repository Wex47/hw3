Jason Derulo is an American singer, songwriter, and dancer, known for his prominent career in pop music. He portrays the Rum Tum Tugger in the 2019 film adaptation of Cats .

Career (2019)

Jason first began writing songs for artists in 2006, releasing his debut single "Whatcha Say" and hit number 1 on the Billboard Hot 100 in November 2009. His debut album was released in 2010. He previously had a guest starring role in the TV series Lethal Weapon. Jason has sold over 30 million records, with eleven songs certified platinum. He cites Michael Jackson as his biggest influence. Derulo released his first single of 2017, "Swalla", featuring Nicki Minaj. His fifth album, 2Sides , is due for release in 2020. Jason was announced to be part of the cast of Cats in November.
