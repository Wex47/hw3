Cat Leaper ( キャットリーパー , Kyatto Rīpā ) , known as Leaper ( リーパー , Rīpā ) in its pre-awakened state, is an Ether Gear that enhances their ability to leap, not just in speed but also in time.

Description

Described as the "power of possibility" by Xiaomei , as well as the forbidden power to change the laws of the cosmos according to Drakken Joe , Cat Leaper drastically improves the user's jumping ability, giving them great speed and agility. The user can become so fast that they leave behind afterimages to confuse or mislead opponents. However, its true power allows the user to potentially change the outcome of past events by rewinding time. When this power is in effect, the user gains catlike ears and a tail, true to its namesake. As a side effect of its activation, whatever footwear the user is wearing is destroyed as a result of the release of energy, leaving them barefoot. When the user time leaps, they enter a new "world". This action may cause a tremendous change around them, but history will converge to its predetermined future. However, its true power transports them into another, parallel world.

The user could unconsciously use Cat Leaper several times, with it even activating if they lose their life, having no recollection of the previous timelines. If they become aware of its true power, they will be able to recall the previous timeline.
