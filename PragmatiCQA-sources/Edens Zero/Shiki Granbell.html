Shiki Granbell ( シキ・グランベル , Shiki Guranberu ) is an adventurer from the planet Granbell and the Demon King ( 魔王 , Maō ) of the warship Edens Zero , as well as the leader of its crew . He's also a member of the Guild Shooting Starlight , the adopted grandson of the former Demon King, Ziggy , and the King of Magimech (魔械の王, Makai no Ō ). By the year X495 , the Interstellar Union Army have recognized him as one of the Oración Seis Galáctica , as well as a major threat to the universe.

Appearance

Shiki is a lean toned young man of average height with spiky black hair which partially covers his ears, slanted dark eyes, and thin eyebrows. He dons a small, white bandage on his left cheek as well as a red pouch around his waist. During his rematch with Shura on Nero 66, the former ripped his left eye out with gravity while in Overdrive. He later replaces it with an artificial eye following the conclusion of the war. By X495, Shiki has grown into a young adult with a more muscular build and his eyes are now slightly narrow.

His initial attire consists of a high-collared, red-and-black open jacket with the sleeves rolled up to his forearms, pockets on either side of the chest, and two black laces attached to the neck of his jacket hanging behind his head. Underneath, he wears a simple white shirt with a v-neck collar and a red design on the front which patterns out into a Y -shape. He also sports baggy, green-colored trousers which possess noticeable white details on both sides. Lastly, his choice of footwear are a pair of simple, black boots. However, his clothes were destroyed after fighting a parasite imitating the space pirate Elsie Crimson .

He later changes into a second outfit which consists of a buttoned-up, short-sleeved, and copper-trimmed black tunic with an open high collar, has two black laces behind the neck of the collar, white round details on the sides, and a zig-zag pattern at the end of the sleeves on either side. He also wears the X-Ray Goggles over his forehead, sports a pair of white baggy trousers with a single line going down in the middle, and tucked into black-and-copper boots.

After the events on planet Guilst, he later returns to his initial outfit which now has a black t-shirt with a silver-white insignia of the Edens Zero's emblem on the center. While in Digitalis, his avatar takes the appearance of a young-looking caveman with his hair in a long and wild style similar to before he met Rebecca and Happy on Granbell . His attire is a set of torn clothes with leopard prints, a necklace with shark teeth, and sandals. When encountering Spider in his Jamilov avatar, his hair is reverted back to his usual hairstyle like in the real world.

Before heading to the planet Mildian, he changes into an ninja outfit, having a short-sleeved, dark-colored jumpsuit with white lining around the shoulders, a Y-shaped white line going down the center, and a pair of diamond-shaped designs on the knees. It is also tied by a white sash, the legs are tucked into a pair of black boots, has a black scarf around his neck and a black-and-white striped handkerchief wrapped around his forehead.

While on the planet Sun Jewel , he wears his signature attire, but now has a white shirt with a red-colored insignia being the number "0" crossed out on the center, resembling the "O" in the Edens Zero's logo and after leaving, later replaces it with a black undershirt with gold-trimmed lines between the lower part. After leaving Belial Gore, he starts wearing a black shirt under his jacket.

On the planet Sandra , Shiki wears a light-colored cloak around both his shoulders.

Before heading to Nero 66 , Shiki wears a dark-colored shirt with a pair of red x-shaped suspenders underneath his jacket along with a pair of light-colored cargo pants instead of his usual green trousers.

In the year X495, He now wears a short-sleeved version of his signature coat, a dark-colored undershirt with a light-colored x-shaped emblem in the chest, a leather belt and two more crossed between his waist, dark colored pants, and shoes.

Personality

Shiki is a carefree, blunt, and eager individual. Because he lived his entire life in the robotic Granbell Kingdom, he never saw another human in his life before meeting Rebecca . Due to this, he's very curious and awkward around people and strongly desired to make a lot of friends - best exemplified by his habit of asking strangers to "be his friend" and his invasion of people's personal space.

Shiki has a strong sense of loyalty and refused to leave Granbell for many years so that he could take care of the robots of the park. And even when he was apparently betrayed by the robots who appeared to turn hostile, he still considered them his friends and tearfully thanked them and promised to always remember them. However, he also has a strong sense of justice as when his friends seemingly betrayed him, he attacked them in order to protect the people he had just met, going as far as to put himself in harm's way to allow strangers to escape.

Despite his fearlessness, Shiki seems to have a crippling fear of bugs , demonstrated when he didn't wish to believe that the Kawpicatt S4 was a species of bug and instead wanted to view them more like an "octopus."

It is also shown that Shiki has a perverted side, which first started at Granbell when he saw Rebecca for the first time. However, it seems to have gotten worse over time thanks to Weisz's influence, to the point he becomes ‘lustful’.

Three years later, Shiki has grown more mature yet stern and serious when in combat. However, he still retains his many of his old traits, such as his habit of abruptly offering to be friends with random people.

Synopsis

History

Many years ago, Shiki was found by his adoptive grandfather, the Demon King Ziggy who brought him to the planet Granbell in order to raise him. One day, as Shiki along with Ziggy and Michael watched comets fly, his grandfather told imparted on him the importance of exploring the universe and finding friends.

Intro arc

Shiki meets Rebecca and Happy after they arrive in the Granbell Kingdom , literally dropping in on them as they were faced with a giant robotic cat monster. After realizing that Rebecca is another human and being punched for invading her personal space, Shiki asks Rebecca to be his friend but is rejected. He then follows Rebecca and Happy as they flee back to the town. After greeting them again, Shiki introduces them to all of his friends before taking part in a large celebration with them and then falling asleep. After waking up to discover that his long hair had been cut. He discovered the robots had captured Rebecca and Happy and tried to kill them in order to take their ship in order to get revenge on humanity for abandoning them. (This was later revealed to be just an act to get Shiki to leave with them before the robots of Granbell ran out of battery power, leaving him alone.) After saving Rebecca and Happy from the robots and offering to stay behind to give them time to escape, Rebecca pulls him along with her acknowledging him as a friend. They then begin their adventure into the Sakura Cosmos and Shiki expresses that he misses his friends but is eager to make one hundred more in the universe.

Now traveling with Rebecca and Happy, Shiki begins to learn about the outside world such as B-Cube and multiple other things, even learning about Rebecca's B-Cube channel . Rebecca eventually landed the Aqua Wing on Blue Garden with hopes of registering Shiki into her Guild Shooting Starlight as an Adventurer.

In the Guild, Shiki was surprised to see so many people that he wanted to touch them, much to Rebecca's dismay. After Rebecca stops him from touching someone's face, he notices the giant hologram of Mother. After Rebecca's explanation, Shiki claims that he met Mother before, causing everyone to laugh much to Rebecca's embarrassment. When Labilia Christy 's insults caused Rebecca to cry, he used his Ether Gear on the latter and told her not to make fun of his friends before Rebecca dragged him out of the place. After obtaining his Adventurer Card, Shiki tells Rebecca that they should find Mother themselves and make it into a video. This excites Rebecca, but tells Shiki that they need find to get a better ship in order to make the trip beyond the Sakura Cosmos . They are later seen flying the Aqua Wing towards Planet Norma .

Powers and Abilities

Shiki Granbell is a member of the Oración Seis Galáctica , a group that is stated to be the cosmos's strongest warriors, being renowned for crushing entire planets. He was made a part of this after the Oración Seis Interstellar determined him to be a threat to the universe. Holy acknowledges his power to be on the same level with the rest of the Galáctica.

Satan Gravity (サタングラビティ Satan Gurabiti ): Renowned as "the power of the Demon King," which he inherited from his adoptive grandfather, Ziggy , Shiki uses this Ether Gear to control and manipulate gravity itself, giving him "Demon King Ether ". He is capable of changing the direction of the gravitational force around himself which enables him to stick to walls, float in the air, and make objects and people weightless to an extent. When using its power throughout his body, Shiki is identified as the new Demon King by those who sensed or scanned it. He also describes it as a power he inherited from his grandfather, and that it took him a month to learn it. After training with Xenolith for seven days, Shiki has shown greater improvement over his Satan Gravity to the point that he is capable of feats such as drastically increasing the weight of multiple people or objects without making contact, changing the direction of bullets fired at him with a flick of the wrist. , and launching multiple opponents with a single punch. He is also capable of sending Poseidon Shura , another Satan Gravity user who is said to be stronger than he is, into the air after being briefly held down by the immense weight of the latter's own gravitational field before sending him downwards with two gravity-empowered punches. Three years later in the Kaede Cosmos, Shiki's gravity powers have increased drastically.

  • Magimech Attack: Gravity Fist (魔械流 重拳 Makai-ryū: Jūken ): Shiki's arm begins to glow for a brief moment and then he punches his opponent with enough force to rupture the ground in several places, destroying nearby buildings.
    • Magimech Attack: Gravity Fist Frenzy (魔械流 重拳乱舞 Makai-ryū: Jūken Ranbu ): Shiki's fists begin to glow and he initiates a barrage of punches onto his opponents causing them to be thrown away.
  • Magimech Attack: Gravity Center (魔械流 重心 Makai-ryū: Jūshin ): Shiki exerts an immense amount of force on an airborne target by boosting its gravity, pulling the target to the ground.
  • Magimech Attack: Gravity Wave (魔械流 重力波 Makai-ryū: Jūryokuha ): Shiki's palm is placed on the ground, creating a spiral force that pulls targets towards him.
  • Magimech Attack: Gravity Windmill Kick (魔械流 重旋脚 Makai-ryū: Jūsen Kyaku ): Shiki's leg begins to glow while in midair and releases a spinning kick onto his targets.
  • Magimech Attack: Gravity Cannon (魔械流 重弾 Makai-ryū: Jūdan ): Shiki creates an energy sphere made of gravity from his fist and throws it onto his target, pushing them back due to the heaviness of the attack.
  • Magimech Attack: Gravity Comet (魔械流 重彗星 Makai-ryū: Jūsuisei ): Shiki enhances his feet with gravity and jumps from above onto their target, causing sheer force and blunt damage.
  • Magimech Attack: Gravity Tackle (魔械流 重身 Makai-ryū Jūshin ): Shiki rotates himself in mid-air and uses gravity to push himself down at the opponent with a downward kick, sending them onto the ground.
  • Magimech Attack: Gravity Impact (魔械流 グラビティインパクト Makai-ryū: Gurabiti Inpakuto ): Shiki absorbs kinetic energy from an external source and then converts it into concentrated gravity to perform a devastating downward punch.
  • Magimech Attack: Gravity Field (魔械流 重力場 Makai-ryū Jūryokuba ): Shiki slams the ground with his fist, generating a gravitational field that crushes and negates attacks.
  • Magimech Attack: Anti-Gravity (魔械流 反重力 Makai-ryu: Hanjūryoku ): Shiki generates an anti-gravitational field around whatever he touches, causing anything that moves into it to fly away in other directions.
  • Magimech Attack: Meteor Breaker (魔械流 星 ( メテオ ) 崩し ( ブレイカー ) Makai-ryu: Meteo Bureikā ): Shiki charges Ether at the end of his fist to create a gravity sphere, which becomes a giant meteor of gravity when fired at the enemy.
  • Super Ultimate Magimech Attack: Black Sky (魔械流秘奥技 黒天 Makai-ryū Hi Ōgi: Kokuten ): Shiki strengthens the weight of his heart, creating a ball of gravity that grows bigger, and slams it at his target.

Overdrive (オーバードライブ Ōbādoraibu ): As an Ether Gearist, Shiki is able to access Overdrive, also known as the true power of Ether Gear. When used throughout his entire body, Shiki gains a transformation identifying him as the new Demon King. In its rudimentary form, his hair and skin color change into a lighter shade, light-colored Ether markings appear all over his body, and a pair of energy horns appear on both sides of his head. In this form, all of Shiki's physical abilities are greatly augmented and he can utilize his Ether Gear techniques to the maximum. However, his grasp of Overdrive is rudimentary, according to Drakken Joe . After having memories of his death from World No. 29 , Shiki uses his Overdrive at its fullest, thus gaining a new appearance that truly identifies him as the Demon King. His hair becomes white, both his arms are darkened with purple Ether markings, and a pair of black horns with the same markings appear out of his head. His clothing also changed into a sleeveless, high-collared tunic and his bandage is gone, revealing a small scar. With it, Shiki's gravity becomes significantly more powerful, comparable to a black hole, as he can pull in surrounded objects and even memories from World No. 29. With his first usage of the complete Overdrive, his Ether capabilities were compared to those of Drakken Joe and Elsie Crimson of the Oración Seis Galáctica and he was able to defeat the former.

Immense Strength : Shiki possesses great physical strength, having enough power to push someone back with a single punch. When using Ether Gear, his strength is greatly increased to the point that a single gravity punch caused enough sheer force and blunt damage to send an opponent flying or destroy the ground beneath them. According to Hermit, Shiki's physical strength is recorded to be 2000 to 2500.

Immense Durability : Shiki possesses a high amount of durability, making him capable of taking heavy damage from opponents such as Sibir with his enhanced mechanical arm , Jinn's wind-based ninjitsu attacks , and also heavy attacks from Kurenai's Knight Gear . As well as being able to take attacks from Justice and survive his Grand Chariot , surprising the soldier.

Enhanced Reflexes : Shiki has great reflexes, making him able to keep up with many opponents in battle and dodge gunfire with ease.

Acrobatic Skills : Shiki has displayed great acrobatic skills. When using his Ether Gear, he can easily jump higher than a normal person due to his changing his own weight.

Master Hand-to-Hand Combatant : Shiki is highly skilled in hand-to-hand combat. When combined with his Ether Gear, he is capable of overpowering large numbers of attackers with ease and can go toe-to-toe with stronger opponents.

Equipment

Edens Zero ( エデンズ・ゼロ , Edenzu Zero ) : A large spaceship given to him by Elsie Crimson which was originally owned by his grandfather .

X-Ray Goggles : Shiki picked up a pair of goggles from Weisz's House which allows him to see-through clothing.

Aquatic Adaptation Lacrima ( 水中適合の 星水晶 ( ラクリマ ) , Suichū Tekigō no Rakurima ) : During his stay on Red Cave , Shiki wore one of these devices which allowed him to breathe and communicate underwater without any issues. After reaching the end of the cave within the Temple of the Mother Beacon , Shiki's Aquatic Adaptation Lacrima became the host of a portion of Mother 's Ether , transforming it into a beacon to find other pieces of Mother's Ether throughout the cosmos.

Artificial Eye : After losing his left eye in his fight with Shura, Sister implants a new eye in its place. It works just the same as a real eye and has an added x-ray function, allowing him to see through clothes.
