The Camelish ( キャメリッシュ , Kyamerisshu ) are giant camel-like creatures that inhabit Sandra , the Planet of Sand.

Appearance

The Camelish are camel-like creatures with three humps, their heads have three fins and fish scales that fade down their necks. They are giant-sized, being about as high as an average Sandra building and towering over humans and bots.

Synopsis

Sandra arc

When the Crew of Edens traverse the city on Sandra, they notice a Camelish carrying around a set of crates in the streets where Pino explains about their capabilities after scanning it. Rebecca and Happy are planning to make a B-Cube video of them riding a Camelish but Laguna bluntly reminds them that they are not here to play around.

Goodwin rides a Camelish into battle where he charges toward the Imperial Army soldiers and armored cars in his path, taking them out with ease.

Powers and Abilities

Environmental Adaptation : The Camelish are capable of functioning in a wide range of environments from arid regions to underwater ones.

Enhanced Strength : They are strong enough to carry giant-sized beings who ride them as transportation.
