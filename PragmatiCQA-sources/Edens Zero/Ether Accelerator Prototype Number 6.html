The Ether Accelerator Prototype Number 6 (エーテル加速器試作６号 Ēteru Kasokuki Shisaku Roku-gō ), also known as the Ether Accelerator Cannon (エーテル加速砲 Ēteru Kasokuhō ), is a massive cannon constructed by Müller Royal Laboratories .

Description

The Ether Accelerator was constructed on Day 18, Month Z of the year X477 by the human and bot workers of Müller Royal Laboratories under Müller , and assisted by Hermit who helped devise the necessary formula to stabilize the vast quantities of Ether used to fuel the weapon.

Hermit and her fellow bots from the neighboring planet Hook were deceived into believing that the Ether from the device would be beamed to receivers of the bot-inhabited world to restore their missing Ether reserves. Instead, the destructive power of the Ether Accelerator Cannon completely destroyed the planet along with all of its inhabitants, revealing the true nature of Newton 's human scientists.
