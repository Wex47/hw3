The Leo Starfighters ( 戦闘機レオン , Sentōki Reon ) are one-man starfighters that are utilized by the crew of the Edens Zero .

Description

The Leo Starfighters are small starfighters, large enough only for a single person to occupy it. They have a large glass dome for the top half, with striped pattern, a small of wings just behind, and a larger pair of wings to the side, each with the Edens Zero emblem. The bottom half has a dark underside, with a pair of blasters mounted infront of the wings. At the very front, the symbol of the Leo zodiac is branded.
