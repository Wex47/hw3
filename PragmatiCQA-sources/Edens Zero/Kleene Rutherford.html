Kleene Rutherford ( クリーネ・ラザフォード , Kurīne Razafōdo ) , is Jinn 's younger sister and a member of the Edens Zero 's crew sharing the title of Winds of Edens . She is a former member of Drakken Joe 's special forces, the Element 4 , known under the codename "Sylph" ( シルフ , Shirufu ) .

Appearance

Kleene is a petite young woman of average height with red eyes and short, green hair in the form of a bob and small bun. She has an electronic hair clip on the sides. Her main attire consists of a plain, light-colored spaghetti strap dress with loose, black-striped sleeves around her arms, black tights, and like her fellow members, she has a pair of dark-colored tattoos on each shoulder that resembles wings.

By the year X495, Kleene has grown older, now having longer hair while retaining her small bun, becoming well-endowed, and has gained a curvaceous figure. She now wears a bodysuit.

When using her Ether Gear, it appears on the base of her nose in the form of flowing wind .

Personality

Initially, Kleene is shown to be a rather emotionless person, barely displaying any emotion on her face beyond a passive, neutral expression. Her only changes in expression came from when she is surprised and her eyes widen more than usual. Other than that, any other emotions are shown through the digital hairpins she wears on her head. Despite this, Kleene appears to be dedicated to her role as one of the Element 4 and takes it seriously, preferring to be called her code name as opposed to her real name. Because of the trauma of seeing her brother mutilated, Kleene developed a unique disorder that causes her to break down after high emotional events. Thus she wears a pair of digital hairpins that suppress her emotions, activating in events such as her becoming hysterical at the thought of Drakken absorbing her for failing.

Despite her outward feelings being suppressed, Kleene is shown to be quite an emotional and easygoing young lady. She has a caring side to her, especially with regards to her brother, as she left with Jinn when he walked away after seeing Fie cut off Weisz's arm. She is also a very open person who is eager to get in on anything fun, such as when she allowed Hermit to dress her up in the Dress Factory or when she wanted to join the rest of the crew at the beach on Red Cave . She is also very forgiving as shown when she told her brother to forgive Homura for hurting her in their fight, as she herself had already forgiven her.

After Sister heals her emotional state, Kleene is able to express her emotions without breaking down, becoming more eager to get along with the rest of the crew and now smiles often. She likes cute things, such as her hairpins, deciding to keep them despite not needing them anymore.

Synopsis

History

As a child, Kleene lived with her family, which consisted of her mother, father, and her brother, Kris Rutherford . The Rutherford Family were patrons of Müller Royal Laboratories and funded their research. However, in the year X479, after Müller was arrested, the Rutherfords found out about the truth of his research, and withdrew their financial support. In the year X482, sometime after Müller's escape, the family was visited by him, where he killed her parents and kidnapped her and Kris to use as guinea pigs for his O-Tech experiments. She was tied to a pole, crying out for help, and forced to watch as Müller dismembered her brother limb by limb, as an experiment to see how long before her emotions break down entirely. After this traumatic event, she developed a unique disorder that caused her to break down after high emotional events.

Eventually, they were rescued by bill collectors under the employ of Drakken Joe . Her brother promised her that he would find someone called "Sister" so that she could make Kleene feel better and left in search of her. At some point, she joined Drakken's Organization and eventually became part of his Element 4 under the codename of "Sylph of the Wind", unaware of the reason the Dark Alchemist needed the Element 4.

Kleene hands over the captured Rebecca and Happy to Drakken Joe. She later intercepts Homura and Shiki back in Material Loading Gate 2 and attempts to capture Homura in her Wind Cage, but Shiki nullifies it. Kleene then evades an attack from Shiki and remarks to her brother that he is just as he described him, the brother is then revealed to be Jinn. She intercepts Homura and absorbs the sword she swung at her. After Homura uses smoke to escape, Kleene tracks her down to Spica , a dance club, and absorbs all the bystanders in the club. The two continue their fight, but Kleene overpowers Homura and captures her and the medicine in her Wind Cage, telling her everyone is powerless before Drakken.

Kleene later appears with Drakken, Jinn, and the rest of the Element 4 before the captured Crew. When Drakken asks what medicine Rebecca is talking about, Kleene shows the case containing the medicine and gives it to him. She later notices her brother walking away and asks him where he is going. Jinn tells her that he doesn't have fond memories of mutilation and she says she'll go with him and leaves with Jinn.

She was brought along to assist Witch, as the Shield of Edens assumed negotiating with Laguna would have been long.

Kleene is in the Briefing Room of the Edens Zero with Jinn and Laguna, where she waves as an agitated Weisz notes of their presence. She expresses disappointment when her brother firmly states to the Crew of Edens that they will leave the ship after she is healed, saying that she will miss them. She then asks Laguna what he is looking for, which he reveals that he seeks to live on a nice, comfortable planet in the Aoi Cosmos, since he is a wanted man in the Sakura Cosmos, as part of the pact he made with Witch. She then leaves with her brother and Laguna, after the two reject Shiki's proposal for friendship. Later on, the three are in the Edens Kitchen conversing with each other while eating at a dining table. While sipping a cup, Kleene remarks that "Laguna" is his real name and not a codename like her and her brother. When Laguna points out that she was a part of Drakken Joe's "business" to Jinn, she asks about her doing evil acts, which Jinn immediately defends her that she did nothing wrong. After Mosco serves them a fresh batch of food at their table, Kleene mischievously tells Laguna that Mosco is her brother's best buddy, much to Jinn's chagrin.

At Sister's infirmary, Kleene remains silent at the Life of Edens who is analyzing her vitals from the check-up finished recently. Putting her shirt back on, she tells Sister that she does not have to fix her. Because if she's healed, she will have to leave, but she likes the Crew of Edens and if she said goodbye, she would miss them. After Sister states that she can stay if she wants, Kleene has doubts because of her hardheaded brother. Suddenly, Kleene and Sister hears an emergency alert from Witch who informs everyone that several hostile attack drones are currently approaching the Edens Zero from the airspace above Foresta and declares that she will fight too. During an aerial dogfight with the attack drones, Kleene boards one of the starfighters from the unlocked hanger and uses her Wind Snatch to protect herself from the Ether bullets, stating that they will not work on her. In retaliation, Kleene redirects the intercepted Ether bullets right back at several attack drones, destroying them which impresses Witch and Sister of the former Element 4 member's power. Kleene warns her brother Jinn that more enemies are approaching at 3 o clock which he noted and took them down swiftly.

Kleene in her starfighter continues shooting down a lot of Foresta's drones, commenting about it. After being informed about the giant server behind the machine virus, she is requested by Witch to back up Weiz and Hermit, who are on their way to dispatch it. Kleene complies in response, stating that she will help. During the orbital battle near the satellite server, Kleene's mental state goes unstable. Panicking in a cold sweat and breathing erratically, she tells her brother and Laguna that somebody bad is here nearby. Kleene in her panic state mutters repeatedly that somebody bad is here, while Jinn contacts Sister that he is bringing her back to the Edens Zero. It is soon revealed that she senses Müller’s presence nearby. After landing their starfighters at the Edens Zero's hanger, a still panicking Kleene clutches her head as her hair clips are unable to suppress her out-of-control emotions. She is taken to the infirmary by Sister carrying her in her arms as Jinn begs the Life of Edens to help her. At the Medical Room, an unconscious Kleene is lying in bed with only a blanket covering her body and hooked on life support as Sister states that she is losing her strength so fast not even her Heal Atomizer can keep up. However, with Hermit’s communication to Edens Zero is back online, Witch can send Sister into Kleene’s mind digitally to find out what caused her trauma in the past, and learn that Müller was behind all of this.

Powers and Abilities

Wind Snatch (ウインドスナッチ Uindo Sunatchi ): Like her brother, Kleene can control the wind and air currents with this form of Wind Ether Gear , allowing her to create winds that surround her starfighter even in space. This wind possesses the power to "steal" objects and people, absorbing them for her own use. Kleene is able to change the wind's color and can raise its quality level by at least one, preventing it from being cleaved. However, the wind can be blocked and blown by an equal amount of wind.

  • Wind Cage (風の檻 Kaze no Ori ): Kleene shrinks her opponents and sucks them into a swirling orb of wind. The cage is virtually impossible to break from the inside, and the use of projectiles simply causes them to ricochet across the wind's surface. It also jams communication signals made from within.
  • Wind Reflect (ウインドリフレクト Uindo Rifurekuto ): Kleene flicks her wrist and catches projectiles with her own wind, before sending them back at the opponent.
  • Variable Cyclone (ヴァリアブルサイクロン Variaburu Saikuron ):

Overdrive (オーバードライヴ Ōbadoraivu ): During her fight against Oceans 6's Lyra, Kleene is able to enter Overdrive which transforms her attire into that of a long, light-trimmed sleeveless black dress, a pair of large, swan-like wings, her two hairpins gain black horn-like appendages, dark-colored stockings, and light-colored feathered ballet shoes. She also has ether lines going from her hands to her shoulders and face, and a black tattoo with an unknown design on her right wrist. Like her brother, Kleene's Ether Gear capabilities and winds are augmented to the point where she creates high-speed cyclones with enough offensive power capable of shredding through Lyra's playing cards.

Piloting Skills : Kleene has displayed the ability to pilot her starfighter with ease, using it for battle.

Equipment

Emotion-Suppression Devices : Kleene has two hair clips with digital displays on either side of her head, that suppress her emotions.

Starfighter : While under Drakken's employ, Kleene uses a bird-themed starfighter for battle, which she is able to shield with her Ether Gear.
