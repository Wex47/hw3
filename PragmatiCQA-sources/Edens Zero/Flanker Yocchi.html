Flanker Yocchi ( フランカー・ヨッチ , Furankā Yotchi ) is a famous B-Cuber who has a part-time job in the Mildian Battle Coliseum .

Appearance

Flanker Yocchi is a thin young man with messy dark hair and bushy eyebrows. He has prominent canines and eyes that are normally closed. He has a pair of round glasses and in his part-time job, wears a pair of dark boxers that has "Bonbobom" written on the front of the waistband and boxing glove guns.

Personality

Yocchi is a perverted young man, using Tatter Slime to show off Rebecca's skin and would record it for his videos on B-Cube. He has a pragmatic fighting style, using a pair of guns disguised as boxing gloves.

Synopsis

Mildian arc

Flanker Yocchi is selected as Rebecca 's opponent in their match at the Mildian Battle Coliseum. The two B-Cubers converse with each other, having heard of each other before and he promises to upload their match on his channel. When the match begins, Yocchi immediately begins shooting with his boxing gun gloves. He activates his Protection Matrix , preventing Rebecca's Ether bullets from her Happy Blasters from hitting him. Soon, Yocchi's bullets release the Tatter Slime and dissolve pieces of Rebecca's clothes, blushing as he thinks about the views he will get from this tattered beauty. An annoyed Rebecca charges after him, he reminds her that Ether bullets do not work him only to be kicked in the face. After Xiaomei declares the match over, he asks Rebecca to do a collab with him sometime and subsequently faints.

Equipment

B-Cube (Ｂ・キューブ B Kyūbu ): As a B-Cuber, he uses a B-Cube terminal to record, upload, and share videos on his channel, Bom Bo-Bom TV.

Boxing Guns : Yocchi has a pair of guns that resembles boxing gloves. The guns fire bullets that contain Tatter Slime . The slime can be used to melt clothing.

Protection Matrix (プロテクトマトリックス Purotekuto Matorikkusu ): Yocchi can create an anti-Ether barrier, to protect himself from Ether-based attacks.
