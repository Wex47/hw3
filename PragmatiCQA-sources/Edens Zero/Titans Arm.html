Titan's Arm ( タイタンズアーム , Taitanzu Āmu ) is a type of Ether Gear that increases the size of its user.

Description

Titan's Arm allows the user to enlarge their body to gigantic proportions, to the point of being able to crush three people with their fist in one hit, and can revert back to normal at will.

Trivia

  • Titans in Greek mythology were the pre-Olympian gods.
