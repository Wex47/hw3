Mirrani Lucra ( ミラーニ・ルクラ , Mirāni Rukura ) is a member of the Oceans 6 and was one of Poseidon Nero 's top officials. She is the adopted granddaughter of the late Cyca.

Appearance

Mirrani is a curvy young woman of average height with braided purple hair with bangs that cover one of her magenta eyes. She also wears a pair of oval-shaped earrings and a black-and-white turban.

Mirrani wears a dark-colored tunic that exposes most of her ample bust, arms and midriff, a choker, a pair of decorative armbands and wristbands, baggy light-colored trousers that expose her legs, and lastly, a pair of pointed shoes.

Personality

Mirrani expresses a calm disposition and has a constant smile on her face, even being unfazed by Cyca 's death at the hands of Shura . Though it's later revealed that she was deeply affected by this and didn't want to go along with Shura's plans to destroy all robots. However, she was too scared of the sadistic prince to say or do anything about it. She often uses terms relating to mirrors, such as shattering her opponents into pieces. She has a pragmatic hunting style, wanting to pick her targets one by one and taking them to her world, while the reflection assaults the others in the real world.

Synopsis

History

As a little girl, Mirrani expressed her dream of becoming an Oceans member like Cyca when she grows up, which her grandfather is supportive and told her that he has no doubt that she can reach it.

Foresta arc

Attending the meeting with Poseidon Nero on Nero 1 , Mirrani learns from the Emperor that Foresta has been violated by Ziggy . After Shura asks why would the Demon King do that, Mirrani tells the prince that it doesn't matter as any violence performed in the Aoi Cosmos is tantamount to rebellion against their Emperor. She remarks that such acts are far from beautiful after all. Mirrani remains silent when Nero declares that he will do nothing after making a roll of the guiding dice and sees the results. When the command of the Oceans 6 has been officially transferred to Shura , she asks the son of the Emperor what he intends to do.

As Shura levitates several of the objects around him upside down in a room, Mirrani is quiet as Ijuna informs the prince that everything is ready for departure for Nero 66 . She remains impassive with a smile when Shura callously kills Cyca with Satan Gravity in front of her and her fellow Oceans. However, Mirrani silently cries for her grandfather's death as his lifeless body falls on the floor.

Sandra arc

One week after the Foresta incident, Mirrani and her fellow Oceans are dispatched to Salton and five other planets where they massacred the innocent bots and covered it up as robot revolts caused by Ziggy in the official Aoi News report.

On Nero 66, Mirrani relaxes at a table with a drink nearby the swimming pool. She explains to Lyra and Nasseh that Fabiano was one of the old Oceans and remarks that he just doesn't understand how the younger generation does things after the old man chose not to come with them and stayed at The Temple instead.

Aoi War arc

Within one of the mirrors of the hallway, Mirrani takes control of Homura with her Ether Gear and remarks that the mirror reflects the swordswoman's other self and tells Homura to set her dark side free. Watching from the Mirror World , she makes Homura's reflection attack her friends. When Pino uses her EMP to erase the reflections' Ether Sword, she pulls Weisz into the mirror and has his reflections prepare to fire at Rebecca with his assault rifle. However, the blonde woman reverses time back to before Homura's reflection started to attack them. After the others flee, Mirrani reveals herself and welcomes Homura to the Mirror World. She introduces herself and explains to the Sword of Edens that the one who attacked her friends is her reflection. Mirrani proclaims that her hunting style is to pick off her prey one by one and, bringing them to her Mirror World and shatter them.

Mirrani explains that the world they are in is from her Ether Gear, Mirror Trick , and claims that no one can defeat her in there. She shatters the mirror behind her and sends the shards flying to Homura. Her opponent looks back to where she was, allowing Mirrani to kick her from one of the mirror shards. She confuses Homura by appearing from all the mirrors, asking where the real her is. Mirrani tells the swordswoman not to think about how she is in a mirror within a mirror and that it will not be long before she shatters. She then breaks the mirror above Homura, sending the shards to her opponent. She is astonished that Homura's able to strike down every shard but quickly realizes that she is reflecting them back at her. Impressed by her opponent, Mirrani moves into another mirror shard and states that when she's inside the mirrors no one can catch her. However, Homura strikes one shard, forcing Mirrani out of it. She asks how she found her, which Homura replies that it was her incessant prattling, making her surprised that the swordswoman managed to pinpoint her exact location.

Mirrani gathers the shards and sends them flying at her opponent, and goes into the mirrors again. When Homura strikes a shard reflecting herself, Mirrani explains that doing so means she is hurting herself. She mocks Homura in that she can only defend herself while Mirrani can do whatever she wants. Quickly, she grabs Homura's wrists through some shards and attacks her with Mirror World Finisher , sending kicks and punches through the other shards in multiple directions. With her opponent down, Mirrani warns her that she and her friends shouldn't have come to fight the empire, stating that she is one of six chosen by Nero as the most powerful soldiers of the Aoi Cosmos. When Homura calls the deeds of the Nero Empire evil, Mirrani simply states that they fight for the people. Though Homura counters that they don't include the lives of its androids, to which Mirrani retorts that they are just machines. She sends more shards at Homura, which the swordswoman strikes, taking the damage from her reflections, much to Mirrani's shock. She retreats into a mirror as Homura disregard that as she doesn't fear the pain and sends swords to break her mirrors. Mirrani is horrified by this and notices her opponent's Ether power reaching its critical point, Overdrive .

After Homura breaks all of her mirrors, the swordswoman tells her that androids have lives because they have hearts. Mirrani tells her to shut up, creating four clones and charges at her. However, her clones are taken out, quickly followed by her. Laying on the ground, Mirrani confesses that she always knew that androids do have lives and what Shura is trying to do is wrong, admitting that she was so scared and that she couldn't do anything when the prince killed Cyca in front of her. She cries and questions why she is telling her opponent this, which Homura replies that they are within the mirror and that it reflects one's truth.

When Nasseh texted to his fellow Oceans members that their enemies can use Overdrive, Mirrani confirms his claim with her texted reply about her defeat in the Oceans Group Chat.

When 20,000 Imperial Antimatter Bombs on Nero 66 are activated by Ziggy with the All-Link System , Mirrani is with her fellow Oceans on an escape pod that Nasseh prepared for them, and asks Lyra if they are leaving Shura behind.

Powers and Abilities

Mirrani is one of the Oceans 6 who acted as a top official of the Nero Empire , and was chosen by the Emperor himself as one of the most powerful soldiers in the Aoi Cosmos.

Mirror Trick (ミラートリック Mirā Torikku ): By using this Ether Gear , Mirrani is able to travel into the Mirror World and trap people there, replacing them with their reflections and make them attack their allies in the real world. The Ether marks manifest on her arms. Mirrani is able to break the mirrors in the Mirror World and send the shards charging to her opponent. She is able to enter and move within the mirrors of this world and can appear in more than one at the same time. She can send parts of her body through the mirror shards and can even send copies of herself. However, if her exact location is found and is assaulted, she will be ejected from the mirror. If her opponent attacks a mirror with their reflection, then they receive the damage from it.

  • Mirror World Finisher (鏡界殺 Kyōkaisatsu ): Mirrani sends a group of mirror shards around her opponent and restrains them. She then sends a barrage of kicks and punches through the shards using her legs and extra hands.
  • Mirage Trigger (ミラージュトリガー Mirāju Torigā ): Mirrani creates four clones of herself and charges towards her opponent together.

Empire Ether (エンパイアエーテル Enpaia Ēteru ): As a member of the Oceans' new generation, Mirrani has the power to tamper her enemies' Ether , being able to forcibly reconfigure it. In her case, she is able to replace people with their reflections and trapping them inside the Mirror World.
