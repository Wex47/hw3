Happy Blaster Solo Mode ( ハッピーブラスターソロモード , Happī Burasutā Soro Mōdo ) is a Happy Blasters mode.

Description

The user begins to charge Ether and then uses it to transform their mechanical body to manifest a small cannon from their back that unleashes a barrage of Ether bullets.
