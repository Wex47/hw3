The Kaede Cosmos ( 楓 ( カエデ ) 宇宙 ( コスモス ) , Kaede Kosumosu , lit. "Maple Cosmos") is a region of space where many planets reside. It is one of the four cosmoses that comprises the Grand Shiki Cosmos .

Description

The Kaede Cosmos is home to several different planets, that has at least one planet with a government system that started a war. The Kaede Cosmos' most distinct feature is the flowing checkered pattern that envelops the entire cosmos, inverting the colors of the stars.
