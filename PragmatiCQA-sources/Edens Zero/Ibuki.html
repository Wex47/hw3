Ibuki ( イブキ , Ibuki ) is a member of the Interstellar Union Army and a subordinate of Holy of the Oración Seis Interstellar .

Appearance

Ibuki is a tall young woman with a slim figure and long dark hair. She wears a dark sleeveless tube top with a light toga along with a pair of arm bands wrapped around her wrists and two headbands on her head. She has a jeweled necklace around her neck.

Personality

Ibuki is a cautious woman, concerned of her leader's team up with two Galáctica members that might have consequences in the long run. However, she concedes with Holy's reassurance and is willing to obey orders from her. She also shows anticipation of the upcoming operation that the female Interstellar member has plotted.

Synopsis

Kaede War arc

In charge of the Midnight fleet, Ibuki expresses her concern to Holy about the other Interstellar's reactions toward her alliance with the Demon King and space pirates . She complies with her commander's order to show their enemies the power of their ships and notes of the "immaculate military operation" coming soon.

Complying with Holy's orders to eliminate all of the Dragons , Ibuki pilots the Midnight alongside Shiro and fires the special anti-Dragon Ether cannons alongside their forces, destroying them in droves. She states that they can buy their allies some time to handle the huge numbers of Dragons.

Powers and Abilities

Ibuki's powers and abilities are currently unknown.
