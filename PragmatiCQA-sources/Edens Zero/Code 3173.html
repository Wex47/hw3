The Etherion ( エーテリオン , Ēterion ) is Edens Zero 's highly classified secret, also known as "Code 3173" .

Description

According to Witch Regret , unlocking the Etherion requires authentication of all the Four Shining Stars . Its capabilities are currently unknown as Witch decides to withhold the information from Homura the new Sword of Edens, who is unable to keep secrets until the time is right.

Witch also speculates that the Edens One has its own version of Etherion after witnessing the destruction of many Dragons left in its wake at Dragonfall .

Trivia

  • Etherion shares its name with the Magic Weapon and the Ultimate Magic from both of Hiro Mashima 's previous manga series, Fairy Tail and Rave Master , respectively.
  • Code 3173 is also a reference to Rave Master , being the experiment number for Elie as well the original basis for her name.
