Turn-to-Timonium ( ターン・トゥ・ティモニウム , Tān tu Timoniumu ) , also known as Tantimo ( タンチモ , Tanchimo ) for short, are a race of horses native to Digitalis .

Appearance

The Turn-to-Timonium are large purple leporine creatures but are called horses in their description. They wear collars with a bell attached and hold a small piece of paper that reads "Auto". Their heads are always shaking left to right frantically.

Trivia

  • This race shares its name, horse-status, backstory and appearance with Tanchimo , a character from Hiro Mashima 's previous series, Rave Master .
