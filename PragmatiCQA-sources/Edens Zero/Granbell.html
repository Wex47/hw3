Granbell ( グランベル , Guranberu ) is a planet that used to be occupied by robots, and is where Shiki Granbell was raised and named after.

Description

The entire planet is a theme park with islands and vast amounts of water surrounding them. After the events in which Rebecca and Happy visit the planet and are joined by Shiki in their adventures, all activity in Granbell comes to an end when its resident robots experience a simultaneous system failure and break down.

Location

The planet is located in an unspecified part of the Sakura Cosmos .
