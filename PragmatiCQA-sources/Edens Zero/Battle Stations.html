Battle Stations ( , Battle Stations ) are stations where the members of the Crew of Edens can fire their guns of the ship against enemies that attack the Edens Zero .

Description

Each Battle Station can use Ether Link Ammo to load the guns with its user's personal Ether by flipping on their Ether Link switches. This allows the user able to transfer the abilities of their Ether Gear into the bullets they fire, and their attacks will become more powerful but they should be careful of overuse.

The user on a Battle Station has access to an objective visor which allows them to hit their targets accurately in battle.

Usage

When the Dragons attack the Edens Zero in Dragonfall , Shiki , Rebecca , Homura , Weisz and Jinn manned their respective Battle Stations to defend the ship. After Hermit unlocks Ether Link Ammo for them and tells them to use it through their Ether Gear abilities, they are able to dispatch the Dragons, allowing the Edens Zero to escape Dragonfall and enter the Aoi Cosmos .
