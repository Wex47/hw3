Description

Smoke Bar is a form of Ether Gear that revolves around smoke, where under the user's command allows them to utilize it for varying purposes. Users of this Ether Gear appear to be able to manifest smoke from various parts of their bodies. They can convert their own body into smoke, enabling them to avoid physical assaults and to hover in the air and fly. The smoke from their bodies can act as a medium to perform other techniques, as well as altering its properties, such as electrically charging the smoke or turning it into poisonous mist without causing any damage to themselves.
