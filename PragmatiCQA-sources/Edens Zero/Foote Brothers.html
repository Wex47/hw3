The Foote Brothers ( フットブラザーズ , Futto Burazāzu ) , also known as the Southern Annihilators ( 南部の壊し屋 , Nanbu no Kowashiya ) , are a pair of brothers and members of the Sibir Family who operated on Planet Norma in the year X442.

Appearance

The larger of the two has a brawny body with muscular arms and wears a tight black shirt with dots decorating it. His right arm is tatooed with a band encircling his upper arm. His hair is tied up into a long stalk with a bun at the end.

The smaller of the has a slimmer build and wears body armor over his torso and a black shirt with the sleeves rolled up. His armor has star patterns at the shoulders and an emblem over his chest.

Both brothers wear armor around one of their legs, the larger one on his left while the smaller one on his right, mirroring each other. The larger one's gives off the appearance of a skull at the knee and has a block for his foot. The smaller one's resemble the typical armor of knights but has a metallic foot with toes.

Personality

Both brothers have an obsession with legs as they instantly became enamoured and distracted by Rebecca Bluegarden 's bare legs during their fight.

They speak with a noticeable accent and have a unique method of giving each other "high fives" by clapping their iron feet together.

Synopsis

Norma arc

The Foote Brothers joined their boss Sibir along with the rest of the Sibir Family in a meeting where they discuss their mission to take over the whole town. Weisz , who is lurking nearby, recognizes them and is surprised that Sibir has them working for him.

After Sibir leaves in the Knight Gear to pursue Weisz, he orders the Foote Brothers to capture Rebecca and the two of them approach her from behind while she's worrying over Shiki . They introduce themselves to her as Sibir's right-hand men and when she is less than impressed by their introduction, the two brothers angrily sentence her to an axe kicking.

Both brothers attack Rebecca, shocking her with the power of their kicks but both keep missing as they become entranced by Rebecca's bare legs. As Rebecca picks up her gun and aims at them, the Foote Brothers quickly call for a break from the fight to admire her legs and express a desire to see her feet as well. Creeped out by the Foote Brothers, Rebecca shoots anyway but they deflect the bullets with kicks. The two of them dive eagerly towards Rebecca's legs but Shiki bursts out of the ground nearby and defeats them both instantly with his Magimech Attack: Gravity Windmill Kick . Even after being defeated, the two brothers still expressed a desire to see her legs, exasperating Rebecca.

Belial Gore arc

World No. 30

Noah Glenfield references Rebecca's battles against the Foote Brothers as being an example of where Rebecca subconsciously changed her location with Cat Leaper in time as her life was threatened or lost.

Powers and Abilities

Enhanced Strength : The Foote Brothers show remarkable kicking power with their foot-based fighting styles, being able to smash through solid stone using the power of their legs alone.

Enhanced Speed : The two brothers were able to deflect Rebecca's Ether Blaster bullets from point-blank range showing off their great reflexes and speed.
