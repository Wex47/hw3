Rogue Out (ローグアウト Rōgu Auto ) were a mercenary squad based on Guilst renowned as space outlaws that would do any job no matter how dirty it is. They were also known as Sister 's "soldiers" and "knights".

History

In the year X482, Sister Ivry was captured while on a mission as a member of Shooting Starlight on Guilst . Her captor, a fanatical female android , captured Sister to take her healing powers so that she could heal her injured comrades. Eventually, the android stole Ivry's identity as Sister along with her powers and began using them for her own profit. Soon after, people started gathering around her, eventually leading to the formation of the mercenary squad Rogue Out. At some point, Sister remodeled the other android that came with Ivry and also made him a member of Rogue Out.

For 10 years, the mercenary squad would operate on Guilst carrying out missions under the command of Sister. Over the years they would gain notoriety in the Sakura Cosmos and their ranks would continue to grow, gaining notable members such as Ganoff and Jinn , who had joined for his own reasons.

Synopsis

Guilst arc

Rogue Out is hired by Illega to kidnap 30 B-Cubers and bring them to Guilst. However, they managed to kidnap 29 (one of them was Rebecca Bluegarden ) because Shiki Granbell stopped Jinn from kidnapping Labilia Christy .

Despite the setback, the mercenary group arrived on Guilst and delivered the girls to Illega. Due to missing one more B-Cuber as part of the job, Rogue Out did not get the payment as promised and Sister decided to settle matters with Illega personally and kill him at Illega Tower.

On the other hand, Shiki, Homura and Weisz pursued Rogue Out and arrived on Guilst where they found their headquarters. They encountered Sister and fought against Rogue Out's soldiers she sent them against. All of her men were defeated by Homura and later Weisz swiftly took down Mosco who arrived Rogue Out Headquarters.

Homura and Weisz would soon discover the real Sister was trapped underneath the headquarters and freed her. Meanwhile, Shiki faced the Sister imposter, Ganoff, and Jinn in a brief battle. Sister Ivry appeared and easily took down her impersonator. Jinn was livid of the deception and ended up killing the faker despite her pleas.

When Chronophage consumed Guilst of 1,200 years of its time with less than 10% of the planet's population escaped alive, the entirety of Rogue Out were erased from existence. Only Jinn and Mosco are confirmed to have escaped with the former at Belial Gore and the latter at Edens Zero respectively.

Members

  • Sister : The leader and founder of the mercenary squad. She has healing powers which she stole from Sister Ivry. She was killed by Kris when he learned of her deception.
  • Ganoff : A sadistic alien mercenary. His main weapon is an assault rifle. His fate after the disaster on Guilst remains unknown.
  • Kris Rutherford : Referred to by his codename Jinn , he is a cyborg ninja mercenary who uses a type of Wind Ether Gear called Wind Rage and hand-to-hand combat. After the disaster on Guilst, he left Rogue Out.
  • Mosco Versa-0 : An android who was originally a servant to Sister Ivry and remodeled by fake Sister as a member of Rogue Out. After being defeated by Weisz, Mosco was healed and reverted back to normal by Sister Ivry.
