Warrior Maiden Ultimate Attack: Odin Strike ( 戦乙女最終奥義 オーディンストライク , Ikusa Otome Saishū Ōgi: Ōdin Sutoraiku ) is a Soul Blade technique.

Description

After activating her Valkyrie Armament System , Valkyrie summons an insurmountable number of Ether blades around herself and launches them upon her enemies, creating explosions on impact.

Homura can also perform the same attack, which she describes as her ultimate secret technique. When using her complete Overdrive , Homura summons a great number of oriental Ether blades and launches them, creating explosions on impact.

Usage

Valkyrie in her Valkyrie Armament System form uses this technique to destroy the rampaging hordes of Stones that were unleashed by Kurenai Kōgetsu from hurting the people of the Labor District .

Homura uses this technique to attack Garrot of the Zaiten Three for desecrating the body of her deceased mentor. She also uses it to knock Valkyrie's Ether sword away high into the air that then strikes Garrot down from above.

Homura, in her first complete Overdrive, uses this technique against Mirrani Lucra in order to shatter all the mirrors in the Mirror World .
