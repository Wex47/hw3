The Element 4 (エレメント ４ ( フォー ) Eremento Fō ) were a special forces of Drakken's Organization that served under Drakken Joe of the Oración Seis Galáctica .

Overview

The Element 4 are known to be Drakken Joe's elite military force. Each member represents one of the four elements that are fundamental to Drakken's alchemy —fire, water, wind, and earth—and are considered by Drakken to be a part of himself , where the Dark Alchemist needed them to help him maintain the balance of his Ether .

After Drakken Joe's defeat and arrest, the Element 4 has been disbanded with Kleene is under the care of Sister Ivry at Edens Zero with her brother Jinn as guests while Laguna starts working again as an actor with a holographic disguise of a middle-aged man at Scheherazade Theatre in Blue Garden , as he becomes a wanted man in the Sakura Cosmos after Drakken's arrest.

Members

  • Laguna Husert : The Spirit of Water (水の精霊 Mizu no Seirei ), an effeminate, young man whose Ether Gear , Tears Lover , gives him the ability to transform others' bodies into water through their tears.
  • Fie : The Flame Sniper (炎の 狙撃手 ( スナイパー ) Honō no Sunaipā ), a young man who uses his Ether Gear, Flame Ammo , to imbue himself and his bullets with the element of fire.
  • Kleene Rutherford : Codenamed Sylph of the Wind (風のシルフ Kaze no Shirufu ) and the Element of Wind (風のエレメント Kaze no Eremento ), a young woman who uses Wind Snatch to steal and absorb objects and people with wind currents, even in the vacuum of space.
  • Daichi : Also known as Daichi of Earth (土のダイチ Tsuchi no Daichi ), a large, alien creature whose Ether Gear, Pain's Tree , gives him the ability to cause intense pain in others by growing branches in their bodies.

Trivia

  • Fairy Tail , one of Hiro Mashima's previous works, also features a group named the Element 4 .
