Rogue Out HQ was a church controlled by the mercenary squad, Rogue Out . After the Chronophage consumed Guilst of its 1,200 years and set it into the past, the building ceased to exist.

Description

This building functioned as the headquarters of Rogue Out. It was a worn church with broken windows and cracked walls. On the front of the building, the organization's name and logo were written in large letters. In the church's main room there was a big statue with an elevator to the basement. In the basement, there was a machine used to transfer a captive Sister Ivry 's healing powers to Sister .

Location

Rogue Out HQ was located within an unpleasant city on Guilst that according to Weisz would make even the slums on Norma look like flower gardens.
