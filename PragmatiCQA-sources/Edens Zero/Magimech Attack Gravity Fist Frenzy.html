Magimech Attack: Gravity Fist Frenzy ( 魔械流 重拳乱舞 , Makai-ryū: Jūken Ranbu , lit. "Magic Machine Style: Heavy Fist Wild Dance") is a Satan Gravity technique used by Shiki Granbell .

  • 4 Site Navigation

Description

The user's fist begins to glow prior to unfolding a barrage of punches onto their opponent(s), causing them to fly about.

Usage

Shiki first used this technique against the collective forces of Sibir's Gang , pummelling several of them back after drawing them close to him with his Gravity Wave .

Shiki uses this technique to defeat Jamilov , saving the city of Crysta and its NPC citizens from being erased from existence.
