Guns Harvest (ガンズハーベスト Ganzu Hābesuto ) is a technique used by Hermit Mio .

Contents

  • 1 Description
  • 2 Usage
  • 4 Site Navigation

Description

The user remotely hacks into any weapons that are connected to the network and takes control of them; levitating them into the air and aiming them simultaneously at their target. The bullets can be coated in an Ether layer, rendering them non-lethal.

Usage

Hermit first used the technique as a finishing move against Fie , taking control of all of the guns in his armory to attack him non-lethally, defeating the Element 4 member.
