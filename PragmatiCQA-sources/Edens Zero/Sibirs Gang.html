The Sibir Family ( シビルファミリー , Shibiru Famirī ) is a group of criminals led by Sibir who operated on Norma in the year X442.

History

In X442, the Sibir Family plotted to take over the town in Norma and DJ Zombie , a loan shark, was their fixer at the time. Weisz defected from the group after a disagreement with Sibir over the theft of his deceased mother 's pendant and stole a case of money from Sibir in retaliation. The money actually belonged to DJ Zombie who found out about the theft and cut off Weisz's right arm as punishment.

Sibir eventually caught the real thief among his gang and tried to return the pendant to Weisz. But Weisz told him to keep the pendant, seeing it as proof that he couldn't trust his former friend and they drifted apart.
