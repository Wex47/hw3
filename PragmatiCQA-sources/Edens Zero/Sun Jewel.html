Sun Jewel ( サン・ジュエル , San Jueru ) , also known as the Planet of Gemstones ( 宝石の惑星 , Hōseki no Wakusei ) , is a mining planet.

Description

Sun Jewel appears to be a light blue rocky world covered in mountainous gemstones. An orbiting band of jewels and gems surrounds the planet.

After surviving two harsh dictatorial regimes at the hands of Baron Mordo and Madame Kurenai , Sun Jewel became a free planet. According to Xiaomei , sometime in the future, the people will elect a new leader who advances the cause of democracy. The planet will also become a famous treasure hunting spot for adventurers and will walk the path of prosperity for ages to come.

Location

Sun Jewel is located in the Sakura Cosmos , and is far away from Mildian .
