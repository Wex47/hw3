Five Sword Fencing: Tiger Claw ( 五形剣 虎の型 , Gogyōken: Tora no Kata ) is a Soul Blade technique used.

Description

The user forms a pair of long Ether claw blades around their wrists for close-range melee combat.

Usage

Homura uses this technique to battle Mora , who notes that she can use all kinds of bladed weapons with her Ether Gear , and attacks him with Tiger Six Claw Strike to no avail.
