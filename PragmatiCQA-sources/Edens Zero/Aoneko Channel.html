Aoneko Channel ( ＡＯＮＥＫＯ ( アオネコ ) チャンネル, Aoneko Chaneru ) is a channel on the video sharing site B-Cube , managed by Rebecca Bluegarden and Happy .

Description

Aoneko Channel is a channel where Rebecca and Happy upload videos, which are generally unscripted, random, and amateur in quality, making them unpopular. By the time Rebecca was kidnapped by the mercenary group Rogue Out , the channel had a total of 105 subscribers. The channel saw a massive spike in viewership when it was promoted by more famous B-Cubers that Rebecca helped rescue from Guilst , although the general response was overwhelmingly negative due to the low-quality content. After receiving some tips from Couchpo , Rebecca now has one of the most popular channels with 3.1 million subscribers.

Trivia

  • Aoneko means "blue cat" in Japanese.
