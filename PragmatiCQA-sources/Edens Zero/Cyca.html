Cyca ( サイカ , Saika ) was a machine reverend who was one of Poseidon Nero 's top officials and a member of the Oceans 6 . He is the surrogate grandfather of Mirrani Lucra .

Appearance

Personality

Cyca is a solemn bot who expresses shame towards Ziggy for daring to oppose the Nero Empire after learning what the Demon King did to Foresta. He always holds his hands in a prayer stance. The reverend is humble to admit to Shura that he was one of the weaker fighters in the Oceans 6 but tries to reassure that he can contribute before he is brutally killed by the prince. When Mirrani was young, Cyca was supportive of her dream to become an Oceans member like him in the future.

Synopsis

History

When Mirrani tells Cyca of her dream of becoming part of the Oceans like him when she grows up, he happily supported her and told his granddaughter that he has no doubt that she would reach it.

Foresta arc

Attending the meeting with Poseidon Nero on Nero 1 , Cyca solemnly expresses that as a fellow machine, he is ashamed of Ziggy 's behavior for baring fangs against the Nero Empire after hearing what the Demon King did to the people of Foresta . Cyca remains stoic when the Emperor declares that he will do nothing and leaves the meeting after making a roll of the guiding dice and sees the results. He is silent when the command of Oceans 6 is officially transferred to Shura and is ready to carry out orders from the prince.

As Shura levitates several of the objects around him upside down in a room, Cyca remains quiet as Ijuna informs the prince that everything is ready for departure. He brings up Nero 66 to Shura and wonders if that remote planet holds clues to finding Ziggy. Cyca is surprised when Shura tells him that he cannot bring him along. The reverend asks what the prince means by that and admits that he is one of the weaker fighters of the Oceans 6. When Cyca tries to reassure him, he is suddenly grabbed in the face by Shura who cruelly crushes his head with Satan Gravity , killing him in front of the Oceans 6 and Ijuna. His headless body falls down on the floor as Fabiano is openly outraged by the prince's callous action.

Powers and Abilities

Cyca is one of the Oceans 6 who acted as a top official of the Nero Empire , and was chosen by the Emperor himself as one of the most powerful soldiers in the Aoi Cosmos. Cyca acknowledged that he was one of the weaker fighters in the group and he was ultimately killed effortlessly by an attack from Poseidon Shura's Satan Gravity, although he was taken by surprise.
