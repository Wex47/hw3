Magimech Attack: Gravity Tackle ( 魔械流 重身 , Makai-ryū: Jūshin , lit. "Magic Machine Style: Heavy Body") is a Satan Gravity technique.

Contents

  • 1 Description
  • 2 Usage
  • 4 Site Navigation

Description

The user spins around in mid-air and uses gravity to pummel themselves at the opponent, striking them with heavy blunt damage.

Usage

Shiki first used this technique to crush one of the flying enemies of the Magical Beast Army that were threatening Hermit .
