Description

Diner Fairy's is a three-level building, where the exterior consists of metal columns, some having pipes coming out and glass railings by the tables, at the top of one side is a sign of the restaurant's name on a picture of chicken leg, a bigger sign is seen on the entrance with two chicken legs plates on the sides. The interior of the restaurant has escalators and stairs that connect the floors, curved ceilings, floating table sets with cushioned seats that are at the sides of the walking area, some tables have machine and there are even drones flying with utensils.

Location

The restaurant is located somewhere in the city of Eternal on Blue Garden.

Menu

The food served in this restaurant has a wide range, from pasta, pizza, to even chicken and other meat products. Rebecca exclaims how great their Cureberry Chicken is, saying that it is to die for.

History

Intro arc

After a tour and registration at Shooting Starlight , Shiki , Rebecca , and Happy visit Diner Fairy's where they get their meals to eat and converse together. At their table, Shiki discusses with Rebecca that he thought Guilds were supposed to be more friendly, like a big family, which Rebecca explains to him that it is more of a place for people to get work.
