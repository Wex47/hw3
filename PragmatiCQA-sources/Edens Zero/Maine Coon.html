Maine Coon ( メインクーン , Mein Kūn ) is the flagship of Goodwin in the Oasis rebel army.

Description

The Maine Coon is a large spaceship with a prominent round head, cat-shaped visor, and ears at the front, a large pair of wings, and has three engines at the back. The flight deck sits at the back and has a curved roof and an insignia of Oasis on the center.

It also houses a squad of Oasis starfighters that can launch into battle in space from its lower hanger bay.

It can fire a large energy beam that destroys several enemy ships in one blast.
