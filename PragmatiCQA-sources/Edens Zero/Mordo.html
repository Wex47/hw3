Personality

Being the previous ruler of Sun Jewel, Mordo is a greedy man, keeping prisoners in the Labor District to collect metals from the Stones for him and is also described as lecherous by Kurenai.

Synopsis

History

Mordo married Kurenai around two years after Valkyrie Yuna took over the woman's place in the Labor District and Kurenai did not return to her daughter. Not long after the marriage, he was assassinated and his position was stolen by his wife, taking over the planet Sun Jewel as its new tyrannical ruler.
