The Taurus FT-003 (タウロスＦＴ００３ Taurosu FT 003 ) are one-man starfighters that are utilized by the crew of the Edens Zero .

Description

The Taurus FT-003 are small starfighters, large enough only for a single person to occupy it. They have a large glass dome for the top half, with a small of wings just behind and a larger pair of wings to the side, each with the Edens Zero emblem. They have a splotch pattern around the glass, reminiscent of that of a cow's pattern. The bottom half has a dark underside, with a pair of blasters mounted infront of the wings. The symbol of the Taurus zodiac is branded on the very front.

Features

20mm Gun Pods : The starfighter's primary weapon is a pair of blasters mounted at the front of the ship on either side of the pilot.

Homing Multi-Missiles : The starfighter's secondary weapon is a cluster of homing missiles that can lock on to approximately twenty separate targets before detonating.

Trivia

  • The name Taurus FT-003 is a reference to Hiro Mashima's previous work, Fairy Tail . Taurus was the name of one of Lucy's celestial spirits whose manga debut was in the third chapter of the installment.
