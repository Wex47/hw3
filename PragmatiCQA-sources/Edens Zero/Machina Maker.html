Description

Machina Maker lets the user change or remodel all kinds of machines, allowing them to upgrade a ship and recreate broken or missing parts of a machine or android. The user can also modify the floor to create guns and bullets and transform a weapon into a flamethrower.

Trivia

  • Several of Weisz's techniques are references to dragons from Hiro Mashima 's previous work, Fairy Tail :
    • Atlas Flame , a flamethrower, is based on the Hell Fire Dragon of the same name .
    • Vise Cannon , which fires "white bullets of sacred power" , is based on the White Dragon Weisslogia .
    • Drum Buster , which fires dark energy "born from shadows" , is based on the Shadow Dragon Skiadrum .
    • Metallica Explosion is based on the Iron Dragon Metalicana .
    • Grandy-Ray and Grandee Wing are based on the Sky Dragon Grandeeney .
