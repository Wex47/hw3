Shiki Granbell vs. Castellan is a fight fought between Shiki Granbell , the late Demon King 's grandson and Castellan , the King of Granbell Kingdom .

Prologue

Following Rebecca and Happy 's kidnapping by the Robots of Granbell , Shiki gets into a heated argument with King and the Robots of Granbell, with King claiming the Robots were infected by a virus, subsequently giving them self-awareness. He then claimed that this self-awareness led to them planning a rebellion on all humans and to create a world led by machines and that machines and humans could never be friends. Shiki then gets dog-piled by the Robots so as to stop him from interfering with their plans to kill Rebecca and steal her ship. Rebecca begins to cry for Shiki, saying that he called all the Robots his friends and that it doesn't matter if you're a machine or a human. Shiki uses his Aether Gear to knock all the Robots off of him and states that his Grandfather told him to do everything he could to keep friends who would cry for him, followed by a claim that he had thought the Robots weren't being serious. King then attacked Shiki, initiating the fight.

Battle

The fight begins with King charging at Shiki, and Shiki dodging the strike. While this is happening, Happy manages to undo the ropes binding him and Rebecca to the posts and they watch the fight from where they are. King strikes at Shiki from above, and Shiki jumps over King's fist and onto the wall. Rebecca mentions that Shiki is somehow stuck to the Shiki, while Shiki runs across it to dodge bullets that King has shot at him. Shiki proceeds to flip over King's head while King exclaims that it shouldn't be possible for humans to dodge bullets shot with a machine's accuracy, to which Shiki replies that he can. King claims there isn't anything Shiki could do to him, calling him someone without the courage to leave the island, after which Shiki jumps off the wall and, after multiple flips in the air, punches King from above with his Demon Machine Style: Heavy Fist, knocking King into the ground and destroying the ground in the process. Shiki catches Rebecca and Happy as they fall into the hole he created and uses his Gravity Aether Gear to levitate, stopping their fall. Shiki sets his gravity to North by Northwest and falls towards the Aqua Wing . He claims that he'll stay on Granbell to fix the Robots but Rebecca pulls him into the ship, stating that friends stick together.

Aftermath

Shiki, Rebecca, and Happy leave Granbell behind as they sail into Space. Shiki exclaims that he still thought of all the Robots as friends, even though they betrayed him at the end, and we see all the Robots power down for the last time.
