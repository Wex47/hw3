Description

Gold Palace is a city of many golden skyscrapers. The citizens of Gold Palace are usually very rich, and dress in expensive clothing. The city has multiple casinos, one example being Casino Paradise .

Location

Gold Palace is located in the wealthy sector of Sun Jewel.
