Appearance

Space Slime are small amorphous creatures, that are capable of taking on human forms if they wish.

Powers and Abilities

Shapeshifting : Space Slimes are able to transform their body and appearance, capable of taking on human forms. This makes it impossible to restrain them with ropes.

  • Elasticity : Being slimes, they can stretch parts of their bodies.
