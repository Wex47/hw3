Happy Blasters (ハッピーブラスター Happī Burasutā ) are the form Happy takes when he shapeshifts into gun mode. It was created by Professor Weisz .

Description

The user begins to charge Ether and then uses it to transform into twin Ether blasters. In this state, the user can fire Ether bullets that are powerful enough to incapacitate their targets but don't kill them. The Happy Blasters are capable of firing Ether bullets on their own while levitating but cannot aim well.
