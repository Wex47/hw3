Shield Ether ( シールドエーテル , Shīrudo Ether ) is an Ether ability used by Witch Regret that allows the user to produce barriers of Ether.

Contents

  • 1 Description
  • 2 Usage
  • 4 Site Navigation

Description

The user raises their arm and creates a shield of Ether that can stop bullets in front of them.

Usage

In World No. 29 , Witch first uses the technique to block a shot from Seth Anderson during the invasion of the Edens Zero by Seth, Diego Reyes and Maria Slime .

In World No. 30 , Witch uses the technique to block a water blade of Tears Lover from Laguna Husert during their battle on Belial Gore .
