Deadend Crow (デッドエンド・クロウ Deddoendo Kurō ), also known as the "Titan of Eternal Darkness" (常闇の巨人 Tokoyami no Kyojin ), is a machine and one of the Oración Seis Galáctica .

Appearance

Standing at 420 meters tall, befitting of his epithet , Deadend Crow is a giant musclebound machine with a few lines going down his metallic face, his neck has three bolts on each side, wears a fur collar, and has a gargantuan wheel on his back.

Personality

Deadend Crow is described to be a monster who thinks of humans as his toys and he is willing to team up with Ziggy.

Synopsis

History

Prior to Ziggy's reawakening, Deadend Crow was considered as the most dangerous machine in the cosmoses and according to the public story, he instigated the "Bloody Atmos Day Incident" which resulted in over a million casualties.

X495 arc

At some point, Crow decides to team up with Ziggy , making Lendard their base of operations. He wanders around the factory, unknowingly passing by Connor as the man flees the planet in his small spaceship.

Deadend Crow stomps on a scanning facility, which killed 99 human collaborators inside much to his sick amusement. He is then confronted by Ziggy where he tells the Demon King that he didn't know he had kept human pets around, which the gigantic bot points out that Ziggy is going to kill them all anyway after being informed that his ally intended to absorb their various abilities. After receiving the stern warning, Crow then notes that a measly hundred humans won't be enough and asks when Ziggy is going to feed him enough to actually fill him up. Reassured that he will have the entire Kaede Cosmos 's human population to himself as a reward, he knows that he will have to destroy the Edens Zero for Ziggy first. Although Crow asks the Demon King why he can't use the Edens One .

Kaede War arc

In the middle of battle across Lendard, Crow roams around the area and confronts the Edens Zero when they approach him, maliciously grinning at them. Inspecting the Demon King's warship in front of him, Crow is unimpressed of Ziggy's claim of the Edens Zero to be equal with the Edens One before attacking it with his laser eyes. He easily shrugs off his enemies' attacks with a grin on his face.

Powers and Abilities

Deadend Crow is a member of the Oración Seis Galáctica , a group that is stated to be the cosmos's strongest warriors, being renowned for crushing entire planets. Holy describes Crow to be a "living fortress" , and Elsie Crimson is wary of his power.

Eye Beams : Crow fires a pair of powerful laser blasts from his eyes toward his enemies.

Immense Durability : Crow is incredibly durable, easily shrugging off attacks from the Edens Zero and its starfighter squad .
