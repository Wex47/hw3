Kris Rutherford ( クリス・ラザフォード , Kurisu Razafōdo ) , also known as Jinn ( ジン , Jin ) , is an O-Tech and former mercenary of the disbanded organization, Rogue Out , who carried out any mission, including abducting B-Cubers from the Planet Blue Garden . After Rogue Out's disbandment, he moved on to working under Drakken Joe as an assistant to his sister, Kleene of the Element 4 . He later betrayed Drakken Joe after learning that he was siphoning the lives of everyone living on the Belial Gore, including his own sister's. He initially stayed on the Edens Zero with his sister temporarily, but after Sister Ivry fulfilled her promise to heal Kleene and Weisz defeating Dr. Muller, they both officially joined the Zero's crew as the Winds of Edens .

Appearance

Jinn is a tall, slim and muscular O-Tech whose body mass is 60% mechanical, with lines around his body and sides of his face. He has red eyes and large spiky green hair with the bangs brushed to the right and is held by a dark-blue headband. His attire in Universe 1 consisted of a dark-colored battlesuit with light-colored armored shoulder pads, gauntlets and boots, an unknown type of chest armor, and a dark-blue cloak around his shoulders that covered most of his body. This overall gives him the appearance of a ninja.

Onboard the Belial Gore in Universe 2 , Jinn appeared in a new attire which consists of a different type of battle armor covering a sleeveless shirt, a new headband, and a scarf with ripped ends wrapped around his neck. After leaving Belial Gore and onwards, Jinn starts wearing a dark-colored, light-trimmed sleeveless tank top and matching trousers, along with his signature headband and scarf.

In the year X495, Jinn's hair has grown to reach his shoulders and now wears a sleeveless, dark-colored ninja outfit under a long cloak, along with a matching headband.

Personality

Jinn appears to be serious and dedicated to any particular assignment he is working on. Though he is a mercenary and is capable of kidnapping people without objection, he does have a degree of morality as he isn't sadistic and doesn't go out of his way to torture them. He is also aware that he did terrible things while working under Sister . Jinn is a loner and claims that he doesn't have friends, except for his sister Kleene, the only person that he has any connections.

After Weisz destroys Müller's body and Sister Ivry healed his sister from her emotional state as promised, Jinn became more open if not friendlier, as shown when he smiled at Shiki who told him they are friends despite his earlier comment about joining the crew as members but changed his mind. This was shown again when he told his sister he would protect her while telling her he can't let the ship down, smiling at her. However, he still retains his serious attitude but with a calmer approach.

Synopsis

History

As a child, Kris lived with his family, consisting of his mother, father, and sister, Kleene. The Rutherford Family were patrons of Müller Royal Laboratories and funded their research. However, in the year X479, after Müller was arrested, the Rutherfords found out about the truth of his research, and withdrew their financial support. In the year X482, sometime after Müller's escape, the family was visited by him, where he killed his parents and kidnapped him and Kleene to use as guinea pigs for his O-Tech experiments. He was strapped onto a cross, warning the mad scientist in vain to not hurt Kleene, just as Müller is about to dismember him while forcing his sister to watch as an experiment to see how long before her emotions break down entirely. Because of the mad scientist's experiments, he became an O-Tech himself with mechanics making up 60% of his body structure.

Eventually, they were rescued by the bill collectors under the employ of Drakken Joe . He promised Kleene that he would find someone called "Sister" so that she could make Kleene feel better and left in search of her. On planet Guilst, he trained at the Skymech Dojo alongside fellow student Callum Steelford. At some point, Kris found Sister and began working for her in her mercenary squad Rogue Out under the codename "Jinn", in exchange for her curing his sister of her unique disorder.

Guilst arc

Rogue Out is contracted by Illega to capture female B-Cubers for him on Blue Garden . In an alleyway, Jinn is sent to abduct Labilia Christy and finds her with her security detail. He states that he will carry out his mission to Sister. He then disposes of Labilla's first pair of bodyguards, followed by the other three with ease, and tells Labilia that his job is to bring her back with him. He grabs her arm, asking her to come quietly, making her scream for help. Shiki comes to her rescue and grabs his arm. Jinn detaches it and does some hand gestures and it starts flying via a rocket thruster; escaping Shiki's grasp and reattaching back to the rest of his arm. He asks Shiki if he intends to interfere with his work and that his target's feelings are irrelevant. The cyborg refutes Shiki's sentiment of connecting with others as he states he will eliminate all obstacles and activates his own Ether Gear . The two prepare to fight as Shiki begins to activate his Ether Gear too.

Jinn is surprised that Shiki slides under him, before being kicked up into the air. He looks back down, unaware that Shiki is behind him as he is punched again. He uses the wind to stop himself from crashing down. He makes some quick gestures with his hand which confuses Shiki, leaving him vulnerable to be hit from behind by Jinn's detached rocket-powered arm, almost sending his opponent flying into the path of a truck but Shiki nimbly corrects his landing and ends up on top of the truck. Jinn pursues him and almost hits him but fails as Shiki moves to the side of the truck. He remarks on Shiki's use of Gravity Ether Gear while sending his rocket arm after him. After getting his arm back, he jumps to attack Shiki. He starts floating on the wind and creates a large X-shaped hole in the ground with his ninjutsu attack and throws the traffic into the air. He lands after Shiki puts down all the cars and is called out by Shiki for recklessly involving innocent people in their fight but he is unconcerned with that. He receives a communication from his associates, stating that he hasn't secured his target and is told to return to the base. He introduces himself to Shiki and that he can find him on Guilst if he wants to challenge him again, before leaving by the wind.

Jinn arrives back on Rogue Out's spaceship, where he, Ganoff , and Mosco check on the group of B-Cubers from the other side of a glass wall. He stays silent as Ganoff explains the situation the B-Cubers are in. When Ganoff enters the cell to threaten the crying B-Cuber and tortures one of them with Digital Bind , he snatches the man's phone, turning off the binds, and reminds him not to damage the merchandise. Though he is admonished by Ganoff for his failure to capture his assigned B-Cuber. Jinn states that a man using Gravity Ether Gear interfered. They leave after Ganoff taunts Rebecca once more. At Illega Tower , Jinn stays silent as Ganoff argues with Illega's soldiers about receiving their payment for kidnapping the B-Cubers until Sister exits the ship to settle the matter with Illega personally. He notices someone watching them, but finds no one when he turns around. Jinn then watches as Mosco flies back to their headquarters.

He appears when the B-Cubers have escaped from their cell but is intercepted by Shiki. He promises to kill Shiki and the two end up fighting again. He gets overpowered by Shiki but before he can continue to fight him, Ganoff and Sister appear and join the fight after being challenged by Shiki. During the fight, Jinn is used as a shield by Sister to block Shiki's gravity balls, then immediately gets healed by her.

Jinn then learns that the Sister he is working with is a fake, after the real one, Sister Ivry , is freed and beats her imposter, around the same time when a Chronophage approaches Guilst. Jinn watches Shiki escape, who promises to settle their fight another time and tells him to escape too, then turns to the fake Sister on the floor. Furious for deceiving him, he kills the fake Sister by crushing her head under his boot.

Powers and Abilities

Wind Rage (ウィンドレイジ Uindo Reiji ): By using this form of Wind Ether Gear , Jinn can control and manipulate the element of wind in a manner similar to a typhoon. He can move swiftly and incapacitate multiple enemies, stop his fall by floating on air, and vanish like the wind. According to Jinn, his wind has the power to obstruct anything, blowing them away to keep them from getting close. Due to him being an O-Tech, the modifications to his own body have increased the power of his Ether Gear capabilities to the point where his winds are capable of cutting through anything, including buildings. He learned to use this Ether Gear as a student of the planet Guilst's Skymech Dojo, using it in conjunction with the dojo's fighting style, Skymech Ninjutsu .

  • Skymech Ninjutsu Attack: Windstorm Slash (天械流 忍術 烈風斬 Tenkai-ryū Ninjutsu: Reppūzan ): Jinn fires a blast of wind on his target that is strong enough to lift various vehicles, leaving a large X-shaped crater behind.
  • Skymech Ninjutsu Attack: Windstorm Palm Strike (天械流 忍術 烈風掌 Tenkai-ryū Ninjutsu: Reppūshō ): Jinn enshrouds his palm in wind and uses it to strike his opponent, creating a cyclone that blows them away.
  • Skymech Ninjutsu Attack: Crushing Gale (天械流 忍術 疾風砕波 Tenkai-ryū Ninjutsu: Shippū Saiha ): Jinn surrounds his entire arm with wind and thrusts forward towards his opponent. However, the full extent of this technique is unknown.
  • Wind God Punch (風神拳 Fūjinken ): Jinn launches his rocket arm while using wind to increase its speed, striking the opponent with a quick punch.
  • Wind God Sky-Rending Slash (風神裂空斬 Fūjin Rekkūzan ): Jinn coats himself in Ether and rushes past his opponent, leaving behind a torrent of powerful windstorms that shreds through the target at immense speeds.

Overdrive (オーバードライブ Ōbādoraibu ): Using the rudimentary grasp of this technique causes the Ether lines on Jinn' body to become three-dimensional and swirl in the air around him like the wind. During his fight with Callum, Jinn enters his real Overdrive state. His clothes change into a dark-colored, high-collared shirt which lacks sleeves and exposes the sides of his torso, it is connected to a pair of matching black trousers that are tucked into a pair of light-colored boots, and wears a dark, armored mask that covers his entire face. Additionally, he gains a pair of large, dark-colored feathered wings. Ether lines appear on his forearms, where right forearm gains a dark mark resembling a flame. In this form, his wind are greatly augmented to the point where he can strike an opponent with enough wind power and speed to completely slash through them.

Cybernetic Enhancements : As an O-Tech experimented on by Müller, Jinn's own body is modified in order to help him in combat. Through further modification following the events on Guilst, Jinn's own power was raised to 320% compared to what it was during his battle with Shiki at Illega Tower.

  • Rocket Arms : Jinn can detach his arms and fire them towards his opponent using rocket thrusters. He can control the movements of the flying arm by making gestures with his other attached arm.

Enhanced Speed : Jinn is a fast fighter; able to dash through a group of bodyguards at high speed and disable them each with a single strike, disappearing from their sight and reappearing behind them to catch them off-guard.

Enhanced Agility : Jinn is incredibly agile, being fast enough to swiftly dodge Shiki's gravity attacks at high speeds.

Enhanced Strength : He has rather impressive strength, being able to effortlessly crush the fake Sister's head into pieces.
