Drimille ( ドリミール , Dorimīru ) , the "Town of Greenery", is a large town located in Rogue Fantasia on Planet Digitalis .

Description

When the MMORPG Rogue Fantasia was in operation, the residents of the town could only perform the jobs assigned to them and say their scripted lines to the players who visited the town.

After the game was discontinued, the non-player characters altered their programming and continued to live in the town, but with the freedom to do their own jobs and say what they wanted to say.

Location

The town is located somewhere on the surface of the virtual planet Digitalis, but not far away from the spawning area.
