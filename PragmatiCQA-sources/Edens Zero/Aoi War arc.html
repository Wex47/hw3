The Aoi War arc is the thirteenth story arc of the Edens Zero series and the last arc the Aoi Cosmos Saga .

The Cosmic Government Army engages in battle with the Imperial Army in space, allowing the Crew of Edens , accompanied by the rebel army Oasis , to advance towards Nero 66 , the Planet of Ash. Their goal is to destroy the All-Link System and take down Poseidon Shura before he can use the network to destroy every machine in the Aoi Cosmos ! To succeed, they must prevail over Shura, the self-proclaimed "King of Gravity", as well as the most powerful soldiers in the Empire, the Oceans 6 ! As the high tide approaches, the fate of the Cosmos hinges on this battle, which may prove to be too much for the Crew to handle!

Summary

Story Impact

  • The Nero Empire is revealed to be the biggest organization in the Oración Seis Galáctica .
  • The Crew of Edens and Oasis join forces and head towards Nero 66.
    • The Interstellar Union Army later cooperates with Oasis for an air force battle against the Nero Empire, lead by Eraser and Justice .
  • The Ether Gears of Eraser, Milani , Nasseh , Lyra , Callum , Jaguar , Fabiano , Ijuna , and Nero are revealed.
  • Homura , Weisz , Kris , and Kleene achieve Overdrive respectively and Happy gains a new mode .
  • Rebecca learns of the multiple futures that her Cat Leaper can take her, momentarily leaping into a future where she lost her legs and meets the older versions of Shiki and Noah Glenfield .
  • Laguna reveals that Empire Ether is a common technique in the Nero Empire that alters the opponent's ether condition.
  • Ziggy appears to face off against Nero.
  • It is revealed that Ijuna was the Princess of Oasis.
  • It is revealed that Creed , whose real name is Seiji Hōjō was from a shogunate family at Oedo.
  • Jesse accidentally killed Creed when the latter trying to calm the situation down on behalf of Homura, his fellow Oedon, despite her other allegiance.
  • Shiki loses his left eye during his fight with Shura.
  • Shura reveals that he plans to activate 20,000 antimatter bombs on Nero 1 to kill his father and Ziggy.
    • Nero knows this and uses his Ether Gear to teleport them to planet Nero 66.
  • Ziggy absorbs Nero's powers, killing him, and takes his Ether Gear for himself.
  • Shiki uses the Black Sky technique for the first time and defeats Shura.
  • Ziggy uses his new stolen power to activate the bombs on Nero 66.
  • Despite being freed by Laguna from Shura’s brainwashing, Ijuna forgives Shura and both die together as the planet explodes.
  • Witch sacrifices herself to ensure Edens Zero’s escape.
  • During a meeting with Elsie on Nero 1, Ziggy reveals it is now Universe 3 .
  • Elsie decides to spare Ziggy, stating that they will be enemies the next time they meet.
  • The names of the last three Oración Seis Galáctica and their silhouettes are revealed.
  • The Edens Zero Crew returns Creed's body to the Interstellar Union Army .
  • Justice and Victory learn of Creed's death, Justice vows to render justice on whoever killed Creed, which Jesse accuses Homura of doing so.
    • Jesse deletes Creed's history about his death in order to hide it from Justice and his organization.
  • Sister gives Shiki a new prosthetic eye.
  • Laguna Husert officially joins the Crew Of Edens.
  • Shiki declares his intentions to stop Ziggy and save the universe.
