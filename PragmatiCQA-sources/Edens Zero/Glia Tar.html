Description

Glia Tar is a black liquid capable of trapping people, who steps into it. It can easily be dispersed by gravity.

Usage

Belial Gore arc

World No. 30

Drakken Joe uses his Ether Gear Alchemist to convert the floor into Glia Tar, trapping the Crew of Edens , Amira and Sibir with it until Shiki unleashed his Ether Gear, Satan Gravity to free them all and surprising the Dark Alchemist.
