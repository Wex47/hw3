The Belial Gore arc is the eighth story arc of the Edens Zero series.

The Four Shining Stars have been assembled, which means the Crew of Edens can begin their journey beyond the Sakura Cosmos . However, they discover that the Edens Zero is being pursued by Drakken Joe 's giant space fortress: the Belial Gore . Itching for answers, Shiki leads a team into the fortress, but they are quickly caught in Drakken's trap as he advances on their ship. Now the crew must fight for their lives against the full might of the Dark Alchemist to protect their home—and Rebecca's new power may be their only hope for survival!
