Description

Norma is a brown planet with a ring system surrounding it. Its atmosphere is abundant with Earth Ether , which crystallizes into pillars and rains down onto the surface, embedding it in the ground. Because of this phenomenon, its residents live deep underground.

The adjectival form of Norma is "Norman".

Location

The planet is located in an unspecified part of the Sakura Cosmos .
