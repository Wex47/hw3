Magimech Attack: Meteor Breaker ( 魔械流 星 ( メテオ ) 崩し ( ブレイカー ) , Makai-ryu: Meteo Bureikā , lit. "Magic Machine Style: Meteor Breaker") is a Satan Gravity technique.

Description

The user charges Ether at the end of their fist to create a gravity sphere, which becomes a giant meteor of gravity when fired.

Usage

Shiki in his complete Overdrive first used this technique to defeat Drakken Joe in his Mutated Form in their fight.

In his complete Overdrive, Shiki uses this technique to defeat Orc in their fight.

Trivia

  • The attack's Japanese name shares its kanji spelling with the spell Sema in Fairy Tail .
