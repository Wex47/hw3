The Temple of the Mother Beacon ( 母なる標の神殿 , Haha naru Shirube no Shinden ) is a large central structure in an underwater city on the planet Red Cave .

Description

The Temple of the Mother Beacon is the largest building in the underwater city on the planet Red Cave. The temple is protected by a special barrier which prevents water from flooding in through the open entrances of the temple. It houses a large door that leads to the Mother Beacon, located pass a cave with odd stalagmite and flora, that can spontaneously combust into a roaring flame.

The Ether of this location prevents the flames in the caves from being crushed by Satan Gravity .
