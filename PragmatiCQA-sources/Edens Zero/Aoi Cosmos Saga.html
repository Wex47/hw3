The Aoi Cosmos Saga is the second saga in the series, Edens Zero . This saga is made up of four story arcs and consists of 64 chapters of the manga.

The saga starts off with the Crew of Edens finally breaking through Dragonfall , the border of the Sakura Cosmos . They find themselves in the Aoi Cosmos , and start making progress on their quest to find Mother . Meanwhile, Ziggy's influence had caused a robotic uprising on Foresta and beyond, causing a war between the Edens One and twin threats from the Interstellar Union Army, and Poseidon Shura, the prince of the Nero Empire and a more powerful user of Satan Gravity!
