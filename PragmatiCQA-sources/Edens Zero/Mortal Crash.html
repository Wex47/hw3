Mortal Crash ( モータルクラッシュ , Mōtaru Kurasshu ) is a Machina Maker technique.

Contents

  • 1 Description
  • 2 Usage
  • 4 Site Navigation

Description

The user touches the target up close with their palm, engulfing them with Ether markings, and detonates them with a fiery explosion, destroying them in the process.

Usage

After being carried over by Laguna Husert 's water , Weisz first uses the technique to obliterate the upper half of the O-Tech body of Müller , defeating the psychopathic scientist.

Weisz in his Arsenal Suit Mark II uses this technique to heavily damage Nasseh 's Sea Devil , bypassing the Ether coating of the Nero Empire 's Knight Gear .
