Metal Bogey ( メタルボギー , Metaru Bogī ) is a steel-skinned warrior from the planet Zorg , and a combatant in the Mildian Battle Coliseum .

Appearance

Bogey is a tall man with a muscular build and steel skin. His eyes appear completely black, lacking any discernible irises or pupils, and has a thin strip of hair and a pointy mustache. He wears a pair of dark wrestling underwear the waistband is lined with diamond studs and the kanji (金関) reading Kanaseki at the front.

Personality

Bogey is a man with good sportsmanship, politely greeting Shiki and hopes for a good battle. He is rather flashy, posing with confidence as he is introduced and cheered on by the Picoratta .

Synopsis

Mildian arc

On Mildian , Bogey is selected as the opponent for Shiki , posing as the crowd cheers him on. He courteously greets the young man and prepares for their match, telling him to do his worst. Almost immediately, Bogey is punched in the gut and faints before he even got the chance to fight Shiki and display his strength. He is then pulled away by a pair of Picoratta for the next match.

Powers and Abilities

Enhanced Durability : Because of his steel skin, Bogey can withstand numerous attacks and is described as being unbreakable by a Picoratta. Rebecca even notes how hard his skin appears. Even Xiaomei expected that it would take more than one hit from Shiki to bring him down.

Immense Battle Abilities : While not shown, Bogey possesses immense battle abilities according to Pino 's scan of him.
