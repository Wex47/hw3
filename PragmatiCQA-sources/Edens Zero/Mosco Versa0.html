Mosco Versa-0 ( モスコ＝ヴェルサー 零 ( レイ ) , Mosuko Verusā-Rei ) is a machine tasked with all the chores on board of the Edens Zero . He is also a former mercenary from the team Rogue Out .

Appearance

Mosco is a medium-sized, heavyset droid with two antennas at the top of his head, a large black egg-like object that looks like a hair bun, a triangle-shaped nose, and small facial features. When under the fake Sister's command, Mosco wore no shirt but a set of short sleeves, a thong-like set of pants with leggings, and was barefoot. His largest distinctive feature is a button where his belly button should be and a message above the button that says "Don't Push". After joining the Edens Zero crew, Mosco starts to wear a suit and tie.

On the trip to Mildian , Mosco changed his clothes into a tight-fitting white, 70's disco-style suit with a tie, and the forearms are decorated with golden tassels. He also sports an afro and a pair of dark, round sunglasses.

Personality

Mosco was programmed for service purposes and is shown to be extremely loyal to anyone he follows. After returning to Sister Ivry, Mosco shows a more gentle nature and is fiercely loyal to his mistress. He seems to be oblivious at times considering Sister's comments that would be considered insulting while he takes it as compliments. Despite this, Mosco does show emotions such as excitement, happiness, and showing he has a crush on Couchpo and sadness when he cried profusely after learning about the death of Valkyrie, and witnessing Witch's death. Mosco also has a habit of saying "Moscoy" at times.

Synopsis

History

At some point after the Four Shining Stars broke up, Sister Ivry took Mosco with her. But when she was kidnapped by Rogue Out, he was remodeled as one of Sister 's "soldiers".

Guilst arc

When Rebecca woke up aboard Rogue Out's spaceship, Mosco was one of the three knights, alongside Jinn and Ganoff, to announce themselves and take credit for kidnapping the 29 B-Cubers. Ganoff noted that Mosco kidnapped Couchpo and has a crush on her.

Upon arriving at Rogue Out HQ , Mosco was swiftly taken out by Million Bullets that was set up by Weisz earlier and crashed on the floor unconscious, riddled with bullets all over his body.

Angered, Mosco threatened to push the button on his belly only to immediately back out instead. An unamused Weisz tells everyone to forget about Mosco's antics which comically offends him.

Powers and Abilities

Expert Sumo Wrestler : Mosco is a skilled sumo wrestler, having the highest rank, Galactic Yokozuna.

  • Sumo Slap (つっぱり Tsuppari ): Mosco delivers fast strikes to his oppents with the palms of his hands.
  • Galactic Yokozuna Style (銀河横綱流 Ginga Yokozuna-ryū ):
    • Meteor Face Slap Shower (流星群張り手 Ryūseigun Harite ): Mosco charges towards his opponent while rapidly thrusting his arms. However, the full extent of this technique is unknown.
    • Gassho Moscoy' (合掌モスコイ Gasshō Mosukoi ): Mosco charges towards his opponent while raising his left hand and his right hand out in front of him. The full extent of this technique is unknown.

Flight : He is able to achieve flight by rapidly thrusting his arms in whatever direction he wishes to move. People who observe this ability question the physics behind it.

"Don't Push" Button : Mosco has a button where his belly button should be that says above it "Don't Push". While the effects of pressing the button are unknown, it is highly stressed by Sister that Mosco or anyone else doesn't push the button.
