"Sister" ( シスター , Shisutā ) was a machine who led the mercenary squad Rogue Out and was impersonating Sister Ivry , one of the Four Shining Stars .

Appearance

Sister is a curvaceous female android who dresses in a nun's habit with a large headdress, and a mechanical appendage that emerges from her back. The cheek flaps on her headdress conceal long slits next to her mouth that are capable of opening, revealing several sharp teeth. Underneath her nun's habit, her body is decorated with a pattern resembling lingerie and high heels.

Personality

Sister is a cold and pragmatic individual, resorting to attempting murder Illega when he refused to pay her for her job after Rogue Out kidnapped 29 out of 30 B-Cubers. She also uses her followers as meat shields, reasoning that she can heal them afterwards.

Sister was obsessed with Sister Ivry enough to worship her, even going as far as to steal her powers and impersonate her. She was also rather afraid of her as she cowered when Sister Ivry appeared before her, causing her to completely lose her normal composure.

Synopsis

History

In the year X482, the then-unnamed android woman discovered and captured Sister Ivry on the planet Guilst , using a device to transfer Sister's Heal Atomizer powers to herself in order to heal her injured comrades. Although she initially intended to return Sister's powers, rumors of her miraculous healing abilities caused her to attract a large following of worshipers; deciding to use Sister's powers she stole for profit, the android took on the Life of Edens' identity and formed the mercenary group Rogue Out , keeping the real Sister imprisoned beneath their headquarters so she could continue to siphon her powers for the next ten years.

Guilst arc

When Shiki , Weisz and Homura entered Rogue Out's headquarters, Sister appeared and greeted the party, asking what their motives are.

After her deception was exposed, Sister asked a livid Jinn to save her only to get stomped on. Afraid, the Pseudo-Sister tried pleading for mercy and claimed that, with the little bit of Sister Ivry's healing powers left, she could heal his friend Kleene , only for Jinn to correct her that Kleene is his sister and he has no friends before crushing the imposter's head into pieces.

Powers and Abilities

Cybernetic Arm : Sister has a large tentacle-like cybernetic arm sprouting from her back with a claw at the end. She has great strength with this arm as she was able to pick up Jinn and use him as a shield to block Shiki's gravity projectiles before they reached her.

Heal Atomizer (ヒールアトマイザー Hīru Atomaizā ): Ten years prior, Sister kidnapped Sister Ivry and attached her to a machine which drew her Healing Ether and giving her Sister Ivry's abilities to heal. Sister only had a bit of Sister Ivry's healing powers left after the latter was freed from the transferring device.
