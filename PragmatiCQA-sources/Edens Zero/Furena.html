Appearance

Furena is a young woman with long lighter hair, and she wears a dark sleeveless turtleneck, lighter pants, and a pair of sandals.

Personality

Synopsis

Sandra arc

Furena and her fellow rebels point their guns at Laguna and the Crew of Edens when they enter the Oasis base. However, she is shocked after Shiki easily disarmed her and and her comrades of their weapons with Satan Gravity .

When Goodwin expresses his fanboy excitement of meeting Rebecca and Happy of the Aoneko Channel in person, Furena and her comrades are shocked. She is stunned after hearing the Oasis leader is providing Rebecca with donations and calls her the best B-Cuber ever. Furena sweatdrops after Goodwin eagerly declares that he will do anything for Rebecca. She smiles when Homura states that she and the members of Oasis are strong.

Furena finds Shiki cute after watching the boy overreact to the Aoi News broadcast about the consequences of Shura 's public announcement across the Nero Empire . After Zoy gets shot in the shoulder by the pursuing Imperial Army troops who followed him to the rebel hideout, she calls him out in concern.

Furena charges out with her fellow rebels to fight the Imperial soldiers at the marketplace of the city. She continues firing her weapon at the enemy with her comrades in battle. When Oasis learns that there are enemy reinforcements on the west side of the city, Furena is concerned about her mother who lives in that area and asks Dylan what do they do. She is shocked after she and her fellow rebels hear Hermit contacting them and states that they are friends of Shiki too.

Furena stands silent as Goodwin asks Lizarre how many members of Oasis are killed with 16 dead , and later explains to the captured Imperial Army soldiers that it is just two sides raising the death count while refusing to give in.

Aoi War arc

Within the bridge of the Maine Coon , Furena waves silently in the background as Ibaraki tells Laguna that all members of Oasis are doing good. She and Dylan board their starfighters and launch out of Maine Coon's hanger bay, heading out first before engaging the Imperial Army ships in space.

During the battle with the Imperial Army nearby Nero 66 , Furena informs her boss that the Interstellar Union Army has spotted them. When Dylan comments that the Aoi Sea does feel awfully close, Furena states that it's almost high tide and this would be much more romantic normally. Smiling, she tells Dylan that she will watch it while fighting by his side and comments that it's not so bad either before an annoyed Ibaraki tells the two to stop flirting with each other.
