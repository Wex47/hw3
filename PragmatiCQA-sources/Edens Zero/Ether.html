Ether ( エーテル , Ēteru ) is the term coined and used to refer to all forms of natural energy. It is also known as Magic ( 魔力 , Maryoku ) in some circles.

Description

Ether is the source of all power, being described by some in "unscientific" terms as " magic "; it is utilized by human and alien beings, the atmosphere of planets, and even in machines . It can be manifested into physical objects such as energy bullets. Ether Gear users are capable of harnessing the power of their Ether by having it flow through their bodies in a specific configuration in a similar manner to a machine. At high enough concentrations, Ether can also form solid matter known as Lacrima .

Known Types

  • Air Ether ( エアエーテル , Ea Ēteru ) : A type of Ether revolving the element of air. Users of Element Ether are able to use this type to enhance their jumps.
  • Demon King ( 魔王 , Maō ) : The identification code of the Ether utilised by users of Satan Gravity , an Ether Gear revolving around gravity.
  • Water Ether ( 水のエーテル , Mizu no Ēteru ) : This revolves around the element of water. Blue Garden is a planet that is abundant in this type, as well as Red Cave when its treasure was stolen.
  • Earth Ether ( 土のエーテル , Tsuchi no Ēteru ) : This Ether revolves around the element of earth. Norma 's atmosphere is rich in this type, where it crystallizes into pillars from the sky and embeds into the planet.
  • Wood Ether ( 木のエーテル , Ki no Ēteru ) : This is an Ether that revolves around wood and trees. Guilst is rich with this type, shown in the form of its Mechdrasil .
  • Fire Ether ( ファイアエーテル , Faia Ēteru ) : A type of Ether revolving the element of fire. This type is utilized in several abilities, such as Flame Bolt and Flame Ammo .
  • Healing Ether ( 治癒のエーテル , Chiyu no Ēteru ) : This is a type of Ether that is utilized healing. The ability Heal Atomizer is a prominent example of its usage.
  • Shield Ether ( シールドエーテル , Shīrudo Ēteru ) : Ether with defensive properties. A prominent example of this are Protection Matrixes or Ether Shields .
  • Ice Ether ( アイスエーテル , Aisu Ēteru ) : A type of Ether that has properties of ice. This is utilized to create ice crystals or freeze a target by users of Element Ether.
  • Lightning Ether ( ライトニングエーテル , Raitoningu Ēteru ) : Also called Thunder Ether ( サンダーエーテル , Sandā Ēteru ) , this Ether has the properties of lightning. This is utilized by Element Ether users to shock their opponents.
  • Light Ether ( 光のエーテル , Hikari no Ēteru ) : Ether revolving around light. When fused with Water Ether, they can create a sparkling effect like rain.
  • Mother Ether ( マザーエーテル , Mazā Ēteru ) : Ether imbued in objects that were on Mother in the past. This acts as a compass to find other Relics, where eventually it could take them to Mother herself.
  • Psychokinetic Ether ( 念動系エーテル , Nentōkei Ēteru ) : Ether with psychokinetic properties. Examples that use this are the cages from Ether Lock .
  • Anti-Dragon Ether ( 竜特効エーテル , Ryūtokkō Ēteru ) : A type of Ether that is specialized to damage Dragons . According to Holy , it took years for the Interstellar Union Army to develop this type. It has also been called DS Ether ( ＤＳエーテル , DS Ēteru ) .
  • Trap Ether ( 設置型エーテル , Setchigata Ēteru ) : Ether with properties surrounding traps, where it is stationed in a spot and activated by the user.
  • Drain Ether ( ドレインエーテル , Dorein Ēteru ) : A type of Ether that siphons the Ether of another being.
