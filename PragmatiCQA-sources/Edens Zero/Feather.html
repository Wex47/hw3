Feather ( フェザー , Fezā ) is a Commander of the Interstellar Union Army and one of the Oración Seis Interstellar .

Appearance

Feather is a curvaceous young woman with long wavy white hair and wears a blue and gray skintight outfit with feather themed headdress, a long cape draped on her shoulders and long legged boots. She normally keeps her eyes closed. When opened, they display a segmented plus-shape.

When activating her Ether Connect or occasionally her Ether Gear, Ether lines appear over her eyes.

Personality

Feather is a reserved woman who rarely speaks, keeping quiet. When she does speak during a Prayer Council, she does so to address important things. Even in battle, she remains calm and collected, not letting anything distract her. Feather is very confident, believing that nothing can get past through both her fleet and her Ether Gear to outrun her pursuit.

Synopsis

Red Cave arc

Feather attends a Prayer Council via transmission with her fellow Oración Seis Interstellar members albeit in silence as they discuss about Elsie Crimson having arrived in the Aoi Cosmos where the Armored Space Pirate's fellow Oración Seis Galáctica member, Poseidon Nero , resides and as Justice immediately volunteers to go after her. Afterward, they pray and end their Prayer Council.

Aoi War arc

In the aftermath of the Aoi War, Feather attends a Prayer Council, opening her eyes and states that now there are four Galáctica members left, surprising Holy . She notes that Elsie is not a significant threat to them at this point, much to Justice's ire. Afterward, they pray and end their Prayer Council.

X495 arc

Feather leads her forces to Blue Garden after locating Shiki and his crew with her Ether Gear while her ships surround the planet. With her troops outside the Shooting Starlight guildhall, she informs them that their targets will emerge from the building soon, ordering to shoot on sight. Using her Ether Gear, she sees them emerging together.

Waiting for her targets to leave the building, Feather silently ignores the nearby civilians gossiping about her. When Shiki's group appears before them, she orders her forces to open fire and reassures that their bullets will not hit anyone, as she can see it. She states to her troops that she was right as the ballistics were a diversion and orders them to keep firing while she uses Ether Connect to Team Alpha . Raising her right hand, she tells them to seize the crew.

After her Connect Team is defeated, Feather is about to resort to using something unspecified but see Shiki appearing right in front of her. She is shocked that he is not in a location she foresaw, noting how there has never been such a large discrepancy before. Feather is patted on the shoulder by Shiki who bids her farewell and leave with his crew. She tries to go after them herself but realizes that she is stuck after Shiki's gravity pinned her to the ground. Watching them leave, she states that she will not let them get away as her ships has the Edens Zero surrounded.

After her armada fails to follow the Edens Zero through a debris field, Feather pilots her starfighter and pursues them at full speed. When the Edens Zero fires at the asteroids to create a cloud of debris in front of her, she states that their attempt will not work, as her Eye of Venus tells her exactly where they are. However, she realizes too late that they are trying to obstruct her path, while evading the asteroids with her starfighter. After the Edens Zero escapes via fast travel, Feather is contacted by Holy who comments that the Edens Zero crew got away and is told to trade places, confusing her. Upon hearing that Shiki's group is going to Lendard , Feather asks where did Holy get this information.

Kaede War arc

After Holy informed her of having an alliance with two Galáctica members Shiki and Elsie , Feather relays this information to Justice who is already at the Kaede Cosmos and shows up on Lendard. The commander also gives her report to Cure and Eraser about the presence of so many Galáctica members in one place before she and her two comrades are on their way to Lendard.

Upon arrival with her fleet of Racer ships, Feather remarks that if they don't shoot, they will be shot at after learning from Victory about Justice's orders to treat Elsie's space pirates and the Edens Zero as allies.

Powers and Abilities

Feather is a member of the Oración Seis Interstellar , a group of six warriors who exist to fight the Oración Seis Galáctica , and in a class of her own even within the Cosmic Government.

Eye of Venus (ヴィーナス・アイ Vīnasu Ai ): Feather's Ether Gear allows her to observe the locations of people similar like the Eye of God . She uses this Ether Gear to predict her enemy's movements in battle. Her Ether marks manifest on her face.

Ether Connect ( エーテルコネクト , Ēteru Konekuto ) : She is capable of temporarily connecting her Ether to her soldiers, letting them use her Ether Gear. When active, Ether marks appear on their faces and they gain glowing auras.

Piloting Skills : Feather has displayed the ability to pilot a starfighter, easily maneuvering through the asteroid field and dodging attacks in a fast pursuit.

Equipment

Racer (レーサー Rēsā ): Feather's spaceship in the Interstellar Union Army, a large commando carrier and it is described to be the fastest ship and a fleet destroyer.

Starfighter : Feather pilots a small starfighter with large angelic wings that she uses it in conjunction with her Eye of Venus via Ether Link in battle and its speed is fast enough to catch up with the Edens Zero piloted by Connor .
