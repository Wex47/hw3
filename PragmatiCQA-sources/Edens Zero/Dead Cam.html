History

Sandra arc

After Poseidon Shura 's public announcement across the Nero Empire , Rickard angrily fumes on live tv that the people have lost all faith in the android manufacturing industry. The CEO of Dead Cam declares that this is utterly deplorable and they cannot allow the Nero Empire to get away with this kind of despotism.

Aoi War arc

Rickard joins forces with Jaguar of the Oración Seis Interestelar where Dead Cam and the Interstellar Union Army attacked Nero 1 as they engaged the Imperial Army ships protecting the Nero Empire's capital planet and landed their troops on the ground for an invasion of The Temple where Poseidon Nero takes refuge at the Deep Snow Imperial palace. When news of Nero's death and Nero 66's destruction spreads among the demoralized Imperial soldiers. Rickard declares that they have won the war and orders his bots to not shoot anyone who lays down their weapons.

At the end of the Aoi War, the Dead Cam androids accompany Rickard to Foresta where the CEO manages to stop the escalating conflict between humans and bots as their mediator, and gradually restore the peace and harmony.
