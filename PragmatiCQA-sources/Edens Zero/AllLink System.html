The All-Link System ( オールリンクシステム , Ōru Rinku Shisutemu ) is a system that commands all of the bots in the Aoi Cosmos and one of the Nero Empire 's weapons.

Description

The All-Link System is a network that connects all networks in the Aoi Cosmos as Ether and electricity combine inside the great sea. It activates when the ocean of the Aoi Cosmos hits high tide within 3 days and engulfs every planet protected by Ether-filled atmospheres in the cosmos.

It is located within the domed city of Nero 66 where it is protected by several Imperial Army ships.
