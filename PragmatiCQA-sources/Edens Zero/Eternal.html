Description

The city is very large, with numerous buildings of varying shapes and sizes, and floating objects with glowing signs circling around them. Many of the buildings here have a predominantly blue and white color scheme and have dome-shaped roofs. At the front of the city is a sign welcoming travelers and visitors to Blue Garden. Above the city is are long "streams" of floating Water Ether , that occasionally form into bubbles.

Location

Eternal is located in an unspecified part of Blue Garden, surrounded by a few mountains and an unspecified body of water nearby.
