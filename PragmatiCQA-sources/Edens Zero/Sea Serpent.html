The Sea Serpent ( シーサーペント , Shī Sāpento ) was a warship of the Nero Empire 's Imperial Army, being the most powerful weapon for the Temple .

Description

The Sea Serpent is a titanic ship resembling a serpent with fins that dwarfs the Imperial Army ships such as the Kraken and the Megalodon . It can fire a concentrated massive laser beam from its mouth cannon that is capable of annihilating enemy ships in its path at once in multiple directions and dissolves them near it.
