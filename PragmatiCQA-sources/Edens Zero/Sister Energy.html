Sister Energy (シスターエナジー Shisutā Enajī ) is a medicine made from Sister Ivry 's Healing Ether .

Description

It can be considered to be Sister's Ether in a medicinal form and can be used as an effective restorative. It is packaged as an energy drink and works on both humans and machines .
