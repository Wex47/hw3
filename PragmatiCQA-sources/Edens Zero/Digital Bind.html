Digital Binds (デジタルバインド Dejitaru Baindo ) are a type of restraint used by the mercenary group Rogue Out on their captives.

Contents

  • 1 Description
  • 2 Usage
  • 4 Site Navigation

Description

The restraints fully immobilize the victims arms, and can be controlled by an app, allowing the user to torture anyone imprisoned by the Digital Bind at will though repeated usage will cause lasting damage.

Usage

They were used by Ganoff and his colleagues in large numbers to restrain all of the B-Cubers they had kidnapped including Rebecca en route to Guilst .
