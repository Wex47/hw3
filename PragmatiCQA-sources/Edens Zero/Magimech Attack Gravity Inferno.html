Magimech Attack: Gravity Inferno ( 魔械流 重烈火 , Makai-ryū: Jūretsuka , lit. "Magic Machine Style: Heavy Fire") is a Satan Gravity technique.

Description

The user launches multiple blasts of gravity at the opponent, striking them all at once.

Usage

Ziggy first used this technique against Elsie during their fight on planet Lendard .
