Kraken ( クラーケン級 , Kurāken-kyū ) is a class of ships and one of the capital ships of the Nero Empire 's Imperial Army.

Description

Krakens are large spaceships resembling giant squids that dwarf the Imperial Army ships nearby and can fire several powerful high-energy laser beams from the tips of their tentacles that are capable of annihilating 42 Interstellar Union Army ships at once in multiple directions.
