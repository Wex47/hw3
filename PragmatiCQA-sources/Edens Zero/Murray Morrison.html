Murray Morison ( マリー・モリソン , Marī Morison ) is a former debtor of Drakken Joe .

Appearance

Murray is a man with an average build and dark hair styled in a bowl cut with the back hair shaved off. During his "meeting" with Drakken, he was only in his boxers, which have a heart pattern. Afterwards he loses several teeth after Drakken smashes the bottle that was in his mouth.

Personality

Murray's monetary sense is extremely poor, as he decided to get a loan of 2,000,000 Glee from Drakken, an infamous loan shark.

Synopsis

Digitalis arc

Murray is tied, gagged, and left in his boxers during his "meeting" with Drakken, when Spider comes in and interrupts it. As it is the final day, he promises to pay back the 2,000,000 Glee with the interest of 3,000,000 Glee. He nods in agreement when Drakken says that it is common sense to return what you borrowed. Murray is tormented when Drakken turns the bottle into Dramour Ore with his Ether Gear , Alchemist and then shatters it, disfiguring his mouth as he screams in pain. After Drakken gives off several ways he could have him work his debt off, Murray is sent away by the Dark Alchemist's assistant.
