History

X495 arc

Feather's forces, including the Connect Team, block the entrance of Shooting Starlight guildhall after locating the Edens Zero Crew on Blue Garden . They wait for their target, Shiki Granbell of the Oración Seis Galáctica , while Agie warns them to not harm the civilians. After being notified by Sam, the commander tells her troops that their targets will emerge from the building soon and orders them to shoot the Crew of Edens on sight.

When Shiki exits the Shooting Starlight with his crew, the other soldiers open fire as a diversion, allowing Feather to connect her Ether with Team Alpha, allowing them to use her Ether Gear and are ordered to seize the crew. However, they are easily taken out in droves. A soldier confidently sees Weisz 's next move but he is then quickly knocked down by the latter's mechanical gauntlet instead, surprising the other troops. Several more soldiers are flattened on the ground by Shiki who dive bombs from above with Satan Gravity . Regardless, they press on the attack against their targets. Several soldiers are assaulted by Happy 's Blaster Solo Mode . More Connect Team soldiers are pushed back by Rebecca using Overdrive with one comically calling her a monster cat. Sam and Agie are swiftly cut down by Homura's Soul Blade , shocking the other Connect Team members.
