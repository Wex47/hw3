Clown ( クラウン , Kuraun ) also known as the Death of Edens ( エデンズの死 , Edenzu no Shi ) , is a machine and one of the Demon King's Four Dark Stars .

Appearance

Personality

Clown is a flamboyant individual, speaking in a casual manner as he tries to convince Connor to shoot down the Edens Zero and reminds the captain that he should be a professional and do what his creator Ziggy tells him to do.

Synopsis

Edens One arc

After a revived Ziggy , boards the Edens One and leaves Granbell , he introduces the Four Dark Stars to the Crew of Edens where Clown stands alongside his fellow Dark Stars. He agrees with Wizard 's statement that they are meant to replace their predecessors and comments that the Four Shining Stars are cute. When Connor hesitates on attacking the Edens Zero by Ziggy's orders, Clown playfully reminds the man that they are only paying him for his piloting skills and that he should be doing his job as a professional.

Foresta arc

At the bridge of Edens One where Ziggy learns that the machine rebellion on Foresta was stopped by the Crew of Edens, Clown asks the Demon King if his resolve to fight against his old family has been shaken. Clown listens to Ziggy who explains to the Dark Stars about his lost memories, his quest to find Mother , and his decision to raise Shiki as his grandson.

X495 arc

On Lendard , Clown muses that it is a glorious sight as more robots for the Skeleton Army are created every minute. stands next to the other Dark Stars as Ziggy informs them that Shiki is coming and that they will stay here and fight him.

Kaede War arc

Powers and Abilities

Clown is built as part of the Demon King's Four Dark Stars ; a group of machines designed to be more advanced than the Demon King's Four Shining Stars .

Machine Physiology : As a machine , Clown should be able to survive in the vacuum of space unprotected with no negative effects like his fellow machine, Wizard .

  • Ether Bullets : Clown can fire a spray of Ether bullets from a gun built into his right arm.

Environment Manipulation : During his battle against Rebecca within Killer's subdimension program, Clown is able to manipulate the circus environment they are in. He usually snaps his fingers to do so, such as making the tightrope snap apart, activate the stage lights, and making rings of fire from the ground or from his hands. He is even capable of making electricity run through the stage.

Battle Dress (バトルドレス Batoru Doresu ): As one of the counterparts to the Four Shining Stars, Clown possesses the ability to requip into a different form of battle suit.

  • Nightmare Clown (ナイトメアクラウン Naitomea Kuraun ): Clown's Battle Dress gives him the appearance of a menacing, dark-skinned clown.

Master Acrobatic : Clown has shown to be able to perform acrobatic feats from high above the ground, such as dancing on a tightrope perfectly fine, and landing safely on the ground.

Expert Juggling : Fitting with his circus motif, Clown has shown impressive juggling skills, tossing several swords in a circle at once and even being capable of throwing them and grabbing more at his target at the same time.

Anti-EMP Coating : Clown, like all members of the Four Dark Stars , is equipped with a defensive coating that protects him from an Electromagnetic Pulse .

Overclock : A power up ability, the Dark Stars need permission in order to use it.
