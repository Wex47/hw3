Description

Glue Tear is capable of producing an adhesive substance from the user's limbs. The user can fire this adhesive substance to glue their opponents down and trap them. The user is also able to turn part of their body into the same substance. They are also able to produce hot glue which has the property of burning their opponent.

It is immune to an Electromagnetic Pulse as the adhesive is not electronic in nature.
