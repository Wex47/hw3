The Galactic Intelligence Agency (銀河系諜報機関 Gingakei Chōhō Kikan )—called the G.I.A. ( Ｇ ( ジー ) Ｉ ( アイ ) Ａ ( エー ) Jī Ai Ē ) for short—is an intelligence agency of the Cosmic Government.

Overview

Agents of the G.I.A. specialize in espionage and intelligence gathering, sending moles to infiltrate dangerous criminal organizations and groups with the intent of having them dismantled from the inside and brought to justice, or use them as a means to an end at the expense of others.

Members

  • Noah Glenfield : The Director of the Sakura Cosmos division of the G.I.A.. He works undercover as the Guild Master of Shooting Starlight .
  • Amira : A female agent with the ability Mirror Face , who worked on bringing down Drakken Joe by whatever means necessary.
  • Jesse : An agent who worked undercover as a member of Elsie Crimson 's space pirate crew . He was eventually caught and excommunicated by Elsie who sent him back to the pursuing Interstellar Union Army in the Aoi Cosmos .
