Sweeds ( スイーズ , Suīzu ) is a planet located in the Kaede Cosmos .

Description

The surface of Sweeds consists of a crowded city with atleast one long river. The planet's most notable feature is the waterfalls coming down from the clouds. It has a sizeable robot population that Ziggy used to start an uprising like he did on Foresta .

History

At an unspecified point Ziggy came and started a robot uprising by brainwashing the robots of the planet. The Crew of Edens visits the planet to stop this and destroy the three cores while defeating the Skeleton Army guarding them, returning the bots back to normal.

Location

The planet is located in an unspecified part of the Kaede Cosmos.
