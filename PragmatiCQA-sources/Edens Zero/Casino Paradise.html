Description

Casino Paradise has many of the games typically found in a casino, like Slot Machines, Blackjack and Roulette. It is described as an exclusive VIP casino by Rebecca . According to the tourist information website StarNavigation.com, Casino Paradise is the place where all the information ends up.

Location

Casino Paradise is located in an unknown location in the city Gold Palace on Sun Jewel.
