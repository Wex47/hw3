Milon ( ミロン , Miron ) is a B-Cuber who was abducted together with Rebecca Bluegarden . She runs Tokkō TV, a B-Cube channel that produces fully scripted suspense videos where she enters into dangerous situations and escape them.

Appearance

Milon is a young woman with short hair. She has an elaborate tattoo on her left arm. She normally wears skimpy, revealing clothing that leaves her arms, legs and midriff exposed.

During her escape from Illega Tower she donned a costume of a Jiangshi including a paper talisman which she wore over her face.

Synopsis

Guilst arc

Milon is one of several B-Cubers who has been kidnapped by Rogue Out and transported on a spaceship heading towards the planet Guilst .

After arriving on Guilst and taken to Illega Tower in a holding chamber, Milon and the B-Cubers meet Illega who proclaims them that they are his property now, and demonstrated a helpless naked girl who is turned into a stone statue with the Petrification Ray which sent Milon and the B-Cuber into fear and panic. Then their clothes and accessories were melted away by the bubbles.

When Rebecca devised a plan to escape, Milon helped by forming a human pyramid for Rebecca to reach the glass window and broke through it. After being freed, Milon and her fellow B-Cubers wore costumes they found nearby as new clothes to wear.
