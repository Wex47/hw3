Antares ( 天蠍槍 ( アンタレス ) , Antaresu ) is a Star Drain technique.

  • 5 Site Navigation

Description

The user summons a spear consisting of a dark-colored shaft and a cross-shaped head with four diamonds adorning it, having three blades made from Ether jutting out. On the other end is a similar diamond with a jewel embedded in it. Justice describes it as a "spear of light to pierce evil."

Usage

Justice first uses this technique in an attempt to execute a heavily injured Shiki on the ground, but Elsie Crimson intervenes with her own Antares and saves the boy's life in time.

Trivia

  • Antares ( Alpha Scorpii ) is the brightest star in the constellation Scorpius and the 16th brightest star in the night sky.
