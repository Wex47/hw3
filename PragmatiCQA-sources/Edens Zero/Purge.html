Purge ( 浄化 , Jōka ) is an ability used by Witch Regret .

Contents

  • 1 Description
  • 2 Usage
  • 4 Site Navigation

Description

The user collects a ball of energy between their palms and expands it to clean the interior of a ship.

Usage

Witch Regret first used this technique to purge the Edens Zero of its Kawpicatt infestation before restoring it to its original form.
