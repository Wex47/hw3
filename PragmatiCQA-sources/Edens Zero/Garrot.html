Garrot ( ギャレット , Gyaretto ) is the warden of Sun Jewel 's Labor District and a member of the Zaiten Three , nicknamed Benzaiten ( 鞭罪天 , Benzaiten , lit. "Whip Sin Deva") , the "Scourge of Heaven".

Appearance

Personality

Garrot is a sadistic and cruel man as he enjoys watching the suffering of others and inflicting pain on them by himself in the Labor District.

Synopsis

Sun Jewel arc

Garrot is ordered to squelch the uprising in the Labor District, which he happily obliges.

Powers and Abilities

Whip Proficiency : Garrot is shown to be highly skilled at wielding a whip.

Equipment

Paralytic Whip : Garrot's main weapon is a paralytic whip that he keeps by his left side. Garrot is able to control the direction the whip travels. The whip is capable of paralyzing victims that come into contact with it, causing their bodies to go numb. The whip delivers two kinds of numbing drugs in contact with someone.

  • Fast-Acting Drugs : These numbing agents take effect almost immediately after contact with someone.
  • Slow-Acting Drugs : These numbing agents take much longer to go into effect.
