Universe 2 is the second universe. Rebecca Bluegarden transported herself here from Universe 1 , making this world one where the crew of Edens Zero defeat Drakken Joe and his organization , however they all die as a result of Nero 66 's detonation.

World No. 30 is the only new world that came about in Universe 2. No. 30 is the first world in which Rebecca Bluegarden has retained her memories of the previous world.

Universe 2 first appears in Chapter 85 but was replaced in the story with Universe 3 following Shiki "making the wrong choice" by choosing not to sacrifice himself in Chapter 165 .

Differences from Universe 1

The Edens One was never destroyed from a horrible accident, as such Captain Connor never met the Crew of Edens . A debris field was not present on the Edens Zero's trip to Sun Jewel and thus the ship arrived in a day.

The Crew of Edens' battle with Drakken Joe's faction on board the Belial Gore went much differently thanks to Rebecca retaining memories of the previous world upon arrival and warned her friends of their enemies' capabilities.

Additionally, Labilia Christy was horrificly tortured for days while imprisoned on the Belial Gore in Universe 1, but Amira (who was disguised as her in Universe 2) was not and Laibilia herself was hidden safely elsewhere. Jinn also notably wore a different outfit from his Universe 1 self. The members of Drakken's Organization also knew Rebecca as No. 30 instead of No. 29.
