Description

These goggles allow the user to see through clothing, rendering the subject of their vision to be nude in their eyes.

Usage

After picking them up in Weisz's House , Shiki uses them to see through Happy 's synthetic cat exterior and see his mechanical skeleton of a Machine , and use them to see through Rebecca 's clothing and ogle her naked body.
