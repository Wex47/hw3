Holy ( ホーリィ , Hōrii ) is a Commander of the Interstellar Union Army , a leader of her own team of soldiers , and one of the Oración Seis Interstellar .

Appearance

Holy is a slender, well-endowed young woman with purple eyes and matching long, wavy hair that covers her left eye. She wears a black and red dress that has a cleavage window, a headdress shaped like horns, and a long slit skirt exposing her left leg. She also has a mark of the Interstellar Union Army on the front of her neck.

When meeting Shiki's crew at Ryuzetsu Land , she is seen wearing a revealing elegant dark-colored swimsuit with two pieces of cloth flowing at the back and another wrapped around her right leg.

Personality

Holy is a flirtatious woman, as she lamented to not have taken down "little Drakky" and teases "little Justice" to be a stickler for rules and being in love even though that went straight over his head, feeling a bit jealous of "precious little Elsie". Holy is also an outspoken woman who sees the big picture as while she doesn't mind Justice's pursuit of Elsie, she reminds him to take the bigger threat of Nero and his son Shura seriously, and advises her fellow Interstellar member to let Jaguar and Eraser help him instead of doing it alone.

Holy is shown to be very secretive and places personal matters on a higher priority, being busy with another agenda, making her unable to help during the Aoi War. When she met up with Shiki and his crew, she puts on a motherly and kind image in order to win them over.

Synopsis

Red Cave arc

Holy attends a Prayer Council via transmission with her fellow Oración Seis Interstellar members where she remarks that Justice is a stickler for rules and laments that someone had defeated Drakken Joe before they got the chance. When Justice immediately volunteers to pursue and apprehend Elsie Crimson who arrived at the Aoi Cosmos where her fellow Oración Seis Galáctica member, Poseidon Nero resides, Holy teases him for being in love with the space pirate which flies over his head. Afterward, they pray and end their Prayer Council.

Foresta arc

After Elsie withdraws from her battle with Justice , Holy contacts her fellow Interstellar member via a holographic screen transmission, commenting that the Armored Space Pirate is tough. She expects nothing less from the youngest member of the Oración Seis Galáctica, afraid to see what the young woman will be capable of within 5 or 10 years from now. Holy tells an injured Justice that she does not mind if he wants to chase after his ex-girlfriend but then reminds him to keep Poseidon Nero in mind. She believes that the Emperor is bound to make some sort of move and he will want revenge on Ziggy for what he did to Foresta .

Contacting a recuperating Justice from a luxury bar somewhere, Holy informs her fellow Interstellar member that Nero's top officials, the Oceans 6 have assembled on Planet Nero 1 : The Temple including the Emperor's son, Shura whom she calls him "a twisted deadbeat". She chides an unconcerned Justice that he should care because Shura could be a real threat. Holy tells the soldier that Nero is old and once he dies, Shura will take over the Nero Empire . She believes that now it is their chance to destroy Shura and if they fail, the Aoi Cosmos will suffer for it later. Holy remarks that with men like Shura, there is no telling what they will do once they get real power. In any case, the female Interstellar member comments that there are far too many big fish in the Aoi Cosmos and tells Justice that he won't be able to handle them on his own. Holy informs the soldier that Jaguar and Eraser are on their way and advises the soldier to let them help him. Smiling impishly, she answers Justice's question that she has other fish to fry.

Aoi War arc

In the aftermath of the Aoi War, Holy attends a Prayer Council where she solemnly learns that Jaguar and Creed are dead along with thousands of Interstellar Union Army troops. She is surprised when Feather speaks out that there are four Galáctica members left, musing how rare it is for her to talk and that Eraser is staying silent. Holy is told by Eraser that he just returned from the battle after so many lives were wiped out, which she makes an "erased" pun. When Eraser lists the other three Galáctica members being a bigger problem than Elsie, Holy smiles after Deadend Crow is brought up. Afterward, they pray and end their Prayer Council.

X495 arc

Relaxing in her bathtub, Holy contacts Feather and remarks about the Crew of Edens ' recent escape from her fellow female Interstellar member's pursuit. She tells the commander to trade places with her, and reveals that Shiki and his friends are heading to Lendard in the Kaede Cosmos . Holy declares that it is her turn for she will launch an "immaculate military operation".

Arriving on Dahlia , Holy in her bikini shows up to tell the group of thugs that it is enough, warning them that they could get arrested if they start a fight in a public place like Ryuzetsu Land . She introduces herself as one of the Oración Seis Interstellar and declares that she will arrest them if they don't cease their foolishness, scaring the thugs off. Facing an apprehensive Shiki and Rebecca , the commander simply reassures the two that she is not their enemy. Reaching out her hand, Holy asks Shiki if he will join forces with her and states that they can be friends. When Shiki and Rebecca express their doubts about it, Holy implores them to at least hear her out, stating that she shares their goal of defeating Ziggy. However, she remarks that they can't talk about it in the open with civilians around. Leaving, the female Interstellar member tells Shiki to meet with her on the rooftop lounge in 1 hour.

Taking a dip in the swimming pool, Holy emerges to notice that Shiki and his friends have arrived early and greet them, exiting the pool. While drying herself up with a towel, she tells them to make themselves comfortable. Cooking food on the grill, Holy apologizes for the delayed meal as she never expected them to be early than intended. The commander then informs Shiki and his friends to make one thing very clear and reveals that she was not in favor of Shiki being named as one of the Galáctica and she was not opposed either. She states that she acknowledges Shiki's strength and his powers are on the same level with Elsie and the rest of the Galáctica. Holy declares that if they are to defeat Ziggy and the Oración Seis Galáctica, working together would be the most efficient. Answering Homura 's question, she explains that the Oración Seis Interstellar are a special team organized specifically to defeat the Oración Seis Galáctica and they have no leader, authorized to act on their own individual judgment. Finished cooking the meal, Holy summarizes that she and her comrades are free to team up with whoever they wish. She appeals with Shiki to not believe everyone in the Cosmic Government to be evil as she doesn't believe him to be evil either. Smiling warmly, Holy allows them to help themselves with the meal on the table. Taking a seat, she decides that it is time to discuss the main order of business and asks Pino if the rest of Edens Zero's crew are listening through her much to their surprise that she is aware of their presence. Holy explains that her ultimate goal is to defeat Ziggy but she has another objective; to track down Deadend Crow and kill him with her own hands. When Shiki objected killing Deadend Crow, she apologizes for being so blunt and settles for simply arresting him instead, remarking that he is sure to get a death penalty anyway. Holy reveals to Shiki and his friends that Deadend Crow is at Lendard and he has joined forces with Ziggy, including the fact that he is a machine. She informs them that Deadend Crow was considered as the most dangerous machine before Ziggy's reawakening and the one responsible behind the "Bloody Atmos Day Incident", which resulted to 1 million casualties. Holy states to an unnerved Rebecca that it was a public story behind a terrible truth and that someone she knew got caught in it. She describes Deadend Crow to be a monster who sees humans as his toys and it is enough reason for him to join Ziggy. The female Interstellar member proposes that she will help the Crew of Edens defeat Ziggy in exchange for helping her defeat Deadend Crow. Accepting Shiki's consideration to talk with his crew about the offer, Holy reminds him that she will not see him as an enemy depending on his answer and it also extends to Elsie too as well. Bidding Shiki farewell, she states that she is looking forward for his response.

Dispatching the group of thugs with her Ether Gear from afar while taking a shower, Holy wonders if her "spell" is working its magic yet and declares that all the filth will be melt away. Smiling ominously, she repeats her previous statement and states that until she has her answer from Shiki, she will go ahead and use his Satan Gravity for herself.

Returning to the Midnight in space, Holy is contacted by Shiki and asks him about his decision. She learns that Shiki accepts her offer with one condition; she will stay aboard the Edens Zero until the battle in Lendard is over. Realizing that the Edens Zero crew intend to have her under their watch, Holy accepts Shiki's terms and states that she is looking forward to work with him.

Boarding the Edens Zero, Holy remarks about the ship's interior before Hermit directs her to her assigned room. She complies with Hermit after learning that she is free to come and go wherever she likes except the main control room or any of the major facilities of the Edens Zero. The female Interstellar member states to a skeptical android that she is being monitored by the Crew of Edens anyway before teasing Weisz to make him fluster. She reassures Hermit that she would never betray their captain at least for now. Entering the Edens Kitchen's mess hall, Holy remarks that something smells good as she does enjoy cooking herself. She informs Kleene Rutherford that she has done her homework on the crew's backgrounds. Holy is delighted when she is brought to the Dress Factory by Pino and finds the small android's change of clothes into a fluffy bunny cute. Taking Weisz's excited suggestion, Holy changes her clothes from that of a playboy bunny, a maid and a kimono and muses that this is fun. She comments that while she has done research on Shiki's crew, she finds none on Pino. After learning from Hermit that Pino is an anti-bot android , the female Interstellar member states that Pino is exactly what they are going to need in the upcoming battle and smiles at the little bot's resolve. At the Spa of Edens, Holy in a towel is amazed and remarks at how refined the place is to have a hot soak while gazing at the skies of Kaede Cosmos. Unfurling her towel to bathe, she learns that Rebecca and her friends are from the Sakura Cosmos and reveals that she is from the Yukino Cosmos . Holy asks the girls if they know what the Four Cosmoses are called together. She explains that they have another name too, stating the meaning behind the four cosmoses' names and then reveals them to be the "Grand Shiki Cosmos", wondering if it is a coincidence with the Demon King's grandson. Finishing her bath and continuing her tour of the Demon King's warship, Holy stands in front of the Infirmary Room's door as Hermit explains to her that it's Sister 's room.

At the Briefing Room, Holy attends the meeting where she playfully scolds Shiki for getting greedy with his conditions but hears him out anyway. When Shiki asks her to take care of Justice, she tells him that she can't do that, reminding him that the Oración Seis Interstellar aren't authorized to give each other orders. But on the other hand, Holy states that Justice can't stop her from joining forces with Shiki and Elsie. The commander retorts to Shiki that attempting to ask the Celestial Judge to stay out of the way will backfire as soon as he knows Elsie is involved and comes running. Noticing Connor, Holy calls him out and notes that he is a fugitive from Lendard, interrogating the captain. She is confused that Connor was working on an all-android planet and learns from him that Ziggy hired human professionals from all kinds of fields to have their knowledge and skills scanned for his growing army of robots much to her disgust. Holy asks the captain if he saw Ziggy which she hears that Connor flew past a giant bot that he assumed it was the Demon King's new weapon during his escape from Lendard. Glaring, she informs Connor that it was Deadend Crow he encountered and that his epithet is the "Titan of Eternal Darkness". Holy reveals to the Crew of Edens that Deadend Crow is 420 meters tall and describes him to be "a living fortress".

When a displeased Elsie expresses her suspicion towards her, Holy tells the Galáctica member to forget about Justice and points out that Ziggy has a powerful army, stating that their enemy have the numerical advantage even if Elsie's space pirate fleet and the Edens Zero join forces. With a cheeky face, she offers to add her Interstellar Union Army fleet to increase their chances of winning. As Hermit explains the strategy to break through Lendard's defense zone, Holy chimes in that she will give orders to her Midnight ships from the Edens Zero. Watching Elsie's shocked expression at Deadend Crow being on Ziggy's side with vindication, she states that it is better to have more allies on their side. Holy silently listens to Hermit explaining to everyone in the Briefing Room about the pattern of Chronophage going to planets whenever someone has acted against the stream of time .

Kaede War arc

When Connor makes a rousing speech to the Crew of Edens and their allies, Holy remains quiet. Later, Holy privately contacts her two subordinates Ibuki and Shiro who are in charge of her Interstellar Union Army forces, addressing their concerns of teaming up with two Galáctica members that Feather already knows and reminding them to do as she instructed. She tells them to show their enemies the power of the Midnight fleet and wait until the time comes for the "immaculate military operation". However, she is shocked when huge swarms of Dragons appear as part of Lendard's defense zone and realizes in horror that Deadend Crow is not the only Galáctica member who joined forces with Ziggy; God Acnoella .

Regaining her composure, Holy orders her fleet to eliminate the Dragons. When Hermit is surprised to see her ships possess anti-Dragon Ether, the commander states that as one of the Oración Seis Interstellar, she considers the possibility that they would fight God Acnoella. After learning that Hermit plans to create her own brand of anti-Dragon Ether, she tells her that it took years for the Interstellar Union Army to develop it. But Holy is shocked when Hermit succeeded shortly and immediately shared the anti-Dragon Ether with the forces of Shiki and Elsie. She internally notes that the Crew of Edens are formidable to grow at such terrific speed. Holy then learns that the Edens Zero has its own Knight Gear ; Warrior Maid 95 .

When Hermit and Sister intend to go through the plan to take down Crow, Holy coldly remarks that they made it sound so easy. She replies to the Mind of Edens that she has the training to pilot a Knight Gear and questions if she's willing to let her use Edens Zero's Knight Gear. The commander is surprised when Hermit reveals that she also researched about her past too as well and advises her to fight alongside them. Holy thanks Hermit for willing to help her.

Upon seeing Crow confronting the Edens Zero through the visual screen, Holy darkly smiles with anticipation. Piloting the Steel Sorceress 94, Holy comments on the female battle suit's exposed chest area that she is currently wearing before launching into battle against Crow.

Powers and Abilities

Holy is a member of the Oración Seis Interstellar , a group of six warriors who exist to fight the Oración Seis Galáctica , and in a class of her own even within the Cosmic Government.

Melting Ether Gear : Holy possesses an unnamed Ether Gear that allows her to melt down human bodies into puddles.

Piloting Skills : Holy states that she has the training to pilot a Knight Gear.

Equipment

Midnight (ミッドナイト Middonaito ): Holy's spaceship in the Interstellar Union Army, a large commando carrier that is described to have a lot of firepower such as the special anti-Dragon Ether cannons.
