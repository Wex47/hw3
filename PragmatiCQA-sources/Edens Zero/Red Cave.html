Red Cave ( レッドケイブ , Reddo Keibu ) , also known as the Fire Planet ( 炎の惑星 , Honō no Wakusei ) , is a planet located in the Aoi Cosmos .

Description

Red Cave is a planet covered by an ocean with small islands, some even floating above the water. The atmosphere of the planet is teeming with Water Ether , and when Light Ether interacts with it, it produces a sparkling effect like a rain of light which has been described as a "spectacular view". The Planet seems to orbit around two suns. The ocean is teeming with various sea life, including fishes, cnidaria , decapods , and sea monsters. In an unspecified part of the ocean floor is an underwater city. In actuality, the city was underwater because the Relic , in the form of a candelabra , had been stolen and it affected the entire planet for 200 years. After the treasure was returned, the ocean around the city recedes to normal levels. The planet gets its name from the cave that can spontaneously combust into intense flames under its surface.

Location

The planet is located in an unspecified part of the Aoi Cosmos .
