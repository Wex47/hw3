Warrior Maiden Six-Claw Attack: Wild Tiger Lightning Claw ( 戦乙女六爪流 猛虎雷爪牙 , Ikusa Otome Rokusō-ryū: Mōko Raisōga ) is a Soul Blade technique.

Contents

  • 1 Description
  • 2 Usage
  • 4 Site Navigation

Description

The user rapidly slashes their opponent in multiple directions with lightning-imbued Ether claw blades, injuring and electrocuting them.

Usage

Homura in her Five Sword Fencing: Tiger Form uses this technique to defeat Mora after tricking him to turn off his Glue Body .
