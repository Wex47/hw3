Jaguar Transformation Ether Gear is an unnamed type of Ether Gear that can transform the user into a humanoid jaguar.

Description

This Ether Gear allows the user to transform into a bipedal jaguar and their clothes changed with them. Their claws are strong enough to tear people into bloody pieces with ease and they become very durable in battle, capable of brushing off a direct hit from hard materials, such as Aoi Crystal .
