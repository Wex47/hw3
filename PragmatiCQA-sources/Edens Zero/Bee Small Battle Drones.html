Bee Small Battle Drones ( 小型戦闘機ビー , Kogata Sentōki Bī ) are small drones and weapons of the Edens One .

Description

The Bee Small Battle Drones are small drones equipped with guns. They can be used to stop enemy ships in their tracks. They are large enough to carry a person like Wizard on top of them.

Usage

After intercepting the Scorpion Missiles , Connor deploys the Bee Small Battle Drones to stop the Edens Zero in its tracks which Hermit also deploys unmanned fighters agains them in response. Wizard stands on one Bee Small Battle Drone to destroy the unmanned fighters of the Edens Zero with Divine Lightning .
