Justice ( ジャスティス , Jasutisu ) , once known as James Holloway ( ジェームズ・ホロウェイ , Jēmuzu Horowei ) , is a former prince from the planet Lendard in the Kaede Cosmos and a member of the Interstellar Union Army . He is the commander of the Commando Carrier Angel Feather , leader of his own team of soldiers , and one of the Oración Seis Interstellar who is known as the Celestial Judge ( 星の断罪者 , Hoshi no Danzaisha ) .

Appearance

Justice is a tall, slim able-bodied young man with light blue eyes and effeminate, bushy white hair. His only attire consists of a brown-and-white armored spacesuit that covers his entire body.

When Justice activates his Ether Gear, his hair turns from white to blue, along with a distinct Ether marking formed around his right eye.

Personality

Justice is shown to be a serious and determined man with no sense of humor, dedicated to capturing Elsie Crimson in his namesake. However, he has shown empathy after chiding Victory for brushing off the erasure of many Guilst 's citizens, even if they were criminals, by the Chronophage devouring the planet's 1,200 years of time, as a life is a life. Despite that, Justice is obsessed with capturing and/or killing Elsie as his one and only interest due to a great loss he suffered from her in the past. It goes to the point that the soldier is also willing to kill Shiki who has ties with the Armored Space Pirate and imprison his friend Homura while stating that Ziggy or Poseidon Nero means nothing to him despite the dire situation happening on Foresta. Justice is confident in his abilities as an Ether Gear master, reminding Victory about it with a small smirk. He is also a stickler for rules, according to Eraser.

History

James Holloway was once a prince of a kingdom on Lendard . He was betrothed to Elsie at a young age, who was a princess from another kingdom on the planet, where their union was a means to unite both kingdoms. During their time together at the castle's courtyard, Elsie proposed to James that they should kiss, flustering the young prince. He retorts how that their parents decided for them to get married so Elsie's kingdom could absorb his, but Elsie prepares for the kiss anyway. Blushing he nervously tries but is stopped by the young princess, saying that they should wait until they are older, flustering him more. Later on, it is revealed that the government had plans which were capable of destroying the entire Kaede Cosmos, as pointed out by Elsie. Later on, after Elsie learns about her kingsdom's obsession of weapon development from, that eventually became a threat to the rest of the Kaede Cosmos. James had planned to change the nation, but Elsie had taken action first and exposed the secrets of her kingdom, which resulted in war. James lost everything and was forced to abandon his planet. As a result of the war, James abandoned his name and took on the codename "Justice" as his new identity upon joining the Interstellar Union Army, living up to his goal to bring justice throughout the Cosmos.

Synopsis

Skull Fairy arc

Leading the Interstellar Union Army fleet within the Angel Feather, Justice receives a report from recon that Elsie's ship has not moved from Point 52-C-N9 and acknowledges it. Having found his target within the vicinity, Justice vows in the name of the Interstellar Union Army that today is the day he will take her in. Victory jokes that Justice's pursuit of Elsie is starting to sound like a love story, but the joke is not understood by Justice. Suddenly, Justice detects an abnormal Ether reaction and knows that it did not come from Elsie after Creed reported that it came from Elsie's ship. He deduces that there is someone else on that ship and fighting, wondering who the person is. After hearing Creed reports that the mystery Ether levels rose up to almost 100,000, Justice is surprised. He wonders what is happening on that ship, while noting that there has not been any change in Elsie's Ether. As the Interstellar Union Army fleet surrounds Elsie's space pirate fleet and is within firing range, Justice declares that it is time to bring down "the hammer of justice" before his forces open fire in a barrage of warning shots. However when one ship that has the Demon King Ether reading managed to get away during the fleet engagement between Justice and Elsie's forces according to Creed, Justice is displeased and activates his Ether Gear for battle, lividly muttering the Armored Space Pirate's name.

Guilst arc

After a Chronophage finished consuming Guilst, Justice asked Creed about the planet's situation and learned from his fellow officer that 1,200 years of Guilst's time have been consumed and less than 10% of the planet's population survived the attack. Justice chided Victory for his lack of empathy for the countless lives that were lost to Chronophage, even if they were criminals from Guilst, as a life is a life. Then he overhears Captain Jaguar commenting, and expressing concern, that due to the disaster on Guilst, an old threat has been set loose in the Sakura Cosmos. When he asks Jaguar who he is referring to, Jaguar answers that he is talking about Drakken Joe of the Oración Seis Galáctica .

Red Cave arc

Justice learns from Creed's report that Elsie has entered the Aoi Cosmos where her fellow Oración Seis Galáctica member, Poseidon Nero , resides and the Edens Zero that has had few dealings with the Armored Space Pirate is headed for the same location too as well. When Victory and Creed were impressed that the Galactic Intelligence Agency 's Sakura Cosmos branch has a team powerful enough to take down Drakken, Justice interjects his fellow soldiers that the report from Noah Glenfield was not telling the whole truth. He reveals that he was monitoring the Ether on Belial Gore , recognizing it as that of the Demon King and belonging to someone from Edens Zero. After Victory expresses disbelief that there is an Ether Gear master as good as Drakken or Elsie that is not part of the Oración Seis Galáctica, Justice reminds him that they have one right here as he briefly manifests his Ether Gear with a smirk. Suddenly, they receive an emergency transmission from Captain Jaguar via a holographic screen and is left alone when his superior officer, who orders both Victory and Creed to step outside the bridge of the Angel Feather for they have a Prayer Council.

After Victory and Creed leave the bridge, Justice is confused because the Prayer Council was scheduled for next week. Jaguar tells him that they can not wait that long and reminds the soldier to not address him as captain for there are no ranks in a Prayer Council. Justice tries to protest but Eraser appears alongside Holy , Cure , and Feather , via holographic transmissions, as he comments that Jaguar's subordinate is a stickler for rules. After the six members of the Interstellar Union Army reveal themselves as the Oración Seis Interstellar, Justice immediately confirms to Eraser that he will take the mission to go to the Aoi Cosmos and chase down Elsie. Holy teases him for being in love with the Armored Space Pirate, which he ignores. Justice activates his Ether Gear as he shows his conviction, declaring that he will apprehend Elsie this time in the name of the Oración Seis Interstellar. Clenching his fist, the soldier angrily states that she took everything from him and he will kill her with his own two hands.

Foresta arc

In the Aoi Cosmos, Justice and his Interstellar Union Army fleet arrived on the Coral Sector , a neutral territory, and gave pursuit to the Skull Fairy as he lividly mutters Elsie's name. However, Justice became solemn after Victory and Creed reported to him that they have detected a space pod that was ejected from Elsie's ship and received a signal that it looks like that her space pirate crew is releasing a prisoner . Upon arriving on Foresta , Justice senses Elsie's Ether reading which gets the attention of Victory and Creed. The two soldiers warn Justice that Foresta is a neutral planet and that it's in Poseidon Nero's territory but he remains undeterred. He declares that by his authority as a member of the Oración Seis Interstellar, Elsie will be arrested.

After Victory and Creed defeated and subdued Beast Squad 1 with ease, Justice walks in and briefly wonders why Poseidon Nero did not deploy Oceans instead of Beast despite the situation with hostile bots on Foresta. Noticing Shiki and Homura , Justice believed them to be civilians at first until Victory pointed out the unusual Ether that they sensed. With a frown on his face, Justice recognizes Shiki's Ether from Elsie's ship and identifies him as the Demon King. Justice pointed out to Shiki that Ziggy is the one destroying the Verdant Planet and learned the young man inherited his adoptive grandfather's Ether Gear. He orders Creed to apprehend Homura with his Ether Lock much to Shiki's shock. Ignoring Victory's protests, Justice orders him and Creed to return to the Angel Feather, declaring that he will be operating as a member of the Oración Seis Interstellar from now on. When Shiki charges at him for taking Homura, Justice reveals that he cares more about Elsie's capture than Ziggy or Nero, claiming that if Shiki has ties to Elsie, that is reason enough for him to kill the young man. He easily blocks Shiki's Satan Gravity-empowered punch with his right hand and declares that in the name of justice, he will not let him see tomorrow.

After blocking the young man's punch, Justice explains that the Interstellar Union Army was formed to strike down the evil that runs rampant through the cosmos, be it the Oración Seis Galáctica, Ziggy, or lawless space voyagers like Shiki. He then throws Shiki back and notes that the Edens Zero is an unregistered ship formerly owned by Ziggy and Elsie. The Oración Seis Interstellar member believes that this will require further investigation which angers Shiki into charging at him again. Easily blocking the gravity wielder's spinning axe kick with his right arm, Justice tells the young man that he is about to die with no future beyond this point, charging an Ether blast from his left palm, and fires at Shiki who dodges it and sticks at the tree in a safe distance, noting how aggressive the government he is working for. Justice questions if Shiki is trying to say that his government is oppressive, but admits that there are certain protocols that must be followed, such as not harming civilians. However, Justice reveals that those of the Oración Seis Interstellar are given certain privileges and they have the right, in any situation, to eradicate any target they determine to be evil. Activating his Ether Gear, Justice believes that despite being a mere boy, with the Demon King's power in his Ether and his link with Elsie, Shiki will certainly become a great evil one day if allowed to live.

In his transformed state, the Oración Seis Interstellar member declares that he will execute Shiki right here and now in the name of justice. Using Meteor, Justice attacks Shiki in a lariat strike with incredible speed and throws him several meters across Foresta. Easily catching up to Shiki, Justice attacks the young man only to miss and hit the ground instead. However, Justice quickly appears behind Shiki and knocks him down from the sky before flying below, and knee the young man in the chin, followed by an elbow strike on the head. Then Justice assaults Shiki in multiple directions with Meteor Frenzy, heavily injuring his opponent. Overhearing a shocked Shiki trying to comprehend his power, Justice asks him in a derisive tone if Elsie hid her abilities from her friends. The soldier reveals to Shiki that he uses the same Ether Gear as hers; Star Drain, allowing him to absorb a planet's Ether and make it his own. Justice explains that this power is equivalent to "Heavenly Body Magic" which was feared in the Dark Ages, a force that could control the stars. Intending to finish off the young man in one powerful attack, Justice unleashes Grand Chariot on the grandson of the Demon King. When the smoke clears away, Justice is surprised to see Shiki is still alive after being struck by his attack. Summoning Antares in his hands, the soldier describes it as "a spear of light to pierce evil" and intends to put the injured boy on the ground out of his misery. Suddenly, his strike is blocked by Elsie wielding her own Antares, becoming enraged upon seeing her and lividly mutters her name.

Confronting the Armored Space Pirate, Justice angrily notes that she has the gall to show her face to him. He changes his Antares into a katana and declares a sentence of death to Elsie for violation of cosmic travel laws and multiple other crimes. Justice clashes his weapon with Elsie's own katana, causing a massive shockwave of Ether that is seen across Foresta. Justice and Elsie clash their swords against each other, causing shockwaves that destroy the surrounding area around them. After Elsie notes that he had gotten better in their fight, using his real name James Holloway. However, Justice states that he abandoned that name long ago and calls her a traitor to the fatherland. When the Armored Space Pirate claims that the nation had no meaning, the soldier powers up and angrily retorts that he lost everything in that so-called meaningless nation because of her treachery. Using Meteor, Justice tackles Elsie, taking them several meters away into an abandoned city in Foresta. He sends Elsie flying into the wall of a building with a hammer fist strike. He tries to hit her with a kick but she uses Meteor and dodges out of the way.

The two continue their sword fight whilst flying in the air. After Elsie expresses that she also lost everything, the Oración Seis Interstellar member retorts back that if she had not betrayed them, they would not have lost everything. Jumping out of Elsie's strike that shattered the entire building into pieces, Justice calls her reasoning behind her betrayal to be twisted logic. While battling in the air and hearing her accusation towards him for not knowing what the government's plotting, the soldier retaliates that with his faith in justice that he was going to change the government and he would have made it a better nation. He points out that Elsie started the war after she told the truth about the government. Engaged in a close range grappling hold against each other, the Interstellar member mocks Elsie as the mighty princess who is now a lowly pirate, commenting that there truly is justice in the cosmos. Justice is confused by Elsie's question if he remembers their past as children. After both have stopped their respective Meteors, Justice is not amused by Elsie's proposal to fulfill their childhood promise in a teasing manner. However, Justice moves closer to Elsie for the kiss and states that it will engender nothing. Upon their first kiss, the two simultaneously stabs each other in the abdomen with their katanas much to their surprise. Staggering from his wound, the soldier comments that he knew it would engender nothing and reaffirms his conviction to kill Elsie.

Resuming their fight, Justice, and Elsie call out each other's names before punching each other in the face that causes a huge shockwave around them. Suddenly, a group of Foresta drones ambushed the two after detecting their powerful Ethers. The Interstellar member dodges out of the way from the machines' bombardment and swiftly eliminating them before clashing with Elsie once more. In mid-air, Justice, and Elsie simultaneously unleash Grand Chariot against each other, causing massive destruction. Grievously injured and exhausted from their destructive battle, Justice notes that the mysterious gravity made himself and Elsie miss each other's vital organs, interrupting their fight. Watching Elsie boarding on Behrman 's flying vehicle, the soldier tries to go after her despite his wounds but the Armored Space Pirate tells him that they will settle their fight later and teases him about their first kiss before bidding farewell with his code name and leaving the battlefield. An annoyed Justice is contacted by Holy where his fellow Interstellar member comments on Elsie's strength as the youngest member of the Galáctica. He is reminded by Holy to keep Nero in mind for she believes that the Emperor will make some sort of move and take revenge on Ziggy for what he did to Foresta.

Returning to the Angel Feather in space, Justice is covered in bandages over his wounds and contacts with Holy in the bridge of his Commando Carrier where he learns from his fellow Interstellar member that Nero's top officials, the Oceans 6 and his son Poseidon Shura have assembled on the planet Nero 1 : The Temple. Unconcerned, Justice states with indifference that he does not care about Shura which Holy playfully chides him that he should because the son of the Emperor could be a real threat. The soldier is warned that Nero is old and once he dies, Shura will take over the Nero Empire . After Holy advises him to let Jaguar and Eraser, who are on their way to the Aoi Cosmos, help him, Justice asks her if she is not going to do anything.

Aoi War arc

After learning that Eraser went ahead of them at Nero 66 where Shura is located, Justice contacts the black-haired Interstellar member alongside Jaguar via holographic screen transmission and scolds Eraser, wondering if he has lost his mind. The soldier tells Eraser not to do anything until he says so when he and Jaguar arrive. Even though Eraser reminded him that there is no chain of command in the Oración Seis Interstellar and each of them has the authority to make their own judgment calls, Justice warns him that he is fighting the Nero Empire, the biggest organization in the Oración Seis Galáctica. The soldier remains silent when Eraser explains to him and Jaguar that there are 3,200 Imperial Army ships on their frontline and they are about ten times the size of his Interstellar Union Army fleet of 250 ships, along with the fact that he has no way of knowing how many ships are hiding behind them or are waiting on the planet's surface but reassures them to not worry about it.

When 10,000 Nero Empire ships appear, Justice arrives in his starfighter and attacks a number of them at full speed. He channels his Star Drain through his starfighter's Ether Link and unleashes Grand Chariot upon the Imperial Army, devastating multiple ships in his path much to the shock of the Oasis rebels. The soldier reports for duty and declares that he is now joining the mission to subdue Nero 66 with his fleet appearing to back up Eraser's forces and Oasis. He easily maneuvers his starfighter around enemy gunfire, noticing unidentified signatures in their friendlies' signals and asks Eraser about them. He is told that they are the rebel army, irritating him that they are working with a civilian organization. He then informs Eraser that Jaguar had another matter to attend to and states to his exasperated teammate that what the captain is doing is to help the Aoi Cosmos. He blasts away more Imperial ships with ease and declares that they are going to put a stop to Nero's ambition.

Flying nearby the Angel Feather with his starfighter, Justice tells Victory that powerful jammers must be in place after his subordinate informs him that he can't get through Creed or Jesse. The Interstelar member remarks that the battle has subsided considerably in this sector but he is alarmed when he sees the Sea Serpent among the Nero Empire forces. After witnessing the Sea Serpent's massive beam attack that wiped out their forces, Justice declares that they are going to stop the titanic Imperial Army ship with Victory and Eraser agreeing as he flies his starfighter towards it at full speed. Attacking the Sea Serpent, Justice asks Eraser if he can't use his Erasing Ether Gear yet.

After the Sea Serpent is disabled by Dylan 's suicidal charge towards it, Justice tells Eraser to use his Ether Gear now. He is surprised when he recognizes the broad-range open transmission to be from the Edens Zero. When Eraser orders all Interstellar Union Army forces to leave the front immediately, the soldier questions him if he actually believe them and states that Creed and Jesse are still on Nero 66, questions if they are abandoning them. However, Justice receives a sound-only transmission from the G.I.A. agent who reports that he is no longer on the planet's surface but Creed is not with him. He is in stunned silence as the Angel Feather and its fleet withdraws. When Jesse boards the ship, Justice is informed that his subordinate is dead and that Edens Zero was responsible. Suddenly, a soldier of the Angel Feather informs him that a transport pod is approaching with a signal from Edens Zero stating that they are returning Creed's body. Justice wonders what the Crew of Edens is plotting as Jesse goes to check on the pod.

In the aftermath of the Aoi War, Justice attends a Prayer Council, discussing about the deaths of Jaguar, Creed, and thousands of Interstellar Union Army soldiers that perished in battle. After Feather states that Elsie is not a significant threat to them at this point in time, Justice asks what she means. He listens when Eraser explains to him that Elsie just happens to be really powerful and has caused almost no harm to their citizens, and that the other three Galáctica members are a bigger problem. After they end their Prayer Council, Justice leaves the room and learns that Victory was eavesdropping on the meeting, scolding him that it is against regulations. However, he agrees with Victory that he knows Ziggy had to have been at Nero 1. He believes that the only way possible for Jaguar to be beaten was if Nero and Ziggy worked together. When Victory brings up Creed's death, Justice remembers when Jesse told them.

Justice answers Victory that they want to make an example of Creed when he questioned why they would return his body. After Victory points out that Creed died from a single gunshot in the middle of a war, Justice asks him what he is trying say and says that Jesse told them the Edens Zero shot him. He brushes off Victory's claim that Jesse may not have been remembering right and that it does not change the fact that Creed is dead. Grieving over his comrade's death, Justice notes that Creed ventured into the cosmos to find a way to change Oedo 's isolationist government and that he was a kind man who fought alongside them to change the world, for that was his creed. Crying in tears, Justice laments that Creed was so young and had his whole life ahead of him, but now his future has been taken away from him. He swears that he will render justice on whoever killed Creed. After hearing Jesse reveals that it was Homura Kōgetsu who murdered Creed, Justice furiously mutters her name.

X495 arc

In the Kaede Cosmos , Justice asks Victory how many planets that the Crew of Edens liberated after hearing his subordinate remarking about their success of stopping the uprising on planet Sweeds on the news. After Victory comments that they are shaping up to be better good guys than themselves, Justice retorts that their goal is to beat Ziggy and they would join forces with Elsie to achieve that end, stating there is no "good" in that. The Interstellar member notes that the Crew of Edens travel in an unregistered ship, recruited former members of Drakken's Organization and their leader inherited Ziggy's powers. He reveals that the Prayer Council determined that Shiki has become already a threat to the universe. Tapping into the holographic terminal to display the image of Overdrive Shiki and his glowing left eye, Justice declares that the boy they met 3 years ago is no more and Shiki Granbell, the "Demon King", has been recognized as the new member of the Oración Seis Galáctica. The soldier states that the time to fight him is drawing near.

Kaede War arc

After being informed by Feather about Holy's alliance with Shiki and Elsie, Justice shows up at Lendard and rescues Elsie from Ziggy's attack , flying her to safety in his arms. He tells the Demon King that she is his quarry and refuses to let him have her before immediately leaving the area. The soldier sternly corrects Elsie that it is his mission in life to kill her and no one will take that away from him.

Taking refuge at the small facility, Justice applies first aid on Elsie's injuries and tied her up with anti-Ether cuffs to prevent her attempted movement. When she wakes up, he advises her to keep her voice down from drawing attention to hostile bot soldiers. The soldier emphasizes that he is not helping her and refuses to kill her at her weakened state, revealing that she will stand trial in the Galactic High Court and will confess and atone for her crimes before he executes her. Justice tells Elsie that his new objective is to get off from Lendard and tells her that Holy can take care of Ziggy. He corrects the space pirate captain that Holy never told him but rather it was Feather who gave her report to him and his fellow Interstellar comrades. The soldier notes that they are surprised that so many Galáctica members are in one place and are on their way to Lendard. Justice comments on Elsie being defeated by Ziggy's hand so easily despite her power and believes that the Demon King fooling her into thinking he was his old self was part of his plan.

Powers and Abilities

Justice is a member of the Oración Seis Interstellar , a group of six warriors who exist to fight the Oración Seis Galáctica , and in a class of his own even within the Cosmic Government. He is able to fight evenly with Elsie in battle.

Star Drain (スタードレイン Sutā Dorein ): Like Elsie, Justice uses this Ether Gear to forge into battle gears by absorbing the Ether of a planet, and manipulating the Ether directly to make astronomical attacks. Justice states that he has mastered his Ether Gear on the same level as Elsie and Drakken Joe, two members of the infamous Oración Seis Galáctica and Shiki using Overdrive .

  • Meteor ( 流星 ( ミーティア ) Mītia ): Justice's body is surrounded by a cloak of Ether, allowing him to move at incredible speeds through the air. With this, Justice assaults his opponents with quick and powerful melee attacks.
    • Meteor Frenzy (流星乱舞 Ryūsei Ranbu ): While using Meteor, Justice rapidly assaults his opponent with incredibly fast melee attacks in multiple directions.
  • Grand Chariot ( 七星剣 ( グランシャリオ ) Guran Shario ): By placing both his hands on top of each other, with the top hand having only the index and middle fingers spread out. Seven beams erupt from the ground around him, dispatching his opponent. When Justice uses it in space with his starfighter's Ether Link, the beams are omnidirectional lasers and have incredible penetrating powers capable of taking down multiple enemies.
  • Antares ( 天蠍槍 ( アンタレス ) Antaresu ): Justice summons a spear called Antares and describes it "a spear of light to pierce through evil". It consists of a dark-colored polearm, a cross-shaped spearhead with four diamonds adorning it, having three blades made from Ether jutting out. On the other end is a similar diamond with a jewel embedded in it.

Immense Strength : Justice possesses tremendous physical strength, as shown when he easily blocks a gravity-enhanced punch from Shiki and easily throws the young man back with his right hand. During his battle with Elsie, the force of their swords colliding together created a massive shockwave across Foresta.

Immense Durability : Justice possesses tremendous physical durability, capable of taking attacks from Elsie and survive a simultaneously Grand Chariot against each other.

Expert Hand-to-Hand Combatant : Justice is highly skilled in hand-to-hand combat, using his Meteor technique in conjunction.

Master Swordsmanship Specialist : Justice is a highly skilled swordsman, being on par with Elsie in a sword fight.

Piloting Skills : Justice has displayed the ability to pilot a starfighter, easily dodging attacks while sending his own at his targets.

  • Katana : Justice can summon a katana with a crisscrossed patterned hilt and a blade made of Ether.

Starfighter : Justice pilots a small starfighter with the insignia of the Interstellar Union Army that he uses it in conjunction with his Star Drain via Ether Link in battle and its speed readings are off the charts to the point Eraser notes that he doubt normal gunfire could hit it.

Trivia

  • Justice shares most of his character design with Jellal Fernandes and Sieghart Caeser , characters from Hiro Mashima 's previous works, Fairy Tail and Rave Master respectively. The similarity becomes more apparent when he activates his Ether Gear, in which his hair changes color to blue and an intricate tattoo appears around his right eye.
    • Justice and Jellal are part of their respective governments; with Jellal having infiltrated the Magic Council to manipulate them, while Justice has dedicated himself to fight evil under the Cosmic Government.
    • Justice has a relationship with Elsie, who shares most of her character design with Erza . However, unlike the two, Justice has a personal vendetta against the Armored Space Pirate.
    • Like Jellal, Justice wields the power of manipulating astronomical objects through Star Drain which was known as Heavenly Body Magic from the Dark Ages.
    • Justice shares the same voice actors, Daisuke Namikawa and Robert McCollum, with Jellal in the Fairy Tail anime.
  • Justice was planned to appear in the Digitalis arc , being the real identity of the fake Homura, but Hiro Mashima decided to create a new character instead.
