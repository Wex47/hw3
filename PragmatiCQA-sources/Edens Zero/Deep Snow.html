Deep Snow ( ディープスノー , Dīpu Sunō ) was an Imperial palace of Poseidon Nero of the Oración Seis Galáctica on Nero 1 .

Description

Deep Snow is a large palace with several tall and medium-sized structures around it and it has several statues of reptilian humanoids with spears decorated within the hallway.

Location

Deep Snow is located on top of a tall, mountain-like rock formations nearby the technologically advanced cities of Nero 1.

Trivia

  • Deep Snow shares the same name with a character from one of Hiro Mashima 's previous works, Rave Master .
