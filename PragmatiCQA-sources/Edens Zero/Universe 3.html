Universe 3 is the current world that came about as a result of Shiki Granbell choosing not to try and stop the destruction of Nero 66 .

World No. 31 was the first world in this universe appearing, coming into existence beginning in Chapter 165 , by Chapter 172 Universe 3 was now up to World No. 416. This number includes all the times Rebecca has used Reverse .

Differences from Universe 2

Ziggy was aware of what would transpire in Universe 2 , but with the change to Universe 3 he no longer can predict what happens next. Per Ziggy, in World No. 30 Shiki opted to try and stop Nero 66 from being destroyed by the 20,000 Imperial Antimatter Bombs , resulting in his death but allowing the Edens Zero to get away without any losses aside from him. Ziggy also needed control of the Nero Empire in World No. 30 for his goals, but regarded it as now being worthless in World No. 31. Ziggy also claimed to Elsie Crimson that in the previous world he would have eventually have been successful in his goal of the destruction of organic life, though he believes this will come to pass eventually in No. 31 as well.

Rather than working for Ziggy as captain of Edens One , Connor was instead a worker in one of his factories until learning that Ziggy was the one it was producing robots for, at which point he fled.
