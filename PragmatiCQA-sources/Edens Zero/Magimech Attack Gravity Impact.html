Magimech Attack: Gravity Impact ( 魔械流 グラビティインパクト , Makai-ryū: Gurabiti Inpakuto , lit. "Magic Machine Style: Gravity Impact") is a Satan Gravity technique.

Contents

  • 1 Description
  • 2 Usage
  • 4 Site Navigation

Description

The user absorbs kinetic energy from an external source and then converts it into concentrated gravity to perform a devastating downward punch.

Usage

Shiki first utilized this technique in his fight against Kurenai Kōgetsu and her Kurenai Dragoon , absorbing the kinetic energy from her Queen Nova missiles and using it against her, crushing the Knight Gear with a blow of concentrated gravity to a single point.
