History

Skull Fairy arc

Stationed on the Angel Feather , Justice leads his Interstellar Union Army fleet to pursue Elsie Crimson and receives a report from recon that the Skull Fairy has not moved from Point 52-C-N9. Victory jokes that his leader's pursuit of the Armored Space Pirate is starting to sound like a love story but Justice ignores it. Creed reports to his teammates that the abnormal Ether reaction they detected is coming from Elsie's ship but it does not belong to her. The team is surprised that the mystery Ether 's numbers are rising up to 100,000 and that its identification code is that of the Demon King . As the Interstellar Union Army fleet surrounds Elsie's fleet of space pirates and are within firing range, Justice declares that it is time to bring down the hammer of justice. During the engagement, one ship escapes from the scene, which Creed informs his teammates about it. Victory believes that Elsie wanted to let that ship get away on purpose while a displeased Justice activates his Ether Gear for battle, lividly muttering the Armored Space Pirate's name.

Guilst arc

After a Chronophage consumed 1,200 years of Guilst 's time, Justice and Victory learn from Creed that less than 10% of the planet's population had survived the attack. Victory has no empathy for the criminals that were erased from existence, but Justice calls his teammate out on for a life is a life.

Red Cave arc

After learning that Elsie is heading for the Aoi Cosmos where Poseidon Nero resides, Victory and Creed agree that things have gotten more complicated with two members of the Oración Seis Galáctica meeting each other just when they have finished up the case with Drakken Joe , who is currently in the Interstellar Union Army's custody. Changing the subject, Victory did not expect Noah from the Galactic Intelligence Agency 's Sakura Cosmos branch to have a team powerful enough to defeat the Dark Alchemist which impresses Creed. But Justice interjects his fellow teammates that the report was not telling the whole truth and reveals that he was monitoring the Ether at Belial Gore , where he identifies it to be that of the Demon King from Edens Zero. Victory finds it insane that there is an Ether Gear master out there as good as Drakken or Elsie that is not part of the Oración Seis Galáctica. Justice reminds Victory with a small smirk that he is also an Ether Gear master as well, briefly manifesting his Ether Gear. Then Creed receives an emergency transmission from Captain Jaguar who orders the two soldiers to step outside of the bridge of the Angel Feather for they are going to have a Prayer Council. Victory and Creed immediately complies, leaving their leader alone with Jaguar to attend a Prayer Council with the Oración Seis Interstellar.

Foresta arc

In the Aoi Cosmos, Justice and his team with the Interstellar Union Army fleet arrive in the Coral Sector , a neutral territory, and they pursue the Skull Fairy . Later on, Victory detects a space pod that was ejected from Elsie's ship, where Creed informs Justice of a signal from the space pirates, saying that they are releasing a prisoner .

Upon arriving on Foresta , Justice senses Elsie's Ether reading which gets the attention of Victory and Creed. The two soldiers warn their leader that the Verdant Planet is not only a neutral planet, but also in Poseidon Nero 's territory. However, their leader remains undeterred. Later, Victory swiftly defeats Beast Squad 1 in one hit with Titan's Arm , saving Shiki and Homura while Creed imprisons them with Ether Lock cages. The two soldiers note that the three members of Beast are a "bunch of small-time hoodlums" and ranked below Oceans which they overhear Justice briefly wondering why Poseidon Nero did not deploy them despite the hostile bots situation on Foresta. When the three Interstellar Union Army soldiers take note of Shiki and Homura, Creed believes that they are tourists judging from their civilian clothes but Victory senses an unusual Ether from one of them. After learning that Shiki is the Demon King and has ties with Elsie, Justice tells Creed to apprehend Homura whom the latter complies with despite Victory's protest. Justice then orders his team to return to the Angel Feather, stating that he is operating from here on as an Oración Seis Interestelar member before fighting an enraged Shiki.

While Justice fights Shiki and later Elsie , Victory and Creed are confronted by Princess Guards , Gowen and Hyoga who are under the Armored Space Pirate's orders to rescue Homura from custody. Seeing this as an opportunity to capture Elsie's #2 and #3, the two Interstellar Union Army soldiers engage them in battle. However, when the Verdant Planet is affected by Xenolith 's Magimech Attack: Gravity Center on a massive scale, Victory and Creed are surprised by the intense gravity that affects them while Homura's Ether Lock cube is accidentally dropped on the ground.

After the intense gravity is gone, Creed releases Homura from his Ether Lock before she is accidentally stepped on by a giant Victory. He retorts to Gowen that unlike the space pirates, the Interstellar Union Army does not kill for no reason. However, Gowen points out that they do lock up people for no reason which Victory states the fact Homura is with the space pirates gives them the right to lock her up, upsetting Hyoga. When Homura escapes with her friends, Victory angrily declares that they will not get away while Creed remains silent with a blush as he watches her leave.

On the other hand, the same intense gravity caused Justice and Elsie to miss each other's organs after unleashing their respective Grand Chariots and interrupt their fight. After the Armored Space Pirate left the battlefield in Behrman 's flying vehicle, Justice attempts to go after her despite his wounds, but Commander Holy contacts him and praises Elsie's strength. While Holy may not mind Justice continue chasing after Elsie, the female Interstellar member reminds the soldier to keep Nero in mind for she believes that the Emperor will make some sort of move and take revenge on Ziggy for what he did to Foresta.

Returning to the Angel Feather and the fleet in space, an enamored Creed looks at the pictures of Homura on his phone before a refreshed Victory walks in and dryly asks if he was watching dirty videos while on the job. The surprised bespectacled soldier claims that he was just analyzing the Crew of Edens which Victory tells him to not bother as they are barely a threat compared to Elsie and Nero. Creed explains to his fellow soldier that the more he learned about them, the more mysteries he finds. After checking out Shiki and his friends, Victory agrees that the Crew of Edens are weird but tells Creed that they don't have time bothering with them especially the fact that Elsie and Nero of the Galáctica and Ziggy are in the Aoi Cosmos. Victory hopes that this does not turn into anything too catastrophic.

Meanwhile at the bridge of Angel Feather, a recuperating Justice converses with Holy who informs him that Nero has assembled his top officials, the Oceans 6 and his son Poseidon Shura on Nero 1 : The Temple. The commander playfully chides the unconcerned soldier that Shura could be a threat and it is their chance to destroy him or else the Aoi Cosmos will suffer for it later, noting that Shura will take over the Nero Empire once his father Nero dies of old age. Commenting that there are far too many big fish for Justice to handle, Holy also tells the soldier that Jaguar and Eraser of the Interestelar are on their way and advises him to let them help him. When Justice asks her if she is not going to do anything, Holy answers with an impish smile that she has other fish to fry.

Aoi War arc

After Justice showed up in time to dispatch multiple Imperial Army ships with his Star Drain-enhanced starfighter, his Interstellar Union Army fleet arrive in Nero 66 to reinforce Eraser's troops and the Oasis rebels. As Justice pilots the starfighter, Victory commandeers the Angel Feather in battle nearby Nero 66 and informs his leader that he can't get through Creed and Jesse who infiltrated Nero 66, hoping they are alright.

After witnessing the Sea Serpent 's massive beam attack that wiped out their forces, Victory agrees with Justice that they are going to stop the titanic Imperial Army ship together. Victory warns his leader that the Sea Serpent is about to unleash a second energy blast and its energy is fully charged.

When Jesse reports to the team that he is no longer on the planet's surface, Victory expresses relief and asks the G.I.A. agent if Creed is with him only to receive silence instead. Realizing that Creed is gone, Victory tells Justice that they are leaving as the Angel Feather and its fleet withdraw quickly from Nero 66 before 20,000 Imperial Antimatter Bombs explode.

In the aftermath of the Aoi War, Justice returns from a Prayer Council with the Oración Seis Interstellar and enters a room where Victory asks him why he did not bring up Ziggy to them, revealing he was eavesdropping much to his superior's chagrin. Victory tells Justice that Jaguar's death is confirmed to be from Nero's dice and that it wasn't the captain who gave the deceased Nero his wounds. Justice agrees and believes that the only way for Jaguar to be defeated if Ziggy and Nero worked together. Changing the subject, Victory states he wants to talk about Creed. Both soldiers remember Jesse telling them that Creed was murdered and they are shocked to see a pod containing Creed's body is sent to them by the Edens Zero. They discuss the hidden motive behind the Crew of Edens, the suspicious nature of Creed's murder and Jesse's shaky testimony. However, Victory agrees with Justice that it doesn't change the fact Creed is dead and mourns for their comrade's death. When Jesse claims that it was Homura who murdered Creed, Justice mutters her name furiously while Victory looks at him with suspicion.

Members

Current

  • Justice : The leader of the team and member of the Oración Seis Interstellar who is obsessed with capturing and killing Elsie Crimson of the Oración Seis Galáctica and uses the Star Drain Ether Gear.
  • Victory : A large and light-hearted member of the team who uses the Ether Gear Titan's Arm .

Former

  • Creed : An anxious and analytical soldier who was part of Justice's team and uses Ether Lock Ether Gear. He died after being shot in the heart by Jesse .
