Agie ( アギイ , Agii ) is a member of the Interstellar Union Army and a subordinate of Feather of the Oración Seis Interstellar .

Appearance

Agie is a large burly man with an afro and a small wing tattoo beside his left eye. He wears a black-and-gray armored spacesuit that covers his upper body, and dark pants and shoes. On his back, he has an angel wing on his right side.

Personality

Agie is a loud and boisterous man who is concerned of civilian safety and orders his comrades to not hurt them. He is very confident when he is granted Feather's powers through Ether Connect.

Synopsis

X495 arc

Surrounding the Shooting Starlight guildhall, Agie loudly informs his fellow soldiers that there are lots of civilians on Blue Garden and they should not be harmed. When the Crew of Edens emerge from the guildhall, Agie announces the group's presence to his team. After Feather uses Ether Connect on him and his comrades, he agrees with Sam , stating how this technique allows them to temporarily use her powers. However, he is baffled and protests that they should be able to see their moves before he is lectured by Homura that just because they can see her friends' movements, it doesn't mean they can handle them. Agie and Sam are then cut down swiftly by the swordswoman.

Powers and Abilities

Eye of Venus (ヴィーナス・アイ Vīnasu Ai ): Through Feather's Ether Connect, Agie is able to temporarily use this Ether Gear , gaining Ether marks on his face and a glowing aura.
