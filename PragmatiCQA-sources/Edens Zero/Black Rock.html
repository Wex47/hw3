Black Rock ( ブラックロック , Burakku Rokku ) was a powerful Stone that was renowned as the lord of Sun Jewel 's mines for never being defeated.

Appearance

True to its name, Black Rock is a giant quadruped Stone whose body is covered with glittering Black Crystals , with the exception of its underbelly, which is smooth and brightly colored. It has an eyeless, funnel-shaped head with concave, glowing "mouth" lined with additional scales on the inside. According to Homura Kōgetsu , Black Rock's body has a vaguely feline shape, although she mainly makes the comparison to ease Shiki's anxiety when he fears facing a giant bug.

Synopsis

Sun Jewel arc

After being attacked by Shiki Granbell for kidnapping Homura Kōgetsu , Garrot transports the two to Black Rock's den in the mines on Sun Jewel , fully expecting the monster to kill them, while also anticipating a massive delivery of metal to Madame Kurenai on the off chance that they defeat it as a win-win. Upon facing the monster, Shiki reckons that he and Homura can fill their quota of 100,000 metals in one fell swoop by beating it. However, Black Rock manages to withstand their attacks, breaking Homura's sword and injuring Shiki's fists, before firing an energy beam from its mouth that they barely evade in time. Unable to find them, Black Rock summons a swarm of wasp-like Stones, which are subsequently shot down by Rebecca Bluegarden with her Happy Blasters .

Powers and Abilities

Black Rock was regarded as the lord of Sun Jewel's mines for being a creature that no hunter had been able to vanquish. It required the combined forces of Rebecca Bluegarden , Happy and Shiki Granbell in order to expose the weak point on its back to Homura Kōgetsu who managed to defeat it by inflicting a critical injury using the power of her Ether Gear .

Immense Strength : Black Rock has enormous physical strength, being able to pulverize the ground beneath itself when it attacks.

Immense Durability : Black Rock's scales are composed of Black Crystal which allow it to withstand powerful Ether Gear attacks without sustaining any damage, while also inflicting damage on its attackers' bodies or weapons. Only a single spot on the creature's back is not as hard as the rest of its body.

Mouth Beam : Black Rock can fire a powerful beam of energy from its mouth.
