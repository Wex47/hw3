Description

It is a tall gothic castle spawning several stories with its height being far superior to most of the other monuments and buildings. The King's Throne with an integrated repair system and energy monitoring system of all the Machines in Granbell, reside within this castle.

Location

It is located at the central region of the Granbell Kingdom.
