Description

TB 7000 is a bot that can have the personality and strengths of other individuals installed into it, allowing it to impersonate them for sparring matches. When the impersonation is finished, the TB 7000 glows brightly and reveals its true appearance.

Synopsis

Mildian arc

During the Crew of Edens ' visit to Mildian , Xiaomei prepares a TB 7000 with the appearance and behavior of a Valkyrie Yuna replica to serve as Homura Kōgetsu 's opponent in their match at the Mildian Battle Coliseum , both to warn the crew about the Four Shining Stars ' unduplicable nature, and to allow Homura an opportunity to speak with the deceased Valkyrie one last time.

Powers and Abilities

Replication : TB 7000 are able to replicate all of the traits of those who they have been installed the information of, although their fighting ability is inferior to the original.

  • Soul Blade (ソウルブレイド Sōru Bureido ): When replicating Valkyrie, the TB 7000 has the ability to create and wield swords made of Ether .
  • Swordsmanship Specialist : When replicating Valkyrie, the TB 7000 is able to hold the upper hand against Homura during most of their battle with skillful fencing before ultimately being defeated by the latter.
