Brigandine ( ブリガンダイン , Burigandain ) , better known as the Armor of Edens (エデンズの鎧 Edenzu no Yoroi ), is a machine and one of the Demon King's Four Dark Stars . After being severely damaged by Jaguar , he was rebuilt as Brigandine Mark II ( ブリガンダイン ＭＫ ( マーク ) Ⅱ ( ツー ) , Burigandain Māku II ) .

Appearance

In Brigandine's deleted memories, he had a tan skin tone, long, light-colored hair in a ponytail, and a matching goatee.

Brigandine is the largest and most physically imposing machine among the Four Dark Stars, appearing as a dark heavily armored knight and his head has a large rhinoceros beetle horn. He carries a high-tech labrys at his back.

Brigandine Mark II remains the largest and most physically imposing machine among the Four Dark Stars, appearing as a heavily armored knight with large pauldrons. His face is shaped like a lion's face with dark wavy "hair".

Personality

Brigandine is a stoic bot with few words and has shown a hint of arrogance, viewing the Four Shining Stars as paltry prototypes and does not consider them a threat. He is relatively humble as shown with his interaction with Homura Kōgetsu , and values honor when fighting much to Killer's annoyance.

Synopsis

Edens One arc

After a revived Ziggy, boards the Edens One and leaves Granbell , he introduces the Four Dark Stars to the Crew of Edens where Brigandine stands alongside his fellow Dark Stars. Brigandine notes that the Four Shining Stars seem to have one member missing which Killer confirms Valkyrie 's death. Later on, Brigandine comments that he hardly sees their predecessors as a threat to them, calling them paltry prototypes.

Foresta arc

At the bridge of Edens One, where Ziggy learns about the machine rebellion being stopped by the Crew of Edens at Foresta , Brigandine stands silently as the Demon King explains to the Dark Stars about his lost memories, his quest to find Mother , and his decision to raise Shiki as his grandson. Brigandine stands stoically as Ziggy remarks that Shura reacted exactly as he expected after watching the prince's public announcement to the Nero Empire .

Aoi War arc

At The Temple , Brigandine is called by Ziggy to attack Jaguar of the Oración Seis Interstellar as he barges through the roof of the Imperial palace, Deep Snow , and swiftly pushes back the captain with a shoulder charge directly. However, he is quickly ripped apart by the man's claws and collapses on the floor from the severe damages.

X495 arc

On Lendard , a rebuilt Brigandine remarks that the machines produced on this planet would surpass even the Four Dark Stars one day and stands next to the other Dark Stars as Ziggy informs them that Shiki is coming and that they will stay here and fight him.

Kaede War arc

Powers and Abilities

Brigandine was built as part of the Demon King's Four Dark Stars ; a group of machines designed to be more advanced than the Demon King's Four Shining Stars . As the one who was designed to be able to counter Valkyrie , Brigandine was given the greatest attack power of the Four Dark Stars.

Machine Physiology : As a machine , Brigandine should be able to survive in the vacuum of space unprotected with no negative effects like his fellow machine, Wizard .

Enhanced Strength : Brigandine is strong enough to briefly push back Jaguar with a shoulder charge attack.

Enhanced Speed : Despite his large size, Brigandine is shown to be rather fast, charging towards Jaguar at full speed.

Immense Durability : As the "Armor of Edens," Brigandine is shown to possess a tremendous amount of durability, as Homura's Soul Blade sword attacks had no effect on him.

Anti-EMP Coating : Brigandine, like all members of the Four Dark Stars , is equipped with a defensive coating that protects him from E.M. Pino 's Electromagnetic Pulse .

Equipment

Labrys : Brigandine wields a high-tech labrys, a double-bladed battle axe, that he carries at his back.
