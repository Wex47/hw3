Midnight ( ミッドナイト , Middonaito ) is the commando carrier of Holy , and a class of ships in the Interstellar Union Army .

Description

Described to have a lot of firepower , the Midnight is a large spaceship with a rounded, almost saucer-like appearance, having a pair of wings below, and a pair of turrets under it. The flight deck sits at the top and has a curved roof. Above the ship is a large square with a smaller square inside and four smaller lines around each side. On the front of the Midnight is the Interstellar Union Army logo.

It can fire a large energy beam from the special anti- Dragon Ether cannons in battle.

Trivia

  • The ship's name is a nod to Midnight , a member of the Oración Seis from Fairy Tail .
