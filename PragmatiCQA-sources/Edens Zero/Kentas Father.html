Personality

He is a reasonable authority figure, in contrast with his son, and is very polite and civilized towards Valkyrie , being far less bigoted towards the android than most of the people on his planet, enough to speak to her on the same terms as a person rather than a machine . Despite his stern appearance, he can crack a joke at his own expense. He worries that he spoiled his son, but still loves him as a father despite his son's attitude.
