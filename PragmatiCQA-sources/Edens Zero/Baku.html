Baku ( バクー , Bakū ) is a member of the Zaiten Three , nicknamed Fuzaiten ( 斧罪天 , Fuzaiten , lit. "Axe Sin Deva") , the "Ax of Heaven".

Appearance

Baku is a giant alien with huge tusks jutting out of his mouth, a hunched back, a ring of tattoos around his neck, and a face that vaguely looks like a lion. He wears a combat outfit with a small hat on his head.

Personality

As a loyal enforcer, Baku gets very zealous when inflicting "divine punishment" on Madame Kurenai 's foes, but he's otherwise fairly laid-back and considerate towards his teammates.

When Baku sees Kurenai walking Cedric as her human pet, he bluntly expresses his wish to be her pet himself.

Synopsis

Sun Jewel arc

Baku is ordered to squelch the uprising in the Labor District, which he obliges. He crashes in the middle of the battle, announcing to Valkyrie's Army that they shouldn't just go and start uprisings and that they have made Madame Kurenai mad. He swings his axe, making some of them fly, and adds that they have made him mad as well. Just as his axe is about to hit Rebecca , it is stopped by an armored man calling himself Arsenal.

Powers and Abilities

Master Axemanship Specialist : Fitting of his title as the Axe of Heaven, he is a highly skilled combatant with his battleaxe.

  • Battleaxe: Sonic Wave (戦斧 ソニックウェイブ Senpu: Sonikku Weibu ): By swinging his battleaxe, Baku is capable of generating blades of Ether that damage its target. These blades, however, cannot harm anything with high Ether coating.

Immense Speed : Despite his bulk, Baku is shown to be extremely fast, being capable of catching up with Shiki Granbell despite the latter's Gravity Ether Gear . During his fight with Weisz as Arsenal , he commented that his strongest weapon was his speed.

Enhanced Strength : Baku possesses superhuman physical strength, the swings from his axe were powerful enough to send the workers of Sun Jewel's labor district flying at ease.

Equipment

Battleaxe : True to his title as "Axe of Heaven", Baku carries a massive battleaxe on his back.
