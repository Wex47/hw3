The Magical Beast Army (魔獣兵団 Majū Heidan ) is an army of monsters that inhabit the Digitalis MMORPG Rogue Fantasia .

History

The Magical Beast Army was programmed into Rogue Fantasia as a horde of enemy monsters for players to defeat as part of an in-game event. By the year X492, the monsters became self-aware along with the rest of the game's non-player characters, eventually resulting in the game being abandoned by its creators.

Synopsis

Digitalis arc

The members of the Magical Beast Army are reprogrammed by Jamilov to serve as his personal army. The army is dispatched to invade the town of Crysta and kill its NPC inhabitants, intending to turn it into a city of monsters so Jamilov can slaughter them for experience points.
