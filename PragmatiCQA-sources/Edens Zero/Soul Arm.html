Soul Arm ( ソウルアーム , Sōru Āmu ) is an Ether Gear that utilizes the creation of arms made of solid Ether .

Description

This Ether Gear allows the user to materialize and control arms of solidified Ether that sprout from their back for combat. These arms are capable of shattering the ground with a single punch. They can also move around freely and stretch quite a distance from the user to reach their desired targets. Soul Arm can be utilized in a defensive manner, allowing the user to block enemy strikes and incoming projectiles, such as Ether bullets.
