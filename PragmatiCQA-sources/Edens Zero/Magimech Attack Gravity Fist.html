Magimech Attack: Gravity Fist ( 魔械流 重拳 , Makai-ryū: Jūken , lit. "Magic Machine Style: Heavy Fist") is a Satan Gravity technique.

Description

The user's arm glows momentarily. Subsequently, the user delivers a power-packed punch onto the opponent, causing sheer force and blunt damage to the latter and the surrounding area.

Usage

Shiki first uses this technique to defeat the Castellan in one blow and destroyed the entire area with it.
