Drum Buster ( ドラムバスター , Doramu Basutā ) is a Machina Maker technique used in combination with the Arsenal Suit .

Description

While wearing the Arsenal Suit , the user reconfigures the suit's forearm, in real-time, into a cannon that fires an explosive blast strong enough to shatter anything in its path, including rocks. When in-character as Arsenal, Weisz describes this attack as "power of darkness, born from shadows" .

Usage

The technique was first used by Weisz Steiner in his fight against Baku but he was unable to deal significant damage due to his bulky opponent's speed.

Weisz attempted to use the technique against Laguna of the Element 4 but his opponent easily evaded the blast by surfing on a wave of water with his Ether Gear Tears Lover .

Trivia

  • The attack's name and description are references to the Shadow Dragon Skiadrum from Hiro Mashima 's previous work, Fairy Tail .
