Ryuzetsu Land ( リュウゼツランド , Ryūzetsu Rando ) is a popular summer vacation resort on Dahlia .

Description

Ryuzetsu Land is a waterpark where the exterior has a large dome with its name on a sign under a huge "WELCOME" sign. Behind it there is a tall hotel building that curves on both sides. There is a rooftop lounge, which is a place with an air of luxury, that is only accessed by guests staying in resort suites.

Location

Ryuzetsu Land is located in an unspecified part of Dahlia.

Trivia

  • The name of the resort "Ryuzetsu Land" translates to Dragon Tongue Land .
  • Ryuzetsu Land shares the same name with its counterpart from one of Hiro Mashima 's previous works, Fairy Tail .
