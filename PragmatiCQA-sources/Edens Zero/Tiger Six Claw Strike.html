Tiger Six Claw Strike ( 虎鉄六連 , Kotetsu Rokuren ) is a Soul Blade technique used.

Description

The user dashes toward their opponent and slashes through them with Ether claw blades.

Usage

Homura in her Five Sword Fencing: Tiger Form uses this technique in an attempt to directly attack Mora but failed to harm him after he uses Glue Body to protect himself.
