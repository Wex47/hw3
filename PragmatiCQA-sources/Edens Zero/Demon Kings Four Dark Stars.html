The Demon King's Four Dark Stars ( 魔王四黒星 , Maō Shikokusei ) are a group of four machines that are said to be a more advanced generation of Four Shining Stars . They are part of Ziggy's crew of the Edens One .

History

At some point, the Demon King's Four Dark Stars were created by the Demon King Ziggy, along with the Edens One before they went into space and waited for their creator's call for them at Granbell .

In Universe 1 , the Four Dark Stars were presumably destroyed in an unknown disaster when Connor explained to Shiki and his friends that he lost his ship and crew and he was the only survivor. However, in Universe 2 the Edens One was not destroyed and the Four Dark Stars are alive onboard.

Synopsis

Edens One arc

When a revived Ziggy declares his intention to conquer the cosmos and boarded the Edens One, he introduces the Four Dark Stars to the Crew of Edens , claiming them to be superior machines compared to the Four Shining Stars.

Foresta arc

At the bridge of Edens One where Ziggy learns about the machine rebellion that he perpetuated was being stopped by the Crew of Edens at Foresta , the Four Dark Stars stand silently by the Demon King's side as Clown asks him if his resolve to fight against his old family has been shaken. He and his fellow Dark Stars listen to Ziggy who explains to them about his lost memories, his quest to find Mother , and his decision to raise Shiki as his grandson. Wizard asks Ziggy if Shiki prevents him from his work to create a world ruled by mechanical lifeforms.

Aoi War arc

At The Temple , Brigandine and Killer attempt to ambush Jaguar of the Oración Seis Interstellar by Ziggy's orders. But they are easily destroyed by the captain's claws, shocking the Demon King of Edens One.

X495 arc

On Lendard , the Four Dark Stars stand with Ziggy as they discuss about the rapid production of the Skeleton Army and the next assault on another planet before the Demon King informs them that Shiki and his crew are coming and that they will stay here to fight them.

Kaede War arc

Members

  • Wizard : The "Spear of Edens", who is a handsome-looking android who manipulates Ether in a manner similar to Magic . He is the counterpart of Witch .
  • Clown : The "Death of Edens", an android dressed in a flamboyant Harlequin outfit with a playful demeanor. He is the counterpart of Sister .
  • Brigandine : The "Armor of Edens", who is a a large armored machine, wielding a large labrys. After being rebuilt into Mark II he becomes a large lion-faced, armored android. He is the counterpart of Valkyrie .
  • Killer : The "Brains of Edens", who is a tall lean machine with a masked face. After being rebuilt into Mark II he appears as a boy wearing a cloak. He is the counterpart of Hermit .
