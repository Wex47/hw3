Description

Rogue Fantasia is an MMORPG that players dive into to take on a digital form. The game allows the players to customize their character and use any abilities they possess in the real word. Any damage received by a player's avatar affects their physical body and, in bad situations, can cause that player's demise. The developers wanted to make the game as realistic as possible, so there is almost no heads-up display, thus removing RPG standards such as the status display for health.

The balance in the game was at some point destroyed after the NPCs became self-conscious with the ability to rewrite their own program and live like normal people. The game was shut down a few decades before X492.

In-game mechanics

Rules

Rogue Fantasia has a set of rules that players are required to follow. Breaking the rules results in the player getting a ban. When a player gets banned, they are kicked from the game and will not be able to return to it.

One of the rules is that players are not allowed to use cheats. Cheats allow the player to change the game in unnatural ways. The only known way of using cheats without getting banned is to run them using another player's account.

Some of the cheats used within Rogue Fantasia are:

  • Being able to kill NPCs without getting banned
  • Invincibility
  • The ability to undo another player's cheat
  • Buffing other players
  • Changing another player's race/appearance
  • Equipping other players with certain items
  • Executing Rogue Fantasia's annihilation program, which erases an area in three minutes

Stats

Rogue Fantasia features stats that affect the outcome of battles within the game. There are currently three known stats in Rogue Fantasia; the defense stat, attack power, and the health stat. The highest defense stat is 999 and the highest health stat is 100,000. None of the stats show up on a display for the player to look at like other RPGs .

Items & Equipment

Rogue Fantasia has different types of items and equipment that players can use in and outside battle. Some of the game's items and equipment is:

  • Turn-to-Timonium (Mount)
  • Maria (Weapon)
  • Various close-combat weapons
  • Crossbows
  • Guns
  • Rifles
  • Light Armor
  • Demon Armor
  • Emperor Armor
  • Scarlet Armor
  • Crystal Armor
  • Bikinis

Quests

The game features different quests that players can complete. The only known quest, Retrieve the city's secret treasure , requires the player to get a secret treasure from the event boss, Trollburglar , only to discover that he protected Crysta using the previously mentioned treasure.
