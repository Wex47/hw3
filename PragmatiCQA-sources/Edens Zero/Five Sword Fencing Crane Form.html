Five Sword Fencing: Crane Form (五形剣 鶴の型 Gokeiken: Tsuru no Kata ) is a Soul Blade technique used by Homura Kōgetsu .

Description

Homura while gripping her sword in one hand, balances on a single leg and performs graceful swordplay that allows her strikes to cleave through the wind itself.

Usage

Homura first used this technique against Kleene to cut through her wind attacks of Wind Snatch .
