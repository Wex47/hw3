The Sakura Cosmos ( 桜 ( サクラ ) 宇宙 ( コスモス ) , Sakura Kosumosu , lit. "Cherry Blossom Cosmos") is the region of space where Granbell , Blue Garden and many other planets reside. It is one of the four cosmoses that comprises the Grand Shiki Cosmos .

Description

The Sakura Cosmos is home to several different planets, each of which is defined by their individuality; such as theme parks where robots resided and where Shiki was raised. Dwelling within these cosmos are the Dragons . It is bordered by Dragonfall where swarms of Dragons exist that prevent travel to the outside universe and other neighboring cosmos.

At an unknown point, the Sakura Cosmos was ruled by the Demon King .

Sectors

  • Dragonfall : This sector is the border separating the Sakura Cosmos from outer space.
  • Emerald Sector (エメラルド宙域 Emerarudo Chū-iki ): This is where Spider 's spaceship was located when he hacked into the Edens Zero .

Planets

In addition to the planets below, several have been mentioned:

  • Excede (エクシド Ekushido ): A planet known to have cats, like Happy , that sell for a lot of money in illegal activities.
  • Genos (ジエノス Jienosu ): Said to be the home to many good-looking men, such as models like Cedric .
  • Pigmoria (ビグモリア Bigumoria ): A planet which has porcine inhabitants.
  • Zorg (ゾルグ Zorugu ): The home planet of Metal Bogey .

Trivia

  • "Sakura" (桜) means Cherry Blossom, a pink flower of many trees of genus Prunus.
