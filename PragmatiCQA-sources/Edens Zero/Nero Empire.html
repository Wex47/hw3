The Nero Empire ( ネロ帝国 , Nero Teikoku ) was a regime that acts as the ruling body of the entire Aoi Cosmos , overseen by Poseidon Nero , the supreme ruler who holds the ultimate authority. It was the biggest organization in the Oración Seis Galáctica .

Description

The Nero Empire was founded at an unspecified time before X492 by Poseidon Nero, who built it in a single generation with the power of his Ether and his Sacred Guiding Dice . There are at least 73 planets that bear the Emperor's name in the empire such as Nero 1 : The Temple , Nero 66 , and Nero 73 . There is also an entire sector in the Aoi Cosmos named after the late Emperor; the Poseidon Nero Sector .

Administration and Government

The state of the former regime was a monarchy, ruled and overseen by Poseidon Nero with his son and heir, Poseidon Shura , set to take Nero's place when he dies of old age. The Nero Empire itself, like any empire, is an autocracy, as such the Emperor's word is law in the Aoi Cosmos. Even though the cosmos has planets with their own monarchs and presidents, the emperor of the Nero Empire outranks every one of them and gets to have the final say in their decisions. Despite Nero's tyrannical rule, the Aoi Cosmos seems to function normally under the Nero Empire's watch and control. The late Emperor employs a group of 6 elite individuals who answer directly to him, acting as his top officials in the empire, known as the Oceans 6 .

History

Foresta arc

When Poseidon Nero finds out about Ziggy causing havoc on the Coral Sector , he deploys Beast Squad 6 and Squad 1 on Foresta to purge the Machines , who turned against the humans by the Demon King's computer virus there, and carried out their mission. However, both squads of the Commando Team are defeated by the Crew of Edens and captured by the Interstellar Union Army respectively.

Assembling the Oceans 6 and his son Shura on The Temple , Nero holds a meeting where he informs them that Foresta has been violated by Ziggy. After hearing their opinions, the Emperor rolls his guiding dice on the table and sees the results with a contemplating look. He leaves the meeting, declaring that he will do nothing much to the surprise of Shura and the Oceans 6. Nero tells his son that he will leave the matter with the Demon King entirely on his hands and that he is not foolish to defy the dice. When the command of Oceans 6 has been officially transferred to Shura by Ijuna, the prince declares to the Oceans members, who are ready to carry out his orders, that he is going to squash Ziggy like a bug and the Demon King will be nothing but a beat-up pile of scrap metal.

Society and Culture

The citizens of the Nero Empire are referred to as the "Children of Nero".

The Nero Empire has several news outlets, such as the Aoi News and the Blue News . These news channels are broadcasted across the Aoi Cosmos and deliver official or special emergency reports on live television. They also hold approval polls where citizens can vote and share their opinions on The Temple and their decisions.

The Nero Empire's despotic actions and unjust treatment of the citizens of the Aoi Cosmos has sparked the birth of a rebel army called Oasis dedicated to freeing the Aoi Cosmos from the tyrannical rule of Poseidon Nero. Its rebel leader Goodwin states that the Nero Empire keeps extending its grasp with millions or tens of millions of people losing their lives and their freedom, and places heavy taxes, oppression, and censorship upon them, plundering the Aoi Sea like they own it. When Jaguar and his fleet invaded Nero 1, Dead Cam and many others are more than willing to join forces with the Interstellar Union Army and mustered their private armies against the Nero Empire and its tyranny.

Military Power

The Nero Empire's military is known as the Imperial Army ( 帝国軍 , Teikoku-gun ) . Like the government of the Nero Empire, it has a hierarchy system, where there are some known rankings such as sergeants , generals , and admirals , and below them are the rank and file soldiers of the army. The Imperial Army also employs child soldiers , alien beings and machines in their ranks.

The Imperial Army soldiers wear full-body light and dark armored suits with togas and face-concealing helmets that have a cyclopean visor and a reverse shark's fin on top of their heads, and they wield assault rifles for combat. Distinguished military officers of the Imperial Army wear full-body armored suits with togas and open face helmets that have a reverse shark's fin on top of their heads or dark formal military uniforms with togas. Troops guarding the Deep Snow Imperial palace also wear dark formal military uniforms with togas and they wield spears and firearms for battle.

The Imperial Army possesses armored cars , high-powered jetpacks , giant airships , and Knight Gear mechanical suits with the Sea Devil as the Nero Empire's latest model in their arsenal. They also possess over 20,000 electronically controlled Imperial anti-matter bombs that are lying unused in The Temple and each of the bombs has enough power to turn a small planet into dust.

The exact size and strength of the Imperial Army are currently unknown, but it is at least large enough to keep the entire Aoi Cosmos under the Nero Empire's tyrannical rule and suppress armed conflicts and rebellions. The Nero Empire possesses a defense fleet of 60,000 ships with the sole purpose of guarding The Temple, with another 100,000 battlecruisers standing by on the capital planet. It is also noted by Elsie Crimson that even the Interstellar Union Army has to be careful in neutral sectors, such as the Coral Sector , controlled by Poseidon Nero , and Fabiano states that a single division of the Interstellar Union Army shouldn't be enough to break through the Imperial defensive zone around Nero 1.

The fleet under Shura's command has over 3,200 ships around Nero 66 on the frontline but according to Eraser , he has no way of knowing if more ships are hiding behind them or on the surface of the planet. Among them is a Kraken -class cruiser, one of the Nero Empire's capital ships, that fires several powerful high-energy laser beams capable of annihilating 42 Interstellar Union Army ships in multiple directions. It is later revealed that there are 10,000 more ships with several aquatic creature themes including Kraken-class cruisers, Megalodon -class mammoth battleships and the Sea Serpent , the most powerful weapon in the Nero Empire , as reinforcements.

Any form of violence in the Aoi Cosmos is tantamount to rebellion against the Nero Empire and will be met with retaliation. Anyone who dares defy the Nero Empire and stands in their way will be deemed an enemy to Poseidon Nero and they would be targeted for elimination.

The Commando Team Beast is divided into 6 individual squads with 3 members each, where Squad 1 is the strongest and highest ranking among them. They are noted to have rather incredible Ether powers in their own right. Some of the Beast members are capable of using Ether Gear , while others are proficient in battle with their mechanical augmentation .

All-Link System

The Nero Empire has a system called the All-Link System ( オールリンクシステム , Ōru Rinku Shisutemu ) , as one of its weapons. When the ocean of the Aoi Cosmos is in high tide every few years, it engulfs every planet. During this event, Ether and electricity combine, connecting all networks in the Aoi Cosmos.
