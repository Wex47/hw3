Description

The Belial Gore is an enormous spaceship, easily dwarfing the Edens Zero in size. The Belial Gore used to be Sector One on Guilst before the disaster forced the ship to escape the lawless planet.

History

Everyone on Belial Gore is in debt to Drakken Joe, the infamous loan shark, to the point they were willing to go after Shiki and his friends when the Dark Alchemist announced that anyone who captures them will be free of their debt.

After the defeat and arrest of Drakken Joe, Belial Gore is currently under investigation by the Interstellar Union Army and the occupants' livelihoods will be protected as members of a free space colony.

Layout

The Belial Gore is large enough to contain an entire city within it, as well as fields surrounding the city on the outside. There are a number of points of interest within the city.

Guest Dock

This is the dock where guests of the Belial Gore arrive. It is closely monitored by Drakken Joe.

Drakken's Residence

A large building, towering above all other nearby buildings in the city, with a spire that somewhat resembles the Belial Gore in design, including a halo hovering over it. It is located in an unspecified location in the city. The building sustained a large amount of damage after Drakken mutated and grew tentacles that ingrained into the building and bursting out of various parts, but remained standing.

Game Belial

Main article: Game Belial

An amusement arcade containing several attractions, such as strength testers, and playing card tables It is located in an unspecified part of the city.

Spica

Main article: Spica

A club with rabbit pole dancers. It became the arena where Homura and Sylph battled in both World No. 29, and World No. 30.

Sibir's Bar

This is a small bar owned and run by Sibir . It is located in an unspecified part of the city.

Materials Loading Gate 02

Materials Loading Gate 02 is an unused materials loading gate and corridor. Its exact location is unknown.

Execution Site

The place where Drakken Joe takes his prisoners to kill them and make an example for those who defy him. It is a dark room with pipes hanging on the tall ceiling and the walls. The execution site is located below Drakken's Residence.

Abilities

Automatic Repair Function : The Belial Gore is capable of repairing itself at high speed in the event of a massive hull breach.

Trivia
