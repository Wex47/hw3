Racer ( レーサー級 , Rēsā-kyū ) is a class of ships in the Interstellar Union Army .

Description

Described to be the fastest ships in the Interstellar Union Army and fleet destroyers, Racer ships are large spaceships with a prominent head, two pairs of wings, one smaller pair above the other, and a pair of turrets under along with Ether shields. The flight deck sits at the back and has a curved roof. Above the front of the ship is a large diamond with a smaller diamond inside. On both sides of the Racer is the Interstellar Union Army logo.

It can fire a barrage of large energy beams from a pair of turrets in battle.

Trivia

  • The ships' name is a nod to Racer , a member of the Oración Seis from Fairy Tail . A character resembling Racer is shown commanding the ships.
