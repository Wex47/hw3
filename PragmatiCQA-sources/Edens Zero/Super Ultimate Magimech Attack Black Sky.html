Super Ultimate Magimech Attack: Black Sky ( 魔械流秘奥技 黒天 , Makai-ryū Hiōgi: Kokuten ) is a Satan Gravity technique.

Contents

  • 1 Description
  • 2 Usage
  • 4 Site Navigation

Description

Described as "Magimech's Ultimate Secret Technique" , the user needs to strengthen the weight of their heart, creating a dark ball of gravity that grows bigger, and slams it at the target.

Usage

Shiki first uses this technique to take down Shura while in Overdrive , defeating him in one blow.
