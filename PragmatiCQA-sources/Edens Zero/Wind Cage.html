Wind Cage ( 風の檻 , Kaze no Ori ) is a Wind Snatch technique.

Contents

  • 1 Description
  • 2 Usage
  • 4 Site Navigation

Description

The user shrinks their opponents and sucks them into a swirling orb of wind. The cage is virtually impossible to break from the inside, and the use of projectiles simply causes them to ricochet across the wind's surface. It also jams communication signals made from within.

The technique can be countered by creating a strong gust in the opposite direction and reverse the flow of wind.

Usage

Kleene first used this technique to trap Rebecca and Happy by surprise after they infiltrated the Belial Gore .

She later used the technique to steal the medicine that Homura was carrying before capturing Homura herself.

In World No. 30 , Kleene attempts to seal Homura through this technique but Homura was able to counter it with her own gust of wind.
