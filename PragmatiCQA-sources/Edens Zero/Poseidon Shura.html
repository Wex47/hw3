Poseidon Shura (ポセイドン・シュラ Poseidon Shura ) was the adopted son of Poseidon Nero of the Oración Seis Galáctica , making him the prince and heir of the Nero Empire . Shura was also a student of Demon King Ziggy , who taught him Satan Gravity . Due to his incredible talent with the Ether Gear , Shura dubbed himself as the King of Gravity (重力の王 Jūryoku no Ō ).

Appearance

Shura is a slim yet well-toned young man with amber eyes, dark eyeliners, studded ears, and spiky green hair with a crew cut. He has a set of distinct, dark-colored tattoos covering half of his upper body. When he is first introduced, Shura is shirtless in a pair of dark-colored pants with a belt, along with a metal chain with a cyclopean skull-shaped pendant around his neck. He has several intricate rings on his fingers and a metal armband on his right wrist. Later on, he wears an open jacket with a fur collar and a symbol of the Nero Empire at the back. When making public appearances, Shura is dressed in a clean, dark suit with a tie.

Personality

Shura is a cruel and sadistic young man who feels entitled to everything in the Aoi Cosmos , including people due to being the son of the emperor. He also murders others in horrific ways just to make an example so people don't get any funny ideas with him.

He is described as a "twisted deadbeat" by Holy and a "spoiled prince" by Eraser. He has been shown to be exasperated by his father always leaving his important decisions to a roll of the dice and letting chance order him around, seeing it as foolish. Shura occasionally acts very jollily and excited when witnessing a large amount of destruction and death. Shura is also very proud of his talent with his Ether Gear, proclaiming himself to be the "King of Gravity".

When in front of Nero and the general populace, Shura effortlessly keeps up the appearance of a respectful son and a charismatic politician.

Shura is also a crafty strategist and manipulator, using the Foresta incident caused by Ziggy to rally public support for the elimination of all robots in the Aoi Cosmos as part of a larger ploy to lure the Demon King into a trap he knows the King of Robots won't be capable of resisting.

However, despite all his clever actions, Shura doesn't appear to be very bright as he still believed everything was going according to plan, nor did he believe Nero's words when his father revealed that he had easily seen through his schemes and transported all of the Antimatter Bombs to Nero 66.

In the past, Shura was a very excitable and innocent child, finding the power of Satan Gravity to be awesome, and constantly asking Ziggy to teach him more.

Synopsis

History

15 years ago, Shura was adopted by Nero who chose him by the guiding dice and intended to pass his power to him, but the boy was incompatible with the Emperor's power. Instead, Shura was taught by Ziggy , an old friend of his father, how to use Satan Gravity. In his first use, the boy is amazed by his gravity powers and excitedly asked the Demon King to teach him more. Although, Shura obeyed his father when he was asked to leave him and Ziggy alone, running off from the room.

Foresta arc

On Nero 73 , Shura is in his luxury room with two girls in his arms where the new girl tries to apologize to him for arriving late for work. He asks her how long has she been working here before the girls beside him tell him that she has only been here for a week and to not pick on her so much. In response Shura asks if they feel bad for her, using his Satan Gravity to make them "fall" to the ceiling, horrifying the new girl in tears. As the blood of the two dead girls spill around him, Shura with a sadistic sneer tells her to not get any funny ideas as he says that women are cheap and everything in the Aoi Cosmos belongs to him.

Attending the meeting with his father Nero and the Oceans 6 on Nero 1 , Shura learns from his father that Foresta has been violated by Ziggy and wonders who that person is. After Nero explains that Ziggy has returned to life but lost his heart, Shura asks his father why would the Demon King do that. The prince is exasperated at his father for using the guiding dice and comments that he does not understand how he can leave important decisions to chance but is told to hold his tongue. After Nero sees the result of rolling the dice and obeys it that he will do nothing, Shura is surprised. He calls out to his father leaving, only to learn that the matter with the Demon King will be left entirely in his hands. Shura asks if his father means that, to which the latter confirms and exits the meeting room. The prince internally remarks with a dark smirk that it is foolish to let the numbers of the dice order his father around, calling Nero a stupid old man. He calls out to his personal secretary Ijuna , who officially transfers the command of Oceans 6 to Shura. When the Oceans members ask him what will be their orders, Shura flattens one of the dice on the table with his gravity-imbued finger and remarks if they even need to ask. With a maniac look, the prince declares to them that he is going to squash Ziggy like a bug and the Demon King will be nothing but a beat-up pile of scrap metal. Shura heads out to find Ziggy and defeat him.

Levitating the objects around him upside down in a room, Shura comments about how Ziggy taught him only the basics, but proudly claims how gifted he is with his gravity powers; enough talent to make him the self-proclaimed "King of Gravity". He is informed by Ijuna that everything is ready for departure to the remote planet Nero 66 where they would search for clues to finding Ziggy. Dropping the items and landing on the floor, Shura apologizes to Cyca , informing the reverend that he can't take him with him. When the reverend tries to reassure him, Shura suddenly grabs Cyca by the head and callously crushes him with gravity in front of the Oceans 6 and his secretary. Smirking, the prince's retort to Fabiano 's outrage is that they're about to fight the "King of Robots" and that he doesn't need robots on his side.

Sandra arc

One week after the Foresta incident, Shura dispatches the Oceans 6 to Salton and five other planets where they massacred all of the innocent bots and covered it up as robot revolts caused by Ziggy on the official Aoi News report. Making a public announcement on live television, Shura in formal attire addresses the people of the Aoi Cosmos as the " Children of Nero " before asking when did they begin to fear the machines. He states that they once had an option to live together in peace but now a virus that affects robots has spread throughout the Aoi Cosmos. The prince passionately declares the human race shall never live in fear of the machine, slamming his fists on the podium with microphones, Shura states that in order to protect the people of the Nero Empire, they've come to a difficult decision. Spreading his arms, the prince proclaims that they'll eliminate all of the bots in the Aoi Cosmos much to the crowd's approval and support. Clenching his fist, Shura declares "Judgement Day" starts now and the age of cowering in fear of these robots has come to an end. At Nero 66 where he sits on his couch casually inside a building, Shura remarks that he knows what Ziggy is after and it is the All-Link System , the system that commands all of the bots in the Aoi Cosmos. The prince smirks as he wonders if Ziggy will take the bait and declares that the King of Robots will be digging his own grave.

At the pool resort in Nero 66 a few days after the consequences of his public announcement on the Nero Empire, Shura is relaxing with four girls in bikinis fawning over him on a large couch, remarking that he is so bored to wait for 3 days until the Aoi High Tide hits. He answers Callum 's question that Ziggy will be here because the Demon King wants the All-Link System and this planet is where he will find it. Shura is notified by Ijuna that he has an uninvited guest approaching Nero 66. He recognizes them to be the Interstellar Union Army and decides with a smirk that this will be the perfect way to kill some time.

Powers and Abilities

Poseidon Shura was the prince and heir of the Nero Empire who is regarded as a real threat to the Interstellar Union Army that is already preparing to take him down with Eraser of the Oración Seis Interstellar leading the full-scale assault before he can ascend to the throne after his father Nero dies of old age. Hermit notes that Shura's Ether numbers are way beyond what she predicted and she then recalculated his battle stats to be four times higher than before.

Satan Gravity (サタングラビティ Satan Gurabiti ): This Ether Gear , taught by Ziggy , gives Shura the ability to alter and change his or other's gravity. He uses it in a much more brutal fashion, such as "dropping" people towards and splattering them on the ceiling. According to Orc , Shura is more powerful than Shiki . Shura noted that 15 years ago, he was taught the basics by Ziggy but discovers that he had enough talent to make him the "King of Gravity" as he casually levitates the objects around him upside down. He is capable of stopping the Edens Zero in mid-air with one hand, much to the Crew of Edens ' shock. His gravity is strong enough to briefly buckle Shiki into the ground with casual effort and Shura finds his attempt of using gravity on him as "lightweight" . Shura states that he and Shiki's gravity powers are a little different for he specializes in moving objects as he is able to lift a large ship buried underground and manipulate the parts with gravity. However, it does not stop him from altering his personal gravity. Shura is able to use gravity to fly in high speeds. His gravity is strong enough to pierce through the Edens Zero's Guarnanium armor with ease and he describes gravity as the "power of destruction" . Witch even notes Shura's gravity to be intense as she and her fellow Shining Stars cannot move under it , and that it is not like that of Shiki's.

  • Heavy Rain (ヘビーレイン Hebī Rein ): Shura uses his gravity to send debris downward at his opponent.
  • Heavy Lance (ヘビーランス Hebī Ransu ): Shura lifts a large rock with gravity and shapes it into a spear before impaling his opponent at full force.
  • Heavy Collision (ヘビーコリジョン Hebī Korijon ): Shura raises the ground beneath his opponent to collide with their body; throwing them into the air and dealing damage at the same time.

Overdrive (オーバードライヴ Ōbadoraivu ): Shura is shown to be capable of using Overdrive, gaining a form similar to that of his adoptive father, Nero. His hair grows into a mass of tentacles, black markings covering the majority of his arms and leads to the sides of his face, where his ears become pointed, and a pair of pants with a fish scale pattern. While in Overdrive, Shura's physical prowess is increased immensely.

Immense Durability : Shura has an impressively durable body, being able to withstand Shiki's two Satan Gravity-empowered punches to the face that sent him crashing into the ground with little sign of injury. He is later shown to be able to block and withstand a direct punch from Shiki in his Overdrive.

Expert Hand-to-Hand Combatant : Shura is skilled in hand-to-hand combat, using his Satan Gravity in conjunction.

Trivia

  • "Shura" is the Japanese name for the Buddhist demigod of war, Asura, and also refers to someone who is in a situation where they must fight endlessly in a relentless and inhuman manner.
  • Shura's design, as well as his sadistic personality, heavily resembles Erigor , a character from Mashima's previous work, Fairy Tail .
