Mechdrasil ( 機械樹 Kikaiju , Mechdrasil ) , is a giant tree, located on the formerly lawless planet Guilst .

Description

Mechdrasil is a gargantuan tree located on Guilst , a result of the planet's abundance in Wood Ether , where it can even be viewed from outer space. The cities on the planet are built on different levels around it. Around the year X442, when Guilst was a popular tourist spot the tree was the main attraction, bringing many visitors just to view it.

Location

The tree is located on the planet Guilst .
