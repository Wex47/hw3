History

Intro arc

After Shiki leeaves Granbell with Rebecca and Happy , Behrman rushes to inform Elsie at the bridge of the Skull Fairy that Ziggy 's adopted grandson has been spotted on Blue Garden , which she tells him to set course to the Planet of Adventurers. Behrman is with Elsie and her small group at the Shooting Starlight guild, surprising the adventurers of their presence. He learns from his leader informing everyone that the planet Norma , where Shiki, Rebecca, and Happy are traveling to, is already dead .

Skull Fairy arc

After Elsie captures the Aqua Wing containing Shiki and his group after escaping Norma, with the intent to sell them as slaves on Guilst , Behrman notes with a smirk that their captives will bring a good price. However when Elsie reveals about the Kawpicatt infestation on the storage ship believed to be the Skull Fairy, a bemused Behrman also reveals that he took pictures of Rebecca in a sticky and compromising situation with the Kawpicatt tentacles, embarrassing the young woman, which his leader calls him an idiot and orders him to delete them. During a space battle between the Interstellar Union Army fleet and Elsie's space pirate fleet, Behrman warns the Crew of Edens that if they do not leave, they will have more trouble than they bargained for.

Edens One arc

After the Skull Fairy saved the Edens Zero from being destroyed by the Edens One , Behrman and Jesse attend a meeting between their leader and the Crew of Edens in the Briefing Room where they explain Elsie's inadequate skill with machines despite her strength and reputation as the Armored Space Pirate, and how their leader became a pirate after stealing food and supplies from multiple spaceports out of desperation.

Foresta arc

After the Skull Fairy arrives in the Coral Sector , Behrman learns from Elsie that Ziggy must have come to the Aoi Cosmos and may have gone to see Poseidon Nero which she hopes she was wrong. Behrman asks why would the Demon King do that which the Armored Space Pirate explains that they are both kings to pay each other respect or to destroy each other. When Elsie accuses Jesse of bringing an Interstellar Union Army fleet led by Justice to their location, Behrman asks what she means. He and his fellow crew members are shocked to learn that Jesse is an agent of the Galactic Intelligence Agency . Behrman asks how long had Elsie known, to which she replies about a month ago. He tells his leader, who intends to send him back to the Angel Feather unharmed, in concern about Jesse giving out all of their secrets to the Cosmic Government but the Princess Guards , Gowen and Hyoga reassure him and the crew that they have made all the important intel untransmittable.

Upon landing on Foresta , Behrman is with Elsie and the crew where they are confronted by a mob of hostile bots which Elsie uses her Star Drain Ether Gear to visibly change her outfit and dispatch them. He is delighted to see such transformation which a flustered Gowen and Hyoga scold him for it. Later on, Behrman shows up in a flying vehicle in the middle of Elsie's fight with Justice to tell his leader that he will get her out of there. He reassures Elsie that Homura will be fine and informs her about a certain situation. After Elsie boards his vehicle, Behrman leaves the battlefield at full speed.

Back at the Skull Fairy, Behrman stands silent as Elsie explains to Shiki and his friends in their holographic screen transmission that the Oración Seis Interstellar are a big deal as each six of them are in a class of their own even within the Cosmic Government and their power supposedly rivals even that of the Oración Seis Galáctica .
