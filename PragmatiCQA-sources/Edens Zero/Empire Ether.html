Empire Ether (エンパイアエーテル Enpaia Ēteru ) is an ability utilized by various Ether Gearists .

Description

Empire Ether is a technique for altering a person's condition. It is a type of power that can forcibly reconfigure another person's Ether and its flow in their bodies to affect their physiology and alter their physical state. Many Ether Gearists from the Aoi Cosmos , including those in the Nero Empire , possesses this type of ability. However, the Empire Ether does have a weakness: If the person can bring the flow of their Ether back to its normal course, they can change back. This can be done by simply knowing themselves, but despite how easy it may sound, it is actually extremely difficult to put into practice.
