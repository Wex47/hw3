Scorpion Missiles ( スコーピオンミサイル , Sukōpion Misairu ) are missiles equipped on the Edens Zero .

Description

Scorpion Missiles are cylindrical missiles launched from the top of the Edens Zero at enemy ships that pursue it.

Usage

During the battle between the Edens Zero and the Edens One , Witch attempts to launch the Scorpion Missiles to deter the pursuing Edens One but Connor intercepts them with his ship's high tech missile defense.
