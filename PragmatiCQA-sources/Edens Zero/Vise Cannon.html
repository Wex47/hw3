Vise Cannon ( バイスキャノン , Baisu Kyanon ) is a Machina Maker technique used in combination with the Arsenal Suit .

Description

While wearing the Arsenal Suit , the user produces a machine gun from the armor's forearm that fires rounds of ammunition. When in-character as Arsenal, Weisz describes this attack as "white bullets of sacred power" .

Usage

Weisz first uses this technique in his battle against Baku but his opponent expertly dodges out of the way of the shots with ease.

In his fight against Laguna , Weisz employs the technique twice against him but both times the bullets are blocked by a wall of water made by his opponent's Ether Gear, Tears Lover .

Weisz uses the technique in his fight with Drakken Joe in Overdrive multiple times, pelting him with a hail of bullets. However, Drakken uses the power of his Ether Gear , Alchemist , to convert the bullets into different matter, rendering the attacks useless against him.

After punching Müller in rage with Metallica Explosion , Weisz uses this technique to bombard the insane scientist, injuring him.

Trivia

  • The attack's name and description are references to the White Dragon Weisslogia from Hiro Mashima 's previous work, Fairy Tail .
