Granbell Kingdom ( グランベル王国 , Guranberu Ōkoku ) is a fantasy theme park on the planet Granbell .

Description

The Granbell Kingdom is ruled by a king and is without any human inhabitants. It was considered a Fantasy World, a Kingdom of Dreams where visitors from other planets come and experience things they dreamed of, before the Machines started breaking down and required frequent maintenance. It is later on called "The Banged Up Theme Park". It then became a place where only Machines lived before Shiki was brought into the Kingdom by the Demon King .

Rebecca and Happy are the first visitors in the last 100 years.

Location

The Granbell Kingdom is on an island surrounded by vast amounts of water.
