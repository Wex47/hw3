Drakken's Organization ( ドラッケンの組織 , Dorakken no Soshiki ) was the criminal organization run by Drakken Joe , one of the Oración Seis Galáctica .

History

By X442, Drakken served as the fixer for countless small-time gangs on Norma, including one run by Weisz's friend Sibir . Following a falling out with Sibir, Weisz Steiner unknowingly stole a briefcase containing the money that belonged to Drakken, mistaking it for Sibir's. Upon discovering the theft, Drakken had Weisz's right arm amputated as punishment, which left a profound impact on Weisz and inspired him to abandon his life of crime.

Shortly afterward, Drakken moved his business away from Norma and found greater success as a loan shark on the beautiful tourist planet Guilst , which then fell into a state of complete depravity under his influence for 50 years.

By X492, the numerous planets ruined in Drakken's wake had earned him a spot among the Oración Seis Galáctica, while his tendency to brutally retaliate against those who defied him solidified his reputation as the "Dark Alchemist", the most powerful and evil figure in the Sakura Cosmos 's criminal underworld.

At some point, Drakken Joe recruited Fie, Laguna, Daichi, and Kleene into his organization as his special forces, the Element 4 because he needed four Ether masters of each element to help him maintain the balance of his Ether .

Guilst arc

Before the Chronophage could eat Guilst's time, the Belial Gore detaches from the planet, with Drakken's group aboard it.

Belial Gore arc

All of the citizens on Belial Gore are in debt with Drakken Joe to the point they were willing to go after Shiki and his friends when the Dark Alchemist formally announced on live broadcast that anyone who captures them will be free of their debt.

After the defeat and arrest of Drakken Joe, the criminal organization officially disbands. An investigation conducted by the Interstellar Union Army surrounding the spaceship occurs as the citizens have been freed from their slavery.
