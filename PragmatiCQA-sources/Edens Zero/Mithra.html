Appearance

Mithra is small in stature and wears a black shawl with lace frills and swirling patterns. She wears lipstick and has a mouth linear in shape, as well as two black circles for eyes. She wears her hair in two large braids framing either side of her face.

Synopsis

History

Mithra was built along with many other machines for the Granbell Kingdom back when it had many visitors. After the park was abandoned a century ago, she and the rest of the machines were left to fend for themselves. By the year X482, when the Demon King discovered an orphaned boy named Shiki , Mithra and the others looked after the boy, who in turn looked after their repairs. When the Demon King's body broke down and he ceased to function, he left Shiki to be looked after by Mithra and the other Granbell machines, requesting that they send Shiki off the planet as he understood that their power supply would soon run out, which would leave Shiki stranded alone.

Intro arc

When the first two guests in 100 years arrive in Granbell, Mithra serves them food and drink. Later, she is one of the three machines that Shiki introduces to Rebecca and Happy . After the introductions are finished, Mithra suggests throwing a party to celebrate the first visitor in Granbell in 100 years. When Shiki falls asleep, Mithra along with the other machines explain to Rebecca exactly why Shiki is living on Granbell before escorting her to bed. The next morning, Mithra, along with the other machines, ties up Rebecca and attacks Shiki before Shiki attacks back to save Rebecca. Mithra and the other machines then pursue Shiki, Rebecca, and Happy as they flee, but they manage to escape. Once they are gone, Mithra and the other machines, who have only attacked Shiki as a ruse in order to make him leave before he ended up alone, express their happiness at him being able to leave before they all cease to function.
