Rickard ( リッカード , Rikkādo ) is the CEO of Dead Cam who protested against Poseidon Shura 's intention to destroy all bots in the Aoi Cosmos .

Appearance

Rickard is a middle-aged man with a bald head and a curved down mustache. He wears a clean striped suit with a white necktie that has a bot emblem, and a right earpiece.

Personality

Rickard is upset at the Nero Empire's despotic decision to eliminate all bots, which severely affects the android manufacturing industry he runs, and calls Shura's actions deplorable. He is willing to join forces with Jaguar and launch an assault with his android army upon the capital planet of the Nero Empire.

Synopsis

Sandra arc

After Shura's public announcement across the Nero Empire , Rickard angrily fumes on live tv that the people have lost all faith in the android manufacturing industry with a line of five bots standing behind him in his office. He declares that this is utterly deplorable and they cannot allow the government to get away with this kind of despotism.

Aoi War arc

Rickard is at Nero 1 where he and his Dead Cam android army joined forces with Jaguar of the Oración Seis Interstellar and his Interstellar Union Army fleet where they landed their troops on the ground for an invasion of The Temple where Poseidon Nero takes refuge at the Deep Snow Imperial palace. When news of Nero's death and Nero 66 's destruction spreads among the demoralized Imperial soldiers, Rickard declares that they have won the war to protect the dignity of machines and orders his Dead Cam bots to not shoot anyone who lays down their weapons.

After the end of the Aoi War, Rickard manages to stop the escalating conflict between humans and machines on Foresta as their mediator, gradually restoring peace and harmony.
