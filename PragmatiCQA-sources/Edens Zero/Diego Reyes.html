Appearance

Diego is an android with the appearance of a large and extremely muscular man with short, light-colored cropped hair and has many metallic plates around his face and over his nose.

He usually wears a dark-colored, sleeveless shirt with a digitized skull on the back,a striped scarf wrapped around his neck, a pair of bands around his elbows, and metallic bracers. He also sports a pair of light-colored pants and black shoes.

Personality

Synopsis

Digitalis arc

Diego and Seth accompanied their boss Drakken Joe over a "meeting" with Murray Morrison over his huge debt to the Dark Alchemist. When Spider enters on the scene, Diego misnames him "Tick" upon trying to remember him from the organization's tech support to the hacker's dismay.

After Seth guns down Spider immediately under Drakken's orders, Diego complements his colleague's excellent marksmanship skills.

Sun Jewel arc

After taking control of Sattelite Blaze, Diego comments that he wouldn't call it a toy, as it is capable of locating and attacking any target. He listens as Drakken is not going to use it to find the Edens Zero, as by coincidence the ship is nearby. He is shown the ship, surprised by how small it is, which Seth points out compared to the Belial Gore. Drakken tells them that they are going to get it by hand-to-hand combat and that they are going in now.

Belial Gore arc

World No. 29

Diego, Maria, and Seth break into the Edens Zero after changing the Ether shields into gas and the ship's metal hull into paper. They exit their ship and Diego warns Maria not to break anything that looks valuable.

After infiltrating the Edens Zero , Diego and his companions confronted Witch Regret and were defeated by her. Diego apparently blew himself along with Seth to avoid interrogation. Later on, it is revealed Diego used Stealth Mode to conceal himself and snuck up on Connor , holding the captain captive at gunpoint while Seth took over the ship from the inside and disabled the Four Shining Stars.

World No. 30

After infiltrating the Edens Zero, Diego and his companions were once again defeated by Witch Regret. But this time, Rebecca Bluegarden warned her fellow crewmates of their attempts to fool them with their defeat and take over the ship from within like in they did in World No. 29 and thwarted them, much to their shock.

Powers and Abilities

Enhanced Strength : With pure physical strength, Diego is capable of ripping through machinery with ease.

Stealth Mode ( 光学迷彩 ( ステルス ) モード Suterusu Mōdo ): Diego is capable of camouflaging his entire body to make himself unseen. When combined with Seth Anderson 's self-destruct mechanism, he is capable of feigning his own death with this technology.

Equipment

Guns : Diego is show to own a gun in his fight with Witch . It is switched out for a smaller gun during the infiltration of the Edens Zero .
