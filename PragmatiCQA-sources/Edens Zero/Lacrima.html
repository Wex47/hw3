Lacrima ( 星水晶 ( ラクリマ ) Rakurima ), also known as Celestial Tears (星の涙 Hoshi no Namida ), is a rare ore of highly condensed Ether that is found on Sun Jewel .

Description

Lacrima is a crystalline ore created when Ether becomes highly condensed through unknown means. On rare occasions, it is found among metal ore produced by Stones , and is noted for being extremely valuable.

Lacrima can be used to create Aquatic Adaptation Lacrima , allowing humans to breathe and talk underwater while wearing them.

Trivia

  • Lacrima shares its name with a type of crystal from Fairy Tail that is capable of being infused with magic power.
