Aoi Crystal ( アオイクリスタル , Aoi Kurisutaru ) is an ore that can be found in the Aoi Cosmos .

Description

Aoi Crystal is an ore that is notable for its hardness. Fabiano utilizes it to knock out his opponents and harden his body with his Crystal Ether Gear .
