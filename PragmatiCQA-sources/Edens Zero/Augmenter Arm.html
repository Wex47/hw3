The Augmenter Arm (強化アーム Kyōka Āmu ) is a type of weapon utilized by Sibir. It was given to him by its creator, Weisz Steiner .

Description

The Augmenter Arm is a piece of equipment that is attached to the user's right arm, hereby granting them increased physical attributes.

History

The arm was used by Sibir during his tenure as leader of the Sibir Family in Norma's slums. It allowed him assert his dominance over the other gang members and even briefly do battle with Shiki Granbell . Although it was eventually destroyed by Weisz, who regretted giving it to Sibir.

Abilities

Enhanced Strength : While the arm is equipped, Sibir gains a tremendous amount of physical strength that is enough to shatter concrete and launch him off the ground at great speed.
