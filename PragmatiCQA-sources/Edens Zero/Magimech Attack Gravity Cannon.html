Magimech Attack: Gravity Cannon ( 魔械流 重弾 , Makai-ryū: Jūdan ) is a Satan Gravity technique.

Contents

  • 1 Description
  • 2 Usage
  • 4 Site Navigation

Description

The user creates a large sphere of energy made of gravity from one of their fists and throws it at the target, pushing them back due to the heaviness of the sphere.

The user can also create various small spheres of the sort to combat multiple targets.

Usage

Shiki first uses this technique to defeat Sibir as a finishing move.

Shiki uses this technique on a small scale to push back Ganoff and attack Sister who uses Jinn as her human shield during their fight. Shiki uses the technique again to send the said three members of Rogue Out flying.
