Weisz Steiner ( ワイズ・シュタイナー , Waizu Shutainā ) is an Adventurer and a member of the crew of Edens Zero , as well as their mechanical engineer. He is also a former thief from the Planet Norma and the younger version of Professor Weisz from the year X442 . He later uses the alias "Arsenal" , when doning an armored suit .

Appearance

Weisz is a tall young adult with a slim build, dark eyes and short blond hair. He has a few bangs brushed to his right and a single earring is worn on his left ear. His initial outfit consists of a long-sleeved, light-blue coat with an open high collar and dark-colored shoulder pads over a black turtleneck. Underneath is a short-sleeved white shirt with black sleeves, sports a pair of dark trousers, and plain, white boots.

Weisz later changes into a mid-sleeved jumpsuit with a chest plate and shoulder pads connected and a held by a leather belt with a pocket on the left. During the rescue mission on Guilst, he is shown to have possession of an assault rifle. After the mission, he starts to wear a light-colored hooded jacket with a black undershirt, a pair of long, dark-colored jeans and shoes.

When entering Digitalis, Weisz's avatar is a curvaceous woman with long, light-colored hair and a dark skin tone. He later switches this avatar for one of himself in a light-colored hooded jacket with black flame-like designs on the end of the sleeves, a pair of knee-length light-colored shorts, and open-toed sandals.

During the Mildian arc, Weisz changes into an outfit similar to a mafia don: A long-sleeved fur coat over a fancy suit with a tie, black dress pants, and leopard-print shoes. He also wears a pair of dark sunglasses.

After his battle with the Zaiten Three 's Baku , Weisz later changes into a dark-colored shirt, a pair of light-colored jeans and shoes. His hair is now in a messy style.

During the Belial Gore arc, Weisz wears a new attire similar to his original. It consists of a long-sleeved, high collared white jacket with black designs on the upper torso and shoulders and sleeves that ends at the elbows. Underneath is a long-sleeved, dark-colored turtleneck with white zigzag designs on the bottoms and white lines on the collar's sides. He also sports a pair of light-colored trousers held by two belts, a holster on his right leg, and lastly, black shoes.

Personality

Unlike his future self, Weisz is a selfish and stubborn person who only cared for himself, acted as if he didn't truly care about the others around him. As a thief, he would steal items he deemed worthy of value. However, it is shown that Weisz does care about his friends and he shows concern for others in his own way such as partially fixing Happy after he was damaged, showing concern for Rebecca when she was kidnapped, saving Sister while she was being held captive, and displaying suspicion of Homura when she showed knowledge of the Edens Zero.

Weisz takes pride in his handsome looks as he was shocked and dismayed when Rebecca showed him the picture of his older counterpart from 50 years later.

As Arsenal, Weisz tends to act heroic and bombastic with flashy speeches, insisting to his crewmates that he is being in-character of his superhero persona.

It is shown that Weisz does have a perverted side such as when he checked out Homura's rear, planned to put Homura in a bunny girl costume when he filled his end of the bargain between them, showing interest when hearing Homura had one of Witch's massages and staring at Rebecca's chest.

The only thing he still shares with his alternate self is their love for their late mother, who was among the victims killed by Drakken Joe .

Synopsis

History

Weisz lived on the planet Norma with his mother . At some point, his mother fell gravely ill with a sickness that even the doctors couldn't reasonably explain. Despite Weisz's pleas, his mom did end up dying due to the sickness.

Sometime after his mother's death, Weisz bought a used robot in order to get a friend. The robot knew lots of stuff and it even taught Weisz how to use the Machina Maker Ether Gear after three years of training. Unfortunately after learning the Ether Gear, he was not very good at using it and ended up breaking his robot teacher by accident.

Many years later, Weisz ended up becoming a thief and found himself part of a gang of criminals lead by Sibir . Weisz and Sibir were good friends and Weisz even showed him the pendant his mother gave him. However, the pendant was stolen and Weisz accused Sibir since he was the only one Weisz told about. After Sibir denied stealing it, Weisz claimed that the gang wasn't his crew, and he was kicked out of the gang. Weisz then promised Sibir that he would steal something he cared about.

Shortly after the falling out, Weisz stole a case from Sibir that he believed was full of money.

Norma arc

Weisz first appears drawing a gun on Shiki , Rebecca , and Happy when they enter his home looking for The Professor . Then announcing that he is Weisz Steiner, confusing the group. As he continues to question Shiki and co. on who they are, the young man accuses them of working for Sibir . Then after Shiki sees someone upstairs, Weisz quickly leaves on his motorbike. Afterwards he is seen clutching a suitcase, stating he will never let Sibir have it.

Rebecca and Shiki later find him at his favorite bar and explain that they are friends of his future self. Shiki then proceeds to steal his suitcase after learning Weisz is a thief. Shiki accidentally opens the suitcase revealing it contained an android, not money as Weisz thought. Weisz then deduces that Sibir was planning on using Pino to make money and decides to follow her in order to steal the score.

Weisz had continues to follow Pino until he is there to witness her return to Sibir and his new recruits, the Foote Brothers . When Sibir threatens to break Pino's other leg, Weisz intervenes, claiming that since he stole her she belongs to him. Then when Shiki breaks into the hideout, Weisz uses the confusion to steal Rebecca's vehicle, along with Pino and Happy and make his escape.

He was later seen speeding down the street, pursued by Sibir in his mech Knight Gear . When the bike he is riding is blown up, he tries to convince Pino to use her EMP before they were saved by Shiki. While Shiki and Sibir battled, Weisz admitted that he was the one that created Sibir's Augmenter Arm . After the battle he feigns shooting Sibir only to then destroy the Augmenter Arm while it is still attached to the gangster. In the aftermath Weisz attempts to convince Shiki to reveal the outcome of major sports games in the future before the police arrived to arrest him.

Later on, Weisz sneaks onto the Aqua Wing , and offers to help the crew escape the police in exchange for ownership of the ship. When Rebecca agrees, he uses his Ether Gear Machina Maker to restructure the ship and escape the atmosphere, as well repair Pino's leg. After Weisz pilots them through the spacetime wall they get a call from Professor Weisz who explains the Chronophage, its effects, and how it makes him and X442 Weisz different entities. The Aqua Wing is then caught in a tractor beam.

Skull Fairy arc

When Elsie Crimson reveals herself as the one who took captured their ship, Weisz comments on her good looks and she precedes to both compliment and insults his skills as a pilot. Then she announces her intentions to sell them into slavery at Guilst and Weisz tries to convince her to let him join her pirate crew like a mechanic. Thereon, Shiki announces his intentions to raid Elsie's ship and Weisz decides to tag along. Upon entering the ship Rebecca is tied up by alien tentacles, Weisz takes the time to ogle at her before being captured himself, and when she frees herself first she begins to strip him comically in retaliation.

After finding Shiki and the defeated parasite copy of Elsie, Weisz is elated by the news that the real Elsie intends to give Shiki the ship. His and Shiki's minds were then blown when she revealed the full size of her fleet, which is then attacked by the Interstellar Union Army . Weisz pulls a lever to help them escape, which causes them to fast travel and he is amazed by the new technology of the future.

Guilst arc

While traveling to Blue Garden the new crew decides to clean the parasites off the ship together and Weisz complains about the work despite not being asked to help. Later while they all show off the new outfits they found, Rebecca asks him about how to learn ether gear but he claims there is no point because it takes years and is annoyed when Shiki proclaims he learned it in a month. Then when Shiki accidentally awakens Witch , Weisz insults her ability to maintain the ship causing her to restore the entirety of the Edens Zero . While exploring the ship he and Shiki come across CORD 3173 and Weisz attempts to open the door with Machina Maker but is unable to open it. Witch then announces the area is off-limits over the comms and Weisz argues with her over the eavesdropping that invades privacy, getting her to stop.

When they finally land on Blue Garden, Weisz is amazed by the changes from his time and in excitement, leaves to go explore the future. In the wake of Rebecca's kidnapping he suddenly appears on the Edens Zero alongside Homura . He proceeds to explain to Shiki that while wandering around the guild he saw the news report of the kidnappings plus Shiki's response but then overheard Homura mention the Edens Zero. He proceeded to question her and she claimed she wants to challenge the Demon King and will help them rescue Rebecca in exchange. So he brought her to the ship while adding another mystery condition. Pino then questions his intentions for returning and he offendedly proclaims he has to help Rebecca.

Upon arriving on Guilst Pino thanks Weisz for repairing the damaged Happy. However, he claims that the current him must be a genius as he was only able to repair the external damage. Later while wandering the streets Weisz claims that the planet makes Norma's slums look good. Then when they find Rogue Out 's base, Weisz refuses to enter without a plan and the others leave him behind.

Powers and Abilities

Machina Maker (マキナ・メイカー Makina Meikā ): By using this Ether Gear , Weisz can control and manipulate machines. He is shown to be able to change or remodel all kinds of machines to his liking, such as upgrading a spaceship or recreating E.M. Pino 's missing leg. After training for seven days, Weisz is now able to alter electronic devices in an instant similar to that of Drakken Joe 's Alchemist . When using Ether Gear on his arms, Weisz's Ether color is yellow.

  • Million Bullets (ミリオンバレット Mirion Baretto ): Weisz touches the floor and modifies it to create numerous guns that he can activate remotely and fire at a selected target.
  • Atlas Flame (アトレスフレイム Atoresu Fureimu ): Weisz touches his weapon and modifies it into a flamethrower that shoots a large stream of flames.
  • Mortal Crash (モータルクラッシュ Mōtaru Kurasshu ): Weisz touches an object while using his Ether Gear, engulfing them with Ether markings, and detonates them with a fiery explosion, destroying them in the process.
  • Arsenal-Enhanced Machina Maker : When Weisz equips his Arsenal Suit it draws out the potential of his Ether Gear , thus allowing him access to more advanced techniques. However, overuse of these abilities decreases the amount of time he can keep Arsenal active.
    • Vise Cannon (バイスキャノン Baisu Kyanon ): While wearing his Arsenal Suit , Weisz produces a machine gun from his forearm plating that fires rounds of physical ammunition.
    • Drum Buster (ドラムバスター Doramu Basutā ): While wearing his Arsenal Suit, Weisz reconfigures the forearm of his suit into a cannon that fires an explosive blast.
    • Metallica Explosion (メタリカエクスプロージョン Metarika Ekusupurōjon ): While wearing his Arsenal Suit, Weisz greatly increases the size of his forearm and punches with a high level of heat energy, creating a massive explosion on impact.
    • Grandy-Ray : While wearing his Arsenal Suit , Weisz produces a laser from his forearm plating that fires a ray beam at his target.
    • Grandee Wing (グランディウィング Gurandi Wingu ): While in his Arsenal Suit, Weisz creates a wing-like blade from his forearm playing that is strong enough to slash an O-Tech in half.

Overdrive (オーバードライブ Ōbādoraibu ): During his battle with Nasseh, Weisz is shown to be able to access Overdrive. He gains an overall mechanical appearance, where his neck, hands, and feet gain intricate mechanical seams, round ear cups, and four dark-colored tentacles sprouting from his back. His hair becomes blue, and is slid back where several Ether lines go across his right eye. His upper legs and parts of his arms become darkened and gain light-colored Ether markings. His outfit inverts in color, where the dark-colored robes of his Arsenal Suit Mark 2 turn into a lighter color.

  • Overdrive-enhanced Machina Maker : Through Overdrive, Weisz's Ether Gear capabilities are augmented to the point where he can reconfigure the floor and launch multiple bullets at high speeds simultaneously.

Hand-to-Hand Combatant : Weisz has basic skills in hand-to-hand combat. When using the Arsenal Armor, his fighting skills are significantly augmented.

Genius Engineer : Even in the past, Weisz is shown to be a prodigious engineer, quickly able to understand most machinery he's come into contact with, which goes hand-in-hand with his Ether Gear ability.

Piloting Skills : Weisz is an excellent pilot, shown to be capable of flying the Aqua Wing past the nearly impenetrable spacetime wall around Norma with ease, which Elsie praises him for at the moment.

Expert Marksman : After training with Xenolith, Weisz's marksmanship has greatly improved.

Equipment

Aqua Wing (アクアウイング Akua Uingu ): A spaceship formerly owned by Rebecca Bluegarden, given to Weisz in exchange for escaping Norma. It resembles a fish and can surf on water, levitate and travel in space.

Assault Rifle : During the rescue mission on Guilst , Weisz is shown carrying an assault rifle which he uses his Machina Maker on to modify into a flamethrower.

Arsenal Suit (アーセナルスーツ Āsenaru Sūtsu ): With Hermit 's help, Weisz constructed a weapon that he can control through a bracelet. When activated, his entire body becomes covered in a powered suit of armor that harnesses the power of his Ether Gear. While donning this armor, he takes on a superhero alias called "Arsenal".

Disguise App : An application on Weisz' device which utilizes the Edens Zero's dress factory tech, allow for simple clothing changes to even full body disguises on himself and others.

Aquatic Adaptation Lacrima ( 水中適合の 星水晶 ( ラクリマ ) , Suichū Tekigō no Rakurima ) : During his stay on Red Cave , Weisz wore one of these devices which allowed him to breathe and communicate underwater without any issues.
