Description

Coral is a sector of space that, like most of the Aoi Cosmos, has a large volume of water, comparable to a sea, and has a variety of space fish. True to its name, the sector also houses a variety of space corals that appears to float on the water's surface. It is said to be a neutral sector, where the government army must be careful.

Location

This sector is located in an unspecified part of the Aoi Cosmos.
