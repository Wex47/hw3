Audrey ( オードリー , Ōdorī ) is a human from Foresta and fiancé of Allen . After the robots took over the Verdant Planet she became the pet of Kevin .

Appearance

Audrey is a slender young woman, who wears her hair short. She wears a dark tank top with pale trousers that are threadbare at the end. Due to the neglect of her robot master Kevin, she has gotten bruised and dirtied.

Personality

After being poorly treated by Kevin, Audrey is shown to have been mentally broken. Her treatment ranging from being made to walk on all fours, beaten for talking, forced to eat dog food, and live in filth, caused her to break into tears at her situation and give up on life.

Synopsis

Foresta arc

While out on a walk with Kevin, the latter is greeted by Willem walking Allen. Kevin notes how healthy Willem's human pet's condition is in contrast to his own. Audrey is asked by her former fiancé if she is alright, confesses that she could not go on anymore. For her disobedience, Kevin repeatedly kicks her. The woman's abuse is momentarily paused when Willem urges Kevin that violence should not be allowed, noting humans are not like robots and could easily break. Kevin refuses to accept that and shoots Willem in the head, killing him. In the distraction, Allen takes the chance to carry Audrey and escape.
