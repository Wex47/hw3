Description

Alchemist allows the user to change any material that they touch into something else such as changing the metal of a ship such as Guarnanium into paper.

The user can convert their own body into a different material for defensive purposes, such as when Drakken Joe converts his hand into Dramour Ore , or iron. Another defensive option is to change incoming projectiles into other forms to lessen the damage. The user has a variety of offensive actions such as converting their opponent's body into solid stone to petrify them. , changing materials into explosives , making electricity flow and turning the environment on his opponents, like making the floor into Glia Tar .
