Description

Tears Lover is a type of Ether Gear that allows the user to manipulate the element of water and transmute their target's bodies into said element through their tears.

People who have been transformed into water by this power can still be allowed to roughly maintain their original form if an Ether coating is applied to them. While in this watery state, they can still utilize their own Ether Gear as well as survive high temperatures.
