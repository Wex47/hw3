The Empire Dice (帝国歴程 Enpaia Daisu ) are Relics that assure the user's victory.

Description

They are a set of three dice, consisting of a d6, d8, and d12 die, that possess mystical powers to assure the user's victory. However, it always comes at a price, thus the more certain the victory, the greater the price has to be. They are also unaffected by gravity . When thrown, they are able to pierce through the user's target with ease and return to their hand.
