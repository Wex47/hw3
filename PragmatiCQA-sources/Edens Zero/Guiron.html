Guiron ( ギロン , Giron ) is the commander of the Magical Beast Army and event boss in the Digitalis RPG Rogue Fantasia . Ordinarily a sub-boss , he is reprogrammed by Jamilov to follow his orders.

Appearance

Guiron is an avian humanoid monster in armor with large wings and talons for feet.

Personality

Due to being reprogrammed by Jamilov, Guiron addresses him as a Lord and follows his orders. He believes that it is a great honor to be chosen to become willing experience fodder for Jamilov after they take Crysta for themselves. He gets peeved when someone brings up the fact that he is just a side-character without a backstory, something he admits that he is very insecure about.

Synopsis

Digitalis arc

When the Magical Beast Army approaches the city of Crysta , Guiron lands on top of the tiny imp soldier who was blowing the war horn while riding on the giant beast, turning him into a bloody stain. He introduces himself to the NPC citizens and declares that by the order of "Lord" Jamilov, the army will be taking Crysta for themselves. He explains that Jamilov is bored with killing NPCs so he allows the army to turn Crysta into a city of monsters, but states that he wants them to capture the players so he can execute them personally. Guiron answers Shiki's question that once they take over the whole city, Jamilov plans to entertain himself by slaughtering all of the Magical Beast Army. Guiron believes that it is a wonderful honor to be chosen to be killed for experience points by Jamilov and he and his forces will gladly give their lives to him. He asks his brethren if they are ready and orders them to take Crysta and kill all of the NPCs.

Overseeing the battle between his army and the Crew of Edens from the sky, Guiron orders his forces to seize the players and kill the NPCs. When Trollburglar shows up to protect the people of Crysta as its guardian, Guiron tries to mock him as a sub-boss for defying Jamilov but then gets upset after the Crysta Boss reminds him that he is a sub-boss too and a side character that does not even have a backstory, feeling very insecure about it. Suddenly, Guiron gets kicked in the face by Shiki and crashes on the ground unconscious.
