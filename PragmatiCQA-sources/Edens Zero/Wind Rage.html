Wind Rage ( ウィンドレイジ , Uindo Reiji ) is a form of Wind Ether Gear that utilizes wind in a manner similar to a typhoon.

Description

Wind Rage allows the user to manipulate the wind, allowing them to move swiftly, float on air, vanish with the wind, fire destructive wind blasts, and create spinning wind blades in a form of tornadoes. According to Jinn, he claims that his wind allows him to obstruct anything away from him.

The user utilizes the Skymech Ninjutsu fighting style in conjunction with Wind Rage.
