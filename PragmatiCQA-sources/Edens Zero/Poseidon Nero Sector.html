Poseidon Nero ( ポセイドン・ネロ , Poseidon Nero ) is a sector in the Aoi Cosmos that is home to a variety of space fish. It is named after Poseidon Nero of the Oración Seis Galáctica .

Description

Poseidon Nero is a sector of space that has a large volume of water, comparable to a sea, and is swarming with a large variety of space fish, including a ginormous toothed-fish where Edens Zero is only about the size of its eye.

Location

This sector is located on the border of the Aoi Cosmos, and is close to Dragonfall , the border of the Sakura Cosmos .

Trivia

  • Poseidon is the god of the sea in Greek mythology , while Nero was a Roman emperor, whose rule is usually associated with tyranny and extravagance.
