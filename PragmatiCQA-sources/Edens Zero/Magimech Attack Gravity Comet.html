Magimech Attack: Gravity Comet ( 魔械流 重彗星 , Makai-ryū: Jūsuisei , lit. "Magic Machine Style: Heavy Comet") is a Satan Gravity technique.

Contents

  • 1 Description
  • 2 Usage
  • 4 Site Navigation

Description

The user's feet is enhanced by gravity, increasing the speed and momentum of their descent from above, causing sheer force and blunt damage onto their target.

Usage

Shiki in his rudimentary Overdrive first used this technique to defeat a Kawpicatt S4 impersonating Elsie Crimson inside the "Skull Fairy" , destroying several floors of the ship.

Shiki uses this technique to attack Drakken Joe but the latter is able to block it with one of his arms that are coated in iron by his Ether Gear , Alchemist .

Ziggy uses this technique to finish off Elsie but Justice intervenes and manages to save her with Meteor , surprising the Demon King.
