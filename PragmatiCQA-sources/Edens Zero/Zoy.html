Zoy ( ゾーイ , Zōi ) is a member of Oasis , a rebel army formed to oppose the tyrannical rule of Poseidon Nero .

Appearance

Zoy is a tall man with a stout build, dark shaggy hair, and a mustache. He wears a dark sleeveless shirt with light suspenders, pants, and shoes. Additionally, he has bandages wrapped around his left arm.

Personality

Zoy is a rash man who unintentionally led the Imperial soldiers to the Oasis hideout out of panic.

Synopsis

Sandra arc

Zoy barges into the Oasis base where he informs the others that there is an enemy attack by Imperial soldiers at the marketplace where they started hunting the bots there. However, he is quickly shot on his right shoulder by one of the Imperial soldiers who followed him to the hideout and begin their assault upon them as Furena calls him out in shock and concern.
