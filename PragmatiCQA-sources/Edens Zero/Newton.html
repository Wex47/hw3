Newton ( ニュートン , Nyūton ) , also known as the Planet of Science ( 科学の惑星 , Kagaku no Wakusei ) , is a planet that is occupied by scientists and researchers.

Description

Newton is a giant planet covered by numerous solar panels with a tower and city underneath, has apples circling around it, and a large tree on top of it.

History

After leaving the Four Shining Stars , Hermit Mio came to Newton where she was tricked by Müller and his associates to help them create the Ether Accelerator Prototype Number 6 that was used to destroy planet Hook and its robot inhabitants.

Two years later, the Interstellar Union Army conducted a raid on the Müller Royal Laboratories in Newton to arrest Müller and his fellow scientists under the charge of genocide, and several other illegal activities they've committed while rescuing Hermit from captivity.

Location

Newton is located next to the now destroyed Hook in an unspecified part of the Sakura Cosmos .

  • The planet's name and orbiting apples is a reference to the scientist Isaac Newton who developed his theory of gravitation by watching the fall of an apple from a tree.
