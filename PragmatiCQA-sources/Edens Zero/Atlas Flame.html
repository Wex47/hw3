Atlas Flame ( アトラスフレイム , Atorasu Fureimu ) is a Machina Maker technique.

Description

The user modifies a weapon and configures it into a flamethrower strong enough to shoot a powerful stream of flames. While in-character as Arsenal, Weisz describes the attack as "royal power enrobed in flames" .

Usage

Weisz first used the technique after modifying his assault rifle into a flamethrower to incinerate the rapidly growing trees after its Wood Ether went out of control on the planet Guilst due to Chronophage 's arrival.

Weisz modifies the arm of his Arsenal Suit to create a flamethrower in his fight against Laguna of the Element 4 , surrounding them both in a ring of fire to cut off his escape and force Laguna to cry from the intense smoke.

Trivia

  • Atlas Flame shares its name with the Hell Fire Dragon of the same name from Hiro Mashima 's previous work, Fairy Tail .
