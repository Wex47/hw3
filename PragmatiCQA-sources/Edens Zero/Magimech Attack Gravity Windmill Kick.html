Magimech Attack: Gravity Windmill Kick ( 魔械流 重旋脚 , Makai-ryū Jūsen Kyaku , lit. "Magic Machine Style: Heavy Rotating Leg") is a Satan Gravity technique.

Description

The user's leg begins to glow and, while in midair, the gravity enhanced leg is aimed at the target; delivering a kick powerful enough to kick back multiple opponents.

Usage

Shiki first used this technique to instantly defeat the Foote Brothers and save Rebecca .
