Five Sword Fencing: Leopard Stance (五形剣 豹の構え Gokeiken: Hyō no Kamae lit. "Five-Form Sword: Leopard Stance") is a Soul Blade technique used by Homura Kōgetsu .

  • 2 Usage

Description

The user makes a stance and splits her Ether sword into two before attacking her opponents with multiple slashes. Its stance is based on leopard that uses swift legs to hunt the prey.

Usage

Homura uses this technique to defeat multiple Rogue Out soldiers with ease.
