Description

The Spa of Eden is a giant round bath located inside Edens Zero. It is enclosed by walls and has a big tree growing in the middle. The tree is inside a structure with water flowing out of it. The spa has many leaves and berries in it. The spa is very hot around 42°C (107.6°F), and steam seems to be pouring out of it. It was named by Rebecca Bluegarden .

Location

The Spa of Eden is located behind the figurehead of the Edens Zero inside a glass dome.
