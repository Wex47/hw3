Description

Anti-Ether Mines are energy mines launched from holes on the sides of the Edens Zero at enemy ships that explode after a short while.

Usage

The Crew of Edens attempts to use the Anti-Ether Mines to bombard the pursuing Edens One and try make their escape but Connor sees through it and presses on the attack, boasting that no ship in the cosmos can outrun him.
