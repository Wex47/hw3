Laguna Husert (ラグナ・ヒューゼルト Raguna Hyūzeruto ) is an actor from the Aoi Cosmos and was a member of the Element 4 that served under Drakken Joe , being known as the "Spirit of Water" (水の精霊 Mizu no Seirei ). He is a former member of the rebel army, Oasis , having left it for a journey to prepare himself against Poseidon Nero . After the Aoi War, he officially joins the crew of the Edens Zero .

Appearance

Laguna is a tall, slim and effeminate young man with short, light-blue wavy hair, matching eyes with eyelashes, and small, full light-blue lips. His attire consists of a leather jacket with a key-shaped insignia at the center of it, checkered sleeves, a pair of leather trousers, and black stilettos . Additionally, he wears a pair of thin rectangular-shaped earrings.

During his stay on the Edens Zero as a guest, he wears a long-sleeved, high-collared dark-colored coat with a pair of zippers on the front and back of its sleeves over a v-neck shirt, a pair of light-colored trousers, and wears his signature stilettos. On the planet Sandra, he wears a shirt with a unique circular pattern around the collar with teardrop symbols below and a triangular cut near the bottom with three straps connecting the two halves.

After the timeskip , his hair has become bushy and scruffier, and now wears a dark-colored turtleneck.

Personality

Laguna is shown to be a kind person, especially when compared to the other members of the Elemental 4 and Drakken's crew. While most laugh alongside Drakken when he commits cruel acts, Laguna holds a solemn look. He also attempts to comfort Rebecca during her imprisonment.

In battle, he is quite a crafty trickster, goading his opponents into crying by any means in order to use his Ether Gear against them, such as making up a sob story or using an Oedo Onion as a last resort.

Synopsis

History

Laguna used to be an actor at some point in his life alongside his best friend, Harold . However, when Poseidon Nero ’s empire invaded his home planet, Laguna became a member of Oasis , a rebel army against Nero's tyranny after Harold died during the invasion. During first time with Oasis, he befriended with many of its members, including the "Princess", Ijuna . When one of his allies, including Ijuna's father were killed by Poseidon's army as well, he left Oasis and ventured into the Sakura Cosmos , in order to become strong enough to defeat Nero. He eventually joined Drakken's Organization and became part of the Element 4 as the Dark Alchemist needed Ether masters of each element to help maintain the balance of his Ether. He used his real name as a codename.

When Weisz approaches Laguna, he steps on Weisz's foot with his stilettos, drawing tears from him and he turns Weisz into water. Homura draws her Soul Blade sword and Laguna summons a water blade, using his Ether Gear, and the two engage in combat. Laguna then attempts a number of tricks to get Homura to cry. He eventually succeeds by crushing an Oedo Onion in her face and turns her into water. Surprised, Laguna asks Weisz what he is wearing before being punched in the face by his armored opponent who reveals it to be Arsenal Mark I, proclaiming to be the hero who soars across the cosmos. He blocks Weisz's bullets with his Ether Gear, telling him not to underestimate the power of water. Directing the water, Laguna attacks Weisz and damages his suit in retaliation, stating that water cuts through metal. The Spirit of Water assaults Weisz with a water whip, pushing him back. He raises the water to block Weisz's Vise Cannon and says that such little pellets will not work on him. Laguna dodges Weisz's Drum Buster with water as his transportation, surfing away to safety. Hearing Weisz about to make another hero speech with another weapon, Laguna asks him if he can load his gun without doing that. When Weisz unleashes Atlas Flame , Laguna believes him to be stupid if he thinks the flamethrower is going to work against his Ether Gear only to learn that his opponent made a ring of fire around them. Trapped, Laguna is shocked that the fire is everywhere and coughs with tears from the smoke which Weisz notices. Wiping away the tears in annoyance at his opponent messing with him, the Spirit of Water states that he will flush Weisz down the toilet after making him cry and turn him into water. Laguna blocks Weisz's Metallica Explosion with his Water Hand and blasts him with Water Shot , injuring his opponent and further damaging his suit. Ready to finish Weisz off, Laguna unleashes Water Laser which his opponent tries to block with the Metallica Explosion gauntlet. Laguna tells Weisz to not bother for his water can crush iron and it is time for him to weep and wail in defeat. Changing his mind with sadistic glee, he proclaims that Weisz will already be dead before he gets the chance to cry. However, Laguna is shocked to see Weisz using the cooling system of his suit to freeze the Water Laser, leaving himself wide open. Later on, Laguna regains consciousness at some point and turns Rebecca into a puddle of water by using her tears, saving her from Daichi's torture with Pain's Tree inside the prison cell. Laguna is with his fellow Element 4 and Drakken Joe at the Execution Site where Shiki and his friends are held captive and at gun point before them. When Drakken destroys the Sister Energy meant for Weisz and laughs, the actor did not laugh with Fie and Daichi, remaining silent along with Jinn and Kleene. Seeing Rebecca cry, he sees her tears which Daichi tells him that it is not the time to not turn her into water. Laguna understands and comments that it is real tears he is seeing. The Spirit of Water stays silent with Daichi as Drakken lectures Shiki and his friends that there are some people in the cosmos they should not mess with. He listens in silence when Shiki in tears tells his friends that it was not supposed to be like this in their adventure and tries to stand up to Drakken. After the Dark Alchemist shoots Shiki in the head for his defiance, Laguna remains impassive while Fie and Daichi sadistically smirk.

A week after Shiki's death, Laguna visits Rebecca's room and finds her cowering, reassuring her that it's just him and not his boss. He tries to get her to eat and that sitting around won't change anything. He informs her that they were told to treat her like one of them and advises her to forget the past, saying that if she stays she will get everything she could want. He reminds Witch to not forget it. Powers and Abilities

Tears Lover (ティアーズラバー Tiāzu Rabā ): As one of the Element 4, Laguna can use Ether Gear to manipulate water by turning his target into said element through their tears, by reconfiguring their Ether through Empire Ether. The power behind his water-based attacks can cut through metal and crush iron.

  • Water Hand (ウォーターハンド Wōtā Hando ): Laguna shapes water into a massive hand to attack his opponent.
  • Water Shot (ウォーターショット Wōtā Shotto ): Laguna throws a barrage of water projectiles at his opponent.
  • Water Laser (ウォーターレーザー Wōtā Rēzā ): Laguna clasps his hands and fires a laser surrounded by a helix of water towards his opponent.
  • Tears Arrow (ティアーズアロー Tiāzu Arō ): Laguna creates multiple balls of water, and fires them at high speed towards his opponent like arrows.
  • Tears Wave (ティアーズウェイブ Tiāzu Weibu ): Controlling the water he created, Laguna sends it as a large wave rushing at his opponent.

Empire Ether (エンパイアエーテル Enpaia Ēteru ): Laguna possesses the ability of Empire Ether, allowing him to forcibly reconfigure another person's Ether, in his case turning them into water if they shed tears.

Enhanced Reflexes : Laguna's reflexes are quick enough to block a hail of Ether bullets with a shield of water at close range.

Enhanced Durability : Laguna is tough enough to take a direct punch to the face from Weisz's Arsenal Suit and be almost unscathed after the attack.

Swordsmanship Specialist : Laguna has demonstrated the ability to expertly use swordplay in conjunction with his Ether Gear. His skill is to such a degree that he is able to keep up with Homura Kōgetsu , a powerful swordswoman and one of the Four Shining Stars, who even complimented his skill.

Equipment

Oedo Onion (オオエドタマネギ Ōedo Tamanegi ): Laguna carries an Oedo Onion as a last resort against his opponents. It is a vegetable native to planet Oedo , which when crushed releases allyl sulfides which make anybody nearby cry.
