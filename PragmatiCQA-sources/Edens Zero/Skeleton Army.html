The Skeleton Army ( スケルトンアーミー , Sukeruton Āmī ) are the robot soldiers of the Demon King Ziggy and they are equipped with anti- Electromagnetic Pulse coating.

Overview

The Skeleton Army are robot soldiers created by Ziggy and set to guard the cores on planets where he starts "uprisings". They all appear to be the same model, having a humanoid shape with basic segments, jointed limbs, and skull faces.

History

X495 arc

Underneath the surface of Sweeds , 600 meters deep, the Skeleton Army is confronted by Shiki and Pino , who plan stop the core that they guard. But they are swiftly defeated by the grandson of the Demon King. Homura states that the Skeleton Army has upgraded since their previous encounters.
