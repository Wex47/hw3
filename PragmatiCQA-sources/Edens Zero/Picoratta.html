Picoratta are a race native to Mildian and serve as the audience in the planet's battle coliseum . They love watching battles, much like their Time Oracle .

Appearance

The Picoratta are small rabbit-like aliens with egg-shaped bodies, short arms, and stubby tentacles. They come in a variety of colors and patterns, each having their own unique combination.
