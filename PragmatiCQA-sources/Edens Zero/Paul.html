Appearance

Paul is a short, chubby man with dark, bushy hair covered by what appears to be a worker's hat and outfit complete with a pair of black goggles.

Synopsis

History

After Valkyrie's sacrifice, she became the symbol of hope in the rebellion against Kurenai with Paul forming the Valkyrie's Army in her name. He and his fellow rebels made sure Valkyrie's remains are left untouched and protected.

Sun Jewel arc

When Flappy returned to the Labor District from scouting in the Rich Sector, Paul is informed that Homura is on Sun Jewel and stated that he always knew she would end up coming here. He then asks the remains of Valkyrie on what they are going to do.

Paul meets Rebecca , Happy and Pino in the Labor District after they were guided to meet him by Flappy in an unusual meditative position by squatting with his head standing on the ground, surprising Rebecca. Paul tells her to be quiet for he is in the middle of meditation which Rebecca corrects him with the proper meditation position she displayed. Suddenly, Paul spins toward Rebecca and quickly snatched her B-Cube in his hand, revealing his Ether Gear, Steal Hand and its capabilities. An annoyed Rebecca calls him a flat-out thief which Paul explains that it is the reason how he was convicted and sent to the Labor District.

After Shiki Granbell and Homura Kōgetsu learned that their collars reset despite they filled up the quota requirement for defeating the Black Rock , Paul uses his Ether Gear to remove the collars for them.

After the trio is impressed by Rebecca, Paul tells them that she's one of Homura's friends and that she even beat Black Rock.

Powers and Abilities

Steal Hand (スティールハンド Sutīru Hando ): By using Ether Gear , Paul has the ability to successfully steal objects from another person without them noticing as far as 10cm.
