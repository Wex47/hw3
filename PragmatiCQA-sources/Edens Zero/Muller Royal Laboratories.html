Müller Royal Laboratories ( ミュラー王立研究所 , Myurā Ōritsu Kenkyūsho ) was a scientific research facility formerly run by Müller on the planet Newton .

Description

Müller Royal Laboratories is centered under a giant dome. Beneath the dome is an Ether Accelerator Cannon capable of destroying an entire planet.

History

In the year X479, Interstellar Union Army conducted a raid on this facility and arrested Müller and his fellow scientists who follows him under charges of the genocide of planet Hook and its bot inhabitants, and several other illegal activities they have committed while rescuing Hermit Mio from captivity. The Rutherford family were patrons of the Müller Royal Laboratories until they withdrew their financial support after Müller was arrested and finding out the true nature of his research.

Location

Müller Royal Laboratories is located in an unknown part of Newton.
