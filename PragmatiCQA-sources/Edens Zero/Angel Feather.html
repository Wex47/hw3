Angel Feather ( エンジェルフェザー , Enjeru Fezā ) is the commando carrier of Justice , and a class of ships in the Interstellar Union Army .

Description

The Angel Feather is a large silver spaceship with a prominent head, an intricate angelic wing theme around it, and a pair of wings below. The flight deck sits at the back and has a curved roof.

It can fire a large energy beam in battle.

Trivia

  • The ship's name is a nod to Angel , a member of the Oración Seis from Fairy Tail .
