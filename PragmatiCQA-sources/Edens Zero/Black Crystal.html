Black Crystal (黒水晶 Kuro Suishō ) is an ore that can be found on Sun Jewel .

Description

Black Crystal is an ore of extraordinary hardness, able to withstand attacks from Shiki Granbell 's Satan Gravity and Homura Kōgetsu 's Soul Blade , shattering the latter on impact.

The scales of Black Rock are made entirely from Black Crystal.
