Description

Satan Gravity allows the user to freely control their personal gravity, enabling them to perform various feats; such as levitating in midair, falling sideways , clinging onto walls and ceilings , altering the density of their body , shooting off blasts of compressed gravity , and repelling incoming projectiles with a field of anti-gravity among others. This effect can also be applied to any person or object the user makes physical contact with.

A strange quirk of this Ether Gear is the unpredictable effects caused when a user activates this power whilst exposed to the force of buoyancy, resulting in the user's gravity acting erratically whenever they use it underwater. Also, whilst incapable of outright healing wounds, skilled users have the potential to make things like pain "lighter", as "true gravity" exceeds even concepts.

It is described by Poseidon Shura as the "power of destruction".

History

A thousand years ago, Satan Gravity was created by Xenolith alongside the Magimech Arts , a form of gravity-based martial arts capable of better harnessing this Ether Gear's capabilities in combat.

Trivia

  • In Chapter 11 , Weisz dubs this Ether Gear "Thwomp Boy" as a joke when requested by Shiki to give his Ether Gear a name.
  • The official name, "Satan Gravity", first appeared on Shiki's shirt on the color page of Chapter 32 .
  • Satan Gravity is the Ether Gear with the highest number of users seen so far, with four individuals confirmed to use it: Shiki Granbell , Ziggy , Poseidon Shura and Xenolith .
  • As noted by Witch Regret , the Demon King is a title for the "King of Magimech", someone who shares his Ether with machines .
