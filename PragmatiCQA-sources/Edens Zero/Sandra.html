Sandra ( サンドラ , Sandora ) is a planet located in the Aoi Cosmos and is known as the "Planet of Sand" ( 砂の惑星 , Suna no Wakusei ) . It is the tenth planet visited by Shiki and the Crew of Edens .

Description

Sandra is a planet where nine-tenths of the planet's surface is covered in deserts and cities surrounded by hills of sand. Because of the arid environment, the inhabitants wear loose, arid clothing, normally consisting of large robes and hoods. The architecture of the planet tends to have many small windows or large openings with wide streets with numerous stands selling an assortment of goods. A stream of space water flows near it, much like the other planets of the Aoi Cosmos.

According to Laguna, Sandra is a special planet.

History

The Crew of Edens visit the planet to explore the city and are lead by Laguna to find help. . Shiki and his friends find the base of Oasis , a rebel army that opposes Poseidon Nero 's tyrannical rule.

Location

The planet is located in an unspecified part of the Aoi Cosmos.
