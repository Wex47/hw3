Guarananium (ガルナニウム Garunaniumu ) is a special material that is used to form the armor of the Edens Zero .

Description

Guaranium is ordinarily a tough material that Witch claims would not even be scratched by average weapons. Drakken Joe was able to circumvent its defensive properties by using his Ether Gear , Alchemist to change it to paper.
