Description

History

Red Cave arc

Sometime after Drakken Joe 's defeat and arrest, Laguna Husert starts working as an actor again at the Scheherazade Theatre on Blue Garden, using a hologram to change his face into that of a middle-aged man as he becomes a wanted man in the Sakura Cosmos . . After his spectacular performance in a theatre play, he is confronted by Witch Regret , Jinn and Kleene backstage. Laguna drops the holographic disguise when Witch explains that once she makes an enemy, she will never stop tracking them. But she then states that she comes to him as a friend and asks for his help.
