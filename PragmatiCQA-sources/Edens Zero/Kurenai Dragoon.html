The Kurenai Dragoon (紅鉄騎 Kurenai Tekki , lit. "Crimson Cavalryman") was a Knight Gear owned and piloted by Kurenai Kōgetsu .

Description

The Kurenai Dragoon was stated to be the latest model of Knight Gear and was Kurenai Kōgetsu's ultimate weapon. She intended to use it to destroy both the Labor District of Sun Jewel and Drakken Joe .

Abilities

Immense Strength : The Kurenai Dragoon is able to send Shiki flying out of a building and across the city with a punch, while another punch could smash a building to pieces.

Immense Speed : The Kurenai Dragoon boasts impressive mobility despite its size, being able to traverse the city with ease to catch up to a flying Shiki and attack him repeatedly.

Immense Durability : Kurenai Kōgetsu has expressed confidence in the Kurenai Dragoon's durability, considering it unthinkable that Shiki's punches could even scratch the surface of it. It is also highly resilient to its own weaponry, including its targeted Queen Nova missile.

Ether Bullets : The Kurenai Dragoon can fire a spray of Ether bullets from guns mounted on its forearm.

Ether Coating : The Kurenai Dragoon is coated in a forcefield of Ether that protects the the Knight Gear from Ether Gear attacks. As a result, Shiki was unable to directly use his Satan Gravity to alter the Kurenai Dragoon's weight.

Optical Lasers : After being pushed to its maximum power, the Kurenai Dragoon can fire a pair of powerful lasers from its eyes that cause whatever the beams touch to explode.

Queen Nova (クイーンノヴァ Kuīn Nova ): The Kurenai Dragoon can fire a targeted missile from the palm of its left hand. The missile will not stop until it reaches its target, and will blast its way through buildings to do so. The kinetic energy of the Queen Nova is so strong than when converted into gravity, Shiki is able to use it to destroy the Kurenai Dragoon.
