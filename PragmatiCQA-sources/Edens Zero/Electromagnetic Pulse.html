Electromagnetic Pulse (電磁パルス Denji Parusu ), also known as EMP ( Ｅ ( イー ) Ｍ ( エム ) Ｐ ( ピー ) Ī Emu Pī ) is an ability used E.M. Pino .

Description

The user releases a surge of electromagnetism from their body, canceling out electronics inside of machinery and allowing them to disable Ether Gear and even large Knight Gear . A drawback is that it has a very short duration, lasting only a few seconds. Another drawback is that while effective against Ether Gear users, it will not affect substances made by an Ether Gear such as glue .
