Ether Bullet Full Burst (エーテル弾 フルバースト Ēteru Dan Furu Bāsuto ) is a Happy Blasters technique.

Description

The user points the Happy Blasters towards the target while collecting energy at the tips. They then fire the energy towards the target, which causes a big explosion.

Usage

Rebecca first used this technique against the weak point of Black Rock to provide an opening for Homura Kōgetsu to attack it.
