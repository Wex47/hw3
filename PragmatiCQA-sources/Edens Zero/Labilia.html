Labilia Christy ( ラビリア・クリスティ , Rabiria Kurisuti ) is an adventurer and B-Cuber who works at the Shooting Starlight guild. She is currently staying in the Edens Zero as a guest.

Appearance

Labilia is a young girl with long blue hair and a ponytail attached to a ball that levitates to her left. She wears a sleveeless black skintight suit with green lines and detached sleeves, two pairs of short, white skirt laces that wrap around her waist, and a pair of pale maroon boots.

By the year X495, her hair is now shorter and styled into a bob-cut, keeping her levitating ponytail. She wears a new, mostly light-colored suit, with dark-colored parts around her neck, elbows, lower legs, and sides, metallic pauldrons with a hole, and light-colored shoes.

Personality

Labilia has an impudent, dismissive, and an overall unpleasant personality, making unprovoked insults to those she believes to be less talented than herself. She is also extremely arrogant vain and self-centered, taking great pride in her film-making abilities and believing there is not a person within the Sakura Cosmos who is unfamiliar with her. She mocks those around her that she believes are inferior to her, a prime example being Rebecca , another B-Cuber and a former friend. She tends to charge her fans copious amounts of money for photographs, autographs (which are shaped as turds), and handshakes. When faced with someone who catches her interest, she becomes resolute in making a video of that subject, even if they have caused her pain or humiliation. Ever since meeting Shiki and seeing him demonstrate his gravity powers , Labilia has developed an admiration for him, which has strengthened after he rescued her from being kidnapped.

By the year X495, Labilia has become more reserved, as a result of her B-Cube channel's decline in popularity, and no longer acting haughty or rude. Labilia confesses that she became a B-Cuber because of Rebecca, wanting to be like her. However, after she became more successful than Rebecca, she expected Rebecca to live up to her ideals. Because of this, her admiration and irritation came together, leading to the way she acted before. Because of her incurable illness, she has long accepted that she does not have much time left.

Synopsis

History

As a child, Labilia had an incurable disease and spent her childhood at the hospital. One day, Labilia watched a video made by Rebecca on B-Cube which inspired Labilia to become a B-Cuber.

At one point in the past, Labilia was friends with Rebecca; however, due to unknown reasons, the girls ended their friendship which lead to Labilia becoming antagonistic and a petty bully towards Rebecca. Labilia is dedicated to completely ruining her day whenever and wherever she can.

Intro arc

Labilia sarcastically greets Rebecca and Happy when they come to Shooting Starlight with Shiki Granbell , eliciting an enthusiastic response from other guild members who are fans of her work. After giving one fan an autograph that consists solely of drawing a picture of turd, she arrogantly tells Rebecca that her videos have not been as interesting as of late and suggests she should quit being a B-Cuber, but then retracts the statement and "encourages" her to continue making videos that would make her own look superior by comparison. When this hurts Rebecca's feelings, a livid Shiki uses his Satan Gravity to levitate Labilia's body upside-down, much to the shock of Labilia and several onlookers. After Rebecca pulls Shiki out of the building to avoid making a further scene, Labilia recomposes herself and decides to make Shiki the subject of her next video. Later, Shiki, Rebecca, and Happy overhear a group of people fawning over a new video Labilia has uploaded, causing Rebecca to begrudgingly compliment the quality of her work and the amount of effort she must put into making them.

Guilst arc

Sun Jewel arc

Labilia approaches Rebecca saying she witnessed the Satellite Blaze transport Shiki and Homura to the labor district. She says she knows the way and will tell Rebecca how to find it if Rebecca does a favor for her. She says she wants to do a collaboration with Rebecca. She then proceeds to misname Happy and Pino. She reminds Rebecca of the B-Cuber kidnappings saying that they came after her and would have been kidnapped had Shiki not saved her. She says that if Rebecca is in a video with her more people will know about her and she will become more popular. Rebecca gives in and asks what Labilia wants her to do. Labilia pulls out a B-cube with an image of Little Mage Girl: Wibble Wobble Ruby Bobble and says she wants Rebecca to cosplay as her.

Belial Gore arc

World No. 29

Labilia was revealed to have been captured and tortured by Drakken Joe. The Dark Alchemist revealed Labilia's bloody broken form to a horrified Rebecca, telling her that she can inflict as much cathartic payback on Labilia as she want but tells her to not kill her.

World No. 30

After the Crew of Edens rescued Labilia who was held captive by Drakken Joe, it turns out that it was Amira , an agent of the Galactic Intelligence Agency who broke her out and took her place with Mirror Face . She banned Labilia from using her B-Cube channel for a week after putting her inside the club for her safety which annoys her.

Powers and Abilities

Dancing Sakura Style Karate ( 桜舞流ケンカ空手 , Ōbu-ryū Kenka Karate ) : Labilia is an expert in hand-to-hand combat which allows her to initially get the edge over Rebecca Bluegarden despite Rebecca's strength and fighting experience over the last three years. She claims not to use this fighting style in public since it doesn't match her influencer persona.

Equipment

B-Cube (Ｂ・キューブ B Kyūbu ): She uses a B-Cube terminal with a diamond-shaped symbol to record, upload, and share videos on her channel, Labilia Channel.
