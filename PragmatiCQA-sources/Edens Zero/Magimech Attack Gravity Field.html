Magimech Attack: Gravity Field ( 魔械流 重力場 , Makai-ryū: Jūryokuba ) is a Satan Gravity technique.

Description

The user places their palm on the ground, generating a strong gravitational field that both crushes and negates attacks.

Usage

Shiki first used this technique to crush all of the wind controlled by Kleene with her Wind Snatch , canceling her attack against Homura .

Shiki uses this technique to free his crew and Couchpo from Mora 's Glue Tear that trapped them on the trees.

Trivia

  • When it was originally used in Chapter 77 , the technique was not prefaced with 'Magimech Attack'. The second time it was used, in Chapter 116 , it did have the preface like the other Satan Gravity techniques.
