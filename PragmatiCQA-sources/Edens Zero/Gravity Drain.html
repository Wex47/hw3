Gravity Drain ( グラビティドレイン , Gurabiti Dorein ) is a Satan Gravity technique.

Contents

  • 1 Description
  • 2 Usage
  • 4 Site Navigation

Description

The user grabs the target with their open palm and uses gravity to absorb the individual's abilities, such as Ether Gear , seemingly also eradicating the target.

Usage

Ziggy first used this technique to absorb Poseidon Nero into the depths of his own gravity and steal his power, Wormhole .
