Description

Relics are pieces of Mother are scattered across the cosmos. These may be objects of worship as is the case with the Relic on Red Cave , or may be concealed on a planet or be wandering through space.

The objects that have become Relics are simply items that were on Mother in the past, and all of them seen so far have been at the size used by humans such as a candelabra or a bottle cap. Some Relics possess singularly mystical powers, such as being able to assure the victory of the user.
