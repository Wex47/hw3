Ether Lock ( エーテルロック , Ēteru Rokku ) is a type of Ether Gear that can imprison indivuals in cages made of psychokinetic Ether .

Description

Ether Lock allows the user to capture individuals in Ether cages, shrinking them to fit in the user's palm, allowing for easy transportation. The user is also able to unlock the cube, releasing the prisoner and restore them to their normal size.
