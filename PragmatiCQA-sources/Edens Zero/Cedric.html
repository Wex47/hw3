Cedric ( セドリック , Sedorikku ) is a former model and butler from the planet Genos . After being disfigured by Kurenai he becomes a leader of his own gang of thugs in Sun Jewel .

Appearance

Cedric is a slim young man with a handsome face, blond hair, and cyan eyes. His attire as consisted of him wearing a clean dark suit, a tie, and shoes. After his was face was burnt by Kurenai, he wears a metallic-toothed mask and a visor to cover his facial scars. His current outfit now consists of a dark leather jacket, a shirt, pants, and a pair of shoes.

Personality

Cedric is a gentleman who is well-mannered and polite. As a model, he takes extreme care in his face, since the slightest injury or imperfection would put him out of a job.

After having his face burned and subsequently losing his job as a model by Kurenai, Cedric becomes quick to anger and sadistic as a brutal leader of his own gang of thugs. Having developed a personal grudge towards Kurenai, he does not hold back from hitting her face with a metal bat when the opportunity for revenge comes and even calls her an old hag.

Synopsis

Sun Jewel arc

At Kurenai's Tower in Gold Palace , Cedric is introduced by Ward to Madame Kurenai , whom he greets courteously. He is humbly flattered by Ward's remark of being the perfect attendant for the Crimson Lady, stating that he is more impressed by her beauty. He complies with pleasure when Kurenai asks him to undress and come closer to her. He compliments the woman for her beauty and authority over the entire planet and its wealth. He declares that he will do anything for her as her attendant, even offering to keep her warm on lonely nights while affectionately touching her face. Unfortunately, Kurenai disapproves, confusing the model, as she explains that he touched her without her consent, believing that he had mistaken her for a sensualist. He tries to apologize to her but Kurenai grabs his face with her gauntlet . He begs the Crimson Lady to spare his face, fearing that he will lose his career if anything happens to it. Unluckily for him, Kurenai shows no mercy and sadistically burns his face, he screams in pain, losing consciousness, and falls to the floor, as his face is turned a deep red.

Later on, Cedric is forced to become Kurenai's pet, where he remains naked, wearing only a leather mask, collar, and leash. He is on his arms and knees when the Zaiten Three arrive to meet with their boss. Cedric escapes sometime before or during Shiki 's successful assault on Kurenai, who brings her to Homura at the Labor District . During this, he obtains new clothes, a metal mask, and formed a gang of thugs under his leadership. In the woods of the Labor District, he ambushes Kurenai by smacking her in the face with a metal bat. He and his gang surround her, where he mocks the former ruler of Sun Jewel as a crazy old hag and intends to get his revenge for what she did to him by making her their pet, calling it karmic retribution.

Equipment

Metal Bat : After escaping and becoming the leader of a gang, Cedric wields a metal bat with a bandaged handle and mechanical seams around the taper and barrel.
