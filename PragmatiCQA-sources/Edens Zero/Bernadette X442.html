Bernadette ( バーナデッド , Bānadeddo ) is a waitress on Norma . She has an older counterpart living outside the post- Chronophage eaten Norma.

Appearance

Bernadette is an alien woman with purple hair and eyes, light skin, and has many rabbit features, including ears, nose, hands, and a tail. She wears a bunny girl outfit with two rabbit paw emblems on the chest. Additionally, she has a white collar around her neck with a black tie.

Personality

Bernadette is a professional woman doing her job as a waitress at the bar in Norma. She is quite knowledgeable of Norma's underworld and shows concern for others, telling newcomers of the bar to not get involved.

Synopsis

Norma arc

When Shiki , Rebecca and Happy hide inside the bar in Norma from Sibir and his gang of thugs , Bernadette passes by their table to serve drinks to the other customers and notices the young man looking flustered at her. She asks Shiki if he wants to order anything from the establishment before moving on to do her job. While issuing an order from a customer, the rabbit woman ignores Shiki's attempt to befriend her. She approaches Shiki, stating that she overheard them talking about Sibir. Bernadette explains that Sibir runs a gang that upended the whole town in Norma, and warns the young man that he and his friends will never get out alive after Sibir sets his sights on them. She confirms to Rebecca that she knows Weisz , calling him a scumbag. Sitting on a couch and smoking on her high-class pipe, the rabbit woman reveals that Weisz used to be a member of Sibir's gang but she heard he quit recently and cannot give any details. She confirms that she knows the location of Sibir's hideout but is annoyed that Shiki and his friends are not heeding her warning to stay away from the gang.
