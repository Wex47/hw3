Edens One ( ＥＤＥＮＳ ( エデンズ ) ＯＮＥ ( ワン ) Edenzu Wan ) is a spaceship created and owned by Ziggy with Connor as its captain. It was originally destroyed in Universe 1 , but somehow avoided this in Universe 2 due to Rebecca Bluegarden ’s time leaping ability, the Cat Leaper .

Description

The ship resembles the Edens Zero but with a fish theme, where the figurehead resembles a dunkleosteus with an open maw and eyeholes with glowing "eyes". Additionally, there is a pair of fin-like protrusions on the side. On the side of the ship the words "EDENS ONE" are inscribed on a plaque.

Abilities

As a large spaceship, the Edens One can fly through space and enter the atmosphere of a planet with ease.

Witch speculated that the Edens One has its own version of Etherion after witnessing the destruction of many Dragons left in its wake at Dragonfall .

Ether Cannons : Edens One is equipped with powerful Ether cannons which can penetrate the shields of the Edens Zero.

Missile Defense : Edens One is outfitted with defenses against missiles which was able to intercept and destroy a barrage of Scorpion Missiles fired by the Edens Zero.

Bee Small Battle Drones (小型戦闘機ビー Kogata Sentōki Bī ): Edens One has a stockpile of armed, unmanned drones which can be sent out to do battle and are large enough to carry a person on top of them.

Star Bringer (スターブリンガー Sutā Buringā ): The Star Bringer is the main cannon of the Edens One. It fires a destructive energy beam from the mouth at the front of the ship.

Crew Members

Current

  • Ziggy : The Demon King who created and owns the Edens One with the intention to conquer humankind and usher the age of machines in the cosmos after being somehow reactivated by a dark Ether.
  • Four Dark Stars : The all-male group of machines created by Ziggy that is said to be the advanced generation of the Four Shining Stars .
    • Wizard : The "Spear of Edens", who is a handsome-looking android who manipulates Ether in a manner similar to Magic . He is Witch 's counterpart.
    • Clown : The "Death of Edens", an android dressed in a flamboyant Harlequin outfit with a playful demeanor. He is the counterpart of Sister .
    • Brigandine The "Armor of Edens", who is a a large armored machine, wielding a large labrys. After being rebuilt into Mark II he becomes a large lion-faced, armored android. He is known to be the counterpart of Valkyrie Yuna .
    • Killer : The "Brains of Edens", who is a tall lean machine with a masked face. After being rebuilt into Mark II he appears as a boy wearing a cloak. He is the counterpart of Hermit .

Former

  • Connor : A man who was the helmsman of the Edens One in Universes 1 and 2. In Universe 3 , he was instead one of the humans recruited by Ziggy to gather their skills to upload into his Skeleton Army.
