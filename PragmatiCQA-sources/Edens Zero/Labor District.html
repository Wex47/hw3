Description

The Labor District is a lawless and run-down city with many broken buildings and factories. The citizens are usually very poor and dressed in rags.

The Labor Distrcit also inlcudes multiple caves where Stones are found. Sub-criminals are sent to the Labor District to mine the stones and get metal for Madame Kurenai . It is patrolled by the Zaiten Three , who punishes the criminals who doesn't collect enough metal.

Valkyrie Yuna 's Memorial Place is located in the Labor District.

Location

The Labor District is located in the poor sector of Sun Jewel .
