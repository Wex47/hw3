The Heavenly Knights of the Dancing Sakura (桜舞星騎士団 Ōbu Seikishi-dan , lit. "Starry Order of Dancing Cherry Blossoms") are a group of twelve legendary heroes who defended the Sakura Cosmos during the Dark Ages. They are also known as the Guardians of Blue Garden .

Synopsis

During their travel to seek out Hermit of the Four Shining Stars , the crew of the Edens Zero spot a group of twelve colossal statues on the Iron Hill that represent the Heavenly Knights, where coincidentally they found the Mind of Edens impassively sitting on the head of one of the statues.
