Maria ( マリア , Maria ) is a scythe used by Spider as Jamilov in Digitalis .

Description

Maria is a dark and slightly jagged scythe. The head of the scythe can transform into a giant beast made of dark particles, which devours anything it touches. In this form, the scythe can fire energy beams powerful enough to destroy buildings in its path.
