God Acnoella (ゴッド・アクネラ Goddo Akunera ), also known as the "Mother of Dragons" (竜の母 Ryū no Haha ), is a machine and one of the Oración Seis Galáctica .

Appearance

God Acnoella is a tall female android with a voluptuous body, long claws and hair, and the top of her face is covered by a triclop eye mask with a pointy dark hood. She wears a flowing long dark dress that has a cleavage window with a large poofy high collar and a symbol of a Dragon skull with large horns at the front.

Personality

God Acnoella is a soft-spoken machine who wholeheartedly supports Ziggy's goal to create a world only for machines and defers to him completely, calling him "Lord Ziggy" in a respectful tone. She views the Dragons as "her children" and is confident in their power. However, she is perceptive as she deduced that the Demon King has a greater reason to kill Shiki Granbell .

Although God Acnoella firmly states to Ziggy that while she may be his ally, she values her independence and does not take orders from him. She also doesn't tolerate betrayal upon her and will act on it in retaliation, making her "a difficult woman" by Ziggy.

Synopsis

Kaede War arc

God Acnoella allies herself with Ziggy at an unknown point. She sends swarms of her Dragons circling around Lendard 's defense zone as the combined forces of the Edens Zero , Holy 's Midnight fleet, and Elsie 's space pirate fleet arrive to invade their base of operations.

Approaching the Demon King on an open balcony, God Acnoella assures him that her children will stand in Shiki 's way and wonders if he will make it that far. When Ziggy questions if she means the Dragons, she states that she brought them into this world and calls them her children. She asks him if there is something wrong when he starts ruminating. She expresses her support for his goal of a world of only mechanical beings, and that humanity has no place in it, but does not understand why Ziggy must kill his adopted grandson. God Acnoella deduces that Ziggy has a greater reason than his apparent claim of Shiki being human, noting that he had raised the boy as his own, nurtured his belief in human-machine equality, and raised him to the skies.

Standing on top of the highest tower with her Dragons flying around in the skies, God Acnoella heeds Ziggy's call and notes of his lost interest to finish off her fellow Galáctica member Elsie Crimson . She states to the Demon King that she is not his subordinate but accepts his request to kill Elsie and Justice . However, the Mother of Dragons warns him that if he betrays her, the Dragons will devour every last machine on Lendard.

Powers and Abilities

God Acnoella is a member of the Oración Seis Galáctica , a group that is stated to be the cosmos' strongest warriors, being renowned for crushing entire planets.

Dragon Taming : She has the ability to control the Dragons through unknown means.
