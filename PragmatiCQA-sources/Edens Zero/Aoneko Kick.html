Aoneko Kick ( アオネコキック , Aoneko Kikku ) is a Cat Leaper technique used by Rebecca Bluegarden .

  • 3 Trivia
  • 5 Site Navigation

Description

The user manifests an Ether projection of cat ears on top of their head and kicks the opponent with enough physical force to send them flying.

Usage

Rebecca first uses this technique to send a captured Britney inside a vacuum cleaner out into the sky and destroying the rooftop of the electronics shop, Elecloth .

Trivia

  • This technique's name came from Aoneko Channel , Rebecca and Happy's B-Cuber channel.
