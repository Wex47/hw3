Element Ether ( 属性エーテル , Zokusei Ēteru ) is an ability that allows the user to change the at properties of their Ether . It has also been called Magic ( 魔法 , Mahō ) .

Description

The user manipulates their Ether in a manner similar to magic. They are able to change the element of their Ether at will, and are capable of utilizing elements such as ice to freeze objects, air to evade attacks, and lightning that shocks from a distance.
