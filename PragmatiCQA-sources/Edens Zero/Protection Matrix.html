A Protection Matrix ( プロテクトマトリックス , Purotekuto Matorikkusu ) is a defensive item.

Description

The Protection Matrix is an anti-Ether barrier formed as a wall of hexagonal panels. The barrier works effectively against Ether bullets but allows physical attacks to pass through unobstructed.
