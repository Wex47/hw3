Oedo ( オオエド , Ōedo ) is a planet located in the Sakura Cosmos . It is not very advanced compared to the other planets of the Sakura Cosmos .

Description

Oedo is a planet that has a civilization similar to that of feudal Japan, having its architecture, fashion, and government modeled after it. Its technology is not very advanced, but is noted to have delicious food, such as sushi and dango .

History

After leaving the Four Shining Stars , Valkyrie Yuna came to Oedo where she rescued young Homura from thugs and took her in as her student, teaching her how to use Soul Blade .

However, the Sword of Edens suffered discrimination from Oedo's populace for being a machine and not an actual blood relative which affected Homura. It prompted Valkyrie to leave planet Oedo and search for Kurenai in order to reunite her with Homura.

Seiji Hōjō, a member of this planet's shogunate family also left Oedo, and joined an intergalactic Cosmic Government's army Interstellar Union, earning the code name: Creed at some point.

Location

The planet is located in an unknown part of the Sakura Cosmos .
