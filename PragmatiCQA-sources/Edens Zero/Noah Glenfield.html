Noah Glenfield ( ノア・グレンフィールド , Noa Gurenfīrudo ) is the Director of the Galactic Intelligence Agency in the Sakura Cosmos . He is currently working undercover as the Guild Master of Shooting Starlight .

Appearance

Noah is a young man of average height with medium-length blond hair and green eyes. As the Guild Master, his attire consists of a long-sleeved dark-colored dress suit over a dress suit with a tie, and sports dress pants held by a belt with a star on the center. Lastly, he wears a light-colored coat draped over his shoulders like a cape with the guild's mark on the back.

Personality

Noah appears to be formal and serene when negotiating with Drakken Joe for information about the Edens Zero. Drakken also calls him 'Master', suggesting a level of respect towards him. However, Noah is a very cautious man, such as realizing how dangerous Drakken Joe can be.

Synopsis

History

By X482, Noah was working as the Guild Master of the Shooting Starlight Adventurers Guild as part of his undercover work for the Galactic Intelligence Agency. That year, he sent Sister Ivry on a job to the planet Guilst to free the petrified women in Illega Tower, where she got captured and held prisoner for 10 years.

In the same year, Noah also happened to witness a car accident where a young girl should have died. But thanks to Noah's Eye of God , he saw that the girl somehow managed to change her location in time subconsciously, and survived the crash. Ever since that day, Noah kept a close watch on the girl's activities with his Ether Gear, Eye of God.

Guilst arc

After Rebecca comes back to the Shooting Starlight Guildhall, Noah sends Clarisse to let Rebecca know that he wants to talk with her. Unfortunately, they are attacked by a member of Rogue Out and are kidnapped. However, he escapes before getting loaded on the spaceship.

Digitalis arc

In his office, Noah is contacted by Drakken Joe for information on the Edens Zero . After hearing his inquiry, Noah comments that the information does not come cheap. In the process, he tells Drakken about Rebecca and her powers.

Belial Gore arc

World No. 29

On Blue Garden, a guild member reminds Noah about his meeting, to which he replies that he will be right there. He then comments to himself that everything has gone according to his calculations so far. He then wonders what will be the next move of No.29.

World No. 30

Noah waits at the roof of Drakken's tower in the Belial Gore, when Rebecca's group arrives he says that he's been expecting her. Just as Shiki is about to attack, the young man goes through him, which he reveals that he's a hologram and that his real body is on Blue Garden. He informs Rebecca that he has somethings to tell her. The first of which is his Ether Gear, Eye of God, that allows him to observe people's location. Noah says that he used to think it was useless, until he realized his powers extend out of time. He specifies that he can track Rebecca's position in time and her movement across it, confirming that he knows her powers and that it was around 10 years ago when he first noticed it. He notes that when she was young she was supposed to pass away in a car crash but subconsciously changed her location in time and does not remember it. He goes on that Rebecca changed her location whenever her life was threatened or the instant she died. Since this is the 30th time, he calls it World No. 30.

Noah confirms that Drakken called Rebecca No. 29 in that he was referring to World No. 29. He notes how Rebecca has retained her memories from the previous world and that this would be the only world where Drakken can be defeated. He corrects Rebecca, in that he does not know how many times she can use her powers left, only that in World No. 30 the situation with Drakken is more perilous. He explains that since she has awakened Cat Leaper, if Drakken wins then her powers will be forefeit. Where she will lose her time leaping ability and that Drakken's World No. 2 would begin. He answers Weisz that he deduced the events based on the locational information from the people involved. He claims that everything he did, selling Rebecca to Guilst and Drakken Joe, was all so that he could defeat Drakken Shiki points out how he put Rebecca in so much danger to defeat Drakken. Noah then asks if there is a problem, saying that they have yet to learn how truly terrifying Drakken is.

Noah states how Drakken earned his title, "Dark Alchemist", from his Ether Gear and underworld connections and that his power is so great that it placed him as a Galáctica. He then informs them of his other name "Undead Joe" and tries to tell them, but his signal is deteriorating. Before he gets completely cut off, Noah manages to tell them that he believes they found Drakken's weakness. Afterward, he remarks how they do not have a next time, and that if they do not defeat Drakken, then he will die and Blue Garden would be destroyed as well.

Edens One arc

In his office, Noah meets Shiki's group and thanks them for defeating Drakken who is now under Interstellar Union Army 's custody. Noah claims they should be rewarded, but because of the use of Cat Leaper, he can not report their involvement to his superiors without causing unnecessary complications, asking them to remain anonymous. He then tells the group that his Guild Master role is a front and reveals the he is the Director of the Galactic Intelligence Agency's branch overseeing the Sakura Cosmos. He hopes that by revealing his identity, it will make up for any damage he did to them. Rebecca then requests to speak to Noah alone despite Shiki's protest.

When asked by Rebecca if he knows if she has a family, Noah reveals that he did look into her to find out where her power came from, but he could not find anything about her family. He is then asked about a man named Connor , which Rebecca explains the situation to him, how he was in World No. 29, but not in No. 30. He theorizes that the events of the past were changed by a power other then Rebecca's, and promises to look into it and allows Rebecca to leave, wishing her fair adventures.

Red Cave arc

After Drakken's defeat and arrest, Noah made a written report about it, claiming to have a powerful team in his branch who were behind it, rather than the Edens Zero Crew .

Aoi War arc

Using his Eye of God, Noah notices for a moment that a large number of people's location suddenly changed around Nero 66 .

X495 arc

Noah is contacted by Rebecca and states her that it is good to see her, commenting that he watches her B-Cube videos from time to time. When Rebecca bluntly asks him what world are they in now, he remarks how she does not hesitate, but notes that it is a good question, revealing that circumstances have changed a bit. He tells her that he has done some research since their last talk and with the numbering system she is familiar with, they are now in World No. 416. He explains they should think of it as the number of times that Rebecca has used a simple time leap, Reverse . He reassures Rebecca that it is her weapon and she needs it to fight, predicting that the number of uses will continue to increase. He pushes this subject to the side for now, explaining that he has now determined that there are two types of changes that result in a new world number. The first type he explains is with Reverse, the 90 second time leap, which causes virtually no changes to the world overall as while it may cause a tremendous change for Rebecca and those around around her, history eventually corrects and converges towards its predetermined future. He then explains that the second type comes from Cat Leaper, where this ability does not only move her through time, but transports her to another, parallel world. He then refers those worlds as Universes, revealing World No. 29 where Shiki died to be Universe 1 and World No. 30 where Drakken was defeated to be Universe 2 .

Noah informs Rebecca and Happy that they are now in Universe 3 where Poseidon Nero was defeated. He learns that Rebecca was not aware of the jump, for he was sure she used Cat Leaper to escape the destruction of Nero 66 . When Rebecca tells him that she had no memory of doing that, Noah finds it strange, noting how he saw a number of people's change just like when they fought Drakken, concluding that they have most certainly moved over to Universe 3. He answers Rebecca's questions that he can technically only predict a world's future based on the positions of the inhabitants, and that she and her friends would have likely perished in the destruction. When Rebecca brings up Connor, Noah notes of the captain's change of roles from Universe 1 and 2. He assures her that there is nothing strange about that, and it is how things work in the Multiverse, stating that if a "world" changes, the histories of its people can change from what they once were.

When the Edens Zero crew visits the Shooting Starlight, Noah greets them, stating that it is good to see them again. Although, the he faces an upset Shiki, while commenting how much he has grown and asks him if he is is still angry with him after that one incident. Taking Shiki's response in stride, he notes that technically they are members of opposing organizations and finds his antagonism towards him fair. Suddenly, his Eye of God activates when Hermit informs Shiki and his friends that the Interstellar Union Army knows that they are on Blue Garden. Noah quickly shuts down Shiki's accusation of tipping them off and tells him that it is Feather of the Oración Seis Interstellar , stating that she possesses the same Ether Gear like him. He also informs Shiki and Weisz that the Eye of God cannot find mechanical lifeforms.

Noah learns that the crew intends to confront Feather and her forces head on, warning them that the Interstellar member not only uses her Ether Gear to see people's locations but also predict enemy movements in battle. He answers Jinn 's question that with his position, he cannot help them escape. However, he offers to buy the crew a little bit of time but Shiki simply declines. He witnesses the battle between Shiki's friends and Feather's forces. Noah approaches Connor, asking him if there is something wrong. He explains to the bewildered captain that they fight is because they are young, finding fewer options for solving problems and that makes them more sensitive and straightforward. He states that they choose to fight to protect their family and prevent the loss of any more of their family. Noah informs Connor that the Edens Zero crew considers each other as family and that every one of them is an orphan, stating that their bonds are more valuable to them than anything.

Powers and Abilities

Eye of God (ゴッドアイ Goddo Ai ): Noah's Ether Gear enables him to observe the locations of people. Unlike the usage of GPS and nanotechnology, Its power extends outside of time, allowing him to observe a person's location in time and their movement across it. However, he cannot find mechanical lifeforms .
