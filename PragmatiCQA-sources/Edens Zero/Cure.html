Cure ( キュアー , Kyuā ) is a member of the Interstellar Union Army and one of the Oración Seis Interstellar .

Appearance

Cure is a vaguely fish-like humanoid being with long wavy green hair and red eyes and has robotic lines on his emotionless face, making him more robotic compared to his fellow Interstellar members. He wears black skintight suit and a distinct headband that bears the symbol of the Interstellar Union Army on the front.

Personality

Cure is an impassive man who stoically reminds his colleagues about their group's existence to fight their lawless counterparts and crush them. He does care for his comrades and suggests that they offer prayers to their recently deceased.

Synopsis

Red Cave arc

Cure attends a Prayer Council via transmission with his fellow Oración Seis Interstellar members where he states that they exist to fight the Oración Seis Galáctica and Jaguar agrees with him as the group discuss about Elsie Crimson arriving in the Aoi Cosmos where her fellow Oración Seis Galáctica, Poseidon Nero resides and that Justice immediately volunteers to pursue her. Afterward, they pray and end their Prayer Council.

Aoi War arc

In the aftermath of the Aoi War, Cure attends a Prayer Council, reassuring Justice that despite the loss of Jaguar , Creed , and thousands of Interstellar Union Army soldiers, the Nero Empire and Poseidon Nero are defeated. He suggests that they pray for their comrades who have joined the stars. After Eraser brings up Deadend Crow , Saintfire Nox , and God Acnoella as the bigger problem, Cure declares that their fight will not end until all of the Galáctica are eliminated. He then requests that they pray, so that their sacred powers will bring peace to the universe and end their Prayer Council.

Kaede War arc

After being informed by Feather , Cure is surprised to learn that so many Galáctica members are in one place before he is on his way to Lendard with Feather and Eraser. Upon arrival with his fleet of Brain Dust ships, Cure tells his comrades that the Dragons unleashed by God Acnoella must be dealt with first.

Powers and Abilities

Cure is a member of the Oración Seis Interstellar , a group of six warriors who exist to fight the Oración Seis Galáctica , and in a class of his own even within the Cosmic Government.

Equipment

Brain Dust (ブレインダスト Burein Dasuto ): Cure's spaceship in the Interstellar Union Army.
