Pain's Tree ( ペインズツリー , Peinzu Tsurī ) is a type of Ether Gear that utilizes the elements of earth and wood to inflict pain on others.

Description

Pain's Tree allows the user to grow roots into their target. These roots are described to eat into its target and add pain wherever it goes.
