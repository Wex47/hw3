Wizard ( ウィザード , Wizādo ) also known as the Spear of Edens ( エデンズの矛 , Edenzu no Hō ) , is a Machine and one of the Demon King's Four Dark Stars .

Appearance

Wizard is a machine with the appearance of a slim young man with long, green hair and his eyes are always shut. His attire consists of a typical wizard's cloak with a light-colored coat draped over his shoulders, and a type of battle armor underneath.

Personality

Wizard is a serious individual who treats the Edens Zero and its crew as a serious threat to their goals, and is not willing to take any chances with them.

Synopsis

Edens One arc

After a revived Ziggy , boards the Edens One and leaves Granbell , he introduces the Four Dark Stars to the Crew of Edens where Wizard stands alongside his fellow superior machines. Wizard states that they are meant to replace their predecessors. When Connor hesitates on attacking the Edens Zero by Ziggy's orders and said that he is there because he agreed with the Demon King's ideals, Wizard reminds the man that their enemy is threatening those ideals and will block their path one day if he does not destroy the ship. During the clash between the Edens Zero and Edens One, Wizard stands on top of one of the Bee Small Battle Drones deployed by Connor and uses Divine Lightning with his staff to destroy the unmanned fighters of the Edens Zero singlehandedly.

Foresta arc

At the bridge of Edens One where Ziggy learns that the machine rebellion on Foresta was stopped by the Crew of Edens, Wizard stands silently as the Demon King explains to the Dark Stars about his lost memories, his quest to find Mother , and his decision to raise Shiki as his grandson. Wizard asks Ziggy if Shiki prevents him from his work to create a world ruled by mechanical lifeforms. Wizard stands silently as Ziggy remarks that Shura reacted exactly as he expected after watching the prince's public announcement to the Nero Empire . He then asks the Demon King if he means the robot massacre.

X495 arc

On Lendard , Wizard remarks how the planet will create life in perpetuity and stands next to the other Dark Stars as Ziggy informs them that Shiki is coming and that they will stay here and fight him.

Powers and Abilities

Wizard is built as part of the Demon King's Four Dark Stars ; a group of machines designed to be more advanced than the Demon King's Four Shining Stars .

Element Ether (属性エーテル Zokusei Ēteru ): As Witch's counterpart, Wizard has the ability to change his Ether's elemental properties and manipulate it in a manner similar to magic.

  • Lightning Ether (ライトニングエーテル Raitoningu Ēteru ): Wizard conjures Ether with his spear to generate lightning and uses it to strike his targets.
    • Divine Lightning (神の雷 Kami no Ikazuchi ): Wizard summons powerful streaks of lightning to destroy numerous targets at once.
    • Cosmic Lightning (宇宙雷光 Uchū Raikō ): Wizard releases streaks of dark lightning from his spear. The effects of this technique is unknown.
  • Ice Ether (アイスエーテル Aisu Ēteru ): Using his spear, Wizard can surrounds his target with ice and encase them in it.
  • Air Ether (エアエーテル Ea Ēteru ): Wizard conjures Ether beneath his feet to create a burst of air, enhancing his jumping capabilities.
  • Fire Ether (ファイアエーテル Faia Ēteru ): Wizard conjures Ether with his spear to generate fire from it.
    • Black Blaze (ブラックブレイズ Burakku Bureizu ): Wizard summons a massive pillar of fire to engulf his target in.
  • Drain Ether (ドレインエーテル Dorein Ēteru ): Wizard uses this to drain his target's Ether, where the more powerful the Ether unleashed upon him, the more powerful the Ether he absorbs.

Battle Dress (バトルドレス Batoru Doresu ): As one of the counterparts to the Four Shining Stars, Wizard possess the ability to requip into a different form of battle suit.

Machine Physiology : As a machine , Wizard is able to survive in the vacuum of space unprotected with no negative effects.

Anti-EMP Coating : Wizard, like all members of the Four Dark Stars , is equipped with a defensive coating that protects him from E.M. Pino 's Electromagnetic Pulse .

Equipment

Spear : Wizard wields a spear that he keeps on his back and uses in combat.
