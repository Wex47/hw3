The Oración Seis Interstellar ( 星系六祈将 ( オラシオンセイス ) , Orashion Seisu ) are a special forces of the Interstellar Union Army that oppose the Oración Seis Galáctica and defeat them.

Overview

The group consists of six powerful members of the Interstellar Union Army who use their "sacred powers" to counteract the threat of their lawless counterparts and crush them for the peace of the cosmos. Each six warriors of the Interstellar are in a class of their own even within the Cosmic Government and their power supposedly rivals even that of the Galáctica. The Interstellar members have command over their personal teams of Interstellar Union Army soldiers and fleets of Commando Carriers respectively.

According to Jaguar, the members' actual ranks in the Interstellar Union Army don't apply to their duties as the Oración Seis Interstellar, and thus direct subordinates and superior officers are able to speak to and work with each other as equals during a Prayer Council ( 祈りの間 , Inori no Ma ) . While there are certain protocols in the Interstellar Union Army that must be followed such as not harming the civilians, members of the Oración Seis Interstellar are given certain privileges and they have the right, in any situation, to eradicate any target that they determine to be evil.

There is no chain of command in the Oración Seis Interstellar as they are not authorized to give each other orders , and each member has the authority to make their own judgment calls. According to Holy, they have no commander and they are free to team up with whoever they wish.

History

Red Cave arc

All members of the Oración Seis Interstellar hold a Prayer Council via holographic screen transmissions a week earlier than intended. They discuss about Elsie Crimson who is heading to the Aoi Cosmos where her fellow Oracion Seis Galáctica member, Poseidon Nero resides, which Justice immediately volunteers to go after her. After Justice's show of conviction with his Ether Gear's partial activation, Jaguar concludes the end of a Prayer Council and that they must pray to use their "sacred powers" for the peace of the cosmos.

Foresta arc

After the Armored Space Pirate leaves the battlefield on her subordinate Behrman 's transport, Justice tries to go after her despite his heavy wounds, but Holy contacts him via transmission and praises Elsie's strength. While Holy may not mind him continue chasing after Elsie, she reminds the soldier to keep Nero in mind for she believes that the Emperor will make some sort of move and take revenge on Ziggy for what he did to Foresta .

Returning to the Angel Feather and the Interstellar Union Army fleet in space, a recuperating Justice converses with Holy, who informs him that Nero has assembled his top officials, the Oceans 6 and his son Poseidon Shura on Nero 1 : The Temple, at the Commando Carrier's bridge. The female Interstellar member playfully chides the unconcerned soldier that Shura could be a threat and it is their chance to destroy him or else the Aoi Cosmos will suffer for it later, noting that Shura will take over the Nero Empire once his father Nero dies of old age. Commenting that there are far too many big fish for Justice to handle, Holy also tells her fellow Interstellar member that Jaguar and Eraser are on their way to the Aoi Cosmos, and advises the soldier to let them help him. When Justice asks her if she is not going to do anything, Holy answers with an impish smile that she has other fish to fry.

Aoi War arc

After arriving on the Aoi Cosmos, Eraser leads his Interstellar Union Army fleet of 250 ships with the Hot Eyes to Nero 66 where they found their target, Shura, and declares to the spoiled prince that it is time to say goodbye to this cosmos. He is then contacted by Justice and Jaguar via transmissions where the soldier scolds him for going ahead of them and tells him to wait for them until they arrive. Eraser simply reminds Justice that the Oración Seis Interstellar has no chain of command and each of them has the authority to make their own judgment calls. Despite the soldier warning him that he is fighting the Nero Empire, the biggest organization in the Oracion Seis Galáctica, Eraser corrects that he is fighting Shura, not Nero, and notes that the Emperor is not the scariest member of the Galáctica that he knows. He answers Jaguar's question that there are about 3,200 ships of the Nero Empire and he has no way of knowing if there are more of them behind the frontlines or on the surface of Nero 66. But the black-haired Interstellar member simply reassures to the captain and the soldier that he will have the Imperial Army forces erased before the two even arrive.

When Justice shows up on time to single-handedly dispatch multiple Imperial Army ships with his white starfighter, Eraser reassures Goodwin that he is an ally coming to their aid, advising him not to shoot the soldier, and later comments with relief that his fellow Interstellar member is late to the party. Reporting for duty, Justice declares that he is joining the mission to capture Nero 66 with his Interstellar Union Army fleet appearing to aid Eraser's forces and the Oasis rebels as reinforcements.

After Eraser informs an irritated Justice that they are working with Oasis and asks when Jaguar is arriving, the soldier informs him that the captain had another matter to attend to. Eraser is exasperated and questions if it is more important than the fate of the Aoi Cosmos which Justice simply reassures that what their fellow Interstellar member is doing is to help the Aoi Cosmos. He declares that they are going to put an end to Nero's ambition.

The two Interstellar members encounter the Sea Serpent appears among the Nero Empire forces which it is not good news for them. After witnessing the Sea Serpent's massive beam attack that wipes out their troops, Justice and Eraser commandeers their ships toward the titanic warship at full speed to stop it. Attacking the Sea Serpent together, Eraser tells Justice to give him a minute until he uses his Ether Gear again.

After witnessing Dylan 's sacrifice that immobilized the Sea Serpent and heeding Justice's call, Eraser activates his recharged Ether Gear to erase the Nero Empire's most powerful weapon. The two Interstellar members are surprised when they receives a broad-range open transmission from the Edens Zero . When Hermit informs all combatants in space that the 20,000 Imperial Antimatter Bombs are about to destroy Nero 66 and its entire sector, Eraser orders the Interstellar Union Army forces to leave the front immediately, while retorting to his fellow Interstellar member that he doubts the android is lying.

In the aftermath of the Aoi War, the remaining five members of the Oración Seis Interstellar attend another Prayer Council where they learn from Justice and Eraser that Jaguar, Creed and thousands of Interstellar Union Army soldiers lost their lives. Cure reassures that despite the losses, the Nero Empire and Poseidon Nero has been defeated, and offers a prayer for their allies who have joined the stars. However, Feather speaks for the first time as she reminds her comrades that there are 4 remaining Oración Seis Galáctica members left, surprising Holy. Feather states that Elsie is not a significant threat to them at this point, irking Justice. Eraser explains to the soldier that compared to Elsie, the other three Galáctica members are a bigger problem. Cure declares to his fellow Oración Seis Interstellar members that their fight is not over until all of the Oración Seis Galáctica are eliminated. Afterward, they pray and end their Prayer Council.

X495 arc

Three years later, Justice mentions that the Prayer Council determined that Shiki Granbell has already become a threat to the universe and the Oración Seis Interstellar recognizes him as a new member of the Oracion Seis Galáctica.

When the Edens Zero escaped to the Kaede Cosmos with fast travel, Feather is contacted by Holy who tells her fellow Interstellar member to switch places with her and declares that it is her turn to launch an "immaculate military operation".

Kaede War arc

Holy informs her two subordinates Ibuki and Shiro , who expressed concern of the other Interstellar members' reactions, that Feather already knows about her team up with two Galáctica members Shiki and Elsie. In turn, Feather relays this information to Justice who is already in the Kaede Cosmos and has arrived on Lendard , and to Cure and Eraser who are surprised to know that so many Galáctica members are in one place before the three members of the Interstellar are on their way to Lendard.

Upon arriving with their respective fleets of Commando Carriers, the three Interstellar members acknowledges Victory 's announcement about Justice's orders to treat Elsie's space pirates and Edens Zero, who joined forces with Holy, as allies for now before focusing on dealing with the Dragons unleashed by God Acnoella .

Members

Current

  • Justice : A member of the Interstellar who is also the leader of his own team of soldiers and a direct subordinate of Jaguar, a fellow Interstellar member. He also shares the same Ether Gear Star Drain with his rival and mutual enemy Elsie Crimson of the Oracion Seis Galáctica, hence his epithet, the " Celestial Judge ".
  • Eraser : A laid back member of the Interstellar who possesses an unnamed Ether Gear that allows him to literally erase anything in his path with blinding white light, hence his namesake and epithet, the " White Flash ".
  • Holy : An outspoken member of the Interstellar who holds the commander rank in the Interstellar Union Army and is the leader of her own team of soldiers . She possesses an unnamed Ether Gear that allows her to melt people into puddles .
  • Cure : A robotic looking member of the Interstellar.
  • Feather : A reserved member of the Interstellar who holds the commander rank in the Interstellar Union Army and is the leader of Connect Team . She possesses Eye of Venus that allows her to observe people's locations and predict enemy movements.

Former

  • Jaguar : A member of the Interstellar who was Justice's superior officer and leader of the Jaguar Unit, holds the captain rank in the Interstellar Union Army, and a former member of the Oracion Seis Galáctica in the past. He possesses an unnamed Ether Gear that allows him to transform into a humanoid jaguar , hence his namesake and epithet, the " Beast Lord ". He was ultimately killed by Poseidon Nero .

Trivia

  • In the simulpub translation, the group's name is translated as Oración Seis Interestelar, which is Spanish for "Interstellar Prayer Six."
  • Their name is simply "Oración Seis" in Japanese, but it's written in kanji as Seikei Rokukishō ( 星系六祈将 , Seikei Rokukishō ) , meaning "Interstellar Six Prayer Generals".
  • All of the Oración Seis Interstellar members' names are codenames.
    • Justice's real name is James Holloway.
  • The Commando Carriers of the Oración Seis Interstellar are named after the codenames of the Dark Guild Oración Seis ' members from Fairy Tail :
    • Justice's Angel Feather is a nod to Angel .
    • Eraser's Hot Eyes is a nod to Hoteye .
    • Feather's Racer is a nod to Racer .
    • Holy's Midnight is a nod to Midnight .
    • Cure’s Brain Dust is a nod to Brain .
