Mother (マザー Mazā ) is a female entity that exists beyond the Sakura Cosmos , and is regarded by adventurers as the Goddess of the Cosmos.

Appearance

Mother is a colossal woman, larger than any planet, with long, flowing cyan hair and five jewels on the crown of her head. She appears to be wearing a tight suit, appearing as if she is robotic, having a white and light blue color pattern across her body, along with grey gloves and boots.

Personality

Mother's personality is currently unknown.

Powers and Abilities

Rebirth : It is said that those who make it to Mother are born again.

Wish Granting : Mother is said to have the ability to grant any wish. So far Xiaomei is the only known person to have their wish granted by Mother.

  • Empowerment : Mother has the ability to gift others with special powers through wishes made to her.
