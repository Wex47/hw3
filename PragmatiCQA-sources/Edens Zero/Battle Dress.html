The Battle Dress ( バトルドレス , Batoru Doresu ) is a form of battle suit utilized by each of the Demon King's Four Shining Stars of Edens Zero and each of the Demon King's Four Dark Stars of Edens One .

Description

As described by Hermit, the Battle Dresses are a collection of battle suits that members of the Four Shining Stars can access via the ability to requip into different forms for combat purposes.
