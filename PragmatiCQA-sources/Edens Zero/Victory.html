Victory (ビクトリー Bikutorī ) is a member of the Interstellar Union Army and a part of Justice's Team .

Appearance

Victory is a burly man with medium brown hair, a square chin, perma-stubble, light brown skin, and brown eyes. He has a thin scar running up his right cheek to the top of his nose. His only attire consists of a black-and-gray armored spacesuit that covers his entire body.

Personality

Victory is a more easygoing and lighthearted man who made a playful joke that Justice is secretly in love with Elsie in spite of the fact that he loathes her, and the joke flew over his head anyway. However, Victory can be confrontational when questioned.

Victory has shown to have a lack of empathy for criminals after stating that the erased victims of Chronophage's attack on Guilst were not to be mourned for.

He believes that this is what victory looks like by defeating his opponents in one hit. On the other hand, Victory has shown to have concern for civilians, telling Shiki and Homura to get down from his ambush on the enemy and asks them if they are not hurt after that.

Synopsis

Skull Fairy arc

Stationed in the Angel Feather among the Interstellar Union Army fleet, Victory jokes that Justice 's pursuit of Elsie Crimson is starting to sound like a love story. Unfortunately, the joke went straight over Justice's head as usual as Victory noted.

Victory sternly questioned Creed of the incompetency of their investigation after his fellow soldier reported that there was an unknown Ether signature detected from the Skull Fairy other than the Armored Space Pirate herself. Then Victory is shocked to hear from Creed that the mystery Ether's levels just keep going up to almost 100,000.

Checking on the Ether readings, Victory notices that there is an abnormal gravity field from Elsie's ship and comments that the numbers don't make any sense. As the Interstellar Union Army fleet surrounds Elsie Crimson's space pirate fleet and are within firing range according to Creed's report, Victory stated that he would like to take a look on the mystery Ether reading more but they've identified the ship carrying the Armored Space Pirate.

However when one ship that has the unknown Ether reading managed to get away during the fleet engagement between Justice and Elsie's forces according to Creed, Victory believed that she wanted to let that ship get away on purpose.

Guilst arc

After learning from Creed that Chronophage had consumed 1,200 years of Guilst 's time and that less than 10% of the planet's population had survived the attack, Victory believes that it is good since most of the victims being erased from existence were criminals, not to shed tears for. But Justice chides him for his lack of empathy as life is still a life.

Red Cave arc

After learning from Creed that Elsie is heading for the Aoi Cosmos where Poseidon Nero resides, Victory is annoyed that things have gotten more complicated with two members of the Oración Seis Galáctica meeting each other just when they have wrapped up the case with Drakken Joe , who is currently in the army's custody. Changing the subject with Creed, Victory did not expect Noah from the Galactic Intelligence Agency 's Sakura Cosmos branch to have a team powerful enough to defeat the Dark Alchemist. But Justice tells his fellow soldiers that the report was not telling the whole truth and reveals that he was monitoring the Ether at Belial Gore , where he identifies it to that of the Demon King from Edens Zero. Victory finds it insane that there is an Ether Gear master out there as good as Drakken or Elsie and is not part of the Oracion Seis Galactica. He is reminded that Justice is also an Ether Gear master too as well, who briefly manifests his Ether Gear. Then Captain Jaguar opens an emergency transmission and orders Victory and Creed to step outside of the bridge of the Angel Feather for they are going to have a Prayer Council. The two immediately complies, leaving Justice alone with Jaguar to attend a Prayer Council with the Oración Seis Interstellar .

Foresta arc

On the Aoi Cosmos, the Interstellar Union Army fleet arrives in the Coral Sector , a neutral territory, to pursue the Skull Fairy. As they chase after it, Victory detects a space pod that has been ejected from Elsie's ship, where Creed informs them of a signal coming from the space pirates , saying that they are releasing a prisoner . Upon arriving on Foresta , Victory asks Justice in concern before hearing from Creed that they have sensed Elsie's Ether reading . Victory reminds Justice that Foresta is a neutral planet with Creed agreeing as it is in Poseidon Nero's territory but the Oración Seis Interstellar member remains undeterred.

Victory ambushes Beast Squad 1 from the sky, telling Shiki and Homura to get down. Using his Ether Gear, he turns into a giant and smashes the three Beast members into the ground, proclaiming this is what victory looks like and surprises the two. He returns to his normal size and asks Shiki and Homura if they are not hurt before telling Creed to apprehend the defeated Beast members. Holding one of the Beast Squad 1's Ether Lock cells to inspect them up close, Victory notes that they are a bunch of small-time hoodlums, which Creed explains to him that the Commando Team ranks under Oceans . Victory and Creed overhear Justice briefly wondering why Poseidon Nero did not deploy Oceans due to the hostile bot situation on Foresta. As the three Interstellar Union Army soldiers take notice of Shiki and Homura, Victory states that he is sensing an unusual Ether from them before Justice realizes that the young man is the Demon King they sensed from Elsie's ship. Victory tries to protest with Justice that he does not see a reason to apprehend the girl when Justice orders Creed to do so. He and Creed are then told by Justice to return to the Angel Feather.

On their way back to the Angel Feather, Victory sees a pillar of Ether rising to the sky and wonders what it is before he and Creed are confronted by the Princess Guards , Gowen and Hyoga . He recognizes them as Elsie's #2 and #3 and finds their brief bickering annoying. However, Victory sees this as an opportunity to capture the two space pirates. He transforms and clashes with Gowen's Flame Bolt , declaring in confidence that victory will be his. However, Victory in his giant form looks around in shock when the Verdant Planet is affected by Xenolith 's Magimech Attack: Gravity Center on a massive scale.

After the intense gravity is gone, Victory in his giant form comments that it almost gave him a heart attack, unaware he is about to step on the Ether Lock cage of Homura unintentionally dropped by Creed on the ground earlier. He overhears Creed telling him to watch his step and realizes his mistake after his fellow soldier released Homura from his Ether Lock before she is stomped on. After Gowen grumpily comments about the Interstellar Union Army locking people up for no reason, Victory retorts the fact Homura is with the space pirates gives them the right to lock her up. When Homura escapes with her friends, Victory angrily states that they won't get away but Gowen declares they won't let the soldiers stop them.

Returning to the Angel Feather in space, a refreshed Victory dryly asks Creed if he is watching dirty videos while on a job upon entering the room. Drinking a small bottle of milk, he simply tells his fellow soldier to not bother with the Crew of Edens as they are barely a threat compared to Elsie and Nero. Although, Victory is curious and takes a look at the phone from Creed where he sees Rebecca the B-Cuber whom he never heard of; Weisz , the man from 50 years in the past; Happy and Pino , two miniature androids; Witch , Sister and Hermit who used to work for Ziggy; Mosco whom he wonders what he is; and finally Shiki, the young man who inherited Ziggy's power. He agrees with Creed that the Crew of Edens are a weird bunch but they don't have time to bother with them. Victory states that Nero and Elsie of the Galáctica and Ziggy are in the Aoi Cosmos. He hopes that this does not turn into anything too catastrophic.

Aoi War arc

Commandeering the Angel Feather in the middle of a space battle, Victory informs Justice in his starfighter that he can't get through Creed and Jesse on Nero 66 . He hopes that the two are alright and asks the Interstellar member if they should go find them. When the Sea Serpent appears, an unnerved Victory thought it was a weapon for The Temple. After witnessing the Sea Serpent's massive beam attack that wiped out their forces, Victory agrees with Justice that they are going to stop the titanic Imperial Army ship. He warns Justice and Eraser that the Sea Serpent is about to unleash a second energy blast and its energy is fully charged.

After Dylan 's sacrifice that immobilized the Sea Serpent, Victory notes that the Nero Empire titanic warship stopped moving. Suddenly, Victory receives a broad-range open transmission from the Edens Zero that addresses to all combatants in space above Nero 66. He is disturbed upon learning from Hermit that 20,000 Imperial Antimatter Bombs are going to destroy Nero 66 and the entire sector around it. When Jesse reports that he is no longer on the planet's surface, Victory expresses relief and asks the G.I.A. agent if Creed is with him only to receive silence instead. He then tells Justice that they are leaving as the Angel Feather and its fleet withdraw.

When Jesse boards the ship, Victory is informed that Creed is dead and that Edens Zero was responsible. Suddenly, a soldier of the Angel Feather informs him that a transport pod is approaching with a signal from Edens Zero stating that they are returning Creed's body. Victory is shocked that his comrade is really dead as Jesse goes to check on the pod.

In the aftermath of the Aoi Cosmos conflict, Victory drinks milk as Justice enters the room and wonders why he did not bring up Ziggy in the Prayer Council, revealing that he was listening, much to his superior's chargin. He notes that while it was Nero's Empire Dice that killed Jaguar, it was not the captain who gave Nero his wounds. Putting down his bottle of milk, Victory tells Justice that he wants to talk about Creed. He wonders why the Edens Zero crew would send Creed's body back to them, which Justice believes that they wanted to make an example of their comrade. Victory counters that it does not make sense, saying that one makes an example when they want to keep people always in a state of fear and then it is better to kill them violently. He suspiciously notes that Creed died from a single gunshot in a middle of a war and tells Justice that he is not really sure they really killed him. He notes that Jesse was in serious shock and might not be remembering things exactly right when Justice brings him up. When Justice states that it does not change the fact Creed is dead, Victory sadly agrees. Victory silently frowns after his superior swears that he will render justice on whoever it was that killed Creed. When Jesse claims that it was Homura who murdered Creed, Victory looks at him suspiciously.

X495 arc

In the Kaede Cosmos , Victory watches a new report, remarking that the robot uprising on the planet Sweeds is quieting down. While noting that the Crew of Edens are ahead of them again, the soldier admits that, with Ziggy's influence growing everywhere, he doesn't know what they would do without them. Victory reports to Justice that 17 planets have been saved by the Edens Zero for 3 years and comments that they are shaping up to be better good guys than themselves much to his superior's protest.

Powers and Abilities

Titan's Arm (タイタンズアーム Taitanzu Āmu ): Victory possesses an Ether Gear that allows him to enlarge his body to gigantic proportions and can revert back to normal at will. The marks appear on the right side of his face.

  • Enhanced Strength : In his giant form, Victory is strong enough to defeat the strongest Beast squad in one hit.

Enhanced Agility : Despite his large size, Victory has shown to be rather agile, having displayed the strength to jump several meters in the air before ambushing Beast Squad 1 from above.
