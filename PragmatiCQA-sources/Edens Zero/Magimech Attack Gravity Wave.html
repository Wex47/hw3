Magimech Attack: Gravity Wave ( 魔械流 重力波 , Makai-ryū: Jūryokuha , lit. "Magic Machine Style: Gravity Wave") is a Satan Gravity technique.

Contents

  • 1 Description
  • 2 Usage
  • 4 Site Navigation

Description

The user's palm is placed on the ground, creating a spiral force that pulls the targets towards the user.

Usage

Shiki first used this technique to draw in all the members of Sibir's Gang around him so that he could beat them back with his Gravity Fist Frenzy .
