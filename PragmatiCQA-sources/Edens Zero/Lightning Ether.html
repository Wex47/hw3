Lightning Ether ( ライトニングエーテル , Raitoningu Ēteru ) , also known as Thunder Ether ( サンダーエーテル , Sandā Ēteru ) , is an Element Ether technique.

Description

The user changes their Ether type to lightning, allowing them to shock their opponents from a distance by conducting electricity through the floor or summoning lightning from the sky or a weapon.
