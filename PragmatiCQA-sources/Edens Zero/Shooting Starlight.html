Shooting Starlight (流星の灯 Ryūsei no Tomoshibi ) is an adventurers' guild located on the planet Blue Garden , where people register to become Adventurers and obtain passports to travel to any sort of planet.

Description

The headquarters building for Shooting Starlight is a tall spire with a round base and a ring levitating around the top of the structure.

Location

Shooting Starlight is located within a plaza inside the city of Eternal .

History

The Shooting Starlight guild was founded on the year X442, already garnering many members.

By X482, Noah Glenfield became the Guild Master of Shooting Starlight as part of his undercover work for the Galactic Intelligence Agency 's Sakura Cosmos branch.

At some point after the Four Shining Stars disbanded, Sister joined Shooting Starlight as its member and did jobs assigned to her by Master Noah . In the year X482, she was sent to Guilst with a task to free the petrified girls in Illega Tower , only to be somehow captured and impersonated by the leader of Rogue Out for 10 years.

Intro arc

After leaving Planet Granbell , Rebecca and Happy brings Shiki to Shooting Starlight at Planet Blue Garden where they register him as a new member of the Adventurers' Guild. Shiki is amazed to see many Adventurers inside the Guild and attempts to befriend them, only to be met with dismissal and ridicule. Not helped when the young man claims to have met Mother before and is mocked for it.

At the Diner Fairy's , the adopted grandson of the Demon King expresses his thoughts that he expected Shooting Starlight to be more friendly like a big family, which Rebecca explains to him that it is more a place for people to get work and she does not know anyone other than Clarisse in the guild personally. When Shiki's online registration for Shooting Starlight membership is finished, he obtains an Adventurer License with a "Class-E" on the card, the lowest rank of Adventurers, just like Rebecca and Happy.

After Shiki, Rebecca and Happy leave for Norma , Elsie Crimson and her space pirate crew pay a visit to Shooting Starlight for information regarding Shiki, surprising the Adventurers in the Guild of her presence. One member of the Guild casually tells the Armored Space Pirate that Shiki and his friends were heading for Norma. Elsie informs her crew that said planet it is already dead .
