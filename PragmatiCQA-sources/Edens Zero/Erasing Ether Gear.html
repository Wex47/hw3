Erasing Ether Gear is an unnamed type of Ether Gear that involves erasing.

Description

This Ether Gear allows the user to erase anything in the user's line of sight with a blinding flash of light, by waving their hand over their target. They need to charge an unspecified amount of Ether for an unknown period of time before using it. However, using this power on many targets can exhaust the user.
