Description

Dragonfall is a sector of space that is swarming with Dragons , acting as a border to the entire Sakura Cosmos. Rumor has it that the dragons found there can swallow small ships in the blink of an eye, while a single dragon can destroy an entire fleet of battleships.

History

Red Cave arc

When the Crew of Edens arrive at Dragonfall they quickly discover that many of the Dragons are dead. Witch analyzes the Ether residue and finds that they are in the aftermath of the Edens One . A flock of surviving Dragons soon chase after the Edens Zero . The Crew of Edens manage to dispath of the Dragons chasing them with the Edens Zero's defenses, allowing them to escape the Sakura Cosmos, leading them to end up in a sea in space which turns out to be the Poseidon Nero Sector in the Aoi Cosmos.
