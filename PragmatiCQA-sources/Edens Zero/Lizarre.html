Lizarre ( リザール , Rizāru ) was a member of Oasis , a rebel army formed to oppose the tyrannical rule of Poseidon Nero .

Appearance

Lizarre is a tall lizard-like alien being with spiky hair. He wears a dark jacket with lighter sleeves and hockey pads, baggy pants with a belt, and a pair of long dark boots.

Synopsis

Sandra arc

Lizarre and his fellow rebels point their guns at Laguna and the Crew of Edens when they enter the Oasis base. However, he is shocked after Shiki easily disarmed him and and his comrades of their weapons with Satan Gravity .

Lizarre simply stands with Russo silently when Homura states that he and the members of Oasis are strong. He is stoically surprised when Zoy barges in to inform everyone that there is an enemy attack where the Imperial Army soldiers are in the marketplace.

Lizarre charges out with his fellow rebels to fight the Imperial soldiers at the marketplace of the city and opens fire on them. He continues firing his weapon at the enemy with his comrades in battle. Lizarre stands silent as he watches over the captured Imperial Army troops inside their cages, and later answers Goodwin's question that they have lost 16 comrades. The lizard-like alien member listens to the Oasis rebel leader explaining that it is just two sides raising the death count while refusing to give in. Lizarre is annoyed when Shiki hugs him and Ibaraki, telling them that they are all friends now.

Aoi War arc

Within the bridge of the Maine Coon , Lizarre stands silent with his arms folded in the background as Ibaraki tells Laguna that all members of Oasis are doing good. He boards his starfighter along with Russo and Gomakichi and declares that he's ready to launch, heading out to engage the Imperial Army ships in space.
