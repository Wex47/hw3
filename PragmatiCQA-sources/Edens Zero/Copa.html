Appearance

Personality

Copa is a scared and helpless girl as she cried in tears of fear when she learns that Rogue Out had kidnapped her and taken her to Guilst.

However, Copa seemed smarter than she looks when she is the first of the kidnapped B-Cubers to notice that the foam Illega used to dissolve their clothes also dissolves the glass and metal, which gives Rebecca an idea to escape through their cell's only glass window.

Synopsis

Guilst arc

Copa is one of several B-Cubers who has been kidnapped by Rogue Out and transported on a spaceship heading towards the planet Guilst . She was crying in fear after learning they were being sold to Illega , which annoys Ganoff who wanted her to shut up. But luckily, Rebecca stood up to him in Copa's defense.

Digitalis arc

Equipment

B-Cube (Ｂ・キューブ B Kyūbu ): As a B-Cuber,she uses a B-Cube terminal to record, upload, and share videos on her channel that has 850,000 subscribers.
