Meteor ( 流星 ( ミーティア ) , Mītia ) is a Star Drain technique.

Description

The user's body is surrounded by a cloak of Ether , allowing them to move at incredible speeds through the air. With this, they assault their opponents with quick and powerful melee attacks.

Derived Techniques

  • Meteor Frenzy

Usage

Justice uses this technique to attack Shiki in a lariat strike with incredible speed and throw him several meters across Foresta in their fight . Elsie also uses this technique to keep up with Justice during their battle .

Justice uses this technique to outspeed Ziggy's Gravity Comet and rescue Elsie from the Demon King's attack.

Trivia

  • This technique shares its name with the Heavenly Body Magic spell of the same name from Hiro Mashima 's previous work Fairy Tail .
