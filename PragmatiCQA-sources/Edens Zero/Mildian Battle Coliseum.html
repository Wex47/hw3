The Mildian Battle Coliseum (ミルディアンバトルコロシアム Mirudian Batoru Koroshiamu ) is an arena accessible from the Temple of Knowledge .

Description

The Mildian Battle Coliseum is an amphitheatre that Xiaomei uses to entertain the Picoratta population of Mildian , and as a payment for knowledge with battles between combatants.

Location

The Mildian Battle Coliseum is accessed through the water-like floor of the Temple of Knowledge , causing visitors to fall from the coliseum ceiling. Inversely, falling through the coliseum floor leads back to the Temple of Knowledge in the same fashion, which also undoes any damage or alteration to the visitors' clothing made since entering the coliseum.
