Description

The Sea Devil is a large mechanical suit that is taller than an average person and is heavily armored with large gauntlets for combat. It has a HUD view for the user within the unique helmet along with two wing appendages and rocket thrusters on the back.

Special Features

Flight : The Sea Devil possesses the ability to fly and hover in the air.

Drone : The suit comes with a small drone that has a circular head with a dark ring and three protrusions under it. It comes equipped with a laser that's strong enough to blast through several structures with ease.

Ether Bullets : The Sea Devil can fire a spray of Ether bullets from guns mounted on its left forearm at a target. By raising its hand upwards, the user can also prepare a huge volley of Ether bullets that hover in the air before directing them at the target in an overwhelming barrage.

Missiles : The Sea Devil can fire a barrage of missiles from its right forearm at a target.

Ether Coating : The Sea Devil is coated in a force field of Ether that protects it from Ether Gear attacks. A powerful blast of Ether from Arsenal Suit was harmlessly deflected after it struck the suit's Ether coating. Despite this protection however, the Ether coating couldn't stop the Sea Devil from being directly manipulated and disassembled by Weisz 's Machina Maker .
