Shiki Granbell & Rebecca Bluegarden vs. Foote Brothers is a fight between two adventurers from Shooting Starlight , Shiki Granbell and Rebecca Bluegarden , and the Southern Annihilators, the Foote Brothers , from Sibir's Gang .

Prologue

After their attck on Sibir's Gang Weisz flees with Happy and Pino , while Shiki and Rebecca Bluegarden are left to survive on their own. Shiki then saves Rebecca from getting crushed by a giant piston, but gets crushed himself. Sibir then proceeds to go after Weisz and orders the Foote Brothers to take care of Rebecca who is attempting to move the piston that crushed Shiki. The brothers then introduces themselfs to the girl after telling her that she got in their way. The hole Shiki ended up in is revealed to hold scrapped robots including Johnny . Shiki then fails to repair Johnny with the latter showing gratitude to the former for at least trying to help him.

Battle

The dark haired Foote Brother kicks out after Rebecca but misses leaving a deep mark in the piston behind her. The other brother instantly attcks her but notices her beautiful legs when she falls to grab a gun. The first, slim brother then also notices this detail about the girl. She is about to shoot them when they point it out, but that just makes her call them perverts and shoot them out of embarrassed rage. They however blocks the attacks with their metal-like legs only for Shiki to reappear. Shiki then uses Magimech Attack: Gravity Windmill Kick to defeat the brothers and making them unable to fight on.

Aftermatch

Shiki asks Rebecca about Pino and Sibir's whereabouts only to dash off. The Foote Brothers then asks Rebecca to show them her legs and the girl responds by calling them stubborn.
