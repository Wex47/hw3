The Sun Jewel arc ( サン・ジュエル編 , San Jueru-hen ) is the seventh story arc of the Edens Zero series.

Homura's long-awaited reunion with Valkyrie appears to be close at hand as the Crew of Edens sets a course for Sun Jewel , the Planet of Gemstones. Once they arrive, the crew quickly runs afoul of the planet's cruel and powerful ruler, Madame Kurenai , who is in the middle of her own struggle for dominance with Drakken Joe , one of the Oración Seis Galáctica . As the Edens Zero itself becomes caught in the crossfire, the mysteries surrounding Valkyrie's disappearance and Homura's past are unveiled. What will Homura learn about her beloved mentor now that she's finally within reach?
