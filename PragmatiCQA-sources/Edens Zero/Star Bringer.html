The Star Bringer ( スターブリンガー , Sutā Buringā ) is the main cannon of the Edens Zero and Edens One .

  • 4 Site Navigation

Description

The Star Bringer is a cannon that can fire powerful beams from the mouth of the Edens Zero and the Edens One . There is a countdown from three before the cannon is fired at the target. It uses enough Ether at 50% charge to exhaust three of the Four Shining Stars when they are used to power the last 11% of it to destroy the Satellite Blaze .

Usage

Witch , Sister and Hermit first use the Star Bringer of the Edens Zero to destroy the Satellite Blaze that Drakken aimed towards Blue Garden .

Connor uses the Star Bringer of the Edens One in an attempt to wipe out the Edens Zero and its crew but missed due to the intervention of the Skull Fairy of Elsie Crimson .
