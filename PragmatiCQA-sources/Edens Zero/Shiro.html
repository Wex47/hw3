Shiro ( シロ , Shiro ) is a member of the Interstellar Union Army and a subordinate of Holy of the Oración Seis Interstellar .

Appearance

Shiro is a short young woman with a curvy figure, a prominent nose and a light shorter hair. She wears a dark sleeveless dress with a poofy collar, light stripes around it and a pair of separate arm gloves along with a fur cap that has a pair of bear ears on both sides and a black-and-white head of a bear at the center.

Personality

Shiro is an enthusiastic woman who is willing to follow orders from Holy, even after expressing concern of her leader taking the risk of being aboard the ship of an Oración Seis Galáctica member , and is excited for the upcoming operation that her commander has plotted.

Synopsis

Kaede War arc

In charge of the Midnight fleet, Shiro asks her commander if she is really giving her orders from the Edens Zero . She enthusiastically complies with Holy's order to show their enemies the power of their ships and of the upcoming "immaculate military operation".

Complying with Holy's orders to eliminate all of the Dragons , Shiro pilots the Midnight alongside Ibuki and fires the special anti-Dragon Ether cannons alongside their forces, destroying them in droves. She mocks the ships of Elsie Crimson 's space pirate fleet to be a bunch of junk. However, she states with a look of annoyance that it will not be easy to defeat so many Dragons.
