The Mildian arc is the sixth story arc of the Edens Zero series.

Hermit officially rejoins the Four Shining Stars, which just leaves the Crew of Edens to find Valkyrie , the "Sword of Edens" and Homura's mentor. With no leads on her whereabouts, the crew seeks out answers from the legendary fortune teller Xiaomei on Mildian , the Planet of Time. But Xiaomei won't give just give them the information for free; they'll have to earn it in her Battle Coliseum, where each crew member must win in a 1-on-1 match against their opponents, including a surprising face !
