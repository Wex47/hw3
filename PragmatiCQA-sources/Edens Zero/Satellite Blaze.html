Satellite Blaze (サテライトブレイズ Sateraito Bureizu ) also known as Oculus Kurenai (紅の目 Kurenai no Me ) was a weapon used by Kurenai Kōgetsu until it was stolen by Drakken Joe .

Description

Satellite Blaze was a weapon, that can be used to track and kill anyone who opposes its user. It is powerful enough to destroy entire planets. There is a Protection Matrix shielding Satellite Blaze from weak attacks, though it can easily be broken by a powerful cannon such as the Star Bringer of Edens Zero .

Satellite Blaze is seen as Divine Retribution in Gold Palace .
