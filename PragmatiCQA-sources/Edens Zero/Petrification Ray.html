Petrification Ray is a type of weapon utilized by Illega .

Description

This gun has an ability of turning a person into solid stone , due to an extracted portion of Drakken Joe 's Ether Gear , Alchemist . The effects can be removed by Heal Atomizer 's Dispel Driver .
