The Aqua Wing (アクアウイング Akua Uingu ) is a spaceship previously owned by Rebecca Bluegarden , and currently owned by Weisz Steiner .

Description

The Aqua Wing is a blue spaceship whose structure resembles that of a fish with a large dorsal fin, labeled with "Aoneko Ch" along with a cat's paw mark indicating its association with the Aoneko Channel .

The spaceship has four nozzles at the rear end serving as the primary propulsion system when flying in space, while four other nozzles (two on each side of the wings) acts as a secondary mode, the ship can both travel in space and surf on the water.

After being upgraded by Weisz with Machina Maker , the Aqua Wing now has an extra set of "eyes", a larger dorsal fin, longer wings with an extra set of nozzles, and turbines under each wing.

Abilities

The Aqua Wing can levitate, surf on water, and travel in space.

Autopilot : As long as the destination coordinates are inputted properly, the ship can pilot itself there.
