Valkyrie Armament System ( ヴァルキリー兵装システム , Varukirī Heisō Shisutemu ) was a Battle Dress used by Valkyrie Yuna .

Description

Valkyrie transforms her mainframe as her body surges with Ether, the upper right portion of her face turning black and her back re-configuring into wing-like ports.
