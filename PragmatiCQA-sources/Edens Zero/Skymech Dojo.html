The Skymech Dojo ( 天械流道場 , Tenkai-ryū Dōjō ) was a training dojo on the planet Guilst .

Description

The Skymech Dojo teaches Skymech Ninjutsu to its students, of which its techniques can be used for Ether Gears related to the wind or mist .

History

Nine years ago, Kris and Callum are among the students that were learning Skymech Ninjutsu, where the former was bullied by the other students for being an O-Tech . The sole exception was Callum, who trained and sparred with him.
