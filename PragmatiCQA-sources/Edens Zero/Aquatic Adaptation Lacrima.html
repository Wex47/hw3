Aquatic Adaptation Lacrima ( 水中適合の 星水晶 ( ラクリマ ) , Suichū Tekigō no Rakurima ) are devices that allow humans to breathe and communicate underwater.

  • 4 Site Navigation

Description

The devices take the form of collars with a refined piece of Lacrima clipped onto the front.

Usage

The Crew of Edens used several of these devices provided by Hermit to allow Shiki , Rebecca , Weisz and Homura to explore underwater on Red Cave and investigate the crashed ship.

Shiki's Aquatic Adaptation Lacrima proved to be a suitable vessel for Mother 's Ether which voluntarily went inside of the Lacrima and glows with the colours of the rainbow.
