The Queen Nova ( クイーンノヴァ , Kuīn Nova ) is a missile launched by the Kurenai Dragoon .

Description

The Queen Nova is a homing missile that is fired from the Kurenai Dragoon's left hand. It is able to do sharp turns and is therefore able to always hit its target, even if they dodge in the last second before impact. The Queen Nova has enough energy to destroy the Kurenai Dragoon under the right circumstances.
