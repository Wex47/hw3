The Imperial Antimatter Bombs ( 帝国式反物質爆弾 , Teikoku-shiki Hanbusshitsu Bakudan ) are bombs used by the Nero Empire .

  • 4 Site Navigation

Description

There are 20,000 electronically controlled Imperial Antimatter Bombs contained within The Temple . Just one bomb has enough power to reduce a small planet to dust.

Usage

After defeating Poseidon Nero , Ziggy activates the 20,000 Imperial Antimatter Bombs with the All-Link System through his recently acquired Wormhole to detonate and destroy Nero 66 and its entire sector.
