Description

The interior consists of a wide space filled with circular tables, curtains, and poles on a stage. A door with an exit sign above it is by a short stage. A dressing room that is filled with mannequins dressed in various revealing outfits, curtains for individual rooms, a dresser for shoes, a board with masks, and carpet on the floor. The main room is decorated with various statues and signs, along with a second floor for viewing. The exterior is a large building with numerous windows and signs, several designed with legs have "Spica" on them, the entrance has a sign reading "Beard" above it.

Location

Spica is located somewhere within the Belial Gore.

History

Belial Gore arc

World No. 29

During their investigation, Weisz and Shiki check this place, before quickly leaving because of Homura and Pino . After escaping Sylph , Homura finds the dressing room and wears a dress from here after her kimono was stolen. The manager finds and mistakes Homura for one of the dancers, making her go out on stage. Sylph finds Homura and places everyone else in a Wind Cage , allowing their fight to continue. Eventually Sylph manages to subdue Homura and captures her. A week later, the manager and a dancer watch in the dressing room the news of Sibir 's execution from a week ago.

World No. 30

The club is used again as Homura and Sylph battle each other, with all the dancers and guests gone because of the arrival of the Edens Zero .
