Description

History

The Crew of Edens uses the inn as a meeting place to discuss about what happened with Hermit after she rejected them. They soon hear the bells ringing and sees outside the window the uproar of the NPC townsfolk. They exit the inn to investigate, where they see a large horde of monsters coming to attack the city of Crysta.
