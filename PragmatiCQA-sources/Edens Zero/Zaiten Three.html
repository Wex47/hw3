The Zaiten Three (三罪天 Sanzaiten , lit. "Three Sin Devas") were a team of enforcers who work directly under Kurenai Kōgetsu on Sun Jewel .

History

Members

  • Garrot : The "Scourge of Heaven", Benzaiten (鞭罪天 Benzaiten , lit. "Whip Sin Deva"), and warden of Sun Jewel's Labor District . He wields a paralyzing whip that stuns anything it touches and moves in whatever direction he wills it to.
  • Nino : The "Fist of Heaven", Kenzaiten (拳罪天 Kenzaiten , lit. "Fist Sin Deva"). He uses a form of Ether Gear called Soul Arm to create extra arms of solid Ether for combat. He is also an anime otaku and famous B-Cuber who reports various anime-related topics. Later decided to defect to the rebels.
  • Baku : The "Ax of Heaven", Fuzaiten (斧罪天 Fuzaiten , lit. "Axe Sin Deva"). He carries a large battle axe and boasts incredible physical speed faster than any human is capable of.
