The X495 arc is the fourteenth story arc of the Edens Zero series and the first arc in the Kaede Cosmos Saga .

Three years following the Aoi War, the Edens Zero 's crew have liberated planets under Ziggy's control, but have yet to find a clue on his whereabouts, until they find a man floating in space. Meanwhile, the Interstellar Union Army have plans to fight Shiki, now recognized as one the Oración Seis Galáctica and a threat to the universe.

Story Impact

  • The Edens Zero Crew has entered the Kaede Cosmos .
  • Shiki has been recently recognized as a member of the Oración Seis Galáctica by the Oración Seis Interestelar .

  • The Ether Gears of Feather and Holy are revealed.
  • Rebecca has achieved her Overdrive powers.
  • Connor decides to join the crew after seeing their resolve.
  • Holy meets the crew on planet Dahlia , offering an alliance with them to fight Ziggy .
  • Deadend Crow is revealed to have teamed up with Ziggy.
  • Labilia is revealed to have an incurable disease.
    • Labilia also reveals to Rebecca that she is the reason why Labilia became a B-Cuber.
  • Holy reveals that she is from the Yukino Cosmos and that it along with the other three cosmoses , make up the "Grand Shiki Cosmos".
