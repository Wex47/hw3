Description

Gate 66 appears to be a cave with its opening being covered by multiple entrances that are labeled as A,B,C and D while also having multiple turrets surrounding it.

Location

The gate is located in an unspecified part on the surface of the planet Norma .

Synopsis

Norma arc

Shiki , Rebecca and Happy made use of this entranceway to access the underground city beneath the surface of Norma after crash-landing nearby.
