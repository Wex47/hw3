Healing Ether is a type of Ether that focuses on healing.

Description

The user uses this Ether as part of their Heal Atomizer , capable of healing multiple targets. It can mend wounds, undo negative status effects, and repair "living" Machines . However, if the target is healed too much, the target ends up damaged instead of healed.

The Healing Ether can be packaged into an energy drink in medicinal form called Sister Energy , which works on both humans and bots as an effective restorative.
