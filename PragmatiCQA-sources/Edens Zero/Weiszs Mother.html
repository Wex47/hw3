Weisz's Mother was the mother of Weisz Steiner . She died after getting her life force slowly stolen by Drakken Joe 's life-support device in the year X432.

Appearance

She had a womanly figure with long light hair. While bedridden in the hospital she wore a dark dress with a frilly collar.

Personality

She loved her son, trying to comfort him while she was sick and told her that he would not be alone if she's gone, telling him of another mother for everyone in the cosmos.

Synopsis

History

In the year X432, Weisz's mother became sick because Drakken Joe 's life support device was slowly draining her, giving her this shorter lifespan. She told her son that he would not be alone because he has another mother , who is the mother to everyone in the cosmos. She gave Weisz a pendant at some point before her death.
