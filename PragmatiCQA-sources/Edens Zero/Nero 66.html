Nero 66 ( ネロ６６ , Nero 66 ) was a planet located in the Aoi Cosmos , noted to be a rather distant planet from Nero 1 . It was known as the Planet of Ash ( 灰の惑星 , Hai no Wakusei ) .

Description

Nero 66 is a planet where the atmosphere above has what appears to be varying masses of clouds reaching out to space and also has streams of space fish flowing towards it, while also having a constant storm of what appears to be ash flowing in the atmosphere. The architecture seems to consist of tall rectangular buildings of varying heights. It houses the All-Link System , a system that commands all of the bots in the Aoi Cosmos and is the last resort allowed only to the rulers of The Temple. Like the other planets of the Aoi Cosmos, a stream of space water flows near it.

Because of the planet's weather, the city is protected by a dome. The ash within Nero 66's atmosphere can affect the human body and it is recommended that there will be no long-term activity outside the dome. Witch attempts to analyze its composition but could not identify all of its components.

History

Foresta arc

Shura has Ijuna prepare their departure to Nero 66 with the Oceans 6 , as it may hold clues to finding Ziggy .

Sandra arc

After his public announcement to the Nero Empire , Shura is at Nero 66 where he knows that Ziggy is after the All-Link System and is preparing for the Demon King's arrival.

Aoi War arc

Location

Prior to its destruction, the planet was located in a unspecified part of the Aoi Cosmos, but is noted to be rather far from Nero 1.
