Dramour Ore (ドラムール鉱 Doramūru-kō ) is the heaviest mineral on Guilst .

Description

Dramour Ore is incredibly tough and heavy, as Drakken chose to transform a glass bottle into this substance by using his Ether Gear to torture Murray Morrison , and later to block Shiki 's heaviest attack of Satan Gravity .
