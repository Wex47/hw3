Ether Gear ( エーテルギア , Ēteru Gia ) is a type of power that harnesses Ether . Those who utilize Ether Gear are known as Ether Gearists .

Description

Similar to a machine , Ether Gear utilizes the Ether that flows from the user's body and rearranges it by turning it into power, making the user into a " mechanical wizard " of sorts. This gives its users the ability to manipulate certain elements such as gravity and wind, reconfigure machines, create swords using Ether, or even completely alter the material composition of objects the user touches. However, Ether Gears have a peculiar weakness, in that they become completely unusable whenever the wielder's hands are bound or wrists are tied. However, these deficits can be remedied via training to overcome physical restraints, but even then utilizing one's Ether Gear when restrained takes a great deal of practice. They are also negatively affected when exposed to an Electromagnetic Pulse , which temporarily disables them from using their powers.

Ether Gears typically require multiple years of training in order to master, although some individuals are talented enough to do so after only a single month. After their initial manifestation, Ether Gears can awaken and reveal more abilities. The power of an Ether Gear can also be extracted by means of special devices and imbued into weapons, such as the Petrification Ray or vehicle-mounted weapons. A majority of Ether Gears typically grant powers that enhance the user's own abilities, whilst Aoi Ether Gears weaken that of the opponent's.

History

Ether Gear is mentioned to have been a power from the Dark Ages.
