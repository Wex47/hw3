Magimech Attack: Anti-Gravity ( 魔械流 反重力 , Makai-ryū: Hanjūryoku ) is a Satan Gravity technique.

Contents

  • 1 Description
  • 2 Usage
  • 4 Site Navigation

Description

The user generates an anti-gravitational field around whatever they touch, serving as a shield to bounce off incoming projectiles.

Usage

Shiki first used this technique to deflect countless missiles from the Belial Gore 's defenses activated by Drakken Joe , protecting his friends and their spaceship, the Aqua Wing .
