Illega Tower ( イリーガタワー , Irīga Tawā ) was the headquarters of the criminal Illega , located on the planet Guilst , where he kept numerous kidnapped B-Cubers as petrified statues . After Chronophage consumed Guilst's 1,200 years into the past, Illega Tower ceased to exist.

Description

Illega Tower consisted of a thin tower that supported a wide, saucer-shaped structure on top of it. It was connected to several of the surrounding buildings by cables or pipes.

Location

Illega Tower was located within a run-down city on Guilst.
