Valkyrie Yuna ( ヴァルキリー・ユナ , Varukirī Yuna ) was a machine and formerly one of the Four Shining Stars . As the Sword of Edens , she was responsible for maintaining Edens Zero 's armaments. She was also the teacher of Homura Kōgetsu , a member of the Crew of Edens , and Paul .

Appearance

Valkyrie is a machine with the figure of a tall yet slim, woman with a dark skin tone, salmon pink eyes, and matching long hair tied in a ponytail with a lock of hair that hangs over the left side of her face. She wears a white armored battle suit that is form-fitting with green gauntlets, pauldrons, and a pair of winged boots with the emblem "E1" on her chest. She has a seam line on her face that goes through her right eye.

Personality

According to Homura, Valkyrie was an awe-inspiring individual who had a mischievous side to her. She also had an air of intensity, and was very encouraging and caring towards her pupil. She believed in the invigorating power of one's own words, as demonstrated when she first encouraged Homura to speak her mind. According to Sister Ivry she liked to look at the stars.

Synopsis

History

Valkyrie was created by the former Demon King, Ziggy , on Planet Granbell as a member of the Demon King's Four Shining Stars . At some point on Oedo , she saved a very young Homura Kōgetsu from three thugs who wanted to sell her on Guilst , and took her on as a disciple, training her until her sudden disappearance around X487. It is later revealed that Valkyrie disappeared in order to find Homura's biological mother after suffering discrimination by the citizens of Oedo for not being an actual blood relative of Homura which affected her young pupil. She traveled to Mildian to see Xiaomei to learn the location of Homura's real mother Kurenai Kōgetsu . She traveled to Planet Sun Jewel , meets Paul who would become her disciple after being inspired by her, and finds Kurenai imprisoned within the Labor District . After learning that Kurenai can't return because she accumulated a huge debt at the wealthy sector casinos, Valkyrie offers to pay off her debt in her place so that she can return to Homura, which she tearfully accepts.

Two years passed and Valkyrie found out from Paul that Kurenai never returned to Homura after being freed. Instead, she became the wife of the then ruler Baron Mordo , assassinated him, and seized his wealth and power for herself as the new ruler of the planet instead. Moments later the Labor District was attacked by a huge swarm of Stones , provoked by Kurenai to force the laborers to mine more metals for her. It was then, Valkyrie fought the swarm entirely on her own to protect the people of the Labor District. She ultimately defeated the swarm but had suffered critical damage during the fight. Thinking about Homura, she knelt down and lamented that she never told Homura that she indeed loved her before she finally succumbed to her wounds and died. Despite this, Valkyrie becomes a symbol of hope to the rebellion with Paul forming the Valkyrie's Army .

Sun Jewel arc

While in the Labor District, Paul talks to Valkyrie's remains about the arrival of Homura, now a member of the Crew of Edens , asking what they should do about it. Later after finding out the truth, Homura and the others find Valkyrie's smiling corpse, kneeling in the center of the mining area where she died heroically and Paul tells them how she died protecting the people. Homura stays with Valkyrie's remains, until she hears the sound of battle and tries to get up but is paralyzed. Garrot arrives and reveals the slow-acting drugs she got from his whip are starting to take effect. Garrot then goes to Valkyrie, saying that he has some respect for her, proceeding to lick her face and say that she was special. He hits Valkyrie's head with his whip, making her head fall off. Homura then uses Odin Strike to knock Valkyrie's sword away into the air, making it pierce through Garrot. Her head is later placed back.

After the short reunion between mother and daughter, Homura goes back to Valkyrie's remains and leaves her doll that she used to carry as a child. Stating that if it represents her bond with her mother, then she wants Valkyrie to have it. Homura thanks her for all she's done, calling her mother. After the liberation of Sun Jewel, the Edens Zero crew decides to leave Valkyrie's corpse on Sun Jewel as a symbol of hope for the planet. The team returns to the Edens Zero, informing the remaining Shining Stars of Valkyrie's death. Sister refuses to accept Valkyrie's death and asks why they did not bring her body back. After explaining their reasons, Sister insists she could fix or rebuild her, only for Witch to tell her that it would not be the same. To honor Valkyrie's memory, Homura steps up to replace her teacher's position as "The Sword of Edens". Afterward at the observatory, Sister and Witch followed and both Shining Stars mourned for their fallen friend in private.

Powers and Abilities

Soul Blade (ソウルブレイド Sōru Bureido ): Valkyrie has the ability to create and wield swords made of Ether .

  • Odin Strike (戦乙女最終奥義 オーディンストライク Ikusa Otome Saishū Ōgi: Ōdin Sutoraiku ): After activating her Valkyrie Armament System , Valkyrie summons an insurmountable number of Ether blades around herself and launches them upon her enemies, creating explosions on impact.

Battle Dress (バトルドレス Batoru Doresu ): As one of the Four Shining Stars, Valkyrie possesses the ability to requip into a different outfit designed for combat purposes.

  • Valkyrie Armament System (ヴァルキリー兵装システム Varukirī Heisō Shisutemu ): Valkyrie transforms her mainframe as her body surges with Ether, the upper right portion of her face turning black and her back reconfiguring into wing-like ports.

Enhanced Senses : As with all modern androids, Valkyrie should be equipped with heat sensors and thermal imaging allowing her to easily detect high temperatures in her surroundings.

Longevity : As a machine, Valkyrie is resistant to aging and lives far longer than humans.

Skin Customization : Valkyrie, along with the other members of the Four Shining Stars, can customize the appearance of her skin at will, allowing her to hide her mechanical seams to appear more human or give herself an instant tan.

Master Swordsmanship Specialist : Valkyrie is a highly accomplished swordswoman and the one who instructed Homura in using her sword-based Ether Gear. A duplicate of Valkyrie held the upper hand against Homura during most of their battle with skillful fencing before ultimately being defeated however Homura is confident that the real Valkyrie would not be so easily defeated.
