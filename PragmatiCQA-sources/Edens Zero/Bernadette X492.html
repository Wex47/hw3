Bernadette ( バーナデッド , Bānadeddo ) is an alien rabbit woman who once worked as a waitress in Norma 's seedy underworld. She is currently traveling with Professor Weisz . She has a younger counterpart living in the post- Chronophage eaten Norma.

Appearance

Bernadette is an alien woman with purple hair and eyes, light skin, and has many rabbit features, including ears, nose, hands, and a tail. She wears a backless dark outfit with a fur collar and a rabbit-shaped cleavage window. She appears to have aged with very little changes, if none at all, after 50 years had passed.

Personality

Bernadette finds Professor Weisz more charming as an older man compared to his younger self from 50 years ago.

Synopsis

Norma arc

After Shiki and his friends escaped from Norma, Bernadette of the pre- Chronophage Norma is with Professor Weisz who finally manages to contact them and informs them about the Chronophage, showing that she is one of the many of the planet's inhabitants who escaped from the cosmic monster that consumed 50 years of Norma's time. She sits next to the professor in his ship, being recognized by Shiki. Bernadette listens to the professor explaining to Pino that it was the Great Demon King who created her and also revealing that he and the younger Weisz are two separate entities that will neither affect each other nor cause time paradoxes. Affectionately embracing him, she comments that Professor Weisz's real charm comes with age.
