Kawpicatt S4 (ニーテルＳ４ Nīteru S4 ) are small mimic-type parasites that tend to infest spaceships.

Appearance

The default appearance of the Kawpicatts are small, lavender amorphous blobs with several stubby tentacles, a pair of thin antennae, a small mouth, and a large blue eye. They are capable of mimicking humans but have numerous imperfections, such as having large lavender tentacles in place of their arms or having skull faces.

Synopsis

Skull Fairy arc

At some point, the Kawpicatts infest Elsie Crimson 's storage ship and replicated the DNA of the space pirate and her crew. Shiki Granbell and his friends encounter the Kawpicatts and take them down. Shiki later fights against a Kawpicatt impersonating Elsie Crimson and defeated it. Afterward, the Kawpicatt reveals its true form and scurries off.

Guilst arc

Later after awakening, Witch Regret uses Purge to cleanse the Edens Zero from its Kawpicatt infestation.

Powers and Abilities

Mimicry : Kawpicatt S4 are able to replicate the appearance of any human by acquiring their DNA. According to Elsie Crimson, the forms they acquire are known to be weaker compared to their original counterpart. In their human forms, they are capable of of changing their arms into tentacles for combat.
