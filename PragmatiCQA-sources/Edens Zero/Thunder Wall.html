Thunder Wall ( サンダーウォール , Sandā Wōru ) is an Alchemist technique used by Drakken Joe .

Contents

  • 1 Description
  • 2 Usage
  • 4 Site Navigation

Description

The user creates marks on the walls and turns the wall into electricity, that zaps anyone who makes contact with it.

Usage

Drakken first used this technique to zap Shiki , making him fall down from the ceiling.
