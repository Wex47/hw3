Eye of Venus ( ヴィーナス・アイ , Vīnasu Ai , lit. Venus Eye) is an Ether Gear that enhances the user's observation.

Description

Eye of Venus allows the user to observe people's locations, similar to the Eye of God . In battle, the user is able to predict their enemy's movements.

However, the user's advantage with this Ether Gear will be rendered useless if their enemy moves too fast for them to properly react to.
