Edens Glitter ( エデンズグリッター , Edenzu Gurittā ) is an Element Ether technique.

Description

The user channels Ether into their open hand and conjures a pillar of light on her opponent, causing great damage.

Usage

Witch first used this technique as finishing move to incapacitate and defeat Laguna in their fight .

Trivia

  • The attack's name is a reference to Fairy Glitter , a powerful magic spell from Hiro Mashima's previous work, Fairy Tail .
