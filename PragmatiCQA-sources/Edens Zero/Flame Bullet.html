Flame Bullet (炎の弾丸 Honō no Dangan ) is a Flame Ammo technique used by Fie .

Contents

  • 1 Description
  • 2 Usage
  • 4 Navigation

Description

The user imbues a bullet with fire to increase its damage and make it explode from within their target.

Usage

Fie first used this technique to try and snipe Hermit but she evaded his attack with her Aerial Move .
