Star Drain: Foresta ( スタードレイン フォレスタ Sutā Dorein: Foresuta , Star Drain: Foresta ) is a form of battle gear created through Star Drain .

Description

Through the use of the Star Drain Ether Gear , this battle gear is equipped to the user after absorbing the surrounding Ether from the Verdant Planet Foresta . For Elsie Crimson , it takes the form of a revealing garb with a collar around the neck area, floral-patterned streaks going from the back to the front, and the edge of the dress. It has a pair of armbands with floral adornment, forearm-length gloves, and thigh-high stock, both of which have a curly patter by the borders. Additionally, a pair of wings are adorned on the side of the head, four long strips of dark cloth with light-colored borders flow from the back, and includes no footwear.
