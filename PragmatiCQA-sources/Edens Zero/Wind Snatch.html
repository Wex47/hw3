Description

Wind Snatch is a form of Ether Gear that allows the user to manipulate the wind to steal anything from people to Ether swords in their vicinity. Wind Flow also enables the user to fire stolen weapons right back at their owner. Users is also capable of changing the wind's color and raising its quality by at least one, preventing it from being cleaved. However, this wind can be blocked and/or blown by an equal amount of wind.
