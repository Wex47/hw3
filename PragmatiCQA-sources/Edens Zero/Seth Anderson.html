Seth Anderson ( セス・アンダーソン , Sesu Andāson ) is a sentient computer program housed within a robotic body and was a top member of Drakken Joe 's Organization .

Appearance

Seth is a tall and slim android whose eyes have pupils shaped like sniper lenses and have a cigarette in its mouth. His physical appearance greatly resembles that of a metallic skeleton. His attire consists of a cowboy hat and light-colored leather jacket over a dark-colored, v-neck collared undershirt, sports a pair of long, multi-colored striped trousers held by a belt that holds his pistol holster on his right side, and boots. Despite being a robot, he has a smoking cigar in his mouth.

Personality

Synopsis

Digitalis arc

Seth and Diego accompanied their boss Drakken Joe over a "meeting" with Murray Morrison over his huge debt to the Dark Alchemist. Seth shrugs nonchalantly when Drakken asked him about Spider's claim that they are brothers after he fixed the loan shark's computer in the past.

Seth witnesses Murray Morrison's torture when Drakken turned the wine bottle's glass properties into Dramour Ore with his Ether Gear , Alchemist , and corrects Spider that they are debtors, not borrowers. After the meeting, Seth reports to the Dark Alchemist that the loss they have suffered from Chronophage devouring Guilst of its 1,200 years is too much to calculate as all of their customers have been erased from existence.

When Drakken Joe ordered Spider 's execution immediately on spot, Seth shot Spider in the head faster than the latter realized he was already dead. Diego and Maria complimented their colleague's marksmanship skills.

Sun Jewel arc

After taking control of Sattelite Blaze, he listens as Drakken is not going to use it to find the Edens Zero, as by coincidence the ship is nearby. He is shown the ship, which he points out to Diego that the ship is small compared to the Belial Gore. Drakken tells them that they are going to get it by hand-to-hand combat and that they are going in now.

Belial Gore arc

World No. 29

Seth, Diego, and Maria break into the Edens Zero after changing the Ether shields into gas and the ship's metal hull into paper. They exit their ship and he asks what they should do to the crew, to which Maria answers that they kill them.

Seth and his companions confronted Witch Regret and were defeated by her. Seth and Diego apparently blew themselves up to avoid interrogation, leaving Maria Slime to be captured in their place.

Later on, it's revealed Seth blew himself up on purpose so he, as a sentient computer program, infiltrated the ship's systems and effectively took over Edens Zero while disabling the Four Shining Stars and the other bots at the same time in the process.

World No. 30

After infiltrating the Edens Zero, Seth and his companions were once again defeated by Witch Regret. But this time, Rebecca Bluegarden warned her fellow crewmates about their attempts to fool them with their defeat and take over the ship from within like what they did in World No. 29 and thwarted them, much to their shock.

Powers and Abilities

Expert Marksmanship : Seth is shown to be an excellent marksman, having shown the ability to draw his pistol at Spider without him noticing that he was already shot in the head.

Self-Destruction : Seth is capable of self-destructing on command, completely obliterating his physical body while his mind remains intact.

Equipment

Pistol : Seth's main weapon is a pistol which he keeps in a holster by his right leg. He is extremely skilled at utilizing it.

Former Equipment

Edens Zero : In World No. 29 , Seth uploaded himself into the computer of the Edens Zero ship, gaining full control over it as a result.
