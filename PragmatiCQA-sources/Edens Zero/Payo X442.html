Payo (パヨ Payo ) is the pet bird of Sibir the leader of his own gang of criminals who operated on Planet Norma in the year X442.

Appearance

Payo is a small pink bird with purple splotches across its chest, a pair of yellow eyes, a black beak, and matching feet. He has a pair of triangular feathers on top, resembling horns.

Personality

Payo often talks to his owner, who can understand him, and normally nags Sibir over his usage of swears.

Powers and Abilities

Flight : Being a bird, Payo is able to fly with the use of his wings and can even lightly hover in a spot.

Equipment

Knight Gear (ナイトギア Naito Gia ): After his owner is defeated, Payo uses the giant battle mech to grab Shiki and activates the self-destruct function. However, this foiled when Pino activates her Electromagnetic Pulse and deactivates the Knight Gear.
