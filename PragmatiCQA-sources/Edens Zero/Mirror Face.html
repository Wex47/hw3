Mirror Face (ミラーフェイス Mirā Feisu ) is an ability that copies a person's appearance, personality and memory.

Description

Mirror Face allows the user to change their features and body mass to be identical to a target of their choosing so long as they have gotten close to the target.

The user cannot copy the abilities of their target, but they do gain the target's memories, personality, thoughts, and voice making it an ideal ability for infiltration and information gathering.
