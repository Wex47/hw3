Shiki Granbell vs. Orc is a fight fought between Shiki Granbell , the Demon King of the Edens Zero , and Orc of Poseidon Nero's Commando Team, Beast Squad 6 .

Prologue

Battle

Orc grabs the fallen drone he was sitting on with his mechanical arm, transforming it, and throws it at Shiki who dodges it. He then charges at Shiki with his mechanical arm and clashed fists with his opponent who pushed him back. Orc comments on the gravity-enhanced punch being heavy but retaliate with his mechanical arm transforming into a Gatling gun to shoot at Shiki point-blank, stating that it has Ether bullets. Orc noted that Shiki is fast after the latter dodges his barrage of Ether bullets with ease, and deduces that Shiki is "falling" in the direction he wants to go with Satan Gravity . Orc got dropkicked in the face by the young man which sent him flying but is able to regain his balance and footing with his mechanical arm as he asks Shiki where did he get that power. Orc learns that Shiki was taught by Ziggy , his adoptive grandpa, and is confused why the young man is crying his heart out all of a sudden although he internally noted that it is Ziggy's power that Shiki used.

Shiki charges at Orc as he tells him that there has to be a reason behind Ziggy going crazy. Blocking Shiki's punch with his mechanical arm, the Beast Squad 6 member retorts that whatever the reason he reminds the young man of what his adoptive grandfather did to Foresta by taking over the machines, killing the humans and burning up the forest, and states that Ziggy's evil is truly worthy of the name "Demon King." Striking the young man with his left arm, Orc transforms his right mechanical arm into an Ether blaster cannon and declares that they are going to kill every bot on the Verdant Planet to protect the humans. The Beast Squad 6 member blasted Shiki into the ground with repeated barrages and said that they are going to show Ziggy what happens if he tries to start a rebellion by destroying all of the robots. After Shiki is down and heavily battered on the ground, Orc proclaims that it is the fate of the Aoi Cosmos decreed by his emperor, Poseidon Nero .

Orc notes that Shiki does not know about Poseidon Nero, which he forgot to mention. He reveals to the young man that they are his commando team, Beast and they are on a mission to purge this planet. Orc stands with his arms folded over Shiki on the ground and when the young man gets up and declares that he will stop his adoptive grandpa, Orc responds that his emperor, Poseidon Nero will stop Ziggy. After Shiki retorts that what he and his teammates are doing is at least as evil as Ziggy, Orc interjects that they are protecting the humans. Suddenly, Orc is grabbed in the face by Shiki, who transforms, before he could use his mechanical arm against him and wonders what happened to the young man's gravity, noting that it's different than before. As he is pushed back by Shiki further, Orc realized in shock that his opponent's Ether went critical point and can use Overdrive . Orc has been struck directly by Shiki's Magimech Attack: Meteor Breaker and sent flying in defeat.

Aftermath

After his defeat, Orc falls on the ground heavily injured and comments in shock and fear that he can not believe there is another one like Shura . The Beast Squad 6 member then smiles and tells Shiki that he will never beat the great Shura. Orc explains to Shiki that Shura is a gravity user more powerful than him, and maybe even more powerful than Ziggy as well. He warns Shiki that gravity attracts gravity and that he, Ziggy, and Shura are destined to find each other.
