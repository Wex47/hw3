Stones ( 鉱石生命体 ( ストーンズ ) Sutōnzu , lit. "Mineral Lifeforms") are creatures that inhabit Sun Jewel .

Description

Stones are subterranean lifeforms that possess a degree of metal ore on their bodies. These creatures vary widely in appearance, ranging from insects to giant beasts. They are the source of all metal on Sun Jewel , where criminals are forced to hunt Stones for a quota of metal as a form of punishment.

Species

  • Bug Stones : The weakest type of Stone, each yielding one metal. They resemble a variety of insects, most commonly large ants, while others include centipedes, beetles, and wasps. Although they appear in large swarms, they are considered by hunters to be an unreliable metal source due to their low value.
  • Black Rock : A powerful Stone that is regarded as the lord of Sun Jewel's mines. It is a giant quadruped with a vaguely feline body and a funnel-shaped "mouth". Most of its body is covered in nearly indestructible Black Crystals , while its underbelly is smooth and brightly colored. It is worth at least 200,000 metals.
