Appearance

Nikora are stout, white creatures with a golden horn for a nose with five lines. They often wear little uniforms of the occupation they handle, such as waiters and chefs.

Synopsis

Intro arc

Diner Fairy's is a restaurant run by Nikora, where one serves Shiki , Rebecca and Happy their meals while several more serve the other customers. Another one is seen in a video from Labilia 's B-Cube channel that's being watched by another customer.

Trivia

  • A special restriction made by Hiro Mashima stated that the number of lines on a Nikora's nose must be 5, no more, no less.
