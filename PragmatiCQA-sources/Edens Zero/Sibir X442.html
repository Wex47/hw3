Appearance

Sibir appears to be a tall muscular man, with a tattoo spelling his name on his head, which he has no hair. He also has some sort of piercings on his chin with dark orb-like earrings. He also appears to have a robotic arm on his right side (which later reveals a skinny arm underneath), with a type of shirt that only covers the sleeve on the left, exposing his abdomen, he also appears to be wearing some sort of futuristic baggy pants.

Personality

Sibir appears to be a ruthless leader, eager to get back what was stolen from him, he doesn't seem to care who he has to hurt or cause massive damage to do it. He also appears to know how to speak bird as he is shown talking to Payo . He is also extremely cruel, as he mocks, tortures both physically and mentally against those weaker than himself, and gets angry when he doesn't get what he wants.

Powers and Abilities

Hand-to-Hand Combatant : Sibir is skilled in basic hand-to-hand combat especially when it's enhanced by the Augmenter Arm.

Piloting Skills : Sibir is a skilled pilot, shown to be capable of flying the Knight Gear he used in combat with ease.

Equipment

Knight Gear (ナイトギア Naito Gia ): Sibir uses a giant battle mech in battle that he can directly control from within the cockpit.

Augmenter Arm (強化アーム Kyōka Āmu ): Created by Weisz , Sibir wields this type of weapon that allows him to assert his dominance over his gang members and even briefly do battle with Shiki Granbell . Although, it was eventually destroyed by Weisz, who regretted giving it to Sibir.
