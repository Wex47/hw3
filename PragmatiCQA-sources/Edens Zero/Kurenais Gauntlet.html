Kurenai's Gauntlet (紅の籠手 Kurenai no Kote ) is a hand brace used and owned by Kurenai Kōgetsu .

Desription

Kurenai's Gauntlet is a mechanical hand brace that generates searing heat, severely burning any surface the wearer's hand touches. The gauntlet also reinforces the wearer's strength, allowing them to withstand special abilities such as Ether Gear .

Usage

When Kurenai disapproves of Cedric for touching her face, she used the gauntlet to grab his face and horrifically burned it to deep red despite the model's pleas of mercy.

After Shiki barged in the Gold Palace and challenged Kurenai, she brandished her gauntlets for battle. Kurenai later used her gauntlet to block Shiki's Satan Gravity -enhanced punch directly, standing her ground against him.
