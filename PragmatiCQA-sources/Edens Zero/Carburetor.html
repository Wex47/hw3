Carburetor ( キャブレター , Kyaburetā ) is a type of Ether Gear that utilizes mist.

Description

Carburetor allows the user to produce and manipulate mist at will, allowing them to perform various feats such as turning themselves into mist for dodging physical attacks , shape it into a rain of projectiles powerful enough to strike an opponent , and capable of turning the target into mist.
