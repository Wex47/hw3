Magimech Attack: Gravity Center ( 魔械流 重心 , Makai-ryū: Jūshin , lit. "Magic Machine Style: Center of Gravity") is a Satan Gravity technique.

Contents

  • 1 Description
  • 2 Usage
  • 4 Site Navigation

Description

The user exerts an immense amount of force on an airborne target by boosting their gravity and pulling the target to the ground.

Usage

Shiki first uses this technique to bring down the flying motorcycle of Happy 's kidnapper from Bro 's gang after catching up to him with ease in the middle of a city on Blue Garden .

Xenolith uses this technique on a massive scale to stop everyone in Foresta from moving for a while before revealing to an astonished Shiki that he is the founder of the Magimech Arts and was Ziggy 's mentor.
