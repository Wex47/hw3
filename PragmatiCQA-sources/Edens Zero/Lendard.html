Lendard ( レンダード , Rendādo ) is a planet located in the Kaede Cosmos . It is known as the Perpetual Motion Machine ( 永久機関惑 , Eikyu Kikan ) .

Description

The planet had at least two kingdoms, belonging to Elsie 's and Justice 's families, with cliffs pointing upward, large castles, and at least two moons. However, at an unknown point, the planet's surface became covered with facilities of varying sizes, some having smokestacks that releases clouds of smoke from the production of robots . There are also several floating structures of different shapes.

History

Lendard has always been an industrial planet, but it stopped operations several years ago. It was once the most prosperous planet in the Kaede Cosmos, but a struggle for supremacy was happening behind this with an obsession for arms development. The weaponeering eventually became a threat to the rest of the cosmos, which made Elsie gave her kingdom's secrets to the others in an attempt to stop her parent's brutality. However, war broke out and the planet was destroyed. Recently, Ziggy has taken over the planet and turned it into his Skeleton Army factory. According to Holy , Deadend Crow of the Oración Seis Galáctica has formed an alliance with Ziggy, using Lendard as their base.

Location

The planet is located in an unspecified part of the Kaede Cosmos.
