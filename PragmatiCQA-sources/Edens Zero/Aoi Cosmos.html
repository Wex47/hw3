The Aoi Cosmos ( 葵 ( アオイ ) 宇宙 ( コスモス ) , Aoi Kosumosu lit. "Hollyhock Cosmos") is a region of space where many planets reside. It is one of the four cosmoses that comprises the Grand Shiki Cosmos . It was ruled by Poseidon Nero of the Oración Seis Galáctica , the supreme leader of the Nero Empire , prior to his death.

Description

The Aoi Cosmos is home to a variety of unique and beautiful planets, as described by Connor . Examples of the unique planets that be can found here include a world that is completely covered in foliage. The Aoi Cosmos' most distinct feature is the various streams of water flowing in its space, and dwelling within these waters are a large variety of spacefaring fish, that come in many sizes.

The Aoi Cosmos is also home to quite a large number of aquatic life, aside from the aforementioned space fish that can also be found on some planets, crustaceans like the Aoi Lobster and the giant octopus guardian on Red Cave .

Once every few years, the ocean that is spread out across the Aoi Cosmos hits high tide, called the Aoi High Tide , which it will swallow up every planet. The planets are protected with their Ether-filled atmosphere, preventing the inhabitants from being submerged in the space water. Inside the great sea, Ether and electricity combine, and all networks become connected through the All-Link System , one of the Nero Empire's weapons.

Sectors

  • Coral Sector : This sector is home to a variety of coral and like most of the Aoi Cosmos has a large body of space water floating in it.
  • Poseidon Nero Sector : This sector has a large volume of water floating in space, where a variety of space fish can be found there.

Trivia

  • "Aoi" (葵) means "hollyhock", a plant associated with abundance and great fortune. "Aoi" may also be derived from (青い), a Japanese word for blue.
