Star Drain ( スタードレイン , Sutā Dorein ) is an Ether Gear that allows its user to absorb and manipulate a planet's Ether , either to forge it into battle gear or perform energy attacks.

Description

Star Drain allows the user to absorb the Ether from the surrounding area of the planet they stand on and forge it into battle gear to use in combat, and make it their own, as well as control and manipulate the Ether directly.

This Ether Gear can be utilized in space where the user can use it in conjunction with Ether Link and increase the speed of their ship to the point that it exceeds speed readings.

History

According to Justice , Star Drain's power is equivalent to "Heavenly Body Magic" which was feared in the Dark Ages, a force that could control the stars.
