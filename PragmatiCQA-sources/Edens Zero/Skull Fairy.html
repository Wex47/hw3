Skull Fairy (スカルフェアリー Sukaru Fearī ) is the flagship of the space pirate Elsie Crimson of the Oración Seis Galáctica .

As part of a ruse, Elsie Crimson tricked the Crew of Edens into believing that her storage ship was the Skull Fairy.

Description

The Skull Fairy is a large pirate-themed ship spacecraft with white wind sails mounted on two masts.

Trivia

  • Skull Fairy’s crest bears a resemblance to Fairy Tail’s guild symbol from Mashima’s previous work of the same name .
