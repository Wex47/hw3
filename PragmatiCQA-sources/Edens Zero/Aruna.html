Appearance

Aruna is a young girl with shoulder-length dark hair. She wears a small dress with a light-colored trim, collar, and three buttons, and a pair of dark shoes. Additionally, she has a pair of hair bun with spirals on them.

Synopsis

Foresta arc

When the robots start eliminating humans due Muller's command via Enormous Satellite Server , Aruna cries for help as she runs away from a robot who targets her. She is saved by Rebecca , Happy , and Pino , who take her with them. Later she falls to ground being affected by the strong gravity caused by Xenolith . On the Edens Zero in the Edens Kitchen, Aruna sits at a table where Witch notices that she has not eaten her food. The little girl tells the Shield of Edens that she asked Rebecca and her friends to show her where Miimi died. Holding up her phone to see the photo again, Aruna grieves for Miimi. She answers Witch's question that Miimi was her friend and explains that she does not have any parents and that Miimi was her only family. She listens to Witch, who tells her that the Crew of Edens recently lost a friend too and that their friend would be sad if they never stop crying. This makes Aruna ask if robots can go to heaven, to which the android affirms as their friend was a robot. Witch tells her to cry out all her tears so that her friend can see her smiles as much as she can. Aruna starts crying and is comforted by Witch who hugs her.
