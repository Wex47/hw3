Synopsis

Guilst arc

Nacchan is one of several B-Cubers who has been kidnapped by Rogue Out and transported on a spaceship heading towards the planet Guilst .

Equipment

B-Cube (Ｂ・キューブ B Kyūbu ): As a B-Cuber, she uses a B-Cube terminal to record, upload, and share videos on her channel.

Trivia

  • Nacchan, along with Ricchan , Icchy , Naru , and Flanker Yocchi , is based on an online personality of the same name from the real-life Japanese YouTube channel "Bom Bom TV".
  • Along with Ricchan, Nacchan does not appear in the anime and their roles are being filled by Icchy and Naru instead.
