The Fairy Drone (妖精型ドローン Yōsei-gata Dorōn ) is a mechanical spying device used by Elsie Crimson .

Description

The Fairy Drone is a small white bot with a round head, red antenna, plain dots for eyes, flat arms and legs. It has a constant smile and is always waving its right arm. The drone can send and record whatever it monitors, as well as being able to replay whatever it had recorded.

History

After sensing traces of dark evil Ether emanating within Ziggy 's remains, Elsie Crimson deployed the Fairy Drone to monitor the deceased Demon King and recorded his ominous words upon his awakening, "I will... go see Mother ."

Trivia

  • The Fairy Drone makes brief appearances in the background of chapters 101 and 102 before its reveal in Chapter 104 .
