The Shinobi Troll ( シノビトロール , Shinobi Torōru ) , also known as Shino ( シノちゃん , Shino-chan ) , is a monster who serves as an event boss near the town of Crysta .

Appearance

The Shinobi Troll is a large troll with a patch of dark skin around his eyes, reminiscent of a mask, a small ponytail, and a pair of small horns on top of his head, pointy ears, and scales covering his body. He has a mouth filled with sharp teeth and has a large pair of fangs protruding from his lower jaw. His arms are large and muscular, ending with a pair of clawed hands, in contrast, his legs are short and thin and ending with small clawed feet.

He wears an outfit that consists of a dark-colored, tattered sleeveless shirt with a large spiral on the back, light-colored pants with a dark diamond pattern by the ankles where it is torn, and a spiked collar around his neck. He also has a pair of dark-colored wristbands and a long cloth tied around his waist.

Personality

Despite his large and intimidating size, the Shinobi Troll is somewhat afraid, especially towards subjects like Jamilov . He tends to get depressed very easily, such as when he mistakenly gave out spoilers to the Crew of Edens , only to hear from Weisz bluntly telling him that they were not interested. He also tends to end his verbs with "-eth" ( de gozaru ).

Despite being afraid, the Shinobi Troll can garner enough courage to protect Crysta from Guiron and the Magical Beast Army . He is devoted to protecting them, to the point of using his body to shield the townspeople from their attacks.

He also has pride as a boss, not allowing Guiron to insult him, where he returns the insult and humiliates him instead, calling him a sub-boss.

Synopsis

Digitalis arc

The Shinobi Troll appears in front of Shiki and his friends as they search for Hermit . Not wanting to fight he tells them that he is not an evil monster, but remembers that he is an event boss, technically making him an evil monster. He explains his quest to them where adventures find his cave where the Sacred Treasure is, but in reality, it is a touching story where he uses the treasure to protect the townspeople. He panics that he is spoiling it to them, but Weisz tells him that they do not care, hearing this he sulks to himself. He snaps out of it after Pino asks what he is doing here, where he warns them that the mass murder Jamilov has appeared in Crysta, killing players and NPCs alike, and has not been banned despite being a player. Now Crysta has become a disaster area and the event flags are a mess, forcing him to run away as fast as he could. They then ask if he knows of Hermit, which he does, and tells them that he has seen her on the hills near Crysta. He warns them again not to go, but Shiki replies that it does not matter what kind of place their soon to be crew member is in, leaving him perplexed.

During the Magical Beast Army's invasion of Crysta, the Shinobi Troll arrives and saves a young girl NPC from several imps. He states his character description of being the city's guardian spirit and declares that he will protect his town, even if he is scared. He insults Guiron, that not only is he a sub-boss as well, but that he is just a side character that does not have a backstory. He tells the young girl to gather the townspeople and escape as Shiki fights the Magical Beast Army. The young girl thanks him and calls him "Burgle", which he blushes upon hearing. However, Jamilov stops them, with the battalion of imps. They fire their crossbows at the townspeople, but he shields them with his body. He takes the barrage of arrows, proclaiming that he is the guardian spirit, before falling on his back, where he lies there. His body along with the town and its people, are slowly erased by Jamilov executing the final code. However this is nullified by Hermit normalizing the code with Master Code , when Jamilov logged out after Shiki defeats him. The Shinobi wakes soon after, complimenting the kimono Homura 's wearing.

Powers and Abilities

Enhanced Strength : As a Digitalis monster and boss of the Crysta Event, he is rather strong, being able to knock down Guiron's battalion with great ease.

Enhanced Durability : He is able to withstand a barrage of arrows from the Magical Beast Army , shortly before fainting.

Equipment

Crysta Sacred Treasure : As part of the quest called "Retrieve the city's Sacred Treasure", he uses the sacred treasure of Crysta to protect the townspeople.
