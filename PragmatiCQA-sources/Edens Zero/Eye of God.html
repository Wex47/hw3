Eye of God ( ゴッドアイ , Goddo Ai , lit. "God Eye") is an Ether Gear that enhances the user's observation.

Description

Eye of God is a type of Ether Gear that allows the user to observe people's locations, but unlike a GPS or nanotechnology, the user's powers of observation extend outside of time itself. However, it cannot find mechanical lifeforms .

According to Noah , Eye of God can be used to observe people's locations in time as well as their movements across it, which is how he knows about Rebecca 's Cat Leaper ability in the first place.
