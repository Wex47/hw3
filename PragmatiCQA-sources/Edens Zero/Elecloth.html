Description

The exterior consists of a large building with a somewhat carved appearance and there are several small square and rectangular windows. A tall sign, displaying the store's name and various products, held with poles stand outside of the store. At the front entrance are a welcome mat and a sign of the store above. The interior consists of a floor with a four-square pattern that is separated with a thick bar between each set, and numerous stands for their products, with two distinct sections; one for electronics and another for clothes.

Location

Elecloth is located in a town somewhere on Foresta.

History

Foresta arc

Rebecca sees the home electronics shop as she is being chased by Britney and heads inside it. She grabs one of the vacuum cleaners to trap the Beast Squad 6 member, but she fails to activate it because it has a user lock. Pino wakes up and analyzes the password, but Britney spreads her Poison Mist , rotting Rebecca's skin away until she unconsciously activates her Ether Gear to rewind time to before she entered the store. Now knowing the password, Rebecca gets activates the vacuum cleaner, sucking the Beast soldier inside it and kicking her away, destroying the store's rooftop. Afterward, Rebecca gets some clothes and leaves money for these and the vacuum cleaner. She then has Pino help her test her powers, rewinding time twice before she falls to her knees in pain. Noting what she learned, she calls this technique Reverse .
