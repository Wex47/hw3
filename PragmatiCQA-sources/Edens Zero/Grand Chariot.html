Grand Chariot ( 七星剣 ( グランシャリオ ) Guran Shario ) is a Star Drain technique.

Description

The user places both hands on top of each other, with the top hand having only the index and middle fingers spread out. Whereupon seven light beams erupt from the ground around the user, attacking their opponent. When used in space, the beams are omnidirectional lasers and have incredible penetrating powers.

Usage

Elsie in her Star Drain: Foresta uses this technique to take down a mob of hostile bots of Foresta in defense of her space pirate crew which got the attention of Justice 's group .

Justice in his high collared battle gear uses this technique in an effort to finish off Shiki during their fight .

Elsie and Justice in mid-air simultaneously unleash this technique against each other, causing massive destruction during their battle .

Justice uses this technique via the Ether Link of his starfighter to wipe out multiple Nero Empire ships in space nearby Nero 66 .

Trivia

  • This technique shares its name with the Celestial Magic and Heavenly Body Magic spell of the same name from Hiro Mashima 's previous works Rave Master and Fairy Tail respectively.
  • The array for this technique resembles the asterism of the Big Dipper which also consists of seven stars. "Grand Chariot" is also the French name for that particular asterism.
