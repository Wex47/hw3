Prologue

Shortly after arriving on Blue Garden , Happy is kidnapped, and Shiki begins to aggressively pursue the thief. After outmaneuvering the catnapper and destroying his hoverbike, the criminal is forced to use a handgun to part the crowd and draw in the rest of the gang. When the gang refuses to return Happy, Shiki activates his ether gear and charges them.
