Miyako ( ミヤコ , Miyako ) is a B-Cuber who manages the STEM Girls Channel, which produces STEM-related content. She was one of the B-Cubers who was abducted together with Rebecca Bluegarden .

Foresta arc

Miyako explains in her STEM Girls Channel video that the air heats up, it gets lighter and creates an upward current which carries smoke upward with it. Rebecca uses this knowledge from Miyako to escape from Britney in her Smoke Bar form at the burning forest in Foresta . Britney is surprised that her opponent is subscribed to Miyako's channel.

Equipment

B-Cube (Ｂ・キューブ B Kyūbu ): As a B-Cuber, Miyako uses a B-Cube terminal to record, upload, and share videos on her channel, Stem Girls Channel that has 610,000 subscribers.
