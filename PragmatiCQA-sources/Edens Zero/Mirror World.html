Description

The Mirror World is in an alternate dimension, where the surroundings are identical to the real world but are mirrored. The world is accessible to users of the Ether Gear Mirror Trick , and seems that there are other worlds in the mirrors within this world.

Location

The Mirror World is located in an alternate dimension and can be accessed by users of Mirror Trick or their targets.

History

Aoi War arc

Milani pulls Homura into this world, replacing her with her reflection to attack her friends. Within this dimension, Milani casually greets Homura and calls the dimension her world, and prepares to battle each other. They face off against one another in a fight, where Milani jumps into the mirrors within this world and sends mirror shards to attack her. However, Homura achieves her Overdrive and destroys the mirrors in this world with Odin Strike .
