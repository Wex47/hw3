Description

Aoi Lobsters are crustaceans native to Red Cave, they resemble normal shrimps, having a carapace that covers their body, four tail fins, ten segmented legs, with the front most pair having thin claws and an abdomen with ten short swimming legs. Unlike normal shrimps they have two pairs of antennae and are very large.

Synopsis

Red Cave arc

Weisz finds one while they search for the ship that crashed into the ocean, using it to scare Shiki repeatedly for his fear of bugs.
