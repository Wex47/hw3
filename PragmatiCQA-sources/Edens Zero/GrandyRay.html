Grandy-Ray is a Machina Maker technique used in combination with the Arsenal Suit .

Description

The user reconfigures the forearm piece of his Arsenal Suit , creating a ray beam that can fire in straight lines and cause explosions to what the beam touches.

Usage

Weisz first used this technique in his battle against Drakken Joe in his Overdrive state, forcing his opponent to back off as he wildly destroyed the floor and walls around them.

Trivia

  • The attack's name is a reference to the Sky Dragon Grandeeney from Hiro Mashima 's previous work, Fairy Tail .
  • When Weisz first used the technique, the display screen on his armor prefixed it as "WEPON" instead of "WEAPON".
