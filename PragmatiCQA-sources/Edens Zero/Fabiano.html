Fabiano ( ファビアーノ , Fabiāno ) was one of Poseidon Nero 's top officials and a member of the Oceans 6 . He was one of the old generation of Oceans.

Appearance

Fabiano is a middle-aged man with light-colored hair, small eyes, and a clean, medium-length mustache. He wears a nobleman's military uniform with a cravat.

Personality

Fabiano is a stern and serious man, believing in protecting the people of the Aoi Cosmos from threats as he suggests to Nero that they should wage war against Ziggy in retaliation for his actions against Foresta . He cares for his comrades, appalled from watching Shura murder Cyca, becoming openly outraged at the prince for murdering his fellow Oceans. He is livid upon seeing the deaths of his men and attempts to avenge them. Fabiano is concerned about the state of the Nero Empire as he tries to appeal with Nero to stop the prince's reckless actions but is disturbed when he learns from the Emperor that he does not care if the empire falls or if billions die so long he remains victorious in the end.

Synopsis

Foresta arc

Attending the meeting with Poseidon Nero on Nero 1 , Fabiano learns from the Emperor that Foresta has been violated by Ziggy. He advises Nero that they should wage war on Ziggy and strike him down, arguing that it is for the people of the Aoi Cosmos . Fabiano asks the Emperor what do the eyes of the guiding dice tell him after seeing the results. When Nero leaves the meeting and the command of Oceans 6 has been officially transferred to Poseidon Shura , Fabiano remains silent.

As Shura levitates several of the objects around him upside down in a room, Fabiano is quiet as Ijuna informs the prince that everything is ready for their departure. He is shocked when Shura callously kills Cyca in front of him and his fellow Oceans. Openly outraged, Fabiano asks the prince if he lost his mind and states that Cyca is one of their own before Shura retorts that they are going to fight the King of Robots and he doesn't need robots on his side.

Sandra arc

Fabiano chooses to not go with Shura and the other Oceans to Nero 66 where he makes an audience with Nero at the throne room and kneels before him. He asks the Emperor if he still refuses to step in after seeing what is happening recently. Fabiano explains that Shura has killed Reverend Cyca and claims he will destroy all robots in the Aoi Cosmos. Fabiano states that the prince has strayed off course, resorting to foolish tactics that could not possibly have come from a rational mind and if this continues, the Nero Empire will fall. He implores Nero to take command and stop Shura. Fabiano is horrified when the Emperor explains to him that the dice will never betray him, and such actions from Shura do not matter for the ultimate result of his victory will remain the same.

Aoi War arc

Fabiano is alarmed when his subordinates informed him that the Imperial defensive zone of Nero 1 has been breached by the Interstellar Union Army after Shura deployed most of the Imperial Army forces to Nero 66. The Oceans old generation member states that a single division of the Interstellar Union Army shouldn't be enough to break through their defenses even after the reduction of the Imperial Army's presence in The Temple. He learns from his subordinates that Dead Cam CEO Rickard and others have joined forces with the Interstellar Union Army and mustered their private armies against the Nero Empire. Fabiano internally remarks that Shura's foolish plan caused this to happen and asks the Emperor for guidance. He glares at Jaguar of the Oración Seis Interstellar who barged inside the Deep Snow Imperial palace and defeated hundreds of Nero Empire soldiers along the way by himself. Livid at the deaths of his men by Jaguar's claws, Fabiano activates his Ether Gear to transform his arms into crystal and punches the captain in the face, briefly pushing him back. However, he is then punched in the stomach by the Interstellar member in retaliation before being thrown into the ground. Fabiano is shocked, stating that his Ether Gear makes his body as hard as Aoi Crystal and tries to free himself from Jaguar's grip to no avail. He is then dragged across the floor, crying out in pain before Jaguar throws him to the wall crashing into a crater, killing him.

Powers and Abilities

Fabiano was one of the Oceans 6 who acted as a top official of the Nero Empire, and was chosen by the Emperor himself as one of the most powerful soldiers in the Aoi Cosmos. He was from the old generation of Oceans. Nero himself states that Fabiano is the strongest soldier in the Nero Empire.

Crystal Ether Gear : Fabiano is shown to use an unnamed Ether Gear that crystallizes his arms for battle, stating that they are as hard as Aoi Crystal . With these arms, he is able to briefly push back Jaguar in his transformed state with a heavy punch to the face. When activated, his Ether marks appear around his left eye.

Enhanced Speed : He has shown to be rather swift, able to briefly catch Jaguar off guard and punch him in the face.

Hand-to-Hand Combat : Fabiano is capable of hand-to-hand combat, using the crystalized arms of his Ether Gear in conjunction.
