Universe 1 was the first universe.

World No. 29 was the previous world where Rebecca finally discovered her Cat Leaper's true abilities. Upon discovering her awakened Ether Gear , Rebecca used Cat Leaper to transport herself into Universe 2 and thus was able to save that universe's crew of Edens Zero from the horrible fates Drakken Joe had bestowed upon their Universe 1 counterparts.

This universe is featured from Chapter 1 up until Chapter 84 , with World No. 29 being featured in the Belial Gore arc through at least Chapter 69 to Chapter 84 .

In World No. 30, due to the power of Shiki's true Overdrive making him like a black hole, his Satan Gravity began to draw in memories from World No. 29.
