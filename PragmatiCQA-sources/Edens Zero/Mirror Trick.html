Description

This form of Ether Gear focuses around mirrors, allowing the user to enter the Mirror World in which they can entrap opponents inside and replace them with their reflections who they can have them attack the victim's allies. Users are able to break the mirrors in this world and send the shards charging towards their target. They are also capable of freely entering and moving within the mirrors of this world, can appear in multiple places at the same time, and can even send parts of their body through the shards, as well as form copies of themselves. However, if their exact location is found and the user is attacked, then they will be forcefully ejected from the mirror. If someone attacks a mirror with their reflection the user will receive the damage from it as though they'd been hit directly.
