Hot Eyes ( ホットアイズ , Hotto Aizu ) is the commando carrier of Eraser , and a class of ships in the Interstellar Union Army .

Description

The Hot Eyes is a large spaceship with a prominent, round head, a pair of wings below, and a pair of turrets under it. The flight deck sits at the back and has a curved roof. Above the front of the ship is a large holographic circle with a ring around it and three squares circling it, overall giving it an impression of an eye. On both sides of the Hot Eyes is the Interstellar Union Army logo.

It can fire a large energy beam from the pair of turrets in battle.

Trivia

  • The ship's name is a nod to Hoteye , a member of the Oración Seis from Fairy Tail .
