Valkyrie's Army ( ヴァルキリー一派 , Varukirī Ippa ) is an army of prisoners that inhabit the Labor District of Sun Jewel and oppose the tyrannical reign of Kurenai Kōgetsu .

Synopsis

History

After Valkyrie Yuna sacrificed herself to protect the people of the Labor District from a horde of monsters unleashed by Kurenai who took over Sun Jewel, Paul forms a resistance movement against the Crimson Lady with the deceased Sword of Edens as their symbol of hope. They even hold Valkyrie in the highest respect and prepared a rebellion against Madame Kurenai for a long time.
