Dispel Driver (ディスペルドライバー Disuperu Doraibā ) is a Heal Atomizer technique.

Contents

  • 1 Description
  • 2 Usage
  • 4 Site Navigation

Description

The user's hands glow and release a golden light capable of reaching throughout an entire skyscraper. The light fixes or resets abnormal status effects such as petrifications and bindings.

Sister Ivry can use the technique on herself or on anyone in the nearby vicinity.

Usage

Sister Ivry first used this technique to undo the petrification afflicting the many women throughout Illega Tower .

During her fight with Daichi , Sister Ivry used this technique to reset her "Bind" status effect, freeing herself from her opponent's Ether Gear Pain's Tree .
