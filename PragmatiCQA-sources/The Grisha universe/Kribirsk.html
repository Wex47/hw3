Kribirsk is a city in central Ravka that serves as one side of the crossing of the Shadow Fold . It is located on the eastern side of the Fold.

History

Shadow and Bone

Kribirsk is where Alina and Mal stay with their regiment before crossing the Fold.

King of Scars

Kribirsk's economy has suffered greatly since the city is no longer a necessary port, as the Shadow Fold is no longer dangerous to cross.
