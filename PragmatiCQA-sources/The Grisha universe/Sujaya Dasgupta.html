Sujaya Dasgupta is an actress. She portrays Zoya Nazyalensky in Netflix's fantasy television series, Shadow and Bone (2021).

Biography

She studied English at the University of Leicester, before going on to train at the Royal Central School of Speech and Drama, graduating in 2015.
