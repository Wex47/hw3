Liliyana Garin was the aunt of Zoya Nazyalensky and the sister of Sabina Garin .

Biography

When Zoya was nine years old, Sabina Garin tried to sell her off to an old man, but Liliyana arrived on the day of the wedding and tried to convince Sabina that it wouldn't be long until Zoya was old enough to become a soldier. Sabina did not relent, so Liliyana tried to convince the townspeople's of the cruelty of this, but the man who is trying to marry Zoya, Valentin Grankin, begins to hit Liliyana repeatedly with a walking stick, sparking a fury inside Zoya that leads to the revelation of Zoya's powers. Liliana is very happy about this, knowing that this means that Valentin now cannot marry her, since she has to be brought to the Little Palace to train.

Following Zoya's demonstration of her powers, Liliyana escorted Zoya to the Little Palace. They had barely any money, so they slept in ditches. When they arrive, a tester does a quick examination of Zoya's abilities before confirming she is a Grisha. They give Liliyana a coin for her troubles, and Liliyana says goodbye to Zoya. Zoya wants her to stay, but Liliyana tells her that she has to return home to tend to her chickens. The two write each other many letters over the years.

A couple years later, when Zoya completes her first travel across the fold, she immediately heads to Liliyana's house/shop, where they embrace and talk, and Liliyana introduces Zoya to Lada, who demands that Zoya help her draw a picture of the Little Palace. When Zoya leaves, she does not know this will be the last time she sees Liliyana.

When the Darkling destroyed Novokribirsk , Liliyana was with a customer, who she shoved out of the way of a volcra as a selfless last act.
