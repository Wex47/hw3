The Taban family is the lineage of the queens of Shu Han .

History

The first Taban queens came down from the highest mountains in the Sikurzoi , accompanied by their tame falcons, to establish their rule over Shu Han.

Etymology

Although the Shu usually carry the names of one or both of their parents, the royal family all take the name of the first Shu queen, the founder of the Taban dynasty. All women of the Taban family carry the name "Kir-Taban".
