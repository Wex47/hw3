Grisha steel is a special metal created by Grisha Fabrikators .

History

Grisha steel was invented by the Fabrikator Ilya Morozova .

Ravkan Corporalki and Materialki conducted experiments to fuse Grisha steel with human bone, in order to allow soldiers to withstand the attacks of nichevo’ya . These failed experiments were conducted on First Army volunteers.

Shu scientists created khergud by combining Grisha steel with ruthenium and plating human bones with the resulting alloy.
