Chernast

General Information

Location

Tsibeya , northeast Ravka

Government

Nation

Ravka

Ruler

Zoya Nazyalensky

Chernast is a settlement in northern Tsibeya , Ravka .

History

Zoya Nazyalensky killed the white tiger in the woods near Chernast.

Malyen Oretsev , Dubrov , and Mikhael were stationed at the army outpost in Chernast.

The King's soldiers tried to attack members of the Second Army , leading to massacres at Chernast and Ulensk .
