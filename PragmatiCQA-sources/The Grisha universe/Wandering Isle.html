History

Zoya Nazyalensky led a team of Grisha Examiners plus Nina Zenik to the Wandering Isle to find and recruit Grisha living in secret. Nina was captured by drüskelle .

Geography

Maroch Glen , Fenford , and Istamere are all locations in the Wandering Isle. Istamere is home to alleged fairy caves.

Settlements

  • Leflin - port settlement

Demographics

The people of the Wandering Isle are typically redheaded and pale-skinned.

Behind the scenes

  • The Wandering Isle is based on Ireland.

Trivia

  • The Kaelish people believe Grisha blood to be a cure-all, so they kill Grisha in order to drink their blood.
  • The nation is often left fairly blank on maps, with only Leflin appearing on some. However, several locations are mentioned in Six of Crows .
  • The Wandering Isle has a ruling marshal, a military chief commander, indicating that the Wandering Isle may be a stratocracy or a military junta/dictatorship.
