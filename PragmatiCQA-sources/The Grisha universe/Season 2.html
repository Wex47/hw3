Production

  • On June 7, 2021, after being watched by more than 55 million member households in its first 28 days, Shadow and Bone was renewed for an 8 episode second season.
  • On August 5, 2021 it was announced that the scripts were written and heading into production.
  • On September 2021 Leigh Bardugo announced that casting had begun for the new characters of season 2, namely Nikolai, Wylan, Tolya, and Tamar.
  • On December 7, 2021 Bola Ogun shared an image of the finished script written by Eric Heisserer on her Instagram account. She announced she would be directing this season.
  • On December 17, 2021 Leigh announced that Nikolai Lantsov and all the other major characters had been cast and filming would begin soon.
