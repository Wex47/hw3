Inferni are Grisha of the Etherealki Order. They can manipulate fire by gathering combustible particles of substances like methane or hydrogen, but they need a spark to start the fire.

In the Second Army , they wear a blue kefta with red embroidery.

  • Harshaw

  • Marie
  • Pavel

  • Zoya Nazyalensky (Post King of Scars )

Trivia

  • The word Inferni comes from the word inferno which means hell, raging fire, or sea of flames.
