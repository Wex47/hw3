Alexei was a junior cartographer's assistant in the First Army along with Alina Starkov and Eva .

Biography

Alexei was one of the most talented assistants and gave Alina one of his assignments in exchange for information about The Darkling's coach that she had seen earlier. He was killed by a Volcra when the regiment tried to cross the Shadow Fold .
