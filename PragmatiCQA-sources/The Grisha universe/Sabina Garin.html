Sabina Garin was the mother of Zoya Nazyalensky . She lived in Pachina , Ravka and had a younger sister, Liliyana , whom she rarely spoke of and gradually grew distant from.

Biography

Sabina married Suhm Nabri , a Suli man whom she loved. However, years of poverty and hardship destroyed the affection between Sabina and her husband and eroded Sabina's spirit, making her tired and distracted. When her daughter was nine years old, Sabina attempted to marry Zoya to Valentin Grankin , a wealthy old man, in order to live a better life, but the proceedings were halted when Zoya's Grisha powers were revealed.

Although it is unknown whether Sabina and her husband are alive, neither of them have maintained contact with Zoya.

Physical description

Sabina formerly possessed incredible beauty.
