The Cult of the Starless Saint are followers of the Darkling . The cult started after the Darkling is defeated on the Fold.

History

The members of the cult wish for The Darkling to be officially recognized as a saint . Yuri Vedenen , a young monk who was in the Priestguard, is seen as their leader. The followers of the cult wear black robes, representing the black kefta the Darkling wore.

Known members

  • Brother Azarov
