Dva Stolba is a valley among the Sikurzoi right along Ravka 's southern border. Due to the Border Wars , the valley or parts of it have occasionally belonged to Shu Han . It has a reputation for being war torn, but despite this, it is home to many settlements.

It is named for the ruined spires at its southern entrance. Some believe the ruins are the remnants of two mills, but Alina Starkov believed them to be an ancient arch.

Etymology

  • "Dva" means "two" in Russian. "Stolba" may be a modification of "stolpov" ("pillars"), making Dva Stolba roughly translate to "two pillars".

Trivia

  • Alina and Mal Oretsev were born near Dva Stolba.
