Tashta is a town in Ravka , located along the Vy . There is a statue of the town's founder in Tashta.

History

Alina Starkov and Nikolai Lantsov stopped in Tashta as part of their procession along the Vy. Nikolai kissed Alina in front of the gathered crowd, leading to the townspeople and pilgrims calling Alina Sol Koroleva (Sun Queen).
