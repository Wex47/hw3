Biography

Ruin and Rising

Stigg stayed at the Little Palace when the Darkling tried to overtake the King of Ravka. When Alina Starkov , with the remaining Grisha and Mal Oretsev , ventured into the tunnels, seeking the surface, Stigg and Sergei were trapped under the ground after their floor collapsed. He was nowhere to be found when Alina and her friends fled Nikolai's hideout in the mountains after Sergei sold them out to the Darkling. Tamar Kir-Bataar later confirmed Stigg had not made it out of the Spinning Wheel .

Personality and traits

He didn't want his fellow Inferni Harshaw near him because he couldn't stand his pet Oncat .

Physical description

Stigg was short and stocky with nearly white blond hair.
