Isenvee

Location Information

Type

Sea

Location

North of Fjerda

The Isenvee is the sea located north of Fjerda . The Stelge River enters the Isenvee at Elling .

History

According to Fjerdan folklore, the northern seas were historically warmer.

A water spirit was a spirit of the Isenvee before it was trapped in Velisyana .

Etymology

Isen means "ice" in Fjerdan . Vee may be inspired by the Dutch zee , meaning "sea".
