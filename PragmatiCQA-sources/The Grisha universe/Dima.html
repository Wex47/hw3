King of Scars

Dima is the point of view character in the first chapter of King of Scars . He is almost devoured by Nikolai Lantsov in his monster form.

Biography

Dima is the youngest of six boys, born considerably later than the rest of his parents' sons. The family lives on a farm ten miles outside Ivets . His older brother Pyotr bullies him for being small and afraid.

King of Scars

One stormy night while Dima is washing dishes, he hears the barn doors open. Unwilling to admit fear to Pyotr, Dima goes outside in the dark to close the barn doors and is trapped inside. Nikolai Lantsov 's monster form almost attacks Dima but is stopped by Zoya Nazyalensky , who Dima believes to be a Saint at first. Zoya gives Dima two silver coins—one for the damage to the barn, and one to keep for himself. Dima quietly follows them outside and watches them enter a coach.
