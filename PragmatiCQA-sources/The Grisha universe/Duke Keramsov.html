Biography

After returning from the war, he converted his estate in Keramzin into an orphanage and a home for the war widows. He has a city house in Os Alta that he goes to in the winter. He times his return to the orphanage to coincide with Butter Week .

Alina Starkov and Malyen Oretsev were two of the orphans on his estate, but it appears that they barely had any interaction with him.

Shadow and Bone

The Duke attended the Winter Fete , but did not recognize Alina upon meeting her.

Physical description

Alina describes him as tired-looking with a broad chest. He uses a cane.
