Balakirev is a town in Ravka , west of Os Alta . It is a popular place for Grisha to visit for festivals.

Notable locations

The main square of the town features the courthouse, the town hall, and the offices of the local governor. There is a large fountain featuring a statue of Sankt Juris defeating the dragon. Balakirev also features a church with a tall bell tower.
