Biography

During his tenure as king, Anastas built roads across Ravka. He also put almost two thousand men to death for heresy.

Trivia

  • In the Netflix show Anastas was the Lantsov king that chased the Grisha trying to control them, to the point where The Darkling created the Shadow Fold trying to stop Anastas' forces.
