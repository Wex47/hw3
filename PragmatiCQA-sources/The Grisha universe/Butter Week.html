Butter Week is a Ravkan event the week before the spring fast. Noblemen are expected to drive dom carts filled with sweets, cheeses and baked bread in a parade around the village, ending in their estate.

Shadow and Bone

Alina Starkov and Malyen Oretsev discover that the village they were passing through on their way to Tsibeya was celebrating Butter Week.
