Arkesk is a settlement in Ravka . It is located along the northern road, north of the ruins of Velisyana .

History

Sankta Anastasia cured the wasting plague in Arkesk. She died there, after using the last of her blood to heal the people.

Arkesk was one of the sites of the supposed miracles three years after the destruction of the Fold. This miracle included a "roof of myrrh."
