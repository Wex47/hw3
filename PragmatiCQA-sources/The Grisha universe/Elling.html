Elling is a port settlement on the northwest coast of Fjerda , where the Stelge river meets the Isenvee . It is one of two locations along the rocky coast that can be safely approached by large vessels from the sea; the other is Djerholm . Elling is known for smuggling and fish.

History

After traveling across Fjerda for weeks, Matthias Helvar and Nina Zenik arrived in Elling. Ravkan Grisha spies were conducting reconnaissance work in the port and recognized them; to save Matthias, Nina told a Kerch trader in the Elling harbor that Matthias was a slaver.

The ship Verstoten regularly docked and traded in Elling for almost a year. This was a cover for the Ravkans who smuggled Grisha refugees to a safehouse in Elling. Nina Zenik worked at a fish cannery in Elling while undercover.
