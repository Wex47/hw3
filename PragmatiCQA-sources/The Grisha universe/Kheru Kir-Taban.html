Kheru Kir-Taban is the younger sister of Makhi and Ehri .

Biography

Kheru was expected to marry soon, which would prevent her from being a possible heir to the Taban throne , but she delayed her wedding. She claimed it was due to difficulty choosing the right silk for her wedding gown, but Yenye claimed it was because Kheru hoped to be named Queen Makhi's heir after Akeni 's death. Kheru refuted this, saying that Makhi would not name any of her sisters as her heir.

When Ehri returned from Ravka to the Taban palace, Kheru and Yenye visited her. They brought Ehri flowers and fruits and talked with her while doing needlework.

Personality and traits

Kheru loves needlework. She is not a schemer, but the power of the Taban queen appeals to her as it does to all her sisters. According to Ehri, the Taban sisters never truly fought and were always polite with each other.

Physical description

Kheru has "coffee-colored" eyes.
