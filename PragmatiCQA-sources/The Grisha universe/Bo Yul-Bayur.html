Bo Yul-Bayur was a Shu chemist and a Fabrikator who created jurda parem .

Biography

Bo Yul-Bayur accidentally created jurda parem while trying to create a drug that would hide the Grisha powers of himself and his son Kuwei Yul-Bo . What he created instead actually enhanced Grisha power, at great cost to the Grisha. Fearing his government would use jurda parem for war and wanting to defect, Bo Yul-Bayur sent samples to Kerch to convince them of the drug's extraordinary effects and to seek asylum for himself and his son.

Kerch agreed to shelter him, but Bo Yul-Bayur was killed in the crossfire between the Fjerdans and the Kerch in Ahmrat Jen . His son Kuwei was captured and taken prisoner at the Ice Court .
