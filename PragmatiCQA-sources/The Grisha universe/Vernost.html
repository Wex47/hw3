Vernost is a small village in Ravka . It is one day's ride east of Kribirsk .

History

Alina Starkov , Malyen Oretsev , and Nikolai Lantsov 's crew met with soldiers from the 22nd Regiment‏‎ at an inn in Vernost before embarking on a procession along the Vy .

At the Spinning Wheel , Alina wrote a request for aid to the Apparat . The message was left beneath the altar at the Church of Sankt Lukin in Vernost.
