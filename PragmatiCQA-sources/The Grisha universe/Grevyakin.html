Grevyakin is a settlement in Ravka .

History

Nikolai Lantsov visited Grevyakin as part of his tour of the country. He convinced the duke to convert two of his fields into cotton farming, as well as build a new mill and a cotton gin.

The duke of Grevyakin was at the treaty meeting in Os Kervo where he backed Zoya Nazyalensky as queen.
