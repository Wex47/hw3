The winter fete , also referred to as the King and Queen's fete , is held at the Grand Palace towards the end of the winter season. It's attended by nobles and Grisha . It is considered by Genya Safin to be the most lavish party of the year, and by the Darkling to be a waste of time. This was the night Baghra revealed herself and the Darkling's secrets to Alina Starkov , helping her flee from Os Alta , and disappear.
