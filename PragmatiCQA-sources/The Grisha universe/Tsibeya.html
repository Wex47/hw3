Tsibeya is the vast wilderness near Ravka 's northeast border with Fjerda . To the north of Tsibeya is permafrost.

History

Shadow and Bone

Tsibeya is the location where the Morozova's herd is first sighted. Mal and Alina spend weeks here tracking the stag .
