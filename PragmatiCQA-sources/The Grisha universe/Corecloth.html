Corecloth is an exceptionally strong and flexible fabric that can withstand bullets. It was a Materialki invention and is used in making kefta for Grisha in the Second Army and the Oprichniki .

There are variations to Corecloth, most notably the clothing worn by Jarl Brum and his drüskelle , specifically made by Fabrikators on jurda parem . This specific Corecloth is designed to block a Corporalki ’s powers, even one on parem .

Trivia

  • Corecloth's properties resemble real-life bulletproof vests used by law enforcement.
