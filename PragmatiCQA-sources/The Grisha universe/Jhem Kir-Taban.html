Jhem Kir-Taban is the younger sister of Makhi and Ehri .

Biography

After her daughter Akeni was killed by the blight, Jhem was in mourning. She did not come to visit Ehri when the princess returned to Ahmrat Jen from Ravka .

Personality and traits

Makhi considered Jhem to be unintelligent and have "the depth of a dinner plate."
