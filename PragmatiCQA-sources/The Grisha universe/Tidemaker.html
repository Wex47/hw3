Tidemakers are Grisha of the Etherealki Order who can manipulate water.

In the Second Army they wear a blue kefta with pale blue embroidery.

"An ordinary Tidemaker can control currents, summon water or moisture from the air or a nearby source." It is shown in The Demon in the Wood that Tidemakers (specifically Annika ) can change water into ice.

Under the effects of jurda parem , a Tidemaker can alter the state of matter, including themselves. This is seen in Six of Crows , when Mikka alters the state of walls in order to pass through them.
