Tsemna was a village in Ravka that was consumed by the Shadow Fold . It was the hometown of Sankta Anastasia .

History

Anastasia lived in Tsemna, where her remarkable beauty was the subject of village gossip. When the wasting plague struck Tsemna, Anastasia cured it by feeding the villagers her blood. On a low hill overlooking the village, a giant stone statue of Sankta Anastasia was built. The area around the monument is a patchy field studded with red boulders.

When the Darkling first created the Fold, Tsemna was lost.

Before journeying into the Fold, Alina Starkov and her companions met up with the Soldat Sol at the monument to Sankta Anastasia.

Elizaveta caused the statue to weep tears of blood, leading Tsemna to be considered the site of another miracle.
