Biography

Shadow and Bone

Upon learning that Alina was nearly hit by the Darkling 's coach, Eva says the Grisha are unnatural and claims the present Darkling is just as bad as his ancestor the Black Heretic. Alexei credits her superstition to her peasant background, not realizing Alina has an even less advantaged origin.

She once told Alina and Alexei that the Darkling had a Healer seal shut a traitor's mouth. They laughed it off as one of Eva's "crazy stories."

Physical description

Eva has pretty green eyes that do little to distract from her piglike nose.

Personality and traits

Eva is superstitious and distrustful of the Grisha .
