Tailors are Grisha of the Corporalki order. They have the ability to change a person's appearance. Tailors were once considered rare and special; however, in Ravka , tailoring is now taught to all Corporalki students of the Second Army . Genya Safin was the first known Tailor and was considered a low-level Grisha who served the Queen of Ravka before the Ravkan Civil War . In the Second Army they wear red kefta with blue embroidery.

Abilities

Tailors can change a person's eyes and hair if they have the right material, or take away or give a tattoo. A primary example of tailoring is Sturmhond , as he was tailored to have red hair, green eyes, and a crooked nose when in fact he has blonde hair, hazel eyes, and just a small bump on his nose. Tailors are capable of changing a person's bone structure and face shape. They can also remove skin flaws and add a blush to the face. A skilled tailor can also change body shape such as shoulder positioning. More skilled tailors can change someone's face for long periods of time and possibly permanently alter features.

When a Corporalki on jurda parem uses their ability to tailor, the changes made to a person are extremely skilled and borderline permanent. In the case of Wylan Van Eck , who was tailored to look like Kuwei Yul-Bo , the only person able to change him back to his original face was Genya Safin.
