Misha is a young Otkazat'sya orphan boy who used to be a servant at the Little Palace . He helped Baghra after the Darkling blinded her.

Biography

Misha used to live at the Little Palace as he and his mother worked as servants there.

Siege and Storm

When the Darkling attacked during Nikolai 's birthday party, Misha escaped with Nikolai, the queen, the king and Baghra on the Kingfisher . His mother was killed during the attack.

After the Darkling blinded Baghra, Misha was assigned to serve her and read her books. Misha first met Alina when she came to visit Baghra.

Ruin and Rising

After Baghra jumps off of the balcony of the Monastery of Sankt Demyan of the Rime, Misha joins Alina and her followers Zoya Nazyalensky , Genya Safin , Malyen Oretsev , Tamar Kir-Bataar , Tolya Yul-Bataar , Harshaw , Nadia and Adrik to help and he stays with them until they destroy the Darkling and the Shadow Fold . After the battle Genya Safin and David Kostyk offer to bring Misha to Kribirsk but he refuses. He insists to go with Alina and Mal , Misha claims that they needed to be looked after.
