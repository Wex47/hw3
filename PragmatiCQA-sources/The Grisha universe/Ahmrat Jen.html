Ahmrat Jen is the capital of Shu Han , located in the center of the country. It is the largest and most prosperous city in Shu Han. It stays warm and sunny all year round, so the city is always filled with sun and blooming plant life. This allows the plum orchards, common in the city, to grow constantly. The palace and the city is described as "lush, green, and full of life".
