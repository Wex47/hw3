The Sea Whip's Fetter is an amplifier that consists of the seven golden scales of Rusalye .

History

Siege and Storm

At The Darkling 's behest, Mal tracks down the Sea Whip. After a violent exchange with the Darkling and his guards, Alina kills Rusalye with a single blow and cuts seven scales from the beast to harness as a cuff-like amplifier that circles her wrists.
