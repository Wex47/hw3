Reyem Yul-Kaat , also known as Locust , is the twin brother of Mayu Kir-Kaat .

Biography

He was a part of the Khergud program, headed by the former Queen Makhi Kir-Taban of Shu Han and her doctor and advisor Minister Yerwei. He was drafted into the Khergud after becoming a rising star in his military regiment, as they thought his talents were too good to waste.

During his transformation to become khergud, he formed a close, romantic bond with Bergin , a Fjerdan Grisha captured and forced to work in the program. They taught each other their languages, swapped stories of home, and kept each other human. He remarks that he would not have survived, or come out with his humanity if he had not had Bergin.

With the resolution of the khergud program by Leyti Kir-Taban and Ehri Kir-Taban , Reyem is currently working with the rest of the surviving khergud and Tamar Kir-Bataar , to find a safe place where they can live without fear of being experimented on or used as weapons.

Skills and abilities

Reyem was a strong combatant before becoming khergud, and the transformation only helped. He had metal pincers fused to his spine, metal alloy bones, and enhanced speed, agility, strength, and senses. He can smell Grisha.
