Ivets is a town beside the Obol River in western Ravka . It is almost 90 miles from Os Alta .

History

Duke Radimov hosted a trade summit with Kerch and Novyi Zem in Ivets.

It was also the location of a miracle. On the Festival of Sankt Grigori , the wooden bridge that crossed the Obol river collapsed while the people paraded across it, and a bridge of bone sprung up in its place.

Trivia

  • Alyosha and Arkady were known as the Horsemen of Ivets.
