Brevno is a village in Ravka .

History

A demon came to Brevno; a priest drove it out, but it took up residence in the nearby lake. Unable to fish or drink from the lake, the townsmen tried to attack the demon with flaming arrows. Sankt Petyr held the demon out of the lake, allowing the villagers to kill it and free the lake. Since then, its waters felt colder than they had before.
