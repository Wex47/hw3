Adaptations

TV series

In January 11, 2019, it was announced that Netflix would be in charge of bringing Leigh Bardugo's Grishaverse to television. The first season of the series consists of 8 episodes and combines the stories of the Shadow and Bone trilogy and the Six of Crows duology . It was adapted by Eric Heisserer.

Film

In September 2012, Holly Bario, president of production of Dreamworks, announced that she had acquired the rights to make a film based on Shadow and Bone . David Heyman with Jeffrey Clifford, president of Heymaker Films would produce. But after several years, no more news were released, most likely the project was scrapped and the rights reverted back to the author.
