Lada Garin was Zoya Nazyalensky 's adoptive cousin.

Biography

Lada was adopted by Liliyana Garin , Zoya's aunt. Zoya met Lada only once, when Zoya crossed the fold and came to visit her Aunt Liliyana . Lada demanded that Zoya help her draw a picture of the Little Palace in extensive detail.

In the Novokribirsk massacre, Lada went missing, and though Zoya asked everywhere, it's most likely she died.
