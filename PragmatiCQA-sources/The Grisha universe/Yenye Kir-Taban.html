Yenye Kir-Taban is the younger sister of Makhi and Ehri .

Biography

Yenye was expected to marry soon, which would prevent her from being a possible heir to the Taban throne .

When Ehri returned from Ravka to the Taban palace, Yenye and Kheru visited her. They brought Ehri flowers and fruits and talked with her while doing needlework. Yenye mentioned that Kheru had ambitions about being named the queen's heir and rumors that Ehri planned to challenge Queen Makhi; Ehri refuted this.

Personality and traits

Yenye is sharp and sly, and sometimes ignores social niceties in favor of speaking cruel truths. She is not a schemer, but the power of the Taban queen appeals to her as it does to all her sisters. According to Ehri, the Taban sisters never truly fought and were always polite with each other.

Physical description

Yenye has a white streak in her hair, which she often touches. She has a "sharp gaze."
