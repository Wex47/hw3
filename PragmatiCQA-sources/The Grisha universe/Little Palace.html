History

Shadow and Bone

Alina is taken to the Little Palace by the Darkling after discovering that she's a Sun Summoner .

Siege and Storm

After the Darkling 's attempted coup and his escape, the Little Palace is seized by the First Army , armed soldiers guarding its perimeter to prevent the few remaining resident Grisha from escaping.

The palace is partially destroyed during the Darkling's attack on Os Alta.
