Durasts are Grisha of the Materialki order. Because Alkemi and Durasts often share their workspaces and do similar work, they usually aren't distinguished and are only called Fabrikators .

Durasts wear purple keftas with gray embroidery.

Abilities

They can manipulate glass, steel, wood, plants, stone, or anything solid on a molecular level. This includes changing a solid's shape, texture, or composition. They can make flowers bloom, remove an object's pigment, move objects without touching them, and even absorb harmful matter from another person's body into their own.

Trivia

  • The word Durast comes from the Latin word durus which means "solid, hard, or strong." This reflects that Durasts are working with solid materials and as well reflects that their products are strong and durable ( durable comes from Latin durus , too).
