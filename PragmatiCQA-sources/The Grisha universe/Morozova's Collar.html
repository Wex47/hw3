Morozova's Collar is a powerful amplifier made from the antlers of a white stag .

History

Shadow and Bone

The Darkling has a Durast named David Kostyk create the amplifier that Alina Starkov wears around her neck. The amplifier was made from the antlers of Morozova's stag , fused together in a circle so that they can not be broken or removed. The Darkling does this to Alina so he can expand the Unsea and they can be connected.

Shadow and Bone (TV)

Kirigan makes the collar for Alina , and the collar is shown physically in Alina's body.
