Amita Suman is a Nepal-born English actress. In Shadow and Bone , she portrays Inej Ghafa .

Biography

She was born in Nepal and moved to England at the age of 7. Her father and step-mother own the Broadwater Fryer, a chip shop in Worthing. Amita attended Sussex Downs College and trained at the Academy of Live and Recorded Arts.
