Biography

She was born in Jerusalem and grew up in Los Angeles. She attended and graduated from Yale University. Shadow and Bone was her first book and made her a popular and respected author. Although writing is her main passion, she also loves makeup artistry and singing with her band, Captain Automatic.

She appears in the third episode of the Netflix adaptation of Shadow and Bone as an unnamed Durast .
