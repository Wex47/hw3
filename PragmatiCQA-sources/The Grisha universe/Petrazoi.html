The Petrazoi are a mountain range in Ravka , northwest of Ryevost and Os Alta .

The mountains reach above tree level and in some places, frost remains year-round. The rocks of the Petrazoi are jagged and gray.

Trivia

  • The glaciers in the Petrazoi melt to feed the Sokol River .
