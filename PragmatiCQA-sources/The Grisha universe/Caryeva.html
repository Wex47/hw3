Caryeva is a settlement in Ravka , located close to the southern border. It is known for its horse races.

Horse races and auctions occur in Caryeva. It is largely deserted after the summer horse sales, though in the past, the horse races lasted late into the fall. The Suli often spent the season in Caryeva, working or performing for the audiences. Vasily Lantsov was said to participate in showings and auctions there.

Trivia

  • Caryevan wine is aged in clay and considered to be fine wine.
  • Natasha Beritrova is the baroness of Caryeva.
