The Sikurzoi are a mountain range across most of Ravka 's eastern and southern border with Shu Han .

The northern Sikurzoi are massive, snowcapped peaks much larger than the Petrazoi . This part of the mountain range is known as the Elbjen, meaning "Elbows", in Fjerdan . The foothills of the southern Sikurzoi are gentle slopes covered in tall grasses, with many valleys. Further south, the mountains are once again rocky, snowcapped giants.

Trivia

  • Snowmelt from the southern Sikurzoi feeds the Cera Huo river.
  • Ivan the Golden , one of the Lantsov kings, lost the Sikurzoi to the Shu during the Border Wars .
  • The first Taban queens came down from the highest mountains in the Sikurzoi to establish their rule over Shu Han.
  • The Monastery of Sankt Feliks is located in the Sikurzoi, though the path was blocked by a landslide nearly three hundred years prior to Rule of Wolves .
  • The Suli regularly travel into the Sikurzoi. When the working men of her caravan were nearly trapped inside the mountain mines, Sankta Marya of the Rock created a path for them to safely exit. Legend holds that at the base of the Sikurzoi, the Suli can always find shelter in the caves that she left behind.
