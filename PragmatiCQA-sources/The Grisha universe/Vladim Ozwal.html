Biography

Ruin and Rising

Vladim, along with other men and women, was part of the guards used by The Apparat to keep Alina hidden and "safe". At some point of her confinement, the Sun Summoner tried to escape with the help of her friends, and in the process she summoned light after a long time. This act made Vladim and his comrades very fearful of Alina, and she, trying to keep their loyalty so The Apparat couldn't use them against her, talked to Vladim in a gentle way, asking his name and offering him to serve her and wear her mark. After obtaining his permission, she burned the mark of her hand in his chest. Since that moment, Vladim used to be near Alina, guarding her door or protecting her in some way, but he stayed with The Apparat when Alina and her friends left.

Vladim and his comrades joined Alina again to help her in her attempt to stop The Darkling in the Shadow Fold . During the fight, Alina obtained the power of the three Amplifiers of Morozova , which made her power grow and then leave her in order to multiply, creating new Sun Summoners. Vladim was one of them, and his hands started to shine in the darkness, surprising him. After the fight, he was shown attending "Alina's" funeral.

King of Scars

Vladim and the other Sun Summoners were brought to Os Alta in order to be trained as Etherealki in the Little Palace . Their mission was to continue Alina's legacy and one of their task is to destroy completely whatever is left of the Fold.

Rule of Wolves

After the Apparat fled to Fjerda , Vladim was appointed the new head of the church and spiritual counselor to the king of Ravka. Vladim performs the marriage ceremony for Genya Safin and David Kostyk in the royal chapel.

Vladim crowns Zoya Nazyalensky with a dragon-shaped crown made of titanium and sapphires. With Zoya as the queen of Ravka, Vladim serves as her Apparat.

Personality and traits

It is shown in his interactions with Alina that he's very devoted, almost zealot following the cause of Sankta Alina. He's also very impressible.

Physical description

Vladim is a young man a little older than Alina. He was starting to grow a beard when he joined the followers of Sankta Alina, and has dimples that appear each time he smiles. He bears the tattoo symbolizing his link to the cause, and after a conflict between the Apparat and Alina, the Sun Summoner used her power to burn the mark of her hand in Vladim's chest, making him loyal to her instead of the priest. Since then, Vladim walked around showing his chest in order to show Alina's mark.
