Sikursk is a settlement in southeastern Ravka , near the Sikurzoi and the border of Shu Han . North of Sikursk are plains.

History

Fedyor Kaminsky was serving at an army outpost near Sikursk. After Novokribirsk was consumed by the Shadow Fold , the King’s soldiers turned on the Grisha in Sikursk, "pulling them from their beds in the middle of the night and mounting sham trials to determine their loyalty." Fedyor helped lead an escape.

Khergud captured three Ravkan Grisha who were undercover in Sikursk.

Sikursk was struck by the blight.
