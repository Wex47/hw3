Inspiration

  • As explained by Leigh Bardugo , Ravkan is not exactly the same as ; the language is heavily inspired by Russian, but is derived from both Russian and . It seems in part to have a mostly Russian vocabulary, with much simplified noun and adjective plural endings, which may come from Mongolian.
  • The written version created by David J. Peterson appears to resemble the (the oldest known Slavic writing system) in addition to and is partially intelligible to Cyrillic readers according to Bulgarian native Julian Kostov .

Trivia
