The Soldat Sol , also known as the Sun Soldiers , are the holy warriors sworn to protect Alina Starkov . They were originally led by the Apparat but now follow Alina.

History

After Alina obtained the power of the three Amplifiers of Morozova , her ability to summon was taken, and given to several of her followers, of which only one is confirmed: Vladim . Now he and the new sun summoners are trained in the capital, and have the duty to completely clean the Fold .

Overview

The Soldat Sol bear a tattoo of Alina's golden sunburst.
