Alkhem Bay is a bay of the True Sea located off the western coast of Ravka . The port city Os Kervo is located on the bay and features a famous lighthouse.

History

Alkhem Bay was a violent part of the ocean and its strong waves often prevented ships from landing at Os Kervo. Sankt Vladimir caused the water to recede from the bay, allowing the base of a lighthouse and a massive seawall to protect it to be built in the bay.
