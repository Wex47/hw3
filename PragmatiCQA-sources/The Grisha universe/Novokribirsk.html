Novokribirsk was one of the cities bordering the Shadow Fold in Ravka. The city was on the western side of the fold, almost directly across from Kribirsk .

History

Zoya Nazyalensky's aunt and niece lived in the city, and died when it was destroyed by The Darkling . Zoya left The Darkling's service after this incident.
