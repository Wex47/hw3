Creator: Harry Potter and the Chamber of Secrets is the sequel to LEGO Creator: Harry Potter . It was released in 2002 as part of the Lego Creator video game series. It was based on Harry Potter and the Chamber of Secrets and its corresponding Lego sets.

Gameplay

In this game, you can explore different parts of the Harry Potter world, customise them with Lego models, and play as many different characters. You can also create you own models and characters. Your goal is to collect chocolate frogs. When you collect enough, you can do a challenge. Upon completing a challenge, you unlock more models, worlds, and characters. There are many more worlds in this game than the previous one, some of which include Knockturn Alley , Gringotts Bank , and The Chamber of Secrets itself.
