The 1990s was a strange, violent period in the history of the wizarding world . The mid-1990s was the era of the second war against Lord Voldemort , though the origins of it go back much farther. In fact, one might conclude the first war against Voldemort had not actually ended and that the second war was a mere continuation.

The 1990s was also the time period when Harry Potter , Ron and Ginny Weasley , Hermione Granger , Neville Longbottom , Luna Lovegood and Draco Malfoy attended Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry . More specifically the years in which this group attended the school were 1991 to 1999 .
