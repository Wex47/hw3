Events

  • Albus Dumbledore was temporarily away, so Minerva McGonagall delivered the welcoming speech instead.
  • Patricia Rakepick was introduced as the new Defence Against the Dark Arts teacher.
  • The outdoor celebration of magic was held.
  • The All-Wizard Tournament was held.
  • The Valentine's Day Ball was held.
  • Equinox Enchanted was performed in the Three Broomsticks Inn .
  • The Great Hogwarts Cook-Off was held.
