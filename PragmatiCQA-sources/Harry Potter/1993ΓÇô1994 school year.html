Notes

  • The following schedule happened in this order, on their first day (likely Wednesday) - at nine o'clock, Divination , Muggle Studies and Arithmancy . They then attended Transfiguration followed by lunch. Care of Magical Creatures was right after lunch and the last class of the day.
  • Double Care of Magical Creatures , Divination and then Defence Against the Dark Arts took place in that order, on the first day of second term. It was likely not a Thursday as Lupin schedules his first Dementor lesson with Harry for then.
  • Finally, Care of Magical Creatures, Charms, then lunch and Divination took place on a final unknown day near Easter.
  • On 16 October (Friday) they had Herbology, Transfiguration, then another lesson.
  • Care of Magical Creatures tends to be one-hour.
  • On at least one day, Arithmancy and Care of Magical Creatures took place at the same time.

Events

  • During the summer holidays before start of term, Sirius Black was reported having broken out of Azkaban . For reasons unknown (to the general student body), the Ministry of Magic ordered Dementors to guard Hogwarts , although they were not permitted on the grounds, as Black apparently had targeted someone at Hogwarts.
  • Despite the order preventing them from entering Hogwarts, one of the first Quidditch matches of the season was disrupted, and Harry Potter was nearly killed, when Dementors attacked during a game.
  • Rubeus Hagrid was appointed as the new Care of Magical Creatures teacher. In his first lesson, Draco Malfoy got injured by the Hippogriff Buckbeak . This led to an inquiry about the danger of Buckbeak, who was sentenced to death, though he escaped due to Hermione's Time-Turner .
  • In the Defence Against the Dark Arts class, while dealing with Boggarts , Neville Longbottom 's Boggart turned into Severus Snape . Neville ridiculed it by making it wear the clothes of his grandmother. The story spread throughout the school and Snape was reported to be furious about it.
  • Strange stories regarding student Hermione Granger circulated during the school year, including apparently impossible reports of her attending two classes at the same time, as well as the rare sign of her storming out of and abandoning a class .
  • After the portrait of the Fat Lady was attacked by Sirius Black, she refused to guard the entrance to the Gryffindor Tower. She was temporarily replaced by a portrait of Sir Cadogan .
  • Newton Scamander visited the school during this year.

Quidditch

Cup-Winner: Gryffindor

During the Gryffindor vs. Hufflepuff match, Harry Potter was affected by Dementors and fell off his broom. Unfortunately his Nimbus 2000 got blown into the Whomping Willow and was smashed by it. Harry received a Firebolt from an (at the time) unknown benefactor. It was later revealed to have been from Sirius Black. The broom, however, got confiscated to be checked for enchantments and was only given back to him a few days before his match against Ravenclaw .
