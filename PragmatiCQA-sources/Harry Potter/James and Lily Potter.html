James and Lily Potter were a married couple and the parents of the famous Harry Potter . They were both Gryffindor students at Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry and members of the Order of the Phoenix . They were both murdered by Lord Voldemort on Hallowe'en in 1981 , while trying to protect their infant son. See their individual articles for more information:
