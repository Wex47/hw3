LEGO Harry Potter: Characters of the Magical World is a book chronicling the various LEGO forms of Harry Potter characters, carrying the tagline "With every LEGO Harry Potter minifigure and creature." It was released on 18 June, 2012 , written by Jon Richards. It is tagged as being for ages 6+, with a warning of a possible choking hazard for those under the age of 3 due to an included Harry Potter minifigure.

The book is organised by sections for every title in the Harry Potter series. It includes numerous variations of key characters such as Harry Potter and Severus Snape . Various magical creatures, including Merpeople and Dementors .

The book also includes a special look at elements unique to the LEGO Harry Potter releases and feature highlighting releases that have double-sided heads.

This title is a followup to LEGO Harry Potter: Building the Magical World .
