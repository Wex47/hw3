The Daily Prophet was a collection of three-page documents available to members of the Official Harry Potter Fan Club in 1998 and 1999. The UK publishers of the Harry Potter series , Bloomsbury, created the Fan Club and it was only available to British citizens. There were four newsletters printed and written by J. K. Rowling herself.

The newsletters were written from an in-universe point of view, detailing aspects of the wizarding world . Some of the information in the newsletters does not fit exactly with information from later books, since they were printed before the release of Harry Potter and the Goblet of Fire . Some of the character names and information did find their way into the later books or other merchandise, such as the Famous Wizard Cards .

The Daily Prophet website

is an online website that produces fictional editorials, written from an in-universe perspective, for a real-world audience. It features contributions from fan-fiction writers in multiple columns meant to mimic the categories found throughout the in-universe newspaper from the Harry Potter series.

Founded in 2018 by Schieler Mew, pen-name Selevas Amagus, the website features over one hundred articles from multiple writers in the following major categories:

  • Classifieds
  • Entertainment
  • Everything Muggles
  • Magical News
  • Magical Places
  • Matters of Magic
  • Recipes
  • Quizzes and Games
