Bulgaria is a country in Southeastern Europe . Its capital city is Sofia. It borders five countries: Romania , Serbia , Macedonia , Greece , and Turkey . The Black Sea defines the extent of the country to the east.

Wizarding world

Education

Some Bulgarian witches and wizards attended Durmstrang Institute and taught there, including the school's founder, Nerida Vulchanova , and famous Quidditch player Viktor Krum .

Games and sports

Bulgaria had a national Quidditch team , the home stadium of which was the Bulgarian National Quidditch Stadium .

The Vratsa Vultures was a popular Quidditch team from this country. This team was known as the pioneers of the long goal (scoring from outside the scoring area), and won the European Cup seven times.
