LEGO Creator: Harry Potter is a video game for Windows released in 2001. It is the third entry in the LEGO Creator video game series. Originally, this was the only game to exist alongside the actual LEGO Harry Potter sets. A sequel was later made entitled Creator: Harry Potter and the Chamber of Secrets .

A demo was available on the DVD-ROM of Harry Potter and the Philosopher's Stone .

Gameplay

Lego Creator: Harry Potter is an open-ended creation game. One explores four different "worlds": Diagon Alley , King's Cross Station , Hogwarts grounds, and the inside of Hogwarts. There are also many mini-worlds, such as Hagrid's Hut , the Gryffindor Common Room , Potions Classroom , third-floor corridor and Underground Chambers . In each of these worlds one is able to use Lego models and minifigures to customise the worlds however is wished. These models are all based upon the Harry Potter and the Philosopher's Stone LEGO sets. There is also a feature which allows for custom-made minifigures and sets. In addition to building, one can control characters, cast spells , and change the weather. There are also multiple challenges available. Upon completion of each challenge, the game will sometimes show a cutscene, and unlock a new spell to use. There is a narrator character to guide and aid the player throughout the game, who is an elderly looking wizard with grey hair, red clothing and a purple wand .
