Harry Potter: The Creature Vault is an official, out-of-universe book by Jody Revenson based on the Harry Potter film series. The book offers a comprehensive, behind-the-scenes look at the various creatures of the Harry Potter series as seen in the films by providing an in-depth look at the development of the beasts, from concept art to real life, along with interviews from the designers.
