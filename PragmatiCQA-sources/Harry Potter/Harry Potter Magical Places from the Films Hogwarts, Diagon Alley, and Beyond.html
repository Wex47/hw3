Harry Potter: Magical Places from the Films: Hogwarts, Diagon Alley, and Beyond is a book by Jody Revenson based on the Harry Potter film series . It was released on 12 May , 2015 . The book offers a comprehensive, behind-the-scenes look at the various locations of the Harry Potter series as seen in the films, such as Diagon Alley , Hogwarts Castle , and The Burrow . In addition to the constructed sets, the book also covers some of the real-world locations used for scouting and filming the series. Included along with the book is a map of Diagon Alley and a catalogue titled "The Paintings of Hogwarts" showcasing some of the portraits used in the films.
