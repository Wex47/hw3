Genghis is Clara Godfrey 's dog who, in the opinion of Nate Wright , somewhat looks like her.

Biography

At some point, Genghis was bought by Mrs. Godfrey . When her student, Nate encountered him, Genghis made an attempt to bite him. Wright was appalled, as he was good with canines, but when Godfrey showed up he realized his mistake. Francis suggests that Genghis is a product of Mrs. Godfrey 's environment.

Behind the Scenes

He first and as of now only appeared on . His name is a reference to Mongol Empire leader Genghis Khan, a reference to Mrs. Godfrey 's occupation as a social studies teacher.
