The "Public School 38 ANGERGRAPH!" is a ranking system created by Nate "Scoop" Wright . It was shown in Big Nate: In The Zone . Nate ranks all the teachers by their frequency of anger, and intensity of anger, and it looks like Mrs. Godfrey is the most angriest person on the chart, while Mr. Rosa is the most calm.

The purpose of Nate's Angergraph was to show the temper of the teachers at P.S. 38 .

Standings

  • Ms. Clarke - Miffed/Annoyed Weekly
  • Mrs. Czerwicki - Miffed as a way of life
  • Mr. Staples - Annoyed/Upset Monthly
  • Mrs. Hickson "Hickey" - Upset Weekly
  • Random Lunch Lady - Upset/Mad as a way of life
  • Coach Calhoun - Mad Monthly
  • Principal Nichols - Mad/Furious Weekly
  • Mr. Galvin - Furious/Psycho Monthly/Weekly
  • Coach John - Furious/Psycho Daily
