Ellen Wright is Nate Wright's older sister who is in highschool. Nate and Ellen are complete opposites and they hate each other, mostly because of their differences in the academic field and personality traits.

Ellen has a part-time job at Dilly Burger , and she has to wear a uniform that looks like "a ventriloquist's dummy" as described by Nate in "Big Nate: On A Roll".

Biography

Early Life

In the mix, she and Nate had a babysitter named Donna, who bought them tickets for a performance of "Peter Pan". Ellen lectured Nate during the show (for instance, she reminded him that intermission was called as such, not half-time), annoying him plenty. In the past, and up to this point, she slept with a teddy bear she named "Mister Puff-a-Lump" (seen in the November 12, 1995 strip).

On her 12th birthday, Ellen received a karaoke set, and she continued to use it constantly, loudly and eccentrically as a teenager, and she still hasn't outgrown it, according to Nate in Big Nate: In A Class By Himself .

Trivia

  • No one else finds her obnoxious as Nate does, as he is annoyed at the fact they all think she's so wonderful.
  • She likes oatmeal, as proven in " Big Nate: In A Class By Himself ".
