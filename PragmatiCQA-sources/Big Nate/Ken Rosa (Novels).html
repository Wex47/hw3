Ken Rosa is the art teacher at P.S. 38 and the husband of Debbie Grundzlweick . Whenever Nate chooses his favorite teacher, he always chooses Mr. Rosa. This is mostly because Mr. Rosa most of the time is too burned out to teach "real" subjects. Nate has got detention several times from Mr. Rosa, but he has never given any to anyone else. He was Nate's homeroom teacher at first, but Nate was moved to Mrs. Godfrey's homeroom later on. Mr. Rosa has given Nate detention only once in the novel, Big Nate: In a Class by Himself .

Biography

Big Nate In A Class By Himself

After class with Ms. Clarke , Nate Wright, Teddy Ortiz , and Francis Pope head off to see normal things at their school. Then they see drawings on a display case and learn that Artur’s shoe drawing is the most popular for two months in a row. In the art class, Nate tries to get Mr. Rosa to put his drawing in the display case, but Rosa claims he doesn’t have time (saying he doesn't have time for frivolous requests). Rosa then assigns everyone to make puppet heads. Nate whispers to Francis to create a distraction, which he does by letting off a balloon, resulting in the class going wild and Mr. Rosa having to calm them down. Nate gets his green light, but when trying to open the display case, the doorknob breaks off. Mr. Rosa hears this and rushes out to find Nate holding the knob in his hand. Rosa gives Nate his third detention of the day. Nate also mentions that Rosa has never given detention before that year.

Passage to Jefferson

In this book, Mr. Rosa is the cartooning club faculty adviser. Nate told Rosa and Mrs. Everett about the Ultimate Snowdown, which was an attempt to defeat Jefferson, convinced them to act as the judges for the contest. The event, which began at 10:00 A.M., was held on Saturday on the Soccer Field. As everyone organized their building posts, Jefferson hung a tarp so no one would notice them using their mascot, which the P.S. 38 students did not notice this at first. Apparently, Rosa and Everett waited in a place where they could not witness the design of both projects. Everybody began work for the next six hours. When it was 3:00 (the time for judging), Jefferson had lost since they had just put snow over the old suit of armor in the storage room, which Mr. Rosa and Nate both noticed. P.S. 38, on the other hand, made their sculpture out of snow, using an icicle for an arrow and nail polish for blood. Everett and Rosa both agreed that P.S. 38 had won for their creativity, and then announced P.S. 38's victory. They had finally surpassed Jefferson Middle School.

Background

Personality and Traits

Nate describes Ken as a "too burned-out teacher, and does not care about what his students do". It is shown that he makes them work on "special projects" often though. He likes Nate as only a small friend although most of the time he is annoyed with Nate . In Big Nate Goes For Broke, he and Nate have a very strong friendship. Nate has even visited his house. Nate notes also, that he has helped them win a contest against Jefferson Middle School .

Trivia

  • He has a Master's Degree and 3 Bachelor Degrees as well.
  • He has been teaching students at P.S 38 for a very long time.
  • He really wanted to be an artist, and he even had his own art show.
  • He has the lowest rating on Nate's 'P.S. 38 ANGERGRAPH!', rated as 'miffed monthly'.
  • He is the manager of P.S 38's chess club.
  • He likes to keep his door open and is an "approachable teacher".
