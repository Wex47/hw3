Klassic Komix is a comic shop in the Big Nate books and comics. Managed by Wayne , and the only known employee is Gordie . Nate tends to go there a lot to chat with Gordie , or buy some comics like Femme Fatality . Nate once thought that he caught a person stealing from the store, when in it was just the store manager - Wayne . This was seen in Big Nate: On a Roll . He actually did once catch Randy shoplifting a comic . Nate has also called Wayne a "caveman" before because of how rough he looks with the beard and his mumbling. Nate is an intern at Klassic Comix. Klassic Comix also appeared in Poptropica, on Big Nate Island.
