Big Nate: Silent But Deadly is a comic strip collection book containing comic strips by Lincoln Peirce from October 6, 2013, to March 29, 2014.

It is the is the 15th comic collection book of the series (20th if you include the older versions) and is preceded by Big Nate: A Good Old Fashioned Wedgie and is followed by Big Nate: Goes Bananas .

Summary

"Everyone's favorite sixth grader is back for more misadventures, and Big Nate: Silent But Deadly smells like a winner!

Whether he's showing the ropes to a detention rookie, campaigning for the Student of the Month Award, or writing hilarious movie reviews for the Weekly Bugle , Nate Wright never fails to make his mark at P.S. 38. But middle school's no bed of roses. In fact, sometimes it just plain stinks. Just ask the Great Nose-ini! Nate's alter ego with a sense for scents can smell trouble a mile away . . . or at the very next desk. Was that you, Gina? Spoiler alert: Peter is smarter than Gina!

Join Nate and the gang for nonstop laughs in this latest collection of Big Nate comics!" - Lincoln Peirce
