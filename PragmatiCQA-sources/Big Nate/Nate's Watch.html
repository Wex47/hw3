Nate Wright has an ''Agent Cody Banks'' wristwatch, which he wears almost every day. There is very limited information known about Nate Wright ’s watch.

Trivia

  • Nate used his watch to track how long he was dating Angie which was 6 months, 11 days, 4 hours, and 32 minutes, from July 14, 1997, to February 12, 1998.
  • He also used it to track the time he was dating Abby Wilson which was 5 minutes, and 10 seconds.
  • He used his watch to time the Neighborhood Olympics .
