Cheez Doodles are a cheddar cheese-flavored corn puff that is produced by Wise Foods™. They are Nate Wright 's favorite snack food by far. The servings are called doodles. They are also Lincoln Pierce' s favorite junk food. Marty Wright once made Nate go on a Cheez Doodle diet, which drove him insane. They are similar to Cheetos, both being puffy, crunchy, and made of real cheese. However, Nate is very particular about Cheez Doodles and will not eat any other cheese-flavored corn puffs.

Trivia

  • Cheez Doodles are located/found in aisle nine of "Wise Foods™" for $1.39.
  • Nate seems to have an addiction to writing poems about Cheez Doodles, which annoys his English teacher, Mrs. Clarke , due to the fact that Nate writes too many poems about Cheez Doodles and he gets the powder on his homework. This also annoys other teachers, such as Mrs. Godfrey .
  • Nate also has a Cheez Doodle collection , consisting of all sorts of different shaped and sized Cheez Doodles. His friends got so disgusted, they went to play basketball in the rain.
  • Once Nate's dad tried to get Nate to cut down on Cheez Doodles, he provided an alternative called Cheezy Weezies . Due to the lack of fat in the content, Nate found Cheezy Weezies to be absolutely disgusting and quoted, "This is like promising a kid a puppy and then coming home with a Scooby-Doo hand puppet."
  • Nate once tried to invent the world's first powder-free Cheez Doodle, but after many failed attempts, he 'invented' cheese.
  • Nate once went through withdrawal when the snack machine ran out of Cheez Doodles.
  • Nate's teeth are often yellow because he eats too much Cheez Doodles.
  • Nate realized that him and Angie were meant for each other after he had discovered that she likes Cheez Doodles.
  • Nate and his ex-girlfriend, Angie , both have Cheez Doodle costumes, and they wore them for Halloween, which surprised Francis.
  • In Jefferson Middle School, there is a vending machine that has Cheez Doodles.
  • There are 6.5 doodles per serving.
  • It is shown that Nate makes a speech about Cheez Doodles every day.
  • Both Nate's and Mrs. Godfrey's favorite snack is Cheez Doodles, much to Nate's dismay.
  • Nate once ate Cheez Doodles so much, he stopped eating them, until he smelled one of them, and went back to eating them.
  • He one time had to go on a diet where his dad got him a fat free version of cheez doodles, but Nate despised them.
  • Nate one time reinvented the cheez doodle, but failed many times. One attempt, he used cottage cheese.
  • Teddy Ortiz claimed once he didn't like Cheez Doodles in a . He then tried one, and changed his mind.
  • Nate made a "J" Cheez Doodle for Jenny.
