The Detention Room is a room at P.S. 38 where students serve detention, located directly across the hall from the faculty lounge. Mrs. Czerwicki is the detention monitor of the room. Nate Wright has an assigned seat in The Detention Room.

Appearance

The room has several posters with messages saying, "Be quiet", "Quiet, please", and "No talking". There is also a portrait of P.S. 38's first principal behind Mrs. Czerwicki's desk which was one of the items of the scavenger hunt.
