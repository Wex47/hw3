Biography

Kelly met Nate at a soccer camp during the summer. Nate dumped Kelly because Nate had a crush on a girl, Jenny Jenkins , for years, and even though she despises Nate, Nate thought he would be able to ask her out.

Nate broke up with Kelly by writing a note on the back of a detention slip during Writing Class, thus infuriating Kelly. Kelly confronted him about it, and while Wright tried to defend his actions, Kelly refused to give in. She also questioned his logic in saying she was awesome, whereas he was acting like a jerk. Before Wright could respond, Kelly left across the lawn of P.S. 38 . It is unknown what happened to Kelly after Nate broke up with her. She was mentioned by Teddy Ortiz when he wanted to break up with Paige on October 18, 2016, and by Francis Pope on June 18, 2013.
