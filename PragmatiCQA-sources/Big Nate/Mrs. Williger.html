Mrs. Williger is the principal of Jefferson Middle School . She welcomed Nate Wright , Francis Pope , Teddy Ortiz , and Dee Dee Holloway when they arrived at the school. She was also present when Nate breaks his hand. She was angry at Nate for what she calls his "horseplay," when it was actually Nolan's fault for bullying Chad. In the comic strip in September 2008, she is afraid that Nate will cause trouble because Principal Nichols warned her about his behavior and how Nate acts in class.
