The Bethany Treasury: Nobody Understands Me , is an comic strip treasury of Bethany, the comic strip which Nate and Ellen enjoy reading.

Plot

Bethany is a high school sophomore. She went out with Jason in the comic, however, at some point in the comic, Jason dumped her.

Characters

  • Bethany
  • Jason
  • Evan

Trivia

  • Nate Wright was forced to get one for his sister Ellen Wright , but was immersed in the book once he took a look at it. Francis Pope caught Nate reading the book and was going to tell everyone but Nate shut him up by saying he would tell everyone something that he only knows about him.
  • The book is a collection of comics.
