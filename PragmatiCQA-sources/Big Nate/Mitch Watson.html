Mitch Watson is a producer, writer, and actor. He wrote the live-action telefilm, Ben 10: Race Against Time (featuring Robert Picardo), and was producer and writer for Beware the Batman (starring JB Blanc). He also was one of the writers Scooby Doo! Mystery Incorporated and produced the Netflix series All Hail King Julien. He is the producer of the Big Nate TV show .
