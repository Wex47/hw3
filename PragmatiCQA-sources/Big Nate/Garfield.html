Garfield is the iconic cat from the long-running Garfield comic-strip created by Jim Davis. The comic started on June 19, 1978 and still goes on to this day. He also makes cameo appearances in a few Big Nate comics.

Personality

Garfield is usually depicted as a lazy and selfish cat who wants nothing more than to relax and eat lasagna. However, he has been shown to have a more sensitive side.

About

Garfield is an overweight orange cat with black stripes. He has huge eyes with black (sometimes green) pupils.

Appearances

  • On May 9, 1993, Nate Wright is shown to have a Garfield sticker on his notebook along with Bart Simpson and Igdoof .
  • On February 6, 1995, Bob Snuggles is shown reading Garfield in the newspaper.
  • On August 20, 1995, Garfield, along with Billy from The Family Circus, make a cameo appearance.
  • On May 16, 1997, Nate Wright reads a Garfield comic featuring Garfield and Odie to Ellen Wright in an attempt to teach her about comics.
  • On July 19, 1997, Angie is seen drawing a picture of Garfield.
  • On April 18, 2004, Francis Pope was seen reading a Garfield comic.
  • On January 13, 2009, Francis Pope calls Nate Wright ‘Garfield’ after Nate Wright says he has cat-like reflexes.
  • On August 15, 2009, Nate Wright uses a Garfield puppet as a saber-toothed tiger while putting on a puppet show with School Picture Guy .
  • On November 11, 2010, Wesley Nichols is seen drawing “the world’s lamest Garfield”, which Nate Wright mistakes for a walrus. This happened in a P.S. 38 Cartooning Club meeting.
  • On Christmas 2010, Nate Wright watches “A Very Garfield Christmas” with Spitsy .
  • On December 26, 2013, Nate Wright buys Spitsy a Garfield chia pet for Christmas.
  • On August 12, 2018, Nate Wright reads Spitsy a Garfield comic.
  • On June 16, 2019, Nate Wright uses his Garfield puppet to distract a dog, who ends up playing with the puppet.
  • On November 15, 2019, Chad Applewhite wishes that if he were in a comic strip, that it would be Garfield.
  • On August 22, 2020, Nate Wright uses scissors disguised as a Garfield hand puppet to clip Spitsy 's nails.
  • On September 22, 2020, Coach Calhoun says: "Garfield really hates Mondays, doesn't he?" when he's reading a newspaper that Nate gives him.
  • In Big Nate: Boredom Buster , Garfield is one of the words you find in a word search.

Trivia

  • Despite the fact that Nate has ailurophobia , he (usually) does not seem to be bothered by Garfield.
  • Nate appeared in the 2018 Garfield book Age Happens: Garfield Hits the Big 4-0 , painting a picture of Garfield.
  • Both Garfield and Big Nate have upcoming TV series on Nickelodeon.
