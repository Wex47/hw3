A Supa-Sno Tube is a special durable tube used for sledding. Teddy Ortiz once bought one using the money he earned shoveling driveway. He Nate Wright , and Francis Pope , used it to sled down Cluffy's Cliff , the steepest sledding hill in town, until Nolan , a Jefferson student, and his gang (other Jefferson students) snatched it from them. Although the tube can only hold two people, Nolan's entire gang sled on it at once. Once they got to the bottom of the hill, it popped, and Nate suggested that they could go over to his house and patch it, but it was obvious that the damage was beyond repairable.

Appearances

Big Nate Goes For Broke

This tube is briefly owned by Teddy Ortiz. Teddy bought it from the money he earned by shoveling various driveways. Nate Wright , Francis Pope , and Teddy used it to sled down Cluffy's Cliff until Nolan and his gang showed up and snatched it from them. Teddy claimed that the tube can only hold two people at once, but the group sledded on it anyway. When they caught air by going over the first bump, it popped when they landed again.

The bullies then flounced off as the trio miserably consulted the damage. It is unknown if Teddy replaced it or patched it, and Nate said he thought it was beyond patching.
