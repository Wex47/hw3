Dr. Arch Enemy is a character that appears in the novel " Big Nate Goes For Broke ", and is known as the enemy and rival of Doctor Cesspool . It is noted that both Doctor Cesspool and Dr. Arch Enemy are characters in a comic Nate Wright made, and are not "real" characters. He was seen when Doctor Cesspool was (illegally) performing surgery on the mayor (which resulted in Doctor Cesspool taking out the mayor's stomach). Half of Dr. Arch Enemy's appearance was drawn by Dee Dee , because Nate Wright broke his wrist after trying to catch Chad Applewhite 's donut (not the food kind, mind you, but the one you sit on if you break your tailbone) from Nolan (however, Nate Wright was the one who came up with the idea for him). Dr. Arch Enemy is not a very big character in the series, and is not shown in the Big Nate comics. He always messes up Dr. Cesspool's plans. He has only been seen a couple times.
