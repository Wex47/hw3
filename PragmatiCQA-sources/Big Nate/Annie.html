Annie is a female student who attends Jefferson Middle School . She got a higher mark in a test than Gina Hemphill-Toms and they became enemies as a result. Later on, however, they became friends.

Annie has appeared to be one of the smartest kids in Jefferson Middle School .

Since Annie got a higher score than Gina, who has the highest G.P.A at P.S. 38, it is likely that Annie could be smarter than every single other student at P.S. 38. However, Gina only got a point taken off because she writes her 7's like 1's, which means their intelligence might be on equal footing.

Physical Appearance

Annie has black hair with a ponytail and has glasses as well as freckles. She's relatively slender and wears a black shirt with earings.
