Big Nate's Greatest Hits is a comic strip collection in the Big Nate series. The book featured Nate going out with Angie, though their break-up was not part of the book. It contained parts of Big Nate: Pray For A Fire Drill, Big Nate: Makes A Splash, and Big Nate: Dibs On This Chair.

Trivia

  • Big Nate's Greatest Hits is a compilation of Big Nate: Dibs On This Chair , Big Nate: Makes a Splash , and Big Nate: Pray for a Fire Drill .
  • It contains all of the strips from 1997.
