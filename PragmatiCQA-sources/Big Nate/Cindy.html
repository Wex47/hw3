Cindy is a female student at P.S. 38 . She has a crush on Teddy Ortiz , though he does not feel the same way. At one point, she began starting the uncomfortable talk with Teddy, asking him to walk her home. Finally, Nate Wright showed up and opened his locker, pummeling her away with the mess. She is never seen again after that. After that, Teddy said to Nate "My hero!".
