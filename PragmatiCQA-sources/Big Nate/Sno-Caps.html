Sno-Caps are sweet chocolate candies made by Nestle that Nate Wright and Teddy Ortiz likes to eat as candy.

Trivia

  • Nate always gets them stuck in his teeth.
  • Ken Rosa stores some in his desk to give to students and once used them to calm down Nate when he was ranting on about Artur.
  • Chad Applewhite likes sno-caps sprinkled on his popcorn.
