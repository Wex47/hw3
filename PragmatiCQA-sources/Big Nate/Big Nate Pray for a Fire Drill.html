Big Nate: Pray For A Fire Drill is a collection of Big Nate comics that was published on September 1, 2003. It is the 3rd comic collection book in the series , preceded by Big Nate: Dibs On This Chair and is followed by Big Nate: Makes a Splash . The comics in the book have also been seen in Big Nate's Greatest Hits . It features a third of the comic strips from the year 1997. The main series is the one where Nate attempts to get out of the test in Social Studies he never studied for. He tried to request one to his principal , but it didn't work, and Nate ended up paying the price for not studying but the bell never rings.
