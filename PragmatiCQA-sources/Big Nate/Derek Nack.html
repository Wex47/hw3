Derek Nack was seen in multiple strips but never appeared in any of the books. He goes to Bailey Junior High . He met Nate through a chess summer camp. He always looked up to Nate Wright and was considered as Nate's "chess groupie." He even once asked Nate for his autograph, even after practicing for 2 years and claiming that he was "Nate's equal" (even though he lost against Nate in 2 strips). Derek, like all others at the summer chess camp, was addicted to chess, and somehow turned every activity chess-related, much to Nate's frustration.

Description

Physical appearance

Derek is shorter than Nate and wears a geeky "I Love Chess" t-shirt. He is quite chubby. He looks similar to Liam , Chad , and Chad Applewhite .

Appearances

Derek was seen in multiple comic strips and was seen in the Big Nate book "All Work And no Play".  He made an occasional appearance and appeared in almost all comics from July 8, 1999 - July 28, 1999. He also appeared in January 2, 2001 - January 6, 2001.
