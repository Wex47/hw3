Nate Wright 's lucky socks were a regular pair of socks that he claims to be lucky. He refuses to wash them, or else they will, according to Nate, "lose their luck." He always wears them while playing sports and for tests for good luck. They are very dirty and smelly to everyone, but Nate claims that never washing lucky socks are what keeps them lucky. He even set up a little booth where he put a sign that says "Rub my lucky socks... 50 cents". Nate questioned why his business was so slow. When buying new socks the store clerk thought that they were gray.
