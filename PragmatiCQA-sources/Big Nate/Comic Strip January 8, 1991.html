Plot:

In this comic strip, Nate drew a comic, called Ultra-Nate: Super Sixth- Grader! , in which he makes a touchdown in a game of football , winning the game for his team. In it, he was then greeted with cheers from his adoring fans, a positive remark from his coach, and a group of girls running toward him. Nate also asked his sister Ellen if the comic is realistic to Nate's life, to which she sarcastically responded that it was. This is also, a reference of the rare Big Nate book, Big Nate: Add More Babes .

Behind the Scenes:

This comic strip contains the first appearance of Ellen Wright , as well as the first appearance of a comic created by Nate Wright, Ultra-Nate: Super Sixth Grader! .
