Moe Mentum: Hollywood Stuntman! is a comic created by Nate Wright . The main character, Moe Mentum, is a stuntman, and his mean director, J.B. , bosses him around a lot. He has a mojo that makes him do all this dangerous stuff. He is one of the most drawn comic strips by Nate Wright in the history of Big Nate .

Moe Mentum was pushed down a waterfall by his boss and punctured a lung, had 6 heart attacks, and broke 17 bones. His boss then said, "Good job, but in the next take, try not to scream so loud."
