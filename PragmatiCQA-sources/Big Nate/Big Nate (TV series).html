Big Nate is an upcoming American CGI-animated television series made by Nickelodeon, based on the comic strip and the Big Nate book series . It is produced by Nickelodeon Animation Studio. There will be 26 episodes. It has been delayed due to COVID-19 and it will premiere on Paramount+ sometime in early 2022, after originally scheduled to premiere on Spetember 2021. According to Peirce, the series will revolve around the books, focusing on a 11 year old boy named Nate and his wacky adventures. The executive producers will be John Cohen and Mitch Watson .

Development

In 1991, when the first Big Nate Comic was published, Lee Mendelson was paid $5,000 to write a "bible" describing the characters and outlining a few story ideas to make a 2D-animated Big Nate television series for Saturday Morning Cartoons at NBC. This idea was shelved for a long time, although being in consideration to be made several times. Finally, in February 19, 2021, an animated TV series based on the comics and books was announced, after a long time in "development limbo", an aexpression used in the media industry of a project or idea that has been in development and consdieration for a very long time, in this case being 29 years. In a news release, Nickelodeon's executive vice president of animation production and development, Ramsey Naito, stated that she had wanted to adapt the Big Nate book series into an animated series for a long time.

The animation model was leaked on October 15, 2020.

On March 18th, 2021 during the Nickelodeon Virtual Upfront Show, the original release month was announced and the bio was also announced

In December 2021, the casting was finally confirmed, as well as the new release date: early 2022.
