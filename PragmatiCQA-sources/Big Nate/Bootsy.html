Biography:

Once, at a park, Mrs. Godfrey and Bootsy bumped into Nate, who was selling drawings of people's pets. Mrs. Godfrey wanted a picture of Bootsy. Nate drew one, and Mrs. Godfrey liked it. Nate explained the way he made his drawing look so much like Bootsy. It turned out Nate drew Mrs. Godfrey, and added facial hair and a collar because he thinks people look like their pets. Bootsy was furious and she chased Nate up a tree.
