Vanessa "The Brain" De Vries is a chess prodigy much like Nate Wright .

Appearance

July 19, 1999 was her first appearance, at summer chess camp, where she beat Nate in a chess match, mainly because Nate was distracted because he was in love with her.

On January 8, 2001, Nate and Vanessa competed against each other on the championship round of a chess tournament. According to Francis Pope , it was "the biggest chess tournament they ever played in."

Nate wasn't primarily concerned about their match and Vanessa's mastery in the game because he was more interested in going on a date with her instead. In fact, he bet her that if he won, they would go on a date. Nate managed to win the match.

However, despite him winning the bet in conjunction to winning the chess match, Vanessa told him that betting wasn't allowed in chess tournament matches. As a result, Nate took home the championship trophy in a depressed mood.
