Sherman is the class pet in Ms. Godfrey's room. He is a gerbil. He was brought to class by Clara Godfrey on , and the class voted his name to be Sherman, being named after . Sherman is one of the few animals in the series that shows up more than twice.

Trivia

  • Nate Wright wanted him to be called "Shaq" (as in Shaquille O' Neal) at first but he got outvoted.
  • He expresses his feelings, or "talks" through thought bubbles, referencing characters Garfield and Snoopy. He is also the only animal in the series to communicate, except Spitsy a little bit, but no one understands him because he doesn't actually speak. Nate understands him the way Jon understands Garfield .
  • Once, Gina Hemphill-Toms brought in a troll doll so Sherman would have a buddy. Sherman found it very humiliating, and Nate later removed it, as he was on gerbil duty. Later, she brought a rubber duck .
  • He has a cousin, Howard who is a lab rat.
  • Nate once forgot to complete his homework, so he tried to get Sherman to shred it. Sherman shredded it, but Nate was caught.
  • Sherman plays Go-Fish with Nate Wright during free periods, as seen on the Big Nate comic strip for September 19, 2016.
  • When Mr. Rosa would not allow Nate to paint a picture of a dog for art, Nate Wright painted a picture of Sherman instead.
  • Sherman once ran away from his cage after Nate handled him on May 7, 2019 .
  • Nate once put a Cheez Doodle in Sherman's cage. Sherman "said" that it was the first day of the rest of his life once he ate it.
  • Spitsy , Pickles , Poco and, Sherman are the only animals to show up more than once in Big Nate.
  • He has a habit of making bitter jokes concerning his living conditions (See comic above).
  • He once ate Nate’s homework.
  • He once escaped because of Nate, but was scared back to Nate by a mouse trap.
  • Mrs.Godfrey babysat him in the summer but Sherman doesn't like it.
