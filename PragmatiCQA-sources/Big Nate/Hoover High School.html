Hoover High School was the high school Martin Wright attended. The school also had a football team, which he was on but wasn't very good.

Trivia

  • Martin was on the school's football team.
  • It is possibly named after "Herbert Hoover," the 31st U.S president.

Behind the Scenes

  • Ellen Wright also attends Hoover High, as shown in a 1995 comic strip.
