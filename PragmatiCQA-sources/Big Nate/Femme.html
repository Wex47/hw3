Femme is the main protagonist of Femme Fatality , a comic made my Brad Linsky . She is immortal, as Nate Said to Mr. Eustis and she is also a vampire. One of her enemies is Doctor Jackal , another character. Both Nate Wright and his father have a crush on her. She also has a life-sized model, which Nate Wright found out in The Complete Big Nate 12 which he fell in love immediately. Nate is in love with her. Nate says she cuts people’s heads off if they are a wussy, but he tells his dad that when he wants the book. There is a cardboard cutout of her that you could win for free.

Attributes

  • She is 6 ft 2 in (188 cm), buxom, immortal, vindictive and is a vampire. Apparently, in one comic, she spends the entire time 'dressing appropriately''.
