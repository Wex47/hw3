Ms. Clarke is the sixth grade English teacher at P.S. 38 . Nate Wright seems to find her class somewhat boring besides writing poems about Cheez Doodles , much to the annoyance of others. Ms. Clarke once dated a man online, but she was enraged to know he was much shorter than her, standing five feet. She later expressed this to the staff, something Nate overheard and wrote about.

Her first appearance was on September 21, 1992 , she was a new teacher at the time.

Biography

Clarke was born and raised in the South. She moved, and she started teaching English at P.S. 38 , replacing Mr. Figueroa .

Personality and Traits

Although she is not a major character in the books like Nate, she is an average teacher like all teachers from P.S. 38 with a soft temper. She is not too strict, but she definitely can be. She is not married yet and it is unknown if she has a boyfriend/spouse.

Appearance

In the early days, Ms. Clarke's head was round, but as time went on, it became a square. She has a dark skin tone and is usually seen wearing a vest, along with earrings.
