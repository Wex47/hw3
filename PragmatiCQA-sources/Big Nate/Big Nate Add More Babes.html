Big Nate: Add More Babes is the official first "Big Nate" book ever released. It is a comic strip collection book . It was released on January 7, 1992. It contains some of the oldest Big Nate strips from Big Nate. Therefore, it is a collector's item. Many characters look way different, and the next book came out 5 years later, so they have different looks. It is followed by Big Nate: Dibs On This Chair .

Characters

  • Nate
  • Martin Wright
  • Francis Pope
  • Mrs. Godfrey
  • Ellen Wright
  • Ken Rosa
  • Kenny Smithson
  • Jenny Jenkins (Appeared only once.)

Events

  • Francis falls in love with Ellen, and ruins Nate's sleepover by doing so.
  • It shows new comics that Nate drew, including Kit and Kaboodle (about two superheroes), Everlovin' Ellen (a soap opera), and The Adventures of Nate, Super Sixth Grader.
  • Nate makes a comic about himself called Nate is Great.
  • Nate gets detention because Francis doesn't let him copy his homework.
  • Nate breaks his wrist.

Trivia

  • The title is a reference to Comic Strip: January 8, 1991 .
