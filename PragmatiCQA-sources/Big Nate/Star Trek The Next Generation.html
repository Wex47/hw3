Star Trek: The Next Generation (abbreviated as TNG or ST:TNG ) is an American science fiction television series in the Star Trek franchise. It is Mrs. Godfrey 's favorite show ever.

Trivia

  • Much to Nate Wright 's horror, the show is both him and his least favorite teacher's favorite show. What is more is that they also have the same favorite character , which apparently ruined the experience for Nate. This was because of Nate's hate for Mrs. Godfrey.
