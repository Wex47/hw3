Plot

Jefferson Middle School has always (except for a debate and a soccer game) beat P.S. 38 at almost everything. Nate decides it is time for P.S. 38 to surpass them for once, but this will not be easy when a sprinkler malfunction has messed up P.S. 38 and caused the students to have to stay at Jefferson until the problem is resolved. This results in many conflicts between the two schools.

Errors

  • On Page 1, Nate and Chad appear too tall, appearing to be the same size as the lockers.
  • On Page 118, Dee Dee does not have long earrings unlike in most of her appearances.
  • On page 208, Nate has his cast off. However, he should have it on, since starting on page 138 and forward, Nate's cast is on.

Trivia

  • During the P.S. 38 Beach Party Dance , Artur and Jenny are seen dancing together.
  • Nate's Comix Crack-up! makes a cameo in Mr. Rosa's house.

Behind the Scenes

  • P.S. 38's mold issues in the novel may have been based off the comic strip arc starting on September 1, 2008 .
  • This sort of issue has happened before when P.S 38 had to close because of toxic mold. At that time, Big Nate and his friends also had to go to Jefferson where they beat them in the big soccer game.
