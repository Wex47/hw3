Mickey Charles Mantle (October 20, 1931 – August 13, 1995), nicknamed The Commerce Comet and The Mick , was an American professional baseball player. Martin Wright claimed to have owned a Mickey Mantle baseball card, an extremely rare card worth thousands of dollars. Nate Wright has an autographed Mickey Mantle card in his locker.

Biography

Mantle played his entire Major League Baseball (MLB) career with the New York Yankees as a center fielder and first baseman, from 1951 through 1968. Mantle was one of the best players and sluggers, and is regarded by many as the greatest switch hitter in baseball history. Mantle was inducted into the National Baseball Hall of Fame in 1974 and was elected to the Major League Baseball All-Century Team in 1999.
