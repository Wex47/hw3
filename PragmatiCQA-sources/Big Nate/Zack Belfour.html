Zack Belfour is a Jefferson student who is the star striker of the Jefferson soccer team. He is very tall with black hair. He may have been mistaken as Randy Betancourt .

MOLD! Story Arc

When the P.S. 38 were relocated to Jefferson, Zack taunted Nate Wright and his friends incessantly until the big soccer game against Jefferson . The game went on without a goal being scored. After the 70-minute match was run, a 10-minute overtime happened but it still did not change anything. Then, during the series of penalty kicks, Mark scored on the Bobcats' last kick, and Nate Wright was left to defend against Zack Belfour's kick. Nate Wright was able to save Zack's goal, and the Jefferson team - especially Zack - was dejected. A few weeks later, when Zack told Nate that he heard that P.S. 38 was about to leave, Nate left Zack with a swelled face.

Personality

Zack may have a posse like Marcus Goode , and he seems to be a showoff and loves to make fun of people. He thinks he's high and mighty while other people aren't as good as him. Once he beats someone at something he automatically thinks he's a lot better then them. This might've changed after he lost to P.S. 38 in a soccer game. But no one is sure.
