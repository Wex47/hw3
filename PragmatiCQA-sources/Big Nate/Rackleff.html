Rackleff , Maine is where Nate Wright and almost all the characters in Big Nate live in. There are street signs seen that say Rackleff. When Enslave the Mollusk tried to get into the Battle of the Bands, there is a banner that says Rackleff. The library , community center, and the town pool have names that start with the word Rackleff. It is a made up town in Maine and does not actually exist in the real world. But there is a place called Rackleff Street in Maine. This is most likely where the author of the comic strip lives.

Rackleff Street

Rackleff Street is located in Portland, Maine. Portland is where Lincoln Peirce lives. That is most likely why the characters in Big Nate live there. It even strikingly resembles the setting of the strip.
