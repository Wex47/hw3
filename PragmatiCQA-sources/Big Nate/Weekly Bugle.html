The Weekly Bugle is P.S. 38 's school newspaper that has gone on for over 38 years. All the students think it stinks and is really boring, especially considering that the weekly bugle has no sense of humor and comes out only once a month. Their faculty advisor is Ms. Clarke . Dr. Cesspool used to be in the bugle, but it was removed after kids complained that it was too violent. The Weekly Bugle also has errors, such as spelling, grammar and wrong pictures.
