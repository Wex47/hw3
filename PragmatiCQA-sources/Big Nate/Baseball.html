Baseball is a bat-and-ball game which is played against two teams. The sport is commonly played by Nate Wright and his friends. He plays as a right fielder for his Little League team; the Little Lulus . Teddy Ortiz is shown playing center field or left field, and Francis Pope is shown playing either left field or as a catcher. Chad Applewhite plays as a relief pitcher. Nate's favorite baseball team is the Red Sox while Teddy's is the Yankees making a rivalry between them.

Trivia

  • Nate's baseball team is currently sponsored by Klassic Komix , and as a result, the team is called the Little Lulus, even though Nate thought that with Klassic Komix as their sponsor, that they would finally get a good name.
  • Nate's baseball team was allegedly once sponsored by McSween's Money Management owned by Mr. McSween . However, there is no proof that this company has ever sponsored Nate's baseball team.
