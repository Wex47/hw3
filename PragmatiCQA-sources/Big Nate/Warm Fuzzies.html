Warm Fuzzies are wall hangings with little sayings seen in Big Nate On a Roll. A competition involved Timber Scouts to sell them to earn extra money to upgrade their camping gear. Prizes would be awarded for those who sold the most.

Prizes

  • Third place: Rockin' Robot kit with over 100 songs.
  • Second place: Telescope with a celestial chart.
  • First place: Customizable skateboard.

Plot

At first, Nate was not enthusiastic about selling warm fuzzies, but when he sees the grand prize (a skateboard), he gets motivated and tries to sell warm fuzzies. Eventually, he couldn't find anyone who was interested (except his sister until he expected her to buy more) and tries selling with comics and doing other people's labor (for money to buy his own warm fuzzies).
