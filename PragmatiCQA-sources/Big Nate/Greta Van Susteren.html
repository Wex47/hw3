Greta Conway Van Susteren (born June 11, 1954) is an American commentator and former television news anchor for CNN, Fox News, and NBC News.

Trivia

  • Mr. Galvin , the sixth grade science teacher at P.S. 38 , has a crush on her, which makes him easily distracted when she is mentioned.
  • Nate Wright once gave him a swimsuit photo of her to bribe Mr. Galvin to not give him a detention. Nate thought this was very clever.
