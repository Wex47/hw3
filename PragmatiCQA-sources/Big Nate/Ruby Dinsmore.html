Ruby Dinsmore is a female student at P.S. 38 who has a crush on Nate . Randy Betancourt had a crush on her but she does not feel the same about him. She is Nate's current girlfriend in the novels but hasn't been shown for five years.

Biography

Upon moving to P.S. 38 , Ruby Dinsmore was constantly chased down by two students, Nate Wright and Randy Betancourt , who both had a crush on her. When Martin Wright gave Nate a gross school lunch, she gave Nate a can of root beer when Artur Pashkov told her Nate was hungry, but Randy secretly shook it to make the can explode in front of Nate (something only witnessed by Drama Club President Dee Dee Holloway ) Dee Dee tried to stop it from happening, but the lunch aide Mrs. Colleti stopped her because she needed to throw out her lunch. . Previously, Martin Wright told Nate he needed to move to California, which resulted in Nate saying he didn't like Ruby anymore. Later, he told Nate that they were not moving to California, which made an excited Ruby kiss Nate.

Physical Appearance

Ruby has long brown hair and styled into a low ponytail. Ruby is dressed in blue and wearing white earrings.

Trivia

  • She appeared in Big Nate: Blasts Off , which was her first and only appearance. She only appears in the novels. She has not appeared in the comic strips.

Relationships

Nate Wright

Ruby Dinsmore is a new student, and she has a crush on Nate. Randy Betancourt has a crush on her, but she doesn't feel the same about him and is more drawn toward Nate. It is possible that she is one of his three girlfriends (alongside Kelly , Angie , and formerly, Gertrude ).

Randy Betancourt

Randy Betancourt had a crush on Ruby Dinsmore, though Ruby did not feel the same way about him. In the end, Dinsmore chose Nate Wright and he waved her off.
