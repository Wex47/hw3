Summary

On May 27, 2000, Big Nate and countless other comic strips like Nancy and Foxtrot, did a tribute to Peanuts due to the death of the iconic strip's author.

Plot

After a baseball game played by Nate Wright , Francis Pope , Teddy Ortiz , and their baseball team, they give the after-game chant to the Peanuts , where Charlie Brown and Nate shake hands saying "nice game." Nate says after that "good ol' Charlie Brown!"

Trivia

  • It's the only Big Nate comic strip where Charlie Brown has made an appearance.
  • The comic was a tribute to the death of the author of the comic strip "Peanuts."
