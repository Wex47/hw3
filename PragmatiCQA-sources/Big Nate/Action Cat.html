Action Cat is a comic made by Nate Wright where a cat constantly gets hurt by getting hit by a truck, car, or some other vehicle. He made this comic due to his ailurophobia and hatred of cats. Most people, after seeing this comic, tell Nate how sick he is. On Christmas, Nate created a Christmas edition and Action Cat was hit by Santa's sleigh. Nate showed it to his sister Ellen Wright and she asked what the red blob was, and she asked if it was Santa, but Nate said no. Ellen now realized what she was looking at, and that resulted her in being grossed out of the comic that Nate made.

Trivia

  • This was the shortest living comic that Nate Wright made.
