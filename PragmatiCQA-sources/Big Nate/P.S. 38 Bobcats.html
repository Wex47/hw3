The P.S. 38 Bobcats represents P.S 38 in sports. Their main rivals are the Jefferson Cavaliers. They play home games at the P.S. 38 soccer field and gym. Their home jerseys are either white or blue, and their alternate jersey are red.

Official P.S. 38 sports teams

Boy's Basketball

The boy's basketball team has been known to be a horrible team. They lose pretty frequently. However, they have Nate Wright , who is a big trash talker that usually burns opponents, and center Chester, who is 6'6" and can dunk. Nate was a benchwarmer in the early comics.

Known roster

  • Nate Wright (Point Guard)
  • Teddy Ortiz (SG)
  • Francis Pope (SF)
  • Chester Budrick (C)

Boy's soccer

The boy's soccer team has been known to be satisfactory. They do sometimes lose and they sometimes win. However, they once won a game at Jefferson in a penalty shootout, when Nate blocked Jefferson star striker Zack Belfour's shot. Jefferson has been undefeated for four years before that.

Known roster

  • Nate Wright (Goalkeeper)
  • Francis Pope
  • Teddy Ortiz
  • Chad Applewhite
  • Artur Pashkov (formerly)

Unofficial P.S. 38 sports teams

Ultimate Frisbee / Mud Bowl

The P.S. 38 Ultimate Frisbee/Mud Bowl team is also called the P.S. 38 Bobcats, and the team represents P.S. 38 in the Mud bowl. They have won the Mud bowl twice, and they are quite good at playing Ultimate Frisbee. They only ever play against the Jefferson Cavaliers.

Roster

  • Nate Wright
  • Randy Betancourt
  • Ruby Dinsmore
  • Francis Pope
  • Teddy Ortiz
  • Dee Dee Holloway
  • Chad Applewhite
