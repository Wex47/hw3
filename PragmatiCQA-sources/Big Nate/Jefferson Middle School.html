Jefferson Middle School is the sister institution and the biggest rival of P.S. 38 , Nate Wright 's school. Most of their students are very boastful and annoying, and they beat P.S. 38 at almost all competitions. Their mascot is the Cavalier. One reason the rivalry between the school is so big is because the school is so close to P.S. 38 , as they are practically in the very same neighborhood. Jefferson is known for bullying, and or picking on P.S. 38 students.

Description

Interior

The school's interior is very fancy, with murals and a skylight in the lobby, as well as a statue of a cavalier (the school's mascot), on a large pedestal in the center. Glass cases with trophies and awards line the walls. There is also a "rec room" for students to use during free periods, with a ping pong table and a vending machine. In the cafeteria, which the school calls a "food court", there is a soft-serve ice cream machine. In the auditorium, the seats recline and all the water fountains have motion-sensors.

Exterior

The school has been mentioned to have both a soccer field and a hockey field. There is also a parking lot designated for school faculty.

School Colors

The colors of Jefferson Middle School are purple and gold, with their school jackets being overall purple with golden sleeves, buttons, and a golden "J".

Trivia

  • Although Nate, Francis, and Teddy hate Jefferson students, they love the food served at Jefferson Middle School, which is way better than the P.S. 38 cafeteria food. They also have a greater selection of food.
  • The mascot of Jefferson is the Cavalier.
  • They have been beaten seven times, the first being before the series began in the first Mud Bowl, the second being in MOLD! (Story arc) in a soccer game, the third being in Big Nate: Goes For Broke in a snow contest, and the fourth being in Big Nate: Blasts Off in the 38th Mud Bowl, and the 5th being in a basketball game between the Jefferson and PS. 38. They have been beaten in another soccer match, according to the story arch from October 25, 2002 to November 02, 2002. It is also mentioned in Big Nate: Goes for Broke, as Francis browses through the school archives, he finds that PS. 38 beat Jefferson in Debate.
  • There is a school in Albuquerque, New Mexico with the same name. The two middle schools look very similar.
