In this story arc Nate plays chess against Pickles. It ran from to .

Arc

Nate and Francis are playing chess, but Francis says that he has to go to a guitar lesson and when Nate asks him to keep playing, he says that his protege can play for him and calls Pickles . Nate says that he will not play with Pickles and calls her a flea-bitten cat. She hisses at him and Nate says that it could be a pleasant diversion, while very scared. Nate tells Teddy that if someone finds him playing chess with a cat, he'll look like a complete idiot. A girl comes over and tells Nate that he looks adorable while playing with Pickles and Nate tells her that he is rather adorable while Teddy rolls his eyes at Nate .

When she walks away, Pickles puts Nate in check, much to Nate's shock and disbelief. Teddy laughs at Nate and tells him that he might actually lose to Pickles but Nate puts Pickles in checkmate soon after, as we know he is a professional, much to Pickles' disbelief so she wedgies Nate in retaliation.
