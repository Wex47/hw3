Mary Ellen Popowski is a student that attends P.S. 38 . She occasionally makes appearances in the comics as a "snot-nosed" friend of Gina. She plays the flute in the band. She is always trying to be like Gina because she likes that. In a few of the books, she appears as a snarky, annoying, teachers pet. This could be why she is friends with Gina.
