The P.S. 38 Cartooning Club , (unofficially called the Doodlers ), is a cartooning club at P.S. 38, a public school. According to Nate Wright , it is the best club at the whole school. Their faculty advisor is Mr. Rosa , and they meet in the art studio every Wednesday.

They mostly draw comics, although they sometimes draw mean cartoons about the teachers, which are mostly drawn by Nate.

Trivia

  • The Cartooning Club is different from the novels as opposed to the comics, as the novels state that there was no girls at the Cartooning Club until Dee Dee Holloway joined, but in the comics, the club has half girls and half boys.
