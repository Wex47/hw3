Brad Linsky , the creator of Femme Fatality , is a cartoonist. He is mentioned during the arc when Nate researches him with Mr. Rosa so he can be more like him.He has acne and wears glasses. He is also shown when he is signing autographs in Klassic Komix . He seems to be a teenager.

Trivia

  • When drawing, he uses non-photo blue pencils, kneaded erasers, two-ply vellum, India ink, and crow quill pens.
  • Nate Wright and his art teacher, Mr. Rosa , researched him to find out what drawing utensils he uses.
  • He likes to eat egg salad finger sandwiches.
  • His mom is named Lois Linsky .
