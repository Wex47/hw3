The story arc MOLD! is one of the most enjoyed story arcs according to the GoComics website. Based on this arc, Big Nate: Goes For Broke was made. This arc is also seen in a book called Big Nate: Here Goes Nothing . Note: This is the arc where Lincoln Peirce started to add colors to the comics.

Read the arc

The Beginning

This story all started when Teddy Ortiz broke the news, that P.S. 38 had mold! It was a big health hazard.

The news channel had declared that it would take months to get rid of the mold in P.S. 38 , and that it would be closed indefinitely .

It was great news that P.S. 38 had closed down (not for Francis Pope , however). But that good news had turned to bad news when the boys heard the news that they had to go to Jefferson Middle School !

Jefferson

It was sad, indeed. But that had turned to good when Nate DIDN'T HAVE MRS. GODFREY FOR SOCIAL STUDIES! In fact, the WHOLE PLACE was FIRST CLASS! Nate was very happy then.

The place wasn't all great. It fact, the Jefferson students are just as sassy as the place itself. P.S. 38 finally gets the chance for revenge against Jefferson Middle School , by winning the big game in penalty kicks as Nate Wright saved the last penalty kick and was a hero. They won the game!

The Game

In the field, Nate leads a prayer before the game starts.

During the game, things were good, but Nate, however, didn't have good luck.

The game had ended in a tie. However, the teams had to each do a penalty kick. Mark had scored and Jefferson was down to their last penalty kick... it was all up to Nate to save the day.

Zack Belfour called Nate a "nothing", so it made him even more motivated to win.

When Zack kicked the ball, Nate jumped in an attempt to catch it.

Unexpectedly, Nate made a heroic save and saved the day! The bobcats went wild! They had beat Jefferson's 4 year unbeaten streak.
