The Book of Facts is a book containing random trivia on all subjects. Francis Pope owns a copy of the book, which he usually reads it in his free time. His friends get annoyed when he reads the facts out loud, as they have no interest in it. They even attempt to have a "book of facts intervention" to take it away. Once Nate could smell Francis coming with a new edition of the book of facts. Francis is apparently "addicted" to thus Book of Facts and he begged to be able to read even one factoid after the "Book of Facts Intervention".
