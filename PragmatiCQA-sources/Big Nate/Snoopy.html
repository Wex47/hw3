Snoopy is the main deuteragonist of Charles' Schulz's Peanuts . He is a Beagle who likes role-playing imaginary games in his head. He lives in a red doghouse. Snoopy originates in a different series.

Trivia

  • He is Charlie Brown's dog.
  • He was only mentioned once in the Cats vs. Dogs debate.
  • He is best friends with a bird named "Woodstock".
