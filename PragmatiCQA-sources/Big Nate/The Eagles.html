The Eagles are a baseball team that Nate Wright got traded to by Joe's Chicken. They are a very good team but Nate wants to stay on Joe's Chicken. The Coach is really strict and the team is not as fun as Joe's Chicken for Nate. The Coach gets mad because Nate talks to Teddy Ortiz and Francis Pope . Nate tries to explain that they're his friends but the coach doesn't care. Eventually, Nate requests to be traded back to Joe's Chicken.
