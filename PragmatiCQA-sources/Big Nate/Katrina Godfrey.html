Katrina Michelle Godfrey is Clara Godfrey 's first daughter. Katrina's younger sister and only sibling is Meghan Godfrey . You never see her in the comics.

Trivia

  • She was born at 10:54 PM on December 5, 1996, and weighed nine pounds, thirteen ounces at birth, according to Nate Wright .
  • In 2021, Katrina is now 24, turning 25 this year.
  • Nate once mistakenly referred to her as "Latrina" instead of Katrina.
  • Nate was very thrilled when she was gone, having lots of fun and fooling around in her absence.
  • Nate also called her “fat” which got Mrs. Godfrey’s “heritage” (Implying that she was fat too, which she wasn't happy about).
  • Unlike her younger sister, she does not have any physical appearance in the comics and is merely mentioned multiple times.
