Poor Nate's Almanac is a column in the Weekly Bugle . It was based off Poor Richard's Almanac, written by Benjamin Franklin . It made an appearance in the novel Big Nate Strikes Again , and it also appeared once in a Sunday comic.

Appearances

  • Big Nate: Strikes Again

Trivia

  • The only customers of this product were Francis Pope and Teddy Ortiz , Nate’s friends. However, when the two realized how much effort Nate actually put into the books, they were outraged because they had been ripped off.
  • Nate likes Ben Franklin so much, he decided to make an almanac just like him.
  • Wesley Nichols made Nate stop selling his almanac.
