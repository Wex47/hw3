Big Nate: Here Goes Nothing is a comic-strip collection that mostly features P.S. 38 's relocation to Jefferson Middle School . It is the 2nd book in the COMIX! series , and is preceded by Big Nate: What Could Possibly Go Wrong? and is followed by Big Nate: Genius Mode .

Featured Plot

The featured plot was where P.S. 38 beat Jefferson in a soccer game after their four-year win-streak, as seen in the story arc known as MOLD!
