Jenny Jenkins is a female student at P.S. 38 and the current girlfriend of Artur Pashkov .

Biography

Before the novels begin, Jenny and Artur start going out.

Jenny took part in the school dance and, the following week, the Ultimate Snowdown , which was intended to defeat Jefferson Middle School in something after seven years.

Trivia
