Nate's Locker is a locker owned by Nate Wright on the second floor. Nate mentioned that it was number 217 in one comic. Nate's father claims that Nate cannot remember the combo of the locker, which is probably untrue since he includes them in his comics as he has every means of access to it. The locker is known to be very messy, although Nate claims that he has a filing system and has it organized, such as having areas for certain classes such as English and science. When it is open, the contents of the locker spill out. According to Gina, people get knocked over. The first strip had the sound "SSHWSSH!" , which evolved. The sound evolved into "SHOOSH!" , "SSSHK!" , "KSSSSH!" , "KSSSCH!" , and finally "FOOOOM!!" It is a anti-bully area because of this.

Abilities

Nate's locker also has a lot of abilities.

Ability Description First seen The ability in action
Antitheft system Nate installed an antitheft system to handcuff anyone except for Nate who tries to search the locker. Comic Strip: October 03, 2004
Shower There appears to be a shower in Nate's locker. Comic Strip: October 10, 2004
Airbag Nate declared his locker as "the only locker in his school equipped with an airbag". Comic Strip: October 10, 1994
Vacuum Nate installed a vacuum inside his locker to clean up the mess after opening it. It can be activated by pressing a button beside it. However, this vacuum is too powerful and it can trap his friends inside. Comic Strip: May 22, 2005
