Ultimate , originally known as the game Ultimate Frisbee , is a non-contact team sport played by players with a frisbee (or known as flying disk). The sport is played among students at P.S. 38 , as well as against Jefferson Middle School in the Mud Bowl . An example of a time when they got to play Ultimate was when the school's detention monitor, Mrs. Czerwicki , had her wig catch on fire, so the school's fire alarm went off. The students evacuated the building, and got to play Ultimate on the school lawn for an hour until normal school resumed. After that everyone is happy expect for Mrs. Czerwicki.
