Bethany Treasury is a comic that Nate Wright hates. Ellen Wright , Nate's older sister, reads the comic and it is her favorite comic strip.

The comic is about the love life of Bethany, a high school sophomore. Francis Pope once caught Nate reading Bethany Treasury in a bookstore and he started making fun of him. Nate then said it was his "guilty pleasure."
