Joe's Falafel is yet another of the "Joe" companies that sponsored Nate Wright 's a baseball team, most of the time this gets them ridiculed by rival teams.

Trivia

  • Francis Pope one time asked if the name was "falafel" or "falafels."
  • Joe's Falafel sells falafels, which is a deep-fried ball, or a flat or doughnut-shaped patty, made from ground chickpeas, fava beans, or both.
