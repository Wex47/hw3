Cinnamon is an 8-year old cat who was in a shelter. At the age of four months, she attacked Nate Wright , who was four years old. This gave Nate his ailurophobia , a fear of cats. When his friends Francis Pope and Teddy Ortiz later wanted to find out more about Nate's ailurophobia, they hypnotized him. Francis also questioned Nate about his phobia, and Cinnamon's name was mentioned.
