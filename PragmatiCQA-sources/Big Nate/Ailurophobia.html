Ailurophobia is a phobia. People that have ailurophobia are afraid of felines. Nate Wright has ailurophobia. This is revealed in the novel, Big Nate: Flips Out , and it is mentioned that he has it many times throughout the novels and strip. Nate was not born with this condition however, he got it when he went to a pet store when he was 4, when a kitten named Cinnamon jumped on top of him and began clawing and scratching him. Nate is known for being terrified of cats, which is why he loves dogs. He hates his fear, often referring to it as a "drag."

Mentions

Nate's ailurophobia is mentioned many times throughout the novels and the strip such as during the Trivia Slam .
