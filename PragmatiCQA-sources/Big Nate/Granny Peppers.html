"Granny Peppers" is a deceased famous folk artist. Her paintings, which mostly consist of oil paintings on canvas, are now sold for millions of dollars.

Biography

Granny Peppers was a folk artist who had no art training. She was known for painting small oil paintings on canvas, but she also made one mural, which ended up being on one of the walls in the storage room next to P.S. 38's Gym. After she died, she gradually increased in popularity. One of her paintings was sold for two million dollars, and her house became part of the National Register of Historic Places. Her mural was discovered by a Nate , Breckenridge Puffington III and their team in the P.S. 38 Centennial Scavenger Hunt , and was sold to the Museum of American Folk Art . She is mentioned many times in Big Nate Lives It Up . She has a unique art style because she had no proper art education.
