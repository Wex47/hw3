The Harry Potter Club was a club at P.S. 38 , based on the Harry Potter book and film series. The club comprises of Nate Wright , Francis Pope , Teddy Ortiz , and Sheila Stapleton .

History

The club was founded by Francis, Teddy, and Sheila. Francis played the role as Harry Potter , Teddy was Ron Weasley , and Sheila was Hermione Granger . When Nate learned about the club, he joined, and wanted to be Harry Potter, but Francis said he already called dibs on him. Francis offered him the role of Neville Longbottom instead.
