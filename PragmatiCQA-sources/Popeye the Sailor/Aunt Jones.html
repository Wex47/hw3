Aunt Jones is a supporting character in Popeye comics. She is the sister of the sailor hero's mother , and first appeared in the comic strip Thimble Theatre in 1941. At this point, it was being written by Tom Sims and drawn by Bela Zaboly , and the character could only be seen in comics produced by the aforementioned team, until she was included in the online comic strip Popeye's Cartoon Club in 2020.
