" Coffee House " is a 1960 episode of Popeye the Sailor .

Plot

Olive Oyl is getting dressed. Popeye comes in and is shocked to see Olive in beatnik attire, speaking in an odd way. Brutus comes in and annoys Popeye with jhis beatnik talk. Popeye is very mad about Brutus and Olive's shared new fashion, and vows to break them up. At a coffee house, Brutus recites Olive a poem. Popeye then tries to have Olive go back to the way she was, but Brutus pushes him away. The sailor attempts to play music to impress Olive, yet nothing works. Finally, Popeye eats some "cultured spinach " and beats up Brutus in a sophisticated manner that impresses Olive.
