This article is about television episodes. For the theatrical short, see Adventures of Popeye .

The Adventures of Popeye , or simply Popeye , are the "classic" Popeye comedy and adventure cartoon shorts from The All-New Popeye Hour , as opposed to its variations such a Stone Age setting or an Army comedy .
