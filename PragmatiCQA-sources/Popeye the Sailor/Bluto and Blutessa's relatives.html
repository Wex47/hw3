Bluto and Blutessa's relatives make an appearance in " Olive's Shining Hour ", an episode in the segment Popeye/The Adventures of Popeye from Hanna-Barbera 's TV series The All-New Popeye Hour . They cheer on Blutessa and Bluto as they score points in their tennis match against Olive and Popeye .

Trivia

  • Both the fact that Bluto and Blutessa are a couple, and that their relatives all look identical albeit with minor palette changes, would suggest that much inbreeding takes place in the family.
