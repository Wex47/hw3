Kickin' the Conga Round is the 102nd Popeye cartoon, produced by Fleischer Studios , which was released on January 16, 1942.

Plot

While on shore leave in South America, Bluto muscles in on Popeye's girl, Olivia Oyla . Popeye muscles him out, but when they get to the conga club, he does not care to dance, so Bluto wins again. A fortuitous can of spinach remedies that, until the shore patrol gets their own little conga line going.
