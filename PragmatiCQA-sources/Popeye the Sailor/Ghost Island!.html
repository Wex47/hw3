Synopsis

Popeye is hired by fellow sailor Bill to deliver 500 ghost traps to his client, Mr. Shiver, on Ghost Island. Popeye refuses at first because he does not want to have anything to do with ghosts . He later changes his mind and sets sail for Ghost Island when the ghosts kidnap Swee'Pea.

The ghosts stow away on Popeye's ship to haunt it and prevent him from getting to their island. When Popeye, Bill and Olive Oyl finally reach Ghost Island, they meet Mr. Shiver and the inventor of the ghost traps, Mr. Jones.

Mr. Jones looks very much like Wimpy, and Popeye later learns it is really Wimpy. He had hired some men and Swee'Pea to haunt the island so he could sell his phony traps.
