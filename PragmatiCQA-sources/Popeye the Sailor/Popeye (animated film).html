This article is about the film. For the character, see Popeye . For other meanings, see Popeye (disambiguation) .

Popeye is a computer-animated feature film starring Popeye , originally from Sony Pictures Animation. Initially scheduled to open in 2012, the film's release was pushed back several times until it was put on hold. Eventually, development on the film was restarted by King Features .
