Corinne Orr (born in Montreal, Quebec in 1936) is a Canadian-born stage, radio, film and television actress, voice artist and narrator living in the United States. In 1972 she provided the voice for both Swee'Pea and Olive Oyl in Popeye Meets the Man Who Hated Laughter , and she also did a majority of the voices in the film, including twelve characters that are owned by King Features and appear in various comic strips and animations.
