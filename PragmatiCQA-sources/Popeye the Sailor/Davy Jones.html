Davy Jones is a character appearing in Popeye comics authored by Tom Sims and Bela Zaboly , inspired by the legend of Davy Jones' Locker . He is depicted as a sea spirit who inhabits the bottom of the ocean as well as his Locker, which is located in a sunken ship . Although he has opposed Popeye in the past, both masters of the sea have grown mostly friendly towards each other. The being appears as an old pirate with a white beard, eyepatch, peg leg and hook.
