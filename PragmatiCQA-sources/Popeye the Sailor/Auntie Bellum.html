Auntie Bellum is Olive Oyl 's late aunt. She married into the wealthy Bellum family, which owned a plantation in New Orleans, and she lived there for most of her days with her servant Ashtray. She plays a part in The All-New Popeye Hour episode " The Treasure of Howe's Bayou ".

After she passed away, she left her niece an amulet in her will, along with a clue on finding the legendary cursed treasure sought after by the Bellums, which had turned many members of the family into zombies.
