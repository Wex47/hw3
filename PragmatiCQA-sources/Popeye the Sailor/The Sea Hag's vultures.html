Vultures are birds of prey that have variously opposed Popeye , mainly in the service of the Sea Hag .

The Sea Hag's vultures

The Sea Hag, one of Popeye's worst enemies, had several vulture minions, her pet Bernard among them, who were used to carry out the Hag's dirty work including attacking her enemies. These vultures are a common type of enemy in several Popeye video games .

Other vultures

The Rocs that feature in Popeye cartoons set in the Middle East appear to be gigantic vultures, as opposed to their eagle-like depiction from the original mythology.
