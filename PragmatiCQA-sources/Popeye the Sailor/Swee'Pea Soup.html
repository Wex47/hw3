" Swee'Pea Soup " is a 1960 episode of Popeye the Sailor .

Plot

The people of the kingdom are protesting against King Blozo , who wants help from Popeye and Olive Oyl . Turns out the people love Swee'Pea and hate Blozo. The king calls Professor O.G. Wotasnozzle for help, after which Popeye notices that Swee'Pea is being kidnapped. He finds out that Wotasnozzle is attempting to make soup out of Swee'Pea. Popeye eats his spinach and rescues Swee'Pea. Blozo says that he quits.
