I-Ski Love-Ski You-Ski is Popeye 's 32nd cartoon, produced by Fleischer Studios . It was released on April 3, 1936.

Plot

While out skiing, Popeye and Olive have to contend with Bluto 's various traps, and he eventually attempts to make them fall off the mountain. When he succeeds in throwing Popeye down the abyss and also getting his hands on Olive, the sailor eats his spinach , soon turning the tables and beating the big man during a ski chase.
