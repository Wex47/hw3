Not to be confused with Aladdin and His Wonderful Lamp .

" Aladdin's Lamp " is a 1961 episode of Popeye the Sailor .

Contents

  • 1 Plot
  • 2 Notes

Plot

Popeye chases down the Sea Hag in order to take back the magic lamp she stole from Olive .

Notes

  • This episode first aired April 29, 1961
  • Directed by Seymour Kneitel
  • This is one of 63 episodes produced by Paramount Cartoon Studios for the Popeye the Sailor TV series.
  • This episode is available from Warner Archive on the Popeye the Sailor The 1960s Classics Volume One DVD set.
