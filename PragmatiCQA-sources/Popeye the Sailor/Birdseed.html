Birdseed is Popeye 's dog, that first appeared in the comic book Popeye in 1956. He was adopted by the sailor after a Spinachovian government incident involved the animal (in the story " The Dog Who Wore a Crown! "), upon which Birdseed stayed in the series as Popeye and Swee'Pea 's pet. However, he was only featured during a brief period. Later, in 1963, he would be re-introduced on the comic strip Thimble Theatre .

Birdseed was created by Bud Sagendorf .
