Bolo is a character created by E. C. Segar for his Thimble Theatre comic strip. Bolo is a monstrous strongman and enemy of Popeye known to have worked in the Sea Hag 's service.

In the 1980 live-action film , Bolo is portrayed by Pietro Torrisi . He also appears as an enemy in the 1994 video game Popeye: Ijiwaru Majo Seahag no Maki .
