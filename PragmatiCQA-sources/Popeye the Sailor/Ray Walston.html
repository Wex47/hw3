Herman Raymond " Ray " Walston (November 2, 1914 - January 1, 2001) was an American actor, best known for starring in television's My Favorite Martian as the Martian visitor known as Uncle Martin. Walston is also remembered for his roles in several films, among them Robert Altman 's Popeye , in which he portrayed the role of Poopdeck Pappy .
