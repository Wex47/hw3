Pietro Torrisi , also known as Peter McCoy (born 20 January 1940), is an Italian actor and stuntman. His first known role was that of Comparsa in The Angel Wore Red (1960) and he acted until 2004, making his final appearance in the television series Incantesimo 7 , where he portrayed Domenico Capuoti.

Torrisi portrayed Bolo in the 1980 live-action film Popeye .
