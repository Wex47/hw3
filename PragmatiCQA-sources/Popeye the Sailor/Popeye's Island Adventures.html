Popeye's Island Adventures , also called Popeye for Kids , is a 2018 animated Popeye web series produced by WildBrain subsidiary WildBrain Spark Studios, which premiered on the Popeye & Friends Official YouTube channel and can currently be seen on its own Popeye for Kids channel. The series features a younger-looking, more child-friendly version of the Popeye franchise characters and their world.

Trivia

  • In this series, Popeye always has a in his mouth instead of his traditional pipe , so as to avoid any depiction of tobacco smoking.
  • When Popeye eats his spinach , the power-up animation is similar to the one in The All-New Popeye Hour .
  • Popeye has blond hair.
  • Bluto is also younger-looking, and beardless.
  • Eugene the Jeep usually floats in the air, which is not an ability that was among his original powers.
  • Unlike in previous media, the characters only make sounds instead of talking.
