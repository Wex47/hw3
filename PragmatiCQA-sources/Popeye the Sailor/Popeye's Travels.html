Not to be confused with Gulliver's Travels .

" Popeye's Travels " is a 1960 episode of Popeye the Sailor .

Plot

Popeye 's journey in this episode is based upon the Lilliputian adventure from the classic novel Gulliver's Travels .

Notes

  • This episode first aired November 13, 1960
  • Directed by Seymour Kneitel
  • This is one of 63 episodes produced by Paramount Cartoon Studios for the Popeye the Sailor TV series.
  • This episode is available from Warner Archive on the Popeye the Sailor The 1960s Classics Volume One DVD set.
