Lizzie (voiced by Marilyn Schreffler ) is Bluto 's wife in the 1987 Hanna-Barbera series Popeye and Son . Lizzie is a short, plump lady and the mother of Tank , Popeye Junior 's adversary. The series's setup and Bluto's family were however dropped in subsequent Popeye productions.
