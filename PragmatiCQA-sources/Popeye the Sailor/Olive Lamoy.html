Olive LaMoy

Occupation

Actress, voice actress
Olive LaMoy was a voice actress who played the role of Olive Oyl on Popeye 's radio show. Olive was a Broadway veteran that was adept at recreating the voice of the character's screen counterpart.

Trivia

  • Wheatena put so much money into the broadcast rights paid to KFS and into leasing national airtime through the NBC-WEAF Network, that their remaining budget did not afford them enough money to contract Jack Mercer or Mae Questel (who had voiced Olive Oyl in the cartoons).
