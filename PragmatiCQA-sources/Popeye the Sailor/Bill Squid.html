Character history

Creation and development in Thimble Theatre

Bill Squid was created by E. C. Segar in 1933 for Thimble Theatre .

Character design

He resembles his peers Popeye and Bill Barnacle, having simple sailor attire with long sleeves along with a sailor's cap and pipe like both.
