Popeye's Cartoon Club is an American comic strip begun in 2019 as a companion piece to Thimble Theatre aka Popeye , 100 years after E. C. Segar 's creation was first published. Unlike its century-older parent strip, Popeye's Cartoon Club is an online-only comic, and has featured various guest artists.

Popeye's Cartoon Club draws from Popeye's long comics and animation history, featuring many obscure characters such as Ham Gravy , Deezil Oyl , Sutra Oyl , Aunt Jones or Whaler Joe (whose physical appearance was first revealed on this strip in 2020).
