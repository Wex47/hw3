Baby Popeye , or, Baby Popeye & Friends is a brand of products for young children, toddlers and infants introduced by King Features Syndicate in 2005. The line includes products like clothing, toys and accesories. Popeye , Olive Oyl , Wimpy , Brutus and Eugene the Jeep are depicted as very young, while Swee'Pea appears as Olive's doll. As with Lunch with a Punch and other like-themed cartoons, this youthful interactions between the characters are contradictory to their original encounters in the comics.
