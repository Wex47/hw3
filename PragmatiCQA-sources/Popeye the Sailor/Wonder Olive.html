Olive Oyl transforms into Wonder Olive after eating a can of spinach in one of the Popeye/The Adventures of Popeye segments in Hanna-Barbera 's TV series The All-New Popeye Hour (" Popeye's Self Defense "). Olive persuades Popeye to join the gym, however, Bluto destroys the gym by pulling pranks on Popeye. Olive then takes Popeye's spinach and transforms into Wonder Olive, and stops both Bluto and Popeye from fighting by throwing them on top of the ceiling fan. Wonder Olive then repairs the gym.
