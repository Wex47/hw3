Nana Oyl is a character created by E.C. Segar in his comic Thimble Theatre prior to Popeye 's introduction. Mrs. Oyl is Cole Oyl 's wife, the mother of Olive and Castor Oyl , and the grandmother of Popeye Junior . A character mostly seen in the comics, Nana also plays a part in the Fleischer Studios Popeye cartoon The Man on the Flying Trapeze , where her voice is provided by Margie Hines . Nana also appeared in the Popeye and Son episode "Happy Anniversary", in a flashback where she is seen helping Olive get ready for her wedding.
