Shelley Alexis Duvall (born July 7, 1949) is an American actress, best known for her acting roles in Thieves Like Us , 3 Women , The Shining , and Popeye (as Olive Oyl ).

Duvall began her career in the 1970s films of Robert Altman , followed by roles in films by Woody Allen, Stanley Kubrick, Terry Gilliam, and Tim Burton. Duvall is also an Emmy-nominated producer, responsible for Faerie Tale Theatre and other child-friendly programming.
