Blutessa is Bluto 's girlfriend and Olive Oyl 's rival in " Olive's Shining Hour ", one of the Popeye/The Adventures of Popeye segments in Hanna-Barbera 's TV series The All-New Popeye Hour .

She and her boyfriend face the doubles team of Olive and Popeye in a tennis match, where they employ all sorts of tricks to gain (temporary) advantage.
