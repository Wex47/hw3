The Popeye Career Awareness Library is a series of educational comic books starring Popeye that has the sailor explain the workings of several careers, which was published by King Features in 1972. A box set was sold by board game manufacturer Milton Bradley containing all 15 issues, as well as the same number of Poster Cards and Job Description Cards.

Trivia

  • Each issue has a different cover title: " Popeye the Sailor and "(...) or " Popeye and " (...), followed by the career in question, while the name " Popeye Career Awareness Library " is taken from the Milton Bradley box set.
  • The series was drawn by George Wildman , who also provided the art for the main Popeye the Sailor book at the time.
  • In this educational series, Popeye speaks more correctly than in most of his appearances.
