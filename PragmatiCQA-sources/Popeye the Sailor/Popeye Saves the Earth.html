Popeye Saves the Earth is a 1994 "widebody" pinball machine designed by Python Angelo and Barry Oursler of Midway Games (released under the Bally brand name), based on Popeye under license from King Features Syndicate .

The game has an environmental theme, with objectives related to saving endangered animals (hence, "Saves the Earth"). Popeye's ship as seen on the pinball's decorations bears the name Popeye's Ark . Character art features Popeye, Wimpy , Olive (in a bikini), Swee'Pea , Eugene the Jeep and Bluto , whose head is also made into a large plastic playfield element.
