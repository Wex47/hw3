Alexandrea "Alex" Borstein (born February 15, 1973) is an American Emmy-nominated actress, voice actress, writer, producer, and comedian. She is well known for her long-running role as Lois Griffin on the animated television series Family Guy , and as a cast member on the sketch comedy series MADtv. She has voiced Olive Oyl as well as Betty Boop in commercials.
