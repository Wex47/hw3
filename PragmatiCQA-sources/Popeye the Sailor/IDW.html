IDW Publishing is an American comic book company founded in 1999, which specializes in the adaptation of properties from other media such as Transformers , Ghostbusters , Star Trek , Doctor Who and various others. From 2012 to 2013, it also published a comic book series based on Popeye , in an attempt to recreate the style of original creator E. C. Segar .

The company also began reprinting Bud Sagendorf 's early issues of the first Popeye comic book series (1948-1984), as Popeye Classics .

In 2013, IDW published Mars Attacks Popeye , one of six crossovers comprising their Mars Attacks IDW event.
