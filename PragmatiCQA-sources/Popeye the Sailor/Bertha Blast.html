Sergeant Bertha Blast (voiced by Jo Anne Worley ) is a character appearing only in Private Olive Oyl . A diminutive yet loud and commanding Army sergeant, she is in charge of new recruits Olive Oyl and Alice the Goon , whose lack of discipline would often unwittingly cause chaos in the base. Sergeant Blast would be left much annoyed and incensed - especially each time Colonel Crumb showed satisfaction at the pair's actions, oblivious to their earlier blunders.

Trivia

  • Sergeant Blast was most likely based on the character of Captain Lewis, originally played by Eileen Brennan, in both the movie and television series Private Benjamin .
  • A running gag is Sergeant Blast being constantly stepped on or trampled.
