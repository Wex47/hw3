Pleased to Meet Cha! is Popeye 's 20th theatrical cartoon, from Fleischer Studios , released on March 22, 1935.

Plot

Popeye and Bluto compete to show Olive the best "trick" at the latter's house, which means dealing out some abuse to each other with increased violence, especially after Popeye eats spinach , and an all-out brawl between both men leaves the house in shambles. After she throws them out, Popeye comes back to fix things.
