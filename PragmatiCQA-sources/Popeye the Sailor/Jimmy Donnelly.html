Jimmy Donnelly

Occupation

Actor, voice actor
Jimmy Donnelly was a child actor who played the role of Matey the Newsboy on Popeye 's radio show.

Trivia

  • Matey (also known as "Sonny") was a non- Segar character who filled in as a substitute for Swee'Pea .
  • Wheatena put so much money into the broadcast rights paid to KFS and into leasing national airtime through the NBC-WEAF Network that their remaining budget did not afford them enough money to contract Popeye voice actor Jack Mercer or Mae Questel , who had voiced Swee'Pea in the cartoons.
