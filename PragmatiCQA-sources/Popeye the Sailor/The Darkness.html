The Darkness is a first-person shooter video game published by 2K Studios for the Playstation 3 and Xbox 360 in 2007, based on the comic book series by Marc Silvestri and starring the comic's protagonist, Jackie Estacado, a young mobster who inherits the supernatural powers of 'the Darkness'.

A special bonus in this game are movies and cartoons that can be viewed on the in-game televisions. They are films that have fallen into the public domain, such as 1974's The Street Fighter and the 1930s Flash Gordon serials. The cartoon channel mostly shows Famous Studios Popeye shorts, these include:

  • Shuteye Popeye (1952)
  • Floor Flusher (1954)
  • Greek Mirthology (1954)
  • Cookin' with Gags (1955)
  • Insect to Injury (1956)
  • Parlez Vous Woo (1956)
  • I Don't Scare (1956)
  • A Haul in One (1956)
  • Nearlyweds (1957)
  • Spree Lunch (1957)
