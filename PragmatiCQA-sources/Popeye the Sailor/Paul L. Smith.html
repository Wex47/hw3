Paul Lawrence Smith (June 24, 1936 – April 25, 2012) was an American stand-up comedian, actor, singer and comedian. He portrayed Bluto in the 1980 Popeye film . He is also known for playing Glossu Rabban in the 1984 science fiction film Dune .

On April 25, 2012, Smith died in his home in Ra'anana, Israel, aged 75. The cause of death is unconfirmed.
