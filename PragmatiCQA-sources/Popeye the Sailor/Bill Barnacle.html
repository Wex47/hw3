"Salty" Bill Barnacle is a character who first appeared in E. C. Segar 's comic strip Thimble Theatre in 1933. He is a friend of Popeye 's and a fellow tough sailor .

Other than Popeye himself, Bill Barnacle is the only known member of the Sea Dog Club .

In the 1980 live-action film , he is portrayed by actor Robert Fortier. In said film, the character is not shown to interact with Popeye much and is portrayed as a local sailor and Sweethaven 's town drunk.
