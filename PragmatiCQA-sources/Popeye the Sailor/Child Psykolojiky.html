Child Psykolojiky is the ninety-seventh Popeye cartoon, produced by Fleischer Studios . It was released on July 11, 1941.

Plot

Popeye and Poopdeck Pappy are trying to play poker, but Swee'Pea 's crying keeps interrupting them. Pappy wants to smack the tot, but Popeye persuades him to try psychology instead. Popeye tells the story of how "George Washlincoln" chopped down the cherry tree. Inspired, Swee'Pea chops a hole in the floor, then tells the truth. His adoptive father rushes out to buy him a reward, leaving Pappy in charge, but the old sailor believes in a rather dangerous style of parenting, introducing the boy to William Tell (from both ends of the gun). Pappy later lies about it to Popeye.
