This article is about the characters. For the comic story, see Cam! .

Created in 1953 by Bud Sagendorf , Axle and Cam on the Planet Meco are father-and-son robot characters starring in a backup feature in early Popeye comic books , before Sagendorf settled on the long-running Sappo (renamed after O. G. Wotasnozzle ) as the supporting feature. They are alien robots from the planet Meco which are very human-like in terms of behavior and self-awareness.

Personalities

Axle is a no-nonsense robot who is loyal to his protocol and always tries to do what is best for his son by making sure he behaves like a proper hard-working robot. Cam is a surprisingly free-spirited robot who always wants to play games and have fun, making him more human-like than other mechanical men, and this often makes him quite mischievous as he would rather play games than work. This often is a source of much conflict between the two, but both care for one another like any good father and son would.
