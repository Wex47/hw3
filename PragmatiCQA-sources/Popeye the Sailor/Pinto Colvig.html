Vance DeBar "Pinto" Colvig (September 11, 1892 - October 3, 1967) was an American actor, voice actor, circus performer and cartoonist. He is best known as the original Bozo the Clown and, as a voice actor, the original voice of Disney character Goofy . He had voice roles in thirteen Fleischer Studios Popeye cartoons, nine of them as Bluto .
