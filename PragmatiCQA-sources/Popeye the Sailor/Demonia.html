Demonia is the homeland of Swee'Pea . It is a kingdom that was originally ruled by a royal family of which Swee'Pea was a part of until his kingly father was killed. The queen of this kingdom would end up leaving her child in the care of Popeye for his own protection. The kingdom itself also seems to be the home of demons that occasionally plague the land, hence its sinister name.

Trivia

  • In a 1972 comic book story by Nick Cuti and George Wildman (as well as in " Swee'Pea Soup ") Swee'Pea's kingdom of origin is instead identified as Spinachovia .
