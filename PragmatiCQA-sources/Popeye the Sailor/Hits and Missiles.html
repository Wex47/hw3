" Hits and Missiles " is a 1960 episode of Popeye the Sailor .

Contents

  • 1 Plot
  • 2 Notes
  • 3 Cast

Plot

Popeye , Olive and Wimpy take an unintended trip to the moon, which is inhabited by cheese-people and tyrannized by the " Big Cheese ".

Notes

  • This episode first aired September 1, 1960
  • Directed by Seymour Kneitel
  • This is one of 63 episodes produced by Paramount Cartoon Studios for the Popeye the Sailor TV series.
  • This episode is available from Warner Archive on the Popeye the Sailor The 1960s Classics Volume One DVD set.
