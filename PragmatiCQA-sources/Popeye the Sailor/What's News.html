" What's News " is a 1960 episode of Popeye the Sailor , adapted from the 1934 Thimble Theatre storyline "Puddleburg, the Laziest Town on Earth".

Contents

  • 1 Plot
  • 2 Notes

Plot

Popeye and Olive travel to the small town of Puddleburg , where Popeye has bought a newspaper, the Puddleburg Splash . Olive plans to teach the townspeople how to read, but bullies tear down the new school. Popeye defeats the bullies, and she starts her classes.

Notes

  • This episode first aired November 14, 1960
  • Directed by Seymour Kneitel
  • This is one of 63 episodes produced by Paramount Cartoon Studios for the Popeye the Sailor TV series.
  • This episode is available from Warner Archive on the Popeye the Sailor The 1960s Classics Volume One DVD set.
