Blunder Below is the 103rd Popeye theatrical cartoon. It was produced by Fleischer Studios and released on February 13, 1942.

Plot

Navy sailor Popeye is on a battleship . He is in class to learn artillery, but it is way too technical for him; when he gets his hands on the gun, he fires everywhere, including at his own ship. That gets him banished to the boiler room. A real Japanese submarine comes along and the other sailors cannot handle it, so Popeye eats his spinach and dives in to fight the submarine by hand.
