Colonel Crumb (voiced by Hal Smith) is a character appearing only in Private Olive Oyl television cartoons. He is a high-ranking Army official who, arriving late at the scene of mischief caused by new recruits Olive Oyl and Alice the Goon , would compliment or congratulate them due to the invariably positive results of their foul-ups, unaware of their recent blunders - much to the chagrin of their immediate superior, Sergeant Bertha Blast .
