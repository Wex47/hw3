" Scairdy Cat " is a 1961 Popeye the Sailor episode.

Contents

  • 1 Plot
  • 2 Notes

Plot

Popeye drinks a potion that turns him into a coward, and Brutus tries to take his place as Olive Oyl 's date. Popeye finally manages to get a hold of some spinach , which gives him back his bravery.

Notes

  • This episode first aired June 4, 1961
  • Directed by Seymour Kneitel
  • This is one of 63 episodes produced by Paramount Cartoon Studios for the Popeye the Sailor TV series.
  • This episode is available from Warner Archive on the Popeye the Sailor The 1960s Classics Volume One DVD set.
