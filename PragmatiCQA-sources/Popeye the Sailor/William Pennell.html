William Pennell (March 3, 1889 - September 5, 1956) was an American voice actor who was the original voice of Bluto in Fleischer Studios ' animated Popeye cartoons, beginning with the first short, Popeye the Sailor . The role of Bluto was later taken over by Gus Wickie , yet Pennell returned to the role for the Famous Studios 1940s cartoons A Hull of a Mess and Too Weak to Work .
