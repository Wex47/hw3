Garry Chalk (born 17 February 1952) is an American actor and voice actor who is best known for his roles as Optimus Primal in Beast Wars: Transformers and Beast Machines , as well as the voice of Optimus Prime in various English dubs of Transformers anime.

He voiced Bluto in Popeye's Voyage: The Quest for Pappy .
