Dufus Jones , although referred to as Popeye 's nephew, is truly the son of old family friend Pooky Jones. A tall, rotund youngster, Dufus is often seen in the company of Swee'Pea . Dufus appears in comics but never in theatrical films or television episodes. He is portrayed as being almost as strong as his "Uncle Popeye".

Dufus was created by Bud Sagendorf .
