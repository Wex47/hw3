Thomas James "Tom" Kenny (born July 13, 1962) is a voice actor and comedian. He is known for his voice acting talent and the many characters he has voiced throughout his career, with his best-known and longest-running role being the title character in SpongeBob SquarePants . He did the voice of Popeye in the animation test for the still-unproduced Popeye animated film , reprising the role for the Popeye Robot's cameo in Samurai Jack (see: list of Popeye references in popular culture ).
