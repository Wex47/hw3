Tank (voiced by David Markus) is a character from Hanna-Barbera 's short-lived TV series, 1987's Popeye and Son . A young bully, he is the son of Bluto and Lizzie and also Popeye Junior 's rival, often seen in the company of Puggy and Rad . The series, however, did not prove too popular and its settings and characters have been ignored by newer Popeye media.
