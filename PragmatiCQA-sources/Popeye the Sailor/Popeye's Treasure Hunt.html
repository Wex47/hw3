Popeye's Treasure Hunt is a segment within The All-New Popeye Hour in which "Popeye & Olive Treasure Seekers Ltd." owners Popeye and Olive Oyl would travel to various parts of the world in search for hidden treasure (often with help from Eugene the Jeep ), following cryptic clues and trailed by Bluto from "Bluto's Treasure Salvage", who seeks the treasure for himself, until solving the final riddle and reaching the prize.

"Treasure Hunts" were always presented in two parts at different points during the same episode, thus lasting twice as long as most other segments (the only other exception being The Popeye Sports Parade ).
