Popeye: Rush for Spinach is a Game Boy Advance video game based on Popeye , licensed from King Features Syndicate . It was developed by Magic Pockets and published by Namco in North America in 2005, and by Atari in Europe in 2006.

Rush for Spinach is a racing game with some platform mechanics, as characters must run towards the right side of the screen, trying to reach a goal first and jumping over and otherwise avoiding obstacles, and also the other racers. Not only is Popeye playable, but also Olive Oyl , Bluto and Wimpy . Helpful items along the way include skateboards, pogo sticks and cans of spinach .

A 3D-racing version entitled Popeye: Hush Rush for the Spinach was in development for the PlayStation 2 console, but was canceled before its release.
