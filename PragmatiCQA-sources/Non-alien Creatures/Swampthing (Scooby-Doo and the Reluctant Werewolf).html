The Swampthing is one of the monsters who regularly participates in Count Dracula 's annual Monster Road Rally across Transylvania, in which he drives the vehicle known as the "Slime Speedster". His origins are unknown, but he resembles a tall, bulky humanoid creature covered in swamp vegetation.
