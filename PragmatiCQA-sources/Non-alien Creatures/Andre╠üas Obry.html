Andréas Obry is a lycanthrope and a hypnotized servant of the vampire Armand Tesla .

History

When Tesla was staked through the heart in 1918 by Prof. Walter Saunders and Lady Jane Ainsley, Andréas collapsed on the floor and became human again, freed from his master's control. With Lady Jane's help, he was eventually rehabilitated from his traumatic experiences and made her assistant at the clinic.

When Tesla returned from the grave in 1942, he once again hypnotized Andréas, causing him to start turning into a werewolf again. However, Andréas later managed to escape from his master's mind control and killed him by staking him through the heart and leaving his body exposed to the sun where it decomposed, preventing Tesla from ever rising again. Andréas himself also died soon afterwards, as he succumbed from wounds sustained previously when he was shot by Inspector Frederick Fleet and injured when the crypt he was in got bombarded.
