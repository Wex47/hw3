The Dragonfly is one of the monsters who regularly participates in Count Dracula 's annual Monster Road Rally across Transylvania, in which he drives the vehicle known as the "Dragon Dragster". Physically, he resembles a human-sized dragon -like creature with an insectoid head and partially transparent wings.
