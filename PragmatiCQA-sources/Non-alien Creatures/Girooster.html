Universe Green Eggs and Ham

Giroosters are a species of flightless birds, which bear a strong resemblance to the friendly and docile Chickeraffes in appearance, but are almost the opposite in terms of behavior.

Biology

Physically, they resemble Chickeraffes with a darker color palette and a mouth filled with sharp teeth. They live in small social groups, making nests on top of tree branches. Their feet have two digits, and they move through the treetops with envious agility. Like Chickeraffes, they can curl up into a feathery sphere. In spite of their teeth, they're actually herbivores, and their diet consists solely of beans.

Described as "nature's deadliest judgivores", Giroosters have a prideful sense of superiority and will hunt down anyone that they judge as inferior. While they don't eat their victims, they still chew them up like bubblegum and blow bubbles with them. The only way to deal with a pack of Giroosters is to make a strong impression, intimidating them into acknowledging that one's not, in fact, inferior to them.
