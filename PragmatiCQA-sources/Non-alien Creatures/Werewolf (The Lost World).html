Werewolves are a mysterious kind of creatures which inhabit the plateau known as Maple White Land. Anyone who is wounded by a Werewolf will slowly transform into one as well.

Biology

The first symptoms of this condition manifest in the form of a strong debilitating fever and painful convulsions, only for the subject to return to normal the next day, but start chasing and attacking others like an animal. Only when exposed to moonlight does the physical transformation occur, with the subject developing inhuman feet and claws. They howl to the moon, their eyes change color and they develop fur. Fire and silver are both effective weapons against them.

Werewolves live in dark places, sleep communally in caves and are mostly active at night, leading Prof. Challenger to speculate that they might be sensitive to light. They come back to normal during the day, and will also revert to their human form at death. An injected fluid based on silver nitrate can be effectively used as a cure for this kind of lycanthropy .
