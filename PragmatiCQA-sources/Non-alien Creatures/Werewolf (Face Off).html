The Werewolf was created by Nicole Chilelli for the spotlight challenge of the Face Off season 3 episode "Scene of the Crime", inspired by the TV series Grimm .

Notes

  • This isn't the only werewolf featured in Face Off . Season 6 had an entire episode focused on them, leading to the creation of the African , Australian , Chinese and Philippine Werewolf varieties. As for the extraterrestrial lycanthropes of Season 4, please consult .
