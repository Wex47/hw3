Franklin Stein is the monstrous butler of Norma Deathman 's mansion. He is a tall humanoid with pale greenish skin and bolts on the sides of his head. Despite his brutish appearance, he appears to be highly efficient and articulate.

Appearances

  • The New Scooby-Doo Mysteries - s01e02 - "Scooby's Peep-Hole Pandemonium" (1984)

Notes

  • Franklin Stein is clearly inspired by Frankenstein's Monster , but appears to be a different character, given that the real one was later seen in the series in the episode "A Halloween Hassle at Dracula's Castle", written by Paul Dini.
  • Additionally, at least three other versions of Frankenstein's Monster have appeared elsewhere in the Scooby-Doo franchise over the years, each with a highly different characterization. These are: the one from The New Scooby and Scrappy-Doo Show episode "Who's Minding the Monster"; the one from Scooby-Doo and the Ghoul School (identified as "Frankenteen Senior"); and the one from Scooby-Doo and the Reluctant Werewolf .
