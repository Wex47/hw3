Dr. Jackyll , a.k.a. Mr. Snyde , is a man who can spontaneously assume an alternate monstrous form with a separate personality. He's one of the competitors in Count Dracula 's annual Monster Road Rally, in which he drives a vehicle known as the Split-Personality Special. He has also developed a serum to create illusory monsters and scare other competitors away.

Appearances

  • Scooby-Doo and the Reluctant Werewolf (1988)

Notes

  • This character is clearly based on Henry Jekyll from Robert Louis Stevenson's Strange Case of Dr Jekyll and Mr Hyde .
