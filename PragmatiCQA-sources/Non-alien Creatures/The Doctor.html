The Doctor is an ancient being and the main protagonist of the TV series Doctor Who .

History

The Doctor was found by a Shobogan named Tecteun as a young, dark-skinned female child beside a monument beneath an interdimensional portal, having fell through from an unknown location. Tecteun took back to her home planet of Gallifrey where she raised her as her own child. On Gallifrey, Tecteun studied The Doctor in an attempt to understand her. A short time later, The Doctor was fighting with another child when she fell from a cliff and died. Rushing to her child, Tecteun witnessed The Doctor regenerating for the first time. Tecteun began to study The Doctor even further for many years witnessing several more regenerations. Tecteun eventually isolated the regeneration gene in The Doctor's DNA and tested it on herself, successfully regenerating. Now a man, Tecteun gave the entire population of Gallifrey this ability but limited it to twelve regenerations. A short time later, time travel was discovered and the species was rechristened the "Time Lords" The Time Lords passed their principle law of observing but not interfering with the peoples and affairs of other planets but also recognized that interference is neccessary in some situations and created the black ops unit The Division with The Doctor as an operative. At some point, in the form of Ruth Clayton, The Doctor fled the Division and settled on Earth in the late 20th century and used a chameleon arch to pose as a human named Ruth Clayton. She eventually was located in the year 2020 by the Time Lords but escaped with the help of the Thirteenth Doctor. At some point after this, The Doctor had their memories forcibly buried within their subconscious and regenerated into the First Doctor, leading to the events of the TV series.

Powers and Abilities

The primary ability that sets The Doctor apart from Humans is her ability to regenerate upon death, coming back with a new body and personality. The Doctor can use this ability an infinite number of times. The Doctor can make psychic links with other lifeforms and even inanimate objects such as doors. The Doctor possesses a respiratory bypass system allowing her to breathe in the absence of oxygen as well as protecting her from strangulation or suffocation. The Doctor has highly developed senses being able to determine the year and location by smelling the air and can tell the composition of a substance by taste it. The Doctor typically speaks in English with either an English or Scottish accent depend on the incarnation. The Doctor can also speak Modern Gallifreyan, Judoon, Delphon ("spoken" by moving the eyebrows), several Chinese dialects, Ancient North Martian, Venusian, Vietnamese, Portuguese, Tritovore, Sycoraxic, an unnamed barking language, French, Sabre-Tooth Tiger, Baby, Cat, Horse, Dinosaur and Semaphore. The Ninth Doctor said he knew five billion languages while The Eleventh Doctor said he spoke everything.
