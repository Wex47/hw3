Winnie Werewolf is a young female lycanthrope , daughter of Papa Werewolf , and one of the students at Miss Grimwood's Finishing School for Ghouls, where she is friends with Sibella , Phantasma , Elsa Frankenteen and Tanis Mummy .

Appearances

  • Scooby-Doo and the Ghoul School (1988)

Notes

  • Unlike most portrayals of werewolves in fiction and folklore, Winnie's lycanthrope form appears to be permanent. She never becomes human.
