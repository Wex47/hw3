Conservation Status is a classification that is used to measure the number of factors that threats a species.

Levels

Unknown

Data Deficient (DD): The number of individuals belonging to this species is totally unknown.

Lower Risk

Least Concern (LC): This species has a very low possibility of being extinct.

Near Threatened (NT): This species has a good possibility of become threatened in a near future.

Conservation Dependent (CD): This species has a great possibility of become threatened without conservation.

Threatened

Vulnerable (VU): This species has a good possibility of become endangered without several conservation.

Endangered (EN): This species has a good possibility of become extinct in the future, most species in this level are rare.

Critically Endangered (CE): This species has a great possibility of become extinct in the future, all the species in this level are extremely rare.

Extinct

Extinct in the Wild (EW): This species has its last specimens living in captivity.

Extinct (EX): This species has been totally banned from the universe.
