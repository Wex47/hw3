The Wulver is a mythological creature from Scottish folklore. Its appearance is similar to that of a werewolf , but unlike traditional werewolves, it never shapeshifts and has no human origin whatsoever; arguably making it more akin to a wolf-like Cynocephalus .

Wulvers are traditionally said to inhabit the Shetland Islands, where they make home in caves dug out of the sides of hills. Their bodies are covered in short brown hair. They're intelligent creatures that acquire most of their food by fishing and never bother humans unless attacked by them. Some sources even suggest that a Wulver will habitually leave fish in the windows of a poor house out of generosity.

Wulvers appear as enemies in the 2018 game God of War , where they're depicted as far more aggressive than their actual folkloric counterparts.
