Universe Real

Arboreal vampires from Ashanti folklore.

Distribution

The Asanbosam, if real, can presumably be found in Ghana, Ivory Coast, and Togo. The Ashanti people live in all three.

Appearance

They have iron claws and teeth and their feet point both ways.
