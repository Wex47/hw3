Alexander Anderson (アレクサンド・アンデルセン, Arekusando Anderusen) is a Vatican priest, working as a vampire purifier and slayer for the Iscariot branch of the Vatican in Hellsing Universe. He lives in Italy, operating an orphanage outside Vatican City. He seem to be very nice with children but reveal a darker side in battle. He is a regenerator capable of regenerating from fatal wounds but his capability seem to be limited as seen when a part of his left arm was blown it didn't grow back. He has rivalry with Alucard.
