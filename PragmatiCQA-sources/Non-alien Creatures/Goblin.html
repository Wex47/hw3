A Goblin is a small monstrous creature from German legend. Unlike Gnomes and Leprechauns , Goblins have been typically portrayed as more sinister and malicious, with a penchant for bad jokes, torturing and scaring people. Additionally, the word has been historically used as a synonym for spirits and apparitions, as seen, for instance, in Washington Irving's "The Legend of Sleepy Hollow".
