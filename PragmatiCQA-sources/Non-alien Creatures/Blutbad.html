Blutbaden (singular: Blutbad ) are a lupine Wesen species whose woged form resembles a werewolf . Actual lycanthropia is a rare genetic condition that affects some of their species.

Biology

Like all Wesen, Blutbaden are liminal beings which alternate between a human and a non-human form in a process known as woging. When they woge, they develop bestial features, coarser hair, sharp teeth, pointed ears, blood-red eyes, and claws.

A predatory species, Blutbaden are known for their superhuman strength and extraordinarily acute sense of smell. Their teeth can cut through flesh easily. Their reputation for savagery makes them feared and respected by other species, and they traditionally prey on both humans and fellow Wesen, particularly Seelenguter and Bauerschweine . House pets generally become agitated in their presence.

Like many canine species, Blutbaden recognize others primarily by scent and use urine to mark their territories. They instinctively become more violent in groups, and also in the presence of the color red. Their only known weak spot is a cluster of nerves on the lower back. Striking this vulnerable spot can debilitate them, albeit only for a short time. Wolfsbane can also be used to hide one's scent from a Blutbad.

After feeding on large prey, such as a human, a Blutbad will generally wait between a week and ten days before feeding again. During that time, some Blutbaden keep live victims in captivity and fatten them up to become their next meal. Despite their preference for meat, Blutbaden can also survive on a strictly vegetarian diet if they choose to.

The Völlige Verzweiflung mushroom, when cooked, becomes lethal to Blutbaden. The toxins released by the fungus provoke an irrational urge to climb trees and causes the victim's stomach to swell up and burst, spreading the spores of the fungus over a wide area.

Lycanthropia is a rare genetic mutation that affects Blutbaden. Those affected are known as Lycanthropes . During the three nights of the full moon, Lycanthropes lose all control of themselves and will indiscriminately attack anyone, even family members.
