Pappag's Mansion

Pappag lives in a mansion in Gyoverly Hills due to his wealth and fame from designing the Criminal clothing line. On the exterior, it looks like a giant seashell. It has a Criminal clothing store on the first floor. The prices of these clothes are apparently very high. According to a store clerk, 10,000 is below half of the original price. Camie usually travels there to bring Pappag orient clams.

Trivia

  • Gyoverly Hills is a portmanteau of the Japanese word for "fish" ( 魚 , gyo ) and Beverly Hills .
