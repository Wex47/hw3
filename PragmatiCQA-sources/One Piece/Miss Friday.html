Miss Friday is a vulture and a member of the criminal organization Baroque Works , being a part of The Unluckies ( 13日の金曜日 （ アンラッキーズ ） , Anrakkīzu ) along with Mr. 13 . They were a team that served as messengers for Mr. 0 and were also responsible for executing any Baroque Works agents that fail their missions. She is classified as Type D creatures, "Small Savage".

Appearance

Miss Friday is a large, black-feathered vulture, with white feathers toward the neck. Her skin is pink, and her beak is a light yellow. She wears a yellow aviator cap dotted with red flowers and black goggles.
