Role and Duties

Pirate apprentices are younger members of a pirate crew that possess the least authority on a ship, since their main purpose is to learn how to become a proper pirate from the rest of the crew. Their training usually involves partaking in habitual pirate activities such as raiding other ships.

Once apprentices are fully educated, either they become full-fledged members and maybe even officers of their respective crews, or leave to become captains of their own crews.

List of Former Apprentices

  • Both Shanks and Buggy were apprentices of the Roger Pirates until their captain chose to disband the crew. Afterwards, they both eventually became infamous captains of their own pirate crews.
    • Shanks built the Red Hair Pirates and became one of the Four Emperors .
    • Buggy built the Buggy Pirates and eventually became a member of the Seven Warlords of the Sea before the system's dissolution.
  • Kaidou was an apprentice of the Rocks Pirates before the crew's disbandment upon their captain 's demise. He then went on to becoming Governor-General of the Beasts Pirates and one of the Four Emperors.
  • Marco was an apprentice of the Whitebeard Pirates before eventually becoming the crew's First Division Commander and subsequently led the remnants after the original captain 's death.
  • Inuarashi and Nekomamushi both became apprentices of the Whitebeard Pirates after sneaking aboard their ship, then later sailed with the Roger Pirates until returning to the Wano Country .

Trivia

  • All of the crews that once had apprentices were ones that existed during Gol D. Roger 's time and are all now disbanded.
