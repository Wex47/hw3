Piepie Island is one of 34 islands surrounding Whole Cake Island as part of the Big Mom Pirates ' main territory known as Totto Land . It is run by Charlotte Compo , the Minister of Pie ( パイ大臣 , Pai Daijin ) .

Layout

Sakkuri Town

Sakkuri Town ( サックリタウン , Sakkuri Taun ) is a city situated on the island. Sakkuri (さっくり) refers to a soft and light texture in Japanese.

History

Past

Less than 62 years ago, Piepie Island became part of the Big Mom Pirates territory also known as " Totto Land ", and its appearance was changed to its current look using Charlotte Linlin 's Soru Soru no Mi and Streusen 's Kuku Kuku no Mi .

Charlotte Compo was then appointed the minister in charge of this island.
