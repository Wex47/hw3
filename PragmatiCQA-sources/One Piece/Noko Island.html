Noko Island is one of 34 islands surrounding Whole Cake Island as part of the Big Mom Pirates ' main territory known as Totto Land . It is run by Charlotte Opera , the Minister of Whipped Cream ( 生クリーム大臣 , Nama Kurīmu Daijin ) .

Layout

White Town

White Town ( ホワイトタウン , Howaito Taun ) is a city situated on the island. "White" is likely a reference to the color of cream.

History

Past

Less than 62 years ago, Noko Island became part of the Big Mom Pirates territory also known as " Totto Land ", and its appearance was changed to its current look using Charlotte Linlin 's Soru Soru no Mi and Streusen 's Kuku Kuku no Mi .

Charlotte Opera was then appointed the minister in charge of this island.

Trivia

  • Noko means "strong-flavored" or "thick", but also has a figurative meaning of "hot", in reference to Opera's Devil Fruit, the Kuri Kuri no Mi .
