The Spiders Cafe is a cafe in the desert of an unknown island. The cafe is owned by Paula, aka Zala and is operated by several former members of Baroque Works . There was another location on Arabasta , which acted as a meeting place for the Baroque Works officer agents, but was later abandoned.

History

Arabasta Location

The Spiders Cafe was originally located on Arabasta and was operated by Miss Doublefinger alone under the alias of Paula. Baroque Works used this as meeting place for the Officer Agents before their attempt to take over Arabasta. During the meeting, Mr. 1 and Mr. 2 had a fight which resulted in holes in the wall and other damage to the building. After Baroque Work's defeat and arrest, the location was presumably abandoned.

Second Location

During the Baroque Works mini-arc , the former members of Baroque Works began a new Spiders Cafe by restoring the building that was the former Cactus Saloon . The current known members there are Zala , Marianne , Babe , Drophy , Lassoo , Gem , and Mikita .

Trivia

  • According to Oda in a response to a fan, the Spiders Cafe is based on the cafe from the movie Bagdad Cafe that was directed by Percy Adlon.
