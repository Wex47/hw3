Shells Town is the third setting of the Romance Dawn Arc . It is located in the Yotsuba Island Region ( ヨツバ島海域 , Yotsuba-tō Kaiiki ) of East Blue . Shells Town is covered with power plant-shaped towers that shoot cannons.

Notable Places

Food Foo

Food Foo is a restaurant in Shells Town.

153rd Branch

The 153rd Branch is a Marine Base at Shells Town and the seat of the 153rd Marine Branch in East Blue. It is where Luffy and Zoro defeated Morgan.

History

Romance Dawn Arc

It was the location of the Marine 153rd Branch , headed by Captain Morgan . Shells Town was being ruined by Morgan's actions until Monkey D. Luffy intervened. Since Morgan's "deposition" by Luffy, the Marine base was last heard to be under the temporary command of Ripper .
