One Piece Color Walk 2 is a book containing past titlepage artwork in color. Originally, these artworks were produced in greyscale as featured in both Shonen Jump and the manga collective volumes like this. This is standard for many mangas in Japan due to the cost of producing color reproductions. The colorwalk presented a unique opportunity to see Oda 's title page artwork in color.

This book contains in particular the color panels and color spreads between Chapter 100 and Chapter 175 .

This book was released in English by VIZ Media on January 3, 2012.

Content

  • Color works between 1999-2001
    • Color spreads between chapter 100 and chapter 175 .
    • Manga covers between Volume 10 - Volume 19 .
  • Monochrome Talk: interview with Eiichiro Oda and Fujiko A. Fujio ( Doraemon ).
