Cheese Island is one of 34 islands surrounding Whole Cake Island as part of the Big Mom Pirates ' main territory known as Totto Land . It is run by Charlotte Mont-d'Or , the Minister of Cheese ( チーズ大臣 , Chīzu Daijin ) . It is the fourth island of the Charlotte Family siblings' secret route to Whole Cake Island .

Layout

Cheese Town

Cheese Town ( チーズタウン , Chīzu Taun ) is a city situated on Cheese Island. Its architecture is themed after Swiss cheese and milk bottles . It also contains structures based on cheese-based fast food such as pizza.

History

Past

Less than 62 years ago, Cheese Island became part of the Big Mom Pirates territory also known as " Totto Land ", and its appearance was changed to its current look using Charlotte Linlin and Streusen 's Devil Fruits (the Soru Soru no Mi and Kuku Kuku no Mi respectively).

Charlotte Mont-d'Or was then appointed the minister in charge of this island.

Whole Cake Island Arc

As the Sanji Retrieval Team followed Charlotte Pudding directions, they passed near Cheese Island on their way to Whole Cake Island.

The day of Sanji 's and Pudding 's wedding, citizens of Cheese Town were eager and happy for the event and discussed about the guests who were invited to their Queen 's Tea Party.

Trivia

  • This island was labelled as "Milk Island" ( ミルク島 , Miruku-tō ) in Chapter 859 and Episode 829. One Piece Magazine Vol.5 reveals that no such island exists in Totto Land, and that Cheese Town is located on Cheese Island.
