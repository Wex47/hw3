History

Origins

This island is actually the feces produced after a giant goldfish ate a whole island.

Past

Sometime over a hundred years ago, the Giant Warrior Pirates landed on this island, mistaking it for dry land.

Whisky Peak Arc

Miss All-Sunday gave Vivi an Eternal Pose to this island to help the Straw Hat Pirates reach Arabasta. However, while Vivi was contemplating whether or not she should accept, Luffy suddenly grabbed the pose and destroyed it, refusing to let an enemy choose their route.

Anime and Manga Differences

The name on the Eternal Pose was shown only in the anime.

Trivia

  • "Nanimonai" means "nothing" in Japanese.
  • Usopp once lied to Kaya about fighting a giant goldfish and landing on its feces, thinking it was actual land.
