One Piece Red: Grand Characters ( ワンピース　レッド　グランド　キャラクターズ , Wan Pīsu Reddo Gurando Kyarakutāzu ) is the first One Piece databook . It was released on January 5, 2002 and covers material from Volume 1 to Volume 21 , comprising all of the East Blue Saga and most of the Arabasta Saga .

As its subtitle suggests, One Piece Red is concerned almost solely with character data. Roughly half its content consists of in-depth profiles on each of the first six Straw Hat Pirates , with the rest mostly consisting of shorter write-ups on all the other pirate crews (and Marines ) encountered thus far. These are interspersed with several bonus stories, games, and the like.

Proper Check

The Straw Hats' profiles are concluded by the Proper Check ( 適正チェック , Tekisei chekku ) , a 43-question personality test with many branching paths leading to nine possible types:

  • Luffy (A)
  • Zoro (B)
  • Usopp (C)
  • Nami (D)
  • Sanji (E)
  • Vivi (F)
  • Chopper (G)
  • Arlong (H)
  • Mr. 2 Bon Clay (I)
