G-1 is a Marine Base that was located in the New World , now on Marineford (where Marine Headquarters used to be), where Vice Admiral Momonga is stationed.

History

After the events of the Summit War of Marineford , the base switched locations with the Marine Headquarters at Marineford , as Fleet Admiral Sakazuki 's desired to have the Headquarters in the sea the Four Emperors are in order to challenge their domination within the second half of the Grand Line .

Its former location, now called New Marineford , is said to be exactly symmetrical to Marineford on the other side of the Red Line .
