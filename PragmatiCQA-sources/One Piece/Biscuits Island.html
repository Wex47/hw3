Biscuits Island is one of 34 islands surrounding Whole Cake Island as part of the Big Mom Pirates ' main territory known as Totto Land . It is run by Charlotte Cracker , the Minister of Biscuit ( ビスケット大臣 , Bisuketto Daijin ) . It is the fifth island of the Charlotte Family siblings' secret route to Whole Cake Island .

Layout

Cookie Town

Cookie Town ( クッキータウン , Kukkī Taun ) is a city situated on the island. It is named after cookies , an alternate term for biscuit or a type of biscuit.

History

Past

Less than 62 years ago, Biscuits Island became part of the Big Mom Pirates territory also known as " Totto Land ", and its appearance was changed to its current look using Charlotte Linlin and Streusen 's Devil Fruits (the Soru Soru no Mi and Kuku Kuku no Mi respectively).

Charlotte Cracker was then appointed the minister in charge of this island.

Four Emperors Saga

Whole Cake Island Arc

As the Sanji Retrieval Team followed Charlotte Pudding directions, they passed near Biscuits Island on their way to Whole Cake Island.
