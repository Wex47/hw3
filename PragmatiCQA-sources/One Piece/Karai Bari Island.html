Karai Bari Island is an island in the New World currently occupied by Buggy's Delivery .

Geography

The island is dotted with palm trees, and has mountains in the background. The pirate dispatch organization Buggy's Delivery has set up a large town named Buggy Town (バギー街 Bagī-gai ) which resembles a gathering of circus tents.

History

At an unknown point during the timeskip, the Buggy and Alvida Alliance set up a Pirate Dispatch Company on the island, and it appears to have become their base of operations.

When the Seven Warlords system was abolished, the island became surrounded by Marine battleships who were sent to apprehend Buggy and his crew.

Trivia

  • The name of the island is derived from the Japanese word "karaibari", which means "false bravado" in English, fitting the way Buggy has conducted himself as of late.
