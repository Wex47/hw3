Milenge Island is one of 34 islands surrounding Whole Cake Island as part of the Big Mom Pirates ' main territory known as Totto Land . It is run by Charlotte Broyé , the Minister of Meringue ( メレンゲ大臣 , Merenge Daijin ) .

Muringe Town

Muringe Town ( ムレンゲタウン , Muringe Taun ) is a city situated on the island.

History

Past

Less than 62 years ago, Milenge Island became part of the Big Mom Pirates territory also known as " Totto Land ", and its appearance was changed to its current look using Charlotte Linlin and Streusen 's Devil Fruits (the Soru Soru no Mi and Kuku Kuku no Mi respectively).

Charlotte Broyé was then appointed the minister in charge of this island.

Trivia

  • Milenge Island, Muringe Town and Broyé's title as Minister of Meringue are all puns on meringues.
