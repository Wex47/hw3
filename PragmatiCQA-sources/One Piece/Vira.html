General Information

Vira is a small country in comparison to Arabasta .

Around four hundred years ago, in 1122 , it was described as a sunny town.

History

Four hundred years ago, Mont Blanc Noland arrived at Vira when it was still a small town. He then departed from there on June 21, 1122.

Over the past few hundred years, Vira expanded into a country, but it was no longer the merry town it used to be. By as early as two years ago, Vira became a dangerous country in the midst of rebellion.

Two years ago, a coup d'état incited by the Revolutionary Army occurred in Vira.

Translation and Dub Issues

Vira has also been spelled as Villa by some scanlators and translators.
