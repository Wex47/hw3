The Four Emperors Saga ( 四皇編 , Yonkō Hen ) is the third saga of the second half of the anime and the manga series, One Piece, and is the ninth saga overall.

The Ninja-Pirate-Mink-Samurai Alliance is formed to take down Kaidou of the Four Emperors and the shogun of Wano Country , and Luffy becomes entangled with another Emperor, Big Mom , as he seeks to retrieve his crewmate Sanji from her plans. Meanwhile, the royalty of the World Government gathers for the Levely , and the Revolutionary Army makes their move, leading to a huge change in the world's powers.
