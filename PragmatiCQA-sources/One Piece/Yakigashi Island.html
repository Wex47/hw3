Yakigashi Island is one of 34 islands surrounding Whole Cake Island as part of the Big Mom Pirates ' main territory known as Totto Land . It is run by Charlotte Oven , the Minister of Browned Food ( こんがり大臣 , Kongari Daijin ) .

Layout

Fukkura Town

Fukkura Town ( ふっくらタウン , Fukkura Taun , Viz: "Puffed Town") is a city situated on Yakigashi Island. Fukkura ( ふっくら , Fukkura ) can mean "plump" or "fluffy".

History

Past

Less than 62 years ago, Yakigashi Island became part of the Big Mom Pirates territory also known as " Totto Land ", and its appearance was changed to its current look using Charlotte Linlin and Streusen 's Devil Fruits (the Soru Soru no Mi and Kuku Kuku no Mi respectively).

Charlotte Oven was then appointed the minister in charge of this island.

Trivia

  • Yakigashi ( 焼き菓子 , Yakigashi ) means "baked goods" in Japanese.
