One Piece: Loguetown Arc ( ONE PIECE ローグタウン編 , Wan Pīsu Rōgutaun Hen ) is the first of the series' tie-in books , a prose adaptation of the Loguetown Arc . It was written by Tatsuya Hamazaki, and released on July 17, 2000 in Japan.

It contains Eiichiro Oda 's unpublished story elements which he did not have time to show in the manga (and later used in the anime as fillers).

Summary

While the novel primarily retells the Loguetown Arc (with several expansions and minor changes), it also draws material from every previous arc of the East Blue Saga , mainly for exposition on the Straw Hat Pirates ' backgrounds.
