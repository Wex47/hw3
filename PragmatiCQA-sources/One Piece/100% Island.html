100% Island is one of 34 islands surrounding Whole Cake Island as part of Big Mom 's territory, Totto Land . It is run by Charlotte Smoothie , the Minister of Juice ( ジュース大臣 , Jūsu Daijin ) .

Layout

Kaju Town

Kaju Town ( カジュータウン , Kajū Taun ) is a city situated on the island. Kajū (果汁) means "fruit juice" in Japanese.

History

Past

Less than 62 years ago, 100% Island became part of the Big Mom Pirates ' territory Totto Land , and Charlotte Smoothie was appointed the minister in charge of this island.

Trivia

  • The name of the island is a possible reference to 100% juice or pure fruit juices, those which do not have artificial additives.
