One Piece Color Walk 3 Lion is a book that contains, in particularly, the Color panels between Chapter 175 and Chapter 284 . They are displayed in order of appearance from April 16, 2001 to August 4, 2003.

It also contains some sketches of the Skypiea Arc .

Content

  • Color works between 2001-2003
    • Color spreads between chapter 175 and chapter 284 .
    • Manga covers between Volume 20 - Volume 30 .
  • Monochrome Talk: interview with Eiichiro Oda and Yoshitaka Amano ( Final Fantasy ).
