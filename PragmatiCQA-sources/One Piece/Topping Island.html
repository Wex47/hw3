Topping Island is one of 34 islands surrounding Whole Cake Island as part of the Big Mom Pirates ' main territory known as Totto Land . It is run by Charlotte Tablet , the Minister of Toppings ( トッピング大臣 , Toppingu Daijin ) .

Layout

Deco Town

Deco Town ( デコタウン , Deko Taun ) is a city situated on the island. Deco likely comes from "decoration".

History

Past

Less than 62 years ago, Topping Island became part of the Big Mom Pirates territory also known as " Totto Land ", and its appearance was changed to its current look using Charlotte Linlin 's Soru Soru no Mi and Streusen 's Kuku Kuku no Mi .

Charlotte Tablet was then appointed the minister in charge of this island.
