Bleach: Official Animation Book VIBEs was released along with the Bleach: Official Character Book SOULs . The book is about the anime series and contains screenshots from episodes 1 to 63, character designs, a short story about Rukia Kuchiki going to the Human World , as well as interviews.

Trivia

  • 0.side-B the rotator was originally published in this databook but was later included in Volume 23: MALA SUERTE! .
  • VIBEs came out at the same time as Bleach: Official Character Book SOULs in Japan, but only SOULs has been released in English.
