The Soul King (霊王, Reiō ) is the creator of Soul Society , the Human World and Hueco Mundo , and served as the very linchpin that sustains the three worlds until he was absorbed by his son, Yhwach . The Soul King is the very source of the power used by the Quincy . Likewise, his severed body fragments are what allow Humans to become Fullbringers .

Personality

A highly enigmatic being, very little is known about the Soul King's personality and motivations, even to the likes of Ichibē Hyōsube and Yhwach. Indeed, despite fully having the power to do so, the Soul King strangely never resisted his sealing and subsequent mutilation, much to the confusion of the culprits. Presumably, this is due to the Soul King's ability to see the future to an extent nobody else could.

Regardless, the Soul King seemed to greatly care about the original inhabitants of his world, killing countless Hollows to protect them regardless of the consequences.

History

Bleach: Can't Fear Your Own World

Over a million years prior to his ultimate demise, the being that became known as the Soul King emerged from a chaotic, primordial world overrun by Hollows . To protect the original inhabitants of that world, the Soul King proceeded to annihilate all the Hollows that preyed upon them. However, these actions eventually started to destabilize the world.

In response, five powerful beings from that time decided to work together to fix everything, and for their own respective reasons, sealed an unresisting Soul King inside a crystal and used his omnipotent powers to split their world into three new ones: Soul Society , Hueco Mundo , and the Human World , and in the process created the cycle of life and death.

Despite the Soul King's lack of resistance, the five beings were unconvinced of the Soul King's cooperation and feared his immense power, and proceeded to first tear off the Right Arm of Stillness and the Left Arm of Progress , before taking their time to carve out his heart , whittle away his legs, and mutilate and tear out his organs, leaving the Soul King trapped in an agonizing state between life and death for millions of years.

Powers & Abilities

Immeasurable Spiritual Power : Being the progenitor of the Quincy, Shinigami, and Fullbringers, the Soul King is stated to be immensely powerful; even the likes of Ichibē Hyōsube admits that the Soul King was a nigh-omnipotent being with no equals. The Soul King's power allowed him to effortlessly destroy numerous Menos -class Hollows, reducing them to mere sand.

The Almighty (全知全能 (ジ・オールマイティ), Ji Ōrumaiti ; Japanese for "All-Knowing and All-Powerful" ): The Soul King's signature power, which granted him nigh-omnipotence and nigh-omniscience, allowing the Soul King to see all futures. Ichibē Hyōsube speculates that the Soul King had foreseen everything that happened up until his death and even allowed for it to happen.

  • World Splitting : Through the Soul King's Almighty powers, the original world that existed millions of years before his death was split into three new ones and the cycle of life and death was created. Had Yhwach been victorious in the Quincy Blood War , he would have merged them back together with the same power.

Undying State : The Soul King is a being that was stated to be "neither alive nor dead", and existed in a state between Stagnation and Progress. Due to this, the Soul King could not die even while his body was mutilated and sealed away by the Five Ancestors. After Yhwach fully assimilated the Soul King and was later cut down by Ichigo Kurosaki , the Quincy 's corpse remained in the same undead state, allowing The Almighty to keep maintaining the three worlds.

Ōken Bestowment : The ability to turn the bones of other beings into an Ōken . Ichibē Hyōsube believes that the Soul King's very will exists, and can be felt, in every Ōken, citing his own Ōken as proof.

Detached Body Fragments

When the Soul King was sealed and dismembered by the Five Ancestors, numerous parts of his body were separated and ended up in various parts of the three worlds. Some of these smaller fragments ended up in the Souls of Humans , granting them the potential to become Fullbringers ; while other, larger parts attained sentience of their own.

  • The Right Arm of the Soul King - Ended up in Soul Society where it was worshiped as a deity in the Rukongai . Eventually bonded with Jūshirō Ukitake .
  • The Left Arm of the Soul King - Ended up in Soul Society. Eventually returned to the Quincy and came to serve Yhwach.
  • The Heart of the Soul King - Returned to the Quincy and bonded with the Sternritter Gerard Valkyrie .
  • The Saketsu (鎖結; Japanese for "Binding Chain" ) of the Soul King - Eventually bonded with the Fullbringer Aura Michibane .
  • The "Nails" (爪; Japanese for "paws" ) of the Soul King - Various fragments that bonded with Humans, turning them into Fullbringers. Most of them were collected and used by Sōsuke Aizen to feed his incomplete Hōgyoku , or by Tokinada Tsunayashiro to aid in the creation of Hikone Ubuginu . Rangiku Matsumoto was bonded to one such fragment until it was forcibly extracted by Aizen; this was the inciting force for Gin Ichimaru to eventually betray Aizen.

Trivia

  • Although never outright confirmed, it is repeatedly implied to varying degrees in Bleach: Can't Fear Your Own World that Gremmy Thoumeaux may have been an aspect of the Soul King, like Gerard Valkyrie or Pernida Parnkgjas were.
  • Tokinada Tsunayashiro calls the Soul King a "sacrificial lamb" that was "both a devil and a savior".
  • The Soul King was the first being to wield the powers of Quincy , Shinigami , and Fullbringers .
