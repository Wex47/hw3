Keikaigi (繫界儀, World-Tying Rite ) is a Kidō spell.

Description

Keikaigi connects the space tying the Human World and Hueco Mundo by creating portal to the Garganta . The opening is generated between two large wooden posts jutting out from two large rock formations.
