Maki Ichinose (一之瀬 真樹, Ichinose Maki ) is a Shinigami ally of Jin Kariya and a former member of the 11th Division under the former captain, Kenpachi Kiganjō .

Appearance

Maki is a tall, fair-skinned, lean-built man with brown eyes, sharp eyebrows, and short, shaggy raven-black hair that frames his face and arches upward on either temple to hang down in the center of his forehead in a large bang. He wears a shihakushō from his days in the 11th Division that he later removed the sleeves from, as well as a white hooded shirt underneath.

Personality

A generally stoic and reserved man, at his core, Ichinose believes strongly in justice and the duty of those with great power to use it to help others instead of pursuing their own selfish desires, a principle instilled in him by his captain, Kenpachi Kiganjō . When Kiganjō was killed by Kenpachi Zaraki in the latter's trial to become the new captain of the Eleventh Division , a distraught Ichinose refused to accept the results and was outraged at the meaninglessness of a battle between two individuals who only sought strength, which only intensified when he later challenged Kenpachi to a duel and failed to even scratch him before Kenpachi disarmed him with a single slash, leaving Ichinose with a deep grudge against Kenpachi and a feeling that he had died at that moment which ultimately caused him to leave the Gotei 13 altogether due to his belief that his captain would always be Kiganjō, followed by him accepting the idea of death until a fierce encounter with a Hollow made him realize he did not want to die. By Kenpachi's analysis, Ichinose is like ivy that cannot live without clinging to trees in that he cannot stand on his own and needs someone else to give him purpose, evidenced by how he became fanatically loyal to Jin Kariya after the latter saved his life and gave him a new ideal to believe in, namely that purging the Shinigami from Soul Society would lead to justice returning to the realm.

Despite his vendetta against Kenpachi and his devotion to Kariya blinding him to his original ideals, Ichinose retains some of his morals; though he initially planned to die in the Human World after leaving Soul Society, upon being alerted to Hollows attacking several Souls in the desert village near him, Ichinose immediately leapt into action to protect a young Plus with what little strength he had left. While ruthless, pragmatic, and confident in combat, Ichinose is not above commending his opponents when impressed by their powers and techniques, and after learning that Rukia Kuchiki was a Shinigami like him, he paused their battle to answer her questions about why he was aiding the Bount . He is also polite to the Bount around him, sharing something akin to friendship with Gō Koga especially, and not only sympathizes with their plight and even considers himself part of their tribe despite not being a Bount, but would treat their injuries before their Bitto empowerment and subsequent Reishi -absorption healing, though he has no compunctions about knocking them out through force in order to protect them from further harm or killing any who turn against Kariya.

Ultimately, Ichinose is closest to Kariya and acts as his right-hand man, willing to follow any order given to him and even act in his stead, but begins acting distracted upon entering Soul Society with the Bount, and when given an opportunity to finally take revenge on Kenpachi at the cost of allowing Ichigo Kurosaki and his friends to pursue Kariya in direct violation of the last order given to him by the latter, Ichinose puts his own goals above Kariya's and chooses to fight Kenpachi while Ichigo's group escapes, where he firmly blames Kenpachi as representing the injustice of Soul Society which Kariya is combatting and unleashes all the wrath that had built up within him; however, Kenpachi exposes Ichinose's reliance on abstract ideals and lack of agency when he points out that the latter's stated desire to wipe away all shadows with his light is not even possible and questions if any of his supposedly noble goals are not just tasks assigned to him by someone else. Through this, Ichinose remembers his desire to improve the Gotei 13 and the Seireitei by even the slimmest margin, and after learning from a newly- empowered Kariya that all of the other Bount were dead, Ichinose stops fighting Ichigo and takes it upon himself to urge Kariya to use his new power to rebuild the Seireitei, having learned from experience that nothing beneficial can come from seeking revenge and seeing himself as the only one who can stop Kariya from going down the same path.

History

Decades ago, Ichinose was a member of the Eleventh Division under Captain Kenpachi Kiganjō . When Kiganjō was challenged to a duel in front of 200 members of the division by Kenpachi Zaraki for the captaincy, Ichinose could not prevent the fight from happening and discussed his unease about Kenpachi with Kaname Tōsen , only to be shocked and distraught when Kenpachi succeeded in killing Kiganjō and took his haori , leading Ichinose to almost attack him right there before his comrades restrained him. Some time later, Ichinose challenged Kenpachi to a duel, but was unable to even scratch him and was left humiliated, leading him to eventually leave the division and the Gotei 13 altogether.

Following this, Ichinose traveled to the Human World without permission, knowing that he could never return to Soul Society , and found himself wandering through a desert area in exhaustion as he contemplated joining Kiganjō in death. Suddenly, Ichinose saw Hollows attacking a group of Souls in a nearby town, and when one of them cornered a young Plus , Ichinose used the last of his strength to engage the Hollow, though his weakened condition led to him being overwhelmed. However, just as the Hollow was moving to kill Ichinose, Jin Kariya intervened and held the Hollow back with a single finger, allowing Ichinose to get up with Gō Koga 's help and purify it prior to joining Kariya on his mission.

Plot

Bount arc (anime only)

Note: Events occurring in this arc are only in the anime and do not constitute canon material.

After Kariya calls together all the remaining Bount , Ichinose sits in a lamp-lit room in the Bount Mansion at nighttime and quietly reads a book, where he is joined by Gō Koga . Amusing Koga with his mention of being able to read in such poor lighting, Ichinose instructs him to let his clothes dry from the rain outside and promises to serve tea at 10 P.M. Later, Ichinose greets a returning Kariya alongside the rest of the Bount and downplays Kariya's subsequent praise of his recent work. The next day, Ichinose listens to Kariya's promise that the Bount can finally leave the shadows of history and discusses Kariya's intentions for Uryū Ishida with Ryō Utagawa .

The following day, when Ichigo Kurosaki and his friends break into the Bount Mansion to rescue Uryū, whom the Bount have kidnapped, Rukia Kuchiki runs off with Ririn , Kurōdo , and Noba to find him, only to be confronted by Ichinose in a dining area. As he reveals to Rukia that he is a former member of the Eleventh Division , Ichinose catches Kurōdo trying to sneak up on him using Ririn's Illusions and incapacitates him with a stab to the stomach before dispatching Ririn and Noba when they attack him as well. With Rukia unable to harm him with her Kidō , Ichinose reveals he is helping Kariya because Kenpachi Zaraki destroyed the ideal he and Kenpachi Kiganjō once held.

Soon afterward, Ichinose senses Ichigo fighting Utagawa and is astonished by his power, leading him to run off out of concern for Kariya. Upon reaching the main foyer of the mansion, Ichinose finds Utagawa attempting to usurp Kariya as the leader of the Bount and disorients him with Blinding Light before stabbing him in the chest with the invisible blade of Nijigasumi and leaving Utagawa's body to fall into a chasm left by Ichigo, whom he prepares to fight. Ichinose quickly overwhelms Ichigo with his rapid and skilled attacks, but is asked to step aside by Kariya, who has become interested in devouring Ichigo's Soul and wishes to fight him personally.

Though Kariya easily overpowers and brutalizes Ichigo, the latter refuses to give up, which leads Ichinose to decide to finish Ichigo off himself with permission from Kariya. However, his attempt to do so is interrupted when Ururu Tsumugiya opens fire on the Bount Mansion with her Senren Bakusatsu Taihō , giving Ichigo and his friends an opportunity to rescue Uryū and Yoshino Sōma before escaping while Ichinose shields Kariya from the falling rubble. In the aftermath, Ichinose stands outside the mansion with the Bount and watches as the residence burns to the ground, and despite Ichinose's concern over the impact of this, he is shocked by Kariya's assurance that Uryū and Yoshino will return to him.

Following this, Ichinose stands guard while Kariya finds a broken grandfather clock in the burnt wreckage and repairs it with a set of tools, which leads Ichinose to interpret the stopped time on the clock as a metaphor for the existence of the Bount and vow that they will move this motionless time to change fate themselves. When Yoshino returns to the mansion to begin fighting Kariya and is followed by Ichigo and 6th Division Lieutenant Renji Abarai , Ichinose confronts them alongside Koga and begins fighting Ichigo to prevent him from interfering in Kariya's ritual. Once Kariya sacrifices Yoshino to create the Bitto , a grinning Ichinose leaves the area alongside the Bount.

Some time later, Ichinose and the Bount regroup with Kariya in a large, elaborate cave system on the outskirts of Karakura Town , where he listens as Kariya explains his plan to massively boost the power of the Bount by having the Bitto harvest and refine living Souls for them to drink. With Kariya violently quelling Mabashi's dissent at the idea of violating the one law of the Bount like this, Ichinose watches the other Bount drink the refined Souls provided by the Bitto and be greatly empowered by them, where he offers to tend to Mabashi's injuries, only for Kariya to assert they will be healed in an instant and force-feed the serum to a still-reluctant Mabashi while Ichinose observes.

That night, Mabashi slips out of the caves while Ichinose is not looking, but when he reports this to Kariya and apologizes, Ichinose is assured that this is alright because it gives Mabashi the opportunity to test his new power regardless of whether or not he goes berserk in the process. After Kariya decides to retire for the night and leaves responsibilities to him, Ichinose intervenes in the fight between Mabashi and 9th Division Lieutenant Shūhei Hisagi by intercepting Hisagi's strike and simultaneously knocking out Mabashi with a blow to the chest using his sheath prior to departing, which leaves Hisagi to recognize him and realize the rumors about him joining the Bount were true.

Shortly afterward, Ichinose enters the Seireitei through the central Senkaimon and creates a distraction by attacking 11th Division 3rd Seat Ikkaku Madarame and his subordinates while invisible , creating an opportunity for him to sneak into the SRDI and erase the data on the Quincy Bangle from 12th Division Captain Mayuri Kurotsuchi so the latter will learn about it upon restoring the data later. The next day, Ichinose joins Kariya and the other Bount in the central chamber of the cave system, where they learn that Uryū has yet to regain his Quincy powers and Ichinose informs Kariya that the Gotei 13 will be making a move soon.

Once Ichigo makes it past Ugaki and to the central chamber, Kariya confronts him while Koga summons an intricate Senkaimon with a lengthy incantation and Ichinose offers to take over the process, only for them both to be shocked when Kariya decides to reveal his plan to an incapacitated Ichigo. Upon seeing Ichigo recover and begin fighting Kariya equally using his Bankai , Tensa Zangetsu , Ichinose is concerned for him, but is instructed to continue preparing the Senkaimon by Koga. After Koga further alters the portal and Uryū gives it Reishi -conversion properties with a Heilig Pfeil , Ichinose departs for Soul Society with the Bount.

The day after the Bount arrive in the Rukongai , Ichinose and Koga accompany Kariya to a gambling hall in the Kusajishi district, where they attempt to defuse his ensuing brawl with the thugs inside and end up fighting them as well. Once all the thugs are dispatched, Kariya convinces Tōba , the hall's bookie and a local criminal leader, to assist in his plan to turn Soul Society upside-down, and after Tōba sets them up in a cottage, Ichinose and Koga see Kariya leave to confront 6th Division Captain Byakuya Kuchiki . That night, Ichinose and Koga hold a town hall with the people of Kusajishi and convince them they will have invincible allies in the Bount.

Over the course of the night, Ichinose and Tōba watch as more and more denizens of Kusajishi respond to Kariya's recruitment call, leading Ichinose to observe that many people are discontented with the state of Soul Society. When Kariya returns, Ichinose greets him with an army of villagers and relays Mabashi and Yoshi watching the actions of the Seireitei to him. With the villagers assembling large siege engines out of the surrounding trees, Ichinose learns of 10th Division Captain Tōshirō Hitsugaya leading a strike force toward them and is granted permission by Kariya to stay behind and engage them in order to buy time, which he does while the Bount and the villagers make their way to the Seireiheki .

Once Ichigo and his friends join Hitsugaya's group, Ichinose traps them all in his Brilliant Void and prepares to take them out one at a time while manipulating light to make them see him in place of their allies and fight among themselves. Suddenly, Kenpachi, having heard of Hitsugaya locating Ichinose, intervenes and confronts Ichinose with a dare to fight him instead of impeding Ichigo and his friends any further. Unwilling to pass up this opportunity, Ichinose allows Ichigo's group to run back to the Seireitei and begins fighting Kenpachi, who sees through his invisibility and effortlessly withstands the Blinding Light attack of Nijigasumi , forcing Ichinose to initiate Saigyoku Nijigasumi .

Though Ichinose manages to briefly match Kenpachi and trap him in a compressing sphere of light using this technique, he is astonished when Kenpachi breaks free by simply removing his eyepatch and destroying the sphere with his unleashed Reiatsu . Furious at Kenpachi having so much power and being a captain when he has no honor, Ichinose begins wildly attacking him, but Kenpachi incapacitates him with a slash to the chest and points out Ichinose's lack of agency in blindly following Kariya's orders instead of standing on his own two feet. After realizing that his desire for revenge outweighs his loyalty to Kariya, Ichinose attacks Kenpachi one last time with all of his strength and is cut down.

However, Ichinose survives this, albeit with severe injuries, and makes his way to the top of Sōkyoku Hill the next day, where he finds Ichigo fighting Kariya after the latter has absorbed the power of a Jōkaishō . Ichinose learns from Kariya that all the other Bount are dead when he gets involved, and with Ichigo easily overpowering him due to his exhaustion, Ichinose turns on Kariya and demands that he use his new power to restore the Seireitei instead of pursuing revenge, but Kariya declines and kills Ichinose when the latter tries to attack him. Afterward, Ichinose's broken Zanpakutō manages to redirect a bolt of lightning from Kariya away from Ichigo before disintegrating.

Powers & Abilities

Great Spiritual Power : Ichinose's Reiryoku is strong enough for him to not only clash evenly with, but frequently overwhelm a Shikai -wielding Ichigo Kurosaki , as well as wound an eyepatch-wearing Captain Kenpachi Zaraki , though he can only withstand direct clashes with the latter while using Saigyoku Nijigasumi . His Reiatsu is uniquely multicolored and will shift between hues while he is exerting it.

Expert Swordsman : After decades of constant training, Ichinose can more than hold his own against Ichigo, whom he frequently outmaneuvered in their first battle; Captain Tōshirō Hitsugaya , who acknowledged his visible skill after only a short skirmish; and even Kenpachi, though he ultimately could not match the latter's strength and Reiatsu.

Hakuda Practitioner : Ichinose possesses some hand-to-hand skill, having knocked out three thugs in Kusajishi with a single strike of his closed fist.

Shunpo Expert : Ichinose can move at considerably high speeds, allowing him to match and outpace Ichigo during their first duel and later keep up with him while preventing him from interfering in Jin Kariya 's duel with Yoshino Sōma as well as effortlessly dodge attacks from several residents of Kusajishi at once and keep Kenpachi on the defensive during their duel with his rapid movements.

Kidō Expert : Though he does not commonly use Kidō , Ichinose is proficient enough in the art to specially alter a Senkaimon so it could be further modified by a Bount and a Quincy 's Heilig Pfeil to allow Bount to enter Soul Society via Reishi conversion, a rare and potent feat, as well as fire a Hadō #31. Shakkahō without reciting its incantation or even saying its name.

Enhanced Strength : Ichinose possesses surprising strength for his build, having not only casually blocked a Hadō #33. Sōkatsui from Rukia Kuchiki with only his sealed Zanpakutō unharmed and incapacitated Mabashi with a blow to the stomach using the sheath of his Zanpakutō , but later knocked out three thugs from Kusajishi with only a blow from his fist and withstood strikes from the notoriously strong Kenpachi.

Highly Perceptive Combatant : Ichinose is an analytical fighter; when faced with Rukia, Ririn , Kurōdo , and Noba , four individuals he had never encountered before, Ichinose first detected Kurōdo sneaking up on him while being obscured by Ririn's Illusions and stabbed him while he was still invisible, then quickly saw through Ririn turning the area around him into a lush jungle in order to attack both her and Noba before detailing their strategy and deducing from Rukia's subsequent Eishōhaki variant of Hadō #33. Sōkatsui , which was powerful despite automatically being weaker than a usage with the spell's incantation, that she did not have access to her true level of power, something Rukia herself internally confirmed. He also realized that Ichigo was dodging his invisible attacks with Nijigasumi not by seeing them, but by instead instinctually reacting to them, and that Ichigo using high-density Reiatsu to escape his Blinding Light took a toll on his body.

Zanpakutō

Nijigasumi (虹霞, Rainbow Mist ): A standard katana with a simple, blue-gray rectangular guard inscribed with a circle around the blade and two inward-curving lines stretching to each corner along the long sides.

  • Shikai : Its release command is Flicker with Splendor (光華閃け, Kōka Hirameke ; Flash and Burst in the English Dub).
  • Bankai : Not Achieved.
