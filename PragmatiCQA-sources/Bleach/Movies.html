There are currently 5 films based on the Bleach series.

Bleach Movies
Title Release Date Description Poster
Bleach: Memories of Nobody December 16, 2006 Set after the events of the Soul Society arc with Ichigo back in the Human World fending off Hollows . The movie is centered around Senna and the activities of a strange group called the " Dark Ones ," who are trying to destroy both Soul Society and the Human World.
Bleach: The DiamondDust Rebellion December 22, 2007 Centers around 10th Division Captain Tōshirō Hitsugaya , who faces severe consequences after his Division loses artefact they were escorting: "the Ōin ". Faced with execution by Soul Society, Hitsugaya goes rogue while a Shinigami linked to his past surfaces in Soul Society.
Bleach: Fade to Black December 13, 2008 Rukia Kuchiki is erased from the memories of Soul Society's citizens, leaving them hostile to Ichigo and Rukia. Unaware of what has happened, they must discover the source of the amnesia and unravel the mystery of Dark Rukia .
Bleach: The Hell Verse December 4, 2010 The movie centers around Ichigo and his friends entering Hell with the assistance of a man named Kokutō to rescue Yuzu , who has been kidnapped. However, while in Hell, Ichigo's Hollow powers prove to be problematic. Tite Kubo stated that he was involved from the planning and scenario stage.
Bleach (live-action film) July 20th 2018 A live action film adaptation of Bleach's first story arc, covering Ichigo's service as a Substitute Shinigami until Rukia's arrest. It is directed by Shinsuke Sato and stars Sōta Fukushi.
