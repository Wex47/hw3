Bleach: Heat the Soul (BLEACH: ヒート・ザ・ソウル) is a video game for the PlayStation Portable Bleach: Heat the Soul series .

It is the first Bleach game and has only been released in Japan. The theme song for the game is *~Asterisk~ by Orange Range , which was also the first opening theme for the anime.
