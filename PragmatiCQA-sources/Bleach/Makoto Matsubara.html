Makoto Matsubara (松原真琴) is a Japanese writer who has authored a number of novels set in the universe of Tite Kubo 's Bleach manga.

Biography

Matsubara was born on the 19th of July 1980 and is married to comedy manga-ka Jigoku no Misawa. Matsubara began writing novels for the Bleach franchise in 2006. Her Bleach books have only been released in Japanese.

Works

  • Soshite Ryuuta wa Nya to Naku (2002)
  • Bleach: The Honey Dish Rhapsody (2006)
  • Bleach: Letters From The Other Side (2009)
  • Nestle to Night ( Official Character Book 3 UNMASKED , 2011)
  • Bleach: The Death Save The Strawberry (2012)
  • Bleach: WE DO knot ALWAYS LOVE YOU (2016).
