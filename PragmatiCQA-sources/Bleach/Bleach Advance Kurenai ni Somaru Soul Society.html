Bleach Advance: Kurenai ni Somaru Soul Society (アドバンス 紅に染まる尸魂界) is a video game published by Sega for Game Boy Advance , based on the Bleach manga and anime series. It was released on July 21, 2005 in Japan.

Gameplay

This is a fighting game and features both a single player story mode and a survival mode.
