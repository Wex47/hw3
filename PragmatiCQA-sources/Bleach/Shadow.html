Shadows (影, Kage ) is a teleportation skill used by Quincy , notably members of the Wandenreich .

Description

The user's shadow expands and becomes a doorway between dimensions, including but not limited to the Schatten Bereich , the Soul King Palace , and even one's inner world , and the user's Reiatsu instantly disappears from the area of departure. The size of these portals can manipulated via gestures, and can become big enough to allow large groups of Soldat to pass through; the larger shadows tend to take the form of a Quincy cross. The technique requires shadows; therefore, it cannot be used in a room where there are no shadows. Individuals are still able to use their abilities even if they're within the shadows, such as when Nianzol Weizol was able to use his The Wind to protect Yhwach .
