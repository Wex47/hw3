Jigokuchō (地獄蝶, Hell Butterfly ) are black butterflies used by Shinigami to serve a variety of purposes.

Overview

The bodies of Jigokuchō are completely black, while the wings are purple with a black outline. They guide the Shinigami through the Senkaimon when traveling between Soul Society and the Human World , thereby saving them from the inconvenience of passing through the Dangai . Jigokuchō are also used as messengers among Shinigami, often carrying important orders. Hell Butterflies are taken care of by Shinigami in Seireitei , this task usually being carried out by new and inexperienced members of the Gotei 13 . Jigokuchō, like the Shinigami themselves, are only visible to spiritually aware beings.
