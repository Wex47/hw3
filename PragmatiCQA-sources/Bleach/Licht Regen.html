Licht Regen (光の雨 (リヒト・レーゲン), rihito rēgen ; German for " Light Rain ", Japanese for " Rain of Light ") is a technique used by the Quincy , Uryū Ishida .

Description

After amassing a large quantity of Reishi from his surroundings along his left arm to well beyond his shoulder, Uryū unleashes a devastating volley of innumerable arrows upon a single target.

After joining the Wandenreich , Uryū no longer needs to visibly collect Reishi along his left shoulder in preparation for using this technique, and can unleash a barrage of Heilig Pfeil upon his target near-instantaneously.
