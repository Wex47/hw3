The Kurosaki Clinic (クロサキ医院, Kurosaki Iin ) is a privately owned medical clinic in Karakura Town , as well as home to the Kurosaki Family .

Overview

This clinic is run by Isshin Kurosaki and is assisted by his two daughters ( Yuzu Kurosaki and Karin Kurosaki ) as acting nurses, as well as sometimes by his son, Ichigo Kurosaki . Isshin is capable of handling anything at his clinic except for major surgeries. Orihime's brother, Sora Inoue , died at the clinic.
