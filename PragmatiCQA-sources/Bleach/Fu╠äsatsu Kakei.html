Fūsatsu Kakei (封殺火刑, Seal Slaying-Fire Penance ) is a Kidō seal created by Kisuke Urahara specifically for use against Sōsuke Aizen .

Description

Appearing as glowing yellow-golden rings around the wrists of the opponent, this spell blocks off the Reiatsu vents located in the wrists of all Shinigami , resulting in the opponent being incinerated from the inside out by their own Reiatsu.
