Hooleer (フーラー, Fūrā ) is a large Hollow in the service of Sōsuke Aizen .

Appearance

Hooleer has a bulbous body, long tufts of hair on its back, and a single, large eye which is purple inside the Garganta and yellow outside of it. Its hair is to be the same or a similar color to its skin. It has Gillian masks for fingernails.

Plot

Soul Society arc

Hooleer is present when Sōsuke Aizen , Gin Ichimaru , and Kaname Tōsen are being extracted from Sōkyoku Hill via Negación . It remains in the shadows of the Garganta , behind the mass of Gillians that await the three captains.

Fake Karakura Town arc

Wonderweiss Margela arrives in the Human World during the Gotei 13 's battle with the Espada , bringing Hooleer with him. It dwarfs the other combatants in terms of size. While Wonderweiss attacks Jūshirō Ukitake , Hooleer uses its breath to extinguish the fire prison which is trapping Aizen, Gin, and Tōsen, freeing them. After Wonderweiss screams, Hooleer releases Gillians from its mouth to attack the Visored . After the Visored kill the last Gillian, Mashiro Kuna attacks it with her Mashiro Super Kick , killing it, as Wonderweiss stares. Following Mashiro's attack, Gin comments on how easily she took out Hooleer even though it was Wonderweiss's favorite.

Powers & Abilities

Regüeldo (レグウェルド, Reguwerudo ; Spanish for "Belch/Eruction"): Hooleer's breath is powerful enough to extinguish the prison of fire made by Genryūsai Shigekuni Yamamoto 's Ryūjin Jakka . Kaname Tōsen stated that its breath " smells of death ."

Menos Generation : It can spawn numerous Gillian-class Menos from its mouth. It looks like a mass of black liquid and masks until the Gillian Menos separate into their individual forms.

Appearances in other Media

Hooleer appears as one of Aizen's special moves in Bleach: Blade Battlers 2nd , where it spits out Gillians to attack.

Trivia

In the anime, Gin refers to it by the name Fura instead of Hooleer.
