Kan (環, Ring ) is the currency of Soul Society . It is the currency in which Shinigami salary is paid and all items in Soul Society are priced.

Contents

Appearance

The Kan coin is circular with a hole in the middle, resembling the ¥5 piece of the Japanese currency.

Known Items Costs

  • Seireitei Communication : A standard issue costs 380 Kan. Special issues costs vary but the special issue featuring the Ryoka Invasion cost 680 Kan.
  • Gin Tonbo Glasses: The known product line-up of Gin Tonbo Eye-wear store costs between 62,800 Kan to 97,300 Kan. It must be noted that Gin Tonbo products are considered expensive.
  • Shinigami Women's Association : Has a budget of 250,000 Kan. They also sell their Captain's Photo Collection books for 2,480 Kan each.
  • Shinigami Men's Association : Has a budget of 90,000 Kan.
  • Calligraphy Club , Ikebana Club , Tea Ceremony Club : They each have a budget of 150,000 Kan.
  • Calendars featuring Gotei 13 Shinigami: The Purgatory Company (煉獄商会, Rengoku Shōkai ) sells these calendars for 2,415 Kan each. Although the one featuring Rangiku Matsumoto costs 3,415 Kan.
  • Anti-Hollow Training Machine: The Purgatory Company sells these, which are essentially punching bags, for 19,800 Kan.
  • Set of weights: The Purgatory Company sells a set of two dumbbells for 2,980 Kan.
  • 4th Division Nurse Outfits: The Purgatory Company sells these, meant for cosplay, for 5,980 Kan.
