Ryūbi no Jōmon (竜尾の城門, The Gate of Dragon Tail ; Viz " Dragon Tail Castle Gate ") is a Kidō spell.

Description

Creates over a dozen pillars, which are arranged by hand gestures, making them form a gigantic shield or door-like barrier between the caster and the opponent. It can be used together with Kokō no Jōmon , Kigai no Jōmon , and Hōyoku no Jōmon to form Shijū no Saimon .
