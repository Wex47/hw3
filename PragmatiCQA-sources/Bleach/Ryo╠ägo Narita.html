Ryōgo Narita is a Japanese light novel author who has written two light novel series and one short story based on Tite Kubo 's Bleach manga. In addition to expanding the Bleach universe, Narita is also the author of several of his own light novel series.

Biography

Born in Tokyo, Japan on 30th May 1980, Ryōgo Narita is a professional light novel author. His works typically involve the supernatural or criminal worlds and are known for featuring vast, expansive casts of characters. Some of his works have been adapted into manga and anime format. Narita won the Gold Prize in the 9th Dengeki Novel Prize for Baccano!
