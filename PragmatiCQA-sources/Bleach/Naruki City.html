Naruki City (鳴木市, Naruki-shi ; lit. Echoing Tree City ) is a city which borders the west of Karakura Town .

Overview

Naruki City is under the jurisdiction of the 10th Division .

Xcution 's headquarters are located in the Chōbara district of Naruki City. Ichigo's friends Keigo Asano and Mizuiro Kojima also live in this city.

History

Over 20 years ago, Sōsuke Aizen and his accomplices conducted Hollowfication experiments in this city, killing many of the Shinigami stationed there. Due to their influence however, all traces of their involvement was covered up, which brought the 10th Division captain, Isshin Shiba , to intervene personally. His battle with White , the Hollow who was responsible for the murders there, eventually led to his encounter with the Quincy , Masaki Kurosaki .

At some point in time after these events, Kūgo Ginjō decided to create his base of operations in this city.
