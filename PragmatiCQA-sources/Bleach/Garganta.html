Garganta (黒腔 (ガルガンタ), Garuganta ; Spanish for " Throat ", Japanese for " Black Cavity ") is the void between worlds that is mainly used by Arrancar and Hollows to move between Hueco Mundo , the Human World , and Soul Society .

Description

The Garganta is a featureless black void connecting Hueco Mundo to other worlds. Due to its nature, the Garganta can be used to access any realm such as the Human World and Soul Society; it can also access the Dangai . A turbulence of Reishi blows within Garganta. To move through it, travelers must create footholds using the Reishi.

Access

There are multiple ways in which one can access the Garganta.

  • Kūmon (空門, Air Gate ): A spatial distortion which Gillians typically use to travel to the Human World.
  • Keikaigi (繫界儀, World-Tying Rite ): This technique connects the space tying two worlds together to open a portal to Garganta. The opening is generated between two large wooden posts jutting out from two large rock formations.
  • Descorrer (解空(デスコレール), Desukorēru ; Spanish for " Drawing Back/Opening ", Japanese for " Loosed Void "): A technique used by Espada -level Arrancar , and sometimes others, to open a portal to Garganta in order to travel between the Human World and Hueco Mundo .
  • 12th Division Captain Mayuri Kurotsuchi opened a portal in to the Garganta using a device he found while in Hueco Mundo. It required him and Nemu Kurotsuchi to work in conjunction with each other to initiate the effect.
