Hadō #99. Goryūtenmetsu (五龍転滅, Five Swirling Dragons of Destruction ; Viz " Five Dragon Destruction ") is a Kidō spell.

Description

Upon activation, energy ruptures a large area around the user, causing the ground to rise up in pillars, before rising into the air to form five enormous, light purple dragons above the user.

Incantation

Unknown.

Known Practitioners

  • Sōsuke Aizen
  • Kisuke Urahara

Appearances in Other Media

  • In Bleach: Can't Fear Your Own World , Shūhei Hisagi states that Goryūtenmetsu is a forbidden spell because it draws upon the Reimyaku of its surroundings to empower itself.
  • When Aizen uses it the dragons are light purple, but when Urahara uses it the dragons are dark red.
