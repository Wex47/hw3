Kigai no Jōmon (亀鎧の城門, The Gate of Turtle Shell ; Viz " Turtle Armor Castle Gate ") is a Kidō spell.

Description

Creates a barrier by combining many small hexagons to form a honeycomb-like structure. It can be used in conjunction with Ryūbi no Jōmon , Kokō no Jōmon , and Hōyoku no Jōmon to form Shijū no Saimon .
