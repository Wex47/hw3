Bleach JET is an artwork collection featuring all the Bleach manga art work by Tite Kubo over the course of the Bleach franchise serialization. It was released on December 4, 2018.

Contents

Split across two volumes, JET features an A4-sized collection of over 700 pieces of artwork that has featured across the entire serialization of Bleach, as well as a calendar and exclusive artwork created for JET.
