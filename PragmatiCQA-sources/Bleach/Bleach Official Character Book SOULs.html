Bleach: Official Character Book SOULs is the first databook to be released for the Bleach manga, recapping the story of Bleach from the start of series through to the end of the Soul Society Arc as well as providing a preview of the Arrancar Arc. Inside is additional information for key story points, especially about Soul Society and the Gotei 13 , as well as provided mini-profiles on the Shinigami encountered during the Soul Society Arc. In addition, the book contains previously unseen content in the form of Omake chapters.

Errors in vital statistics

There are a number of errors in vital statistics in SOULs for multiple characters. These statistics contradict information presented in earlier published character profiles within manga volumes and are subsequently corrected in the later published Bleach: Official Bootleg KaraBuri+ . The discrepancies included:

  • Byakuya Kuchiki's birthday
    • SOULs - August 31
    • Bootleg and Character Profile in Volume 9 - January 31
  • Kenpachi Zaraki's height
    • SOULs - 198 cm (6'6")
    • Bootleg and Character Profile in Volume 13 - 202 cm (6'7½")
  • Renji Abarai's birthday
    • SOULs - January 31
    • Bootleg and Character Profile in Volume 9 - August 31
