Bleach DS 4th: Flame Bringer (BLEACH DS 4th:フレイム･ブリンガ, Burīchi DS 4th: fureimu buringa ) is an Action game, developed and published by SEGA . It was released in Japan on August 6 2009 for Nintendo DS. The opening theme song for Flame Bringer is Shōjo S by Scandal .

Controls

  • B: Jump
  • Y: Attack
  • X: Shikai
  • Up+X: Bankai (then Down+X for Ichigo's Hollow form)
