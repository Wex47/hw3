Hadō #91. Senju Kōten Taihō (千手皎天汰炮, Thousand-Hand Incandent Heaven Culling-Sear ; Viz " Thousand-Hand Bright Heaven Cannon ") is a Kidō spell.

Description

Generating ten pink energy points around themselves, the practitioner fires them all at their target, resulting in an exceedingly devastating explosion.
