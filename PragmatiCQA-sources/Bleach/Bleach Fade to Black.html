Bleach: Fade to Black (BLEACH Fade to Black 君の名を呼ぶ, Burīchi: Fade to Black - Kimi no Na o Yobu ; Full Japanese title " Bleach Fade to Black, I Call Your Name ") is the third Bleach film. It was released in Japan on December 13, 2008, and on DVD on September 30, 2009. The film's screenplay was written by Natsuko Takahashi, who is a screenwriter for the anime series. The official film website gives the tagline of " Sayonara, Rukia ".

To promote the film, the opening and closing credits of the Bleach anime from Episodes 197-201 use footage from the film.

Plot Summary

Mayuri's Breakdown

In the Seireitei , as 12th Division Captain Mayuri Kurotsuchi presses dozens of keys, several gauges fill up and pipes expel steam around him. Continuing to type, Mayuri leans toward his computer as he notices something and puts his finger to the screen. Electricity crackles from the system as Mayuri turns around and watches a flower-like machine emerge from the ground and open to reveal a vial. Appearing next to the flower with Shunpo , Mayuri ecstatically states that he has completed it. While a laughing Mayuri picks up the vial, the power in the building suddenly cuts off.

Mayuri demands to know who is there and looks around, Shizuku appears behind him and slashes through him with a large scythe. As Mayuri stumbles, the vial drops from his hand and falls to the ground, losing its glow. Looking up, Mayuri sees the massive machine in his laboratory and wonders what it is. Calling out to him, 12th Division Lieutenant Nemu Kurotsuchi runs up to him and asks if everything is alright, only for a shocked Mayuri to demand to know who she is. Confused by this, Nemu begins to approach Mayuri, who tells her to stay back.

Claiming that Nemu came here under the impression that she would apprehend him, Mayuri runs out of the room as Nemu calls after him. As he runs through the hall, Mayuri trips and falls while Nemu asks him what is wrong, and he begins screaming in horror while telling her to stay away before running to his computer. Mayuri turns around and tells Nemu to stay away while Shizuku smiles with the mysterious vial glowing in his hand. Suddenly, the power comes back on, leading Mayuri to back away from the console as someone stares at him from an alcove.

As the system crackles with electricity, Mayuri grabs his Zanpakutō and releases its Shikai , Ashisogi Jizō , while Nemu appears behind him and grabs him. Nemu asks Mayuri he is doing, but Mayuri tells her to let him go and begins slashing at the air in front of him before breaking free and stabbing his Zanpakutō into his computer. Several loud pops are heard as a voice states abnormally high levels of Reiatsu have been confirmed inside building seven of the SRDI before detailing that destructive activity has been detected on the top floor and an extremely dense cloud of Reishi is currently spreading.

Dozens of Shinigami gather around the building as the voice requests emergency assistance from all of the available Shinigami of each division. Running up to 9th Division Lieutenant Shūhei Hisagi , 6th Division Lieutenant Renji Abarai asks him what this is all about. As Hisagi admits that he does not know, a white substance oozes from the clouds above and coalesces into a snake crackling with electricity, which attacks the Shinigami below. Several more snakes form and attack, creating blobs of white ooze everywhere they strike, as Hisagi tells the other Shinigami to retreat.

Renji wonders what the snakes are as the assembled Shinigami move away with Shunpo to dodge an attacking snake while 7th Division Lieutenant Tetsuzaemon Iba tells the others to get back. Destroying the head of one of the snakes, Iba continues to slash at the snake before he is enveloped by the ooze. As the Shinigami flee from the spreading ooze, several SRDI members surround Mayuri as one of them begs him to put down his sword. Mayuri tells them to not come near him and begins slashing at them while demanding to know what they are trying to do to him.

As Mayuri pants, 11th Division Captain Kenpachi Zaraki greets him from behind. Turning around, Mayuri slashes at Kenpachi, only for Kenpachi to block with his own Zanpakutō. Mayuri continues to attack Kenpachi, who admits that he always wondered when Mayuri would finally lose his mind, and screams before leaping back and activating his Bankai , Konjiki Ashisogi Jizō , which extends blades from its chest and attacks Kenpachi. Blocking this assault, Kenpachi is forced back before being pushed out of the building as several snakes approach him.

A grinning Kenpachi sees Konjiki Ashisogi Jizō become enveloped by the ooze and wonders what happened before seeing the snakes coming at him. Slashing them away, Kenpachi is dragged down by the encased Konjiki Ashisogi Jizō . The ooze swells up from where Konjiki Ashisogi Jizō hits the ground as Kenpachi lands and becomes stuck in the ooze. While expressing his annoyance at this, Kenpachi looks up as several snakes crash down on him. The ooze pours through the streets of the Seireitei as more snakes rise from it and crash into nearby areas, prompting the Shinigami to flee.

The snakes move toward the center of the Seireitei as Rukia Kuchiki runs down a set of stairs and wonders what is going on. Putting a piece of paper in her shihakushō , Rukia prepares to join the other Shinigami, only to suddenly hear Homura 's voice stating that she cannot go. As she turns around with her hand on her Zanpakutō, Rukia demands to know who is there and begins to draw her Zanpakutō. Homura rises up from the ledge in front of Rukia and floats in front of her while claiming that they came to erase her existence. As Rukia moves away, Shizuku appears and slashes through her when she turns around.

Elsewhere, as Captain-Commander Genryūsai Shigekuni Yamamoto , 4th Division Captain Retsu Unohana , 8th Division Captain Shunsui Kyōraku , 13th Division Captain Jūshirō Ukitake , 7th Division Captain Sajin Komamura , and their lieutenants and 3rd Seats someberly watch the ooze spread from the roof of a building, the snakes attack more Shinigami and destroys several buildings, which 6th Division Captain Byakuya Kuchiki watches as well. Homura and Shizuku appear on top of another building, with Shizuku carrying Rukia under one arm.

Crouching down, Homura asserts that it serves them right and that she hopes every Shinigami dies, prompting Shizuku to claim that all the Shinigami have ever done is take and all the two of them have ever done is lose as he drops Rukia, whose pupils have dilated. In the Human World , as Ichigo Kurosaki looks out of his window, he senses something and notes that something seems odd tonight. When he sees Kon looking at a piece of paper, Ichigo asks him what is so fascinating, leading Kon to reveal that he is reading a letter which Rukia left behind.

Rukia is Forgotten

As Kon admits that he has no idea what is written because it is in some kind of code, Ichigo takes the letter and decides that he will take a look. Observing that it is a bunch of gibberish, Ichigo wonders if the drawing she provided is her clue and asserts that it is ridiculous before tossing the letter away, prompting Kon to grabs it and tell Ichigo that he needs to treat Rukia's message with more respect because it could be something important. However, Ichigo merely tells him to not be stupid before asking him who this " Rukia " person he keep referring to is.

Expressing surprise at this, Kon asks Ichigo what he is talking about and states Rukia is Rukia as he wonders if Ichigo has lost his mind. Ichigo claims that Kon is the one who has lost his mind and says that he is tired before deciding that he will call it a day and turning off the light. As he tells Ichigo to wait a minute, Kon asserts that he is not done here and that Ichigo cannot just ignore this, but Ichigo knocks him off of the bed, sending him flying into the corner. Lying down and closing his eyes, Ichigo suddenly sees Rukia and recalls the time he spent with her.

Remembering how he saved her from her execution and how she gave him her Shinigami powers , Ichigo bolts up in his bed and declares that he remembers. Ichigo turns the light on and tells Kon to wake up, and as Kon tells him to leave him alone and asks what he wants, Ichigo takes the crumpled note from him and looks at it. Reasserting that he remembers, Ichigo notes that he has gotten messages like this from Rukia before and the drawings she provided were used to decipher the code. As he decodes the note, Ichigo learns that Rukia is returning to Soul Society to take care of some business and will be back soon.

Kon looks at Ichigo, who expresses confusion at this. Soon afterward, at the Urahara Shop , Ururu Tsumugiya gives Ichigo a cup of tea, which he thanks her for. As a blushing Ururu takes the plate and leaves the room, closing the door behind her, while Jinta Hanakari watches, Kisuke Urahara notes Ichigo wants to know about someone whose name he does not recognize. When Ichigo reveals that her name is Rukia Kuchiki, Urahara pretends to recognize her before asking Ichigo who she is, leading a surprised Ichigo to ask him in turn what he is talking about because Rukia is one of his best customers.

Asking Ichigo if he is sure about this because the name is not familiar to him, Urahara pulls out his customer list and notes that there is such a name on the list. Though Ichigo triumphantly asserts that he was right, Urahara apologizes and admits that he really does not recall any such person. Ichigo wonders what is going on, leading Kon to remind Ichigo that he also forgot who Rukia was for a period of time. When Ichigo theorizes that something may have happened to Rukia, Urahara reveals that something incredible happened in the Seireitei last night, though he is not sure Rukia was involved.

While Urahara details how Mayuri went crazy and destroyed his own lab, Jinta, Ururu, Yoruichi Shihōin , and Tessai Tsukabishi listen outside the door as Urahara reveals that a massive amount of Reishi was released as a result and a third of the Seireitei has been heavily damaged. Wondering if the two situations are somehow connected, Urahara notes that it could explain why their memories are being affected because they may be under attack. As Ichigo expresses surprise at this idea, Urahara asks him what his relationship with Rukia is.

Ichigo asks him if this is really important, and when Urahara merely stares at him, Ichigo asserts that Rukia is a very close friend of his. Climbing up to Ichigo's shoulder, Kon demands that Urahara to help them because they are wasting time and need to go to Soul Society, leading Ichigo to grab him by the head and pull him off. When Ichigo asks him to open the Senkaimon , Urahara agrees to do so. In the Seireitei, 1st Division Lieutenant Chōjirō Sasakibe approaches Yamamoto and reports that the unusual leakage of Reishi has finally settled.

Revealing that they have begun investigating, Chōjirō details how each of the Gotei 13 has begun deploying inside of the grounds. Elsewhere, as a hole opens in the sky, Ichigo and Kon fall out and scream as they plummet towards the ruined section of the Seireitei, creating a small dust cloud on impact.

When Kon asks him if he thinks everyone has been killed, Ichigo admits that it does not look good, and when Kon wonders if Rukia is alright, he states that he does not know and tells Kon to stay quiet before promising that they will try to find her. When Shizuku asserts that their future with Rukia awaits them, Homura declares that they will go and walks forward.

While Kon wonders what the snake was and claims that it was weird, Ichigo agrees as several Shinigami surround them, leading Kon to wonder why they are doing so. The Shinigami draw their Zanpakutō, prompting Kon to asks what is going on here, and when Hisagi tells Ichigo to identify himself, Kon notes that he thought the Shinigami were Ichigo's friends. After he dodges several subsequent attacks, Ichigo jumps into the air and launches a counterattack.

Landing on the back of a Shinigami, Ichigo proceeds to disarm and incapacitate five more before being confronted by Hisagi, who attacks him. When Ichigo blocks his attack, Hisagi slashes and stabs at him while Kon wonders how Ichigo's enemies treat him if these are his friend. Ichigo blocks another attack from Hisagi and asserts that he does not want to fight him before pushing Hisagi's blade to the side and leaping away. As Hisagi tells the Shinigami to stop him, Ichigo runs away and begins moving through the corridors, only to be forced to block Zabimaru when it suddenly crashes down on him.

Retracting Zabimaru , Renji declares that he has caught Ichigo as Hisagi runs up with several other Shinigami and tells him to not let Ichigo get away. Renji points Zabimaru at Ichigo and demands to know if he is responsible for all of this. When Ichigo asks Renji if he recognizes him, Renji claims that he does not know what Ichigo is talking about and attacks him. Stating that he does not know anyone by this name, Renji asks Ichigo who she is, prompting a shocked Ichigo to note that he does not understand what is happening here and ask Renji if he does not know Rukia. As he repeats the name, Renji recalls going to the Shin'ō Academy with Rukia while Ichigo says there is no way he could forget about her and tells him to try to remember, but Renji tells him to shut up and stop babbling.

After Renji extends Zabimaru towards Ichigo, Zabimaru crashes into a building as Ichigo lands on top of a nearby roof while Kon wonders what is going on around here. Ichigo asserts that this is what he would like to know as Renji jumps up and attacks him. While Ichigo leaps away with Renji following him, Hisagi tells the Shinigami they will follow Ichigo. With Ichigo running, Kon asks him what they are going to do, prompting Ichigo to say they will run away for now. Suddenly, Komamura lands in front of Ichigo and tells him to surrender as Ichigo recognizes him.

Seeing Renji, Hisagi, and the other Shinigami approach from behind, Ichigo tells Kon to hang on tight and launches himself into the air with his Reiatsu. When Ichigo dodges, the blade crashes into the buildings below as Ichigo looks down at it. Kon tells Ichigo to watch out, prompting him to move away in order to dodge the giant's hand. Reappearing, Ichigo fires a Getsuga Tenshō .

With Kokujō Tengen Myō'ō pushed back by this attack, Ichigo continues to fire Getsuga Tenshō at the giant, only for Kokujō Tengen Myō'ō to suddenly slash at Ichigo, who is sent flying away, before collapsing, forcing the Shinigami on the ground to run away.

Rukia Awakens

Elsewhere, Rukia wakes up and clutches her head. Looking around, she gets up and looks out of the window, where she sees that she is in Rukongai . As she notes that she remembers this place, Rukia recalls helping Renji and his friends steal water by tripping their pursuer and helping them get away, leading her to realize that she grew up here. Suddenly, Homura and Shizuku appear as Homura tackles and hugs Rukia, who demands to know what her problem is as she tries to push Homura away. Asking Homura who she is, Rukia sees that she is crying and wonders who they are.

Shizuku tells Homura that Rukia will not recognize her if she cannot see her face, which Homura acknowledges as she sits up and tells Rukia to look at her. When Homura asks Rukia if she remembers her now, Rukia expresses surprise. Meanwhile, at a captains meeting in the Seireitei, Yamamoto states that he needs a damage report from each captain, prompting Ukitake to reveal the destruction from the Reishi leak covers two hundred spirit miles, centered in the east-west direction, and extends over a third of the Seireitei, which leads Yamamoto to ask what the status of the casualties is.

Admitting that they have never seen anything like it because the victims were enveloped by the Reishi and turned into petrified stone, Unohana reveals that they cannot even confirm if they are dead or alive. When Yamamoto asks her what Mayuri's status is, Unohana details how he appears to be in some sort of daze because he cannot remember anything about the current situation or even important facts about himself before asserting that he appears to have lost a portion of his memory, though they are not sure how it happened. Yamamoto brings up Komamura's confrontation with a stranger wearing a shihakushō .

Confirming this, Komamura reveals that the intruder was wearing a Hollow's mask and his Reiatsu was equivalent to a captain's. When a shocked Shunsui claims that this is impossible, Komamura affirms that he is sure of it and admits that he was struck by the intruder's demeanor because he seemed very familiar with them. Komamura notes that it was as though he belonged here and details how he spoke to Renji as though they had a history together. As he states that they questioned Renji, Byakuya notes that he was adamant about not having seen the intruder before and him being a complete stranger.

When 10th Division Captain Tōshirō Hitsugaya asserts that this is very disturbing, Ukitake asks him what is wrong, prompting Hitsugaya to note that they are missing something here. Declaring that there is a crisis at hand, Yamamoto tells the captains to consider this the most serious threat in the history of the Seireitei and proclaims that all divisions are to coordinate their efforts to find the intruder.

Ichigo's Journey Continues

Grinning, Homura declares that they ran over as fast as possible and asks Rukia what she wants to tell them, which Shizuku echoes. Rukia states that she remembered neither of them have names yet and reveals that she would like to name them if they do not mind because not having names can be quite difficult. When Homura starts to cry, Rukia says they do not have to if they do not want, prompting Homura to state they have always wanted Rukia to give them names because they love her. As Homura thanks her, Rukia says she has some names if they are ready, prompting Homura and Shizuku to hug her.

In the present, Rukia states that it is all coming back to her now and reveals that she remembers Homura and Shizuku because they lived near her when she was young. When Homura asks her what names she gave them, Rukia wonders if they never got the names she picked out before suddenly recalling being attacked by a possessed Shinigami. Rukia falls to her knees and clutches her head, leading a concerned Homura to ask her what is wrong as Shizuku wonders if she is in pain. Promising that nothing will ever separate them again, Homura tells Rukia she can relax because they will always be together from now on.

Encounter with Byakuya

At the Kuchiki Manor , Byakuya walks into his chambers before suddenly stopping, which prompts Nobutsune Seike to ask him if something is bothering him. Claiming that it is nothing, Byakuya goes through several rooms in his quarters before arriving at his study, where he sees a desk with papers on it and one of Rukia's drawings carved into the wood. Byakuya stands in front of a shrine and looks at a portrait of Hisana Kuchiki . When Kon voices his belief that this picture is of Rukia, Byakuya tells him to keep his voice down as he turns around, leading Ichigo to apologize for bothering him.

Ichigo asserts that he is trying to not cause a commotion here and notes that this is a picture of Hisana before observing that she looks a lot like Rukia. As Ichigo asks Byakuya if he has noticed something strange is going on, Byakuya's attendant sees him from afar and moves away. When Byakuya asks Ichigo what is talking about, Ichigo tells him to not play ignorant and affirms that Rukia is Hisana's sister, which Byakuya should know very well. While Byakuya says Rukia's name, Ichigo details how she is his younger sister-in-law whom he tried to protect even if it meant breaking the rules.

Walking up to Byakuya, Ichigo notes he does not remember her either as Byakuya turns to the shrine and asks Ichigo how he knew his wife's name was Hisana. As Ichigo begins to answer, the other doors in the room open, revealing Shinigami standing behind them. When the Shinigami move in, Ichigo moves outside with Shunpo . Byakuya tells the Shinigami to stand down as Renji calls out to him and asks why Ichigo is here. While Ichigo tells him to wait, Renji jumps down and tells him to shut up before sending Zabimaru at Ichigo, who deflects it.

As he jumps in the air, Ichigo notes that he should have remembered Renji was never the type to listen to reason while Renji sends Zabimaru at him once again. Dodging Zabimaru , Ichigo declares that he will have to force Renji to listen to him and jumps off of Renji's back before landing behind him and activating his Bankai, Tensa Zangetsu , prompting the surrounding Shinigami to shield themselves from the resulting energy storm and Renji to curse. When Ichigo wonders what is wrong and asks him if he will release his Bankai, Renji asserts that he has never had a Bankai.

Ichigo reminds Renji that he trained to develop one in order to save Rukia, but Renji declares that he has no idea what he is talking about and sends Zabimaru at Ichigo, who deflects it once more. As Ichigo appears behind Renji and tells him to remember, Renji slashes at him, forcing Ichigo to block as the segments of Zabimaru grind across the blade of Tensa Zangetsu . While Renji demands to know what Ichigo is trying to make him remember, Ichigo pushes Zabimaru away and proclaims that he is trying to make him remember his life as he raises his sword above his head.

Reminded of the end of their final fight by this pose, Renji strikes at Ichigo in the exact same way as he did in the end of their previous battle, only to be blown away by Ichigo's subsequent attack, which creates a large dust cloud. As the dust settles, Byakuya unsheathes his Zanpakutō and utters the release command, but after a tense moment, he reveals Hisana was born in the 78th district of Rukongai, Inuzuri , prompting Ichigo to thank him as he vanishes. Meanwhile, Rukia sits on a roof. As Rukia sighs, Homura calls out to her and asks if she can join her, which Rukia affirms.

Moving to Rukia, Homura asks her if something is wrong, prompting Rukia to admit that she feels like a stranger because she knows almost nothing about herself even though she has come home to this town for the first time in many years. Homura puts her head on Rukia's shoulder as Rukia notes that they waited for her and the names she promised to give them for many years. While Homura looks up with tears in her eyes, Rukia promises she will give her and Shizuku those names, leading Homura to thank her as the sun rises. Looking at the sun, Rukia gets up, prompting Homura to ask her what is wrong.

Rukia sees a hill in the distance and remembers standing with Renji beside the graves of her deceased friends on top of it. As Rukia says she remembers it now, Homura whispers her name. While Renji recalls his fight with Ichigo, the spirit of Zabimaru materializes beside him and notes that Renji did not use Bankai before asking Renji why he hesitated. Elsewhere, at a park, Byakuya sits and recalls his past with Hisana. In the past, Byakuya and Hisana walk over a bridge. When Byakuya asks if Hisana went to Rukongai again today, Hisana confirms this and apologizes, but Byakuya clarifies that he is just concerned about her health.
