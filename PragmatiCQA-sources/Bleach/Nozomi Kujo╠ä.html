Appearance

Nozomi Kuj≈ç is a short and petite young woman with chin length dark green hair and purplish-red eyes. She wears a red skirt that is thigh length with a cream colored cardigan over it. She also wears long black thigh-high socks. After gaining a Zanpakut≈ç, Nozomi wears a short shihakush≈ç that only goes a little below her waist with a white band around the waist and knee high white socks.

Personality

Nozomi has quite a defensive personality, shown during the several attempts by Kon to find out her name or where she is from. She is also very independent, shown when Kon tells her that he went to all this trouble to help her out, to which she says that she didn't even ask for his help. However, when Ichigo and his friends open up to her, she is revealed to have more of an exuberant personality.

History

Nozomi was created during Project Spearhead, the project that created the Modified Souls . She was the first Mod-Soul created during the project. Initially, she was a different being but somehow split in two, the other half becoming Kager≈çza Inaba .
