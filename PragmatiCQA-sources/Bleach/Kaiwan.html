Kaiwan (嵬腕, Kaiwan ) is one of the four gatekeepers that guard the entrance to Seireitei . He is the guardian of the Blue Stream Gate (青流門, Shōryūmon ).

Appearance

Kaiwan is a very tall man who dwarfs most other Shinigami. He has long black hair and black facial hair styled in to two bushy stripes on both cheeks but is otherwise clean shaven. He wears black hakama with a white obi sash, but is topless.

Plot

Soul Society arc

Sōsuke Aizen hypnotizes him and two other Gatekeepers and used them to cause trouble for Ichigo Kurosaki and his friends. However, he is stopped by Jidanbō Ikkanzaka and Kūkaku Shiba .

Powers & Abilities

Spiritual Power : As a Shinigami and gate keeper, Kaiwan possess some level of Reiatsu.
