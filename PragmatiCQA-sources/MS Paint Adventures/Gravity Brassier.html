The Gravity Brassier is a scale bodice that was originally worn by Madame Murel . She was dispatched before she could use it. Soon after, it was acquired by Mobster Kingpin who used it to become Dark Matter Mobster Kingpin , and later, Black Hole Mobster Kingpin . Unlike normal bodices, instead of modifying height and vim , mass and volume are modified. So far this has only been used to increase mass for defensive purposes.

Just as the Aspect Corset and Scale Bodice lead to the Gutterpipe Projects , the Gravity Brassier corresponds to the great volcano Mount Saint Lardass .
