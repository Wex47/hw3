The Ham Needle is a giant needle with a ham impaled on it in the middle of the Imaginary City Streets with three main purposes:

  • Work as giant obstacle for Hysterical Dame and Nervous Broad as they go about their inexplicable whore adventure, trying to get a Fan plugged in for some reason.
  • Godhead Pickle Inspector uses it to sew the imaginary universe together, after DMK rips it in half using his Comb Rave , Fill 'Em With Daylight .
  • The Ham Needle conveniently stands under where DMK is defeated and also where Mobster Kingpin is ejected on the other side of the imaginary universe from Mount Saint Lardass , impaling him and slaying him once and for all.

This allows Ace Dick to obtain the Megaton Key and lets Team Sleuth go to the Real City Streets .
