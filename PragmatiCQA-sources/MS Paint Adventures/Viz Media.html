Viz Media is an American distributor of anime and manga. Owned by the Japanese publishers Shueisha and Shogakukan, the company's catalog includes popular titles like Dragon Ball , One Piece and Naruto .

On September 14, 2017, it was announced that What Pumpkin Games and Homestuck, Inc. , had entered into with Viz to produce "a comprehensive array of additional entertainment content and licensed merchandise" related to Homestuck and Hiveswap . As part of this deal, Viz acquired the Homestuck publishing rights ( on October 6) and unveiled plans to publish a new line of hardcover books beginning in 2018. Additionally, Andrew Hussie stated that the company would in the release of the Homestuck epilogue project .

Simultaneously with the initial September 14 announcement, the Viz-owned went live as the official website for the partnership, initially serving as a basic FAQ for new readers. On April 2, 2018, the site was and became the new host of MS Paint Adventures , with mspaintadventures.com being shut down and turned into a redirect.
