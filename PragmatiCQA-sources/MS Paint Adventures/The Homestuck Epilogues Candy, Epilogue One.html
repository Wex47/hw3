Candy, Epilogue One is the first epilogue in the Candy narrative of The Homestuck Epilogues . It consists of the first 3 chapters of the Candy route.

Synopsis

  • John eats candy and decides to stay on Earth C. Calliope tells him, if he wants this world to turn over a new leaf, he needs to save Gamzee.
  • John returns to Act 6 Act 6 Intermission 5 and lets Gamzee out of the fridge. Back in the present, Gamzee begins his apologetic “redemption arc.” Roxy and Calliope love it, but John’s disgust leads him to message Terezi.
  • Dirk, noticing something strange happening to the canon, has an existential crisis. He calls Jane and tells her to cancel her presidential campaign.
