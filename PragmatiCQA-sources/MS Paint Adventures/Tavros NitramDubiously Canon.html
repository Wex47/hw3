Meat

We first see Tavros immediately after Vriska containing the Juju . The two of them, along with Meenah , are standing off to the side waiting for it to open. As Vriska stares down L o rd English , Meenah leaves to rejoin the rest of the army, and Tavros remains at Vriska's side. He clings to her arm and flinchs as L o rd English puts another crack in reality. Almost immediately, Vriska is abruptly knocked back as one of the falling shards of reality hits her hard in the head, causing her to bleed profusely. Tavros asks if she's okay, and she falters, checking her serious wounds. He tries again to help her, but she snaps at him and sends him off to find Meenah. He obliges, running to join the rest of the army. He is vaporized along with other three ghosts by L o rd English .
