Wizardology is a real book about wizards, a copy of which was extensively vandalized by Andrew Hussie (and subsequently sent to his friend Tynic as a ).

An archive of the images Hussie posted of his alterations can be found . The image with which Hussie concluded is the source of the "You're welcome" ongoing gag in Homestuck . The tradition of humorously vandalizing books was continued in Homestuck by Dirk Strider , who sent Jane a personalized copy of Pony Pals: Detective Pony for her birthday, and by Caliborn when taking control of the website.
