Mobster Kingpin's office is the base of operations for his many scandals. Inside, Mobster Kingpin has a desk that, like so many others, has become a fort instead. On the desk was the brass knuckles/megaton key and on a nearby table there was a dollhouse and a jacuzzi. While the office was unharmed during Mobster Kingpin's battle with the female characters , Fiesta Ace Dick accidentally wrecked the fort and awakened Mobster Kingpin. The battle raged out of Mobster Kingpin's office, which has never been seen again.
