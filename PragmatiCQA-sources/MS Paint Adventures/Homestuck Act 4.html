Epilogue

The following events are not part of an official act of Homestuck , but constitute a short epilogue to the events in the fourth act.

  • Rose ends her GameFaqs walkthrough, believing the trolls and the gods to be correct in their opinion that the kids will fail to win Sburb . Instead of going through her first gate and playing by the rules of the game, she decides to explore the Land of Light and Rain in search of answers on how to avert their fate. She seals her walkthrough in a server situated in the Furthest Ring so the gods there may transmit the message to other players throughout the cosmos.
  • We skip forward in time to see the Warweary Villein being exiled on Earth. In the present he discovers Dream John's " Accursed Mascot " doll and rips it apart. He also finds Dream John's blanket, which he fashions into the signature robes he wears in the future.
  • Dream John, now awake, discovers Dream Jade's body on The Battlefield and removes the White Queen 's Ring from her finger. He sees a vision of his immediate future and heads off towards a burning castle.
  • The Parcel Mistress decapitates the Hegemonic Brute and takes his communication device to organise a rendezvous with Jack Noir . He upholds his end of the deal and trades Jade's Package for the White King and Queen's crowns.
  • Dream John reaches the castle, just in time to meet with the Parcel Mistress. She slams the box into his chest, fulfilling her final duty before her exile and her transformation into the Peregrine Mendicant .
  • Dream John opens the box, finding two letters inside: one from Jade and one from an unknown author . He is interrupted by Jack, who demands he relinquish the White Queen's Ring.
  • The contents of the box are revealed to be a cyborg version of the bunny , wielding four powerful weapons that match the strife specibi of the kids . The bunny turns on Jack.
  • The Authority Regulator escapes the Frog Temple , traveling to the ectobiology lab where he finds John's sleeping body. As the Reckoning gains pace, the lab shoots towards Skaia , disabling its Transportalizers .
  • AR? straps John to Bro 's Rocket Board with caution tape and launches him to safety. AR? is sent through a portal and into exile, becoming the Aimless Renegade .
  • Jack flies away from John and the bunny as Grandpa 's battleship lands. John's Dad and Rose's Mom disembark as Grandpa retrieves, and subsequently stuffs, Dream Jade's body.
  • The White Queen leaves her subjects behind to becomes the Windswept Questant . She finds the Eggy-Looking Base , which is split into two distinct halves. WQ uses a spirograph-shaped key to recombine the base to its complete form.
  • The Questant joins the other Exiles in the future .
  • The Wayward Vagabond pulls out the Queen's Ring , all this time safely hidden under the bandages holding his Trusty Knife together. Serenity exclaims "OH MY".
  • Dream John witnesses the ring in a Skaian cloud. Another cloud shows him the remains of Jade's house .
  • We peek inside the house, seeing the stuffed bodies of both Dream Jade and Halley. We also discover Jack's fourth wall. If this device were turned on, we would have seen...
  • ...another recap from Andrew . Act 4 ends.
