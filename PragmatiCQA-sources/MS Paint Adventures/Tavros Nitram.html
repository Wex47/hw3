Tavros Nitram , also known by his Trollian handle “ adiosToreador ” , is one of the trolls . His handle may refer to bullfighting . “ Toreador ” means bullfighting. His huge bull horns having to do with his associated zodiac sign, Taurus (♉), The Bull, could be the reasoning behind “ adios ” , meaning "goodbye", and an instinctual disliking towards bullfighters.

Due to an accident during one of his Flarping sessions, he has ended up a paraplegic.

He types in reverse case, replacing periods with commas. However, he uses an excess of commas, giving the impression of uncertainty or stuttering.

He was a member of the Red Team .

Etymology

The name Tavros was suggested by and Nitram was suggested by . Tavros is Greek for "bull" and is also the Modern Greek pronunciation of the name Taurus . His name resembles that of the Doctor Who villain Davros , the wheelchair-using creator of the Daleks - this allusion may not be intentional, but it fits with his disability as well as his later acquisition of robotic parts. Nitram is Martin written backwards. There are two possible connections for this. Operation Taurus was the name of a planned prosecution by the Royal Ulster Constabulary against Martin McGuinness. Also, Mary Martin played Peter Pan in the 1954 musical . Nitram may also be derived from the word nitrate ; it is suspected that nitrates can cause a condition that causes one's blood to turn brown.
