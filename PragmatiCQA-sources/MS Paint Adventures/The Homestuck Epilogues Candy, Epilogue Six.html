Candy, Epilogue Six is the sixth epilogue in the Candy narrative of The Homestuck Epilogues . It consists of chapters 27 to 28 of the Candy route.

Synopsis

  • Ten years later, Harry Anderson and Vriska sneak out past curfew and flirt. Aradia, Sollux, and Alt!Calliope watch this canonically inconsequential world evolve. John is the best man at Dave and Jade’s wedding, though Roxy has left him. John meets Karkat at the wedding, and the two talk break-ups and politics. Dave approaches John, barely missing Karkat escaping, and the two talk relationships. John is still deeply unhappy.
  • Karkat’s army declares war on Harry Anderson’s birthday. John watches as onto Earth C.
