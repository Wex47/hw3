The Intellibeam Laserstation is an item designed to extract otherwise unreadable captcha codes from the back of certain captchalogue cards , containing items such as Sburb discs or pumpkins . It costs 100,000 build grist to create and seems to become available in the Phernalia Registry for all server/client connections after the second, along with the Holopad . Dave used this to create a second SBURB Server CD, so that they could progress in the game.
