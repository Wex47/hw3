The Prisoners are the main characters from Jailbreak . There are three Prisoners. Over the course of the game a mechanic of passing your awareness back and forth between them is introduced, but it has no deeper meaning as you're merely going from one's perspective to the other, at one point for a short number of pages the story splits into two distinct paths and then goes back to being one.

The First Guy

You start as this Prisoner when first beginning Jailbreak . He is in his cell with absolutely nothing, useful or otherwise. Over the course of the game this Prisoner murders a Dungeon Master, makes himself a suit of armor out of some pumpkins , and gets harpooned.

The Other Guy

This Prisoner starts out in between two of the prison buildings, while still locked, it's not a regular-sized cell or probably not even a cell at all. Over the course of the game he gets peed on, is vomited on, and builds a robot .

Partway through the story this guy finds a secret door into an adjacent cell, where he sees the skeletons of two long-dead fellow prisoners .

The Drill Guy

The last Prisoner starts in the cell above the first guy. In his room is a giant drill which he has never thought to use as a tool for escape. He manages to snap his own neck after his head gets stuck in a hole in the floor. His blood trickles down into The First Guy's room.

The First Guy, Again

After getting a Game Over, Jailbreak starts over with the first guy again. This time he manages to escape fairly easily after beseeching the help of an elf . His eventual fate is unknown during the course of Jailbreak , though he appeared to have been in a steady downward spiral into madness after partnering with a Pony (which he received from the elf) and stealing children from a school bus, fittingly with his madness, in Problem Sleuth this prisoner becomes the MSPA Reader as is confirmed by his surroundings and book commentary from Andrew Hussie .
