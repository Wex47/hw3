Physical attributes and abilities

The highest land dwelling caste, purple blood is a relative cool purple in comparison to the warmer-toned violet.

The are that allow them to psychically amplify fear, such as by inside the dreams of targets.

Most purple bloods have long, curved horns, often resembling those of a goat or other ungulate. Barzum and Baizli Soleil 's horns are shorter than those of other known purple bloods, but still display the signature curve.
