The hemospectrum is the series of twelve blood colors possessed by trolls , forming the basis of the Alternian caste system. Lusii share these blood colors, and often or always have the same blood color as their charge.

Cherubs also possess multiple blood colors, however in a binary rather than a spectrum, with only two colors.

Trivia

  • Most of the blood colors on the hemospectrum are distributed at roughly 30° angles on the HSV color system , deviating by only a few degrees. Lime and olive blood have the largest deviation, sitting at 73° and 82° rather than 90° and 120°, respectively. Olive sits between gold and jade rather than lime and jade due to being introduced later than the other 11 castes; however these hue deviations also help the two colors appear more visually distinct (as green has the largest hue range on the color wheel out of all primary and secondary colors).
