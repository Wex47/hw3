Aysha U. Farah is an author who has written for multiple recent Homestuck works, including The Homestuck Epilogues , Hiveswap Friendship Simulator , Hiveswap , and her role as Narrative Director for Pesterquest . She is also currently working on Homestuck^2 . Her father is Geezey .
