James Roach , sometimes credited as James "soselfimportant" Roach , is a musician and writer who has contributed tracks for Homestuck and its related content. While few of his tracks were featured in the comic proper, he became a prominent musician and sound designer for Hiveswap , Hiveswap Friendship Simulator , and Pesterquest .
