The Midnight Crew have many weapons and objects that they use throughout their missions. All of these have a playing card associated with them. Much like Problem Sleuth 's weapon/object duality , the cards transform into the weapon at will. Each character has a deck of cards. Their decks of cards are similar to sylladexes , except they hold playing cards instead of Captchalogue Cards.

War chest

Spades Slick 's deck of cards transforms into the war chest that holds all of his objects. This chest is reminiscent of John 's Magic Chest , Jade 's Gadget Chest , and Jane 's Baking Chest . Taped to the inside of the lid is his vendetta itinerary of all the members of the Felt he plans to kill, and his heist map.

Cast iron horse hitcher

Card Ace of spades

The cast iron horse hitcher is Slick's weapon of choice. In the intermission it is in his first inventory slot. Its innocuous double is the ace of spades, as one might expect from Slick's primary weapon. Itchy fell quickly to the might of the horse hitcher after Slick . Sn o wman later caught Slick pretending the hitcher was a horse and using it to joust, embarrassing him. Despite this, he later used the hitcher in the same fashion to skewer Stitch .

Slick attempts to use it to bash in the head of D o c Scratch , but fails. After Scratch gets tired of this senseless drubbing, he takes the hitcher and bends it into a useless heap of iron. The new pretzeled horse hitcher appears in Openbound in a chest; when given to Andrew Hussie while crying in the middle of a crater, he will follow you.

Backup Hat

Clubs, like many of the other Midnight Crew members, keeps a back-up hat with him at all times. Whether all backup hats are the jack of diamonds is unclear.

Brawlsoleum

Diamonds Droog 's deck of cards is the brawlsoleum . He keeps all of his items in this mausoleum. It looks like Rose 's mausoleum for Jaspers . He has covered the entrance of his brawlsoleum with a black velvet curtain. On the door is a row of his white ties.

Ultra-Violence Cuestick

Card Ace of diamonds

This is Diamonds' weapon of choice, as its innocuous double is the ace of diamonds. It is in his first inventory slot. Its title also reflects his short temper, as Diamonds is known to for no particular reason. The term "ultra-violence" is also a reference to a common phrase from A Clockwork Orange , the book which Diamonds' name is somewhat inspired by.

Guns

Radio

Card Four of diamonds; ten of spades

This radio is used by many of the members, including Diamonds. It is created by combining a ten card and a four card, an obvious pun on the term " 10-4 ".
