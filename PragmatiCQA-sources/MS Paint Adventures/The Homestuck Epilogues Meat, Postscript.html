Meat, Postscript is final, postscript chapter of the Meat route of The Homestuck Epilogues . Despite being in the Meat route, its content actually pertains to the Candy route (the inverse is true of the Candy postscript ), reflecting the interwoven dual nature of the epilogues.

Synopsis

  • Inside the black hole, Alt!Calliope in Jade's body consumes L o rd English . She summons a vortex, out of which flies Davebot . Alt!Calliope! (via possesed Jade) flies into the vortex. Davebot invites Aradia Megido along; they're going to discuss plans. They fly, and the vortex closes–never again shall that which matters meet that which doesn't.
