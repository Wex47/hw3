Toby Fox , credited as Toby "Radiation" Fox throughout most of Homestuck and also known as FwugRadiation , is a musician and programmer who has contributed many tracks for Homestuck and its related content. He is most well known for his indie video game hit Undertale . Toby was given a shout out in the Homestuck credits for "most custom sound work".
