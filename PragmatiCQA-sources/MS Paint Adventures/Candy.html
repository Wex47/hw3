In Andrew Hussie's commentary

In his commentary for Homestuck: Book 3: Act 4 , Andrew Hussie refers to a “ meat/candy binary of storycraft theory ” of his own creation. In this theory, meat is a symbol for “ exceptionally violent, high-octane, game-changing ” content, which began to become more and more prevalent in Homestuck after . Presumably candy represents its own kind of storytelling in this theory, likely contrasting with meat and relating moreso to the kind of content found in Homestuck before [S] Jack: Ascend; however its exact meaning has not yet been explained.
