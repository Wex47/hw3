To reach the Real City Streets and actually get back to your job is the goal of the game in Problem Sleuth . It's unknown as to how you got off the Real City Streets or how Mobster Kingpin got you in your odd little office in the first place, but there are dames to be rescued! You better hurry!

The Real City Streets were located in the fictional city of Chicagopolis (revealed in the commentary in the books), relating to the Chicago Overcoat .
