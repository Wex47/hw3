Matespritship is one of the four quadrants recognised in troll romance. It is a form of red romance, like moirallegience , and is a concupiscent quadrant like kismesissitude .

Matespritship is represented by the heart ( ♥ or <3 ) symbol and a flushed red color; as such it is known as the flushed quadrant .

Etymology

Like the names of the other quadrants, matespritship plays on the term soulmate , with -sprit likely deriving from the French word esprit , meaning 'spirit' or 'soul'.

The suffix -ship denotes a special state or union between two people or entities (such as a relationship or friendship ), from which derives the term shipping ; interestingly however, on Alternia the concept of shipping involves not just matesprit ships but the other three quadrants as well .

The specific emotions behind matespritship are sometimes called “ MATING FONDESS ” (sic) , which may be most readily recognised by a human as feelings of 'love'; Karkat Vantas however more pessimistically considers it just one of many “ NUANCES OF PITY ” , which is in turn “ JUST THE TONED DOWN VERSION OF [...] HATE ” , the emotion central to black romance.

Also like human romance, flushed feelings are closely associated with sex and reproduction. As such, alongside material from a kismesissitude, genetic material from a matespritship must be supplied when the imperial drone arrives at a troll's home with his two filial pails, failure to comply resulting in swift execution .
