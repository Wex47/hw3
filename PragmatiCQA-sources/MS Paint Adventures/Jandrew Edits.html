Jandrew Edits was a website created by Andrew Hussie to host edits of Star Trek: The Next Generation and other media from the 80s, created by himself and Jan Van den Hemel. The duo were active from December 2008 to June 2010, with the latter returning in 2015.

Initially, Hussie posted the videos on MS Paint Adventures , but after a while he moved them to their own site. While the site no longer exists, the videos were hosted by YouTube and most of the videos, excluding the Back to the Future edits, still exist as their original uploads.

The main page was removed from MSPA at an unknown point, but the ability to watch YouTube videos through the Jandrew Edits section remained until August or September 2014. The remaining original uploads could be found , , and . On May 22nd, 2015 a new edit was released, the first in almost five years. On Twitter Hussie confirmed that the series will be returning, . Jan returned to his main channel in 2015 to begin re-uploading lost episodes (including the long-lost first episode) and creating new ones. His channel was deleted at some point in 2017, most likely in May. On May 12, 2017, Byron Hussie re-uploaded every TNG edit produced at that point on . The Knight Rider and Fresh Prince edits were not re-uploaded.
