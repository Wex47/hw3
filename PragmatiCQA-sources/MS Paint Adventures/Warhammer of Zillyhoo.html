In Problem Sleuth

The Warhammer of Zillyhoo is a non-canon item first seen in . When Zombie Ace Dick transforms into Gummy Worm Zombie Ace Dick , Candy Corn Vampire Problem Sleuth becomes angered by ZAD's Truffle Shuffle -ing about. To defeat this foe, PS summons the Warhammer of Zillyhoo, a magical hammer with a name that is just as ridiculous as it looks.

PS is overcome by the Warhammer's beauty, and he and ZAD collapse crying in each others arms because of how beautiful it is.

In Homestuck

The Warhammer of Zillyhoo has also appeared in Homestuck under hammerkind . Gamzee appears to have the Warhammer of Zillyhoo in his jokerkind specibus and John has also obtained this weapon, after the bunny hugens it for his use.

The warhammer's beauty dazzled the Warweary Villein , the Courtyard Droll , and Karkat Vantas , John and Gamzee showing themselves to be the only ones who are not too overcome to wield it. The majesty of the hammer was such that John felt compelled to its name in a light, tenor gregorian chant towards a (imaginary) cathedral window.

John had intended to use it against Jack, and show him its true might, but he was incapacitated before he could.

Some time after his resurrection, John is with a new hammer, named the Pop-a-matic Vrillyhoo Hammer . Its alchemisation clearly involved the Warhammer of Zillyhoo and one or more Vriska related objects .

The Warhammer is eventually revealed to be one of seven legendary weapons from cherub mythology. It was named for its place of manufacture, the continent of Zillyhoo, and shares the same that it did in . The post-scratch kids alchemize it and the other weapons while under the influence of Trickster Mode .
