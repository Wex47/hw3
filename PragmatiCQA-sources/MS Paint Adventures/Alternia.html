Alternia (and its pre- scratch counterpart Beforus ) are planets in Universe A from which trolls originate.

Alternia is the centre of the Alternian Empire and has a green moon and a pink moon (with its own, smaller pink moon). It is the setting of much of the first half of Hivebent , and years sweeps in the future , it is the setting of the Midnight Crew Intermission . Alternia is also a significant setting in Hiveswap , Hiveswap Friendship Simulator , and Pesterquest .

Beforus is almost identical to Alternia, although it lacks an interstellar empire due to its greatly differing troll society. It also lacks Alternia's Green moon. Beforus has never been depicted, but the pre-scratch trolls of the A1 session play a significant role in the plot of Homestuck .

Society

Alternian Troll society is similar to insectoid and is dictated by the spectrum of 11 ( once 12 ) blood colours that trolls hatch with, known as the Hemospectrum . The highest colour on the spectrum gives orders to the colour below them, who gives orders to the colour below them, and so on, securing the oppression of the much more plentiful (but weaker) lower colours. Trolls exhibit significant symbiosis with other organisms on Alternia, and these also play pivotal roles in their society. One example of this is the Lusus naturae which have evolved alongside trolls to act as caretakers for the young . One unusual recent feature of Alternian society is the fact that the planet is populated only by minors. During the time of Homestuck and Hiveswap, the empress of the Alternian empire is Her Imperious Condescension , Meenah Peixes.

Beforan Troll society differs greatly from Alternian troll society due to its much kinder and more peaceful system where higher blooded trolls take care of the lower blooded trolls. Trolls of all ages live and remain on Beforus. During the time of the Alpha trolls, the empress of Beforus is implied to be a pre-scratch Feferi Peixes .

History

Beforus existed in the pre-scratch iteration of Universe A, and featured a peaceful but weaker society. The trolls of Beforus who would go on to play their own session of Sgrub would find that Beforan society left them unequipped to handle its intense difficulty, and they were forced to scratch their session, effectively erasing Beforus with it, and creating Alternia.

Due to the Session Glitch that affected Universe A, post-scratch Alternia has D o c Scratch as a first guardian , who artificially introduced the green moon as well as Gl'bgolyb who would become the lusus of the fuchsia caste . The threat of global extinction from Gl'bgolyb allowed The Condesce (or perhaps an even earlier Empress) to set up the oppressive system of the hemospectrum, which was rebelled against on a large scale on at least two occasions. The second rebellion resulted in the banishment of all adult trolls into outer space to conquer new planets for the Alternian empire.

The major events of Alternia's history were the intended outcome of D o c Scratch 's minor alterations, ensuring that the trolls would excel at playing Sgrub by turning them into an aggressive race, as well as ensuring the arrival of his master .

During the events of Hiveswap, a third, smaller rebellion would occur at the advent of the royal heiress, Trizza Tethis .

Alternia was destroyed by meteors due to the Reckoning and eventually repopulated with exiles from the trolls' session . As such, the Intermission takes place on Alternia.

Physical characteristics

Planetary system

Alternia's solar system is located in an unknown galaxy in post-scratch Universe A , a continuum separate to that of Universe B , which contains Earth. Its location inside its galaxy is unknown, although D o c Scratch at one point that a location at "the edge of the galaxy" visited by the Condesce is particularly far from Alternia. While this could imply that Alternia is located deep within its galaxy, it could equally be on an opposite edge.

Alternia's system appears to have a single, large, red star, which bathes Alternia in sunlight that appears far brighter than that experienced by Earth. The large, red depiction of its sun could indicate that it is a red giant , although this is difficult to determine due to its stylized depiction.

If there are any other planets or celestial bodies orbiting Alternia aside from it and its complement of moons, they are unknown.

Moons

Alternia has two moons, one green and one pink. The green moon is home to D o c Scratch and his apartment that resembles Felt Manor . This moon first appeared in the aborted, non-canon Felt Forum Adventure created with input from Andrew , and has since appeared in canon. It appears that a whole felt city is on the green moon that looks a lot like Real City Streets in Problem Sleuth .

Little to nothing has been revealed about the pink moon thus far except that according to Aranea it was the only moon that existed in her life on Beforus (the green one being an addition after the scratch of her game session), that Meenah lived there while she was in hiding, that it of the pre-scratch Frog Temple , and that it has a wee pink moonlet orbiting it in turn. Aranea also leads Terezi to the pink moon in a dream bubble so they may confront her session's Thief of Life .

Climate

Alternian sunlight is so intense most creatures on the planet, including trolls, can't withstand it, preferring night and shady places; looking directly into the sun causes instant blindness, as experienced by Terezi . Simply dawn's rays also causes severe sunburn and blistering (Dubiously Canon) . Kanaya is mentioned as being one of the few members of the species to enjoy its rays, with the entire Jadeblood caste being able to withstand them (Dubiously Canon) .

Lusii, however, are fine going out in the sun for short periods of time, because "their coats reflect light, or somethin' to that effect" (Dubiously Canon) .

It is shown in Hiveswap Friendsim that Alternia is prone to downpours of highly corrosive acid rain that is strong enough to dissolve a human down to the bone. However, presumably due to some impressive native biology, trolls seem to be little affected by this (although Stelsa Sezyat does note that acid "ruins her skin" ). (Dubiously Canon)

Alternian biosphere

Inhabitants

Alternia is the homeworld of the Troll race; a species of grey-skinned orange-horned humanoid aliens who have a major role in both Homestuck and Hiveswap. While adult trolls lived on Alternia in the past, in Homestuck's time period it is inhabited almost exclusively by the young, the adults of the species being offworld conquering planets for the Alternian Empire.

In the future , Alternia is home to Carapacians in the form of the trolls' Exiles , including the Midnight Crew , as well as The Felt .

Inhabitants of Alternia are known as Alternians.

Fauna

Aside from trolls and mother grubs , the lusus population shows us that Alternia is home to a greatly diverse population of terrifying creatures, including dragons; giant spiders; hermaphrodite cow-centaurs; sea-goats; fairy-winged miniature bulls; rams, multi-mouthed felines; bicyclopes; crabstrosities; giant flying whales; giant flying seahorses; and eldritch abominations such as Gl'bgolyb. Two other lusii include the cuspidated grimalkin (colloquially known as a deercat) and the glacial treetrudger (a giant sloth-like creature).

Many types of Alternian wildlife are used in the Fiduspawn game, including fidusuckers and horsaronis .

Feferi , who is quite fond of aquatic life, cares for a great number of cuttlefish and a few aquatic hoofbeasts , who have fishlike hindquarters and tails, but otherwise are anatomically identical to Earth horses such as Maplehoof from the withers forward, including the forelegs.

Terezi accused Senator Lemonsnout of embezzling beetles , and then produced a sack of beetles as evidence.

Other creatures mentioned include hoofbeasts and musclebeasts , mentioned to roam the planet at night. Both are used interchangeably with the Earth concept of a horse, in phrases such as “ GET OFF YOUR HIIGH HOOFBEA2T ” and “ fuck [his] dou8ledealing and the stupid muscle8east he rode in on ” , although generally musclebeasts are depicted as more centaurian. Based on the appearance of their aquatic variety, hoofbeasts appear to be roughly identical to Earth horses. . Also, Kanaya once alludes to muscular “ Erect Livestock ” when , and she also describes them as hermaphroditic - this may be a more detailed description of musclebeasts. Another phrase used by Alternians is "the lion's share", but it is unclear whether they have lions.

Terezi and Nepeta mention cholerbears in their roleplaying , and Karkat refers to one while trolling Jade . Cholerbears are probably similar to the panserbjørne , or armored bears, in that they have thick forearms and may wear armor. When roleplaying, Terezi refers to herself as a dragonyy'yd ; however, Terezi's unhatched lusus is referred to merely as a dragon, and it is pointedly made clear that dragons are real. This suggests that the "dragonyy'yd" race is an alternate term only used in roleplaying, or a fabrication of Terezi's entirely, the details of which may not necessarily match up with real dragons. Dragonyy'yds may be reminiscent of Drakonids commonly present in Earth roleplaying games, and thus they may be half-troll, half-dragon creatures, just as Drakonids are half-human and half-dragon creatures to us.

According to Kanaya's introduction page, undead beings rise from the sands at sunrise, stalking the land and feeding on light and the living. Those who hunt the undead use chainsaws as their weapons of choice, although such activity is said to be as dangerous as hunting the nightly musclebeasts.

Alternia was once home to prehistoric serpents, which play a major role in Alternian (and Beforan) mythology. Their green bones can be seen in the area around the blue Frog Temple , and a set of ancient serpent bones can be found near Aradia in part 1 of Openbound .
