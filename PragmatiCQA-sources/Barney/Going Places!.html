" Going Places! " is the eighth episode from the first season of Barney & Friends .

  • 3 Trivia

Plot

When Min leaves for a trip to her grandma's to celebrate her birthday, Michael wishes that everyone can go on a trip. With some help from Airline Pilot Captain Barney, everyone is flown in to Imagination City. While there, they explore different transportation methods, from trains and boats to just walking around.

Trivia

  • A different Barney doll (the same one used at the beginning of " Hop To It! ") is used during this episode.

International Edits

  • In the Hebrew version, all of the songs were a semitone lower except for the Barney Theme Song. In that same version, The Wheels on the Bus wasn't sung, but it was used as an underscore. The Airplane Song and The Happy Wanderer were both shortened. The Sailing Medley was also shortened to just A Sailor Went to Sea, Sea, Sea.
