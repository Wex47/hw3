Zoomba in Toyland is 1995 children's picture book that was written by Monica Mody and illustrated by Alvin S. White. The book was one of two books to be a part of the Bedtime With Barney book series alongside The Turtle Who Lost His Shell .

Plot

Follow the adventures of a shiny little car named Zoomba, She is a baby taxi who is driving for the first time. Join her as she travels along the Toyland Highway. In this story, Zoomba learns quickly how important it is to know how to read (road signs for example).

Trivia

  • This book was also released with an audio cassette.
  • The audio cassette for this book includes the song " Growing ".
  • This was the second Barney book to have a main character that's not from the television series , following The Turtle Who Lost His Shell .
