" Little Red Rockin' Hood " is the first part of the fourth episode from the eleventh season of Barney & Friends .

Plot

Barney, Baby Bop, BJ, Riff and the kidsÂ put on twist on a classic fairy tale story of " Little Red Rockin' Hood " where "Little 'Hood" (Baby Bop), a country singer, and a rock-obsessed Wolf (BJ) try to convince Grandpa (Riff) that their respective music choices are better. He ends up deciding that both music is great and helps them create "Country Rock".
