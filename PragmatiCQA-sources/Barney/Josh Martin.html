James Joshua "Josh" Martin (born March 4, 1975) was the fifth Barney costume actor, playing the Barney suit from 1996 until 2009. He also was the Blue-Jay puppeteer in Come on Over to Barney's House and the polar bear in " Riff's Musical Zoo ". He performed Barney in most of the last episodes of Season 4 and It's Time For Counting while David Joyner and Bob West both left to film Barney's Great Adventure . Outside of the Barney franchise, he voiced Majin Buu and Kid Buu in the Funimation dub of the Dragon Ball franchise.
