" Welcome, Cousin Riff " is the first half of the first episode from the tenth season of Barney & Friends .

Plot

Barney informs Baby Bop and BJ that their cousin, "Riff" has moved into town!

Songs

  1. Barney Theme Song
  2. Good Morning
  3. I Just Can't Wait
  4. Me and My Family
  5. I Hear Music Everywhere
  6. A Friend Like You

Trivia

  • This episode marked:
    • The first episode where Barney , Baby Bop , BJ , and Riff are the only main characters.
  • After not being used for seven years, the song, " Good Morning " returns in this episode, since Barney's A Great Day for Learning .
  • Camille and Cosette Goldstein (the actresses who portray Sophie and Sadie ) make cameo appearances in this episode.
