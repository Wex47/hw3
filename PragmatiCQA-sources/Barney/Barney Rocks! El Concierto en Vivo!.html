Barney Rocks! - El Concierto en Vivo! (Barney Rocks! Live in Concert) is a stage show that was performed in Mexico from 2010 until 2011. The show's title and premise are inspired by the album Barney Rocks! , which had been released in 2001 in Latin America. In addition, many of the songs in the show are taken from the album as well as other Barney media.

Plot

Barney and his friends form their own band and perform their favorite songs for the audience, giving a new rock twist to old Barney favorites.
