" On the Road Again " is the nineteenth episode from the ninth season of Barney & Friends .

Plot

To help teach the kids about car safety, Barney allows his friends to play in his make-believe car. Meanwhile, BJ enters a go-kart race!

Trivia

  • When Barney says the dialogue line "It's a key", his mouth doesn't move.
  • During the song " Riding in a Race Car ", BJ rode a Go-Kart at the Adventure Speedway in Wheatfield, New York.
  • Footage of this episode was included in one of Jimmy Kimmel's segments of Unnecessary Censorship: http://www.youtube.com/watch?v=iDkfxe4L2KM
  • This is the first episode to have only the dinosaur characters sing the " I Love You " song, entirely without the kids, due to the fact that they left early before the song.
  • This is the first time BJ and Baby Bop leave after the kids leave.
  • This episode was featured in Planes, Trains & Cars .

International Edits

  • In some international versions, this episode was shortened.
