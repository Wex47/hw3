Movin' Along is the fourth episode from the ninth season of Barney & Friends .

Plot

Barney and the children learn how they can move their bodies in different activities.

Ending Intervals : Cover Your Sneeze Please and Be a Friend

Trivia

  • This episode was originally part of Movin' and Groovin' before it was split into this episode and " Let's Make Music! " It was also featured in Dance with Barney .

International Edits

  • In some international versions, this episode was shortened.
