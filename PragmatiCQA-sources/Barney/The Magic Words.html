" The Magic Words " is the first half of the second episode from the eleventh season of Barney & Friends .

Plot

When Baby Bop forgets to use "Please" and "Thank You", Barney tells her story about a princess who could not say the magic words because she did not use them often.

Trivia

  • This episode is featured in Please & Thank You .
  • This episode marks the first appearances of Sofia and Victor .
  • After not being sung for 4 years, " Good Manners " returns in this episode since " Play Piano With Me! ".
