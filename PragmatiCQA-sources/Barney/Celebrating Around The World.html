Plot

Ben and Tracy are friends that can't decide on anything! So when they decide to throw a party, they can't decide how to celebrate it. So, Barney takes the kids on his Imagination Train to show them how other countries throw parties. They visit Brazil, Ireland, Japan, Kenya and India and it ends with a grand party inside the caboose.
