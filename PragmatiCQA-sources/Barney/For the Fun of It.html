" For the Fun of It " is the first part of the tenth episode from the eleventh season of Barney & Friends .

Plot

After BJ sees Terry play basketball, he wants to learn his moves and play just as well. But despite all of BJ's attempts to emulate him, he cannot seem to manage it. He ends up getting so focused on his inabilities that the whole game ceases to be any fun for him. However, after some good advice from Barney and Terry, BJ decides to do his best and persevere, eventually winning in the end.
