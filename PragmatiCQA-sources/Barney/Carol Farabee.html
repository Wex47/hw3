Carol Lynn Farabee (born October 9, 1960 in Dallas County, Texas) is an actress who performed Baby Bop 's speaking voice in Imagination Island . She was also the understudy for Baby Bop and BJ in Kids' Playfest and Barney Live! In New York City , voiced the parrot in " Ship, Ahoy! " and played the Queen of Hearts in Be My Valentine, Love Barney .
