Plot

Melanie helps Grace's food drive by making a movie with help from all of her friends! Everyone tries to help out with Baby Bop wanting a movie with dancing, Riff wanting a movie with music, and BJ wanting an adventure movie, but so many scenes are bound to become confusing. Though they struggle along the way, Melanie decides to combine all of their scenes into a comedy that everyone can see and enjoy.

Trivia

  • This episode marked:
    • The final appearance of Melanie .
    • The first appearances of Grace , Sharon , and Fetch the dog.
    • The only episode appearance of Grace, she will appear in the new content of " A-Counting We Will Go ", which was filmed in late 2009.
    • The first time since season 3 where the Barney doll at the end is shown from the waist up.
    • The last time David and Melanie appear together.
  • When the movie is about to start, Baby Bop says that she's never been to the movies before, but in " Grandpa's Visit ", Barney, Baby Bop and the kids were on their way to the movies.
