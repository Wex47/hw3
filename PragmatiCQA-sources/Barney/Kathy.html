Katherine “Kathy” Morson was a character who appeared in the first three seasons of Barney & Friends . Earlier than that, she appeared as one of the new friends in the Barney & The Backyard Gang video Rock with Barney . She was played by Lauren King . She was replaced by Emily .

About Kathy

  • Family : Lives with mother, grandmother (Nana), and a parakeet ( Priscilla) in an apartment. Has no siblings. Her grandmother appeared in Grandparents Are Grand! .
