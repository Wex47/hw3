" It's Showtime! " is the nineteenth episode from the eighth season of Barney & Friends . It is a semi-remake of the late first generation home video Barney's Talent Show .

Plot

Barney and the children spend the day preparing for a performance for their friends in the park. BJ has trouble deciding what talent he'll perform in the show, but when he's reminded to just be himself, he discovers a talent that rocks the park!
