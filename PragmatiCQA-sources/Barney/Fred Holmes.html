Fred Holmes

Nationality

American

Occupation

Former director and writer

Years Active

1996-present

Frederick Oliver "Fred" Holmes (born February 4, 1951) is was a former director and writer for the Barney franchise. He began working for the Barney franchise in 1996.

About Fred Holmes

Fred Holmes is an Emmy Award winning writer and director with experience in everything from television documentaries shot all over the world, to network television, to feature films, and everything in between.

Specialties

Writing and directing motion pictures and television. I am also a published author and have written and directed a play.
