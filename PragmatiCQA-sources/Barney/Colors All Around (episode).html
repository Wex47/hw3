" Colors All Around " is the eighth episode from the fifth season of Barney & Friends .

Plot

Mr. Boyd needs help deciding what color to paint the cafeteria wall. The children help him decide by exploring the colors with Barney's special color hat. Choosing colored items from the hat prompts them to act out nursery rhymes and sing songs. They also explore new colors that are created when mixing two colors together when they start painting pictures. Barney throws the hat into the air and a rainbow spills out onto the playground: a perfect mural for the hallway.

Trivia

  • This is the only time Mr. Boyd sees Barney coming to life.

International Edits

  • In the Korean episode, there is a wooden battery powered train instead of the one in the English version.
  • I Put a Smile On is omitted from the Korean episode, although the beginning of the instrumental can be heard.
  • This is one of the few Korean episodes to have an American format of the ending in which the kids actually say goodbye to Barney and leave before he turns back into a doll. (Normally, the Korean episodes would have Barney disappear during the second verse of "I Love You", and then pan over to him as a doll when the song ends).
