" Mr. Knickerbocker " is a popular children's rap about a silly man who likes to "boppity-bop." In the Barney franchise, it first appeared in Three Wishes . It subsequently appeared in Barney in Concert , where it introduces Baby Bop . Her name was inspired by the song lyrics.
