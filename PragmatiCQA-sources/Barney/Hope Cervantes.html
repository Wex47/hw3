Hope Lashee Cervantes (born August 19, 1984 (age 37) in Dallas, Texas ) is a former child actress who played Tosha from the first season to the third season of Barney & Friends with one return appearance in the home video, Sing and Dance with Barney .

In 1994, she appeared in the video, Kids Telling Kids.

She is of mixed African-American and Latin American descent. She has a brother named Juan.

Personal Life

In 1999, Cervantes started attending Booker T. Washington High School for the Performing and Visual Arts in Dallas, Texas, graduating in 2002.
