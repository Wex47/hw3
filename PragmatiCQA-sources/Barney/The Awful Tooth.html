" The Awful Tooth " is the second part of the sixteenth episode from the eleventh season of Barney & Friends .

Plot

After Riff experiences a toothache, Barney suggests for him to go see a dentist. However, Riff is afraid of the dentist, so Barney and his friends show him that the dentist is his friend.
