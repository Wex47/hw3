" The Misbegotten Moon: A Space Adventure " is the second episode from the twelfth season of Barney & Friends .

Plot

After noticing parts of the moon are missing in the night sky, Baby Bop is determined to find the rest of it. It's up to Barney and the kids to help her take a trip to outer space where they meet Mr. Moon.
