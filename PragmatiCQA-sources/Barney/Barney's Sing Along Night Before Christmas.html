Barney's Sing Along: Night Before Christmas

Audio Cassette

Released

1999
Barney's Sing Along: Night Before Christmas is an album/the fourth and final tape in the series of sing along tapes. It was released on October 26, 1999. This particular one coincides with the release of Barney's Night Before Christmas . Along with the cassette came a lyric book was included.

Songs

  1. Winter's Wonderful
  2. Oh, Christmas Tree
  3. Christmas is Our Favorite Time of Year
  4. Jolly Old St. Nicholas
  5. Look into Santa's Book
  6. Jingle Bells
  7. We Wish You a Merry Christmas
  8. I Love You

Trivia

  • Despite not singing the song in the video, Barney's Night Before Christmas , Barney sings "We Wish You a Merry Christmas " in this album.
  • This was the last Barney album of 1999 .
