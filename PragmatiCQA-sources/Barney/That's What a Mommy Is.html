" That's What a Mommy Is " is the second part of the fifteenth episode from the eleventh season of Barney & Friends .

Plot

After seeing many moms playing with their kids in the park, Baby Bop is curious as to what makes somebody a mommy. With Barney's help, she learns all about what makes mommies so special.

Trivia

  • This episode marks:
    • The only appearance of Heidi .
  • This episode is a play on the song, " That's What an Island Is ".
  • Summer Selby, the actress that portrayed Miss Crisp in " Ready, Set, Go! ", returns to play Mrs. Charles in this episode.
  • It is revealed that Tracy's last name is Charles.
  • After not being used for 12 years, the song, " Hush, Little Baby " returns in this episode, since Barney's Sleepytime Songs .
    • Additionally, " Hooray for Moms and Dads " returns after another 12 year hiatus since " At Home with Animals ".
