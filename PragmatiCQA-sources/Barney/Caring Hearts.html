" Caring Hearts" is the second episode from the ninth season of Barney & Friends .

Plot

David, Whitney, and Nick are working on a school project: "A Friends and Family Tree". They hang a paper heart with a person's name on it. From brothers and sisters to doctors and grandparents, Barney shows his friends that there are a lot of people that care for them.

Trivia

  • This episode was featured in Dino-Mite Valentine's Sing-Along .
  • This episode was featured in Please & Thank You .
  • This episode was originally part of Happy Mad Silly Sad .
  • This is the only season 9 episode where Barney doesn't turn back into a doll.
  • It is revealed that Whitney's grandmother is a doctor.

International Edits

  • In some international versions, this episode was shortened.
