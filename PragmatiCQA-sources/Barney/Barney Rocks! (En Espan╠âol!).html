Barney Rocks! (retitled Barney Rocks! (En Espanol!) for its digital release) is a Spanish album released that was released on March 2, 2001. The album was re-released in 2009 under HIT Entertainment and Universal Music Group International.

Barney Rocks! is the Spanish version of Barney Rocks! .

Trivia

  • This is the first spanish album in which Barney is voiced by Ruben Cerda
  • Duncan Brannan , Julie Johnson , and Patty Wirtz are mentioned to be voicing Barney , Baby Bop , and BJ respectively on the back of the album. This is an error as this album is dubbed in Spanish.
