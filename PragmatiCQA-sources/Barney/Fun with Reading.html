" Fun with Reading " is the second part of the twelfth episode from the tenth season of Barney & Friends .

Plot

BJ plays pretend as his alter ego, Captain Pickles , in the park. However, after a crash hurts his foot, BJ is discouraged to learn that he will need to spend the rest of his day indoors, resting. Barney , Baby Bop and Riff suggest reading as a way to pass the time. Afterwards, the group writes and illustrates their own book about Captain Pickles.

Songs

  1. Captain Pickles
  2. Books Are Fun!
  3. Won't You Imagine with Me
  4. The Library
  5. I Love You

Trivia

  • This episode marked:
    • The only appearance of Audrey.
    • The final appearances of Bridget and Emma.
    • Episode 1012, which combines this and " Playing Games ", marked the two-hundredth episode of Barney & Friends .
    • The second episode to feature an all female cast of the kids, the first time being " Vets '.
