"Sleep Song" is an original Barney song that first appeared in " Doctor Barney Is Here! ".

Trivia

  • Tosha, Derek, Kathy and Michael sing this song in " Doctor Barney Is Here! ". Barney also sings this song in Barney's Sleepytime Songs .
  • This song shares its tune with " Swingin' Up to the Stars ". Coincidentally, both songs appeared on the album Barney's Sleepytime Songs .
