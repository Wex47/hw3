Gameplay

Barney and the player visit six super-dee-duper spots on the farm in two fun ways. In Activity Mode, the player can learn about animal names and sounds, different fruits and vegetables, and more. And in Explore Mode, the player can be more creative in their gameplay. This game consists of the following activities:

  • Professor Tinkerputt's Duck Pond
  • BJ's Pig Pen
  • Grandpa Tractor's Garden
  • Baby Bop's Chicken Coop
  • Hootin' Annie's Musical Barn
  • Video Farmhouse: Features clips from The Dentist Makes Me Smile and Barney's 1-2-3-4 Seasons .
