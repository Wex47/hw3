" Playing Games " is the first part of the twelfth episode from the tenth season of Barney & Friends .

Plot

Barney and the other dinosaurs decide to spend the day playing games in the park. However, after several attempts at playing where Baby Bop does not follow rules, BJ becomes frustrated with her. Barney and Riff show her the importance of following rules, and the group is finally able to play a successful game of Ring Around The Rosie .

Songs

  1. Barney Theme Song
  2. London Bridge
  3. Games
  4. Taking Turns
  5. Ring Around the Rosie
  6. A Friend Like You

Trivia

  • This is included in the Barney episode video Play with Barney .
  • Episode 1012, which includes this episode alongside Fun with Reading , marks it as the two-hundredth episode of Barney & Friends .
