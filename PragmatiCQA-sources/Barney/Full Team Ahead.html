" Full Team Ahead " is the second half of the first episode from the eleventh season of Barney & Friends .

Plot

After not putting Riff on his baseball team, BJ hurts Riff's feelings. BJ learns that playing baseball with friends is the best way to have fun!

Trivia

  • This was the last episode written by Stephen White .
  • In the beginning of this episode, Barney stands in the caboose waiting to come out of it.
  • This episode was featured in Most Huggable Moments .
  • This was the first Season 11 episode being filmed, as a shooting photo for this is in the scrapbook from Dino-Mite Birthday .
