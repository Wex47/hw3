The Land of Make-Believe is a soundtrack with songs from the home video of the same name and is Barney's fifteenth US album. It was released on CD and cassette on August 23, 2005 by Koch Records and HIT Entertainment .

It was the fourth Barney US soundtrack to be released based on a video and the final Barney album to be released on cassette.
