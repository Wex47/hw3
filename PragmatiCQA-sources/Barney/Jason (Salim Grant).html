Jason (Salim Grant)

Series Information

First appearance

Last appearance

Jason was a character who only appeared in the first three videos in the Barney & The Backyard Gang series before being replaced by Derek . He was played by Salim Grant . None of the videos explained why Jason is not with the gang anymore, or why he never appeared on Barney & Friends .

Appearances

  1. The Backyard Show (first appearance)
  2. Three Wishes
  3. A Day At The Beach (last appearance)

Trivia

  • He never appeared with Derek , Baby Bop , or Jeffrey .
