" Arts " is the second half of the sixth episode from the tenth season of Barney & Friends .

Plot

It's a special day in the park as Barney and his friends enjoy an Art Festival. BJ and Riff share some "musical art". Baby Bop shares a painting of a very special friend, "Barney"! As an art festival comes to an end, Barney reminds his friends everywhere that no matter what kind of art they make, it's always special when it comes from their hearts!

Songs

  1. The Fiesta Song
  2. What Shall We Make Today?
  3. Colors Make Me Happy
  4. The Dino Dance
  5. I Love You

Trivia

  • This episode marks the only appearance of Jessica.
  • After not being used for seven years, the song, " The Fiesta Song " returns in this episode, since " Birthday Olé ".
