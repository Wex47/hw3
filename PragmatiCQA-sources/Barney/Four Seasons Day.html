" Four Seasons Day " is the sixth episode from the first season of Barney & Friends .

Plot

Talk about the weather! The kids do much more than that as Barney squeezes all four seasons into one rollicking make-believe holiday. You won't believe how much fun you can have celebrating the wind, the rain and the snow. There is is a barrel-full of songs about Spring's showers, Summer's warmth, Fall's leaves and Winter's chill.

Trivia

  • This episode marks:
    • The first episode to use the magic words Shimbaree, Shimbarah , from the Barney & The Backyard Gang series.
    • The second episode where the Barney doll doesn't wink.
    • The second episode to contain a song during the Barney Says segment.
    • In this episode, Barney's voice sounds a bit dry, as in the last 2 Barney & The Backyard Gang videos. This may mean this was one of the first episodes filmed for this season, and that would happen for some other episodes as well.
    • The first time the Barney doll wears something.
    • The first episode where Barney and the kids stay outside on the playground the whole episode.
  • During the Barney Says segment, there is an alternate angle shot of Michael and Shawn together after Barney sings the first two lines of " Skating, Skating ".
  • We learn that winter is Luci's favorite time of the year.
  • The Barney doll doesn't wink at the end; instead a snowflake lands on his nose, and melts off with a spark.
  • This is the second time Barney falls asleep. The first was in Waiting for Santa .
  • The kids at the beginning of the episode whom Luci says goodbye to were named Sarah and Louis.
  • In the Hebrew version, all of the songs were a semitone lower except for the Barney Theme Song. In that same version, If It Never Ever Rained Again and Fall Song weren't sung. Mister Sun was sung, but it was shortened, plus, the Barney doll does actually wink from that version.
