Barney's Sing Along: In Outer Space

Sing-Along Cassette

Released

May 18, 1999
Barney's Sing Along In Outer Space is a sing-along cassette. It's the third of the Barney's Sing Along series of cassette tapes. It was released on May 18, 1999 and coincides with the home video, Barney in Outer Space .

Songs

  1. The Rocket Song
  2. Mr. Star
  3. Floating Together So Free
  4. The Sun
  5. Way Up in Outer Space
  6. I Love You
