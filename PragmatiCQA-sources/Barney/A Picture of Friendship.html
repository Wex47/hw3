" A Picture of Friendship " is the tenth episode from the eighth season of Barney & Friends .

Plot

Barney and the children learn what it takes to be a good friend after accidentally hurting BJ's feelings for forgetting to add him in a drawing.

Trivia

  • This is the last season eight episode to air in the US in 2003.
  • After not being sung for eleven years, the song, " This Is Not My Day " returns in this episode since " Oh, What A Day! ".
  • After not being sung for six years, the song, " That is What it Means to Be A Friend " returns in this episode since Camp Wannarunnaround .
  • This episode was featured in Sharing is Caring (2009) and I Love My Friends (2012).
