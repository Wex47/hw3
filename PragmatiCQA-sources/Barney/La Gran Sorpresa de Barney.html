La Gran Sorpresa de Barney is a live spanish album with songs from the Mexican production of Barney's Big Surprise . The album was released in 1998 by Tycoon Music. It was later re-released in 2005 when the show toured in Argentina.

La Gran Sorpressa de Barney was the second live album released by Barney, after its english counterpart, Barney's Big Surprise! Live on Stage , and the first live spanish album released.
