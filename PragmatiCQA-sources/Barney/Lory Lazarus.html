Lory Lazarus is a composer and lyricist who worked on Barney and the Backyard Gang as well as on Barney & Friends . Working on the first three videos with the late Stephen Bates Baltes , he wrote the first Barney song, " Friends Are Forever ". They were convinced by friends that "this dinosaur thing will never get anywhere" and they left the franchise. In 1995 , he did return to the show, again collaborating with Stephen Bates, working on new songs for the third season of Barney & Friends .
