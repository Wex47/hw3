Joseph K. Phillips (born August 7, 1959) is an American musical director, composer and songwriter for the Barney franchise.

About Joseph Phillips

Joseph Phillips is the owner and Operator of Joe Phillips Productions. It was founded in 1996 in Dallas, Texas from 1996-Present. Joe was also a composer/ instructor at Full Sail University (a private college in Orlando, Florida). Joe's music composition for the Barney franchise was formerly done at Soundelux Florida, Inc.

Trivia

  • The music for Kids for Character was all done by Joe Phillips.
  • Joseph also did the music for The Wiggles Video and Audio Collection trailer with his instrumental arrangements of "Get Ready to Wiggle" and "Hot Potato"
  • Sometimes, he was credited as Joe Phillips or Joseph K. Phillips.
  • The music for the new material of Barney Songs was done by Soundelux Florida, Inc.
