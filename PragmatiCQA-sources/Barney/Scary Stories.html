Scary Stories is an original Barney song that first appeared in " A Camping We Will Go! ".

Lyrics

Origin of Song

The melody for the song was originally used for " The Stranger Song ", which was first sung in Playing It Safe , making it one of the first original Barney songs to have its melody recycled for another original Barney song.
