Shawn was a character that appeared in the first three seasons of Barney & Friends. He was played by John David Bennett II . He was replaced by Chip .

About Shawn

  • Favorite Food : Birthday Cake
  • Best Friend : Kathy
  • Began wearing glasses in Picture This!
  • Allergic to animal hair
  • Used to be scared of dogs, due to being scratched by one.
