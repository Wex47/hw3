" I'm A Builder " is the tenth episode from the ninth season of Barney & Friends . It is a semi-remake of " Let's Build Together ".

Plot

With a little imagination, Barney and his friends use boxes to build a castle for Baby Bop's teddy bear who is dressed up as a princess!

Trivia

  • This is the last episode written by Mark S. Bernthal .
  • This is also the last season nine episode to air in 2004.
  • An alternate scene of the kids greeting Barney was filmed during the production for this episode and Just Imagine . In the TV version, the kids greet and hug Barney when he arrived while in the home video version, David was the only one to greet and hug Barney. This is because the video, Just Imagine features this episode and " Keep On Truckin' ", with David appearing in the second half of the video after Jackson left in the first half of the video.
  • This is the only season nine episode where David and Rachel appear together. They would later appear together in some season ten and eleven episodes.

International Edits

  • In some international versions, this episode was shortened.
