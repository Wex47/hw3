" The New Kid " is the first part of the sixth episode from the eleventh season of Barney & Friends .

Plot

After Riff makes friends with new kid Noah, BJ becomes jealous and thinks he is not Riff's friend anymore. Barney teaches him that you can always keep your old friendships and make new ones too.

Songs

  1. Barney Theme Song
  2. The Friendship Song
  3. I Miss You
  4. The Rocket Song
  5. A Friend Like You

Trivia

  • This episode marks the first appearances of Eva and Noah.
