Madison Michelle Pettis (born July 22, 1998 in Arlington, Texas) is an American actress who portrayed Bridget in Season 10 on Barney & Friends . She is best known for playing Sophie Martinez in the Disney sitcom Cory in the House and as Peyton Kelly in the 2007 film The Game Plan . She currently lives in Los Angeles, California. She also worked for Life with Boys and Jake and the Neverland Pirates . She is also a model for Love Pastry with Alli Simpson and Cody Simpson.

She is African American and also of French and Italian descent (which makes her a Latina actress).
