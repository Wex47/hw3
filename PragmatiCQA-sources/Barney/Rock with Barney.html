Rock with Barney is the eighth and final video in the Barney & The Backyard Gang series. It was released exclusively in Neiman Marcus stores in August 1991 . This video had a wide release on January 27, 1992 .

Plot

Adam's mother takes the Backyard Gang and Barney to a TV studio where she works. While there, Barney and the Backyard Gang tour the studio on their own, while producing their own show. From a cereal commercial set and a puppet show and the six little ducks and teddy bears and the US flag and an island paradise and a make up place to a jungle, the show is filmed along with many props in the studio. With the power of imagination and some movie magic, anything is possible when Barney's around.

Educational Theme : The parts of a Movie Studio / Ways to Protect the Earth
