Yankee Doodle is a well-known American song, the early versions of which date to before the Seven Years' War and the American Revolution (1775–83). This song first appeared in " Alphabet Soup! ". It is best known in Barney media as the tune for the " Barney Theme Song " (The Barney theme wasn't the first theme song to the tune of Yankee Doodle, Roger Ramjet was the first kids show to use a theme song in the tune of Yankee Doodle).
