" No, No, No! " is the second part of the eleventh episode from the eleventh season of Barney & Friends .

Plot

It's just "one of those days" for Baby Bop! Every time she turns around, someone is telling her "no!" As her frustrations grow, Barney explains that the word "no" can help you avoid making mistakes, getting hurt and more. He tells her the story of " Cinderella ".

Stories : Cinderella

Songs

  1. The Mad Song
  2. Clean Up
  3. Nobody Likes To Hear No!
  4. The Dino Dance
  5. I Love You

Trivia

  • This episode was featured in This Is How I Feel .
  • Lacy Cavalier, Sinjin Vengas, and Leesa Zimmerman (the actors/actresses that portray Megan , Ethan , and Lacey respectively) appear as some of the "Royal Ball Dancers".
  • When everyone is suppose to be frozen (when the clock stroke 12), you can see the kids moving in the background.
  • This is the first time BJ disappears alongside Baby Bop and Riff while they leave with Melanie and Ryan .
