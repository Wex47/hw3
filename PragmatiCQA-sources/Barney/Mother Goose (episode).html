" Mother Goose " is the first part of the sixteenth episode from the tenth season of Barney & Friends .

Plot

The characters from The Land of Mother Goose come to the caboose in search for their lost things.

Songs

  1. Barney Theme Song
  2. The Land of Mother Goose
  3. Little Miss Muffet
  4. Polly Put the Kettle On
  5. Little Boy Blue
  6. I'm Mother Goose
  7. Mother Goose Nursery Rhyme Medley: Little Miss Muffet / Polly Put the Kettle On / Little Boy Blue
  8. A Friend Like You

Trivia

  • This episode marks:
    • This episode marks the only appearance of Kaitlyn.
    • This is the first time we see Mother Goose wear an outfit that's not in the style from 1992-2000.
  • Even though BJ says The Land of Mother Goose isn't real, he has been there in Barney's Rhyme Time Rhythm .
  • The actors who played Nick , Kami , and Beth on Barney & Friends appear as Little Boy Blue, Little Miss Muffet, and Polly respectively, which marks the formal departure of Grayson Lee Vanover , Makayla Crawford and Katherine Pully from the Barney franchise.
  • Mother Goose is played by Julie Johnson , who also performs the voice of Baby Bop.
  • After not being used for six years, the song, " The Land of Mother Goose " returns in this episode, since Barney's Dino Dancin' Tunes .
  • After not being used for six years, the song, " I'm Mother Goose " returns in this episode, since " A "Little" Mother Goose ".
  • This episode was featured in Egg-Cellent Adventures .
