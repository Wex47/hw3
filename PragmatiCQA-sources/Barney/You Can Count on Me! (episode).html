" You Can Count On Me!" is the thirteenth episode from the ninth season of Barney & Friends .

Plot

Nick shows Barney and the rest of the kids his number cards that he and his mom had put together. He doesn't really think they're fun at first, but Barney shows him how numbers and counting can be lots of fun!

Trivia

  • This is the only episode in which the song, " A Silly Hat " is shortened.
  • This episode was shown in Dinos in the Park .

International Edits

  • In some international versions, this episode was shortened.
