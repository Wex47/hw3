Grayson Lee Vanover (born May 29, 1994 in Coppell, Texas) is the actor who portrayed Nick on Barney & Friends from Seasons 7 to 9 . He has a brother named Alex.

Before his appearance on the television series, he appeared in Come on Over to Barney's House , a Barney Home Video released in 2000. He also made a cameo appearance in Season 10 , playing Little Boy Blue in the episode Mother Goose , which means overall he worked for the franchise from 2000 to late 2005.
