" Falling For Autumn!" is the first episode from the second season of Barney & Friends .

Plot

Barney and his friends prepare for the Fall Festival at school.

Trivia

  • This episode marks:
    • The first appearance of David .
    • The first appearance of a new costume for Baby Bop.
    • The first appearance of a new Barney doll, with yellow toes being used for the first time, since A Day at the Beach .
    • The first time Jeff Ayers performs as Baby Bop .
    • The first appearance of the redesigned Barney Bag.
    • The only episode to have the Barney doll's tail "twinkle" at the beginning.
    • The ninth time where Time Lapse is used. Tina and Baby Bop were on a peanut race before Shawn caught up to them quickly.
  • Filming for this episode took place in March 1993.
  • This Episode Starts:
    • The camera fades to the doll, rather than pan towards it once the kids leave.
    • The pictures in the end credits are the same in the credits for Seasons 2 and 3 .
