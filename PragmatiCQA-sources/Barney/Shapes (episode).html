Plot

Barney and his friends prepare for the Shapes Carnival in the park. Riff knows he will do well at the carnival because he is wearing his lucky medal. Suddenly, Riff is panicked that his good luck medal is gone, so Barney and the others help him find it. When Riff can't find his good luck medal anywhere, he won't be able to play the games. With Barney's guidance, Riff has fun playing the games and he realized he didn't need his lucky medal play and have fun.
