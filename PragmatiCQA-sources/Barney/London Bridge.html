" London Bridge " is a nursery rhyme or children's song and playground singing game. It first appeared in print in 1881, but it is reported that a version was already being sung to the current tune in the 1790s and similar rhymes are known from across Europe. The song first appeared in the Barney franchise in Three Wishes .
