Trivia

  • Before making its actual debut in Come on Over to Barney's House , this song was used in " Barney's Theatre ".
  • This song was included in the Barney book, " I Love You Songs ".
  • The song, " The Dino Bengal Dance " uses the same tune as this song, but the lyrics were changed to better fit in Barney Live! World Tour - A Celebration ! .
  • This song has been shortened in some occasions.
