Min is a character who appeared in the first 3 seasons of Barney & Friends . She was played by Pia Hamilton . She appeared in a total of 50 episodes, making her the kid with the most number of appearances on Barney & Friends .

About Min

  • Family : Lives with her mom, her dad, two brothers, two sisters, her grandma and grandpa, and a golden laborador (Princess) as well as a parakeet and a cat. Later in the series, her dog had puppies. One of her sisters appeared in What's That Shadow? .
