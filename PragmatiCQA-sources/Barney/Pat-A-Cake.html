" Pat-A-Cake " is one of the oldest and most widely known surviving English nursery rhymes. It was published in 1698 but it is unknown who wrote the song. The song first appeared in the Barney franchise in Three Wishes .

Lyrics

Pat-A-Cake, Pat-A-Cake, Baker's man

Bake me a cake as fast as you can.

Roll it, and pat it, and mark it with a "B",

and put it in the oven for Moonkin/Barney/baby and me.
