" How Does Your Garden Grow? " is the sixteenth episode from the sixth season of Barney & Friends .

  • 1 Plot
  • 2 Songs

Plot

Barney and his friends experience the fun of gardening. With some help from Barney and Mr. Boyd, the kids harvest their crop from the large garden on the playground. All in all, the group shares a day of fun and hard work. They also reap the many fruits-and vegetables-of their labor.

Trivia

  • This is the only time Jill appeared with Kim and Mr. Boyd.
  • This is the second time the Barney doll gets wet, the first time being " A Day At The Beach ".
  • This is the second time Scooter McNutty appeared without Miss Etta Kette . The first time was Waiting For Mr. MacRooney .

International Edits

  • In some international versions, this episode was shortened.
