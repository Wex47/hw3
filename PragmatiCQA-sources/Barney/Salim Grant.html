Salim Dolphine Ulysses Grant (born May 28, 1977 in Dallas, Texas) is an actor who played the character Jason in the first three Barney & The Backyard Gang videos before being replaced by Rickey Carter as Derek .

Salim appeared in a commercial for Sesame Street when he was 4 years old. When he was 5, he appeared in several theater productions with a local children's group called Too Short for Prime Time . He was also in the 1990 Bill Cosby movie, Ghost Dad . He starred as R.J. "Hollywood" Collins on Saved by the Bell: The New Class from 1995 to 1996.
