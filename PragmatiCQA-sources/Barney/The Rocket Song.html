The Rocket Song is an original Barney song that debuted in Three Wishes . The song is sung to the tune of The Caissons Go Rolling Along (also known as The Army Song , The Caisson Song or The Army Goes Rolling Along ).

Trivia

  • Whenever this song is performed, nobody really travels into space, rather, graphic effects are used to make the viewers (children) think that they are in space.
  • In Stick with Imagination! , Stephen and Keesha wore bike helmets, jackets and winter gloves.
  • In Read with Me , Gianna wore an orange suit, a football helmet and rain boots.
  • In Grown-Ups for a Day! it is easy to see that the rocket ship Derek flew in was on earth the whole time. Cause the Prodution crew used a Blue screen
