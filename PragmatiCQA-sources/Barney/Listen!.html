" Listen! " is the second half of the seventh episode from the eleventh season of Barney & Friends .

Plot

When Riff hears a mysterious sound in the park, he plays detective, trying to solve the mystery. Barney and the children join in the fun. Along the way, they explore the sense of sound while identifying sounds of nature and the neighborhood.

  2. I'm a Builder (Scene Taken from: " I'm A Builder " / Just Imagine )

  4. You Can Make Music with Anything
  5. I Love You
