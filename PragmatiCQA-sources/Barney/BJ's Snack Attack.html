" BJ's Snack Attack " is the second half of the seventh episode from tenth season of Barney & Friends .

Plot

After BJ gets sick from eating too many sweet treats, he has a dream of a fantasy world where he can have as many sweet treats as he wants. Except, even in the dream, he develops a stomach-ache.

Trivia

  • This episode marks the only appearance of Hope.
  • When BJ feels sick, the green effect is shown on his face.
  • This is the second time BJ gets a stomach ache, the first time being Circle of Friends .
