Brushing My Teeth is an original Barney song that first appeared in Our Earth, Our Home .

Lyrics

Trivia

  • The instrumental track of this song is heard during "The Three Little Pigs" story in Home Sweet Homes .
  • In Room for Everyone , the last verse and chorus wasn't sung and so did Barney's Sleepytime Songs
  • A Bear in the Big Blue House song Brush Brush Bree has the same situation as this one shown here since they’re both about brushing teeth. In fact, Barney met Bear in a We Are Family A Musical Message For All promo.
