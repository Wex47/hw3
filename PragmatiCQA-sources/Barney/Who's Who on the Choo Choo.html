" Who's Who On The Choo Choo? " is the sixteenth episode from the third season of Barney & Friends .

Plot

Barney and his friends keep hearing trains passing through the playground, but they can't seem to get outside in time to see them. Stella the Storyteller stops by to tell one of her wonderful stories about trains.

Behind the Scenes

Trivia

  • This is the only time Kenneth appeared with Baby Bop and Stella the Storyteller .
  • This is the first time Barney, BJ, and Baby Bop all meet at the same time. In addition, this is the only time they appear immediately after Barney comes to life.
  • On January 23, 1996, this episode was released on home video as Barney's All Aboard For Sharing .

International Edits

  • In some international versions, this episode was shortened.
  • The songs Little Red Caboose and I've Been Working on The Railroad were sung, but shortened, however, Have a Snack wasn't sung.
