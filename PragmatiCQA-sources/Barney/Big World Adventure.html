Big World Adventure is a Barney Clip Show that was released on September 13, 2011. It features scenes from Season 13 of Barney & Friends .

Plot

When all their friends are out of town, Ben and Emma are trying to have fun. Barney soon arrives in his hot air balloon, willing to take the two kids on a worldwide adventure, recalling memories of various trips from season thirteen episodes.
