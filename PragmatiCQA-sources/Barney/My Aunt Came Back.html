My Aunt Came Back is a children's song that first appeared in Going Places! . It is the tune of On the Lone Prairie.

Trivia

  • My Aunt Came Back (along with its variation, Oh, We Came Back ) is considered one of the most difficult songs to translate into other languages, hence its absence from the Spanish-language dubbed versions of The Alphabet Zoo and On the Move , as well as the Korean version of Our Earth, Our Home .
