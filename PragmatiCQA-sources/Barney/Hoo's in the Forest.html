Plot

Behind the Scenes

Trivia

  • This episode reveals that Kathy 's favorite sound is the owl sound.
  • The same bear costume would later return in Super Singing Circus .

International Edits

  • In some international versions, this episode was shortened.
  • In the Hebrew version, Sarasponda and The Frog on the Log weren't sung. In that same version, the Season 1 version of I Love You was used, with the vocals being taken from the Hebrew version of A Splash Party, Please .
  • Also in the Hebrew version, the Barney doll appears to be playing drums at the end.
