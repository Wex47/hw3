Trivia

  • The 1988 audio was used again in " Rock with Barney ", " Down on Barney's Farm " and the companion album, "Barney's Favorites".
  • In "Barney's Big Surprise" and "Grown-Ups For A Day!", this song is renamed to "Four Little Ducks".
  • In "Twice is Nice!", this song is renamed to "Five Little Ducks".
