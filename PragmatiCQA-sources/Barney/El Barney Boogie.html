El Barney Boogie is the fifteenth Spanish album from Barney, released on December 1, 2007 under Tycoon Music and HIT Entertainment . It was later re-released in 2010.

On April 24, 2020, the album was transferred to Warner Music Group's Arts Music division as Mattel entered into an exclusive, multi-year global music licensing agreement between the company, announced on May 1, 2020.

El Barney Boogie is the Spanish version of The Barney Boogie . Unlike the original album, all the songs on this album were taken from the home video Movin' and Groovin' with " The Barney Boogie " added in the end.

It is the tenth Spanish Barney album to have an English counterpart.
