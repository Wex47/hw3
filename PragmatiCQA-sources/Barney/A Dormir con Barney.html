A Dormir con Barney is the eighth Spanish album from Barney, released on January 1, 2001 under HIT Entertainment .

On April 10, 2020, the album was transferred to Warner Music Group's Arts Music division, along with other Barney albums, as Mattel entered into an exclusive, multi-year global music licensing agreement between the company, announced on May 1, 2020.

A Dormir con Barney is the Spanish version of Barney For Baby: Love and Lullabies . Like it's English counterpart it is also made exclusively for babies.
