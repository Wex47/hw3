Leah Montes (born Leah Mikel Gloria on March 29, 1978 in Keller, Texas) was the actress who played Luci in the Barney & The Backyard Gang videos and the first season of Barney & Friends and was on the Season 2 episode " The Exercise Circus! " which was her last appearance on the show.

Montes decided to quit acting to become a hair stylist. She owns her own hair salon in Fort Worth, Texas. Montes as well has two daughters.
