" Sharing " is the second part of the fourteenth episode from the tenth season of Barney & Friends .

Plot

When Riff accidentally wrecks BJ's beloved scooter, he feels terrible! BJ loved that scooter more than almost anything! Riff and Baby Bop decide to have a sale in the park to raise money to buy BJ a new scooter.

Songs

  1. Together, Together
  2. What a Big Mistake!
  3. Pennies in My Pocket
  4. Best of Friends
  5. I Love You

Trivia

  • Mario makes a return/guest appearance in this episode.
  • This episode marks the final appearance of Matt. This is also his only appearance on the show.
  • This is the only time Riff appeared with Mario and Matt.
  • This episode was included in Dinos in the Park .
