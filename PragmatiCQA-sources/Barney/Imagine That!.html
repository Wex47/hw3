"Imagine That!" is the sixth episode from the ninth season of Barney & Friends .

Plot

By using their imagination, Barney and the kids turn Laura's picture into an island adventure!

Trivia

  • This is the last episode to use the Barney costume from seasons seven and season eight .
  • This episode was originally part of Let's Pretend with Barney before it was split into this episode and " Let Your Creativity Fly! " It was also featured in Imagine with Barney .

International Edits

  • In some international versions, this episode was shortened.
