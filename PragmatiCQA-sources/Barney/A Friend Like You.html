" A Friend Like You " is an original Barney song that first appeared in " Welcome, Cousin Riff ".

Lyrics

Trivia

  • The song is sung at the end of the first half of every episode in Seasons 10 and 11, except in " Gift of the Dinos ", where it is replaced with " We Wish You a Merry Christmas ".
  • This is among the few original songs written for the franchise which serve as a secondary theme song to the program.
