Plot

Captain Pickles and Wonder Princess both wanted to be the super-est superhero. When Captain Pickles said he could jump over the moon, everyone wanted to see. BJ knew he couldn't really jump that high, but he could jump high enough to save Emma's balloon. Everyone was proud of him, even if he couldn't jump over the moon. BJ was a hero.
