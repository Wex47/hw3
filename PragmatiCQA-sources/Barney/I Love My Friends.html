I Love My Friends is a Barney Clip Show that was released on January 3, 2012. It features scenes from Seasons 8 - 11 of Barney & Friends .

Plot

Katie, Logan and Olivia are planning to take their stuffed animal friends for a tour around the park. Baby Bop brings the wagon for the animals to ride in. But when their wagon breaks, it takes a friend like BJ to put it back together. However, not all animals can fit in the wagon. Learning about the horrible feeling of being left out, BJ proclaims that no animal should be left out. Barney produces a bigger wagon for all the animals to fit in.

    Trivia

    • Production for this video took place in October 2010.
    • This video marked:
      • The first video since Come on Over to Barney's House to be in the Classic Collection series. Incidentally, both videos omit the usage of the Barney Theme Song at the beginning.
      • The only appearances of Katie, Logan and Olivia.
      • The official end of the fourth generation of the Barney franchise (September 5, 2006 - January 3, 2012).
    • A widescreen version to this can be only seen if you download this in HD.
    • After not being used for 10 years, the season six Barney doll returns in this video, since Barney's Christmas Star .
    • Even though Riff doesn't appear in the new content of this video, he is mentioned.
    • Although this video was released in 2012, you can actually see "Copyright 2011 Lyons Partnership, L.P." at the end of the credits. This may mean that this video was completed in 2011.
