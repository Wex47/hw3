Booker T. Bookworm is a character who debuted in the home video It's Time for Counting and appeared in four episodes of the fifth season of Barney & Friends . He was voiced by Earl Fisher .

Bio

Booker T. is the imaginary librarian of the school library. As his name suggests, he is a literal bookworm, as in a worm who likes to feed on books. His name is a pun on the name Booker T. Washington , a well-known African American educator.
