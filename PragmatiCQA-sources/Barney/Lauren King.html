Lauren Elizabeth King (born June 28, 1985 in Plano, Texas ) is a former child actress who played Kathy in the Barney & The Backyard Gang video Rock with Barney and in Seasons 1 - 3 of Barney & Friends . she was replaced by Hannah Owens .

She was born to Pamela and Damon King. She is an only child.

She likes to Dance (especially Ballet, Jazz and Tap).

She married Joseph Saatkamp in 2015. She now lives in Nashville, Tennessee and works as an Account Executive for Vanderbilt IMG Sports Marketing at IMG College.

  • Lauren King shares a birthday with another Barney actress, Becky Swonke .
