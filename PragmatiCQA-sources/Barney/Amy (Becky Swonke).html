Amy was a character who only appeared in the Barney & The Backyard Gang video series. She was Michael 's sister and she was played by Becky Swonke .

Trivia

  • She never appeared in " Barney & Friends " because her actress had to leave the Barney franchise, due to an illness.
  • She was replaced by Min .
  • She is the only girl in the Backyard Gang who is of pure American descent, since the other 2 girls ( Tina and Luci ) were sisters of Mexican descent.
