Las Canciones de Barney is the debut Spanish album of Barney . It was released in 1997 on CD and cassette by Tycoon Music and Polydor Records. The album features a compilation of songs from the Spanish dub of the television show. The album was later released in Argentina in 2004 by HIT Entertainment and Universal Music Group Argentina.

Las Canciones de Barney is the Spanish equivalent to Barney's Favorites, Volume 1 , sharing the album's box-art as well as most of the songs.

Following the release of Las Canciones de Barney a sequel was released the same year.
