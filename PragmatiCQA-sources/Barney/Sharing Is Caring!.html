" Sharing Is Caring! " is the third episode from the eighth season of Barney & Friends .

Plot

The children bring toys to donate to Mr. Boyd's toy drive. Baby Bop doesn't want to give away any of her things at first, but the others help her discover the joy of sharing.

Trivia

  • Mr. Boyd is mentioned in this episode.
  • Although Robert doesn't appear in this episode, he is seen on the front book cover of "The Grasshopper and the Ants" from the episode " Seven Days A Week ".
  • After not being sung in the franchise for 3 years, " Home on the Range " returns in this episode since You Can Do It! . After this episode, it would not be sung for another 5 years until Riff to the Rescue!: A Wild West Adventure .
  • This episode was featured in the home video of the same name .
  • This is the last episode since Season 3 where the Barney doll waves at the end.
  • This group (Beth, Gianna, Scott) also appeared in Tea-riffic Manners .
