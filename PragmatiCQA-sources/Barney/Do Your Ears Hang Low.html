" Do Your Ears Hang Low? " is a children's song that is often sung in schools and at camps. The melody of this song is usually a shorter version of "Turkey in the Straw", but it can also be sung to the "Sailor's Hornpipe". It first appeared in the Barney franchise in " Three Wishes ".
