" Keep On Truckin' " is the ninth episode from the ninth season of Barney & Friends .

Plot

Barney and his friends keep on truckin'! BJ enjoys helping with his tow truck, while Barney shows everyone what it's like to be behind the wheel of a real truck. When Jackson's special truck loses a tire, Barney wheels in to the rescue!

Trivia

  • This episode marks:
    • The first appearance of Rachel .
    • The last episode in which Barney uses his closet.
    • The last episode and only epsiode from season nine where Jackson and Nick appear together.

International Edits

  • In some international versions, this episode was shortened.
