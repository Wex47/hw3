" Bop 'til You Drop " is the first part of the third episode from the eleventh season of Barney & Friends .

Plot

Barney steps in when Baby Bop gets a little too bossy with her friends when she had accidentally lost her pretty pink balloon. BJ gives Baby Bop a whistle for her to blow whenever she wants to play. However, she learns that being bossy isn't a nice way to behave, and at the end, Baby Bop gets a yellow balloon.
