" Boats " is the second half of the second episode from the tenth season of Barney & Friends .

Plot

Baby Bop has always dreamed of riding in a boat. BJ and Riff build a pretend sailboat for Barney and Baby Bop to sail on it.

Songs

  1. Row, Row, Row Your Boat
  2. Sailing, Sailing / A Sailor Went to Sea, Sea, Sea
  3. Together, Together
  4. I'd Love to Sail
  5. I Love You

Trivia

  • This episode marked the first appearances of Allison and Scott.
  • This is the first episode to have two kids after Let's Go to the Farm .
