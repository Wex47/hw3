El Parque Musical de Barney y Sus Amigos is a Spanish album with songs from the live stage show production of Barney's Musical Park . The album was released in 2004 by HIT Entertainment and EXIM Licensing Group.

El Parque Musical de Barney y Sus Amigos is the seventh Barney soundtrack to be released based on a live stage show production. It was the fourth Spanish soundtrack to be based on a live production after La Gran Sorpresa de Barney, El Castillo Musical de Barney , and Barney y su Mundo de Colores and it was the first soundtrack to be released based on a live show not done in the US.
