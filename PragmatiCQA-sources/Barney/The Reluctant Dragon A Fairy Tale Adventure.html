" The Reluctant Dragon: A Fairy Tale Adventure " is the tenth and final episode from the twelfth season of Barney & Friends .

Plot

After the kids judge a spider by its creepy looks, Barney tells them the story of " The Reluctant Dragon " where a shepard became friends with a dragon, who was judged by her appearance.

Trivia

  • The Barney costume used in this episode is similar to one of the two costumes used in season eleven .
  • The two girls in this episode ( Emma and Tracy) were also the only girls who appeared in the new content of The Best of Barney .
  • This is the final episode to air in 4:3 and standard definition. Starting with the next episode, the series will now switch into 16:9 high definition.
