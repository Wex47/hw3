" It's Hot! It's Cold! " is the sixth episode from the eighth season of Barney & Friends .

  • 1 Plot
  • 2 Songs

Plot

Barney and his friends learn about hot and cold. Later, Barney sets up an imaginary winter and summer wonderland in the park.

Trivia

  • This episode marked:
    • The first appearance of Mr. Brantley .
  • The version of " It's C-C Cold BRRRR! " is played in a different arrangement and key. This was done to keep this song and Mister Sun in the same key (since they were sung side by side).
  • This episode was featured in All About Opposites .
  • This group ( Scott , Whitney , Beth ) also appeared in Play for Exercise! .

International Edits

  • In some international versions, this episode was shortened.
