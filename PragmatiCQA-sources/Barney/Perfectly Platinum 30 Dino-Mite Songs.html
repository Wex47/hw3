Perfectly Platinum: 30 Dino-Mite Songs is the eighteenth US studio album from Barney, released on February 24, 2009 under Koch Records and HIT Entertainment . The album features a compilation of songs from seasons nine to thirteen of the television series .

It was the final Barney album to be released under Koch Records and the final US Barney album to be produced by longtime music director, Joseph Phillips and executive produced by Karen Barnes .

The album was later released in the United Kingdom on April 2, 2012 by Demon Records with an alternate album cover.

On April 10, 2020, the album was transferred to Warner Music Group's Arts Music division, along with other Barney albums, as Mattel entered into an exclusive, multi-year global music licensing agreement between the company, announced on May 1, 2020.

Trivia

  • The name is a pun on "perfectly purple", a part of the song, " A Perfectly Purple Day ", which it is also in this album.
  • During the week of Aug. 31, 2020 - Sept. 6, 2020, " Twinkle Twinkle Little Star " and " One Two Buckle My Shoe " peaked at No. 67 and No. 71 on the iTunes Top 100 Songs in Singapore respectively.
