" And the Green Grass Grows All Around " is a song that was first published in 1912. It's first appearance on Barney was in the episode " The Treasure of Rainbow Beard ".

Trivia

  • In Tree-Mendous Trees , instead of singing: "The nest on the branch", they sang the bird on the branch to make the song even shorter, most likely in an attempt to save time.
  • In Barney's Jungle Friends and Home Sweet Earth: The Rainforest , instead of singing, "The egg in the nest", they sang the nest in the branch to make the song shorter.
