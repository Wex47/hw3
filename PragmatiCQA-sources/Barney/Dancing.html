" Dancing " is the first half of the eighteenth episode from the tenth season of Barney & Friends .

Plot

At the big dance party, Jamal feels that he can't dance, so Barney encourages him that he can dance if he tries.

Trivia

  • This episode marked:
    • The last appearance of Allison.
    • The only appearance of Jamal.
  • Erica Rhodes , the actress who played Kim made a guest appearance in this episode.
