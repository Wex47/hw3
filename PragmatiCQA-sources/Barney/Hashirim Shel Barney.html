Hashirim Shel Barney (השירים של ברני; Barney's Songs) is a hebrew album containing songs from the Israeli coproduction H achaverim Shel Barney . The album was released in 1998, through Clasikaletet, alongisde two other hebrew Barney albums

The songs are from the first season of the series, which contains most of the American season one episodes.
