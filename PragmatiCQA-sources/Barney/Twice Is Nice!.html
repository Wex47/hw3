" Twice Is Nice! " is the seventh episode from the third season of Barney & Friends .

Plot

Barney, Baby Bop, BJ and the kids are going on a scavenger hunt. They run into Ashley, a new friend Tosha met in school, as she comes into the classroom for a brief moment. Throughout the scavenger hunt, they seem to see Ashley everywhere. In the end, the kids notice that Ashley has an identical twin sister, Alissa. Barney tells the kids that, even though twins may look alike, everyone is special in his or her own way.

Trivia

  • This episode marked:
    • The first appearance for both Ashley and Alissa, the twin girls. This is also the only time they appeared with Julie and Tosha. They later became main characters in Seasons 4 and 5.
    • Another time something happens to the Barney doll before coming to life. It was Julie puts the hula hoop at the center, Barney comes to life, and he gets stuck.
    • This is the first time that Barney gets stuck in a hula hoop, he will get another one on " Circle of Friends ".
  • During the first verse of " Friends of Mine ," Barney lip synches to a lyric that he doesn't sing.
  • Even though Baby Bop learned about twins in " " (Tosha even references it), she seemed to have forgotten about it until this episode.
