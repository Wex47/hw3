Colors is the second half of the fourth episode from the tenth season of Barney & Friends .

Plot

When it's raining outside, Baby Bop joins Barney inside the caboose alongside BJ and the kids who have just painted pictures. Baby Bop pokes her head out to see if the rain has stopped and spies a beautiful rainbow in the sky! But when the others come to see it...it's gone. Baby Bop is very disappointed because she liked all the pretty colors, so she, BJ and the kids paint their own "life-sized" rainbow. When it's raining outside again, the homemade rainbow falls down and gets it all wet. Luckily, a real rainbow takes its place!
