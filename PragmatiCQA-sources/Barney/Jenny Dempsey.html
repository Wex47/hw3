Jenny Dempsey (born 1964) was a props master for the Barney & The Backyard Gang series and Barney & Friends .

She was also the costume actress for Baby Bop for Season 1 of Barney & Friends and for BJ for Season 2 . In addition, Dempsey performed the character of Mr. Honey Bear in the episode " Hoo's in the Forest? ".
