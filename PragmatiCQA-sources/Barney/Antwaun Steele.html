Antwaun Steele (born January 6, 1971) has performed as Barney the Dinosaur in various Barney home videos and many Barney live show appearances, including Barney's Musical Castle , Barney's Pajama Party , and Barney Live in Concert - Birthday Bash! .

Personal Life

Trivia
