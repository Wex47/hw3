" Things I Can Do " is the first half of the seventeenth episode from the tenth season of Barney & Friends .

Plot

Baby Bop gets upset that there's one more thing that she can't do, so Barney and his friends help her find something that she can do.

  6. A Friend Like You

Trivia

  • This episode marked:
    • The only appearance of Becky .
    • The last appearance of Lindsey .
  • According to this episode, Baby Bop is unable to play piano, skate, clap, hoop, or juggle.
  • While doing " The Baby Bop Hop ", an extra in the background hops too early.
  • Several scenes from this episode are shown in I Can Do It! .
