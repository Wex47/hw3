Vamos Juntos a Cantar ( Let's Sing Together ) is a Spanish album from Barney that was released in 1999 through EMI Music in Columbia and Tycoon Music in Mexico.

Trivia

  • The album features some newly arranged versions of songs by Joseph Phillips, some of which appear on the album exclusively.
  • This is the second Spanish album to not include dubbed audio tracks from the series as in Feliz Navidad con Amor Barney , therefore the vocals from the voice actors were re-recorded on every track.
  • Hello, Goodbye , The Clean Up Clock , Down By the Bay and Someone to Love You Forever were re-arranged and recorded exclusively for this album, although none of them appear in Seasons 4 to 6.
