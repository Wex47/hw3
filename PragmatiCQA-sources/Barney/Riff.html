Riff is a main recurring character on the television show Barney & Friends .

A six-year-old orange hadrosaur , Riff made his first appearance in the home video Let's Make Music (2006), with a subsequent introduction in the episode " Welcome, Cousin Riff ". He was added to Barney Live! - The Let's Go Tour shortly thereafter. His name is kind of music that can be played repeatedly. Because of his name, his words in his speech sometimes is repeated, most often adding "itty" to the end of a word, before repeating the word properly.

Riff is a good friend of Barney's and the cousin to both Baby Bop and BJ .

Riff is all about music. He hears music everywhere he goes, which ties directly into his theme song " I Hear Music Everywhere ." He is also able to mimic and make various sounds. Riff loves music so much that his crest blinks whenever he is creating music or is excited. Like the other dinosaurs, he is an amicable character, though can also be rather shy and doubtful of himself, often needing encouragement from his friends. Riff also enjoys inventing things on the side.

Creation

According to HIT Entertainment and executive producer Karen Barnes , Riff was created to add musical diversity to the series. Additionally, his creation was supposed to aid in bringing the show to a wider international audience.

Costume

Riff is orange with a yellow tummy, green spots, and green spikes. Riff is known for his crest spots lighting up with green lights, making music each time they do. He wears green sneakers with yellow socks and purple laces on them.

Riff is the only dinosaur from the show to not be designed, created and built by Irene Corey Design Associates .

Portrayal

Body Portrayal History

To differentiate body movement from BJ, due to him being another male little dino, the taps on Riff's stomach was created when Adam Brown portrayed the character.

Voice Portrayal History

During auditions for Riff, the character was described as edgy and urban.

Trivia

  • Riff was the first dinosaur to be created in over thirteen years.
  • He is the only dinosaur whose name does not start with the letter B.
  • He is the only little dinosaur in the franchise to not start out tall as Barney.
