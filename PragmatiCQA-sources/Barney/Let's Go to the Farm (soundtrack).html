Let's Go to the Farm is a soundtrack with a few songs from the home video of the same name and is Barney's seventeenth US album. It was released on May 2, 2006 by Koch Records and HIT Entertainment .

It was the sixth, and so far the final, Barney US soundtrack, based on a home video .

On April 10, 2020, the album was transferred to Warner Music Group's Arts Music division, along with other Barney albums, as Mattel entered into an exclusive, multi-year global music licensing agreement between the company, announced on May 1, 2020.
