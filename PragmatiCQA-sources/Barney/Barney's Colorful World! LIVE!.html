Barney's Colorful World! LIVE! is a live album with with songs from the stage show of the same name . The album was released on CD on September 7, 2004 by Koch Records and HIT Entertainment .

Barney's Colorful World! LIVE! was the fourteenth US studio album released by Barney and the second US live album after Barney's Big Surprise! Live on Stage .

It was the first album to have the Barney character be voiced by Dean Wendt for a live album.

Following the release of Barney's Colorful World! LIVE! a spanish language version, titled Barney y su Mundo de Colores , was released in 2006. In 2008, the album was released for Malaysia and Singapore but were separated and released in two volumes, with volume one being Act 1 (the first twelve songs) of the show and volume two being the remaining songs from Act 2 of the show.
