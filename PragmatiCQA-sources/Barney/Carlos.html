Carlos is a character on Barney & Friends , played by Corey Lopez . He debuted in Barney Live! In New York City , and only appeared in Season 3 . He was replaced by Robert .

Bio

Carlos has a younger brother named Juan . They only appeared together in " If the Shoe Fits... " and " Any Way You Slice It ". He also has a cat named Fluffy and a bowl of goldfish (named Super, Dee, and Duper; a play on Barney's famous phrase) who he mentions in " Our Furry Feathered Fishy Friends ".

Carlos lives with Juan, his mother, and his father, as mentioned in the episode " Room for Everyone ", where he paints a picture of them. He also has a grandmother. Like Tina and Luci , Carlos and Juan are of Mexican descent.
