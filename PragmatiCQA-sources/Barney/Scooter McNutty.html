Scooter McNutty is a character who appeared in Seasons 4 - 6 of Barney & Friends . He is a squirrel who lives next door in the tree where Miss Etta Kette lives and is known for his scatterbrained adventures, which are the consequences of acting first and thinking later .

He was puppeteered and voiced by Todd Duffey .

Trivia

  • Scooter appeared in every Season 4 and Season 5 episode along with Miss Etta Kette , but only appeared in 6 episodes of Season 6 .
  • Scooter was also featured in Barney's ABC, 123 and More! , Barney's Happy Valentine's Day , Barney's Peekaboo Halloween! and Playtime at Barney's House! .
  • In Oh, Brother...She's My Sister , Scooter said his favorite book was "The Squirrel That Can Whirl and Twirl."
