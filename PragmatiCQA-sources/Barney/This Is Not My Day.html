This Is Not My Day

Written by

Philip Parker

Usage

1992, 2003

This Is Not My Day is an original Barney songlet that only appeared in " Oh, What A Day! " and " A Picture of Friendship ". It uses the same tune as " Tina's Sad Song ". BJ sang this song in its only other appearance.
