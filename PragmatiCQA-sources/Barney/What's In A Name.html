" What's In A Name? " is the eighteenth episode from the fifth season of Barney & Friends .

Plot

Linda is learning the alphabet with a little help from Barney, her friends, and her big brother Chip. Chip sets out to help Linda learn the letters in her name while Barney and the kids discover other fun ways to learn the letters of the alphabet. With a little Barney magic, they even create their own game show.
