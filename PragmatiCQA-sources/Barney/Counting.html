" Counting" is the first part of the eighth episode from the tenth season of Barney & Friends .

Plot

BJ and Riff find a pirate map, but in order to find the treasure, they have to be able to count. Since Baby Bop hasn't learned to count yet, Barney offers to help teach her so she is not left behind. In the end, Baby Bop learns to count.

Trivia

  • This episode marks:
    • The first appearances of Ryan, Bethany and Peter.
    • The only appearance of Morgan.
    • The first time Sadie and Sophie appear together.
  • Even though BJ and Riff appear in this episode, they don't sing songs in this episode.
