Barney y su Mundo de Colores is a Spanish album with songs from the Mexican production of Barney's Colorful World . The album was released in 2004 by Tycoon Music and HIT Entertainment .

Barney y su Mundo de Colores is the sixth Barney soundtrack to be released based on a live stage show production. It was the third Spanish soundtrack to be based on a live production after La Gran Sorpresa de Barney and El Castillo Musical de Barney .

Unlike other soundtracks, it does not contain audience effects in the background.
