Sandra Kay "Sandy" Duncan (born February 20, 1946) is an American singer and actress of stage and television. In the Barney & The Backyard Gang videos, she provided introduction and ending speeches for the first three videos , as well as portraying Michael and Amy's Mom . Duncan was invited to appear in the Barney & Friends television series, but she declined. She is well remembered for roles including the title character in the 1979 Broadway revival of Peter Pan , Sandy Hogan on The Hogan Family , and Vixey in Disney's The Fox and the Hound .
