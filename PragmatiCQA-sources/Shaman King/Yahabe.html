Yahabe ( ヤハべえ , Yahabe e ) is a one shot manga and anime OVA released by Hiroyuki Takei .

About

The oneshot tells the story about a young boy named Kamogawa Yosuke who meets a mysterious creature, known as Yahabe .

Characters

OVA

In addition to the one-shot manga chapter, a special CGI OVA was released as well, containing the same story.

Trivia

  • Like the rest of Takei's one-shots, Yahabe ties in to the Shaman King franchise in the later sequels and spin-offs from Flowers to Super Star with the Yahabe main characters appearing as the main antagonists. However, Yahabe' s name is changed to YVS/Yabisu , and his origins are seemingly changed as well.
