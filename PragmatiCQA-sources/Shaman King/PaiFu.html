About

Pai-Fu is a short, childlike spirit and one of the Four Holy Beasts , a group of Kami-class spirits belonging to the Dong Family . Like the others, Pai-Fu is controlled through a small bell with a carving that corresponds to the spirit's animal, a tiger for Pai-Fu.

Pai-Fu shares Dong Yonhon 's penchant for manipulating others, including through lies and illusions, and assists Yonhon in manipulating Dong Honhon by placing the siblings' mother in a coma and blaming it on the Tao Family .

Oversouls and Attacks

O.S.

O.S.

  • Shaman: Dong Yonhon
  • O.S. Type: Spirit Type
  • Spirit Ally: Pai-Fu
  • Medium used: Bell

Description: Pai-Fu has the ability of "sensation control" ( 感覚操作 , kankaku sōsa ) , which allows the spirit to manipulate the senses of a target and create realistic illusions. This ability can also be used to completely suppress the senses, leaving the target in a coma.

Attacks

  • All Trance ( 大虎 （ ） , Ōru Toransu , lit. "Big Tiger") : Pai-Fu takes control of the target's five senses and traps them in an illusion that exploits their own feelings.
  • Boost All Senses ( 全神経ブースト , Zen Shinkei Būsuto ) : Pai-Fu enhances the target's nervous system, enabling them to move with greater speed and strength.

Trivia

  • Pai-Fu's appearance and abilities are based on Sophia from Takei's ULTIMO series.
