History

Shaman Fights Second Round

At the Corn King restaurant, Bookila-T is shocked to hear Asakura Yoh boast that he will defeat Icemen with only one shot.

During his team's match against Team "Hana-Gumi" , Bookila-T expresses discomfort and says that he wants to go home. However, his teammate Kaizer-T goes ahead in the match and is quickly and brutally defeated by the girls, who are outraged and disgusted with Kaizer-T's pervertedness. When Miracle-T suggests that they forfeit, Bookila-T agrees and remarks that the Hana-Gumi are dangerous. However, Team "Hana-Gumi" still beat them up too.
