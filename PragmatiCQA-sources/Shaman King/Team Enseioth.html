Team Enseioth (エンセイオス, Enseiosu , Team Spartacus in the English anime) are a group of three Greek shamans from the anime series of Shaman King .

About

History

Shaman Fights Second Round

The team came from Greece in order to become Shaman King. The team never rest and transfer their Furyoku to the leader too much. The team won against Team "LCT" in the Shaman Fights but lost against Team "Funbari Onsen" .

The team makes a cameo appearance during Yoh 's final fight with Hao .
