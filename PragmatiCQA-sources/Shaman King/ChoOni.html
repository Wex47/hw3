A Cho-oni ( 超鬼 , Chō Oni , lit. "Ultra Ogre") is a type of oni in the Shaman King manga and anime series.

About

The Cho-Onis are introduced during Asakura Yoh 's training in hell. These onis seem to be the strongest onis within the Great Spirit , capable of slaying Asakura Yohken with ease. These creatures appear rather sadistic and seem to employ underhanded tactics, striking Yoh with an arrow while he is distracted.

Though not a singular entity like the Oh-Oni , these creatures claim to run the hell Yoh is in and employ a variety of weapons, including hammers, bows and arrows, iron clubs, and axes.

Anime/Manga Difference

Cho-Onis do not appear in the 2001 anime series.
