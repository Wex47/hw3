Space Shot ( スペースショット , Supēsu Shotto ) is a fictional character in the manga and anime series of Shaman King . He is a member of Ryu's Gang .

Appearance

Space Shot wears a white shirt and a black coat.

Personality

He is very loyal to Ryu .

History

Past

Apache was born on January 12, 1981, and was from Saitama, Japan. One night during a summer festival, he was surrounded by a gang and is saved by Ryu. The two became friends.

Meeting Yoh

Ryu tells Apache and Space Shot to attack Yoh . The two are defeated after Yoh uses Hyoi Gattai with Amidamaru .

Meeting with Tokageroh

Space Shot and the others stop Tokageroh from killing Manta so they can save Ryu.

Shaman Fights in Tokyo

Before Ryu goes to America, Space Shot and the others write him a farewell card.

Anime/Manga Difference

In the manga series, Apache and Space Shot attack Yoh with knives. However, in the 2001 anime series, they use their fists.

Trivia

  • According to the character files, Apache and Space Shot make a good tag team since they both attack Yoh.
