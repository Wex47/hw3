Shaman King Kang Zeng Bang (シャーマンキング　完全版, SHAMAN KING Kanzenban meaning " Shaman King, the Complete Collection ") is the reprint of Shaman King and has finally a "true" end. The first two volumes were released on March 4, 2008, and two volumes were released each month afterward. The final one, volume 27, was released on April 3, 2009, and contains previously unpublished material.

In addition to providing the series with a true ending, the volumes each have a new cover and two extra pages with a small amount of extra information. Also starting from volume 22, there is a single-paged image called " Visionz " towards the end of the volume, revealing the appearance of the characters as they are seen in the Funbari no Uta sequel.

Poem

The Kang Zeng Bang Poem is a series of small poems featured in each volume of the Kang Zeng Bang edition. Each volume features a colourspread with the character on the cover and a poem relating to the character. An additional poem appears on the ad wrapper on each volume, which also relates to the character on the cover.

Visionz

The Visionz are a series of five images that show how Asakura Yoh , Tao Ren , Usui Horokeu , Lyserg Diethel and Chocolove McDonell look in the Funbari no Uta sequel, with the added page containing a shadow image of them together with a poem.
