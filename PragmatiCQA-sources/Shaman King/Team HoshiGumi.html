Team "Hoshi-Gumi" ( チーム「星組」 , Chīmu "Hoshi-gumi" , lit. "Team 'Star Team'") is the team led by Asakura Hao in the Shaman Fight. The other members are Opacho and Luchist Lasso .

History

Shaman Fights

Not much is known about their early history but each member was able to receive an Oracle Bell, qualify for the Tournament by landing a hit on a Patch Officiant, and won at least 2 of their 3 matches allowing them all to advance to the Second Round; although it can be assumed Hao himself was able to win all 3 of his matches by himself.

Members

Team "Hoshi-Gumi"
Asakura Hao ( 麻倉ハオ , Asakura Hao )
In his third life, Asakura Hao recruits his own faction to compete in the Shaman Fight. His Guardian Ghost is the Spirit of Fire , one of the Patch Tribe 's Five Grand Elemental Spirits .
Luchist Lasso ( ラキスト・ラッソ , Rakisuto Rasso )
The former leader of the X-Laws , Luchist Lasso wields the comp hunter pistol "Sword No-000" as a medium for the Fallen Angel Lucifer .
Opacho ( オパチョ , Opacho )
An African shaman, Opacho was an orphan rescued by Hao. She channels the spirit Mama into her hair.
