About

The Magical Princesses are three elderly short wrinkled female shamans. They resemble fairy-tale witches.

They are descendants of three witches that aided Hao during the previous Shaman Fight. Their ancestors loved Hao, but were abandoned. Each of them married and formed families. Because of the era of fierce and ruthless witch hunts, they stopped their work and simply pass on spells useful in everyday life orally.

They want to win the Shaman Fight in order to become young and beautiful again.

History

Shaman Fight Second Round

Team "Magical Princess" is the seeded team in the same block as Team "Hoshi-Gumi" and Team "Hana-Gumi" . Their first match in the tournament is against Team "Hana-Gumi", who slaughter Team "Magical Princess".
