Short Summary

Over the summer, Asakura Yoh has undergone Kyōyama Anna 's torturous training, but returning to school is no reprieve since Anna transfers into Yoh's class.

Umemiya Ryunosuke and his gang try to take over the abandoned Chinese restaurant, but Tao Ren kicks them out. Ren's sister, Tao Jun offers to use her Jiang Shi , Lee Pyron , to retrieve Amidamaru .
