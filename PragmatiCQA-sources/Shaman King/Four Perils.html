The Four Perils ( 四凶 , Shikyō , Chinese pinyin: Sì Xiōng ; Four Evils in the English manga) are a group of four spirits in Shaman King Red Crimson . They were sealed by Emperor Shun and wielded by Tao Ching .

About

Kyuuki ( 窮奇 , Kyūki , Chinese pinyin: Qióngqí ) is a tiger-shaped spirit.

Konton ( 混沌 , Konton , Chinese pinyin: Hùndùn ) is a dog-shaped spirit.

Toutetsu ( 饕餮 , Tōtetsu , Chinese pinyin: Tāotiè ) is a sheep-shaped spirit.

Toukotsu ( 檮杌 , Tōkotsu , Chinese pinyin: Táowù ) has a human face, tiger legs, and boar tusks.
