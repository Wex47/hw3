Short Summary

In the usual Funbari Hill Cemetery sits Asakura Hana looking aimlessly as he is approached by Ichihara Ryuji and his gang. Using Amidamaru , Hana easily defeats them all and returns home to be scolded by his guardian Tamao Tamamura , who forbids him to use his shaman skills in public. That same night, two unknown figures appear at the graveyard with one cleaving Amidamaru's tombstone in two with his Over Soul . The next day, Hana finds the ruined tombstone and is confronted by a middle school student, who reveals himself to be a shaman and challenges Hana to a fight. Meanwhile, Ponchi and Conchi warn Tamao about the strange presence earlier, the arrivals of Yohane and Luca , Asakura Hao 's direct descendants of the branch Asakura Family !
