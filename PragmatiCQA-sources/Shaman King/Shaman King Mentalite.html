Shaman King Kazenban Final Official Guide Book Mentalite ( シャーマンキング完全版 最終公式ガイドブック マンタリテ , Shaman Kingu Kazenban Saishū Kōshiki Gaidobukku Mantarite ) is an official guide book authored by Hiroyuki Takei and published by Shueisha. It contains all the data of the Shaman King Kang Zeng Bang volumes with bonus stories. The bonus story is a short called "Snake's Legs" which serves as an epilogue to the Funbari no Uta short stories, followed by a bonus one-shot which serves as an epilogue to the series overall.
