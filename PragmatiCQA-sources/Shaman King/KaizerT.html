History

Shaman Fights in Tokyo

On the Patch Jumbo , Kaizer-T is woken by Goldva 's announcement that the jet Over Soul will dematerialize while mid-air, dropping all the gathered shamans over America.

Shaman Fights Second Round

After surviving the fall, Kaizer-T makes his way to Mesa Verdede and the Patch Village, where he teams up with Bookila-T and Miracle-T , forming Team "T-Production" .

At the Corn King restaurant, Kaizer-T is shocked to hear Asakura Yoh boast that he will defeat Icemen with only one shot. Umemiya Ryunosuke , who has been wandering around the restaurant and threatening random shaman who catch his eye, grabs hold of Kaizer-T, who panics. However, Yoh then tells Ryu that they are going.

Kaizer-T attempts to defeat Team "Hana-Gumi" using specially-crafted plastic models of the three girls that have been imbued with voodoo magic. They are so disgusted and outraged by his perverted attitude that they brutally defeat him as quickly as possible, although he seemingly enjoys the pain. Meanwhile, Kaizer's teammates decide to withdraw from the match. Team "Hana-Gumi" defeat all three men, winning the match.

Trivia
