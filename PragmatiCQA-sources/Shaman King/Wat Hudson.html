Appearance

Personality

Wat believed that one could be happy by loving someone and justified his silence about Oknox 's criminal actions because he took care of him and because he loved him.

History

Past

As a child, Wat lived with his alcoholic abusive father, an absent mother and two younger siblings who eventually died of starvation. He eventually killed both of his parents in order to survive and ended up living on the streets. Oknox found and took him in and though he knew about Oknox criminal actions, he never told anyone.

After Lyserg Diethel lost his grandfather and was taken in by Oknox, they became roommates.

Anime/Manga Difference

Wat Hudson never appears in the anime series.

Trivia

  • His name and relationship with Lyserg Diethel is a nod to Dr. John H. Watson from the Sherlock Holmes novels.
