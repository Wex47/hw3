Team "Tsuchi-Gumi" ( チーム「土組」 , Chīmu "Tsuchi-gumi" , lit. "Team 'Earth Team'") is one of Asakura Hao 's teams. The members are the BoZ Brothers and Peyote Diaz .

History

Shaman Fights Second Round

In the first match of the tournament, Team "Tsuchi-Gumi" faced Team "The Ren" . Chocolove McDonell singlehandedly knocked the BoZ Brothers unconscious, so Peyote Diaz used their bodies as puppets for two of his spirits .

Members

Team "Tsuchi-Gumi"
Peyote Diaz ( ペヨーテ・ディアス , Peyōte Diasu )
A Mexican shaman, Peyote Diaz 's Guardian Ghosts are his former mariachi bandmates . During the first match, he puppeteers Ryo's and Zen's bodies after they are knocked out.
Sugimoto Ryo ( 杉本良 , Sugimoto Ryō )
One half of the BoZ Brothers , Ryo manipulates Chimimoryo while playing an electric biwa. After losing the first match, Zen joins Gandhara .
Yoneda Zen ( 米田善 , Yoneda Zen )
The other half of the BoZ Brothers , Zen manipulates Chimimoryo while using a shakujō-turned-microphone. After losing the first match, Ryo joins Gandhara .
