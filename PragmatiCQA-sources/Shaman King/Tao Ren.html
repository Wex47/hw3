Tao Ren ( 道蓮 （ ） , Tao Ren , Chinese pinyin: Dào Lián , Len Tao in the English version and English video games) is a fictional character in the manga and anime series of Shaman King . Ren is the 43rd head of the Tao Family , and during the Shaman Fights , he was a member of Team "The Ren" . Sometime after the tournament, Ren married Iron Maiden Jeanne and together they had a son, named Tao Men .

Appearance

Tao Ren was shorter than Yoh and most of his friends but he seems to look taller when the spike of his purple hair (a hair style called "Tongari") is added up. He has a relatively thin yet muscular build with visible abs. Ren's eye color is golden yellow in the anime series, however, in the manga colored pages series, they are usually gold, brown, or the same color as his hair color.

His casual clothes were his school uniform; a pair of short black pants, a tucked-in, white dress shirt, a yellow school jacket, dark green tie and a pair of brown leather shoes. Like Yoh, Ren wears his school clothes as a casual outfit during the Second Rounds of the Shaman Fights. While traveling to America, Ren began wearing long pants and a yellow scarf and as his old jacket was worn out, his mother gave him a new similar one.

When he left for America, he also brought with him a new battle uniform, that is inspired by kung fu cloth. Both the pants and the vest are black and both have a triangular pattern with a yellow lining, and the pants are tied with a white silk belt. The vest also has an opening in the back outlining his Tao tattoo. Even though he brought it with him from before the start of the Shaman Fight, he never wore it during the fights in America because they were all so sudden, that he never had the chance to change.

In the seven years that passed, he reluctantly grew tall and his long hair was braided into a three-pronged Tongari. In his adult years, he commonly wears a black suit with a purple shirt and a yellow necktie.

Personality

Ren is serious, logical, and quite aloof. He tries hard to maintain a tough and confident exterior, but deep down would risk his own life for his allies. Ren is a particularly impatient person with a short temper and isn't afraid to hurt the source of the anger. Because of how he was raised amongst dead corpses and spirits he began applying the survival of the fittest rule as a way of overcoming death. He also believed that his guardian ghost Bason, was only meant to be used as a tool and would to treat him as a friend would only result in the spirit taking highly advantage over him. Ren's anger and hatred came from his father Tao Yúan . Forced to train and study at a very youthful age, Ren had his childhood stolen from him and thus, hated his father for all of his suffering.

Ren is an egotistical person and usually doesn't care that people think that he's overly proud. He even named his own team, Team "The Ren", much to the annoyance of his teammate, Usui Horokeu . In addition, Ren is also very intuitive, quick-witted, sometimes quite stubborn and he can also be very defiant. He rarely listens to what anyone says or takes their instructions. Before and during the process in which he befriended with Asakura Yoh , he was even bold enough to disobey his father on various occasions.

As the Shaman Fight progresses, Ren begins to trust others even more. Out of all his friends, his bonds with Chocolove and Horohoro seem to be the strongest even though Ren often fights with Horohoro. However, after Ren was fatally impaled by Peyote Diaz , it becomes apparent that they really do care for one another as Horohoro tried his best to protect Ren until the rest could help him.

Abilities and Powers

As a normal human being, Ren has a very strong body as evidenced by his tough and constant training. He is very skilled in martial arts and sword fighting, in addition to also learning techniques by just watching them as he was able to learn many of Asakura Mikihisa 's techniques simply by experiencing them firsthand, and also learning to use Over Soul by just seeing Chrome use his. It is mentioned that Ren is skilled in more than one area of combat and is a powerful shaman capable of performing feats most shamans of his age would not be able to, such as staring straight into the heart of the Great Spirit without fainting, a feat only a handful of shamans could do.

Ren becomes one of the Five Elemental Warriors and attunes his Furyoku to that of lightning, giving him the skills to move and attack in the blink of an eye. While fighting his way through the Plants, Kyōyama Anna looked through the Furyoku values of Ren and the others. There it is revealed that his Furyoku level is about 100,001.

Weapons

At the beginning of the series, Ren fights with the Basontou , a guandao that Bason used to wield and had been passed down in the Tao Family for generations. Ren used it for both his Hyōi-Gattai and later as a medium for his first Over Soul. During the second round of the Shaman Fights, his guandao was incinerated by Asakura Hao , but Ren was later wielded another one, during his fight with Nichrom .

The Bâo-Lèi Sword is a silver sword and the most treasured heirloom of the Tao Family. It was given to Ren by his father. After Asakura Hao incinerated his guandao, Ren switched to the Bâo-Lèi Sword. With this sword, he creates two new Over Souls the "Super Bushin" and the "Bushin Fish Fin".

Spirit

Bason is Ren's guardian ghost. A fierce warrior from the era of Kublai Khan , Bason has always served the Tao Family, even now after his death. When Ren used the Bâo-Lèi Sword as a medium for his Over Soul, it increased the strength of Bason several times and evolved him into a Seirei Class spirit.

Ren used his first Over Soul for most melee attacks and later created the Super Bushin that was able to use Bason's military experience to create dozens of different weapons to overwhelm his opponent. Ren´s third Over Soul with Bason, the Bushin Fish Fin, allowed him to generate lightning and electricity to either strike down from the sky or channeled through his over soul in close combat.

After becoming one of the Five Elemental Warriors , Ren gains the Spirit of Thunder , which according to Pascal Avaf , follows the way of Taoism .

After the battle against Xingtian , Tao Ching released the Four Perils for Jun and Ren, hoping the siblings would manage the spirits together.

Not as satisfied, Ren decides to kill the gangster but is stopped by Yoh, who told him "that was enough". Ren tells Yoh that humans deserve to die while cutting their hearts only seek pleasure and that when he becomes the Shaman King, he would purify the world. He adds that in order to do that, he needed many strong spirits and requests that Yoh give him Amidamaru. Yoh refuses too, stating that Amidamaru was his best friend, and Ren faces him in a battle. however, the fight is equal until Ren, excited at Amidamaru's strength, applies 100% acceptance, the ability to draw all of the strength of the spirit into the world, as Ren claims, and succeeds in landing a fatal strike on Yoh. This angers Ren and he charges at Yoh, determined to kill him, but Amidamaru shields Yoh with his body. Ren is surprised by this, however, he mocks Amidamaru's action and uses 100% acceptance again to strike Yoh. Yoh, however, also applies 100% acceptance, much to Ren's shock, and the two clash into battle, with Ren being beaten. After his defeat, Ren stays in an abandoned Chinese restaurant that he bought from its last owner, still angered at his first defeat and brooding over his loss in acquiring Amidamaru, which he believed was Bason's fault.

Trivia

  • Ren's name in Chinese means "benevolent", "tough", and "edge of a knife", all of which are reflected by his character. His actual Chinese name, "Lián", means "lotus" and is usually a more feminine name.
  • In the Anime series, Ren has an obsession with milk and the number three.
  • A running gag in the shaman king anime series is that Ren's Tongari hairstyle grows longer whenever he gets irritated or shocked.
  • It is mentioned in the interview part in the official Shaman King character book, that he would like a girl if she is smaller than him and was not very annoying.
  • Coincidentally, Ren's voice actor, Romi Paku, has also voiced Bleach 's Tōshirō Hitsugaya and Fullmetal Alchemist 's Edward Elric . All three characters are short for their ages and lose their tempers whenever someone mentions this.
