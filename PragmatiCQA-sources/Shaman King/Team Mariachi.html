Team "Mariachi" ( チーム・マリアッチ , Chīmu Mariatchi ) were a group of three shaman that were entered in the Shaman Fight .

About

History

Shaman Fight Second Round

The team initially formed just before the conclusion of the first round. In the second round, Team "Mariachi" were paired against Team "Kabbalahers" in their first fight of the tournament. However, Team "Kabbalahers" were late to the match and so it was delayed several times. Eventually, only Asakura Mikihisa arrived to fight but proved to be enough to defeat and eliminate the team. No matter how passionately they attacked Mikihisa with their Over Souls and techniques, Team "Mariachi" were still unable to defeat him. Mikihisa even turned Imari and Shigaraki into a guitar and played a little in the middle of their fight.

After such a defeat, the team proceeded to quickly leave the island.

Members

Team "Mariachi"
Bourbon ( バーボン , Bābon )
The leader of the team, Bourbon is a Spanish mariachi and channels the horse spirit McEwen through his guitar.
Rameau ( ラモー , Ramō )
A French musician, Rameau was trained as a mariachi in Mexico and channels the weasel spirit Carse through his trumpet.
Frisch ( フリッシュ , Furisshu )
Frisch is a Swiss hip-hop MC and channels the archerfish spirit Triplepack through a water gun.

Anime/Manga Difference

Team Mariachi does not appear in the 2001 anime series.
