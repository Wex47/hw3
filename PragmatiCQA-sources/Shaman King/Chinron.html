About

Chinson is a short, childlike spirit and one of the Four Holy Beasts , a group of Kami-class spirits belonging to the Dong Family .

Like the others, Chinron is controlled through a small bell with a carving that corresponds to the spirit's animal, a dragon for Chinron.
