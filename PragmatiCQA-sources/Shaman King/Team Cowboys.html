The Cowboys ( COWBOYS （ ） , Kaubōizu ) were a group of three shamans that competed in the Shaman Fight in Tokyo 2000.

About

Not much is known about this team. All the members are from the United States and their names all begin with the letter "J" in honor of the infamous outlaw, Jesse James .

The team fought against Team "Deva" and lost.
