Fallen Angels ( 堕天使 , Datenshi ) are the Archangels of defected X-Laws members. They are created from "supercars" that gained a spirit from their importance to Marco Lasso and Luchist Lasso . They are called fallen angels because they are used by X-Laws members who have defected from the group.

About

The Fallen Angels were created by having a priest call upon the spirits of the real angels and then through a hard process, fusing them with the spirits of the cars, thus giving birth to mechanical nature spirits. In truth, it was Luchist Lasso who once ventured into the Big Heaven, the second highest stage within the Great Spirit, and took "pieces" of the real Archangels and brought to this world to create the artificial angels. Michael and Lucifer were created from the cars that gained their own spirit. Asakura Yoh speculates that the cause of this was some kind of importance to Marco and Luchist. While not real angels, their reiryoku are almost at Kami Class.

Marco himself reveals that the true form of the Archangels is their car form. Using the Over Soul, the Archangel's car form can be driven by their Shamans. If the shamans' minds focus more on the devices than their religious pieties in angels when summoning the spirits, the colorations of the mechanical archangels may alter.

Their main ability is to shatter other spirits when they attack them, as seen when Michael impales Boris Tepes Dracula and makes him disappear.

Spirits

Lucifer

O.S. "Fallen Angel Lucifer" (O.S. 堕天使・ルシファー, Datenshi Rushifā )

  • Shaman: Luchist Lasso
  • O.S. Type: Spirit Type
  • Spirit Ally: Lucifer (ルシフェル Rushiferu )
  • Reiryoku Level: 200,000
  • Medium Used: Sword No-000 "Luchist Comphunter" - A bullet shot from Luchist's Colt

Description : Lucifer means " Morning Star ", also known as Helel in Hebrew. It wields a large club in one of its hands, and with its black colors, black wings, and large claws greatly stand out from the other nine Archangels. It is the first and with its Reiyoku level is also the second most powerful of all of the Archangels. Its first appearance was when Luchist attacked Teams "X-I" and "X-II" on the Ark X .

Like Michael, Lucifer was created from a car: a yellow Lamborghini Countach LP400 that due to its importance to Luchist Lasso also gained a spirit.

Attacks

  • Lucifer's Hammer ( ルシファーズハンマー , Rushifāzu Hanmā ) : A simple attack, Lucifer smashes down its hammer on an Over Soul and crushes the spirit used in it. However, the full extent of this attack has not been seen because Asakura Yoh is quickly shielded by Shamash before the hammer could connect.

Azazel

O.S. "Fallen Angel Azazel"(O.S. 堕天使・アザゼル, Datenshi Azazeru )

  • Shaman: Hans Reiheit
  • O.S. Type: Spirit Type
  • Spirit Ally: Azazel
  • Reiryoku Level: 470,000
  • Medium Used: Sword No-009 "Reihit Steyr" - A bullet fired from Reiheit's Steyr AUG

Description : Azazel name means " God strengthens ", he is also known as Rameel and Eblis, or Azaz'il and Iblis in Arabic. Azazel is, because of its Reiryoku level, the strongest of all of the ten Archangels, and even stronger than the Spirit of Fire 's Reriyoku level of 300,000. When over souled it becomes gigantic and far bigger than any other of the Archangels, equipped with various weapons all over its body.

Because of its huge Reiryoku level, Reiheit can only maintain his Over Soul for 3 seconds and if the Over Soul is destroyed, the resulting damage is enough to kill Reiheit.

Anime/Manga Difference

In the 2001 anime series, Lucifer uses a spiked mace instead of a club and it is never mentioned that Lucifer is a Fallen Angel. Also in the 2001 anime series, Lucifer is colored white with large black horns and have black gauntlets with small blue crosses in the middle. He is given a black color in the Kang Zeng Bang Shaman Files. Because Reiheit is a manga only character, the tenth Archangel Azazel is never shown in the anime 2001 series.
