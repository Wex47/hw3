About

Namaha is the spirit of a girl who, once she senses a battle, turns into a demonic being and calls herself Nama-Hage ( ナマハゲ , Nama-Hage ) to intimidate her opponents.

Over Souls and Attacks

O.S. Ogre Hunter

O.S. Ogre Hunter (オーガハンター Ōga Hantā )

  • Shaman: Ibuki Gakko
  • O.S. Type: Armor Type
  • Spirit Ally: Namaha
  • Medium used: Strange Mask (kept in Gakko's tracksuit top)

Description: Gakko turns Namaha into an armor that he wears. Gakko gains a helmet with longer hair and straws that wraps around his shoulders and along with his ankles, a cannon that envelopes his left hand, a gauntlet and a large knife that he wields with his right, a chest plate on his chest, and a ropes with three teasels that he wears on his wrist. According to Gakko, it is fully equipped to fight oni .

Attacks

  • König Defenser ( KÖNIG （ ） DEFENSER （ ） , Kēnihhi Difensā ) : KD ( ケデ , Kede ) for short, this special straw cloak reacts to evil Furyoku and is capable of both absorbing and dissolving it. König is German for "king".
  • Oni-Cutter Knife "DIVA" ( 鬼斬り包丁「 DIVA （ ） 」 , Onikiri-bōchō Dība ) : A large engraved knife, it is capable of slicing any oni in half.
  • Ogre Kracher ( OGRE （ ） KRACHER （ ） , Ōga Kurakkā ) : OK ( オケ , Oke ) for short, this large cannon is capable of firing a blast of Furyoku that has been absorbed by the König Defenser. Kracher is German for "cracker".
    • Anti-Oni Drum Roll ( 逆鬼太鼓 , Gyaku Oni Daiko ) : Gakko condenses all the Furyoku that he has absorbed and fires it at his opponent.
