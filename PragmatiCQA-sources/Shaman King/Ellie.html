Ellie ( エリー , Erī ) is a fictional character in the Shaman King 2001 anime series . She is the tomboy of the group of Lily Five , something that is in contrast to her former life in high school when she was a cheerleader.

Appearance

Ellie wears a dark green short top, a black miniskirt with an orange T-shirt around it and wears black boots. She has golden brown hair with a small high ponytail and paints her nails with pink nail polish. Her Oracle Bell color is green.

Personality

As the tomboy of the group, Ellie is not afraid to talk. She seems very outspoken, just like Sally but she loves to hang out with Millie .

Abilities and Powers

Spirit

Her guardian ghost is a pink sickle weasel called Kamaitachi . Her medium are her pink French nails, that when Over Souled turn into bear-like claws.
