Izanami ( イザナミ , Izanami ) is a fictional character in the Shaman King series. She is one of the Four Kings of Hell, and the guardian of the Spirit of Thunder .

About

Izanami is one of the Four Kings of Hell. When Saigan Sati and Team "Myooh" invaded Hell to obtain the four remaining Great Elemental Spirits , Jackson faced off against Izanami in order to retrieve the Spirit of Thunder .

Anime/Manga Difference

Izanami does not truly appear in the manga and both anime series.

Trivia

  • Izanami is the goddess of creation and death in Japanese Shinto myths.
