About

The Spirit of Wind is one of the five powerful elemental spirits known as the Grand Elemental Spirits of the Patch Tribe . They are derived from the Great Spirit itself, and like it, constitute a collective of souls. They can be controlled by their respective Elemental Warrior, or a shaman of extraordinary power. Their power is unmatched by any other spirit, except for the Great Spirit itself. Like the Spirit of Fire , the Spirit of Wind has the ability to become stronger by consuming souls.

After Hao stole the Spirit of Fire , the Patch Tribe hid the other Grand Elemental Spirits to prevent them from being stolen as well. Within the depths of hell, the Spirit of Wind was guarded by Mephistopheles , one of the four Kings of Hell. Before the finals of the Shaman Fight 2000 in Tokyo , Sati Saigan and Team "Myooh" went to Hell to retrieve the Five Elemental Spirits and deliver them to the Five Elemental Warriors. Kado succeeded in defeating Mephistopheles and freeing the Spirit of Wind for Chocolove McDonell .

Oversouls and Attacks

O.S. Spirit of Wind

O.S. Spirit of Wind (O.S スピリット・オブ・ウィンド)

  • Shaman: Chocolove McDonell
  • O.S. Type: Spirit Type
  • Spirit Ally: Spirit of Wind(Kami Class Spirit)
  • Medium: Air

Description: When Over Souled by Chocolove McDonell, it gains its entire body. Its movements seem to follow that of Chocolove himself. In this form, the Spirit of Wind's winds are so strong that even the surroundings become its weapon by throwing it at the opponent.

Attacks

  • God Breath Hurricane ( ゴッドブレスハリケーン , Goddo Buresu Harikēn ) : Using its incredible speed, the Spirit of Wind can quickly attack the opponent from many directions, using the slicing wind itself and whatever debris is thrown away with the wind. The wind produced is so powerful that its gust peaks at 120m/s, with a pressure of 900hPa.
  • Ice Age ( 氷河期 （ ） , Aisu Eiji ) : Combining its violent winds with the freezing cold of the Spirit of Rain , both spirits create a violent freezing wind.

Anime/Manga Difference

The Spirit of Wind does not appear in the anime series, as the Spirit of Fire appears to be a single, powerful, entity under the Patch's control rather than a member of this group, though it was mentioned that there were four other spirits worshiped by the Patch other than the Spirit of Fire.
