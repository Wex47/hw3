Ibuki Gakko ( 伊吹ガッコ , Ibuki Gakko ) is a fictional character from the manga series Shaman King Flowers .

Appearance

Gakko has multicolored hair with one side red on top while blue on the side. He wears a black tracksuit, created by a company called Babylon. This suit has stripes on the sides of the pants. He also has the word Babylon printed on one of the sleeves, along with a panda where his heart is covered.

Personality

Gakko is aloof, likes to fight, and doesn't care much for tradition as seen in his conversation with Namaha.

He declares his wish to be the strongest man alive.

Abilities and Powers

Spirit

Gakko's Guardian ghost is a girl who can change her appearance in battle in order to intimidate her opponents. He creates an Over Soul which gives him an armor-like appearance with a straw cloak. He holds a large cannon in one hand and a large knife in the other.

History

Flowers

Gakko is introduced to Hana at Funbari graveyard, where he fights him. Using Namaha, and forming O.S. Ogre Hunter, he defeats Hana by cutting his Oni in half. It is here that he is revealed as the leader of Hao's Anti-Oni Division and as a member of Team Hao in the FoM. with Hana as his boss. Alumi Niumbirch finishes the job, and is welcomed by her onto the team. He then listens as Alumi informs Hana, Yohane, and the others on what exactly the situation is.

Having stayed over at the Funbari Onsen, the next day after having eaten together with the other members of his team, both Nahama and Gakko explains the Flower of Maize rules to Hana.
