History

After noticing an increase in Onis in Kyoto, Japan. Hamo used his powers to locate the source and found a boy lying in the streets surrounded by Onis. Recognizing the boy was the source of the Onis, he had his apprentice, Daitaro exterminate them. After the Onis suddenly increase in huge numbers Hamo is forced to step in and then summons Zenki and Goki to deal with them all. He then notices the boy's potential for being able to summons all these Onis and realize that he must be Mappa Douji , the boy who killed Densen Hoshi .
