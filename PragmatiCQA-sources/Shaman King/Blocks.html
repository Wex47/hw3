About

Brocken Meyer's guardian ghosts are a pack of rats that he Over Souls in his building block pieces. Using the blocks, he can create many kinds of constructs for combat, collectively called O.S. Die Block ( OS 組換自在塊 （ ） , O.S. Die Block , lit. "Recombinant Mass") .

Attached to his Lego body, the blocks also serve as prosthetics for his removed limbs.

Oversouls and Attacks

O.S. Die Saurus

O.S. Die Saurus (O.S. DIE （ ） ·ザウルス Dai Zaurus )

  • Shaman: Brocken Meyer
  • O.S. Type: Spirit Type
  • Spirit Ally(s): Rat Spirits
  • Medium: Hao Blocks

Description: Using the many Hao Blocks at his disposal, Brocken Meyer creates a giant Tyrannosaurus rex , where his own body is attached to the middle of the T-Rex's chest.

O.S. Die Tank

O.S. Die Tank (O.S. DIE （ ） ·タンク Dai Tanku )

  • Shaman: Brocken Meyer
  • O.S. Type: Spirit Type
  • Spirit Ally(s): Rat Spirits
  • Medium: Hao Blocks

Description: Using the many Hao Blocks at his disposal, Brocken Meyer creates a tank which he himself drives like a normal chauffeur.

O.S. Die Bird

O.S. Block Bird (OS ブロックバード Burokku Bādo )

  • Shaman: Brocken Meyer
  • O.S. Type: Spirit Type
  • Spirit Ally(s): Rat Spirits
  • Medium: Hao Blocks

Description: Using the many Hao Blocks at his disposal, Brocken Meyer creates a large bird. This Over Soul has mainly been seen used as a means of transportation.

Anime/Manga Difference

His spirits do not appear in the 2001 anime series directly; however, in the end, Brocken is shown using his Die Block O.S. form.
