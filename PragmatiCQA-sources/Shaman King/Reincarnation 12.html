Short Summary

Asakura Yoh , Kyōyama Anna , Oyamada Manta , and Amidamaru are sent free tickets to see a film starring Lee Pyron . After watching it, they are confronted by Tao Jun who commands the Jiang Shi made from Pyron's corpse.
