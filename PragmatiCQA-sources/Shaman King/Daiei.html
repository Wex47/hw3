Daiei (大栄, " Daiei ") is a fictional character in the manga series of Shaman King . He is a senior priest of Chinese origin and is the oldest member of the Gandhara group and is part of Team "Nyorai" .

Appearance

An old stubby man, Daiei is completely bald. He dresses in orange monk robes of the Gandhara order.

Personality

He fancies tofu ryori and his favorite pastime is to recite Buddhist sutras. Due to his wisdom, it appears that he is Sati's adviser. As with other members of Gandhara, he treats her with great reverence.
