Short Summary

Umemiya Ryunosuke and his gang take up residence in an abandoned bowling alley, while Asakura Yoh and Oyamada Manta buy a replacement wooden sword for Ryu. After Ryu is turned down by Kyōyama Anna , he finds out that she lives with and is engaged to Yoh. That night, a ghost haunting the bowling alley appears to Ryu and offers to kill Yoh.
