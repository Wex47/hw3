Dong Honhon ( 東 紅紅 （ ） , Don Honhon , Chinese pinyin: Dōng Hónghóng ) is a fictional character in the Shaman King The Super Star manga series. She is the youngest child of the Dong Family , a member of Red Crimson, and one of Kamogawa Yosuke 's Helpers for Team YVS .

Appearance

Honhon is a slender woman with red eyes, messy black hair, and burn scars all over her face. She wears a black turtleneck and white pants that are cinched at the waist. She also carries a bottle gourd that contains her Guardian Ghost; it is tied with red cord.

Personality

Honhon deeply hates the Tao Family and seeks to kill them all.

Abilities and Powers

Initially the weakest of her siblings, Honhon eventually becomes the strongest after her fight with Tao Jun , in which she briefly died and was revived.

Spirit

Her Guardian Ghost is Keiten , a spirit that lives in her bottle gourd. Her Over Soul with him takes the shape of a robot-like armor.

History

After Enki spots Usui Horokeu in China, he sends a picture to Honhon, who tells him to mark Horokeu. She then heads to a mountain range near the Tao Family estate. There, she watches Enki attack Horokeu, but Lee Pyron interferes and destroys Enki's Jiang Shi . After Tao Jun shoots Enki, her group gets in a helicopter to leave. Honhon refuses to allow them to escape, so she releases her Guardian Ghost , Keiten . Jun fires an RPG at Honhon, who defends herself with her Over Soul "Zantsahan". Honhon throws its giant axe and destroys the tail of the helicopter. Hang Zang-Ching has Shion-Shion grab the helicopter and swing it at Honhon, but her Over Soul jumps over it and flies after Jun. Honhon and Keiten engage in battle with Pyron. Withstanding the Jiang Shi's kicks and punches, the Armor Over Soul cuts off Pyron's legs and then blows off his lower body. Despite her extreme exhaustion, Honhon refuses to give up and continues fighting Pyron. However, the rest of the Tao Family arrive and several Jiang Shi tackle Honhon's Over Soul. With so many of the Tao Family nearby, Honhon attempts to take them all out at once and unleashes all of her Furyoku in a giant blast. Her brother, Dong Yonhon , interferes and moves her away from the battlefield, and the blast merely covers their escape. Honhon falls asleep from exhaustion.

When she later wakes in the Dong Family's residence, she immediately begs for Yonhon's forgiveness in not killing the Tao Family. Yonhon instead thanks her for bearing the burden, and then introduces her to Kamogawa Yosuke and YVS . Although she thanks them for helping her family, she is unnerved by Yosuke.

When Jun travels to meet with Director Ten , Honhon watches the ensuing fight from a ledge outside. Honhon considers killing the Director once he is captured in order to keep him from talking, but decides to focus on killing Jun. When the three reach the roof of the building, Honhon ambushes them; however, Dong Dehon and Yonhon interrupt. While time is stopped, Honhon strikes Director Ten and, per Yonhon's request, leaves him alive on the roof. Dehon then moves Jun and Pyron to the base of the building, where Dehon and Yonhon were confronting Horokeu, Hang Zang-Ching , Brocken Meyer , and Bill Burton . Honhon is upset with Dehon's interference, but Dong Shahon point out that he organized this ahead of time. After the Four Holy Beasts introduce themselves, Jun expresses sympathy for Honhon, which infuriates Honhon because she thinks Jun is mocking her. Honhon unleashes Keiten and attacks Jun, while calling out the absence of Tao Ren . Although Jun claims Ren could not come, Ren then arrives and brings the fighting to an end. Keiten warns Honhon not to attack Ren, but she admits that she is too scared to move against him. After Dehon and Shahon warn Ren about their plans for the Flower of Maize , Honhon leaves with Dehon. As they walk away, Honhon asks if the Dong Family would free of their suffering if the Tao Family were gone, which he affirms.
