About

Tamegoroh is the spirit of an old man and the previous owner of the onsen where Asakura Yoh resides. During Tamegoroh's time, it was known as the "Flame Inn ( 民宿 炎 , Minshuku-En ) . He died when the inn mysteriously burned down and Tamegoroh refuses to divulge what caused the fire, though it is believed that he was responsible.

Since his death, he has haunted the inn, moving from bathroom to bathroom, until he was forcibly exorcised and now awaits reincarnation within the Great Spirit . Upon the establishment of Funbari Onsen , Tamegoroh's picture has been placed at the Onsen's altar alongside portraits of Asakura Mikihisa and Johann Faust VIII .
