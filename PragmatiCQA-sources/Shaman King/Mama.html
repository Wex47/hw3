About

Mama is the guardian spirit of Opacho. Although her true form is not seen in the series, its believed to take the form of a sheep.

Oversouls and Attacks

O.S. "Hair Mama"

O.S. Hair Mama (O.S. ヘヤーイン MAMA （ ） Heyāin Mama )

  • Shaman: Opacho
  • O.S. Type: Spirit Type
  • Spirit Ally(s): Mama
  • Medium: Opacho's hair

Description: Using her own hair, Opacho creates an Over Soul in the shape of a sheep around her body. The Over Soul has great offensive power as a single hit from it was able to launch Umemiya Ryunosuke up into the air and to defeat Renim , one of the Ten Patch Officiants .

Trivia

  • Mama's name is presumably a reference to both of Opacho's mother and Asanoha ; Opacho is an orphan, and Asakura Hao was raised by Ohachiyo after his mother died, and Ohachiyo became his first guardian spirit.
  • In the video game Shaman King: Master of Spirits 2 , Mama makes an appearance as an enemy in one of the stages leading to the final boss.
