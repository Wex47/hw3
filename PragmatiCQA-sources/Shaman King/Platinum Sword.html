Platinum Sword ( プラチナムソード , Purachinamu Sōdo ) is the spirit of a pelican and the Guardian Ghost of Radim .

About

Platinum Sword was apparently a normal pelican until somewhere along the line, it later evolved into a Serei-Class spirit.

Oversouls and Attacks

O.S. Platinum Sword

O.S. "Mike in Platinum Sword"(O.S. マイク・イン・プラチナムンード)

  • Shaman: Radim
  • O.S. Type: Weapon
  • Spirit Ally: Platinum Sword
  • Medium Used: Radim's Microphone

Description: Platinum Sword becomes a dense blade of Furyoku . This Over Soul concentrates all of Radim's Furyoku into the microphone to produce the blade, this carries numerous risks but Radim's martial arts skills compensate for these. Due to this nature, any attacks made are extremely powerful, being able to cut through both Yoh 's White Swan O.S. and Lyserg 's Mastema Dolkeem easily. It also increases Radim's speed, making him faster than Chocolove McDonell . These powers are further increased using the Patch Song .

Unnamed O.S.

Unnamed O.S.

  • Shaman: Radim
  • O.S. Type: Spirit
  • Spirit Ally: Platinum Sword
  • Medium Used: Radim's Microphone

Description: Radim creates a gigantic pelican beak to swallow the opponent. The full ability of this Over Soul is unknown as it was nullified by Yoh.

Anime/Manga Difference

While Radim had a brief appearance in the 2001 anime series, Platinum Sword was not shown.
