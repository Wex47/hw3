Kanta (かん太 Kanta ) is a fictional character in the manga series of Shaman King . He is the spirit of a billboard painter. He was called Shiro (シロ Shiro ) when he turned into a self-bound spirit.

About

Kanta was a billboard painter who accidentally tipped over his ladder, fell onto the street, and was abruptly hit and killed by a truck. Unable to finish the billboard, Kanta could not find peace, and his obsession turned him into a self-bound spirit, haunting his place of death and knocking down his billboard onto unwary passersby who could sense spirits.

A year after his death, Kanta pushed the sign down onto Asakura Yoh and later Oyamada Manta . Amidamaru protected Manta from Kanta, while Yoh found out about the spirit's past. Yoh returned with white paint and allowed Kanta to use his body to finish painting the billboard.

Kanta was later temporarily summoned by Kyōyama Anna as part of a demonstration of her powers as an Itako.

After Asakura Hao became the Shaman King, Kanta boarded the Soul Train and helped keep Yoh and the others from being pulled into oblivion.

Anime/Manga Differences

Although both anime series skip over Kanta's story with Yoh, Kanta does briefly appear in Episode 3 of the 2021 series, when Anna summons the various spirits that have interacted with Yoh.

He does appear in the video games Shaman King: Master of Spirits and Shaman King: Master of Spirits 2 .

Trivia

  • Kanta is the first and only self-bound spirit to appear in the series.
