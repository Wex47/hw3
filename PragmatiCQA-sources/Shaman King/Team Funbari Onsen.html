Team "Funbari Onsen" ( ふんばり温泉チーム , Funbari Onsen Chīmu ) is a team formed by Asakura Yoh during the second round of the Shaman Fight .

About

For the second round of the Shaman Fight , the shaman competitors were required to form teams of three. Yoh's current group consisted of five people after Lyserg Diethel left the group. When Ryu insists teaming up with the missing Lyserg, Ren takes Horohoro and Chocolove McDonell .

To complete the numbers for his team, Yoh agrees to Johann Faust VIII 's request to let him join Yoh when it becomes apparent that Lyserg cannot be found. Named personally by Kyōyama Anna , Team "Funbari Onsen" is composed of Asakura Yoh , Umemiya Ryunosuke , and Faust VIII.

Members

Funbari Onsen Team
Asakura Yoh ( 麻倉 葉 , Asakura Yō )
The leader of the team, Asakura Yoh wields Harusame and Futunomitama no Turugi , through which he channels Amidamaru , an ancient samurai.
Umemiya Ryunosuke ( 梅宮 竜之介 , Umemiya Ryūnosuke )
Known as "Wooden Sword Ryu", Umemiya Ryunosuke wields a wooden sword, through which he channels Tokageroh , an ancient Japanese bandit.
Johann Faust VIII ( ヨハン ファウストVIII世 , Yohan Fausuto Hassei )
A descendant of the legendary Faust , Johann Faust VIII is a German necromancer who utilizes the bones and spirits of his wife and pet dog .
