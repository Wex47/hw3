Purple Kick ( パープルキック , Pāpuru Kikku ) is the spirit of a locust and was originally the Guardian Ghost of Chrom of the Ten Patch Officiants. After his death, Purple Kick was inherited by his younger brother Nichrom .

About

It is stated by Nichrom that Purple Kick is the fastest of the ten Patch Official's guardian spirits. It originally belonged to Chrom and after his death, Nichrom still kept his older brother's Guardian Ghost.

Oversouls and Attacks

O.S. Purple Kick

O.S. Purple Kick (O.S. パープルキック)

  • Shaman: Chrom
  • O.S. Type: Weapon Type
  • Spirit Ally: Purple Kick
  • Medium Used: Unknown

Description: From what little has been revealed of the fight that occurred between Chrom and Tao Ren, this oversoul seemingly demonstrates the ability to shoot a sort of energy beam of concentrated Furyoku. Chrom also appeared to demonstrate the ability to leap tremendous distances using this Over Soul, avoiding an attack by jumping several stories into the air, leaving only a trail of Furyoku emitted from his feet behind.

O.S. Purple Kick (O.S. パープルキック)

  • Shaman: Nichrom
  • O.S. Type: Weapon Type
  • Spirit Ally: Purple Kick
  • Medium Used: Unknown

Description: Using the spirit Purple Kick that originally belonged to his dead brother, Chrom, Nichrom creates a new form of Over Soul over his legs which provides a boost of strength to Nichrom's legs, resulting in an incredible gain in speed.

Attacks

  • Insect Dance ( 踊る虫 （ ） , Dansu Insekuto ) : Nichrom extends the forelegs upon Purple Kick, before kicking an opponent. This not only results in an increase in the kick's range but by using the incredible boost in both strength and speed to Nichrom's legs, provided by Purple Kick, he generates tremendous power behind each kick.

Anime/Manga Difference

Because of the violence of the fight between Tao Ren and Chrom, it was toned down in the 2001 anime series and Purple Kick was never shown.
