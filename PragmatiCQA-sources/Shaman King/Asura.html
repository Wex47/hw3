About

Little is known about Asura, except that he is the guardian ghost of Gandhara shamans Mamy and her partner Samy ; he appears to have originally been a Buddhist statue like Dainichi Nyorai . He is described as having an evil passion so strong that it difficult to form an Over Soul with him .
