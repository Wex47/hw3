The Leidi Group ( 雷帝グループ , Raitei Gurūpu , lit. "Lightning Emperor Group") is a large conglomerate in China. Tao Ren is the current CEO of the company, while his sister Tao Jun is the Vice President.

About

After graduating from university, Tao Ren , together with Bill Burton , Hang Zang-Ching , and Brocken Meyer , formed the Leidi Group as an electronics manufacturing company. Eventually the Leidi Group began expanding, buying other companies and turning them into subsidiaries. A news report watched by Usui Horokeu mentions that in under a year it had made 18 acquisitions and that amongst its subsidiaries are at least three IT companies, two iron and steel manufacturers, and twelve food corporations.
