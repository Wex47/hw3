Shaman King (シャーマンキング, Shaman King ) is a shonen manga created by Hiroyuki Takei which was serialized in Weekly Shonen Jump from 1998 to 2004, when it was abruptly cancelled. The first volume was released on December 3, 1998. The final one, volume 32, was released on January 5, 2005.

  • Manga Volumes: 32
  • Languages: Japanese, english
