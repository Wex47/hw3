Shaman Fights Second Round

During his team's match against Team "Hana-Gumi" , Miracle-T witnesses the quick and brutal defeat of his teammate Kaizer-T , whose pervertedness outraged and disgusted their opponents. As Kaizer-T loses consciousness, Miracle-T suggests to team leader Bookila-T that they surrender, to which Bookila-T immediately agrees. However, Team "Hana-Gumi" still beat them up too.
