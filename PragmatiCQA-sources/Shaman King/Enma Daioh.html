Enma Daioh ( 閻魔大王 , Enma-daiō ) is a fictional character in the Shaman King manga and anime series. He is the greatest of the Four Kings of Hell, and the guardian of the Spirit of Earth .

About

As a gigantic being, Emna wears traditional Japanese robes, has a big beard and wears a crown with the kanji "王", which means "king", on it.

When Asakura Hao reincarnated for the first time, he was forced to face Enma Daioh.

When Saigan Sati and Team "Myooh" invaded Hell to steal the four remaining Grand Elemental Spirits , Sati faced off against Enma Daioh in order to retrieve the Spirit of Earth .

Anime/Manga Difference

While he is mentioned, Enma does not appear in person in the 2001 anime series.
