Short Summary

After Oyamada Manta and Asakura Yoh witness Tobinai Tatsushi bully another student, Yoh decides to recruit the ghost of a boxer. Yoh asks Tatsushi about his dead teacher, Gussy Kenji , but Tatsushi instead punches Yoh. That night, Tatsushi finds that Ryu and his gang have taken over the old gym as their new hideout, so Tatsushi beats them up for it. Yoh challenges Tatsushi to a boxing match and channels the spirit of Kenji in order to fight and knock out Tatsushi. After his defeat, Tatsushi reforms his attitude.
