The Patch Song ( パッチソング , Patchi Songu ) is a hypnotic technique utilized by the Patch Tribe for the final rounds of the Shaman Fight .

About

The Patch Song is a song taught by Renim and Bron to the other Patch Priests in order to increase their Furyoku and their overall spiritual strength, as well as to alter their mental states and personalities. With this Magna , whose body was otherwise damaged beyond normal movement, could use his full body Over Soul to move freely.

When Silva attempted to steal the Grand Elemental Spirits , he was captured and forced to listen to the song until his doubt about the Shaman Fight disappeared. It also forced him to fight Asakura Yoh and his friends without any emotions.

Later Johann Faust VIII copied the effects of the song to create the Funbari no Uta which he also planned to use as a jingle for the Funbari Onsen .
