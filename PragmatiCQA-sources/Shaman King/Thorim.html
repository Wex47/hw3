Thorim ( トリム , Torimu ) is a fictional character from the Shaman King Flowers manga series. He is the captain of the Knights Templar , a division of the Patch Tribe loyal to YVS .

Appearance

Thorim has long black hair, a long black mustache and a goatee. His face is very gruff and Kalim describes it as more terrifying than his own.

Like his fellow members of the Knights Templars, Thorim wears a long white cloak and heavy armor, both with a cross motif. Unlike his fellow members, his helmet has long horns, resembling the ones on the skull, worn by their god, YVS.

Abilities and Powers

Spirit

With his spirit, he can create an extremely powerful Over Soul called O.S. Machete Dragon ( マチェーテドラゴン , Machēte Doragon ) .

History

Past

Thorim was once a member of the Patch Tribe and one of the ten priests responsible for the previous Shaman Fight . After YVS won and became Shaman King, Thorim and his fellow priests, following the tribe's laws, converted to YVS' religion and became the Knights Templar , with Thorim also becoming captain of the Divine Guard.

Trivia

  • Like the other Patch Priests, Thorim's name is derived from an element from the periodic table ; his name is derived from thorium .
