About

They are the nine hundred ninety-nine members of the Japanese Imperial Infantry that were slaughtered by Tao Ching during World War II and were turned into Jiang Shi.

Ching would later present them as a gift to his grandson, Tao Ren , on his ninth birthday and had him cut them down to test his fighting skills in preparations for the upcoming Shaman Fights. Private Shimamura Jōtarō was amongst nine hundred ninety-nine corpses.
