Shaman King (Remix)

Information

Author

Hiroyuki Takei

Publisher

Shueisha

Number of Chapters

300 16 (Remix Tracks)

Years

2011

The Shaman King Remix are a set of 16 omnibus volumes as revised compilations of the Shaman King Kang Zeng Bang with the Shaman King Remix Tracks shorts.

About

Shueisha decided to release revised editions of the Shaman King Kang Zeng Bang volumes into new omnibus compilations to a total of 16 volumes each containing a Shaman King Remix Tracks omake and a special UV postcard.
