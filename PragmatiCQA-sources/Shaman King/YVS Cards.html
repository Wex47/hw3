The YVS Cards ( ヤービスカード , Yābisu Kādo ) are special cards created by YVS and used by Kamogawa Yosuke . They possess a variety of powerful effects.

About

The YVS cards utilized by Yosuke are purchased from YVS, with costs ranging from ¥500 to several trillion. In addition, the cards are finite in use as Yosuke can only use each card once, though he can combine their various effects. However, he can purchase as many as he likes, so long as he can pay for them. The cost of the cards is of little concern to Yosuke, who confidently asserts that he can use the cards themselves to win more money.

Cards

  • YVS Mini ( ミニ ヤービス , Mini Yābisu ) : Costs 100,000,000 points.
  • Erase the Memory
  • View the Soul
  • Copy 180 ( コピー180 , Kopī 180 ) : When used, the card has a validity of 180 seconds and can create a perfect copy of a target only once. However, the cost of the card is high and may be difficult to control.
  • Read the Mind 10 ( 読心10 , Dokushin 10 ) : It allows the user to read his target's mind.
  • Materialization 180 ( 具現180 , Gugen 180 ) : A Golden Card that costs 200,000 Furyoku points. It allows Yosuke to use only one technique before the effect of the card dissipates.
  • Mimic 1 ( ミミック1 , Mimikku 1 ) : It allows the user to recreate the technique of a particular object.
  • View the Soul ∞ : Costs 10,000,000 points. It allows the user to see spirits.
  • Obelisk ( オベリスク , Oberisuku ) : With a physical power level 99, YVS is placed on the user's arm and a large obelisk pillar extends from YVS' body, extending forward, becoming an incredible length and size. Referred to by YVS as his most powerful card. Costs $100,000,000.
  • Time Stop 30 ( タイムストップ30 , Taimu Sutoppu 30 ) : Costs 25,000 points. For 30 seconds, time stops for everything except YVS and the card's user. However, the side effect means that the user ages faster than other people.
  • Archive Viewing ( アーカイブ ビューイング , Ākaibu Byūingu ) : Costs 2,500 points. It allows the user to access the Great Spirit and view any location on Earth within the past 100 years. It is limited to one location and has a duration of three hours.
  • Time Stop 180 ( タイムストップ180 , Taimu Sutoppu 180 ) : Costs 15,000,000 points. For 180 seconds, time appears to stop for everything except YVS and the card's user. However, this is an illusion as the user and YVS are actually moved into a higher dimension that allows them to interact with the real world. Other shamans can evade the illusion by using Cloud to access the dimension.
  • Runaway Old Man ( 暴走する老人 , Bōsōsuru Rōjin ) : Costs ¥300. It forcefully turns a target's remaining life into Furyoku.
  • Time Warp (タイムワープ,) - Allows the user to jump ahead or back 12 hours in time. Costs 500 yen.
  • X-Ray 125 (X線125,): - With a usage duration of 12 seconds, it allows the user to see through any kind of material at will. It can be used as many times as the user wants within the time limit, but the effect of disappearing immediately when the time is up.

Anime/Manga Difference

The YVS Cards debuted in the Yahabe one-shot/OVA, and returns in Shaman King Flowers .

Trivia

  • They are initially known as "YHVH Cards" in Yahabe .
