Team "Tsuki-Gumi" ( チーム「月組」 , Chīmu "Tsuki-gumi" , lit. "Team 'Moon Team'") is one of Asakura Hao 's team of followers that participated in the Shaman Fight . The members are Mohamed Tabarsi , Hang Zang-Ching , and "Big Guy" Bill Burton .

About

Team "Tsuki-Gumi" would have fought Team "Funbari Onsen" in the second round of fights, but Peyote Diaz betrays the other followers of Hao and kills Hang Zang-Ching and Bill Burton , with Tamurazaki Midori killing Mohamed Tabarsi .

Members

Team "Tsuki-Gumi"
Mohamed Tabarsi ( モハメド・タバルスィー , Mohamedo Tabarusī )
A mercenary from the Middle East, Mohamed Tabarsi was recruited by Asakura Hao . His Guardian Ghost is Djinn .
Bill Burton ( ドル・バートン , Biru Bāton )
An American football player, Bill Burton wields the spirits of his former teammates, the Big Guys .
Hang Zang-Ching ( ハン・ザンチン , Han Zan Chin )
A Chinese shaman, Hang Zang-Ching wields the spirt of a panda, Shion-Shion .
