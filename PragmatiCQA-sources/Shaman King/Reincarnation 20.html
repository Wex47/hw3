Short Summary

Tokageroh , with Umemiya Ryunosuke and Oyamada Manta as hostages, confronts Asakura Yoh and Amidamaru at the Flame Inn . When Yoh and Amidamaru integrate , Tokageroh reveals he has Harusame and cuts Yoh's chest.
