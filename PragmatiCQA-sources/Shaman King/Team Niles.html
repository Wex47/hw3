About

A team from Giza, Egypt , they use the power received from ancient times as part of their shamanic abilities. They were placed against X-Laws team X-I in their first match in the second round. Lyserg was given the order to defeat them at all costs and quickly, destroys all their mediums, and cripples Anatel in one foul attack. However, a combination of both his and Morphine's hesitation allowed leader Anatel to release a deadly Over Soul that would kill him. With no other choice, Iron Maiden Jeanne released her full power, killing the leader with her guardian ghost Shamash and the others members with other torture devices.

Members

Team "Niles"
Anatel Pokki ( アナテル・ポッキ , Anateru Pokki )
The leader of Niles, Anatel Pokki wears a pharaoh mask in battle and commands ten thousand spirits as part of his Over Soul abilities.
Khafre Puljiz ( カフラー・プリツ , Kafura Puritsu )
Considered the lord of all mummies, Khafre Puljiz is an embalmer and wears an Anubis mask in combat. His Guardian Ghost is Kitka , an ancient Egyptian warrior.
Nakht Pitrah ( ナクト・ピトラ , Nakuto Pitora )
Nakht Pitrah wears a large Egyptian pyramid mask, through which he channels the power of Scarab .

Anime/Manga Difference

In their only appearance in the 2001 anime series, they proclaim that Ancient Egypt was the place of birth of shamanism, while in the manga and 2021 anime series, Anatel only states it about reincarnation, astrologist, and alchemy.
