Comet (彗星 Suisei )

About

This technique produces a large comet, composed of ice and other frozen gases, within one of the O.S. Great Spirit's hands. However; rather than throwing it towards an enemy, it makes a slashing motion with significant force, using the comet's tail in a similar manner to a sword's blade.
