The ikupasuy ( イクパスイ , ikupasui ) is a prayer stick and good luck charm used by the members of the Ainu Tribe .

About

An ikupasuy is a carved wooden stick used for prayer by the Ainu Tribe and the carvings have a very deep meaning to the Ainu. It is also a good luck charm and the one carrying it will be protected by the gods.

The one carried by Usui Horokeu was crafted to him by his little sister, Usui Pirica , and would later become the main medium for two of his Over Souls .

Anime/Manga Difference

In the 2001 anime series, it also served as the medium for Horokeu's Giant Over Soul.

Trivia

  • In the Ainu language, ikupasuy means "libation stick" since they would be dipped into alcohol during religious ceremonies.
