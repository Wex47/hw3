About

Ju-Che is a short, childlike spirit and one of the Four Holy Beasts , a group of Kami-class spirits belonging to the Dong Family . Like the others, Ju-Che is controlled through a small bell with a carving that corresponds to the spirit's animal, a pheasant for Ju-Che.

Oversouls and Attacks

O.S.

O.S.

  • Shaman: Dong Dehon
  • O.S. Type: Spirit Type
  • Spirit Ally: Ju-Che
  • Medium used: Bell

Description: Ju-Che has the ability of "space-time control" ( 時空操作 , jikū sōsa ) , which allows the spirit to manipulate the flow of time for a target.

Attacks

  • Tokidome ( 時止 （ ） , Tokidome , lit. "Time Stop") : Ju-Che stops the progression of time for one or multiple targets. Those who are frozen in time can be moved around and will be unaware of what is happening until they are unfrozen.

Trivia

  • Ju-Che's appearance and abilities are based on Ultimo from Takei's ULTIMO series.
