About

He is a koropokkuru . Because of his unnatural size, he has been shunned by the other koropokkurus.

When Kurobe Tamiko died in the mountains and refused to leave this world, Gororo appeared before her. He gave her the choice to stay and become a nature spirit who would the protect the land.

Anime/Manga Difference

Because Lycan never appeared in the 2001 anime series, Gororo did not either.
