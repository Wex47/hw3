Dong Shahon ( 東 狭紅 （ ） , Don Shahon , Chinese pinyin: Dōng Xiáhóng ) is a fictional character in the Shaman King Red Crimson manga series. She is the eldest daughter of the Dong Family , a member of Red Crimson, and a captain for Team YVS . Her Guardian Ghost is Chinron , one of the Four Holy Beasts .

Appearance

Shahon is a slender woman with long, dark-colored hair. On the left side of her head, she has three feathers and strands of beads pinned in her hair. She wears a tight-fitting dress with flowers on the lower left side. She also wears a small, sleeveless jacket that is pinned closed at her neck.

Personality

Unlike her siblings, Shahon is not preoccupied with destroying the Tao Family and considers it a "trifling dispute." She chooses to focus on the long-term goals rather than the more immediate ones.

Abilities and Powers

Spirit

Her Guardian Ghost is the Holy Beast Chinron of the East and has the ability to manipulate memories. She controls Chinron through a small bell that has a carving of a dragon.

History

Shahon accompanied her brother Dehon to the Patch Village. After Asakura Hana killed Kamogawa Yosuke , Dehon retrieved Kamogawa's body while Shahon used Chinron 's ability to erase Hana's memories of the events.

Red Crimson

Years later, at Dehon's request, Shahon and her siblings gather together to confront Tao Jun . As fighting breaks out, Tao Ren interrupts. After seeing that neither Dehon nor Ren intended to seriously fight, Shahon leaves to attend to more pressing business that night.
