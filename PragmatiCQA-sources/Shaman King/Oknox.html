Appearance

Oknox is a rather large and obese man who was barely able to fit his head through a door in his own home. Fitting his title as a count, he is well dressed in black and white clothes and has short black hair and a small moustache.

Personality

Though he initially appear to be a benevolent and charitable man, he is actually rather ruthless when it comes to his criminal activities, killing Wat on a whim to test if Lyserg posses the same skills as his father and attempting to kill Lyserg once he became a threat.

History

Past

He used his wealth to take in orphans from the street and give them a comfortable home to live in, though it is only a front to cover the fact that he deals in human trafficking. His day job appears to be working as a notary clerk. Oknox once hired Liam Diethel , who would later get a hunch of his dealings though Liam's premature death stopped any possible investigation.

He once took in Wat Hudson who lived in the sewers and later Liam's son Lyserg Diethel after the latter's grandfather died.
