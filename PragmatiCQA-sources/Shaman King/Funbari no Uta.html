The Funbari no Uta ( ふんばりの詩 , Funbari no Uta , lit. "Funbari Poem") is a jingle created by Johann Faust VIII , in imitation of the Patch Song .

Contents

  • 1 About
  • 2 Lyrics

About

While fighting Radim , Johann Faust VIII came up with this song as an equal to the Patch Song and to give their own group an advantage over the Patch Priests . Faust also intended for it to become the jingle for Funbari Onsen , which it eventually did.
