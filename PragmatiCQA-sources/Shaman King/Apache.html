Apache ( アパッチ , Apacchi ) is a fictional character in the manga series of Shaman King . He is a Member of Ryu's Gang .

Appearance

Apache is a muscular man and wears a tank top.

Personality

He is very loyal to Ryu .

History

Past

Apache was born on April 26, 1980, and was from Saitama, Japan. He ran away from home and went to the riverbank road. He was starving to death when Ryu came and gave him ice cream mochi. The two later became friends.

Meeting Yoh

Ryu tells Apache and Space Shot to attack Asakura Yoh . The two are defeated after Yoh uses Hyōi-Gattai with Amidamaru .

Meeting with Tokageroh

Apache and the others stop Tokageroh from killing Oyamada Manta so they can save Ryu.

Shaman Fights in Tokyo

Before Ryu goes to America, Apache and the others write him a farewell card.

Anime/Manga Difference

In the manga series, Apache and Space Shot attack Yoh with knives. However, in the anime series, they use their bare fist.

Trivia

  • According to the character files, Apache and Space Shot make a good tag-team since they both attack Yoh.
