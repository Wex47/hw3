About

Not much is known about this team. All the members are from South America.

History

Shaman Fight Second Round

NAZCA managed to qualify for the tournament round of the Shaman Fight and was the seeded team in the block with the X-Laws 's Team X-I and Gandhara 's Team "Deva".

Though their first scheduled match in the tournament was against Team "Deva", which had defeated Team "Cowboys" , NAZCA withdrew from the tournament, fearing the possibility of fighting Iron Maiden Jeanne of X-I after they witnessed her power.
