The Shaman King Remix Tracks are small omake chapters that were released every two weeks within the omnibus tankobon reprints of the Shaman King series called Shaman King (Remix) . Each volume contains a single chapter of 3-9 pages.

About

This mini-series takes places between the last chapter of the Kang Zeng Bang and Shaman King Flowers . Almost all of the chapters are told through a video on the IPatch , a traditional art craft made by the Patch Tribe that resembles the iPad , as it is being viewed by some of the main characters.
