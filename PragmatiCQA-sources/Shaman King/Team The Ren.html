Team "The Ren" ( チーム「 THE （ ） 蓮 （ ） 」 , Chīmu "Za Ren" ) is a team formed by Tao Ren during the second round of the Shaman Fights.

About

During the 2nd round of the Shaman Tournament, it was mandatory to be in a team of three. After Lyserg Diethel leaves Asakura Yoh 's group, the remaining shamans of the group are forced to split into two teams. Because Umemiya Ryunosuke initially insists on teaming up with the missing Lyserg, Ren immediately forms his own group with Usui Horokeu and Chocolove McDonell , as he would rather deal with a sure thing rather than wasting time on trying to find Lyserg.

Members

Team "The Ren"
Tao Ren ( 道蓮 （ ） , Tao Ren )
The leader of the team, Tao Ren hails from the Tao Family and wields the Basontou and Bâo-Lèi Sword , through which he channels Bason , an ancient Chinese warrior.
Usui Horokeu ( 碓氷ホロケウ , Usui Horokeu )
A member of the Ainu Tribe , Usui Horokeu manipulates ice with the help of his Guardian Ghost , Kororo .
Chocolove McDonell ( チョコラブ・マクダネル , Chokorabu Makudaneru )
Chocolove McDonell is an American and former gang member. He commands two Seirei class spirits: Mic the Jaguar and Pascal Avaf .

Anime/Manga Difference
