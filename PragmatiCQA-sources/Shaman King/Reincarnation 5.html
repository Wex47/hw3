Short Summary

While Asakura Yoh and Oyamada Manta are eating, a fire breaks out in the restaurant above them. With the fire trucks delayed due to traffic, Yoh rushes into the building to save some children trapped on the roof. He manages to succeed with the help of Amidamaru . From another building, a boy and his spirit watched Yoh and Amidamaru.
