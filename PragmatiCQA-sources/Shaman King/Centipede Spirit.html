Centipede Spirit (ムカデの精霊, " Mukade no seirei ") is a spirit of a large teal centipede that is Kouji Yamada 's Guardian Ghost in the Shaman King 2001 anime series .

About

The large teal centipede spirit had a hundred flammable like-legs that can melt ice; however, not much of it is known. After Damayaji's death, it later vanished before it could hurt Ellie and Millie .

Anime/Manga Difference

The Centipede Spirit is a 2001 anime-only character and as such does not appear in the manga series.
