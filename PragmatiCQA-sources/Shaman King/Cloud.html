Cloud ( クラウド , Kuraudo ) is a dimensional art ( 次元法 , jigen-hō ) in the Shaman King The Super Star manga series. It enables the user to multiply their Furyoku .

About

The user accesses the Great Spirit and projects duplicates of themselves in their immediate surroundings. These duplicates each carry the same total Furyoku as the original, thus multiplying the amount that the original can utilize. Skilled users can create a copy of someone else in order to utilize that person's particular skills.

During usage, the user may appear to have multiple sets of limbs and heads, akin to deities from Indian mythology.
