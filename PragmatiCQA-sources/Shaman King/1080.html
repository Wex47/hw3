About

After the Oh-Oni 's defeat by Asakura Yoh and Matamune 's hands and Kyōyama Anna 's open heart, the scattered bead remains that were mediums for the demon sprites to create the Oh-Oni were strung together by Asakura Kino as the ultimate Itako tool. It

Powers and Abilities

  • Demon Subjugation (降魔調伏 Gōma Chōbuku): Anna uses this technique with the 1080 beads to gain control over Zenki and Goki .
  • Sealing Barrier (呪縛結界): Anna's most powerful sealing technique. Used only in the 2001 Anime, which was unsuccessful as Hao managed to overpower Anna.

Anime/Manga Difference

In the 2001 Anime, its origins are traced back to the Asakura Family heritage, where each bead is a medium containing an Asakura shaman's spirit. It was created for the sole purpose of destroying Asakura Hao , until it was destroyed as Hao managed to overpower them.
