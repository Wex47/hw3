About

Not much is known about this team. All the members are from Japan.

History

Shaman Fights Second Round

The team fought against Team "Hana-Gumi" in the Second Round of the Shaman Fight , and after Kaizer-T was brutally attacked, the other two fearing for their lives, forfeited. However, Team "Hana-Gumi" still beat them up too.

Members

T-Production
Bookila-T ( ブッキラ・ T （ ） , Bukkira Tī )
The leader of the team, Bookila-T is a parody of Hiroyuki Takei , the creator of Shaman King .
Kaizer-T ( カイゼル・ T （ ） , Kaizeru Tī )
Kaizer-T is a parody of Yusuke Takeyama, one of Takei's assistants.
Miracle-T ( ミラクル・ T （ ） , Mirakuru Tī )
Miracle-T is a parody of Yoichiro Tanabe, one of Takei's assistants.

Anime/Manga Difference

The team made only a cameo in Episode 52 of the 2001 anime series. They fight against Team "Hoshi-Gumi" and end up getting killed off-screen by Asakura Hao .
