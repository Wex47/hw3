The Four Holy Beasts ( 四聖獣 , Shiseijū ) are a group of Kami Class spirits that are said to protect the four corners of the world. The title is also shared by the four members of the Dong Family that wield these spirits.

About

The four spirits have short, childlike appearances, which belie their god-like powers, such as space-time control and sensory control. They are controlled by bells which bear carvings that correspond to the spirits' animals.

Members

  • Ju-Che of the South and Dong Dehon
  • Shen Woo of the North and Dong Keihon
  • Pai-Fu of the West and Dong Yonhon
  • Chinron of the East and Dong Shahon

Trivia

  • The Four Holy Beasts are in opposition to the Four Perils , which belong to the Tao Family .
  • The spirits' appearances are based on characters from Takei's ULTIMO .
