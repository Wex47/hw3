Shaman King The Super Star ( シャーマンキング ザ スーパースター , SHAMAN KING THE SUPER STAR ) is a manga series by Hiroyuki Takei and a sequel to the series Shaman King Flowers . The chapters are serialized in Shonen Magazine Edge magazine. The first chapter was released on May 17, 2018.

About

A shaman is a person who has the mysterious ability to communicate with gods, spirits, and the spirits of the dead, connecting this world and the next. The third generation shaman, the Third Itako Anna, a.k.a. Alumi Niumbirch , is looking for a super class spirit to win the proxy war of the gods.
