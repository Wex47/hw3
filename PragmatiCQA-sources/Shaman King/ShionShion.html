About

Shion-Shion is the spirit of a giant panda that used to belong to a Japanese zoo. He used to be everyone's attraction but he was extremely unhappy, as he did not have the freedom he desired. One day, he attempted to escape but in the process attacked a person, resulting in there being no option left except to kill him.

Just like Shion-Shion, Hang Zang-Ching was incredibly unhappy, as no one seemed to like him. When the two first meet at the Japanese zoo, they soon instantly understood each other's feelings and so Hang Zang-Ching decided to take Shion-Shion as his guardian ghost. In respect to Shion-Shion, Hang Zang-Ching makes use of a golden gong, that Shion-Shion used to love to play with, as his medium.

Oversoul and Attacks

O.S. Shion-Shion Gong Xiong-Mao

O.S. "Shion-Shion Gong Xiong-Mao" (O.S. ドラクマ ネコ Dorakuma Neko )

  • Shaman: Hang Zang-Ching
  • O.S. Type: Weapon Type
  • Spirit Ally: Shion-Shion
  • Medium used: Chinese Gold Gong

Description : When Zang-Ching places his guardian ghost into his golden gong, which has Shion-Shion's face and claws. Even when the gong is split in half, it can still attack.

Attacks

  • Black Panda Paw ( 黒いパンダの手 , Kuroi Panda no Te ) : Zang-Ching attacks his opponents using Shion-Shion's claws.
  • Double Black Panda Paws ( 黒いパンダの両手 , Kuroi Panda no Ryōte ) : After the gong is split, Zang-Ching throws both halves.
  • Panda Bomb ( 爆パンダ , Baku Panda ) : Zang-Ching throws his gong up and it creates a dangerous explosion.
  • Black Panda Bomb: (ブラックパンダの爆弾," Burakku panda no bakudan "): Zang-Ching launches the Over Souled gong into the air, attached to an iron chain. He then swings it forward, so the gong smashes into anyone in front of him.
