Densen Hoshi (田浅 法師, Densen Hōshi ) is a fictional character in the manga series of Shaman King . He is a monk who was responsible for the death of Asaha Doji 's mother, Asanoha .

Appearance

Densen is a very chubby man, who appears to be in his late 60's. He wears a traditional Japanese robes, he has short grey hair.

Personality

Despite his false self-claimed skills he did actually not believe in demons himself and considered them only to be a symbol of man's own dark thoughts.

Abilities and Powers

Densen is a fraud who does not have shamanic powers, despite his claims.

History

Mappa Doji

Densen Hoshi was supposedly a monk, held in high esteem for his "ability" to see demons and kill them. People went to him to tell him about a woman called Asanoha who spoke to things others could not see and was considered as a demon, a fox disguised as a woman. Densen and his men burnt her alive in her house but did not kill her infant son, Asaha Doji , believing that it was not really necessary.

Soon afterward, he was asked to kill him too but Asaha, having formed his first Over Soul with Ohachiyo , fought them off and killed Densen to avenge his mother.
