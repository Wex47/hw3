Oyamada Mannoko ( 小山田 まんの子 , Oyamada Mannoko ) is a fictional character in the Shaman King series. She is the 5-year-old daughter of Oyamada Mansumi and Keiko and the younger sister of Manta .

Appearance

Like her brother Manta, she is short, has sandy-brown hair and she has large eyes. She has red bows in her hair and wears a red dress with matching shoes.

Personality

She is a master at computers. When Mansumi noticed it, he put her in charge of the Oyamada Company's software department and built up a fortune with stocks. Although she doesn't like that problematic job at all, she uses it to tease Manta when they are together. She loves bespectacled men.

History

Shaman Fights in Tokyo

She is seen in the hospital with Manta and Keiko. She tells her mother about Asakura Yoh and how he and Manta ditch class so they can be together.

Anime/Manga Difference

Mannoko does not appear in the 2001 anime series.
