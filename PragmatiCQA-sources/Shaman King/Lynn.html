"Buffalo" Lynn ( バッファロー・リン , Baffarō Rin ) is a fictional character in the manga series of Shaman King . He was a member of Chocolove 's former gang, Shaft .

Appearance

He is a slim man with an afro.

Personality

Like all the members of Shaft, he lost everything in his life and ended up becoming a gang member.

History

Past

When Chocolove decides to go out for a walk, Lynn and Ben try to stop him so he will not kill another person but they are stopped by another member, who tells them that Chocolove is walking because he is thinking of how his parents were killed on Christmas Day.

Shaman Fight Second Round

Lynn and the other Shaft members travel to Tokyo for the Shaman Fight and cheer for Chocolove during his match against Team "Tsuchi-Gumi" .

Redseb Munzer with Golem later kills Shaft to avenge his father's death.

Invading the Plants

He along with the other Shaft members are seen on the Soul Train supporting Chocolove.

Anime/Manga Difference

He does not appear in the 2001 anime series.
