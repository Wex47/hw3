Nakht Pitrah ( ナクト・ピトラ , Nakuto Pitora ) is a fictional character in the manga and anime series of Shaman King . He is part of Team "Niles" .

Appearance

Like most members of Team "Niles" , Nakht wore a large white suit and tie, with a blue dress shirt and green tie. He was mostly seen with an Egyptian mask pyramid with a single blue Egyptian eye in the center, and large stone gloves and shoes the same color as the pyramid.

Personality

Abilities and Powers

He was a user of Egyptian witchcraft and Anatel considers him to have the greatest Pyramid Power. Ironically, even though Pyramid Power can give infinite Furyoku, Nakht's Furyoku is very low. However, thanks to his powers, he can recover faster than any other shaman.

History

Past

Nakht comes from Giza, Egypt and was born on December 3, 1967. He was one of Anatel's childhood friends and before he left for the Shaman Fights, he was the president of a traveling agency.

Shaman Fights Second Round

When the group entered the arena to fight Team X-I, Nakht easily noted how Anatel was quite unhappy about their opponents and laughed a little. When Anatel presented his and Khafre's powers he demonstrated his powers. When Lyserg Diethel entered the fight Nakht was the first to be attacked by his Wire and had a hole was blown in his pyramid mask revealing his face. Later he watched Iron Maiden Jeanne being released from her Iron Maiden and how easily he was caught by her guardian ghost Shamash and was cut in half by its axe.

After Anatel was killed by Jeanne she offered Nakht and Khafre to leave, although it only enraged them and attacked her with Max Pyramid Power intending to turn her into a mummy. However, both of them were quickly caught in a pair of spiked interrogation chairs and tied down. Knowing how Anatel went through worse, both of them endured and insulted Jeanne's ideology, leaving her no choice but pass judgment and to kill them. While Khafre had his head cut off by a guillotine, Nakht is crushed by a Statue of Apega.

Anime/Manga Difference

In the 2001 anime, his guardian ghost was the sphinx.
