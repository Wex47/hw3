An oni ( 鬼 , oni ) is a type of spirit in the Shaman King series. They are typically spirits filled with negative emotions that eventually build up until they materialize and lash out.

Shaman can create oni-like beings called shikigami ( 式神 , shikigami ) as a method of combating oni.

According to Alumi Niumbirch , Ike-Oni 's power may surpass that of the current Shaman King , Asakura Hao .
