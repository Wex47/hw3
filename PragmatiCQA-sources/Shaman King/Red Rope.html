About

Red Rope is a giant cobra spirit. It has the ability to coil itself like a boa constrictor to absorb blunt force.

When used in combat Namari can create Red Rope's head over his arm to use as a claw-like weapon or guide its body around freely. Even though it is a spirit it still has poisonous fangs that can paralyze and kill whatever it bites.

Oversouls and Attacks

O.S. Red Rope

O.S. "Red Rope" (O.S. レッドロープ Reddo Rōpu )

  • Shaman: Namari
  • O.S. Type: Weapon Type
  • Spirit Ally: Red Rope
  • Medium used: Snakeskin

Description: Using the snake skin on his hand, Namari creates the head of Red Rope over his arm. He can extend the snake under sand for sneak attacks, and its fangs are strong enough to pierce armor-type Over Souls and can inject deadly venom into his opponent. The head can be changed into metal. After striking with the snake's head, Namari can whip the opponent with the snake's tail.

Attacks

  • Boa Mode ( ボアの様 , Boa no Sama ) : When Namari is about to receive a blunt force attack, he can make Red Rope coil up like a boa constrictor and absorb the damage of the attack.

Anime/Manga Difference

Because Namari was never introduced in the 2001 anime, neither was Red Rope.

In the 2021 anime, Red Rope's Over Soul form does not have the metallic, angular design like it does in the manga.
