Mesa Verdede ( メサ·ヴェルデデ , Mesa Verudede ) is an American national park in Colorado and the location of the hidden Patch Village.

About

Mesa Verdede is a popular tourist destination due to the abandoned ruins of a village built into the side of the mesa. The village once housed an advanced, native civilization that disappeared long before European colonists arrived. Hidden cameras are placed around the ruins so the Patch Tribe can monitor people outside.

A roped off path leads into a labyrinth of staircases and ruins which lead deep underground. The maze is protected by traps, as well as hidden doors and passages, and the true path can only be sensed by the chosen shamans .
