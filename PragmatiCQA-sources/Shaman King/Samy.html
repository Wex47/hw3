Toda Hisami ( 戸田 久美 , Toda Hisami ) , or simply Samy ( サミ , Sami ) , is a fictional character in the Shaman King manga and anime series. She is a member of Gandhara and a part of Team "Deva".

Appearance

Samy is a young girl with a slim frame who wears spectacles and has long brown hair. Her attire consists of a long orange dress with a flower print, which she wears over patterned green trousers along with a pair of black sandals.

Personality

Abilities and Powers

Spirit

Her guardian ghost is Asura, who appears to have originally been a Buddhist statue like Dainichi Nyorai. He is described as having an evil passion so strong that it difficult to form an Over Soul with him. herself and Mamy both share him as their guardian ghost. When she forms an Over Soul they wield the other seven Devas as weapons in combat.

History

Past

Samy originates from Fukuoka in Japan and was born on February 23, 1980. She was once an aspiring soprano singer, she teaches music at Gandhara's schools.

Shaman Fights Second Round

She is apart of Gandhara , specifically a member of Team "Deva" alongside Ozam and Mamy . Her team makes it to the second round and soundly defeats Team "Cowboys" and Nazca before withdrawing from the Tournament to go along with Yoh's plan to kill Hao.

Anime/Manga Difference

Like many of the Gandhara members, Samy does not appear in the 2001 anime series.

Trivia

  • Her name is a reference to Summit, INC.
  • Originally, Team Deva was made up of Mamy, Aeon Lee , and Sei-Kyow ; this was later changed and Samy and Ozam replaced Aeon and Sei-Kyow.
