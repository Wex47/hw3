Short Summary

While cutting through the cemetery, Oyamada Manta encounters Tao Ren , who asks him to deliver a message to Asakura Yoh : that Ren will take Amidamaru 's spirit. The next day, Yoh and Manta see Ren attack some men and slice apart a car. Yoh intervenes before Ren can kill one of the men.
