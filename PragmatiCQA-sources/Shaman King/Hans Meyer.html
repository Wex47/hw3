Hans Meyer ( ハンス・マイヤー , Hansu Maiyā ) is a fictional character in the Shaman King The Super Star manga series.

About

During World War II, Hans was a soldier with the Feldgendarmerie . He died at the age of 25 after three bullets pierced the metal gorget . After his death, his spirit began possessing his old motorcycle , and while borrowing it, Alumi Niumbirch had his spirit possess her body using Hyōi-Gattai in order to drive it safely.

Because camping was common to Hans during the war, Alumi also uses him to help her camp.

Before the war, Hans wanted to be a racer.
