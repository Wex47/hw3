Anubis ( アヌビス , Anubisu ) is a fictional character in the Shaman King series. He is one of the Four Kings of Hell, and the guardian of the Spirit of Rain .

About

When Saigan Sati and Team "Myooh" invaded Hell to steal the four remaining Great Elemental Spirits , Yainage faced off against Anubis in order to retrieve the Spirit of Rain .

Anime/Manga Difference

Anubis does not truly appear in the manga and both anime series.

Trivia

  • In Ancient Egyptian mythology, Anubis is the god of death and the underworld.
