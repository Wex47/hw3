Tona Papik Cadimahide ( トナ・パピク・カジマヒデ , Tona Papiku Kajimahide ) is a fictional character in the manga and anime series of Shaman King . He is a shaman from Akranes, Iceland and is a member of The Icemen .

Appearance

Cadimahide wears Icelandic robes that have the chest open and fur boots. His street clothes are a t-shirt and jeans.

Personality

Cadimahide was known as the tough guy and was proud of his Icelandic heritage. He has lightened up after his fight against Yoh's team.

Abilities

Tona was taught runic magic and the techniques of the Norse chief god Odin by his grandmother, who herself was an expert.

Spirit

His guardian ghost Deht , is a Viking and the helmet he uses as a medium is a family heirloom passed down through many generations.

History

Past

Tona is from Akranes, Iceland and was born on April 26, 1971. He used to help his father who is a fisherman until his grandmother initiated him in the Norse Mythology and its techniques.

Shaman Fight in Tokyo

Not much is known about his involvement of the first round of the Shaman Fight but he did win two matches and went to the second round of the fight.

Traveling through America

He boarded the plane head to the Patch village and was dumped somewhere in American. Not much is known about what he did or how he got to the Patch village, but he did beat the three-month time limit and entered the Patch village with a power increase from the Great Spirit.

Shaman Fight Second Round

He found out about the team stipulation and joined forces with Pino Graham and Zria Gagarik to form the Team "Icemen". They have paired against Team "Funbari Onsen" . The Icemen used combos after combos but were beaten back by gigantic Over Souls . As Yoh asked the Icemen to fight with their full power which they did. The Icemen were finally defeated by the Spirit of Sword and was knocked out of the fight.

Assault on the Beach

When the Icemen were defeated, Cadimahide was seen in street clothes and socializing with everyone. He stayed to watch the rest of the fights. When "Big Guy" Bill Burton attacked, Horohoro helped the Icemen from killed and have their souls fed to the Spirit of Fire . Although Horohoro intervened, the Icemen was still beaten up and knocked out.
