Anna Y. Kyouyama ( アンナ・Y・キョーヤマ , Anna Y Kyōyama ) is a fictional character in the Shaman King manga series. She is an Itako trained by Asakura Kino and the predecessor of Kyōyama Anna .

History

Past

She was raised and trained by Asakura Kino in Osorezan to become an Itako. She eventually moved to Los Angeles, where she started a successful business.

Travel through America

When Yoh and his friends traveled to America for the Shaman Fights , Anna I began investigating and sent all the information she obtained to the Asakura Family . This would eventually help her junior Kyōyama Anna find the Patch Village.

Funbari no Uta

Some time after the Shaman Fights ended, she took in the daughter of Silva , Alumi Niumbirch , and trained her to be an Itako.

Anime/Manga Difference

She is never mentioned in the 2001 anime series.

Trivia

  • She bears the following similarities to the "Kyoyama Anna" from from Hiroyuki Takei 's previous series Butsu Zone .
