Super Marco LLC is a former car dealership owned by Marco Lasso .

About

The dealership was made with financing from Luchist Lasso but owned and run by Marco Lasso . It only dealt with supercar, including special cars that could not be bought anywhere else. Thanks to Marco´s management it increased it sales and it reached a point where it was about to produce its very own car with its own signs. It was then Asakura Hao appeared and somehow caused the lose of the dealership.
