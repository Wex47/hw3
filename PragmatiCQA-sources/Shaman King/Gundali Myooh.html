Gundali Myooh ( 軍荼利明王 , Gundari Myō-ō ) , or simply Gundali ( グンダリ , Gundari , Kundali in the English version of the manga series) , is one of nine Buddhistic god spirits used by the Gandhara and is the Guardian Ghost of Yainage .

About

According to Yainage , Gundali has the Palms of Salvation ( 救いの掌 , Sukui no Te ) , which will nullify any Furyoku-based attack or defense.

Oversouls and Attacks

O.S.

O.S

  • Shaman: Yainage
  • O.S. Type: Spirit Type
  • Spirit Ally: Gundali
  • Medium used: Vajra

Description: A gigantic Kami type spirit, the Over Soul follows the movements of the small-medium wielded by Yainage. If Yainage pushes his palm forwards so will Gundali. Its main abilities consist of its palm strikes that nullify any kind of Furyoku , be it offensive or defensive.

Attacks

  • Six Elbow Barrage ( 六臂ダム , Roppī Damu ) : Gundali thrusts all six of its palms into an opponent. The full capacity of this attack was never seen as Tao Ren used Shamanic Oracle to escape the strike.
