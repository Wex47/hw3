Short Summary

Tokageroh possesses Umemiya Ryunosuke so he can get revenge on Amidamaru who killed him 600 years ago. When Ryu's Gang notice the change and refuse to steal Harusame for him, Tokageroh stabs Muscle Punch and threatens the others, so they comply. Tokageroh then takes Oyamada Manta as a hostage.
