Anahol Pokki ( アナホル・ポッキ , Anahoru Pokki ) is a fictional character in the Shaman King manga and anime series. He is the younger twin brother of Anatel Pokki .

Appearance

Anahol strongly resembles his late twin brother, Anatel Pokki , to the point where both Iron Maiden Jeanne and Lyserg Diethel , at first sight, mistook him for Anatel, although he proclaims that his twin brother's nose was 3.cm longer. Anahol wore a golden colored suit that had hieroglyphic patterns texture adorned all over the suit, a magenta dress shirt with a white tie and brown formal shoes.

Personality

Anahol is quite similar to his twin brother in terms of vanity. It is apparent he has great respect for his late brother, as he describes Anatel's nose as "beautiful". while he seeks revenge against Team "X-I" , particularly Jeanne, as she were responsible for killing his twin brother.

He is confident in his abilities and acts calmly when he have the upper hand, but Anahol is easily frightened, such as when facing people stronger than him or when he was driving with Luchist Lasso who was speeding both times.

History

Shaman Fights Second Round

Anahol left Patch Stadium after watching Team "Hana-Gumi kill Team "Magic Princess" . After the SDI X-Laser struck the island, Anahol appeared on the bridge in front of the Lincoln-X and used his whip to pierce through the windshield and Marco Lasso 's chest. Iron Maiden Jeanne mistook Anahol for his twin brother, Anatel , and Anahol proclaimed his desire for revenge for his twin brother. He pursued the car when Lyserg Diethel had Zelel fly it away. His sphinx Over Soul eventually caught up to and destroyed the car, killing both Lyserg and Jeanne. Tamamura Tamao and Oyamada Manta , however, survived.

When Anahol moved to destroy the X-Laws ' corpses as part of his deal with Asakura Hao , Tamao chose to stay and protect their bodies because she believed that either Johann Faust VIII or Saigan Sati would be able to revive them. Anahol dismissed her but when she refused to back down, he lifted her off the ground and kicked Manta. Before Anahol's sphinx could fire at Tamao, Ponchi kicked Anahol in the face, and Marco—who had been revived before Jeanne's death—used Michael destroyed the sphinx. Anahol restored his Over Soul and attacked Marco, who continued destroying the sphinx until he held Anahol at gunpoint. He demanded that Anahol take them to Sati Saigan , but Hao arrived and informed them that she was dead as well. While Marco was distracted, Anahol stole his gun, punched him to the ground, and held him down at gunpoint.

Invading the Plants

After Team "Funbari Onsen" , Team "The Ren" , Team "X-I" and Team "Hoshi-Gumi" was transported to the Mū by the Patch Tribe , Kyōyama Anna holds Anahol as a hostage and forces him to use his Over Soul as a submarine for her and her friends to travel to the Continent of Mu with four of the renaming the Five Grand Elemental Spirits .

Anime/Manga Difference

Anahol does not appear in the 2001 anime series, although his twin brother, Anatel, does.
