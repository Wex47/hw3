About

Blue Net is the ghost of a small spider, that would eventually evolve into a Seirei Class and become the guardian ghost of Bron . It can create an indestructible webbing, that can also be used as a projectile weapon.

Oversouls and Attacks

O.S. Blue Net

O.S. Blue Net

  • Shaman: Bron
  • O.S. Type: Weapon Type
  • Spirit Ally: Blue Net
  • Medium used: Spider hair

Description: By placing his spider ghost, Blue Net, inside the spider-hair bracelet on his right arm, Bron is able to materialize it in its original form on his arm. Naturally, it is oversized, but because of that, it allows Bron to use its web as a projectile weapon, and create invisible webs that allow him not only to move freely in the air but also to ensnare his opponents. The nets created by this Over Soul cannot be destroyed and cannot disappear unless the Over Soul itself disappears.

Bron can also use this Over Soul to create numerous small spiders, that can easily reconfigure the positioning of his nets. It would seem that the individual legs move according to the movement of his own fingers tips.

Attacks

O.S. Blue Net Big Over Soul

O.S. Blue Net Big Over Soul

  • Shaman: Bron
  • O.S. Type: Full Body Type
  • Spirit Ally: Blue Net
  • Medium used: Spider hair

Description: After Iron Maiden Jeanne cut off both of Bron's arms, and confined his knees and head in torture devices, Bron changed his mediums to the two remaining bracelets, in order to gain a full spider's body, with his main body as the head of the spider's body. With this Over Soul, Bron can shoot thousands of projectile webs.

However, as Bron was killed shortly after forming this Over Soul, the full extent of its abilities was never seen.
