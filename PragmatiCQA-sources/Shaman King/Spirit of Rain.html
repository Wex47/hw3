The Spirit of Rain ( スピリット・オブ・レイン , Supiritto Obu Rein ) is one of the Five Grand Elemental Spirits that was created by the Great Spirit itself. Usui Horokeu inherits the spirit due to his connection with water and ice.

About

The Spirit of Rain is one of five powerful elemental spirits known as the Grand Elemental Spirits of the Patch Tribe . They are derived from the Great Spirit itself, and like it, constitute a collective of souls. They can be controlled by their respective Elemental Warrior, or a shaman of extraordinary power. Their power is unmatched by any other spirit, except for the Great Spirit itself. Like the Spirit of Fire, the Spirit of Rain has the ability to become stronger by consuming souls.

After Hao stole the Spirit of Fire, the Patch Tribe hid the other Grand Elemental Spirits to prevent them from being stolen as well. Within the depths of hell, the Spirit of Rain was guarded by Anubis , one of the four Kings of Hell. Before the finals of the Shaman Fight 2000 in Tokyo, Sati Saigan and Team "Myooh" went to Hell to retrieve the Five Elemental Spirits and deliver them to the Five Elemental Warriors. Yainage succeeded in defeating Anubis and freeing the Spirit of Rain for Usui Horokeu .

After the final fight, Horokeu kept the Spirit of Rain and, years later, lent the spirit to Opacho so she could combat the starvation and thirst of the people and land in Africa.

Anime/Manga Difference

The Spirit of Rain does not appear in the 2001 anime series, although the Spirit of Fire appears to be a single, powerful entity under the Patch's control rather than a member of this group, though it was mentioned that there were four other spirits worshiped by the Patch other than the Spirit of Fire.
