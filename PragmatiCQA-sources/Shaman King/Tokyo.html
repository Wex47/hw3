Tokyo ( トーキョー , Tōkyō ) is a fictional island in the Shaman King manga and anime series. This uninhabited island off the coast of Japan is the location of the main tournament for the Shaman Fight , hence the full name "Shaman Fight in Tokyo 2000".

About

The island is located 23 nautical miles south-southeast of the city of Tokyo. The island has various abandoned buildings, including a factory, ports, and houses. The Patch Tribe remodeled several buildings in order to host the competitors and spectators, as well as built a large stadium for the main fights themselves. Due to being in a volcanic region, the island has natural hot springs.

Patch Stadium is a multisided arena with an open roof. At the center is a circular, raised fighting ring. Four totem poles generate a powerful Over Soul that protects the spectators from the competitors' attacks and prevents outsiders from interfering in the fights. This force field allows Radim and the competitors to pass through and is capable of successfully containing the force of a massive explosion; however, Lyserg Diethel 's O.S. "Mastema Dolkeem" is strong enough to break through the force field during a match.

The restaurant Corn King ( 王様のトウモロコシ , Ōsama no Tōmorokoshi ) was built in an abandoned schoolhouse and sells food to the competitors.

The Patch's Café Bean ( パッチズ カフェ豆 , Patchizu Cafe Mame ) is a small coffee shop run by Thalim .

There is an old Japanese Navy port with a dry dock, where the Patch Marine is constructed.
