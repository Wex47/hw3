About

Djinn is a genie that has been passed down through generations of Mohamed's family through a magical lamp, becoming a family heirloom. Because of this, he will follow and protect his master no matter what, as well as listening to his every wish.

Oversoul and Attacks

O.S. Beam

O.S. Beam

  • Shaman: Mohamed Tabarsi
  • O.S. Type: Spirit Type
  • Spirit Ally: Djinn
  • Medium used: Glass Jewel

Description: By using the glass jewel medium on the palm of his right hand, Mohamed is able to Over Soul Djinn into it and fire powerful energy beams from it.

Attacks

  • Beam ( ビーム , Bīmu , Mystic Beam in the English version of the 2001 anime series) : Mohamed fires a powerful energy blast from the glass jewel medium in his palm.

O.S. Djinn

O.S. Djinn

  • Shaman: Mohamed Tabarsi
  • O.S. Type: Giant O.S.
  • Spirit Ally: Djinn
  • Medium used: Glass Jewel

Description: By using the glass jewel medium on the palm of his right hand, Mohamed can materialize Djinn in a Giant Over Soul form. According to Mohamed himself, he can only use it once per day as per his "wish".

Anime/Manga Difference

In the 2001 anime series, neither Djinn himself or his Giant O.S. form appeared.
