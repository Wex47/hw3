Bow and arrow

Archery is a practice by which its user propels arrows through the use of a bow. It is used for both hunting and combat, though it is also known to be practiced as a recreational activity. Notable archers shown to be extremely proficient; include Oliver Queen , Yao Fei Gulong , Shado , Nyssa al Ghul and Malcolm Merlyn , although when John Diggle checked the A.R.G.U.S. database, they apparently listed some twenty other individuals in the bracket.
