Danielle Nicole Panabaker (born September 19, 1987) is an American actress. She portrays Caitlin Snow / Killer Frost in Arrow , The Flash , DC's Legends of Tomorrow , and Supergirl . Panabaker also portrayed Hannibal Bates/Everyman (as Caitlin Snow of Earth-1) and Killer Frost of Earth-2 in the first to second seasons respectively.
