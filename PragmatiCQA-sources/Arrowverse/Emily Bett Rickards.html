Emily Bett Rickards (born July 24, 1991) is a Canadian actress. She has portrayed Felicity Smoak/Overwatch in Arrow , The Flash , DC's Legends of Tomorrow , and Supergirl . She has also portrayed Felicity Smoak's Earth-X doppelgänger in Crisis on Earth-X .

Trivia

  • Initially, Felicity Smoak was only set to appear in the episode " Lone Gunmen "; however, after Rickards' on-screen chemistry with Stephen Amell became apparent, the character was given roles in subsequent episodes, before finally being promoted to a full-time regular in the second season.
  • Rickards is the only Arrow cast member to have never sat out an episode in her entire run as a series regular.
  • She's also the second arrowverse character to share a birthday with her character only in a different year the first is Candice Patton also known as Iris West Allen
