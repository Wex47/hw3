Dominic Haakon Myrtved Purcell (born 17 February 1970) is an English-born Australian actor of Irish and Norwegian descent. He has portrayed Mick Rory/Heat Wave/Chronos in The Flash , DC's Legends of Tomorrow , Arrow , and Supergirl . He also portrayed Charlie disguised as Mick and Hank Heywood 's spirit through Mick's body in DC's Legends of Tomorrow and Mick Rory (Earth-74) in Crisis on Infinite Earths .
