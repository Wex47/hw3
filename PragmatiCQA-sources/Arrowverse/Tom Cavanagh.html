Thomas Patrick "Tom" Cavanagh (born October 26, 1963) is a Canadian actor and director. He portrayed Eobard Thawne/Reverse-Flash and various iterations of Harrison Wells in The Flash , Supergirl , Arrow , DC's Legends of Tomorrow , and Batwoman .

He directed " The Once and Future Flash ", " Elongated Journey Into Night " and " What's Past is Prologue ", whose final scene was also used as the final scene in " Bunker Hill " and " Unmasked " and the opening of " Elseworlds, Part 1 ". He also directed " Last Sons of Krypton ", the first season finale of Superman & Lois .

Trivia

  • In comparison to the other series regulars on The Flash and the Arrowverse , Cavanagh has portrayed the most alternate versions of a character.
    • Cavanagh has played multiple versions of Dr. Harrison Wells: Harrison Wells of Earth-1 in Seasons 1 and 7, Eobard Thawne/Reverse-Flash (disguised as Harrison Wells) since Season 1 , Harrison "Harry" Wells of Earth-2 since Season 2 , Harrison "H.R." Wells of Earth-19 in Season 3 , every member of the Council of Wells and the Council of Harrisons in Season 4 , Harrison Sherloque Wells of Earth-221 since Season 5 and Harrison Nash Wells/Pariah from an unknown earth in Season 6 .
    • Cavanagh also played Hannibal Bates/Everyman (disguised as Eobard Thawne) in " The Trap ", three other versions of Wells in " The New Rogues ", Grodd (while controlling Earth-2 Wells and impersonating Eobard Thawne respectively) in " Attack on Gorilla City " and " Grodd Friended Me ", and Iris West (disguised as Earth-19 Wells) in " Infantino Street " and " Finish Line ".
  • In the Arrowverse , Tom Cavanagh, Matt Letscher , and Victor Garber portray Professor Eobard Thawne/Reverse-Flash; Cavanagh portrays the character disguised as Harrison Wells throughout The Flash , Letscher played the character's original likeness on 5 episodes of The Flash and in DC's Legends of Tomorrow Season 2 , and Garber briefly plays him disguised as Martin Stein in " The Chicago Way ". The three actors previously have played roles on Eli Stone ; Cavanagh portrayed the title character's father, Letscher played Eli's brother and Garber played his boss.
  • Tom Cavanagh is tied with David Harewood for having directed the most episodes of their respective shows, The Flash , and Supergirl , with a total of three: " The Once and Future Flash "; " Elongated Journey Into Night "; and the 100th episode, " What's Past is Prologue ".
  • Since Season 3 he has been absent in every of the first 2 episodes of every season (" Flashpoint ", " Paradox ", " The Flash Reborn ", " Mixed Signals ", " Nora ", " Blocked ", " Into the Void " and " A Flash of the Lightning ").
    • However, this trend was broken in Season 7 with " All's Wells That Ends Wells ".
    • He does appear in archive footage as Eobard Thawne in " Flashpoint ", " Nora " and " Blocked " and as Harry Wells in " The Flash Reborn ".
    • His only absences that aren't a part of the first 2 episodes of a season are " True Colors "; and, with the exception of " Timeless ", " Heart of the Matter, Part 2 ", and archive audio and footage in " Fear Me " and " Good-Bye Vibrations ", every episode after with " Mother ".
  • Cavanagh reprised his role of Eobard as Wells in the show Robot Chicken , voicing his character in the episode, "Ants on a Hamburger".
  • Cavanagh's departure from The Flash was his decision, as he felt his role in the show had run its course.
  • Cavanagh and Kimberly Williams-Paisley also starred in the Darrow & Darrow series.
