Susanna Thompson (born January 27, 1958) is an American actress. She has portrayed Moira Queen and Moira Queen (Earth-2) in Arrow . She also has voiced Moira Queen in Arrow and the Wellenreiter artificial intelligence in DC's Legends of Tomorrow .
