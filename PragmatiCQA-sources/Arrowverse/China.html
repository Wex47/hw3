Chinese government
China , officially the People's Republic of China ( PRC ), was a sovereign state located in East Asia. It was the world's most populous country. It was also the country that Chien Na Wei , Mei , the late Zhishan , the late Yao Fei Gulong and his family were from. Since 1997, China included the Special Administrative Region of Hong Kong , the island which was, for the preceding 155 years, a British colony.
