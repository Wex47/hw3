David Harewood (born 8 December 1965) is an English actor of Barbadian descent. He portrays J'onn J'onzz/Martian Manhunter in Supergirl , The Flash , Arrow , and DC's Legends of Tomorrow . He also portrayed Hank Henshaw/Cyborg Superman in Supergirl .
