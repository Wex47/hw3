John Scot Barrowman (born March 11, 1967) is a Scottish-American actor, singer, dancer, and writer. He portrayed Malcolm Merlyn/Dark Archer in Arrow and The Flash . He also portrayed Malcolm Merlyn in Arrow and Cutter Moran in The Flash .

Also, with his older sister, Carole E. Barrowman , they wrote Arrow 's third comic book tie-in series, Arrow: The Dark Archer .

Barrowman signed a deal that would make him a series regular across all of the Berlanti shows (like Katie Cassidy and Wentworth Miller ). He had the freedom to move across the fifth season of Arrow , the third season of The Flash and the second season of DC's Legends of Tomorrow .

In May 2017, Barrowman announced he was not returning to the Arrowverse for the 2017-18 season after his character was killed off in " Lian Yu ", despite the ambiguous nature of the event, and he stated that he was unhappy with the producers' decision to do so. However, since then, Barrowman appeared in " Elseworlds, Part 2 " as a special guest star. And in August 2019, Barrowman confirmed his character would be back for " Starling City ", the Season 8 premiere episode.
