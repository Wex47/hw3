Brandon James Routh (born October 9, 1979) is an American actor. He portrayed Ray Palmer/The Atom in Arrow , The Flash , and DC's Legends of Tomorrow , and voiced him in Vixen . He also portrayed Clark Kent/Superman of Earth-96 in the crossover event Crisis on Infinite Earths , reprising his role from the 2006 film Superman Returns .

Routh also portrayed Charlie and John Constantine as Ray Palmer, and Neron possessing the character.

Trivia

  • In 2006, Routh previously played Clark Kent/Superman in the film, Superman Returns .
    • Routh made a nod to his previous Superman role during " Invasion! " when he said Kara Danvers resembled his cousin.
    • In the episode " All Star Team Up ", upon Ray Palmer's arrival, Caitlin and Cisco had an exchange when they saw something flying in the air. First asking if it's a bird, then a plane, and Felicity replied that it's her boyfriend. This is a reference to the iconic Superman phrase.
    • Routh would later reprise his role as Superman in Crisis on Infinite Earths .
  • Routh is married to Courtney Ford .
