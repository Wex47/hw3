Matt Ryan (born April 11, 1981 as Matthew Darren Evans ) is a Welsh actor. He portrayed John Constantine in Constantine , Arrow , DC's Legends of Tomorrow , Batwoman , and The Flash . He also portrayed Konstentyn and Dr. Gwyn Davies in DC's Legends of Tomorrow .
