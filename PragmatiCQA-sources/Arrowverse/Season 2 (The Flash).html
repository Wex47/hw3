Synopsis

Following the defeat of Barry Allen's arch-nemesis Eobard Thawne (aka Reverse Flash), Team Flash quickly turned their attention to the singularity swirling high above Central City , which fans last saw consuming everything in its path. Armed with the heart of a hero and the ability to move at super speeds, Barry charged into the eye of the singularity, but will he actually be able to save his city from impending doom?

Trivia

  • This season introduced villain turned anti-hero, Black Siren who is Laurel Lance's Earth-2 counterpart. She would go on to be a special guest character in the fifth season of Arrow ​ and a series regular in the sixth season and seventh season in addition to making a special appearance in the eighth season .
  • This season introduces the multiverse which would become a key part in future seasons of The Flash and the Arrowverse itself.
  • This is the only season of The Flash to not feature Danielle Nicolet ( Cecile Horton ) .
  • Season 2 marked the first to feature new regular Keiynan Lonsdale whose credited from " Running to Stand Still " onwards.
  • Gabrielle Stanton was originally going to be the co-showrunner for Season 2, after a few episodes and was replaced by Todd and Aaron Helbing .

Home media release

A boxset of the second season, titled The Flash: The Complete Second Season , was released on DVD and Blu-ray on September 6, 2016. It contains additional features, deleted scenes, a gag reel, audio commentaries, and the panels from the San Diego Comic-Con International and PaleyFest 2015.
