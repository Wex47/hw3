" Awakenings " is the eighth chapter of the comic book series Arrow: Season 2.5 , the eighth chapter in the Blood arc, and contains the sixth chapter in the Suicide Squad: Crisis in Kahndaq arc. It was released digitally on December 8, 2014.

Synopsis

The Canary is back! But is it really her this time? And what the heck is going on with Oliver ? All will be revealed!
