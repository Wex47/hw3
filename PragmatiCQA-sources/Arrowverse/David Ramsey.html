David Paul Ramsey (born November 17, 1971) is an American actor. He portrayed John Diggle/Spartan/Green Arrow in Arrow , The Flash , DC's Legends of Tomorrow , Supergirl , Batwoman , and Superman & Lois . He also portrayed Clifford DeVoe as John Diggle in The Flash and Bass Reeves in DC's Legends of Tomorrow .

Trivia

  • Ramsey is among the actors who made their directorial debut within their respective show, including Melissa Benoist , Tom Cavanagh , David Harewood , Caity Lotz , and Danielle Panabaker .
    • Ramsey is also the first actor to direct an episode from a separate show followed by Tom Cavanaugh.
  • Ramsey is one of three actors to have been in all six Arrowverse TV series that were made for the Arrowverse (except Black Lightning ), alongside Tyler Hoechlin and Elizabeth Tulloch .
