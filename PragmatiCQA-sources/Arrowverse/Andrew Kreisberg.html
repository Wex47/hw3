Termination

Andrew Kreisberg was suspended from the WB and all of his shows on November 10, 2017, after nineteen reports of sexual misconduct surfaced. On November 29, Kreisberg's employment with Warner Bros. TV was officially terminated with Greg Berlanti taking over his responsibilities on both Supergirl and The Flash.
