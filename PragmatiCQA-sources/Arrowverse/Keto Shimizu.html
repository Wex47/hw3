Keto Shimizu (born December 23, 1984) is an American writer and producer. She was a story editor in Arrow and Vixen ; a writer in Arrow , The Flash , and DC's Legends of Tomorrow , a producer in Arrow and DC's Legends of Tomorrow , and a script writer in Arrow: Season 2.5 .
