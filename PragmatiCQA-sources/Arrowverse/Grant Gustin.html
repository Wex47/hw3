Thomas Grant Gustin (born January 14, 1990), known professionally as Grant Gustin , is an American actor and singer. He portrays Barry Allen/The Flash in Arrow , The Flash , Vixen , Supergirl , DC's Legends of Tomorrow , and Batwoman .

Trivia

  • Gustin's favorite villains are Captain Cold ( Wentworth Miller ), Weather Wizard ( Liam McIntyre ) and The Trickster ( Mark Hamill ).
  • One of Gustin's favorite crossovers was the Supergirl crossover, " Worlds Finest " and he really enjoyed working in crossovers with Brandon Routh .
  • Grant attended Elon University alongside Supergirl actor Chris Wood , where they were both musical theater majors.
  • Grant has .
  • Before the arrival of Keiynan Lonsdale , Grant was the youngest member of the main cast of The Flash . He is now the youngest again after Lonsdale was demoted to a special guest in 2017.
  • On The Flash ​, Gustin also portrayed Hannibal Bates , as Barry Allen/The Flash, in the first season and Barry Allen of Earth-2 in the second season .
  • Grant and Nolan Funk previously worked together of FOX's Glee , in the show they were part of the same fictional singing chorus Acappella: The Warblers. The group was a bitter rival with The New Directions, another choir, that included characters played by Melissa Benoist , Darren Criss , and Blake Jenner .
  • The participation of Grant and Melissa Benoist in Glee was the major inspiration for the creation of the musical episode " Duet "; The episode also has as a guest star Darren Criss , whose character in Glee was rival to the Grant character for most of the series.
  • Grant is the most recurring male actor as he appeared in 15 episodes of Arrow ​, 4 episodes of Supergirl ​, 4 episodes of DC's Legends of Tomorrow ​, and 1 episode of Batwoman .
  • Prior to playing the role of Barry Allen , Grant Gustin auditioned for the role of Roy Harper on Arrow . The role would later on be given to Colton Haynes .
