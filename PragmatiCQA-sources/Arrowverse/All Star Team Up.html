Trivia

  • This episode takes place between " The Offer " and " Suicidal Tendencies ".
  • The "All Star" phrase in the title is a reference to various DC Comics team-up books, most notably All-Star Comics , All Star-Squadron , All-Star Superman , and All-Star Western .
  • Cisco 's quote, "Bees, why did it have to be bees?" is a reference to Indiana Jones ' quote, "Snakes. Why did it have to be snakes?" from Raiders of the Lost Ark .
  • Upon Ray Palmer 's arrival, Caitlin and Cisco have an exchange when they see him flying in the air. They first asking if it's a bird, and then a plane, and Felicity replies that it's her boyfriend. This is a reference to the iconic Superman phrase, referencing Brandon Routh 's former title role as Superman in the 2006 film adaptation Superman Returns .
  • The Atom saying, "We have the technology!" is a reference to The Six Million Dollar Man .
  • During their conversation inside S.T.A.R. Labs , Caitlin remarks to Ray (after telling him that he's lucky he didn't break his neck), "What is it with billionaires being superheroes?", referencing Oliver Queen .
  • The hostile usage of bees against human targets by Brie Larvan has been conceptually exploited before in the first season of Smallville during the episode " Drone ", which coincidentally also carried the episode number 1x18.
  • After Barry reminds everyone they're up against a meta-human killer who can control a swarm of bees, Ray says, "Cool. I mean awful". Barry and Felicity uttered those exact words during the " Flash vs. Arrow " crossover.
