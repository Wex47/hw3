Biography

Original multiverse

Early life

At some point, Bethany Snow became an anchorwoman for Channel 52 .

Job at Channel 52

This section is a stub. You can help expand this section by .

Bethany Snow was seen anchoring the news on Channel 52, announcing Oliver Queen 's return to Starling City .

Bethany on the arrest of China White , praising the SCPD for the efforts.

Bethany also reported the kidnapping of Quentin and Laurel Lance by the Dollmaker .

Later, Bethany reported on the Glades shooting on the Cash for Guns Event by the crime lord known as the Mayor .

During the Moira Queen trial, the Count attempted to hold the city hostage for vertigo, leading to Channel 52 reporting on both events. Bethany later reported on the campaigns of both Sebastian Blood and Moira Queen .

Bethany reported other stories that year, including the destruction of Queen Consolidated Applied Science Division. Bethany also reported the events of the Siege .

Bethany reported on the attack on the Glades by Danny Brickwell and the efforts of resistance by Arsenal and the Black Canary.

When Starling City turned against the Arrow, Bethany reported on developments.

During the events of the Outbreak , Bethany reported on the events.

Flashpoint

Bethany reported the fight between the Flash and Rival in Central City .

Post-Flashpoint

During Oliver Queen's trial, Bethany interviewed Quentin Lance.

Bethany reported on the attack on Star City by Grant Wilson and the Deathstroke gang .

Anti-Monitor Crisis

During the Anti-Monitor Crisis , Bethany as well as everyone in the multiverse except for the seven Paragons , was killed in an antimatter wave by the Anti-Monitor on December 10, 2019, only to be restored a month later, after the Paragons and the Spectre created a new universe .

New multiverse

Bethany reported on Oliver's son William being taken from his home in Central City. After a female Green Arrow saved him from John Byrne , who recently escaped from Iron Heights Prison and kidnapped William as revenge on The Hood for bringing him to justice eight years ago, Team Arrow watched the rescue footage that was shot by a news crew member in Bethany's latest report. She concludes by speculating that Oliver's legacy inspired a new generation of heroes to carry on his mission.

Abilities

  • Journalism: As the anchorwoman for Channel 52 , Bethany reported on numerous stories.

Behind the scenes

  • In the DC comics, Bethany Snow is a journalist who becomes a member of the Church of Blood. On behalf of Brother Blood , she tries to discredit the Teen Titans on TV.
  • After DC's New 52 reboot, Bethany appears at the end of every DC comics as part of the Channel 52 news group to report the events that are happening in the DC Universe.
  • Bethany has appeared in every season of Arrow except Season 1 .
  • " Docket No. 11-19-41-73 " remains Bethany's only onscreen appearance.
