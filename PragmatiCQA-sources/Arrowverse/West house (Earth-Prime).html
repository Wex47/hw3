History

When Iris West-Allen took a break from college, she returned to the house but had a hard time living with Joe because she was an adult with her own lifestyle that was not totally complimentary with his.

At some point in time, Joe West , Cecile Horton and Jenna West moved into this house.

The house was used by Joe, Frost and Kamilla Hwang , as a hologram of Iris West-Allen , to set a trap for Kimiyo Hoshi . However, she attacked Joe and Frost before getting the order from Joseph Carver to cancel the assassination.

Wally West came to the house to visit and impressed everyone with his ability to create holograms.

Since he was evicted by " Iris ", the Mirrorverse version of his wife, Barry came here to get his thoughts together. When Cecile arrived, she could not enter the house because Barry had blocked the door with so many boxes and crates as he worked on how to prove that his wife was replaced.

While working at home, Cecile became terrified when Psych emitted a wave of energy until Joe arrived and calmed her.

After the death of the Speed Force , a replica of the house that looked like it had been abandoned for years was created, Barry Allen traveled to this point of time to hide Iris, Bashir Malik and Alexa Rivera to protect them from the future reborn Speed Force. However the replica was changed after the Speed Force apologized for her actions, its interior now looking exactly like its Earth-Prime counterpart.

During a storm created by the Forces of Nature, Cecile was struck by a bolt of energy. The bolt was from the Psycho Pirate Mask bonding with Cecile.

Joe researched Kristen Kramer . Later, Cecile and Joe sat down and discussed her past.

After Kristen Kramer confirmed that she was a meta-human, she visited Joe in his house and told everything. She apologized and announced her wishes to take a leave of absence to reflect on her handling of meta-humans. Time later, Barry and Iris took their wedding vow renewal in that house officiated by Cisco Ramon .
