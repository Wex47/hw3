Synopsis

After defeating the demon Mallus by cuddling him to death with a giant stuffed animal named Beebo , the Legends are ready to ease off the gas. Sara and her team join Ava Sharpe and the Time Bureau to help clean up the last few remaining anachronisms . The job seems straightforward enough until Constantine arrives to inform them that, in solving one major problem, they have created another, much larger one. When the Legends let time crumble in order to release and defeat Mallus, the barrier between worlds softened. History is now infected with " Fugitives " – magical creatures from myths, fairy tales, and legends. Having been expelled throughout time by people like Constantine, these Fugitives are now returning to our world in droves and making a real mess of things. As the Time Bureau is distrustful of and ill-equipped to deal with magic, the Legends must team up with everyone's favorite demonologist to set history back on track.

Sara and Constantine are joined by compassionate inventor Ray Palmer , hotheaded ex-con Mick Rory , rebellious totem-bearer Zari , and heartbroken historian-turned-superhero Nate as they set out to save the world – and their legacy. It's the familiar fun of the Legends time-traveling across historical events and encountering famous figures with an added shot of magical craziness!

Trivia

  • Prior to the season premiere, The CW aired a to promote this season as well as the return of the character John Constantine that was titled Constantine: The Legend Continues .
  • Despite being credited in the "main cast" bill, Courtney Ford ( Nora Darhk/Fairy Godmother ) and Ramona Young ( Mona Wu / Wolfie ) were only credited for their respective episode appearances.
    • In addition, the former was also credited in the "special appearance by" bill in " The Virgin Gary ".
  • Maisie Richardson-Sellers ( Amaya Jiwe/Vixen ) originally left the cast at the end of the last season until " Dancing Queen " where she was credited in the "special appearance by" bill. She then rejoined the main cast in the following episode as Charlie .
  • Despite being credited in the "special appearance by" bill, Matt Ryan ( John Constantine ) appeared more frequently than Maisie Richardson-Sellers, Jes Macallan ( Ava Sharpe ) , Courtney Ford, Ramona Young, and Nick Zano ( Nate Heywood/Steel ) , all of whom were series regulars.
    • Adam Tsekhman ( Gary Green ) also appeared more frequently than Courtney Ford despite being a recurring actor.
  • Brandon Routh ( Ray Palmer/The Atom ) , Caity Lotz ( Sara Lance/White Canary ) , Tala Ashe ( Zari Tomaz ) , Amy Pemberton ( Gideon ) , Dominic Purcell ( Mick Rory/Heat Wave ) , and Matt Ryan were the only actors to appear in every episode this season.
  • This was the first season of DC's Legends of Tomorrow :
    • To feature new co-showrunner Keto Shimizu .
    • To not feature Stephen Amell ( Oliver Queen/Green Arrow ) , Arthur Darvill ( Rip Hunter ) , Franz Drameh ( Jefferson Jackson / Firestorm ) , Victor Garber ( Martin Stein / Firestorm ) , Isabella Hofmann ( Clarissa Stein ) , Graeme McComb (young Martin Stein) , Neal McDonough ( Damien Darhk ) , Wentworth Miller ( Leonard Snart/Captain Cold and Leo Snart ) , Emily Bett Rickards ( Felicity Smoak ) , Johnathon Schaech ( Jonah Hex ) , and Carlos Valdes ( Cisco Ramon ) in any capacity.
    • To not have an introduction at the beginning of any episode.
    • Since Season 1 to not to include a crossover episode.
    • To not include a Western-themed episode.
  • Season 4 marked the only season where Ramona Young is credited as a regular.
