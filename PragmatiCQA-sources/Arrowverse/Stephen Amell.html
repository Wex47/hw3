Stephen Adam Amell (born May 8, 1981) is a Canadian actor. He portrayed Oliver Queen in Arrow ​​, The Flash ​​, DC's Legends of Tomorrow ​​, Vixen ​​, Supergirl , and Batwoman . He also briefly portrayed Werner Zytle and Christopher Chance as they impersonated Oliver. He portrayed Oliver's Earth-16 counterpart in DC's Legends of Tomorrow and Supergirl , and his Earth-X counterpart in the Crisis on Earth-X ​​ crossover.

Trivia

  • Stephen's cousin, Robbie Amell , portrays Ronnie Raymond/Firestorm in the first season of The Flash ​​. The two appeared on-screen together later that season in the episode, " Rogue Air "​.
  • Stephen has been the subject of a number of articles on the internet highlighting his extreme physical exercise regimen, describing him as a performer who trains like a real-life superhero.
  • Stephen Amell was cast as Casey Jones , another vigilante character from the Teenage Mutant Ninja Turtles franchise in Teenage Mutant Ninja Turtles: Out of the Shadows , the second film in the rebooted live-action Ninja Turtles film series. Stephen Amell compared the roles, stating Casey Jones is "almost like the Oliver Queen people know from the older comic books".
  • He is one of only thirteen actors to have been in all five Arrowverse TV series that were made for the Arrowverse, along with Grant Gustin , Caity Lotz , Candice Patton , Dominic Purcell , LaMonica Garrett , Melissa Benoist , Tom Cavanagh , Tyler Hoechlin , Brandon Routh , Elizabeth Tulloch , Ruby Rose and David Ramsey .
  • Stephen is a huge fan of WWE. During the summer of 2015, he made a few appearances on the program and even competed in a Tag Team Match against former enemy Cody Runnels who later appears in season 5.
  • Stephen is married to Cassandra Jean Amell , who portrayed Nora Fries .
  • Stephen appeared in every single episode of Arrow, except for the Penultimate epsiode " Green Arrow & The Canaries " as that was a backdoor pilot for a potential sequel show, Green Arrow and the Canaries .
