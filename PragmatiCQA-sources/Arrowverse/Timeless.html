Synopsis

After a devastating betrayal, Barry turns to Timeless Wells for help. Meanwhile, Iris leads Team Citizen down a dangerous road in search of answers, and Cisco confides his biggest fear to Kamilla .

Plot

Seventeen days ago, as the Speed Force was revived, Timeless Wells removed his glasses when he saw something unusual; the Flash and Wells himself had just teleported into S.T.A.R. Labs and had gone to the roof to see the birth of the other Forces of Nature.

Yesterday, Alexa Rivera was killed by the Speed Force who tells Barry and Iris that it will hunt down the other Forces of Nature to protect itself and will kill the hosts as well if it must.

Presently, Cisco Ramon and Kamilla Hwang have a breakfast date at Jitters where she tells him that she has an opportunity in San Francisco in photography; on that subject, Cisco gives Kamilla a camera that can identify the Forces of Nature so that she will not be surprised if she encounters one.

Barry has a meeting with Team Flash and has decided that he will go back in time and stop the birth of the Forces in order to save the lives of the hosts. Iris West-Allen disagrees with this tactic, thinking that Barry should talk to the Speed Force and convince it to stand down; Barry is adamant, causing Iris to have a meeting with Team Citizen to use Kamilla's camera to find the Speed Force.

Chester P. Runk and Cisco theorize that time travel will not work correctly in this case, so Barry goes to Star City , 2000, and asks for help from Timeless Wells since he is an expert. As Timeless, Chester, and Cisco work on the Tachyon prototype that could be used to destroy the Forces without destroying Barry's powers, Deon Owens appears. Barry explains to Deon his plan; Deon becomes irate and declares himself a god because he and the Still Force are one. Deon then attempts to destroy the prototype before disappearing but Chester saves it. Cisco understands Deon's desire to keep his powers, but ultimately, Cisco decides that he will stand with his best friend.

Team Citizen ultimately goes to the Allen house , but, instead of finding the Speed Force, they find Psych . Iris tries to talk civilly with Psych about the threat of the Speed Force, but he uses the Sage Force against Kamilla and Allegra Garcia for fun. When he uses his power against Iris, Psych is not as successful as she is able to resist him and focuses on their conversation.

Timeless and the Flash go back in time and see the birth of the Forces. Barry begins using the prototype to kill the Forces, but the beauty of watching their birth overwhelms him. Barry destroys the prototype and returns to 2020.

That moment in time when Barry almost killed the Forces weakened Psych's hold on Team Citizen, allowing them to flee. That same moment made all of the hosts blink out of existence, but they returned.

Iris and Barry apologize to one another for not working together and not talking to each other before pursuing a plan. Suddenly, the building quakes. Caitlin Snow tells Iris and Barry that Alexa was animated for a second when the quake occurred. As a unified couple, Barry and Iris touch the body of Alexa, sending a spark which returns her to life.

After Timeless talks to Cisco and returns to 2000, Cisco and Kamilla decide that they are leaving together.

The Speed Force finds Deon and threatens him.
