Kirk M. Acevedo (born November 27, 1971) is an American actor. He has portrayed Ricardo Diaz/The Dragon in Arrow .

Trivia

  • Acevedo is one of few actors who played both in DC Entertainment and Marvel.
    • He played Agent Tomas Calderon in the television show Marvel's Agents of S.H.I.E.L.D ., which is set in Marvel Cinematic Universe.
  • Acevedo portrayed Mitch Dolgen in the television show, The Walking Dead.
    • Acevedo, Juliana Harkavy , and Audrey Marie Anderson all previously worked together on The Walking Dead.
  • Acevedo’s chair on set was labeled "The Dragon", unlike all others, which had the actors’ names on them.
