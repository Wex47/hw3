Echo Kellum (born August 29, 1982) is an American actor and comedian. He has portrayed Curtis Holt/Mr. Terrific in Arrow and DC's Legends of Tomorrow . He has also portrayed Curtis Holt's Earth-2 doppelgänger in Arrow .
