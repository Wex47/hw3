Synopsis

After a violent shipwreck, billionaire playboy Oliver Queen was missing and presumed dead for five years before being discovered alive on a remote island in the Pacific. He returned home to Starling City , bent on righting the wrongs of his family and fighting injustice. To do this, he creates the persona of the Arrow and allies himself with former military man John Diggle , computer-science expert Felicity Smoak , street brawler Roy Harper and international assassin Sara Lance , while keeping his secret from those closest to him including his sister Thea , who has allied herself with his most deadly enemy . Rivalries and allegiances will shift as Oliver faces his most difficult challenge yet.

Trivia

  • This is the first season to not have a main character killed off. Although Colton Haynes who played Roy Harper left the show, which technically doesn't count as being killed off. But it does continue the pattern of losing a main cast per season.
  • This is the first season where said season's main antagonist dies.
  • With the flashbacks in " Suicidal Tendencies " being revolved around Deadshot , this season marks the first time flashbacks have centered entirely around a recurring or guest character.

Home media release

A boxset of the third season, titled Arrow: Complete Third Season , was released on DVD and Blu-ray on September 22, 2015. It contained additional features, deleted scenes, a gag reel and the Arrow panel from the San Diego Comic-Con International 2014.
