Keiynan Lonsdale (born 19 December 1991) is an Australian actor, dancer, and singer-songwriter. They portrayed Wally West in The Flash , DC's Legends of Tomorrow , and Supergirl .

After Season 3 of DC's Legends of Tomorrow and Season 4 of The Flash , it was announced they would not be a regular in either series in the next seasons. Lonsdale announced they would not leave the role altogether, but preferred to take a break from the character.
