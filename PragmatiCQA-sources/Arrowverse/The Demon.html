  • Talia al Ghul mentions having been captured after running afoul with "an old enemy" in Gotham . This is most likely a reference to either Batman or Batwoman . The latter is more likely since Batman has been missing from Gotham fot a few years at this point.
  • Its no surprise Oliver and Talia were able to resist the psychological torture Parker put them through; they have been through worse , with Oliver confirmed to have repeatedly been drugged and tortured by criminals and his mentors for over ten years.
