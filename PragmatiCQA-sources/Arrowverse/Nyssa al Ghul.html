Nyssa Raatko (Arabic: نسا راتكو ‎) (born May 10, 1985), better known as Nyssa al Ghul (Arabic: نسا أل غول) , is a former member of the League of Assassins and an ally of Team Arrow . Nyssa is also the daughter of the late " Ra's al Ghul " and the late Amina Raatko , the younger paternal half-sister of Talia al Ghul , and the younger maternal half-sister of Saracon. She is also the ex-lover of Sara Lance and the former mentor/close friend of the late Laurel Lance . Nyssa rescued Sara from Lian Yu and brought her to Nanda Parbat to be trained by the League. Nyssa was briefly " Ra's al Ghul " and the last person to have the title, making the personal choice to disband the League of Assassins and destroy the ring of Ra's al Ghul. Shortly after Sara Lance ’s resurrection, she destroyed the Lazarus Pit to prevent it from being used ever again.

Nyssa decided to leave with Thea Queen and Roy Harper to find and destroy Lazarus Pits that were discovered by Malcolm Merlyn .

Biography

Original multiverse

Early life

Nyssa Raatko was born on May 10, 1985 in China , to Ra's al Ghul and one of his concubines, Amina Raatko . Nyssa was born a sickly infant and her father took her away from her mother about as soon as she was born. Nyssa was raised in the League of Assassins at Nanda Parbat , becoming a proficient swordswoman by the age of eight. Among those she trained with was Athena .

Nyssa had an older half-sister, Talia al Ghul , and the two were implied to be close. However, at some point when Nyssa was a child, Talia left Nanda Parbat to forge her own path after realizing Ra's would never pass his mantle to her. Nyssa was subsequently left to endure her father's harsh nature alone and grew to despise Talia for her abandonment. After Talia left, Nyssa became next in line to succeed her father in the event the prophecy of someone surviving a battle against him didn't come true.

Nyssa was very mischievous in her youth, having the "panache of a thief". She would often steal extra atayef at meal times and stow the sweets away in her room, but Ra's always found her hiding spots. Nyssa would grow to learn that she couldn't hide anything from him. From a young age, Nyssa loathed her father and lived in constant fear of him. She harbored a burning desire to one day kill Ra's and take his place as the Demon's Head. Nevertheless, she was proud of her title as the Daughter of the Demon and a member of the League.

In 1993, Nyssa brought Malcolm Merlyn to her father to be trained as a League member. In their first meeting, Nyssa named Malcolm "Al Sa-her" ("The Magician") after he pulled a coin out of her ear as a magic trick.

On her father's behest, Nyssa traveled to Lian Yu in October 2009, a few months after the Amazo sank. There, she met Sara Lance dying of starvation, having seemingly given up on living. This event was unknowingly orchestrated by Sara herself when a future version of her told her father back in 1960 to send Nyssa to Lian Yu to find her younger self a year after the Queen's Gambit was sunk. She took Sara in and nursed her back to health and Sara decided to join the League of Assassins. However Ra's never saw Sara worthy enough for the League as Sara never fully renounced her past life, as part of the League's code, and still remembered who she was and where she came from, and Ra's even tried to make Sara leave with a demonstration of his power. However Sara simply laughed whereas others had quaked in fear and from this point on, Nyssa deeply fell in love with Sara. At some point, Sara begun to return the affection and they became lovers. Ra's however never approved of Nyssa's relationship with Sara as he saw Sara as weakness to his future heir, knowing that Sara would one day leave the League, either by choice or by death.

In 2013, following Malcolm Merlyn 's Undertaking , Sara left Nanda Parbat to check on her family but failed to return after several weeks. Nyssa eventually learned that she'd refused to return home. Following this, she set out to find Sara and use any means to find her beloved.

Looking for Sara

Nyssa arrived in Starling City to find Sara. A scan of her passport declared her an active A.R.G.U.S. threat. The security attempting to detain her were met with brutal force at her hands. Following this, Nyssa quickly left the airport.

Nyssa eventually found Sara, and kissed her. Nyssa revealed she hadn't decided which she would do to Sara, also indicating that she knew Oliver Queen was watching from afar. Sara asked Oliver to let her handle Nyssa. The two walked away and discussed their former relationship. Nyssa expressed how hurt she was that Sara had just left her without saying goodbye. Sara responded to her with warmth and feeling, apologizing. She begged Nyssa to release her from the League just as Ra's al Ghul had done previously with Malcolm Merlyn , but Nyssa brushed this off as a horrible one-time mistake. After sharing another close moment, Sara told Nyssa she wouldn't go back, and to do what she had to. Sara stood waiting as Nyssa drew her dagger from her thigh. A surprise to her, Nyssa wouldn't kill her, leaving.

Later on, a member of the League kidnapped Sara's mother, Dinah Lance , working with Nyssa. Oliver and Sara tried to stop them as they drove away, however Nyssa drove them back with a deadly stream of arrows. Nyssa called Sara, telling her that she had 24 hours to make her decision of rejoining the League or facing the consequences for deserting them. Nyssa had no choice in the matter as she also would face consequences for not attempting to bring Sara back properly.

It is revealed Nyssa tricked Sara into coming back to Starling by poisoning Laurel Lance with snake venom, and having witnessed the other League member kill himself earlier, Sara decided to take his poison vial and told Nyssa she would rejoin the League. Upon arriving with her father , Nyssa released Dinah. She was happy to see Sara back, only to be shocked to see Sara had taken the poison. This sent Nyssa into a despairing sadness and rage. When Sara's parents arrived she immediately and swiftly disarmed Quentin and was about to kill them before being blocked by The Arrow . The two engaged in archery and hand-to-hand combat where Nyssa disarmed and almost killed Oliver with her dagger. However Oliver managed to trap her in a chokehold. As this happened, Sara struggled towards them, begging Oliver to spare her former lover. Oliver hurried to Sara's side, saving her with the herbs taken from Lian Yu . Touched by Sara's would-be final act, Nyssa released her vows to the League. As the police arrived, Nyssa was nowhere to be found.

Sara later contacted her and agreed to join the League again if Nyssa would help save Starling City. Nyssa and a group of League members joined Oliver's team, including John Diggle , Felicity Smoak , and Roy Harper . They armed themselves with arrows containing the Mirakuru cure to combat Deathstroke's soldiers, and to cure the Mirakuru serum in their bodies. Oliver instructed Nyssa and her group that no one should be killed, She did not agree at first, telling him that the League did not take any prisoners, but eventually decided to follow his orders. They then confronted Slade in his office and beat all of his soldiers while he managed to escape. Nyssa and Sara managed to quickly take out Isabel Rochev who began to mock Oliver. Although technically acceding to Oliver's demand that there be no killings, Nyssa herself delivered the coup de grâce on a disarmed and neutralized Isabel Rochev, telling Oliver that "your reticence to do what is necessary is why your city burns".

The group then went to the tunnel to engage Slade 's Mirakuru-infused thug army. The group used their archery and close combat skills to beat the soldiers. Nyssa personally made several shots during the melee in the tunnel which saved Oliver and Roy. When Slade's plan had been derailed, Nyssa swore to Sara's father, Quentin Lance , to keep Sara safe. She then took a willing Sara away with her on a ship to rejoin the League.

After Sara returned to the League, she and Nyssa resumed their relationship. When Ra's heard of news of Malcolm being in Starling City, he ordered Sara to go investigate. The night before she left, Sara and Nyssa slept with one another. Nyssa offered to take Sara's place as she felt that something bad was going to happen to Sara. However, Sara passed it off as Nyssa worrying.

Not long after, Sara was killed by Malcolm through Thea Queen . Nyssa decided to find out what happened to Sara after there was no contact from her lover for some time.

Disbanding the League

Malcolm and Nyssa were sparring before Laurel and Thea arrived in Nanda Parbat to try and convince Malcolm to use the Lazarus Pit to resurrect Sara which Malcolm refused and Nyssa agreed to with his decision. Nyssa later explained to Laurel that using the pit has serious consequences and her father paid a dark price for using the pit and she didn't want to see Sara go through that pain. After Malcolm finally agreed to use the pit on Sara, Nyssa furiously barged in and told Laurel that all that was happening to Sara was her responsibility. Nyssa later destroyed the pit and was taken away by Malcolm.

After she was held prisoner, Nyssa refused to eat anything, only waiting for the correct time to strike against Malcolm and his men, all the while asking Talibah to assist her in gathering loyalists for her war against Malcolm. During her imprisonment, Nyssa was visited by Sara, whose soul had been restored. Stunned and overjoyed to see her beloved whole again, Nyssa embraced her. Sara promised to find a way to get her out of Nanda Parbat but Nyssa reminded Sara that her own actions led to her predicament. Nyssa declared that if Sara truly loved her, then she would leave and never look back to find a new life for herself untethered to her past. Sara tearfully agreed and the former lovers hugged one last time before she left.

Nyssa eventually escaped from Nanda Parbat with the help of an assassin Talibah, who slipped a key to her via a hollowed out pepper in her dinner. The pair searched for the Lotus in Kyushu, guarded by Tatsu Yamashiro . Tatsu and Nyssa found their skills to be too evenly matched for an outcome and Nyssa gave the option to keep fighting or give up and listen to the news she had on a situation concerning Oliver. Later that night, Nyssa showed up at Thea's hospital room offering Oliver the Lotus elixir, which counters the effects of the Lazarus Pit, on the condition he kills Malcolm.

After Laurel was tragically killed by Damien Darhk , Nyssa returned to Star City to pay her respects. However Nyssa grew concerned when Quentin asked if there was any way to bring her back i.e. the Lazarus Pit, but Nyssa told him she'd destroyed it and there is no other means to bring Laurel back. Concerned for Quentin's well-being Nyssa approached Oliver and asked him to speak to Quentin, to convince him he's in denial and needs to embrace the grief. Later Nyssa attended Laurel's funeral along with Diggle, Thea, Felicity, Quentin, Dinah and Oliver before leaving Star City yet again.

A matter of family

In May 2017, Nyssa was contacted by Oliver to help defeat Adrian Chase/Prometheus and rescue his family and friends. Malcolm was reluctant to work with her at first, but accepted it after some persuasion from Oliver. Malcolm revealed to that they would also be fighting against Chase's mentor, Talia al Ghul , Nyssa's paternal half-sister, much to her shock. After tracking Chase's plane, the trio flew to Lian Yu , where Chase and his allies were. During the flight, Nyssa briefly mentioned that Ra's al Ghul sent her to Lian Yu nine years ago in 2008, and she found Sara on the island.

On the island, Nyssa had intercepted Talia, who taunted her sister for her foolishness for giving up the mantle of Ra's al Ghul and stated she only left because it became clear that her father would never pass on the title to a woman including his daughter, but Nyssa blamed her for selfishly abandoning her to suffer at the hands of her father when she needed her most. Even though Talia expressed how Nyssa brought shame to their father by aligning with Oliver, Nyssa simply stated that she was fighting alongside her friends and that Ra's from the very beginning has always been ashamed of her. Nyssa then dueled and defeated her sister, but as Talia's acolytes lunged at her, Slade had arrived just in time to slaughter them. As Nyssa held her sword to her throat, Talia taunted her into killing her by stating Ra's al Ghul's approval, but as Nyssa couldn't bring herself to do it, she knocked her unconscious. She then assisted Team Arrow and Deathstroke in defeating Talia's acolytes with Adrian Chase and like everyone else, she was shaken when Chase stated Oliver's son was dead.

Chase rigged the island with explosives, with his death as the trigger, and he eventually shot himself in the head, committing suicide and destroying the island, with Nyssa still on it.

Nyssa survived the island's destruction by taking shelter with the other survivors in Chase's plane. Afterwards, she went off to search for Evelyn Sharp .

Thanatos Guild

Nyssa was not a member of the Thanatos Guild , however she went undercover to get into their organization and exploit them. She triggers an explosion in the Guild headquarters and engages in a fight with them before escaping and traveling to Star City . Nyssa intercepts Thea Queen and Roy Harper as they are on their way out of the city and informs Thea of the Guild's threat against her as she is also an Heiress to the Demon. She enlists Team Arrow's help to obtain Malcolm's last sift, a mysterious box in which they initially were unaware of what it encased, before the Thanatos Guild can retrieve it. With the help of Oliver Queen , Thea Queen, Felicity Smoak , Roy Harper, and John Diggle , she uncovers a map revealing the existence of three more Lazarus Pits around the world. She has set out to destroy the remaining Lazarus Pits alongside Thea and Roy as a way to ensure that they are not used for corrupt purposes and to right the wrongs done by her father, Ra's al Ghul .

After they successfully destroyed all the Lazarus Pits, Nyssa went to Thailand in order to deal with some leftover League business.

Anti-Monitor Crisis

Nyssa assisted The Flash , Kid Flash , Batwoman , The Ray , and The Atom on a mission to stop Outkast from destroying more Earths. Despite help from the Justice Alliance , they were unable to prevent Outkast from destroying Earth-D and Earth-76 .

Nyssa was killed when the remainder of the multiverse was destroyed by antimatter .

New multiverse

Nyssa was restored to life when the multiverse was restored. Nyssa later attended Oliver Queen's funeral with her sister Talia and reunited with Sara. Despite Sara having moved on by having a relationship with Ava Sharpe , Nyssa still called Sara her "beloved".

Erased future

In an erased future where Oliver died in the Crisis , Nyssa filled in the role of training his and Felicity's daughter, Mia Queen , as she grew up. Under Nyssa's tutelage, Mia became a master in combat by 2040.

Rewritten reality

In a reality created by Eobard Thawne using the Spear of Destiny , Nyssa's life was drastically altered so that she was living a "miserable, closeted life" in the middle of Ohio, courtesy of Malcolm and the spite that he had for her.

Personality

In the heat of battle, Nyssa shows a stoic demeanor and a cold-blooded killer side. When not in the heat of battle, contrasting what people would expect from her being in the League of Assassins as well as Ra's al Ghul 's daughter, Nyssa shows a warm, kind, and caring side to those she loves such as her friends such as Laurel alongside Team Arrow , and her love interests such as Sara Lance .

Nyssa's pain is evident in her first appearance, heartbroken that Sara had left her behind, she was more upset over this than any apparent betrayal of the League, she refuses to kill Sara, even when she is offered the opportunity to do so, she finds herself stricken with grief when she discovers that Sara had poisoned herself, rushing to her aid and crying, an ultimate act of "kindness" on her part was releasing Sara from her allegiance and oaths to the League as well as the threat of the League of Assassins; touched by Sara's undying care for her as the woman begged the Arrow not to kill her; despite being on the verge of dying herself, her "friendliness" is also seen in her debut; giving a warm smile and a playful wink to a young boy waiting in line near her at the airport. Nyssa also seems to be very moral as she simply subdued the guards that tried to arrest her during her debut and didn't involve innocent people and simply made her way out after she subdued them.

Nyssa can be just as cold as any other member of the League of Assassins. She acts more so out of personal vendetta than loyalty to the League. She wouldn't kill Sara, but she resorted to kidnapping her former lover's mother in retaliation, she delivers an ultimatum, declaring that she would show Sara how it felt to have someone she loves taken from her if she doesn't return, this act of vengeance for Sara leaving her continues, she feels once again abandoned when Sara would rather die than return to the league, and to her. She declares that if this were to be the case, she would kill Sara's family. However, she quickly abandoned this when she realized Sara would rather die than return to the league and chose to free her rather than letting her die, this implied she still cares for Sara despite them no longer being lovers. Even when Sara agreed to return to the League of Assassins in exchange for them helping subdue Slade's army , she told Quentin Lance she would die before she lets Sara die.

After Sara got killed she became furious and was convinced Malcolm Merlyn was the killer. In the process she even abducted Thea to draw Malcolm out. When Oliver didn't believed Malcolm did it she quickly considered him an enemy as well, and was prepared to kill him. She had a deep hatred for Malcolm as she was convinced he had killed Sara despite him swearing on his daughter he wasn't responsible, it was later revealed she was partly correct as he had drugged Thea and had her kill Sara. This revelation made her hate Malcolm to the point that despite him now being Ra's al Ghul, she made it clear she is still bent on killing him and tried to do so on all their duels. By the time Sara was resurrected by the Lazarus Pit, much to her horror, as she (correctly) knew that her former lover would only suffer in her new life, though this has since been annulled as Sara was able to regain control of herself, Nyssa's grudge towards Malcolm grew to the point that she literately swore to kill Malcolm in a manner that would have no coming back.

At first, Nyssa had a contentious relationship with Laurel Lance , since the latter held the former responsible for Sara's death while Nyssa believed Laurel wasn't worthy to wear Sara's jacket. Later though, the two began to grow closer, with Nyssa admitting that Laurel reminded her of Sara and offered to train her in becoming Black Canary. The two remained on friendly terms even when Nyssa considered Oliver an enemy, often sharing their memories about Sara.

Nyssa is proven to be knowledgeable of cultures and respectful even when she is on a vendetta. This was seen when she referred to "Jansen" with the honorable "sensei" before threatening to kill him over Malcolm's location.

Despite Nyssa's intelligent and calculating traits, she is prone to allow herself to be emotionally blind, especially when it comes to Sara, she even tried to attack Ra's al Ghul in a fit of rage upon learning he denied her what she believes to be her heritage, according to Malcolm, Nyssa's ultimate dream was to succeed her father, which she lost all chances of doing when she chose a relationship with Sara, Nyssa believes this as well and believed Ra's making Oliver his successor was a way to punish Nyssa (however, this is untrue, as Ra's' reasons for making Oliver his successor, is because he survived their duel and because Nyssa has proven herself unfit to become the leader of the league, as she has time and again proved that she allows her emotions to cloud her judgment) however, Nyssa is not always without compassion or reason, she did spare Thea for physically killing Sara since she was mind controlled by Malcolm to do so. She also put down the sword Thea gave her to subdue Roy and Laurel non-lethally instead of going for lethal methods.

While not completely explicit, Nyssa's animosity towards Oliver might be lessening, as she has seen multiple times that she trusts and cares for him, examples include when she willingly listened to his every word when they crashed the plane; her immediately raising her bow to defend her new husband when Diggle punched Oliver to the ground; and later when she treated his sword wound after his battle with Ra's, notably, Nyssa held no ill-will against Oliver when he killed her father despite wanting to do it herself and even quickly changed the subject to his surviving getting shot due to his league armor, conveying a warm smile during which.

Despite not being together, Nyssa still sees Oliver as her husband and Thea as her sister-in-law, this is also further proven that they both care about each other when Oliver requests they use an ancient set of rules so that he would have to face Malcolm, instead of Nyssa, Nyssa has demonstrated that though she can be emotionally blinded by her desires she is capable of change as she realized from Laurel's advice that she was turning into her father in her quest to remove Malcolm from his throne, and instead of continue the league's ways she used her newfound authority as Ra's al Ghul to disband the League of Assassins and parted with Oliver and Laurel on civil terms.

Nyssa has grown to care for Oliver and his team, having gone through the trouble of coming to Oliver to help him when he reached out to her, even going as far as to calling them her friends and that she stands with them when her sister, Talia , questioned her resolve for allying with their father's murderer.

Despite being forced into their marriage, Nyssa still acknowledges Oliver as her husband, even though she does not love him like one, implying that she still commits to vows. This seems to be in an effort to embarrass Oliver as she even told Slade Wilson about their marital status, causing Slade to question Oliver's love life.

Abilities

  • Peak of human physical condition: As a highly skilled assassin, Nyssa is in top physical condition with fast reflexes. She is noticeably much faster than Oliver Queen and Malcolm Merlyn , despite Oliver being the same age as her. Nyssa was strong enough to break the neck of Isabel Rochev .
    • Acrobatics/Free-running: Nyssa is extremely agile and graceful, able to easily parkour on her surroundings during fights. Like Sara Lance , Nyssa can easily descend from large heights with a scarf.
    • Honed senses: As a trained assassin, Nyssa has highly developed senses, able to detect the presence of others before they approach her. For example, Nyssa deduced Oliver was watching her meeting with Sara without turning around.
  • Master hand-to-hand combatant/Martial artist: Having trained with the League of Assassins since childhood, Nyssa is a highly skilled hand-to-hand combatant and martial artist, having gone through the same training that all league members do, though being the daughter of Ra's al Ghul meant that she was subjected to a higher level of training. Her style seems to comprise of Boxing, Karate, Muay Thai, Hapkido, Taekwondo, Judo, Sambo, Kali, Kickboxing, Kung Fu, Aikido, and Krav Maga. Nyssa was able to easily beat five armed security guards in a matter of seconds. She also contended against Oliver as the Arrow head-on in both of their two close quarters fights, even managing to briefly gain the upper hand and almost beat him until he eventually turned the tides. Nyssa proved herself an agile fighter, using her light weight and flexibility to her advantage. She also even able to hold her own against Malcolm repeatedly, although Malcolm would always gain the upper hand over her. Nyssa once defeated Malcolm when she had a sword while he only had a stick and caught him off-guard. However, during the League civil war, Nyssa confirmed that she couldn't defeat Malcolm in unarmed combat. Nyssa was also able to overwhelm Maseo Yamashiro in their fight, only losing since she became briefly distracted by Laurel Lance being defeated. Nyssa repeatedly displayed the ability to hold off and defeat several members of the league and the Thanatos Guild by herself at once, though she was defeated in a confrontation against the guild's leader, Athena . She eventually trained Mia Queen how to fight.
    • Master archer : Nyssa demonstrated skills in archery that seemed to rival that of Oliver and Malcolm's. She was able to keep up with Oliver in their first one-on-one archery battle and almost won. Nyssa can also easily shoot arrows from a moving vehicle.
    • Master stick-fighter : Nyssa has proven to be a very proficient stick-fighter, she was able to take down multiple armed guards with a police baton. Like Oliver, she offen uses her bow as a blunt weapon in close combat.
    • Master swordswoman : As a former member of the League of Assassins, Nyssa is an excellent swordswoman, she was able to overpower and capture Malcolm when she had a sword while he only had a stick. In their sparring sessions though, Malcolm had repeatedly defeated her, also, during the League civil war, Nyssa confirmed that she couldn't defeat Malcolm in a sword duel. Furthermore, in her sword duel against Oliver, who had greatly improved his swordsman skills, Nyssa was disarmed in seconds. Nyssa was able to defeat some assassins from the League using Oliver's sword by herself and proved able to fight on par with, even defeating her older half-sister, Talia al Ghul , despite the latter's much greater experience; though it was only because she simply caught Talia off-guard. Nyssa was able to put up a good fight in her confrontation against Athena, but she was soon bested.
    • Master knife welder/Knife thrower: Nyssa is a highly proficient knife welder, as she was able to easily slit the throat of a League member and is also able to throw her darts with great accuracy.
  • High-level intellect/Master tactician/Leader: Nyssa is intelligent and well-trained in strategy and subterfuge. She devised a plan to poison Laurel and later kidnap Dinah Lance to force Sara Lance into returning to her and the League. She also kidnapped Thea Queen to lure out Malcolm. As the former heir to the Demon, Nyssa displayed leadership skills as she lead a team of assassins to battle Slade Wilson's Mirakuru army and retake the league from Malcolm. Nyssa was also able to infiltrate the Thanatos Guild and discover their endgame, even having devised a fail-safe to help her escape the guild if she was discovered.
    • Explosives expert: Nyssa has become highly trained with the usage of plastic explosives, as she placed a network bombs in the base of the Thanatos Guild to cover her escape while she infiltrated the group.
    • Expert medic: Nyssa is highly trained in first aid. She nursed Sara, who was dying of starvation on Lian Yu , back to health, and treated a sword wound on Oliver's hand after his battle with Ra's.
    • Toxicology: Nyssa displayed a familiarity with poisons. She used a small amount of Tibetan viper venom to poison Laurel in a ruse to bring Sara back, and also immediately figured out that Sara had poisoned herself with said venom. Nyssa later used a dart laced with a sedative to make Roy Harper pass out.
    • Multilingual: Nyssa is capable of fluently speaking English , Arabic , Japanese , and Russian .
  • Meditation: Nyssa is skilled in the art of meditation, which she used to stay calm while being held captive in a cell in the Arrowcave by Team Arrow .
  • Stealth/Infiltration/Escape artist: Nyssa is highly experienced in entering and leaving buildings undetected, like Sara, she uses a scarf to quickly descend down from ceilings. Nyssa was able to break into the Arrowcave undetected and ambush Oliver and Roy. She also infiltrated the Thanatos Guild undetected long enough to learn of their plans.

Equipment

  • Customized hunting arrows : Nyssa carries a number of black arrows with her at all times. These arrows feature a special red fletching and tip.
  • Customized PSE Chaos AD Compound bow : Nyssa wields a personal compound bow, similar to that of Malcolm Merlyn . It is possible that this bow is the signature bow to the League of Assassins .
  • Dagger: Nyssa carries a personalized dagger at her right thigh. It features a gold trim and a marked blade. She drew this dagger upon reuniting with Sara Lance and during her battle against the Arrow .
  • League of Assassins suit : Nyssa wears a protective suit of black robes, unique from the usual garb worn by League assassins. Her suit differs in that it features loose fitting garbs and a red shoulder pauldron. The red color indicates Nyssa's seniority in the League.
  • Sword : Nyssa can proficiently wield a sword. Her sword of choice is an Arabian Sands scimitar.
  • Trick arrows : Shortly after her return to Starling City , Nyssa uses a bola arrow to save Laurel from a thug, who was about to use a second knife, after the former underestimated her opponent.
  • Quiver: Nyssa wears a black quiver to keep arrows on her back at all times.

Former equipment

  • Scarf: Nyssa uses a scarf to descend down a ceiling. She also used it to block Malcolm's arrows.
  • Darts: Nyssa used a dart to sedate Roy Harper , making him pass out.
