Caitlin Marie "Caity" Lotz (born December 30, 1986) is an American actress. She portrays Sara Lance/The Canary/White Canary in Arrow , The Flash , DC's Legends of Tomorrow , Supergirl , and Batwoman . She also portrayed Werner Zytle , Oliver Queen , and Felicity Smoak as Sara in Arrow , and Sara Lance (robot) in DC's Legends of Tomorrow .

Trivia

  • Lotz directed episode 5 of Season 5 of DC's Legends of Tomorrow , " Mortal Khanbat ". For this, she shadowed James Bamford and Kevin Mock , and talked with Danielle Panabaker , who was the first female cast member in the Arrowverse to direct.
    • Lotz joined Panabaker, David Harewood , Melissa Benoist , Tom Cavanagh , and David Ramsey as actors who have directed episodes of their respective shows.
