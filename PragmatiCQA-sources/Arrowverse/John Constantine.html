John Constantine (10 May 1982 - 1925; resurrected 1925), nicknamed El Diablo (the Devil) by the gangsters of Mexico City , is an exorcist, demonologist, and warlock, self-styled as "master of the dark arts". John is the son of the late Tommy Constantine and the late Mary Anne , the brother of an unnamed woman , and a descendant of Konstentyn . He is also the ex-lover/good friend of Anne Marie Flynn , the late Sara Lance , and Gary Green , as well as the ex-boyfriend of Desmond , the late Natalie Logue , and Zari Tarazi . Having been raised by an abusive father since John's mother died in childbirth, he grew up highly cynical and cunning, soon developing an interest in the dark arts.

When John was 15 years old, he was introduced into the occult world by Anne Marie. He learned various spells and became an exorcist with the hopes of one day using his powers to resurrect his mother. John became a figure of respect among the dabblers of magic and began leading the Newcastle Crew .

One night, John attempted an exorcism on a young girl named Astra Logue by summoning the demon Nergal , only for the demon to kill Astra and drag her soul to Hell , damning her for eternity, an action which damned John's soul for Hell upon death. Overwhelmed with guilt, he initially retired but after hearing of the Rising Darkness from the angel Manny , John returned to face this threat, with Manny giving him hope that, should he succeed, John's soul may be spared eternal damnation. While hunting a hunger demon , which he suspected may be connected to the Rising Darkness , John briefly operated under the alias Lee Drygas .

After the Legends allowed Mallus to escape his prison in order to destroy the demon, it opened a door to other evils. Joined by Gary of the Time Bureau , John confronted the Legends about their latest screw-up. Five months later, he would assist the Legends in capturing the escaped magical " fugitives ", sending them to Hell. He initially refused to join the team, but after being pursued by a demon named Neron in 2018, John agreed to move into the Waverider , though still insisted that he's "not a bloody Legend".

While helping the Legends return the Encores to Hell, John nearly had his life cut short by a grown-up Astra, who sped up the 10 years of lung cancer he would accumulate. However, he got his life back by promising her to find the Loom of Fate and make Charlie alter the Logue family's fates. When the Legends had to destroy the Loom to end Loomworld , John was unable to cure his future cancer, though Astra came to accept her mother's passing and returned his soul token . He also started a relationship with fellow Legend Zari.

Following an encounter with Aleister Crowley , John lost his powers and became reliant on the Scarlet Lady . Eventually, wanting to reclaim his original magic, he betrayed the Legends and teamed up with Bishop to find the Fountain of Imperium , but was killed in the process. In an attempt to return to the land of the living, John used his token to sell his soul to a demon, becoming damned again. John then chose to end his relationship with Zari and leave the Legends to walk his own path alone once more, using a time stone to return to 2021.

Biography

Original multiverse

Early life

John Constantine was born in Liverpool , England on 10 May 1982, as the only son of Tommy Constantine and Mary Anne . His mother died giving birth to him, causing his father to develop a deep resentment towards John, whom he derisively referred to as "killer" his whole life. Whenever Tommy came home from drinking, which John stated was "every night", he would physically abuse his son, reminding him that Mary Anne died because of him. Also, to punish John, Tommy would put out cigarettes by burning them on his arms.

John had an older sister , although she eventually abandoned him for unknown reasons.

During his childhood, John met and befriended a girl named Natalie . The two would play together while wearing masks and telling stories about the mythical Loom of Fate , and how they would use it.

As a teenager, John formed the band Mucous Membrane with Bernie Reed . The main goal was desire to gain more attention from girls, but they only managed to release one album. When he was 15 years old, John met a girl named Anne Marie Flynn , who introduced him into the occult world. The two also became lovers for a time. John then began reading every book he could find on the occult, learning how to conjure dead beings in the hopes of one day conjuring his mother.

At some point in John's life, he met and became friends with both Chas Chandler and Esrin Fortuna , the latter of whom he eventually came to owe a substantial sum of money to.

In adulthood, John's friendship with Natalie developed into a romance. However, they broke up when he chose practicing magic over their relationship.

John and Chas spent their time in one of the bars getting drunk in the process. When John decided to go out with a girl he met, he asked his friend to pay the bill for him again. At the same time, John cast a protective spell on Chas, fearing that he might do something to himself by returning to his family. Before leaving, Chas wanted to listen to a song by a certain band that ended tragically. The pyrotechnic effects set off a fire that occupied an entire building. The man began to help people evacuate, but did not manage to save all of them and himself.

However, Chas woke up in the hospital with his family. After leaving the facility, he met with John, trying to understand the whole incident. He showed him the newspaper of the 47 people who died in the fire, declaring himself a victim - the 48th. It turned out that the spell John cast on Chas caused his body to swallow up all the dead souls. It was because of this that Chas's relationship with his family deteriorated and his wife divorced him. Chas followed John's advice and, unable to live normally with the souls of others, decided to fight evil alongside his friend in order to repay the gift and use it in a good way.

John once had a brutal encounter with Fennel and a sorcerer named Felix Faust . He survived, but left Faust with a grudge against him. John also became bitter enemies with voodoo priest Papa Midnite after several nefarious dealings. At one point, he came to learn of Damien Darhk , a practitioner of dark magic who was the leader of H.I.V.E. . John grew to fear Darhk so much he considered leaving whatever city the latter sought to destroy as a preferable option to facing him. John once put Beebo under a spell that made the toy sentient. Apparently, Beebo told him things that "will make your toes curl". Over the course of his adventures, John found the Grim Reaper in person and stole his sickle for unknown reasons. He also met a " Swamp Thing " in Maine .

Visit to Lian Yu

In 2010, Seeking an artifact called the Orb of Horus , an ancient spell book, John traveled to Lian Yu to retrieve it, but was caught by Baron Reiter 's men and brutally interrogated. Conklin interrupted the interrogation to tell Reiter of an A.R.G.U.S. communications device he found and accused newcomer Oliver Queen of being a spy. Reiter presented Oliver with a map John possessed and after confirming his knowledge of their locations, John performed a spell to free himself from his handcuffs and held Oliver at gunpoint and used him as leverage to escape Reiter's compound. He had Oliver forcibly take him to the area he sought and performed a spell to uncover the hidden passageway, much to Oliver's shock.

Once inside, they explored the cave where John explained that there are areas of the world as old as it including Lian Yu, possessing things corrupt men aren't supposed to have such as Reiter. John eventually found what he sought and retrieved it but set off a trap which Oliver saves him from being killed by, insisting he's not one of Reiter's men. After exiting the cave John thanked Oliver for saving his life and broke off the crystal from the rod to give to Reiter, as the rod itself is what contains the magic. John also warned Oliver about Reiter, insisting his narcotics aren't his only interest for being on the island. As insurance, John also used the rod to transfer a spell on his arm onto Oliver's torso, insisted he'd know what to do when the time came and on Oliver's request, John punched him out cold as part of his "escape" and left on his boat, but the two would keep in contact.

Incident at Newcastle

In 2011, Constantine crossed over to Earth-666 and made a deal with Lucifer Morningstar involving his friend Maze . Afterwards, Lucifer owed Constantine a favor. At some point between 2012 and 2014, John stopped the Monkey King from eviscerating an entire family and killed him with the help of Chas.

In 2014, Natalie , was killed by a drunk driver. John and his friends, Anne Marie Flynn , Gary Lester , Ritchie Simpson , Judith, and Chas Chandler , attempted to resurrect her. While Natalie was successfully resurrected, the ritual was botched and a demon possessed Natalie's daughter, Astra . John then decided to summon a more powerful demon named Nergal and extort him into dragging the lesser demon back to Hell, but unfortunately, Nergal was able to seize Astra and violently pulled her into Hell in front of everyone, damning her for eternity. This action also damned John's soul for Hell upon his eventual death. Natalie then committed suicide out of guilt.

This action severally traumatized John and he placed himself in Ravenscar Psychiatric Facility for the Mentally Deranged out of guilt. The rest of the crew also came to resent John, some more than others. Because of his actions and what happened to Astra, the group was haunted for the rest of their lives.

Personality


Having witnessed the horrors of the world, John is very cynical and seemingly unconcerned with the value of human life, often deflecting his real feelings with sarcastic jokes even towards the possibility of his own death. Due to the number of people who have died around him John prefers to work alone and anyone he does work with, he avoids attachment so he won't be as hurt if they get killed. He is also known for having a very dry sense of humor.

John is noted for being brutally honest with individuals, even angels and never being shy of expressing his attraction to individuals he encounters. It has been seen a few times, that he openly expresses an attraction to Zed Martin , after meeting Team Arrow he openly refers to all the female members of the team, such as Laurel Lance , Felicity Smoak and Thea Queen , as "pretty girls", and even openly flirted with Leo Snart .

Despite John's bleak view of the world and cynicism, he is a very honorable man as he is always there for the few friends he has, helping Chas to retrieve his daughter from Felix Faust and helping Oliver Queen to restore Sara Lance 's soul, he also did a protection spell on Oliver despite his refusal to have attachments to individuals when he saw Oliver had a pure heart. He also has a strong capability for regret and remorse, as he was so overwhelmed with guilt after Astra was damned to Hell that he placed himself in Ravenscar Psychiatric Facility for the Mentally Deranged and seems to express some kindness to those who suffered because of him, as he was shown to be trying considerably to make amends with Anne Marie Flynn when they reunited as he knew of the pain he inflicted on her and the anger she felt for him.

John tends to be a pessimistic realist as when Oliver thanked him for bringing Sara back he simply told Oliver he owed him only for Oliver to rebuttal that instead of that, they should always have each other's back when the need is, not as business acquaintances, but as friends.

However, John is arrogant and overconfident in his bluffing, to the point of having questionable morals, such as when he threatened the life of an innocent child, Diego, to get Lamashtu (one of Eve 's sisters) to reveal why she was working for the La Brujeria , which forced his ex-lover, Anne Marie Flynn , to shoot him and leave John to be eaten by an Invunche .

As John's journey to stop the Rising Darkness continued, he appeared to have developed possible genuine romantic feelings for Zed Martin, as observed by Manny, who pointed out that John was jealous upon seeing Jim Corrigan kiss her, since he seemed to be genuinely shocked and upset to see it. He also admitted that due to rarely encountering evil humans, he was troubled by the La Brujeria as they were quite possibly the worst of humanity, which worried him.

John has caused so much pain to those closest to him that if there is ever a chance to end that pain he would accept it in no matter what the consequences are, this is best seen when demons stated they would release Astra from hell should he stop interfering in their plans. Also, when he went back in time to save his boyfriend, Desmond from being sent to hell even though it could cause damage to the timeline.
