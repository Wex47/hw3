Juliana Jay Harkavy (born January 1, 1985) is an American actress. She portrayed Sarah in Constantine and Dinah Drake/Black Canary in Arrow , The Flash , and DC's Legends of Tomorrow . She also portrayed Dinah Drake (Earth-2) in Arrow and was set to reprise her role as Dinah Drake/Black Canary in the potential spin-off, Green Arrow and the Canaries , before its cancellation.
