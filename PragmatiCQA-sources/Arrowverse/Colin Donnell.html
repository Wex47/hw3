Colin Donnell (born October 9, 1982) is an American actor. He portrayed Tommy Merlyn , Christopher Chance as Tommy, and Tommy Merlyn/Dark Archer in Arrow . He has also portrayed Tommy Merlyn/Prometheus in Supergirl .
