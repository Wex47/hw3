History

Original multiverse

The Queen family is possibly the wealthiest and the most powerful family in Star City, however; due to Moira Dearden 's involvement with the Undertaking , the family lost most, if not all of their influence in Star City and they have gained a negative reputation.

The Queen family's reputation had been repaired to the point where Moira ran for mayor. The Queen family lost control of Queen Consolidated (now Palmer Technologies ). Felicity Smoak later became a new member through her marriage to Oliver . Later Oliver learns that he has a son named William Clayton with Samantha Clayton .

New multiverse

In the new multiverse, the amalgamation of different universes, primarily Earth-1 , Earth-2 and Earth-TUD5 , the Queen family had a reunion of sorts at the funeral of Oliver. There, Moira and Laurel Lance both discussed being alive with Thea and Tommy, respectively. Felicity met Mia, her daughter's possible adult self, who had saved her younger half-brother, William, from a kidnapper . Also, Emiko introduced herself to Moira and Thea, gaining her desire to be recognized as a Queen.

Trivia

Earth-1/Earth-Prime

  • Each member of this family (except Mia Queen ) was affected by Malcolm Merlyn ;
    • Malcolm tried to kill Robert and Oliver Queen by sabotaging the Queen's Gambit .
    • Malcolm threatened Moira Queen in his plans by threatening her family.
    • Malcolm kidnapped Walter Steele and held him hostage for months.
    • Malcolm drugged Thea Queen into killing Sara Lance and she learned that Malcolm was responsible.
    • Nyssa al Ghul lost Sara when Malcolm used Thea to kill her.
    • Malcolm kidnapped William Clayton and handed him over to Damien Darhk .
    • Malcolm has endangered Felicity Smoak 's life on multiple occasions.
  • Although Thea is a Queen by name, she is not a member of the Queen family's bloodline; as Robert was not her biological father.
  • Although Emiko was not a Queen by name, she is a member of the Queen family's bloodline; as Robert was her biological father.
  • Although William is not a Queen by name, he is a member of the Queen family's bloodline; as Oliver was his biological father.

Earth-2

  • Every member of the Queen family is either dead or presumed dead.

Earth-X

  • Kara is the only non-human member of the Queen family, due to her marriage to Oliver .
