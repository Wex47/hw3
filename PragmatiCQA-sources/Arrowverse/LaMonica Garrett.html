LaMonica Garrett (born May 23, 1975) is an American actor. He portrayed Mar Novu/The Monitor and Mobius/Anti-Monitor in Supergirl , Arrow , The Flash , DC's Legends of Tomorrow , and Batwoman .

Trivia

  • In order to ensure his availability for appearances prior to and in Crisis on Infinite Earths , Garrett was upgraded to series regular on all shows involved in the crossover. As such, Garrett is the second actor to be credited as main cast in multiple Arrowverse series, after Keiynan Lonsdale ( The Flash and DC's Legends of Tomorrow ).
    • He is the first, and so far, only actor to be credited as a series regular on more than two series, as well as the first, and so far, only actor to be credited as a series regular on all Arrowverse series (up to the 2019-2020 television season).
    • He is also the first, and so far only, actor to simultaneously be credited as a regular on multiple series, as he received main cast credits on The Flash , Arrow , Supergirl , Batwoman , and DC's Legends of Tomorrow in the same television season (2019-2020).
    • However, he is only credited in the main cast in episodes in which he appeared.
