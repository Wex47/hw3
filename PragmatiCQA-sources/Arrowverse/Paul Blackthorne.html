Paul Blackthorne (born March 5, 1969) is an English actor. He has portrayed Quentin Lance in Arrow , The Flash , and DC's Legends of Tomorrow . He has also portrayed Werner Zytle appearing as Quentin in a hallucination in Arrow and a Sturmbannführer in The Flash .
