Biography

McSnurtle was given to Barry Allen and Iris West at their new home by Harrison Wells as a housewarming gift.

Following the damage the apartment sustained during the Speed Force storm, McSnurtle's status is unknown.

Personality

According to H.R. Wells , McSnurtle can bite people. It is unknown if she bites everyone or if it is out of a personal dislike for H.R..

Behind the scenes

  • In the comics, McSnurtle the turtle is a speedster vigilante and a parody of Jay Garrick known as the Terrific Whatzit . It first appeared in Funny Stuff #1 in 1944, which was 12 years before the introduction of Barry Allen .
    • In the comics, the gender of McSnurtle is actually male, unlike the TV counterpart, which is female.
  • In " Revenge of the Rogues ", Iris West references a turtle-like fictional character called McSnurtle.
  • There's been a turtle reference in every tenth episode of each season, which includes " Revenge of the Rogues ", " Potential Energy ", " Borrowing Problems from the Future ", and " The Trial of The Flash ".
