Chyler Leigh West (born April 10, 1982), known professionally as Chyler Leigh , is an American actress. She portrayed Alex Danvers/Sentinel in Supergirl , Arrow , The Flash , and DC's Legends of Tomorrow . She also portrayed Alex Danvers (Earth-1) as well as a White Martian , J'onn J'onzz , and Malefic J'onzz who all shapeshifted as Alex.
