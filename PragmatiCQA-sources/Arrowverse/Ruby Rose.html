Ruby Rose Langenheim (born March 20, 1986), better known as Ruby Rose , is an Australian actress. She portrayed Kate Kane/Batwoman in The Flash , Arrow , Supergirl , Batwoman , and DC's Legends of Tomorrow .

On May 19, 2020, it was revealed that Rose would not return for the second season of Batwoman , later being replaced by Wallis Day .
