Merlyn Global Group was a company that was owned and run by the Merlyn family . It was one of the prime businesses in Star City . In the wake of the Undertaking and Malcolm Merlyn 's involvement, the status of the company is currently unknown.

History

Merlyn Global Group sponsored a fundraiser for CNRI after their largest donor, Stagg Industries , pulled out.

The company helped to create the Markov Device that caused the Undertaking , resulting in the death of 503 people, including Malcolm 's son, Tommy Merlyn .

With Malcolm's role in the Undertaking revealed to the public, Tommy's death, and Thea traveling the world, it is unknown if Merlyn Global Group is still operational.

Alternate reality

In the alternate reality created by the Dominators , the company still existed in Star City and Malcolm was presumably the CEO.

Rewritten reality

In the rewritten reality created by the Legion of Doom using the Spear of Destiny , Malcolm was still the well-respected CEO of Merlyn Global Group.
