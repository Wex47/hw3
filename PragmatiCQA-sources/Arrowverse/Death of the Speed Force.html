  • Cisco mentions that by the events of the episode, all doppelgängers from the old multiverse who ended up on Earth-Prime are supposed to be dead because of cellular degeneration.   • This episode was originally scheduled for March 3, 2020, but was later confirmed to air on March 10th, 2020. This could be due to the Super Tuesday elections. Instead, a rerun of " Love Is A Battlefield " aired on March 3.
