The Amazo was a ship led, however not captained, by a scientist named Anthony Ivo . It was manned by a crew of pirates under Ivo's employ.

History

It held various prisoners such as Oliver Queen and Anatoly Knyazev , on whom he wished to test Mirakuru , a serum that gave its users superhuman strength. Ivo wished to seek out the Mirakuru, located on a lost submarine somewhere around the island. The freighter was eventually taken over by Slade Wilson , a Mirakuru-enhanced individual, after he killed its former captain, " The Butcher " and cut off Ivo's hand, leading to his 'mercy killing' by Oliver Queen on Lian Yu. Following Slade's defeat by Oliver and it being hit and severely damaged by a torpedo fired from a WWII submarine manned by Anatoly Knyazev, the ship became wrecked on the coastal shores of Lian Yu .

Behind the scenes

  • In the main DC comics continuity, Amazo is a robotic entity, capable of duplicating all the powers of the members of the Justice League. The robot was created by Anthony Ivo , who is associated with the ship in Arrow .
