Trivia

  • This is the fifth penultimate episode to be named after a Bruce Springsteen song. The song originally appeared on the soundtrack to the 1995 film The Crossing Guard .
    • The title refers to Prometheus's team kidnapping those close to Oliver and bringing them to Lian Yu .
  • This episode marks the first appearance of Byron Mann as Yao Fei Gulong in four years, as he last appeared in the Season 1 episode " Darkness on the Edge of Town "​​.
  • When Diggle and Felicity were in a car and saw Talia al Ghul , Felicity said that she has flashbacks from Isabel Rochev . When they tried to hit Talia with the car and it didn't work, Felicity remarked that it worked better with Isabel. This is a reference to when Felicity hit Isabel with a van and saved Diggle in the Season 2 penultimate episode " Streets of Fire ".
  • Felicity got Oliver's birthday cake from Lord Mesa Bakery , a nod to Lord Mesa, an artist who frequently created art of the Arrowverse . His artwork appears on the cake.
    • Lord Mesa was commissioned by Marc Guggenheim to design the artwork, but was unaware of the shout-out.
    • Also, the number 6 appears on the cake beside the Green Arrow character. This signifies the current number of members on Team Arrow and the fact that that Arrow ​​ will be returning for a sixth season .
  • This episode marks the return of Manu Bennett as Slade Wilson , as he last appeared in the Season 3 episode " The Return ". However, a clip of Bennett's performance was seen in " Invasion! ".
  • Curtis says that he always wanted to see Florence , clarifying that meant the city and not "the woman or her weird machines", referencing the indie rock band Florence + the Machine .
  • Kovar touches Oliver's various scars, causing him to flash back to each of the moments the wounds were inflicted.
    • A scar on his upper right torso from when Yao Fei shot him with an arrow in " Honor Thy Father ".
    • A scar on his stomach, where Billy Wintergreen stabbed him with a katana in " Damaged "​​.
    • Scars on his back from when Conklin whipped him in " Blood Debts "​​.
  • After a number of their friends have been kidnapped, Felicity likens their situation to a "horror version" of the 1983 film Groundhog Day .
  • Kovar describing Oliver "like a piece of iron" is reminiscent of another Dolph Lundgren character, Ivan Drago, when he described Rocky in Rocky IV .
  • This episode marks the first appearance of Malcolm Merlyn in the present storyline of Season 5 . He previously appeared in flashbacks of " Kapiushon ", a dream in " Invasion! " and joined the Legion of Doom during Season 2 of DC's Legends of Tomorrow ​​ after the events of " Schism ", which means that this is a year later for him as Sara mentioned having dropped him off to 2016 before wiping his memory.
  • Talia is not shown unmasked because Lexa Doig does not play her until next episode.
  • Nyssa al Ghul tells Oliver that in 2008, her father instructed her to visit Lian Yu, where she found Sara Lance . When Sara joined the League of Assassins in 1958 and left in 1960, as seen in the Season 1 DC's Legends of Tomorrow ​​ episode " Left Behind "​​, she told Ra's al Ghul to make sure that Nyssa would go to Lian Yu to keep her timeline intact.
  • When Oliver showed Malcolm and Nyssa his progress in tracking Chase's plane, a map of the West Coast of the United States appeared, placing Star City in the approximate location of the real-world San Francisco .
  • This episode shares similarities to The Flash ​​ episode " Infantino Street "​​, which aired the same week:
    • Oliver asks an old foe, Slade, for help in defeating Prometheus, similar to how Barry Allen asked Leonard Snart to help him defeat Savitar .

    • Both are the 22nd episode of their respective season.

    • Oliver also confronts Malcolm over giving up William to Damien Darhk , referring to the events of " Taken ".
  • Oliver and Thea refer to the last time she had a birthday party, which was 4 years ago in Season 1 's " Trust but Verify ".
  • Through Black Siren 's interactions with Quentin , it is suggested her own father is deceased, which was confirmed in the Season 6 episode " Irreconcilable Differences "​​.
  • Right before escaping, Chase tells Oliver that he is going to "see [him] on the boat". This clearly foreshadows their ultimate showdown during the concluding scene of the season finale, " Lian Yu ".
