Wlliam Clayton (born 2006 ) is the son of the late Oliver Queen and the late Samantha Clayton , the step-son of Felicity Smoak , the older paternal half-brother of Mia Queen , the grandson of Frank and Irene Clayton and Robert and Moira Queen , and the nephew of Emiko Adachi and Thea Queen . After William's kidnapping at the hands of Damien Darhk , he and his mother were relocated to an undisclosed location where he adopted the name Matthew .

In May 2017, he was tracked down by Adrian Chase , who kidnapped him in order to threaten his father. Soon after being rescued by his father, an explosion occurred on Lian Yu where his mother, who had also been captured, was killed. This resulted in William having to move in with Oliver. However, in early 2019, he moved back to Central City , going to live with his maternal grandparents.

In an erased future he had changed his name to William Harris and founded Harris Consolidated by 2040.

In the new multiverse, William got the chance to grow up with his half-sister, Mia, and live with Felicity after his father's death. He attended Oliver's funeral to honor his father. He got the chance to get to know his grandmother, Moira, and his aunt, Emiko.

Biography

Original multiverse

William was conceived during Oliver Queen 's affair with Samantha Clayton in 2005. Oliver shared the news with his mother, Moira , expressing that he had messed up. Thinking this could potentially ruin Oliver's life, Moira paid Samantha one million dollars to pretend she had miscarried and "asked" her to move away to Central City and never to see Oliver again.

Although Samantha never took the money, she agreed to lie to Oliver, and moved to Central City. She also denied Moira access to the child; as Samantha believed the former would be a bad influence on her son.

Early life

William Clayton was born in 2006, and was solely raised by his mother with the aid of her parents Frank and Irene.

In late-2007, William's father and paternal grandfather were presumed deceased after their yacht, the Queen's Gambit, sunk on a three week long planned trip, when he was roughly still a year old, so even if something had changed his mother's mind, William would not be able to meet his father for several years to come.

Samantha loved him and did her best to make sure William lived a well adjusted, happy life.

Meeting his father

Eight years later, William's mother visited CC Jitters . She called him, promising to be back with a hot chocolate soon. A year later, William was running through CC Jitters with his action figure and ran into his father, Oliver , unbeknownst to both of them. He dropped it and Oliver went to give William back his figurine of The Flash and he ran to his mother. The sight of him with his mother made Oliver start to mentally connect some dots.

On their way to see a Central City Diamonds game, William and his mother were briefly stopped by Oliver. While his mother was conversing with Oliver, William told her mother to hurry or they'd miss the game. Unknowingly, Oliver managed to get one of William's hairs so that he could run some DNA tests. Later, while playing with his action figures on his home patio, William and his mother were interrupted by Oliver once again. He was then told to go play with his toys inside the house. Unknown to him, his mother and Oliver got into an argument as Oliver had found out that William was his son.

The following day, William was formally greeted by Oliver, who told him he was an old friend of his mother's. He was asked if it was okay if he visited him once in a while and the two played with his Flash and Captain Cold action figures.

Kidnapped by Damien Darhk

Later on, Malcolm Merlyn , bitter that Oliver gave the Demon's Head ring to Nyssa al Ghul who ended up disbanding the League of Assassins , revealed William's identity to Damien Darhk in order to get revenge.

On Damien's orders, Malcolm Merlyn kidnapped William from his home, where he was staying, and Damien brought him to the hotel where he, Damien, was staying, to be his daughter, Nora 's playmate. Damien confronted Oliver and Felicity Smoak and demanded that Oliver get out of the race for mayor but Oliver refused. He then got a video on his phone of William. After his mother was told the truth about Oliver's secret identity and the team received the help from Vixen , his action figure was used to try and magically track him. However, he was moved by Darhk to another location before he could be rescued minutes prior to when they arrived.

At Darhk's hideout, William heard the commotion made by Team Arrow as they fought against Darhk and his Ghosts. He ran outside to find Darhk was magically choking the Green Arrow. When Darhk's magic was cut off he saw the Green Arrow begin beating Darhk in anger. William and the Green Arrow spent a moment staring at each other before he left when the police began to arrive. At the SCPD , he was reunited with his mother who had his Flash action figure. He told her that he was bored with the Flash and now wanted one of the Green Arrow . She agreed with him that the Green Arrow was indeed amazing, not knowing that the actual Green Arrow was standing not too far off from them. After that Oliver sent him and his mother away to keep them safe, also making a recording that was meant for William to hear on his 18th birthday, revealing their relation and his identity as the Green Arrow to his son.

William and his mother soon began living in a new city under different identities. In this period, he attended Cadena Hall Elementary.

Taken by Prometheus

William was later tracked down by Adrian Chase at his bus stop who called him by his name William, though he said that his name was Matthew. Adrian claimed that he was a friend of Oliver's and tricked William into going with him. When he was arrested, he sent a phone message to Oliver with a video attached of William screaming for help, which prompted him to try to free him from custody. However, the kidnapper didn't divulge where he was keeping William.

Soon after being kidnapped, William was put on a boat that was anchored off the shore of Lian Yu , which Adrian would attempt to use to escape the island. Arriving on the island, Oliver demanded Prometheus to give up William, but he evaded the answer and battled Oliver instead. The kidnapper claimed that William was dead so that he could convince Oliver to kill him, but the Green Arrow didn't give in. Later, Prometheus escaped the battle and headed to the boat in which William was in, but Oliver quickly chased after him and fought him for William's location. Prometheus grabbed William, who was kept inside the boat, and dared Oliver to kill him for William's safety, but it would also mean that Prometheus's kill-switch would blow the island up along with everyone on it.

However, if Oliver chooses not to kill Prometheus, everyone on the island would survive, but William would not. Oliver shot Adrian in the leg and secured William who jumped to safety in the arms of his now revealed father. Prometheus told Oliver that William now knows who his father really is, though he is younger than Oliver was when he learned the truth. He explained that it was a good thing because he would now have to live without his mother. Oliver shielded William's view of Prometheus as he took a gun and then shot himself in the head. Soon after killing himself, the killswitch was triggered and the two of them look on as a series of explosions are set off all over Lian Yu.

Moving in with Oliver

Following his mother's death during the explosions on Lian Yu, William was taken in by Oliver. Oliver enlisted the help of his former nanny, Raisa , to care for William as his grueling schedule of being the mayor and Green Arrow leaves little time for child care. For the next five months, William had trouble adjusting, coping with the loss of his mother, starting at a new school, and getting to know his father. At first, William had frequent nightmares and referred to Oliver as the "bad man." However, he slowly started warming up to Oliver.

When it was revealed Oliver was supposedly the Green Arrow, at his school, William was bullied by eighth graders that claimed Oliver was not there for him, recieving a black in the process. Oliver picked up William in his limo, and attempted to get him to open up, with him giving a lesson in self defense; to punch the largest of the group next time in the nose, ultimately getting the rest to back off. Not before Oliver needing to leave due to conflict with Cayden James, leaving William alone, angry, and to be taken home by his driver and security. That night, Oliver attempted to reach out to William and apologize, with him claiming his father could not gurantee he would come back, thusly leaving William an orphan. William scared of losing his father, Oliver promised his son to be the best father he could be and to do everything in his power to ensure he comes back, leading to father and son playing video games together as William accepts his apology.

Oliver eventually decided to give up being Green Arrow in order to take care of William. John Diggle took on the role of Green Arrow in his absence. However, though improving, Oliver still had trouble connecting with William, particularly because he has never been any good at school, while William excels at it. At Oliver's request, Felicity steps in and becomes William's math tutor. Seeing how good she is with William prompted Oliver to reconsider his relationship with Felicity, and ultimately led to them getting back together.

When Slade Wilson asked for help from Oliver, Felicity convinced Oliver to tell William the truth as to why he will be gone, as helping a friend. A few days later, William called Oliver asking when he was coming home, Oliver then said that he is on his way back, William asked if he was able to help his friend, which Oliver told him he couldn't. William celebrated Thanksgiving with his father. At this time, Oliver's sister, Thea awoke from her coma, and William got to meet his aunt for the first time. When Oliver and Felicity celebrated their wedding with a party, William got to know his aunt Thea. William asked if he could have champagne, to which Thea promised him a sip, but only during the toasts at the party, to which Oliver agreed was alright for Robert and Moira allowed the two of them to do so. William later danced with Thea.

By 2018, William called Oliver "Dad," and was emotionally attached to him as his father. At one point, Oliver lost his temper with Felicity and William, causing him to accidentally break William's science fair project. Felicity, upset with Oliver, tells him to get out while she takes William to the fair. When they come home and realize that Oliver was drugged, William gives Felicity sneakers telling her to go stop him from starting an all out battle with the Star City Police Department . William later took a field trip outside Star City, and on his bus bullies attempted to mess with him but he ignored. Later, the tunnel was being attacked by Cayden James' hack attack, and he bravely got the other kids of the bus, but was later saved by his father as Green Arrow. Though angry, back at his father's bunker, William reconciled his own desires with help from Felicity and as he saw Oliver be a hero on the Overwatch cameras. Later back home, William encourages his father to be a hero and assures he's fine.

Moving to Central City

While away at boarding school in 2019, William realized that he was gay as he developed an attraction to another boy. This resulted in him getting into a fight and expelled. He resulting anger and confusion was something William took out on Oliver for never being around and all the bad things that happened since Adrian Chase and Ricardo Diaz. Believing Oliver couldn't understand him, William called his grandparents; believing they could give him a normal life. After Stanley Dover nearly killed him, Oliver agreed to send him away for William's own safety.

Anti-Monitor Crisis

During the Anti-Monitor Crisis , William as well as everyone in the multiverse except for the seven Paragons , was killed in an antimatter wave by the Anti-Monitor on December 10, 2019, only to be restored a month later, after the Paragons and the Spectre created a new universe .

New multiverse

Early life

William Clayton was the son of Oliver Queen and Samantha Clayton . History for his early life remains mostly the same; William's hometown was Central City and he still moved back there to live with his maternal grandparents for a time prior to Oliver's death. In late-2019, his father, Oliver Queen, sacrificed himself for the sake of the multiverse in the Crisis, and to ensure the future of his family, William included. William also got to know his maternal grandmother, Moira Queen, as she was never killed in the new timeline of Earth-Prime, and his other paternal aunt besides Thea, Emiko Queen.

Instead of losing contact with his family, William grew up together with his half-sister, and the two became very close. Mia claimed that William always tolerated and loved her despite mischief she got into or committed. At some point, Felcitity had a tracking device implanted within his arm in case he fell into danger.

Kidnapped by John Byrne

A day before his father's funeral, William is kidnapped by John Byrne as revenge against Oliver for sending him to prison 7 years earlier. He is almost thrown off the roof of a building but is saved by a female archer . Later he is in the bunker where his future half-sister (unknown to him) shows him her bow. He is present during the unveiling of the Green Arrow statue and his father's funeral.

New future

At some point in or before 2040, William joins Smoak Tech and helps his step-mother, Felicity run the company.

William shows up to Mia's college graduation and her engagement party with Zoe to congratulate Mia on her engagement with JJ while revealing he broke up with his boyfriend, Keven , since he did not seem to understand that William wanted a monogamous romance. Later on, during a discussion where William mocked that he was considered a Green Arrow successor, William was kidnapped by a mysterious group. Since he remained missing for two years, Mia travelled back to 2021 and worked with Team Flash in order to save him.

Erased future

In 2019, William and Felicity lost touch after Oliver died.

Arriving on Lian Yu and finding Roy Harper

At some point in 2040, William sought the help of a Chinese fisherman to sail to Lian Yu searching for Roy Harper . While venturing on the island, he was caught in a trap and interrogated by Roy, before revealing that he is Oliver Queen 's son. While speaking to Roy, William mentions that his ex-boyfriend wonders why he has commitment issues.

He and Roy later uncover Oliver's Green Arrow equipment buried on Lian Yu, which came with a note telling both of them to return to Star City.

Personality

As a child, William is kind, caring and polite, like his father, Oliver Queen , he is a big fan of baseball, he is a huge fan of Green Arrow and the Flash respectively, he also took an immediate liking to Oliver when they first met; prior to him discovering that the latter is his father, following his kidnapping by Adrian Chase and the death of his mother, Samantha Clayton , initially William had become emotionally traumatized and distant, he suffers frequent nightmares about Adrian and his mother's death, but puts up a tough façade around Oliver, however, when his father is not around, he broke down crying. These problems of William's are symptomatic of Post-Traumatic Stress Disorder, fitting, considering the experiences he endured.

Initially, William blamed Oliver for what happened to him and his mother, Samantha, due to this, despite knowing that Oliver is his father, he referred to him by his name and mostly ignores him as he display signs of anger whenever he talks to his father, calling him the "bad man", William also refused to talk to Oliver about his problems in school, he eventually forgives Oliver for what happened to him and Samantha, knowing that his father did his best to protect them from the individual who was truly responsible for his mother's death, according to Raisa ; William has difficulty fitting in - much like Oliver did at his age. William has also come to despise Oliver's vigilante career; fearing for his father's life.

However, eventually, William warms up to Oliver and Felicity Smoak , bonding with them respectively, by Thanksgiving, he starts calling Oliver "dad", when his paternal aunt, Thea Queen came out of her coma, he called her "auntie Thea", despite the fact that he has not formed a bond with her, upon getting to know his aunt, William asks her if he is allowed to drink champagne at his dad and Felicity's wedding reception, when William tells his father, Thea reassures her brother she told William he can have a sip, but only during the toasts. He comes to love his step-mother, Felicity and feels he can talk to her when he can't talk to his father.

Abilities

Original multiverse

  • Genius-level Intellect: William is highly intelligent for his age; William is also top of his class, as he excels at school work far better than his father, Oliver Queen did at his age. Felicity Smoak has stated that William displays prodigy-level skills in mathematics.
    • Programming/Computer skills: While not as skilled as Felicity , William was able to make a very convincing (and very fake) version of his school's website. He has some knowledge of malware and firewall systems, as stated when he found a breach and a virus on his tablet.
  • Culinary skills: William is a capable cook; as he made cookies once.
  • Skilled hand-to-hand combatant: Oliver has taught William how to defend himself; giving him insight on how to quickly incapacitate large bullies.

Erased future abilities

  • Genius-level intellect/Great business acumen: In an erased future, William proclaimed to his half-sister, Mia Queen , that he is indeed a genius.
    • Computer specialist/Master computer hacker: In an erased future, William was able to hack a police communications channel to distract their focus while he and Mia infiltrated the Star City black market.
    • Bilingualism: In an erased future, William is capable of fluently speaking English and Russian .
    • Skilled Hand to hand combatant: Oliver trained William to defend himself when he 13.
    • Culinary skills: William is a capable cook; as he made cookies once.

Weaknesses

  • Gulibility: William is a very bright student, but has proven to be very gullible, having been kidnapped by Malcolm Merlyn in his house for Damien Darhk under the pretense his mother asked him to watch over him not knowing the individual or being told by his mother it was happening and again while in hiding from being kidnapped under the alias "Matthew", Adrian Chase confronted him while leaving the bus calling him William and talked to him after believing he knew his mom or dad.

Trivia

  • William is a fan of the Flash , as he owns an action figure of the superhero and also has posters from " Flash Day " in his room.
    • After being saved from Damien Darhk by Green Arrow , William stated he is "kind of over the Flash", preferring his rescuer instead.
  • In an erased timeline, William, presumably along with everyone else in Central City , was killed by Vandal Savage using the Staff of Horus in his efforts to kill Carter Hall and Kendra Saunders . However, due to Barry Allen changing the time continuum, this disaster never occurs.
  • According to Malcolm Merlyn , William is the person that Oliver Queen loves and cares about most in the world, even more so than Thea Queen and Felicity Smoak .
  • Like his father, William loves baseball and was a fan of the Central City Diamonds . After moving to Star City , he became a fan of the Starling City Rockets .
  • William likes his eggs scrambled.
  • His favorite ice cream is rocky road.
  • In a possible future, William doesn't drink alcohol, but he'll make an exception if it's been a "tough week".
  • Felicity and Zoe both call him "Will".
  • William is gay, which both Oliver and Felicity were aware and accepting of. The couple wanted William to tell them himself, but he never got the chance until he traveled back in time and saw his father again.
  • William has been kidnapped three times in the series, all of which were connected to his father.
    • In " Code of Silence ", he was kidnapped by Malcolm (under the orders of Damien Darhk ) to blackmail Oliver into stepping down from his mayoral campaign.
    • Between " Underneath " and " Missing ", he was kidnapped by Adrian Chase in his plan to make Oliver suffer and force the latter to kill him on Lian Yu .
    • In " Fadeout ", he was kidnapped by John Byrne to get revenge on Oliver for arresting him.
  • William went to a boarding school in Cambridge for a while but got expelled after punching a kid in the nose

Behind the scenes

  • In the DC comics, Oliver has two sons; one named Robert Queen II (who was named in honor of his late paternal grandfather) and Connor Hawke . In the Arrowverse , his name is William instead of Robert or Connor.
    • In the Arrowverse , Connor is Ben Turner 's son. In addition, the name was used as the alias for the John Diggle, Jr. of Earth-16 .
    • William's name and him being the son of Green Arrow is similar to the character of William King/White Feather , who was the son of Green Arrow's Earth-12 counterpart.
  • William was originally credited as "Young Boy" in his first on-screen appearance in part one of the Heroes Join Forces crossover " Legends of Today ". His full name wasn't revealed until part two of the crossover.
  • The flashbacks in " Seeing Red "—when Samantha is pregnant with William—are set in 2007, implying that he was born in 2007. However, this was later retconned and for the rest of the series, William is stated to have been born in 2006.
  • The character has the same name Colton Haynes ' late real-life father: William Clayton.
