Sebastian Blood (1977 - May 2014) was the mayor of Starling City and an alderman, who rose to power following The Undertaking . Sebastian was a devoted alderman whose desire was to help people and dedicated himself to restoring Starling City following the Undertaking and allied himself with Slade Wilson to do so. As Slade's third-in-command and the leader of the Church of Blood , Sebastian, given the name Brother Blood , was to acquire an army of Mirakuru soldiers to cause enough destruction in Slade's name to enforce a new era where he would guide the people of the city. However, when Sebastian became mayor, Slade betrayed him and decided to lay waste to the city completely, finishing with an airstrike from A.R.G.U.S. . Feeling betrayed that the original plan having been changed, Sebastian stole the Mirakuru cure and gave it to Oliver Queen . Shortly after, he was killed by Isabel Rochev .

Biography

Early life

Sebastian Blood was born and raised in the crime-riddled neighborhood in Starling City known as the Glades . His father, Sebastian Sangre , was an abusive alcoholic who would often beat Sebastian and his submissive mother, Maya Resik . Sebastian was terrified of his father, so much so that he would often have nightmares in which he envisioned the man in the image of a skull. At some point, Sebastian befriended a teenager named Cyrus Gold and his mentor, Father Trigon .

One day on May 15, 1986 when Sebastian was nine years old, his father came home extremely enraged and threatened to kill Sebastian and his mother both with a gun. Two men in masks, who were actually Cyrus and Trigon disguised as the Acolyte and the Devil, broke in and fought Sebastian's father. Sebastian got a hold of his father's gun and, at Trigon's suggestion, shot him dead, yet let his mother live. When the police arrived, Maya received the blame for her husband's death and was arrested, eventually sent to St. Walker's Hospital . Sebastian was then placed in Zandia Orphanage .

At the orphanage, Sebastian met with Cyrus, now a preacher, who he began to view as an older brother-like figure. One day when Sebastian couldn't take the nightmares of his father, he created a skull mask made the same way he saw his father in his dreams, as a means of conquering his fears and to remind himself to never give up. The mask was placed upon him by Trigon in a ceremony, where Sebastian officially became a member of the Church of Blood as Brother Blood, swearing to protect the poorest of the city and the orphans of Zandia, no matter of the cost.

Working with Deathstroke

At some point in time, Sebastian met the mercenary Slade Wilson/Deathstroke , who offered to make him the mayor of Starling City. Seeing that the city needed to be saved, Sebastian agreed and began his mayoral campaign after the Undertaking occurred. His campaign drew a large following as people branded "Blood for mayor" on the walls, as Mayor Altman 's reputation had been tarnished after the Undertaking. Slade also promised him an army of soldiers , strength enhanced by Mirakuru within Slade's blood, to lay waste to Starling City and further support his following. Sebastian re-organized the Church of Blood to re-develop the serum from Slade's blood and test it on unwilling Glades residents found through a blood drive. His followers included Cyrus, Officer Daily and other Zandia orphans. Despite Sebastian becoming the new leader of the cult after Roger Trigon 's death, Slade became the man behind the man, always having some degree of power over him, making Sebastian cower in his presence. Sebastian also became associated with Slade's right-hand woman Isabel Rochev/Ravager .

Legacy

Oliver used Sebastian as an example when talking to Roy Harper about crucibles, like Sebastian once told him. Oliver referred to him as "a friend", implying that he forgave Sebastian in the end.

Oliver and Team Arrow were able to defeat Slade Wilson and his army with the Mirakuru cure , thus ensuring that Sebastian's death was not in vain. During the battle, Isabel Rochev was killed by Nyssa al Ghul , avenging Sebastian's death.

Clinton Hogue succeeded Sebastian as Brother Blood.

Sometime after Sebastian's death, Celia Castle succeeded him as mayor of Starling City . It can be assumed that Sebastian's involvement in the Siege was exposed to the public, albeit he did not know that Slade was planning to destroy Starling City .

About a year and a half after Sebastian's death, Oliver himself ran for mayor of Star City using Sebastian's old campaign office. Sebastian's lair underneath the office was converted into the new Arrowcave .

Personality

Due to the abusiveness of his father, Sebastian was a scared, frightened child but used that fear as motivation to fix his home city at any personal cost but his childhood trauma clearly damaged him as it drove him to megalomania. Sebastian was shown to be charismatic and commanding, being able to win over crowds so large they wanted him to become mayor and branded his name on walls. Though he initially resented Oliver due to his family's involvement in the Undertaking, sometime later he apologized for once it turned into a riot, and he slowly began to form a friendship with the billionaire as they both wanted to fix Starling City. He also became close friends with, Laurel Lance , though she always thought something wasn't right about him (it was later revealed that the only reason why he became friends with Laurel, was because Slade Wilson ordered him to get close to the latter in order to hurt Oliver Queen ; therefore, he did not truly care about Laurel). However, he was also a cult leader who was a ruthless, cold, sadistic and megalomaniac to his subordinates, one who cared little for body counts to further his own needs. He also used his own personal skull mask as a means of intimidation and inspiration among his followers.

Sebastian's plan was to use an army of Mirakuru soldiers to worsen the already broken state of the city, to cause enough damage to make the whole city suffer, so when ready Sebastian would command the people to rebuild Starling City into a better city, one stronger, with no economic unbalance or poverty. Upon being elected mayor, it became ever more apparent that he was a megalomaniac, truly believing that his plan was what's best for Starling City. However, ultimately he truly desired to help the people of Starling City and upon realizing Slade would destroy the city and kill everyone in it, Sebastian immediately betrayed Slade by giving the Mirakuru cure to Oliver, expressing remorse for what he did to Oliver as he confessed his childhood trauma and stressed his desire to help people, regretful of how he ended up causing Starling City to be on the verge of destruction. Ultimately achieving at least a measure of redemption, when he was killed by Isabel Rochev , Sebastian declared his love for Starling with his last breath, confirming that despite his obvious megalomania, he truly had Starling City's future at heart. His act of selflessness lead to Oliver's victory over Slade. Oliver himself acknowledged this as after his death, he referred to Sebastian as a "friend", showing Sebastian had managed to earn Oliver's forgiveness in the end.

Abilities

  • High-level intellect/Business acumen/Skiled tactician/Leader: Sebastian was able to become a member of the Church of Blood and mayor of Starling City . He proved to be an effective leader and politician, having a large following in Starling City who wanted him to become mayor. He even helped Slade Wilson plan the Siege . Sebastian also managed to get away with killing both his mother and father , even claiming his mother to be a mentally ill aunt of his.
  • Intimidation: As a secret cult member, Sebastian was evil and ruthless. He often had criminals captured and injected them with Mirakuru . If they survive the initial injection, Sebastian forced them to work for him.
  • Skilled marksmanship : Even as a child, Sebastian was able to handle a gun given to him by Father Trigon to slay his abusive father .

Equipment

  • Brother Blood mask : Sebastian wore a skull mask as his villain alter-ego, Brother Blood, to hide his identity from his victims.
  • Voice filter: Sebastian had this device fitted in to the mask, and spoke to anyone using it when he wore the mask.
