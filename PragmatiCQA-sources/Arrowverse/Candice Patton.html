Candice Kristina Patton (born June 24, 1988) is an American actress. She portrays Iris West-Allen , Iris West-Allen (Earth-2) , Millie Foss , Iris West-Allen (Mirrorverse) , and Hannibal Bates and H.R. Wells as Iris in The Flash . She has also portrayed Iris West-Allen in Supergirl , Arrow , DC's Legends of Tomorrow , and Batwoman .
