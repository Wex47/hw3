The United States of America ( USA ), commonly referred to as the United States ( US ) or America , was a country located on the continent of North America of the Earth of Earth-1 . During the times of the Civil War , it was referred to as The Union , with its southern separatists calling themselves the Confederacy. The county ceased to exist along with the destruction of the rest of Earth-1 during the Anti-Monitor Crisis .

Overview

The United States was one of the great powers on the Earth of Earth-1 , and was one of the nations of this Earth possessing nuclear weaponry, along with Markovia , Corto Maltese and USA's own NATO allies. A.R.G.U.S. , a special ops devision in the United States, created Rubicon , an override protocol, for its ability to control all of the world's nuclear weaponry, as the organisation viewed them as one of the greatest influences on the politics of the world.

The United States Army was a powerful force on the world arena. They also had other global interests, such as those connected to the Alpha-Omega bioweapon and the rise of meta-humans .

The United States was home to a number of vigilantes and superheroes, such as The Flash , The Atom. , Vixen and Green Arrow , along with teams of their own. It was also a base of operations for the meta-human and enhanced criminals like the Reverse-Flash and Deathstroke , as well as powerful terrorist organizations like H.I.V.E. and Shadowspire .
