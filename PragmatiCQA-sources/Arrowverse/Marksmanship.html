Marksmanship

Information

Function

Weaponry

Methods of use

Guns

Marksmanship refers to the skills of a marksman, markswoman or sharpshooter, this being a person who is skilled in precision-based shooting, using projectile weapons, usually with a rifle, but most commonly with a designated marksman rifle or a sniper rifle, to shoot at long range targets. Notable marksmen/markswomen shown to be extremely proficient include Slade Wilson , Billy Wintergreen , Maseo Yamashiro , Chien Na Wei , John Diggle , Lyla Michaels , Floyd Lawton , Ricardo Diaz , and Oliver Queen .
