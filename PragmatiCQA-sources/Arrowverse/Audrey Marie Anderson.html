Audrey Marie Anderson (born March 7, 1975) is an American actress and model. She has portrayed Lyla Michaels/Harbinger in Arrow , The Flash , Supergirl , Batwoman , and DC's Legends of Tomorrow . She has also portrayed Barry Allen and Mobius/Anti-Monitor as Lyla in The Flash and voiced Prometheus as Lyla in Arrow .

Trivia

  • Anderson, alongside series regulars Stephen Amell , Katie Cassidy , David Ramsey , Willa Holland , Emily Bett Rickards , and Paul Blackthorne , are the only actors who appear in every season of Arrow .
    • She is also the only one out of the six to appear in every season and not be a series regular.
