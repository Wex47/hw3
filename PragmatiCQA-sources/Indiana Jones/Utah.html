Adventures in Utah

Indiana Jones lived in Utah with his father in 1912 . While there, he was a member of the Boy Scouts of America . On one outing with his troop he ran into a team of looters digging up the Cross of Coronado , and attempted to steal it back from them, but was unsuccessful.

In 1928, Jones returned to Utah, where he met Oscar "Smitty" Smithers and his wife Rosie .
