Behind the scenes

The real Ernest Hemingway arrived in Italy as an ambulance driver in June 1918 (and injured on July 8, 1918) as correctly shown in the " Northern Italy, June 1918 ". However the episode was edited to become the first part of Tales of Innocence , dated 1917 .
