" Barcelona, May 1917 " is the tenth episode of The Young Indiana Jones Chronicles , and the fourth episode in season two. It originally aired on ABC on October 12, 1992 . In 1996 , it was combined with " Prague, August 1917 " to form Espionage Escapades , which was released on DVD in 2007 .

Plot

Indiana Jones bungles the opening to a speech for the Pennsylvania Historical Society , leading him to recall the many blunders he made as a spy in Barcelona .
