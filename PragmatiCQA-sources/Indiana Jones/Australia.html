Australia is a country that covers the continent of Australia and several nearby islands, located southeast of Asia between the Indian and Pacific Oceans . While the eastern coast of the country has many cities, much of the interior of the continent is a sparsely populated desert. Because of its history and relative geographic isolation, Australia is home to many unique plant and animal species.
