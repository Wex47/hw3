Lucasfilm Ltd. (LFL) is an American film production company founded by George Lucas in 1971 , based in Marin County, California .

The company was responsible for producing the four Indiana Jones movies as well as the The Young Indiana Jones Chronicles television series.

In 2012 , the company was sold by Lucas to The Walt Disney Company .
