Adventures in Nevada

In 1957 , Soviet soldiers lead an assault on Hanger 51 in the deserts of Nevada. There, they threatened famous archaeologist Indiana Jones into stealing the remains of an alien being for them.

After Jones escaped the Soviets, he found himself in Doom Town where a nuclear test was about to start. Jones barely escaped by hiding in a lead-lined King Cool refrigerator .

After his escape, Jones was interrogated by FBI Agents who suspected him to cooperate with the Soviets.

Behind the scenes

The outdoor scenes which are indicated in the movie as being in Nevade were not filmed in Nevada, but in New Mexico where similar deserts as in Nevada can be found. The scenes first shown in the movie also were the first scenes filmed.
