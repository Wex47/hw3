Lhasa (capital)

Tibet is a country located in the Himalayan mountains, in central Asia. It has been an independent nation ruled by the Dalai Lama for centuries. Since 1950, it has been under the Chinese rule, and is widely known for its mountains, monks, yaks, snow leopards and other furry creatures .
