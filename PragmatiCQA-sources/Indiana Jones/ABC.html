The American Broadcasting Company ( ABC ) is one of the major television networks in the United States of America . It was founded on May 15, 1943 .

ABC was the network that originally aired the first 24 episodes of The Young Indiana Jones Chronicles from 1992 to 1993, and debuted the first trailer for Indiana Jones and the Kingdom of the Crystal Skull on its morning show Good Morning America on February 14, 2008.
