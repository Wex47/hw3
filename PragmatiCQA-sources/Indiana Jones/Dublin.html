Adventures in Dublin

Indiana Jones and his friend Remy Baudouin found themselves in Dublin in 1916 while raising money to get to London and enlist in the Belgian Army during World War I . During his enforced stopover, he met Sean Lemass while trying to buy the affection of Lemass's sister, Maggie , and witnessed the Easter Rebellion first hand. By 1943 , Jones had become a professor and had received teaching offers from within Dublin.
