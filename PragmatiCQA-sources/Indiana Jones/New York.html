New York is one of the United States of America , located in the northeastern region of the country, and touching the Atlantic Ocean . Its largest city is New York City , located on the coast, at the mouth of the Hudson River. The upstate area is home to Barnett College .

Indiana Jones regularly visited the area in and around New York City during his lifetime and was still living there by the early 1990s.
