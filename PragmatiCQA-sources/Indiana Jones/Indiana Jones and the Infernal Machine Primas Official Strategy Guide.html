Indiana Jones and the Infernal Machine: Prima's Official Strategy Guide is a companion guide to the video game of the same title , written by Jo Ashburn and Rick Barba .

In addition to the solutions it presents, the book is notable for its introduction, which describes a short episode immediately preceding the events of the game: As Indiana Jones prepares to depart Barnett College , Henry Jones, Sr who will be looking after his son's classes while he is away warns Indy about "those godless Communists."
