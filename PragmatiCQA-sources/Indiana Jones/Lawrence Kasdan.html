Lawrence Kasdan wrote the screenplay for Raiders of the Lost Ark in 1981, and the Star Wars films The Empire Strikes Back and Return of the Jedi . He returned to the latter franchise with The Force Awakens and Solo: A Star Wars Story .

On December 30, 1997, the website Ain't It Cool News sparked the rumor that Kasdan was again hired by Steven Spielberg and George Lucas to write the the fourth Indiana Jones film. However, the rumor was later revealed as false.
