The Himalayas are a mountain range in central Asia , containing some of the highest peaks on Earth including the world's highest Everest . Running east-west, the range forms the border between India and Tibet , with the nations of Nepal and Bhutan sandwiched in between.

Indiana Jones , Short Round and Wu Han were once set upon by nomadic thieves when they journeyed through the range during a yak ride, and later Jones and Sophia Hapgood discovered the lost city of Chanri-Ha there in 1938 .
