Once the cradle of Genghis Khan 's empire (one of the biggest in human history) Mongolia became a communist country subjected to the full influence of the Soviet Union . In the 1920s an American expedition uncovered the biggest deposits of dinosaur eggs in Mongolia's Gobi Desert .
