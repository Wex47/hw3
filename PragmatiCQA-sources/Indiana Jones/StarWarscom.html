StarWars.com is the official site of the Star Wars saga.

StarWars.com has occasionally posted Indiana Jones related news. Since the redesign of IndianaJones.com to focus on Indiana Jones and the Kingdom of the Crystal Skull , StarWars.com has been the main source for official online Indy content not relating to the new film.
