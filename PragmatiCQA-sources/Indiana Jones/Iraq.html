Adventures in Iraq

Indiana Jones and René Emile Belloq first met on an expedition in Iraq while studying at the Sorbonne in 1922 .

In 1934 , Indiana Jones recovered the Staff of Aaron from the Yezedi , a satanic cult active in the country who had come into possession of the relic some time after its disappearance from the Temple of Solomon .

Two years later, Jones and Marion Ravenwood 's flight to Egypt from Nepal included a stop over in Baghdad .
