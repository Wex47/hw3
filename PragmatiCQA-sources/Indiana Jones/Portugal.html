Adventures in Portugal

In 1938 , Indiana Jones tracked down Panama Hat to Lisbon in search of the Cross of Coronado , stolen by Fedora's Gang to Panama Hat in 1912 Utah , USA .

It was off the Portuguese coast that Jones finally reclaimed the artifact about which he was adamant should be in a museum.

Later that year, he and Marcus Brody 's flight to Venice , Italy included a stopover in Lisbon.
