Austria-Hungary , also known as the Austro-Hungarian Empire or the Austro-Hungarian Monarchy, and described as the Dual Monarchy or the k.u.k. Monarchy, was a monarchic union between the crowns of the Austrian Empire and the Kingdom of Hungary in Central Europe.

Austria-Hungary was broken up 1918 following the secession of Hungary from Austria .
