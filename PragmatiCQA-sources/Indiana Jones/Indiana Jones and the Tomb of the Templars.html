Indiana Jones and the Tomb of the Templars is a supplement to West End Games ' World of Indiana Jones roleplaying game. It contains three original adventures:

  • Indiana Jones and the Tomb of the Templars
  • Indiana Jones and the Druids' Curse
  • Indiana Jones and the Sword in the Stone

Indiana Jones himself appears briefly in the first two stories only, where he introduces the quest by asking for the player characters' help. The book's introductory teaser story, however, focuses on Indy, as he attempts to recover the Ring of the Nibelung from beneath Neuschwanstein Castle .

Publisher's summary

Danger, Death and Double-Cross...

When the Knights Templar fled France in the 14th century, they took their treasure with them. A treasure so powerful — so deadly — so evil that it was locked away from the sight of mankind. Now a race has begun to find this mysterious treasure before it falls into the wrong hands.

But finding it will be the easy part. Surviving it is the problem.

And all of this is only the beginning — this first adventure/source material collection for The World of Indiana Jones also contains Indiana Jones and the Druids' Curse and Indiana Jones and the Sword in the Stone. Three harrowing, action-packed adventures to satisfy your appetite for thrills!
