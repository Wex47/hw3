A dog is a four legged domesticated animal.

Indiana Jones , himself nicknamed after his pet dog, an Alaskan Malamute named Indiana , encountered various different breeds in his travels around the globe.

Adventures with dogs

In 1936 , Indiana Jones used dogs to pull a sled across the northern coast of Siberia in his race to find the Tomb of the Gods before the Nazis .
