The Young Indiana Jones Chronicles comic is the Dark Horse Comics adaptation of the television series of the same name . The comic scripts were written by Dan Barry , while different artists were used through the run of the comic. Twelve issues total were published in monthly installments from February 1992 to February 1993 (with a hiatus in November 1992):

  • Issue 1 (February 1992): " Egypt, May 1908 "
  • Issue 2 (March 1992): " Mexico, March 1916 "
  • Issue 3 (April 1992): " British East Africa, September 1909 " (Part 1)
  • Issue 4 (May 1992): " British East Africa, September 1909 " (Part 2)
  • Issue 5 (June 1992): " Verdun, September 1916 " (Part 1)
  • Issue 6 (July 1992): " Verdun, September 1916 " (Part 2)
  • Issue 7 (August 1992): " German East Africa, December 1916 "
  • Issue 8 (September 1992): " German East Africa, January 1917 "
  • Issue 9 (October 1992): " Vienna, November 1908 / March 1917 "
  • Issue 10 (December 1992): " Vienna, March 1917 "
  • Issue 11 (January 1993): " Peking, March 1910 " (Part 1)
  • Issue 12 (February 1993): " Peking, March 1910 " (Part 2)

Each issue included a four-page article written by Kurt Busiek about a real world connection to the history or themes presented in the issue.
