The Battle of the Somme took place in 1916 , during World War I , in which the armies of Britain and France clashed with the Imperial German Army at the Somme River .

Indiana Jones took part in the battle when the 9th Belgian Infantry was absorbed into the French 14th Company under Alain Moreau but was captured by enemy forces.
