Sweden is a Nordic country on the Scandinavian Peninsula in Northern Europe. It was home to the ancient Norse cultures. During the time of Indiana Jones , Sweden had lost control of Norway in 1905 , remained neutral during World War I , and was officially neutral (though under strong German influence) in World War II , though many Swedes contributed toward the defense of Finland from the Soviet Union, and nearly all of Denmark's Jews were able to escape to Sweden. Throughout the Cold War , Sweden continued a policy of neutrality.

Adventures in Sweden

Indiana Jones visited Stockholm in December, 1909 as part of his father's world lecture tour and met Selma Lagerlöf .

As a spy during World War I , Jones adopted the cover identity of Swedish reporter Nils Anderson in Istanbul , 1918 .

In 1930 , Indiana Jones met Theresa Lawrence in Sweden, while trying to stay ahead of Forrestal in the exploration of the Temple of Old Uppsala .
