Indiana Jones and the Iron Phoenix is a four-issue comic book written by Lee Marrs , with art by Leo Durañona . It was published by Dark Horse Comics in monthly installments from December 1994 to March 1995 .

The story was based on a video game sequel to Indiana Jones and the Fate of Atlantis in development at LucasArts . Though the game was never realized, the LucasArts staff is thanked in the credits of the comic.
