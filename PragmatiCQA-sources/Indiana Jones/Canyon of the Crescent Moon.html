The Canyon of the Crescent Moon was a crescent-shaped canyon in Hatay .

The resting place of the Holy Grail, the Temple of the Sun , was located inside the canyon. After a violent confrontation between Indiana Jones and a tank -equipped expedition of Nazis in 1938 , the canyon also became the final resting place of German Colonel Ernst Vogel .
