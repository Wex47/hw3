Adventures in Iceland

The Jastro expedition , on which Indiana Jones worked as field supervisor, took place in Iceland in 1929 where Sophia Hapgood recovered the necklace of Atlantean Nur-Ab-Sal . Dr. Bjorn Heimdall believed Iceland was what the Greeks refered to as Hyperborea .

Another expedition in 1932 saw Jones recover a death mask from Iceland, which was kept on display in his Marshall College campus apartment by 1957 .

Jones returned to the country in 1934 .
