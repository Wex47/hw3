Bantam Books is the publisher of twelve original Indiana Jones novels, set before the films:

  1. Indiana Jones and the Peril at Delphi (1991) by Rob MacGregor
  2. Indiana Jones and the Dance of the Giants (1991) by Rob MacGregor
  3. Indiana Jones and the Seven Veils (1991) by Rob MacGregor
  4. Indiana Jones and the Genesis Deluge (1992) by Rob MacGregor
  5. Indiana Jones and the Unicorn's Legacy (1992) by Rob MacGregor
  6. Indiana Jones and the Interior World (1992) by Rob MacGregor
  7. Indiana Jones and the Sky Pirates (1993) by Martin Caidin
  8. Indiana Jones and the White Witch (1994) by Martin Caidin
  9. Indiana Jones and the Philosopher's Stone (1995) by Max McCoy
  10. Indiana Jones and the Dinosaur Eggs (1996) by Max McCoy
  11. Indiana Jones and the Hollow Earth (1997) ) by Max McCoy
  12. Indiana Jones and the Secret of the Sphinx (1999) by Max McCoy

The first six of these were released under the company's Falcon imprint.

Bantam is also responsible for the Choose Your Own Adventure series of gamebooks, which includes eight titles adapted from episodes of The Young Indiana Jones Chronicles :

  1. The Valley of the Kings
  2. South of the Border
  3. Revolution in Russia
  4. Masters of the Louvre
  5. African Safari
  6. Behind the Great Wall
  7. The Roaring Twenties
  8. The Irish Rebellion
