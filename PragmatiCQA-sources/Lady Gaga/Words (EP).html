Words is a demo EP that was sold at The Bitter End in New York on January 20, 2006. The recording of the demo began in November 2005 when Lady Gaga was studying at New York University, and struggling to find a record deal.

Release

The demo was sold at The Bitter End in New York in January 20, 2006. It serves as a demo/concept for the Stefani Germanotta Band 's future EP, Red and Blue . The main difference is an alternate track listing order and an different versions of " No Floods ".

On January 27, 2010, there was an advertisement out on Ebay where the seller, created a copy of the demo, Words , one of the tracks being " Retro-Physical ". To prove the existence of the disc, the user uploaded a snippet of the song. Some hours later, after the mass hysteria, the item was taken down. The first bid on the item was over £450.00. The track listing on the EP did not match that of the original track listing, therefore, causing confusion as to what disc the seller had.
