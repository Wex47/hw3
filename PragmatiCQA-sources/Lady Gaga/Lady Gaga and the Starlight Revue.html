The Lady Gaga & The Starlight Revue was a low-fi tribute to 1970s variety acts which featured Lady Gaga on synth, Lady Starlight spinning beats, choreographed "go-go moves", shiny, and hairspray lit on fire and sprayed into the audience. They wanted to make a show they would be so jealous if they saw it.
