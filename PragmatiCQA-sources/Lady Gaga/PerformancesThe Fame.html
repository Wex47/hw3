The performances during The Fame era consisted of two promo concert tours as opening act and her first headliner and world tour with The Fame Ball. This was accompanied by performances on various shows across the world.

Gaga debuted the year with her promo club tour promoting "Just Dance" which consisted of many clubs in the United States and a few select venues around the world. Tours overview.

At the end of the era, Gaga had performed in many countries around the world.
