The Fame era

The first part of 2008 included light makeup with lipstick and eyeliner. She also had many variation of a lightning bolt on her face which was a tribute to David Bowie. She alterned with light natural pink, red, lavender and "Pink Nouveau" by MAC . Her nails had some bright color or simply black when they were not hidden by a pair of gloves.

Her makeup changed in October as she started working with Sharon Gault . The look consisted of pink cheeks and fake eyelashes by Shu Uemura .
