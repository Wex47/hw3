" Scheiße " (alternate titles: " Scheisse ", " ScheiBe ", and " Shiza ") is a song written in 2010 by Lady Gaga and produced by RedOne . Gaga and DJ White Shadow remixed a short portion of the song and premiered it on January 19 , 2011 , at the Anatomy of Change by Thierry Mugler show, although, Gaga purposely left out the chorus during this remix. The studio version of the song is featured on Born This Way . The title and parts of the song are in German. Scheiße means shit/'dammit in English. Parts of the lyrics were posted by Nicola Formichetti on Twitter . The song was registered on Gaga's BMI on March 18 , 2011 .

Background
