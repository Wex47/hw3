Twitter is an online social networking and microblogging service created in March 2006 by Jack Dorsey. Twitter enables its users to send and read messages called tweets that are limited to 280 characters.

Custom emoji and stickers

Hashflags (Emoji)

In February 2017, To celebrate her highly-anticipated performance at Super Bowl Halftime show, Lady Gaga launched her own emoji in collaboration with Twitter. The set included five custom emoji for each of the Gaga's albums that appeared when users tweeted their titles in the form of a hashtag, such as #TheFame or #BornThisWay . There were some hidden tags as well, including #DiscoStick , #CallMeJoanne , or #ShowMeYourTeeth .
