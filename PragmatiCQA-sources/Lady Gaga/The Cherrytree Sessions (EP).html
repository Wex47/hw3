The Cherrytree Sessions is the first official live EP from Lady Gaga . "The Cherrytree Sessions" are special performances by the artists of the Cherrytree Records label, inside the Cherrytree House. During the performance, Gaga performed the songs on the track-list of the EP, as well as " Brown Eyes ", and had an interview with Martin Kierszenbaum . Gaga performed a beatbox rendition of " Eh, Eh (Nothing Else I Can Say) ", with help from Vincent Herbert , Troy Carter , and Space Cowboy . It was the first, and only time this version has been performed.

Release

Starting January 31 , the EP was available for streaming on Gaga's official MySpace page. It was released digitally on February 3 , 2009 onto iTunes. The EP was scheduled to have a physical release exclusively at Borders on March 3 , 2009, but was moved up to February 24 . The EP was released with a limited print which sold out, with a later re-release of the EP available from August 3 , 2010 . Outside of the United States, the EP only got a digital release. The EP was released on November 29 , 2009 in Australia. Not all the songs on the EP were available for download in every country. In France, only the rendition of Eh, Eh (Nothing Else I Can Say) was available. Another official release of the EP comes from a deluxe edition of The Fame released in Germany on June 26 , 2009. The EP later got a wide distribution in the US on August 3, 2010 with The Remix . The EP was also included in The Singles on a disc with " Christmas Tree ".
