Orange Colored Sky

Nat King Cole

General information

Writer(s)

Milton DeLugg Willie Stein


" Orange Colored Sky " is a song written by Milton DeLugg and Willie Stein in 1950. The best known version of the song was recorded by Nat King Cole for his album The Nat King Cole Story in 1961.

Lady Gaga has covered the song several times. Her 2011 live performance during A Very Gaga Thanksgiving was also released on A Very Gaga Holiday EP on November 22, 2011.
