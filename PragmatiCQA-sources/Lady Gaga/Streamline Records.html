Streamline Records is an imprint of Interscope Records founded in 2007 by Vincent Herbert . In November 2007, Vincent has signed a joint-venture agreement with Interscope to promote his five acts. Under the terms of the agreement, Interscope will provide promotion, marketing and distribution for the multigenre label.

Discography

The only album not involved with Streamline Records is A Star Is Born (2018).
