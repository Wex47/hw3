Michael Tucker (born August 15, 1990), known professionally as BloodPop® , is an American musician, record producer, and songwriter. He has previously used the monikers Blood Diamonds, Blood, and Michael Diamond. Tucker is mostly known for his work with singer, songwriter, and producer Grimes, and on the Justin Bieber track "Sorry". Tucker worked with Gaga on Joanne and Chromatica .
