Red and Blue is the Stefani Germanotta Band 's first official EP. It was sold at The Bitter End on March 9, 2006. The lyrics were written by Lady Gaga , and was produced by Joe Vulpis . On December 17, 2005, they played several of the songs from the EP at The Lion's Den . It features female-vocal ballads with a glam-rock edge. Most songs have a very present piano and heavily feature, the voice of Lady Gaga. This piano-heavy musical style was notably absent from Lady Gaga's debut album, The Fame , except for the track " Brown Eyes ", but the motif resurfaced later in acoustic performances of " Poker Face " and " Paparazzi ".

Release

The CD did not have an official widespread distribution. A handful of copies were sold at The Bitter End in 2006. The CD is a follow-up/update to the Stefani Germanotta Band's previous EP, Words . The main difference is an alternate track listing order and different versions for " No Floods ".
None of the songs from the EP have been released as singles, or have been promoted, although " Wish You Were Here " entered some unofficial international charts from countries such as Romania, Poland, and Hungary in early 2009.

  • The song, " Something Crazy ", was played live by the SGBand under the title, "When You're Not Around".
  • Several songs from the EP were performed live by the band through the years of 2006 and early 2007.
