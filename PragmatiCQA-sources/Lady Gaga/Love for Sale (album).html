Love for Sale is the second and final collaborative album by Tony Bennett and Lady Gaga following Cheek to Cheek . It was released on October 1 , 2021 , through Interscope and Columbia Records. The album was recorded between 2018 and early 2020 , and consists of cover versions of jazz standards by Cole Porter, to whom the record is a tribute.

" I Get a Kick Out of You " was released as the album's lead single in tandem with it's official announcement on August 3 , 2021 , followed by two live performances called One Last Time: an Evening with Tony Bennett and Lady Gaga at Radio City Music Hall in New York City.

Background

Bennett and Gaga first met at the Robin Hood Foundation gala in New York City in 2011 . After the two later recorded together a rendition of " The Lady Is a Tramp " which appeared on Bennett's album Duets II , they began discussing plans for working on a jazz project. In September 2012 , Bennett confirmed to Rolling Stone that Gaga wanted to record a jazz album with him and there was a well-known composer associated with the project, and although not on a par with songwriters like George Gershwin or Cole Porter, he did have several hits to his name. In September 2014 , the duo released a collaborative jazz album titled Cheek to Cheek , which debuted at number one on the Billboard charts and won a Grammy Award for Best Traditional Pop Vocal Album.

The album was scheduled for release in the second quarter of 2021. However, the album was not released in the spring of 2021. There were no announcements about the postponement of the album's release in the spring of 2021. In the morning, on August 3 , 2021 , an advertisement with the artwork and title of the album, " Love for Sale ", and its available pre-order appeared on one of the billboards in Times Square in New York City. Later that day, the official artwork and tracklist, as well as information about the future pre-order of various sets and editions of the album became available online. Just a few hours later, it was officially announced via Lady Gaga's Twitter and Instagram accounts that any version of the album and new merch became available for pre-order. It was also announced via Lady Gaga's social networks that "I Get a Kick Out Of You" is the first single from Love for Sale and an audio version of this song has become available on Lady Gaga's YouTube channel and on streaming services (Apple Music, Spotify).

Recording

On August 3 , 2021 , Lady Gaga revealed via her Twitter and Instagram accounts that the idea of making a new album, which became Love for Sale in the future, began with a phone call from Tony Bennett to Lady Gaga on the day when Cheek to Cheek was released:

“ The day we released "Cheek To Cheek" in 2014, @itstonybennett [Tony Bennett] called me and asked me if I wanted to record another album with him, this time celebrating the songs of Cole Porter. I'm always honored to sing with my friend Tony, so of course I accepted the invitation. ”

— Lady Gaga via Twitter / Instagram

In 2015 Bennett has revealed to Vulture that he and Lady Gaga are working on a follow up to Cheek to Cheek :

“ Yeah, we’re doing an album. It’s going to be all Cole Porter songs. He was the best writer of all of them in the Great American Songbook. Highly intelligent. We’re going to have a lot of fun doing that. ”

— Tony Bennett to Vulture

Three years later, the duo were seen at Electric Lady Studios in May of 2018. In February 2021 , an article in AARP Magazine revealed that Bennett was diagnosed with Alzheimer's disease in 2016 , though he continued to perform and record. Despite being diagnosed over four years ago, the article confirms second collaborative album of jazz standards. The songs were recorded in " widely spaced sessions " between 2018 and early 2020 at New York's Electric Lady Studios , between Gaga's promotional tour for A Star Is Born and the making of her sixth studio album, Chromatica .

Thanks Notes

“

I want to thank Danny and Dae and my family and everyone involved in making this recording. Thank you to my beautiful wife Susan - it's all for you.

Singing for me has always been about love... love for the music and lyrics of these songs and sharing them with people around the world. And love for the talented musicians I have worked with - and for one of the absolute best... Lady Gaga.

”

—Tony Bennett

“

Dear Tony, I wish to thank you and you only for this album. You called me once a week, for many weeks, advocating for the importance of honoring Cole Porter as one of the greatest songwriters in American history-you said we should make an entire record of his music. We then made this album together as we promised each other. We made it with your family, the Benedettos, who embraced me as family-for this I am forever grateful. Your passion, your talent, your gift has shined bright upon the world your whole life. I am deeply grateful to have witnessed, stood next to, and sang along a glimmer of your soul- your commitment to people, to racial justice, to your family and to your friends. You are my teacher, my friend, my partner in music. I love and cherish you. Thank you for all you have shared with me, I will carry it forever and someday will do for someone what you've done for me. Whenever I'm down I remember what you always say: Straight Ahead. You remind me to keep going. I will. We all will. Thank you Tony, your legacy is of a legend that has blessed the world for all of time.

Love,

Lady

”

—Lady Gaga
