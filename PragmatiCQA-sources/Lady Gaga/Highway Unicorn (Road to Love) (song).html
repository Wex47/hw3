" Highway Unicorn (Road to love) " is a song by Lady Gaga from the album, Born This Way . The song title was revealed through Gaga's Twitter right before the release of the Born This Way album artwork on April 16 , 2011 . The song was registered on her BMI on May 17 , 2011 .

Background

NME reported that the song will have " a soaring melody, nods to Springsteen and an industrial techno production represent the blueprint for ‘Born This Way’. An unicorn is mentioned. "
Gaga also said that " The sax in 'Highway Unicorn (Road to Love)' and in ‘ Hair ’ and in ‘ Edge Of Glory ’ – is not just there for no reason. [She] grew up listening to much Springsteen and Billy Joel, so in a way, it represents [her] innocence. "

Gaga revealed on Twitter that the song was about herself, traveling on the road with nothing but a dream.
