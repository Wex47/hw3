" Cut The Cake " is a song written by Lady Gaga and Rob Fusari ( Team Love Child ) sometime between 2006-2007. The song title was revealed through Fusari's personal Twitter account. The song is not registered on any music service. The title of the song is mentioned in " We Are Plastic ".
