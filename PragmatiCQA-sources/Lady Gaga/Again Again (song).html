" Again Again " is a song written in 2006 by Lady Gaga and Rob Fusari ( Team Love Child ). The clean version was first released on select pressing of The Fame (2008), and as a bonus track on select deluxe editions of The Fame Monster (2009). Lady Gaga played the song multiple times in New York, in 2006, with the original lyrics. One performance was held at the Knitting Factory . The song, " Brown Eyes " is referenced in the lyrics.

Writing and inspiration

Inspired by John Lennon, after hearing one of his final interviews, Gaga calls "Again Again" a tribute to The Beatles' song, "Oh Darlin".

Release

The clean version was first released on the Canadian pressing of The Fame (2008) and later on the UK, Irish, and Japanese editions of The Fame and the UK and Japanese deluxe edition of The Fame Monster (2009) as a bonus track. However, it was omitted from the Standard edition of The Fame (2008).
