Bobby Campbell (born May 27, 1985) is an American music manager from West Hollywood, California. In 2007 to 2010, he worked in marketing at Interscope Records where he met and worked with Lady Gaga. He joined Atom Factory in April of 2010 until November of 2013 as Chief Marketing Officer on artist management including Gaga. Since then, Bobby is the manager of Lady Gaga for the Haus of Gaga . He and Gaga signed to Artist Nation in June of 2014.
