" On a Clear Day (You Can See Forever) " is a song written by Burton Lane and Alan Jay Lerner from the musical of the same name, which concers a woman who has ESP and has been reincarnated.

The song was recorded by Tony Bennett as a solo song and available as the bonus track on the Target deluxe edition and International physical deluxe edition of his and Lady Gaga 's jazz collaborative album, Cheek to Cheek .
