The Singles is a limited edition compilation box set of 9 CDs by Lady Gaga containing all of her physically released singles from " Just Dance " to " Alejandro " it also includes The Cherrytree Sessions with " Christmas Tree " as a bonus and fourth track.

The set was released exclusively in Japan on December 7 , 2010 , and had a limited edition of 5000 copies. The artwork of the CD are reproduction of previously issued singles. Every CD in the box is housed in a slimline jewel case with accompanying English and Japanese lyric booklets, the original singles did not have any lyrics printed. The Cherrytree Sessions CD is the only one with no lyrics. The CDs in the box do not have barcodes, as it is printed on the outside of the box instead.
