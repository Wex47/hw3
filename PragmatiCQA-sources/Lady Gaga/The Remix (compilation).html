The Remix was a Japanese exclusive compilation , later released worldwide by Lady Gaga . It includes remixes of tracks from her previous albums, The Fame and The Fame Monster . The compilation sold around half a million copies worldwide and 141,000 copies in the United States alone. It is the tenth best-selling remix compilation sold worldwide ever, tied with Nine Inch Nails' Further Down The Spiral .
