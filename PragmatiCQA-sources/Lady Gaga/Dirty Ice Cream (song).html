" Dirty Ice Cream " is a song written by Lady Gaga and Rob Fusari ( Team Love Child ) in 2006. The song was posted on Gaga's MySpace on April 21, 2007, however, it was removed sometime before 2008. In 2007, Gaga performed the song live at Lollapalooza , alongside " Disco Heaven ", " Summerboy ", and " Blueberry Kisses ". The line "stop calling" was later reused in the song, " Telephone ".
