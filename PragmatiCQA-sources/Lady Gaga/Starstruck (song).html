" Starstruck " is a song written by Lady Gaga , Space Cowboy , and Martin Kierszenbaum , with a rap verse by Flo Rida . This was one of the last tracks to be recorded for The Fame , in 2008 . The song features vocals by Flo Rida and Space Cowboy and was first released on the US version of the album.

Backdrop and promotion

During the US promotion of Lady Gaga 's debut album, The Fame , a short video was made by the Haus of Gaga in order to have a third video in the promotion. A full version of the video was used as a backdrop for New Kids on the Block: Live . It was used as the theme in the performance. The video features Gaga with her straight, blonde wig, and a star over her right eye. She is wearing regular black sunglasses with glitter falling around. Stars move as a tunnel and three cherries fall onto the screen at the beginning like a slot machine.

A short clip of the video was used in the US promotion commercial for The Fame .
