The HitMixes is the limited edition second remix EP by Lady Gaga , made and sold exclusively in Canada. It was released on August 25 , 2009 . The cover of the EP uses similar artwork to LoveGame (The Remixes) , the only difference being the text. The physical packaging is made from paper and each song is a remix from The Fame . The album peaked at number 8 on the Canadian Albums chart.
