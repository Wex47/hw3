The Haus (intro) is the second introduction video used by Lady Gaga during her live performance. This interlude had only audio at the beginning around May of 2008. The video was shot on October 1st, 2008. The Haus Introduction was used on every performance until The Monster Ball Tour in 2009.

Description

The introduction used a sample of "Devastated" by Space Cowboy with spoken vocals by Lady Gaga. The song was released on his album Digital Rock Star in 2009.

Countdown

Usually before the Haus Introduction, a countdown is shown with various videos used including The Fame: Part One and the behind the scene of one of the photo shoot for the album. This video was shown after The Heart .

Video

The first version had only Gaga wearing a black hood paired with her commonly worn . The second version had various mannequin graphics cut between the old video and was used during The Fame Ball Tour .
