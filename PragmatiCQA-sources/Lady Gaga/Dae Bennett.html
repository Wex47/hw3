Dae Bennett (born 1955, The Bronx, New York, USA) is an engineer, producer and owner of the Bennett Studios . He is son of Tony Bennett .

Work with Lady Gaga

He worked with Lady Gaga on the following song(s):

  • " The Lady Is a Tramp " ( 2011 )
  • Cheek to Cheek ( 2014 )
  • Love for Sale ( 2021 )
