" Come To Mama " is a song by Lady Gaga made for her fifth studio album, Joanne . The song was written by Gaga, with co-writing credits from Josh Tilman and Emile Hayne. Production was handled by Gaga, Haynie Mark Ronson and BloodPop . Partial lyrics were first released by Gaga in a tweet in response to a fan tattoo.

Writing

Come to Mama is about two people loving one another, and, how in troubled times, love can help people overcome the hardships of life and what it means to move forward and build a future.
