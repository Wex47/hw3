A Very Gaga Holiday is an acoustic jazz EP that was scheduled to be released on November 26 , 2011 , but was released 4 days ahead of schedule. All the songs were recorded during the ABC special, A Very Gaga Thanksgiving .

Release

The EP received a release in the United States on November 22, 2011, and later a worldwide release on the 26th.
