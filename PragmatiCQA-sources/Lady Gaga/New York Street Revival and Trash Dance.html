The New York Street Revival and Trash Dance is the second concept-show Lady Gaga and Lady Starlight created together. It's a burlesque pop, rock 'n roll, glam and metal party. Gaga started dying her hair blonde at this time. The show lasted only for a month, Gaga started her own revue after it. This was the last time the pair collaborated together until Lady Starlight rejoined Gaga on The Fame Ball Tour in 2009.
