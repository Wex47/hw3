The Just Dance Promo Tour is the unofficial name given to a set of live performances in 2008 . Most of the tour was held in clubs or very small venues with DJ Space Cowboy and two female dancers joining Lady Gaga onstage. The tour began on March 11 , 2008 and ended on December 18 , 2008 across 9 countries.

Set list

The setlist changed from show to show. Following are the songs she performed during the entire tour:

  1. Music for the Funeral of Queen Mary (Remix) (Interlude)
  2. LoveGame
  3. DJ Space Cowboy (Interlude) [contains elements of " Starstruck "]
  4. Beautiful, Dirty, Rich (contains elements of "Bang Bang (My Baby Shot Me Down)")
  5. Eh, Eh (Nothing Else I Can Say)
  6. Paparazzi
  7. Technology (Interlude) [contains elements of "My Egyptian Lover"]
  8. Just Dance
