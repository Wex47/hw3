Brandon Maxwell is a stylist and fashion designer who founded his eponymous ready-to wear label in 2015 in NYC. He worked as stylist assistant to Nicola Formichetti where he met Lady Gaga in 2010. He was the Fashion Director at the Haus of Gaga from 2012 to 2018. Prior to that, he studied photography at St. Edward's University in Austin, TX., and began assisting stylist Deborah Afshani in 2009, followed by Edward Enninful.
