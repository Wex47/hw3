" Hollywood " is a song written in 2005 by Lady Gaga , and produced by Joe Vulpis . The song was never released on any platform. The first time she performed the song was with her band ( SGBand ), at The Bitter End on January 20 , 2006 . They played the song along side " D'yer Mak'er ". It was performed a second time for the Songwriters Hall of Fame Presents: The 57th New Writers Showcase, along with many other artists. The song was performed again at the Cutting Room on March 23 , 2006 . It is the only song from this era that's registered on BMI.

Background

According to Joe Vulpis , he was on a ski trip to Utah when he got a phone call from Gaga telling him that she had just landed her first big record appointment with Island Def Jam .
