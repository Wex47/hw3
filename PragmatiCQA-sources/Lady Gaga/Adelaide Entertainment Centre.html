The Adelaide Entertainment Centre (AEC) is an indoor arena located in the South Australian capital of Adelaide used for sporting and entertainment. It is the principal venue for concerts, events and attractions for audiences between 2,000 and 12,000. It is located on Port Road in the suburb of Hindmarsh, just north of the Adelaide city centre.
