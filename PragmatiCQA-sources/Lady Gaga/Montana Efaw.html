Montana Efaw (born Montana Michelle Efaw) grew up in Lake Oswego, Oregon. She attended Lakeridge High School, and at the age of nineteen, in 2009 , she began dancing for Lady Gaga for the Monster Ball. Her dance training is primarily hip hop, she has been dancing since the age of ten.
