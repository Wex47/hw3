" Just Another Day " is a song written by Lady Gaga for her fifth studio album Joanne . The song was produced by Mark Ronson . Brian Newman appears in track playing on trumpet. The song is the 13th track on deluxe edition of the album.

Background

Lady Gaga performed a small teaser of the song October 4 , 2016 during her interview with On the Air with Ryan Seacrest . The hashtag #JustAnotherDay trended on Twitter the same day, with Gaga tweeting her thanks saying " Thank you SO MUCH for trending #JustAnotherDay From my new album. I LOVE YOU GUYS CAN'T WAIT TILL OCT. 21 to drop #JOANNE #music ".

“ Just Another Day ” is the only song on Joanne that Lady Gaga wrote by herself. Also Lady Gaga teased some info about the song. She explained to Ryan Seacrest: " [“Just Another Day” is] probably the most New York-glam-pop song on the album. It's the one that heirs from [David] Bowie and Marc Bolan [of T Rex] and then maybe from some obscure London metal stuff, the Beatles. It also kind of airs from the electroclash movement from New York and that's kind of when I started coming up, was right after that. "

Before performing " Just Another Day " at Dive Bar Tour in New York City on October 20 , 2016 Lady Gaga talked about this song and about Mark Ronson , and she shared her memories from her childhood.

“ I think this is a real special tune. I love this song so much. I wrote this song looking out over Central Park and from my apartment now, I can see my old house where I grew up with my family. And it just so happens that Mark [Ronson] and I went to the same school at one point. He did go to a boy’s school and I went to a girl’s school, but the pre-school was co-ed. So I went to pre-school at Mark’s school and we’re really neighbors. Because also, I lived two blocks away from one of his best friends, Sean Lennon, and I used to walk by the Imagine Memorial probably three times a week and just sit there and look at the memorial, and I would catch Yoko sometimes with really wide brimmed hat on, walking in Central Park, and she would bring flowers, I think every day. And there was this homeless guy, I think he’s still there, he would decorate the memorial every day with the flowers that she brought, so beautiful. And I don’t know why, but even though Mark and I didn’t know each other then, I used to kind of, when we were writing this record [album "Joanne"] , I would imagine us as kids, kickin’ it around in the city. ”

—Lady Gaga
