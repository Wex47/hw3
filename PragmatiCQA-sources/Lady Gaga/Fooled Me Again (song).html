" Fooled Me Again " is a song written by Lady Gaga and Rob Fusari ( Team Love Child ). The song was used for the promotional video, The Fame: Part One during the intro. The song didn't make it onto Gaga's debut album, The Fame , because it would be upsetting to the main central theme of the album. The song talks about the same relationship described in " Brown Eyes ".
