Tour package

For the tour, Live Nation created five packages with a different seating or General Admission option. Each package came with a Lady Gaga merchandise item, a collectible laminate and onsite VIP host.

  • Ultimate Backstage
  • Come to Mama
  • Perfect Illusion
  • Dancin' In Circles
  • VIP Party

Package includes:

  • Top priced seated ticket to see Lady Gaga live
  • Access to band Soundcheck
  • Exclusive backstage tour
  • Dinner at backstage tour catering
  • Pre show group professional photo with Lady Gaga (groups of 6)
  • Souvenir Lady Gaga tour laminate + lanyard
  • Merchandise item designed exclusively for package purchasers
  • Designated check-in with our on-site event staff
