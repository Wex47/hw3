" Monster " is a song written by Lady Gaga , Space Cowboy , and RedOne around March 2009 for The Fame Monster . The song represents her "Fear of Sex Monster " as every songs from the album refer to one or more monsters in the lyrics. The song was released on Rock Band 2 along with " Just Dance ", " Poker Face " and " Bad Romance " on March 18 , 2010 .

Background

Lady Gaga described the song as the first for the second album and the inspiration theme also during an interview with Nova radio in 2010. The song use the main part of the " Pop Ate My Heart " interlude used in 2008 and 2009 during her live performances : " He ate my heart/ He a-a-ate my heart. " Gaga makes a small self-reference to her song " Just Dance " in her lyrics when she's singing " I wanna just dance but he took me home instead." and maybe also the music video of LoveGame in her lyrics:" We french kissed on a subway train "

Remixes

Chew Fu "Paws Up" Fix

On August 8 , 2010 , DJ Chew Fu took to his Twitter and stated that he finished the first official remix (Chew Fu "Paws Up" Fix), and is waiting on Cherrytree Records to allow him to release it.

“

gaga "Monster"(Chew Fu "Paws Up"fix) will be released, but i have no permission to put up a link yet..Guess its up to CherryTree rec

”

—ChewFu

The remix was intended to accompany "Monster" as a single, but as the track did not receive the commercial release as intended, the remix was shelved for two and a half years. On February 22 , 2013 , Chew Fu posted the remix on his SoundCloud page.
