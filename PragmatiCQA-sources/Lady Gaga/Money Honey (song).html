" Money Honey " is a song written by Lady Gaga with RedOne 's frequent collaborator, Bilal Hajji in 2007 . The song was intended to be released exclusively as the eighth track on the Canadian version of The Fame , however, it was released on all versions. The Canadian version of the album features an extended introduction, whereas the worldwide release has a shortened intro. The song is registered on Gaga's BMI as "Thats Money Honey".

Background

The song was originally intended for another artist, however, Gaga thought it felt too much like a "Gaga song", so she decided to keep it. While in the demo process, the title was "That's Money Honey", which is also the name registered on her BMI. The song deals about love being described as money for a relationship. Before her live performance of the song, she generally says that the thing she hates the most in the world is money.
