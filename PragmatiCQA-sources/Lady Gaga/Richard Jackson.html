Richard "Richy" Jackson is an American dancer, creative director, and choreographer from the San Francisco Bay Area. Jackson has danced for several artists and was an assistant to Michael Rooney and Laurieann Gibson . Since November of 2011, Richy is the creative director and choreographer at the Haus of Gaga . He was previously a dancer and assistant choreographer to Gaga and working for Laurieann Gibson from 2008 to 2011. He usually works with creative coordinator and choreography assistant Lacee Franks .
