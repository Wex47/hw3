The Lion's Den was a music club located at 214 Sullivan Street, between Bleecker Street and West 3rd Street, in the Greenwich Village section of New York City. It opened in 1990 and closed in December, 2007. In January 2008, the nightclub re-opened as the Sullivan Hall .
