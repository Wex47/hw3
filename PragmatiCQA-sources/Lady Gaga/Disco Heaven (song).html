" Disco Heaven " is a song written by Lady Gaga , Rob Fusari ( Team Love Child ), and Tom Kafafian , in 2007 . The song was first made available on her MySpace page, and eventually released as an international bonus track on The Fame , and again as a bonus track on the US iTunes edition of the album and on the deluxe edition of The Fame Monster .Â The lyrics describe the days Gaga had with Lady Starlight , like the Lady Gaga & The Starlight Revue in New York.
