" We Are Plastic " is a song written by Lady Gaga and Rob Fusari ( Team Love Child ) in 2007. The title of the song can be heard in the song, " Paparazzi ". The verses and choruses bear musical similarity to " Retrosexual ", verse reference " Cut The Cake ", while the bridge lyrics reference " Rockshow ".
