Victor Rojas was born in Lima, Peru (December 18, 1979) and moved to New York City at the age of 7. His training began a year after arriving in the United States by joining The 52nd Street Project, an organization that takes inner city kids and teaches them the arts. From there, he went on to study drama at both Fiorello H. Laguradia High School for The Arts and New York University Tisch Program (Lee Strausberg Theater and Film Institute). He is one of the male dancers who work with Lady Gaga . He is part of the Haus of Gaga since February of 2010. He worked on some live performances with Lady Gaga before joining The Monster Ball Tour : Arena version in 2010. Victor also played the part of Posh at The Monster Ball Tour HBO special .
