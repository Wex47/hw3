Anna Trevelyan is a fashion stylist and consultant based between London and New York. She contributes regularly to several influential publications including Elle USA , V Magazine , Japanese Vogue and 10 Magazine. She is fashion director of of London concept boutique MACHINE-A.

Previously working under Nicola Formichetti , she has assisted with his editorial work and styling Lady Gaga. Trevelyan consults and styles for several international brands including Nike, GCDS, MAC, Casablanca, Puma, and Maybelline and has worked with multiple celebrities including Rihanna, Nicki Minaj, Kylie Jenner, Dua Lipa, Bella Hadid and Lizzo amongst many more.

Work with Lady Gaga

Anna Trevelyan’s big break came when Nicola Formichetti noticed her outré personal style on MySpace. Formichetti promptly cast her in a Uniqlo campaign. Trevelyan ended up assisting Formichetti when he was Lady Gaga’s stylist for three years, a period during which she worked closely with the famed popstar. “Working with Gaga was massive training. She works incredibly hard, non-stop, and you have to keep up with that. But after doing that, everything is achievable,” Trevelyan told BoF.
