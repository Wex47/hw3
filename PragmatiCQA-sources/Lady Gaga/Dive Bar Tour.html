The Dive Bar Tour is the mini-tour headliningÂ by Lady Gaga , in support of her fifth studio album, Joanne . The tour was sponsored by Bud Light. The tour visited three dive bars in the United States in Nashville, New York and Las Vegas, on October 5 , 20 and 27 . All performances were live streamed on Bud Light's Facebook page, as well as Gaga's.
