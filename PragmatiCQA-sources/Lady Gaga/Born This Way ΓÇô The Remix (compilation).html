Born This Way — The Remix is a remix compilation by Lady Gaga including remixes of songs from Born This Way . It was released worldwide on November 21 , 2011 in conjunction with the Blu-Ray/DVD release of Lady Gaga Presents the Monster Ball Tour: At Madison Square Garden , the HBO concert special originally aired in May. The album is also available in a special package with the concert special and the 17-track deluxe edition of Born This Way in a special release titled Born This Way – The Collection that was released the same day.
