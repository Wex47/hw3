Nasir Mazhar is a British fashion designer from East London and brought up in Leytonstone, London, to Turkish Cypriot parents. Once he left school, Mazhar began an apprenticeship at Vidal Sassoon. While working in a Brick Lane salon, he started designing headpieces and soon progressed to hats. He created the hats for the 2012 Summer Olympics opening ceremony (the clubbing scene), and launched his first full clothing for men and women in his 2013 collection at London Fashion Week.
