Mackin Pulsifer is credited as the first band by Lady Gaga in her liner notes of her debut album, The Fame .

Trivia

  • The time period of the band is unknown.
  • During her freshman year of high school, Gaga sang in a classic-rock cover band which might be Mackin Pulsifer .
  • She did covers of Led Zeppelin's songs along with Pink Floyd and Jefferson Airplane.
