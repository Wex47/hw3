Description

The screen descends back down to the front of the stage and a black and white close-up of Gaga's face appears. Throughout the video, purple paint is seen dripping in reverse up Gaga's face. At the end of the film, Gaga smears the paint all over her face with a scared facial expression at the end.

Outfit

Lady Gaga seemingly doesn’t wear any seen clothes in this Interlude but it is seen that she is wearing caps for her hair and smears a purple liquid over her face.

Soundtrack

This Interlude has a special soundtrack, produced by DJ White Shadow and BeazyTymes .
