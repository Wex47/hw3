" I Can't Give You Anything But Love" is an American popular song and jazz standard by Jimmy McHugh (music) and Dorothy Field (lyrics).

The song was introduced by Adelaide Hall at Les Ambassadeurs Club in New York in January 1928 in Lew Leslie's Blackbird Revue , which opened on Broadway later that year as the highly successful Blackbirds of 1928 (518 performances, wherein it was performed by Adelaide Hall, Aida Ward, and Willard McLean.

Lady Gaga and Tony Bennett covered the song for their jazz collaborative album, Cheek to Cheek .

Release

"I Can't Give You Anything But Love" was released as the second single from Cheek to Cheek on August 19 , 2014 . Gaga announced the release on Twitter, accompanied by the single's cover art on the same day. An official music video for the song was released on August 26, 2014.

The artwork follows the Cheek to Cheek newspaper concept by Modern Construct with a photograph from Steven Klein from June 17 , 2014 and the text is written using Plantin . The date of the single release is also written on the single cover. For the remixes, the original release date is used.

A month after the album release, two remixes were digitally released on October 6 , 2014. The Giorgio Moroder Remix premiered on while the Parov Stelar Mix premiered on . The following day, both songs were uploaded to Gaga's YouTube and released on digital music stores for purchase.

An official music video for the song was released on August 26, 2014. The video was shot in the recording studio and the first half showed Gaga in numerous outfits and wigs, while recording the song and roaming around. Bennett joins the studio sessions later on, singing the song. The final chorus finds the two singers belting together. The footage was shot on various dates in June of 2013 and in June of 2014.
