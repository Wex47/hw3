Live From The Apollo (As Broadcast On SiriusXM) is an live EP released by Lady Gaga . It was made available for streaming in the United States on June 28 , 2019 by Interscope Records , exclusively on music streaming service Pandora .

3-song EP live album contains " Poker Face ", " Born This Way " and " Yoü and I " from her live special performance of Enigma at the Apollo Theater in collaboration with Sirius XM and Pandora. The audio from the live performance was originally broadcasted on the Sirius XM radio.
