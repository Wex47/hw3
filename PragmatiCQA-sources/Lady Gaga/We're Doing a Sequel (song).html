We're Doing a Sequel also known as just " Sequel Song " is a song written by Bret McKenzie and performed by Lady Gaga , Tony Bennett & the Muppets released on the soundtrack for Muppets Most Wanted on March 18 , 2014 . The song breaks the fourth wall as Kermit and the Muppets sing about making a sequel. The film's original soundtrack album features a demo version of the song, performed by Bret McKenzie, as a bonus track. A music video teasing a portion of the song along with clips from the film was released online on February 27 , 2014 and featured only Muppets.
