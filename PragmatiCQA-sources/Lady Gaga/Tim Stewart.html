Tim Stewart is a guitarist that started with Lady Gaga on the Born This Way Ball tour to present. Also is producer, and writes with Tricky Stewart and Rodney Jerkins . He was part of the Jazz trio that includes Pino Palladino and Chris Dave and has played with everyone from Infectious Grooves to Britney Spears.

On November 16 , 2019 he married Lady Gaga's makeup artist, Sarah Nicole Tanno .

Work with Lady Gaga

  • The Born This Way Ball ( 2012 - 2013 )
  • Lady Gaga Live at Roseland Ballroom ( 2014 )
  • artRAVE: The ARTPOP Ball ( 2014 )
  • Dive Bar Tour ( 2016 )
  • Joanne World Tour ( 2017 - 18 )
