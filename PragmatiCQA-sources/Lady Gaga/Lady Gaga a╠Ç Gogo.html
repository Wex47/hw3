Lady Gaga à Gogo was the first Lady Gaga art and fashion exhibition . It was held at Galerie Chappe, 4, Rue Andre Barsacq, 75018 Paris during October 21 through October 30 of 2010 . The gallery was directed by Alexandre Gilbert and the show was curated by Alexandra Boucherifi.

The purpose of this one-off event was to let people see “in person” some of the iconic outfits Gaga wore in the music video of Telephone, Bad Romance and Alejandro, and outfits she wore in her live shows and day to day. All the outfits were exposed on black vernice or chrome Cofrad mannequins. Designers and photographers that worked with Gaga, some famous and others up and coming at the time, that were featured: Thierry Mugler , Jean-Charles de Castelbajac , Viktor & Rolf , Mouton Collet , On Aura Tout Vu , Oliver Bobin , Gary Card , Vera Thordardottir , Charles Youssef , Alun Davies, Vava Dudu , Stephanie Paterek , Bernard Chandran , Vilsbol de Arce , Lauren Machen and Marko Mitanovski .

October 21-30, 2010
