" Sinner's Prayer " is 8th song from Lady Gaga fifth studio album Joanne . The song was written by Gaga, Josh Tillman, Mark Ronson, and Thomas Brenncek and produced by Mark Ronson and BloodPop . This track was the first non-single song from the album performed live.

Background

Gaga debuted the song during the first concert of her mini-tour Dive Bar Tour in Nashville sponsored by BudLight. The song was only performed once and was excluded from Joanne World Tour setlist.

This county-pop come-on finds a vulnerable Gaga, acknowledging her weaknesses, but wanting her man to love her warts and all. Speaking to Beats 1 's Zane Lowe, Gaga explained:

“ This song is about singing to a man just telling him, 'Look I just don't want to break the heart of any other man but you, but I know that I'm a sinner.' ”

—Lady Gaga
