" Radio Ga Ga " is a rock song performed by Queen, written in 1983 by their drummer, Roger Taylor.

Related to Lady Gaga

Rob Fusari , Lady Gaga 's then producer and boyfriend, said she was " so theatrical, so dramatic, so Freddie Mercury, so Radio Ga Ga " while she was recording " Again Again ". Shortly after, Fusari began to call her "Gaga" in the studio as a nickname, to which she later prefixed the "Lady", to give it a feminine touch.
