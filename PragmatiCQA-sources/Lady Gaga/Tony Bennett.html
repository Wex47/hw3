Tony Bennett (born Anthony Dominick Benedetto ; August 3, 1926) is an American singer of popular music, standards, show tunes, and jazz.

Relationship with Lady Gaga

On May 9, 2011 at the Robin Hood Gala , Tony Bennett who was also performing that night, saw Gaga's rendition of " Orange Colored Sky ". He was impressed by her performance and soon after, asked her to do a song for his second album of duets. On July 31, 2011, Bennett and Lady Gaga recorded a cover of " The Lady is a Tramp ", which was released on Bennett's album, Duets II (September 20, 2011). The two later appeared on the ABC special, " A Very Gaga Thanksgiving " (2011) and at the Inauguration ball (2013) performing the same song.

On September 12 , 2011 , Tony sketched a portrait of Gaga with charcoal at his studio during a cover story with photographer Annie Leibovitz for Vanity Fair . Bennett's sketch was later auctionned on eBay and the proceeds went to Bennett’s Exploring the Arts charity and Lady Gaga’s Born This Way Foundation .

In 2014, Gaga asked Bennett to draw a trumpet so she could get it tattooed. Bennett sketched Miles Davis' trumpet and signed it with his last name, Benedetto. On June 18 , 2014 , Gaga got a trumpet on the top inside of her right arm.

Bennett sketched three portraits of Gaga based on their Cheek to Cheek photoshoot by Steven Klein .

Lady Gaga and Bennett's collaborative album, Cheek to Cheek , was released on September 23 , 2014 . At the end of 2014, Bennett and Lady Gaga kicked off their co-headlining Cheek to Cheek Tour.
