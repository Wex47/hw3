Sloan-Taylor Rabinor (born on 10 December, 1988 in Phoenix, Arizona) is one of Lady Gaga 's main dancers who toured with Gaga throughout the entire duration of The Monster Ball Tour . Her first appearance as a dancer for Gaga was at the 2009 MTV Video Music Awards when Gaga performed " Paparazzi "; Sloan was the girl being pushed on the wheel chair during the performance.
