Alexis Bittar is the CEO and Creative Designer of the eponymous jewelry company, known for his signature Lucite bangles and high fashion runway collaborations. In 2004, Bittar was named the "Rising Star of the Year" by the Accessories Council of Excellence (ACE). In 2010, he won the CFDA Accessory Designer of the Year award. In November 2014, he received the ACE Award for Brand of the Year.
