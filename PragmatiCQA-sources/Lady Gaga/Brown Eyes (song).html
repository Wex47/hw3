" Brown Eyes " is a song by Lady Gaga . It is known as her most autobiographical track on The Fame . The concept behind the song is something most people can relate to, a love lost. The title of the song can be heard in " Again Again ". Vocals for the song were recorded after "a lot of red wine and crying", in one single take, and first preformed live at The Mirage Club on August 18 , 2008 .

“ I wrote that song a long time ago and I had never had my heart broken like I had when I wrote that record. That was one of those 3 AM, crying in my apartment at the piano… one foot on the bed and one foot on the floor. ”

—Lady Gaga
