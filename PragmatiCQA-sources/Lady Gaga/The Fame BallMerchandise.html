Sold online and at the shows. Inspired by the artwork for "The Fame", lyrics from Just Dance.

A few unique items were sold during these tours. Among them, handmade hairbows by the Haus of Gaga available in black, brown and blonde. It was also possible for people who bought "The Fame" to meet Gaga after the show to get is signed.

One of the notable item was a bubble solution, it was meant to "break the separation of exhibitionist and observer creating a seamless interaction between the audience and the performer." The bubble solution was meant to be used during the "Bubble Dreams" act of the show.
