Joanne Trattoria is the name of Joe Germanotta 's restaurant located on 68th Street, between Central Park West and Columbus Avenue, in New York City. The restaurant opened on February 1 , 2012 . The restaurant chef is Art Smith (featured in A Very Gaga Thanksgiving in 2011).

Background

In 2011, Joe Germanotta bought Vince & Eddie's Fireplace (1989-2011), which was reputed for serving quality New American comfort cuisine to a well-heeled Lincoln Center audience. In November of 2010, Lady Gaga became an investor/part-owner of the restaurant in Manhattan’s Upper West Side. Her parents, Joe and Cynthia , have also become shareholders of the establishment. Gaga is the only silent partner. Mrs. Germanotta told W , “ When she’s in town she likes to come here with her friends because it’s very homey and private. She’ll come here on her time off. ”

In May of 2010, Gaga hosted a contest where people could win tickets to see the Monster Ball at Madison Square Garden. The first message was posted on her Facebook on May 21st. " NYC monsters, you can still win tickets to the SOLD OUT Monster Ball at Madison Square Garden from one of Gaga's favorite hometown restaurants, Vince & Eddie's! Eat at Vince & Eddie's to enter the drawing & get all of the contest details! Click below for restaurant location, hours & information! . "

On May 29th, another announcement was posted: " Eat at one of Lady Gaga's favorite NYC restaurants, Vince & Eddie's, for a chance to win tickets to The Monster Ball at Madison Square Garden! Only a few weeks left to win! Click below for giveaway details & restaurant info. . "

Vince & Eddie's Fireplace to Joanne

In March of 2011, they closed the restaurant to do renovation. The restaurant reopened as "Joanne" in February of 2012.

The chef

Charles Arthur "Art" Smith, born March 1, 1960, is an American chef. He has worked for former Florida governors, Bob Graham and Jeb Bush, and until 2007, was the personal chef to Oprah Winfrey.
