" Cheek to Cheek "


" Anything Goes " is a song written by Cole Porter from his musical of the same name in 1934. Many of the lyrics are humorous references to various figures of scandal and gossip in society in high-society of the time.

Lady Gaga and Tony Bennett covered the song for their collaborative album, Cheek to Cheek . This song was released as the first single from the album on July 29 , 2014 .
