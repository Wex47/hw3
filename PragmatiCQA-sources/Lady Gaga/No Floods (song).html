" No Floods " is a song written during the last trimester of 2005 by Lady Gaga . The title of the song was inspired by the victims of Hurricane Katrina.

Versions

Studio versions

There are two versions of the song, the first version is the demo, played with a guitar and piano. This version was released on the demo, Words . Gaga later re-recorded it with her band. This version was produced by Joe Vulpis , and was released on the extended play, Red and Blue . There are multiple differences between the two versions. Not only the instrumental back-track, but the lyrics were also changed. Some of the lines were condensed, while new lines replaced others.
