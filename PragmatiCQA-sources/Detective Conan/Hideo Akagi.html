Background

Hideo Akagi has made a name for himself as a Professional Soccer Player of Tokyo Spirits. He had scored at least one goal in 17 games in a row before the Grand-Finals of the Sunday Cup. In addition, while competing for the Emperor Trophy, the Tokyo Spirits defeated Big Osaka with a score of 2:1, with Hideo's Header taking the first lead. Furthermore, in the Semi-Finals of another Sunday Cup, the team was subjected to a penalty kick, with Hideo managing to perform a decisive penalty shootout.

Nevertheless, Hideo has large fan base behind him which gave birth to his Fan-Made Name "Hide". The Detective Boys are also fans of Tokyo Spirits and views Hideo as a potential player for the Japanese National Soccer Team.

Appearance

Hideo Akagi appears to be a fairly tall man with a fair skin tone, blue eyes and a light brown hair. Furthermore, he is usually seen wearing his Tokyo Spirits Jersey with Number "11" imprinted on it.

Personality

Hideo Akagi is a kind and loving person. During a match with Big Osaka, Hideo decided to sacrifice his fame and soccer performance just to save his brother, Mamoru Akagi , from a kidnapping situation.

Abilities

Physical Abilities

Hideo Akagi is a Professional Soccer Player with an outstanding skill and remarkable performance. As one of Tokyo Spirits' exceptional aces, he has the potential of becoming a player of the Japanese National Soccer Team.
