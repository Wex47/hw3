Akako Koizumi ( 小泉 紅子 , Koizumi Akako ) an ominous Witch who wishes and strives to enslave all men especially Kaitou Kid , is a supporting character in the manga and anime franchise Magic Kaito and a minor character in the manga and anime franchise Detective Conan .

Trivia

  • Akako Koizumi's given Name "Akako" (紅子) means "Red Child".
  • Akako Koizumi's Cobras' Emotional Expressions in Magic Kaito Specials & Magic Kaito 1412 differs.
    • In Magic Kaito Specials, the Three Cobras attached to Akako's Headband and Magical Costume are able to adjust their Emotional Expressions to match Akako's.

    • In Magic Kaito 1412, however, this is not the case for Akako.

  • Akako Koizumi is right-handed.
