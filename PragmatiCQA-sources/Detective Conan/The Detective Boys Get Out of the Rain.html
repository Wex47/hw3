The Detective Boys Get Out Of The Rain (少年探偵団の雨宿り, Shōnen tantei-dan no amayadori) is the 824th overall episode of the Detective Conan animated series based from the manga of Gosho Aoyama. The episode aired in Japan on July 09, 2016.
