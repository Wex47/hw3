Midori Kuriyama ( 栗山 緑 , Kuriyama Midori ) , a Professional Attorney and the Legal Secretary of Eri Kisaki , is a recurring character in the manga and anime franchise Detective Conan .

Background

Midori Kuriyama is a young professional attorney who has studied law in an unknown university in Tokyo.

After Eri Kisaki separated from her husband Kogoro Mouri , Eri created her own law firm and integrated Midori as her Secretary. Since then, Midori has become busy, working all week and usually comes a bit early at the office to tidy up and organize things for Eri before she comes to work.

As the Legal Secretary of Kisaki Law Firm, she handles the phone calls, visits, publicity and many other programs that Eri cannot necessarily take care of.

Appearance

Midori Kuriyama appears to be a fairly tall woman with a fair skin tone, dark violet eyes and a long dark brown hair. Furthermore, she is usually seen wearing business suits whenever she works in the office.

Personality

Midori Kuriyama is a fine lady with a kind attitude whenever she is or not at work. She has a habit of teasing Eri about her current relationship with Kogoro and likes to have chit-chats about Eri's romantic life.

She always keeps her good mood, in addition of liking to help other people and give them advises when they are in trouble. Furthermore, she does not get angry easily and always treats her guests a warm welcome.
