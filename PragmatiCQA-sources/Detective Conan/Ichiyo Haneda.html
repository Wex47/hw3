Ichiyo Haneda ( 羽田 市代 , Haneda Ichiyo ) is a character in the manga and anime franchise Detective Conan . She is Kohji Haneda 's mother and Shukichi Haneda 's mother-in-law. His husband is Yasuharu Haneda .

Although she has not appeared in the manga yet, her name is written on Shukichi's marriage registration.
