Korn (コルン, Korun) , a Codenamed Member of the Black Organization , is an antagonist in the manga and anime franchise Detective Conan .

Background

Nothing is known about Korn's past but he appears to be working under the Kaasuma Group's External Division of Assassination together with Chianti . Korn could have formerly worked with Calvados as well since Calvados used to work under the same division.

Appearance

Korn appears to be an average thin old man with a pale skin and a grayish hair color. Furthermore, he is usually seen wearing his glasses whose eyes cannot be seen through, a black cap which covers his forehead and a black suit underneath his dark green vest.

Personality

Korn appears to be an anti-social person since he rarely interacts with other members of the Black Organization unless spoken to. Korn has also a cold personality who enjoys killing people and gets disappointed when he does not get the opportunity to. Furthermore, Korn is less impatient than Chianti and appears to loath Vermouth as well.
