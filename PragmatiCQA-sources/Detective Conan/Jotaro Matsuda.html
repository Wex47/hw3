Background

Jotaro Matsuda is the father of Jinpei Matsuda , a deceased detective from the Tokyo Metropolitan Police Department. One day, Jotaro saw a skirmish between two men on his way home, but he ignored it because he had a title fight after a few days. However, one of them, who was a former professional boxer, was killed. The police arrested Jotaro because someone told the police that he saw Jotaro near the scene around the time of the crime and the victim hated him. Although the true culprit was arrested and the false accusation against him was cleared up, the title fight was cancelled. In addition, nothing came to go well for him after the case and he finally retired his job.
