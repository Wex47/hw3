Background

Nothing is known about Chianti's past but she appears to be working under the Karasuma Group's External Division of Assassination. She is often seen working with Korn . Chianti might have formerly worked with Calvados as well since Calvados used to work under the same division.

Appearance

Chianti appears to be an average slim woman with a pale skin and light orange hair. Furthermore, she is usually seen wearing her black leather suit with a collar. Her left eyelid bears the tattoo of a swallowtail butterfly's wing.

Personality

Chianti is a wild, ruthless assassin who enjoys killing people. Unlike Korn, she is very impatient and appears to loath Vermouth as well.
