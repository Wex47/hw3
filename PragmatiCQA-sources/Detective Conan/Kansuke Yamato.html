Kansuke Yamato ( 大和 敢助 , Yamato Kansuke ) is a Inspector from Nagano Prefecture Police and is a character in the anime and manga franchise Detective Conan.

Background

Yamato lost his left eye in a snow avalanche and now has an X-shaped scar in its place. This same avalanche caused severe and permanent damage to his left leg, forcing him to use a forearm crutch to walk around.

Appearance

Yamato has black hair and wear a ponytail he also has a X shape scar

Personality

Kansuke is serious and hard boiled compared to other police inspectors like Juzo Megure from Tokyo Metropolitan Police, Sango Yokomizo from Shizuoka the Prefecture Police, and Misao Yamamura from the Gumma Prefecture Police He is often short-tempered and has been known to yell at and intimidate suspects because of this He can become quite visibly annoyed with Kogoro's ignorance or Yamamura's Goofyness.

Despite this He does have softer spots, though he does not show them often, and there are hints that he is both aware and accepting of Uehara's affection towards him.
