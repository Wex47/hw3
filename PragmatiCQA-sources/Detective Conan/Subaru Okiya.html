Subaru Okiya (沖矢 昴, Okiya Subaru ), a disguised FBI Agent who is also known by his real name Shuichi Akai (赤井 秀一, Akai Shūichi ), is a supporting character in the manga and anime franchise Detective Conan .

Background

Subaru was said to be a graduate student attending Tohto University to earn a doctorate degree in engineering. He currently reside in Shinichi's House since the inconspicuous apartment he formerly lived in was burnt down. However, he is actually a FBI Agent. He took on this alias after faking his death at Raiha Pass. This persona was created with the help of Shinichi 's parents, Yukiko and Yusaku Kudo.

Appearance

Personality

Subaru is generally shown to be quiet and reserved, often seeming unfazed by normally stressful situations, such as when Ran attacked him while he was brushing his teeth, believing he had broken into Shinichi's house[9], or when a murderer grabbed Ayumi as a hostage. He often tends to behave in a friendly and modest manner, smiling and rubbing the back of his head whenever someone compliments him or says anything about him. He enjoys cooking and seems ready and willing to help with various tasks. He doesn't mind interacting with the Detective Boys, is always polite to them, and listens to their theories during cases.
