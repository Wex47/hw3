Detective Conan OVAs (名探偵コナンOVA, Meitantei Konan OVA ), unique anime episodes that feature its own original plot with the general theme focused on Shinichi Kudo and Conan Edogawa , are animations created by TMS Entertainment related to the manga and anime franchise Detective Conan written and illustrated by Gosho Aoyama. Detective Conan OVAs began broadcasting from 2000 to April 14, 2012.
