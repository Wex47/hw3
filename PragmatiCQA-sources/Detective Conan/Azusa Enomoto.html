Background

Azusa is a kind and young woman who works with Tooru Amuro at Café Poirot, the Coffee Shop that is directly under the Mouri Detective Agency.

Kogoro Mouri often goes there for a quick coffee break while Ran Mouri , Conan Edogawa and the Detective Boys hang there for snacks and drinks. Azusa sometimes gives Kogoro a case to solve, mostly involving Missing Cell Phones and Threatening Cell Phone Messages.

Appearance

Azusa Enomoto appears to be a fairly tall woman with a fair skin tone, green eyes and a brown hair black hair. Furthermore, she is usually seen wearing her waitress uniform while working at Café Poirot.

Personality

Azusa Enomoto has a kind personality. She is devoted to the people she is close with, like her brother Sugihito Enomoto , and is willing to defy authority for them but at the same, she is also willing to help the police in their investigation.

Abilities

Physical Abilities

Azusa Enomoto is experienced when it comes to managing a Coffee Shop as she is able to work under pressure from satisfying the customers to managing the café's supplies, contributing a profitable and successful business.

Trivia

  • Azusa Enomoto's Family Name "Enomoto" (榎本) comes from her Voice Actress Mikiko Enomoto .
    • "Azusa" (梓) means "Catalpa Tree".
    • "Enomoto" (榎本) means "Hackberry" and "Origin".
  • Azusa Enomoto's Character Design has drastically been changed throughout the Anime.
    • Azusa Enomoto has three different looks.
  • Azusa Enomoto is right-handed.
