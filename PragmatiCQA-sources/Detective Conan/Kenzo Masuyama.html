Pisco ( ピスコ , Pisuko ) , an Executive Member of the Black Organization and Car Company CEO who was also known by his real name Kenzo Masuyama ( 枡山 憲三 , Masuyama Kenzō ) , is an antagonist in the manga and anime franchise Detective Conan .

Background

Not much is known about Pisco's past, although Pisco was acquainted with the syndicate's lead researchers Atsushi Miyano and Elena Miyano . Pisco also used to finance the Black Organization with his wealth as a Car Company CEO.

​Appearance

Pisco appears to be a fairly tall old man with a slim build, pale skin, black eyes, white hair and mustache.

Personality

Pisco was a loyal member of the Black Organization who would have revealed that Ai Haibara is Sherry if only Gin had not killed him first. He was also shown to be careless as he did not ensure that there was no photograph of him performing an assassination. This was further evidence when he expressed an interest to the caller who tipped the police about the murder case, leading Conan Edogawa to conclude that Kenzo Masuyama was the culprit behind the assassination.

Plot Overview

Haibara Arc

Hotel Party Murder Case

The Boss orders the assassination of the Politician Shigehiko Nomiguchi after the police found out that he was accepting bribes to keep sensitive information. Gin then relays the order to Pisco and told him to use APTX 4869 when necessary. After successfully assassinating the politician, he encounters Ai Haibara and locked her inside a wine storage room, suspecting that she is the escaped scientist Shiho Miyano . Moments later, he witnesses Shiho's shrinking to Haibara and was amazed with the drug's de-aging effect. Despite enlisting Vermouth 's help, he was cornered by Conan and was accidentally caught on camera in the act of committing the murder. Fearing that Pisco may expose the Black Organization's existence and goals to the public, the Boss ordered Gin to silence Pisco and his family, which the latter does even though Pisco knew who Sherry is.
