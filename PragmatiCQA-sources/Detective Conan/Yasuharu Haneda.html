Yasuharu Haneda ( 羽田 康晴 , Haneda Yasuharu ) is a character in the manga and anime franchise Detective Conan . He is Kohji Haneda 's father and Shukichi Haneda 's father-in-law. His wife is Ichiyo Haneda .

Although he has not appeared in the manga yet, his name is written on Shukichi's marriage registration.
