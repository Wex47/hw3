Juno Glass ( ジュノガラス , Juno Gurasu ) , the Mother of the Famous and Professional Tennis Player Minerva Glass , is a minor character in the manga and anime franchise Detective Conan .

Background

Juno Glass is the Mother of both Minerva Glass and Apollo Glass , with Mr. Glass being her German Husband.

At some point, Apollo and Minerva lived together with Juno after she was hospitalized at Japan for Eye Surgery. After several months, Juno grew accustomed to Japanese Culture, being able speak the Japanese Language very well.

Appearance

Juno Glass appears to be a fairly tall woman with a fair skin tone, silver eyes and a tied blonde hair. Furthermore, she is usually seen wearing casual clothes with a cap and shades whenever she watches her daughter, Minerva, compete in an Official Tennis Match.

Personality

Juno Glass is a friendly and cheerful person. She has also a kind and caring personality.
