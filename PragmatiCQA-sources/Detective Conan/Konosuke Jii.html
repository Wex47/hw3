Konosuke Jii (寺井 黄之助, Jii Kōnosuke ), Blue Parrot Bar 's Owner and Kuroba Family's Assistant, is a supporting character in the manga and anime franchise Magic Kaito and a minor recurring antagonist in the manga and anime franchise Detective Conan .

Trivia

  • Konosuke Jii's Full Name means/comes from the following:

    • The "Ko" (黄) of Konosuke Jii's Given Name means "Yellow".

    • The "Suke" (助) of Konosuke Jii's Given Name means "Assistant".

    • "Jii" (寺井) means "Gramps".

  • Konosuke Jii's Vehicle is a Gray Vanden Plas Princess 1100 .

  • Konosuke Jii is right-handed.
