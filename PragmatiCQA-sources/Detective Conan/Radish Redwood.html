Radish Redwood

Age

†

♂ ♀

Radish Redwood (ラディッシュ・レッドウッド Radisshu Reddouddo?) is a police officer in plain clothes from New York City who was in charge of a murder case at a Broadway play that Shinichi solved one year before the current timeline. He can speak Japanese because his wife is from Japan. Sharon Vineyard disguised as him to help Yukiko escape a traffic ticket. He appeared in the Golden Apple case.
