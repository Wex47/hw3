Background

Appearance

Goro is a Russian Blue kitten and has bright green eyes, pinkish lavender or mauve paws, two layers of short thick fur, and a blue-grey coat.

Personality

Goro is a shy cat and is shyer than Taii and is cute. He only likes to play with his relatives and the people he is accustomed to. He was once tempted by a stranger who put catnip to lure him but after a few minutes, the catnip effect wore off.

He doesn't like to interact with others and run from strangers. He likes the care provided by Ran, Eri, and Midori. He has a strong sense of protection after he tried to stop kidnappers to take Eri. He is tempted to go on treats and candies.

Goro is also somewhat similar to Kogoro.
