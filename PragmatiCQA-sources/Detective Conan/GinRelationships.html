Black Organization

Renya Karasuma

Gin's Superior. Gin often acts as the intermediary between the Boss and other Black Organization Members. He follows the Boss' orders closely and never acts against the syndicate's greater interests. In return, the Boss entrusts Gin to direct critical missions and key assassinations.

The Boss issues orders to Gin directly by text message for him to disseminate to his subordinates. The two are also implied to contact each other fairly often about instructions and suspicions.

Chianti

Gin's Subordinate. Chianti's respect for Gin coerces her and the others into obeying his orders.

Korn

Gin's Subordinate. Korn's respect for Gin coerces him and the others into obeying his orders.

Pisco

Gin's Colleague. Although Gin and Pisco are both Executive Agents, Gin dislikes and looks down on Pisco.

Rum

Gin's Superior. Gin implies that the murder of Kohji Haneda is something that Rum had screwed up, stating that he does not care about Rum's mistake as long as it does not put the Black Organization at risk. Gin also seems to be aware of Rum's current agenda and whereabouts, stating that the latter had changed his appearance and went with a silly name.

Tequila

Gin's Former Subordinate. Tequila's respect for Gin coerces him and the others into obeying his orders.

Vermouth

Gin's Colleague. Gin and Vermouth have rather different personalities. Although they get well enough during missions, Gin does not like Vermouth's habit of keeping secrets or her teasing. Gin and Vermouth mostly trusted one another, however, Gin's trust seems to have eroded after Vermouth failed to inform even the Boss of her activities and intentions on several occasions.

At one point, Gin told Vodka to kill her if he noted her doing anything suspicious, and he didn't particularly care about how she would escape a train heading towards a station packed with explosives. Gin and Vermouth are or were in a sexual relationship with one another, which was hinted when Vermouth asked Gin if they could make a Martini one night, noting that a Martini is a mix of Gin and Vermouth.

Vodka

Gin's Partner. Gin and Vodka frequently work together and often communicate by phone. Vodka shows Gin absolute respect, using honorifics while speaking with Gin rather than simply using his codename like other members do. Gin in turn, values Vodka for his obedience. Thus, Gin occasionally orders Vodka to go on missions where he needs objective observation or that are annoying.

Gin views and treats Vodka as an "inexperienced little brother". Despite this, Gin does not truly trust anyone, including Vodka. He once interrogated his actions at gunpoint before. Although Vodka is slower than Gin and frequently jumps to incorrect conclusions, Gin patiently explains his deductions to Vodka, creating a sort of Sherlock Holmes and Watson dynamic between the two. In exchange, Vodka covers for Gin's weakness of not remembering the names or faces of those he has killed.

Akemi Miyano

Gin's Former Subordinate. Akemi, who once dated FBI Agent Shuichi Akai, was given a task by Gin to pull off a 10 billion yen bank robbery in exchange for her and Sherry's freedom from the Black Organization.

Initially, the Black Organization could not do anything to Akemi since they needed the loyalty of her sister Sherry, who was very valuable as the lead researcher of the APTX 4869 Project. Soon later, the Black Organization decided that the risk of her contact with Akai was too much. They then decided to create a situation where they could eliminate her by having her do a bank heist that she was supposed to fail and Gin was put in charge of making a deal with her.

After successfully completing the heist, Akemi confronts Gin at the shipping yard to complete the transaction. She tries to tell Gin to hold up the end of his bargain. However, he tells her the syndicate would not let her Shiho leave anyway since she was too valuable. He then kills Akemi and flees the scene.

Law Enforcement

Shuichi Akai

Gin's Rival. Although Gin respects Akai for his ability, Gin is also contemptuous of Akai and has a lesser opinion of the damage he could cause to Black Organization compared to the Boss and Vermouth's impressions. Vermouth and the Boss fear that Akai could become a Silver Bullet while Gin does not believe a single person who could take down the syndicate exists. That said, Gin considers Akai to be the FBI 's trump card.

In the past, FBI Agent Shuichi Akai who was on a infiltration mission, was promoted to the Black Organization Codename "Rye". He was called to work under Gin and a meeting for them was set in a warehouse. The FBI staked out the warehouse hoping to capture Gin as it was believed that he could lead them to the Boss. However, when FBI Agent Andre Camel mistakenly revealed himself in front of an old man who was actually Rum scouting the area in disguise, the Black Organization discovered Akai's true allegiance and Gin never appeared.

Ethan Hondou

Gin's Former Subordinate. While recalling Kir's past which included her confronting a spy, Gin remembers the spy's surname which was "Hondou".

Hidemi Hondou

Gin's Subordinate. Even though Kir has managed to prove her loyalty by "murdering" Shuichi Akai at Raiha Pass, Gin still appears to be suspicious of Kir's allegiance to the Black Organization.

In the past, Gin and Vodka went to investigate the syndicate's new member, Rena Mizunashi, when the tracker placed on jacket indicated her in a location where she was not supposed to be. At the time, Rena Mizunashi was meeting with her father and fellow CIA Agent, Ethan Hondou. Anticipating this kind of situation, Ethan committed suicide to protect his daughter's undercover status by making it look like Rena Mizunashi had confronted him for being a spy and had killed him as a result. When Gin arrived on the scene, he witnesses CIA Agent Barney who mourned on his fellow and also committed suicide thereafter. Rena Mizunashi then reports her fabricated story to the Black Organization which Gin believed and relayed to the Boss, promoting her to the Black Organization Codename "Kir".

Rei Furuya

Gin's Subordinate. Bourbon, who shares a similar personality with Vermouth, is disliked by Gin and finds him annoying for being secretive and for always doing what he pleases. At the same time, Gin respects Bourbon's perception and deductive abilities. The two do not seem to be in contact with one another, as Gin has no idea about Bourbon's current agenda and whereabouts.

Others

Shinichi Kudo

Gin's Former Target. Gin is responsible for inducing Shinichi with APTX 4869. During their first encounter, Shinichi has described Gin as someone who has the appearance and air of a cold-blooded killer.

Gin and Vodka's suspicious actions caused Shinichi to follow and encounter them completing the transaction with the businessman. Gin, who seems to have been keeping watch, knocks down Shinichi with a pipe he found and force-feeds him APTX 4869 , shrinking him into Conan Edogawa.

Shiho Miyano

Gin's Former Subordinate. Gin has an abnormal attachment for Shiho and seems to enjoy hunting her. Shiho, in return, seems to fear and hate him. They appear to know each other well despite being in different divisions of the syndicate, implying that the two had a mysterious relationship in the past. Gin can identify Shiho from a single auburn hair and can also easily predict her movements.

Kogoro Mouri

Gin's Target. Apparently, Gin seems to have taken an interest on Kogoro Mouri after the latter's recent involvement in cases surrounding the Black Organization. He also demands to have him killed as soon as possible before he even grows to a greater threat.
