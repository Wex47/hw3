Background

Ginshiro is the Chief Criminal Investigator (or Chief Detective) in the Osaka police district, and is close friends with the police chief of Osaka, Heizo Hattori.

Trivia

  • Ginshiro Toyama's Given Name "Ginshiro" (銀司郎) is a play on "Kinshiro" (金四郎), the Given Name of the Protagonist in the Fictionalized Toyama no Kin-san Stories based on the Historic Kagetomo Toyama's Life.
    • Ginshiro's Family Name "Toyama" (遠山) comes from the Fictional Edo Period Police Detective Kagetomo Toyama .
  • Ginshiro Toyama is right-handed.
