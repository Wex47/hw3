Background

Koichi Zenigata is a descendant of Heiji Zenigata, a police officer known for throwing coins to arrest people. Zenigata is an inspector from the ICPO (aka Interpol) where his job is to arrest Arsène Lupin III .

Appearance

Personality

Zenigata often appears to be grouchy and serious, when angered he shouts towards people or waves his handcuffs. The only time when he shows different emotions is when he is with Lupin, where he is happy when Lupin is about to or being arrested and upset if anything happens to Lupin, sometimes even crying.
