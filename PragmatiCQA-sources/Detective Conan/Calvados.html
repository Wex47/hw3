Background

Nothing is known about Calvados' past, but judging on Chianti and Korn 's reaction toward Vermouth's involvement on Calvados' death, it is likely that the three of them knew each other since Chianti, Korn & Calvados work under the Black Organization's Assassination Division.

Appearance

Calvados appears to be an average man with a pale skin, although little is only known about Calvados' appearance since he only appeared once as a silhouette. Furthermore, Calvados wears a dark green trenchoat with a green turtleneck underneath, as well with a beige cap and headphones used for communication.

Personality

Calvados appears to be a ruthless assassin has he did not stop shooting Ran Mouri when Vermouth ordered him to hold his fire. In addition, Calvados is shown to be courageous and loyal to the Black Organization as he showed determination to commit suicide, despite being crippled by Shuichi Akai and left by Vermouth, in order to prevent the Law Enforcement from questioning him about the Black Organization's identity.
