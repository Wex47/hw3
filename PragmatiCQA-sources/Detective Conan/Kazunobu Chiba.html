Kazunobu Chiba (千葉 和伸, Chiba Kazunobu ; English TV: "Detective Chiba") is a Tokyo Metropolitan Police Department 's First Division Police Sergeant & Detective and the childhood friend and the romantic interest of Naeko Miike , is a supporting character in the manga and anime franchise Detective Conan .

Trivia

  • Kazunobu Chiba's Full Name comes from his Voice Actor Isshin Chiba .

    • "Kazunobu" (和伸) can also be read as "Isshin" (和伸).

  • Kazunobu Chiba's Vehicle is a Green Fiat 500 .

    • Kazunobu Chiba also has a Nissan GTR, as seen in episodes 971, 972, 973, and 974.

  • Kazunobu Chiba is right-handed.
