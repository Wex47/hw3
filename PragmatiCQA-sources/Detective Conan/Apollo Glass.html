Apollo Glass ( アポロ・グラス , Aporo Gurasu ) , the Brother of the Famous and Professional Tennis Player Minerva Glass , is a minor character in the manga and anime franchise Detective Conan .

Background

Apollo Glass and her sister, Minerva Glass, are British - German of descent. Before Apollo was born, his German Father died in a tragic accident while on his way to Ares Ashley 's Official Tennis Match.

At some point, Apollo and Minerva lived together in Japan with their British Mother Juno Glass after she was hospitalized there for Eye Surgery. After several months, Apollo grew accustomed to Japanese Culture, being able speak the Japanese Language very well.

Appearance

Apollo Glass appears to be a child with an average height and has a pale skin, gray eyes, and a blond spiky hair. Furthermore, he has freckles and is usually seen wearing various casual outfits whenever he watches her sister, Minerva, compete in an Official Tennis Match.

Personality

Apollo Glass, like most of the children, appears to be a cheerful and energetic child. Furthermore, Apollo seems to have a solid determination and strong resolve as shown when he persistently searched for " Sherlock Holmes " to ask him for help to decipher the note that a suspicious stranger left him.
