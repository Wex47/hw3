Background

Andre Camel is an FBI Agent who owns a debt to Shuichi Akai due to him unintentionally blowing his cover when Shuichi was infiltrating the Black Organization.

Appearance

Personality

Despite his rather hulking appearance, Camel is a friendly person who cares about the feelings of his fellow FBI agents. He can react quickly, as shown by his driving skills and his role in returning Kir to the black Organization. However, he does not always make the best choices as shown when his concern for an old man wound up ruining Shuichi Akai's cover in the Black Organization.

Trivia

  • Andre Camel's full name comes from Lieutenant Dren , a fictional character from the manga and anime franchise Mobile Suit Gundam .
    • Andre comes from "Dren" ( ドレン , Doren ) which is fabricated by adding an "A" ( ア ) and moving the "N" ( ン ) in front of "Dore" ( ドレ ) , resulting "Andore" ( アンドレ )
    • Camel comes from the "Camel Squadron" which Lieutenant Dren is in charge of.
  • Andre Camel is right-handed.
