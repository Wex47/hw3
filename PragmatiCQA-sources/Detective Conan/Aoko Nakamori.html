Aoko Nakamori (中森 青子, Nakamori Aoko ), the childhood friend and the romantic interest of Kaito Kuroba , is a protagonist in the manga and anime franchise Magic Kaito and a minor character in the manga and anime franchise Detective Conan .

Trivia

  • Aoko Nakamori means/comes from the following:

    • "Aoko" (青子) means "Blue Child"

    • "Nakamori" (中森) comes from the Japanese Actress & Pop Singer Akina Nakamori .

  • Aoko Nakamori's Character Design is derived from the Fictional Character Aoko Holmes from Gosho Aoyama's Manga Nonchalant Lupin .

  • Aoko Nakamori and Ran Mouri have similar Character Designs.

  • Aoko Nakamori is right-handed.
