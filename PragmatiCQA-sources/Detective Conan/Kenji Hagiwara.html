Background

Kenji Hagiwara was a member of the Tokyo Metropolitan Police Department bomb squadron and Jinpei Matsuda's explosive disposal partner. He died in the first of a series of bombings seven years prior to the current timeline.

Appearance

Kenji is an average height man with long black hair and white skin. He is pretty well-built and strong.

Personality

Kenji is a pretty carefree guy who always has time to joke a bit. He is a heavy smoker and does the job right. He prevents other people so they can be saved before him and always respect his friends. He is also rather flirtatious during his days at the Police Academy
