Background

Goemon is the 13th descendant of Goemon Ishikawa, a famous thief who was around 400 years ago. Being a samurai, he trains and uses the Zantetsuken, an iron cutting sword that he practises using that can cut through various objects.

Appearance

Personality

Goemon is stoic who spends much of his time meditating, often sat down on a wooden floor with his eyes closed or by a waterfall. He has pride on his abilities and shows shame whenever he fails.

Being a traditional person, he only accepts food that is Japanese.

His weakness is women where he gets shy and blushes when they are within his company.
