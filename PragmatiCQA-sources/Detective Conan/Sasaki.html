Sasaki ( 佐々木 , Sasaki ) , a Professional Attorney and the Legal Secretary of Reiko Kujo , is a non-canon recurring character in the manga and anime franchise Detective Conan .

Background

Sasaki is a young professional attorney who has studied law in an unknown university in Tokyo.

Sasaki is the Substitute Prosecutor at the Tokyo District Court who often accompanies Reiko when she performs at court. With his help, Reiko vowed to defeat her rival, Eri Kisaki , whenever a chance would present itself.

As the Legal Secretary of Reiko Kujo, he handles the phone calls, visits, publicity and many other programs that Reiko cannot necessarily take care of.

Appearance

Sasaki appears to be a fairly tall man with a fair skin tone, black eyes and a short black hair hair that is constantly slicked back with three loose locks hanging across his forehead. Furthermore, he is usually seen wearing business suits whenever performing in the court.

Personality

Sasaki is a fine diligent person who is rarely seen interacting with others.

It has been clearly hinted that Sasaki is infatuated with Reiko. However, it is currently unknown if she reciprocates his feelings.
