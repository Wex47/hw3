Miwako Sato ( 佐藤 美和子 , Satō Miwako ) is a Tokyo Metropolitan Police Department 's First Division Assistant Inspector and the romantic interest of Wataru Takagi , is a supporting character in the manga and anime franchise Detective Conan .

Personality

Sato is considered one of the most dangerous members of the police, being not at all afraid to get tangled with dangerous men in fights, which she almost always wins.

However, she does have a softer personality, in which she is very friendly with the Detective Boys, and especially Conan, whom she considers is a big help in difficult cases.

When in action, however, Sato can be quite reckless and daring, which has earned her the title 'The Silver Witch' among car racers on Mount Fuyuna due to her driving style.

Trivia

  • Miwako Sato's Full Name means the following:

    • "Miwako" (美和子) means "Beautiful", "Child" and "Peace".

    • "Sato" (美和子) means "Help" and "Wisteria". Written with Different Characters, "Sato" (砂糖) can also mean "Sugar".

  • Miwako Sato's Vehicle is a Red Mazda RX-7 , the same Car Model as Rei Furuya.

    • Miwako's Vehicle Color was previously White but later changed into Red so that it would be more visible in the fog.

    • Miwako has a Habit of Shrieking while Drifting without pulling the Handbrake, as she feels that Shrieking helps her make the Drifting easier even though she finds it Embarrassing.

    • Miwako's Shrieking Habit caused the "Mountain Witch" Story to spread, as people reported that the Mountain Witch would Drift using a White Mazda RX-7 and Scream really Loud.

  • Miwako Sato is one of the Police Officers to take Conan Edogawa seriously due to his Extraordinary Intelligence and Keen Observation.

  • Miwako Sato is a Fan of the Master Thief Arsène Lupin III and has stated that he was her First Love.

  • Miwako Sato dislikes Expensive Restaurants.

  • Miwako Sato dislikes Shiny and Expensive Jewelry.

    • However, Miwako currently wears a Shiny Expensive Ring that Wataru Takagi had bought for her, not realizing that he intended it to be an Engagement Ring.

  • Miwako Sato is Bad at Drawing.

  • Miwako Sato is right-handed.
