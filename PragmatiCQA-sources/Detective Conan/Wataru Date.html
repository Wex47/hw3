Background

Date's early life is unknown While attending the Police Academy he became acquainted with Rei Date later became a police officer from the Tokyo Metropolitan Police District under the Criminal Investigation First Division.

He was later assigned to be the mentor of Wataru Takagi,[2] and together they became known as the "Wataru Brothers." He was dating an English teacher, Natalie Kuruma. Unfortunately, he got caught up in a car accident after one of his stakeouts with Takagi and died one year ago. One day after his death His Girlfriend Natalie commits suicide in despair over his death.
