Spider (スパイダー , Supaidā ), a member of the Magic Kaito Organization who is also known by his alias Gunter Von Goldberg II (ガンター・フォン・ゴールドバーグ 2世, Gantā Fon Gōrudobāgu 2-Sei ), is a non-canon antagonist in the manga and anime franchise Magic Kaito .
