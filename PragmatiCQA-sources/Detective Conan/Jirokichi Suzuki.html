Trivia

  • Jirokichi's Given Name means/comes from the following:
    • "Jirokichi" (園子) comes from Historial Edo Period Thief Rat Boy Jirokichi .
    • Jirokichi's Family Name "Suzuki" (鈴木) may have come from the name of the Famous Violinist "Shinichi Suzuki" .
  • Jirokichi Suzuki, a rich and powerful man who focuses his sole career around chasing the Phantom Thief Kaitou Kid , is comparable to Tokyo Metropolitan Police Department's Second Division Inspector Ginzo Nakamori and Interpol Police Inspector Koichi Zenigata who does the same to the Master Thief Arsène Lupin III from the manga and anime franchise Lupin III .
  • Jirokichi Suzuki's Power & Wealth as the Senior Adviser of the Suzuki Financial Group rivals that of Renya Karasuma , the Boss of the Karasuma Group .
  • Jirokichi Suzuki is right-handed.
