Baby Doll was one of Kay Challis 's many alters. She is a childlike personality with an affinity for Cyborg . While in control of their body, Jane's hair immediately transforms into twin pigtails. In The Underground , Baby Doll appears as young woman with blond hair wearing a pink onesie pajama, decorated with hearts with a tiara headband. She was killed by Candlemaker after fighting with Dorothy Spinner .

Biography

Baby Doll is one of the many alters of Kay Challis whose mind fragmented due to suffering abuse from her own father at the young age of five, somewhere along this time period her mind created baby doll, an incredibly innocent and childish personality, whose mental state is comparable to that of a small child.

During Dorothy Spinner 's time in Doom Manor , Niles Caulder introduced Baby Doll to her and the two initially hit it off, playing various games and running around the manor causing mischief. However, Dorothy began to get tired, upsetting Baby Doll. Dorothy then revealed one of her imaginary friends, Manny , and asked her to be polite when meeting him. Despite agreeing, Baby Doll poked Manny, causing him to retreat back to Dorothy's mind and angering Dorothy herself, running away. Baby Doll then pursued her but left when Niles lied to her about being asleep. Later on, Baby Doll, still upset, resurfaced in Jane's stead when Dorothy arrived into the kitchen to greet Niles. The two began playing again, though the games turned sour when Dorothy scared Baby Doll from within a furnace in revenge for hurting Manny. Angry at this, Baby Doll telekinetically pushed Dorothy back into the furnace and switched it on, causing a panic. Dorothy re-summoned Manny, though Baby Doll had Flaming Katy kill Manny. Realizing what she'd done, she attempted to apologize, but to no avail. Dorothy, who was angry at the time and distraught at Manny's death, was then easily manipulated by her worst imaginary friend, the Candlemaker to call upon him and make a wish. Baby Doll was then killed by the Candlemaker whilst in the underground , due to her attempting to retreat there to hide alongside the other alters. During this battle, Flaming Katy was also killed trying to defend her.

Eventually Baby Doll and Flaming Katy were then given a proper funeral procession and their bodies were transported to the Well, were despite Jane's insistence not to, their remains were thrown in and encouraged due to the reappearance of Miranda , Who confirmed that the Well's true purpose was to "replenish" them. With that Baby Doll's Station within the Underground collapsed due to it no longer being needed.

Powers and abilities

Powers

  • Metahuman physiology : Baby Doll was one of the many alters residing within Crazy Jane and whenever she becomes the dominant one in their shared body, Baby Doll can manifest her own unique metahuman power.
    • Telekinesis : Baby Doll was able to levitate Dorothy into the fire furnace of the Doom Manor , when she angered her. Baby Doll showcased enough control over this power to telekinetically shut and lock the furnace door shut and open the gas valve of the fire furnace when she threatened Dorothy.

Weaknesses

  • Psychological childishness: Due to Baby Doll essentially forever being psychologically frozen at the age of a small child, she retains the same childish traits of being innocently selfish, doing impulsive acts, throwing tantrums, and generally engaging in irresponsible activities and possesses a general lack of introspection. As seen when Baby Doll threw a violent fit, after getting angry with Dorothy who caused her to trip, and she as a result angrily reacted and telekinetically threw her into the fire furnace. Even when Dorothy attempted to get her to let her out by telling "Papa", Baby Doll impulsively threatened to burn her alive . Later her temper and lack of control was shown where she had Flaming Katy kill Manny when Dorothy summoned him to defend her, that later caused Baby Doll to fall into a grief/guilt stricken panic due to her not realizing the full implications of what she had done.
