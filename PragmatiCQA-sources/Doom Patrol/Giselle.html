Biography

Giselle was employed as the nanny of Clara Steele . While at work, she began having an affair with Clara's father, Cliff . However, when nearly caught by Cliff's wife Kate , they were able to play it off. Following Cliff and Kate's split, Cliff went back to having sex with Giselle.
