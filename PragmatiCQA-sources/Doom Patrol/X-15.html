History

Larry Trainor was chosen to test out the X-15, flying it up into the upper atmosphere of Earth . While there, he ran into the Negative Spirit , which entered his body and rendered him unconscious, causing the plane to crash to the ground and explode on impact.

Behind the scenes

  • In real life, the North American X-15 was a hypersonic rocket-powered aircraft operated by the United States Air Force and NASA. Rather than 1961, its first flight was actually in 1959, by co-pilots Scott Crossfield and William Dana.
