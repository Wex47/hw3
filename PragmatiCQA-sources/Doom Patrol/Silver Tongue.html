Biography

Silver Tongue is one of the many alters of Kay Challis who developed DID due to childhood trauma.

History

She is one of the many alters who killed someone, she killed Heinrich Von Fuchs .

Powers and abilities

Powers

  • Metahuman physiology: Silver Tongue is one of the many alters residing within Jane each of whom manifests their own power when in control of their shared body.
    • Ferrogenesis : Silver Tongue has the ability to generate sharp metal versions of words she says, having been shown to primarily use them as projectile weapons. However Silver Tongue has shown much more intricate control over her power, as seen she displayed the ability to manipulate the form of her silver words and shape them into actual weapons. As seen where she shaped the word "Underground" into a samurai sword.
