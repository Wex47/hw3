Grid

Creator(s)

Silas Stone

Owner(s)

Victor Stone

User(s)

Victor Stone

Function(s)

AI System
Grid is Vic Stone 's AI System. Grid manages Vic's mechanical parts and is also capable of searching the internet, hacking into secured communication channels, and responding to Cyborg's verbal and subconscious commands.
