History

After a car accident that decapitated his wife , Cliff Steele was left with only his brain remaining, and so Niles Caulder put his brain into a robotic body.

After a lab explosion, Vic Stone was badly hurt, and so he was implanted with cybernetics in his leg and head, turning him into a cyborg, even taking on the moniker "Cyborg" during his superhero undertaking.
