Zip Callahan was a cosmonaut who went into the Space at 1955 in a spacecraft designed by Niles Caulder . He was also a good friend with Valentina Vostok and Specs .

He died in space but an alien spore bonded with his body, artificially extending his life. As a result of this, Zip would seemingly play around and rough house with Spec in a childish manner and when someone would try and speak to him, he would simply repeat phrases one sidedly like a pre-recorded message due to him technically being dead.

After he came back to Earth, the spore lost its effect and he died. He was then buried with Spec's in the Doom Manor 's backyard.

Larry Trainor told him that, and at some point when he was a kid, he appeared at the box of his cereals.
