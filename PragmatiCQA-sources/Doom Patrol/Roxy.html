Biography

Creation

When Rita Farr was a child she was inspired by her mother, Addie Cramp to be an actress so Rita made a paper doll and glued her mother's eyes from a magazine and called her paper doll, Mademoiselle Roxy and Roxy became the imaginary friend of Rita but as time went by, Rita became a famous actress so she forgot her imaginary friend.

Reunion with Rita

When the Doom Patrol arrived at the fair, Mademoiselle Roxy is one of the imaginary friends that Candlemaker summoned to fight the Doom Patrol. When Rita searched for the carousels to find Niles and Dorothy , Roxy surprised her with pride that Rita became an actress as she wished as a child and Rita told her that she is over that dream and that she never wanted to be like her mother and Roxy were shocked and Rita and Roxy faced off in a dance contest on stage. Roxy and Rita danced together and Roxy was impressed by Rita's steps and Rita took it out on Roxy because her mother, Addie made many mistakes and Rita told her that she would never make mistakes again and Roxy was happy that Rita finally He learned the lesson of following his instincts and Candlemaker possessed Roxy and told Rita that it would always be a mistake and a failure leaving Rita in shock and Candlemaker waxed Rita.
