Joshua "Josh" Clay is a Metahuman physician who looks after the Doom Patrol at their headquarters after they retired. Despite having powers, he refuses to use them.

Behind the scenes

  • In the DC Comics, Joshua Clay was a superhero known as Tempest, member of the second Doom Patrol, who retired and became the team's medic. His first appearance in the comics was Showcase #94 (September, 1977).
