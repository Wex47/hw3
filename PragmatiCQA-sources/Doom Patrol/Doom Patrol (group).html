The Doom Patrol is a group of superheroes active in the 50's. They had many battles with the Brotherhood of Evil , before a disastrous fight with Mr. Nobody caused them all to go insane. Now, there was an original member of the Doom Patrol and it was Beast Boy but now he is a former member of the Doom Patrol and later left and joined the Titans. And now some of them members of the Doom Patrol lived in a shared illusion created by Mento , with Joshua Clay as their caretaker. Throughout the decades, Jane , the dominant identity of a traumatized woman with dissociative identity disorder ; Rita Farr , who struggles to prevent her body from turning to a gelatinous state; Larry Trainor , who has an entity of negative energy living inside of him; and Cliff Steele , whose brain was placed in a robot body following a car crash, started to live in Doom Manor in order to heal themselves from their traumatic experiences.

In 2019, the Chief is captured by the malevolent Mr. Nobody, sending the Doom Patrol on a journey to rescue him. Along the way, the team is later joined by cybernetically-enhanced superhero Vic Stone . They discover secrets about themselves and the Chief, whom they eventually learn is responsible for the tragic events that gave them their powers.

In 2020, after having defeated the Brotherhood of Dangerous Animals , the Doom Patrol with the help of Willoughby Kipling returned to its normal size and with its new member, Dorothy Spinner , Niles' daughter but with Niles dying, the Doom Patrol would do everything possible for Niles to continue living and protect Dorothy and prevent the dangerous being that lives inside Dorothy, the Candlemaker from being released and each one would amend their past and become better people.

In 2021, Niles eventually passed away and the team learned to overcome his death until they were visited by Laura De Mille , a time traveler, who recruited them to stop the Sisterhood of Dada . The team would learn to discover who they really are and who they want to be. By reforming Laura, who was Madame Rouge, the team finally agreed to become a true team of superheroes.

History

The First Doom Patrol

Early Years

During the 50s, the Doom Patrol was a minor superhero team organized by Niles Caulder . They dueled frequently with the Brotherhood of Evil , specifically its leader Ultimax . Eventually, they managed to capture Ultimax's helm, though the Brain got away.

During this time Niles also met former actress Rita Farr after her cellular structure became completely unstable due to being exposed by a toxic gas in the Amazon. Niles took Rita to his mansion in order to help her with her situation, during this time Rita also became romantically involved with Mento but their relationship ended after he found out her secret.

Against Mr. Nobody

The Doom Patrol's final fight was against Mr. Nobody . On Memorial Day weekend, Nobody deployed a jukebox on a hot air balloon shaped like a butt into a crowded park. The jukebox played "Hawt Diggity" by Perry Como on repeat, and within 20 minutes had driven anyone in earshot insane. When the police arrived on the scene, they were hit with a Transformer Ray, changing them all into Piñatas. The crazed crowd then ripped open the Piñatas, eating the candy inside. Arani Desai described this as the most horrible thing she'd ever seen.

When the Doom Patrol finally did arrive on the scene, they were met by Mr. Nobody and a horde of Vinyl Warriors , entities made of long-playing records. Mr. Nobody used the Doom Patrol's fears against them, causing them to turn on each other.

In the aftermath of the fight, the Doom Patrol grew even more volatile, frequently lashing out using their powers. This lead Niles to decide that they were too dangerous to be kept free, so he placed them in their own house, where they inhabited a shared delusion created by Mento while they were kept sedated. Eventually, the Metahuman Joshua Clay was brought on board to live with look over the aging Doom Patrol, and he continued to look after the aging Doom Patrol after the Chief's disappearance.

Residents of Doom Manor

Rescue of Larry Trainor

After Captain Lawrence Trainor from the United States Air Force became possessed by an unknown type of radiation during a failed test flight and was taken to the Ant Farm by the Bureau of Normalcy to test him, Niles rescued Larry from the Ant Farm in 1966 and took him to Doom Manor where he met Rita.

Saving Kay Challis

In 1976, Niles got a visit from Forsythe while he was celebrate the new year with Larry and Rita. Forsythe told Niles that they had found a new test subject in Kay Challis after he ordered a decade ago. In 1977, while some medics believed they were taking Jane in an ambulance for a lobotomy, Niles was actually the ambulance driver and he told Jane that he will take her to a safe place to heal. In 1978 Niles took Jane to the Manor and Jane met Rita and Larry. Niles did numerous tests on the Underground with Jane and he treated Jane very well by playing with her personalities .

Rebuilding Cliff Steele

In 1988, the race car driver Clifford Steele suffered a horrific car accident along with his wife and daughter , while Kate Steele died, his daughter survived but Cliff's body was given zero chance of life by his doctors. Niles took Cliff's brain, built him a new robotic body and used one of Cliff's voice recordings to copy his voice into the machine, all of this happened in secret as the world thought he died in 1988. After a painful relearning process and recovery through the years, Cliff eventually managed to control his primitive robotic body aswell as meeting Rita and Larry on Doom Manor, but he was left on a terrible state after remembering his accident and being told by Niles that both his wife and daughter died on said accident.

Capture of Niles Caulder

Revealing themselves

By 2019, Cliff had accustomed to Doom Manor and Niles returned to doing secretive missions away from Covington and the mansion, trusting the rest to stay out of trouble while he does it. At this point Jane also Jane revisited Doom Manor as she had been gone for a long time, she met Cliff while she made an obnoxiously made a scene with two of her personalities. Jane eventually convinced the rest to sneak out into town while the Chief went into one of his missions, but it all backfired as Rita lost control of herself and was rampaging on the town, Cliff (who during this trip also found out his daughter was still alive) managed to stop Rita by pulling up a piece of the road and stopping her in her tracks. The Chief scolded the team on the consequences that revealing themselves to the public would cause as now his enemies would be brought to the town.

Return of Mr. Nobody

Niles said it was time to move so his enemies couldn't find them, everyone agreed except fot Cliff who chose to stay and Niles took them on Larry's bus, but they all changed their minds and decided to help Cliff defend the town from any enemy that comes near the locals. The team saw an unusual albino donkey fart in the middle of the road the message "The mind is the limit", while Niles was escaping in Larry's bus he encountered Eric Morden , who opened a portal leading to the White Space that began to envelope a large part of the road in the town while claiming that this time he wouldn't hurt this team like he did last time as that wouldn't be any fun. To find Niles, the superhero Vic Stone , aka Cyborg, joined the patients to help.

Trip to Paraguay

To find a clue, the team made a wild trip to Paraguay to discover the connection between Niles and Nobody. When the bus broke down, Cliff, Jane and Larry went to Paraguay while Vic and Rita stayed. The trio met scientist Heinrich Von Fuchs , who revealed that Niles was the creator of Nobody and responsible for its current condition. While Larry tried to remove his powers, Cliff and Jane confronted the descendants of Von Fuchs murdering them all. Soon after, the trio returned to meet Vic and Rita and they left on Silas Stone 's jet.

The Return of the Cult of the Unwritten Book

While doing a group meeting, the team ran into Willoughby Kipling , who was an old friend of Niles and a wizard. Kipling warned about the Cult of the Unwritten Book , who planned to use the Decreator to destroy the world. The team saved Elliot Patterson to prevent the end of the world and Cliff and Jane would take care of preventing the rest of the cult from passing while Kipling, Rita, Vic and Larry protected Elliot. At the manor, Vic, Kipling, and Larry faced off against the Dry Bachelors , but learned that the Hooded Ones had passed into the world and Kipling realized that the world was doomed due to Cliff and Jane's failure.

Failing to save Elliot, the cult released the Decreator, initiating the end of the world. After learning that Niles and Morden made an alliance, the team searched for the rewritten book in order to save the world. When they encountered a pug, which turned out to be the rewritten book, Kipling managed to free the Recreator by defeating the Decreator and saving the world. After all the chaos, the team once again lost the Chief, who was again kidnapped by Morden.

Meeting the veterans

To search for answers from Nobody, Jane, Larry, and Rita came across the originals and believed that it all became a school for young heroes, but were tortured by their past by Mento until the Negative Spirit removed Mento's helmet. Joshua explained to the trio the entire first team's loss to Nobody and the abandonment of Niles.

Therapy

To get over his past, Cliff convinced everyone to do group therapy, but Cliff had an aggressive behavior due to Admiral Whiskers . Rita, Larry, and Vic overcame their problems and grew more as a team. Jane showed her painting of a possible future, where Vic kills them all, this caused Jane to leave due to Cliff's criticism until Vic, Larry, and Rita discovered Whiskers inside Cliff and took the rat out to return Cliff to normal.
