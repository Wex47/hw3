Biography

When Dorothy was having fun racing around a miniature race track, Darling appeared, much to Cliff 's shock. She later appeared after Dorothy was terrified by a mother rat eating its runt baby, ready to defend her, but she was promptly calmed down by her father and Darling disappeared. She later appeared and told Jane of Niles Caulder 's imminent death.

Powers and abilities

Powers

  • Flight: As seen during her waking up Jane, Darling Come Home possess the ability to either fly or levitate, having been seen to fly downward to wake up the sleeping Jane and later fly up to leave her room after upsetting her.
  • Phasing: Similar to Herschel , Darling Come Home can also pass through solid matter. As seen where she floated downward into Jane's room by passing through the floor and through Jane's ceiling and later left by floating back up through her ceiling.
