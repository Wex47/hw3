Karen is one of Kay Challis's many alters. Karen is shown to be a blonde, ditzy and giddy alter with an obsession with romances from the 90s. When she's in control of their shared body, Jane (Kay)'s hair immediately changes to blonde. In the Underground, she more or less appears to share Jane's physical appearance, only possessing blond hair styled into a 90s bob and wearing a wedding dress.

Biography

Karen is one of the many alters of Kay Challis who developed DID due to childhood trauma.

Somewhere along this time Karen was seemingly formed as a natural defense for Kay and her Primary alter whenever she/they fall into deep depression. Resulting in Kay's mind creating the ditzy, happy and energetic alter of Karen to help her cope with her unhappiness.

Upon obtaining their Metahuman powers, Karen seemingly developed a deep, unbalanced fixation/obsession with Douglas Calhoun and frequently made use of her Metahuman powers to have him engage in a "romantic" relationship with her. And during the course of his life, Karen would frequently appear and disappear from his life — a strange on and off relationship due to her eventually being restrained and returned to The Underground by the other alters once she had successfully given the "primary" time to heal or when she would begin to get out of control.

Powers and abilities

Powers

  • Metahuman physiology: Karen is one of the many alters residing within Crazy Jane each of whom manifests their own power when in control of their shared body.
    • Affection Manipulation : When in control of their shared body, Karen has the ability to make others love her, with the degree of this love ranging from romantic to merely friendly love. This is accomplished via eye contact with the victim, with Karen's eyes glowing pale ice white to indicate when she's using the power and her victims also glowing indicating that they have been affected. While it isn't explicitly confirmed, Karen's power likely has a time limit of some sort because Doug , despite being under Karen's spell many times, was free of her influence when they first reunited in 2019. Robotman was shown to be immune to this power, likely because of his largely robotic body.

Weaknesses

  • Non-Biological Physiology: Due to her power not working on Cliff due to his largely robotic physiology, its likely that Karen's power only works on a physical level to affect a person's affection for her and her power itself is not psychic in nature.
  • Obsession: Apparently Karen had a long and sick obsession with Doug Calhoun for multiple years. When she affected Dirk she called him Doug which proves she might never get over him and anyone may be able to use him against her with threats.
