Powers and Abilities

Powers

  • Meta-human physiology: Through unknown means, Sachiko's DNA was altered turning her into a meta-human.
    • Matter Manipulation : Sachiko can shape and manipulate matter.
      • Transmutation : Sachiko is able to manipulate and transform the matter of objects and convert it to something else, as seen when she turned the normal clothes of Bureau employees into clown costumes.
    • Telepathy : Sachiko can mentally interact and communicate with the mind of others.
      • Telepathic Translation : Sachiko is able to translate languages by linking her mind with the person she is communicating with, causing them to understand and speak her native Japanese to her without conscious thought.

Behind the scenes

  • In the DC comics, Quiz was an unknown Japanese woman who suffered from a pathological fear of germs. She was a member of the original Brotherhood of Dada , with the unique ability to harness any power her opponents didn't think of. Her first appearance in the comics was Doom Patrol #26 (September, 1989).
