Shelley Byron aka The Fog is a member of the Sisterhood of Dada , hosting the rest of its members inside her mind.

Shelley and the Sisterhood were betrayed by Laura De Mille , which caused them to initiate the Eternal Flagellation. After the plan was successful and people got over their embarrassments, Shelley helped Jane to help personalities. Shelley and Jane began to fall in love.

Powers and Abilities

Powers

  • Meta-human physiology: Through unknown means, Byron's DNA was altered turning her into a meta-human.
    • Transformation : Like her name indicates, Shelley can turn into a fog that renders people unconscious for the purpose of absorbing their consciousness into her own.
    • Psychic Construction: As seen during her meeting with Crazy Jane , Shelley was able to create and manifest psychic constructs within her mind. As seen where she created a sweet shop. Shelley was also responsible for creating the Sisterhood of Dada 's own private club room.

Behind the Scenes

  • In the DC comics, The Fog was originally a man named Byron Shelley . A member of the Brotherhood of Dada , he could transform into a cloud, absorbing the souls of anyone he touched in his fog form. His first appearance in the comics was Doom Patrol #26 (September, 1989).
