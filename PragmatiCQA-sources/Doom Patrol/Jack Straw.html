Biography

Jack Straw is one of the many alters of Kay Challis who developed DID due to childhood abuse.

Although unknown why, Jack Straw was shown to have also been imprisoned by presumably Hammerhead in her Jail within The Underground by the time of Karen 's forcible return and imprisonment in the Underground after her attempt at marriage to Douglas Calhoun .

It's been implied that Jack Straw is one of the more troublesome alters within the Underground. As seen where she willingly assisted Cliff Steele escape from his cell by throwing him a chisel to break down his brick wall to escape.
