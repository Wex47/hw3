Spinning Jenny is one of Kay Challis's many alters. Jenny is an alter that, while dominant, constantly screamed and slammed their hands into their head due to what seems to be constant panic attacks. In the Underground, she is an African-American woman wearing a white mesh dress and white blindfold.
