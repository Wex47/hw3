The Sisters are some of the many alters of Kay Challis . They exist as the heads of three old women conjoined at the shoulders of a single body. The Sisters provide counsel to primary alters who feel lost, however this advice is usually destructive, suggesting their role as a Persecutor in the system.

Biography:

The Sisters are one of the many alters of Kay Challis, a shy and introverted young woman whose mind fragmented due to suffering sexual attacks from her own father at the young age of five.

The Sisters have been known to give "advice" to any alters in the Underground who seek it, having done so in the past for the former primary alter, Miranda . When Jane, the current Primary, sought help, the Sisters gave the same advice, which was to be seeking out the Well , where they will ultimately be destroyed.
