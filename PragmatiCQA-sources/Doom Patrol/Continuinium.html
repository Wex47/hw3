Continuinium

Owner(s)

Jonathan Tyme Niles Caulder (formerly)

Function(s)

manipulates time

Continuinium is an element does not exist naturally on Earth. It was discovered in a meteorite on a beach in Bali, by Jonathan Tyme . It is a rare mineral that can bend time. It is not to be touched, because as Niles Caulder explains, "A meteorite struck, and when he ( Jonathan Tyme ) laid his hands on the glowing crystals therein, he went mad. Able to manipulate the time itself."

It is also a part of Jonathan Tyme 's helmet, that is shaped like a clock. It is located on the head's forehead.

The mineral is first seen in the second episode of Season 2 called Tyme Patrol by Niles Caulder . It has a purple-ish texture, as seen in the episode.
