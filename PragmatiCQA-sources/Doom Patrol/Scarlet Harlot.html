Biography

Scarlet Harlot is one of the many alters of Kay Challis who developed DID due to childhood trauma.

This section is a stub. You can help expand this section by .

Scarlet Harlot was later seen to be one of the alters that surfaced during her first meeting with Cyborg and was later seen to be in the group of alters that appeared during Karen 's forcible return to the Underground .

Later during the events of Sex Patrol , Scarlet Harlot was seen to be the second alter to surface following Jane's imprisonment in the Underground, and after the Hangman's Daughter failed to make a decisive enough choice of where to go and how to live if they were to leave Doom Manor . After surfacing, Scarlet proved to be immediately drawn to the debauchery party thrown by the Dannyzens in order to revive Danny the Street and immediately began flirting with Cyborg after he arrived. She then proceeded to go dancing and enjoy herself and completely forgot her original intent was to leave. Eventually Scarlet was returned to the Underground by Doctor Harrison after the other persona's realized she wasn't leaving.

Scarlet Harlot then appeared after the Candlemaker 's attack on the Underground that left Baby Doll and Flaming Katy dead and proceeded to help the other alters prepare their funerals and was present during their march to the Well in order to dispose of their bodies. She also witnessed Miranda 's reemergence and retaking of the role of Primary away from Jane.

However sometime after Miranda's takeover, Pretty Polly noticed that she was MIA after she was absent from their Book Club meeting, leading her to become worried and tell Jane about it. Suspicious, Jane then proceeded to travel to her station in order to check on her, only to then see that for some reason, her station was now closed and barricaded. Later when Miranda pushes Jane into the Well, she saw that Miranda had also pushed Scarlet and Lucy Fugue in as well and killing them in the process.
