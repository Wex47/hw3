Immortus Initiative

Base of operations

Doom Manor

Aim(s)

Researching different forms of immortality

Leader(s)

Niles Caulder

The Immortus Initiative is a project helmed by Niles Caulder in conjunction with the US Military and the Bureau of Normalcy . Its objective was to create a workable method of obtaining immortality.
