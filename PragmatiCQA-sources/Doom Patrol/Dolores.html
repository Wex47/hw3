Biography

Dolores was the Wife of Flex Mentallo during the late 50s. Following the abduction of Flex by the Bureau of Normalcy, Dolores was also captured to keep him in line, by threatening her life and well being. She was never released.

During her captivity, she became a brainwashed agent of the Bureau, capturing unsuspecting victims with her cybernetic enhancements.

Decades later she was rescued by Flit, while manifested in Jane's Body, and brought to Doom Manor to reunite with Flex. Her laugh trigger's Flex's memories and he calls her by her nickname, Dolly, awakening her from her brainwashing. They finally embrace, before Dolores tragically disintegrates in Flex's arms at the hands of the Bureau.
