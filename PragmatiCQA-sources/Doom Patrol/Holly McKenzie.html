Holly McKenzie aka Sleepwalk is a member of the Sisterhood of Dada . She, like all the members, resides inside Shelley Byron 's mind.

Powers and abilities

Powers

  • Meta-human physiology: Through unknown means, McKenzie's DNA was altered turning her into a meta-human.
    • Superhuman Strength : Holly possesses immense strength, as seen when she physically overpowered and threw the much larger and heavier Cliff Steele through a glass box with just one punch.

Weaknesses

  • Power Limitation: She can only access her powers when she is sleepwalking. In order to maintain herself in this state, she constantly wore headphones that played Barry Manilow.

Behind the scenes

  • In the DC comics, Sleepwalk was Holly McKenzie , a member of the Brotherhood of Dada who had superhuman strength and stamina, but could only access her powers when asleep. Her first appearance in the comics was Doom Patrol #26 (September, 1989).
