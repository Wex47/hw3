Charles Forsythe is an agent of the Bureau of Normalcy , who was responsible for containing and weaponing the Negative Spirit inside Larry Trainor . Forsythe also oversaw the operation to acquire Flex Mentallo and he took Mentallo’s wife Dolores hostage to force him to cooperate with the Bureau.
