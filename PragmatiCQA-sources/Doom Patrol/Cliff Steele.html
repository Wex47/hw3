Clifford "Cliff" Steele (born May 23, 1956) is a former race car driver who got into a car accident engineered by Dr. Niles Caulder . While Cliff's body was decimated beyond repair, Caulder was able to salvage his brain and placed it into a robotic body. When the Chief was kidnapped by Mr. Nobody , Cliff and his friends started looking for him.

Cliff visited Florida to reunite with his daughter, Clara , but Cliff was unable to reveal the truth to Clara because he was nervous. When they confronted Nobody, Cliff and the team found out that Niles was responsible for their accidents, causing him and the others to get upset with Niles and split up. But once again the team came together to save Dorothy Spinner , but by saving her they became diminutive in size.

Returning to his normal size, Cliff tried to work things out with Clara to no avail and Cliff worked alongside Chief to try to feel again. Upon learning that Clara discovered the truth, Cliff and Clara reconciled. Cliff and the team went to a fair to help Niles and Dorothy, but were defeated by Candlemaker .

When he began to suffer from Parkinson's, Cliff began using pills to heal himself while helping the team and Laura De Mille stop the Sisterhood of Dada . Cliff tried to be a good grandfather. After the Eternal Flagellation ended, Cliff promised his daughter to give up the pills, but he was kidnapped by the Brotherhood of Evil , who took his robot body from him. Upon obtaining a giant body, Cliff and the team decided to become superheroes.

Losing his body

The Car Accident

At one point, Cliff was a famous race car driver. Once a loving father and husband, his fame and success went to his head and he began to neglect his family and involved himself in multiple affairs, including one with his daughter’s nanny, Giselle . During one of these races in 1988, he found out his wife Kate was having an affair with his best friend Bump as a form of revenge for Cliff's infidelity. Distracted by this revelation, he nearly crashed into another car, sent out of control by the Chief to mutilate Cliff and make him immortal, but managed to dodge it at the last minute, thus gaining the lead and winning the race.

After the race was over, Cliff assaulted Bump with his trophy and once again had sex with Giselle. However, Cliff felt guilty for his treatment of his family and wanted to reconnect with them. He called Kate, leaving a voicemail in which he promised to be better for them, which spurred Kate to answer his call. Kate decided to give him another chance and allowed Cliff to pick up both her and her daughter that same night. While driving, Cliff was distracted by his family and not focused on driving. He heard his wife scream his name right before he noticed a semi truck that had jack knifed in the road. Once again, the accident was purposely caused by Caulder in a second, note successful, attempt at destroying Cliff's human body. The car plowed under the truck's trailer, decapitating Kate and destroying Cliff's entire body, save for his brain, which was recovered by Dr. Niles Caulder .

Revived by Dr. Caulder

In 1995, Cliff's body was completed and he was awakened by Niles in his home . He met Rita Farr and Larry Trainor , who tried to help him adjust to his new body and home. Cliff believed the accident that destroyed his body was the car landing on him in his final race. After Larry helped him walk, however, listening to a recording of his voice reawakened the repressed memories of what really happened. This realization drove him into hiding in his room for years. Larry brought him a set of remote control cars to play with, which he at first ignored, but by 2019, he'd assembled them and possibly implemented some additions of his own.

Personality

Cliff is an complex person, just like the rest of his "family" , although he is kind, loyal, open minded, accepting and has a strong paternal nature to not only is own daughter but to Jane, Cyborg and later Dorothy. Despite his good personality traits he is what is described as an adrenaline junkie, originally aspiring to the thrill seeking career of a race car driver, but his fast rise to success went to his head and his compulsion evolved into sex, alcohol and drugs destroying his once happy marriage. Even as a robot, when he was fighting Von Fuchs he lost control of himself and slaughtered dozens and instantly regret it afterwards.

After his "accident" and death of his wife, Cliff experienced severe survivor's guilt that subsequently heightened his underlying anger issues, which leaves him fragile, prone to bursts of rage and to speak almost in constant profanity.

Cliff is though very self aware to his faults and even though no matter how miserable he is in his robotic body, he see it makes him a better person, even refusing Mr Nobody's offer to change his history and regain his humanity.

Powers and abilities

Powers

  • Cybernetic enhancements : After his accident, Cliff was left with only his brain functioning, prompting Niles Caulder to build him a brand new body, the outer shell formed by multiple layers of special flexible ceramic metal.
    • Enhanced strength : Due to his robotic physiology, Cliff is capable of lifting much more than an average human, which was demonstrated when he lifted up a piece of street in order to create a barrier against Rita Farr . He was able to overpower and tackle Cyborg through a solid wood wall. He has also shown to be able to throw and tear apart human bodies with ease and send a far larger animal than himself flying across the air, such as the rats when he was shrunk.
    • Enhanced durability : Cliff’s robot body is highly durable, described by the chief as an "Indestructible Man" and dose indeed seem to be resistant if not impervious to concussive force, so much so that he fell to earth from space and the only injury he received was his legs temporary malfunctioning hours later. Cliff's vulnerability is limited though to edged superior metals e.g. Cyborgs robotic finger projected by an explosion and Silver Tongue 's words.
    • Superhuman stamina : Due to his robot body, Cliff has no need for food or sleep and he does not tire.
    • Psychic immunity: Cliff's robot body gives him at least some degree of immunity to mind control as he was able to resist Karen's ability to make others feel affection for her. He was also immune to Flex Mentallo's ability to make the rest of the team orgasm.
    • Immortality : Cliff's robot body makes him immune to ageing and means he will live indefinitely.
    • Disease Immunity : Cliff's robot body makes him immune to all diseases and toxins.
    • Oxygen independence: Cliff’s Robot body allows him to be able to breath in space.

Abilities

  • Driving: Being a former race car driver, Cliff possesses finely honed driving capabilities.
  • Dancing: Prior to his accident, Cliff watched the movie Flashdance a total of 87 times, and as a result is familiar with a variety of dances.
  • Hand-to-hand Combat: Cliff has shown to possess moderate fighting skills, using his enhanced strength and highly durable robot body to his advantage. He was able to easily overpower Heinrich Von Fuchs ' clones and tear them apart.

Weaknesses

  • Loss of senses: Without an organic body, Cliff lost three of the human body's five senses: taste, smell, and touch.
  • Reduced speed: Cliff is slower than an average human since his metal body weighs a substantial amount.
  • Trauma: Cliff's experiences have left him very emotionally fragile and he still struggles greatly with the emotional toll of his accident, as well as losing his body and being unable to experience most of the typical human senses.
  • Malnutrition: While being only a brain in a robot body makes Cliff effectively immortal, his brain still requires nutrition to stay alive. His body is fitted with a nutrient tank to keep him alive. Any disruption of that tank can cause him to go hallucinate, or worse, like having a stroke.
