Willoughby Kipling is a friend of the late Niles Caulder , and a practiced chaos magician. He is "very big in the world of international strangeness". He is cynical and pragmatic, and is willing to make sacrifices and morally grey choices to ensure the safety of himself and the world.

Zombies and Werebutts

Later, Kipling spoke with the head of Caulder, whom he managed to revive, to help him win Baphomet in love, but he was unsuccessful. Kipling was visited by Darren Jones , a vengeful enemy of Caulder's patients, who stole Niles' head and Kipling's coat, to send a message to the team to rescue the Chief. Kipling went to the manor to warn the team about Jones' threat, but was surprised to see that they turned into zombies. Kipling met Laura De Mille , who was the new recruit for the team. Annoyed, Kipling showed the video that Jones wanted them to see. He revealed that he had a perfect plan to save Niles, but as usual, the team ruined everything by turning zombies.

Kipling and Laura wore hats and scents to avoid being killed by the zombies. Upon arriving at Jones' farm, Kipling gave Laura dragon urine to make Jones explode. Kipling and the team entered the farm, but were found by Jones. Kipling heard the whole story from Darren and was upset when Laura failed her plan. When an army of Were-Butts led by Darren attacked, Kipling and the team teamed up to brutally fight the butts, until they recovered Niles' head. Kipling and Laura discovered that the team could be cured if they ate Niles' brain. Kipling understood that Niles would not be able to return, so he had an emotional goodbye with his friend, before Niles told him that Baphomet loves him. Kipling thanked Niles and went through the portal to return home.

Personality

Despite being firmly on the side of good, Kipling is often blunt and disrespectful even when his life is in danger. He often employs risky strategies and is perfectly willing to manipulate his friends to achieve his goals and is flippant when called upon to defend his actions.

Powers and abilities

Powers

  • Chaos magic : Kipling is a powerful magic user who is well-versed in the occult.
    • Conjuration: He is able to conjure various weapons and spirits out of thin air.
      • Spirit conjuring: Kipling was able to conjure a blue horse head to tell him where Elliot was.
      • Weapon summoning: Kipling has conjured weapons like a flaming sword in order to fight the cult of the unwritten book.
    • Portal generation: Using a magic cigarette, Kipling can generate magical portals to various locations, like when he opened a portal to take the doom patrol to the cult of the unwritten book.
    • Cloaking spell: Kipling used a cloaking spell to hide Elliot and could not find it.
    • Longevity: Kipling stated in Wax Patrol he was a child in the 1500s

Abilities

  • Mystical knowledge: Kipling possesses advanced knowledge of the nature and workings of supernatural forces and artifacts.
  • Master manipulator: Even without magic, Kipling is very good at getting his allies and enemies to do as he wishes.
  • Master swordsman: Using a flaming sword that he conjured, Kipling defeated many Cult of the Unwritten Book members.

Equipment

  • Mystical artifacts: Kipling has several mystical artifacts that he has obtained throughout his life, like a mystical cigarette. He uses them as a focal point for his spells.

Behind the scenes

  • Willoughby is an adaptation of the comic character Willoughby Kipling . Originally, the role of Willoughby Kipling was supposed to be filled by John Constantine, but editorial prevented writer Grant Morrison from using the character, so Kipling was created as a stand-in.
