Biography

Jill-In-Irons is one of the many alters of Kay Challis whose mind fragmented due to suffering sexual abuse from her own father at the young age of five.

Although unknown when exactly Jill-in-Irons first manifested in Kay's psyche, its been shown that she has existed since prior to Kay's experimentation that gave them their Metahuman powers and was present during Miranda 's time as the Primary.

As seen during the catastrophic incident of Miranda's sexual experience with her boyfriend Johnny, Jill-in-Irons was one of the alters present during Miranda's forcible return to The Underground and glared at her in anger as she was berated for her failure to "keep them safe" by Hammerhead and The Secretary .
