Biography

Mama Pentecost is one is one of the many alters of Kay Challis who developed DID due to childhood abuse.

Powers

Metahuman physiology: Mama Pentecost is one of the many alters residing within Kay Challis , each of whom manifests their own power when in control of their shared body.

  • Multilingualism : According to herself and The Secretary , Mama Pentecost is fluent in most languages which includes light and color. She was able to understand Keeg.
