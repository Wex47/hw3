Biography

After Scants infected a piece of canvas, they were visited by Vic Stone , Roni Evers , Miranda , Larry Trainor , and Willoughby Kipling , taking all of them prisoner. They were taken to the queen, who had them hooked up to machines, sucking out idyat to convert into uma jelly . Tasting some of the uma jelly, she began to calmly taunt all of them. However, Miranda was unaffected, and had Silver Tongue take control of her body, destroying the queen's water bed, before killing them all with Lucy Fugue 's electricity.

Powers

  • Scant Queen Physiology: As Scant Queen, she has displayed various unique powers and abilities unique to her in comparison to the regular Scants .
    • Superhuman Abilities: By drinking and ingesting the highly purified version of Idyot called Uma Jelly, the Scant Queen can gain numerous superhuman abilities and powers once she consumed enough.
      • Teleportation: As seen during her meeting with Larry , Roni Evers , Vic and Miranda , the Scant Queen possesses the ability to appear out of thin air to her desired location. As seen where she appeared before them inside her tent in the White Space together with her water bed.
      • Levitation: The Queen later showcased the power to levitate when she dranked the newly harvested Uma jelly from Vic, Roni, Larry and Willoughby Kipling .
