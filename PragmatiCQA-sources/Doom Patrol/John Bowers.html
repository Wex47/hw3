Biography

John Bowers enlisted in the Air Force as a mechanic, being promoted to the rank of Staff Sergeant. During his time there, he developed a secret relationship with Larry Trainor . One day, when Larry tested the X-15 , John monitored the comms. When the plane began to crash down to Earth , he raced out to the crash site, discovering Larry's burnt self, crawling from the wreckage.

In 2019 John was visited by Larry who hoped to make peace while they had a chance. After a happy reunion, John passed away peacefully and left Larry saddened their reunion didn't last
