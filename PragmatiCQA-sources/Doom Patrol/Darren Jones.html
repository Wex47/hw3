Darren Jones is an ex-agent of the Bureau of Normalcy , an organization dedicated to eradicating anything they deem to be abnormal. Darren has a particular vendetta against Danny the Street , whom he blames for his friend Morris Wilson 's disappearance. When he attempted to destroy Danny many years later, he was thwarted by Maura Lee Karupt and the Dannyzens .
