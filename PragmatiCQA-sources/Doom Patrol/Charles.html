Biography

During the filming of Forbidden Congo , Rita Farr talked to Charles about having the amputee focus puller removed from the set, believing him to be an eyesore. Charles attempted to reason with her, but she wouldn't budge, so he had the man removed. Following that, they filmed a different scene, during which Rita fell through a gangway into a river. Shocked, Charles ordered crew members to help her, and they had her brought ashore. When she came to, they all stood at a distance, shocked at her face suddenly deforming.
