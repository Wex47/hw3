Valentina Vostok (a.k.a. Moscow ) was a cosmonaut who went into Space in 1955 in a spacecraft designed by Niles Caulder . Like Larry Trainor does, she has a Negative Spirit . She learned how to be in harmony with the Negative Spirit in five years. She was also a good friend with Zip Callahan and Specs .

Powers & Abilities

Powers

  • Meta-human Physiology: Upon being exposed a radioactive cloud while in deep space, Valentine soon found herself possessed by her own Negative Spirit that then gave her a number of superhuman powers and abilities.
    • Longevity: Similar to Larry Trainor , upon being possessed and radiated by the Cloud and possessed by her Negative Spirit, Valentina's aging processes has now either been completely halted or slowed down. As seen where despite being in space for over sixty years by the time of her return in 2020 to earth, Valentina has still retained the physical form of a woman in her prime.
    • Radiation: Similar to Larry, as a direct result of her exposure to the cloud, Valentina now produces and creates highly dangerous and toxic radiation within her body that could indiscriminately kill anyone and everyone around her.
      • Radiation Control: However unlike Larry, Valentina has instead shown to have obtain a level of control over her condition. As seen where she stated to have full conscious control over her radiation levels and emissions and keep it contained underneath her skin to prevent it from killing others uncontrollably.
    • Symbiotic Relationship: Also unlike Larry, Valentina was shown to have gained a complete understanding with her own Negative Spirit and unlike Larry and his own, can now completely understand and communicate with her and gain full access to her powers and knowledge possessed by the spirit.

Behind the scenes

  • In DC comics, Valentina Vostok is a Soviet Air Force Lieutenant who later becomes the superhero known as Negative Woman. Her first appearance was in DC Showcase #94 (August 1977).
