Biography

It is unknown specifically when Driller Bill split. When she met Cliff in the Underground, Driller Bill helped Hammerhead apprehend him.

Powers and Abilities

Powers

  • Metahuman physiology: Driller Bill is one of the many alters residing within Kay Challis , each of whom manifests their own power when in control of their shared body.
  • Enhanced strength : Driller Bill is shown to have above average strength, being able to easily punch Cliff's abdomen.
