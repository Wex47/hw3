Biography

Flaming Katy is one of the many alters of Kay Challis who developed DID due to childhood abuse.

Although unconfirmed, it was shown that Katy split off some time during Jane's stay being institutionalized in Asylums. Having been created as a protector alter when they are "questioned" under high amounts of stress.

Its been hinted that along with Hammerhead , Katy had been one of the primary alters that defended and protected Jane whenever she faced harassment. This however instead resulted in Jane being blamed for her typically violent outbursts and punished harshly by those around her.

Eventually after Jane with Doctor Harrison 's help was liberated from the Asylum by Niles Caulder , Katy was stated to have made an appearance when Jane had been clothes shopping, where due to an incident, Katy ended up appearing and presumably attacked whoever was "harassing" Jane.

Later on, after Baby Doll and Dorothy Spinner 's friendship turned sour, she surfaced to protect Baby Doll from Dorothy's attacks. Which unfortunately resulted in the death of one of Dorothy's imaginary friends.

Angry and distraught at the loss of her friend and blaming Baby Doll and Flaming Katy, Dorothy proceeded to then summon her worst imaginary friend, the Candlemaker , to kill them. Leading him to then make his way into the Underground to hunt down and kill Baby Doll after she retreated to the Underground. Flaming Katy then appeared and attempted to defend the alters, but was instead overpowered and put out by the Candlemaker.

Eventually the other alters collected her body and Baby Doll's and proceeded to give her a funeral procession as they made their way to the Well in order to dispose of their bodies. Despite Jane's own attempt to prevent the other alters from throwing their remains into the Well, Miranda 's reappearance and claims that the Well revived her through time only convinced them to throw their bodies in the Well, where Flaming Katy's Station immediately collapsed due to them no longer being needed.

Powers and abilities

Powers

  • Metahuman physiology : Flaming Katy is one of the many alters residing within Crazy Jane each of whom manifests their own power when in control of their shared body.
    • Pyrokinesis : Flaming Katy has showcased immense pyrokinetic powers. Having been shown to primarily ignite herself on fire and create destructive powerful blasts of fire to attack and create large fire balls to hurl at her targets.
      • Flight : Flaming Katy has the power to fly via using her pyrokinesis towards the ground.
