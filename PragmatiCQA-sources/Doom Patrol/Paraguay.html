History

In 1948, Eric Morden visited Heinrich Von Fuchs to undergo an experiment that turned him into a being known as "Mr. Nobody".

The Doom Manor group set out for Paraguay, where they discovered Von Fuchs, and the connection between the Chief and the two villains.

Steve Larson gained his superpowers in Paraguay.
