Biography

After Rita Farr entered Classic Cafe , Debra quipped how much she reminded her of Rita Farr, unbeknownst to her that it actually was. She mentioned how much she'd loved her films growing up, prompting Rita to urge her to continue. The two sat chatting for a while, before Debra brought up her father 's claim that Rita had gone into pornography, causing Rita to lose control of her gelatinous body and leave.
