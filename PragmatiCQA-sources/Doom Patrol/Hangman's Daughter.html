Biography

The Hangman's Daughter is one of the many alters of Kay Challis who developed DID due to sexual abuse from her father. Hangman's Daughter is shown to be one of the more cordial and kindhearted alters that does not shown much aggression to those around her.

Years later, after it started raining, the Hangman's Daughter lamented not being normal to Cliff , finishing a painting started by Crazy Jane .

Powers and abilities

Powers

• Psychic Paintings : The Hangman's Daughter has the power to create psychic paintings. This was proven when made a painting of Cyborg killing the whole team

Abilities

  • Painter: Like with the primary alter of Crazy Jane , the Hangman's Daughter has been shown to be a talented painter.
