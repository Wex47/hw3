Biography

Flit is one of the many alters of Kay Challis whose mind fragmented due to suffering sexual abuse from her own father at the young age of five.

Personality

Flit seems to be one of the more cooperative alters as she didn't hesitate to help the others members of the team and listening to Jane and the others request for transportation. This is seen where she would teleporting them to Paraguay when she could've easily teleported anywhere. She also helped Larry try and visit his son in the hospital despite being dangerous for her.

Powers and abilities

Powers

  • Metahuman physiology: Flit is one of the many alters residing within Crazy Jane each of whom manifests their own power when in control of their shared body.
    • Teleportation : Flit is able to teleport between locations and can bring others with her. The exact limits of this power are unknown but Flit was able to teleport from the United States to Paraguay with little effort, indicating that distance is not an issue. It also seems that she can teleport any living being, human or not, as long they are either in direct contact with her or is touching another she about teleport. This was seen in their battle at the Ant Farm where Flit easily teleported the Negative Spirit. The amount of creatures she can teleport also seems to be unknown as she easily teleported a large quantity of beings from the Ant Farm back to Doom Manor.
    • Enhanced strength : Flit is also capable of tossing around grown men with ease and breaking their necks with her bare hands.

Abilities

  • Hand-to-Hand Combat: As seen during her battle against Heinrich Von Fuchs 's Clones, Flit was also a highly capable brawler and hand-to-hand fighter. Having been seen to combine her teleportation with her fighting skills to easily constantly launch several surprise attacks against multiple attackers and showed enough skill to fend off them off and severely injure/kill them.
