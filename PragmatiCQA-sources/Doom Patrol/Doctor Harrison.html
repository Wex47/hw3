Doctor Harrison is one of Kay Challis's many alters and as of the end of season 3 the current Primary. Doctor Harrison is a manipulative, yet charming personality with the ability to manipulate people's minds. When in control of their shared body, Doctor Harrison possesses a white streak in her hair and glowing blue eyes. In The Underground , she appears a middle aged woman wearing a business suit.

Biography

Doctor Harrison is one of the many alters of Kay Challis who developed DID due to childhood trauma at the hands of her father.

It is unknown when exactly Doctor Harrison came to be, but she was shown to primarily manifest during Kay's (Jane's) time being institutionalized. Where due to her psychiatrists and doctors being less than kind to her during her "treatments" and "therapies", Doctor Harrison would surface and seemingly and ironically manipulate and defend her by psychoanalyzing her doctor's instead and convincing them to instead commit immense self injury using her powers. Then Mister Nobody freezes time and undo her restraints. Mister Nobody tell her to start a cult called the recreator. Doctor Harrison convinces most the other psychiatrist patient and employs . During a meet they decided what the book for recreator and how to summon it, a fellow cult member, Marilyn suggested the writing be should summon by ring of giant bell. Doctor Harrison choose her to remind her of what she needs to do. One night Dr. Harrison gets taken to a facility because of her actions. When she was put the van,the driver is none other, Niles Caulder himself. Niles frees her and promise no one will hurt her. While Niles was driving, Mister Nobody speak to Doctor Harrison about after stopping the Decreator, he want her to find something.

Powers and abilities

Powers

  • Metahuman physiology: Doctor Harrison is one of the many alters residing within Crazy Jane each of whom manifests their own power when in control of their shared body.
    • Persuasion : Doctor Harrison is able to use her metahuman charm to manipulate people, instilling ideas that her victims find they must act on, as seen where she was able to "convince" one of the psychiatrists in the Asylum to shove his pen into his eye, when he began psychologically abusing Jane. At her peak, Doctor Harrison used this power to convert an entire psychiatric ward to her newly invented Cult of the Recreator .
      • Empathy Manipulation: Doctor Harrison can feel other people emotion and manipulate them
      • Mind Control: this show that doctor Harrison can take control of the person when she make eye contact with the person

Abilities

  • Psychoanalysis: Doctor Harrison is adept at observing people's behavior, and from that concluding what trauma they have endured, which she can then use to manipulate them.
  • Charisma: Doctor Harrison is a talented and charismatic leader who is able to form a cult at the mental hospital.
