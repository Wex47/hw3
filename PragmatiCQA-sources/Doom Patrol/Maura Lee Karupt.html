Maura Lee Karupt (born Morris Wilson ) was an agent of the Bureau of Normalcy , until a search and destroy mission took Morris to Danny the Street . It was there that Morris felt comfortable enough to embrace her true self, and she warned Danny of the Bureau's attack in time for them to escape. Years later, when Darren Jones attempted to destroy Danny again, Maura thwarted him with the help of the Dannyzens .
