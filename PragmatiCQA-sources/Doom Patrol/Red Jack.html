Red Jack , also known as Jack the Ripper , was a powerful entity that fed on pain. He met Niles Caulder in 1888, and was the being who sparked Caulder's idea years later to form the Bureau of Oddities . He was killed by Caulder in 2020, who stabbed Jack with his own knife.

Murders in London

Red Jack came to earth and went to London to feed on people's pain by murdering them and started a murder spree and stealing people's souls and turning them into butterflies and Red Jack had been nicknamed Jack the Ripper and many other nicknames.

Encounter with Niles Caulder

In 1888, while murdering a woman, Red Jack realized that a boy named, Niles Caulder witnessed the murder and Jack smiled excitedly at Niles because he did not run away or get scared when he saw him and Red Jack showed Niles his razor and Red Jack finished for murdering his victim and said goodbye to Niles and Red Jack disappeared.
