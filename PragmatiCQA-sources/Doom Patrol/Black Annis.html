Biography

Black Annis is one of the many alters of Kay Challis who developed DID after abuse from her father.

Although unknown when she was manifested within Kay, Black Annis was implied to have been one of the oldest alters present within The Underground , as it was implied that she knew about the Weird Sisters and knew what it meant when they sent an alter to pass through her hallway into the Well. As seen where she allowed Jane passage through her path and told her goodbye, further implying that she had also done the same to Miranda .

Powers and abilities

Powers

  • Metahuman physiology : Black Annis is one of the many alters residing within Jane, each of whom manifests their own power when in control of their shared body.
    • Enhanced Sense of Smell : Black Annis is able to easily detect when someone is approaching her purely by scent and was able to easily tell the difference between a man and a woman, as seen when she was able to immediately detect Cliff and identify that he was a man after one whiff of his scent.
