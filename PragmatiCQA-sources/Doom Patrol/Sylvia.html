Biography

Sylvia is one of the many alters of Kay Challis who developed DID due to childhood abuse.

Years later, when Kay returned to Doom Manor , Sylvia began to cause chaos around the mansion, but was soon overtaken by Hammerhead .

Later after the revelation of Niles Caulder 's involvement with the "experimental accidents" that gave Jane and the others their powers was revealed, Slyvia briefly manifested during Jane's confrontation with Joshua Clay where she attempted to physically harm him.
