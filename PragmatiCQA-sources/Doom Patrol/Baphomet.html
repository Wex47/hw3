Biography

Meeting Caulder and Kipling

At some point, Baphomet met Willoughby Kipling and Niles Caulder , with whom he felt an attraction. Secretly, Kipling loved her.

The Decreator

Baphomet was summoned by Kipling at Doom Manor to report in song form on the plan of the Cult of the Unwritten Book and the book, who was Elliot Patterson .

She was summoned once more and was glad to see Niles and the two flirted. Upon learning of the existence of the Rewritten Book, Baphomet informed his friends how to find it.

Kipling's Message

Kipling subsequently sent Baphomet a message inviting her out on a date, before being summoned by Niles.
