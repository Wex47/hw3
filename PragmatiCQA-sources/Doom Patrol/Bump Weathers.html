Bump Weathers was Cliff Steele's best friend and his crew-mechanic when he was a famous race car driver. He had an affair with Kate Steele , which led to a fall out between him and Cliff. After her parents' supposed deaths, he became the adoptive father of Clara Steele .

Biography

Bump began working as a member of Cliff Steele 's pit crew around 1988. At the same time, Kate started an affair with him as a form of revenge for Cliff's own infidelity. During one race, Kate chose to hang all over him and grab his crotch to distract Cliff, which completely threw a racing Cliff off. However, Cliff eventually won, and assaulted Bump with his celebratory wine, knocking him out cold. After Cliff and Kate's death in an accident, Bump adopted Clara and raised her as his own daughter, so that she wouldn't feel the absence of her parents. Nearly 20 years after the crash, Cliff is angry to learn that Bump is Clara's adoptive father but reflects that he probably would have been a better father than Cliff himself. Cliff later sees on the news that Bump was allegedly attacked and killed by an alligator named Frances, leaving Cliff's daughter Clara without a father, again. In a bar at Cypressville, Florida, Big D describes Bump as someone that would rush right into danger without thinking straight and Clara describes him as having an urgent way of looking at life. Clara gifted him a gold watch (that Cliff won in the Dallas Speedway in 1976 against Flip Jackson) inscribed with "To Dad, you're my hero." Cliff learns that Bump really was a great father and a good friend, who took in Clara under his wing without asking her for anything in return. Cliff later avenges Bump by killing Frances and retrieves the golden watch for Clara.
