Behind the scenes

  • In DC Comics, Little Sisters of Our Lady of the Razor are children drawn from the cult who want eternal youth. They are operated on to stay young. By the time they realize that being kids forever isn't all that great, there is nothing they can do; hence, they decide to take the pain out on others with their razor blades.
