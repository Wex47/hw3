Biography

The Balladeer is one of the many alters of Kay Challis whose mind fragmented due to suffering sexual abuse from her own father at a young age. Its unknown when exactly the Balladeer herself was created, but due to her station being located in the deepest parts of The Underground , none of the other alters have ever interacted with her.

After the apparent deaths of Baby Doll and Flaming Katy , the Balladeer led a procession to the well. Once they arrived there, all of the alters were startled by the shock appearance of Miranda from the well, who they'd all believed to have perished. Later she was present during the destructions of Flaming Katy and Baby Dolls Stations upon their bodies being thrown into the Well and during Miranda's retaking of her previous position as Primary from Jane. Ballader can be seen in the form of a puppet in Kay's mind in episode 8, along with the other alters she destroys Harry, holds his head and laugh.
