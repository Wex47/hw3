Doom Manor is a residence owned by Dr. Niles Caulder in Covington , Ohio . The property is located on Danny the Street and is locked up to the outside world.

History

After an accident after 1988, Cliff Steele was given a robotic body, and began living in Doom Manor. After the disappearance of Niles Caulder , Vic Stone began staying in the guest room.
