Biography

Penny Farthing is one of the many alters of Kay Challis who developed DID due to childhood abuse.

Although unknown when Penny Farthing was created, it was shown that her main duty and "job" within The Underground 's hierarchy is to "run away from danger". Seemingly being Kay Challis's submissive/cowardly alter that surfaces whenever she or her system desires to avoid conflict and instead prioritize their own survival instead.

Penny is shown to be one of the eldest alters of Kay, having been present during her adolescents during Miranda 's time as primary.

During 1969, Penny was one of the alters that surfaced when Miranda was pressured into having swinger sex with one of her boyfriend's friends when she was triggered by the experience of Kay's past trauma, with her surfacing during mid sex and attempted to plead him to stop before she was replaced by Pretty Polly .
