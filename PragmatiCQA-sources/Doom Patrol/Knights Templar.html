History

The Knights Templar created an instructional video regarding how to avoid being infested by Scants . Grand Master Willoughby Kipling showed it to the residents of Doom Manor .

The Knights Templar later joined for a meeting and conferred with Bunbury , who expressed how it had heard of rumblings of a cataclysmic event.
