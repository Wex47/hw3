Sinornithomimus was an Ornithomimid from late Cretaceous China. It was about 8.2 feet (2.5 meters) long and weighed about as much as a sheep. Many individuals, including nearly complete skeletons, were found together, suggesting that Sinornithomimus lived in herds.
