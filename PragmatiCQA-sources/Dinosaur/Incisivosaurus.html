Incisivosaurus
Scientific classification

Incisivosaurus was a basal Oviraptorosaur Maniraptoran Theropod . It had feathers, a beak, and two enlarged incisors.

Description

The Incisivosaurus had a skull that showed buck teeth. Its resemblence to Protarchaeopteryx may mean close relation.
