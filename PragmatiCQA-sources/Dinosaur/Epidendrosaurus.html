Epidendrosaurus is a Maniraptoran from middle Jurassic China. It was 12 inches (30 cm) and weighed about 5 lbs. The original Epidendrosaurus was a hatchling. There is an ongoing argument about wether Epidendrosaurus is its own species or the same species as Scansoriopteryx .

Popular Culture

  • Epidendrosaurus made an appearance on the documentary called " Bizarre Dinosaurs ".
