Dinosaur King is an arcade game based on the Japanese beetle-fighting arcade game Mushiking , but usesÂ dinosaurs instead of beetles. It is made by SEGA, who also created Mushiking , Love and Berry and Sonic the Hedgehog . Eventually, it became a popular anime . With the popularity of the and the arcade game, SEGA and Upper Deck decided to build a trading card game around it, as well as a DS Game . To accompany the anime, a manga adaption has been made.
