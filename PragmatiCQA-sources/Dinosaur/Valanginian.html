In the geologic timescale, the Valanginian is an age or stage of theEarly or Lower Cretaceous. It spans between 139.8 ± 3.0 Ma and 132.9 ± 2.0 Ma (million years ago). The Valanginian stage succeeds the Berriasian stage of the Lower Cretaceous and precedes theHauterivian stage of the Lower Cretaceous.
