The Late Cretaceous was the last era of the Cretaceous , spanning from 100.5 - 66 million years ago. Many famous animals, such as Tyrannosaurus , Velociraptor and Triceratops lived during this period.

Epochs

  • Cenomanian (~100.5-93.5 ma)
  • Turonian (93.5 - 89.8 ma)
  • Santonian (89.8 - 83.6 ma)
  • Campanian (83.6-72.1 ma)
  • Maastrichtian (72.1-66 ma)

Biostratigraphy

  • Assemblage zone of Carnotaurus sastrei ( Campanian )
