Maniraptorans (hand snatchers) are a group of dinosaurs that include the most advanced Coelurosaurs.  The following genera are not Alvarezsaurs, Oviraptorosaurs, Therizinosauroids, Deinonychosaurs, or Avialians. This are the miscellaneous members of the Maniraptoria .

Time Period

Maniraptorans lived from middle Jurassic to present (when including birds), all other groups died out in the Cretaceous-Paleogene extinction event.

Common Members

Common members include Archaeornithoides , Epidendrosaurus , Euronychodon , Kakuru , Nuthetes , Palaeopteryx , Paronychodon , Pedopenna , Yaverlandia , and Yixianosaurus
