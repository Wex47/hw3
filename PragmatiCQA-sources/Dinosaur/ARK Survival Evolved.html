Ark: Survival Evolved is set on an island in space called an ARK. Players must survive in a world filled with roaming dinosaurs and other extinct animals.

In September 2016, an expansion pack called Scorched Earth was released. The new animals introduced in it are a mixture of living animals and fantasy creatures.

Reception

ARK: Survival Evolved received "mixed or average" reviews on Metacritic getting a metascore of 70/100 on PC and 69/100 on both PS4 & Xbox One. However, its Nintendo Switch version received "generally unfavorable" reviews holding a metascore of 29/100.
