Thyreophora is a group of armoured Dinosaurs, part of the Ornitischia.

Groups

  • Ankylosauria
    • Ankylosauridae
    • Nodosauridae
    • Scelidosauridae ?
  • Stegosauria
    • Stegosauridae
    • Huayangosauridae
  • Scutellosaurus *

*Scutellosaurus has no actual family. He is the first thyreophoran despite not being part of the groups listed above.

They lived from the Triassic to the Cretaceous (229-65 Mya).

Trivia

  • The Stegosaurs are the only thyreophorans that did not survive until the great extinction, since they died out 125 Mya.
