Nqwebasaurus is a Primitive Coelurosaur from early Cretaceous South Africa. It was 12 inches (30 cm) and weighed about 10 lbs. Possibly an early relative of the Ornithomimosaurs . Nqwebasaurus has the unofficial nickname "Kirky", due to being found in the Kirkwood.
