The Dilophosauridae ("double-ridged lizards") is a family of carnivorous theropod dinosaurs, possibly belonging to the Ceratosauria. The only 2 members are Dilophosaurus (North America, Asia), Dracovenator (Africa) and Sinosaurus (Asia) which all lived from the Late Triassic to the Early Jurassic . All five of them also have crests. Perutyrannus , Shuangbaisaurus and Zupaysaurus possibly also belong to this group.
