Squamata is the order of diapsid reptiles that includes lizards (excluding the tuatara), snakes, and amphisbaenians.  This order has existed from the Triassic to today.  A few members of the order are Pristiguana , Ardeosaurus , Chilingosaurus , Adamisaurus , Chamops , Eupodophis , Dolichosaurus , Pontosaurus , Opetiosaurus , Primaderma , and Sineoamphisbaena .
