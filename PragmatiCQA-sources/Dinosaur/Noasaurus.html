Noasaurus
Scientific classification

Noasaurus leali (meaning northwest Argentina reptile) is a Noasaurid from late Cretaceous Argentina, 70-65 mya. It was about 7.9 feet (2.4 meters) long and weighed about as much as a beaver. A large claw on this dinosaur was once thought to be a Deinonychosaur -like foot claw, but it is actually a hand claw.
