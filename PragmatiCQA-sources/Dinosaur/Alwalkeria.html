Alwalkeria
Name Alwalkeria
Order Saurischia
Suborder Theropoda
Class Nomen Dubium
Name Translation for Alick Walker
Period Late Triassic 228-204 Million Years Ago
Location India
Diet Carnivore
Size 1 m long, 0.3 m tall, 3 kg in weight

Alwalkeria is a very primitive theropod from late Triassic Lestho and Madagascar. It was about 1.6 feet (50 centimeters) long and weighed about as much as a turkey.

it was named after Alice Walker by Walkeria Chatterjee and Creisler in 1993. Its fossils were discovered in India. It was a bipedal carnivore. It had a long tail which it used to use to ambush its prey and to run fast.
