See also: C-T Extinction The Cretaceous-Tertiary Transition , or the Cretaceous-Paleogene Boundary , is a mass extinction event that happened around 66.038 mya. It caused many fatalities to every single group but fish and amphibia. The pulpy animals and reptilians received the most damage. Mammals survived but 60% died out, including Nemegtebaar and Alphadon. There are many theories.
