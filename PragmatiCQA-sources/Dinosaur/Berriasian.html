In the geological timescale, the Berriasian is an age or stage of theEarly Cretaceous. It is the oldest, or lowest, subdivision in the entireCretaceous. It spanned the time between 145.0 ± 4.0 Ma and 139.8 ± 3.0 Ma (million years ago). The Berriasian succeeds the Tithonian (part of theJurassic) and precedes theValanginian.
