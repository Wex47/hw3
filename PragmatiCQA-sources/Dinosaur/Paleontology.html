Paleontology , also spelled palaeontology or palæontology ( ), is the scientific study of life that existed prior to, and sometimes including, the start of the Epoch (roughly 11,700 years ). It includes the study of fossils to classify organisms and study interactions with each other and their environments (their ). Paleontological observations have been documented as far back as the 5th century BCE. Simple Defination

The simplest definition of "paleontology" is "the study of ancient life". The field seeks information about several aspects of past organisms: "their identity and origin, their environment and evolution, and what they can tell us about the Earth's organic and inorganic past".
