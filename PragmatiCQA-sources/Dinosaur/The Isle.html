The Isle is an in-development dinosaur indie game where you can play as either a human or dinosaur. In it, you have to use your wits in order to survive. The environment is extremely dangerous, and the world is not your friend here. Carnivores have the ability to turn into strains, terrifying mutations that can give them strange abilities. Hyperendocrin Dinosaurs (Type-H/Hypo)

Pros: Extremely Strong, Lots Of Health, Mouth Splitting, Fierce Roars, Can Swim.

Cons: Gets Hungry Fast, Fall Damage.

  • Hyperendocrin Tyrannosaurus
  • Hyperendocrin Spinosaurus
  • Hyperendocrin Giganotosaurus
  • Hyperendocrin Utahraptor
  • Hyperendocrin Carnotaurus
  • Hyperendocrin Colossus

Magna Dinosaurs (Type-M)

Pros: Cannot Damage Other Magnasaurs, Good Health, Decent Speed, Good Turn Rate.

Cons: Less Health Than Type-H, Gets Hungry At A Decent Speed, Fall Damage.

  • Magna Rex
  • Magna Utahraptor

Neurotic Dinosaurs (Type-N/Neuro)

Pros: EMP Charge Can Change The Weather, Slightly Stronger Than Normal Dinosaurs, the ability to use telekinesis, has the ability to talk with any species.

Cons: Weaker Than Other Types.

  • Neurotenic Spinosaurus
  • Neurotenic Rex
  • Neurotenic Echidna
  • Neurotenic Mastermind

Tissoplastic Dinosaurs (Type-T/Tisso)

Pros: Tissoplastic has the ability of camouflage, has the ability of invisibility, has the ability to have its speed increased dramatically, has the ability gives it a small boost to its health, may have to ability to fire venom from its fangs, may have venomous bite.

Cons: Unknown

  • Tissoplastic Spinosaurus
  • Tissoplastic Rex
  • Tissoplastic Reaper
