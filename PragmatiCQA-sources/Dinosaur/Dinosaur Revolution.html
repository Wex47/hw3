Episode One - Evolution's Winners

Episode Two - The Watering Hole

  • Time Period - Late Jurassic, 150 Million Years Ago
  • Location - Portugal

In Late Jurassic Portugal, an Allosaurus with a shattered jaw named Broken Jaw lives at a watering hole, on a hill with a tree, only for his peaceful life to be interrupted by the return of an old enemy, a male Dinheirosaurus named Woodstock and the arrival of a Torvosaurus named Savage.

Episode Three - Survival Tactics

Episode Four - End Game

  • Time Period - Late Cretaceous, 65 million years ago
  • Location - Montana

In Late Cretaceous Montana, A mated pair of Tyrannosaurus rex named Stumpy and Tinkerbelle make several attempts to raise a family, only to be interrupted and menaced by their rival, Jack Palance, and the upcoming K-T Event asteroid that will end the reign of the dinosaurs.
