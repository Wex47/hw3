Plot

The show is about a kid named Dan, who could see dinosaurs in the real world. The show teaches kids about dinosaurs and pterosaurs.

Sequels

The show continued on with Dino Dan: Trek's Adventures, which introduces kids about marine reptiles. And the 3rd show, Dino Dana, introduces kids to Cenozoic animals.
