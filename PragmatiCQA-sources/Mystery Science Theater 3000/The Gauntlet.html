The Gauntlet was a series of experiments devised by Kinga Forrester to break the spirit of test subject Jonah Heston and his robotic companions. Unlike early experiments on Mystery Science Theater 3000 , which inflicted bad movies one at a time, the Gauntlet forced Jonah and the bots to watch six movies back-to-back without rest. The process was a prelude to Kinga's plans to take MST3K on the road.

Whenever discussing the program, either directly or in passing, Kinga waved her arms and spoke very emphatically whenever she said, "the Gauntlet".

The series of experiments were:

  • Experiment #1201 : Mac and Me
  • Experiment #1202 : Atlantic Rim
  • Experiment #1203 : Lords of the Deep
  • Experiment #1204 : The Day Time Ended
  • Experiment #1205 : Killer Fish
  • Experiment #1206 : Ator, the Fighting Eagle

Between experiments, Kinga brought Jonah to Moon 13 to modify the containment vessel for the six new episodes as well as ready it for travel for their upcoming tour. The Gauntlet appeared to come closest to breaking the test subject and bots than most previous individual experiments had, mostly due to fatigue from being forced to watch six movies in a row.

Behind the Scenes

  • The concept of the Gauntlet is a reference to the practice of binge-watching, championed by Season 12 distributor Netflix .
