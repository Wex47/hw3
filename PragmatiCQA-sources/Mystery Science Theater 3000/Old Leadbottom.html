Played by

none

First appearance

K09 - Phase IV

Character type

Guest

Old Leadbottom is a (possibly former) employee at Gizmonic Institute . He had some sort of authority over Dr. Clayton Forrester and Dr. Laurence Erhardt , as they got into trouble when he learned of their experiment Project Morpheus .

Appearances

  • MST3K K09 - Phase IV

Behind-the-scenes

  • "Old Leadbottom" is a nickname from the 1960s sitcom McHale's Navy . It was used for the character Captain Binghamton, who was portrayed by actor Joe Flynn .
  • Old Leadbottom never actually appeared on screen. Dr. Erhardt is seen speaking to him on the phone.
