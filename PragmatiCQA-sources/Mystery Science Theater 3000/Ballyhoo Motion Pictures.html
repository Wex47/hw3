Ballyhoo Motion Pictures is a film company founded by Daniel Griffith. They specialize in documentary pieces about film and television personalities and properties that are of a cult or otherwise obscure nature, ranging from DVD/Blu-ray extras to feature length documentaries. Many of the extras on the Mystery Science Theater 3000 DVD and Blu-ray sets released by Shout! Factory were produced by Ballyhoo.
