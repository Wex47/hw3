Ardy is a worker at the Moon 13 research base who is responsible for sending the movie to Jonah and the Bots. He is located in Moon 14, which is a level below Moon 13. He has a dog named Bonesy , who is gifted with the power of speech.

Behind the scenes

  • Ardy is played by series creator Joel Hodgson , marking only the second character he portrayed on Mystery Science Theater 3000 .
  • His catchphrase "Movie in the hole!" is a take on the old phrase " Fire in the hole ", a warning that an explosive detonation in a confined space is imminent, comically warning the audience how bad the week's experiment will be. (Sadly for Jonah, They all have the same warning.)
  • Ardy pronounces his name phonetically ("R.D."), a reference to "Research & Development", which is a crucial part of any scientific research.
  • Using forced perspective, Ardy is made to look much further away from the tank, as shown in relation to the size of the utility truck parked next to the tank.
