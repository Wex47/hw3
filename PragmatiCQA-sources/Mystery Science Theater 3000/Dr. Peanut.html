Dr. Peanut is a sentient talking ape who served as the assistant to Professor Bobo in Deep Ape .

Although a "doctor", Dr. Peanut often exhibited less intelligence and common sense than the not-much-brighter Bobo. He also often irritated the "Lawgiver", Pearl Forrester , with his stupidity and antics, often incurring her wrath.

Dr. Peanut made his final appearance in The Deadly Mantis . Mike inadvertently helped the bomb-worshiping mutants fix their deadly icon and caused the destruction of Earth and Deep Ape; while it appeared Peanut did not escape the blast, the crew of the Satellite of Love receives a letter from him at the end of the episode. Apparently, the bomb caused a time rift and transported him back into the Wisconsin of Earth's past, where he fell in love with and married a member of Mike's family, perhaps leading to the all-simian Earth seen in the future. Just as Deep Ape and the future earth is a reference to Planet of the Apes , and the bomb-worshiping mutants a reference to its sequel Beneath the Planet of the Apes , Dr. Peanut's time travel is a reference to the second sequel, Escape from the Planet of the Apes .
