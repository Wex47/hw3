Turkey Day '21 was a marathon of eight Mystery Science Theater 3000 episodes that streamed on various online services on November 25th, 2021.

The marathon was hosted by Jonah Heston , Crow T. Robot , Tom Servo , GPC1 and GPC2 and Emily Connor . Throughout the day, they announced the films that would be used in MST3K Season 13 .
