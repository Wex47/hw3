Season 6 is the sixth national season (seventh overall) of Mystery Science Theater 3000 . It aired on Comedy Central from July 16 , 1994 to March 25 , 1995 . It consists of twenty-four episodes, the same as Seasons 3 - 5 (and longer than any other seasons).

Season debuts:

  • Mary Jo Pehl as Pearl Forrester
