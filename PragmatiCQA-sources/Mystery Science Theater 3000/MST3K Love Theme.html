The "MST3K Love Theme" is the main theme song of Mystery Science Theater 3000 , which serves to summarize the general concept and backstory behind the show as well as set the mood. The lyrics are regularly altered to reflect changes in the cast or plot, but the song has been present in every season of the show.

Ashley Holtgraver version

As part of Turkey Day '15 , Ashley Holtgraver performed an acoustic version of the "Love Theme" with new lyrics. Her band, Freezepop , then covered it (with lyrics slightly altered again) for The Final Countdown . These versions were released on the digital collection Songs in the Key of MST under the titles "#BringBackMST3K Theme" and "#WeBroughtBackMST3K Theme", respectively.

Notes

  • The theme song originated as a way to quickly establish the show's premise in the vein of Gilligan's Island or Land of the Lost . The characters Dr. Forrester and Dr. Earhardt came into being through writing the lyrics.
  • In seasons 8-10, Crow says "I'm different!", referring to the fact that he was now being performed by Bill Corbett instead of Trace Beaulieu
  • shows a storyboard from a new episode that confirmed that the "Love Theme" would be back for the new season of MST3K. (See the "First Look: The New Opening Sequence!" section on that page.)
  • At the beginning of the introductory video for Let's Make More MST3K & Build The Gizmoplex , Jonah Ray can be heard performing end of the "Love Theme."
