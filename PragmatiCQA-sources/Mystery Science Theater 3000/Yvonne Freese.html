Yvonne Ingrid Freese (born May 19 , 1991 ) is an American actor, singer, writer, and puppeteer from Minnesota. Her name is pronounced "ya-VONN". She is a graduate of Northern State University in South Dakota.

Yvonne Freese played Mega-Synthia and GPC in the Great Cheesy Movie Circus Live Tour , the event, and promos for Pluto TV . She played GPC in the bumper segments of the 2nd Annual Puppet Camp Livestream (in which she also appeared as herself). She was also credited with co-writing the two new shorts that debuted during the Puppet Camp 2 livestream.

In April of 2021 , it was announced that Yvonne would be performing as GPC on the new episodes of Mystery Science Theater 3000 funded by the second Kickstarter campaign . She is planned to appear with the rest of the cast from the Great Cheesy Movie Circus live tour. It was later confirmed that she would also appear as Mega-Synthia.

In July of 2021 it was announced that Yvonne would perform as GPC and Mega-Synthia as part of the upcoming live Time Bubble Tour .
