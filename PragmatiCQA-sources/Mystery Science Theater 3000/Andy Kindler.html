Andy Kindler (born 1956 ) is an American actor, writer, and comedian. He is known for his material about his family, his self-deprecating style, and his often obscure references. Among his other notable achievements, he delivers the annual "State of the Industry" address at the Just for Laughs comedy festival.

Andy Kindler has had recurring roles on several popular TV series including Everybody Loves Raymond and The Wizards of Waverly Place . He has provided the voice of animated characters on many shows including Bob's Burgers , Home Movies , and Dr. Katz (as himself).

Andy Kindler is a frequent collaborator of Josh Weinstein . He and Weinstein co-host the podcast Thought Spiral . In 2019, they both appeared in the feature film The Fiddling Horse . In December of 2020 , Weinstein and Kindler joined the Mads before and after their streaming riff of Santa Claus vs. the Devil .

Andy Kindler also appeared as a performer on The TV Wheel (as one of the golfers).
