Deanna Rooney is an American actor, writer, artist and animator. She portrays Dr. Donna St. Phibes and Bonehead #3 in MST3K .

She has appeared on shows such as The Joel McHale Show with Joel McHale , Hidden America wih Jonah Ray and Townies .

Her art has appeared on shows such as Cosmos and Rad Lands .

She has been married to Jonah Ray since 2013 .
