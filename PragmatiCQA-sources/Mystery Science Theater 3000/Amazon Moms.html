The Amazon Moms are a pair of good-natured Amazon ladies who visited The Satellite of Love during Experiment #502 . They cleared up some misconceptions that Crow and Tom had about Amazons.

Notes

  • The Amazons' ship seemed to be based on an Earth truck or possibly station wagon.
  • The Amazons wear shirts with the phrase "Amazons Make Better Hotdish" printed on them.
  • This marked Mary Jo Pehl 's first on-screen appearance.
