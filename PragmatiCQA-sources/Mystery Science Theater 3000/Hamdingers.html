Hamdingers are rumored to be a local culinary treat in the Wisconsin area. They have been described as a sort of Spam sandwich. Hamdingers are notable for their appearance in the television series Mystery Science Theater 3000 , where the cast occasionally referred to Hamdingers with both nostalgia and revulsion -- a crate supposedly full of them housed an escape pod (the Deus ex Machina ) in one episode; the storyline stated that it hadn't been found before because no one wanted to go anywhere near the box. While several recipes for Hamdingers are available online, as of this writing no Hamdinger has ever been spotted "in the wild," although claims they were made by Swift-Premium, sold by the block, and often used for fish bait.
