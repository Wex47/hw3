"Gooch" (actually pronounced Cooch) is a character in The Side Hackers .

Gooch is a henchman of the hot tempered stunt motorcycle promoter J.C. Gooch is asked to gain the trust of J.C.'s arch rival Vince Rommel when Rommel assembles a gang to come after J.C. Gooch is a very obedient henchman, so he will. When Gooch is found out to be a spy, J.C. shoots him.

Gooch visits the SOL during the third movie break with J.C. J.C.'s plan is to help bring the crew back to Earth until they see them imitating Rommel. J.C. asks Gooch to get Rommel. Gooch will . He will get him. He will get him.
