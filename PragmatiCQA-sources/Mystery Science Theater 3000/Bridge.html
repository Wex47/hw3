The Bridge is the area on the Satellite of Love where most of the host segments take place. A series of doors lead from the bridge to the theater , and there appear to be many other ways to get from the into the theater. Based on the opening sequence of Seasons 8 - 10, where the crew is briefly seen looking out a window, the bridge is located in the upper-right sphere of the dog-bone-shaped ship.

The Bridge contains a desk where Joel Robinson / Mike Nelson / Jonah Heston and the Bots are standing behind during the host segments, with the first of the doors that lead to the theater directly behind them. On the desk is a panel of three colored lights. In some episodes in Season 8 , the desk also contains a steering wheel for controlling the SOL. On the right (left for the viewer) of the door are the Hexfield Viewscreen and a window, and in the corner to the left (right for the viewer) of the door is the airlock .

In Mystery Science Theater 3000: The Movie , the bridge is seen in more detail. It turned out there is also an area in front of the desk with another steering wheel.
