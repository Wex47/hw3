Season 8 is the eighth national season (ninth overall) of Mystery Science Theater 3000 . It aired on The Sci-Fi Channel from February 1 , 1997 to December 6 , 1997 . It consists of twenty-two episodes.

Season debuts:

  • Bill Corbett as Crow T. Robot and Brain Guy
  • Kevin Murphy as Professor Bobo
  • Beez McKeever as Magic Voice
  • Patrick Brantseg as Gypsy
