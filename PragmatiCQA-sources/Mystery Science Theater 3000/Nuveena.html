Nuveena , the Woman of the Future, is a character from Design for Dreaming , a musical short featured in Experiment 524 - 12 to the Moon . The unnamed woman (played by Tad Tadlock ) sang and danced her way through a futuristic world of cars, clothes, and kitchen appliances.

Later in the same episode, Nuveena (played by Bridget Jones ) appeared on the Satellite of Love during the host segments. As in the original short, Nuveena's primary means of communication was through singing and dancing, and she wouldn't register Mike Nelson's presence unless he sang also. She expressed her intention to bring Mike and the 'Bots to her utopian future world.

Nuveena's visit ended on a sour note after she turned Crow T. Robot , Tom Servo , and Gypsy into household appliances, and Mike was ultimately forced to tell Nuveena to leave. She then appeared briefly in Deep 13 , where she taunted Frank with the promise of affection then vanished.

Nuveena later made a brief appearance on the SOL during San Francisco International , where she joined the other characters in uproarious laughter over Mike's Steve Urkel impression until Torgo arrived and put a damper on things.
