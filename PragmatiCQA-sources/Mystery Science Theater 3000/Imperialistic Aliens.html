The Imperialistic Aliens are two humanoids who communicated with the SOL via the Hexfield Viewscreen during Experiment #203 .

Imperialistic Alien 1 ( Michael J. Nelson ) made the demands while Imperialistic Alien 2 ( Jim Mallon ) played around with his gun. Despite their threats and condescension, they made little impression on Joel and the Bots.
