Season 13 is the upcoming thirteenth national season of Mystery Science Theater 3000 (fourteenth overall). It will be the first to be released via the Gizmoplex streaming platform and was crowdfunded via the Let's Make More MST3K & Build THE GIZMOPLEX! campaign on Kickstarter. The season will consist of thirteen episodes and is scheduled to begin on March 4 , 2022 .

Season 13 will feature three test subjects sharing duties across the thirteen experiments: Jonah Heston , Emily Connor and Joel Robinson .

During Turkey Day '21 , the thirteen films were announced and their planned release order.
