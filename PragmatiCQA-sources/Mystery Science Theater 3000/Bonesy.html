Recurring

Bonesy is a dog gifted with power of speech. He is a companion to Ardy and stays with him in Moon 14 , a sub-section of Moon 13 . Though he briefly appeared in the first issue of the comic book series , there was no indication he was anything other than a regular dog in a spacesuit.

Behind the scenes

  • Bonesy is played by MST3K producer Ivan Askwith.
