Jeff Morrow ( 1907 - 1993 ) was an American actor.

Jeff Morrow appeared in several westerns and starred on the TV series Union Pacific as Major Bart McClelland.

Mr. Morrow starred in several science fiction/monster movies, including The Giant Claw and The Creature Walks Among Us.
