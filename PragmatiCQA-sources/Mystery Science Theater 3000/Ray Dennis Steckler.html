Ray Dennis Steckler ( 1938 - 2009 ) was an American filmmaker known for his low-budget "cult" films, including Rat Pfink a Boo Boo and Lemon Grove Kids Meet the Monsters (with Coleman Francis ). He sometimes used the pseudonym Cash Flagg for his acting credits.
