Clayton Forrester is one of the test subjects, alongside TV's Frank on the Satellite of Love in the mirror universe at the time of Last of the Wild Horses .

Clayton is the mirror universe version of Dr. Clayton Forrester from the prime universe.

He and the mirror Frank were good at riffing, but obsessed with Joey the Lemur . After the movie, they made contact with the prime universe's Satellite of Love , but Mike and the bots quickly found them annoying while trying to read a letter. Afterwards, Mike says, "Wow! In a way, really, it's kinda comforting to know that, uh... no matter what universe you're from, you can still be really annoying," to which the bots readily agree.
