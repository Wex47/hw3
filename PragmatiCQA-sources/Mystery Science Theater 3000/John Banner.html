Johann "John" Banner ( 1910 - 1973 ) was an Austrian-born actor who is best remembered for his portrayal of Sergeant Hans Schultz on Hogan's Heroes . Other television shows he appeared on include The Lone Ranger , Alfred Hitchcock Presents , Perry Mason , The Man from U.N.C.L.E. , and The Partridge Family .

"John Banner" appeared in the Hexfield Viewscreen and Deep 13 in host segments during Crash of Moons . He was played by Mike Nelson .
