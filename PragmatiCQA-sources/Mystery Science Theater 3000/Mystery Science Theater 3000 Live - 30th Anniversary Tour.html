The Mystery Science Theater 3000 Live - 30th Annversary Tour was the second live touring show based on Mystery Science Theater 3000 . It kicked off on October 9, 2018 in Portland, Maine.

As with the previous tour , there were be two films featured. At most stops, the film The Brain was riffed, while Deathstalker II was riffed at a variety of stops.

Of note is Joel Hodgson 's return in character as Joel Robinson , riffing alongside Jonah Ray 's Jonah Heston .

The second feature was originally announced as Deathstalker , the first film in the series. After reviewing the film and realizing that editing it for content (graphic violence and nudity) would reduce the run-time too much, Joel decided to replace it with the second film in the series.

Growler was addressed during the show, as he was supposedly supplying the music, but he did not appear on stage. M. Waverly appeared on screen only.

Cast

  • Joel Hodgson - Joel Robinson
  • Jonah Ray - Jonah Heston
  • Rebecca Hanson - Synthia / Gypsy
  • Tim Ryder - Tom Servo
  • Grant Baciocco - Crow T. Robot / M. Waverly
  • Deanna Rooney - Dr. Donna St. Phibes
