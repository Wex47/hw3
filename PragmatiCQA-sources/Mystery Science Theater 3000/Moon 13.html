Moon 13 is the research station run by Kinga Forrester . It serves as the base of operations for the revived "Mystery Science Theater 3000" experiment.

Layout

Moon 13

  • Base for Kinga and Max to conduct each Experiment and Invention Exchange .
  • Performance space for the Boneheads band.
  • Storage vault containing past experiments and a secret theater for watching them.
  • Tether for Iron Butterfly surveillance drone.
  • Has several subterranean levels.
  • Features a phony "Moon Base 13" location on the moon's surface.

Moon 14

  • Site of the Mesozoic Ranch dinosaur breeding center.
  • Site of the development of Kingachrome .
