Season 2 is the second national season (third overall) of Mystery Science Theater 3000 . It aired on The Comedy Channel from September 22, 1990 to February 2, 1991. It consists of thirteen episodes, the same length as Seasons 1, 9 10 and 13.

Season debuts:

  • Frank Conniff as TV's Frank
  • Kevin Murphy as Tom Servo
  • Alexandra Carr as Magic Voice
