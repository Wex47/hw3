MST3K Watch Out for Snakes! Tour Soundtrack is a playlist assembled by Joel Hodgson and Jonah Heston that serves as a soundtrack for the Mystery Science Theater 3000 Live! - Watch Out for Snakes! Tour . It was created for and is available through Spotify and Apple Music
