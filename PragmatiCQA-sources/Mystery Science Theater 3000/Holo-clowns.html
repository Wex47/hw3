The Holo-clowns are holographic clowns created by Joel as part of his " rainy-day fun sketch " in an attempt to cheer up Tom Servo and Crow T. Robot . Unfortunately, an error in their programming caused them to stay active long past their intended existence and they went mad, forcing Joel to pull the plug on them .

Behind the scenes

The Holo-clowns were portrayed by Michael J. Nelson and Paul Chaplin (the latter making his onscreen debut) in their two appearances.
