The unnamed Delivery Man for Coffins Etc. appeared on the Satellite of Love to deliver the coffin that Tom Servo had attempted to acquire for free under false pretenses.

The Delivery Man was impressed by the SOL as a workplace. He left without incident.

How the Delivery Man got to and from the SOL is not clear.
