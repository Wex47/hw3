Season K (also known as "Season 0") is the first season of Mystery Science Theater 3000 . It aired from 1988-1989 on KTMA - Channel 23 from Minneapolis, Minnesota. It consists of twenty-one episodes. The original pilot is also generally considered part of the season, but is not included in the episode count because it is only a proof-of-concept and not a complete episode.

During the Facebook live-stream following the 2020 event, Joel stated that he does not consider the KTMA season to be "canon". However, the episode count used by Kinga Forrester in Experiment #1103 (the "200th episode") includes the KTMA episodes.

Season debuts:

  • Joel Hodgson as Joel
  • Josh Weinstein as Crow T. Robot , Gypsy , Tom Servo and Dr. Laurence Erhardt
  • Trace Beaulieu as Crow T. Robot and Dr. Clayton Forrester
