Carolina Hidalgo is an actress and comedian. She is long-time friend and collaborator of Frank Conniff . She was also a co-host of the podcast Movie Sign with the Mads with Conniff and Trace Beaulieu .

In February 2021 , Carolina joined Frank and Trace for a Q&A session during the livestream of The Mads Are Back presentation of The Choppers . She joined them again in September of 2021 for The Brain Eaters .
