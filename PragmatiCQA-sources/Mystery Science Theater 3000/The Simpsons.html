The Simpsons is a long-running American animated TV comedy. It airs on the the Fox network. The show is about a suburban family in the town of Springfield.

MST3K Connections

  • In the 1995 episode "Who Shot Mr. Burns? (Part 1)", a movie with an MST3K silhouette can be seen on the television screen at Moe's Tavern. The broadcast schedule of Comedy Central would also become relevant in the episode.
  • Previous MST3K cast member Josh Weinstein changed his performing name to "J. Elvis Weinstein" to avoid confusion with a Simpsons writer also named Josh Weinstein.
  • Several riffs made by MST3K characters (as well as Cinematic Titanic and Rifftrax ) reference The Simpsons , such as "Mendoza!!!" and various quotes that mimic the voices of Snake (the jailbird) and Comic Book Guy.
  • Dana Gould was a long-time writer and co-producer on The Simpsons , for which he received several Emmy Awards. He also provided voices in a number of episodes.
  • Patton Oswalt has provided voiced for 2 episodes "The Day the Earth Stood Cool" and "The Cad and the Hat".
