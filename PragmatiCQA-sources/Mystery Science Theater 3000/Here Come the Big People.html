Here Come the Big People is a one-shot comic written by Trace Beaulieu with art by Amanda Conner and Jimmy Palmiotti. It was published in September of 1997.

Story

The comic tells the story of scientist/inventor Austin Milcsop who attempts to make artificial "mothers" to take care of humanity. Alien interference causes the experiment to go better than planned and mankind becomes dependent on their gigantic caregivers.
