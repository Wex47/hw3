"Idiot Control" (aka "Idiot Control Now") is a song sung by Joel Robinson and the Bots that parodies the song "Rugen Los Motores" ("Engines Roar") a.k.a. "Hear the Engines Roar" a.k.a. "Burning Rubber Tires" from the movie Pod People during Experiment #303 .

Decades later, Dr. Laurence Erhardt made a surprise visit to Moon 13 to claim the remains of the deceased Dr. Clayton Forrester , who wished for "Idiot Control Now" to play during his burial. After an extensive search, the song was located in the Kingachrome archive by Synthia Forrester , who distilled it into a beverage dubbed "songria" . Dr. Erhardt then played the song as he traveled to Mearth to carry out his mission .
