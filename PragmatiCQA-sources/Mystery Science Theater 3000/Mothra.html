Mothra is one of the most famous of all giant movie monsters, and had a small role in the movie Godzilla vs. the Sea Monster . As his/her name implies, Mothra is a giant moth.

Unlike most such daikaiju, Mothra is usually portrayed as a benevolent protector of mankind, and is called to action by a pair of foot-tall priestesses when the world is threatened.

During Experiment #213 , Tom and Crow dressed as the priestesses and mockingly pretended to summon Mothra, but were shocked when it worked and Mothra appeared on the Hexfield . While Mothra advised them not to call him unless they mean it, Mothra proved an amiable sort who traded a few jokes with the bots before leaving again. Joel didn't believe the bots when they tried to tell him what happened.
