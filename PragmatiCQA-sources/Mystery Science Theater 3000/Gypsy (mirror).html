Gypsy is a mirror version of prime-universe Gypsy . In Last of the Wild Horses , she is shown to be one of the Mads based in Deep 13 . She and Tom Servo were pulled into the prime universe in a matter transference device accident. She helped Tom plot, hoping to become Captain's woman . After the movie, she and Tom were returned to the mirror universe.
