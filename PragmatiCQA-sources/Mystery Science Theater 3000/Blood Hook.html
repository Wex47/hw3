Blood Hook (originally titled Muskie Madness ) is a comedy-horror film directed by Jim Mallon , filmed in 1984 and released by Troma Films in 1987.

Kevin Murphy worked on the film as a key grip and lyricist; Mallon and Murphy would later work together at KTMA in Minneapolis, where they ultimately became involved in the production of Mystery Science Theater 3000 .
