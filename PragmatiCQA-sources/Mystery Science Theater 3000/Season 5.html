Season 5 is the fifth national season (sixth overall) of Mystery Science Theater 3000 . It aired on Comedy Central from July 24 , 1993 to February 2 , 1994 . It consists of twenty-four episodes, the same as Seasons 3, 4 and 6 (and longer than any other seasons).

Season debuts:

  • Michael J. Nelson as Mike Nelson
