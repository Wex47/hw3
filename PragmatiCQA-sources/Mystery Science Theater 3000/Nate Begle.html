Nate Begle is an actor, puppeteer, and voice performer. His last name is pronounced "BEE-gull".

Begle played Crow T. Robot in the Great Cheesy Movie Circus live tour and the 2020 streaming event. He also performed as Crow in promotional spots for Pluto TV and during the bumper segments of the 2nd Annual Puppet Camp Livestream . Additionally, Mr. Begle operated the Crow puppet during the 2 new riffed shorts that appeared during the Puppet Camp 2 livestream (with the voice provided by Bill Corbett ). He was also credited with co-writing the two shorts.

In April of 2021 , it was announced that Nate would be performing Crow as part of Emily Connor's crew in Season 13 of Mystery Science Theater 3000 . He participated in several of the Livestream events to promote the second Kickstarter campaign .

In July of 2021 it was announced that Nate would perform as Crow as part of the cast of the upcoming live Time Bubble Tour . Begle became a father in November 2021, and he was replaced on the tour by understudy Kelsey Ann Brady while he took family leave.
