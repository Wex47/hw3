Tim Blaney (born April 23rd, 1959) is an American puppeteer and voice actor.

Mr. Blaney operated the Gypsy puppet in Season 11 and Season 12 of Mystery Science Theater 3000 . After the announcement that there would no additional seasons of MST3K produced for Netflix, Blaney's potential involvement with any future MST3K projects is unclear.

Tim Blaney has had a long association with the Jim Henson Company . His other prominent roles were as the robot Johnny Five in the Short Circuit movies and one of the Worm Guys in the Men in Black movies.
