Earth is the third planet from the Sun and the location of most "lairs" used by the various " Mads " running the "Mystery Science Theater 3000" experiments, including the Gizmonic Institute , Deep 13 , Deep Ape and Castle Forrester . The various test subjects have also been natives of Earth and returning to the planet has often been the goal of those subjects. When Mike Nelson , Crow T. Robot and Tom Servo were facing an imminent return to Earth following the decommissioning of The Satellite of Love , Crow's nervousness prompted Mike to sing a tribute to the planet, " To Earth ". Later, in the future, the Earth would be destroyed by a nuclear bomb , although this future may have been averted by changes in the timeline.
