Season 7 is the seventh national season (eighth overall) of Mystery Science Theater 3000 . It aired on Comedy Central from February 3 , 1996 to May 18 , 1996 . Due to Night of the Blood Beast having standard and Turkey Day versions with different host segments, the season is considered to consist of seven episodes, the shortest season of MST3K until Season 12 .
