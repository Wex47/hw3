Movie Jo Night is a comedy livestream starring Mary Jo Pehl that debuted on September 27 , 2021 . On the show, Mary Jo watches a movie she has never seen before live on Twitch . It is presented under the umbrella of her primary stream, The Mary Jo Pehl Show . Mary Jo comments and riffs on the show while interacting with live chat.

The livestreams are produced by Chris Gersbeck of Dumb Industries .
