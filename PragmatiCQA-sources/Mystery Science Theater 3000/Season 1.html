Season 1 is the first national season (second overall) of Mystery Science Theater 3000 . It aired on The Comedy Channel from November 18 , 1989 to February 20 , 1990 . It consists of thirteen episodes, the same length as Seasons 2, 9 10 and 13.

Episode #104 - Women of the Prehistoric Planet , the last episode to be produced and to air, nonetheless retained its earlier production number and is, therefore, still considered the fourth episode of the season.

Season debuts:

  • Jim Mallon as Gypsy
  • Jann Johnson as Magic Voice
