MST3K

During Experiment #302 , Gamera (Michael J. Nelson) appears outside The Satellite of Love . While doing his laundry, he calls the SoL to try and borrow a Cling-Free dryer sheet. He discusses some of his struggles with his size and how he uses Kenny to "soften up his image". Gamera receives a call from Godzilla to discuss the script for a possible crossover film. Gamera suggests the title Gamera vs Godzilla , which Godzilla appears to dislike.

Throughout Experiment #312 , the crew of the SoL sing variations of " The Gamera Song ", with Michael Feinstein (Michael J. Nelson) providing his own version at the experiment's conclusion.

Gamera appears once more outside the SoL near the end of Experiment #316 with Kenny and Helen riding on his back.

Notes

  • The films were shown out of order during their initial run during Season K . When Best Brains decided to redo the films in Season 3 , they showed them in release order.
  • Three Showa Era films were not initially used on MST3K : Gamera vs. Viras , Gamera vs. Jiger and Gamera: Super Monster . Jiger was eventually announced to be a part of Season 13 .
