Turkey Day '18 is a marathon of Mystery Science Theater 3000 episodes streaming live through various online services on November 18, 2018.

It is the first Turkey Day marathon not to air on Thanksgiving itself, due to the debut of Season 12 on Netflix on that day.

Host segments feature Joel Hodgson and Jonah Ray and were filmed in LA on November 5, 2018.
