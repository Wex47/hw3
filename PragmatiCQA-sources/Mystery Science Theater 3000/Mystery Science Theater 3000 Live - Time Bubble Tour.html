The Mystery Science Theater 3000 Live - Time Bubble Tour is a live touring show based on Mystery Science Theater 3000 . It began on October 30 , 2021 in York, PA. The tour is directed by Tim Ryder .

Unlike previous live tours, only one film is planned to be riffed: the 1985 horror-fantasy film Making Contact .

Cast

  • Emily Marsh - Emily Connor
  • Conor McGiffin - Tom Servo
  • Nate Begle - Crow T. Robot
  • Yvonne Freese - GPC / Mega-Synthia
  • Kelsey Ann Brady - Swing Puppeteer
