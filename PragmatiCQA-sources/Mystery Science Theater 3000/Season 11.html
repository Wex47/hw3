Season 11 (also known as Mystery Science Theater 3000: The Return ) is the eleventh national season (twelfth overall) of Mystery Science Theater 3000 . Following a preview for Kickstarter backers of Experiment #1101 a week earlier, the entire season was released on April 14, 2017 by Netflix . Consequently, it is the first season to be released directly to streaming services instead of a traditional TV network or channel.

The season consists of fourteen episodes, making it longer than Seasons 1, 2, 7, 9, & 10.

It is the first season to be released to home media in a complete season set .

Season debuts:

  • Jonah Ray as Jonah Heston
  • Baron Vaughn as Tom Servo
  • Hampton Yount as Crow T. Robot
  • Rebecca Hanson as Gypsy / Synthia Forrester
  • Felicia Day as Kinga Forrester
  • Patton Oswalt as Max
  • Joel Hodgson as Ardy
  • Grant Baciocco as M. Waverly
  • Russ Walko as Growler
