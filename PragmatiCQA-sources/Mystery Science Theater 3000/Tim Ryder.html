Tim Ryder is an actor, writer, improvisor, and musician. He has worked on Mystery Science Theater 3000 since the revival . Tim Ryder is married to Rebecca Hanson .

Ryder's principal role is that of Bonehead #1 , one of Kinga Forrester's henchpersons. He also voiced and operated Tom Servo for the Watch Out for Snakes! Tour and the 30th Anniversary Tour . He directed the Great Cheesy Movie Circus Tour , and also wrote for it. He was also credited with co-writing the two new shorts that debuted during the Puppet Camp 2 livestream.

After the announcement that there would no additional seasons of MST3K produced for Netflix , Ryder's potential involvement with any future MST3K projects became unclear. In July of 2021 , it was announced that he would be the director of the upcoming live Time Bubble Tour . On Twitter, Ryder has revealed that he worked as a writer for some episodes during Season 13 .
