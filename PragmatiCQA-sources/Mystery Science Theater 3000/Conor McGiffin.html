Conor McGiffin is an actor and puppeteer from Dover, DE.

Conor performed as Tom Servo during the Great Cheesy Movie Circus Live Tour and the 2020 streaming event. He also performed Tom in promos for Pluto TV and during the bumper segments of the 2nd Annual Puppet Camp Livestream . Additionally, Mr. McGiffin operated the Tom Servo puppet during the 2 new riffed shorts that appeared during the Puppet Camp 2 livestream (with the voice provided by Josh Weinstein ). He was also credited with co-writing the two shorts.

In April of 2021 , it was announced that Conor would be performing as Tom Servo on the new episodes of Mystery Science Theater 3000 funded by the second Kickstarter campaign . He is planned to appear with the rest of the cast from the Great Cheesy Movie Circus live tour and alongside the cast from Seasons 11 and 12 , including Baron Vaughn as a separate Tom Servo.

In July of 2021 it was announced that Conor would perform as Tom Servo as part of the cast of the upcoming live Time Bubble Tour .
