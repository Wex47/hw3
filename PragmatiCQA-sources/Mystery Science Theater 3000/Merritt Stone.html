Merritt Stone ( January 23 , 1915 - January 23 , 1985 ) was an American actor. He appeared in several movies in small roles, giving rise to "The Great Merritt Stone Mystery" that occurred in Experiment #419 The Rebel Set (in which he does not appear).

Riffed Movies

  • Earth vs the Spider ( Experiment #313 ) as Jack Flynn
  • War of the Colossal Beast ( Experiment #319 ) as a policeman (uncredited)
  • The Magic Sword ( Experiment #411 & RiffTrax ) as the King
  • Tormented ( Experiment #414 ) as the Clergyman

Notes

  • The man identified as "Merritt Stone" during Tormented and The Rebel Set is actually actor Gene Roth .
