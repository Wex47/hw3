M. Waverly (sometimes just Waverly ) is a robot built by Jonah Heston who debuted in Season 11 of MST3K .

Waverly appeared during The Loves of Hercules , where he attempted to mingle with Jonah and the Bots during one of their sketches. Tom and Crow took offense to Waverly and did not like how Jonah was attempting to ruin the show's character dynamic by introducing another robot. They then forced Waverly to shut down, ripping his arm off in the process. Despite this, Waverly continues to appear throughout Season 12 in small roles, usually by being lifted in and out of the theater in each episode by Gypsy and occasionally making a riff on the movie.

In the Watch Out for Snakes! Tour , Waverly could be seen sweeping the stage during intermission at the Eegah show, and appeared before Act 2 during performances of Argoman the Fantastic Superman . In the latter appearance, it was revealed that Tom had rebuilt him and intended to keep this a secret from Crow.

M. Waverly appeared in Mystery Science Theater 3000: The Comic and in publicity photos for Season 12 .

M. Waverly was also present during the 30th Anniversary Live Tour . He was not part of the Great Cheesy Movie Circus Tour . He and Growler appeared in a short segment during The Final Countdown, Part II .

Behind the Scenes

The creation of Waverly was revealed during updates to the Bring Back MYSTERY SCIENCE THEATER 3000 campaign.

In update #25, a video of a cardboard mock-up of Waverly was shown, but no more information was provided.

Waverly was built by engineer and maker . Joel gave Justin a model of Waverly using random dollar store parts, and Justin then scaled the model. Most of his parts were made with 3D prints, PVC, and wood. Justin was also responsible for building Growler .

In update #27, Joel revealed images of Waverly's maquette, a digital rendering and a video showing a prototype of the puppet in motion. At that time, Joel referred to the robot as "Mr. Waverly", revealing that his name was inspired by the character of Alexander Waverly from The Man from U.N.C.L.E. .

Waverly made his broadcast debut in a Facebook Live video, introducing Crow T. Robot and Tom Servo .
