The Mystery Science Theater 3000 Live! - Watch Out for Snakes! Tour was the first live touring show based on Mystery Science Theater 3000 . It kicked off on July 6, 2017 in Boston.

At every stop, the film Eegah (previously riffed in episode 506 ) was riffed, with all-new sketches and riffs. The infamous line "Watch out for snakes." from which the tour takes its name comes from Eegah .

At many of the stops, a second film, Argoman the Fantastic Superman was riffed. This is a film that had never been riffed before on MST3K or by any of the spin-off projects.

Joel Hodgson and Jonah Heston assembled the playlist MST3K Watch Out for Snakes! Tour Soundtrack for .

On June 4, 2018, it was announced that the Watch Out for Snakes Tour would be followed by the Mystery Science Theater 3000 Live - 30th Anniversary Tour .
