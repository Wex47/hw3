Season 3 is the third national season (fourth overall) of Mystery Science Theater 3000 . It aired on Comedy Central from June 1 , 1991 to January 25 , 1992 . It consists of twenty-four episodes, the same as Seasons 4 - 6 (and longer than any other seasons).

In between Season 2 and 3, The Comedy Channel , MST3K 's original national network, merged with rival Ha! to form "CTV: The Comedy Network", then had to almost immediately rebrand to avoid confusion with the Canadian network of a similar name .
