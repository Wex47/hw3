Tom Servo was one of the Mads in the mirror universe. In Last of the Wild Horses , he and Gypsy were transported to the prime universe in a matter transference device accident on the prime universe's Satellite of Love . He was not pleased at having to riff the movie, and plotted with Gypsy to take over. He tried to kill Mike (but failing miserably, as he is so small and like the original Servo has no working arms) while Evil Crow tried to kill Evil Mike . He and Gypsy were returned to their proper continuum at the end of the movie.
