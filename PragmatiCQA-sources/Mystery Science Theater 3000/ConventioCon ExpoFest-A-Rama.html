This was the first official MST3K convention and was held on September 16th to 18th, 1994. All the recorded footage of the event can be seen here .

Out of all the questions asked, the cast gets to answer which movies were their personal favorites to riff:

  • Trace Beaulieu , Michael J. Nelson , Kevin Murphy and Frank Conniff - "Manos" The Hands of Fate
  • Mary Jo Pehl - The Human Duplicators

The convention also featured guest appearances by Beverly Garland , David Worth (director of Warrior of the Lost World ), and John Humphries (Mikey from Teen-Age Strangler ).

On Saturday night, Mike Nelson and The Bots riffed This Island Earth live.
