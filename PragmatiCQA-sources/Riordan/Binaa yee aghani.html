The following article/section is from the Race to the Sun continuity under Rick Riordan Presents and not the Riordanverse canon.

Bináá' yee aghání are malevolent shapeshifting birds from Navajo folklore. They are the enemies of the original heroes twins, Monsterslayer and Born of Water.

Race to the Sun

A Bináá' yee agháni tries to kill Nizhoni Begay and kidnap her brother to keep his plans in motion.

Appearance

In their true form the Bináá' yee aghání resemble human-sized vultures will black wire like hair dangling from their heads and bulging eyes covered in blue veils.

Abilities

  • Shapeshifting : They can appear in either their true bird forms, as humans, and as anything or anyone else they wish to turn into.
  • Paralyzing Gaze : They can paralyze anyone who looks into their eyes, with the exception of Monsterslayer and the inheritors of his power.
