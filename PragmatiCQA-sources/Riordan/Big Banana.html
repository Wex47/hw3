The Big Banana is a ship that was gifted by Frey to his son, Magnus Chase . It has five rows of oars and a carved dragon figurehead. For an easy way to travel the ship itself to different places on land, it can turn into a napkin which the user brings along with them. The ship is completely neon-yellow, which led Alex Fierro to name it "The Big Banana." Halfborn Gunderson named the ship Mikillgulr , meaning "The Big Yellow" in Old Norse , as Vikings hadn't discovered bananas yet.

Trivia

  • The ship can row on its own. This is revealed by Njord after he rescues the heroes from the underwater palace of Aegir .
  • The ship can go to any destination that its passengers wish in their mind.
  • Being able to fold up is similar to Frey's own ship, Skidbladnir .
