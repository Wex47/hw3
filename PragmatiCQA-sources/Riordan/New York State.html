The Kane Chronicles

Brooklyn , one of the boroughs of New York City, is the location of Brooklyn House , the twenty-first nome of the House of Life . Brooklyn House makes an appearance in all the three books of the trilogy.

The Heroes of Olympus

The main locations of the state seen are Camp Half-Blood on Long Island and Manhattan as seen in visions from the dagger of Piper McLean . Keeseville in upstate New York is the prime location of the short story Son of Magic in The Demigod Diaries .
