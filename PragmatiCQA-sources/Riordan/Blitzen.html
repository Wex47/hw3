Blitzen , also known as Blitz , is a Norse demigod , son of the goddess Freya and the dwarf Bili . He is a skilled craftsman and fashion consultant.

History

Birth

Blitzen was born to Bilì, a dwarf, and Freya, the goddess of love, beauty, and magic. He lost his father to the jaws of the wolf Fenrir when he went to ensure that Gleipnir, the rope that bound the wolf, was still working, costing him his life. Freya, his mother, is alive but distant and calls on him mainly for favors and assignments.

He studied fashion in school, aspiring to one day open his own fashion label.

Meeting Hearthstone

When he first met Hearthstone , his fellow hero and friend, he made the elf a custom tanning bed so that he could stay with him in the subterranean Dwarf realm of Nidavellir and not waste away, as elves need sunlight to survive.

Serving Mimir

Blitz, as well as Hearth, drank from Mimir's well of wisdom so that he could avenge his father, restore his family's honor, and realize his dream of fashion design without mockery from the other dwarves. In exchange, he had to work for the wise but unkind severed head for four years.

During that time, he and Hearth were ordered to watch over Magnus Chase after his mother died. He took night shifts, since as a dwarf the sunlight petrified him, while Hearth took the day shifts.

Appearance

Blitzen is a Svartalf, making him taller than average Nidavellir dwarves and generally more handsome because of his Vanir blood. He has a dark complexion and a beard.

When he posed as a homeless man, Blitz usually looked like he had just finished running through a dirty hurricane. He has wiry black hair, full of paper scraps and twigs, a beard that curls in all directions, and dilated eyes. His face is the color of saddle leather with flecks of snow and looks like he is permanently alarmed. His nose has been broken so many time it looks like a lightning bolt. He is about five feet and five inches tall and wears a snow-caked trench coat.

After pretending to be a homeless man, however, Blitzen has a much cleaner appearance. His chaotic hair had been washed and combed back. His beard was trimmed. His Cro-Magnon unibrow had been plucked and waxed. His boots were alligator leather. His black wool suit was tailored to fit his stocky five-feet-five frame and looked lovely with his dark skin tone. Under the jacket, he was rocking a charcoal paisley waistcoat with a gold watch chain, a smart turquoise shirt and a skinny bolo tie. However, he is often anxious to cover his skin during the day, as he gets petrified in direct sunlight.

Personality

Blitzen is extremely loyal his friends and went face-to-face with Surt to protect Magnus. He also has a sense of style. He is willing to sacrifice himself for the sake of preventing Ragnarök . He has been shown to be amazingly caring, healing his best friend Hearthstone the Alf back to health after he found him lost and injured with the use of a sun bed, made specifically for his friend. He also learned sign language for him and has been there for him in his darkest times.

Abilities

  • Crafting Skills : Blitzen is skilled at crafting. When he first met Hearthstone, he made a tanning bed so the elf could survive in the subterranean dwarf world of Nidavellir , as elves need sunlight. However, he is bad under pressure, and automatically builds ducks when he is nervous.
  • Adept Sense of Fashion : Blitzen is quite skilled at fashion, having studied it in school. With a little improvisation in The Sword of Summer , he was able to design fashionable clothes from chain mail.
  • Alf Sign Language : Blitzen can understand Alf Sign Language (ASL).
  • Geokinesis : As a dwarf, Blitzen has some control over the earth.
    • Underground Intuition : Blitzen is able to sense how old a rock formation is as well as entrances and exits to underground chambers.
    • Rock Shaping : As a dwarf Blitzen can shape rocks with his hands so he may climb on them.

Magical Items

  • Bone Steel Harpoon : Blitz bought a bone steel harpoon when he is sent to Nidavellir by Thor. He uses it to fight fire giants on Fenrir's island.
  • Armored Clothing : Blitzen has clothing that changes from silk to chain mail, such as his ascot.

On the orders of Mimir, Blitzen watched over the son of Frey with Hearthstone and they formed a strong bond. Blitz would watch out for Magnus and Magnus would stand up for Blitz.

When Blitz was stabbed with Skofnung , he ventured to Alfheim to find the Skofnung Stone to cure him, showing the dedication to saving his friend.

The two met when the elf accidentally sent himself to Nidavellir and he built him a tanning bed to keep him alive in the subterranean world. The two became fast friends. They the two started to serve Minir at the same time and both helped watch out for Magnus. The dwarf even learned Alf Sign Language for the elf.

When Blitz was stabbed with Skofnung, he agreed to return to his father’s home, a place he dreads, to get the stone to cure him, showing his dedication to his friend.

Blitzen and Hearthstone have a very romantically-coded relationship, and due to the unreliable nature of Magnus' narration, it is safe to assume the two were in fact in a long term romantic relationship, possibly since before the beginning of the series.
At first, the two saw one another as untrustworthy. However as there quest went on, the two started to trust each other and eventually saw each other as valuable allies. Within weeks Blitz and the daughter of Loki demonstrate concern for one another.

Trivia

  • Blitzen gave Hearth his red and white scarf.
  • When he is under pressure while crafting he automatically makes waterfowl. (Ducks, geese, swans, etc.)
  • He is unaffected by the light in the fields of Folkvanger, as said light is not from the sun and is not natural, because of the goddess of love, Freya, uses her own beauty to light up Folkvanger. As Nidavellir lacks natural light, he is fine while inside of it. He often wears clothing that slow the petrification caused by the sun, notably wearing a pith helmet.
  • He is good at frisbee.
  • Blitzen is the first demigod created for a series with a non-human mortal parent, being half-Norse Vanir god and half-dwarf.
  • His name is German for lightning.
  • In The Sword of Summer when the cast visits Freya’s abode, Blitzen wears a green carnation in his lapel. The green carnation is a symbol meaning “a man who loves other men,” which was begun by Oscar Wilde in 1892. This detail combined with his and Hearthstone’s romantically coded relationship leads many readers to the well-supported assumption that Blitzen is LGBTQ+.
