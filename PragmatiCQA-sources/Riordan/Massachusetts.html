Massachusetts is a state in the United States of America . It was one of the thirteen colonies.

Magnus Chase and the Gods of Asgard

Boston , the state capital, is the main Midgard setting for the series, with a few chapters in The Hammer of Thor taking place in Provincetown in Cape Cod .

Trivia

  • Massachusetts is considered an American commonwealth, states with laws modeled after British Laws, along with the states and Kentucky , Virginia , and Pennsylvania .
