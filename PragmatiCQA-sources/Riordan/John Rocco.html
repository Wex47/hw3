John Rocco is the cover artist for different books, including the American versions of the Percy Jackson and the Olympians , The Heroes of Olympus , The Kane Chronicles , Magnus Chase and the Gods of Asgard , and The Trials of Apollo books. He has also done the cover and interior art for the American versions of Percy Jackson's Greek Gods , Percy Jackson's Greek Heroes and the illustrated edition of The Lightning Thief .
