Hondo Obispo is the younger brother of Mrs. Obispo and the maternal uncle of Zane Obispo . He is a main character in the Storm Runner Trilogy under Rick Riordan Presents . Hondo, despite being human, often accompanies his nephew on quests. He is currently in a relationship with Quinn .

History

Early Life

Hondo was born into a family with at least one older sibling, an unnamed sister. In his high school days he was a wrestler and hoped to become a professional. However Hondo could not afford to attend college and he ended up working as a janitor at a local bank.

Moving in with His Sister

At one point after graduation high school, Hondo moved into his sister's house, however he would only watch wrestling all day when he was not at work. He would invite friends over to watch wrestling matches, Hondo would also smoke cigars with them whenever his sister was out and hide the evidence when she returned. Hondo would also teach his nephew wrestling moves to help him fight off bullies.

Appearance

Hondo is a short Hispanic man in his early twenties (although he looked seventeen) and was built like a tank with boulder-size biceps, abs of steel, and hands of iron. However despite his muscular build he was considered short.

By The Shadow Crosser, he has grown his hair out slightly, now reaching his ears.

After acting as a Shadow Crosser so that Zane and his companions can get to 1987 and back safely, he ages drastically, looking like an old man after a few hours. However he is healed and returned to normal by Tlaltecuhtli .

Personality

Hondo is a lazy man who lounges around the house whenever he is out of work. However he has the well-being of others at heart, such as teaching his nephew wrestling moves.

Despite his laziness, Hondo can give his all whenever he is needed, such as defending his sister and nephew from an Alux and accompanying Zane and Brooks to Los Angeles to meet with the hero twins.

He is blindly courageous at times, such as threatening Ixtab if her deal with Zane backfired on his nephew.

During his time on Isla Holbox , Hondo has become more tranquil, taking a liking to meditation.

He can be stern at times, such as scolding the students of SHIHOM for having a food fight.

Relationships

Love Interests

Quinn

Hondo finds Quinn to be attractive and flirts with her whenever they meet. He also shows hostility towards anyone else who flirts with her.

However by the time of The Shadow Crosser , the two are dating, albeit in secret due to a rule forbidding sobrenaturals and humans from dating. Hondo becomes enraged when he learns she was captured and forcibly wed to Jordan . However after the immortal godborn and his mother were trapped in a time loop, the marriage was considered null and void and, due to Hondo's bravery, the rule preventing sobrenatural/human relationship is lifted and they date openly.

In Cave of Doom , he reveals his plans to propose to Quinn during their vacation to Mount Kilimanjaro.

Family

  • Unnamed Sister
  • Zane Obispo

Hondo cares greatly for his older sister . She allowed him to live in her house. When she and Zane were attacked by an Alux , Hondo fended it off while they escaped.

Zane Obispo is Hondo's nephew. He acts as an older brother figure during his life. He is supportive of his nephews actions and often accompanies the son of Hurakan quests. when he learned Zane was a godborn, he immediately believed him and accepted Zane.

He also stands up for Zane, such as defending him in front of the ruler of Xib'alb'a . He shows concern for his wellbeing, such as worrying when Zane underwent the Death Ceremony .

Friends

  • Brooks
  • Ren Santiago

When they first met, though she was a friend of Zane’s and teased them about being in a romantic relationship. After learning she was a Nawal , he accepted what she is and helps her and Zane go to Los Angeles .

When Zane left them in San Miguel de Allende and went with Ah-Puch and Ren, the two tracked them down to New Mexico , showing they can work well together.
When she first arrived at Isla Holbox , Hondo was surprised to see another godborn. However as the set out to free Hurakan and find the missing godborns, he acts protective of her. He teaches her yoga to help came the daughter of Pacific and she in turn finds him to be a great teacher.

Etymology

  • Hondo is a name of from the Shona tribe in Southern Africa meaning “warrior”.
  • Obispo is Spanish for bishop.

Trivia

  • He smokes cigars and drinks beer.
  • Prior to relocating to Isla Holbox , Hondo drove an F150.
  • Hondo has some similarities to Gabe Ugliano , being lazy and letting others do his work for him. However unlike Gabe, Hondo helps others in need.
  • In his portrait revealed on ReadRiordan , he is incorrectly referred to as Zane’s older brother.
  • In his youth, Hondo watched the original Blue's Clues.
  • Hondo speaks in Spanish when he is stressed out.
  • Hondo is the second human character, first in the Rick Riordan Presents imprint, to have an active role in at least one quest with a demigod or legacy. The first being Rachel Elizabeth Dare .
