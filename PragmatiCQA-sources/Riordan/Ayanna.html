History

Ayanna was born in MidPass and trained to be a pilot. She befriended another pilot in training during this time. However when the Iron Monster Attacks began, he became infuriated about would attack any iron monster he saw. They learned of this and laid in wait one day outside the Thicket and, despite her warnings, he went out and was killed.

Appearance

Ayanna is a round, short girl with honey-brown skin who is around Tristan's age, maybe a year old. When he first meets her, she has dark hair styled in two thick braids that are pulled behind her neck, and wears gold snake bangles curling around her arm, a sleeveless gold tank top, black pants with gold trim that stop mid-calf, and brown sandals with beaded straps that tie around her ankles. Later, after the climactic fight, she wears her hair half-braided and half curly afro.

Personality

Ayanna is shown to be a cautious but caring person. She care for the well-being of her people and is willing to do anything to help them.
