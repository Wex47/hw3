Camp Jupiter Classified: A Probatio’s Journal is a companion book under The Trials of Apollo . It is structured as a diary that tells the story of Claudia , a legacy of Mercury and Probatio in the Fourth Cohort . It was released on May 5th, 2020.

Synopsis

It’s the February after Gaea 's defeat, and mysterious incidents are wreaking have throughout Camp Jupiter . If the Romans don’t figure out who- or what- is behind the episodes soon, the Twelfth Legion could implode.

Is the culprit Claudia , the Fourth Cohort 's newest Probatio ? After all, the mischief began shortly after Claudia stumbled into camp. Plus, she’s a great-granddaughter of Mercury , the god of thieves and trickster...

To find out whether she’s guilty or innocent, delve into the pages of this personal journal. Visit the crime scenes- the mess , the Colosseum , the baths, and more - to see how these bizarre events unfold. You just may discover a secret so ancient, not even the Lares know about it... a secret that holds the key to Camp Jupiter’s safety.
