Trivia

  • The concept of the Thousand Worlds is similar to the Galactic Republic in the Star Wars franchise.
    • The planet Jinju is located in the Thousand World’s outer rim region, which shares it’s name with the region where the planets Tatooine and Lothal are located.
  • The official language of the Thousand Worlds is Hangeul.
