History

Early Life

Pahua was born the oldest child to a Hmong couple. When she was was four her parents had a son, her brother Matt. When she was five she started to see spirits, she showed one to her mom, who thought she had an imaginary friend. Her father would draw pictures of her and Miv.

Her father would tell her stories about Hmong folklore when she would go to bed. When she was by a lake she saw a water spirit and inadvertently insulted it, causing it to nearly drown her before her father saved her.

When she was seven, she went with her mother, brother, and aunt to a Hmong neighborhood in St. Paul, Minnesota.

Moving to Merdel

When she was seven her dad left the family, she would spend years believing he might have stayed if she did something different. After gossip and harassment from relatives about being a single parent, her mother moved the three to the rural town of Merdel, Wisconsin. Her classmates would mock her for her appearance and what her mother packed for lunch. She once went to school sick and vomited on the child in front of her, causing the other kids to tease her about it for the rest of the year.

She once has a bad reaction after eating at Burger King, developing a fever and hives. Her mother stayed home for two days to care for her.

Her mother would take her and Matt to a local amusement park every summer, but her work and shaman duties would prevent them from going.

When she was nine, she saw her aunt Kalia for the last time as she and her mother did not get along.

Appearance

Pahua is an eleven-year old Hmong girl with long black hair and dark eyes. When she uses her powers a spiral appears on her wrist. She often wears a purple P shaped hair clip her mother gave her before trading to try and save Miv.

Personality

Due to the fact that she sees spirits that others can't, Pahua has a reputation as a weirdo. She tends to fantasize herself in different situations, such as being an adventure or a royal, as she feels isolated.

Pahua is sensitive about what others think of her, being mocked for both her ability to see spirits and for being the one Asian student in her grade.

She is protective and dedicated to her loved ones, risking her own life and safety for their sake.

Abilities

  • Battle Prowess: Despite not having any formal training, Pahua can hold her own in battle.
  • Mediumship : Pahua has the unique ability to see spirits.
  • Atmokinesis : Pahua can tap into the sky God’s domain and control the weather.

Items

  • Ceremonial Sword: Pahua uses a blunt sword meant to hurt spirits.
  • Neutralization Gloves: Pahua has a pair of gloves that protect her from drastic temperature changes when going into a new realm. They also keep her soul in her body. After using the thunder ax for the first time, the gloves start to fall apart. When she saves Matt most of the fingers have holes in them.
  • Thunder Ax : After venturing into the Tree of Souls , Pahua obtains the ax used by Shee Yee to imprison Xov. She can wield it as long as she is wearing her neutralization gloves. After donating the sole leaf of the Tree of Souls , the queen of the mountain nature spirits turns it into a comb so that she can transport it easier. It can turn into an ax if a ruby is touched.
