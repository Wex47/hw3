Prophecy (aka Clairvoyance or Remote Viewing ) is the ability to see the future through a variety of means.

Percy Jackson and the Olympians

Through out the series, the Oracle of Delphi issued prophecies to demigods going on quests. Rachel became the Oracle of Delphi following Apollo 's blessing.

The Heroes of Olympus

Various characters, such as Ella and Rachel Elizabeth Dare , issue prophecies to help predict quests though Ella just memorized many prophecies .

The Trials of Apollo

Through the series, Apollo was given different prophecies by various oracles such as the Grove of Dodona , Trophonius , Herophile , and the Cumaean Sibyl .

Magnus Chase and the Gods of Asgard

The Sword of Summer

When Magnus Chase first arrived at Hotel Valhalla , the Norns issued him a prophecy.

The Hammer of Thor

When Magnus and Samirah al-Abbas reunite with Blitzen and Hearthstone , Hearth reveals that he saw a prophecy about Blitz through his runes .

The Ship of the Dead

When meeting Frigg on a train in Flåm with Magnus Chase and Samirah al-Abbas , the queen of the Aesir tells Mallory Keen that she would need her knives that day.

The former Mayan goddess of time, Pacific , spoke the Prophecy of Fire .

The Gifted Clans series

Not Part of Riordanverse

The following article/section is from the Gifted Clans continuity under Rick Riordan Presents and not the Riordanverse canon.

The Last Fallen Star

Members of the Samjogo Clan can see the future by touching an object or person. Jennie Byun , a member of the aforementioned clan, had a vision that something was about the happen at the Gi Sanctuary and recruited Noah Noh , David Kim , and Cosette Chung to help.
