Chicago is a city in Northeastern Illinois . It is the largest city in the state, and the third-largest in the United States , with 2.7 million residents. It's among the top five most economically powerful cities in the world, according to Forbes.

The Lost Hero

Leo Valdez , Piper McLean , and Jason Grace visit the city during their Quest for Hera . They go to a mall owned by Medea , a servant of Gaea , underneath the crown fountain. Piper defeated Medea by combining potions that caused the mall to explode. Jason, Piper, and Leo escape on Festus .
