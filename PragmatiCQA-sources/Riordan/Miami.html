Percy Jackson and the Olympians

The Sea of Monsters

When returning from the Sea of Monsters , Percy Jackson , Annabeth Chase , Grover Underwood , Clarisse La Rue , and Tyson find themselves sailing into the city. After Clarisse tells them the prophecy, Percy gives her five hundred dollars and tells her to fly back to Camp Half-Blood on her own. Seconds later they remaining four are captured by Luke Castellan and taken aboard the Medea . Percy expose him as the true poisoner of Thalia's Pine Tree . Enraged, the son of Hermes attacks the son of Poseidon . However, soon after that, Chiron and the Party Ponies arrive and help them escape.
