Percy Jackson and the Olympians

The Lightning Thief

Percy Jackson , Annabeth Chase and Grover Underwood go to Los Angeles , the entrance to the Underworld to convince Hades to return Zeus' Master Bolt and Sally Jackson . After escaping the three end up on Santa Monica Beach .

The Titan's Curse

Percy, along with Thalia Grace and Zoë Nightshade travel to San Francisco to save Annabeth and Artemis from the Titan strong hold at Mount Othrys .

The Battle of the Labyrinth

When trying to find Daedalus , Percy, Annabeth, Grover, and Tyson end up in Alcatraz , just outside of San Francisco.

The Heroes of Olympus

The Lost Hero

Jason Grace , Piper McLean , Leo Valdez and Gleeson Hedge arrive in San Francisco and climb Mount Diablo to rescue Tristan McLean from the Gigante Enceladus .

The Son of Neptune

Camp Jupiter , located near San Francisco, is present at the start and conclusion of the book.

The Mark of Athena

Camp Jupiter is the setting of the first few chapters of the book.

The Trials of Apollo

The Burning Maze

Apollo , Meg McCaffrey and Grover travel around Southern California for the book. They visit Palm Springs , Malibu , Los Angeles, Pasadena , and Santa Barbara . At the end of the book, Apollo and Meg travel on a plane to the Bay Area.

The Tyrant's Tomb

The San Francisco Bay Area serves as the main setting of the book, with most of the scenes taking place in Camp Jupiter or in the surrounding cities of San Francisco or Berkeley .

The Tower of Nero

After Apollo regains his godhood, he goes to the Bay Area to check in on Camp Jupiter and see Percy Jackson and Annabeth Chase at New Rome University . He also visits Meg in Palm Springs.

Storm Runner Trilogy

Not part of Riordanverse

The following article/section is from the Storm Runner / Shadow Bruja continuity under Rick Riordan Presents and not the Riordanverse canon.

The Storm Runner

After freeing Ah-Puch , Zane Obispo is teleported to Los Angeles where he is tricked into serving the former ruler of Xib'alb'a . A few days later he ends up in San Diego and Venice Beach with his uncle Hondo and Brooks .

The Shadow Crosser

When the Shaman Institute of Higher-Order Magic comes under attack, Zane, Ren Santiago , Alana , and Louie escape via a gateway and head to the crystal caves, however they take a detour to the Matterhorn ride at Disneyland in Anaheim.

Trivia

  • Southern California is colloquially known as "SoCal".
  • California currently has the most seats in the House of Representatives, with fifty-three seats.
  • California is a part of the Mexican Cession, land obtained by the United States following the Mexican-American War, along with Arizona , Nevada , New Mexico , and Utah . Along with Western Colorado and Southwestern Wyoming.
