Roshani Chokshi is a New York Times bestselling author from Atlanta, Georgia. She is the author of the Pandava Quintet , which drew from the Mahabharata , the Ramayana , the plays of Kalidasa, the Vishnu Purana, and stories her grandmother used to tell her.
