History

Sebin was born on the planet Yonggi into the Juhwang clan, a prominent clan of tiger spirits. They were raised in a strict household with traditions upheld by their grandmother, the clan's leader.

Sebin is a teenager with a stocky build and short black hair. After being accepted into the Spece Forces, their grandmother scares their hand as a sign of loyalty to the clan.

Personality

Sebin cares greatly about their family's honor.

Abilities

  • Shapeshifting : As a tiger spirit, Sebin can change form between a human and a tiger.
  • Superhuman Strength : They are extremely strong.
