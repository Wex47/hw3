Percy Jackson and the Olympians is a television series based on the novel series of the same name by Rick Riordan . It is being developed by Disney+ and produced by 20th Television.

Production

It was first reported on We Got This Covered in September 2019, and three months later, Rick Riordan confirmed that he is in talks with Disney.

On May 14th, 2020, Rick Riordan and his wife Becky announced that an adaptation for Disney+ is in development with the two of them being involved.

On July 6th, 2020, Rick Riordan stated in a tweet response to that a likely release date for the series would be 2022 at the earliest.

Rick Riordan stated that it will be live-action and he is writing a pilot script. On June 13, 2021, in a blog post, Riordan confirmed that the pilot script has been completed and the season outline is "in good shape".

On December 10th, 2020, the show's logo was revealed.

It's reported that the budget will be higher than MCU and Star Wars and that it initially took forever to "get off the ground".

The casting process has started for the title role of Percy Jackson on April 27, 2021. That same day, it was announced that Jonathan E. Steinberg, the co-creater of Black Sails , will executive produce and co-write the series' pilot with Riordan. Steinberg along with Dan Shotz will be the series' showrunners.

On May 12, 2021, it was announced that season 1 would consist of 8 episodes.

The Percy Jackson writing room has started as of July 12, 2021. The next day on his blog, Riordan confirmed that other writers have joined the project: Daphne Olive, Monica Owusu-Breen, and Joe Tracz (writer of The Lightning Thief musical ). Additionally, Xavier Stiles, Zoë Neary, and Stewart Strandberg will serve as assistant writers for the series.

On October 24, 2021, Riordan announced that the pilot of the show will be directed by James Bobin. He also stated that they have just turned in the script for episode 2 to the studio, and are now working on the outlines for episodes 3 and 4, which he expects to be completed and approved by January.

On December 9, 2021, Riordan announced that the casting process has begun for characters Annabeth Chase and Grover Underwood.

On January 25th, 2022, the project was officially given a green light at the studio. The pilot episode will be written by Riordan and Jon Steinberg while James Bobin will be directing.

On April 11th, 2022, it was announced that Walker Scobell was cast as Percy Jackson .

On May 5th, 2022, it was announced that Leah Sava Jeffries and Aryan Simhadri were cast as Annabeth Chase and Grover Underwood respectively.

On June 2nd, production has officially begun. It was also revealed that Sally Jackson will be portrayed by Virginia Kull , Gabe Ugliano will be portrayed by Timm Sharp , Dionysus will be portrayed by Jason Mantzoukas , Alecto will be portrayed by Megan Mullally , and Chiron will be portrayed by Glynn Turman .
