Harding-Pencroft Academy , also known as HP, is a five-year high school that graduates the best marine scientists, naval warriors, navigators, and underwater explorers in the world. Ana and Dev Dakkar attend Harding-Pencroft Academy. HP has a rival school called Land Institute , which they have been fighting a cold war with for a hundred and fifty years. It was founded by Cyrus Harding and Bonaventure Pencroft.

Houses

The academy is divided into 4 houses based on what you're studying:

  • House Dolphin — communications, exploration, cryptography, counterintelligence
  • House Shark — command, combat, weapons systems, logistics
  • House Cephalopod — engineering, applied mechanics, innovation, defensive systems
  • House Orca — medicine, psychology, education, marine biology, communal memory
