Thousand Worlds series

Dragon Pearl

Kim Jun joins the space forces to explore the Thousand Worlds and help his home planet Jinju . Once he supposedly goes a-wall, his sister decides to track him down. After she is rescued from the pirate raid on the Red Azalea , she ends up on the Pale Lightning , the same ship her brother was on.

Tiger Honor

Juhwang Sebin is accepted into the Space Forces as a cadet shortly after their uncle, Juhwang Hwan , is branded a traitor.

Trivia

  • The Space Forces enlisted ranks (corporal, sergeant) are ranks within the army and marine corps while the officer ranks (ensign, lieutenant commander) are ranks withinin the navy.
