Description

The Bay Area of San Francisco contained green-and-golden hills, fog-swept coastline, glowing lacework of bridges and crazy zigzag neighborhoods shouldered up against one another like subway passengers at rush hour.

The Nimitz Freeway snaked through the East Bay flatlands, past warehouses and docklands, strip malls and rows of dilapidated bungalows. San Francisco's neighbor, Downtown Oakland had, a small cluster of high-rises. On Highway 24, the elevated lanes soared past neighborhoods of winding streets and tall conifers, white stucco houses clinging to the sides of grassy ravines.

San Francisco was first mentioned by Annabeth Chase when she told Percy Jackson how her dad was planning to move there for his studies. When he asked why she was so reluctant, she told him that every demigod should know that San Francisco is the most dangerous place a half-blood could be. Thalia Grace later explained to Percy that San Francisco was where the Mountain of Despair lies (which is why the Mist is so strong), and where many monsters dwell. The Heroes of Olympus

The Lost Hero

When Jason Grace , Piper McLean , and Leo Valdez are transported to a sidewalk cafe there by Aphrodite , they all went to Mount Diablo in San Francisco to find Piper's father, Tristan McLean , and fight Enceladus . It is also mentioned that Camp Jupiter is located there, the other reason why Greek demigods were warned not to go to, live in, or even be near this area.

The Son of Neptune

Having been swapped with Jason Grace and his memory stolen by Hera , Percy meets Lupa at the Wolf House and he is told to use his senses to find Camp Jupiter. For three days, Percy has made it to San Francisco while being chased and attacked by the Gorgon sisters, Euryale and Stheno , after they met at a Bargain Mart.

The Trials of Apollo

The Tyrant's Tomb

After their plane landed at Oakland Airport , Apollo and Meg McCaffrey have the corpse of Jason Grace loaded into a hearse. Apollo drives it to the Caldecott Tunnel witnessing reflecting on good memories he had in the city. Just when things were going smoothly for the the two, a euryomos breaks the vehicle open and demands Jason's corpse to eat. They drove into a canyon where Meg uses eucalypti trees to break their fall and Lavinia kills the euryomos.

Trivia

  • Greek demigods are warned to stay away from the city due to the large amount of monsters located around the Mount Othrys area and the Roman Camp nearby.
  • Despite the area being a huge danger to demigods, Annabeth stays there during the school semester, but she did mention she was attacked by a lot of monsters during that time.
  • The San Francisco Bay area is home to Camp Jupiter , located east of Berkeley, west of Mount Diablo, south of Berkeley Hills, and north of Oakland Hills.
  • In San Francisco lies the Golden Gate Bridge, one of the most famous bridges in the whole world.
  • The city has nicknamed the fog that constantly covers it Karl.
