Gabrielle "Gabi" Reál is one of the protagonists of the Sal & Gabi Duology , along with Sal Vidón . She's student council president and editor of the school paper.

Personality

Gabi as aspirational and self-assured leader girl who does the right thing. She is shown to be very thorough in her investigations, as she spoke with students before going to Principal Torres’ office. She is always eager to help others, as she offered to have Yasmany stay at the hospital with her when he has no where to go.

However she has a darker side to her, she is shown to be somewhat narcissistic as she hates losing a debate and is shocked when Sal did not want to talk to her. She does not let things go easily, as she hooked Sal up to a lie detector for a class performance in order to get him to confess to putting a chicken in Yadmany’s locker. She is also impatient, as she came to the Coral Castle close to midnight to show Sal what is happening with Iggy and Meow-dad.

She is aware of her negative qualities and does what she can to keep her from acting on them, however she has very little control over it.

Appearance

Gabi Real has poofy black hair covered in Shaolin monk barrettes and a 'WELL-BEHAVED WOMEN SELDOM MAKE HISTORY' T-shirt. She also wore jeans and running shoes, and every fingernail on her hands were painted a different color. Her smile made up three-quarters of her lower face. According to Sal Vidon, her skin was the color of the Pinewood Derby race car he made in shop class the previous year, after he brushed on two layers of stain.

Relationship

Family

  • Reina Real
  • Iggy Real
  • Father Figures

Gabi is close with her mother . She inherits her desire to know the truth from Reina, a journalist, which she expresses by being editor for The Rotten Egg .

When her brother was born, Gabi feared for his life due to his weak immune system. She was happy with every improvement he made and became sad when it worsened. When Iggy's immune system shut down and he was close to death, she begged Sal to switch him out with a healthier version of him from another universe. She was beyond happy when Sal cured him.

When the Remembranation Machine started to hurt him, she again became concerned for his life.

Gabi takes knowledge and comfort from her father figures , using their fields of expertise and connections to broaden her opportunities.

Friends

  • Sal Vidón
  • Yasmany Robles

When they first met, Gabi tried to get Sal in trouble for putting a chicken in Yasmany's locker, using facts to prove Sal's guilt. After Yasmany gets three days detention for slamming Sal's diabetes bag to the ground. To get the truth out of him, she pestered him and strapped him to a lie detector in order learn what really happened, stopping Sal from eating and landing him in the hospital that night.

After he learns of Iggy's condition and her father figures, the two start to bond and he helps explain her new abilities to her. He also talks her out of switching out a dying Iggy with a healthier one. They vow to explore the multiverse together.
Gabi and Yasmany have been close with one another for years. She even had a crush on him in the fourth grade. When he was sent to the principal's office for slamming Sal's diabetes bag to the ground, she acted as his lawyer and tried to pin the incident on Sal. Whenever his home life became unstable, she would step in and help him however she could, from letting him spend the night with her family to getting child cervices involved.

Etymology

  • Gabriella is an Italian feminine given name meaning “messenger of god”.
  • Reál is a surname derived from the Latin name regis meaning “royal”.

Trivia

  • Gabi was on a character from the short fiction of Carlos Hernandez, "a journalist and her beat is the cryptic and the fantastic—unicorns, uploaded brains, that sort of thing."
  • She has arachnophobia.
  • Gabi is a vegetarian.
  • She hates emojis, believing they are making the population dumber.
  • Gabi is an atheist, and does not believe in an afterlife.
