Trivia

  • Since The Fire Keeper and The Shadow Crosser , both deal with Mexica, or Aztec, gods in addition to the Mayan Gods , this is the second series to have two pantheons, the first being the Camp Half-Blood series .
  • All titles in the trilogy refer to a person in each of the books:
    • The Storm Runner - Zane Obispo
    • The Fire Keeper - Antonio Marcel De la Vega
    • The Shadow Crosser - Hondo Obispo
