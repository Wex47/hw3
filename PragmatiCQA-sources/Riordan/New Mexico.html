Trivia

  • The state flag of New Mexico consists of the Zia Sun Symbol, a tribe within the state, and is colored yellow and red, the colors of the Spanish flag.
  • Before becoming a U.S. State, New Mexico was known as the Santa Fe de Nuev México. It served as a province of New Spain and a territory of Mexico .
  • New Mexico is part of the Mexican Cession, land obtain by the United States following the Mexican-American War, along with what would become the states of Arizona , California , Nevada , and Utah along with sections of western Colorado and southwestern Wyoming.
