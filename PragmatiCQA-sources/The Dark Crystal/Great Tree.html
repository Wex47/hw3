The Great Trees , also known as Patron Trees , were sentient trees native to Skarith . Among the oldest organisms of Thra , they carried the memories of the world in their vast root systems, which mirrored the structure of a brain. They had multiple, often contradictory origin stories. One story credited Aughra with planting them all, and two tales included Jarra-Jen 's involvement.

While they never favoured one race over another, they were a constant source of guidance and inspiration to the Gelfling . During the Age of Division , the trees sensed the coming of the Darkening and consciously began to absorb it in an attempt to prevent it from spreading. As the Crystal weakened, however, the trees began to falter, prompting the Sanctuary Tree, Vliste-Staba , to call for the formation of the Gelfling resistance .
