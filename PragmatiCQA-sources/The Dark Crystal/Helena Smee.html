General Information

Worked On:

The Dark Crystal: Age of Resistance

Puppeteered:

Seladon Mira SkekLach
Helena Smee is a puppeteer, puppet builder and writer who performed Seladon , Mira and SkekLach in The Dark Crystal: Age of Resistance . She also provided additional voices in the series. She is the sister of Katherine Smee .
