The Dark Crystal: Age of Resistance Vol. 2 - Original Music from the Netflix Series is the second of two albums that feature the musical score written for The Dark Crystal: Age of Resistance . It features music by Daniel Pemberton and Samuel Sim is preceded by The Dark Crystal: Age of Resistance Vol. 1 - Original Music from the Netflix Series .
