SkekHak was, along with skekYi , one of the first Skeksis to die shortly after the Great Division . Immediately after the separation, skekHak choked urYa to death, causing the corresponding death of skekYi . He then proceeded to push urHom down the shaft of the Castle , and was subsequently burned to death.
