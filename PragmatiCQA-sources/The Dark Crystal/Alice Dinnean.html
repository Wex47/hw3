Alice Dinnean-Vernon (born May 23, 1969) is an American puppeteer and creative artist who works at The Jim Henson Company . She is the puppeteer for Brea , Maudra Fara , SkekEkt and Bobb'N in The Dark Crystal: Age of Resistance . She also voiced SkekEkt and provided additional voices in the series.
