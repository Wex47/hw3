Taron David Egerton (born November 10, 1989) is a Welsh actor and singer who voices Rian in The Dark Crystal: Age of Resistance .

Trivia

  • Egerton was already a fan of the original The Dark Crystal when he was cast as Rian, having been introduced to it at a young age by his father.
  • Upon being introduced to Toby Froud , the first thing he said was "You're the baby from Labyrinth !"
