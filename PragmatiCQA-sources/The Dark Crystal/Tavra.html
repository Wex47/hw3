Tavra was a female Gelfling who was born shortly before the Age of Harmony , and was a good friend of Raunip . As was typical of her people, "those who live without knowledge of the future", she was short-sighted, and was unconcerned with the implications of the first Great Conjunction and the arrival of the Fallen urSkeks . Unlike her friend Raunip, she believed in the urSkeks promises of good will. Her friendship with Raunip became strained as the trine went by, as he never aged. After raising a family and becoming a grandmother, Tavra invited Raunip to visit her on her deathbed. Before dying, she declared that she would always remember him, even in the next life.

She was the namesake of a princess of the Vapra Clan .
