Sifan separatist slogan

The Age of Sifans was a plan first devised by Fenth to have the Sifa Clan secede from the authority of the All-Maudra while maintaining friendly relations with the Skeksis Empire .

Tensions between the Sifa and the ruling Vapra Clan had been strained during the reign of Seladon I , who had ignored the Sifan's concerns of losing their distinct culture and had initially expressed their defiance by poaching fish off the Vapran coast. After the All-Maudra's death, Fenth, a former pretender to the Living Crown and advisor to the Sifan Maudra, refused to recognize the authority of the newly crowned Mayrin , and quickly garnered a following among young, disaffected comrades.

With the assistance of her advisor Dot'leth , Mayrin attempted to negotiate with Fenth on neutral ground, but her transport, the Scalene Anchor , was destroyed by the Mondo Levidian .
