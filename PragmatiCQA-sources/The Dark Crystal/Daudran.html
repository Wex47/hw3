Unnamed Daudran was the Gelfling overseer of the Order of Lesser Service during the late Age of Division .

Biography

He was the Draudren of the vapran Order of Lesser Service branch, when Brea was consigned there for a trine , as punishment for wiping Elder Caida 's memory. With good cheer, he assigned his order to wash a Podling village who were filthy. Later, he attended the Second Battle of Stone-in-the-Wood with the rest of the Vapra Clan .
