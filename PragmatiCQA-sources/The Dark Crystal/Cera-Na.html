Cera-Na was a village located on the Sifa Coast , nestled in a bay on the ocean side of the Claw Mountains . It was the home port of the Sifa Clan , and was protected from the harsh ocean winds by the cliffs surrounding it.

It was an important center of commerce with a population in a continual state of flux, consisting of sailors coming in and out of port, where they would trade their catches and finds.

The Sifa would congregate there for important occasions, such as the boat races following the arrival of migrating Hooyim , and the docking of the flagship Omerya .

Other continuities

In Tides of the Dark Crystal , Cera-Na is described as an "Ever Changing dock" with ships coming in and out daily. It is said to be home to a vast amount of Gelfling and the Skeksis lord SkekSa . Cera-Na is evacuated by Maudra Ethri but is then un-evacuated once the Sifan fire is lit.
