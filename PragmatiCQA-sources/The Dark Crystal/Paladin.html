Paladins were a class of Gelfling warriors who served directly under the All-Maudra . Their primary task was to defend the Gelfling capital of Ha'rar , though they also guarded trade caravans undertook rescue missions and captured fugitives. Male paladins were typically granted the title of "sir", while females were referred to as "lady". Paladins who acted dishonestly could be stripped of their title.
