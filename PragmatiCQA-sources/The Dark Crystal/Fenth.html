Fenth was a male Gelfling of the Sifa Clan and advisor to the Sifan Maudra , who lived during the late Age of Division .

In his youth, he became the first male Gelfling to make a credible claim for the Living Crown . Many trine later, encouraged by the death of All-Maudra Seladon and inspired by promises of Skeksis support for Sifan autonomy, Fenth masterminded the Age of Sifans , encouraging his aging Maudra to withdraw from the authority of the ruling Vapra Clan , believing that the newly crowned All-Maudra Mayrin was in no position to oppose him due to her inexperience.

After being informed of Mayrin's disappearance in the Silver Sea by skekSa , Fenth sought to exploit the power vacuum by skipping negotiations and declaring immediate Sifan independence, but was thwarted by Dot'leth , who took Mayrin's place as regent. During the negotiations aboard skekSa's Dreadnought , Fenth was shocked to see Mayrin escape from the Mondo Levidian pursued by a swarm of Zoa . In the ensuing battle, Fenth attempted to kill Mayrin, but was thwarted by his former supporter Kam'lu . He attempted to convince his followers that Mayrin's actions proved that she was a worse ruler than her mother, but was silenced by the Sifan Maudra, who pledged allegiance to Mayrin. He was subsequently abducted by skekSa, who lost interest in Gelfling politics and threatened to take him to skekTek for his failure.
