Skarith , also known as the Skarith Region or Skarith Land , was a vast landmass of Thra surrounded by the Silver Sea and bisected by the Black River . It was naturally divided into several regions which were named by Gelfling settlers. Aside from the Gelfling, its sentient races included the Podlings and Arathim . During the Age of Division , most of Skarith was conquered by the Skeksis Empire .

Main regions

Crystal Desert

A desert region enclosed in the Claw Mountains , inhabited by the Dousan Clan during the Age of Division .

Grot

A mountain range containing a vast network of caves which were the home of the Arathim during the Age of Harmony , but appropriated by the Grottan Clan during the Age of Division .

Endless Forest

A vast forest which was the original homeland of the Podlings and home to the Stonewood Clan during the Age of Division .

Northern Mountains

A mountain range and location of Ha'rar , home of the Vapra Clan and capital of the Gelfling during the Age of Division.

Sifa Coast

A coastline containing Cera-Na , home port of the Sifa Clan .

Spriton Plains

A vast plain containing grasslands and a number of woodlands, including Kep and Sami Thicket , home of the Spriton Clan .

Sog

A swampy area inhabited by the Drenchen Clan during the Age of Division.
