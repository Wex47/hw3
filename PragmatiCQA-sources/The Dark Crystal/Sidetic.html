SkekTek never forgets a true friend.

skekTek

The Sidetic was a small, green-furred bird-like animal kept in a cage within the Chamber of Life during the late Age of Division . SkekTek uncharactaristically developed a fondness for the creature, comforting it after skekSo strangled it. Its cage served as the Scientist’s secret stash of essence . It later witnessed its master's brutal murder of the last Gruenak .
