The Dark Crystal

General Information

Author:

Ted Kryczko Jymn Magon

Series:

Read-Along Adventures

Publisher:

Buena Vista Records

Publication Date:

1982

Pages:

24
The Dark Crystal read-along book was released in 1982. The original release included a 7" 33 1/3 RPM record, and a 24-page storybook. The book and audio was also released as a book and tape in a "read-along double set" paired with The Black Stallion Returns .

Voices

  • William Woodson as the narrator
  • Cam Clarke as Jen
  • Tony Pope as SkekSil
  • Cherry Davis as Aughra
