The apeknot tree was a tree native to Thra specifically the Swamp of Sog where they formed a labyrinth web around the Great Smerth . Apeknot trees have multiple canopies, and are large enough to home a small Gelfling family. They are easy to climb and usually act as pathways for the Drenchen Clan .

The trees were mostly was composed of huge vines that snaked up from the forest below like tentacles, gripping the highlands and rocks as if the wood itself were climbing. They were sensitive to movement in their surroundings; when disturbed young trees would ball into knotted fists, though older trees that were armored in centuries of harden bark would shudder, creak and crack.
