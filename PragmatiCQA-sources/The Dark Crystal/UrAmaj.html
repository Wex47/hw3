UrAmaj was the urRu 's Cook, and counterpart to skekAyuk the Gourmand.

While sharing his Skeksis counterpart's love of food, urAmaj believed that such pleasure arose through its preparation rather than consumption. As such, his meals were typically best served cold. He approached his work slowly, blending the flavours and textures of various foodstuffs till the desired soothing properties were attained. UrAmaj's meals were however shunned by Jen , as he was unable to cook Gelfling food. He was a close friend of urNol the Herbalist, and the two of them would attend to the greater and lesser balances of the bodies and spirits of their companions.
