Sog , also known as the Swamp of Sog , was a region in southern Skarith bordering the Spriton Plains and the Misty Mountains . It was characterized by its tropical climate and consisted primarily of dense Apeknot jungles growing from freshwater lakes. It saw little seasonal variation, and maintained the same humid climate, high temperature and daily rainfall throughout the trine .

It was the homeland of the Drenchen Clan , who, according to the story Ipsy and the Great Seed , colonized the area under the leadership of Maudra Ipsim Vlisabi-Nara , who was also credited with having planted the seed of Smerth-Staba , Sog's Great Tree .

Ecology

Fauna

Thriya considered the swamp of Sog an area that had "created a hunting environment unlike any other" , being rich in animal life, with fish being particularly abundant. Among its animals were:

  • Blindfish
  • Drumlur
  • Gizzizzy
  • Gridit
  • Guffle
  • Flingit
  • Muski
  • Nebrie
  • Pimlin
  • Ruswha
  • Sogbird
  • Sontonic
  • Squiddleitch

Flora

Because of its invariably warm climate and plentiful rainfall, Sog was rich in flowers, which were larger and grew more abundantly than anywhere else in Skarith. Sogflowers were among the most common, and grew from the swamp beds.

Much of Sog was covered by a dense canopy of Apeknot Trees , which grew there exclusively. Their outgrowth was so densely packed that it was hard to discern where one tree began and another ended. It was popularly believed that they originated from Smerth-Staba , the region's Great Tree and patron of the Drenchen Clan.
