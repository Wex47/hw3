The Tale of the Dark Crystal is an illustrated book adaptation of the film The Dark Crystal .

The book was originally released in a hardback edition under the "Weekly Reader Books presents" banner. The book was also released in a slightly larger paperback edition (with a dust jacket) as part of the "Weekly Reader Book Club".
