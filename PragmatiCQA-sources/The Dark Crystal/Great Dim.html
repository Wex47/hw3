The Great Dim was an ecological catastrophe that befell Mithra during the Age of Power . Caused by the accumulation of offerings to the Crystal blocking its light from nourishing Mithra's Mother Sun , the Dim caused temperatures to fall and, consequently, the petrification of all living creatures below Thra's surface. It resulted in the extinction of numerous Hallows , as well as the deaths of all Ember Queens , including Thurma's mother . The Dim was averted when Thurma cleared the shaft of the Crystal of the accumulated offerings.
