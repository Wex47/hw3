Songs of the Seven Gelfling Clans is a book by J.M. Lee published on July 28, 2020.

This book explains, with the art of Brian Froud , the different living habits of the different Clans told by the Song teller Thriya .

J.M. Lee stated in an interview that his initial intention was to have the book narrated by an adult Kylan after the events of Flames of the Dark Crystal , but opted for an original character instead. He further elaborated that the events described in the book take place "early in the Age of Division ".
