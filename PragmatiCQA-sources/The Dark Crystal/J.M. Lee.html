J.M. Lee is an American author who wrote the Dark Crystal young adult novels after winning the Author quest . He subsequently wrote Songs of the Seven Gelfling Clans , Heroes of the Resistance , Aughra's Wisdom of Thra and The Dark Crystal Author Quest . He was a creative consultant and staff writer on The Dark Crystal: Age of Resistance . He wrote the second episode, " Nothing Is Simple Anymore ," and created the Podling language used in the show.
