The Royal Guard was a military order of Gelfling established by Jen and Kira during the early Age of Power . Originally intended to protect the Castle of the Crystal , the Royal Guard became corrupt under the influence of the Crystalline Eminence , using its authority to requisition food and goods from the Gelfling Nation 's citizens under the guise of keeping the Crystal satiated.
