Solobes were reptiles native to the Crystal Desert . They were frequently sighted lying on their bellies with their mouths gaping in order to keep their bodies cool, while the crystalline sails on their backs absorbed nutrients from the Three Suns . The Dousan often interpreted this as laziness, and derogatorily called them "slobs".

During the Age of Division , Solobes were exploited by skekTek as living batteries for the machines he created.

A roasted Solobe was served to skekEkt during a banquet in honor of their return, shortly before the formation of the Gelfling resistance .
