Brian Desmond Froud (born May 6, 1947 in Winchester, England) is an English fantasy illustrator. He served as a cover artist on Shadows of the Dark Crystal and a conceptual designer on The Dark Crystal and the cancelled Power of the Dark Crystal . His wife is Wendy Froud and his son is Toby Froud .
