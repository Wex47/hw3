The Scepter of Office was a scepter of Garthic design traditionally held by the Emperor of the Skeksis . The top of the scepter incorporated a sculpture of the abiranariba serpent swallowing the world. Its body had nine segments, the symbolic number of instability.

Use of the Scepter dated back to the very beginning of skekSo 's rule, and was used by him to beat skekShod for questioning his rule. During the time of the Gelfling resistance , skekSo incorporated a small crystal onto the scepter which allowed him to gather the Darkening and use it to lethal effect during the Second Battle of Stone-in-the-Wood .

After the the death of skekSo, skekSil attempted to take the Scepter during his dispute with skekUng , thus prompting the latter to challenge him to Trial by Stone , resulting in his ascension as the new emperor. When Jen accidentally dropped the Shard of the Division in the Crystal Chamber while attempting to heal the Crystal , skekUng tossed the Scepter aside to take the Shard.

During the Age of Power , the Scepter was housed alongside other Skeksis relics in skekTek 's former laboratory . Jen used the Scepter to arrest the newly returned Skeksis and imprison them, but had it taken from him by skekSil . It was eventually lost in the Great Divide during skekSil's pursuit of Thurma and Kensho .

Behind the scenes

According to Brian Froud , the scepter was based on a broken ouroboros, symbolizing the Skeksis' fractured view of the world.
