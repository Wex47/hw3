The Mother Sun also known as the Inner Sun was a star located in Mithra which nourished all life within it. It was continuously lit by the Crystal 's light through the Wishing Well , but was subsequently blocked during the Age of Power by offerings being piled up around the Crystal, thus causing the Great Dim . It was reignited when Thurma went through the well and removed the offerings from the other side.

According to SkekGra , he and UrGoh forged the Dual Glaive in the fires of this Sun.
