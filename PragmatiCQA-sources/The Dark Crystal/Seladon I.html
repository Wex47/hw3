Seladon

Seladon I was the Maudra of the Vapra Clan and All-Maudra of the entire Gelfling race, who lived during the late Age of Division .

She was respected by her courtiers for her efforts to bring unity to the divided Gelfling race, and was considered a kind and brave ruler, skilled with a spear and unmatched in her ability to traverse Raunip's Pass with ease. She was also the dearest friend of her advisor, Dot'leth . Seladon was, nevertheless, a cruel and dismissive mother, who mocked her daughter's inability to match her skill in flying. Furthermore, despite her dreams of a unified Gelfling culture, she was neglectful of her Sifan constituents, on whose vessels she would never deign to set foot on. She was also a staunch conservative, having her throne decorated with flowers in the manner of the Ha'rar traditionalists, just as her ancestors did, and did not heed her non-Vapran subjects expressing concern that her reign was eroding their distinctive cultures.

Her death was exploited by Fenth to encourage the Sifa Clan to secede from the authority of the Vapra Clan. After the crisis was averted, Mayrin pledged to name her first daughter after her mother.
