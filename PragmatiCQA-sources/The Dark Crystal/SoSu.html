The original urSkek identity of Emperor skekSo and Master urSu . He was heavily implied to be the leader of the UrSkek group due to his split counterparts both being the leaders of their respective races, the Skeksis and the Mystics . After the deaths of both leaders UngIm became the leader of the remaining eight urSkeks due to Garthim-Master skekUng becoming the next Emperor and leader of the Skeksis .
