Old Gelfling was the archaic language of the Gelfling . By the late Age of Division , it had fallen into disuse in favor of Modern Gelfling , though words from the language were used in titles and personal names, while full sentences were used for ritual and ceremonial purposes.

  • dodotraba - "very big table"
