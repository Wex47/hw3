David Odell (born July 28, 1943) is an Emmy Award-winning screenwriter and film director. He wrote the screenplay for The Dark Crystal and Power of the Dark Crystal . Odell first worked with Jim Henson as part of the writing team on The Muppet Show , and also received a "special thanks" credit on The Muppet Movie .

Odell's other credits include screenplays for the feature films Supergirl and Masters of the Universe and several episodes of the TV anthology Monsters . He also directed the 1990 comedy film Martians Go Home .
