The Maudras were the matriarchs of each Gelfling clan during the Age of Division , and were tasked with governing their clan members, as well as representing them before the Skeksis . The Maudras themselves were subservient to the All-Maudra who, as well as acting as Maudra to her own clan, also doubled as ruler of all Gelflings.

Behind the Scenes

The word "Maudra" means mother, clan mother. The word was created by Joe Lee when he began writing the young adult novels. The production set used the concept from Joe Lee's novels as groundwork for the series, using words such as Maudra, or Unum.
