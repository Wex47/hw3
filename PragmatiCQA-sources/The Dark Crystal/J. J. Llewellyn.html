J. J. Llewellyn was a professor from Oxford on Earth , notable for having participated in deciphering the Book of Aughra , which he annotated and published in 1981.

While passionate and open minded about the implications of the subject, Llewellyn approached his work with extreme caution, fearing the consequences of accidentally establishing contact with Thra or summoning the Garthim , which he suspected were really thought projections which could materialize upon being visualized.
