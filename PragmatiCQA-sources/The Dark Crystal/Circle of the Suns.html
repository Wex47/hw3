Circle of the Suns

General Information

Location(s) on Thra

Crystal Desert

The Circle of the Suns was a ruin located in the Crystal Desert . It was situated on a cavern leading to the Breath of Thra , and was avoided by the Dousan Clan , whose members considered it cursed. It became the home of the exiles skekGra the Heretic and urGoh the Wanderer, who cultivated hallucinogenic urdrupes there in order to see into the future. UrGoh erected copies of the Standing Stones within the Circle, but they proved ineffective in repelling skekMal 's attack.
