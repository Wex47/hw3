Kevin Jeffrey Clash (born September 17, 1960) is an American puppeteer, director and producer who performed Aughra , The Librarian , SkekVar , SkekMal and Gruenak #1 in The Dark Crystal: Age of Resistance . He also voiced Gruenak #1 and provided additional voices in the series.
