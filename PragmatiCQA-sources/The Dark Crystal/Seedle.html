Seedle

General Information

Location(s)

Endless Forest
Seedles were large quadrupeds native to the Endless Forest . Their skins were coated in plants and fungi, which would grow as the creatures aged. Upon attaining maturity, Seedles would root themselves to the ground and become the foundation of a new forest. By the late Age of Division , Seedles had become so rare that only a few Gelfling knew of their existence.

An immature specimen was discovered years after the Arathim Wars near a Spriton village by the healer Veara , who kept its existence a secret. Once the Darkening began to spread, the creature became agitated and sought out Veara in her village, unintentionally implicating itself in a series of destructive acts perpetrated by contaminated Fizzgigs .
