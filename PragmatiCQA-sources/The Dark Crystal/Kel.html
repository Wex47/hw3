Lady Kel was a female Gelfling of high birth, being the daughter of Vedev the Elder . She was born during the final few trine of the Age of Harmony .

When the Fallen urSkeks were due to return to their homeworld, Kel was invited to witness the event. She was joined on the way by Raunip , with whom she shared a suspicion of the urSkek's motives, and song-teller Gyr , with whom she formed a bond. When they arrived at The Castle of the Crystal , Kel was one of the few to witness The Great Division . She managed to fight off the Skeksis alongside Gyr, and escaped from the Castle atop Landstriders .
