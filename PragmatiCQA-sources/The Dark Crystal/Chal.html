Chal

Chal was a Gelfling explorer of the Dousan Clan who lived during the Age of Division . In his youth, he left the Crystal Desert and founded what would one day become Kensho 's village in a forest. He had a reputation for generosity, seeking out Thra 's treasures only to share them with all Gelflings.

He became a fugitive after taking an outsider, Saluna , as a mate. During their escape, the lovers were found by Kataal 's men, who injured Chal with an arrow. Chal and Saluna took refuge in Mithra , and eventually had three children, among which was Cindrah , the first Fireling .
