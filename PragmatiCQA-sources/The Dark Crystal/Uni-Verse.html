Single Song

The Uni-Verse , also known as the Single Song , was the location of Thra , the urSkek homeworld, Earth , and countless other worlds, as well as suns, moons and stars.

According to the urSkeks , the power of the Uni-Verse could be manipulated by those who dared to control and shape their destinies, with only the completely initiated being able to fully wield that power. Reaching such a level of proficiency required great willpower, as well as many trine of meditation and silence.

During the Age of Division , Aughra spent several ninets exploring the Uni-Verse through astral projection, a task which she described as "no easy feat, not even for Aughra".
