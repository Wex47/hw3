The Field of Fire was a remote valley of Thra far from Skarith , made up of smoke and molten rock. Its environment was extremely hostile, with only the strongest creatures being able to survive there.

After the Great Division , urGoh claimed to have heard about the place during his wanderings, and proposed it as a new homeland for the Makraks . Raunip volunteered to lead them there, despite the risks to his own life. On the way, he traversed the mountain of Kral and encountered the Pirate birds of Lados . He eventually found the Field, but lost half his face in the process and developed a respiratory disorder.
