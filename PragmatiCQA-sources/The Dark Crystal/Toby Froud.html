Tobias Matthew Froud (born June 27, 1984) is a visual artist, performance artist and filmmaker who worked as a production designer on The Dark Crystal: Age of Resistance . He is the son of Brian Froud and Wendy Midener .

As an infant, he portrayed Toby in the Jim Henson film Labyrinth .
