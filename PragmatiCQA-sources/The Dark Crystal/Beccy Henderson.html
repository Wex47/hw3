Beccy Henderson is a Northern Irish actress and puppeteer. She is the puppeteer for Deet , Naia and Maudra Seethi in The Dark Crystal: Age of Resistance . She also voiced Bobb'N and Maudra Ethri and provided additional voices in the series.
