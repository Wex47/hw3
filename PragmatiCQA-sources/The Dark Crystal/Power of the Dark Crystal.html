The Power of the Dark Crystal is a comic book series based on the screenplay for the unproduced movie Power of the Dark Crystal by David Odell , Annette Odell and Craig Pearce . It was written by Simon Spurrier and Phillip Kennedy Johnson with artwork by Kelly and Nichole Matthews . It was released in twelve issues between October 31st, 2017 and March 14, 2018. It was followed by a sequel, Beneath the Dark Crystal .
