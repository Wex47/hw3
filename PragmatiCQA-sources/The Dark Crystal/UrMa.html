UrMa was the urRu 's Peacemaker, and counterpart to skekVar the General.

He was one of the few urRu to disagree with urSu 's policy of non-interference in the affairs of other races, and frequently acted as an arbiter during conflicts. He further distinguised himself in believing that the Skeksis , for all their flaws, could be reasoned with.

He died during the Second Battle of Stone-in-the-Wood , when SkekSil the Chamberlain stabbed skekVar through the chest.
