Trunk

Dagger Root was a coastal town located in the periphery of Gelfling territory. Founded in the early Age of Power , it was originally a port vital for local trade, until the father of Trunk took over by subjugating the populace with Skeksis relics. The town was heavily fortified, and could only be accessed by those who demonstrated their worthiness by breaking in.
