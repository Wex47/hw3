Warrick Brownlow-Pike (born January 28, 1985) is a British puppeteer who performed SkekSil , Maudra Laesid , Lath'N , the Red-Haired Paladin and Podling Servant #1 in The Dark Crystal: Age of Resistance . He also voiced the Red-Haired Paladin and Podling Servant #1 provided additional voices in the series.
