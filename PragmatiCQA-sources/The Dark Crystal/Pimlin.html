Pimlins were mammals native to Sog . They would build their nests using swamp mosses and feed on tree leaves and insects. They superficially resembled Sidetics , with fine coats of thick green fur accentuated by long pale feathers. They were among Thra 's most elusive creatures, with the last known captive specimen being kept in the Chamber of Life during the late Age of Division , where it was dissected by skekTek , who merely noted that the color of its blood matched that of its fur.
