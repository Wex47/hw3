Louise Gold (born August 19, 1956) is an English puppeteer, actress and singer who performed skekAyuk in The Dark Crystal and The Dark Crystal: Age of Resistance . She also performed Maudra Argot , All-Maudra Mayrin , Onica , Maudra Ethri and Podling Servant #1 in The Dark Crystal: Age of Resistance . She also voiced Maudra Argot and Podling Servant #1 and provided additional voices in the series.
