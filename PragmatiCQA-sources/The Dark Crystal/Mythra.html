Mythra is the only daughter of Ordon and Shoni and the younger sister of Rian.

Biography

She came across Kylan and an injured Naia as they fled the Hunter and took them back to her home so Naia could recover. The next day, she invited Naia and Kylan to dinner and introduced them to her mother and little brother. Later, as Rian, Naia, and Kylan were leaving for High Hill, she told her brother that she when she was bigger she would come and fight the Skeksis with him, to which he playfully responded that once she was big enough not to be eaten in one gulp, he would welcome her.
