Armaligs were burrowing creatures related to Nurlocs . The common variety was originally native to the root networks of the Endless Forest , where they formed large colonies, while a dwarf variant inhabited the caves of Grot .

Common Armaligs were domesticated during the Age of Division by the Skeksis to act as wheels to their carriages , which they could propel at great speeds, while the dwarf variety was hunted as a food source by the Grottan Clan . Wild Armaligs suffered a great decline throughout this period, with Thriya noting that Grottan dwarf Armaligs were becoming scarce, and wild common Armaligs had been largely driven to extinction in the trine leading up to the Garthim War .

The paladin, Barfinnious , and his squire, Hup , rode an armalig known as Marengax .
