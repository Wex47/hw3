Peeper beetles were carnivorous invertebrates native to much of Thra , including the Crystal Desert and being particularly common in the Spriton Plains . They were robust creatures with segmented bodies and stick-thin legs which they used to burrow into soft soil, where they would wait for unsuspecting prey. Most of their victims consisted of smaller animals, though Gelfling were also vulnerable, as the beetles had a craving for the jelly found within eyeballs. Among their few natural predators were Armored Bubs .

The Skeksis kept starved Peepers as part of their Five Ocular Castigations , using them exclusively against each other as punishments for failure. One specimen was used to gouge out skekTek 's right eye as punishment for failing to prevent Rian from stealing Mira 's Essence Vial .
