Bellanji , Naia and Gurjin ’s father, is husband of Maudra Laesid of the Drenchen Clan .

Biography

In shadows of the dark crystal, When Tavra first appeared at Great Smerth , Bellanji greeted her along with Naia , making a joke about the all-maudra having hundreds of children.

He later accompanied Naia and Tavra on their quest to find Gurjin , but had to turn back because a darkened Nebrie attacked him leaving Naia to leave on her own.
