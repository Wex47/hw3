SkekLi was the Skeksis ' satirist and counterpart to urLii the Storyteller.

History

SkekLi was not present at the Castle of the Crystal during the breakdown of the Alliance of the Crystal . Prior to the Second Battle of Stone-in-the-Wood , when the Skeksis were considering recalling their absent brethren before going to war with the Gelfling, skekOk asked whether or not it would have been productive to summon skekLi.

Other appearances

In J.M. Lee's Song of the Dark Crystal , skekLi was the mastermind behind the Skeksis- Arathim alliance, and took pride in his plot, resenting the way his comrades mocked him as a mere jester.
