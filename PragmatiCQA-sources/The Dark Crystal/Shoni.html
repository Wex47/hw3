Shoni was a female Gelfling of the Stonewood Clan who lived during the late Age of Division . She was the wife of Ordon and mother of Rian .

When Ordon was instructed by Maudra Vala to retrieve the Dual Glaive during the Arathim Wars , Shoni lamented his inability to see any role for himself outside the battlefield.

During the First Battle of Stone-in-the-Wood , Shoni was evacuated from Stone-in-the-Wood along with other non-combattants. After Ordon defeated the Arathim , he proposed having a child with Shoni, who suggested the name "Rian" in reference to the name's etymological root in Old Gelfling , meaning "a little solution to a big problem".
