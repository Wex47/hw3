Unglm was the urSkeks original form of skekUng and urIm . After SoSu 's divided counterparts' deaths, he became the leader of the urSkeks, being the one who directly speaks to Jen and explains the actions that led to the Great Division , thanking Jen for reuniting them. After he and his colleagues revive Kira , he tells Jen to be with her and that they now are the masters of The Crystal of Truth before leaving.
