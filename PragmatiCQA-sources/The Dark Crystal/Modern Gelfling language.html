Modern Gelfling was the native language of the Gelfling , coming into use about halfway through the Age of Harmony , completely substituting Old Gelfling in everyday parlance.

It was used throughout the Age of Division as a lingua franca among the sentient races of Skarith and influenced the demotic speech of the Skeksis . It was however not widely used by the Podlings until the Age of Power .

While there were no major dialectal differences among the Gelfling Clans , the Vapra were noted for speaking in an accent resembling that of some of the Skeksis . They also differed in their writing systems, with the Vapra using logograms , while the Spriton had an actual alphabet.
