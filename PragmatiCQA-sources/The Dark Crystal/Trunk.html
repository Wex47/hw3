Trunk was the leader of Dagger Root . He commanded the Branches and was in charge of the daily Tithes within the village. He was also a collector of Skeksis artifacts, which eventually corrupted him into the greedy man he was.

While trying to "torture the light out of" Kensho , the latter was able to withstand the abuse inflicted upon him and healed Trunk of his corruption. He subsequently left, not knowing what to do with himself.
