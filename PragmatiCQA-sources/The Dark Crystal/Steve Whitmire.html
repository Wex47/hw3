Steve Whitmire (born September 24, 1959) joined The Jim Henson Company as a puppeteer in 1978. He is best known for his roles as Rizzo the Rat, Kermit the Frog and Beaker on The Muppets and Wembley and Sprocket in Jim Henson's Fraggle Rock .

Whitmire worked on The Dark Crystal , providing the puppetry and voice for SkekTek (the Scientist).
