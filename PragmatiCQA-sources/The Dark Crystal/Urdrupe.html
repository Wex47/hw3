Urdrupes were red hallucinogenic berries native to the Crystal Desert . Their mystical properties were popular with Dousan shamans, who used them to converse with Thra and to glimpse into the future. Urdrupes grew in large numbers in the Circle of the Suns , where skekGra and urGoh consumed copious amounts, leading them to see that Gelfling would be the ones to bring about the downfall of the Skeksis .
