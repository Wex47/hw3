Neil Sterenberg is a British puppeteer at The Jim Henson Company . He is the puppeteer for Rian , Tavra , SkekOk , Maudra Mera and Gruenak #2 in The Dark Crystal: Age of Resistance . He also voiced SkekOk and Gruenak #2 and provided additional voices in the series.
