Frank Oz (born May 25, 1944) is a British-born American film director, actor and puppeteer. Oz co-directed The Dark Crystal along with Jim Henson and puppeteered several characters in the film, mainly taking on the roles of Aughra and SkekSil .

Oz is known for his work as a puppeteer. His characters have included Miss Piggy and Fozzie Bear on The Muppet Show ; Grover, Cookie Monster and Bert on Sesame Street ; and Yoda in the Star Wars saga.

Oz began his behind-the-camera work when he co-directed The Dark Crystal . Oz further employed those skills in directing The Muppets take Manhattan and Little Shop Of Horrors . Oz also went on to direct Dirty Rotten Scoundrels , What About Bob? , HouseSitter , The Indian in the Cupboard , In & Out , Bowfinger , The Score , The Stepford Wives , and Death at a Funeral .
