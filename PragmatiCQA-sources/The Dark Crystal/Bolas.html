The bola was a throwing weapon of varying design made of weights on the ends of interconnected cords.

The urRu used bolas as divinatory objects, made by twining uncut threads into a trifurcated knot resembling the symbol for the Great Conjunction .

Bolas were the tool of choice of Drenchen hunters, alongside the spear .

After the Garthim War , some Podling villages developed the practice of using a single weighted design to dispatch Crystal Bats , thus preventing Garthim raids.

A three-weighted variant was adopted during the Age of Power by the Royal Guard .
