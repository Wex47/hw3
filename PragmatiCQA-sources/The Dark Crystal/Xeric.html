A Xeric is one of many small close-knit groups of the Dousan Clan . Xerics are each led by a Sandmaster who has been trained and trusted by the Dousan Maudra herself. Single Xeric are made up of between twelve and thirty Gelfling , and split into crews that pilot their sand skiffs. Larger Xerics often employ the aid of a Crystal Skimmer , sometimes using more than one as the larger number improves their traveling ability, due to the Skimmer’s sharp desert instincts and tireless endurance.

In a Xeric’s council, which is composed of a Sandmaster and two second Sandmasters, all three must agree on any important decisions before action is taken
