Kathryn "Kathy" Mullen (born February 10, 1940) is a puppeteer and actress who worked on many Jim Henson projects. Mullen joined the Muppets in 1978, starting with The Muppet Movie , and later became a full-time puppeteer on The Muppet Show . She advanced to performing major roles on Fraggle Rock , where she played Mokey Fraggle and Cotterpin Doozer, among others.

Mullen puppeteered Kira in The Dark Crystal .

She later worked with The Jim Henson Company on The Wubbulous World of Dr. Seuss , and was one of the creators and performers of the PBS series Between the Lions . In 2004, she and husband Michael Frith worked with No Strings International to create and perform an educational puppet show for children in Afghanistan.
