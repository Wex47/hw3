Juni was a Gelfling member of the Order of Lesser Service who lived during the late Age of Division .

Biography

Juni met Brea in the Order of Lesser Service, where she had been placed after trying to have a Spriton lover, to her parents’ disapproval. She was surprised that Brea's hair was not as shiny as she expected, considering her own hair to be superior, and was shocked that Brea was placed in the service for creating a "diplomatic incident" by wiping Elder Cadia 's memory. She continued to chatter constantly until Brea left, leaving Juni to clean a runaway Podling who refused to wear pants.
