More Tales to Give You Goosebumps
Tales to Give You Goosebumps is the first book in the Goosebumps series of the same name . It was first published in 1994 , and was originally sold with a Goosebumps book light.

Four of the ten short stories in the book were adapted for television. Those stories being " The House of No Return ", " Teacher's Pet ", " Strained Peas ", and " Click ".

Blurb

READER BEWARE—YOU'RE IN FOR TEN SCARES!

From an evil baby sister, to a remote control that can control more than just the television set, to a teacher who's obsessed with snakes, to a cute, cuddly, teddy bear gone bad, here are ten creepy, spooky stores guaranteed to give you Goosebumps all night long!

Featured stories

  • " The House of No Return " - Three children dare a new boy into entering a house where a couple had died upon losing their baby daughter.
  • " Teacher's Pet " - Two friends get a new teacher who has an interest in snakes.
  • " Strained Peas " - A boy receives a baby sister that is not what it seems.
  • " Strangers in the Woods " - While staying with her great-aunt, a girl notices some unusual stuff happening.
  • " Good Friends " - Two friends plan to scare the sister of one of them with a prank.
  • " How I Won My Bat " - A boy gains a magical baseball bat.
  • " Mr. Teddy " - A young girl discovers that her new teddy bear is alive.
  • " Click " - A boy gets a remote control that controls everything around him.
  • " Broken Dolls " - A girl with dolls discover that her young brother is acting strangely.
  • " A Vampire in the Neighborhood " - A group of middle school students think the new girl at school is a vampire.

Trivia

  • The ghost on this book's cover is considered to be an early prototype of what would later become Curly the Skeleton .
  • The book's cover art would later be reused for the 2005 cover for the DVD "Scary House".
