The Ice Vampire is the main antagonist of the third short story from More & More & More Tales to Give You Goosebumps , " The Ice Vampire ".

History

Sam Weaver and Billy Liff compete in an ice sculpting contest, but their ice cobra is unable to win. Instead, the winner is an ice sculpture of a vampire by someone named Bram Stokeman. While looking at the ice sculptures with their friend Michelle Ahlberg, they soon discover that the Ice Vampire is alive and is looking to suck the warmth from them. The kids run to Billy's house for protection.

The next day, Billy's sister Ashley is outside taking down icicles when the Ice Vampire arrives. The Ice Vampire attacks Ashley, draining her heat, before Billy manages to stop it by using an electric hair dryer, melting the monster. The kids breathe a sigh of relief when suddenly their ice cobra shows up, looking for warmth.

General Information

Powers

Much like other vampires, the Ice Vampire drains his victims for sustenance. Only instead of blood, he drains all warmth from their body. Merely touching the Ice Vampire can drain someone's warmth. The Ice Vampire also appears to be almost indestructible, his only weakness being exposed to high levels of heat like that of a hair dryer.

Physical Appearance

The Ice Vampire's design looks very similar to most stock vampire designs with a long cape, fangs, long nose and thin lips. His skin is blue due to being made of ice.

Trivia

  • Bram Stokeman is a reference to Bram Stoker , author of Dracula .
