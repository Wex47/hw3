Lorraine Conyers is Zach Cooper 's Aunt played by Jillian Bell in the film Goosebumps .

History

Conyers is attacked by Fifi the Vampire Poodle . After this, she goes to the grocery store in confusion. She is later frozen by Slappy and The Body Squeezers .

Conyers is also the love interest of R.L. Stine . At the end of the film, she is unfrozen, and she talks with Stine at the high school.
