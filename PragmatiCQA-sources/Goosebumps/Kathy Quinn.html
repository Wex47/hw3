Kathy Quinn is a supporting character in Goosebumps 2: Haunted Halloween . She is the mother of Sarah Quinn and Sonny Quinn . She is played by Wendi McLendon-Covey, who played Miss Stout in The Search for Santa Paws and Nancy Green in Big City Greens .
