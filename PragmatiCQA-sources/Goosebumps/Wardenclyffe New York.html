Description

Wardenclyffe is a small town in New York. The town originated in 1901, by famous electrical engineer, Nikola Tesla, whom often experimented with wireless technology and built the Wardenclyffe Tower.

However, in Goosebumps 2: Haunted Halloween , Slappy used the Wardenclyffe Tower in the wrong way, by creating a new army of Goosebumps monsters as his family.

Locations

The Quinn house

The house where the Quinns (Kathy, Sarah, and Sonny) live.

The Chu house

The house where Mr. Chu lives; it is directly next door to the Quinn house. There was a line of trick-or-treaters (including two witches and a Gothic version of Little Red Riding Hood) that are shown right to Mr. Chu's house where he is passing out candy.

The Stine house

The house where R.L. Stine used to live.

Daniel Webster Middle School

Daniel Webster Middle School is a middle school that Sam, Sonny, and Tommy go to.

Wardenclyffe High School

The unnamed high school where Sarah and Tyler go to. In the novel adaptation, it was named Wardenclyffe High School.
