Night of the Living Monsters is a picture book written by Kate Howard . The book is a tie-in with the 2015 film Goosebumps .

The cover of the book features Zach , R.L. Stine , Hannah , and Champ , and behind them are a Pumpkinhead and two Scarecrows .

Overview

It's similar to the Goosebumps Movie Novel ; however, it's shortened and there are photos from the film inside. Unlike the other movie novels (or any other Goosebumps book), this book is targeted for children ages 7–9. Also, the book is based on the film with the movie's deleted scenes and alternate ending, although the book doesn't mention anything about Hannah being a ghost or her returning in the book.

Author

Kate Howard, the author of this book, is an American children's author. She has primarily written picture books for children, ages 7–9. Some of her most noteworthy works include her short book adaptions of the children's television show LEGO Ninjago: Masters of Spinjitzu .
