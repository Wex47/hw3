Taylor is a supporting character in the Goosebumps film. She is portrayed by Halston Sage .

History

During the attack on the school, when many monsters follow Slappy 's command to raid the school, she and her boyfriend are cornered by the Werewolf of Fever Swamp . This prompts her date to run in fear and cowardice, leaving her behind. As he escapes, she yells to him that he is her boyfriend no longer. As she backs away upon noticing the wolf cornering her, she is then saved by Champ , who drives the beast away by biting it with his silver fillings. Out of gratefulness, she kisses him, much to his surprise. Soon after this Champ becomes her new boyfriend, because of his heroic deeds and winning her heart.
