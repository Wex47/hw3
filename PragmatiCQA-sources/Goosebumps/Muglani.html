History

The Muglani are a tribe of misanthropic witch doctors who reside in the Amazon Rainforest. They often seek to decapitate other humans, later shrinking their heads after that they will put them in their collection of Shrunken Heads , or turn their victims into animals or monsters.

They are seen in three bad endings:

  • The reader and Ben are captured by the Muglani, who plan to decapitate both of them and add their heads to their Shrunken Head collection.
  • The first bad ending has the Muglani turning the reader and Ben into monkeys.
  • The second consists of the reader using a vine to cross a river bank to escape the Muglani but ends up grabbing a boa snake instead which wraps around the reader squeezing the reader to death.

Other appearances

  • A Muglani Witch Doctor also appears in the Goosebumps movie, portrayed by Sheldon Brown (credited as "Monster #3"). He sports the Shrunken Head on his staff.
  • A civilized and well-dressed version of the Muglani appears in Goosebumps HorrorTown .

Trivia

  • The Muglani's attire in Goosebumps HorrorTown of a top hat, coat and skull face paint is a reference to Baron Samedi .
    • In the game, Muglani is the name of one character instead of a whole tribe. He is seen during the How I Got My Shrunken Head questline, taking a similar role to Dr. Richard Hawlings . He was mentioned prior, being mentioned as the person who cursed Amaz-O .
