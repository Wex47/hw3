The Story-Keeper (also called the Listener ) is a character featured in the Goosebumps Hall of Horrors series.

History

The Story-Keeper is a Horror that resides in the Hall of Horrors in HorrorLand . He book-ends the Goosebumps Halls of Horrors series by hearing and collecting the stories told by the main character who visits the Hall of Horrors.

In Claws! , the Story-Keeper hears Mickey Coe 's story. After the story is over, the Story-Keeper informs Mickey that he will receive a gift for visiting the Hall Of Horrors. The gift will come in a carrying case and be named "Shredder".

In Night of the Giant Everything , the Story-Keeper hears Steven Sweeney's story. After the story is over, the Story-Keeper escorts Steven to his own room.
