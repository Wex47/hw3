It Came from New Jersey! is the autobiography of Goosebumps illustrator Tim Jacobus . It was published in 1998 . The book explains how Jacobus created the Goosebumps covers. It also talks about Jacobus' childhood and what he used to draw. The book features many colored Goosebumps drawings and step-by-step guides to how he makes a cover.

The cover art features a pinkish creature painting.
