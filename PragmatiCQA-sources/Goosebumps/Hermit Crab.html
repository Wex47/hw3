The Hermit Crab or simply Hermit Crab , is the main antagonist of the eighth story from More Tales to Give You Goosebumps , " Shell Shocker ".

History

Tara Bennett sees a beautiful seashell on the beach which she proceeds to pick up. She raises the shell to her ear, and she hears a voice begging for help. The voice inside the shell says it's a prisoner, and it begs Tara to take it to a cave on the beach. Tara doesn't know if she should, as a talking shell could be valuable, but the being promises that she will find the biggest shell in the world if they go to the cave.

The voice in the shell lures Tara into the cave. Inside the cave, Tara is attacked by spiders, but she continues on in the hopes of finding the giant shell. Tara falls into a pile of bones, but the voice assures her that the bones belonged to some fish that washed into the cave. Tara hears a thunderous noise, but she pushes forward anyways. Finally, Tara spots the biggest shell in the world. Unfortunately, it belongs to the biggest hermit crab in the world. As the massive hermit crab grabs Tara, the voice in the small shell celebrates at its successfully lured another victim for its mother to feed.

General information

Physical appearance

It takes on the form of any type of crustacean . In this case, it takes on the form of a common hermit crab with a color palette of a lobster and a green algae-ridden shell.

Personality

It uses coaxing and lies to lure unsuspecting victims to feed its mother, making it cunning and deceptive.
