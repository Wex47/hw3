Goosebumps Monster Survival Guide was released as a tie-in to the Goosebumps movie . It features Zach Cooper informing the reader on how to survive an all monster invasion. The book consists of information on all of the Goosebumps monsters featured in the movie (although it excludes seven of the monsters).

The cover has three photos, one of Will Blake , The Giant Praying Mantis , and The Abominable Snowman , the top of the book has three Lawn Gnomes and a pair of strangely green Pumpkin Head hands.
