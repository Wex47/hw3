Craig White

Occupation

Illustrator Graphic designer
Craig White illustrated the covers for books 25 through 42 in the series Give Yourself Goosebumps and books 1 through 8 in the series Give Yourself Goosebumps: Special Edition . In addition to this, White also illustrated the cover to an unpublished forty-third Give Yourself Goosebumps book.

Craig White replaced Mark Nagata as illustrator for the series Give Yourself Goosebumps . Unlike Nagata, White used computers to create digital illustrations.

The first book illustrated by White was Shop Till You Drop...Dead! , and the final book to be illustrated by him was All-Day Nightmare . White also illustrated the cover for a forty-third book for the series, but the cover was never used because of the series' cancellation in the year 2000. The title and plot of this book remain unknown.
