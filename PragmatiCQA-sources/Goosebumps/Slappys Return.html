Goosebumps 2: Haunted Halloween Movie Novel
Slappy's Return is a shortened novelization of Goosebumps 2: Haunted Halloween . It was written by Kate Howard , and was released on August 28, 2018 .

Plot

When Sonny and his best friend Sam find a ventriloquist dummy labeled "Slappy" in an abandoned home, they decide to take him home. What they don't realize is that they've brought him to life and he's evil. Even worse, it's Halloween. Slappy uses his dark magic powers to make Halloween decorations come alive all across town-- and they start to attack! Can Sonny and Sam stop him before it's too late.

Trivia

  • Kate Howard also wrote Night of the Living Monsters , one of the tie in books for the film Goosebumps .
