Wolf Boy is a humanoid werewolf -like creature/ animatronic created by Mr. Wright , and is a minor antagonist that only appears in the thirty-fifth book in the original Goosebumps series , A Shocker on Shock Street .

History

He, alongside Wolf Girl , appear when Erin and Marty are drowning in a quicksand pit. At the last minute, they pull the kids out of the sandpits and are saved. The kids then instantly recognize the two as they are stars from the Shock Street franchise. But the kids slowly begin to realize that these figures are a not fake, like many other monsters they've encountered and make a horrifying discovery that even they're real. The werewolves try to attack, but Erin uses one of the red toy guns and tries to freeze both creatures... But the gun turns out to be useless . The kids climb a wall in order to escape the wolves. After doing this, they spot their tram in the distance. Erin and Marty run to the tram while being chased by the werewolves. Fortunately, the kids get to the tram unharmed. After that, both him and his sister are presumably still roaming around Shock Street.

Personality

He is shown to be programmed to be vicious and ravenous when he and his sister are shown attacking Erin and Marty only after they save the two.

Physical appearance

In the Graphix comic , He is shown to be a humanoid wolf-like or werewolf-like creature with grey, black fur. He is mostly seen without any clothes. In HorrorTown , he is blue and wears in what looks like a schoolboy outfit with a black tie.

Trivia

  • In the original book, he's been described as being much more wolf-like as opposed to HorrorTown where he is now an animatronic figure.
  • His design choice seems to mostly inspired by the from the Five Nights At Freddy's franchise.
  • Both him and Wolf Girl were both excluded from the television adaptation of Shock Street .
