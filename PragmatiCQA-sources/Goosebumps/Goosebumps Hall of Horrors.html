Goosebumps Hall of Horrors is a Goosebumps mini-series consisting of six books written by R.L. Stine . The first two books in the series were originally printed as " Goosebumps HorrorLand: Hall of Horrors ", but assioation with Goosebumps HorrorLand was eventually dropped from the title.

Each book features a story told by a child visiting the titular Hall of Horrors in HorrorLand where their story is collected by a Horror called the Story-Keeper . Unlike the Goosebumps HorrorLand books, this series have no overarching plot. Each book tells an independent story, similarly to the original Goosebumps books.

Trivia

  • This is the third Goosebumps book series to not feature Slappy the Dummy in it. The other series being Tales to Give You Goosebumps and the Goosebumps Triple Header series.
  • The name " Hall of Horrors " is used again for the name of a fun house in the film, R.L. Stine's Monsterville: Cabinet of Souls .
