Sonny Quinn is one of the main protagonists in Goosebumps 2: Haunted Halloween . He is one of the residents of Wardenclyffe, New York , and one of the Junk Bros with his best friend, Sam Carter .

Personality

Sonny is nice and kind. When evil struck his town, he turned into a confident hero.

Physical appearance

Sonny is wide and has short brown hair and dark brown eyes.

Trivia

  • Jeremy Ray Taylor played the character Ben Hanscom in the 2017 horror film, It .
  • During the Trick or Trap! event in Goosebumps HorrorTown , Sonny mentions having multiple older siblings, even though in both the film and game, Sarah is his only sibling.
