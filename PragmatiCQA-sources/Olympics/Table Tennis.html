Table Tennis was first featured during the 1988 Summer Olympics . During the 2008 Summer Olympics , the doubles events were replaced by a team event. In Tokyo 2020 , a mixed event was added. Its governing body International Table Tennis Federation was founded in 1926.

China has been a strong nation from the first edition in 1988, winning 53 out of a possible 100 medals in table tennis, and 28 out of a possible 32 gold medals. The nation with the second-most medals, South Korea has won 18 medals.
