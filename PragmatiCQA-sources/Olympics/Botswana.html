NOC

Botswana first participated at the Olympic Games in 1980. This nation has not participated at the Winter Olympic Games yet.

Botswana has won two medals at the Olympic Games, a silver one won in 2012 and a bronze medal won in 2021.

The Botswana National Olympic Committee was created in 1979 and recognized in 1980.
