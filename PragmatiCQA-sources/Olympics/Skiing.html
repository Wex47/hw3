Skiing is the category name for the skiing at the Olympic Games . It is the biggest category of the Winter Olympic Games , covering six sports and 51 disciplines. These sports are coordinated by the FIS (Fédération Internationale de Ski), which was founded in 1924. This category entails the following six sports:

  • Cross Country Skiing
  • Alpine Skiing
  • Ski Jumping
  • Nordic Combined
  • Freestyle Skiing
  • Snowboarding
