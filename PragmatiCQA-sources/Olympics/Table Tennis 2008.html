Table Tennis at the 2008 Summer Olympics was held from 13 to 23 August 2008 at Beijing University Gymnasium .

Statistics

  • There were 171 total athletes from 56 countries.
  • Marcelo Aguirre was the youngest participant with 15 years and 210 days .
  • Zhiwen He was the oldest participant with 46 years and 80 days .
  • China won 8 out of 8 possible medals.
    • China won 4 out of 4 gold medals.
  • 5 athletes were chosen to be a flag bearer at the opening ceremony.
