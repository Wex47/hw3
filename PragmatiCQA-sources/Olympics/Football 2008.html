Statistics

  • There were 469 total athletes from 21 countries.
  • Lou Jiahui was the youngest participant with 17 years and 72 days .
  • Brian McBride was the oldest participant with 36 years and 49 days .
  • No participants were chosen to be a flag bearer at the opening ceremony.
