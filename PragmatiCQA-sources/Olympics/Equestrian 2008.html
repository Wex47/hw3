Statistics

  • There were 193 athletes from 42 countries.
  • Luiza Almeida was the youngest participant with 16 years and 341 days .
  • Hiroshi Hoketsu was the oldest participant with 67 years and 138 days .
  • Jillian Terceira was chosen to be a flag bearer at the opening ceremony for Bermuda .
