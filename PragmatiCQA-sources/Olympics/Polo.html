Polo has been featured five times at the Olympic Games , with which it is one of two discontinued sports with the most appearances, together with Tug of War . It is also the most recent featured sport, having appeared in Berlin 1936 .

Although Mexico was the first non-mixed team to win a medal in polo, Great Britain won six medals in the following four editions, their worst result being a third place in 1924.
