Speed Skating has been featured at every Winter Olympic Games . It has the highest amount of events in the Winter Olympics, with 14 events. Women have been allowed to compete since the 1960 Summer Olympics . Speed skating is part of the Skating program, together with Figure Skating and Short Track .

The Netherlands is the only country to have won over 100 medals in speed skating, with Norway falling behind with 84 medals, and the United States with 68 medals.
