NOC

Indonesia first participated at the Olympic Games in 1952. This nation has not participated at the Winter Olympic Games yet.

Indonesia has won a total of 37 medals, but they have only won medals in badminton , weightlifting , and archery .

The Indonesian Olympic Committee was created in 1947 and recognized in 1952.
