NOC

Bermuda first participated at the Olympic Games in 1936, and has appeared in every event since 1992.

Bermuda has won two medals at the Olympic Games, which made it the country with the lowest population to ever have won a medal, and Flora Duffy became the first Bermudian athlete to win a gold medal when she won the women's triathlon in 2021.

The Bermuda Olympic Association was created in 1935 and recognized in 1936.
