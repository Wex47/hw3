Bohemia was a team consisting of athletes from Czechoslovakia and later Czech Republic , which first participated at the Olympic Games of 1900. Their last appearance was at the Olympic Games of 1912.

In the two editions of the Olympic Games, Bohemia won 4 medals, of which none gold. Bohemia has not attended any Winter Olympic Games , as the first edition was in 1924, when Bohemia was dissolved into Czechoslovakia and later Czech Republic and Slovakia .
