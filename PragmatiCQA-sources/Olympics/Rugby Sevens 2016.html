Statistics

  • There were 288 total athletes from 14 countries.
  • Jiowana Sauto was the youngest participant with 18 years and 146 days .
  • Julien Candelon was the oldest participant with 36 years and 32 days .
  • Osea Kolinisau was the only participant to be chosen to be a flag bearer at the opening ceremony.
  • Fiji won their first ever medal.
