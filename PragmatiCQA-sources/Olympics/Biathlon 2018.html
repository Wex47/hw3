Biathlon at the 2018 Winter Olympics was held between 7 and 23 February 2018 at Alpensia Biathlon Centre .

Statistics

  • There were 219 total athletes from 28 countries.
  • 5 athletes were chosen to be a flag bearer at the opening ceremony.
  • Germany won 7 medals, more than in any other sport.
  • France won 5 medals, more than in any other sport.
  • Belarus won 2 medals, more than in any other sport.
  • Slovakia won their only 3 medals of Pyeongchang 2018 .
