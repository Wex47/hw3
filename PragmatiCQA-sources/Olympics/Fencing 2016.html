Statistics

  • There were 245 athletes from 47 countries.
  • Mohamed Hamza was the youngest participant with 15 years and 335 days .
  • Géza Imre was the oldest participant with 41 years and 234 days .
  • 6 participants were chosen to be a flag bearer at the opening ceremony.
  • Russia won 5 gold medals, more than any nation since the 1920 Summer Olympics .
  • Tunisia won their first ever medal in Fencing.
  • Germany failed to win a medal for the first time since 1980 .
