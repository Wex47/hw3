Cuba first participated at the Olympic Games in 1900. This nation has never competed in the Winter Olympic Games , and is the most successful nation of the countries that have never competed at the Winter Olympics.

Cuba has won over 200 medals at the Summer Olympic Games , of which 150 in Boxing , Athletics and Judo .

The Cuban Olympic Committee was created in 1926 and recognized in 1955.
