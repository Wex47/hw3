Czechoslovakia was a team consisting of athletes from Czech Republic and Slovakia , which first participated at the Olympic Games of 1920, although athletes competing for Czechoslovakia might have appeared before under Bohemia . Their last appearance was at the Olympic Games of 1992.

Czechoslovakia has won 168 medals in the Olympic Games, of which 75 in Gymnastics , Athletics and Wrestling .
