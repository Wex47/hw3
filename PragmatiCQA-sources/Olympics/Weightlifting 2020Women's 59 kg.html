The women's 59 kg of Weightlifting 2020 during the 2020 Summer Olympics was held on 27 July 2021 in the Tokyo International Forum . With 14 athletes competing, the event was won by Kuo Hsing-chun from Chinese Taipei , with Polina Guryeva from Turkmenistan taking the silver medal and Mikiko Andoh from Japan taking bronze.

Format

The competition exists out of two rounds in which athletes try to lift as much weight as possible. In the snatch round, this lift needs to be done in one continuous motion, while the competitors are allowed to rest the barbell on the chest in the clean and jerk. The athlete with the highest combined weight lifted wins the event.
