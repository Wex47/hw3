Trivia

  • There were 289 total athletes from 61 countries.
  • Zaineb Sghaier was the youngest participant with 18 years and 311 days .
  • Burmaa Ochirbat was the oldest participant with 39 years and 66 days .
  • 5 athletes were chosen to be flag bearers at the opening ceremony.
