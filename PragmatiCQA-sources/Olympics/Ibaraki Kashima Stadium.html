Ibaraki Kashima Stadium (茨城カシマスタジアム) is a stadium in Kashima City, Ibaraki Prefecture, Japan, used by the Japanese football team the Kashima Antlers. The venue was one of the stadiums selected to host the football competitions at the 2020 Summer Olympics . The stadium was opened in 1993, and was also one of the stadiums hosting the football World Cup in 2002. The stadium has a capacity of 40,000.
