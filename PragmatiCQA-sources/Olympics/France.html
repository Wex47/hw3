NOC

France first participated at the Olympic Games in 1896. The nation also organized the very first Winter Olympic Games in 1924.

France has won over 800 medals at the Summer Olympic Games and the Winter Olympic Games, with most medals in Fencing , Cycling , and Athletics .

The French Olympic Committee was created and recognized in 1894, with as its first president Baron Pierre de Coubertin .
