Trivia

  • There were 350 total athletes from 65 countries.
  • The twins Eya Guezguez and Sarra Guezguez were the youngest participants with 16 years and 138 days .
  • Santiago Raul Lange was the oldest participant with 59 years and 310 days .
  • 12 athletes were chosen to be flag bearers at the opening ceremony.
