Fencing is a sport that has been featured non-stop since the 1896 Summer Olympics . It's governed by the Fédération Internationale d'Escrime, which was founded in 1913. Since Athens 2004 , the three team events have been rotating, so that two out of three are featured in an edition of the Olympic Games .
