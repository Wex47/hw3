Caeleb Dressel is a swimmer who has competed for the United States. He competed at the 2016 and 2020 Summer Olympic Games. He won five gold medals at the 2020 Summer Olympic Games, making him the most successful athlete of Tokyo 2020 in terms of gold medals.
