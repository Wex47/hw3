Trivia

  • There were 197 total athletes from 77 countries.
  • Nancy Genzel Abouke was the youngest participant with 18 years and 27 days .
  • Laurel Hubbard was the oldest participant with 43 years and 175 days .
  • 14 athletes were chosen to be flag bearers at the opening ceremony.
  • Turkmenistan won its first-ever Olympic medal.
