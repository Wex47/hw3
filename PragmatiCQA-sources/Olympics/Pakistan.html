National Olympic Committee of Pakistan
Pakistan first competed at the Olympic Games in London 1948 and has competed at every Summer Olympics ever since (with the exception of Moscow 1980 due to the boycott). The country competed in their first Winter Olympics at Vancouver 2010 with Alpine Skiing.

The country has won 10 medals , 3 gold, 3 silver, and 3 bronze. Since Barcelona 1992 , Pakistan has not won a single medal.

The National Olympic Committee for the country was recognized in 1948.
