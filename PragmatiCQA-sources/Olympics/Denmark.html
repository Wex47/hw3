NOC

Denmark first participated at the Olympic Games in 1896, and in every summer edition since 1908. This nation has competed in Winter Olympic Games since 1948.

Denmark has not won a total of 1 medal at the Winter Olympic Games, won in 1998, but they have won 205 medals at the Summer Olympic Games . They have won the most medals in sailing, cycling and rowing, gathering a total of 80 medals in those three sports.

The Danmarks Idrætsforbund was created (and recognized) in 1905.
