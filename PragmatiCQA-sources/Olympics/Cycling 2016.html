Statistics

  • There were 513 total athletes from 79 countries.
  • Frederik Madsen was the youngest participant with 18 years and 203 days .
  • Sabine Spitz was the oldest participant with 44 years and 239 days .
  • 6 participants were chosen to be a flag bearer at the opening ceremony.
  • Great Britain won 12 medals, more than in any other sport.
  • Netherlands won 6 medals, more than in any other sport.
  • Denmark won 3 medals, more than in any other sport.
  • Belgium and Switzerland won 2 medals, more than in any other sport.
