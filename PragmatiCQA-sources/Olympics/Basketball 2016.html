Statistics

  • There were 280 total athletes from 17 countries.
  • Zou Yuchen was the youngest participant with 20 years and 32 days .
  • Manu Ginobili was the oldest participant with 39 years and 9 days .
  • Luis Scola was chosen to be a flag bearer at the opening ceremony.
  • Only 3 nations won a medal in Basketball, the lowest amount possible.
  • The United States won 2 out of 2 gold medals.
