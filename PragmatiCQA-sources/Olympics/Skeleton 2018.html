Statistics

  • There were 50 total athletes from 24 countries.
  • Vladyslav Heraskevych was the youngest participant with 19 years and 34 days .
  • Dave Greszczyszyn was the oldest participant with 38 years and 150 days .
  • Akwasi Frimpong and Lizzy Yarnold were chosen to be a flag bearer at the opening ceremony.
  • Great Britain won 3 medals, more than in any other sport.
  • 7 nations appeared for the first time in Skeleton .
