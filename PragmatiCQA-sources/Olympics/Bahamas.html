Bahamas first participated at the Olympic Games in 1952. This nation has not participated at the Winter Olympic Games yet.

Bahamas has won a total of 16 medals, but they have only won medals in athletics and sailing.

The Bahamas Olympic Committee was created and recognized in 1952.
