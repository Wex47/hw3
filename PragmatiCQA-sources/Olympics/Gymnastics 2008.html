Gymnastics at the 2008 Summer Olympics consisted of artistic, rhythmic and trampoline disciplines. Events were held from 9 to 24 August 2008 at the National Indoor Stadium and the Beijing University of Technology Gymnasium .

Statistics

  • There were 323 total athletes from 40 countries.
  • Jo Hyun-joo was the youngest participant with 15 years and 240 days .
  • Yordan Yovchev was the oldest participant with 35 years and 166 days .
  • Albert Azaryan and Wania Monteiro were chosen to be flag bearers at the opening ceremony for Armenia and Cape Verde .
  • China won 18 medals, more than in any other sport.
