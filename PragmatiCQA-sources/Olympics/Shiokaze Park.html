Shiokaze Park (潮風公園) is a park on the beaches of Tokyo. The park is located near the Odaiba Marine Park and has view of the iconic Rainbow Bridge. The park itself opened in 1974 as a natural recreation area in Tokyo. Within the park, a temporary venue was constructed during the 2020 Summer Olympics to host the beach volleyball events. The temporary venue has a capacity of 12,000 during the event.
