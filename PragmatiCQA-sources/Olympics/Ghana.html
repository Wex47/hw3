Ghana Olympic Committee

Ghana first participated at the Olympic Games in 1952. It has also competed in several editions of the Winter Olympic Games , the first time being Vancouver 2010 .

Ghana has won a total of 5 medals, of which four in boxing and one in football .

The Ghana Olympic Committee was created and recognized in 1951.
