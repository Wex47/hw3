Skeleton has been featured at the Olympic Games since the 1924 Winter Olympics , and aside from being featured at St. Moritz 1948 , it did not appear again until Salt Lake City 2002 . Women have been allowed to compete since Salt Lake City 2002 .

Great Britain is the only country to have won a medal at every appearance of skeleton at the Winter Olympic Games , but only has won a single medal more than the United States , with the same number of gold medals. Together, they have won 17 out of 36 medals.
