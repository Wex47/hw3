NOC

Canada first participated at the Olympic Games in 1900. This nation has competed in Winter Olympic Games since 1924, and has only missed the 1980 summer edition, due to a boycott.

Canada has won over 500 medals at the Summer Olympic Games and the Winter Olympic Games, and it has won a medal in every edition it has competed in.

The Canadian Olympic Committee was created in 1904 and recognized in 1907.
