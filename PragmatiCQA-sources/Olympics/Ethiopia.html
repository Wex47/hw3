Ethiopian Olympic Committee

Ethiopia first participated at the Olympic Games in 1956. This nation has participated twice at the Winter Olympic Games .

Ethiopia has won a total of 58 medals, but only in athletics at the Summer Olympic Games .

The Ethiopian Olympic Committee was created in 1948 and recognized in 1954.
