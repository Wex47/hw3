NOC

Italy first participated at the Olympic Games in 1896, and only missed St. Louis 1904 since then. Since London 1908 , the nation has won an Olympic medal in every edition of the Summer Olympic Games , and has won a medal in every Winter Olympic Games since St. Moritz 1948 .

Italy has won over 700 medals at the Olympic Games, with over 100 medals in fencing , and also over 50 medals in athletics and cycling . Other sports with Italian success have been boxing , shooting , rowing , and cross-country skiing .

The Italian National Olympic Committee was created in 1908 and recognized in 1915.
