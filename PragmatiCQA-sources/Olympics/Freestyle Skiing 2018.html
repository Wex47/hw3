Freestyle Skiing at the 2018 Winter Olympics was held between 9 and 23 February 2018 at the Phoenix Snow Park .

Statistics

  • There were 269 total athletes from 27 countries.
  • Wu Meng was the youngest participant with 15 years and 140 days .
  • Alla Tsuper was the oldest participant with 38 years and 305 days .
  • Alla Tsuper and Beau-James Wells were chosen to be a flag bearer at the opening ceremony.
  • Great Britain , Kazakhstan , New Zealand , and Ukraine won their first ever medals in freestyle skiing .
  • Canada won 7 medals, more than in any other sport.
