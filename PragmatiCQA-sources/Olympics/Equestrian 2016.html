Statistics

  • There were 200 athletes from 43 countries.
  • Giovana Pass was the youngest participant with 18 years and 135 days .
  • Julie Brougham was the oldest participant with 62 years and 84 days .
  • 4 participants were chosen to be a flag bearer at the opening ceremony.
  • Germany won 6 gold medals, more than any nation.
  • Netherlands failed to win a medal, for the first time since 1988 .
