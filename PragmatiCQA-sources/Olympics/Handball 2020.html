Trivia

  • There were 368 total athletes from 18 countries.
  • Stelvia Pascoal was the youngest participant with 18 years and 278 days .
  • Humberto Gomes was the oldest participant with 43 years and 205 days .
  • 3 athletes were chosen to be flag bearers at the opening ceremony.
