The men's tournament of Baseball 2020 during the 2020 Summer Olympics was held on 28 July-7 August 2021 in the Fukushima Azuma Baseball Stadium and Yokohama Baseball Stadium . With 6 teams competing, the event was won by the team from Japan , with United States taking the silver medal and Dominican Republic taking bronze.

Format

The competition exists out of two different phases. The first phase is a group of three, in which teams play each other once. The two winners of the groups get a bye to the quarterfinals, while the numbers two and three play each other in a preliminary round. In every round, the winners continue towards the final, while the losers of a match continue in a losers bracket. Once there is only one team remaining in the winners bracket, they play the winner of the losers bracket for the gold medal.

Preview

Six teams qualified for the event for its return after thirteen years with the home nation Japan as the big favorite for the title, though all teams could contend for a medal. South Korea was the world number three and the last Olympic champion in the event, when the United States won bronze. Mexico and the Dominican Republic were also in the top 10 of the world rankings, with Israel being the European representation.
