Statistics

  • There were 2268 total athletes from 200 countries.
  • Jacob Kiplimo was the youngest participant with 15 years and 278 days .
  • Jesús Ángel García was the oldest participant with 46 years and 308 days .
  • 56 participants were chosen to be a flag bearer at the opening ceremony, the highest number out of all sports.
  • 42 nations won a medal in Athletics , more than any other sport.
  • South Africa won 4 medals, more than in any other sport.
  • Croatia and Poland won 3 medals, more than in any other sport.
  • Kenya won their only 13 medals of this edition of the Olympic Games.
  • Jamaica won their only 11 medals of this edition of the Olympic Games.
  • Ethiopia won their only 8 medals of this edition of the Olympic Games.
  • Algeria , Bahrain and the Bahamas won their only 2 medals of this edition of the Olympic Games.
  • Burundi , Grenada , Qatar , Tajikistan and Trinidad and Tobago won their only medal of the 2016 Summer Olympics.
