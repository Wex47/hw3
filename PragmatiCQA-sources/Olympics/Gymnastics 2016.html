Gymnastics at the 2016 Summer Olympics consisted of artistic, rhythmic and trampoline disciplines. Events were held from 6 to 21 August 2016 at Arena Olímpica do Rio .

Statistics

  • There were 324 total athletes from 60 countries.
  • Senna Deriks was the youngest participant with 15 years and 221 days .
  • Oksana Chusovitina was the oldest participant with 41 years and 50 days , the oldest female gymnast in Olympic history.
  • 5 participants were chosen to be a flag bearer at the opening ceremony.
  • Russia won 11 medals, more than in any other sport.
  • Ukraine won 3 medals, more than in any other sport.
