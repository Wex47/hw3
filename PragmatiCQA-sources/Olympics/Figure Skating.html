Figure Skating was first featured during the 1908 Summer Olympics , and it is one of two sports to have been featured in both the Winter Olympic Games and the Summer Olympic Games . Nowadays, the figure skating is part of the Skating program, together with Speed Skating and Short Track .

The United States and Russia have proven to be competitive in figure skating, the United States winning 51 medals, of which 15 gold, and Russia, with its predecessor Soviet Union , winning 54 medals, of which 26 gold.
