NOC

Egypt first participated at the Olympic Games in 1912, and it has only missed two editions since then. This nation has competed in Winter Olympic Games in Sarajevo 1984 .

Egypt has won a total of 38 medals at the Summer Olympic Games , winning the most medals in Weightlifting and Wrestling , the only sports the country has won a gold medal in.

The Egyptian Olympic Committee was created (and recognized) in 1910.
