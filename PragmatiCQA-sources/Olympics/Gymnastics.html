Gymnastics is the category name for the gymnastic events at the Olympic Games . These sports are coordinated by the FIG (Fédération Internationale de Gymnastique), which was founded in 1881. This category entails the following three disciplines:

  • Artistic Gymnastics
  • Rhythmic Gymnastics
  • Trampoline
