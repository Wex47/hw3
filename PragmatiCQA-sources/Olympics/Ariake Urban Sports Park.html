Ariake Urban Sports Park (有明アーバンスポーツパーク) is a venue in the Ariake district in Tokyo, located in the Tokyo Bay Zone. It was opened in October 2019, after being constructed specifically for the 2020 Summer Olympics to host the BMX and inaugural skateboarding competitions. After the Olympic Games, it is intended to be used as one of the main centres for urban and developing sports in Ariake, Tokyo. During Olympic competition, the venue has a venue of 5,000 for the BMX racing disciplines, 6,600 for the BMX freestyle disciplines, and 7,000 for the skateboarding events.
