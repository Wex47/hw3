Statistics

  • There were 258 total athletes from 14 countries.
  • Paula Leitón was the youngest participant with 16 years and 104 days .
  • Camila Pedrosa was the oldest participant with 41 years and 150 days .
  • Josip Pavić was the only participant to be chosen to be a flag bearer at the opening ceremony.
  • Italy won 2 medals, more than any other nation.
