NOC

Argentina first participated at the Olympic Games in 1900. This nation has competed in Winter Olympic Games since 1928, and in every winter edition since 1960.

Argentina has not yet won any medals at the Winter Olympic Games, but they have won over 70 medals at the Summer Olympic Games . They have won the most medals in boxing, more than any other country.

Argentina was one of the countries who founded the IOC, but the Comité Olímpico Argentino was only created (and recognized) in 1923.
