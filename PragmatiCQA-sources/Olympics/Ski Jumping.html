Ski Jumping has been featured since the 1924 Winter Olympics . It is part of the Skiing program. Women have been allowed to compete since Sochi 2014 .

Finland , Austria , and Norway have won 77 out of a total 135 medals in ski jumping, with Norway winning the most medals and Finland winning the most gold medals.
