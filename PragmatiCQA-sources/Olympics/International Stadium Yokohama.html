International Stadium Yokahama (横浜国際総合競技場) is the second-largest multi-purpose sports stadium in Japan, located in Yokohama City, Kanagawa Prefecture, Japan. The stadium hosts the Yokohama F. Marinos. The venue was one of the stadiums selected to host the football competitions at the 2020 Summer Olympics . The stadium was opened in 1998, and hosted the final of the football World Cup in 2002, as well as several matches at the football Club World Cup in 2005 until 2016. The stadium has a capacity of 72,000.
