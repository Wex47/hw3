Statistics

  • There were 234 athletes from 45 countries.
  • Anissa Khelfaoui was the youngest participant with 16 years and 348 days .
  • Jujie Luan was the oldest participant with 50 years and 28 days .
  • Jesika Jimenez and Alexandr Romankov were chosen to be flag bearers for Panama and Belarus at the opening ceremony.
  • Italy won 7 medals , more than in any other sport.
  • China won their first gold medal in fencing .
