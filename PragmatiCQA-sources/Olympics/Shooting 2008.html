Shooting at the 2008 Summer Olympics was held from 9 to 17 August 2008 at the Beijing Shooting Range Hall and the Beijing Shooting Range Clay Target Field .

Statistics

  • There were 390 total athletes from 103 countries.
  • Thanyalak Chotphibunsin was the youngest participant with 17 years and 263 days .
  • Afanasijs Kuzmins was the oldest participant with 61 years and 146 days .
  • 10 participants were chosen to be a flag bearer at the opening ceremony.
  • Kuwait won their only gold medal at the edition of the Olympic Games.
