NOC

Brazil first participated at the Olympic Games in 1920. This nation has competed in every Winter Olympic Games since 1992.

Brazil has won over 100 medals at the Summer Olympic Games , but they have yet to win a medal at the Winter Olympic Games.

The Brazilian Olympic Committee was created in 1914 and recognized in 1935.
