Gymnastics at the 2020 Summer Olympics consisted of artistic, rhythmic and trampoline disciplines. Events were held from 24 July to 8 August at the Ariake Gymnastics Centre .

Trivia

  • There were 324 total athletes from 64 countries.
  • Jutta Verkest was the youngest participant with 15 years and 287 days .
  • Oksana Chusovitina was the oldest participant with 46 years and 35 days .
  • 3 athletes were chosen to be flag bearers at the opening ceremony.
