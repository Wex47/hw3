Volleyball at the 2008 Summer Olympics was held from 9 to 24 August 2008 at the Capital Indoor Stadium and Beijing Institute of Technology Gymnasium (indoor) and Chaoyang Park Beach Volleyball Ground (beach).

Statistics

  • There were 379 total athletes from 25 countries.
  • Roslandy Acosta was the youngest participant with 16 years and 166 days .
  • Ivan Tasev was the oldest participant with 41 years and 144 days .
  • None of the participating athletes were chosen to be a flag bearer at the opening ceremony.
  • United States won a medal in all 4 events .
