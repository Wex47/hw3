The 1984 Winter Olympics (officially the XIV Olympic Winter Games and also known as Sarajevo 1984 ) were an international winter multi-sport event held between 8 and 19 February 1984 in Sarajevo, Bosnia-Herzegovina, Yugoslavia. These were the first Winter Games and the second Olympics to be held in a Communist state (the first being the 1980 Summer Olympics in Moscow).
