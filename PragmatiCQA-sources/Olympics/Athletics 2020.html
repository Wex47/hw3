Trivia

  • There were 2038 total athletes from 177 countries.
  • Erriyon Knighton was the youngest participant with 17 years and 187 days .
  • Jesus Angel Garcia was the oldest participant with 51 years and 292 days .
  • 69 athletes were chosen to be flag bearers at the opening ceremony.
  • Burkina Faso won its first-ever Olympic medal.
