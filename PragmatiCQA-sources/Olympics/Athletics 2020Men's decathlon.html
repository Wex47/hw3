Format

The competition exists out of ten disciplines of modern track and field athletics. Athletes have to compete in several events, including the 100 metres sprint, the high jump, and the javelin throw. Points are given out based on the individual performance of an athlete, relative to the year's best result. The competitor with the most points after ten disciplines is the winner of the event.

Preview

Twenty-three athletes qualified for the event with defending bronze medalist Damian Warner as one of the favorites for the title, but the entire top 7 of the world rankings was featured in the competition. Niklas Kaul and Maicel Uibo as two of the big challengers, just like Pierce Lepage , Ilya Shkurenyov , defending silver medalist Kevin Mayer , and Kai Kazmirek . Other potential outsiders and medal contenders also included Thomas van der Plaetsen , Lindon Victor , and Zachery Ziemek .
