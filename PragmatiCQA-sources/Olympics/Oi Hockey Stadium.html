Oi Hockey Stadium (大井ホッケー競技場) is a new venue in the Oi Pier Ocean Park in Tokyo, located in the Tokyo Bay Zone. It was opened at the end of 2019, after being constructed for the 2020 Summer Olympics to host the hockey competitions. The venue has two pitches, with the north pitch hosting group stage and knockout matches throughout the tournament, and the south pitch only hosting group stage matches. After the Olympic Games, it is intended to be used as a multi-purpose sports facility in the pier region and park. The venue itself has a venue of 15,000 during the Olympic Games.
