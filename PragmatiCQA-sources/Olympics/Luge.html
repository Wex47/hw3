Luge has been featured at the Olympic Games since the 1964 Winter Olympics . The program did not change, until Sochi 2014 , when a team relay was added. The doubles event is officially noted as a mixed event, although only men have participated in it.

Germany and its predecessors have won a total of 81 out of a possible 141 medals in luge. Other than that, only Austria and Italy have won more than ten medals in Olympic luge.
