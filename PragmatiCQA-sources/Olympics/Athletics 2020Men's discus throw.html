The men's discus throw of Athletics 2020 during the 2020 Summer Olympics was held on 30-31 July 2021 in the Tokyo Olympic Stadium . With 32 athletes competing, the event was won by Daniel Stahl from Sweden , with Simon Pettersson from Sweden taking the silver medal and Lukas Weisshaidinger from Austria taking bronze.

Format

The competition exists out of two separate rounds. In the qualification, each competitor gets three attempts to throw a discus as far as possible. After three attempts, the twelve best performance advance to the final, including athletes tied for twelfth place. In the final, the qualified athletes once again get three attempts to reach as far as possible, but contrary to the qualification, the eight best-placed athletes get another three attempts. The athlete with the farthest throw wins the event.

Preview

Thirty-two athletes qualified for the event with reigning world champion Daniel Stahl as one of the big favorites for gold, but other big names also included defending silver medalist Piotr Malachowski , silver medalist at the world championships Fedrick Dacres , bronze medalist at the most recent world championships Lukas Weisshaidinger , and reigning European champion and 2017 world champion Andrius Gudzius . Potential outsiders and medal contenders also included Kristjan Ceh , Simon Pettersson , Reggie Jagers III , and Alex Rose .
