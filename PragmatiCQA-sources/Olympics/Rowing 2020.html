Trivia

  • There were 206 total athletes from 80 countries.
  • Mutiara Putri was the youngest participant with 17 years and 16 days .
  • Olaf Karl Tufte was the oldest participant with 45 years and 88 days .
  • 16 athletes were chosen to be flag bearers at the opening ceremony.
