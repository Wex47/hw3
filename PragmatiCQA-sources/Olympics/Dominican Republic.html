NOC

Dominican Republic first participated at the Olympic Games in 1964. This nation has never participated at the Winter Olympic Games .

Costa Rica has won 12 medals at the Summer Olympic Games , in three different sports. Up to the 2016 Summer Olympics, only men have won medals for the nation.

The Comité Olímpico Dominicano was created in 1953 and recognized in 1954.
