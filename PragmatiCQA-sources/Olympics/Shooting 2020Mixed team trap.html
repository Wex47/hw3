The mixed team trap of Shooting 2020 during the 2020 Summer Olympics is planned to be held on 31 July 2021 in the Asaka Shooting Range . 16 pairs are planned to compete in the event.

Format

The competition exists out of two separate rounds. In the qualification, the shooters try to reach a score as high as possible, with the top two teams of the second qualification round shooting against each other in the final, and with the third- and fourth-placed teams competing in the bronze medal match. The winner of the final wins the event.

Preview

Sixteen pairs qualified for the event with plenty of medal contenders. Australia were the reigning world championships, but ROC took silver at the previous two editions of the world championships. In 2018, they were defeated by Slovakia , with Italy just finishing next to the podium in both editions. The United States , Great Britain , and Spain belonged to the potential medal candidates who could win the inaugural medals in the event.
