Archery at the 2020 Summer Olympics will be held between 23 and 31 July 2021 at the Yumenoshima Park Archery Field . In comparison to the previous edition, a mixed team event was added, to promote gender equality and the growing dynamics in the sport.

Trivia

  • There were 128 total athletes from 51 countries.
  • Kim Je-deok was the youngest participant with 17 years and 100 days .
  • Slawomir Naploszek was the oldest participant with 52 years and 359 days .
  • 5 athletes were chosen to be flag bearers at the opening ceremony.
  • Turkey won its first-ever medal in the sport.
