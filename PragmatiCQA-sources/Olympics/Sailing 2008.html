Statistics

  • There were 400 total athletes from 62 countries.
  • Peter Burling was the youngest participant with 17 years and 222 days .
  • John Dane was the oldest participant with 58 years and 31 days .
  • 6 participants were chosen to be a flag bearer at the opening ceremony.
  • Israel won their only medal in the edition of the Olympic Games.
