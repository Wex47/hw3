Tennis at the 2008 Summer Olympics was held between 10 and 17 August 2008 at the Olympic Green Tennis Centre .

Statistics

  • There were 169 total athletes from 48 countries.
  • Caroline Wozniacki was the youngest participant with 18 years and 30 days .
  • Pavel Vizner was the oldest participant with 38 years and 27 days .
  • Roger Federer and Fernando Gonzalez were chosen to be flag bearers at the opening ceremony.
