Triathlon at the 2020 Summer Olympics was held on 26 and 31 July 2021 at the Odaiba Marine Park .

Trivia

  • There were 110 total athletes from 38 countries.
  • Oscar Coggins was the youngest participant with 21 years and 293 days .
  • Gillian Sanders was the oldest participant with 39 years and 284 days .
  • No athletes were chosen to be flag bearers at the opening ceremony.
