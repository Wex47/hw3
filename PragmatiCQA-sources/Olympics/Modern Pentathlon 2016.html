Statistics

  • There were 72 total athletes from 28 countries.
  • Haydy Adil was the youngest participant with 16 years and 333 days .
  • Donna Vakalis was the oldest participant with 36 years and 232 days .
  • Yane Marques was the only participant to be chosen to be a flag bearer at the opening ceremony.
  • 6 nations won a medal, with all six countries winning 1 medal.
