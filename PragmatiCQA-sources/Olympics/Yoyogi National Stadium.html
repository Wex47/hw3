Yoyogi National Gymnasium (国立代々木競技場)was built from 1961 to 1964 to be used as the venue for the diving , swimming , and basketball competitions of Tokyo 1964 . Since then, the venue has been used for mainly several basketball and volleyball competitions, as well as many pop concerts, but the arena is also famous for it's unusual roof and design. During Tokyo 2020 , the venue hosts the handball competitions, and while the stadium has a regular maximum capacity of 13,291, this was brought down to 10,200 during the Olympic competition.
