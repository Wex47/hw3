Statistics

  • There were 386 total athletes from 92 countries.
  • Ali Khousrof was the youngest participant with 16 years and 157 days .
  • Jose Mba Nchama was the oldest participant with 42 years and 299 days .
  • 17 participants were chosen to be a flag bearer at the opening ceremony.
  • Japan won 7 medals , more than in any other sport.
  • The Netherlands won 5 medals , more than in any other sport.
  • North Korea won 3 medals , more than in any other sport.
  • Algeria won its only medals of Beijing 2008 .
  • The United States won its first-ever medal in the sport.
