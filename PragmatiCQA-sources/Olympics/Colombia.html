NOC

Colombia first participated at the Olympic Games in 1932. This nation has competed at the Winter Olympic Games twice, since 2010.

Colombia has won 33 medals at the Summer Olympic Games , but they have yet to win a medal at the Winter Olympic Games.

The Colombian Olympic Committee was created in 1936 and recognized in 1948.
