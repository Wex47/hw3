Trivia

  • There were 120 total athletes from 42 countries.
  • Pia Babnik was the youngest participant with 17 years and 209 days .
  • Rory Sabbatini was the oldest participant with 45 years and 119 days .
  • Gabriela Lopez and Fabrizio Zanotti were chosen to be flag bearers at the opening ceremony for Mexico and Paraguay , respectively.
