Boxing has been featured at the Olympic Games since the 1904 Summer Olympics , although it was banned during Stockholm 1912 . It's governed by the Association Internationale de Boxe Amateur, which was founded in 1946. Women have only been allowed to compete since London 2012 , which makes it the most recent sport that allows women to compete.
