Triathlon was introduced during the 2000 Summer Olympics . The triathlon consists out of a modified version of:

  • Marathon Swimming
  • Cycling
  • Athletics

Great Britain and Switzerland have won the same amount of medals (two gold, one silver, two bronze), although Great Britain has won four medals in the last two events. Australia has also won five medals, winning one gold, two silver, and two bronze.
