Kasai Canoe Slalom Centre (カヌー・スラロームセンター) is a new venue in the Tokyo Bay Zone as the first man-made slalom course in Japan. It was constructed next to the Kasai Rinkai Park to host the canoe slalom events during the 2020 Summer Olympics . After the Olympic Games, it was intended to facilitate a range of water sports in the Kasai region in Tokyo, including leisure activities and sports events. As the first man-made course in Japan, the sport has become more open and closer to the public in Japan. The course itself is 200 metres in length, but the venue also includes a warming-up course of 180 metres. The capacity of the venue during the Olympic Games was 7,500.
