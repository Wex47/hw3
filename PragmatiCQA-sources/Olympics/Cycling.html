Cycling is the category name for the cycling at the Olympic Games . These sports are coordinated by the UCI (Union Cycliste Internationale), which was founded in 1900. This category entails the following five sports:

  • BMX
  • Mountain Biking
  • Road Cycling
  • Track Cycling
