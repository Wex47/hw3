Makuhari Messe Hall (幕張メッセ A・Bホール) is a convention centre in Chiba, Japan, as part of the Tokyo Bay Zone. The venue has three main halls, spamming over 210,000 square metres, mainly used for international exhibits and conferences. The three halls were used for Olympic and Paralympic competition during the 2020 Summer Olympics , with hall A hosting wrestling and taekwondo competitions, hall B hosting Olympic fencing , and hall C hosting the Paralympic goalball. The three halls had a capacity of 10,000, 8,000, and 5,500, respectively.
