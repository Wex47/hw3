Comité National Olympique de Côte d'Ivoire

Côte d'Ivoire , also known as Ivory Coast , first participated at the Olympic Games in 1964. This nation has never participated at the Winter Olympic Games .

Côte d'Ivoire has won 4 medals at the Olympic Games, of which 3 in Taekwondo , after having won a silver medal in the 400 metres in 1984.

The Olympic Committee of Ivory Coast was created and recognized in 1963.
