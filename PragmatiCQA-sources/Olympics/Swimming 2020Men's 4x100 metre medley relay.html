The men's 4x100 metre medley relay of Swimming 2020 during the 2020 Summer Olympics was held on 30 July-1 August 2021 in the Tokyo Aquatics Centre . With 16 teams competing, the event was won by the team from United States , with Great Britain taking the silver medal and Italy taking bronze.

Format

This competition exists out of two rounds, each with eight teams. The eight teams with the fastest times in either semifinal advance to the final. The fastest team in the final is the winner of the event.

Preview

Sixteen teams qualified for the event with defending Olympic champions the United States and reigning world champions Great Britain as the two main medal contenders, though many other teams were also close. ROC won bronze at the world championships and silver at the European championships, ahead of a strong Italy , with host team Japan , defending bronze medalists Australia , and Brazil also having recorded fast times throughout the seasons. Other potential outsiders also included China and France .
