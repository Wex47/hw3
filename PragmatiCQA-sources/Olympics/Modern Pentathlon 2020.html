Modern Pentathlon at the 2020 Summer Olympics was held from 5 to 7 August 2021 at the Musashino Forest Sport Plaza and the Tokyo Stadium .

Trivia

  • There were 72 total athletes from 31 countries.
  • Amira Kandil was the youngest participant with 18 years and 139 days .
  • Laura Asadauskaite was the oldest participant with 37 years and 160 days .
  • No athletes were chosen to be flag bearers at the opening ceremony.
