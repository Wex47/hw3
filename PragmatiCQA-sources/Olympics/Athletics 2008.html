Statistics

  • There were 2056 total athletes from 200 countries.
  • Anastasiya Svechnikova was the youngest participant with 15 years and 333 days .
  • Ellina Zvereva was the oldest participant with 47 years and 272 days .
  • 59 participants were chosen to be a flag bearer at the opening ceremony, the highest number out of all sports.
  • 40 nations won a medal in Athletics , more than any other sport.
  • Kenya won their only 16 medals of this edition of the Olympic Games.
  • Jamaica won their only 11 medals of this edition of the Olympic Games.
  • Ethiopia won their only 7 medals of this edition of the Olympic Games.
  • Bahamas , Belgium , Morocco , and Trinidad and Tobago won their only 2 medals of this edition of the Olympic Games.
  • Cameroon , Ecuador , Panama , South Africa , and Sudan won their only medal of this edition of the Olympic Games.
  • Nigeria won 2 medals , more than in any other sport.
