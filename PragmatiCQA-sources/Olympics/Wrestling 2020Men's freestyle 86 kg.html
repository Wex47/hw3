The men's freestyle 86 kg of Wrestling 2020 during the 2020 Summer Olympics was held on 4-5 August 2021 in the Makuhari Messe Hall . With 16 athletes competing, the event was won by David Morris Taylor III from United States , with Hassan Yazdanicharati from Iran taking the silver medal and Artur Naifonov from ROC and Myles Nazem Amine from San Marino taking bronze.

Format

The competition exists out of a knockout phase with the first athlete to throw their opponent's shoulders down onto the mat first winning the bout and advancing to the next round. The winner of the final wins the event.
