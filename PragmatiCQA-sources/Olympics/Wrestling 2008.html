Statistics

  • There were 343 total athletes from 59 countries.
  • Xu Li was the youngest participant with 18 years and 242 days .
  • TC Dantzler was the oldest participant with 37 years and 291 days .
  • 10 participants were chosen to be a flag bearer at the opening ceremony.
  • Bulgaria and Georgia won 4 medals, more than in any other sport.
  • Azerbaijan and Kazakhstan won 3 medals, more than in any other sport.
  • Kyrgyzstan won their only three medals of London 2012 .
