Musashino Forest Sport Plaza (武蔵野の森総合スポーツプラザ) is an indoor venue and sport plaza next to the Tokyo Stadium in the Tokyo Heritage Zone. It was constructed for the 2020 Summer Olympics to host the badminton and the first fencing round of the modern pentathlon events, with the remaining events of the modern pentathlon being hosted by the stadium next-door. Even though it would have a full capacity of 10,000, this was limited to 7,200 during the Olympic events. After the Olympic Games, it would be used as a sport facility to aid the Tama area as one of the core needs of the region.
