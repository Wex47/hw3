Trivia

  • There were 81 total athletes from 36 countries.
  • Nurkanat Azhikanov was the youngest participant with 20 years and 164 days .
  • Sandra Sanchez Jaime was the oldest participant with 39 years and 324 days .
  • Antonio Jose Diaz Fernandez was chosen to be a flag bearer at the opening ceremony for Venezuela .
