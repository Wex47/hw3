Cross-Country Skiing has been featured at every Winter Olympic Games . It is the biggest discipline of the Skiing program, with 12 events. Women have been allowed to compete since the 1952 Summer Olympics .

Scandinavian countries have won the majority of medals in cross-country skiing. Norway, Sweden and Finland have achieved a total of 281 out of a possible 508 medals, with Russia (and the Soviet Union ) having won another 109 medals.
