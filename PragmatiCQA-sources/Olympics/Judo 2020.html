Trivia

  • There were 393 total athletes from 128 countries.
  • Soniya Bhatta was the youngest participant with 19 years and 202 days .
  • Sabrina Filzmoser was the oldest participant with 41 years and 44 days .
  • 30 athletes were chosen to be flag bearers at the opening ceremony.
