NOC

India first participated at the Olympic Games in 1900. The nation has attended Winter Olympic Games since 1964, and at every edition since 1988.

India has won 35 medals at the Summer Olympic Games , but is yet to win a medal at the Winter Olympics. The nation has won almost half of its medals in field hockey , with the most recent gold medals being won in shooting and athletics .

The Indian Olympic Association was created and recognized in 1927.
