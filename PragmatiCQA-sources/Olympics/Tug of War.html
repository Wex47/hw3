Tug of War has been featured five times at the Olympic Games , with which it is one of two discontinued sports with the most appearances, together with Polo .

Great Britain has won the most medals in tug of war, winning two gold, two silver and one bronze, including a so-called clean sweep. United States is the only country to have won a clean sweep, winning their only three medals in St. Louis 1904 .
