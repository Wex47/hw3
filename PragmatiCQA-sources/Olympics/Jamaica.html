NOC

Jamaica first participated at the Olympic Games in 1948, but the nation appeared as part of British West Indies at Rome 1960 . The nation has also appeared at almost every edition of the Winter Olympic Games since Calgary 1988 , only missing out on Torino 2006 .

Jamaica has won 86 medals at the Olympic Games, of which almost every single one in athletics , except for a bronze medal in cycling .

The Jamaica Olympic Association was created and recognized in 1936.
