Sapporo Odori Park (札幌大通公園) is a park in the centre of Sapporo, Japan selected to host the race walking and marathon events of the 2020 Summer Olympics . Located in the north of the country, the weather conditions in the summer are more bearable than the humid and excessively hot weather in Tokyo for the race walking events and marathons. The park itself is about 1.5 kilometres long, covering an area of 7.8 hectares. It has also hosted several cultural events, such as the annual Sapporo Snow Festival, the Lilac Festival, and the Hokkaido Marathon.
