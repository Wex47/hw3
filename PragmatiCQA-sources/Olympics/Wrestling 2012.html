Statistics

  • There were 339 total athletes from 70 countries.
  • Rosalie Benie Tano was the youngest participant with 18 years and 230 days .
  • Dremiel Byers was the oldest participant with 37 years and 329 days .
  • 8 participants were chosen to be a flag bearer at the opening ceremony.
  • Russia won 11 medals, more than in any other sport.
  • Azerbaijan won 7 medals, more than in any other sport.
  • Georgia and Iran won 6 medals, more than in any other sport.
  • Armenia won their only two medals of London 2012 .
