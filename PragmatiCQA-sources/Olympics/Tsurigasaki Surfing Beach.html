Tsurigasaki Surfing Beach (釣ヶ崎海岸サーフィンビーチ) is a beach venue in Ichinomiya town in Chiba Prefecture, and one of the biggest surfing locations in Japan, with over half a million surfers every year. The natural beach has hosted several international competitions in the world of surfing, and was selected to host the inaugural surfing competitions at the 2020 Summer Olympics . The venue had a capacity of 6,000 during the Olympic Games.
