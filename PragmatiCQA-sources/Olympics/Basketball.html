Basketball was introduced during the 1936 Summer Olympics , but it was held as a demonstration event during the 1904 Summer Olympics and the 1924 Summer Olympics . It has only changed its program twice, by adding the women's tournament during Montreal 1976 , and by adding a 3vs3 version in Tokyo 2020 .

United States is only one of two current nations to have won a gold medal in the Basketball events, the other one being Argentina , which won the gold medal in Athens . The United States has shown to be a dominant nation, winning 28 out of 30 possible medals.
