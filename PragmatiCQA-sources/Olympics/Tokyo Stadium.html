Tokyo Stadium (東京スタジアム) is a multi-purpose stadium next to the Musashino Forest Sport Plaza in the Tokyo Heritage Zone during the 2020 Summer Olympics , hosting the rugby sevens competitions, as well as the majority of the modern pentathlon competition, and some of the football matches. It was constructed in 2001 and was named Ajinomoto Stadium from 2003 to 2019, used for football club FC Tokyo, as well as an alternate stadium for the 2002 FIFA World Cup. The stadium was also used for several rugby matches, such as the 2019 Rugby World Cup. The capacity of the stadium during the Olympic Games in 2021 added up to 48,000, although this was not used during Olympic competition due to health concerns. It was the second-biggest stadium of Tokyo 2020, after the Tokyo Olympic Stadium .
