The women's trap of Shooting 2020 during the 2020 Summer Olympics is planned to be held on 28-29 July 2021 in the Asaka Shooting Range . 26 athletes are planned to compete in the event.

Format

The competition exists out of two separate rounds. In the qualification, the shooters try to reach a score as high as possible, with the eight athletes with the highest scores qualifying for the second round. In the second round, the athletes compete with the shooter with the lowest score being eliminated. The last competitor standing wins the event.

Preview

Twenty-six athletes qualified for the event with Wang Xiaojing as one of the big favorites for the title, after winning the silver medal at the previous two editions of the world championships. Zuzana Rehak Stefecekova was the world champion of 2018, but Sandra Bernal lead the qualification world rankings, ahead of Alessandra Perilli and Fatima Galvez . Laetisha Scanlan and Silvana Stanco had also proven strong with places in the final of recent world championships.
