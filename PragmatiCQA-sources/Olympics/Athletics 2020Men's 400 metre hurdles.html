The men's 400 metre hurdles of Athletics 2020 during the 2020 Summer Olympics was held on 30 July-3 August 2021 in the Tokyo Olympic Stadium . With 36 athletes competing, the event was won by Karsten Warholm from Norway , with Rai Benjamin from United States taking the silver medal and Alison dos Santos from Brazil taking bronze.

Format

The competition exists out of three rounds, each with races of up to nine athletes. In each round, the top two with the fastest time advance to the next round, together with a number of fastest non-qualifiers. The fastest competitor in the final is the winner of the event.

Preview

Thirty-six athletes qualified for the event with world record holder Karsten Warholm and third-fastest ever Rai Benjamin as the big favorites for the title. Alison dos Santos and Kyron McMaster were the only two athletes who had stayed within a second of the world record of Warholm, with other medal contenders being Abderrahman Samba , Yasmani Copello , Rasmus Magi , and Thomas Barr . Other outsiders also included Kenneth Selmon , Jaheel Hyde , Abdelmalik Lahoulou , and David Kendziera .
