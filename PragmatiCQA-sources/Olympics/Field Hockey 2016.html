Statistics

  • There were 390 total athletes from 16 countries.
  • Jorrit Croon was the youngest participant with 17 years and 362 days .
  • Jamie Dwyer was the oldest participant with 37 years and 147 days .
  • No participants were chosen to be a flag bearer at the opening ceremony.
  • Great Britain won their first gold medal in women's field hockey .
  • Argentina won their first gold medal in men's field hockey .
  • Germany won 2 medals, more than any other nation.
