NOC

Japan first participated at the Olympic Games in 1912, and has since only missed London 1948 and Moscow 1980 since then. Since Antwerp 1920 , the nation has won an Olympic medal in every edition of the Summer Olympic Games . The nation has also participated at almost every edition of the Winter Olympic Games .

Japan has won over 500 medals at the Olympic Games, with most medals in Gymnastics , Judo , Swimming , and Wrestling . Other sports in which the nation has been successful are Athletics , Weightlifting , and Speed Skating .

The Japanese Olympic Committee was created in 1911 and recognized in 1912.
