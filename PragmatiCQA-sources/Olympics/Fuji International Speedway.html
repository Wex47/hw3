Fuji International Speedway (富士スピードウェイ) is an international motor and race track in Oyama City, Shizuoka Prefecture in Japan. It was opened in 1963, and has hosted several international sportscar and motor races, including the Formula One races in Japan in 2007 and 2008. It hosted the finish of the road cycling events at the 2020 Summer Olympics , with the start of these events at Musashinonomori Park , for which the venue had a capacity of 22,000.
