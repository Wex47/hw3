Tokyo Olympic Stadium (オリンピックスタジアム) is a stadium originally used as the main stadium for Tokyo 1964 , hosting the athletics events. The stadium was later completely rebuilt to be able to host the athletics events and some of the football matches of Tokyo 2020 , as well as hosting the opening and closing ceremonies. After the Olympic Games, it is intended to use for several sporting events in mainly athletics and football, as well as other cultural events. It was the second-biggest stadium of Tokyo 2020 with a capacity of 68,000.
