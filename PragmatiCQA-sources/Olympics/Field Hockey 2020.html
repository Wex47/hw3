Trivia

  • There were 384 total athletes from 14 countries.
  • Valentina Raposo Ruiz de los Llanos was the youngest participant with 18 years and 178 days .
  • Juan Manuel Vivaldi was the oldest participant with 42 years and 7 days .
  • 3 athletes were chosen to be flag bearers at the opening ceremony.
  • 6 nations won an Olympic medal, the highest possible number.
