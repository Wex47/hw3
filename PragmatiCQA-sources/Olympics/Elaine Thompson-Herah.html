Elaine Thompson-Herah is a track and field athlete who has competed for Jamaica. She competed at the 2016 and 2020 Summer Olympic Games. She was the first female Olympian since Seoul 1988 to win a gold medal in both the 100 metres and the 200 metres sprint.
