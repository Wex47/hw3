NOC

Jordan first participated at the Olympic Games in 1980, and has participated at every edition of the Summer Olympic Games since Moscow. This nation has not participated at the Winter Olympic Games yet.

Jordan has won three medals at the Olympic Games, won in taekwondo and karate in 2016 and 2020.

The Jordan Olympic Committee was created in 1957 and recognized in 1963.
