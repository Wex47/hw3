Weightlifting has been featured at the Olympic Games since the 1896 Summer Olympics , although women have only been allowed to compete since the 2000 Summer Olympics . The weightclasses have been introduced during the 1920 Summer Olympics .

Weightlifting has attracted some controversy in recent years--the majority of positive doping cases stem from athletics and weightlifting, with a significant number of reassigned medals in recent Olympic Games in weightlifting.
