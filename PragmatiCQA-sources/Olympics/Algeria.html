NOC

Algeria first participated at the Olympic Games in 1964. This nation has participated in three Winter Olympic Games since 1992.

Algeria has won a total of 17 medals, although five of them have been won by just two people, with the big majority coming in athletics and boxing . Algeria has never won a medal at the Winter Olympic Games.

The National Olympic Committee of Algeria was created in 1963 and recognized in 1964.
