Guyana Olympic Association

Guyana first participated at the Olympic Games in 1948, and has only missed participation at Montreal 1976 . The nation has not appeared at the Winter Olympic Games yet.

Guyana has won one medal at the Olympic Games, a bronze medal in boxing at Moscow 1980 .

The Guyana Olympic Association was created in 1935 and recognized in 1948.
