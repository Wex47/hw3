Golf was introduced during the 1900 Summer Olympics , but it was removed from the Olympic Games after the 1904 Summer Olympics . It was reintroduced during the 2016 Summer Olympics .

The United States has won 11 out of a possible 19 medals, although six out of these medals were won at the 1904 Summer Olympics , where 74 out of 77 competing athletes were from the United States.
