NOC

Kenya first participated at the Olympic Games in 1956. This nation has participated at the Winter Olympic Games four times.

Kenya has won a total of 113 medals, of which the majority in athletics . The nation has also won a few medals in Olympic boxing .

The National Olympic Committee Kenya was created in 1954 and recognized in 1955.
