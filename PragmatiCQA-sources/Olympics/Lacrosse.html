Lacrosse has only been featured twice at the Olympic Games . It was also featured as a demonstration event three times. Despite appearing during St. Louis 1904 and London 1908 , it was a demonstration event during the 1928 Summer Olympics , the 1932 Summer Olympics and the 1948 Summer Olympics .

Canada was the only nation that participated both times, with Great Britain and United States winning silver, in 1904 and 1908, respectively.
