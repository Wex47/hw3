Trivia

  • There were 174 total athletes from 57 countries.
  • Hend Zaza was the youngest participant with 12 years and 205 days .
  • Xia Lian Ni was the oldest participant with 58 years and 31 days .
  • 9 athletes were chosen to be flag bearers at the opening ceremony.
