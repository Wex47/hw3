Volleyball is the category name for the volleyball events at the Olympic Games . These sports are coordinated by the FIVB (Fédération Internationale de Volleyball), which was founded in 1947. This category entails the following two disciplines:

  • Beach Volleyball
  • Indoor Volleyball
