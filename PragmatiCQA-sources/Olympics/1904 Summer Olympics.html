The 1904 Summer Olympics, officially known as the Games of the III Olympiad, was celebrated in St Louis, Missouri in the USA from 1 July 1904, to November 23, 1904, on the campus of Washington University in St Louis. The Games were held in conjunction with the St. Louis World's Fair.
