NOC

Belgium first participated at the Olympic Games in 1900. This nation has competed in Winter Olympic Games since 1924, and has been in every edition of the Olympic Games since 1972.

Belgium has won over 150 medals at the Summer Olympic Games and the Winter Olympic Games.

The Belgian Olympic and Interfederal Committee was created and recognized in 1906.
