Golf at the 2016 Summer Olympics was held from 11 to 20 August 2016. This is the first time golf had appeared at the Olympic Games since 1904.

Statistics

  • There were 120 total athletes from 41 countries.
  • Aditi Ashok was the youngest participant with 18 years and 141 days .
  • Catriona Matthew was the oldest participant with 46 years and 357 days .
  • Siddikur Rahman and Julieta Granada were chosen to be flag bearers at the opening ceremony.
  • 6 nations won a medal, with all six countries winning 1 medal.
