Sea Forest Waterway (海の森水上競技場) is a new venue in the Tokyo Bay Zone, on the same island as the Sea Forest Cross-Country Course , to host the canoe sprint and rowing events at the 2020 Summer Olympics . It was opened in June 2019 just ahead of the junior world championships in 2019, and the venue is planned to become one of the main locations to practice water sports in Asia after Olympic competition. During the Olympic Games, the capacity of the venue was 12,800 for the canoe sprint competition and 16,000 for the rowing competitions.
