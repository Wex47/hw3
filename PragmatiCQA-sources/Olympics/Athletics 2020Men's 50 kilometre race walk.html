The men's 50 kilometre race walk of Athletics 2020 during the 2020 Summer Olympics was held on 6 August 2021 in the Sapporo Odori Park . With 59 athletes competing, the event was won by Dawid Tomala from Poland , with Jonathan Hilbert from Germany taking the silver medal and Evan Dunfee from Canada taking bronze.

Format

The competition exists out of a single race, in which all competitors conduct a racewalking course of 50 kilometres. The competitor with the fastest time is the winner of the event.

Preview

Fifty-nine athletes qualified for the event with medal contenders looking to come from the host nation. Japanese runners had walked to the three fastest times of 2021, with Satoshi Maruo and Hayato Katsuki as the fastest two. Jonathan Hilbert was the only other one to have stayed within 3:45:00, but with the silver medalist of the most recent world championships Joao Vieira , the bronze medalist Evan Dunfee , the 2017 world champion and world record holder Yohann Diniz , and the defending Olympic champion Matej Toth also in the field, many athletes could contend for a medal. Other potential outsiders also included Maryan Zakalnytskyy , Andres Chocho , Havard Haukenes , Brendan Boyce , and Rafal Augustyn .
