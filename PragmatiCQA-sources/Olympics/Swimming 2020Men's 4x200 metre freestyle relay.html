The men's 4x200 metre freestyle relay of Swimming 2020 during the 2020 Summer Olympics was held on 27-28 July 2021 in the Tokyo Aquatics Centre . With 16 teams competing, the event was won by the team from Great Britain , with ROC taking the silver medal and Australia taking bronze.

Format

This competition exists out of two rounds, each with eight teams. The eight teams with the fastest times in either semifinal advance to the final. The fastest team in the final is the winner of the event.

Preview

Sixteen teams qualified for the event with Australia as the reigning world champion, though the entire top 5 of the most recent world championships were not to be messed with. The teams from ROC , the United States , Italy , and Great Britain were not more than 0.23 seconds apart in South Korea in 2019, with also Japan as the defending bronze medalists featured in the field. Other potential outsiders also included China , Brazil , and France .
