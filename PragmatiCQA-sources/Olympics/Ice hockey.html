Ice Hockey was first featured during the 1920 Summer Olympics , and it is one of two sports to have been featured in both the Winter Olympic Games and the Summer Olympic Games .

Canada is the definite record holder in ice hockey, winning 21 medals, of which 13 gold. The United States has won 17 medals, but only 4 gold.
