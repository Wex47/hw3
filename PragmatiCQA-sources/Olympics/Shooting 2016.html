Statistics

  • There were 390 total athletes from 97 countries.
  • Aislin Jones was the youngest participant with 16 years and 186 days .
  • Paulo Reichardt was the oldest participant with 56 years and 2 days .
  • 11 participants were chosen to be a flag bearer at the opening ceremony.
  • 7 Olympic records were broken.
  • Italy won 7 medals, more than in any other sport.
  • Greece won 2 medals, more than in any other sport.
  • Vietnam and the Independent Olympic Athletes won their only 2 medals.
