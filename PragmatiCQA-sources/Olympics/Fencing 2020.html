Trivia

  • There were 261 total athletes from 42 countries.
  • Jessica Zi Jia Guo was the youngest participant with 16 years and 32 days .
  • Aida Mohamed was the oldest participant with 45 years and 139 days .
  • 7 athletes were chosen to be flag bearers at the opening ceremony.
