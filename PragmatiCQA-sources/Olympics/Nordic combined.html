Nordic Combined has been featured since the 1924 Winter Olympics . It is part of the Skiing program. It is the only discipline from this program to be men-only.

Norway, Finland , and Austria have won 60 out of a total of 111 medals in Nordic combined. These three countries are also powerful nations in Ski Jumping .
