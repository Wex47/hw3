Statistics

  • There were 353 total athletes from 20 countries.
  • Đurđina Jauković was the youngest participant with 19 years and 163 days .
  • Oh Yeong-ran was the oldest participant with 43 years and 334 days .
  • 4 participants were chosen to be a flag bearer at the opening ceremony.
  • Denmark won their first gold medal in handball .
  • France won 2 medals, more than any other nation.
