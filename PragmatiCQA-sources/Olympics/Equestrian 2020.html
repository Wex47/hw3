Equestrian at the 2020 Summer Olympics was held from 24 July to 7 August 2021 at Tokyo Equestrian Park .

Trivia

  • There were 234 total athletes from 50 countries.
  • Zhang You was the youngest participant with 20 years and 128 days .
  • Mary Hanna was the oldest participant with 66 years and 237 days .
  • 3 athletes were chosen to be flag bearers at the opening ceremony.
