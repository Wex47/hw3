Athletics has been featured at every Summer Olympic Games . It has the highest amount of events, with 48 events. According to legend, Athletics is the oldest discipline of the Olympic Games, tracing back to thousands of years ago. Governing body the International Association of Athletics Federations was founded in 1912. Women have been allowed to compete since the 1924 Summer Olympics .

Athletics is divided into four disciplines:

  • Track (Running; 100m, 200m, 400m, 800m, etc.)
  • Field (Jumping and throwing; high jump, triple jump, javelin throw, shot put, etc.)
  • Road running (Long distance running; marathon)
  • Racewalking (Long distance walking; 20km race walk, 50km race walk)
