Tokyo Metropolitan Gymnasium (東京体育館) is a stadium which was originally used as one of the multi-purposed stadiums of Tokyo 1964 , hosting the gymnastics and water polo events. Since it was rebuilt in 1990, the venue has hosted several sports competitions and pop concerts, and the indoor swimming pool has been used for the national water polo championships. At Tokyo 2020 , the venue hosts the table tennis tournaments at the Olympic and Paralympic Games. The stadium has a regular capacity of 10,000, but this was lowered to 7,000 during the Olympic competition.
