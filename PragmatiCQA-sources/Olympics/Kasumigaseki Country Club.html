Kasumigaseki Country Club (霞ヶ関カンツリー倶楽部) is a golf course in the Musashino Hills, in Saitama. It was opened in October 1929 with the purpose of preserving and maintaining the traditions of golf throughout generations. As the oldest golf course in the region, the venue has hosted several international golf competitions from 1930 to 2010, and was selected to host the golf competitions at the 2020 Summer Olympics . The venue had a capacity of 25,000 during the Olympic Games.
