Statistics

  • There were 386 total athletes from 138 countries.
  • Joshua Katz was the youngest participant with 18 years and 220 days .
  • Ramón Pileta was the oldest participant with 39 years and 145 days .
  • 20 participants were chosen to be a flag bearer at the opening ceremony.
  • Japan won 12 medals, more than in any other sport.
  • Slovenia won 2 medals, more than in any other sport.
  • Israel won their only two medals.
  • United Arab Emirates won their only medal of Rio de Janeiro 2016 .
  • Kosovo won their first ever medal.
