Yumenoshima Park Archery Field (夢の島公園アーチェリー場) is an outdoor venue next to the Yumenoshima marina in the Tokyo Bay Zone. It was opened in 2020, after being built specifically for the 2020 Summer Olympics to host the archery tournaments. With less than 20 minutes walking to more than 10 other venues, it is located at a very convenient location close to the harbour. After the Olympic Games, it is intended to use for archery competitions and other miscellaneous activities. It has a capacity of 5,600.
