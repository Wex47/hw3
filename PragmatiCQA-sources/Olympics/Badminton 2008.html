Badminton at the 2008 Summer Olympics was held between 9 and 17 August 2008 at the Beijing University of Technology Gymnasium .

Statistics

  • There were 173 total athletes from 50 countries.
  • Deyanira Angulo was the youngest participant with 17 years and 159 days .
  • Jens Eriksen was the oldest participant with 38 years and 225 days .
  • Kevin Cordon and Georgie Cupidon were chosen to be the flag bearers for Guatemala and the Seychelles at the opening ceremony.
  • Malaysia won its only medal at Beijing 2008 .
