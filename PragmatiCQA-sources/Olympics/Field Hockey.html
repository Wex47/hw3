Field Hockey was introduced during the 1908 Summer Olympics , but it was removed from the Olympic Games after the 1920 Summer Olympics , after which the Fédération Internationale de Hockey was founded. After this, it has been featured non-stop since Amsterdam 1928 .

Although there have been several dominant forces in field hockey, the women's team from the Netherlands has won eight out of a possible ten medals, after not competing once, and once finishing sixth.
