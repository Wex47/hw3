Trivia

  • There were 142 total athletes from 30 countries.
  • Quan Hongchan was the youngest participant with 14 years and 128 days .
  • Ken Terauchi was the oldest participant with 40 years and 355 days .
  • 3 athletes were chosen to be flag bearers at the opening ceremony.
