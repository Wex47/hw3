Bahrain first participated at the Olympic Games in 1984. This nation has not participated at the Winter Olympic Games yet.

Bahrain has won a total of 4 medals, but they have only won medals in athletics.

The Bahrain Olympic Committee was created in 1978 and recognized in 1979.
