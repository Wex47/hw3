Trivia

  • There were 384 total athletes from 30 countries.
  • Ana Cristina Menezes Oliveira de Souza was the youngest participant with 17 years and 110 days .
  • Mehdi Ben Cheikh was the oldest participant with 42 years and 73 days .
  • 10 athletes were chosen to be flag bearers at the opening ceremony.
