NOC

Kazakhstan first participated at the Olympic Games in 1996, although Kazakh athletes have participated before, as Soviet Union (until 1988) and Unified Team (1992). This nation has participated in every Winter Olympic Games since 1994.

Belarus has won a total of 80 medals, of which the majority in boxing , with also over 10 medals in Wrestling and Weightlifting . It has won a medal at every edition of the Summer Olympic Games it has participated in, and won medals in five out of seven editions of the Winter Olympic Games it has participated in.

The National Olympic Committee of the Republic of Kazakhstan was created in 1990 and recognized in 1993.
