Trivia

  • There were 537 total athletes from 69 countries.
  • Payton Ridenour was the youngest participant with 19 years and 61 days .
  • Amber Neben was the oldest participant with 46 years and 161 days .
  • 3 athletes were chosen to be flag bearers at the opening ceremony.
