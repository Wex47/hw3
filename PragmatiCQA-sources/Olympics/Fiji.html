NOC

Fiji first participated at the Olympic Games in 1956. This nation has participated at the Winter Olympic Games since 1988. At the 2016 Summer Olympics , Fiji won its first Olympic medal when they became Olympic champion in the Rugby Sevens event, and won two medals in the same sport five years later.

The Fiji Association of Sports and National Olympic Committee was created in 1949 and recognized in 1955.
