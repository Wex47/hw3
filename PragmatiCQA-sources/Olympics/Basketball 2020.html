Trivia

  • There were 352 total athletes from 24 countries.
  • Wan Jiyuan was the youngest participant with 19 years and 11 days .
  • Laia Palau was the oldest participant with 41 years and 319 days .
  • 11 athletes were chosen to be flag bearers at the opening ceremony.
