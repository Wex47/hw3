Emma McKeon is a swimmer who has competed for Australia. She competed at the 2016 and 2020 Summer Olympic Games. Winning seven medals at the 2020 Summer Olympic Games, she was the most decorated athlete at Tokyo 2020 . She also won six medals at the Youth Olympic Games in 2010.

Her brother David McKeon and her father Ron McKeon are both two-time Olympians, with her uncle Robert Woodhouse having won a bronze medal at Los Angeles 1984 in swimming.
