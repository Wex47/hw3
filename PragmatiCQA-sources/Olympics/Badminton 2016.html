Statistics

  • There were 172 total athletes from 46 countries.
  • Akane Yamaguchi was the youngest participant with 19 years and 66 days .
  • Robert Mateusiak was the oldest participant with 40 years and 211 days .
  • 5 athletes were chosen to be the flag bearer at the opening ceremony.
  • Malaysia won 3 medals, more than in any other sport.
