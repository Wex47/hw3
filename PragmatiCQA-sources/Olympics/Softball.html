Softball was introduced during the 1996 Summer Olympics , and it was featured at the Olympic Games until Beijing 2008 . It is one of the two sports to only feature one event, and one of the two sports to be women-only.

United States is one of four countries to have won a medal in Softball. Winning three gold and one silver, the United States have achieved a better result than Australia (one silver, three bronze) and Japan (one gold, one silver, one bronze) and China (one silver).
