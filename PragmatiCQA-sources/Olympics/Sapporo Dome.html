Sapporo Dome (札幌ドーム) is a stadium in Sapporo City, Japan, mainly used for football and baseball. It was opened in 2001, and has since hosted several big events, such as the football World Cup, the Asian Games, and the rugby World Cup, and the stadium was the first-ever to have a retractable flooring, allowing both football and baseball matches. The stadium also an observatory and a restaurant. It was selected as one of the stadiums to host football matches at the 2020 Summer Olympics , and has a capacity of 41,000.
