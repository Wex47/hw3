Nippon Budokan (日本武道館) is a world-renowned arena and the spiritual home of martial arts. Despite being originally built for the inaugural judo competitions at Tokyo 1964 , the arena is probably most famous for its use as a venue for Japanese wrestling and competitions of martial arts such as judo, kendo, and karate, as well as hosting several pop concerts. During Tokyo 2020 , the venue hosts the judo and karate competitions. The stadium has a capacity of 13,291, but 11,000 places could have been used during the Olympic Games in 2021. After the event, the arena is intended to be continued to be used as arena for Japanese martial arts.
