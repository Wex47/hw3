NOC

Estonia first participated as an independent country at the Olympic Games in 1920, although athletes might have appeared before as Russian Empire . Between 1948 and 1988, it competed as a part of Soviet Union . Since 1992, it once again appeared as independent.

Estonia has won 43 medals at the Olympic Games, of which 11 in Wrestling , and another 20 in Weightlifting , Cross-Country Skiing and Athletics .

The Estonian Olympic Committee was created in 1923 and recognized in 1924. After the invasion of the Soviet Union, the committee was reinstated in 1989, and recognized in 1991.
