NOC

Cameroon first participated at the Olympic Games in 1964. This nation has participated at the Winter Olympic Games in 2002.

Cameroon has won a total of 6 medals at the Olympic Games.

The National Olympic Committee of Cameroon was created in 1963 and recognized in 1964.
