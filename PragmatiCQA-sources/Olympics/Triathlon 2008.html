Triathlon at the 2008 Summer Olympics was held on 18 and 19 August 2008 at the Ming Tomb Reservoir .

Statistics

  • There were 110 total athletes from 37 countries.
  • Hollie Avil was the youngest participant with 18 years and 128 days .
  • Ana Burgos was the oldest participant with 40 years and 236 days .
  • No participating athletes were chosen to be a flag bearer during the opening ceremony.
