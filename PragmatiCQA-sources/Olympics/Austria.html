Austria has participated in every Olympic Games , except for the 1920 edition, where they were banned, due to their influence in the First World War.

Austria has won over 300 medals, of which 200 at the Winter Olympic Games , and 100 of them at the alpine skiing.

The Österreichisches Olympisches Comité was created in 1908 and recognized in 1912.
