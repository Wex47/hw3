Trivia

  • There were 130 total athletes from 61 countries.
  • Lauren Lee was the youngest participant with 17 years and 165 days .
  • Pita Taufatofua was the oldest participant with 38 years and 265 days .
  • 14 athletes were chosen to be flag bearers at the opening ceremony.
