NOC

Armenia first participated at the Olympic Games in 1996, although Armenian athletes have participated before, as Soviet Union (until 1988) and Unified Team (1992). This nation has participated in every Winter Olympic Games since 1994.

Armenia has won a total of 18 medals, although 15 of them have only been won in two sports. Armenia has never won a medal at the Winter Olympic Games.

The National Olympic Committee of Armenia was created in 1990 and recognized in 1993.
