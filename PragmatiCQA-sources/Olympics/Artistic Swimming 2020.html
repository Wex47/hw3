Trivia

  • There were 104 total athletes from 22 countries.
  • Maryam Maghraby was the youngest participant with 16 years and 299 days .
  • Kseniya Sydorenko was the oldest participant with 35 years and 32 days .
  • No athletes were chosen to be flag bearers at the opening ceremony.
  • The 6 medals were only shared between 3 countries, the lowest amount possible.
