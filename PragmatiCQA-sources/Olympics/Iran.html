NOC

Iran first participated at the Olympic Games in 1948, after which the nation only missed the Olympic Games in 1980 and 1984. Iran has also competed at several Winter Olympic Games , of which every edition since 1998.

Iran has won 76 medals at the Olympic Games, of which the big majority in wrestling and weightlifting . The remaining medals were won in taekwondo , athletics , and karate .

The National Olympic Committee of the Islamic Republic of Iran was created and recognized in 1947.
