Dr. Stone ( ドクターストーン , Dokutā Sutōn ) is a manga series written by Riichiro Inagaki , illustrated by Boichi and published in Weekly Shonen Jump . The series has been running ever since March 6th of 2017 in the 14th issue of Shuiesha Inc's manga anthology, Weekly Shonen Jump .

Synopsis

Several thousand years after a mysterious phenomenon petrified all of humanity, a brilliant, scientifically-minded teen wakes up in this new world alone. Vowing to bring back all of civilization, Senku sets out to revive all 7 billion people on Earth. With millions of years of human development to rebuild and countless challenges to be faced, will Senku and his friends succeed in reviving everyone, or is their fate set in stone?
