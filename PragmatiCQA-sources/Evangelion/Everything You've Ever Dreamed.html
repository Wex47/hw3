Everything You've Ever Dreamed

Artist

Arianne, Shiro Sagisu

Writer

Hideaki Anno (original) Mike Wyzgowski (adaptation)

Length

6:42


"Everything You've Ever Dreamed" is a song composed by Shiro Sagisu , with vocals by Arianne . The original lyrics were written by Hideaki Anno and later adapted to English by Mike Wyzgowski for End of Evangelion , like Komm, süsser Tod . It was recorded along with KSD, but ultimately not used. According to some , an unspecified song was planned to play after the movie's last scene for the credits, probably this one.

Eventually, the song was released in the S² Works box set, as the final track of Disc 6, listed in the tracklist under the codename "M-11". KSD was codenamed M-10. It was later re-released as part of the Refrain of Evangelion compilation. The Refrain describes it as "This CD concludes with this phantom song that we once planned to use as “one other possibility” along with Track 25. [KSD]."

Notes

  • The introduction of this song can be heard during the Evangelion: Symphony version of Fly Me to the Moon, after a phrase from Misato is played.

Analysis

  • The lyrics of Everything You've Ever Dreamed (both Anno's original lyrics as well as the English adaption) seem to be about the relationship between Shinji Ikari and Asuka Langley Soryu .
