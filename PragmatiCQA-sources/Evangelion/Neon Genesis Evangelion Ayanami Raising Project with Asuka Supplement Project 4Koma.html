Neon Genesis Evangelion: Ayanami Raising Project with Asuka Supplement Project 4Koma ( 新世紀エヴァンゲリオン綾波育成計画Withアスカ補完計画4コママンガ劇場 , " Shin Seiki Evangerion: Ayanami Ikusei Keikaku with Asuka Hokan Keikaku 4koma ") is a 4koma manga anthology series based on the Neon Genesis Evangelion: Ayanami Raising Project game. It was published by Square Enix in two volumes in 2003.
