Mayumi Yamagishi ( 山岸マユミ , " Yamagishi Mayumi ") is an original character that appears in the Sega Saturn game Neon Genesis Evangelion: Second Impression . She is a student who enjoys reading and somehow contains the Core of the Insubstantial Angel within her body. At base she is a female version of Shinji Ikari .

History

Mayumi is the adopted daughter of a father working at the United Nations . She is transferred to Shinji Ikari 's class. Depending on the actions of the player, she may be part of the Earth Defense Group, a musical group consisting of Toji Suzuhara , Kensuke Aida and Shinji Ikari for the school's cultural festival. She then plays the song "Kimi ga, Kimi ni Umareta Wake" . In one of the scenarios, Mayumi contains the Core of the Insubstantial Angel . Shinji's dilemma is then to decide whether he should kill her or not.
