Kingshitoel ( キングシトエル ) is an Angel that appears in the episode 31 of Shinkansen Henkei Robo Shinkalion THE ANIMATION . It appears to mostly attack with slashes but not much is shown. Its origin is unknown.

Profile

Kingshitoel is a angel that uses elements of various angels of the Rebuild of Evangelion movie series. The lower part is based of the design of the Third Angel , the torso uses parts of the Fifth Angel , the head/arms uses parts of Tenth Angel and Fourth Angel . There is a smaller violet octahedron similar to the Sixth Angel . The "wings" are based on the design of the Eighth Angel .

History

Kingshitoel is defeated by Hayato Hayasugi piloting the Shinkalion E5 Hayabusa after the A.T. Field is destroyed by Shinji Ikari piloting the Shinkalion 500 TYPE EVA.

Trivia

  • The name is most likely a portmanteau of the english word "King" and the japanese term for Angels, "Shito" and the suffix "El", which was used to name angels in Neon Genesis Evangelion TV series.
