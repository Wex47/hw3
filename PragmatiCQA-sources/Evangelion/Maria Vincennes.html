Marie Vincennes ( マリイ・ヴァンセンヌ ) is an original character from Neon Genesis Evangelion RPG: The NERV White Paper game. She is an American Eva pilot, but lacks any actual battle experience. A prodigy with a P.H.D., she acts meek and polite, but in actuality is haughty and domineering. Something of a reverse on Asuka 's personality.

Notes

  • Vincennes is the name of four different U.S. naval vessels, all of which are no longer in service, and one of which is particularly infamous.
  • The RPG game from which she originates only goes up until the Tenth Angel, Sahaquiel , following the story before diverging into two Angels designed solely for the game. Without Unit-03 becoming Bardiel , it stands to reason for the purposes of the game, Marie is the Fourth Child and not Toji Suzuhara .
  • She wears a cross similar to Misato Katsuragi though for entirely different reasons.
