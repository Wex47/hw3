Hajime Suruga ( 駿河ハジメ ) is an original character from Secret of Evangelion game.

Profile

An engineer belonging to the first section of the NERV Technology Development Department. He is the he leader of a group who is dissatisfied with the treatment within NERV. He is also in charge of negotiations with SEELE (with the proxy Hawk ).
