Evangelion: Breaking Dawn was an officially-licensed mobile action game created by Sina Games, based on the Rebuild series. The player collects several Evangelions and different mecha to play battles from the first 2 Rebuild movies Evangelion: 1.0 You Are (Not) Alone and Evangelion: 2.0 You Can (Not) Advance , It also features playable Evangelions/Mecha from Neon Genesis Evangelion: ANIMA

The Asian version (Google Play, Chinese) of this game was removed on 13th June, 2020 due to the constant decrease in players. An announcement was made in May 12, 2020, informing players that servers would be shut down on 12th June, 2020. However, the actual time of shutdown turned out to be the next day. The game is present on the store but unavailable to download.

Appearance

The player goes through the stories and battles from the first 2 Rebuild movies Evangelion: 1.0 You Are (Not) Alone and Evangelion: 2.0 You Can (Not) Advance , which the player collects items such as armour parts, Evangelion or character fragments to unlock different Evangelions and characters. Players are required to reach a certain level before they can unlock the upcoming levels.

Each Evangelion and character has a rarity ranking: N >R>SR>SSR>UR. The ease of obtaining those fragments vary from their rarity. Fragments can be used to unlock or leveling-up Evangelions and characters. Characters provide additional support to their respective Evangelion units.

The game also features an arena, which players around the world challenge their battle skills. Each player sends three Evangelion units and three respective support weapons for battle. Upon completing or winning a certain amount of battles, players will be awarded money, diamonds and Evangelion fragments (N-R). Results are concluded at the end of each month. Prizes and titles (Activation for 25 days) will be distributed to players of different arena rankings, including diamonds and SR and SSR Evangelion fragments.

Players can also join guilds which they can enjoy various advantages such as guild points for purchasing different valuables, 'research' to create fragments, and participate in the guild war every Saturday 20:00 (for timezone GMT +8).
