History

Neon Genesis Evangelion

  • First Impact was caused by the impact of the Black Moon containing Lilith on to what would eventually become Tokyo-3 .
  • Second Impact was intentionally orchestrated by SEELE , the impact was caused by a Contact Experiment done on adam at the North Pole causing him to turn into an embryo. This impact also turned the northern seas red, misaligned Earths orbit leading

to the ending of seasons and killed over half of the human race.

  • While the effects of Third Impact can be seen throughout episodes 25 and 26 of Neon Genesis, it is most clearly demonstrated during the events of The End of Evangelion . The impact began when Adam and Lilith's bodies and souls merged resulting in a massive Anti- A.T.-Field being formed reverting life on Earth to LCL leading to the completion of the Human Instrumentality Project . This impact and the project were ended by Shinji Ikari who chose to reject instrumentality in favor of individuality.

Rebuild of Evangelion

  • In Rebuild Continuity, the first two impacts are essentially the same as in Neon Genesis with two exceptions, instead of only a single Adam there are multiple adams and no embryo was produced through the contact experiment.
  • Near Third Impact was unintentionally triggered by Shinji through awakening of Evangelion Unit-01 after trying to save Rei Ayanami from the core of Tenth Angel . This impact is stopped by Kaworu Nagisa in the Evangelion Mark.06 through the use of the Spear of Cassius . Although the scope of the damage caused by this impact is unclear, the damage caused by this impact was minimal as life in Tokyo-3 was able to continue without much issue.
  • Some time after Near Third Impact stopped, Third Impact was caused Kaworu in the Mark.06 when he fused with Lilith . Like Near Third, this impact was also stopped by sacrifice of Ryoji Kaji . Despite his sacrifice, Third Impact was still a nearly extinction level event which caused the world to turn red and eliminated almost all of humanity by transforming them into the Failures of Infinity .
  • Fourth Impact is triggered by the awakening of Evangelion 13 . This impact was stopped by the piercing of Evangelion 13 by two Spears of Longinus , the death of Kaworu by the DSS Choker and the force ejection of Shinji from the unit by Mari Makinami Illustrious in the Evangelion Unit-08/Unit-08β .

  • Additional impact is the sixth and final impact in the rebuild series caused by Gendo Ikari through the use of Evangelion 13, Nebuchadnezzar's Key and the Adams to open the Minus Space and cause instrumentality. Shinji controls this impact using the Spear of Gaius and reforms the world into one without Evangelion.

Neon Genesis Evangelion (manga)

  • While the manga shares the same impacts as Neon Genesis Evangelion, Third impacts ending is quite different as instead of being ambiguous on what the future of the world looks like, in the Manga, Shinji chooses to recreate the world without Evangelions leading him to live an uneventful life as a regular student.

  • In the rebuild continuity, each impact was done to cleanse an aspect of the planet to prepare for instrumentality. Those aspects are the earth, the air, the sea and humanity.
