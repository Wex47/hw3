Neon Genesis Evangelion: Pikopiko Chuugakusei Densetsu (新世紀エヴァンゲリオン ピコピコ中学生伝説, Shinseiki Evangelion: Pikopiko Chuugakusei Densetsu ?, Lit. Neon Genesis Evangelion: The Legend of the Students of Piko Piko High School ) is a manga based on the Neon Genesis Evangelion franchise.

The manga was written by Yushi Kawata and drawn by Yukito. It was serialized in Young Ace magazine between April 4, 2014, and August 4, 2018. It has 70 chapters that were subsequently collected in five volumes.

Synopse

Mysterious invaders are threatening Earth...so recruit a group of elite school kids to play video games that secretly train them for the real fight that lies ahead! Does this plan sound familiar? Well, they'd better get a new plan, because the elite school kids are Shinji , Rei, Asuka, and Kaworu , and they're too busy playing games to ever get in the fricking robot!
