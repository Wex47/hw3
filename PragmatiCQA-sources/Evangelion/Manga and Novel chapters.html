Several manga or novel series have been developed based on the various Evangelion series. While the first manga is a direct adaptation of the TV anime series, the following ones are the other manga and novels based on Evangelion.

Manga

  • Neon Genesis Evangelion (1995)
  • Neon Genesis Evangelion: Angelic Days (2003)
  • Neon Genesis Evangelion: Ayanami Raising Project with Asuka Supplement Project 4Koma (2003)
  • Neon Genesis Evangelion: Eva & Eva 2 Anthology (2004)
  • Neon Genesis Evangelion: Shinji Ikari Raising Project (manga) (2005)
  • It's A Miraculous Win (2006)
  • Petit Eva (Manga) (2007)
  • Neon Genesis Evangelion: Gakuen Datenroku (2007)
  • Petit Eva Bokura Tanken Dōkōkai (2009)
  • Neon Genesis Evangelion: The Shinji Ikari Detective Diary (2010)
  • Tony Takezaki’s Neon Genesis Evangelion (2010)
  • Neon Genesis Evangelion: Comic Tribute (2010)
  • Neon Genesis Evangelion: Legend of the Piko Piko Middle School Students (2014)

Novels

  • Sore o Nasumono (1998)
  • Neon Genesis Evangelion: ANIMA (2008)
  • Transformers Mode “Eva” (2013)
