Human is a term in the Evangelion universe which is widely used to designate various life forms :

  • Lilin : the humans born with the Fruit of Knowledge , or simply put, the Homo Sapiens Sapiens , and children of Lilith .
  • Angels : the humans born with the Fruit of Life , and children of Adam .
  • Evangelion : the humans made by the Lilins from Adam and Lilith.
  • Seed of Life : the humans based on either a Fruit of Knowledge or a Fruit of Life.
  • First Ancestral Race : the proto-humans who created Adam, Lilith and the other Seeds of Life.
