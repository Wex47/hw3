Hitomi Kaga ( 加賀ヒトミ ) is an original character from Secret of Evangelion game.

Profile

She is a talented woman with a PhD in metaphysics. After entering NERV, she was appointed to the Project E. Her main job is to further develop Evangelion units like being involved in production of dummy systems and dummy plugs Recognized for her excellence by Kōzō Fuyutsuki , who knew her father who was also a physicist. She is clumsy, for example using green tea leaves when making black tea blends and using the men's shower. She has a strong emotional personality, and she often lets her emotions acts and later regrets it. She is not good with men, and her first priority is to live as a scientist, not as a woman.

Trivia

  • Her surname, , comes from the Japanese aircraft carrier Kaga of the Japanese Imperial Navy.
  • She shares the first name with the character Hitomi Amagi from the Evangelion Battlefields mobile game.
