Hawk ( ホーク ) is an original character from Secret of Evangelion game.

Profile

A subordinate of Kyoya Kenzaki , who belongs to the special agency NERV Security Intelligence Department. "Hawk" is not his real name. Depending on the path you choose, he becomes a SEELE spy for money and confronts Kyoya. He is a talented subordinate who faithfully performs his duties.
