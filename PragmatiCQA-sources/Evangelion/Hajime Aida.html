Hajime Aida ( 相田ハジメ , " Aida Hajime ") is a fictional character from the Rebuild of Evangelion movie series. He is the father of Kensuke Aida .

Story

Not much is known about Hajime Aida, in Evangelion: 3.0+1.0 Thrice Upon A Time , it is revealed that he survived the Near Third Impact but died in an accident. Kensuke laments that he should have talk more with him, drink with him and listen to his complains. Hajime's tomb is in a hill with a single tree on it. Other people tombs are also on the same hill.
