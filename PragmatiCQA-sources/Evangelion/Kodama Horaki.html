Kodama Horaki ( 洞木コダマ , " Horaki Kodama ") is a college student and older sister of Hikari Horaki and Nozomi Horaki .

In other media

Neon Genesis Evangelion Proposal

She was cited for the first time in Hikari Horaki profile in Neon Genesis Evangelion Proposal .

Neon Genesis Evangelion: ANIMA

In the chapter 15 of the first volume of printed novel, Kodama was traveling with her sisters in a little town in Germany when Armaros attacked Earth using the Spear of Longinus . Just like other millions of people around the world, she was also turned into salt by the particles in the sky.

Shinkalion

Hikari and her sisters, Kodama and Nozomi, cameo in episode 31 of the kids train mecha TV anime Shinkansen Henkei Robo Shinkalion THE ANIMATION . They give a car ride to main Shinkalion protagonist Hayato Hayasugi. The cameo is notable for being Kodama and Nozomi's first on-screen appearances in any Evangelion -related media. Junko Iwao, Hikari's voice actress in the Evangelion TV anime, voices her and her sisters during the episode.
