Tetsuya Iwanaga (岩永 哲哉) is a voice actor who provides the voice for Kensuke Aida in the Neon Genesis Evangelion TV Series. He also provides the voice for Keita Asari from the Girlfriend of Steel video game. As of April 1, 2009, he is affiliated with maxmix.

Puella Magi Madoka Magica (2011) as Tomohisa Kaname
