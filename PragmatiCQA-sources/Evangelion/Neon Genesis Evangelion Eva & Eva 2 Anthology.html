Neon Genesis Evangelion: Eva & Eva 2 Anthology ( 新世紀エヴァンゲリオンエヴァ&エヴァ2アンソロジー , " Shin Seiki Evangerion: Eva & Eva 2 anthology ") is a manga, Illustration and essays anthology series based on the Neon Genesis Evangelion and Neon Genesis Evangelions . It is compiled into a single volume and it was published by Kadokawa Shoten in April 27, 2004. Various collaborators featured in would create other Evangelion spinoffs later like Osamu Takahashi and Tony Takezaki.
