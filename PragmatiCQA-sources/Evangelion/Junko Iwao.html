Junko Iwao (岩男 潤子 Iwao Junko, born February 18, 1970 in Beppu, Ōita Prefecture) is a Japanese voice actress who has also released several CD albums as a J-pop singer. Her most notable role is in Cardcaptor Sakura providing the voice to Sakura's best friend, Tomoyo.
