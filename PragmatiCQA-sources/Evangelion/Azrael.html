Azrael ( アズラエル ) is an Angel that is exclusive in the Neon Genesis Evangelion RPG: Decisive Battle in Tokyo-3 RPG game.

Profile

This angel has physical characteristics that make it different from others. The shape of it's body is very reminiscent of a sperm. In the lower part it has a flagellum, which seems to be formed by several masks of Sachiel joined together. In the upper part is the structure called "head", which has several cavities similar to craters. Its core lies exposed in the middle part of the body, between the flagellum and the head, being protected by 6 ribs that surround it. This angel makes a powerful attack by manipulating the force of gravity.
