Evangelion RPG ~Angel Raising Project~ ( エヴァンゲリオンRPG～使徒育成計画～ , " Evangerion RPG ~ shito ikusei keikaku ~ ") is a video game based on the Neon Genesis Evangelion TV Series. It was released exclusively on Japan on the I App and V App mobile phone series in September 1, 2006. It was developed by Gainax and Tin Machine Inc. The service is no longer available. The monthly fee was 315 yen per month (with tax included).

Overview

"Evangelion RPG ~Angel Raising Project~" is a dungeon exploration action RPG. The "Angel Experimental Body" that appears as an enemy in the dungeon area can be captured by using an item called "Capture Unit". The captured "Angel Experimental Body" can be made allies by taking it back to the breeding facility. You can nurture and grow the "Angel Experimental Body" that you have allied with. You can also create new angels by synthesizing multiple fellow angels.
