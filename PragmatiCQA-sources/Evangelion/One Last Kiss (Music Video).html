One Last Kiss is a of the song of same name by Hikaru Utada . It was directed by Hideaki Anno . and released on the Hikaru Utada official YouTube channel in March 9, 2021. The song was used as the main theme of the film Evangelion: 3.0+1.0 Thrice Upon A Time .
