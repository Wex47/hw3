|game= Secret of Evangelion Mitsuru Wakatake ( 若岳ミツル ) is an original character from Secret of Evangelion game.

Profile

An engineer belonging to the first section of the NERV Technology Development Department. He is a members of a group who are dissatisfied with the treatment within NERV. He was in charge of sending a threatening letter to the children, but was discovered, and he was killed by suffocation.
