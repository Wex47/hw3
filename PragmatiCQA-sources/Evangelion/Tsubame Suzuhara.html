Tsubame Suzuhara ( 鈴原ツバメ , Suzuhara Tsubame) is a fictional character that appears in the movie Evangelion: 3.0+1.0 Thrice Upon A Time . She is the daughter of Hikari Suzuhara and Tōji Suzuhara . Sakura Suzuhara , Kodama Horaki and Nozomi Horaki are her aunts. She is the granddaughter of Bunzaemon Horaki .

Appearance

Tsubame is a chubby baby with short brown hair and rosy cheeks. She wears striped pants, a white robe with an adult size blue vest over it. An orange scarf with the remains of her vomit and saliva rests over her clothes.

History

Evangelion: 3.0+1.0 Thrice Upon A Time

During Rei Ayanami (tentative name)'s stay with Hikari and Toji, Tsubame took a liking to her. Rei in turn found the baby curious and helped Hikari take care of her. Upon Rei's final moments, Tsubame seemed to have sensed it, as it shows her crying the moment Rei Ayanami (tentative name) disolved into LCL .

She is last seen with her parents as the waves of Failures of Infinity crashes against the barrier set inside the Village 3 .

Trivia

  • Tsubame is named after the Tsubame trains at service in the Kyushu Shinkansen .
