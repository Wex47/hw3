Secret of Evangelion ( シークレット オブ エヴァンゲリオン , " Shīkuretto Obu Evangerion ") is a video game first released in December 21, 2006 for the PlayStation 2. It is based around the events of TV series and the movie The End of Evangelion . It follows the original character Kyoya Kenzaki discovering secrets of the Human Instrumentality Project and other events regarding Evangelion Unit-04 . Depending on the the choices made, there are 10 different endings.
