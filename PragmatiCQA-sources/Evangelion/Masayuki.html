Masayuki (摩砂雪, birthname : Masayuki Yamaguchi) was an episode director for Neon Genesis Evangelion as well as being credited with storyboard work and the animation of the series ending sequence. Aside from Evangelion , Masayuki was the character designer for the Macross Plus series, has worked on several other Gainax works such as FLCL , Diebuster (Aim for the Top 2), and Tengen Toppa Gurren-Lagann . He helped with directing Evangelion 1.0 , 2.0 and 3.0 .

Masayuki also produced the "Full-Length Opening" and "Full-Length Ending" AMVs for the 01 Test Type DVD , and the AMVs on NGE MUSIC & REMIX DVD , which include:

  • ASUKA : Tamashii no Refrain (Soul Refrain)
  • REI : Tengoku no Kioku (Memories of Paradise)
  • MISATO : Thanatos -If I Can't Be Yours-
