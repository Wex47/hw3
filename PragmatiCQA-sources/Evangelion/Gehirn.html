Gehirn ( ゲヒルン , " Gehirun ") ( German : brain ) is NERV 's predecessor located outside NERV Headquarters and tasked with research and development of the Evangelion units via Project E, and the Magi , amongst other things. Branch 3 is located in Germany. Two other branches are located in the USA, and one probably in China. A minor branch is located in Matsushiro, Japan. Many of its facilities and members (including prominently Gendo Ikari , Yui Ikari , Kōzō Fuyutsuki , Kyoko Zeppelin Sohryu , and Ritsuko Akagi ) would become NERV personnel later. It was converted into NERV shortly after Naoko Akagi's death and the MAGI were declared operational; moving from it's original location to the inside of what, today, is NERV HQ. Shinji Ikari , Ritsuko Akagi , and Misato Katsuragi would later see a "graveyard" filled with Gehirn's Failed Evangelion Prototypes within Terminal Dogma , of which line of research Unit-00 and later Evangelions would be made from the collected results of.
