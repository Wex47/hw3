Makoto Hyuga ( 日向 マコト , " Hyūga Makoto ") is a fictional character from the Neon Genesis Evangelion franchise. He is a First Lieutenant at NERV and one of the three main computer technicians directly serving under Commanders Ikari and Fuyutsuki in the command post of Central Dogma , the others being Aoba and Ibuki .

  • 1 Profile
  • 2 In other media

Profile

Hyuga at first gives the impression of being something of an otaku, reading manga while on his break time, but is a highly skilled technician. His quick thinking and bravery help save NERV Headquarters from the attack by Matarael , and he acts as a spy for Misato , helping her obtain information regarding NERV's machinations. The true extent of his feelings for Misato is shown in The End of Evangelion as Rei / Lilith takes the form of Misato during Instrumentality and passionately kisses him; his body reverts to LCL .

In other media

Neon Genesis Evangelion: ANIMA

In Neon Genesis Evangelion: ANIMA , Hyuga is in the combat control section of the newly formed NERV JAPAN .

Makoto Hyuga ( 日向マコト , " Hyūga Makoto ") is a fictional character from the Rebuild of Evangelion movie series. He is a First Lieutenant at NERV and one of the three main computer technicians directly serving under Commanders Ikari and Fuyutsuki in the command post of Central Dogma , the others being Shigeru Aoba and Maya Ibuki .

Contents

  • 1 Profile
  • 2 Story
  • 3 Notes

Profile

Hyuga is a highly skilled technician when working at his post. After 14 years since Near Third Impact, he looks older in person and his hairline is shown to have been receded.

Story

In the first two Rebuild of Evangelion movies, the story of Hyuga is the same as the original series with minor changes in 2.0 . So far, he does not have a crush or romantic feelings for Misato like in NGE.

In 3.0 Hyuga is now a member of WILLE , fighting NERV alongside his colleagues under the command of Misato Katsuragi aboard the AAA Wunder . He manifests a grudge against Shinji the first time he sees him after the fourteen years timeskip following the Near Third Impact , probably under the influence of Misato.
