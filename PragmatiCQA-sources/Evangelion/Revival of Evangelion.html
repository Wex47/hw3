Revival of Evangelion ( 新世紀エヴァンゲリオン劇場版 , " Revival of Evangelion ") Revival of Evangelion was released March 8, 1998 and is a unification of the 1997 Neon Genesis Evangelion movies Death(true)² (largely the same as Death(true) , except with the re-addition of the image of Adam on Gendo 's hand and several other minor edits) and The End of Evangelion . It is considered the final form of the Evangelion movies, which were released in several forms prior to Revival. The two were released on the ninth and tenth discs of the Renewal of Evangelion box set with the labels of Revival of Evangelion and Evangelion - The Feature Film .
