Koji Katori ( 香取コウジ ) is an original character from Secret of Evangelion game.

Profile

An engineer belonging to the first section of the NERV Technology Development Department. He is a members of a group who are dissatisfied with the treatment within NERV. He was in charge of stealing the dummy plug activation code, but was discovered and killed.
