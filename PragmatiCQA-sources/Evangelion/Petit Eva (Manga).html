Petit Eva ( ぷちえう゛ぁ ) is a 4koma manga series based on the Neon Genesis Evangelion written and illustrated by Ryusuke Hamamoto. It was compiled into 2 volumes. It was published by Kadokawa Shoten in October 25, 2008 and December 26, 2009.
