Matsukaze Nene ( 松風ネネ ) is an original character from Neon Genesis Evangelion Gaiden Vol. 2 ~ Feast of Dolls ~ visual novel game. She is a transfer student that moved in 2-A class. She always carries a teddy bear, which she believes the spirit "Sandy-san" lives inside it.

Trivia

  • Her character design is based on a contest winner sent idea.
  • Her blood type is O.
  • Her surname, , comes from the Japanese destroyer Matsukaze of the Japanese Imperial Navy.
