Adovaquiel ( アドヴァキエル ) is an Angel that is exclusive in the Neon Genesis Evangelion RPG: Decisive Battle in Tokyo-3 RPG game.

Profile

This angel is similar to a gigantic jellyfish. Its head has the shape of a bell, and many eyes are all over in it. From the lower part of the head hangs a structure with a rhomboidal shape, and several tentacles start at its side. Its core is not visible, possibly being inside it.

In addition to having a standard Absolute Terror Field , this angel attacks using its tentacles to catch its enemies and launch them. You can also perform an attack with high-pressure energy bullets.
