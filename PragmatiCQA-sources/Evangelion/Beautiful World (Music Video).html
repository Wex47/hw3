Beautiful World (ビューティフル・ワールド) is a of the song of same name by Hikaru Utada . It was directed by Kazuya Tsurumaki and released on the official in december 2, 2014. The song was used as the main theme of the film Evangelion: 1.0 You Are (Not) Alone .
