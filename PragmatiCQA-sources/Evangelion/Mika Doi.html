Mika Doi (土井 美加 Doi Mika), married name Mika Itō (伊藤 美加 Itō Mika, born on August 4, 1956) is a veteran voice actress who was born in Sendai.

A selection of other characters voiced by Mika Doi

  • Aura Battler Dunbine as Marvel Frozen
  • Cowboy Bebop as Arisa
  • Genesis Climber Mospeada as Houquet Erose
  • Kiddy Grade as Eclipse
  • Mickey Mouse franchise as Daisy Duck (only on the Japanese dub)
  • Rurouni Kenshin as Megumi Takani
  • Sailor Moon (and Sailor Moon R) as Queen Serenity
  • SDF Macross as Misa Hayase
