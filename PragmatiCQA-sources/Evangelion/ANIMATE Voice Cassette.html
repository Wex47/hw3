ANIMATE VOICE CASSETTE ( アニメイト ボイス カセット 04 新世紀 エヴァンゲリオン シンジ＆アスカ編 , " ANIMATE VOICE CASSETTE 04: Shin Seiki Evangelion Shinji & Asuka Version ") is an audio drama published by Movic in 1996. Side A has a runtime of 13:31 minutes and it focuses on Shinji Ikari . Side B has a runtime of 17:57 minutes and it focuses on Asuka Langley Sohryu .

It features the characters, voiced by Megumi Ogata and Yuko Miyamura , recording messages to the listener, like a "good morning" and "happy birthday" message, while the other comments on the messages, and they discuss each other and some events of the series that happened between them. It is a lighthearted humorous recording.

Despite the "04" number, this is actually part of a multi-franchise series by Movic, and the "Shinji & Asuka Version" is the only release related to Evangelion.
