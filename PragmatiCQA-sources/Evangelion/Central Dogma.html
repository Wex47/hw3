Central Dogma is the codename for the primary subsection of the NERV Headquarters building complex. NERV 's primary Command Center room is located inside, though there is also a backup command center located several levels down. The Magi supercomputers are housed in Central Dogma.

Notes

  • In real life, the Central Dogma of molecular biology deals with the transfer of DNA information within a cell: DNA is transcribed into RNA, which is in turn translated (at ribosomes) into amino acid sequences that construct proteins, which are the main structural constituent of living cells. Put more simply, "DNA to RNA to Proteins".
