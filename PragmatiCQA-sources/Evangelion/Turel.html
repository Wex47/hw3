Turel ( テゥレル ) is a conceptual Angel which was never used, only appearing in Neon Genesis Evangelion Proposal . It is designed as a floating white "monolith" with an appearance like a face crowned by four spikes and wearing a very tall, steep miter adorned with a blue gem. It is extremely powerful, seems impervious to all attacks, and steadfastly seeks out its targets with the aim of self-destruction.

Trivia

  • Given its combat style involving seeking out enemies and self destructing, it's possible Turel was used as inspiration for Sahaquiel , being another aerial Angel whose ultimate goal involved its own demise.
