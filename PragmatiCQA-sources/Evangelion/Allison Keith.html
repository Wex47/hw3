Allison Keith is an American actor and voice actor who is best known for her English-dubbing work with ADV Films on such anime movies and television series as Neon Genesis Evangelion , in which she voiced the character Misato Katsuragi . She majored in theater at the University of Houston.

Recently, she reprised her role as Misato Katsuragi in the first two Rebuild of Evangelion films for FUNimation Entertainment and has been doing work at Seraphim Digital.

Other Roles

Some (but not all) of Allison Keith's roles include:

  • All Purpose Cultural Cat Girl Nuku Nuku as Nuku Nuku
  • Blue Seed as Sakura Yamazaki
  • The Book of Bantorra as Ia, Old Woman
  • Strange Love as Azumi Yamada
  • Dirty Pair Flash as Lilly
  • .hack//Legend of the Twilight as Rena
