EVANGELION: 3.333 Breakdown is a video included in the home release of Evangelion: 3.333 You Can (Not) Redo . It was released in August 25, 2021. It is a making-of video that breaks down and reconstructs the filming and special effects work process in EVANGELION:3.333, which was included in the special features disc of the blu-ray release . The video was also published for free in in August 17, 2021.
