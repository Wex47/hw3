Neon Genesis Evangelion RPG: The NERV White Paper ( 新世紀エヴァンゲリオンRPG NERV白書 ) is a card-based role-playing game written by Mitsuhiro Nakazawa released by Kadokawa Shoten on April 20, 1996. The set consists of 138 pages dedicated to explaining the rules and various story inserts, plus 20 pages of cut-out cards for playing. A map of Tokyo-3 that serves as the game board is also included in the set.

This game introduces the new pilot Marie Vincennes and two new Angels not included in the series: Baraqijal and Iblis .
