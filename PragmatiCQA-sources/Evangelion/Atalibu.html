Atalibu ( アタリブ , " Ataribu ") is an Angel that is exclusive in the Neon Genesis Evangelion RPG: Decisive Battle in Tokyo-3 RPG game.

Profile

His body has an exotic, vaguely anthropomorphic form. Its thoracic region is very thin, and its arms are long with huge and sharp claws. Atalibu has only one eye located above his chest. It has a very wide hip. Their legs are thin at the bottom and they widen considerably on the thighs. Its core lies exposed in the region of the solar plexus.

It has the ability to expand its Absolute Terror Field , manipulating the properties of the environment and generating cold air around it.
