Fumihiko Tachiki (立木 文彦 Tachiki Fumihiko, born April 29, 1961 in Nagasaki Prefecture) is a Japanese voice actor and narrator. Formerly a blues singer and known for his low voice, Tachiki is best known as the narrator for the pre-fight movies for Pride Fighting Championships and currently for Dream (Japanese broadcasts only.)

In 2003, he teamed up with Toshiyuki Morikawa to form the band "2HEARTS", one of their works being the opening theme of Blue Seed (under the name "Takada Band").

As part of the band "2HEARTS" with Toshiyuki Morikawa, one of their songs was featured as an ending theme for the video game "Dynasty Warriors 4: Empires"
