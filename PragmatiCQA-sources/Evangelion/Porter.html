Porter ( ポーター ) is an original character from Secret of Evangelion game.

Profile

Belongs to the special agency NERV Security Intelligence Department. Like Hawk , he is a subordinate of Kyoya Kenzaki , and the name "Porter" is not his real name. Unlike Hawk, he does not betray Kyoya. He is a competent worker who can perform his duties properly.
