Miki Nagasawa (長沢 美樹 Nagasawa Miki, born July 11, 1970) is a female Japanese voice actress born in Hokkaidō and raised in Fukushima. Formerly affiliated for Haikyo, she is now affiliated with Atomic Monkey.
