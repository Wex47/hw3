Neon Genesis Evangelion: Shinji Ikari Raising Project ( 新世紀エヴァンゲリオン 碇シンジ育成計画 , " Shin Seiki Evangerion: Ikari Shinji Ikusei Keikaku ") is a manga series loosely adapting the video game of the same name . It was written and drawn by Osamu Takahashi (高橋脩). It was serialised in Shōnen Ace and published in 18 tankobon volumes by Kadokawa. It was serialized in the same magazine publication as the Evangelion manga by Yoshiyuki Sadamoto .

The manga diverges from the original Neon Genesis Evangelion, being an erotic romantic comedy full with tropes of the genre.

Story

The manga, much like Neon Genesis Evangelion: Angelic Days , is similar to the alternate reality seen at the end of Episode:26 . However, it is different from both, showing aspects of the Evangelion world like NERV and other characters related to it.

The series follows Shinji, Rei and Asuka as they go through their high school life while helping out with research at the Artificial Evolution Research Lab , headed by Shinji's parents, Yui and Gendo. Meanwhile, SEELE plans to disrupt the Artificial Evolution Research Lab's experiments in order to reach its goals.

Characters

The characters of Shinji Ikari Raising Project are alternate versions of characters in the anime series .
