Satsuki Ooi ( 大井サツキ , " Ōi Satsuki ") is an original character that appears in the video game Neon Genesis Evangelion: Shinji Ikari Raising Project .

Profile

Technician responsible of the Casper . She is in charge of Shinji's sensitivity training program. She is half Russian and half Japanese, she also enjoys drinking vodka.
