"Liar" is a song by American recording artist Britney Spears , taken from her ninth full length album, Glory .

Background

On May 10, 2016, "Liar", along with " Clumsy ", were registered in ASCAP databases under Britney's portfolio. Britney wanted Liar to be track 12 but it was replaced with “ What You Need ”.

Trivia

  • Sometime in 2020 a producers demo of the song was leaked containing backing vocals to the official song. The demo was later found out to be sang by Livvi Franc.
  • It is one of the few songs by Spears which has her deep/natural singing voice. This can be heard in the background vocals.
