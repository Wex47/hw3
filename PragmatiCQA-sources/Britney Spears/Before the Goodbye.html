" Before the Goodbye " is a song by American recording artist Britney Spears , taken from her third full length studio album, Britney . The song is a bonus track on the album.

Background

Before the Goodbye was originally meant to be the lead single but due to it being unfinished it was later demoted to a bonus track.

Trivia

  • Due to Britney only recording the chorus, first and second verse the song had to be repeated. Britney's team then turned it into a more remix song to help mask up the unfinished parts.
