" Outta This World " is a song by American recording artist Britney Spears , taken from her fifth full length album, Blackout . The song is a bonus track on the album.

Background

"Outta This World" was originally titled "A Song About You". It was originally going to have a spoken intro by writer Keri Hilson , which was in the lyrics sheet included with the Japanese version of Blackout . This spoken intro was possibly part of the original demo.

“ I know you hear me on your radio / well here we go / please turn up your radio / wanna tell you somethin' ”

—Keri Hilson, The Japanese lyrics sheet included with Blackout
