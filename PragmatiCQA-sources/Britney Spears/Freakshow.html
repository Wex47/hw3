" Freakshow " is a song by American recording artist Britney Spears , taken from her fifth full length studio album, Blackout .

Background

An instrumental version of the song leaked on Bloodshy & Avant 's website a year before Blackout was released. At the time, there was no indication that it would end up on the album a year later. It is also the shortest song off the album.
