Welcome To Me is an unreleased song by Britney Spears . It's written by Britney Spears, Carole Sager and Wade Robson. It was intended for her third studio album Britney but failed to make the final cut.

Background

A 6-second snippet leaked on October 6, 2011 under the title " Love ". The full demo was leaked on May 11, 2014.
