Impossible is an unreleased demo for Britney Spears performed by Ne-Yo, it was written by Ne-Yo and Shea Taylor, intented for her fifth studio album Blackout , but didn't make the cut.

Background

Demo pitch performed by Ne-Yo has leaked online

Later given to and released by Tiffany Evans on her 2008 self-titled album

This song, with Question Existing , Happily Never After and Good Girl Gone Bad , were songs that Ne-Yo presented for Britney, but none was recorded.

Despite this, it is said that Britney chose Happily Never After and Good Girl Gone Bad to record them, but according to Ne-Yo, she never showed up to record them, which annoyed him.
