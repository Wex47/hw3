" Passenger " is a song by American recording artist Britney Spears , taken from her eighth full length studio album, Britney Jean .

Background

The song could have been intended for Katy Perry's fourth studio album, Prism (2013). On June 18, 2017, a studio version of Perry's demo of the song was leaked on MusicMafia. The untagged version leaked on SoundCloud on January 20, 2018.

Britney Spears told MTV how she ended up collaborating with Katy Perry, saying, "we actually didn't get in the studio together. My people and her people worked it out, but I'm a huge fan of hers. I think she's an amazing vocalist and she has a beautiful voice." She then added, "And I love the song. From the moment I heard the song, I was like, 'This is really beautiful.' So I was really proud I was able to do it."
