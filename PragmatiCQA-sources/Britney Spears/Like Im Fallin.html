Like I'm Fallin' known alternatively as "Fallin'" is an unreleased song by Britney Spears . It's written by Britney Spears and Michelle Bell. Produced by Bloodshy & Avant. It was originally intended for her fourth studio album In The Zone but was also being considered for Original Doll at the time.

Background

It's registered on ASCAP. Songwriter Michelle Bell said it has a country sound.
