Dangerous is an unreleased song by Britney Spears . It's written by Nicole Morier and Greg Kurstin. It was intended for her sixth studio album, Circus , but didn't make the cut.

Background

This song leaked in 2011. "Rock The Boat" a song by Japanese girl group Momoiro Clover Z sampled the instrumental.
