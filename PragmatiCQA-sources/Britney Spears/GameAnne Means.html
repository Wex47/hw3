Anne Means is Britney Spears 's manager, and currently your manager, in the Britney Spears: American Dream game.

Background & Trivia

Britney Spears introduces you to Anne in the beginning, and Anne offers to give you an audition as a favor to Britney. Once you audition, she agrees to represent you.
