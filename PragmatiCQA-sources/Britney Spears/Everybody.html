" Everybody " is a song by American recording artist Britney Spears , taken from her fifth full length album, Blackout . The song is a bonus track on the album. It features a sample of the Eurythmics ' song " Sweet Dreams (Are Made of This) ", written by Annie Lennox and Dave Stewart .

Background

The Cheetah Girls sang the demo of "Everybody". It is rumored that Britney herself made changes to the lyrics, however, she was uncredited on the track. The song was originally intended for Rihanna 's second album, A Girl Like Me . After writer/producer J.R. Rotem began working with Britney, he decided that it should be on Blackout instead.
