See My Side is a unreleased demo for Britney Spears, it was written by Robyn, Klas Ahlund, Pontus Winnberg and Bloodshy, it was intented for her fifth studio album Blackout , but it was rejected and not recorded.

Background

It was given and released by Jordin Sparks for her 2007 self-titled album.

Britney almost record this song, but when she was reading the lyrics before recording it, she begin to cry and decided not to record it.
