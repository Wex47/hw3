King Of My Castle is an unreleased song by Britney Spears & Shayne Ward. It's written by Chris Brann, Dave Lambert, Carsten Mortensen & Lucas Secon. It was intended for The Singles Collection but didn't make the cut.

Background

It was revealed in a podcast with Lucas Secon that the song was recorded in 2009 for the singles collection. The song was released by Shayne Ward featuring JPearl in 2011 as "Must Be a Reason Why". Britney's version leaked on June 25, 2021.
