"Hold on Tight " is a song by American recording artist Britney Spears , taken from her eighth full length studio album, Britney Jean . The song is a bonus track on the album.

Background

On the back cover of early deluxe pressings of Britney Jean , " Hold on Tight " was misprinted as " Hold On Tite ". This was later corrected on the Japanese edition and the Chinese edition of the album.
