Dance Your Fuck Off also known as DYFO is an unreleased song by Britney Spears . It was written by Britney Spears, Nicole Morier, Vincent Pontare and CJ Baran. It was produced by Max Martin and CJ Baran for Britney's ninth studio album Glory but didn't make the cut.

Background

The song was recorded in 2014. Nicole Morier's demo and Britney's version have both leaked online. The first version of the track leaked December 8th, 2021 with bridge vocals by Nicole Morier, the solo version leaked May 26th, 2022.
