Dramatic is an unreleased song by Britney Spears . It was written by Britney Spears and Damon Elliot. It was recorded in 2006 for her fifth studio album that would eventually become Blackout but didn't make the cut.

Background

In March 2008, an unofficial duet version with Heidi Montag was sent to Ryan Seacrest, who premiered it on his show On Air with Ryan Seacrest . Both Jive Records and Montag's representatives denied having knowledge of the recording. The official version leaked in 2010.
