" Big Fat Bass " is a song by American recording artist Britney Spears , taken from her seventh full length studio album, Femme Fatale . It features American rapper and producer will.i.am.

Background

"Big Fat Bass" was written and produced by will.i.am . Spears recorded her vocals for the song at Ethernet Studios in Los Angeles, California and at The Record Plant in Hollywood, California. On February 8, 2011, Spears tweeted she was in the studio with the producer, recording a song for Femme Fatale . Later, will.i.am described the collaboration as "a monster. It's mean, pretty, edgy, next level. [...] She's singing fresh over it. It's something that today needs," and unveilead a teaser of the song through a blog message on February 28, 2011. In an interview with Rolling Stone , Spears commented that she is a fan of The Black Eyed Peas , and would love to work with will.i.am again in the future. will.i.am also praised Spears for the song, saying, "Thank you Britney for collaborating it was a pleasure working with you. Thank you for trusting my instincts. You're a doll.

Live Performances

On March 25, 2011, Spears made a special show at Rain Night Club in Las Vegas, Nevada. The setlist of the show consisted of three songs from her seventh studio album, Femme Fatale , including " Hold It Against Me ", "Big Fat Bass" and " Till the World Ends ". The performance of "Big Fat Bass" consisted on Spears emerging

from a speaker box and dancing to a remix version of the song, which included the chorus of " 3 " as a medley, and a few elements of " Gimmie More " and " I'm a Slave 4 U" . The singer was wearing a latex bodysuit during the performance. The same remix was later performed on March 27, 2011, on a pre-taped Good Morning America special concert that aired on March 29, 2011. The same day, Spears performed the song on Jimmy Kimmel Live! "Big Fat Bass" was also performed on 2011's Femme Fatale Tour . Spears was wearing a pink latex and lace jacket and emerged from inside a bass to perform the song, while will.i.am appeared in the backdrops.
