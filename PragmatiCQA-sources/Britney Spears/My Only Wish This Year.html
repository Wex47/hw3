My Only Wish (This Year) is a song by Britney Spears from Jive Records ' compilation album Platinum Christmas . It was released as a promotional single on November 14, 2000.

Background

On October 13, 2000, American publishing and media company Forbes reported that Spears had plans to record a song called "My Only Wish (This Year)" for a Christmas album titled Platinum Christmas . The song was included in the album, which was released on November 14, 2000. Since its release, the song has been included in more than eight Christmas music compilations, including Now That's What I Call Christmas! (2001), Super Christmas Hits (2006) and Christmas Top 100 (2009). The song didn't have a music video.
