" Scream & Shout " is a song recorded by will.i.am and Britney Spears for will.i.am 's fourth studio album #willpower although the official single cover credits the song to will.i.am and Britney Spears.

Background

The song was written by will.i.am, Jef Martens and Jean Baptiste and produced by Lazy Jay, with additional production by will.i.am. After RCA Records executive vice president of promotions announced that Spears was going to take a break in 2012, the singer signed a contract to be a judge on the second season of American music competition show The X Factor. A few months later, she announced that she had collaborated with will.i.am on a new track; they worked together the previous year on two tracks for Femme Fatale, with "Big Fat Bass" making the final cut. Initially rumored to be called "Sexy Sexy" and set to be released on October 15, 2012, will.i.am pushed back the release date following unauthorized leaks of the album. "Scream & Shout" was then confirmed as a single, premiering on US and UK radio on November 19, 2012. The song was sent to mainstream radio in the United States through Interscope Records on November 27, 2012.
