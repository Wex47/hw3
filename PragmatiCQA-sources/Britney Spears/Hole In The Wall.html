Hole In The Wall is an unreleased demo for Britney Spears . It's written by Cathy Dennis, Taylor Momsen & Christopher Rojas. It's rumoured to be a song for her scrapped studio album Original Doll .

Background

"Hole in The Wall" is referenced in the Do Somethin' music video as the name of the club. There's a song registered in ASCAP with Taylor Momsen from The Pretty Reckless as a co-writer.
