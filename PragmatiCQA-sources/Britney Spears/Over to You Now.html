" Over to You Now " is a song by American recording artist Britney Spears , taken from her debut extended play, Britney & Kevin: Chaotic . The song is a bonus track on the EP.

Background

The song was recorded in 2003. Although the song could be about an exclusive club, It's speculated to be play on words about anal sex.
