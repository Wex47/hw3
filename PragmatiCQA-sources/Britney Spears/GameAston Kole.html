Aston Kole is an A-List singer, a bitch and your rival in the Britney Spears: American Dream game.

Background

In the beginning, you are asked to perform at the Venom Room instead of Aston, because she's a no-show. You do very well, and when you leave, Aston confronts you and sasses you, telling you to "dress for your funeral". She becomes your main rival in the game.

Later, a rumor spreads that you use auto-tune and get a computer to modify your singing, or sing for you. You shut down these rumors by performing live and unplugged, and Aston admits to have started the rumor, with zero regret.

She has tried to undermine you and ruin your career several times by getting in your head, and she's a BAD person.

Notable events

  • Aston tries to steal the love song that you're supposed to sing the hook for.
  • Aston spreads a rumor that you use autotune, and names you 'Robo-Flop'.
