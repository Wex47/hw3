Rockstar is an unreleased song by Britney Spears . It's written by Britney Spears, Dorian Michelle Hardnett, Thabiso Nkhereanye & Christopher Stewart. It was intended for her fourth studio album In The Zone but didn't make the cut.

Background

The song was written as a response to the Limp Bizkit's song ”Just Drop Dead" (2003). The song leaked in 2011.
