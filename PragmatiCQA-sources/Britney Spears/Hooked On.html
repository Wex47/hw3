Hooked On is an unreleased song by Britney Spears featuring Pharrell Williams. It was recorded in 2006 for her fifth studio album Blackout but didn't make the cut.

Background

The song is known alternatively as " Sugarfall ". It leaked in 2007, eight days before the release of Blackout . The final mix leaked in April 2021.
