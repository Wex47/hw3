" Hot as Ice " (originally titled " Cold as Fire ") is a song by American recording artist Britney Spears , taken from her fifth full length album, Blackout .

Background

"Hot as Ice" was originally titled "Cold as Fire". After an early demo leak of the song, the title was changed in order to try to stop the spread of unreleased material. The two versions of the song have differences in the vocals. While T-Pain was promoting his album Epiphany , he was frequently asked about his confirmed involvement with Spears on Blackout . During a "TRL" interview, he hinted that "Hot as Ice" would become a lead single from the album.
