" How I Roll " is a song by American recording artist Britney Spears , taken from her seventh full length studio album, Femme Fatale .

Background

How I Roll is an mid-tempo dance-pop, synth-pop, bubblegum pop and R&B song with elements of electropop and dream-pop.
