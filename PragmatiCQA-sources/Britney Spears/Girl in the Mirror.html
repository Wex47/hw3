" Girl in the Mirror " is a song by American recording artist Britney Spears , taken from her second full length album, Oops!... I Did It Again . The song is included on the international version of the album. It was released in the U.S. on the McDonald's exclusive CD *NSYNC and Britney Spears: Your #1 Requests...And More!
