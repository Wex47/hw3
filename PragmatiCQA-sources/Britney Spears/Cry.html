Cry a.k.a. Tear is an unreleased song by Britney Spears . It's written by Britney Spears, Kara DioGuardi & J.R. Rotem. It was intended for her fifth studio album Blackout but didn't make the cut. It’s rumoured to be one of the last songs cut

Background

Britney's former publicist said that Britney recorded this song in 2007. In a interview they said... "My answer is that I heard 5 of Britney's recorded songs and they all sound like Britney, but more grown-up. Now I warn you if "Tear" makes the cut no one will listen to that song without crying."

According to People.com , Britney was supposed to debut the song at the 2007 MTV Awards with Gimme More but was replaced with the Trouble cover.
