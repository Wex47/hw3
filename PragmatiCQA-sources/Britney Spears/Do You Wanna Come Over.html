Audio

" Do You Wanna Come Over? " is a song by American recording artist Britney Spears . It severed as the third promotional single from her ninth full length studio album, Glory . It was released on August 18, 2016, by RCA Records .

Background

On August 15, 2016, Britney confirmed the song via Twitter.

On August 16, 2016, websites such as iTunes and Amazon confirmed the track number and length.

It was released as the third promotional single on August 18, 2016. Many fans quickly tweeted that it was their favorite release from the album so far, making the song an instant fan favorite.
