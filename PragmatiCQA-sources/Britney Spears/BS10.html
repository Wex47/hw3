BS10 is Britney's tenth studio album. Not much is known about this album yet. On December 27, 2021, Spears announced via Instagram that she's quitting the music industry but later Britney hinted in a caption of a music comeback in a caption saying " New song is in the works , I'm gonna let you know what I mean ."

On 02/02/2022, Britney posted a caption on Instagram thanking her lawyer for respecting and freeing her. She also went to discuss "exciting projects".

Background

In November 2016, during an interview with Las Vegas Blog, Spears confirmed she had already begun work on her next album, stating: "I’m not sure what I want the next album to sound like. [...] I just know that I’m excited to get into the studio again and actually have already been back recording."

In 2017, Grammy-nominated producer RoccStar , teased an unknown hip-hop demo track for B10 in the studio. captioning it "La Britney Spears". It's likely it's been submitted for the album but little is known of this song and its song title.

In January 2018, Justin Tranter who had previously worked with Spears on her previous album Glory had told Billboard that this album is full of bops.

Danja (Producer of "Gimme More", "Break The Ice" and "Get Naked (I Got A Plan)") is working on B10 with apparent confirmation on twitter by unveiling 4 titles of B10 songs.

In November 2019, Spears requested alternatives for the production team to work on B10 in the future.

On February 4, 2021, Danja previewed the demo " Welcome To The Jungle " on Instagram live without label consent which suggests that the song won't appear on B10 as Danja and Justin Tranter are no longer attached to the production team. Everything Justin Tranter, Julia Michaels, and Danja's worked on in the 2017-18 period were scrapped.

In December 2021, Britney hinted in a Instagram caption of a music comeback in a caption saying " New song is in the works , I'm gonna let you know what I mean ."

Scrapped 2018 Songs

There's no confirmed songs as of yet for the album. Four song titles were revealed by Danja, they were demos written and recorded by Julia Michaels, Justin Tranter and Danja and were submitted to RCA for B10 in 2018 but were eventually scrapped. Altogether Danja worked on eight songs for Spears . The other 4 titles are yet to be revealed.

  • Welcome To The Jungle : Written by Julia Michaels, Justin Tranter & Danja.
  • Demonstrate : Written by Julia Michaels, Justin Tranter & Danja.
  • Fuck What You Know : Written by Julia Michaels, Justin Tranter & Danja.
  • Marilyn Monroe : Written by Julia Michaels, Justin Tranter & Danja.

These song titles were revealed online, they're suspected to also been pitched for the album in 2018 but were also scrapped.

  • Beauty Is A Beast : Written by Melanie Fontana & Danja.
  • Be Yourself : Written by Melanie Fontana & Danja.
  • It's Not Enough : Written by Adam Soskin.
  • Malibu : Written by Adam Soskin.
  • Night Sweats : Justin Tranter, Rami Yacoub, Albin Nedler & Kristoffer Fogelmark.
  • Psychotic : Written by Julia Michaels, Justin Tranter, Rami Yacoub, Albin Nedler & Kristoffer Fogelmark.
  • Real Real : Justin Tranter, Rami Yacoub, Albin Nedler & Kristoffer Fogelmark.
  • Sequel : Justin Tranter, Rami Yacoub, Albin Nedler & Kristoffer Fogelmark.
