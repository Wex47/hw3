" Swimming in the Stars " is a song by American recording artist Britney Spears . It severed as the fourth single from her ninth full length studio album, Glory . It was released on December 2, 2020, by RCA Records .

Background

Urban Outfitters announced the pre-order release on November 11, 2020, through their website and on social media.
