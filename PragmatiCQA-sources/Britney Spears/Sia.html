Sia Kate Isobelle Furler (born December 18, 1975), referred to mononymously as Sia , is an Australian singer, songwriter, voice actress and director. She has written songs with Britney Spears such as Perfume , Passenger and Brightest Morning Star . Sia has also covered the 2007 single, Gimme More .
