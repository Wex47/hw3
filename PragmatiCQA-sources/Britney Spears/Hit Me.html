Hit Me is an unreleased song by Britney Spears . It's written by Britney Spears and Christopher "Notes" Olsen. Produced by Christopher "Notes" Olsen. It was intended for her fifth studio album Blackout but didn't make the cut.

Background

The song is registered with BMI and ASCAP with Britney as the performer. Notes is well known for producing Britney's controversial famous unreleased song Rebellion .
