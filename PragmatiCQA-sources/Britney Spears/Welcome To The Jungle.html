Welcome To The Jungle is a demo for Britney Spears written by Julia Michaels, Justin Tranter and Danja. It was intended for Britney's Tenth Studio Album . It's one of the 8 songs Danja has submitted to RCA for Britney to record in 2018.

Background

On February 4, 2021, Blackout producer Danja went live on Instagram and previewed the demo "Welcome To The Jungle" performed by Julia Michaels. Sonically it has a urban and grungy hip hop sound.
