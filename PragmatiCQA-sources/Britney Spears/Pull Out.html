Pull Out is an unreleased song by Britney Spears . It was recorded in 2006 for her fifth studio album Blackout but didn't make the cut.

Background

The song is known alternatively as " When U Gon Pull It " and " Pull It ". It leaked on October 17, 2007, along with other outtakes from Blackout .
