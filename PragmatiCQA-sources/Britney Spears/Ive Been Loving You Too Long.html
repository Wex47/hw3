I've Been Loving You Too Long is an unreleased cover song by Britney Spears .

Background

The original song is by Otis Redding and Jerry Butler from 1965. Britney's tribute was produced by Christopher "Notes" Olsen in 2006. The song leaked online on March 9th, 2019.
