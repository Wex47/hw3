" Overprotected " is a song by American recording artist Britney Spears . It severed as the second single from her third full length studio album, Britney , though the release excluded German speaking countries. It was released on November 12, 2001, by Jive Records .

Background

The song was written and produced by Max Martin and Rami Yacoub . Lyrically, the dance-pop song, is about a girl who is tired of being overprotected and just wants to be herself. It received mixed reviews from contemporary critics, as some reviewers noticed the song as way for Spears to "break free", despite coming from a singer that is known worldwide since childhood. A version of the song, "The Darkchild Remix", was released in the United States and Canada in April 2002.

Chart Performance

While the song did not perform well on the Billboard charts in the United States, the song peaked at number twenty-two in Canada, while reaching the top five in Italy, Romania, Sweden and United Kingdom.

Music Video

An accompanying music video, directed by Billie Woodruff , portrays Spears dancing inside an abandoned factory, while singing about being overprotected; the music video for "The Darkchild Remix", directed by Chris Applebaum , portrays Spears dancing and having fun with her friends.

Live Performances

Spears has performed "Overprotected" on her Dream Within a Dream Tour and The Onyx Hotel Tour . On the Dream Within a Dream Tour, she it while dressed in a brown catsuit. Its original version was performed on the first leg while its Darkchild remix was performed on the second leg. On the Onyx Hotel Tour, she performed the Darkchild remix of the song right after the show's opening number " Toxic " while dressed in a black catsuit. Spears also performed the song on Viva Interaktiv, EuroDisney and twice on Top of the Pops.
