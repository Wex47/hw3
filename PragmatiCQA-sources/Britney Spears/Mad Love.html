Mad Love is an unreleased song by Britney Spears . It's written by Lisa Greene, Brad Daymond, Alex Greggs, Rodney Price and Dennis Shaw. It was originally intended for her fifth studio album Blackout and was going to be reconsidered for Circus but didn't make the cut.

Background

The song leaked in 2010. It samples Lafayette Afro Rock Band's "Darkest Light".
