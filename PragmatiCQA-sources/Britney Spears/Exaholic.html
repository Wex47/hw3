" Exaholic " is an unreleased song by Britney Spears . It was written by Spears, Stephanie Jones, Joseph Spargur, Brad Tursi and produced by Rock Mafia around 2014 for Spears' ninth studio album Glory (2016). The unfinished demo leaked on February 2, 2021.
