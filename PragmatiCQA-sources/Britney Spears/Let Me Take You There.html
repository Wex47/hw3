Let Me Take You There is an unreleased song by Britney Spears which was meant to be featured on her debut album ...Baby One More Time , it was recorded in 1997 and was produced by Full Force .

Background

Let Me Take You There was part of a cassette tape that was put on auction on the site "Julien's Auctions" on May 3, 2018, by Britney's first boyfriend Reg Jones along with other personal items. The cassette would be sold by $2,560 on May 19, 2018 and some of the contents would be leaked half a month later on July 9, 2018. The leaked material included ''Let Me Take You There'' and alternate versions of Luv the Hurt Away and You Got It All and a short snippet of Britney's cover of I Have Nothing originally by Whitney Huston. The rest of the recordings on the cassette remain unleaked.
