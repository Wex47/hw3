Dem Chicks Be Like is an unreleased song by Britney Spears . It's written by Britney Spears and Sam Bruno. It was intended for Britney's ninth studio album Glory but didn't make the cut.

Background

Sam Bruno confirmed she was recording music with Britney in 2015 and she confirmed the title on Facebook in 2018. In November 2021, Sam Bruno said on Instagram the song is a "FIRE unreleased BRITNEY SMASH" and wants the fans to ask Britney to release it.
