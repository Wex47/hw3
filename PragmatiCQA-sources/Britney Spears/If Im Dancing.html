Audio

" If I'm Dancing " is a song by American recording artist Britney Spears , taken from her ninth full length album, Glory .

Background

On August 7, 2016, a fan asked Chantal Kreviazuk to describe her work with Britney, her answer was "rockandrolltoatee".

On August 14, 2016, Chantal described the song as "an all time fave of mine".
