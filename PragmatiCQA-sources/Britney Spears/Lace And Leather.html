" Lace and Leather " is a song by American recording artist Britney Spears , taken from her sixth full length studio album, Circus .

Background

"Lace and Leather" is an electro-funk track. This song also featured a then-unknown Kesha as a .

Live Performances

Spears performed "Lace and Leather" several times at the Femme Fatale Tour during 2011. It was the seventh track on tour's setlist. During each performance, Spears and her dancers selected a male member from the audience. Then, she bursted into "Lace and Leather" and performed sensually for him.
