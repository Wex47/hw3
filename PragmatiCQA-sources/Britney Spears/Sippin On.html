Sippin' On is an unreleased song by Britney Spears . It's written by Britney Spears, Tricky Stewart, Thabiso Nkhereanye & Penelope Magnet. It was intended for her fourth studio album In The Zone but didn't make the cut.

Original version

"Sippin' On", under its original title "Tell Me What You're Sippin' On" was initially to be included on select versions of the In the Zone DVD but was omitted. The original intended for In the Zone surfaced in September 2011. The song, a mid-tempo R&B track, was produced by Tricky Stewart and co-written by Spears.

Remix feat. AC

Spears later considered including a new version of the song featuring vocals from rapper AC on her fifth studio album Blackout . Critics noted an influence from Janet Jackson on the song. This version leaked from a Blackout demo CD in 2007.
