" When Your Eyes Say It " is a song by American recording artist Britney Spears , taken from her second full length album, Oops!... I Did It Again .

Background

The song was planned to be released as the album's fourth single, however, " Don't Let Me Be the Last to Know ", was eventually chosen instead. A promotional CD Single for "When Your Eyes Say It" was released in the UK in January 2001.

Music Video

While the song was planned to be released as a single, an accompanying music video was directed by Jonathan Dayton and Valerie Faris. However, after "Don't Let Me Be The Last to Know" was chosen as the fourth single, the release of the single and the music video was cancelled. There is no evidence as to what was in the video or if it was leaked. Jonathan Dayton has mentioned he has a copy of the video on a VHS tape.
