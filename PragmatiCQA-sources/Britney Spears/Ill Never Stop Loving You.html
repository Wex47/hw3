" I'll Never Stop Loving You " is a song by American recording artist Britney Spears , taken from her debut full length studio album, ...Baby One More Time . The song is a bonus track on the album.

Background

Written by Jason Blume and Steve Diamond, the song was first released in 1996 by J'Son.
