Audio

" Clumsy " is a song by American recording artist Britney Spears . It severed as the second promotional single from her ninth full length studio album, Glory . It was released on August 11, 2016, by RCA Records .

Background

On May 10, 2016, "Clumsy", along with " Liar ", was registered in ASCAP under Britney's profile.
