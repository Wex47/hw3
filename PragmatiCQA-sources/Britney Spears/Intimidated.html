" Intimidated " is a song taken from Britney Spears single I'm A Slave 4 U (B-side). It was recorded for her 2001 album " Britney " but did not end up making the final tracklist. The song however appeared on the score for " Jimmy Neutron Boy Genius " released in 2001.
