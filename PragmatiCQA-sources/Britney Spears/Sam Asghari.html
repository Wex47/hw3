Hesam "Sam" Asghari (born March 23, 1994) is an Iranian model, personal trainer and fiancé of Britney Spears. Born in Tehran. He moved to Los Angeles in 2006 to live with his father, who was a truck driver. He is the youngest of four siblings.

On September 13, 2021, Sam announced his engagement to Britney by gifting her a round solitaire diamond ring with Lioness engraved inside it, made by Israel based designer Roman Malayev .

Career

In 2015, Asghari modeled in Micheal Costello's show. He also starred as a construction worker in Fifth Harmony's "Work from Home" music video. He is an expert at working out as seen on his social media videos.

Asghari started his full-time career at Gold's Gym as a personal trainer. He later met Britney Spears after she picked him to appear in her " Slumber Party " music video in 2016.
