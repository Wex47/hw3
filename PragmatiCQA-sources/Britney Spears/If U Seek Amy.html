" If U Seek Amy " is a song by American recording artist Britney Spears . It severed as the fourth single from her sixth full length studio album, Circus . It was released on March 10, 2009, by Jive Records .

Background

The song was written by Max Martin , who also wrote some of Spears' biggest hits (" ...Baby One More Time ", " Oops!... I Did It Again "). It caused controversy due to the "If U Seek Amy" part is interpreted as "F-U-C-K Me", resulting in the song beimg renamed for radio as " If U See Amy " and the physical single getting a Parental Advisory warning in some shops.

Music Video

The music video for "If U Seek Amy" was filmed on February 7, 2009, at Pacific Palisades, California, and was directed by Jake Nava, who previously worked with Spears for her " My Prerogative " music video.
