" Chillin' With You " is a song by American recording artist Britney Spears , taken from her eighth full length studio album, Britney Jean . The songs features vocals from Spears' sister, Jamie Lynn .

Background

Although Britney is credited as a writer, It's revealed that the vocals on the song are actually sung by Britney's backing singer Myah Marie . It's unknown if Britney recorded a version and if Jamie Lynn is aware that it isn't her sister on the song.

Trivia

  • This is Britney 's least favorite song on Britney Jean .
  • It's considered by fans to be one of Britney's worst songs.
