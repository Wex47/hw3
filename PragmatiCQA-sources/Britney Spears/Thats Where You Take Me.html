" That's Where You Take Me " is a song by American recording artist Britney Spears , taken from her third full length studio album, Britney .

Background

The song had a limited release in the Philippines and possibly other Asian markets. The CD single is extremley rare and it Spears' most rare CD out of her entire discography. However, fake copies exist. It's rumoured a music video for the song was made, with montages of Spears' performances from her Dream Within a Dream Tour and backstage footage.
