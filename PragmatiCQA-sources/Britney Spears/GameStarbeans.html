Starbeans is a location in the Britney Spears: American Dream game.

Background

In the first minute of the game, your friend Sara Williams calls to tell you that Britney Spears just walked into Starbeans! You excuse yourself from work, and Sara convinces you to sing for the open mic. Soon, you catch Britney's attention and she invites you to a party. This is how it all started!
