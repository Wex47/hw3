Telephone is a song by Lady Gaga and Beyoncé from Gaga's extended play The Fame Monster. It was originally recorded by Britney Spears but removed from the final cut of her sixth studio album Circus .

Background

The song's first demo (Spears's solo version) was leaked on internet in May 2, 2010. Another version of the song was shared on January 6, 2015 and featured both Gaga and Spears, which was most likely created from meshing both of their demos together. The song was recorded for Circus, and was also considered for inclusion on The Singles Collection . After Spears rejecting to release the song, Gaga eventually recorded the song replacing Spears vocals with Beyoncé's and released it on her EP: The Fame Monster . It was rumoured that Spears traded the song for " Quicksand ", originally recorded and written by Gaga.
