When I Say So is an unreleased song by Britney Spears . It was recorded in 2001 for her third studio album Britney but failed to make the final cut.

Background

The song is known alternatively as " Till I Say So ". The song leaked in 2010. It's speculated that this song inspired the sound of In the Zone and was being considered to appear on it but didn't come to fruition.
