Ouch is an unreleased song by Britney Spears . It's written by Britney Spears, Michelle Bell and Nanna Kristiansson. Produced by RedOne. It was intended for her scrapped studio album Original Doll .

Background

The song is speculated to be written about her relationship with Kevin Federline and how he was already with his girlfriend Shar Jackson . 2 snippets of the song leaked in early April of 2012 then the full version leaked on April 11, 2012.

Co-writer Nanna Kristiansson wrote on Tumblr about the song, "I still remember the phone call when they told me that Britney had chosen one of my tracks. It was an amazing feeling! I live in Sweden and unfortunately I never got the opportunity to meet her in person."
