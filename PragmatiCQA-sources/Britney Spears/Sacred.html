" Sacred " is an unreleased song by American recording artist Britney Spears , intended for her fourth full length studio album, In the Zone .

Background

The song was registered with ASCAP in 2004 and was rumored to eventually appear on Original Doll . In December 2021, Producer Guy Chambers discussed the background of the song in a interview.

"She [Britney] said ‘f***’ in it. This was in about 2003, her imperial phase, when she was huge. She was trying to rebel from the cleaner side of Britney, I suppose, and I should have said no, but I didn’t, I let her do what she wanted".
