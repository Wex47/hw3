Diary Of... is an unreleased demo for Britney Spears . It was written by Britney Spears, Michelle Bell, Peter Wade, Bloodshy & Avant in 2004 for her 5tj studio album Blackout . The producer demo leaked in 2017. It's talks about a pop star that feels misunderstood.

Background

In 2017, Michelle Bell stated that this was the last song she and Britney worked on for the album. The song was inspired by the MTV Diary documentary from 2001.
