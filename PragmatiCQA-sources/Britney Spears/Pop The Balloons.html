Pop The Balloons is an unreleased demo for Britney Spears . It was written by Charli XCX, Sarah Hudson, Sophie, Noonie Bao, MNDR and produced by John Hill. It was pitched in 2014 for Britney's ninth studio album Glory but didn't make the cut.

Background

The song was originally intended for Charli XCX but was pitched to Britney and Little Mix. Demo pitch by Sarah Hudson has leaked online.
