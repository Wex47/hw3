All That She Wants is an unreleased song by Britney Spears . It was intended for Britney's fifth studio album, Blackout .

Background

The chorus samples Ace of Base's 1993 hit " All That She Wants " . Verses are taken from a poem called " Remembrance of who I am ", written by Spears herself back in 2006.

The song was recorded in Denmark, Ace of Base front man Ulf Ekberg says the song was recorded in 2006, but remained unreleased because it was a “difficult time” for Spears.

On August 26, 2016, Britney spontaneously sung the chorus of the song in James Corden's Carpool Karaoke while discussing wanting more children.
