" Breathe on Me " is a song by American recording artist Britney Spears , taken from her fourth full length studio album, In the Zone .

Background

The song was planned to be released as a single from In the Zone (possibly after " Outrageous "), but when Britney sprained her knee at a music video shoot, the single's release was canceled.

The song is one of Britney's personal favourites songs and she would perform it on most of her live shows.
