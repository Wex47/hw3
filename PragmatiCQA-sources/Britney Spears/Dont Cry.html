" Don't Cry " is a song by American recording artist Britney Spears , taken from her eighth full length studio album, Britney Jean .

Background

The song received critical acclaim, with critics praising Britney’s vocals and lyrics while also complimenting will.i.am’s production. The track became a fan favorite for several reasons, with one of those reasons being that it is one of two songs that were left untouched by Myah Marie .

Theme

'Don't Cry' discusses ending a romantic relationship in good terms, with both parties wishing to remain as friends after parting ways.
