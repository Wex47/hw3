Take Off is an unreleased song by Britney Spears . It was written by Michelle Bell, Henrik Jonback, Bloodshy & Avant. It was originally intended for her fourth studio album In The Zone but was also being considered for Original Doll at the time.

Background

The song was leaked online on May 21, 2017. In 2014, Bell talked about the song, “I think it was ahead of Lady Gaga. I think people would have looked at her and thought she had something to say. It was ahead of its time. She talked about war and how war is wrong.”
