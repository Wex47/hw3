Wallace Scott

Name

Wallace Scott

Gender

Male
Wallace Scott is the owner of Microbe Music , and your old colleague.

Background

You start the game working at Microbe Music , and Wallace is your boss. You soon leave with the news that Britney's at a nearby Starbeans .

Later, you continue to appear at Microbe Music for appearances and meeting new musicians to network with.

Personality

He is very kind and helpful to you, and supportive of your journey to become a star. He also notifies you if he sees something that you might want to know.
