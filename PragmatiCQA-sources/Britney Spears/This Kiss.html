This Kiss is an unreleased song by Britney Spears . It's written by Britney Spears, Nicole Morier and Greg Kurstin. It was intended for her sixth studio album Circus but didn't the cut.

Background

A 16-second snippet leaked on April 26, 2012 under the title "Kiss". Full Version leaked on April 29, 2012.
