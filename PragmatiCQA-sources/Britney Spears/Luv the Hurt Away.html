Luv the Hurt Away is an unreleased song by Britney Spears . It was recorded in 1998 for her debut studio album ...Baby One More Time , but didn't make the final cut. It features Full Force .

Background

The song was leaked in 2006, via Full Force's official website. It was published by Forceful Music and Universal Music Z Songs. It's also known as " Love the Hurt Away ".
