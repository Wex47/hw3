Music Video

" Kill the Lights " is a song by American recording artist Britney Spears , taken from her sixth full length studio album, Circus .

Background

Kill the Lights is an urban and dance-pop song produced by Danja . This song alludes to the singer's relationship with the paparazzi and the news media, while also addressing the consequences of becoming a celebrity.

Music Video

A promotional music video was released in 2009, was directed by PUNY and features an animated Spears running away from the paparazzi. It was heavily compared to the song " Piece of Me ". Kill the Lights is the least viewed music video on Britney Spears' YouTube Channel.
