Casually is an unreleased demo for Britney Spears . It was written by Myah Marie, John Lock and Jon Asher. It was Intended for Britney's eighth studio album Britney Jean (2013). A 33 second snippet leaked on October 11th, 2021. Full demo leaked on January 2nd, 2022.
