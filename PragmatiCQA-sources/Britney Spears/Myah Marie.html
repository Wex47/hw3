"Myah Marie Langston" , known professionally as "Myah Marie" , is an American singer-songwriter who has written and recorded several songs for Britney Spears throughout 2008-2013. Langston wrote demos and recorded backing vocals on Circus (2008) and Femme Fatale (2011).

In 2013, Langston became heavily involved in writing and recording final versions of songs on Britney Jean , in fact, Langston can be heard performing not only backing but lead vocals alongside Spears on Work Bitch , Passenger, Body Ache , Til It's Gone, Brightest Morning Star and Chillin' With You .

Opinion on Britney Spears

It is highly speculated that Langston had a low opinion of Spears until 2013, despite claiming to have been a fan since the Oops!... I Did It Again era. In 2011, Langston recorded a parody of Spears' song " Piece Of Me ", in which she mocks Spears, titled, " Don’t Take My Kids From Me ” .
