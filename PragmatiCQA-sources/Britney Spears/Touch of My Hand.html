" Touch of My Hand " is a song by American recording artist Britney Spears , taken from her fourth full length studio album, In the Zone .

Background

Touch of My Hand was the first song recorded for In The Zone album, it contains elements of Middle Eastern music. Spears previewed the song to Quddus Philippe of MTV 2003, alongside " Brave New Girl " and " Everytime ". Spears claimed this sets the mood for this album and also co-wrote this song. Lyrically this song talks about masturbation.

Trivia

  • Britney Spears says "It's one of my favorite tracks on the album," Spears said. "I like to compare it to ' That's the Way Love Goes ,' kind of a Janet Jackson thing". Spears described the themes of masturbation as "tastefully done". She pinpointed the song as one of the most personal to her, saying, "I really love the vibe of "Touch of My Hand." I love the subject that I'm touching on because no one's really talked about some of those things in a lot of songs written lately because people are scared to express themselves in that way. I think it's an empowering thing for girls. "In reference to these themes of autoeroticism, Spears stated, "It's a positive thing to indulge in yourself in a sexual way sometimes".
