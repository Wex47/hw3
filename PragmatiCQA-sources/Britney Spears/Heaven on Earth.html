" Heaven on Earth " is a song by American recording artist Britney Spears , taken from her fifth full length album, Blackout .

Background

Britney said on a interview that this is her favorite song from Blackout .

There's a version registered on ASCAP with Freescha as the performer, and would be the writer's demo.
