My Big Secret is an unreleased song by Britney Spears . It was intended for her third studio album Britney but failed to make the final cut.

Background

Originally recorded for Janet Jackson for her album "All For You" (2001) but failed to make the final cut. Registered with ASCAP. A snippet leaked on January 1, 2012.
