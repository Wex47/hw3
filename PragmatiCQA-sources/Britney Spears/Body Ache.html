Audio

" Body Ache " is a song by American recording artist Britney Spears , taken from her eighth full length studio album, Britney Jean .

Background

The lead vocals on the song are sung by Myah Marie . It is unknown if Britney is aware of this.

Trivia

  • The released version is the demo version sung by Myah Marie, it is unknown why Britney ' final version was not used.
