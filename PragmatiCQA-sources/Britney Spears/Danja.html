" Floyd Nathaniel Hills ", professionally known as Danja , is an American record producer and songwriter from Virginia Beach, Virginia. He is respected in the Britney universe for Blackout which is considered as one of the best Britney Spears albums ever. His signature song he worked on for Britney is Gimme More (2007).
