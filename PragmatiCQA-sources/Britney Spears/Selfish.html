" Selfish " is a song by American recording artist Britney Spears , taken from her seventh full length studio album, Femme Fatale . The song is a bonus track on the album.

Background

Selfish is an up-tempo dance-pop and electropop song with an pop-rock influenced bridge and an electronic R&B breakdown.
