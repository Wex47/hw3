" Phonography " is a song by American recording artist Britney Spears , taken from her sixth full length studio album, Circus . The song is a bonus track on the album.

Background

It is more likely that Britney is equating the word “phonography” to phone sex, as the song deals heavily in metaphor and double entendres between telephone technology and sexuality. In this context, “Phonography” can be seen a combination of the words ‘phone’ and ‘pornography’.

Within the song, Britney explains her unabashed interest in phonography to her man.

The release of the track coincided with the boom of production of new smart phones in 2008.
