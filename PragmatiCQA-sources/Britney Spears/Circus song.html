" Circus " is a song by American recording artist Britney Spears . It severed as the second single from her sixth full length studio album, Circus . It was released on July 6, 2008, by Jive Records .

Background

This song was written by Łukasz Gottwald, Claude Kelley and Benjamin Levin and produced by Dr. Luke and Benny Blanco. "Circus" charted number three on the Billboard Hot 100 and number one on Pop Songs and is Spears' second best-selling digital song in the country, having sold over 3.1 million copies.
