Money, Love and Happiness is an unreleased song by Britney Spears . It's written by Britney Spears, Michelle Bell, Nanna Kristiansson and RedOne. It was intended for her scrapped studio album Original Doll .

Background

The song leaked in 2012. It's the first song Britney worked on with RedOne in early 2004 and he submitted other songs for the album but it's unknown if Britney recorded them.
