" It Should Be Easy " is a song by American recording artist Britney Spears , taken from her eighth full length studio album, Britney Jean .

Background

The song received mixed reviews from critics. While complimenting its production, the song's heavy use of autotune has been criticized. Although Spears has stated that the track would not be released as a single, the song has been released as a single exclusively in Italy.
