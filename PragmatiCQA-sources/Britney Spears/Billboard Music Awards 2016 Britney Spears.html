Britney Spears performed at the 2016 Billboard Music Awards.

Background

Britney was originally set to perform her new single, Make Me (Oooh) , but plans were cancelled due to a production delay, and the song was pushed back to a later summer release. A medley of hits was later decided as the replacement for the single.

Set list

  • Work Bitch (hat toss)
  • Womanizer (outfit change)
  • I Love Rock 'n' Roll (guitar prop)
  • Breathe on Me
  • I'm A Slave 4 U (pole prop)
  • Touch of My Hand
  • Toxic (finale)
