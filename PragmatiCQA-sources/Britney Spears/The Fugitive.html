The Fugitive is an unreleased song by Britney Spears . It's rumoured to be written by Britney Spears, Henrik Jonback, Bloodshy & Avant. It was intended for her scrapped studio album Original Doll .

Background

Britney was photographed holding a CD with ‘The Fugitive’ written on it while on The Onyx Hotel Tour in May 2004.
