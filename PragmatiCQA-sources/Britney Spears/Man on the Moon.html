Audio

" Man on the Moon " is a song by American recording artist Britney Spears , taken from her ninth full length album, Glory .

Background

Throughout the past months, Britney had been posting pictures on her Instagram of the Moon and Mars, usually without a caption.

On May 7, 2016, ASCAP Recording confirmed the new Britney track on their database! On May 15, 2016, BreatheHeavy wrote an article about a new track called 'Man on the Moon'.
