Siren is a demo song written by Myah Marie for Britney Spears for her eighth studio album Britney Jean but was rejected. A snippet of the demo leaked in February 2021. Full demo leaked on January 2nd, 2022.

Background

Myah Marie released a snippet through her Instagram account, the full track is available on her Patreon.
