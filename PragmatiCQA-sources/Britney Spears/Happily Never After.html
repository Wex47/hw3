Happily Never After is a unreleased demo for Britney Spears, it was written by Shea Taylor and Ne-Yo, it was intented for her fifth studio album Blackout , but didn't make the cut.

Background

It was given and released by the band " The Pussycats Dolls " for their album " Doll Domination "

According to Ne-Yo, Britney choose this song with Good Girl Gone Bad to record them, but she never showed up to record them, and this annoyed to Ne-Yo.
