Tinashe Jorgensen Kachingwe (born February 6th 1993) is a Zimbabwean-American R&B & hiphop singer-songwriter, dancer & actress who has scored hits such as "2on", "All Hands on Deck", "All My Friends" (as a featured artist), "Freal Love" (with Marshmello & Far East Movement) & "Slumber Party" , a collaboration with Britney Spears .

Early life

She was born in Lexington, Kentucky, but moved to Los Angeles with her family, when she was eight years old.

She attended Crescenta Valley High School, but left in 9th Grade, due to being bullied by her peers at school & to pursue her music career.

Career

Early career

All since she was young, Tinashe always wants to be an entertainer.

In 2004, Tinashe participated at Star Search as a Junior Vocalist.

Although she had ambitions as a singer, she had worked as an actress on tv-shows such as Avatar: The Last Airbender , Rocket Power , Two & a Half Men , etc. She had also starred in some movies, such as The Polar Express & Masked & Anonymous .

From 2007 to 2011, she was a member of the girl group "The Stunners".

She also had a guest appearance in Justin Bieber's debut music video "Baby".

After the disbandment of "The Stunners", she released as a solo artist three mixtapes In Case We Die , Reverie & Black Water.

She signed to RCA Records in 2012.

Debut album & other released albums & projects within RCA Records.

In 2014 she became successful with her song "2on" featuring rapper Schoolboy Q and the same year she released her debut album Aquarius.

Her second album Joyride was supposed to be released somewhere in 2015/2016, but her label kept pushing back the album, so instead, she released a mixtape called Amethyst in 2015 and a digital album called Nightride in 2016. Eventually, Joyride was released in 2018 with additional changes within the album.

In November 2016, she collaborated with Britney Spears on the song "Slumber Party" and the accompanying music video.

She was also planning to release a mixtape called Nashe , but the project was cancelled, due to disagreements of RCA Records.

After leaving RCA Records, Songs for You & album 333 (2019-)

After her contract ended on RCA Records in 2019, in the same year, Tinashe signed in by ROC Nation and released her album Songs For You , the first released album after her departure with RCA & the first album where she had more creative control over it. The album became a fan favorite as well.

In 2021, she teased with several promos of her new album 333 on her Instagram and stated that 333 will be released in August 2021. Her album 333 is released on the 6th of August.

Due to the succes of 333 , Tinashe decided to brought out a deluxe-version of 333 in March 2022, including newer songs such as “Naturally”.
