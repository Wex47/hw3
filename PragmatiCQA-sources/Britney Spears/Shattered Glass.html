" Shattered Glass " is a song by American recording artist Britney Spears , taken from her sixth full length album, Circus .

Background

This song was debuted on Billboard Hot 100 and peaked 70. Next week it left the chart. This is the only non-single song by Britney Spears, which was debuted.

Charts

After the release of Circus, Shattered Glass debuted on several charts around the world due to strong rate of digital download in its debut week.

For the dated week of December 20, 2008, the song debuted at number 70 of Billboard Hot 100 chart, being the first and only Spears track (non released as single) to debut on the chart. In the same week, also debuted at Billboard Pop 100 chart (now discontinued) at number 57, along with other five Circus tracks.
