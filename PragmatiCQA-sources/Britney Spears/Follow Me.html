Follow Me is an song originally for Britney Spears . It's written by Britney Spears, Henrik Jonback, Bloodshy & Avant around 2005 that became the theme song for the Nickelodeon hit television show Zoey 101 (2005-08). The song is performed by Jamie Lynn Spears in the opening credits.

Background

The song was originally intended for Britney for her scrapped studio album Original Doll but was offered to Jamie Lynn to record as the theme song. A version with Spears's vocals are registered at BMI and ASCAP .

In October 2020, Jamie Lynn released a music video of the song with the Zoey 101 cast and TiK ToK personalities.
