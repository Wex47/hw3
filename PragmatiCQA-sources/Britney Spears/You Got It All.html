" You Got it All " is a song by American recording artist Britney Spears , taken from her second full length album, Oops!... I Did It Again . The song is originally by The Jets.

Background

The cover of the song was recorded in 1997, when Spears was recording her first album ...Baby One More Time . The song was supposed to be included on the album, but was cut at the last minute. However, it was included on a limited edition version of Oops! ...I Did It Again.
