" Girls and Boys " is a song by American recording artist Britney Spears , taken from her fourth full length studio album, In the Zone . The song is a bonus track on the album.

Background

It's the only bonus song on the DVD version on the album that isn't available on streaming services.

A piano rendition of the song by Linda Perry has leaked online.
