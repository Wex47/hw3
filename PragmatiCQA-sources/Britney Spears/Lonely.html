" Lonely " is a song by American recording artist Britney Spears , taken from her third full length studio album, Britney .

Background

Britney said in a promo Interview in 2001, that Lonely was her favourite song on the album.

On July 18, 2021, a few days after a court hearing, boyfriend Sam Asghari posted an Instagram story of Britney jamming out to Lonely in his car and told him it's one of songs she wrote that she's proud of.
