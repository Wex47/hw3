My Love Was Always There is an unreleased song by Britney Spears . It's written by Britney Spears in 2001 . It was intended for her album Britney but didn't make the cut.

Background

It's one of the songs that Britney wrote during the Dream Within A Dream Tour and she only performed live once. The song was eventually re-written and titled Maybe and given to Enrique Iglesias .
