All The Way is an unreleased song by Britney Spears . It was written by Cathy Dennis, Jack Splash, Dr. Luke. It was intended for her fifth studio album Blackout but didn't make the cut.

Background

In 2007, Dr. Luke described this song as a “feel good, upbeat song” in an issue of US Weekly.
