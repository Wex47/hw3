Britney's Dance Beat is a one or two player Music Video Game featuring songs and video appearances by Britney Spears . The story is that players must pass "auditions" to tour as Spears' dancers.

Gameplay involves pressing buttons in time with music. Symbols for buttons are arranged in a circle, resembling a clock dial. A pointer sweeps around the dial, indicating when to press each button. The game contains five songs: " ...Baby One More Time ", " Oops!... I Did It Again ", " Stronger ", " Overprotected " and " I'm A Slave 4 U ". Successful play is rewarded with "backstage passes" which unlocks features such as backstage video footage of Britney. There's also a video vault. When you complete auditions you get backstage passes to unlock 360 immersive videos and behind the scenes footage. The game also includes various remixes during behind the scenes footage, menus, and credits. It also has photoshoots during credits Versions of the game were released for the Xbox, PlayStation 2, Game Boy Advance and PC .

Game Description

Prove your skills of style and rhythm to Britney Spears herself in this cross-platform dancing game from THQ. The pop music icon is preparing for her next tour and she needs back-up dancers. Use the game controls to follow the rhythm and movement of some of Britney's hottest singles and earn a place on stage with the young superstar. Keep up with the beat to unlock never-before-seen concert and video footage. Featured songs include hits like " Oops!... I Did It Again ", " ...Baby One More Time ", " Overprotected , " Stronger , and " I'm A Slave 4 U ".
