Giving It Up For Love is an unreleased song by Britney Spears . It was written by Britney Spears, Kara DioGuardi, Robert “EST” Waller of Three Times Dope & Scott Storch. It was intended for her scrapped album Original Doll .

Background

The song was confirmed in March 2004 and registered to EMI in August the same year. It's registered with ASCAP.
