" Private Show " is a song by American recording artist Britney Spears . It severed as the first promotional single from her ninth full length studio album, Glory . It was released on August 4, 2016, by RCA Records .

It was written to go hand-in-hand with Spears' new fragrance of the same name. "I was like 'let's do something different' and 'let's do a really cool song' to go with the fragrance...and it just clicked."

Background

On May 15, 2016, rumors started circulating of a song called "Private Show" on the then untitled " Glory ". . Fans exclaim that it sounds "so blackout", and it soon becomes evident that this is one of the most anticipated songs for fans from the album.

On May 16, 2016, BreatheHeavy published an article about two songs confirmed on the album, Man on the Moon , and Private Show.

Soon after, the song was registered in the ASCAP database under Britney's profile, confirming rumors.

On June 18, 2016, Carla Marie Williams confirmed that she and Youngman have written the song. Soon after, she confirmed that Young Fyre produced it.

However, the real writers were Britney Spears, Carla Marie Williams, Young Fyre & Simon Smith.

On June 19, 2016, Spears confirmed her new fragrance, Private Show on Twitter, and that the song was created to coincide with the fragrance's release.
