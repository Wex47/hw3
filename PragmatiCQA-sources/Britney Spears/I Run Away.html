" I Run Away " is a song by American recording artist Britney Spears , taken from her third full length studio album, Britney .

Background

This song was written based on the script of CROSSROADS, Writer and Producer Joshua Schwartz talked about the creation of the song on THE ORIGINAL DOLL With JAMES RODRIGUEZ. www .TheOriginalDoll.com
