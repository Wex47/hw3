Conservations With Myself is an unreleased demo for Britney Spears . It's written by Cathy Dennis, Henrik Jonback, Christian "Bloodshy" Karlsson and Pontus "Avant" Winnberg. Produced by Bloodshy & Avant. It was intended for her scrapped studio album Original Doll .

Background

The demo was pitched in 2004. The song is registered with ASCAP.
