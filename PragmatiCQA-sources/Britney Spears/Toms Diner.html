Tom's Diner is a single by Giorgio Moroder featuring Britney Spears from his album Déjà Vu .

Background

The song is a cover of Suzanne Vega's song of the same name. In an interview, Moroder praised Spears's vocals and said that she did a "good job" with the song. Moroder also stated that Spears "sounds so good that you would hardly recognize her."
