Britney: Live in Concert

General information

Legs

1
Britney: Live in Concert is the ninth concert tour by American recording artist Britney Spears . The tour, which largely mirrors Spears' Las Vegas residency show Britney: Piece of Me , kicked off in Japan. Spears will perform in the Philippines, Taiwan, Thailand, Hong Kong, Singapore and Israel for the first time during the tour.

Rumors of a world tour began circulating in 2016, coinciding with the release of Spears' ninth studio album, Glory, when Spears expressed interested in performing internationally again. In March 2017, after numerous publications began leaking details about Spears' planned international shows, she confirmed the planned tour by announcing concert dates in the Philippines and Israel. In the weeks following the announcement, Spears revealed additional Asian tour dates in Japan, Thailand, Taiwan and Hong Kong, with additional dates to follow.
