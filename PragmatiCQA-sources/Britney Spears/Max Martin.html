" Karl Martin Sandberg ", professionally known as Max Martin , is an Swedish record producer and songwriter from Stenhamra, Sweden. He is highly regarded in the Britney universe for his hit songs over the years. His signature song he worked on for Britney is her debut single ...Baby One More Time (1999).
