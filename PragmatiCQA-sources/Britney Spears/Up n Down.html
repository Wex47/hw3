" Up n' Down " is a song by American recording artist Britney Spears , taken from her seventh full length studio album, Femme Fatale . The song is a bonus track on the album.

Background

Up n' Down is an up-tempo dance-pop, electropop and R&B song with elements of trap-pop, synth-pop and EDM.

Charts

After the release of the album, Up N' Down debuted on several American charts, issued by Billboard. On the dated week of April 16, 2011, Up N' Down failed to hit the Billboard Hot 100 chart, and instead, debuted and peaked at number ten on Billboard Bubbling Under Hot 100 chart.

In the same week, the song also debuted and peaked at number five on Billboard Dance/Electronic Digital Song Sales , spending 2 weeks on the chart. Up N' Down was the highest peaking track among other 5 tracks from Femme Fatale that debuted in that week (In order of peak position: (Drop Dead) Beautiful , Big Fat Bass , Selfish , Seal It with a Kiss and Trip to Your Heart )
