State of Grace is an unreleased song by Britney Spears . It was recorded in 2006 or 2007 for her fifth studio album Blackout but didn't make the cut.

Background

In a paparazzi video from 2006, Britney is seen playing the song in her car. As much as Britney appeared to enjoy the song, it was taken off the album's final track list last minute. The song is known alternatively as " (Love Is A) State of Grace ". In 2009, French singer Christophe Willem recorded it as " Entre Nous Et Le Sol " for his second studio album Caféine (2009).
