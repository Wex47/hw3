Untitled Lullaby is an unreleased song by Britney Spears . It was recorded in 2006 for her fifth studio album Blackout but didn't make the cut.

Background

The song is known alternatively as " Baby Boy ". the demo leaked in 2007. There's a writer's demo "Beautiful Lies" with the same instrumental recorded by Kim Ross has leaked online.
