Planet Hollywood: The Axis is a location in the Britney Spears: American Dream game, located in The Strip .

Background

In the first minute of the game, there is a flash-forward to your character performing with Britney Spears at The Axis for her residency show. Later in the game, this can be unlocked in Vegas.

! This article relates to the Britney Spears: American Dream game featuring the character of Britney Spears , and not the real world.
