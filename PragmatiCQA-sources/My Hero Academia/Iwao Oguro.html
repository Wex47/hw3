Iwao Oguro ( 雄 ( お ) 黒 ( ぐろ ) 巌 ( いわお ) , Oguro Iwao ) , commonly known as Master ( 師匠 , Shishō ) , is the vigilante known as Knuckleduster ( ナックルダスター , Nakkurudasutā ) . He was formerly the Pro Hero High-Speed Hero: O'Clock ( 超速ヒーロー・オクロック , Chōsoku Hīrō Okurokku ) in Japan, and Hyper Quadfist ( 超 ( チャオ ) 頻 ( ピン ) 四 ( スー ) 拳 ( チェン ) , Chaopin sūchen ) in China.

Appearance

Knuckleduster is a towering, heavily muscled man. He has short dark hair and visibly thick eyebrows, and also possesses heavy stubble and a prominent scar running diagonally down his left cheek.

His costume consists of a simple wrap-around black mask tied over the top of his head, a tight-fitting dark shirt, jeans, boots, and a long hunter green trench coat. On his hands, he wears a pair of dark gloves topped with a pair of brass knuckles, or knuckledusters, from which he derives his name.

After his initial encounter with Number 6 , his right leg is busted to the point of requiring a cane to walk, and also appears to have his left eye completely scarred over.

As O'Clock he wore a full-body red suit and a mask that covered his eyes and half of his face. He also wore gloves and boots, and his suit had several lines around the arms, legs, and torso. On his shoulders, he had what appeared to be his symbol. He was noticeably slimmer at the time, though still quite muscular.
