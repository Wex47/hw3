Qing Qing City, China ( 中国 軽 ( けい ) 慶 ( けい ) 市 ( し ) , Chūgoku Keikei-shi ) is a fictional city in China where the first Quirk manifested.

History

At some point in the past, a glowing baby was born in an unnamed hospital. This baby had the strange power to emit light, which in turn, would be the very beginning of superpowers manifesting in humankind.
