Eddie Soul ( エディ・ソウル , Edi Sōru ) was the father of Rody Soul and was a researcher for Humarise in My Hero Academia: World Heroes' Mission .

Appearance

Eddie was a tall, adult man with short dark brown hair and gray eyes. He was seen wearing a white buttoned-up shirt underneath a dull blue overcoat.

Personality

Eddie Soul was a genius engineer, and outside of his work, he was also a kind and loving father to his three children, Rody, Roro and Lala. While his job kept him busy, he was sure to prioritise his children and was seen preparing food for them in one scene. He was grateful to his eldest son, Rody, for looking after his younger siblings and gave him a puzzle to solve as a gift, which turned out to be a pendant containing a photo of them together.

For an unknown reason however, Eddie left his children and disappeared to work as a researcher for the terroristic cult Humarise, tarnishing his family's reputation, something that Rody would grow to resent him for. However, it turned out this was not Eddie's choice and that Flect Turn had threatened the lives of his children had he not pledged his allegiance to cult and aided the scientists in creating the Ideotrigger Bombs . Eddie's love for his children caused him to devote himself to Humarise's cause, making him aid them in their plans to wipe out Quirks and humanity.

However, Eddie also created a disarming key for the Trigger Bombs, something that he entrusted to his colleague Alan Kay . While he did aid Humarise, he was also willing to plot against them as to stop their plans of global devastation, an act that would end up costing him his life. In the end, Eddie Soul was a true hero who sacrificed his life to save the world, and was painted as a criminal disgrace. Although his son Rody, with the help of hero student Izuku Midoriya , managed to succeed in his place and stop Humarise's genocidal plans, redeeming Eddie's name in the process.

Synopsis

Abilities

Engineering : Eddie Soul was a genius engineer who was successful enough to buy a large house for him and his children. A small display of his handiwork was displayed when he created a puzzle gift for his son Rody to solve. His talents also garnered the attention of Humarise, causing the sect to kidnap him and force the man to work for them. While Eddie helped in creating the Trigger Bombs, explosive devices that would release deadly amounts of Ideotrigger, he also secretly managed to create a disarming key that would shut down the devices Humarise had hidden around the world.
