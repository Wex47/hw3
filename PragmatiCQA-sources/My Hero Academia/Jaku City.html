Description

Jaku City is a populous area located in Japan. Besides the Jaku General Hospital , the ward does not have any other known landmarks.

This city is where one of the fronts of the Paranormal Liberation War takes place. A large urban area, roughly one third of the city, was destroyed by Tomura Shigaraki 's Decay .
