Sea Rescue Hero: Selkie ( 海難ヒーロー セルキー , Kainan Hīrō Serukī ) is a Pro Hero and Captain of the Oki Mariner .

Appearance

Selkie is a man with a muscular physique, but with the appearance of a humanoid spotted seal that has a gray beard.

He sports a full body wetsuit as his hero costume , featuring yellow goggles, retractable flippers and a stylized "S" featured on the left shoulder.

Personality

Selkie is a confident, kind, and professional individual who prioritizes his hero work and protecting his crew. He has a tendency to try (and succeed in) making himself look cute, despite his macho appearance, much to the annoyance of his sidekicks . Even so, he can be quite stern when the situation calls for it.

Abilities

Quirk

Spotted Seal ( ゴマフアザラシ , Gomafuazarashi ) : Selkie's Quirk grants him the attributes and abilities of a spotted seal. He can hold his breath for long periods of time, dive as far as fifteen hundred meters below sea level, and utilize echolocation when traversing dark waters or when his sight is hindered during combat. He also has above human strength.

  • Skilled Swimmer : Having the attributes of a spotted seal, Selkie is an excellent swimmer.

Super Moves

  • Selkie Punch ( セルキーパンチ , Serukī Panchi ) : Selkie punches his opponent with his spotted seal strength.

Equipment

Retractable Flippers : A pair of shoes with water flippers that flip back behind his legs when he is not in the water.

Trivia

  • Selkie is based on the mythical creatures of Scotland that resemble a seal when in water, but when they emerge, they resemble humans.
  • He was initially an anime only character, but was later included in the manga.
