Mako ( 真 ( ま ) 子 ( こ ) , Mako ) was the maternal grandmother of Tenko Shimura and Hana Shimura , mother of Nao Shimura and wife of Chizuo . Not much is known about her currently, except that she died when her grandson's Quirk went out of control.
