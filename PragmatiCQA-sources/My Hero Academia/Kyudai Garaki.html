Dr. Kyudai Garaki ( 殻 ( がら ) 木 ( き ) 球 ( きゅう ) 大 ( だい ) , Garaki Kyūdai ) , also known by the pseudonym of Daruma Ujiko ( 氏 ( うじ ) 子 ( こ ) 達磨 ( だるま ) , Ujiko Daruma ) , is a villain affiliated with the League of Villains . He is a faithful servant of All For One and later Tomura Shigaraki .

Appearance

Kyudai is a fairly old man of a portly and short stature. He is completely bald and has a very large and bushy mustache. As a villain, he wears a rather peculiar set of goggles that are shaped somewhat like gears, along with a white, buttoned-up labcoat. He looks very similar to Dr. Tsubasa, the doctor who told Izuku Midoriya that he was Quirkless .

As the chairman of Jaku General Hospital , Kyudai switches out his gear-like goggles in place of circular and black regular-looking ones. He also adorns a shirt and tie underneath his regular labcoat.

Kyudai's Quirk , Life Force, makes him appear younger than he actually is. Without this Quirk, he has many more wrinkles and his eyes are sunken.
