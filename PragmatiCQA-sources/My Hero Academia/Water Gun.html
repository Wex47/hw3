Water Gun ( 水鉄砲 , Mizudeppō ) is the Quirk used by Kota Izumi .

Contents

  • 1 Description
  • 2 Usage
  • 4 Site Navigation

Description

Water Gun allows the user to shoot a stream of water from their hands.

Usage

Kota utilizes this Quirk as a way to distract someone by splashing water in their face, first seen to save Izuku Midoriya after Muscular slams him to the ground.

Following the defeat of Muscular, Izuku asked Kota to use his Quirk to help put out the fires that were spreading across The Beast's Forest .
