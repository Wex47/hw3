Hisashi Midoriya ( 緑 ( みどり ) 谷 ( や ) 久 ( ひさし ) , Midoriya Hisashi ) is Izuku Midoriya 's father and the husband of Inko Midoriya .

History

At some point in his life, Hisashi married Inko, who eventually gave birth to their son, Izuku. He took a work post abroad.

His current status and activities are unknown.

Trivia

  • His name, Hisashi, roughly means "a long time ago". It is a reference to the sentence hisashiburi ( 久しぶり , hisashiburi ) , which means "it has been a while (since I last saw you)", hinting at his absence in the story.
    • It is also the same kanji as the "Ku" in Izuku's name.
  • In 2018, Horikoshi stated he will be revealed at some point.
