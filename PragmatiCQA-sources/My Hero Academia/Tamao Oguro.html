Tamao Oguro ( 雄 ( お ) 黒 ( ぐろ ) 珠 ( たま ) 緒 ( お ) , Oguro Tamao ) is the daughter of Iwao Oguro and the former host of Kuin Hachisuka .

Appearance

Tamao is a young girl with green eyes and brown hair. Before she became the host of Kuin Hachisuka , she had long hair, but as Kuin's host, she had short hair with bangs that covered one of her eyes. She wears an eyepatch over where her left eye used to be.

After being freed and sometime after being admitted in the hospital, her hair grew out and was put in a ponytail.
