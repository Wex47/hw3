Pinpoint Focus ( 一 ( いっ ) 点 ( てん ) 特 ( とっ ) 化 ( か ) , Itten Tokka ) is a Super Move developed and used by Izuku Midoriya using the Blackwhip Quirk .

Description

Pinpoint Focus is a move developed by Izuku, which consists of quickly shooting a tendril from his arm while focusing on a specific point as a means of attacking the target.

Izuku first used this Super Move during his fight against Lady Nagant . After his opponent attempted to get out of Izuku's range, he shot Pinpoint Focus at Nagant and then used it to propel himself towards her.
