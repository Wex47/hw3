Black Abyss ( 深淵 ( ブラック ) 闇 ( アン ) 躯 ( ク ) , Burakku Anku ) is a Super Move performed by Fumikage Tokoyami using his Dark Shadow Quirk .

Description

Fumikage orders Dark Shadow to envelop his body. Dark Shadow covers the upper half of Fumikage's body and protects him like a suit of armor. This compensates for Fumikage's weaknesses at close range. By expanding the claws that envelop his hands, Fumikage can attack or defend from a distance as well as propel himself to increase his mobility .

Originally, this technique was named Abyssal Black Body ( 深 ( しん ) 淵 ( えん ) 闇 ( あん ) 躯 ( く ) , Shin'en Anku ) , but was amended in favor of a more pronouncable name. It was developed during Class 1-A 's training in Gym Gamma .
