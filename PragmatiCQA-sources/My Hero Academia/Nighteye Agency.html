Description

Sir Nighteye's Hero Agency is a large, five-story building controlled by Sir Nighteye. He employs two sidekicks and two U.A. students to work for him.

Inside the building contains Sir Nighteye's personal office that is decorated with multiple posters of All Might, as well as his main desk where his laptop sits and a large bookshelf. On the second floor, there is a large meeting room.
