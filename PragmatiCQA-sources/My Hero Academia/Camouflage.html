Camouflage ( 保 ( ほ ) 護 ( ご ) 色 ( しょく ) , Hogoshoku ) is a Super Move used by Tsuyu Asui using her Frog Quirk .

Description

Tsuyu uses her frog-like body to match the temperature and color of her surroundings in order to mask her presence. She does not become completely invisible, but she is still able to sneak up on her targets.
