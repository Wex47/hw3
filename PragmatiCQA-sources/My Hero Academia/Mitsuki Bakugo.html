Mitsuki Bakugo ( 爆 ( ばく ) 豪 ( ごう ) 光 ( みつ ) 己 ( き ) , Bakugō Mitsuki ) is Katsuki Bakugo 's mother and the wife of Masaru Bakugo .

Appearance

Mitsuki is a woman with spiky ash-blonde hair and red eyes; the same aforementioned traits that her son Katsuki inherited. Due to her Quirk, she maintains a youthful appearance despite being in her late thirties.
