Saito ( 斎 ( さい ) 藤 ( とう ) , Saitō ) was a classmate of Himiko Toga . Not much is known about him at present, except that he was the first of Himiko's victims.

Appearance

Saito bears a surprisingly strong resemblance to Izuku Midoriya , possessing the same wide eyes, a mess of fluffy hair with prominently dark shadows cast over it, as well as a similar physique. He differs in that his hair is gray instead of green, and lacked freckles.

He wore the official uniform of his institute, which consisted of a light-colored dress shirt and tie, as well as a dark blue blazer.
