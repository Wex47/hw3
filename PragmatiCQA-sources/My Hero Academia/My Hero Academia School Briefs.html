My Hero Academia: School Briefs ( 僕のヒーローアカデミア 雄英白書 , Boku no Hīrō Akademia Yūei Hakusho ) is a spin-off light novel series written by Anri Yoshi and illustrated by Kohei Horikoshi .

Summary

Join the U.A. Students, Teachers, and Pro Heroes in various short stories that take place between main story events. Many of these stories cover off-screen moments readers haven't seen before, so feast your eyes on these hijinks and antics!
