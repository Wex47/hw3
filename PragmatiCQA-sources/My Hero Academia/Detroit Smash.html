Detroit Smash ( デトロイトスマッシュ , Detoroito Sumasshu ) is a Super Move performed by All Might , Izuku Midoriya , and Katsuki Bakugo using the One For All Quirk .

Description

Detroit Smash is All Might's signature move that Izuku later mimics after inheriting One For All. The move is a simple punch focusing One For All's stockpiling power into the fist. All Might's punches at 100% are capable of creating wind pressure so powerful it can change the weather.

All Might first uses this technique to save Izuku and Katsuki from the Sludge Villain . Izuku first uses this technique at 100% in the Battle Trial before dialing it down to 5% against Stain . Izuku would later use this again during the Forest Training Camp Arc while training with Tiger . Katsuki first uses this technique in conjunction with Izuku forming an uppercut while creating a beam to dispel a thunderstorm created by Nine .

All Might back at his time studying abroad in America, has been seen using this move as a straight forward punch, when he was in mid-air to creating wind pressure that redirect missiles to make them collide with each other.

During All Might's final battle with All For One , All Might used this move to cancel out All For One's enhanced Air Cannon while moving Gran Torino out of the way.

During the Paranormal Liberation War , Izuku uses this move using 100% of his power while battling with Tomura.

Variations

5% Detroit Smash ( 5% デトロイトスマッシュ , Go Pāsento Detoroito Sumasshu ) : With One for All: Full Cowling active, Izuku delivers a quirk enhanced punch directly to his opponent. Izuku first used this technique in the air and hit Stain with a downwards punch. .
100% Detroit Smash ( 100% デトロイトスマッシュ , Hyaku Pāsento Detoroito Sumasshu ) : Izuku activates One For All at 100% and delivers a straightforward lunging punch to the opponent. He first used this at the training camp he participated in to save Kota Izumi .
Double Detroit Smash ( ダブルデトロイトスマッシュ , Daburu Detoroito Sumasshu ) : Izuku and All Might combine their Detroit Smashes into a single, devastating charge. They performed this final attack against Wolfram , shattering through a humongous chunk of metal and causing a huge explosion that destroyed Izuku's Full Gauntlet .
Full Cowl - 100% Detroit Smash ( フルカウル 100% デトロイトスマッシュ , Furu Kauru 100 Pāsento Detoroito Sumasshu ) : Izuku draws out 100 percent of his power while having Full Cowl - 100% activated and delivers a straightforward lunging punch at his opponent.
