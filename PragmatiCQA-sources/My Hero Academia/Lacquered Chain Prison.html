Description

This move is shot out from the user‘s arms and forms a wooden prison around the enemy as an absolute preemptive confinement. It takes the enemy by surprise, restricting their ability to move at every opportunity. It can also be used to restrain multiple opponents simultaneously.

Trivia

  • This is the earliest named Super Move in the series, although it was interrupted before it could be displayed fully.
