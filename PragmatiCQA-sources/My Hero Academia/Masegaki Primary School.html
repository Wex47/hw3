Masegaki Primary School ( 市立 間 ( マ ) 瀬 ( セ ) 垣 ( ガキ ) 小学校 , Shiritsu Masegaki Shōgakkō ) , as its name suggests, is a school attended by several younger students.

Overview

Masegaki Primary School has a reputation for housing problematic children who have trouble respecting those who are older than them. The children of this school are noted to be precociously aware of social concerns and relationships.

Trivia

  • The name of the school contains the word 'gaki' which can be translated as "brat" or a troublesome child.
    • In fact, the whole name 'masegaki' means “precocious brat”, appropriately describing the children studying there.
