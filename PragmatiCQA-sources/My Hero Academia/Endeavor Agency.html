Description

Endeavor's Hero Agency is a luxurious skyscraper type building with various floors. The front entrance is decorated with a stylized flaming "E" for Endeavor.

Inside, there is a large office space with several desks and computers. Over thirty sidekicks including Burnin are constantly dealing with requests and other hero work for Endeavor. The " Flaming Sidekickers " are very passionate and their catchphrase is "Flame On". As the agency of the new No. 1 Hero, the office stays very busy with many of them remaining on standby or going out on patrol.

Endeavor's personal office is a very large room with only a relatively small table and Endeavor's desk inside.

Many of the sidekicks working at the agency have fire and heat type Quirks.
