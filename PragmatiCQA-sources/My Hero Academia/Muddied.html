Muddied ( 混 ( こん ) 濁 ( だく ) , Kondaku ) is the Quirk used by Kagero Okuta .

Description

Muddied grants the user the ability to cause minor amnesia to a person if they make contact with their head, making their memories of the previous five minutes and the next five minutes vague.
