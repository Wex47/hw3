Samidare Asui ( 蛙 ( あ ) 吹 ( すい ) 五月雨 ( さみだれ ) , Asui Samidare ) is Tsuyu Asui 's younger brother.

Appearance

Samidare has black hair that obscures his right eye, dark green eyes that are seen half closed, and a frog like appearance. He has blush marks on his cheeks inherited from his mother.

Trivia

  • Samidare's kanji in his name means "Early May rain".
  • His name is also similar to the pronunciation and spelling of "Salamander", the name of an amphibian. This goes along with his family's froggy theme.
