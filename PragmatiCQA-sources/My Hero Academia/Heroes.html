This article is about the ending song. For the profession, see Heroes .

HEROES is the first ending theme of My Hero Academia anime adaptation. It is performed by Brian the Sun as their major debut.

Differences

  • On the first four episodes, Izuku is shown running on his middle school uniform. In Episode 5 onward, however, he is shown wearing his U.A. uniform.
  • Episode 3 features an extended intro as Izuku mentally prepares for his entrance exam.
