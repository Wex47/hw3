Chizuo ( 千 ( ち ) 津 ( づ ) 夫 ( お ) , Chizuo ) was the maternal grandfather of Tenko Shimura and Hana Shimura , the father of Nao Shimura and husband of Mako . Not much is known about him currently, except that he died when his grandson’s Quirk went out of control.
