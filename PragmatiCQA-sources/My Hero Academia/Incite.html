Incite ( 煽 ( せん ) 動 ( どう ) , Sendō ) is the Meta Ability used by Koku Hanabata .

Description

Incite lines the user's voice with an electromagnetic pulse. If someone who considers the Meta Ability user a worthy leader hears the user's voice, then their physical and mental state will be significantly enhanced. The louder the user's voice is, the more effective the Meta Ability becomes.

Weaknessess

Using this power to strengthen someone else and their Meta Ability takes a toll on the recipient's body.

Incite only works on people who are devout followers of the Meta Ability's user. Likewise, if the loyalty of the Meta Ability's targets is overcome by other emotions, such as fear, then the effects of Incite are nullified.

Usage

Monikered "Trumpet" for his loud influence, Koku uses his Meta Ability to increase the prowess of his followers in the Meta Liberation Army . He speaks from his political campaign van and rallies his followers against specific targets. Mr. Compress compares him to a preacher leading his flock.

Koku can push his followers to their limits by enhancing his Meta Ability using Detnerat 's patented Sevens Loud mask.
