Abegawa Tenchu Kai ( 阿 ( あ ) 辺 ( べ ) 川 ( がわ ) 天 ( てん ) 忠 ( ちゅう ) 會 ( かい ) , Abegawa Tenchū-kai ) was a former Yakuza organization appearing in My Hero Academia: Vigilantes .

Plot

After the Tenchu Kai was categorized as a villainous organization by the police, their headquarters is invaded by the vigilante Stendhal and its high-ranking executives were all killed in combat.

Trivia

  • Each member's Quirk and appearance is based on that of an Avenger :

    • Tetsu resembles Iron Man .
    • Haruhisa resembles the Hulk .
    • Soji resembles Thor .
    • The remaining three unnamed members resemble Winter Soldier , Black Widow , and Hawkeye .
