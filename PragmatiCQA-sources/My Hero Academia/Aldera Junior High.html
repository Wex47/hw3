Aldera Junior High ( 折 ( おる ) 寺 ( でら ) 中学校 , Orudera Chūgakkō ) is the school where Izuku Midoriya and Katsuki Bakugo attended in Musutafu, Japan , before entering U.A. High School .

Trivia

  • Orudera is similar to the Japanese pronunciation to Alderaan, a notable world in the Star Wars universe.
  • The school's name is spelled in an ateji manner to make it look like a native Japanese name, its two characters meaning "fold" (折) and "temple" (寺).
