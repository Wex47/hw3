Juzo Moashi ( 百 ( も ) 足 ( あし ) 従 ( じゅう ) 造 ( ぞう ) , Moashi Jūzō ) , also known as the hero Centipeder ( センチピーダー , Senchipīdā ) , was a sidekick affiliated with Sir Nighteye 's Hero Office, who later became the successor to the company after his mentor passed away.
