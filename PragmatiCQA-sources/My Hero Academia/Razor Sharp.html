Razor Sharp ( 刃 ( じん ) 鋭 ( えい ) , Jin'ei ) is the Quirk used by Togaru Kamakiri .

  • 1 Description
  • 2 Usage
  • 4 Site Navigation

Description

Razor Sharp allows the user to produce large blades from their body.

Togaru's blades can come in a variety of shapes and sizes, and from any part of his body. These blades are strong enough to cut through metal pipes (which are commonly made up of steel or iron), and durable enough to withstand a point-blank explosion.

Usage

Razor Sharp is useful in close-range combat, allowing Togaru to quickly and easily slice through objects in his path.

Togaru has been seen producing blades from his forearms in order to attack, and producing blades from his feet in order to stabilize his footing.
