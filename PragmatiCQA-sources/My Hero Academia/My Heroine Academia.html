My Heroine Academia ( 私のヒーローアカデミア , Watashi no Hīrō Academia ) is a spin-off gag manga written and illustrated by Yoco Akiyama and is published in Weekly Shonen Jump and in Jump Giga .

Summary

The manga includes various comedy gags mostly involving the female cast of the series.
