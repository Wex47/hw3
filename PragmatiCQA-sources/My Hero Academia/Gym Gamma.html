Gym Gamma ( 体育館 γ ( ガンマ ) , Taiikukan Ganma ) , commonly known as the Training Kitchen Land ( トレーニングの台所ランド , Torēningu no Daidokoro Rando ) or TDL , is a training area in U.A. High School .

Description

Gym Gamma consists of a large, empty, and enclosed space. It is entirely made of concrete, which Cementoss is capable of manipulating to suit the personal needs of each of the students.

History

The gym was created by Cementoss as a place where students could develop new techniques that could be applied on the Provisional Hero License Exam , under the supervision of Ectoplasm , Midnight , and Cementoss himself. It was used by both Class 1-A and Class 1-B .

Trivia

  • Similar to the Unforeseen Simulation Joint , the initials TDL are a reference to the theme park Tokyo Disneyland .
