Ibara Shiozaki ( 塩 ( しお ) 崎 ( ざき ) 茨 ( いばら ) , Shiozaki Ibara ) , also known as Vine ( ヴァイン , Vain ) , previously Maria ( マリア , Maria ) , is a student in Class 1-B at U.A. High School , training to become a Pro Hero .

Appearance

Ibara is a girl of medium height with green, thorn-covered vines for hair, one set wrapped around her forehead a couple of times in the shape of a cross. Because of her Quirk , she can grow them at will but she likes to keep her vines at a reasonably manageable length: just above her waist. Her eyes are dark green with long lower eyelashes, and she's regarded as cute by Denki Kaminari .

Her hero costume consists of a plain white robe and black knee-high boots.

Personality

Ibara is a very modest and courteous person who first makes her humility clear after Present Mic calls her an assassin. Although graceful and soft-spoken, she is not afraid to make her pure intentions known. She seems to be of a highly religious inclination, due to her talking about heaven and divine beings in passing on multiple occasions, and her super moves having somewhat religious names, such as "Faith's Shield" and "Crucifixion".

Despite initially seeming very pure and kind, Ibara shows an unexpected vindictive side, best demonstrated in the Joint Training Arc, with her harsh judgement of Class 1-A , as well as her telling Jurota Shishida that she hoped the "fires of hell" would relieve him of his sins and viewed his strategy of using her as a decoy to be "sinful".

Ibara showed her gratitude after receiving a second chance to participate in the U.A. Sports Festival and made her objective to spread well across the world apparent before her fight with Denki. She also prefers to be fair and square, as she disagreed with Neito Monoma 's plan to throw the Obstacle Race and placed fourth instead. She also viewed having discreetly stolen Minoru Mineta 's headband with her Quirk as an underhanded tactic and displayed some sense of regret for it.

Ibara values honesty and is heavily fixated on the concept of truth, often refusing (if not being reluctant) to participate in strategies she deems as deceitful. This is demonstrated when she said that Shihai Kuroiro needs to 'drastically re-examine' himself for his sneaky tactics, as well as apologizing to Gigantomachia during the War for their ambush.

Abilities

Overall Abilities : Ibara has proven herself to be a highly capable individual, as her skills allowed her to earn fourth place in the U.A. Entrance Exam , having a fair balance of both villain and rescue points. She has incredible proficiency over her Quirk's applications, utilizing it in the same manner as Kamui Woods ' Arbor Quirk. Ibara seems to be the stationary-type combatant, relying on the restricting properties of her vines in battles while she stands still. However, due to this fighting style, Ibara leaves herself vulnerable to attacks from an opponent with superior speed, or close-quarters combat abilities, such as Tenya Ida . Her abilities eventually garnered the attention of The Lurkers (consisting of Edgeshot , Kamui Woods, and Mt. Lady ), giving Ibara the opportunity to intern under the trio of powerful heroes and improve on close and efficient team maneuvers.

Quirk

Vines ( ツル , Tsuru ) : Ibara's Quirk gives her hair-like vines that grow on her head. She can lengthen and manipulate the vines, allowing her to attack, defend and grapple. She also has the ability to detach them at will. The vines will grow back, provided her head receives proper sunlight and water.

Super Moves

  • Via Dolorosa ( ヴィアドロローサ , Via Dororōsa ) : Ibara covers an entire area with her vines, trapping anything that touches them.
  • Crucifixion ( 磔刑 ( クルセフィクション ) , Kurusefikushon ) : Ibara uses her vines to trap her opponent in a ball of vines suspended in the air to restrict their movement. The shape resembles that of a rubber band ball. She uses this move on Denki to insulate him, making him unable to discharge his quirk.
  • Faith's Shield ( 信仰の盾 ( フェイスズシールド ) , Feisuzu Shīrudo ) : Ibara swiftly creates a wall of vines to protect herself before detaching the vines. She used it as a second line of defense against Denki.
  • Fig Tree ( いちじくの 木 ( き ) , Ichijiku no ki ) : Ibara creates a wall of vines to block an upcoming attack while the roots capture her oppenent.

Trivia

  • Ibara's name contains the kanji for "salt" ( 塩 , shio ) , "promontory" ( 崎 , zaki ) , and "thorn" ( 茨 , ibara ) .
  • Ibara ranked fourth during the U.A. Entrance Exam with 36 Villain Points and 32 Rescue Points.
  • Ibara's favorite food is bread.
  • Denki refers to Ibara as "acorn-eyed", a slang term for someone with large, dark eyes.
  • Notably, the only person Ibara ever deceives is Minoru, both taking his headband in a deceitful manner during the Sports Festival, as well as lying to lure him out during the Summer Training Camp. She only expresses regret for the first occasion, likely due to him attempting to peep on her and the other 1-B girls in the second.
  • Ibara was originally planned to be in Class 1-A.
  • Horikoshi finds it hard to draw Ibara's hair.
  • Ibara's religious nature, appearance, and Quirk allude to Christianity in several ways:
    • Ibara's thorny hair is similar to the crown of thorns that is said to have been placed on Jesus Christ 's head at the time of his crucifixion .
    • Her Hero Costume consists of a plain white robe, which looks similar to the one worn in many depictions of Jesus Christ.
    • Her favorite food, bread, is said to resemble the body of Jesus and is often given in church communions.
    • Her former hero name "Maria" is the Latin name for the Virgin Mary .
    • Her birthday is September 8, which is also the birthday of the Virgin Mary.
    • All of her Super Moves are named after biblical terms.
