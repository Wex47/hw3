Pliabody ( 軟 ( なん ) 体 ( たい ) , Nantai ) is the Quirk used by Yawara Chatora .

Description

Pliabody allows the user to stretch and flatten their own body at will, granting them inhuman flexibility.

Pliabody is useful for rescue operations, allowing Tiger to get through narrow passages. In combat, it can be used to easily dodge blows and restrain opponents.

Trivia

  • Pliabody could be considered the inverse of Elasticity , as it turns one's body into elastic, whereas the latter turns only the surroundings of the user.
