History

The Luminescent Baby was born in Qing Qing, China and was the first person in the world to be born with a Quirk.

Abilities

Quirk

Unnamed Luminescence Quirk : This Quirk allows the Luminescent Baby to emit a bright light from their skin. This Quirk was used back when the Baby was a newborn. It likely became stronger as the Baby grew up.
