Ganma Asui ( 蛙 ( あ ) 吹 ( すい ) 頑 ( がん ) 馬 ( ま ) , Asui Ganma ) is the father of Tsuyu , Samidare and Satsuki Asui and the husband of Beru Asui .

Appearance

Ganma has a very frog-like appearance, more so than the rest of his family, as he appears to have the head of a brown toad. He is a tall, stocky, middle-aged man with black, short-cropped hair and black eyes.

He wears a plain business suit with a blue-dotted tie. In the anime, a green frog clip rests in the middle of his tie.

Personality

Not much is known about his personality at this time. It is likely that he exhibits a caring demeanor, much like a typical good father stereotype. The reading of his name as stubborn horse could allude to his possible obstinate demeanor.

History

At some point, he met Beru and the two married and had three children.

As described by Tsuyu, her father and mother have to travel for work often. He lets his eldest child, Tsuyu, take care of her younger siblings, Samidare and Satsuki.
