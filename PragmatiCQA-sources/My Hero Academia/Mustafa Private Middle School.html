Mustafa Private Middle School ( 私立 結 ( むす ) 田 ( た ) 府 ( ふ ) 中学校 , Shiritsu Musutafu Chūgakkō ) is the school where Eijiro Kirishima and Mina Ashido attended before entering U.A. High School . Its location is unknown.
