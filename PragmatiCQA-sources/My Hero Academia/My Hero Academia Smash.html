My Hero Academia Smash!! ( 僕のヒーローアカデミアすまっしゅ！！ , Boku no Hīrō Academia Sumasshu!! ) is a spin-off gag manga written and illustrated by Hirofumi Neda and is published in Weekly Shonen Jump and the Jump+ App. The manga debuted on November 9, 2015 and ended in November 2017 with the 5th volume.

On November 17, 2018, Viz Media announced that it has obtained the license for My Hero Academia: Smash!! . The first volume was released on August 6, 2019.
