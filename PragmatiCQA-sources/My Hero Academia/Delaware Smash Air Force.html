Delaware Smash Air Force ( デラウェアスマッシュエアフォース , Derawea Sumasshu Eafōsu ) is a Super Move performed by Izuku Midoriya using the One For All Quirk .

Description

After training with All Might , Izuku learns to concentrate and draw out twenty percent of his power for a short time. Using support gloves made by Mei Hatsume , Izuku can direct a blast of compressed air against his foe from his fingers.

The attack possessed enough concussive force to stun Gentle Criminal while he was in mid-air, giving Izuku an opening to catch up to him.

By using all five of his fingers, he can launch up to four Delaware Smash Air Forces simultaneously.
