Appearance

Tomoko is a young woman of medium height with long, emerald green hair and big round yellow eyes that are almost always open all the way, with three small eyelashes located toward the top of each eye. As a part of her costume, she draws pink marks on her cheeks that resemble whiskers and a pink circle around her right eye.

Ragdoll sports a yellow version of the Pussycats' hero costume .
