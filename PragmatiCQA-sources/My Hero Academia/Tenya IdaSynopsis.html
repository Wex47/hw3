History

Born as the second son of the Ida Family , a family that has been in heroics for generations, Tenya aspired to be like his older brother, Tensei , who became a famous hero in his family. Tenya once asked his older brother what motivated him to be a hero, to which Tensei replied that he wanted to be a 'cool' type of Hero that helps others when they are in need. When Tenya speaks of his admiration for his older brother, Tensei is joyful that Tenya looks up to him and says that he must be a great hero since Tenya admires him.

Before attending U.A. High School , Tenya previously went to Soumei Junior High School, a prestigious, private school.

Synopsis

Entrance Exam Arc

Tenya first appears at the U.A. Entrance Exam . In the auditorium before the practical portion, Tenya questions Present Mic about the four villains listed during the explanation. After Present Mic answers his question, Tenya yells at another participant, Izuku Midoriya , for muttering too much as he is being a distraction. Tenya later confronts Izuku just before the practical portion begins. He notices Izuku trying to talk to a female participant, Ochaco Uraraka , but stops him from ruining her concentration with him drawing more bad attention to Izuku.

Tenya manages to do very well during the test, scoring forty five points well into the exam by using his Engine Quirk . When the giant robot worth zero points shows up, Tenya flees the area despite noticing that Izuku was on the ground paralyzed with fear. He is soon shocked to see Izuku spring into action, where he saves Ochaco from the zero pointer bot by destroying it with one devastating blow.

Tenya is the only one among the viewing participants to notice that Izuku saved Ochaco. This leads Tenya to question the nature of the exam and ponders whether heroic acts had a part in the exam all along. He says to himself that he would have done the same if he hadn't been on the exam and that maybe it's part of the test.

Quirk Apprehension Test Arc

April arrives and the U.A. academic year begins. On the very first day in Class 1-A , Tenya argues with Katsuki Bakugo over putting his feet on the desk. Tenya explains that this action is disrespectful to the school, but Katsuki doesn't care. Tenya tries to calm down and introduce himself properly, but Katsuki's only reply is that he'll simply crush him. Tenya is shocked that Katsuki would threaten his own classmate just as Izuku enters the class, surprised he is classmates with both Katsuki and Tenya. Seeing him, Tenya decides to re-introduce himself to Izuku instead. He apologizes for his previous treatment and commends him for seeing what he had not during the practical exam, that saving others was just as important as defeating the villains. Tenya even goes on to say that Izuku is the superior student.

Ochaco arrives and interrupts them by introducing herself to Izuku just before Shota Aizawa arrives and reveals himself to be Class 1-A's teacher. Shota takes the students to the P.E. field for a Quirk Apprehension Test . Tenya completes the fifty meter dash in 3.04 seconds, but questions why Izuku is struggling through them. When Izuku finally uses his Quirk, it shocks Tenya and the rest of the class with his impressive ball toss.

He notices that Izuku has barely trained with his Quirk, due to his finger injury. In the test, Tenya sees that he has placed 4th. Later on, he asks Izuku if his fingers had healed. He tells Izuku that Shota can encourage students through deceptions to get the best out of a student. He then sees Ochaco walk to them and remembers that Shota gave her an infinity score on the test. She calls Izuku "Deku," as Izuku tells them it's an insult Katsuki gave him as children, but changes his view on the name as Ochaco calls it cute, as Tenya is surprised at this change of mind. The next day, Tenya is seen wearing his hero costume .

Battle Trial Arc

During the Battle Trial Arc, Tenya is paired up with Katsuki as the villains. Tenya complains that Katsuki left him alone to guard the fake Nuclear Bomb. He contacts Katsuki and asks about the situation, but Katsuki just tells him to guard the fake bomb and then hangs up, much to Tenya's annoyance.

While guarding the bomb, Tenya decides to act more like a villain. He takes his villain role so seriously that Ochaco can't help but laugh. This makes him realize that she is in the room, and says that he has been expecting her. He goes on to say that he is prepared to counter her Quirk by cleaning the floor and starts to laugh like a villain.

Suddenly, Tenya is distracted by Katsuki's Explosion , shocked by his action. Ochaco uses his distraction to her advantage and uses Skill Release to jump over Tenya to grab the bomb, but Tenya uses his Engine Quirk to quickly snatch it away before she could reach it.

The floor is destroyed by Izuku's Detroit Smash and Ochaco uses the opportunity to launch her Home Run Comet attack, which distracts Tenya long enough for Ochaco to grab the bomb, making Tenya and Katsuki lose the battle trial.

Tenya goes to the monitor room to have his critique. All Might says that the best participant in the battle trial was Tenya, which greatly shocks him. Momo Yaoyorozu , says that Katsuki and Izuku using large ranged attacks in an indoor area was too idiotic; Ochaco's game got loose halfway and her last attack was reckless.

She continues on to say that he was the best due to him adapting the most to the situation by organizing a counter strategy against his opponent that would allow him to protect the bomb the best way possible while hindering his opponent. Tenya can't help but feel enormously happy being praised in that way.

U.S.J. Arc

Tenya is approached by reporters, asking him what he thinks of All Might as a teacher, to which he replies that he's happy to have an impressive role model as a teacher. In class, Tenya says that a leader is only worthy from a democratic choice of the people's will and proposes a vote, to which is allowed. After the vote, he is disappointed to see that he didn't get any votes.

At lunch, Tenya assures Izuku that he will do a fine job as Class President, going on to say that he has guts and right judgment, which is why Tenya voted for him (even though he wanted to be Class President). After Ochaco asks if he's from an upper-class family, Tenya confirms that he is, telling Ochaco and Izuku that he's from the Ida Family, a prestigious family that's been in heroics for generations. He then reveals that Turbo Hero Ingenium is his older brother and aspires to be like him one day.

After the siren goes off, he gets pushed around during the evacuation, eventually pushed against the window, which he sees that the "infiltration" is just the media. Tenya decides to step up and stop the chaotic evacuation, asking Ochaco to make him float, allowing him to jump up into the air and then uses his Engine Quirk to sprint onto the exit sign, where he is finally able to tell everybody to calm down since its just the media, which stops the chaotic evacuation.

In class, Izuku decides to step down as Class President and makes Tenya his replacement, saying that he is ideally the right choice after stopping the chaos in the lunchroom to which everyone agrees. Thus, Tenya officially becomes the Class President of 1-A.

Tenya changes into his Hero Costume in preparation for the rescue training trial and orders his classmates to get into two lines according to numbers to get into the bus smoothly (although this turned out to be pointless due to the layout of the bus). Tenya is later seen applauding Thirteen's speech. However, before anything else could progress, the trail is interrupted by the arrival of a group of actual villains, much to Tenya's surprise.

Tenya tries to evacuate with his class, but their attempt is foiled when Kurogiri begins to separate the class with his warping ability. However, he manages to escape from the villain's ability, saving Ochaco and Rikido Sato in the process. They are further joined by Hanta Sero , Mina Ashido , Mezo Shoji and Thirteen , who all confront Kurogiri himself.

Thirteen asks Tenya to dash back to U.A. as fast as he could and inform the school of their situation. Tenya is at first reluctant to do so as it would be disgraceful to abandon his classmates, but after being encouraged by Rikido, Hanta, Ochaco and Thirteen, with the latter being taken down by Kurogiri, Tenya decides to run back to U.A., with Kurogiri in pursuit of the student.

As Tenya approaches the exit, he is intercepted by one of Kurogiri's portals, but Mezo distracts the villain long enough for Tenya to reach the exit, which he is forced to open manually, however, Kurogiri tries to stop him once again, only for his attempt to be foiled by a combined effort coming from Ochaco, Hanta and Rikido. With the villain finally being taken off his back, Tenya manages to escape the U.S.J. building.

Tenya, on his way to U.A., runs into All Might and tells him about the situation. Tenya manages to get to U.A. and gets the Hero some backup. He arrives back at the U.S.J. with the hero backup, just in time to save Izuku and All Might. Tenya is then seen with the rest of his class after the battle with the Villain Alliance ends.

U.A. Sports Festival Arc

Tenya then says that he will be challenging himself against Izuku so that he can grow. Tenya is then seen teaming up with Shoto Todoroki , Momo and Denki Kaminari . Shoto explains that Tenya will be in the front using his Quirk to act as a physical defense. After the Human Cavalry Battle begins, Tenya and his team confront Izuku and his team.

With only a minute left, Tenya tells his team to hold on tight as he uses his move, Recipro Burst, to forcibly accelerate his movement to blazing speeds allowing him and his team to speed past their enemies in a blink of an eye which catches Izuku's team off guard and allows Shoto to grab Izuku's 10 million headband in the confusion. Tenya then explains his move but adds that he will suffer from engine stall for a short time, meaning he and his team are unable to move. He then says reminds Izuku that he is challenging himself against him. Izuku's team attack Tenya and his team. Unable to move, Tenya and his team are forced to fight Izuku's team for the remaining minute of the Human Cavalry Battle.

After Izuku manages to get a headband from Shoto, Tenya tells Shoto to pull himself together which snaps Shoto back to reality. For the last ten seconds, Tenya's team prepares to battle, but the Human Cavalry Battle ends before they could battle. Tenya's team places first, allowing him and his team to participate in the final event. After the lunch break begins, Ochaco talks to him about him being sneaky with his Recipro Burst move, but Tenya replies that he was not being sneaky and all he wanted to do was be a proper rival to Izuku.

The final event is a tournament event and Tenya's opponent in the first round is Mei Hatsume . Mei meets her opponent, asking if he is Tenya, to which he replies that he is. Mei gives Tenya some support items so that their fight can be on equal terms, which he accepts. During the recreational events, Tenya is seen drinking cool drinks to renew his energy. After Shoto defeats Izuku, Tenya, along with Ochaco, Tsuyu, and Minoru Mineta , go to Kango's office to see Izuku. However, Tenya is forced to leave as Kango states that Izuku is going to undergo surgery.

Tenya faces his opponent, Ibara Shiozaki in his second round match . During the match, Ibara attacks Tenya with her thorns, but Tenya's maneuverability allowed him to avoid her thorns. While in the air, Tenya notes that now that Shoto is using his firepower, his options are very narrow, causing Tenya to think that his best option is using Recipro Burst to finish the match quickly.

However, along the way, Tenya realizes that there is ice in his mufflers and he slows down. Shoto then freezes Tenya's entire body. Due to being unable to move, Tenya is declared defeated and is eliminated from the tournament event. Tenya is angry at himself for not thinking more about his opponent's tactics and apologizes to his brother for losing.

Tenya, along with Izuku and Ochaco, watches the match between Fumikage and Katsuki. After Fumikage surrenders, Tenya tells Izuku and Ochaco to watch the final match closely for their revenge later, to which Izuku and Ochaco agree. Suddenly, Tenya's phone starts ringing and excuses himself.

However, Tenya's mother tells him that she is not phoning about that, though she does apologize for his loss and tells Tenya to stay calm for her real reason. Tenya leaves the Sports Festival and goes to the Hosu General Hospital where his brother is.

At the hospital, Tenya immediately barges into the room his brother is in and is shocked at Tensei's injuries. Tensei tells Tenya he cannot stand the idea either and asks him to carry on the torch and use Ingenium as his Hero name.

After arriving inside, Tenya tells Izuku that he doesn't have to be worried about his older brother as he will be fine and apologizes for causing him unnecessary concern. During the Hero Informatics Period, Shota shows the students the Pro Hero nominations, which reveals that Tenya received 301 Pro Hero nominations to which Ochaco congratulates him.

Afterwards, Class 1-A has to decide on hero names. Tenya and Izuku are the last ones to reveal their Hero names. Tenya struggles on whether to use his brother's Hero name, Ingenium or not. Tenya is unable to bring himself to write down Tensei's Hero name Ingenium and instead writes down and shows his classmates his own name.

Tenya chooses the Hosu City Hero Office to train at. The day of the work place training arrives and Tenya is at the train station with his class. As Tenya goes to board his train, Izuku tells Tenya that if he needs someone to talk to, he can talk to him and Ochaco (with Ochaco nodding in agreement) as they are friends. Tenya says that he will. He then walks away to his train with a scowl on his face.

Tenya arrives at Hosu City's office. In the borough of Hosu City, Tenya in his Hero costume goes on patrol with Manual . Tenya ponders that the Hero Killer Stain was the one who critically injured his older brother Ingenium, deciding that he himself will chase after Stain, unable to forgive the Hero Killer for his crime, declaring that he will avenge his older brother and kill him.

Tenya concludes that Stain will without a doubt be in Hosu to attack more Pro Heroes. Tenya, in a silent rage, tells Stain to appear so that he himself can dispose of Stain with his own hands.

Normal Hero apologizes for his question to which Tenya replies that he appreciates his warning. However, Tenya ponders that if he doesn't get revenge, what should he do with his rage and emotions.

Chaos emerges in Hosu and Normal Hero, who at the time was patrolling with Tenya, tells Tenya to go with him. Suddenly, Tenya sees someone in an alley. he approaches closer and sees that it is the Hero Killer Stain. Tenya immediately charges at Stain. Stain prepares to walk away, but Tenya, seething with rage, yells at Stain that he is the brother of a Pro Hero that he cut down and he is here in his older brother's stead. Stain reveals to Tenya that he let Tensei live in order to feed rumors. Stain says that both him and his brother are weak because they are fake Heroes. Tenya, even though he is defeated, rebukes Stain's claim that his older brother Tensei is weak, yelling that Tensei was a splendid Hero and that he will kill Stain to avenge his older brother. Stain tells Tenya to save the injured Pro Hero behind him, advising the defeated younger brother to act selflessly by rescuing others first and not be selfish by brandishing his power for his own sake. Stain states that seeking revenge just to satisfy one's selfish desires is the act furthest from the title of "hero" which is why he will die now.

Tearing up, Tenya refuses to believe Stain's advise, yelling that Stain is just criminal scum who hurt his older brother. Stain is about to deliver the finishing blow, but is punched in the face by the timely appearance of Izuku, which stops Stain from killing Tenya, shocking Tenya.

Tenya orders Izuku not to get involved since his fight with Stain has nothing to do with him. Izuku responds to Tenya's order, saying that Heroes would not be able to do anything by following Tenya's logic. Tenya watches the battle between Izuku and Stain. After Izuku lands a powerful blow on Stain, Tenya is shocked at Izuku's fast movements, comparing Izuku's movements to Katsuki's. However, Stain managed to get some blood from Izuku when Izuku was dodging his slashes, causing Izuku to become immobilized. Stain prepares to kill Tenya once again, but suddenly, a column of fire and ice attacks Stain, forcing Stain to stop his attack on Tenya and dodge the fire and ice. Shoto reveals himself as the one that attacked Stain.

Shoto then proceeds to battle Stain. As their battle gets underway, Tenya begs Shoto to stop fighting, as he inherited Ingenium's Hero name. Shoto criticizes Tenya for making a vengeful face, saying that the Ingenium he knows would not make such a face. Izuku and Shoto team up to battle Stain, stating that they will protect Tenya. Tenya starts to feel regret for his actions as he has not been acting like a Hero should be. Stain paralyzes Izuku with his Quirk, causing a regretful Tenya to tell them to stop. Shoto replies that if Tenya wants to stop them he must stand up. As Stain breaks through his wall of ice, Shoto encourages Tenya to set his eyes on the person he wants to be. Tenya remembers that he wants to be like his brother, Ingenium and tries to break free of Stain's Quirk.

Tenya is ashamed that he needs protection from his friends, Izuku and Shoto. He then remembers his brother's motivation for being a Hero; that helping people is the best motivation for being a Hero. Tenya agrees with Stain; that he is a failure as a Hero and pales in comparison to Izuku and Shoto.

Stain's Quirk wears off and Tenya gets up with the motivation of not wanting to be outpaced as a Hero by Izuku, Shoto and his brother. Tenya uses Recipro Burst and smashes his leg into Stain's katana, breaking the katana in half. Tenya then kicks Stain away. Tenya apologizes to Izuku and Shoto for getting them involved in his problems, not wanting them to spill anymore blood for his sake.

Stain criticizes Tenya for having a change of heart, saying that a man's character never changes. Shoto tells Tenya to pay no heed to Stain, but Tenya says that Stain is correct about him not being a true Hero. Tenya tells Stain that he cannot give up so easily, as doing so means that Ingenium would die. Tenya asks Shoto to cool down his leg, but Stain attacks Shoto with daggers to which Tenya intercepts and falls down to the ground. Shoto cools Tenya's leg, restoring Tenya's mobility. Tenya uses Recipro Extend and leaps into the air towards Stain. He hits Stain in the stomach with his speed enhanced kick while Izuku punches Stain in the face with his enhanced might.

Stain makes one last ditch attempt to kill Tenya, but Tenya retaliates with a kick and Shoto shoots a stream of fire into Stain's face. Tenya and Shoto's additional attacks finally cause Stain to lose consciousness, defeating him. Tenya and Izuku land safely onto the ground thanks to Shoto's ice ramp. Shoto decides to tie up Stain with Izuku suggesting that they remove Stain's weapons.

Izuku, Shoto, Tenya and Native leave the alley with the unconscious restrained Stain in tow. Tenya offers to pull Stain but Shoto tells Tenya that his arms are in bad shape. After Gran Torino and Endeavor's sidekicks arrive, Tenya apologizes for his un-heroic actions with Izuku saying that he is also sorry for overlooking his pain, causing the speedy teenager to burst into tears; Shoto tells Tenya to pull himself together because he is the Class President, with Tenya complying. Out of the blue, the winged Nomu-like creature grabs Izuku; suddenly, Stain wakes up and defeats the winged Nomu-like creature while saving Izuku in the process.

After Endeavor arrives, Stain's bandaged mask falls off and reveals his true face, shocking Izuku. Stain walks forward and declares that the only one who is allowed to kill him is All Might, unleashing a bloody malefic aura in the process. Tenya falls to his knees out of fear, along with several other heroes. After Stain loses consciousness for the last time, Tenya becomes shocked that Stain is still standing. Tenya, along with Izuku and Shoto are taken to the Hosu General Hospital to have their wounds treated.

The next day at Hosu General Hospital, the recuperating Izuku states to Shoto and Tenya that it is a miracle that they are still alive as Stain could probably have killed them if he wanted to. Shoto praises Tenya for his bravery but Tenya admits that he doesn't deserve any praise. The Chief of Hosu Police, Kenji Tsuragamae arrives and tells Izuku, Shoto and Tenya that the Hero Killer is undergoing treatment. He states that Izuku, Shoto and Tenya acted on their own without the supervision of their superiors and as such violated the rules, thus they along with their superiors (Gran Torino, Endeavor and Manual due to their negligence) will be given punishment.

However, Kenji states that there were limited witnesses and as a result there will not be any punishment for Izuku, Shoto and Tenya but they won't be receiving any praise either from the public as the events that occurred yesterday will be covered up.

Tenya apologizes to Manual but Manual criticizes Tenya for his actions, asking him to never do it again. Tenya along with Izuku and Shoto thank the Chief of Hosu Police for not punishing them. Kenji states that Tenya along with Izuku and Shoto have a promising future and thanks them for their heroic actions.

The next day of the newspapers publication of Stain's defeat, Izuku returns to the room where Tenya says his physical examination reveals that his left hand's fingers won't move properly, although a surgery will fix the damage. Tenya states that until he becomes a true Hero, he will leave his left hand in its injured state as a reminder of his un-heroic actions during the encounter with the Hero Killer; he will only undergo the surgery when he become a true Hero.

Izuku thinks that apologizing would have little effect and sees that Tenya realizes his mistake. Izuku says to Tenya that his hand is also like his; Izuku states that they must become stronger together to which Tenya agrees. Shoto apologizes causing Izuku to ask why; Shoto states that whenever he is involved, people's hands go bad. Tenya and Izuku are surprised that even Shoto can crack a joke but Shoto says he is serious, causing the three young Heroes to laugh together.

The workplace training comes to an end and Tenya returns to U.A.. In Class 1-A, after Denki makes a remark about the Hero Killer, Tenya says its fine because he understands if people find the Hero Killer "cool" due to his convictions.
