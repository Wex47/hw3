Bombify ( 爆弾化 , Bakudan-ka ) was one of the Quirks possessed by Number 6 .

Description

This Quirk allows the user to manipulate parts of their body to grow bombs of various sizes out of them.

It appears to be some semblance of an artificial Quirk, created due to the Bomber cells that reside within its user, Number 6.

Usage

Thanks to the Bomber cells within his body, as well as the multiple other Quirks he possesses, Number 6 most frequently uses this Quirk in-conjunction with his Self-Detonation Quirk in order to explode the bombs he manifests on his fists, followed by his Regeneration Quirk to re-grow them back good as new.
