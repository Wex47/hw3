Klayd ( クレイド , Kureido ) is a fictional country in Europe where some events of My Hero Academia: World Heroes' Mission take place. Klayd neighbors another fictional country called Otheon .

Description

Klayd is located on an island at the southwestern rim of the Celtic Sea. The island is roughly egg-shaped and slightly larger than Ireland. It shares the island with the neighboring country of Otheon, being the larger of the two nations.

Not much is known about its culture and geography, although it appears to be similar to that of Otheon.

Trivia

  • The name of the country is a reference to the Star Wars planet Crait .
