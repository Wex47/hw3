Shinya Kamihara ( 紙 ( かみ ) 原 ( はら ) 伸 ( しん ) 也 ( や ) , Kamihara Shin'ya ) , also known as the Ninja Hero: Edgeshot ( 忍者ヒーロー エッジショット , Ninja Hīrō Ejjishotto ) , is the former No. 5 Pro Hero , and the current No. 4 Pro-Hero. He is also the leader of The Lurkers .

Appearance

Shinya is a slim, muscular man who stands a bit shorter than most of his top-class heroic peers. He has gray hair shaped into three sharp points that cover the right side of his face and dark gray eyes, and he wears a mask over his lower face, covering his nose and mouth.

Shinya's hero costume adapts the appearance of a traditional ninja. He wears a blue mask that covers his mouth, tied at the back of his head with red yarn. Shinya sports red shinobi shozoku underneath a blue robe. His arms are covered in a thin, dark cloth that tucks into his white forearm sleeves. There are red straps attached to his belt and his robe that extend outward.

Shinya has a gold mask attached to his shoulder and sports a signature red scarf that features a white patch attached to the front of it. Edgeshot finishes his costume with blue socks and yellow zori sandals.
