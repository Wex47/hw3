Audio

"The Story of Tonight (Reprise)" is the 12th song of Act One of the musical Hamilton .

At the Schuyler-Hamilton wedding, Aaron Burr confesses under pressure to Alexander Hamilton , that he is having an affair with the wife of a British officer. Hamilton advises him to listen to his heart and "go get her", but Burr decides to wait for the right opportunity as he has in the past.
