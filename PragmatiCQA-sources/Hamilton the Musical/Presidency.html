The Presidency is a top administrative and governmental seat in the office of the president of a nation. The first president of the United States was George Washington , who contributed in the establishment of a stable government with a president to make important decisions in part with the nation. The musical depicts two elections, George Washington and John Adams , leading up to the Election of 1800 when President Thomas Jefferson is elected after he is endorsed by Secretary Alexander Hamilton .

List of US Presidents

  2. John Adams (" I Know Him " to " The Election of 1800 ")
  3. Thomas Jefferson (" The Election of 1800 " to " Who Lives, Who Dies, Who Tells Your Story ")
  4. James Madison (" Who Lives, Who Dies, Who Tells Your Story ")
