Audio

"Your Obedient Servant" is the 20th song of Act Two of Hamilton , and the 43rd song of both acts.

Furious at Hamilton for endorsing Jefferson and thus losing the campaign for himself, Burr accuses Hamilton of purposely speaking badly of him to the public to interfere with his success through a series of letters . Hamilton, in turn, sneakily lays down his own accusations, saying Burr does not believe in anyone cause unless it is for personal gain, so it was only truthful of him to do so.

Enraged, Burr challenges Hamilton to a duel between their matters to settle.
