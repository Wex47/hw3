Thomas Kail is an American theater and film director, best known for his direction of productions of In The Heights and Hamilton (both written by Lin-Manuel Miranda ). He has won a Drama Desk Award and Tony Award for Best Director of a Musical for his direction regarding Hamilton.

Awards and Nominations

  • 2015 Lucille Lortel Award for Outstanding Director (WON)
  • 2015 Outer Critics Circle Award for Outstanding Director of a Musical
  • 2015 Drama Desk Award for Outstanding Director of a Musical (WON)
  • 2016 Tony Award for Best Direction of a Musical (WON)
