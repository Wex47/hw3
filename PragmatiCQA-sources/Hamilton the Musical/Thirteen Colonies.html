The Thirteen Colonies were a group of thirteen colonies that settled in America, originally from Great Britain . After the American Revolution , the colonies eventually gained independence from their homeland in 1776, and the colonies united and became the United States to signify their ability to stabilize as a nation without the help of Britain.

Thirteen Original Colonies

  • Delaware
  • Pennsylvania
  • Georgia
  • New Jersey
  • Connecticut
  • Massachusetts Bay
  • South Carolina
  • Maryland
  • New Hampshire
  • Virginia
  • New York
  • North Carolina
