Angelica Hamilton was the eldest daughter and second child of Alexander Hamilton and Elizabeth Schuyler Hamilton . She succumbed to a mental breakdown after her older sibling, Philip, died, and never recovered from her brother's death. Angelica had to be sent to an asylum. Alexander Hamilton bought her many birds because she liked them.
