Sasha Hutchings was an ensemble member of the Original Broadway Cast of Hamilton and had been with the cast since the Off-Broadway Production at The Public. While a cast member, she assumed the role as an understudy for Peggy Schuyler / Maria Reynolds . Sasha's last show was on August 27, 2016.
