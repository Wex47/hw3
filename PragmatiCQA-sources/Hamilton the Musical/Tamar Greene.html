Tamar Greene is an American actor, singer, and pianist. He is currently playing the role of George Washington in the Broadway Production of Hamilton . He assumed the roles from Bryan Terrell Clark on January 21, 2020.

Greene previously played the role of Washington in the Chicago Production of the show, having assumed the role from Jonathan Kirkland on September 25, 2018. He remained in the production until it closed on January 5, 2020.
