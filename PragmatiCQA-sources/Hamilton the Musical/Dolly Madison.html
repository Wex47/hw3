Dolly Madison is a minor character in Hamilton , seen onstage only once and never named by any character.

Musical

Dolly is seen in Blow Us All Away when Philip Hamilton asks for information as to where George Eacker might be. After Martha Jefferson tells him where he is, Dolly blurts out her attraction to Philip. When Philip reciprocates this and suggest they meet up after he is finished with Eacker, the ladies eagerly agree. Dolly is played by Sasha Hutchings who also plays the ensemble.

Trivia

  • Dolly Madison is named after James Madison's wife, Dolley Madison.
