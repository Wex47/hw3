Hamilton's Hamildrops is a project Lin-Manuel Miranda started in December 2017; one track was released during every month of 2018 (except July). It serves as the sequel to The Hamilton Mixtape ; and the songs in Hamildrops are inspired by the Hamilton the musical.

Similar to the Mixtape, each track features different collaborations by both Broadway actors/actresses and independent artists. Hamildrops also explores different genres of music as well as political issues.

Release

Lin-Manuel Miranda announced the release of Hamildrops in a Twitter thread on December 14, 2017, the day before the release of the first song " Ben Franklin's Song " by The Decemberists .
