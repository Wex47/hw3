" Congratulations (Off-Broadway) " is the 17th song in Act 2 and the 41st song overall in the Off-Broadway

Angelica angrily and tearfully confronts Hamilton about his actions after he had published The Reynolds Pamphlet and destroyed Eliza , who had cared so deeply about him. She vows she will never trust Hamilton the same way and makes him swear to realize what he had been given and what he had lost when he shattered his bond with Eliza.

Trivia

  • Part of "Congratulations" still exists in the musical in the song " The Reynolds Pamphlet ":
  • According to Lin-Manuel Miranda , the main reason that "Congratulations" was removed from the show was because the audience "desperately wanted to see Eliza's reaction" after "The Reynolds Pamphlet", so the song was cut and some lines from it were reused in "The Reynolds Pamphlet".
  • This song was sung in the last Ham4Ham show.
