Mandy Gonzalez is an American actress and singer. She is currently playing Angelica Schuyler in the Broadway Production of Hamilton , having assumed the role from Renée Elise Goldsberry on September 6, 2016.

Biography

Mandy Gonzalez starred in Lin-Manuel Miranda 's first play, In The Heights , as Nina Rosario.
