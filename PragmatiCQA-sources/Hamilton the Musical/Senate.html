The Senate (of the United States ) is legislative chamber bicameral legislature of the United States of America. The Senate, along with the House of Representatives make up the United States Congress ; however the Senate is considered the more prestigious part of the legislature, as it holds relatively more power in decisions and is more involved with the events happening in sessions. Unlike the House of Representatives, each state holds two seats totaling at 100 senators.
