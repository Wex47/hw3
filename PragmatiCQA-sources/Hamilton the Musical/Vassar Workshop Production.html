The Vassar Workshop Production , also known as The Hamilton Mixtape , is the first creation by Lin-Manuel Miranda which illustrates the life of Secretary Alexander Hamilton . The workshop was presented as a strictly two-performance reading as part of Vassar & New York Stage and Film's Powerhouse Theater Program, an annual theater development program. Conducted by Alex Lacamoire and directed by Tommy Kail , the workshop presented the entirety of Act 1 as it existed and four songs from Act 2.
