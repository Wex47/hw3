"Yorktown (The World Turned Upside Down)" is the 20th song of Act One of Hamilton .

In this song, The Battle of Yorktown (of 1781) occurs, as well as all of the events during this period. For the Tony Awards, Bayonets were removed from the dance for more family friendly theme and safety.
