Trivia

  • When Kelly Clarkson was asked to be part of the mixtape, she didn't agree with the idea, she said, "I hadn’t seen [Hamilton], hadn’t heard anyone talk about it because where I live [in Nashville], nobody had seen it. He [Atlantic Records Chairman & CEO Craig Kallman] sent the song to me at like 1 a.m., and I sent an email back to him with a lot of expletives! I was so angry, because I was pregnant with my son, and it’s all about their son dying. And I didn’t really know the situation, so I didn’t know if it was a baby, I didn’t know what it was." Yet, she agreed to do so.
