Martha Jefferson is a minor character in Hamilton , seen onstage only once and never named by any character.

Musical

Martha is seen in Blow Us All Away , when Philip Hamilton asks for information as to where George Eacker might be. She informs Philip that she saw him on Broadway, when he was going to see a play. Philip tells her and Dolly Madison that they "look pretty good in their frocks" and suggests that they meet up after he deals with Eacker, which they both eagerly agree to.

Trivia

  • Martha Jefferson is named after Thomas Jefferson's wife.
