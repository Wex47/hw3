The Hamilton Mixtape is a collaboration of various artists and bands recreating original Broadway songs of Hamilton , as well as cut songs from the original The Hamilton Mixtape (released 2013). The Hamilton Mixtape 's name comes from by the original name of Hamilton. (Hamilton was originally a concept album to be titled The Hamilton Mixtape . See Hamilton's origins .)

A production of the Mixtape was performed on December 1st 2016 for Ham4Ham .
