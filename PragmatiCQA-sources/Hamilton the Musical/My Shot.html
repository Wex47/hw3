"My Shot" is the third song of Act One of the Broadway musical Hamilton . It is the third song of the musical overall.

The main characters' ambitions are further established, particularly Hamilton's. Hamilton's verbal dexterity is also showcased here. This is the first time we learn of his way with words.

Trivia

  • "My Shot" was the longest song in the musical for Lin-Manuel Miranda to write, spanning a year until the song was at the lyrical state and level it is now. Presumably, this is because the song contains the most intricate language and internal rhyming, conveying as much meaning as possible through a single song.
  • The song is one of the songs that were not cut from the Off-Broadway and later productions from the original The Hamilton Mixtape production.
  • Hercules Mulligan 's lines from Yorktown (The World Turned Upside Down) were originally written for this song
