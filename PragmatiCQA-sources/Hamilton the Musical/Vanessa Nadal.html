Vanessa Adriana Nadal is an American lawyer and Lin-Manuel Miranda’s wife.

Biography

Vanessa Nadal was born in New York City, NY and attended Hunter College High School. Having a very educated background, she eventually studied law at Fordham School of Law and earned a bachelor's degree in chemical engineering at Massachusetts Institute of Technology.
