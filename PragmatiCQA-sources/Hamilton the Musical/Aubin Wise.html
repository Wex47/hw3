Aubin Wise is an American actress, singer, and dancer. She is currently playing the dual roles of Peggy Schuyler / Maria Reynolds in the Broadway Production of Hamilton . She assumed the roles from Elizabeth Judd on January 21, 2020. Wise also played the same roles in the Chicago Production , having assumed the roles from Samantha Marie Ware in 2017, and remained in the production until January 5, 2020, when it closed.

Trivia

  • Wise performed in Hamilton in Chicago until she was seven months pregnant.
