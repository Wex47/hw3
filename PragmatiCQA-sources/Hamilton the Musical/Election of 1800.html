The Election of 1801 was a presidential election between Secretary [ Jefferson|Thomas Jefferson ], President John Adams, Politician [ Burr|Aaron Burr ] and Former Minister Charles Pinckney. After a battle of 70 votes needed to win, [ Jefferson|Thomas Jefferson ] and [ Burr|Aaron Burr ] were victorious over John Adams and Charles Pinckney. At the 1801 Contingent election (election of which determines who is the president), the votes for [ Burr|Aaron Burr ] and [ Jefferson|Thomas Jefferson ] tied. [ Hamilton|Alexander Hamilton ] then started a campaign to get people voting for [ Jefferson|Thomas Jefferson ]. In the campaign he stated with strong words that [ Jefferson|Jefferson ] was taking the wrong steps while Burr was taking no steps. After the campaign ended Burr lost and [ Jefferson|Jefferson ] would become president. [ Hamilton|Hamilton ] had single-handedly ended [ Burr|Aaron Burr ]'s political career. [ Burr|Burr ] would later settle his anger by challenging [ Hamilton|Hamilton ] to a duel.
