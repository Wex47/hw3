Jonathan Groff is an American actor and singer and portrays King George III in Hamilton . Groff has also performed in other Broadway hit musicals such as Spring Awakening , Little Shop of Horrors , and Hair .

Biography

Jonathan Groff acted in Glee from 2010-2015 as Jesse St. James, though only appeared in 15 episodes. He later lent his voice to the Frozen franchise as Kristoff.
