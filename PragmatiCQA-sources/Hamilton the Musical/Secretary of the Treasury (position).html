Secretary of the Treasury is a position in the USA presidential cabinet . The secretary is the member responsible for financial matters. Here is a list of "Treasury Secretaries" during Hamilton 's life:

  • 1789 - 1795: Alexander Hamilton
  • 1795 - 1800: Oliver Wolcott, Jr.
  • 1801: Samuel Dexter
  • 1801 - 1814: Albert Gallatin
