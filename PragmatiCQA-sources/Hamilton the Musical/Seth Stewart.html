Seth Stewart is an American actor and currently portrays Thomas Jefferson and Marquis de Lafayette in Hamilton , having previously been a part of the ensemble and understudy.

Biography

Stewart debuted on Broadway as a part of the ensemble of Sweet Charity . He originated the role of "Graffiti Pete" in Lin-Manuel Miranda 's musical In the Heights . He later assumed the lead role of Usnavi.
