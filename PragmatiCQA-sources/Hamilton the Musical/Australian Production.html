The Australian production is a production of Hamilton that is taking place at the Sydney Lyric Theatre in Sydney, Australia and currently the only running performance of the show. The official cast list was announced on November 9, which was ritically acclaimed for its diverse cast, ranging from Indigenous Australians to Maori and South African performers. Previews began on March 17, with the run officially kicking off on March 27.
