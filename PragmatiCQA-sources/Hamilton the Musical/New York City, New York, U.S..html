New York City is a city located in New York, United States, and is one of the oldest and most populated cities in the U.S. New York City served as the headquarters of many famous events in the American Revolution . Alexander Hamilton is buried at Trinity Church , located in New York City.

Events

One of the most well-known events to happen in New York City is the Compromise in 1790, in which Secretary Alexander Hamilton , James Madison , and Secretary Thomas Jefferson come to an agreement over a meeting so that Hamilton could activate his financial plan regarding Congress .
