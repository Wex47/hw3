The Founding Fathers , more fully Founding Fathers of the United States , were a group of male philosophers, politicians and writers who shaped what the USA is today. They include all Signers of the Declaration of Independence . Historian Richard B. Morris in his 1973 book Seven Who Shaped Our Destiny: The Founding Fathers as Revolutionaries outlines the following people as the key Founding Fathers:

  • John Adams
  • Benjamin Franklin
  • Alexander Hamilton
  • John Jay
  • Thomas Jefferson
  • James Madison
  • George Washington
