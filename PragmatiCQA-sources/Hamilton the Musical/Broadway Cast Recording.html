The Broadway Cast Recording of Hamilton is the official audio album of Hamilton and its musical numbers/songs. The Broadway cast recording is out now; most

notably it reached #1 at the top of album charts, breaking records for one of the highest album rankings of its kind.

Trivia

  • Hamilton is featured in an of the podcast "Musicals with Cheese" on July 29, 2020.
  • Many of the song titles or the words used in them have not been written by Lin-Manuel Miranda. They are historic writings, found in documents, newspapers, letters or any other written accounts of extreme historical importance.
  • The song "One Last Time" contains parts of George Washington's farewell speech.
  • The song "Best of Wives, Best of Women" is actually a sign of line of Alexander Hamilton's last letter to his beloved wife Eliza. The original going, " Adieu best of wives and best of Women. Embrace all my darling Children for me. Ever yours A H"
