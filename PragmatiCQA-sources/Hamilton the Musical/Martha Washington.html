Martha Washington was the wife of George Washington , the first President of the United States. Although the title was not coined until after her death, Martha Washington served as the inaugural First Lady of the United States. During her lifetime she was often referred to as "Lady Washington".

Musical

Martha is an unseen character in Hamilton , but is an influential figure in multiple of the characters' lives. As the wife of George Washington, she had an important role in the beginnings of the United States of America.

She is only referenced by name once, in A Winter's Ball , when Aaron Burr tells the audience that she "named her feral tomcat after" Alexander Hamilton .
