Karen Olivo is an American stage and screen actress and singer. They portray Angelica Schuyler in the Chicago production of Hamilton . They also portrayed Vanessa in Lin-Manuel Miranda 's first musical, In The Heights .

Trivia

  • The song " Satisfied " uses music that Lin-Manuel Miranda originally wrote for Olivo using her own lyrics. When Miranda was writing "Satisfied", he realized the music he wrote two years earlier would be perfect for the song. Coincidentally, Olivo ended up playing Angelica Schuyler, the primary character in "Satisfied".
  • Olivo uses they/them pronouns.
