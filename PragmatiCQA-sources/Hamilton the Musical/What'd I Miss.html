Audio

"What'd I Miss?" is the first song of Act Two of the musical Hamilton .

Thomas Jefferson returns from his somewhat lengthy trip to France and trying to make himself helpful there. A friend of his, Madison , rants to him about Hamilton's financial plan , which he claims is "nothing less than government control"; the men will later find a way, along with Burr , to completely trash Hamilton 's reputation.
