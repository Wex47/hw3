" It's Quiet Uptown " is the 18th song of Act Two of Hamilton . It is the 41st song overall.

Heartbroken after Philip's death, the Hamilton family moves uptown and copes with their loss. Alexander begs for Eliza's forgiveness for all the pain that he has caused her, which she eventually accepts, and they mend their relationship, taking on their loss together.
