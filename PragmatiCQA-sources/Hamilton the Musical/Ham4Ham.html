Ham4Ham , stylized with social media tag #Ham4Ham Lottery, was originally a daily lottery in which entrants could win front-row seats to the Broadway production of Hamilton . To start off and to promote the lottery, various members of Hamilton would put on performance outside The Richard Rodgers Theater . Some of the more popular performances include:

When the lottery performances ended, the Ham4Hams continued in London, Chicago, Puerto Rico and most frequently, on YouTube.

Lottery Performances

July 2015

07/13/15

The first Ham4Ham can be seen , in which participants are interviewed, Lin-Manuel Miranda thanks the crowd for coming and the winners are drawn

07/16/15

The first Ham4Ham performance can be seen . In this Alex Lacamoire plays his melodica while Lin-Manuel Miranda sings along at Miranda's and then the audience's request. Among the melodies he plays are the Super Mario effects, Bohemian Rhapsody and 96,000

07/17/15

The first Hamilton performance can be seen . In this, Lin-Manuel Miranda and Daveed Diggs swap their roles from Cabinet Battle 1 . Chris Jackson plays himself.

07/18/15

  • The first Ham4Ham Q&A can be seen . In this, Lin-Miranda question takes questions and responds in Les Misrables quotes
  • Anthony Ramos ' first performance was also on this day and can be seen . He sings his audition song, Grenade by Bruno Mars.

07/20/15

The first Hamilton actor-swap performance can be seen . Rene Elise Goldsberry plays George Washington in Right Hand Man , Phillipa Soo beatboxes and Lin-Manuel Miranda provides backing vocals.

07/21/15

  • The first non- Hamilton vocal performance can be seen . Jonathan Groff and Lin-Manuel Miranda perform an original Miranda rap which was initially produced as a promotion for In The Heights .
  • The first female solo performance can be seen . Jasmine Cephas Jones sings Living For The City by Stevie Wonder.

07/22/15

The first non- Hamilton musical performance can be seen . Lin-Manuel Miranda asks the crowd to sing Happy Birthday to Alan Menken who wrote the music for The Little Mermaid and more relevantly, Newsies a 1992 Americanmusicalcomedy-dramafilm. As a couple of actors from the movie were in The Original Broadway Cast of Hamilton , they performed part of the song The World Will Know from said musical. Miranda plays Jack Kelly and the crowd play the Ensemble

07/23/15

The first cover of pop artists song can be seen . In it, Phillipa Soo sings Halo by Beyonc as she plays the ukulele. She is intoduced by Christopher Jackson .

07/24/15

The first Freestyle Friday can be seen . In this, company members perform Freestyle dancing outside the theater to H-A-M (Album Version Edited) by Jay-Z and Kanye West from Lin-Manuel Miranda's phone.

07/25/15

  • The first impersonation song can be seen . In this, Okieriete Onaodowan sings Defying Gravity from Wicked as Mickey Mouse. Lin-Manuel Miranda introduces Okieriete as "The Mighty Oak". He is rather loose with the lyrics and is to be seen as a comedy bit rather than a song.
  • This is also the date of the second Q&A, seen . In this, Lin-Manuel Miranda answers questions with only quotes from A Chorus Line to celebrate the 40 year anniversary of its Broadway opening.

07/27/15

The second actor-swap of actors in the same song can be found . To celebrate Thayne Jasperson's (who played Samuel Seabury ) birthday, Lin-Manuel Miranda asks the audience to sing Happy Birthday to Thayne and then the pair swap roles in Farmer Refuted .

07/28/15

The first understudy/actor-swap can be seen . In this Betsy Struxness, the understudy for Angelica Schuyler , sings Hercules Mulligan's segment of Yorktown (The World Turned Upside Down) . Okieriete Onaodowan's who usually plays Mulligan introduced her for this.

07/29/15

  • The first Freestyle Rap can be seen . Lin-Manuel Miranda offers the microphone to "one of the best rappers alive", Daveed Diggs. Daveed proceeds to rap for about one minute.
  • The first Bring It On Wednesday performance can be seen . In this, Lin-Manuel Miranda, Jon Rua & Ariana DeBose sing a selection of songs from the Bring It On musical

07/30/15

The first one-man multi-part song can be seen . In this, Andrew Chappelle a swing for Hamilton , plays all the parts in a partial version of Washington On Your Side .

07/31/15

The first all-female Freestyle Friday can be seen . The ladies can be seen dancing to 1 Thing by Amerie.
