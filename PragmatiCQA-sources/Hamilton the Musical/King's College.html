King's College , now called Columbia University , is one of the oldest university colleges of the United States. Founded in 1754, it educated some of the most influential or famous people in American history, such as Alexander Hamilton and his son, Philip Hamilton . Columbia University now is considered one of the most prestigious colleges of the United States, and has extremely high qualification standards and low acceptance rates.
