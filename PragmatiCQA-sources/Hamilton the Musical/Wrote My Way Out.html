"Wrote My Way Out" is the 3rd song of The Hamilton Mixtape , performed by Nas and featuring Aloe Blacc and Dave East. It is inspired from the original song " Hurricane ".

Trivia

  • "Wrote My Way Out" is meant to incorporate the lives of the artists in relation to that of Alexander Hamilton.
  • A remix of this song, " Wrote My Way Out (Remix) " was released in January 2018 as part of the Hamildrops .
