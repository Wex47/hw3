James Alexander Hamilton was the third son and fourth child of Alexander Hamilton and Elizabeth Schuyler Hamilton .

  • Hamilton was born on April 14, 1788
  • When James was sixteen, his father was killed in a duel with Vice President Aaron Burr . Along with his mother and siblings , James was present in the room, sitting at his father's bedside, when he died a few hours after the duel .
  • Hamilton served as acting Secretary of State to President Andrew Jackson and sided with Jackson in opposing the Second Bank of the United States, the successor to the bank which had originally been invented by his father
  • His father's life and career, friends and rivals, are discussed at length in Hamilton's memoirs, Reminiscences of James A. Hamilton: or, Men and events, at home and abroad, during three quarters of a century
  • He had 5 children, 3 of whom were named Elizabeth "Eliza" Hamilton, Alexander Hamilton Jr. and Angelica Hamilton
