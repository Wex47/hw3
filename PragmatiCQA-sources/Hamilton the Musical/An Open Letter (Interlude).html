"An Open Letter (Interlude)" is the 5th song of the 2016 album The Hamilton Mixtape , featuring Watsky and ShockWave.

The lyrics themselves come from Hamilton's cut rap in the Off-Broadway version of " The Adams Administration ". The lyrics had to be cut in order to keep the musical within the time limits and also because the little jibe at John Adam's wife, Abigail Adams was disliked by the people during the Workshop recitals.
