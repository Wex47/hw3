Lana Zoe Jensen , known professionally as Zoe Jensen , is an American actress and singer. She is currently playing the role of Elizabeth Schuyler Hamilton in the Angelica Tour of Hamilton . She assumed the role from Hannah Cruz on September 17, 2019.
