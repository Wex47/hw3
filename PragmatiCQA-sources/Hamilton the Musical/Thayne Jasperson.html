Thayne Riley Jasperson is an American actor and singer who portrayed the character Samuel Seabury in Hamilton . Outside of theater he is known for being a finalist of Season 4 of So You Think You Can Dance . He has also been in High School Musical , Newsies , Matilda and Finding Neverland . His Instagram (thaynejasperson) has 88.7k followers.

He portrays different characters each show. He has had appearances as Seabury, King George III and John Laurens/Phillip Hamilton.
