Miguel Cervantes is an American actor and singer who is known for many TV and Broadway appearances, as well as in 2010's Broadway Idiot . Cervantes currently plays Alexander Hamilton in the Chicago Production , previously serving as the character's alternate on Broadway.

Biography

Cervantes appeared as part of the ensemble in the original Broadway productions of If/Then and American Idiot , and was the understudy for David in the former. He also portrayed Angel in the Off-Broadway production of Giant , Chip Tolentino in the first national US tour of The 25th Annual Putnam County Spelling Bee , Eric in the Off-Broadway production of Cam Jansen , Eli in the Off-Broadway production of Wish You Were Here , and an ensemble member in the Off-Broadway production of Fanny .
