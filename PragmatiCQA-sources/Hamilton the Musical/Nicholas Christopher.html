Nicholas Christopher is an American actor and singer. He assumed the role of George Washington from Christopher Jackson in the Broadway Production of Hamilton on November 15, 2016. He remained in the role until January 8, 2017. He later played the role of Aaron Burr in the National Tour from January 2018 to September 2018.
