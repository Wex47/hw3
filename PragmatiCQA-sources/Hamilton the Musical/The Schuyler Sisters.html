"The Schuyler Sisters" is the fifth song of Act One and the fifth song of Hamilton overall. The Schuyler sisters, Angelica, Margarita (Peggy), and Elizabeth (Eliza) Schuyler, are introduced as they reveal their background and ambitions.

Trivia

  • The Schuyler Sisters was one of the songs which were not cut from the musical from the original songs of The Hamilton Mixtape , and made it into the Off-Broadway and later productions of Hamilton .
  • The way the sisters sing their names is echoed in following songs, such as " Satisfied ", is a cross-reference to "The Schuyler Sisters".
