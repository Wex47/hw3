Albany, New York, U.S. is a city located in New York , U.S. and was founded in 1686 by Governor Thomas Dongan.

Events

The marriage of Secretary of State Alexander Hamilton and daughter of Philip Schuyler , American politician and as well as war hero, Elizabeth Schuyler took place in the Schuyler mansion in Albany. Following this, the wedding between Vice President Aaron Burr and Theodosia Prevost was held in Albany, New York.

Locations

The perhaps most well-known location regarding the American Revolution in Albany is the Schuyler Mansion , the house of members of the prominent military and political Schuyler family .
