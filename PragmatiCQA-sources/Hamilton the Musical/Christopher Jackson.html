Christopher Jackson is an American singer and actor. He has taken part in many Broadway hits such as In The Heights and The Lion King , as well as Hamilton , where he played as George Washington in the original Broadway Production Cast .

Biography

Jackson is also a member of Freestyle Love Supreme, the hip hop comedy troupe which includes fellow Hamilton artists Lin-Manuel Miranda and James Monroe Iglehart.
