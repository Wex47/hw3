Sydney Harcourt is an American actor who played James Reynolds , Philip Schuyler , and the doctor in Hamilton . He also acted as a replacement for Aaron Burr and understudied the latter and the roles of Thomas Jefferson / Marquis de Lafayette and George Washington .
