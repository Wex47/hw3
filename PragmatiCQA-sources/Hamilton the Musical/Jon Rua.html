Jon Rua is an American actor and singer. He portrayed Charles Lee in the Broadway Production of Hamilton until June 29, 2016. During this time, he was also an understudy for Lin-Manuel Miranda in the role of Alexander Hamilton . He briefly returned in February of 2017 as an alternate for Jevon McFerrin in the role of Hamilton, since Javier Muñoz was on leave due to injury. Previously, he was an understudy in 2008 for Miranda's musical In The Heights , later becoming the replacement for Sonny and Graffiti Pete.
