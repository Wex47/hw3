The United States (or in the time of the American Revolution and its subjective provinces, called British America ) is the territory British and other European immigrants settled in for various reasons, from job opportunities and religious freedom to beginning a new life altogether. It is the setting in which the American Revolution is to take place. United States before the American Revolution was known as the Thirteen Colonies .
