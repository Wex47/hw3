Andrew Scott Rannells is an American actor, singer, and voice actor. He is best known for roles in shows such as The Book of Mormon , the revival of Falsettos (2016) and Hairspray (2002). Rannells temporarily replaced Jonathan Groff as King George III for about a month in 2015.

Accolades

Rannells was nominated for the Tony Award for Best Performance by a Leading Actor in a Musical for his performance as Elder Price in the 2011 Broadway musical The Book of Mormon .

He won the 2012 Grammy Award for Best Musical Theater Album as a featured soloist on the musical's original cast recording.

In 2017 he received his second Tony nomination for his performance as Whizzer in the 2016 Broadway revival of Falsettos .
