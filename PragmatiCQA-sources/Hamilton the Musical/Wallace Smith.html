Wallace Smith is an American actor who has made many onstage appearances, including Godspell . He played the role of Hercules Mulligan and James Madison in the Chicago production of Hamilton .

Biography

Smith was Simba on The Lion King's national tour and later a replacement on Broadway. In 2009, Smith appeared on Broadway in Ragtime , followed by a stint as the replacement for Hud in Hair . Smith has also portrayed Favorite Son in American Idiot and Judas in the 2011 Broadway revival of Godspell .
