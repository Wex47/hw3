Andrew Chappelle is a cast member of the Original Broadway cast. He was originally part of the ensemble but recently replaced Daveed Diggs as Marquis de Lafayette / Thomas Jefferson and Leslie Odom, Jr. as Aaron Burr , making occasional appearances as these characters in the show.
