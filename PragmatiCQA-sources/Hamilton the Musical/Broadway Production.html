The Broadway Production is where Hamilton made its Broadway premiere and debut, gaining attraction and visibility as becoming one of the most sought shows on theater.

The show remained the same in transition from the Off-Broadway show in The Public Theater , but two songs were cut from the original Vassar Workshop production The Hamilton Mixtape , as well as the opening song, Alexander Hamilton , undergoing minor changes. Other lyrical and musical changes were also present throughout the show. The Original Broadway cast remained the same from Off-Broadway, minus the portrayal of King George III changing twice.
