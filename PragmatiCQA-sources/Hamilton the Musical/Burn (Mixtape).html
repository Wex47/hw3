"Burn (Mixtape)" is the 17th song in the album The Hamilton Mixtape , sung by Andra Day .

The music video was released in July 2018 as the eighth of the Hamildrops , a series of songs Lin-Manuel Miranda produced to offer a new interpretation or tell a new story off the back of Hamilton .
