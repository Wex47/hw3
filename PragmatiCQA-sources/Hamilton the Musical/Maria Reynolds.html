Biography

In " Say No To This ", she is portrayed as helpless, apologetic, and confused. She begs Hamilton to stay with her because Reynolds would abuse her and her daughter Susan. After he walks her home, she seduces him and convinces him to sleep with her, starting a secret affair that lasted for a month before her husband, James Reynolds , found out. When he found out; however, he blackmailed Hamilton to pay him so that the scandal would be kept a secret. This started their affair, which was from 1791 to 1792.

She is portrayed in " Say No To This ", participating in the affair above.

She stands in the background of " We Know " and " Hurricane ", where she then hands Hamilton the pen he uses to write the document detailing their affair (and the subject of the next song) " The Reynolds Pamphlet ".

The reason why Maria did not divorce Reynolds earlier is because he threatened to take away and put up their daughter, Susan.
