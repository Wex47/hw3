"The Reynolds Pamphlet" is the 14th song of Act Two of Hamilton . It is the 37th song overall.

Making his decision, Hamilton decides to publicize his scandal to the public in his The Reynolds Pamphlet , thoroughly explaining his account of the incident and preventing Jefferson from doing so. Through the publication, Elizabeth Schuyler finds out about the affair, along with Angelica Schuyler , who sails from her home in England for her sister and to oppose Hamilton.
