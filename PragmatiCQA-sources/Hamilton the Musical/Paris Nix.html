Paris Nix is an American stage and show actor, known for playing the role of Marquis de Lafayette and Thomas Jefferson in Chicago's production of Hamilton from January 29, 2019 to its closing in January 5, 2020. After the production's closing, he was cast to play the role again in San Francisco's production of Hamilton.

Biography

While being in Chicago, he worked with Miguel Cervantes , the second man to take the role of Alexander Hamilton in Hamilton, and gained popularity in the Chicago area after taking the role of Alexander Hamilton for 3 years, only taking a break when his 3 year old daughter, Adelaide, passed away in October of 2019 due to infantile spasm, and is now taking the role on Broadway.

Now, Paris Nix is not in Hamilton due to the COVID-19 outbreak, but will most likely be back to reprise the role in a production of Hamilton.
