"First Burn" is the fifth song in Hamildrops and was released in April, 2018. This was the first draft of the song "Burn" sung by Eliza in Act 2. It was written by Lin-Manuel Miranda , produced by Alex Lacamoire and Mike Elizondo and performed by Lexi Lawson , Arianna Afsar , Shoba Narayan , Rachelle Ann Go , and Julia Harriman , who have each played Eliza in the show in various Hamilton companies.
