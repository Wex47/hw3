Hamilton: The Revolution is the book written by Lin-Manuel Miranda and Jeremy McCarter.

Content

The book features 32 chapters, each focusing on 1-4 songs of the musical. It is split into 2 sections for Act I and Act II, each with 16 chapters. The book, being written by 2 people, switches off between Miranda and McCarter and narrates details from each of their points of views. Often, McCarter explains Lin-Manuel Miranda's process of writing Hamilton from McCarter's own point of view.

The book contains an introduction that explains Lin-Manuel Miranda 's process throughout reading Ron Chernow's Alexander Hamilton , and, with gained inspiration, going on to write the entirety of the musical.

Each chapter also includes entries written by Lin-Manuel Miranda or Jeremy McCarter explaining an aspect of the musical.
