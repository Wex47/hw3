Audio

" Cabinet Battle #1 " is the second song of Act Two of Hamilton and the 25th song in the musical overall.

Secretary of the Treasury Hamilton and Secretary of the State Jefferson engage in a cabinet meeting designated to a discussion of Hamilton's financial plan. Madison and Jefferson get sassy and taunt him, reminding him that for congressional approval he would need the majority of the votes, which he didn't have. Washington pulls him aside during a recess and tells him to cool it, and that though he was worked up he did indeed have to find his votes to avoid removal.
