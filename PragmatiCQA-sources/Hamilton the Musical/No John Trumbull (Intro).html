"No John Trumbull (Intro)" is an intro song based off of an original cut song of Hamilton . It is the first song of the collaboration The Hamilton Mixtape .

Trivia

  • Part of the first lyrics spoken by Washington in the song is still used in the Broadway Production of Hamilton , in the song Cabinet Battle #1 .
  • The original version of the song was used in its entirety in the Off-Broadway Production of Hamilton but was cut for the Broadway Production .
  • The background music is similar to that of The Reynolds Pamphlet .
