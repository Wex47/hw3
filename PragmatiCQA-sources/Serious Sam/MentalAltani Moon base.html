The Mental-Altani Moon base was a large military base and research facility constructed by the Mental-Altani corporation on the Moon . It served as a midpoint for transporting Mental and Mental-Altani troops onto Earth by utilizing portal technology.

Overview

Structure

The Moon base itself was a large, complex metallic structure. Outside the base, there were long pipelines connected to the base underground, which provided power to either the portals or the base itself. If these pipelines or the power core were destroyed, this would prevent Mental-Altani units from being sent to Earth.

The base was made up of two floors. On the first floor, there were large bio-labs where Mental-Altani scientists conducted their research on the ooze project. Here, they performed bio-experimentation and various tests on Mental's units in attempts to enhance their abilities and make them tougher opponents. So far, the only test subjects that had been used in these experiments were Gnaars . These mutated versions were kept preserved in stasis tanks. One scientist, Dr. Daniel Platt , had his own laboratory where he conducted field tests on enhanced subjects against Orc Cadets .

Station command

On the second floor was the station command where Mental-Altani housed multiple missiles, known as Interplanetary Renewal Devices . Each warhead was loaded with Exolyse bacteria, which were kept suspended under intense pressure. These organisms consume everything in their path, which they convert into nitrogen. As these pathogens have such an aggressive nature, the corporation planned to use them as a biological weapon, and to wipe out the entire human civilization with these bacteria so Mental could finish off humanity in the past.

Appearances

Serious Sam's Bogus Detour

Sam learns from NETRICSA that there is a portal on top of Mount Olympus, which is where Mental's troops are being transported to Earth. He heads there and discovers it is guarded by one of Mental's commanders, Tetrachoke the Wizard . He kills him and obtains a green keycard , which he uses to access the portal.

After going through the portal, Sam realizes he has ended up on the Moon. He infiltrates the base and has to find four keycards to expose the power core's outer shielding. He hacks the shield with the keycards and Mental's reinforcements arrive to try and stop him. He eliminates the resistance and is able to destroy the power core and prevent any more troops from being teleported to Earth.

Having completed his first objective, Sam takes the elevator down to station command, where he has to hack and recalibrate four sets of missiles in succession so that, instead of being programmed to destroy the Earth, they will blow up the base, sparing humanity from what would have resulted in their extinction. Once Sam has re-calibrated the missiles, he then confronts The Director , the head of the Risk Assessment Body of Mental-Altani. He engages Sam in a fight, but ends up teleporting to the launch command room before Sam can finish him off. Sam confronts him again in the command room. This time, the Director threatens Sam, telling him that he intends to finish off humanity for good, though he is not aware that Sam has already hacked the missiles.

Before the Director launches the missiles and fights Sam again, he sends in two war-fitted industrial mechs, PRIME-209 and Tech-X , from the Mech Replacement Program to battle Sam. He manages to destroy both of them, but not long after the Director launches the missiles, which starts a countdown. This time, Sam is close to defeating him, but the Director teleports in the middle of the fight to an unknown location. As the missile launch is still in the countdown phase, Sam runs to the nearest hangar and manages to escape from the base in a spaceship seconds before the missiles destroy it.
