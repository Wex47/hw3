The Saggitarian Sidewinder, commonly referred to as the Metal Snake , is an enemy that appears in Serious Sam VR: The Last Hope and Serious Sam 4 , appearing as a miniboss in both games.

Overview

The Saggitarian Sidewinder is a large mechanical creature with unknown origins. It was used as a machine of war during one of Mental 's previous attempts to wipe out all civilized life in the galaxy. The last remnants of the Sidewinder was thought to have been destroyed in the Arcadia Minor campaign, but they survived, and were deployed to Earth along with many other aliens in Mental's army.

Appearance-wise, the Sidewinder has a body that resembles a snake that is covered with rows of multiple flapping metal spines, which act as wings and help it remain in the air as it flies around. It also has three small red mechanical eyes located in the center of its head.

In Serious Sam VR: The Last Hope , the first Sidewinder appears as a miniboss in the first Arcadia Minor level, Sea Breeze , and has much higher health than its regular counterpart. A group of them appear in the third Arcadia Minor stage, prior to the battle with the Spaceship .

In Serious Sam 4 , the Sidewinder first appears in Death from Below where it bursts out of a building and floats around in the air. It does not engage with the player and flies away. Later, at the end of the chapter, the same Sidewinder is encountered again and begins attacking Father Mikhail , Jones , Kenny , Rodriguez and Sam . To help defeat the Sidewinder, Jones lends Sam Hellfire 's XM214-A Minigun to kill it. Much later on in the game, three more Sidewinders appear by the end of the canyon in From Earth With Love , before their arrival a loud siren can be heard.

Behavior and skills

The Sidewinder does not attack the player directly, instead it floats around in the sky and launches multiple projectiles at the player, depending on the game that is being played on. In The Last Hope , the Snake will launch a group of drones at the player and they will try to crash onto the player and explode upon impact. In Serious Sam 4 , it will fire a barrage of homing rockets that do a fair bit of damage.

Tactics

Serious Sam VR: The Last Hope

  • The miniboss variant has a large amount of health, as such, explosive arrows like the Bow and the XPML21 Rocket Launcher are recommended to use against it. Power-ups, such as Serious Damage , can help to defeat it quickly.
  • The drones can be avoided either by shooting at them or by using a shield.
  • Rapid-fire weapons, such as the M29 Infantry Assault Rifle and minigun, are effective against groups of Saggitarian Sidewinders, as their rapid-fire state and damage lets the player quickly kill each Saggitarian Sidewinder. The AS-24 Devastator is also effective against groups of them.

Serious Sam 4 / Serious Sam: Siberian Mayhem

Miniboss

  • The Sidewinder is a fairly easy boss, though the rockets fired from it can be dangerous and quickly drain the player's health or even kill them if they don't pay attention during the fight.
  • Continuously strafing and sidestepping during the fight makes it much easier to take down the Sidewinder while avoiding its rockets at the same time.

Regular enemy

  • Strafe sideways to avoid being hit by the Sidewinder's rockets.
  • The rockets fired by the Sidewinder have limited range. Moving backwards far enough will make them harmless and easy to take down.
  • On Serious difficulty, a few Sidewinders appear at the climax of the quarry fight during One for the Road . If available, the FRPCL is recommended to be used against them; if not, the player should use the A-24 Devastator , Assault Rifle, or RAPTOR Sniper Rifle .
  • In From Earth With Love , Sidewinders are a much greater threat as a large amount of enemies support them on the ground. Additionally, the FRPCL won't be available in this level as it is removed from the player's arsenal after Anathema Unto God .
  • In groups, Sidewinders can be easily killed with the SBC Cannon or the XL2 Lasergun if the player has the death ray upgrade for the latter.
  • In Siberian Mayhem , the TCC2 "Perun" Crossbow and XPMR "Burner" Raygun are highly effective against Sidewinders, especially when dual-wielded.

Trivia

  • Its name is derived from the sidewinder , a venomous rattlesnake native to North America.
