Overview

The circular saw, like the P-LAH Chainsaw from Serious Sam 1 , is a tool that has been modified for use in combat. It is circular in shape with a chrome blade and a yellow body, the latter of which having several attachments such as a small screen and exhaust pipes on the side. It is a starting weapon, which replaces the Military Knife .

Tactics

  • The saw is not a recommended weapon against Beheaded Kamikazes and Clowns or long-ranged firing enemies like T-Mechs , The General , Zombie Soldiers , and many others at close range.
  • The saw can be used to take down groups of Lizards , Spiky Bulldogs , or Kleer Skeletons without wasting any ammo.
  • The saw is additionally handy for breaking apart crates and other destructible props without wasting any ammo.

Behind the scenes

  • It's possible that at some point, the blade for the saw would have used the projectile that the Martial Arts Zombie throws at the player.
  • As seen in beta screenshots of SS2 , the chainsaw was originally going to be a weapon that launched circular saw blades. This was likely changed to function as a saw instead due to the P-LAH otherwise being absent.
    • The original concept of a saw blade launcher was revisited with the FRPCL in Serious Sam 4 .

Trivia

  • The saw's model shares similarities with the spiral of death from the Ratchet & Clank game, A Crack in Time .
  • Due to the way the chainsaw's attack is calculated in the game engine, the player can actually damage themselves if they crouch and attack the floor with the weapon.
  • The circular saw is mounted on the Beheaded Rocketeer 's neck in Serious Sam HD .
