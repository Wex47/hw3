Plot

Sam and the team are close to retrieving the Holy Grail , but General Brand appears and stops them from getting their hands on it. Brand reveals his true intentions and plans to use the Grail for himself and to present it to Mental as a gift. Sam and his friends try to stop him, but Brand turns himself into a monster after using the Grail on himself and captures Sam in the process. Sam awakens to find himself on Brand's transport plane. He manages to escape from Brand's clutches and parachutes down near an abandoned Russian oil rig in the Arctic Circle. Luckily for Sam, there is a boat nearby, so he needs to fight his way to get to the boat and get to Tunguska and stop Brand before it's too late.

Side objectives

Twisted Little Passages

After getting inside the oil rig's interior for the second time, the player will come across a locked storage room. To open the door, they have to complete a text-adventure game named "Sorcerouse Samuel" on a computer next to the door. The reward inside the storage room are some armor, ammo, and a H.E.A.R.T. gadget.
