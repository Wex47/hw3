Judy Mental is an unseen minor character who appears in Serious Sam 3: BFE and Serious Sam 3 VR: BFE .

Overview

Judy Mental is the daughter of Mental , though it is unknown if she is the same species as her father. She only makes an appearance in Serious Sam 3: BFE and the VR version, and is not seen in-game. She can only be heard when Sam is talking to her on his phone. He was going to call Mental, but instead she answers the phone, asking if she can take a message. Sam says he's coming over to "play", and by play, he means he's going to kill him. Judy asks if Sam is serious, to which he replies that he is. But, before he can kill Mental, Judy warned him that he had to make it quick, as her father was about to "moon" the Earth.
