Medium Armor ( Blue Vest in Serious Sam: Double D ) is a reoccuring pickup in the Serious Sam series.

Overview

Medium armor is an item that gives the player 25-50 armor points when picked up, depending on the game that is being played. Like other types of armor, it will not exceed beyond the 100 armor points cap.

Medium armor is most often found throughout levels as a common pickup item so that the player will have some armor. Some pieces of medium armor are booby trapped, so the player should be careful when picking up one that's far away.

A few medium armors can be found in secrets, but are always found among other items, like ammo. Medium armor gives away too little armor points to be in a secret by itself.

Behind the scenes

  • Medium armor had two different models before the final release of Serious Sam: The First Encounter . In the Alpha version, it was a red shield with white checkerboards that had a gold lion coat of arms in front of it. Back then, it was labelled as small armor. In Test 1 , medium armor was a harness with silver stripes on it.
