Overview

The Fleet Fighter is a black vehicle that has two lights on its sides.

Behavior and skills

It behaves very much like the Drone , as it moves around very fast and fires a projectile at the player. Like the drone, fleet fighters usually attack the player in large numbers.

Tactics

  • The XM214-A Minigun is effective against groups of fleet fighters, due to its rapid rate of fire and insta-hit nature, considering how the Fighters fly around very fast.
  • The Laser Sword is also effective against fleet fighters, as the shockwave it launches is very fast and can destroy multiple fighters at once.
  • Their projectiles can be destroyed by shooting them or they can be deflected using a shield.
  • The RAPTOR Sniper Rifle is useful for destroying fighters at long range.
