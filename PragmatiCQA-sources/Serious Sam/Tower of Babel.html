Tower of Babel is the ninth level of Serious Sam: The Second Encounter , Serious Sam HD: The Second Encounter and Serious Sam VR: The Second Encounter , the twenty-third level of Serious Sam Classics: Revolution , and the twenty-fourth level of Serious Sam: Xbox . It is the fourth and final level set in the Ancient Persia era.

Plot

Sam has finally made it to the Tower of Babel , but he must collect three tablets in order to open its main door. Once he does, he will have to face the monster guarding the time portal within.

Secrets

  1. After the starting room, head towards the exit of the ceremonial hall but don't enter it, then turn and find an opening in the wall with a few cannonballs.
  2. In the Chamber of Cosmos, between a pillar and the first staircase, there is floor that you can jump on. After doing so, stick to the right or left walls and move along to get the Extra Large Health . If you try to get it by moving through the middle, you will be teleported back to the staircase (use this to leave the secret area after collecting the health).
  3. After the narrow bridge, before the second staircase, head left of it to collect some secret sniper bullets.
  4. In the room accessed using the elevator, visit one of its corners, in the middle of them should be mouse-sized holes. Shoot them with any weapon to summon "deadly midgets" (extremely small Sirian Werebulls ).
  5. Before exiting the Chamber of Cosmos, turn left after sliding down the ramp and spot a bouncer pad behind a lamp. Use it to access a secret area with some ammo, but immediately use the teleport located there after picking up the ammo as a Major Bio-mechanoid will spawn.
  6. When you exit the palace and are outside, look to your left and you will see a humongous pillar with a warrior that has glowing eyes carved onto it. Destroy the pillar with a rocket to reveal a secret teleport. Inside the set, destroy all miniature enemies and trees, and an extra large health will spawn. Be careful, shooting at Sam or destroying the pedestal he stands on will kill you!
  7. In the courtyard with the third tablet, in the wall opposite of the entrance behind a statue is a darker section on the wall. Blast it to reveal an Invulnerability power-up.
  8. In the courtyard following from secret #7, there is a health pill in a corner before entering the building and exiting the yard. Pick it up to spawn a "Mighty Gizmo" (a giant Marsh Hopper that will spawn many extremely smaller ones upon death).
  9. After revisiting the first yard, go to the left of the entrance of the Tower of Babel to find a secret invulnerability power-up.
  10. After collecting all tablets, go to the door that leads to the yard with the first tablet again for a secret "round 2".
  11. ( Serious Sam HD only) After entering the Tower of Babel, go behind the ramp-slide, behind the last pillar and look up. Shoot a rocket at the platform with the cannonball ammo to bring it down.
  12. ( Serious Sam HD only) From #11, shoot the wall behind the ammo with any weapon to make it push the cannonballs to you, or else you will be teleported and unable to pick it up.

Trivia

  • The first half of the level, from the start of the level all the way to the checkpoint just before the player sees the Tower of Babel, is known as the Ceremonial Halls in Serious Sam: Xbox . The second half of the level, from seeing the Tower of Babel to defeating the Exotech Larva, is known as Tower of Babel .
  • In the Egypt secret, the player can destroy all the miniature palm trees in the teleport to spawn an extra large health, and destroy the miniature version of Ugh-Zan III to spawn a Super Armor (only works in the classic and Fusion versions). Shooting the miniature version of Sam will instantly kill the player.
  • The music used in the Serious Sam: Xbox second half of the level is reused in the Serious Sam Classics: Revolution -exclusive Bright Island level Crystal Cove .
