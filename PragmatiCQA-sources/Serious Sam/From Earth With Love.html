Plot

After escaping the oil rig "Thunderbird", Sam manages to get to Tunguska, and, after getting help from Drmitry and making radio contact with Quinn , he gets to an Earth Defense Force outpost. His mission is to make it to the Tunguska portal and stop former EDF General Brand from giving the Holy Grail to Mental , while dealing with hordes and legions of enemies on the way. However, Sam is unaware that he will have to face an even bigger threat at the end.

Side objectives

Canyon Fodder

On the right of the starting area is a small opening in the cliffs with a side quest marker, leading to a forward operating base used by the EDF. After heading there, Quinn will call you to ask to investigate it, since she lost contact with it. Completing the mission will reward you with three Rage Serums .
