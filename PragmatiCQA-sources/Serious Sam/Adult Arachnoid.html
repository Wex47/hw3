The Adult Arachnoid is, as its name implies, a fully-grown Arachnoid. They are first introduced in Serious Sam 3: BFE and later appear in Serious Sam 3 VR: BFE , Serious Sam VR: The Last Hope , Serious Sam 4 , and Serious Sam: Siberian Mayhem . In Mental 's army, they often serve as heavy assault units and to make use of tactics like suppression fire on the enemy.

Overview

Adult Arachnoids are powerful holy warriors from a theocratic alien civilization that worships Mental as a Messiah. The Arachnoid religious leadership values brute strength and mindless devotion above all else, creating a violent society of relentless conflict and constant power struggles.

Appearances

In Serious Sam 3: BFE , Sam first encounters the Arachnoid at the end of Into the Spider's Nest , just as he is about to exit the Egyptian Museum . Later in the game, they are somewhat common enemies, though they usually appear in small groups and spawn far away from the player.

In Serious Sam 4 , the first Arachnoid that appears is encountered in Gates of Hell along with Hatchling Arachnoids . They soon become more common, replacing the latter in the later levels. An informant Arachnoid working alongside the French Resistance, called Charlie , also appears in the game, where she helps Sam and his team to get to the castle in Carcassonne.

Application

On their own, Adult Arachnoids are used to patrol the streets of cities such as Cairo and to provide support for lightweight troops such as Cloned Soldiers and Octanian Gamma Troopers . Depending on the situation, they can be seen one at a time or in large numbers. In large, open areas and in the countryside, Arachnoids are generally positioned on top of cliffs or rock formations in order to scout the area for enemies and to provide covering fire.

Behavior and skills

Being much tougher than the hatchlings, fully grown Arachnoids are able to withstand a substantial amount of small-arms fire and can survive a few direct hits from sniper rounds and explosives, unless they are shot in the head. Unlike the hatchlings, the adults possess a fully-developed scarlet exoskeleton which is further augmented by additional armor.

Like hatchling Arachnoids, the adults have the abiliy to dig underground. When they sense the target nearby, they will immediately come up from under the surface to engage them.

The Arachnoids have a different attack pattern compared to their Serious Sam 1 versions; while the ones in Serious Sam 1 shoot decently-damaging bullets at a rather average rate, the Adult Arachnoid shoots low-damaging bullets at a very fast rate. Additionally, they are faster, can often avoid slower projectiles, and are not as susceptible to flinching while damaged.

In Serious Sam VR: The Last Hope , Arachnoids have their arms replaced with rocket launchers instead of chainguns. As soon as they see the player, they will raise their arms up and fire a burst of rockets.

Weaponry

Adult Arachnoids carry advanced chainguns (or rocket launchers in The Last Hope ) that are equipped with techno-magical ammunition replenishers , which they use as their primary weapons in combat. When they spot the target, they will charge up their chainguns and open fire, releasing 20 bullets per hand (40 in total), then stop, and repeat that sequence after a short interval. Driven by their fanatical belief in their sacred task, an Arachnoid can occasionally enter a berserker-like rage, shooting indiscriminately regardless of who or what is in their line of fire.

Their second weapon is either their large pincers or using their powerful sting from their tail, which they will use at point-blank range if the target is right in front of them.

Tactics

Serious Sam 3: BFE

Boss

  • The Arachnoid boss has three times as much health than the normal Arachnoid.
  • This version of the Arachnoid is able to destroy cover. If cover has been too badly damaged, the player should sprint to another pillar or move to another side of the pillar, depending on their situation.
  • If using the 12 Gauge Pump-Action Shotgun , reload while behind cover or between Arachnoid bursts.
  • The hidden Double Barrel Coach Gun , and XL2 Lasergun found earlier in the campaign are very helpful against the Arachnoid boss. The coach gun does much more damage against the Arachnoid than the regular shotgun, while the lasergun can tear apart the Arachnoid within seconds.
    • In Serious Sam Fusion , if the player obtained the secret XPML21 Rocket Launcher from Summer in Cairo , they can use it against the Arachnoid and deal heavy damage to it.
  • Remember that there are supplies scattered throughout the arena, so try and grab some while the boss is between minigun blasts if they're needed.

Enemy

  • When facing off with an Arachnoid, the player's first priority should be to find cover. The Arachnoid can quickly slice up the player because of how many bullets it fires if the player is unable to find cover. It's recommended to shoot the Arachnoid after the machine gun volley is over. Once the Arachnoid has stopped firing, open fire on it until it has been killed.
  • One can tell the Arachnoid is about to open fire as soon as it opens its pincers and starts shooting. The player should keep note of this and use it to their advantage whenever they need to find cover.
  • The M29 Infantry Assault Rifle is somewhat effective against Arachnoids, but it takes more than an entire magazine to kill one. It is recommended to fire at the Arachnoid after it has fired its guns.
  • The AS-24 Devastator or XPML21 Rocket Launcher are very effective against the Arachnoid, but the player needs to be at medium range or closer in order to hit it with the rocket launcher. As most Arachnoids strafe frequently, one is able to dodge slow projectiles, such as rockets from the rocket launcher. The Devastator's projectiles are fast enough that the Arachnoid can't avoid them in time, so the player is free to engage it at nearly any range.
  • If the player found the secret RAPTOR Sniper Rifle in the early levels, it will be very effective against the Arachnoid. Rapid fire will make it flinch, but several rounds will be needed to take them down. Do note that the RAPTOR's ammo is quite rare, thus making it more useful as a last resort.
  • A C-4 Demolition Charge is also effective against Arachnoids. All it takes is one attached charge to kill an Arachnoid. Without being attached, it will take roughly two or three to kill one depending on how close the Arachnoid was to the detonation.
  • Multiple Arachnoids can be dealt with powerful rapid-fire weapons, such as the lasergun and the XM214-A Minigun . The lasergun and minigun can quickly kill Arachnoids because of their high rate of fire and damage per shot. A single charged SBC Cannon shot is enough to kill one, which makes it another effective weapon for groups.
  • The Arachnoid is a significant threat when paired with other enemies. Its miniguns can inflict a large amount of damage, even from far away. It should be one of the first enemies targeted so that it can't quickly chip away at the player's health and armor.

Serious Sam 4

  • The same tactics for the Serious Sam 3 Arachnoid work just as well on the Arachnoid in this installment.
  • Strong weapons such as the MK III Grenade Launcher , sniper rifle and rocket launcher are effective at eliminating Arachnoids.
  • If using the sniper rifle, fire at their head whenever possible, as they take extra damage this way and it can be a very useful weapon for picking them off if they are in groups.
  • As the lasergun appears as a regular weapon in this game, it works very well against Arachnoids due to the weapon's high damage output, especially if it is fired continously.
  • The FRPCL can be used to destroy groups of Arachnoids and is especially useful as a last resort.

Behind the scenes

  • Early concept sketches of the Serious Sam 4 Arachnoid show different designs of the creature, with parts of its body exposed and using different weapons aside from the chainguns it normally uses.

Trivia

  • Illegally downloaded or cracked versions of Serious Sam 3 have a DRM mechanic that features a miniature invincible Arachnoid that will chase the player. It attacks identically to a regular Arachnoid, but it travels at an extreme speed. Despite being invincible, it can be killed with the "kill all" console command, and attacking it enough can also still stun it like a regular enemy.
  • Major Slick Stinger , the first boss of Serious Sam's Bogus Detour , is a slightly larger version of the Adult Arachnoid, and is equipped with a chaingun and energy launcher.
  • In The Last Hope , Adult Arachnoids use the same sounds as the classic Arachnoids rather than their own sounds.
    • Additionally, they use the same rocket launchers as the Major Bio-mechanoid .
  • In Serious Sam 4 , Adult Arachnoids' death sounds are reused from the ScorpSoldier from Serious Sam 2 .
