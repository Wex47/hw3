The Machinegun is a submachine gun that appears in Serious Sam: Tormental .

Overview

The machinegun is an automatic weapon with a very high rate of fire. It has a higher ammo capacity than most other weapons, but has terrible accuracy due to its spread and rate of fire. It also consumes ammo quickly when used too often.

The machinegun has a random chance to appear from blue Mental chests in Mental Woods or it can be purchased from shops in the later levels.

Tactics

  • The machinegun is excellent for dealing with hordes of weak enemies in groups, like Gnaars , Gonks and Snorklers , but not so effective with tougher ones.
  • The player should use the machinegun sparingly, or else they will end up burning through ammo quickly.
