Sand Canyon is the second level of Serious Sam: The First Encounter , Serious Sam: Xbox , Serious Sam HD: The First Encounter , Serious Sam VR: The First Encounter , and Serious Sam Classics: Revolution .

Plot

Sam reaches the other side of the mountain, and he must enter the Temple of Thutmose to obtain the first element. Little does he know though, is that Mental has left a surprise for him inside the temple.

Secrets

  1. From the start, at the opposite side of the hill, on the very left corner of the other hill, the one that you cannot climb on. In the HD and VR versions of the game, just walk up to the very left corner, until you see a tree and the "secret passage" message is shown. In the classic versions though, you'll need to go to the ledge of the building you're supposed to be entering, and jump on the cliff from there. Once you're in, there's some secret ammo, such as rockets and bullets there. If you pick up the last pickups, this will spawn Beheaded Kamikazes , or Harpies on Serious difficulty .
  2. From the start, from the opposite side of the hill, go up to the ledge of the building you're supposed to enter, walk forward a bit, turn left and touch the wall. That will open a secret room containg ammo, armor and two Harpies.
  3. In the Marsh Hopper room, as you enter it, on the right wall, touch it. This will reveal an Thompson submachine gun and some bullets.
  4. Right after the Marsh Hopper room, turn left, and walk next to the column, you'll see the floor is differently colored. That's a bounce pad. Walk on it to bounce over the columns, and grab a secret Extra Large Armor , which is on the right column.
  5. In the smaller room after the Marsh Hopper room, there's a tiny ramp in it. Under this ramp is a secret hole with a Medium Health .
  6. Before entering the tomb in the end of the level, go to the left part, and jump rapidly on the cliff. If you do it correctly, you will be on top of the tomb, it may take a few tries though. There's an Extra Small Health there, picking it up will cause a bunch of male Gnaars to spawn. Like #1, you can pretty much walk straight up to it in the HD version of the game and get on top of the tomb.

Trivia

  • There are three giant boulders in this level: one inside the entrance to the first temple, and two outside towards the second temple. All three of them are booby-trapped.
  • Sam whistles the Indiana Jones theme after narrowly avoiding the first boulder.
