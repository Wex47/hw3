Land of the Damned is the eleventh level of Serious Sam: The Second Encounter , Serious Sam HD: The Second Encounter and Serious Sam VR: The Second Encounter , the twenty-fifth level of Serious Sam Classics: Revolution , and the twenty-sixth level of Serious Sam: Xbox .

Plot

With the Book of Wisdom in his hands, Sam must now acquire the Holy Grail , which lies within a legendary ice castle, to ensure Mental 's defeat.

Secrets

  1. In the first village, on the left side at the start there is a house with a squeaking, moving door. Blast it with a rocket to reveal a secret Serious Damage .
  2. At the windmill, there is a switch. Press it to summon a secret Santa , and shoot the Santa to get some bonus items (best done with either the single Schofield .45 , Tommygun , or XOP Flamethrower , to get as many items as possible).
  3. On the left side at the other half of the village, you may hear coughing noises. Keep moving to where you hear them louder, or simply check the chimneys of every house there is. You will find Santa's legs in one of these. Shoot them to discover a "jammed Santa" which will pop out an Extra Large Health .
  4. In the same area, blast the snowman with a rocket to reveal a secret armor.
  5. In the second village, at the start, pick up some armor to trigger a "snowman avenger". Quickly destroy it to avoid taking major damage or dying.
  6. The house on the right of the snowman avenger has a squeaking door. Blast it open to reveal an Invulnerability power-up.
  7. Opposite of #6, there is a another snowman with a switch on it. Press it to summon another secret Santa and repeat the procedure like secret #2. Note that if the snowman is destroyed in the classic versions of the game, the switch will disappear and the player will be unable to use it.
  8. Before entering the lava caves, continuously shoot rockets at the snowman to reveal an "incredible shrinking snowman" and an extra large health, which in turn will spawn two Major Bio-mechanoids .
  9. ( Serious Sam Classics: Revolution only) again near the entrance, there is a ledge near the top of the mountain. Shoot the ledge with a rocket to reveal a secret Minelayer .
  10. In the first lava cave, you'll notice a gap in the stone bridge. Jump on it to be teleported to a secret extra large health.
  11. As you enter the second lava cave, look for a glow in the distance up ahead. Zoom in with the sniper rifle to spot a platform with some cannonballs on it, then blast it with a rocket. Proceed and collect it afterwards to register the secret.
  12. Before exiting the second cave, go behind the rocks on the left of the exit to discover a secret SBC Cannon .
  13. In the third cave, when you are near the floating rock platforms, jump to the leftmost farthest one and wait until it transports you to a rock platform with a secret Extra Large Armor on it.
  14. Before leaving the cave, go around the base of the arch bridge you crossed to discover a secret extra large health.
  15. ( Serious Sam HD only) Before leaving to the ice castle, go to the right side of the cave, behind the second pillar is a teleport. Jump through it, and prepare to kill two Lava Golems as you do. After picking up the secret items there, jump in the middle of the four stone pillars to be teleported back.

Trivia

  • This level is named Winter Wonderland in Serious Sam: Xbox .
  • Additionally, the second half of the level, lasting from when the player enters the lava caves all the way to the end when they reach the ice castle, is named Lava Caves . It uses different music from the first half of the level.
  • The music for Lava Caves was later reused in the final level of the Bright Island campaign for Revolution , The Steps of Despair .
