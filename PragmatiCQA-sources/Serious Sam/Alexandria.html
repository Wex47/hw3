Alexandria is the second-largest city in Egypt , located in the northern part of the country which borders the Mediterranean Sea . The city itself plays a role in Serious Sam's Bogus Detour .

Overview

Founded by Alexander the Great, Alexandria is a large, ancient Egyptian city, but it is slightly smaller in terms of size compared to Memphis and Thebes . The city is known for its large lighthouse and the Great Library , both of which were destroyed or damaged by natural disasters and conflicts.

During the events of Serious Sam's Bogus Detour , the Mental-Altani corp have taken over the city as their base of operations in Egypt. There are several Orc bases scattered around, guarded by Towers , Orc soldiers and Mental's troops, which indicates that Mental's forces have already taken over the city before Sam gets there.

Appearances

Serious Sam's Bogus Detour

Alexandria is the last location in Egypt that Sam visits before he makes his way to Greece. Here, Sam learns from NETRICSA that Mental has stationed one of his commanders, Major Slick Stinger , in the Great Library and he has been leading Mental's forces down from Northern Egypt. Fighting his way through the alien-infested streets, he enters the Great Library, where a fight between him and Slick Stinger commences. Sam is able to kill him and obtain the keycard that he needs to escape. Once he makes it out of the library, Sam finishes off the rest of Mental's forces before boarding a ship to Greece.
