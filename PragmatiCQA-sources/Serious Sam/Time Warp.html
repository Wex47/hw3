The Time Warp , or Serious Time , is a gadget featured in Serious Sam 4 .

Overview

The time warp is a strange, ancient spherical device that was designed by the Sirians Its purpose is not fully understood, but Professor Gottlieb Kiesel believes it was created as part of the Sirians' research into the properties of time.

When activated, the time warp will generate a large chronon distortion field around the user. Within this "time bubble", all hostiles and their projectiles will be dramatically slowed down, while the player and any NPCs will continue to behave normally.

Appearances

The time warp is an uncommon gadget. The player first obtains them after completing the final side quest in Four Horsemen of the Apocalypse , when Dr. Kiesel informs the player about finding an alien artifact that Hermann Stein was researching on. In the later levels, it's often found in secrets.

Tactics

  • The time warp is most effective against a large horde of enemies right before they overwhelm the player. Once it's been activated, the player will be able to easily eliminate them.
  • It can also be useful in some levels when the player needs time to recover any lost health or armor while their companions can deal with any enemies.

Trivia

  • The achievement related to the gadget, "Max Pain", is a reference to the Remedy video game series Max Payne , which is famous for its use of bullet-time.
  • The other achievement, "That Belongs In A Museum", refers to a quote said by Indiana Jones in Indiana Jones and the Last Crusade .
  • The time warp has a similar shape to the plasma grenade in Counter-Strike Online .
