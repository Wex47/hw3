Large Health ( First-Aid Box in Serious Sam: Double D ) is a healing item that appears in the Serious Sam series .

Contents

  • 1 Overview
  • 2 Appearances
  • 4 List of appearances

Overview

Large health is the second highest healing item in the Serious Sam games. When picked up, it gives the player 50 health points. While very useful, it should be reserved for until the player has around 60 or less health, or else its large amount of health points will be wasted.

Appearances

This item usually appears before the player gets into a large battle or between waves of enemies in a large battle. It is also found in secrets, with some secrets containing just a large health.
