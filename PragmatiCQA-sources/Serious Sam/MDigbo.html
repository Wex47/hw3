Overview

M'Digbo is a tropical, humid planet that has many jungles. The natives of the planet are Simbas , bluish humanoid creatures that leave a peaceful life with primitive technology. They live in either small villages with houses made out of straw or hay, or in Ursul, a large city that was made by them. One of Mental's units, which are Primitives , are also native to the planet. Kwongo , a massive gorilla-like creature, may also be home to the planet.

Appearances

Serious Sam 2

M'Digbo is the first planet Sam visits in the game, where he is sent to the planet in order to get the Medallion piece from the Simbas.

Notable locations

M'keke village

The M'keke village is a small village located near a river. The Simba Chief lives in this village, and Sam visits the village in order to ask him about the location of the medallion piece.

Ursul

Ursul is a megacity, and is seperated into two parts, the suburbs and the gardens. The suburbs contain very small houses, and leads to the Kukulele prison.

The gardens contains large buildings and several large areas, and leads to the ceremonial area, where Sam goes to fight Kwongo.
