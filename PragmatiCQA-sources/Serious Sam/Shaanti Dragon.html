Overview

The Shaanti Dragon is a large, chinese dragon like creature with orange skin, glowing red eyes and a very long body. It is encountered in the level The Holy Serpent .

Appearances

The battle with the Dragon takes place on large floating islands in the level The Holy Serpent . The island the player is on moves around until it reaches the last part of the level. Enemies such as Adult Arachnoids , Lava Golems and Major Bio-mechanoids will appear in order to distract the player while they are fighting the Dragon.

Behavior and skills

Considering the size of the Dragon, it has a very high amount of health and requires a ton of heavy firepower to kill. Unlike many other bosses in the game, the Dragon only has one attack, which is to fire several projectiles at the player.

Tactics

  • Like other bosses, the player can avoid the Dragon's projectiles either by shooting at them or by using a Shield .
  • The Dragon is easy to hit with projectile weapons due to its large size. The XL2 Lasergun is useful as it can quickly deplete the Dragon's health. The XM214-A Minigun and XPML21 Rocket Launcher are also effective against it.
  • The Drone Squad power-up can be effective, as the summoned drones can attack the enemies if the player wants to focus on killing the Dragon quickly.
