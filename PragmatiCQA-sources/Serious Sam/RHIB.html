Overview

The RHIB is a small, black inflatable boat resembling a dinghy. The RHIB appears to carry up to 8 or 9 passengers. The player cannot drive in the RHIB, nor it is seen being drivien in any cutscenes.

Appearances

Serious Sam 3: BFE

When Sam is dropped of on the island of Philae by Wilson , the RHIB can be seen anchored to a pier next to the entrance of the island. After Sam gains access to what he thought was the Time-Lock control room, he asks Quinn about the possibility of being extracted. She regrets to tell Sam that there no are helicopters available to take him to Abu Simbel at the time, much to his annoyance, but she says there is a motorboat not far on the island. After taking her advice, he takes the RHIB to Abu Simbel.

Serious Sam 4

Having parachuted out of Howard Brand 's transport plane, Sam finds himself stuck at an abandoned oil rig somewhere in the Arctic Circle. He is in luck, though, after spotting an RHIB nearby. Mental 's forces are spawned in to stop him, but he kills them all and uses the boat to drive to the location of the massive portal and to put an end to Brand's schemes.
