The Octanian Harvester Leviathan , colloquially known as the Harvester Mothership or just Harvester , is a living spacecraft that was used by the Octanians during the Tunguska Offensive . It appears as an unkillable enemy in Serious Sam 4 .

Overview

The Octanian Harvester Leviathan is a massive alien creature that resembles a cuttlefish. In terms of size, it is approximately 268 meters tall and 865 meters long, making it one of the largest creatures in the Serious Sam series. It has small blue eyes, two long arms on either side and multiple tentacles hanging from its underside. Its body has been fitted with machinery and artificial parts to suit the Octanians' needs.

Appearances

Serious Sam 4

The first Mothership seen in the game is seen clearly visible in the sky on the first level of the game, Death from Above . Kenny comments on the Mothership, saying that it's much larger than he anticipated. A second one appears in Carcassonne and can be seen from a distance when Sam makes his way to the town through the French countryside. In the final level, From Earth With Love , several Motherships can be seen in the background providing support for the Octanian soldiers during the Battle of Tunguska .

Appliication

The Harvester Mothership is essentially a living, flying aicraft carrier that is used for many purposes, such as reconnaissance and storing Octanian Raiders . They were deployed in large numbers during the Mental War . Its highy likely that the Motherships seen on Earth arrived through the portal (considering how large it was) that appeared in Tunguksa. During the war, they were flown over cities with a large human population, where large squadrons of Octanian raiders were scrambled from the Leviathan to take control of the skies and ensure superiority over Earth's airspace. For control on the ground, the Leviathan deployed Harvester Drones to capture anyone hiding in the streets and take them back to the ship so they could be converted into Processed Anthropolyps . If the target got within close proximity to a Leviathan on the ground, it could release swarms of Combat Drones to eliminate them.

Behavior and skills

The Mothership can be seen slowly flying around in the sky whenever they appear. During the final level, it will fire many rockets at the player. Unlike other enemies in the game, the Mothership is immune to damage from the player's weapons. Even if it were killable, it would take a ton of firepower to take one down.
