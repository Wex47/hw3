The Great Sphinx of Giza , also known as the Sphinx of Giza or just Sphinx , was a statue of a reclining sphinx made out of limestone located in the Giza pyramid complex in Egypt .

Overview

Believed to have been built by the ancient Egyptians in the Old Kingdom era during the reign of the Pharaoh Khafre, the Great Sphinx was the oldest monumental sculpture in Egypt, as well as one of the world's largest and oldest statues.

Despite the infrastructural damage dealt to Earth 's cities and other settlements during the three-year long war, the Sphinx, the Great Pyramid and other ancient Egyptian monuments had remained relatively intact. This was most likely due to Mental's armies targeting large cities and other settlements, whilst ignoring sparsely populated areas.

Appearances

Serious Sam 3: BFE

Sam contacts Quinn after he manages to "secure the bird". She tells him that the data they extracted from Stein 's cell phone indicates that there is a secret chamber located inside the Great Pyramid , but there is no direct way in. She tells him that "the Sphinx is the key" but is unable to know what this means.

As Sam makes his way to the pyramid, Quinn asks him if he has reached the Sphinx yet. He says that is making his way there and he asks himself if the scientists have solved the riddle of the Sphinx. Quinn says they've translated one of the lines but they haven't yet reached a solution. She says that "the true riddle of the Sphinx has many questions but only one answer". Disinterested, Sam sarcastically remarks if that is supposed to help him on his mission, then cuts her off after she says its the best they have at the moment.

Getting closer to his objective, Sam grows impatient. Utilizing a crate of C-4 charges nearby, he decides its time for him to switch to "Plan B"; by strapping the Sphinx with dynamite and blowing it up. Quinn has a go at him for trying to blow up one of humanity's oldest artifacts, but he ignores her and triggers the detonator, blowing the timeless Sphinx to pieces. Despite how disastrous this may have seemed, it is revealed that there was a secret passage underneath the Sphinx all along. Sam then proceeds down into the abandoned passageway and heads to the secret chamber inside the pyramid.
