Tomb is the second level of Serious Sam: Tormental . It is the second part of the tutorial.

Plot

Sam is now inside the tomb that is rumored to hold the Mentaloptican, a cursed artifact. He fights his way through and finds the artifact he was looking for. He destroys it, but is killed from the resulting explosion, sending his soul into Mental 's mind.
