Plot

As Sam gets closer on the city of Troy , he dicovers a multi-mission aircraft located somewhere near the city's entrance, which he can use to get to Greece. However, as the aircraft is missing several parts, Sam has to find these parts which are scattered throughout the city.

Secrets

  1. Proceed through the level until you come across a sunflower field. Follow the path south-east of the field until you come to the top of the base you were in during the previous level. Shoot the breakable wall on the very left here and collect the star behind it to trigger the secret.
  2. When you first come across Kleers in the level, you will eventually see another sunflower field below you, and a star on the ledge above it. Shoot the breakable wall between the two houses above it and collect the star to trigger the secret.
  • There are two secret parchments that spawn in random locations throughout the city. Once these documents have been found, it will unlock the secret level, Serious Sabotage .
