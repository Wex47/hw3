Overview

Plasma generators are massive structures that are pyramid-shaped in design, much like the Great Pyramid . Being powered by plasma , they are very powerful, capable of transmitting energy to the Time-Lock on a massive scale. Only two of these generators are known to exist; the Anubis Generator and Horus Generator . The Anubis generator is colored orange, while its sister generator Horus is blue.

Activation

For someone to activate the generators, they would first need the Sirian Mutilator bracelet in their possession. Once they have acquired it, they then have to obtain seals located near the excavation site of the respective generator to open the door that leads to it. After entering the generator room inside the Sirian structure beneath the excavation site, all the user needs to do is flick their wrist above a hologram-type screen, which will then power up the generator.

Appearances

Serious Sam 3: BFE

After succesfully locating the hidden chamber within the Great Pyramid, Sam is told by Quinn that there are two generators, both of which are located in Karnak and Luxor, and also tells him that someone will take him by helicopter. Once he clears out the area for extraction, he is greeted by Hellfire , who takes him to Karnak, the location of the first generator. Sam fights his way through the temples and is able to activate the first one after acquiring the four seals needed to open the door.

Over in Luxor, Sam repeats the same process to unlocking the door to the previous generator, and activates the second one. With the Time-Lock now online, Sam is congraluted by Quinn, who tells him that Charlie Team are now ready for insertion.

History

These generators were designed by the Sirians in Ancient Egypt at least two thousand years prior to the events of Serious Sam 3: BFE . When they arrived on Earth , the Sirians began making contact with the Egyptian people. During this time, they were treated like gods. Using this to their advantage, the Sirians had the Egyptians begin massive building projects all over Egypt. Meanwhile, the Sirians themselves constructed their own devices underground, namely the plasma generators. Two of them were built; the Anubis generator and Horus generator, located underneath their respective shrines in Karnak and Luxor.

Trivia
