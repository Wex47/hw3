The Destruction of Earth was a brief but cataclysmic event that took place after the Battle for the Time-Lock , during the final day of the Mental War .

Prelude

With Ugh-Zan IV and Mental's alien hordes defeated, Sam gets out Stein 's cell phone to call Mental and threaten him one last time. However, Mental is not there to answer his call. Instead of him answering, his daughter Judy Mental receives the call. Sam asks if her father was home. She said that he is "out destroying some pissant planet in the middle of nowhere" and asks if she could leave a message. Sam tells Judy to tell her father that he was coming over to play, and by play, he meant that was going to kill him.

Judy said that he has to make it quick, as her father was going to "moon" the Earth at any moment. Sam didn't quite understand the concept, but after seeing the Moon about to collide with Earth, he realizes he has no choice. Destined to get to the Time-Lock fast, Sam says he is next going to see Mental in 3000 BC and manages to escape seconds before the Earth is destroyed.

Aftermath

Having destroyed both the Earth and its Moon, Mental succeeded in finishing off the human race in this timeline. However, not all is lost, as Sam made it through the Time-Lock before he could be killed, giving mankind another chance.

Trivia

  • The shattered remains of Earth's mantle and core are still orange-hot after the Moon collides with it, but they visibly cool down within ninety seconds as the credits roll. With no atmosphere in space, there's nowhere the Earth's fragments could possibly vent that heat.
