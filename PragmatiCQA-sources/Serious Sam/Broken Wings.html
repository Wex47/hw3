Broken Wings is the third level of Serious Sam 3: BFE and Serious Sam 3 VR: BFE .

Plot

With the death of professor Stein and the defeat of the Adult Arachnoid , Sam manages to get hold of professor Stein's research and uploads the data to headquarters. Sam has been informed that an extraction helicopter is ready to pick him up, so he heads through the streets to find a suitable landing zone at the Mosque of Ibn Tulun so that he can escape the city.

Secrets

  1. The first secret can be found on the sniper's perch (identified with many sandbags and Cloned Soldiers on it) nearby the starting area. To get to it, jump onto the nearby dumpster closest to the wall with sandbags, and then jump on the nearby utility box propped against the wall, and make another hop onto the top of the wall from the utility box. From there, jump over, or go around the sandbags to pick up the hidden shells.
  2. In the yard after the sniper perch, there's a dumpster on the right. Jump on it and over the wall and pick up a Small Armor .
  3. Before the overpass, the ruined building on the right can be climbed into. On the right hand side is some health.
  4. Just after the overpass, on the left are a bunch of shells. If you turn around — concealed by a column supporting the overpass — you will note a pill bottle that counts as a secret and spawns a Kleer Skeleton .
  5. Just after the overpass, on the road to the right there is a building on the right that is blocked off with planks. Break them with the sledgehammer and collect the electricity.
  6. Before the popup Cloned Soldiers, after the first overpass and before passing under it a second time, in the building on the right is a small armor.
  7. Under the overpass, continuing on past secret #4 and beyond a ruined car is an armor shard that counts as a mini secret and spawns a Kleer Skeleton. In Serious Sam Fusion , the Kleer only spawns on Serious difficulty .
  8. Beside the overpass, just after passing under the overpass a second time, turn right and in a little alley is a shotgun.
  9. Beside the overpass,just after passing under the overpass a second time, jump on the garbage bin and over the wall. Once you reach the barrels on the other side the message "secret flanking performed!" shows up. This takes you around a small fight.
  10. A building beside the overpass is blocked off with planks. Break them with the Sledgehammer and collect the armor and shells inside.
  11. When Sam says "Don't have a party without me!", climb up the stairs until there's a break in the wall back towards the overpass. A Large Health pickup is hanging off it; jump across to pick it up.
  12. Before the archway behind a barricade is some rockets that counts as a mini secret.
  13. ( Serious Sam Fusion only) the secret is behind the barricades, to get there, climb up using the skip and then sprint-jump over to get 200+ electricity. Beware that by doing so, an Adult Arachnoid will spawn.
  14. In the courtyard with three broken columns, there is a stack of boxes. Jump on them then over the wall to collect an M29 Infantry Assault Rifle .
  15. Before the mosque, along an alley just a bit before the mosque is a white wall with turned-over gray couch under a dark metal sign embedded in the wall. On the leftmost portion of the same wall past two chairs and mattress will be a drum (the cylindrical container), jump on it, and look directly through the small hole in the wall. There will be a small electrical box directly in sight; shoot it and it will unlock a secret speedrun door. Head to the nearby mosque entrance. Along the left side of the mosque wall prior to the stairs will be a large group of boxes. Break them to expose a ladder ("secret speedrun found") which leads to the end of the outer mosque area.
  16. At the outer mosque, around the second corner of the outer halls of the mosque is a stack of crates. Hidden among them is an armor shard.
  17. At the inner part of mosque, there some scaffolding on the building in the center. Crawl under it and then to the top, then jump up to grab a small armor.

Trivia

  • If the player is speedrunning, they can go out of bounds near the starting area. They need to follow the left-hand wall to find a place where they can climb onto the roof of the building.
  • On Tourist difficulty , the player can quick-kill the boss using the sledgehammer.
