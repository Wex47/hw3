The Medallion is a powerful artifact featured in Serious Sam 2 and serves as the main plot of the story.

Overview

The medallion is a strange item that, apparently, has the ability to weaken Mental enough to kill him. It was created by Gromlin, a blind blacksmith. When it was completed, the Sirian Great Council put a spell on it. It was then split into five different pieces and then given to five different species; the ChiChe , Elvians , Kleers , Simbas , and Zixies .

Mental still managed to get a hold of each of the pieces, but couldn't destroy them. Instead, he guarded them from anyone that could challenge him. If the five pieces were put together again, Mental would become vulnerable. Despite the Council's doubts, they decided to find Serious Sam , bring him to their home, and have him find the five pieces and re-create the medallion so that he can finally defeat Mental.

Appearances

Serious Sam 2

After being sent from one planet to another by the Great Council and defeating many of Mental's forces, Sam finally manages to gather all five parts of the medallion and put them back together, finally making Mental weak enough to be defeated normally. When Sam completes the medallion, the Great Council start their plan to take back Sirius and defeat Mental once and for all, since he is finally vulnerable. Unfortunately, Sam never gets the chance to see the medallion's powers in effect, as Mental escapes Sirius when Sam breaches his compound, the Mental Institution .

In the end, it turns out the medallion is seemingly worthless. Sam finds several boxes containg many medallions in the Great Council's home after he failed to kill Mental, suggesting that there's nothing special about them. Enraged by how the Sirians made Sam go through all of that trouble for a piece of junk, he chases the Sirians around their home to show them what he thinks about their Easter Egg hunt for the medallion's parts.

Trivia

  • It's unclear why the Great Council made Sam go through the ordeal of getting the medallion's pieces in the first place. However, it is possible the council wanted Sam to dispose of Mental's troops and thus weaken his military power in the galaxy.
