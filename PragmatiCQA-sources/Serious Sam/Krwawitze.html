Krwawitze was a small town located in Poland , Central Europe . While it is a minor location in Serious Sam: The Second Encounter , it does play an important part in the plot.

Overview

Krwawitze was probably home to several hundred residents before Mental 's forces arrived. The architecture of the town was like that of any other medieval settlement, with a large castle dominating the area, positioned on top of a hill. At the highest point in this castle was the Book of Wisdom, an important object which revealed the location of the Holy Grail . NETRICSA mentioned that the Arc-Al-Magi lived here, and they had received contact from the Sirians to keep the Holy Grail safe from thieves.

The town was quite dangerous to enter unprepared, with several "kill-o-matic" traps placed within the buildings. These were likely placed here to stop people from stealing the Book of Wisdom.

Appearances

Serious Sam: The Second Encounter

Krwawitze is the location that Serious Sam arrives at after entering the Time-Lock in ancient Babylon. He fights his way through Mental's forces and obtains the Book of Wisdom, which is vital for completing his mission.

As there are no inhabitants in the town by the time Sam arrives, this suggests that the town was attacked by Mental's forces, as Mental was aware of the Holy Grail's importance.

Notable features

The Citadel

This large structure stood out from the rest of the town. It was situated on top of a rocky hill. The citadel consisted of several floors and is laden with "kill-o-matic" traps, which made the citadel a challenge for any intruders who set foot inside it.
