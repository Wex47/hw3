Ugh Zan VI, also known as The Great Warlock , was the sixth incarnation of the Ugh-Zan giants, and one of Mental 's dreadful servants. He is the final boss of Serious Sam 4 .

Overview

Ugh-Zan VI, also known by his titles the Gargantuan Guardian, Warlock of the Portal, and Tah-Um’s Foot (the latter losing something of its originally quite threatening tone in translation) was until very recently known from second-hand accounts. An informant working with the French Resistance suggested the existence of an enormous humanoid, possibly related to General Ugh-Zan IV (presumed to be killed in the Battle of Alpha Centauri) and Ugh-Zan V (obliterated in Oslo , Norway), but details were scarce and General Brand suggested the information might be faulty. In actuality, he does exist, and is perhaps the largest version of the Ugh-Zan giants known to date.

In appearance, he is a massive humanoid creature standing approximately 475 meters tall. He is covered in metal plating and nine conductive metal rods have been placed on several regions of his body, such as his arms, legs, back and head. These rods serve as electrical conductors that attract lightining strikes, which in turn are used to heal Ugh-Zan.

Appearances

Serious Sam 4

Ugh-Zan VI is summoned through the massive portal in Tunguksa by former Earth Defense Force General Brand (now transformed into a large humanoid monster due to having used the Holy Grail on himself) and presents Ugh-Zan with two gifts; the Holy Grail and Sam . Brand attempts to negiogate with the creature and hope that the two can forge an alliance. Unfortunately for Brand, Ugh-Zan shows no interest in gifts or the like, and flicks him off with his finger.

Sam, now free from Brand's grasp and having regained consciousness, is contacted by professor Gottlieb Kiesel , who tells him that Ugh-Zan is using the metal rods on his body to recharge his vital energy. To make him vulnerable, Sam must destroy all nine rods. Thanks to the efforts of Hellfire , who sends him a parachute and grappling hook, he uses these tools to attach himself to the rods on Ugh-Zan's body and destroy them with C-4 charges . With Ugh-Zan now unable to regenerate, Sam finishes him off with either the mech Hellfire supplied him with or his own weapons (the choice is completely up the player's decisions in the end) and is able to temporarily stun him.

Kiesel warns Sam that if he does not act quickly enough, Ugh-Zan will recover and begin attacking again. Thinking of a plan, Sam grabs the Grail and requests support from Hellfire. She lands her helicopter next to him and he hitches a ride. Hellfire flies just close enough for Sam to throw the Grail into Ugh-Zan's mouth. The Grail begins reacting within his body and his eyes start glowing due to the power of the Grail overwhelming him. Within a matter of seconds, the Grail causes his head to explode, killing him.

Powers and abilities

Like other members of the Ugh-Zan dynasty, Ugh-Zan VI is extremely powerful and highly resistant to conventional weapons. He also has the ability to regenerate after losing a substantial amount of his health. He can shoot two laser beams from his eyes that can deal a lot of damage and are hard to avoid. His secondary attack consists of conjuring several red homing projectiles from his arms, which will begin tracking the player until they hit them. These projectiles also deal a fair amount of damage, but they can be destroyed mid-air.

During the fight with Ugh-Zan, he will become aggressive towards the player and begin firing red projectiles at them as soon as the player begins destroying his metal rods.

After his metal rods have been destroyed, Ugh-Zan will take the metal plating off his face and begin firing homing blue projectiles from his mouth. They are more numerous and smaller than the red ones, but like them they can also be shot down.

Tactics

  • During the first phase of the battle, when the player is tasked to destroy the source of Ugh-Zan's regeneration, they should plant all 9 C-4 explosives and then detonate them all at once to make the process easier.
  • Avoid his projectiles by sidestepping or shooting them down. They can deal a lot of damage to both the player and the mech should the player choose to use it.
  • His lasers are hard to dodge and deal more damage than his projectiles. However, with precise timing, they can be avoided.
  • The player should be sure to stock up on health and armor from the crates, as they will be taking a lot of damage from both the drones, Octanian troopers and Ugh-Zan's attacks during this fight.
  • The mech's rockets are more effective on Ugh-Zan than the miniguns, so the player should use them on him whenever they get the chance.

Behind the scenes

  • Early concept designs show Ugh-Zan walking on all fours in a similar manner to an ape and having his face covered in metal plating.

Trivia

  • He is the only member of the Ugh-Zan dynasty to have two arms instead of the usual four.
  • He is the tallest member of the Ugh-Zan giants.
