The Mortuary Temple of Hatshepsut , or just Temple of Hatshepsut , was an ancient Egyptian temple located in Upper Egypt , where it lied beneath the cliffs at Deir el-Bahari on the west bank of the Nile near the Valley of the Kings .

Overview

The Temple of Hatshepsut was a mortuary temple dedicated to the female ruler Hatshepsut during the Eighteenth Dynasty . It was situated next to the mortuary temple of Mentuhotep II.

The Hatshesput temple also served as the designated location for where the Time-Lock would send someone back to upon its activation. It could be found in front of the temple's entrance.

Appearances

Serious Sam: The First Encounter

This temple is where Serious Sam arrives after entering the Time-Lock during the events of Serious Sam 3 , just seconds before the Earth is destroyed by its moon .

Upon arriving at the temple, Sam has to the four elements of air, water, fire and earth, for which he will need to gain access to the city of Thebes . Luckily for him, the first element he is looking for is inside the temple.

Serious Sam 3: BFE

The temple does not have much of a role in the game's campaign compared to The First Encounter . In the game, it is shown that the Time-Lock is located in approximately the same spot as the Time-Lock in The First Encounter .
