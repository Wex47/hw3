The Rocket is a rocket launcher that appears in Serious Sam: Tormental .

Overview

The rocket functions just like the rocket lanchers from other Serious Sam games: it fires an unguided rocket each time the fire key is pressed. The rockets will keep travelling in the direction they are fired until they hit something and explode. Like other serious weapons, the rocket can receive two upgrades which increases the damage the rockets deal to the target as well as an increased blast radius.

The rocket can be obtained from blue Mental chests or bought from shops with keys. The upgraded versions will be available to buy from the shops in the V2 and V3 levels during loops, but only if the player is equipped with the weapon. They also have a very rare chance to appear in a large Mental chest in the Frontal Lobe right before entering Mental Woods .

Tactics

  • The rocket is very useful on tough enemies like Cerebulls , Croakueens , Helioporas , Gunrillas and Red Widows .
  • The rocket is an excellent weapon against the Brainstorm boss; the rockets fired from it can blast away at the Brainstorm's body, shield and turrets whilst dealing tons of damage to the boss itself.
  • It is also a great weapon for eliminating groups of weak or explosive enemies such as the Boombat while they are paired together.
  • The upgraded rocket can take on bosses, tougher foes and their elite variants much more easily than the standard serious weapons due to the damage each projectile fired deals.
