Overview

The combine harvester appears as an ordinary harvester with a long header that, instead of being used for harvesting crops, can be used to grind up hordes of enemies into pieces.

In Serious Sam: Next Encounter , when it takes a critical mass of damage or runs out of fuel, the harvester will explode. If it's destroyed by enemies the level will instantly end regardless of how far the player has progressed, though they can exit it at any time and leave it aside somewhere it would be safe. Also, the harvester will take damage from bumping into objects, though besides trees, towers and the city walls at the two sides of the level, there aren't many potential dangerous hazards.

In Serious Sam 4 , the harvester is a slow vehicle with a slow turn speed. However, its cutting platform can mow down weak enemies with ease, as well as destroy obstacles such as bushes and trees, though performing these actions depletes the vehicle's health. In addition, the player can use firearms to shoot any targets in the distance. It's perfect for "harvesting" swarms of enemies such as Gnaars , Kleer Skeletons and Processed Anthropolyp , though it's slow movement and steering speed makes it an easy target for Beheaded Kamikazes and Major Bio-mechanoids , and Kleers and Processed can easily catch up with the harvester from behind and climb onto the vehicle. Once on the harvester, enemies will be able to damage the player behind the wheel unless they are shot off.

Like other vehicles in Serious Sam 4 , the player will die if the harvester is destroyed.

Appearances

Serious Sam: Next Encounter

The combine harvester appears in The Silk Road lost level.

Serious Sam 4

The combine harverster appears in A Breakfast in France . After Sam helps the hippie farmer named Filibert defend his farm from Mental 's forces (which he himself summoned, believing they were some of sort of peaceful angels and not invaders), he thanks Sam by providing location of a military base and allows Sam to take his harvester.

Tactics

Serious Sam 4

  • It's an ideal weapon for mowing down hordes of weak enemies, though the player should try to kill most of the enemies in one mow and keep an eye out for any enemies they missed, as they can climb onto the harvester from behind all the way to the driving cabin. They must be shot as soon as they appear.
  • It's not advisable to run over Emetic Antropolyps , as their explosion blast damages both the player and the vehicle.
  • Major Bio-Mechanoids can be very dangerous when using the harvester, as the vehicle's slow speed makes it hard to dodge their rockets. They should be treated as high-priority targets when in the harvester and should be killed as quickly as possible. When they fire their rockets, steer the harvester to the other side to dodge them.
  • Kamikazes should be high-priority targets along with Major Bio-mechanoids.
  • It's better to switch to third-person when shooting, as being in first-person will limit the player's field of view. The player can also fire in any direction when in third-person.
  • Unless needed to, do not run over obstacles such as trees and bushes, as they damage the harverster.
  • If the harvester's health is too low, exit the harvester and fight on foot.
