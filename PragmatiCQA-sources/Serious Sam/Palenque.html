Overview

Palenque was a Maya city state located in Chiapas , Mexico . Like the city of Teotihuacan , the area was designed with Mayan architecture, such as statues, terraced pyramids and various other details. Some of these statues featured giant heads or alligators. Palenque was much larger in size compared to Teotihuacan, but had much less features. There didn't appear to be any houses, so it's unknown where the residents lived in at the time, or if they only visited Palenque for religious purposes.

Just like Teotihuacan, Palenque was very difficult to navigate through. Crusher traps, walls with spikes and floating bouncers made it extremely challenging for anyone who ventured into it.

Notable features

Temple of Teoquocatlian

This temple was dedicated to Teoquocatlian, the Mayan god of courage. It was built deep in the bowels of the Earth and filled with traps and puzzles. It served as the training ground for the Mayan army. Inside was a massive cave system with floating bouncers and large sliding objects that were powered by magic. It's possible that the Sirians placed them there, as these devices didn't look like they were designed by the Mayans.

Secret Valley of the Jaguar

The Valley of the Jaguar wvs a large, flat plateau hidden deep within Palenque. There were three large pyramids located to the left, right, and center areas of the valley. Here, the Mayans kept a crystal skull inside the largest of the three pyramids as a sacred relic. This skull was found in a crater around 300 A.D., which was made from an alien material found on Sirius used to construct starships. According to NETRICSA , there were reports of a Sirian scout ship that was lost over the hills of Sierra de Chiapas during this time.

Appearances

Serious Sam: The Second Encounter

Palenque is the location that the SSS Centerprice crashes into after a Croteam crate bus full of Croteam Heads collides with the spaceship. Sam survives the crash, and NETRICSA informs him that the Sirians left an important item, a crystal skull, that can be used to activate the Time-Lock in the ancient city of Teotihuacan .

Sam fights his way through some temples and an underground complex which eventually leads him to the Valley of the Jaguar. NETRICSA says that to open the pyramid contaning the skull, he must find two golden jaguars that have been placed inside two smaller pyramids on the left and right parts of the plateau. He makes it into the pyramids and places the statues on top of the ceremonial temple, which activates a mechanism that destroys the pyramid door. Sam deals with more of Mental's forces before getting to the pyramid and collects the skull from within. Now that he has the skull in his possession, he makes it to the city of Teotihuacan.
