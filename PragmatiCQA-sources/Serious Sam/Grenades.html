Grenades are a type of ammunition that serve as ammo for grenade launchers (including the one used for the 12 Gauge Pump-Action Shotgun in Serious Sam 4 , but not for the RB-45 Hand Grenades that appear in Serious Sam 2 .

Overview

The amount of rounds in each box of grenades depends on the game and (in some cases) the difficulty level. In all games except for Serious Sam 2 , Serious Sam: Double D and Serious Sam: Double D: XXL , each box gives out 5 rounds on Normal and Hard difficulties and 10 on Tourist, Easy and Serious difficulties.

Appearances

Grenades are fairly frequent throughout the series, though they are not as common as rockets . They mostly appear before a big fight or in "break" rooms to give the player enough ammo to resupply after a fight. Grenades usually appear along with their respective weapon as well.

Behind the scenes

  • In Test 1 , the grenade's model can be found in the game's files, but cannot be picked up. It uses the same model, but the box is wood brown and there are four oval shapes on it. The grenades were used for the scrapped Pipebomb as it also might have been supposed to be in the other level for testing. But the files are cut or locked because they had cut the level from Test 1 several months before the release.
