Overview

Charlie Team were a group of five soldiers who were supposed to be sent into the Time-Lock in the past to change the future of humanity and prevent Mental from ever discovering Earth and sending his forces there.

Appearances

Serious Sam 3: BFE

Charlie Team is first mentioned by Quinn after Sam activates the second plasma generator under Horus, telling him that they are ready for insertion. They are briefly mentioned a second time Wilson while taking Sam back to headquarters in his helicopter.

Unfortunately for the team, the Earth Defense Force's base is overrun by Mental's forces in a sneak attack , and their whereabouts after this are not known. Sam, learning of their fate, decides to go to the Time-Lock in order to start their mission on his own, giving humanity one last chance.

Trivia

  • It is unknown how Charlie Team would have completed their mission with only five people or how they could locate and assassinate Mental.
  • It's possible that all the weapons, ammo and supplies that appear in Ancient Egypt were originally meant for Charlie Team to use, which would explain why there is so much equipment appearing during that period.
