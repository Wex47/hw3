Overview

The Octanian Commander belongs to a race of humanoid polyp-controlled creatures originating from an unnamed planet in the Sigma Octantis system. They have allied themselves with Mental during the Mental War as part of his goal to eradicate all knowledge from the universe.

Unlike many other forces in Mental's Horde , the Octanians follow a strict line of command. They are elite soldiers recruited exclusively from the ranks of the secretive Order of Octus Dei. Like other Octanian soldiers, Commanders are equipped with standard-issue body armor, which provides them with moderate to substantial protection in combat.

Commanders also wear respirators and goggles to help them survive Earth 's atmospheric conditions. They carry a powerful deflective energy shield which they use as their main equipment. This shield can withstand small-arms fire, but can be easily broken with explosive projectiles like grenades or rockets.

In-game, Octanian Commanders are first encountered in Death from Below , where one can be heard speaking words of encouragement to a squad of Octanian Troopers. Throughout the game, they appear few at a time as the game progresses, and then in large groups in the later levels. They are usually seen with squads of Gamma Troopers.

Application

Octanian Commanders are deployed as foot soldiers to take control of human settlements like towns and cities. They will often lead a squad of Octanian Gamma Troopers to secure the area and monitor enemy movements. When not engaged in combat, Commanders patrol the cities or the countryside, leading squads of Gamma Troopers with them.

Behavior and skills

When confronted with a target, a Commander will get out his deflective shield and crouch on the ground, keeping a safe distance from the player. This shield can withstand a fair amount of damage. As the shield takes damage, it will turn red until it shatters. If the shield is destroyed, the Commander will deploy another one. When he is using the shield, if the player is close to the Commander, he will fire several homing projectiles from his gun. Further away, he will fire a rapid volley of projectiles. Unlike Gamma Troopers, the Commander does not have a melee attack, even if the player is right in front of him.

Tactics

  • When dealing with Commanders, take out any other enemies first, circle around the Commander and past his shield, then quickly finish him off before he turns around.
  • The projectiles fired by the Commander can be dodged by sidestepping.
  • Because Commanders fire a lot of projectiles, they can quickly drain the player's health if they don't pay attention to where they are coming from.
  • It may be tempting to fire at his shield, but this is actually a waste of ammo. His shield is tough and can take a few rounds from shotguns or the assault rifle before it can break. Therefore, it is best to use explosive weapons like C-4 Demolition Charges , the MK III Grenade Launcher , or the XPML21 Rocket Launcher to eliminate him. These weapons also work very well against groups of Commanders due to their low health.
  • When using explosive weapons against Commanders, it is best to aim at the Commander's feet rather than directly at their shield.
  • With other enemies and on lower difficulties, Commanders are a low to medium-priority threat. On higher difficulties, they should be treated as a high-priority threat and eliminated as soon as possible.
