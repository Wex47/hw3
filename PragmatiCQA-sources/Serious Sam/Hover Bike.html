The Hover Bike is a vehicle scrapped from Serious Sam 2 .

  • 1 Overview
  • 2 Fate

Overview

The hover bike was a hovering motorbike. It came with two color schemes, one that would be used by the Alliance (blue), and the other was to used by Mental's Horde , probably Orcs (orange).

Fate

The hover bike was removed from the final version of the game for unknown reasons and replaced by the CH-181 Hover Bike .

Though it has been removed from the game, its models can still be found in the game files.
