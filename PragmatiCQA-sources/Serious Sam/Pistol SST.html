Overview

The pistol, along with the Sledgehammer , is the first weapon the player obtains in the game. It functions just like any other handgun from other Serious Sam games; it has a decent rate of fire and infinite ammo (unless the player changes this option at the beginning of Mental Woods ) but low damage output. Thus, it is only useful against weak enemies or as a last resort. With mods, however, it becomes much more useful.

The pistol can receive many modifications which are usually obtained after defeating the boss of a level. They can be obtained from shops using figments . Some mods have upgrades as well that can improve that mod. These mod upgrades are much rarer than the usual pistol mods and have a rare chance to appear from large Mental chests. After being upgraded, the pistol's name will correspond to the name of the upgrade. For instance, a pistol with the freeze shot and burstenize upgrades would be called "icey megaburstgun".

Tactics

  • The pistol is useful for clearing out weak enemies such as Gonks , Lizards , Snorklers , etc., but not great against tough enemies like Cerebulls , Gunrillas or Red Widows unless the player has good upgrades for it.
  • If the player has to use the pistol, they should only use it if no other weapons or ammo is available.
  • With upgrades, this increases the pistol's potential during the later levels.
