Overview

Valtos is a lush tropical planet covered in dense forets and swamps. During the Mental War , Valtos was one of many planets that was invaded by Mental 's forces, as Earth Defense Force intelligence reports suggest its inhabitants have been "bombed back to the Stone Age", but continue to resist the invasion.

Not many species are known to inhabit the planet except for Sirens , a species of fish-like creatures that live in the swamps. The Tentacle could also be native to the planet.

Appearances

Serious Sam VR: The Last Hope

After learning of reports that Mental's forces are attacking Valtos, Sam visits the planet and ends up in a swamp, where he clears the area of enemies like usual. Later, Sam travels further and meets up with the leader of the resistance on Valtos. He shows Sam the location of his next objective, so he goes there and cleans out any enemies that stand in his way. Once he finally makes it to his mission objective, he encounters the Swamp Hive and faces even tougher enemy resistance than usual. He emerges victorious after some tough fighting. Sam later heads back to the Battlecruiser Saratoga and heads to his next location: Arcadia Minor .
