The Egyptian Museum , also known as the Museum of Egyptian Antiquities , was a history museum that housed an extensive collection of Ancient Egyptian antiquities. It became abandoned during the Mental War .

Overview

The Egyptian Museum housed the world’s largest collection of Ancient Egyptian treasures and artifacts. Some of these artifacts gave various clues about how to activate the Time-Lock , making it serve as an important location for Hermann Stein , a scientist who did his research on the very matter.

History

The Egyptian government established the museum built in 1835 near the Ezbekeyah Garden and was later moved to the Cairo Citadel. In 2015, the museum was turned into an auxiliary depot. Over 100 years later, Mental invaded Earth with his alien army, spreading chaos and panic throughout Cairo as the city became overrun with Mental's forces. Many buildings, including the museum, became abandoned and were left to the elements.

After the museum became abandoned, the front entrance was barricaded and the windows were boarded up. Inside the museum, foliage was left to grow rampant and the museum's interior had slowly begun to deteriorate. Antaresian spiders also began to make the museum their home, laying hundreds of cocoons on the floor, the walls, and ceilings. This made the museum a dangerous place to venture into unprepared.

Appearances

Serious Sam 3: BFE

Prior to the events of the game, professor Stein and a band of soldiers known as Bravo Team , sent by the Earth Defense Force , are tasked with protecting and escorting him into the museum so he can conduct further research on the Time-Lock . Sometime later, Bravo Team lose contact with HQ after they come under attack and are killed by Mental's forces, leaving Stein all alone. With Stein's life on the line, the EDF sends out Team Alpha to recon with Bravo and rescue Stein from the museum. The plan ends in faliure when Team Alpha's helicopter is shot down and most of its members are killed.

Sam, the only survivor, makes it to the museum, where Quinn informs Sam that the professor will not hand over his information until he is rescued. Sam ventures down into the basement to extract him, though it turns out he is too late, as the professor has already been killed by Antaresian Spiders. Despite this outcome, Sam manages to upload the data from Stein's cell phone and sends it to headquarters.

After uploading the data, Sam continues through the alien-infested basement, killing more of Mental's forces along the way until he makes it back to the surface. Before he can exit the museum, he encounters an Adult Arachnoid , but he kills it. After killing the Arachnoid, Sam makes it out of the museum and onto the streets of Cairo, where he awaits extraction by Garret .
