Plot

Professor Stein 's research indicates that there's a secret chamber beneath the Great Pyramid , accessed using the Sphinx . While scientists at the Earth Defense Force headquarters scramble to find the answer to the riddle of the Sphinx, Sam journeys to it to blow it up and find out what's inside.

Secrets

  1. On a building in the first village, climb up the wall on the left side of the main street on its first break. Jump across the buildings towards the one with the satellite dish and a collection of aerials, then climb up it to find a RAPTOR Sniper Rifle to get a "King of the Hill secret found!" message.
  2. Just outside the first village, a dirt road runs perpendicular to the village. Looking to the right you can see a truck, and if you're at the truck you should see a black spike in the desert, which is a utility pole. If the Sandwhale hasn't surfaced recently, run out until you hear rumbling and then run back, let it come up and go back down, then race out to get the weapon at the pole. The Sandwhale seems to take a bit longer than usual to start burrowing towards you if you make a beeline directly towards the minigun from the truck.
  3. On the left side of the second village, behind a building, is an explosive barrel partially buried in the sand. Shooting it will reveal a Medium Armor .
  4. Further into the second village there is a walled off yard with a couple of different ways in (3 holes in the wall and an open gate; in the sixth row of houses, relatively central). In this area is a light blue dumpster against a building. Between the dumpster and the building is an almost stuck Medium Health .
  5. ( Serious Sam Fusion only) at the far right of the village ,just before the entrance to the ancient ruins, head right and you will see a building, take another right through a small gap in the building to find an Extra Large Armor . Keep in mind that picking the armor up will spawn a Major Bio-mechanoid so be sure to get out of there as soon as you pick the armor up.
  6. At the entrance to the monument, the kiosk at the entrance to the monument has a caged door on it, but can be opened by pressing the Use key when prompted. Entering it shows the message "secret ticket booth found!" and spawns a Beheaded Rocketeer , who gives you a bag of money when you approach the counter, but gets angry when you take it. Killing him gets a quip from Sam and spawns more Beheaded Rocketeers.
  7. At the entrance to the monument, there's a locked yard to the left of the entrance that's also opened with the keycard. Inside the base of the crane is a Small Health .
  8. In the tunnels after the stairs, there's a long hall with dozens of blind alcoves. In one on the left near the end is another hallway with a spooky hall and an A-24 Devastator .
  9. In the final area, if you go down the stairs to the door to the Sphinx, there's a Large Health tucked in the corner on the right.

Trivia

  • There is a way to break out of the map in this level: if the player times the Sandwhale right and gets lucky in the last area, they can run around the left wall and make it all the way back to the Sphinx. If they stop just before going in the pit, the Sandwhale will go away and they can walk along the wall. There isn't anything there, though. If the player heads back through the Sphinx, when the elevator reaches the top they will fall through the floor to their death after a moment.
  • If the player wishes to speedrun, they need to stand by the ticket booth, and then sprint along the right side of the desert rock wall, which lets themflank past the labyrinth (from the outside). Clipping becomes missing, and the player can drop into the labyrinth, and even continue.
  • With good timing, a Sirian Werebull can be used to launch the player over the locked gate behind the Sphinx, skipping the whole trek through the pyramid and making them land near the secret #9. The Sirian Mutilator will still get equipped for the final fight, even though the player doesn't actually pick it up.
