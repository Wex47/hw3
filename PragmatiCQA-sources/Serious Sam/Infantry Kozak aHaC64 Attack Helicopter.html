Overview

The "Kozak aHa-C64" is the latest helicopter in Mental 's line, thanks to him putting in an order for "one big mo-fo of a helicopter" on the planet Doomsday. It can fly stealthily, has excellent maneuverability, and uses a set of very deadly weapons. It is presumably piloted by Orcs .

Kozaks first appear in Road to Ursul , and every now and then throughout the game. They are much more common in Kronor and Sirius .

A rideable version of this helicopter exists, which is needed to fight Hugo during the boss fight.

Weaponry

The Kozak helicopter is equipped with rocket launchers, a machine gun and a bomb. It will use the rockets at a distance, but will switch to the machine gun if an enemy is close to it. It can drop a bomb when the player is underneath it.

Tactics

  • The RAPTOR Sniper Rifle is devastating against the helicopter. Two scoped shots will make short work of it.
  • If the player doesn't have a sniper rifle, other insta-hit weapons, like the Beam Gun , Double Shotgun or XM214-A Minigun , work well against the helicopter, as their insta-hit nature prevents the helicopter from dodging the attack.
  • If using the double shotgun, it is best to get close to the helicopter so that the maximum amount of pellets will hit the Helicopter. However, this puts the player within its machine gun range.
  • Klawdovics also work well against the helicopter, as it will track the helicopter and blow up when it reaches the helicopter.
  • Its rockets can be shot down by regular weapons, which is very handy when fighting one.
  • Its rockets can be dodged by strafing at an angle, but there are times when the angle of the rockets and/or their explosion radius will make a rocket or two damage the player.
  • The SBC Cannon can be used as a last resort, but it will require a good aim from the player as the Helicopter can dodge the player's cannonballs.
  • Helicopters can become quite annoying if left unchecked because of their rockets and the damage they do, so the player should try to eliminate them quickly.

Trivia

  • Despite clearly being named after the Boeing AH-64 Apache , the helicopter is actually modeled after the Boeing-Sikorsky RAH-66 Comanche , but with rounder, goofier, and more cartoonish proportions, so as to give it a more alien and unrealistic look.
