Overview

The Klawdovic is a species of bird native to the planet M'Digbo . Originally trained as a hunting weapon for the Simbas , the Klawdovic was modified when Mental 's forces arrived on their planet, by feeding it a poisonous flower that will cause it to explode when it hits something.

In-game, the Klawdovic is essentially a homing projectile. When launched, it will go for the enemy the player is looking at. However, the Klawdovic's homing ability is not perfect; sudden changes at medium or long range can mess up its homing ability and cause it to fly straight into the sky.

The Klawdovic is first obtained at the beginning of Road to Ursul and does not appear again until Floaterra .

Tactics

  • When using the Klawdovic, the player should try to make sure there isn't too much space between themselves and the target before firing, as the Klawdovic's homing abilities cannot adjust to sudden changes at long range.
  • The Klawdovic is very useful against strong enemies, such as the Albino Cyclops or the large Spider Mechanoid , mostly because of how powerful it is.
  • While it is useful against groups of weaker enemies, using it against them is a waste, as the SBC Cannon or the XPML30 Rocket Launcher can do the same thing without wasting valuable Klawdovic ammo.
  • It is quite effective against the Infantry "Kozak aHa-C64" Attack Helicopter . The Klawdovic's homing abilities allow it to hit the helicopter, no matter where if its moving or not. In addition, it does a very large amount of damage to the helicopter.

Behind the scenes

  • The Klawdovic had a different model in the beta; one was blue and one was green.

Trivia

  • Klawdovic was named after Klodovik, a parrot in the Alan Ford comics, which is very popular in Croatia.
