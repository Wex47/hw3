Thomas Jones , or just Jones , was a member of the Earth Defense Force and part of the Alien Artifact Acquisition squad. He appears as a supporting character in Serious Sam 4 .

Biography

Jones was born in Beaumont on October 13th, 2079. Before the Mental War , he worked as a cook in a variety of restaurants up and down the Gulf of Mexico .

In his adult years, he decided to enlist during the fight for the colonies, being the first in his family to do so. He was assigned into the Colonial Defense Force and the 22nd Spaceborne division, and fought in several battles, such as the Battle of Alpha Centauri, the Battle of Beijing, and the Fall of Orbital 7. An unknown amount of time after that, he joined the Earth Defense Force and met Rodriguez, who became his best friend. During his time in the EDF, he was picked by General Brand to serve in his Alien Artifact Acquisition team.

He had a brother, also called Jones, who appears in Serious Sam 3: BFE .

Appearances

Serious Sam 4

Jones, along with Hellfire, Kenny , Rodriguez, and Sam, are sent to find Father Mikhail , a Russian priest who has information about the location of the Holy Grail , which could assist humanity in wining the battle against Mental . On their way there, Hellfire warns them about incoming Octanian Raiders that attack the convey with bombs, destroying several vehicles. In the chaos, Hellfire, Jones and Rodriguez are separated from Kenny and Sam. Jones is later seen with Rodriguez fighting off a wave of aliens in the Piazza della Repubblica before Sam and Kenny arrive with Father Mikhail. They all fight off more waves of Mental's forces before killing a Saggitarian Sidewinder .

Once the fight is over, Hellfire appears in a stolen bus and extracts the team to an Earth Defense Force base outside of Rome. There, they meet up with Brand and Quinn in the briefing room to discuss how to safely get to the Vatican Library without being attacked by more Octanian Raiders. Carter discusses using the HAARP to make Mount Vesuvius to make it erupt. They all ultimately agree and head to Pompeii to plant the beacons. Jones plant his first beacon and makes his way to plant the second, but before he can, he goes quiet.

The team get worried about Jones' status, so they have Sam go to the colosseum which was the location of Jones' last GPS reading. Sam then finds a Khnum with Jones in his hand. The Khnum devours Jones before Sam defeats the Khnum in a boss fight. Having finished the Khnum, he shows no mercy for the creature and shoves Jones' beacon straight through its skull, avenging the death of his former comrade.

Jones is then briefly mentioned by Rodriguez later in the game.

Appearance

Jones was an African-American man with shaved black hair and had a striking resemblance to his brother. He wore a camo jacket equipped with a bulletproof vest and pants along with black combat boots.

Personality and skills

Jones had a calm and collected man, though he was a bit quiet, as mentioned by a few of his friends, especially Rodriguez. He was noted for keeping calm even under extreme circumstances, a trait he claimed to have developed while working as a sous-chef in a popular restaurant in Havana. He was often paired with Rodriguez, to whom he formed an ideal counterweight.

Relationships

Sam Stone

Jones was a member of Sam's squad and his personal friend. Although being the most silent out of the bunch, he had a good relationship with everyone in the team, including Sam. Jones and Sam were exchanging a few words and even jokes throughout the mission on stopping Mental's hordes attacking the planet.

After seeing Jones being eaten by a Khnum, this enraged Sam. During the fight with the Khnum, Sam was furious, constantly saying "I do not like my friends being eaten by monsters." throughout the battle with the Khnum, which showed how close his relationship was with Jones.

Rodriguez

Rodriguez was Jones' best friend. He was seen with Jones the most out of everyone on the team, to the point where Sam first discovers them in Rome together after finding Father Mikael in Serious Sam 4 . They would often exchange one-liners with each other.

Rodriguez was the first member to worry about Jones' whereabouts on the mission on planting the beacons in Pompeii, and just like Sam and the rest of the team, he became both mad and sad after discovering on what truly happened to Jones. On their way to Rome, Rodriguez told Sam that he was still in denial on the fact that Jones is dead, stating that he was a "good and strong man".

Kenny

Jones and Kenny didn't speak much to each other, and had a sort of neutral relationship. Like Rodriguez, he became stern with him with his bad use of one-liners, telling him that "you gotta survive with style".
