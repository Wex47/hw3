Hard difficulty is a difficulty setting in most Serious Sam games.

Overview

Hard difficulty is the second hardest difficulty in the game. Hard is best played by players with at least some experience in the Serious Sam games, not just FPS games, and have quick reflexes as well as have memorized most enemy spawn locations, which will make it easier for them to survive.

Advantages

You can rocket jump (except in Serious Sam 2 , Serious Sam 3: BFE and Serious Sam 3 VR: BFE ), as self-damage is enabled.

Disadvantages

Enemies are more numerous than on Normal difficulty , and are stronger (in Serious Sam 3 and Serious Sam 3 VR: BFE , the enemy amount increases usually by only a few though) and faster. There is also a health and armor degeneration system in Serious Sam: Xbox and Serious Sam: Next Encounter , so that if your health or armor is above 100, it starts degenerating slowly, by 1 point at short intervals. Self damage is also enabled.
