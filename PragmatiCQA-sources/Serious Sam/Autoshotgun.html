The Autoshotgun is an automatic combat shotgun used by the Earth Defense Force . It makes its first appearance in Serious Sam 4 , and later appears in Serious Sam: Siberian Mayhem .

Overview

The autoshotgun is, hence its name, an automatic shotgun. Unlike the 12 Gauge Pump-Action Shotgun and Double Barrel Coach Gun , it doesn't need to be cocked or manually reloaded after each shot.

The weapon uses 12-gauge shells as ammunition and fires seven pellets per shot every time the fire key is pressed. Holding down the fire key allows for continous fire. The weapon utilizes drum magazines with a capacity of 20 shells, allowing it to fire shells in quick succession, giving it a very high damage output in a short space of time. It also has a very tight spread, which allows for most of the pellets to reach past medium range. Given these advantages, this makes the autoshotgun a more ideal choice compared to the other two shotguns. However, the weapon still has to be manually reloaded after its magazine has been expended. The weapon also has a slightly long reload time, which preludes it use when faced against large swarms of enemies or when battling tougher opponents. Also, using the weapon often will also drain ammunition quickly. Since ammunition for the weapon is not very common, the shotgun should be saved for when the player is dealing with large hordes of enemies clustered together, especially weak targets.

Appearances

The autoshotgun is first obtained in Morituri Te Salutant . Later on, it is given to the player by Sayid in Viva la Résistance after the player completes the first side quest. After that, it appears for the final time in The Package .

In Siberian Mayhem , the autoshotgun can only be found as a secret in Roadside Picnic , during the "Alpha and Omega" sidequest.

Tactics

  • The autoshotgun is a very useful weapon for clearing out groups or hordes of weak to medium targets, such as Beheaded Bombers , Beheaded Firecrackers , Beheaded Kamikazes , Beheaded Rocketeers , Emetic Anthropolyps , Processed Anthropolyps , Aludran Microhoppers , and Space Mummies .
  • The weapon can also be used against medium and tougher enemies like Aludran Reptiloids , Kleer Skeletons , Sirian Werebulls , Draconian Burners , or Scrapjacks , but for tough enemies, the player needs to fire at their head if they want to take them out quickly.
  • Due to the weapon's reload time, it is best used to quickly resolve high-danger situations, such as being surrounded or facing a large group of weak enemies.

Trivia

  • The weapon's model is based on the Origin-12 , an American semi-automatic shotgun which is widely known as " the fastest shotgun in the world ".
