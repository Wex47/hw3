Overview

The General is a modified Bull Soldiers that has a Tank Bio-mechanoid 's lower body. This combines the Bull Soldier's rocket launchers with the Tank Bio-mechanoid's maneuverability, which makes them deadly foes.

The General is apparently, besides bosses, the strongest and biggest enemy in the game. It is only smaller than the Aludran Reptiloid: Highlander's Bride from Serious Sam: The Second Encounter .

Occurrence

The normal variants of The General appear throughout the game starting from Giant Junkyard ( Road to Ursul in alpha version), the large variant appears in Shield Generator and Siriusopolis Downtown , and the monster version appears in Coast 2 Cost and Command Center .

Behavior and skills

In a similar manner to the Bull Soldier and Major Bio-mechanoid , the General's main attack is to fire a rocket from one arm, then quickly fire another one from the opposite arm. This lets it fire two rockets almost at once. In addition, it will also smack the player with its guns if they get too close to it.

The General has three variants; normal, large, and monster. The large version has more health and is larger, while the monster version is much larger than the normal version, has a lot more health, can regenerate its health to a certain point when it runs low, and is equipped with much-larger rockets that deal more damage than a T-Mech 's rockets.

Tactics

  • The General's rockets can be avoided by strafing in one direction. Make sure there's enough room to dodge, as close-range rockets are a bit harder to dodge than medium or long range ones.
  • The General's rockets can also be blown up by shooting them, which is handy if the player can't deal with the General at the moment, but they still need a way to neutralize his rockets so they don't hit the player while they're dodging other enemies and their attacks.
  • Making use of the combo weapons system to dual wield powerful weapons together like the SBC Cannon , Beam Gun , and XPML30 Rocket Launcher is a very useful tactic to destroy Generals, whether alone or in groups.

Normal

  • Multiple Generals can be taken out with the MK IV Grenade Launcher or the XPML30 Rocket Launcher. The XM214-A Minigun works better if they need to be taken out ASAP, while the rocket launcher works better if the player has some time to kill them. This is because the rockets fired by the rocket launcher are slower than the minigun's insta-hit bullets. Dual-wielding both the rocket launcher and minigun is a great tactic.
  • When paired with other enemies, The General is a medium threat. While its rockets are dangerous, they can be shot down, which makes it easier to handle it when swarmed by other enemies.

Large

  • The large variant's rockets are very powerful, but at the same time, their larger size makes it easier to shoot them down. The player should take advantage of this if they need to use rapid-fire weapons to wipe out close-range annoyances before focusing on the large variant. The rapid-fire gun can also shoot down the rockets fired by the monster variant, making it easier to deal with him while under attack from other enemies.
  • When paired with other enemies, the large variant is a top-priority target because of how powerful its rockets are and how its size can restrict player movement if it starts moving forward. Get rid of it ASAP in order to make it easier to dodge other enemy attacks.

Monster

  • Powerful weapons are needed in order to kill the monster General. Charged up SBC Cannon shots and Klawdovics are the most effective against the monster variant. The cannon can inflict a very large amount of damage with one shot, while the Klawdovic can do a lot of damage but has a lower maximum ammo limit. Once again, dual wielding powerful weapons together will help against the monster General.
  • The monster variant can regenerate its health when it runs low, which means the player needs to constantly focus on it in order to destroy it. If the player stops firing to focus on killing other enemies, this'll give the monster variant some time to regenerate some health, making it somewhat more difficult to finish it off.
  • Use the CH-181 Hover Bike 's speed to avoid the monster variant's rockets. The rockets fired by it are huge, but the Hover Bike is fast enough that it can avoid its rockets if the player is constnatly either circle strafing or moving in one direction.
  • Keeping some distance between the monster General and the player is quite important with the monster variant, more so than with the other ones. The monster's rockets are harder to dodge at close range than at long because they have a very large explosion radius and they'll hit something much sooner if they're shot at close range than at longer. Being at long range when it fires its rockets gives the player time to avoid their large explosion radius.
  • Try to target the monster first when facing other enemies. Its size gives the player less room to move around and dodge other enemies, and its large rockets can be dangerous because of their large explosion radius.
  • It is not advisable to engage a General monster up close, as its melee attack can deal a whooping 200 damage to the player on Serious difficulty .

Trivia

  • It is unknown what kind of bull/demon-like creature makes up the body of the Bull Soldier, General and the Tank Bio-mechanoid from the waist up. One possible theory is that, like the Sirian Werebull , they are made from Sirian and cattle DNA.
