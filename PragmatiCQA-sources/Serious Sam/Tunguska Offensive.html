The Tunguska Offensive was a major conflict involving the Earth Defense Force and Mental's Horde after the sudden appearance of a massive portal and additional smaller ones that opened up in random locations around the world. The largest portal opened in Tunguska , Russia . The conflict lasted for over a month until all known portals were successfully destroyed and most, if not all of Mental 's troops had been killed.

Prelude

In the year 2112, a huge portal appeared in the Tunguska Plateau, which served as a bridgehead for Mental's forces to invade Earth . One of Mental's servants and generals, Lord Achriman , was put in charge of Mental's forces on Earth and began establishing smaller portals across Europe to ensure the invasion was a success.

The invasion

After the smaller portals began to appear in more densely populated countries like France and Italy, this allowed Achirman's forces to spread more quickly and gain control over large cities like Carcassonne , Paris , and Rome . It's not known if any other portals appeared, since only two small ones were seen during the events of the conflict. When the invasion began, the overwhelming number of Mental's hordes forced EDF troops to retreat further back towards Irkutsk . Meanwhile, France was already in its fourth week of being occupied by the alien hordes, and Paris had taken heavy infrastructural damage, leading to the Battle of Paris.

Luckily for the EDF, they were able to contact some of their sources, and were being told that the bulk of Lord Achriman's troops was starting to move out of the capital, presumably to aid the forces occupying the south, which had been debaliated by constant attacks from the French Resistance. In Paris itself, things were not turning out so well, with civilians struggling to find any food or clean water. In response, the EDF sent relief efforts their way to help out any survivors, but they soon became hampered by the constant swarms of Combat Drones and the presence of an Octanian Harvester Leviathan .

Stopping the threat

With the sudden appearance of the portal in Russia, the Earth Defense Force scrambled across the globe to respond to the threat. Thousands of troops, tanks, and other military vehicles were sent to the location to provide fire support. Supply points and forward outposts were set up in various locations to ensure that any troops out on the battlefield were provided with plenty of ammunition, equipment and medical supplies.

Thanks to the efforts of one of humanity's strongest soldiers, Sam Stone , most of Mental's forces that came through the portal were killed. Sam was also able to destroy the two small portals that had appeared in France and Italy. Given these advantages, the Earth Defense Force had less of Mental's forces to deal with, and were finally able to push Mental's troops back towards the larger portal in Russia.

One last push

With victory almost in the Earth Defense Force's favor, they started sending their men, along with a few M1A1 Abrams in support, closer towards the portal. Mental retailiated by sending in his toughest units such as Khnums and Major Bio-mechanoids , as well as hundreds of Octanian Gamma Troopers , in a last-ditch effort to wipe out the EDF.

During this final battle, Sam joins up with Carter , Hellfire and Rodriguez , and leads the rest of the EDF soldiers towards the portal. Suddenly, a transformed Howard Brand (who had betrayed his fellow comrades) appears and knocks Sam unconscious. Brand summons Ugh-Zan VI , one of the Warlock giants, in the hope that they can forge an alliance. Brand also presents Sam and the Holy Grail as a gift to Mental, in the hope that the powers contained within the Grail will be enough to finish off the human race. Ugh-Zan, however, is not interested and flicks Brand aside.

Sam, having regained consciousness, is contacted by professor Gottlieb Kiesel , who tells him that he will need to destroy Ugh-Zan's metal rods in an effort to weaken the creature. He succeeds in incapacitating him, so to finish him off, he breaks the Holy Grail (which results in a chain reaction slowly building up inside) and throws it into Ugh-Zan's mouth, which blows up inside his head and kills him. With Mental's last line of defense now gone, Sam and the team bring out a missile vehicle with an ICBM warhead being carried by it. They find Brand and strap him to the missile shortly before launching it into the portal. The warhead flies straight through the portal and detonates, destroying the portal for good.

Aftermath

With all known portals destroyed, this prevented Mental from being able to take over Earth completely. The fate of any survivors of Mental's forces is left unclear, though they were likely hunted down and killed by the EDF.

Unfortunately for the EDF, their victory was short-lived, as their beloved planet would soon be invaded again the following year . Thus the victory had Delayed the inevitable.
