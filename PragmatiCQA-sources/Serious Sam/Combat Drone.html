The Combat Drone is a small and fully automatic Octanian drone equipped with laser turrets. They are featured as common enemies in Serious Sam 4 and Serious Sam: Siberian Mayhem .

Overview

The combat drone is an automatic aerial vehicle used by Octanian troops. While they are capable of some autonomous behavior, combat drones are relatively crude and require guidance, which they receive from ground-based drone control hubs. During the Tunguska Offensive , they were deployed in swarms to help ensure aerial superiority during the war.

Appearances

Combat drones first appear in a large group if the player decides to help the Italian Resistance in the side quest in Death from Below , and then in large swarms throughout the game. They become rarer in the later levels. They are sometimes mounted by Octanian troops; the drones will then land on the troops on rooftops or to other places where they are needed if there are any buildings in the vicinity.

In the final level, From Earth With Love , a large swarm of drones, some with Octanian Gamma Troopers mounted on them, will fire at the player to prevent them from destroying the metal rods attached to Ugh-Zan VI .

Behavior and skills

The combat drone behaves very similar to the Drone from Serious Sam VR: The Last Hope , but it fires three red projectiles instead of one. Once it fires at the player, it will fly to another spot and do it again. These projectiles are easy to avoid, do not track the player and are fairly weak. On its own, a single drone is a minimal threat, but swarms of them can become a nuisance if they are not eliminated quickly.

Tactics

  • The drone's projectiles can be avoided so long as the player is constantly strafing in one direction.
  • While a single drone is barely a threat, they can be harder to deal with in larger numbers.
  • The XM214-A Minigun is not recommended against drones, as they are easy to destroy and it can be considered a waste of ammo. The weapon should only really be used if the player is being overwhelmed by them or other enemies or if there is a large swarm of drones present.
  • As they have low health, one close to medium-range blast from the 12 Gauge Pump-Action Shotgun or Double Barrel Coach Gun will destroy it.
  • For groups of drones, the Autoshotgun , M29 Infantry Assault Rifle or MK III Grenade Launcher can quickly elliminate them. If the player is accurate enough, a well-timed C-4 Demolition Charge can destroy multiple drones at once.
  • Drones are a low-priority target due to their health and low damage output, but they can become annoying and even dangerous if the player is playing on high diffculties or if they are focusing on other enemies.
