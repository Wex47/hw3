Serious Eight (or Serious 8 ) is a DLC pack for Serious Sam HD: The Second Encounter . It was released on July 2011.

Overview

Serious Eight is a DLC pack that contains eight characters for multiplayer (co-op and versus) in Serious Sam HD: The Second Encounter . When downloaded, it enables the player to select these models in the “multiplayer model” screen.

The Serious Eight DLC was only available during the Steam Summer 2011 event. In this event, one could collect a ticket for getting an achievement in a certain game. With three tickets, one could get the Serious Eight DLC, among many other items. After numerous fan requests, the DLC was released and can be purchased for $4.99, or £3.99 in the United Kingdom.

Characters

  • Canned Cain
  • Cobalt Cinderella
  • Dark Delilah
  • Green Greta
  • Meesterious Mia
  • Silver Sarah
  • White Whytee
  • Ugh-Zan III

Behind the scenes

  • Several of the models, such as Canned Cain and Cobalt Cinderella, can be found in Serious Sam HD: The Second Encounter 's model files. This suggests that the models were supposed to be in the game from the very start, but were removed for an unknown reason.
  • The Ugh-Zan III model was originally given to people that had pre-ordered Serious Sam HD: The Second Encounter .
