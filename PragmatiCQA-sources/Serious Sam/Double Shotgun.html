The Double Shotgun is a double-barreled shotgun that appears in Serious Sam 2 . It is the successor to the Double Barrel Coach Gun from the other mainline games.

Overview

The double shotgun fires both barrels at once when the fire key is pressed. After the gun has been fired, there will be a brief delay between shots (slightly longer than the Auto Shotgun 's), then the gun will be able to fire again.

The double shotgun has a surprisingly tight spread, making it useful at up to medium range. However, the actual spread is more horizontal than compared to the auto shotgun's spread.

Appearances

The double shotgun first appears in Riverdance . It is then featured in the first or second levels in subsequent episodes.

A blunderbuss version of the double shotgun exists, which is used by Flying Kleers .

Tactics

  • The double shotgun is quite powerful against medium-strength enemies, such as small Bone Snakes and Levitators . It takes two to three shots to finish off most medium-strength enemies with the double shotgun.
  • Because of its tight spread, this weapon can be used at up to medium range. However, it should only be used against medium or large-sized enemies because of the spread. Otherwise, at least a few pellets will miss every shot.
  • Its short reload time makes it a good rapid-fire weapon against medium-strength enemies like Kleer Skeletons and Martial Arts Zombies .

Behind the scenes

  • The double shotgun had two different models in the earlier versions of the game, one resembling a blunderbuss (similar to the model used by Flying Kleers) and another with a display with numbers on it.
