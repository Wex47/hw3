Dancing Denzell is a playable multiplayer character who makes an appearance in many Serious Sam games.

Biography

Dancing Denzell, along with his brother Groovy Gregory , formed the popular “Venilli Manilli” band back in the 20th century. However, their fame ended when it was discovered that Serious Sam was the one who sang their songs and that they were just lip-syncing. Even though their career was over, Sam liked them and their style, so he allowed them to fight with him against Mental 's forces.

Appearance

Dancing Denzell is a black man with a large afro in a tacky yellow (pink in Serious Sam 1 , green in Serious Sam 2 ) 1970s disco outfit, complete with stars on the bottom of his feet. In Serious Sam HD , Serious Sam VR and Serious Sam 3: BFE .
