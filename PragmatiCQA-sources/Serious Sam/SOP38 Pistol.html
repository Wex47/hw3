The SOP38 Pistol is a semi-automatic handgun and the standard sidearm used by the Earth Defense Force , despite having been superseded by newer models. It is one of the first weapons the player spawns with in a new game in Serious Sam 3 VR: BFE and Serious Sam 4 .

Overview

The SOP38 is a special-issue model of Special Operations Pistol from 2038 that has proven its combat efficency on numerous battlefields across the universe. Although the SOP38 is no longer in production, its high reliability in adverse conditions guarantees its ongoing popularity with EDF soldiers.

The SOP38 fires one round per shot. It holds 10 rounds in its magazine and has to be reloaded after expending the magazine, but has unlimited ammunition. The player is able to use its iron sights to fire more accurate shots. With a very efficient semi-automatic mechanism, the pistol's ROF is virtually only limited by the speed of how fast the fire key is pressed. The .45 ACP rounds are decent enough for last-resort defense or elimination of weak enemies, but its small magazine capacity precludes its use in massive fights. The pistol's firepower is equal to that of the Schofield .45 and Colt Anaconda , as it takes two shots from the pistol to kill a Beheaded Kamikaze or a Beheaded Rocketeer .

Appearances

Sam first acquires the pistol in the first level of the Serious Sam 3: BFE campaign, Summer in Cairo , next to a fallen EDF soldier. Later in the campaign, Sam reacquires the pistol again in The Lost Temples of Nubia . The pistol makes an appearance in the first level of Jewel of the Nile , Gathering of the Gods , where it can be found next to an office. A second pistol is located inside a small building.

In Serious Sam 3 VR: BFE and Serious Sam VR: The Last Hope , the pistol is dual-wielded and equipped with laser sights. It also doesn't have to be reloaded.

In Serious Sam 4 , the pistol, along with the combat knife , are the first weapons the player starts with in the campaign or in a cooperative game.

Tactics

Serious Sam 3: BFE

  • Iron sights are needed for long-range shots. Otherwise, the bullet will most likely miss. In Serious Sam Fusion , the pistol's spread has been improved, allowing for more accurate hip-firing.
  • The pistol's low recoil makes it relatively easy to quickly fire the gun while retaining some accuracy past short range, though the bullet spread means that they won't always hit a target. Instead, one or two shots should be fired at a time to hit enemies at medium to long range.
  • It is only effective against very weak enemies such as Beheaded Kamikazes , Beheaded Rocketeers , Cloned Soldiers , and Hatchling Antaresian Spiders . It is also effective on Gnaars , but it takes almost an entire magazine to kill one, making it ineffective against groups unless as a last resort.
  • The pistol is effective against close-range Kamikazes. One shot will stun him, giving the player enough room to get far enough away to shoot and kill the Kamikaze while away from its explosion radius.
  • Its infinite ammo makes it useful for when the player is either out of ammo or wants to conserve ammo in levels where ammo is scarce.
  • On Tourist difficulty , it kills Kamikazes, Hatchling Spiders and Rocketeers with one shot, and Cloned Soldiers with two shots.
  • Keeping track of how many shots are necessary to kill an enemy can be very helpful on Serious difficulty . This could allow the player to use the pistol to quickly dispatch an enemy when they are a reasonable distance away, before they become a real threat, and before the player needs to use finite ammo to kill them.

Serious Sam 4

  • Much like in Serious Sam 3 , the pistol is a useful asset against weak enemies like beheaded soldiers.
  • Just like the Serious Sam 3 version, it is best to aim with the iron sights to fire as accurately as possible.
  • When dual-wielded, the pistols become a useful asset and can deal rapid damage to even medium or tough enemies like Common Aludran Reptiloids , female Gnaars, Kleer Skeletons or even Sirian Werebulls . The only downside is their accuracy is greatly reduced, so the player should really use it against enemies from close to medium range. Also, when using the pistols against a Werebull, the player should shoot at their head as much as they can to ensure a quick kill.
  • It works well on other weak targets such as male Gnaars, Octanian Gamma Troopers , Processed Anthropolyps and Zubenhakrabian Spider Nymphs .
  • The weapon is a one-shot kill against Processed or Gamma Troopers if they are shot in the head with the pistol.
  • It is quite effective against the Emetic Anthropolyp , but it takes more than an entire magazine to kill one. Therefore it is best to use it as a last resort or against a single Belcher.

Trivia

  • The pistol appears to be modeled after the Desert Eagle , though it has a larger magazine size than any real-life variation of the Desert Eagle and is chambered to fire .45 ACP rounds.
  • Even though Sam refers to the pistol as ' Smith & Wesson ', an American company that produces revolver-type handguns, the Desert Eagle was not designed by S&W, but the Israel Military Industries and Magnum Research .
  • The pistol's grip has the Serious Sam logo on it.
  • Since SOP38 have infinite ammo supply, it can be assumed that it uses TMAR technology just like the Colts from previous installments, but there's no evidence of that in any documented materials in Serious Sam 3 and Serious Sam 4.
