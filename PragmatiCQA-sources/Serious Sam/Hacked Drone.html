Overview

The hacked drone is a reconfigured Octanian Combat Drone . Its morphological identification of friend-or-foe system has been hacked and its definitions have been swapped so it will attack its own side. Once deployed, it will follow the player and protect them from any nearby enemies, firing at them until its batteries run out. After the drone's timer expires, it will explode.

Appearances

Hacked drones are uncommon gadgets. They are first obtained in A Breakfast in France in the side quest mission that involves hacking the Propaganda Drones . Four of them can be acquired once the player completes the side quest. They can occasionally be found in secrets in the later levels.

Tactics

  • Hacked drones are best used in situations such as when the player is low on ammo for most of their weapons or if they are struggling to fight off a large horde of enemies.
  • As hacked drones don't last very long, another drone should be deployed as soon as the previous one explodes if the player is planning on using many of them.

Trivia

  • The hacked drone fires green lasers instead of red ones like the normal Octanian drones do.
