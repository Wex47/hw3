The Arachnoid Chaingun is a rapid-firing automatic weapon used by Arachnoids .

  • 1 Overview
  • 3 List of appearances

Overview

This chaingun is the primary weapon used by all types of Arachnoids. It is highly accurate and can deal rapid damage to the target. The weapon has impressive range, accuracy, and low spread, allowing the Arachnoid to fire at an enemy from a distance without missing, even on a moving target. Chainguns are fitted with a techno-magical ammunition replenisher , which gives the gun unlimited ammunition. Despite these advantages, the weapon is susceptible to overheating due to its high fire rate.

The chaingun is used by both juvenile and adult Arachnoids in Serious Sam 1 and the HD and VR remakes, and both Adult Arachnoids and Hatchling Arachnoids in Serious Sam 3: BFE , Serious Sam 3 VR: BFE and Serious Sam 4 . In Serious Sam 3 . Adult Arachnoids use a slightly different variation of the chaingun, which are built into their pincers. When they spot a target, they will open their pincers and fire the chainguns simultaneously.

For balancing purposes, the player cannot pick up or use the chaingun, as it is not scripted to be used by the player.
