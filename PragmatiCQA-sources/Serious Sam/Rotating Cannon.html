The Rotating Cannon is an autonomous artillery weapon that appears in Serious Sam: The Second Encounter and various remakes of the game. Unlike the Static Cannon , which does not move, the rotating cannon can rotate 180 degrees on its base.

Overview

The rotating cannon is a medieval cannon attached to a rotating wooden base. Mental was amazed with the concept of Sam 's most powerful weapon and thus tried to create something similar; he enhanced an ordinary medieval cannon with an auto-loading system, radar and ammunition replenisher, making it an effective and dangerous stand-alone defense unit.

Application

The static cannon is often positioned in faraway places such as on the top of buildings or walls. As the cannonballs fired from the cannons travel great distances, this can pose a threat to anyone caught in its line of fire and make them a deadly 'sniping' weapon. This can also make the cannons difficult to find unless the player has the RAPTOR Sniper Rifle equipped so they can take them out safely from medium to far range.

Like the static cannon, the rotating cannon is fairly rare, and only appears in Land of the Damned and The Grand Cathedral , where they are often placed in far away places, such as on the top of buildings or walls. They are often placed in pairs of three, though more are encountered on higher difficulties.

Operation

If the cannon detects the player while it is spinning around, it will fire a fast cannonball at them; the cannonballs can deal a lot of damage to an unsuspecting player, and can easily take away a lot of health and/or armor or kill and gib the player if the cannon is not destroyed soon enough. In Serious Sam HD , the cannonballs do 75 base damage to the player (50 from the direct impact + 25 from splash damage) instead.

Tactics

  • As with the static cannon, the RAPTOR is the best anti-rotating cannon weapon, as it will destroy it in a single shot if it is shot while zoomed. This is the ultimate weapon and should always be used against cannons whenever possible.
  • If the player is low on sniper ammo, they can also use the XL2 Lasergun , XPML21 Rocket Launcher or XM214-A Minigun against it.
  • Rotating cannons aren't always encountered alone, so the player should expect to fight more when they see a cannonball whizzing past them.
  • If the player is playing on Classics Revolution and has found the secret CDF-ST-005 Plasmathrower in The Citadel , they can use the weapon's alternate fire mode on the cannon from a distance; one shot is enough to take it out.
  • The rotating cannon should be a high-priority target and destroyed as quickly as possible. Its cannonballs can deal a huge amount of damage and easily kill the player on higher difficulty settings, and their rotating mechanism makes it easier for them to detect and shoot at the player. The rotating cannon makes it harder to avoid other enemy projectiles, as the player needs to juggle avoiding the rotating cannon's line of sight and its projectiles.
