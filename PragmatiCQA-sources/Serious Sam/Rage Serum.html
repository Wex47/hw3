Rage Serum , or Serious Rage , is a gadget featured in Serious Sam 4 .

Overview

The rage serum is an ergogenic compound contained within a drinking bottle. When consumed, it allows the user to enter a state of euphoric rage, which makes them stronger, faster, more agile, more perceptive, and more resistant to damage, functioning like the combination of Serious Damage and Serious Speed from Serious Sam: The Second Encounter .

According to NETRICSA , it is claimed that overuse of rage serum may cause sterility. This, of course, is entirely unfounded, as this issue has never been researched within the Earth Defense Force .

Appearances

Rage serum is not as common as other gadgets, such as L.I.F.E. , and is mostly found in secrets or after completing side quests. The player can first obtain after completing the first side quest in Four Horsemen of the Apocalypse , where two of them are kept inside a locked supply cache.

Tactics

  • Rage serum is best reserved before a big fight begins, especially when large swarms of common enemies or groups of tough enemies are present. It's especially useful in boss fights, since it shreds through their health in seconds when combined with dual-wielding weapons like the Autoshotgun .
  • Complete side quests and find as many secrets as possible to ensure the chances of obtaining multiple rage serums.

Trivia

  • Rage serum usage rumored to cause sterility is likely a reference to the side effects caused by taking anabolic steroids .
