Plot

Sam is teleported back in time to Ancient Egypt to investigate a strange beacon that has been discovered. However, Sam soon stumbles upon Mental 's forces, which makes NETRICSA a bit suspicious, as she believes that Sam has already defeated Mental. It turns out later that it was actually a farce; Sam took a vacation and shut down NETRICSA, then reactivated her when he was done, saying he had already killed Mental. Sam eliminates the enemies that are in his way, and enters a pyramid to investigate.

Secrets

  1. As soon as you start the level, go left and you will find a Super Armor .
  2. Still in the secret location, you can see some semi-invisible walls. Jump on all of them to find an Extra Large Health .
  3. In the same area, continue moving forward and you will be in an empty room. This will register a 'Shai-Hulud' secret.
  4. Before you will exit the secret area, you will see more semi-invisible walls above you. Jump on them until you will be in a room that contains a few Small Health and a Gunstack Connector . Note that in XXL , picking up the gunstack connector will cause two Kleer Skeletons to spawn.
  5. When you enter the area where you need to stand on a switch in order to open a door, you will see a platform that you can jump on. Go forwards until you will be in a small area that contains an extra large health. Pick up the health to spawn a Kleer Skeleton, then jump on its head in order to find a Boston cream doughnut. You can also get this secret by using the Jump Pad if you have it.
  6. After you kill the first 2 Chimputees that appear in the level, go back to the area where a Beetle appears. Another group of Chimputees will appear. Jump on each Chimputee until you will be in a secret area that contains another gunstack connector.
  7. When you reach the area that needs another switch to open a door, go forwards and you will find a secret 'useless machine'.
  8. You will need the jump pad in order to get this secret. After you obtain 2 gunstack connectors and kill all the enemies in the room, wall jump and you will register a secret 'rooftop rumble'. It is possible to get this secret by jumping on a Bio-mechanoid's head, through it can also be registered by using the jump pad.
  9. Before you end the level, you will see a wall above you. Reach onto it and jump onto another wall to register a secret 'monkey dash'.
  • In Serious Sam: Double D: XXL , there are two crates that appear in the level. Shooting them will open the crate which will reward you with a few Cerebral Controllers . The first one is at the very top platform not far from where you get the Thompson submachine gun . The second one is on a semi-invisible platform. To get to it requires jumping to the left of the first crate until you come across the crate floating in mid-air.
