Overview

The H.E.A.R.T. is a portable automated external defibrillator that can instantly revive a soldier in combat. It appears as a small rectangular prism with rounded edges, some buttons with an unknown purpose, and the icon of a heart and a lightning bolt. When a soldier is near death, the H.E.A.R.T. activates and acts as a defibrillator, restarting the heart and allowing the soldier to keep on fighting.

If the player is equipped with the gadget, when they die, the player is given 10 seconds to choose whether to activate the H.E.A.R.T. or reload from the last save point. If they choose to use the H.E.A.R.T., Sam slowly reaches towards one of the buttons on the gadget which activates it. The player's health is then restored to 100.

The shock the H.E.A.R.T. does to restart the heart is said to be "painful, but pain is better than death".

Appearances

H.E.A.R.T. is one of the rarest gadgets in the game, and appears exclusively as a side quest and in secrets. It first appears in a side quest in One for the Road , and then is found occasionally in secrets and side quests in the later levels.

Tactics

  • As the H.E.A.R.T. is a rare gadget, it should be used extremely sparingly. If the player dies due to a mistake on their part, it is recommended to reload to the last save instead of wasting the gadget.

Trivia

  • The fact the player can choose whether to activate the gadget or not when already dead is most likely a gameplay decision, and its assumed other EDF soldiers' H.E.A.R.T. activates automatically when near death.
  • H.E.A.R.T. is called "extralife" in the game files.
