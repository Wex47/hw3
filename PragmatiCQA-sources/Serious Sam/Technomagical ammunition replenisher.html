The techno-magical ammunition replenisher , sometimes abbreviated as TMAR is a device mentioned in the Serious Sam series .

Overview

The ammunition replenisher is a device that was created by Mental . It is powered by both technology and magic, and provides a weapon with unlimited ammunition, no matter what type of ammo it uses.

It is used primarily by Mental's forces to ensure that they never run out of ammo on the battlefield. The only enemies in the series that are known to use them are Arachnoids (including Adult Arachnoids and Hatchling Arachnoids ) and Beheaded Rocketeers . Serious Sam also uses one for his Schofield .45s , which he salvaged from Mental's forces.

As the ASU 7 Automatic Sentry Unit and SOP38 Pistol have infinite ammo, it can be assumed that they use an ammunition replenisher as well.
