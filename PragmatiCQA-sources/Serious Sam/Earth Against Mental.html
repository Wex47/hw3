Earth Against Mental , or EAM , is an organization and resistance force being led by Sam Stone against Mental and his army .

Overview

Earth Against Mental is a small organization or a small band of fighters who want to fight back for humanity against the evil overlord Mental . Most, if not all of its members are from Earth and are humans, though a few members are humanoids or other species, cyborgs or have cybernetic parts.

EAM was firstly mentioned in Serious Sam 2 and after that, was never mentioned. All multiplayer characters are part of it, except for Mental Mate . Nothing else is known about this organization by now.
