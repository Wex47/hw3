Overview

The Aludran Microhopper is a medium-sized green tripedal amphibious creature originating from the Aludran System of the Canis Major constellation. Its most noticeable feature is the Mental's Horde symbol on its head, which appears to have been painted on.

Despite their similarity to the Marsh Hoppers of the planet Rigil Kentaurus , research conducted at the Gnosis Alpha Orbital University has conclusively demonstrated that Aludran Microhoppers are actually distant relatives of the Aludran Reptiloid .

Appearances

Microhoppers can appear in large numbers in the levels that they appear in, and can pose a great threat to the player when they are encountered.

Behavior and skills

Microhoppers, like amphibians found on Earth , are cold-blooded, and are used to the harsh, hot environments of their home planet, thus they rely heavily on external heat sources. Microhoppers enter a state of hibernation during periods of strong cold and can spend the whole winter season asleep. Any interruption of their hibernation cycle makes them extremely hostile, a tendency exacerbated by their highly-developed territorial instincts. Upon spotting a target, a MicroHopper will leap at them, attacking them by spitting toxic acid from a distance. At closer range, they can use their strong limbs to attack the target. They can also jump side-to-side to dodge the player's attacks.

Tactics

  • An excellent solution for killing large groups of Microhoppers is to use explosive weapons such as the A-24 Devastator , C-4 Demolition Charge , or XPML21 Rocket Launcher .
  • The Autoshotgun is also a decent choice for killing groups of Microhoppers, although the player is vulnerable during reloads.
  • The XM214-A Minigun , XPMR "Burner" Raygun , and XL2 Lasergun are also useful for killing hordes of Microhoppers. The Burner can especially take out a lot of Microhoppers due to its explosive effect.
  • As a last resort, the FRPCL can be used against Microhoppers, although it can only be used either via cheats or by playing the moddable version of the game where the FRPCL can be inserted into custom maps.

Behind the scenes

  • Internally known as the Hopper, this enemy was originally created for Serious Sam 4 before it was cut.

Trivia

  • The Microhopper is similar in behavior and appearance to the Lizard and the Marsh Hopper, albeit with an added acid projectile attack.
    • The Microhopper boss fight in the Alpha and Omega sidequest in Roadside Picnic additionally reuses the latter's music "Fight 05 (Marshfreakinhoppers)", originally used in the Marsh Hopper boss fight in the Serious Sam: The First Encounter level Oasis .
