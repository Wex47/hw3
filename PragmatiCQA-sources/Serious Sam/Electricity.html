Electricity is a type of ammunition that supplies ammo for the Beam Gun , The Shaft , XL2 Lasergun , XL4 Lasergun , and XPMR "Burner" Raygun .

Overview

Electricity is an ammo pickup that gives 50 cells on Normal, Hard, and Mental difficulties, and 100 on Tourist, Easy, Serious, and I Am Ugh-Zan difficulties.

Appearances

Electricity is somewhat common in most games, though it usually appears before a big fight begins, and after obtaining the lasergun, which first appears in Dunes . In The Second Encounter , electricity first appears in The Pit . In the Persepolis levels, it appears in the Courtyards of Gilgamesh (or The Elephant Atrium via a secret ammo pack ), and in the Medieval levels, the Land of the Damned .

In Serious Sam: Double D , electricity is very rare and only appears in the Pompeii levels.

In Serious Sam 3: BFE , electricity is scarce, appearing only in secrets, as the lasergun can only be found by secrets. In Jewel of the Nile however, electricity is more common.

In Serious Sam's Bogus Detour , electricity first appears in Illogical Biological , though it can be obtained in Serious Sabotage as a secret.

In Serious Sam 4 , electricity appears along with the lasergun in In Carcassonne , becoming slightly more common in the later portions of the game.

In Serious Sam: Siberian Mayhem , electricity is now used for the Burner, commonly appearing in the later stages of the game. It also has an orange glow, to distinguish from the XL2's green energy projectiles.

Behind the scenes

  • A model for electricity appeared in Test 1 , but wasn't actually used. It was darker when compared to its final version. It was supposed to be used for the Ghostbuster as it shares the same color as the Ghostbuster, blue-green). The Ghostbuster was supposed to fire an electric beam.

Trivia

  • The Serious Sam 3 model for electricity has a radioactive warning on it, suggesting that it is made out of a highly radioactive substance. Despite this, Sam doesn't seem to be affected by the radiation.
