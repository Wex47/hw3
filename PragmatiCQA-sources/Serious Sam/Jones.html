Jones was a soldier in the Earth Defense Force , appearing as a minor character in Serious Sam 3: BFE and Serious Sam 3 VR: BFE .

Biography

Jones was born in Beautmont, sometime before his younger brother, Thomas Jones . During the Mental War , he joined the Earth Defense Force and met up with Hellfire , Rodriguez and Sam .

Appearance

Jones was an African-American man with shaved black hair. He wore the standard EDF military outfit and black shades.

Appearances

Serious Sam 3: BFE

Jones, along with an unidentified EDF soldier, Rodriguez and Sam , are sent to extract Dr. Hermann Stein from a museum in Cairo . He is skeptical of professor Stein's apparent information and research to help power the Time-Lock to aid in killing Mental in the past, opting for a more head-to-head approach. When Team Alpha's helicopter gets shot down, Jones and Rodriguez survive the crash and try to fend off against Mental's Horde as long as they can. Jones calls Sam on Rodriguez's comm-link, but he is cut short after a Beheaded Kamikaze explodes into him, killing him in the process.

Relationships

Dwayne Rodriguez

Not much is known about his relations with Rodriguez, though it can be assured they got on with each other. He covered for Rodriguez when he was busy fighting off Mental's forces after their helicopter crashed.

Sam Stone

Jones and Sam got along, though Jones disapproved of Sam's approach to handling the situation at the museum, saying that he would rather kill aliens than bother with research.

Personality

Jones had a rather skeptical personality. He didn't believe in professor Stein's apparent information and research to help power the Time-Lock to aid in killing Mental in the past, opting for a more head-to-head approach to fighting the alien threat.

Trivia

  • Jones, along with Hellfire and Rodriguez, can be selected as the player's character model in Serious Sam 3 co-op. If the player bought Serious Sam Fusion , he can be played in Serious Sam HD and Serious Sam VR .
  • His ghost appears as a secret in The Silent Riddler , in the hidden chamber of the Great Pyramid , where the player can also find the Devastator.
  • He was one of the shortest-lived characters in the series, along with Miller .
  • He does not appear in Serious Sam 4 . Although a character who also happens named Jones appears in that game, it is actually his twin-brother.
