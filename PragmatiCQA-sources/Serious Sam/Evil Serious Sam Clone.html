The Evil Serious Sam Clone is the main antagonist of Serious Sam: Next Encounter .

Overview

The Evil Serious Sam Clone is, as his name implies, a "bad" version of Serious Sam . He was in the hands of Mental until he sneaked into Mental's Time-Lock which Mental did not permit him to enter. Though Mental is Serious Sam 's ultimate nemesis, other than his enemy creations and the first cutscene, he is not involved in the game at all.

He appears to be causing mischief in many different cutscenes throughout the game, however, he doesn't have any major occurrences until the end of the game; in which Sam finds him taking control of the Sirian Darklord . After this, Sam sends his evil clone alongside him back to the scientists. Though a glitch in the machine causes more evil Sam clones to spawn and be released in Atlantis. Sam's evil clone has no appearance from this game forth.
