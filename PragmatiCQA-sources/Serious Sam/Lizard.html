Overview

The Lizard is a strange reptilian creature that resembles a dinosaur, with sharp teeth and a long tongue. The origin of these creatures is not fully understood, but it's believed that any planet rich in oxygen, water and plant life is home to Lizards or creatures with the same characteristics as them.

In Serious Sam 2 , Lizards are encountered on various planets throughout the game, usually involving surroundings of water. They are most often found swarming in packs and spawn from eggs that are thrown from sewer pipes or fall from trees. Sometimes they can also be hiding in crates. There are smaller variants of this type, however there are no major differences other than its speed and height. The first appearance of the Lizard is in Riverdance .

In Serious Sam: Tormental , Lizards appear in Mental Woods . They almost always appear in groups and, along with Gonks , are the most frequently occuring enemies in the level. A larger and tougher version of the Lizard, called the Rowtusk, has a chance to appear in the Mental totem fights in the same level.

Behavior and skills

The Lizard's only attack is to run up to the target and bite them when they are close enough. They are fast and can easily catch up with the player, but they can be taken out from a distance as they do not have any ranged attacks. The Rowtusk variant can deal a lot more damage than the normal Lizard.

Tactics

Serious Sam 2

  • Since they only have a melee attack, simply stay away from them and kill them from a distance.
  • The Auto Shotgun is the more proper approach to these enemies. Even at a distance it can still do a fair amount of damage to these creatures.
  • If the player has a choice between killing a Lizard or a larger threat, they should definitely target the Lizards first, but as fast as possible.

Serious Sam: Tormental

  • Lizards are weak enemies and can easily be taken out with the Pistol from medium to close range.
  • Another great weapon to use against Lizards is the Sledgehammer . One or two hits from it can kill them.
  • Serious weapons such as the Shotgun are also effective against Lizards.

Trivia

  • The Marsh Hopper in Serious Sam: Double D reuses the Lizard's sounds.
    • Interestingly, both enemies are named "Gizmo" in the game files.
