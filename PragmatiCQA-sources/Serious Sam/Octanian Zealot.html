The Octanian Zealot is a heavy Octanian soldier that has been bred for iconoclasm, and wields a large hammer that he uses as his main form of attack. He is introduced as an enemy in Serious Sam 4 .

A tougher version of this enemy, the Octanian Legionnaire , debuted in Serious Sam: Siberian Mayhem .

Overview

The Octanian Zealot is a large humanoid creature equipped with medieval-style armor that covers most of his body except for his back. His face is covered in a mask that reveals two small glowing eyes.

Having been trained from youth, Octanian Zealots wield a mighty hammer blessed by the Octifex Maximus himself, and are devoted to smashing all that offends Mental . When they are not smashing things, they self-flagellate to punish themselves for not smashing enough things.

Appearances

Octanian Zealots first appear in Four Horsemen of the Apocalypse . Later on, they usually appear alongside weak enemies, sometimes in medium to large groups.

Application

Zealots serve as heavy assault units due to their endurance and frightening speed, with the intention of charging straight into the enemy and smashing them to pieces with their sledgehammer. Depending on the threat, Mental will send a few out at a time or spawn in multiple Zealots at once.

Behavior and skills

The Zealot's behavior is similar to both Cucurbito the Pumpkin and the Porcine Berserker , in that he runs towards the player and smashes the ground with his hammer, which deals moderate damage. The Zealot has a 'charge-up' period each time he attacks with his hammer. At closer range, he will whack the player with his hammer, which deals twice as much damage.

The Zealot is covered in heavy armor to protect his front and flanks when being attacked. His back, however, is exposed, so the player can easily defeat him from behind.

Tactics

  • Sidestep to avoid his hammer blows, then attack his exposed back with strong weapons to finish him off.
  • The Double Barrel Coach Gun can kill a Zealot in one shot at close range if all the pellets connect when shooting at his back.
  • Though it is recommended to go for his back, this makes it impractical against groups, as this leaves the player vulnerable to their attacks and the player won't be able to predict where each Zealot is coming from at once.
  • Several Zealots can easily be taken out with the C-4 Demolition Charge , MK III Grenade Launcher , or XPML21 Rocket Launcher . It only takes two grenades or rockets to kill one.
  • The A-24 Devastator and TCC2 "Perun" Crossbow are perfect anti-Zealot weapons. They can kill Zealots with a single shot (unlike other explosive weapons, which deal only half of their maximum damage) and can even penetrate large groups of them if they are lined up.
  • The Zealot's armor isn't immune to the XL2 Lasergun and XPMR "Burner" Raygun , making them the only effective rapid-firing weapons to use against him while he charges towards the player. The Burner can even overheat a Zealot to the point of him exploding, severely damaging nearby enemies as well.
  • Against massive groups of Zealots, the SBC Cannon , FRPCL , and Burner are highly effective.
