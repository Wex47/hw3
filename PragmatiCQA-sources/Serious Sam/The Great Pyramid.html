The Great Pyramid is the final level of Serious Sam: The First Encounter , Serious Sam HD: The First Encounter , and Serious Sam VR: The First Encounter , the thirteenth level of Serious Sam Classics: Revolution , and the fourteenth level of Serious Sam: Xbox .

Plot

Sam has finally reached the Great Pyramid . He must clear his way to the pyramid, get inside, and get beamed up by the Sirian spaceship, the SSS Centerprice . But, there is an unexpected surprise waiting for him: Ugh-Zan III . After killing him, Sam gets beamed up on the spaceship, gives a phone call to Mental , taunting him that he's coming to kill him, but it turns out that Mental is not at home right now so he leaves him a voice message, and leaves for Sirius , or so he thinks.

Secrets

  1. ( Serious Sam HD only) In the arena, from the middle tunnel on the left, after the Major Bio-mechanoid bursts through it, enter it to find a Large Health . You don't need to do this the moment they burst through the tunnels, but it's suggested you do it after all the fights in the arena are complete.
  2. ( Serious Sam HD only) Opposite of #1, there's an Extra Small Armor . This will cause 8 Minor Bio-mechanoids to spawn behind you, 2 groups of 4 at a time.
  3. ( Serious Sam HD only) When approaching the pyramid, look to your right, far in the desert. You'll see an Aztec head. There's an Extra Large Health near it, but collecting it will spawn a large number of Beheaded Kamikazes .
