The Gunstack Connector is an item unique to Serious Sam: Double D and Serious Sam: Double D: XXL . It allows the creation of Gunstacks .

Overview

The gunstack connector is a metal pole with two grips on each side. This item lets the player attach one extra gun to a Gunstack . There are 28 gunstack connectors in the game; enough to have almost every weapon connected to two other guns.

Gunstack connectors are quite rare, with most of them appearing in secrets, though a few can be found on the main course of a level. Ones that appear normally are sometimes a sign of a big fight about to begin.

Tactics

  • Gunstack connectors are often hidden in secrets, so the player should go exploring if they want or need more connectors to build a larger or create a new gunstack.
