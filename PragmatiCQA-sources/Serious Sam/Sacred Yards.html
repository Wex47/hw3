Sacred Yards is a secret level in Serious Sam: The First Encounter , Serious Sam: Xbox , Serious Sam HD: The First Encounter , Serious Sam VR: The First Encounter , and Serious Sam Classics: Revolution .

Plot

Sam has entered the Sacred Yards, but it has nothing to do with his mission. He only entered them to fight more monsters.

Secrets

  1. In the area with 'Pinky' (a very small Common Aludran Reptiloid ), shoot a rocket at the pillar/small obelisk in the middle to reveal a secret Large Health . In Serious Sam HD , this secret will not register if you destroy the statue in the previous yard.
  2. When you're in the left area with the sphinx, as you see the platform rising, immediately shoot a rocket at it to blow it up. It will explode, and you'll be able to pick up the sphinx just like that. Be aware that this secret does not work on the Xbox 360 port of Serious Sam HD . As soon as the rocket touches the platform, the game will crash, and the only solution is to reset your console. There is no way around this bug, so this secret must be skipped.
  3. After you hit all the switches in the left area with the sphinx, go behind the platform that has the sphinx on it (or had, if you destroyed it), and use the switch. That will rise a platform which will allow you to pick up the secret Extra Large Health and Extra Large Armor .
  4. ( Serious Sam HD only) in the right area with the sphinx, as you enter, turn left, you'll see a Serious Pack . Pick it up to register the secret.
  5. As you're pushed by the wall into an open area with a pyramid, clear all the enemies, then look for some Croteam Heads coming out from a door. Enter the door and hit the switch to turn the pyramid into a bunch of crates.
  6. Walk behind the pyramid, to its right is an extra large health
  7. Opposite of #6, to the left behind of the pyramid is a serious pack.
  8. Back in the right area with the sphinx, do not hit any switch to make the water level rise. Instead, go to the "middle" platform with the switch, jump on it, and from there jump behind the wall into the sphinx and pick it up. This will not count as a secret if you have raised the water level.
  9. Still in the right area with the sphinx, go to the left platform, and jump on the button, and then on the ledge on what the button is on, and from there, jump onto the ledge sticking out of the wall to get a Super Armor .
  10. Opposite #9, there's an extra large health.
  11. Still in the same area, there's a platform sticking out on the left, near the middle platform. Shoot it with a rocket after you've collected #10. This rises a platform in the opposite position of where the blown up one used to be. Shooting the left platform without first having picked up #10 will make this secret unattainable.

Trivia

  • In the area where the player first arrives at, there is a health pill in the corner to the right. Collecting this pill will show a "not-so-silent-pill has been collected!" message, which will spawn a Major Bio-mechanoid .
    • However, picking it up before any enemies spawn will instead cause it to show a "Silent-walk pill has been activated and enemies deactivated!" message, which prevents all enemies from spawning in the first area. This is also the first step in the special speedrunning secret known as the "Geek Secret".
  • This map can be completed without killing any enemies.
