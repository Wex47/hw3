Overview

The SSS Centerprice was a large, black saucer-shaped spaceship. The ship seemed to be controlled automatically, as a transmitter had to be activated in Egypt for the ship to come to Earth .

History

Several thousand years ago, the Sirians used the Centerprice as one of their main forms of transportation during their interstellar travels. Eventually, they came across the Solar System and discovered Earth. They would use the ship to visit Earth and fly over the Great Pyramid in Egypt whenever the Egyptians took part in their religious rituals.

Soon after the Sirians found out that Mental had attacked Sirius, the survivors took the Centerprice back into space and left it on Saturn's moon, Titan , as a temporary measure. They believed that, at the time, humans were too primitive to learn space travel, so they would leave the ship until humans became technologically advanced enough to find the ship and while Mental laid dormant.

Weaponry

The Centerprice was equipped with a laser cannon, which the ship used against Ugh-Zan III during the fight inside the Great Pyramid . This cannon was capable of killing or damaging certain beings, and magnetize other ones, for example, it could kill Ugh-Zan III but it could also magnetize humans on-board. It was extremely powerful, as demonstrated when it was used on Ugh-Zan, where it could deal a lot of damage to him, despite his colossal size and ability to regenerate.

Appearances

Serious Sam: The First Encounter

In The First Encounter , after Sam activates the transmitter, the SSS Centerprice is shown in a cutscene flying through space and making its approach towards Earth.

Having bought the ship to Earth, Sam waits for the ship to arrive at the Great Pyramid. However, Mental is not going to let Sam slip away so easily, so he sends out one of his Grand Admiral, Ugh-Zan III, to stop him. After Ugh-Zan is killed, Sam departs onto the ship and heads to Sirius .

Serious Sam: The Second Encounter

Just as the Centerprice is about to leave Earth, the ship is hit by something (the original and Serious Sam Fusion intro show a Croteam Crate Bus crashing into it after going into "hypercrate" mode), which deals heavy damage to the ship. With the ship unable to function, it falls back down to Earth and crash-lands in Palenque, Sierra de Chiapas . Though the ship was destroyed, its main computers were still active, as NETRICSA told Sam she was still connected to the ship's database.

What happened to the ship after Sam left the Mayan period is unknown.

Trivia

  • The name of the ship is based on the famous Star Trek ship, the USS Enterprise .
  • Its design is also based on classic alien motherships .
