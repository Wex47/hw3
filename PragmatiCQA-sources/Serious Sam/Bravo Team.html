Bravo Team were a group of soldiers tasked with escorting professor Stein into the Egyptian Museum . They were presumed either KIA or missing during the events of Serious Sam 3: BFE .

Overview

Bravo Team were one of the many squads of soldiers sent into Cairo from the EDF 's headquarters. Their main objective was to protect professor Stein from Mental 's troops while he conducted research on the Time-Lock in the museum. As the EDF was a relatively small military force, they didn't have the resources or the firepower to get take back Cairo from Mental's armies. As a result, the EDF had to deploy a small number of soldiers into the city to avoid being detected.

Appearances

Serious Sam 3: BFE

According to Sam , Bravo Team had lost contact with HQ at around 0900 hours. The EDF sends in Team Alpha, which consists of Jones , Rodriguez , Sam, and another soldier, to reestablish contact with Bravo and bring them back to headquarters. Things don't turn out as planned when Sam's helicopter gets shot down by enemy gunfire as the chopper makes its way into the city.

As Sam is the sole survivor of his team, he has to make his way to the museum through the abandoned streets and rendezvous with Bravo Team on his own. However, by the time makes his way to the museum, it turns out that Bravo have been taken out by Mental's forces before Alpha Team could rescue them.
