Overview

The shotgun is a weapon that uses shells and fires with a tight spread when the fire key is pressed. The shotgun has good damage, spread and decent rate of fire. The pellets fired by the shotgun can also richocet off walls. Two other variants of shotgun exist, called the shotgun L2 and shotgun L3, which can be upgraded if the player vists the NPC shops in the V2 levels after completing a run. These shotguns fire more pellets than the normal shotgun and deal a lot more damage, but have a lower rate of fire.

Appearances

The weapon has a random chance of appearing from a blue Mental chest. It can also be purchased from NPCs in the shops using keys.

Tactics

  • The shotgun is an ideal weapon for quickly taking out weak enemies like Gonks , Hoppers , Jormungandrs or Lizards , especially when they are lined up or clustered together.
  • The shotgun works great for depleting the health of tough foes like Cerebulls , Gunrillas , Helioporas and Red Widows .
  • The upgraded shotguns are very effective against tough enemies and their elite variants in the V2 and V3 levels.
