Plot

Now that Sam has met up with his friends and Father Mikhail , all they need to do is fight through the citadel to reach the church, the location of the Ark of the Covenant and the Holy Grail . Things seem to be going to plan, until a familiar face pays them a visit, and someone else shows up to steal the Grail for their own evil purposes.

Side objectives

Lock and Key

Before going through , on the shack to the right is a note and a set of keys. Interact with it to trigger the side quest. Follow the waypoint marker to the warehouse to find some supplies and two Time Warps .

What the Hellfire

When Sam meets up with Hellfire , she will lead him to the location of the second side quest where the player can get some supplies. Go to the convoy of Humvees which contains a crate. Open up the crate to obtain a Mini-Nuke .
