Normal difficulty is a difficulty setting found in almost all Serious Sam games.

Overview

Normal difficulty is the third level of difficulty in the game series. It is considered the average level of difficulty in every game it appears in. This difficulty is suggested for people with some experience in the Serious Sam games, or generally FPS games.

Advantages

You can rocket jump (except in Serious Sam 2 , Serious Sam 3: BFE and Serious Sam 3 VR: BFE ), as self-damage is enabled, and everything is equal, as neither enemies nor the player is stronger or weaker than what they should've been.

In the Xbox version of Serious Sam 2 , enemies have 25% less health and damage dealt per attack, along with a 10% lower movement speed and 25% lower thinking frequency.

Disadvantages

Health and armor degeneration in Serious Sam: Xbox and Serious Sam: Next Encounter , if your health or armor is above 100, and self damage is enabled.

In Serious Sam 3: BFE , the maximum amount of ammo for most weapons is lower than in easier difficulty settings.
