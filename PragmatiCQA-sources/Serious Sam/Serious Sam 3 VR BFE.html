Gameplay

Serious Sam 3 VR: BFE functions just like Serious Sam 3: BFE , with the same gamemodes, enemies, weapons and levels from the original game.

Many new features have been included, such as Serious Sam Fusion 2017 integration, which means that player can play cross-platform with players on the original version of the game. True locomotion support is also included, such as teleport, instant teleport, blink teleport, and classic full locomotion. Like Serious Sam VR: The First Encounter and Serious Sam VR: The Second Encounter , Serious Sam 3 VR: BFE also includes Steam Workshop support, where people can create their own personalised content or download new player-created content and modifications using Serious Sam Fusion 2017 .

Like the other virtual reality games in the Serious Sam series, the player is able to dual-wield weapons, examples include combining the M29 Infantry Assault Rifle with the RAPTOR Sniper Rifle , or holding C-4 Demolition Charges in one hand and the SBC Cannon in the other. This way, the player can mow down hordes of enemies much faster than the non-virtual reality games. There is an ammunition counter on top of most weapons as a holographic display to let the player know how much ammo they have for that weapon.
