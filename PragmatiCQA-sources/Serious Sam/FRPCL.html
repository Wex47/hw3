Overview

The FRPCL is a rather strange weapon compared to the others seen in Sam 's arsenal. It is versatile and is able to fire projectiles and be used in close-quarters for melee attacks.

When the player aims at the target with the FRPCL, it will lock onto them in a similar manner to the XPML21 Rocket Launcher 's homing rocket upgrade. Pressing the fire key will launch a chainsaw projectile that will track the target, sawing through it and repeatedly draining its health until it dies. Some enemies are gibbed after they are killed by the projectile. The projectile will then seek out any additional targets until the projectile explodes. This projectile deals very high damage in a short space of time, being able to rip tough enemies to shreds in a matter of seconds. If the player holds the secondary fire key, the player can use the weapon to gib enemies up close, in the same manner as the P-LAH Chainsaw from Serious Sam: The Second Encounter .

Unlike the rockets fired by the XPML21 Rocket Launcher , the player does not take any damage from the projectiles fired from the FRPCL.

Appearances

The weapon is first obtained after the player completes the optional side quest in One for the Road at the abandoned farm. Inside the barn, a cutscene will play showing the headless corpse of Fulbert le Fou, who was decapitated by his own invention. The weapon can then be acquired along with ammo for it scattered around the barn. Ammo for the weapon appears sparingly later through the game and sometimes in secrets or acquired from dead enemies if the Looter Shooter skill is active. It is usable all the way up to Anathema Unto God , after which the weapon is permanently removed from the player's inventory for the remainder of the game.

Tactics

  • The FRPCL, aside from the SBC Cannon , is the most powerful weapon in the game. It can destroy and slice up entire hordes of weak enemies or many groups of tough enemies if the player constantly fires the weapon.
  • It is extremely effective against groups of Bio-mechanoids , Kleer Skeletons , Aludran Reptiloids , Khnums , Aludran Microhoppers , and Scrapjacks , especially if the player is low on A-24 Devastator rounds, TCC2 "Perun" Crossbow bolts, grenades, or rockets.
  • The FRPCL's chainsaw is useful for grinding up many weak enemies in a row such as Gnaars , Octanian Gamma Troopers , or Processed Anthropolyps .
  • The FRPCL's chainsaw is also great for blocking enemy projectiles like Reptiloid fireballs and Technopolyp missiles without the player being severely damaged.
  • If used against Lord Achriman , it can deal a lot of damage to him, although the player will need to time and aim their shots such that they actually hit him.

Trivia

  • The FRPCL is the first weapon of its kind in the entire series.
  • The weapon is likely based on an internet meme started by Sergey31.
  • The FRPCL can kill Ugh-Zan VI with only one shot at close range, but only if the player activates cheats and gives themselves all weapons.
  • The weapon appears in Serious Sam: Siberian Mayhem , but it can only be obtained with cheats or by being added into the level with mods, such as the Serious Editor .
