Overview

Cave Demons are a species of monkey-like aliens that inhabit dark and cold caves in the bowels of their home planet located in Beta Aurigae , of the Sirius Supercluster. They have a small body, consisting of only veins and muscles, numerous bony growths on the nape and the line of the spine, a mouth full of sharp teeth and eyes that glow in the dark. During the Mental War , Mental discovered their planet and bought them over to Earth , though the exact reasons for doing this are not clear.

Appearances

Sam first encounters a Cave Demon in an underground chamber in Unearthing the Sun . Later on, Cave Demons are commonly spawned in rooms with lots of pillars or columns, sometimes in small to medium groups. Their main purpose is to harass the player while trying to solve puzzles or distract them from other enemies. They can be considered a nuisance whenever they appear.

Behavior and skills

On their home planet, Cave Demons are known to be bloodthirsty and dangerous predators. Being primarily nocturnal, they are used to hunting their prey in dark environments, and will refrain from open fights. Their sharp claws enable them to cling to walls and ceilings, and wait in ambush until unsuspecting prey is near, and then use their claws to attack their victims. Using their strong hind legs, they jump down on the victim from far away.

Compared to other enemies, the Cave Demon does not charge at the player like most enemies do, but instead lurks on columns, waiting for its opportunity to leap towards the player and attack. If there are no columns around, they will instead latch onto walls or jump onto the roof of buildings. They can leap great distances as well. If the player fights back, they will quickly jump back away and hide in the dark. When they lurk hidden in the dark, only their glowing eyes and monkey-like chatter give away their presence.

Tactics

  • Cave Demons usually don't usually appear with other enemies. When they do, they are low-priority targets. Trying to chase after them leaves the player vulnerable to more dangerous and aggressive enemies.
  • Killig Cave Demons is not necessary, but it is recommended if they are lots of them present in the room and the player needs to solve a puzzle quickly.
  • If the player is quick enough, the Double Barrel Coach Gun can be used to gib a Cave Demon at close-range. However, as they are quick, time and accuracy is needed to take them down with this weapon. Also, due to the gun's high spread and long reload time, it isn't very efficient against groups and it makes for a poor choice at medium range.
  • The Sirian Mutilator is an ideal weapon against a Cave Demon or small groups of them; the player should wait for the Cave Demon to leap out at them, then immediately step sideways and use the Mutilator on it. The beam will stun it for as long as it has been lassoed by the beam. It can be an effective way of quickly dispatching Cave Demons without having to waste ammo when attempting to shoot them.
  • When a Cave Demon jumps at the player, they should shoot it quickly before it gets the chance to hide away.
  • Due to their low health, they can be killed easily with the 12 Gauge Pump-Action Shotgun or M29 Infantry Assault Rifle ; their health is low enough to be taken out at close to medium range with the shotgun, so as long as most pellets connect.
  • The SOP38 Pistol can be used to take out individual Cave Demons that are clinging onto walls, though because it takes many shots to kill one, this makes the gun ineffective for groups.
  • Using C-4 Demolition Charges to kill multiple Cave Demons that are hiding behind columns can be a bit of a gamble, as they may leap to another column or onto a wall just as soon as the C-4 explodes.
  • Their loud shrieks are a good indicator when they are about to leap at the player. An experienced player can kill the Demon in mid-air or avoid it by strafing or moving backwards.

Trivia

  • Due to their complex AI, the Cave Demon will sometimes glitch as it attempts to leap onto a pillar or wall, causing them to get stuck.
  • The Aurigan Haemovore , another strange creature that makes up Mental's armies, shares the same home planet as the Cave Demon.
  • The Cave Demon is the only new enemy in Serious Sam 3 that doesn't have a special player obituary message associated with it. If a player dies to a Cave Demon, the resulting obituary message will always read "[player name] is missing in action."
