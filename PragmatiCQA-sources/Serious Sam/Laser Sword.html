The Laser Sword is a weapon that appears in Serious Sam VR: The Last Hope .

Overview

The laser sword is a lightsaber-like weapon with a glowing blue blade. When the player uses the sword, it will unleash a wide blue arc that deals high damage to any enemy in its way, instantly killing weaker enemies and draining the health of tougher opponents. The arcs can also destroy incoming enemy projectiles. Occasionally, the arcs that come from the sword will produce blue crystals. If the sword is used too often, the blades will turn red for a brief amount of time before the weapon can be used again.

Tactics

  • The laser sword is extremely useful for wiping out groups of weak enemies, such as Beheaded Kamikazes , Beheaded Rocketeers , Cloned Soldiers , Drones , Kleer Skeletons , small Lava Golems , and Skeletons . It can also be used to destroy the Lava Golem's projectiles before they hit the player.
  • Against Scythian Witch-Harpies , the laser sword is an excellent weapon for clearing large groups of them out at a time.
  • Despite its effectiveness against weak enemies, it's not recommended to be used against tougher enemies, such as Adult Arachnoids , medium Lava Golems or Major Bio-mechanoids .
