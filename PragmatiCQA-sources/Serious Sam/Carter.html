Daniel Hieronymus Carter , or just Carter , was a member of the Alien Artifact Acquisition team and the Earth Defense Force , where he worked behind the front lines, contributing his "unique talents". He is a supporting character in Serious Sam 4 .

Biography

Carter was born on the Icaria Planum of Mars. Other than that, not much is known about his early life.

Sometime during his life, he became a conspiracy theorist and became obsessed with theories relating to the Illuminati . He participated in a redacted leak of some affair that happened in 2103, after which the government barred him from government service, while he himself also vowed not to work for any government organization. During the Mental War , he accepted a pardon and joined the EDF when his "unique talents" were needed during their struggle against the invaders. One assignment including the Hermann Stein Alien Technologies Survey. He also founded the "Mothership Malware Masquerade" charity hackathon which lasted from 2106 to 2111.

Appearances

Serious Sam 4

Carter is first seen on the bus Hellfire is on when she comes in to extract Father Mikhail , Jones , Kenny , Rodriguez , and Sam . He then appears in the briefing room with the rest of the Alien Artifact Acquisition squad. There, he suggests using the experimental HAARP program to erupt Mount Vesuvius. This would then ground the Octanian Raider fleet flying over Europe and allow the team to safely escort Father Mikhail to the Vatican.

While Sam is placing his beacons, Carter contacts him to tell him that he will be taking Father Mikhail to Rome to find information on the Holy Grail . While in Rome, however, Quinn informs Sam that they lost contact with Carter on his way there. She speculates that making Mount Vesuvius erupt caused earthquakes to hit Rome, which would have forced Carter and Father Mikhail to hide in the catacombs under the Colosseum. Sam goes to Rome and Rodriguez tags along with him to find Carter and Mikhail. Rodriguez succeeds in finding them, and Mikhail assists Sam further in their quest to find the Grail.

After Sam obtains the book from the Vatican and the location of the Grail is revealed, Carter joins his team and boards a plane to the French fortified city of Carcassonne . Their plane is shot down along the way, forcing them to parachute out into the open French countryside. At some point, Carter ends up in Carcassonne along with Mikhail, and they make it to the castle. Sam makes contact with them and soon Hellfire and Rodriguez tag along. They fight their way through tough enemy resistance until they make it to the church. Once they get there, however, Lord Achriman suddenly appears, and they fight him. Sam finishes Achriman off, then the team proceed to the church. Carter plays on an organ in a church to add some atmosphere, but Sam, annoyed, tells him to stop.

The team find the altar inside the church, where Mikhail begins his ritual to activate the mechanism hiding the the Ark of the Covenant. The Ark is revealed, but Brand appears with his men and intends to take the Grail for himself and to bestow it to Mental as a gift. Carter and the rest of his team are incapacitated by Brand, who then orders his men to take Carter, Hellfire and Rodriguez be taken by Harvester Drones so they can be processed inside Octanian Harvester Leviathans .

Once Sam makes it to Tunguska to stop Brand, he joins up with Carter and the others along with hundreds of Earth Defense Force soldiers to stop the threat. They succeed in killing Ugh-Zan VI after Sam tosses the Grail into his mouth. With Ugh-Zan neutralized, Brand is captured by Sam's team and they strap him to an ICBM missile. Carter writes "from Earth with love" on the missile and they send Brand and the missile through the portal, which destroys it. With their mission complete, Carter, Hellfire, Rodriguez and Sam chat over a campfire. Carter tells them that they were able to escape the Harvester, but doesn't give much details about how they got out. He says that the Octanians wanted to process him first because he had a lot of intel. He tells them he misses Father Mikhail, as he enjoyed conversing with him. The team then share a toast to remember their fallen friends. While they are drinking, Carter asks where the EDF will send them for their next mission.

Carter does not make any more appearances in Serious Sam games, so it is not known what happened to him after the events of Serious Sam 4 .

Personality and skills

Carter was intelligent and extremely paranoid. These 2 values combined to make him a conspiracy theorist. The fact he was a conspiracy theorist often hid his natural intelligence with insane theories, such as believing that lizards ruled the world and most of the world leaders are descendants of lizard-people from the planet Sirius . Those theories were sometimes even proven true, such as the theory that HAARP was an 'earthquake machine' used to make the volcano of Eyjafjallajökull erupt in Iceland in 2010 to ground all the air traffic in Europe. He was also an expert hacker, leaking classified government information and being able to hack into NETRICSA 's database.

Relationships

Mikhail Obruchev

He was close to Mikhail, and liked to share his theories with him on all sorts of subjects, to which Mikhail expressed interest in. In fact, Mikhail was the only one who took his theories seriously.

Trivia

  • His middle name is named after Saint Jerome , a Latin priest, confessor, theologian, and historian.
  • Carter knew how to play the organ, as seen in Anathema Unto God .
  • As Carter was born on Mars, he could be a reference to Edgar Rice Burroughs' John Carter of Mars .
