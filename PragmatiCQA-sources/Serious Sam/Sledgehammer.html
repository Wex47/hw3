The Sledgehammer is a melee weapon that appears in Serious Sam 3: BFE , Serious Sam's Bogus Detour , Serious Sam 3 VR: BFE , and Serious Sam: Tormental .

Overview

The sledgehammer can function as both a melee weapon and a tool. Despite being a melee weapon, the amount of attacks the sledgehammer has makes it a versatile choice for clearing multiple weak enemies in groups, making it significantly more useful than the combat knife from previous games. It is also the only weapon the player can use while sprinting at the same time.

In Serious Sam 3 and the VR version of the game, the weapon has three separate attacks: a swift downward swing that, if enemies are close together in front of the player, can kill/damage multiple enemies at once; a horizontal right-to-left swipe that can take out several enemies at a time; and by pressing the reload key, a spinning swing that can clear multiple enemies within close proximity all around the player. Each attack has a varying speed of execution, with the downward and spinning swing taking slightly more time to initiate. When the player is not around enemies, performing swings will take much more time. However when in close proximity to hostiles, the playable character will automatically raise the sledgehammer above their head, making swings much quicker to deliver.

In Serious Sam: Tormental , the player can switch to the sledgehammer by pressing the scroll button key. Bad Bulb uses a weaker version of the sledgehammer called melee bulb. This weapon has a piercing effect that can damage multiple enemies at close range.

There are two varieties of sledgehammers in Serious Sam 3 and Serious Sam 3 VR ; one with a wooden shaft, and one made entirely of metal which is obtained late in the campaign. They are identical in statistics and performance. The wooden one is first obtained in Summer in Cairo , both as a regular weapon and as a secret. The metal one appears in The Lost Temples of Nubia .

In Serious Sam's Bogus Detour , the sledgehammer spawns in one of two locations in the first level, Knee Deep in Sand . The first location is near a beach on the eastern part of the map, and the second spawn is next to a lake opposite a pyramid. It then appears again in Path of Feast/Fear .

In Serious Sam: Tormental , the sledgehammer is one of the default weapons the player starts with after finishing the Tomb stage.

Tactics

Serious Sam 3: BFE

  • The sledgehammer is a brilliant weapon to use against swarms of Hatchling Antaresian Spiders or small groups of Juvenile Antaresian Spiders .
  • The weapon is powerful enough to kill traditional enemies, such as Gnaars and Kleers , in a single strike. Slightly tougher enemies, such as Hatchling Arachnoids and Sirian Werebulls , only require two.
  • It is not very effective against large or tougher enemies, such as Adult Arachnoids , Major Bio-mechanoids or Scrapjacks , especially considering the fact that Scrapjacks have a powerful melee attack that does a lot of damage. Reserve using the sledgehammer on these stronger enemies just to finish them off after weakening them.
  • The weapon shouldn't be used against explosive enemies, such as Beheaded Kamikazes , for obvious reasons.

Serious Sam's Bogus Detour

  • The sledgehammer is a more powerful melee weapon than Fists , but the player still needs to be careful when using it against small or large groups of enemies to avoid being swarmed.
  • It is a particularly effective weapon against melee enemies, such as Gnaars, Infected Fish and Orc Butchers .
  • It can also be effective against Infected Tortoises , provided that the player avoids their bite attack in time.
  • When attacking Infected Tortoises with this weapon, go around them and attack with the sledgehammer at the same to reduce the chances of taking damage from them.
  • It is not recommended to use the sledgehammer against tough enemies like Orc Captains or Scrapjacks due to their high health and dangerous attacks.
  • The sledgehammer is a great weapon for clearing large groups of Crabs , should the player wish to use it against them.

Serious Sam: Tormental

  • The sledgehammer is good to use on weak enemies like Croakueens or Gonks or to whack away tougher enemies like the Cerebull .
