Overview

The Draconian Burner is a large version of the Draconian Pyromaniac with a red colored shirt and a different mask. They are members of the newly-formed Pyronihilistic Order of Ashes, created in the later months of the European campaign to lay waste to the continent. Only the most fanatical Draconians are allowed in the ranks, each swearing an oath to sacrifice their bodies and souls to the Great Flaming Crusade.

Mental , seeing the potential of such a dedicated, elite force, elected to elevate them by implanting dual thermal beam guns into their arms, which are linked to the brain via neural connections. Furthermore, Burners are equipped with a jetpack, which allows them to easily traverse difficult terrain and attack head-on in one-to-one combat.

Appearances

The first Burner encountered by the player appears in The Song Of The Stormbringer as a miniboss. Burners become slightly more common later throughout the game, with many of them appearing in Crime And Punishment .

Behavior and skills

Burners have two main methods of attack; a vertical sweep with their laser flames in the air and a horizontal sweep with their laser flames on the ground. Both can be dodged by sprinting away. At close range, they will do a short jump before slamming into the ground for an explosive burst of flames that functions as their melee attack.

Like the Draconian Pyromaniac , the gas tanks on their backs can be shot to blow them up faster.

Tactics

  • The XPML21 Rocket Launcher is very useful against the Burner early in the game, especially after receiving a multirocket upgrade.
  • Their forward laser attack can be avoided by sidestepping into the opposite direction.
  • Horizontal lasers, however, should be dodged by jumping over them with a perfect timing or by damaging the Burner quickly to make him flinch and cancel the attack.
  • The TCC2 "Perun" Crossbow is very useful for shooting the Burner's tanks. The tanks will explode after two hits, which allows the player to kill him more quickly.
  • Both the C-4 Demolition Charge and SBC Cannon can be used as emergency weapons against Burners. One charged up cannonball or attached C-4 charge will kill them instantly, regardless of the difficulty settings.
  • Burners are high-priority targets when appearing with other enemies, as their attacks can give the player less room to dodge other enemies and deal high damage to them if they are not paying attention to the Burner. Therefore, it's recommended to kill them quickly.
  • Burners can be ridden when the Rodeo skill is active. Their high health and damage output makes them useful for fending off large hordes of enemies without the player being seriously damaged in the process, although their slow ground speed makes them easy targets for Gnaars , Kleer Skeletons , Beheaded Kamikazes , Khnums , and especially Hatchling and Adult Arachnoids . When riding a Burner, the player must fly upwards with the Burner so as to maximize his chances of survival and avoid enemy projectiles.

Trivia

  • The Burner's characteristics are very similar to that of the Lasergewehr -wielding Super Soldiers from Wolfenstein II: The New Colossus , since they also duel-wield beam weapons, have jetpacks, and are similar in height.
  • The Burner's appearance is based on one of the Draconian Pyromaniac 's concept designs.
