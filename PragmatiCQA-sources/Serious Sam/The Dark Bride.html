Plot

After activating the Horus generator , Sam must now face a powerful enemy before he can continue to his next objective to activate the second generator in Luxor .

Secrets

  1. As soon as you get outside, climb up the stairs and run behind the temple you came out of to find a secret Large Armor .
  2. There's a place you can't get to at the pond area with some columns in front of it. Go to the right hand side. Jump around onto the window, and duck to get through. An alternate path is to go to the left hand side onto some stairs. Sprint them up and jump for the window there. The secret on the right side still needs to be triggered though). There's a "secret Hall of developers" with Croteam Heads and an Extra Large Health .
  3. In the area where you fight the Witch-Bride, there are two portables behind a lockup. The portable on the right has a destructible door. Destroy the door to pickup some electricity.
  4. To the right of the exit gate for this area, there's a shed with a health pickup in it. If you climb onto the boxes beside it, you can pick up the secret keys. They're used after the second locked door, where you're facing another door with a "no entry" next to it. Walk through it to collect an Extra Large Armor .
  5. You need the key from secret #4. At the end of the path is a door you can now walk through and collect some minigun ammo.
  6. When you get to the portable where you pick up the keys there's a Scrapjack on the far wall. To his left (your right) indoors is a wall with a slightly darker texture. This wall can be destroyed to reveal some rocket ammo.
  7. In the area after secret #6, where a very large number of Cloned Soldiers spawn, there is a portable with the next keys in it that has a health pickup on top of it. Jump on some rubble next to the portable and then jump towards the health to collect it.
  8. As you enter the area after secret 7, where a multi-wave fight takes place, on the right side of where you enter are some cannonballs.
  9. In the same area as secret #8, on the left side where you enter, behind a tumbled stone is a Small Armor .
  10. In the same area as secrets #8 and #9, on the left side of the exit is some Devastator ammo.
