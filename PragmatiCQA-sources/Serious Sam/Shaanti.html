Overview

Shaanti is a planet that is known to harbor life and is extremely abundant in resources. The planet's biodiversity rivals that of Earth . Recent research undergone on the planet indicates that its inhabitants may still be active, as it was assumed that they went extinct.

Appearances

Serious Sam VR: The Last Hope

Sam , Captain of the Earth Defense Force , is sent to Shaanti after he learns that Mental is conducting experiments on a new super weapon. With the help of professor Hermann Stein , Sam is able to locate the weapon, though he is unable to understand any of the professor's notes.

Sam fights his way through Mental's forces and ends up on some flying islands. There, he finds out that the superweapon is actually a dragon . A fight between them begins, and Sam is able to defeat it before it can wreck havoc.
