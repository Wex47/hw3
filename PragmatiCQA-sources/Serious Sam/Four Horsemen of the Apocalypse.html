Plot

Hellfire extracts Father Mikhail and the rest of the Alien Artifact Acquisition squad out of Rome and takes them to an Earth Defense Force base located near Mount Vesuvius . There, they meet up with General Brand , Quinn and professor Gottlieb Kiesel to discuss how to get to the Vatican Library without being attacked by the Octanian Raiders . Carter proposes using the HAARP, a seismic weapon fired from a satellite, to ground the raider fleet. Brand accepts the idea (though Kiesel doesn't approve) despite knowing how dangerous it is. Once dicussions are over, they have the the team place 8 beacons around the ruins of Pompeii to activate the satellite, while Carter escorts Father Mikhail back into Rome.

Side objectives

Key to Success

Right after leaving the first arena, the player will come across a locked door. Interact with it to activate the side quest which involves finding the key. Inside the depot is some supplies and a crate containing three Rage Serums .

Thin Red Line

In the shack next to the radio, there will be a note about a missing man called Alessandro. Follow the waypoint to his last known location. Finding him will award the player with the grenade launcher upgrade for the 12 Gauge Pump-Action Shotgun .

Amateur Archeology

Progress through the level normally and Dr. Kiesel will contact Sam about retrieving an alien artifact for him. Follow the blue waypoint marker to get to the artifact. Finding it will reward the player with a few Time Warps .

Trivia

  • The name of this level is a reference to the four figures who appear in the Book of Revelation .
  • The achievement "That Belongs In A Museum" is a play on a well-known quote said by Indiana Jones in the 1989 film Indiana Jones and the Last Crusade .
