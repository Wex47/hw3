Overview

The Sirian Great Council are the last of the Sirians after Mental discovered their home and wiped the rest of the species out. The Council has only three members: Jebediah , Rajiv , and Rolanda . Rolanda appears to be the leader of the three, and has a more sensible and patient approach to the objective than her two brothers, Rajiv is very skeptical, and seems to underestimate Sam's abilities, as he doubts that one man can actually take down Mental, and Jebediah is the least intelligent out of the three and has a rather clumsy personality, though like Rolando, he too has high hopes for Sam.

Appearances

Serious Sam 2

The Great Council are heard talking about Sam as they watched him battling Mental's Hordes on Earth in The First Encounter and The Second Encounter . They believe he is their last hope for saving the universe from Mental's evil clutches. Once they are ready, they teleport him in, and tell him that there is actually a way to defeating Mental: a strange object known as the Medallion of Power . They say it was forged by a blind Gromlin blindsmith and split into five pieces; each piece of the medallionwas given to five different species, the Simbas , ChiChe , Zixies , Kleers , and Elvians . Unfortunately for them, Mental found these parts, but as they are indestructible, he could only have his forces guard them. Once all five parts of the medallion have been put together, it will activate and release its power, making Mental vulnerable.

The wizards teleport Sam to the planet M'digbo. There, he fights his way and learns from the Simba Shaman that a massive gorilla-like creature known as Kwongo has taken the piece of the medallion. Sam manages to kill Kwongo, and the wizards, impressed with Sam, teleport him back so he can continue on his mission to obtain the second piece from the planet Magnor . They tell him that is currently in possession of the Zixies, but they warn him that going there will be no easy task, considering the hostile environment. Even worse, Mental has sent in his forces to try and search for the piece of the medallion, and he also had one of his minions to scout the area.

Sam asks why they can't just teleport him to the town, but as they are unable to as they don't know its current location. They suggest that the best place to search for Branchester is near Mental's swamp base. Sam heads there and, with the help of the Zixies, locate the second piece of the medallion, though it is stolen by ZumZum. Sam kills him and his minions and gets the second piece. Afterwards, Ronaldo warns her brothers that it is Prince Chan 's birthday party and that they must send Sam in. After doing so, they tell him to go there, kill Prince Chan and retrieve the medallion piece. The residents of the city will help him, but they won't be able to assist Sam in stopping Prince Chan as they fear they will be eaten by him.

The wizards set up a plan for Sam so that he enters the town, arrives at Prince Chan's birthday party, kills him and his servants and retrieve the medallion part. He goes along with their plan, and interrups Chan's party. He flies away to his palace and sends more of his servants after Sam. He kills them and confronts Chan one last time. Some heavy fighting insues, with Sam emerging victorious now that he has the third part of the medallion. Once the council teleport Sam back, he catches a glimpse of what appears to be a hologram, but the Council cover it up and say that it's nothing. Wanting to know how to get the fourth medallion piece, the wizards tell him it is the possession of Count Kleerofski . Before they send him there, the hologram malfunctions and it turns out the wizards are in a house wearing the same outfit as Sam. He knows that something was up, but they are hesistant for him to find out any further and kick him out.

Meanwhile, Jebediah and Rajiv are watching the Galaxy Cup finals. Rolanda berates them and says that saving the universe is more important than watching TV. Sam arrives and asks who won, much to Rolanda's dismay. She then has Sam retrieve the last part of the medallion, which was stolen from the Elvians by Cecil the dragon. They say that once Sam has killed him, this will be proof that he is the one .

The wizards have Sam arrive at a nearby village to find Cecil, who comes there once a month to collect taxes from the residents. They tell him this will Sam's chance to catch him. After he defeats Cecil and obtains the last piece of the medallion, Sam arrives at the wizards' home, and asks how they can send him to Sirius. They say it is no easy task, as the planet is protected by an impenetrable shield . To get there, he would need an entire Alliance Fleet and to take control of an extremely powerful laser cannon, which will be able to penetrate the shield. They say this cannon is located on Kronor , the moon of Sirius. There, there are thousands of Alliance troops that have been imprisoned by Mental's forces. Before Sam can get to Kronor, though, he has to free the prisoners first. Once he has done that, they will capture enemy jet fighters and wait for Sam in orbit to activate the cannon.

Once Sam arrives on Kronor, Rolanda arrives in an aircraft and gives him a bomb to detonate inside the facility. Sam manages to make it out just as the base is blown to bits. Later on, Sam enters a large warehouse and encounters Hugo . He destroys the robot and activates the cannon, causing a hole to open up inside the Sirius shield. Sam and the Alliance try to fly through, but only Sam gets there in the end. The wizards were watching these events unfold and Rajiv blame Sam for his failure. Jefediah defends Sam though, telling Rajiv that he is no ordinary human, but Rajiv replies with a cocky remark, still believing that Sam doesn't stand a chance against Mental. With Sam stuck in Siriusopolis, he is lured into a trap by some Orc soldiers and sent into a death arena. Whilst it is being broadcasted, the wizards watch Sam live on their TV, much to their disappointment in him. Soon, the arena is suddenly interrupted as Alliance ships rescue Sam from the arena. They take him to Mental's residence, the Mental Institution , but are shot down along the way. Sam makes it to Mental's home and finishes him off for good. The wizards are then seen cheering now that Mental is gone. However, Mental had actually set up a trap for Sam and manages to escape.

In a post-credits scene, Sam arrives with the medallion in hand, only to find out that there were cardboard boxes full of them sitting in the corner, which meant that Sam's mission was all for nothing. Sam gets enraged with the wizards and can be seen chasing them around the room.
