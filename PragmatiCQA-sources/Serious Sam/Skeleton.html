The Skeleton is an enemy first introduced in Serious Sam VR: The Last Hope , and then later added to Serious Sam 4 in the Halloween patch.

Overview

Skeletons are humanoid variants of Kleer Skeletons that stand on their hind legs and use their front legs to carry a sword and shield. They have glowing blue crystals on their backs, which makes them look as if they are actually blue.

A christmas themed variant of the Skeleton appears in the christmas version of Death from Above in Serious Sam 4 . This variant has red crystals inside its chest instead of blue, wears a santa hat and its sword is replaced with a candy cane. These changes are purely cosmetic, and don't change its stats.

Skeletons are common enemies in Serious Sam VR: The Last Hope , mostly appearing in large groups.

In Serious Sam 4 , they can be encountered only during Halloween and Christmas, first appearing in Death from Above , where they will spawn once the player collects one of the Jack-o'-Lantern/gift pickups spread across the maps.

Behavior and skills

Skeletons are equipped with swords that they use to attack their targets at close range. This sword deals moderate-high damage and they can be dangerous in groups to an unsuspecting player. Their shields can withstand a certain amount of damage before they destroyed.

Tactics

Serious Sam VR: The Last Hope

  • Skeletons are much easier to deal with than their regular counterparts, as they run slower, have less health than their regular counterparts, and don't have any ranged attacks. As such, they can be defeated more easily with small arms fire, such as the SOP38 Pistol .
  • The Laser Sword is one of the best weapons to wipe out hordes of Skeletons at a time, especially if they are lined up in a row.
  • Take note that Skeletons usually drop their shields before taking actual damage, as such, rapid-fire weapons are recommended to use against them.
  • Individual to small-sized groups of Skeletons can be easily defeated with shotguns.
  • Large groups of Skeletons can be taken down with rapid-fire weapons, such as the Thompson submachine gun or M29 Infantry Assault Rifle . These weapons can easily kill them because of their high rate of fire, damage and the Skeleton's low health.
  • The XPML21 Rocket Launcher can kill multiple Skeletons in one shot, making it an excellent weapon against groups.

Serious Sam 4

  • Groups of them can be taken out with the assault rifle, A-24 Devastator , MK III Grenade Launcher or rocket launcher.
  • Don't get too close to a Skeleton, as its sword attack can deal a lot of damage.

Trivia

  • Skeletons are the only non-boss enemies in Serious Sam 4 that does not appear in Serious Sam: Siberian Mayhem
