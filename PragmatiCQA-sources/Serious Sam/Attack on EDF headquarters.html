The Attack on EDF heaquarters was an ambush lead by Mental 's forces during the final days of the Mental War between human armies and Mental's forces . As the Earth Defense Force were unprepared for this attack, the hordes proved to be too much for them to handle and suffered heavy losses, thus the EDF was all but wiped out.

Prelude

Wilson escorts Sam back to base through the desert in his helicopter, having a short conversation until a Major Bio-mechanoid suddenly emerges from the sand. Sam tries to warn Wilson, but he is caught off-topic and the helicopter is hit by one of its rockets, sending Sam flying out of the helicopter as he loses consciousness. After Sam recovers from the fall, he tries to establish contact with HQ for immediate extraction. Unfortunately, his earpiece was damaged during the fall, and has no means of contacting them.

Sam escapes through some tombs, where he comes across the corpse of an EDF soldier and picks up his earpiece. This time, Sam manages to contact HQ, but instead of Quinn who responds to his usual calls, Hellfire picks up and is amazed at the fact that Sam is still alive.

The attack

Hearing explosions and gunfire, Sam asks Hellfire what's happening over at headquarters. She tells him that a sneak attack came out of nowhere and that Mental's forces have penetrated their defenses. Quinn has been killed and Charlie Team have been wiped out. Hellfire tells Sam to stock up on supplies and hide. If anyone manages to survive, they will find him. This is cut short when a Gnaar sneaks up behind and kills her.

Aftermath

Despite the Earth Defense Force and Hellfire's best attempts to repel the alien threat, they were no match against the horde. After Hellfire's death, Sam tries to get a response from Hellfire, but instead of her responding, the Gnaar talks to Sam through Hellfire's earpiece, telling Sam that it is over and Mental has already won. After a brief conversation between them, with Charlie Team and the Earth Defense Force gone, Sam decides to head to the Time-Lock on his own.
