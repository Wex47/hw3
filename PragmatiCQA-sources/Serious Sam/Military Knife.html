The Military Knife , or Combat Knife , is a melee weapon appearing in several titles in the Serious Sam series. It is one of the first weapons the player spawns with when they start a new game in Serious Sam 1 , Serious Sam 4 and Serious Sam: Siberian Mayhem .

Overview

This weapon is a standard-issue Earth Defense Force single-bladed knife used for close-combat. Despite seeming like the most underwhelming weapon in Sam 's arsenal, the military knife should never be underestimated. Not only does the weapon have a slightly longer range than one would expect, but the damage it deals per slash is devastating to all but the largest of enemies. Enemies with at most 100 health can be instantly killed, without alerting the whole group or horde of the player's presence.

In Serious Sam 4 , the player can perform a finishing move on certain types of enemies with the knife. This is activated by the pressing the use key (E by default) when right in front of the enemy. Also in the game, a single swipe from the knife can hit multiple enemies at once.

Tactics

Serious Sam 1

  • The knife should be used only to silently kill smaller enemies or wound bigger ones. For example, the Sirian Werebull requires only three slices, which means one could preserve ammo by using the knife.
  • The knife should never be used for stronger enemies, such as Major Bio-mechanoids or ranged opponents. However, with skill, it can be used to destroy Arachnoids and Common Aludran Reptiloids without them hitting the player once.
  • The knife also shouldn't be used against explosive enemies, such as the Beheaded Bomber (unless the player immediately backs away from him after killing him), Beheaded Kamikaze , and Zumb'ul .
  • The knife is heavily effective against both types of Gnaars , Scythian Witch-Harpies , and Zorgs , with the capability of killing them with one swipe. Against Kleers , two slices will do. The knife does more damage per minute than both shotguns, so it's a good alternative when the fighting arena is spacious for evading enemy attacks.
  • If combined with Serious Damage in Serious Sam: The Second Encounter , the knife can be a very devastating weapon that can almost kill medium-powered targets. With serious damage activated, it can even gib smaller enemies.

Serious Sam 4

  • The knife is a useful weapon against lone individuals or small groups of weak enemies, such as the Beheaded Firecracker , Beheaded Rocketeer , and the Processed Anthropolyp .
  • The knife is useful as a last-resort weapon along with the pistol if they don't have very much ammo for their other weapons.
  • The weapon comes in handy in the first and second levels of the game, especially against the weaker enemies that spawn in groups.
  • The knife in this installment is more powerful than its Serious Sam 1 counterpart, being able to take down medium targets like the Aurigan Haemovore and Emetic Anthropolyp in one slice, making it useful against small groups of them. It can also be dual-wielded once the player has unlocked the Dual Wield skill.
  • It is an ideal weapon for cutting down groups of Gnaars when they are charging straight towards the player, so long as any dangerous enemies aren't present.
  • The knife shouldn't be used against ranged enemies, even weak ones like Hatchling Arachnoids and Octanian Gamma Troopers , as the player risks running straight into their line of fire by doing so. It can, however, be used to quickly slash Octanian Commanders , so long as the player is quick enough to get behind their shields and dodge their projectiles.

Behind the scenes

  • Back in the Alpha phase of Serious Sam 1 , it was possible to change the position of the knife in the hands. When used like this, the knife could deal much higher damage to enemies and gib weak enemies like the Rocketeer. While useful, the impact speed is noticeably lower than in the original. It was removed when the public test demo was released.
  • In Test 1 , the knife could be swung at a faster rate, though it dealt less damage.
  • In Test 2 , the knife's damage was increased substantially, being able to gib enemies from across the room, which was likely an oversight. The knife was later nerfed to deal 100 damage in the final release.

Trivia

  • The knife is very similar in appearance to the OKC-3S bayonet or KA-BAR knife .
  • Though NETRICSA states that the player can use the knife for stealth attacks on enemies from behind, this isn't usually possible most of the time, as enemies are alerted to the player's presence even if they are behind them.
  • If the knife is ported into Serious Sam 3: BFE with the use of mods, and the player enters third-person view, they can see that their player character will use the Sledgehammer animations when using the knife.
  • In Serious Sam 4 , the player can use the knife while sprinting even without the Born To Run skill.
