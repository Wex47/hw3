Overview

Troy was a large city designed with Turkish-themed architecture. When Sam visited Troy, the city was set during the Trojan War , as indicated by the Trojan Horse that had broken through the outer wall. The city had also been laid siege and damaged by either Trojan soldiers or Mental 's forces.

There were many Mental-Altani bases here, which indicated their military presence in the city and surrounding outer areas. They had set up large drills and various machinery around the outskirts of Troy, where Mental-Altani terraformed the area to suite the needs of Mental's forces; the drills were used for pumping up molten lava onto the Earth 's surface, making it a suitable habitat for Lava Golems . Radioactive barrels of ooze laid everywhere, and, like other parts of the region, had succumbed to major enviromental damage, resulting in the beaches and surrounding landscape subjected to severe pollution.

Appearances

Serious Sam's Bogus Detour

Sam visits the city briefly to find parts to repair the damaged Multi-mission aircraft so he can fly it to Greece. Here, he has to find four keycards to access the doors leading to the bases, where the aircraft parts are being kept. Sam's presence in the city has Mental send huge waves of enemy forces at his disposal in an attempt to stop him.

Once Sam has acquired all the aircraft parts, he comes face-to-face with an abnormally large Scrapjack, the Fattie Swallower . After a tough fight, he kills the creature and obtains the last keycard before making it back to the aircraft. He manages to repair it and get to Mount Olympus to get the portal on top of the mountain.

Trivia

  • There are two pieces of document hidden throughout the city. If the player manages to find both of them, they will unlock the secret level Serious Sabotage , where they can obtain the Auto Shotgun . Completing the level also prevents the Orcs from sending reinforcements from their multi-mission aircraft later throughout the campaign.
