The Juvenile Antaresian Spider is an enemy that first appears in Serious Sam 3: BFE , and later on in several Serious Sam games; Serious Sam's Bogus Detour , Serious Sam VR: The Last Hope , and Serious Sam 3 VR: BFE . It is a grown version of the Hatchling Antaresian Spider .

Overview

The Juvenile Antaresian Spider is a young Antaresian Spider , a species of strange arachnid-ike aliens from Antares Prime, a planet that orbits a red supergiant . Due to the sheer size of this star, this has led to the planet's organisms to become extremely large.

Appearances

Juvenile Spiders are deployed along with other creatures in Mental 's army in groups of any size, from a few individuals to huge swarms. They usually serve as main ranged/melee hybrid frontline units or are used in small ambushes.

Serious Sam 3: BFE

In Serious Sam 3 , they first appear in Into the Spider's Nest along with Hatchlings. Later on, they appear much more frequently, with giant swarms in the last level.

Serious Sam's Bogus Detour

In Serious Sam's Bogus Detour , Juveniles are just as common in this game as they are in Serious Sam 3 , though this time they come out of holes from under the ground. Each hole contains up to 3 Spiders. Large groups of Spiders appear during the fight with Tetrachoke . Their role during this fight is to try to distract the player and attempt to have the player concentrate their fire on the Spiders rather than Tetrachoke himself.

Serious Sam VR: The Last Hope

In Serious Sam VR: The Last Hope , Juveniles appear on the first level of the game, Monolith of Time . Like the previous games, they are fairly common enemies, appearing on most levels. They are usually paired with Hatchlings or other enemies.

Behavior and skills

Compared to Hatchling Antaresian Spiders, Juveniles are larger, have stronger jaws and a more developed exoskeleton, which grants them decent protection. Despite this, they can still be killed with one hit with a close-range Double Barrel Coach Gun blast or with strong melee weapons such as the Axe or Sledgehammer . Juveniles can be destroyed with the AS-24 Devastator , C-4 Demolition Charge or XPML21 Rocket Launcher . They can also be killed instantly with the Melee Grab feature; if the player presses the use key when they get close to a Juvenile, Sam will rip off its casing, which will instantly kill it.

When a Juvenile Spider spots the player, it will fire a ball of acid as its primary attack, which is produced by glands in their tail. These projectiles do not home in on the player, and can be destroyed by gunfire. If the Juvenile gets too close to the player, they will bite them with their jaws, which deals moderate damage. Like Hatchling spiders and other species of arachnids, Juveniles can cling to walls and ceilings. This way, they can fire their acid spit at unsuspecting prey.

In the Serious Sam Fusion version of Serious Sam 3 on Serious difficulty , Juveniles have the chance to fire green acid balls that can bounce off surfaces and ceilings and are much harder to avoid. They can be seen doing this when they stay still momentarily and start charging up the acid from their mouths.

Tactics

Serious Sam 3: BFE

  • While having almost the same health as Kleer Skeletons and filling the same role in combat, these enemies are significantly easier to deal with. They are not as fast and lack the Kleer's deadly lunging attack. Their only advantages are that they can climb on walls and have faster projectiles, which can be more dangerous in Serious Sam Fusion where they can bounce off surfaces and ceilings.
  • Two close to medium-range shots from the 12 Gauge Pump-Action Shotgun or one shot from the coach gun are enough to kill one at close range, although larger packs of them can be dealt with the rocket launcher, especially if an ammo crate containing unlimited rockets is present.
  • Smaller groups can effectively be dealt by using melee attacks or the sledgehammer. Care should be taken to dodge their acid spit when they are about to fire. Their green acid projectiles in Serious Sam Fusion are especially more difficult to avoid.
  • C-4 Demolition Charges are useful for flushing out medium groups of Juveniles.
  • Extremely large groups (such as the horde in The Guardian of Time ) can be dealt with using either the XM214-A Minigun or XL2 Lasergun , although the latter's ammo is quite rare owing to being only in secrets.
  • Oddly, their acid spit attack can be shot out of the air.
  • When they appear with other enemies, they are usually a low or medium-low priority target. Most of the other enemies are more powerful, faster or have harder-to-dodge attacks. However, despite their low profile, they have a tendency to block more dangerous enemies like Beheaded Kamikazes , Kleers, and Scrapjacks , making their movement more unpredictable.

Serious Sam's Bogus Detour

  • Unlike the Juveniles in Serious Sam 3 , their projectiles cannot be shot out of the air and must be avoided by sidestepping or dashing.
  • The player should be careful when engaging Spiders in areas with large holes, as this is where they emerge from and they can quickly swarm the player in seconds.
  • The Devastator, Erasergun , Flamethrower , Laser Cannon (especially the upgraded version) and Thompson submachine gun are great weapons against large groups of Juveniles, as they have much lower health in this game compared to Serious Sam 3 .
  • The Auto Shotgun , pump-action shotgun or Super Shotgun are effective to use against small groups of Spiders.
  • During the fight with Tetrachoke, the player should only concentrate on the Spiders if they are low on health or if their numbers get too big. Shooting at them constantly with strong weapons is a waste of ammo that should be really used on the boss.

Serious Sam VR: The Last Hope

  • When paired with Hatchlings, the P-LAH Chainsaw or Laser Sword can quickly take them out, especially if both weapons are dual-wielded.
  • Dual-wield assault rifles, miniguns or rocket launchers are useful for taking out swarms of Juveniles at a time.
  • The RAPTOR Sniper Rifle is also a great weapon for eliminating groups of Juveniles.
