Overview

The static cannon is a medieval cannon that has been altered by Mental after witnessing the incredible power of Sam 's cannon . Mental enhanced the cannon with an auto-loading system, radar and ammunition replenisher. Unlike its rotating counterpart , the static cannon is stationary and does not move; it only fires when someone walks by it.

Appearances

Like the rotating cannon, the static cannon is only found in the Medieval levels: The Citadel , Land of the Damned , and The Grand Cathedral , though it can be found earlier as a secret in The City of the Gods ; funnily enough, entering the hidden doorway to the secret in The City of the Gods puts the player directly in front of the secret cannon. If they don't act fast, they either die or take extreme damage. This cannon also does not trigger a NETRICSA description upon destruction and additionally does not count towards the "Mine's bigger than yours" achievement because the static cannon here is not counted as an enemy but as a prop instead.

Application

The static cannon is usually given the same role as the rotating cannon: to stop anyone who enters its line of sight within their tracks. However, as the static cannon does move, this makes them less reliable than the latter and they are often placed on the ground rather than on top of walls or buildings.

Tactics

  • The RAPTOR Sniper Rifle can destroy a static cannon in one shot and it can reach a static cannon that's placed at any range thanks to its perfect accuracy. As this is the case, the player should always use the RAPTOR against the static cannon.
  • The cannonballs fired by the cannon can be dodged by strafing or sidestepping.
  • If the player doesn't have enough ammo for the sniper rifle, other great weapons to use on the static cannon are strong weapons or ones with a decent rate of fire like the Thompson submachine gun , XL2 Lasergun , and XPML21 Rocket Launcher . If the player is accurate enough, they can even take one down with the MK III Grenade Launcher .
  • If playing on Revolution , another effective weapon to destroy a static cannon is with the CDF-ST-005 Plasmathrower . Its alternate fire mode can destroy the cannon in one shot, although this requires the player not be too far away from the cannon.
  • Static cannons should be a high-priority target when facing other enemies because of how much damage its cannonballs can do, especially on Hard and Serious difficulties. Taking it out as soon as possible makes things much easier, as the player doesn't have to worry about being gibbed or severely wounded by a static cannon while fighting other enemies.
