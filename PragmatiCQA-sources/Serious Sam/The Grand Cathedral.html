The Grand Cathedral is the final level of Serious Sam: The Second Encounter , Serious Sam: Xbox , Serious Sam HD: The Second Encounter , Serious Sam VR: The Second Encounter , and Serious Sam Classics: Revolution .

Plot

The Holy Grail was stolen from the Ice Castle by Mental , who has taken it to the Church of Sacred Blood , where it is guarded by an evil sorcerer . With help from NETRICSA , Sam is able to find the church, but must first pass through the Corridors of Death, which owe up to their name.

Secrets

  1. From your starting position, turn right and blast a darker-colored section on the wall to reveal an Extra Large Health and Serious Damage .
  2. At the end of the second corridor, you can see Groovy Gregory hanging out from one of the windows of a tower. Shoot him to spawn a serious damage power-up.
  3. In the middle of the third corridor, on the wall at the left, shoot a darker colored section of it to reveal a teleport to a secret pumpkin field. Watch out for Static Cannons placed there, and do not move out of the path, unless you want to spawn some extremely small Cucurbitos .
  4. ( Serious Sam HD only) in the fifth corridor, on the right wall where Beheaded Kamikazes spawn, at the base of a pillar is a darker colored section. Shoot it with any weapon to reveal some secret items.
  5. In the sixth corridor, on the right side of the wall is another darker section. Blast it to reveal a teleport to an embryo museum. Picking up any power-ups there will spawn two Highlander Aludran Reptiloids : one on the left and one on the right.
  6. At the end of the sixth corridor and at the start of the seventh and final one, at the tower on the left there is a Scythian Witch-Harpy mooning at the player from a window. Shoot her with the sniper rifle or a rocket to spawn an Invulnerability power-up.
  7. During the fight with Mordekai, after approximately 85-90 seconds, a message will appear saying "Pink secret has been enabled!", and a small section of the wall on the right will open up. Pick up the health there to register the secret.
