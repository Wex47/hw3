The Humvee is a light utility vehicle that appears in Serious Sam 4 . It was the standard land transportation vehicle used by members of the Earth Defense Force .

Overview

The Humvee is a lightly armored four-wheel drive utility vehicle that was used by the Earth Defense Force. Originally, they belonged to the United States military, but were bought into action during the Mental War .

Application

Humvees were primarily used for escorting military personnel and squads of soldiers from one location to another, or for carrying light cargo transport, supplies and weapons behind the front lines. They often traveled in a convey to reduce the chances of separation or isolation during travel.

Humvees were lightly armored, and were only equipped with heavy machine guns, such as the M2 Browning . This machine gun was useful for elimating threats on the ground, but proved to be ineffective against fast or flying targets. Since Humvees had little armor, this made them extremely vulnerable to attacks from Mental 's forces, such as bombs dropped by Octanian Raiders . It only took at least one bomb to destroy a Humvee.

Appearances

Serious Sam 4

Two Humvees are seen escorting a convey of Alien Artifact Acquisition SUVs through the streets of Rome as they rendezvous with Father Mikhail . Many more Humvees are seen later in the game in A Breakfast in France and Anathema Unto God .

The player cannot drive the Humvee, unlike other vehicles that appear in the game, since they are only scripted for cutscenes.
