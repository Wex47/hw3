Small Armor ( Ancient Helmet in Serious Sam: Double D ) is an item that gives the player a small amount of armor points when picked up.

Overview

Small armor is a relatively weak item that gives the player 5-10 armor points when picked up, depending on the game that is being played on. In all of the games it appears in, it will not go past the default maximum armor limit (100 in all other games, 200 in Serious Sam 2 ). It seems to give the player some armor points so that they can get to the 100 armor point limit without picking up a stronger set of armor. This can be useful if the player has a large amount of armor points and doesn't want to use up a larger set of armor in order to get to the 100 armor points cap.

Appearances

Small armor is very rare in Serious Sam: The Second Encounter , only appearing on some levels, such as The Elephant Atrium and the Legend of the Beast levels. In later games, it becomes much more common, especially in the early levels. In other games, small armor occurs more frequently than in The Second Encounter .

Like Medium Armor , small armor is never booby trapped in any of its appearances.
