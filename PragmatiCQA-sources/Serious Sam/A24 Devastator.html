The A-24 , commonly referred to as the Devastator , is an automatic shotgun that fires explosive rounds that can pierce through multiple weak targets at once. It first appears in Serious Sam 3: BFE , and later seen in Serious Sam's Bogus Detour , Serious Sam VR: The Last Hope , Serious Sam 3 VR: BFE , and Serious Sam 4 .

Overview

This automatic 24-gauge shotgun is often referred to as the 'Devastator' due to the effect that it can have on a crowded battlefield if fired continuously over a prolonged period of time. The original 1972 Maxwell Atchisson design has been heavily modified over the last 150 years, resulting in a compact, fast weapon with a lot of stopping power. The state-of-the-art firing mechanism allows for exit velocities great enough to literally punch through many smaller targets. This effect is enhanced by the Autoshotgun 's standard cartridges, which contain high-piercing heavy-explosive (HPHE) rounds which are designed to pierce smaller hostiles and deliver an additional burst of fragmentation damage upon impact with larger foes.

Being an automatic shotgun, the Devastator does not need to be pumped each time a round is fired. It can carry up to 20 or 25 rounds (depending on the game it appears in), which come in drum magazines. The Devastator's rounds travel at a much faster speed than rockets and grenades, allowing them to be used effectively at a further distance, though aim compensation is still required as the rounds do not hit instantly.

As the Devastator is one of the most powerful weapons in the game, ammo for the Devastator is quite scarce, though it appears more frequently in later levels, often before a large battle starts.

If the Serious Digital edition or Bonus Content was purchased for Serious Sam 3 , players are given access to a limited exclusive DLC, which adds a sniper scope onto the Devastator. In the Xbox 360 and Serious Sam Fusion versions of the game and in Serious Sam 4 , the scope comes by default.

Appearances

Serious Sam 3: BFE

In Serious Sam 3 and Serious Sam 3 VR: BFE , the Devastator first appears in Unearthing the Sun , but can be acquired earlier in The Silent Riddler via a secret inside The Great Pyramid. The weapon is then acquired again in The Last Man on Earth .

Serious Sam's Bogus Detour

In Serious Sam's Bogus Detour , the weapon first appears in Trouble in Troy .

Serious Sam VR: The Last Hope

Like the majority of weapons that appear in the game, the Devastator can be purchased from the weapons menu, along with ammo.

Serious Sam 4

In this installment, the Devastator is first obtained in One for the Road , and then for the second time in From Earth With Love at an Earth Defense Force outpost.

Serious Sam: Siberian Mayhem

The Devastator does not officially appear in Serious Sam: Siberian Mayhem , having been replaced by the TCC2 "Perun" Crossbow in the campaign. However, like with the M29 Infantry Assault Rifle , XL2 Lasergun , and RAPTOR Sniper Rifle , the Devastator is usable in the moddable version of the game.

Tactics

Serious Sam 3: BFE

  • The Devastator is extremely effective against Technopolyp helicopters, as it can take one down in about five hits and its projectiles move fast enough that the helicopter can't dodge. It also works very well against Aludran Reptiloids , Adult Arachnoids , Minor and Major Bio-mechanoids , and Scrapjacks at long distances.
  • As ammunition isn't always in abundance, it is best to save the weapon for use against tougher enemies.
  • The Devastator is somewhat effective against Khnums , as it takes at least 10 shots to kill one. It is best to first fire a fully charged cannonball at the Khnum and then finish it off with the Devastator.
  • It is very effective against hordes of common enemies such as Kleer Skeletons , as the projectiles fired from the Devastator can pierce through several enemies at once.

Serious Sam's Bogus Detour

  • The Devastator is useful against big groups of weak enemies, like Kleers and Gnaars .
  • It also works well against strong enemies, like Bio-mechanoids, Orc Captains and Scrapjacks.
  • It's also an effective weapon to use against bosses, due to its fast rate of fire and decent damage output.

Serious Sam VR: The Last Hope

  • The Devastator is a great weapon for clearing out groups of Drones , Fleet Fighters , Skeletons , and other fast or weak enemies.
  • Its fast projectiles are good for eliminating small groups of Kalopsies , especially when dual-wielded.
  • Like in Serious Sam 3 , the Devastator is a brilliant anti-Technopolyp weapon.

Serious Sam 4 / Serious Sam: Siberian Mayhem

  • The Devastator is a brilliant weapon for clearing out hordes of Gnaars, Processed Anthropolyps , and Kleers if they are lined up together. It also works well against armored enemies like Octanian Zealots and Octanian Legionnaires .
  • Since this weapon deals more damage to enemies if they are shot in the head, it is especially effective against Khnums, Reptiloids, Scrapjacks, and Sirian Werebulls. Shooting a Werebull, Scrapjack, or Reptiloid in the head with this weapon is a guaranteed one-shot kill.
  • When dual-wielded, the Devastator should be used to quickly eliminate dangerous and tough enemies like Draconian Pyromaniacs , Draconian Burners , and Lava Golems .

Trivia

  • In Serious Sam 3 , the Devastator sometimes has a very large, but weak splash damage range when compared to the rocket launcher. Even if the player is at a normal safe distance a rocket can explode, the Devastator shell explosion may still afflict the player, and deliver a very negligible amount of damage. This has been fixed in Serious Sam 4 .
  • When Jones cocks his Devastator in the opening cutscene of Serious Sam 3 , a cocking sound is heard, but no animation is seen.
  • The Devastator is a possible nod to the Devastator in Duke Nukem 3D due to the fact that both weapons shoot very fast projectiles with a high rate of fire.
  • In Serious Sam 3 , the Devastator can only penetrate those enemies that can't survive the initial round fired (in case of the Hatchling Arachnoid and Minor Bio-mechanoid, it's required to hit a specific hitzone to penetrate them, like a head or pelvis). In Serious Sam 4 however, the Devastator will always penetrate weak and medium enemies no matter where it hits and is also guaranteed to gib them.
  • There are a lot of misconceptions among the fans about which type of ammunition this weapon uses. Despite being an upgraded version of an automatic shotgun, it actually uses modified 40mm rounds, as seen on its ammo worldmodel and on the concept art from the artbook. It's also worth mentioning that fired rounds use a recycled rocket model from Serious Sam HD both in Serious Sam 3 and Serious Sam 4 , but it's hard to notice in normal gameplay due to the projectile's extreme speed. To make things even more confusing, the NETRICSA log in Serious Sam 4 points out that the Devastator uses modified 24 gauge Autoshotgun shells despite them being much smaller than the usual 40mm rounds. However, it is entirely possible that this version of the weapon actually uses explosive shotgun slugs or reinforced FRAG-12 ammunition.
