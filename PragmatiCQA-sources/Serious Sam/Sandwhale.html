The Sandwhale is an unkillable enemy featured in Serious Sam 3: BFE and Serious Sam 3 VR: BFE . Its main purpose is to prevent players from wandering too far away from the map. If the player gets too far from the level, the Sandwhale will start growling and shake the ground. If the player continues to wander further from the playing area, the Sandwhale will suddenly appear and eat them.

Overview

The Sandwhale is a massive worm-like creature that lives in the sands of Egypt . It is unknown if they are part of Mental 's forces, as they were brought to Earth in their microscopic larval state from an unnamed desert planet in Gamma Pegasi , probably due to landing pad contamination.

Appearances

A Sandwhale first appears in the opening cutscene, where it attempts to devour the UH-60 Black Hawk that Sam and his team are flying on. Luckily, the pilot manages to steer the helicopter away from it and sparing the team from being devoured alive. Later in the campaign, Sandwhales become common in the desert areas outside of Cairo . They also sometimes randomly appear, diving in sand and probably devouring some random prey, far in the background. In the final level, a Sandwhale will come bursting out of the sand when Ugh-Zan IV appears and they begin fighting.

Behavior and skills

Despite their name, Sandwhales are not related to whales, but are in fact worms of polychaeta class. Sandwhales mostly feed on minerals and sediments, and don't usually prey on living organisms. However, they are highly territorial creatures, and if they are disturbed, an aggravated Sandwhale can swallow a heavy assault tank whole, without chewing. They are so well protected by their thick scales that there are no known ways to kill one short of nuclear weapons . The short stubs along their backs are thought to be a kind of very sensitive cilia, allowing them to feel presence of intruders walking on sand from some distance away. These scales are sensitive enough to detect a cat walking on sand from up to 10 miles away.

Sandwhales are the most powerful enemies in the game. If the player is devoured by a Sandwhale, they will be killed instantly. They cannot be fought, nor does the player have the firepower necessary to destroy it, but ultimately the player can use it to help them defeat the final boss, as Ugh-Zan IV sometimes walks up to the sand and growls, calling the Sandwhale to wrestle with him. Despite this, the player still has to be careful as being in the area of the Sandwhale will cause it to devour them.

Tactics

  • The player should not stray too far from the map, even if they are being outnumbered by enemies, as death by a Sandwhale is always certain.
  • When the player starts to see the sand shaking and the Sandwhale coming towards them, they should get back within the map boundary immediately.
  • Sandwhales are not present in Serious Sam: The First Encounter due to being set in a different timeline. Even if the player wanders from away from the map in desert areas, they will instead slowly start to lose health due to heat damage.

Trivia

  • In the game's files, there's a "wound" and "death" sound in the Sandwhale's folder.
  • This enemy seems to be heavily inspired by the Sandworm from Dune and the Graboid from the Tremors franchise.
  • There are certain tactics to avoid the Sandwhale's attack on some levels, which can help speedrunners.
  • Some of the Sandwhale's sounds seem to be recycled from the Serious Sam 2 Kwongo boss stage.
  • The jetpack will overheat instantly if the player flies far enough into the desert to provoke the Sandwhale.
  • After Sam defeats Ugh-Zan IV and escapes just before the Moon is sent crashing into the Earth , it is presumed that any Sandwhales on the planet at the time were also killed.
