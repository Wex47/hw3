Extra Small Health ( Multi-vitamin in Serious Sam: Double D ) is a healing item that appears in the Serious Sam games. It is the weakest healing item in the series.

Overview

In most of its appearances, extra small health gives the player 1 health point when picked up, while in Serious Sam 2 and Serious Sam: Next Encounter , it gives the player 5 health points when picked up. Extra small health is one of the only two health items that can boost the players health beyond the usual maximum of 100, the other being Extra Large Health .

Appearances

In Serious Sam 1 , Serious Sam 3: BFE , Serious Sam 3 VR: BFE and Serious Sam 4 , extra small health is often used as a trap to spawn in extra enemies (e.g. groups of Kleer Skeletons or even Major Bio-mechanoids ). Therefore, the player should avoid picking them up unless they have enough health/armor/ammo to deal with extra enemies that may spawn in. In addition, chains of extra small health appearing one after another when one is picked up is often a trap. After a certain amount of extra small health has been picked up, an enemy or group of enemies will suddenly appear in front of or near the player.

In Serious Sam 2 , extra small health are often bundled up in groups of four or so to give the player a decent amount of health if all of them are picked up at once.

In Serious Sam 3: BFE , Serious Sam 3 VR: BFE , and Serious Sam 4 , extra small health is the most common healing item in the first two or so levels. They become less common later in the games.
