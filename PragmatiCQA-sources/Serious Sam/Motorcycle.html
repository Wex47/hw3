Overview

The motorcycle is a chopper motorcycle that appears to be based on a 1970s Harley-Davidson (possibly a softail) powered by a V-twin engine . It can be ridden by pressing the Use key when near it. It has decent speed and is useful for getting from one destination to the next. When the player exits the bike, it is marked with a wheel icon to show its location.

The player can use some of their weapons when riding on the motorbike by pressing the fire key, which will bring up the selected weapon. The player cannot use two-handed weapons like the RAPTOR Sniper Rifle or dual-wield weapons while driving.

Appearances

Serious Sam 4

Sam first acquires the bike at an intersection after parachuting down into the French countryside. It proves to be a useful asset for him as he ventures out onto the open road. After some driving, he reaches a bridge with a spike strip on it, forcing him to ditch the bike and make it to the town on foot.

Tactics

  • The bike has limited health, so don't let enemies swarm all over it or allow their projectiles to hit it.

Trivia

  • The word "serious" can be seen on the motorcycle's fuel tanks and speed dial.
