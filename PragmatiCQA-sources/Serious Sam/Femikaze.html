The Femikaze is an enemy featured in Serious Sam: Double D and Serious Sam: Double D: XXL . She is a larger, more dangerous version of the normal Beheaded Kamikaze .

Overview

The Femikaze is a large headless woman who appears to be shirtless. She holds two bombs, which are strategically placed in order to cover her breasts. Like the Beheaded Kamikaze, she emits a high-pitched scream.

The first Femikaze appears in Pyramid Looting , and then many times throughout the game. They seem to spawn at the same rate as normal Kamikazes.

Behavior and skills

The Femikaze behaves much like the normal Beheaded Kamikaze; she will quickly run towards the target while screaming. When she touches the target, she will explode.

Tactics

  • Femikazies are essentially larger Beheaded Kamikazes and should be treated as such.
  • The Femikaze's size makes it easier to hit her compared to the regular Kamikaze, but it also gives her a larger vertical explosion radius if she explodes.
  • Explosive weapons are not recommended against the Femikaze. At close range, a Femikaze killed by an explosion will have the player be harmed by both the Femikaze and weapon's explosion.
  • When paired with other enemies, the Femikaze should be a high-priority target. They are quite fast, and their explosion does a good amount of damage. A Femikaze or two that manage to explode near the player can do a significant amount of damage, making the player much more vulnerable to other enemies.
