Plot

A massive alien portal has opened up in Tunguska , allowing Mental 's forces to enter and invade Earth , beginning the Tunguska Offensive . Lord Achriman , Mental's dreaded servant and the one in charge of leading the Europe campaign, has his troops invade Europe, including Italy and France.

Meanwhile, in war-torn Rome , an Earth Defense Force convey are en route to meet up with Father Mikhail , a Russian priest and expert in apocryphal texts, who may know the location of the Ark of the Covenant , which itself contains the Holy Grail . Using this an alien artifact, the EDF hope that they can use it turn the tide in the war and give humanity a fighting chance. On their way, the convey is bombarded by Octanian Raiders . Sam 's car is hit, and separating him and Kenny from the rest of the team. With the Octanian raiders dominating the airspace, they have to meet up with the priest on their own.

Side objectives

Over the Wall

When Kenny mentions about exploring an old EDF facility and offers Sam a lift up the wall, the player should proceed until they reach a radio. Once they get there, they need to open up the crate which will reward them with two Holodecoys .
