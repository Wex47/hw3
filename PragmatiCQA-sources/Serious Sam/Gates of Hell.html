Plot

With the activation of the HAARP weapon a success, the satellite sends out a large seismic wave which causes Mount Vesuvius to erupt, filling up the ruins of Pompeii with lava and ash, just like it did back in 79 AD . Meanwhile, a glitch with communications has caused the Alien Artifact Acquisition squad to end up stuck in Pompeii. With no time to waste, Sam must now hurry back to the car with his team before he burns to death or ends up buried in the ash.

Side objectives

Collect samples

Dr. Kiesel will contact Sam in the early part of the level about collecting tissue samples from fallen enemies. The player needs to find five samples to complete the side mission. Completing it will award them with an upgrade for the XPML21 Rocket Launcher , which is received at the beginning of the next level.

Trivia

  • This is the first and only level that the Lava Golem appears in throughout the entire game.
  • The Witch-Bride of Achriman miniboss fight at the end of the level uses the same music as the Adult Arachnoid boss fight at the end of Into the Spider's Nest from Serious Sam 3: BFE .
  • There is an Easter egg in this level; using the sniper rifle, if the player aims the sniper scope at Mount Vesuvius, they will be able to barely make out a small figure on top of the volcano. Shooting the figure with the sniper rifle will teleport the player to a forest with dead trees in a fiery environment. They will be teleported back to their location shortly after. This can only be done once.
