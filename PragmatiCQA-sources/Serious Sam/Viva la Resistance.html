Plot

Sam and Kenny make it to Carcassonne , but not longer after Lord Achriman shows up with Kenny in his clutches. He kills Kenny, which deeply saddens and angers Sam . Achriman teleports away and Sayid meets up with Sam shortly after. He introduces Sam to the French Resistance and Michelle , their leader, in a bar. Michelle plans to lead her rebels and Sam through the city and fight their way to the castle, the location of the Holy Grail . Once everyone is ready, they head out and make their way to the castle to meet up with the informant .

Side missions

The Art of War

Progress normally through the canal with Michelle and Sayid until reaching a split. Go right under the bridge to start the side quest. Completing it will reward the player with the Autoshotgun , which is given to Sam by Sayid.

Let's Workshop This

Later on in the level, Michelle will mention about an abandoned workshop nearby. Follow the blue waypoint marker to start the side quest and eliminate all the enemies afterwards to complete it. Finishing the side quest will reward the player with a Holodecoy .

Trivia

  • The name of this level is a play on "Vive la résistance", a French expression which means "long live the resistance!". It is sometimes used in English.
