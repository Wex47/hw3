Overview

The Ark of the Covenant is an ornate chest made of gold-plated wood, and topped with two large, golden angels. It was carried using poles inserted through rings on its sides. The Ark was described in the Book of Exodus as containing the two stone tablets of the Ten Commandments, but is actually an alien artifact created by the Sirians to house the Holy Grail and to shield its evil power from the world.

Appearances

Serious Sam 4

The Alien Artifact Acquisition squad arrive in the church with Father Mikhail . With the book containing the old Slavic text in hand, he follows the instructions and performs a ritual, which involves activating hidden mechanisms on the statues. He completes the ritual, and the Ark is revealed as it rises up from the floor. Sam and Mikhail approach the Ark and inspect it, with Mikhail commenting that he can feel its immense power. Sam opens the Ark, which reveals the Holy Grail. As he is about to grab the grail, Mikhail warns him not to touch it, as the Ark was intended to seal away the Grail's powers.

Trivia

  • Sam's quote when he is about to open the Ark is a reference to the spirits in the Ark melting off Arnold Toht's face in Raiders of the Lost Ark .
