Charch-gach-zrallma Gchhuck-iargh , or more commonly called Charlie , is an Adult Arachnoid who defected from Mental's army and decided to work as an informant for the French Resistance. She is a minor supportive character in Serious Sam 4 .

Biography

Charlie was born on the home planet of the Arachnoids , called Scorpius. The planet was home to a faction of religious fanatics who were persuaded by Mental . The religious fanatics then destroyed the High Council of Scorpius, as well as destroying thousands of years of culture in the process, and became allies to Mental and joined his horde. When Mental started drifting young arachnoids for his army, the Resistance saw an opportunity to sabotage Mental's operations from the inside by cooperating with a defector to Mental's army, who happens to be Charlie.

Appearances

Serious Sam 4

Sam , Michelle and Sayid go to the sewers to meet-up with the informant. Sam wants to know how long will it take for him to come before being surprised at the sight of an Adult Arachnoid and aims at him with his pistol. Michelle tells him that the Arachnoid is the informant and the Arachnoid tells Sam to calm down and that she's not a servant of Tah-Um .

Sam is perplexed but finally agrees to go to the castle with the arachnoid. He asks him his name and the arachnoid tells him Charch-gach-zrallma Gchhuck-iargh , but Sam decides to call him Charlie. Charlie then remembers that her brood mother used to call him Charlie when he was a hatchling. Charlie explains why he defected to the French Resistance, which Sam finds "cool".

After meeting up with Father Mikhail and arriving at the castle, Sam wants Charlie to tell Carter that the Arachnoids faked the moon landing. Charlie tells Sam that he is known for his practical jokes but before he can finish talking, some Raiders ambush them and destroy the bridge, creating a gap between Sam and the rest of his team. Sam wants to cross the gap but Michelle tells him not to and that he should go to the castle since it's very important. Sam agrees and Charlie tells Sayid, Michelle and Father Mikhail that they will show Tah-Um how the free people of the galaxy stand together.

Charlie is not seen again nor mentioned afterwards. It is probable that she survived and goes around helping the French Resistance the best she can.

Relationships

Sam Stone

Charlie was fond of Sam, proudly helping him in combat alongside the French Resistance. His dislike of Mental's army and practices led him to help humanity against their fight with Tah-Um. Although they never spoke a lot to each other, Sam was impressed by Charlie's backstory and motivations.

Trivia

  • Although Charlie's fate is left unknown afterwards in the game, it is not unlikely to believe that she died following Mental's "mooning" of Earth at the end of Serious Sam 3: BFE .
  • Her mother was known to have called her Charlie, probably for the same reason why Sam preferred to call her by it.
  • She is the only known member of Mental's Horde to have defected from his army.
  • She does not have a NETRICSA description, unlike many of the other characters.
  • In a cut piece of dialogue it was revealed that all of the arachnoids on Earth, including Charlie (as of In Carcassonne ) were female.
