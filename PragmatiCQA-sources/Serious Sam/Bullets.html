Overview

Bullets are a type of ammo pickup that provides bullets for automatic weapons, such as the Thompson submachine gun . The amount of ammo in each box of bullets depends on the game and (in some cases) the difficulty level. In Serious Sam 1 , Serious Sam: Xbox , Serious Sam: Next Encounter , Serious Sam HD and Serious Sam VR , each box supplies 50 rounds (100 rounds on Tourist, Easy and Serious difficulties in SS1 and SSHD ), while in Serious Sam 2 , it gives the player 100 rounds per box. In Serious Sam: Double D and Serious Sam: Double D: XXL , it gives the player 125 rounds.

Bullets are fairly common throughout the games, and usually appear in abundance in "break" rooms before or after a big fight.

In Serious Sam 3: BFE , Serious Sam 4 , and Serious Sam: Siberian Mayhem , bullets have been split up into Assault Rifle Bullets and Minigun Bullets . Assault Rifle Bullets supply ammo for the M29 Infantry Assault Rifle , Tommygun, and AK74MX ; while Minigun Bullets now instead supply ammo for the XM214-A Minigun.

Variants

In Next Encounter , two other variants of bullets exist; Homing Bullets and ricochet bullets. homing bullets will home in on targets when they are fired, and ricochet bullets can bounce off of walls, floors or ceilings.
