Appearances

Serious Sam 3: BFE

During the last days of the Mental War , Mental manages to launch a sneak attack on the Earth Defense Force 's headquarters, wiping out everyone in it. With the death of Hellfire , Sam attempts to contact her again, only to hear the Gnaar in response; he tells Sam that Chaad-Sheen has won and it is over, but Sam does not give up. He says it will not be over until he kills every last one of the alien invaders, but the Gnaar says that Mental is going to "moon" him, though Sam does not quite understand what the Gnaar means by "mooning".

Later on, after defeating Ugh-Zan IV , Sam gives Mental a call on Hermann Stein 's cell phone, but Judy Mental , Mental's daughter answers instead. She tells Sam that he has to get off the planet as soon as possible, as her father is going to "moon" the Earth. Sam is confused once again by what this means, but, after seeing the Moon about to collide with Earth, she meant that Mental was literally going to moon the Earth. Having no other choice, Sam makes it as fast as he can to the Time-Lock just as the planet is destroyed.

Serious Sam's Bogus Detour

In another timeline, dating back to the Ancient Egyptian period, the Mental-Altani corporation constructed a large base on the Moon. The corporation used this base as a safe research facility and transportation system, in the hope that no human will ever be able to find it. Portals were established on Earth in specific places, with one of them being located on top of Mount Olympus, to transport Mental's troops down to Earth.

Mental-Altani also performed experiments on the ooze in the laboratories. The scientists attempted to enhance some of Mental 's ordinary units, such as Gnaars, into stronger variants to deploy into battle. These Gnaars were kept in stasis tanks, filled with ooze.

In Greece, Sam defeats the troops guarding the portal on Mount Olympus and enters the portal, ending up on the Moon base. NETRICSA tells him that to disable the portals, he has to find a way to the power core which supplies power to them. After destroying the shield, Sam takes an elevator down to Station Command, where he discovers the corporation are planning to use Interplanetary Renewal Devices , weapons of mass destruction, to destroy Earth and rid the planet of the human race and thus changing the future of humanity forever.

Luckily, Sam, with help from NETRICSA, hacks and recalibrate the missiles so that they will destroy the Orcs' base instead of Earth. However, before Sam is able to launch the missiles, he is confronted by The Director , who fights Sam, though the Director teleports away before Sam can defeat him. Once Sam finds him again, he has Sam battle with two war utility mechs; PRIME-209 and Tech-X . After Sam destroys the mechs, the Director begins launching the missiles, initiating a countdown in the process, though he is unaware that Sam has already tampered with them. The Director confronts Sam again, but just as he is about to kill the Director, he teleports away once more, this time to an unknown location. With just minutes to go before the base explodes, Sam makes his way to a hangar, where he commandeers a spaceship to escape the Moon base shortly before it is destroyed.
