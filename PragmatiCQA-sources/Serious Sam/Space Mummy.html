Overview

The Space Mummy is an undead humanoid creature that resembles a mummy with a flame glowing from within their chest. They from the planet Gamma Pegasi, and have been brought to life with dark magic, most likely with Mental 's powers. There are two variants of Space Mummies, the normal Space Mummy and Giant Space Mummy. The Giant Space Mummy only appears as a boss in The Last Hope .

Appearances

Space Mummies are common enemies in the games they appear in. In The Last Hope , the giant Space Mummy is encountered in Desert of the Lost . In Serious Sam 4 , they first appear in Gates of Hell , and then become increasingly more common throughout the game, and are often seen in small to medium groups or even large hordes.

Behavior and skills

When a Space Mummy spots the player, their only attack is to throw a fireball at them from their chests. They tend to strafe when throwing their fireballs, which can make it hard to fire at them accurately. The glow illuminating from their chest serves as their weak spot, and shooting at it will deal extra damage to the Mummy.

The Giant Space Mummy, unlike the nornmal variant, has three attacks. The first attack is occasionally throwing buildings at the player, in a similar manner to Kwongo , and its second attack is firing several fireballs from its chest. If the player doesn't kill it quickly, it will use its third attack; trying to stomp on the player with its feet.

Tactics

Serious Sam VR: The Last Hope

  • Lone Space Mummies can be taken down with the Laser Pistol or SOP38 Pistol . It takes about 10 shots to kill one.
  • The Space Mummy's projectiles can be shot down. This can be useful if you are low on health and make sure you won't be killed by it.
  • Multiple Space Mummies can be taken down with rapid-fire weapons, such as the minigun. Shotguns are also a good choice if the mummies are very close to the player, as they deal a lot of damage and have a high rate of fire.
  • Be sure to destroy the Giant Space Mummy's projectiles whenever possible, as they deal a lot of damage.
  • The minigun and rocket launcher are the best weapons to use against the Giant Space Mummy. Their high rate of fire and damage allows the player to quickly kill it before it starts coming close to them.

Serious Sam 4

  • Unlike most enemies in Serious Sam 4 , their weak spot is their fire glowing from their torso, so shoot at it whenever possible.
  • Their fireballs cannot be destroyed, but they can easily be avoided.
  • Space Mummies have half the health in this installment than the ones that appear in The Last Hope , making them easier to kill than before.
  • The 12 Gauge Pump-Action Shotgun , Autoshotgun , Double Barrel Coach Gun and M29 Infantry Assault Rifle are great weapons against groups of Space Mummies.
  • They can be insta-killed with a melee attack without any additional skills.
  • For larger groups, the grenade launcher on the pump-action shotgun or the XPML21 Rocket Launcher are effective to use on them.
