Overview

The Floater is a small floating robot with a large green "eye" in the center of their head, two arms and twin engines.

Attacks

The Floater has two attacks: fire a laser at the target, or attack the player with one its arms if the player gets close to it.

Tactics

  • Floaters can be dangerous if the player encounters a large group of them, as they will constantly fire lasers at the player. Otherwise, Floaters are a little threat.
  • Floaters frequently collide with the map geometry, which makes them unable to attack and stay in place for a few seconds. This is a perfect opportunity to kill one.
  • Groups of Floaters can be dealt with by using the Auto Shotgun or the Uzi . The auto shotgun works well for eliminating a Floater in one hit, while the uzi works if the player needs to quickly hit multiple Floaters.
