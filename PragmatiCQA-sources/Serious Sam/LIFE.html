L.I.F.E. ( L ightweight I ntravenous F ibroblast E nergizer) is a gadget featured in Serious Sam 4 . It is the first gadget obtained in the game.

Overview

L.I.F.E. appears as a small vial with a blue glowing liquid suspended in it and is injected into the veins like a syringe. The device employs the latest advances in medical nanotechnology to deliver a rapid improvement to the user's health status by boosting the performance of their tissue-producing cells. Its extremely sturdy and portable design means it can be used anywhere at any time. It also contains built-in tweezers.

When the player uses the gadget, it will give them a boost of 50 health points. The gadget can allow the player's health to go past 100 up the maximum health limit of 200 without having to rely on health pickups.

Appearances

L.I.F.E. is the most common gadget in the game, appearing in the early levels in the game and then more frequently in the later levels. It is often found in secrets and rewarded after completing side quests.

Tactics

  • As the player can only carry up to ten of these pickups, they should be used sparingly, such as when a big fight is about to start.
  • These gadgets can be very useful if the player is low on health and there aren't many health items around.
  • The player should look around the map to increase the chances of finding L.I.F.E. pickups, as they are one of the most common secret items in the game.
