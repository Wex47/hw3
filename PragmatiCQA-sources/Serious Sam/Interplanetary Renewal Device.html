An Interplanetary Renewal Device , or IRPD , is a devastating weapon of mass destruction used by the Mental-Altani Corporation .

Overview

Made by Mental-Altani, these missiles are capable of wiping out an entire civilization from the face of a planet. Each warhead is packed tight with Exolyse bacteria, which are kept suspended under intense pressure. These organisms originate from the planet Slake, located in one of the farthest edges of the known universe.

These missiles were first used by Mental 's forces on Ratio City on the planet Minerva. In no time the entire metropolis was wiped from the surface of the planet, leaving no trace of it ever existing. The only trace left of Ratio City was the crater impact left by the actual missile.

As these missiles are so destructive, there are few countermeasures available, and in the time it would take to take to apply them the effect would be tremendous.

Appearances

Serious Sam's Bogus Detour

At least a dozen or so IRPDs were constructed or bought to the Mental-Altani Moon base . Here, the corporation intend to use these missiles to wipe out humanity on Earth , creating an alternate timeline in which no humans would exist in the future. Luckily, Sam , with NETRICSA 's assistance, is able to prevent this from happening by hacking and redirecting the missiles to destroy the Moon base, saving humanity once again from Mental's evil schemes.
