Overview

The pistols fires a single bullet every time the attack key is pressed. Like the Colt Anaconda and Schofield .45 , they are always dual-wielded. They are fairly weak weapons and have to be reloaded manually, but they have unlimited ammunition.

The pistols can receive several upgrades to increase their damage output, reduce reload speed or for increased rate of fire.

Occurrence

The pistols are first acquired in Knee Deep in Sand , and then again in Path of Feast / Fear .

Tactics

  • The pistols are useful against weak enemies, such as Infected Fish , Gnaars , Marsh Hoppers or small groups of Orc Cadets or Orc Troopers , though its slow rate of fire preludes its use in large firefights.
  • As the pistols are quite weak and have a low rate of fire, they are best used as a last resort if the player is low on ammunition.
  • The pistol upgrades make the weapon more effective against large groups of Gnaars, Orcs or Marsh Hoppers, though it is sometimes best to switch to a more powerful weapon if possible.

Upgrades

Gunslinger
Your Pistols do 25% more damage.
Piercing Pistols
Your Pistols will pierce up to 2 enemies. Damage is reduced by 50% per enemy pierced.
Trigger Happy
You can fire you Pistols more rapidly by repeatedly pulling the trigger. Pistol damage is reduced by 50%.

Related achievements
