History

The island of Philae was originally located near the First Cataract of the Nile in Upper Egypt and was the site of an Egyptian temple complex. In Ancient Egyptian times, the Sirians constructed two secret chambers underneath the Temple of Isis, where they placed a seal deep within. This seal was needed for unlocking a temple in Abu Simbel and in another tomb in case the Time-Lock didn't activate properly.

In 1960, the Aswan Dam was completed, which began to flood the nearby Lake Nasser. These rapids and the surrounding area had been variously flooded since the initial construction of the Aswan Low Dam in 1902. Since then, the temple complex on Philae was dismantled and moved to nearby Agilkia Island , which protected this and other complexes before the completion of the Aswan High Dam in 1970.

Like many other places in Egypt, Philae became abandoned after Mental 's armies invaded Earth . Sam was tasked of visiting this island by the Earth Defense Force to search for what they believed was the location of the Time-Lock control room.

Appearances

Serious Sam 3: BFE

Having activated both Sirian plasma generators for the Time-Lock , Wilson takes Sam back to headquarters. On the way there, Wilson receives new orders from Quinn , telling Sam that the Time-Lock didn't come online as expected, so he needs to go to the island of Philae to find out how to activate it. After some protesting, he does so.

On the island, Sam finds a key to unlock a gate located at the entrance to Isis' tomb. Upon entering the tomb, Sam comes across a metallic alien door like the ones found at other excavation sites throughout Egypt. Inside, he finds a statue of Isis . Picking it up, he places it on a pedestal in the temple back on the surface, which activates a system that eventually lets him open a door hidden deeper in the tomb.

Sam makes his way back into the tomb and enters the second Sirian room. Unfortunately, it is not the control room he was looking for. The room, however, does contain a seal, a holographic map showing the possible location for the control room, and an XL2 Lasergun . Exiting the tomb, Sam contacts HQ and tells them that the control room is not there. Quinn says that there is an RHIB anchored on the other side of the island. With no other options, he takes the boat to Abu Simbel to find the control room there.
