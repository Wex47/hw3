The Super Shotgun , or Boomstick , is a double-barreled shotgun that appears in Serious Sam's Bogus Detour . It functions just like the Double Barrel Coach Gun from the main series.

Overview

The super shotgun is a side-by-side shotgun that uses 12-gauge shells as ammunition. Like the coach gun, the super shotgun fires two shells each time the fire key is pressed. It deals very high damage at close range, though it scatters the pellets too much due to the shotgun's high spread. Thus, it is only useful for dealing high damage to single tough enemies or quickly wiping out groups of weak enemies clustered together.

Tactics

  • The super shotgun is an excellent weapon against individual or small groups of Beheaded Kamikazes , Gnaars , Kleer Skeletons and Obsidian Elite Kamikazes , though its rate of fire is not recommended against larger groups of enemies.
  • The weapon is effective against Orc Captains , though it is best to dash or circle around him whilst firing.
  • With the Accurate Boomsticks upgrade, the super shotgun becomes more useful at longer range, giving it roughly the same range as the regular shotgun .
