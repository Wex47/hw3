Metropolis is the ninth level of Serious Sam: The First Encounter , Serious Sam: Xbox , Serious Sam HD: The First Encounter , Serious Sam VR: The First Encounter , and Serious Sam Classics: Revolution .

Plot

Sam , now in the metropolitan area of Memphis , must make his way to the Temple of Ptah, where an ancient Sirian artifact is located, and hopefully, it will aid him in his mission.

Secrets

  1. After exiting the village area with the four scarabs, you pass through a doorway into a long alleyway with a spike pit further on. Before going down the steps to start the run, turn right and you'll see a differently-coloured wall beside the doorway. Blow it up with a rocket to reveal a secret ammo depot containing an SBC Cannon and some cannonballs .

Trivia

  • The Lava Golem boss spawns in the end of this level instead of in the end of the Alley of the Sphinxes in Serious Sam: Xbox .
  • In the classic version of the game, this level has bluish lighting and the sky is more clouded when playing on Serious difficulty . In the HD and VR versions, in co-op, Metropolis has cloudy dark weather and it's raining when playing on Serious difficulty.
