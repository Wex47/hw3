The Orc Scientist is a passive type of Orc that appears in Serious Sam's Bogus Detour .

Overview

Orc Scientists are responsible for developing and maintaining the technology for the Mental-Altani Corporation, such as the advanced Starskate system. They also worked on the Ooze project and attempted to enchance some of Mental 's units with it, like the Infected Gnaar .

Occurrence

Orc Scientists are frequently found on the Orc's Moon base , where they perform biological experiments with the ooze in an attempt to enhance Mental 's units and conduct research on Mental-Altani technology.

In Illogical Biological , there is an Orc Scientist who wears a blue lab coat called Daniel Platt . Killing him will drop the certified eye, which can be used to access his secret lab.

During the boss fight with Tech-X and PRIME-209 , Orc Scientists are teleported in by the Director to repair the mechs after they have taken sufficient damage.

Behavior and skills

Like the humans encountered earlier in the game, Orc Scientists are passive and will not attack the player, instead running away from them and avoiding conflict whenever possible.
