Nightmare Caves is the fifth level of Serious Sam: Tormental .

Plot

The heroes have arrived now in a darker part of Mental 's mind in a cave-type environment filled with bats , snakes , spiders and where unstable thoughts run rampant. They need to get to the end of the dungeon where will be forced to deal with a much worser nightmare: the Arachnithid Matriarch .
