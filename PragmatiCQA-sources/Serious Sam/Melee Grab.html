The Melee Grab is a unique feature introduced in Serious Sam 3: BFE and Serious Sam 4 .

Overview

The melee grab feature is initiated when an enemy is close enough and an on-screen prompt to press the 'use' key (E by default). This move will always kill the intended target and sometimes leaves Sam holding a 'trophy' which is usually some part of the enemy's body. The trophy can be dropped or thrown, although using it as a thrown projectile is fairly weak. The melee grab only works on certain types of enemies. Other enemies are either invulnerable to the move or too large (no prompt will appear to execute a melee grab).

In Serious Sam 3 , when it is Christmas, the player can "decorate" the Christmas tree that appears on every level with each type of trophy, including a Gnaar's eye, a Kleer's skull, and a Rocketeer's heart. When the tree has been decorated, it will light up and the Christmas theme from Serious Sam: The Second Encounter will play.

In Serious Sam 4 , the feature returns, but the mechanics are slightly different compared to Serious Sam 3 . Most finishing moves are done with the Combat Knife , where Sam will grab the enemy and stab it in the head, but if the player has acquired "The Bigger They Are" and "The Harder They Fall" S.A.M. skills, they can perform unique finishing moves on larger enemies.

Effect

Serious Sam 3: BFE

In Serious Sam 3 , the following effects take place when using the melee grab:

  • Beheaded Rocketeer : depending on the weapon Sam has equipped, Sam will either kick the enemy away, stunning it temporarily, or rip its heart out of its chest, leaving Sam with a held trophy.
  • Cloned Soldier : depending on the weapon Sam has equipped, Sam will either kick the enemy away, stunning it temporarily or snap its neck.
  • Gnaar : this move will allow Sam to grab the Gnaar and rip out its eyeball, leaving Sam with a held trophy.
  • Hatchling Antaresian Spider : Sam will perform a stomp that kills the spider instantly. This move is very fast and can be repeated in quick succession.
  • Juvenile Antaresian Spider : Sam will rip off the spider's carapace, killing it instantly.
  • Kleer Skeleton ; Sam will sidestep and grab the Kleer in a headlock and promptly pull the Kleer's head off, leaving Sam with a held trophy.
  • Hatchling Arachnoid ; Sam will break the Hatchling's neck, which instantly kills it. This move cannot be performed on the adult Arachnoid.
  • Scrapjack ; this move can only be performed if the Scrapjack is stunned (he appears to be hunched over, but still standing). Sam will rip off Scrapjack's faceplate and kiss it, leaving Sam with a held trophy.

Serious Sam 4

When using the melee grab, the following may happen:

  • Aurigan Haemovore : Sam will take out a woooden stake and impale it in the Haemovore's heart, instantly killing it.
  • Beheaded Bomber : depending on the weapon Sam is equipped with, he will either kick the enemy away or rip its heart out.
  • Beheaded Firecracker : Sam will either kick the enemy away or rip its heart out.
  • Beheaded Rocketeer: Sam will either kick the Rocketeer away or rip its heart out.
  • Gnaar: Sam, using his knife, will stab the Gnaar in the eye with it.
  • Kleer Skeleton: Sam will grab the Kleer's skull and rip apart its head by snapping its jaws.
  • Octanian Commander : Sam will kick the enemy away or stab it in the head.
  • Octanian Gamma Trooper : Sam will kick the enemy away or stab it in the head.
  • Octanian Sniper : Sam will kick the enemy away or stab it in the head.
  • Processed Anthropolyp : Sam will stab it in the head.
  • Space Mummy : Sam will grab his knife and stab it in the head.
