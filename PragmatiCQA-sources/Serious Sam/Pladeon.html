Overview

Pladeon is a dry desert planet similar in appearance to Mars, but has a diameter more similar to that of Earth . The planet also has 5 moons.

Pladeon is said to be rich in minerals, and was once the capital of a great industrial civilization, but since the Mental War , only scattered tribes and half-buried ruins remain of its former glory.

Appearances

Serious Sam VR: The Last Hope

Serious Sam visits Pladeon soon after the Battlecruiser Saratoga is sent out of orbit due to a magnetic anomaly originating from the planet. There, he deals with Mental 's forces in an industrial area before heading to the botanical gardens.

Levels

There are four levels set on Pladeon:

  • Pladeonic Oil Works
  • Pladonis Botanical Gardens
  • Highway to Hell
  • Core's Nest
