Large Armor is an armor pickup that appears in the Serious Sam series.

Overview

Depending on the game being played, medium armor will give the player betwen 50 and 100 armor points. Unlike Super Armor , it does not go beyond 100 armor points.

Appearances

Large armor is usually found scattered throughout levels. It is usually placed in "breather" rooms between hordes and as a booby-trapped item. It also appears in secrets, but usually mixed in with other items, like ammo or health.

Behind the scenes

  • Large armor had a completely different model in the Alpha phase of Serious Sam 1 . It used a golden roman-style helmet similarly to what the Small Armor in The Second Encounter uses. In Test 1 , it used the Extra Large Armor Test 1 model, but with a brown-colored texture.
