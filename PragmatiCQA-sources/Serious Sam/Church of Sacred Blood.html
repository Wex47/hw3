The Church of Sacred Blood , also known as the Grand Cathedral , was a Gothic-style European cathedral which is where the Holy Grail is kept. It was also the location of the Sirian backup spaceship .

Overview

Design

The Church of Sacred Blood is a large cathedral that was constructed sometime in the 12th century by a clan of wizards known as the Arc-Al-Magi , the original keepers of the Holy Grail .

The most recognizable features are the two tall towers that stand out from the rest of the exterior. The cathedral also has some walls and watchtowers, which makes the cathedral appear to be more similar to a castle than a church, and, as the design is not very inspired; it seems like a mix of some European Gothic cathedrals.

Outside the entrance to the cathedral are two statues of Fiendian Reptiloid Demons .

Appearances

Serious Sam: The Second Encounter

After crashing the SSS Centerprice in Sierra de Chiapas , Sam discovers that the Sirians had left a backup ship in the cathedral in case the original ship was destroyed, but this happened to be in another historical period: medieval Europe.

To get there, the Sirians left a Time-Lock with every great civilization, so Sam has to go through two Time-Locks to get to the 12th century. In the Citadel , Sam discovers that to activate the backup rocket in the cathedral, he needs the Holy Grail. The Grail was originally located at the Ice Castle, but by the time Sam gets to the castle, he finds out that Mental has stolen it and placed it within the cathedral, where he had Mordekai the Summoner guard it.

Here, Sam makes it through the Corridor of Death (a castle near the cathedral). As Mental is aware of the Grail's importance to Sam, he sends in his toughest forces against Sam, but he slaughters all of them and finally makes it to the inner garden of the church, where Mordekai awaits him.

After a final standoff, Sam finally gets hold of the Holy Grail, and after that, a phone rings. Sam answers it, and confesses his "sins" to Mental, and sarcastically remarks how he was sorry for annihilating his forces. Sam then threatens Mental, saying that he is going to finish him once and for all. Finally, Sam activates the backup rocket, and departs to Sirius .
