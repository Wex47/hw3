Dan Huffington (also known as Huff ) is a playable character who appears in Serious Sam: Double D: XXL .

Overview

Huff is a Caucasian man in a torn yellow shirt with a tie and sunglasses. He first appeared in the Gunstacker video where he tells players about how the gunstacker changes the style they play video games. He later appeared in a trailer of Serious Sam: Double D: XXL , where he tells about the changes and new features added in the game. He also appears in a few promos and screenshots of the game.

Huff can be added as the second player by pressing the A button (İn Xbox). He can be played in single-player modes, though the player needs to finish the campaign first to unlock him.

In-game, Huff does not have any changes from Sam beside being cosmetic. The only noticeable change is the quotes. His quotes are usually humorous, and even some of them contain curse words (which can be censored or uncensored in the game settings).
