Overview

Among the drifting schema horde in Mental 's mind, the Gonk is arguably the most straightforward of them all. Whilst Mental brainstorms other diabolical creatures, this simple quadruped creature is essentially fodder in comparison to the other schemas.

Gonks are encountered in groups in Mental Woods . When the player loops for the first time and enters Mental Woods V2 , they appear much more frequently. An elite variant also has a chance to spawn in this level, becoming more common in Mental Woods V2 and Mental Woods V3 .

Behavior and skills

When the Gonk sees the player, it starts moving in random directions, and when it has the chance to attack, it fires a single red bullet at them. Despite its cyclopic vision, it has fairly decent eyesight and is capable of spotting intruders from an impressive distance. This doesn't translate to their depth perception, and their projectiles can be easily be avoided by jumping or sidestepping. The elite variant fires two projectiles instead of one and has slightly more health.

Tactics

  • The Gonk is a very weak enemy, as it has low health and thus can be easily killed with even basic weapons, such as the Pistol ; even groups of them can be taken out easily. Their main threat is that their projectiles deal relatively high damage, so the player should try to avoid them by sidestepping.
  • When encountered with other enemies, Gonks are a low-priority threat, as their low health means the player can easily kill them in the crossfire between the player and other enemies, and their projectiles can be easily dodged.

Trivia

  • Gonks are named after novelty toys originating from the United Kingdom in the 1960s.
