The Octanian Gamma Trooper is a cybernetically-controlled soldier and the most commonly encountered Octanian infantry. They are equipped with military armor and equipment and are usually led by Octanian Commanders . They appear as common enemies in Serious Sam 4 and Serious Sam: Siberian Mayhem , replacing the Cloned Soldiers from Serious Sam 3: BFE .

Overview

The Gamma Trooper belongs to a race of humanoid creatures originating from the Sigma Octantis system, and have allied themselves with Mental during the Tunguska Offensive as part of his goal to eradicate knowledge from the universe. As their name suggests, they are armed with gamma-ray laser weapons, with which they are linked to via their species' cybernetic parasites. Their weapons are of particular religious significance to them, as they symbolize the cleansing fire with which Mental will purify the universe.

Like other Octanian soldiers, Gamma Troopers are equipped with standard-issue body armor, which provides them with moderate to substantial protection in combat. They also wear respirators and goggles to help them survive Earth 's environment.

Application

Gamma Troopers are the most common Octanian soldiers, and are usually seen in combat zones or towns or cities that have been overrun by Mental's forces.

In the event of a threat, they are sent out to patrol these areas from any enemies that enter it. They are found almost anywhere, from large urban cities to the rural countryside. In the final level, From Earth With Love , Gamma Troopers are seen in their highest numbers while being spawned by the Legion System .

Combat

Gamma Troopers usually work in squads and are often led by Commanders in small skirmishes, but when dealing with a threat to Mental's forces, such as Sam , there can be many Gamma Troopers encountered at once.

In combat situations, they will take cover behind anything they can find in their environment, such as behind a car or sandbags. If there is a threat nearby, they will patrol the area to search for the enemy, aiming their weapons out of cover. When directly confronting the target, they will strafe sideways to avoid suppressive fire and will attempt to flank them. In cover, they will pop out of cover occasionally to fire at the target.

Troopers use their gamma rifles as their main weapon, though it is not entirely accurate nor is it powerful, as Octanian rifles aren't perfectly adjusted to Earth conditions and therefore the individual beams only do limited amounts of damage. They can, however, deal moderate damage to a target that is stationary. If the target gets within point blank range of a Gamma Trooper, he will kick them or use his rifle as a melee weapon. Outside of combat, Gamma Troopers will occasionally patrol the streets in urban environments or the countryside to keep a look out for trouble.

Tactics

  • Lasers fired by from the Trooper's gun are not very accurate, and can be easiy be avoided by sidestepping or hiding behind cover.
  • These enemies are fairly weak: three shots from the SOP38 Pistol or a single shot from the 12 Gauge Pump-Action Shotgun is all it takes to kill one. A single shot to their head from the pistol can kill one instantly.
  • The pump-action shotgun or M29 Infantry Assault Rifle is effective against medium or large groups of Gamma Troopers.
  • Gamma Troopers are low-priority targets, even on higher difficulties. In groups though, they can become a nuisance if not taken out soon enough.

Trivia

  • Gamma Troopers, along with other Octanian soldiers, appear to have a particular scent which is reminiscent to that of seafood. This is noted by both Father Mikhail and Sam .
  • Early concept designs show the Gamma Trooper and other Octanian soldiers in different outfits and carrying different kinds of weapons. One of these weapons appears to be an assault rifle.
