Overview

The Earth Giant is a large monster that is made out of multiple large rocks. Its body glows green, and it has a large amount of grass on its left arm. The Earth Giant doesn't have legs, and as such it moves by crawling with its right arm.

Appearances

The Earth Giant is fought as a boss in the final level of the game, The Final Breath .

Behavior and skills

The Earth Giant has two attacks; its first attack is to fire several projectiles from its mouth. After firing, it then grabs a rock from the ground and launches it at the target. Both of these attacks can deal a high amount of damage.

If the Earth Giant is not killed quickly and comes within close range of the player, it will crush them with its right hand, which kills them instantly.

When the Earth Giant is killed, its left arm wil break and its body will split in half.

Tactics

  • The Earth Giant's projectiles can be avoided by shooting them.
  • The A-24 Devastator , SBC Cannon and XPML21 Rocket Launcher are effective and strong weapons against the Earth Giant, as they can deal a large amount of damage to it.
  • An useful strategy is to use a Serious Damage power-up (if the player has one) and the weapons mentioned above. This way, the player can easily kill the Earth Giant as soon as it appears.
