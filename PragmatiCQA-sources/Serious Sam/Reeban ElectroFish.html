Overview

The Reeban Electro-Fish is an aquatic piranha-like fish from the planet Reeb . They are greenish-orange in color, and have two large eyes and a gaping mouth that reveals rows of many sharp teeth.

Being aquatic enemies, Electro-Fish are always found where there is water. They are common in Serious Sam: The First Encounter , appearing from Valley of the Kings onwards. In Serious Sam: The Second Encounter , they first appear in Sierra de Chiapas and make their final appearance in The Citadel . Electro-Fish of various sizes also appear in the "secret yard of strange heads" in The City of the Gods .

The Electro-Fish reappears in Serious Sam: Siberian Mayhem , albeit only in a secret where it appears in a small hole on an icy lake in Siberiade . Because the hole is deep and the player runs out of oxygen very quickly, there is little time for the player to return to the surface without dying after encountering the Electro-Fish.

Behavior and skills

On their home planet, the Electro-Fish is an aggressive predator that preys on smaller underwater species. They have an effective camouflage that helps to conceal themselves. This way, they can sneak up on and disorient their target with electrical attacks. If an Electro-Fish is out of water and stays on the ground for too long, it will eventually die from a lack of oxygen. It is possible for the Electro-Fish to knock the player back without damaging them.

Tactics

  • Electro-Fishes can attack the player even if they are on the ground if they are very close to one.
  • Due to the ability of their electric attacks, this can make the Electro-Fish very dangerous in groups, as a constant barrage of the electric shocks will disorient the player's vision, making it difficult for the player to fight back without losing a lot of health and/or armor, especially on higher difficulties.
    • In the classic versions of the game, the Electro-Fish's electric shock can deal more than the standard 15 base damage and even gib the player if it kills them.
  • Both individual Electro-Fish and groups of them can easily be taken out with the Thompson submachine gun , XM214-A Minigun , and XL2 Lasergun . Their good rate of fire allows the player to quickly eliminate them without wasting ammo.
  • Single Electro-Fish can be taken out with the 12 Gauge Pump-Action Shotgun with ease. It usually takes about one or two shots to kill an Electro-Fish with it.
  • If a few Electro-Fishes are grouped together, a rocket or grenade can eliminate the entire group in one hit.
  • The XOP Flamethrower must not be used since they are encountered in water and the flamethrower would obviously be ineffective.
  • In Serious Sam: Siberian Mayhem custom levels featuring the Electro-Fish as an enemy, the Autoshotgun , A-24 Devastator , and TCC2 "Perun" Crossbow are highly effective against large groups of Electro-Fish.

Trivia

  • The Electro-Fish got its name from Croteam CEO Roman Ribarić's nickname - "Riba" (pronounced reeba). "Riba" is also the Croatian word for fish.
  • The Electro-Fish can be gibbed and gib the player in Serious Sam 1 and Serious Sam Classics: Revolution but not the HD or VR remakes or in any subsequent releases.
