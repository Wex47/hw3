Extra Large Health ( Mega Heart in Serious Sam: Double D ) is a healing item in the Serious Sam series.

Overview

Extra large health is a healing item that gives the player the highest amount of health points out of any healing item in the series. When picked up, extra large health always gives the player 100 health points, though in Serious Sam: Xbox and Serious Sam: Next Encounter on higher difficulties, the player's health will decrease by 1 HP per second after picking up extra large health. Thus, strategy is required before grabbing extra large health.

Health pickups that appear in Serious Sam VR: The Last Hope use the extra large health model from Serious Sam HD , but they only provide a random amount of health rather than 100.

Appearances

Extra large health is the rarest healing item in the series, though they can be found occasionally. They are usually found in secret areas such as in Serious Sam: The First Encounter in Tomb of Ramses III , which holds 2 of them, both of which are secrets. Sometimes it is found usually when a large battle is following. Otherwise, they mostly trigger traps, such as a lot of Kleer Skeletons rushing at the player.
