Miller was a pilot working for the Earth Defense Force . He and his co-pilot flew an UH-60 Black Hawk that transported team Alpha to Cairo so they could extract professor Hermann Stein from a museum .

Biography

Not much is known about Miller's past, though at some point in his life he trained to be a helicopter pilot for the EDF as they fought against Mental 's army.

Appearances

Serious Sam 3: BFE

Miller and his co-pilot are first seen flying their UH-60 Black Hawk into the now-abandoned city of Cairo , where they narrowly avoid being devoured by a Sandwhale . As they make their way into the city, Miller sees rockets being fire directly at his chopper. As more rockets appear, Miller breaks right in hopes of reducing the chances of being hit, though one of the rockets hits the tail of the helicopter and chucking Sam out of due to not wearing a seatbelt.

After the helicopter goes down, it can be implied that Miller either dies from the impact or is killed my Mental's Horde moments later.

Trivia

  • Miller was the only pilot that flew the helicopter with a co-pilot.
