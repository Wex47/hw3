Serious Speed is a power-up that appears in the Serious Sam series .

Overview

When picked up, this item drastically increases the player's movement speed for 20 seconds in Serious Sam: The Second Encounter , 25 seconds in Serious Sam 2 , and 30 seconds in Serious Sam's Bogus Detour .

In Serious Sam: The Second Encounter , Serious Sam: Xbox , Serious Sam HD: The Second Encounter , Serious Sam VR: The Second Encounter and Serious Sam Classics: Revolution , serious speed usually appears in secrets, during boss fights or in large open environments. One such level is Ziggurat . In The Grand Cathedral , it is found in the middle of the field with Serious Damage and respawns approximately 2 minutes after the player picks it up.

In Serious Sam 2 , it is found in objects like crates and lightposts.

In Serious Sam's Bogus Detour , serious speed is often found among other power-ups or before a large fight begins.

Tactics

  • Serious speed can let the player circle strafe enemies while attacking them. This lets the player easily avoid attacks and constantly harm enemies. However, when the player hears a beep, they should make sure that they're in a safe location before its power runs out. Otherwise, the player might end up in a bad position when it runs out.
  • In Serious Sam: The Random Encounter , make sure to preserve serious speed for the Highlander and Major Mechanoid . The player should also use it when they are close to death and enemies are about to impact with them, or if there are too many projectiles.

Trivia

  • Serious speed is the shortest lasting power-up in the series.
