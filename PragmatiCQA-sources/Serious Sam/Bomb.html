Bomb is a major character featured in Serious Sam: Tormental . He is one of the four playable characters available in the game.

Biography

Bomb is a small anthropomorphic bomb with yellow eyes, white gloves, red boots and a fuse on his head. How he ended up in Mental 's mind is not fully understood, but he's been keeping to himself by pretty much blowing up everything he comes across. He claims to have worked in construction, and likes horticulture and is a fan of thrash metal.

Appearances

Serious Sam: Tormental

Bomb is one of the characters who has to be rescued from inside Mental's mind. He appears in a locked dungeon in Sea of Thoughts , and it costs several keys to unlock his prison. Once he has been rescued, the player can switch to play as him in the Frontal Lobe .

Personality and skills

Bomb has a confident personality and has a tendency to make puns about explosives. Like other playable characters, he has a special ability, which is activated by pressing the jump button. Pressing the key allows the player to remotely detonate any bombs inside the room. The bombs do not have to be in his line of sight, allowing him to detonate them behind corners or walls. Enemies also drop bombs upon death, which can either be useful or dangerous to the player, depending on the situation.

Because of his ability, it makes it easy to eliminate tough enemies and deal high damage to bosses. However, Bomb has half as much health as NETRICSA and Sam , which makes him vulnerable to enemy attacks as well as the bombs that he detonates.
