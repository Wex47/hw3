Overview

The holodecoy is a hologram emitter designed by the Earth Defense Force . It is used by EDF personnel to communicate with others from far distances. Gottlieb Kiesel can be seen using one in the briefing room after the Alien Artifact Acquisition squad arrive at the EDF base after leaving Rome.

The device can also be used in combat situations to distract enemy forces. When deployed, the holodecoy will display a hologram of Sam and move around in random directions for a short period of time. After its timer expires, it will explode. Any enemies in the vicinity will attack the decoy instead of the user. It has been optimized for maximum annoyance so that even the smarter members of Mental's Horde will be irritated enough to attack it.

Appearances

The holodecoy is a common gadget that can often be found in secrets as a reward for completing side quests.

Tactics

  • Ideally, the holodecoy should be used in situations where the player is being overwhelmed or cornered by enemies. When the enemies are distracted by it, finish them off before it explodes.
  • The holodecoy works well with other gadgets, such as Hacked Drones and Psychotropic Grenades .
