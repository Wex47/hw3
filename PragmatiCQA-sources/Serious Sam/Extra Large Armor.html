Extra Large Armor ( Mega Vest in Serious Sam: Double D ) is an item that gives the player 100 armor points when picked up. The item appears in many Serious Sam games.

Overview

When picked up, extra large armor gives the player 100 armor points. In most games, it will not exceed the 100 normal armor cap, though it can in Serious Sam 3: BFE , Serious Sam 3 VR: BFE , Serious Sam 4 and Serious Sam: Siberian Mayhem .

In Serious Sam: Siberian Mayhem , two extra large armor pickups will instead provide 200 armor points rather than the standard 100. The first can be found after completing the Dark Souls easter egg secret in Siberiade , while the other can be found in Crime And Punishment just before the boss fight against Howard Brand .

Appearances

Extra large armor is usually given to the player before a large fight occurs, which is a big hint to the player that they're going to be involved in a big fight. It also appears between waves in certain difficult levels, such as Metropolis . When picked up, it triggers the next wave.

It can also be found in secrets as often as Super Armor , sometimes with power-ups.

Behind the scenes

  • Extra large armor had a completely different model in the Alpha version of Serious Sam 1 , appearing as a silver and gold knight cuirass.
