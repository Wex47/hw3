Overview

The Red Widow is a mechanical creature of an ancient schema of Mental 's, likely with some ideas incorporated from the Arachnithid. It's basically a gun turret with legs that resembles a spider. The Red Widow is based on one of the first ideas Mental had to create soldiers for his army. Back when Mental was more sane, he thought up of a simple idea by having a gun turret placed on legs and program it to kill everything in its path and let it loose.

Since then, this idea has gone through many iterations and the resulting Red Widow is the latest one. The idea being a heavy turret on many legs. The real-life projections of this schema are many different versions of the Bio-mechanoids , which are similarly iterated on constantly, changing shape with every new version.

They are only encountered in Nightmare Caves . An elite version of the Red Widow exists that has more health. It has a high chance to spawn when the player loops for the first time.

Behavior and skills

When the Red Widow sees the target, it will warm up and fired rapid burst of bullets. It takes time for the Widow to charge its bullets again, so once it unleashes its barrage, it won't change direction until it has fired all its bullets. Once the Widow has taken enough damage, the player can finish it off by performing a brutal kill when its body flashes yellow.

Tactics

  • The Red Widow has high health, so serious weapons are recommended to use against it. The Pistol can be used against the Widow, but it takes a longer time to kill it unless the player has good mods for it.
  • With the pistol, it is recommended to have upgrades such as the burst-fire, flaming bullets of freezing bullets to slow it down and finish it off.
  • The Freezegun is a useful weapon for slowing down the Widow before it has the time to fire its projectiles.
  • The Bomber , Grenade Launcher and Rocket are effective weapons for damaging Red Widows from a safe distance and destroying their bullets at close range.
  • The Sledgehammer should not be used against the Widow, as it can fire projectiles at the player while they are trying to get close to it, which can deal a lot of damage. The latter should be only used as a last resort.
  • Another way of killing Red Widows is by activating the bombs in the area and letting the Spider get too close to them or whacking the bombs towards them with the sledgehammer. When the bomb explodes, it can deal a large amount of damage to the Widow and destroy their bullets as well.
  • When encountered with other enemies, the Red Widow is a high-priority threat, as the large amount of projectiles it fires gives the player much less room to dodge other enemies. It should be one of the first enemies targeted in a fight.
