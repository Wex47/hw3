The ATV is a land vehicle that was cut from Serious Sam 2 during development, but appears as a functioning vehicle in Serious Sam 4 .

Overview

Serious Sam 2

No stats nor code for the ATV exist, so it is impossible to know how the ATV behaved. The only sign that the ATV existed at one point is that its HUD image can be found in the game's files. Why it was cut is unknown. On the Steam release, screnshots from the beta of Serious Sam 2 were uploaded. One of the photos shows three ATVs being used by Orc Gunners and one Orc Commander.

Serious Sam 4

This all-terrain vehicle appears to be powered by either a two-stroke or four-stroke engine . Like the combine harvester and motorcycle , it has 100 health and can be used to perform drive-bys with any weapon the player has equipped, though they cannot use the scope when using the A-24 Devastator or RAPTOR Sniper Rifle . The ATV will take damage from enemy projeciles or explosions, and will also take damage from any of the player's weapons.

The ATV has a slower speed than that of the motorbike, but is able to retain its speed on rough terrain and off-road environments.

Appearances

Serious Sam 4

The ATV appears in One for the Road , which Sam uses to drive through the French countryside and to the city Carcassonne to meet up with Kenny . He ditches the vehicle once he establishes contact with him. This is the only appearance of the vehicle in the game.

Behind the scenes

  • In Serious Sam 2 , the ATV was supposed to be seen at the end of the level Unreal Wasteland .
  • An unused Serious Sam 2 screenshot shows Sam wielding Uzis on an ATV, suggesting that the player would be able to use weapons while driving the ATV.

Trivia

  • The Alien Artifact Acquisition logo can be seen on the front and back of the ATV.
  • The words "Viva la Resistance" can be seen on the ATV's dials. This is a reference to the level that takes place in Carcassonne.
