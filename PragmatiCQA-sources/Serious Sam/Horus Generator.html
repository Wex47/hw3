Overview

The Horus generator was a large, blue pyramid-shaped structure that was located underneath the Temple of Horus.

History

The Horus generator was constructed several thousand years by the Sirians soon after they visited Earth . The exact date of its construction is not known.

During the 21st centruy, strange constructions of Sirian origin were discovered underneath the shrine of Horus . In the excavation site was a gate made from a strange metallic material. This door was impenetrable to any known technology, and only four seals from the House of Life could open it.

Appearances

Serious Sam 3: BFE

Hellfire takes Sam to Karnak to activate the first generator, having revealed their locations in a secret chamber inside the Great Pyramid . Meanwhile, NETRICSA uploads some new information about the excavation site. Sam finds out that there the door underneath the shrine of Horus requires four seals which need to placed on the door to gain access.

After finding a keycard, Sam acquires all four Sirian artifacts and uses them to open the door. He takes an elevator-type platform down to an enormous area. At the bottom of the elevator, Sam comes across a hallway that takes him to another door. Sam enters the next room, which contains a massive pyramid-shaped object, which presumably powers the generator. Sam waves the Sirian Mutilator over the console, which activates the generator. After turning it on, Quinn calls Sam and is amazed that that the first generator for the Time-Lock is back online, as humans have never been able to activate Sirian technology before. She tells him that the second plasma generator is over in Luxor. Sam then proceeds to Luxor to activate it.
