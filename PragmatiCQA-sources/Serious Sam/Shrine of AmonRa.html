Overview

The Shrine of Amon-Ra is a valuable item that once belonged to the people of Thebes . It also has a connection to the Sirians , and is used for summoning the SSS Centerprice to Egypt.

History

The Shrine of Amon-Ra was originally kept by the Thebians, which they used to remain in contact with the Sirians. To contact the Sirians, the people of Thebes would perform a ritual; this involved the Amon-Ra sign and would activate a rising obelisk, which would trasmit a signal to the SSS Centerprice. Once the Sirians received the signal, they would come down from the skies in their ship and fly it over the Great Pyramid, iluminating it with a beam of light. After that, they would fly back up into space.

The riot

As the city of Thebes began to rise into power, the rulers of Memphis attempted to influence power and religion over the people of Thebes, but were not successful in doing so. As a result, the Memphis leaders staged a massive riot and stole the Sign of Amon-Ra, which they sealed away within the Temple of Ptah in their city. As the sacred item belonging to the Thebians was lost, they were never able to contact the Sirians again.

Appearances

Serious Sam: The First Encounter

The Amon-Ra item plays a significant part in the plot in The First Encounter . After collecting all four elements, Sam learns from NETRICSA that their is an important item kept inside the Temple of Ptah in Memphis. As it is crucial for his mission, he is forced to fight his way through Mental 's forces and traps that were laid by the Memphis leaders near the temple.

Making his way to the temple, Sam finds out that the Amon-Ra item was not in the temple at all, but instead it is located somewhere in the Grand Obelisk . After clearing his way through the city, Sam finally reaches the obelisk, but finds out that there is a Ram-God in possession of it. After Sam kills the beast, he takes the Amon-Ra item and makes his way to Thebes, where he uses the artifact to get the SSS Centerprice to Earth. With the spacehip on its way, NETRICSA tells Sam that it will arrive at the Great Pyramid of Giza . Getting there won't be easy, though, as Mental seems to already be aware of this. To stop Sam from boarding the ship, he sends his Great Admiral Ugh-Zan III to kill him.

As Sam is no match for this massive creature, he rushes into the Great Pyramid and activates the ship's systems with the Shrine of Amon-Ra. He places it on a pedestal and makes his way to the top of the pyramid, where he encounters Ugh-Zan once again.
