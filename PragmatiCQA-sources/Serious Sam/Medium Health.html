Medium Health ( Rejuvenative Flask in Serious Sam: Double D ) is a healing item that appears in the Serious Sam series .

Contents

  • 1 Overview
  • 2 Appearances
  • 4 List of appearances

Overview

Medium health is a healing item that, when picked up, grants the player 25 health points. It provides enough health that the player can withstand an extra hit or two, depending on how much armor the player has and the difficulty level being played, which is useful without overpowering the player.

Appearances

In most of its appearances, medium health is one of the most common healing items found throughout the games, and occasionally appears in secrets, usually with more than one in them.
