Overview

The treasure chest is a large treasure chest filled with gold and other valuables. Its point value depends on the game it is in; in Serious Sam: Xbox , it gives the player 50,000 points, while the Serious Sam 2 version gives the player 5000 points when picked up.

Occurrence

The treasure chest appears in single player and co-op modes in Serious Sam: Xbox and Serious Sam 2 , but can only be found in coin-op co-op mode in Serious Sam HD , Serious Sam VR , Serious Sam 3: BFE and Serious Sam 3 VR: BFE .

Its rarity depends on the game. In Serious Sam: Xbox , treasure chests are usually tucked into corners and semi-hidden alternate paths, while in Serious Sam 2 and Serious Sam: Next Encounter , they are only found in secrets.

Tactics

  • A treasure chest will give half of the points needed to get an Extra Life , making it very valuable. The player should be on the lookout for them if they need to get extra lives for whatever reason.
  • It is also very useful for those that're trying to get a high score, as the amount of points a treasure chest gives can make a significant impact on one's final score.
