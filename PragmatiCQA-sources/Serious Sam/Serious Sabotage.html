Serious Sabotage is a secret level in Serious Sam's Bogus Detour .

Plot

Sam has reached Mental-Altani 's secret communications island. It's here where the Orcs control all air traffic in the region. On the center of this island, there's a huge satellite dish. Sam needs to lower the energy field and disable the satellite dish. If this satellite dish is disabled, this will prevent the Orcs from sending in reinforcements from their multi-mission aircraft .

Secrets

  1. There is a small locked structure north of the structure where you disable the shield generator. Head inside the generator building and go to the area where you collect the jammer. There is a window through which you can shoot a broken piece of wall on the locked structure. Once it's broken, get the jammer and position it so it is aimed at the glowing floor tile inside the locked structure. It may be a little tricky to aim, but position the jammer on the very right side of the window and you should get it. Once you do, kill the Orc Captains and the Light Orc Zone Trooper , then head inside and collect the Auto Shotgun to trigger the secret.
