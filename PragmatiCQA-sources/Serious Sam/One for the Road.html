Plot

Sam manages to establish contact with Kenny , and must now venture further through the countryside to reach Carcassonne and meet up with him and the French Resistance.

Side objectives

Armed, Farmed, and Alarmed

Before the player reaches the quarry, they will come across some large containers and a small white shack to the right. Going to the shack and reading the note there will activate the side quest. Follow the road past the shack and get to the farm. When the player reaches the barn, a cutscene will play and the player will be able to obtain the FRPCL along with its ammunition.

La Route de L'enfer

Once on the ATV, keep driving towards the main objective and the player will get a distress call. If they don't get it, they can simply drive towards the large smoke trail in the distance. The player needs to continue to the smoke trail and they will come across the remains of a convey that was hit by Octanian Raiders . When they get there, if they open the crate next to one of the Humvees, they will obtain a H.E.A.R.T. gadget.
