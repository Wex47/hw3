Plot

Father Mikhail has discovered that the Holy Grail , resting within the Ark of the Covenant , was not in Rome, but actually in the French fortified city of Carcassonne . So, Mikhail and the rest of the Alien Artifact Acquisition squad board a plane and depart for France. On the way there, Brand tells the team they will be meeting up with the leader of the French Resistance, Michelle , once they arrive. Once they make it into France, a few Octanian Raiders show up and open fire on the plane. The team are forced to ditch the plane and bail out. Sam parachutes down to the ground and ends up stuck in the French countryside. To make things worse, he has lost radio contact with his team, so now he must get to Carcassonne on his own, in the hope that his friends will be there.

Side objectives

A Fun Guy

This side quest is actually easy to miss. When the player gets the bike, instead of going straight on, they need to drive in the opposite direction on the road until they get to some small ruins to the side and an audio log. Listen to the log to activate the side quest. Exploring the compound will take the player to a crate. Inside the crate are a few Rage Serums .

Southern Belle Tower

After getting the bike and driving down the main road, the player will come across an abandoned house. From the house, they will see a church in the distance. The player will get a call from Annabelle while driving towards the main objective, but if they don't, they can simply head to the tower and they will get to the side quest. Helping Annabelle and her friend Aubrey out will reward the player with the RAPTOR Sniper Rifle .

Droning On

After the combine harvester segment, before the exit, the player will see a radio tower off in the distance. Go there to activate the side quest. Completing it rewards the player with 4 Hacked Drones .

Trivia

  • This level has one of the largest maps in the entire Serious Sam series.
  • If the player rides the motorbike in the opposite direction of the main objective and stay on the road past the secondary objective, they will eventually reach a collapsed tunnel. In the tunnel is a small radio playing a distorted version of the Croteam theme song.
  • The achievement "Harvest Noon" is a pun on "harvest moon". Its description references the song " Don't Fear the Reaper " by Blue Oyster Cult .
