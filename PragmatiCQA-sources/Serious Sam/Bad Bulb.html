Bad Bulb is a major character who appears in Serious Sam: Tormental . Bad Bulb is one of the four playable in Serious Sam: Tormental .

Biography

He is a white light bulb with a single blue eye and mouth. He was once part of a dying civilization that attempted to create robotic life, but instead accidentally produced a race of sentient life bulbs. After a short period of enlightenment, Mental discovered this species and wiped them out with his armies. Thus, Bad Bulb's mind is the only remnant of his once-proud people.

Appearances

Serious Sam: Tormental

Like the other two characters, Bomb and NETRICSA , Bad Bulb has to rescued inside Mental's mind, where he is kept in a prison in Nightmare Caves . It costs 9 keys to unlock the prison. When Bad Bulb has been rescued, the player can switch to play as him anytime they wish in the Frontal Lobe , though sometimes he can be switched later on if he appears.

Personality and skills

Bad Bulb has the ability to electrocute enemies and damage them in a wide area with his energy gun, but he has to get close to them to be able to use it. This attack deals very rapid damage to the target and shock any enemies near it, which can be useful for taking out multiple enemies at once. Using it drain's Bad Bulb's HP, though. His HP can be refilled by collecting figments that are dropped from enemies.

Bad Bulb doesn't use the regular Pistol like the other characters do, instead he has a weapon called The Energy Gun which he uses to attack enemies. He is also equipped with a weaker version of the Sledgehammer .
