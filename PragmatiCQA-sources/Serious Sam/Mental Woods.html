Mental Woods is the third level of Serious Sam: Tormental , and the first level set inside Mental 's mind. After completing the Tomb stage, this level becomes the player's initial starting point.

Plot

After destroying the Mentaloptican and having his soul taken inside Mental 's mind, Sam must go traverse through a forest-like enviroment while he fights Mental's imagined creatures along the way.
