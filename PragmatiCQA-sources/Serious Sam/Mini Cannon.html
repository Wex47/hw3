The Mini Cannon is a weapon that appears in Serious Sam: Tormental .

Overview

The mini cannon is a weaker variant of the SBC Cannon from the main series, hence its name. When the player presses the fire key, the mini cannon launches a fast cannonball that can pierce through several enemies in a row. Unlike the cannon, the player cannot charge the mini cannon.

The mini cannon has a random chance of being obtained from blue Mental chests or it can be bought from shops.

Tactics

  • As the mini cannon does a lot of damage with its cannonballs, it is great for flushing out multiple weak enemies in a row. It can also be used for dealing damage to tough enemies as well.
  • Due to its low ammo count, it shouldn’t be used too often to conserve ammo.
