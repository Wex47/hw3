The Laser Pistol is an energy weapon that appears in Serious Sam VR: The Last Hope .

Overview

The laser pistol shoots a green laser each tine the fire key is used. The beams fired by the laser pistol are slightly less powerful than the ones fired by the XL2 Lasergun . Repeatedly pulling the trigger makes the laser pistol fire lasers much faster. Like other handguns, it has infinite ammo.

The laser pistol is similar to the SOP38 Pistol in most aspects, but it fires lasers instead of bullets. Unlike the SOP38, the laser pistol does not have to be reloaded.

Tactics

  • The pistol is an ideal weapon for taking out Kamikazes , Rocketeers , Cloned Soldiers , and Hatchling Antaresian Spiders .
  • When dual-wielding, the pistol is effective against medium-strength enemies, such as Juvenile Antaresian Spiders , Gnaars , Kleer Skeletons , small Lava Golems and Space Mummies . It also allows the player to quickly kill groups of weak enemies and small groups of medium-strength ones.
  • Its infinite ammo makes it useful for preserving ammo for other weapons.
