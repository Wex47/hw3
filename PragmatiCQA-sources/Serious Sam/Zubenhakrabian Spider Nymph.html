The Zubenhakrabian Spider Nymph is a newborn Zubenhakrabian Spider . They appear in very large swarms whenever they are encountered. They are introduced as a common enemy in Serious Sam 4 and Serious Sam: Siberian Mayhem .

Overview

The Nymph belongs to a race of arachnid-like aliens called Zubenhakrabian Spiders, which themselves originate from an unknown planet. They were originally docile creatures kept as exotic pets by the Sirians during their interstellar travels, and grew no larger than a few inches. It seems that Mental has enlarged these creatures by unknown means so he could use them in his army, which resulted in them becoming more aggressive.

Appearances

Spider Nymphs are very common enemies whenever they appear. They are first encountered in The Die is Cast , along with Adults, where they both appear in small to medium groups in the earlier levels. In the later portions of the game, they can be encountered in huge swarms consisting of up to a hundred individuals or more.

Behavior and skills

The Nymphs are smaller and weaker than Adult Zubenhakrabian Spiders , and also have thinner exoskeletons. The Spider Nymph's main form of attack is spitting an acid projectile at the target. At closer range, the Spider can deal a bite with its jaws, though this attack is weaker in comparision to that of the Adult's bite. Since they have a tendency to appear in swarms, they can easily overwhelm and/or corner the player. Like other arachnids, the Nymphs have adhesive pads in their feet, allowing them to cling to walls and ceilings.

Like Hatchling Antaresian Spiders , they can be squished by getting close to the Nymph and then pressing the 'Use' key. This will instantly kill the Nymph.

Tactics

  • Nymphs are the weakest enemies in the game, and can killed with one shot from any bullet-firing weapon, which makes it extremely easy to kill one. However, they never come alone, so the player should prepare accordingly when they encounter them.
  • Groups of Nymphs can be taken out with the 12 Gauge Pump-Action Shotgun , Double Barrel Coach Gun , M29 Infantry Assault Rifle and SOP38 Pistol , especially when dual-wielding them.
  • Don't let Nymphs get too close; groups of them can be dangerous.
  • If the player is being overwhelmed by Nymphs, they should use any available gadgets (the Hacked Drone or Time Warp are recommended) to quickly eliminate them.
  • Large groups of Nymphs can be destroyed with C-4 Demolition Charges , grenades or rockets.
  • The Nymph's acid spit can be avoided by sidestepping. It can also be destroyed by shooting at it in mid-air.

Trivia

  • The Nymphs, along with the Adults, use the same acid projectile fired by Antaresian Spiders .
