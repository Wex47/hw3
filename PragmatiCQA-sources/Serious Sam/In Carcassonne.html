Plot

Sam has finally made it to the castle, the location of the Ark of the Covenant and the Holy Grail . Now all he needs to do is a find a way deeper into the castle and meet up with his pals and continue the mission.

Side objectives

The Princess and The Pie

Progress through the castle until a cutscene plays, which shows a princess in a window who requests help from Sam. The player needs to make their way up to the tower where she is residing. Rescuing the princess rewards the player with a master key that can be used to unlock any doors leading to supply depots.

Portal Too

After the arena where the player firsts get the XL2 Lasergun , they will see the side quest marker on their right, which will lead to an enemy portal. Just like with the side quest in The Die Is Cast , this mission involves deactivating five transdimensional anchors to overload a portal. Completing the side quest awards the player with three Black Holes .

Trivia

  • The name of the first side quest is a play on the fairy tale The Princess and the Pea .
  • The name of the second side quest is a play on the popular puzzle-platform video game Portal 2 .
  • This level, along with Anathema Unto God , has the most secrets out of all levels in the entire series.
