The Ammo Crate is a crate filled with military supplies. Several varieties of crates exist, which either supply C-4 charges, rockets, or health and armor. The crate first appears in Serious Sam 3: BFE and then reappears in Serious Sam 3 VR: BFE and Serious Sam 4 .

Overview

Ammo crates appear to be have been manufactured by the Earth Defense Force and use an unknown generator technology to resupply the crates. The color of the crate depends on its contents; green boxes have rockets, dark red boxes have C-4 charges, white boxes contain health, ammo and armor, and black boxes contain a parachute. The white and black variants only appear in Serious Sam 4 .

When an ammo crate is opened, it will completely refill the specified ammo that's shown on the box. The box will then close, which can then be reopened to get more ammo. It never runs out of ammo. In co-op, it will refill the ammo type for everyone, no matter who opens it.

Ammo crates are often found near enemies that require explosives, such as the Khnum and Technopolyp .

In Serious Sam 4 , crates filled with C-4 first appear in Gates of Hell , and then scattered in the later levels. The rocket crates are much rarer than the latter, only appearing in From Earth With Love . The white and black crates are used during the fight with Ugh-Zan VI .

Tactics

  • When the player is fighting an enemy that's vulnerable to explosives, they should try to find an ammo crate and note its location in case they run out of explosives and need to grab more.
  • Ammo crates are also handy for filing up ammo to use for clearing out large groups of enemies, such as restocking on C-4 charges to lay traps and getting extra rocket ammo to clear out groups of enemies without having to use ammo for other weapons.
