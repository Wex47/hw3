Overview

The Battlecruiser Saratoga is a large space vessel that was constructed sometime before or during the early stages of the Mental War. During this time, it was piloted by Sam Stone , Captain of the Earth Defense Force. The Saratoga has many advanced weaponry and capabilities. During gameplay, the player can make use of the some of the Saratoga's abilities, such as deploying an orbital laser strike to eliminate large groups of enemies or a stun field to incapacitate enemies for a short period of time.

Appearances

Serious Sam VR: The Last Hope

The BC Saratoga is where the player first begins their missions. From the ship's bridge, they can select which planet to embark on and what missions to start. The player can also purchase power-ups, ammunition and weapon upgrades whilst on the ship.
