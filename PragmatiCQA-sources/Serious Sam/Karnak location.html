The Karnak Temple Complex , or simply Karnak , is a location in the Serious Sam series. It was the location of the first Time-Lock generator in Serious Sam 3: BFE .

Overview

Karnak was a mix of Ancient Egyptian buildings, such as temples, chapels, and shrines, located near Luxor in Egypt . In real life, it is one of the most frequently visited places in Egypt.

History

Construction at the complex began during the reign of Senusret I in the Middle Kingdom and continued into the Ptolemaic period , although most of the extant buildings date from the New Kingdom .

When the Sirians visited Earth and Egypt during their travels, they had the Egyptians build various shrines dedicated to the Egyptian gods, which is where the Sirians themselves placed four seals within. They also began construction of a massive plasma generator, the Horus Generator , underneath one of the larger temples. They hoped that one day, when humans became advanced enough, they would be able to activate the generator and in turn power up the Time-Lock .

Many millennia later, in the 22nd century, humans discovered strange constructions of Sirian origin underneath the Shrine of Horus.

Like many other places in the world, Karnak was abandoned during the Mental War . It remained surprisingly intact, along with other temples and complexes in Egypt.

Appearances

Serious Sam: The First Encounter

Karnak is the first location that Sam visits soon after he enters the city of Thebes . He explores the various temples and makes his way deeper into the city to find out the purpose of the valuable item that is currently in his possession. After digging through some clues the Sirians left behind in one of the temples NETRICSA tells Sam that the Sirians lived in Luxor, so she tells him to go there.

Serious Sam 3: BFE

Having found the secret chamber within the Great Pyramid of Giza , Sam is escorted by Hellfire via helicopter to activate the first power generator in Karnak. He fights Mental 's forces through the various temples and complexes until he makes it to the generator. With all four seals in tow, he opens the gate to the generator and turns it on before heading to Luxor to turn on the second generator.
