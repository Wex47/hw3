Overview

Arcadia Minor is a dwarf planet with a predominantly temperate climate and large bodies of water. It is home to an unknown species of aliens that abandoned technology in favor of a pacifist philosophy centered on the veneration of elemental spirits.

Appearances

Serious Sam VR: The Last Hope

While the Battlecruiser Saratoga is being resupplied, Sam arrives on the planet to discuss a diplomatic solution and get the High Priest 's people to fight against the invasion of their planet. He seems disinterested, and would rather focus on worshipping the spirits. This annoys Sam, so he tries again to convince him, but he refuses, which increases the tensions between the High Priest and Sam's offer.

Later on, the High Priest informs Sam about a particular elemental spirit that he worships. He tells him that this spirit came from the stars and was sent by the "One Infinite" ( Mental ) and it had laid dormant for millenia. Sam knew that this was no spirit, but in fact a huge elemental giant . Sam goes to face this threat and eventually destroys it along with any of Mental's remaining forces on the planet.
