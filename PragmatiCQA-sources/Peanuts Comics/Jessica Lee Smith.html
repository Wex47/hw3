Jessica Lee Smith (also known as Jessica Smith and Jessie Lee Smith) is a former child actress and singer who provided the voice of the Peanuts character, Lucy van Pelt in two animated television specials. Smith voiced Lucy in It's Flashbeagle, Charlie Brown in 1984 and in the 1985 television adaptation of the musical, .

She also appeared in a recurring role on Silver Spoons as a cheerleader named Lisa in 1983. Jessica Lee Smith was featured in The Selling Of Vince D’Angelo , written, directed and starring Danny DeVito. She was cast in the 3rd National Touring company of the musical Annie where she played the role of Pepper under contract for two years. In 1983 Smith sang the National Anthem at Shea Stadium for the NY Mets opening game.

Today Jessica Lee Smith can be found on Facebook and on her blog "Postcards From The Bottom: What Ever Happened To Jessica Lee Smith?".

She is an entrepreneur and a single mother of four children.
