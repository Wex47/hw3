The Mask Downs is a boss appearing in The Peanuts Movie: Snoopy's Grand Adventure , residing in the Peppermint Jungle .

Appearance

The Mask Downs takes her design from Peppermint Patty . She appears to be made of wood. Behind her body-less "face" is a curtain of leaves.

Combat

The Mask Downs attacks by throwing baseball-shaped boulders out of her mouth, referencing Peppermint Patty's baseball skills. However, she can be damaged if Snoopy launches a baseball in her direction (by running up against a catapult. If she gets hit, she will take damage. After taking three hits, the Mask Downs is defeated, and Peppermint Patty appears in the center of the arena so the player can complete the level.

Trivia

  • The Mask Downs is the one of only two bosses in The Peanuts Movie: Snoopy's Grand Adventure to attack from the background. The Muck Monster (who resides in the Underground Area) is the other.
