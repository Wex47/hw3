"Don't Be Anything Less than Everything You Can Be" is a song from Snoopy!!! The Musical . It was the eighteenth musical number in the 1983 London production, but is the first in the . It is sung by Sally Brown , Linus , Peppermint Patty and Charlie Brown . In the reprise, it is sung by the cast.
