Serena Berman is an American actress and voice actress. She attended school in Santa Monica and Los Angeles, California.

Berman voiced the Peanuts character, Lucy van Pelt in two animated television specials, Charlie Brown's Christmas Tales in 2002 and Lucy Must Be Traded, Charlie Brown in 2003. However, most of her voice work was for the Disney animated TV series W.I.T.C.H. , providing the voice for the character, Elyon Brown in forty-four episodes.
