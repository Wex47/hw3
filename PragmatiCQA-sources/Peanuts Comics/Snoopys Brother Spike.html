"Snoopy's Brother Spike" is the fifthteenth episode of The Charlie Brown and Snoopy Show . It premiered on September 21, 1985.

Shorts

"The Pelicans"

Peppermint Patty recruits Charlie Brown to sell popcorn to people watching her team's baseball games and to wear the costume of their team's pelican mascot.

"Great Pumpkin"

Linus , Sally and Snoopy await for the Great Pumpkin 's arrival while Peppermint Patty and Charlie Brown go bowling.

"Spike"

"Spike" is the longest short in the episode.

Snoopy's brother Spike comes for a visit, and is helped to get in shape to fight the cat next door .
