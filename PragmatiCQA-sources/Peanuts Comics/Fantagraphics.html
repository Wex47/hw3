Fantagraphics is a United States-based comic book publisher. They are known for publishing underground and alternative comics as well as the long-running magazine The Comics Journal . They began publication of The Complete Peanuts in 2004 and its companion volume Peanuts Every Sunday in 2012. In addition, they have produced a handful of other Peanuts publications:

Seasonal Peanuts

These treasury books are usually 64 pages and feature a theme based on a holiday or time of year:

  • Batter Up, Charlie Brown! (2014 )
  • Charlie Brown's Christmas Stocking (2012 )
  • Snoopy Vs. the Red Baron (2015 )
  • Snoopy's Thanksgiving (2014 )
  • A Valentine for Charlie Brown (2015 )
  • Waiting for the Great Pumpkin (2014 )

In preparation for the release of the first volume of The Complete Peanuts , they published the comic Unseen Peanuts for Free Comic Book Day 2004.
