"Fundamental Friend Dependability" is a song from the movie Snoopy, Come Home . It was written by Richard M. Sherman and Robert B. Sherman . It is sung by the character Clara when she takes Snoopy (whom she renames "Rex") into her house, bathes him and cross-dresses him for a tea party.
