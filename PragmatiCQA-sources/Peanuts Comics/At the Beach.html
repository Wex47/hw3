"At the Beach" is a song from the 1972 animated film Snoopy, Come Home . It was written by Richard M. Sherman and Robert B. Sherman . It plays during the film's opening scene, in which Snoopy , Charlie Brown , Linus and Peppermint Patty can be seen enjoying a day out at the beach.
