The Big Stompy is the boss of the Lunar Surface stage in The Peanuts Movie: Snoopy's Grand Adventure . It is a robot that slightly resembles Lucy van Pelt , who hosts the area.

Appearance

The Big Stompy is very large, about 20 times Snoopy's size. She takes her design from Lucy van Pelt 's dress, and her "legs" match Lucy's legs in shape. Her head also resembles lucy as a robot. She has a space helmet resembling a UFO in design.. The Big Stompy does not have arms.

Battle

The Big Stompy chases after the player. As she does so, she can change her speed. Her other attack is shooting lasers from his antenna. Her huge frame makes her nearly invincible. To defeat the boss, Snoopy has to make his way all the way to the right without getting trampled under the robot's feet. This done, Lucy van Pelt will scream at the Big Stompy, destroying the boss as she does so and allowing Snoopy to go to the Skies Above Paris, which Franklin Armstrong hosts.

Trivia

  • The Big Stompy's attack pattern is similar to the Porky Statue from Super Smash Bros. Brawl and the Heavy Lobster (before the main fight) in Kirby Super Star .
