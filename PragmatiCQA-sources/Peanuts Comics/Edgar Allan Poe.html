"Edgar Allan Poe" is a song from the stage show Snoopy!!! The Musical and the animated TV special of the . In the song, the children hope that their teacher will not call on them to ask them questions about the famous American poet, novelist and short story writer Edgar Allan Poe . The information which Linus gives about the writer's life in the song is correct, whereas the information which Charlie Brown gives is comically inaccurate. In the 2017 version, when Charlie Brown says Edgar Allan Poe wrote Black Sambo , was changed to Harry Potter .
