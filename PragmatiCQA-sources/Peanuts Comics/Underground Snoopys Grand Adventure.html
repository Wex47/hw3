Snoopy visits a subterranean area in the fifth world in the Story Mode of The Peanuts Movie: Snoopy's Grand Adventure on his journey to recover the Peanuts gang. It comes after the Skies Above Paris area, and its own completion unlocks the Melody Chasteau . "Pig-Pen" is trapped in this subterranean wasteland, which the Muck Monster (who must be defeated in order to complete the level) resides in.

Stages

  1. Dripping Ducts
  2. Soggy Station
  3. Dust Magnets
  4. Goopy Gutters
  5. Burrow of the Muck Monster (boss battle)

Trivia

  • This area is akin to many subterranean stages in the Super Mario franchise.
