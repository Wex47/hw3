Daniel Anderson is a former child actor who provided the voice of Linus van Pelt in the 1980 film Bon Voyage, Charlie Brown (And Don't Come Back!!) and in three Peanuts television specials: It's Your First Kiss, Charlie Brown (for which he also provided the voice of Schroeder ), You're the Greatest, Charlie Brown , and She's a Good Skate, Charlie Brown . He also provided Linus's voice for the short educational film Tooth Brushing , and for newly-recorded material on the Disney Read-Along adaptations of Peanuts cartoons.
