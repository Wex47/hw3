Other media

In the television commercials for MetLife , Snoopy often appears and is addressed by Charlie Brown and the gang as "The World Famous MetLife Representative." As such, Snoopy is typically responsible not only for selling policies, but also delivering claim payouts, arranging advertising, and participating in the company's health initiatives.
