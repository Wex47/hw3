"Pigpen Hoedown" is a song from the 1984 Peanuts TV special It's Flashbeagle, Charlie Brown . It is sung in G Major. The song's tempo is 128 beats per minute.

The song talks about how the character "Pig-Pen" can kick up dust when he dances.
