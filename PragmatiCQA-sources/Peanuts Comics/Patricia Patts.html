Patricia Patts (born on July 31, 1967 in California) is a former child actress who provided the voice of Peppermint Patty in the 1980 film Bon Voyage, Charlie Brown (And Don't Come Back!!) and two Peanuts television specials, You're the Greatest, Charlie Brown and She's a Good Skate, Charlie Brown .

Patts has guest-starred on the television shows Archie Bunker's Place and TV 101 . She appeared in the films and television movies A Place to Call Home , Student Exchange , For Keeps? and Party Line .
