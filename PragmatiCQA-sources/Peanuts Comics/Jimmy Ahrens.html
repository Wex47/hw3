Jimmy Ahrens is a former child actor who provided the voice of Marcie in the 1977 film Race for Your Life, Charlie Brown and in five Peanuts television specials, There's No Time for Love, Charlie Brown , A Charlie Brown Thanksgiving , It's a Mystery, Charlie Brown , It's the Easter Beagle, Charlie Brown and You're a Good Sport, Charlie Brown .
