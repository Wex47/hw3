NBC is a major American television network, which started as a radio network. It is sometimes nicknamed as the "Peacock Network" as a reference to the network's logo. It is the flagship property of and owned by Comcast through NBC Universal .

NBC debuted You're in the Super Bowl, Charlie Brown on January 18, 1994, due to NBC having the rights to air Super Bowl XXVIII . To date, it remains the only Peanuts animated television special to have premiered on the network. The live-action film The Big Stuffed Dog , the original television adaption of You're a Good Man, Charlie Brown and the ice shows Snoopy at the Ice Follies , Snoopy's International Ice Follies and Snoopy Directs the Ice Follies all premiered on NBC as well.

The Macy's Thanksgiving Day Parade airs annually on NBC.
