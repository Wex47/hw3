"Poor Sweet Baby" is a song from the stage production Snoopy!!! The Musical and the TV special of the It is sung by Peppermint Patty to Charlie Brown . It is sung from the viewpoint of the type of girl Charlie Brown would like: a girl who would cuddle up close to him, kiss his ear and whisper "Poor, sweet baby". At the end of the song, it is revealed that Peppermint Patty was being sarcastic. The song is based on the strip from April 8, 1973 .

The Little Red-Haired Girl makes a brief appearance during the song in the television version of Snoopy!!! The Musical .

Faith Hill performoned this song for a documentary and tribute special, Here's to You, Charlie Brown: 50 Great Years , broadcast on CBS on May 10, 2000
