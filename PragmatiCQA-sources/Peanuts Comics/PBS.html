PBS is an American private, nonprofit media enterprise owned by its member public television stations. In 2020, it got the rights to air some Peanuts specials after ABC lost the rights to air them.

PBS began airing A Charlie Brown Thanksgiving and A Charlie Brown Christmas in 2020. Even though Apple TV+ has the rights to Peanuts content, a deal with PBS enabled the company to air the two specials. In 2021, PBS also began airing It's the Great Pumpkin, Charlie Brown after it had been absent from American broadcast television the previous year. PBS has no plans to air Happy New Year, Charlie Brown! , Be My Valentine, Charlie Brown or It's the Easter Beagle, Charlie Brown .
