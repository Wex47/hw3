Jeremy Miller (born October 21, 1976 in Covina, California) is an actor who provided the voice of Linus van Pelt in the 1986 television special Happy New Year, Charlie Brown! and the second season of The Charlie Brown and Snoopy Show . Miller returned as Linus in 1988 in the special Snoopy!!! The Musical and in the episode The Birth of the Constitution from the series This is America, Charlie Brown . Miller also provided the voice of Linus for the Worlds of Wonder talking Snoopy doll and audiotapes.

Miller is well known for his role as Ben Seaver on the hit ABC sitcom Growing Pains . Since Growing Pains ended, Miller has mostly taken a hiatus from acting, but occasionally acts now and then.
