"Come on, Snoopy!" (original title: Allez, Snoopy! ) is episode 1a of the French-Italian animated TV series Peanuts , produced by Normaal animation and based on the comic strip Peanuts by Charles M. Schulz . . It first aired in the United States on October 2, 2014. The main theme of the episode is everything related to Snoopy .

Plot

Sally has a class show and tell with Snoopy. Lucy helps Snoopy confront his fear of the dark. Lucy wakes up hating the world and Snoopy antagonizes her. Charlie Brown complains about an unappreciative Snoopy. Snoopy gets in the way of Charlie Brown's TV time. Snoopy does not sit in the usual way when Charlie Brown gives the command "sit".

Shorts

The episode is made up of five shorts related to Snoopy:

  • La belle et la bête
  • Psycho pattes
  • L'amour du risque
  • Independance day
  • Télévision
