The Ace Elementary School Cheerleading Squad are five very minor female Peanuts characters who has never appeared in the strip, but appear in the TV special, It's Your First Kiss, Charlie Brown . They are five very beautiful girls who cheer on Snoopy and the Peanuts' football team during the homecoming game. They are all voiced by Gail Davis , Michelle Muller , Sarah Beach , Rosaline Rubens and Casey Carlson . They make reappearances in You're in the Super Bowl, Charlie Brown .

History

They are five very beautiful girls who are members of their school's cheerleading squad. Like any cheerleader, they wave their pom-poms around and cheer for their team to win. They shout traditional cheers such as "Two, Four, Six, Eight, Who Do We Appreciate?" and "Gimme me S!" They're mainly seen with Snoopy.

They make reappearances in You're in the Super Bowl, Charlie Brown , cheering on The Birds football team to win the game.

Physical appearance

Like with The Four Princesses , each girl has eyelashes, rosy cheeks and a pointed nose. They all wear matching long sleeve red shirts with a yellow "A" on them (possibly meaning "Ace"), white and red skirts, red socks and saddle shoes.

Cheerleader #1

The first cheerleader has long blonde hair that goes all the way down to her shoulders. She bares a striking resemblance to Princess #4.

Cheerleader #2

The second cheerleader has short curly brown hair. She bares a striking resemblance to Princess #2.

Cheerleader #3

The third cheerleader has short dark brown hair. She bares a striking resemblance to Princess #3.

Cheerleader #4

The forth cheerleader has red hair tied in a ponytail. She bares a striking resemblance to Evelyn .

Cheerleader #5

The fifth and final cheerleader has short light brown hair in a flipped hairdo.
