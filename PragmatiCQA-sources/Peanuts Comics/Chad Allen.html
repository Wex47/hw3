Chad Allen (born June 5, 1974 in Cerritos, California) is an actor who provided the voice of Charlie Brown in the 1986 Peanuts television special Happy New Year, Charlie Brown!

Allen is well known for his roles on St. Elsewhere , Our House , My Two Dads , and Dr. Quinn, Medicine Woman . He has guest-starred in various television shows and movies.
