Jason Mendelson is the son of animation executive Lee Mendelson . He provided the voice of Rerun van Pelt in the 1983 season of The Charlie Brown and Snoopy Show . He was the voice of Marcie in Happy New Year, Charlie Brown! , the voice of Milo in It's an Adventure, Charlie Brown and the voice of Peppermint Patty in This is America, Charlie Brown .

Later on, Jason Mendelson worked as a story developer on He's a Bully, Charlie Brown , I Want a Dog for Christmas, Charlie Brown , Lucy Must Be Traded, Charlie Brown , Charlie Brown's Christmas Tales and A Charlie Brown Valentine . He worked as a producer on It's Your 50th Christmas, Charlie Brown , in which he also appears in archive footage, and as a producer and editor on The Making of A Charlie Brown Christmas .
