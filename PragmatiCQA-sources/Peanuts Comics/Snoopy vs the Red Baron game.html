Snoopy vs. the Red Baron is a PS2 and Windows game which made a big hit. Although it is a Peanuts themed game, it is rated E 10+ for cartoon violence. This game featured several iconic Peanuts characters, and Snoopy's recurring and imaginary foe, the Red Baron. The game also supports multiplayer, with two friends being able to team up to claim victory against an enemy team in a dogfight, or fight one another in a battle for triumph. Claiming to be in Snoopy's fantasy, the timeline is confusing, like The Battle of Verdon Gorge, but not everything had to be accurate.

Story

Charlie Brown and Lucy are watching Snoopy sleep on his doghouse, wondering what kind of wild dream the beagle is having. Snoopy suddenly jumps up, moving his arms as if firing the guns of a Sopwith Camel, before going back to sleep. Charlie Brown then deduces that Snoopy is pretending to be a World War I Flying Ace .

Aerodrome Island

By now, Snoopy has started writing a novel on his typewriter, one about the tale of the Flying Ace. Linus has finished proofreading the manuscript, and offers to let Charlie Brown take a look. Brown accepts, and begins reading. However, he is shocked to see that his own dog made him play the part of the Allied janitor in the book. At Aerodrome Island, Scientist Marcie , General Lucy, and Officer Linus help Snoopy hold back an attack being led by the Red Baron, eventually turning the tide and allowing Snoopy, Allied Bomber Peppermint Patty , and Naval Seaman Schroeder to destroy the Baron's base nearby. The Axis powers attempt to avenge their base with a battleship attack on the aerodrome, but Snoopy sinks the ship with help from Woodstock .

The Woods of Montsec

Charlie Brown the janitor is still working in the aerodrome, looking at the blueprints of the mighty Doodlebug bomb Marcie had designed. However, a mysterious hand appears, kidnapping Charlie Brown, and the Axis claims the plans to the Doodlebug bomb as well. Meanwhile, Allied Spy Sally Brown has discovered enemy activity in the Woods of Montsec, and Snoopy is sent to destroy the base the Baron's soldiers have built. The beagle burns the fort down, and with advice from Linus, manages to destroy the machine needed to build a new headquarters, ruining the operation in the woods.

The Front Lines of Verdun

The Allies and Axis are prepared to clash at the field of Verdun, an important battle that could change the war's results. The Allied pillboxes are initially threatened, but soon saved by Snoopy, allowing Peppermint Patty to take down the front lines of the enemy. The Red Baron's troops attempt to retrieve lost ground with an enormous tank, but the plan is foiled when Snoopy sends the tank to the bottom of a river, ending the battle in an Allied victory.

Mines of the Matterhorn

Although defeated at Verdun, the Axis still has the Doodlebug blueprints, and has begun preparing to build the aforementioned bomb. However, the Allies have noticed enemy movement in the Mines of the Matterhorn, and Snoopy and Sally are sent to destroy an important shipment of parts needed to build the weapon. Snoopy quickly forces the train's crew to surrender, and defeats the drilling bot building the tunnels for navigation.

Verdon Gorge

Woodstock successfully sneaks into Charlie Brown's cell, and the round-headed kid gets a message to the Allies with no interception, revealing the location of an important base and where he is imprisoned. Lucy, Rerun van Pelt , and Franklin attack the gorge, but are captured by the Red Baron's forces. Snoopy then attacks, and with directions from Linus, ends the operations and destroys an aircraft carrier. However, the Red Baron then takes full command of the Axis, and reveals a flying fortress before leaving to execute his plan.

Flying Fortress

Snoopy has no choice but to follow, and manages to get past the Baron's forces and free Lucy, Rerun, and Franklin. However, the Red Baron chooses to engage in a dogfight himself, and with Rerun and Franklin both stripped of their weapons, only Snoopy and Lucy can challenge him. The Baron makes quick work of Lucy, shooting her down. After this, he reveals that a Doodlebug was indeed finished, and it is launched at Allied turf, with Charlie Brown strapped on. Snoopy, unable to take down the bomb without harming his owner, does just enough damage for Brown to get loose and escape. He then defeats the Baron and shoots the bomb down, saving the mainland and winning the war.

Ending

Snoopy arrives back at the aerodrome, and a celebration begins. Snoopy then hears Charlie Brown, realizing that it's Suppertime , and he ends writing his story. Later, Charlie Brown and Linus watch Snoopy write on his typewriter, with Charlie Brown explaining that the beagle is now working on a sequel.

Characters

Snoopy

A flying ace on a mission to stop the evil Red Baron, Snoopy rides on the Sopwith Camel (a type of plane used by the Royal Flying Corps). He is a capable fighter in the air, rivaled only by the Red Baron himself.

Linus

Linus serves as the intelligence for the allies and rides in a Zeppelin or a glider and is pretty capable of being unpredictable in flight. Linus can also act as a second in command if commander Lucy is absent. His vehicles are a powered glider and a cargo blimp that carries cargo from base to base.

Pig Pen

Pig Pen owns a supplies ship and helps bring cargo and supplies throughout the allies battleground, as well as working with these supplies as a mechanic. He also owns a shop where Snoopy can purchase new items and characters for use in the game.

Schroeder

Acting as the navy enforcer with his battleship, Schroeder can be a real help when U-boats and PT boats are in the area. In battle he will do a perimeter check on the coast.

Lucy

The general of the Allied Armies, Lucy gives all the orders. She is also a skilled flyer, though not quite at the same level as Snoopy or the Red Baron.

Charlie Brown

Charlie Brown is the janitor for the Allies, working in the bases to keep things clean and tidy. However, he soon becomes a target for the Baron, as he has the plans for the Doodlebug bomb. However, Brown is still able to assist, giving needed info to his friends and allowing Snoopy to perform the final action for victory needed.

Marcie

Marcie is the scientist for the Allies, and the designer of the Doodlebug bomb. She is an important adviser and guide for Snoopy, explaining what Snoopy must do to prevail.

Peppermint Patty

The Allied Bomber pilot, Peppermint Patty is a force against the enemy bases, able to quickly destroy them once Snoopy clears the opposition.

Sally

One of the two Allied spies, Sally provides the Allies they info they need on enemy areas, and also assists Snoopy on stealth related missions, such as the attack on the Mines of the Matterhorn.

Rerun

Rerun is one of the three flying aces of the Allied Armies, along with Snoopy and Franklin. He and Franklin often assist Snoopy in clearing out the enemy, and also help in the attack on Verdon Gorge.

Franklin

Franklin is a flying ace for the Allies, just like Rerun and Snoopy are. Along with Rerun and Snoopy, he can take on the Baron's air forces with skill.

Woodstock

One of the two Allied spies, Woodstock is able to effectively sneak past the Baron's security and into important areas. He also assists Snoopy in important battles on the Woodstock gliders, being of great aid against the battleship, giant tank, and aircraft carrier.

Enemies

Blue Fighters

The blue fighter is the most numerous of all of the enemies you face. They are weak and slow but they can be overwhelming in numbers. Often seen in fleets they usually attack in groups doing a circle around you. Although hard to defeat in numbers, it is easy to pick them off. Sometimes they just crash before you even get the slightest, smallest chance to shoot the specific blue fighter down.

Green Fighters

These dangerous planes have back gunners to make them even more dangerous in numbers as the blue fighters. The front gunner fires a machine gun while the back gunner fires a bomb.

Purple Fighters

Even more dangerous than the green fighters, they are one of the worst enemies you can fight and have the same life level as a blimp. Unlike the blue fighter and the green fighter, they can launch missiles, which can be very deadly towards Snoopy. Like the green fighter they have a secondary weapon.

Blimps

The blimps are powerful opponents, but they themselves do not attack. Instead, they deploy enemy robots to slow Snoopy down. However, they are also able to unleash massive bombs, dealing intense damage to Snoopy, or destroying Allied areas.

Copter Bots

These helicopter-like robots are weak and mostly harmless, but in great numbers they're dangerous.

Bomb Bots

These are flying mine-like Copter Bots that have an explosive payload.

Mines

These mines are just like sea ones, only these instead fly in the air and have magnets on them. If Snoopy and Woodstock get too close to them, they will explode.

P.T. Boats

These enemy boats are dangerous. They attack from the waterways and also launch missiles.

U-Boats

These enemy submarines are dangerous just like the P.T. Boats. They also attack from the waterways and launch missiles.

Driller Bots

These robots walk on two legs have mining drills that they use to drill underground. They also use their drills to try to destroy allied things, like the lighthouse and root beer factory on Aerodrome Island.

Crawler Bots

These giant three legged robots have machine guns to destroy allied things.

Surveillance Bots

These flying spy robots get send onto Aerodrome Island to take surveillance photos of the lighthouse and root beer factory, so Snoopy had to immediately destroy them. They only appeared in the first mission level on Aerodrome Island, Defend Island .

Tanks

They are capable on any terrain and have huge cannons they could use to shot Snoopy and Woodstock down. So they must be careful.

Bomber Reinforcements

These giant flying machines have grabber claws they can use to carry and drop anything the flying circus uses to fight against the allies.

Battleship

The first boss of the game. The Commander brings the ship to Aerodrome Island to use it to destroy the Aerodrome Tower. Snoopy flying the Sopwith Camel pulling Woodstock on the Woodstock Glider on a string, fly to the battleship to sink it before the tower gets destroyed. Woodstock first uses his glider's machine gun to destroy all the cannons on the battleship, then the ship activates it's bomb blaster and a Bomber Reinforcement drops two Crawler Bots onto it to try to shot them down. Woodstock then destroys the bomb blaster and the Crawler Bots, but after destroying them, the battleship activates it's giant cannon and starts shooting the Aerodrome Tower destroying it, and Blue Fighters show up and start to give chase to shot Snoopy and Woodstock down. But Woodstock shots down the Blue Fighters first and Woodstock then starts shooting at the battleship to sink it. After Woodstock keeps on shooting at it, the battleship starts blowing up and the ship sinks to the ocean floor and the Aerodrome Tower is saved.

Harvester

A giant robotic bug-like vehicle, that is the second boss of the game. Snoopy and Woodstock fly after the Harvester since they couldn't let it escape, because if it does, the Red Baron will use it to build another Tree Base. First the Harvester tries to shot Snoopy and Woodstock down by using a balloon launcher to throw giant water balloons at them. Then it tries to shot them down by using it's water cannon, shooting water that will tear the Sopwith apart. The Harvester would then exceed it's water pressure limits, causing it's controls to become unable to respond. Snoopy then shots the Harvester's abdomen while it was defenseless, destroying it. After destroying the abdomen, the Commander turns the Harvester around to try to get Snoopy and Woodstock feel the blast of the Harvester. While the Harvester tries to destroy Snoopy's plane with it's chomping metal teeth, Snoopy shoots at it's mouth and destroys it, which also destroys it's balloon launcher. After destroying the mouth, the Harvester then tries to destroy Snoopy's plane with it's giant chain saws, but Snoopy then shoots at it's arms while it was having more mechanical problems. When shooting it's arms, the Harvester's armor cracks, and Snoopy keeps shooting at the Harvester to finish it off. After keeping on shooting it, the Harvester explodes into smithereens and Snoopy and Woodstock have succeeded to stop it.

Major Catastrophe

A giant tank, that is the third boss of the game. Snoopy flying the Sopwith Camel pulling Woodstock on the Woodstock Glider had to stop it, before the Commander uses it to destroy the city of Verdun. First Woodstock shoots the guns on Major Catastrophe destroying them, then the Commander activates the giant tank's flame cannons to try to burn Snoopy and Woodstock. Woodstock though manages to shot the flame cannons stopping them from firing their flames at them, and then a bunch of ovens on Major Catastrophe flip open. Woodstock then shots the ovens on the tank and destroy them all in the process, but the tank isn't getting hurt. After destroying all of the ovens on the giant tank, the Commander drives Major Catastrophe onto a bridge as the flying circus goes back to it's mission to destroy the allied base. The giant tank then starts firing it's giant cannon destroying the city. Woodstock then gets an idea and thinks they should shoot the bridge supports to make the bridge collapse with the tank on it so it will fall into the river bellow. Lucy thinks it's a brilliant idea and Woodstock starts shooting at the supports underneath the bridge. After Woodstock shoots all of the supports under the bridge on both sides, the bridge collapses and Major Catastrophe falls into the river and sinks to the bottom of the river and Verdun is saved.

Armored Train

A military train that was carrying a shipment of unobtainium that is the fourth boss of the game. Snoopy and Woodstock fly after it in the Sopwith Camel in determine to stop it from escaping. The engine was pulling eight train cars and Snoopy has to shoot and destroy one car at a time. The first car of the train has a snow blower on it that uses to try to shoot Snoopy and Woodstock down by blowing snow at them, that Snoopy can destroy by shooting it. Each train car has weapons that try to shoot Snoopy and Woodstock down on it, and some of the cars are flatbeds with metal pieces that seem to be parts of the Doodlebug. After Snoopy keeps on shooting at a train car, it gets destroyed and uncouples from the rest of the train and Snoopy and Woodstock fly towards the next car. After Snoopy shoots and destroys each of the train cars, Snoopy and Woodstock reach the locomotive. The engine also has a snow blower, just like the eighth car and it also deploys those flying mines. After Snoopy keeps on shooting at the locomotive, the engine explodes and all of the train is finally destroyed and stopped from escaping.

General Mayhem

A giant robotic driller machine that is the fifth boss of the game. Snoopy and Woodstock fly after it in the Sopwith Camel through a tunnel in the Mines of Matterhorn. General Mayhem first activates it's robotic hands and throws electric balls at Snoopy and Woodstock and then tries to zap them with electric bolts. Snoopy though manages to shoot the machine's robotic hands and destroy them. After General Mayhem's robotic hands are destroyed. The Commander activates the driller machine's robotic arm that throws giant power saw blades like frisbees. Every time General Mayhem throws it's giant power saw blades to try to defeat Snoopy and Woodstock with them, Snoopy shoots at the robotic arm that throws them and destroys it, causing the machine's armor to crack, revealing it's not invincible. After General Mayhem's robotic arm that throws it's giant power saw blades is destroyed, the Commander activates the machine's robotic arms that look like excavator arms. General Mayhem then digs it's robotic arms into the ground, making balls of dirt fall from the tunnel ceiling. Then the driller machine makes a big ball of dirt as the Commander gets ready to squish Snoopy and Woodstock, but Snoopy shoots the robotic arms, making the machine drop it's big dirt ball and Snoopy keeps on shooting General Mayhem's armor. The Commander then turns the machine around, making it's giant mining drill face Snoopy and Woodstock, and the machine's drill starts drilling into the tunnel walls, making balls of dirt fly everywhere. After the driller machine's drill keeps trying to destroy Snoopy's plane, Snoopy shoots at the machine's left arm, making it spin out of control causing Snoopy to face it's now destroyed armor and Snoopy keeps on shooting at it. After Snoopy keeps shooting at the machine's armor, General Mayhem finally explodes and the machine is finally destroyed, leaving all that's left of it is it's caterpillar tracks.

Aircraft Carrier

The sixth boss of the game. Snoopy flying the Sopwith Camel again pulling Woodstock on the Woodstock Glider, start flying around the aircraft carrier to sink it. First, the carrier unleashes Blue, Green and Purple Fighters to try to shoot Snoopy and Woodstock down, but Woodstock manages to shoot down all the enemy fighter planes first. While fighting them, Marcie can't find a weak spot on the ship, so Woodstock could just shoot anywhere. U-Boats also emerge out of the water on both sides of the ship while fighting the Blue, Green and Purple Fighters, which Woodstock could sink by shooting at them. After all the enemy fighter planes are shot down, two Bomber Reinforcements arrive at the aircraft carrier. But Woodstock manages to shoot both of them and destroy them in the process. After the Bomber Reinforcements are destroyed, the Commander activates the aircraft carrier's guns to try again to defeat Snoopy and Woodstock. While shooting at the ship's guns destroying them, Woodstock finds a weak spot on the carrier and shoots there to damage the carrier to sink it. After Woodstock keeps on shooting the ship's weak spot, the aircraft carrier finally takes damage and starts sinking. The carrier's stern rises up into the air and it sinks straight down to the bottom of the river. After the aircraft carrier is gone however, the Red Baron tells his flying circus to release his Flying Fortress. The Flying Fortress then emerges out of the water and flies off with the kidnapped pilots. Woodstock then tells Marcie what they're suddenly seeing and Snoopy and Woodstock fly after it.
