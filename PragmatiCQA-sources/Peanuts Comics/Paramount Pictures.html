Paramount Pictures Corporation is a film and television production/distribution company, consistently ranked as one of the largest (top-grossing) movie studios. It is the main namesake subsidiary of Paramount Global. One of its subsidiaries is Nickelodeon , the network that aired You're on Nickelodeon, Charlie Brown .

Paramount was the theatrical distributor of the films Race for Your Life, Charlie Brown (1977) and Bon Voyage, Charlie Brown (And Don't Come Back!!) (1980), and also owns the rights to A Boy Named Charlie Brown (1969) and Snoopy, Come Home (1972). Paramount Home Entertainment also distributed Peanuts specials on video from 1994 to 2006.
