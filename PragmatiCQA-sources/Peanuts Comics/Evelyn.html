Evelyn is a very minor female character who never appeared in the Peanuts comic strip but appears in the animated TV special She's a Good Skate, Charlie Brown . She is one of the figure skaters in the competition which Peppermint Patty enters. She skates very well and scores 3. Evelyn has red hair which she wears in a bun, a yellow shirt, a yellow skirt and a yellow blouse. As is the case with Sandy and Mora , who came before her, Evelyn's name is revealed only at the start of the competition.

Trivia

  • She bares a striking resemblance to Violet Gray .
