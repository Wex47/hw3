Corey Padnos (born March 20, 1989 in California) is an American animator, musician, youtuber and actor. He voiced Linus van Pelt in It's the Pied Piper, Charlie Brown , A Charlie Brown Valentine , Charlie Brown's Christmas Tales , Lucy Must Be Traded, Charlie Brown , and I Want a Dog for Christmas, Charlie Brown . He appears as himself in the documentary Here's to You, Charlie Brown: 50 Great Years .

Padnos also played George Little in Stuart Little 3: Call of the Wild , Young Cory Matthews in Boy Meets World , Joey Eldelman in Diagnosis Murder , Billy in Suddenly Susan , Boy/Narrator in The Cat in the Hat , PCA Student/Smart Kid in Zoey 101 , Porkchop in Batman: Gotham Knight and Cooper in Ben 10: Allen Force .
