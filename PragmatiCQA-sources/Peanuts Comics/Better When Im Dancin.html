"Better When I'm Dancin'" is a song written and sung by pop star Meghan Trainor for The Peanuts Movie . The song strays from Trainor's usual doo-wop-influenced musical style, being described as "breezy and harmless" with traces of house music. The song is used in the film during Charlie Brown 's dance practice montage, the school dance scene, and end credits.
