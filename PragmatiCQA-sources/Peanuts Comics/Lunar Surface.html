The Lunar Surface is the third stage in The Peanuts Movie: Snoopy's Grand Adventure . It takes place on the Earth's moon, hence the name. It can be unlocked when Snoopy clears the second stage, Temple of the Bunnies , and its completion unlocks the fourth stage, Skies Above Paris . He will be subjected to gravity strength changes due to the Moon's lower mass on his way. The Big Stompy appears as the stage boss. Once he suffers defeat, Lucy Van Pelt is freed.

Stages

This area's stages have a baseball theme for their names.

  1. Fly Balls
  2. Right Field Space
  3. Cosmic Punters
  4. Blue Cosmos
  5. Big Stompy's Rapid Run (Boss Battle)

Trivia

  • Despite the area's lack of air, Snoopy is able to survive in this level with no helmet.
