Gini Holtzman is a former child actress who provided the voice of Peppermint Patty during the 1985 season of The Charlie Brown and Snoopy Show (for which she won a Young Artist Award for "Outstanding Young Actress: Animation Voice-Over" in 1986) and in two Peanuts television specials: It's Flashbeagle, Charlie Brown and Snoopy's Getting Married, Charlie Brown .

Holtzman did voice-over work for the television movies Cabbage Patch Kids: First Christmas and Happily Ever After . Gini Holtzman appears as herself in the 1985 Peanuts television documentary It's Your 20th Television Anniversary, Charlie Brown and the 1990 documentary You Don't Look 40, Charlie Brown .
