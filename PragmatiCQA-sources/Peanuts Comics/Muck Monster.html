The Muck Monster is an enemy appearing in The Peanuts Movie: Snoopy's Grand Adventure . He is the boss of the Underground zone.

Appearance

The Muck Monster is a green monster covered in slime. His hair is full of junk, making the creature slightly resemble a familiar dirty semi-major character .

Battle

The Muck Monster attacks by causing the slime in his arena to rise. To escape, Snoopy has to make his way upward. In Snoopy's way, however, are smaller versions of the Muck Monster. They leave behind slime in their wake, slowing down anyone who touches their trail. Snoopy can jump on their heads to destroy them, but there is no need to do so.

When Snoopy reaches the platform that "Pig-Pen" is standing on, the Muck Monster is defeated.

Trivia

  • The Muck Monster is one of two bosses to "chase" the player during the battle. The Big Stompy (Lunar Surface) is the other, though it chases the player to the right during his battle, whereas the Muck Monster chases the player upward.
  • The Muck Monster is one of two bosses to attack from the background. The Mask Downs in Peppermint Jungle is the other.
