Tracy Stratford (born January 19, 1955 in Los Angeles, California) is a former child actress who was the first voice of Lucy van Pelt .

Stratford provided the voice for Lucy in the 1963 documentary A Boy Named Charlie Brown (singing voice only), and in the first Peanuts animated television special, A Charlie Brown Christmas , in 1965. Sally Dryer replaced Stratford as the voice of Lucy in subsequent Peanuts television specials of the 1960s.

As of 2014, Stratford resides in Washington State where she works as a high school librarian and has taught and coached drama.
