Fox is an American broadcast television network formerly owned by 20th Century Fox parent 21st Century Fox and currently owned by Fox Corporation . It is based in Los Angeles, California.

Fox was the network that aired the television debut of Happiness Is a Warm Blanket, Charlie Brown on November 24, 2011. It was later reran near the Christmas holiday in 2012 and 2013. Fox has also aired the 2015 film The Peanuts Movie since February 2018.
