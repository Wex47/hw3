Duncan Waston (born 1963) is a former child actor who voiced Charlie Brown between 1975 and 1977. Watson provided the voice for the character in the Peanuts animated television specials, Be My Valentine, Charlie Brown (1975), You're a Good Sport, Charlie Brown (1975) and Happy Anniversary, Charlie Brown (1976). He was also the voice of the same character in the 1977 feature length Peanuts movie, Race for Your Life, Charlie Brown .

He currently resides in Southwest New Hampshire and works for the City of Keene, NH in Public Works. He has two daughters, Samantha and Selma.
