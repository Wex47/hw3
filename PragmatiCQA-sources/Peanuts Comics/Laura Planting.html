Laura Planting (born April 18, 1966 in Santa Clara, California) is a former child actress who provided the voice of Peppermint Patty in the Peanuts television special It's Your First Kiss, Charlie Brown and Lucy van Pelt in She's a Good Skate, Charlie Brown and Bon Voyage, Charlie Brown (And Don't Come Back!!) .
