Wesley Steven Singerman (born August 23, 1990) is an American voice actor. He is most recognized for his role in the 2007 Walt Disney animated film Meet the Robinsons as Wilbur Robinson. Singerman also voiced Charlie Brown in A Charlie Brown Valentine , Charlie Brown's Christmas Tales and Lucy Must Be Traded, Charlie Brown . He is also in the technical metal band Fate by Fire, for which he plays lead guitar.
