Violette Honfleur is a very minor female character who never appeared in the Peanuts comic strip but is a girl from France, She only appears in the movie Bon Voyage, Charlie Brown (And Don't Come Back!!) , in which she is voiced by Roseline Rubens , and makes a cameo appearance in What Have We Learned, Charlie Brown? . She has orange hair and wears a white shirt, pink pinafore dress and tan shoes.
