Linda Ercoli (born on March 7, 1960 in California) is a former child actress who provided the voice of Violet in You're Not Elected, Charlie Brown , Be My Valentine, Charlie Brown (for which she also provided the voice of Frieda ), and Happy Anniversary, Charlie Brown .

Ercoli provided the voice of Peppermint Patty in It's the Easter Beagle, Charlie Brown and Race for Your Life, Charlie Brown (archival audio; uncredited only) and the voice of Clara in the 1972 film Snoopy Come Home .
