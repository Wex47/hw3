"Hark! The Herald Angels Sing" is an English Christmas carol which first appeared in print in 1739.

In A Charlie Brown Christmas , the tune is hummed by Christopher Shea , Tracy Stratford , Kathy Steinberg , Chris Doran , Karen Mendelson , Sally Dryer , Ann Altieri and Bill Melendez . It is sung by Peter Robbins , Christopher Shea, Tracy Stratford, Kathy Steinberg, Chris Doran, Karen Mendelson, Sally Dryer and Ann Altieri. In It's Your 50th Christmas, Charlie Brown , it is sung by Pentatonix and the All-American Boys Chorus in celebration of the special.
