Kristie Baker is a former child actress known for providing the voice of Peppermint Patty in two Peanuts television specials: Happy New Year, Charlie Brown! (for which she won a Young Artist Award for "Exceptional Young Actress in Animation: Series, Specials, or Feature Film" in 1987) and .

In 1990, Baker appeared as herself in the 1990 television documentary special, You Don't Look 40, Charlie Brown . Baker's last film credit was in the 1992 film Killer Image .
