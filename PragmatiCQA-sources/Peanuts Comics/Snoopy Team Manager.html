"Snoopy: Team Manager" is the second episode of The Charlie Brown and Snoopy Show . It premiered on September 24, 1983.

Shorts

"Shoveling"

In "Shoveling", Linus and Snoopy try to help Lucy plant her garden .

"Re-Run"

In "Re-Run", Rerun shares some of his anxieties while he rides on the back of his mom's bike.

Some of his bike-riding anxieties would later be reused in the special I Want a Dog for Christmas, Charlie Brown .

"Lost Blanket"

Linus' blanket-hating grandmother is coming to visit. Linus mails his blanket to himself so that he have it back after his grandmother has left. However, the blanket does not return. The whole time, Snoopy has it.

"The Manager"

This is the longest short in the episode and gives the episode its name.

Snoopy is the new manager of the baseball team .

Notes

  • This is one of only two episodes that Sally Brown does not appear in. The other is " Snoopy and the Giant ."
