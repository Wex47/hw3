Richard M. Sherman and Robert B. Sherman are world renowned music composers who composed the songs for Snoopy, Come Home . Other animated features where they composed music include such famous musical movies like The Many Adventures of Winnie the Pooh , The Sword in the Stone , The Aristocats , The Jungle Book , Mary Poppins , Chitty-Chitty-Bang-Bang , The Aristocats , Bedknobs and Broomsticks , Charlotte's Web , The Happiest Millionaire and Tom Sawyer .
