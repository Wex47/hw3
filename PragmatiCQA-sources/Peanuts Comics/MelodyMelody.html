Melody-Melody is a minor female character who has never appeared in the Peanuts comic strip, but appears as the hidden main antagonist in the TV special You're in the Super Bowl, Charlie Brown . She is voiced by Crystal Kuns .

History

In the special, Melody-Melody catches the attention of Linus van Pelt and resulting in his development of a crush on her right away. Linus and Charlie Brown go out to get Melody-Melody a hot fudge sundae. They tell her about a contest which they are entering, where the winner wins free tickets to the Super Bowl. She thinks it is exciting. She tells them that she will come watch them and will be rooting for them. What Charlie Brown and Linus do not realize is that Melody plans on entering the contest herself. She ends up winning the contest, and leaves Linus heartbroken and betrayed.

Personality

In the beginning of the special, Melody-Melody appears to be a very nice and sweet girl when she first the two . When the three go out for ice cream, they talk about the Punt, Pass and Kick contest, to which Charlie Brown, Linus and the other Peanuts are entering, to which Melody promises she'd be rooting for them. However, her true colors are shown where it's revealed that she's the final contestant, completely showing she is nothing more then a deceitful backstabber. She also shows no regrets for actions as she happily brags to Linus about she's going to the Super Bowl, displaying her arrogance.

Physical appearance

Melody-Melody has curly red hair which she wears in two buns, making her look almost similar to Frieda . Throughout most of the special, she is seen wearing a purple shirt, green pants, and white shoes. At the end of the special, she is seen a sleeveless white shirt with a star motif and shorts.

Trivia

  • She's revealed to be the main antagonist of the TV special, as she lies to and betrays Linus and Charlie Brown in order to win the contest and doesn't regret her actions. She's also shown to be somewhat of a femme fatale; a very beautiful female character who causes disaster for the boys she becomes involved with; this is proven true based on her actions.
  • She bares a striking resemblance to Frieda and The Little Red-Haired Girl.
  • She is one of the many characters in the series to have a pointed nose. The others are Frieda , Lila , Lydia , Peggy Jean , Babette , Jaques , Dolly , Janice Emmons , Mimi , Evelyn , Mary Jo , Nell , The Four Princesses , The Cheerleading Squad , Charlotte Braun , C.A.R.A. and The Little Red-Haired Girl.
