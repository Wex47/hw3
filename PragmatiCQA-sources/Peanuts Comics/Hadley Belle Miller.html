Hadley Belle Miller is an actress who has provided the voice of Lucy van Pelt in The Peanuts Movie .

Her television credits include the 2014 TV pilot Branson the Sitcom and voice-over work for Barbie: Dreamtopia and Sofia the First . Her theater credits include appearing in plays such as Annie , The Sound of Music , Shrek the Musical and A Christmas Carol .
