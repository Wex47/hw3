Francesca Capaldi (born Francesca Angelucci Capaldi on June 8, 2004 in La Jolla, California) is an American child actress who provided the voices of the Little Red-Haired Girl and Frieda in The Peanuts Movie .

She began her acting career with small roles on the Disney Channel 's ANT Farm and CBS 's How I Met Your Mother , and is best known for portraying Chloe James in Dog with a Blog .
