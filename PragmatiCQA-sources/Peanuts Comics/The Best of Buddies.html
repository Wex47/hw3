"The Best of Buddies" is a song from the 1972 animated film Snoopy, Come Home . It was written by Richard M. Sherman and Robert B. Sherman . The song refers to the friendship between Snoopy and Woodstock the first time that it is sung in the film. The song's reprise also references the feelings that Charlie Brown , Sally , Linus , Lucy and Schroeder have towards Snoopy.
