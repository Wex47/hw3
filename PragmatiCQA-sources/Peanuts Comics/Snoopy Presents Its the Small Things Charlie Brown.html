Snoopy Presents: It's the Small Things, Charlie Brown is the Peanuts Earth Day TV special and the forty-seventh Peanuts TV special based on the comic strip by Charles M. Schulz . It began streaming on Apple TV+ on April 15, 2022. The special also features an original song by American singer-songwriter Ben Folds.

Charlie Brown wants to play a baseball game, but Sally Brown shows great dedication to a dandelion flower that resembles her.

Trivia

  • When Sally sprays water on Frieda, the way Frieda cries "My naturally curly hair!" sounds identical to the way she cried the same quote in The Peanuts Movie .
  • This marks the first animated appearances of "Badcall" Benny , Cormac , Shirley , Larry, and Ethan .
  • This is the first speaking appearance of 5 in 38 years as he last spoke in It's Flashbeagle, Charlie Brown .
  • Sally became the assistant manager of her brother's baseball team "The Dandelions".
