Sally Dryer (born February 10, 1957 in San Mateo, California) is a former child actor. She provided the voices for several Peanuts characters between 1963 and 1969. She was the second voice of Lucy van Pelt from 1966 to 1968.

Dryer provided the voice for Violet in A Boy Named Charlie Brown and A Charlie Brown Christmas before going on to voice Lucy in Charlie Brown's All-Stars , It's the Great Pumpkin, Charlie Brown , You're in Love, Charlie Brown and He's Your Dog, Charlie Brown . Dryer was also the voice of Patty in the movie A Boy Named Charlie Brown and voiced Shirley , Sophie , and Clara in It Was a Short Summer, Charlie Brown .

As of 2014, Dryer volunteers for the Humane Society and does optical sculpture as well. She also owned her own picture frame business in Burlingame, California.
