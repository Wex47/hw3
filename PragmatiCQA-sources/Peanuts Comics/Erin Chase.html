Erin Chase is an American former child actress. She voiced Charlie Brown in " The Mayflower Voyagers ", the first episode of the This Is America, Charlie Brown miniseries. She is also the first female to voice Charlie Brown.

Chase began her career when she was 6 months old, modeling in an advertisement for Buffums department store.

She also played Martha Miller in the TV series Aaron's Way and was in Edith Ann: Homeless Goes Home .
