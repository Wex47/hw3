Snoopy Presents: To Mom (and Dad), With Love is the Peanuts Mother's Day TV special and the forty-eighth Peanuts TV special based on the comic strip by Charles M. Schulz . It began streaming on Apple TV+ on May 6, 2022.

Mother's Day is coming, and everybody is excited, all except Peppermint Patty , who never had a mother, so she decides to celebrate her father instead since he does mother things.
