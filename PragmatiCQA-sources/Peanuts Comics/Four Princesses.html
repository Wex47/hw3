The four unnamed princesses are four very minor female Peanuts characters who has never appeared in the strip, but appear in the TV special, It's Your First Kiss, Charlie Brown . They are four very beautiful girls who are the homecoming princesses of their school's homecoming event. They are mainly seen with the Little Red-Haired Girl .

History

They are four very beautiful little girls who are the princesses of their school's homecoming event. They are mainly seen with the queen, the Little Red-Haired Girl . As tradition, they are paired up with a escort and watch as the queen's escort kisses the homecoming queen on the cheek. They take great pride in their beauty as they are often shown flipping their hair and smiling.

Physical appearance

Each girl has eyelashes, rosy cheeks and a pointed nose. They all wear matching magenta dresses, white socks and black Mary Jane shoes. At the homecoming dance, they all wear their regular attire with pink robes and white gloves. Their appearances are very different in the book adaptation of the TV special.

Homecoming Princess #1

The first princess has long light brown hair with bangs covering her forehead. Her escort is Franklin . In the book adaptation, she has short blonde hair and her dress is colored yellow.

Homecoming Princess #2

The second princess has short curly blonde hair, making her look almost similar to a blonde Frieda and Tapioca Pudding . Her escort is Linus . In the book adaptation, she wears a bright green dress and her hair is red.

Homecoming Princess #3

The third princess has short dark brown hair with bangs covering her forehead, making her look almost similar to both Pattys . Her escort is "Pig-Pen" . She retains her dress in the book adaptation, except her hair is light brown.

Homecoming Princess #4

The fourth and final princess has long straight blonde hair, making her look almost similar to Lila . Her escort is Schroeder . In the book adaptation, she wears a seafoam green dress and her hair is black.
