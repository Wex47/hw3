The Temple of the Bunnies is the second stage in Story Mode in The Peanuts Movie: Snoopy's Grand Adventure . It is set in a ruin.

The stage is unlocked when Snoopy clears the Peppermint Jungle stage, and its completion unlocks the Lunar Surface stage. It is where Linus van Pelt resides before his liberation from the area. King Totem is the boss of this level. Once he is defeated, he will relinquish Linus.
