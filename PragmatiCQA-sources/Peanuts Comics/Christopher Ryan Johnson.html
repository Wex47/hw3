Christopher Ryan Johnson is an American former child actor. He voiced Schroeder in A Charlie Brown Valentine , Charlie Brown's Christmas Tales and Lucy Must Be Traded, Charlie Brown . He also played Chris Partridge in The David Cassidy Story , Flower Seller and Young Boy in General Hospital , Boy in Dickle Roberts: Former Child Star , Gage McAllister in Franklin Charter , Bully #1 in The War at Home , Taji in the video game Tokobot Plus: Mysteries of Karakuri , Friend #1 in Wizards of Waverly Place , Willy in The Riches , Chorus #5 in House M.D , Young Tom in Criminal Minds and appeared in a gospel choir in Faster .
