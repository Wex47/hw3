"The Great Writer" is a song from the stage musical Snoopy!!! The Musical and the animated TV special of the . It is sung by Snoopy while he is sitting on top of his doghouse and imagining that he is a world-famous author .

The story that Snoopy writes during the course of the song makes absolutely no sense whatsoever. At the conclusion of the song, Snoopy mails off his story. He receives a rejection slip which tells him that his story is "stupid", that he is a "terrible writer" and that he should "drop dead". However, the beagle believes that this is the same standard rejection letter that everyone receives and announces that he will start work on an unnamed trilogy the next day.
