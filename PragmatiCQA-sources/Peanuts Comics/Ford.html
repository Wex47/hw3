The Ford Motor Company , often referred to simply as Ford , is a multinational company based in Dearborn, Michigan which manufactures and sells automobiles. It was founded by Henry Ford in 1903.

Animated commercials for the Ford Motor Company featuring the Peanuts characters, which were produced by Bill Melendez , were made for American television in early 1959. They were the first Peanuts animated cartoons, pre-dating A Charlie Brown Christmas , the first TV special, by some six years.
