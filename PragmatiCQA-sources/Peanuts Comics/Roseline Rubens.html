Roseline Rubens is the actress who provided the voice of Violette in the 1980 film, Bon Voyage, Charlie Brown (And Don't Come Back!!) She provided the voices of Patty , Sophie , and Violet in the film as well. She also voiced Frieda (laughing only) one of the cheerleaders in It's Your First Kiss, Charlie Brown .
