Greg Felton is a former child actor who provided the voice of Schroeder in the 1977 film Race for Your Life, Charlie Brown (for which he also provided the voice of the camp announcer) and in three Peanuts television specials, It's the Easter Beagle, Charlie Brown , Be My Valentine, Charlie Brown and It's Arbor Day, Charlie Brown .
