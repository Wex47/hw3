It's Your 20th Television Anniversary, Charlie Brown is a documentary about the Peanuts comic strip and the animated TV specials which are based on it. It is hosted by Charles Schulz . It originally aired on CBS on May 14, 1985.

Summary

The documentary talks about the history of the comic strip series and the making of the TV specials. Interviews with past voice actors, such as Peter Robbins , Pamelyn Ferdin and Chris Inglis , are included.

Notes

  • The opening titles for this special would later be used as one for the second season of The Charlie Brown and Snoopy Show .
  • Clips from this documentary and You Don't Look 40, Charlie Brown would be used for An Enduring Classic: Peanuts Creator Charles Schulz .
  • This special shows some behind the scenes footage for the classroom scene in It's Flashbeagle, Charlie Brown .
