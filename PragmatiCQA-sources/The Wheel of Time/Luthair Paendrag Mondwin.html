Luthair Paendrag Mondwin is the son of the famous Artur Hawkwing . He was born in FY 967 to Artur Hawkwing and Tamika . His birth name is Luthair Paendrag Mondwin (pronunciation: LEW-thair PAY-ehn-DRAG MON-dwihn).

A spread-winged hawk clutching three lightning bolts in its claws and displayed on a white background was the banner of Luthair Paendrag. His banner would later serve as the basis for the banner of Seanchan itself, the only difference being the addition of a wide blue border.

History

In FY 992 , Artur Hawkwing sent a massive army of 2,000 ships across the Aryth Ocean to Seanchan ; this army was headed by Luthair. Lords and ladies of Seanchan are said to be of his blood .

Oaths to protect Luthair's son were sworn over Luthair's dead body, beginning the tradition of the Deathwatch Guards .

Deain introduced her invention, the A'dam , to Luthair.
