Taringail Damodred was the nephew of King Laman Damodred , the son of Dalresin Damodred , and the half-brother of Anvaere , Innloine , and Moiraine Damodred . His sign was a golden double-bitted battleaxe.

History

He married Tigraine Mantear , then the Daughter-Heir of Andor , and sired a son, Galadedrid . After Tigraine's disappearance and Tigraine's mother Queen Mordrellen 's death, he married the new Queen , Morgase Trakand , in order to solidify her claim to the Lion Throne . In 978 NE , his uncle Laman was killed in the final battle of the Aiel War , but Taringail was prevented by law from acceding to the Sun Throne due to his marriage with the Queen of Andor. As Taringail was barred from the throne; Moiraine did not wish to be queen, and their two other sisters were unsuited to ruling, the Sun Throne passed from House Damodred , and Galldrian Riatin became the next king.

The following year, his first child with Morgase was born; a son named Gawyn , and in 981 NE , a Daughter-Heir of Andor was born to the couple, named Elayne . In 984 NE Taringail died in a "hunting accident" supposedly orchestrated by Thomdril Merrilin after the gleeman discovered Taringail's intent to murder Morgase and see himself as the first king of Andor.

In 1000 NE , Taringail's daughter Elayne became Queen of Cairhien by virtue of her claim through her father.
