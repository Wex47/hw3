The Royal Palace of Andor is located in the very center of Caemlyn . Like most of the Inner City , it was originally built by Ogier stonemasons. It holds the Lion Throne within the Grand Hall, the Royal Apartments, and several courtyards and stables.

It also houses a number of reception rooms, including the Blue Reception Room .

The Palace was heavily damaged during the fight between Rand and Rahvin , with the use of balefire .

The Palace was occupied and probably destroyed or heavily damaged when the Shadowspawn conquered Caemlyn .
