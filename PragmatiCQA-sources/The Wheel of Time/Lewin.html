History

Lewin was the father of Jeordam and the grandson of Adan .

On an ill-fated attempt to rescue some kidnapped girls, including Lewin’s sister Maigran, Lewin and a group of friends killed the kidnappers in self defense, thus becoming the first Aiel to stray from the Way of the Leaf. After being exiled from the other Da'shain Aiel , Lewin declared that he is still Aiel, and went on to shadow the Da’shain camp to provide protection. The dust veils Lewin and his friends wore to keep off the dust became the shoufa worn by the Aiel in the later years.

Lewin was an ancestor of Rand al'Thor .
