Toman Head is a peninsula that extends of the west coast of Westlands . It lies in between Arad Doman and Tarabon , claimed by neither and both, depending on the state of relations between the two nations. The only notable city on the peninsula is Falme , at the very tip.

Toman Head became famous across the Westlands when the Seanchan armies met their first defeat, at the hands of the Heroes of the Horn .
