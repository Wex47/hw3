Braem Wood is an extensive area of woodland on the northeastern borders of Andor , east and southeast of Caralain Grass and along the western banks of the River Erinin , south of Tar Valon .

Braem Wood is quite large and consists of numerous lesser forests with many towns and villages located within its boughs. The largest town of the region is New Braem , located near the site of Braem , an Ogier -built city of Coremanda that was destroyed in the Trolloc Wars . The main highway linking Caemlyn to Tar Valon runs through Braem Wood.

Other Andoran towns and villages located within Braem Wood include Kore Springs and Jornhill .
