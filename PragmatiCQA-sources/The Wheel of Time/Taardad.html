The Taardad are one of the twelve Aiel clans. Their clan hold is Cold Rocks Hold . The Taardad are the staunchest of Rand’s supporters, sending 15,000 of their spears with Rand as he travels to Alcair Dal . They are not friendly with the Shaido Aiel, finding them shifty and underhanded. They were one of the four clans to cross the Dragonwall during the Aiel War . They were led by Janduin during the Aiel War.
