V'saine was a city that existed during the Age of Legends . It was best known as the home of Collam Daan , a great university of silver and blue domes whose architecture was overshadowed only by the Sharom that floated overhead. Like all other cities of the Age of Legends, it was utterly ruined and only legends of it have survived.

The only known descriptions of the city of V'saine are given by those who visit the Glass Columns of Rhuidean and the Visions shown within and of those, only Rand al'Thors vision have been spoken aloud.
