Channelers are those capable of using the One Power . About 2-3 percent of the population in the Age of Legends had the ability to learn to channel. This category provides a comprehensive list of all characters who can channel. This category includes - but is not limited to - Aes Sedai , Windfinders , Kinsfolk , Some Wise Ones , Damane , Asha'man , Forsaken , and Aes Sedai from the Age of Legends . It also includes channelers who are unaware of their ability to channel such as Sul'dam or wilders as well as former channelers who have been either burned out or stilled .

Additional subcategories help break this master group down into more narrowly specific groups of channelers.
