This timeline attempts to lay out the history of the Westlands and the world based on dates and information given in the novels and supporting material.

Dates are given in the three calendar systems given in the books, namely the Toman, Gazaran and Farede calendars. For events in the Age of Legends , dates are given prior to the Breaking , since no information has been retained about dating systems used at that time.
