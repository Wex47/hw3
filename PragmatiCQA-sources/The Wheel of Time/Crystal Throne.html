The Crystal Throne is the seat of the Emperor or Empress at the Court of the Nine Moons in Seanchan . It was a great ter'angreal that caused anyone who approached it to feel immense awe and wonder. Of course, only the reigning monarch was ever allowed to use it. Its disposition was unknown after the chaos in Seanchan and the death of Empress Radhanan . The term could also be used for any throne on which the Emperor or Empress sat.

The Throne does not require a Channeler or the One Power in any form to work.
