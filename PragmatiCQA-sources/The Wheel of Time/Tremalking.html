Tremalking is a large and highly mountainous island found southwest of the main landmass of the Westlands . It is the largest of the Sea Folk islands. The female statue of the Choedan Kal stands on the island. It is populated by the peaceful Amayar .

History

The peaceful Amayar lived on Tremalking for centuries, and manufactured precious porcelain. They were governed by the Atha'an Miere , and had a friendly relationship, though did not interact much on a personal basis.

During the cleansing of saidin , the Amayar observed the hand of the female Choedan Kal glowing as Nynaeve al'Meara used the access key in Shadar Logoth . The Amayar interpreted this as the end of the Time of Illusion , and committed mass suicide.
